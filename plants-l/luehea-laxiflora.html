Luehea laxiflora

Luehea laxiflora, commonly known as the silky ceiba, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a fibrous, light-colored wood that is sometimes used in construction. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Luehea laxiflora plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
