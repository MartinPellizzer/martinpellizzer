Lippia callensii

Lippia callensii, commonly known as the Mexican blue sage, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its use in traditional medicine.

Lippia callensii is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in perfumery and aromatherapy.
