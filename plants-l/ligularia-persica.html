Ligularia persica

Ligularia persica, commonly known as Persian ligularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants.
