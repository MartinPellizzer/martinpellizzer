Languas suishaensis

Languas suishaensis, commonly known as the Suisha flamebush, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters along the branches. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Languas suishaensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
