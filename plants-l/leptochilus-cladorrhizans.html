Leptochilus cladorrhizans

Leptochilus cladorrhizans, commonly known as the clustered root orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit and possesses a short, fleshy rhizome. The plant typically produces a single, erect stem bearing several oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a greenish-yellow coloration.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
