Liparis sessilis

Liparis sessilis, commonly known as the sessile orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces a spike of small, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are fibrous and often grow in dense clusters.

Liparis sessilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
