Leucas oligocephala var. bowalensis

Leucas oligocephala var. bowalensis, commonly known as the Bowalensis leucas, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual appeal.
