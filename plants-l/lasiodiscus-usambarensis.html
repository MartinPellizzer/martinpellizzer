Lasiodiscus usambarensis

It belongs to the family Viscaraceae and is characterized by its hemiparasitic nature, deriving some nutrients from its host plant while photosynthesizing independently. Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
