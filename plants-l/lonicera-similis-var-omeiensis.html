Lonicera similis var. omeiensis

Lonicera similis var. omeiensis, commonly known as the Omei honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in clusters, typically yellow or orange in color, which bloom in late spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
