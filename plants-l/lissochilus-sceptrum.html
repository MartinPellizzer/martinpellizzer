Lissochilus sceptrum

Lissochilus sceptrum, commonly known as the royal orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of white, purple, and pink hues. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern around the base of the plant.

Lissochilus sceptrum thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in high-altitude cloud forests.
