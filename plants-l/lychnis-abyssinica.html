Lychnis abyssinica

Lychnis abyssinica, commonly known as Abyssinian catchfly, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features small, pink to reddish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Lychnis abyssinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
