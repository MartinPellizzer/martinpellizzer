Lagerstroemia inopinata

Lagerstroemia inopinata, commonly known as the Chinese crape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered panicles of pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has smooth, grayish bark that peels in thin, papery layers. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and turn vibrant yellow in the fall.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
