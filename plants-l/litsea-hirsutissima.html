Litsea hirsutissima

Litsea hirsutissima, commonly known as the hairy leaf litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of China, India, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, hairy leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. It typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The fruit is a small, smooth, yellow drupe that ripens in the fall.
