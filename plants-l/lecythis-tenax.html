Lecythis tenax

Lecythis tenax, commonly known as the rubber tree or Brazilian rubber tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. Its fruits are large, woody capsules that split open to reveal bright red, edible seeds.
