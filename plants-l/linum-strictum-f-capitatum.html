Linum strictum f. capitatum

Linum strictum f. capitatum, commonly known as the clustered flax, is a flowering plant native to regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate blooms and compact growth habit.
