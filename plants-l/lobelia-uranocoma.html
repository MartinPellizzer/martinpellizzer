Lobelia uranocoma

Lobelia uranocoma, commonly known as the silverleaf lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or alpine habitats. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
