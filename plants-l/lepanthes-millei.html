Lepanthes millei

Lepanthes millei, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Lepanthes millei is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
