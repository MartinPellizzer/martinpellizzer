Ligularia palmatifida

Ligularia palmatifida, commonly known as the broad-leaved ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in clustered inflorescences during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in shaded gardens.
