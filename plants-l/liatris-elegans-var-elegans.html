Liatris elegans var. elegans

Liatris elegans var. elegans, commonly known as the showy blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the central and eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features tall, slender stems with whorled, grass-like leaves and produces striking purple to pink spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Liatris elegans var. elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
