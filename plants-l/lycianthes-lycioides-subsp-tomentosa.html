Lycianthes lycioides subsp. tomentosa

Lycianthes lycioides subsp.

tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers.

The plant is characterized by its hairy, tomentose leaves and stems, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into orange or red berries.
