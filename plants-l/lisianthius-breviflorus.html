Lisianthius breviflorus

Lisianthius breviflorus, commonly known as the short-flowered lisianthus, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
