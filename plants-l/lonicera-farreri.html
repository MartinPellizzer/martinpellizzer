Lonicera farreri

Lonicera farreri, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to gardens.

The fruit, which follows the flowers, is a red, berry-like structure that contains several seeds.
