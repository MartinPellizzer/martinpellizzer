Leymus interior

Leymus interior, commonly known as bluegrass, is a cool-season grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, bluish-green blades and tufted growth habit. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. Leymus interior is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to form dense, sod-like vegetation.

It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
