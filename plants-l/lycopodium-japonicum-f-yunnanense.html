Lycopodium japonicum f. yunnanense

Lycopodium japonicum f. yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan clubmoss, is a fern-like plant belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and distinctive spore cones that are clustered at the tips of its branches. Native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its foliage often forms dense mats, contributing to soil stabilization in its natural habitat.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
