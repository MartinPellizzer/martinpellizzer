Lobelia parvifolia

Lobelia parvifolia, commonly known as the small-flowered lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Lobelia parvifolia is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and forested areas.
