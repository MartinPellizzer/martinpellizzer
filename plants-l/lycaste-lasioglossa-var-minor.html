Lycaste lasioglossa var. minor

Lycaste lasioglossa var. minor, commonly known as the miniature yellow lycaste, is a rare and compact orchid species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, narrow pseudobulbs and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellow-orange flowers with prominent, hairy sepals and petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often cultivated by experienced orchid growers.
