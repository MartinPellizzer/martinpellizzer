Lemaireocereus setispinus

Lemaireocereus setispinus, commonly known as the Christmas star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, thick stems and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant has thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its leaves are typically small and spineless, and it produces small, white flowers that develop into red berries.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and seasonal blooming.
