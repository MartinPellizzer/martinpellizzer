Lunania divaricata

Lunania divaricata, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers, and is known for its toxic properties. Its leaves and berries contain alkaloids that can be harmful to humans and animals.
