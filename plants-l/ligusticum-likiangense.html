Ligusticum likiangense

Ligusticum likiangense, commonly known as the Yunnan lovage, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its aromatic, compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in Chinese medicine to treat ailments such as rheumatism and digestive issues. The plant thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.
