Laserpitium siculum

Laserpitium siculum, commonly known as Sicilian grass-vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant that typically grows in dry, rocky, or disturbed soils. The plant features slender stems and small, pea-like flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate and pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Laserpitium siculum is native to southern Europe and is often found in Mediterranean regions.
