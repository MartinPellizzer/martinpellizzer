Laelia anceps var. calvertiana

Laelia anceps var. calvertiana, commonly known as the Calvert's laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms in winter and early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are broad, leathery, and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in moderate light conditions.
