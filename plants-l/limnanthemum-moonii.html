Limnanthemum moonii

Limnanthemum moonii, commonly known as the moonflower.

This flowering plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its nocturnal blooming habit. It produces large, fragrant, white flowers that open at night and close by morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
