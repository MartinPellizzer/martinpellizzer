Ladenbergia tenuiflora

Ladenbergia tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered legume, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its bipinnate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces elongated, slender pods that contain several seeds. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, known for its nitrogen-fixing abilities.

Ladenbergia tenuiflora is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
