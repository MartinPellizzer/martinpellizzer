Limonium corsicum

Limonium corsicum, commonly known as sea helene, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces showy, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, preferring sandy or gravelly soils and full sun exposure. Its flowers bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to coastal landscapes.

Limonium corsicum is also valued for its ornamental use in gardens and its ability to thrive in saline environments.
