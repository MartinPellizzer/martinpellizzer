Lastrea diversisora

Lastrea diversisora, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its woody stems and large, showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in dry environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in hot, dry climates.
