Libinhania arachnoides

Libinhania arachnoides, commonly known as the spider-scale tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, scale-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its bark is thick and rugged, often featuring a rough, fissured texture.

Libinhania arachnoides is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
