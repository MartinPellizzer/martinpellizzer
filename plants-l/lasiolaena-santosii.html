Lasiolaena santosii

Lasiolaena santosii, commonly known as the Santosii grass, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its flowers are small and clustered, often forming dense inflorescences.

Lasiolaena santosii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
