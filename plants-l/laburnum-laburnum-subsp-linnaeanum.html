Laburnum laburnum subsp. linnaeanum

Laburnum laburnum subsp.

linnaeanum, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe. It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear in spring.

The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its seeds are enclosed in long, slender pods that split open to release the seeds.
