Listrostachys cirrhosa

Listrostachys cirrhosa, commonly known as the silky broomweed, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Listrostachys cirrhosa is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
