Lepturus filiformis var. subcurvatus

Lepturus filiformis var. subcurvatus, commonly known as the slender-leaved spurge, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or stream banks.
