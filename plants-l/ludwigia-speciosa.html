Ludwigia speciosa

Ludwigia speciosa, commonly known as the pretty Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Ludwigia speciosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in water gardens and ponds due to its aesthetic appeal.
