Lastrea tenuicula

Lastrea tenuicula, commonly known as the slender lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, branching stems that are covered in small, rounded leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
