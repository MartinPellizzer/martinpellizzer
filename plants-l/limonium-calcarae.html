Limonium calcarae

Limonium calcarae, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiky, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in coastal areas and saline soils. Limonium calcarae is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
