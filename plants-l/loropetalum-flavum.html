Loropetalum flavum

Loropetalum flavum, commonly known as the golden flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant foliage, which turns bright yellow in the fall. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are typically ovate with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.

Loropetalum flavum is well-suited for ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
