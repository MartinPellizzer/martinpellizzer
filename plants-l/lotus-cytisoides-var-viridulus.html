Lotus cytisoides var. viridulus

Lotus cytisoides var. viridulus, commonly known as the green lotus, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and compact growth habit.
