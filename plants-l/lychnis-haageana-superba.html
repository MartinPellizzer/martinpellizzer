Lychnis haageana superba

Lychnis haageana superba, commonly known as the showy lychnis, is a flowering plant belonging to the carnation family, Caryophyllaceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in meadows and woodland edges. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are ovate and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branched.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
