Linosyris acutissima

Linosyris acutissima, commonly known as the sharp-leaved linosyris, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a spike. It is often found in coastal and inland areas with well-drained soils.

Linosyris acutissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
