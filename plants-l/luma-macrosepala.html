Luma macrosepala

Luma macrosepala, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the plant a fiery appearance. The leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, with a dark green hue. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
