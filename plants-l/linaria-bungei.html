Linaria bungei

Linaria bungei, commonly known as the Bunge's toadflax, is a flowering plant native to central and eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, linear leaves and produces capsules that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and rocky areas.

Linaria bungei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
