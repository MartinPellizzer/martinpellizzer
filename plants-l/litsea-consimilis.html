Litsea consimilis

Litsea consimilis, commonly known as the similar-laurel, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature.

Litsea consimilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a pleasant, spicy aroma.
