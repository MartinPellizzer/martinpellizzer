Lysimachia dauriensis

Lysimachia dauriensis, commonly known as Daurian yellow loosestrife, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing or ascending growth habit. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, yellow flowers are clustered in terminal cymes, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Lysimachia dauriensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
