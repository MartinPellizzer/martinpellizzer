Lasianthus griffithii var. scabridus

Lasianthus griffithii var. scabridus, commonly known as the rough lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
