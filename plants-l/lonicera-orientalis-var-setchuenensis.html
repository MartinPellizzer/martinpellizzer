Lonicera orientalis var. setchuenensis

Lonicera orientalis var. setchuenensis, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it versatile for various landscaping applications.
