Lantana maximiliani

Lantana maximiliani, commonly known as the yellow lantana, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are oval to lance-shaped. It is a shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall and is often found in disturbed soils and open areas.

Lantana maximiliani is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in landscaping, although it can be invasive in certain regions.
