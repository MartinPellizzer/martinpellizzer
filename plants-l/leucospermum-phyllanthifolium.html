Leucospermum phyllanthifolium

Leucospermum phyllanthifolium, commonly known as the yellow star jelly, is a South African shrub belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is a notable species in its native habitat.
