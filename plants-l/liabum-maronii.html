Liabum maronii

Liabum maronii, commonly known as the Maron's liabum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or bean family, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. Liabum maronii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
