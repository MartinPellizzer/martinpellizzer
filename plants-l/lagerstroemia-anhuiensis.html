Lagerstroemia anhuiensis

Lagerstroemia anhuiensis, commonly known as the Anhui crape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

Lagerstroemia anhuiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
