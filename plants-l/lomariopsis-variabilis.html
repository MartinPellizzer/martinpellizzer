Lomariopsis variabilis

Lomariopsis variabilis, commonly known as the variable lomariopsis, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family, characterized by its fern-like appearance.

Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of many fern species.

Lomariopsis variabilis is notable for its variability in leaf shape and size, which contributes to its common name.
