Limodorum hyacinthinum

Limodorum hyacinthinum, commonly known as the hyacinth orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features a basal rosette of lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces a tall, slender stem that bears a cluster of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of violet or blue. The flowers have a distinct, sweet fragrance and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is often found in woodland areas and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
