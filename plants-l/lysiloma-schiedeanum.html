Lysiloma schiedeanum

Lysiloma schiedeanum, commonly known as the yellow shower tree, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces long, slender pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
