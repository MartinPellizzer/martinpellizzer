Lacosteopsis orientalis var. naseana

Lacosteopsis orientalis var. naseana, commonly known as the Nasean rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long flowering period and drought tolerance.
