Lespedeza eriocarpa var. polyantha

Lespedeza eriocarpa var. polyantha, commonly known as the purpletop vervain, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open woodlands and prairies. The plant produces small, clustered purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered with fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
