Lustrinia

Lustrinia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Lustrinia thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
