Lygodium volubile var. lucens

Lygodium volubile var. lucens, commonly known as the shining climbing fern, is a tropical fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its wiry, climbing stems and pinnately compound leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The fronds are typically light green and can grow quite large, sometimes reaching lengths of up to 2 meters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant in warm climates.
