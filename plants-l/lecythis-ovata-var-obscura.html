Lecythis ovata var. obscura

Lecythis ovata var. obscura, commonly known as the dark lecythium, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and produces heavy, woody fruits that contain a starchy, edible pulp. The plant is part of the Sapotaceae family and is known for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
