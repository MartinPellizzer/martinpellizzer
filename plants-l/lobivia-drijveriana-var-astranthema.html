Lobivia drijveriana var. astranthema

Lobivia drijveriana var. astranthema, commonly known as the Astranthema Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and dense, fleshy stems. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, which help it conserve water in its harsh environment.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to rock gardens and well-drained soil.
