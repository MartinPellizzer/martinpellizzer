Lobelia nuzana

Lobelia nuzana, commonly known as the New Zealand lobelia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features whorled leaves and produces spikes of small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Lobelia nuzana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native pollinators.
