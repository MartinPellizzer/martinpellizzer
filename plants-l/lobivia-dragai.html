Lobivia dragai

Lobivia dragai, commonly known as the Dragai Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, spiny stems and fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves. The plant typically produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. Lobivia dragai is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its thick stems.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
