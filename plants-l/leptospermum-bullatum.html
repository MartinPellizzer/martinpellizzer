Leptospermum bullatum

Leptospermum bullatum, commonly known as the white bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

The plant produces small, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and heathlands.
