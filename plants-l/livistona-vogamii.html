Livistona vogamii

Livistona vogamii, commonly known as the Vogam palm, is a species of cycad native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feathery leaves. The plant has a distinctive, palm-like appearance with a smooth, green stem that can grow up to several meters in height. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with long, narrow segments arranged in a fan shape.

Livistona vogamii is adapted to the tropical climate of Madagascar and plays a role in the local ecosystem as a habitat for various animals.
