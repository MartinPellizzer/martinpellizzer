Lastrea filix-mas var. panda

Lastrea filix-mas var. panda, commonly known as the panda plant, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or silver markings that resemble a panda's coat. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
