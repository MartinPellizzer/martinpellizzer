Lathrocasis tenerrima

Lathrocasis tenerrima, commonly known as the slender larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and open woodlands.
