Lampranthus vanheerdei

Lampranthus vanheerdei, commonly known as the Van Heerde's lantern plant, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tubular leaves that store water, adapting to its arid environment. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a desirable addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
