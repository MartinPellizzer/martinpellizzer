Lespedeza daurica var. beipiaoensis

Lespedeza daurica var. beipiaoensis, commonly known as the Beipiao lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to northeastern China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native landscapes.
