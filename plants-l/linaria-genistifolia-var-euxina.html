Linaria genistifolia var. euxina

Linaria genistifolia var. euxina, commonly known as the Turkish scabious, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branched stems that often grow in clusters, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, resembling those of the genus Genista, hence the species name.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and wildflower meadows.
