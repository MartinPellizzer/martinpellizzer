Limonium × lucentinum

Limonium × lucentinum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads, which are surrounded by narrow, spiky leaves. The plant is typically found growing in coastal areas, salt marshes, and sandy soils, showcasing its adaptability to saline environments. Limonium × lucentinum is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and coastal gardens.
