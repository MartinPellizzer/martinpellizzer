Laelia wetmorei

Laelia wetmorei, commonly known as the Wetmore's orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, Laelia wetmorei is considered a rare and delicate species in the wild.
