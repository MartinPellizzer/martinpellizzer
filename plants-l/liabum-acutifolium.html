Liabum acutifolium

Liabum acutifolium, commonly known as the sharpleaf liabum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and appear in clusters.

Liabum acutifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
