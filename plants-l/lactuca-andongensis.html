Lactuca andongensis

Lactuca andongensis, commonly known as the Andong lettuce, is a species of lettuce native to Korea.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically pale green with a slightly bitter taste. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes other members such as daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in cool climates and is often cultivated for its edible leaves.

The botanical features of Lactuca andongensis make it a unique addition to both traditional and modern culinary practices.
