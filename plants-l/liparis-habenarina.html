Liparis habenarina

Liparis habenarina, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, leafy stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Native to Madagascar, this species thrives in humid, shaded environments within montane forests. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant blooms during the warmer months.

Its unique floral structure and habitat make it a distinctive subject of botanical study.
