Launaea Sarmentosa

Launaea sarmentosa is a unique and resilient plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East. It is known for its creeping growth habit and ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. This plant features small, spiny leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its adaptability makes it an important species in ecological restoration projects. Launaea sarmentosa is also valued for its potential medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
