Lovoa corbisieriana

Lovoa corbisieriana, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow inflorescences that appear during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
