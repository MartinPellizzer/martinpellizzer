Lachanodendron

Lachanodendron, commonly known as the vegetable tree, is a unique plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its ability to produce edible leaves and fruits, making it a valuable resource in traditional cuisine. The plant has a woody structure with compound leaves that can be harvested throughout the growing season. Its fruits, resembling small tomatoes, are rich in nutrients and have a mild, tangy flavor.

Lachanodendron thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to flourish.
