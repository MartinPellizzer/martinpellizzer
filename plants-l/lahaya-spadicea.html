Lahaya spadicea

Lahaya spadicea, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, showy flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and emits a mild fragrance. Its flowers are typically five-petaled and have a prominent central disk.

Lahaya spadicea is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
