Lamium chinense var. tuberiferum

Lamium chinense var. tuberiferum, commonly known as Chinese deadnettle, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of pale purple to pink flowers. The plant has tuberous roots that store nutrients, aiding in its survival during adverse conditions. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along streambanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
