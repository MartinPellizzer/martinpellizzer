Lomaria lineariifolia

Lomaria lineariifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lomaria, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Lomaria lineariifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
