Lachenalia hirta var. exserta

Lachenalia hirta var. exserta, commonly known as the Cape bell orchid, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of bell-shaped, brightly colored flowers that range from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, adding a vibrant splash of color to the landscape.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
