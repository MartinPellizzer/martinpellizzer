Liabum sericolepis

Liabum sericolepis, commonly known as the silky leaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
