Libanotis laticalycina

Libanotis laticalycina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Libanotis laticalycina is often found in rocky or limestone areas, making it a hardy and resilient species.
