Leucospermum medium

Leucospermum medium, commonly known as the yellow star flower, is a South African native shrub belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces woody, branching stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and birds, contributing to its ecological significance.
