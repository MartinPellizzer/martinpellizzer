Lisianthius amplissimus

Lisianthius amplissimus, commonly known as the giant lisianthus, is a striking flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
