Lantana leucocarpa f. anomala

Lantana leucocarpa f. anomala, commonly known as the white-fruited lantana, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink.
