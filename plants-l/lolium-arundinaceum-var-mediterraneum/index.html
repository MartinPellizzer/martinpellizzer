Lolium Arundinaceum Var. Mediterraneum

Lolium arundinaceum var. mediterraneum is a hardy grass species native to the Mediterranean region. It is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and withstand drought. This plant is often used in agricultural settings for forage and erosion control. Its dense growth habit makes it an effective ground cover. Despite its benefits, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
