Lonicera bungeana

Lonicera bungeana, commonly known as the Bunge honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are typically elliptical to ovate in shape. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall, attracting various wildlife species.

Lonicera bungeana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to a range of soil conditions.
