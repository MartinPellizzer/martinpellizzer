Lobivia maximiliana var. westii

Lobivia maximiliana var. westii, commonly known as the Westii Lobivia, is a compact cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to grayish spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
