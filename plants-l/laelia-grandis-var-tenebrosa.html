Laelia grandis var. tenebrosa

Laelia grandis var. tenebrosa, commonly known as the dark-stemmed laelia, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, often velvety leaves and striking, fragrant flowers that range from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It prefers cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
