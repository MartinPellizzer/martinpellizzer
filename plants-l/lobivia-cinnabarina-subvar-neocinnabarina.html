Lobivia cinnabarina subvar. neocinnabarina

Lobivia cinnabarina subvar. neocinnabarina, commonly known as the Neocinnabarina Lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or pinkish hue, especially under intense sunlight. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
