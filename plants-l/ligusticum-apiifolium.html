Ligusticum apiifolium

Ligusticum apiifolium, commonly known as Chinese lovage, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features a robust, aromatic rhizome and large, compound leaves that resemble those of parsley. The plant produces yellow flowers in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage and roots are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.
