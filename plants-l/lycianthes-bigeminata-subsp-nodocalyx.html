Lycianthes bigeminata subsp. nodocalyx

Lycianthes bigeminata subsp.

nodocalyx, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a species native to Central and South America. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves that often exhibit a purple or reddish hue.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange or red berries containing several seeds. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and peppers.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and fruit.
