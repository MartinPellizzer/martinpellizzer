Laelia × elegans

Laelia × elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a hybrid orchid species belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the Andes Mountains, where it thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and ample sunlight. This plant is characterized by its vibrant, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and purple. Laelia × elegans is a sympodial orchid, meaning it grows in a horizontal pattern with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
