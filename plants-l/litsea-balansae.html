Litsea balansae

Litsea balansae, commonly known as the Balansae laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Vietnam and China. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves and small white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed.
