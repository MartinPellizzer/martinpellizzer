Lepidium desvauxii var. gracilescens

Lepidium desvauxii var. gracilescens, commonly known as the slender wall rocket, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, white or yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, round seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
