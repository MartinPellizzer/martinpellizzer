Litsea szechuanica

Litsea szechuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan litsea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically red when mature, and it is often found in forested areas with moist, well-drained soil.

Litsea szechuanica is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
