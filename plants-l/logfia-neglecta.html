Logfia neglecta

Logfia neglecta, commonly known as the neglected logfia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Logfia neglecta is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
