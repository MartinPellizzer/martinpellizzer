Ligustrum lucidum f. aureomarginatum

Ligustrum lucidum f. aureomarginatum, commonly known as the golden-margin privet, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive golden-yellow margin that contrasts with its dark green center. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by black, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil types.

This variety is valued for its dense growth habit and ability to thrive in both full sun and partial shade.
