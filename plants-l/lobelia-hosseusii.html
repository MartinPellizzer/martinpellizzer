Lobelia hosseusii

Lobelia hosseusii, commonly known as the Sierra Lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular blossoms that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Lobelia hosseusii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
