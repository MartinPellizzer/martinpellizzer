Lampranthus Glaucus Var. Tortuosus

Lampranthus glaucus var. tortuosus is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers, it adds a bold touch to any garden or collection. This variety is prized for its unique, twisting stems and drought-resistant nature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers. Its beauty and resilience make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
