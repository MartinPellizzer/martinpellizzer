Liatris novae-angliae f. alba

Liatris novae-angliae f. alba, commonly known as the white panicled blazing star, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, spike-like inflorescence composed of white to pale pink flowers. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
