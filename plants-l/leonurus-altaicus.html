Leonurus altaicus

Leonurus altaicus, commonly known as the Siberian hedgehog.

This perennial herb is native to the temperate regions of Asia, including parts of Russia and China. It features a hairy, upright stem with small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months.

It is adapted to grow in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
