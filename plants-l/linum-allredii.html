Linum allredii

Linum allredii, commonly known as Allred's flax, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Linaceae family and is characterized by its slender, herbaceous stems and delicate blue or purple flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. Linum allredii is often found in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
