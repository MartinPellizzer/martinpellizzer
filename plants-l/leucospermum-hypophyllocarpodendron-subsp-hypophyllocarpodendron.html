Leucospermum hypophyllocarpodendron subsp. hypophyllocarpodendron

Leucospermum hypophyllocarpodendron subsp.

hypophyllocarpodendron, commonly known as the silverflame kangaroo paw, is a striking South African plant characterized by its unique, flame-like inflorescences. It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is native to the Western Cape region, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vivid orange to yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is a perennial shrub that prefers full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
