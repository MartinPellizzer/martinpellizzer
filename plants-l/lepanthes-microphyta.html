Lepanthes microphyta

Lepanthes microphyta, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically measure less than an inch in diameter. The plant produces a single spike of flowers, which are usually white or pale yellow in color. Lepanthes microphyta thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and role in the orchid family's diversity.
