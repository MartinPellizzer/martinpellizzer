Laugeria dependens

Laugeria dependens, commonly known as the hanging laugeria, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous inflorescences, which give it its common name. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. Its flowers are small and often clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.

Laugeria dependens is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical gardens for its distinctive appearance.
