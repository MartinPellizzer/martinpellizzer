Liatris × deamii

Liatris × deamii, commonly known as the Deam's blazing star, is a hybrid perennial grassland plant native to the central United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, spires of purple or pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and woodlands. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color.

Liatris × deamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting native pollinators.
