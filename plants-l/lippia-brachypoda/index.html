Lippia Brachypoda

Lippia brachypoda is a small, aromatic herb native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. It is known for its distinctive appearance, featuring narrow, gray-green leaves and small, yellowish flowers. This plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its unique flavor and versatility in culinary and medicinal applications make it a noteworthy plant to explore.
