Loudetia densispica

Loudetia densispica, commonly known as the dense-haired grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, hairy foliage that gives it its common name. The plant has narrow, linear leaves with prominent veins and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for landscaping in regions with a subtropical climate.
