Lamyra firma

Lamyra firma, commonly known as the rigid lamyra, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, slender stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, contributing to its overall delicate appearance.

Lamyra firma is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
