Lepidium setipetalum

Lepidium setipetalum, commonly known as the hairy cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as stream banks and wet meadows. It produces small, round seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Lepidium setipetalum is often found in temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.
