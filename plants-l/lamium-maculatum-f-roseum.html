Lamium maculatum f. roseum

Lamium maculatum f. roseum, commonly known as the spotted deadnettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features striking foliage with variegated leaves that display shades of white, pink, and green, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, often found in woodland gardens.

Its attractive appearance and ease of care make it a favored choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and functionality.
