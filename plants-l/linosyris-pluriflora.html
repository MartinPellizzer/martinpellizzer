Linosyris pluriflora

Linosyris pluriflora, commonly known as the many-flowered linosyris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Linosyris pluriflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
