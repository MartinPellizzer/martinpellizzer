Lepturus repens var. latifolius

Lepturus repens var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf spreading lepturus, is a variant of the creeping lepturus plant.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in moist, shaded environments. The leaves are broad and oval-shaped, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

This plant is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
