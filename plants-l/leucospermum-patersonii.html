Leucospermum patersonii

Leucospermum patersonii, commonly known as the Paterson's firecracker, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves that are densely packed along its stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Leucospermum patersonii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
