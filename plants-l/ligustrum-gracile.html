Ligustrum gracile

Ligustrum gracile, commonly known as the slender privet, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by black, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Ligustrum gracile is also valued for its use in hedges and as a landscape plant due to its dense growth habit.
