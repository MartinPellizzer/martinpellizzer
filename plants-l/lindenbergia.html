Lindenbergia

Lindenbergia, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to various regions in Africa, Madagascar, and parts of Asia, often growing in rocky or coastal environments. Lindenbergia species are characterized by their small, often fragrant flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. They typically have slender stems and are epiphytic or lithophytic, meaning they grow on other plants or rocks.

Due to their unique appearance and habitat, Lindenbergia species are popular among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
