Leontodon visianii

Leontodon visianii, commonly known as the Visianii's hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow ray florets and a central disk of tubular flowers, typical of the genus Leontodon. The plant is native to southern Europe and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has a compact growth habit with basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape.
