Lysimachia europaea

Lysimachia europaea, commonly known as European chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, often white or pink flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its stems are prostrate, and it produces numerous small, bead-like seeds.

Lysimachia europaea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
