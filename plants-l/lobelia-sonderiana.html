Lobelia sonderiana

Lobelia sonderiana, commonly known as Sonder's lobelia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often hairy, while its stems are erect and branching.

Lobelia sonderiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
