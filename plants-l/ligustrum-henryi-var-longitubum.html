Ligustrum henryi var. longitubum

Ligustrum henryi var. longitubum, commonly known as the Chinese privet, is a shrub native to China.

It features small, opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a dark blue to black berry that contains several seeds.

It is often used in horticulture for its dense growth and adaptability to various soil types.
