Landolphia myrtifolia var. perrieri

Landolphia myrtifolia var. perrieri, commonly known as the purple star vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a star-like appearance. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical gardens.
