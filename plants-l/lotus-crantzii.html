Lotus crantzii

Lotus crantzii, commonly known as the dwarf lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, often covered in a fine layer of silky hairs. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink or white, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies such as lakes and rivers.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and wetland restoration projects.
