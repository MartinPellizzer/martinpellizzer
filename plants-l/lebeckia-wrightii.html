Lebeckia wrightii

Lebeckia wrightii, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a South African plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions. It has hairy, narrow leaves that help reduce water loss in its arid environment.

Lebeckia wrightii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and unique appearance.
