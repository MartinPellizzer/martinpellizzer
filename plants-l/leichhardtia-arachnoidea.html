Leichhardtia arachnoidea

Leichhardtia arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance, with long, thin petals that resemble delicate threads. The plant belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its unique and intricate floral structure. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with high humidity and moderate temperatures.
