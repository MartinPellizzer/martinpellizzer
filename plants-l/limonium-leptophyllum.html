Limonium leptophyllum

Limonium leptophyllum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in coastal environments and well-drained soils, often found in dunes and rocky shores. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense spikes, and the leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.

Limonium leptophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes.
