Limonium erythrorrhizum

Limonium erythrorrhizum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, preferring sandy or gravelly soils. It has a short, upright stem with narrow, linear leaves.

Limonium erythrorrhizum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and coastal landscapes.
