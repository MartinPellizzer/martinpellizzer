Levieria acuminata

Levieria acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and pointed, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with six petals that are usually pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Levieria acuminata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
