Lasianthus loeiensis

Lasianthus loeiensis, commonly known as the Loei River beauty, is a flowering plant native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in wetlands and along riverbanks, thriving in moist, tropical environments.
