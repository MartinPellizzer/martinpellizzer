Lycaste schilleriana

Lycaste schilleriana, commonly known as Schiller's lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a greenish tinge. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Lycaste schilleriana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
