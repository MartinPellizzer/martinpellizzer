Lisyanthus

Lisyanthus, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Lisyanthus is cultivated for its ornamental value and is often used in garden landscaping and floral arrangements.
