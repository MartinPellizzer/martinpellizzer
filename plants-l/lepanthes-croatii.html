Lepanthes croatii

Lepanthes croatii, commonly known as the Croatian nightshade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender stem with a few leaves and produces delicate, pale green or white flowers. Its roots are often mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient absorption.

Lepanthes croatii is native to Central America and is valued for its unique floral structure and ecological significance.
