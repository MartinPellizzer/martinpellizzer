Leucopogon yorkensis

Leucopogon yorkensis, commonly known as the York's heath, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. Leucopogon yorkensis thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and pine flatwoods.
