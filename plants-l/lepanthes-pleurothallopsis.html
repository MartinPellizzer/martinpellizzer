Lepanthes pleurothallopsis

Lepanthes pleurothallopsis, commonly known as the pleurothallopsis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within forests. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a short rhizome and several leaves emerging from it. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale green, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

Lepanthes pleurothallopsis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
