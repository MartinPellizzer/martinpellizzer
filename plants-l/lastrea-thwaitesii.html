Lastrea thwaitesii

Lastrea thwaitesii, commonly known as the rock fig, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting to its arid environment. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Its growth habit is typically compact, forming a rosette shape.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
