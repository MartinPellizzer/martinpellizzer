Lissochilus homblei

Lissochilus homblei, commonly known as the Hombrei orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, often in rocky or mountainous environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
