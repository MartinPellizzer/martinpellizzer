Liliago

Liliago, commonly known as the orchid lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, often fragrant flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically has strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Liliago thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring high humidity and well-drained soil.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both garden cultivation and horticultural display.
