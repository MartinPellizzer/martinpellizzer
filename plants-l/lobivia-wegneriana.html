Lobivia wegneriana

Lobivia wegneriana, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often used for propagation. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the winter months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Lobivia wegneriana is well-suited for indoor cultivation and is prized for its long blooming period.
