Ligulariopsis

The plant lacks true leaves and instead relies on its roots to absorb nutrients from the surrounding water and air. Its flowers are typically white or pale green, with a delicate, orchid-like structure.

Due to its specialized habitat requirements, Ligulariopsis is considered endangered and is often found in secluded, undisturbed areas.
