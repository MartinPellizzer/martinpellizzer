Lysimachia drynarifolia

Lysimachia drynarifolia, commonly known as the drynaria leaved yellow loosestrife, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
