Limonium cymuliferum var. uniflorum

Limonium cymuliferum var. uniflorum, commonly known as sea helene, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often found in dune environments. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
