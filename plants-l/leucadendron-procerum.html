Leucadendron procerum

Leucadendron procerum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange cone-like flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
