Luzula vernalis

Luzula vernalis, commonly known as spring loosestrife, is a flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant produces slender, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. Luzula vernalis is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and ability to form dense colonies.

It is often found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
