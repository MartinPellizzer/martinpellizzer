Lomaria spicata

Lomaria spicata, commonly known as the spike lomaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiky, cylindrical flower heads that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its distinctive appearance and drought tolerance.
