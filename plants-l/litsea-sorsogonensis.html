Litsea sorsogonensis

Litsea sorsogonensis, commonly known as the Sorsogon Island-laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found on the island of Sorsogon. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round berries.

Litsea sorsogonensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine in local communities.
