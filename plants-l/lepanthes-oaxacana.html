Lepanthes oaxacana

Lepanthes oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as cloud forests. Its leaves are usually grass-like, and the flowers are borne on slender stems.

Lepanthes oaxacana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
