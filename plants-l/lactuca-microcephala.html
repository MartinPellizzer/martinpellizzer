Lactuca microcephala

Lactuca microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its small, compact rosette of dark green, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry, disturbed soils.

Lactuca microcephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
