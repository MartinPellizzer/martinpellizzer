Limonium argentarium

Limonium argentarium, commonly known as silver limonium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, spindly stems and clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal areas and sandy habitats.

Limonium argentarium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add a touch of elegance to gardens.
