Lepervenchea tenuifolia

Lepervenchea tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf vining plant, is a species characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and climbing growth habit.

It typically features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Lepervenchea tenuifolia is a popular choice for trellis coverage and hanging baskets due to its graceful appearance.
