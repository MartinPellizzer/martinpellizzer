Lavatera olbia var. hirsutissima

Lavatera olbia var. hirsutissima, commonly known as the woolly lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy, and hairy foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, fluffy clusters of pale pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
