Limeum linifolium

Limeum linifolium, commonly known as the slenderleaf lime, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that give it its scientific name, "linifolium" meaning "thread-like leaves.". The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance contribute to its popularity among gardeners.
