Lantana origanoides var. sampaionis

Lantana origanoides var. sampaionis, commonly known as the Sampaion lantana, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features small, fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, blooming throughout the year. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
