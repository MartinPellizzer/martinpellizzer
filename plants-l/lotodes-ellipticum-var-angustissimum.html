Lotodes ellipticum var. angustissimum

Lotodes ellipticum var. angustissimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water lily, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are narrow and elongated, with a waxy surface to repel water. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its botanical structure supports both aquatic and terrestrial ecosystems by providing habitat for various organisms.
