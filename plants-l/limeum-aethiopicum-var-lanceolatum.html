Limeum aethiopicum var. lanceolatum

Limeum aethiopicum var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically velvety and dark green. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom from the center of the leaves. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
