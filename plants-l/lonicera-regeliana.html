Lonicera regeliana

Lonicera regeliana, commonly known as the Regel's honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow, often appearing in clusters. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that matures in late summer.

Lonicera regeliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and adaptability.
