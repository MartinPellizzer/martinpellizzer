Lamarchea sulcata

Lamarchea sulcata, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Lamarchea sulcata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
