Lightia lemniscata

Lightia lemniscata, commonly known as the Christmas star or star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a low-growing habit with fleshy, succulent leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. Lightia lemniscata is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
