Lonicera acuminata var. giraldii

Lonicera acuminata var. giraldii, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a climbing shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins and produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is known for its rapid growth and is often used in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological biodiversity.
