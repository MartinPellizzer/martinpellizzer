Ligularia polycephala

Ligularia polycephala, commonly known as the mountain ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color and a rough texture. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a dense, terminal inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
