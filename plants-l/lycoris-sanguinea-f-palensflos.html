Lycoris sanguinea f. palensflos

Lycoris sanguinea f. palensflos, commonly known as the Crimson Lily or Red Spider Lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, often with a subtle spider-like appearance due to their narrow, elongated petals. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous rhizome, typical of the Amaryllidaceae family. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
