Lepidaploa vernicosa

Lepidaploa vernicosa, commonly known as the glossy leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, waxy leaves that give it a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance gardens.
