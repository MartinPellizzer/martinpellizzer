Leiothrix hirsuta var. tonsilis

Leiothrix hirsuta var. tonsilis, commonly known as the hairy leiothrix, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine pubescence.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual and aromatic qualities.
