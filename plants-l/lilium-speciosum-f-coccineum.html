Lilium speciosum f. coccineum

Lilium speciosum f. coccineum, commonly known as the red-flowered tiger lily, is a striking cultivar of the tiger lily species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a deep red color with orange and yellow markings. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
