Lissochilus platypterus

Lissochilus platypterus, commonly known as the flat-winged orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a loose inflorescence. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

This orchid prefers cool, humid environments and is often found growing in shaded, forested areas.
