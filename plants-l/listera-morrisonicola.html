Listera morrisonicola

Listera morrisonicola, commonly known as Morrison's basketwort, is a species of flowering plant in the Primulaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Listera morrisonicola is native to eastern North America and is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its aesthetic value in garden settings.
