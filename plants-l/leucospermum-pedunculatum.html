Leucospermum pedunculatum

Leucospermum pedunculatum, commonly known as the yellow star flower, is a striking shrub native to South Africa.

It features bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are densely packed along its stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
