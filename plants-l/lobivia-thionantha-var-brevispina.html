Lobivia thionantha var. brevispina

Lobivia thionantha var. brevispina, commonly known as the short-spined lobivia, is a compact, spineless cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a grayish-green color and produces small, yellow flowers during the spring. The plant has a shallow root system adapted to absorb water from the soil quickly. It requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cactus collections.
