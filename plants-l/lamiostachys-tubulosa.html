Lamiostachys tubulosa

Lamiostachys tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular lamiostachys, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of purple or blue. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a strong aromatic scent.

Lamiostachys tubulosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or rocky slopes.
