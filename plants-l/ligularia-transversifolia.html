Ligularia transversifolia

Ligularia transversifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, broad leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base, often with a velvety texture. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in wetlands.

Its foliage is popular in ornamental gardening for its striking appearance.
