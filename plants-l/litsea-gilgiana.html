Litsea gilgiana

Litsea gilgiana, commonly known as the Chinese sandalwood or Chinese litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers and elliptical leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.

Litsea gilgiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
