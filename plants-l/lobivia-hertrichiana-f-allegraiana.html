Lobivia hertrichiana f. allegraiana

Lobivia hertrichiana f. allegraiana, commonly known as the Allegraian Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and fleshy, ribbed stems. The plant produces small, vibrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually reduced to spines, a feature common in many desert plants for water conservation.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and adaptability to dry environments.
