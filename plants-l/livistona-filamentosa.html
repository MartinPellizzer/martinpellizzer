Livistona filamentosa

Livistona filamentosa, commonly known as the slender palm or filamentous palm, is a species of cycad native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, branched inflorescences. Its seeds are enclosed in fleshy, red berries that are dispersed by animals.

Livistona filamentosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
