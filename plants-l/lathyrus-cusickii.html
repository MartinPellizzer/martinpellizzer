Lathyrus cusickii

Lathyrus cusickii, commonly known as the Cusick's vetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial legume that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to violet. Its leaves are pinnately compound, and it has a fibrous root system.

Lathyrus cusickii plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its ecological and potential medicinal properties.
