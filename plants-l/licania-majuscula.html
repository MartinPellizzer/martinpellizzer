Licania majuscula

Licania majuscula, commonly known as the giant licania, is a woody plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and can grow to significant heights, often reaching over 20 meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by fruit that contains several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its size and ecological importance, it plays a vital role in supporting local biodiversity.
