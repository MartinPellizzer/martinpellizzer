Limonia linearis

Limonia linearis, commonly known as the narrowleaf lily or narrowleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.

Limonia linearis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
