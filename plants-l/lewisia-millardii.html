Lewisia millardii

Lewisia millardii, commonly known as Millard's lewisia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Montiaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
