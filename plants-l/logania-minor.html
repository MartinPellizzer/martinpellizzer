Logania minor

Logania minor, commonly known as the small logania, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
