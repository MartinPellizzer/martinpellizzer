Lasianthus subglobosus

Lasianthus subglobosus, commonly known as the globe-headed orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, subglobular shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
