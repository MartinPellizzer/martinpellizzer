Lindmania micrantha

Lindmania micrantha, commonly known as the pink river hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
