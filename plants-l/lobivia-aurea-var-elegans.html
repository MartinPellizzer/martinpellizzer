Lobivia aurea var. elegans

Lobivia aurea var. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a rounded, ribbed stem with a distinctive golden hue, which becomes more pronounced under direct sunlight. The plant produces small, delicate yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to hot, dry climates and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated in succulent gardens and as a houseplant.
