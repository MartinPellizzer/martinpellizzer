Litsea cuipala

Litsea cuipala, commonly known as the Mexican mountain laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite, and often aromatic leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Litsea cuipala typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
