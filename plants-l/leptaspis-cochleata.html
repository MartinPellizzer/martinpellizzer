It is characterized by its distinctive spiral-shaped markings on its elytra, which give it its common name. The plant it feeds on is typically a species of the genus *Cochlearia*, which are flowering plants in the family Brassicaceae. Its larvae are often found feeding on the leaves of these host plants, contributing to their ecological role as herbivores.
