Litsea jaswirii

Litsea jaswirii, commonly known as the Malayan litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers and smooth, dark green leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests.

Its fruits are small, round, and contain a single seed, making it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential horticultural use.
