Laennecia lasseriana

Laennecia lasseriana, commonly known as the Lasserian rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

Laennecia lasseriana thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the harsh, sunny conditions of its native habitat.
