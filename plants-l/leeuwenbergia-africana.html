Leeuwenbergia africana

Leeuwenbergia africana, commonly known as the African bell orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Leeuwenbergia africana is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
