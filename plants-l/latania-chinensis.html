Latania chinensis

Latania chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo palm, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and clustered, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
