Lepisorus macrosphaerus var. minimus

Lepisorus macrosphaerus var. minimus, commonly known as the miniature rock fern, is a small, epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, clustered growth habit. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on tree trunks or rocks. Its spores are released in clusters, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

Botanically, it is characterized by its compact size and unique morphology, making it a popular choice for terrariums and miniature gardens.
