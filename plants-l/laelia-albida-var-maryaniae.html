Laelia albida var. maryaniae

Laelia albida var. maryaniae, commonly known as the Maryaniae Laelia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinct, fragrant scent. The plant typically blooms during the summer months and is prized for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements. It prefers cool, bright conditions with moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor cultivation.
