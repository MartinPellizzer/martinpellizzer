Lindsaea orbiculata var. commixta

Lindsaea orbiculata var. commixta, commonly known as the broad-leafed lindsaea, is a fern belonging to the family Bromeliaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery, and often spirally arranged leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with inconspicuous flowers, typical of many bromeliads. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to epiphytic or terrestrial growth, depending on the specific habitat.
