Limonium afghanicum

Limonium afghanicum, commonly known as Afghan sea holly, is a flowering plant native to Afghanistan and parts of Central Asia.

Limonium afghanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
