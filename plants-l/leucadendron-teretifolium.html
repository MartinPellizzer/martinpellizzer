Leucadendron teretifolium

Leucadendron teretifolium, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its pale silver-green, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flower heads, which are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
