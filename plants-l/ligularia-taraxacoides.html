Ligularia taraxacoides

Ligularia taraxacoides, commonly known as the yellow pattypan, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green underside. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
