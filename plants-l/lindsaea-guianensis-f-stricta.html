Lindsaea guianensis f. stricta

Lindsaea guianensis f. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lindsaea, is a tropical epiphytic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically has a rhizome that is short and produces a single, compact rosette of leaves. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with small, clustered sori on the underside.

This fern is often found in shaded, humid environments in the understory of tropical rainforests.
