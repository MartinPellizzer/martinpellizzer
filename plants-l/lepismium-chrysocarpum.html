Lepismium chrysocarpum

Lepismium chrysocarpum, commonly known as the golden fruit lily, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Lepismium chrysocarpum is relatively low maintenance and can be grown in shaded garden beds or as a houseplant.
