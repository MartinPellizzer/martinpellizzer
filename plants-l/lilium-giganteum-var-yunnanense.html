Lilium giganteum var. yunnanense

Lilium giganteum var. yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan giant lily, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of white, pink, or red. The plant features robust, upright stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters.

The botanical interest of this species lies in its ornamental value and its role in the biodiversity of its native habitat.
