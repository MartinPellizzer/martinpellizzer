Lobelia polyphylla var. coquimbana

Lobelia polyphylla var. coquimbana, commonly known as the Coquimbo lobelia, is a flowering plant native to Chile.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are often narrow and slightly hairy, adapting to its arid environment.
