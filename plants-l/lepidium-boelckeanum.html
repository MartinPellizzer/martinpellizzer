Lepidium boelckeanum

Lepidium boelckeanum, commonly known as the Boelcke's cress, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress species. The plant is characterized by its small, white or yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
