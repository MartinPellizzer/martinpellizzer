Lespedeza thunbergii subsp. formosa

Lespedeza thunbergii subsp.

formosa, commonly known as the Chinese lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to East Asia. It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen and prevent soil erosion.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
