Leucosmia acuminata

Leucosmia acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spider orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its unique, spider-like flower structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical rainforests. Its flowers, which are usually white or pale pink, bloom during the summer months.

Leucosmia acuminata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
