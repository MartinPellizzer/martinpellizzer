Lockhartia micrantha

Lockhartia micrantha, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, fragrant flowers with bright yellow petals and a deep red lip. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding substrate.

Lockhartia micrantha is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
