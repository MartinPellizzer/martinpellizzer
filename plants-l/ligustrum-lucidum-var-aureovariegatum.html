Ligustrum lucidum var. aureovariegatum

Ligustrum lucidum var. aureovariegatum, commonly known as the golden variegated privet, is a decorative shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking golden-yellow margins, creating a vibrant visual contrast. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, followed by black berries that attract birds. It is often cultivated as a hedge or ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and attractive foliage.

This variety thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance.
