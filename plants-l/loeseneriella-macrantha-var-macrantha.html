Loeseneriella macrantha var. macrantha

Loeseneriella macrantha var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered loeseneriella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that are covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.
