Liparis confusa var. amboinensis

Liparis confusa var. amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in some regions.
