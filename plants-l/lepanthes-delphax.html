Lepanthes delphax

Lepanthes delphax, commonly known as the Delphax orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor.

Lepanthes delphax is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
