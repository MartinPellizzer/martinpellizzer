Lessertia Frutescens

Lessertia frutescens is a small, shrub-like plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. This species thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for gardeners. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
