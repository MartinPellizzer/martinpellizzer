Liatris ligulistylis f. leucantha

Liatris ligulistylis f. leucantha, commonly known as the white-stemmed blazing star, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features tall, slender stems that are typically covered in a fine white or grayish down, giving it its distinctive appearance. Its showy pink to purple flower heads bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils in full sun.
