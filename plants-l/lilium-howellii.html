Lilium howellii

Lilium howellii, commonly known as Howell's lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Nevada. This perennial herbaceous plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
