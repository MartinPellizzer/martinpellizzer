Ligularia hookeri f. latiligulatum

Ligularia hookeri f. latiligulatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf ligularia, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length, with a distinctive yellowish-green coloration. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by broad, lance-shaped bracts, which are often a deep orange or red. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage.

This cultivar is prized for its robust growth and ability to add dramatic visual interest to shaded garden spaces.
