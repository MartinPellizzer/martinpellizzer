Laelia grandiflora var. alba

Laelia grandiflora var. alba, commonly known as the white laelia, is a tropical orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and a yellow lip, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. This variety thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and bright, indirect light. It produces fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
