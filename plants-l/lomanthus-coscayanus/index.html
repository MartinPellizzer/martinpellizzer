Lomanthus Coscayanus

Lomanthus coscayanus is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America. It is known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring season. This species thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare in the wild, making conservation efforts essential for its survival.
