Licuala longicalycata

Licuala longicalycata, commonly known as the longicycle palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in diameter. Its inflorescences are typically long and branched, bearing both male and female flowers.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and potential use in landscaping.
