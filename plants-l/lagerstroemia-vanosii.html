Lagerstroemia vanosii

Lagerstroemia vanosii, commonly known as the Vanosii crape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that bloom in summer, typically in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that turn reddish in the fall, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its compact size and vibrant floral display make it a desirable addition to landscapes in temperate climates.
