Limonium pruinosum var. glabrum

Limonium pruinosum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. The plant has soft, fuzzy leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white or grayish hairs. It thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, often found in sandy or gravelly habitats.

Limonium pruinosum var. glabrum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate salt and wind.
