Laelia × fidelensis

Laelia × fidelensis, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Laelia purpurea and Laelia alba.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant produces strikingly large, showy flowers with intricate patterns of purple and white. It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant colors and relatively easy care requirements.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and pseudobulbs.
