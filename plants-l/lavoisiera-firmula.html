Lavoisiera firmula

Lavoisiera firmula, commonly known as the slender lily, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces small, bulbous underground structures called bulbs.

Lavoisiera firmula is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
