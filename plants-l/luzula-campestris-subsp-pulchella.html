Luzula campestris subsp. pulchella

Luzula campestris subsp.

pulchella, commonly known as the elegant wood rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage.
