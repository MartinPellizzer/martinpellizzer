Lantana aristata f. brachypoda

Lantana aristata f. brachypoda, commonly known as the dwarf lantana, is a compact shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its textured appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
