Lonchocarpus muehlbergianus

Lonchocarpus muehlbergianus, commonly known as the yellow trumpet tree, is a tropical legume native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that resemble trumpets, which bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces pods that contain toxic seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland tropical forests.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
