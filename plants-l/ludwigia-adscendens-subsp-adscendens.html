Ludwigia adscendens subsp. adscendens

Ludwigia adscendens subsp.

adscendens, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a tropical aquatic plant native to South America. It is characterized by its bright red or orange-red leaves, which can vary in color depending on light exposure and nutrient levels.

The plant typically grows in slow-moving freshwater habitats, such as ponds and streams. It has opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. Ludwigia adscendens subsp.

adscendens is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.
