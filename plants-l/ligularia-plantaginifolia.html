Ligularia plantaginifolia

Ligularia plantaginifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf ligularia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and serrated edges. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants.
