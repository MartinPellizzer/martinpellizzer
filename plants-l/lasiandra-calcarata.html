Lasiandra calcarata

Lasiandra calcarata, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers with a distinctive white center. It is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal areas.

Lasiandra calcarata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
