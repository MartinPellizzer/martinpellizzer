Lamium maculatum f. brevidens

Lamium maculatum f. brevidens, commonly known as the spotted deadnettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive purple or red markings, often variegated with white or cream. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or violet in color. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and vibrant foliage.
