Leucas glabrata

Leucas glabrata, commonly known as white leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
