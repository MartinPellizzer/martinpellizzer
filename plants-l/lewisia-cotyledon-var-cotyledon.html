Lewisia cotyledon var. cotyledon

Lewisia cotyledon var. cotyledon, commonly known as the "Cotyledon Lewisia," is a succulent plant native to the western United States.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a valuable addition to both garden landscapes and botanical collections.
