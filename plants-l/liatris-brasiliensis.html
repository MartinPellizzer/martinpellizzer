Liatris brasiliensis

Liatris brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian blazing star, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, spiky inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodlands. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color and bloom during the summer months.

Liatris brasiliensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
