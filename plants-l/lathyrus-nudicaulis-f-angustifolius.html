Lathyrus nudicaulis f. angustifolius

Lathyrus nudicaulis f. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pea, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, pea-like flowers. The foliage is typically narrow and elongated, giving it a distinctive appearance. This plant is often found in grassy areas, along roadsides, and in disturbed soils.

It has a fibrous root system and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
