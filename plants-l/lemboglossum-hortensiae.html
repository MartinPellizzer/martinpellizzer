Lemboglossum hortensiae

Lemboglossum hortensiae, commonly known as the silver-haired lemboglossum, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among gardeners.

The flowers are typically pale blue or purple, adding a subtle yet elegant touch to its ornamental value.
