Lomaria mollis

Lomaria mollis, commonly known as the soft lomaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Lomaria mollis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
