Lycoris wulingensis

Lycoris wulingensis, commonly known as the Wuling lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in the autumn. The plant features strap-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous rhizome, and its flowers are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in woodland environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

Lycoris wulingensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and cultural significance in traditional Chinese horticulture.
