Lasianthus eriocalyx

Lasianthus eriocalyx, commonly known as the hairy-lipped lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, with opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
