Lotus oligoceratos

Lotus oligoceratos, commonly known as the narrow-lobed water lily, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves and showy, white or pink flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are typically rounded with a waxy surface that helps repel water, while its roots anchor it in the mud at the bottom of the water body.

Lotus oligoceratos plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
