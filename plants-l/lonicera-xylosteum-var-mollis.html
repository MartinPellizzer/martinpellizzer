Lonicera xylosteum var. mollis

Lonicera xylosteum var. mollis, commonly known as the soft honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are yellowish-white with orange stamens, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy drupe that ripens in late summer, attracting various wildlife.

This species is often found in moist woodlands and along stream banks, where it thrives in partial to full shade.
