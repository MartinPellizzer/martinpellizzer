Lysionotus ikedae

Lysionotus ikedae, commonly known as the Ikedae's sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous small seeds in its composite flower head.

Lysionotus ikedae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
