Lepanthopsis pristis

Lepanthopsis pristis, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, sword-shaped leaves and its striking, showy flowers that often feature vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical forests. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its complex floral structure that attracts specific pollinators.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
