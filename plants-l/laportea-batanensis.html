Laportea batanensis

Laportea batanensis, commonly known as the Batanese ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Laportea batanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
