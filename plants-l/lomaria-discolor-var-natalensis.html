Lomaria discolor var. natalensis

Lomaria discolor var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal lomaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It has lance-shaped leaves with toothed margins and is often found in coastal regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly seen in its natural habitat.
