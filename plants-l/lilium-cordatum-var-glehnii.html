Lilium cordatum var. glehnii

Lilium cordatum var. glehnii, commonly known as the Glehn's fawn lily, is a rare and beautiful variant of the fawn lily.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves and striking, pale yellow to white flowers with red spots. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. It is typically found in rocky soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a delicate and sought-after species in horticulture.
