Lonicera etrusca var. etrusca

Lonicera etrusca var. etrusca, commonly known as the Etruscan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in early spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
