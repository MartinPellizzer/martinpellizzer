Limonium hyssopifolium

Limonium hyssopifolium, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky purple or blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Limonium hyssopifolium is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
