Linharia

Linharia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a distinctive waxy coating that reflects light.

Linharia is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
