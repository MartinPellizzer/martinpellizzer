Lathyrus annuus var. angustifolius

Lathyrus annuus var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaf pea, is a flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, angular stems and small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The leaves are compound, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and the plant produces cylindrical pods containing small, edible seeds. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
