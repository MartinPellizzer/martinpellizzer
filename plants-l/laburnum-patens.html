Laburnum patens

Laburnum patens, commonly known as the weeping laburnum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with slender, pointed leaflets and produces long, slender pods that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The weeping laburnum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
