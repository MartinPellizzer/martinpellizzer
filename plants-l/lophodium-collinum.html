Lophodium collinum

Lophodium collinum, commonly known as the African orchid or the mountain orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly found in regions like South Africa, Namibia, and Botswana. This plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, often in shaded environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

Lophodium collinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
