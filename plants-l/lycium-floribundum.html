Lycium floribundum

Lycium floribundum, commonly known as the evergreen nightshade, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible but should be consumed in moderation. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Lycium floribundum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.
