Lastrea augescens

Lastrea augescens, commonly known as the ascending lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant features fleshy, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in length. Its leaves are typically small, scale-like, and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often blooming in spring.
