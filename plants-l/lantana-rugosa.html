Lantana rugosa

Lantana rugosa, commonly known as rough lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, featuring opposite leaves that are rough to the touch. The plant produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, usually in shades of yellow, orange, or red. Lantana rugosa is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.

It is also valued for its use in ornamental gardening and as a source of nectar for pollinators.
