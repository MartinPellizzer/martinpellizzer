Lysimachia rubiginosa

Lysimachia rubiginosa, commonly known as redstem filaree, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Eurasia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has slender, reddish stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or wet meadows.

Lysimachia rubiginosa is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in gardens for its delicate appearance.
