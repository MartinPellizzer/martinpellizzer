Lolium loliaceum

Lolium loliaceum, commonly known as slender ryegrass, is a cool-season grass native to Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted leaves and dense, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and pastures.

Lolium loliaceum is valued for its use in forage and erosion control due to its rapid growth and hardiness.
