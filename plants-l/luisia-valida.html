Luisia valida

Luisia valida, commonly known as the showy lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf structures and often vibrant floral displays. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders in horticulture.
