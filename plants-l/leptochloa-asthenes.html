Leptochloa asthenes

Leptochloa asthenes, commonly known as slender grass, is a small annual grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural areas or along roadsides. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often considered a weed in cultivated fields.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
