Ligustrum xingrenense

Ligustrum xingrenense, commonly known as Chinese privet, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.

Ligustrum xingrenense is often used in landscaping due to its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.
