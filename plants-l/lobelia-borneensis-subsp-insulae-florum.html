Lobelia borneensis subsp. insulae-florum

Lobelia borneensis subsp.

insulae-florum, commonly known as the island lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the island of Borneo. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
