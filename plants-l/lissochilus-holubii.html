Lissochilus holubii

Lissochilus holubii, commonly known as the Holub's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in the Andean regions of countries like Peru and Bolivia. This orchid is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically feature a white or pale pink coloration with intricate patterns. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often growing in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
