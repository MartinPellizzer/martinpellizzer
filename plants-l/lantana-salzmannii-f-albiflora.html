Lantana salzmannii f. albiflora

Lantana salzmannii f. albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower lantana, is a compact shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which distinguish it from other lantana species. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its flowering period extends throughout the growing season, adding continuous visual interest to the landscape.
