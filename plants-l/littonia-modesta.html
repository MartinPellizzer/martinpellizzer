Littonia modesta

Littonia modesta, commonly known as the modest littonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright orange or red blooms, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy texture.
