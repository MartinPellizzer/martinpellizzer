Lespedeza anhweiensis

Lespedeza anhweiensis, commonly known as Chinese lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Lespedeza anhweiensis is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
