Leea philippinensis var. pauciflora

Leea philippinensis var. pauciflora, commonly known as the Philippine Leea, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Leeaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It typically grows in moist, shaded areas of lowland forests.

Its botanical significance lies in its limited distribution and role in the local ecosystem.
