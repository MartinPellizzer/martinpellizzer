Lupinus Tegeticulatus Var. Grandiflorus

Lupinus tegeticulatus var. grandiflorus is a striking member of the legume family known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers. This variety is native to the western United States and thrives in dry, rocky habitats. Its showy blooms make it a popular choice for native gardens and restoration projects. The plant is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing abilities, which enrich the soil. With its unique appearance and ecological benefits, it stands out as a remarkable wildflower.
