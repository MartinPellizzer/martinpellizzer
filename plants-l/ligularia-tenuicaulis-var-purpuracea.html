Ligularia tenuicaulis var. purpuracea

Ligularia tenuicaulis var. purpuracea, commonly known as the purple-stemmed ligularia, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple hue on the stems and undersides. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shaded garden landscapes.
