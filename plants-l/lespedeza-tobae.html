Lespedeza tobae

Lespedeza tobae, commonly known as the Toba lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Lespedeza tobae is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in soil stabilization.
