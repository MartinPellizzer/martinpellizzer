Lysimachia × producta

Lysimachia × producta, commonly known as the hybrid yellow loosestrife, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers in the summer. It is a member of the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit.
