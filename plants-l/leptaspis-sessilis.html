Leptaspis sessilis

Leptaspis sessilis, commonly known as the sessile leaf beetle, is a species of leaf beetle belonging to the family Chrysomelidae.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped body and typically metallic green or bronze coloration. The plant it feeds on is primarily the leaves of various species of the genus *Eucalyptus*. Its larvae are also leaf feeders, contributing to the plant's ecological interactions.

This beetle plays a role in the natural herbivory of eucalyptus forests, influencing plant growth and biodiversity.
