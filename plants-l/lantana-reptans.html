Lantana reptans

Lantana reptans, commonly known as creeping lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. Lantana reptans thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ground cover in gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions contribute to its widespread use in landscaping.
