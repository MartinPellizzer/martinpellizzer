Leiothrix flavescens var. distichophylla

Leiothrix flavescens var. distichophylla, commonly known as the golden leiothrix, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
