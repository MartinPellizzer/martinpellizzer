Ligustrum morrisonense

Ligustrum morrisonense, commonly known as the Morrison's privet, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.

Ligustrum morrisonense is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
