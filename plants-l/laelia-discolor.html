Laelia discolor

Laelia discolor, commonly known as the white laelia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and sepals, which contrast with its greenish-yellow lip. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom during the spring and early summer.

Laelia discolor is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.
