Licuala insignis

Licuala insignis, commonly known as the giant pandan, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts and as a flavoring agent in local cuisines.

Botanically, it belongs to the Araceae family and is noted for its robust growth and ornamental value.
