Laelia inconspicua

Laelia inconspicua, commonly known as the inconspicuous laelia, is a species of orchid native to the high altitudes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often pale-colored flowers. The plant typically produces a single or few flowers per stem, which are usually small and not particularly showy. It prefers cool, humid environments and is often found growing on rock surfaces or in shaded forest areas.

Laelia inconspicua is valued for its resilience and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
