Lonicera tatarica var. major

Lonicera tatarica var. major, commonly known as the Tatarian honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, appearing in early spring. Its berries are red or black and are edible, though not particularly flavorful.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
