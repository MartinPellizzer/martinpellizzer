Leonurus bungeanus

Leonurus bungeanus, commonly known as the lesser hedgehog urine plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
