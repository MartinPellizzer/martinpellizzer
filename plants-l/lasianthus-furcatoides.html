Lasianthus furcatoides

Lasianthus furcatoides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
