Linum rigidum var. compactum

Linum rigidum var. compactum, commonly known as the compact flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a member of the Linaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its hardy nature, it is suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
