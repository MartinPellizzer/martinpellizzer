Lathyrus laetiflorus subsp. alefeldii

Lathyrus laetiflorus subsp.

alefeldii, commonly known as the Alefeld's sweet pea, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe. It features slender, climbing stems with trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets.

The plant produces clusters of small, pea-like flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for garden borders or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
