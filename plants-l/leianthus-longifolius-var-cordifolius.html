Leianthus longifolius var. cordifolius

Leianthus longifolius var. cordifolius, commonly known as the heart-leaf leianthus, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically large and glossy, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
