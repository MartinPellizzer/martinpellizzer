Laelia anceps var. oweniana

Laelia anceps var. oweniana, commonly known as the Owen's Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, with flower spikes emerging from the base of the pseudobulbs. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant colors and long-lasting blooms.
