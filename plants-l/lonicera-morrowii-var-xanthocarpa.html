Lonicera morrowii var. xanthocarpa

Lonicera morrowii var. xanthocarpa, commonly known as the yellow-flowered honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer, attracting various wildlife.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to a range of soil conditions.
