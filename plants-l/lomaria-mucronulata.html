Lomaria mucronulata

Lomaria mucronulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lomaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Lomaria mucronulata is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
