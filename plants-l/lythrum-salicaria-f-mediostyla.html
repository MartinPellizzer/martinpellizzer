Lythrum salicaria f. mediostyla

Lythrum salicaria f. mediostyla, commonly known as the intermediate rose willow.

This botanical variant is a flowering plant in the Lythraceae family, characterized by its distinctive pink to red flowers that bloom in spikes. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and streambanks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for wetland gardens and ecological restoration projects.
