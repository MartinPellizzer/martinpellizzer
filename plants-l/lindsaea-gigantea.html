Lindsaea gigantea

Lindsaea gigantea, commonly known as the giant lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its large, elongated fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant exhibits a unique growth habit, often forming dense, sprawling mats on the forest floor. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are typically located on the underside of the fronds.

Lindsaea gigantea is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
