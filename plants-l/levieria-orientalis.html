Levieria orientalis

Levieria orientalis, commonly known as the oriental levieria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

The botanical characteristics of Levieria orientalis make it an attractive and low-maintenance addition to gardens.
