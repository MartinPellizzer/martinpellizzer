Lowiorchis aphylla

Lowiorchis aphylla, commonly known as the leafless orchid, is a unique orchid species characterized by its lack of foliage.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to specific regions in South America. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Despite its name, it thrives by relying on its specialized root system for nutrient absorption.

Its rarity and distinctive appearance make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
