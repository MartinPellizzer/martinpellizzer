Laennecia araneosa

Laennecia araneosa, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, thread-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in Mediterranean climates. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive, intricate structure that aids in pollination by specific insects.

Laennecia araneosa is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unusual beauty.
