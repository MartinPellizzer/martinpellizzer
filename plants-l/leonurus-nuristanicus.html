Leonurus nuristanicus

Leonurus nuristanicus, commonly known as the Nuristan hedgehog.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan and Afghanistan. The plant is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
