Liparis Correana

Liparis correana is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. Known for its delicate, bell-shaped flowers and vibrant green foliage, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This plant thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of both scientific study and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, Liparis correana remains a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
