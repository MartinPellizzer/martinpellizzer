Litsea aestivalis

Litsea aestivalis, commonly known as the summer litsea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by elliptical, dark green leaves. Its fruit is a small, smooth, yellow berry that ripens in the summer months.

Litsea aestivalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
