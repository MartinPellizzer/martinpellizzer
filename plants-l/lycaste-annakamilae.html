Lycaste annakamilae

Lycaste annakamilae, commonly known as the Kamilae lycaste, is a rare and exotic orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive in dry conditions.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
