Lomaria vestita

Lomaria vestita, commonly known as the silverleaf lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for rock gardens.

Lomaria vestita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscape design for its unique texture and color.
