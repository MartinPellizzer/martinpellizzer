Leucadendron empetrifolium

Leucadendron empetrifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf candle bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and conical, candle-like flower heads. The plant belongs to the Proteaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often showy floral structures. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to coastal gardens.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
