Limonium senkakuense

Limonium senkakuense, commonly known as the Senkaku sandflower, is a flowering plant native to the Senkaku Islands in the East China Sea.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of small, showy blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal environments.
