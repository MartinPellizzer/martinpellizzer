Litsea muelleri

Litsea muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's litsea, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The fruit is a small, smooth drupe that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.
