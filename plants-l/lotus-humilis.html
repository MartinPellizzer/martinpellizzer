Lotus humilis

Lotus humilis, commonly known as the dwarf lotus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers with a distinctive yellow center. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
