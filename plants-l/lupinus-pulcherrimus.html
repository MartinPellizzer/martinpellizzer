Lupinus pulcherrimus

Lupinus pulcherrimus, commonly known as the beautiful lupin, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and clusters of vibrant blue to purple pea-like flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open grasslands and rocky slopes.

Lupinus pulcherrimus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and role in ecological restoration.
