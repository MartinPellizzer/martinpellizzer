Ligularia dictyoneura

Ligularia dictyoneura, commonly known as the giant leopard plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 3 feet in length, with a distinctive leopard-like pattern of dark green veins against a lighter background. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Due to its size and visual impact, it is a popular choice for water gardens and shaded borders.
