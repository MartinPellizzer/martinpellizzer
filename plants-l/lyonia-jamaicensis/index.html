Lyonia Jamaicensis

Lyonia jamaicensis is a small evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean islands, particularly Jamaica and parts of the Greater Antilles. It is known for its delicate white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a touch of elegance to its rugged coastal habitat. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny areas along the shoreline. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public and is not widely cultivated. Its unique characteristics make it a fascinating subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
