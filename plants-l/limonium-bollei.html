Limonium bollei

Limonium bollei, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. Limonium bollei is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
