Lobelia puberula f. candida

Lobelia puberula f. candida, commonly known as the white lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
