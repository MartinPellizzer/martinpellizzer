Lasianthus euneurus

Lasianthus euneurus, commonly known as the slender-flowered lasianthus, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves. The flowers are typically arranged in whorls along the stem, with tubular corollas that are often pink or purple in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including countries like Thailand and Vietnam.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
