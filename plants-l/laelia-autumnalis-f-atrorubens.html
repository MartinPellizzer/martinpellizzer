Laelia autumnalis f. atrorubens

Laelia autumnalis f. atrorubens, commonly known as the dark red autumn laelia, is a unique orchid hybrid originating from Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking dark red to almost black petals and sepals, which contrast with its yellow-green lip. This cultivar is prized for its vibrant coloration and compact growth habit, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It typically blooms in the fall, adding a dramatic touch to winter gardens.

The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
