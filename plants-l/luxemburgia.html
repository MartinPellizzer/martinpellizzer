Luxemburgia

Luxemburgia, commonly known as the Luxembourg bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or wooded areas and prefers well-drained soil. Luxemburgia has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in the summer.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
