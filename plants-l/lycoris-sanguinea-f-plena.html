Lycoris sanguinea f. plena

Lycoris sanguinea f. plena, commonly known as the Chinese lantern lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It is a cultivar of the species Lycoris sanguinea, characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that emerge in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its unique appearance and seasonal bloom make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
