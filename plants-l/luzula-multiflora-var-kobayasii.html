Luzula multiflora var. kobayasii

Luzula multiflora var. kobayasii, commonly known as the Kobayashi's woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as grasses, and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems that bear small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
