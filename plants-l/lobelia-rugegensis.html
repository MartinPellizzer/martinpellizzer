Lobelia rugegensis

Lobelia rugegensis, commonly known as the Rupestris lobelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in mountainous regions.

Lobelia rugegensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
