Lotus corniculatus f. crassifolius

Lotus corniculatus f. crassifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved bird's-foot trefoil, is a variant of the common bird's-foot trefoil.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features trifoliate leaves with broad, lance-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
