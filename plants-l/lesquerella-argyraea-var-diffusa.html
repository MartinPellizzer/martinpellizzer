Lesquerella argyraea var. diffusa

Lesquerella argyraea var. diffusa, commonly known as the silverleaf lesquerella, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

Lesquerella argyraea var. diffusa is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water absorption.
