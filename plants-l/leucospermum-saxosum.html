Leucospermum saxosum

Leucospermum saxosum, commonly known as the rock lily, is a South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers and narrow, needle-like leaves. The plant is typically found growing on rocky outcrops and in sandy soils, thriving in full sun. Its flowers bloom in spring and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
