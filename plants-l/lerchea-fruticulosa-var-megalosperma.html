Lerchea fruticulosa var. megalosperma

Lerchea fruticulosa var. megalosperma, commonly known as the large-seeded lerchea, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces distinctive, large, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
