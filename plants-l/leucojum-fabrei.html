Leucojum fabrei

Leucojum fabrei, commonly known as the Chinese snowflake, is a perennial bulbous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features strap-shaped, evergreen leaves that remain throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Leucojum fabrei is valued for its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
