Liparis beddomei

Liparis beddomei, commonly known as the Beddome's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip that is often brightly colored. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and relatively easy-to-care-for orchid for enthusiasts.
