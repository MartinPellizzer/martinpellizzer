Lathyrus Leptophyllus

Lathyrus leptophyllus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is known for its slender stems and small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodlands or rocky habitats. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
