Lamium Vulgatum Var. Album

Lamium vulgatum var. album is a striking perennial plant known for its delicate white flowers. It belongs to the mint family and is commonly found in moist, woodland areas. This variety is often mistaken for its more common green-leaved counterpart due to its similar growth habits. The plant's elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a touch of refinement. Its blooming season typically spans from spring to early summer, adding a fresh, floral presence to any landscape.
