Lycaste virginalis f. alba

Lycaste virginalis f. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered lycaste, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

This hybrid orchid is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that contrast beautifully with its dark green, strap-like leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. The plant is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and unique floral display.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or controlled environments.
