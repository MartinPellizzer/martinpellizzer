Lasiobema scandens

Lasiobema scandens, commonly known as the climbing lasiobema, is a member of the Fabaceae family.

It is a deciduous, woody vine characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds.

Lasiobema scandens is native to parts of Asia and is often found in forested areas.
