Lindsaea ambigens

Lindsaea ambigens, commonly known as the ambiguous lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds that are typically light green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spores are produced in clusters on the underside of the fronds, a feature typical of many ferns.

Lindsaea ambigens plays a role in maintaining the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
