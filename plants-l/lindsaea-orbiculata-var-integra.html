Lindsaea orbiculata var. integra

Lindsaea orbiculata var. integra, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage composed of small, overlapping leaves that form a compact rosette. The plant produces delicate, pendulous inflorescences with small, white to greenish flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
