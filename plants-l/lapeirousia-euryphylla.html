Lapeirousia euryphylla

Lapeirousia euryphylla, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

Lapeirousia euryphylla is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its ornamental value and resilience.
