Lindsaea falcata var. leprieurii

Lindsaea falcata var. leprieurii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated fronds that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, clustered sporangia on the underside of its fertile fronds, which are typically found near the tips. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variety is distinguished by its narrow, leathery leaves and is commonly found in forest understories.
