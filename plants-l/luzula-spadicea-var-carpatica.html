Luzula spadicea var. carpatica

Luzula spadicea var. carpatica, commonly known as the Carpathian loosestrife, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
