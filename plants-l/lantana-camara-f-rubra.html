Lantana camara f. rubra

Lantana camara f. rubra, commonly known as red lantana, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and South America.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
