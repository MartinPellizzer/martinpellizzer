Lycopersicon peruvianum

Lycopersicon peruvianum, commonly known as the tomato, is a herbaceous annual plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which also includes potatoes and peppers. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that develop into bright red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in a fine pubescence.

The tomato is widely cultivated for its edible fruit, which is rich in vitamins and antioxidants.
