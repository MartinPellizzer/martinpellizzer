Lastrea kunzeana

Lastrea kunzeana, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and is often found growing on rocky outcrops. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve water in arid conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular species among succulent and orchid enthusiasts.
