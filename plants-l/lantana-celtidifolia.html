Lantana celtidifolia

Lantana celtidifolia, commonly known as the common lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, red, and purple, depending on the variety. It is known for its strong scent and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The plant is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
