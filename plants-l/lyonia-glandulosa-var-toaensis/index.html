Lyonia Glandulosa Var. Toaensis

Lyonia glandulosa var. toaensis is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions in the southeastern united states. it belongs to the heath family and is known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. this variety is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and dense, evergreen foliage. it thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories. due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
