Lasionema glabrescens

Lasionema glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth leaved lily, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and smooth, while its flowers are usually white or pale pink.

Lasionema glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
