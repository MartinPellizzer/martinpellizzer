Litsea paoueensis

Litsea paoueensis, commonly known as the Polynesian-laurel, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy upper surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in its native habitat.
