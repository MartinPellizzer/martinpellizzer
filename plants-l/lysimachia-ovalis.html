Lysimachia ovalis

Lysimachia ovalis, commonly known as the oval leaf speedwell, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the spring and early summer. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Lysimachia ovalis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
