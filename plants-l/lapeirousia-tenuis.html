Lapeirousia tenuis

Lapeirousia tenuis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Lapeirousia tenuis is a popular choice for rock gardens and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
