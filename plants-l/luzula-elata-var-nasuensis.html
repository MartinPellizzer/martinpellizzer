Luzula elata var. nasuensis

Luzula elata var. nasuensis, commonly known as the Japanese wood rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family, and is characterized by its clump-forming growth habit. This variety is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
