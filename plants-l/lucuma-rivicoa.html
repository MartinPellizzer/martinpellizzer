Lucuma rivicoa

Lucuma rivicoa, commonly known as the Andean sweet potato, is a tropical plant native to the highlands of Peru.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-colored berry that contains numerous seeds.
