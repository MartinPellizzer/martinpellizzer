Lactuca longirostra

Lactuca longirostra, commonly known as the long-beaked lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a loose cluster, which bloom in the summer months. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive appearance and hardy nature.
