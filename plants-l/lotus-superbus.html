Lotus superbus

Lotus superbus, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to Asia, particularly found in wetlands and ponds.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the surface of the water, while its roots anchor in the mud below. Lotus superbus is known for its ability to thrive in still water and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its flowers are also used in traditional medicine and religious ceremonies in various cultures.
