Lyonia ovalifolia var. formosana

Lyonia ovalifolia var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan lyonia, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are typically pink or white, clustered in umbels, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
