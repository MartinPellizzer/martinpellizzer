Limonium caprariae

Limonium caprariae, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and silvery-gray foliage. The plant thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often found in dunes and rocky shores. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Limonium caprariae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
