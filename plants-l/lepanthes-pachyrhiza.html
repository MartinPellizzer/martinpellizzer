Lepanthes pachyrhiza

Lepanthes pachyrhiza, commonly known as the broad-rooted orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit. The plant typically features showy, fragrant flowers that are often brightly colored, with a distinctive lip petal. Its roots are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Lepanthes pachyrhiza thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
