Lamarchea hakeifolia var. brevifolia

Lamarchea hakeifolia var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, urn-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
