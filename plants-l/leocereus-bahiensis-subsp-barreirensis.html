Leocereus bahiensis subsp. barreirensis

Leocereus bahiensis subsp.

barreirensis, commonly known as the Barreirensis Cactus, is a species native to Brazil. It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring a smooth, ribbed stem and spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the rainy season, typically in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
