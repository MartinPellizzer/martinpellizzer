Labordia tinifolia var. waialuana

Labordia tinifolia var. waialuana, commonly known as the Waialuana banyan, is a variant of the banyan tree native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant exhibits a distinctive aerial root system that allows it to form a dense canopy, creating a unique microhabitat. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variant is notable for its ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
