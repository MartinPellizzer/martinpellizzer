Lasianthaea helianthoides var. nayaritensis

Lasianthaea helianthoides var. nayaritensis, commonly known as the Nayarit sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that attract pollinators.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native range.
