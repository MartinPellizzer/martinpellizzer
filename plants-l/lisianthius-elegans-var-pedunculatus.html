Lisianthius elegans var. pedunculatus

Lisianthius elegans var. pedunculatus, commonly known as the elegant lisianthius, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage consists of alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
