Licuala leptocalyx

Licuala leptocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found growing in lowland rainforests.

The inflorescence produces small, fragrant flowers that are pollinated by insects and birds.
