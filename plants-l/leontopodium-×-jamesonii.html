Leontopodium × jamesonii

Leontopodium × jamesonii, commonly known as the edelweiss, is a flowering plant native to the Alps.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering of white bracts that surround the small yellow flowers. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a symbol of resilience and purity in Alpine cultures.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species.
