Lactuca scarioloides

Lactuca scarioloides, commonly known as scarlet lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae).

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its upright, branching stems and serrated, lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.

Lactuca scarioloides is not typically cultivated for food but is occasionally used in ornamental gardening due to its distinctive appearance.
