Lomaria onocleoides

Lomaria onocleoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lomaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, white or pale blue flowers in clusters. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in rock gardens.

Lomaria onocleoides is generally hardy and drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
