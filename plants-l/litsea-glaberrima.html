Litsea glaberrima

Litsea glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, often aromatic leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dry drupe that contains a single seed.

Litsea glaberrima is often found in lowland evergreen forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
