Lastrea platypus

Lastrea platypus, commonly known as the flat-leaved lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically flat and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
