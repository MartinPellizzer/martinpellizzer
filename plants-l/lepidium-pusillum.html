Lepidium pusillum

Lepidium pusillum, commonly known as dwarf cress, is a small flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few centimeters, with small, rounded leaves and tiny white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.

Lepidium pusillum is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed soils.
