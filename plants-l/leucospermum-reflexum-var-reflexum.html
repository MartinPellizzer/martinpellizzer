Leucospermum reflexum var. reflexum

Leucospermum reflexum var. reflexum, commonly known as the silverflame or firewheel plant, is a member of the Proteaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble wheels, which bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature contribute to its ornamental value in horticulture.
