Litsea salmonea

Litsea salmonea, commonly known as the red-flowered litsea, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers, which are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature, making it visually striking.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
