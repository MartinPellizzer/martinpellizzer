Lobularia maritima f. densiflora

Lobularia maritima f. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered coastal mustard, is a compact, low-growing plant native to coastal regions.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom profusely in summer, giving it a dense and showy appearance. The plant has hairy, lobed leaves that are adapted to tolerate salty air and sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is often found in dunes or along the coastline.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
