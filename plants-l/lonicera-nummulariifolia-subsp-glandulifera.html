Lonicera nummulariifolia subsp. glandulifera

Lonicera nummulariifolia subsp.

glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular honeysuckle, is a shrub native to parts of East Asia. It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, glandular hairs.

The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable candidate for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes used in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
