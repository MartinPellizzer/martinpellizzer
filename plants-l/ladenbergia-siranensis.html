Ladenbergia siranensis

Ladenbergia siranensis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.
