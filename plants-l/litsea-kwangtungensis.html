Litsea kwangtungensis

Litsea kwangtungensis, commonly known as Guangdong litsea, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by drupes that are typically yellow or red when mature.

Litsea kwangtungensis is often valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and fragrance.
