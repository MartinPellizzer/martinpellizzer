Lindera tzumu

Lindera tzumu, commonly known as Chinese wild laurier.

It is a deciduous shrub native to China, characterized by its aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Lauraceae family and is often found in forested areas. Its leaves are elliptical with a smooth surface and a fragrant scent when crushed.

Lindera tzumu is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine.
