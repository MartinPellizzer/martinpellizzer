Lobelia chinensis var. tetrapetala

Lobelia chinensis var. tetrapetala, commonly known as the four-petaled Chinese lobelia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features showy, blue to violet flowers that bloom in spikes during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are oppositely arranged along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
