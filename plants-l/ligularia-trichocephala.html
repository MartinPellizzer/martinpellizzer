Ligularia trichocephala

Ligularia trichocephala, commonly known as the hairy-headed ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive hairy covering on the underside. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a single, terminal inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to wetland gardens and aquatic plant collections.
