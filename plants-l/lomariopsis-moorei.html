Lomariopsis moorei

Lomariopsis moorei, commonly known as the Moore's fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Tanzania and Kenya. The plant is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
