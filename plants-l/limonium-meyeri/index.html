Limonium Meyeri

Sea lavender, scientifically known as Limonium meyeri, is a striking coastal plant that thrives in salty, windy environments. With its vivid purple-blue flowers, it adds a splash of color to beachscapes and dunes. Native to regions like the Mediterranean and the Atlantic coast, it has adapted to harsh maritime conditions. This resilient plant is often used in landscaping to stabilize soil and prevent erosion. Its unique beauty and ecological importance make it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
