Lycaste skinneri var. picturata

Lycaste skinneri var. picturata, commonly known as the picturata lycaste, is a hybrid orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that often display intricate patterns and hues of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, providing a lush, green backdrop to its showy blooms.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
