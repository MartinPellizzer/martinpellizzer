Lindsaea commixta

Lindsaea commixta, commonly known as the mixed lindsaea, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic. Lindsaea commixta is native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
