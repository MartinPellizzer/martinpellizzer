Lantana cinerea

Lantana cinerea, commonly known as silver lantana, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late winter to early spring. The plant has aromatic, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Botanically, it belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite leaf arrangement and showy inflorescences.
