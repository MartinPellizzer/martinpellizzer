Leipoldtia compressa

Leipoldtia compressa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small perennial herb with a distinctive rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
