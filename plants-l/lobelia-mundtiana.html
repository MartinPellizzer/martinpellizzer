Lobelia mundtiana

Lobelia mundtiana, commonly known as the Mountain Lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, tubular blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia mundtiana thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or woodland habitats.
