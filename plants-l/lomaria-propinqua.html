Lomaria propinqua

Lomaria propinqua, commonly known as the slender lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing herb with thin, wiry stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale blue or violet flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Lomaria propinqua is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
