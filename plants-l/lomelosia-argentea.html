Lomelosia argentea

Lomelosia argentea, commonly known as silverleaf lomelosia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which is covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Lomelosia argentea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
