Lespedeza maximowiczii var. tricolor

Lespedeza maximowiczii var. tricolor, commonly known as the tricolor lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Lespedeza maximowiczii var. tricolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide habitat for pollinators.
