Languas javanica

Languas javanica, commonly known as the Javanese orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with intricate patterns, and it produces small, round fruits.

Languas javanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
