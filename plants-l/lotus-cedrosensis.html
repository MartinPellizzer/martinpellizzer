Lotus cedrosensis

Lotus cedrosensis, commonly known as the Cedros Island lotus, is a flowering plant native to the island of Cedros in the Gulf of California.

It is a perennial herb that grows in coastal areas, often in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are palmately lobed and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Lotus cedrosensis is adapted to arid conditions and is considered a rare and endemic species.
