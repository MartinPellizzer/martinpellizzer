Laelia anceps var. barkeriana

Laelia anceps var. barkeriana, commonly known as Barker's Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
