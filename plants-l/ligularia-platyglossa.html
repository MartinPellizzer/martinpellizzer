Ligularia platyglossa

Ligularia platyglossa, commonly known as the broad-leaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for water gardens and shaded landscapes.
