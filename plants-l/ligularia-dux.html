Ligularia dux

Ligularia dux, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant features a distinctive, arrowhead-shaped leaf structure with a prominent midrib and serrated edges. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are typically surrounded by a spathe.

Ligularia dux thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage.
