Lindsaea cyathicola

Lindsaea cyathicola, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lindsaea, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are wiry and help anchor it to its host substrate.

The leaves are usually narrow and elongated, with a soft, velvety texture.
