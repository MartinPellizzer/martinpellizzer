Lilium speciosum var. roseum

Lilium speciosum var. roseum, commonly known as the roseum lily, is a striking variety of the species Lilium speciosum.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red, often with a distinctive white throat. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in well-drained, loamy soil. Its foliage consists of long, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
