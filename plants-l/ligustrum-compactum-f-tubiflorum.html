Ligustrum compactum f. tubiflorum

Ligustrum compactum f. tubiflorum, commonly known as the compact privet, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is valued for its dense foliage and adaptability to various soil types.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its ornamental appeal and hardiness.
