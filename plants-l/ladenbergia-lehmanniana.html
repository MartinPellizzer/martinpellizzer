Ladenbergia lehmanniana

Ladenbergia lehmanniana, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a tropical shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its unique appearance and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to ecological landscapes.
