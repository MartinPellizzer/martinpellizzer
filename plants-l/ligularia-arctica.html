Ligularia arctica

Ligularia arctica, commonly known as Arctic ligularia, is a perennial herb native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It features a rosette of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months, which are arranged in a distinctive ligulate structure. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

Ligularia arctica is often found in rocky soils and tundra habitats.
