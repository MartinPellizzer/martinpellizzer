Leutea laseroides

Leutea laseroides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pellitory, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy and bear small, lance-shaped leaves arranged in a basal rosette. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with five petals and a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
