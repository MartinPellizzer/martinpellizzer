Lychnophorella sericea

Lychnophorella sericea, commonly known as the silky lychnophore, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lychnophoraceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Uganda. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along riverbanks.
