Lasiochlamys reticulata

Lasiochlamys reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate network of veins on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat requirements, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
