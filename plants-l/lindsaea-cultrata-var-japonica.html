Lindsaea cultrata var. japonica

Lindsaea cultrata var. japonica, commonly known as the Japanese feather-fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble feathers, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in controlled indoor settings.
