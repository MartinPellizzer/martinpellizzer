Lactaria nakaiana

Lactaria nakaiana, commonly known as the Nakaian milkcap, is a species of mushroom belonging to the family Tricholomataceae.

It is characterized by its white to pale yellow cap, which often has a distinctive central bump or umbo. The gills are initially white but turn pale yellow with age, and the stem is typically slender and often slightly bulbous at the base. This mushroom is found in forested areas, particularly in regions with coniferous or mixed woodland vegetation.

It is saprophytic, decomposing organic matter and playing a role in nutrient cycling within its ecosystem.
