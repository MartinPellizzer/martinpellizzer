Lepanthes aciculifolia

Lepanthes aciculifolia, commonly known as the needle-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually short-lived, opening in the early morning and closing by midday.

Lepanthes aciculifolia is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
