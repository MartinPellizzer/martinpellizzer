Lomatium brandegeei

Lomatium brandegeei, commonly known as the Townsend's lomatium, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its fern-like foliage and umbel-shaped flowers.

Its flowers are small and white to pale green, often blooming in late spring to early summer.

The species is known for its aromatic properties and has been used historically by indigenous peoples for medicinal purposes.
