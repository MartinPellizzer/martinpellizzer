Lastrea dilatata var. recurva

Lastrea dilatata var. recurva, commonly known as the curled lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that often curl or twist, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
