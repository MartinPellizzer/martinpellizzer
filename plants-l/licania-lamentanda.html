Licania lamentanda

Licania lamentanda, commonly known as the weeping licania, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its drooping, cascading foliage that gives it its common name. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of yellow or green.

Licania lamentanda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
