Lerisca

Lerisca, commonly known as the silverleaf vine, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and distinctive silvery-gray foliage that reflects sunlight, helping it to retain moisture in harsh environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and pale yellow. Lerisca is a climbing plant that can be found along rocky outcrops and in dry woodlands.

Its adaptability to extreme conditions makes it an important component of its native ecosystems.
