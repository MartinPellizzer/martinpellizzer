Lavoisiera centiformis var. leucantha

Lavoisiera centiformis var. leucantha, commonly known as the white-flowered lavoisiera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a silvery underside. The plant produces clusters of showy, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
