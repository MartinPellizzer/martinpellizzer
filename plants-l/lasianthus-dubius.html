Lasianthus dubius

Lasianthus dubius, commonly known as the slender flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are clustered in whorled inflorescences.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.
