Lycium spinulosum

Lycium spinulosum, commonly known as the spiny lycium, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces red berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. Lycium spinulosum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and horticulture.
