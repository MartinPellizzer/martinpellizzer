Lepanthes hydrae

Lepanthes hydrae, commonly known as the Hydrae orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees and derives its nutrients from the air, rain, and debris around it. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the surrounding environment.

Lepanthes hydrae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
