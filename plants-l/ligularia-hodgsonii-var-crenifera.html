Ligularia hodgsonii var. crenifera

Ligularia hodgsonii var. crenifera, commonly known as the broad-leaved ligularia, is a perennial plant native to the Himalayas.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin, which give it its scientific name "crenifera," meaning "crenulated.". The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in streamside habitats.

Its foliage is known for its striking coloration, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
