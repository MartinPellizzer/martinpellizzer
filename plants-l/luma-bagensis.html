Luma bagensis

Luma bagensis, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically bloom at night. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.
