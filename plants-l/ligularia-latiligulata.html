Ligularia latiligulata

Ligularia latiligulata, commonly known as the broad-leaved ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed, with a velvety texture and a striking green color. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers in late summer, arranged in clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
