Labatia Beaurepairei

Labatia beaurepairei is a rare and striking member of the Malvaceae family known for its vibrant purple and pink flowers. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Cultivation of Labatia beaurepairei requires careful attention to soil and climate conditions to ensure its survival.
