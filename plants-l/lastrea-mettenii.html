Lastrea mettenii

Lastrea mettenii, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its segmented stems that develop into thornless, fleshy branches. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming around Christmas time, hence its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Lastrea mettenii is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
