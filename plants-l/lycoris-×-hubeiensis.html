Lycoris × hubeiensis

Lycoris × hubeiensis, commonly known as the Hubei lycoris, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in the autumn season. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30–60 cm, with narrow, strap-like leaves that are evergreen. Its bulbs are underground and serve as the storage organs for nutrients.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
