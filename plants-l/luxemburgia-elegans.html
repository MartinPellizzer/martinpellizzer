Luxemburgia elegans

Luxemburgia elegans, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America, characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Luxemburgia elegans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes used in landscape design.
