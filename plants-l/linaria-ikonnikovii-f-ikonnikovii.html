Linaria ikonnikovii f. ikonnikovii

Linaria ikonnikovii f. ikonnikovii, commonly known as the Ikonnikov's toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Linaria, which includes various species of flowering plants commonly found in Mediterranean and temperate climates. The plant produces striking, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
