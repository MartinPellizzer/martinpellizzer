Lycaste × smeeana

Lycaste × smeeana, commonly known as the hybrid nightshade, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from the cross between Lycaste and Smeeana.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
