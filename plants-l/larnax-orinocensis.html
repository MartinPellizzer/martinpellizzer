Larnax orinocensis

Larnax orinocensis, commonly known as the Orinoco larnax, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in the Orinoco River basin.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its woody stems and broad, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the dry season. Larnax orinocensis is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
