Lophiaris × teaboana

Lophiaris × teaboana, commonly known as the hybrid lophiaris, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are often spiny or toothed.
