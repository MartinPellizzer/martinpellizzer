Ledebouria atrobrunnea

Ledebouria atrobrunnea, commonly known as the dark-brown ledeburia, is a species of bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces white or pale yellow blooms during the summer months, which are followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
