Lotononis glabrescens

Lotononis glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth lotononis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. It has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
