Lampranthus furvus

Lampranthus furvus, commonly known as the firecracker plant, is a member of the Aizoaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often resembling firecrackers. Lampranthus furvus is also known for its ability to bloom profusely during the summer months.
