Loricaria stenophylla

Loricaria stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water lily, is a species of aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that provide habitat for various aquatic organisms. The plant has a well-developed root system that anchors it in shallow water. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and they bloom during the warmer months.

Loricaria stenophylla plays an important role in maintaining water quality and supporting aquatic ecosystems.
