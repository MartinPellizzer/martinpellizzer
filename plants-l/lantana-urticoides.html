Lantana urticoides

Lantana urticoides, commonly known as the shrub lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a common sight in tropical and subtropical regions.

Lantana urticoides is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
