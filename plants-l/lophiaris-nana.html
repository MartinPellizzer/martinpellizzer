Lophiaris nana

Lophiaris nana, commonly known as the dwarf lophiaris, is a compact, mat-forming succulent native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces delicate, yellowish-green flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
