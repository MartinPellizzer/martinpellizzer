Lobelia horsfieldiana

Lobelia horsfieldiana, commonly known as Horsfield's lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand.

It features showy, tubular blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
