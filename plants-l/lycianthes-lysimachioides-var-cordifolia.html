Lycianthes lysimachioides var. cordifolia

Lycianthes lysimachioides var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf nightshade, is a perennial herb native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by berries that range in color from green to red. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
