Lysiloma latisiliquum subsp. latifoliolatum

Lysiloma latisiliquum subsp.

latifoliolatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed trumpet tree, is a tropical species native to Central America. It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color.

The plant has compound leaves with broad, ovate leaflets that provide a lush, green canopy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The subspecies is distinguished by its broader foliage compared to other members of the Lysiloma genus.
