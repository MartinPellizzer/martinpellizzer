Lacinaria × platylepis

Lacinaria × platylepis, commonly known as the hybrid lacinato kale, is a cultivated variety of Brassica oleracea.

It is characterized by its dark green, crinkled leaves that are rich in nutrients such as vitamins A, C, and K. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and thrives in cool climates with well-drained soil. It is a biennial plant that is often grown as an annual in temperate regions.

The leaves are commonly used in cooking, adding a mild, earthy flavor to various dishes.
