Leucocoryne quilimarina

Leucocoryne quilimarina, commonly known as the white-flowered leucocoryne, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Brazil.

Leucocoryne quilimarina prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and rockeries.
