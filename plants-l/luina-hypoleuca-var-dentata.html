Luina hypoleuca var. dentata

Luina hypoleuca var. dentata, commonly known as the toothed lichen, is a species of lichen that exhibits distinct botanical characteristics.

It belongs to the family Parmeliaceae and is typically found on tree bark in temperate regions. The thallus is foliose, with a leathery texture and a pale green to yellowish coloration.
