Liparis refracta

Liparis refracta, commonly known as the refractable orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
