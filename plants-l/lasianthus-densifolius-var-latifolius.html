Lasianthus densifolius var. latifolius

Lasianthus densifolius var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a glossy surface that enhances its ornamental appeal. This species thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
