Limonium vigoi

Limonium vigoi, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and spikes of blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in coastal environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils. It is well adapted to salt spray and can tolerate drought conditions.

Limonium vigoi is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive, long-lasting blooms.
