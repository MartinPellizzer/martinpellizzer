Leucas paucicrenata

Leucas paucicrenata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved leucas, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine for various health purposes.
