Loeselia glandulosa subsp. glandulosa

Loeselia glandulosa subsp.

glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular loeselia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in spring.
