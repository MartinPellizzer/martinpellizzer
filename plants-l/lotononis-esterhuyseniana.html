Lotononis esterhuyseniana

Lotononis esterhuyseniana, commonly known as the Esterhuysen's lotononis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial herb that produces small, narrow leaves and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
