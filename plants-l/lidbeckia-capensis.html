Lidbeckia capensis

Lidbeckia capensis, commonly known as the Cape lidbeckia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of succulent, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched stems bearing clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water storage.

Lidbeckia capensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and resilience.
