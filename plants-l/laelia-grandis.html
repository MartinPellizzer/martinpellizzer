Laelia grandis

Laelia grandis, commonly known as the giant laelia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its mountainous habitat.

Laelia grandis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
