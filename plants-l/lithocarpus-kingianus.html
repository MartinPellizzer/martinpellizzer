Lithocarpus kingianus

Lithocarpus kingianus, commonly known as the King's tallow tree, is a member of the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and chestnuts.

It is a large evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The tree is also valued for its timber and is often used in traditional medicine.
