Lasthenia macrantha subsp. bakeri

Lasthenia macrantha subsp.

bakeri, commonly known as Baker's sticky fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy yellow to orange blooms.
