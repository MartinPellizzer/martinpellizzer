Laelia autumnalis f. alba

Laelia autumnalis f. alba, commonly known as the white autumn laelia, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom during the cooler months, typically in late autumn. The plant features strap-like leaves that are dark green and densely packed, providing a striking contrast to its pale blooms. It thrives in cool, humid environments with good air circulation and requires specific light conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
