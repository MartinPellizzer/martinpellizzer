Laelia anceps var. veitchiana

Laelia anceps var. veitchiana, commonly known as the Veitch's orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange blossoms with intricate patterns. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high light conditions.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
