Licuala calciphila

Licuala calciphila, commonly known as the calciphile palm, is a unique species adapted to grow in calcium-rich soils.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including the Philippines and Indonesia. This palm has a slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized plant.
