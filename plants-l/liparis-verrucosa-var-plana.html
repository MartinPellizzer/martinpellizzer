Liparis verrucosa var. plana

Liparis verrucosa var. plana, commonly known as the flat-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, often with a slight fragrance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and relatively rare orchid within its native range.
