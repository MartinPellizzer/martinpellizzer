Lactuca yemensis

Lactuca yemensis, commonly known as Yemen lettuce, is a species of lettuce native to Yemen.

It is characterized by its crisp, edible leaves that are typically light green in color. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes other members such as daisies and chicory. It thrives in arid and semi-arid climates, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique flavor and nutritional value, it is occasionally cultivated for local consumption and traditional use.
