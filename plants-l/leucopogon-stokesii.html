Leucopogon stokesii

Leucopogon stokesii, commonly known as Stokes' heath, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
