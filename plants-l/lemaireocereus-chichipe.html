Lemaireocereus chichipe

Lemaireocereus chichipe, commonly known as the Chichipe cactus, is a species native to Ecuador.

It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the evening, adding a striking visual contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
