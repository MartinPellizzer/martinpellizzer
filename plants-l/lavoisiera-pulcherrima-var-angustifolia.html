Lavoisiera pulcherrima var. angustifolia

Lavoisiera pulcherrima var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lavoisiera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.
