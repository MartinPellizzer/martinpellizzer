Lepidium rhytidocarpum

Lepidium rhytidocarpum, commonly known as the rough-seeded cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant has small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in tiny, raised dots.
