Lettsomia ionantha

Lettsomia ionantha, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, fleshy leaves that are covered in a dense layer of silvery-white hairs, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of rare orchids.
