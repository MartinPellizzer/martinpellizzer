Laserpitium prutenicum var. glabrum

Laserpitium prutenicum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth meadowgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually green to reddish in color and bloom during the summer months.

This variety is notable for its lack of pubescence, giving it a smooth, glabrous appearance.
