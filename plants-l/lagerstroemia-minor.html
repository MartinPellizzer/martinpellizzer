Lagerstroemia minor

Lagerstroemia minor, commonly known as the dwarf crape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, panicle-like flowers that range in color from pink to red, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval to lance-shaped and turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Lagerstroemia minor is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden environments.
