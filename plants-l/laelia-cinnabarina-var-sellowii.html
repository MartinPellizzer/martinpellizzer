Laelia cinnabarina var. sellowii

Laelia cinnabarina var. sellowii, commonly known as the Sellow's Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers with contrasting yellow lips, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, forming compact, upright pseudobulbs. Its foliage consists of narrow, fleshy leaves that help store moisture in its natural habitat.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
