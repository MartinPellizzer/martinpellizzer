Lepanthes biglomeris

Lepanthes biglomeris, commonly known as the clustered slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green in color. The plant produces numerous tiny flowers arranged in dense clusters, giving it its common name. Lepanthes biglomeris thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject for both horticultural study and conservation efforts.
