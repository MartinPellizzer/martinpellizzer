Luzula jilongensis

Luzula jilongensis, commonly known as the Jilong grass, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Jilong River region, where it thrives in moist, temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish flowers.

Luzula jilongensis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in wet environments.
