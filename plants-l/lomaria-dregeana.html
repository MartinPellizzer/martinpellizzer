Lomaria dregeana

Lomaria dregeana, commonly known as the yellow star lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a star-like shape. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features strap-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Lomaria dregeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
