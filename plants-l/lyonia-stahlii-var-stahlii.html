Lyonia stahlii var. stahlii

Lyonia stahlii var. stahlii, commonly known as the Stahli's lyonia, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California and northern Baja California.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal climates with mild winters and warm, dry summers.

Lyonia stahlii var. stahlii is often found in chaparral and coastal sage scrub habitats.
