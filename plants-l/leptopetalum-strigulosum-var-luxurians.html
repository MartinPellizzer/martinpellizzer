Leptopetalum strigulosum var. luxurians

Leptopetalum strigulosum var. luxurians, commonly known as the dense-leaved evergreen holly, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are densely packed, giving it a thick, lush appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its dense foliage and seasonal interest.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for rock gardens and as a border plant in woodland settings.
