Lactuca jamaicensis

Lactuca jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican lettuce, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and has heart-shaped, pale green leaves. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its seeds are dispersed by wind.

Lactuca jamaicensis is not commonly cultivated for food but is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
