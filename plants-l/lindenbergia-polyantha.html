Lindenbergia polyantha

Lindenbergia polyantha, commonly known as the pink root or purple root, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, tuberous roots and striking violet to pink flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture.

Lindenbergia polyantha is valued both for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
