Lonicera cochinchinensis

Lonicera cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow, typically blooming in spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains numerous small seeds.

Lonicera cochinchinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
