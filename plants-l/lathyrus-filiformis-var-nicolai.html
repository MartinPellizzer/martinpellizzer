Lathyrus filiformis var. nicolai

Lathyrus filiformis var. nicolai, commonly known as the Nicolai slender vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, trailing stems that produce small, pea-like flowers in shades of pink or violet. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen, benefiting soil fertility.
