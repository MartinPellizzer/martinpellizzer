Lepanthes hubeinii

Lepanthes hubeinii, commonly known as the Hubein's slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its compact appearance.

Lepanthes hubeinii is often grown as a terrestrial orchid in shaded, humid environments.
