Larix olgensis f. pubibasis

Larix olgensis f. pubibasis, commonly known as the Siberian larch, is a subspecies of the larch tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly twisted. The cones of this plant are woody and often hang downward, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in cold climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils.

This subspecies is valued for its hardiness and is used in reforestation efforts in its native habitat.
