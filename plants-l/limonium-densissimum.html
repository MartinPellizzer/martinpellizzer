Limonium densissimum

Limonium densissimum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant features silvery-gray, fuzzy leaves that help reduce water loss in saline environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and rocky shores.

Limonium densissimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal habitats.
