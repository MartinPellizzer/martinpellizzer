Lespedeza hedysaroides

Lespedeza hedysaroides, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, elongated pods containing several seeds. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Lespedeza hedysaroides is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and nitrogen-fixing abilities.
