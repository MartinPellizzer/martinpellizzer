Linaria reflexa var. cirtensis

Linaria reflexa var. cirtensis, commonly known as the Cirtensis toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and delicate, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
