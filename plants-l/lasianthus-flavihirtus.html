Lasianthus flavihirtus

Lasianthus flavihirtus, commonly known as the golden-haired lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
