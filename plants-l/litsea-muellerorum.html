Litsea muellerorum

Litsea muellerorum, commonly known as Mueller's litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

The fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that develops from the flower.
