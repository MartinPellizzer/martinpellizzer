Liparis reflexa var. cuneilabris

Liparis reflexa var. cuneilabris, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and North America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and a dense spike of pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are arranged in a spiral pattern, a feature common among orchids in the genus Liparis.

It is a perennial species that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
