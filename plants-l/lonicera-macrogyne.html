Lonicera macrogyne

Lonicera macrogyne, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are yellowish-green to orange-red in color. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

Lonicera macrogyne is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
