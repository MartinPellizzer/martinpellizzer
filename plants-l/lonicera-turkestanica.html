Lonicera turkestanica

Lonicera turkestanica, commonly known as Turkistan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer, attracting various wildlife.

Lonicera turkestanica is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
