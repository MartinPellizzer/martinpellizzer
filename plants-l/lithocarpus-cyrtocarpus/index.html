Lithocarpus Cyrtocarpus

Lithocarpus cyrtocarpus is a rare and enigmatic evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Fagaceae, which includes oaks and chestnuts, though it is less well-known than its more common relatives.
