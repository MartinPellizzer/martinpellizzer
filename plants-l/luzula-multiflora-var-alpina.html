Luzula multiflora var. alpina

Luzula multiflora var. alpina, commonly known as alpine woodrush, is a perennial herb native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. The plant features dense, grass-like foliage with narrow, dark green leaves that form a rosette at the base. It produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers in spike-like clusters during the summer months.

Luzula multiflora var. alpina is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
