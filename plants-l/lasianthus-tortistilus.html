Lasianthus tortistilus

Lasianthus tortistilus, commonly known as the twisted-stemmed lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, twisted stems and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.
