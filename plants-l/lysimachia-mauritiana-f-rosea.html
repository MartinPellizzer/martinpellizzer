Lysimachia mauritiana f. rosea

Lysimachia mauritiana f. rosea, commonly known as the rose-flowered yellow loosestrife, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking pink to rose-colored flowers, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.
