Lycaste lindeniana

Lycaste lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindenhall lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and pink. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in seasonal environments. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, providing a striking visual contrast to its floral display.

Lycaste lindeniana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
