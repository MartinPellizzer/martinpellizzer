Laelia × batemanniana

Laelia × batemanniana, commonly known as the Batemann's Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate for experienced growers.
