Lonicera wardii

Lonicera wardii, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruits are red berries that contain several seeds.

Lonicera wardii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine.
