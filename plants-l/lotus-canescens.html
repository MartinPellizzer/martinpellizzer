Lotus canescens

Lotus canescens, commonly known as the silver lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has heart-shaped, silver-veined leaves that are covered in a fine, fuzzy texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

The lotus canescens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowers.
