Lepanthes vilchezii

Lepanthes vilchezii, commonly known as the Vilchez's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact, low-growing habit and produces small, delicate flowers. The plant typically blooms in the spring, with white or pale pink petals and a yellow lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Lepanthes vilchezii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
