Ligularia virgaurea var. virgaurea

Ligularia virgaurea var. virgaurea, commonly known as the heartleaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive white or yellowish midrib. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and shaded landscapes.
