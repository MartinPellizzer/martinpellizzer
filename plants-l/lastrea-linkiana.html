Lastrea linkiana

Lastrea linkiana, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, Lastrea linkiana is a popular choice for low-maintenance gardening.
