Leutea polyscias

Leutea polyscias, commonly known as the broadleaf boxwood, is a evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dense, dark green foliage with elliptical leaves that are leathery in texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedging plant.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
