Lysimachia huttonii

Lysimachia huttonii, commonly known as Hutton's chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, hairy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rock crevices.

Lysimachia huttonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
