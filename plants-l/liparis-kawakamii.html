Liparis kawakamii

Liparis kawakamii, commonly known as Kawakami's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Japan.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant has a slender, upright stem with several oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in a loose inflorescence.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
