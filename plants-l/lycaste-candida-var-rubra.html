Lycaste candida var. rubra

Lycaste candida var. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered lycaste, is a hybrid orchid species originating from the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant typically produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of red and pink, often blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
