Luzula multiflora f. umbelliformis

Luzula multiflora f. umbelliformis, commonly known as the umbellate woodrush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences, giving it its distinctive umbelliform appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
