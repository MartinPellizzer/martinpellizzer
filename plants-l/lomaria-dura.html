Lomaria dura

Lomaria dura, commonly known as the hard lomaria, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its robust, woody stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Lomaria dura is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
