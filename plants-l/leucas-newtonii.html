Leucas newtonii

Leucas newtonii, commonly known as Newton's leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white, arranged in dense spikes. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
