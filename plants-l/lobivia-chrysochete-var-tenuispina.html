Lobivia chrysochete var. tenuispina

Lobivia chrysochete var. tenuispina, commonly known as the Narrow-spined Golden Lobivia, is a succulent native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It features a compact, branching structure with thick, fleshy stems that store water, typical of desert plants. The leaves are narrow and spiny, providing protection against herbivores and reducing water loss. Its flowers are yellow, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid environments.
