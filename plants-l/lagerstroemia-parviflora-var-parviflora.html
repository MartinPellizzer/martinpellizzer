Lagerstroemia parviflora var. parviflora

Lagerstroemia parviflora var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered crape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has smooth, mottled bark that peels to reveal a reddish inner layer, enhancing its visual appeal throughout the year. Its leaves are ovate, with a glossy green surface and a slightly serrated edge.

It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
