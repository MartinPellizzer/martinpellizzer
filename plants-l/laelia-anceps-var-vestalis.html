Laelia anceps var. vestalis

Laelia anceps var. vestalis, commonly known as the White Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its elegant white or pale pink flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant typically grows as a compact, sympodial orchid with fleshy, dark green leaves. It thrives in cool, bright conditions with moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
