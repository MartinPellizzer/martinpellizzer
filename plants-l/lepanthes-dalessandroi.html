Lepanthes dalessandroi

Lepanthes dalessandroi, commonly known as the Dalessandro's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a miniature orchid, with slender stems and clustered blooms. Its flowers often display vibrant hues of red, orange, and yellow, making it a visually appealing species.

Lepanthes dalessandroi is adapted to humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to thrive.
