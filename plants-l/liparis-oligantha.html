Liparis oligantha

Liparis oligantha, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, creeping rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base, while its flowering stems are short and upright.

Liparis oligantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
