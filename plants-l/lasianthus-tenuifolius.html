Lasianthus tenuifolius

Lasianthus tenuifolius, commonly known as the slender-leafed lasianthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
