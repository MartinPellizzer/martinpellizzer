Lightfootia calcarea

Lightfootia calcarea, commonly known as the limestone rockrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are small, succulent, and often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

Lightfootia calcarea is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
