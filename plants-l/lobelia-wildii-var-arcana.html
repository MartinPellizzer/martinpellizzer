Lobelia wildii var. arcana

Lobelia wildii var. arcana, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the high elevations of the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, rocky habitats. The plant produces clusters of delicate, sky-blue to purple flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system adapted to its alpine environment.

This variety is notable for its limited distribution and is considered a rare and endangered species.
