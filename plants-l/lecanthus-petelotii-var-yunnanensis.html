Lecanthus petelotii var. yunnanensis

Lecanthus petelotii var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan lecanthus, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Lecanthaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a rough texture, and it produces small, yellowish-white flowers.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
