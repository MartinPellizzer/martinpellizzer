Litsea morobensis

Litsea morobensis, commonly known as the Morobe litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of the South Pacific, particularly in Papua New Guinea and parts of Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that contains a single seed.

Litsea morobensis is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
