Linaria tournefortii f. minor

Linaria tournefortii f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf toadflax, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, often thriving in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
