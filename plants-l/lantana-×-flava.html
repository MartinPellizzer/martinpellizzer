Lantana × flava

Lantana × flava, commonly known as the yellow lantana, is a hybrid shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are often eaten by birds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

However, it can be invasive in some areas, requiring careful management to prevent ecological disruption.
