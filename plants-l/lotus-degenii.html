Lotus degenii

Lotus degenii, commonly known as the pink lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or purple flowers that bloom above the water's surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water, while its roots anchor it in the muddy substrate. Lotus degenii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in ponds and water gardens.

It plays a significant role in traditional medicine and cultural symbolism in various regions.
