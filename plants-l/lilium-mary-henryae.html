Lilium mary-henryae

Lilium mary-henryae, commonly known as Mary Henry's lily, is a rare and striking species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant belongs to the Liliaceae family and is known for its ornamental value and botanical significance. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in cool, temperate climates.
