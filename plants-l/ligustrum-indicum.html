Ligustrum indicum

Ligustrum indicum, commonly known as Indian privet, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters, followed by small, dark berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dense foliage and adaptability to various soil types.

Ligustrum indicum is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
