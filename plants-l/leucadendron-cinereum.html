Leucadendron cinereum

Leucadendron cinereum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It is a member of the Proteaceae family and is known for its adaptability to dry conditions.

The silver tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
