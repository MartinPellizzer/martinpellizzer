Lespedeza wilfordii

Lespedeza wilfordii, commonly known as Chinese stragglerbush, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
