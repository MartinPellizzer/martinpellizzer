Lachnaea pudens

Lachnaea pudens, commonly known as the meadow lichen, is a species of lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It belongs to the family Lecanoraceae and is characterized by its crust-like growth form. The thallus of Lachnaea pudens is typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a dense, tightly packed structure. This lichen is often found on soil, rocks, or decaying wood in temperate regions.
