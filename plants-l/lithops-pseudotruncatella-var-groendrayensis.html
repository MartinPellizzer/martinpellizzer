Lithops pseudotruncatella var. groendrayensis

Lithops pseudotruncatella var. groendrayensis, commonly known as the Groenday's ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features paired, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
