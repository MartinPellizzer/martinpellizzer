Lilium canadense var. pluriflorum

Lilium canadense var. pluriflorum, commonly known as the Canadian white lily, is a variant of the Canada lily native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its white to pale pink flowers, which typically bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves and grows from a rhizomatous root system. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
