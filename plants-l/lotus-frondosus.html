Lotus frondosus

Lotus frondosus, commonly known as the water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has broad, floating leaves that provide shade and support for aquatic life. Its flowers are typically white or pink, with showy petals that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it anchor in muddy substrates.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by improving water quality and providing habitat for various species.
