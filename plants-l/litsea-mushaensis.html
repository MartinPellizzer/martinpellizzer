Litsea mushaensis

Litsea mushaensis, commonly known as the Musha laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Malaysian rainforests. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dark reddish-brown drupe, containing a single seed.

Litsea mushaensis plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
