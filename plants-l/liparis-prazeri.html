Liparis prazeri

Liparis prazeri, commonly known as the Pazer's orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the genus Liparis in the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, often greenish or yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces small, strap-like leaves.

Liparis prazeri is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
