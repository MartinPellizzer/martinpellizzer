Lepanthes cosmos

Lepanthes cosmos, commonly known as the cosmos orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air.

Lepanthes cosmos is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
