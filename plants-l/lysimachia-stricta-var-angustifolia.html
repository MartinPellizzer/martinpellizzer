Lysimachia stricta var. angustifolia

Lysimachia stricta var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf yellowstar, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
