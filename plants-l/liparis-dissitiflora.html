Liparis dissitiflora

Liparis dissitiflora, commonly known as the scattered-flower orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, where it thrives in humid and cool conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a greenish-yellow color and a distinct lip that aids in pollination.

The species is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and is often found in rocky or clayey soils.
