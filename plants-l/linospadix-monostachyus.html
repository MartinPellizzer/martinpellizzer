Linospadix monostachyus

Linospadix monostachyus, commonly known as the African arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in a dense, cylindrical structure on the spadix. Its leaves are large, arrow-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

The plant is cultivated for its starchy tubers, which are used as a food source in some regions.
