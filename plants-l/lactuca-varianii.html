Lactuca varianii

Lactuca varianii, commonly known as the Persian lettuce, is a species of lettuce native to the Middle East and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its rosette growth habit and dark green, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is a hardy annual that thrives in temperate climates and well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and valuable addition to both ornamental and edible gardens.
