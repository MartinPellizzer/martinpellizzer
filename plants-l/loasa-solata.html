Loasa solata

Loasa solata, commonly known as golden loasa, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange inflorescences that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a grayish or hairy texture.

Loasa solata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
