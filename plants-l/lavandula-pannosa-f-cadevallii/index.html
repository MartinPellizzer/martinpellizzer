Lavandula Pannosa F. Cadevallii

Lavandula pannosa f. cadevallii is a rare and striking plant known for its unique silver-blue foliage and fragrant purple flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, this cultivar thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens and small spaces. The plant's ornamental value has made it a sought-after addition to many horticultural collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively uncommon in cultivation due to its specific growing requirements.
