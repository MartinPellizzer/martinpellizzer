Linderniella Brevidens

Linderniella brevidens is a small, delicate flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Linderniaceae and is known for its unique, compact growth habit. Despite its modest size, it produces striking clusters of tiny, pale blue flowers. The plant is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rarity and beauty make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
