Lepidoneuron sesquipedale

Lepidoneuron sesquipedale, commonly known as the giant flame grass, is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant produces elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its size and vibrant blooms, it is often used as a focal point in floral arrangements and ornamental gardens.
