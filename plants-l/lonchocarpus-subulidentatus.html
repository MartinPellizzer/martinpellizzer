Lonchocarpus subulidentatus

Lonchocarpus subulidentatus, commonly known as the slender awl-leaf legume, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features finely divided, awl-shaped leaves that give it its common name. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive papilionaceous structure.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological roles in its native habitats.
