Lyonsia reticulata

It is characterized by its intricate, web-like thallus structure, which gives it its scientific name. It is typically found on bark, rocks, and soil surfaces in temperate and boreal regions.

Due to its slow growth and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is often used as an indicator species for ecological health.
