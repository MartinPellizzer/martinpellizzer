Lactuca porphyrea

Lactuca porphyrea, commonly known as purple lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its rosette of lance-shaped, bluish-green leaves with a purple tinge. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Lactuca porphyrea is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
