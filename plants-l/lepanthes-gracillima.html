Lepanthes gracillima

Lepanthes gracillima, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, contributing to its overall slender appearance.

Lepanthes gracillima is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
