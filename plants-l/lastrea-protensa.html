Lastrea protensa

Lastrea protensa, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented stems that resemble leaves. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and orange, typically during the winter months. Its blooming period is influenced by shorter daylight hours and cooler temperatures.

Lastrea protensa is well-suited for indoor cultivation and requires moderate watering and bright, indirect light.
