Lobelia linifolia

Lobelia linifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces small, blue to purple flowers in the summer months. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.

Lobelia linifolia is often used in native plant gardens and restoration projects due to its adaptability and beauty.
