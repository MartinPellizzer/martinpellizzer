Laserpitium asperum

Laserpitium asperum, commonly known as rough hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is often used in herbal medicine for its potential diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties.
