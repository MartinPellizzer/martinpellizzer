Lithocarpus tubulosus

Lithocarpus tubulosus, commonly known as the tubular evergreen oak, is a species of evergreen tree belonging to the Fagaceae family.

It is characterized by its hard, dense wood and leathery, evergreen leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, cup-shaped fruits that contain a single seed, a feature common among members of its family. It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and the Philippines.

Lithocarpus tubulosus is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its durable timber in local crafts and construction.
