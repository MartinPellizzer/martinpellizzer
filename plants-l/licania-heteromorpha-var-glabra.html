Licania heteromorpha var. glabra

Licania heteromorpha var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth licania, is a species of flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and lanceolate leaves with a prominent midrib. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to orange flowers arranged in clusters, which attract various pollinators. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
