Lappula tenuis

Lappula tenuis, commonly known as slender lappula, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Eurasia.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, meadows, and riverbanks. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a slender, beak-like capsule that contains several small seeds.

Lappula tenuis is characterized by its simple, erect stem and is often found in disturbed or wetland environments.
