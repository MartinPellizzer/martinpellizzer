Lasiopetalum compactum

Lasiopetalum compactum, commonly known as the compact honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. The plant features small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

The compact honeysuckle is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
