Lithops eberlanzii var. aiaisensis

Lithops eberlanzii var. aiaisensis, commonly known as the Aiais lithops, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rock-like appearance, with two fleshy leaves that resemble stones. The plant stores water in its thick, fleshy leaves, allowing it to survive in arid environments. It produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
