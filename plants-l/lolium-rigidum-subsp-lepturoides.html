Lolium rigidum subsp. lepturoides

Lolium rigidum subsp.

lepturoides, commonly known as slender ryegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Australia. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny florets. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Its rapid growth and high nutritional value make it a valuable forage crop.
