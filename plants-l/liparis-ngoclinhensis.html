Liparis ngoclinhensis

Liparis ngoclinhensis, commonly known as the Ngọc Linh orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found in the Ngọc Linh mountain range in Vietnam.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has linear to lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species.
