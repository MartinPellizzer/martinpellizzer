Limonium didimense

Limonium didimense, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, salt marshes, and sandy soils, showing a high tolerance for saline conditions. Limonium didimense is a perennial herb that blooms in summer, producing clusters of small, showy flowers.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
