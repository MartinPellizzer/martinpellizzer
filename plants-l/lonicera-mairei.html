Lonicera mairei

Lonicera mairei, commonly known as the Maire honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, appearing in late spring. Its berries are red and edible, though they are not commonly consumed by humans.

Lonicera mairei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
