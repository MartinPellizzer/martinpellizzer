Liparis viridipurpurea

Liparis viridipurpurea, commonly known as the green-purple orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant features long, slender stems with lance-shaped leaves that are usually a mix of green and purple hues. Its flowers are small, clustered in racemes, and display a striking combination of green and purple petals.

Liparis viridipurpurea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
