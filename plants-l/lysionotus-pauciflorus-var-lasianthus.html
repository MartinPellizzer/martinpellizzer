Lysionotus pauciflorus var. lasianthus

Lysionotus pauciflorus var. lasianthus, commonly known as the white-flowered starling orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticulture.
