Leucospermum reflexum var. luteum

Leucospermum reflexum var. luteum, commonly known as the yellow star jelly, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
