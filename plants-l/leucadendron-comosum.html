Leucadendron comosum

Leucadendron comosum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, feathery foliage that gives it a unique, almost ethereal appearance. The plant produces cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the species. It is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system to access groundwater.

Leucadendron comosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a symbol of the fynbos vegetation in South Africa.
