Limeum viscosum var. kotschyi

Limeum viscosum var. kotschyi, commonly known as the sticky lime tree, is a shrub native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its sticky, resinous leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

This species is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.
