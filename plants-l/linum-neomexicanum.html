Linum neomexicanum

Linum neomexicanum, commonly known as New Mexico flax, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are linear and often have a slight pubescence.

Linum neomexicanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
