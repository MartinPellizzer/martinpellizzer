Leonotis velutina var. raineriana

Leonotis velutina var. raineriana, commonly known as the Raineriana lion's tail, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features soft, velvety foliage that adds a tactile element to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
