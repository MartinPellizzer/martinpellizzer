Lobularia maritima var. densiflora

Lobularia maritima var. densiflora, commonly known as coastal lobularia, is a compact, low-growing flowering plant native to coastal regions.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in clusters. The plant has silvery-gray foliage with a dense, mat-forming habit that helps it thrive in sandy or rocky soils. It is well adapted to salt spray and wind, making it a hardy choice for coastal gardens.

This variety is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant due to its ornamental value and resilience.
