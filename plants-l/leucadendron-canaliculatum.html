Leucadendron canaliculatum

Leucadendron canaliculatum, commonly known as the silver leaf leucadendron, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, hair-like structures. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flower heads that are surrounded by papery bracts. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
