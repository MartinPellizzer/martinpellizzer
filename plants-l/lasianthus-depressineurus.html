Lasianthus depressineurus

Lasianthus depressineurus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has opposite, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
