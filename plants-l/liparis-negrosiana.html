Liparis negrosiana

Liparis negrosiana, commonly known as the Negros orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Liparis negrosiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
