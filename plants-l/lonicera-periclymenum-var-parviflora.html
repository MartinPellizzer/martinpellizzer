Lonicera periclymenum var. parviflora

Lonicera periclymenum var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flower honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually hairy beneath. It produces red or orange berries that are attractive to birds.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
