Lilium martagon f. roseum

Lilium martagon f. roseum, commonly known as the roseum peony lily, is a striking hybrid cultivar of the martagon lily.

It features showy, fragrant flowers with a unique rose-pink hue, often with a white or pale pink center. The plant produces bell-shaped, clustered blooms that are highly ornamental and attract pollinators. It is a perennial herbaceous plant with strap-like, dark green foliage.

Native to hybrid gardens, it thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
