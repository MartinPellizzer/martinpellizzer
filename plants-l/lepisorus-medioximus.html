Lepisorus medioximus

Lepisorus medioximus, commonly known as the broad-leafed rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments. Its botanical features make it well adapted to humid, temperate climates.
