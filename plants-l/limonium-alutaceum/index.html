Limonium Alutaceum

Limonium alutaceum is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region known for its delicate, clustered blooms. It thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often found along shorelines and dunes. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping. Despite its modest appearance, Limonium alutaceum holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitats.
