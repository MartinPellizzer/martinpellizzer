Lophanthus scrophulariifolius

Lophanthus scrophulariifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lophanthus, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
