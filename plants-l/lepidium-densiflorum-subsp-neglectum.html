Lepidium densiflorum subsp. neglectum

Lepidium densiflorum subsp.

neglectum, commonly known as slender rocket, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and pungent aroma.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands. It produces small, elongated leaves and has a compact growth habit.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.
