Ligusticum Mongholicum

Ligusticum mongholicum is a rare and valuable medicinal plant native to the mountainous regions of northern China. Known for its distinctive aromatic leaves and roots, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries. This plant is particularly noted for its potential therapeutic properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Due to over-harvesting and habitat loss, it is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this important species for future generations.
