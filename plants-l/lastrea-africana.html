Lastrea africana

Lastrea africana, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, branching structure with fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and purple during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance, it is popular among gardeners in dry climates.
