Leptorkis loeselii

Leptorkis loeselii, commonly known as the Loesel's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Leptorkis loeselii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
