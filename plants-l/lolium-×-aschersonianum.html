Lolium × aschersonianum

Lolium × aschersonianum, commonly known as perennial ryegrass, is a hybrid grass species widely used in turfgrass and forage production.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green blades and dense growth habit, making it ideal for lawns and sports fields. The plant typically grows to a height of 20–30 cm and has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in temperate climates, preferring well-drained soils.

Lolium × aschersonianum is also valued for its high nutritional value, making it a popular choice for livestock grazing.
