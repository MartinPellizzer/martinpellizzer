Ligularia changiana

Ligularia changiana, commonly known as the Chinese ligularia, is a striking perennial plant native to China.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in the summer, which are clustered in a spire-like inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
