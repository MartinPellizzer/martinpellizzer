Leucadendron elimense subsp. elimense

Leucadendron elimense subsp.

elimense, commonly known as the yellow starling leucadendron, is a shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its striking, cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the season.

The plant produces small, pale green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in fynbos vegetation.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
