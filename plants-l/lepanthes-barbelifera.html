Lepanthes barbelifera

Lepanthes barbelifera, commonly known as the feather-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a dark violet or purple coloration. The plant produces small, delicate leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette pattern. Lepanthes barbelifera thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
