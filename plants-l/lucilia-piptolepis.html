Lucilia piptolepis

Lucilia piptolepis, commonly known as the common green bottle fly, is a species of fly belonging to the family Calliphoridae.

It is characterized by its metallic green body and is often found near decaying organic matter. The larvae of this species are known as maggots and play a role in the decomposition process. The plant aspect refers to its association with certain plants that provide suitable substrates for oviposition.

It is not a plant itself but is often found in proximity to plants in its habitat.
