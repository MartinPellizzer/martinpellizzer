Lobelia aemula

Lobelia aemula, commonly known as the common lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Lobelia aemula is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
