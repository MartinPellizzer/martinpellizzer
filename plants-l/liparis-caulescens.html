Liparis caulescens

Liparis caulescens, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and the flowers are usually greenish-white with a subtle fragrance.

Liparis caulescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
