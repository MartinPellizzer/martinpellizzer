Lobelia fulgens var. glabriuscula

Lobelia fulgens var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the glossy lobelia, is a striking perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy but become smooth as the plant matures. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

This variety is prized for its vibrant color and is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value.
