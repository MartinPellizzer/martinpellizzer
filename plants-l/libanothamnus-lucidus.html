Libanothamnus lucidus

Libanothamnus lucidus, commonly known as the goldenbush, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that is typically bright yellow to golden in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal climates with mild winters and warm, dry summers.

Libanothamnus lucidus plays an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for local wildlife.
