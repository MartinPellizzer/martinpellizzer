Ligularia caloxantha

Ligularia caloxantha, commonly known as the purple ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive purple underside, which becomes more pronounced in shade. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and shaded landscapes.
