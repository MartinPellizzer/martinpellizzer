Lastrea nitens

Lastrea nitens, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with high sunlight exposure. Its flowers, which are usually white or pale yellow, bloom in late summer and early autumn.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, Lastrea nitens is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
