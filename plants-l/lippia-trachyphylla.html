Lippia trachyphylla

Lippia trachyphylla, commonly known as roughleaf lippia, is a shrub native to Central and South America.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves with a rough texture, and small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid environments.
