Linaria riffea

Linaria riffea, commonly known as the rock everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Linaria riffea is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid environments.
