Limonium lowei

Limonium lowei, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often used in coastal landscaping due to its salt tolerance and ability to withstand harsh maritime conditions.

Limonium lowei is a perennial species that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
