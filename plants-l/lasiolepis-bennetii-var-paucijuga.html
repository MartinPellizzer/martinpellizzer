Lasiolepis bennetii var. paucijuga

Lasiolepis bennetii var. paucijuga, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lasiolepis, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous root system. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
