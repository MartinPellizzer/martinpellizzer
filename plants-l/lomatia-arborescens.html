Lomatia arborescens

Lomatia arborescens, commonly known as the mountain daisy or rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it is adapted to harsh environmental conditions.

Lomatia arborescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
