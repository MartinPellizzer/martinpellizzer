Ligusticum kellerianum

Ligusticum kellerianum, commonly known as Chinese lovage, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

Its rhizomes and roots are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Ligusticum kellerianum thrives in moist, shady environments and is valued both for its medicinal properties and ornamental appeal.
