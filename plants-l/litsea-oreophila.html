Litsea oreophila

Litsea oreophila, commonly known as the mountain litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Vietnam, Thailand, and Malaysia. This evergreen shrub or small tree typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments at higher elevations. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a distinctive aromatic scent when crushed.
