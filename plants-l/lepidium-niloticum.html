Lepidium niloticum

Lepidium niloticum, commonly known as water cress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small white or pink flowers. The plant is typically found in moist environments such as stream banks and wetlands. It has a peppery flavor and is often used in culinary applications.

Botanically, it is noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in aquatic or semi-aquatic conditions.
