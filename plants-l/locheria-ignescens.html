Locheria ignescens

Locheria ignescens, commonly known as the flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its trunk is often smooth and greyish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including birds and insects, contributing to its ecological significance.
