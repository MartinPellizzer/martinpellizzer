Lasiorrhiza Candidissima

Lasiorrhiza candidissima is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its striking white blooms that contrast with its green foliage. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh environment. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this unique species for future generations.
