Lecosia formicarum

Lecosia formicarum, commonly known as the ant-mimic plant, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its unique leaf structure that resembles the appearance of ants, providing it with effective camouflage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and evolutionary studies.
