Lophosoria prostrata

Lophosoria prostrata, commonly known as the golden thread orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, with slender, creeping stems that root at the nodes. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often resembling a thread-like structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Lophosoria prostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
