Larryleachia felina

Larryleachia felina, commonly known as the cat's paw plant, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that resemble a cat's paw, which help it store water for long periods. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its root system is shallow and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil quickly.

Larryleachia felina is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-water conditions.
