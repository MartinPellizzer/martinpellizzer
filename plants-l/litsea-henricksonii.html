Litsea henricksonii

Litsea henricksonii, commonly known as the Philippine laurier or white litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves and small white flowers. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to yellow when mature.

Litsea henricksonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
