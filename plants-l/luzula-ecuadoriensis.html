Luzula ecuadoriensis

Luzula ecuadoriensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian woodrush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. Native to the Andean regions of Ecuador, it thrives in cool, moist environments such as mountain forests and rocky slopes. Its botanical features make it an important component of high-altitude ecosystems in South America.
