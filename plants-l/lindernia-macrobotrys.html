Lindernia macrobotrys

Lindernia macrobotrys, commonly known as the showy lindernia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Lindernia macrobotrys is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
