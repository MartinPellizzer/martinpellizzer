Leontodon asper var. setulosus

Leontodon asper var. setulosus, commonly known as the hairy rough hawk's-beard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, hairy stems and yellow composite flowers. The leaves are typically rough and serrated, contributing to its rugged appearance. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

This variety is distinguished by its dense, bristly hairs, which provide protection against herbivores and environmental stress.
