Lowea berberifolia

Lowea berberifolia, commonly known as the yellow-leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow foliage and small, daisy-like white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and has adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
