Lepidium menziesii

Lepidium menziesii, commonly known as coastal rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. This annual herb typically grows in coastal areas, preferring sandy or gravelly soils and full sun. Its small, yellow flowers appear in early spring, and it produces elongated pods that contain several seeds.

The plant is adapted to coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and strong winds.
