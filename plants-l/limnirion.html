Limnirion

Limnirion, commonly known as the waterweed, is a genus of aquatic plants found in freshwater environments.

These plants typically have slender, branching stems that float or root in shallow waters. Their leaves are often small, ribbon-like, and arranged in whorls around the stem. Limnirion species are known for their ability to thrive in nutrient-rich, still or slow-moving waters.

They play an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen for various aquatic life forms.
