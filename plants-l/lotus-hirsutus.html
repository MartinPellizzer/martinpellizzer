Lotus hirsutus

Lotus hirsutus, commonly known as the hairy lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and marshes. The plant features broad, floating leaves with a hairy texture, which helps reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with a prominent central stigma and showy petals.

Lotus hirsutus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
