Lapathum venosum

Lapathum venosum, commonly known as the veiny betony, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive veining pattern. Its flowers are typically purple or pink, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It is often found in moist meadows and woodland edges, and it has been used historically in traditional medicine.
