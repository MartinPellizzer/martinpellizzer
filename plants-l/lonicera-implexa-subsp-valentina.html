Lonicera implexa subsp. valentina

Lonicera implexa subsp.

valentina, commonly known as the Valentina honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia. It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside.

The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
