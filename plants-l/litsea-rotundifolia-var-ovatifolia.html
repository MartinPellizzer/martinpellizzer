Litsea rotundifolia var. ovatifolia

Litsea rotundifolia var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the ovate-leafed litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like China, Vietnam, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, ovate leaves that are usually aromatic and have a glossy surface. It produces small, white flowers followed by edible, round berries that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
