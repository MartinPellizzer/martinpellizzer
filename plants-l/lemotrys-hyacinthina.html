Lemotrys hyacinthina

Lemotrys hyacinthina, commonly known as the blue hyacinth, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear clusters of vibrant blue to violet flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
