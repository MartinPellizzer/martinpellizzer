Litsea chunii var. latifolia

Litsea chunii var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Vietnam, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue berries.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitats.
