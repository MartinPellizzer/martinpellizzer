Lepidium chinense

Lepidium chinense, commonly known as Chinese cress, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant features small, yellow flowers and is often found in moist, disturbed soils. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery taste, similar to that of watercress.

Lepidium chinense is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
