Ligularia stenocephala

Ligularia stenocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed ligularia, is a perennial herb native to eastern Africa.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are deeply veined and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a dense, terminal inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
