Lepanthes splendida

Lepanthes splendida, commonly known as the Splendid Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and a distinctive scent to attract pollinators.

Lepanthes splendida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
