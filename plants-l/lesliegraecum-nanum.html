Lesliegraecum nanum

Lesliegraecum nanum, commonly known as the dwarf lesliegraecum, is a small, herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South Africa.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features compact, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in shades of white or pale pink, and bloom during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
