Lycaste youngii

Lycaste youngii, commonly known as the young's lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically has broad, elliptical leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Lycaste youngii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
