Leonotis spectabilis

Leonotis spectabilis, commonly known as the lion's tail, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. Leonotis spectabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also used in traditional medicine.
