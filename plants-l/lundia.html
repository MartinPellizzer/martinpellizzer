Lundia

Lundia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety, waxy leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with contrasting markings. Lundia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
