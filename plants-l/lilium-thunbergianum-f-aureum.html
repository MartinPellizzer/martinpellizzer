Lilium thunbergianum f. aureum

Lilium thunbergianum f. aureum, commonly known as the Aureum lily, is a cultivar of the Asian tiger lily.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers with a yellow throat, which contrast vividly with its green, strap-like leaves. This plant is native to East Asia but is widely cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, thriving in temperate climates.

The foliage is evergreen in mild winters, adding visual interest throughout the year.
