Luisia magniflora

Luisia magniflora, commonly known as the showy luisia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Luisia magniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
