Leuzea australis

Leuzea australis, commonly known as the coastal leuzea, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in coastal environments, often found on sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Leuzea australis is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
