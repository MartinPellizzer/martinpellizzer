Leptopteris × intermedia

Leptopteris × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid fern, is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Leptopteris filix-ma and Leptopteris trichomanes.

Due to its sterile nature, it does not produce viable spores and is often found as a natural hybrid in the wild.
