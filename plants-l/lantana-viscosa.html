Lantana viscosa

Lantana viscosa, commonly known as rough lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to yellow, red, or orange. Its leaves are usually rough to the touch, hence the common name, and are often covered in fine hairs.

Lantana viscosa is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed habitats.
