Lepanthes longipedicellata

Lepanthes longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-stemmed nightshade orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale green to white flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

Lepanthes longipedicellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
