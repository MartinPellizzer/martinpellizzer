Lightfootia thunbergiana

Lightfootia thunbergiana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety, waxy leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Lightfootia thunbergiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
