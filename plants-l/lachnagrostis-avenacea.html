Lachnagrostis avenacea

Lachnagrostis avenacea, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along riverbanks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to colonize new areas effectively.

Lachnagrostis avenacea is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide forage for livestock.
