Laburnum anagyroides f. bullatum

Laburnum anagyroides f. bullatum, commonly known as the weeping laburnum, is a decorative cultivar of the golden chain tree.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of bright yellow flowers that hang from long, drooping branches. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is often used as a ornamental tree or shrub due to its striking visual appeal.
