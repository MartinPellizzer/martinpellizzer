Lygistum congestum

Lygistum congestum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red, inflated calyces that resemble small lanterns. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Lygistum congestum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
