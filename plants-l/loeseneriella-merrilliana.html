Loeseneriella merrilliana

Loeseneriella merrilliana, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in montane forests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and air.

Loeseneriella merrilliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a subject of interest in botanical research.
