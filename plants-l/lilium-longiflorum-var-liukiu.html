Lilium longiflorum var. liukiu

Lilium longiflorum var. liukiu, commonly known as the Liukiu lily, is a cultivar of the Asiatic lily species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink, with a subtle fragrance. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces sturdy, upright stems. Native to Japan, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

This variety is known for its reliability in blooming and its adaptability to a variety of growing conditions.
