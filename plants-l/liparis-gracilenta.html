Liparis gracilenta

Liparis gracilenta, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Liparis gracilenta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
