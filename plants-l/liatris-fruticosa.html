Liatris fruticosa

Liatris fruticosa, commonly known as purple blazing star, is a native North American plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and woodland edges. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Liatris fruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
