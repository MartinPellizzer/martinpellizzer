Lippia Macrostachya

Lippia macrostachya is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. It belongs to the mint family, known for its aromatic properties and medicinal uses. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered white or light purple flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in open woodlands and disturbed soils. Due to its unique appearance and potential health benefits, it has gained attention among botanists and herbalists alike.
