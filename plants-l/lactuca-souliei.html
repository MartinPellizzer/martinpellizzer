Lactuca souliei

Lactuca souliei, commonly known as the Chinese lettuce or mountain lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette of broad, fleshy leaves that are often used in traditional Chinese cuisine. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are typically harvested for their crisp, mild flavor.

Lactuca souliei is also valued for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses attributed to its potential anti-inflammatory effects.
