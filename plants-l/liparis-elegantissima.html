Liparis elegantissima

Liparis elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are delicate, with white or pale pink petals and sepals, and a distinctive yellowish lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate moisture and light conditions.
