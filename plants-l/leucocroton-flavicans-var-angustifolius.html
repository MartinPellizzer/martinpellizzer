Leucocroton flavicans var. angustifolius

Leucocroton flavicans var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf leucocroton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings for its unique foliage and floral display.
