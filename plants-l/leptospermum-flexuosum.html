Leptospermum flexuosum

Leptospermum flexuosum, commonly known as the slender tea tree, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Tasmania.

It is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pinkish flowers. The plant is part of the Myrtaceae family, which includes other well-known species like eucalyptus and tea tree. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry forest regions.

The plant has aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
