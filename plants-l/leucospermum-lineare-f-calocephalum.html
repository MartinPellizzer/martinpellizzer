Leucospermum lineare f. calocephalum

Leucospermum lineare f. calocephalum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star flower, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its linear, narrow leaves and produces bright yellow to orange flowers in clusters. The plant is adapted to arid environments, with its succulent structure allowing it to store water. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The foliage and flowers are often used in traditional medicine and horticulture.
