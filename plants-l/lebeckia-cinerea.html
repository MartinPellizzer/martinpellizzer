Lebeckia cinerea

Lebeckia cinerea, commonly known as the silver lebeckia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silvery-gray foliage due to dense, fine hairs covering its leaves. It produces daisy-like flowers with pale yellow centers and white to pale blue petals.

Lebeckia cinerea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive, low-maintenance appearance.
