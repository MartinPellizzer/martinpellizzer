Lastrea brunoniana

Lastrea brunoniana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-like growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant features thick, velvety leaves that are often a deep green color. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom in clusters.

Lastrea brunoniana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
