Lasiandra petiolata

Lasiandra petiolata, commonly known as the slender leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. It has narrow, linear leaves that are usually arranged in a basal rosette.
