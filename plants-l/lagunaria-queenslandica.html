Lagunaria queenslandica

Lagunaria queenslandica, commonly known as the Queensland lagoon tree, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palm-like appearance with long, slender leaves. The plant produces small, white or cream-colored flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the cooler months. It thrives in coastal and wetland environments, demonstrating a high tolerance for saline conditions.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various native wildlife species.
