Litsea albescens

Litsea albescens, commonly known as the white litsea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small drupes that mature to a reddish color.

Litsea albescens is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental beauty and potential medicinal properties.
