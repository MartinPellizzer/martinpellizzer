Lycaste hennisiana

Lycaste hennisiana, commonly known as the Hennis' lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen pseudobulbs and clustered, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Lycaste hennisiana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
