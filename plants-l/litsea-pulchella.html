Litsea pulchella

Litsea pulchella, commonly known as the fragrant leaf tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Vietnam, Thailand, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruits are small, round drupes that turn from green to red as they mature.
