Ligustrum suspensum

Ligustrum suspensum, commonly known as the hanging privet, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its opposite, simple leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by black, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ligustrum suspensum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
