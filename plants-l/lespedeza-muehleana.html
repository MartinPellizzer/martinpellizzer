Lespedeza muehleana

Lespedeza muehleana, commonly known as Chinese lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas. The plant produces small, pink to reddish-purple flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, trifoliate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Lespedeza muehleana is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support pollinators.
