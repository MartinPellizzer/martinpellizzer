Lessertia cryptantha

Lessertia cryptantha, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
