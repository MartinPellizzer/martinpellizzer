Laelia × caloglossa

Laelia × caloglossa, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Caloglossa species.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit and thick, fleshy pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Laelia × caloglossa is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its resilience and ornamental value.
