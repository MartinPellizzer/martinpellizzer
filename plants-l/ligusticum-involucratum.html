Ligusticum involucratum

Ligusticum involucratum, commonly known as the clustered lovage, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its aromatic, compound leaves. Ligusticum involucratum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
