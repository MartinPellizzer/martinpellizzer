Lathyrus ovalifolius

Lathyrus ovalifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved pea, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and produces striking violet to purple pea-like flowers. The plant has a climbing habit, often twining around supports, and its stems are hairy. Its small, pod-like fruits contain several elliptical seeds.

Lathyrus ovalifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in ecological gardens.
