Leontodon ehrenbergii

Leontodon ehrenbergii, commonly known as the yellow hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and rough, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and rocky soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers consist of a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Leontodon ehrenbergii is often found in open habitats and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens.
