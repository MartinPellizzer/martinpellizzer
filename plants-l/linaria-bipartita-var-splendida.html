Linaria bipartita var. splendida

Linaria bipartita var. splendida, commonly known as the Splendour Toadflax, is a striking annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features vibrant, spurred flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom from late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
