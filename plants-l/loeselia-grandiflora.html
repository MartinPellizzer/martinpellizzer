Loeselia grandiflora

Loeselia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered loeselia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and sandy soils, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Loeselia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
