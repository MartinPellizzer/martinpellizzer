Lauro-cerasus otinii

Lauro-cerasus otinii, commonly known as the cherry laurel, is a species of flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Prunus genus and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are edible but often considered bitter. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal.

Lauro-cerasus otinii is also valued for its ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
