Loasa Urbanii

Urban's loasa, scientifically known as Loasa urbanii, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America. It is characterized by its vibrant purple flowers and distinctive leaf structure, making it a standout species in its ecosystem. Despite its beauty, it remains largely unknown to the general public due to its limited distribution. This plant thrives in specific microhabitats, often found in shaded, moist environments. Its conservation status highlights the importance of preserving biodiversity in urban and natural landscapes alike.
