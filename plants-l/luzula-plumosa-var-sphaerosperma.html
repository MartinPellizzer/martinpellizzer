Luzula plumosa var. sphaerosperma

Luzula plumosa var. sphaerosperma, commonly known as the spherical spike sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescence that bears small, spike-like clusters of flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow, grass-like, and arranged in a tight rosette.

The variety sphaerosperma is distinguished by its spherical, seed-bearing structures, which contribute to its unique botanical appearance.
