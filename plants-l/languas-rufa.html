Languas rufa

Languas rufa, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and a distinctive, fragrant aroma. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are relatively small but clustered in dense inflorescences, making it visually appealing.

Languas rufa is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
