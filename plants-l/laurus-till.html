Laurus till

Laurus till, commonly known as the common laurel.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Lauraceae family, characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its leaves are widely used in cooking and traditional medicine due to their strong scent and potential health benefits.

The fruit, a small drupe, is also utilized in various culinary applications.
