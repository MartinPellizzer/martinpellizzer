Leonardendron

Leonardendron, commonly known as the leopard tree, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, large, leathery leaves that resemble the spots of a leopard. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Due to its distinctive appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
