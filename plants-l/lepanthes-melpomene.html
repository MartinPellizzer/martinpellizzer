Lepanthes melpomene

Lepanthes melpomene, commonly known as the Crimson Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically display shades of red and orange. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Lepanthes melpomene thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
