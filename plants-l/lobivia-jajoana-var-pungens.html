Lobivia jajoana var. pungens

Lobivia jajoana var. pungens, commonly known as the Pungent Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features distinctive, spiny stems and thick, fleshy leaves adapted for water storage. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the growing season, adding visual appeal. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xerophytic gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
