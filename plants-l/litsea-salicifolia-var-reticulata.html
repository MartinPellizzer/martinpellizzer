Litsea salicifolia var. reticulata

Litsea salicifolia var. reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined litsea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate, ovate leaves with a distinct net-like venation pattern. Its small, white flowers are typically clustered in terminal panicles, and it produces small, dark blue berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage and seasonal interest.
