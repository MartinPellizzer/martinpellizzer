Liparis taronensis

Liparis taronensis, commonly known as the Taron orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, pale green or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky areas. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and specific ecological requirements.

Liparis taronensis is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique adaptations to its habitat.
