Lantana × weberbaueri

Lantana × weberbaueri, commonly known as the yellow lantana, is a hybrid shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow, orange, or red. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are lance-shaped to ovate. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Lantana × weberbaueri is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
