Laelia geraensis

Laelia geraensis, commonly known as the Geraens laelia, is a species of orchid native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, often in spring or early summer. Its pseudobulbs are robust and help it survive in its arid environment.

Laelia geraensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to cultivation.
