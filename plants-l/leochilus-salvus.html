Leochilus salvus

Leochilus salvus, commonly known as the California milkweed.

It is a perennial herb native to the western United States, particularly California. The plant features slender, branching stems that grow up to 1.5 meters in height. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.
