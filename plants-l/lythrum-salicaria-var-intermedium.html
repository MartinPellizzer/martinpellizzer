Lythrum salicaria var. intermedium

Lythrum salicaria var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate rosebay.

This variant of the common rosebay is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features tall spikes of pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom from late summer to early autumn. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows, marshes, and along stream banks.
