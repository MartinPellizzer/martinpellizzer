Lantana viburnoides

Lantana viburnoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf lantana, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow, pink, and red, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is known for its strong fragrance and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Lantana viburnoides is also adapted to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions, making it a versatile plant in the landscape.
