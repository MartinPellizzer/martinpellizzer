Lamium hakkiarense

Lamium hakkiarense, commonly known as the Hakkiar Valley deadnettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink and purple. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
