Leptochloa mucronata

Leptochloa mucronata, commonly known as the narrowleaf grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

Leptochloa mucronata is adapted to arid conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its drought tolerance and soil stabilization abilities.
