Lathyrus niger var. heterophyllus

Lathyrus niger var. heterophyllus, commonly known as black vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, twining stems and produces small, dark purple to black flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used for forage or soil stabilization.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
