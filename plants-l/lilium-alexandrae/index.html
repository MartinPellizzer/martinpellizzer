Lilium Alexandrae

Lilium alexandrae is a rare and beautiful species of lily native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. Known for its striking white petals and a faint pink tinge, this flower is both visually captivating and botanically unique. It thrives in high altitude environments, often found in rocky soils and alpine meadows. Due to its limited distribution and habitat fragility, it is considered an endangered species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this delicate and enchanting plant for future generations.
