Leucas thymoides

Leucas thymoides, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its aromatic, lemon-scented leaves and small white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is often used in culinary and aromatic applications due to its distinctive fragrance.

Leucas thymoides is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
