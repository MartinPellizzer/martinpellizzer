Lonicera × plantierensis

Lonicera × plantierensis, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and adaptability contribute to its widespread cultivation in temperate regions.
