Licuala bidentata var. elegantissima

Licuala bidentata var. elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant fan palm, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 2 meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic visual presence. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-draining, fertile soil. It is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both landscaping and indoor decoration.
