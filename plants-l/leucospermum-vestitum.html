Leucospermum vestitum

Leucospermum vestitum, commonly known as the yellow star bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem and a preference for well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to coastal environments.
