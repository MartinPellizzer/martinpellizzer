Lonchocarpus guatemalensis

Lonchocarpus guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan legume, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its striking yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
