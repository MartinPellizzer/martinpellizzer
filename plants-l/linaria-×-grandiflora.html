Linaria × grandiflora

Linaria × grandiflora, commonly known as the hybrid everlasting or garden everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Linaria vulgaris and Linaria dalmatica, combining traits from both parent species. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. It is typically herbaceous, with upright, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Linaria × grandiflora is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
