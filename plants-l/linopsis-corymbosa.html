Linopsis corymbosa

Linopsis corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Linopsis corymbosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
