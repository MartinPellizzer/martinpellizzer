Lobelia filiformis var. luzoniensis

Lobelia filiformis var. luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon slender lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its slender, herbaceous stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in montane forests. It has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its delicate appearance.
