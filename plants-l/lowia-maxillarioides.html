Lowia maxillarioides

Lowia maxillarioides, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
