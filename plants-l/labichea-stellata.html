Labichea stellata

Labichea stellata, commonly known as the star-like labichea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it blooms in the spring.

Labichea stellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual beauty.
