Lobostemon lucidus

Lobostemon lucidus, commonly known as the glossy lobostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a shiny surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in humid, warm climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Lobostemon lucidus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
