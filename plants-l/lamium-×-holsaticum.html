Lamium × holsaticum

Lamium × holsaticum, commonly known as the purple deadnettle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple underside and clusters of pale purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and gardens. It is a perennial herb that spreads through stolons, forming dense mats.

Its flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to ecological landscapes.
