Liparis damingshanensis

Liparis damingshanensis, commonly known as the Daming Mountain orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments at high altitudes. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to anchor in rocky or soil substrates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
