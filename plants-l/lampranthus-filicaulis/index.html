Lampranthus Filicaulis

Lampranthus filicaulis is a strikingly beautiful succulent native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant, showy flowers, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. This plant is popular among gardeners for its ease of care and long-lasting blooms. Its common name, "Fireplant," reflects the fiery red hues of its flowers. It's a great choice for rock gardens or containers due to its compact size and drought tolerance.
