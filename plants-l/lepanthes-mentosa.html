Lepanthes mentosa

Lepanthes mentosa, commonly known as the Mentosa Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as white, yellow, and green. The plant belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is known for its epiphytic growth habit, often growing on trees in humid tropical environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it thrive in shaded, moist conditions.

Lepanthes mentosa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
