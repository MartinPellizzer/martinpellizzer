Liatris densispicata

Liatris densispicata, commonly known as the densehead blazingstar, is a native North American plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and dense, spike-like clusters of small, lavender to pinkish-purple flowers. The plant typically grows in prairies, meadows, and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

Liatris densispicata is known for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
