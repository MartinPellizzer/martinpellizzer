Loeflingia

Loeflingia, commonly known as the false dandelion, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are native to southern Africa. The plants typically have basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. They produce yellow composite flowers that resemble those of true dandelions.
