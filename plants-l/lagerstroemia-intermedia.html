Lagerstroemia intermedia

Lagerstroemia intermedia, commonly known as the Crape Myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, long-lasting inflorescences that bloom in summer, often in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that turn yellow in autumn before dropping. Its bark is smooth and mottled, becoming fissured with age.

Lagerstroemia intermedia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
