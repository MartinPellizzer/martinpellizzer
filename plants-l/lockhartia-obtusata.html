Lockhartia obtusata

Lockhartia obtusata, commonly known as the orchid with the broad, rounded leaves.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Lockhartia obtusata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
