Lactuca lactucarii

Lactuca lactucarii, commonly known as the prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant is native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields and along roadsides.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its wide distribution.
