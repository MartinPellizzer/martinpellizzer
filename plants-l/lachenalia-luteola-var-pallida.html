Lachenalia luteola var. pallida

Lachenalia luteola var. pallida, commonly known as the pale yellow lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and clustered, tubular flowers. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
