Lindmania steyermarkii

Lindmania steyermarkii, commonly known as the Brazilian pineapple plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its rosette of grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects water. It produces small, white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local communities.
