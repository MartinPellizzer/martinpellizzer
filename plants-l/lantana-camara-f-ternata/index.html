Lantana Camara F. Ternata

Variegated lantana, scientifically known as Lantana camara f. ternata, is a strikingly ornamental plant prized for its vibrant, multicolored foliage. Native to the tropical regions of the Americas, it has become widely cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a unique blend of green, white, and yellow, creating a visually appealing contrast. It is often used in gardens and landscapes to add color and texture to various environments. Despite its beauty, it is important to note that it can be invasive in certain regions, requiring careful management.
