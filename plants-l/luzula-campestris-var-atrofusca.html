Luzula campestris var. atrofusca

Luzula campestris var. atrofusca, commonly known as the dark-leaved wood rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features slender, upright stems that bear dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences. The leaves are typically dark green to almost black in color, which gives the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

This variety is often found in wet meadows and is valued for its ornamental qualities in garden settings.
