Linaria concolor f. rubioides

Linaria concolor f. rubioides, commonly known as the red-flowered sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the plantain family, Plantaginaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or arctic environments.

This variety is noted for its striking color and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
