Litsea japonica

Litsea japonica, commonly known as Japanese litsea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Litsea japonica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
