Lauradia ericoides

Lauradia ericoides, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. Its leaves are typically small, leathery, and arranged in whorls around the stems.

Lauradia ericoides is well adapted to poor, acidic soils and can thrive in coastal or sandy environments.
