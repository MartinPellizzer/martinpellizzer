Lepidium integrifolium

Lepidium integrifolium, commonly known as the smooth wallflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has small, smooth, oval leaves. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, round pods containing seeds.

Lepidium integrifolium is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
