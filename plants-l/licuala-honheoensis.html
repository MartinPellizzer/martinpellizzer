Licuala honheoensis

Licuala honheoensis, commonly known as the Honheo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to several meters in diameter. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The fruit of Licuala honheoensis is a bright red drupe that contains a single seed, making it an important food source for various wildlife species.
