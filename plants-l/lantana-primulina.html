Lantana primulina

Lantana primulina, commonly known as the common lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow, orange, or red, depending on the variety. Lantana primulina is known for its strong fragrance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
