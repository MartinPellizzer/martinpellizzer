Lamium stenosiphon

Lamium stenosiphon, commonly known as narrow-mouthed deadnettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple. Its flowers are arranged in whorled clusters, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.

Lamium stenosiphon is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can also serve as a food source for certain pollinators.
