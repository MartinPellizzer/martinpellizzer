Lastrea pulchra

Lastrea pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in a powdery white coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
