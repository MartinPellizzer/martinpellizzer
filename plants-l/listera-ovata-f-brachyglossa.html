Listera ovata f. brachyglossa

Listera ovata f. brachyglossa, commonly known as the short-lipped woodland star, is a rare and distinctive variant of the woodland star species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and delicate white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its foliage is shorter and more compact compared to the typical form, giving it a unique appearance.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements.
