Leptochilus morsei

Leptochilus morsei, commonly known as the Morsei's rock cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant typically found in rocky or alpine environments. The plant has slender, hairy stems and small, green, ovate leaves. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, arranged in racemes, and are typical of the mustard family.

It is often found in crevices and on exposed rock faces, where it thrives in well-drained, nutrient-poor soils.
