Lobivia jajoana var. elegans

Lobivia jajoana var. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Lobivia, is a compact, short-stemmed cacti native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated body with a smooth, greenish-gray skin that develops a whitish coating over time. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise subdued appearance. It has shallow roots and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and unique form, it is a prized addition to many succulent collections.
