Leonotis leonitis

Leonotis leonitis, commonly known as lion's tail, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that resemble a lion's tail, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. It typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters.

Leonotis leonitis is known for its showy blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
