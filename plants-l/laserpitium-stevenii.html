Laserpitium stevenii

Laserpitium stevenii, commonly known as Steven's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
