Leonotis ocymifolia var. ocymifolia

Leonotis ocymifolia var. ocymifolia, commonly known as the red-hot poker or fireplant, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features tall, spiky inflorescences that are typically bright orange or red, giving it its common name. The plant has lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its showy flowers attract pollinators like bees and birds, contributing to its ecological value.
