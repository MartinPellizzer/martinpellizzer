Ligularia oligantha

Ligularia oligantha, commonly known as the slender ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green underside, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers in late summer. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas along streams or in rocky soils. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in partial shade.

Ligularia oligantha is also noted for its ecological importance, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
