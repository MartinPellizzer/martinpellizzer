Leiothrix polystemma var. robusta

Leiothrix polystemma var. robusta, commonly known as the robust leiothrix, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces distinctive, elongated fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in tropical and subtropical regions.
