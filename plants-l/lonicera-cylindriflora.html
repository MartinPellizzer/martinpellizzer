Lonicera cylindriflora

Lonicera cylindriflora, commonly known as the cylindrical honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are yellowish-green with orange streaks, blooming in early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer, attracting birds and other wildlife.

Lonicera cylindriflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
