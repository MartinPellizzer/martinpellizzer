Loxothysanus sinuatus

Loxothysanus sinuatus, commonly known as the sinuous lichen, is a member of the Lecanoraceae family.

It is a foliose lichen that forms a thallus with a distinctive, undulating, sinuous pattern. The plant exhibits a complex symbiotic relationship between a fungal component and green algae or cyanobacteria. It typically grows on bark, stones, or soil in temperate and boreal regions.

Its morphology and growth habits make it an important indicator species for environmental conditions.
