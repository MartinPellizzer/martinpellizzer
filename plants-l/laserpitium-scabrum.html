Laserpitium scabrum

Laserpitium scabrum, commonly known as rough hawkbit, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a rough texture.
