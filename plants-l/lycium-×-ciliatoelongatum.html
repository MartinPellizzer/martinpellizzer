Lycium × ciliatoelongatum

Lycium × ciliatoelongatum, commonly known as the Chinese boxthorn, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and showy yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Lycium × ciliatoelongatum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
