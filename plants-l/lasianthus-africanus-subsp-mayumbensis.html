Lasianthus africanus subsp. mayumbensis

Lasianthus africanus subsp.

mayumbensis, commonly known as the Mayumbensis lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Central and West Africa. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
