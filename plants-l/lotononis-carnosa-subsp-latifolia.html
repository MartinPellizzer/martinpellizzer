Lotononis carnosa subsp. latifolia

Lotononis carnosa subsp.

latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved lotononis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features slender, erect stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-gray underside.

The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and striking foliage.
