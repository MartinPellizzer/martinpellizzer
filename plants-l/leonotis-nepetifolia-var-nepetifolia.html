Leonotis nepetifolia var. nepetifolia

Leonotis nepetifolia var. nepetifolia, commonly known as the blanket flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features a bushy growth habit with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.

The flowers are rich in nectar and attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and hummingbirds.
