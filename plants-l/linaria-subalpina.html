Linaria subalpina

Linaria subalpina, commonly known as the alpine toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky soils at high elevations. Linaria subalpina is adapted to cold climates and is often found in areas with poor soil conditions.
