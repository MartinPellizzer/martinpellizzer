Leucas tsavoensis var. tsavoensis

Leucas tsavoensis var. tsavoensis, commonly known as the Tsavo leucas, is a flowering plant native to East Africa, particularly found in Kenya and Tanzania.

It is a member of the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit with woody stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often blooming in the dry season. The leaves are typically trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.
