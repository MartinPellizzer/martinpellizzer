Lyonsia vaccinioides

Lyonsia vaccinioides, commonly known as the red-flowered lyonsia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant features small, leathery leaves and produces clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
