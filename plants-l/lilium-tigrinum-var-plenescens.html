Lilium tigrinum var. plenescens

Lilium tigrinum var. plenescens, commonly known as the Chinese tiger lily, is a striking perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically orange or red with dark markings, resembling a tiger's stripes. The plant produces a tall, sturdy stem with narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its bulbs are edible when young and have a mild, sweet flavor, making it both ornamental and culinary.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in moist, temperate climates.
