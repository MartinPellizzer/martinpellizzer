Lewisia alba

Lewisia alba, commonly known as the white lewisia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Montiaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces showy, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
