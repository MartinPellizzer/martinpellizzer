Lasianthus ciliatus var. rostratus

Lasianthus ciliatus var. rostratus, commonly known as the rostratus lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It has opposite, ovate leaves with cilia along the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
