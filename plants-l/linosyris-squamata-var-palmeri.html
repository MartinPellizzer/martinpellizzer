Linosyris squamata var. palmeri

Linosyris squamata var. palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and hairy, grayish-green foliage. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as sandy soils and rocky slopes. It has adapted to survive in drought conditions with its deep root system and water-conserving features.

This variety is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
