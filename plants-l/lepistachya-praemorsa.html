Lepistachya praemorsa

Lepistachya praemorsa, commonly known as the African feather grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, silver-gray blades that are often used in ornamental landscaping due to their elegant appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the grass family. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Lepistachya praemorsa is valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
