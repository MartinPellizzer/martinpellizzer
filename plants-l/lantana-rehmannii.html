Lantana rehmannii

Lantana rehmannii, commonly known as the yellow lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, which bloom throughout the year in many climates. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Lantana rehmannii is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its vibrant flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
