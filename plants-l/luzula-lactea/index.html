Luzula Lactea

Luzula lactea is a unique plant known for its striking white flowers and delicate foliage. Native to high-altitude regions, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This species belongs to the grass family and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its name, "lactea," refers to its milky white appearance, which is particularly noticeable in its inflorescence. Despite its beauty, Luzula lactea is a rare and elusive plant that requires specific growing conditions to flourish.
