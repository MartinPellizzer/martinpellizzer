Limodorum tancarvilleae

Limodorum tancarvilleae, commonly known as the Tancarville's lily, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the temperate regions of Europe, particularly in France and Italy. The plant is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
