Lonicera buchananii

Lonicera buchananii, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange-red, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

Lonicera buchananii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
