Linaria bourgaei

Linaria bourgaei, commonly known as the Cape Hedgehog.

This flowering plant is native to South Africa and belongs to the Plantaginaceae family. It is characterized by its spiny, branched stems and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
