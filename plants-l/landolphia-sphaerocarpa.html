Landolphia sphaerocarpa

Landolphia sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the giant African bubble tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant produces large, spherical, orange fruits that contain numerous seeds. It has a thick, woody stem and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are typically large and heart-shaped, with a smooth, glossy surface.
