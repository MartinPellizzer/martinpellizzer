Leptosiphon floribundus subsp. hallii

Leptosiphon floribundus subsp.

hallii, commonly known as the Hall's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It is a member of the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers.
