Leptostachya heterophylla

Leptostachya heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaved bamboo, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and varying leaf shapes, which can range from narrow to broad. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually alternate and can exhibit different morphological features depending on environmental conditions.

Leptostachya heterophylla is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid climates.
