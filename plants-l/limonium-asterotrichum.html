Limonium asterotrichum

Limonium asterotrichum, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and its profuse display of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Limonium asterotrichum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in well-drained, sunny conditions.
