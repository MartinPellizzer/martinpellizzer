Linochilus huertasii

Linochilus huertasii, commonly known as the Huertasii orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on rocky substrates or in forest understories. Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a short, compact stem.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
