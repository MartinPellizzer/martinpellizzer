Lysimachia orbicularis

Lysimachia orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf chickweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to August. The plant has rounded, succulent leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Lysimachia orbicularis is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and modest floral display.
