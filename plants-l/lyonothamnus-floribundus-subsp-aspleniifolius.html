Lyonothamnus floribundus subsp. aspleniifolius

Lyonothamnus floribundus subsp.

aspleniifolius, commonly known as the silver felt bush, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that feels velvety to the touch.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscaped areas.
