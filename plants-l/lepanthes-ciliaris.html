Lepanthes ciliaris

Lepanthes ciliaris, commonly known as the Ciliated Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has a short, erect stem with a few leaves and produces small, clustered blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing in the understory of tropical forests.

Lepanthes ciliaris is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
