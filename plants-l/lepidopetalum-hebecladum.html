Lepidopetalum hebecladum

Lepidopetalum hebecladum, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, arching stems and heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Lepidopetalum hebecladum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for native pollinators.
