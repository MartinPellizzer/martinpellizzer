Ligularia lancifera

Ligularia lancifera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with prominent veins and a serrated margin. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, upright stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
