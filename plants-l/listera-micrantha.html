Listera micrantha

Listera micrantha, commonly known as the lesser springbeauty, is a small, herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant produces delicate, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on slender stems. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often covered in fine, white hairs.

Listera micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
