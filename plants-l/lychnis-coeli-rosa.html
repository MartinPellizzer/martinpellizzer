Lychnis coeli-rosa

Lychnis coeli-rosa, commonly known as the sky-blue aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy, violet-blue to pink flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with toothed edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Its botanical name reflects its celestial beauty, with "coeli-rosa" meaning "sky rose.".
