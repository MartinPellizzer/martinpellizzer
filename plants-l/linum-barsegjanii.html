Linum barsegjanii

Linum barsegjanii, commonly known as the Armenian flax, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flax and blue flowers. The plant produces delicate blue or white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, typically with a slightly hairy texture.

Linum barsegjanii is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
