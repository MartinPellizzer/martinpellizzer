Limonium siculum

Limonium siculum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has silvery-gray foliage and is often found growing in coastal areas and sandy soils. Limonium siculum is a member of the Plumbagaceae family and is known for its drought tolerance.
