Lastrea neglecta

Lastrea neglecta, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. Lastrea neglecta thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments.
