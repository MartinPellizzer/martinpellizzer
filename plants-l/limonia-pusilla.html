Limonia pusilla

Limonia pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf lemon tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, edible fruits resembling lemons. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of citrus-like fruit.

Limonia pusilla is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for container gardening.
