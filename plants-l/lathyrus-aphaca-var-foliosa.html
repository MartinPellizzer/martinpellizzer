Lathyrus aphaca var. foliosa

Lathyrus aphaca var. foliosa, commonly known as the broadleaf sweet pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, pink to purple flowers. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure. It produces elongated, cylindrical seed pods that contain small, brown seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and foliage.
