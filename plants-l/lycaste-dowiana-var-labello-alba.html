Lycaste dowiana var. labello-alba

Lycaste dowiana var. labello-alba, commonly known as the White Labello Lycaste, is a hybrid orchid species native to the humid forests of Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a pale yellow lip, which are highly fragrant and attract various pollinators. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks and branches in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright indirect light, making it a popular choice for tropical greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its beauty and unique floral structure.
