Lampranthus dregeanus

Lampranthus dregeanus, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like birds and insects. The plant has fleshy, cylindrical leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance make it a standout in both cultivated and natural landscapes.
