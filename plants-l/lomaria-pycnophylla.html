Lomaria pycnophylla

Lomaria pycnophylla, commonly known as the dense-leaf lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
