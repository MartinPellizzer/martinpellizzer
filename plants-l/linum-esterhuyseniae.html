Linum esterhuyseniae

Linum esterhuyseniae, commonly known as the Esterhuysenia flax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Linaceae family, which includes other flax species. The plant is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers and linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
