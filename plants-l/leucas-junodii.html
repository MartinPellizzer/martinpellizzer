Leucas junodii

Leucas junodii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved leucas, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pale blue or lilac in color, arranged in whorled clusters along the stem.

Leucas junodii is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
