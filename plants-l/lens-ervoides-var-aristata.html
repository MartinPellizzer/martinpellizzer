Lens ervoides var. aristata

Lens ervoides var. aristata, commonly known as the slender pea or mountain pea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the pods contain several small, edible seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its nutritional value and is also used in traditional medicine.
