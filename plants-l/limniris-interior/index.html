Limniris Interior

Limniris interior is a rare aquatic plant found in shallow freshwater environments. It is characterized by its delicate, ribbon-like leaves that float just beneath the water's surface. This plant plays a vital role in maintaining water quality by absorbing excess nutrients. Its presence is often an indicator of a healthy ecosystem. Due to habitat loss, Limniris interior is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
