Lasersisia seretii

Lasersisia seretii, commonly known as the desert aster, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant has silver-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It produces showy yellow or white flowers that bloom in the spring and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Lasersisia seretii is well adapted to drought conditions and is often found in rocky soils and open plains.
