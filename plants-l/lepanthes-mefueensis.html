Lepanthes mefueensis

Lepanthes mefueensis, commonly known as the Mefue orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features delicate, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers. Its flowers are usually arranged in a loose raceme and are characterized by their intricate, spurred petals.

Lepanthes mefueensis is part of the orchid family Orchidaceae and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in its native habitat.
