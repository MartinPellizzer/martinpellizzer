Lilium-convallium

Lilium convallium, commonly known as the European lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces a tall, upright stem. It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often growing in woodland areas.

Lilium convallium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
