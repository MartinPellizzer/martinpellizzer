Lophanthus virescens

Lophanthus virescens, commonly known as the green lophanthus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually a vibrant green, and its flowers are small, clustered, and often have a yellowish or greenish hue. Lophanthus virescens is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.
