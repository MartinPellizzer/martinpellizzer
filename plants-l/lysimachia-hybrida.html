Lysimachia hybrida

Lysimachia hybrida, commonly known as the golden thread, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, creeping stems and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Lysimachia hybrida thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
