Liparis gracilipes

Liparis gracilipes, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, upright stem and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soils.

Liparis gracilipes is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
