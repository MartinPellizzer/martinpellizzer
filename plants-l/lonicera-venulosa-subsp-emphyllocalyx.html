Lonicera venulosa subsp. emphyllocalyx

Lonicera venulosa subsp.

emphyllocalyx, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia. It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape.

The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive red or orange calyx. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

The subspecies is closely related to other honeysuckle species and shares similar botanical characteristics.
