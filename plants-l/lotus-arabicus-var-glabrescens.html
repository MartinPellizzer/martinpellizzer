Lotus arabicus var. glabrescens

Lotus arabicus var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth lotus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate pink to purple flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is an herbaceous perennial that produces edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisine.

The variety glabrescens is distinguished by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves compared to other varieties of Lotus arabicus.
