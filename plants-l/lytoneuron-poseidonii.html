Lytoneuron poseidonii

Lytoneuron poseidonii, commonly known as the sea hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found growing along rocky shores and dunes. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to retain water in arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
