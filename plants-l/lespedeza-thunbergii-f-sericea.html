Lespedeza thunbergii f. sericea

Lespedeza thunbergii f. sericea, commonly known as silverleaf lespedeza, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fluffy, silver-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink to reddish-purple flowers in late summer, which are arranged in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens and erosion control.

Its delicate appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate regions.
