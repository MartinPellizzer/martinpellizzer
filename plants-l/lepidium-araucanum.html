Lepidium araucanum

Lepidium araucanum, commonly known as the Andean rocket, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are pinnately divided with a peppery aroma. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its edible leaves and seeds.
