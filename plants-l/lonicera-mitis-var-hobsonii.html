Lonicera mitis var. hobsonii

Lonicera mitis var. hobsonii, commonly known as the hobson's honeysuckle, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that remain throughout the year, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for native plant gardens.
