Lilium pulchrum

Lilium pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Asia, including China, Korea, and Japan. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a yellow or orange center. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Lilium pulchrum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
