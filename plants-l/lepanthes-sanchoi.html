Lepanthes sanchoi

Lepanthes sanchoi, commonly known as the Sancho's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with white petals and a yellow lip, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. Lepanthes sanchoi is known for its compact size and is often found in association with mosses and other epiphytic plants.
