Lycaste schilleriana var. alba

Lycaste schilleriana var. alba, commonly known as the White Schiller's Lycaste, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with intricate, waxy petals and a prominent, elongated lip. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, forming pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its foliage consists of broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its unique beauty and challenging cultivation requirements.
