Logania stenophylla

Logania stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved logania, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually narrow and lance-shaped. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
