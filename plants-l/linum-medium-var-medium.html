Linum medium var. medium

Linum medium var. medium, commonly known as the common flax.

It is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

The flowers are blue to purple in color, with five petals and a prominent central yellow stamen.
