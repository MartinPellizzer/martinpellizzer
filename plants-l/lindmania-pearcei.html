Lindmania pearcei

Lindmania pearcei, commonly known as the Peacock fern, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, feather-like foliage that resembles a peacock's tail. The plant features a thick, rhizomatous stem that supports its lush, compound leaves. Its leaves are typically bright green with a waxy texture and often have a slight sheen.

Lindmania pearcei thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.
