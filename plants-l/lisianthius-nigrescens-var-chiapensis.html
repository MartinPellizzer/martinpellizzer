Lisianthius nigrescens var. chiapensis

Lisianthius nigrescens var. chiapensis, commonly known as the Chiapan lisianthius, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within cloud forests. It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its delicate appearance.
