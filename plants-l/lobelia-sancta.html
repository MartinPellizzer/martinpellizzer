Lobelia sancta

Lobelia sancta, commonly known as sacred lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

Lobelia sancta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
