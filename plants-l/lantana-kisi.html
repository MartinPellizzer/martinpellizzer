Lantana kisi

Lantana kisi, commonly known as the yellow lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a common ornamental plant in gardens.

Lantana kisi is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
