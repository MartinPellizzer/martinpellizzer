Litsea whiteana

Litsea whiteana, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.
