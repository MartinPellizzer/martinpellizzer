Lavatera ramosissima

Lavatera ramosissima, commonly known as the common lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its showy flowers are usually pink or white, appearing in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Lavatera ramosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
