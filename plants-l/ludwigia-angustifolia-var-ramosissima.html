Ludwigia angustifolia var. ramosissima

Ludwigia angustifolia var. ramosissima, commonly known as the slender Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in aquatic gardens due to its vibrant color and adaptability.

Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in shallow water make it a popular choice for pond landscaping.
