Lycaste macrophylla subsp. plana

Lycaste macrophylla subsp.

plana, commonly known as the broad-leafed lycaste, is a species of orchid native to Mexico. It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture.

The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This subspecies is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
