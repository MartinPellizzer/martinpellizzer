Litsea alba

Litsea alba, commonly known as white litsea, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Litsea alba is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
