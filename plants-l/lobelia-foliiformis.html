Lobelia foliiformis

Lobelia foliiformis, commonly known as the leafy lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is a perennial herb that often forms dense mats, contributing to soil stabilization.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
