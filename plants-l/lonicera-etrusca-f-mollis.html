Lonicera etrusca f. mollis

Lonicera etrusca f. mollis, commonly known as the soft-fruited honeysuckle, is a compact shrub native to southern Europe.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are soft and hairy, giving it a distinctive texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable choice for ornamental gardens.
