Lastrea pilosissima

Lastrea pilosissima, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
