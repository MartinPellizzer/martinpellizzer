Lycaste barbifrons

Lycaste barbifrons, commonly known as the Barbifrons Cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear clusters of showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces large, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinct fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Lycaste barbifrons is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
