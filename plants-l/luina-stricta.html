Luina stricta

Luina stricta, commonly known as the slender lily, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, white to pale pink flowers with six petals, often arranged in clusters at the top of the stem. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Luina stricta thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas or near water bodies.
