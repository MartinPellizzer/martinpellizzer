Litsea salicifolia var. ellipsoidea

Litsea salicifolia var. ellipsoidea, commonly known as the elliptic-laurel, is a variant of the Chinese-laurel species native to East Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree characterized by its lanceolate, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, fragrant yellowish-green flowers followed by elliptical, woody fruits containing smooth, oval seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its aromatic foliage and adaptability contribute to its use in horticulture and traditional medicine.
