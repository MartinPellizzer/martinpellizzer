Ligusticum coniifolium

Ligusticum coniifolium, commonly known as Chinese lovage, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its aromatic, fern-like leaves. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
