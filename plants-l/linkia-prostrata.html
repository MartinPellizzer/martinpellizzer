Linkia prostrata

Linkia prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate linkia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, clustered in loose inflorescences.

Linkia prostrata is adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments, making it a common species in forest undergrowth.
