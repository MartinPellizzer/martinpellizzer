Lotus hamatus

Lotus hamatus, commonly known as the spurred lotus, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals, which give it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are rounded and floating, while its flowers are usually pink or purple in color.

Lotus hamatus is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in some regions.
