Litsea sibuyanensis

Litsea sibuyanensis, commonly known as the Sibuyan bay leaf, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically the island of Sibuyan. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dark reddish-brown drupe containing a single seed.

Litsea sibuyanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
