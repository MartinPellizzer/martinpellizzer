Lotus nudatus

Lotus nudatus, commonly known as the naked lotus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Nelumbonaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a distinctive pinkish-red center. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The lotus is also admired for its symbolic significance in various cultures.
