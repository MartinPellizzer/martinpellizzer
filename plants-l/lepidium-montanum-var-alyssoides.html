Lepidium montanum var. alyssoides

Lepidium montanum var. alyssoides, commonly known as the alpine cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, this plant thrives in alpine and subalpine habitats. It has a low-growing habit and is often found in rocky soils and meadows at high elevations.

The plant is known for its peppery taste and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
