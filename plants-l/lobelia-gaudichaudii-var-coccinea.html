Lobelia gaudichaudii var. coccinea

Lobelia gaudichaudii var. coccinea, commonly known as the red lobelia, is a strikingly colorful flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It features vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual impact to gardens. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shady garden beds.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
