Leptaleum longisiliquosum

Leptaleum longisiliquosum, commonly known as the long silique leptaleum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated silique fruits that contain multiple seeds. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and has small, alternate leaves with a serrated margin. It produces small white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.
