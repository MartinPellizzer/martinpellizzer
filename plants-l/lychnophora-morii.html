Lychnophora morii

Lychnophora morii, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a small, red drupe that contains a single seed.

Lychnophora morii is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas.
