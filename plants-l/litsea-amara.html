Litsea amara

Litsea amara, commonly known as bitter laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like India, China, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are small, berry-like structures that mature to a dark red color.

Litsea amara is often used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
