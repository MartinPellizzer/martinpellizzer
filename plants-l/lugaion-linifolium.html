Lugaion linifolium

Lugaion linifolium, commonly known as the slender leaf lugaion, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific alpine regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, providing structural support for its small, pale blue flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil, often found in rocky or mountainous terrain. Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a subtle floral presence to its habitat.
