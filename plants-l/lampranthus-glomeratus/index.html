Lampranthus Glomeratus

Lampranthus glomeratus is a strikingly beautiful plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. This species belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly called the ice plant family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its unique appearance and easy care requirements have made it a favorite among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
