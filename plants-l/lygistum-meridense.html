Lygistum meridense

Lygistum meridense, commonly known as the southern lygistum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Lygistum meridense is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
