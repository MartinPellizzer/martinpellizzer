Lactuca thibetica

Lactuca thibetica, commonly known as Tibetan lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas and is characterized by its rosette of dark green, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in rocky, alpine environments and is adapted to cold, harsh climates.

Lactuca thibetica is used in traditional medicine and is also cultivated for its edible leaves in some regions.
