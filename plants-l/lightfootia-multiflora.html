Lightfootia multiflora

Lightfootia multiflora, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences of violet flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Lightfootia multiflora is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
