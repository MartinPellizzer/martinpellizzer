Lasianthus thwaitesii var. nitidus

Lasianthus thwaitesii var. nitidus, commonly known as the glossy lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture.
