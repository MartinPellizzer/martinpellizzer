Lapeirousia graebneriana

Lapeirousia graebneriana, commonly known as the Graebner's rain lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant has a short, fleshy stem and produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, blooming in late winter to early spring.

Its unique appearance and seasonal flowering make it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.
