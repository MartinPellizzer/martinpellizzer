Linociera insignis

Linociera insignis, commonly known as the Mexican silverleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to Mexico and belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Linociera insignis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.
