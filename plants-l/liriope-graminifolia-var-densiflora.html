Liriope graminifolia var. densiflora

Liriope graminifolia var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered lilyturf, is a perennial evergreen grass-like plant native to East Asia.

It features grass-like leaves that are narrow and dark green, often forming dense clumps. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers in late summer, which are clustered in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form dense ground cover.

This variety is particularly noted for its compact growth and adaptability to various garden conditions.
