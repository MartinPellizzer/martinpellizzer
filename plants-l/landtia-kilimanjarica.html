Landtia kilimanjarica

Landtia kilimanjarica, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro lily, is a striking flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Tanzania.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in shades of yellow and orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
