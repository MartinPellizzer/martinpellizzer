Laelia gracilis

Laelia gracilis, commonly known as the slender laelia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and long, slender pseudobulbs. The plant produces delicate, pendulous flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive fragrance. Laelia gracilis thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with bright, indirect light.

It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
