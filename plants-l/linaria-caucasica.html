Linaria caucasica

Linaria caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its erect stems and clusters of yellow-orange flowers. Linaria caucasica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
