Lippia turnerifolia var. normalis

Lippia turnerifolia var. normalis, commonly known as the common lippia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in wetlands, riverbanks, and disturbed areas due to its adaptability.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
