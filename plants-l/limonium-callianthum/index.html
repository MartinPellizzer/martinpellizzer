Limonium Callianthum

Limonium callianthum is a striking flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is known for its clusters of small, colorful blooms that range from white to pink and purple. This species thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often found in maritime climates. Its delicate appearance contrasts with its hardy nature, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. The plant's resilience and beauty have made it a subject of both botanical study and horticultural admiration.
