Lysimachia lewisii

Lysimachia lewisii, commonly known as Lewis's chickweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features small, delicate white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has alternate, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in ornamental gardening for their delicate appearance.
