Leptogastrodia gracilis

Leptogastrodia gracilis, commonly known as the slender gasteria, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically the Western Cape province, where it grows in rocky, well-drained soils. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in terminal clusters during the summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
