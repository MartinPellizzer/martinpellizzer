Linanthus concinnus

Linanthus concinnus, commonly known as the elegant linanthus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the evening primrose family (Onagraceae) and is characterized by its delicate, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
