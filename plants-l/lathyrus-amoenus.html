Lathyrus amoenus

Lathyrus amoenus, commonly known as the sweet pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several angular seeds. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Lathyrus amoenus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower.
