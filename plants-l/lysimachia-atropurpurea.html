Lysimachia atropurpurea

Lysimachia atropurpurea, commonly known as purple false forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple and pink. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, ornamental ground cover.
