Lastrea polystichoides

Lastrea polystichoides, commonly known as the silver leaf sedum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, silver-gray leaves that help it conserve water in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It is well adapted to rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
