Limonium × coincyi

Limonium × coincyi, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads, which are surrounded by finely divided, silvery leaves. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and coastal grasslands. It belongs to the Plumbagaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
