Lyonia myrsinifolia

Lyonia myrsinifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf lyonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub with elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Lyonia myrsinifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat for various wildlife species.
