Laser carduchorum

Laser carduchorum, commonly known as the desert flame plant, is a unique succulent native to arid regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its root system is adapted to anchor it in loose, sandy soils and access deep water sources.

Due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
