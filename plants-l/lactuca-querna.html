Lactuca querna

Lactuca querna, commonly known as the lesser lettuce, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of broad, bluish-green leaves and small, yellow composite flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks.

It is a biennial or short-lived perennial, with the potential to spread through self-seeding.
