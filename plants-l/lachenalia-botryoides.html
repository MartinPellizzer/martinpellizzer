Lachenalia botryoides

Lachenalia botryoides, commonly known as the bulbous lachenalia, is a striking South African bulbous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves and its showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant produces a tall flowering stem with multiple blooms, often appearing in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Lachenalia botryoides is well-suited for rock gardens and containers, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
