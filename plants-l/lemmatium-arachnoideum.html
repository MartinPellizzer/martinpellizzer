Lemmatium arachnoideum

Lemmatium arachnoideum, commonly known as the spider lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It belongs to the family Parmeliaceae and is characterized by its intricate, web-like thallus structure. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship with algae and fungi, which contribute to its growth and survival. It is often found on tree bark, rocks, and soil in temperate regions.
