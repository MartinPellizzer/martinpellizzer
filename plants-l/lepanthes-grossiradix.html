Lepanthes grossiradix

Lepanthes grossiradix, commonly known as the broad-rooted slipper orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a short, slender rhizome and produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. Its leaves are usually ovate and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Lepanthes grossiradix is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique morphology and ornamental value.
