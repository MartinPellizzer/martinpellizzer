Lomaria falciformis

Lomaria falciformis, commonly known as the sickleleaf lomaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and low-maintenance addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
