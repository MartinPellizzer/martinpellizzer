Lobivia pugionacantha var. haemantha

Lobivia pugionacantha var. haemantha, commonly known as the Crimson Ghost Cactus, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a compact, rounded stem with thick, ribbed segments that store water, adapting to its harsh environment. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. Its leaves are typically small and spiny, providing additional protection from herbivores.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and resilience, making it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
