Lanugothamnus artemisioides

Lanugothamnus artemisioides, commonly known as the woolly thistle, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
