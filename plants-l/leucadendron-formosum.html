Leucadendron formosum

Leucadendron formosum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-white, fuzzy leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
