Leonurus dschungaricus

Leonurus dschungaricus, commonly known as the Chinese hedgehog, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and clusters of small, pinkish-purple flowers. It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often covered with fine hairs.
