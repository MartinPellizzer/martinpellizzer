Lantana camara f. sanguinea

Lantana camara f. sanguinea, commonly known as the red lantana, is a compact shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

However, it is considered an invasive species in many regions due to its aggressive spreading habit.
