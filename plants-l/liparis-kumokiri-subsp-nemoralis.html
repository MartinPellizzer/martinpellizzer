Liparis kumokiri subsp. nemoralis

Liparis kumokiri subsp.

nemoralis, commonly known as the Japanese twayblade, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae). It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, creeping rhizome and a single, lance-shaped leaf.

The plant produces a spike of small, pale green to white flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories across Japan.

Its botanical features reflect adaptations to its specific ecological niche, making it a notable species in the genus Liparis.
