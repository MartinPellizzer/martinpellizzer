Lespedeza maritima f. uekii

Lespedeza maritima f. uekii, commonly known as the coastal lespedeza, is a rare variant of the common lespedeza species.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to coastal regions of Japan. The plant typically features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to maritime climates with high salinity.
