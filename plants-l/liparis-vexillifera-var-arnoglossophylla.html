Liparis vexillifera var. arnoglossophylla

Liparis vexillifera var. arnoglossophylla, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
