Lespedeza schindleri

Lespedeza schindleri, commonly known as Schindler's lespedeza, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
