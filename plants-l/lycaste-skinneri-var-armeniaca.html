Lycaste skinneri var. armeniaca

Lycaste skinneri var. armeniaca, commonly known as the Armenian lycaste, is a rare and highly sought-after orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Armenia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces pendulous, showy blooms with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
