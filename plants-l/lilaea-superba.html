Lilaea superba

Lilaea superba, commonly known as the noble lily, is a striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Lilaea superba thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for alpine gardens.
