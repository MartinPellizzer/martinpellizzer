Locheria multiflora

Locheria multiflora, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, cylindrical fruit capsules. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Locheria multiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
