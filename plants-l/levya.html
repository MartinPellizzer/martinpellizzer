Levya

Levya, commonly known as the "spear lily," is a striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to three meters. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in summer, adding a dramatic visual impact to its surroundings. Levya has narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine by local communities.
