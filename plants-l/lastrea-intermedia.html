Lastrea intermedia

Lastrea intermedia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. It is often cultivated as a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
