Legousia hybrida

Legousia hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid legousia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically display vibrant colors such as red, orange, or yellow. Legousia hybrida thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
