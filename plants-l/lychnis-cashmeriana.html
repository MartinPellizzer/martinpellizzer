Lychnis cashmeriana

Lychnis cashmeriana, commonly known as the Kashmir bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Lychnis cashmeriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
