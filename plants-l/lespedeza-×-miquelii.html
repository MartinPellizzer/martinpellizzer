Lespedeza × miquelii

Lespedeza × miquelii, commonly known as the Chinese lespedeza, is a hybrid shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils, where it thrives in partial shade to full sun. Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and erosion control.

Lespedeza × miquelii is also cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
