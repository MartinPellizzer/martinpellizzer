Ludisia discolor var. dawsoniana

Ludisia discolor var. dawsoniana, commonly known as the Dawson's flame orchid, is a striking terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting markings. The plant features broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
