Linum strictum f. cymosum

Linum strictum f. cymosum, commonly known as the clustered flax, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that produce clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and compact growth habit.
