Lepechinia cocuyensis

Lepechinia cocuyensis, commonly known as the Cocuy mountain shrub, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
