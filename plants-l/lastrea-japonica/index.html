Lastrea Japonica

Lastrea japonica is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the forests of Japan. It is known for its striking purple blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and requires careful cultivation. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered in the wild. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical wonder.
