Lellingeria pumila

Lellingeria pumila, commonly known as the dwarf daisy or small daisy, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling daisies. The plant has a compact rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves that help it conserve water. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Lellingeria pumila is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
