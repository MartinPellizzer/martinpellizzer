Leptopetalum coreanum var. coreanum

Leptopetalum coreanum var. coreanum, commonly known as the Korean leaved honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in white or pale yellow hues. The plant has elliptical leaves that are oppositely arranged on the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ornamental and ecological landscaping.
