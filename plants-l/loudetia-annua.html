Loudetia annua

Loudetia annua, commonly known as the annual jointvetch, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Africa.

It grows up to 1 meter tall and has slender, branching stems that are often hairy. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers in clusters, typically pink to purple in color.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields and along roadsides.
