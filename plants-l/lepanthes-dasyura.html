Lepanthes dasyura

Lepanthes dasyura, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, dark purple to almost black petals and sepals, which contrast with its white or pale yellow lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom during the rainy season.

Lepanthes dasyura is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
