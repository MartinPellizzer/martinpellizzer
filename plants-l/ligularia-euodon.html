Ligularia euodon

Ligularia euodon, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base, often with a waxy surface. The plant produces yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in wetlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for water garden landscapes.
