Lepidium africanum var. aethiopicum

Lepidium africanum var. aethiopicum, commonly known as African cress, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant produces small, green, edible leaves and yellow flowers that attract pollinators. It is often grown as a leafy vegetable in various parts of Africa due to its nutritional value.

The plant is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
