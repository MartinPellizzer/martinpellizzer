Lampranthus esterhuyseniae

Lampranthus esterhuyseniae, commonly known as the Esterhuysenia's lantern plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and showy flowers. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
