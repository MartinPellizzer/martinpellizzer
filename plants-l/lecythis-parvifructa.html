Lecythis parvifructa

Lecythis parvifructa, commonly known as the small-fruited lecyth.

It is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly found in the Amazon rainforest. The plant is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and produces a unique, hard-shelled fruit. The fruit contains a single, large seed and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of Amazonian flora.
