Litsea neesiana var. corymbifera

Litsea neesiana var. corymbifera, commonly known as the clustered litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. Its fruits are small drupes that mature to a dark purple color.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
