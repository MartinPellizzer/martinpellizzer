Limonium cordovillense

Limonium cordovillense, commonly known as the Cordovillense sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, linear leaves and has a preference for coastal or saline environments. It is often found in dunes and rocky coastal areas where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Limonium cordovillense is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate harsh, maritime conditions.
