Luzula spicata var. italica

Luzula spicata var. italica, commonly known as Italian woodrush, is a perennial plant belonging to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and compact, cylindrical inflorescences that bear small, white to pale green flowers. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings for its ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
