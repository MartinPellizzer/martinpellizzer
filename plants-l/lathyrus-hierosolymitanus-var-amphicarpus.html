Lathyrus hierosolymitanus var. amphicarpus

Lathyrus hierosolymitanus var. amphicarpus, commonly known as the Jerusalem thorn, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive thorned stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
