Lomatophyllum antsingyense

Lomatophyllum antsingyense, commonly known as the Madagascar star cactus, is a unique succulent native to Madagascar.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with clusters of star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its arid environment. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for dry climates.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
