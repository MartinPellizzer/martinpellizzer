Lotus tenuifolius

Lotus tenuifolius, commonly known as the slender-leaf lotus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and striking pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in shallow water or moist soil, often found in wetlands and along riverbanks. Its rhizomes are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The lotus tenuifolius is also appreciated for its ornamental value in aquatic gardens.
