Lactuca graminea

Lactuca graminea, commonly known as slender lettuce, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is characterized by its upright, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Lactuca graminea is not commonly cultivated for food but is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
