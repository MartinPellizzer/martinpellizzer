Lobelia zelayensis

Lobelia zelayensis, commonly known as the Zelaya lobelia, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
