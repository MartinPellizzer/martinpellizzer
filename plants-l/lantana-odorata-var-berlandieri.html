Lantana odorata var. berlandieri

Lantana odorata var. berlandieri, commonly known as the Mexican lantana, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow, orange, and red, depending on the variety. The plant has aromatic leaves and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in full sun. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

However, it can be invasive in some regions and may require careful management.
