Luzula atlantica subsp. tibestica

Luzula atlantica subsp.

tibestica, commonly known as the Tibetan woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau. It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit.
