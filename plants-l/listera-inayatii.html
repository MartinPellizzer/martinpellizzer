Listera inayatii

Listera inayatii, commonly known as the Inayat's basket.

It is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
