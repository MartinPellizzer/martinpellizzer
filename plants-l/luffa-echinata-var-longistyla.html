Luffa echinata var. longistyla

Luffa echinata var. longistyla, commonly known as the long-styled luffa, is a variant of the common sponge gourd.

It is a tropical vine that produces large, fibrous fruits with a spongy interior. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires a long growing season to mature. Its flowers are typically yellow with five petals and are followed by the characteristic sponge-like fruit.

The fruit is harvested when fully mature, and the fibrous material inside is used for sponges and other household purposes.
