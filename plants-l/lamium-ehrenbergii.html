Lamium ehrenbergii

Lamium ehrenbergii, commonly known as purple deadnettle, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces clusters of purple to pink flowers. The plant is characterized by its opposite leaf arrangement and the presence of both stamens and petals in its flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Lamium ehrenbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
