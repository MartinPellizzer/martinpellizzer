Lithocarpus tenuinervis subsp. blaoensis

Lithocarpus tenuinervis subsp.

blaoensis, commonly known as the Blaoensis stone oak, is a member of the Fagaceae family, characterized by its evergreen nature and hard, woody fruit. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in Vietnam and China.
