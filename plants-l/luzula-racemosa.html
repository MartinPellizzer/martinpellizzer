Luzula racemosa

Luzula racemosa, commonly known as blue flag iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family. The plant features tall, slender stems that bear dense clusters of blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

Luzula racemosa typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and stream banks.
