Liparis capensis

Liparis capensis, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers well-drained environments. It has linear to lance-shaped leaves and a short, erect stem.

Liparis capensis is part of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
