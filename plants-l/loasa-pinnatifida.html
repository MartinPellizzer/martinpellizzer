Loasa pinnatifida

Loasa pinnatifida, commonly known as the featherleaf loasa, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude regions, often in rocky or forested areas. The plant features compound leaves with a distinctive feathery appearance, giving it its common name. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with colors ranging from white to pink.

Loasa pinnatifida is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
