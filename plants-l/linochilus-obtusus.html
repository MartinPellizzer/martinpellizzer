Linochilus obtusus

Linochilus obtusus, commonly known as the blunt-lipped orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Linochilus obtusus is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
