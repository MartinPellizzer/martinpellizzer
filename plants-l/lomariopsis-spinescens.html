Lomariopsis spinescens

Lomariopsis spinescens, commonly known as the spiny fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that are often covered in fine, spiny structures. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as rainforests or dense undergrowth. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help it anchor to the forest floor while absorbing nutrients.

The botanical features of Lomariopsis spinescens make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany and ecological research.
