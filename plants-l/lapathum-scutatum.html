Lapathum scutatum

Lapathum scutatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved betony, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, opposite leaves and spikes of blue or purple flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, woodland habitats. The plant has a long history of use in traditional herbal medicine.

Its aromatic leaves and flowers are often used to make teas and tinctures.
