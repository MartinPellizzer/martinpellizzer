Luzula sylvatica var. gracilis

Luzula sylvatica var. gracilis, commonly known as slender woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and arranged in dense tufts. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
