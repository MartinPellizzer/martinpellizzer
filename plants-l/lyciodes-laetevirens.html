Lyciodes laetevirens

Lyciodes laetevirens, commonly known as the happy green lyciodes, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Lyciodes laetevirens is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in Mediterranean climates.
