Lonicera sibirica

Lonicera sibirica, commonly known as the Siberian honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its red berries are edible and attract birds, aiding in seed dispersal.

Lonicera sibirica is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
