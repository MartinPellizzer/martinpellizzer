Livistona mindorensis

Livistona mindorensis, commonly known as the Mindanao palm, is a species of palm native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible fruits that are important in local ecosystems. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species.
