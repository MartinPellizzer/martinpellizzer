Liparis rostrata

Liparis rostrata, commonly known as the rostrated orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. It has a rhizomatous root system and possesses lance-shaped leaves.

Liparis rostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
