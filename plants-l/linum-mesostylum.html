Linum mesostylum

Linum mesostylum, commonly known as the slender flax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Linaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale blue or white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, oval seeds. Linum mesostylum is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in textile production.
