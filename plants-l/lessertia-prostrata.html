Lessertia prostrata

Lessertia prostrata, commonly known as the African violet, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, dark green, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it suitable for rock gardens and container planting.
