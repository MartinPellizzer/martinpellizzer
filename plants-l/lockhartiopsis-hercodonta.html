Lockhartiopsis hercodonta

Lockhartiopsis hercodonta, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces small, white or yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its seeds are small and dispersed by wind, aiding in its survival in challenging habitats.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
