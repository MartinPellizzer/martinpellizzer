Lilium speciosum var. rubrum

Lilium speciosum var. rubrum, commonly known as the red tiger lily, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often with a distinctive black blotch at the base. The plant produces sword-shaped, lanceolate leaves that are typically a vibrant green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late summer into early autumn.
