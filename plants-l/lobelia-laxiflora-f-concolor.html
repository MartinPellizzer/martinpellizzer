Lobelia laxiflora f. concolor

Lobelia laxiflora f. concolor, commonly known as the smooth lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, wooded areas. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers. Its flowers are adapted for pollination by bees and other small insects.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
