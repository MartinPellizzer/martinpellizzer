Lepanthes golbasto

Lepanthes golbasto, commonly known as the Golbasto orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within forest understories. The plant features delicate, elongated leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns on their petals. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white, with a distinctive spur at the base of the lip.

Lepanthes golbasto is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
