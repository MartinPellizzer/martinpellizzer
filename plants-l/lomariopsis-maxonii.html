Lomariopsis maxonii

Lomariopsis maxonii, commonly known as the Kauai feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky areas. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or brown in color.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Lomariopsis maxonii is considered a rare and endangered species.
