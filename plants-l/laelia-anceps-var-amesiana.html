Laelia anceps var. amesiana

Laelia anceps var. amesiana, commonly known as the Amesiana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically display a combination of white, yellow, and pink hues. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its humid, shaded habitat. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for intermediate orchid growers.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and reliable blooming cycle.
