Lotus cytisoides

Lotus cytisoides, commonly known as the yellow lotus or golden lotus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange flowers and feathery, fern-like leaves. The plant is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
