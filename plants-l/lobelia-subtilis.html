Lobelia subtilis

Lobelia subtilis, commonly known as the slender lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Lobelia subtilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its subtle beauty.
