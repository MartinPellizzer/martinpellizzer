Liparis shaoshunia

Liparis shaoshunia, commonly known as the Shaoshun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or mountainous regions.

Liparis shaoshunia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
