Liabum canarense

Liabum canarense, commonly known as the Canary Island mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and rocky soils, adapting well to arid and saline conditions. Liabum canarense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in Mediterranean climates.
