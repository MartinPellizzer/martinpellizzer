Lellingeria micropecten

Lellingeria micropecten, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisies, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a compact appearance. It is native to South Africa and thrives in dry, sandy soils.

Lellingeria micropecten is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
