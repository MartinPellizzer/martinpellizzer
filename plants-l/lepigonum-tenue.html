Lepigonum tenue

Lepigonum tenue, commonly known as slender lepigonum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf shapes and often edible rhubarb-like stems. Lepigonum tenue is typically found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
