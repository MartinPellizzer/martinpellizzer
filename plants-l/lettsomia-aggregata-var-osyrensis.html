Lettsomia aggregata var. osyrensis

Lettsomia aggregata var. osyrensis, commonly known as the Osyrenian thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features spiny, upright stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an adaptable and resilient species within its native habitat.
