Lepanthes davidsei

Lepanthes davidsei, commonly known as the David's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Lepanthes davidsei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
