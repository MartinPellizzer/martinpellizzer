Lepanthes elegantula

Lepanthes elegantula, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically feature a white or pale pink coloration. The plant grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its natural habitat.

Lepanthes elegantula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
