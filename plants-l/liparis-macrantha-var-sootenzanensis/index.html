Liparis Macrantha Var. Sootenzanensis

Liparis macrantha var. sootenzanensis is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the high altitudes of southern Japan. This variant is known for its striking purple and white flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky mountainous regions. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
