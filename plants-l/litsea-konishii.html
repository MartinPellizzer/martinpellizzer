Litsea konishii

Litsea konishii, commonly known as the Japanese sweet olive, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green surface with a subtle aromatic scent.

Litsea konishii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Japanese gardens.
