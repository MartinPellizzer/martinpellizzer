Lepanthes denticulata

Lepanthes denticulata, commonly known as the denticulate orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and white coloration. Its flowers are usually borne on slender stems and are known for their intricate petal structure.

Lepanthes denticulata is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique botanical characteristics.
