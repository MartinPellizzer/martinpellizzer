Lotus argyraeus

Lotus argyraeus, commonly known as the silver lotus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly India and Nepal.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds. Lotus argyraeus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

Its growth habit includes both aquatic and terrestrial forms, depending on the environment.
