Lithops karasmontana subsp. bella

Lithops karasmontana subsp.

bella, commonly known as the "Cape Town Lithops," is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape, often displaying a range of colors from pale green to reddish-brown.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
