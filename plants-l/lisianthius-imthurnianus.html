Lisianthius imthurnianus

Lisianthius imthurnianus, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright yellow or orange disk flowers surrounded by white to pink ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and winter flowering habit.
