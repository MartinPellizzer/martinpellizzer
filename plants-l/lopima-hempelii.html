Lopima hempelii

Lopima hempelii, commonly known as the Malagasy starfish plant, is a unique succulent native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, fleshy leaves that are often blue or purple in color. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
