Limonium cancellatum

Limonium cancellatum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, preferring sandy or gravelly soils. Its flowers bloom in summer, and the plant is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value.

Limonium cancellatum is also valued for its ability to thrive in harsh, saline environments.
