Lysimachia pentapetala

Lysimachia pentapetala, commonly known as five-petaled chickweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Lysimachia pentapetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
