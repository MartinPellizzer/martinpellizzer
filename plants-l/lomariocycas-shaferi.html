Lomariocycas shaferi

Lomariocycas shaferi, commonly known as the Shafer's cycad, is a rare and ancient plant species native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and is adapted to the dry, seasonal climate of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
