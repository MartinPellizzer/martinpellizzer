Linanthus nashianus

Linanthus nashianus, commonly known as Nash's linanthus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon.

It belongs to the evening primrose family (Onagraceae) and is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer.
