Limonia demeusei

Limonia demeusei, commonly known as the Demeuse's lime, is a small evergreen shrub native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces edible, citrus-like fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting well to the unique climate of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
