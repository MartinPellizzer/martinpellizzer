Lecanorchis javanica

Lecanorchis javanica, commonly known as the Javan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant has a short rhizome and produces a single spike with several pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are ovate and arranged in a rosette, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Lecanorchis javanica is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
