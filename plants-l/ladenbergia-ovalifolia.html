Ladenbergia ovalifolia

Ladenbergia ovalifolia, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It features a dense, rounded canopy with glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody capsules containing flat, elliptical seeds. Its bark is thick and fissured, often with a distinctive reddish or brownish color.

Ladenbergia ovalifolia is valued for its durable wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
