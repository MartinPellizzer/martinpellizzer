Lepidium lipskyi

Lepidium lipskyi, commonly known as the Andean cress, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and medicinal plants. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pinnate leaves. It produces small, round seeds that are often used in traditional Andean cuisine.
