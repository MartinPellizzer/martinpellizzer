Lacinaria scariosa var. multiplex

Lacinaria scariosa var. multiplex, commonly known as the hairy lacinaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.
