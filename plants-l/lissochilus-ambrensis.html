Lissochilus ambrensis

Lissochilus ambrensis, commonly known as the Ambrensis orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andes of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
