Logania longifolia var. subsessilis

Logania longifolia var. subsessilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved logania, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes milkweeds and related species. The plant is characterized by its slender, woody stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. Its leaves are elongated and sessile, giving it a distinctive appearance.
