Linaria yunnanensis var. caerulea

Linaria yunnanensis var. caerulea, commonly known as the blue-flowered sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves are adapted to arid environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
