Leiospora saposhnikovii

Leiospora saposhnikovii, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream sides.
