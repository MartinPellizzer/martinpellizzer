Landolphia trichostigma var. oxyacra

Landolphia trichostigma var. oxyacra, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a striking deep green color. The plant produces large, showy flowers that range from white to pink, often with a faintly veined pattern. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release bright orange seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
