Lolium perenne var. rigidum

Lolium perenne var. rigidum, commonly known as rigid fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and rigid, often hairy stems. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Rigid fescue is often used in landscaping and as a forage crop due to its hardiness and adaptability.
