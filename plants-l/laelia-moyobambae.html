Laelia moyobambae

Laelia moyobambae, commonly known as the Moyobamba orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of the Peruvian Andes.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a contrasting lip. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to the cool, humid conditions of its habitat.

Laelia moyobambae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
