Lysimachia helleri

Lysimachia helleri, commonly known as Heller's chickweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Asia and North America.

It features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.

Lysimachia helleri is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate foliage and early blooming habit.
