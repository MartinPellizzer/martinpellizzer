Lamium uraiense

Lamium uraiense, commonly known as the Uraic deadnettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Lamium uraiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
