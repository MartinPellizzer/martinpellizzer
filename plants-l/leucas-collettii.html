Leucas collettii

Leucas collettii, commonly known as the Collett's leucas, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
