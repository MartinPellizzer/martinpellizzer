Limoniastrum guyonianum

Limoniastrum guyonianum, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, demonstrating high drought tolerance. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access water from deeper soil layers.

Limoniastrum guyonianum is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
