Ludwigia natans var. rotundata

Ludwigia natans var. rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf water primrose, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, rounded leaves that are typically bright green and often have a waxy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom at the water's surface, attracting pollinators like bees and insects. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with ample sunlight.

This species is often used in aquariums and ponds for its ornamental value and ability to help control algae growth.
