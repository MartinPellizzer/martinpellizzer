Liliago abyssinica

Liliago abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian lily, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a waxy surface. It produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
