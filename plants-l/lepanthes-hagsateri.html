Lepanthes hagsateri

Lepanthes hagsateri, commonly known as the Hagsater's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns, often in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Lepanthes hagsateri is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat.
