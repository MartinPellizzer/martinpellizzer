Ligularia cuneata

Ligularia cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ligularia, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a terminal inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Ligularia cuneata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
