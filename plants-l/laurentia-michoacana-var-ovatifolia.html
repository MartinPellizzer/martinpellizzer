Laurentia michoacana var. ovatifolia

Laurentia michoacana var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the Michoacan laurel, is a species native to Mexico, specifically the state of Michoacan.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lauraceae, characterized by its evergreen foliage and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are ovate in shape, with a smooth margin and a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental value.

It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, dark blue berries.
