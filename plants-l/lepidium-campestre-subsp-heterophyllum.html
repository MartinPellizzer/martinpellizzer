Lepidium campestre subsp. heterophyllum

Lepidium campestre subsp.

heterophyllum, commonly known as the slender field cress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family. It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and linear, alternate leaves.

The subspecies is distinguished by its variation in leaf morphology compared to the typical form. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and grasslands.

This plant is an annual herb that plays a role in soil stabilization and provides nectar for pollinators.
