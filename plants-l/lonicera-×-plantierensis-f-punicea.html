Lonicera × plantierensis f. punicea

Lonicera × plantierensis f. punicea, commonly known as the red-flowered honeysuckle, is a hybrid species within the genus Lonicera.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 2–4 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
