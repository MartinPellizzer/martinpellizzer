Lepanthes sybax

Lepanthes sybax, commonly known as the silver star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, white flowers with a distinctive yellow center, resembling a star. Its leaves are typically broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture.

Lepanthes sybax is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
