Lembotropis nigricans subsp. australis

Lembotropis nigricans subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian black lembotropis, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Australia. It is characterized by its dark, almost black, foliage and small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rainforest or coastal habitats. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
