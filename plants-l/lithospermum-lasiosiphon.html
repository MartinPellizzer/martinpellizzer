Lithospermum lasiosiphon

Lithospermum lasiosiphon, commonly known as red root, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its reddish-brown roots and slender, branching stems. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Lithospermum lasiosiphon is often found in dry, open habitats such as fields and rocky areas.
