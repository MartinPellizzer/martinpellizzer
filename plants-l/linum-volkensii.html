Linum volkensii

Linum volkensii, commonly known as the Volkens' flax, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and linear leaves. The plant belongs to the Linaceae family and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Linum volkensii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
