Lindsaea deltoidea

Lindsaea deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid lindsaea, is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically divided into narrow, linear segments. The plant produces small, clustered spores on the underside of its leaves, which are essential for its reproductive cycle. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
