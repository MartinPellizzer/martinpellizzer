Lotus argensis

Lotus argensis, commonly known as the silver lotus, is a species of aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the surface of water, while its roots are anchored in the mud below. Lotus argensis is known for its ability to thrive in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

It is also valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
