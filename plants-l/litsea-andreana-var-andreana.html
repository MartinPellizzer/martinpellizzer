Litsea andreana var. andreana

Litsea andreana var. andreana, commonly known as the Philippine lauraceae, is a species of flowering plant in the laurel family.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in lowland to montane forests, preferring shaded and humid environments. Its fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage and fragrance.
