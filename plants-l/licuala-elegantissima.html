Licuala elegantissima

Licuala elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant palm or the golden fan palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that are often a striking yellow or greenish-yellow in color. The plant typically grows in wet, lowland areas and thrives in humid, warm climates. It has a tall, slender trunk with a smooth, pale bark.

Licuala elegantissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal.
