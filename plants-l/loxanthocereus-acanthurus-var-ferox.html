Loxanthocereus acanthurus var. ferox

Loxanthocereus acanthurus var. ferox, commonly known as the Thorny Night-Blooming Cereus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and nocturnal blooming habit, with large, white flowers that open at night. The plant has a columnar growth form and produces bright yellow flowers with a strong fragrance. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique flowering behavior.
