Leea luzonensis

Leea luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon leea, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Leeaceae and is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Leea luzonensis is adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
