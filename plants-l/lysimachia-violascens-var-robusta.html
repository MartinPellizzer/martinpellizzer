Lysimachia violascens var. robusta

Lysimachia violascens var. robusta, commonly known as the robust yellow archangel, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, often in clusters at the tips of its stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variant is noted for its vigorous growth and is often used in wetland gardens or as a ground cover.
