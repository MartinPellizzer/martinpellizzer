Lonicera omeiensis

Lonicera omeiensis, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
