Lemaireocereus hystrix

Lemaireocereus hystrix, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented stems that resemble leaves, which are actually modified stems adapted for storing water. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Lemaireocereus hystrix is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
