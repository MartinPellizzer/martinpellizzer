Lomatopodium lessingianum var. tenuifolium

Lomatopodium lessingianum var. tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaf lomatopodium, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, displaying a low, sprawling growth habit.
