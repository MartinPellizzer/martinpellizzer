Lobivia chrysantha var. caspalasensis

Lobivia chrysantha var. caspalasensis, commonly known as the Caspala Lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a compact, rounded stem with fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
