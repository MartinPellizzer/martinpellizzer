Lobivia rowleyi

Lobivia rowleyi, commonly known as the Rainbow Plant, is a succulent native to South America.

It features vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant has thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. It typically grows in rocky soils and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
