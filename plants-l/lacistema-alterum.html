Lacistema alterum

Lacistema alterum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features distinctive silver-gray foliage and produces small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the evening. Its fruit is a green, fleshy berry that turns red when ripe.

Despite its ornamental appeal, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
