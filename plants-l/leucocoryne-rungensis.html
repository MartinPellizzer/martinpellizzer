Leucocoryne rungensis

Leucocoryne rungensis, commonly known as the wandering lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and a unique, elongated shape that resembles a lily. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces tubular leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
