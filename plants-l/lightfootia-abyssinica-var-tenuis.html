Lightfootia abyssinica var. tenuis

Lightfootia abyssinica var. tenuis, commonly known as the Abyssinian lightfoot, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Ethiopia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapted to its harsh environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
