Lactuca melanantha

Lactuca melanantha, commonly known as black lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its dark green, slightly bitter leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found growing in dry, rocky soils and is considered a weed in some areas.

Due to its unique appearance and flavor, it is occasionally used in local cuisines.
