Lophocereus mieckleyanus

Lophocereus mieckleyanus, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its columnar stem and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers, which are usually yellow or orange, bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators.

Lophocereus mieckleyanus is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
