Lissochilus buchananii

Lissochilus buchananii, commonly known as the Buchanan's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers that often have a distinctive white or pale pink coloration. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is typically found in cloud forests or high-altitude grasslands.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
