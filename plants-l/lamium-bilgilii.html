Lamium bilgilii

Lamium bilgilii, commonly known as Turkish dead nettle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves.

Lamium bilgilii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
