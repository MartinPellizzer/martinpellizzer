Licuala tenuissima

Licuala tenuissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, branched clusters near the base of the leaves.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional crafts due to its durable leaves.
