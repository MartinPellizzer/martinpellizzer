Ligularia alpigena

Ligularia alpigena, commonly known as the alpine ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and a distinctive yellowish-white inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain meadows and rocky slopes. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Ligularia alpigena is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
