Leria caespitosa

Leria caespitosa, commonly known as the tufted leria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features tufted, wiry stems with small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often greenish or brownish in color.

Leria caespitosa is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in dry, open habitats.
