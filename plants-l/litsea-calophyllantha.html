Litsea calophyllantha

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like India, Indonesia, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which emit a pleasant scent when crushed. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by oval-shaped berries.
