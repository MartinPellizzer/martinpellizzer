Ligularia yesoensis var. pulchella

Ligularia yesoensis var. pulchella, commonly known as the Yesso ligularia, is a herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
