Lycaste × imschootiana

Lycaste × imschootiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Lycaste mackayana and Lycaste skinneriana.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a complex structure, including a prominent lip and numerous petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Lycaste × imschootiana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively ease of cultivation.
