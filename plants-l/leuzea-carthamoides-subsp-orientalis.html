Leuzea carthamoides subsp. orientalis

Leuzea carthamoides subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as oriental leuzea, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Central Asia. It features upright, branched stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height.

The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
