Lonicera brachypoda var. aureoreticulata

Lonicera brachypoda var. aureoreticulata, commonly known as the golden netleaf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often tinged with yellow or white veins. The plant produces small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in early summer. Its berries are orange-red and edible, though they are generally not consumed by humans.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
