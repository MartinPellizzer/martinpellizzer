Linaria bubanii

Linaria bubanii, commonly known as the Balkan toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats. The plant features slender stems and produces bright yellow or orange flowers in the spring. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs.

Linaria bubanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
