Lellingeria tunguraguae

Lellingeria tunguraguae, commonly known as the Tunguragua orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in cloud forests, where it thrives in the cool, moist environment. Its botanical features include slender stems and lance-shaped leaves, which help it adapt to its high-altitude habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered an endangered species.
