Languas pulchella

Languas pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful languas, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil in partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Languas pulchella is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and easy care requirements.
