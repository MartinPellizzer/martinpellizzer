Lotus maroccanus

Lotus maroccanus, commonly known as the Moroccan lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North Africa.

It features palmate leaves with a waxy surface and produces striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments. Its flowers have a delicate fragrance and are sometimes used in traditional medicine.

The Moroccan lotus is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological role in its native habitat.
