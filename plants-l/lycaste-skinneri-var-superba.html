Lycaste skinneri var. superba

Lycaste skinneri var. superba, commonly known as the Skinner's lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
