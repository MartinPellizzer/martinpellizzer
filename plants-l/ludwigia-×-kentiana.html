Ludwigia × kentiana

Ludwigia × kentiana, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange-colored leaves, which can vary in intensity depending on light and nutrient conditions. This plant is often cultivated in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life. It prefers moderate to high water temperatures and can thrive in a range of water hardness levels.

Ludwigia × kentiana is also known for its rapid growth rate, making it a popular choice for both freshwater and brackish water environments.
