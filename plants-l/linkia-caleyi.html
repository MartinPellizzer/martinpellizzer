Linkia caleyi

Linkia caleyi, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the island's tropical rainforests. Its flowers are often small but striking, featuring intricate patterns and a sweet fragrance.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Linkia caleyi is considered a vulnerable species.
