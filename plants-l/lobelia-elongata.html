Lobelia elongata

Lobelia elongata, commonly known as the slender lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are usually blue or purple and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Lobelia elongata is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
