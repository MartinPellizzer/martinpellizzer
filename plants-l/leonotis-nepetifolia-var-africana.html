Leonotis nepetifolia var. africana

Leonotis nepetifolia var. africana, commonly known as the African heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling those of the common heliotrope. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces attractive, fragrant blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
