Limonium zanonii

Limonium zanonii, commonly known as the Sicilian sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer, typically from July to September. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found growing in coastal areas and sandy soils. Limonium zanonii is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and coastal landscaping.
