Lilium pilosiusculum var. alboviridiflorum

Lilium pilosiusculum var. alboviridiflorum, commonly known as the white-green-flowered lily, is a rare variant of the woolly lily.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale green flowers with a distinctive greenish tinge. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of western North America, particularly in California. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats with well-drained soil.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its unique floral coloration.
