Leucas montana f. scaberula

Leucas montana f. scaberula, commonly known as the rough leucas, is a variant of the leucas montana species, which belongs to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured foliage and small, often purple or pinkish flowers. This plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, preferring well-drained soils. It is native to regions of southern Europe and parts of the Mediterranean.

The f. scaberula form is distinguished by its more pronounced pubescence compared to the typical form of the species.
