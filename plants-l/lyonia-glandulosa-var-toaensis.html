Lyonia glandulosa var. toaensis

Lyonia glandulosa var. toaensis, commonly known as the Toaensis Lyonia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, glandular leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The Toaensis Lyonia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
