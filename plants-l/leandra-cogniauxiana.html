Leandra cogniauxiana

Leandra cogniauxiana, commonly known as the Cogniaux's leandra, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Strelitziaceae family and is characterized by its striking, large, banana-like leaves that are often used in ornamental horticulture. The plant produces showy, pendulous inflorescences with white or pale pink flowers, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant in tropical climates.
