Lecythis nobilis

Lecythis nobilis, commonly known as the royal lecythium, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves and produces distinctive, spiny fruits that contain a single large seed. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and has a thick, buttressed trunk. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, and it plays an important ecological role in the rainforest ecosystem.

The plant is also valued for its wood and has been used in traditional medicine.
