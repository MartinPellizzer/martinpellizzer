Ludwigia anastomosans

Ludwigia anastomosans, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-red leaves, which are often used in aquariums and water gardens. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense mats in aquatic environments. Its small, yellow flowers are typically borne on spikes and attract various pollinators.

Ludwigia anastomosans is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a range of water conditions.
