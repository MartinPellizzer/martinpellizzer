Lantanopsis tomentosa

Lantanopsis tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly lantana, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown in gardens and landscapes.
