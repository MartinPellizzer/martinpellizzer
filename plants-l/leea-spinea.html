Leea spinea

Leea spinea, commonly known as the spiny leeah, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Leeaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with five petals, and it produces small, fleshy fruits.

Leea spinea is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along streambanks.
