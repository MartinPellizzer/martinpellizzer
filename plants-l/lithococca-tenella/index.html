Lithococca Tenella

Lithococca tenella is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is known for its striking violet-blue petals. This species is often overlooked due to its small size and subtle appearance. Its unique habitat requirements make it a challenging plant to cultivate. Despite its elusive nature, Lithococca tenella holds significant ecological value in its native ecosystem.
