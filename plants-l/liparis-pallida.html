Liparis pallida

Liparis pallida, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale green to white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Liparis pallida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
