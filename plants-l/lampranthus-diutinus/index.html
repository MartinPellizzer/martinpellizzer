Lampranthus Diutinus

Lampranthus diutinus is a strikingly colorful plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its unique adaptations to arid environments. This species is prized for its vibrant flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its ability to store water in its fleshy leaves makes it a resilient addition to any drought-tolerant garden.
