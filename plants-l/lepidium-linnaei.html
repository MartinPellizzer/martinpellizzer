Lepidium linnaei

Lepidium linnaei, commonly known as the Linnaean cress, is a small annual flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. Lepidium linnaei is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and meadows.

Its seeds are small and contain compounds that have been studied for potential medicinal properties.
