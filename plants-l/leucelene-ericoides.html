Leucelene ericoides

Leucelene ericoides, commonly known as the purple heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple and white. Its leaves are leathery and arranged in whorls, typical of many Ericaceae species.

Leucelene ericoides thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
