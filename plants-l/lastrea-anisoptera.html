Lastrea anisoptera

Lastrea anisoptera, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough or hairy texture.

The flowers bloom in the summer months and are pollinated primarily by birds and insects.
