Leucopogon coelophyllus

Leucopogon coelophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in sandy or acidic soils and is often found in heathland environments.
