Litsea Veitchiana Var. Trichocarpa

Litsea veitchiana var. trichocarpa is a rare and captivating plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its striking foliage and delicate flowers, this variant stands out among its relatives in the Lauraceae family. Its leaves are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a unique texture and appearance. The plant thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in shaded, forested areas. Due to its limited distribution and ornamental value, it is a sought-after species among plant enthusiasts and botanists alike.
