Leandra Dichotoma

Leandra dichotoma is a striking plant native to the cloud forests of South America. Known for its vibrant, showy flowers, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This species is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests, where it relies on specific ecological conditions to flourish. Its unique growth pattern and ornamental appeal make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, Leandra dichotoma remains a relatively rare and under-studied plant in the botanical world.
