Lachenalia aloides var. vanzyliae

Lachenalia aloides var. vanzyliae, commonly known as the Van Zyl's lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth habit. This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
