Lepanthes deleastes

Lepanthes deleastes, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are often yellow or orange in color. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as cloud forests, where it attaches itself to trees or rocks. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Lepanthes deleastes is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of tropical flora.
