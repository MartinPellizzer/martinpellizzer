Lotus odoratus

Lotus odoratus, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Nymphaeaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, slender stems. The plant thrives in shallow freshwater environments, often found in ponds and lakes. Its leaves are broad and floating, while its flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

The lotus is also notable for its symbolic significance in various cultures and religions.
