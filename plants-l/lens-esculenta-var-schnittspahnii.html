Lens esculenta var. schnittspahnii

Lens esculenta var. schnittspahnii, commonly known as the Schnittspahnii lentil, is a cultivated variety of the common lentil.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped seeds that are typically pale green to yellow in color. The plant is an annual legume that thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil. It produces clusters of small white or pale pink flowers, followed by pods containing the edible seeds.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and is often used in various culinary applications.
