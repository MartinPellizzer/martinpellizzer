Lasianthus speciosus

Lasianthus speciosus, commonly known as the ornate lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are often brightly colored, with a distinctive two-lipped structure, and are arranged in dense clusters.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
