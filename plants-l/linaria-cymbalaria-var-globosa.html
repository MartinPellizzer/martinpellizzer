Linaria cymbalaria var. globosa

Linaria cymbalaria var. globosa, commonly known as the globular yellowtop, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit. The plant produces bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
