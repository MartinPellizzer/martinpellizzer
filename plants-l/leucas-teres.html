Leucas teres

Leucas teres, commonly known as the narrow-leaved leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Leucas teres is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
