Ligularia kantingensis

Ligularia kantingensis, commonly known as the Chinese ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a composite inflorescence, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species in the botanical world.
