Linanthastrum nuttallii subsp. pubescens

Linanthastrum nuttallii subsp.

pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent flaxvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves.
