Lonicera weigelia

Lonicera weigelia, commonly known as the Weigela, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the honeysuckle family (Caprifoliaceae) and is characterized by its showy, funnel-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall.
