Lampranthus paucifolius

Lampranthus paucifolius, commonly known as the firecracker plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features bright orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. The plant has small, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
