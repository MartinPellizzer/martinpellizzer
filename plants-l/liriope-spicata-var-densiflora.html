Liriope spicata var. densiflora

Liriope spicata var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered lilyturf, is a perennial grass-like plant native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that form dense clumps, and its flowering spikes consist of small, pale blue to white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate partial shade.

Liriope spicata var. densiflora is also valued for its ability to suppress weeds and its low maintenance requirements.
