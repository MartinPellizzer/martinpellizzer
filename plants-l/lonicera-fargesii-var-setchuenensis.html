Lonicera fargesii var. setchuenensis

Lonicera fargesii var. setchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces orange-red berries that are edible for birds but not typically for humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
