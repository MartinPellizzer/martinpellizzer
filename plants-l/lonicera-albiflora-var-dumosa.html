Lonicera albiflora var. dumosa

Lonicera albiflora var. dumosa, commonly known as the silver honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, woody stems that are often covered in a silvery, fuzzy coating, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by red berries in the fall. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
