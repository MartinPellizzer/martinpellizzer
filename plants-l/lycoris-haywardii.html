Lycoris haywardii

Lycoris haywardii, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red flowers that resemble spiders. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and has a bulbous root system typical of its genus. It blooms in the autumn season, often after the leaves have turned yellow and withered.

Lycoris haywardii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
