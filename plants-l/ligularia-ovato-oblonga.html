Ligularia ovato-oblonga

Ligularia ovato-oblonga, commonly known as the broad-leaf ligularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped to ovate-oblong leaves that are typically dark green with a pronounced veining pattern. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers arranged in a spires, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in forest understories.

Its foliage is popular in ornamental gardening for its dramatic appearance and texture.
