Lindsaea rubescens

Lindsaea rubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed fern, is a species of epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

Lindsaea rubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
