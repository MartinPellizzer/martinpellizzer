Limonium mouretii

Limonium mouretii, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a white center. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, showing a high tolerance for salt and wind. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Limonium mouretii is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
