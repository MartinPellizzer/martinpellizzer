Linanthus dichotomus var. meridianus

Linanthus dichotomus var. meridianus, commonly known as the Sierra daisy, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The Sierra daisy is adapted to arid environments and is often found in mountainous regions of California and Nevada.
