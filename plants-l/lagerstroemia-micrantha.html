Lagerstroemia micrantha

Lagerstroemia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered crape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Lagerstroemia micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
