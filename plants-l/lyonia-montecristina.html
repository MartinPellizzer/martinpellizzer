Lyonia montecristina

Lyonia montecristina, commonly known as the Montecristi lyonia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains in South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude regions, often in rocky or well-drained soils. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are usually white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Lyonia montecristina is also known for its hardy nature, able to withstand cold and harsh environmental conditions.
