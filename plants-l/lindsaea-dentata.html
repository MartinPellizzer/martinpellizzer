Lindsaea dentata

Lindsaea dentata, commonly known as the toothed lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Bromeliaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are often adorned with small, tooth-like projections along the margins. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features make it a popular specimen among collectors and enthusiasts of tropical flora.
