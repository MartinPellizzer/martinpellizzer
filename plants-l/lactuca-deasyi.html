Lactuca deasyi

Lactuca deasyi, commonly known as the Deasy's lettuce, is a flowering plant species native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers in the summer. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
