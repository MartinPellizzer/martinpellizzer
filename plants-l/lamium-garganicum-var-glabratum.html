Lamium garganicum var. glabratum

Lamium garganicum var. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth dead-nettle, is a low-growing perennial herb native to central and southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped, smooth, and glossy leaves that are often tinged with reddish or purple hues. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stems. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its leaves are edible and have been used in traditional herbal remedies.
