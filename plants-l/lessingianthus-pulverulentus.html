Lessingianthus pulverulentus

Lessingianthus pulverulentus, commonly known as the dusty lessingia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is known for its hairy, grayish foliage that gives it a dusty appearance. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

This species is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique texture and color.
