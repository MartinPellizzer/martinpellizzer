Leucanthemopsis boissieri

Leucanthemopsis boissieri, commonly known as the rock dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Leucanthemopsis boissieri is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
