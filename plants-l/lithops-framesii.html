Lithops framesii

Lithops framesii, commonly known as the "living stone," is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that resemble stones, helping it blend into its arid environment. The plant stores water in its leaves, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. It produces small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months.

Lithops framesii is well adapted to rocky, sandy soils and requires minimal water to thrive.
