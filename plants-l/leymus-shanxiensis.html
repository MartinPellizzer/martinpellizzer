Leymus shanxiensis

Leymus shanxiensis, commonly known as Shanxi needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to China, specifically found in the Shanxi province.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in dry, temperate climates and is often found in open, rocky areas or along the edges of forests. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Leymus shanxiensis plays a minor but important role in local ecosystems, providing forage for grazing animals and habitat for small invertebrates.
