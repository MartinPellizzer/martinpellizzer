Licania aperta

Licania aperta, commonly known as the open licania, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and palmate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
