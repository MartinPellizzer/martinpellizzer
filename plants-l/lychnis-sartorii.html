Lychnis sartorii

Lychnis sartorii, commonly known as the garden sorrel, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features upright stems with alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are usually pink or red, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
