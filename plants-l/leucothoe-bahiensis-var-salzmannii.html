Leucothoe bahiensis var. salzmannii

Leucothoe bahiensis var. salzmannii, commonly known as the Salzmann's leucothoe, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, with a glossy surface.

It produces small, bell-shaped white or pale pink flowers in the spring, followed by dark blue berries.
