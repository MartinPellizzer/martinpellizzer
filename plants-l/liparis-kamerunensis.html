Liparis kamerunensis

Liparis kamerunensis, commonly known as the Cameroon orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the forests of Cameroon.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
