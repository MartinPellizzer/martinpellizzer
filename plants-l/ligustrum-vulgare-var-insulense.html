Ligustrum vulgare var. insulense

Ligustrum vulgare var. insulense, commonly known as the Mediterranean privet, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by black berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
