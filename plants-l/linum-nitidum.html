Linum nitidum

Linum nitidum, commonly known as the velvetleaf flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Linaceae family, which includes other flax species. It thrives in warm, dry climates and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Linum nitidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
