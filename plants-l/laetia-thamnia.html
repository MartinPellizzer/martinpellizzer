Laetia thamnia

Laetia thamnia, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers with white centers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Laetia thamnia is often found growing on rocky outcrops and in rocky soils, making it a characteristic plant of its habitat.
