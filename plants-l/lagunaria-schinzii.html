Lagunaria schinzii

Lagunaria schinzii, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often blooming during the rainy season.
