Licaria

Licaria, commonly known as the "starfruit" or "carambola," is a tropical fruit-bearing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Oxalidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped fruit. The plant produces small, green leaves with a heart-shaped base and clusters of yellowish flowers. The fruit develops from these flowers and turns yellow or orange when mature.

Licaria is valued not only for its unique appearance and flavor but also for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
