Lysimachia remota var. remota

Lysimachia remota var. remota, commonly known as the remote yellow-eyed grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as stream banks, wet meadows, and woodland edges. The plant features slender, branching stems with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
