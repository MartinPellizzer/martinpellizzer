Laelia purpurata var. rosea

Laelia purpurata var. rosea, commonly known as the rose-flowered Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andes mountains.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to rose-colored flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, forming compact clusters with pseudobulbs. Its leaves are grass-like, arranged in a rosette, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
