Ligularia Sagitta

Ligularia sagitta is a striking plant known for its unique leaf shape and vibrant color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. The plant's name refers to its arrow-like leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. It is a popular choice for gardeners looking to add texture and interest to their landscapes. With proper care, Ligularia sagitta can become a standout feature in any garden.
