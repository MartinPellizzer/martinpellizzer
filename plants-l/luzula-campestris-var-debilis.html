Luzula campestris var. debilis

Luzula campestris var. debilis, commonly known as slender woodrush, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial that belongs to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. This variety thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in late spring or early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wetland habitats.
