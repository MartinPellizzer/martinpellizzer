Loreya nigricans

Loreya nigricans, commonly known as the black lorea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries, which are the primary feature of the plant. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
