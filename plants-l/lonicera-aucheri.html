Lonicera aucheri

Lonicera aucheri, commonly known as the Austrian honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Europe.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually white or yellow, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that matures in late summer, attracting birds.

Lonicera aucheri is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
