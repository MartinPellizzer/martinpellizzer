Laelia anceps var. alba

Laelia anceps var. alba, commonly known as the White Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and a contrasting greenish-yellow lip, which make it highly sought after in horticulture. This plant typically grows at high altitudes, thriving in cool, humid environments with well-draining soil. It produces fragrant, long-lasting blooms that can last several weeks during the flowering season.

Due to its beauty and resilience, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
