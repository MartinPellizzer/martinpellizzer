Luciliocline subspicata

Luciliocline subspicata, commonly known as the spiky broom.

It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky habitats. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can be invasive in certain areas if not properly managed.
