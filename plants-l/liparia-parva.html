Liparia parva

Liparia parva, commonly known as the small lip orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has a slender, leafless stem that bears a single or few flowers. Its flowers are small, with a lip that is often marked with intricate patterns.

Liparia parva is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other small flowering plants.
