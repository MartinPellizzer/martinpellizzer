Lyonia urbaniana

Lyonia urbaniana, commonly known as the rock rose or mountain lyonia, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in spring. The plant has aromatic, dark green leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for landscaping in coastal and sandy environments.
