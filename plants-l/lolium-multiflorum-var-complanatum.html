Lolium multiflorum var. complanatum

Lolium multiflorum var. complanatum, commonly known as slender ryegrass, is a cool-season grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, slender blades and a distinctive seed head that is often flattened or compressed. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in pastures and lawns. It has a tufted growth habit and produces small, greenish-white flowers during the spring and early summer.

This variety is valued for its rapid establishment and high forage quality in agricultural settings.
