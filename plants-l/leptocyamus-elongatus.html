Leptocyamus elongatus

Leptocyamus elongatus, commonly known as the slender leaved orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, elongated leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is often found growing on tree trunks or in rocky crevices, demonstrating its epiphytic nature.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
