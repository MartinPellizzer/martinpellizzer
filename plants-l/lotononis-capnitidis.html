Lotononis capnitidis

Lotononis capnitidis, commonly known as the Cape Town daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like appearance with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in rocky, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale blue to white ray florets.

Lotononis capnitidis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
