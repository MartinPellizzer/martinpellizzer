Lissochilus volkensii

Lissochilus volkensii, commonly known as the Volkens's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes, particularly in countries such as Bolivia and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
