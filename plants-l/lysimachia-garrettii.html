Lysimachia garrettii

Lysimachia garrettii, commonly known as Garrett's chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often appearing before the leaves are fully developed. The plant has hairy stems and oval-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Lysimachia garrettii is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
