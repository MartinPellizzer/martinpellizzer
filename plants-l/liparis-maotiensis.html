Liparis maotiensis

Liparis maotiensis, commonly known as the Maotian orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its botanical features include slender stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
