Laelia × tresederiana

Laelia × tresederiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Miltonia species.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of the Laelia genus. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
