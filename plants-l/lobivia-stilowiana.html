Lobivia stilowiana

Lobivia stilowiana, commonly known as the Stilowian Lobivia, is a species of cactus native to South America, specifically found in Argentina.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and is often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
