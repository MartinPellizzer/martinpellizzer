Lecanorchis multiflora var. multiflora

Lecanorchis multiflora var. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
