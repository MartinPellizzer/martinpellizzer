Laelia purpurata var. blenheimense

Laelia purpurata var. blenheimense, commonly known as the Blenheim orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, velvety petals.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to the mountainous regions of South America. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are characterized by a deep purple hue with intricate patterns, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

It requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to thrive.
