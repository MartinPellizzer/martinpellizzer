Lacellia Montana

Lacellia montana is a rare and fascinating plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. It thrives in cold, rocky environments where few other species can survive. Known for its unique spiral-shaped leaves, this plant has captured the interest of botanists and horticulturists alike. Its slow growth and striking appearance make it a prized specimen in specialized collections. Despite its beauty, much about its biology and ecological role remains a mystery.
