Leucas stricta var. clarkei

Leucas stricta var. clarkei, commonly known as the Clarke's leucas, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
