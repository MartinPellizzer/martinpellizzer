Lavenia decumbens

Lavenia decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate lavenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its sprawling, decumbent stems that lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or scrubland habitats.

Lavenia decumbens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
