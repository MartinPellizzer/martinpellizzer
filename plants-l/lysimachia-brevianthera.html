Lysimachia brevianthera

Lysimachia brevianthera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved yellow-eyed grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
