Lactuca × denticulatoplatyphylla

Lactuca × denticulatoplatyphylla, commonly known as the curly endive, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its loosely curled, tender leaves that are typically harvested before they fully mature. The plant has a rosette growth habit, with leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The botanical features of this plant make it a popular choice for gourmet salads and culinary applications.
