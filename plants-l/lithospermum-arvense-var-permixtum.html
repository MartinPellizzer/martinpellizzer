Lithospermum arvense var. permixtum

Lithospermum arvense var. permixtum, commonly known as the mixed field lithosperm, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features slender, branching stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, pink to purple flowers are borne in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

The species is characterized by its medicinal properties, with roots containing compounds used in traditional herbal remedies.
