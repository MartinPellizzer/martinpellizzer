Laserpitium luteolum

Laserpitium luteolum, commonly known as yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Laserpitium luteolum is often considered a weed in agricultural areas due to its aggressive growth habit.
