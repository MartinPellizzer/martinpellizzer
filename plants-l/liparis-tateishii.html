Liparis tateishii

Liparis tateishii, commonly known as the Japanese marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan.

It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often near water bodies. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a distinct three-lobed lip.

Liparis tateishii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with moist soil.
