Ligularia angusta

Ligularia angusta, commonly known as narrowleaf ligularia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive narrow leaf blade. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a spike, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in shady gardens.
