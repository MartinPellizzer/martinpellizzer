Lonicera subspicata var. subspicata

Lonicera subspicata var. subspicata, commonly known as the red honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular red flowers in the spring, which are attractive to pollinators. Its berries are orange-red and contain seeds, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

It thrives in partial to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.
