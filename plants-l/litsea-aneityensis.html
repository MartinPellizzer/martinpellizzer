Litsea aneityensis

Litsea aneityensis, commonly known as the broadleaf litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, white flowers followed by aromatic, egg-shaped berries.

Litsea aneityensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
