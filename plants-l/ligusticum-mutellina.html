Ligusticum mutellina

Its flowers are small and white, arranged in umbrella-shaped inflorescences, and it produces small, dry fruits known as schizocarps.

Ligusticum mutellina is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal remedies for digestive and respiratory ailments.
