Ligustrum fengjieense

Ligustrum fengjieense, commonly known as the Fengjie privet, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Sichuan province, and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by dark blue berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.

Ligustrum fengjieense is also valued for its role in ecological restoration projects.
