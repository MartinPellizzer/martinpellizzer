Litsea playfairii

Litsea playfairii, commonly known as the Chinese tallowtree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its opposite, simple, and often aromatic leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange to red berries that contain several seeds. It is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
