Lupinus elegans var. elegans

Lupinus elegans var. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant lupine, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of vibrant blue to violet pea-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Lupinus elegans var. elegans plays an important role in ecological systems by providing nectar for pollinators and supporting local wildlife.
