Lotus torulosus

Lotus torulosus, commonly known as the crested lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or red flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically floating or submerged, depending on the water conditions. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Lotus torulosus thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, making it a popular ornamental plant in ponds and water gardens.
