Leersia denudata

Leersia denudata, commonly known as the slender leersia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland understories and wetlands.

Leersia denudata is often found in riparian zones and is adapted to periodic flooding.
