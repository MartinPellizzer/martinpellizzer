Linaria heterophylla f. gattefossei

Linaria heterophylla f. gattefossei, commonly known as the Gattefossei toadflax, is a compact, mat-forming perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has finely divided, grass-like leaves that are aromatic when crushed, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant color make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
