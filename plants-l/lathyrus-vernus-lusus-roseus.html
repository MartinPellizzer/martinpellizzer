Lathyrus vernus lusus roseus

Lathyrus vernus lusus roseus, commonly known as the pink-flowered vernal pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a perennial vine that produces delicate, pea-like flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in woodland and hedgerow environments. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
