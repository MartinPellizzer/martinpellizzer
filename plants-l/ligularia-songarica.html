Ligularia songarica

Ligularia songarica, commonly known as the Songarica arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base, often adorned with a prominent white or yellow vein. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water garden enthusiasts.
