Lithocardium villicaule

Lithocardium villicaule, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing form. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Lithocardium villicaule is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its resilience and unique appearance.
