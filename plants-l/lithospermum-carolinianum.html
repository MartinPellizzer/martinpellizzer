Lithospermum carolinianum

Lithospermum carolinianum, commonly known as Carolina ginseng, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, ovate leaves with slightly serrated edges. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional herbal medicine.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
