Lissochilus scriptus

Lissochilus scriptus, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, sinuous leaf patterns that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers are small and delicate, often appearing in clusters with a subtle fragrance.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, Lissochilus scriptus is considered a rare and endangered species.
