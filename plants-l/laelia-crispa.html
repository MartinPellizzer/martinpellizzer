Laelia crispa

Laelia crispa, commonly known as the crinkled orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, branched stems and numerous pendulous flowers. The plant features showy, fragrant blooms that range in color from white to pink and violet. Laelia crispa thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses and gardens.
