Laurus hundensis

Laurus hundensis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and dark green, with a fragrant scent when crushed.

Laurus hundensis produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue berries that are edible.
