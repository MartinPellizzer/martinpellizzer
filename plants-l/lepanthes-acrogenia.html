Lepanthes acrogenia

Lepanthes acrogenia, commonly known as the Acrogenia Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, adapted for capturing moisture from the air.

Lepanthes acrogenia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
