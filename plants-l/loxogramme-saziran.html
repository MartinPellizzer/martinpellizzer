Loxogramme saziran

Loxogramme saziran, commonly known as the Saziran water clover, is a unique aquatic fern native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in marshy areas or along the edges of ponds.
