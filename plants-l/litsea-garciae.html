Litsea garciae

Litsea garciae, commonly known as the Guangxi litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to southern China, particularly Guangxi and Guangdong provinces, as well as northern Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a drupe, typically containing a single seed, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Litsea garciae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential in ecological restoration projects.
