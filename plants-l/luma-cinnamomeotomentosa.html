Luma cinnamomeotomentosa

Luma cinnamomeotomentosa, commonly known as the cinnamon-leafed luma, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
