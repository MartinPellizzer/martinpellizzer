Lysionotus microphyllus var. microphyllus

Lysionotus microphyllus var. microphyllus, commonly known as the small-leafed lychnis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial with small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically less than 1 centimeter in length. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact size and delicate flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
