Linociera ramiflora f. caudatifolia

Linociera ramiflora f. caudatifolia, commonly known as the trailing linociera, is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its trailing growth habit and dense foliage.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white, bell-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a delicate floral display. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its unique trailing form and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
