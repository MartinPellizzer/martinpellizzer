Lychnis triflora f. glabra

Lychnis triflora f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-flowered carnation, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of small, pink or white flowers with five petals. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered with a fine, smooth texture, distinguishing it from other forms of Lychnis triflora. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
