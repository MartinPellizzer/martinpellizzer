Lycoris × jacksoniana

Lycoris × jacksoniana, commonly known as the Rain Lily or Chinese Everlasting, is a hybrid perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features striking red or pink flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and unique foliage.
