Lightfootia glomerata

Lightfootia glomerata, commonly known as the clustered lightfoot.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, sunny environment.

Lightfootia glomerata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance and unique flower structure.
