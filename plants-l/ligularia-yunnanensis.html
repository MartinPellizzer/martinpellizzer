Ligularia yunnanensis

Ligularia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often green with a metallic sheen, and it produces showy yellow flowers in the summer. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in forest understories. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.

Ligularia yunnanensis is also noted for its ability to tolerate a range of soil types, making it a versatile choice for gardeners.
