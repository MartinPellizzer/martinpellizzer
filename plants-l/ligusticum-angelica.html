Ligusticum angelica

Ligusticum angelica, commonly known as love-in-a-mist, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, feathery leaves and clusters of pale blue or white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided foliage and is often grown for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Ligusticum angelica is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
