Lepargyrea argentea

Lepargyrea argentea, commonly known as silverleaf acanthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant features showy, spire-like inflorescences with purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature contribute to its appeal in both cultivated and natural settings.
