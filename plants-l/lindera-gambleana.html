Lindera gambleana

Lindera gambleana, commonly known as Chinese wild ginger, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features aromatic, narrow elliptic leaves with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during early spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and seasonal color changes.
