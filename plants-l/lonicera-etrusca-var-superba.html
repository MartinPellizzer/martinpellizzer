Lonicera etrusca var. superba

Lonicera etrusca var. superba, commonly known as the Etruscan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a small, red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
