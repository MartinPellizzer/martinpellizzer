Lobelia assurgens subsp. santa-clarae

santa-clarae, commonly known as Santa Clara lobelia, is a rare and endemic species native to the Santa Clara Valley in California. It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, upright stems and small, tubular blue to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and riparian areas. Its foliage consists of opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture.
