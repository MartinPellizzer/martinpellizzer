Liparis loeselii var. lutosa

Liparis loeselii var. lutosa, commonly known as the Lutosa orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.

Its flowering period usually occurs during the spring and early summer months.
