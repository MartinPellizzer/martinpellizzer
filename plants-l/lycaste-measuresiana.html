Lycaste measuresiana

Lycaste measuresiana, commonly known as the Mexican night-blooming orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, white flowers that open at night and close in the morning. The plant has long, slender pseudobulbs and arching, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Lycaste measuresiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique nocturnal blooming habit.
