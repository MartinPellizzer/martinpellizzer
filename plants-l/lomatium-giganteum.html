Lomatium giganteum

Lomatium giganteum, commonly known as the giant lomatium, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and large, fern-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Lomatium giganteum is valued for its ecological role and has been used traditionally by Indigenous peoples for its medicinal properties.
