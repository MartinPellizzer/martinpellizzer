Lemaireocereus standleyi

Lemaireocereus standleyi, commonly known as the Star of Bethlehem cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant features a globular or cylindrical stem covered in a thick, waxy coat that helps it retain moisture. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, appearing in late winter or early spring.

This cactus is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
