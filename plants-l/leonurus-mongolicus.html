Leonurus mongolicus

Leonurus mongolicus, commonly known as Mongolian hedgehog.

It is a flowering plant native to Central Asia, particularly found in regions of Mongolia, China, and Russia. The plant is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are typically opposite and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Leonurus mongolicus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
