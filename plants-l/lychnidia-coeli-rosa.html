Lychnidia coeli-rosa

Lychnidia coeli-rosa, commonly known as the celestial rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that produces showy, pinkish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The botanical name reflects its ethereal appearance, reminiscent of a rose in the sky.
