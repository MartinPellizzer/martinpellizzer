Lysimachia febrifuga

Lysimachia febrifuga, commonly known as Chinese goldthread, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its creeping rhizomes and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Lysimachia febrifuga is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
