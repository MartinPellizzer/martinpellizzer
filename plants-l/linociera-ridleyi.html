Linociera ridleyi

Linociera ridleyi, commonly known as the Malayan white wood, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and alternate, simple leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in yellowish-green cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Linociera ridleyi is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
