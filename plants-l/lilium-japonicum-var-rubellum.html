Lilium japonicum var. rubellum

Lilium japonicum var. rubellum, commonly known as the red-flowered Japanese lily, is a striking variant of the Japanese lily species.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals that contrast with its green, strap-like leaves. This perennial plant is native to Japan and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. The flowers typically bloom in summer, adding a bold splash of color to gardens.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticulture.
