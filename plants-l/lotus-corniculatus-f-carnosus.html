Lotus corniculatus f. carnosus

Lotus corniculatus f. carnosus, commonly known as the fleshy-leaved bird's-foot trefoil, is a variant of the common bird's-foot trefoil.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that distinguish it from the typical form of the species. This plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. The flowers are yellow and consist of a central ovary with five spreading petals.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.
