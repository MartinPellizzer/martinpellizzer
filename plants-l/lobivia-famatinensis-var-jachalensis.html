Lobivia famatinensis var. jachalensis

Lobivia famatinensis var. jachalensis, commonly known as the Jachal Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant features spiny, ribbed stems that store water, typical of desert-adapted species. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in spring.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
