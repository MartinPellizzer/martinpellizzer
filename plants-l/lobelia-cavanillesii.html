Lobelia cavanillesii

Lobelia cavanillesii, commonly known as the Mexican bluebell, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
