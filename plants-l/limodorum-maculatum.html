Limodorum maculatum

Limodorum maculatum, commonly known as the spotted lily or the fragrant lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or white with a distinctive spotted pattern on the petals. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Limodorum maculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its subtle, sweet fragrance.
