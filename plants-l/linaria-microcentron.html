Linaria microcentron

Linaria microcentron, commonly known as the narrowleaf toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Linaria microcentron is often found in rocky soils and open habitats, such as hillsides and coastal areas.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
