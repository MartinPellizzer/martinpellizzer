Lorostemon stipitatus

Lorostemon stipitatus, commonly known as the white stilted lily, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
