Laburnum anagyroides

Laburnum anagyroides, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of 10-20 small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Laburnum anagyroides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
