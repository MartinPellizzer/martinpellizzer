Leucadendron comosum subsp. comosum

Leucadendron comosum subsp.

comosum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its distinctive silver-blue foliage and striking cone-like inflorescences.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
