Lonchocarpus macrocarpus var. sericophylla

Lonchocarpus macrocarpus var. sericophylla, commonly known as the silky pod senna, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
