Lotus unifoliolatus

Lotus unifoliolatus, commonly known as the single leaf lotus, is a rare and unique species within the legume family.

It is characterized by its singular, palmate leaf structure, which sets it apart from other lotus species. The plant typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats, such as ponds and marshes, where it thrives in sunny conditions. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with a delicate appearance that adds to its ornamental value.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a species of conservation concern in certain regions.
