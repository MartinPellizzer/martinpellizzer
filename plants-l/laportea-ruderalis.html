Laportea ruderalis

Laportea ruderalis, commonly known as the roughleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and waste areas. The plant features upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. Its leaves are palmately lobed and have a rough texture, contributing to its common name.

Laportea ruderalis is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to stabilize soil in ecological restoration projects.
