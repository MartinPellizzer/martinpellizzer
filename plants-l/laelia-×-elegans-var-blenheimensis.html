Laelia × elegans var. blenheimensis

Laelia × elegans var. blenheimensis, commonly known as the Blenheim orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of fragrant, long-lasting flowers. The plant typically produces showy, brightly colored blooms in shades of pink, white, and red, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in cool, bright environments with high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
