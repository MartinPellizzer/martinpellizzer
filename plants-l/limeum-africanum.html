Limeum africanum

Limeum africanum, commonly known as the African lime tree, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters and are followed by spherical, green fruits that turn yellow when mature.

The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy and resilient species.
