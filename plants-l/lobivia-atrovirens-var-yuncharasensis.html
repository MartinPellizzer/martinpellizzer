Lobivia atrovirens var. yuncharasensis

Lobivia atrovirens var. yuncharasensis, commonly known as the Yunchara Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a rounded body with prominent, ribbed stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is well adapted to dry conditions, with spines that help reduce water loss and provide protection from herbivores.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
