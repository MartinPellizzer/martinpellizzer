Lissochilus endlichianus

Lissochilus endlichianus, commonly known as the Endlich's lissochilus, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the high Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Bolivia and Peru. The plant is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests at elevations above 3,000 meters.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
