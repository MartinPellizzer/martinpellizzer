Lygodium giganteum

Lygodium giganteum, commonly known as the giant water fern, is a tropical fern belonging to the family Lygodiaceae.

It is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant thrives in wet, shady environments, often found near water bodies in its native range. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that release spores for propagation.

The plant's unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an important species in aquatic and riparian ecosystems.
