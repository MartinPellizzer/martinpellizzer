Lawrencia helmsii

Lawrencia helmsii, commonly known as the Helms' rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and pungent scent. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats, often in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.
