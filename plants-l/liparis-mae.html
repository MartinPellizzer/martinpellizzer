Liparis mae

Liparis mae, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air, a feature common in epiphytic orchids.

Liparis mae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
