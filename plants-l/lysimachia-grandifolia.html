Lysimachia grandifolia

Lysimachia grandifolia, commonly known as the largeleaf chickweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Lysimachia grandifolia is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
