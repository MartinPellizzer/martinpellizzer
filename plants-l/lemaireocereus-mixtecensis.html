Lemaireocereus mixtecensis

Lemaireocereus mixtecensis, commonly known as the Mixtec cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, green stems and sparse, spiny areoles. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought and intense sunlight. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, appearing in clusters during the warmer months.

This cactus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens due to its hardy nature.
