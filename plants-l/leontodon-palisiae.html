Leontodon palisiae

Leontodon palisiae, commonly known as the Palisade hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its yellow flowers are surrounded by pale green ray florets, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in disturbed areas and is considered a rare species in some regions.
