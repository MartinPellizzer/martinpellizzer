Lathyrus gouanii

Lathyrus gouanii, commonly known as the mountain bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is a perennial herb that produces striking blue to violet flowers in early spring. The plant has slender, wiry stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

Lathyrus gouanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
