Linaria bipartita subsp. linogrisea

Linaria bipartita subsp.

linogrisea, commonly known as the grey-green toadflax, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive grey-green hue.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers with two lips, typical of the Scrophulariaceae family. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and gravelly soils.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and wildflower displays.
