Lotus diffusus

Lotus diffusus, commonly known as the broadleaf lotus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
