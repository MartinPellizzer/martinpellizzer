Leiophyllum

Leiophyllum, commonly known as the smoothleaf mistletoe, is a parasitic plant belonging to the Viscaraceae family.

It grows on the branches of various host trees, primarily in the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. Its berries, which are usually red or orange, contain seeds that are dispersed by birds.
