Limbarda japonica

Limbarda japonica, commonly known as Japanese spurge, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white or yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.

Limbarda japonica is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
