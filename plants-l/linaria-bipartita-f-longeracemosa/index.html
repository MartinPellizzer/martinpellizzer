Linaria Bipartita F. Longeracemosa

Linaria bipartita f. longeracemosa is a striking variant of the common toadflax plant known for its elongated, cascading flower clusters. This unique form showcases a more extended inflorescence compared to the standard variety, making it a prized addition to gardens and botanical collections. Its vibrant yellow-orange flowers bloom in late spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape. The plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soils with ample sunlight. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts alike.
