Leandra strigilliflora var. australis

Leandra strigilliflora var. australis, commonly known as the Australian leandra, is a rare and striking plant native to the rainforests of eastern Australia.

The plant produces showy, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual element to its natural habitat.
