Linaria rubioides

Linaria rubioides, commonly known as the red sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces striking clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Linaria rubioides thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
