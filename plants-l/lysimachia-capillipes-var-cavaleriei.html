Lysimachia capillipes var. cavaleriei

Lysimachia capillipes var. cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese threadleaf speedwell, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, creeping stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
