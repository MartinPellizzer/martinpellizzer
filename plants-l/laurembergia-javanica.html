Laurembergia javanica

Laurembergia javanica, commonly known as the Javanese laurel, is a species of flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy berries.

Laurembergia javanica is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses.
