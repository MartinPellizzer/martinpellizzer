Lantana rosea

Lantana rosea, commonly known as the rose lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and usually have a slightly hairy texture.

Lantana rosea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
