Lagerstroemia speciosa subsp. intermedia

Lagerstroemia speciosa subsp.

intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its showy, pink to red bract-like flowers that appear in late spring to early summer.

The plant has smooth, greyish-brown bark that becomes fissured with age, and its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adaptable to a range of soil types.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly planted in gardens and urban landscapes.
