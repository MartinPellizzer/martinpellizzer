Laelia × marriottiana

Laelia × marriottiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Miltonia species.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant typically produces compact, upright pseudobulbs with lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are showy, with a variety of colors including white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under proper conditions.
