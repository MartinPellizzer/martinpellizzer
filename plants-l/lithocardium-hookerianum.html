Lithocardium hookerianum

Lithocardium hookerianum, commonly known as the Hooker's lithocardium, is a perennial shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

Lithocardium hookerianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
