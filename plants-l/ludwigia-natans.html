Ludwigia natans

Ludwigia natans, commonly known as water spiderwort, is an aquatic plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its floating leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that emerge from the water surface. Ludwigia natans thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

It is often used in aquariums due to its ornamental value and ability to oxygenate water.
