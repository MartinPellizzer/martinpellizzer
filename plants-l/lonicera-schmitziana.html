Lonicera schmitziana

Lonicera schmitziana, commonly known as Schmitz's honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

Lonicera schmitziana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
