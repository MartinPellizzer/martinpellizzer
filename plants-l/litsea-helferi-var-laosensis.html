Litsea helferi var. laosensis

Litsea helferi var. laosensis, commonly known as the Laos litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Laos and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and a distinctively fragrant scent. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by dark blue berries.

This species is often valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
