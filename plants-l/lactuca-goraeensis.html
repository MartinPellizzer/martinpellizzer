Lactuca goraeensis

Lactuca goraeensis, commonly known as the Gora lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus, particularly in Georgia and Turkey. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. It features basal rosettes of dark green, spoon-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in the summer.

Lactuca goraeensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
