Lepanthes neillii

Lepanthes neillii, commonly known as the Neill's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, showy flowers with intricate patterns and a wide range of colors. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Lepanthes neillii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
