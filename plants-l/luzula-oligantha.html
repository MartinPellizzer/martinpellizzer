Luzula oligantha

Luzula oligantha, commonly known as slender woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

Luzula oligantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
