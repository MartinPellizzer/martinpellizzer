Linosyris vulgaris var. patulus

Linosyris vulgaris var. patulus, commonly known as the slender linosyris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to xeriscapes and native plant gardens.
