Lobelia volkensii var. ulugurensis

Lobelia volkensii var. ulugurensis, commonly known as the Uluguru lobelia, is a striking flowering plant native to the Uluguru Mountains in Tanzania.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of bright blue to purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, thriving in humid and cool conditions. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the local ecosystem by supporting pollinators.
