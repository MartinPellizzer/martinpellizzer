Lythrum argyi

Lythrum argyi, commonly known as Chinese rose or purple loosestrife, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spikes, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite on the stems, and it typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. Lythrum argyi is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
