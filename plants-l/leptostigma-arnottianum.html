Leptostigma arnottianum

Leptostigma arnottianum, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its most striking feature is the bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
