Lycaste zacapana

Lycaste zacapana, commonly known as the Zacapan lycaste, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are usually borne on a single stem and are known for their elegance and delicate structure.

Lycaste zacapana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique botanical characteristics.
