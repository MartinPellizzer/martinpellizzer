Limniris sibirica

Limniris sibirica, commonly known as Siberian water lily.

It is a perennial aquatic plant native to the colder regions of Asia, particularly Siberia. The plant features broad, floating leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with showy petals that attract pollinators.

Limniris sibirica plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various water organisms.
