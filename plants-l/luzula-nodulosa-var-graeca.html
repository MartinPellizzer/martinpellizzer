Luzula nodulosa var. graeca

Luzula nodulosa var. graeca, commonly known as the Greek sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses and sedges, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, spike-like flowers that bloom in early summer.
