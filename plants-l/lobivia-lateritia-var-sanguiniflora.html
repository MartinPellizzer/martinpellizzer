Lobivia lateritia var. sanguiniflora

Lobivia lateritia var. sanguiniflora, commonly known as the Bloody Flower Lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, adapting to its harsh environment. The plant produces vibrant red flowers, which contrast strikingly with its green foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
