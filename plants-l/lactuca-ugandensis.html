Lactuca ugandensis

Lactuca ugandensis, commonly known as Uganda lettuce, is a species of lettuce native to East Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant with upright stems and alternate leaves that are typically lobed or serrated. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster at the top of the stem. It is characterized by its milky sap, which is a common feature among the Lactuca genus.

This species is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
