Leucospermum calocephalum

Leucospermum calocephalum, commonly known as the firewheel plant, is a flowering shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white powder. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
