Lastrea brassii

Lastrea brassii, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
