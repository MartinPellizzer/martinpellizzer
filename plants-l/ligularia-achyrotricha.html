Ligularia achyrotricha

Ligularia achyrotricha, commonly known as the narrowleaf ligularia, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a serrated margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
