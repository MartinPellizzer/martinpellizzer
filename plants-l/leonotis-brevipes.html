Leonotis brevipes

Leonotis brevipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lion's tail, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its unique appearance and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
