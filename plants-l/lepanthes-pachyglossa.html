Lepanthes pachyglossa

Lepanthes pachyglossa, commonly known as the broad-lipped orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as cloud forests. Its flowers feature a distinctive, broad lip that is often brightly colored.

Lepanthes pachyglossa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
