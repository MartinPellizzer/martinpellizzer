Lachenalia arbuthnotiae

Lachenalia arbuthnotiae, commonly known as the Cape Town lachenalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces tall, branched stems bearing numerous bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Lachenalia arbuthnotiae is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
