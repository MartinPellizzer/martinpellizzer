Lotodes andongense

Lotodes andongense, commonly known as the Andong water lily, is a rare aquatic plant native to the Andong region of South Korea.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves and showy white flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are typically rounded with a waxy surface, and its flowers have a unique structure with multiple petals and a central cluster of stamens.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it is considered an endangered species requiring conservation efforts.
