Lonicera ferdinandi f. franchetii

Lonicera ferdinandi f. franchetii, commonly known as the Franchetii honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late spring. Its fruits are red berries that mature in autumn, attracting various bird species.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to different soil types.
