Liparis Petricola

Liparis petricola is a rare and delicate orchid species native to rocky and mountainous regions. It thrives in harsh environments where few plants can survive. This small, terrestrial orchid is often found growing on stone or in crevices. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered plant from habitat loss.
