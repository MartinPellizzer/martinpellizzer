Lantana mollissima

Lantana mollissima, commonly known as silky lantana, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring dense, compact growth. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the summer. Its leaves are soft and fuzzy, giving it a distinctive, almost silken texture.

Lantana mollissima is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
