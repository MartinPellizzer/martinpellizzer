Lonchocarpus broadwayi

Lonchocarpus broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes many legumes known for their nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Its pods are typically elongated and contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.
