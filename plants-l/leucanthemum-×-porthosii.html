Leucanthemum × porthosii

Leucanthemum × porthosii, commonly known as the Shasta daisy, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features showy, white ray flowers surrounding a central disk, giving it a daisy-like appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is drought-tolerant once established.

Leucanthemum × porthosii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
