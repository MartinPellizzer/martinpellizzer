Lantana involucrata f. rubella

Lantana involucrata f. rubella, commonly known as the red-leaved lantana, is a compact, flowering shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that typically range from yellow to orange in color, often with a reddish tinge. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant foliage make it an attractive addition to landscapes.
