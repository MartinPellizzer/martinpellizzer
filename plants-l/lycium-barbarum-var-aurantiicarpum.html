Lycium barbarum var. aurantiicarpum

Lycium barbarum var. aurantiicarpum, commonly known as the red-fruited boxthorn, is a variant of the Chinese boxthorn plant.

It is a shrub native to parts of Asia, characterized by its thorny stems and small, oval leaves. The plant produces bright orange-red berries that are rich in antioxidants and bioactive compounds. These berries are often used in traditional medicine and as a food supplement.

Its botanical features make it distinct from the standard Lycium barbarum species.
