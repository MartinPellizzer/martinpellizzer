Lespedeza dielsiana

Lespedeza dielsiana, commonly known as Chinese clover, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Lespedeza dielsiana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
