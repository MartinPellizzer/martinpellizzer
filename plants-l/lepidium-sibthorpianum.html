Lepidium sibthorpianum

Lepidium sibthorpianum, commonly known as the Sibthorp's cress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, green, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in moist, temperate regions and is often found in mountainous areas.

Lepidium sibthorpianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
