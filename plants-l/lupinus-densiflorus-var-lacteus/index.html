Lupinus Densiflorus Var. Lacteus

Lupinus densiflorus var. lacteus is a striking member of the legume family known for its dense clusters of soft white flowers. This variety is native to the western united states and thrives in rocky and coastal environments. its foliage consists of narrow, compound leaves that provide a lush green contrast to its blooms. the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. due to its beauty and ecological benefits, it is often cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
