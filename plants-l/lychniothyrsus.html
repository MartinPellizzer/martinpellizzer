Lychniothyrsus

Lychniothyrsus, commonly known as the nightshade or false nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia and typically grows in moist, shady environments. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually purple or violet in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains small, toxic seeds, making it important to handle with care.
