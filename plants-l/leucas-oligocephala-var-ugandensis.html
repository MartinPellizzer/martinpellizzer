Leucas oligocephala var. ugandensis

Leucas oligocephala var. ugandensis, commonly known as the Uganda leucas, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or semi-deciduous habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
