Lampranthus prominulus

Lampranthus prominulus, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail" or "Rainbow Plant," is a striking member of the Aizoaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade. It is a succulent, capable of storing water in its fleshy leaves, making it drought-tolerant.

Its unique appearance and ability to bloom prolifically make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
