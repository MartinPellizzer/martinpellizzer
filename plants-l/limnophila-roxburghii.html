Limnophila roxburghii

It is characterized by its creeping, herbaceous growth habit and is often found in wetland environments such as marshes and stream banks. Limnophila roxburghii is adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic conditions, with rhizomes that help it spread and colonize new areas.
