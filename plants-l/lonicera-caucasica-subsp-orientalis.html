Lonicera caucasica subsp. orientalis

Lonicera caucasica subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the Caucasian honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region. It features opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy.

The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The subspecies is closely related to other honeysuckle species and shares similar growth habits.
