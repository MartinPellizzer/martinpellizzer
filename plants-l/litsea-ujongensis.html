Litsea ujongensis

Litsea ujongensis, commonly known as the Bornean-laurel, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel, avocado, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by spherical, dark purple berries.

Litsea ujongensis is often found in lowland and montane forests, contributing to the biodiversity of its habitat.
