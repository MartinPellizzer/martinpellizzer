Lidbeckia lobata

Lidbeckia lobata, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces showy yellow flowers with a distinctive cup-like structure. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Lidbeckia lobata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
