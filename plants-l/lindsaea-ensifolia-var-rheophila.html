Lindsaea ensifolia var. rheophila

Lindsaea ensifolia var. rheophila, commonly known as the stream lindsaea, is a rare epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically growing on tree trunks or rocks in humid forests. The plant features delicate, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its reproductive structures are borne on specialized leaf-like bracts, and it produces small, greenish spore-bearing structures.

Due to its limited habitat and specific environmental requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
