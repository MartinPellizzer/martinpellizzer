Lissochilus streptopetalus

Lissochilus streptopetalus, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, twisted floral structure. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. Its flowers are small, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

Lissochilus streptopetalus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unusual appearance.
