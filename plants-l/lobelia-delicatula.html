Lobelia delicatula

Lobelia delicatula, commonly known as the slender lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, erect stems that bear small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple. The plant has finely divided, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Lobelia delicatula is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
