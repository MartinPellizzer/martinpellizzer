Ligularia oligonema

Ligularia oligonema, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ligularia, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often mottled with silver or gray veins. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to shaded gardens. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in water gardens or as a border plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for woodland and bog gardens.
