Ligularia cymbulifera

Ligularia cymbulifera, commonly known as the broadleaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tint on the underside. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in the summer, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Ligularia cymbulifera is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
