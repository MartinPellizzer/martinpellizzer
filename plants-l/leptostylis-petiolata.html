Leptostylis petiolata

Leptostylis petiolata, commonly known as the slender-styled pteris, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds with a slender, elongated rachis. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a smooth, leathery texture.

Leptostylis petiolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.
