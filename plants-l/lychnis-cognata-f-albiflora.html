Lychnis cognata f. albiflora

Lychnis cognata f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered redstem, is a horticultural form of the common lychnis.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its dense, mat-forming habit and vibrant red stems. The foliage is typically dark green, and the flowers are small, clustered in umbels, with a striking white coloration. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens, particularly for its contrasting colors and compact growth.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively easy to maintain, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
