Leontodon tenorei

Leontodon tenorei, commonly known as the Tenore's bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, with a bell-shaped appearance.
