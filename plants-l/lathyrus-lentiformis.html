Lathyrus lentiformis

Lathyrus lentiformis, commonly known as the broadleaf pea or lentil pea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to violet, followed by pods containing edible seeds.

Lathyrus lentiformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
