Limnosipanea schomburgkii

Limnosipanea schomburgkii, commonly known as the Schomburgk's water lily, is a species of aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.

The species is notable for its ecological importance in maintaining water quality and supporting biodiversity in its native habitats.
