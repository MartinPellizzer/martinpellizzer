Lythrum thymifolia var. erecta

Lythrum thymifolia var. erecta, commonly known as upright lythrum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, covered with dense, soft hairs. The plant produces spikes of showy, pink to purple flowers, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped, serrated, and arranged alternately along the stem.

Lythrum thymifolia var. erecta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils in full sun.
