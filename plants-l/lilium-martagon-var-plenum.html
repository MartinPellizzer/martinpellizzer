Lilium martagon var. plenum

Lilium martagon var. plenum, commonly known as the full-flowered Turk's cap lily, is a variant of the Turk's cap lily native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, recurved flowers that are typically pink to red in color. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and grows as a herbaceous perennial. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
