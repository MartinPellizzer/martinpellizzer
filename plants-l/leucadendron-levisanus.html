Leucadendron levisanus

Leucadendron levisanus, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Cape Province of South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the season. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Leucadendron levisanus is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in garden landscapes.
