Logania vaginalis var. laxior

Logania vaginalis var. laxior, commonly known as the broadleaf logania, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments.

It is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive floral display.
