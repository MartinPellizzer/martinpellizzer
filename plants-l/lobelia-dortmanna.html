Lobelia dortmanna

Lobelia dortmanna, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are tubular and usually blue or violet, arranged in dense spikes.

Lobelia dortmanna is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in wet conditions.
