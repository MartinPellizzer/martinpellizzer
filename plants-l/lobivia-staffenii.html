Lobivia staffenii

Lobivia staffenii, commonly known as the Desert Rose, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features compact, branching stems with thick, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Lobivia staffenii is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
