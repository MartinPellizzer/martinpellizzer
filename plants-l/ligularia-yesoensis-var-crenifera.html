Ligularia yesoensis var. crenifera

Ligularia yesoensis var. crenifera, commonly known as the Japanese ligularia, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy edge, which give it its scientific name "crenifera," meaning "crenated.". The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in the summer, which are clustered in a spire-like inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and is a popular choice for water gardens and shaded borders.
