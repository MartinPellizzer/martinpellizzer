Ligularia hodgsonii var. pulchella

Ligularia hodgsonii var. pulchella, commonly known as the purple-leaved ligularia, is a striking perennial plant native to the Himalayas.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple hue on the upper surface and a lighter green underside. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in the summer, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to garden conditions.
