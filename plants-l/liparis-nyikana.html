Liparis nyikana

Liparis nyikana, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, creeping rhizome and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or rocky outcrops. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base, while the flowers are arranged in a spike.

Liparis nyikana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral structure.
