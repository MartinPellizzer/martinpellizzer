Limonium narynense

Limonium narynense, commonly known as the Naryn Skyflower, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Plumbagaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. Limonium narynense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
