Lygos raetam var. bovei

Lygos raetam var. bovei, commonly known as the Redstem lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive red stems and showy, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
