Lysimachia violascens var. violascens

Lysimachia violascens var. violascens, commonly known as violetleaf mosquito plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in wetland habitats.
