Lonchocarpus neurophyllus var. neurophyllus

Lonchocarpus neurophyllus var. neurophyllus, commonly known as the yellow spurred legume, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive pods containing flat, yellow seeds that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and pharmacological research.
