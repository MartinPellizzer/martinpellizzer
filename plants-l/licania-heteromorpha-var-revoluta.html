Licania heteromorpha var. revoluta

Licania heteromorpha var. revoluta, commonly known as the scarlet balsa, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by distinctive, winged fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
