Luffa batesii

Luffa batesii, commonly known as the African horned luffa, is a tropical plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and gourds. The plant produces large, lobed leaves and distinctive, spiny fruits that mature to a pale yellow or orange color. The fruit's inner fibrous material becomes spongy and is used as a natural sponge after drying.

It is valued for its versatility in both culinary and cosmetic applications.
