Limeum africanum subsp. canescens

Limeum africanum subsp.

canescens, commonly known as the African lime or African hibiscus, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy and greyish in appearance.

The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. Its fruits are capsules that release small, hairy seeds when mature.

This species is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
