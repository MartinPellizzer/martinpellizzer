Lasiandra regnellii

Lasiandra regnellii, commonly known as the silverleaf lantana, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in shrubland and coastal habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in regions with mild, temperate climates.

Lasiandra regnellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
