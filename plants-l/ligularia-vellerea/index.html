Ligularia Vellerea

Velvet ligularia, scientifically known as Ligularia vellerea, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. Its lush, velvety foliage and vibrant yellow flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and shaded landscapes. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it ideal for woodland gardens or boggy areas. With its unique texture and color, it adds a dramatic visual element to any garden setting. Its resilience and aesthetic appeal have earned it a place among the most sought-after shade-loving plants.
