Lepidotis dendroidea

Lepidotis dendroidea, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like red flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. The tree has a broad, spreading canopy and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a leathery texture.

The plant is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and thrives in well-drained soils.
