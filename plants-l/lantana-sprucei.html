Lantana sprucei

Lantana sprucei, commonly known as the Hawaiian lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, depending on the variety. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lantana sprucei thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adapted to the tropical and subtropical climates of its native habitat.
