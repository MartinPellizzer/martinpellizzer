Leandra Sulfurea

Leandra sulfurea is a rare and striking plant known for its vivid yellow flowers and unique growth patterns. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its name, which translates to "sulfur yellow," reflects the intense color of its blooms. This plant is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists for its beauty and ecological significance. Despite its allure, Leandra sulfurea remains a challenging species to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
