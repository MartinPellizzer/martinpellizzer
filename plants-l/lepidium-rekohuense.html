Lepidium rekohuense

Lepidium rekohuense, commonly known as the Rekohuena cress, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many mustard and cress species. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
