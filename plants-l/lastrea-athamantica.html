Lastrea athamantica

Lastrea athamantica, commonly known as the Cape Flats daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is adapted to dry conditions.

Lastrea athamantica is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
