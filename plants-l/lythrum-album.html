Lythrum album

Lythrum album, commonly known as white lythrum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family, which includes other flowering plants such as the poinsettia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of pale pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lythrum album is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in wet meadows.
