Latouria muscifera

Latouria muscifera, commonly known as the musk orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its unique scent, reminiscent of musk, is a distinguishing feature that attracts specific pollinators.

Latouria muscifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its delicate appearance.
