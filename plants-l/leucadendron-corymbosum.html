Leucadendron corymbosum

Leucadendron corymbosum, commonly known as the silver leaf candle bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and striking, cone-shaped yellow flowers that appear in winter. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and attractive foliage, which provide year-round interest.

It is also valued for its resilience and low maintenance requirements in garden settings.
