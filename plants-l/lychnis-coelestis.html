Lychnis coelestis

Lychnis coelestis, commonly known as skyblue star, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, star-shaped blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, branching stems and heart-shaped leaves with toothed margins. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Lychnis coelestis is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
