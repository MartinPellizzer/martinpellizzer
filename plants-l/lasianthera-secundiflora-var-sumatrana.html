Lasianthera secundiflora var. sumatrana

Lasianthera secundiflora var. sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its habitat. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests or along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in tropical regions.
