Lonicera menelii

Lonicera menelii, commonly known as the Menelii honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically gray-green in color and have a fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white, which bloom in early spring. Its berries are red and edible, often attracting wildlife.

Lonicera menelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
