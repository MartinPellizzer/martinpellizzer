Limonium besserianum

Limonium besserianum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers and silver-green foliage. The plant thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, often growing in saline environments. Its flowers bloom in late summer and early autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Limonium besserianum is widely cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
