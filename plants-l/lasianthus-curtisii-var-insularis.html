Lasianthus curtisii var. insularis

Lasianthus curtisii var. insularis, commonly known as the Island Lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth, glossy surface.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
