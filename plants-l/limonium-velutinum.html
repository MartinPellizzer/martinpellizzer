Limonium velutinum

Limonium velutinum, commonly known as velvet limonium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry grasslands.

Limonium velutinum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
