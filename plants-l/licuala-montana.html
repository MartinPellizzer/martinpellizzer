Licuala montana

Licuala montana, commonly known as the mountain palm, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland and hill forest areas.

The fruit of Licuala montana is a drupe that contains a single seed, making it an important species for both ecological and potential horticultural use.
