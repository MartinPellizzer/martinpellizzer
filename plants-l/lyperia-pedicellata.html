Lyperia pedicellata

Lyperia pedicellata, commonly known as the slender lyperia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the stems are often hairy.

Lyperia pedicellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
