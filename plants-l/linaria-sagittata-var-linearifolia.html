Linaria sagittata var. linearifolia

Linaria sagittata var. linearifolia, commonly known as the slender arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are often arranged in a zigzag pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow-orange flowers in late spring to early summer, which are borne on slender, branching stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in temperate climates.
