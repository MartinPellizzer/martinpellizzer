Lacinaria cylindracea var. solitaria

Lacinaria cylindracea var. solitaria, commonly known as the solitary cylinder lichen, is a foliose lichen characterized by its thin, leaf-like thallus.

It typically grows on bark, stone, or soil in temperate forest environments. The plant has a pale to dark green or brown coloration, with a smooth or slightly wrinkled surface. It lacks distinct lobes and has a simple, elongated structure.

This variety is often found in shaded, moist habitats and is relatively uncommon in most regions.
