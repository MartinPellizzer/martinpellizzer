Lantana × strigocamara

Lantana × strigocamara, commonly known as the shrub lantana, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between *Lantana camara* and *Lantana indica*.

It is a flowering shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, often aromatic foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, usually in shades of yellow, orange, or red, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are generally narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

Lantana × strigocamara is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes.
