Linaria insularis

Linaria insularis, commonly known as the island toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb that typically reaches a height of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Linaria insularis is adapted to rocky and coastal environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
