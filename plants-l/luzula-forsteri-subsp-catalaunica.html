Luzula forsteri subsp. catalaunica

Luzula forsteri subsp.

catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalaunica woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae. It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces small, greenish-yellow inflorescences.

This subspecies is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has fibrous roots and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant is adapted to temperate climates and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
