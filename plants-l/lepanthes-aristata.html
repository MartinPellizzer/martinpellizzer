Lepanthes aristata

Lepanthes aristata, commonly known as the aristate orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often feature bright colors and intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as montane forests. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Lepanthes aristata is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
