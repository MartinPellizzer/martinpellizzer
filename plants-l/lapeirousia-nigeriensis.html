Lapeirousia nigeriensis

Lapeirousia nigeriensis, commonly known as the Nigerien orchid, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Niger.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a mix of yellow and purple hues. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
