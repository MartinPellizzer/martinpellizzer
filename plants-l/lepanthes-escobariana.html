Lepanthes escobariana

Lepanthes escobariana, commonly known as the Escobaria orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Lepanthes escobariana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
