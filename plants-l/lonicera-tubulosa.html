Lonicera tubulosa

Lonicera tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. These flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
