Loxogramme antrophyoides var. rheophila

Loxogramme antrophyoides var. rheophila, commonly known as the water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to tropical regions.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and wetlands. The plant has finely divided, delicate fronds that are typically submerged or floating. Its rhizomes form dense mats, helping to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

It plays an important ecological role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and small aquatic life.
