Laelia × turneri

Laelia × turneri, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its vibrant and long-lasting blooms.

It is a hybrid between Laelia and Miltonia, resulting in a plant that combines the hardiness of Laelia with the colorful flowers of Miltonia. The plant typically produces showy, semi-closed flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Laelia × turneri is popular among orchid growers for its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
