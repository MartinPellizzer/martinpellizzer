Lavatera thuringiaca var. palmata

Lavatera thuringiaca var. palmata, commonly known as the palmate lavatera, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features palmately lobed leaves that are often hairy and have a silvery-white underside. The plant produces showy, fluffy white or pink flower clusters that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its unique foliage and delicate, airy blooms.
