Lonicera caprifolium italica

Lonicera caprifolium italica, commonly known as the Italian honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a small, red berry that ripens in the fall.

Lonicera caprifolium italica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
