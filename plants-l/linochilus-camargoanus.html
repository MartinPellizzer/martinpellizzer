Linochilus camargoanus

Linochilus camargoanus, commonly known as the Camargo's linochilus, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically found in regions of Brazil and Peru.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family, which includes over 25,000 species of orchids. This species is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
