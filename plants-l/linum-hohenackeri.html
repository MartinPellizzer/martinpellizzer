Linum hohenackeri

Linum hohenackeri, commonly known as Hohenacker's flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flax, and is characterized by its delicate blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in racemes.

Linum hohenackeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
