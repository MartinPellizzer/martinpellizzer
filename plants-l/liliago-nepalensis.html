Liliago nepalensis

Liliago nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, preferring cool and moist environments. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with six petals and numerous stamens, making it visually striking.

Liliago nepalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in alpine meadows and forest edges.
