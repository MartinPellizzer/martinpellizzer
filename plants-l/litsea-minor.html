Litsea minor

Litsea minor, commonly known as the dwarf litsea, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries.

Litsea minor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
