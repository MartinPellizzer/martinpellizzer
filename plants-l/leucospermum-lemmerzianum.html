Leucospermum lemmerzianum

Leucospermum lemmerzianum, commonly known as the yellow starling or yellow torch, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, which are arranged in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its botanical characteristics include narrow, leathery leaves and a preference for well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
