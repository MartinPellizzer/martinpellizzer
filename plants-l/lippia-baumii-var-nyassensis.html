Lippia baumii var. nyassensis

Lippia baumii var. nyassensis, commonly known as African verbena, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. The plant is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and container planting.
