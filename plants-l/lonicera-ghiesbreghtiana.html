Lonicera ghiesbreghtiana

Lonicera ghiesbreghtiana, commonly known as the Dutch honeysuckle, is a deciduous climbing shrub native to Europe.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and hairy underneath. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

Lonicera ghiesbreghtiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
