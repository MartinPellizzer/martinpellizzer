Lithocardium foliolosum

Lithocardium foliolosum, commonly known as the leafy stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Lithocardium foliolosum is drought-tolerant and requires minimal watering once established.
