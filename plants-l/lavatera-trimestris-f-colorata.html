Lavatera trimestris f. colorata

Lavatera trimestris f. colorata, commonly known as the colored lavatera, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, fluffy blooms that range in color from white to pink and even crimson. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, hairy seeds. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
