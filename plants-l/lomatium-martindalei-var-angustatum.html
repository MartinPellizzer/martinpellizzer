Lomatium martindalei var. angustatum

Lomatium martindalei var. angustatum, commonly known as narrowleaf lomatium, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its fern-like leaves and umbel-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and forest edges. Its leaves are finely divided and have a distinctive, aromatic scent.
