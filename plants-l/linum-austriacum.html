Linum austriacum

Linum austriacum, commonly known as Austrian flax, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flowering plants commonly referred to as flaxes. The plant produces delicate blue or purple flowers and has linear, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Linum austriacum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
