Lindera pricei

Lindera pricei, commonly known as the Chinese wild laurier, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant features aromatic, simple leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly serrated edge. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by red berries that are edible but not widely consumed by humans.

Lindera pricei is often found in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests.
