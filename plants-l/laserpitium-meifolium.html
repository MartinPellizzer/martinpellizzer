Laserpitium meifolium

Laserpitium meifolium, commonly known as meadow bell, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. It produces small, elongated leaves that are often hairy to the touch.

Laserpitium meifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
