Lindera varmae

Lindera varmae, commonly known as the Varmar's spicebush, is a deciduous shrub native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves that emit a strong scent when crushed. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters and are followed by dark, glossy berries.

Lindera varmae is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
