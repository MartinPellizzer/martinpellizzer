Lepanthes niesseniae

Lepanthes niesseniae, commonly known as the Niessen's slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, small flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its terrestrial growth habit, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in conditions with fluctuating moisture levels.

Lepanthes niesseniae is also noted for its ornamental value, making it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
