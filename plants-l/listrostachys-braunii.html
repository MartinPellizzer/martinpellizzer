Listrostachys braunii

Listrostachys braunii, commonly known as the Braun's spurge, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features erect, branching stems covered with small, pale green leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, greenish-yellow inflorescences.

It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
