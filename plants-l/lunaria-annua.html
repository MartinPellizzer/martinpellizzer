Lunaria annua

Lunaria annua, commonly known as the honesty plant, is a biennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive seed pods that turn a striking blue-gray color when mature. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers in the spring, followed by the development of its unique, papery seed capsules. Lunaria annua thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically beneficial, attracting pollinators and supporting biodiversity.
