Licuala bellatula

Licuala bellatula, commonly known as the golden palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that are often a striking golden-yellow color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Licuala bellatula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
