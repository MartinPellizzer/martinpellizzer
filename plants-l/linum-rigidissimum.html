Linum rigidissimum

Linum rigidissimum, commonly known as rigid flax, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Linum.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its erect, spiny stems and small blue or purple flowers. The plant produces capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Linum rigidissimum is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal remedies.
