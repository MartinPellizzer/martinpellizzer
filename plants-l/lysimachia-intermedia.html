Lysimachia intermedia

Lysimachia intermedia, commonly known as goldenthread, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and covered with fine hairs.

Lysimachia intermedia is often found in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
