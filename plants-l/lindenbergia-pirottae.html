Lindenbergia pirottae

Lindenbergia pirottae, commonly known as the Cape Peninsula daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, daisy-like blooms with white ray florets surrounding a central disk of yellow tubular flowers. It typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Lindenbergia pirottae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
