Lamium Maculatum F. Longearistatum

Lamium maculatum f. longearistatum is a striking ornamental plant known for its vibrant foliage. This variant of the common deadnettle features elongated, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive red or purple edge. It thrives in partial shade and is often used in garden landscapes for its visual appeal. The plant is easy to care for and adapts well to a variety of soil conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among gardeners seeking colorful, low-maintenance plants.
