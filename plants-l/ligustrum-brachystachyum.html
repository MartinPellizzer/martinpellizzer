Ligustrum brachystachyum

Ligustrum brachystachyum, commonly known as the dwarf privet, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, opposite leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, dark berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dense foliage and adaptability to various soil types.

Ligustrum brachystachyum is also used in horticulture for hedging and as a landscape plant.
