Linociera verticillata

Linociera verticillata, commonly known as the clustered bristlyleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, bristly leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are typically arranged in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or forest margins.

Linociera verticillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
