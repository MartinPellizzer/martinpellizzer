Linaria odora

Linaria odora, commonly known as sweet William, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden beds and borders.

Linaria odora is also valued for its pleasant, spicy fragrance, which adds both visual and olfactory appeal to gardens.
