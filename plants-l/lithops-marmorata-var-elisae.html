Lithops marmorata var. elisae

Lithops marmorata var. elisae, commonly known as the "Living Stone," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that resemble stones, aiding in camouflage within its natural habitat. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
