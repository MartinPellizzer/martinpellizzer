Leucocoryne modesta

Leucocoryne modesta, commonly known as the modest lily, is a rare and endangered plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Brazil.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces delicate, white flowers with a distinctive, narrow shape. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to grow in shaded, moist environments. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and its role in the biodiversity of its native habitat.

Due to habitat loss, it is highly threatened and requires conservation efforts to survive.
