Loxanthocereus hoxeyi

Loxanthocereus hoxeyi, commonly known as the Mexican night blooming cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive ribbed structure and spiny areoles. The plant produces large, showy flowers that open at night, typically in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique nocturnal blooming habit makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and desert gardens.
