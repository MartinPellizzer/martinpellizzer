Leonardoxa africana subsp. gracilicaulis

Leonardoxa africana subsp.

gracilicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed African legume, is a woody shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its slender stems and bipinnate leaves contribute to its distinctive appearance.

It is also valued for its potential in soil stabilization and as a source of fodder.
