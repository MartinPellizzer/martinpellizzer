Lastrea tuberculifera

Lastrea tuberculifera, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers. The flowers are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
