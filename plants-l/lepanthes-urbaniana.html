Lepanthes urbaniana

Lepanthes urbaniana, commonly known as the Urban's slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Lepanthes urbaniana is often found in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests.
