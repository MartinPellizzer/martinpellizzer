Leersia hackelii

Leersia hackelii, commonly known as the hackel's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks.
