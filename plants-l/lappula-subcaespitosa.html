Lappula subcaespitosa

Lappula subcaespitosa, commonly known as the tufted lappula, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Chenopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, sparsely branched stems that often form a compact, herbaceous growth. Lappula subcaespitosa is typically found in arid or semi-arid regions, growing in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
