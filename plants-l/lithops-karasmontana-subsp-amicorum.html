Lithops karasmontana subsp. amicorum

Lithops karasmontana subsp.

amicorum, commonly known as the "Mossy Lithops," is a species of succulent native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that resemble stones, which help it blend into its arid environment.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, pale yellow flowers during the cooler months, usually in spring.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
