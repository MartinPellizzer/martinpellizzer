Logania buxifolia

Logania buxifolia, commonly known as the waxleaf logania, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.

Logania buxifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
