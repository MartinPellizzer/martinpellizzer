Lomelosia simplex subsp. simplex

Lomelosia simplex subsp.

simplex, commonly known as the simple lomelosia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences and are usually white or pale green, with a subtle fragrance.
