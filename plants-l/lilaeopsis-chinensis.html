Lilaeopsis chinensis

Lilaeopsis chinensis, commonly known as Chinese grassvetch, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in wet meadows and along riverbanks.

Lilaeopsis chinensis is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects.
