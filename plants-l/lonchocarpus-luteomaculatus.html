Lonchocarpus luteomaculatus

Lonchocarpus luteomaculatus, commonly known as the yellow-spotted legume, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow spots on its foliage. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
