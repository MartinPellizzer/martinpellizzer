Lespedeza patens var. rotundifolia

Lespedeza patens var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the roundleaf lespedeza, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its small, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has alternate, oval-shaped leaves that are often rounded at the tips, giving it its varietal name. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and wet meadows.

Lespedeza patens var. rotundifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
