Lanugothamnus helichrysoides

Lanugothamnus helichrysoides, commonly known as the silverleaf thyme, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, aromatic leaves covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its foliage is used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.
