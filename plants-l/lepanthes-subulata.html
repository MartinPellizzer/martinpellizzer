Lepanthes subulata

Lepanthes subulata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid tropical forests. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, adapted for climbing and water retention.

Lepanthes subulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
