Lightfootia rubens

Lightfootia rubens, commonly known as the red-stemmed fern, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

Lightfootia rubens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
