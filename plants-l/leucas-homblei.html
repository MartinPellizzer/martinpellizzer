Leucas homblei

Leucas homblei, commonly known as the Hombli's leucas, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Leucas homblei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild aromatic properties.
