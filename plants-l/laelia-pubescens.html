Laelia pubescens

Laelia pubescens, commonly known as the hairy laelia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and moderate humidity.

Laelia pubescens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
