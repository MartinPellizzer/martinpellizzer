Lysimachia bodinieri

Lysimachia bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese wallflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features upright, branching stems covered with small, opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Lysimachia bodinieri is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant color and easy cultivation.
