Ludwigia didymosperma

Ludwigia didymosperma, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-pink flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Ludwigia didymosperma is a popular choice for aquatic gardens due to its vibrant color and ease of cultivation.
