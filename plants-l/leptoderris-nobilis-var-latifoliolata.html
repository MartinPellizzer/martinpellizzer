Leptoderris nobilis var. latifoliolata

Leptoderris nobilis var. latifoliolata, commonly known as the broad-leafed evergreen, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and glossy leaves that are broad and elongated. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in shaded garden settings.
