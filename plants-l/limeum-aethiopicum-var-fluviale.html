Limeum aethiopicum var. fluviale

Limeum aethiopicum var. fluviale, commonly known as the African waterwheel, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical Africa.

It features whorled leaves that radiate from a central stem, creating a wheel-like appearance. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in marshes and along riverbanks. Its leaves are typically dark green with a waxy coating, aiding in water retention.

It is valued in aquariums for its ornamental appearance and ability to oxygenate water.
