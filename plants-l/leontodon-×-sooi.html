Leontodon × sooi

Leontodon × sooi, commonly known as the hybrid dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate, hairy leaves and a flowering head that produces yellow composite flowers. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world, often growing in disturbed soils and open areas. Its flowers develop into fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds, aiding in its spread.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
