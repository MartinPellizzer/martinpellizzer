Linum Biforme F. Suffruticosum

Linum biforme f. suffruticosum is a unique variant of the common flax plant known for its distinctive shrub-like growth habit. This form exhibits a more compact and bushy structure compared to the standard annual flax, making it stand out in botanical collections. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in temperate regions with mild climates. The plant produces showy blue or purple flowers, adding ornamental value to gardens and natural landscapes. Its rarity and horticultural appeal make it a fascinating subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
