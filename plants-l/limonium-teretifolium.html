Limonium teretifolium

Limonium teretifolium, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found growing in sandy or saline soils. It is a member of the Plumbagaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Limonium teretifolium is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
