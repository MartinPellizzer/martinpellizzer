Lolium perenne var. sphaerostachuym

Lolium perenne var. sphaerostachuym, commonly known as perennial ryegrass variant with spherical spikelets, is a grass species characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense foliage.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that often form a compact, mat-like structure, making it suitable for turfgrass applications. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, with the spherical spikelets being a distinguishing feature of this variety. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly folded, with a bluish-green coloration when mature.

This variant is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil conditions, though it may require specific care to maintain its unique morphological traits.
