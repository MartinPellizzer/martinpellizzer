Lactuca nipponica

Lactuca nipponica, commonly known as Japanese lettuce, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a composite head. It is often cultivated for its edible leaves, which have a mild, slightly bitter flavor.

Lactuca nipponica is also noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
