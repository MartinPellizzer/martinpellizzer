Lomelosia crenata subsp.

breviscapa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lomelosia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within forests or on rocky slopes.

The plant features narrow, serrated leaves that are often hairy on the underside, and it produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Campanulaceae family.
