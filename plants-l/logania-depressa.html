Logania depressa

Logania depressa, commonly known as the flat logania, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically orange or yellow in color. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are often elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
