Lomandra Sororia

Lomandra sororia is a striking evergreen perennial native to eastern Australia. It is known for its dense, grass-like foliage and its ability to thrive in harsh, coastal environments. This plant is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal. Its narrow, sword-shaped leaves form tight rosettes that provide a unique visual contrast. Lomandra sororia is a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated gardens.
