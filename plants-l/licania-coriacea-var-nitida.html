Licania coriacea var. nitida

Licania coriacea var. nitida, commonly known as the glossy licania, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are typically a deep green and have a smooth, shiny surface. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies and horticulture.
