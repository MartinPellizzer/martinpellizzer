Lobelia conraui

Lobelia conraui, commonly known as the dwarf lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of only a few inches, making it a compact and delicate plant. The species features small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
