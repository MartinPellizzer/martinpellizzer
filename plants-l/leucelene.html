Leucelene

Leucelene, commonly known as the white orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, delicate white flowers with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Leucelene is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
