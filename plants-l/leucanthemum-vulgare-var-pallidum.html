Leucanthemum vulgare var. pallidum

Leucanthemum vulgare var. pallidum, commonly known as the pale oxeye daisy, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features white ray florets surrounding a central yellow disk, giving it a distinctive daisy-like appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soil in full sun. Its flowers bloom from late spring to early autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate.
