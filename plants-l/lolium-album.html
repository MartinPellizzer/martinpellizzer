Lolium album

Lolium album, commonly known as white grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, pale green stems and small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in fields and along roadsides. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Lolium album is an annual herb that plays a role in soil stabilization and can sometimes be considered a weed in agricultural settings.
