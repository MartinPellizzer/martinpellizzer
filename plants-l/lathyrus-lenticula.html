Lathyrus lenticula

Lathyrus lenticula, commonly known as the lentil pea or slender vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, pea-like flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and small, stipitate pods that contain lens-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Lathyrus lenticula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
