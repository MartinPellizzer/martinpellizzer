Lobivia cinnabarina subsp. prestoana

Lobivia cinnabarina subsp.

prestoana, commonly known as the Prestoana Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to the arid regions of South America. It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are typically tinged with red or orange hues, especially under direct sunlight.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually thick and waxy, adapted to store water in its native desert environment.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardiness and attractive appearance.
