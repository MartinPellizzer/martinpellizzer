Lonicera × brownii var. youngii

Lonicera × brownii var. youngii, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a hybrid plant native to China.

It is a deciduous vine characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.

Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in gardens.
