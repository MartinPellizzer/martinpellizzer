Lycium strandveldense

Lycium strandveldense, commonly known as the Strandveld lycium, is a shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshade plants, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and red berries. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions. Its leaves are usually small and succulent, aiding in water conservation.
