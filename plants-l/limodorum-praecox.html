Limodorum praecox

Limodorum praecox, commonly known as the early purple tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, fragrant aroma.

Limodorum praecox is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
