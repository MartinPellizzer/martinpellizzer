Ladenbergia stenocarpa

Ladenbergia stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited legume, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with stipules and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a slender, woody pod that splits open to release seeds.

It is often found in dry, open woodlands and is valued for its traditional uses in local medicine.
