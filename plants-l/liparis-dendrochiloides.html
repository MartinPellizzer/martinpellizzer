Liparis dendrochiloides

Liparis dendrochiloides, commonly known as the tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the presence of a single, thick rhizome. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for both botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
