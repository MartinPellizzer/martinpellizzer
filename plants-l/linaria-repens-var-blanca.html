Linaria repens var. blanca

Linaria repens var. blanca, commonly known as White Snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its trailing stems and compact growth habit. The plant produces showy, two-lipped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
