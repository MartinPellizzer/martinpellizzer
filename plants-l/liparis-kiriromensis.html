Liparis kiriromensis

Liparis kiriromensis, commonly known as the Kirirom orchid, is a species of orchid native to Cambodia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
