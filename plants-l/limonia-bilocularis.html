Limonia bilocularis

Limonia bilocularis, commonly known as the two-lobed lime, is a species of flowering plant in the Rutaceae family.

It is native to the island of Socotra, which is located in the Arabian Sea. The plant is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and small, round, yellow fruits that are edible. Its leaves are simple and have a rough texture, while its branches are often thorny.

Limonia bilocularis is adapted to arid environments and is considered a unique example of flora found only in Socotra.
