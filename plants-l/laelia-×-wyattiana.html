Laelia × wyattiana

Laelia × wyattiana, commonly known as the Wyatt's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the high altitudes of Central America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces showy, pseudobulbous structures with multiple leaves and clusters of pendulous, brightly colored blooms. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
