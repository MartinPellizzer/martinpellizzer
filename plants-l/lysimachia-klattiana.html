Lysimachia klattiana

Lysimachia klattiana, commonly known as the Klatt's yellow loosestrife, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, woodland environments, often near streams or in shaded areas. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Lysimachia klattiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
