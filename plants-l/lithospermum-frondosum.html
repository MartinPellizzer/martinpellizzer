Lithospermum frondosum

Lithospermum frondosum, commonly known as red root, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dark red roots and its clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Lithospermum frondosum is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
