Ligularia alatipes

Ligularia alatipes, commonly known as the broadleaf ligularia, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and a rough texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its showy yellow flowers appear in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
