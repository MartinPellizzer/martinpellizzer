Lens lamottei

Lens lamottei, commonly known as the Lamotte's lentil, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, lens-shaped seeds. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces pods that contain the edible seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
