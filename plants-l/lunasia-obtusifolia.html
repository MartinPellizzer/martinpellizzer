Lunasia obtusifolia

Lunasia obtusifolia, commonly known as the blunt-leaved lunaria, is a species of flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically growing in moist, calcareous soils. The plant features basal leaves that are broad and rounded, with a smooth, waxy surface. Its flowers are small, blue or violet, and arranged in racemes, contributing to its ornamental value.

Lunasia obtusifolia is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
