Leptaspis lanceolata

Leptaspis lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate leaf beetle, is a species of leaf beetle in the family Chrysomelidae.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves, which are typically green with a slightly serrated margin. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions. Its foliage serves as a food source for various herbivorous insects.
