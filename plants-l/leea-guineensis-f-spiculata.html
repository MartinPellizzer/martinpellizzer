Leea guineensis f. spiculata

Leea guineensis f. spiculata, commonly known as the spiny leaf leeah, is a variant of the species Leea guineensis, characterized by its distinctive spiny leaves.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Leeaceae, native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its leaves are usually opposite, simple, and have a leathery texture with prominent spines along the margins.

The flowers are small and white, often clustered in axillary inflorescences, and the plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.
