Litsea cubeba f. glabrata

Litsea cubeba f. glabrata, commonly known as smooth litsea, is a variant of the litsea cubeba plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces aromatic fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both ecological and commercial contexts.
