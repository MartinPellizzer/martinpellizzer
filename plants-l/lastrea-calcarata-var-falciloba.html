Lastrea calcarata var. falciloba

Lastrea calcarata var. falciloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a branching structure with fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
