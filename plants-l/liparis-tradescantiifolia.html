Liparis tradescantiifolia

Liparis tradescantiifolia, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with a silver-green coloration and long, slender leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, warm environments.

Liparis tradescantiifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
