Leucojum aestivum

Leucojum aestivum, commonly known as the summer snowflake, is a perennial bulbous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features grass-like, narrow leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in early summer. The plant is characterized by its fragrant blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile addition to woodland gardens.

Leucojum aestivum is also valued for its ability to naturalize and spread easily in suitable conditions.
