Lamprococcus miniatus

Lamprococcus miniatus, commonly known as the miniature scale insect, is a small, sap-sucking insect that belongs to the family Coccidae.

It is typically found on the leaves of various host plants, where it feeds on plant sap. The plant's botanical significance lies in its role as a host for this insect, which can affect the health of the host plant. Lamprococcus miniatus is often associated with plants in the families Acanthaceae and Malvaceae.
