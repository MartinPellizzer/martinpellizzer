Lessingianthus mollissimus var. microcephalus

Lessingianthus mollissimus var. microcephalus, commonly known as the small-headed lessingia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features finely hairy stems and leaves, giving it a soft, velvety appearance. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
