Lablab javanicus

Lablab javanicus, commonly known as the Java lablab, is a leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds. The plant is often cultivated for its nutritious seeds, which are rich in protein and can be used in various culinary preparations. It thrives in warm climates and is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types.

Lablab javanicus also plays a role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
