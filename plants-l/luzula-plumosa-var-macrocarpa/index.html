Luzula Plumosa Var. Macrocarpa

Feather grass, scientifically known as Luzula plumosa var. macrocarpa, is a striking ornamental plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. Its feathery, silver-green foliage creates a delicate and ethereal appearance, making it a popular choice for garden design. This variety is distinguished by its longer, more robust leaves compared to the standard Luzula plumosa, enhancing its visual impact. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it ideal for woodland gardens or as a ground cover. With its elegant form and low maintenance requirements, Feather grass adds a touch of natural beauty to both cultivated and wild landscapes.
