Licaria bahiana

Licaria bahiana, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit or Brazilian star-apple, is a tropical shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Licaria bahiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit in its native region.
