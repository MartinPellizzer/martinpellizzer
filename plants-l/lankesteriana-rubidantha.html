Lankesteriana rubidantha

Lankesteriana rubidantha, commonly known as the red-flowered flame orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, vibrant red petals and sepals. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Lankesteriana rubidantha thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air circulation.
