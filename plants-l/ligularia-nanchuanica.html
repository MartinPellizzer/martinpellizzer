Ligularia nanchuanica

Ligularia nanchuanica, commonly known as the Nanchuan ligularia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers in the summer, which are clustered in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly found in the wild.
