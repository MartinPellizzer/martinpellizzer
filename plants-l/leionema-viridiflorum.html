Leionema viridiflorum

Leionema viridiflorum, commonly known as the greenflower leionema, is a small shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine, silvery bloom. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
