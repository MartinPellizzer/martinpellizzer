Lithops maughanii

Lithops maughanii, commonly known as the "living stone," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, stone-like leaves that help it store water in its native arid environment. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
