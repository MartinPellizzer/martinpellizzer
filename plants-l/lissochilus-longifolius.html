Lissochilus longifolius

Lissochilus longifolius, commonly known as the long-leaved lissochilus, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. The plant is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species among orchid enthusiasts.
