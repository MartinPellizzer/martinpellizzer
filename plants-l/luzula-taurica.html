Luzula taurica

Luzula taurica, commonly known as the Taurian sedge, is a perennial plant native to the Caucasus region and parts of southern Russia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wet meadows. Its slender, triangular stems are often covered in dense, grass-like foliage.
