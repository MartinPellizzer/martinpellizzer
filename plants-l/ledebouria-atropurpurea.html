Ledebouria atropurpurea

Ledebouria atropurpurea, commonly known as the Purple-veined African bulb.

It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to southern Africa. This succulent plant features a rounded, fleshy bulb that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.
