Lasianthus penicillatus

Lasianthus penicillatus, commonly known as the slender-flowered lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
