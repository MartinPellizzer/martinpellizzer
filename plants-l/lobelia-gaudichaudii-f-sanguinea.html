Lobelia gaudichaudii f. sanguinea

Lobelia gaudichaudii f. sanguinea, commonly known as the red-flowered lobelia, is a striking perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.

Its vibrant floral display makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
