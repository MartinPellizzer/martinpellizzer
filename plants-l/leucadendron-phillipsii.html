Leucadendron phillipsii

Leucadendron phillipsii, commonly known as the yellow fire bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has needle-like leaves that are densely packed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Leucadendron phillipsii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
