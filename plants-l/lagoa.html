Lagoa

Lagoa, commonly known as the water lily, is a flowering aquatic plant belonging to the family Nymphaeaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom above the water surface. Lagoa plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
