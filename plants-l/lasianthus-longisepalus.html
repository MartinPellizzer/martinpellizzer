Lasianthus longisepalus

Lasianthus longisepalus, commonly known as the long-sepaled lasianthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
