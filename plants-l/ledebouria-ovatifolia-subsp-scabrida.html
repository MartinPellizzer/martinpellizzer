Ledebouria ovatifolia subsp. scabrida

Ledebouria ovatifolia subsp.

scabrida, commonly known as the rough-leaved African lily, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are ovate in shape and covered with a rough, scaly texture.

The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically orange or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry conditions.
