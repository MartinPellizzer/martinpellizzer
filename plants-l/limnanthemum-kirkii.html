Limnanthemum kirkii

Limnanthemum kirkii, commonly known as the African water star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy habitats. The plant features showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, often with a waxy surface.

Limnanthemum kirkii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in water gardens.
