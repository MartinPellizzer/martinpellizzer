Loiseleria indica

Loiseleria indica, commonly known as the Indian spider flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Loiseleria indica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
