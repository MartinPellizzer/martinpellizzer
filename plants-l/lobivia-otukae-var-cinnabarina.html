Lobivia otukae var. cinnabarina

Lobivia otukae var. cinnabarina, commonly known as the Cinnabar Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features dense, cylindrical stems that are typically reddish-brown in color, often with a striking orange or pinkish hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
