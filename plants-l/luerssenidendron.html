Luerssenidendron

Luerssenidendron, commonly known as the "sunlight tree," is a unique plant species characterized by its striking, spiral-shaped leaves that resemble the arms of a sun.

It thrives in full sunlight and is native to tropical regions with high humidity and warm climates. The plant's foliage is often a vibrant yellow or gold, giving it its common name. Luerssenidendron is known for its slow growth and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
