Laelia × amesiana

Laelia × amesiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Laelia purpurea and Laelia anceps.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant produces compact, sympodial growth with pseudobulbs and is known for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its hardiness and ornamental value.

The species is often cultivated for its beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
