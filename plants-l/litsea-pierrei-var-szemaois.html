Litsea pierrei var. szemaois

Litsea pierrei var. szemaois, commonly known as the Szechuan litsea, is a species native to China.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with a temperate climate. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small and white, producing a subtle scent, while the fruit is a small, round drupe.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
