Lotus polyphyllos

Lotus polyphyllos, commonly known as the broadleaf water lily, is a perennial aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. The plant features broad, floating leaves that are typically green with a waxy surface, and its flowers are large, showy, and often pink or white. Lotus polyphyllos has a complex root system that helps stabilize the sediment in its habitat.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing shelter for various aquatic organisms.
