Leonurus chaituroides

Leonurus chaituroides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hedgehog plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.
