Ligularia ruficoma

Ligularia ruficoma, commonly known as the red-stemmed ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in a terminal inflorescence, which bloom in late summer. Ligularia ruficoma thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in cool, humid climates.
