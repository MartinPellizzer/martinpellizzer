Lycianthes luteynii

Lycianthes luteynii, commonly known as the yellow lycianthes, is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades.

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are edible and have a tangy flavor. Its leaves are ovate and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are typically white or pale yellow.

Lycianthes luteynii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant foliage and fruit.
