Lepanthes foveata

Lepanthes foveata, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical rainforests. The plant features slender, upright stems that often bear a single or few flowers. Its flowers are usually small, with a distinctive yellowish-green coloration and intricate patterns.

Lepanthes foveata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
