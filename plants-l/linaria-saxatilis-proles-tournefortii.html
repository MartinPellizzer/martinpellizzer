Linaria saxatilis proles tournefortii

Linaria saxatilis proles tournefortii, commonly known as the rock everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces vibrant yellow or orange tubular flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas. Its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
