Lodoicea callypige

Lodoicea callypige, commonly known as the Socotra cactus, is a unique plant native to the island of Socotra in the Arabian Sea.

It is a spiny, shrub-like plant that thrives in arid and rocky environments. It has adapted to the harsh climate with specialized structures such as areoles and spines.
