Laportea fujianensis

Laportea fujianensis, commonly known as the Fujian laportea, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like composite flowers. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its flowers bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
