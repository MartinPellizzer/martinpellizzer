Limonium Corinthiacum

Limonium corinthiacum is a striking coastal plant known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. It thrives in sandy and saline environments, often found along Mediterranean shores. This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in coastal ecosystems. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it a resilient addition to gardens. Despite its hardiness, it remains a relatively rare and underappreciated plant in horticultural circles.
