Ligularia yakla

Ligularia yakla, commonly known as the Yakla arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, broad leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is often found in marshy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in ponds and water gardens.
