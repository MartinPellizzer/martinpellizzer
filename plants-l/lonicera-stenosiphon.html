Lonicera stenosiphon

Lonicera stenosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-siphon honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and often elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or yellow, which bloom in late spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

Lonicera stenosiphon is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
