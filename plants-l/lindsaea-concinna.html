Lindsaea concinna

Lindsaea concinna, commonly known as the slender lindsaea, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Bromeliaceae and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that form a rosette structure. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in a spike, typical of many bromeliads. Lindsaea concinna is often found growing in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.
