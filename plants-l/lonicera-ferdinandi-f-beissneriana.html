Lonicera ferdinandi f. beissneriana

Lonicera ferdinandi f. beissneriana, commonly known as the Beissner's honeysuckle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
