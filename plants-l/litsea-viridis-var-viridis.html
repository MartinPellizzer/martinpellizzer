Litsea viridis var. viridis

Litsea viridis var. viridis, commonly known as the green litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers and elliptical, glossy green leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
