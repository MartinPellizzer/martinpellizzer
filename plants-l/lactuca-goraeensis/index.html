Lactuca Goraeensis

Lactuca goraeensis is a rare and lesser-known species of lettuce native to the mountainous regions of eastern Africa. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, slightly hairy leaves that vary in color from pale green to a deep, almost reddish hue. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky soils at higher altitudes. Despite its limited distribution, it holds potential for agricultural use due to its nutritional value and resilience to harsh conditions. Further study is needed to fully understand its ecological role and conservation status.
