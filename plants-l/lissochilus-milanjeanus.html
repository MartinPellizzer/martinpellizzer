Lissochilus milanjeanus

Lissochilus milanjeanus, commonly known as the Milanjean's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to anchor in rocky substrates, while its leaves are narrow and grass-like.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
