Lonicera tatarica f. alba

Lonicera tatarica f. alba, commonly known as white honeysuckle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.
