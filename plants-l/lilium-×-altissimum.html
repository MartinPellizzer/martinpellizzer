Lilium × altissimum

Lilium × altissimum, commonly known as the giant white lily, is a hybrid species belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces large, white, bell-shaped flowers with a distinctive greenish-yellow throat. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is native to the mountainous regions of western North America and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
