Ligularia arnicoides

Ligularia arnicoides, commonly known as the golden shield, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green underside, often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tall, slender stems topped with clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a dramatic, shade-tolerant plant.
