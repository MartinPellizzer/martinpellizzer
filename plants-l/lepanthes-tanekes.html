Lepanthes tanekes

Lepanthes tanekes, commonly known as the Tanekes orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Lepanthes tanekes is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
