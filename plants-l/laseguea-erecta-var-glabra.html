Laseguea erecta var. glabra

Laseguea erecta var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth laseguea, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and smooth, glabrous stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
