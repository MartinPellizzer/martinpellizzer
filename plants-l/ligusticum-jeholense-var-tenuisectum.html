Ligusticum jeholense var. tenuisectum

Ligusticum jeholense var. tenuisectum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Chinese lovage, is a perennial herb native to northern China.

It features slender, aromatic leaves that are finely divided, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in umbels, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species within the Apiaceae family.
