Lobivia varians var. rubroalba

Lobivia varians var. rubroalba, commonly known as the red-leaved lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features compact, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with a distinctive reddish hue, especially under bright sunlight. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, making it highly drought-resistant.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and ease of cultivation in dry environments.
