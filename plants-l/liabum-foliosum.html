Liabum foliosum

Liabum foliosum, commonly known as the leafy liabum, is a herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is characterized by succulent leaves and flowers adapted to dry environments. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture, and it produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique adaptations, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
