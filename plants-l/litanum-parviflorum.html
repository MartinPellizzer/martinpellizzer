Litanum parviflorum

Litanum parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered litanum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, delicate white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a basal rosette.

Litanum parviflorum is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
