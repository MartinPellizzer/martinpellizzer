Lepicephalus humilis

Lepicephalus humilis, commonly known as the dwarf lichen, is a small, crustose lichen that typically grows on soil, rocks, or wood in moist, shaded environments.

Lepicephalus humilis is often used as an indicator species for environmental health due to its sensitivity to pollution.
