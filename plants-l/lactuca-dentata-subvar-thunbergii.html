Lactuca dentata subvar. thunbergii

Lactuca dentata subvar. thunbergii, commonly known as Thunberg's lettuce, is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features rosettes of dark green, toothed leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly bitter taste. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
