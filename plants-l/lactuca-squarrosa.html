Lactuca squarrosa

Lactuca squarrosa, commonly known as the woolly lettuce, is a flowering plant native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, lobed leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and it produces small, nutlet-like fruits. Lactuca squarrosa is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and fields.
