Lomatogonium rotatum var. fontanum

Lomatogonium rotatum var. fontanum, commonly known as the Fontana lomatogonium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

This variety is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
