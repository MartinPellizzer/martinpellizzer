Lilium auratum var. rubrum

Lilium auratum var. rubrum, commonly known as the crimson lily, is a striking variety of the tiger lily.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals that often feature a distinctive orange or yellow base, creating a bold and eye-catching appearance. The plant is native to eastern Asia and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. Its showy flowers bloom in late summer, adding color to gardens and landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its dramatic visual impact.
