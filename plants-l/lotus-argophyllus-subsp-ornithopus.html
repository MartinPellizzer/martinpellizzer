Lotus argophyllus subsp. ornithopus

Lotus argophyllus subsp.

ornithopus, commonly known as the ornate lotus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple.

The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often floating on the water's surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and wetland restoration projects.
