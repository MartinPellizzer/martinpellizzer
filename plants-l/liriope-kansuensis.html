Liriope kansuensis

Liriope kansuensis, commonly known as the Chinese lily-turf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its clumping growth habit and grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Liriope kansuensis is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in wetland areas.
