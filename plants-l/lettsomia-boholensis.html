Lettsomia boholensis

Lettsomia boholensis, commonly known as the Bohol stiltgrass, is a herbaceous plant native to the Philippines.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems.

Lettsomia boholensis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates.
