Lespedeza bicolor f. rosea

Lespedeza bicolor f. rosea, commonly known as the pink lespedeza, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its bi-color leaves, which typically feature a green upper surface and a pink or red lower surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for native landscaping.

This variety is often used in erosion control due to its vigorous growth and ability to stabilize soil.
