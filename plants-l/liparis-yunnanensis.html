Liparis yunnanensis

Liparis yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces a single flower with a greenish-yellow perianth and a distinctive spur. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticultural and conservation studies.
