Litsea densiflora

Litsea densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or red when mature.

Litsea densiflora is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental and aromatic properties.
