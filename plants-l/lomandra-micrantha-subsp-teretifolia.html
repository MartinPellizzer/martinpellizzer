Lomandra micrantha subsp. teretifolia

Lomandra micrantha subsp.

teretifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lomandra, is a perennial herb native to southeastern Australia. It features dense, grass-like foliage with narrow, strap-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color.

The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers during late winter to early spring. It is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
