Licuala kirsteniana

Licuala kirsteniana, commonly known as the golden palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by dark blue, edible fruits.

Licuala kirsteniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
