Limonium × garciae

Limonium × garciae, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking spikes of blue or purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found growing in coastal areas and sandy soils. It is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained conditions and full sun.

Limonium × garciae is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
