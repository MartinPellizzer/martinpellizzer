Lobivia ferox var. ferox

Lobivia ferox var. ferox, commonly known as the Ferox Lobivia, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of spines, which provide protection from herbivores and extreme weather conditions. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its rugged appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for rocky or sandy environments.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
