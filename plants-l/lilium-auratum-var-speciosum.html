Lilium auratum var. speciosum

Lilium auratum var. speciosum, commonly known as the showy lily, is a striking perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically trumpet-shaped and come in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It blooms in late spring to early summer, and its bulbs are adapted for storing nutrients.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
