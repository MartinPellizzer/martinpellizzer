Lepidagathis hyalina var. mollis

Lepidagathis hyalina var. mollis, commonly known as the silverleaf lepidagathis, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
