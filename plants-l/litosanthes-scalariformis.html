Litosanthes scalariformis

Litosanthes scalariformis, commonly known as the scalloped rock heather, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Litosanthes scalariformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils.
