Linaria pelliceriana

Linaria pelliceriana, commonly known as the desert snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and bright yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
