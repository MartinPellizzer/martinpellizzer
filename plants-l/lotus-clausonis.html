Lotus clausonis

Lotus clausonis, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a species native to southern and eastern Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with large, showy flowers that typically bloom in the summer months. The plant thrives in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds and wetlands. Its leaves are broad and floating, while its roots are often submerged.

The lotus is notable for its ability to purify water and is widely used in traditional medicine and spiritual practices.
