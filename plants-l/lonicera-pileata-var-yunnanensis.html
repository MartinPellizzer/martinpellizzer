Lonicera pileata var. yunnanensis

Lonicera pileata var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, rusty-brown hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for small gardens or as a container plant.

The variety is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to a range of soil conditions.
