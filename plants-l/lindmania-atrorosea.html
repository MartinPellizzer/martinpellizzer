Lindmania atrorosea

Lindmania atrorosea, commonly known as the pink-rose velvet fern, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that ranges in color from deep rose to almost black. The plant produces showy inflorescences with bright red flowers, which are highly attractive to pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and indoor collections.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
