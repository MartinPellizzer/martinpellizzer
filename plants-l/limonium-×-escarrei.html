Limonium × escarrei

Limonium × escarrei, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, spike-like inflorescences that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, often found in sandy or gravelly habitats. Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Limonium × escarrei is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate salt spray.
