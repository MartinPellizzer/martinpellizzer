Linochilus ocanensis

Linochilus ocanensis, commonly known as the Ocanensis orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to eastern Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. This herbaceous plant typically grows in forest understory environments, where it thrives in shaded, moist conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Linochilus ocanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
