Lygistum ignitum

Lygistum ignitum, commonly known as the flamevine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red, often resembling flames. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
