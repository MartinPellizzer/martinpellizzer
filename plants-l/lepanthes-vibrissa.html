Lepanthes vibrissa

Lepanthes vibrissa, commonly known as the vibrissa orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale green, with a distinctive, hairy structure on the petals.

Lepanthes vibrissa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
