Leucanthemum delarbrei subsp. delarbrei

Leucanthemum delarbrei subsp.

delarbrei, commonly known as the Delarbre's white marguerite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in summer.

The plant has a basal rosette of leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
