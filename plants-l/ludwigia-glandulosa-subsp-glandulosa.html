Ludwigia glandulosa subsp. glandulosa

Ludwigia glandulosa subsp.

glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular Ludwigia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and bright, red or orange-colored flowers that bloom throughout the year.

The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a waxy surface and prominent glandular dots, which contribute to its common name. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in swampy or marshy environments.

This species is popular in aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats due to its ornamental value and adaptability.
