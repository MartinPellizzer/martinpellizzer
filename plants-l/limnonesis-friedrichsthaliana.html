Limnonesis friedrichsthaliana

Limnonesis friedrichsthaliana, commonly known as the Friedrichsthaliana water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical regions.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its roots anchor in the muddy substrate, absorbing nutrients from the surrounding water.

Limnonesis friedrichsthaliana plays a vital role in maintaining water quality and supporting biodiversity in its ecosystem.
