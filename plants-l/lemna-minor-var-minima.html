Lemna minor var. minima

Lemna minor var. minima, commonly known as the least duckweed, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its tiny, oval-shaped leaves that are typically less than 1 millimeter in length. The plant floats on the surface of freshwater bodies and reproduces rapidly through vegetative propagation. It lacks true roots and has a simple, stemless structure.
