Lobelia chinensis var. hirta

Lobelia chinensis var. hirta, commonly known as the hairy lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in shady garden settings.
