Leea acuminata

Leea acuminata, commonly known as the slender leea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Leeaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or water.
