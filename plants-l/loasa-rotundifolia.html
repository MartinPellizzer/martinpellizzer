Loasa rotundifolia

Loasa rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf loasa, is a flowering plant native to the Andes of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. Its botanical classification places it in the family Polygonaceae, known as the buckwheat family.
