Lycopodium hupeanum

Lycopodium hupeanum, commonly known as the Chinese clubmoss, is a member of the Lycopodiaceae family and is native to eastern Asia.

It is a perennial evergreen plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with numerous upright, spore-bearing cones at the tips of its branches. Its foliage consists of small, scale-like leaves that form dense, compact clusters.

Lycopodium hupeanum plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various small organisms.
