Lomatia ilicifolia var. hyposericea

Lomatia ilicifolia var. hyposericea, commonly known as the silky lomatia, is a rare and unique plant species native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, followed by woody, cone-like fruits. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in coastal heathlands, where it plays a role in stabilizing dunes.
