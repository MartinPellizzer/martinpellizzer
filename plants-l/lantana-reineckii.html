Lantana reineckii

Lantana reineckii, commonly known as the silver lantana, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, compact foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, depending on the variety. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance.

Lantana reineckii is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.
