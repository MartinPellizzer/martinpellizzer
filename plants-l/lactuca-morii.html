Lactuca morii

Lactuca morii, commonly known as the Morii lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of broad, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine white powder. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Lactuca morii is primarily cultivated for its edible leaves, which have a mild, slightly bitter flavor.
