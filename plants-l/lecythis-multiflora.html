Lecythis multiflora

Lecythis multiflora, commonly known as the Brazilian walnut or yellow star fruit, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and produces clusters of yellow, star-shaped flowers. The tree bears fruit that is covered in a thick, spiny husk containing a single large seed. Its bark and seeds have been used traditionally for their medicinal properties.
