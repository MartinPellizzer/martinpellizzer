Lycaste arnoldiae

Lycaste arnoldiae, commonly known as the Arnold's lycaste, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically features broad, leathery leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Lycaste arnoldiae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
