Lessingia arachnoidea

Lessingia arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider-like flowering plant, is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.
