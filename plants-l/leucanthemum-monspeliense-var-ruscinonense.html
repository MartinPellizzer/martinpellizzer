Leucanthemum monspeliense var. ruscinonense

Leucanthemum monspeliense var. ruscinonense, commonly known as the Ruscinonense Everlasting, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white ray florets and yellow disk florets. The plant produces a profusion of blooms from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for dried flower arrangements.
