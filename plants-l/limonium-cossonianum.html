Limonium cossonianum

Limonium cossonianum, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in coastal environments and is adapted to saline and well-drained soils. Its flowering period typically occurs in late summer to early autumn.

Limonium cossonianum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
