Ligularia franchetiana

Ligularia franchetiana, commonly known as the Chinese heartleaf arum, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin and a prominent vein system. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white spadices surrounded by a showy, petal-like bract. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to gardens with suitable conditions.
