Lissochilus smithii

Lissochilus smithii, commonly known as the Smith's lissochilus, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in high-altitude grasslands.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
