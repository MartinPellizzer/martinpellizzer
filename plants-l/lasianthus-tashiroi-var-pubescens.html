Lasianthus tashiroi var. pubescens

Lasianthus tashiroi var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy foliage and showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine pubescence, giving it a distinctive texture.
