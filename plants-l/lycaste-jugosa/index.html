Lycaste Jugosa

Coral bells, scientifically known as Lycaste jugosa, are strikingly beautiful plants native to the tropical regions of South America. With their vibrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, they add a dramatic touch to any garden or indoor space. These plants are prized for their unique foliage, featuring velvety leaves that range from dark green to deep purple. While they thrive in warm, humid environments, they can also be cultivated successfully in cooler climates with proper care. Their ornamental value and ease of care make them a popular choice among both novice and experienced gardeners.
