Lupinus Rothmaleri

Lupinus rothmaleri is a rare and unique species of lupine native to the high altitudes of the Andes mountains. This plant is known for its striking blue to purple flowers and its ability to thrive in harsh, rocky environments. It plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators and stabilizing soil in mountainous regions. Due to its limited habitat and threats from climate change, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival for future generations.
