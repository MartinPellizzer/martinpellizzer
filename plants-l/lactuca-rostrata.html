Lactuca rostrata

Lactuca rostrata, commonly known as the rostrate lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its rosette of spoon-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Lactuca rostrata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
