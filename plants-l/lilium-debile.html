Lilium debile

Lilium debile, commonly known as the slender lily, is a species of flowering plant in the lily family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in China and the Himalayas. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It typically grows in rocky or forested areas and thrives in cool, moist conditions.

Lilium debile is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant blooms.
