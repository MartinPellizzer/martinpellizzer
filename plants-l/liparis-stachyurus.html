Liparis stachyurus

Liparis stachyurus, commonly known as the Japanese orchid or stachyurus orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Liparis stachyurus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
