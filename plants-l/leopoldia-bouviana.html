Leopoldia bouviana

Leopoldia bouviana, commonly known as the Bovian orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid native to the eastern regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, pale greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with short, hairy stems and narrow leaves. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires careful cultivation due to its specific habitat needs.

Leopoldia bouviana is listed as endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection.
