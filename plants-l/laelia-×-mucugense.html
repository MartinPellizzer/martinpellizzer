Laelia × mucugense

Laelia × mucugense, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant resulting from the cross between Laelia anceola and Laelia purpurea.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru, where it thrives in cool, shaded environments. This orchid is characterized by its vibrant, often purple or pink flowers with intricate patterns, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It requires well-draining soil and moderate to high humidity to flourish.

The plant is also valued for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
