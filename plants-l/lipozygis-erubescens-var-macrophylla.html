Lipozygis erubescens var. macrophylla

Lipozygis erubescens var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf lipozygis, is a shrub native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly pubescent underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or red, arranged in clusters along the stems. Its botanical features make it a notable species in tropical horticulture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest environments.
