Lathyrus emodi var. majalis

Lathyrus emodi var. majalis, commonly known as the Himalayan larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its flowers are typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens in temperate regions.
