Leucadendron truncatulum

Leucadendron truncatulum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces yellow to orange flower heads that are clustered in dense, spherical shapes. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is drought-tolerant.

The silver tree is also known for its attractive bark and is often used in ornamental gardening.
