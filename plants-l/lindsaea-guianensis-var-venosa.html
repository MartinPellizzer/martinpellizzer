Lindsaea guianensis var. venosa

Lindsaea guianensis var. venosa, commonly known as the Guianan velvet fern, is a terrestrial fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety fronds that are covered in dense, soft trichomes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are short and often form dense clumps, while the fronds are pinnately divided with a leathery texture.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in humid, shaded gardens.
