Ligularia melanothyrsa

Ligularia melanothyrsa, commonly known as the black-throated ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive dark purplish-black margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a dense, terminal inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
