Linum macraei var. oligophyllum

Linum macraei var. oligophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf flax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Linaceae family and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and delicate blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts well to harsh environmental conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
