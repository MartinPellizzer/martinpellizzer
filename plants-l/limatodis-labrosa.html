Limatodis labrosa

Limatodis labrosa, commonly known as the broad-leaved dandelion, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of broad, spoon-shaped leaves with a waxy coating. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its leaves and flowers are edible, making it a valuable plant in both culinary and medicinal contexts.
