Lilium gracile

Lilium gracile, commonly known as the slender lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped blossoms that are often clustered at the top of the stem. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Lilium gracile is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
