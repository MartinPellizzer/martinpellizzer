Lilium heldreichii

Lilium heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It is a perennial herb with showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, or red. The plant features a tall, slender stem that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, supporting a cluster of blooms at the top. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Lilium heldreichii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
