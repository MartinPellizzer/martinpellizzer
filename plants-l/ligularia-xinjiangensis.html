Ligularia xinjiangensis

Ligularia xinjiangensis, commonly known as Xinjiang ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Xinjiang, China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, which are typically borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
