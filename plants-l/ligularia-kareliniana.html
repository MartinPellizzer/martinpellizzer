Ligularia kareliniana

Ligularia kareliniana, commonly known as the Karelin's ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple underside, which adds visual interest to the garden. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and shaded landscapes.
