Lepanthes liesiae

Lepanthes liesiae, commonly known as the Mexican violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in cloud forests. It has small, elliptical leaves and produces fragrant blooms during the spring and summer seasons.

Lepanthes liesiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of tropical flora.
