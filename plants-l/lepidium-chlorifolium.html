Lepidium chlorifolium

Lepidium chlorifolium, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and pinnately compound leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard family plants.

Lepidium chlorifolium is an annual or biennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.
