Laelia crispa var. cauwelaertiae

Laelia crispa var. cauwelaertiae, commonly known as the Cauwelaert's Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that produce multiple leaves and flowering stems. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
