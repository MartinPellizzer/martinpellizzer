Luffa cylindrica var. multiflora

Luffa cylindrica var. multiflora, commonly known as the sponge gourd or Chinese sponge gourd, is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cylindrical fruit that matures to a fibrous, spongy texture. The plant produces yellow flowers and thrives in warm, tropical climates with well-drained soil. Its fruit is often used in cooking and for making sponges due to its porous structure.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
