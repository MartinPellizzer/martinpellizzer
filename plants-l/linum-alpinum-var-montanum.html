Linum alpinum var. montanum

Linum alpinum var. montanum, commonly known as the alpine flax, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats. The plant produces showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapting to cold and arid conditions.

Linum alpinum var. montanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
