Lasianthus gialaiensis

Lasianthus gialaiensis, commonly known as the Gialai mountain gentian, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Lasianthus gialaiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
