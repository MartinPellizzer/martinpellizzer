Ligeria caulescens

Ligeria caulescens, commonly known as the slender ligeria, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are linear to lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and usually have a pale yellow to greenish color.

Ligeria caulescens is adapted to high-altitude environments and thrives in cool, moist conditions.
