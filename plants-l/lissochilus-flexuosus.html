Lissochilus flexuosus

Lissochilus flexuosus, commonly known as the flexible lissochilus, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, drooping stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a slight purple or greenish tinge. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
