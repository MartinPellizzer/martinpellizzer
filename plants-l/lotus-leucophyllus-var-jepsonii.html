Lotus leucophyllus var. jepsonii

Lotus leucophyllus var. jepsonii, commonly known as the Sierra lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant has basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies in its native range.
