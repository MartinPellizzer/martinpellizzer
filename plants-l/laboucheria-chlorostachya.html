Laboucheria chlorostachya

Laboucheria chlorostachya, commonly known as the silver-stemmed mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Loranthaceae and is characterized by its pale green stems and small, waxy leaves. Due to its parasitic nature, it can cause significant stress to its host plants if left unchecked.
