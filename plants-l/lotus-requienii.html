Lotus requienii

Lotus requienii, commonly known as the Sierra Madre lotus, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking violet-blue petals and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in high-altitude environments. Its flowers bloom in summer, adding a unique ornamental value to its natural habitat.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
