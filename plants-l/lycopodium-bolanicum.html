Lycopodium bolanicum

Lycopodium bolanicum, commonly known as the Bolan clubmoss, is a small evergreen shrub belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Pakistan, India, and Afghanistan. The plant features whorled leaves that are needle-like and arranged in spirals around its stems. It produces spore-bearing cones at the tips of its stems, which are typically clustered in small, cylindrical structures.

Lycopodium bolanicum is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
