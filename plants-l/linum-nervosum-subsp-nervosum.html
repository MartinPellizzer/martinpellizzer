Linum nervosum subsp. nervosum

Linum nervosum subsp.

nervosum, commonly known as the slender flax, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate blue or purple flowers.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its fibrous stems and seeds have been used traditionally for various purposes, including textile production and seed harvesting.
