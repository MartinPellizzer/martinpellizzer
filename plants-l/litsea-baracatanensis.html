Litsea baracatanensis

Litsea baracatanensis, commonly known as the Malayan litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruit is a small, smooth, berry-like drupe that ripens to a dark red color.

Litsea baracatanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
