Loeseneriella africana var. africana

Loeseneriella africana var. africana, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces small, edible berries that are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
