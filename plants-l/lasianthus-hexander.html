Lasianthus hexander

Lasianthus hexander, commonly known as the purple-flowered lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
