Ludwigia × lacustris f. aquatilis

Ludwigia × lacustris f. aquatilis, commonly known as the water Ludwigia, is a semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It features striking red or pink floral bracts that are highly attractive to pollinators. The plant has elongated, narrow leaves that are typically submerged or floating, adapting well to aquatic environments. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater habitats with moderate to high light conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquascaping and freshwater gardens.
