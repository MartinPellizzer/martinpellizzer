Limnanthemum coreanum

Limnanthemum coreanum, commonly known as the Korean water star.

It is a flowering plant native to East Asia, particularly Korea, China, and Japan. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often near water bodies. Its leaves are heart-shaped and slightly hairy, while its stems are slender and often trailing.

Limnanthemum coreanum is typically found in wetland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
