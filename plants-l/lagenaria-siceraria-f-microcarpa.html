Lagenaria siceraria f. microcarpa

Lagenaria siceraria f. microcarpa, commonly known as the miniature gourd or baby gourd, is a variant of the bottle gourd characterized by its small, compact size.

This plant is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers, squash, and melons. It produces oval or spherical fruits with a smooth, warty surface that can vary in color from pale green to yellow. The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and ease of growth.
