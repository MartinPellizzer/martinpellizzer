Liparis mengziensis

Liparis mengziensis, commonly known as Mengzi orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, flowering spikes. The plant typically grows in forested areas with high humidity and moderate temperatures. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip structure.

Liparis mengziensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
