Lamourouxia integerrima

Lamourouxia integerrima, commonly known as the smooth-leafed bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and slightly hairy along the edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Lamourouxia integerrima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
