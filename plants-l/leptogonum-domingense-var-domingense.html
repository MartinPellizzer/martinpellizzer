Leptogonum domingense var. domingense

Leptogonum domingense var. domingense, commonly known as the Caribbean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered fruits.

This species is often found in tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained environments.
