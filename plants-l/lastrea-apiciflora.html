Lastrea apiciflora

Lastrea apiciflora, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its white, star-shaped flowers that resemble snowflakes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, cylindrical fruit capsules. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
