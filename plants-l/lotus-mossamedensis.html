Lotus mossamedensis

Lotus mossamedensis, commonly known as the African lotus, is a unique aquatic plant native to the freshwater ecosystems of sub-Saharan Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, often floating on the water's surface. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps anchor it in shallow waters while absorbing nutrients from the surrounding environment. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.

Lotus mossamedensis plays an important ecological role in maintaining water quality and supporting biodiversity in its native habitats.
