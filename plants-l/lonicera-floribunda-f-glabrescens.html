Lonicera floribunda f. glabrescens

Lonicera floribunda f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth honeysuckle, is a flowering shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and oval, dark green leaves. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It blooms in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in temperate climates.
