Listera × veltmanii

Listera × veltmanii, commonly known as the hybrid yellow basketwort, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are small and bell-shaped, often arranged in a raceme, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Listera × veltmanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
