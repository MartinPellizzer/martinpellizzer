Loudetia annua var. cerata

Loudetia annua var. cerata, commonly known as the curly knotweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, wiry stems that often curl and twist, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.
