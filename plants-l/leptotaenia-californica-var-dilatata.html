Leptotaenia californica var. dilatata

Leptotaenia californica var. dilatata, commonly known as the California leaved spurge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green and covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are often broad and have a slightly lobed or notched appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
