Lissochilus arenicola

Lissochilus arenicola, commonly known as the sandstone orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are often pale green or yellowish in color. It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is adapted to thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
