Lophospermum grandiflorum

Lophospermum grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered iron vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible for some wildlife. Lophospermum grandiflorum thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its ornate appearance.
