Lobivia chrysantha subvar. fleischeriana

Lobivia chrysantha subvar. fleischeriana, commonly known as the Fleischer's Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a cylindrical stem with a greenish-gray color, often covered in sparse, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which bloom at the tips of the stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
