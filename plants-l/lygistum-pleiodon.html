Lygistum pleiodon

Lygistum pleiodon, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to East Asia and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red berries that resemble small lanterns. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically yellow or white. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

The berries are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as ornamental plants due to their striking appearance.
