Leucopogon striatus

Leucopogon striatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heaths, heathers, and blueberries. Leucopogon striatus is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
