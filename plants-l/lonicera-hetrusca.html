Lonicera hetrusca

Lonicera hetrusca, commonly known as the Italian honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its fruits are red berries that mature in late summer and are edible for birds.

Lonicera hetrusca is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
