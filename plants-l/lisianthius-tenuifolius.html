Lisianthius tenuifolius

Lisianthius tenuifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lisianthius, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its delicate appearance and seasonal blooming, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
