Lycopodiastrum

Lycopodiastrum, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Lycopodiaceae.

It consists of a single species, Lycopodiastrum laeve, which is native to South America. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable addition to botanical gardens and natural habitats.
