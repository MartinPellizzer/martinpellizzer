Linaria scariosa

Linaria scariosa, commonly known as the scarlet linaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and has slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are linear to lance-shaped and often have a slightly rough texture.

Linaria scariosa thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
