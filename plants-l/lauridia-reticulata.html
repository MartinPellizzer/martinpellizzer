Lauridia reticulata

Lauridia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined laurid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a dark blue drupe that contains a single seed.

Lauridia reticulata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and fragrant properties.
