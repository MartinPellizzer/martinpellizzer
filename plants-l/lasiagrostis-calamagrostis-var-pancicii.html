Lasiagrostis calamagrostis var. pancicii

Lasiagrostis calamagrostis var. pancicii, commonly known as the Pancici's reed, is a grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy areas, thriving in moist, temperate climates. Its flowering spikes are usually pink or purplish, adding ornamental value to its habitat.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local biodiversity.
