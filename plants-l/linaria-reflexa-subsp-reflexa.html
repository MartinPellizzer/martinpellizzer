Linaria reflexa subsp. reflexa

Linaria reflexa subsp.

reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed toadflax, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

The plant produces bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its slender, branching stems are covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental flowers and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
