Lycopersicon lycopersicum

Lycopersicon lycopersicum, commonly known as the tomato, is a flowering plant in the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is native to South America and is characterized by its hairy stems, alternate leaves, and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small green fruits that mature to a bright red color. Its fruit is the primary part used in culinary applications.

The tomato is an annual herb that thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil.
