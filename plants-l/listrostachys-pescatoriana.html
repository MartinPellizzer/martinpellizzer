Listrostachys pescatoriana

Listrostachys pescatoriana, commonly known as the sea hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

The plant thrives in saline and humid environments, often growing along shores and in mangrove swamps. Its adaptability to coastal conditions makes it an important species in coastal ecosystems.
