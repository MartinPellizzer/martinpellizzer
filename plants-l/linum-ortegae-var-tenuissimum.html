Linum ortegae var. tenuissimum

Linum ortegae var. tenuissimum, commonly known as the slender flax.

It is a flowering plant species native to the Andean region of South America. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale blue. It belongs to the Linaceae family, which includes other flax species.

This variety is often found in rocky or arid habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
