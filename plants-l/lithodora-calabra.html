Lithodora calabra

Lithodora calabra, commonly known as the Calabrian stonecrop, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet, often in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Lithodora calabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
