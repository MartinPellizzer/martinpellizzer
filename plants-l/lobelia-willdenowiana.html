Lobelia willdenowiana

Lobelia willdenowiana, commonly known as the blue lobelia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Botanically, it belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
