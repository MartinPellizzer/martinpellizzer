Lathyrus anhuiensis

Lathyrus anhuiensis, commonly known as the Anhui vetch, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Anhui province.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its characteristic pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with a distinctive spurred petal.

Lathyrus anhuiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in soil enrichment.
