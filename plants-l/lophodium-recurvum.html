Lophodium recurvum

Lophodium recurvum, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of pinnae, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.
