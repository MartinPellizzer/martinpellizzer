Lobivia haagei var. nazarenoensis

Lobivia haagei var. nazarenoensis, commonly known as the Nazarenoensis Lobivia, is a rare and compact cactus native to the arid regions of northern Chile.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure that helps it store water efficiently. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer. The plant has a slow growth rate and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
