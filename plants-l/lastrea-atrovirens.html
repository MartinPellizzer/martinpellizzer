Lastrea atrovirens

Lastrea atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green leaf cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of small, green leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability to harsh conditions make it a popular choice for succulent gardens.
