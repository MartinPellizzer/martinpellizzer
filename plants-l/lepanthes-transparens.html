Lepanthes transparens

Lepanthes transparens, commonly known as the transparent orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent petals that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are small and fragrant, blooming during the warmer months.

Lepanthes transparens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
