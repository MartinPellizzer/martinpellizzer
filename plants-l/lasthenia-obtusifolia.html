Lasthenia obtusifolia

Lasthenia obtusifolia, commonly known as the bluntleaf rayhead, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leaves with a distinctive, rounded shape.
