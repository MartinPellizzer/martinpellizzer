Lonicera xylosteum var. xanthocarpa

Lonicera xylosteum var. xanthocarpa, commonly known as the yellow-flowered honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
