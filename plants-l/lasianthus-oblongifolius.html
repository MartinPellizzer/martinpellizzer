Lasianthus oblongifolius

Lasianthus oblongifolius, commonly known as the broad-leafed lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are usually violet or purple in color and attract various pollinators.
