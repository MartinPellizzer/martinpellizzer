Lobivia nigrispina var. rubriflora

Lobivia nigrispina var. rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered nightshade, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features thick, ribbed stems and fleshy, spiny leaves that help it retain water in harsh environments. The plant produces striking, vibrant red flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is highly sought after by succulent enthusiasts.
