Lobelia stenophylla

Lobelia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and prefers partial to full shade. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Lobelia stenophylla is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in native gardens.
