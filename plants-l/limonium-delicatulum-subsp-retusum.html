Limonium delicatulum subsp. retusum

Limonium delicatulum subsp.

retusum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean. It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered blue or purple flowers.

The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime environments. It is typically low-growing, with a compact habit that helps it withstand coastal winds and salt spray.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize dune ecosystems.
