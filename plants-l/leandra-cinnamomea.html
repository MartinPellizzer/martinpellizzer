Leandra cinnamomea

Leandra cinnamomea, commonly known as cinnamon tree or sweet cinnamon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, reddish-barked trunk and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that are often clustered in panicles. Its fruit is a small, round, and fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

Leandra cinnamomea is valued for its aromatic bark, which has been used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
