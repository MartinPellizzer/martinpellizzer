Lastreopsis davalliiformis

Lastreopsis davalliiformis, commonly known as the rock tamarisk, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, clustered leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.
