Lychnis acaulis

Lychnis acaulis, commonly known as the cushion pink, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and is often found in alpine and rocky habitats. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically small, rounded, and clustered closely at the base.

Lychnis acaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
