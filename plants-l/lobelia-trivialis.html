Lobelia trivialis

Lobelia trivialis, commonly known as narrowleaf lobelia, is a small flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Lobelia trivialis is known for its ability to thrive in wet conditions and is often used in native plant gardens.
