Lantana achyranthifolia f. lilacina

Lantana achyranthifolia f. lilacina, commonly known as the lilac-flowered lantana, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to lilac. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
