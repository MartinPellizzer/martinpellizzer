Ludwigia ramosissima

Ludwigia ramosissima, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, branching stems and small, bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and pond edges, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are often submerged or floating, contributing to its role in aquatic ecosystems.

Ludwigia ramosissima is also popular in aquariums due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
