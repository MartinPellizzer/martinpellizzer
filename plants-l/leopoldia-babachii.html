Leopoldia babachii

Leopoldia babachii, commonly known as the Babachii orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to specific regions in Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, hairy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive yellow throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
