Lapeirousia porphyrosiphon

Lapeirousia porphyrosiphon, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in the coastal regions of the Western Cape. Its flowers are borne on tall, slender stems and are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is cultivated by plant enthusiasts for its beauty.
