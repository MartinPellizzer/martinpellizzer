Lucya

Lucya, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Leguminosae and is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the pods contain seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Lucya thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
