Litsea kerrii

Litsea kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's lime or mountain lime, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, round, and turn from green to orange when mature.

Litsea kerrii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
