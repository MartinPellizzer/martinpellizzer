Leucadendron cryptocephalum

Leucadendron cryptocephalum, commonly known as the Cape rose or silver leaf, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage and vibrant yellow-orange flower heads that bloom in late spring. The plant belongs to the Proteaceae family, which is known for its showy, often long-lasting flowers. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, particularly in coastal and well-drained soils.

Leucadendron cryptocephalum thrives in full sun and requires minimal water once established.
