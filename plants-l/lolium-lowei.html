Lolium lowei

Lolium lowei, commonly known as the lowei ryegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green blades and dense growth habit, making it suitable for forage and erosion control. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along streambanks. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny florets, which are wind-pollinated.

Lolium lowei is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
