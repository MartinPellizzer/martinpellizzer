Limeum frutescens

Limeum frutescens, commonly known as the dwarf lime or rock lime, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and often covered in a fine white powder.

Limeum frutescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in drought-prone environments.
