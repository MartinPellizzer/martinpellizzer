Lactuca sororia

Lactuca sororia, commonly known as the lesser lettuce, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of dark green, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Lactuca sororia is often found in disturbed soils and open areas, and it has a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin.
