Lindsaea christii

Lindsaea christii, commonly known as the Christ's lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, intricate fronds that are typically divided into small, linear segments. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often covered in fine, hairy structures, which aid in moisture retention.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
