Lesquerella engelmannii subsp. alba

Lesquerella engelmannii subsp.

alba, commonly known as white lesquerella, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits.
