Linaria dalmatica subsp. tessala

Linaria dalmatica subsp.

tessala, commonly known as Dalmatian toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas.

The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.
