Lathyrus linifolius var. varifolius

Lathyrus linifolius var. varifolius, commonly known as the variable leaf pea, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, followed by elongated pods containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
