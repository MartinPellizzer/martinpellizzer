Limnanthemum stygium

Limnanthemum stygium, commonly known as the water starflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to humid conditions. Its leaves are usually opposite and elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Limnanthemum stygium is often found near water bodies, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
