Leucosyke forbesii

Leucosyke forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' silverleaf.

It is a small shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and compact growth habit. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellowish flowers in terminal clusters, which are followed by spherical, dry fruits.

Leucosyke forbesii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
