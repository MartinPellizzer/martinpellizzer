Lepidium juilletii

Lepidium juilletii, commonly known as the July cress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress species. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Lepidium juilletii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can also serve as a food source for certain wildlife.
