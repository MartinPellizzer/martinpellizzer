Lotus kunkelii

Lotus kunkelii, commonly known as the Chinese everlasting, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, spherical seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the plant is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Lotus kunkelii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
