Lavatera neapolitana

Lavatera neapolitana, commonly known as the Naples lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring gray-green, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy, fluffy white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are composed of numerous small petals arranged in a spherical shape, giving it a soft, cloud-like appearance.

Lavatera neapolitana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
