Laelia lundii f. alba

Laelia lundii f. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered Laelia lundii, is a hybrid orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, white petals and a pale yellow lip, which distinguish it from other varieties of Laelia. This orchid thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper light and watering conditions.

The plant is also noted for its ability to bloom reliably under suitable greenhouse conditions.
