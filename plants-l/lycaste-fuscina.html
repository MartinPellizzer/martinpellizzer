Lycaste fuscina

Lycaste fuscina, commonly known as the dark lycaste, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a deep purple hue. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, clinging to tree trunks in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of blooms, often blooming during the warmer months.

Lycaste fuscina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
