Limonium subanfractum

Limonium subanfractum, commonly known as the coastal blueflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, preferring sandy or gravelly soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in dune environments.

Limonium subanfractum is well adapted to salt spray and can thrive in harsh maritime conditions.
