Leonotis sexdentata

Leonotis sexdentata, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Leonotis sexdentata is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine.
