Laelia anceps var. kienastiana

Laelia anceps var. kienastiana, commonly known as the Kienastian orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining, acidic soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its hardiness and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.

This variety is also noted for its resilience to temperature fluctuations, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
