Lithops hookeri var. susannae

Lithops hookeri var. susannae, commonly known as the "Fairy Fan" or "Living Stone," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, leaf-like structures that resemble stones, helping it blend into its arid environment. The plant produces small, pale yellow or orange flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
