Lepanthes truncata

Lepanthes truncata, commonly known as the truncated urn orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and is often found in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single, erect spike bearing several flowers.

Lepanthes truncata is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
