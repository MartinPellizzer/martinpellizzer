Lemmaphyllum piloselloides

Lemmaphyllum piloselloides, commonly known as the silky filbert, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas due to its drought tolerance.

The plant's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
