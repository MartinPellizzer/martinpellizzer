Locheria magnifica

Locheria magnifica, commonly known as the golden torch lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, with a distinctive torch-like shape. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in moisture retention. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and indoor cultivation.
