Lygodium finlaysonianum

Lygodium finlaysonianum, commonly known as the Finlayson's water fern, is a climbing fern native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the family Lygodiaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like fronds that resemble those of a water fern. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and swampy areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat.

The species is notable for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and its unique reproductive structures.
