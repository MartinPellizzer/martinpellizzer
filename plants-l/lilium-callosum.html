Lilium callosum

Lilium callosum, commonly known as the scarlet lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers with a white throat, which bloom in late summer. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern along its stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

Lilium callosum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
