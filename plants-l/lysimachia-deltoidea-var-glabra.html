Lysimachia deltoidea var. glabra

Lysimachia deltoidea var. glabra, commonly known as smooth wood sorrel, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are smooth to the touch, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
