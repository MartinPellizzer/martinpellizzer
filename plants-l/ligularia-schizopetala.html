Ligularia schizopetala

Ligularia schizopetala, commonly known as the Chinese heartleaf, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern and a waxy surface. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in the summer, which are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and shaded landscapes.
