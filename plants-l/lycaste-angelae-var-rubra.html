Lycaste angelae var. rubra

Lycaste angelae var. rubra, commonly known as the Red Angel's Tears, is a hybrid orchid native to tropical regions of Central America.

It features striking, velvety red flowers with a contrasting white throat, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with broad, waxy leaves that are dark green and often speckled with silver. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
