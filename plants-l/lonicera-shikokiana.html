Lonicera shikokiana

Lonicera shikokiana, commonly known as the Shikokian honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to Japan.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its berries, which develop in late summer, are orange-red and contain small, hard seeds.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
