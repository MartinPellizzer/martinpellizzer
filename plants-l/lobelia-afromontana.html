Lobelia afromontana

Lobelia afromontana, commonly known as the African mountain lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its erect stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, shaded environments. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia afromontana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
