Leontodon mucronatus

Leontodon mucronatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and woodland edges.
