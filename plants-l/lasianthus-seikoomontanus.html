Lasianthus seikoomontanus

Lasianthus seikoomontanus, commonly known as the Kikuyu lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in montane regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
