Limnanthes rosea var. candida

Limnanthes rosea var. candida, commonly known as the white sunflower.

This variant is a flowering plant native to California, characterized by its large, showy white petals and yellow centers. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found near ponds or marshes. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
