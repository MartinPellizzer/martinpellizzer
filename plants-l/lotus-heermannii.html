Lotus heermannii

Lotus heermannii, commonly known as the Hermann's lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically submerged or floating, with long, slender stems. It thrives in shallow, still waters such as ponds and wetlands.

Its botanical structure supports both aquatic and terrestrial ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
