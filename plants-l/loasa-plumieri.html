Loasa plumieri

Loasa plumieri, commonly known as the velvetleaf loasa, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, often with a pink or purple hue. Loasa plumieri produces small, oval fruits that contain a single seed.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
