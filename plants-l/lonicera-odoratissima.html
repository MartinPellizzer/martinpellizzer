Lonicera odoratissima

Lonicera odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet-scented honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its berries are red and edible, often consumed by birds and small mammals.

Lonicera odoratissima is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
