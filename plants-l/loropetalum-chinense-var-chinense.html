Loropetalum chinense var. chinense

Loropetalum chinense var. chinense, commonly known as Chinese loropetalum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and its striking clusters of pink or red flowers that appear in spring. The plant has finely textured, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall, adding seasonal interest. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it suitable for hedges, borders, and foundation plantings.
