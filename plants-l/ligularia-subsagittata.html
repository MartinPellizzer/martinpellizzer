Ligularia subsagittata

Ligularia subsagittata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant typically produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in shallow water and moist soil, making it a popular choice for pond and water garden settings.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a favored plant among aquatic gardeners.
