Linaria fragrans

Linaria fragrans, commonly known as the sweet willie or sweet scentflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Linaria fragrans is valued for its ornamental appeal and fragrant blooms.
