Lonicera turcomanica

Lonicera turcomanica, commonly known as the Turkmen honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and usually ovate leaves that are dark green on top and hairy underneath. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a small, red, fleshy berry that matures in late summer.

Lonicera turcomanica is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
