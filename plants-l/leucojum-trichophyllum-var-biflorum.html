Leucojum trichophyllum var. biflorum

Leucojum trichophyllum var. biflorum, commonly known as the white squill, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and low maintenance requirements.
