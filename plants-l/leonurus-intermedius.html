Leonurus intermedius

Leonurus intermedius, commonly known as redstem hedgehog.

It is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China, India, and Nepal. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils, and it has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
