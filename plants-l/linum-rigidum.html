Linum rigidum

Linum rigidum, commonly known as rigid flax, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flax known for their showy blue or white flowers. The plant is characterized by its erect, slender stems and narrow, linear leaves. It produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees.

Linum rigidum is often found in open habitats like grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
