Lightfootia multicaulis

Lightfootia multicaulis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Tanzania.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a low, compact shrub with multiple stems arising from the base. Its leaves are ovate and have a waxy texture, while the flowers bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white.

Lightfootia multicaulis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
