Lantana crocea var. superba

Lantana crocea var. superba, commonly known as the showy lantana, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees add to its horticultural value.
