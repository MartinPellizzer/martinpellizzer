Lasiocorys royleoides

Lasiocorys royleoides, commonly known as the Kashmir rose, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities. It has a woody stem with thorny branches and produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
