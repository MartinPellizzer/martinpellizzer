Lyciodes obtusifolium

Lyciodes obtusifolium, commonly known as the blunt-leaf lycopus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with a blunt or rounded tip, giving it its species name. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and typically have a purple or blue hue.

Lyciodes obtusifolium is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
