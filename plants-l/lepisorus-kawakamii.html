Lepisorus kawakamii

Lepisorus kawakamii, commonly known as Kawakami's rock fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, creeping growth habit. The plant typically grows on rock surfaces or in shaded, moist environments, forming dense mats. Lepisorus kawakamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
