Laelia anceps stella

Laelia anceps stella, commonly known as the Stella orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes mountains.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms during the summer months and prefers cool, high-altitude environments. It has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, and its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air.

This orchid is popular among growers for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
