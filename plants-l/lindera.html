Lindera

Lindera, commonly known as spicebush, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Lauraceae.

It consists of several species, most notably Lindera benzoin and Lindera melissifolia, which are native to eastern North America. These shrubs typically have aromatic, serrated leaves and produce small, yellowish flowers in the spring. The fruits are usually red or yellow berries that contain several seeds.

Lindera species are often found in moist, wooded areas and are valued for their ornamental and ecological benefits.
