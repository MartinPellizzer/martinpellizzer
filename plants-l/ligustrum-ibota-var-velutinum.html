Ligustrum ibota var. velutinum

Ligustrum ibota var. velutinum, commonly known as the velvet privet, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, dark green, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue berries that are often consumed by birds. It is often used in landscaping due to its adaptability and dense growth habit.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
