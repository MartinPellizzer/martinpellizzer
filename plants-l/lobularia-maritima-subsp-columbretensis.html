Lobularia maritima subsp. columbretensis

Lobularia maritima subsp.

columbretensis, commonly known as the Columbretes beach flower, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in coastal environments, particularly on sandy or gravelly soils.

The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, adapted to withstand salty sea air and strong winds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes.
