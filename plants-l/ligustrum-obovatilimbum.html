Ligustrum obovatilimbum

Ligustrum obovatilimbum, commonly known as the ovalleaf privet, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by black, fleshy berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dense growth habit and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not managed properly.
