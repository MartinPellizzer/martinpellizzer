Lomaria orbiculata

Lomaria orbiculata, commonly known as the globe lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its rounded, orb-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand saline conditions.

Lomaria orbiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
