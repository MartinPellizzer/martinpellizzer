Lyonia densiflora

Lyonia densiflora, commonly known as denseflower lyonia, is a species of flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub that typically reaches heights of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features small, clustered white or pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are evergreen, leathery, and have a distinctive waxy texture.

Lyonia densiflora is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
