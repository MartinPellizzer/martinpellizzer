Lirianthe lasia

Lirianthe lasia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Lirianthe lasia is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
