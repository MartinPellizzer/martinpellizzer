Lithops naureeniae

Lithops naureeniae, commonly known as the Nauereeni Lithops, is a species of succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, stone-like leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, typically blooming from September to November. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
