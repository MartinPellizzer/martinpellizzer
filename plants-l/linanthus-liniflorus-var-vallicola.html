Linanthus liniflorus var. vallicola

Linanthus liniflorus var. vallicola, commonly known as the valley linanthus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the evening primrose family (Onagraceae) and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers.
