Linaria genistifolia var. polystachya

Linaria genistifolia var. polystachya, commonly known as the silverleaf toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves and clusters of yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is an herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas due to its drought tolerance and adaptability.

Its floral structure is similar to other members of the Plantaginaceae family, featuring showy, spurred petals.
