Lysimachia linum-stellatum

Lysimachia linum-stellatum, commonly known as starflower or yellow starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has slender, creeping stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and along stream banks.

Lysimachia linum-stellatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscaping.
