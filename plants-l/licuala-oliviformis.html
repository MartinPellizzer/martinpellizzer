Licuala oliviformis

Licuala oliviformis, commonly known as the olive-shaped palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that are broad and have a distinctive olive-green color. The plant typically grows in wet, lowland areas and thrives in humid conditions. Its trunk is relatively short and stout, often covered with persistent leaf bases.

Licuala oliviformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
