Lepanthes viebrockiana

Lepanthes viebrockiana, commonly known as the Viebrock's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that are typically spurred at the base. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size and display a variety of hues, including white, yellow, and pink.

Lepanthes viebrockiana is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique botanical features.
