Ludisia Otletae

Ludisia otletae is a rare and striking epiphytic plant native to the cloud forests of Colombia. Known for its vibrant, velvety foliage, it displays a range of colors from deep red to purple and green. This plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice among advanced growers. Its unique appearance and delicate growth habits set it apart from other orchids. Due to its limited habitat and conservation status, Ludisia otletae is both a botanical wonder and a symbol of ecological fragility.
