Licaria polyphylla

Licaria polyphylla, commonly known as the Brazilian licorice or licorice tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense foliage, with leaves that are typically opposite and elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and range in color from white to pale yellow.

The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
