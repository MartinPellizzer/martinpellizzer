Linaria masedae

Linaria masedae, commonly known as the Cape gold of the mountain, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the plantain family, Acanthaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its slender, branched stems and delicate foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Linaria masedae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
