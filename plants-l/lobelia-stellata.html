Lobelia stellata

Lobelia stellata, commonly known as star lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, star-shaped blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Lobelia stellata is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
