Lastrea arguta

Lastrea arguta, commonly known as the smooth lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
