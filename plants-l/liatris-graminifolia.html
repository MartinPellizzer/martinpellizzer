Liatris graminifolia

Liatris graminifolia, commonly known as slender blazing star, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, spikelike inflorescence composed of numerous small, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, bogs, and marshes. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy blooms in late summer to early fall.

Liatris graminifolia is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
