Lotus berthelotii var. subglabratus

Lotus berthelotii var. subglabratus, commonly known as the African lotus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is typically found in wetlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in moist, sandy soils. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, white pubescence, while the stems are slender and erect.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
