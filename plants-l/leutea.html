Leutea

Leutea, commonly known as the common vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. The plant produces small, yellowish or greenish flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated pods, typical of the legume family.

Leutea is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some regions for forage or soil stabilization.
