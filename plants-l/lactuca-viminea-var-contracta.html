Lactuca viminea var. contracta

Lactuca viminea var. contracta, commonly known as the curled lettuce, is a variant of the common lettuce species.

It is characterized by its tightly curled, compact leaves that form a dense, leafy rosette. The plant is native to temperate regions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are typically pale green with a slightly bitter taste, making it suitable for both culinary and decorative purposes.

This variety is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in cool climates.
