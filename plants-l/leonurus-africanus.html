Leonurus africanus

Leonurus africanus, commonly known as African mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features small, hairy leaves and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in grasslands and shrublands.

Leonurus africanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some African cultures.
