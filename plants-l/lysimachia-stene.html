Lysimachia stene

Lysimachia stene, commonly known as golden thread, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Lysimachia stene is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wet areas.
