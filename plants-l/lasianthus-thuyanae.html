Lasianthus thuyanae

Lasianthus thuyanae, commonly known as the Vietnamese flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the tree a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
