Leucas calostachys var. schweinfurthii

Leucas calostachys var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's leucas, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grassland habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species in the Lamiaceae family.
