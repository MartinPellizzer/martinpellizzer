Lisowskia welwitschii var. melanotoessa

Lisowskia welwitschii var. melanotoessa, commonly known as the black-leaved lisowskia, is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive dark green to almost black foliage, which contrasts sharply with its pale, sometimes hairy stems. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers, typically blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh environment.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
