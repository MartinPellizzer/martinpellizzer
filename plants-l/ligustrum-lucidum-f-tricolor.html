Ligustrum lucidum f. tricolor

Ligustrum lucidum f. tricolor, commonly known as the variegated privet, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that exhibit a striking tricolor pattern of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.

Ligustrum lucidum f. tricolor thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance.
