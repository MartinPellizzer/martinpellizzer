Lathyrus spissus

Lathyrus spissus, commonly known as the dense vetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial legume that typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers in shades of purple or pink. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Lathyrus spissus is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
