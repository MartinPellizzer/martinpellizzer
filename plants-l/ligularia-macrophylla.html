Ligularia macrophylla

Ligularia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, with a distinctive arrowhead-shaped base. The plant typically thrives in moist, shady environments, making it a popular choice for wetland gardens. Its leaves are often a deep green with a reddish tint on the underside, adding visual interest.

While it does not produce showy flowers, its foliage is the main attraction, making it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens.
