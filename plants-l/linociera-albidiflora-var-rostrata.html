Linociera albidiflora var. rostrata

Linociera albidiflora var. rostrata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved linociera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its flowers are usually arranged in clusters and bloom during the rainy season.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
