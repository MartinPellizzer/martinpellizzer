Laburnum + adami

Laburnum + adami, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Laburnum + adami is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
