Lirianthe mariusjacobsia

Lirianthe mariusjacobsia, commonly known as the Marius Jacobs lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, slender stems that support its vibrant blooms.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
