Lasianthus subinaequalis

Lasianthus subinaequalis, commonly known as the unequal-flowered lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in whorls, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
