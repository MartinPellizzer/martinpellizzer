Lastrea presliana

Lastrea presliana, commonly known as the red-stemmed nightshade, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its fruits are berries that turn red when mature, containing several seeds.

The plant is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to arid conditions.
