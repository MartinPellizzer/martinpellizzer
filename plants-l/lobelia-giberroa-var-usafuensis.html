Lobelia giberroa var. usafuensis

Lobelia giberroa var. usafuensis, commonly known as the Usambara lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Usambara Mountains in Tanzania.

It is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a yellow throat. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats within montane forests. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
