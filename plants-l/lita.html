Lita

Lita, commonly known as the common lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and red. The plant typically has strap-like leaves and produces bulbous underground stems called bulbs. Lita is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological studies.
