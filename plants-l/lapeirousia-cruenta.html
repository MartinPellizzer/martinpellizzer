Lapeirousia cruenta

Lapeirousia cruenta, commonly known as the red tussock orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red and yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has a rosette of grass-like leaves that form a dense tuft, from which the flowering stems emerge. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands.

The orchid is noted for its ornamental value and is a popular subject in botanical gardens and collections.
