Lycaste citrina

Lycaste citrina, commonly known as the yellow lycaste, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their vibrant color and pleasant fragrance.

Lycaste citrina is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
