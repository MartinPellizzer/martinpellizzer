Lysimachia minoricensis

Lysimachia minoricensis, commonly known as the Minorca primrose, is a flowering plant native to the island of Minorca in the Balearic Sea.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with delicate, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Lysimachia minoricensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
