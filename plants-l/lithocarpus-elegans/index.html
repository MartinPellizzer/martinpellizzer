Lithocarpus Elegans

Lithocarpus elegans is a striking evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its glossy dark green leaves and attractive, woody fruits, it is a popular ornamental plant in horticulture. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts and landscapers. Lithocarpus elegans not only adds visual appeal but also supports local wildlife, contributing to ecological balance.
