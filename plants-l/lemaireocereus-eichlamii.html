Lemaireocereus eichlamii

Lemaireocereus eichlamii, commonly known as the Eichler's cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and typically features white or pale pink flowers. The plant has a dense, spiny coat that helps protect it from herbivores and excessive sunlight. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Lemaireocereus eichlamii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience.
