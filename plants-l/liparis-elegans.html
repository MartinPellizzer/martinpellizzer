Liparis elegans

Liparis elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a terrestrial growth habit, with long, grass-like leaves and a rhizomatous root system. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Liparis elegans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its striking floral display.
