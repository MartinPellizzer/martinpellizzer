Leucanthemum fontanesii

Leucanthemum fontanesii, commonly known as the alpine oxeye, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its white ray florets surrounding a yellow disc floret, giving it a daisy-like appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. It produces small, herbaceous stems and has a compact growth habit.

Leucanthemum fontanesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
