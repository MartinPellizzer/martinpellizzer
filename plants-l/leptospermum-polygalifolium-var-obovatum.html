Leptospermum polygalifolium var. obovatum

Leptospermum polygalifolium var. obovatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a shrub native to Tasmania.

It features small, narrow, obovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white wax. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pale yellow to cream in color. It is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, thriving in full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
