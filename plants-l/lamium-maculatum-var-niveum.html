Lamium maculatum var. niveum

Lamium maculatum var. niveum, commonly known as white nettle or snowflake plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or silver veining pattern, which gives it its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in spring. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, contributing to its value in landscaping.
