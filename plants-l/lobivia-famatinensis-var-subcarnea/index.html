Lobivia Famatinensis Var. Subcarnea

Lobivia famatinensis var. subcarnea is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of northern Mexico. This unique variety is distinguished by its pale pink to white stems and a dense covering of short, woolly hairs. It thrives in harsh desert conditions, showcasing remarkable adaptations to survive with minimal water. Its beauty makes it a sought-after specimen among cactus enthusiasts and collectors. Despite its allure, it remains under-studied and often overlooked in botanical research.
