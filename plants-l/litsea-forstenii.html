Litsea forstenii

Litsea forstenii, commonly known as the Malayan litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia, where it thrives in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruits are small, round, and contain a single seed, which is dispersed by animals.

Litsea forstenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
