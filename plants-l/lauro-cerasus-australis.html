Lauro-cerasus australis

Lauro-cerasus australis, commonly known as the Australian cherry laurel, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and subtropical regions. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring. Its fruit is a small, red drupe that ripens in summer.

Lauro-cerasus australis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping due to its adaptability and resilience.
