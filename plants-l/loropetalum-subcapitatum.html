Loropetalum subcapitatum

Loropetalum subcapitatum, commonly known as the Chinese loropetalum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and emit a subtle fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The shrub is known for its compact growth habit and seasonal display of flowers and foliage.
