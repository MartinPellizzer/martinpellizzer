Lissochilus antunesii

Lissochilus antunesii, commonly known as the Antunes' slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pouch-shaped lip, a feature typical of many slipper orchids. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and moderate humidity.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
