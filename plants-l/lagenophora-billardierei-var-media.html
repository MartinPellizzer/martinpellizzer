Lagenophora billardierei var. media

Lagenophora billardierei var. media, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lagenophora, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or heathlands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
