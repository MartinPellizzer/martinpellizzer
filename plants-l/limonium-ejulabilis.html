Limonium ejulabilis

Limonium ejulabilis, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its spiny, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime environments. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.

Limonium ejulabilis is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh conditions.
