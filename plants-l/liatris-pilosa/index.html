Liatris Pilosa

Liatris pilosa, also known as prairie blazingstar, is a native flowering plant that thrives in open prairies and dry woodlands. It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Liatris pilosa is also an important species for ecological restoration projects due to its hardiness and low maintenance requirements. Its presence in native habitats highlights the importance of preserving diverse plant communities.
