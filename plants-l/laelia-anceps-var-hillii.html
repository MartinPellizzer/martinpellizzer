Laelia anceps var. hillii

Laelia anceps var. hillii, commonly known as the Hill's Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically range from yellow to orange with red and purple markings. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in a tropical climate.
