Leucojum fontianum

Leucojum fontianum, commonly known as the snowflake lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Europe.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that resemble snowflakes. The plant is characterized by its fragrant blooms, which appear in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Leucojum fontianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
