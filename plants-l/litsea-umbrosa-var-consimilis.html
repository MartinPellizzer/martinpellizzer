Litsea umbrosa var. consimilis

Litsea umbrosa var. consimilis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Chinese laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are opposite and elliptical, with a smooth surface and a strong, spicy aroma.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
