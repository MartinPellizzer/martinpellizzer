Ligularia emeiensis

Ligularia emeiensis, commonly known as the Emei Mountain Ligularia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its striking foliage and ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
