Linaria microcalyx var. orphanidiana

Linaria microcalyx var. orphanidiana, commonly known as the dwarf toadflax, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred petal, typical of the Linaria genus. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is widely cultivated in gardens across temperate climates.
