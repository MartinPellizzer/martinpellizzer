Laurus cymosa

Laurus cymosa, commonly known as sweet bay or Turkish bay, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green, glossy leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Laurus cymosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its pleasant fragrance and compact growth habit.
