Linochilus mutiscuanus

Linochilus mutiscuanus, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, intricate flowers that often feature a mix of colors, including white, yellow, and purple. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests or on rocky slopes.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
