Linaria aeruginea var. atrofusca

Linaria aeruginea var. atrofusca, commonly known as the dark-flowered linaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, erect stems that bear small, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The flowers are typically dark purple to almost black, with a distinctive bilabiate structure, and bloom in late spring to early summer. This variety is often found in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

It is valued in horticulture for its striking color and ability to attract pollinators.
