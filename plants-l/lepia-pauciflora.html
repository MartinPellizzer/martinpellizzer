Lepia pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered lepia, is a unique member of the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance and compact growth habit.

Lepia pauciflora is relatively rare and is often found in specific ecological niches, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
