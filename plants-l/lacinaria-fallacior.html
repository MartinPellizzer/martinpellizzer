Lacinaria fallacior

Lacinaria fallacior, commonly known as the false lichen, is a species of lichen that exhibits a unique symbiotic relationship between a fungus and a green alga.

It typically grows on rocks, trees, or soil in temperate and subtropical regions. The plant's thallus is usually crust-like or foliose, with a firm, leathery texture. Its color ranges from pale green to dark green, depending on environmental conditions.

Lacinaria fallacior plays a role in nutrient cycling and serves as a habitat for various microorganisms.
