Licuala klossii

Licuala klossii, commonly known as the Kloss palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in lowland rainforests. Its trunk is short and stout, often covered with remnants of old leaf bases.

Licuala klossii is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural and traditional significance in its native region.
