Liriope graminifolia var. albiflora

Liriope graminifolia var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered lilyturf, is a perennial grass-like plant native to East Asia.

It features grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and grass-green, forming dense clumps. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers during the summer months. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.

This variety is prized for its low maintenance and ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions.
