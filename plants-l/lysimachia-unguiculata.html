Lysimachia unguiculata

Lysimachia unguiculata, commonly known as yellow loosestrife, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy surface.

Lysimachia unguiculata is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies.
