Lonicera periclymenum f. verticillata

Lonicera periclymenum f. verticillata, commonly known as the creeping honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a compact, trailing shrub that produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

The flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
