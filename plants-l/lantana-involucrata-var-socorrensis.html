Lantana involucrata var. socorrensis

Lantana involucrata var. socorrensis, commonly known as Socorro lantana, is a shrub native to the Caribbean island of Socorro.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow, often with a subtle pink or red tinge. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, dry fruits called capsules, which contain numerous tiny seeds.

This species is adapted to coastal and rocky habitats, showing resilience to saline and drought conditions.
