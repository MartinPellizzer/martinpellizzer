Linaria alpina var. odoratissima

Linaria alpina var. odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet willie or sweet linaria, is a perennial flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It produces vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive bicolor pattern. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its fragrant foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental value.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
