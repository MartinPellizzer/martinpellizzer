Lespedeza japonica f. retusa

Lespedeza japonica f. retusa, commonly known as Japanese lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, pink or reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets.

Lespedeza japonica f. retusa is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and floral display.
