Lepidium pinnatum

Lepidium pinnatum, commonly known as the slender cress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky environments and features small, delicate white or yellowish flowers. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a feathery appearance.

Lepidium pinnatum is often found in alpine regions and is adapted to thrive in cooler climates.
