Ludisia discolor var. ordiana

Ludisia discolor var. ordiana, commonly known as the Ordiana's flame orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

This variant is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which contrast beautifully with its dark green, velvety leaves. It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is known for its epiphytic growth habit, often growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates with high air circulation and indirect light.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
