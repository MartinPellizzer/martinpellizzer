Lachenalia unicolor var. purpurea

Lachenalia unicolor var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple-flowered lachenalia, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, branched stems topped with clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically a vibrant purple or pink, with a distinctive yellowish throat, adding to its ornamental appeal. This species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

It is often cultivated for its dramatic blooms and is a favorite among succulent enthusiasts.
