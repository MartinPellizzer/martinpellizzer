Ligustrum massalongianum var. lindleyi

Ligustrum massalongianum var. lindleyi, commonly known as the Lindley's privet, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a glossy dark green surface. It produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue berries that are edible for birds.
