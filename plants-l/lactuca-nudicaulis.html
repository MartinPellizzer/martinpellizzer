Lactuca nudicaulis

Lactuca nudicaulis, commonly known as the nakedstem lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its upright, slender stems that are nearly hairless, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a single head, typical of the Compositae family. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in a rosette at the base.

Lactuca nudicaulis is native to Europe and Asia and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
