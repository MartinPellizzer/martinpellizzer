Liparis punctilabris

Liparis punctilabris, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and forest floors. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and often have distinctive dark spots or markings.

Liparis punctilabris is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
