Lastrea buchananii

Lastrea buchananii, commonly known as the Buchanan's lastrea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow-orange flower heads. The plant produces daisy-like blooms that consist of a central disc surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in the wild.
