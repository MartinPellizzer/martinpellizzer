Lysimachia fragrans

Lysimachia fragrans, commonly known as sweet cicely, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a member of the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, fern-like leaves and small, fragrant white or yellow flowers. Lysimachia fragrans is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its use in herbal medicine.
