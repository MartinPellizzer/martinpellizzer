Liparis togashii

Liparis togashii, commonly known as the Japanese marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its roots are tuberous, and it produces a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves.

Liparis togashii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native environment.
