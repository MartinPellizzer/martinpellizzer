Lockhartia tenuiflora

Lockhartia tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pale green or white, with a slight pinkish tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Lockhartia tenuiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
