Lathyrus setifolius proles gouanii

Lathyrus setifolius proles gouanii, commonly known as the Gouanii grass vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in early summer. The plant produces cylindrical pods containing several elliptical seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
