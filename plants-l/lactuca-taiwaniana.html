Lactuca taiwaniana

Lactuca taiwaniana, commonly known as Taiwanese lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Taiwan and is characterized by its rosette of spoon-shaped, pale green leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the dandelion family. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and streambanks.

Lactuca taiwaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its edible leaves.
