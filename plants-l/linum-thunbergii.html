Linum thunbergii

Linum thunbergii, commonly known as the blue flax or Japanese flax, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, oval seeds. Linum thunbergii prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
