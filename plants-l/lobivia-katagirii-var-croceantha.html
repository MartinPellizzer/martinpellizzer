Lobivia katagirii var. croceantha

Lobivia katagirii var. croceantha, commonly known as the Croceantha Lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features compact, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white to pale greenish-blue spines. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are short and thick, adapted to store water in its native desert environment.

It is prized by collectors for its unique appearance and hardy nature.
