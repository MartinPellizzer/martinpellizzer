Lissochilus barombensis

Lissochilus barombensis, commonly known as the Barombi orchid, is a flowering plant native to the highland regions of Cameroon.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in montane forests. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns on the petals.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
