Licuala distans

Licuala distans, commonly known as the broad-leafed palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in diameter. The plant typically grows in wet, tropical environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas. Its trunk is short and stout, with a fibrous covering, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered fruits.

Licuala distans is valued for its ornamental leaves and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
