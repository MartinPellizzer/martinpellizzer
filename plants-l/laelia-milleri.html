Laelia milleri

Laelia milleri, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright, grass-like growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant typically blooms during the winter and early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. It prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light to thrive.

Laelia milleri is known for its resilience and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
