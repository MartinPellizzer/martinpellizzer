Lindelofia spectabilis var. falconeri

Lindelofia spectabilis var. falconeri, commonly known as the Falconeri's Lindelofia, is a rare flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky slopes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, which helps reflect sunlight.
