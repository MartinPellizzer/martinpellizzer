Lycium deserti

Lycium deserti, commonly known as the desert枸杞, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of China.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellow-orange berries. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh desert conditions, with deep roots that help it access underground water.
