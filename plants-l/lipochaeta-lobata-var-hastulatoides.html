Lipochaeta lobata var. hastulatoides

Lipochaeta lobata var. hastulatoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lipochaeta, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture, and it produces small, yellowish flowers.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
