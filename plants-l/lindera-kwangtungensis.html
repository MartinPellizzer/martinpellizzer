Lindera kwangtungensis

Lindera kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong Lindera, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to southern China, particularly the Guangdong province, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its fruits are small, dark berries that mature in the fall, and the plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
