Lepisorus thunbergianus var. angustus

Lepisorus thunbergianus var. angustus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to regions of East Asia, including China and Japan. The plant typically grows on rock surfaces or in shaded, moist environments, thriving in humid climates. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
