Lychnis gracillima

Lychnis gracillima, commonly known as the slender campion, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, typically with a hairy surface. The plant produces small, pink to white flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Lychnis gracillima is known for its delicate appearance and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic value.
