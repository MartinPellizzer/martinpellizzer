Lepanthes erythroxantha

Lepanthes erythroxantha, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its striking red petals and a delicate, fragrant flower structure. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique adaptations to its arboreal habitat.

Due to its beauty and rarity, Lepanthes erythroxantha is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
