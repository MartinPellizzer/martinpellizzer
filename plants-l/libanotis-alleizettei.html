Libanotis alleizettei

Libanotis alleizettei, commonly known as the Zambian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered blooms.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ornamental species.
