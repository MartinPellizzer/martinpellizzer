Loasa illapelina var. laciniata

Loasa illapelina var. laciniata, commonly known as the laceleaf loasa, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Chile.

It features finely dissected, palmately lobed leaves that give it a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub habitats.

Its unique foliage makes it a distinctive and ornamental species in its native environment.
