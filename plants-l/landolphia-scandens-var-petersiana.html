Landolphia scandens var. petersiana

Landolphia scandens var. petersiana, commonly known as the Petersian flame vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its long, trailing stems and is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains seeds covered in a sticky, viscid substance.
