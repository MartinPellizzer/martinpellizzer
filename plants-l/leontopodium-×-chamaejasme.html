Leontopodium × chamaejasme

Leontopodium × chamaejasme, commonly known as the edelweiss, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the Alps and is characterized by its dense, woolly white foliage and showy yellow flower heads. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions and well-drained soil. Its unique appearance makes it a symbol of resilience and alpine beauty.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species in many regions.
