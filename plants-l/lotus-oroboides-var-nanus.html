Lotus oroboides var. nanus

Lotus oroboides var. nanus, commonly known as the dwarf water lily, is a compact aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, floating leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom above the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often in ponds or wetlands.

Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system that helps anchor it in the substrate.
