Leucadendron album

Leucadendron album, commonly known as the silver leaf leucadendron, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white foliage and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Proteaceae family and is well adapted to Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
