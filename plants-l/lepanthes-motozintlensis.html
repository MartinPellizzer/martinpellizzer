Lepanthes motozintlensis

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests. Its flowers are often pale pink or white with intricate patterns, and it has a compact, terrestrial growth habit.
