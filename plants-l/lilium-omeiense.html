Lilium omeiense

Lilium omeiense, commonly known as the Omei Lily, is a species of lily native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in horticultural displays.
