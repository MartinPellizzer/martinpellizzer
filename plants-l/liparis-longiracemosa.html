Liparis longiracemosa

Liparis longiracemosa, commonly known as the long raceme orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The orchid's unique floral structure and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
