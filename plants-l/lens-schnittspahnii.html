Lens schnittspahnii

Lens schnittspahnii, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lentil, is a flowering plant in the legume family.

It is characterized by its small, lens-shaped seeds and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.

This species is valued for its nutritional content and is cultivated for both food and forage purposes.
