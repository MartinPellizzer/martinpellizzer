Linum kaynakiae

Linum kaynakiae, commonly known as the Kaynak's flax, is a rare flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Linaceae family, which includes other flax species. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and delicate blue or purple flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
