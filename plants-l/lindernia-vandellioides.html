Lindernia vandellioides

Lindernia vandellioides, commonly known as the Vandelli's lindernia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Linderniaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-white to purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.

Lindernia vandellioides is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to cascade from containers or hanging baskets.
