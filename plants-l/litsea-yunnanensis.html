Litsea yunnanensis

Litsea yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan litsea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas at moderate elevations.

Litsea yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
