Lomelosia Divaricata

Lomelosia orchid, scientifically known as Lomelosia divaricata, is a rare and unique species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This captivating orchid is distinguished by its intricate floral structure and vibrant coloration, making it a favorite among botanical enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, Lomelosia divaricata is critically endangered due to habitat destruction and over-collection. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this elusive plant. Its survival is essential for maintaining biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
