Ludwigia ravenii

Ludwigia ravenii, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more intense under strong lighting and nutrient-rich conditions. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions.

Ludwigia ravenii is also known for its rapid growth rate and ease of propagation through stem cuttings.
