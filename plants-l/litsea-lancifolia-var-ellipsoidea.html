Litsea lancifolia var. ellipsoidea

Litsea lancifolia var. ellipsoidea, commonly known as the broad-leafed litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is characterized by its elliptical, lance-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, dark blue berries. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
