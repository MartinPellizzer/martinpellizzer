Limonium cyrenaicum

Limonium cyrenaicum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Limonium cyrenaicum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
