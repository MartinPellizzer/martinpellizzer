Lastrea elegans

Lastrea elegans, commonly known as the elegant lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and pointed, reducing water loss through transpiration.

Lastrea elegans is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
