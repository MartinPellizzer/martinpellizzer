Lysimachia × polyantha

Lysimachia × polyantha, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a hybrid evergreen perennial shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that provide ornamental value throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Lysimachia × polyantha is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ability to stabilize soil in wet areas.
