Limonium solanderi

Limonium solanderi, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Europe and North Africa.

It is characterized by its striking spikes of blue or purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage and is adapted to saline and well-drained soils. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Limonium solanderi is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
