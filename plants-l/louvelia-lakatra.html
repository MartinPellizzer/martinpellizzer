Louvelia lakatra

Louvelia lakatra, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to purify the air.
