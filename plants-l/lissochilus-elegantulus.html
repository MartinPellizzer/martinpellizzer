Lissochilus elegantulus

Lissochilus elegantulus, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions and is often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single inflorescence with several flowers.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
