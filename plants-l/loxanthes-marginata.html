Loxanthes marginata

Loxanthes marginata, commonly known as the marginate loxanthe, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with contrasting margins. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Loxanthes marginata thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
