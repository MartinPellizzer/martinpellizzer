Leucaena × spontanea

Leucaena × spontanea, commonly known as the wild leucaena, is a hybrid shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions, making it useful for erosion control. It produces pods containing edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients.

However, it can be invasive in some areas, posing a challenge to native vegetation.
