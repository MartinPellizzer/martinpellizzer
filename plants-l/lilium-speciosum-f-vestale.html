Lilium speciosum f. vestale

Lilium speciosum f. vestale, commonly known as the White Lily, is a striking variant of the species Lilium speciosum.

It is characterized by its large, pure white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
