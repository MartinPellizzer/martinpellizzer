Lithospermum graminifolium

Lithospermum graminifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved lithospermum, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It features slender, creeping stems that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread across rocky or sandy soils. Lithospermum graminifolium is adapted to survive in harsh environments, with deep root systems that help it access water from deeper soil layers.
