Ladenbergia sericophylla

Ladenbergia sericophylla, commonly known as the silky-leafed legume, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its finely textured, silvery-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
