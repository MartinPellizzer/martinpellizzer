Lasianthemum bijugum

Lasianthemum bijugum, commonly known as the twin-leaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue or purple. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, clustered fruits known as capsules.
