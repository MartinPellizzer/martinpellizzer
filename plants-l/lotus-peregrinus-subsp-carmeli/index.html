Lotus Peregrinus Subsp. Carmeli

The lotus peregrinus subsp. carmeli is a rare and striking aquatic plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is known for its large, showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. This subspecies thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
