Lyperanthus suaveolens

Lyperanthus suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from poor soil conditions.

Lyperanthus suaveolens is valued for its ornamental beauty and unique aromatic properties.
