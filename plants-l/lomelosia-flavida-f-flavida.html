Lomelosia flavida f. flavida

Lomelosia flavida f. flavida, commonly known as the yellow-flowered lomelosia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in countries like Ecuador and Peru, this plant thrives in high-altitude environments. The inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small disk flowers surrounded by the showy yellow petals.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
