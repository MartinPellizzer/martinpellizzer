Limonium Scoparium Var. Meyeri

Sea lavender, scientifically known as Limonium scoparium var. meyeri, is a hardy coastal plant that thrives in salty, windy environments. It is commonly found along shorelines and dunes where it plays a vital role in stabilizing sand and preventing erosion. With its clusters of blue or purple flowers, it adds a striking visual element to maritime landscapes. The plant's ability to tolerate harsh conditions makes it a resilient symbol of coastal ecosystems. Its beauty and ecological importance have made it a favorite among botanists and landscape designers alike.
