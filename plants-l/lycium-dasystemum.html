Lycium dasystemum

Lycium dasystemum, commonly known as the Chinese boxthorn, is a shrub native to China and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species adapted to harsh conditions.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and useful in herbal remedies.
