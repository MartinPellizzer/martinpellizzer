Lepidium strictum

Lepidium strictum, commonly known as narrowleaf wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters and bloom in the spring.

Lepidium strictum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
