Lotus valdepilosus

Lotus valdepilosus, commonly known as the silky lotus, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to parts of South America, particularly in regions with wetland habitats. The plant is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy pink to purple flowers. It thrives in moist environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
