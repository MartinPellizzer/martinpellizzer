Lyrolepis diae

Lyrolepis diae, commonly known as the slender leaf lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its thin, leaf-like thallus with a distinctive greenish-yellow color. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship with algae, which provides the necessary photosynthetic function. It is typically found on tree bark, rocks, and soil in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its delicate structure, it is sensitive to environmental changes and air pollution.
