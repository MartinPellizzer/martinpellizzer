Loxidium galegifolium

Loxidium galegifolium, commonly known as the golden ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Loxidium galegifolium is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to arid conditions.
