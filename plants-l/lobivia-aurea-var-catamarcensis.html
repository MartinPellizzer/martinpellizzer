Lobivia aurea var. catamarcensis

Lobivia aurea var. catamarcensis, commonly known as the Catamarcensis Lobivia, is a succulent native to Argentina.

It features cylindrical stems with a pale green to yellowish coloration, often adorned with reddish or purple markings. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
