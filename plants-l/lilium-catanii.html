Lilium catanii

Lilium catanii, commonly known as the Catanii lily, is a rare and striking species native to the high altitudes of the Eastern Mediterranean.

This perennial plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem, contributing to its elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Lilium catanii is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
