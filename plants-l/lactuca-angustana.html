Lactuca angustana

Lactuca angustana, commonly known as narrowleaf lettuce, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa, and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Lactuca angustana is an annual or biennial plant that can be invasive in some regions.
