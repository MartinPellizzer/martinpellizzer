Lepanthes mekynochila

Lepanthes mekynochila, commonly known as the Mekynochila orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically display a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments, often growing as an epiphyte on trees or rocks. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its natural habitat.

The orchid's blooming period varies depending on the region but often occurs during the rainy season.
