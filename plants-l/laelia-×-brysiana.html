Laelia × brysiana

Laelia × brysiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Brassia species.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes, thriving in high-altitude environments. The plant features dense, compact growth with broad, leathery leaves. Its flowers are typically large, showy, and come in a variety of colors including white, pink, and yellow.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
