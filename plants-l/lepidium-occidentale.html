Lepidium occidentale

Lepidium occidentale, commonly known as Western cress, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and has small, green, lobed leaves. Its flowers are white or pale yellow and arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round, peppery fruits.

It is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and along roadsides.
