Lepanthes schizura

Lepanthes schizura, commonly known as the Schizura orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical forests. Its flowers are usually small, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale pink.

Lepanthes schizura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
