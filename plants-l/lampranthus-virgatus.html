Lampranthus virgatus

Lampranthus virgatus, commonly known as the Firecracker Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range from red to orange. The plant has fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help reduce water loss.
