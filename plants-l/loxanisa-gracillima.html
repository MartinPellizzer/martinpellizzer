Loxanisa gracillima

Loxanisa gracillima, commonly known as the slender lichen, is a rare and delicate species of lichen that thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its finely branched thallus and a pale to dark green coloration. This plant forms a symbiotic relationship with algae, which provides it with essential nutrients through photosynthesis. Loxanisa gracillima is typically found on tree trunks and rocks in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its fragile nature, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes and is considered an indicator species for ecosystem health.
