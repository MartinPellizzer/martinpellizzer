Leucospermum oleifolium

Leucospermum oleifolium, commonly known as the yellow star flower, is a South African native plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand dry conditions. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to coastal and sandy soils.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
