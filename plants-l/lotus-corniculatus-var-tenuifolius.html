Lotus corniculatus var. tenuifolius

Lotus corniculatus var. tenuifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved birdsfoot trefoil, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, and its leaves are characterized by their slender, trifoliate structure. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration due to its nitrogen-fixing abilities.

Its seeds are also used in some traditional herbal remedies.
