Lomaria capensis var. bauerlenii

Lomaria capensis var. bauerlenii, commonly known as the Cape lomaria, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its succulent leaves and flowering stems. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.
