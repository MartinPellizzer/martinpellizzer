Leptospermum sericophyllum

Leptospermum sericophyllum, commonly known as silver-leaved tea tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Tasmania and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine habitats.

The leaves are used in traditional herbal remedies and are also valued for their aromatic properties.
