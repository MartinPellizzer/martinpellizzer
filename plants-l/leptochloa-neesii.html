Leptochloa neesii

Leptochloa neesii, commonly known as the slender bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland areas near water bodies. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color, and its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are loosely arranged. It is characterized by its fine texture and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Leptochloa neesii is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
