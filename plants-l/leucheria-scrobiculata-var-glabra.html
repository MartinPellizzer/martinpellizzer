Leucheria scrobiculata var. glabra

Leucheria scrobiculata var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth leucheria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
