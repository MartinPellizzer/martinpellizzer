Lessingianthus valenzuelae

Lessingianthus valenzuelae, commonly known as the Valenzuela's sunflower, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
