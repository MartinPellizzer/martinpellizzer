Luffa subangulata

Luffa subangulata, commonly known as the angular luffa, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, angular stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that give way to elongated, fibrous fruits. The fruit matures to a pale green color and can be harvested when it is fully dry.

It is valued for its spongy interior, which is used in various culinary and cosmetic applications.
