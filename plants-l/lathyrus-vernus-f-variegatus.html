Lathyrus vernus f. variegatus

Lathyrus vernus f. variegatus, commonly known as the variegated sweet pea, is a flowering plant native to Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of fragrant, pea-shaped flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a distinctive variegated pattern of green and white. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
