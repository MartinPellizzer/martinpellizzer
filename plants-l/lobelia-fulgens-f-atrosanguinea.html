Lobelia fulgens f. atrosanguinea

Lobelia fulgens f. atrosanguinea, commonly known as the dark red cardinal flower, is a striking perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its vivid, tubular red flowers that bloom in late summer.
