Lysimachia lichiangensis var. robusta

Lysimachia lichiangensis var. robusta, commonly known as the robust lichiang yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
