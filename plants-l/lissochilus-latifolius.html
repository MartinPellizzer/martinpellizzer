Lissochilus latifolius

Lissochilus latifolius, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its flowers are usually clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the warmer months.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
