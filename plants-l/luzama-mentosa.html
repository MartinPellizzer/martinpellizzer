Luzama mentosa

Luzama mentosa, commonly known as the white-flowered luzama, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its simple, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

Luzama mentosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
