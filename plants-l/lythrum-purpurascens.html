Lythrum purpurascens

Lythrum purpurascens, commonly known as rosepink lythrum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family, which includes plants known for their showy, often vibrant flowers. The plant features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of pink to purplish-red flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

Lythrum purpurascens is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in wet meadows.
