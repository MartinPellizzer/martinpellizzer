Lobelia filiformis var. rusticana

Lobelia filiformis var. rusticana, commonly known as the slender lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, tubular flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators like butterflies and bees.
