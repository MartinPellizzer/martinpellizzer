Lunania ekmanii

Lunania ekmanii, commonly known as the Cape Town daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence composed of white ray florets surrounding a yellow disc. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Lunania ekmanii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
