Laurembergia walkeri

Laurembergia walkeri, commonly known as the African violets, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid environments. It has a compact growth habit and is popular for its ornamental value.

Laurembergia walkeri requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight to thrive.
