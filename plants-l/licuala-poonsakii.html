Licuala poonsakii

Licuala poonsakii, commonly known as the Poon Sakii palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant typically grows in wet, lowland areas and thrives in humid climates with well-drained soil. Its trunk is slender and covered with persistent leaf bases, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking foliage.
