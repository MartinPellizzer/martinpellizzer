Linum perenne f. albiflorum

Linum perenne f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered flax, is a perennial cultivar of the flax plant.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers, which contrast with its broad, dark green foliage. The plant produces slender, fibrous stems that can reach heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in fiber production.
