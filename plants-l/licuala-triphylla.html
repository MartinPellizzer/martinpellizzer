Licuala triphylla

Licuala triphylla, commonly known as the three-leaf palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive three-lobed leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant grows as an ornamental species, valued for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
