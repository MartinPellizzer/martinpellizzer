Limonium athinense

Limonium athinense, commonly known as the Thessalian sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Aegean region of Greece.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and sandy environments. The plant features upright, branched stems and produces clusters of blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Limonium athinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its colorful blooms.
