Ledebouria junnarensis

Ledebouria junnarensis, commonly known as the Junnare bulbous lily, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent and bulb enthusiasts.
