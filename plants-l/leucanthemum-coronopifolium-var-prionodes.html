Leucanthemum coronopifolium var. prionodes

Leucanthemum coronopifolium var. prionodes, commonly known as the narrow-leafed oxeye daisy, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America.

It features showy white ray flowers surrounded by yellow disk flowers, typical of the Asteraceae family. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in open woodlands and meadows.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
