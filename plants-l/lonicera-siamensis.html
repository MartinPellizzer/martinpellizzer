Lonicera siamensis

Lonicera siamensis, commonly known as the Siam honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, elliptic to ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Lonicera siamensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
