Lepidium pseudopapillosum

Lepidium pseudopapillosum, commonly known as the narrowleaf shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family plants. The plant is characterized by its small, erect stems and pinnately divided leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow and occur in clusters, and it produces small, rounded pods that contain several seeds.
