Lobivia klimpeliana

Lobivia klimpeliana, commonly known as the Klimpeliana Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of Argentina.

It features a cylindrical stem that is typically greenish-gray in color, adorned with sparse, pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range from pink to purple, typically blooming in spring. It is well adapted to harsh desert conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
