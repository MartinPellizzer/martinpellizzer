Lemeea haworthioides

Lemeea haworthioides, commonly known as the Haworthia-like leaf plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and fleshy, thick leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or pale markings. The leaves are typically a bluish-green color, providing a striking visual contrast against the lighter patterns. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
