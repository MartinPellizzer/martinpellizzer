Lindernia yaoshanensis

Lindernia yaoshanensis, commonly known as the Yaoshan Lindernia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Linderniaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale pink to white flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
