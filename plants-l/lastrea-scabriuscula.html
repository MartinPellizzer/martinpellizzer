Lastrea scabriuscula

Lastrea scabriuscula, commonly known as the rough-leaved lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is characterized by its fleshy leaves and adaptations to arid environments. The plant features small, gray-green, spine-tipped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months.
