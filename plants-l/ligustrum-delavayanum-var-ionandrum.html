Ligustrum delavayanum var. ionandrum

Ligustrum delavayanum var. ionandrum, commonly known as the Ionandrum privet, is a shrub native to the southern United States.

It features small, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely on the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for hedges and ornamental landscaping.
