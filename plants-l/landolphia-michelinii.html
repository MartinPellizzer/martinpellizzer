Landolphia michelinii

Landolphia michelinii, commonly known as the African Crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, yellow-orange flowers arranged in a crown-like inflorescence. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Landolphia michelinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
