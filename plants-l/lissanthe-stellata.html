Lissanthe stellata

Lissanthe stellata, commonly known as star flower, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, arranged in clusters. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Lissanthe stellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
