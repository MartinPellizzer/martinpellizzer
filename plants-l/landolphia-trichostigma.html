Landolphia trichostigma

Landolphia trichostigma, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple, often opening in the early morning. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind and animals. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Landolphia trichostigma is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
