Lysiandra Involuta

Lysiandra involuta is a rare and delicate flowering plant known for its intricate, spiral-shaped petals. Native to the misty highlands of Southeast Asia, it thrives in cool, shaded environments. Its unique structure has captivated botanists and plant lovers alike for centuries. The plant's name, derived from Greek, reflects its elegant and winding form. Due to habitat loss, Lysiandra involuta is now classified as endangered, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
