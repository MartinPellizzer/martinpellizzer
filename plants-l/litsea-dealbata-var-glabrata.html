Litsea dealbata var. glabrata

Litsea dealbata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Vietnam, Cambodia, and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are smooth and glossy on both sides. It produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by small, rounded fruits.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
