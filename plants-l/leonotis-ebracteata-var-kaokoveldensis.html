Leonotis ebracteata var. kaokoveldensis

Leonotis ebracteata var. kaokoveldensis, commonly known as the Kaokoveld larkspur, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
