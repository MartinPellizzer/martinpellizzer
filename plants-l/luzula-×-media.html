Luzula × media

Luzula × media, commonly known as the intermediate woodrush, is a hybrid plant species within the grass family (Poaceae).

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and slender, often silver-gray stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish to brownish flowers. Luzula × media is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
