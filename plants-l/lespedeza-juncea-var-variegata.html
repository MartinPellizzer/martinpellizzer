Lespedeza juncea var. variegata

Lespedeza juncea var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive variegated pattern of green and white. Its small, pinkish-white flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
