Lafoensia replicata f. pohlii

Lafoensia replicata f. pohlii, commonly known as the Pohlii flame tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a variant of Lafoensia replicata, characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in dense, showy clusters. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets that provide a lush green backdrop to its flamboyant flowers.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical gardens.
