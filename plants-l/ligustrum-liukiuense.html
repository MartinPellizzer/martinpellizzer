Ligustrum liukiuense

Ligustrum liukiuense, commonly known as the Ryukyu privet, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 2 to 4 meters, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, dark berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often used in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
