Limonium tianschanicum

Limonium tianschanicum, commonly known as the Tianshan sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Tianshan mountain range in Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white powder.

Limonium tianschanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
