Litsea versteeghii

Litsea versteeghii, commonly known as the Malayan litsea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically dark red when mature, containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forests.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
