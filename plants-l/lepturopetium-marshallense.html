Lepturopetium marshallense

Lepturopetium marshallense, commonly known as Marshall's leucaena, is a flowering plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is typically shrub-like, with woody stems and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
