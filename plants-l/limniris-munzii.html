Limniris munzii

Limniris munzii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly California.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. Limniris munzii is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in areas with well-drained soil.
