Leucospermum cordatum

Leucospermum cordatum, commonly known as the firewheel plant, is a flowering shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red, tubular flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

The flowers attract birds and insects, contributing to its ecological importance in its native habitat.
