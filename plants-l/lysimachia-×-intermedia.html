Lysimachia × intermedia

Lysimachia × intermedia, commonly known as golden thread, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has creeping stems that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread across the ground. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.
