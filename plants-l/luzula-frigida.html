Luzula frigida

Luzula frigida, commonly known as the alpine goatgrass, is a perennial flowering plant native to cold regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

This plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils at high elevations. Luzula frigida produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish or brownish flowers, which are adapted for wind pollination.
