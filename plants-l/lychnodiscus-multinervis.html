Lychnodiscus multinervis

Lychnodiscus multinervis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.
