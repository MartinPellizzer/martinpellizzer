Linodendron aronifolium

Linodendron aronifolium, commonly known as the golden leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves that often display a striking golden hue, especially under sunlight. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warm seasons. Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

However, it requires well-drained soil and a humid environment to thrive.
