Lysimachia verticillata

Lysimachia verticillata, commonly known as whorled chickweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with slender stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in whorls around the stem, giving it its common name. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, often with hairy surfaces.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
