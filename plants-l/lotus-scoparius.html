Lotus scoparius

Lotus scoparius, commonly known as the water lentil, is an aquatic plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its small, floating leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in diameter. The plant produces tiny yellow flowers that bloom above the water surface. Lotus scoparius is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats.

It is valued for its ability to thrive in nutrient-rich environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
