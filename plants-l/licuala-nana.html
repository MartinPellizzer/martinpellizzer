Licuala nana

Licuala nana, commonly known as the dwarf palm or small-leaf palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its short stature, typically reaching only about 1 meter in height, with a cluster of narrow, feather-like leaves. Its trunk is short and often covered with remnants of old leaf bases, giving it a unique appearance.

Licuala nana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact size.
