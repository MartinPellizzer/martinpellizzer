Lonicera × micranthoides

Lonicera × micranthoides, commonly known as red honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to red, depending on the variety. These flowers are followed by small, orange-red berries that are attractive to birds.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
