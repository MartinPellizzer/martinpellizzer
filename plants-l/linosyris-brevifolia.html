Linosyris brevifolia

Linosyris brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved linosyris, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Linosyris brevifolia is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its resilience in harsh environments.
