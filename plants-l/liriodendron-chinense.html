Liriodendron chinense

Liriodendron chinense, commonly known as the Chinese tulip tree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow-green flowers that resemble tulips. The tree has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The plant is notable for its rapid growth and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking floral display.
