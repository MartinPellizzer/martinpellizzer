Lepidium hirtum var. afrum

Lepidium hirtum var. afrum, commonly known as African cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is an annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in racemes, and its leaves are usually lobed or toothed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

The plant is notable for its peppery flavor, making it a popular ingredient in some traditional cuisines.
