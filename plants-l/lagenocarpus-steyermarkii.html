Lagenocarpus steyermarkii

Lagenocarpus steyermarkii, commonly known as the Mexican starfruit or yellow starfruit, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico and parts of Central America. The plant is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, clustered in cyathia, a typical feature of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to reveal bright yellow, fleshy seeds, which are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor.
