Liriope angustissima

Liriope angustissima, commonly known as narrowleaf lilyturf, is a perennial grass-like plant native to eastern Asia.

It features grass-like leaves that are narrow and dark green, often forming dense clumps. The plant produces small, grass-like flowers in late summer, typically in shades of white or pale blue. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Liriope angustissima is also valued for its ability to suppress weeds and improve soil stability.
