Linum scabrinerve

Linum scabrinerve, commonly known as the rough nerve flax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flax plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and have a rough texture due to the presence of stiff hairs.

Linum scabrinerve is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
