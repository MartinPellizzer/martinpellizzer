Lindsaea obtusa

Lindsaea obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leaved lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Bromeliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its reproductive structures, known as spore-bearing capsules, are typically clustered at the ends of the leafy shoots.

Lindsaea obtusa is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
