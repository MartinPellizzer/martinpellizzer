Lysimachia vulgaris subsp. davurica

Lysimachia vulgaris subsp.

davurica, commonly known as golden thread, is a low-growing perennial herb native to eastern Asia. It features slender, creeping stems that produce small, bright yellow flowers clustered in whorls.

The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a ground-cover plant.
