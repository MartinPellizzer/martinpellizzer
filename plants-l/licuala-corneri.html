Licuala corneri

Licuala corneri, commonly known as the broadleaf palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic visual presence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its trunk is typically short and robust, with a smooth, greyish-brown surface.

Licuala corneri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
