Lepanthes morae

Lepanthes morae, commonly known as the Morae's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

Lepanthes morae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
