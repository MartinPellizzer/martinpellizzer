Lenbrassia australiana var. glabrescens

Lenbrassia australiana var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved lenbrassia, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy, with a dark green coloration. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are followed by small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is adapted to tolerate salt spray and coastal conditions.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often used in native gardens and coastal landscapes.
