Leucophrys

Leucophrys, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. Leucophrys thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
