Loeseneriella griseoramula

Loeseneriella griseoramula, commonly known as the gray ramular lichen, is a foliose lichen that belongs to the family Parmeliaceae.

It is characterized by its pale gray to olive-green thallus, which is typically covered with a fine, powdery layer of granules. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship with algae, specifically Trebouxia species, which contribute to its photosynthetic capabilities. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on bark or soil in temperate regions.

Due to its delicate structure, it is sensitive to air pollution and serves as an indicator of environmental health.
