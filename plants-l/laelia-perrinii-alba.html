Laelia perrinii alba

Laelia perrinii alba, commonly known as the white laelia, is a hybrid orchid species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking white flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as a compact, evergreen orchid with fleshy, dark green leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Laelia perrinii alba is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and ease of care.
