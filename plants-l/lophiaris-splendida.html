Lophiaris splendida

Lophiaris splendida, commonly known as the splendid lophiaris, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and vibrant, showy inflorescences. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Lophiaris splendida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
