Luzula luzulina

Luzula luzulina, commonly known as the slender woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

Luzula luzulina is known for its ornamental value and is often used in horticulture for its elegant appearance.
