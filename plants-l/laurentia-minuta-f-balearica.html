Laurentia minuta f. balearica

Laurentia minuta f. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic dwarf laurel, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a slight aromatic scent. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value lies in its dense foliage and seasonal floral display.
