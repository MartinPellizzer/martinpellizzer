Lobelia exilis

Lobelia exilis, commonly known as slender lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers. Lobelia exilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.

It plays an important role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
