Lavatera punctata var. maroccana

Lavatera punctata var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan hibiscus, is a shrub native to the arid regions of North Africa.

It features showy, large, yellow flowers with a distinctive red center, which bloom throughout the summer. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens in dry climates.
