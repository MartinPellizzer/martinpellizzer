Lepidium aegrum

Lepidium aegrum, commonly known as the rock cress, is a species of flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with small, alternate leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, rounded fruits called siliques, which contain several seeds. Lepidium aegrum is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions.

It is a hardy plant that can thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
