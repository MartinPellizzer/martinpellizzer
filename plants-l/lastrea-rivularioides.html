Lastrea rivularioides

Lastrea rivularioides, commonly known as the rock lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale yellow or greenish in color. It has a fibrous root system and linear, grass-like leaves.

Lastrea rivularioides is adapted to survive in harsh environments with minimal water availability.
