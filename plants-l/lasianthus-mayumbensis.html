Lasianthus mayumbensis

Lasianthus mayumbensis, commonly known as the Mayumbensis flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
