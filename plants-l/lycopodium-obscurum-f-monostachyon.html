Lycopodium obscurum f. monostachyon

Lycopodium obscurum f. monostachyon, commonly known as the slender clubmoss, is a variant of the clubmoss family (Lycopodiaceae).

It is a small, evergreen fern-like plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features slender, upright stems with whorled leaves that are often dark green and slightly curled. Its reproductive structures are small cones, which contain spores for propagation.

This form is characterized by its reduced strobilus, which is typically single and terminal on the stem.
