Lupinus termis

Lupinus termis, commonly known as the Termis lupine, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces tall spikes of blue to purple flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Lupinus termis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
