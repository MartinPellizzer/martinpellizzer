Limlia uraiana

Limlia uraiana, commonly known as the Uraiana water lily, is a species of aquatic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its roots anchor it in the mud at the bottom of freshwater bodies.

Limlia uraiana plays an important role in maintaining water quality and supporting local ecosystems.
