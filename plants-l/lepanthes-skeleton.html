Lepanthes skeleton

Lepanthes skeleton, commonly known as the skeleton orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant has a delicate, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale green or white flowers. Its unique structure and coloration make it a rare and visually striking species.
