Libanotis subsimplex

Libanotis subsimplex, commonly known as the simple leaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to full sun conditions.

Libanotis subsimplex is a perennial herb that is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
