Lansium kostermansii

Lansium kostermansii, commonly known as the Kostermans' langsat, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers.
