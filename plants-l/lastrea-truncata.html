Lastrea truncata

Lastrea truncata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and showy, tubular flowers. Lastrea truncata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
