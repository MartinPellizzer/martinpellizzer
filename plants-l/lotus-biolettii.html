Lotus biolettii

Lotus biolettii, commonly known as the desert lotus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its striking pink or red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a robust root system that helps it survive in dry conditions. Its leaves are typically broad and succulent, storing water to reduce transpiration.

Lotus biolettii plays an important role in stabilizing soil in desert ecosystems.
