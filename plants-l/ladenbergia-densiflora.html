Ladenbergia densiflora

Ladenbergia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered legume, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its dense foliage and robust growth make it suitable for erosion control in degraded soils.

The species is also valued for its potential in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
