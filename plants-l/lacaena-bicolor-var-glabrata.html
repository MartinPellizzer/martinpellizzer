Lacaena bicolor var. glabrata

Lacaena bicolor var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth lacaena, is a variant of the lacaena species native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinct bi-colored foliage, typically featuring a mix of green and white or silver hues on the leaves. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its leaves are usually smooth and glossy, contributing to its visual appeal in both garden and horticultural settings.
