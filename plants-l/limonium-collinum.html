Limonium collinum

Limonium collinum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showing a high tolerance for salt and wind. Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Limonium collinum is often used in garden landscaping due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
