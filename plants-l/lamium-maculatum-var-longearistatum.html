Lamium maculatum var. longearistatum

Lamium maculatum var. longearistatum, commonly known as the Variegated Deadnettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with striking variegation of white, cream, and green, and it produces small, two-lipped, purple or pink flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to spread through rhizomes, forming dense ground cover.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
