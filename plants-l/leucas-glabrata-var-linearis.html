Leucas glabrata var. linearis

Leucas glabrata var. linearis, commonly known as the slender leucas, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of India.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
