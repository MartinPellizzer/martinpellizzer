Lampranthus curviflorus

Lampranthus curviflorus, commonly known as the zigzag firecracker, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is an excellent plant for xeriscaping in arid climates.
