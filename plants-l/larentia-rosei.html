Larentia rosei

Larentia rosei, commonly known as the roseate larentia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Larentia rosei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
