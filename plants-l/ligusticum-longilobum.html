Ligusticum longilobum

Ligusticum longilobum, commonly known as Chinese lovage, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves with a strong, spicy scent. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its roots are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Ligusticum longilobum is valued both for its culinary uses and its medicinal properties.
