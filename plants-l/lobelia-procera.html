Lobelia procera

Lobelia procera, commonly known as tall lobelia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 feet, featuring slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Lobelia procera thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
