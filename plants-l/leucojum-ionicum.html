Leucojum ionicum

Leucojum ionicum, commonly known as the spring snowflake, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and prefers well-drained, slightly acidic soils. It is often used in gardens for its delicate appearance and ability to naturalize.

Leucojum ionicum is relatively low maintenance and attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
