Licuala longiflora

Licuala longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant produces long, slender inflorescences with small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its growth habit is typically upright, with a single trunk and a crown of leaves.

Licuala longiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
