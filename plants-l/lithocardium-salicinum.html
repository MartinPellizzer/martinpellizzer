Lithocardium salicinum

Lithocardium salicinum, commonly known as the silky lichen, is a unique lichen that thrives in rocky and arid environments.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety growth form, which provides a protective layer against harsh environmental conditions. The plant exhibits a symbiotic relationship between a green alga and a fungus, allowing it to survive in nutrient-poor substrates. Its coloration ranges from pale green to bluish-gray, depending on environmental factors.

Lithocardium salicinum plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing microhabitats for other organisms in its ecosystem.
