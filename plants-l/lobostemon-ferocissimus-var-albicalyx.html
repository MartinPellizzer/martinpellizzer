Lobostemon ferocissimus var. albicalyx

Lobostemon ferocissimus var. albicalyx, commonly known as the white-flowered lobostemon, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle pinkish tinge. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its beauty and adaptability, it is a popular choice for gardens and tropical landscapes.
