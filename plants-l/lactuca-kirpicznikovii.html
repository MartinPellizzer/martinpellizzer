Lactuca kirpicznikovii

Lactuca kirpicznikovii, commonly known as Kirpicznikov's lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a composite head. It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Lactuca kirpicznikovii is not commonly cultivated for food but is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
