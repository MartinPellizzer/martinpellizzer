Lactuca boissieri

Lactuca boissieri, commonly known as the Boissier's lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to regions of the Mediterranean, including parts of southern Europe and North Africa. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are generally narrow and grass-like, with a mild, slightly bitter taste.

It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, contributing to its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
