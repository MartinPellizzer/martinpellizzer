Lithocarpus echinulatus

Lithocarpus echinulatus, commonly known as the spiny stone oak, is a species of evergreen tree belonging to the Fagaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of India, China, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves and produces small, hard nuts enclosed in a cupule. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

The tree is valued for its durable wood and is also used in traditional medicine.
