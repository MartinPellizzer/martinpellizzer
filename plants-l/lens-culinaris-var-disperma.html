Lens culinaris var. disperma

Lens culinaris var. disperma, commonly known as the dispersed lentil, is a variant of the common lentil.

It is characterized by its smaller, more dispersed seeds compared to the typical lentil variety. The plant is an annual legume that thrives in temperate climates. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain the edible seeds.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and nutritional content.
