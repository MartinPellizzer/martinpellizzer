Lobelia chilawana

Lobelia chilawana, commonly known as the Chilawana lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the highland regions of Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple.
