Ludia myrtifolia

Ludia myrtifolia, commonly known as the red-flowered lantana, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of small, showy red or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Ludia myrtifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
