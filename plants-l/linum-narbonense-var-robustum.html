Linum narbonense var. robustum

Linum narbonense var. robustum, commonly known as the robust larkspur, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking floral display and historical use in herbal medicine.
