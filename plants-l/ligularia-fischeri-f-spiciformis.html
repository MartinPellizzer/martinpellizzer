Ligularia fischeri f. spiciformis

Ligularia fischeri f. spiciformis, commonly known as the spiciform ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a pronounced central vein. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences that are often yellow-green in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
