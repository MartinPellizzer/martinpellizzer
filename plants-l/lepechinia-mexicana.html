Lepechinia mexicana

Lepechinia mexicana, commonly known as Mexican lepechinia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
