Leptoglossis schwenckioides var. linifolia

Leptoglossis schwenckioides var. linifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is widely cultivated as a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
