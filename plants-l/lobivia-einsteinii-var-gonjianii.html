Lobivia einsteinii var. gonjianii

Lobivia einsteinii var. gonjianii, commonly known as the Einstein's Lobivia, is a rare and striking succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to survive in drought conditions. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the growing season. Its leaves are typically rounded and fleshy, contributing to its unique appearance.

This variety is prized by collectors for its ornamental value and adaptability to dry climates.
