Lonicera etrusca superba

Lonicera etrusca superba, commonly known as the Etruscan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Italy.

It features slender, arching stems that bear opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in clusters, typically yellow or orange, which bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer, attracting various wildlife.

Lonicera etrusca superba is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a range of soil conditions.
