Lonicera carpatica

Lonicera carpatica, commonly known as the Carpathian honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features opposite, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. Its berries are red or orange and are edible, though not as commonly consumed as other honeysuckle species.

Lonicera carpatica is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
