Lampranthus elegans

Lampranthus elegans, commonly known as the elegant trident.

This succulent plant is native to South Africa and belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its diverse and often showy flowers. It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. The flowers are typically bright red or orange, with three petals that resemble a trifoliate pattern.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
