Lampranthus herrei

Lampranthus herrei, commonly known as the Orange Sunset Kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual display. It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Lampranthus herrei prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure for optimal growth.
