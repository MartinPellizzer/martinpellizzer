Leuzea caulescens

Leuzea caulescens, commonly known as the slender leuzea, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Leuzea caulescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
