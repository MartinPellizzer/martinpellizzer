Liparis nugentiae

Liparis nugentiae, commonly known as the noble orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and requires well-drained, humus-rich soil. Its flowers are often arranged in a spike and have a subtle fragrance.

Liparis nugentiae is a rare and endangered species, making conservation efforts essential for its survival.
