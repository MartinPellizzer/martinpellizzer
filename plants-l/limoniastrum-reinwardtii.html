Limoniastrum reinwardtii

Limoniastrum reinwardtii, commonly known as the desert star or desert milkweed, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and clusters of yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is well adapted to dry environments, with a deep root system that helps it survive in poor soil conditions. Its flowers are attractive to pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it an important species in its ecosystem.

Limoniastrum reinwardtii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in xeriscaping.
