Lotus corniculatus f. minor

Lotus corniculatus f. minor, commonly known as the lesser bird's-foot trefoil, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow or orange flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. It has small, hairy stems and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
