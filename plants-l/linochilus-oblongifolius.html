Linochilus oblongifolius

Linochilus oblongifolius, commonly known as the broad-leafed linochilus, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its elongated, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a contrasting throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
