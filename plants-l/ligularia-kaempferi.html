Ligularia kaempferi

Ligularia kaempferi, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which appear in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland areas.

Its foliage is popular in ornamental horticulture for its striking appearance and adaptability to wet conditions.
