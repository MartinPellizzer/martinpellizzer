Litsea magnifica var. pahangensis

Litsea magnifica var. pahangensis, commonly known as the Pahang litsea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in greenish-white or yellowish-green hues.

The fruit is a small, woody drupe that develops from the flower and is an important food source for certain wildlife species.
