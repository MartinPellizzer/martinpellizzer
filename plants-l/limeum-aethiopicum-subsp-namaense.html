Limeum aethiopicum subsp. namaense

Limeum aethiopicum subsp.

namaense, commonly known as the Namibian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Namib Desert. It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant is adapted to arid environments, with a deep root system to access water. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in coastal dunes.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an important component of the desert flora.
