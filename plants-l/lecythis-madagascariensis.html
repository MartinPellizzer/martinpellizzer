Lecythis madagascariensis

Lecythis madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar lecythium, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has economic importance in local communities.
