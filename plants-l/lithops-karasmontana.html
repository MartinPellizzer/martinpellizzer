Lithops karasmontana

Lithops karasmontana, commonly known as the Karas Mountains lithops, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of Namibia and South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves that resemble stones, providing excellent camouflage in its natural habitat. The plant produces small, vibrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts and collectors.
