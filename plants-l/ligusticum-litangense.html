Ligusticum litangense

Ligusticum litangense, commonly known as the Litang lovage, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescence.

Native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan and Yunnan in China, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. It features slender, hollow stems and broad, pinnately compound leaves that emit a distinctive fragrance. The flowers are small and white to greenish in color, arranged in umbrella-like clusters.

This species is valued both for its medicinal properties and its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
