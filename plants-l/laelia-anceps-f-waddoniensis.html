Laelia anceps f. waddoniensis

Laelia anceps f. waddoniensis, commonly known as the Waddoniensis Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright orange to yellow flowers with intricate patterns, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and specialized orchid collections.
