Lobivia thionantha var. variiflora

Lobivia thionantha var. variiflora, commonly known as the variegated stone cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to South America.

It features a rounded, segmented stem covered in light green to white mottled markings. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
