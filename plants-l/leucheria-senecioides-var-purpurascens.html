Leucheria senecioides var. purpurascens

Leucheria senecioides var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purpleleaf ironweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, purple-tinged flower heads. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a rough texture and a distinct purple-tinged underside.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland areas.
