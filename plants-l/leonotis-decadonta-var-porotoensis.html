Leonotis decadonta var. porotoensis

Leonotis decadonta var. porotoensis, commonly known as the Porotona lantana, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in dense, terminal clusters.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
