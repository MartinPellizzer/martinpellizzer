Liatris pycnostachya var. pycnostachya

Liatris pycnostachya var. pycnostachya, commonly known as purple top blazing star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the central and eastern United States.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear dense, spiky inflorescences of purple to pink flowers, which bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for native gardens.

Its striking appearance and ecological value attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
