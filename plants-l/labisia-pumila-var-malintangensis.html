Labisia pumila var. malintangensis

Labisia pumila var. malintangensis, commonly known as the Malintang mountain tea, is a low-growing shrub native to the highlands of North Borneo.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in montane forests. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
