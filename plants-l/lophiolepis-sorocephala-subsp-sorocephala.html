Lophiolepis sorocephala subsp. sorocephala

Lophiolepis sorocephala subsp.

sorocephala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mountain grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for erosion control in marginal soils.
