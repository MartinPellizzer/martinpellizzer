Liparis stolzii

Liparis stolzii, commonly known as the Stolz's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its terrestrial growth habit and rhizomatous root system. The plant produces delicate, often greenish or yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in a spike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Liparis stolzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
