Litsea fawcettiana

Litsea fawcettiana, commonly known as the Fawcett's litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, simple leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It produces berries that are typically dark red when mature, containing small, black seeds.

Litsea fawcettiana is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
