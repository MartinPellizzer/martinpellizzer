Lithops villetii var. deboeri

Lithops villetii var. deboeri, commonly known as the "Living Stone," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that resemble stones, helping it blend into its arid environment. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months. It requires minimal water and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for dry climates.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
