Leucomeris celebica

Leucomeris celebica, commonly known as the Celebes moonflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a fragrant scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while its fruits are capsule-like and contain numerous small seeds.

Leucomeris celebica is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its potential medicinal properties.
