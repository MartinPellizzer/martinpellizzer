Lantana punctulata

Lantana punctulata, commonly known as the spotted lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lantana punctulata is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often used in ornamental gardening.
