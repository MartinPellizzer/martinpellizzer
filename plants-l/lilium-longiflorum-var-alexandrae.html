Lilium longiflorum var. alexandrae

Lilium longiflorum var. alexandrae, commonly known as the Alexandra lily, is a cultivar of the Asiatic lily group.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white with a pinkish tinge, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, sword-like leaves that are arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its elegant appearance. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
