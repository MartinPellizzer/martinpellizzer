Lindernia maxwellii

Lindernia maxwellii, commonly known as Maxwell's lindernia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Linderniaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental ground cover.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its value in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
