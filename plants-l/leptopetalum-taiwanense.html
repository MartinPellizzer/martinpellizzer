Leptopetalum taiwanense

Leptopetalum taiwanense, commonly known as the Taiwan leektree, is a shrub native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
