Lowellia

Lowellia, commonly known as the "snowflake plant," is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Lowellia is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
