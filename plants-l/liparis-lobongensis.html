Liparis lobongensis

Liparis lobongensis, commonly known as the Lobong orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale green to white in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by insects.

Liparis lobongensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
