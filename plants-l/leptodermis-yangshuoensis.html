Leptodermis yangshuoensis

It is native to China, specifically the Yangshuo region in Guangxi Province. The plant is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
