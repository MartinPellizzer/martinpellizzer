Lasianthus kurzii var. sylvicola

Lasianthus kurzii var. sylvicola, commonly known as the forest lasianthus, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered inflorescences produce showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and violet.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
