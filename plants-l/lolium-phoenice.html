Lolium phoenice

Lolium phoenice, commonly known as purple grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

The flowers are small and clustered, producing a distinctive purple tinge that gives the plant its common name.
