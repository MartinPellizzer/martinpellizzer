Lathyrus Linifolius Lusus Variegatus

Lathyrus linifolius lusus variegatus is a striking variant of the sweet pea known for its vibrant, multicolored foliage. This plant is prized for its ornamental value and unique leaf patterns that add visual interest to gardens. While it shares the climbing habit of its parent species, it exhibits a more compact growth form. Its variegated leaves come in combinations of green, white, and gold, making it a standout in any landscape. Though not widely cultivated, it remains a rare and intriguing choice for horticultural enthusiasts.
