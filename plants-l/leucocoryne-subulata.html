Leucocoryne subulata

Leucocoryne subulata, commonly known as the slender leucocoryne, is a small, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue, blooming in late winter or early spring. It is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Leucocoryne subulata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardeners.
