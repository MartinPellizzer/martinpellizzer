Leucas chinensis var. oliganthos

Leucas chinensis var. oliganthos, commonly known as the narrow-leaved leucas, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and it produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
