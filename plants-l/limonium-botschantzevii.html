Limonium botschantzevii

Limonium botschantzevii, commonly known as the Caucasian sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spiny bracts beneath its flower heads. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and maritime climates.

Limonium botschantzevii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
