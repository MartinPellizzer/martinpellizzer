Lychnisilene rosea

Lychnisilene rosea, commonly known as the pink sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, delicate pink to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a mat-forming habit with succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh environments. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters at the ends of its stems.

Lychnisilene rosea is well adapted to cold climates and is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils.
