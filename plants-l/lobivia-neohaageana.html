Lobivia neohaageana

Lobivia neohaageana, commonly known as the Snowy Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a cylindrical stem with a bluish-green hue, often adorned with white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
