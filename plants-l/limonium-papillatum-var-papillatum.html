Limonium papillatum var. papillatum

Limonium papillatum var. papillatum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has silvery-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, and its stems are often covered in small, rounded projections. It thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Limonium papillatum var. papillatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
