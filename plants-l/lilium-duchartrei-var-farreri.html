Lilium duchartrei var. farreri

Lilium duchartrei var. farreri, commonly known as the Farrer's lily, is a variety of the Chinese lily native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its striking, pale pink to white flowers with a distinctive red throat, which bloom in late summer. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern along its stem. It is a herbaceous perennial that prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its elegance and fragrance.
