Lactuca mulgedioides

Lactuca mulgedioides, commonly known as the slender lettuce, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, leafy stems and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are edible and have a mild, slightly bitter taste, similar to other lettuces.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in arid environments.
