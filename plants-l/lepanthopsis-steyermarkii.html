Lepanthopsis steyermarkii

Lepanthopsis steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically has broad, strap-like leaves and is often found in shaded, humid environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural range.
