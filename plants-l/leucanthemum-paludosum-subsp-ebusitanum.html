Leucanthemum paludosum subsp. ebusitanum

Leucanthemum paludosum subsp.

ebusitanum, commonly known as the Ebusi thimbleweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Japan. It typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and along stream banks.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white flowers with yellow centers. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in shaded, humid habitats.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique ecological adaptations.
