Linum castroviejoi

Linum castroviejoi, commonly known as the Castroviejoi flax, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Linaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, adapting to the harsh environmental conditions of its habitat. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, while its stems are slender and erect.

Linum castroviejoi is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
