Litsea luzonica

Litsea luzonica, commonly known as the Luzon litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, red berries that are edible.

Litsea luzonica is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
