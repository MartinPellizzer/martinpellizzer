Lepanthopsis stellaris

Lepanthopsis stellaris, commonly known as the star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Lepanthopsis stellaris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
