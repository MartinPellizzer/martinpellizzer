Laelia anceps var. leucosticta

Laelia anceps var. leucosticta, commonly known as the white-streaked orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the high altitudes of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with contrasting colorations on the petals and sepals. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stem.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a container plant due to its specific care requirements.
