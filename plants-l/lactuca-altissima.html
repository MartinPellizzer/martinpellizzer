Lactuca altissima

Lactuca altissima, commonly known as the giant lettuce or prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its large, lobed leaves that are often covered with fine hairs, giving them a slightly prickly texture. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Lactuca altissima is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
