Lotus angustissimus var. glaber

Lotus angustissimus var. glaber, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water lotus, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that float on the water surface. The plant produces striking pink to purple flowers that bloom above the water. Its roots are submerged, anchoring it in shallow water bodies.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
