Laurus gracilis

Laurus gracilis, commonly known as slender laurel, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are glossy and aromatic, often used in culinary applications. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by black, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hedging plant.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
