Lobaria

Lobaria, commonly known as the "liverworts," is a genus of non-vascular plants belonging to the family Porellaceae.

These plants are characterized by their small, lobed thallus, which is typically attached to rocks or trees through a basal holdfast. Their unique morphology and habitat preferences make them valuable indicators of environmental conditions.
