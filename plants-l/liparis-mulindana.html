Liparis mulindana

Liparis mulindana, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually grass-like, and the flowers are small, clustered in racemes.

Liparis mulindana is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the local biodiversity of Madagascar.
