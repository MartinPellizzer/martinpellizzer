Lewisia rediviva subsp. minor

Lewisia rediviva subsp.

minor, commonly known as the dwarf lewisia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in alpine or coastal environments. Its leaves are basal, rosette-forming, and covered in a fine, white powder.

It is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
