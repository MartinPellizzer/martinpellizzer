Leptotes unicolor

Leptotes unicolor, commonly known as the single-colored orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, single-colored flowers that typically range from yellow to orange. The plant has a compact growth habit with linear, grass-like leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Leptotes unicolor is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
