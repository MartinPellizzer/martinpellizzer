Luffa caledonica

Luffa caledonica, commonly known as the giant luffa or caldron luffa, is a tropical vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and long, cylindrical fruits that mature to a fibrous, spongy texture. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires a long growing season to develop its mature fruit. The plant's rapid growth and unique appearance make it a popular choice for both gardeners and agricultural settings.
