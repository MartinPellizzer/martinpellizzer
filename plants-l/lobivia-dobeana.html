Lobivia dobeana

Lobivia dobeana, commonly known as the Dobeana lobivia, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and spiny stems. The plant produces small, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. It is well adapted to dry conditions, with a shallow root system that allows it to absorb moisture efficiently.

Due to its striking appearance and resilience, it is often cultivated as an ornamental cactus in arid gardens.
