Lenidia alata

Lenidia alata, commonly known as the winged orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that often have a waxy, blue-green appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Lenidia alata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical or subtropical forests.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
