Lisianthius zeylanicus

Lisianthius zeylanicus, commonly known as the Ceylon lisianthus, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, cylindrical flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
