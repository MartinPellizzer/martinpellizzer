Leonohebe ramosissima

Leonohebe ramosissima, commonly known as the coastal leonohebe, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the coastline. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to withstand the harsh coastal environment.

Leonohebe ramosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging, saline conditions.
