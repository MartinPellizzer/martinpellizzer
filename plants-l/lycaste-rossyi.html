Lycaste rossyi

Lycaste rossyi, commonly known as the Ross's lycaste, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its native habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and lance-shaped, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

Lycaste rossyi is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
