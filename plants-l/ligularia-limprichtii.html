Ligularia limprichtii

Ligularia limprichtii, commonly known as the Chinese ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense, flat-topped inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its foliage is notable for its striking coloration and texture, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
