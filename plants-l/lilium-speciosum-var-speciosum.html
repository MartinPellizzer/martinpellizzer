Lilium speciosum var. speciosum

Lilium speciosum var. speciosum, commonly known as the showy lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, supporting large, showy flowers with six petals. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with a distinctive recurved shape and a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has lance-shaped, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
