Leea laevis

Leea laevis, commonly known as the smooth leea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It belongs to the family Leeaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically smooth and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered yellowish-white flowers that are often borne on short pedicels. Its fruits are capsules that split open to release small, winged seeds.

Leea laevis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
