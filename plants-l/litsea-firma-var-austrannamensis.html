Litsea firma var. austrannamensis

Litsea firma var. austrannamensis, commonly known as the Australian litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to eastern Australia and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a fragrant aroma.

The fruit is a small, dark berry that develops from the flowers and is an important food source for local wildlife.
