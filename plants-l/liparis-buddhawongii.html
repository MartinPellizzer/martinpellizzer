Liparis buddhawongii

Liparis buddhawongii, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Botanically, it exhibits a complex floral structure with showy sepals and a specialized reproductive system typical of orchids.
