Lastrea falconeri

Lastrea falconeri, commonly known as the Falconeri's lastrea, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in drought conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for collectors and gardeners in dry climates.
