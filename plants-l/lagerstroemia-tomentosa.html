Lagerstroemia tomentosa

Lagerstroemia tomentosa, commonly known as the silky flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fluffy, silver-white pubescent leaves and showy, pink to red bract-covered inflorescences that appear in late spring. The plant belongs to the Lythraceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

The species is also noted for its adaptability to various climates and its ability to attract pollinators.
