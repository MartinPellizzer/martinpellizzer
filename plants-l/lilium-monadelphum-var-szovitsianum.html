Lilium monadelphum var. szovitsianum

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink to red in color, with a distinctive white throat and a broad, recurved tepal arrangement. The plant produces a single, upright stem that bears several flowers, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountain meadows at elevations above 3,000 meters.
