Loxostigma dongxingensis

Loxostigma dongxingensis, commonly known as the Dongxingensis Begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its unique appearance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
