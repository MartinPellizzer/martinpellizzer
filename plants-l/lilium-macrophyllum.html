Lilium macrophyllum

Lilium macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant white lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its large, white, fragrant flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas. It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.

The bulbs of this plant are also used in traditional medicine.
