Limodorum graminifolium

Limodorum graminifolium, commonly known as the grass-leaved lily of the valley, is a herbaceous perennial belonging to the family Asparagaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
