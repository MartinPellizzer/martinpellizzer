Limeum kwebense

Limeum kwebense, commonly known as the Kweben lime tree, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn yellow or orange when mature. Limeum kwebense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.

It plays an important ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
