Loasa heptamera var. albiflora

Loasa heptamera var. albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower loasa, is a species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
