Leianthus axillaris

Leianthus axillaris, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Leianthus axillaris thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
