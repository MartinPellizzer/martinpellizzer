Lasianthus purpureocalyx

Lasianthus purpureocalyx, commonly known as the purple-flowered lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to violet flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its inflorescences are terminal and consist of clusters of tubular flowers.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
