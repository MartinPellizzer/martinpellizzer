Lophogyne isoetifolia

Lophogyne isoetifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf water fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a small, aquatic or semi-aquatic fern that typically grows in shallow water or moist soil. Its unique morphology and adaptability to wet conditions contribute to its ecological significance in riparian zones.
