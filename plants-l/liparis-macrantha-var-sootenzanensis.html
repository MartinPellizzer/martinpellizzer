Liparis macrantha var. sootenzanensis

Liparis macrantha var. sootenzanensis, commonly known as the Sootenzen Mountain orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded, humid environments. Its flowers are often pale green or yellowish with intricate patterns, and it is known for its ornamental value.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
