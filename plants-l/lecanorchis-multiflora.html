Lecanorchis multiflora

Lecanorchis multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.

Lecanorchis multiflora is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is appreciated for its delicate floral structure and ecological significance.
