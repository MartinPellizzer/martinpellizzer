Leptohyptis pinheiroi

Leptohyptis pinheiroi, commonly known as Brazilian hyptis, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It often grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.

Leptohyptis pinheiroi is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
