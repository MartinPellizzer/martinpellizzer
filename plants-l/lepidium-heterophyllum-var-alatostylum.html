Lepidium heterophyllum var. alatostylum

Lepidium heterophyllum var. alatostylum, commonly known as the slender wall pepper, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial species characterized by its small, white or yellowish flowers and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, rounded pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
