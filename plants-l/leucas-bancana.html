Leucas bancana

Leucas bancana, commonly known as the African buttonweed, is a flowering plant native to Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is a small herbaceous plant that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features opposite, narrow leaves and produces small, white or pinkish flowers in clusters. It is often found in disturbed areas and can be invasive in some regions.
