Lachnostachys dempsteri

Lachnostachys dempsteri, commonly known as the Demptson's grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats, showcasing a clump-forming growth pattern. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
