Lessingianthus mollissimus var. mollissimus

Lessingianthus mollissimus var. mollissimus, commonly known as the velvetleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers with a distinctive red center. The leaves are velvety to the touch, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
