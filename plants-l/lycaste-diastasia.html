Lycaste diastasia

Lycaste diastasia, commonly known as the Diastasia lycaste, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in vibrant shades of pink and white. The plant features a sympodial growth pattern, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, providing a lush green backdrop to its dramatic blooms.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
