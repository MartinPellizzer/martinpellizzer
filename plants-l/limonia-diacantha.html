Limonia diacantha

Limonia diacantha, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant's fruit is a small, yellowish drupe that turns red when ripe, containing a single seed. Limonia diacantha thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
