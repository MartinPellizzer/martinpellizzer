Lockhartia robusta

Lockhartia robusta, commonly known as the Robusta Orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Lockhartia robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
