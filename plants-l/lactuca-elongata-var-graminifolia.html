Lactuca elongata var. graminifolia

Lactuca elongata var. graminifolia, commonly known as slender lettuce, is a variant of the lettuce genus characterized by its grass-like foliage.

It features narrow, elongated leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in length, with a bluish-green coloration. This plant is native to temperate regions of Europe and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as meadows and woodland edges. Its flowering stems are slender and can reach up to 30 cm in height, bearing small, greenish-white flowers.

The species is notable for its medicinal properties, including potential anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.
