Lobivia hertrichiana f. echinata

Lobivia hertrichiana f. echinata, commonly known as the Echinata Lobivia, is a compact, spineless cactus native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive greenish-gray color and a rough, waxy texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
