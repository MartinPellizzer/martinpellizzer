Lipotriche marlothiana

Lipotriche marlothiana, commonly known as the mountain sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are usually narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

Lipotriche marlothiana is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
