Lotus Preslii

Lotus preslii is a rare and striking member of the legume family known for its unique pink and white flower clusters. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this plant thrives in cool, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its delicate foliage and showy blooms make it a sought-after addition to botanical gardens and private collections. Despite its beauty, Lotus preslii is critically endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this rare species for future generations.
