Lysimachia vulneraria

Lysimachia vulneraria, commonly known as the woundwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a slightly hairy texture.
