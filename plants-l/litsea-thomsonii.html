Litsea thomsonii

Litsea thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's lime, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by round, orange berries that are edible but not commonly cultivated for food.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
