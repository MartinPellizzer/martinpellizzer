Lomaria Hancockii

Lomaria hancockii is a rare and intriguing plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Mexico. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and ecological importance. This species is characterized by its striking blue and purple flowers, which bloom in late spring. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
