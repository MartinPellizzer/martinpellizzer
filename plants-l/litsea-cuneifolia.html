Litsea cuneifolia

Litsea cuneifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as China, India, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often glossy and aromatic. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, round berries.

Litsea cuneifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
