Lotus varius

Lotus varius, commonly known as the variable lotus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and ponds, where it thrives in moist soil. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often float on the water's surface, while its roots are submerged.

The lotus is also known for its symbolic significance in various cultures, representing purity and enlightenment.
