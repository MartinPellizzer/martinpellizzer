Limonium insigne

Limonium insigne, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to salty, well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating.

Limonium insigne is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
