Lippia purpurea

Lippia purpurea, commonly known as purple verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and clusters of small, vibrant purple flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Lippia purpurea is also valued for its aromatic properties and use in herbal teas.
