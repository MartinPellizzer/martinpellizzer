Lysimachia monelli

Lysimachia monelli, commonly known as the yellow loosestrife, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Lysimachia monelli is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
