Liparis hainanensis

Liparis hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan orchid, is a species of orchid native to China's Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on rocky substrates or in crevices. Its leaves are usually long and strap-like, and it produces a single flower spike with several blooms.

Liparis hainanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
