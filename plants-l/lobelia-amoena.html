Lobelia amoena

Lobelia amoena, commonly known as the common lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, tubular blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with slightly hairy surfaces, and it thrives in moist, shady environments. It is often found in woodlands and along stream banks, where it contributes to the ecological diversity of these habitats.

Lobelia amoena is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
