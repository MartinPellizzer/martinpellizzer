Linum perenne roseum

Linum perenne roseum, commonly known as the pink flax or rose-flowered flax, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It produces showy, pink to lavender-colored flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and delicate appearance.
