Leucadendron singulare

Leucadendron singulare, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, silver-white, fuzzy foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flower heads, which are highly ornamental and attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it is a popular choice in horticulture and garden design.
