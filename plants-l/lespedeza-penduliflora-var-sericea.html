Lespedeza penduliflora var. sericea

Lespedeza penduliflora var. sericea, commonly known as silverleaf lespedeza, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and small, pale green to silver-white leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces delicate, pendulous clusters of small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Its foliage provides seasonal interest, and it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
