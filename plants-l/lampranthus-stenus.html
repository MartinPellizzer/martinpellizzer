Lampranthus stenus

Lampranthus stenus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, typically during the summer months. It is a member of the Aizoaceae family, known for its adaptive features to harsh environments.

Lampranthus stenus is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
