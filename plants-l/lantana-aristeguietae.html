Lantana aristeguietae

Lantana aristeguietae, commonly known as the Canary Islands lantana, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to coastal and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in dry, sunny environments and is known for its resilience to drought.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations and limited distribution.
