Lobivia haagei var. elegantula

Lobivia haagei var. elegantula, commonly known as the Elegant Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South America.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that are typically pale green with a subtle bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.
