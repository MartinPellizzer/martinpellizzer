Lippia scaberrima

Lippia scaberrima, commonly known as rough lippia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, tubular flowers. The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are covered in fine, stiff hairs, giving them a rough texture.

Lippia scaberrima is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
