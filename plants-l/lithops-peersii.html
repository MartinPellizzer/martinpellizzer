Lithops peersii

Lithops peersii, commonly known as the "peers' ice plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that resemble stones, which help it blend into its arid environment. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It requires minimal water and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
