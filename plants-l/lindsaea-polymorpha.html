Lindsaea polymorpha

Lindsaea polymorpha, commonly known as the variable lindsaea, is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas, particularly in Central and South America. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with a creeping stem that produces both roots and aerial shoots. Its fronds are typically pinnately compound, with lance-shaped pinnae that are often gland-dotted.

Lindsaea polymorpha is often found growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments.
