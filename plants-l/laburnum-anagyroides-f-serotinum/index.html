Laburnum Anagyroides F. Serotinum

Laburnum anagyroides f. serotinum is a striking variant of the golden chain tree known for its delayed flowering habit. This plant features long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear later in the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. The foliage consists of finely cut, dark green leaves that add visual interest throughout the year. Its unique blooming pattern sets it apart from the typical form of the species, offering a delayed but dazzling display of color.
