Lantana camara f. splendens

Lantana camara f. splendens, commonly known as the splendens lantana, is a flowering shrub native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are oval to lance-shaped. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

The botanical features of this plant make it both ornamental and invasive in many regions.
