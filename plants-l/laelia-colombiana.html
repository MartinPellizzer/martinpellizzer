Laelia colombiana

Laelia colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian laelia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of Colombia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, showcasing its vibrant colors against the high-altitude environment. Laelia colombiana has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its elegance and resilience.
