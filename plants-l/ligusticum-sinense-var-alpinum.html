Ligusticum sinense var. alpinum

Ligusticum sinense var. alpinum, commonly known as alpine Chinese lovage, is a perennial herb native to high-altitude regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine soils, thriving in cool, moist environments.
