Lepanthes granulosa

Lepanthes granulosa, commonly known as the granular orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, arranged in a rosette pattern.

Lepanthes granulosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
