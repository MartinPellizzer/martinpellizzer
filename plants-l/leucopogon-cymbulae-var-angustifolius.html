Leucopogon cymbulae var. angustifolius

Leucopogon cymbulae var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, evergreen leaves that are narrow and leathery, adapted to the dry, sandy soils of its natural habitat. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
