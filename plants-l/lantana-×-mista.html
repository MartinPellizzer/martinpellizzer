Lantana × mista

Lantana × mista, commonly known as the hybrid lantana, is a flowering plant belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant is evergreen, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Lantana × mista is also known for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators to the garden.
