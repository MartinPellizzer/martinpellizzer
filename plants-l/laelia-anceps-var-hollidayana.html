Laelia anceps var. hollidayana

Laelia anceps var. hollidayana, commonly known as the Holliday's Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the highlands of Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces vibrant, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. It prefers cool, bright environments with moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
