Leucopogon microphyllus var. microphyllus

Leucopogon microphyllus var. microphyllus, commonly known as the small-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to the dry, sandy soils of its natural habitat.

This species is often found in heathlands and coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
