Ligularia Songarica

Ligularia songarica is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It is known for its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its vibrant yellow flowers appear in late summer, adding a bold splash of color to the landscape. Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it has gained popularity among gardeners and botanists alike.
