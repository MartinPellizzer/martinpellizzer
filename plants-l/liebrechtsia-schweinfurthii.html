Liebrechtsia schweinfurthii

Liebrechtsia schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's liebrechtia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage that forms a compact, bushy shape. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically oval to lance-shaped and have a glossy texture.

This species is well-suited for coastal gardens due to its tolerance of salt spray and well-drained soils.
