Lycaste xanthocheila

Lycaste xanthocheila, commonly known as the yellow lycaste, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically borne on pendulous inflorescences. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
