Lonicera xylosteum var. brachypoda

Lonicera xylosteum var. brachypoda, commonly known as the dwarf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features slender, arching stems that produce opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant blooms in late spring with fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
