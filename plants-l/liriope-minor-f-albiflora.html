Liriope minor f. albiflora

Liriope minor f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered lilyturf, is a perennial grass-like plant native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that form dense clumps, and its flowers are small, white or pale lavender blossoms that appear in late summer. The plant produces grassy, strap-like foliage that remains evergreen throughout the year. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its botanical characteristics make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically beneficial in garden settings.
