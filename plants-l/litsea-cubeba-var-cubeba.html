Litsea cubeba var. cubeba

Litsea cubeba var. cubeba, commonly known as wild lime or mountain pepper, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into aromatic, lemon-scented fruits with a rough, spiny exterior. The fruit contains a single seed and is often used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

Its essential oils, extracted from the fruit and leaves, are valued for their fragrance and medicinal properties.
