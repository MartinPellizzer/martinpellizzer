Licuala penduliflora var. aruensis

Licuala penduliflora var. aruensis, commonly known as the Aru palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Indonesia, particularly the Aru Islands. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in diameter. It produces pendulous inflorescences with small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense spikes.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional practices in its native habitat.
