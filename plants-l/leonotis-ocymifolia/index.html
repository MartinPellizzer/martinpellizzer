Leonotis Ocymifolia

Leonotis ocymifolia, also known as the flame violet, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is prized for its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. This plant is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and birds. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among horticulturists and nature enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
