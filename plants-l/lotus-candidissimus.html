Lotus candidissimus

Lotus candidissimus, commonly known as the white lotus, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, white, floating flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically submerged or floating on the water's surface. Its roots are anchored in the mud at the bottom of shallow water bodies.

Lotus candidissimus is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in various Asian traditions.
