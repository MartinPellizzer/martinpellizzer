Lactuca soongorica

Lactuca soongorica, commonly known as the Soongor lettuce, is a species of lettuce native to Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically pale green with a slightly woolly texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster at the top of a slender stem. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Lactuca soongorica is valued for its edible leaves, which have a mild, slightly bitter flavor.
