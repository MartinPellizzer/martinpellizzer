Limnanthes gracilis

Limnanthes gracilis, commonly known as the slender marsh plant, is a herbaceous annual native to California.

It features showy, yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, dry fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Limnanthes gracilis plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
