Lespedeza thunbergii subsp. thunbergii

Lespedeza thunbergii subsp.

thunbergii, commonly known as Thunberg's lespea, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
