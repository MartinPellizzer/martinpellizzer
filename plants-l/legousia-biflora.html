Legousia biflora

Legousia biflora, commonly known as the twin-flowered legousia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often with a slight pubescence.

Legousia biflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
