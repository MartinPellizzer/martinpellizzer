Lodhra leiostachya

Lodhra leiostachya, commonly known as the white-flowered lodhra, is a deciduous shrub native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Styracaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Lodhra leiostachya is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in Ayurveda.
