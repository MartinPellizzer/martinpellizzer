Leucas carsonii

Leucas carsonii, commonly known as Carson's leucas, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, square stems and opposite leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.
