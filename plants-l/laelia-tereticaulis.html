Laelia tereticaulis

Laelia tereticaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow during the spring season. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and moderate humidity.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species within the Laelia genus.
