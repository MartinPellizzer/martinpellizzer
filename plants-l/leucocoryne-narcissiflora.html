Leucocoryne narcissiflora

Leucocoryne narcissiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered leucocoryne, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its strap-shaped, evergreen leaves. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
