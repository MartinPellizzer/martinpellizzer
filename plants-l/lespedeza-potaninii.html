Lespedeza potaninii

Lespedeza potaninii, commonly known as the Chinese stragglerbush, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, spreading growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, hairy pods. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Lespedeza potaninii is noted for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it ecologically significant in soil enrichment.
