Leucopogon polystachyus

Leucopogon polystachyus, commonly known as the broad-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers.
