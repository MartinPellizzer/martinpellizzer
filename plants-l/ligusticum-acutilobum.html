Ligusticum acutilobum

Ligusticum acutilobum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed angelica, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbel-shaped flowers. The roots are thick and fleshy, often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
