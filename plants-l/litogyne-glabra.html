Litogyne glabra

Litogyne glabra, commonly known as the smooth litogyne, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are smooth and often have a waxy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in greenish-white or pale yellow hues, and are borne on short pedicels.

Litogyne glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and subtle floral display.
