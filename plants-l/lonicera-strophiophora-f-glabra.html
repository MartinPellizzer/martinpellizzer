Lonicera strophiophora f. glabra

Lonicera strophiophora f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth honeysuckle, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a smooth texture, which distinguishes it from other honeysuckle varieties. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early spring blossoms and attractive foliage.
