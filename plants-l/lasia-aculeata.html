Lasia aculeata

Lasia aculeata, commonly known as the spiny lasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, spiny leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
