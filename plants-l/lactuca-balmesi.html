Lactuca balmesi

Lactuca balmesi, commonly known as Balmes lettuce, is a species of lettuce native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its loose, pale green leaves that are slightly curled and have a mild, slightly sweet flavor. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has a rosette-like structure. It thrives in cool climates and is often cultivated for its edible leaves.

The botanical features of Lactuca balmesi make it a unique and flavorful addition to salads and other culinary dishes.
