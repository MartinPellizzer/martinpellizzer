Lepanthes cuatrecasasii

Lepanthes cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in cloud forests. Its flowers are usually small and have intricate patterns, with vibrant colors that attract specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
