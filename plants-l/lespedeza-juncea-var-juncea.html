Lespedeza juncea var. juncea

Lespedeza juncea var. juncea, commonly known as slender lespedeza, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks. The plant features slender, ascending stems with opposite, pinnately compound leaves that are often finely divided. Its small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers are arranged in spikes and bloom in late summer.

The species is noted for its ability to thrive in wet conditions and is sometimes used in wetland restoration projects.
