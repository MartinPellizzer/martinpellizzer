Lotononis calycina

Lotononis calycina, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, often blooming from late autumn to early spring.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
