Lycaste skinneri var. labelloalba

Lycaste skinneri var. labelloalba, commonly known as the White-veined Lycaste, is a rare and striking orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its large, showy flowers with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

This variant is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique coloration and ornamental value.
