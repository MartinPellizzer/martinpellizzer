Lycopodium × sabinifolium var. superfertile

Lycopodium × sabinifolium var. superfertile, commonly known as the fertile larch fern, is a hybrid fern species that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a member of the Lycopodiaceae family and is notable for its high fertility, which is reflected in its name "superfertile.". The plant features upright, branched stems covered in small, scale-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are well-developed, contributing to its success in natural habitats.
