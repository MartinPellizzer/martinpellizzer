Lasiosiphon djuricus

Lasiosiphon djuricus, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant is a climbing vine with opposite, heart-shaped leaves and produces showy blooms that open in the morning. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Lasiosiphon djuricus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
