Luzula alpestris

Luzula alpestris, commonly known as alpine woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to mountainous regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. Its foliage is generally dark green and grass-like, with a dense arrangement that helps it withstand harsh environmental conditions.
