Linaria broteri

Linaria broteri, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Linaria broteri is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun.
