Lathyrus azureus

Lathyrus azureus, commonly known as the Azure Pea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Lathyrus azureus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
