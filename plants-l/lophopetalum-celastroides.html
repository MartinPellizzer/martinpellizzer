Lophopetalum celastroides

Lophopetalum celastroides, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple berries that are edible for birds but generally not for humans. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The shrub is valued for its attractive foliage and persistent berries, which add visual interest throughout the year.
