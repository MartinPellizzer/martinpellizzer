Lothiania bilabiata

Lothiania bilabiata, commonly known as the two-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive two-lipped flowers, which are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. The plant has slender, erect stems that bear several leaves along their length. Its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences and are pollinated by specific insect species.

Lothiania bilabiata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
