Ligustrum quihoui var. glabrum

Ligustrum quihoui var. glabrum, commonly known as the Chinese privet, is a shrub native to China.

It features small, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue berries. It is often used in landscaping due to its hardiness and adaptability.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common ornamental plant in temperate regions.
