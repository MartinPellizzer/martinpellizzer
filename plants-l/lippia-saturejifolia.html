Lippia saturejifolia

Lippia saturejifolia, commonly known as Mexican blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features small, blue or purple flowers arranged in spike-like clusters. Its leaves are often aromatic and can be used in traditional medicine.

Lippia saturejifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
