Limonium retusum

Limonium retusum, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of the Mediterranean and the western Atlantic.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in sandy or salty soils and is often found in dune environments.
