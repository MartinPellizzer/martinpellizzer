Lonicera finlaysoniana

Lonicera finlaysoniana, commonly known as the Finlayson honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow, typically blooming in late spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Lonicera finlaysoniana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
