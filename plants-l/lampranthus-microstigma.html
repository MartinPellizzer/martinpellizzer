Lampranthus microstigma

Lampranthus microstigma, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
