Lathyrus odoratus-zeylanicus

Lathyrus odoratus-zeylanicus, commonly known as the sweet pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and clusters of fragrant, colorful flowers that range from white to purple. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
