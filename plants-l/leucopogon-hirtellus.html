Leucopogon hirtellus

Leucopogon hirtellus, commonly known as the hairy leucopogon, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, commonly known as the heath family, and is characterized by its erect, shrub-like growth habit. Leucopogon hirtellus is adapted to acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.
