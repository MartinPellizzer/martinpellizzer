Licuala longipes

Licuala longipes, commonly known as the longipes palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 2 meters in diameter. The plant typically grows as a solitary stem with a short trunk, and its leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated for decorative purposes.
