Lophiaris Flavovirens

Yellow-green rush, scientifically known as Lophiaris flavovirens, is a striking member of the Cyperaceae family that thrives in moist, shaded environments. This plant is characterized by its vibrant yellow-green foliage, which sets it apart from other rush species. Native to regions of East Asia, it has gained popularity among horticulturists for its ornamental value and adaptability. Its dense, grass-like appearance makes it an excellent choice for erosion control and ground cover. Despite its beauty, it requires careful management to prevent overgrowth in natural habitats.
