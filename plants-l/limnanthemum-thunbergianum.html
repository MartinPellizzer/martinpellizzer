Limnanthemum thunbergianum

Limnanthemum thunbergianum, commonly known as the Japanese star anise, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its fruit is a star-shaped capsule that contains aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
