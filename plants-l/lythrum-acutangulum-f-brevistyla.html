Lythrum acutangulum f. brevistyla

Lythrum acutangulum f. brevistyla, commonly known as the narrow-styled purpleweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lythraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to deep violet. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the inflorescence consists of numerous tiny, tubular flowers.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
