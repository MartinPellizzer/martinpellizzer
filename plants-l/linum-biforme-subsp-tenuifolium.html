Linum biforme subsp. tenuifolium

Linum biforme subsp.

tenuifolium, commonly known as slender-leaf flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and delicate blue or purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny environments. It belongs to the Linaceae family, which includes other flax species. Linum biforme subsp.

tenuifolium is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand coastal conditions such as salt spray and strong winds.
