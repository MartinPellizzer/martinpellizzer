Linagrostis polystachia

Linagrostis polystachia, commonly known as the clustered silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and silvery-green foliage that gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, pale flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Linagrostis polystachia is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
