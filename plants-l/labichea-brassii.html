Labichea brassii

Labichea brassii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Labichea brassii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
