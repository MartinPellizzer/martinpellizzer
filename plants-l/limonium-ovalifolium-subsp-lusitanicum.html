Limonium ovalifolium subsp. lusitanicum

Limonium ovalifolium subsp.

lusitanicum, commonly known as the Iberian sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant produces small, spherical seed capsules that are typical of the Limonium genus. It thrives in coastal and saline environments, often found in dunes and rocky shores.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.
