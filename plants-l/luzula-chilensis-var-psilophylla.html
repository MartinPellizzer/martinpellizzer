Luzula chilensis var. psilophylla

Luzula chilensis var. psilophylla, commonly known as the Chilean slender woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the plant family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family. This variant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. It typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas.
