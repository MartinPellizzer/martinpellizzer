Lasianthus obliquinervis var. taitoensis

Lasianthus obliquinervis var. taitoensis, commonly known as the Taitoensis lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly serrated margin, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
