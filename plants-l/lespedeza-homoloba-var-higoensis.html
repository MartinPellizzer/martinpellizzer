Lespedeza homoloba var. higoensis

Lespedeza homoloba var. higoensis, commonly known as the Higoensis Lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy surface.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
