Ligularia longihastata

Ligularia longihastata, commonly known as the long-stemmed ligularia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with a distinctive ligulate (strap-like) ray floret. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
