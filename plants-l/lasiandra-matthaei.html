Lasiandra matthaei

Lasiandra matthaei, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers with a central disk. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Lasiandra matthaei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
