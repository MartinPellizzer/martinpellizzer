Lankesteriana involuta

Lankesteriana involuta, commonly known as the twisted lankesteriana, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Colombia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, spirally twisted flower spikes. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a subtle purple tinge. Its leaves are elliptical and covered in a fine, velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Lankesteriana involuta is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
