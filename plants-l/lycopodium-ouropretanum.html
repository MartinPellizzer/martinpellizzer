Lycopodium ouropretanum

Lycopodium ouropretanum, commonly known as the Ouropretan clubmoss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant features a creeping stem with whorled leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape. It produces sporophylls that bear spores, which are released into the air for dispersal.

Lycopodium ouropretanum plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
