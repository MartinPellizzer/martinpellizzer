Leandra miconiastrum

Leandra miconiastrum, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Due to its rapid growth and invasive potential, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
