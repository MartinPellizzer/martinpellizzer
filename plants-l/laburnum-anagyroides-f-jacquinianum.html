Laburnum anagyroides f. jacquinianum

Laburnum anagyroides f. jacquinianum, commonly known as the golden chain tree.

This ornamental plant is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is a cultivar of the laburnum tree, known for its striking visual appeal in gardens. The foliage consists of pinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental planting.
