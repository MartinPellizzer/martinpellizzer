Lunaria pachyrrhiza f. dalmatica

Lunaria pachyrrhiza f. dalmatica, commonly known as the Dalmatian moonflower, is a rare and unique variant of the moonflower species.

It is characterized by its striking, orb-shaped seed pods that resemble small, translucent moons. The plant is native to the Balkan region, specifically parts of Croatia and Bosnia and Herzegovina. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
