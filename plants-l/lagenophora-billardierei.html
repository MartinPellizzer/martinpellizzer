Lagenophora billardierei

Lagenophora billardierei, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mistflower, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is found in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Lagenophora billardierei is also an important plant for local pollinators and contributes to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
