Luzula seubertii

Luzula seubertii, commonly known as the Seubert's woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. Luzula seubertii is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil.
