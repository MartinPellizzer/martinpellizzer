Linaria praealta

Linaria praealta, commonly known as the tall toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, making it a tall and showy species. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Linaria praealta is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.
