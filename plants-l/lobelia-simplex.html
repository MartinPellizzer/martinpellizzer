Lobelia simplex

Lobelia simplex, commonly known as narrowleaf lobelia, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its showy blue to purple flowers bloom in racemes during the summer months.

Lobelia simplex is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
