Ludwigia pulvinaris

Ludwigia pulvinaris, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that range in color from deep green to vibrant red, depending on light exposure and nutrient levels. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, and it thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high lighting. It is often cultivated in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.

Ludwigia pulvinaris is a hardy species that prefers warm water temperatures and is relatively easy to care for in both natural and artificial settings.
