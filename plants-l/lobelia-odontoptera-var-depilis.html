Lobelia odontoptera var. depilis

Lobelia odontoptera var. depilis, commonly known as the smooth lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple.
