Lophostachys cyanea

Lophostachys cyanea, commonly known as the blue leaved lophostachys, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green foliage and produces small, delicate white flowers. The plant is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
