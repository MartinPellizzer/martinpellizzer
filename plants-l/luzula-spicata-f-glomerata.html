Luzula spicata f. glomerata

Luzula spicata f. glomerata, commonly known as the clustered wood rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and spike-like inflorescence.
