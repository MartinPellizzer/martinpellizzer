Lysimachia stellata

Lysimachia stellata, commonly known as starwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Lysimachia stellata thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
