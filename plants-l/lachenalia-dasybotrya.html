Lachenalia dasybotrya

Lachenalia dasybotrya, commonly known as the Woolly Lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of fleshy, woolly leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear clusters of small, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts.
