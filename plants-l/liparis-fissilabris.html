Liparis fissilabris

Liparis fissilabris, commonly known as the fissured orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, upright stem with oval, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive split lip that gives it its scientific name.

Liparis fissilabris is known for its adaptability and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
