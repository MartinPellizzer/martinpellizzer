Lindera menghaiensis

Lindera menghaiensis, commonly known as Menghai Lindera, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan province, where it grows in humid, subtropical forests. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.

Lindera menghaiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its fragrance and medicinal properties.
