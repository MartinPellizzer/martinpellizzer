Lycianthes herbert-smithii

Lycianthes herbert-smithii, commonly known as Herbert-Smith's bitterleaf, is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades.

It is a shrub native to Mexico and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries containing seeds.

Its botanical features make it distinct within the genus Lycianthes, though it is less commonly cultivated than other members of the family.
