Ligularia liatroides var. shifangensis

Ligularia liatroides var. shifangensis, commonly known as the Shifang ligularia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a rough texture. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in late summer, which are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
