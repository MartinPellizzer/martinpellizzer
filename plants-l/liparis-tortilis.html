Liparis tortilis

Liparis tortilis, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a unique orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in the spring. Liparis tortilis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its botanical features make it a fascinating subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
