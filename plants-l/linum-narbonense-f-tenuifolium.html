Linum narbonense f. tenuifolium

Linum narbonense f. tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flax.

It is a subspecies of the flax plant native to the Mediterranean region. This plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun. Its leaves are slender and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

It produces blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
