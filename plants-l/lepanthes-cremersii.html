Lepanthes cremersii

Lepanthes cremersii, commonly known as the Creamy Pansy Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically feature a white or cream-colored lip with intricate patterns. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it thrive in low-light conditions.

Lepanthes cremersii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate, fragrant blooms.
