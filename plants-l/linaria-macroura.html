Linaria macroura

Linaria macroura, commonly known as the long-beak sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branching stems and small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. Linaria macroura thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments.

Its delicate appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
