Ledermanniella onanae

Ledermanniella onanae, commonly known as the Hawaiian starling orchid, is a rare and endemic species found exclusively in Hawaii.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories, where it relies on specific symbiotic relationships with fungi for nutrient uptake.
