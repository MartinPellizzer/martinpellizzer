Lindmania stenophylla

Lindmania stenophylla, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often hidden within its leaf rosette.
