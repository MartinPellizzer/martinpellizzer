Lepanthes ortegae

Lepanthes ortegae, commonly known as the Ortegae's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically display vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in low-light conditions.

Lepanthes ortegae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
