Lobivia densispina f. wessneriana

Lobivia densispina f. wessneriana, commonly known as the Wessner's dense-spined lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
