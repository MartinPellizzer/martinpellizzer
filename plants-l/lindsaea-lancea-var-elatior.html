Lindsaea lancea var. elatior

Lindsaea lancea var. elatior, commonly known as the tall lindsaea, is a fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its long, slender fronds that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces small, clustered spores on the underside of its leaves, a typical feature of ferns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouses.
