Lipozygis tenella var. sericea

Lipozygis tenella var. sericea, commonly known as the silky lipozygis, is a variant of the lipozygis genus native to specific regions in Africa.

It is characterized by its finely hairy, or sericeous, leaves that give it a silky appearance. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh climatic conditions. Its small, clustered flowers are usually yellow or green, and it produces small, round fruits.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
