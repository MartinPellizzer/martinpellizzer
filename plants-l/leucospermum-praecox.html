Leucospermum praecox

Leucospermum praecox, commonly known as the early torch flower, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a shrub-like appearance with woody stems and narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant color and long-lasting nature.
