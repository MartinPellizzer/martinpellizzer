Linochilus schultzii

Linochilus schultzii, commonly known as the Schultze's linochilus, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, tubular flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, where it thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, fragrant blooms during the spring and early summer.
