Linzia gerberiformis subsp. gerberiformis

Linzia gerberiformis subsp.

gerberiformis, commonly known as the Gerber's linzie, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces numerous yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

This subspecies is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
