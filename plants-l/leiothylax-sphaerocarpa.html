Leiothylax sphaerocarpa

Leiothylax sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and showy, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant produces small, round, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Leiothylax sphaerocarpa is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
