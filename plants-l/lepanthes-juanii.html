Lepanthes juanii

Lepanthes juanii, commonly known as the Juanii orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features delicate, greenish-white flowers with intricate patterns, often adorned with purple or red markings. Lepanthes juanii has a short, fleshy rhizome and produces a single spike of flowers during the wet season.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
