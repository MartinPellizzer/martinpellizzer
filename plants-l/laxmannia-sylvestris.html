Laxmannia sylvestris

Laxmannia sylvestris, commonly known as the forest laxmannia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Laxmannia sylvestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
