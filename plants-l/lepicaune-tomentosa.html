Lepicaune tomentosa

Lepicaune tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly lepicaune, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for ornamental purposes.
