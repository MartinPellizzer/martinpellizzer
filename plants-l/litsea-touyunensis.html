Litsea touyunensis

Litsea touyunensis, commonly known as the Yunnan litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to southwestern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by yellowish drupes.

Litsea touyunensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
