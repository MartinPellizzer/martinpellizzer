Lippia macrostachya

Lippia macrostachya, commonly known as Mexican blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. The plant features aromatic leaves and small blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its botanical characteristics include opposite leaves with serrated edges and a branched inflorescence.

Lippia macrostachya is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
