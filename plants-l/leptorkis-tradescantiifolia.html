Leptorkis tradescantiifolia

Leptorkis tradescantiifolia, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are silver-white with a distinctive, spider-like pattern, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light. It is relatively low maintenance and can be propagated through division or by seed.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
