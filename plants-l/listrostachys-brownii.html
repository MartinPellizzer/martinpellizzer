Listrostachys brownii

Listrostachys brownii, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, woody capsules containing seeds. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
