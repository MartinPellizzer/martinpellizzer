Lepidium nitidum var. howellii

Lepidium nitidum var. howellii, commonly known as Howell's smooth wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many mustard species. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in open areas.

This variety is notable for its compact growth habit and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.
