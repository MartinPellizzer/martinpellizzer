Lithocardium fresenii

Lithocardium fresenii, commonly known as the rock lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or white flowers. The plant thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It is often found in coastal areas and mountainous regions.

Lithocardium fresenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
