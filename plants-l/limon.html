Limon

Limon, commonly known as the lemon tree, is a citrus plant native to Asia and widely cultivated for its fruit.

It is a small evergreen tree with fragrant, dark green leaves and yellow-orange fruits. The fruit contains a high concentration of citric acid, making it an essential ingredient in cooking and beverages. Limon also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.

Its flowers are white and fragrant, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
