Limodorum thunbergii

Limodorum thunbergii, commonly known as the Japanese ghost orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to Japan and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pale green, strap-like leaves and its unique, showy flowers that resemble ghostly apparitions. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually a combination of white and green hues, with intricate patterns that enhance its ethereal appearance.

Limodorum thunbergii is a delicate species that requires specific growing conditions to thrive.
