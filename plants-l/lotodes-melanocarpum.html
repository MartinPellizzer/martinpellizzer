Lotodes melanocarpum

Lotodes melanocarpum, commonly known as the black-fruited water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide shade on the water surface. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, fragrant odor. The plant produces dark, berry-like fruits that contain seeds.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various water organisms.
