Lindsaea hornei

Lindsaea hornei, commonly known as the Horne's spikemoss, is a species of fern ally native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Lindsaeaceae and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features whorled leaves that are typically lanceolate and have a smooth, glossy surface. It produces small, clustered spore capsules that are borne on specialized structures called sporophylls.

Lindsaea hornei thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.
