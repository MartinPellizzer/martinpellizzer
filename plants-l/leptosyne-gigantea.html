Leptosyne gigantea

Leptosyne gigantea, commonly known as the giant leptosyne, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Leptosyne gigantea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
