Lens esculenta var. vulgaris

Lens esculenta var. vulgaris, commonly known as the common pea.

This plant is a member of the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its small, lens-shaped seeds. It is an annual herb that produces small, green pods containing the edible seeds. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is widely cultivated for its nutritional value.

Its seeds are rich in protein and are commonly used in various cuisines around the world.
