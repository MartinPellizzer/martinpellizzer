Lycaste mastersiana

Lycaste mastersiana, commonly known as the Masters' lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically blooms in the summer months, adding a striking visual element to gardens or greenhouses. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Lycaste mastersiana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
