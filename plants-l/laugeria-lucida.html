Laugeria lucida

Laugeria lucida, commonly known as the shining laurel, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes other aromatic plants like cinnamon and bay laurel. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by dark blue berries.

Laugeria lucida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
