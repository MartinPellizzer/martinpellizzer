Listera taiwaniana

Listera taiwaniana, commonly known as Taiwan basket lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are heart-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are slender and often bear the flowers at the tips.

Listera taiwaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate growing conditions.
