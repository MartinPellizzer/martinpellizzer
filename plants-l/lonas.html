Lonas

Lonas, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the genus *Calliandra* in the legume family.

It is characterized by its drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that resemble cascading showers, hence its common name. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets, giving it a feathery appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers are not only visually striking but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
