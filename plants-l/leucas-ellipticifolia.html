Leucas ellipticifolia

Leucas ellipticifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf leucas, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or shaded areas.

Leucas ellipticifolia is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
