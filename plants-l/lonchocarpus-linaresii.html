Lonchocarpus linaresii

Lonchocarpus linaresii, commonly known as the Linares' sensitiveplant, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its sensitive leaves that fold when touched. The plant produces striking yellow flowers followed by distinctive pod fruits. It is often found in humid, lowland areas and thrives in partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
