Leersia oryzoides f. latifolia

Leersia oryzoides f. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf leersia, is a variant of the leersia species native to parts of Asia.

It is a grass-like plant characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands or wetlands. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, spike-like flowers.

The botanical features of this variant make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
