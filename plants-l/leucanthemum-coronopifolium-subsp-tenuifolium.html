Leucanthemum coronopifolium subsp. tenuifolium

Leucanthemum coronopifolium subsp.

tenuifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved oxeye daisy, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features showy white ray flowers surrounding a central yellow disk, typical of the Asteraceae family.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
