Liliago japonica

Liliago japonica, commonly known as the Japanese lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Liliago japonica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
