Leucas kassneri

Leucas kassneri, commonly known as the yellow leucas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
