Lacandonia schismatica

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has a short stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers during the spring season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to the specific microclimate of its native habitat.
