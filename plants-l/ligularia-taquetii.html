Ligularia taquetii

Ligularia taquetii, commonly known as the African arrowhead, is a striking perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in water gardens or bog landscapes.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
