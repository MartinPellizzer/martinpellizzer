Laelia albida var. labellopurpurea

Laelia albida var. labellopurpurea, commonly known as the Purple-leafed White Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking white petals with a distinctive purple-tinted base, giving it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often with a subtle fragrance, and it prefers warm, humid conditions with good air circulation.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
