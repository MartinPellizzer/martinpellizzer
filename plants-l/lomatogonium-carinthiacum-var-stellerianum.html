Lomatogonium carinthiacum var. stellerianum

Lomatogonium carinthiacum var. stellerianum, commonly known as the Steller's lomatogonium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, and its flowers are typically yellow to orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it contributes to the biodiversity of rocky and grassy environments.
