Lithocarpus Synbalanos

Lithocarpus synbalanos is a unique evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Fagaceae, which includes oaks and chestnuts, but it stands out due to its distinctive appearance and ecological role. Despite its beauty and resilience, Lithocarpus synbalanos remains relatively understudied, making it an intriguing subject for botanical research.
