Lithocarpus pachyphyllus var. fruticosus

Lithocarpus pachyphyllus var. fruticosus, commonly known as the thick-leaf stone oak, is a member of the Fagaceae family, characterized by its evergreen broad leaves and hard, woody fruits.

The fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed, typical of the genus Lithocarpus.
