Lobelia filiformis var. krebsiana

Lobelia filiformis var. krebsiana, commonly known as the slender lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. This variety thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. It is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other damp habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
