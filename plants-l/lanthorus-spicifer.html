Lanthorus spicifer

Lanthorus spicifer, commonly known as the spiky lantus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It features upright, branched stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Lanthorus spicifer is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
