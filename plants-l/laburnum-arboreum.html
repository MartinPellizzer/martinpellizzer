Laburnum arboreum

Laburnum arboreum, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring. The leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets arranged along a central stem. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain flattened, kidney-shaped seeds.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
