Lobelia erinus var. atrorosea

Lobelia erinus var. atrorosea, commonly known as the dark rose moss plant, is a compact, trailing perennial native to South Africa.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep purple, often with a contrasting dark throat. The plant produces fluffy, white seed heads that are visually striking and attract pollinators. Its foliage is typically gray-green, covered in fine hairs, giving it a unique, moss-like appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and hanging baskets.
