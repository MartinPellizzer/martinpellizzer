Liparis balansae

Liparis balansae, commonly known as the Balansae orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by insects.

Liparis balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
