Lantana achyranthifolia f. grandifolia

Lantana achyranthifolia f. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered in fine pubescence. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in full sun.

It is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
