Litsea nigrescens

Litsea nigrescens, commonly known as black-laurel or black-litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
