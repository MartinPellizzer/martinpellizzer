Lactuca crassifolia

Lactuca crassifolia, commonly known as broadleaf lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves that are often used in culinary applications. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Lactuca crassifolia is typically found in moist habitats such as meadows and stream banks.
