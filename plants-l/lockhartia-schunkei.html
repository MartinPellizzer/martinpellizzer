Lockhartia schunkei

Lockhartia schunkei, commonly known as the Schunke's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces erect, slender stems that bear several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and are often violet or purple in color.

Lockhartia schunkei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique and delicate floral display.
