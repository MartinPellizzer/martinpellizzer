Landolphia Myrtifolia

Landolphia myrtifolia is a striking member of the Malvaceae family known for its vibrant orange flowers and thick, waxy leaves. Native to tropical regions of Africa, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections. Despite its beauty, it is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine. This article explores the characteristics, care, and cultural significance of Landolphia myrtifolia.
