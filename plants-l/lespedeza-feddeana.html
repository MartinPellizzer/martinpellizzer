Lespedeza feddeana

Lespedeza feddeana, commonly known as the Fedde's lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it has a fibrous root system.

Lespedeza feddeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
