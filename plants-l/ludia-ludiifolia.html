Ludia ludiifolia

Ludia ludiifolia, commonly known as the joyweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
