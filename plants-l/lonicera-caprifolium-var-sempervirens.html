Lonicera caprifolium var. sempervirens

Lonicera caprifolium var. sempervirens, commonly known as evergreen honeysuckle, is a deciduous to evergreen shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that remain green throughout the year in its evergreen form. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters during spring. Its berries are red or orange and serve as a food source for birds.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
