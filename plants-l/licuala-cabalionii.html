Licuala cabalionii

Licuala cabalionii, commonly known as the golden bamboo palm, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, fan-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically a vibrant green with a subtle golden hue on the underside, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
