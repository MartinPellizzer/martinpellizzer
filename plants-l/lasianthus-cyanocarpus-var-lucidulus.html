Lasianthus cyanocarpus var. lucidulus

Lasianthus cyanocarpus var. lucidulus, commonly known as the glossy blue-fruited hibiscus, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-black berries that remain on the plant throughout the year. The leaves are typically glossy and dark green, with a smooth texture. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique fruiting display.
