Lilium miquelianum var. flavum

Lilium miquelianum var. flavum, commonly known as the yellow Asian lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or pink throat. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in two rows around the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
