Lycium friesii

Lycium friesii, commonly known as the African wolfberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshade plants. The plant features small, yellow-orange berries that are edible and often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy texture.

Lycium friesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
