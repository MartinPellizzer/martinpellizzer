Landtia rueppellii

Landtia rueppellii, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a compact growth habit.
