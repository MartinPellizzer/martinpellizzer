Lasianthus Tawadae

Tawada's lasianthus, scientifically known as Lasianthus tawadae, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This species belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes over 13,000 species of flowering plants worldwide. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters, adding a subtle elegance to its foliage.
