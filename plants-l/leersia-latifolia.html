Leersia latifolia

Leersia latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf arrowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

Leersia latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
