Lathyrus Montanus F. Albiflorus

Lathyrus montanus f. albiflorus is a rare and striking plant known for its distinctive white flowers. It is a variant of the mountain larkspur, native to specific regions in the western United States. This plant thrives in rocky soils and high elevations, often found in alpine environments. Its unique floral coloration sets it apart from other members of the Lathyrus genus. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
