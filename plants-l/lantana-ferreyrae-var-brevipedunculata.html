Lantana ferreyrae var. brevipedunculata

Lantana ferreyrae var. brevipedunculata, commonly known as the Mexican lantana, is a shrub native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, blooming throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant flowering make it suitable for borders and container planting.
