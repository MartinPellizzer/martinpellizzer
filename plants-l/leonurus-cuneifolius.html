Leonurus cuneifolius

Leonurus cuneifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved hedgehog.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Leonurus cuneifolius is also known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.
