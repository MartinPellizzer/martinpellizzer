Lycoris × jinzheniae

Lycoris × jinzheniae, commonly known as the Jiangnan lycoris, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to eastern China and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in the autumn season. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that remain throughout the year, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its unique floral display and foliage make it a popular choice in horticulture.
