Lilium bakerianum var. yunnanense

Lilium bakerianum var. yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan lily, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces large, white or pale pink blossoms that bloom in late summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
