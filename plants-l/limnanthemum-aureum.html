Limnanthemum aureum

Limnanthemum aureum, commonly known as the golden star-of-Bethlehem, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
