Ligularia subspicata

Ligularia subspicata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a rough, textured surface and a dark green coloration. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a spiky inflorescence, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a dramatic foliage plant.
