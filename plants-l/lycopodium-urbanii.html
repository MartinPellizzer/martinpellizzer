Lycopodium urbanii

Lycopodium urbanii, commonly known as the urban clubmoss, is a small evergreen shrub belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves that form a low, compact mat. The plant produces spore-bearing cones, which are typically found at the base of the foliage. Lycopodium urbanii thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in urban parks and gardens.

It is valued for its ornamental appearance and ability to tolerate urban conditions.
