Lotononis involucrata subsp. bracteata

Lotononis involucrata subsp.

bracteata, commonly known as the bracteate daisies, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while the stems are erect and often covered in fine hairs.
