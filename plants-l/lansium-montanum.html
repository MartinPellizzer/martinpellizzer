Lansium montanum

Lansium montanum, commonly known as the mountain Lansium, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. This plant is often grown for its fruit, which is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
