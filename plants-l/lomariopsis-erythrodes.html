Lomariopsis erythrodes

Lomariopsis erythrodes, commonly known as the red-stemmed fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically green with a reddish tinge on the underside. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, spore-bearing leaves.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and ease of care.
