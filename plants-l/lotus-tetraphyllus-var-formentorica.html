Lotus tetraphyllus var. formentorica

Lotus tetraphyllus var. formentorica, commonly known as the Mediterranean lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features palmately lobed leaves and produces striking pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to dry, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its root system is fibrous, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
