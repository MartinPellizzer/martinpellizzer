Lathyrus pilosus var. miyabei

Lathyrus pilosus var. miyabei, commonly known as the Japanese vetch, is a perennial legume native to Japan.

It features slender, climbing stems that bear small, pea-like flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant has trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valued species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
