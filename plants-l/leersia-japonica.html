Leersia japonica

Leersia japonica, commonly known as Japanese leersia, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear delicate, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Leersia japonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
