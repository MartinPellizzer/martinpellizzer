Lactuca intybacea

Lactuca intybacea, commonly known as the slender lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry, open habitats. The plant has upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster at the top of the stem.

The species is sometimes cultivated for its edible leaves, though it is less commonly consumed than other lettuce varieties.
