Lysimachia herbemontii

Lysimachia herbemontii, commonly known as the Alpine yellow loosestrife, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It typically grows in moist, rocky soils and thrives in high-altitude environments. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

Lysimachia herbemontii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
