Lepidium Karelinianum

Lepidium karelinianum is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia. It thrives in harsh, arid environments where few other species can survive. Its small, clustered flowers and unique seed structure make it a subject of scientific interest. Despite its ecological significance, it remains poorly studied and critically endangered. This article explores its biology, habitat, and conservation challenges.
