Liparis stenoglossa

Liparis stenoglossa, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Liparis stenoglossa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
