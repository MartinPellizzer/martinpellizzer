Lasianthus tomentosus var. hiiranensis

Lasianthus tomentosus var. hiiranensis, commonly known as the Hiranensis lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
