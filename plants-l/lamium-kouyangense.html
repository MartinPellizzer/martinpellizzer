Lamium kouyangense

Lamium kouyangense, commonly known as the Yunkiang deadnettle, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, two-lipped flowers in shades of pink to purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and interesting species within the genus Lamium.
