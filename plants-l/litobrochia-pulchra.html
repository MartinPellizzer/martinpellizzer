Litobrochia pulchra

Litobrochia pulchra, commonly known as the pink rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

Litobrochia pulchra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
