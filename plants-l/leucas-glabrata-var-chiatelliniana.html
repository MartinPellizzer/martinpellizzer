Leucas glabrata var. chiatelliniana

Leucas glabrata var. chiatelliniana, commonly known as the Chiatellinian leucas, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying shades of purple or pink.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
