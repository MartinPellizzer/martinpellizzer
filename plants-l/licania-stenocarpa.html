Licania stenocarpa

Licania stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruit licania, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces distinctive, elongated pods that contain seeds surrounded by a fibrous covering. It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
