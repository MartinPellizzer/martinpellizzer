Lathyrus laxiflorus var. angustifolius

Lathyrus laxiflorus var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The leaves are narrow and trifoliate, contributing to its ornamental appeal in gardens. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

It is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, benefiting the surrounding soil ecosystem.
