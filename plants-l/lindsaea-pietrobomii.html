Lindsaea pietrobomii

Lindsaea pietrobomii, commonly known as the Pietrobomii's spikemoss, is a species of fern ally native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Lindsaeaceae and is characterized by its erect, branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces tiny, clustered spore capsules that are typically found at the tips of its branches. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant plant.
