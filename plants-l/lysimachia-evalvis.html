Lysimachia evalvis

Lysimachia evalvis, commonly known as marsh woundwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist or wet environments such as marshes, stream banks, and woodland areas. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.
