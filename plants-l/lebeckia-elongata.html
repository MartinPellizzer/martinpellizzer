Lebeckia elongata

Lebeckia elongata, commonly known as the slender lebeckia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are small and yellow, forming dense clusters at the tips of the branches.

Lebeckia elongata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
