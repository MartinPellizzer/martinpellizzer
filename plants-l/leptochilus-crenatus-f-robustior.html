Leptochilus crenatus f. robustior

Leptochilus crenatus f. robustior, commonly known as the robust leafed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This variant is noted for its more robust growth compared to the typical form of Leptochilus crenatus.
