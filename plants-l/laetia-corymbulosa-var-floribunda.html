Laetia corymbulosa var. floribunda

Laetia corymbulosa var. floribunda, commonly known as the showy laetia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, branched inflorescences that bear numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
