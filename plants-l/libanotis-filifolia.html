Libanotis filifolia

Libanotis filifolia, commonly known as the silky filifolium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscaping.
