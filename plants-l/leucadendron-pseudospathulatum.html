Leucadendron pseudospathulatum

Leucadendron pseudospathulatum, commonly known as the silver leaf leucadendron, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and conical, spiky flower heads. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers are typically yellow or red, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
