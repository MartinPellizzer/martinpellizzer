Lonicera xylosteum f. subcordata

Lonicera xylosteum f. subcordata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf honeysuckle, is a compact shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically narrow and ovate, with a slightly curled base, giving it its subspecies name. The plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.
