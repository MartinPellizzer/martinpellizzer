Ligularia yoshizoeana

Ligularia yoshizoeana, commonly known as Yoshizoe's ligularia, is a rare and striking herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas with rich, well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and delicate growth requirements, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species in botanical collections.
