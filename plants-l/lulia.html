Lulia

Lulia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Lulia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
