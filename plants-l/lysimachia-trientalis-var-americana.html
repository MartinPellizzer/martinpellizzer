Lysimachia trientalis var. americana

Lysimachia trientalis var. americana, commonly known as American yellow loosestrife, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, wooded areas and wetlands. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the summer months. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in swampy or marshy habitats.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in wet environments and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
