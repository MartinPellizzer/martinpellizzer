Leptopteris × Intermedia

Leptopteris × intermedia is a hybrid fern known for its unique combination of traits from its parent species. It typically features delicate fronds with a striking contrast between light and dark green hues. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. Its ability to adapt to various soil types adds to its appeal among horticulturists. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to maintain its health and vibrancy.
