Leucantha

Leucantha, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Leucantha is often found in savannah and woodland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
