Ludwigia glandulosa var. typica

Ludwigia glandulosa var. typica, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-red foliage, which becomes more vibrant in low light conditions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically borne on short, erect spikes. It is often grown as an aquatic or semi-aquatic plant in freshwater environments.

Its rapid growth rate and ornamental value make it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens.
