Leptogramma Jinyunensis

Jinyun leptogramma, scientifically known as Leptogramma jinyunensis, is a rare and unique fern species native to the mountainous regions of China. This plant belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, intricately shaped fronds. Discovered relatively recently, it has captured the attention of botanists due to its distinctive morphology and limited distribution. The species thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on rocky substrates or in dense forest undergrowth. Its discovery highlights the ongoing importance of biodiversity research in understudied regions of the world.
