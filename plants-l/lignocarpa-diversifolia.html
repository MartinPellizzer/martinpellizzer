Lignocarpa diversifolia

Lignocarpa diversifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed lignocarp, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by woody fruits containing several seeds. Lignocarpa diversifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its unique foliage and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
