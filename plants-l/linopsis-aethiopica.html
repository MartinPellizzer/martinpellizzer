Linopsis aethiopica

Linopsis aethiopica, commonly known as the African orchid, is a species of orchid native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers. The plant produces intricate, fragrant blooms that are typically violet or purple in color. Linopsis aethiopica thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in montane forests.

Its unique floral structure and habitat make it a notable species in botanical studies.
