Linaria syspirensis

Linaria syspirensis, commonly known as the slender toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves are adapted to arid conditions.

Linaria syspirensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
