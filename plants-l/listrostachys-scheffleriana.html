Listrostachys scheffleriana

Listrostachys scheffleriana, commonly known as the giant umbrella plant, is a striking member of the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like compound leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. Due to its size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
