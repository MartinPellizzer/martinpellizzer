Lepanthes discolor

Lepanthes discolor, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces showy, pale yellow or white flowers with a distinctive greenish hue. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom during the spring or early summer.

Lepanthes discolor is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
