Lonicera johnstonii

Lonicera johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and hairy underneath. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange-red, blooming in early spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

Lonicera johnstonii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
