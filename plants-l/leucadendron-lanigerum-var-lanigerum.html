Leucadendron lanigerum var. lanigerum

Leucadendron lanigerum var. lanigerum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a South African shrub or small tree characterized by its silvery-gray foliage.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces fluffy, yellow to orange flower heads that bloom in spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.

The silver tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native habitats.
