Lightia

Lightia, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange, resembling flames. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Lightia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.
