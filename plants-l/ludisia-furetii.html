Ludisia furetii

Ludisia furetii, commonly known as the silver lace plant, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that resemble lace, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Ludisia furetii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardeners.
