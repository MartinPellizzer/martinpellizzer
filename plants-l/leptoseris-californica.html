Leptoseris californica

Leptoseris californica, commonly known as the California sea hare, is a species of sea slug found in the coastal waters of California.

It belongs to the family Aplysiidae and is characterized by its soft, gelatinous body and vibrant coloration. This marine mollusk lacks a shell and is typically found in shallow, rocky intertidal zones. Its diet consists mainly of algae, and it plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat.
