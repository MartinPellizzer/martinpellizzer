Lantana × flava f. sandersii

Lantana × flava f. sandersii, commonly known as the sandersii lantana, is a hybrid shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, blooming throughout the growing season. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can be invasive in some regions and requires careful management to prevent spread.
