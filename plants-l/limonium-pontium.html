Limonium pontium

Limonium pontium, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has silvery-gray foliage and is often found growing in coastal areas and sandy soils. Limonium pontium is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained conditions.

It is widely cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
