Lomatogonium oreocharis

Lomatogonium oreocharis, commonly known as the mountain morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to blue flowers that bloom in the early morning, hence its common name. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces distinctive, spirally arranged fruits. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
