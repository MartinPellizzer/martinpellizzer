Lebeckia leucoclada

Lebeckia leucoclada, commonly known as the white-flowered lebeckia, is a South African native plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Lebeckia leucoclada is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its elegant floral display.
