Lysimachia coreana

Lysimachia coreana, commonly known as Korean yellow loosestrife, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Korea.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy appearance.
