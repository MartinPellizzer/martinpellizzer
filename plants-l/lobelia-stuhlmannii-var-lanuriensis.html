Lobelia stuhlmannii var. lanuriensis

Lobelia stuhlmannii var. lanuriensis, commonly known as the Woolly Lobelia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique texture.
