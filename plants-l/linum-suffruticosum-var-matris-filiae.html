Linum suffruticosum var. matris-filiae

Linum suffruticosum var. matris-filiae, commonly known as the mother's love plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, trailing stems that produce clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its common name reflects its historical use in herbal medicine for its purported healing properties.
