Lonchocarpus argyrotrichus

Lonchocarpus argyrotrichus, commonly known as the silver-haired legume, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive silver-hair covered stems. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
