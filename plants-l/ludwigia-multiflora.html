Ludwigia multiflora

Ludwigia multiflora, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant typically grows in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, such as ponds and wetlands. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped and can vary in color from deep green to reddish hues.

Ludwigia multiflora is often cultivated in water gardens and aquariums for its ornamental value.
