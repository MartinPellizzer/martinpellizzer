Laserpitium verticillatum

Laserpitium verticillatum, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Laserpitium verticillatum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
