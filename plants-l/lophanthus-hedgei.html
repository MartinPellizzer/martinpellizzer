Lophanthus hedgei

Lophanthus hedgei, commonly known as the Hawaiian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

Lophanthus hedgei is adapted to grow in coastal and dry forest habitats, often on rocky or sandy soils.
