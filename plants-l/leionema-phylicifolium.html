Leionema phylicifolium

Leionema phylicifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf leionema, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rainforest or woodland areas.

Leionema phylicifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
