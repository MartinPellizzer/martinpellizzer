Lefrovia

Lefrovia, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight. Its unique leaf structure helps it retain moisture in arid environments.

Lefrovia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home garden.
