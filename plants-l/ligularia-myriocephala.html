Ligularia myriocephala

Ligularia myriocephala, commonly known as the broad-leaf ligularia, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy surface and serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to moist garden settings.
