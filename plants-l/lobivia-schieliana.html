Lobivia schieliana

Lobivia schieliana, commonly known as the Schiel's lobivia, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to the arid regions of Argentina.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short stem and dense, spiny foliage. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Lobivia schieliana is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative succulent.
