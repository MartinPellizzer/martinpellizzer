Lomariopsis pollicina

Lomariopsis pollicina, commonly known as the small-leaf fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, rosette-like formation. Lomariopsis pollicina thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
