Lycopersicon ochranthum

Lycopersicon ochranthum, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are toxic to humans and animals. Its leaves are broad and lobed, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often found in disturbed soils.
