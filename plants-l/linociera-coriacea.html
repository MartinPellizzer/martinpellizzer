Linociera coriacea

Linociera coriacea, commonly known as the rough linociera, is a shrub native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that give it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Linociera coriacea is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
