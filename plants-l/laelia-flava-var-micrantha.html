Laelia flava var. micrantha

Laelia flava var. micrantha, commonly known as the yellow laelia, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant typically produces multiple flower spikes with showy, fragrant blooms during the spring and early summer. It prefers cool, high-altitude environments with moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and resilience.
