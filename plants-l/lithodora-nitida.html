Lithodora nitida

Lithodora nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf germander, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, fragrant blue or violet flowers. The leaves are narrow, opposite, and covered with fine, silvery hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Lithodora nitida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
