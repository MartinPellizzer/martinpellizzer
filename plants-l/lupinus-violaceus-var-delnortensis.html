Lupinus violaceus var. delnortensis

Lupinus violaceus var. delnortensis, commonly known as the Del Norte lupine, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of violet to purple flowers. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting local pollinators.
