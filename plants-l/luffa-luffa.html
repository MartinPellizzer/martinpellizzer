Luffa luffa

Luffa luffa, commonly known as sponge gourd, is a flowering vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is cultivated for its fibrous, sponge-like fruit. The plant produces large, lobed leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The fruit matures to a pale yellow or greenish color, with a spongy interior that becomes usable after drying.

It is valued for its versatility in both culinary and cosmetic applications.
