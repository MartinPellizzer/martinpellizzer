Lens nigricans

Lens nigricans, commonly known as the black lentil, is a species of legume belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark-colored seeds, which are typically used in various culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain the edible seeds. Native to regions of southern Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in temperate climates.

Its botanical features include compound leaves and flowers that are usually pale in color.
