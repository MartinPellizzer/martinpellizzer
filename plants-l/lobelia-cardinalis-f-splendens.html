Lobelia cardinalis f. splendens

Lobelia cardinalis f. splendens, commonly known as the Scarlet Cardinal Lobelia, is a striking flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a cultivar of the cardinal lobelia, characterized by its vibrant red, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches and features lance-shaped, dark green leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and naturalized areas.
