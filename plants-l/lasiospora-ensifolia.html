Lasiospora ensifolia

Lasiospora ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It has a distinctive greenish-gray thallus with a leathery texture and is often found on bark or rocks. The plant's morphology includes a dense, branching structure with leaf-like lobes that are narrow and sword-shaped. It is characterized by its ability to form symbiotic relationships with algae, which provide the necessary photosynthetic function.

This lichen is an indicator species for environmental conditions and is often used in ecological studies.
