Lyperia mollis

Lyperia mollis, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Lyperia mollis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
