Lantana minasensis var. hispida

Lantana minasensis var. hispida, commonly known as the Brazilian lantana, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
