Lestibudesia philippica

Lestibudesia philippica, commonly known as the Philippine star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped blossoms. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its roots are often epiphytic, meaning they grow on the surfaces of trees rather than in soil.

Lestibudesia philippica is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
