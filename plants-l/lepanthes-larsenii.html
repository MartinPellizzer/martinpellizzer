Lepanthes larsenii

Lepanthes larsenii, commonly known as the Lara orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Lepanthes larsenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
