Lonicera trichosantha var. deflexicalyx

Lonicera trichosantha var. deflexicalyx, commonly known as the velvet honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. Its fruit is a small, red berry that ripens in late summer, attracting birds and other wildlife.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
