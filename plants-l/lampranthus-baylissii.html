Lampranthus baylissii

Lampranthus baylissii, commonly known as the Orange Sunset or Cape Sunset, is a striking member of the Aizoaceae family native to South Africa.

This succulent plant is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red, blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. The plant has fleshy, cylindrical leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a desirable ornamental plant in regions with Mediterranean climates.
