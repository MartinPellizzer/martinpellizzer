Linaria nigricans

Linaria nigricans, commonly known as the blackface toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a perennial herb with slender, branched stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are spurred at the base, a characteristic feature of the Linaria genus.

Linaria nigricans is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
