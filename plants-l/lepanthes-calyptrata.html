Lepanthes calyptrata

Lepanthes calyptrata, commonly known as the calyptrate orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often feature vibrant colors and intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as cloud forests, where it thrives in the understory. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with sepals and petals that are often marked with contrasting hues.

Lepanthes calyptrata is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
