Lysimachia japonica

Lysimachia japonica, commonly known as Japanese chickweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to May. The plant has alternate, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Lysimachia japonica is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
