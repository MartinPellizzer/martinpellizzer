Lockhartia goyazensis

Lockhartia goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces inflorescences with several blooms, each featuring a velvety, dark purple to reddish hue. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
