Leptogramma mollissima

Leptogramma mollissima, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to the wetlands of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grasses. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in swampy or boggy habitats.
