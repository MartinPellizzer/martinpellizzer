Leptomeria rubescens

Leptomeria rubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed broom, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, reddish stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and long-lasting flowers.
