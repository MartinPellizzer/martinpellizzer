Liparis rizalensis

Liparis rizalensis, commonly known as the Rizal's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
