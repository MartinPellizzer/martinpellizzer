Lathyrus Asphodeloides Var. Varius

Lathyrus asphodeloides var. varius is a striking member of the legume family known for its vibrant purple and white flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. The variety 'varius' stands out for its distinctive leaf patterns and extended blooming period. Cultivated for both its beauty and ecological benefits, it adds a touch of elegance to any landscape.
