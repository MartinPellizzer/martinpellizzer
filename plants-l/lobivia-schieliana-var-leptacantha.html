Lobivia schieliana var. leptacantha

Lobivia schieliana var. leptacantha, commonly known as the slender-spined lobivia, is a compact cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid conditions.

It is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
