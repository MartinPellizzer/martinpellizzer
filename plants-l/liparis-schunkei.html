Liparis schunkei

Liparis schunkei, commonly known as the Schunke's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

Liparis schunkei is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique floral structure.
