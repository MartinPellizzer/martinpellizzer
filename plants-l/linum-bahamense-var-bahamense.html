Linum bahamense var. bahamense

Linum bahamense var. bahamense, commonly known as the Bahamian flax, is a flowering plant native to the Bahamas.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces attractive blue to violet flowers. The plant has slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often growing in the wild along the shoreline.

Its flowers bloom in the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
