Latouria forbesii

Latouria forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and is adapted to a variety of soil types. It produces a distinctive seed head that resembles a dandelion, with small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
