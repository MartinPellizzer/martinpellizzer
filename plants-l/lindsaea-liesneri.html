Lindsaea liesneri

Lindsaea liesneri, commonly known as the slender lindsaea, is a species of epiphytic fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lindsaeaceae and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that form dense, mat-like structures on tree trunks or branches. The plant produces small, clustered sporangia on the undersides of its leaves, which are typically concealed within the leaf tissue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high atmospheric moisture for successful spore dispersal.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, Lindsaea liesneri is of interest to botanists studying tropical rainforest ecosystems.
