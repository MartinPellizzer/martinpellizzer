Lepanthes posadae

Lepanthes posadae, commonly known as the Posada's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in cloud forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

Lepanthes posadae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
