Leptospermum lucidum

Leptospermum lucidum, commonly known as the silver honey bush, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and heathlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
