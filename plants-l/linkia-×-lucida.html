Linkia × lucida

Linkia × lucida, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Linkia sanguinea and Linkia wilsonii, characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically features large, velvety petals in shades of red, pink, or white, with a prominent yellow center. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and have a soft, velvety texture.
