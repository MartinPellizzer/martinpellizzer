Lathyrus pallescens var. pilosior

Lathyrus pallescens var. pilosior, commonly known as the soft-haired grass vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, ascending stems covered in soft, silky hairs, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale pink to white in color. Its trifoliate leaves are compound, with three oval-shaped leaflets.

This species is often found in dry, open habitats such as rocky soils and disturbed areas.
