Lilium speciosum f. roseum

Lilium speciosum f. roseum, commonly known as the roseum lily, is a hybrid cultivar of the species Lilium speciosum.

It is characterized by its striking pink to reddish-pink petals that bloom in late summer. The plant is a bulbous perennial with strap-like, grassy foliage that emerges in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant, showy flowers that add a dramatic touch to floral displays.
