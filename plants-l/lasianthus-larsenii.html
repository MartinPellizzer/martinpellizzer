Lasianthus larsenii

Lasianthus larsenii, commonly known as the mountain lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous growth habit. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically clustered in whorls and are often brightly colored, ranging from pink to purple.

Lasianthus larsenii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
