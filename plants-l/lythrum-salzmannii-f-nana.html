Lythrum salzmannii f. nana

Lythrum salzmannii f. nana, commonly known as dwarf lythrum, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, slender stems covered in dense clusters of vibrant pink to purple flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive addition to gardens and wetland landscapes.
