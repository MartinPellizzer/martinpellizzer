Lindenbergia abyssinica

Lindenbergia abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian sandmat, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, star-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are typically covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss.

Lindenbergia abyssinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in drought-tolerant gardens.
