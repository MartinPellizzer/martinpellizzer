Leucas abyssinica var. brachycalyx

Leucas abyssinica var. brachycalyx, commonly known as the short-calyx leucas, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, glandular leaves and clusters of pale blue to violet flowers. It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showing a high degree of adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its botanical features make it of interest for both ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
