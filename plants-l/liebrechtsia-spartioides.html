Liebrechtsia spartioides

Liebrechtsia spartioides, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its drought tolerance.

The species is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions, making it a hardy choice for landscaping.
