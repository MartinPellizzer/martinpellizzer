Linaria asparagoides

Linaria asparagoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant produces showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Linaria asparagoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
