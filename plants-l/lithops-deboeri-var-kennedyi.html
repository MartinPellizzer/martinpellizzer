Lithops deboeri var. kennedyi

Lithops deboeri var. kennedyi, commonly known as the "Cape Town beauty," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a pair of closely fused, rock-like leaves that are typically pale green with distinctive markings. The plant produces tall, spindly stems bearing clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
