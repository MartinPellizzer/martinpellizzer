Leptohyptis macrostachys

Leptohyptis macrostachys, commonly known as the large-stalked hyptis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features aromatic leaves and produces spikes of small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties.
