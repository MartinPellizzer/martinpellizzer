Lobivia boedekeriana

Lobivia boedekeriana, commonly known as the Boedeker's lobivia, is a small, compact cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, thick stem with spiny, rounded tubercles and produces yellow flowers during the growing season. The plant has a shallow root system adapted to absorb moisture from the dry soil. It is often cultivated as a decorative succulent due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Lobivia boedekeriana thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
