Limonium bonduellei

Limonium bonduellei, commonly known as the Mediterranean sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. The plant features gray-green, fuzzy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Limonium bonduellei is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
