Litsea sarawacensis

Litsea sarawacensis, commonly known as the Sarawak litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Borneo, particularly in the state of Sarawak, Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 15 meters.

Litsea sarawacensis plays a role in the local ecosystem, providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
