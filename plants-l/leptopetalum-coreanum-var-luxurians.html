Leptopetalum coreanum var. luxurians

Leptopetalum coreanum var. luxurians, commonly known as the Korean snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a delicate ornamental value to gardens. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture in temperate climates.
