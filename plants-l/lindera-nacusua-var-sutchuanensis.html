Lindera nacusua var. sutchuanensis

Lindera nacusua var. sutchuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan Lindera, is a variant of the Lindera genus native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within mountainous regions. Its fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed enclosed in a fleshy pulp.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
