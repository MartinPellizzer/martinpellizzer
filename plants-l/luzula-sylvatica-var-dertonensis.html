Luzula sylvatica var. dertonensis

Luzula sylvatica var. dertonensis, commonly known as the Dertonese sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its triangular stems and grass-like leaves. This variety is distinguished by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in woodland soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in garden settings.
