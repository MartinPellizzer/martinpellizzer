Luxemburgia ciliosa

Luxemburgia ciliosa, commonly known as the silky luxemburgia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
