Lobelia hancei

Lobelia hancei, commonly known as the Hance's lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Lobelia hancei is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
