Lampranthus globosus

Lampranthus globosus, commonly known as the firecracker flower, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant orange or red tubular flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Lampranthus globosus is prized for its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
