Lobivia bruchii var. nivalis

Lobivia bruchii var. nivalis, commonly known as the Snow Lobivia, is a compact cactus native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale blue spines that give it a frosty appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and low precipitation.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus enthusiasts.
