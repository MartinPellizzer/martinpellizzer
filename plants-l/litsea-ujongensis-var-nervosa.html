Litsea ujongensis var. nervosa

Litsea ujongensis var. nervosa, commonly known as the veined litsea, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves with prominent venation patterns. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in axillary inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, berry-like drupe that ripens to a dark color, often attracting various wildlife species.
