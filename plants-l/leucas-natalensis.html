Leucas natalensis

Leucas natalensis, commonly known as Natal leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves, and it produces small, clustered flower heads.

Leucas natalensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
