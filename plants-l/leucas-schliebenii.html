Leucas schliebenii

Leucas schliebenii, commonly known as Schlieben's leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.
