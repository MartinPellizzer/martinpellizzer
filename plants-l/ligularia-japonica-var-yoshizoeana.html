Ligularia japonica var. yoshizoeana

Ligularia japonica var. yoshizoeana, commonly known as the Yoshizoe's Japanese ligularia, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive yellowish-white margin. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flower heads that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in temperate climates.
