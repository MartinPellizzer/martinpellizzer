Lavatera triloba var. rotundata

Lavatera triloba var. rotundata, commonly known as the round-leaf lavatera, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its broad, rounded leaves and showy, fluffy flowers. The flowers, which are typically pink or white, bloom in late spring to early summer. This variety is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile addition to various landscapes.
