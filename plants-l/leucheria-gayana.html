Leucheria gayana

Leucheria gayana, commonly known as the African snowbell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
