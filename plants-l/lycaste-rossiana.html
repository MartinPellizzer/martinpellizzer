Lycaste rossiana

Lycaste rossiana, commonly known as the Rossiana Lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often with a waxy texture.

Lycaste rossiana prefers warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
