Leucosceptrum ningpoense

Leucosceptrum ningpoense, commonly known as the Ningpo bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.
