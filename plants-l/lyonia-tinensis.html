Lyonia tinensis

Lyonia tinensis, commonly known as the New Zealand kowhai, is a small evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Lyonia tinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
