Litsea guatemalensis

Litsea guatemalensis, commonly known as Guatemalan litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Central America, particularly Guatemala, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small white flowers. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface with a distinct aromatic scent.

The fruit is a small, round drupe that contains a single seed, contributing to its ecological and potential horticultural value.
