Lomatia opaca

Lomatia opaca, commonly known as the mountain lomatia, is a shrub native to Australia, particularly found in the south-western region.

It is characterized by its dense, woody growth and distinctive, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Lomatia opaca is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
