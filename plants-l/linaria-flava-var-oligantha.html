Linaria flava var. oligantha

Linaria flava var. oligantha, commonly known as the yellow sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
