Lycaste tricolor var. alba

Lycaste tricolor var. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered lycaste, is a rare and unique hybrid orchid species.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and a distinctive three-colored flower structure, which gives it its common name. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil. It requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to flourish.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
