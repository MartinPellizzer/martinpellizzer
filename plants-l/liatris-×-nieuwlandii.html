Liatris × nieuwlandii

Liatris × nieuwlandii, commonly known as the Newland's blazing star, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescences composed of numerous purple or pinkish flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade, preferring well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Liatris × nieuwlandii is valued in gardens for its ornamental display and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
