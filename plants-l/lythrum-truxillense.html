Lythrum truxillense

Lythrum truxillense, commonly known as the Trinidadian lythrum, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean island of Trinidad.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family, which includes plants known for their showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and are pollinated primarily by insects.

Lythrum truxillense is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral displays.
