Licuala lamdongensis

Licuala lamdongensis, commonly known as the Lamdong palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the mountainous areas of Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in diameter. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and montane forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
