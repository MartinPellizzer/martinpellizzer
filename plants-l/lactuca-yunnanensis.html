Lactuca yunnanensis

Lactuca yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its rosette of large, pale green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, followed by fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
