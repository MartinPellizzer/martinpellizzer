Lepidium calycotrichum var. brachystylum

Lepidium calycotrichum var. brachystylum, commonly known as the dwarf peppergrass, is a member of the Brassicaceae family and is native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
