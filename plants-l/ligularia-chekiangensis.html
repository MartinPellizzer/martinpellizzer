Ligularia chekiangensis

Ligularia chekiangensis, commonly known as the Chinese Ligularia, is a striking perennial plant native to China.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to shaded areas. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in water gardens or as a border plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both aesthetic and ecological value.
