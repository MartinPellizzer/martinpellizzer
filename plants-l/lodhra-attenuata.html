Lodhra attenuata

Lodhra attenuata, commonly known as the Indian privet, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

Lodhra attenuata is often found in moist, shaded areas and is used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
