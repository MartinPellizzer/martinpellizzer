Lirianthe Nana

Lirianthe nana is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its small, pale blue flowers that bloom in early spring. This species thrives in cool, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Lirianthe nana is now critically endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique plant.
