Laelia mixta

Laelia mixta, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features erect, branched stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are usually bright and showy, coming in shades of yellow, orange, and red.

Laelia mixta is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
