Ligustrum yunnanense

Ligustrum yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan privet, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by black, spherical berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.

Ligustrum yunnanense is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
