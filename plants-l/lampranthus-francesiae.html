Lampranthus francesiae

Lampranthus francesiae, commonly known as the Orange Sunset, is a member of the Aizoaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, tubular stems and vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Lampranthus francesiae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
