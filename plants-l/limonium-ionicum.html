Limonium ionicum

Limonium ionicum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, bract-like leaves and spikes of blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, often found in sandy or gravelly habitats. Limonium ionicum is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early autumn.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
