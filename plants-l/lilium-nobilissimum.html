Lilium nobilissimum

Lilium nobilissimum, commonly known as the Turk's cap lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, growing from a bulbous underground stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The Turk's cap lily is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
