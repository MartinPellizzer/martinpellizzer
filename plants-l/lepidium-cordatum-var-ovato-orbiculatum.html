Lepidium cordatum var. ovato-orbiculatum

Lepidium cordatum var. ovato-orbiculatum, commonly known as the heartleaf cress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often slightly rounded at the tips. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and early spring blooms.
