Lasianthus inconspicuus

Lasianthus inconspicuus, commonly known as the slender leaved gentian, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features opposite, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are blue to purple in color, with five petals and a distinctive tubular shape.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
