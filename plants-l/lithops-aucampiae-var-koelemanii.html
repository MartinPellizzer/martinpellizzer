Lithops aucampiae var. koelemanii

Lithops aucampiae var. koelemanii, commonly known as the "Cape Town Lithops," is a species of stone plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rock-like appearance, with two closely fused, fleshy leaves that resemble small stones. The plant produces a single flower stem with yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
