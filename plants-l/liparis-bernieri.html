Liparis bernieri

Liparis bernieri, commonly known as the Bernier's orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Madagascar, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short rhizome with fleshy roots and produces delicate, greenish-white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Liparis bernieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
