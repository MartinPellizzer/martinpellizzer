Lannea schimperi

Lannea schimperi, commonly known as the African lac tree, is a large, deciduous tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, rugged bark and can grow up to 25 meters in height. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain a single seed. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a leathery texture.

Lannea schimperi is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it an important component of local ecosystems.
