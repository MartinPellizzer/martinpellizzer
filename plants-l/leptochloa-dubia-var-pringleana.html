Leptochloa dubia var. pringleana

Leptochloa dubia var. pringleana, commonly known as Pringle's slender grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and wetlands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
