Leucanthemum croaticum

Leucanthemum croaticum, commonly known as the Croatian leucanthemum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features white ray florets surrounding a yellow disk, giving it a daisy-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often growing in meadows and woodland edges.

Leucanthemum croaticum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
