Litsea cylindrocarpa

Litsea cylindrocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed litsea, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green on top. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in腋生 (axillary) inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, elliptical drupe that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.
