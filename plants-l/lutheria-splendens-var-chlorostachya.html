Lutheria splendens var. chlorostachya

Lutheria splendens var. chlorostachya, commonly known as the golden lutheria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a dense, bushy habit. The plant produces striking clusters of bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It is well-suited for container gardening or as a specimen plant in rock gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant foliage and showy blooms.
