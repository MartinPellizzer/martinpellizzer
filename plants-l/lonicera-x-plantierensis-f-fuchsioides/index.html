Lonicera × Plantierensis F. Fuchsioides

Lonicera × plantierensis f. fuchsioides is a unique hybrid honeysuckle known for its striking floral display. This variety features vibrant red blooms that contrast beautifully with its glossy green foliage. Native to France, it thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its compact size makes it ideal for small gardens or container planting. Gardeners often appreciate its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
