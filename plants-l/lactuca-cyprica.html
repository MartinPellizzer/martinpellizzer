Lactuca cyprica

Lactuca cyprica, commonly known as Caucasian lettuce, is a species of annual herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically pale green to blue-green in color. Lactuca cyprica is cultivated for its edible leaves, which have a mild, slightly bitter taste.
