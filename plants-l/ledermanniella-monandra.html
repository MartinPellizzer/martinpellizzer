Ledermanniella monandra

Ledermanniella monandra, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Ledermanniella monandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in dry, sunny conditions.
