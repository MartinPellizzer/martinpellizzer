Lagophylla ramosissima

Lagophylla ramosissima, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
