Lamourouxia brachyantha

Lamourouxia brachyantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lamourouxia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
