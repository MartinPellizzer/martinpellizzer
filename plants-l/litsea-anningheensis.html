Litsea anningheensis

Litsea anningheensis, commonly known as the Anningheen's lime.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces small, round berries that are typically green when mature, turning dark when ripe.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments within lowland forests.
