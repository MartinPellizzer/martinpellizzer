Lantana hodgei

Lantana hodgei, commonly known as the Christmas lantana, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, compact foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often blooming in late summer and autumn. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture, while the stems are woody and often branched.

Lantana hodgei thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability.
