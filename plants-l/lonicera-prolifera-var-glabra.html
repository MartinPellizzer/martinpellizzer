Lonicera prolifera var. glabra

Lonicera prolifera var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features slender, woody stems that are smooth and often reddish-brown in color. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy, smooth surface.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
