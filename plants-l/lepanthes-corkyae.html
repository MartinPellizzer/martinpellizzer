Lepanthes corkyae

Lepanthes corkyae, commonly known as the Corky's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as cloud forests, where it thrives on tree bark or among leaf litter. Its flowers are usually small and showy, with intricate patterns and a wide range of colors.

Lepanthes corkyae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
