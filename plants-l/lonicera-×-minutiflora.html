Lonicera × minutiflora

Lonicera × minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, typically in shades of yellow and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often used in gardens and as a ground cover.
