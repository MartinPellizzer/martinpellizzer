Ligularia heterophylla

Ligularia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green underside, and its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in paniculate inflorescences. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas near streams or marshes. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to wet conditions.

The species exhibits significant morphological variation, which contributes to its common name.
