Lantana santosii

Lantana santosii, commonly known as the Santos lantana, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Lantana santosii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
