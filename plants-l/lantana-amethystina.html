Lantana amethystina

Lantana amethystina, commonly known as the purple lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are usually lance-shaped. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Lantana amethystina is also known for its fragrant flowers and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
