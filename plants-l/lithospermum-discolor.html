Lithospermum discolor

Lithospermum discolor, commonly known as the pale puccoon, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and opposite leaves. Lithospermum discolor thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.
