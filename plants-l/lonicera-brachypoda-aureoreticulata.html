Lonicera brachypoda aureoreticulata

Lonicera brachypoda aureoreticulata, commonly known as the golden netted honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated margin. This plant is known for its ornamental value, particularly in gardens where its delicate appearance and seasonal bloom make it a desirable addition.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of garden conditions.
