Lamium maculatum var. grenieri

Lamium maculatum var. grenieri, commonly known as the silver vein plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking silver-green mottling that contrasts beautifully with its deep green veins. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a valuable addition to both garden and landscape design.
