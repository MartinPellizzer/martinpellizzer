Lebeckia carnosa

Lebeckia carnosa, commonly known as the woolly lebeckia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Lebeckia carnosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
