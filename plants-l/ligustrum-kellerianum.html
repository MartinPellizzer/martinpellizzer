Ligustrum kellerianum

Ligustrum kellerianum, commonly known as the Chinese privet, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, opposite leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
