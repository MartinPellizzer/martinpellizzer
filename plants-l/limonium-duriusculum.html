Limonium duriusculum

Limonium duriusculum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its spiky, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in sandy or salty soils. It is often found in dunes and along shorelines, where it withstands harsh coastal conditions.

Limonium duriusculum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal ecosystems.
