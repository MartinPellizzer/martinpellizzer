Lilium canadense var. walkeri

Lilium canadense var. walkeri, commonly known as the Canadian white lily, is a variant of the showy lady's slipper lily.

It is native to eastern North America and typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands. The plant features large, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, recurved shape. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight pubescence.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in native habitats.
