Lobivia thionantha var. aurantiaca

Lobivia thionantha var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the Orange Ghost Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a flattened, ribbed stem that can grow up to 15 cm in height, with a distinctive orange to reddish coloration during the growing season. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
