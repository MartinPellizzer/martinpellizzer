Leianthus silenifolius

Leianthus silenifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid conditions. It has a compact growth habit with small, narrow leaves that help reduce water loss.

Leianthus silenifolius is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
