Listrostachys pellucida

Listrostachys pellucida, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in shrubland and woodland habitats.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in African herbal remedies.
