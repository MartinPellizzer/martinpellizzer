Ludwigia stricta

Ludwigia stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf water primrose, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in shallow water or damp soil, making it a common sight in wetlands and ponds. Ludwigia stricta is often used in aquatic gardens and as a decorative plant in water features.

It is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions and water temperatures.
