Leptostachya leptostochya

Leptostachya leptostochya, commonly known as the slender-stemmed bamboo, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in panicles.

Leptostachya leptostochya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
