Leucanthemella

Leucanthemella, commonly known as the white ragwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which consist of a central yellow disk surrounded by white ray florets. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grasslands. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and have a slightly serrated edge.

It is often found in sunny, open habitats and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
