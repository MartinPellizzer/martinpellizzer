Ligularia amplectens var. holmii

Ligularia amplectens var. holmii, commonly known as the Holmii variegated ligularia, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with striking variegation of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces showy, spadix-like flowers surrounded by broad, green bracts. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for shade gardens and water features.
