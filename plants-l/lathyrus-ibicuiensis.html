Lathyrus ibicuiensis

Lathyrus ibicuiensis, commonly known as the Iberian vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in rocky or dry habitats. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three leaflets, and it has a fibrous root system.

Lathyrus ibicuiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
