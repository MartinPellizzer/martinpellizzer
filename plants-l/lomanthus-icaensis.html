Lomanthus icaensis

Lomanthus icaensis, commonly known as the Ica flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Peru.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has a woody stem and broad, palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Lomanthus icaensis is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
