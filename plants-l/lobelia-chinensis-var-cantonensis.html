Lobelia chinensis var. cantonensis

Lobelia chinensis var. cantonensis, commonly known as the Canton lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to southern China.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky hillsides. The plant features slender stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, tubular, and clustered in dense spikes, often displaying shades of blue or purple.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
