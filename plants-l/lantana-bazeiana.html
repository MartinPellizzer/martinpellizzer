Lantana bazeiana

Lantana bazeiana, commonly known as the Bazei lantana, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, usually in shades of yellow, orange, or red. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Lantana bazeiana is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
