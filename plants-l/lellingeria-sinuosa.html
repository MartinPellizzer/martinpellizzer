Lellingeria sinuosa

Lellingeria sinuosa, commonly known as the sinuous lily, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Lellingeria sinuosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
