Linum jimenezii subsp. willkommii

Linum jimenezii subsp.

willkommii, commonly known as the Willkommer flax, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It belongs to the Linaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological niche.
