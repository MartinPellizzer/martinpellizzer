Lepidium africanum

Lepidium africanum, commonly known as African cress, is an annual herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its seeds are small and oval, often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Lepidium africanum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in gardens and along riverbanks.
