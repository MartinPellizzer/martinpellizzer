Lobivia kuehnrichii

Lobivia kuehnrichii, commonly known as the Kuehnrich's lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming during the cooler months. Its leaves are usually short and rounded, and it thrives in full sun with minimal water.

Lobivia kuehnrichii is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
