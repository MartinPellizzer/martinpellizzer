Lastrea aristeguietae

Lastrea aristeguietae, commonly known as the Aristeguietae lastrea, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to small tubercles, a feature common in many desert plants.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
