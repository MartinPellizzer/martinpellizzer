Liparis nokoensis

Liparis nokoensis, commonly known as the Noko's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomatous structure allows it to spread and form colonies.

The species is noted for its small, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in humid, temperate climates.
