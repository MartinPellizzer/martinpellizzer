Lambertia rariflora

Lambertia rariflora, commonly known as the rare-flowered lambertia, is a unique species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates with dry summers. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
