Lacistema floribundum

Lacistema floribundum, commonly known as the showy lacistema, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers, which range in color from white to purple, attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
