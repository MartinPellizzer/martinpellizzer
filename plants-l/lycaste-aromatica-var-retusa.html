Lycaste aromatica var. retusa

Lycaste aromatica var. retusa, commonly known as the fragrant lycaste, is a hybrid orchid species originating from the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, velvety leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, spicy scent. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fragrance, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
