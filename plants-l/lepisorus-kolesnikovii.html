Lepisorus kolesnikovii

Lepisorus kolesnikovii, commonly known as the Kolesnikov's polypody, is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically bright green and covered with fine hairs. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or on rocky slopes. Its rhizome system is extensive, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

Lepisorus kolesnikovii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental fronds and ability to thrive in humid, partially shaded conditions.
