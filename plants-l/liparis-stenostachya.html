Liparis stenostachya

Liparis stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stalked orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Liparis stenostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
