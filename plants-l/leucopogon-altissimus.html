Leucopogon altissimus

Leucopogon altissimus, commonly known as the tall heath, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.
