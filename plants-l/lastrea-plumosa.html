Lastrea plumosa

Lastrea plumosa, commonly known as the woolly lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fluffy white or pale green foliage that resembles a cloud or a tuft of wool. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the cooler months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
