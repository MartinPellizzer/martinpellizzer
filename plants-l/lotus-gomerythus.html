Lotus gomerythus

Lotus gomerythus, commonly known as the Chinese lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It thrives in freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes, where it forms dense floating mats. The plant has large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a distinctive purple center. Its leaves are broad and rounded, typically floating on the water surface, while its roots anchor in the sediment below.

The Chinese lotus is also valued for its symbolic significance in Asian cultures and its use in traditional medicine.
