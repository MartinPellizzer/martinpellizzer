Laelia × oweniae

Laelia × oweniae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Laelia purpurea and Laelia anceola.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the high-altitude regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces strikingly colorful, fragrant flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
