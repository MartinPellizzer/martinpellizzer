Leontodon eriopodon

Leontodon eriopodon, commonly known as the woolly leontodon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tufted, hairy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish composite flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Native to Europe, it is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
