Lonchocarpus lucidus

Lonchocarpus lucidus, commonly known as the shining legume, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant is notable for its bright green, glossy leaves and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often appearing in shades of pink or white.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
