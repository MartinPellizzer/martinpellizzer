Lessertia macrostachya

Lessertia macrostachya, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by edible, starchy tubers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
