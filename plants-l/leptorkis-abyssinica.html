Leptorkis abyssinica

Leptorkis abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian orchid, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
