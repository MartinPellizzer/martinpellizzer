Lindsaea stenomeris

Lindsaea stenomeris, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lindsaea, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pendulous fronds. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and have a soft, velvety texture.

Lindsaea stenomeris is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in controlled, moist conditions.
