Laosanthus graminifolius

Laosanthus graminifolius, commonly known as the grass-leafed Laosanthus, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
