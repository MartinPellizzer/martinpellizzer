Lithocardium mirandum

Lithocardium mirandum, commonly known as the fairy's lace plant, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that shimmer with a subtle iridescence. Lithocardium mirandum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
