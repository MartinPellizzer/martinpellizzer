Liparis densa

Liparis densa, commonly known as the dense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are often fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Liparis densa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
