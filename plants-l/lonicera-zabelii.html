Lonicera zabelii

Lonicera zabelii, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a red throat, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that matures in late summer, containing numerous small seeds.

Lonicera zabelii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
