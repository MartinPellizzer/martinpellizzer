Luzula campestris var. mannii

Luzula campestris var. mannii, commonly known as the meadow woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous pale green to yellowish flowers. Luzula campestris var. mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland habitats.
