Leucadendron foedum

Leucadendron foedum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray bark and feathery, needle-like leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flower heads, which are often grouped into spherical or conical shapes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is widely cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
