Lisianthius axillaris

Lisianthius axillaris, commonly known as the axillary lisianthius, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madeira.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. Lisianthius axillaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
