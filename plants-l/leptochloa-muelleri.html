Leptochloa muelleri

Leptochloa muelleri, commonly known as Mueller's slender grass, is a species of grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, narrow leaves and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas. It has small, spike-like inflorescences that produce numerous tiny flowers.

Leptochloa muelleri is adapted to arid conditions and is resilient to drought.
