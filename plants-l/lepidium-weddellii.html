Lepidium weddellii

Lepidium weddellii, commonly known as Weddell's cress, is a flowering plant species native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, white or yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and forest edges.
