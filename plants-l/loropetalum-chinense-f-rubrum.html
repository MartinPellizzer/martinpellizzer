Loropetalum chinense f. rubrum

Loropetalum chinense f. rubrum, commonly known as the redleaf loropetalum, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and its striking, deep red to purple foliage that persists throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, fragrant, pale pink flowers in spring, which are followed by ornamental red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its foliage color makes it a standout plant in gardens, particularly in regions with mild winters.
