Leontodon aureus

Leontodon aureus, commonly known as the golden leontodon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a single, showy head. The plant has basal leaves that are often hairy and linear in shape. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
