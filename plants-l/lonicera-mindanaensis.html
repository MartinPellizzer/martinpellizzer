Lonicera mindanaensis

Lonicera mindanaensis, commonly known as Mindanao honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a vigorous climber with opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruits are small, red berries that mature in the fall and are edible.

Lonicera mindanaensis is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
