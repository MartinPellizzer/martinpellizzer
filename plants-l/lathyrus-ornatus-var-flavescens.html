Lathyrus ornatus var. flavescens

Lathyrus ornatus var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered sweet pea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale yellow to orange. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its attractive blooms and moderate growth habit, it is often cultivated for both its aesthetic value and its ability to attract pollinators.
