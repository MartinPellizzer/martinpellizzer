Libertia insignis

Libertia insignis, commonly known as the golden lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple throat. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to both cultivated and natural landscapes.
