Litsea glaucescens var. schaffneri

Litsea glaucescens var. schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's litsea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and fruit-bearing habits. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within lowland forests. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a distinctive bluish-green color on the upper surface.

The flowers are small and clustered, followed by oval, fleshy fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.
