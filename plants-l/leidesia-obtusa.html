Leidesia obtusa

Leidesia obtusa, commonly known as the narrowleaf leidesia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers.
