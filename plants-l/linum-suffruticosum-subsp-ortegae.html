Linum suffruticosum subsp. ortegae

Linum suffruticosum subsp.

ortegae, commonly known as the Ortega's flax, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It is a low-growing shrub with slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for local biodiversity and ecological studies.
