Linum arboreum

Linum arboreum, commonly known as the tree flax, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Linum arboreum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
