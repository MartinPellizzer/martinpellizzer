Ligularia glauca

Ligularia glauca, commonly known as blue ligularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a bluish tinge on the upper surface. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clustered inflorescences, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Ligularia glauca is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
