Lastrea filix-mas var. lachoongensis

Lastrea filix-mas var. lachoongensis, commonly known as the Lachoongensis Snake Plant, is a variant of the Snake Plant genus characterized by its upright, sword-like leaves.

It is native to tropical regions and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. The plant is known for its air-purifying qualities, capable of removing toxins from the air. Its leaves are typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers under optimal conditions.

This variety is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal water and fertilizer.
