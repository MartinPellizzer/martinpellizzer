Linochilus rhomboidalis

Linochilus rhomboidalis, commonly known as the rhomboid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a short, erect stem with clustered blooms.

Linochilus rhomboidalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
