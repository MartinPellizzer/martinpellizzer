Lithospermum leonotis

Lithospermum leonotis, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces small, bright orange flowers with five petals. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are opposite and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or disturbed areas.

The flowers close at night and on cloudy days, a behavior known as nyctinasty.
