Lycianthes floccosa

Lycianthes floccosa, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruits are typically berries that turn from green to red as they mature.
