Litsea impressa

Litsea impressa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of China, India, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate, and often narrow leaves with a slightly serrated margin. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, spherical drupe that turns dark blue when mature, containing a single seed.
