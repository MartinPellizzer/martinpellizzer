Lathyrus schimperi

Lathyrus schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian vetch, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia and parts of Eritrea.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 1 meter tall, featuring slender, branching stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers in shades of pink or purple, which are arranged in racemes. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind.

Lathyrus schimperi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential agricultural and medicinal uses.
