Lindsaea macraeana

Lindsaea macraeana, commonly known as the Macrae's spikemoss, is a species of fern ally native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Lindsaeaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems covered in scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered sporangia on specialized branches called sporophylls. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rock crevices or on tree trunks.

Lindsaea macraeana plays a role in maintaining the biodiversity of its native habitats.
