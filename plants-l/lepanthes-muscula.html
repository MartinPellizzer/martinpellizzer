Lepanthes muscula

Lepanthes muscula, commonly known as the Muscular Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces delicate, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a unique structure that includes a spurred lip.

Lepanthes muscula is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
