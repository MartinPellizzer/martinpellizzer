Lophiolepis kosmelii

Lophiolepis kosmelii, commonly known as the mountain grass or Kosmelii grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green coloration, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils in mountainous regions, thriving in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy and low-maintenance nature.
