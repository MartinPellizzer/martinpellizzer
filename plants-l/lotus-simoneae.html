Lotus simoneae

Lotus simoneae, commonly known as the desert lotus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to survive in harsh, drought-prone environments. Its leaves are narrow and waxy, reducing water loss through transpiration.

Lotus simoneae is also valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
