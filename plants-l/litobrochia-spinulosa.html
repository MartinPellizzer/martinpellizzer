Litobrochia spinulosa

Litobrochia spinulosa, commonly known as the spiky rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is found in arid or semi-arid regions. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
