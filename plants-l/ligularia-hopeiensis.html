Ligularia hopeiensis

Ligularia hopeiensis, commonly known as the Hope's ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
