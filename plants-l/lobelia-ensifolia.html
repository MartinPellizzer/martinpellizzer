Lobelia ensifolia

Lobelia ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Lobelia ensifolia is part of the Campanulaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with showy blooms.
