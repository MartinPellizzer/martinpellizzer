Ludwigia linifolia

Ludwigia linifolia, commonly known as slender Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its linear, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red in color. The plant thrives in wetland environments, often growing in shallow water or moist soil. It is commonly found in marshes, ponds, and along the edges of rivers.

Ludwigia linifolia is often used in aquatic gardening and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
