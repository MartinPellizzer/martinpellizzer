Lepanthes frigida

Lepanthes frigida, commonly known as the cold orchid, is a species of orchid native to high-altitude regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant thrives in cool, shaded environments and requires high humidity to flourish. Its leaves are usually broad and slightly fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its native habitat.

Lepanthes frigida is a rare and delicate species, making it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
