Lobelia orbiculata

Lobelia orbiculata, commonly known as the globe lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, tubular, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has rounded, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Lobelia orbiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
