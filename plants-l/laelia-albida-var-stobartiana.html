Laelia albida var. stobartiana

Laelia albida var. stobartiana, commonly known as the Stobart's white laelia, is a hybrid orchid species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its elegant white petals and a pale yellow lip, which contribute to its striking appearance. This plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a touch of beauty to its natural habitat.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
