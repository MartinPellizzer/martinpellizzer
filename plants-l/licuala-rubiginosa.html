Licuala rubiginosa

Licuala rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-veined palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves with prominent red veins, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and as ornamental foliage.

Licuala rubiginosa is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
