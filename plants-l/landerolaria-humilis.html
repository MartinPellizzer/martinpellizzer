Landerolaria humilis

Landerolaria humilis, commonly known as the slender landerolaria, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it well-adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions.
