Lastrea Affinis

Lastrea vine, scientifically known as Lastrea affinis, is a striking climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America. With its long, trailing stems and vibrant, heart-shaped leaves, it adds a dramatic touch to any garden or indoor space. This plant is often favored for its ability to thrive in humid environments and its relatively low maintenance requirements. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and interior designers alike. Whether grown as a houseplant or in a greenhouse, Lastrea vine offers both aesthetic appeal and a sense of natural beauty.
