Ligustrum sinense var. dissimile

Ligustrum sinense var. dissimile, commonly known as the Chinese privet, is a shrub native to China.

It features small, opposite, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by black, spherical berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in a variety of soil conditions.

However, it can be invasive in some regions outside its native habitat.
