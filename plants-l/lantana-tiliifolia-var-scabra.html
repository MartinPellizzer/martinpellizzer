Lantana tiliifolia var. scabra

Lantana tiliifolia var. scabra, commonly known as roughleaf lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant is part of the Verbenaceae family and is known for its adaptability to various soil types. It thrives in full sun and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
