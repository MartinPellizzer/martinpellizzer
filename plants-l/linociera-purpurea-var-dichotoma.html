Linociera purpurea var. dichotoma

Linociera purpurea var. dichotoma, commonly known as the purple cypress, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and reddish-brown bark. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink and purple. Its leaves are narrow, needle-like, and arranged in opposite pairs along the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
