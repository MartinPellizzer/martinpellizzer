Lobelia splendens var. ignea

Lobelia splendens var. ignea, commonly known as the fiery lobelia, is a strikingly colorful plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which bloom in dense clusters throughout the growing season. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and hummingbirds.
