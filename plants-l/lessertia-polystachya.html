Lessertia polystachya

Lessertia polystachya, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in forested areas and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with intricate patterns, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
