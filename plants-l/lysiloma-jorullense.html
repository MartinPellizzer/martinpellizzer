Lysiloma jorullense

Lysiloma jorullense, commonly known as the Jorullense acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and pod fruits. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.
