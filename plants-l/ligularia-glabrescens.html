Ligularia glabrescens

Ligularia glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
