Liparis nervosa subsp. nervosa

Liparis nervosa subsp.

nervosa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.

The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish or yellowish flowers with three petals and a distinctive lip. It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in temperate climates.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
