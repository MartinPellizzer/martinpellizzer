Leucas neumannii

Leucas neumannii, commonly known as the Neumann's leucas, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
