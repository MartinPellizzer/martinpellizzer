Ligustrum lianum

Ligustrum lianum, commonly known as Chinese privet, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or vine that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and usually have a slightly serrated edge.

Ligustrum lianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
