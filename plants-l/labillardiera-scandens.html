Labillardiera scandens

Labillardiera scandens, commonly known as the climbing labillardiera, is a woody vine native to Australia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understoreys.
