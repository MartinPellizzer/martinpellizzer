Lonicera orientalis f. caucasica

Lonicera orientalis f. caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian honeysuckle, is a compact shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in late spring. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It is well-suited for growing in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets.
