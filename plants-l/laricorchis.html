Laricorchis

Laricorchis, commonly known as the pine orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the northern hemisphere, particularly in regions with coniferous forests. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in association with pine trees. Its flowers are usually small and greenish-yellow, with a distinctive structure that facilitates pollination by insects.

Laricorchis species are known for their ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
