Lycaste viridescens

Lycaste viridescens, commonly known as the green lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen pseudobulbs and narrow, dark green leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive greenish tinge, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Lycaste viridescens is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
