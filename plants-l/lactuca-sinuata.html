Lactuca sinuata

Lactuca sinuata, commonly known as the curled leaf lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, ruffled, and curly leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in clusters, and it produces small, smooth seeds.

Lactuca sinuata is cultivated for its edible leaves, which have a mild, slightly bitter taste.
