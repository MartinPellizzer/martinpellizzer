Lobelia purpurea

Lobelia purpurea, commonly known as purple lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Lobelia purpurea is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
