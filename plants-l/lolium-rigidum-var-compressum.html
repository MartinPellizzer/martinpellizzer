Lolium rigidum var. compressum

Lolium rigidum var. compressum, commonly known as rigid ryegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It is often found in disturbed soils and is commonly used in forage and erosion control.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.
