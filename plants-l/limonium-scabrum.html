Limonium scabrum

Limonium scabrum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, showing a high tolerance for salt and wind. Limonium scabrum is a perennial species that produces small, tubular flowers arranged in dense clusters.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and coastal landscaping.
