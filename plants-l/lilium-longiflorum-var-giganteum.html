Lilium longiflorum var. giganteum

Lilium longiflorum var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant Easter lily, is a striking cultivar of the lily family (Liliaceae).

It is characterized by its large, white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters, making it a prominent feature in gardens. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth, glossy surface.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for floral arrangements and garden displays.
