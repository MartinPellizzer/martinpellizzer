Lactuca erythrocarpa

Lactuca erythrocarpa, commonly known as the red-fruited lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its rosette of spoon-shaped, hairy leaves. The plant produces bright red, fleshy fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions.

Lactuca erythrocarpa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for both food and medicinal purposes.
