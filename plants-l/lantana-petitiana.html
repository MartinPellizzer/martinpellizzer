Lantana petitiana

Lantana petitiana, commonly known as the dwarf lantana, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are aromatic when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Lantana petitiana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
