Lotus borkouanus

Lotus borkouanus, commonly known as the Sahel lotus, is a flowering plant native to the Sahel region of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing remarkable drought tolerance. The plant features palmate leaves with a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with a showy appearance that attracts pollinators.

Lotus borkouanus plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
