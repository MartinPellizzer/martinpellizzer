Litsea euosma

Litsea euosma, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers and its distinctive, glossy, elliptical leaves. It produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Litsea euosma is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and showy blooms.
