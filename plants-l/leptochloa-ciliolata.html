Leptochloa ciliolata

Leptochloa ciliolata, commonly known as the silky grass or slender feathergrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, soft, and often hairy leaves that give it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Leptochloa ciliolata is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate foliage and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
