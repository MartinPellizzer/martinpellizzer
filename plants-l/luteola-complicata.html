Luteola complicata

Luteola complicata, commonly known as the twisted yellow orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spiral-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. Luteola complicata is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
