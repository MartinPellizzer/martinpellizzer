Lupinus tricolor var. elegans

Lupinus tricolor var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant lupine, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue and yellow flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant landscapes.

Lupinus tricolor var. elegans is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators and supporting local wildlife.
