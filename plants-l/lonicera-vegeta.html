Lonicera vegeta

Lonicera vegeta, commonly known as the evergreen honeysuckle, is a shrub native to parts of East Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in early summer. Its berries are red and edible, often attracting birds.

Lonicera vegeta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
