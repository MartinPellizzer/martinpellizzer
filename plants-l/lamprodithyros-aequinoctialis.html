Lamprodithyros aequinoctialis

Lamprodithyros aequinoctialis, commonly known as the equinox lily, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that are narrow and linear, adapted to conserve moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure to flourish.
