Lachnaea pomposa

Lachnaea pomposa, commonly known as the showy lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It has a distinctive bright orange to yellow thallus with a velvety texture. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship with algae, which provides photosynthetic capabilities. It is often found on decaying wood or rock surfaces in temperate regions.

Its vibrant color makes it a visually striking component of forest ecosystems.
