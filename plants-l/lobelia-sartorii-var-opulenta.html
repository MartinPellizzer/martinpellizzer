Lobelia sartorii var. opulenta

Lobelia sartorii var. opulenta, commonly known as the showy lobelia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet, often with a contrasting yellow throat. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is valued in native plant gardens for its beauty and ecological benefits to pollinators.
