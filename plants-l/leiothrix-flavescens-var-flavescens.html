Leiothrix flavescens var. flavescens

Leiothrix flavescens var. flavescens, commonly known as the golden leiothrix, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its bright, showy flowers and ease of care.
