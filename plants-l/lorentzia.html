Lorentzia

Lorentzia, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling mist or smoke. The plants are native to the Americas, with species found across North and South America. Lorentzia species typically have hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and they thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

They are often used in gardens for their ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
