Lonicera tatarica f. latifolia

Lonicera tatarica f. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are broad and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in early summer. Its berries are small, red, and edible, often attracting birds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
