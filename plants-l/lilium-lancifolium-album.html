Lilium lancifolium album

Lilium lancifolium album, commonly known as the white lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy white flowers with a pleasant fragrance. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

The white lily is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
