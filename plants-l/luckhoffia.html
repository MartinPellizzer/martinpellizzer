Luckhoffia

Luckhoffia, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features vibrant yellow and orange flowers that resemble flames, hence its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

Luckhoffia is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
