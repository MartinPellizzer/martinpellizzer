Loeseneriella africana var. odorata

Loeseneriella africana var. odorata, commonly known as the fragrant African orchid, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or rocky crevices. Its leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture efficiently.
