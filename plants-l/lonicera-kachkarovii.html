Lonicera kachkarovii

Lonicera kachkarovii, commonly known as the Kachkarov's honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and showy flowers.
