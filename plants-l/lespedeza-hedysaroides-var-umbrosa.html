Lespedeza hedysaroides var. umbrosa

Lespedeza hedysaroides var. umbrosa, commonly known as the shade lespedeza, is a perennial shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often found in woodlands and moist soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in shaded environments.
