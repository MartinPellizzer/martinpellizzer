Lonicera koehneana var. pallescens

Lonicera koehneana var. pallescens, commonly known as the white honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a pale green underside. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.

This plant is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
