Lyonsia albifora

Lyonsia albifora, commonly known as the white-flowered lyonsia, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
