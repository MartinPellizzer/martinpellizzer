Lotus maritimus

Lotus maritimus, commonly known as the sea lotus, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the eastern Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and thrives in saline or brackish environments. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The sea lotus is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils.
