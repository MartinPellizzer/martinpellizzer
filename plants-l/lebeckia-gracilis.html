Lebeckia gracilis

Lebeckia gracilis, commonly known as the slender lebeckia, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling daisies. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and full sun exposure.

Lebeckia gracilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
