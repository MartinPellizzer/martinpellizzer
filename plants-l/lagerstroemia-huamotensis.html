Lagerstroemia huamotensis

Lagerstroemia huamotensis, commonly known as the Huamotense Crape Myrtle, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and lavender during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, turning red in the fall.

The bark is smooth and mottled, adding to its ornamental appeal.
