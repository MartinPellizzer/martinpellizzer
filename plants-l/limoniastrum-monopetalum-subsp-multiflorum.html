Limoniastrum monopetalum subsp. multiflorum

Limoniastrum monopetalum subsp.

multiflorum, commonly known as the multiflowered sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, yellow flowers.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
