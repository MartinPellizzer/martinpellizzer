Litsea steenisii

Litsea steenisii, commonly known as the Java litsea, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy white flowers. Litsea steenisii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
