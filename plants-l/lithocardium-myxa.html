Lithocardium myxa

Lithocardium myxa, commonly known as the sticky lithocardium, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its glandular, sticky leaves and stems. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
