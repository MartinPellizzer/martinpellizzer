Lessingianthus exiguus

Lessingianthus exiguus, commonly known as the slender lessingia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer.
