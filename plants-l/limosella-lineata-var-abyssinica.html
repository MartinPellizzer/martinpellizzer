Limosella lineata var. abyssinica

Limosella lineata var. abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.
