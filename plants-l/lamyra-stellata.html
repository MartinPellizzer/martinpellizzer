Lamyra stellata

Lamyra stellata, commonly known as the star-shaped lamyra, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-like flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Lamyra stellata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
