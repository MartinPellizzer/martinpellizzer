Linum carteri var. smallii

Linum carteri var. smallii, commonly known as the Carter's flax.

It is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly California. This variety is characterized by its slender stems and small, delicate blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

The plant is part of the Linaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
