Lavatera cretica var. genuina

Lavatera cretica var. genuina, commonly known as the Cretan Lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1-3 meters. The plant features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive yellow center. Its leaves are usually hairy and gray-green in appearance, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Lavatera cretica var. genuina is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
