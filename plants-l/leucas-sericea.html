Leucas sericea

Leucas sericea, commonly known as silky leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray, hairy leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Leucas sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
