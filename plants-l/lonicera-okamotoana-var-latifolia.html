Lonicera okamotoana var. latifolia

Lonicera okamotoana var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features slender, arching stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense thickets. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth texture and a glossy green upper surface. It produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun.
