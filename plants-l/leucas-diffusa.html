Leucas diffusa

Leucas diffusa, commonly known as wild thyme, is a flowering plant native to southern and eastern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fragrant leaves and pink or purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like mint and sage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its aromatic properties make it useful in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.
