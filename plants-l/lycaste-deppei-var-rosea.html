Lycaste deppei var. rosea

Lycaste deppei var. rosea, commonly known as the Rosea Lycaste, is a hybrid orchid native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking pink to rose-colored flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It requires bright, indirect light and consistent humidity to thrive.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
