Ligularia dalaolingensis

Ligularia dalaolingensis, commonly known as the Dalaoling ligularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers that appear in late summer, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
