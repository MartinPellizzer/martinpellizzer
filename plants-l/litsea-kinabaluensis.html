Litsea kinabaluensis

Litsea kinabaluensis, commonly known as the Bornean litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of Borneo, particularly in the vicinity of Mount Kinabalu. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, woody drupes that contain a single seed.

Litsea kinabaluensis plays a significant role in the ecological systems of its native habitat, supporting various forms of wildlife and contributing to the biodiversity of the region.
