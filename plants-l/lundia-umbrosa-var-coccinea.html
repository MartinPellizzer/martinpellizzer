Lundia umbrosa var. coccinea

Lundia umbrosa var. coccinea, commonly known as the red-flowered lundia, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its unique coloration makes it a sought-after ornamental species in horticulture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled greenhouse settings.
