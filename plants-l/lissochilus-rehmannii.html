Lissochilus rehmannii

Lissochilus rehmannii, commonly known as the Rehmann's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
