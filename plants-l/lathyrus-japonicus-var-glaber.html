Lathyrus japonicus var. glaber

Lathyrus japonicus var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth Japanese vetch, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial vine that produces clusters of small, pea-like flowers in shades of pink or purple. The plant has smooth, glabrous stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
