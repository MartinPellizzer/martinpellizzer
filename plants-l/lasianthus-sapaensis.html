Lasianthus sapaensis

Lasianthus sapaensis, commonly known as the Sapa beauty, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Vietnam.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to deep purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while its stems are slender and often hairy.

Lasianthus sapaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
