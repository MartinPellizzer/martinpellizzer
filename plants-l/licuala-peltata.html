Licuala peltata

Licuala peltata, commonly known as the broad-leaf palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in diameter. The plant has a short trunk and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil.

Licuala peltata is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
