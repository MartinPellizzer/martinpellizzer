Liparis cuneilabris

Liparis cuneilabris, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often arranged in a loose raceme.

Liparis cuneilabris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
