Lotononis globulosa, commonly known as the globular dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, yellowish flower head that resembles a dandelion, though it is botanically distinct. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in a rounded shape. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
