Lolium perenne var. whitworthiense

Lolium perenne var. whitworthiense, commonly known as the Whitworth's perennial ryegrass, is a variant of perennial ryegrass native to South Africa.

It is a cool-season grass characterized by its dense growth habit and fine, bluish-green foliage. This plant is typically found in grassland ecosystems and is adapted to temperate climates. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in landscaping and forage production.
