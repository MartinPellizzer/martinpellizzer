Lonicera harae var. coreana

Lonicera harae var. coreana, commonly known as the Korean honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the Korean Peninsula.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow, often appearing in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and its role in supporting pollinators.
