Lactuca rosularis

Lactuca rosularis, commonly known as the Rosular lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Compositae).

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, where it grows in dry, open habitats. The plant features a rosette of silver- or gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, forming composite heads that bloom in the summer months.

Lactuca rosularis is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value and has historical uses in herbal medicine.
