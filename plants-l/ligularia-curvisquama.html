Ligularia curvisquama

Ligularia curvisquama, commonly known as the wavy-leaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a wavy margin, often adorned with a distinctive yellow or white border. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or near streams.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and shaded borders in horticulture.
