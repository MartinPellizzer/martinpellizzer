Liparis densiflora

Liparis densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with numerous oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, often with greenish-yellow petals and a distinctive lip.

Liparis densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
