Lewisia × whiteae

Lewisia × whiteae, commonly known as the hybrid Lewisia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Montiaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and showy, cup-shaped flowers. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
