Lampranthus marginatus

Lampranthus marginatus, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of bright orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
