Lonicera purpurascens

Lonicera purpurascens, commonly known as the scarlet honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to the southeastern United States.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange-red, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall and is attractive to birds.

The plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and rapid growth habit.
