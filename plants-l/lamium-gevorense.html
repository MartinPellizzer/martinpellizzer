Lamium gevorense

Lamium gevorense, commonly known as the Dutch deadnettle, is a perennial herb native to Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. It produces small, two-lipped flowers in shades of pink or purple, which attract pollinators like bees.

Lamium gevorense thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
