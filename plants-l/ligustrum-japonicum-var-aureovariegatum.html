Ligustrum japonicum var. aureovariegatum

Ligustrum japonicum var. aureovariegatum, commonly known as the golden variegated privet, is a shrub native to Japan.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellow and white variegation pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, followed by dark blue berries in the fall. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
