Loeflingia pentandra

Loeflingia pentandra, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its showy, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Loeflingia pentandra is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
