Lindernia kinmenensis

Lindernia kinmenensis, commonly known as Kinmen Lindernia, is a species of flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Linderniaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale pink to white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Lindernia kinmenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
