Leersia sinensis

Leersia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually green or brown in color. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants.

Leersia sinensis is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to wet soil conditions.
