Lepanthes micropetala

Lepanthes micropetala, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, greenish-white flowers with intricate patterns on the petals. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, adapted for absorbing moisture from the air.

Lepanthes micropetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
