Lonicera flammea

Lonicera flammea, commonly known as the flame honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its fragrant flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Lonicera flammea is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can grow up to 6 feet tall.
