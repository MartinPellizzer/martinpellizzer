Lonicera syringantha

Lonicera syringantha, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in the spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

Lonicera syringantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
