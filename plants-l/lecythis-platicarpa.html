Lecythis platicarpa

Lecythis platicarpa, commonly known as the flat-leaf lecythis, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The tree produces showy, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by large, woody fruits containing a single, edible seed. The plant is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
