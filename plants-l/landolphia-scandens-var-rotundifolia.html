Landolphia scandens var. rotundifolia

Landolphia scandens var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in the morning and close by midday. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds covered in a sticky, viscous substance.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant floral display.
