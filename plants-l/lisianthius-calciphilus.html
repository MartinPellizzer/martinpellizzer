Lisianthius calciphilus

Lisianthius calciphilus, commonly known as the limestone lisianthius, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It thrives in calcareous soils, showcasing its calciphile nature. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It is often found in rocky or coastal habitats where it withstands harsh environmental conditions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
