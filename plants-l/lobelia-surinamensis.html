Lobelia surinamensis

Lobelia surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam lobelia, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Lobelia surinamensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
