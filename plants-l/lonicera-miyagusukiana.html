Lonicera miyagusukiana

Lonicera miyagusukiana, commonly known as the Japanese honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to East Asia.

It features opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and velvety in texture. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often appearing in pairs. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

However, it is considered an invasive species in many regions due to its aggressive spreading habit.
