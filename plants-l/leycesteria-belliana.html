Leycesteria belliana

Leycesteria belliana, commonly known as the silver leaf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are silver-white underneath, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to cream in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Leycesteria belliana is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
