Lomatopodium karelinianum

Lomatopodium karelinianum, commonly known as the Karelin's lomatopodium, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
