Licuala arbuscula

Licuala arbuscula, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that eventually develop into spiky, fibrous fruits. Its robust and architectural form makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Botanically, it belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms.
