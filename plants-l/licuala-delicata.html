Licuala delicata

Licuala delicata, commonly known as the delicate palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The leaves are typically a deep green with a smooth, waxy texture and are arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor cultivation.
