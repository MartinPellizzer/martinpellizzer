Lasiorrhiza cerberoana

Lasiorrhiza cerberoana, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
