Ligustrum Ciliatum Var. Microphyllum

Ligustrum ciliatum var. microphyllum is a rare and delicate plant native to specific regions of southern China. It is known for its small, glossy leaves and clusters of tiny, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. This variant of the Ligustrum species is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
