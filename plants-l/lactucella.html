Lactucella

Lactucella, commonly known as the lettuce plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically crisp and succulent. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are often yellow or white in color. Lactucella is cultivated for its edible leaves, which are used in various culinary applications.

It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
