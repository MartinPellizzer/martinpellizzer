Lamottea polystachia

Lamottea polystachia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by winged seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist lowland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
