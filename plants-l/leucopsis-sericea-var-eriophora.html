Leucopsis sericea var. eriophora

Leucopsis sericea var. eriophora, commonly known as the silky leucopsis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its soft, silvery foliage and delicate, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, white to pale yellow flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
