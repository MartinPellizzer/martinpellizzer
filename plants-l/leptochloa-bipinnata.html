Leptochloa bipinnata

Leptochloa bipinnata, commonly known as the two-leaf grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces spikelets that are usually arranged in a dense, compact inflorescence. It is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist or wetland habitats.
