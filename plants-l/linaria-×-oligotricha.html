Linaria × oligotricha

Linaria × oligotricha, commonly known as the hybrid snapdragon, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Linaria vulgaris and Linaria maroccana.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces numerous spikes of flowers throughout the growing season. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.

Linaria × oligotricha is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is also used in floral arrangements.
