Lonchocarpus katangensis

Lonchocarpus katangensis, commonly known as Katanga legume, is a tropical shrub native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its distinctive yellow to orange flowers and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. It has been traditionally used in local medicine for its potential health benefits.

However, it contains toxic compounds and requires careful handling.
