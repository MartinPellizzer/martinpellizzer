Lobivia gonjianii

Lobivia gonjianii, commonly known as the Gonji's lobivia, is a compact, succulent cactus native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure and spiny, paddle-shaped leaves that are adapted for water storage. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. Its growth is slow, and it thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

Lobivia gonjianii is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and resilience in dry environments.
