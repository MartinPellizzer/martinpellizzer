Litsea cauliflora

Litsea cauliflora, commonly known as the clustered litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences, which bear small, fragrant white flowers. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface.

Litsea cauliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
