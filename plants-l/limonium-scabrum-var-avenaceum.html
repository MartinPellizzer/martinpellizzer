Limonium scabrum var. avenaceum

Limonium scabrum var. avenaceum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in coastal areas and saline soils. It is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and coastal landscapes.
