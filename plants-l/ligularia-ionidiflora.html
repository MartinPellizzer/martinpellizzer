Ligularia ionidiflora

Ligularia ionidiflora, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a prominent central vein. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in wetlands.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
