Litsea viridis var. clemensii

Litsea viridis var. clemensii, commonly known as the glossy privet, is a variant of the litsea genus native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by spherical, yellowish fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
