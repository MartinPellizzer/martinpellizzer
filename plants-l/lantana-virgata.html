Lantana virgata

Lantana virgata, commonly known as the silver lantana, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, silver-gray leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. It is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in full sun.

Lantana virgata is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
