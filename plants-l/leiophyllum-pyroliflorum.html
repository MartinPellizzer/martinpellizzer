Leiophyllum pyroliflorum

Leiophyllum pyroliflorum, commonly known as the flameflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant.

Its unique floral structure makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
