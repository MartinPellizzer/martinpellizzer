Linum thracicum

Linum thracicum, commonly known as Thrace flax, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces delicate blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Linaceae family, which includes other species of flax. Linum thracicum typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its slender stems and small, showy flowers make it a notable addition to gardens and natural habitats.
