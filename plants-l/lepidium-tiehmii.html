Lepidium tiehmii

Lepidium tiehmii, commonly known as the Sierra cliff mustard, is a small annual plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many mustard plants, and is characterized by its tiny, clustered flowers and narrow, linear leaves.
