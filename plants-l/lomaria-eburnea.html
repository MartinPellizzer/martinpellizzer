Lomaria eburnea

Lomaria eburnea, commonly known as the white lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers. Lomaria eburnea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
