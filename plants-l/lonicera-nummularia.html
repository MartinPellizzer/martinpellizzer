Lonicera nummularia

Lonicera nummularia, commonly known as evergreen honeysuckle, is a low-growing shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, giving it a decorative appearance. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Lonicera nummularia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
