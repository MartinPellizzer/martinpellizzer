Logfia tenuifolia

Logfia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf logfia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a branching stem that supports its delicate foliage.

Logfia tenuifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
