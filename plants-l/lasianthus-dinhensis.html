Lasianthus dinhensis

Lasianthus dinhensis, commonly known as the Dinh's flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
