Licuala campestris

Licuala campestris, commonly known as the broadleaf palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant has a short trunk and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Licuala campestris is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of fibers.
