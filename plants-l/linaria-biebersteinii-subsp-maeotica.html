Linaria biebersteinii subsp. maeotica

Linaria biebersteinii subsp.

maeotica, commonly known as the Crimean toadflax, is a perennial herb native to the Black Sea region. It features slender, branching stems and produces delicate, yellow-orange flowers with two lips, typical of the Scrophulariaceae family.

The plant has opposite, linear leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and is adapted to coastal and steppe environments.

Its ornamental value and ecological significance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
