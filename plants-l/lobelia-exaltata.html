Lobelia exaltata

Lobelia exaltata, commonly known as the tall lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Lobelia exaltata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
