Lampranthus tegens

Lampranthus tegens, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features vibrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its dramatic appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
