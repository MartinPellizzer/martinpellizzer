Litsea greenmaniana

Litsea greenmaniana, commonly known as the greenman's laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It typically grows as a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub.

The fruit is a small, smooth, yellow drupe that matures in late summer.
