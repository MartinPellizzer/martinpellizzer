Lauradia

Lauradia, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Nymphaeaceae.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and yellow. The plant has floating leaves that are broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy surface to repel water. Lauradia thrives in still freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes.

Its cultural significance is profound, symbolizing purity and spiritual awakening in various traditions.
