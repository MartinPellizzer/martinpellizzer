Laelia × adrienne

Laelia × adrienne, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and other orchid species, resulting in a unique combination of traits.

It is a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in varied environmental conditions. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with intricate patterns. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and adaptability.
