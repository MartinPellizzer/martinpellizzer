Lilium longiflorum var. harrisii

Lilium longiflorum var. harrisii, commonly known as the Harris's lily, is a cultivated variety of the Easter lily.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, showy white flowers with a subtle fragrance. The petals are broad and recurved, creating a dramatic appearance. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in floral arrangements.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring moderate watering for optimal growth.
