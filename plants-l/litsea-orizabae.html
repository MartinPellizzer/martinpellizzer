Litsea orizabae

Litsea orizabae, commonly known as the Mexican wild lime, is a small evergreen tree native to Central America and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the citrus family, Rutaceae, and is characterized by its fragrant, narrow leaves and small, yellowish-green fruits.
