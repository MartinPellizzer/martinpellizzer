Lepanthes pelorostele

Lepanthes pelorostele, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a delicate, erect stem that produces a single, showy flower. The flower has a distinctive golden-yellow coloration with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
