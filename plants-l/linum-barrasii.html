Linum barrasii

Linum barrasii, commonly known as the Barras flax, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Linaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant has linear, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsules that contain small, flat seeds. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptation to harsh conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
