Luzula × winderiae

Luzula × winderiae, commonly known as the Winder's wood rush, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense clusters of grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to horticultural collections.
