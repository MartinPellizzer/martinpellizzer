Lythrum salicaria subsp. intermedium

Lythrum salicaria subsp.

intermedium, commonly known as intermediate rosebay, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of vibrant pink to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom from late spring to early summer.

It is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other damp habitats.
