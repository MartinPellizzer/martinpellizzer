Lespedeza liukiuensis

Lespedeza liukiuensis, commonly known as the Ryukyu lespea, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky slopes. It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides nectar for pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
