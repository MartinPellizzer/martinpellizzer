Lamium westphalium

Lamium westphalium, commonly known as purple deadnettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple underside and produces spikes of bright purple flowers. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic foliage. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Lamium westphalium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
