Leucas Lamioides

Leucas lamioides is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, commonly known as the mint family. This species is characterized by its slender stems and small, opposite leaves. It produces tiny, pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. Despite its modest appearance, it holds cultural and medicinal significance in some regions.
