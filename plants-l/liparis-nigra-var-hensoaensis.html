Liparis nigra var. hensoaensis

Liparis nigra var. hensoaensis, commonly known as the black orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black petals and sepals, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as under forest canopies. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
