Litsea chunii var. likiangensis

Litsea chunii var. likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to southwestern China, particularly in Yunnan Province, where it grows in moist, temperate forests. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are drupes, typically containing a single seed, and are often used in traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
