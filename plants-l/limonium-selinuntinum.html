Limonium selinuntinum

Limonium selinuntinum, commonly known as the Sicilian sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its striking blue to purple flower heads. The plant produces small, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color. Limonium selinuntinum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms, which can persist for several weeks during the summer months.
