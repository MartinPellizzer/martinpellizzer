Lobivia chrysantha var. muhriae

Lobivia chrysantha var. muhriae, commonly known as the Muhriae Lobivia, is a compact, succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and produces vibrant yellow flowers during the growing season. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and ability to store water in its leaves and stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
