Linum strictum var. spicatum

Linum strictum var. spicatum, commonly known as spicata linn.

or spike flax, is a flowering plant in the family Linaceae. It is characterized by its erect, slender stem and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant produces slender, cylindrical seed capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds. It is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in textile production.
