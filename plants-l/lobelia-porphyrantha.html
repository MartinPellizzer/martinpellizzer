Lobelia porphyrantha

Lobelia porphyrantha, commonly known as the purple lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple to blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia porphyrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
