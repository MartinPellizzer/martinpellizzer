Leptomischus parviflorus

Leptomischus parviflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered leptomischus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is a perennial herb with slender, often hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
