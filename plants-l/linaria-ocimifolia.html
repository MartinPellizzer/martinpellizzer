Linaria ocimifolia

Linaria ocimifolia, commonly known as blueweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Linaria ocimifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
