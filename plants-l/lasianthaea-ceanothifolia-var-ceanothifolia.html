Lasianthaea ceanothifolia var. ceanothifolia

Lasianthaea ceanothifolia var. ceanothifolia, commonly known as the coastal ceanothus, is a flowering plant native to the western coast of North America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal scrub and dune environments, thriving in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a bluish-green hue with a waxy coating.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
