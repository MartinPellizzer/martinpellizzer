Latrobea hirtella

Latrobea hirtella, commonly known as the hairy latrobea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has hairy leaves and stems, which give it its common name. It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and woodlands.
