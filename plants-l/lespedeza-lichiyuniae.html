Lespedeza lichiyuniae

Lespedeza lichiyuniae, commonly known as the Yunnan lespezea, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
