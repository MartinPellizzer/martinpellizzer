Lecythis ovata var. ovalifolia

Lecythis ovata var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf lecythis, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on the upper surface. The tree produces heavy, woody fruits that contain a single, large, edible seed enclosed in a hard, bony shell. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a yellowish or greenish coloration.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has also been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
