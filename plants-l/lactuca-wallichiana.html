Lactuca wallichiana

Lactuca wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan lettuce, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, alpine environments and has a rosette of spoon-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a head that resembles a dandelion.

Lactuca wallichiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
