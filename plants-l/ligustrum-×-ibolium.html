Ligustrum × ibolium

Ligustrum × ibolium, commonly known as the Chinese privet, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to China.

It features small, dark green leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers in the spring, followed by black, berry-like fruits. It is often used in landscaping for its dense growth and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can become invasive in some regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
