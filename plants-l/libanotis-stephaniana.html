Libanotis stephaniana

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has hairy leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
