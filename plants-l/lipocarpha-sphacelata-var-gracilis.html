Lipocarpha sphacelata var. gracilis

Lipocarpha sphacelata var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender marsh sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences.
