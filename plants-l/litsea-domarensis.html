Litsea domarensis

Litsea domarensis, commonly known as the Domarensis Laurier, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dark berry that contains several seeds.

Litsea domarensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
