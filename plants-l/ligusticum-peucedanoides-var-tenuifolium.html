Ligusticum peucedanoides var. tenuifolium

Ligusticum peucedanoides var. tenuifolium, commonly known as slender-leaved lovage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Asia.

It features aromatic, pinnately compound leaves with slender, elongated leaflets. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
