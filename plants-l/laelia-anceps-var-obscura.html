Laelia anceps var. obscura

Laelia anceps var. obscura, commonly known as the dark-stemmed orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dark green, often almost black, stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, violet to purple flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals and lip. It prefers high altitudes and grows in shaded, humid environments.

This variety is appreciated for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
