Liriope ringens

Liriope ringens, commonly known as the golden lilyturf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and form dense clumps. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in late summer, which are arranged in racemes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Liriope ringens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate partial shade.
