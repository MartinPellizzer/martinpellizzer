Lobivia saltensis var. multicostata

Lobivia saltensis var. multicostata, commonly known as the Multicostate Lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a compact, cylindrical stem with a distinctive ribbed structure that enhances its water storage capacity. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the cooler months, adding ornamental value. Its leaves are typically sparsely distributed and adapted to minimize water loss.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
