Limonium delicatulum

Limonium delicatulum, commonly known as the slender sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, preferring sandy or gravelly soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Limonium delicatulum is often used in garden settings for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in saline conditions.
