Lagenaria leucantha var. makinoi

Lagenaria leucantha var. makinoi, commonly known as the Japanese bottle gourd, is a variant of the bottle gourd species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, cylindrical fruit that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant is a climbing vine with large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. Its fruit develops from the flowers and matures to a smooth, light green or white color.

This variety is often cultivated for its edible fruit, which is used in various culinary traditions.
