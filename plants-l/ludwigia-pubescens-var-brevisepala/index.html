Ludwigia Pubescens Var. Brevisepala

Ludwigia pubescens var. brevisepala is a unique aquatic plant known for its striking red and green foliage. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is popular among aquarium enthusiasts. This variant is distinguished by its short, broad leaves and vibrant coloration. It requires moderate lighting and regular nutrient supplementation to flourish. Its beauty and ease of care make it a favorite in both natural and artificial aquatic settings.
