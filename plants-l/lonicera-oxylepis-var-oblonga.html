Lonicera oxylepis var. oblonga

Lonicera oxylepis var. oblonga, commonly known as the oblong honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features opposite, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in clusters, which are usually yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
