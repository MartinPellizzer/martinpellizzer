Linaria prestandreae

Linaria prestandreae, commonly known as the Cape Flats dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, dandelion-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and sandy soils, often in coastal areas. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Linaria prestandreae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
