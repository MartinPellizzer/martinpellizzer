Limeum aethiopicum

Limeum aethiopicum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Limeum aethiopicum is also valued for its ability to bloom year-round under proper conditions.
