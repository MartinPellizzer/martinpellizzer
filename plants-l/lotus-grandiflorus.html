Lotus grandiflorus

Lotus grandiflorus, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or white flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, while its roots anchor in the mud beneath. Its flowers are known for their fragrance and are often associated with purity and spiritual significance in various cultures.

The lotus is also valued for its edible seeds and ornamental beauty in water gardens.
