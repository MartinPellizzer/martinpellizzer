Leptactina delagoensis

Leptactina delagoensis, commonly known as the Delagoa grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and savanna environments, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers.

Leptactina delagoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
