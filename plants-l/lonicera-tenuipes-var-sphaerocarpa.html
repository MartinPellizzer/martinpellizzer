Lonicera tenuipes var. sphaerocarpa

Lonicera tenuipes var. sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the spherical honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable choice for garden cultivation.
