Lotus purshianus

Lotus purshianus, commonly known as the Chinese lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, often floating on the surface of still water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that provide a striking visual contrast to its blossoms. Its roots are edible and have been used in traditional Chinese cuisine and medicine for centuries.

The lotus is also symbolically significant in various cultures, representing purity and enlightenment.
