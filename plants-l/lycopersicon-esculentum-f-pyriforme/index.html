Lycopersicon Esculentum F. Pyriforme

Lycopersicon esculentum f. pyriforme is a unique variety of tomato known for its distinctive pear-shaped fruits. This decorative form of the common tomato is often cultivated not for its taste but for its ornamental value in gardens. Its elongated, rounded form sets it apart from the typical round tomatoes found in markets. The plant retains the same robust growth habits as its standard counterpart, making it easy to grow for both novice and experienced gardeners. While not suitable for heavy harvesting, it adds a striking visual element to any vegetable patch.
