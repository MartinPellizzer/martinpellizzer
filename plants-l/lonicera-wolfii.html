Lonicera wolfii

Lonicera wolfii, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

Lonicera wolfii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
