Lathyrus henanensis

Lathyrus henanensis, commonly known as Henan vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Lathyrus henanensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in garden settings.
