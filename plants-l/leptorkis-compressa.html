Leptorkis compressa

Leptorkis compressa, commonly known as the slender lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It has a dark green to blackish upper surface and a lighter, often white or pale yellow lower surface. The plant's thallus is loosely branched and features a distinct, flattened structure. It is typically found on decaying wood or bark in temperate forests.

Leptorkis compressa plays a role in nutrient cycling and serves as a habitat for various microorganisms.
