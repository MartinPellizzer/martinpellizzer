Lachenalia liliiflora

Lachenalia liliiflora, commonly known as the Lily-flowered Lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces vibrant, often pink or red blooms during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts and gardeners.
