Lysichlamys

Lysichlamys, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, often silver-gray leaves and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The fruits are typically berries that contain toxic compounds, making them unsuitable for human consumption. Lysichlamys species are native to various regions in Africa, Asia, and the Americas.
