Laserpitium nestleri var. orbiculatum

Laserpitium nestleri var. orbiculatum, commonly known as the Orbiculate Nestler's grass, is a flowering plant native to the high Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in specialty gardens for its unique appearance.
