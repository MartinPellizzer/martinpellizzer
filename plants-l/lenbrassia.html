Lenbrassia

Lenbrassia, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, brightly colored flowers with a unique, intricate pattern resembling flames. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
