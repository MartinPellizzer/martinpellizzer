Lychnis parciflora

Lychnis parciflora, commonly known as the slender campion, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pink to red, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Lychnis parciflora is characterized by its simple, showy inflorescences and is often found in disturbed or nutrient-rich soils.
