Licuala palas

Licuala palas, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a large, tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the Arecaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts and as thatching material.

The plant thrives in humid, coastal environments and requires well-drained soil.
