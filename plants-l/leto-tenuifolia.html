Leto tenuifolia

Leto tenuifolia, commonly known as slender leaf lettuces, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance.

Leto tenuifolia prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
