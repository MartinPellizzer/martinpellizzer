Linaria flavissima

Linaria flavissima, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, tube-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its slender, branching stems are covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped.

Linaria flavissima is often used in dried floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and vibrant color.
