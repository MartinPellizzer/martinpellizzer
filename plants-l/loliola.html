Loliola

Loliola, commonly known as the "flowering rush," is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grasses.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Loliola is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense stands in suitable habitats.
