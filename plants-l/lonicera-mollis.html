Lonicera mollis

Lonicera mollis, commonly known as the soft honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically appearing in late spring. Its red berries are an important food source for birds and wildlife.

Lonicera mollis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
