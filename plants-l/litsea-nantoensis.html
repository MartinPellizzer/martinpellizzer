Litsea nantoensis

Litsea nantoensis, commonly known as the Nantoensis lemon tree, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado plants. The tree is characterized by its fragrant, ovate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its potential use in traditional medicine.
