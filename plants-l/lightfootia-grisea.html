Lightfootia grisea

Lightfootia grisea, commonly known as the gray lightfoot, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in sandy or rocky soils. It is often found in tropical and subtropical climates.

Lightfootia grisea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
