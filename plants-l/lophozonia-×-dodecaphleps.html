Lophozonia × dodecaphleps

Lophozonia × dodecaphleps, commonly known as the hybrid lophozonia, is a unique plant species resulting from the crossbreeding of two distinct lophozonia species.

It exhibits a combination of morphological traits from its parent plants, including a compact growth habit and vibrant foliage. The plant typically produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, adding ornamental value to gardens.
