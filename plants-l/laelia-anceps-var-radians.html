Laelia anceps var. radians

Laelia anceps var. radians, commonly known as the radiant laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It is characterized by its striking, bright pink to red flowers with intricate patterns and a strong, spicy fragrance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
