Limonium bonnetii

Limonium bonnetii, commonly known as the Bonnet's sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Limonium bonnetii has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, showy blooms that attract pollinators.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
