Lonicera xylosteum f. lutea

Lonicera xylosteum f. lutea, commonly known as the yellow honeysuckle, is a variant of the honeysuckle plant characterized by its yellow flowers.

It is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Asia, particularly China. The plant features opposite, simple, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters and emit a sweet fragrance.

The fruit is a small, red drupe that ripens in late summer.
