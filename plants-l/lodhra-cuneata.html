Lodhra cuneata

Lodhra cuneata, commonly known as the Indian privet, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Oleaceae and is characterized by its opposite, simple, ovate to lanceolate leaves. The plant's ornamental value and adaptability make it a common species in horticulture and landscaping.
