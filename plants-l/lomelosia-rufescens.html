Lomelosia rufescens

Lomelosia rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed lomelosia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to red in color, adding visual interest to its foliage. Lomelosia rufescens thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking color contrast between the foliage and floral display.
