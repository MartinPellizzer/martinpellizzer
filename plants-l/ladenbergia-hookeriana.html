Ladenbergia hookeriana

Ladenbergia hookeriana, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant is a perennial herb with a short, erect stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
