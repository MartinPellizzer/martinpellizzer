Linaria heterophylla subsp. aurasiaca

Linaria heterophylla subsp.

aurasiaca, commonly known as the aurasiaca linaria, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Central Asia. It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, varying in shape depending on environmental conditions.
