Ludia leandriana

Ludia leandriana, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, cylindrical flower heads.

Ludia leandriana is often used in ornamental gardening due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
