Leucospermum gerrardii

Leucospermum gerrardii, commonly known as the yellow star flower, is a member of the Proteaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with narrow, needle-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
