Lastrea filix-mas subvar. clarkei

Lastrea filix-mas subvar. clarkei, commonly known as the Clarke's snake plant, is a striking evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow variegation. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it highly suitable for indoor environments. Its foliage is often used in horticultural arrangements due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.

The subvariety clarkei is noted for its unique leaf patterns and robust growth habit.
