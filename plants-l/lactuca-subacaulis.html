Lactuca subacaulis

Lactuca subacaulis, commonly known as the dwarf lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with slender stems and small, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a single head. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Lactuca subacaulis is a perennial herb that can be identified by its milky sap and delicate appearance.
