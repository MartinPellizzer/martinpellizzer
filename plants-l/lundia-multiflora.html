Lundia multiflora

Lundia multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered lundia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and opposite leaves. Its flowers are often brightly colored, ranging from pink to purple, attracting various pollinators.
