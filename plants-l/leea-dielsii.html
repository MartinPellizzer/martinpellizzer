Leea dielsii

Leea dielsii, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a member of the Leeaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a small, deciduous shrub that typically grows up to 2 meters in height. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in axillary inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds, and the plant is often found in moist, wooded areas.
