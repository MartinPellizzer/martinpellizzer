Lepanthes zongoensis

Lepanthes zongoensis, commonly known as the Zongo orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and decaying organic matter.

Lepanthes zongoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
