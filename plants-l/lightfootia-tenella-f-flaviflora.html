Lightfootia tenella f. flaviflora

Lightfootia tenella f. flaviflora, commonly known as the golden-flowered lightfootia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months, which add a striking contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
