Leersia oryzoides var. purpurascens

Leersia oryzoides var. purpurascens, commonly known as purple leersia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, erect stems that often produce panicles of small, delicate florets. The plant is characterized by its striking purple tinge in the inflorescence, which becomes more pronounced as it matures. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This ornamental grass is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
