Lasianthus strigosus var. nitidus

Lasianthus strigosus var. nitidus, commonly known as the glossy lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and covered with a smooth, glossy surface. This variant is distinguished by its more compact growth habit and the presence of a waxy coating on its foliage.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
