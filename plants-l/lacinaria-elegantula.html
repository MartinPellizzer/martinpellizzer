Lacinaria elegantula

Lacinaria elegantula, commonly known as the elegant lace fern, is a delicate and ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It features feathery, finely divided leaves that create a lacy appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its growth habit is compact, allowing it to be suitable for small spaces or hanging baskets.

Lacinaria elegantula is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
