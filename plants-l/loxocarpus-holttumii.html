Loxocarpus holttumii

Loxocarpus holttumii, commonly known as the Java flame tree, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and Malaysia, and thrives in tropical climates. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy display.

The seeds are enclosed in pod-like fruits, which contribute to its ecological and botanical significance.
