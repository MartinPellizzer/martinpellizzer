Lilium Canadense F. Uniflorum

Canada lily, scientifically known as Lilium canadense f. uniflorum, is a striking member of the lily family native to North America. This unique cultivar is distinguished by its single, showy bloom that stands out against its foliage. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas across the eastern United States. The plant's elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and ecological value. Its delicate petals and vibrant colors add a touch of natural sophistication to any landscape.
