Lobeira

Lobeira, commonly known as the Brazilian morning glory, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom in the early morning. Lobeira thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its rapid growth habit makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and naturalistic landscapes.
