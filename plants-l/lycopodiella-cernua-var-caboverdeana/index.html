Lycopodiella Cernua Var. Caboverdeana

Lycopodiella cernua var. caboverdeana is a rare and unique species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae. It is native to the island of Cabo Verde and thrives in humid, shaded environments. This variant is distinguished by its low-growing habit and delicate, spirally arranged leaves. Its reproductive structures are particularly intricate, showcasing the plant's evolutionary adaptations. Despite its small size, it plays a vital role in its ecosystem and is of great interest to botanists and conservationists.
