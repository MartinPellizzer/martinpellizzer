Laserpitium pilosum

Laserpitium pilosum, commonly known as the silky goat's beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically hairy and lance-shaped, contributing to its common name.

Laserpitium pilosum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, open habitats.
