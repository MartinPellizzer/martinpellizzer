Lagenandra peradeniyae var. peradeniyae

Lagenandra peradeniyae var. peradeniyae, commonly known as the Sri Lanka orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the island of Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in the summer months. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical forests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
