Liparis plicata

Liparis plicata, commonly known as the pleated orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique pleated leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often arranged in a spike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Liparis plicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
