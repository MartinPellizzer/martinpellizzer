Leptopoda denticulata

Leptopoda denticulata, commonly known as the toothed leathery fern, is a species of fern characterized by its unique, leathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid, tropical woodlands.
