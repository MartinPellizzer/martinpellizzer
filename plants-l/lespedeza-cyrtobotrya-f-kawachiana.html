Lespedeza cyrtobotrya f. kawachiana

Lespedeza cyrtobotrya f. kawachiana, commonly known as the Kawachiana Lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod-bearing fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. It produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, and its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and ability to improve soil quality.
