Lespedeza japonica f. angustifolia

Lespedeza japonica f. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf Chinese clover, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features slender, upright stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three oval to lance-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for erosion control and ornamental use.

Its delicate appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice in native plant gardens.
