Liparis prianganensis

Liparis prianganensis, commonly known as the Priangan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance.

Botanically, it has a complex root system and relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient absorption.
