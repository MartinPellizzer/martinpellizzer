Liparis retusa

Liparis retusa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a raceme, often with a slight fragrance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Liparis retusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
