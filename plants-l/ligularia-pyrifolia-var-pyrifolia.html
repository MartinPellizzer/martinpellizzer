Ligularia pyrifolia var. pyrifolia

Ligularia pyrifolia var. pyrifolia, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed and have a distinctive silvery-white underside. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland areas.

Its foliage is popular in ornamental gardening for its striking visual appeal.
