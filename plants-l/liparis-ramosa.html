Liparis ramosa

Liparis ramosa, commonly known as the ramose orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often fragrant. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Liparis ramosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
