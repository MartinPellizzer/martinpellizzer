Lepanthes urania

Lepanthes urania, commonly known as the Urania Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are often adorned with intricate patterns and have a distinctive, spurred shape.

Lepanthes urania is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and unique botanical features.
