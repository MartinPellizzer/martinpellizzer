Lemeltonia

Lemeltonia, commonly known as the silver leaf fern, is a striking plant characterized by its delicate, arching fronds that shimmer with a silvery sheen.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense mats or clusters over time. Lemeltonia requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil to flourish, making it a rewarding plant for those who can provide the right conditions.
