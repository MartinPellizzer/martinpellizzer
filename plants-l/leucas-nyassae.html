Leucas nyassae

Leucas nyassae, commonly known as African bushveld daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Leucas nyassae is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
