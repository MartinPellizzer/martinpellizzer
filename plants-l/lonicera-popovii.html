Lonicera popovii

Lonicera popovii, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its berries are red and edible, often consumed by birds and small mammals.

Lonicera popovii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
