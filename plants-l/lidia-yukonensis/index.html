Lidia Yukonensis

Lidia yukonensis is a rare flowering plant native to the Yukon region of Canada. It thrives in cold, alpine environments and is known for its delicate, pinkish-white blooms. This species is highly sensitive to environmental changes, making it a valuable indicator of climate shifts. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Lidia yukonensis faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and endangered plant.
