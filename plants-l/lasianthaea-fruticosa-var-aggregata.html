Lasianthaea fruticosa var. aggregata

Lasianthaea fruticosa var. aggregata, commonly known as the clustered lasianthaea, is a shrub native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, clustered inflorescences with showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a subject of interest in both horticultural and ecological studies.
