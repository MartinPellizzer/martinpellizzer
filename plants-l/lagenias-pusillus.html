Lagenias pusillus

Lagenias pusillus, commonly known as the dwarf yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it prefers well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
