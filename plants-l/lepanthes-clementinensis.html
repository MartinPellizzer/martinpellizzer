Lepanthes clementinensis

Lepanthes clementinensis, commonly known as the Clementine Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in cloud forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown.

Lepanthes clementinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
