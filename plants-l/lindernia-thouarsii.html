Lindernia thouarsii

Lindernia thouarsii, commonly known as the Madagascar lindernia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Linderniaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

Lindernia thouarsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
