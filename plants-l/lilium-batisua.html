Lilium batisua

Lilium batisua, commonly known as the Batisua lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

The Batisua lily is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
