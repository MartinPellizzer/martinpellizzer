Ligustrum kumaonense

Ligustrum kumaonense, commonly known as the Kumaon privet, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy dark green surface.

Ligustrum kumaonense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its hardy nature and adaptability.
