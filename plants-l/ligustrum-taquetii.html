Ligustrum taquetii

Ligustrum taquetii, commonly known as the Taquet's privet, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by black, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Ligustrum taquetii is also valued for its use in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
