Landolphia owariensis var. leiocalyx

Landolphia owariensis var. leiocalyx, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds covered in a sticky, fleshy aril.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
