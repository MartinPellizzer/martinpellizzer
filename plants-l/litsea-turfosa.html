Litsea turfosa

Litsea turfosa, commonly known as the dwarf Chinese evergreen, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, glossy, dark green foliage that is highly ornamental and often used in horticultural landscapes. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries, which are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for container gardening.

Its foliage remains lush year-round, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor ornamental purposes.
