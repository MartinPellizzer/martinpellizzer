Laelia domingensis

Laelia domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of the Dominican Republic.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments with well-drained soil. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores water, allowing it to thrive in its native habitat. Its flowers are usually fragrant and come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

Laelia domingensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
