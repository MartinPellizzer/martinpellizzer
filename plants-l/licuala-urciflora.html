Licuala urciflora

Licuala urciflora, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in branched inflorescences. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.
