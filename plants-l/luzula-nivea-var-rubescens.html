Luzula nivea var. rubescens

Luzula nivea var. rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered goat's beard, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and dense clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers that often turn reddish at the base. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-gray hue that gives the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
