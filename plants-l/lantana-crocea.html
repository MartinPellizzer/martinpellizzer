Lantana crocea

Lantana crocea, commonly known as the yellow lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, typically yellow or orange, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Lantana crocea is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, depending on the climate.

It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
