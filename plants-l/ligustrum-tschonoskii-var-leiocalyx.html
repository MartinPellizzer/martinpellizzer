Ligustrum tschonoskii var. leiocalyx

Ligustrum tschonoskii var. leiocalyx, commonly known as the Chinese privet, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval in shape. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not managed properly.
