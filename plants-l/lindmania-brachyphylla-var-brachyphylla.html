Lindmania brachyphylla var. brachyphylla

Lindmania brachyphylla var. brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-stemmed arrowroot, is a species native to the wet tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Cannaceae family, characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, broad leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.

This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
