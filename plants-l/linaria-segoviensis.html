Linaria segoviensis

Linaria segoviensis, commonly known as the Segovia toadflax, is a flowering plant native to Spain.

It belongs to the genus Linaria in the family Plantaginaceae. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and bright yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in open habitats.

Linaria segoviensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
