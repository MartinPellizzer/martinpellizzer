Lepanthes sibundoyensis

Lepanthes sibundoyensis, commonly known as the Sibundoy orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale green with intricate patterns. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and specialized root system.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
