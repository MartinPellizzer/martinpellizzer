Limonium gummiferum

Limonium gummiferum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has silver-gray foliage that helps it withstand salty air and coastal winds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and rocky shores.

Limonium gummiferum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems.
