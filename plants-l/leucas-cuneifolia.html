Leucas cuneifolia

Leucas cuneifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved leucas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun.

Leucas cuneifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
