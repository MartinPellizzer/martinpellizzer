Lespedeza merrillii

Lespedeza merrillii, commonly known as the Chinese lespedeza, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas. Lespedeza merrillii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

It is also used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and adaptability.
