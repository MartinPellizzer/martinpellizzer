Lycopodium Chamaeleon

Lycopodium chamaeleon is a unique member of the clubmoss family known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. This small, evergreen plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in temperate regions. Its spore-bearing cones and primitive structure offer insights into early plant evolution. Despite its modest size, it plays a vital role in forest ecosystems. Studying Lycopodium chamaeleon helps scientists understand ancient plant life and its adaptation strategies.
