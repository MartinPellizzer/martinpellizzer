Lathyrus odoratus var. vulgaris

Lathyrus odoratus var. vulgaris, commonly known as sweet pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, slender stems, and clusters of fragrant, colorful flowers that range from white to deep red. The leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are typically used for propagation.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
