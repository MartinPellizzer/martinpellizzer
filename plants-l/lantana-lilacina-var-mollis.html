Lantana lilacina var. mollis

Lantana lilacina var. mollis, commonly known as silky lantana, is a shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and lavender, often with a slight bluish tinge. The leaves are typically hairy and felt-like, giving the plant its "silky" appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This variety is known for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
