Licuala yiiana

Licuala yiiana, commonly known as the golden palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its leaves are smooth and have a golden-green hue, making it visually striking.

Licuala yiiana is primarily valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
