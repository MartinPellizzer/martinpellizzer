Leucopogon melaleucoides

Leucopogon melaleucoides, commonly known as the silver-leafed heath, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. It produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months.
