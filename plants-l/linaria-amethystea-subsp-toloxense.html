Linaria amethystea subsp. toloxense

Linaria amethystea subsp.

toloxense, commonly known as the Tolexense Snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and borders due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
