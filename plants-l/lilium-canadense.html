Lilium canadense

Lilium canadense, commonly known as the Canadian tiger lily, is a species of flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in open woods or meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

The Canadian tiger lily is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
