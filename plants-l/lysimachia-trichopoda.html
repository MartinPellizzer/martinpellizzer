Lysimachia trichopoda

Lysimachia trichopoda, commonly known as hairy yellow-eyed grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features slender, creeping stems covered in fine hairs, giving it its common name. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in whorls along the stems and bloom in early spring.

Lysimachia trichopoda is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
