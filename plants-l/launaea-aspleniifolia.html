Launaea aspleniifolia

Launaea aspleniifolia, commonly known as the silver feather plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers arranged in composite heads. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.

Launaea aspleniifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and drought tolerance.
