Lagerstroemia javanica

Lagerstroemia javanica, commonly known as the Javan flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that bloom in summer. The plant features smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves that turn red in the fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its flowers are often pink to red, adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
