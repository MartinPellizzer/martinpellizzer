Lepanthes rotundata

Lepanthes rotundata, commonly known as the rounded slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and is often found in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Lepanthes rotundata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
