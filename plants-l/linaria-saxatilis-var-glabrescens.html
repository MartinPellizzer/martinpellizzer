Linaria saxatilis var. glabrescens

Linaria saxatilis var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth linaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal and dry garden environments.
