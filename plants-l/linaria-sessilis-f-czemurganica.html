Linaria sessilis f. czemurganica

Linaria sessilis f. czemurganica, commonly known as the Czermurganica linaria, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow to orange, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
