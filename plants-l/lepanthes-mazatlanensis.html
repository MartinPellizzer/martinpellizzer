Lepanthes mazatlanensis

Lepanthes mazatlanensis, commonly known as the Mazatlan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as cloud forests. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, while its flowers are small and clustered.

Lepanthes mazatlanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
