Ledebouria sandersonii

Ledebouria sandersonii, commonly known as the Sanderson's bulbous lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous base and strap-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.
