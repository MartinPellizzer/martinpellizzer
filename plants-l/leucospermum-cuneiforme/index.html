Leucospermum Cuneiforme

Leucospermum cuneiforme, commonly known as the firewheel plant, is a striking member of the Proteaceae family native to South Africa. This vibrant plant is celebrated for its fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a stunning visual display. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. The plant's resilience and unique appearance have made it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. With its ability to withstand drought and coastal conditions, it is a versatile and eye-catching addition to any garden.
