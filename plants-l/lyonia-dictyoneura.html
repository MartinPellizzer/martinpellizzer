Lyonia dictyoneura

Lyonia dictyoneura, commonly known as the mountain lyonia, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and arranged in a compact, dense growth habit.

Lyonia dictyoneura thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded forest environments.
