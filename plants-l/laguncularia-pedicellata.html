Laguncularia pedicellata

It is a deciduous tree that thrives in coastal areas, particularly in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its aerial roots, which help it anchor in soft, waterlogged soils. Its small, elliptical leaves are typically opposite and have a glossy green appearance.
