Lonicera limprichtii

Lonicera limprichtii, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
