Linanthus aureus var. decora

Linanthus aureus var. decora, commonly known as the showy linanthus, is a striking annual plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs to reduce water loss.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
