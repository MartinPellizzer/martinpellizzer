Liabum rosulatum

Liabum rosulatum, commonly known as the roseleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has linear, gray-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
