Litsea shweliensis

Litsea shweliensis, commonly known as the Shweli litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Myanmar and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, smooth drupe that turns dark when mature.

Litsea shweliensis is often valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
