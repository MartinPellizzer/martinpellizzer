Lepanthes fuertesii

Lepanthes fuertesii, commonly known as the Fuertes's slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped pouch that aids in pollination. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its flowers are usually small and come in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Lepanthes fuertesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its habitat.
