Leptospermum Pubescenes Var. Grandifolium

Leptospermum pubescens var. grandifolium is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southeastern Australia. Known for its vibrant red flowers and dense, aromatic foliage, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and hardiness. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of climates. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits have made it a favorite in both ornamental and native landscaping. With its resilience and visual appeal, it stands out as a valuable addition to any garden.
