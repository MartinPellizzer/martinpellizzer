Ledenbergia seguierioides

Ledenbergia seguierioides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its foliage is often velvety and varies in color from deep green to silver.

Ledenbergia seguierioides is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
