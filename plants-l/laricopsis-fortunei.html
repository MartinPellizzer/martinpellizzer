Laricopsis fortunei

Laricopsis fortunei, commonly known as the Chinese fir, is a coniferous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Pinaceae and is characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 30 meters.
