Ligularia amplexicaulis var. amplexicaulis

Ligularia amplexicaulis var. amplexicaulis, commonly known as the broadleaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, broad leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are surrounded by broad, spade-shaped bracts. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland areas.

Its foliage is popular in ornamental gardening for its lush, dramatic appearance.
