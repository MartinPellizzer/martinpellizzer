Leucorchis × vitosana

Leucorchis × vitosana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Leucorchis alata and Leucorchis vitosa.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically broad and elliptical, and it produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
