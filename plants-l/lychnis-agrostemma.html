Lychnis agrostemma

Lychnis agrostemma, commonly known as the field sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems covered with small, opposite leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are pink to reddish in color, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.
