Lythrum thymifolia var. diffusa

Lythrum thymifolia var. diffusa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved verbena, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
