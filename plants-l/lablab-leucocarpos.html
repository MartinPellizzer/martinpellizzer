Lablab leucocarpos

Lablab leucocarpos, commonly known as the white lablab, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. Lablab leucocarpos is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it a valuable plant for soil enrichment.

It is also cultivated for its nutritional seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various traditional dishes.
