Lepidium smithii

Lepidium smithii, commonly known as Smith's cress, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and has small, rounded leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are white or pale purple and are arranged in clusters, often blooming in late spring to early summer.
