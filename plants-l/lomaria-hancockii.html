Lomaria hancockii

Lomaria hancockii, commonly known as Hancock's lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the spring and early summer.

Lomaria hancockii is often found in chaparral and desert scrub habitats.
