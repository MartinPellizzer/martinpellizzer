Lespedeza penduliflora var. albiflora

Lespedeza penduliflora var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered lespedeza, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
