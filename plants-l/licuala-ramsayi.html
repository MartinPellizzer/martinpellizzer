Licuala ramsayi

Licuala ramsayi, commonly known as the Ramayani palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The fruit of Licuala ramsayi is a small, yellowish drupe that contains a single seed.
