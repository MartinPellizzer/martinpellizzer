Lapeirousia divaricata var. tenuis

Lapeirousia divaricata var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed marsh daffodil, is a striking South African bulbous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular subject for botanists and gardeners interested in native flora.
