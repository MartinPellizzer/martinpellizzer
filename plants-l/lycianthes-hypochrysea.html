Lycianthes hypochrysea

Lycianthes hypochrysea, commonly known as the yellow bell pepper, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous annual plant that belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae. The plant produces yellow, bell-shaped fruits that mature to a bright orange-red color. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy surface, and it typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters.
