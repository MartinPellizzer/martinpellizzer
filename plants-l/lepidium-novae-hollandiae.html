Lepidium novae-hollandiae

Lepidium novae-hollandiae, commonly known as the Australian cress, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils. The plant features slender stems and small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tiny, white or yellowish, and arranged in clusters, producing small, edible seeds.

It is part of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants.
