Lingnania cerosissima

Lingnania cerosissima, commonly known as the waxleaf lingnania, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
