Lomariopsis requiniana

Lomariopsis requiniana, commonly known as the reef fern, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. Its rhizome is creeping and covered with a dense layer of rhizomorphs, which help in anchoring the plant and absorbing nutrients.

Lomariopsis requiniana is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
