Lonicera tenuipes var. chichibuana

Lonicera tenuipes var. chichibuana, commonly known as the Chichibu honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often used as ornamental features. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its unique appearance and seasonal bloom make it a distinctive addition to botanical collections.
