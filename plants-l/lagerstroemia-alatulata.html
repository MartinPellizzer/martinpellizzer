Lagerstroemia alatulata

Lagerstroemia alatulata, commonly known as the Chinese flowering maple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, mottled bark and clusters of showy, panicle flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that split open to release seeds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Lagerstroemia alatulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping for its vibrant seasonal display.
