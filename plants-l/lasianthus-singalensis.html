Lasianthus singalensis

Lasianthus singalensis, commonly known as the Singalense lasianthus, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically display vibrant colors such as red, orange, or yellow. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant in some parts of the world.
