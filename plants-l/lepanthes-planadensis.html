Lepanthes planadensis

Lepanthes planadensis, commonly known as the Planadense Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Lepanthes planadensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
