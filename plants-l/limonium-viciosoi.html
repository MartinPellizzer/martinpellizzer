Limonium viciosoi

Limonium viciosoi, commonly known as the Sicilian sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in saline environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette.

Limonium viciosoi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and coastal landscapes.
