Litsea nigricans

Litsea nigricans, commonly known as black litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a dark purple drupe, which contains a single seed.
