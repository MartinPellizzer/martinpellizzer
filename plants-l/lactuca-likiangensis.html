Lactuca likiangensis

Lactuca likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan lettuce, is a species of lettuce native to China.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically pale green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a head, which are followed by spherical, dry fruits containing seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

This species is valued for its edible leaves and is used in traditional Chinese cuisine.
