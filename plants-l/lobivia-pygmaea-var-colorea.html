Lobivia pygmaea var. colorea

Lobivia pygmaea var. colorea, commonly known as the Rainbow Lobivia, is a compact cactus native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a greenish-gray color and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple. The plant has spiny areoles and is adapted to thrive in arid, high-altitude environments. It is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Due to its specific habitat needs, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
