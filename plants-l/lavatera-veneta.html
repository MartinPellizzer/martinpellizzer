Lavatera veneta

Lavatera veneta, commonly known as the Italian woolly mallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has soft, hairy leaves and produces fluffy, cotton-like seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Lavatera veneta is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
