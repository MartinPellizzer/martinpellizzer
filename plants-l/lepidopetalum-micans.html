Lepidopetalum micans

Lepidopetalum micans, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-green leaves that shimmer in the light, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.

Lepidopetalum micans is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
