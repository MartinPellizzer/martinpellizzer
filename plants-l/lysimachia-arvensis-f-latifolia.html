Lysimachia arvensis f. latifolia

Lysimachia arvensis f. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf yellow sorrel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
