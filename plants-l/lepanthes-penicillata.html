Lepanthes penicillata

Lepanthes penicillata, commonly known as the penicillate orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually small and come in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Lepanthes penicillata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
