Ladenbergia schomburgkii

Ladenbergia schomburgkii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange floral display during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and furniture.
