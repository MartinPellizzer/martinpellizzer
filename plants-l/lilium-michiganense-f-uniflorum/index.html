Lilium Michiganense F. Uniflorum

Michigan lily, scientifically known as Lilium michiganense f. uniflorum, is a rare and elegant flowering plant native to the eastern United States. This unique cultivar is characterized by its single bloom per stem, distinguishing it from other lily varieties. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas and along stream banks. The plant features striking white to pale pink petals with a subtle fragrance, making it a prized addition to gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure, highlighting the importance of preserving such botanical treasures.
