Lyonia Compta

Lyonia compta is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky streambanks. Known for its small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, it adds a subtle beauty to its natural habitat. This plant is often overlooked due to its modest size and quiet presence. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this species from habitat loss and environmental degradation.
