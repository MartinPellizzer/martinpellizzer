Lastreopsis davallioides

Lastreopsis davallioides, commonly known as the rock fern, is a perennial fern native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, creeping rhizomes and small, clustered fronds. The leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive, narrow shape. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
