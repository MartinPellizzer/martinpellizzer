Laelia × devoniensis

Laelia × devoniensis, commonly known as the Devonensis orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its native high-altitude environment. It prefers cool, bright conditions with moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Laelia × devoniensis is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
