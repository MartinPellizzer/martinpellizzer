Lonchocarpus sylvestris

Lonchocarpus sylvestris, commonly known as the red root or sylvestris legume, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive red-colored roots. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, tropical forests and can grow up to several meters in height.

Due to its toxicity, it is important to handle the plant with care.
