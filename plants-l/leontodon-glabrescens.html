Leontodon glabrescens

Leontodon glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth leontodon, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Leontodon glabrescens is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a characteristic species of certain grassland ecosystems.
