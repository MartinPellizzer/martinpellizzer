Lastrea zeylanica

Lastrea zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon spurge, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that is toxic and can cause skin irritation. It typically grows in moist, shaded areas and is often found in forest understories.
