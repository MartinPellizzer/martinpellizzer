Lantana pretoriensis

Lantana pretoriensis, commonly known as the Pretoria lantana, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow, orange, or red. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are aromatic when crushed. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Lantana pretoriensis is also known for its ability to attract birds and insects, contributing to local biodiversity.
