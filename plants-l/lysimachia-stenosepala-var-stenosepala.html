Lysimachia stenosepala var. stenosepala

Lysimachia stenosepala var. stenosepala, commonly known as the narrow-sepaled yellow loosestrife, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features creeping rhizomes and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wetland areas.
