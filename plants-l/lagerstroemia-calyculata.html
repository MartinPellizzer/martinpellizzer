Lagerstroemia calyculata

Lagerstroemia calyculata, commonly known as the silky willow or red willow, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, silky-haired leaves that turn red in the autumn. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.

Lagerstroemia calyculata is also valued for its adaptability to different climate conditions.
