Laelia × meavei

Laelia × meavei, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Laelia purpurata and Laelia anceps.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is native to high-altitude regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Laelia × meavei is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
