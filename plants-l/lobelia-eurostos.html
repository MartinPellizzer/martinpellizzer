Lobelia eurostos

Lobelia eurostos, commonly known as European lobelia, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple, arranged in whorled clusters along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
