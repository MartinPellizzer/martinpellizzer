Lomelosia crenata subsp. crenata

Lomelosia crenata subsp.

crenata, commonly known as the crested lomelosia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America. It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
