Lemaireocereus stellatus

Lemaireocereus stellatus, commonly known as the star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a low-growing, columnar cactus with a distinctive appearance featuring white to pale yellow spines and a ribbed structure. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the evening, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Lemaireocereus stellatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
