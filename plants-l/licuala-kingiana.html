Licuala kingiana

Licuala kingiana, commonly known as the giant pandan, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to three meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts and for making natural dyes.

Botanically, it belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms.
