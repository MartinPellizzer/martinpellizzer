Lobivia formosa

Lobivia formosa, commonly known as the Mexican beauty, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features compact, rounded stems with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Lobivia formosa is prized for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh conditions.
