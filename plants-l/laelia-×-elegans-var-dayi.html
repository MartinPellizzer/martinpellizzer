Laelia × elegans var. dayi

Laelia × elegans var. dayi, commonly known as the Dayi hybrid orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often intensely colored flowers that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and segmented, supporting its growth habit.

The flowers are borne on lateral spikes and are known for their strong, sweet fragrance.
