Lamprotis loddigesii

Lamprotis loddigesii, commonly known as the golden trumpet, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers.

Native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its flowering season typically spans from spring to early summer, making it a popular choice for those seeking bold, tropical flair in their landscapes.
