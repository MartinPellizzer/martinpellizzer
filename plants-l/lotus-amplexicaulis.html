Lotus amplexicaulis

Lotus amplexicaulis, commonly known as the clasping lotus, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive leaves that wrap around the stems, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
