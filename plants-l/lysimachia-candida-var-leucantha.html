Lysimachia candida var. leucantha

Lysimachia candida var. leucantha, commonly known as white starwort, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has opposite, rounded to ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
