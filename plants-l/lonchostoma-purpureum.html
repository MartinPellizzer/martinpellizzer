Lonchostoma purpureum

Lonchostoma purpureum, commonly known as the purple leaf tamarisk, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found growing along coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
