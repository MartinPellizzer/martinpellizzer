Lomaria gracilis

Lomaria gracilis, commonly known as the slender lomaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to orange. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, where it forms dense, low-growing mats.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes.
