Ligularia fangiana

Ligularia fangiana, commonly known as the Chinese heartleaf, is a striking perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white margin and a dark green center. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a dramatic touch to gardens. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in water gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts.
