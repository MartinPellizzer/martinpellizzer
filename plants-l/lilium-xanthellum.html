Lilium xanthellum

Lilium xanthellum, commonly known as the yellow lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.

Lilium xanthellum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
