Lasiochlamys fasciculata

Lasiochlamys fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
