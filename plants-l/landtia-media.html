Landtia media

Landtia media, commonly known as the common landtia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Landtia media is known for its hardiness and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
