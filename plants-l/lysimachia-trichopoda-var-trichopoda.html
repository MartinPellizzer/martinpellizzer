Lysimachia trichopoda var. trichopoda

Lysimachia trichopoda var. trichopoda, commonly known as golden thread, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of Asia.

It features slender, prostrate stems that are often covered with fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers clustered in terminal racemes, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and often have a slightly wavy margin.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or along stream banks.
