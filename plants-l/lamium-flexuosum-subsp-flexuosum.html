Lamium flexuosum subsp. flexuosum

Lamium flexuosum subsp.

flexuosum, commonly known as purple deadnettle, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple underside and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple.

The plant thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are pollinated by insects, and it spreads through both seeds and rhizomes.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
