Lonicera brandtii

Lonicera brandtii, commonly known as Brandt's honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that matures in late summer.

Lonicera brandtii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
