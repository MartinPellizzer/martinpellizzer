Luzula sylvatica var. dertosensis

Luzula sylvatica var. dertosensis, commonly known as the Dertos sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, rushes, and grass-like plants. This variety is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and cylindrical, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, three-lobed flowers. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting biodiversity.
