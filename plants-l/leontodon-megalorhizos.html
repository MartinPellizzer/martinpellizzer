Leontodon megalorhizos

Leontodon megalorhizos, commonly known as the giant-rooted toothbrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

The plant produces yellow flower heads that are surrounded by pale green ray florets, typical of the genus Leontodon.
