Lagotis glauca var. kunawurensis

Lagotis glauca var. kunawurensis, commonly known as the Kunawurensis Lagotis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

The plant typically grows as an understory shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, terminal spikes and are pollinated primarily by insects, particularly bees and wasps.
