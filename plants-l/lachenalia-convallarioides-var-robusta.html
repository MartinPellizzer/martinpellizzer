Lachenalia convallarioides var. robusta

Lachenalia convallarioides var. robusta, commonly known as the robust lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, linear leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water during the dormant season.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid environments.
