Lindernia delicatula

Lindernia delicatula, commonly known as the slender lindernia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually pink or purple, arranged in dense spikes.

Lindernia delicatula is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.
