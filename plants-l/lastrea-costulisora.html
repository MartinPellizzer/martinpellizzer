Lastrea costulisora

Lastrea costulisora, commonly known as the wrinkled lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and may have a slightly wavy or wrinkled texture.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
