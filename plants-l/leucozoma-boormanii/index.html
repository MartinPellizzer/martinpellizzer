Leucozoma Boormanii

Leucozoma boormanii is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its unique white and green foliage, it stands out among other flora due to its unusual coloration. This plant thrives in cool, shaded environments and is often found in humid, forested areas. Its delicate appearance makes it a sought-after specimen among botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, Leucozoma boormanii remains poorly understood and is increasingly threatened by habitat loss.
