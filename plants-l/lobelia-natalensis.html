Lobelia natalensis

Lobelia natalensis, commonly known as Natal lobelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and riverbanks. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.
