Lotononis densa subsp. gracilis

Lotononis densa subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as the slender lotononis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit.

The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.
