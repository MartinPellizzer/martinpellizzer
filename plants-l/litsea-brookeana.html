Litsea brookeana

Litsea brookeana, commonly known as the Brooke's litsea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by spherical, yellowish fruits.

Litsea brookeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and aromatic properties.
