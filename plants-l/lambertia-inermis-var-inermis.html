Lambertia inermis var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth lambertia, is a species of flowering plant in the Proteaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in coastal and sandy environments. The plant is characterized by its smooth, woody stems and clusters of bright red, lantern-shaped flowers. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds.
