Lachnaea oliverorum

Lachnaea oliverorum, commonly known as the Oliver's lachnaea, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Lachnaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows or along the edges of woodlands.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a relatively rare and specialized species.
