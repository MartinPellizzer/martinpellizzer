Lobivia marsoneri var. haageana

Lobivia marsoneri var. haageana, commonly known as the Desert Rose, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and purple during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically small and rounded, often with a bluish-gray hue to reduce water loss.

This variety is prized for its resilience and ornamental value in xeriscaping and rock gardens.
