Lonicera hypoleuca var. glabrata

Lonicera hypoleuca var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in early summer. These flowers are followed by red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds.

It is often found in moist woodlands and along stream banks, thriving in partial to full shade.
