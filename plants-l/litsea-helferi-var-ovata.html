Litsea helferi var. ovata

Litsea helferi var. ovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed litsea, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are broadly ovate in shape. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by round, black berries.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
