Lotus edulis var. subannularis

Lotus edulis var. subannularis, commonly known as the subannular lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant produces edible pods containing seeds, which are sometimes used in local cuisines. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and hairy.

This variety is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
