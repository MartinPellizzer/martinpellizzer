Limonium isidorum

Limonium isidorum, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showing a high tolerance for salt and wind. Its slender stems and grass-like leaves are adapted to thrive in harsh maritime environments.

Limonium isidorum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
