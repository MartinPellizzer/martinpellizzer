Lygistum tweedieanum

Lygistum tweedieanum, commonly known as the Tweedie's lygistum, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.
