Leucospermum rochetianum

Leucospermum rochetianum, commonly known as the firewheel plant, is a flowering shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces numerous small, fluffy seeds enclosed in a spherical capsule. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
