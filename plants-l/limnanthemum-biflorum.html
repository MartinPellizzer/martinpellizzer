Limnanthemum biflorum

Limnanthemum biflorum, commonly known as the two-flowered water geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often near water bodies. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Limnanthemum biflorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with moist conditions.
