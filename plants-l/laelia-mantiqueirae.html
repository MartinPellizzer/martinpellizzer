Laelia mantiqueirae

Laelia mantiqueirae, commonly known as the Mantiqueira laelia, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its inflorescence consists of a raceme of clustered flowers. Laelia mantiqueirae prefers cool, humid environments and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy cultivation requirements.
