Leonotis decadonta var. decadonta

Leonotis decadonta var. decadonta, commonly known as the red-hot poker or fireplant, is a striking perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in dense spikes, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its resilience and ability to withstand drought conditions.
