Lobelia reinwardtiana

Lobelia reinwardtiana, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a contrasting yellow or white throat. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia reinwardtiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
