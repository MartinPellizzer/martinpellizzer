Lilium leichtlinii var. pseudotigrinum

Lilium leichtlinii var. pseudotigrinum, commonly known as the tigrine lily, is a rare and striking variant of the leopard lily.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange streaks on pale yellow petals, creating a tiger-like pattern. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. It is a perennial bulbous plant with slender stems and showy flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by horticulturists and conservationists alike.
