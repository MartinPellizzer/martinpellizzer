Loeflingia hispanica f. faurei

Loeflingia hispanica f. faurei, commonly known as the Spanish thyme.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula. The plant features aromatic, small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow, serrated, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.
