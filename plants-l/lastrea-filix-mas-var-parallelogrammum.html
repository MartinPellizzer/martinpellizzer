Lastrea filix-mas var. parallelogrammum

Lastrea filix-mas var. parallelogrammum, commonly known as the Parallelogrammed Snake Plant, is a variant of the snake plant genus Lastrea.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight variegation. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in well-draining soil with moderate watering. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that can tolerate low light conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
