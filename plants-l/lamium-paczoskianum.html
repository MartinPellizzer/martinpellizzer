Lamium paczoskianum

Lamium paczoskianum, commonly known as the Paczoskian dead-nettle, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and opposite leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm, with lance-shaped leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small, tubular, and pinkish-purple in color, appearing in clusters at the leaf axils.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
