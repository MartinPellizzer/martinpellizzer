Linum narbonense proles barrasii

Linum narbonense proles barrasii, commonly known as the Barrasii flax, is a rare and endemic species native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Linaceae family, characterized by its slender stems and delicate, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its capsules contain numerous small, oval seeds.
