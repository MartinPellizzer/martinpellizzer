Lepanthes orion

Lepanthes orion, commonly known as the Orion violet, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with intricate patterns and a distinctive white lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Lepanthes orion is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
