Lebeckia canescens

Lebeckia canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shrubland and grassland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Lebeckia canescens is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
