Livistona fengkaiensis

Livistona fengkaiensis, commonly known as the Fengkai palm, is a species of palm native to China, specifically the Guangdong province.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible fruits that are an important food source for local wildlife.

The Fengkai palm is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and parks.
