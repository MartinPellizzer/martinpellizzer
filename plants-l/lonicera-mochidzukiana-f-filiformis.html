Lonicera mochidzukiana f. filiformis

Lonicera mochidzukiana f. filiformis, commonly known as the Japanese honeysuckle filiformis form, is a unique variant of the Japanese honeysuckle.

It is characterized by its slender, vine-like growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This form is notable for its distinct foliage and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
