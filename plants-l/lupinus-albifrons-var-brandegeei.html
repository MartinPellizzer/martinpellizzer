Lupinus albifrons var. brandegeei

Lupinus albifrons var. brandegeei, commonly known as the Brandegge's lupine, is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly in California.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect stem and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub and grassland habitats.

Lupinus albifrons var. brandegeei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
