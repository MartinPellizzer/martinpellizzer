Helichroa elatior

Helichroa elatior, commonly known as the golden helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, tubular florets surrounded by a ring of ray florets, which give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The plant is also known for its aromatic properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
