Hieracium carolinianum

Hieracium carolinianum, commonly known as the Carolina hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium carolinianum is also known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
