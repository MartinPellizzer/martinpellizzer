Horsfieldia novoguineensis

Horsfieldia novoguineensis, commonly known as the New Guinea orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of New Guinea.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, aerial roots and compact, evergreen foliage. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green with red or purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Horsfieldia novoguineensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
