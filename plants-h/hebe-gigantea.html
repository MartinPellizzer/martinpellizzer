Hebe gigantea

Hebe gigantea, commonly known as the giant hebe, is a striking evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped, glossy, and often have a bluish-green hue.

Hebe gigantea thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
