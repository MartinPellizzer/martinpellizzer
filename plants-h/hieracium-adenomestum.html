Hieracium adenomestum

Hieracium adenomestum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive leaf structures. The plant features rough, hairy leaves that are typically alternate and lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Hieracium adenomestum thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
