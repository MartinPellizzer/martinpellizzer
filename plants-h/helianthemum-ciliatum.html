Helianthemum ciliatum

Helianthemum ciliatum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, rounded foliage covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardening.
