Hemionitis persica

Hemionitis persica, commonly known as the Persian hemionitis, is a rare and delicate fern-like plant native to the arid regions of Persia.

It features small, delicate leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a unique appearance. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome that helps it anchor in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to thrive in dry, hot environments with minimal water.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
