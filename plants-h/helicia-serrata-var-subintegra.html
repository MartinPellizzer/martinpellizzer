Helicia serrata var. subintegra

Helicia serrata var. subintegra, commonly known as the serrated helicia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
