Haworthia schmidtiana var. elongata

Haworthia schmidtiana var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, tubular leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
