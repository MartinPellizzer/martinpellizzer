Hubertia rosellata

Hubertia rosellata, commonly known as the Rosella's hubertia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warm season, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. Its growth habit is typically low and spreading, forming a dense mat of foliage.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
