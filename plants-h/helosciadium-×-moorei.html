Helosciadium × moorei

Helosciadium × moorei, commonly known as the hybrid rockcress, is a perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pinnately lobed leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. Native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. The plant is typically found in alpine and subalpine habitats, often growing at elevations between 1,500 and 2,500 meters.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine plant collections.
