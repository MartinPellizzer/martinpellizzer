Haworthia tessellata f. brevior

Haworthia tessellata f. brevior, commonly known as the compact haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically shorter and broader than those of the typical form, forming a rosette shape. The leaves are often marked with intricate patterns of white or pale green markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its compact form.

This cultivar is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique aesthetics and low maintenance requirements.
