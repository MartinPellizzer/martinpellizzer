Helianthemum marifolium var. alpinum

Helianthemum marifolium var. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine sandwort, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features small, fleshy, rose-colored flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh, rocky environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in mountainous areas.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and alpine displays.
