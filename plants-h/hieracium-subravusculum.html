Hieracium subravusculum

Hieracium subravusculum, commonly known as the small yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often glandular, which can aid in its survival in various environments.

This species is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it plays a role in supporting pollinators.
