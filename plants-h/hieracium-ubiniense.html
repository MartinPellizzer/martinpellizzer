Hieracium ubiniense

Hieracium ubiniense, commonly known as the Ubinian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers that attract pollinators. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and can grow up to 30 centimeters tall.

It is often found in moist, well-drained soils and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
