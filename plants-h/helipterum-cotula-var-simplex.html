Helipterum cotula var. simplex

Helipterum cotula var. simplex, commonly known as the common helipterum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
