Heteromorpha abyssinica var. simplicifolia

Heteromorpha abyssinica var. simplicifolia, commonly known as the Abyssinian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are usually simple and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
