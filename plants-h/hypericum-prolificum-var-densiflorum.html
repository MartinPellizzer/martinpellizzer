Hypericum prolificum var. densiflorum

Hypericum prolificum var. densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and abundant yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features opposite, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for gardens and natural landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and ecologically valuable addition to diverse plant communities.
