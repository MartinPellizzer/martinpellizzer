Homalomena kinabaluensis

Homalomena kinabaluensis, commonly known as the Kinabalu arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by spadices, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in primary rainforests.

Due to its restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
