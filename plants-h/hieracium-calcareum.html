Hieracium calcareum

Hieracium calcareum, commonly known as the limestone hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in calcareous soils, thriving in rocky or limestone-rich environments. Its yellow flowers bloom in early summer, and it is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium calcareum is known for its robust growth and adaptability to harsh, nutrient-poor conditions.
