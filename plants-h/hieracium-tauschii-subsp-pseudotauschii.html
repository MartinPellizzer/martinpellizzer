Hieracium tauschii subsp. pseudotauschii

Hieracium tauschii subsp.

pseudotauschii, commonly known as the Tausch's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has a fibrous root system and produces achenes as its fruit.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and plant taxonomy.
