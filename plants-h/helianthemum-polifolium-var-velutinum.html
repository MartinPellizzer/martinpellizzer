Helianthemum polifolium var. velutinum

Helianthemum polifolium var. velutinum, commonly known as the velvet rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has succulent, hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
