Hosta sieboldiana

Hosta sieboldiana, commonly known as Siebold's plantain, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, slender stems. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its visual appeal.

It prefers partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.
