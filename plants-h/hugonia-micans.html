Hugonia micans

Hugonia micans, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Hugonia micans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
