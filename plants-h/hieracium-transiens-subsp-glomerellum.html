Hieracium transiens subsp. glomerellum

Hieracium transiens subsp.

glomerellum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and flowering heads that appear in clusters.

The plant produces yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disc florets, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Its subspecific name, *glomerellum*, refers to the clustered arrangement of its flower heads.
