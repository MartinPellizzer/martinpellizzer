Hieracium exsiliusculum

Hieracium exsiliusculum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright stem and basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typical of the dandelion family. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its invasive potential, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
