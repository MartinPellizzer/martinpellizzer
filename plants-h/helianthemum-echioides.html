Helianthemum echioides

Helianthemum echioides, commonly known as the prickly carnation, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white, typically blooming in spring and early summer. The plant has dense, spiny foliage that provides a protective covering for its delicate blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardening.
