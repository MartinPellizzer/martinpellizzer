Heuchera nivalis

Heuchera nivalis, commonly known as the snow heather, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant features heart-shaped, fuzzy leaves that are often variegated with silver or red tones. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Heuchera nivalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture and color to shaded landscapes.
