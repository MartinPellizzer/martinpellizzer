Heuchera elegans

Heuchera elegans, commonly known as the silver queen heuchera, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features striking foliage with a silvery-blue hue and red veins, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, preferring moist conditions.

Heuchera elegans is valued for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
