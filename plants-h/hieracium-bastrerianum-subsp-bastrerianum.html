Hieracium bastrerianum subsp. bastrerianum

Hieracium bastrerianum subsp.

bastrerianum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are often reddish in color.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ability to produce a large number of seeds.
