Habenaria pachyneura

Habenaria pachyneura, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with showy, violet to purple flowers that have three sepals and three petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it requires specific humidity and light conditions to flourish.
