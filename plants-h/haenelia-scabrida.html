Haenelia scabrida

Haenelia scabrida, commonly known as the rough-henbane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces striking violet or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grassland and shrubland habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
