Helenium insulare

Helenium insulare, commonly known as the island sunflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the United States.

It features showy, rayed flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, with a distinctive central disk. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet, producing numerous blooms from late summer to early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

Helenium insulare thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
