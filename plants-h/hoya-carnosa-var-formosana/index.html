Hoya Carnosa Var. Formosana

Hoya carnosa var. formosana is a striking and popular houseplant known for its waxy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, star-like flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, this variety is prized for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying qualities. Its unique foliage and elegant blooms make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts and interior designers alike. Easy to care for, it requires minimal watering and well-draining soil to flourish. This versatile plant adds a touch of beauty and serenity to any indoor space.
