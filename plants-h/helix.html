Helix

Helix, commonly known as the helix plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its spiral-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that often display bright colors. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. Helix species typically thrive in moist, well-drained soils and require partial to full sunlight.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
