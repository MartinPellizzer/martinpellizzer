Havetiopsis caryophylloides

Havetiopsis caryophylloides, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its thick, segmented stems and vibrant, showy flowers. Its leaves are usually arranged in a zigzag pattern, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with well-draining soil.
