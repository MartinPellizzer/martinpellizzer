Hamelia latifolia

Hamelia latifolia, commonly known as the flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
