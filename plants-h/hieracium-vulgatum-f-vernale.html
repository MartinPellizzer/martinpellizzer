Hieracium vulgatum f. vernale

Hieracium vulgatum f. vernale, commonly known as the early hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

This perennial herb is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
