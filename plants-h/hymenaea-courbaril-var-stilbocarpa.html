Hymenaea courbaril var. stilbocarpa

Hymenaea courbaril var. stilbocarpa, commonly known as the Brazilian桃花心木 or rosewood, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark, aromatic wood that is highly valued for furniture and musical instruments. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces distinctive yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release winged seeds.

The tree is also known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
