Haworthia tenuifolia

Haworthia tenuifolia, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, narrow leaves that are often marked with white or yellow tubercles resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers on short stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
