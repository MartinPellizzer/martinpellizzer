Heterophragma longipes

Heterophragma longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed spider-orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Australia.

This plant is characterized by its unique, spider-like floral structure with long, slender petals and sepals. It typically grows in sandy soils and is often found in open forests or heathlands. The flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive, elongated spur.

Heterophragma longipes is a perennial herb that blooms during the cooler months, making it a notable species in Australian flora.
