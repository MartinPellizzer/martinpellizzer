Habenaria riparia

Habenaria riparia, commonly known as the slender marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to wetland habitats in North America.

It typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is often found in bogs, marshes, and along the edges of lakes. The plant features slender, upright stems with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, pale green or white, and are arranged in a loose cluster.

Habenaria riparia is a long-lived perennial that plays an important role in wetland ecosystems.
