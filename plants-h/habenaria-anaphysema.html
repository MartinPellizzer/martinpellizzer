Habenaria anaphysema

Habenaria anaphysema, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base of the stem, and it produces a single spike of flowers during the spring or early summer.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in humid, tropical regions.
