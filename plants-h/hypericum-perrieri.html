Hypericum perrieri

Hypericum perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's St. John's wort, is a species of flowering plant in the family Hypericaceae.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous growth habit. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, with four petals and numerous stamens. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum perrieri is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
