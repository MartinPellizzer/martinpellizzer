Hieracium tistamense

Hieracium tistamense, commonly known as the Tistamense hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of serrated, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers. Native to specific regions in Central Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. It is often found in mountainous areas where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions.

The plant's structure and growth habits reflect its evolutionary adaptations to its native habitat.
