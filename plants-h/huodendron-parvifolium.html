Huodendron parvifolium

Huodendron parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaved bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes many species of bellflowers.

Huodendron parvifolium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
