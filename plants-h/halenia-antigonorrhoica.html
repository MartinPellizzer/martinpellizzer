Halenia antigonorrhoica

Halenia antigonorrhoica, commonly known as the Indian bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Halenia antigonorrhoica is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a specimen plant in botanical collections.
