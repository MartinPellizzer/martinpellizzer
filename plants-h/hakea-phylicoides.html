Hakea phylicoides

Hakea phylicoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces striking spikes of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Hakea phylicoides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-water conditions.
