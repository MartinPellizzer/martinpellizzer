Haworthia indurata

Haworthia indurata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are often marked with distinct white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant is known for its compact size and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect light and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Haworthia indurata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid environments.
