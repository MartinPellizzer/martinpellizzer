Helianthemum montanum var. australe

Helianthemum montanum var. australe, commonly known as the southern rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to coastal and Mediterranean climates. It produces small, spherical fruits that contain numerous seeds, facilitating its spread in suitable habitats.

Due to its hardy nature and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
