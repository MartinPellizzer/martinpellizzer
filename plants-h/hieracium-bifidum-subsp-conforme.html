Hieracium bifidum subsp. conforme

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

conforme, commonly known as the conforming hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts.

The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows.
