Helianthemum scorpioides

Helianthemum scorpioides, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant has small, needle-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs, helping to reduce water loss in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Helianthemum scorpioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes.
