Helianthus floribundus

Helianthus floribundus, commonly known as the clustered sunflower, is a species of flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant produces showy yellow-orange ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to gardens and a valuable plant for pollinators.
