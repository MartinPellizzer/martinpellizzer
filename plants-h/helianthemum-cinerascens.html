Helianthemum cinerascens

Helianthemum cinerascens, commonly known as the ash-gray rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its rugged appearance. The plant has aromatic, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery sheen. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
