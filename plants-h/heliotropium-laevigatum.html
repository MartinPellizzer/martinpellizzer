Heliotropium laevigatum

Heliotropium laevigatum, commonly known as the smooth heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial species that typically grows in moist to wet habitats. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often appearing in clusters. Its leaves are usually opposite, smooth, and elliptical in shape.

Heliotropium laevigatum is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of streams.
