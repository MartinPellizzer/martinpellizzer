Homalomena gillii

Homalomena gillii, commonly known as the Gill's arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Homalomena gillii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
