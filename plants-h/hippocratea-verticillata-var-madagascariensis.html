Hippocratea verticillata var. madagascariensis

Hippocratea verticillata var. madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with intricate patterns resembling a star. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in whorls around the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique and showy blooms.
