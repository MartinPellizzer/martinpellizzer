Hibiscus keilii

Hibiscus keilii, commonly known as the Kew hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers with five deeply notched petals that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Hibiscus keilii thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
