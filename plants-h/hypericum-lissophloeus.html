Hypericum lissophloeus

Hypericum lissophloeus, commonly known as smooth-stemmed St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum in the family Clusiaceae, characterized by its yellow flowers and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and has opposite, simple leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers bloom in summer, producing bright yellow petals and prominent stamens.

It is valued both for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
