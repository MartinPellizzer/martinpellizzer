Hedyosmum steinii

Hedyosmum steinii, commonly known as the Brazilian star-anise, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and small, clustered flowers. Hedyosmum steinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
