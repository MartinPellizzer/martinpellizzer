Hieracium porphyranthes

Hieracium porphyranthes, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium porphyranthes is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
