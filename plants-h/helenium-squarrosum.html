Helenium squarrosum

Helenium squarrosum, commonly known as the rough helenium, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, ray-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture.

Helenium squarrosum produces orange to yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
