Hieracium eugenii

Hieracium eugenii, commonly known as the Eugen's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers and is native to parts of Europe. The plant typically grows in meadows and grassy areas, preferring well-drained soils. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium eugenii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
