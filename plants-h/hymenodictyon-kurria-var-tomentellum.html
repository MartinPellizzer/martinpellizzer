Hymenodictyon kurria var. tomentellum

Hymenodictyon kurria var. tomentellum, commonly known as the hairy hymenodictyon, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine, white hairs, giving the plant a woolly appearance. This variant is distinguished by its dense pubescence, which contributes to its common name.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
