Huperzia sprengeri

Huperzia sprengeri, commonly known as the crested huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen appearance with dark green, leathery leaves that often have a distinctive crest or ridge. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, it thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant's rhizome is thick and fleshy, which helps it store water and nutrients.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and conservation efforts.
