Hieracium anisotrichum

Hieracium anisotrichum, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium anisotrichum is often found in sunny habitats and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
