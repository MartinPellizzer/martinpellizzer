Hemigyrosa pervillei

Hemigyrosa pervillei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb, often forming a rosette of leaves with a central stem bearing blooms. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flower colors, which range from white to purple.
