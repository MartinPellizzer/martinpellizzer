Hebe × carsei

Hebe × carsei, commonly known as the rock hebe, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, evergreen foliage that is typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, violet, or white, often blooming from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Hebe × carsei is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
