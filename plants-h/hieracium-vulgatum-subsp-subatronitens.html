Hieracium vulgatum subsp. subatronitens

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

subatronitens, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm.

The plant features basal and cauline leaves that are lanceolate to ovate, with hairy surfaces. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and are borne on slender peduncles.

The subspecies is characterized by its distinctive yellow coloration and robust growth habit.
