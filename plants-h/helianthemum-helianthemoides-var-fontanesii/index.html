Helianthemum Helianthemoides Var. Fontanesii

Helianthemum helianthemoides var. fontanesii is a rare and charming plant native to the rocky regions of southern Europe. This evergreen shrub is known for its small, vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a low-maintenance plant for gardeners. Despite its hardiness, this variety remains relatively obscure, often overlooked in favor of more common garden plants.
