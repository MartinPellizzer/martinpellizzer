Hypericum thoralfii

Hypericum thoralfii, commonly known as the narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

Hypericum thoralfii is part of the Hypericaceae family, which includes other species of St. John's wort known for their medicinal properties.
