Heterotropa variegata

Heterotropa variegata, commonly known as the variegated heterotropa, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Violaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped leaves with a striking variegation pattern of green and white. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
