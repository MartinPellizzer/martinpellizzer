Hieracium nigrescens var. eurotatrense

Hieracium nigrescens var. eurotatrense, commonly known as the European yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is considered a weed in some regions.
