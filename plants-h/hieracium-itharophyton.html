Hieracium itharophyton

Hieracium itharophyton, commonly known as the lesser yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Hieracium. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

The species is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate climates.
