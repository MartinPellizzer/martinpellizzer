Horsfieldia globularia var. minahassae

Horsfieldia globularia var. minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, globular flower structure that resembles a small, rounded orb. The plant produces vibrant, colorful flowers that are typically yellow or orange with intricate patterns, making it highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern in its native range.
