Halothamnus Lancifolius

Halothamnus lancifolius is a hardy shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It is known for its ability to thrive in harsh, saline environments where few other plants can survive. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that help reduce water loss through transpiration. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in late spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape. This resilient species plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
