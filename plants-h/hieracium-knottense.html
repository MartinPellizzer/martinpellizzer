Hieracium knottense

Hieracium knottense, commonly known as the knotted hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and its leaves are typically heart-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe, it is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and can form dense colonies.
