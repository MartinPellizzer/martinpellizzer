Hieracium spinosum

Hieracium spinosum, commonly known as the spiked hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny leaves and produces yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
