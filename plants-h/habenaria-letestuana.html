Habenaria letestuana

Habenaria letestuana, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in the spring, producing pinkish-white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It prefers shady, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and is of interest for conservation efforts.
