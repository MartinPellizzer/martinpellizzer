Hieracium murorum subsp. stilbocephalum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

stilbocephalum, commonly known as the purplehead hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its distinctive purple-tinged flower heads, which are composed of numerous small yellow florets.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the stems are often reddish in color.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is noted for its ornamental value in horticulture.
