Hydrangea shaochingii

Hydrangea shaochingii, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features serrated, ovate leaves that are dark green in color. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Hydrangea shaochingii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
