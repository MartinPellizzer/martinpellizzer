Hyacinthus pouzolzii var. albiflorus

Hyacinthus pouzolzii var. albiflorus, commonly known as the white-flowered hyacinth, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
