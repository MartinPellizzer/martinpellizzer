Hedyotis horneriana

Hedyotis horneriana, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Hedyotis horneriana thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
