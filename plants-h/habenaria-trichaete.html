Habenaria trichaete

Habenaria trichaete, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Botanically, it is noted for its complex floral structure and is part of the Orchidaceae family.
