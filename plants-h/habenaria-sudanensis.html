Habenaria sudanensis

Habenaria sudanensis, commonly known as the Sudan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
