Haworthia badia var. joleneae

Haworthia badia var. joleneae, commonly known as the Joleneae Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pale green to bluish-gray leaves with distinctive white to yellowish marginal teeth. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and indoor gardeners.
