Hyptis peduncularis

Hyptis peduncularis, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
