Haworthia nortieri var. giftbergensis

Haworthia nortieri var. giftbergensis, commonly known as the Giftberg Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pale green leaves with distinctive white to yellowish markings and a subtle waxy coating. The plant produces tall, sparsely branched inflorescences with small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and green. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
