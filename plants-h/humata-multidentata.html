Humata multidentata

Humata multidentata, commonly known as the multi-toothed fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features a creeping rhizome with numerous branching stems and feather-like fronds. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with multiple small, serrated leaflets.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
