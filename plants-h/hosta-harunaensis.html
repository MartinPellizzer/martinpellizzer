Hosta harunaensis

Hosta harunaensis, commonly known as the Haruna hosta, is a species of plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is prized for its ornamental foliage. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in early summer.

Hosta harunaensis is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, shade-tolerant plant with striking visual appeal.
