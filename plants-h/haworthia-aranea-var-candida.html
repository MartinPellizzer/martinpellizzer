Haworthia aranea var. candida

Haworthia aranea var. candida, commonly known as the white-leaved Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pale green leaves with distinctive white or pale markings, giving it a striking, almost translucent appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
