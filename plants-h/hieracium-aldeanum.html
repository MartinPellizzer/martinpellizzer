Hieracium aldeanum

Hieracium aldeanum, commonly known as the Aldeanum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers consist of yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Hieracium aldeanum is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
