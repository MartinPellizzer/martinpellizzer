Halimium heterophyllum var. leptocladum

Halimium heterophyllum var. leptocladum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fleshy, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and leaves that are often clustered at the base. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
