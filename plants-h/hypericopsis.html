Hypericopsis

Hypericopsis, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Hypericopsis is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
