Hyptis Salvioides

Hyptis salvioides is a versatile plant native to the warm climates of the Americas. Known for its aromatic leaves and vibrant flowers, it is often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its ability to repel insects makes it a valuable companion plant in gardens. With its wide range of uses, Hyptis salvioides stands out as both a practical and ornamental plant.
