Hieracium leiopogon subsp. leiopogon

Hieracium leiopogon subsp.

leiopogon, commonly known as the smoothleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.
