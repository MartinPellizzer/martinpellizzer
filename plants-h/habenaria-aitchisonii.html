Habenaria aitchisonii

Habenaria aitchisonii, commonly known as the Aitchison's orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable climate.
