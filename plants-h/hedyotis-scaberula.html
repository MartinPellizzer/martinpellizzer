Hedyotis scaberula

Hedyotis scaberula, commonly known as rough hedyotis, is a small herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in short, rough hairs, giving it its common name. Its small, opposite leaves are usually elliptical or ovate in shape.

Hedyotis scaberula produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
