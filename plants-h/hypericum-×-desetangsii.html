Hypericum × desetangsii

Hypericum × desetangsii, commonly known as the hybrid St. John's wort, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and opposite leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its botanical features include reddish stems, elliptical leaves, and numerous small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
