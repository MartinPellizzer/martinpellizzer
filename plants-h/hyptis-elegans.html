Hyptis elegans

Hyptis elegans, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. Its leaves are often aromatic and may have a mint-like scent.

Hyptis elegans is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and pleasant fragrance.
