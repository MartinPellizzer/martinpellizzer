Hieracium rupicola var. foliiferum

Hieracium rupicola var. foliiferum, commonly known as the leafy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to rocky and alpine environments in Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow disc flowers surrounded by ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as cliffs, grasslands, and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in harsh conditions.

It is a hardy species adapted to poor soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
