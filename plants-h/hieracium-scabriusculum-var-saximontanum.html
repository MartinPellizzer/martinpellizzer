Hieracium scabriusculum var. saximontanum

Hieracium scabriusculum var. saximontanum, commonly known as the saximontanum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, exhibiting a low, spreading growth habit. Its leaves are rough and hairy, and its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.

It is often found in open, sunny areas with well-drained soils.
