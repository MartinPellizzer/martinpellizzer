Hieracium flagellare subsp. amoeniceps

Hieracium flagellare subsp.

amoeniceps, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Hieracium.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
