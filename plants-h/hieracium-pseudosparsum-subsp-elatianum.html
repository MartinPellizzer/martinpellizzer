Hieracium pseudosparsum subsp. elatianum

Hieracium pseudosparsum subsp.

elatianum, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.

The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with a rough, hairy texture.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in disturbed habitats.
