Haemanthus lindenii

Haemanthus lindenii, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescences that bear bright orange to red flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens around the world.
