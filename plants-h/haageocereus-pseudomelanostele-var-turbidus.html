Haageocereus pseudomelanostele var. turbidus

Haageocereus pseudomelanostele var. turbidus, commonly known as the Turbidus Cactus, is a rare and specialized cactus species native to specific regions of Mexico.

The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months, typically in summer.
