Helichrysum cerastoides var. cerastoides

Helichrysum cerastoides var. cerastoides, commonly known as the golden marguerite or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

The flowers are long-lasting and can be used in dried flower arrangements.
