Hieracium bichloricolor

Hieracium bichloricolor, commonly known as the two-colored hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium bichloricolor is often considered an invasive species in certain regions due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
