Helichrysum Nitens Subsp. Robynsii

Helichrysum nitens subsp. robynsii is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa. It is known for its dense clusters of golden-yellow flowers that resemble tiny suns. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its drought tolerance and long-lasting blooms make it a favorite among gardeners. With its vibrant color and easy care requirements, it adds both beauty and resilience to any garden setting.
