Hypoestes linearis

Hypoestes linearis, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, linear, white or pink flowers that resemble snowflakes. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes linearis is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
