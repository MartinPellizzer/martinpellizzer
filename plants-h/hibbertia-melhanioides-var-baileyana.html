Hibbertia melhanioides var. baileyana

Hibbertia melhanioides var. baileyana, commonly known as the Baileyan Needlebush, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and resilience in harsh conditions.
