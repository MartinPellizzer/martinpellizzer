Hydriastele Vitiensis

Hydriastele vitiensis is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the tropical regions of Vanuatu. This unique plant is known for its striking appearance and specialized adaptations to its humid, mountainous habitat. It thrives in the shaded understory of rainforests, where it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake. Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species. Studying Hydriastele vitiensis offers valuable insights into orchid ecology and conservation efforts in biodiversity hotspots.
