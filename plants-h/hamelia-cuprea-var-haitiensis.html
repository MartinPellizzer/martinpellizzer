Hamelia cuprea var. haitiensis

Hamelia cuprea var. haitiensis, commonly known as the Haitian flamebush, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly pubescent underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to grow in harsh, saline conditions.
