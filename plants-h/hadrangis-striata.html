Hadrangis striata

Hadrangis striata, commonly known as the striped fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are often adorned with distinctive stripes or markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests or along stream banks. Its rhizomatous structure allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Hadrangis striata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
