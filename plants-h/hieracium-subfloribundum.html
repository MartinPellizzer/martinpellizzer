Hieracium subfloribundum

Hieracium subfloribundum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems produce numerous yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Hieracium subfloribundum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
