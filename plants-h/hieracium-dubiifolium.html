Hieracium dubiifolium

Hieracium dubiifolium, commonly known as the doubtful leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is sometimes considered invasive in certain regions.
