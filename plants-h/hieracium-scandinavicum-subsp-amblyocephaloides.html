Hieracium scandinavicum subsp. amblyocephaloides

Hieracium scandinavicum subsp.

amblyocephaloides, commonly known as the Scandinavian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It has basal and upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its flowering period occurs from late spring to early summer, and it is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
