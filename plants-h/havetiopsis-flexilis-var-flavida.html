Havetiopsis flexilis var. flavida

Havetiopsis flexilis var. flavida, commonly known as the golden Havetiopsis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences that bear small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
