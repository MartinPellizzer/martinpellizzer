Hieracium × calypterotum

Hieracium × calypterotum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the hawkweed genus. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It has a creeping growth habit and can form dense mats, which can sometimes be invasive.

Its botanical structure includes alternate leaves and a basal rosette, making it easily identifiable in the field.
