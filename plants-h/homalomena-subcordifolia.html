Homalomena subcordifolia

Homalomena subcordifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in an inflorescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
