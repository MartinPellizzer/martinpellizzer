Heuffelia pyrenaica

Heuffelia pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean heuffelia, is a rare and endemic plant species found primarily in the Pyrenees mountain range.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its small, narrow leaves and delicate, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain meadows and forest understories. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a compact growth habit.
