Hypericum senkakuinsulare

Hypericum senkakuinsulare, commonly known as the Ryukyu goldthread, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes species commonly referred to as St. John's wort. The plant is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its woody stems covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.

The leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.
