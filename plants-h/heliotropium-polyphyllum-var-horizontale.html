Heliotropium polyphyllum var. horizontale

Heliotropium polyphyllum var. horizontale, commonly known as the horizontal heliotrope, is a low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions.

It features prostrate stems that spread along the ground, often forming a dense mat. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from purple to pink, typically blooming in the spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is well adapted to moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or wetland environments.
