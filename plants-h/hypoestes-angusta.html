Hypoestes angusta

Hypoestes angusta, commonly known as the "Purple Heart" plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, tubular, violet to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes angusta is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
