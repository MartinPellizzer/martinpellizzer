Hieracium levicaule subsp. anacron

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

anacron, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy.

Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
