Homalomena pulleana

Homalomena pulleana, commonly known as the purple-leafed arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that range in color from deep green to purplish-red. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
