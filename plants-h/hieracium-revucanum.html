Hieracium revucanum

Hieracium revucanum, commonly known as the Balkan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium revucanum is often found in the Balkan Peninsula and has been the subject of botanical studies due to its ecological significance and medicinal potential.
