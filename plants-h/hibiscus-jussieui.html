Hibiscus jussieui

Hibiscus jussieui, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to deep red, with a prominent central pistil and stamens. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are velvety to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hibiscus jussieui is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
