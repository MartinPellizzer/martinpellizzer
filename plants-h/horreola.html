Horreola

Horreola, commonly known as goatweed, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Amaranthaceae.

It consists of several species that are native to various regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plants typically have simple, alternate leaves that are often lance-shaped or ovate. They produce small, clustered flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color.

Horreola species are often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
