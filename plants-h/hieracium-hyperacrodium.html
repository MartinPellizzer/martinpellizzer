Hieracium hyperacrodium

Hieracium hyperacrodium, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy leaves and produces small, elongated achenes as its seeds. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and thrives in moist, open habitats.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
