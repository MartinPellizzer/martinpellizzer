Hemsleya macrocarpa var. clavata

Hemsleya macrocarpa var. clavata, commonly known as the Clavate Hemsleya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
