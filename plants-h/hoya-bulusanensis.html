Hoya bulusanensis

Hoya bulusanensis, commonly known as the Bulusan hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Hoya bulusanensis is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
