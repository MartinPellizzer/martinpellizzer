Hepetis Undulata

Hepetis undulata, also known as the zigzag mallow, is a striking flowering plant native to the western united states. it is characterized by its vibrant purple and pink blossoms that bloom in late spring and early summer. the plant's name comes from the undulating pattern of its leaves, which create a unique visual effect. it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for native gardens. due to its beauty and ecological benefits, it is often used in restoration projects and pollinator habitats.
