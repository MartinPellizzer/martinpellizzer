Hieracium erigentiforme

Hieracium erigentiforme, commonly known as the erect hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish composite flowers. The plant typically has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Hieracium erigentiforme is a perennial species that spreads through both seeds and rhizomes.
