Heteranthera dubia f. terrestre

Heteranthera dubia f. terrestre, commonly known as the terrestrial heteranthera, is a variant of the species native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquatic and bog gardens.
