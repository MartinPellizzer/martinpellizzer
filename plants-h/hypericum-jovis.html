Hypericum jovis

Hypericum jovis, commonly known as the joyous St. John's wort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Hypericaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, which are typical of the genus Hypericum. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum jovis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in herbal medicine.
