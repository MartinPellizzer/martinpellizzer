Hatiora salicornoides

Hatiora salicornoides, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often used for propagation. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically during the holiday season. It prefers indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
