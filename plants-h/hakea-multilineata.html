Hakea multilineata

Hakea multilineata, commonly known as the multi-line hakea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical or cylindrical-branching stems covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers in winter, which are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often have a waxy coating.

Hakea multilineata is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.
