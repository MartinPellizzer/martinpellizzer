Hieracium modiciforme

Hieracium modiciforme, commonly known as the Modiciform Meadowhawk, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its daisy-like appearance, featuring yellow disk flowers surrounded by white to pale yellow ray florets. Native to Europe, it typically grows in meadows and grassy areas, preferring moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces achenes as its seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It is often found in association with other wildflowers in open, sunny habitats.
