Hopea maranti

Hopea maranti, commonly known as the Malayan hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often dark green in color. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its ecological significance in tropical forests.
