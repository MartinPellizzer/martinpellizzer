Hieracium ventanianum

Hieracium ventanianum, commonly known as the Ventanian hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, typically growing in meadows and grasslands. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
