Habenaria cruegeri

Habenaria cruegeri, commonly known as the Crueger's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and swamps. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are pale green to white, with a distinctive yellow lip, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and specific ecological requirements.
