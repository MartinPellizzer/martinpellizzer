Hovea splendens

Hovea splendens, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has compound leaves with multiple oval-shaped leaflets, and its bark is smooth and often peels in thin layers. Hovea splendens thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
