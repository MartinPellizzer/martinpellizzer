Henslowia glabra

Henslowia glabra, commonly known as the smooth henslowia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. Its small, white to pale purple flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

Henslowia glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
