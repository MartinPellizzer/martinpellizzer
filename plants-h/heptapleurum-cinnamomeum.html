Heptapleurum cinnamomeum

Heptapleurum cinnamomeum, commonly known as cinnamon wood or Chinese evergreen, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated.
