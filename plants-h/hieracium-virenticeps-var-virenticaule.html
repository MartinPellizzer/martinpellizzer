Hieracium virenticeps var. virenticaule

Hieracium virenticeps var. virenticaule, commonly known as the green-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, slender stem that is often tinged with a green hue, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the daisy family. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.
