Hebe macrantha

Hebe macrantha, commonly known as the mountain hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Hebe macrantha is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its foliage is often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance.
