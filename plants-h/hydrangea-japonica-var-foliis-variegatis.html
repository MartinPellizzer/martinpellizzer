Hydrangea japonica var. foliis-variegatis

Hydrangea japonica var. foliis-variegatis, commonly known as the Variegated Japanese Hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves that feature white, yellow, and green patterns. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from blue to pink depending on soil pH. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, adding visual interest even when not in bloom.
