Horsfieldia motleyi

Horsfieldia motleyi, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical rainforests. The plant features a pseudobulb with long, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in the undergrowth of dense forest canopies.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
