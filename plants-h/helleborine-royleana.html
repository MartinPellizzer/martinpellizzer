Helleborine royleana

Helleborine royleana, commonly known as the Indian mace orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers with a deep purple hue. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure.

Helleborine royleana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
