Hieracium × grohii

Hieracium × grohii, commonly known as the hybrid meadowhawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Hieracium pilosellum and Hieracium aurantiacum, resulting in a species with a mix of characteristics from both parents. The plant typically grows in meadows and grassy areas, thriving in temperate climates. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are usually hairy and lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
