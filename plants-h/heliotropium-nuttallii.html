Heliotropium nuttallii

Heliotropium nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a fragrant scent. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Heliotropium nuttallii is adapted to moist, sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
