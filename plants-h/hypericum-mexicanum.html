Hypericum mexicanum

Hypericum mexicanum, commonly known as Mexican St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, sunny environments, often found in rocky soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum mexicanum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
