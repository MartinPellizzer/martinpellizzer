Habenaria modica

Habenaria modica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, pale green or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance.

Botanically, it has a complex structure with specialized roots and a unique floral morphology.
