Helianthemum origanifolium f. serrae

Helianthemum origanifolium f. serrae, commonly known as the serrated rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, aromatic leaves with serrated edges, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, adding a bright touch to rocky or coastal habitats. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact size and hardy nature make it a popular choice for Mediterranean-style gardens.
