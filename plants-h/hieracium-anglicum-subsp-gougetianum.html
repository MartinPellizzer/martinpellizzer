Hieracium anglicum subsp. gougetianum

Hieracium anglicum subsp.

gougetianum, commonly known as the English hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It has hairy stems and produces small, elongated flower heads.

Due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various conditions, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
