Heliotropium leptaleum

Heliotropium leptaleum, commonly known as the slender heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and gardens.

Heliotropium leptaleum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
