Hydrangea scandens subsp. kwangtungensis

Hydrangea scandens subsp.

kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong climbing hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China. It features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink or blue, depending on soil pH.

The plant has arching stems that can climb or trail, making it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover. Its foliage is typically dark green and becomes bronze in autumn, adding seasonal interest.

It prefers partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, thriving in temperate climates.
