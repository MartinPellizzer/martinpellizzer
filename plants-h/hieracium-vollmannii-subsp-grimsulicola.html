Hieracium vollmannii subsp. grimsulicola

Hieracium vollmannii subsp.

grimsulicola, commonly known as the Grimsulicola hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of North America. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky slopes.

The plant has basal and cauline leaves that are usually hairy and have a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets that are shorter than the central disk florets.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in association with other alpine or subalpine flora.
