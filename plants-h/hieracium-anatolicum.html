Hieracium anatolicum

Hieracium anatolicum, commonly known as Anatolian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium anatolicum is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
