Helichrysum salviifolium

Helichrysum salviifolium, commonly known as the blue helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum salviifolium is also valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
