Habenaria gigantea

Habenaria gigantea, commonly known as the giant marsh orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant prefers wet, marshy habitats and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with prominent purple or red markings.

The orchid has a complex root system and relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient absorption.
