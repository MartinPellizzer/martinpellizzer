Habenaria boltonii

Habenaria boltonii, commonly known as the Bolton's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a loose raceme, often with a distinct lip petal. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate species with limited distribution.
