Hieracium almquistianum

Hieracium almquistianum, commonly known as Almqvist's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves and produces yellow flower heads composed of many small florets. Native to the mountainous regions of Scandinavia, it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant is generally short-lived, with a lifespan of one to two years.

Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and flies.
