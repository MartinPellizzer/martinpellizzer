Hieracium glossodes

Hieracium glossodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of yellowish-green leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers in the summer months. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in dry conditions.

Hieracium glossodes is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
