Holostemma tuberculatum

Holostemma tuberculatum, commonly known as the warty holostemma, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its distinctive warty nodes along the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by distinctive, spiny capsules containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Holostemma tuberculatum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
