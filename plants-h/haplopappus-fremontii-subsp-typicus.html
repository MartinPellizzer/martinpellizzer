Haplopappus fremontii subsp. typicus

Haplopappus fremontii subsp.

typicus, commonly known as the Fremont's pappus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive bracts.
