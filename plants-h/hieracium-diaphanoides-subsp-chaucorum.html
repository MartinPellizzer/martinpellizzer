Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. chaucorum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

chaucorum, commonly known as the transparent hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a creeping growth habit and produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
