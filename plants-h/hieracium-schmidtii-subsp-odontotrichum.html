Hieracium schmidtii subsp. odontotrichum

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

odontotrichum, commonly known as Schmidt's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its finely toothed leaves and dense, glandular pubescence. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
