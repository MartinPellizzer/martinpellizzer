Hieracium aequipodum

Hieracium aequipodum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant features basal and alternate leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering heads consist of numerous yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets.

Hieracium aequipodum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
