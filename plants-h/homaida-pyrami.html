Homaida pyrami

Homaida pyrami, commonly known as the Pyramidal Homaida, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive pyramidal growth form.

It belongs to the family Homaidaceae and is native to arid regions of the Middle East. The plant features narrow, pointed leaves that reduce water loss, adapting it to its harsh environment. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of yellow and orange.

Homaida pyrami is valued for its resilience and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
