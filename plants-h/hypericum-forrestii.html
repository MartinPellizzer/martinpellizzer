Hypericum forrestii

Hypericum forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese St. John's Wort, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum forrestii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
