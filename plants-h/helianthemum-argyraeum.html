Helianthemum argyraeum

Helianthemum argyraeum, commonly known as silver thyme or silver mat thyme, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with silvery-white undersides and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover. Its aromatic leaves release a subtle scent when crushed, and it is drought-tolerant once established.

Helianthemum argyraeum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in Mediterranean climates.
