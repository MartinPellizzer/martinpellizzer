Helichrysum rosmarinifolium var. erubescens

Helichrysum rosmarinifolium var. erubescens, commonly known as the scarlet everlasting, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are aromatic and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that transition to a vibrant red or orange hue as they dry. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting, brightly colored flowers that retain their shape and color when dried.
