Hieracium zizianum subsp. leptophyllum

Hieracium zizianum subsp.

leptophyllum, commonly known as the slenderleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters.

The plant features basal and alternate leaves that are usually lanceolate to ovate in shape, with hairy surfaces. Its flowering stems bear yellow composite flowers, composed of numerous small disk flowers surrounded by ray florets.

The species is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
