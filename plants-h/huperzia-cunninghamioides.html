Huperzia cunninghamioides

Huperzia cunninghamioides, commonly known as the Cunningham's huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and distinctive, clustered growth habit. The plant features lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of tiny, scale-like structures. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices.

Huperzia cunninghamioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
