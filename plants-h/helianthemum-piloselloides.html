Helianthemum piloselloides

Helianthemum piloselloides, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Helianthemum piloselloides is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for xeriscaping.
