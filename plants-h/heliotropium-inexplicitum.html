Heliotropium inexplicitum

Heliotropium inexplicitum, commonly known as the blue heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from blue to violet, often with a white center. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Heliotropium inexplicitum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
