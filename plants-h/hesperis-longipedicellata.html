Hesperis longipedicellata

Hesperis longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-stemmed honesty, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Hesperis longipedicellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and delicate appearance.
