Hieracium laetifrons

Hieracium laetifrons, commonly known as the cheerful face dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble a smiling face, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring temperate climates. It has hairy stems and leaves that are alternate and serrated.

Hieracium laetifrons is known for its ornamental value and is often found in gardens and natural habitats.
