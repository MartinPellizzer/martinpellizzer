Hieracium orarium var. fulvum

Hieracium orarium var. fulvum, commonly known as the golden hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has hairy, upright stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is known for its distinctive color and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
