Hieracium lachenalii subsp. meppelense

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks. It is a perennial species with a low-growing habit, and its leaves are usually hairy and lance-shaped.
