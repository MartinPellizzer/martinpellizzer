Helichrysum gunnii subsp. paralium

Helichrysum gunnii subsp.

paralium, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to retain color and form when dried.
