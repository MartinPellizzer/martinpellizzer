Habenaria iyoensis

Habenaria iyoensis, commonly known as the Iyoensis Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces a single spike of small, fragrant blooms.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
