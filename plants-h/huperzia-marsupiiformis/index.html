Huperzia Marsupiiformis

Huperzia marsupiiformis is a unique and ancient fern species known for its distinctive, moss-like appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, it thrives in shaded, moist environments. This plant is often mistaken for a moss due to its dense, carpet-like growth pattern. Despite its small size, it plays an important role in forest ecosystems. Its resilience and adaptability make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
