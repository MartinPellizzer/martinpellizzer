Hyospathe sodiroi

Hyospathe sodiroi, commonly known as the African peace lily, is a tropical plant native to Central and Southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves that are typically broad and elliptical in shape. The plant produces striking white or pale green spathe flowers, which are often mistaken for petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hyospathe sodiroi is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
