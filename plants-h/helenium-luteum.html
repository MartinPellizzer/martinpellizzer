Helenium luteum

Helenium luteum, commonly known as the Orange Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright orange to yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It grows in a variety of habitats, including meadows, prairies, and disturbed soils.

Helenium luteum is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy, composite flower heads.
