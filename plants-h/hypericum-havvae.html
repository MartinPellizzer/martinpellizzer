Hypericum havvae

Hypericum havvae, commonly known as the Hawaiian goldenseal, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Hypericaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a smooth texture.

Hypericum havvae is valued for its potential health benefits and is often used in traditional Hawaiian medicine.
