Helichrysum rhodellum

Helichrysum rhodellum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even when dried. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is usually grey-green, with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

Helichrysum rhodellum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
