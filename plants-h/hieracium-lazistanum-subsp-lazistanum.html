Hieracium lazistanum subsp. lazistanum

Hieracium lazistanum subsp.

lazistanum, commonly known as the Lazistan hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to parts of the Caucasus region, including Georgia and Azerbaijan.

This subspecies is often found in meadows and dry grasslands, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
