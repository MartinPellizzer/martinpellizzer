Hedera helix f. cavendishii

Hedera helix f. cavendishii, commonly known as the Cavendish ivy, is a varietal form of the common English ivy.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves with a slightly wavy margin and a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically not showy, followed by orange berries. It is a climbing or trailing plant that thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various indoor and outdoor conditions.
