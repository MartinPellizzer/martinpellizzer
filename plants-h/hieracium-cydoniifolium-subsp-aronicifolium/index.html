Hieracium Cydoniifolium Subsp. Aronicifolium

Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp. aronicifolium is a lesser-known member of the dandelion family known for its striking yellow flowers and unique leaf structure. Found primarily in the mountainous regions of Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and dry habitats. Its name reflects its leaf shape, which resembles both cydonia fruit and a sharp leaf blade. Despite its beauty, it remains a rare and underappreciated species in botanical studies. Understanding this plant offers insights into the adaptability of flora in challenging environments.
