Hieraciodes ruprechtii

Hieraciodes ruprechtii, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer, and its leaves are typically narrow and hairy. Hieraciodes ruprechtii thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found in meadows and slopes at high elevations.

It is well adapted to cold climates and can withstand harsh weather conditions.
