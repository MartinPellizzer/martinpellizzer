Hieracium × obornyanum subsp. superpratense

Hieracium × obornyanum subsp.

superpratense, commonly known as the tall meadow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae). It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations across its distribution.
