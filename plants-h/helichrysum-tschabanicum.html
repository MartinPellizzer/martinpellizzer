Helichrysum tschabanicum

Helichrysum tschabanicum, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is aromatic and often used in traditional medicine.

It is also valued as a ornamental plant for its long-lasting, colorful blooms.
