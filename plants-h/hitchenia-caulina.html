Hitchenia caulina

Hitchenia caulina, commonly known as the hairy stalked orchid, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. Hitchenia caulina is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water.
