Haworthia mirabilis var. paradoxa

Haworthia mirabilis var. paradoxa, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, pale green leaves with distinctive dark green stripes that resemble a zebra's pattern. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
