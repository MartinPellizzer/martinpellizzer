Haworthia angustifolia var. altissima

Haworthia angustifolia var. altissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are narrow and pointed. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white to yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
