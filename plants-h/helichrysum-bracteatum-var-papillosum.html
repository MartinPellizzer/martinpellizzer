Helichrysum bracteatum var. papillosum

Helichrysum bracteatum var. papillosum, commonly known as the woolly helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and vibrant yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and attractive color.
