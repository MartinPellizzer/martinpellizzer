Hereroa willowmorensis

Hereroa willowmorensis, commonly known as the Willowmore hereroa, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. The plant produces delicate, pale yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
