Hieracium carcarophyllum

Hieracium carcarophyllum, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium carcarophyllum is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates.
