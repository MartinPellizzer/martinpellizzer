Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. subtenebrosum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

subtenebrosum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It is known for its delicate, translucent appearance, which gives it its common name.
