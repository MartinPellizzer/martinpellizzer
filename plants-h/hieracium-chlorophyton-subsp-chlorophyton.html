Hieracium chlorophyton subsp. chlorophyton

Hieracium chlorophyton subsp.

chlorophyton, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaf arrangement.

It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
