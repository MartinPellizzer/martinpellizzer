Hieracium atratum subsp. pannucium

Hieracium atratum subsp.

pannucium, commonly known as the black hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and produces showy, yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has a rosette of basal leaves and a flowering stem that can reach up to 30 cm in height.

The subspecies is distinguished by its darker coloration and specific leaf morphology compared to other Hieracium species.
