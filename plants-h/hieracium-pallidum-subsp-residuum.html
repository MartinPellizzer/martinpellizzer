Hieracium pallidum subsp. residuum

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

residuum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to whitish flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a taproot system and produces numerous flowering stems that bear small, clustered flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species in many regions.
