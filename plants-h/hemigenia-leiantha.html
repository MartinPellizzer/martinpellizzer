Hemigenia leiantha

Hemigenia leiantha, commonly known as the slender hemigenia, is a small shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually pink or violet in color, blooming in spring.

The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
