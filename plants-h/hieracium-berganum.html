Hieracium berganum

Hieracium berganum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense patches. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and somewhat hairy.

Hieracium berganum is known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it a common weed in various regions.
