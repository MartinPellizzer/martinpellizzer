Habenaria novemfida

Habenaria novemfida, commonly known as the nine-lobed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with six petals and a distinctive labellum. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is notable for its complex floral structure and ecological significance.
