Habenaria dentirostrata

Habenaria dentirostrata, commonly known as the toothed beak orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive elongated lip that resembles a beak. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a notable subject in both horticulture and ecological studies.
