Hypericum roraimense

Hypericum roraimense, commonly known as the Roraima goldenseal, is a perennial shrub native to the humid forests of northern South America.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its rarity and unique habitat, it is considered an important species for ecological studies in the region.
