Hebe rakaiensis

Hebe rakaiensis, commonly known as the Rakai hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its erect, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, attracting native pollinators. Hebe rakaiensis typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.
