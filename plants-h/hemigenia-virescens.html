Hemigenia virescens

Hemigenia virescens, commonly known as the green hemigenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forested areas. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive aromatic scent.

Hemigenia virescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
