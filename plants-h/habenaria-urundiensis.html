Habenaria urundiensis

Habenaria urundiensis, commonly known as the Urundi orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Africa, particularly in the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its terrestrial growth habit and rhizomatous structure. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern, typically featuring a greenish-yellow or brownish hue. Its floral structure includes a spurred lip, a distinguishing feature of many orchid species.

Habenaria urundiensis is adapted to grow in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.
