Hypericum vaniotii

Hypericum vaniotii, commonly known as the Vaniot's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes numerous species known for their yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum vaniotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
