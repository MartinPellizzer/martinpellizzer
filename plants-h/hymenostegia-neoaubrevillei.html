Hymenostegia neoaubrevillei

Hymenostegia neoaubrevillei, commonly known as the African velvet tamarind, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant has a dense, rounded crown and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and craftsmanship.
