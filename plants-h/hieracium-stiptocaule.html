Hieracium stiptocaule

Hieracium stiptocaule, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a single terminal head. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium stiptocaule is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates.
