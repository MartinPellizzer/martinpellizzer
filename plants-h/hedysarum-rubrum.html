Hedysarum rubrum

Hedysarum rubrum, commonly known as red vetch, is a flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in agricultural settings for its nitrogen-fixing abilities.

Hedysarum rubrum is also valued in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and blooms.
