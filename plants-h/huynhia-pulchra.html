Huynhia pulchra

Huynhia pulchra, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped flower structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

Huynhia pulchra is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
