Hemigraphis zwickeyae

Hemigraphis zwickeyae, commonly known as the Zwickie's iron plant, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and display a variety of colors, including shades of green, red, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Hemigraphis zwickeyae is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects and its use in traditional medicine in some cultures.
