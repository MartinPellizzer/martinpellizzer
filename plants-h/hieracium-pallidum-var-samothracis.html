Hieracium pallidum var. samothracis

Hieracium pallidum var. samothracis, commonly known as the Samothrace hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in certain parts of its native range.
