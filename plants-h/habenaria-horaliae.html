Habenaria horaliae

Habenaria horaliae, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid to semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapting to its harsh habitat.

The orchid's unique coloration and habitat make it a notable species in botanical studies.
