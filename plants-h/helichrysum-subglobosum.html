Helichrysum subglobosum

Helichrysum subglobosum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that resemble tiny suns. The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its long-lasting flowers make it a favorite in floral arrangements and dried flower crafts.
