Hemihabenaria radiata

Hemihabenaria radiata, commonly known as the radiate helleborine, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Hemihabenaria radiata is native to parts of Asia and is often found in regions with temperate climates.
