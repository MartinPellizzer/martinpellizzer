Huernia smithersii

Huernia smithersii, commonly known as the Smithers' horned orchid, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems covered in small, spiky tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers during the warm season, typically in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors.
