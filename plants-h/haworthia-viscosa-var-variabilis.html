Haworthia viscosa var. variabilis

Haworthia viscosa var. variabilis, commonly known as the variable Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
