Habenaria convallariifolia

Habenaria convallariifolia, commonly known as the ivy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that resemble those of ivy, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique and attractive addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
