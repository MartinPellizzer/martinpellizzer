Hieracium zonulatiforme

Hieracium zonulatiforme, commonly known as the zonular hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium zonulatiforme is also known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some regions.
