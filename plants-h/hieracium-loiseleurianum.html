Hieracium loiseleurianum

Hieracium loiseleurianum, commonly known as the Loiseleur's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are arranged in a single head, with ray florets that are often shorter than the central disk florets.

Hieracium loiseleurianum is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
