Hypoestes leptostegia

Hypoestes leptostegia, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with bright, showy flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoestes leptostegia is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
