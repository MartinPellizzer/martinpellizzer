Hieracium glaucinum var. pseudobrunelliforme

Hieracium glaucinum var. pseudobrunelliforme, commonly known as the glaucous hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers and hairy, often lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It is native to parts of Europe and has been introduced to other regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
