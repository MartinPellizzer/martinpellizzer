Hieracium retyezatense subsp. vrontousense

Hieracium retyezatense subsp.

vrontousense, commonly known as the Vrontous hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the island of Cyprus. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, displaying a low, spreading growth habit. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rough texture and serrated edges.
