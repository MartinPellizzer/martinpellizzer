Hieracium pilosella subsp. australe

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

australe, commonly known as the Australian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellowish to reddish flowers.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has small, alternate leaves and produces numerous small, dandelion-like flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different morphology compared to other members of the Hieracium genus.
