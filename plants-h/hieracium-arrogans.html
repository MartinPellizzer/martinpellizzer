Hieracium arrogans

Hieracium arrogans, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features upright stems with alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are hairy and serrated. Its flowers are yellow with a central disk, blooming from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium arrogans is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can spread aggressively through its rhizomes.
