Hemipogon laxifolius

Hemipogon laxifolius, commonly known as the slender-leafed hemipogon, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically herbaceous growth habit. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and it produces small, dry fruits known as cypsela.
