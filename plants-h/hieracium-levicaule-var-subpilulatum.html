Hieracium levicaule var. subpilulatum

Hieracium levicaule var. subpilulatum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with ray florets.

It is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes and is often found in temperate climates.
