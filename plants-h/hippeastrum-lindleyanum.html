Hippeastrum lindleyanum

Hippeastrum lindleyanum, commonly known as the Brazilian belladonna lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, with a distinctive bell-shaped form. The plant produces bulbous roots and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that emerge from the base of the plant.

Hippeastrum lindleyanum is a popular choice for gardeners due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
