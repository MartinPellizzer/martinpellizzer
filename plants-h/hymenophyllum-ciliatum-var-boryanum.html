Hymenophyllum ciliatum var. boryanum

Hymenophyllum ciliatum var. boryanum, commonly known as the Boryanum fern, is a small, epiphytic fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant has a unique structure with a short, creeping rhizome and densely packed, overlapping leaves. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it contributes to the biodiversity of forest ecosystems.
