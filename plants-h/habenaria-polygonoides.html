Habenaria polygonoides

Habenaria polygonoides, commonly known as the polygon-like orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Botanically, it is characterized by its unique floral structure and adaptation to humid, tropical climates.
