Hieracium tauschii subsp. brachysciadium

Hieracium tauschii subsp.

brachysciadium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow composite flowers that attract various pollinators.

The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as meadows and along streambanks. It has a fibrous root system and is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through stolons.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies due to its adaptability and role in plant succession.
