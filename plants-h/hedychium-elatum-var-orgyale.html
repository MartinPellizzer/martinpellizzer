Hedychium elatum var. orgyale

Hedychium elatum var. orgyale, commonly known as the Royal Flame ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a red throat, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade to full sun.

This ornamental plant is prized for its ornate flowers and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
