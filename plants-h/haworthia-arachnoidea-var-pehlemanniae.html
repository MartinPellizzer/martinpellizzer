Haworthia arachnoidea var. pehlemanniae

Haworthia arachnoidea var. pehlemanniae, commonly known as the spider haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves covered in a fine, white powdery coating called farina, which helps reduce water loss. The leaves are often adorned with translucent, spider-like markings, giving it its common name. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the cooler months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
