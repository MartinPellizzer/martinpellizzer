Hieracium atelodes

Hieracium atelodes, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Hieracium atelodes is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
