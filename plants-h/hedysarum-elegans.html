Hedysarum elegans

Hedysarum elegans, commonly known as the elegant vetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the western United States.

It is a perennial legume that produces clusters of pea-like flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and a trailing growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or open habitats.

Hedysarum elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
