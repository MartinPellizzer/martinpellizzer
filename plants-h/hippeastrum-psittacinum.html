Hippeastrum psittacinum

Hippeastrum psittacinum, commonly known as the golden arrow or yellow hurricane, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or orange stripe running down the center of the petals. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its strap-shaped leaves and robust, underground bulb. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hippeastrum psittacinum is relatively easy to grow, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
