Hieracium heterolepis

Hieracium heterolepis, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can form dense colonies. Hieracium heterolepis is an annual or biennial herb that plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
