Hieracium vennicontium

Hieracium vennicontium, commonly known as the deadly nightshade.

This plant is a flowering species belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its yellowish-orange flowers and rough, hairy leaves. It is native to certain regions of Europe and is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream sides. The plant contains toxic compounds, making it harmful to both humans and animals if ingested.

Its presence in the ecosystem can influence local biodiversity due to its role as a food source for specific insects.
