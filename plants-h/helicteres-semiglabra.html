Helicteres semiglabra

Helicteres semiglabra, commonly known as the velvetleaf or smoothleaf helicteres, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its palmately compound leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
