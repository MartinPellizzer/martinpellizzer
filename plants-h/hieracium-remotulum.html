Hieracium remotulum

Hieracium remotulum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture. It grows to a height of about 20 to 50 centimeters and has a creeping growth habit.

Hieracium remotulum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive spreading nature.
