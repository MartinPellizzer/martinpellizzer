Hypericum holtonii

Hypericum holtonii, commonly known as Holton's St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, typically growing in rocky or dry habitats. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly rough texture.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal properties.
