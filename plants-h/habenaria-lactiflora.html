Habenaria lactiflora

Habenaria lactiflora, commonly known as the milk orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories and has a rhizomatous root system. The plant produces pale green to white flowers with a distinctive, often fragrant, lip. Its leaves are usually linear and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is characterized by its unique floral structure and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
