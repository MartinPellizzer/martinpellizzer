Haworthia cymbiformis var. planifolia

Haworthia cymbiformis var. planifolia, commonly known as the flat-leaved Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, typically with a pale green to blueish hue and white to yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
