Hieracium echinodermum

Hieracium echinodermum, commonly known as the spiny hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features basal leaves that are typically lobed or dissected, and its stems are usually hairy. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its seeds are dispersed by wind.

Hieracium echinodermum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.
