Hieracium leucopterum var. stolonipes

Hieracium leucopterum var. stolonipes, commonly known as the white-winged hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette growth habit and distinctive white-pink flowers. The plant produces stolons, which allow it to spread vegetatively and form dense mats. Its leaves are typically basal, linear to lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.
