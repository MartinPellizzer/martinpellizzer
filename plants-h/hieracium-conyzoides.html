Hieracium conyzoides

Hieracium conyzoides, commonly known as the common groundsel, is a flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale green to brown ray florets. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It has a taproot system and produces achenes as its seeds.

Hieracium conyzoides is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.
