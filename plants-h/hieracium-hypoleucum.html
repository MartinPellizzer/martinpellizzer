Hieracium hypoleucum

Hieracium hypoleucum, commonly known as the white hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with white centers.

It is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
