Haworthiopsis coarctata

Haworthiopsis coarctata, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a whitish powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Haworthiopsis coarctata is popular as a low-maintenance houseplant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
