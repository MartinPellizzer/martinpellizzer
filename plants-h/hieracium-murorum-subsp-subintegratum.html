Hieracium murorum subsp. subintegratum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

subintegratum, commonly known as the smooth leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth margin, distinguishing it from other hawkweed species. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

This subspecies is known for its ability to spread through both vegetative growth and seed production.
