Hesperis lechleri

Hesperis lechleri, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and produces a milky sap that contains natural rubber. The plant is a member of the Malvaceae family, which includes other latex-producing plants. Its growth is typically found in humid, tropical climates with high rainfall.

Due to its latex content, it has been historically used in the production of rubber products.
