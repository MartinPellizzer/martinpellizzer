Hypolepis scabristipes

Hypolepis scabristipes, commonly known as the scaly shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Davalliaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The fronds are usually bipinnate, with a dense covering of small, scale-like structures on the underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
