Hieracium digenes

Hieracium digenes, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands across Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow daisy-like flowers with ray florets. It has a fibrous root system and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves.

Hieracium digenes is often found in disturbed soils and can be an indicator of nutrient-rich environments.
