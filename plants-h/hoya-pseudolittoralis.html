Hoya pseudolittoralis

Hoya pseudolittoralis, commonly known as the Shiny Waxplant, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hoya pseudolittoralis is popular among plant enthusiasts for its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
