Hybanthus velutinus

Hybanthus velutinus, commonly known as the velvetleaf hybanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Bindweed family (Convolvulaceae) and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. Hybanthus velutinus is often found in disturbed habitats and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain areas.
