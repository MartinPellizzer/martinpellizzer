Hypoxis volkmanniae

Hypoxis volkmanniae, commonly known as the Volkmann's yellowstar, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
