Hieracium incisum subsp. subpulchelliforme

Hieracium incisum subsp.

subpulchelliforme, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

This subspecies is distinguished by its smaller size and more delicate appearance compared to other hawkweed species.
