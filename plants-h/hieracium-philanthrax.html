Hieracium philanthrax

Hieracium philanthrax, commonly known as the deadly nightshade.

This plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, hairy stems and yellow to orange flowers. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. The plant contains toxic compounds, making it harmful to both humans and animals.

Despite its toxicity, it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for certain pollinators.
