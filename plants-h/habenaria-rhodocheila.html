Habenaria rhodocheila

Habenaria rhodocheila, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is an epiphytic orchid native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It features a dense, dark green to reddish-brown rhizome with numerous small, clustered roots. The plant produces a tall, erect stem bearing a spike of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that resemble a tresses of hair. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base of the stem.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of forests.
