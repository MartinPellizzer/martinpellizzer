Hibiscus bathiei

Hibiscus bathiei, commonly known as the Congo hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to central Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers have five distinct petals.

Hibiscus bathiei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
