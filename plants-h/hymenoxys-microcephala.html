Hymenoxys microcephala

Hymenoxys microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Hymenoxys microcephala typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
