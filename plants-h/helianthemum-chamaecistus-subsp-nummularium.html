Helianthemum chamaecistus subsp. nummularium

Helianthemum chamaecistus subsp.

nummularium, commonly known as the silver carpet cushion plant, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to its compact form. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
