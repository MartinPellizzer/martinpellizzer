Helleborine orbicularis

Helleborine orbicularis, commonly known as the broad-leaf helleborine, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and its striking, greenish-yellow flowers with purple markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and meadows. It is a perennial herb that produces its flowers in late spring to early summer.

The orchid is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its preference for well-drained, humus-rich soil.
