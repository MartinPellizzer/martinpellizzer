Hydrostachys longipoda

Hydrostachys longipoda, commonly known as the long-stemmed hydrostachy, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in dense spikes, and it produces small, round fruits.

Hydrostachys longipoda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
