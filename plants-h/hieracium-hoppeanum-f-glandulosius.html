Hieracium hoppeanum f. glandulosius

Hieracium hoppeanum f. glandulosius, commonly known as the glandular hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and yellow flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is found in temperate regions of Europe. It has a rosette growth habit with basal leaves that are often covered in fine, sticky hairs.
