Hypericum penthorodes f. lucidum

Hypericum penthorodes f. lucidum, commonly known as the glossy St. John's wort.

This variant is characterized by its smooth, shiny leaves that give it a distinct appearance compared to other forms of Hypericum. It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in well-drained, sunny soils. The plant produces small yellow flowers in late summer, which are followed by orange-red berries.

It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats across its native range in Europe.
