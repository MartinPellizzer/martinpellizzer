Haworthia reinwardtii subsp. coarctata

Haworthia reinwardtii subsp.

coarctata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings, giving it a striking appearance.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
