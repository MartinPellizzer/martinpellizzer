Habenaria mac-owaniana

Habenaria mac-owaniana, commonly known as the Mac-O-Wan orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial herb, often found in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with a distinctive, elongated spur.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated for horticultural purposes.
