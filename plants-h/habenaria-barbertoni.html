Habenaria barbertoni

Habenaria barbertoni, commonly known as the Barberton orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces long, slender spikes bearing multiple flowers.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
