Haworthia diversifolia

Haworthia diversifolia, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, fleshy, triangular leaves with white or yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
