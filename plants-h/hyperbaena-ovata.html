Hyperbaena ovata

Hyperbaena ovata, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces showy, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
