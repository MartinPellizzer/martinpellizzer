Hypericum brasiliense var. latifolium

Hypericum brasiliense var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf St. John's wort, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are broad and oval-shaped. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
