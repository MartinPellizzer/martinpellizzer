Hieracium Dolichocephalum

Hieracium dolichocephalum is a lesser-known species of hawkweed native to the mountainous regions of Europe. This flowering plant is characterized by its elongated, cylindrical flower heads and vibrant yellow petals. It thrives in rocky and acidic soils, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. Due to its rarity and specific environmental needs, it is considered a vulnerable species in many areas. Understanding and protecting this plant is essential for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
