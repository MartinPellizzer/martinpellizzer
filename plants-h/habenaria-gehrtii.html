Habenaria gehrtii

Habenaria gehrtii, commonly known as the Gehrtrian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with a distinctive spur, and it produces small, rounded seed pods.

Habenaria gehrtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
