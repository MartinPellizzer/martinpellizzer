Hieracium hoppeanum subsp. virentisquamum

Hieracium hoppeanum subsp.

virentisquamum, commonly known as the green-squamose hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are usually covered with dense, greenish-white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is a relatively small plant, rarely exceeding 30 cm in height.
