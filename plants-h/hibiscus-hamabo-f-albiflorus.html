Hibiscus hamabo f. albiflorus

Hibiscus hamabo f. albiflorus, commonly known as the white-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are predominantly white with a slight pink or red hue at the base. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces fruits that are capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hibiscus hamabo f. albiflorus is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
