Helia pulchra

Helia pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful helia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, bright yellow to orange ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Helia pulchra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
