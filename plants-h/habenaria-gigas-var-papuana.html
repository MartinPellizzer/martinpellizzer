Habenaria gigas var. papuana

Habenaria gigas var. papuana, commonly known as the Papuan giant orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Papua and nearby islands.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant produces intricate, fragrant blooms that attract various pollinators, including bees and moths. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

This orchid thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
