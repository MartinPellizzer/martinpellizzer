Huperzia × buttersii

Huperzia × buttersii, commonly known as the hybrid huperzia, is a fern-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Huperzia selago and Huperzia squamata. The plant exhibits a creeping growth habit with dark green, leathery foliage that is often covered in a dense layer of rhizomes. Its sporophylls are typically arranged in whorls and are characterized by their narrow, lance-shaped structure.

Huperzia × buttersii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices.
