Helleborus × lucidus

Helleborus × lucidus, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a hybrid evergreen perennial plant native to the Alps.

It produces striking, waxy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring, often around Christmas time. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that remain throughout the year, contributing to its evergreen nature. It thrives in cool, moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Helleborus × lucidus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom during the winter months, making it a unique addition to gardens.
