Hieracium orthophyes

Hieracium orthophyes, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium orthophyes is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
