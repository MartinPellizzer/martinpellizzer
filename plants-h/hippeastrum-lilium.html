Hippeastrum lilium

Hippeastrum lilium, commonly known as the Amazon lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of red, white, or pink. The plant features strap-like, sword-shaped leaves that emerge from a bulbous stem, and it thrives in warm, humid environments. Hippeastrum lilium is often cultivated for its dramatic blooms and is relatively easy to care for in a home garden.

It prefers well-draining soil and indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
