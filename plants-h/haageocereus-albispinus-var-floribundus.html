Haageocereus albispinus var. floribundus

Haageocereus albispinus var. floribundus, commonly known as the white-spined cactus, is a columnar cactus native to Mexico.

It features a tall, unbranched stem with distinctive white spines and a greyish-green color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun with well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
