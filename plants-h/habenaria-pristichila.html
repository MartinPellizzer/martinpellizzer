Habenaria pristichila

Habenaria pristichila, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet and green foliage, which features prominent veins. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats, such as forest undergrowth.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
