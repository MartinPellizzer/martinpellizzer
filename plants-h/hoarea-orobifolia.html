Hoarea orobifolia

Hoarea orobifolia, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Hoarea orobifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
