Hieracium rhombicum

Hieracium rhombicum, commonly known as the rhombic hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, with narrow, rhomboid-shaped leaves. It is a perennial herb that can grow up to 60 cm tall, with hairy stems and basal rosettes of leaves.

Hieracium rhombicum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
