Hovenia acerba var. acerba

Hovenia acerba var. acerba, commonly known as the Chinese date tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its small, yellowish-green flowers are followed by edible, fleshy fruits. The fruits, which resemble dates, are rich in sugars and have a sweet, sometimes tart flavor. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates.
