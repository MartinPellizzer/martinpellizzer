Hartogia craspedata

Hartogia craspedata, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant orchid flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Hartogia craspedata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
