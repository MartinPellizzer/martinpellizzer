Hymenocallis stenophylla

Hymenocallis stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, narrow leaf shape. The plant belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its tall, upright stems. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are narrow and often recurved.

Hymenocallis stenophylla is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
