Habenaria attenuata

Habenaria attenuata, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is an orchid species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping rhizomes and dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often arranged in a loose spike. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
