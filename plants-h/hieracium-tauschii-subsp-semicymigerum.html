Hieracium tauschii subsp. semicymigerum

Hieracium tauschii subsp.

semicymigerum, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its herbaceous growth habit and distinctive yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

This subspecies is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.
