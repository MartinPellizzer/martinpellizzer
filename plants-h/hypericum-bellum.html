Hypericum bellum

Hypericum bellum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and opposite, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and thrives in woodland or streamside habitats. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Hypericum bellum is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments, making it a common sight in damp, forested areas.
