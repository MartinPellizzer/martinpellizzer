Hypericum diosmoides

Hypericum diosmoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial herb native to regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers with four petals. Its stems are often reddish and covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum diosmoides is part of the genus Hypericum, which is known for its medicinal properties and ecological importance.
