Hieracium stenothyrsum

Hieracium stenothyrsum, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish to orange composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
