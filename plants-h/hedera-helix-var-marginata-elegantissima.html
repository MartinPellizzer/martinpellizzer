Hedera helix var. marginata-elegantissima

Hedera helix var. marginata-elegantissima, commonly known as the marginata-elegantissima ivy, is a cultivar of the common ivy plant.

It is characterized by its variegated leaves, which display white and yellow margins against a dark green background. This plant is a climbing or trailing evergreen that thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions. It is often used in hanging baskets, as a ground cover, or trained on walls and trellises.

The leaves are typically glossy and can grow up to 5 inches in length, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
