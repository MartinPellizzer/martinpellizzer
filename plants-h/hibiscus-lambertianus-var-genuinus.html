Hibiscus lambertianus var. genuinus

Hibiscus lambertianus var. genuinus, commonly known as the Lambert's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

The flowers bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
