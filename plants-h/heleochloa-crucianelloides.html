Heleochloa crucianelloides

Heleochloa crucianelloides, commonly known as the Crucianelloid grass, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies. Its flowering structures consist of small, spike-like inflorescences that produce tiny grains.

Heleochloa crucianelloides plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms in its native environments.
