Hieracium orariiceps

Hieracium orariiceps, commonly known as the whispering hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, typical of many hawkweed species. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
