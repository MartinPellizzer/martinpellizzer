Haworthia glauca f. jacobseniana

Haworthia glauca f. jacobseniana, commonly known as the Jacobsen's Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves with distinctive white or pale yellow markings. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
