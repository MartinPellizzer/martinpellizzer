Hieracium praeviride

Hieracium praeviride, commonly known as green hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers and basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils. It produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal, aiding in its spread.

Hieracium praeviride is typically perennial and can form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
