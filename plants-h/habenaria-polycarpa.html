Habenaria polycarpa

Habenaria polycarpa, commonly known as the many-fruited sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant produces multiple fruits, which are elongated and contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its complex floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
