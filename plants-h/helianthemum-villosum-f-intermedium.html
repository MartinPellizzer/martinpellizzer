Helianthemum villosum f. intermedium

Helianthemum villosum f. intermedium, commonly known as the Intermediate Woolly Sunrose, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, woolly foliage that is typically gray-green in color, providing a distinctive visual texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a cheerful touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in mild climates.
