Homalomena jacobsiana

Homalomena jacobsiana, commonly known as the Jacobs' arrowleaf.

It is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are usually bright green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its foliage. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
