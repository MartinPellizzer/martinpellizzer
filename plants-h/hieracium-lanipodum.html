Hieracium lanipodum

Hieracium lanipodum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, sometimes reddish stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Hieracium lanipodum is often found in temperate regions and is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
