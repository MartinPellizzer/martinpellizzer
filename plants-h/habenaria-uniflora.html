Habenaria uniflora

Habenaria uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia and parts of Australia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors. The plant has a slender, erect stem with a single, showy floral spike that bears a single, distinctive orchid flower. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its compact appearance.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
