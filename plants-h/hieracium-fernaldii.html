Hieracium fernaldii

Hieracium fernaldii, commonly known as Fernald's hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the United States, particularly found in the eastern and central regions. This plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and forest edges. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

The leaves are generally hairy and have a serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
