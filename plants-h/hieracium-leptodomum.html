Hieracium leptodomum

Hieracium leptodomum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain regions.
