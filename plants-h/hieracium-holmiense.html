Hieracium holmiense

Hieracium holmiense, commonly known as Holm's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium holmiense is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to colonize various habitats.
