Hieracium atratum subsp. stenodon

Hieracium atratum subsp.

stenodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, with a distinctive dark green color.

The flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
