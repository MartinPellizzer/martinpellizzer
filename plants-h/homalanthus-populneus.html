Homalanthus populneus

Homalanthus populneus, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences of red or orange flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also known for its rapid growth and ability to tolerate various soil types.
