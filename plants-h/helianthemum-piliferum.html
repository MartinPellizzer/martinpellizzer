Helianthemum piliferum

Helianthemum piliferum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous areas.

Helianthemum piliferum is well adapted to dry and sunny conditions, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
