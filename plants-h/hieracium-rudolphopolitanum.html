Hieracium rudolphopolitanum

Hieracium rudolphopolitanum, commonly known as Rudolph's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are generally hairy and alternate along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
