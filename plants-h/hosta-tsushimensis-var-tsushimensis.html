Hosta tsushimensis var. tsushimensis

Hosta tsushimensis var. tsushimensis, commonly known as the Tsushimen hosta, is a species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy, and often variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 18 inches and produces small, fragrant, white flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to garden conditions.
