Hieracium villosum var. intermedium

Hieracium villosum var. intermedium, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
