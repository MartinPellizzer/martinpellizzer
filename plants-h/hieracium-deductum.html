Hieracium deductum

Hieracium deductum, commonly known as the lesser spearthistle, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has hairy, upright stems and produces yellow flowers that are surrounded by leaf-like bracts. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

Hieracium deductum is often considered a weed but can provide nectar for pollinators.
