Hippeastrum mandonii

Hippeastrum mandonii, commonly known as the Mandon's amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South America, specifically Brazil.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive white or yellow center. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are broad and slightly wavy in texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to bloom successfully.

Hippeastrum mandonii is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
