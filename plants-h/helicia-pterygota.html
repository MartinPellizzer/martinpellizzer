Helicia pterygota

Helicia pterygota, commonly known as the winged helicia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its distinctive winged seeds, which are adapted for wind dispersal. Its flowers are small and yellowish, typically arranged in clusters, and it produces fruit that matures to a reddish color.

Helicia pterygota is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
