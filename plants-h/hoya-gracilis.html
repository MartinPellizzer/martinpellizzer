Hoya gracilis

Hoya gracilis, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, thin, waxy leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Hoya gracilis is a climbing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
