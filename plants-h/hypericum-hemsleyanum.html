Hypericum hemsleyanum

Hypericum hemsleyanum, commonly known as the Hemsley's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. Its botanical features include small, elliptical leaves and a distinctive, upright growth habit.

It is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
