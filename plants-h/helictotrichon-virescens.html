Helictotrichon virescens

Helictotrichon virescens, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and grasslands. It has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Blue grama is an important component of native grassland ecosystems and is often used in restoration projects.
