Habenaria hetaerioides

Habenaria hetaerioides, commonly known as the Hetaerioides orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually pale green or white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
