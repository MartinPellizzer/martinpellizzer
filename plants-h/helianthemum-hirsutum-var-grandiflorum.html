Helianthemum hirsutum var. grandiflorum

Helianthemum hirsutum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with silvery-gray foliage covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces showy, pink to reddish-orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
