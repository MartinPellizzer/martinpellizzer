Habenaria valida

Habenaria valida, commonly known as the showy lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and its distinctive inflorescence of small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes, forming colonies over time.

Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and flies.
