Hieracium visianii

Hieracium visianii, commonly known as the Visianii hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Hieracium visianii is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.
