Haworthia maraisii

Haworthia maraisii, commonly known as the Maraisii Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branching flower stalks that bear small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
