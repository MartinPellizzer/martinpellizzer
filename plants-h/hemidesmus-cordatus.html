Hemidesmus cordatus

Hemidesmus cordatus, commonly known as the heartleaf sida, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers. It is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to withstand saline conditions.

Hemidesmus cordatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
