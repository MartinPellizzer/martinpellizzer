Heteropterys Orinocensis Var. Eglandulosa

Orinoco heteropterys, scientifically known as Heteropterys orinocensis var. eglandulosa, is a rare and unique plant native to the Orinoco River basin in South America. This evergreen shrub is distinguished by its striking foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its ecological importance and ornamental value make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
