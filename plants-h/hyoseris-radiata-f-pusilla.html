Hyoseris radiata f. pusilla

Hyoseris radiata f. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf radiate hyoseris, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are aromatic and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
