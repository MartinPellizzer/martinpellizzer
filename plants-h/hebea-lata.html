Hebea lata

Hebea lata, commonly known as the slender hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of blue or purple during the spring and summer months. Hebea lata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
