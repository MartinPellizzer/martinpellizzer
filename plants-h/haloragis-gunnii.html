Haloragis gunnii

Haloragis gunnii, commonly known as the silverweed, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb with a low-growing habit, featuring dense, silver-gray foliage that reflects light. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Haloragis gunnii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
