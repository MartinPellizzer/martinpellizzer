Hieracium cineritium

Hieracium cineritium, commonly known as the cinerary hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy and somewhat sprawling.

The flowers are usually yellow or pale green, and they bloom from late spring to early autumn.
