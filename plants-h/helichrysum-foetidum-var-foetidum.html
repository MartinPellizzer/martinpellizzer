Helichrysum foetidum var. foetidum

Helichrysum foetidum var. foetidum, commonly known as stinking everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers that are often mistaken for daisies. The plant produces a strong, pungent odor, which is most noticeable when the flowers are in bloom. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
