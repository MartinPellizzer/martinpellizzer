Haworthia attenuata var. caespitosa

Haworthia attenuata var. caespitosa, commonly known as the compact Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with white markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
