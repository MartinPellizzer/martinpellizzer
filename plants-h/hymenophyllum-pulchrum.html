Hymenophyllum pulchrum

Hymenophyllum pulchrum, commonly known as the silver moss, is a delicate fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its small, intricate leaves that are typically less than 1 centimeter in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rock surfaces or tree trunks in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hymenophyllum pulchrum is a rare and specialized species, often requiring specific microclimatic conditions for successful growth.
