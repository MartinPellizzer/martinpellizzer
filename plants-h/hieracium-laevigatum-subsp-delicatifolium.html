Hieracium laevigatum subsp. delicatifolium

Hieracium laevigatum subsp.

delicatifolium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its finely divided, delicate leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.

Native to Europe, it often grows in meadows and grassy areas. The plant is a perennial herb with a creeping rootstock, and it produces numerous flowering stems.

Its botanical features make it an attractive but sometimes invasive species in certain regions.
