Hieracium tenuilingua

Hieracium tenuilingua, commonly known as slenderleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium tenuilingua is often found in temperate regions and is considered a common weed in some areas.
