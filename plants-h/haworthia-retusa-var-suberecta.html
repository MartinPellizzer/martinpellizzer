Haworthia retusa var. suberecta

Haworthia retusa var. suberecta, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, translucent, waxy leaves with dark green to black markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
