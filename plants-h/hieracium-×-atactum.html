Hieracium × atactum

Hieracium × atactum, commonly known as the hybrid hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish-orange composite flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant features basal and stem leaves that are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variability.
