Hieracium atrichocephalum

Hieracium atrichocephalum, commonly known as the blackhead hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black central flower head, which contrasts with its yellow ray florets. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides. It has rough, hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped.

Hieracium atrichocephalum is often found in temperate regions of Europe and is noted for its ornamental value in horticulture.
