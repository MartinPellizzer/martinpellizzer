Hypoxis milloides

Hypoxis milloides, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late summer, which are arranged in a star-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is widely grown in horticulture.
