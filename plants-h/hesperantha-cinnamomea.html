Hesperantha cinnamomea

Hesperantha cinnamomea, commonly known as the cinnamon lily, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a striking cinnamon-colored stripe down the center. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves that are dark green and strap-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hesperantha cinnamomea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
