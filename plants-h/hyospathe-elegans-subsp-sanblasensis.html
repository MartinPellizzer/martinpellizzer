Hyospathe elegans subsp. sanblasensis

Hyospathe elegans subsp.

sanblasensis, commonly known as the San Blas elegant hyoscyamus, is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae). It is native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the San Blas Islands of Panama.

This subspecies is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in sandy soils with ample sunlight.

It is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
