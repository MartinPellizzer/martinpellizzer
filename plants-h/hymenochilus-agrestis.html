Hymenochilus agrestis

Hymenochilus agrestis, commonly known as the rough-stemmed orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, hairy stems and clustered leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in loose inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
