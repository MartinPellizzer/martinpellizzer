Haworthiopsis variabilis

Haworthiopsis variabilis, commonly known as the Variegated Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth pattern. The plant features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often variegated with white, pink, or yellow markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Haworthiopsis variabilis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
