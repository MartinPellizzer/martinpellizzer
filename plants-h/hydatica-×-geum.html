Hydatica × geum

Hydatica × geum, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a perennial plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its fleshy, often succulent leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
