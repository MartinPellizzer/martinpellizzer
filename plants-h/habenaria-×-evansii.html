Habenaria × evansii

Habenaria × evansii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Habenaria uniflora and Habenaria radiata.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often among leaf litter. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
