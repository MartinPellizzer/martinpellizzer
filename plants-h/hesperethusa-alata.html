Hesperethusa alata

Hesperethusa alata, commonly known as the slender honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fragrant, tubular flowers are usually yellow or orange and bloom in late summer.

Hesperethusa alata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
