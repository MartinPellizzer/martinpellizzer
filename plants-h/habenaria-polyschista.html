Habenaria polyschista

Habenaria polyschista, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of pale green or yellowish flowers. Its roots are often mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitats.
