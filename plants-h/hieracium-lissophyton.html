Hieracium lissophyton

Hieracium lissophyton, commonly known as the smoothleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe, particularly in regions such as France, Italy, and Spain. Its flowers are typically yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium lissophyton is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
