Hyptis siderotricha

Hyptis siderotricha, commonly known as silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color.

Hyptis siderotricha is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its aromatic properties.
