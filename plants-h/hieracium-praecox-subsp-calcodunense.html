Hieracium praecox subsp. calcodunense

Hieracium praecox subsp.

calcodunense, commonly known as the early hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow ray florets and a central disk of tubular flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has hairy stems and basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

This subspecies is distinguished by its pale greenish-yellow flowers and is often found in calcareous soils.
