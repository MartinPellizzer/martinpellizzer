Hydrangea Macrophylla F. Cuspidata

Hydrangea macrophylla f. cuspidata is a striking variation of the common hydrangea known for its large, showy blooms. This cultivar is prized for its ability to change flower color based on soil pH, creating a dynamic display throughout the growing season. The pointed, cone-shaped bracts give it a unique appearance that sets it apart from other hydrangea varieties. It thrives in partial shade and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant foliage and flowers. Gardeners often choose this hydrangea for its ornamental value and adaptability to various landscapes.
