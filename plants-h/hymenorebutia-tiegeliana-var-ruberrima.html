Hymenorebutia tiegeliana var. ruberrima

Hymenorebutia tiegeliana var. ruberrima, commonly known as the red-flowered crown of thorns, is a compact cactus native to South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive red or pinkish hue, often adorned with spines. The plant produces small, vibrant orange flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
