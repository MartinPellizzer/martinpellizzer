Hieraciodes glomerata

Hieraciodes glomerata, commonly known as clustered rock-rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to withstand dry conditions.
