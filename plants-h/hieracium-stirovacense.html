Hieracium stirovacense

Hieracium stirovacense, commonly known as the Stirovac Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a cluster of small flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are often lobed and covered in fine hairs, and its yellow flowers bloom from June to August.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native habitat.
