Helmia trifoliata

Helmia trifoliata, commonly known as the three-leaf helmet, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shady areas.
