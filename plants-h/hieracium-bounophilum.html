Hieracium bounophilum

Hieracium bounophilum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in the summer, and the plant produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Hieracium bounophilum is often considered a weed due to its rapid spread and ability to colonize various habitats.
