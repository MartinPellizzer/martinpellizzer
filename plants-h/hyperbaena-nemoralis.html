Hyperbaena nemoralis

Hyperbaena nemoralis, commonly known as the common hyperbaena, is a small shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, capsule-like fruits. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hyperbaena nemoralis is often found in dry, open habitats such as scrublands and woodland edges.
