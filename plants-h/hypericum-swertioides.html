Hypericum swertioides

Hypericum swertioides, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum swertioides is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.
