Hieracium senecioides

Hieracium senecioides, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies. Its yellow flowers consist of numerous small florets surrounded by bracts, and it produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

Hieracium senecioides is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
