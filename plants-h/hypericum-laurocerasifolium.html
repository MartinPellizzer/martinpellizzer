Hypericum laurocerasifolium

Hypericum laurocerasifolium, commonly known as the evergreen St. John's wort, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant.
