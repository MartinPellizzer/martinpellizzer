Habenaria milnei

Habenaria milnei, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces a spike of small, white to pale pink flowers that are arranged in a loose raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
