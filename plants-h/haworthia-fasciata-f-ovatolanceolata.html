Haworthia fasciata f. ovatolanceolata

Haworthia fasciata f. ovatolanceolata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent belonging to the Asphodelaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact rosette of thick, fleshy leaves with distinct white or pale green stripes. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape, giving the plant its specific epithet. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for dry environments.

This cultivar is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
