Hypericum lalashanense

Hypericum lalashanense, commonly known as the Lalashan St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique habitat and potential medicinal properties.
