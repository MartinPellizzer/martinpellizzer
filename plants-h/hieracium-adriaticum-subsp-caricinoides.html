Hieracium adriaticum subsp. caricinoides

Hieracium adriaticum subsp.

caricinoides, commonly known as the Adriatic hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. It has basal leaves that are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs.

Its flowering period occurs from late spring to early summer, making it a notable presence in its native range.
