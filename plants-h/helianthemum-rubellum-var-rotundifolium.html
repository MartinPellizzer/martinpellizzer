Helianthemum rubellum var. rotundifolium

Helianthemum rubellum var. rotundifolium, commonly known as the roundleaf rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a hardy and ornamental choice for gardeners.
