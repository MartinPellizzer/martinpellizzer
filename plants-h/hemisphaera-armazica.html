Hemisphaera armazica

Hemisphaera armazica, commonly known as the Armazica hemisphere fern, is a unique member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive hemispherical shape, which is formed by the overlapping of its fronds. Native to the Andean region of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
