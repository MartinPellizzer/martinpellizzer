Hieracium peruanum

Hieracium peruanum, commonly known as the Peruvian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, mountainous areas and has hairy, alternate leaves. Its flowers are yellow to orange in color and are surrounded by bracts that give it a distinctive appearance.

Hieracium peruanum is often found in meadows and grasslands at high altitudes.
