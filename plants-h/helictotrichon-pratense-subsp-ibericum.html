Helictotrichon pratense subsp. ibericum

Helictotrichon pratense subsp.

ibericum, commonly known as the Iberian meadow grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its small, spike-like inflorescence bears tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

It is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and maintaining biodiversity in its native range.
