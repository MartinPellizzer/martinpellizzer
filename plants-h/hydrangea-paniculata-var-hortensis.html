Hydrangea paniculata var. hortensis

Hydrangea paniculata var. hortensis, commonly known as the smooth hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, cone-shaped flower clusters that range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes. Its leaves are large, ovate, and dark green, providing ornamental value throughout the growing season.

The hydrangea is also noted for its adaptability and ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the summer.
