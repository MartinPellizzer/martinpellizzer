Hemionitis lindigii

Hemionitis lindigii, commonly known as the Lindig's hemionitis, is a rare and unique fern native to the arid regions of North America.

Hemionitis lindigii is known for its ability to survive in harsh, dry conditions due to its specialized adaptations.
