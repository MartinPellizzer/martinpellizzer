Haworthia truncata var. tenuis

Haworthia truncata var. tenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow, bluish-green leaves with white to pale yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
