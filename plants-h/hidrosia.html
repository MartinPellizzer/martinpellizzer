Hidrosia

Hidrosia, commonly known as the water hyacinth, is an aquatic plant native to South America.

It has floating leaves that are broad and rounded, with a waxy coating that helps it stay afloat. The plant produces showy purple or blue flowers that bloom above the water surface. Hidrosia is known for its rapid growth rate, which can lead to dense populations in still or slow-moving water bodies.

Its roots are often used in traditional medicine and for water purification.
