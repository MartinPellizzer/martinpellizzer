Hibbertia tricornis

Hibbertia tricornis, commonly known as the three-lobed hibbertia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring woody stems and hairy leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive three-lobed shape, which bloom in spring. Hibbertia tricornis is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
