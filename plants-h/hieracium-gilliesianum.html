Hieracium gilliesianum

Hieracium gilliesianum, commonly known as the Gillies' hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clustered inflorescences. It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

Hieracium gilliesianum is known for its adaptability and is often found in disturbed habitats.
