Halimione

Halimione, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, pink or purple flower clusters that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in saline or brackish environments, such as coastal areas and salt marshes. Its succulent leaves are adapted to retain moisture in arid or salty conditions.

Halimione plays an important role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various marine and terrestrial species.
