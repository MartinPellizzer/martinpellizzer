Habenaria alagensis

Habenaria alagensis, commonly known as the Alagense orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces small, clustered pseudobulbs.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
