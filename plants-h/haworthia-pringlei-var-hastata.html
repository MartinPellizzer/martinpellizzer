Haworthia pringlei var. hastata

Haworthia pringlei var. hastata, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with white or light green markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, making it well-suited for arid environments. It produces small, white flowers on tall stalks during the cooler months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
