Helichrysum hypoleucum

Helichrysum hypoleucum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that retain their color when dried. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its aromatic properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its resilience and ornamental value have made it a favored plant in horticulture.
