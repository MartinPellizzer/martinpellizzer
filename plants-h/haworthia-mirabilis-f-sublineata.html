Haworthia mirabilis f. sublineata

Haworthia mirabilis f. sublineata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with white or silver markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique foliage, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
