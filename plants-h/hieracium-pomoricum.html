Hieracium pomoricum

Hieracium pomoricum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium pomoricum is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
