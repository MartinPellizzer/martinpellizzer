Hippeastrum herbertianum

Hippeastrum herbertianum, commonly known as the Herbertian amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that can be red, orange, or yellow, often with contrasting white or pink markings. The plant produces large, fleshy leaves that are typically broad and strap-like. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum herbertianum is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
