Hieracium sepositum

Hieracium sepositum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium sepositum is often found in open habitats and can spread through its rhizomatous growth system.
