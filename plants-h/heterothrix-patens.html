Heterothrix patens

Heterothrix patens, commonly known as the mountain morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of violet and blue. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its rapid growth habit. It thrives in humid, temperate climates and is often found in shaded forest understories.
