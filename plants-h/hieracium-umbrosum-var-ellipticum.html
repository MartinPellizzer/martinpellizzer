Hieracium umbrosum var. ellipticum

Hieracium umbrosum var. ellipticum, commonly known as the shadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features dark green, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ability to thrive in diverse soil conditions.
