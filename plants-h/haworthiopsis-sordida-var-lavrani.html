Haworthiopsis sordida var. lavrani

Haworthiopsis sordida var. lavrani, commonly known as the Lavran's Haworthiopsis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically a mix of green and grayish tones, with a textured surface. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks that bear small, white to pale yellow flowers. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
