Hubbardia heptaneuron

Hubbardia heptaneuron, commonly known as the giant rat's tail plant, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its long, trailing stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and ability to climb or trail make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and garden displays.
