Heliotropium portulacoides

Heliotropium portulacoides, commonly known as the silverleaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb with succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. Its root system is shallow, allowing it to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.

Heliotropium portulacoides is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or open habitats.
