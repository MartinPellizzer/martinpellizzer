Habenaria laxiflora

Habenaria laxiflora, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique and attractive species for horticultural interest.
