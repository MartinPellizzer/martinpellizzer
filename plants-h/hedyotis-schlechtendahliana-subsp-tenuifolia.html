Hedyotis schlechtendahliana subsp. tenuifolia

Hedyotis schlechtendahliana subsp.

tenuifolia, commonly known as slender leaf blushing geranium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America. It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with red or purple markings.

The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a soft, hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
