Hieracium grenierianum

Hieracium grenierianum, commonly known as Grenier's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are lance-shaped with rough textures.

Hieracium grenierianum is often found in open habitats and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain areas.
