Habenaria myriotricha var. confluens

Habenaria myriotricha var. confluens, commonly known as the clustered helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces a single spike bearing several flowers, each with a distinctive, elongated labellum. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforests or along creeklines.

The species is notable for its intricate floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
