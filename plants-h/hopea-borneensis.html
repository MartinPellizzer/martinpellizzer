Hopea borneensis

Hopea borneensis, commonly known as Bornean hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo, where it thrives in humid, warm climates. The tree can grow up to 40 meters tall and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, elliptical leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces woody fruits containing seeds.

Hopea borneensis is valued for its durable timber and plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
