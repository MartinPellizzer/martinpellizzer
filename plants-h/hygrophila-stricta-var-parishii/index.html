Hygrophila Stricta Var. Parishii

Hygrophila stricta var. parishii is a unique aquatic plant known for its striking appearance and adaptability. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments. This variant stands out with its dense, lance-shaped leaves and vibrant green coloration. It is popular among aquarium enthusiasts for its ease of care and aesthetic appeal. Despite its beauty, it requires proper water conditions to flourish fully.
