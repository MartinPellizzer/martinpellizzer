Hyospathe macrorhachis

Hyospathe macrorhachis, commonly known as the large-ribbed hyospathe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves with prominent veins, which are often a striking feature of the plant. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded, moist habitats.

Its botanical structure makes it a notable species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
