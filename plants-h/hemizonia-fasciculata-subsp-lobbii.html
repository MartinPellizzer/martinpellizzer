Hemizonia fasciculata subsp. lobbii

Hemizonia fasciculata subsp.

lobbii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hemizonia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base.

The plant produces delicate, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
