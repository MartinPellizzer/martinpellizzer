Habenaria nuttallii

Habenaria nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's butterfly orchid, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces showy, butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with a distinct lip that aids in pollination by insects.

The genus Habenaria is characterized by its intricate floral structures and specialized relationships with pollinators.
