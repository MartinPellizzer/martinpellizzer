Habenaria taiwaniana

Habenaria taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
