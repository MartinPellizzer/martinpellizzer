Hemigraphis hirsuta var. crenata

Hemigraphis hirsuta var. crenata, commonly known as silver mounding begonia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver or white underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for container gardening and ground cover.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a valuable addition to shaded garden spaces.
