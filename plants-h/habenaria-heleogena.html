Habenaria heleogena

Habenaria heleogena, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Habenaria, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with a single or few leaves, and it produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its flowers are characterized by their spurred lip, a distinctive feature in many orchid species.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
