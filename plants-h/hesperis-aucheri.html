Hesperis aucheri

Hesperis aucheri, commonly known as the hoary bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of bell-shaped, pale purple or white flowers. It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun.

Hesperis aucheri is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
