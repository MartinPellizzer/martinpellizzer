Hoarea lancifolia

Hoarea lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hoarea, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Hoarea lancifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
