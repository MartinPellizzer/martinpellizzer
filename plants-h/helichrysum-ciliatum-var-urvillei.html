Helichrysum ciliatum var. urvillei

Helichrysum ciliatum var. urvillei, commonly known as the Urville's everlasting flower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of yellow to golden-orange flowers that remain intact for extended periods, giving it its common name. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
