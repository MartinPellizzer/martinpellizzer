Hypoestes viguieri

Hypoestes viguieri, commonly known as the "Magic Blue" or "Blue Baby" plant, is a flowering shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking blue and white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Hypoestes viguieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
