Hatiora salicornoides f. stricta

Hatiora salicornoides f. stricta, commonly known as the compact holiday cactus, is a unique hybrid plant that exhibits a more upright growth habit compared to its typical spreading form.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is native to Brazil, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features short, thick stems covered in small, spiny tubercles, and its leaves are modified into fleshy, rounded structures. It produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the holiday season, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for indoor cultivation with proper light and watering conditions.
