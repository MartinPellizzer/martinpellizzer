Hieracium mattiroloanum

Hieracium mattiroloanum, commonly known as the Mattirolo's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, particularly Italy and the Balkans. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow flowers in the summer months. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
