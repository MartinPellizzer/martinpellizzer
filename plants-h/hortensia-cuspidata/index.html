Hortensia Cuspidata

Hortensia cuspidata is a striking member of the Hydrangea family known for its vibrant floral displays. This plant thrives in partial shade and moist soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates. Its unique cone-shaped blooms come in shades of pink, blue, and white, depending on soil pH. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators. With proper care, Hortensia cuspidata can provide seasonal color and beauty to any landscape.
