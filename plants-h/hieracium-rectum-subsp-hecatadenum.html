Hieracium rectum subsp. hecatadenum

Hieracium rectum subsp.

hecatadenum, commonly known as the six-rayed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow flowers with six ray florets, which give it its common name.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges.

This subspecies is native to Europe and parts of western Asia.
