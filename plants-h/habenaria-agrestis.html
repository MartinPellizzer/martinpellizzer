Habenaria agrestis

Habenaria agrestis, commonly known as the marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and along stream banks. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Habenaria agrestis is known for its ability to thrive in moist, nutrient-poor soils.
