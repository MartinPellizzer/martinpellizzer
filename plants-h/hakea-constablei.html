Hakea constablei

Hakea constablei, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces woody, cylindrical seed pods that remain on the plant for an extended period. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resistance to drought.

Hakea constablei is often found in heathlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
