Herminium carnosilabre

Herminium carnosilabre, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a species of orchid belonging to the genus Herminium.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green to reddish-brown stems and its clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually arranged in a tight spiral along the stem, and it produces a single, elongated spike of flowers.

This orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in the temperate regions of Europe and Asia.
