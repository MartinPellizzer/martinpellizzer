Hieracium epimedium subsp. subtenellipes

Hieracium epimedium subsp.

subtenellipes, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow and arranged in a clustered inflorescence.

It is known for its low-growing habit and is often found in disturbed or marginal habitats.
