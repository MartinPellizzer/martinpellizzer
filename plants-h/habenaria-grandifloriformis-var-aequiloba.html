Habenaria grandifloriformis var. aequiloba

Habenaria grandifloriformis var. aequiloba, commonly known as the broad-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and ecological role in its native habitat.
