Hosiea sinensis

Hosiea sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese lily, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm, with lance-shaped leaves arranged in a basal rosette. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with six petals and a prominent central stamen.

Hosiea sinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
