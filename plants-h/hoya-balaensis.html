Hoya balaensis

Hoya balaensis, commonly known as the Bornean Wax Plant, is a tropical climbing plant native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Hoya balaensis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
