Hieracium absonum

Hieracium absonum, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows up to 1.5 meters tall, with yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. Its leaves are broadly lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Hieracium absonum is known for its aggressive growth and can become invasive in certain regions.
