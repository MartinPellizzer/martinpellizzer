Hieracium sabaudum subsp. hostianum

Hieracium sabaudum subsp.

hostianum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has a fibrous root system and produces numerous flowering stems that bloom in late summer.

Its leaves are usually hairy and have a slightly rough texture.
