Hoya barbonii

Hoya barbonii, commonly known as the wax plant or the sweetheart plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Hoya barbonii is a slow-growing vine that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular among indoor gardeners for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
