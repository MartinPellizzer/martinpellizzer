Hieracium lanudae

Hieracium lanudae, commonly known as the Lanudae hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowering heads, which consist of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium lanudae is native to parts of Europe and is often found in regions with temperate climates.
