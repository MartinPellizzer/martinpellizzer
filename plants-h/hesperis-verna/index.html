Hesperis Verna

Hesperis verna, also known as the spring candytuft, is a delicate flowering plant that blooms in early spring. It is native to regions across Europe and parts of western Asia. This plant is known for its clusters of small, white flowers that resemble candy drops. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Hesperis verna is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
