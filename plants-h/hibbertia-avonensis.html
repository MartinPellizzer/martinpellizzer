Hibbertia avonensis

Hibbertia avonensis, commonly known as the Avon sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, ovate leaves.
