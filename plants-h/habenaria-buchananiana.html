Habenaria buchananiana

Habenaria buchananiana, commonly known as the Buchanan's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
