Heracleum ossethicum

Heracleum ossethicum, commonly known as the Ossethian cowparn, is a herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and large, compound leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in umbels, typical of the family, and its fruits are small, dry schizocarps.
