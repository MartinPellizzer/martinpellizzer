Hydnocarpus heterophyllus subsp. philippensis

Hydnocarpus heterophyllus subsp.

philippensis, commonly known as the Philippine ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dense, hard wood, which is highly valued for its durability and strength.

The plant has compound leaves with alternate arrangement and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

This species plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitats.
