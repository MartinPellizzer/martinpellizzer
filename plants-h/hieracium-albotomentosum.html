Hieracium albotomentosum

Hieracium albotomentosum, commonly known as the white-tomentose hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant features a hairy stem covered in white or grayish pubescence, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and also bear a dense covering of fine hairs.

It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn.
