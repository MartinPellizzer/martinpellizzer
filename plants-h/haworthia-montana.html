Haworthia montana

Haworthia montana, commonly known as the mountain haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, green leaves that are often marked with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Haworthia montana is prized for its compact size and unique appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
