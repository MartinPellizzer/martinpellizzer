Hieracium murorum subsp. daniciforme

Hieracium murorum subsp.

daniciforme, commonly known as the Danish hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosettes of narrow, gray-green leaves.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators. It is native to northern Europe and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
