Hydrangea formosana

Hydrangea formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan hydrangea, is a species native to Taiwan.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences composed of sterile flowers surrounded by fertile ones, which can range in color from white to pink or blue depending on soil pH. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth margin and a glossy green surface.

Hydrangea formosana thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in subtropical regions.
