Herpetophytum vonroemeri

Herpetophytum vonroemeri, commonly known as the Van Roemeri's grass.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to tropical Africa, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its inflorescence consists of dense clusters of small, spikelet-like flowers.

While not widely cultivated, it is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
