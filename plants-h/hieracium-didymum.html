Hieracium didymum

Hieracium didymum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to Europe, including parts of the United Kingdom. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Hieracium didymum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
