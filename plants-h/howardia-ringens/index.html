Howardia Ringens

Howardia ringens is a unique and striking plant known for its vivid red and green coloration. Native to southern Africa, this species thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water and withstand harsh conditions. The plant's distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, Howardia ringens remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
