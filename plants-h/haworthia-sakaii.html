Haworthia sakaii

Haworthia sakaii, commonly known as the Sakai's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Haworthia sakaii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
