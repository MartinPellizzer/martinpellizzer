Habenaria dolichoceras

Habenaria dolichoceras, commonly known as the long-headed marsh orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent, spurred lips. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as mountain meadows and forest floors.

Botanically, it is notable for its intricate floral structure and its role in local ecosystems.
