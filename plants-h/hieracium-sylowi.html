Hieracium sylowi

Hieracium sylowi, commonly known as the Sylow's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers, which are typically arranged in a single, showy head. The plant has a robust root system and hairy stems that help it thrive in various soil types. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Hieracium sylowi is known for its adaptability and can sometimes become invasive in certain environments.
