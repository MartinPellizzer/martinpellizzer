Hieracium amiculatum

Hieracium amiculatum, commonly known as the friendly dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into fluffy seed heads, which are dispersed by the wind. It has rough, hairy leaves and a short, sturdy stem.

Hieracium amiculatum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
