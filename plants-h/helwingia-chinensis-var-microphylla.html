Helwingia chinensis var. microphylla

Helwingia chinensis var. microphylla, commonly known as the Chinese helwingia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length. The plant produces delicate, pendulous flowers in clusters during the spring, which are usually white or pale yellow. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
