Helenium bigelovii var. festivum

Helenium bigelovii var. festivum, commonly known as the golden rod, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It features bright yellow to orange ray florets and dark brown disk flowers, typical of the Asteraceae family. The plant has narrow, upright leaves and produces showy composite flower heads that attract pollinators. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in dry grasslands and open woodlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native gardens and landscape designs.
