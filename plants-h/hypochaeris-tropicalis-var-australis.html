Hypochaeris tropicalis var. australis

Hypochaeris tropicalis var. australis, commonly known as the Australian false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant produces yellow flowers with a characteristic ray floret structure, similar to dandelions. Its leaves are usually alternate, lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a hardy and adaptable species.
