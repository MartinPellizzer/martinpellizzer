Hieracium crocanthes

Hieracium crocanthes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium crocanthes is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid spread and adaptability.
