Haworthia chloracantha var. zantneriana

Haworthia chloracantha var. zantneriana, commonly known as the Zantner's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration in arid environments. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers on tall, leafless stems during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
