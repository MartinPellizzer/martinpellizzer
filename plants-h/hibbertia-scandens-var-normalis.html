Hibbertia scandens var. normalis

Hibbertia scandens var. normalis, commonly known as the common hibbertia, is a climbing shrub native to Australia.

It features woody stems with thorny branches and heart-shaped, glossy green leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with red markings, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Hibbertia scandens var. normalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
