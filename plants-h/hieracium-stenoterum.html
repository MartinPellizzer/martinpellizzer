Hieracium stenoterum

Hieracium stenoterum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies. It features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, with yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Hieracium stenoterum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in agricultural and natural areas.
