Hypericum kohlianum

Hypericum kohlianum, commonly known as the Kashmir goldenclub, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, typical of many Hypericum species. It thrives in alpine and subalpine regions, often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
