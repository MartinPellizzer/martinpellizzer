Hieracium sarcophyllum

Hieracium sarcophyllum, commonly known as the purple meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium sarcophyllum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
