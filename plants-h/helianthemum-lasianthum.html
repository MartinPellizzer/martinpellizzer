Helianthemum lasianthum

Helianthemum lasianthum, commonly known as the woolly rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in spring, typically in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has hairy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, sunny conditions.
