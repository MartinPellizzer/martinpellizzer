Hypericum melanostictum

Hypericum melanostictum, commonly known as the black-stained St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers with prominent yellow stamens. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hypericum melanostictum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
