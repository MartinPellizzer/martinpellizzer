Hieracium waldsteinii subsp. lubokanum

Hieracium waldsteinii subsp.

lubokanum, commonly known as the Lubokanum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine, white hairs, giving them a fuzzy appearance. Its inflorescence consists of yellow disc flowers surrounded by pale yellow to orange ray florets.

This subspecies is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in high-altitude environments.
