Hieracium tauschii subsp. actinophorum

Hieracium tauschii subsp.

actinophorum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rough texture and hairy surfaces.

It is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain regions.
