Haworthiopsis scabra var. starkiana

Haworthiopsis scabra var. starkiana, commonly known as the Stark's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or gray markings. The leaves are arranged in a tight cluster, giving the plant a compact and attractive appearance. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
