Hieracium petrosum

Hieracium petrosum, commonly known as rock hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. The plant has rough, hairy leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine bristles. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Hieracium petrosum is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
