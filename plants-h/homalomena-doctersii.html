Homalomena doctersii

Homalomena doctersii, commonly known as the Doctersii arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
