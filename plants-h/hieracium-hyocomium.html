Hieracium hyocomium

Hieracium hyocomium, commonly known as the water dropwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, wetland habitats such as marshes and streambanks. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems rise above the foliage, bearing clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers.

Hieracium hyocomium is known for its ability to thrive in waterlogged soils and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
