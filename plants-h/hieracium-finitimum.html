Hieracium finitimum

Hieracium finitimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has linear to lanceolate leaves that are usually hairy, and it often grows in disturbed soils or open habitats. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
