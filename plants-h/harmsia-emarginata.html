Harmsia emarginata

Harmsia emarginata, commonly known as the pink ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Harmsia emarginata is drought-tolerant and is often used in rock gardens or as an ornamental plant.
