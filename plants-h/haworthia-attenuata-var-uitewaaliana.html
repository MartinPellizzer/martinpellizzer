Haworthia attenuata var. uitewaaliana

Haworthia attenuata var. uitewaaliana, commonly known as the Uitenhage Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
