Habenaria brevifolia

Habenaria brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and swampy areas. The plant features a slender, erect stem with few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in wetland environments.
