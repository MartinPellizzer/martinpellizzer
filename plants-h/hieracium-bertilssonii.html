Hieracium bertilssonii

Hieracium bertilssonii, commonly known as the Bertilsson's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flower heads and is native to specific regions in Europe. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native habitat.
