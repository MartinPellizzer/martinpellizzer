Helenium grandiflorum

Helenium grandiflorum, commonly known as the swamp sneezeweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features bright orange to red ray florets surrounded by yellow disk florets, giving it a striking appearance. The plant typically grows in wet or moist soils, often found in marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. Its flowering period occurs from late summer to early fall, and it is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions.

Helenium grandiflorum is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
