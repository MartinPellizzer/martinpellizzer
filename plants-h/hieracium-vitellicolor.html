Hieracium vitellicolor

Hieracium vitellicolor, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are hairy and upright.

Hieracium vitellicolor is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
