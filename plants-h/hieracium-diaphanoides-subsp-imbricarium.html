Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. imbricarium

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

imbricarium, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower.
