Habenaria × mixta

Habenaria × mixta, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Habenaria uniflora and Habenaria radicans.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical study.
