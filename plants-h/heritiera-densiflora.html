Heritiera densiflora

Heritiera densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered heritiera, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its woody structure and is typically found in moist, lowland areas of Southeast Asia. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Heritiera densiflora is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
