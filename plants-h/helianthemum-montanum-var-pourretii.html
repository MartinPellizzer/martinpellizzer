Helianthemum montanum var. pourretii

Helianthemum montanum var. pourretii, commonly known as the rock rose or mountain helianthemum, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in spring and early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to withstand arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
