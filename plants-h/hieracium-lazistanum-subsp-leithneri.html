Hieracium lazistanum subsp. leithneri

Hieracium lazistanum subsp.

leithneri, commonly known as the Leithner's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads that produce yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showing a preference for well-drained soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, hairy flowers that bloom in summer.
