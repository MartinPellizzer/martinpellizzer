Hieracium Racemosum Subsp. Athanasii

Hieracium racemosum subsp. athanasii is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the daisy family, Asteraceae, and is known for its delicate yellow blooms. This subspecies is often found in moist, calcareous habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered in many areas. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical species.
