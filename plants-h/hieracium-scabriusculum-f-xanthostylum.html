Hieracium scabriusculum f. xanthostylum

Hieracium scabriusculum f. xanthostylum, commonly known as the yellow-hairy hawkweed, is a variant of the hairy hawkweed species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine, stiff hairs.

This form is distinguished by its more yellow floral coloration compared to the typical greenish-yellow of the species.
