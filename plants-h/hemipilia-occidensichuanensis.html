Hemipilia occidensichuanensis

Hemipilia occidensichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan hemipilia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow blossoms clustered in racemes. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
