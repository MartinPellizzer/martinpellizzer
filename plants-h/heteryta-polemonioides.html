Heteryta polemonioides

Heteryta polemonioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has linear to lanceolate leaves that are usually hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Heteryta polemonioides is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
