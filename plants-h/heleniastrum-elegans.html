Heleniastrum elegans

Heleniastrum elegans, commonly known as the elegant helene, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. It blooms from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Heleniastrum elegans is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
