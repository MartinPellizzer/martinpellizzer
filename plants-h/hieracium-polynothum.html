Hieracium polynothum

Hieracium polynothum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or toothed. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, growing in meadows and grassy areas.
