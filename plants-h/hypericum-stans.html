Hypericum stans

Hypericum stans, commonly known as Stans' St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes numerous species known for their bright yellow flowers and medicinal properties. The plant features woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers, which bloom in summer, are typically bright yellow with five petals and numerous stamens.

Hypericum stans is often found in rocky or alpine habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential herbal uses.
