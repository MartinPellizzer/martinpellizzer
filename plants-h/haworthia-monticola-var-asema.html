Haworthia monticola var. asema

Haworthia monticola var. asema, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
