Hieracium pellucidum var. lucidulum

Hieracium pellucidum var. lucidulum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a rough texture, while the stems are upright and branching.

The species is noted for its small, clustered flowers that bloom in the summer months.
