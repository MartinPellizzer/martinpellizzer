Habenaria dinklagei

Habenaria dinklagei, commonly known as the Dinklage's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as forest floors or undergrowth. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from decomposing organic matter in the soil.

The flowers usually appear in clusters and can vary in color from white to pale pink, depending on the specific variety.
