Hovea nana

Hovea nana, commonly known as the dwarf hovea, is a small shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, with small, glossy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are borne in axillary inflorescences. Hovea nana is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and semi-arid environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
