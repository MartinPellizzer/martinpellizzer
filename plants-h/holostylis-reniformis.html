Holostylis reniformis

Holostylis reniformis, commonly known as the broad-leafed holostylis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its distinctive, broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. Holostylis reniformis is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
