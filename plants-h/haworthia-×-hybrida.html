Haworthia × hybrida

Haworthia × hybrida, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped foliage. The leaves are typically fleshy and have a distinctive zebra-like pattern of white or yellowish markings on a green background. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
