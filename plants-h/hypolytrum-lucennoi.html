Hypolytrum lucennoi

Hypolytrum lucennoi, commonly known as the golden star sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its triangular stems and grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. Hypolytrum lucennoi thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
