Hieracium cinerosum

Hieracium cinerosum, commonly known as smokeweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its grayish-green, hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, disturbed soils such as meadows and roadsides. The plant's common name derives from the smoky appearance of its foliage.

It is often found in temperate regions and is noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
