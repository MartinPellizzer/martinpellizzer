Hypoestes floribunda var. distans

Hypoestes floribunda var. distans, commonly known as the "Snowflake" plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, triangular, brightly colored leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This species is often grown as a houseplant due to its compact size and vibrant foliage.
