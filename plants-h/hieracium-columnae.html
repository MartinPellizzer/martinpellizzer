Hieracium columnae

Hieracium columnae, commonly known as the columnar hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, often with a central disc surrounded by ray florets.

Hieracium columnae is known for its robust growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
