Hypericum nakaii

Hypericum nakaii, commonly known as the Nakai's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features yellow flowers with prominent orange stamens, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Hypericum nakaii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
