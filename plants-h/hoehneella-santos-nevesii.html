Hoehneella santos-nevesii

Hoehneella santos-nevesii, commonly known as the Santos-Nevessia plant, is a rare and enigmatic species native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance. Hoehneella santos-nevesii is known for its limited distribution and is considered endangered due to habitat loss and fragmentation.
