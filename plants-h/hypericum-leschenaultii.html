Hypericum leschenaultii

Hypericum leschenaultii, commonly known as the Leschenault's St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.
