Habenaria avicula var. peruviana

Habenaria avicula var. peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian bird's beak orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers with a distinctive bird-like beak-shaped lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings.
