Hieracium paichei

Hieracium paichei, commonly known as the Paiche hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Brazil. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Hieracium paichei is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in disturbed habitats.
