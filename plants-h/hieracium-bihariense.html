Hieracium bihariense

Hieracium bihariense, commonly known as the Bhutan buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high elevations. It has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, daisy-like blooms.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
