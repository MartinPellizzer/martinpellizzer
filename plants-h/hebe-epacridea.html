Hebe epacridea

Hebe epacridea, commonly known as the slender hebe, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, often in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color, blooming from late winter to early spring. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Hebe epacridea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and visual interest.
