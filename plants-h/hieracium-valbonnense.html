Hieracium valbonnense

Hieracium valbonnense, commonly known as the Valbonnese hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in a flat head, often with a central brown disc.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its characteristic appearance.
