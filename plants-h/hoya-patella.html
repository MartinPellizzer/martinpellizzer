Hoya patella

Hoya patella, commonly known as the "Paddle Hoya," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink, emerging from the leaf axils. Hoya patella is a climbing or trailing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
