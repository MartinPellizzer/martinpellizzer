Hippochaete praealta var. pseudohyemalis

Hippochaete praealta var. pseudohyemalis, commonly known as the Christmas Star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in winter. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and has a shrub-like appearance. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a lush green backdrop to its seasonal blooms.

It is well adapted to full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
