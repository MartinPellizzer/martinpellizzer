Hydrangea hydrangeoides var. hydrangeoides

Hydrangea hydrangeoides var. hydrangeoides, commonly known as the Japanese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink or blue depending on soil pH. The plant has broad, serrated leaves that turn red in the fall. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
