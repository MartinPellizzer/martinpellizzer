Hedysarum creticum

Hedysarum creticum, commonly known as the Cretan vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb with slender, branching stems and compound leaves that are often trifoliate. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers in racemes, which bloom in early summer. Hedysarum creticum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
