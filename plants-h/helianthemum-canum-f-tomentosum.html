Helianthemum canum f. tomentosum

Helianthemum canum f. tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a low-growing, mat-forming shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has a compact growth habit with dense, hairy foliage that gives it a fuzzy appearance. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, white to grayish hairs, contributing to its distinctive look.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
