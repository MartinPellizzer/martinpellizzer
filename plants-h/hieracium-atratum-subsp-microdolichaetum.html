Hieracium atratum subsp. microdolichaetum

Hieracium atratum subsp.

microdolichaetum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and yellowish to orange flowers.
