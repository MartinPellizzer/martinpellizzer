Hieracium chalcidicum subsp. macropannosum

Hieracium chalcidicum subsp.

macropannosum, commonly known as the large-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its robust growth and distinctive, often hairy, dark green leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
