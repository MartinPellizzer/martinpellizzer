Hyptissa rosea

Hyptissa rosea, commonly known as the pink hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, narrow leaves and clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Hyptissa rosea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in some regions for its medicinal properties.
