Hippocrepis comosa var. rossetii

Hippocrepis comosa var. rossetii, commonly known as the Rossetti's helmet orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, particularly in Italy and parts of the Balkans. The plant features a compact, upright growth habit with dark green, lance-shaped leaves. Its most notable characteristic is the showy, helmet-shaped flowers that range in color from violet to deep purple.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
