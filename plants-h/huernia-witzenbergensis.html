Huernia witzenbergensis

Huernia witzenbergensis, commonly known as the Witzenberg moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its swollen, fleshy stems that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for collectors and cactus enthusiasts.
