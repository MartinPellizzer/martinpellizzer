Helianthemum morisianum

Helianthemum morisianum, commonly known as the Morisian rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Helianthemum morisianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
