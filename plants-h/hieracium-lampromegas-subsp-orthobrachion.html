Hieracium lampromegas subsp. orthobrachion

Hieracium lampromegas subsp.

orthobrachion, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrow, elongated leaves and yellowish to reddish flowers. It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
