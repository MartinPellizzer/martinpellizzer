Hieracium subramosum subsp. orarium

Hieracium subramosum subsp.

orarium, commonly known as the garden hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
