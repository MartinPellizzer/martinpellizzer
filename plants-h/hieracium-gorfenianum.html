Hieracium gorfenianum

Hieracium gorfenianum, commonly known as the Gorfenian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are usually hairy and covered in fine pubescence.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
