Habenaria eustachya

Habenaria eustachya, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swamps. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it has a rhizomatous growth habit.

Habenaria eustachya is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
