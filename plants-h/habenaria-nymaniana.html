Habenaria nymaniana

Habenaria nymaniana, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and ecological significance in its native environment.
