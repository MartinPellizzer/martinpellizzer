Hieracium armerioides subsp. ustulatum

Hieracium armerioides subsp.

ustulatum, commonly known as the spotted hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while the stem is usually hairy.
