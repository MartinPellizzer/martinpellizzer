Hypolepis thysanochlaena

Hypolepis thysanochlaena, commonly known as the golden sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated fronds that are typically bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies. Its spores are produced on the undersides of the fronds, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
