Hypericum madagascariense

Hypericum madagascariense, commonly known as the Madagascar goldenseal, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and woody stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, often in clusters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Hypericum madagascariense is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
