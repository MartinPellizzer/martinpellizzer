Hieracium calcareum subsp. calcareum

Hieracium calcareum subsp.

calcareum, commonly known as the limestone hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in calcareous soils, thriving in open, sunny environments such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant features upright stems with alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

It is known for its adaptability to alkaline conditions and is often found in disturbed or semi-natural habitats.
