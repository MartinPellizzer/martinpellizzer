Hoya tauensis

Hoya tauensis, commonly known as the silver star hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically variegated with shades of white, green, and pink. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Hoya tauensis is a trailing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
