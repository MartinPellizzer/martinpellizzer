Hoya sana

Hoya sana, commonly known as the wax plant or the porcelain flower, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant blossoms that are typically white or pink in color. Hoya sana is a climbing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular among indoor gardeners for its ease of care and ornamental beauty.
