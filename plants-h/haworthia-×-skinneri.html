Haworthia × skinneri

Haworthia × skinneri, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or yellow stripes. The plant belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is well adapted to arid environments. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
