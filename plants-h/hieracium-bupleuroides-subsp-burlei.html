Hieracium bupleuroides subsp. burlei

Hieracium bupleuroides subsp.

burlei, commonly known as Burle's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and flowering heads that produce small, yellowish to reddish flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in temperate regions. It has finely divided leaves and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
