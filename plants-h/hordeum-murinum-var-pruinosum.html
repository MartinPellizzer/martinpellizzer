Hordeum murinum var. pruinosum

Hordeum murinum var. pruinosum, commonly known as mouse barley, is a species of grass that belongs to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This variety is known for its adaptability to harsh conditions and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
