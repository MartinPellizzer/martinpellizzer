Homalomena succincta

Homalomena succincta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and have a waxy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Homalomena succincta is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
