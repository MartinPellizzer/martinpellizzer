Haworthia cooperi var. gordoniana

Haworthia cooperi var. gordoniana, commonly known as the Gordon's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or cream-colored markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, star-shaped white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
