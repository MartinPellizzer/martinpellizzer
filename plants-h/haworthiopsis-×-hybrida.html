Haworthiopsis × hybrida

Haworthiopsis × hybrida, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or yellow stripes. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
