Habenaria defleurii

Habenaria defleurii, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The flowers are pollinated by insects, particularly bees, and the plant is valued for its ornamental beauty.
