Hydrolea verticillata

Hydrolea verticillata, commonly known as the spiral hydrolea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Hydrolea verticillata is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
