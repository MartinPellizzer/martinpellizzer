Hypericum nepalense

Hypericum nepalense, commonly known as the Nepal goldenseal, is a perennial shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, which are clustered in cymes. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Hypericum nepalense is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
