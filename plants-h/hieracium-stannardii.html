Hieracium stannardii

Hieracium stannardii, commonly known as Stannard's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of western Europe, particularly in the Pyrenees and the Alps. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive lobed shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
