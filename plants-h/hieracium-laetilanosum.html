Hieracium laetilanosum

Hieracium laetilanosum, commonly known as the cheerful yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in the summer.
