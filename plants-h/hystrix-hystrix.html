Hystrix hystrix

Hystrix hystrix, commonly known as the porcupine or hedgehog plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiky foliage that resembles the quills of a porcupine. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Hystrix hystrix is known for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
