Helichrysum devium

Helichrysum devium, commonly known as the everlasting daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant is perennial and thrives in well-drained soils, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Its foliage is aromatic and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The flowers are known for their long-lasting nature, making them popular in floral arrangements.
