Haworthia tenera var. major

Haworthia tenera var. major, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped stem covered in striking, white to light green tubercles that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
