Hieracium levicaule subsp. sublucidum

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

sublucidum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to central and eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a prominent central disc.

It is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
