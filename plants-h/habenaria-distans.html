Habenaria distans

Habenaria distans, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is notable for its delicate appearance and adaptability to humid climates.
