Hieracium podolasium

Hieracium podolasium, commonly known as the Podolasian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium podolasium is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
