Hebe × leiosala

Hebe × leiosala, commonly known as the silver fern, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple or blue during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, Hebe × leiosala is widely cultivated in gardens across temperate regions.
