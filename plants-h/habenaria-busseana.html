Habenaria busseana

Habenaria busseana, commonly known as the Busse's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and along stream banks. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green to white in color. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern around the base of the plant.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with specific mycorrhizal fungi that aid in nutrient uptake.
