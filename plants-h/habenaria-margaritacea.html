Habenaria margaritacea

Habenaria margaritacea, commonly known as the pearl orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
