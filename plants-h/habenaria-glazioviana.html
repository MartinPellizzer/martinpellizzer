Habenaria glazioviana

Habenaria glazioviana, commonly known as the Glaziov's orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright, grass-like leaves and a single, showy flower spike. The plant produces small, pale yellow or greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with darker pigmentation. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The botanical features of Habenaria glazioviana make it a notable species within the Orchidaceae family.
