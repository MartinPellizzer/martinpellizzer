Hieracium arctogeton

Hieracium arctogeton, commonly known as the Arctic hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, hairy leaves that are often clustered at the base. It thrives in cold, dry environments and is adapted to survive in harsh alpine or tundra conditions.

Hieracium arctogeton plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats and is part of the Asteraceae family.
