Hypericum ledifolium

Hypericum ledifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
