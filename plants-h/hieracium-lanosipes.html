Hieracium lanosipes

Hieracium lanosipes, commonly known as the downy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, woolly appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the genus Hieracium. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium lanosipes is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its role in supporting pollinators.
