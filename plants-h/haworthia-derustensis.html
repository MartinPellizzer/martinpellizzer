Haworthia derustensis

Haworthia derustensis, commonly known as the Rusty Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive rust-colored band near the base. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
