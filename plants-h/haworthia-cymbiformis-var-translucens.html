Haworthia cymbiformis var. translucens

Haworthia cymbiformis var. translucens, commonly known as the translucent Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with translucent, window-like areas. The leaves are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on slender stems during the cooler months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
