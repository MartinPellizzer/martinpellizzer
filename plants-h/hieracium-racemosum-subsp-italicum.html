Hieracium racemosum subsp. italicum

Hieracium racemosum subsp.

italicum, commonly known as the Italian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. It has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The subspecies italicum is distinguished by its smaller flower heads and a more compact growth habit compared to other members of the species.
