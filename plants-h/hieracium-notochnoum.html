Hieracium notochnoum

Hieracium notochnoum, commonly known as the Southern Common Hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, blooming from late winter to early spring.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many regions of southern Australia.
