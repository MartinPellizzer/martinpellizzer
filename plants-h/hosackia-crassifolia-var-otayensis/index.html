Hosackia Crassifolia Var. Otayensis

Hosackia crassifolia var. otayensis is a rare and unique plant species native to the coastal regions of southern California. This variant of Hosackia crassifolia is distinguished by its thick, fleshy leaves and striking yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in coastal scrub habitats along the Otay and San Diego mountains. Due to habitat loss and climate change, this plant faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important species.
