Hypericum lythrifolium

Hypericum lythrifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features upright, branched stems covered in small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are opposite or alternate along the stem. Its flowering period typically occurs from June to August, and it thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

Hypericum lythrifolium is often found in dry meadows and rocky habitats.
