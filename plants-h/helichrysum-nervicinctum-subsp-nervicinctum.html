Helichrysum nervicinctum subsp. nervicinctum

Helichrysum nervicinctum subsp.

nervicinctum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf everlasting, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa. It features small, golden-yellow flowers that are borne in dense, compact clusters.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
