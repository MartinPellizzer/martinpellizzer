Hieracium derasum

Hieracium derasum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow flowers arranged in a single head. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered with glandular dots.

Hieracium derasum is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is often found in disturbed or nutrient-rich soils.
