Hydrangea peckinensis

Hydrangea peckinensis, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern China.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, blue, or purple depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth texture and a dark green color.

Hydrangea peckinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
