Habenaria strictissima

Habenaria strictissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, where it thrives in the undergrowth. The plant produces slender, erect stems that bear several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white to pale pink, and are arranged in a spike, often with a distinctive lip.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
