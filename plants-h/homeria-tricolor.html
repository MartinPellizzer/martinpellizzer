Homeria tricolor

Homeria tricolor, commonly known as the three-colored homeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is characterized by its succulent leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, yellow, and white, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique coloration and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
