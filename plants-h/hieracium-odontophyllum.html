Hieracium odontophyllum

Hieracium odontophyllum, commonly known as the toothed hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges, and the flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Hieracium odontophyllum is often found in open habitats and can sometimes be invasive in certain regions.
