Hieracium pichleri subsp. chromoneuron

Hieracium pichleri subsp.

chromoneuron, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its distinctive purple to reddish flower heads, which are composed of numerous small florets.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring well-drained soils and sunny exposures. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and hardy nature.
