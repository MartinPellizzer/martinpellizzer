Hartogia martiana

Hartogia martiana, commonly known as the Hartog's milkbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical, hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting well to poor soil conditions.

Hartogia martiana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
