Hippeastrum × griffinii

Hippeastrum × griffinii, commonly known as the red valentine.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to South America. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with contrasting streaks. The plant produces bulbous roots and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight.

It is popular as a decorative plant due to its striking blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
