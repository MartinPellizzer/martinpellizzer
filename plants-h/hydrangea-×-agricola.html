Hydrangea × agricola

Hydrangea × agricola, commonly known as the panicle hydrangea, is a hybrid shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, cone-shaped flower clusters that range in color from white to pink, red, or blue depending on soil pH. The plant thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes. Its foliage is typically dark green and provides attractive visual interest throughout the growing season.

Hydrangea × agricola is known for its hardiness and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
