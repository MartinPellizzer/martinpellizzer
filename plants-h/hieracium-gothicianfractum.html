Hieracium gothicianfractum

Hieracium gothicianfractum, commonly known as the Gothic Fractured Hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like composite flowers, which consist of a central disc surrounded by ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branched.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in certain regions.
