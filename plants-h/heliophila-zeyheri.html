Heliophila zeyheri

Heliophila zeyheri, commonly known as Zeyher's heliophyte, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Heliophila zeyheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in water gardens.
