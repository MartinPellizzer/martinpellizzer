Helichrysum albidobracteatum

Helichrysum albidobracteatum, commonly known as the white bract helichrysum, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, yellowish-orange flowers that are often covered in fine, hairy bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has aromatic, silver-gray foliage that is both drought-tolerant and heat-resistant. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms, which retain their color even when dried.
