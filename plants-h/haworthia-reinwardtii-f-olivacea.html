Haworthia reinwardtii f. olivacea

Haworthia reinwardtii f. olivacea, commonly known as the Olive Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically a striking olive-green color. The leaves are adorned with white to pale green markings, which give the plant its distinctive appearance. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance and unique aesthetic.
