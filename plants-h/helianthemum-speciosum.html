Helianthemum speciosum

Helianthemum speciosum, commonly known as the showy rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub with evergreen foliage and produces showy yellow or orange flowers in spring. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in rock gardens.

Helianthemum speciosum is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
