Hieracium interpositum

Hieracium interpositum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Hieracium interpositum is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense colonies, which can sometimes become invasive.
