Hieracium zollikoferi

Hieracium zollikoferi, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

Hieracium zollikoferi is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens that mimic alpine environments.
