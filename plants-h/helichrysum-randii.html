Helichrysum randii

Helichrysum randii, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, fluffy yellow flowers that resemble small daisies. The plant is evergreen, with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ability to retain color after drying has made it a favored plant for floral arrangements and crafts.
