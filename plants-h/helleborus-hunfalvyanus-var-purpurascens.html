Helleborus hunfalvyanus var. purpurascens

Helleborus hunfalvyanus var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered hellebore, is a perennial plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that are deeply lobed and glossy in appearance. Its flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring, are typically purple or reddish with a distinctive waxy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded woodland environments.
