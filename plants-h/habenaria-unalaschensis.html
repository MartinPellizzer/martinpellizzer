Habenaria unalaschensis

Habenaria unalaschensis, commonly known as the Alaskan marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the western United States and Canada.

It typically grows in wet meadows and marshy areas, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces tall, erect stems with several narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often with a faintly colored lip.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its specialized root system and mycorrhizal associations.
