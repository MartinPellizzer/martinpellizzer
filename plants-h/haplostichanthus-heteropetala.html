Haplostichanthus heteropetala

Haplostichanthus heteropetala, commonly known as the variable petal orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring petals of varying sizes and shapes. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, waxy coating.

The orchid produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually striking addition to botanical collections.
