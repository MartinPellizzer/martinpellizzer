Hystrix kunlunensis

Hystrix kunlunensis, commonly known as the Kunlun mountain hedgehog cactus, is a unique cactus species native to the arid regions of China.

It is characterized by its spiny, segmented stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in harsh desert environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a touch of color to its otherwise desolate habitat. Its adaptations include thick, waxy skin to reduce water loss and deep root systems for accessing underground moisture.

Hystrix kunlunensis plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing shelter and food for local wildlife.
