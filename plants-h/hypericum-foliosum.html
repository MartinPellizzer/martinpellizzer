Hypericum foliosum

Hypericum foliosum, commonly known as the leafy St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features woody stems covered in small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in woodland edges and open areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
