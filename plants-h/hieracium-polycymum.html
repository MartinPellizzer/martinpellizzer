Hieracium polycymum

Hieracium polycymum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium polycymum is often considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive growth.
