Hebe × erecta

Hebe × erecta, commonly known as the compact hebe, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the plantain family (Plantaginaceae) and is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit. The plant features small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round visual interest.

Hebe × erecta is well-suited for rock gardens and borders due to its compact size and hardy nature.
