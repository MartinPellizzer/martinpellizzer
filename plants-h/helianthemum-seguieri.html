Helianthemum seguieri

Helianthemum seguieri, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, blooming in the spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and grayish-green, adapted to thrive in rocky and well-drained soils.

Helianthemum seguieri is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
