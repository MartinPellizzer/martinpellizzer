Hippeastrum puniceum var. minus

Hippeastrum puniceum var. minus, commonly known as the miniature red amaryllis, is a compact variety of the amaryllis genus native to South America.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red and pink, often with a contrasting white throat. The plant produces broad, strap-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. It is a bulbous plant that thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering.

This variety is popular for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation indoors.
