Hypoxis laikipiensis

Hypoxis laikipiensis, commonly known as the Laikipia starflower, is a perennial herb native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hyacinthaceae and is characterized by its compact rosette of grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped white or yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
