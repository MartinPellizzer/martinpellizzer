Habenaria porphyricola

Habenaria porphyricola, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet and green foliage, with flowers that have a unique, intricate structure. The plant produces small, delicate orchid flowers that are often pink or purple in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
