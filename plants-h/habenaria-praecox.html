Habenaria praecox

Habenaria praecox, commonly known as the early spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces a single, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are distinctive, with a yellow-green lip and a striking purple and white dorsal sepal.

This orchid is known for its early blooming period, often appearing in late spring.
