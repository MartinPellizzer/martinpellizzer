Habenaria gonatosiphon

Habenaria gonatosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-leafed helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, flowering stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces striking, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
