Hieracium pallidum subsp. costeanum

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

costeanum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has alternate leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs.
