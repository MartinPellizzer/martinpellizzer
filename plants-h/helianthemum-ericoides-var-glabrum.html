Helianthemum ericoides var. glabrum

Helianthemum ericoides var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth heath sunflower, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compact, evergreen foliage with a leathery texture, and its stems are typically covered in fine, short hairs. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
