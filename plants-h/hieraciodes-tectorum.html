Hieraciodes tectorum

Hieraciodes tectorum, commonly known as the common rock rose, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early summer. The plant has a mat-forming habit with dense, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in mild climates.
