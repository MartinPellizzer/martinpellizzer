Hieracium peninnum

Hieracium peninnum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky soils at high altitudes. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces numerous small, herbaceous stems.

Hieracium peninnum is adapted to cold climates and is often found in areas with poor soil conditions.
