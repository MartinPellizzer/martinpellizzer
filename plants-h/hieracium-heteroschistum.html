Hieracium heteroschistum

Hieracium heteroschistum, commonly known as the variable hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and stems that produce numerous yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a composite head.

Hieracium heteroschistum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate regions.
