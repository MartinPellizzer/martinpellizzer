Hemitelia microphylla

Hemitelia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved hemitelia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.
