Homalomena corneri

Homalomena corneri, commonly known as the Red-veined Snake Plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves with prominent red veins. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter.

Homalomena corneri is well adapted to low-light conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.
