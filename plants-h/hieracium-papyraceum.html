Hieracium papyraceum

Hieracium papyraceum, commonly known as the paper dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.

Its common name comes from the papery texture of its leaves, which are often used in traditional herbal remedies.
