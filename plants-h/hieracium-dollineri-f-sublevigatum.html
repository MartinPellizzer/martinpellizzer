Hieracium dollineri f. sublevigatum

Hieracium dollineri f. sublevigatum, commonly known as the soft-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features finely hairy, ovate leaves that are generally softer than those of other hawkweed species. Its flowers are typically yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
