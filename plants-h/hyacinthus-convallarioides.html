Hyacinthus convallarioides

Hyacinthus convallarioides, commonly known as the sweet violet, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Violaceae.

It is characterized by its fragrant, violet-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.
