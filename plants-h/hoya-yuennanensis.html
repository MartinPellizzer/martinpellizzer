Hoya yuennanensis

Hoya yuennanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Wax Plant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, elliptical leaves. Hoya yuennanensis is popular among collectors for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
