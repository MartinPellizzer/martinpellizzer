Habenaria setacea var. ecalcarata

Habenaria setacea var. ecalcarata, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, and often curved floral spurs, which are a key botanical feature. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate petal structures.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
