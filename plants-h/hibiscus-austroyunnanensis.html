Hibiscus austroyunnanensis

Hibiscus austroyunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Hibiscus austroyunnanensis is a member of the Malvaceae family, which includes other hibiscus species.
