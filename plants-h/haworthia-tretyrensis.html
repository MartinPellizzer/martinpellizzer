Haworthia tretyrensis

Haworthia tretyrensis, commonly known as the Tretyrensis Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
