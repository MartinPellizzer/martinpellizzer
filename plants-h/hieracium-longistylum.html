Hieracium longistylum

Hieracium longistylum, commonly known as the long-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, and its leaves are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. Hieracium longistylum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in sunny conditions.

It is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
