Heteroneuron preslianum

Heteroneuron preslianum, commonly known as the Presleian starwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing herb with small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically arranged in a single umbel at the tip of the stem.

Heteroneuron preslianum is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
