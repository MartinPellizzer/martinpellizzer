Hyptis amethystoides

Hyptis amethystoides, commonly known as purple hyptis, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, violet or purple flowers. Its leaves are often aromatic and may have a mint-like scent.

Hyptis amethystoides is often used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and fragrance.
