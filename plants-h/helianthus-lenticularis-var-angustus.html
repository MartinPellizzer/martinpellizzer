Helianthus lenticularis var. angustus

Helianthus lenticularis var. angustus, commonly known as narrowleaf sunflower, is a perennial plant native to the central and eastern United States.

It features narrow, linear leaves and produces vibrant yellow flowers with a distinct central disk. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as prairies and roadsides. It has a taproot system that allows it to thrive in well-drained soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
