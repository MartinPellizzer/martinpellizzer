Helichrysum angustifolium

Helichrysum angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved immortelle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers that are often arranged in dense, compact clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Helichrysum angustifolium is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
