Hoplophytum purpureoroseum

Hoplophytum purpureoroseum, commonly known as the purple-rose gloxinia, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically purple or rose-colored, with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple, often resembling those of the gloxinia genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
