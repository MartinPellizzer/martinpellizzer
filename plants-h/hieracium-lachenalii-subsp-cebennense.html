Hieracium lachenalii subsp. cebennense

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

cebennense, commonly known as the Cebenna hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow flowering heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with ray florets that are often pale yellow.
