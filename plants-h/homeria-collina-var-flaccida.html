Homeria collina var. flaccida

Homeria collina var. flaccida, commonly known as the slender homeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
