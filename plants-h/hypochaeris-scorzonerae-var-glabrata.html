Hypochaeris scorzonerae var. glabrata

Hypochaeris scorzonerae var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is native to parts of Europe and has been introduced to other regions, including North America. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

The smooth, hairless stems and leaves distinguish it from other varieties within the Hypochaeris genus.
