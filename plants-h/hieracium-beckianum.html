Hieracium beckianum

Hieracium beckianum, commonly known as Beck's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Beck's hawkweed is an herbaceous perennial that plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
