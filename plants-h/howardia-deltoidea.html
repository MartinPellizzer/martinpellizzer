Howardia deltoidea

Howardia deltoidea, commonly known as the delta leaf cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded stem covered in small, spiny tubercles. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, round fruits. Its leaves are typically small and triangular, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
