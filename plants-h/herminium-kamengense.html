Herminium kamengense

Herminium kamengense, commonly known as the Kameng orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pale green to white flowers with prominent yellow sepals and petals. It typically blooms in late summer and is often found in moist, shaded forest habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
