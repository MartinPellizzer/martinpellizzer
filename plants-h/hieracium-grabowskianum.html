Hieracium grabowskianum

Hieracium grabowskianum, commonly known as the Grabowski's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
