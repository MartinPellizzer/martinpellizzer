Heisteria dumosa

Heisteria dumosa, commonly known as the hairy heister, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its stems are often prostrate or ascending, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
