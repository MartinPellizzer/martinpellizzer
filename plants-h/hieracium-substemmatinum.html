Hieracium substemmatinum

Hieracium substemmatinum, commonly known as the lesser spearthistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, hairy stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a composite head. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Due to its prickly nature, it can be challenging to handle without protection.
