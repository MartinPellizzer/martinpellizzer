Helianthemum laeve

Helianthemum laeve, commonly known as the smooth rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are covered in a waxy coating, helping it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Helianthemum laeve is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
