Hieracium amphitiltum

Hieracium amphitiltum, commonly known as the mountain hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

Hieracium amphitiltum is often found in open, rocky areas and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
