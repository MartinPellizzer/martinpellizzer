Habenaria dolichostachya

Habenaria dolichostachya, commonly known as the long-stalked marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to wetland habitats in tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a slender, upright stem with a single or few racemes of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system and possesses a pseudobulb, which stores water and nutrients. Its leaves are linear and often covered with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

This orchid thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to seasonal flooding.
