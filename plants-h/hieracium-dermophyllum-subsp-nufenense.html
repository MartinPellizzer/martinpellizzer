Hieracium dermophyllum subsp. nufenense

Hieracium dermophyllum subsp.

nufenense, commonly known as the Nufenen hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has a taproot system and is adapted to cold, harsh environments.

Its subspecies status highlights its distinct morphological and ecological traits compared to other Hieracium species.
