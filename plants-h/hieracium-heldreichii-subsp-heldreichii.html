Hieracium heldreichii subsp. heldreichii

Hieracium heldreichii subsp.

heldreichii, commonly known as the Heldreich's hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountains of the Balkans. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, and its flowering stems bear yellow flowers with a distinctive involucre.

This subspecies is notable for its horticultural value and adaptability to marginal conditions.
