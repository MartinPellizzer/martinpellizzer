Hieracium favrei

Hieracium favrei, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the Hieracium genus. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It blooms from late spring to early autumn, producing numerous small, yellow ray florets.
