Helichrysum pholidotum

Helichrysum pholidotum, commonly known as the golden crown, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even after drying, making it popular in floral arrangements. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Helichrysum pholidotum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine.
