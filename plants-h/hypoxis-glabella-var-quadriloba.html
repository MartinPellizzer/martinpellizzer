Hypoxis glabella var. quadriloba

Hypoxis glabella var. quadriloba, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellowish-white flowers in a dense, star-shaped cluster. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.
