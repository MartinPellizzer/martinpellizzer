Hippeastrum soratense

Hippeastrum soratense, commonly known as the Sorato lily, is a species of amaryllid native to South America, specifically Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green and can grow up to 1 meter in height. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum soratense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home garden.
