Helleborine ovata

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is native to parts of Europe and Asia.

Helleborine ovata is often found in woodlands and along stream banks, where it plays a role in supporting local biodiversity.
