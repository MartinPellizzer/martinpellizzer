Hydatica stellaris

Hydatica stellaris, commonly known as the star hydatica, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its star-shaped flowers, which typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Hydatica stellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
