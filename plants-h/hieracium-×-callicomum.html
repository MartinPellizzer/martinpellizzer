Hieracium × callicomum

Hieracium × callicomum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to various parts of the world, often growing in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in some regions, outcompeting native plant species.
