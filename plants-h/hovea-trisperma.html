Hovea trisperma

Hovea trisperma, commonly known as the three-seeded hovea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain three seeds, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Hovea trisperma is often found in open woodlands and is adapted to semi-arid environments.
