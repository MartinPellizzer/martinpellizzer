Hugonia oreogena

Hugonia oreogena, commonly known as the mountain hugonia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Lygophyllaceae and is characterized by its erect, slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
