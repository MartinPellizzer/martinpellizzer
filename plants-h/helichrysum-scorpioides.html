Helichrysum scorpioides

Helichrysum scorpioides, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, flat clusters. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

The dried flowers are also used to make tea and are valued for their ornamental and medicinal properties.
