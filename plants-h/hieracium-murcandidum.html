Hieracium murcandidum

Hieracium murcandidum, commonly known as the Murcandidum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. It produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium murcandidum is often found in open habitats and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain areas.
