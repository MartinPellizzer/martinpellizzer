Hypericum przewalskii

Hypericum przewalskii, commonly known as the Przewalski's St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to central Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in summer. Its botanical characteristics include small, elliptical leaves and a preference for well-drained soils.

It is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.
