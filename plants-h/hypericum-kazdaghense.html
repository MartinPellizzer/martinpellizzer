Hypericum kazdaghense

Hypericum kazdaghense, commonly known as the Kazdagh St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the high-altitude regions of eastern Turkey.

It belongs to the Hypericaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and potential medicinal properties.
