Hieracium catocerinthe

Hieracium catocerinthe, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Hieracium catocerinthe is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.
