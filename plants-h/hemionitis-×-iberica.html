Hemionitis × iberica

It belongs to the Hemionitidaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming structure with fleshy, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.
