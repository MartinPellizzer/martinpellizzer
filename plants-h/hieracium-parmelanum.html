Hieracium parmelanum

Hieracium parmelanum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and woodland edges. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs, while its stems are usually hairy as well.

Hieracium parmelanum is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, sometimes becoming invasive.
