Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. longilingua

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

longilingua, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is known for its delicate appearance and ecological adaptability.
