Hicorius diguetii

Hicorius diguetii, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the dense forests of Madagascar. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and specialized roots adapted for nutrient absorption.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened and is of significant interest to botanists and conservationists.
