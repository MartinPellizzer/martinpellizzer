Hieracium schmidtii subsp. argaeum

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

argaeum, commonly known as Schmidt's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
