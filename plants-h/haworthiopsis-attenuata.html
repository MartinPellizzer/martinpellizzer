Haworthiopsis attenuata

Haworthiopsis attenuata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with distinct white or yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall stems during the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal water, making it ideal for low-maintenance gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
