Hieracium pallidum subsp. brunelliforme

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

brunelliforme, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

It is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
