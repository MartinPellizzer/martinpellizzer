Hoya nicobarica

Hoya nicobarica, commonly known as the Nicobar Woundwort, is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is native to the Nicobar Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is a trailing plant with thick, fleshy stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a slight red or purple tinge. Hoya nicobarica is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among succulent enthusiasts.
