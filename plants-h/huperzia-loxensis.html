Huperzia loxensis

Huperzia loxensis, commonly known as the Loxense spike moss, is a species of fern-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and dense clusters of spore-bearing leaves, which are typically arranged in a spiral pattern. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including China, India, and Southeast Asia. Huperzia loxensis thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or on rocky outcrops.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
