Hieracium substellatum subsp. subsetibifidum

Hieracium substellatum subsp.

subsetibifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish-orange composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrow, linear leaves and reduced floral structure compared to other hawkweed species. It typically thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
