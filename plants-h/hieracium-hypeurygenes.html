Hieracium hypeurygenes

Hieracium hypeurygenes, commonly known as the purple meadowhawk, is a flowering plant belonging to the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple to pink flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows and grassy areas, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Hieracium hypeurygenes is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
