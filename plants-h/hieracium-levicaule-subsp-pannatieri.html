Hieracium levicaule subsp. pannatieri

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

pannatieri, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in moist meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.

The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while the upper leaves are smaller and more upright. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and the plant produces small, oval seeds.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
