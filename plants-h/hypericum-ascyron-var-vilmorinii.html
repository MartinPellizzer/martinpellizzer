Hypericum ascyron var. vilmorinii

Hypericum ascyron var. vilmorinii, commonly known as the Vilmorin's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late summer, which are composed of numerous tiny petals arranged in a cup-like structure. Its botanical characteristics include glandular dots on the leaves and stems, which are typical of the Hypericaceae family.

It is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and woodland edges.
