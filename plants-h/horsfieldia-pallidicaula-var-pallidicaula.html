Horsfieldia pallidicaula var. pallidicaula

Horsfieldia pallidicaula var. pallidicaula, commonly known as the pale-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizomatous structure. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to white flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical botanical gardens.
