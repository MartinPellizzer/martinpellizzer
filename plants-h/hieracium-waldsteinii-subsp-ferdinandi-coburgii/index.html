Hieracium Waldsteinii Subsp. Ferdinandi-Coburgii

Hieracium waldsteinii subsp. ferdinandi-coburgii is a rare and elegant flowering plant native to central and southern europe. it belongs to the daisy family and is known for its striking yellow blooms. this subspecies is often found in meadows and rocky areas where it thrives in well-drained soil. its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. protecting its habitat is crucial for preserving this beautiful and endangered plant.
