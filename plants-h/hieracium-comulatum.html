Hieracium comulatum

Hieracium comulatum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium comulatum is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to colonize new areas.
