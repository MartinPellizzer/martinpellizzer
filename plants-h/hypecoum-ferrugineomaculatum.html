Hypecoum ferrugineomaculatum

Hypecoum ferrugineomaculatum, commonly known as the rusty-spotted hellebore, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a short, thick rhizome and dark green, slightly hairy leaves. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers with a distinctive yellowish-green center. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
