Hosta minor f. albiflora

Hosta minor f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered woodland plant, is a cultivar of the hosta genus native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

This variety is prized for its unique floral display and adaptability to woodland gardens.
