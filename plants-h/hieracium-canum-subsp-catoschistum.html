Hieracium canum subsp. catoschistum

Hieracium canum subsp.

catoschistum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in dry meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate, hairy, and have a distinctive whitish or pale coloration, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
