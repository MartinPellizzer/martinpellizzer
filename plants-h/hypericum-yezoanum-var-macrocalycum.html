Hypericum yezoanum var. macrocalycum

Hypericum yezoanum var. macrocalycum, commonly known as the large-calyx St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Japan.

It features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy. The plant has woody stems that are often branched and covered in glandular dots. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to horticultural collections.
