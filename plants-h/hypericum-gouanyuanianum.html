Hypericum gouanyuanianum

Hypericum gouanyuanianum, commonly known as the Guangyuan St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Hypericaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
