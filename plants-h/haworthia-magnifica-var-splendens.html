Haworthia magnifica var. splendens

Haworthia magnifica var. splendens, commonly known as the splendens Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with distinct white or yellowish markings. The leaves are covered in fine, translucent tubercles that give the plant a unique, almost glassy appearance. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the warmer months.

This species is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and striking visual appeal.
