Hebeclinium macrophyllum

Hebeclinium macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant hebe, is a herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers in clusters during the spring and early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
