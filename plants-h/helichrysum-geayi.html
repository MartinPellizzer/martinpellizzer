Helichrysum geayi

Helichrysum geayi, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers that resemble small cups. The plant typically grows in shrubland and rocky areas, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil. Its foliage is usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small, seed-filled bracts that persist after flowering.

Helichrysum geayi is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
