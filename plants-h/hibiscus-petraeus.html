Hibiscus petraeus

Hibiscus petraeus, commonly known as the rock hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight. Hibiscus petraeus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance.

It also has cultural significance in various parts of its native range.
