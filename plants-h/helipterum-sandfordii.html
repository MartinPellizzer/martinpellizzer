Helipterum sandfordii

Helipterum sandfordii, commonly known as the sandford's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal regions.

Helipterum sandfordii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
