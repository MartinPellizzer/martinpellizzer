Helichrysum tomentosulum subsp. aromaticum

Helichrysum tomentosulum subsp.

aromaticum, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and bright yellow, dandelion-like flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.

Its foliage emits a subtle, aromatic scent, contributing to its unique botanical profile.
