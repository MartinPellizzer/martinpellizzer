Holographis pueblensis

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in its arid environment.
