Hieracium muretii subsp. ganderi

Hieracium muretii subsp.

ganderi, commonly known as the Gander's Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, often on rocky slopes and meadows.

The plant has basal and upright flowering stems that bear yellow flowers arranged in a single head. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its robust growth and adaptability to harsh environments.
