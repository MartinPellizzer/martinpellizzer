Hakea dielsiana

Hakea dielsiana, commonly known as the slender hakea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers in clusters, which appear in late summer to early autumn. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Hakea dielsiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
