Hedyotis cynanchica

Hedyotis cynanchica, commonly known as the Chinese knotweed, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hedyotis cynanchica is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
