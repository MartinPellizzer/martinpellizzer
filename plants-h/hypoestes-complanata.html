Hypoestes complanata

Hypoestes complanata, commonly known as the Marvelous Marvels or the African Violet.

This plant is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated leaves. It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in indirect light and well-draining soil. The leaves are typically broad and have a velvety texture, with patterns that can range from green with white veins to more elaborate combinations of colors.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
