Hieracium subcaesioides

Hieracium subcaesioides, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its flowers are usually yellow or pale orange, and it produces small, elongated achenes as its seeds.

Hieracium subcaesioides is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many regions.
