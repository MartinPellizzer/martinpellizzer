Hypoestes tubiflora

Hypoestes tubiflora, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and red. The leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, often with a slight waxy coating. It produces small, tubular flowers that are clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
