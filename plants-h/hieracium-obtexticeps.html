Hieracium obtexticeps

Hieracium obtexticeps, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering on the stems and leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. Native to Europe, it is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

It is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates.
