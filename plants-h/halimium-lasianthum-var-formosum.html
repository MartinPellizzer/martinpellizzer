Halimium lasianthum var. formosum

Halimium lasianthum var. formosum, commonly known as the pink sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It has small, linear leaves and a low-growing habit, often forming dense mats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive flowers.
