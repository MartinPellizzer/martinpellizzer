Hieracium kalsoense

Hieracium kalsoense, commonly known as the Kalsoen hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant features basal rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in a composite head. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
