Hymenocoleus globulifer

Hymenocoleus globulifer, commonly known as the globular cushion plant, is a small, mat-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
