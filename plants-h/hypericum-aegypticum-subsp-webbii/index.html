Hypericum Aegypticum Subsp. Webbii

Hypericum aegypticum subsp. webbii is a lesser-known member of the Hypericaceae family known for its unique botanical characteristics. This subspecies is native to specific regions in the Mediterranean, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its striking yellow flowers and medicinal properties have sparked interest among botanists and herbalists alike. Despite its potential, it remains under-researched compared to more common hypericum species. Understanding this subspecies could provide valuable insights into plant adaptation and traditional medicine.
