Haworthia calitzdorpensis

Haworthia calitzdorpensis, commonly known as the Calitzdorp Haworthia, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a small, succulent plant characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a white, waxy coating. The leaves are usually green with distinct white or pale markings, which help in reflecting sunlight and reducing water loss. It produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, and it thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.
