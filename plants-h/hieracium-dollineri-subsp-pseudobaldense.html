Hieracium dollineri subsp. pseudobaldense

Hieracium dollineri subsp.

pseudobaldense, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in harsh environmental conditions.

The plant has basal rosettes of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in mountain meadows and alpine tundra.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its habitat.
