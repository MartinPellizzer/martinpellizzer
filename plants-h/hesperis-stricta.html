Hesperis stricta

Hesperis stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf sweet rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early summer. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Hesperis stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
