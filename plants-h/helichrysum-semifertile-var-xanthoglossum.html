Helichrysum semifertile var. xanthoglossum

Helichrysum semifertile var. xanthoglossum, commonly known as the golden-leafed everlasting, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with bright yellow to gold-colored flowers that resemble small suns. The plant produces small, fibrous roots and has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often used in rock gardens or as a decorative plant.

The flowers are highly aromatic and can retain their color for extended periods after drying.
