Hieracium Subramosum Subsp. Rhynchellum

Hieracium subramosum subsp. rhynchellum is a rare and fascinating member of the dandelion family. It thrives in specific habitats, often found in rocky or alpine environments. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf structure and delicate yellow flowers. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native regions.
