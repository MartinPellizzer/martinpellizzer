Hypericum recognitum

Hypericum recognitum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has slender, branched stems covered in small, glandular hairs. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptic with a slightly toothed margin.

Hypericum recognitum is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and rocky soils, preferring partial to full sun.
