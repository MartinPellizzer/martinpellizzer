Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. vogesorum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

vogesorum, commonly known as the Voges hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has finely divided, grass-like leaves and produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by wind.
