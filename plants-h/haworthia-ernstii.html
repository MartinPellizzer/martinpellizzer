Haworthia ernstii

Haworthia ernstii, commonly known as the Ernest's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The leaves are often marked with distinct white or pale markings, which help in identifying the species. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers on tall stems, usually in shades of yellow or orange.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and minimal care requirements.
