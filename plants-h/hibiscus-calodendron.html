Hibiscus calodendron

Hibiscus calodendron, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy flowers with five petals that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm, arid climates.

Hibiscus calodendron is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
