Hypericum arborescens

Hypericum arborescens, commonly known as creeping St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum arborescens is often found in woodland areas and along the edges of forests.
