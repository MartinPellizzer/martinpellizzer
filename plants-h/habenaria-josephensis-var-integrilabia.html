Habenaria josephensis var. integrilabia

Habenaria josephensis var. integrilabia, commonly known as the Joseph's Habenaria, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale green to white, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown spots. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is relatively rare and is often found in montane forests.
