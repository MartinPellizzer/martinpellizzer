Hydrangea yunnanensis

Hydrangea yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hydrangea, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range from white to pink and blue depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with broad, serrated leaves. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Hydrangea yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
