Hieracium leptotephrum

Hieracium leptotephrum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

Its small, slender stems and hairy leaves are distinguishing characteristics.
