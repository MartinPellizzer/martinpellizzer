Helianthemum violaceum

Helianthemum violaceum, commonly known as the purple rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and vibrant purple or pink flowers. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its small, fragrant blooms appear in spring, adding color to gardens with its ornamental value.

Helianthemum violaceum is also valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand drought conditions.
