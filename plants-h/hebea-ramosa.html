Hebea ramosa

Hebea ramosa, commonly known as the slender hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and slender, branching stems. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the variety. Hebea ramosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage is typically gray-green, adding visual interest even when not in bloom.
