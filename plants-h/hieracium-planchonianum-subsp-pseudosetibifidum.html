Hieracium planchonianum subsp. pseudosetibifidum

Hieracium planchonianum subsp.

pseudosetibifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its clustered, yellowish to orange flowers.

The plant has basal leaves that are linear to narrowly lanceolate, often with a hairy texture. It typically grows in grassy habitats and is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the hawkweed group within the genus Hieracium.
