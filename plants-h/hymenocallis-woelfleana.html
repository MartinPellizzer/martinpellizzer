Hymenocallis woelfleana

Hymenocallis woelfleana, commonly known as the Woelflean spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous base, typical of many Amaryllidaceae species. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Hymenocallis woelfleana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
