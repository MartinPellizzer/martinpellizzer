Haworthia mundula var. calcarea

Haworthia mundula var. calcarea, commonly known as the calcarea haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy rosettes of pale green to grayish-blue leaves with white to pale yellow markings. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a potted plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
