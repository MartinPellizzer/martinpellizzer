Habenaria roseotincta

Habenaria roseotincta, commonly known as the pink-streaked orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers with distinctive streaks and spots. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it produces small, fragrant blossoms during the warmer months.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
