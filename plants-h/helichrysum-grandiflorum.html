Helichrysum grandiflorum

Helichrysum grandiflorum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets, giving it a daisy-like appearance. The plant has finely divided, silver-gray leaves that are aromatic and often used in herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum grandiflorum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and resilience in arid conditions.
