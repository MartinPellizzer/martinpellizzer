Hieracium angustatum subsp. metaliceps

Hieracium angustatum subsp.

metaliceps, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinctive, slightly serrated edge.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in certain environments.
