Helichrysum pomelianum

Helichrysum pomelianum, commonly known as the golden crown or African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces small, yellow-to-orange flowers resembling tiny sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

The plant's resilience and drought tolerance make it a popular choice in xeriscaping.
