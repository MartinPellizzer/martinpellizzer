Hieracium levicaule subsp. erubescens

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

erubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, while its stems are distinctly reddish in color.

It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
