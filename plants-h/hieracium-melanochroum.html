Hieracium melanochroum

Hieracium melanochroum, commonly known as the black-veined hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The leaves are usually alternate, with a rough texture and a dark green color. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and disturbed soils.

This plant is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
