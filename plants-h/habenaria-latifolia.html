Habenaria latifolia

Habenaria latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments and features linear to lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip petal. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
