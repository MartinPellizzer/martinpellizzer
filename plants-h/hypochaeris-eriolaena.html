Hypochaeris eriolaena

Hypochaeris eriolaena, commonly known as the common sowthistle, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of spoon-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa but has been naturalized in many parts of the world. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in fields, roadsides, and gardens.

Hypochaeris eriolaena is an annual or biennial plant that spreads through seeds dispersed by wind and animals.
