Helianthemum nummularium var. grandiflorum

Helianthemum nummularium var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered sandmat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white, woolly hair. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
