Hieracium carneum var. chihuahuense

Hieracium carneum var. chihuahuense, commonly known as the Chihuahua hedgehog.

This plant is a flowering species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico, particularly in the state of Chihuahua. It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
