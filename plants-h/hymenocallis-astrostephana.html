Hymenocallis astrostephana

Hymenocallis astrostephana, commonly known as the star-step lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a yellow center, which bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
