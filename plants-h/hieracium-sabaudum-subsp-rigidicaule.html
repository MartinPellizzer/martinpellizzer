Hieracium sabaudum subsp. rigidicaule

Hieracium sabaudum subsp.

rigidicaule, commonly known as the rigid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are rigid and often branched.

It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
