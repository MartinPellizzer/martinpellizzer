Hieracium petteri

Hieracium petteri, commonly known as the Petter's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are usually basal, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium petteri is often found in high-altitude meadows and is adapted to withstand cold and harsh climatic conditions.
