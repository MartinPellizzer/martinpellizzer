Habenaria macilenta

Habenaria macilenta, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, pale green or white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its inflorescence consists of a single spike bearing several flowers, which are often fragrant.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
