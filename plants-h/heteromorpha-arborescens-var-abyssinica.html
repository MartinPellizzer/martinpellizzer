Heteromorpha arborescens var. abyssinica

Heteromorpha arborescens var. abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian tree heath, is a shrub or small tree native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage.
