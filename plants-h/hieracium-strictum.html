Hieracium strictum

Hieracium strictum, commonly known as upright hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are broadly ovate to lance-shaped. Its flowers are yellow, with a distinctive central disk surrounded by ray florets, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

Hieracium strictum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many temperate regions.
