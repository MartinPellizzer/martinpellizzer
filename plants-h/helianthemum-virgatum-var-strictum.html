Helianthemum virgatum var. strictum

Helianthemum virgatum var. strictum, commonly known as the narrowleaf helianthemum, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has aromatic, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
