Hakea lissopleura

Hakea lissopleura, commonly known as the smooth-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces cylindrical, red or yellow flower spikes that bloom in spring. It is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water retention.

Hakea lissopleura is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
