Hieracium rostanii

Hieracium rostanii, commonly known as the Persian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to regions of Central Asia, including parts of Turkey, Iran, and Afghanistan. The plant features basal rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flower heads composed of numerous small florets. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often growing in mountainous areas.

Hieracium rostanii is characterized by its adaptability to harsh climates and its role in local ecosystems.
