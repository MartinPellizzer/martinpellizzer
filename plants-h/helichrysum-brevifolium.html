Helichrysum brevifolium

Helichrysum brevifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that are often arranged in dense, compact clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Helichrysum brevifolium is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting, dried flowers.
