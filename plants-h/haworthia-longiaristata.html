Haworthia longiaristata

Haworthia longiaristata, commonly known as the Long-leaf Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings, often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
