Haemanthus albiflos var. pubescens

Haemanthus albiflos var. pubescens, commonly known as the silver leaf haemanthus, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves covered in a dense layer of silvery-white hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces umbellate inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
