Hermesias grandiceps

Hermesias grandiceps, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often with a waxy texture.

Hermesias grandiceps thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
