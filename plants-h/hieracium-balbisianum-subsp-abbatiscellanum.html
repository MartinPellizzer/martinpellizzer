Hieracium balbisianum subsp. abbatiscellanum

Hieracium balbisianum subsp.

abbatiscellanum, commonly known as the Abbatiscellanum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and rocky areas, preferring temperate climates. It features yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and its leaves are usually basal with a rough texture.
