Hedypnois polymorpha var. hyoseris

Hedypnois polymorpha var. hyoseris, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and floral arrangements.
