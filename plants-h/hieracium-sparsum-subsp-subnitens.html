Hieracium sparsum subsp. subnitens

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

subnitens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
