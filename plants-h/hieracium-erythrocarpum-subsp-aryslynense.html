Hieracium erythrocarpum subsp. aryslynense

Hieracium erythrocarpum subsp.

aryslynense, commonly known as the Aryslyn yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It produces small, hairy, and often reddish-brown achenes that are dispersed by wind.
