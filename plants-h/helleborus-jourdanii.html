Helleborus jourdanii

Helleborus jourdanii, commonly known as the Corsican hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are typically lobed or notched. The plant produces showy, nodding flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Helleborus jourdanii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom during the dormant season.
