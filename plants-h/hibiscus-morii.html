Hibiscus morii

Hibiscus morii, commonly known as the Japanese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Hibiscus morii thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
