Hieracium subphrixocomum

Hieracium subphrixocomum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head.
