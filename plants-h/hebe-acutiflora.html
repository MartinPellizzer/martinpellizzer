Hebe acutiflora

Hebe acutiflora, commonly known as the sharp-flowered hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Hebe acutiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
