Hieracium lampromegas subsp. krivanense

Hieracium lampromegas subsp.

krivanense, commonly known as the Krivanense Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and is often found in regions with temperate climates.
