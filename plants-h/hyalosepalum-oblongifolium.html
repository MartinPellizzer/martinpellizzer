Hyalosepalum oblongifolium

Hyalosepalum oblongifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hyalosepalum, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Australia, specifically found in the southern regions of Western Australia. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and produces small, white or pale green flowers. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as shrublands and coastal areas.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
