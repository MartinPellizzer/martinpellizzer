Heteropterys pteropetala f. orbicularis

Heteropterys pteropetala f. orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular heteropterys, is a variant of the species Heteropterys pteropetala, characterized by its distinctive orbicular leaf shape.

This plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The leaves are typically a deep green, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface that aids in moisture retention. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to the plant.

Due to its unique foliage and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
