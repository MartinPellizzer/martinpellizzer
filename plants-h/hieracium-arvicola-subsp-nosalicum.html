Hieracium arvicola subsp. nosalicum

Hieracium arvicola subsp.

nosalicum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. It has a compact growth form and is often found in open meadows and slopes at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
