Haworthia schmidtiana

Haworthia schmidtiana, commonly known as the Schmidthaworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white tubercles and translucent windows, which aid in light absorption. The plant produces short, raceme-like inflorescences with pale yellow to white flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
