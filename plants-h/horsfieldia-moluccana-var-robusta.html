Horsfieldia moluccana var. robusta

Horsfieldia moluccana var. robusta, commonly known as the robust horsfieldia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. This orchid typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in inflorescences, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
