Helianthemum ventosum

Helianthemum ventosum, commonly known as the windy rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

Helianthemum ventosum thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and open landscapes.
