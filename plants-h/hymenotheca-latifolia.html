Hymenotheca latifolia

Hymenotheca latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf hymenotheca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

Hymenotheca latifolia is typically found in grassy areas and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
