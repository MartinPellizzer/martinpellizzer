Hieracium × rostafinskii

Hieracium × rostafinskii, commonly known as the hybrid meadowhawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosettes of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow to orange composite flowers, which are composed of numerous small florets arranged in a disk. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations.
