Haworthia koelmaniorum var. mcmurtryi

Haworthia koelmaniorum var. mcmurtryi, commonly known as the Koelman's Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, typically with a bluish-green hue and distinctive white or yellowish markings. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
