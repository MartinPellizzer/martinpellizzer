Hesperantha karooica

Hesperantha karooica, commonly known as the Karoo lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions. It produces a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and blooms during the winter months.

Hesperantha karooica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
