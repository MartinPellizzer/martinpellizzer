Hugonia brewerioides

Hugonia brewerioides, commonly known as the Mexican water hyacinth, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that float on the surface of water. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain aquatic ecosystems.
