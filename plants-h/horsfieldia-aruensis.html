Horsfieldia aruensis

Horsfieldia aruensis, commonly known as the Aru orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
