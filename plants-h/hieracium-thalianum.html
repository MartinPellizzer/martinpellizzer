Hieracium thalianum

Hieracium thalianum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, including North America. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium thalianum is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
