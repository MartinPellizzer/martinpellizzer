Habenaria hyperborea

Habenaria hyperborea, commonly known as the Arctic orchid, is a rare and cold-adapted species native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, nutrient-poor soils, often in tundra or alpine habitats. Its survival depends on specific environmental conditions, including short growing seasons and low temperatures.
