Habenaria floribunda

Habenaria floribunda, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on the forest floor or in rocky crevices. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces tall, slender flowering stems with numerous clustered flowers. Its inflorescence consists of showy, pale pink to white blooms with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
