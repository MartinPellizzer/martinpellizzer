Haworthia obtusa var. salina

Haworthia obtusa var. salina, commonly known as the salt haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
