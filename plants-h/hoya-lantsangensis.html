Hoya lantsangensis

Hoya lantsangensis, commonly known as the Lantsang hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, often variegated leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.

Hoya lantsangensis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
