Hieracium pseudosparsum

Hieracium pseudosparsum, commonly known as the South African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and has hairy stems and leaves. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in spring and are followed by fluffy seed heads that disperse easily.

It is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
