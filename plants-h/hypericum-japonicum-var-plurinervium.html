Hypericum japonicum var. plurinervium

Hypericum japonicum var. plurinervium, commonly known as Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional herbal medicine.
