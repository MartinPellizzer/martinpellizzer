Habenaria sagittifera var. linearifolia

Habenaria sagittifera var. linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arrowroot orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and produces pseudobulbs that bear leaves and flowers. The leaves are linear and often have a dark green color with a glossy surface. The inflorescence consists of a raceme of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip.

This orchid thrives in moist, shaded environments and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
