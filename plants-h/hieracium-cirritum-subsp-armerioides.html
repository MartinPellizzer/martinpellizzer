Hieracium cirritum subsp. armerioides

Hieracium cirritum subsp.

armerioides, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, yellowish-green flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender leaves and reduced flowering heads compared to other hawkweed species. It typically grows in moist meadows and grassy areas, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
