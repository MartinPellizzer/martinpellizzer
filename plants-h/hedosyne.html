Hedosyne

Hedosyne, commonly known as the mountain holly, is a shrub native to the temperate regions of the Americas.

It features small, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Hedosyne thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
