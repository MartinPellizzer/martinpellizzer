Haworthia calcarea

Haworthia calcarea, commonly known as the rock haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, pale green leaves with distinct white or pale markings. The plant produces tall, spindly flower stalks that bear small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
