Hypoxis boranensis

Hypoxis boranensis, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Hypoxis boranensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
