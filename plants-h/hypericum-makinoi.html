Hypericum makinoi

Hypericum makinoi, commonly known as Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include small, elliptical leaves and a preference for moist, well-drained soils.

It is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical medicinal uses.
