Habenaria uncinata

Habenaria uncinata, commonly known as the hooked orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive greenish-blue coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is notable for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
