Heracleum yunnanense

Heracleum yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan cowparn, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescence.

It is native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province, where it thrives in moist, temperate climates. The plant features large, compound leaves with a serrated margin and can grow up to several meters in height. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in umbrella-like clusters, and it produces oval, dark brown fruits.

Heracleum yunnanense is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
