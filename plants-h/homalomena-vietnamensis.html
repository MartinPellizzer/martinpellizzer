Homalomena vietnamensis

Homalomena vietnamensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese arrowhead plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and garden cultivation.
