Hypericum mutilum var. floribundum

Hypericum mutilum var. floribundum, commonly known as the clustered St. John's wort, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from June to August. The plant has narrow, opposite leaves that are slightly hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental landscaping.
