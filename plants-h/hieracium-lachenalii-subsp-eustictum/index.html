Hieracium Lachenalii Subsp. Eustictum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp. eustictum is a rare and delicate plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the sunflower family and is known for its striking yellow flowers. This subspecies thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
