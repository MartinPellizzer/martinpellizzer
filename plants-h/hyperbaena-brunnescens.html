Hyperbaena brunnescens

Hyperbaena brunnescens, commonly known as the brown bristled hyssop, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, hairy textures. Its flowers are clustered in whorls along the stems and are usually violet to purple in color.

Hyperbaena brunnescens is known for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
