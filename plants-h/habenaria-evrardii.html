Habenaria evrardii

Habenaria evrardii, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and North Africa.

It features a dense, mat-forming growth habit with long, slender stems that produce clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

The species is notable for its delicate appearance and ecological significance in its native habitats.
