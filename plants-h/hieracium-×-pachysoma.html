Hieracium × pachysoma

Hieracium × pachysoma, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a hybrid species within the genus Hieracium, which belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its robust growth habit and is often found in disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough, hairy texture.

Hieracium × pachysoma is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in agricultural and urban settings.
