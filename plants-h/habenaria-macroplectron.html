Habenaria macroplectron

Habenaria macroplectron, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in two rows along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

The orchid is notable for its intricate floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
