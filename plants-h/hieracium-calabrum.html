Hieracium calabrum

Hieracium calabrum, commonly known as Calabrian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, dandelion-like seed heads.

Hieracium calabrum is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates.
