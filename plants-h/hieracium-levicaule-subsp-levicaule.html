Hieracium levicaule subsp. levicaule

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

levicaule, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade.

The plant features upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and often have a reddish or purple tinge. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and are produced in late spring to early summer.

The species is characterized by its relatively small size and distinctive leaf morphology.
