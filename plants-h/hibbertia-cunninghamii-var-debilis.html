Hibbertia cunninghamii var. debilis

Hibbertia cunninghamii var. debilis, commonly known as the slender hibbertia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces yellow-orange flowers with five petals. The leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
