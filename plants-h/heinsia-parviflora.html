Heinsia parviflora

Heinsia parviflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal or mountainous regions. It produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer.
