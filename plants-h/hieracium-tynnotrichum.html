Hieracium tynnotrichum

Hieracium tynnotrichum, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rough texture.

Hieracium tynnotrichum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun.
