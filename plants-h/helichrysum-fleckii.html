Helichrysum fleckii

Helichrysum fleckii, commonly known as the yellow helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble small suns. The plant produces small, papery bracts that give it a distinctive appearance, often mistaken for actual petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum fleckii is also valued for its aromatic foliage and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
