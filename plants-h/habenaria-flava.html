Habenaria flava

Habenaria flava, commonly known as the yellow tailed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Habenaria flava is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
