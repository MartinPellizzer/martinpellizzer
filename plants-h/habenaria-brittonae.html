Habenaria brittonae

Habenaria brittonae, commonly known as the Britton's orchid, is a species native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are often pale green or white with a distinct lip, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This orchid is noted for its delicate appearance and ecological importance in its native habitat.
