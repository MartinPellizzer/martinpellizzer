Hieracium sulphureum subsp. rodnense

Hieracium sulphureum subsp.

rodnense, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow to orange flowering heads.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

The subspecies rodnense is distinguished by its slightly different floral morphology and geographic distribution compared to other subspecies of Hieracium sulphureum.
