Helicia uganensis

Helicia uganensis, commonly known as the Uganda holly.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of East Africa. The plant features glossy, elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along its branches. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, adding ornamental value.

Helicia uganensis is valued for its hardy nature and is often used in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
