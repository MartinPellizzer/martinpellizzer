Helichrysum bellidiastrum

Helichrysum bellidiastrum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant is perennial and thrives in well-drained soils, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and the flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters.

Helichrysum bellidiastrum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in dried flower arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
