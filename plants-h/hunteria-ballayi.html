Hunteria ballayi

Hunteria ballayi, commonly known as the golden torch cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its cylindrical stems and vibrant yellow flowers. Hunteria ballayi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
