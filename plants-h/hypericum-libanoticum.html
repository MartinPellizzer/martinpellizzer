Hypericum libanoticum

Hypericum libanoticum, commonly known as the Lebanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. Its botanical features include small, elliptical leaves and a compact inflorescence that bears multiple flowers.

Hypericum libanoticum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
