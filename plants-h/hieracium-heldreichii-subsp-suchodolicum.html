Hieracium heldreichii subsp. suchodolicum

Hieracium heldreichii subsp.

suchodolicum, commonly known as the Heldreich's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
