Hosackia Tomentella

Hosackia tomentella is a rare flowering plant native to the western United States. It is known for its delicate, woolly leaves and showy pink or purple flowers. This species thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open meadows. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and fragile species.
