Hieracium levicaule subsp. austericolor

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

austericolor, commonly known as the pale-centred hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and meadows.

The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clustered inflorescences. Its flowers are tubular with five lobes and are visited by various pollinators.

The subspecies is distinguished by its pale yellow flower centers and hairy foliage.
