Haworthia erii

Haworthia erii, commonly known as the spiral Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, pale green leaves with distinctive spiral patterns and translucent windows. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on a tall, slender stem during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
