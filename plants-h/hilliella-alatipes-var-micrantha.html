Hilliella alatipes var. micrantha

Hilliella alatipes var. micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hilliella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
