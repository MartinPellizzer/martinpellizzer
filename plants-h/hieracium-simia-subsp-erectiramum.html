Hieracium simia subsp. erectiramum

Hieracium simia subsp.

erectiramum, commonly known as the erect-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has rough, hairy stems and basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed.
