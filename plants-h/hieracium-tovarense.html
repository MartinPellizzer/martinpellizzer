Hieracium tovarense

Hieracium tovarense, commonly known as the Tovar's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces numerous yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has small, rough leaves and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

It is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
