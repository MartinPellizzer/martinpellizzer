Hedysarum cretaceum

Hedysarum cretaceum, commonly known as the Cretaceous vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny conditions. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three leaflets, and it has a distinctive, slender stem.

Hedysarum cretaceum is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.
