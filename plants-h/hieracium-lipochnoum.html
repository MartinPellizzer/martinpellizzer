Hieracium lipochnoum

Hieracium lipochnoum, commonly known as the lipochnoum hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

Hieracium lipochnoum is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, sometimes becoming invasive.
