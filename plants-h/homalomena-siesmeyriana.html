Homalomena siesmeyriana

Homalomena siesmeyriana, commonly known as the Siesmeyrian arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a waxy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical structure supports its role in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
