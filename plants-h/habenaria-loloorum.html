Habenaria loloorum

Habenaria loloorum, commonly known as the Lolo orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and grasslands. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with three petals and three sepals.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
