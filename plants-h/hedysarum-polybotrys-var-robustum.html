Hedysarum polybotrys var. robustum

Hedysarum polybotrys var. robustum, commonly known as the robust Chinese vetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in mountainous regions and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

It has been used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities.
