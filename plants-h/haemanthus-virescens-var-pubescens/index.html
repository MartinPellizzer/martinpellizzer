Haemanthus Virescens Var. Pubescens

Haemanthus virescens var. pubescens is a unique plant species known for its striking appearance and adaptability. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun conditions. This variant is distinguished by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive texture. Its blooming period typically occurs in late winter or early spring, producing clusters of bright red flowers. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles, making it a rare find for plant enthusiasts.
