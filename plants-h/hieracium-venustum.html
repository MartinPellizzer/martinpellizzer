Hieracium venustum

Hieracium venustum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a single, showy head. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grasslands.

It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, making it both ornamental and potentially invasive in certain regions.
