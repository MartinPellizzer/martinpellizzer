Hedyotis rubra

Hedyotis rubra, commonly known as red flame or red tamarisk, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, spreading stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along roadsides.

Hedyotis rubra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
