Hieracium lamprochaetum

Hieracium lamprochaetum, commonly known as the silver-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it thrives in temperate climates. The flowers are yellow and are surrounded by a ring of pale green to white ray florets.
