Hieracium barbelliceps

Hieracium barbelliceps, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and open woodlands.
