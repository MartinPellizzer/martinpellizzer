Hieracium schemachense

Hieracium schemachense, commonly known as the Schemach's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are yellow, with a central disc surrounded by petal-like bracts, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
