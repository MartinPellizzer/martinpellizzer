Haworthia coarctata

Haworthia coarctata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, pointed leaves that are often marked with white or yellow stripes, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant is relatively small, typically growing to a height of about 10-15 centimeters. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Haworthia coarctata is known for its ability to store water in its leaves, allowing it to survive in arid conditions.
