Hypoxis ledermannii

Hypoxis ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's yellow star, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its dense, yellowish-white flower clusters that resemble stars. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are basal, narrow, and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are short and fibrous.

Hypoxis ledermannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
