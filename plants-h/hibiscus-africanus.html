Hibiscus africanus

Hibiscus africanus, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, sunny climates. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and attractive foliage.

Hibiscus africanus is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
