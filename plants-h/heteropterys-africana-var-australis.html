Heteropterys africana var. australis

Heteropterys africana var. australis, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are typically large and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by distinctive, elongated seed pods. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of African flora.
