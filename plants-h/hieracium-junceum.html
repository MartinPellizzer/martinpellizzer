Hieracium junceum

Hieracium junceum, commonly known as slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture. It is characterized by its creeping growth habit and ability to form dense mats.

Hieracium junceum is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
