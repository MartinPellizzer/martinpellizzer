Hibiscus diversifolius var. granatensis

Hibiscus diversifolius var. granatensis, commonly known as the granate hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are palmately lobed, with a waxy texture and a deep green color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
