Habenaria cornuta

Habenaria cornuta, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, horn-like spur that extends from the base of its flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streamside areas. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a sweet, vanilla-like fragrance.

Habenaria cornuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
