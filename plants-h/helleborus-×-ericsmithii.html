Helleborus × ericsmithii

Helleborus × ericsmithii, commonly known as Eric Smith's hellebore, is a hybrid evergreen perennial plant native to the United Kingdom.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, leathery foliage and striking, waxy flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments and has a long blooming period from late winter to early spring.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and as a shade-tolerant ornamental plant.
