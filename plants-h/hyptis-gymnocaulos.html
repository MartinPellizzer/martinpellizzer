Hyptis gymnocaulos

Hyptis gymnocaulos, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed hyptis, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, hairless stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the branches.

Hyptis gymnocaulos is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.
