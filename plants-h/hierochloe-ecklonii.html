Hierochloe ecklonii

Hierochloe ecklonii, commonly known as the Cape grass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and have a distinctive blue-green color.

Hierochloe ecklonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
