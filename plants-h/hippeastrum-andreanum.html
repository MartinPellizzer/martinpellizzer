Hippeastrum andreanum

Hippeastrum andreanum, commonly known as the red hurricane lily, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red flowers with yellow centers. The plant produces large, showy blooms that typically appear in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, emerging from a bulbous stem.

Hippeastrum andreanum is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
