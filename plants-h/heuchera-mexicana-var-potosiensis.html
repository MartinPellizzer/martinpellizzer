Heuchera mexicana var. potosiensis

Heuchera mexicana var. potosiensis, commonly known as the Potosi heuchera, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils. The plant features striking foliage with deep green, red, or purple leaves, often variegated in color. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to shaded environments.
