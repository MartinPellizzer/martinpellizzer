Hieracium bifidum subsp. vallense

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

vallense, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to mountainous regions of Europe. It typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments.

The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers with a distinctive petal arrangement. It is characterized by its hairy stems and is often found in high-altitude ecosystems.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
