Habenaria stolzii

Habenaria stolzii, commonly known as the Stolz's orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland or shaded garden environments. Its roots are often mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
