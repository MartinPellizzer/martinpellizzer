Hieracium kennethii

Hieracium kennethii, commonly known as the Kenneth's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Hieracium kennethii is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions and plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
