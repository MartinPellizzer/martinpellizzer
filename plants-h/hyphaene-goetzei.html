Hyphaene goetzei

Hyphaene goetzei, commonly known as the African palm or the giant hyphaene, is a tall, slender palm native to southern Africa.

It features a straight, unbranched trunk that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant produces large, fan-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and thatching. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces edible fruits that are consumed by both humans and animals.

The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it an important species in its native ecosystems.
