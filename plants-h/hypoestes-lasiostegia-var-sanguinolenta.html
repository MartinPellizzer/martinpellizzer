Hypoestes lasiostegia var. sanguinolenta

Hypoestes lasiostegia var. sanguinolenta, commonly known as the red-flowered Hypoestes, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, often with contrasting white or green markings. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
