Hieracium rupicoliforme

Hieracium rupicoliforme, commonly known as the rock dogsthorn, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is often found growing in rocky or limestone habitats. The plant features a compact growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a composite head with ray florets.

It is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
