Hieracium atrum

Hieracium atrum, commonly known as black hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant has dark green, hairy leaves and produces small, dark purple to black flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Hieracium atrum is noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
