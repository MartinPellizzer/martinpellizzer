Hedyotis pedunculata

Hedyotis pedunculata, commonly known as the small flame plant, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial with small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Hedyotis pedunculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
