Hieracium chlorellum subsp. itharophyton

Hieracium chlorellum subsp.

itharophyton, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically discoid and arranged in a composite head.

The plant features basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape, with serrated margins. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides, preferring well-drained soils.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a common weed in various temperate regions.
