Helichrysum anthemoides

Helichrysum anthemoides, commonly known as the Cape goldball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble small sunflowers. The plant produces small, herbaceous leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum anthemoides is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
