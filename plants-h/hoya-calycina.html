Hoya calycina

Hoya calycina, commonly known as the Wax Plant or the Porcelain Flower, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink, blooming in late spring or early summer. Hoya calycina is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
