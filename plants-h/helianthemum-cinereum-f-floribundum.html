Helianthemum cinereum f. floribundum

Helianthemum cinereum f. floribundum, commonly known as the silver thyme or coastal thyme, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage with a strong aromatic scent, and its small, fragrant flowers bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes. Its compact form and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental and medicinal uses.

The variety 'floribundum' is particularly noted for its profuse flowering and adaptability to various garden conditions.
