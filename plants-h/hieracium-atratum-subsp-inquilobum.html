Hieracium atratum subsp. inquilobum

Hieracium atratum subsp.

inquilobum, commonly known as the black hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and yellow to orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides. It has a rosette growth form with basal leaves and produces a single stem that bears the inflorescence.
