Hieracium alpinum var. ramosum

Hieracium alpinum var. ramosum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features a rosette of basal leaves that are hairy and often have a rough texture. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, flat-topped clusters, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens as an ornamental plant.
