Habenaria brevicalcar

Habenaria brevicalcar, commonly known as the short-spurred pterostylis, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and heathlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a distinctive, narrow spur.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its subtle appearance and specialized pollination mechanisms.
