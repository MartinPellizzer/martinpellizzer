Hermione xanthea

Hermione xanthea, commonly known as the golden dawn orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soil.

Hermione xanthea is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
