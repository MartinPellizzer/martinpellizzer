Herminium hongdeyuanii

Herminium hongdeyuanii, commonly known as the Hongdeyuan orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountainous regions and forest floors. Its blooming period occurs in late spring to early summer, and it is highly valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
