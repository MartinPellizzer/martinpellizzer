Hieracium polystilbum

Hieracium polystilbum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and somewhat woolly.

The flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster, and the plant is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
