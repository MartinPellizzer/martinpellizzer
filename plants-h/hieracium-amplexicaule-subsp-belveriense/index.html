Hieracium Amplexicaule Subsp. Belveriense

Hieracium amplexicaule subsp. belveriense is a rare and lesser-known member of the daisy family found primarily in specific regions of Europe. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf structure and compact growth habit. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival. Understanding and protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native habitats.
