Hieracium xanthicum

Hieracium xanthicum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas.

It is an annual or biennial plant, depending on the climate and growing conditions.
