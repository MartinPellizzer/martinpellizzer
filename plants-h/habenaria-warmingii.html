Habenaria warmingii

Habenaria warmingii, commonly known as the Warming's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its terrestrial growth habit. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often considered a conservation concern.
