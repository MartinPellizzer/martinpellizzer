Haworthia reticulata var. attenuata

Haworthia reticulata var. attenuata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, fleshy, triangular leaves with white to yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant grows slowly and compactly, often forming a rosette shape. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
