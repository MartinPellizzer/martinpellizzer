Helenium autumnale var. parviflorum

Helenium autumnale var. parviflorum, commonly known as the narrowleaf helenium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features striking orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has feathery, finely divided leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant fall blooms.
