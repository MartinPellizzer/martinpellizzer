Hakea calliantha

Hakea calliantha, commonly known as the glossy hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that is typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces striking cylindrical flower spikes that range in color from white to pink, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Hakea calliantha is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is drought tolerant.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal and Mediterranean climates.
