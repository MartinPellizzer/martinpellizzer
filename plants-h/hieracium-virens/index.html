Hieracium Virens

Field hawkweed, scientifically known as Hieracium virens, is a common flowering plant found in meadows and grassy areas across Europe and parts of Asia. It is easily recognized by its bright yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Despite its beauty, it is considered a weed in many regions due to its aggressive growth habits. The plant has been studied for its ecological impact and its role in supporting pollinators. Its resilience and adaptability make it a notable presence in natural landscapes.
