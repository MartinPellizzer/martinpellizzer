Habenaria pachyglossa

Habenaria pachyglossa, commonly known as the broad-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with distinctive broad lips and a spurred column.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral structure.
