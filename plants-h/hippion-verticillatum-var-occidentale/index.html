Hippion Verticillatum Var. Occidentale

Hippion verticillatum var. occidentale is a unique plant species known for its distinctive spiral growth pattern and vibrant foliage. This variant of the original Hippion verticillatum thrives in specific environmental conditions that support its unusual morphology. Its leaves spiral around the stem in a tight, curling formation, creating an eye-catching visual effect. The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and interesting growth habit. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the botanical world.
