Haworthia attenuata var. glabrata

Haworthia attenuata var. glabrata, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are pale green with distinctive dark green to black markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
