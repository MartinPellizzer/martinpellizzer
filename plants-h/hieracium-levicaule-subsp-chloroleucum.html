Hieracium levicaule subsp. chloroleucum

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

chloroleucum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered with fine hairs.

Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and can reach heights of up to 40 centimeters.
