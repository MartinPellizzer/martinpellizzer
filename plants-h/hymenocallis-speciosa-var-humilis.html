Hymenocallis speciosa var. humilis

Hymenocallis speciosa var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf spider lily, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a prominent yellow throat.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to Central America, particularly Mexico. The plant features strap-like, broad leaves that are often a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage and blooms.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements contribute to its popularity in both cultivated and natural settings.
