Habenaria szechuanica

Habenaria szechuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan butterfly orchid, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its inflorescence bears a cluster of small, fragrant blooms.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
