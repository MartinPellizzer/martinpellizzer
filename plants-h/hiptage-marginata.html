Hiptage marginata

Hiptage marginata, commonly known as the silver leaf hiptage, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Hiptage marginata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
