Hypericum olympicum var. viride

Hypericum olympicum var. viride, commonly known as the green hypericum, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
