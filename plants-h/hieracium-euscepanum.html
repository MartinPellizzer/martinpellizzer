Hieracium euscepanum

Hieracium euscepanum, commonly known as the lesser spear-thistle, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features a spiny stem and basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a composite head, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium euscepanum is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
