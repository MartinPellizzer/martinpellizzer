Hydrangea japonica var. plena

Hydrangea japonica var. plena, commonly known as the bigleaf hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences composed of both sterile and fertile flowers, which can range in color from white to pink and blue depending on soil pH. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular ornamental in gardens. Its foliage is dark green and provides an attractive backdrop to the blooms.

The plant is also valued for its ability to change flower color, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.
