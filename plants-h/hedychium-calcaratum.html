Hedychium calcaratum

Hedychium calcaratum, commonly known as the rock spikenard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Hedychium calcaratum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
