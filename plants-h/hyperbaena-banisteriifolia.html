Hyperbaena banisteriifolia

Hyperbaena banisteriifolia, commonly known as the banana leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that resemble banana leaves, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are thick and leathery, providing a striking visual presence in its natural habitat.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage.
