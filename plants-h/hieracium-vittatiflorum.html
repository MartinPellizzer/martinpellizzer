Hieracium vittatiflorum

Hieracium vittatiflorum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium vittatiflorum is often found in temperate regions and can sometimes be considered a weed in agricultural areas.
