Hieracium amblycranum

Hieracium amblycranum, commonly known as the narrow-crown hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
