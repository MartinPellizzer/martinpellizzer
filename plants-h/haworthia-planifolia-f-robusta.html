Haworthia planifolia f. robusta

Haworthia planifolia f. robusta, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or pale blue markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The leaves are typically shorter and more robust compared to the standard variety, giving it a more compact appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
