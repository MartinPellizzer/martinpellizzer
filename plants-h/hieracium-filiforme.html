Hieracium filiforme

Hieracium filiforme, commonly known as slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, while the flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium filiforme is generally a short-lived perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
