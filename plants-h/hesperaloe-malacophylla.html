Hesperaloe malacophylla

Hesperaloe malacophylla, commonly known as the silver ponytail palm, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of long, narrow leaves that give it a distinctive, palm-like appearance. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and appear in late spring or early summer.

Hesperaloe malacophylla is well-suited for xeriscaping and requires minimal water once established.
