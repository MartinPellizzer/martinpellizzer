Habenaria fluminensis

Habenaria fluminensis, commonly known as the Brazilian marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pale pink to white flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in wet, boggy habitats and is often found in marshes and along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the Orchidaceae family.
