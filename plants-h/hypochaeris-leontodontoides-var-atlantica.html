Hypochaeris leontodontoides var. atlantica

Hypochaeris leontodontoides var. atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer, attracting various pollinators.

Its variability in morphology makes it an interesting subject for botanical study.
