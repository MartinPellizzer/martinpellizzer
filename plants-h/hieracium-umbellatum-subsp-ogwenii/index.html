Hieracium Umbellatum Subsp. Ogwenii

Hieracium umbellatum subsp. ogwenii is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of Wales. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, known for its composite flower heads and diverse ecological roles. This subspecies is characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical gem.
