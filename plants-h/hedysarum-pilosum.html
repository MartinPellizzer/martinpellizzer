Hedysarum pilosum

Hedysarum pilosum, commonly known as the silky vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces, giving it its common name. Its flowers are pink to violet in color and are clustered in racemes.

Hedysarum pilosum is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun.
