Hieracium adenodes

Hieracium adenodes, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features a hairy, upright stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are yellow and typically bloom from June to August, attracting various pollinators.

Hieracium adenodes is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
