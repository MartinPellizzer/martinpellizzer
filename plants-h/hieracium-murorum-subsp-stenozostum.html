Hieracium murorum subsp. stenozostum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

stenozostum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
