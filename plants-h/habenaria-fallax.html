Habenaria fallax

Habenaria fallax, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single or few flowers that are usually white or pale pink. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette at the base of the stem.

The orchid has a complex root system and relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.
