Hieracium seringeanum

Hieracium seringeanum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has basal leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped, while the stem is upright and often branched. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
