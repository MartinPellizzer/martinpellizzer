Hieracium jurassicum subsp. borreri

Hieracium jurassicum subsp.

borreri, commonly known as the Jurassic hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow composite flowers, which are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges.

This subspecies is relatively rare and is found in specific regions of Europe, particularly in areas with well-drained soils.
