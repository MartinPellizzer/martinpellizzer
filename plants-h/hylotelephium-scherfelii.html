Hylotelephium scherfelii

Hylotelephium scherfelii, commonly known as the Scherfel's stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to Central Asia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and garden design.
