Hieracium obscuribracteum

Hieracium obscuribracteum, commonly known as the dark bract hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is often found in disturbed habitats and can be an indicator of soil conditions.
