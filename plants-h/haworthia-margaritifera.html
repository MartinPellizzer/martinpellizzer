Haworthia margaritifera

Haworthia margaritifera, commonly known as the "Pearl Plant," is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are pale green with white tubercles, giving it a distinctive, pearlescent appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for dry, sunny environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
