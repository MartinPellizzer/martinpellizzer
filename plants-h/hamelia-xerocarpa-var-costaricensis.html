Hamelia xerocarpa var. costaricensis

Hamelia xerocarpa var. costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican flamebush, is a shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.
