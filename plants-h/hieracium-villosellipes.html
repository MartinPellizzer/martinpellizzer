Hieracium villosellipes

Hieracium villosellipes, commonly known as the woolly nipplewort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, rugged appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in moist, grassy habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
