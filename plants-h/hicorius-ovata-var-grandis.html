Hicorius ovata var. grandis

Hicorius ovata var. grandis, commonly known as the large-leafed hibiscus, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
