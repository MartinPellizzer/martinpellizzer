Habenaria chusua

Habenaria chusua, commonly known as the yellow tailed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
