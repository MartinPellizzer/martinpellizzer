Helichrysum aureofolium

Helichrysum aureofolium, commonly known as the golden leaf helichrysum, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers and fuzzy, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like blooms that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The species is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
