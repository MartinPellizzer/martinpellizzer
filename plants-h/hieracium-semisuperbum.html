Hieracium semisuperbum

Hieracium semisuperbum, commonly known as the semi-superb hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It produces yellow flowers with a central disc and is known for its hairy stems and leaves.

Hieracium semisuperbum is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
