Heterocaryum szovitsianum

Heterocaryum szovitsianum, commonly known as the Hungarian sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, fens, and along water bodies. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.
