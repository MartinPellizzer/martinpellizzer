Helicia tonkinensis

Helicia tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin helia.

It is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam and Laos. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries.

Helicia tonkinensis is often found in moist, shaded forest environments, where it thrives in well-drained soil.
