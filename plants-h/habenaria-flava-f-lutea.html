Habenaria flava f. lutea

Habenaria flava f. lutea, commonly known as the yellow lady's tresses, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and long, spiraling inflorescence of yellowish-green to golden flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. It is native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan, and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
