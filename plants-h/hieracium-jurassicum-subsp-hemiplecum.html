Hieracium jurassicum subsp. hemiplecum

Hieracium jurassicum subsp.

hemiplecum, commonly known as the Jurassic hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate leaves and produces yellow flowers arranged in composite inflorescences.

The plant is typically found in moist meadows and along stream banks in temperate regions. It has a fibrous root system and exhibits asexual reproduction through rhizomes.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies of plant adaptation and succession.
