Hypericum faberi

Hypericum faberi, commonly known as the Chinese St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features simple, opposite leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antidepressant properties.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
