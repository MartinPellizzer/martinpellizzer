Haworthia dielsiana

Haworthia dielsiana, commonly known as the Diels' Haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, pale green to blue-gray leaves with prominent white tubercles and translucent windows. The plant produces slender, branching inflorescences with white or pale yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
