Hieracium Bosniacum Subsp. Pelliculatum

Hieracium bosniacum subsp. pelliculatum is a rare and unique plant species found primarily in specific alpine regions of the Balkans. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, known for its diverse and often showy flower structures. This subspecies is distinguished by its delicate, hairy leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Due to its limited habitat and vulnerability to environmental changes, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical treasure.
