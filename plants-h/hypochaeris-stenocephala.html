Hypochaeris stenocephala

Hypochaeris stenocephala, commonly known as narrow-headed weed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months.
