Hypopitys

Hypopitys, commonly known as the giant sequoia, is a species of coniferous tree native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and thick, fibrous bark that provides protection against fire. The tree has needle-like leaves and produces cones that contain its seeds. Hypopitys is known for its longevity and can live for thousands of years.
