Habenaria goyazensis

Habenaria goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
