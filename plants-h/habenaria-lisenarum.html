Habenaria lisenarum

Habenaria lisenarum, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and long, pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and forest floors. It has a rhizomatous root system that helps it anchor in soil and absorb nutrients.

The species is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
