Habenaria praestans

Habenaria praestans, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Europe.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that often display vibrant colors such as pink, purple, or white. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as woodlands and grasslands. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
