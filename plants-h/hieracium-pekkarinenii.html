Hieracium pekkarinenii

Hieracium pekkarinenii, commonly known as the Pekkarinen's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Finland.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy structure. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous habitats such as meadows and forest edges. It features basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, and its yellow flowers are arranged in a single head.
