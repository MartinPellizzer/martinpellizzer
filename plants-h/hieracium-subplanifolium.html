Hieracium subplanifolium

Hieracium subplanifolium, commonly known as the flat-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable habitats.
