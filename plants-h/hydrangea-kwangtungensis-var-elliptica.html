Hydrangea kwangtungensis var. elliptica

Hydrangea kwangtungensis var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptical hydrangea, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink or blue depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with broad, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to change flower color, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.
