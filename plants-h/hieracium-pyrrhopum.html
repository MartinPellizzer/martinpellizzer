Hieracium pyrrhopum

Hieracium pyrrhopum, commonly known as the red hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are typically lance-shaped with hairy surfaces. Native to Europe, it is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

It is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
