Habenaria hircina

Habenaria hircina, commonly known as the goat orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often near streams or in damp undergrowth. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and elongated leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown.

The orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
