Hieracium olivaceum subsp. globulariiforme

Hieracium olivaceum subsp.

globulariiforme, commonly known as the globular milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spherical flower heads.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers are surrounded by pale green bracts.

This subspecies is known for its adaptability and is often found in disturbed habitats.
