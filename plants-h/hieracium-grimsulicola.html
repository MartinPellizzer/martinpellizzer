Hieracium grimsulicola

Hieracium grimsulicola, commonly known as the Grimsulicola hawkweed, is a rare and specialized plant species native to the alpine regions of Europe.

This plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it faces harsh environmental conditions.
