Hieracium saxifragum grex onosmoides

Hieracium saxifragum grex onosmoides, commonly known as the Onosmoides hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or subalpine habitats. It features a basal rosette of leaves that are usually grayish-green with a woolly texture.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
