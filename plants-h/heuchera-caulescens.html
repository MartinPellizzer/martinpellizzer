Heuchera caulescens

Heuchera caulescens, commonly known as the slender alumroot, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features upright, branched stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink or red. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and vary in color from green to reddish hues.

It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
