Hieracium melanocinereum

Hieracium melanocinereum, commonly known as the black-centred hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black central flower head, which contrasts with its yellowish outer ray florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium melanocinereum is often found in Europe and parts of Asia, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
