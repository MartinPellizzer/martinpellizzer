Hieracium vulgatum var. caesium

Hieracium vulgatum var. caesium, commonly known as Caesar's weed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open areas, such as roadsides and fields. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

The species is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.
