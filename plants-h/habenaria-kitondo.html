Habenaria kitondo

Habenaria kitondo, commonly known as the Kitondo orchid, is a species native to East Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
