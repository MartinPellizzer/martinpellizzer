Hibiscus waterbergensis

Hibiscus waterbergensis, commonly known as the Waterberg hibiscus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a prominent yellow center. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are velvety to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Hibiscus waterbergensis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
