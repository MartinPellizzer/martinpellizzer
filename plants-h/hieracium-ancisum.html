Hieracium ancisum

Hieracium ancisum, commonly known as the ancient dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers consist of yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Hieracium ancisum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
