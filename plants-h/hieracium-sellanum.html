Hieracium sellanum

Hieracium sellanum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and is typically found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are usually rough and hairy. It produces achenes as its fruit, which are dispersed by wind.

Hieracium sellanum is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its invasive growth habits.
