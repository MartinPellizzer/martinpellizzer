Hosta decorata f. normalis

Hosta decorata f. normalis, commonly known as the common hosta, is a cultivar of the Hosta plant known for its broad, heart-shaped leaves.

It is native to East Asia and thrives in shaded, moist environments. The leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture, and the plant produces small, fragrant flowers in the summer. It is a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and ability to enhance garden landscapes with its lush foliage.
