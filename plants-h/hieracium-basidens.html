Hieracium basidens

Hieracium basidens, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while the flowers consist of both ray and disk florets.

Hieracium basidens is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
