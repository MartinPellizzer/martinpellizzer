Hordeum bobartii

Hordeum bobartii, commonly known as Bobart's barley, is a species of grass native to the western United States.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences that are typically green to reddish in color. Hordeum bobartii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils and rocky areas.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
