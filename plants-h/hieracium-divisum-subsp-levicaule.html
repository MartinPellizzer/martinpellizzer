Hieracium divisum subsp. levicaule

Hieracium divisum subsp.

levicaule, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads that produce yellow disk flowers.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils. It has a taproot system and produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and naturalized in various regions.
