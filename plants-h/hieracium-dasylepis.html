Hieracium dasylepis

Hieracium dasylepis, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. It has small, lance-shaped leaves covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hieracium dasylepis is often found in open habitats and is relatively hardy, adapting to various environmental conditions.
