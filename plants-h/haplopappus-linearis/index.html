Haplopappus Linearis

Haplopappus linearis is a hardy flowering plant native to the arid regions of North America. It thrives in sandy and rocky soils, often found in desert and steppe environments. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its slender, linear leaves help it conserve water in harsh conditions. This resilient species is a key component of its ecosystem and is valued for its ecological and ornamental qualities.
