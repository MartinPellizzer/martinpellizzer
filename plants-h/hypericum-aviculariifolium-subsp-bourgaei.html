Hypericum aviculariifolium subsp. bourgaei

Hypericum aviculariifolium subsp.

bourgaei, commonly known as the Bourgae's St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its narrow, leaf-like leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
