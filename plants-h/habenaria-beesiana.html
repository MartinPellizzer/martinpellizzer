Habenaria beesiana

Habenaria beesiana, commonly known as the bees' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with a distinct lip that attracts pollinators like bees.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, it is often found in humid tropical and subtropical regions.
