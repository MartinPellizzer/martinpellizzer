Hieracium intermixtum

Hieracium intermixtum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Hieracium intermixtum is often considered a weed due to its rapid spread and ability to thrive in various conditions.
