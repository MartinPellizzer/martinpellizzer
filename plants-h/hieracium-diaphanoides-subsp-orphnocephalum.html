Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. orphnocephalum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

orphnocephalum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its finely divided, grass-like leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters.

This subspecies is typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It has a creeping growth habit with rhizomes that allow it to spread vegetatively.

The plant is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
