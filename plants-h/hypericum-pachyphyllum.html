Hypericum pachyphyllum

Hypericum pachyphyllum, commonly known as the thick-leaved St. John's wort, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates. Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
