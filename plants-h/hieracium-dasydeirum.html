Hieracium dasydeirum

Hieracium dasydeirum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a rough texture, contributing to its common name.

Hieracium dasydeirum is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.
