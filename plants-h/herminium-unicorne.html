Herminium unicorne

Herminium unicorne, commonly known as the unicorn orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to various regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a perennial herb with a distinctive, single-spurred flower that resembles a unicorn's horn, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its flowers are usually purple or violet in color.

Herminium unicorne is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
