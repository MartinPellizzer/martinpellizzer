Hieracium beckhausii

Hieracium beckhausii, commonly known as the Beckhaus's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and the Tien Shan range. The plant features basal rosettes of hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. It thrives in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
