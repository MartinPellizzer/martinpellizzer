Hieracium pseudosulphureum

Hieracium pseudosulphureum, commonly known as the sulfur yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium pseudosulphureum is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
