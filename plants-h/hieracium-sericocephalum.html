Hieracium sericocephalum

Hieracium sericocephalum, commonly known as the silkyhead hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. It has small, alternate leaves covered in fine, silky hairs, which give it its common name.

The plant is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to colonize various environments.
