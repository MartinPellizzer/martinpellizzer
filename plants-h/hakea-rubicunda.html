Hakea rubicunda

Hakea rubicunda, commonly known as the red-stemmed hakea, is a shrub native to southwestern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescences that produce bright red or orange flowers. The plant has leathery, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. Hakea rubicunda is adapted to arid environments, with deep roots that help it survive in dry conditions.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
