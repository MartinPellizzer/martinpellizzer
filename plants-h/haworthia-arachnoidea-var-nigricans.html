Haworthia arachnoidea var. nigricans

Haworthia arachnoidea var. nigricans, commonly known as the Black Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a bluish or purplish tinge. The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern and are covered in a fine, almost spider-like coating of white, waxy hairs. This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and striking visual appeal.
