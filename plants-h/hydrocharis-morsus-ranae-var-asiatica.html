Hydrocharis morsus-ranae var. asiatica

Hydrocharis morsus-ranae var. asiatica, commonly known as the frog-bit, is a floating aquatic plant native to Asia.

It has small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and it is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats.
