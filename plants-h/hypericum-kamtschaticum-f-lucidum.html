Hypericum kamtschaticum f. lucidum

Hypericum kamtschaticum f. lucidum, commonly known as the shining St. John's wort, is a variant of the St. John's wort species native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial shrub with woody stems and is often found in mountainous areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in horticultural settings.
