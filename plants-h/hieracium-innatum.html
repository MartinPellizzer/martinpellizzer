Hieracium innatum

Hieracium innatum, commonly known as the mountain hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring well-drained soils and sunny conditions. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Hieracium innatum is known for its robust growth and ability to thrive in harsh mountain environments.
