Hieracium badalii

Hieracium badalii, commonly known as the Badalii hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
