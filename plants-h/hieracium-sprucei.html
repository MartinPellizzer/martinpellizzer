Hieracium sprucei

Hieracium sprucei, commonly known as Spruce's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its clustered, yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Hieracium sprucei is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
