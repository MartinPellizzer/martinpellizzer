Hieracium farinulentiforme

Hieracium farinulentiforme, commonly known as the farinulentiform hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across temperate regions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy appearance.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and can form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
