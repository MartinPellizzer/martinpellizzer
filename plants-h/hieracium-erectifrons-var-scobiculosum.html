Hieracium erectifrons var. scobiculosum

Hieracium erectifrons var. scobiculosum, commonly known as the scobiform hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a distinctive central disc.

This variety is notable for its compact growth habit and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
