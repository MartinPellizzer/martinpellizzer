Habenaria stenostachya

Habenaria stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stalked orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a short, stocky rhizome and multiple vertical pseudobulbs. Its leaves are linear to ovate and are arranged spirally around the stem.

The flowers are typically pale green to white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
