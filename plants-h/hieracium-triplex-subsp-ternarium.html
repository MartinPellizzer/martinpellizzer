Hieracium triplex subsp. ternarium

Hieracium triplex subsp.

ternarium, commonly known as the three-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and small, yellowish composite flowers.

The species is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. It has three leaflets on each leaf, giving it its subspecies name *ternarium*.

The plant is typically herbaceous and can be found in a variety of habitats, including meadows and along roadsides.
