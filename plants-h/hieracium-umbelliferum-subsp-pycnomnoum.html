Hieracium umbelliferum subsp. pycnomnoum

Hieracium umbelliferum subsp.

pycnomnoum, commonly known as the densehead hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

The plant produces a dense cluster of yellow flowers at the top of its stem, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
