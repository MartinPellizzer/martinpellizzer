Hippomarathrum petraeum

Hippomarathrum petraeum, commonly known as the rock dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant features basal rosettes of fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and appear in late spring, followed by small, oval-shaped seed pods.
