Hoffmannseggella fournieri

Hoffmannseggella fournieri, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage and its delicate, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Hoffmannseggella fournieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
