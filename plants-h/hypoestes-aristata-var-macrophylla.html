Hypoestes aristata var. macrophylla

Hypoestes aristata var. macrophylla, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from white to pink and purple, often with contrasting veining. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
