Helianthemum thibaudii

Helianthemum thibaudii, commonly known as the Thibaud's rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with woody stems and small, fleshy leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helianthemum thibaudii is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
