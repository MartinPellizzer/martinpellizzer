Hieracium niveiceps

Hieracium niveiceps, commonly known as the white-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its pale green to white hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium niveiceps is an herbaceous perennial that can spread by both seeds and rhizomes.
