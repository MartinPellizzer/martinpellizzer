Hesperanthemum spinosum

Hesperanthemum spinosum, commonly known as the spiny hesperanthemum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
