Habenaria elegantula

Habenaria elegantula, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a slender, erect stem with several oval-shaped leaves arranged along its length. Its flowers are borne on lateral branches and are known for their intricate, lip-like structures.

Habenaria elegantula thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
