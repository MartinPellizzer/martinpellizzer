Heliconia curtispatha

Heliconia curtispatha, commonly known as the red-flowered heliconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
