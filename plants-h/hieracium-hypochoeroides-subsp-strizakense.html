Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. strizakense

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

strizakense, commonly known as the Strizak's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has small, serrated leaves and produces achenes as its fruit type.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species in its native range.
