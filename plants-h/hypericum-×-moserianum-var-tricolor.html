Hypericum × moserianum var. tricolor

Hypericum × moserianum var. tricolor, commonly known as the tricolor St. John's wort, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe.

It is characterized by its striking tricolored foliage, featuring red, yellow, and green tones on the same plant. The plant produces small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in summer, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile ornamental plant.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
