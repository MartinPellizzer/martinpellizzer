Habenaria petromedusa

Habenaria petromedusa, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in mountainous regions across countries like Thailand and Vietnam.
