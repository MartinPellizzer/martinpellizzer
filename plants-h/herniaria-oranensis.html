Herniaria oranensis

Herniaria oranensis, commonly known as the Mediterranean carpetweed, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fleshy, succulent leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tiny white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
