Hosta longipes var. caduca

Hosta longipes var. caduca, commonly known as the slenderleaf hosta, is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with some varieties displaying subtle variegation. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers on slender stems in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice for shady garden landscapes.
