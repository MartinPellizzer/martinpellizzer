Hornstedtia garbosa

Hornstedtia garbosa, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Hornstedtia garbosa is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
