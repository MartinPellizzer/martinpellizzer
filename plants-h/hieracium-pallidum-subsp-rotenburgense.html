Hieracium pallidum subsp. rotenburgense

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

rotenburgense, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to central and eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It has basal leaves that are broadly ovate to lanceolate in shape, and its flowering period occurs from late spring to early autumn.

This subspecies is distinguished by its pale coloration and relatively small, clustered flower heads.
