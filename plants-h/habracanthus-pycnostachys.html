Habracanthus pycnostachys

Habracanthus pycnostachys, commonly known as the dense-stemmed habracanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, woody stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a glossy surface.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
