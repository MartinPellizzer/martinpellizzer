Hypoestes suaveolens

Hypoestes suaveolens, commonly known as the "pink shower" or "pink flame," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green.

Hypoestes suaveolens is well-suited for rock gardens, containers, and as a ground cover due to its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
