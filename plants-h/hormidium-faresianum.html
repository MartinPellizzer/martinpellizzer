Hormidium faresianum

Hormidium faresianum, commonly known as the African orchid, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its botanical features include a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves.

Hormidium faresianum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
