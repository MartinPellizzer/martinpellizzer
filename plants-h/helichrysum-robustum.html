Helichrysum robustum

Helichrysum robustum, commonly known as the golden marguerite or African daisy, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas. Its foliage is aromatic and often used in traditional medicine.

Helichrysum robustum is also popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
