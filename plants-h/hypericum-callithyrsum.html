Hypericum callithyrsum

Hypericum callithyrsum, commonly known as the yellow archangel, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are square and often reddish in color.

Hypericum callithyrsum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in partial shade.
