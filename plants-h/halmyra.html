Halmyra

Halmyra, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with a dense canopy that provides shade.

The leaves contain compounds that have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.
