Hebe hectorii subsp. demissa

Hebe hectorii subsp.

demissa, commonly known as the Hector's hebe, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are usually blue or violet, appearing in clusters and attracting native pollinators.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its natural habitat.
