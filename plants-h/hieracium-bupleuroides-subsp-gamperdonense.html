Hieracium bupleuroides subsp. gamperdonense

Hieracium bupleuroides subsp.

gamperdonense, commonly known as the Gamperdon hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.
