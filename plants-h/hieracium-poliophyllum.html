Hieracium poliophyllum

Hieracium poliophyllum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium poliophyllum is often found in moist environments and can form dense colonies, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
