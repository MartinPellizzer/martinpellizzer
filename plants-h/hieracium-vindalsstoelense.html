Hieracium vindalsstoelense

Hieracium vindalsstoelense, commonly known as the Vindalsstoel dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has a hairy stem and lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine pubescence.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is considered a common weed in certain regions.
