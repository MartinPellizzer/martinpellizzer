Hasseltia tomentulosa

Hasseltia tomentulosa, commonly known as the woolly hakea, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
