Hieracium picenorum

Hieracium picenorum, commonly known as the Picene hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in clusters.

Hieracium picenorum is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various ailments.
