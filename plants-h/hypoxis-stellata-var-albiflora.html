Hypoxis stellata var. albiflora

Hypoxis stellata var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant is characterized by its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
