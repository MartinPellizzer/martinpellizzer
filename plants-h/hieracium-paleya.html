Hieracium paleya

Hieracium paleya, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

It is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
