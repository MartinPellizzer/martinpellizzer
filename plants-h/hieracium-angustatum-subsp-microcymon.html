Hieracium angustatum subsp. microcymon

Hieracium angustatum subsp.

microcymon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring well-drained conditions.

The plant features slender stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a compact cluster, typical of the genus Hieracium.

It is characterized by its small, tightly packed flower heads and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
