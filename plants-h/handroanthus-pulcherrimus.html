Handroanthus pulcherrimus

Handroanthus pulcherrimus, commonly known as the pink trumpet tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. It produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
