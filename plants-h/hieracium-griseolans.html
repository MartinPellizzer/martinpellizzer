Hieracium griseolans

Hieracium griseolans, commonly known as the gray-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Hieracium griseolans is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
