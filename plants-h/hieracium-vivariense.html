Hieracium vivariense

Hieracium vivariense, commonly known as the vivarion hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium vivariense is often found in disturbed habitats and can spread through rhizomes, making it a resilient species.
