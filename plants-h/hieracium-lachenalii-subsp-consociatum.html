Hieracium lachenalii subsp. consociatum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

consociatum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its clustered growth habit and smaller, more compact flower heads compared to other members of the species. It typically thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant has a fibrous root system and is often found in association with other herbaceous plants in its native range.
