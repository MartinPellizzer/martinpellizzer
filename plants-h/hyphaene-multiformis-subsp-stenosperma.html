Hyphaene multiformis subsp. stenosperma

Hyphaene multiformis subsp.

stenosperma, commonly known as the narrow-fruited fan palm, is a species native to Madagascar. It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves and robust trunk, which can reach significant heights.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by elongated, narrow fruits. It thrives in tropical climates with well-drained soils and is often found in dry forest or scrubland habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
