Habenaria hamiltoniana

Habenaria hamiltoniana, commonly known as the Hamilton's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale green to yellowish flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soil conditions.

The orchid belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
