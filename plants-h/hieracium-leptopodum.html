Hieracium leptopodum

Hieracium leptopodum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and basal rosettes of leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, which are typically arranged in a single capitulum. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its low-growing habit and ornamental flowers, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
