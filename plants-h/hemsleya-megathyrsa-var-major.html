Hemsleya megathyrsa var. major

Hemsleya megathyrsa var. major, commonly known as the giant hemslay's orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 cm in diameter, with vibrant purple and white petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
