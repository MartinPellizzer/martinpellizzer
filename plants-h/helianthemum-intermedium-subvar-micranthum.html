Helianthemum intermedium subvar. micranthum

Helianthemum intermedium subvar. micranthum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dense, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to withstand hot, dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

This subshrub is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in garden settings.
