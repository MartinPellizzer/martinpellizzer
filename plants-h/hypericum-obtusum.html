Hypericum obtusum

Hypericum obtusum, commonly known as bluntleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with rounded tips. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Hypericum obtusum is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
