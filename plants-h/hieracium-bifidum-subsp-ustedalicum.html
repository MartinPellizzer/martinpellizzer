Hieracium bifidum subsp. ustedalicum

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

ustedalicum, commonly known as the Ustalitic Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves.
