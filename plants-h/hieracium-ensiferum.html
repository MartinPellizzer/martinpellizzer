Hieracium ensiferum

Hieracium ensiferum, commonly known as the sword-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium ensiferum is typically found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in sunny conditions.
