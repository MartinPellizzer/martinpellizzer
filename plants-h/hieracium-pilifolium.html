Hieracium pilifolium

Hieracium pilifolium, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium pilifolium is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
