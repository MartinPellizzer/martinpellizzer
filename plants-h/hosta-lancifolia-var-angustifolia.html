Hosta lancifolia var. angustifolia

Hosta lancifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf sword fern hosta, is a variety of hosta with elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are narrower than the typical hosta.

It features a dense, upright growth habit with deep green foliage that often has a slight blue tint. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers on tall spikes during the summer months. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to a range of garden conditions.
