Hieracium discolor

Hieracium discolor, commonly known as the variable hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange flowers and hairy, alternate leaves. Native to Europe, it has been introduced to other parts of the world, often growing in disturbed soils and meadows. The plant typically reaches heights of 20 to 60 centimeters and has a creeping growth habit.

Its flowers bloom from late spring to early autumn, attracting various pollinators.
