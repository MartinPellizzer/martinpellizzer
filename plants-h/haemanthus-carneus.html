Haemanthus carneus

Haemanthus carneus, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright spikes of bright orange-red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a standout in both garden and horticultural settings.
