Helleborus abchasicus var. purpureo-laevigato

Helleborus abchasicus var. purpureo-laevigato, commonly known as the Abkhazian hellebore, is a rare and striking plant native to the Caucasus region.

The plant produces striking, waxy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late winter or early spring. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forested areas with rich, well-drained soil.
