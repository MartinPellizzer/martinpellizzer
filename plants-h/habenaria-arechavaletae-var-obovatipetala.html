Habenaria arechavaletae var. obovatipetala

Habenaria arechavaletae var. obovatipetala, commonly known as the Obovate-petalled Orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive obovate-shaped petals and a compact, upright growth habit. The plant typically features a single stem with several flowering spikes, each bearing a cluster of fragrant, violet to purple flowers. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forest habitats.
