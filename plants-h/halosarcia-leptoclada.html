Halosarcia leptoclada

Halosarcia leptoclada, commonly known as the slender bladder bladder, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive bladder-like structures called calyces. The plant is native to South America and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with tendrils that aid in climbing.
