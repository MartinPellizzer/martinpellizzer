Hedysarum davuricum

Hedysarum davuricum, commonly known as the Mongolian milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in alpine and steppe regions. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Hedysarum davuricum is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
