Haworthia reinwardtii var. herrei

Haworthia reinwardtii var. herrei, commonly known as the "Cape Racer," is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct, white to pale yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
