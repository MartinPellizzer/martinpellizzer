Heloniopsis

Heloniopsis, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flower heads, which are surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves.
