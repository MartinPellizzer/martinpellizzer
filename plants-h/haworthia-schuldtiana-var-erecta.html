Haworthia schuldtiana var. erecta

Haworthia schuldtiana var. erecta, commonly known as the upright Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care in indoor gardening.
