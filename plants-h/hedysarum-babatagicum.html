Hedysarum babatagicum

Hedysarum babatagicum, commonly known as the Babatag red clover, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks. It plays a role in nitrogen fixation, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
