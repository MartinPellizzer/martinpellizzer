Hieracium pheristum

Hieracium pheristum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow or orange composite flowers that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can thrive in a variety of soil types. It has hairy stems and basal leaves that are often lobed or divided.

Hieracium pheristum is an annual or biennial plant that spreads through seeds and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
