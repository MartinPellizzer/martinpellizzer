Habenaria brandisii

Habenaria brandisii, commonly known as the Brandis' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
