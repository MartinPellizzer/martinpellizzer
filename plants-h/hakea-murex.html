Hakea murex

Hakea murex, commonly known as the red-stemmed hakea, is a shrub native to southwestern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, woody stems that are often reddish in color, and its dense, needle-like leaves. The plant produces striking, urn-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in spring. Hakea murex is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
