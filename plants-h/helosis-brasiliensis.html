Helosis brasiliensis

Helosis brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian helosis, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange in color. The plant has palmate leaves with a smooth, waxy surface, which helps it retain moisture in the humid environment. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Helosis brasiliensis plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a habitat for various small organisms.
