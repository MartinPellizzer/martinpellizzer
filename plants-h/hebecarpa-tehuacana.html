Hebecarpa tehuacana

Hebecarpa tehuacana, commonly known as the Mexican starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the family Caricaceae and is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and clusters of yellow-orange fruits. The fruits, which are edible, have a sweet and tangy flavor similar to that of the common starfruit. The plant is often grown for its ornamental value as well as its fruit production.
