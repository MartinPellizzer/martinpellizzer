Hordeum pubiflorum var. blomii

Hordeum pubiflorum var. blomii, commonly known as Blom's barley, is a variant of the barley species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy inflorescence and is typically found in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
