Hedysarum hedysaroides subsp. austrosibiricum

Hedysarum hedysaroides subsp.

austrosibiricum, commonly known as the Siberian milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia. It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in steppe and grassland habitats. It has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in poor soils.

This subspecies is valued for its role in soil stabilization and as a forage plant for livestock.
