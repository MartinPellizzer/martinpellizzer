Haworthia retusa f. longibracteata

Haworthia retusa f. longibracteata, commonly known as the long-bract haworthia, is a compact succulent native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or translucent markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
