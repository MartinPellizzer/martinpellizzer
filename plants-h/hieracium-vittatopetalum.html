Hieracium vittatopetalum

Hieracium vittatopetalum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in dense, flat-topped clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium vittatopetalum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and its role in supporting pollinators.
