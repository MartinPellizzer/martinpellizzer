Hypericum cerastoides subsp. meuselianum

Hypericum cerastoides subsp.

meuselianum, commonly known as the Meuselian St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers.

The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its native range.
