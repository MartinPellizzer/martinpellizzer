Himeranthus

Himeranthus, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Himeranthus thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and vibrant colors make it a standout addition to any botanical collection.
