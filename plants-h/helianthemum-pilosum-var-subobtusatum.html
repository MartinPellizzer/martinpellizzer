Helianthemum pilosum var. subobtusatum

Helianthemum pilosum var. subobtusatum, commonly known as the woolly rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with silvery-gray leaves covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a bright touch to rocky or coastal landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments.
