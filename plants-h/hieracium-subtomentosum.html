Hieracium subtomentosum

Hieracium subtomentosum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features hairy, gray-green leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is sometimes grown in gardens.
