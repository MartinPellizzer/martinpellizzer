Habenaria sanbornii

Habenaria sanbornii, commonly known as the Sanborn's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as pine flatwoods and wetlands. The plant features a sympodial growth pattern with a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in a spike with a distinct, spurred lip.

Botanically, it is characterized by its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
