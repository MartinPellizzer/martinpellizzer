Hieracium praecox var. nansicum

Hieracium praecox var. nansicum, commonly known as the early hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers and hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It produces small, dark seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to spread across various habitats.

The species is often found in temperate climates and is relatively hardy, making it a common sight in many regions.
