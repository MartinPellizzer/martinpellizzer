Hypochaeris tenuifolia var. linearifolia

Hypochaeris tenuifolia var. linearifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf weed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers with a central disk.

This species is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
