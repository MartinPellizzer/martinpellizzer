Horsfieldia solomonensis

Horsfieldia solomonensis, commonly known as the Solomon Islands orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to the Solomon Islands.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, evergreen stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments on tree branches or rocks. Its flowers, which are usually white or pale pink, bloom in clusters and attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Horsfieldia solomonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
