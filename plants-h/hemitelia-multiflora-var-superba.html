Hemitelia multiflora var. superba

Hemitelia multiflora var. superba, commonly known as the glossy leaf hemitelia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an understory species in humid, shaded forest environments. It has a creeping growth habit with aerial roots that help it attach to trees or other supports.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.
