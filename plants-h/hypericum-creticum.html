Hypericum creticum

Hypericum creticum, commonly known as the Cretan St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

Hypericum creticum is often found in rocky soils and dry habitats, thriving in full sun.
