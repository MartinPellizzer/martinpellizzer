Hieracium bifidum subsp. parcevestitum

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

parcevestitum, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It features upright stems with basal leaves that are lanceolate and often hairy.
