Habenaria recta

Habenaria recta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Habenaria recta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
