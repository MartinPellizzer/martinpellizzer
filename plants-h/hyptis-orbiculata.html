Hyptis orbiculata

Hyptis orbiculata, commonly known as the globe hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its round, velvety leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Hyptis orbiculata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
