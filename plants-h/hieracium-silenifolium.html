Hieracium silenifolium

Hieracium silenifolium, commonly known as the silvery hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in moist, grassy habitats. It has a creeping growth habit with a central stem that produces multiple flowering stems.

The plant is known for its ability to spread rapidly, often forming dense mats in its native environment.
