Hibiscus pseudoferox

Hibiscus pseudoferox, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Hibiscus pseudoferox is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
