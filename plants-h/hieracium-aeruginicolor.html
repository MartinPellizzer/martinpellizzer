Hieracium aeruginicolor

Hieracium aeruginicolor, commonly known as the blue hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its blue to purple flowers bloom in clusters.

Hieracium aeruginicolor is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
