Habenaria rosulata

Habenaria rosulata, commonly known as the Rosulate Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are oval and arranged in a tight rosette, which helps conserve moisture.

The orchid is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and unique appearance.
