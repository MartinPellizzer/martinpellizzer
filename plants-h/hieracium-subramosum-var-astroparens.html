Hieracium subramosum var. astroparens

Hieracium subramosum var. astroparens, commonly known as the star-veined hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant features yellow ray florets and a central disk of tubular flowers, typical of the genus Hieracium. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its unique leaf venation patterns give it its common name and make it a distinctive species within its habitat.
