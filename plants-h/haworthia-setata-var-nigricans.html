Haworthia setata var. nigricans

Haworthia setata var. nigricans, commonly known as the Black Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dark green, fleshy leaves with a distinctive black or dark purple coloration along the edges and tips. The plant produces small, bell-shaped white flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
