Hieracium tapeinops

Hieracium tapeinops, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium tapeinops is often found in temperate climates and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
