Hieracium exanthinum

Hieracium exanthinum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands.

Hieracium exanthinum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
