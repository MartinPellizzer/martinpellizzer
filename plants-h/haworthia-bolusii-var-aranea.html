Haworthia bolusii var. aranea

Haworthia bolusii var. aranea, commonly known as the spider haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white to pale green markings that resemble spider legs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pale green flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
