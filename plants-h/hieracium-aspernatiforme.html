Hieracium aspernatiforme

Hieracium aspernatiforme, commonly known as the rough-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.
