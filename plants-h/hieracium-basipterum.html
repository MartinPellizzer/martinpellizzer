Hieracium basipterum

Hieracium basipterum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish composite flowers. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium basipterum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
