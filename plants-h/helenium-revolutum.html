Helenium revolutum

Helenium revolutum, commonly known as the purple topsoil plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a reddish tinge, adding visual interest to gardens.

It is a perennial that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
