Hornemannia

Hornemannia, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plants typically have narrow, grass-like leaves and produce striking, showy flowers with a distinctive horn-like spur. They are often found in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests.

Hornemannia species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
