Habenaria macroptera

Habenaria macroptera, commonly known as the large-lobed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers clustered along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
