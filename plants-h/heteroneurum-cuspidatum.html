Heteroneurum cuspidatum

Heteroneurum cuspidatum, commonly known as the spike moss, is a type of vascular plant belonging to the family Heteroneuraceae.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and a spore-bearing structure called a strobilus. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms.

Heteroneurum cuspidatum is often found in temperate regions and is an important component of certain ecosystems.
