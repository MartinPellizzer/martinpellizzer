Hieracium hyalotrichum

Hieracium hyalotrichum, commonly known as the glaucous hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
