Hermannia modesta var. elatior

Hermannia modesta var. elatior, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its compact size and long-lasting blooms.

Hermannia modesta var. elatior is often cultivated as a houseplant and is relatively easy to care for with proper watering and temperature conditions.
