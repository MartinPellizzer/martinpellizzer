Homalomena prolixa

Homalomena prolixa, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking greenish-white spadices with bright yellow spathes, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Homalomena prolixa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
