Hedyotis glabella

Hedyotis glabella, commonly known as the smooth hedyotis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features slender stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tiny, clustered in dense inflorescences, and often have four petals.

Hedyotis glabella is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
