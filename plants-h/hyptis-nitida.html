Hyptis nitida

Hyptis nitida, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers and aromatic, hairy leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hyptis nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
