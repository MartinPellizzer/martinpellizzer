Habenaria hystrix

Habenaria hystrix, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a complex structure typical of the Orchidaceae family. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Its pseudobulbs and linear leaves are adapted to store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in diverse habitats.
