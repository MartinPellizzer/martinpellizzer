Hernandia labyrinthica

Hernandia labyrinthica, commonly known as the labyrinth tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, tangled growth pattern, which gives it its common name. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a fleshy, spiny capsule that contains several seeds.

Hernandia labyrinthica is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance and medicinal properties.
