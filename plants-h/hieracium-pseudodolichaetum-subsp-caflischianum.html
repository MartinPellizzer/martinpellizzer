Hieracium pseudodolichaetum subsp. caflischianum

Hieracium pseudodolichaetum subsp.

caflischianum, commonly known as the Caflisch's Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering head composed of yellow disk flowers surrounded by ray florets.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils. It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in mountainous areas.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many parts of its range.
