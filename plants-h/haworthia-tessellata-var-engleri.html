Haworthia tessellata var. engleri

Haworthia tessellata var. engleri, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features pale green to white tubular leaves with distinctive red or pink markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for dry environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
