Hypoestes psilostachys

Hypoestes psilostachys, commonly known as the "blue snowflake," is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its striking, bi-colored flowers that resemble snowflakes. The plant produces clusters of small, white petals with a deep blue center, creating a visually appealing contrast. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Hypoestes psilostachys is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
