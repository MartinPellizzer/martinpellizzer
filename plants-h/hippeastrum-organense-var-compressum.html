Hippeastrum organense var. compressum

Hippeastrum organense var. compressum, commonly known as the compressed amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically pink or white with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant produces strap-like, narrow leaves that are often a deep green in color. Its bulbs are robust and capable of storing significant water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
