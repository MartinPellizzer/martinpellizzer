Hypericum simonsii

Hypericum simonsii, commonly known as Simons' St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, slightly hairy leaves and produces small, orange-red fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

Hypericum simonsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some historical use in traditional medicine.
