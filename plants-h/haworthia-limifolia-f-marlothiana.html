Haworthia limifolia f. marlothiana

Haworthia limifolia f. marlothiana, commonly known as the Marloth's leaf haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often adorned with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
