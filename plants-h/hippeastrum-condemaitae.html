Hippeastrum condemaitae

Hippeastrum condemaitae, commonly known as the Red-veined Amazon Lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It features large, showy flowers with vibrant red and white petals, often with intricate veining patterns. The plant produces tall, sturdy stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. Its bulbs are adapted to store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in the humid, tropical environment.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
