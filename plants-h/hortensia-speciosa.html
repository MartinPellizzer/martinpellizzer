Hortensia speciosa

Hortensia speciosa, commonly known as the Japanese anemone, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pink flower heads that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features palmately divided leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Hortensia speciosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
