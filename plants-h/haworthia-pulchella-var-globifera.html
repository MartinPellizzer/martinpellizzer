Haworthia pulchella var. globifera

Haworthia pulchella var. globifera, commonly known as the globe-shaped Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often tinged with red or pink at the tips. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
