Hippeastrum Microstigmum

Rainbow lily, scientifically known as Hippeastrum microstigmum, is a strikingly colorful plant native to South America. It is prized for its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of hues, from deep reds to soft pinks and yellows. This species is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family, which includes many popular ornamental plants. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among gardeners and florists alike. The Rainbow lily is not only visually appealing but also relatively easy to care for, making it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
