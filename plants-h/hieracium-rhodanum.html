Hieracium rhodanum

Hieracium rhodanum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium rhodanum is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable environments.
