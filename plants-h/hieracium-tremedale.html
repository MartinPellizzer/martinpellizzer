Hieracium tremedale

Hieracium tremedale, commonly known as the trembling hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow daisy-like flowers with ray florets. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture. This species is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
