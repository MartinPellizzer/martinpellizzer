Hieracium norvegicum subsp. haploschemon

Hieracium norvegicum subsp.

haploschemon, commonly known as Norwegian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

This subspecies is typically found in moist meadows and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soil.
