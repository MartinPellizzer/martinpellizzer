Helianthemum polifolium var. rupiniculum

Helianthemum polifolium var. rupiniculum, commonly known as the rock helianthemum, is a low-growing shrub native to the rocky habitats of southern Europe.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with small, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are typically clustered in small, showy inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils and is often found in limestone-rich areas.

This species is well adapted to harsh, sunny environments and is relatively drought-tolerant.
