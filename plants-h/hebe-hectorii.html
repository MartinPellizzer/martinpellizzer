Hebe hectorii

Hebe hectorii, commonly known as Hector's hebe, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white. The leaves are typically narrow and evergreen, providing year-round interest. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
