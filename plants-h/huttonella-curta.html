Huttonella curta

Huttonella curta, commonly known as the short-stemmed huttonella, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or brownish in color. Huttonella curta is often found in sagebrush communities and is an important component of its ecosystem.
