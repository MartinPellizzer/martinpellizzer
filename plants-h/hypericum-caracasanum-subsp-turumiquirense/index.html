Hypericum Caracasanum Subsp. Turumiquirense

Hypericum caracasanum subsp. turumiquirense is a rare and lesser-known member of the Hypericaceae family. Native to the high-altitude regions of South America, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. The subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and vivid yellow flowers. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it faces threats that highlight the need for further study and protection.
