Habenaria ernestii

Habenaria ernestii, commonly known as the Ernest's orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and relatively small, greenish-white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for absorbing nutrients from the soil.

Habenaria ernestii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
