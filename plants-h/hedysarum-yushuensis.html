Hedysarum yushuensis

Hedysarum yushuensis, commonly known as the Yushu milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to high-altitude environments, showcasing resilience to cold and arid conditions. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and is of interest for its potential ecological and medicinal value.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a subject of study in botanical research.
