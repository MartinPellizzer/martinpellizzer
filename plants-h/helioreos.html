Helioreos

Helioreos, commonly known as the sunbloom, is a flowering plant native to arid regions.

It features striking yellow-orange petals that resemble the sun, hence its name. The plant has a compact, bushy structure with thick, waxy leaves adapted to retain moisture. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Helioreos is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in hot, dry climates.
