Helianthemum rhodax var. canum

Helianthemum rhodax var. canum, commonly known as the Canary Island rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub with woody stems and evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to coastal and sunny environments.

Its hardy nature makes it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
