Hieracium leptophyton subsp. subbauhiniiflorum

Hieracium leptophyton subsp.

subbauhiniiflorum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers in clustered inflorescences.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, giving the plant a rough texture. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

This subspecies is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in disturbed environments.
