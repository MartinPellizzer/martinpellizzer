Hymenachne leptachne

Hymenachne leptachne, commonly known as slender hymenachne, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its inflorescence consists of a loose cluster of spikelets, which are often covered in fine hairs.

Hymenachne leptachne plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
