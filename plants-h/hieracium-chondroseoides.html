Hieracium chondroseoides

Hieracium chondroseoides, commonly known as the chondroseoid dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads made up of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are basal, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
