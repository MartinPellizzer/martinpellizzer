Hypoxis dinteri

Hypoxis dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's yellowstar, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Hypoxis, which includes over 100 species of flowering plants. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow, star-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Hypoxis dinteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
