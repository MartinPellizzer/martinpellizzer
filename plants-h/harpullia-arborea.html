Harpullia arborea

Harpullia arborea, commonly known as the broad-leafed harpullia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. Harpullia arborea is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
