Helichrysum antennaria

Helichrysum antennaria, commonly known as the silverleaf helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its aromatic leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscapes.

Its ornamental value and resilience make it a favored plant in both horticulture and ecological settings.
