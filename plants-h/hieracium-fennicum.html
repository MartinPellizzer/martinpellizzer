Hieracium fennicum

Hieracium fennicum, commonly known as the Fennoscandian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe and parts of Siberia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows, bogs, and tundra regions. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. Its flowers are typically arranged in a single head and are surrounded by phyllaries.

Hieracium fennicum is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
