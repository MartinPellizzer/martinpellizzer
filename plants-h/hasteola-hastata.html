Hasteola hastata

Hasteola hastata, commonly known as the slender wild oat, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a spikelet-bearing panicle, which produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.
