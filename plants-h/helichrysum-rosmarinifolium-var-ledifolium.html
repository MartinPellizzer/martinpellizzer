Helichrysum rosmarinifolium var. ledifolium

Helichrysum rosmarinifolium var. ledifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features silver-gray, needle-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that are often arranged in dense clusters. The plant is known for its aromatic properties and is often used in herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
