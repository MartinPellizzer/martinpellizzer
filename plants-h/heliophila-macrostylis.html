Heliophila macrostylis

Heliophila macrostylis, commonly known as the broad-styled heliophyte, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Heliophila macrostylis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic contribution.
