Hibiscus mechowii

Hibiscus mechowii, commonly known as the Mechoew hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Hibiscus mechowii is also noted for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types, making it a versatile choice for horticultural use.
