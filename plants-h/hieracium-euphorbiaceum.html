Hieracium euphorbiaceum

Hieracium euphorbiaceum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium euphorbiaceum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to colonize various habitats.
