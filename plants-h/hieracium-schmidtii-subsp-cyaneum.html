Hieracium schmidtii subsp. cyaneum

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

cyaneum, commonly known as the blue hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features bright blue to violet ray florets surrounded by yellow disk florets, giving it a striking appearance. It typically grows in grassy habitats and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical structure includes a basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that can reach up to 30 cm in height.
