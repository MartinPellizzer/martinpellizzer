Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp. delasoiei

Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp.

delasoiei, commonly known as the Delasoie's hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers.
