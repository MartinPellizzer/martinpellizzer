Hypericum chamaemyrtus

Hypericum chamaemyrtus, commonly known as creeping St. John's wort, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to Europe and western Asia.

It features dense, rounded foliage with small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in summer, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its aromatic leaves and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
