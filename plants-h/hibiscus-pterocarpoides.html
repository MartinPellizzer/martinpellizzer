Hibiscus pterocarpoides

Hibiscus pterocarpoides, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Hibiscus pterocarpoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
