Habenaria gymnadenia var. densiflora

Habenaria gymnadenia var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered helmet orchid, is a species native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a dense cluster of small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a spike. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
