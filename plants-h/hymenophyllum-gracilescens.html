Hymenophyllum gracilescens

Hymenophyllum gracilescens, commonly known as the slender filmy fern, is a small, epiphytic fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in small, clustered structures called sori.

The plant's unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an important component of forest understory ecosystems.
