Hochenwartia

Hochenwartia, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to South America, particularly Brazil, and are characterized by their unique, helmet-shaped inflorescences. Hochenwartia species typically grow as epiphytes or lithophytes, attaching themselves to trees or rocks in their natural habitat. They produce showy, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, which bloom during the warmer months.

Due to their striking appearance and specific care requirements, they are popular among orchid enthusiasts.
