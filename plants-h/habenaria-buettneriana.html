Habenaria buettneriana

Habenaria buettneriana, commonly known as the Buetternerv orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and the leaves are elongated and strap-like.

The flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinct lip that aids in pollination.
