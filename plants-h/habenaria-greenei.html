Habenaria greenei

Habenaria greenei, commonly known as the Greene's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is a terrestrial herb with a distinctive inflorescence that bears showy, fragrant flowers. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically oval and arranged in a rosette pattern around the base.

The flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.
