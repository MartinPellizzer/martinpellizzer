Habenaria guadalajarana

Habenaria guadalajarana, commonly known as the Guadalajara orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
