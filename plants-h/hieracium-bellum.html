Hieracium bellum

Hieracium bellum, commonly known as the pretty hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Hieracium. The plant typically grows in meadows, woodlands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are arranged in a composite head.

Hieracium bellum is an herbaceous perennial that thrives in temperate climates.
