Hydrangea moellendorffii

Hydrangea moellendorffii, commonly known as the mountain hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink or blue depending on soil pH. The plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens. Its foliage turns vibrant shades of red in the fall, adding seasonal interest.

Hydrangea moellendorffii is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to bloom reliably in cooler climates.
