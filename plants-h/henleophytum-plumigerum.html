Henleophytum plumigerum

Henleophytum plumigerum, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a rare and unique plant species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that resemble clouds, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas. Its reproductive structures are small and inconspicuous, typical of many ferns.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Henleophytum plumigerum is considered a valuable subject for botanical research.
