Hieracium dovrense var. australius

Hieracium dovrense var. australius, commonly known as the Australian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous florets in its flower heads.

Due to its adaptability, it can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
