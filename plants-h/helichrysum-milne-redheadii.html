Helichrysum milne-redheadii

Helichrysum milne-redheadii, commonly known as the golden marguerite or yellow oxeye, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that resemble daisies, which bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The species is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
