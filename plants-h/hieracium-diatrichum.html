Hieracium diatrichum

Hieracium diatrichum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features basal leaves that are broadly lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Hieracium diatrichum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
