Habenaria goodyeroides

Habenaria goodyeroides, commonly known as the Goodyera orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and features showy, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

The orchid has lance-shaped leaves and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
