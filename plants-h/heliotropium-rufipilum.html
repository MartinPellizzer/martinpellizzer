Heliotropium rufipilum

Heliotropium rufipilum, commonly known as red-hair heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in clusters at the ends of stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Heliotropium rufipilum is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
