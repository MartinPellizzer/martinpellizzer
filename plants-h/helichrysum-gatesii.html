Helichrysum gatesii

Helichrysum gatesii, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flowers that resemble miniature suns. The plant is evergreen, with small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum gatesii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
