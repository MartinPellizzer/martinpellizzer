Hippeastrum latissimifolium

Hippeastrum latissimifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that can be white, pink, or red, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and can grow quite large. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hippeastrum latissimifolium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
