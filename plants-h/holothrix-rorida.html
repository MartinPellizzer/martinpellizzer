Holothrix rorida

Holothrix rorida, commonly known as the rough hairbush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
