Haplopappus mustersi

Haplopappus mustersi, commonly known as the desert hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Its small, yellowish flowers bloom in the spring and are followed by bract-like structures that aid in wind dispersal of its seeds.

Haplopappus mustersi plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and provides nectar for pollinators in its native habitat.
