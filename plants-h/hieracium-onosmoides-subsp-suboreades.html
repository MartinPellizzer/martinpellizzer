Hieracium onosmoides subsp. suboreades

Hieracium onosmoides subsp.

suboreades, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands, meadows, and rocky slopes.

The plant features upright stems with alternate, lanceolate to narrowly elliptic leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and are produced in late spring to early summer.

It is characterized by its small, persistent basal leaves and a distinctive, often branched root system.
