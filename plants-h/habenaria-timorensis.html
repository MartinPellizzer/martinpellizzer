Habenaria timorensis

Habenaria timorensis, commonly known as the Timor Habenaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to the island of Timor in Indonesia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
