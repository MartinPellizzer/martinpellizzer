Haworthia inconfluens var. habdomadis

Haworthia inconfluens var. habdomadis, commonly known as the "Sunburst Haworthia," is a compact, leafy succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, light green leaves with distinct white to pale yellow markings. The leaves are covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
