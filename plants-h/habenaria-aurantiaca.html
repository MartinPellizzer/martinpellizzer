Habenaria aurantiaca

Habenaria aurantiaca, commonly known as the Orange Ladies' Tresses, is an orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, orange-colored flowers that bloom in dense, trussed-like clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it produces a single, long flowering stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
