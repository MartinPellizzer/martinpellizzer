Hieracium hopense

Hieracium hopense, commonly known as the Hop Field Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in the summer, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Hieracium hopense is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to colonize new areas.
