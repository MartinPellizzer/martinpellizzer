Heuchera ribifolia

Heuchera ribifolia, commonly known as red veined heuchera, is a perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with prominent red veins. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a valuable addition to shade gardens and containers.
