Hieracium svanlundii

Hieracium svanlundii, commonly known as Svanlund's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions of Europe and North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, rocky, or sandy habitats, often in alpine or tundra environments. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer, and its leaves are usually narrow and hairy.

Hieracium svanlundii is adapted to cold climates and has a low-growing habit to withstand harsh weather conditions.
