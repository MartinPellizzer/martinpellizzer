Hystrichophora macrophylla

Hystrichophora macrophylla, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, feathery plumes that create a dramatic visual impact in gardens. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a silver-green hue that adds texture to landscapes.

Hystrichophora macrophylla thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
