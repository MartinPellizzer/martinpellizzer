Huernia tubata

Huernia tubata, commonly known as the tuberous huernia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. Its unique, tuberous structure helps it endure periods of drought.

Huernia tubata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
