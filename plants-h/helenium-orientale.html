Helenium orientale

Helenium orientale, commonly known as the oriental sneezeweed, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in moist, sunny environments. The plant features bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple.

Helenium orientale is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
