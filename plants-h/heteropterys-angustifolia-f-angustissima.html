Heteropterys angustifolia f. angustissima

Heteropterys angustifolia f. angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heteropterys, is a compact shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Its foliage is dense and makes it an attractive choice for ornamental gardening.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.
