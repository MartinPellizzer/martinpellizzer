Hieracium sennenianum

Hieracium sennenianum, commonly known as the Sennen's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring sunny to partially shaded conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is often found in habitats with well-drained soil.
