Habenaria protusorostrata

Habenaria protusorostrata, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often in the understorey of tropical rainforests. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces a single spike of flowers with elongated, hooded petals.

This orchid is notable for its delicate appearance and specific ecological requirements.
