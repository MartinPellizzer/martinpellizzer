Hardenbergia digitata

Hardenbergia digitata, commonly known as the bluebonnet, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple pea-shaped flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Hardenbergia digitata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
