Hypoestes floribunda subsp. paniculata

Hypoestes floribunda subsp.

paniculata, commonly known as the panicle hypoestes, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its striking, branched inflorescences that bear small, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
