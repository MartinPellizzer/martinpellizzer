Hibiscus bacalusius

Hibiscus bacalusius, commonly known as the sacred hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers with five petals that range in color from white to deep red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers bloom during the summer months and are known for their strong, fragrant scent.

Hibiscus bacalusius is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
