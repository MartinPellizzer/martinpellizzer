Helichrysum plicatum subsp. plicatum

Helichrysum plicatum subsp.

plicatum, commonly known as the golden marguerite or African daisy, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features wiry, branched stems and is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of golden-yellow flowers.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its drought tolerance and long-lasting flowers make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
