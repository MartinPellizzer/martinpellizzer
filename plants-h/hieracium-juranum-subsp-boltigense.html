Hieracium juranum subsp. boltigense

Hieracium juranum subsp.

boltigense, commonly known as the Jura hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.
