Holopetalon

Holopetalon, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, slender stalks rising from water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, while its roots anchor in the mud beneath. Holopetalon is known for its symbolic significance in various cultures, often representing purity and enlightenment.

Botanically, it belongs to the Nymphaeaceae family and is closely related to water lilies.
