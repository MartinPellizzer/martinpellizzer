Habenaria medioflexa

Habenaria medioflexa, commonly known as the medium flexed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow markings. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it is part of the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its delicate floral structure and specific pollination mechanisms.
