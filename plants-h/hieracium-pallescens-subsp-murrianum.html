Hieracium pallescens subsp. murrianum

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

murrianum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

It is a flowering plant that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
