Haworthia davidii

Haworthia davidii, commonly known as the Zulu Jade or Zulu Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Haworthia davidii is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
