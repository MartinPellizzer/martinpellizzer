Hieracium inuloides subsp. platamodes

Hieracium inuloides subsp.

platamodes, commonly known as the flat-topped hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped. Its inflorescence consists of a flat, disc-like arrangement of yellow flowers, which bloom in summer.

It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
