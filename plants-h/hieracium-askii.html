Hieracium askii

Hieracium askii, commonly known as the Askia hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its erect stems and clusters of yellowish to reddish flowers. It typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high elevations.
