Hedyotis capitellata var. dacdoaensis

Hedyotis capitellata var. dacdoaensis, commonly known as the Philippine dwarf billygoat weed, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a small herbaceous perennial with slender, ascending stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along streambanks.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies of tropical flora.
