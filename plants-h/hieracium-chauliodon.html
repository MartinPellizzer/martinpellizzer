Hieracium chauliodon

Hieracium chauliodon, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange flowers and basal rosette of leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grasslands. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is sometimes considered invasive in certain regions.
