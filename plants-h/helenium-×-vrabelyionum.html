Helenium × vrabelyionum

Helenium × vrabelyionum, commonly known as the New Zealand aster, is a hybrid perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flower heads, which resemble small sunflowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with lance-shaped, dark green leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Helenium × vrabelyionum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
