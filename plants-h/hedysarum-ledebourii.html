Hedysarum ledebourii

Hedysarum ledebourii, commonly known as the desert pea, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces distinctive yellow to orange pea-like flowers. Hedysarum ledebourii is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with low water availability.

Its root system is deep and robust, allowing it to access groundwater and thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
