Hieracium zizianum

Hieracium zizianum, commonly known as the Zizian's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering stems that bear yellow flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are arranged in a single head.

Hieracium zizianum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various regions.
