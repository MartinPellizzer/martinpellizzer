Hosta rectifolia var. sachalinensis

Hosta rectifolia var. sachalinensis, commonly known as the Sachalin hosta, is a variant of the upright hosta species native to Japan.

It features narrow, upright leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late summer, which are borne on slender stems. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to garden conditions.
