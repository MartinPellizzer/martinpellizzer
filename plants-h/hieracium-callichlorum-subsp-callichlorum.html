Hieracium callichlorum subsp. callichlorum

Hieracium callichlorum subsp.

callichlorum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while its stems are upright and often branched.

It is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
