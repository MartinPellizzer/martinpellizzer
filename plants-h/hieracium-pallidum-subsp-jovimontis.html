Hieracium pallidum subsp. jovimontis

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

jovimontis, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes, where it forms dense mats. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets that are often shorter than the central disk florets.

It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in mountainous areas across the Pyrenees and Alps.
