Habenaria dryadum var. major

Habenaria dryadum var. major, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 cm in length, with a distinctive greenish-yellow coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its complex floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
