Hieracium heldreichii

Hieracium heldreichii, commonly known as the Heldreich's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and it produces small, ribbed achenes as seeds.

Hieracium heldreichii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
