Homalomena expedita

Homalomena expedita, commonly known as the red-veined arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves with prominent red veins, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies. It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like caladium and peace lily.

Homalomena expedita is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
