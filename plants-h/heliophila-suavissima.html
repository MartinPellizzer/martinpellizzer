Heliophila suavissima

Heliophila suavissima, commonly known as sweet marsh woundwort, is a flowering plant native to wetland habitats in Europe and parts of Asia.

Heliophila suavissima is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with moist conditions.
