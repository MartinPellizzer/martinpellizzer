Huernia penzigii var. schimperi

Huernia penzigii var. schimperi, commonly known as the Schimper's moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems covered in a waxy cuticle that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warm seasons. Its leaves are typically spineless and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its unique appearance.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
