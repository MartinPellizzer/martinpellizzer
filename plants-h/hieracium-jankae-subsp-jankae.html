Hieracium jankae subsp. jankae

Hieracium jankae subsp.

jankae, commonly known as the Janka's Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clustered inflorescences, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species in its native habitat.
