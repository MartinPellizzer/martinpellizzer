Hylotelephium Purpureum

Hylotelephium purpureum is a striking plant known for its vibrant purple foliage and showy pink flowers. Native to eastern europe and western asia, it thrives in rocky and sunny environments. this plant is often mistaken for a succulent due to its thick leaves and compact growth habit. it is a popular choice for rock gardens and borders due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal. its ability to survive in poor soil makes it an excellent addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
