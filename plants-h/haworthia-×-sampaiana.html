Haworthia × sampaiana

Haworthia × sampaiana, commonly known as the Zulu fairy veil, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pinkish markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange, which bloom during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf patterns and compact growth habit make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
