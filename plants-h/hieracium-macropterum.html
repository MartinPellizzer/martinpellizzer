Hieracium macropterum

Hieracium macropterum, commonly known as the large-winged hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features bright yellow flowers with ray florets and a central disk, often blooming from June to August. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium macropterum typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
