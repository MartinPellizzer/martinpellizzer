Habenaria ciliatisepala

Habenaria ciliatisepala, commonly known as the ciliated-sepal Habenaria, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant features long, slender stems with several grass-like leaves, and its flowers are usually pink or purple in color. The sepals and petals are adorned with fine, ciliate (fringed) edges, giving it its scientific name.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
