Hirtella racemosa var. metallica

Hirtella racemosa var. metallica, commonly known as the shining hirtella, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, metallic-green foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are typically covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its textured appearance.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
