Haworthia chloracantha subsp. angustifolia

Haworthia chloracantha subsp.

angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or pale markings.

The leaves are narrow and pointed, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the summer months.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and container growing due to its low maintenance requirements.
