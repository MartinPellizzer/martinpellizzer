Hieracium salangense

Hieracium salangense, commonly known as the Salangian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, often in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single terminal head. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Hieracium salangense is characterized by its hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
