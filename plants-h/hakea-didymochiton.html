Hakea didymochiton

Hakea didymochiton, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are arranged in dense, cylindrical spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in coastal and heathland environments.

Hakea didymochiton is adapted to arid conditions and is often used in native landscaping due to its hardy nature.
