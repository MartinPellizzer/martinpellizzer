Hieracium sclerophyllum

Hieracium sclerophyllum, commonly known as the hard-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant has rough, stiff leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Hieracium sclerophyllum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
