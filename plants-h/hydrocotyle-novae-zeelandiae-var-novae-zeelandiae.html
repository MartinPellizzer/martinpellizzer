Hydrocotyle novae-zeelandiae var. novae-zeelandiae

Hydrocotyle novae-zeelandiae var. novae-zeelandiae, commonly known as New Zealand water shield, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and lake margins. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often floating or submerged, with a waxy surface to reduce water loss. It produces small white or yellow flowers that bloom from late winter to early summer.

The species is characterized by its ability to thrive in aquatic or semi-aquatic conditions, making it an important component of New Zealand's wetland ecosystems.
