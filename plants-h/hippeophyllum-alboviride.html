Hippeophyllum alboviride

Hippeophyllum alboviride, commonly known as the white-green leaf cycad, is a rare and ancient plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its cycad-like structure includes a stout, unbranched stem and a crown of large, fern-like leaves.
