Hieracium Lactucaceum Subsp. Aurigeranum

Hieracium lactucaceum subsp. aurigeranum is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive leaf patterns. It belongs to the dandelion family, which includes many familiar garden weeds and wildflowers. This subspecies is often found in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in harsh environments where few plants can survive. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and nature enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and poorly understood by the general public.
