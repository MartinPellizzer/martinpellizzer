Hieracium sparsum subsp. idae

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

idae, commonly known as the Idaho hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as meadows and roadsides. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.
