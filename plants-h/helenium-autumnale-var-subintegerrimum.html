Helenium autumnale var. subintegerrimum

Helenium autumnale var. subintegerrimum, commonly known as the Orange Hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in meadows and open woodlands.

Its showy flowers make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
