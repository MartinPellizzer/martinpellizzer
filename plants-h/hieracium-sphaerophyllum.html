Hieracium sphaerophyllum

Hieracium sphaerophyllum, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, and it thrives in dry, rocky soils. The plant is characterized by its spherical, spiky flower heads that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
