Hieracium gronovii var. hirsutissimum

Hieracium gronovii var. hirsutissimum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its hairy, woolly leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun.
