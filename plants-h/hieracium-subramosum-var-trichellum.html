Hieracium subramosum var. trichellum

Hieracium subramosum var. trichellum, commonly known as the hairy yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy foliage. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade.
