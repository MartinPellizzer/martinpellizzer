Hypericum hubeiense

Hypericum hubeiense, commonly known as the Hubei goldthread, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Hypericum.

It is native to China, specifically the Hubei province, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers, which bloom in summer, and its woody stems covered in small, orange-red berries. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

The plant also has traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
