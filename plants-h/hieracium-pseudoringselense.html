Hieracium pseudoringselense

Hieracium pseudoringselense, commonly known as the false ringed hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Central and Eastern Europe and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.
