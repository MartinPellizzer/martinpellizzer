Haworthia bronkhorstii

Haworthia bronkhorstii, commonly known as the Bronkhorst's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

Haworthia bronkhorstii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
