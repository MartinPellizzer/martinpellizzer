Hoya mitrata

Hoya mitrata, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of star-shaped, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, white to pale pink blooms that are often followed by red or orange berries. Hoya mitrata is a popular houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
