Hieracium praevittatiflorum

Hieracium praevittatiflorum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium praevittatiflorum is often found in temperate regions and can sometimes become invasive in non-native habitats.
