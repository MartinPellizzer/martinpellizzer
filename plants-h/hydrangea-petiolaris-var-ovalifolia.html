Hydrangea petiolaris var. ovalifolia

Hydrangea petiolaris var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf hydrangea, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
