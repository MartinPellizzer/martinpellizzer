Huperzia linifolia var. tenuifolia

Huperzia linifolia var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaf huperzia, is a species of fern-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its small, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along its stems. This variety is native to regions in East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant is notable for its simple, non-vascular structure and its ability to survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
