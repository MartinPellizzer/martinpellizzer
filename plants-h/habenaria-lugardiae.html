Habenaria lugardiae

Habenaria lugardiae, commonly known as the Lugard's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are typical of the Orchidaceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
