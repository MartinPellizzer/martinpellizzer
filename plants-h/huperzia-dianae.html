Huperzia dianae

Huperzia dianae, commonly known as the Chinese rock fern, is a species of clubmoss belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is native to China and is often found growing on rocky substrates in humid environments. Huperzia dianae produces sporangia in terminal clusters, which are typically found on the underside of its leaves.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shady, moist gardens.
