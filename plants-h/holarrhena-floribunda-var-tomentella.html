Holarrhena floribunda var. tomentella

Holarrhena floribunda var. tomentella, commonly known as the woolly flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with elliptical leaflets and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its striking appearance.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
