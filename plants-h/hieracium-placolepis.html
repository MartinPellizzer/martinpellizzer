Hieracium placolepis

Hieracium placolepis, commonly known as the smallleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has basal and upright stems that are hairy, and its leaves are lance-shaped with rough textures. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are composed of many small florets.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
