Haworthiopsis attenuata var. radula

Haworthiopsis attenuata var. radula, commonly known as the Moonstone plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green with a waxy texture. The leaves are often marked with distinctive white or pale green markings, resembling a moonlit surface. It produces small, pale green flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is drought-tolerant and prefers bright, indirect sunlight.
