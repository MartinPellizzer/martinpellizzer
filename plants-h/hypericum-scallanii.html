Hypericum scallanii

Hypericum scallanii, commonly known as the Scottish St John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, usually from July to August. Its leaves are alternate, oval-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

Hypericum scallanii is often found in moist, calcareous soils in woodland and heathland habitats.
