Himantoglossum hircinum f. calamistratum

Himantoglossum hircinum f. calamistratum, commonly known as the hairy goat's beard, is a rare and distinctive plant belonging to the orchid family.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy leaves and striking, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in calcareous grasslands and open woodlands. Its foliage is often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
