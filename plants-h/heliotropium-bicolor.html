Heliotropium bicolor

Heliotropium bicolor, commonly known as the purple heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to purple, often with a distinct central purple spot. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny environments such as wetlands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and usually have a hairy texture.

Heliotropium bicolor is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in various conditions.
