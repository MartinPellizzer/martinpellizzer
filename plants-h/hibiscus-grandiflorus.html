Hibiscus grandiflorus

Hibiscus grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a yellow throat. Hibiscus grandiflorus is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
