Haworthia reinwardtii var. archibaldiae

Haworthia reinwardtii var. archibaldiae, commonly known as the archibaldiae haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white to pale yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
