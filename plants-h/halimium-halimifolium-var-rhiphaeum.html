Halimium halimifolium var. rhiphaeum

Halimium halimifolium var. rhiphaeum, commonly known as the purple sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
