Humbertacalia voluta

Humbertacalia voluta, commonly known as the spiral bellflower, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Humbertacalia voluta is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
