Hieracium subspeciosum subsp. jaborneggii

Hieracium subspeciosum subsp.

jaborneggii, commonly known as the Jabornegg's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate, with serrated margins, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.
