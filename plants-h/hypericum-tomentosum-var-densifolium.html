Hypericum tomentosum var. densifolium

Hypericum tomentosum var. densifolium, commonly known as the dense-leaved St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is known for its medicinal properties, though it contains phototoxic compounds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable addition to gardens and natural habitats.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping.
