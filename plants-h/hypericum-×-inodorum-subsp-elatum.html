Hypericum × inodorum subsp. elatum

Hypericum × inodorum subsp.

elatum, commonly known as the showy St. John's wort, is a hybrid species within the genus Hypericum. It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
