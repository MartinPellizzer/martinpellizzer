Habenaria flaccida

Habenaria flaccida, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base of the stem.

Habenaria flaccida is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
