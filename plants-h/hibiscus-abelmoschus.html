Hibiscus abelmoschus

Hibiscus abelmoschus, commonly known as okra, is a herbaceous annual plant native to warm temperate and tropical regions.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves and produces vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to deep red. The plant is characterized by its capsule fruit, which contains numerous seeds and is often used in cooking. Hibiscus abelmoschus thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Its botanical structure supports its role as both an ornamental and a valuable vegetable crop.
