Habenaria kitimboana

Habenaria kitimboana, commonly known as the Kitimbo orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats, often in humus-rich soil. Its floral structure includes three sepals and three petals, with the latter often being more showy.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its native ecosystem.
