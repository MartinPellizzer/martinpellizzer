Hieracium tenuiflorum subsp. pictoprasinum

Hieracium tenuiflorum subsp.

pictoprasinum, commonly known as the greenish hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

The plant features basal rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. Its subspecies is distinguished by its smaller, more compact inflorescences compared to other members of the species.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
