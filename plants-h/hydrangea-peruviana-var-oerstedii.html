Hydrangea peruviana var. oerstedii

Hydrangea peruviana var. oerstedii, commonly known as the Andean hydrangea, is a compact shrub native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique and hardy plant.
