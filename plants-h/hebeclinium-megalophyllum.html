Hebeclinium megalophyllum

Hebeclinium megalophyllum, commonly known as the giant hebe, is a striking plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in forested areas, often in the understorey where it thrives in partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, white bloom, giving them a silvery appearance.

Hebeclinium megalophyllum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
