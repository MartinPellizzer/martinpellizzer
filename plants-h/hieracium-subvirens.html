Hieracium subvirens

Hieracium subvirens, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this species thrives in meadows and grassy areas. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters.

Its flowers bloom from late spring to early autumn, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
