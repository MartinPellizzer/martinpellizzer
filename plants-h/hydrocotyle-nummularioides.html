Hydrocotyle nummularioides

Hydrocotyle nummularioides, commonly known as silver dollar plant, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of round, silver-green leaves that are slightly hairy and measure about 1 to 2 inches in diameter. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
