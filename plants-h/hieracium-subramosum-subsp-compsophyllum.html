Hieracium subramosum subsp. compsophyllum

Hieracium subramosum subsp.

compsophyllum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments.

The plant features upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow and form a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched structure and is often found in open, nutrient-poor habitats.
