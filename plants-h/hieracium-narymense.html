Hieracium narymense

Hieracium narymense, commonly known as the Narym hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are rough and hairy, and the flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is often found in regions with temperate climates and is adapted to withstand seasonal variations.
