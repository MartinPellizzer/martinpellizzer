Hibbertia stellaris

Hibbertia stellaris, commonly known as the star hibbertia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Hibbertia stellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
