Hypericum venosum

Hypericum venosum, commonly known as the veiny St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features erect, branched stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or woodland habitats.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
