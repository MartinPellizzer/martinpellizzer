Helianthemum assurgens

Helianthemum assurgens, commonly known as the rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often found growing on rocky slopes and coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
