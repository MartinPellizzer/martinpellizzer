Heuchera × easthamii

Heuchera × easthamii, commonly known as the 'Coral Bells' hybrid, is a popular ornamental plant in the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mounding growth habit and striking foliage that ranges from deep purples to bright greens and reds. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Heuchera × easthamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a variety of garden conditions.
