Hypericum kiusianum var. kiusianum

Hypericum kiusianum var. kiusianum, commonly known as the Kiusianum St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
