Hermione × bifrons

Hermione × bifrons, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a striking plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers bloom in spring and are highly sought after by both horticulturists and pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
