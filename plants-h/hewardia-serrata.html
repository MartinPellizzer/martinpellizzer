Hewardia serrata

Hewardia serrata, commonly known as the serrated hewardia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight red or purple tinge, especially in cooler temperatures. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Hewardia serrata thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored indoor plant.
