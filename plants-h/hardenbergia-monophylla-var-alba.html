Hardenbergia monophylla var. alba

Hardenbergia monophylla var. alba, commonly known as the white everlasting pea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late summer to autumn. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, trifoliate leaves. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

The variety alba is distinguished by its notably lighter flower color compared to other varieties of Hardenbergia monophylla.
