Hieracium murorum subsp. semisilvaticum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

semisilvaticum, commonly known as the wood hedgehog, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its spiny, glandular stems and leaves with a rough, hairy texture.
