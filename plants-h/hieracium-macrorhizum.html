Hieracium macrorhizum

Hieracium macrorhizum, commonly known as the large-rooted hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Hieracium macrorhizum has a taproot system and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
