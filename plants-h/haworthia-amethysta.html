Haworthia amethysta

Haworthia amethysta, commonly known as the purple haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with prominent white or purple markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
