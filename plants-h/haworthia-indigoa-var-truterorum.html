Haworthia indigoa var. truterorum

Haworthia indigoa var. truterorum, commonly known as the Truter's Haworthia, is a compact, dwarf succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale blue markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique leaf patterns and compact growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
