Hieracium seusanum

Hieracium seusanum, commonly known as the Suesan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring sunny and well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers with ray florets and is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes.

Hieracium seusanum is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its aggressive growth habit.
