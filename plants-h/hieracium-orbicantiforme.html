Hieracium orbicantiforme

Hieracium orbicantiforme, commonly known as the broad-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, spoon-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has a taproot system and is often found in open habitats with moderate moisture.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
