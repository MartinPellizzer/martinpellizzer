Hieracium cymanthum

Hieracium cymanthum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium cymanthum is often found in disturbed soils and can form dense colonies in suitable environments.
