Habenaria cylindrocalyx

Habenaria cylindrocalyx, commonly known as the cylindrical-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it is notable for its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
