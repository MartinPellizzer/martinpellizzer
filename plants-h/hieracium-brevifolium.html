Hieracium brevifolium

Hieracium brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium brevifolium is often found in disturbed soils and can form dense colonies in suitable environments.
