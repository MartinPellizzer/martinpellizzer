Hieracium megardense

Hieracium megardense, commonly known as the Megardens hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces numerous yellowish to reddish flower heads. The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has a taproot system and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.

Its botanical features make it easily identifiable within the Hieracium genus.
