Homeria papillosa

Homeria papillosa, commonly known as the silky homeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Homeria papillosa is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to attract pollinators.
