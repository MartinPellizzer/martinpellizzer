Heteroblemma capillipes

Heteroblemma capillipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and slender, often branching stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
