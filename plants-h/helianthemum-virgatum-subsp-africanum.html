Helianthemum virgatum subsp. africanum

Helianthemum virgatum subsp.

africanum, commonly known as the African rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Africa. It features small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, blooming in spring and summer.

The plant has aromatic, glandular leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and showy flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
