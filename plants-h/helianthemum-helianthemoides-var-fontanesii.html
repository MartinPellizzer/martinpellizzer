Helianthemum helianthemoides var. fontanesii

Helianthemum helianthemoides var. fontanesii, commonly known as the Fontanesii rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are covered in fine white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value in rock gardens and as a hardy ground cover.
