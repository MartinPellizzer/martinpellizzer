Heterotis vogelii

Heterotis vogelii, commonly known as the African waterwheel, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive whorled arrangement of leaves, which give it a wheel-like appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, swampy habitats and is often found near water bodies. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Heterotis vogelii is known for its ability to thrive in wetland environments, making it an important component of aquatic ecosystems.
