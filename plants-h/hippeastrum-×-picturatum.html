Hippeastrum × picturatum

Hippeastrum × picturatum, commonly known as the painted amaryllis, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its striking, colorful floral displays. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture.

Hippeastrum × picturatum is a popular ornamental plant cultivated for its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
