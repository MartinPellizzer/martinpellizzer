Hieracium prasinops subsp. supraglaucum

Hieracium prasinops subsp.

supraglaucum, commonly known as the greenleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are usually green, sometimes with a bluish or whitish underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

It is a perennial plant that can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable habitats.
