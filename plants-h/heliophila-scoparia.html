Heliophila scoparia

Heliophila scoparia, commonly known as narrowleaf heliophila, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It is a perennial herb that plays a role in supporting local biodiversity and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
