Helictotrichon Sedenense Var. Gervaisii

Gervais' bluegrass, scientifically known as Helictotrichon sedenense var. gervaisii, is a rare and delicate grass species native to the mountainous regions of southern California. This variety is part of the Poaceae family, which includes many other grasses found across the globe. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in high elevation habitats. Due to its limited range and specific environmental requirements, it is considered vulnerable to habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique plant for future generations.
