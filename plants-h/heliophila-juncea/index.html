Heliophila Juncea

Heliophila juncea is a delicate flowering plant known for its vibrant yellow blooms. It thrives in sunny environments and is often found in meadows and grasslands. This species belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. Its name reflects its love for sunlight, as "heliophila" means sun-loving. The plant is not only beautiful but also holds ecological significance in supporting pollinators.
