Heliotropium vulgare

Heliotropium vulgare, commonly known as common heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple or pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a common weed in disturbed areas.

Heliotropium vulgare is also known for its ability to produce a pleasant, vanilla-like scent.
