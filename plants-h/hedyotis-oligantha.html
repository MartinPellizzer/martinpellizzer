Hedyotis oligantha

Hedyotis oligantha, commonly known as the narrowleaf bedstraw, is a small, herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, ascending stems with opposite, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom in the summer months.

Hedyotis oligantha is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in humid, temperate climates.
