Hippocratea decussata

Hippocratea decussata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with contrasting purple or red markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
