Hieracium rapunculoides subsp. columnare

Hieracium rapunculoides subsp.

columnare, commonly known as the columnar hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its upright, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height.

The plant produces yellow flowers in a dense, flat-topped inflorescence, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are basal, with a rough, hairy texture and a distinctive lobed shape.

It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides, and is often found in temperate climates.
