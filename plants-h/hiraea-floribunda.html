Hiraea floribunda

Hiraea floribunda, commonly known as the clustered hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Hiraea floribunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
