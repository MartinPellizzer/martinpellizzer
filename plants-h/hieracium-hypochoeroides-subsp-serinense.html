Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. serinense

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

serinense, commonly known as the Serinense Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has small, rough leaves and produces numerous seeds dispersed by wind.

This subspecies is often found in regions with Mediterranean climates.
