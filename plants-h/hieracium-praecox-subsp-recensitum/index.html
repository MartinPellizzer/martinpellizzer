Hieracium Praecox Subsp. Recensitum

Early hawkweed, scientifically known as Hieracium praecox subsp. recensitum, is a delicate flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Europe. It thrives in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often blooming in early spring. With its yellow-orange flowers and finely divided leaves, it is both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically significant. This subspecies is distinguished by its early flowering habit and unique morphological traits. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
