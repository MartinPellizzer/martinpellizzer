Haworthia Retusa Var. Springbokvlakensis

Springbokvlakte haworthia, scientifically known as Haworthia retusa var. springbokvlakensis, is a unique succulent native to the semi-arid regions of South Africa. This plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate white markings and a subtle translucency. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens and xeriscaping. The variety's name honors the Springbokvlakte, a region renowned for its diverse succulent flora. Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts and collectors.
