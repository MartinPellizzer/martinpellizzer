Haworthia exilis

Haworthia exilis, commonly known as the Little Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a bluish-green color with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Haworthia exilis is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care.
