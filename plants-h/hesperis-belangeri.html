Hesperis belangeri

Hesperis belangeri, commonly known as the Belanger's honesty, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hesperis belangeri is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
