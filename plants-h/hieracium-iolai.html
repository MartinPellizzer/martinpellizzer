Hieracium iolai

Hieracium iolai, commonly known as the Iolaus hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium iolai is often found in areas with well-drained soil and moderate moisture levels.
