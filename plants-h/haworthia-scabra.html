Haworthia scabra

Haworthia scabra, commonly known as the bare-leaf Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the summer months.

Haworthia scabra is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
