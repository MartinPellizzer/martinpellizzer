Hypericum peplidifolium var. oblongifolium

Hypericum peplidifolium var. oblongifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, yellow, four-petaled flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is notable for its compact growth habit and medicinal properties.
