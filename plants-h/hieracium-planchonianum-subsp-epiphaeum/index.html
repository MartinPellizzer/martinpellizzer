Hieracium Planchonianum Subsp. Epiphaeum

Hieracium planchonianum subsp. epiphaeum is a rare and striking plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive leaf structure. This subspecies thrives in rocky and moist habitats, often found in mountainous areas. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its fragile ecosystem.
