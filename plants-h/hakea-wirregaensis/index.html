Hakea Wirregaensis

Hakea wirregaensis is a striking shrub native to the arid regions of Western Australia. It is known for its unique, bottle-shaped stems and vibrant red or yellow flowers that bloom in spring. This plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions with minimal water. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for drought-resistant gardens. Hakea wirregaensis plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and preserving native ecosystems.
