Haworthia herrei var. poellnitzii

Haworthia herrei var. poellnitzii, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy, triangular leaves with distinctive white to yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers on short, erect stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
