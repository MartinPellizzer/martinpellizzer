Hermione weinmannii

Hermione weinmannii, commonly known as the silver fern, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
