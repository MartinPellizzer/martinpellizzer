Hypoestes bojeriana

Hypoestes bojeriana, commonly known as the "Snowflake plant," is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It features striking, velvety, waxy leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
