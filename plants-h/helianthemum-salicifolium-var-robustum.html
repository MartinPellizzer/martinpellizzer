Helianthemum salicifolium var. robustum

Helianthemum salicifolium var. robustum, commonly known as the robust stonecrop, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
