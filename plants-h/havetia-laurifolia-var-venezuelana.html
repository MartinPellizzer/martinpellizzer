Havetia laurifolia var. venezuelana

Havetia laurifolia var. venezuelana, commonly known as the Venezuelan evergreen hibiscus, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Venezuela.

It is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and emit a subtle fragrance. The plant produces striking red-orange flowers with five petals, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
