Heracleum sphondylium subsp. australe

Heracleum sphondylium subsp.

australe, commonly known as the Australian cowparade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Australia. It is characterized by its tall, hollow stems and large, umbrella-like compound leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a flat-topped infloreshcence. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes make it a common weed in disturbed areas.
