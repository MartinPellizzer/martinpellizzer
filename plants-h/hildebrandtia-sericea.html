Hildebrandtia sericea

Hildebrandtia sericea, commonly known as the silky hildebrandtia, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hildebrandtia sericea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its delicate foliage and vibrant blooms.
