Helleborine consimilis

Helleborine consimilis, commonly known as the similar helleborine, is a species of orchid belonging to the genus Helleborine in the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that often lack distinct petals. This plant is typically found in woodland habitats, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. Helleborine consimilis is a perennial herb that produces a single flower spike with several blooms.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive yet subtle member of the orchid family.
