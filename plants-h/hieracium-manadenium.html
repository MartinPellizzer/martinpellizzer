Hieracium manadenium

Hieracium manadenium, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

Hieracium manadenium is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh, high-altitude environments.
