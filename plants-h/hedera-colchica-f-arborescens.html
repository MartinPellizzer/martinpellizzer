Hedera colchica f. arborescens

Hedera colchica f. arborescens, commonly known as the tree ivy, is a unique variant of the Persian ivy.

It is a climbing plant that can develop a shrub-like appearance, making it distinct from its typical ivy relatives. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves with a waxy texture, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red berries. Native to the Caucasus region, it thrives in shady, moist environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a striking yet adaptable plant.
