Haworthia cymbiformis var. transiens

Haworthia cymbiformis var. transiens, commonly known as the Transient Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or pinkish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are often arranged in a rosette pattern, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
