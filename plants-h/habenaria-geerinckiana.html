Habenaria geerinckiana

Habenaria geerinckiana, commonly known as the Geerinck's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often reddish or purple in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
