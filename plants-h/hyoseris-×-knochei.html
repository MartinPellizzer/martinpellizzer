Hyoseris × knochei

Hyoseris × knochei, commonly known as the hybrid yellowhead dandelion, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that contribute to its spread.

Hyoseris × knochei is often found in association with other members of the dandelion tribe.
