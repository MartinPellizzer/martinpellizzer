Hieracium murorum subsp. steloides

Hieracium murorum subsp.

steloides, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies.

The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges, and the stems are usually hairy.

It is characterized by its clustered growth habit and is part of the dandelion family (Asteraceae).
