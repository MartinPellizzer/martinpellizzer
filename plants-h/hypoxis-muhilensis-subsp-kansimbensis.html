Hypoxis muhilensis subsp. kansimbensis

Hypoxis muhilensis subsp.

kansimbensis, commonly known as the Kansimbe starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Hyacinthaceae and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or white, arranged in a cluster at the base of the plant. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
