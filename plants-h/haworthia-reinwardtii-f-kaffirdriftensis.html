Haworthia reinwardtii f. kaffirdriftensis

Haworthia reinwardtii f. kaffirdriftensis, commonly known as the Kaffir drift haworthia, is a compact, succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, pale green to bluish-gray leaves with prominent white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
