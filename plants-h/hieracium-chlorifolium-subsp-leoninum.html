Hieracium chlorifolium subsp. leoninum

Hieracium chlorifolium subsp.

leoninum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It features basal rosettes of dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common weed in agricultural and urban environments.
