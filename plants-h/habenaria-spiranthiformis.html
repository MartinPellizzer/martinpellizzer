Habenaria spiranthiformis

Habenaria spiranthiformis, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spiral-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

Habenaria spiranthiformis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
