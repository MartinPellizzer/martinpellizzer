Hieracium persimile

Hieracium persimile, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant is perennial, with a creeping growth habit that allows it to spread vegetatively.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and natural landscapes.
