Hymenocallis obtusata

Hymenocallis obtusata, commonly known as the bluntleaf hurricane lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a prominent yellow throat, which bloom in the summer months. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly waxy in texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Hymenocallis obtusata is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
