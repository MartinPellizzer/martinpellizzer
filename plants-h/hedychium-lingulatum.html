Hedychium lingulatum

Hedychium lingulatum, commonly known as the golden ginger or flame ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, fragrant, yellow-orange flowers with a red center. The plant features large, lance-shaped leaves and has a rhizomatous root system. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hedychium lingulatum is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
