Homalosciadium verticillatum

Homalosciadium verticillatum, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
