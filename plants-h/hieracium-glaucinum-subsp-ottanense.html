Hieracium glaucinum subsp. ottanense

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

ottanense, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, showcasing its adaptation to harsh environmental conditions.

The plant features basal rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in mountainous areas of the Alps.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
