Heterospathe scitula

Heterospathe scitula, commonly known as the swamp fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near water bodies in its natural habitat. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it in the soil while absorbing nutrients.

Heterospathe scitula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to tolerate humid conditions.
