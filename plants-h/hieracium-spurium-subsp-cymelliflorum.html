Hieracium spurium subsp. cymelliflorum

Hieracium spurium subsp.

cymelliflorum, commonly known as the clustered flower hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a clustered inflorescence, which is a distinguishing characteristic. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

It belongs to the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
