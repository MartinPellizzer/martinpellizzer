Huernia barbata var. griquensis

Huernia barbata var. griquensis, commonly known as the Grootfontein moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the cooler months. Its leaves are usually modified into spines, reducing water loss and providing protection from herbivores.

The species is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
