Hordeum berteroanum

Hordeum berteroanum, commonly known as Bertero's barley, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical spikelets and small, wind-dispersed seeds. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

While not widely cultivated, it plays a role in local ecosystems and has been studied for its potential in ecological restoration.
