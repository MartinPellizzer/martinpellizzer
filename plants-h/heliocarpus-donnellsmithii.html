Heliocarpus donnellsmithii

Heliocarpus donnellsmithii, commonly known as the Brazilian burr palm, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Palmae and is characterized by its robust, clustered growth habit. The plant produces large, compound leaves and distinctive, woody fruits that enclose the seeds. Its botanical features make it well adapted to wetland and flood-prone environments.
