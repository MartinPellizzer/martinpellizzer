Helianthemum marifolium var. oblonguifolium

Helianthemum marifolium var. oblonguifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, evergreen leaves that are oblong and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a hardy and ornamental choice for gardeners in mild climates.
