Hieracium dovrense subsp. groenlandicum

Hieracium dovrense subsp.

groenlandicum, commonly known as Greenland hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, rocky, or sandy habitats, often in alpine or tundra environments. It has basal leaves that are often woolly or hairy, and its stems are usually erect.

This subspecies is adapted to cold climates and is considered a hardy, low-growing plant.
