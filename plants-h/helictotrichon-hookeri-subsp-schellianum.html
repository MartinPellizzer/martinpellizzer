Helictotrichon hookeri subsp. schellianum

Helictotrichon hookeri subsp.

schellianum, commonly known as Schell's needlegrass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a perennial grass that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green coloration.
