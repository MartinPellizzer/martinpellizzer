Haworthia Viscosa F. Subobtusa

Haworthia viscosa f. subobtusa is a unique and striking succulent native to southern Africa. Known for its compact rosette shape and fleshy, blue-green leaves, this plant is a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts. Its leaves are covered in a fine, powdery coating that helps reduce water loss in arid environments. This particular form of Haworthia is prized for its compact growth and subtle coloration. It requires minimal care, making it ideal for both novice and experienced plant growers.
