Hesperis magellanica

Hesperis magellanica, commonly known as the Magellan's honesty, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, and it prefers well-drained soil.

Hesperis magellanica is often cultivated for its delicate appearance and historical use in herbal medicine.
