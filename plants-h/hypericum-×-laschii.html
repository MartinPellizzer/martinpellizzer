Hypericum × laschii

Hypericum × laschii, commonly known as the hybrid St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Hypericum × laschii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
