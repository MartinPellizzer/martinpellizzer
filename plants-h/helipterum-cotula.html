Helipterum cotula

Helipterum cotula, commonly known as the common helipterum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features showy, yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer to autumn. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in a rosette at the base.

Helipterum cotula is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
