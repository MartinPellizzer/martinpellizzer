Helenium cuneifolium

Helenium cuneifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sunflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the central and eastern United States.

It features bright orange to red ray flowers surrounded by yellow disk flowers, typical of the Asteraceae family. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and native plant landscapes.

Helenium cuneifolium is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
