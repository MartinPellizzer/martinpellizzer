Hieracium inopifolium

Hieracium inopifolium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium inopifolium is known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
