Herderia stellulifera

Herderia stellulifera, commonly known as the star-leaf tamarisk, is a shrub native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a woody stem and is often found in dry, sandy soils. Its leaves are opposite and scale-like, providing a unique appearance.

It is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.
