Hypoxis camerooniana

Hypoxis camerooniana, commonly known as the Cameroon groundstar, is a flowering plant native to Cameroon and neighboring regions in Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional African medicine.

Hypoxis camerooniana is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
