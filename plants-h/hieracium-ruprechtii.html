Hieracium ruprechtii

Hieracium ruprechtii, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flower heads. The plant typically produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in mountainous areas.

Hieracium ruprechtii is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand cold climates.
