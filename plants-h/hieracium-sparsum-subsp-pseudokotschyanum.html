Hieracium sparsum subsp. pseudokotschyanum

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

pseudokotschyanum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

This subspecies is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in grasslands and open woodlands. It has a low-growing habit and is adapted to temperate climates.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
