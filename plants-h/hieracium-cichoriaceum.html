Hieracium cichoriaceum

Hieracium cichoriaceum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It produces numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds, contributing to its widespread distribution.

Hieracium cichoriaceum is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
