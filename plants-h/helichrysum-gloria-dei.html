Helichrysum gloria-dei

Helichrysum gloria-dei, commonly known as the glory of the desert, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flower heads that resemble miniature suns, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its aromatic, hairy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.

The plant's unique appearance and hardy nature make it a striking addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
