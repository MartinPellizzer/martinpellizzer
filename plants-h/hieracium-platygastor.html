Hieracium platygastor

Hieracium platygastor, commonly known as the flat-lobed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny environments.
