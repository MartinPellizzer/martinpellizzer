Hexacentris coccinea

Hexacentris coccinea, commonly known as the red orchid.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers. Its inflorescence consists of multiple small, showy flowers arranged in a spike.

Hexacentris coccinea is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant colors and unique floral structure.
