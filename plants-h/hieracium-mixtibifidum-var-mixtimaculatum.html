Hieracium mixtibifidum var. mixtimaculatum

Hieracium mixtibifidum var. mixtimaculatum, commonly known as the spotted hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and bright yellow flower heads. The leaves are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges, and the plant produces numerous small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, riverbanks, and along roadsides.
