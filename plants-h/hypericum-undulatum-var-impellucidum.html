Hypericum undulatum var. impellucidum

Hypericum undulatum var. impellucidum, commonly known as the smooth St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, distinguishing it from other St. John's wort varieties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
