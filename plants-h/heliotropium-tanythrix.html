Heliotropium tanythrix

Heliotropium tanythrix, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Heliotropium tanythrix is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
