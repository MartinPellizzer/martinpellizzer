Hieracium nigrescens subsp. succisum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

succisum, commonly known as the purple meadowhawk, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae). It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and clustered, yellowish-green to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges and often have a rough texture.

It is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early summer.
