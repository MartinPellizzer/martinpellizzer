Hieracium valoddae subsp. valoddae

Hieracium valoddae subsp.

valoddae, commonly known as the Valodda hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and milky sap.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has basal rosettes of lanceolate leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single head.
