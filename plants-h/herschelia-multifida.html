Herschelia multifida

Herschelia multifida, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, cylindrical seed pods that split open when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a bedding plant.

Herschelia multifida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
