Hoya phuluangensis

Hoya phuluangensis, commonly known as the Thai star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its waxy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has thick, succulent stems and dark green, glossy leaves. It is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Hoya phuluangensis prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
