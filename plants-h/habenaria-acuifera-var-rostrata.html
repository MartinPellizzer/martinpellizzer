Habenaria acuifera var. rostrata

Habenaria acuifera var. rostrata, commonly known as the rostrated marsh orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage. The plant produces slender, greenish-yellow flower spikes that bear small, fragrant orchid blooms. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and habitat preferences make it a notable species in botanical studies.
