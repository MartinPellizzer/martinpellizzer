Hieracium torquescens

Hieracium torquescens, commonly known as the twisted hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows and grassy areas. Hieracium torquescens is a perennial plant that thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
