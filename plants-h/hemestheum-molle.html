Hemestheum molle

Hemestheum molle, commonly known as the soft hemestheum, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Hemestheum molle is adapted to rocky and coastal environments, showing resilience to harsh conditions.
