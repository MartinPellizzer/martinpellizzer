Hieracium peasei

Hieracium peasei, commonly known as the Pease's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium peasei is often found in open habitats and can thrive in temperate climates.
