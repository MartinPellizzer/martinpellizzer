Hyalosema arfakianum

Hyalosema arfakianum, commonly known as the Arfak mountain orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the highlands of New Guinea.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is typically found growing on rocky substrates or in shaded forest areas. Its botanical significance lies in its limited distribution and potential ecological role in its native habitat.
