Hemsleya macrocarpa var. grandiflora

Hemsleya macrocarpa var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered hemslay, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and grows as an herbaceous perennial. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and its role in local ecosystems.
