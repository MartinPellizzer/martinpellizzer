Homalium stipulaceum

Homalium stipulaceum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Brazilian cherry, is a tropical evergreen tree belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions worldwide.
