Hieracium molluscum

Hieracium molluscum, commonly known as the mouse ear hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant has hairy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Hieracium molluscum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in a variety of light conditions.
