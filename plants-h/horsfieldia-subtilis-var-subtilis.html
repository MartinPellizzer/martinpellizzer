Horsfieldia subtilis var. subtilis

Horsfieldia subtilis var. subtilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, pendant-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often have a yellowish-green hue with dark markings.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for horticultural purposes.
