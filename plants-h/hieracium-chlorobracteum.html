Hieracium chlorobracteum

Hieracium chlorobracteum, commonly known as the green bract hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. Its flowers are typically yellow, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Hieracium chlorobracteum is often found in grasslands and open habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
