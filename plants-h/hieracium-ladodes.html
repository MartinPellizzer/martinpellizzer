Hieracium ladodes

Hieracium ladodes, commonly known as the Lad's Daisy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often glandular, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium ladodes is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
