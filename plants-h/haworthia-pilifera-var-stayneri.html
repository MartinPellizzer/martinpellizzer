Haworthia pilifera var. stayneri

Haworthia pilifera var. stayneri, commonly known as the Stayner's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white to pale yellow tubercles and translucent tips. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
