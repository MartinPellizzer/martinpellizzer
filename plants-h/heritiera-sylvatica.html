Heritiera sylvatica

Heritiera sylvatica, commonly known as wild silk cotton tree, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, pale yellow to white flowers. Heritiera sylvatica is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in some cultures.
