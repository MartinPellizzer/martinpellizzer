Hieracium procerellum

Hieracium procerellum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium procerellum is often found in disturbed habitats and can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in some regions.
