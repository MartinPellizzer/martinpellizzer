Hoya maximowayetii

Hoya maximowayetii, commonly known as the "Coral Cloud Hoya," is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with shades of white, pink, and green. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hoya maximowayetii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
