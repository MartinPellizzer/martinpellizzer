Hieracium praecox subsp. fragiliforme

Hieracium praecox subsp.

fragiliforme, commonly known as the fragile hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellowish to reddish flowers that appear in clusters.

The leaves are typically alternate, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture. This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

It is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
