Habenaria filicornis

Habenaria filicornis, commonly known as the feather-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, pale green to yellowish flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
