Hieracium bifidum var. subcaesium

Hieracium bifidum var. subcaesium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellowish to reddish composite flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

This variant is distinguished by its smaller, more compact inflorescence compared to other hawkweed species.
