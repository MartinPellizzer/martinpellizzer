Hieracium ochroleucum

Hieracium ochroleucum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Hieracium. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in meadows and grasslands. It produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across various habitats.

The leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
