Hieracium liptoviense subsp. amaurotheca

Hieracium liptoviense subsp.

amaurotheca, commonly known as the dark-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dark green, often hairy leaves and a compact inflorescence. It typically grows in dry, calcareous soils in meadows and rocky areas.
