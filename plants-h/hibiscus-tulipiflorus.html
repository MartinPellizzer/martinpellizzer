Hibiscus tulipiflorus

Hibiscus tulipiflorus, commonly known as the tropical hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Hibiscus tulipiflorus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
