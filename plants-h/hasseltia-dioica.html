Hasseltia dioica

Hasseltia dioica, commonly known as the mountain hawthorn, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, white to pink flowers that bloom in spring. The species is notable for its dioecious nature, with male and female flowers on separate plants.
