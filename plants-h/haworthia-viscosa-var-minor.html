Haworthia viscosa var. minor

Haworthia viscosa var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf Haworthia or miniature Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short, erect stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
