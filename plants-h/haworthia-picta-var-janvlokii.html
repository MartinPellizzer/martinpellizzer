Haworthia picta var. janvlokii

Haworthia picta var. janvlokii, commonly known as the Janvlokii Haworthia, is a compact, leafy succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The leaves are adorned with intricate, geometric patterns that give the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its unique aesthetics and low maintenance requirements.
