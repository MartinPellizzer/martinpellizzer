Hieracium Levicaule Subsp. Lanosiceps

Hieracium levicaule subsp. lanosiceps is a lesser-known member of the dandelion family known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. This plant is commonly found in grasslands and open habitats across Europe and parts of Asia. Its hairy stems and yellow flowers make it easily recognizable among similar species. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and soil health. Studying this subspecies offers insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in temperate regions.
