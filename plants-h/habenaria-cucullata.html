Habenaria cucullata

Habenaria cucullata, commonly known as the hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features a slender rhizome and produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive hood-like structure over the lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with a faintly colored lip.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
