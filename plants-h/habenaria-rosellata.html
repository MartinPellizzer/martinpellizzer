Habenaria rosellata

Habenaria rosellata, commonly known as the Rosella orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

Habenaria rosellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
