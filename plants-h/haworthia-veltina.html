Haworthia veltina

Haworthia veltina, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped foliage with distinctive white to yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, making it well-suited for arid environments. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
