Habenaria ochrantha

Habenaria ochrantha, commonly known as the red-tongued orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a deep red lip and white or pale pink petals. The plant grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

The orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
