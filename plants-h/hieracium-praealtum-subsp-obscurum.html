Hieracium praealtum subsp. obscurum

Hieracium praealtum subsp.

obscurum, commonly known as the dark hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in grasslands and woodland edges. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months.

The subspecies is distinguished by its darker coloration and slightly different morphology compared to other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
