Hieracium pallidum subsp. subcomatulum

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

subcomatulum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to pale orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The subspecies subcomatulum is distinguished by its shorter, more compact growth habit compared to other forms of H. pallidum.
