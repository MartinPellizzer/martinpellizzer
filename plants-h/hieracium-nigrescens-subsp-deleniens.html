Hieracium nigrescens subsp. deleniens

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

deleniens, commonly known as the Deleniens hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers arranged in a dense cluster. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species in certain regions.
