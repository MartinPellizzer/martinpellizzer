Haworthia arachnoidea var. translucens

Haworthia arachnoidea var. translucens, commonly known as the translucent Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are pale green with a translucent, waxy coating, giving it a unique appearance. The leaves are adorned with white to pale blue tubercles that resemble a spider's web, contributing to its common name. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
