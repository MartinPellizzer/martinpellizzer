Heterocaryum echinophorum var. oligacanthum

Heterocaryum echinophorum var. oligacanthum, commonly known as the spiny bramble, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dense, sprawling growth habit. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and have a rough, hairy texture.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystems.
