Hypericum bolivaricum

Hypericum bolivaricum, commonly known as the Bolivian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Bolivia and Peru.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that contain the seeds. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum bolivaricum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
