Hypericum ascyron var. micropetalum

Hypericum ascyron var. micropetalum, commonly known as the small-petaled St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, blooming from June to August. Its stems are woody and often branched, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
