Hydrangea taiwaniana

Hydrangea taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan hydrangea, is a species native to Taiwan.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, showy inflorescences composed of sterile flowers surrounded by fertile ones, which can range in color from white to pink or blue depending on soil pH. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth margin and a glossy green surface.

Hydrangea taiwaniana thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
