Hypericum incertum

Hypericum incertum, commonly known as uncertain St. John's wort, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

Hypericum incertum is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
