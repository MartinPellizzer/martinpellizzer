Hyptis trichopes

Hyptis trichopes, commonly known as the hairy hyptis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along stream banks.

Hyptis trichopes is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
