Hoarea revoluta

Hoarea revoluta, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Hoarea revoluta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and vibrant blooms.
