Hieracium aurantiacum subsp. neriedowae

Hieracium aurantiacum subsp.

neriedowae, commonly known as the red hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright orange to reddish yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a composite head.

The plant has basal and stem leaves that are usually hairy and have a serrated margin. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

This subspecies is native to Central and Eastern Europe and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
