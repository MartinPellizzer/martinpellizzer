Hieracium kerneri subsp. abbatiscellanum

Hieracium kerneri subsp.

abbatiscellanum, commonly known as the Abbatiscellanum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Italy. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped, with a rough texture.
