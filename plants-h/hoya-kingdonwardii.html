Hoya kingdonwardii

Hoya kingdonwardii, commonly known as the "Candy Cane Hoya," is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Hoya kingdonwardii thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a favored ornamental plant among horticulturists.
