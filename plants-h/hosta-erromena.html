Hosta erromena

Hosta erromena, commonly known as the Erromena Hosta, is a species native to Korea.

It is a perennial plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family, characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically a deep green with a silvery-white margin, creating a striking visual contrast. It produces small, fragrant, white flowers in late summer, which are clustered in loose racemes.
