Hieracium ovaliceps

Hieracium ovaliceps, commonly known as the oval-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium ovaliceps is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
