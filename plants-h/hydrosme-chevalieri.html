Hydrosme chevalieri

Hydrosme chevalieri, commonly known as the Madagascar water lily, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Madagascar.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide a habitat for various aquatic organisms. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the water, supporting its growth in shallow, still waters.

Hydrosme chevalieri plays an important role in maintaining the ecological balance of its native wetland environments.
