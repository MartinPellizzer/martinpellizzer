Hardenbergia huegelii

Hardenbergia huegelii, commonly known as the blue flax lily, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive blue to violet flowers. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Hardenbergia huegelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
