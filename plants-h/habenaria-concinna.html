Habenaria concinna

Habenaria concinna, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Philippines.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological significance.
