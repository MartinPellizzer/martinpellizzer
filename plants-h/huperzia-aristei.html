Huperzia aristei

Huperzia aristei, commonly known as the mountain huperzia, is a species of moss-like fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its upright, branching structure and small, lance-shaped leaves. Huperzia aristei is typically found in temperate regions and is adapted to thrive in cool, humid climates.

Its unique morphology and ecological niche make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
