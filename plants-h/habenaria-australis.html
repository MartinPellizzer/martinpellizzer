Habenaria australis

Habenaria australis, commonly known as the Australian marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in wetland and swampy environments, thriving in moist, shaded conditions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green or yellowish in color. Its roots are fibrous, and it often forms dense colonies in its natural habitat.

The orchid's flowering period occurs during the cooler months, contributing to its ecological significance in native ecosystems.
