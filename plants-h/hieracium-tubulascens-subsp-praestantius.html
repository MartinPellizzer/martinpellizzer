Hieracium tubulascens subsp. praestantius

Hieracium tubulascens subsp.

praestantius, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers, which consist of both ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, and it produces achenes as its seed dispersal unit.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is often found in temperate regions of Europe.
