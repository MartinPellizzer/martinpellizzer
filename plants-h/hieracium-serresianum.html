Hieracium serresianum

Hieracium serresianum, commonly known as the Serresian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is a perennial species that produces achenes as its seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
