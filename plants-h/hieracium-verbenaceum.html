Hieracium verbenaceum

Hieracium verbenaceum, commonly known as common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium verbenaceum is known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it an invasive species in some regions.
