Heritiera macrophylla

Heritiera macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf heritiera, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, and its fruits are capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
