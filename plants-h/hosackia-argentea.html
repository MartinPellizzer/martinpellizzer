Hosackia argentea

Hosackia argentea, commonly known as silver vetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Hosackia argentea is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, which helps improve soil fertility.
