Hieracium neyranum subsp. neyranum

Hieracium neyranum subsp.

neyranum, commonly known as the Neyran's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-red composite flowers.

The plant typically has basal and stem leaves that are broadly lance-shaped with rough, hairy surfaces. It is native to Central and Eastern Europe and thrives in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
