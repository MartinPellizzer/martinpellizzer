Hieracium orthocolon

Hieracium orthocolon, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and numerous yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant typically has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium orthocolon is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it an invasive species in some regions.
