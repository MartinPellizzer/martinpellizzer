Hymenocallis × festalis

Hymenocallis × festalis, commonly known as the hybrid spider lily, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with strap-like, narrow leaves that are evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The flowers bloom in the summer months, producing a fragrant, showy display that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
