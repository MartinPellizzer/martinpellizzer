Hieracium murorum subsp. sylvivagum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

sylvivagum, commonly known as the woodland hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and along stream banks.

The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, with leaves that are deeply lobed and hairy. It has a robust root system that helps it thrive in nutrient-rich soils.

This subspecies is characterized by its distinct morphology and ecological adaptability.
