Hypoxis villosa var. canescens

Hypoxis villosa var. canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf starflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy, silver-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers with a distinctive star-like shape, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
