Hieracium × longistolonosum

Hieracium × longistolonosum, commonly known as the long-stemmed hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its creeping growth habit and the production of yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically forms dense mats and has hairy stems and leaves. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, often becoming invasive.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
