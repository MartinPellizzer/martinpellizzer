Habenaria gymnadenioides

Habenaria gymnadenioides, commonly known as the Broad-leafed Pterostylis, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The flower spike produces a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

The plant is noted for its specialized pollination mechanism involving specific insect species.
