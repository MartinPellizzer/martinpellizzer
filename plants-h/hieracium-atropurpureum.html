Hieracium atropurpureum

Hieracium atropurpureum, commonly known as purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright purple to reddish flower heads that are composed of numerous small florets. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it often grows in meadows and disturbed soils.

Hieracium atropurpureum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
