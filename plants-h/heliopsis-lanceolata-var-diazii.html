Heliopsis lanceolata var. diazii

Heliopsis lanceolata var. diazii, commonly known as the Mexican firewheel, is a perennial flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its bright orange to yellow ray flowers and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun. It is drought-tolerant and well-suited for xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.

The botanical features of this variety make it a striking addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
