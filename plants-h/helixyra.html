Helixyra

Helixyra, commonly known as the spiral vine, is a unique plant characterized by its helical growth pattern, where the stems twist around supports in a spiral formation.

It belongs to the family Solanaceae, which includes nightshade plants, and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple.

Helixyra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in partial shade with well-drained soil.
