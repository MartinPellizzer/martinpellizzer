Hirtella paraensis

Hirtella paraensis, commonly known as the Brazilian hirtella, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. Hirtella paraensis is often found in shaded understory environments within its natural habitat.
