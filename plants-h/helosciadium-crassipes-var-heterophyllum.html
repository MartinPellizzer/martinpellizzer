Helosciadium crassipes var. heterophyllum

Helosciadium crassipes var. heterophyllum, commonly known as the broad-leaved helosciadium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a robust root system and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. The leaves are broad and lanceolate, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology compared to other varieties within the species.
