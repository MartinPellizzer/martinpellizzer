Hieracium glaucinum subsp. montolearense

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

montolearense, commonly known as the Montoleare hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow ray florets.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It is distinguished by its pale greenish-blue leaves and small, clustered flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
