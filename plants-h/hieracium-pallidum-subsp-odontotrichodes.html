Hieracium pallidum subsp. odontotrichodes

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

odontotrichodes, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine, soft hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

The subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and floral structure, making it a notable species in botanical studies.
