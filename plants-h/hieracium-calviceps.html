Hieracium calviceps

Hieracium calviceps, commonly known as the smooth hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragweed, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium calviceps is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in disturbed habitats.
