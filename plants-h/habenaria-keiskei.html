Habenaria keiskei

Habenaria keiskei, commonly known as the Keiske's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often spotted or streaked. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Habenaria keiskei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
