Hosta lancifolia

Hosta lancifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf hosta, is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-white margin. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers on tall, slender stems in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, offering ornamental value in both summer and winter.
