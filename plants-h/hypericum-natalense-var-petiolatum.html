Hypericum natalense var. petiolatum

Hypericum natalense var. petiolatum, commonly known as Natal goldenstar, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
