Hieracium lachenalii subsp. rudicaule

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

rudicaule, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and leaves.
