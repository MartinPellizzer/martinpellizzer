Hieracium tephrochlorellum, commonly known as the tephrochlorellum hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in meadows and open grasslands. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers arranged in a single head. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.
