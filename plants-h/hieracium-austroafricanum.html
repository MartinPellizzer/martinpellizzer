Hieracium austroafricanum

Hieracium austroafricanum, commonly known as the southern African groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, showing a preference for temperate climates. Its leaves are generally hairy and alternate, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.
