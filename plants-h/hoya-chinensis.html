Hoya chinensis

Hoya chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese Wisteria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing vines and clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, green, egg-shaped berries that mature to a dark purple or black. Hoya chinensis thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking blooms and foliage, which add a touch of elegance to indoor and outdoor spaces.
