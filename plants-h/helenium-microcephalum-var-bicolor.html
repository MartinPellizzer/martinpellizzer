Helenium microcephalum var. bicolor

Helenium microcephalum var. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor helenium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, orange to red flower heads with a yellow center. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet and features finely divided, fern-like leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
