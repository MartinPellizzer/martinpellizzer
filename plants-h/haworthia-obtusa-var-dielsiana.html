Haworthia obtusa var. dielsiana

Haworthia obtusa var. dielsiana, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking white to light green tubercles with dark markings, giving it a distinctive zebra-like appearance. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapted to its arid native habitat. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the growing season.

This variety is highly sought after for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements in indoor gardening.
