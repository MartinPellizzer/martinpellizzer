Helianthemum polyanthum

Helianthemum polyanthum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, gray-green leaves that are adapted to withstand hot, dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its compact form and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
