Habenaria chrysea

Habenaria chrysea, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which are often adorned with green or brown markings. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring moist, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Habenaria chrysea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
