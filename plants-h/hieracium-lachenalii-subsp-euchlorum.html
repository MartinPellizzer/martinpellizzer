Hieracium lachenalii subsp. euchlorum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

euchlorum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowering stems bear clusters of small, yellow disk flowers.

This subspecies is known for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its ornamental value in gardens.
