Hieracium pseudosvaneticum subsp. kharthlicum

Hieracium pseudosvaneticum subsp.

kharthlicum, commonly known as the Kharthlicum hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems, typical of many hawkweed species.

This subspecies is native to Central Asia and is often found in meadows and open grasslands. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in the summer.

The plant is generally herbaceous and grows to a height of about 20 to 50 centimeters.
