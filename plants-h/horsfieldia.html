Horsfieldia

Horsfieldia, commonly known as the orchid tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and includes species such as Horsfieldia zollingeri, which is widely cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Horsfieldia species typically have a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves.
