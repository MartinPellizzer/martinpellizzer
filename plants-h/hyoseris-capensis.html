Hyoseris capensis

Hyoseris capensis, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow ray flowers and a central disk of tubular flowers, typical of the family. It grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils.

Hyoseris capensis is often found in coastal dunes and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
