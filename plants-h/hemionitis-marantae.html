Hemionitis marantae

Hemionitis marantae, commonly known as the silverleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-variegated leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rhizomes are sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a starch source.
