Habenaria acalcarata

Habenaria acalcarata, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate floral structure, featuring a distinctive spur that extends from the base of the flower. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a smooth texture and a waxy appearance.

Habenaria acalcarata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
