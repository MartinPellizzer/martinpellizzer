Hopea minima var. perakensis

Hopea minima var. perakensis, commonly known as the Perak hopea, is a small evergreen tree native to the Malay Peninsula.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family, which is characteristic of tropical rainforests. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule containing several winged seeds, which aid in wind dispersal.
