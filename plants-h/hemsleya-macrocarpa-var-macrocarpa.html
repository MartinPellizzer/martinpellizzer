Hemsleya macrocarpa var. macrocarpa

Hemsleya macrocarpa var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant arrowleaf, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length, with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
