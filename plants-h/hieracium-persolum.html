Hieracium persolum

Hieracium persolum, commonly known as the Persian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium persolum is often found in open habitats such as meadows and slopes, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
