Hibiscus diplocrater var. sinuatilobus

Hibiscus diplocrater var. sinuatilobus, commonly known as the narrow-lobed hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers with five broad, wavy-edged petals that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are ovate with a distinctive sinuate base, giving the plant its specific epithet. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
