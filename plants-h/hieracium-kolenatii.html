Hieracium kolenatii

Hieracium kolenatii, commonly known as the Kolenatii hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. This plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its flowers are yellow and bloom from late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.

Hieracium kolenatii is notable for its hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in its preferred habitat.
