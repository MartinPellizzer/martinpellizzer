Hosta sieboldii f. polycarpellata

Hosta sieboldii f. polycarpellata, commonly known as the Siebold's Hosta with multiple carpels, is a variant of the Siebold's Hosta, native to Japan.

It is a perennial plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family, characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in late summer, which are clustered in spikes and can be white or pale blue in color. It thrives in partial to full shade and is well-suited for moist, well-drained soils in gardens.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
