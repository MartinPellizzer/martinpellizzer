Hieracium rottii

Hieracium rottii, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped with a rough texture. Hieracium rottii has a robust root system that helps it thrive in various environmental conditions.

It is also known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
