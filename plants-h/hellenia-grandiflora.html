Hellenia grandiflora

Hellenia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered helene, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features upright, branched stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers with prominent, petal-like bracts that resemble petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticultural enthusiasts.
