Helicia kjellbergii var. kjellbergii

Helicia kjellbergii var. kjellbergii, commonly known as the Kjellberg's helicia, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in inflorescences, while the fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and resilience in shaded environments.
