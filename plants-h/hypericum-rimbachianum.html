Hypericum rimbachianum

Hypericum rimbachianum, commonly known as the yellow hypericum, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Hypericum rimbachianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
