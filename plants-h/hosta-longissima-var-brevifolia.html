Hosta longissima var. brevifolia

Hosta longissima var. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed giant hosta, is a striking variety of the hosta plant characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves.

This cultivar features broad, waxy, and often variegated leaves that can reach impressive lengths, though its foliage is more compact compared to other hosta varieties. The plant produces upright, bell-shaped flowers in late summer, typically in shades of white or pale blue. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its unique leaf structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among gardeners.
