Hieracium valdepilosum subsp. alfenzinum

Hieracium valdepilosum subsp.

alfenzinum, commonly known as the Alfenzin's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has basal and stem leaves that are usually hairy and lanceolate in shape.
