Hypericum × desetangsii var. ambiguum

Hypericum × desetangsii var. ambiguum, commonly known as the hybrid St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has woody stems and alternate, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal use.
