Hypericum oliganthum var. kiusianum

Hypericum oliganthum var. kiusianum, commonly known as the Kiusi Hypericum, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, reddish-brown berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens in suitable climates.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated for both aesthetic and conservation purposes.
