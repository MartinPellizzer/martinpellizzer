Hieracium ornatissimum

Hieracium ornatissimum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are basal, rough, and hairy, with a distinctive yellowish-green color.

The showy hawkweed produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn.
