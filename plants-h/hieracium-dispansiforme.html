Hieracium dispansiforme

Hieracium dispansiforme, commonly known as the spreading hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow ray florets. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a hairy stem and leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

Hieracium dispansiforme is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
