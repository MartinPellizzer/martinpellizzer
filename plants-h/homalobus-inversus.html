Homalobus inversus

Homalobus inversus, commonly known as the upside-down plant, is a unique member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Vietnam. Due to its rare and unusual appearance, it is a sought-after species among botanists and plant enthusiasts.
