Haworthia cooperi subsp. batteniae

Haworthia cooperi subsp.

batteniae, commonly known as the Batten's Haworthia, is a compact, leafy succulent native to South Africa. It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white tubercles and translucent windows, which aid in photosynthesis and water retention.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collectors and indoor gardens.
