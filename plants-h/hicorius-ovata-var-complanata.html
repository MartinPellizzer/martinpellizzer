Hicorius ovata var. complanata

Hicorius ovata var. complanata, commonly known as the flat-leaf hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, ovate leaves that are typically flat and smooth, with a waxy surface. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a prominent central stamen. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
