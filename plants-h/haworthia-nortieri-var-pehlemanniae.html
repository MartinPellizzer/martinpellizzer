Haworthia nortieri var. pehlemanniae

Haworthia nortieri var. pehlemanniae, commonly known as the Pehlemann's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or pale markings. The leaves are arranged in a rosette shape, providing a compact and visually appealing appearance. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.
