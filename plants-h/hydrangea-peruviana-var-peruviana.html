Hydrangea peruviana var. peruviana

Hydrangea peruviana var. peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian hydrangea, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. Its foliage consists of serrated, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy.

The plant thrives in partial shade and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.
