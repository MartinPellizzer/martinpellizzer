Helichrysum dockeri

Helichrysum dockeri, commonly known as the coastal helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flower heads, which resemble small sunflowers. The plant typically grows in coastal regions, where it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. It is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal gardens.

The leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs that help reduce water loss.
