Hieracium levicaule subsp. sublonchodes

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

sublonchodes, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides, meadows, and pastures.

The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in early summer. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and bear several flower heads.

The species is characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology and is often found in dry, sunny environments.
