Hedysarum tenellum

Hedysarum tenellum, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow or orange flowers in early summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in soil stabilization efforts.

Hedysarum tenellum is also valued for its role in nitrogen fixation, benefiting surrounding vegetation.
