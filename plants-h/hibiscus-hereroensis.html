Hibiscus hereroensis

Hibiscus hereroensis, commonly known as the Herero hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soils, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Hibiscus hereroensis is a perennial shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
