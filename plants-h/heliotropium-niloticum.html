Heliotropium niloticum

Heliotropium niloticum, commonly known as the Nile heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of the Middle East.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs.

Heliotropium niloticum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
