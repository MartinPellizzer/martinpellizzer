Hieracium asciadium

Hieracium asciadium, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish to reddish-orange flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it a rough texture. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the hawkweed genus.
