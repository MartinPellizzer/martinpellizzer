Habenaria bermejoensis

Habenaria bermejoensis, commonly known as the Bermejo orchid, is a species of orchid native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in montane forests. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning it attaches itself to tree trunks or branches without being parasitic.

The orchid's flowers are usually pale green or white with a distinctive, intricate pattern.
