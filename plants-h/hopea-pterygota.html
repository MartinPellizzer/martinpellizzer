Hopea pterygota

Hopea pterygota, commonly known as the winged hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The tree is characterized by its distinctive winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation. It typically grows in lowland tropical rainforests and plays a role in the ecological dynamics of these ecosystems.

The plant is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which has been used in traditional craftsmanship and construction.
