Hibiscus cooperi

Hibiscus cooperi, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to China and Taiwan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a prominent yellow center. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and glossy. Hibiscus cooperi is a deciduous shrub that thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
