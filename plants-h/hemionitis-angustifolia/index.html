Hemionitis Angustifolia

Hemionitis angustifolia is a rare and unique fern found primarily in specific regions of Asia. It is known for its delicate, narrow leaves that give it its scientific name, meaning "narrow leaf." This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often associated with mountainous areas. Due to its limited habitat and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
