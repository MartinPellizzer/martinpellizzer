Hibiscus natalitus

Hibiscus natalitus, commonly known as Natal hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers bloom throughout the summer months.

Hibiscus natalitus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
