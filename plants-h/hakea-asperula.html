Hakea asperula

Hakea asperula, commonly known as the rough hakea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine, rough hairs. The plant produces small, urn-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Hakea asperula is often found in coastal and heathland ecosystems.
