Huberia piranii

Huberia piranii, commonly known as the Piranii nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its fruit is a bright red berry, which contains several seeds and is often consumed by local wildlife.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
