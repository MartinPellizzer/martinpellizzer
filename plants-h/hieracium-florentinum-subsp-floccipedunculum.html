Hieracium florentinum subsp. floccipedunculum

Hieracium florentinum subsp.

floccipedunculum, commonly known as the Florentine hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy stems.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is considered a herbaceous perennial.
