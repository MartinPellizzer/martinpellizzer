Hibiscus ellisii

Hibiscus ellisii, commonly known as the Ellis's hibiscus, is a rare flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in wetland or swampy habitats. Hibiscus ellisii is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in moist, acidic soils.
