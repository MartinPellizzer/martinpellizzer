Hesperantha tenuifolia

Hesperantha tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf hesperantha, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant blooms during the summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

Hesperantha tenuifolia prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
