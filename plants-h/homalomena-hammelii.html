Homalomena hammelii

Homalomena hammelii, commonly known as the purple-veined arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features arrowhead-shaped leaves with a deep green upper surface and a lighter underside, often marked by purple veins. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Homalomena hammelii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
