Helichrysum zivojini

Helichrysum zivojini, commonly known as the Zivojno golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow to orange flowers that remain vibrant even after drying. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its foliage is usually grey-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small, narrow leaves.

Helichrysum zivojini is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
