Haworthia angustifolia f. baylissii

Haworthia angustifolia f. baylissii, commonly known as the Bayliss's narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with white or pale markings along the edges. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
