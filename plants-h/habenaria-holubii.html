Habenaria holubii

Habenaria holubii, commonly known as the Holub's orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched, bearing several showy, orchid-like blooms.

The flowers are typically greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, adapted for specific pollination mechanisms.
