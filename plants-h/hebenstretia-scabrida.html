Hebenstretia scabrida

Hebenstretia scabrida, commonly known as the rough hebenstretia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in terminal inflorescences, often displaying shades of yellow or orange.

Hebenstretia scabrida is adapted to grow in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in grassland or shrubland environments.
