Helichrysum rugulosum

Helichrysum rugulosum, commonly known as the rough helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy, rugose leaves. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is aromatic and can be used in traditional medicine.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
