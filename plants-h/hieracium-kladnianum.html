Hieracium kladnianum

Hieracium kladnianum, commonly known as the Kladnian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small yellow flowers in the summer, followed by seed heads that disperse easily.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
