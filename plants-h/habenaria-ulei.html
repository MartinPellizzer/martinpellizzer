Habenaria ulei

Habenaria ulei, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Habenaria ulei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts.
