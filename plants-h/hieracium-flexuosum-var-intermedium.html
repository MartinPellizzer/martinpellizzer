Hieracium flexuosum var. intermedium

Hieracium flexuosum var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate bentleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats and has a low-growing, sprawling habit. Its leaves are usually curled or folded, giving it the common name "bentleaf.".

It is often found in meadows, wetlands, and along riverbanks.
