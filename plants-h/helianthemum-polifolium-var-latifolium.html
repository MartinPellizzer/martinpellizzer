Helianthemum polifolium var. latifolium

Helianthemum polifolium var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has woody stems covered in fine, white hairs, and its leaves are broad, ovate, and often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
