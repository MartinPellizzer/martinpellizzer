Hieracium sterrochaetium

Hieracium sterrochaetium, commonly known as the rough-haired hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Hieracium sterrochaetium is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
