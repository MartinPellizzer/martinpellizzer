Hemionitis cochisensis

Hemionitis cochisensis, commonly known as the Chihuahuan fern, is a rare and endemic plant species found primarily in the Chihuahuan Desert of North America.

It belongs to the family Hemionitidaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble those of a traditional fern. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments with low precipitation.
