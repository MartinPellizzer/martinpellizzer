Hedychium speciosum

Hedychium speciosum, commonly known as the white ginger lily, is a tropical perennial plant belonging to the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its striking, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and can grow up to 3 meters in height. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense clumps.

Hedychium speciosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
