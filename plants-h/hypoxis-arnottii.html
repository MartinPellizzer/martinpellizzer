Hypoxis arnottii

Hypoxis arnottii, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense cluster at the top of a leafless stem. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Hypoxis arnottii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
