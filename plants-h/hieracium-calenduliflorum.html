Hieracium calenduliflorum

Hieracium calenduliflorum, commonly known as the golden hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium calenduliflorum is often found in open habitats and can spread through its rhizomes, making it a common weed in some regions.
