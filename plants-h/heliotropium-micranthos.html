Heliotropium micranthos

Heliotropium micranthos, commonly known as the small-flowered heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often opposite in arrangement. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Heliotropium micranthos is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
