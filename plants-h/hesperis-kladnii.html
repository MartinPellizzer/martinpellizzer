Hesperis kladnii

Hesperis kladnii, commonly known as the Alpine honesty, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant produces small, white flowers with four petals, typically blooming in late summer. Its foliage consists of basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hesperis kladnii is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
