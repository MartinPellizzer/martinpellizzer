Haworthia graminifolia

Haworthia graminifolia, commonly known as the grass-leaved Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
