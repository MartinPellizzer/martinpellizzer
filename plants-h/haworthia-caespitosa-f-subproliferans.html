Haworthia caespitosa f. subproliferans

Haworthia caespitosa f. subproliferans, commonly known as the compact Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative houseplant.
