Hieracium subulatidens subsp. cuneifrons

Hieracium subulatidens subsp.

cuneifrons, commonly known as the slender spearthistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features narrow, spiny leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are typically yellow, arranged in a composite head.

The plant thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is characterized by its subulate (needle-like) leaves and a cuneiform (wedge-shaped) base, giving it its specific epithet.

This subspecies is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its diverse floral structures and ecological significance.
