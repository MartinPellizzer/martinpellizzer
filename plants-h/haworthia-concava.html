Haworthia concava

Haworthia concava, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with distinctive white or yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Haworthia concava is prized for its compact size and unique appearance, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
