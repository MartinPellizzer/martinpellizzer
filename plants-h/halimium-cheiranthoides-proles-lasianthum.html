Halimium cheiranthoides proles lasianthum

Halimium cheiranthoides proles lasianthum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
