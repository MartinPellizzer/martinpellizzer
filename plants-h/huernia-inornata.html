Huernia inornata

Huernia inornata, commonly known as the smooth horned cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, cylindrical stem covered in small, white to yellowish tubercles. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers that range from white to pale pink, typically blooming in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
