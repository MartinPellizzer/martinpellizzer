Haworthia pilifera f. acuminata

Haworthia pilifera f. acuminata, commonly known as the needle Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with sharp, needle-like tips that give it its distinctive appearance. The leaves are covered in a white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
