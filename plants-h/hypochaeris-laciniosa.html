Hypochaeris laciniosa

Hypochaeris laciniosa, commonly known as the fringed weed or yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas and has hairy stems and leaves. Its yellow flowers bloom in the spring and summer, attracting various pollinators.

Hypochaeris laciniosa is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds.
