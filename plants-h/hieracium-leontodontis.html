Hieracium leontodontis

Hieracium leontodontis, commonly known as the leopard's bane, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.
