Hippeastrum aulicum f. robustum

Hippeastrum aulicum f. robustum, commonly known as the robust hippeastrum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
