Hypoxis bampsiana subsp. bampsiana

Hypoxis bampsiana subsp.

bampsiana, commonly known as the Bamps' yellowstar, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.
