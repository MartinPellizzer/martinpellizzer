Hieracium lyratum

Hieracium lyratum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, rough-textured leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in a variety of habitats including meadows and woodland edges. The plant's inflorescence consists of a single, showy flower head surrounded by bracts.

It is often found in moist, well-drained soils and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
