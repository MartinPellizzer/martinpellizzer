Hoya acanthominima

Hoya acanthominima, commonly known as the "Coral Hoya," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, thick leaves that are often variegated with white or pink markings. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Hoya acanthominima is a climbing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular among indoor gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
