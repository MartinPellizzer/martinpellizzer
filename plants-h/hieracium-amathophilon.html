Hieracium amathophilon

Hieracium amathophilon, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas, often forming dense colonies.

It is a perennial species that spreads through both seeds and rhizomes.
