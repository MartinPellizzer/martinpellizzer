Habenaria barbata

Habenaria barbata, commonly known as the feather-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its roots are often mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
