Hieracium melannephes

Hieracium melannephes, commonly known as the black-centred hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black central disc florets, which contrast with its yellow ray florets. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It has hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often have a reddish tinge.

Hieracium melannephes is a perennial species that produces small, dandelion-like seeds dispersed by the wind.
