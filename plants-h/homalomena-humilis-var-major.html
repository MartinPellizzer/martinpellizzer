Homalomena humilis var. major

Homalomena humilis var. major, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with red spathes, which appear in the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This varietal is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
