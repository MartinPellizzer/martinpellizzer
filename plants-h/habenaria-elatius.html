Habenaria elatius

Habenaria elatius, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa, including South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinct lip that is often darker.

Habenaria elatius thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or woodland habitats.
