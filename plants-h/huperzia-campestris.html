Huperzia campestris

Huperzia campestris, commonly known as the field huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is evergreen and can be found growing in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. Huperzia campestris has a rhizomatous root system that helps it anchor in soil and absorb nutrients.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitats.
