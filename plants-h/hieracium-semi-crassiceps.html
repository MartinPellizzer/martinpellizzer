Hieracium semi-crassiceps

Hieracium semi-crassiceps, commonly known as the rough hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
