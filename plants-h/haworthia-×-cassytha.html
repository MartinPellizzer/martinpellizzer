Haworthia × cassytha

Haworthia × cassytha, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette shape and distinctive zebra-like markings on its fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is prized for its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal in succulent gardens. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
