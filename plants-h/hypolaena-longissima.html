Hypolaena longissima

Hypolaena longissima, commonly known as the long-stemmed hypolaena, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence. The plant features a slender, erect stem that can reach up to several meters in height. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Hypolaena longissima is often cultivated for its dramatic appearance and is used in both horticultural and traditional medicinal contexts.
