Hieracium dublanense

Hieracium dublanense, commonly known as the Dublan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of the Balkans.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its clustered, yellowish to reddish flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and meadows. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium dublanense is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable environments.
