Hieracium vulgatum f. longipedicellatum

Hieracium vulgatum f. longipedicellatum, commonly known as the long-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the genus Hieracium. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This form is a variant of the common hawkweed, exhibiting slightly different floral and vegetative features.
