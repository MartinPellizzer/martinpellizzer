Hortensia macrophylla f. isonotaki

Hortensia macrophylla f. isonotaki, commonly known as the Japanese hydrangea, is a cultivar characterized by its large, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Hydrangeaceae family and is native to Japan. The plant is known for its ability to change flower color based on soil pH, with blue flowers in acidic soil and pink in alkaline conditions. It thrives in partial shade and requires regular watering to maintain its vibrant blooms.

This variety is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
