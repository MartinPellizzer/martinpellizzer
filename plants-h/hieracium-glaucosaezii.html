Hieracium glaucosaezii

Hieracium glaucosaezii, commonly known as the glaucous hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy, grayish-green leaves and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It is native to regions of Europe and western Asia.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
