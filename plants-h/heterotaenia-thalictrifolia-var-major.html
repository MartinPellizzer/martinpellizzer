Heterotaenia thalictrifolia var. major

Heterotaenia thalictrifolia var. major, commonly known as the large-leafed meadow rue, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear compound leaves with finely divided, fern-like foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense, branched clusters. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive addition to gardens with appropriate growing conditions.
