Hieracioides Olympica

Hieracioides olympica is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece. It is known for its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Its unique appearance and limited habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to its vulnerability, efforts are underway to protect and study this fascinating plant.
