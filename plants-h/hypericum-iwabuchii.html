Hypericum iwabuchii

Hypericum iwabuchii, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a reddish or purple hue.

The flowers are usually small and occur in clusters, blooming in late spring to early summer.
