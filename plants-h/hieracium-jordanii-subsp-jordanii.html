Hieracium jordanii subsp. jordanii

Hieracium jordanii subsp.

jordanii, commonly known as Jordan's hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open grasslands.

The plant features upright stems with rough, hairy surfaces and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often lobed. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in dense, flat-topped inflorescences.

It is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
