Habenaria cadetiorum

Habenaria cadetiorum, commonly known as the slender helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in forested areas with high humidity and partial shade. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single or few leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and often have a faintly colored lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
