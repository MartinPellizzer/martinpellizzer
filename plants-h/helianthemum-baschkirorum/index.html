Helianthemum Baschkirorum

Helianthemum baschkirorum is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the rockrose family and is known for its vibrant yellow blooms that thrive in harsh, arid environments. This plant is often found growing on rocky slopes and in dry, sunny habitats where it adapts well to extreme conditions. Its scientific name honors the Bashkir people, reflecting its geographical significance in that region. Due to its limited distribution and ecological importance, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
