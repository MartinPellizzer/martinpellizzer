Hibiscus ficulneus

Hibiscus ficulneus, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a contrasting yellow center. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Hibiscus ficulneus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
