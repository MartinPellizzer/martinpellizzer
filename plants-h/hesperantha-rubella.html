Hesperantha rubella

Hesperantha rubella, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers with a white throat and prominent stamens. The plant produces strap-like, dark green leaves that are often mistaken for grass. Hesperantha rubella is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance and blooming habits make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
