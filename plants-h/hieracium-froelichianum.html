Hieracium froelichianum

Hieracium froelichianum, commonly known as Froelich's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and flowering heads that produce yellow ray florets. Native to Central Asia, this species thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has a creeping rootstock and can form dense colonies, which may impact local vegetation.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies of alpine and arid environments.
