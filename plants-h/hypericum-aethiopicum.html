Hypericum aethiopicum

Hypericum aethiopicum, commonly known as the Ethiopian rose or yellow hypericum, is a perennial shrub native to Africa.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has narrow, slightly hairy leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to Mediterranean climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal use.
