Hieracium pulcherrimum

Hieracium pulcherrimum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium pulcherrimum is often found in temperate climates and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
