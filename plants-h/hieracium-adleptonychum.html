Hieracium adleptonychum

Hieracium adleptonychum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late spring to early summer. Its flowering heads consist of numerous small florets arranged in a disc-like structure surrounded by pale green to yellow bracts.

Hieracium adleptonychum is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
