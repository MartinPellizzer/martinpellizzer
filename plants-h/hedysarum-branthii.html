Hedysarum branthii

Hedysarum branthii, commonly known as the Ethiopian milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow to orange flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its seeds are also utilized as a food source in certain regions.
