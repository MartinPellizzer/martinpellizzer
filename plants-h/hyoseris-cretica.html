Hyoseris cretica

Hyoseris cretica, commonly known as the Cretan ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range from yellow to reddish-brown.

Hyoseris cretica is known for its hardy nature and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance.
