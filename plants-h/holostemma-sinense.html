Holostemma sinense

Holostemma sinense, commonly known as the Chinese holostemma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. Holostemma sinense thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.
