Hymenocyclus flavocroceus

Hymenocyclus flavocroceus, commonly known as the golden crocus, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

Hymenocyclus flavocroceus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
