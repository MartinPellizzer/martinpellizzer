Herniaria oranensis subsp. oranensis

Herniaria oranensis subsp.

oranensis, commonly known as the Mediterranean carpetweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, rounded leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces tiny white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
