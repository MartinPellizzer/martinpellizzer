Hieracium eulissum

Hieracium eulissum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium eulissum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in agricultural areas.
