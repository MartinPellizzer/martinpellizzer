Habenaria alinae

Habenaria alinae, commonly known as the Alina's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
