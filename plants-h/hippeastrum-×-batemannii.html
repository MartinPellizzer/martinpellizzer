Hippeastrum × batemannii

Hippeastrum × batemannii, commonly known as the batemannii amaryllis, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces striking, strap-like leaves that are broad and lance-shaped. It is a bulbous plant that thrives in well-draining soil and requires a period of dormancy for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum × batemannii is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
