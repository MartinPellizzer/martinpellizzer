Hieracium pictum

Hieracium pictum, commonly known as the painted hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium pictum is known for its ornamental value and has been cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
