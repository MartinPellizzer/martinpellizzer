Hoya wightii subsp. palniensis

Hoya wightii subsp.

palniensis, commonly known as the Palni hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India. It is a trailing or climbing plant with thick, fleshy stems and produces clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers.

The leaves are typically thick and leathery, often with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts.

The flowers are usually pale green to white, with a slight pink or red hue at the base.
