Hieracium Neocerinthe Subsp. Lagascanum

Hieracium neocerinthe subsp. lagascanum is a rare and striking member of the dandelion family known for its vibrant yellow flowers. Found primarily in the mountainous regions of southern Europe, this subspecies thrives in rocky and alpine habitats. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Understanding and protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
