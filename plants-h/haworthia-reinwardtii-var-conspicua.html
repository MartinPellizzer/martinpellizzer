Haworthia reinwardtii var. conspicua

Haworthia reinwardtii var. conspicua, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellow markings. The leaves are covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps the plant retain moisture in arid environments. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
