Helianthemum nummularium f. multiplex

Helianthemum nummularium f. multiplex, commonly known as the silver carpet carnation, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to southern Europe.

It features small, rounded leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces numerous showy, pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a desirable plant for gardeners seeking a hardy, drought-tolerant ground cover.
