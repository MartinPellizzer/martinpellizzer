Hibiscus katguhriensis

Hibiscus katguhriensis, commonly known as the Katguhri hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and can grow up to several meters in height. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Hibiscus katguhriensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
