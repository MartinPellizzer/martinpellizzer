Helianthemum × monspessulanum

Helianthemum × monspessulanum, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a hybrid between Helianthemum nummularium and Helianthemum canariense, resulting in a plant with distinctive holly-like leaves and yellow flowers. The species is often found growing in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is also valued for its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
