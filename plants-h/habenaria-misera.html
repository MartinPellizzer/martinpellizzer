Habenaria misera

Habenaria misera, commonly known as the sad orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces a single spike of flowers during the growing season.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
