Hieracium alpinum var. subeximium

Hieracium alpinum var. subeximium, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.

Due to its ecological role, it is sometimes considered a valuable plant for supporting local biodiversity.
