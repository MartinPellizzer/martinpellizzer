Haworthia angustifolia f. grandis

Haworthia angustifolia f. grandis, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically bluish-green with white or pale markings. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative houseplant.
