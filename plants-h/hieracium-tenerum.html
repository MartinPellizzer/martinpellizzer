Hieracium tenerum

Hieracium tenerum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish to reddish-orange flowers. The plant typically has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in wetlands.

Hieracium tenerum is an herbaceous perennial that plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
