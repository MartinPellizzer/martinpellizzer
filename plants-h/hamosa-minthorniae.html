Hamosa minthorniae

Hamosa minthorniae, commonly known as the silverleaf mint.

It is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features silver-gray foliage due to a dense covering of fine hairs. It produces small, pale blue to purple flowers in clusters during the summer months.

Hamosa minthorniae is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional medicine.
