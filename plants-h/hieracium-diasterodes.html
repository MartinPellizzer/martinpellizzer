Hieracium diasterodes

Hieracium diasterodes, commonly known as the starray hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its flowers are yellow and daisy-like, with ray florets that give it a distinctive appearance.

Hieracium diasterodes is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
