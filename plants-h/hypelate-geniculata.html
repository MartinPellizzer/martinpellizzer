Hypelate geniculata

Hypelate geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging stems that help it climb or trail along surfaces. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Hypelate geniculata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive growth habit and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
