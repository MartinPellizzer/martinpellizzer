Hieracium sparsiflorum subsp. simplicicaule

Hieracium sparsiflorum subsp.

simplicicaule, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in dry grasslands, open woodlands, and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense heads, which bloom from late spring to early summer.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ability to produce a large number of seeds.
