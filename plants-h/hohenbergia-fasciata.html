Hohenbergia fasciata

Hohenbergia fasciata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, zebra-striped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or silver markings. The plant produces striking orange or red flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Hohenbergia fasciata is relatively low maintenance and thrives in humid environments.
