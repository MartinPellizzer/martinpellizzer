Hieracium transiens subsp. heterodontoidiforme

Hieracium transiens subsp.

heterodontoidiforme, commonly known as the variable hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-red composite flowers.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and hairy, with a rough texture. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

This subspecies is known for its variability in leaf shape and flower color, which contributes to its common name.
