Hieracium koehleri

Hieracium koehleri, commonly known as the Koehler's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, moist environments. Hieracium koehleri is often found in high-altitude regions where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
