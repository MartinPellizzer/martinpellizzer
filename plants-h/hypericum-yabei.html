Hypericum yabei

Hypericum yabei, commonly known as the Yabei St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
