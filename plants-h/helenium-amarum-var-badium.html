Helenium amarum var. badium

Helenium amarum var. badium, commonly known as the Badium helenium, is a variant of the common sneezeweed.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to North America, particularly found in the eastern and central regions of the United States. The plant features showy, orange to red-orange ray flowers surrounded by yellow disk flowers, blooming from late summer to early fall. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
