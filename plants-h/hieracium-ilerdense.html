Hieracium ilerdense

Hieracium ilerdense, commonly known as the Ilerdene hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer.

Hieracium ilerdense is often found in southern regions of Spain and Portugal, where it thrives in sunny, temperate conditions.
