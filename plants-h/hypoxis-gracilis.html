Hypoxis gracilis

Hypoxis gracilis, commonly known as slender yellowstar, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to dry environments and has a short growing season.

Hypoxis gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
