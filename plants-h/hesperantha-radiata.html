Hesperantha radiata

Hesperantha radiata, commonly known as the star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, fragrant blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and moths.

Hesperantha radiata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
