Hieracium borshomiense

Hieracium borshomiense, commonly known as the Borshomiense Hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
