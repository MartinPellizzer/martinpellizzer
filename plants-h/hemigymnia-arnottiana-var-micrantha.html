Hemigymnia arnottiana var. micrantha

Hemigymnia arnottiana var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered hemigymnia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. This variety is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small fauna.
