Habenaria longistigma

Habenaria longistigma, commonly known as the long-stemmed marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often spotted or streaked. It typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and forest floors.

Its flowering period usually occurs in the spring and early summer.
