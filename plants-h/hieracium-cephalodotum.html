Hieracium cephalodotum

Hieracium cephalodotum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a cluster of yellow flowers at the top of the stem. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Hieracium cephalodotum is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
