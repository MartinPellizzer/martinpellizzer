Helenium griffithii

Helenium griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's sneezeweed, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has lance-shaped, hairy leaves. Its flowering period occurs in late summer to early autumn, and it produces numerous small, disk-like flowers.

Helenium griffithii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
