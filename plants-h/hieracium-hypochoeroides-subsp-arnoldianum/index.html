Hieracium Hypochoeroides Subsp. Arnoldianum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. arnoldianum is a lesser-known species of dandelion found primarily in western europe. it belongs to the asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers. this plant thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats such as roadsides and fields. despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators. its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
