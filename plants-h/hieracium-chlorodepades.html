Hieracium chlorodepades

Hieracium chlorodepades, commonly known as the paleleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue, giving it its common name.

Hieracium chlorodepades produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.
