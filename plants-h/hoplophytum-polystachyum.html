Hoplophytum polystachyum

Hoplophytum polystachyum, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in summer. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in rocky or sandy soils.

It is often used in rock gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental appearance.
