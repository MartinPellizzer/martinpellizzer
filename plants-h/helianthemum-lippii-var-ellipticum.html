Helianthemum lippii var. ellipticum

Helianthemum lippii var. ellipticum, commonly known as the elliptic rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fleshy, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is drought-tolerant.

This species is often used in xeriscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
