Howardia maxima

Howardia maxima, commonly known as the Natal orchid or Natal spider plant, is a unique succulent native to southern Africa.

It features a distinctive appearance with a thick, fleshy stem that often branches into clusters of long, thin leaves resembling spider legs. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Howardia maxima is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
