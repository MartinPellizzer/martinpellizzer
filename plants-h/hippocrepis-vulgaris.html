Hippocrepis vulgaris

Hippocrepis vulgaris, commonly known as the common rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapting to arid environments.

The common rock rose is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens.
