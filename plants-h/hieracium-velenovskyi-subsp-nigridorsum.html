Hieracium velenovskyi subsp. nigridorsum

Hieracium velenovskyi subsp.

nigridorsum, commonly known as the black-veined hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It features bright yellow composite flowers that are typically clustered in a single head, surrounded by leaf-like bracts.

The plant has basal and alternate leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
