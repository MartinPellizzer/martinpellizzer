Hoffmannia amplexifolia

Hoffmannia amplexifolia, commonly known as the clinging leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cucurbitaceae.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring leaves that wrap around the stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in humid environments with well-drained soil. The flowers are typically small and yellow, with a structure typical of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
