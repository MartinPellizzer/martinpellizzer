Hieracium canonigrum

Hieracium canonigrum, commonly known as the blackfoot hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and its leaves are typically lance-shaped with hairy surfaces. The species is characterized by its dark-colored stems and is considered a perennial herb.
