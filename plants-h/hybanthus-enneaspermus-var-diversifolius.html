Hybanthus enneaspermus var. diversifolius

Hybanthus enneaspermus var. diversifolius, commonly known as the variable leaf hybanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf morphology, with leaves varying in shape and size across different populations. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical significance lies in its morphological variation and potential ecological roles within its native habitats.
