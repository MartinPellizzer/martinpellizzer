Hieracium deanum

Hieracium deanum, commonly known as the Dean's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium deanum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth habit and ability to colonize various habitats.
