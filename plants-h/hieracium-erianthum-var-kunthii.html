Hieracium erianthum var. kunthii

Hieracium erianthum var. kunthii, commonly known as the Kunth's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers, which are composed of many small florets arranged in a circular pattern. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

This variant is distinguished by its specific morphological traits and geographic distribution compared to other hawkweed species.
