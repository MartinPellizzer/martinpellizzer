Haemanthus puniceus var. fortuita

Haemanthus puniceus var. fortuita, commonly known as the firecracker lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features vivid red, orange, and yellow flowers that resemble firecrackers, blooming in summer. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that form a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice among gardeners.
