Hieracium Caesium Subsp. Deltoideum

Hieracium caesium subsp. deltoideum is a lesser-known member of the dandelion family known for its unique leaf shape and subtle beauty. This plant thrives in moist, open habitats and is often found in meadows and along streambanks. Its leaves are deeply lobed and form a distinctive deltoid pattern, giving it its scientific name. While not as popular as other dandelion species, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Its delicate flowers and hardy nature make it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
