Haemanthus Virescens

Haemanthus virescens is a striking plant native to southern Africa known for its vibrant green leaves and dramatic red flower stalks. This species belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both gardeners and botanists alike.
