Hypericum acutum var. rotundifolium

Hypericum acutum var. rotundifolium, commonly known as the rounded-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has oval-shaped, waxy leaves that are often arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
