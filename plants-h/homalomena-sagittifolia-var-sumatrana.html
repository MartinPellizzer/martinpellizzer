Homalomena sagittifolia var. sumatrana

Homalomena sagittifolia var. sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran arrowleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with broad, arrow-shaped foliage. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor cultivation.
