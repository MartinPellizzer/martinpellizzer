Haworthia pulchella

Haworthia pulchella, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or cream-colored markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the growing season. It prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight.

Haworthia pulchella is drought-tolerant and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal choice for low-maintenance indoor gardening.
