Hieracium splendens

Hieracium splendens, commonly known as the splendour hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers that are typically surrounded by a ring of pale green to white ray florets. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium splendens is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in many environments.
