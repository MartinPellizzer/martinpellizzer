Hieracium murorum subsp. platydiaphanoides

Hieracium murorum subsp.

platydiaphanoides, commonly known as the broad-leafed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, lanceolate leaves and yellow disk flowers with ray florets.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has a taproot system and produces achenes as its seeds.

The subspecies is distinguished by its broader leaves and more open inflorescence compared to other hawkweed species.
