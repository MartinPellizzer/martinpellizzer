Helichrysum bracteatum var. eriopodum

Helichrysum bracteatum var. eriopodum, commonly known as the woolly everlasting or African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and bright yellow, dandelion-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its flowers are typically long-lasting when dried, making it popular in floral arrangements.
