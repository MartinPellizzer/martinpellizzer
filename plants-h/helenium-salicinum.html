Helenium salicinum

Helenium salicinum, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and bright yellow ray flowers that resemble an arrowhead shape. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils along stream banks and wetlands. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Helenium salicinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in wetland environments.
