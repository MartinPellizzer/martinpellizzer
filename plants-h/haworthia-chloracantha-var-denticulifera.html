Haworthia chloracantha var. denticulifera

Haworthia chloracantha var. denticulifera, commonly known as the toothed Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
