Horsfieldia congestiflora

Horsfieldia congestiflora, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and the plant often grows in shaded, humid environments.

Horsfieldia congestiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
