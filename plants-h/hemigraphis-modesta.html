Hemigraphis modesta

Hemigraphis modesta, commonly known as velvetleaf or redleaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep red to purple and green, often with contrasting veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant foliage.

Hemigraphis modesta is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
