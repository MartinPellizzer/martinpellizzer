Heptaptera crenata

Heptaptera crenata, commonly known as the crested heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, leathery leaves with a distinctive wavy or toothed edge. Its flowers are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters, and bloom in late spring to early summer.
