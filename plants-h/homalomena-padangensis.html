Homalomena padangensis

Homalomena padangensis, commonly known as the Padang arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Homalomena padangensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in some cultures.
