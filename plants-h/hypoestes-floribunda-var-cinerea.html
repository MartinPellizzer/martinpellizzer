Hypoestes floribunda var. cinerea

Hypoestes floribunda var. cinerea, commonly known as silver trumpets, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling small trumpets. The plant has hairy, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
