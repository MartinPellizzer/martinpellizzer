Huernia brevirostris

Huernia brevirostris, commonly known as the short-beaked horned cucumber, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in small, spiky tubercles. The plant produces pale green, cylindrical flowers that are typically clustered at the ends of the stems. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Huernia brevirostris is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
