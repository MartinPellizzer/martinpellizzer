Helichrysum attenuatum var. attenuatum

Helichrysum attenuatum var. attenuatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
