Heteropterys escalloniifolia var. ochnifolia

Heteropterys escalloniifolia var. ochnifolia, commonly known as the Ochnifolia variant, is a tropical shrub native to the Amazon basin.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically alternate and pinnate, with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential horticultural use.
