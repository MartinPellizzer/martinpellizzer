Hubertia tomentosa var. tomentosa

Hubertia tomentosa var. tomentosa, commonly known as the wooly hubertia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers the stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, which helps it survive in arid conditions.

It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its rugged appearance.
