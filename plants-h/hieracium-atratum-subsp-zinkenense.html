Hieracium atratum subsp. zinkenense

Hieracium atratum subsp.

zinkenense, commonly known as the Black-centred Hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dark purple to black central flower head, which contrasts with its yellow ray florets.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Its flowers bloom from June to August, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
