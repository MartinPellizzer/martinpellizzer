Hedysarum kasteki

Hedysarum kasteki, commonly known as the Kastekis vetch, is a perennial legume native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with three oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. The plant has a robust root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion. It is adapted to grow in dry, rocky environments and is often found in alpine and steppe habitats.

Hedysarum kasteki is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
