Hydrangea hortensia f. otaksa

Hydrangea hortensia f. otaksa, commonly known as the Otaksa hydrangea, is a cultivar characterized by its unique floral structure and color variation.

It belongs to the Hydrangeaceae family and is native to Japan, where it thrives in humid, temperate climates. The plant features large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink or blue, depending on soil pH. Its foliage is typically dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to different growing conditions.
