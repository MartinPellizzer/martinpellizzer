Hieracium neritodon

Hieracium neritodon, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright stems and basal rosette of leaves, which are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows and woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
