Haworthia caespitosa var. consanguinea

Haworthia caespitosa var. consanguinea, commonly known as the clustered Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on tall, slender stalks during the warmer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or container cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
