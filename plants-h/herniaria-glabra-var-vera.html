Herniaria glabra var. vera

Herniaria glabra var. vera, commonly known as the smooth herniaria, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white, woolly hairs.

This species is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny environments.
