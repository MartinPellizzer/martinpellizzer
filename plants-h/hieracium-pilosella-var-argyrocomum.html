Hieracium pilosella var. argyrocomum

Hieracium pilosella var. argyrocomum, commonly known as the silverleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It is a perennial herb that can form dense colonies, often spreading through rhizomes.

Its botanical features make it both a common weed and a subject of ecological study.
