Haworthiopsis viscosa var. variabilis

Haworthiopsis viscosa var. variabilis, commonly known as the Variegated Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant is characterized by its slow growth and ability to store water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
