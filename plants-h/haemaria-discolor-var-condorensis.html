Haemaria discolor var. condorensis

Haemaria discolor var. condorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian violet, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in cloud forests at high altitudes. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.
