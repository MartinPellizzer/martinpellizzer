Hymenolepis dregeana

Hymenolepis dregeana, commonly known as the African nettle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other nettle species. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and it is typically found in moist, shaded environments.

Hymenolepis dregeana is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
