Hieracium aethalolepium

Hieracium aethalolepium, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to pale green flower heads, which are typically composed of numerous small florets. The plant has basal leaves that are often heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other open habitats.

Hieracium aethalolepium is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is native to Europe and parts of Asia.
