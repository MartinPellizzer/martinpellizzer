Hunga minutiflora

Hunga minutiflora, commonly known as the tiny flower vine, is a small, climbing plant native to tropical rainforests.

It produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often clustered in small inflorescences. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
