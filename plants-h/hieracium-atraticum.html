Hieracium atraticum

Hieracium atraticum, commonly known as the black hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. The plant produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.

It is considered a perennial species, with a life cycle that spans multiple growing seasons.
