Helianthemum melanothrix

Helianthemum melanothrix, commonly known as the black-stemmed rockrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in rocky or limestone soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its dark green leaves are covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in coastal areas and is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
