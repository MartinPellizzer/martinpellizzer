Habenaria stylites subsp. rhodesiaca

Habenaria stylites subsp.

rhodesiaca, commonly known as the Rhodesian slender orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa. It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, erect stem and produces a single spike of small, greenish-white flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it has a fibrous root system.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
