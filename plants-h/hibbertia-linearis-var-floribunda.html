Hibbertia linearis var. floribunda

Hibbertia linearis var. floribunda, commonly known as the slender hibbertia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, angular stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal regions. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are clustered in racemes.

Hibbertia linearis var. floribunda is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for gardens and coastal landscapes.
