Hieracium pulmonarioides proles pseudocerinthe

Hieracium pulmonarioides proles pseudocerinthe, commonly known as the false lungwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

Its foliage and floral structure make it a distinctive and often ornamental species in its native habitat.
