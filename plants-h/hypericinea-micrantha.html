Hypericinea micrantha

Hypericinea micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is known for its medicinal properties.

The plant's compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and small spaces.
