Huernia guttata subsp. calitzdorpensis

Huernia guttata subsp.

calitzdorpensis, commonly known as the Calitzdorp horned orchid, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems and small, fleshy leaves that help it store water.

The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive horn-like shape. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among succulent enthusiasts.
