Hieracium expansum

Hieracium expansum, commonly known as the broadleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

Hieracium expansum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun.
