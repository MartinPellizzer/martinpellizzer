Herrea laticalyx

Herrea laticalyx, commonly known as the broad-leafed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are broad and often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Herrea laticalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
