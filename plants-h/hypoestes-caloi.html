Hypoestes caloi

Hypoestes caloi, commonly known as the "Snowflake" or "Fairy Fan," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored bracts that resemble small flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous true flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Hypoestes caloi thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
