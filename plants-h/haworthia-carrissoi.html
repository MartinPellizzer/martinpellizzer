Haworthia carrissoi

Haworthia carrissoi, commonly known as the Carrissoi Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white tubercles and a central spine. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
