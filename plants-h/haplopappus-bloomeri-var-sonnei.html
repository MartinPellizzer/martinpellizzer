Haplopappus bloomeri var. sonnei

Haplopappus bloomeri var. sonnei, commonly known as Sonne's hairy pappus, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is distinguished by its soft, hairy foliage and small yellow to orange composite flowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in desert flora studies.
