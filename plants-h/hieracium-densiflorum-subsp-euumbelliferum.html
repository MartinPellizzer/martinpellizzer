Hieracium densiflorum subsp. euumbelliferum

Hieracium densiflorum subsp.

euumbelliferum, commonly known as the European wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
