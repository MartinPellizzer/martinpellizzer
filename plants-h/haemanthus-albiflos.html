Haemanthus albiflos

Haemanthus albiflos, commonly known as the white-flowered haemanthus, is a striking evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of glossy, dark green leaves that form a dense, compact base. The plant produces tall, slender stems topped with clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, thriving in Mediterranean climates.
