Homoianthus bonplandi

Homoianthus bonplandi, commonly known as the Bonplandi's flame tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in coastal areas and dry forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
