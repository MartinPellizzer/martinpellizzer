Hieracium cymosum subsp. subglomeratum

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

subglomeratum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of yellow flowers.

The plant typically has hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Its botanical features make it identifiable by its dense inflorescences and distinctive leaf morphology.
