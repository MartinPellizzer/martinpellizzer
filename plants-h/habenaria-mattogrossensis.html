Habenaria mattogrossensis

Habenaria mattogrossensis, commonly known as the Brazilian marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in wetlands and marshy areas. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient absorption.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
