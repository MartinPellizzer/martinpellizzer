Hieracium luculentum

Hieracium luculentum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features upright stems with alternate, heart-shaped or ovate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are bright yellow, arranged in a single head, and are surrounded by a cluster of bracts.

It is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early summer.
