Haworthia albicans

Haworthia albicans, commonly known as the White Haworthia, is a species of succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically white or pale green with distinctive markings. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short, erect stems. Haworthia albicans is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
