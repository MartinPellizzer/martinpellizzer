Habenaria lacei

Habenaria lacei, commonly known as the lace orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, intricate flowers that often have a pale green or white color. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and wetlands. Its flowering period usually occurs during the summer months.

Habenaria lacei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
