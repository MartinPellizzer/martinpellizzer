Hedysarum Ferganense Var. Minjanense

Hedysarum ferganense var. minjanense is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. This variant of the Hedysarum species is known for its small, pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in rocky and semi-arid environments, often growing in alpine meadows and steppe areas. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
