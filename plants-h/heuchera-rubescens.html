Heuchera rubescens

Heuchera rubescens, commonly known as the red sunset alumroot, is a perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, mounding growth habit and its striking foliage, which features deep red to burgundy tones. The plant produces upright, showy spikes of white to pale pink flowers in the spring. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Heuchera rubescens is valued for its ornamental foliage and its ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
