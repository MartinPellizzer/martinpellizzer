Helicia peltata

Helicia peltata, commonly known as the broad-leaved helicia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Helicia peltata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
