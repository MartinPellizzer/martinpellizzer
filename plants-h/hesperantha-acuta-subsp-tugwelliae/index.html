Hesperantha Acuta Subsp. Tugwelliae

Hesperantha acuta subsp. tugwelliae is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family native to southern Africa. This rare plant is known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that range from white to deep pink. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known species in the world of cultivated plants.
