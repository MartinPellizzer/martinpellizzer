Holcolemma canaliculatum

Holcolemma canaliculatum, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that are often veined with a distinctive narrow rib. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom during the day and close at night. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Holcolemma canaliculatum plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
