Hymenocallis limaensis

Hymenocallis limaensis, commonly known as the yellow hurricane lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces strap-like, dark green leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Hymenocallis limaensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
