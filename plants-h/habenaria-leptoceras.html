Habenaria leptoceras

Habenaria leptoceras, commonly known as the slender-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with prominent spurred petals. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall herbaceous appearance.

Botanically, it is characterized by its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
