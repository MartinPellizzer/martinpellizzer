Hieracium umbellatum subsp. kluchoricum

Hieracium umbellatum subsp.

kluchoricum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and rough to the touch, with hairy stems.

The subspecies kluchoricum is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology and flowering pattern compared to other subspecies of Hieracium umbellatum.
