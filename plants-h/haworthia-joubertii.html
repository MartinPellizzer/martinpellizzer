Haworthia joubertii

Haworthia joubertii, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white to yellowish tubercles and marginal teeth. The plant produces small, white flowers on thin, upright stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Haworthia joubertii is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
