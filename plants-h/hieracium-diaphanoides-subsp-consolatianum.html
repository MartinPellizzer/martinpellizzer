Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. consolatianum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

consolatianum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-red composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring moist to moderately dry conditions. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a rough texture, while the stems are upright and often branched.

This subspecies is distinguished by its translucent, waxy leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
