Hieracium murorum subsp. tholerotrichum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

tholerotrichum, commonly known as the woolly groundsel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, often reddish stems and leaves.

The plant produces yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its subspecies status reflects distinct morphological traits that set it apart from other members of the Hieracium genus.
