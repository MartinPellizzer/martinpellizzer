Haworthia reinwardtii

Haworthia reinwardtii, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy, tubular leaves that are often marked with white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
