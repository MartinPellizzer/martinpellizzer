Hieracium subtardans

Hieracium subtardans, commonly known as the late hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its flowers are yellow with a central disk, and it produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Hieracium subtardans is often found in open areas where it can receive ample sunlight.
