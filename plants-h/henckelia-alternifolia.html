Henckelia alternifolia

Henckelia alternifolia, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stems. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and indoor plant collections.
