Habenaria costaricensis

Habenaria costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that resemble butterflies. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in its native habitat.
