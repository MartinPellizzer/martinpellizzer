Hieracium lachenalii subsp. amaurochlorum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

amaurochlorum, commonly known as the dark green hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dark green, often hairy leaves and yellow disk flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dense, compact growth habit and the presence of glandular trichomes on its foliage. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
