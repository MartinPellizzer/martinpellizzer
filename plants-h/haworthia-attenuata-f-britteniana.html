Haworthia attenuata f. britteniana

Haworthia attenuata f. britteniana, commonly known as the Britteniana Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with distinct, white or pale yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
