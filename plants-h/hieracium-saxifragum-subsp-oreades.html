Hieracium saxifragum subsp. oreades

Hieracium saxifragum subsp.

oreades, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

The plant has hairy, glandular leaves and produces yellow flowers in a cluster at the end of each stem. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in high-altitude meadows and scree slopes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its range.
