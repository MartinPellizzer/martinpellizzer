Hibiscus volkensii

Hibiscus volkensii, commonly known as the Kenyan hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring large, showy flowers with five petals. The leaves are ovate and dark green, often with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces bright yellow stamens and a red or orange pistil, which attract pollinators.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.
