Helenium latifolium

Helenium latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf ironweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces bright orange to red flower heads, which are composed of numerous small, tubular flowers arranged in a spherical shape. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a rough texture and serrated edges.

Helenium latifolium thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
