Hieracium Bourgaei Subsp. Bicolor

Hieracium bourgaei subsp. bicolor is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern France. It belongs to the dandelion family, known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive leaf structures. This subspecies is characterized by its two-toned coloration, with white and yellow petals that set it apart from other similar species. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable plant in its native environment. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful flora for future generations.
