Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp. trichojuranum

Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp.

trichojuranum, commonly known as the goldenrod hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped with a rough texture. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and plant identification.
