Henriettella venosa

Henriettella venosa, commonly known as the veined henriettella, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

The plant produces clusters of pale yellow flowers with prominent veins, which are a distinguishing feature. Henriettella venosa thrives in cool, humid environments and is typically found in shaded, forested areas.
