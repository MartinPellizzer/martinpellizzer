Helichrysum attenuatum

Helichrysum attenuatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers that resemble daisies and are often used in floral arrangements due to their longevity. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum attenuatum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
