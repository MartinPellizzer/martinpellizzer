Hieracium plumbeum

Hieracium plumbeum, commonly known as the blue hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive blueish tinge.

Hieracium plumbeum is often found in moist environments and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
