Habenaria falcicornis

Habenaria falcicornis, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and linear, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with pendulous flowers that have a unique, intricate structure. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with red or purple markings, and they emit a faint, sweet fragrance.

Habenaria falcicornis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
