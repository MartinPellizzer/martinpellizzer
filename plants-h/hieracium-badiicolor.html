Hieracium badiicolor

Hieracium badiicolor, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking purple to pinkish-red flowers, which are typically clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils.

It is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early summer.
