Hieracium virenticeps var. appendiculare

Hieracium virenticeps var. appendiculare, commonly known as the green-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright green, hairy leaves and yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive.

Its botanical features make it identifiable by its unique leaf coloration and floral structure.
