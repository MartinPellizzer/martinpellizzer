Hieracium canum subsp. anosciadium

Hieracium canum subsp.

anosciadium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
