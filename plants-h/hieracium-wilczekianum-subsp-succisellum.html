Hieracium wilczekianum subsp. succisellum

Hieracium wilczekianum subsp.

succisellum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of dark green, hairy leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It is native to Central and Eastern Europe, where it is often found in open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural areas.
