Herniaria grimmii

Herniaria grimmii, commonly known as the kidney vetch, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, kidney-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed ground. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to anchor firmly in harsh environments.

Herniaria grimmii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
