Hieracium villosiceps subsp. sericotrichum

Hieracium villosiceps subsp.

sericotrichum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy, erect stems and basal rosettes of leaves, which are typically covered in fine, white pubescence.
