Helicia obovatifolia var. mixta

Helicia obovatifolia var. mixta, commonly known as the mixed-leaf helicia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptical to ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
