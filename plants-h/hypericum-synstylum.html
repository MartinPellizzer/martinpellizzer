Hypericum synstylum

Hypericum synstylum, commonly known as the narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with four petals. The plant is often found in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. It has a woody stem and is part of the Hypericaceae family, known for its medicinal properties.

Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
