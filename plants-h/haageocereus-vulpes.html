Haageocereus vulpes

Haageocereus vulpes, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive silver-blue coloration due to its dense covering of fine, white hairs. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Haageocereus vulpes is relatively drought-tolerant, making it a hardy choice for arid environments.
