Hieracium ramosissimum subsp. cryptadenophyllum

Hieracium ramosissimum subsp.

cryptadenophyllum, commonly known as the cryptadenophyllum hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dense, glandular hairs on the stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in moist, grassy habitats and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

This plant is also noted for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
