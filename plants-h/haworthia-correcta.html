Haworthia correcta

Haworthia correcta, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces tall, slender stems with clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
