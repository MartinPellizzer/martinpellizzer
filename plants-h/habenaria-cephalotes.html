Habenaria cephalotes

Habenaria cephalotes, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive helmet-like structure on the flower, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

Habenaria cephalotes is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
