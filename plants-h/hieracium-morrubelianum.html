Hieracium morrubelianum

Hieracium morrubelianum, commonly known as the Scottish hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Scottish Highlands.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Hieracium morrubelianum is often found in association with other alpine and subalpine flora.
