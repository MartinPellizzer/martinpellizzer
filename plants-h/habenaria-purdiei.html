Habenaria purdiei

Habenaria purdiei, commonly known as the Pink Pterostylis, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and heathlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy pinkish-white flower. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it produces a distinctive, spurred labellum.

Habenaria purdiei is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with specific mycorrhizal fungi.
