Hydrangea mangshanensis

Hydrangea mangshanensis, commonly known as the Mangshan hydrangea, is a rare species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a glossy surface.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is considered a botanical curiosity due to its limited distribution.
