Herrania nitida f. nitida

Herrania nitida f. nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf hibiscus, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are densely covered in fine, silvery-white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically blooming throughout the warm seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its foliage and flowers attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
