Hieracium apricans

Hieracium apricans, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, rough stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
