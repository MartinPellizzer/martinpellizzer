Hieracium Atratum Subsp. Gudbergii

Hieracium atratum subsp. gudbergii is a lesser-known member of the dandelion family known for its striking dark purple flowers. Found primarily in northern regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in the wild. Studying this species offers insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in temperate climates.
