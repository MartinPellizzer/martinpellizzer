Himatanthus bracteatus

Himatanthus bracteatus, commonly known as the yellow starburst, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble a star, with five petals and numerous stamens. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
