Hypericum stenobotrys

Hypericum stenobotrys, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Hypericaceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, particularly in regions with temperate climates. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are narrow and slightly succulent. Hypericum stenobotrys is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and rocky habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
