Helichrysum baxteri

Helichrysum baxteri, commonly known as the Natal goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. It is well adapted to arid conditions and is drought tolerant.

The flowers are visited by various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
