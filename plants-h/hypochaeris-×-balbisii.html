Hypochaeris × balbisii

Hypochaeris × balbisii, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or toothed. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hypochaeris × balbisii is known for its ability to spread rapidly through both seeds and rhizomes.
