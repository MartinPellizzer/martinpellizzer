Haageocereus achaetus

Haageocereus achaetus, commonly known as the moon cactus, is a cacti species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its swollen, rounded stem and spiny, cylindrical stems. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant flowers and unique appearance.

However, it is important to note that the plant is not typically long-lived and may require careful care to thrive.
