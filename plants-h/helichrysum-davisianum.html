Helichrysum davisianum

Helichrysum davisianum, commonly known as the Cape goldring, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, linear leaves and has a compact, shrub-like growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

The flowers are often used in dried floral arrangements due to their long-lasting color and texture.
