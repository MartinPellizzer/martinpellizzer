Hieracium monophylliscum

Hieracium monophylliscum, commonly known as the single-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its single, large basal leaf and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Native to Europe, this plant thrives in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense colonies.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
