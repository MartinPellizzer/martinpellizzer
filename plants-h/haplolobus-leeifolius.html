Haplolobus leeifolius

Haplolobus leeifolius, commonly known as the silky leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery foliage and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it suitable for container gardening as well.

Haplolobus leeifolius is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
