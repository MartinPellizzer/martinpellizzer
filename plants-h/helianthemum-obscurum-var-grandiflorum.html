Helianthemum obscurum var. grandiflorum

Helianthemum obscurum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the Greater Rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, solitary flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
