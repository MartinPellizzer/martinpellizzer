Habenaria beharensis

Habenaria beharensis, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its roots are fibrous, and it has a short rhizome that helps it anchor in the soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
