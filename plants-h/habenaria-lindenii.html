Habenaria lindenii

Habenaria lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories where it thrives in humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that allow it to spread gradually over time. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts interested in rare orchids.
