Haworthia indigoa

Haworthia indigoa, commonly known as the Blue Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, with white or light green markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
