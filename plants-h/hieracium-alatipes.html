Hieracium alatipes

Hieracium alatipes, commonly known as the winged hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium alatipes is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in certain regions.
