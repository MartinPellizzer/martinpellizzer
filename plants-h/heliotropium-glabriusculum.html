Heliotropium glabriusculum

Heliotropium glabriusculum, commonly known as the smooth heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
