Hieracium incisum subsp. pallescens

Hieracium incisum subsp.

pallescens, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a distinct silvery or pale green coloration, which helps in identifying the species.

The flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental appeal in gardens.
