Huperzia subulata

Huperzia subulata, commonly known as the spike moss, is a species of fern-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in dense, compact clusters. This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Huperzia subulata is an evergreen plant that can live for many years, with its rhizomes forming a network that helps it survive in various conditions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for small invertebrates.
