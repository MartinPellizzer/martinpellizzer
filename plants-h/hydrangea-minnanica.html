Hydrangea minnanica

Hydrangea minnanica, commonly known as the Minnan hydrangea, is a species native to southern China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink or blue, depending on soil pH. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth margin and a glossy green surface.

Hydrangea minnanica thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
