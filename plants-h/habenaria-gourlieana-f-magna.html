Habenaria gourlieana f. magna

Habenaria gourlieana f. magna, commonly known as the giant habenaria, is a striking orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 cm in diameter, featuring a vibrant combination of purple and white hues. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it can receive indirect sunlight. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, contributing to its lush appearance.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
