Helichrysum pedunculare

Helichrysum pedunculare, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum pedunculare is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
