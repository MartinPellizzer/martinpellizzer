Hebanthe paniculata f. ovatifolia

Hebanthe paniculata f. ovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed hebanthe, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens.
