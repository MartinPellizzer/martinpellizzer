Hypoestes parvula

Hypoestes parvula, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, evergreen shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 inches. The plant features bright, colorful bracts that resemble tiny flowers, which are actually modified leaves. Its leaves are oval-shaped, glossy, and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Hypoestes parvula thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
