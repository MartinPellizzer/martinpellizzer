Halocnemum strobilaceum

Halocnemum strobilaceum, commonly known as sea saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to arid and saline environments.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and succulent leaves. Halocnemum strobilaceum plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils and is often found in salt marshes and dunes.
