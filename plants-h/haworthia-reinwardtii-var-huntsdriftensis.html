Haworthia reinwardtii var. huntsdriftensis

Haworthia reinwardtii var. huntsdriftensis, commonly known as the Huntsdrift Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The leaves are often adorned with translucent windows, which allow light to penetrate to the plant's interior for photosynthesis. It produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect sunlight.
