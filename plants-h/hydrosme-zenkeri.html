Hydrosme zenkeri

Hydrosme zenkeri, commonly known as the Zanzibar lily, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale blue flowers arranged in a dense cluster. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils near the coast, often in areas with high humidity and salt spray. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in warm, tropical climates.

Hydrosme zenkeri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
