Hieracium isomeles

Hieracium isomeles, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium isomeles is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a common weed in agricultural areas.
