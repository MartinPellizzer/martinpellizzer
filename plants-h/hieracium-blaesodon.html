Hieracium blaesodon

Hieracium blaesodon, commonly known as the bladderleaf hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and basal rosettes of leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and disturbed soils.
