Hieracium flocculosum

Hieracium flocculosum, commonly known as the fluffhead hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are basal and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive fluffy appearance.

Hieracium flocculosum is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts.
