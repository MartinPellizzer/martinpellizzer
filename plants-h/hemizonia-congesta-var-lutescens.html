Hemizonia congesta var. lutescens

Hemizonia congesta var. lutescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered hemizonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while the inflorescence consists of numerous small florets arranged in a dense cluster.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
