Hydrangea paniculata f. praecox

Hydrangea paniculata f. praecox, commonly known as the early panicle hydrangea, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy panicles of white to pinkish flowers that bloom early in the season, typically in late spring. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in garden settings.
