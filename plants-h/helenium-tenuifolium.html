Helenium tenuifolium

Helenium tenuifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved sneezeweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant has slender, upright stems with finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and ecologically valuable addition to the landscape.
