Hakea oligantha

Hakea oligantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces cylindrical, red or pink flower spikes that bloom in spring. Hakea oligantha is adapted to dry, sandy soils and can thrive in full sun.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
