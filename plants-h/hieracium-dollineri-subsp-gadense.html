Hieracium dollineri subsp. gadense

Hieracium dollineri subsp.

gadense, commonly known as the Gadensian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy structure.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of basal leaves that are often hairy and have a serrated edge.

The flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
