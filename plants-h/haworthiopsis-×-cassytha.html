Haworthiopsis × cassytha

Haworthiopsis × cassytha, commonly known as the hybrid Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is a hybrid between Haworthiopsis and Haworthia species, resulting in a unique combination of traits. The plant features compact rosettes of fleshy, often blue-green leaves with white or yellowish markings. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the growing season.

Due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
