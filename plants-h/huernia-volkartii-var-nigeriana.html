Huernia volkartii var. nigeriana

Huernia volkartii var. nigeriana, commonly known as the Nigeriana moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a swollen, fleshy stem that resembles a cactus, often with a distinctive blue or purple hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
