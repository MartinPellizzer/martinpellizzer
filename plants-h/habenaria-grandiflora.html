Habenaria grandiflora

Habenaria grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces tall, erect stems with numerous lance-shaped leaves. Its most striking characteristic is its large, showy flowers, which are usually pale green to yellow with intricate patterns.

Habenaria grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
