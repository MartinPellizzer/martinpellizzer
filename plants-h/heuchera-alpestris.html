Heuchera alpestris

Heuchera alpestris, commonly known as the alpine heuchera, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or alpine environments. The plant typically features broad, heart-shaped leaves that can vary in color from green to red or purple, depending on the variety. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the summer months.

Heuchera alpestris is well adapted to cold climates and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover in alpine settings.
