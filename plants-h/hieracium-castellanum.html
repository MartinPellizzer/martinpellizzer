Hieracium castellanum

Hieracium castellanum, commonly known as the Castellane hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a single, upright stem that bears yellow flowers. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in many regions.
