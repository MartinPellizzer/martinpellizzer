Hieracium mnoolepium

Hieracium mnoolepium, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and basal rosettes of leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is often found in meadows and grassy areas, preferring moist, well-drained soils.

Hieracium mnoolepium is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
