Habenaria unalascensis subsp. elata

Habenaria unalascensis subsp.

elata, commonly known as the tall lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to the western United States. It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and dense, linear leaves.

The plant produces a showy inflorescence of white to pale pink flowers arranged in a loose raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
