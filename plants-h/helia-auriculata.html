Helia auriculata

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.
