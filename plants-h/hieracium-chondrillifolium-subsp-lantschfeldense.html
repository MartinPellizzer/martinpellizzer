Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp. lantschfeldense

Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp.

lantschfeldense, commonly known as the Lantschfelden hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine meadows, preferring well-drained soils. It is known for its small, narrow leaves and is often found in high-altitude environments.
