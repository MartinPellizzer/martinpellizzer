Habenaria latipetala

Habenaria latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petalled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream sides. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is broader than the other petals.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral structure.
