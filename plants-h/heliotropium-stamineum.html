Heliotropium stamineum

Heliotropium stamineum, commonly known as the staminal heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually violet or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
