Hirtella macrophylla

Hirtella macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf hirtella, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its glossy, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Hirtella macrophylla is typically found in humid, tropical environments, often growing in shaded understory conditions.
