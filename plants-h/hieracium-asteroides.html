Hieracium asteroides

Hieracium asteroides, commonly known as the star dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a cluster of small flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in early summer, and the plant produces fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Hieracium asteroides is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
