Haworthiopsis koelmaniorum

Haworthiopsis koelmaniorum, commonly known as the Koelman's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or pale markings. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
