Hypoestes catatii

Hypoestes catatii, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful, and velvety bracts that resemble small snowflakes. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which are clustered in whorls along the stems.

Hypoestes catatii thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
