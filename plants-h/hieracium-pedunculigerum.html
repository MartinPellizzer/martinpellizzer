Hieracium pedunculigerum

Hieracium pedunculigerum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soil. It features upright stems with numerous flowering heads that bloom in shades of yellow to orange.

The leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
