Hieracium paulophyton

Hieracium paulophyton, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Hieracium paulophyton is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions, making it a common sight in high-altitude ecosystems.
