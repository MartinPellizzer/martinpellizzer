Hieracium loedingense

Hieracium loedingense, commonly known as the Loedingen hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy, rough-textured leaves. Native to Europe, particularly Germany, it thrives in moist, grassy habitats. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base.

It is typically found in meadows and along riverbanks, where it plays a minor role in local ecosystems.
