Hippeastrum lineatum

Hippeastrum lineatum, commonly known as the zigzag amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves that are arranged in a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hippeastrum lineatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home garden.
