Habenaria weileriana

Habenaria weileriana, commonly known as the Weiler's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, green, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
