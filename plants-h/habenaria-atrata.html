Habenaria atrata

Habenaria atrata, commonly known as the black orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which are often adorned with white or pale markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and wetlands. Its roots are fibrous, and it has slender, erect stems with a few leaves.

The flowers are relatively small but have a distinctive, intricate structure typical of the Orchidaceae family.
