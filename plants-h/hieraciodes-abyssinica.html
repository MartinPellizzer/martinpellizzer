Hieraciodes abyssinica

Hieraciodes abyssinica, commonly known as Abyssinian mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
