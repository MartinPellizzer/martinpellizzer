Hieracium rubellum subsp. adenocymosum

Hieracium rubellum subsp.

adenocymosum, commonly known as the red hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It produces bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Its leaves are usually hairy and alternate, with a serrated margin, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
