Hydrangea antioquiensis

Hydrangea antioquiensis, commonly known as the Antioquian hydrangea, is a species native to the Andes mountains of Colombia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. Its leaves are ovate and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Hydrangea antioquiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
