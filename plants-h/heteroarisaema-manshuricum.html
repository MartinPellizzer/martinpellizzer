Heteroarisaema manshuricum

Heteroarisaema manshuricum, commonly known as the Manchurian arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for both horticultural and traditional uses.
