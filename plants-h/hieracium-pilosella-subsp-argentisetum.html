Hieracium pilosella subsp. argentisetum

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

argentisetum, commonly known as the silver-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts.

The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically hairy and have a silvery-white underside, giving it its common name.

It thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies.
