Helichrysum sphaerocephalum

Helichrysum sphaerocephalum, commonly known as the globe everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, yellow-orange flower heads that resemble small globes, which are composed of numerous tiny, tubular flowers. The plant produces feathery, aromatic leaves that are often used in herbal teas and traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its long-lasting flowers make it a favored plant for dried floral arrangements.
