Hyacinthus kopet-daghi

Hyacinthus kopet-daghi, commonly known as the Kopet-Dag hyacinth, is a bulbous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet or blue flowers. The plant produces tall, slender stems with clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped blooms. Its bulbs are adapted to survive in arid conditions, storing water and nutrients.
