Hieracium lobetanicum

Hieracium lobetanicum, commonly known as the lobed hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow daisy-like flowers with ray florets. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lobed, giving it its common name.

Hieracium lobetanicum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
