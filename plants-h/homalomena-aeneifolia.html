Homalomena aeneifolia

Homalomena aeneifolia, commonly known as the silver-leaf arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
