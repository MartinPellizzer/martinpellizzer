Hypolepis punctata var. henryi

Hypolepis punctata var. henryi, commonly known as the Henry's sword fern, is a variant of the sword fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender fronds that are typically green with a subtle metallic sheen. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps. Its leaves are bipinnate, with small, rounded pinnae that give it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
