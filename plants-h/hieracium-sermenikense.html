Hieracium sermenikense

Hieracium sermenikense, commonly known as the Armenian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and parts of Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats and is often found in alpine environments. It produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.
