Heteropterys leschenaultiana var. lanceolata

Heteropterys leschenaultiana var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf huito, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The leaves are lanceolate, with a leathery texture and dark green upper surface. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and clustered in terminal inflorescences.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
