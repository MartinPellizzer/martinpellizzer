Hieracium gravastelliceps

Hieracium gravastelliceps, commonly known as the Gravastelles hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in high-altitude environments. It has pale yellow to reddish flowers and hairy stems, which help it withstand harsh climatic conditions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
