Hieracium perangustum

Hieracium perangustum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium perangustum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting pollinators.
