Hosta sieboldii f. okamii

Hosta sieboldii f. okamii, commonly known as the Okamii Hosta, is a compact, ornamental plant native to Japan.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with striking white or light green variegation. The plant produces tall, upright stems with white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in midsummer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its elegant foliage and is a popular choice for shady garden landscapes.
