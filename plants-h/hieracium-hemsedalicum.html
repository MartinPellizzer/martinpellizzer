Hieracium hemsedalicum

Hieracium hemsedalicum, commonly known as the Hemsedal hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the alpine regions of New Zealand.

Hieracium hemsedalicum is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
