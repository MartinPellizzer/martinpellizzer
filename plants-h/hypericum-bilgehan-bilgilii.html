Hypericum bilgehan-bilgilii

Hypericum bilgehan-bilgilii, commonly known as the Turkish St. John's wort, is a rare shrub native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are alternate and simple, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is known for its medicinal properties, similar to other members of the Hypericum genus.
