Hyperthelia edulis

Hyperthelia edulis, commonly known as the edible wild fig, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Moraceae family, which includes other fig species. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that develop into edible fruits. The fruits are typically green when mature and have a sweet, fig-like flavor.
