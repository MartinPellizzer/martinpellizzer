Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. balanbanyae

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

balanbanyae, commonly known as the Balanbanyan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its pale yellow petals and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in open habitats.
