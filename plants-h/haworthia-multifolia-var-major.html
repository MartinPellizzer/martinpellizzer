Haworthia multifolia var. major

Haworthia multifolia var. major, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or yellow stripes. The plant has a thick, fleshy stem and is well adapted to arid environments. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
