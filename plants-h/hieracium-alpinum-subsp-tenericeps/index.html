Hieracium Alpinum Subsp. Tenericeps

Hieracium alpinum subsp. tenericeps is a unique variant of the common hawkweed found in alpine regions. It thrives in rocky and grassy slopes at high elevations where few plants can survive. This subspecies is notable for its dense, soft foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its ability to adapt to harsh climates makes it an important part of alpine ecosystems. Despite its hardy nature, it remains a relatively rare and specialized plant.
