Helenium aschersonianum

Helenium aschersonianum, commonly known as the Mexican fireweed, is a striking perennial plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, rayed flowers in shades of orange, red, and yellow, which bloom from late summer to early fall. The plant has upright, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Helenium aschersonianum is well-suited for gardens and landscapes due to its vibrant colors and drought tolerance.
