Hermas lanata

Hermas lanata, commonly known as the woolly herb, is a flowering plant characterized by its soft, hairy foliage.

It typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to dry conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its leaves are broad and covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Hermas lanata is often found in rocky soils and is a common sight in Mediterranean climates.
