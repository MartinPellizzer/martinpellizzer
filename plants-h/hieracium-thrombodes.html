Hieracium thrombodes

Hieracium thrombodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium thrombodes is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate climates.
