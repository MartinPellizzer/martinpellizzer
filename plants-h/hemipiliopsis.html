Hemipiliopsis

Hemipiliopsis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads. The plant typically grows in moist, often shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and have a distinctive aromatic scent.
