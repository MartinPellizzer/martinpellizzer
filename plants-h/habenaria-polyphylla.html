Habenaria polyphylla

Habenaria polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are often broad and lance-shaped, and it produces numerous small, elongated pseudobulbs.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
