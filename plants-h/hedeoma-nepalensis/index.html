Hedeoma Nepalensis

Nepal mint, scientifically known as Hedeoma nepalensis, is a rare and aromatic herb native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and northern India. This plant is prized for its distinctive mint-like fragrance and its use in traditional medicine and culinary practices. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. Despite its limited distribution, Nepal mint holds cultural significance and is increasingly recognized for its potential in herbal remedies. Its unique properties make it a subject of growing interest among botanists and herbalists alike.
