Hexastylis heterophylla

Hexastylis heterophylla, commonly known as the variable threadleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and delicate, greenish-white flowers.
