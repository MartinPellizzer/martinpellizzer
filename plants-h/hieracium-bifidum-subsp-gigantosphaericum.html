Hieracium bifidum subsp. gigantosphaericum

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

gigantosphaericum, commonly known as the giant globe hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has a rosette of basal leaves that are broadly ovate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems are tall and erect, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter.

It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.
