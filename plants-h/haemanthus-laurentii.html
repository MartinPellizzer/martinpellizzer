Haemanthus laurentii

Haemanthus laurentii, commonly known as the Natal star or Natal glory, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or cream with a red or purple center. The plant has a short, thick stem and produces clusters of bright, showy blooms during the summer months. Its leaves are strap-like and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.

Haemanthus laurentii is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
