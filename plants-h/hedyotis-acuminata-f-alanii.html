Hedyotis acuminata f. alanii

Hedyotis acuminata f. alanii, commonly known as the Alan's blushing geranium, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features small, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a contrasting yellow center, making it visually striking. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, preferring a Mediterranean climate.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and containers.
