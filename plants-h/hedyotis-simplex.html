Hedyotis simplex

Hedyotis simplex, commonly known as the small flame violet, is a small herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features tiny, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy surface.

Hedyotis simplex is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
