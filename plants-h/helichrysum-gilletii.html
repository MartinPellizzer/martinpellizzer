Helichrysum gilletii

Helichrysum gilletii, commonly known as the golden ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in grasslands and fynbos vegetation. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and the flowers are arranged in dense, compact clusters.

Helichrysum gilletii is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
