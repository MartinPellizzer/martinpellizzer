Heliotropium astrotrichum var. astrotrichum

Heliotropium astrotrichum var. astrotrichum, commonly known as the starry heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its flowers have a distinctive star-like appearance, with five petals and a central tuft of stamens.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
