Haworthia turgida f. suberecta

Haworthia turgida f. suberecta, commonly known as the compact Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with white or pale green markings. The plant grows in a rosette form, with leaves that are often slightly curved and have a waxy texture. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
