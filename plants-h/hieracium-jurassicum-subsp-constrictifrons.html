Hieracium jurassicum subsp. constrictifrons

Hieracium jurassicum subsp.

constrictifrons, commonly known as the Jurassic hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, lobed leaves.

The subspecies is native to certain regions of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a compact growth habit with a rosette of basal leaves.

The plant's botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
