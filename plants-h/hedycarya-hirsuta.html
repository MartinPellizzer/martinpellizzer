Hedycarya hirsuta

Hedycarya hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy hedycarya, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rutaceae family.

Hedycarya hirsuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
