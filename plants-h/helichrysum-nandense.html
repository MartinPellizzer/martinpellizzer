Helichrysum nandense

Helichrysum nandense, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain intact even after drying. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sunlight. Its foliage is usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small, narrow leaves.

Helichrysum nandense is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting appearance and vibrant color.
