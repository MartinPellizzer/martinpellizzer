Helenium undulatum

Helenium undulatum, commonly known as the zigzagged helenium, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its striking, orange to red-orange ray flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often has a zigzag or undulating pattern, giving it its common name.

Helenium undulatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
