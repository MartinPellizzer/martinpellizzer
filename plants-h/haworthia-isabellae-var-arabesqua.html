Haworthia isabellae var. arabesqua

Haworthia isabellae var. arabesqua, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically light green with distinct white or pale blue markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
