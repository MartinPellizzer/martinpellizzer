Hosta caerulea f. aureovariegata

Hosta caerulea f. aureovariegata, commonly known as the Variegated Blue Hosta, is a variegated cultivar of the Hosta genus.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a striking combination of blue-green and yellow variegation. The plant is native to eastern Asia and thrives in shady, moist environments. It produces small, fragrant white flowers in late summer.

This ornamental plant is popular in gardens for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
