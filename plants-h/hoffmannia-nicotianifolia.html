Hoffmannia nicotianifolia

Hoffmannia nicotianifolia, commonly known as the nightshade-like plant, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are often borne in clusters at the ends of the branches. It produces small, berry-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Hoffmannia nicotianifolia is primarily grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and unique floral structure.
