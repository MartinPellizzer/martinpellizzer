Hypericum nikkoense

Hypericum nikkoense, commonly known as the Nikko goldthread, is a perennial flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering shrubs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum nikkoense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
