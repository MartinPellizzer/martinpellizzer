Helipterum adpressum

Helipterum adpressum, commonly known as the narrowleaf helipterum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers. The flowers are typically pink or purple and are arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.
