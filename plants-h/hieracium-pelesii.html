Hieracium pelesii

Hieracium pelesii, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring moist to moderately dry conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in early summer.

Hieracium pelesii is often found in open habitats and can sometimes form dense colonies.
