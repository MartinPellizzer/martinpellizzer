Haworthia turgida var. pallidifolia

Haworthia turgida var. pallidifolia, commonly known as the pale leaf Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, pale green to bluish-gray leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
