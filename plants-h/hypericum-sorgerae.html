Hypericum sorgerae

Hypericum sorgerae, commonly known as the Sauerland St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes various species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, typical of many Hypericum species. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
