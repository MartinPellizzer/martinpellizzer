Helicia formosana f. acuminata

Helicia formosana f. acuminata, commonly known as the Formosan helicia, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant features elliptical, glossy leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological significance in its native habitat.
