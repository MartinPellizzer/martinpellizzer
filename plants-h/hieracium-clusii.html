Hieracium clusii

Hieracium clusii, commonly known as the Clusius's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence. Native to Central and Eastern Europe, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has serrated leaves and is often found in moist to moderately dry environments.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
