Haworthia marxii

Haworthia marxii, commonly known as the Marx's Haworthia, is a compact, leafy succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tubular leaves that are typically green with white or pinkish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Haworthia marxii is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
