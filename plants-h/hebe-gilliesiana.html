Hebe gilliesiana

Hebe gilliesiana, commonly known as the silver hebe, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet during the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Hebe gilliesiana is relatively low-maintenance and resistant to pests and diseases.
