Habenaria cubitalis

Habenaria cubitalis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with intricate patterns, and they bloom during the warmer months.

Habenaria cubitalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
