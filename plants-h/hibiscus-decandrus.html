Hibiscus decandrus

Hibiscus decandrus, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces fruit that is capsule-like in structure. Hibiscus decandrus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
