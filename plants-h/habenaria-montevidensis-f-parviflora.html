Habenaria montevidensis f. parviflora

Habenaria montevidensis f. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

This orchid is of interest to botanists due to its unique floral structure and ecological adaptations.
