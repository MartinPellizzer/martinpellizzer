Hieracium solanum

Hieracium solanum, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from yellow to purple.

Hieracium solanum is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed habitats and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
