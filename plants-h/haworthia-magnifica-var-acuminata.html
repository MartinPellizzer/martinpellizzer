Haworthia magnifica var. acuminata

Haworthia magnifica var. acuminata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped foliage with distinctive white to pale blue markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, and are typically a vibrant green with a waxy coating. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
