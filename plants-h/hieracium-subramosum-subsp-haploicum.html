Hieracium subramosum subsp. haploicum

Hieracium subramosum subsp.

haploicum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant typically has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Due to its holarctic distribution, it is a common species in temperate regions.
