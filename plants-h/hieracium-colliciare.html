Hieracium colliciare

Hieracium colliciare, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, which is a defining feature of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapting to cold and high-altitude environments.

Hieracium colliciare is often found in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.
