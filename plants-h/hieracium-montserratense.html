Hieracium montserratense

Hieracium montserratense, commonly known as the Montserrat hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, showing a preference for well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinct central disk surrounded by ray florets.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
