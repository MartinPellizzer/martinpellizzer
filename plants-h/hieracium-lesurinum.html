Hieracium lesurinum

Hieracium lesurinum, commonly known as the Lesur's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodland areas, preferring well-drained soils. It features upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in summer, contributing to its role in pollinator habitats.
