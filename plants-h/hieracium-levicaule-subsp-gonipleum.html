Hieracium levicaule subsp. gonipleum

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

gonipleum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies.

The plant has basal leaves that are narrow and linear, with a hairy surface, and produces yellow flowers in late summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single flower per stem, surrounded by leaf-like bracts.

It is characterized by its slender stem and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
