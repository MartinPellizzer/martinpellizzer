Hieracium pseudodontotrichum

Hieracium pseudodontotrichum, commonly known as the false toothed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a single, erect stem that bears yellow disk flowers. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are usually rough and hairy, with a distinct basal arrangement.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes, making it somewhat invasive in certain regions.
