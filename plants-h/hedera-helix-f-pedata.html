Hedera helix f. pedata

Hedera helix f. pedata, commonly known as the giant ivy or broad-leaf ivy, is a cultivar of the common ivy plant.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and often lobed leaves that are wider than they are long. This variety thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions and is known for its vigorous growth habit. The leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to climb or trail along surfaces.
