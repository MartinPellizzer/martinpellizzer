Hieracium pleistocephalum

Hieracium pleistocephalum, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a rosette of basal leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered a weed in some regions.
