Hellenia caerulea

Hellenia caerulea, commonly known as blue hellena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Hellenia caerulea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
