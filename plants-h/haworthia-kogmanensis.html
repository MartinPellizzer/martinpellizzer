Haworthia kogmanensis

Haworthia kogmanensis, commonly known as the Kogman's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct white or pale markings. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

Haworthia kogmanensis is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
