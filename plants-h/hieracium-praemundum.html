Hieracium praemundum

Hieracium praemundum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

Hieracium praemundum is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates.
