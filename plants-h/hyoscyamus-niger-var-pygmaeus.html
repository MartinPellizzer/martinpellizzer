Hyoscyamus niger var. pygmaeus

Hyoscyamus niger var. pygmaeus, commonly known as the dwarf black henbane, is a small, herbaceous annual plant belonging to the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers and hairy, branched stems. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few inches, making it a compact and distinctive species. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Like other members of its genus, it contains toxic alkaloids, which can be harmful to humans and animals.
