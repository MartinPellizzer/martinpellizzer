Hieracium fuckelianum

Hieracium fuckelianum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its yellow flowers are surrounded by a ring of pale green ray florets, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds in a fluffy pappus.

Hieracium fuckelianum is often found in temperate climates and can spread aggressively in certain regions.
