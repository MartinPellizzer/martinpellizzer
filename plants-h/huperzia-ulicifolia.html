Huperzia ulicifolia

Huperzia ulicifolia, commonly known as the rock huperzia, is a species of clubmoss belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and is native to regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rocky crevices and forest floors. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate in shape and often have a waxy coating.

Huperzia ulicifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
