Haplopappus rydbergii

Haplopappus rydbergii, commonly known as Rydberg's goatweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as sagebrush steppe and rocky areas. It has small, narrow leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.
