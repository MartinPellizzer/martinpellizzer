Heterostemma maculatum

Heterostemma maculatum, commonly known as the spotted stem vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and distinctive spotted leaves. The plant features small, white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its stems are often marked with irregularly shaped spots, which give it its common name.

Heterostemma maculatum is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
