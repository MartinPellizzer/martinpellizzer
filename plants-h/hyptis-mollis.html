Hyptis mollis

Hyptis mollis, commonly known as white hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hyptis mollis is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
