Hypolytrum giganteum

Hypolytrum giganteum, commonly known as the giant false lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant features large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle pink or purple hue. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth, waxy texture.

Hypolytrum giganteum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
