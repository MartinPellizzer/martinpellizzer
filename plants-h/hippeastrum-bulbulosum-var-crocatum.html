Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. crocatum

Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. crocatum, commonly known as the Crocodile Aroid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has large, strap-like leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
