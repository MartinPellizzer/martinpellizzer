Hieracium floribundum subsp. muratowense

Hieracium floribundum subsp.

muratowense, commonly known as the Muratowense Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

The plant features basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to ovate, with rough, hairy surfaces. Its inflorescence consists of yellow composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the Asteraceae family.

The subspecies is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and subtle morphological differences from other Hieracium species.
