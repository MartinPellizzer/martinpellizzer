Hieracium thrineia

Hieracium thrineia, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are upright and often branched.

Hieracium thrineia is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
