Hieracium scandinavicum subsp. subfloribundum

Hieracium scandinavicum subsp.

subfloribundum, commonly known as the Scandinavian hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to northern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

The subspecies is distinguished by its more compact growth habit and clustered flower heads compared to other hawkweed species.
