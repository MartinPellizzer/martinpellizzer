Habenaria leon-ibarrae

Habenaria leon-ibarrae, commonly known as the Leon-IBarra Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in racemes, often with a faintly fragrant scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
