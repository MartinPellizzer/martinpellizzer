Hieracium bohatschianum

Hieracium bohatschianum, commonly known as Bohatsch's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lanceolate and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is often found in clusters.
