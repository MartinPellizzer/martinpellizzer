Helichrysum obtusum

Helichrysum obtusum, commonly known as the woolly sunburst, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its aromatic, resinous scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its long-lasting flowers also make it a favored plant for dried floral arrangements.
