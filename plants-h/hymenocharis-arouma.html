Hymenocharis arouma

Hymenocharis arouma, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Hymenocharis arouma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
