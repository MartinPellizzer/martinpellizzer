Hypolytrum attiense

Hypolytrum attiense, commonly known as the Attiense violet, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often with a distinctive fragrance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
