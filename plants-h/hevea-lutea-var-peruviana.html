Hevea lutea var. peruviana

Hevea lutea var. peruviana, commonly known as the yellow rubber tree, is a species native to South America, particularly Peru.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, pale yellow bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
