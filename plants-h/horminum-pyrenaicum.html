Horminum pyrenaicum

Horminum pyrenaicum, commonly known as the Pyrenean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine downy coating.

Horminum pyrenaicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in mountain ecosystems.
