Holothrix harveyana

Holothrix harveyana, commonly known as the Harvey's holothrix, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
