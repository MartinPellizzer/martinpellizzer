Homalomena triangularis

Homalomena triangularis, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly textured surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white or pale yellow, which are borne on spathes typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular houseplant.
