Haworthia obserata

Haworthia obserata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in white tubercles, giving it a distinctive zebra-like appearance. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Haworthia obserata is also noted for its ability to store water in its fleshy leaves, allowing it to survive in arid conditions.
