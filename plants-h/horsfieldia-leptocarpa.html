Horsfieldia leptocarpa

Horsfieldia leptocarpa, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the rainforests of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically elongated and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Horsfieldia leptocarpa is often epiphytic, growing on tree trunks and branches in humid, shaded environments.
