Haloragis uniflora

Haloragis uniflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Haloragaceae and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salty and windy conditions.
