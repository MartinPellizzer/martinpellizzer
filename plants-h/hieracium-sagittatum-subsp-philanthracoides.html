Hieracium sagittatum subsp. philanthracoides

Hieracium sagittatum subsp.

philanthracoides, commonly known as the arrowhead hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies.
