Heyfeldera

Heyfeldera, commonly known as the goldenbush, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellowish-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in the spring. Heyfeldera is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
