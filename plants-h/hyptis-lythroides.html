Hyptis lythroides

Hyptis lythroides, commonly known as purple hyptis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, violet or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in open woodlands and disturbed areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and vibrant floral display.
