Horsfieldia polyspherula var. maxima

Horsfieldia polyspherula var. maxima, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter, featuring a unique combination of colors and intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, typically arranged in a rosette pattern around the base of the plant.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its rarity and ornamental value.
