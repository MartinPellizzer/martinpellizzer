Helichrysum gofense

Helichrysum gofense, commonly known as the Cape goldflax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats. It is well adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in full sun.

Helichrysum gofense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
