Helichrysum xerochrysum

Helichrysum xerochrysum, commonly known as the common everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that produces small, brightly colored yellow to orange flowers. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and aromatic, silver-gray foliage. Its flowers are known for their long-lasting nature, making it a popular choice for dried floral arrangements.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.
