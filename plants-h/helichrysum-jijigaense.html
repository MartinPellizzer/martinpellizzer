Helichrysum jijigaense

Helichrysum jijigaense, commonly known as the Jijiga everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often arranged in dense clusters. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

Its foliage is typically covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
