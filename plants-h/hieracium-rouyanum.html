Hieracium rouyanum

Hieracium rouyanum, commonly known as the Rouyan's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are yellow and are surrounded by bracts that resemble leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hieracium rouyanum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
