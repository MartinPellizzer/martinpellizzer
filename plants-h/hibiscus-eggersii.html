Hibiscus eggersii

Hibiscus eggersii, commonly known as the Eggers hibiscus, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features showy, five-petaled flowers that are usually bright red or orange in color. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Hibiscus eggersii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
