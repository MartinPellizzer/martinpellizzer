Hieracium filidens

Hieracium filidens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in the summer months. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant is known for its ability to spread via rhizomes and seeds, making it a persistent weed in agricultural areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and horticultural research.
