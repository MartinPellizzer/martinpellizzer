Haworthia asperiuscula var. patagiata

Haworthia asperiuscula var. patagiata, commonly known as the Patagonian Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy rosettes of bluish-green leaves with white or pale markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, slender flower stems with white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
