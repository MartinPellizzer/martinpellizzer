Hieracium pallescens subsp. subpatulum

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

subpatulum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish-green leaves.

The plant produces small, dandelion-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade.

Its foliage is often covered with fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
