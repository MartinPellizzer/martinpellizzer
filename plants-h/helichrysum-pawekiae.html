Helichrysum pawekiae

Helichrysum pawekiae, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal golden bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of golden-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance.

Helichrysum pawekiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
