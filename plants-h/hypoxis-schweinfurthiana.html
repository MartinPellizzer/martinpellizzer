Hypoxis schweinfurthiana

Hypoxis schweinfurthiana, commonly known as the star lily or African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in late summer. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in grasslands and rocky soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
