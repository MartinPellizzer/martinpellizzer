Hypericum rigidum subsp. sellowianum

Hypericum rigidum subsp.

sellowianum, commonly known as the Brazilian St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to South America. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers with prominent stamens.

The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
