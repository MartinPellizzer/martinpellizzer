Hypochaeris meyeniana var. leucantha

Hypochaeris meyeniana var. leucantha, commonly known as white buttercup, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and disturbed areas. The plant features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are bright yellow with a central disk and are arranged in a cluster.

The species is notable for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting pollinators.
