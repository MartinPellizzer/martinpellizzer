Hieracium vietulifolium

Hieracium vietulifolium, commonly known as the Vietnamese hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family.

Hieracium vietulifolium is often found in forest understories and along stream banks, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
