Hypericum Webbii

Hypericum webbii, also known as the yellow flowering shrub, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of western China. This species is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and dense, woody stems that thrive in harsh alpine environments. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from climate change and human activity. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Despite its beauty, Hypericum webbii remains largely understudied and underappreciated in the broader plant world.
