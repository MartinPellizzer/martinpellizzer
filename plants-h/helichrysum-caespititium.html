Helichrysum caespititium

Helichrysum caespititium, commonly known as the dwarf goldenhead, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features compact, tufted growth with small, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has aromatic, silver-green foliage that is often used in herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a valuable addition to both gardens and traditional herbal practices.
