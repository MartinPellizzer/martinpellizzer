Helianthemum umbellatum var. verticillatum

Helianthemum umbellatum var. verticillatum, commonly known as the clustered rockrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features dense clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are small, leathery, and arranged in whorls around the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
