Hieracium schmidtii subsp. labillardierei

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

labillardierei, commonly known as the Schmidt's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its dense clusters of yellowish-white flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, grassy habitats and has hairy stems and leaves. It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread in suitable environments.

This subspecies is noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its role in local ecosystems.
