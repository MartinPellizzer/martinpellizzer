Hieracium ancylodes

Hieracium ancylodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a composite head composed of numerous small florets. The plant has basal leaves that are broadly lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.

Hieracium ancylodes is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
