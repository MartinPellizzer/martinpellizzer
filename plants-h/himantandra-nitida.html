Himantandra nitida

Himantandra nitida, commonly known as the slender spurge or narrowleaf spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a waxy, glossy appearance. It produces clusters of tiny, greenish-yellow flowers that are surrounded by a cup-like structure called a cyathium.

This plant is native to parts of Central America and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.
