Hesperis sintenisii

Hesperis sintenisii, commonly known as the Alpine honesty, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family plants. This species is characterized by its delicate, white or pale pink flowers and pinnately lobed leaves. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ease of care.
