Helichrysum pondoense

Helichrysum pondoense, commonly known as the Pondo golden bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow or orange flowers that resemble small sunflowers. The plant is evergreen, with narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum pondoense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
