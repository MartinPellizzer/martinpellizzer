Hypericum peninsulare

Hypericum peninsulare, commonly known as the Peninsular St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of shrubs and herbs. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with prominent orange stamens, typical of the Hypericum family. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Hypericum peninsulare is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
