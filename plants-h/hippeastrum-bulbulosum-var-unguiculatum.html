Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. unguiculatum

Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. unguiculatum, commonly known as the Red-veined Dragon's Breath.

This plant is a striking bulbous species native to South America, particularly Brazil. It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically deep red with contrasting white veins. The leaves are broad, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture.

It is cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-draining soil with adequate sunlight.
