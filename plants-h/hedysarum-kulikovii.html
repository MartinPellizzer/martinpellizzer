Hedysarum kulikovii

Hedysarum kulikovii, commonly known as the Kulikovii sweet clover, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in harsh conditions.

Hedysarum kulikovii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
