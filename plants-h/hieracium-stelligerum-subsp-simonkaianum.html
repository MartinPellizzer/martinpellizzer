Hieracium stelligerum subsp. simonkaianum

Hieracium stelligerum subsp.

simonkaianum, commonly known as the Simonkaian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellowish to reddish composite flowers.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are often reddish in color. This subspecies is native to Central Asia and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to soil stabilization.
