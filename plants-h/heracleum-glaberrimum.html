Heracleum glaberrimum

Heracleum glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth cowparsnip, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its tall, hollow stems and large, compound leaves.

Heracleum glaberrimum is often mistaken for other similar species due to its similar appearance and growth habits.
