Hemerocallis flava var. yezoensis

Hemerocallis flava var. yezoensis, commonly known as the Yezo yellow daylily, is a variant of the daylily species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are often a deep green color. It is a perennial herbaceous plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
