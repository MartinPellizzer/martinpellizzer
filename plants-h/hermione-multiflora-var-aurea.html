Hermione multiflora var. aurea

Hermione multiflora var. aurea, commonly known as the golden Hermione, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant yellow foliage.

This variety belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to regions with warm, humid climates. The plant features dense, compact growth with lance-shaped leaves that emit a subtle, pleasant fragrance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked in favor of its showy leaves.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
