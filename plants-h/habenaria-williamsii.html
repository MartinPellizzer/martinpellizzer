Habenaria williamsii

Habenaria williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green or yellowish in color. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
