Haworthiopsis limifolia

Haworthiopsis limifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on short stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
