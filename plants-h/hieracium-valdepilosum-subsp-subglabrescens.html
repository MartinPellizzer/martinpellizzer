Hieracium valdepilosum subsp. subglabrescens

Hieracium valdepilosum subsp.

subglabrescens, commonly known as the slender spearthistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant has alternate, linear to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy but become gradually smoother towards the upper parts. Its inflorescence consists of yellow composite flowers arranged in a head, with both ray and disk florets.

It is native to southern Europe and is known for its ecological importance in supporting pollinators and other wildlife.
