Hieracium erucophyllum

Hieracium erucophyllum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow daisy-like flowers. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny environments.
