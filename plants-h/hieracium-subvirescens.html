Hieracium subvirescens

Hieracium subvirescens, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its yellowish to pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and somewhat hairy.

Hieracium subvirescens is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
