Hyacinthus lypskyi

Hyacinthus lypskyi, commonly known as the Lypsky hyacinth, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of blue, violet, and white, typically blooming in spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is well-suited for rock gardens or containers. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Hyacinthus lypskyi is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
