Helichrysum Teydeum

Helichrysum teydeum is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the Canary Islands. It is known for its vibrant yellow and orange flowers that resemble small suns. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Despite its hardiness, it requires careful care to flourish in different climates.
