Hemigraphis viridis

Hemigraphis viridis, commonly known as the Emerald Spotted Begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking silver or white spots. The plant produces small, delicate pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Hemigraphis viridis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
