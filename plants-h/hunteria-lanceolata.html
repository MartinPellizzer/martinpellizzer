Hunteria lanceolata

Hunteria lanceolata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Hunteria lanceolata is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under optimal conditions.
