Hieracium suecicum subsp. bergense

Hieracium suecicum subsp.

bergense, commonly known as the Bergens hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to northern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in mountainous regions. It has lance-shaped leaves with rough textures and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in many areas.
