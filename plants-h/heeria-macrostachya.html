Heeria macrostachya

Heeria macrostachya, commonly known as the giant heeria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture, while its stems are sturdy and branched.

Heeria macrostachya is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
