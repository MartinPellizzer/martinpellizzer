Huperzia hamiltonii

Huperzia hamiltonii, commonly known as the Hamilton's huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, erect, and sparsely branched stems, which often bear clusters of small, ovate leaves. The plant is native to regions in Asia, including China, Japan, and the Indian subcontinent. Its leaves are typically lanceolate to ovate in shape and have a leathery texture.

Huperzia hamiltonii is often found growing in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
