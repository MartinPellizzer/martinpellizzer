Helianthemum ledifolium var. corsicum

Helianthemum ledifolium var. corsicum, commonly known as the Corsican rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
