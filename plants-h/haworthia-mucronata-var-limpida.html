Haworthia mucronata var. limpida

Haworthia mucronata var. limpida, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green to bluish-green in color. The leaves are covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its arid native environment. It produces small, white to yellowish flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
