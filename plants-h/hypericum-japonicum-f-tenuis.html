Hypericum japonicum f. tenuis

Hypericum japonicum f. tenuis, commonly known as narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to Japan.

It features slender, upright stems covered in small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit and attractive flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
