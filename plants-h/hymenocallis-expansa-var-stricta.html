Hymenocallis expansa var. stricta

Hymenocallis expansa var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spider lily, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
