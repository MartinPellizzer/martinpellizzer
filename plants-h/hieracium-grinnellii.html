Hieracium grinnellii

Hieracium grinnellii, commonly known as Grinnell's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has basal leaves that are usually hairy and linear, with a height ranging from 10 to 30 centimeters.

Its flowers are yellow, appearing in the summer months, and it is considered a perennial species.
