Hieracium brachiatum subsp. abbreviatum

Hieracium brachiatum subsp.

abbreviatum, commonly known as the short-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit and produces yellow composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its shorter leaves and reduced flower heads compared to other members of the species. It is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.
