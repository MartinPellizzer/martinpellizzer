Hymenopus arachnoideus

Hymenopus arachnoideus, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the wet tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance, with long, thin leaves and a slender, twisted stem. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive red or purple throat, and they bloom in the dry season.

This orchid is epiphytic, meaning it grows on trees without deriving nutrients from them, and it relies on specific environmental conditions for successful growth.
