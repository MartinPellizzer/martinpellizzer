Habenaria erythraeae

Habenaria erythraeae, commonly known as the Red Sea orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
