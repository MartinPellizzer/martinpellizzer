Hieracium porrectum subsp. adenodon

Hieracium porrectum subsp.

adenodon, commonly known as the腺叶蒲公英, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering head composed of yellow ray florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It has a taproot system and produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

The subspecies is distinguished by its glandular-dotted leaves and the presence of a short, hairy stem.
