Hieracium ajmasianum

Hieracium ajmasianum, commonly known as the Ajmasian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates. It produces small yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.
