Hypericum nordmannii

Hypericum nordmannii, commonly known as Nordmann's St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers in late summer. The plant features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. Its flowering period typically occurs from July to August, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils.

Hypericum nordmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
