Helleborus × ballardiae

Helleborus × ballardiae, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its evergreen, holly-like leaves and its ability to bloom in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive central boss of stamens. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, it thrives in shady, moist environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
