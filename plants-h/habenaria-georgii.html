Habenaria georgii

Habenaria georgii, commonly known as the George's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its floral structure includes a single, showy bloom with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Botanically, it is noted for its specialized symbiotic relationships with certain fungi, which are essential for its growth and development.
