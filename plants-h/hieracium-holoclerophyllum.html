Hieracium holoclerophyllum

Hieracium holoclerophyllum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and hairy stems, typical of many hawkweed species. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has alternate leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs.

Hieracium holoclerophyllum is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
