Homalomena alba

Homalomena alba, commonly known as the white arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its foliage. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
