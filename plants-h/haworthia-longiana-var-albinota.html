Haworthia longiana var. albinota

Haworthia longiana var. albinota, commonly known as the white-tipped haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The leaves are fleshy and covered in a layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
