Hieracium sylvaticum subsp. sublividum

Hieracium sylvaticum subsp.

sublividum, commonly known as the forest hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowering heads and hairy stems. The leaves are typically lanceolate and have a rough texture, while the plant prefers moist, shaded environments such as woodlands.

It is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-rich soils.
