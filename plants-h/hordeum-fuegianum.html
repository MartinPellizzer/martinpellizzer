Hordeum fuegianum

Hordeum fuegianum, commonly known as the Falkland Island barley, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has adapted to the harsh climate of the Falkland Islands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Hordeum fuegianum plays a role in the local ecosystem, providing food for grazing animals and contributing to soil stability.
