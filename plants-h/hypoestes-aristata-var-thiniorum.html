Hypoestes aristata var. thiniorum

Hypoestes aristata var. thiniorum, commonly known as the Christmas Hypoestes, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble small flowers, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is popular in gardens and as a potted plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
