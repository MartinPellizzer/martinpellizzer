Helenium stenopterum

Helenium stenopterum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to the central and eastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils in open woods or along streambanks. Its striking orange to red flower heads bloom in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

Helenium stenopterum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
