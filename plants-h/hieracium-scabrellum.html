Hieracium scabrellum

Hieracium scabrellum, commonly known as rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has rough, hairy stems and leaves that are often covered with fine bristles. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it is commonly found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

It is a perennial species that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in many habitats.
