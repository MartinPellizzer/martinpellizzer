Hydrangea platyarguta

Hydrangea platyarguta, commonly known as the flatleaf hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, rounded leaves that are typically 5 to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Hydrangea platyarguta prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its foliage remains green throughout the growing season, adding ornamental value to gardens.
