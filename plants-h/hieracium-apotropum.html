Hieracium apotropum

Hieracium apotropum, commonly known as the devil's bit scabious, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and western Asia and typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and woodland edges. Hieracium apotropum plays an important role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
