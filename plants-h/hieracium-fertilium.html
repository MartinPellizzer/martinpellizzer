Hieracium fertilium

Hieracium fertilium, commonly known as the fertilized hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, rough leaves. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

It is considered a weed in some agricultural areas due to its rapid growth and invasive tendencies.
