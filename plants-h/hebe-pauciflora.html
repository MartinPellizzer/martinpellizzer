Hebe pauciflora

Hebe pauciflora, commonly known as the slender hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. Hebe pauciflora thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
