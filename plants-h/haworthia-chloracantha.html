Haworthia chloracantha

Haworthia chloracantha, commonly known as the Zulu beauty or the chloracantha Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
