Hieracium adarnelli

Hieracium adarnelli, commonly known as the Adurnelli's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in the summer months. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist meadows and grasslands. The plant has a hairy stem and is often found in disturbed soils.

It is a common weed in agricultural areas and can be difficult to control due to its extensive root system.
