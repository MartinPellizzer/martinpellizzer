Hieracium niveum subsp. fuliginellum

Hieracium niveum subsp.

fuliginellum, commonly known as the sooty hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in dense white or greyish hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically yellow to orange, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.
