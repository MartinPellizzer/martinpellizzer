Hakea Leiophylla

Hakea leiophylla is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It is known for its unique, needle-like leaves that are both tough and drought-resistant. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its vibrant yellow flowers appear in spring, adding a burst of color to the landscape. Hakea leiophylla is not only ornamental but also plays an important role in supporting local wildlife.
