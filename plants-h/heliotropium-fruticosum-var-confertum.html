Heliotropium fruticosum var. confertum

Heliotropium fruticosum var. confertum, commonly known as the dense heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive blooms.
