Haworthia cymbiformis f. ramosa

Haworthia cymbiformis f. ramosa, commonly known as the Rosy Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy rosettes of pale green to bluish-gray leaves with white to pinkish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
