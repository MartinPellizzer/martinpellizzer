Hieracium asperulum

Hieracium asperulum, commonly known as rough hawksbeard, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.
