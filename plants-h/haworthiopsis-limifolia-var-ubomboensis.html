Haworthiopsis limifolia var. ubomboensis

Haworthiopsis limifolia var. ubomboensis, commonly known as the Ubombo Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often adorned with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a potted plant.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
