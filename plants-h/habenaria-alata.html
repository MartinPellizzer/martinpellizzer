Habenaria alata

Habenaria alata, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with distinctive spurred petals. It typically blooms in late spring or early summer.

Habenaria alata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
