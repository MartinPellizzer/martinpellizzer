Hemicarpha Micrantha Var. Minor

Purple lovegrass, scientifically known as Hemicarpha micrantha var. minor, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America. Its vibrant purple foliage and delicate flower plumes make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking visual interest. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to a variety of climates. Its low maintenance requirements and drought tolerance further enhance its appeal. Whether used in borders, containers, or as a ground cover, Purple lovegrass adds a unique and colorful element to any landscape.
