Hieracium wilczekianum subsp. neveanum

Hieracium wilczekianum subsp.

neveanum, commonly known as the Nevean hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It is native to Central and Eastern Europe, where it thrives in meadows and grassy habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species in parts of its range.
