Hieracium crocatum subsp. stenacrum

Hieracium crocatum subsp.

stenacrum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish-orange composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and a more compact growth form compared to other hawkweed species. It typically thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.
