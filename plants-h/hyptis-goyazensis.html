Hyptis goyazensis

Hyptis goyazensis, commonly known as Goyaz mistflower, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Hyptis goyazensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
