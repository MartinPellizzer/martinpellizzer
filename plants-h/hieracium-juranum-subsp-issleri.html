Hieracium juranum subsp. issleri

Hieracium juranum subsp.

issleri, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in rocky and gravelly soils at high altitudes, often in mountainous areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
