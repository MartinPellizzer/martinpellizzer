Heladena multiflora

Heladena multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered heladena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands.

Heladena multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
