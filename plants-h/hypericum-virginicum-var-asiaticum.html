Hypericum virginicum var. asiaticum

Hypericum virginicum var. asiaticum, commonly known as the Asiatic St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in woodland edges or disturbed sites.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties, with compounds that have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.
