Heteropterys syringifolia var. piauhiensis

Heteropterys syringifolia var. piauhiensis, commonly known as the Piauhi trumpet tree, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with a dense, rounded crown. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, followed by orange-red berries.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical landscapes.
