Hieracium cinerum

Hieracium cinerum, commonly known as the cinerary hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its pale blue to white flowers and is native to Europe, including regions of the United Kingdom and southern Scandinavia. The plant typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies. It has hairy leaves and stems, and its basal leaves are usually lance-shaped.

Hieracium cinerum is considered a perennial plant, with a short growth cycle and a tendency to spread vegetatively.
