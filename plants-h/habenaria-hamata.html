Habenaria hamata

Habenaria hamata, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive spur at the base of the lip. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Habenaria hamata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
