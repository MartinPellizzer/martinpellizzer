Hoarea virginea

Hoarea virginea, commonly known as the silver grass or silver hair grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-tomentose stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Hoarea virginea thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its ornate, feathery appearance.
