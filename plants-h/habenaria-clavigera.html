Habenaria clavigera

Habenaria clavigera, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pale green to yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Habenaria clavigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
