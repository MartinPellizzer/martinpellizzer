Hohenbergia ituberaensis

Hohenbergia ituberaensis, commonly known as the Itubera Bergia, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. Hohenbergia ituberaensis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
