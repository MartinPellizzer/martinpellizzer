Heliotropium lehmannianum

Heliotropium lehmannianum, commonly known as the desert heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing shrub with hairy, often glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and limited water availability.

Heliotropium lehmannianum is often found in desert scrub and coastal dunes environments.
