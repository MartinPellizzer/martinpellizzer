Huperzia Mollicoma

Huperzia mollicoma is a unique fern species known for its soft, moss-like foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, it thrives in humid and shaded environments. This plant is often found in cloud forests where it plays a vital role in the ecosystem. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, Huperzia mollicoma remains a relatively obscure plant in the world of horticulture.
