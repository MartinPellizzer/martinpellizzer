Hesperis bungei

Hesperis bungei, commonly known as the Bunge's horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with four-petaled flowers. The plant features distinctive, showy flowers with a horn-like structure at the base, giving it its common name. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun.

Hesperis bungei is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
