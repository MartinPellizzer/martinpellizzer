Harrachia macrothyrsus

Harrachia macrothyrsus, commonly known as the African orchid grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Harrachia macrothyrsus is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
