Hieracium peleterianum

Hieracium peleterianum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium peleterianum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to colonize various environments.
