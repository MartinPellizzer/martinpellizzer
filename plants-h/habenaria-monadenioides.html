Habenaria monadenioides

Habenaria monadenioides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest floors and thrives in humid environments. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
