Hieracium telekianum

Hieracium telekianum, commonly known as the Telekian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It produces yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are broadly lance-shaped with hairy surfaces.

Hieracium telekianum is often found in moist or calcareous soils and is a common weed in agricultural areas.
