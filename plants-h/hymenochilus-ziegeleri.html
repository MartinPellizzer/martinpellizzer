Hymenochilus ziegeleri

Hymenochilus ziegeleri, commonly known as the Ziegeler's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in a raceme, with prominent sepals and petals. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the rich, organic soil in its natural habitat.

Hymenochilus ziegeleri is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
