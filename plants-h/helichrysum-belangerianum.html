Helichrysum belangerianum

Helichrysum belangerianum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even when dried. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Helichrysum belangerianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
