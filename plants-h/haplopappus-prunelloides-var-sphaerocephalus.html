Haplopappus prunelloides var. sphaerocephalus

Haplopappus prunelloides var. sphaerocephalus, commonly known as the globe-thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It features striking spherical blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, and its stems are upright and branched. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The species is known for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
