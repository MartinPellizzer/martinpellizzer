Habenaria ludens

Habenaria ludens, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and striking, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive spurred lip that aids in pollination.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
