Hypochaeris tenuiflora

Hypochaeris tenuiflora, commonly known as slenderleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by elongated, cylindrical seed pods.

Hypochaeris tenuiflora is often considered a weed due to its rapid spread and ability to colonize various habitats.
