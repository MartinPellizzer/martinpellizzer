Habenaria nyikana subsp. nyikana

Habenaria nyikana subsp.

nyikana, commonly known as the Nyika orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude grasslands of eastern Africa. It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulbous structure, typically growing in open, well-drained soils.

The plant produces a single, upright stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the dorsal sepal and petals.

This subspecies is notable for its adaptability to harsh, alpine conditions and its role in local ecosystems.
