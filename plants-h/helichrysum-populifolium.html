Helichrysum populifolium

Helichrysum populifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its foliage and flowers are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
