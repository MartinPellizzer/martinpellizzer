Hoya padangensis

Hoya padangensis, commonly known as the Padang Hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. Hoya padangensis prefers indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
