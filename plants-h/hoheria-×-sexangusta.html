Hoheria × sexangusta

Hoheria × sexangusta, commonly known as the silver fern, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves that are velvety to the touch and its clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. It is known for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions.

The flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and birds, contributing to its ecological value.
