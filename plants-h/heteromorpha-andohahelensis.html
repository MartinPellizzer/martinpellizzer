Heteromorpha andohahelensis

Heteromorpha andohahelensis, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Madagascar.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are highly specialized, with intricate petal arrangements that aid in pollination by specific insect species.
