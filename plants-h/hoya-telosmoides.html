Hoya telosmoides

Hoya telosmoides, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces clusters of small, waxy, white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are elongated, glossy, and often have a silvery or variegated appearance.

Hoya telosmoides is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
