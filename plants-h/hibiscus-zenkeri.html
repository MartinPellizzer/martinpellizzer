Hibiscus zenkeri

Hibiscus zenkeri, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain seeds. Hibiscus zenkeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils.

It thrives in full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
