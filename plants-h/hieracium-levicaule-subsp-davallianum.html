Hieracium levicaule subsp. davallianum

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

davallianum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer.
