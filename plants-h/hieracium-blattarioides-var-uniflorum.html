Hieracium blattarioides var. uniflorum

Hieracium blattarioides var. uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flowered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically has a low-growing habit with a central stem and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape. Its inflorescence consists of a single yellow disk flower surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets.

It thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and is often found in temperate climates.
