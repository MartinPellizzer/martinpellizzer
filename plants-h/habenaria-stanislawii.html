Habenaria stanislawii

Habenaria stanislawii, commonly known as the Stanislaw's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its flowering period typically occurs during the summer months.
