Hieracium gracilidens

Hieracium gracilidens, commonly known as slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this species thrives in meadows and grasslands. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
