Hoya alata

Hoya alata, commonly known as the Porcelain Flower, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of white, pink, and green. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, waxy flowers that are typically white or pink, resembling porcelain. Hoya alata thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid conditions.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
