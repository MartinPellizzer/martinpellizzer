Huernia zebrina subsp. magniflora

Huernia zebrina subsp.

magniflora, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features a thick, ribbed stem with a distinctive zebra-like pattern of green and yellowish-white stripes.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white, blooming in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
