Heliosperma pusillum var. diklicii

Heliosperma pusillum var. diklicii, commonly known as the dwarf sunspore, is a small, herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually yellow or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
