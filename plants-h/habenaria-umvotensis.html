Habenaria umvotensis

Habenaria umvotensis, commonly known as the Umvoti orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single or few leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty.
