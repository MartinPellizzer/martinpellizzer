Helicteres lhotzkyana

Helicteres lhotzkyana, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its vibrant floral display.
