Hieracium stachyoideum

Hieracium stachyoideum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium stachyoideum is often found in temperate climates and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
