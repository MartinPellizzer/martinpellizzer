Hyptis maya

Hyptis maya, commonly known as Mayan hyptis, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. Hyptis maya is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
