Hieracium gigantium

Hieracium gigantium, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium gigantium is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
