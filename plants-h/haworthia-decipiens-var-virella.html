Haworthia decipiens var. virella

Haworthia decipiens var. virella, commonly known as the "Green Haworthia," is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
