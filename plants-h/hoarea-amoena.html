Hoarea amoena

Hoarea amoena, commonly known as the white-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its elegant, white petals and a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Hoarea amoena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
