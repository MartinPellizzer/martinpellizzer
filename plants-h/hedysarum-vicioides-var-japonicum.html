Hedysarum vicioides var. japonicum

Hedysarum vicioides var. japonicum, commonly known as Japanese vetch, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Hedysarum vicioides var. japonicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
