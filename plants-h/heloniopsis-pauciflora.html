Heloniopsis pauciflora

Heloniopsis pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered heloniopsis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
