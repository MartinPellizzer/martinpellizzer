Helianthemum oelandicum var. micranthum

Helianthemum oelandicum var. micranthum, commonly known as the Oeland thyme, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features compact, evergreen foliage with a strong aromatic scent, and its small, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
