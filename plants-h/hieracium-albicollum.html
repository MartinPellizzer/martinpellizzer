Hieracium albicollum

Hieracium albicollum, commonly known as the white-necked hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, upright stems. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and grasslands. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

It is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in disturbed soils.
