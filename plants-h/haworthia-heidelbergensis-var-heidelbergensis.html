Haworthia heidelbergensis var. heidelbergensis

Haworthia heidelbergensis var. heidelbergensis, commonly known as the Heidelberg Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The leaves are often adorned with translucent windows, which allow light to penetrate for photosynthesis. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its drought tolerance and minimal care requirements.
