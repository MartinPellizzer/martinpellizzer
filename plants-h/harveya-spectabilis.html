Harveya spectabilis

Harveya spectabilis, commonly known as the showy harveya, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of vivid orange-red flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. Its unique floral structure attracts a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Harveya spectabilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
