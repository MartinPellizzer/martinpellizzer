Hecatandra

Hecatandra, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the surface of water. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Hecatandra is also revered in various cultural and religious practices for its symbolic significance.
