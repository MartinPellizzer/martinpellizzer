Hieracium schmidtii subsp. brunelliforme

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

brunelliforme, commonly known as the Schmidt's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies brunelliforme is distinguished by its compact growth habit and specific leaf morphology. It thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, often growing in rocky or grassy slopes.
