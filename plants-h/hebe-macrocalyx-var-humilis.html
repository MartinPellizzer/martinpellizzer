Hebe macrocalyx var. humilis

Hebe macrocalyx var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf hebe, is a compact shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
