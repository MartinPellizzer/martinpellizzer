Hypolytrum leptocalamum

Hypolytrum leptocalamum, commonly known as the slender-leaved spikerush, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to Australia, this species thrives in wetland and marshy environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the sedge family.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
