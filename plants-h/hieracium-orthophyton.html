Hieracium orthophyton

Hieracium orthophyton, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are upright and often reddish in color.

Hieracium orthophyton is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
