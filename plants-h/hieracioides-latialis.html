Hieracioides latialis

Hieracioides latialis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved helenia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense heads, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
