Hypericum origanifolium var. candolleanum

Hypericum origanifolium var. candolleanum, commonly known as the Corsican St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
