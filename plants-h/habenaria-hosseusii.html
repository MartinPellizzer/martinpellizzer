Habenaria hosseusii

Habenaria hosseusii, commonly known as the Hosseus' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
