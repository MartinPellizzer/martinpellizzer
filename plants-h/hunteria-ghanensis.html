Hunteria ghanensis

Hunteria ghanensis, commonly known as the Ghana hunteria, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with smooth, grayish bark and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with a prominent red or purple corona, making it a striking ornamental plant.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.
