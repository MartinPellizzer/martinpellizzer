Hieracium melanotrychum

Hieracium melanotrychum, commonly known as the black-haired hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are rough and hairy, with a dark green color, and the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is often found in temperate regions and can be a common weed in agricultural areas.
