Hieracium striatisquameum

Hieracium striatisquameum, commonly known as the striped hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium striatisquameum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in disturbed soils.
