Herorchis collina

Herorchis collina, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a unique structure that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

Herorchis collina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
