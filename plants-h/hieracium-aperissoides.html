Hieracium aperissoides

Hieracium aperissoides, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy, often lobed leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

It is sometimes considered a weed due to its aggressive growth habit.
