Hieracium calolepideum

Hieracium calolepideum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Hieracium calolepideum thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often in temperate climates.
