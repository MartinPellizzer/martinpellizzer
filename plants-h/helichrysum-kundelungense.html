Helichrysum kundelungense

Helichrysum kundelungense, commonly known as the Kundelungue everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant produces fluffy, seed-filled heads that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, typically growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
