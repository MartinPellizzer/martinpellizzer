Hydrocotyle scutellifera

Hydrocotyle scutellifera, commonly known as the silverleaf dewflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and shady woodland areas.

Its rapid growth and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for water gardens and landscaping.
