Hosta longipes var. gracillima

Hosta longipes var. gracillima, commonly known as the graceful giant hosta, is a cultivar of the Hosta plant renowned for its striking, elongated leaves.

It features broad, lance-shaped foliage that can grow up to 3 feet in length, showcasing a vibrant green color with a subtle variegation pattern. This variety thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it an excellent choice for shaded garden areas. Its foliage remains lush throughout the growing season, providing ornamental value from spring through fall.

While it produces small, fragrant flowers, the plant is primarily cultivated for its impressive foliage rather than its blooms.
