Hypochaeris odorata var. eurylepis

Hypochaeris odorata var. eurylepis, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow composite flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in meadows and along roadsides.
