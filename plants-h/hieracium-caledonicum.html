Hieracium caledonicum

Hieracium caledonicum, commonly known as the Caledonian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. Its leaves are rough and hairy, with a distinctive basal rosette structure.

It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
