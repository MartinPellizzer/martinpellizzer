Hieracium subglomeratum

Hieracium subglomeratum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a cluster of small, yellowish to reddish flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils. The plant is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along streambanks.

It is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes and is considered a common weed in some regions.
