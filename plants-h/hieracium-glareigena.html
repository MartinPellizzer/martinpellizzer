Hieracium glareigena

Hieracium glareigena, commonly known as the glaucous hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in moist, grassy habitats. It is often found in meadows, pastures, and along riverbanks.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
