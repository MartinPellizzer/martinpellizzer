Hieracium riolagoanum

Hieracium riolagoanum, commonly known as the Riolagoan hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pale green, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers with ray florets. Native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile, it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, showing adaptability to various environmental conditions.

Its growth pattern and floral structure are typical of the genus Hieracium, which includes many species of hawkweeds.
