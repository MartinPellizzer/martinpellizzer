Hackelia scaberrima

Hackelia scaberrima, commonly known as the rough hackelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine, stiff hairs. Its showy clusters of pink to purple flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Hackelia scaberrima is adapted to arid environments and is often found in chaparral and woodland habitats.
