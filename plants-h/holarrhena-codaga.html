Holarrhena codaga

Holarrhena codaga, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its striking, flame-colored flowers that appear in clusters. The tree is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
