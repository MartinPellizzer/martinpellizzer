Hieracium adenozum

Hieracium adenozum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Hieracium adenozum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common and sometimes invasive species in certain regions.
