Habenaria obtusata var. collectanea

Habenaria obtusata var. collectanea, commonly known as the clustered helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, flowering stems that bear several spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive, elongated labellum that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

The plant is notable for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in horticulture.
