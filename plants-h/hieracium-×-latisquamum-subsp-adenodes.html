Hieracium × latisquamum subsp. adenodes

Hieracium × latisquamum subsp.

adenodes, commonly known as the broad-scale hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its robust growth and distinctive, broad, rounded leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations.
