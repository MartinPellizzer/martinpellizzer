Hymenocallis biflora

Hymenocallis biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered spider lily, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features striking, white, spider-like flowers with a prominent yellow center. It produces two to three flowers per stem, which bloom in summer.

Hymenocallis biflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained, sandy soils.
