Hieracium spatalops

Hieracium spatalops, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal rosettes of broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Hieracium spatalops is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
