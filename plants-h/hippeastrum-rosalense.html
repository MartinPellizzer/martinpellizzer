Hippeastrum rosalense

Hippeastrum rosalense, commonly known as the Rosalense amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous stem. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hippeastrum rosalense is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
