Hoya tjampeaensis

Hoya tjampeaensis, commonly known as the Javanese Wax Plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asclepiadaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, elliptical leaves that are often variegated with cream or white markings. Hoya tjampeaensis is a popular houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
