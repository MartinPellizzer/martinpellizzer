Homalomena metallica

Homalomena metallica, commonly known as the silver arrow plant, is a tropical evergreen herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a metallic sheen. The plant features a striking inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its foliage is prized for its ornamental value and its ability to purify indoor air.
