Hypericum seniawinii

Hypericum seniawinii, commonly known as the Seniawini St. John's Wort, is a species of flowering plant in the family Hypericaceae.

It is native to parts of East Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly rough texture.

Hypericum seniawinii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
