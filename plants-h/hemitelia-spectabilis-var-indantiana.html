Hemitelia spectabilis var. indantiana

Hemitelia spectabilis var. indantiana, commonly known as the purple-flowered hemitelia, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are clustered in whorls along the stems, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
