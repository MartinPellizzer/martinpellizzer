Hydrangea yaoshanensis

Hydrangea yaoshanensis, commonly known as the Yaoshan hydrangea, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from blue to pink depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of serrated, ovate leaves that are dark green in color.

Hydrangea yaoshanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
