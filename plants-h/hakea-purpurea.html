Hakea purpurea

Hakea purpurea, commonly known as the purple hakea, is a shrub native to southwestern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical flower spikes that range in color from pink to purple. The plant has leathery, often blue-green leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It produces woody, cone-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Hakea purpurea is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal and sandy soils.
