Hieracium sarracenicum

Hieracium sarracenicum, commonly known as the water hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow, dandelion-like flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as wet meadows and stream banks. It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is often found in temperate regions.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native habitats.
