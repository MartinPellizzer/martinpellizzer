Habenaria gabonensis

Habenaria gabonensis, commonly known as the Gabon orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on tree trunks or in the undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with intricate patterns, and it produces small, rounded pseudobulbs.

Habenaria gabonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems as a habitat for various insects.
