Hymenophyllum brevidens

Hymenophyllum brevidens, commonly known as the narrow-leaf filmy fern, is a small, delicate fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, translucent fronds that are often less than 10 centimeters in length. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. The fronds are covered with a thin, waxy cuticle that helps reduce water loss.

Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
