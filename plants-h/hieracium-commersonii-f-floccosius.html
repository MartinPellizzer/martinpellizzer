Hieracium commersonii f. floccosius

Hieracium commersonii f. floccosius, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, fluffy white to pale yellow flower heads, which are composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. It has hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name.

This fern-like plant is native to South America and is often found in temperate regions.
