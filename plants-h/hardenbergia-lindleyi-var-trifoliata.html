Hardenbergia lindleyi var. trifoliata

Hardenbergia lindleyi var. trifoliata, commonly known as the trifoliate burr legume, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets, and its distinctive yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces woody pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
