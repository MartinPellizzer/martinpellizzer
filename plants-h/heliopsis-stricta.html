Heliopsis stricta

Heliopsis stricta, commonly known as stiff heliopsis, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features upright, branching stems that produce bright yellow, daisy-like flowers in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Heliopsis stricta is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
