Haworthia dimorpha

Haworthia dimorpha, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with distinct white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Haworthia dimorpha is popular as a low-maintenance indoor plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
