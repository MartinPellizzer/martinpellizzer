Hieracium leucoplethum

Hieracium leucoplethum, commonly known as the whitefield hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium leucoplethum is known for its ornamental value but can also be invasive in certain regions.
