Homalomena tonkinensis

Homalomena tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking, pale yellow to white flowers arranged in a spadix, enclosed by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
