Howardia cynanchifolia

Howardia cynanchifolia, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with smooth, grey-green bark and opposite, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Howardia cynanchifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions.
