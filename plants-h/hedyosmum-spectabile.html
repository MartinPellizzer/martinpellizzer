Hedyosmum spectabile

Hedyosmum spectabile, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in long, pendulous clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Hedyosmum spectabile is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
