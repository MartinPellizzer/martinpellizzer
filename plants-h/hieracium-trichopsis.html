Hieracium trichopsis

Hieracium trichopsis, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium trichopsis is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to colonize various habitats.
