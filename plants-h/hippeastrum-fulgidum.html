Hippeastrum fulgidum

Hippeastrum fulgidum, commonly known as the red banana.

This tropical plant is native to South America and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter. The leaves are broad, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hippeastrum fulgidum is a popular ornamental plant, often cultivated for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped blooms.
