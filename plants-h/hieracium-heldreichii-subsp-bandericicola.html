Hieracium heldreichii subsp. bandericicola

Hieracium heldreichii subsp.

bandericicola, commonly known as the Heldreich's hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring calcareous soils. It has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is distinguished by its unique floral structure and is of interest in botanical studies due to its limited distribution and ecological specificity.
