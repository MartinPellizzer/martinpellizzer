Hieracium enantiodon

Hieracium enantiodon, commonly known as the devil's bit scabious, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium enantiodon thrives in moist meadows and woodland edges, often forming dense colonies in its native habitat.
