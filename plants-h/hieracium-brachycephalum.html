Hieracium brachycephalum

Hieracium brachycephalum, commonly known as the short-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

Hieracium brachycephalum is a perennial species that produces seeds through its composite flower heads.
