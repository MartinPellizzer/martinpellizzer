Heterospathe ramulosa

Heterospathe ramulosa, commonly known as the slender feather-plant, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments. Its rhizomes are often covered in small, hair-like structures, contributing to its common name.

Heterospathe ramulosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in water gardens.
