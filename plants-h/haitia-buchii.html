Haitia buchii

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has oval, dark green leaves. It is relatively small, reaching heights of about 30 to 60 centimeters.
