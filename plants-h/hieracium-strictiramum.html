Hieracium strictiramum

Hieracium strictiramum, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, thriving in temperate climates. It features lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom from June to August.

Hieracium strictiramum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable habitats.
