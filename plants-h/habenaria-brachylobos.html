Habenaria brachylobos

Habenaria brachylobos, commonly known as the short-lobed marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant has a slender rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it often forms dense colonies in suitable habitats.

The species is notable for its delicate appearance and ecological importance in its native range.
