Hieracium communifrons

Hieracium communifrons, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Hieracium communifrons is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize open areas, making it both a common weed and an important nectar source for pollinators.
