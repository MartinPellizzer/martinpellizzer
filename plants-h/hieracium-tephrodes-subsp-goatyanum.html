Hieracium tephrodes subsp. goatyanum

Hieracium tephrodes subsp.

goatyanum, commonly known as the goat's horn hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides, preferring well-drained soils.
