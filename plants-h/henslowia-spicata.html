Henslowia spicata

Henslowia spicata, commonly known as the spiky henslowia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Henslowia spicata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
