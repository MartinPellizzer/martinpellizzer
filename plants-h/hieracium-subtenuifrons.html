Hieracium subtenuifrons

Hieracium subtenuifrons, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grassy areas and open woodlands. The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium subtenuifrons is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates and is often considered a minor weed in agricultural settings.
