Hemionitis schaffneri

Hemionitis schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's hemionitis, is a rare and unique fern native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically gray-green in color. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, rocky habitats. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, a common feature among ferns.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Hemionitis schaffneri is considered a valuable subject for botanical research.
