Hypericum formosum

Hypericum formosum, commonly known as the Formosan St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has opposite, simple leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing seeds.

Hypericum formosum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
