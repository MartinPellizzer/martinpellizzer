Habenaria Muricata

The Habenaria muricata, commonly known as the rough-leafed orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to various regions across Asia. This plant is characterized by its unique, hairy leaves and vibrant, fragrant flowers that bloom in a range of colors including yellow, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas with rich, organic soil. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is both admired by botanists and sought after by gardeners. However, habitat destruction and over-collection have led to its decline, making conservation efforts essential for its survival.
