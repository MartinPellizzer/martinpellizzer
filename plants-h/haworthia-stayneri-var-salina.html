Haworthia stayneri var. salina

Haworthia stayneri var. salina, commonly known as the salt and pepper Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, allowing the plant to thrive in arid conditions. It produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
