Habenaria hastata

Habenaria hastata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Habenaria hastata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
