Hymenorchis caulina

Hymenorchis caulina, commonly known as the purple lady's tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its distinctive inflorescence of violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and rocky areas. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

Hymenorchis caulina is known for its fragrant blooms and is often found in temperate regions of Asia and North America.
