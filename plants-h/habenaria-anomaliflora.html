Habenaria anomaliflora

Habenaria anomaliflora, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring showy, often violet or purple flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-like, adapted for efficient water absorption.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitats.
