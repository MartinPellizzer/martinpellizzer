Hieracium murorum subsp. pseudopolyschistum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

pseudopolyschistum, commonly known as the false hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology and flowering habits compared to other Hieracium species. It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
