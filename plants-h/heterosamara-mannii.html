Heterosamara mannii

Heterosamara mannii, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing one or two seeds.
