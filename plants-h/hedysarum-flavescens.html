Hedysarum flavescens

Hedysarum flavescens, commonly known as yellow vetch, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Hedysarum flavescens is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
