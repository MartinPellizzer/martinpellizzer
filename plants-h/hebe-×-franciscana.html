Hebe × franciscana

Hebe × franciscana, commonly known as the Franscana hebe, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features dense, evergreen foliage with a dark green hue. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, violet, or white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Hebe × franciscana is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
