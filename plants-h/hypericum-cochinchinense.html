Hypericum cochinchinense

Hypericum cochinchinense, commonly known as the Vietnamese St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is known for its medicinal properties.

Its use in traditional medicine is attributed to the presence of bioactive compounds like hypericin and pseudohypericin.
