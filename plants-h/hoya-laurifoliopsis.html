Hoya laurifoliopsis

Hoya laurifoliopsis, commonly known as the laceleaf hoya, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink, which bloom during the summer months. Hoya laurifoliopsis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
