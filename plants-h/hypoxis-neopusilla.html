Hypoxis neopusilla

Hypoxis neopusilla, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Hypoxis neopusilla is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
