Habenaria maculifera

Habenaria maculifera, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant produces small, greenish or yellowish flowers that are often adorned with distinctive spots or markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
