Hieracium florentinum var. aquilonare

Hieracium florentinum var. aquilonare, commonly known as the Windflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers with dark centers. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
