Hieracium stefanssonii

Hieracium stefanssonii, commonly known as Stefansson's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions of North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, rocky habitats and is adapted to cold climates with short growing seasons. Its leaves are usually basal, forming a rosette, and its stems are often hairy.

Stefansson's hawkweed is known for its small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer.
