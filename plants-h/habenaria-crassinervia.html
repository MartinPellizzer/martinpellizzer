Habenaria crassinervia

Habenaria crassinervia, commonly known as the broad-veined orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves with prominent, parallel veins. The plant produces striking, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
