Hydrostachys insignis var. congolana

Hydrostachys insignis var. congolana, commonly known as the Congo hydrostachys, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Central and Western Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along riverbanks. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves with a smooth surface and a distinct, aromatic scent. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often with a yellowish or greenish hue.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local medicine.
