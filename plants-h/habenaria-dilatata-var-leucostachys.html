Habenaria dilatata var. leucostachys

Habenaria dilatata var. leucostachys, commonly known as the White-stemmed Sword Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences of white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
