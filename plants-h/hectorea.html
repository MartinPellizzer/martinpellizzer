Hectorea

Hectorea, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-purple flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces seeds in a distinctive cone-shaped structure. Hectorea thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
