Hieracium bergsetense

Hieracium bergsetense, commonly known as the Bergsetense Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in early summer, attracting various pollinators.

The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
