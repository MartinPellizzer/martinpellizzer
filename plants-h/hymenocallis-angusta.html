Hymenocallis angusta

Hymenocallis angusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spider lily, is a bulbous plant native to Mexico.

It produces striking, white, bell-shaped flowers with a greenish throat, often adorned with a yellowish or reddish stripe. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hymenocallis angusta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
