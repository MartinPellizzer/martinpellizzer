Homalomena grabowskii

Homalomena grabowskii, commonly known as the Grabowsky arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves. The plant produces striking spadix flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the arum family. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Homalomena grabowskii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
