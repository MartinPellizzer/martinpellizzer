Helichrysum steetzianum

Helichrysum steetzianum, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Helichrysum steetzianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
