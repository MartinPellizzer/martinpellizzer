Hieracium juranum subsp. glaucocinereum

Hieracium juranum subsp.

glaucocinereum, commonly known as the glaucocinereum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped, and its stems are often hairy as well.

The subspecies is distinguished by its glaucous (bluish-green) appearance, particularly on the underside of its leaves.
