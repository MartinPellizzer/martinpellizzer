Hieracium murorum subsp. erysibodes

Hieracium murorum subsp.

erysibodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which typically bloom from June to August.

The plant has a rosette of basal leaves that are typically hairy and lobed. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its presence often indicates a habitat that has been recently disturbed or is in a early successional stage.
