Hylodesmum densum

Hylodesmum densum, commonly known as the dense hylodesmum, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Hylodesmum densum is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
