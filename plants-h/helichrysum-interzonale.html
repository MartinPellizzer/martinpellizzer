Helichrysum interzonale

Helichrysum interzonale, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting color. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Helichrysum interzonale is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
