Hieracium dispergens

Hieracium dispergens, commonly known as the scattered hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has upright stems with lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It is characterized by its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain areas.

Its botanical features make it a common species in temperate regions.
