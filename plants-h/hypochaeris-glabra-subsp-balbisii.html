Hypochaeris glabra subsp. balbisii

Hypochaeris glabra subsp.

balbisii, commonly known as the smooth weed or yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. It produces small, yellow flowers with ray florets and is often found in temperate climates.

The subspecies balbisii is distinguished by its smooth, hairless leaves and shorter stems compared to other varieties of Hypochaeris.
