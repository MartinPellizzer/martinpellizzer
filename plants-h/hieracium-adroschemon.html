Hieracium adroschemon

Hieracium adroschemon, commonly known as the mountain hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in dense clusters.

Hieracium adroschemon is often found in open, sunny areas and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
