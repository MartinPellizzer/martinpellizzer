Hydrangea hortensis var. mariesii

Hydrangea hortensis var. mariesii, commonly known as the Mariei hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flower clusters that range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade. Its foliage is dark green and turns red in the fall, adding seasonal interest.

It is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
