Helicteres kombolgiana

Helicteres kombolgiana, commonly known as the Kombolgie tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often arranged in a pinnate pattern, and its clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces smooth, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Helicteres kombolgiana is valued for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
