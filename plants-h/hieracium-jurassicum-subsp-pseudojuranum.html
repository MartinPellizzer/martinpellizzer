Hieracium jurassicum subsp. pseudojuranum

Hieracium jurassicum subsp.

pseudojuranum, commonly known as the Jurassic hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow flower heads composed of numerous small florets.

The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and is found in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It is native to parts of Europe and has been introduced to other regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in some areas.
