Homalomena elliptica

Homalomena elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic caladium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically brightly colored with shades of red, orange, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental foliage plant. Its leaves are often used in floral arrangements due to their striking appearance.

Homalomena elliptica prefers well-draining soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for indoor or shaded garden settings.
