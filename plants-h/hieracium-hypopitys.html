Hieracium hypopitys

Hieracium hypopitys, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and sometimes branched.

Hieracium hypopitys is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in many regions.
