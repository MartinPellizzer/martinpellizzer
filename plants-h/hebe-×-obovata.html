Hebe × obovata

Hebe × obovata, commonly known as the mountain hebe, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant typically features broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Hebe × obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
