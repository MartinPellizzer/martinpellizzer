Habenaria lawii

Habenaria lawii, commonly known as the Singapore orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green or white flowers with distinct sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Habenaria lawii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
