Hieracium tridentatum subsp. lotharingum

Hieracium tridentatum subsp.

lotharingum, commonly known as the Lotharingian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow flowering heads.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks. Its flowers are usually discoid, with no ray florets, and it produces achene seeds with pappus for wind dispersal.

It is often found in disturbed habitats and can form dense colonies in suitable conditions.
