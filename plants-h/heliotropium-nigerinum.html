Heliotropium nigerinum

Heliotropium nigerinum, commonly known as black heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple and often have a faintly fragrant scent. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and have a rough texture.

Heliotropium nigerinum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in many areas.
