Herrea fusiformis

Herrea fusiformis, commonly known as the slender leaf cactus, is a species native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often covered in small, spiny tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically clustered at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent gardens.
