Helenium uniflorum

Helenium uniflorum, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow to orange flowers that resemble arrowheads. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower landscapes.

Helenium uniflorum is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
