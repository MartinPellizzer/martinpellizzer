Hieracium × pui

Hieracium × pui, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is often found in meadows and disturbed soils. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and has hairy stems and leaves. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Hieracium × pui is an invasive species in many regions due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
