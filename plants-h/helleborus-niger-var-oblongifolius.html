Helleborus niger var. oblongifolius

Helleborus niger var. oblongifolius, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It features dark green, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy. The plant produces striking white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to bloom during the holiday season.
