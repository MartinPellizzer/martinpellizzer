Haageocereus aureispinus var. rigidispinus

Haageocereus aureispinus var. rigidispinus, commonly known as the rigid spined hedgehog cactus, is a cacti species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in long, rigid spines, which provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its spiny structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
