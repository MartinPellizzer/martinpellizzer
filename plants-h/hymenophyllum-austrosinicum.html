Hymenophyllum austrosinicum

Hymenophyllum austrosinicum, commonly known as the Chinese spore moss, is a small, delicate fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length. The plant exhibits a unique reproductive structure known as a sorus, which contains the spore-producing sporangia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocky substrates in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its intricate morphology and specialized adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
