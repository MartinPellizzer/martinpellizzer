Hirculus aristulatus

Hirculus aristulatus, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive, sword-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a rigid, upright structure.

Hirculus aristulatus is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
