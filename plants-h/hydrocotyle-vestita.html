Hydrocotyle vestita

Hydrocotyle vestita, commonly known as the velvetleaf water shield, is a perennial aquatic plant native to wetlands and marshy areas.

It has small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces yellowish flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its roots help stabilize the soil, making it important for wetland ecosystems.

Hydrocotyle vestita is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments.
