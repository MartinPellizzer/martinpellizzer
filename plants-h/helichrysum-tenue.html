Helichrysum tenue

Helichrysum tenue, commonly known as the slender everlasting.

It is a perennial herb native to southern Africa, characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers that dry to a striking golden hue. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and a compact growth habit, often forming dense mats. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
