Hondbesseion tomentosum

Hondbesseion tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly moss.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant that thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage, which gives it a soft, fuzzy appearance. Its leaves are typically small, overlapping, and covered in fine, silky hairs.

This species is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
