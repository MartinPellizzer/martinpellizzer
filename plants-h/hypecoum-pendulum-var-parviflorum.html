Hypecoum pendulum var. parviflorum

Hypecoum pendulum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered bleeding heart, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It features delicate, heart-shaped flowers that hang in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its blooming period usually occurs in late spring to early summer.
