Hieracium marginelliforme

Hieracium marginelliforme, commonly known as the marginelliform hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
