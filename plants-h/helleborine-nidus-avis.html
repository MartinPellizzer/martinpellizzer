Helleborine nidus-avis

Helleborine nidus-avis, commonly known as the bird's nest helleborine, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a slender, underground rhizome with a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive, hood-like structure resembling a bird's nest.

The species is characterized by its unique morphology and is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.
