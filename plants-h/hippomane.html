Hippomane

Hippomane, commonly known as balata, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its latex-producing properties. The plant produces a milky sap that is used in the production of rubber and other industrial materials. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy coating.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.
