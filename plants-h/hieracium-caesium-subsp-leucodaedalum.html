Hieracium caesium subsp. leucodaedalum

Hieracium caesium subsp.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are erect and often branched.
