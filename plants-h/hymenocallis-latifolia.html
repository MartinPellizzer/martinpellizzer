Hymenocallis latifolia

Hymenocallis latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf hurricane lily, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant has strap-like, broad leaves that are typically green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hymenocallis latifolia is also known for its ability to bloom multiple times throughout the growing season.
