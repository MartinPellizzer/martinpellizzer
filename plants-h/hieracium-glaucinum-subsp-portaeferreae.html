Hieracium glaucinum subsp. portaeferreae

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

portaeferreae, commonly known as the silver hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its silver-white, woolly leaves and yellowish to orange flower heads.

This subspecies is typically found in moist meadows and grasslands across Europe. It has a creeping growth habit with a rosette of basal leaves.

The plant produces numerous small, composite flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
