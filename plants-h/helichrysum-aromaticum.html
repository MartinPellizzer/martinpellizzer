Helichrysum aromaticum

Helichrysum aromaticum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow or golden flowers that remain intact even after drying, giving it a long-lasting appearance. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its aromatic leaves and flowers are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.

Helichrysum aromaticum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
