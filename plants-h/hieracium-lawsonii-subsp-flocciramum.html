Hieracium lawsonii subsp. flocciramum

Hieracium lawsonii subsp.

flocciramum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
