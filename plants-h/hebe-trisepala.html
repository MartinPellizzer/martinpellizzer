Hebe trisepala

Hebe trisepala, commonly known as the silver spruce hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
