Haworthia pilifera var. columnaris

Haworthia pilifera var. columnaris, commonly known as the columnar haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, cylindrical leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
