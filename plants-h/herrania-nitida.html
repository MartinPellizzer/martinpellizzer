Herrania nitida

Herrania nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Herrania nitida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
