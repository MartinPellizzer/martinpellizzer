Hieracium balbisianum subsp. issense

Hieracium balbisianum subsp.

issense, commonly known as the Balbisian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
