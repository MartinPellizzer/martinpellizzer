Hedysarum junceum

Hedysarum junceum, commonly known as narrowleaf sweetclover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in racemes. The plant has a deep taproot system, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils and dry conditions. It is often found in rocky or open habitats, such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Hedysarum junceum is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
