Hieracium paratocum

Hieracium paratocum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces yellow composite flowers with ray florets, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges. It has a hairy stem and is often characterized by its clustered growth habit.

Hieracium paratocum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and seeds, making it a common weed in various habitats.
