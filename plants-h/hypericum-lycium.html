Hypericum lycium

Hypericum lycium, commonly known as the Chinese burnet, is a perennial shrub native to Asia.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and red or orange berries that appear in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Hypericum lycium is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
