Hesperis pyrenaica

Hesperis pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean honesty, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in alpine or subalpine habitats. Its flowers are white or pale pink, and it produces small, round seed pods that split open to release seeds.

Hesperis pyrenaica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in mountain ecosystems.
