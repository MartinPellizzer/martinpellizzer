Haxtonia phlogopappa

Haxtonia phlogopappa, commonly known as the flameleaf haxtonia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and its striking, fiery orange to red foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
