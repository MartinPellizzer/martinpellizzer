Helianthemum apenninum var. roseum

Helianthemum apenninum var. roseum, commonly known as the pink rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, showy pink or rose-colored flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has silver-green foliage with glandular hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
