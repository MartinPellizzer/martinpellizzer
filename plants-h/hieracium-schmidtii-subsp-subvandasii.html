Hieracium schmidtii subsp. subvandasii

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

subvandasii, commonly known as the Schmidt's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.
