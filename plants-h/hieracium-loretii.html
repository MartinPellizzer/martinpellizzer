Hieracium loretii

Hieracium loretii, commonly known as the Loretto hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads made up of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. Hieracium loretii is known for its hardy nature and is often found in high-altitude meadows and scree slopes.
