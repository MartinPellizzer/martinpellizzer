Hosta japonica var. lancifolia

Hosta japonica var. lancifolia, commonly known as the Japanese lanceleaf hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-white margin. The plant produces upright, bell-shaped flowers in late summer, usually in shades of white or pale blue. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, offering ornamental value in both garden and landscape settings.
