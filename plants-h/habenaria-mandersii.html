Habenaria mandersii

Habenaria mandersii, commonly known as the Mander's butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Habenaria mandersii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
