Hieracium speciosum

Hieracium speciosum, commonly known as showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It produces bright yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Hieracium speciosum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
