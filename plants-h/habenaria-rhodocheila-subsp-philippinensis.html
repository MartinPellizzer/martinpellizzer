Habenaria rhodocheila subsp. philippinensis

Habenaria rhodocheila subsp.

philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines. It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and its distinctive inflorescence of pinkish-purple flowers arranged in a loose raceme.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and along stream banks. Its flowers are highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators, and it is known for its ornamental value.

This subspecies is considered rare and is often found in specific microhabitats within its native range.
