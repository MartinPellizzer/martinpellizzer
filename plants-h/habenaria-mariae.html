Habenaria mariae

Habenaria mariae, commonly known as the Marie's butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its roots are often mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
