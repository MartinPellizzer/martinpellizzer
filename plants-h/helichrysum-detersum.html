Helichrysum detersum

Helichrysum detersum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that retain their color even after drying. The plant has narrow, silver-gray leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.

Helichrysum detersum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
