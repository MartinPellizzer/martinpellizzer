Heliophytum stenophyllum var. myosotifolium

Heliophytum stenophyllum var. myosotifolium, commonly known as the blue bell flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped foliage with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers during the warmer months, adding a vibrant touch to rock gardens or dry landscapes. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
