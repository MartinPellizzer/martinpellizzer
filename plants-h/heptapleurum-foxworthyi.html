Heptapleurum foxworthyi

Heptapleurum foxworthyi, commonly known as the golden bamboo or Chinese evergreen, is a striking evergreen shrub native to China.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a yellowish or golden hue on the underside. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its foliage provides year-round visual interest, making it a valuable addition to shaded landscapes.
