Haworthia marumiana var. archeri

Haworthia marumiana var. archeri, commonly known as the Cape Town Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
