Hieracium pseudoeversianum

Hieracium pseudoeversianum, commonly known as the false hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium pseudoeversianum is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain regions.
