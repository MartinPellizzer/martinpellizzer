Horsfieldia samarensis

Horsfieldia samarensis, commonly known as the Samarensis orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Indonesia, specifically the island of Sumba. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees, drawing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. It produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration, often in shades of yellow and orange.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
