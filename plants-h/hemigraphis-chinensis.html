Hemigraphis chinensis

Hemigraphis chinensis, commonly known as Chinese nettle or shining leaves, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Hemigraphis chinensis is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
