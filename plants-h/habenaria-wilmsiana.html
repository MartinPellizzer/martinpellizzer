Habenaria wilmsiana

Habenaria wilmsiana, commonly known as the Wilms' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
