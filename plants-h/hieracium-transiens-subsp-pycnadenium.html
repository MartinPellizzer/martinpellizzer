Hieracium transiens subsp. pycnadenium

Hieracium transiens subsp.

pycnadenium, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are broadly ovate to lanceolate in shape. Its flowering stems bear a cluster of yellow flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence.

The subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and the presence of glandular hairs on its foliage.
