Hieracium extensifrons

Hieracium extensifrons, commonly known as the broad-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium extensifrons is often considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive growth.
