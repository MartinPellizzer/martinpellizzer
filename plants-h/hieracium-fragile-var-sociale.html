Hieracium fragile var. sociale

Hieracium fragile var. sociale, commonly known as the social蒲公英, is a variant of the common dandelion belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided leaves and yellow flowers that develop into fluffy white seed heads. This plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open areas, thriving in temperate climates. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, a common trait among plants in the dandelion family.

The species is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny habitats.
