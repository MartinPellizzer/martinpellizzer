Hydrangea neesiana

Hydrangea neesiana, commonly known as the Nees's hydrangea, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features large, showy inflorescences composed of sterile flowers that form a rounded or conical cluster. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
