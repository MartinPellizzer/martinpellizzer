Hieracium levicaule subsp. euroum

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

euroum, commonly known as the European slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

The plant has slender, erect stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, consisting of numerous small florets.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread vegetatively and form dense colonies.
