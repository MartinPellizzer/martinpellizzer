Habenaria japonica var. montana

Habenaria japonica var. montana, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single, terminal spike bearing several white to pale pink flowers with a distinct lip. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
