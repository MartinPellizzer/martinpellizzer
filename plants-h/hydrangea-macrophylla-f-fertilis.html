Hydrangea macrophylla f. fertilis

Hydrangea macrophylla f. fertilis, commonly known as the fertilis hydrangea, is a cultivar characterized by its large, showy flower heads.

This variety is prized for its ability to produce vibrant blooms in shades of blue, pink, and red, depending on soil pH. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and provide a striking contrast to its colorful flowers. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Hydrangea macrophylla f. fertilis is a popular choice for gardeners looking to add a dramatic focal point to their landscapes.
