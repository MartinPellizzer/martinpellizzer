Hakea Integrifolia

Hakea integrifolia is a unique and resilient plant native to the coastal regions of southeastern Australia. Known for its striking appearance and ecological importance, it thrives in harsh coastal environments. This evergreen shrub features needle-like leaves and produces vibrant red or orange flowers. It plays a vital role in stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for native wildlife. Its adaptability makes it a valuable species for coastal restoration projects.
