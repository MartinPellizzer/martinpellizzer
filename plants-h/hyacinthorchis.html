Hyacinthorchis

Hyacinthorchis, commonly known as the hyacinth orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to various regions in Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. Hyacinthorchis species are characterized by their showy, fragrant flowers that resemble those of the hyacinth, hence their common name. They typically grow in wet, shaded environments such as forest floors or along streams.

The genus includes several species, each with unique flower colors and patterns, making them popular among orchid enthusiasts.
