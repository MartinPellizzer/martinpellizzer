Hereroa carinans

Hereroa carinans, commonly known as the African horned lily, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It features a tall, slender stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces large, showy flowers with prominent, horn-like projections, giving it its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is valued both for ornamental purposes and in traditional medicine.
