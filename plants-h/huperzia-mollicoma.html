Huperzia mollicoma

Huperzia mollicoma, commonly known as the soft moss huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its creeping, rhizomatous growth habit and its ability to form dense mats on forest floors. The plant features simple, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a soft, woolly texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate and subtropical regions.

Huperzia mollicoma plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitats for various small organisms.
