Hedysarum abyssinicum

Hedysarum abyssinicum, commonly known as Abyssinian vetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to Ethiopia.

It features trifoliate leaves and produces yellow to orange pea-like flowers. The plant has a climbing habit and possesses a well-developed root system. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Hedysarum abyssinicum is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
