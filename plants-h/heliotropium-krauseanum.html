Heliotropium krauseanum

Heliotropium krauseanum, commonly known as the tropical heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, violet or purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in warm, humid climates.
