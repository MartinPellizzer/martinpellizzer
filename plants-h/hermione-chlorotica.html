Hermione chlorotica

Hermione chlorotica, commonly known as the green mist plant, is a unique species characterized by its dense, chlorophyll-rich foliage that gives it a vibrant green appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forest understories. The plant's leaves are broad and leathery, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, blending in with its foliage.

Despite its subtle appearance, Hermione chlorotica plays an important role in supporting local insect populations and maintaining ecosystem balance.
