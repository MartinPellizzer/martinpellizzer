Hypoxis luzulifolia

Hypoxis luzulifolia, commonly known as the blue star grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Hypoxis luzulifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
