Hieracium cophodon

Hieracium cophodon, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Hieracium cophodon is often found in open habitats and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
