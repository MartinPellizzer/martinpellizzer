Haworthia dekenahii var. argenteomaculosa

Haworthia dekenahii var. argenteomaculosa, commonly known as the silver-spotted haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with distinctive white and silver markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
