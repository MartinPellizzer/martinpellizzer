Hieracium sphaeroideum

Hieracium sphaeroideum, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiny flower heads that are typically blue to purple in color. The plant has a robust root system and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium sphaeroideum is often found in dry, rocky soils and is considered a hardy, drought-tolerant species.
