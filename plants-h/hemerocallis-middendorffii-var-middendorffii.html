Hemerocallis middendorffii var. middendorffii

Hemerocallis middendorffii var. middendorffii, commonly known as the Siberian daylily, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Siberia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late summer. It is well adapted to cold climates and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its hardiness and ornamental value in gardens.
