Habenaria goetzeana

Habenaria goetzeana, commonly known as the Goetze's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often with a distinctive coloration pattern.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
