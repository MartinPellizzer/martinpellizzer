Helichrysum plantaginifolium

Helichrysum plantaginifolium, commonly known as the golden everlasting or field everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain colorful even after drying, making it popular in floral arrangements. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and open areas.

Helichrysum plantaginifolium is also valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine.
