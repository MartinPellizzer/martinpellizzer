Hieracium cymosum

Hieracium cymosum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and the plant is often found in clusters.

Hieracium cymosum is a perennial species that can spread through rhizomes, making it somewhat invasive in certain regions.
