Haworthiopsis coarctata var. adelaidensis

Haworthiopsis coarctata var. adelaidensis, commonly known as the Adelaidensis Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent with dense rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves.

Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, a trait typical of many succulents.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
