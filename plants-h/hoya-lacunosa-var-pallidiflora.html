Hoya lacunosa var. pallidiflora

Hoya lacunosa var. pallidiflora, commonly known as the pale-flowered spider plant, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or cream streaks, and it produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in humid environments.
