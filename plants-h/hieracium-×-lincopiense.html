Hieracium × lincopiense

Hieracium × lincopiense, commonly known as the Lincopi goldenrod, is a hybrid plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has basal and sometimes erect stems with serrated leaves.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
