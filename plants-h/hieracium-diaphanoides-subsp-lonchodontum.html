Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. lonchodontum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

lonchodontum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its delicate, yellowish flowers and finely divided, hairy leaves.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. It has a rosette growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous fruits.

The plant is adapted to temperate climates and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens.
