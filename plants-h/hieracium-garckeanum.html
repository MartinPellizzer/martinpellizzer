Hieracium garckeanum

Hieracium garckeanum, commonly known as the Garcke's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium garckeanum is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
