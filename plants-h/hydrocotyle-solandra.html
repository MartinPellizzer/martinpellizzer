Hydrocotyle solandra

Hydrocotyle solandra, commonly known as the silverleaf groundcherry, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, silver-veined leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a round, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds.

Hydrocotyle solandra is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks.
