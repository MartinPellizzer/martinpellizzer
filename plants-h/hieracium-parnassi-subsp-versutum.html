Hieracium parnassi subsp. versutum

Hieracium parnassi subsp.

versutum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is a perennial herb with basal rosettes of spoon-shaped leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant thrives in rocky and mountainous environments, often growing at high altitudes. It is characterized by its hairy stems and small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its habitat, it is adapted to cold climates and is often found in meadows and open slopes.
