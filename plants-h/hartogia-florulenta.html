Hartogia florulenta

Hartogia florulenta, commonly known as the Hartog's flowering plant, is a species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and yellow. The plant has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand prolonged drought periods.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
