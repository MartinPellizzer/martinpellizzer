Helichrysum ramosum

Helichrysum ramosum, commonly known as the golden torch or everlastings, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features wiry, branched stems covered in small, bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble tiny torches. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves that emit a strong, spicy scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The flowers are long-lasting and often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant color and durability.
