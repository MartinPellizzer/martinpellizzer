Hieracium Pallescens Subsp. Suboreites

Hieracium pallescens subsp. suboreites is a lesser-known member of the dandelion family found primarily in the mountainous regions of Europe. This plant is characterized by its pale yellow flowers and hairy stems, which help it survive in harsh alpine environments. It thrives in rocky soils and open areas where competition from other plants is minimal. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this unique plant from habitat loss and climate change.
