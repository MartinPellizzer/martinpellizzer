Hypochaeris glabra var. erostris

Hypochaeris glabra var. erostris, commonly known as the smooth hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides, and it produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and early summer.

Its leaves are usually smooth and alternate, with a slightly hairy texture, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
