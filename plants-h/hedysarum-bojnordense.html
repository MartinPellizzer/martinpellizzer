Hedysarum bojnordense

Hedysarum bojnordense, commonly known as the Bojnord sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of northern Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods, a typical feature of the Fabaceae family, and it plays a role in soil stabilization due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
