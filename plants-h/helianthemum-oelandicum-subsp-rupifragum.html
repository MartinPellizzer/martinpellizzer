Helianthemum oelandicum subsp. rupifragum

Helianthemum oelandicum subsp.

rupifragum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe. It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has fleshy, evergreen leaves that are adapted to withstand arid conditions. It typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xerophytic environments and rock gardens.
