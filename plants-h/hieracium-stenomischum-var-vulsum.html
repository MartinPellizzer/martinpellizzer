Hieracium stenomischum var. vulsum

Hieracium stenomischum var. vulsum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive basal arrangement.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
