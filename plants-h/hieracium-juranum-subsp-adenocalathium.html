Hieracium juranum subsp. adenocalathium

Hieracium juranum subsp.

adenocalathium, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. It has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
