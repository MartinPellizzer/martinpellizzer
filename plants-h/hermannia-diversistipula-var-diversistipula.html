Hermannia diversistipula var. diversistipula

Hermannia diversistipula var. diversistipula, commonly known as the dwarf red hot poker, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically evergreen and emit a mild, spicy fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
