Hedera helix var. marginata-rubra

Hedera helix var. marginata-rubra, commonly known as the red-veined ivy, is a variant of the common ivy characterized by its striking foliage.

Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a dark green background and prominent red veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. This plant is a climbing vine that thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark berries, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental leaves.

Hedera helix var. marginata-rubra is well-suited for indoor cultivation and can be trained to grow in hanging baskets or as a decorative vine.
