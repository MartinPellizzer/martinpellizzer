Hieracium attenuatifolium

Hieracium attenuatifolium, commonly known as the slender leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are linear and gradually tapering, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.
