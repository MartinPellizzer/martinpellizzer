Hieracium caniforme

Hieracium caniforme, commonly known as the bear's garlic or bear's buttercup, is a flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges. Hieracium caniforme is a perennial plant that thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
