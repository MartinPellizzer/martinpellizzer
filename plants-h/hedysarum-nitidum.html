Hedysarum nitidum

Hedysarum nitidum, commonly known as the smooth vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Hedysarum nitidum is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
