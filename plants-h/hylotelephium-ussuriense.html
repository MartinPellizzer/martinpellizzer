Hylotelephium ussuriense

Hylotelephium ussuriense, commonly known as the Ussuri stonecrop, is a perennial succulent plant native to eastern Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems and dense clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Hylotelephium ussuriense is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
