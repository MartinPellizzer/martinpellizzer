Hieracium arrosum

Hieracium arrosum, commonly known as the spreading hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, surrounded by pale green to white ray florets. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it often grows in meadows and along roadsides.

Hieracium arrosum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
