Hieracium leucotrichothyrsum

Hieracium leucotrichothyrsum, commonly known as the white-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
