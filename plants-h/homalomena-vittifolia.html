Homalomena vittifolia

Homalomena vittifolia, commonly known as the spotted arrowhead plant, is a tropical evergreen herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or light green veining pattern, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense foliage in suitable conditions.
