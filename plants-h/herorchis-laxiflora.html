Herorchis laxiflora

Herorchis laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in specific ecological conditions.
