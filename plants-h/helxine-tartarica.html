Helxine tartarica

Helxine tartarica, commonly known as the silver thread plant, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is a low-growing, evergreen perennial that features small, ovate leaves with a distinctive silvery-white underside. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers well-draining, moist soil.

Helxine tartarica is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, compact mats.
