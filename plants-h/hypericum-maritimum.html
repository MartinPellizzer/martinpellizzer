Hypericum maritimum

Hypericum maritimum, commonly known as sea thrift, is a perennial flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime environments. Its stems are typically prostrate or upright, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits containing seeds.

Hypericum maritimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
