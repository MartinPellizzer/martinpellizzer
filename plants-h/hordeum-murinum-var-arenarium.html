Hordeum murinum var. arenarium

Hordeum murinum var. arenarium, commonly known as sand barley, is a wild grass species native to arid and coastal regions.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed habitats. Its leaves are narrow and tough, adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

It plays a role in stabilizing dunes and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
