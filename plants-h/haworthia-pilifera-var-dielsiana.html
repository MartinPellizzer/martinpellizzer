Haworthia pilifera var. dielsiana

Haworthia pilifera var. dielsiana, commonly known as the Diels' Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white to pale yellow markings, often resembling the texture of leather. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
