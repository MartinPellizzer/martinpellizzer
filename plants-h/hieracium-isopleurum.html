Hieracium isopleurum

Hieracium isopleurum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed sites.
