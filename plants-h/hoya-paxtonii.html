Hoya paxtonii

Hoya paxtonii, commonly known as the "Paxton's Wreath," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Hoya paxtonii is popular as a houseplant due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
