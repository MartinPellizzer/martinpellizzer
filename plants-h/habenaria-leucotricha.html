Habenaria leucotricha

Habenaria leucotricha, commonly known as the white-haired orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers with a distinct, often fragrant scent. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
