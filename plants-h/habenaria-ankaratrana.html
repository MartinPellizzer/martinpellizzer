Habenaria ankaratrana

Habenaria ankaratrana, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a rare and endemic species found primarily in the rainforests of Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a slender, upright stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or white with purple or brown markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened and is the subject of conservation efforts.
