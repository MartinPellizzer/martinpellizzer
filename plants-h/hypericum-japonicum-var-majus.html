Hypericum japonicum var. majus

Hypericum japonicum var. majus, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers with orange stamens, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
