Haemanthus eurysiphon

Haemanthus eurysiphon, commonly known as the red-flowered haemanthus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a bulbous plant characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Haemanthus eurysiphon is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
