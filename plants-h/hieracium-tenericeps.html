Hieracium tenericeps

Hieracium tenericeps, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has slender, hairy stems and produces yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

The leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
