Hyptis microsphaera

Hyptis microsphaera, commonly known as the small globe hyptis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas, particularly Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and small, aromatic leaves. It produces clusters of tubular flowers that are typically white or purple in color.
