Horsfieldia oligocarpa

Horsfieldia oligocarpa, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia, where it thrives in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color, often with a distinctive lip-shaped petal. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor, and it is often found growing on trees or in dense undergrowth.
