Helleborine atropurpurea f. borbasii

Helleborine atropurpurea f. borbasii, commonly known as the purple-flowered helleborine, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its striking violet to purple petals.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in woodland areas. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.
