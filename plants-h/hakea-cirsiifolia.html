Hakea cirsiifolia

Hakea cirsiifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces cylindrical, woody fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and inland regions.

Hakea cirsiifolia is often used in native landscaping due to its hardy nature and unique appearance.
