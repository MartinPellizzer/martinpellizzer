Hieracium incanum subsp. sosnowskyi

Hieracium incanum subsp.

sosnowskyi, commonly known as the Sosnowsky's weed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy, glandular leaves.

Native to Central Asia, this subspecies thrives in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky areas. Its small, bristly stems and lobed leaves are adaptations to its arid environment.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
