Habenaria tenuispica

Habenaria tenuispica, commonly known as the slender-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, upright stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream sides. Its flowers are usually arranged in a spike and bloom during the summer months.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with specific mycorrhizal fungi.
