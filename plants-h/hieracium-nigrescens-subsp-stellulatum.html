Hieracium nigrescens subsp. stellulatum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

stellulatum, commonly known as the starry hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in meadows and grasslands, often forming dense colonies.
