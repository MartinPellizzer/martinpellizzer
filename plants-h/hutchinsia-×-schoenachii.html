Hutchinsia × schoenachii

Hutchinsia × schoenachii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Hutchinsia monachii and Schoenorchis × schweinfurthii.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the island of Socotra, characterized by its distinctive, narrow leaves and striking, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, thriving in arid conditions. Its floral structure is complex, with showy petals and a unique column that facilitates pollination by specific insect species.

Due to its rarity and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
