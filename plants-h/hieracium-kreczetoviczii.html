Hieracium kreczetoviczii

Hieracium kreczetoviczii, commonly known as the Kreczetowicz's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains in Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads made up of numerous small florets.
