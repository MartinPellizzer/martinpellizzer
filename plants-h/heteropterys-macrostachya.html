Heteropterys macrostachya

Heteropterys macrostachya, commonly known as the large-stemmed pittosporum, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature, containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
