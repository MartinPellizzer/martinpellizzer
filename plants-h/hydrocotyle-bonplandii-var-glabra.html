Hydrocotyle bonplandii var. glabra

Hydrocotyle bonplandii var. glabra, commonly known as smooth hydrocotyle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features a rosette of smooth, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces small, white, umbellate flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or wetlands.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
