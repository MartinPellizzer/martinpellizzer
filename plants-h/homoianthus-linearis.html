Homoianthus linearis

Homoianthus linearis, commonly known as the slender feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, linear leaves that are typically less than 1 cm in width. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and ability to form dense, low-growing mats.
