Hieracium roseum

Hieracium roseum, commonly known as the rose hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa. The plant features basal and alternate leaves that are typically lance-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster, with ray florets that are often pink or red.

Hieracium roseum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
