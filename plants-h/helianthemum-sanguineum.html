Helianthemum sanguineum

Helianthemum sanguineum, commonly known as the crimson rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are small, oval, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
