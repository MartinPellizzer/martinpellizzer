Hippomane dioica

Hippomane dioica, commonly known as the balsa tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lightweight, porous wood, which is among the lightest known woods in the world. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds.

The balsa tree thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
