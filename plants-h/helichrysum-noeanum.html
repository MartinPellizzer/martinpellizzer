Helichrysum noeanum

Helichrysum noeanum, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum noeanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
