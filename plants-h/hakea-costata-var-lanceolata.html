Hakea costata var. lanceolata

Hakea costata var. lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces cylindrical, woody cones that contain numerous small, edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and sandy environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
