Hedysarum retusum

Hedysarum retusum, commonly known as the smooth vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods that contain several elliptical seeds. Native to regions of Europe and western Asia, it thrives in dry, sunny environments.

Hedysarum retusum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
