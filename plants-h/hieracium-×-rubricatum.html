Hieracium × rubricatum

Hieracium × rubricatum, commonly known as the red hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright red to orange-yellow flowers, which are typically ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of disc florets. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. Its leaves are usually hairy and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Hieracium × rubricatum is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas, where it can spread rapidly due to its prolific seed production.
