Hieracium confragosum

Hieracium confragosum, commonly known as the ragged robin, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and deeply lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in moist meadows, wetlands, and along riverbanks. It produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that bloom from June to August.

Hieracium confragosum is often found in disturbed habitats and can spread rapidly through vegetative reproduction.
