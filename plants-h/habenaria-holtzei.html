Habenaria holtzei

Habenaria holtzei, commonly known as the Holtze's orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple or brown markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
