Hypoxis erecta var. leptocarpa

Hypoxis erecta var. leptocarpa, commonly known as the slender-stemmed star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
