Hibiscus azbenicus

Hibiscus azbenicus, commonly known as the Azben hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Hibiscus azbenicus prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
