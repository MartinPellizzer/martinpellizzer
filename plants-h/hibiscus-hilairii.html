Hibiscus hilairii

Hibiscus hilairii, commonly known as the Madagascar hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. Hibiscus hilairii thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
