Hyphaene macrosperma

Hyphaene macrosperma, commonly known as the African palm or the date palm of the genus Hyphaene, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into hard, woody fruits containing edible seeds. Hyphaene macrosperma is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
