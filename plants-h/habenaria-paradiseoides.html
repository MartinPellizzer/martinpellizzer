Habenaria paradiseoides

Habenaria paradiseoides, commonly known as the paradise orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and produces multiple erect, linear leaves. The plant bears showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a greenish throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its delicate appearance.
