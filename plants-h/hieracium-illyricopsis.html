Hieracium illyricopsis

Hieracium illyricopsis, commonly known as the Illyrian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, reaching heights of up to 50 centimeters. Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer, and the leaves are usually lance-shaped with hairy surfaces.

It is considered a relatively rare species, often found in specific microhabitats within its native range.
