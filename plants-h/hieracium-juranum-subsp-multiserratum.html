Hieracium juranum subsp. multiserratum

Hieracium juranum subsp.

multiserratum, commonly known as the mountain hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-red composite flowers.

The plant has serrated leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. It is known for its robust growth and ability to colonize disturbed or open areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of alpine flora and a subject of ecological study.
