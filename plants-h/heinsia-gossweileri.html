Heinsia gossweileri

Heinsia gossweileri, commonly known as the desert honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, adapting well to harsh, dry environments. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, allowing it to conserve water efficiently.

Heinsia gossweileri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-water conditions.
