Hieracium adriaticum subsp. aitonense

Hieracium adriaticum subsp.

aitonense, commonly known as the Adriatic hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. It has hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a single head.

This subspecies is often found in the Adriatic region, including parts of Italy and Croatia.
