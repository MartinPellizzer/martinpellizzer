Hieracium ostii-bucurae

Hieracium ostii-bucurae, commonly known as the Ostii-Bucurae hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers that attract pollinators. Native to specific regions in Europe, this plant thrives in moist, calcareous soils. It is often found in meadows and along riverbanks, where it contributes to local biodiversity.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species.
