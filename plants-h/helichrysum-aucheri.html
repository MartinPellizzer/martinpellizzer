Helichrysum aucheri

Helichrysum aucheri, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that resemble daisies, giving it a cheerful appearance. The plant has finely divided, silver-gray foliage that adds visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its drought tolerance and long-lasting blooms make it a valuable addition to low-maintenance landscapes.
