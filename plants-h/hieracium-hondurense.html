Hieracium hondurense

Hieracium hondurense, commonly known as the Honduran hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America, particularly Honduras.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. It features basal leaves that are often heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
