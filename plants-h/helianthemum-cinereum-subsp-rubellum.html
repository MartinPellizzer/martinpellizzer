Helianthemum cinereum subsp. rubellum

Helianthemum cinereum subsp.

rubellum, commonly known as the red sandwort, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe. It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety.

The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
