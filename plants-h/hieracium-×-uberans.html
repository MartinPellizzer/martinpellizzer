Hieracium × uberans

Hieracium × uberans, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, including North America. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered a weed in some areas.
