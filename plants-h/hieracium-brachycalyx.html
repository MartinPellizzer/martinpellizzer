Hieracium brachycalyx

Hieracium brachycalyx, commonly known as the short-horned hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosettes of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium brachycalyx is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates.
