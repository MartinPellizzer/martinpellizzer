Hieracium robustum subsp. pallonianum

Hieracium robustum subsp.

pallonianum, commonly known as the Pallonian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm.

The plant features basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to elliptic in shape, with hairy surfaces. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and are borne on long, slender peduncles.

It is characterized by its robust growth and is often found in sunny, open habitats.
