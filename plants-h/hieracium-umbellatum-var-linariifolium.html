Hieracium umbellatum var. linariifolium

Hieracium umbellatum var. linariifolium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is sometimes considered invasive in certain regions.
