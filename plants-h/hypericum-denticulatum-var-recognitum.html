Hypericum denticulatum var. recognitum

Hypericum denticulatum var. recognitum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are narrow and serrated, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and woodland edges.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
