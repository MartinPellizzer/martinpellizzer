Helictotrichon alpinum var. ausserdorferi

Helictotrichon alpinum var. ausserdorferi, commonly known as the Alpine hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to high-altitude regions of Europe.

It typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant features fine, hair-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to harsh climates and has a low-growing habit to withstand strong winds and temperature fluctuations.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
