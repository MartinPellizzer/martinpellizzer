Hieracium jebronense

Hieracium jebronense, commonly known as the Jebron's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers and is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. The plant has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to parts of Europe and has been introduced to other regions, sometimes becoming invasive.

Hieracium jebronense is often found growing in clusters, with multiple flowering stems arising from a single root system.
