Huodendron

Huodendron, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to various regions of Asia, including China, Korea, and Japan. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, lantern-shaped berries that change color as they mature, from green to orange to red. These berries are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Huodendron species are also cultivated for their ornamental value due to their striking foliage and colorful fruits.
