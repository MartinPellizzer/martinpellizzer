Hypericum acutum var. undulatum

Hypericum acutum var. undulatum, commonly known as the wavy-stemmed St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly wavy margin, and its flowers are bright yellow with prominent orange stamens. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens.
