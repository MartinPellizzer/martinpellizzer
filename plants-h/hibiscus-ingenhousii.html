Hibiscus ingenhousii

Hibiscus ingenhousii, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Hibiscus ingenhousii thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
