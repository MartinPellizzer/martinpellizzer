Habenaria militaris

Habenaria militaris, commonly known as the soldier orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a distinctive yellowish or reddish hue.

Habenaria militaris is known for its unique morphology and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
