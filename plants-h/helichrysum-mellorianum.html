Helichrysum mellorianum

Helichrysum mellorianum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers and silver-green foliage. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters and are often used in dried floral arrangements due to their long-lasting appearance.

Helichrysum mellorianum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
