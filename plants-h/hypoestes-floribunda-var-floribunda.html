Hypoestes floribunda var. floribunda

Hypoestes floribunda var. floribunda, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are typically velvety and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This plant is known for its ability to bloom year-round under proper care.
