Hibiscus × hongjinma

Hibiscus × hongjinma, commonly known as the Hongjinma hibiscus, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from various hibiscus species.

It is native to China and is prized for its large, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, with dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sunlight to flourish.

Hibiscus × hongjinma is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
