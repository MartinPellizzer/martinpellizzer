Hibiscus rhodopetalus var. chillagoensis

Hibiscus rhodopetalus var. chillagoensis, commonly known as the Chillago hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas.

Hibiscus rhodopetalus var. chillagoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
