Hypolytrum latifolium var. depauperatum

Hypolytrum latifolium var. depauperatum, commonly known as the slender flat sedge, is a grass-like plant native to parts of Australia.

It typically grows in wet or damp environments, such as swampy areas or along the edges of water bodies. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often green or brown in color. It is characterized by its reduced foliage and compact growth habit.

This variant is part of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants.
