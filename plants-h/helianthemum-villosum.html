Helianthemum villosum

Helianthemum villosum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.
