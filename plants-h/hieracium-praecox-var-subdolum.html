Hieracium praecox var. subdolum

Hieracium praecox var. subdolum, commonly known as the early hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed sites such as meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It has finely toothed leaves and produces numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.

This variant is often found in temperate regions of Europe and is noted for its adaptability to various soil types.
