Hypolytrum fuscum

Hypolytrum fuscum, commonly known as the blackleaf lily, is a herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
