Hieracium × seckauense

Hieracium × seckauense, commonly known as the Seckauer hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flower heads and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and meadows. It is native to Central Europe and is often found in regions with temperate climates.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
