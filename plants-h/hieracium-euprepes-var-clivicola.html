Hieracium euprepes var. clivicola

Hieracium euprepes var. clivicola, commonly known as the cliff dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive leaf morphology. The plant typically grows on rocky or coastal soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

This variant is noted for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in well-drained, sunny conditions.
