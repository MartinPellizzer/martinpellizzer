Haworthia major

Haworthia major, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Haworthia major is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
