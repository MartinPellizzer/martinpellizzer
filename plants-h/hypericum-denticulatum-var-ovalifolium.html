Hypericum denticulatum var. ovalifolium

Hypericum denticulatum var. ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and western Asia.

It features narrow, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated margin, and its flowers are typically bright yellow with prominent orange stamens. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands or rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
