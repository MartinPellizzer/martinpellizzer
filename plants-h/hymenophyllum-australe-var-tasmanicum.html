Hymenophyllum australe var. tasmanicum

Hymenophyllum australe var. tasmanicum, commonly known as the Tasmanian filmy fern, is a small, delicate fern native to Tasmania.

It is characterized by its thin, translucent fronds that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in rock crevices or on tree trunks. Its spores are released from small, club-like structures called sporangia located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is part of the Hymenophyllaceae family, which includes other species of filmy ferns known for their unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
