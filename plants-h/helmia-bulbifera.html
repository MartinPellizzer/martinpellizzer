Helmia bulbifera

Helmia bulbifera, commonly known as the bulbous helmet, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive helmet-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, rounded bulbs beneath its leaves, which can be used for propagation. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland habitats.
