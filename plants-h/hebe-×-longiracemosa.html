Hebe × longiracemosa

Hebe × longiracemosa, commonly known as the long raceme hebe, is a hybrid plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Hebe × longiracemosa is also valued for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
