Hugelroea linophylla

Hugelroea linophylla, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are soft and covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or near water sources.
