Habenaria troyana

Habenaria troyana, commonly known as the Troy orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the lip. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, often in full sun. It has a short, underground rhizome and produces small, lance-shaped leaves.

Habenaria troyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
