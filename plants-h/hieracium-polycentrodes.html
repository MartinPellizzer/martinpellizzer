Hieracium polycentrodes

Hieracium polycentrodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, with ray florets that are often longer than the disk florets. It has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges.

Hieracium polycentrodes is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in agricultural and urban areas.
