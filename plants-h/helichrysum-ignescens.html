Helichrysum ignescens

Helichrysum ignescens, commonly known as the firewheel bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that resemble small wheels, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic, hairy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Helichrysum ignescens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
