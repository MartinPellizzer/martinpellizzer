Hieracium asplenioides

Hieracium asplenioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Hieracium asplenioides is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
