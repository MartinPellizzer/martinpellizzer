Hypericum stenopetalum

Hypericum stenopetalum, commonly known as narrow-petaled St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers with five narrow petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is part of the genus Hypericum, which includes many species known for their medicinal properties.

The flowers bloom in spring and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.
