Habenaria dives

Habenaria dives, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
