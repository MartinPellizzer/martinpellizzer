Hieracium atrocranum

Hieracium atrocranum, commonly known as black-capped hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium atrocranum is often found in moist environments and can form dense colonies, sometimes becoming invasive in certain regions.
