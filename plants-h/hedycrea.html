Hedycrea

Hedycrea, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with prominent stamens that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
