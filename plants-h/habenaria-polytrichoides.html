Habenaria polytrichoides

Habenaria polytrichoides, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped flower spike. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a fragrant scent that attracts pollinators.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
