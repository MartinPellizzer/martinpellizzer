Hypericum chapmanii

Hypericum chapmanii, commonly known as Chapman's St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features woody stems and small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum chapmanii is often found in rocky soils and open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
