Huernia humilis

Huernia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, fleshy tubercles that resemble small, rounded bumps. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically small and rounded, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Huernia humilis is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
