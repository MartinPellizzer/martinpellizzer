Hypericum × desetangsii nothof. maculatiforme

Hypericum × desetangsii nothof.

maculatiforme, commonly known as the spotted yellow hypericum, is a hybrid shrub native to southern China. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with distinctive red or orange spots, which appear in clusters during the summer months.

The plant has elliptical, opposite leaves that are dark green and slightly glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
