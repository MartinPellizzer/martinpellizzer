Hieracium leptomeres

Hieracium leptomeres, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in composite inflorescences. The plant has basal and alternate leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Due to its invasive potential in some regions, it is considered a weed in agricultural and natural habitats.
