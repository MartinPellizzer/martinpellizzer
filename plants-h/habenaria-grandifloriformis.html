Habenaria grandifloriformis

Habenaria grandifloriformis, commonly known as the large-flowered orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as pink, purple, or white. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure, including a specialized lip that aids in pollination. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
