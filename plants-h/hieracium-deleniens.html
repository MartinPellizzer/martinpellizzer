Hieracium deleniens

Hieracium deleniens, commonly known as the Deleniens hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has alternate, hairy leaves that are often lobed or toothed. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and grasslands.

It is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
