Hieracium salayense

Hieracium salayense, commonly known as Salayens hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium salayense is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
