Homalium involucratum var. lucidum

Homalium involucratum var. lucidum, commonly known as the glossy homalium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that give it a glossy appearance. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
