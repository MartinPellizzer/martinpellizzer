Habenaria macrostele

Habenaria macrostele, commonly known as the large-stemmed marsh orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas. The plant features slender, erect stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base of the stem.

The flowers are pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is usually darker in color.
