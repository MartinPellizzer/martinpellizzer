Hieracium flammula

Hieracium flammula, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features yellow flowers that are clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence, often with a reddish or orange tinge. Its leaves are usually basal, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Native to Europe, it is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.
