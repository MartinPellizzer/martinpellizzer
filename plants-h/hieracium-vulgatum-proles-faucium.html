Hieracium vulgatum proles faucium, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Hieracium vulgatum proles faucium is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
