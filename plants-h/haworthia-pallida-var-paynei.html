Haworthia pallida var. paynei

Haworthia pallida var. paynei, commonly known as the Payne's Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, pale green leaves with distinct white to pale blue markings. The leaves are typically rounded and have a smooth texture, with a tendency to curl slightly at the tips. It produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers on short stems during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
