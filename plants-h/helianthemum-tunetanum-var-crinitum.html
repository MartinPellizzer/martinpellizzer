Helianthemum tunetanum var. crinitum

Helianthemum tunetanum var. crinitum, commonly known as the hairy rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, typically from April to June. The plant has dense, hairy foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardening.
