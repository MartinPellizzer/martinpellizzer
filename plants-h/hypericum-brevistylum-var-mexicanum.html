Hypericum brevistylum var. mexicanum

Hypericum brevistylum var. mexicanum, commonly known as Mexican St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant is often found in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in well-drained environments. It is part of the Hypericaceae family, known for its medicinal properties in various traditional uses.

The varietal form, mexicanum, distinguishes it from other species within the Hypericum genus.
