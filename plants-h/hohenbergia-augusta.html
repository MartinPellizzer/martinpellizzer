Hohenbergia augusta

Hohenbergia augusta, commonly known as the silver rosette, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves that form a central cup to collect water. The plant produces striking red and yellow inflorescences that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Hohenbergia augusta is relatively low maintenance and adds a unique, elegant touch to any collection.
