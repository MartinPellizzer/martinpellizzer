Hypolytrum paucistrobiliferum

Hypolytrum paucistrobiliferum, commonly known as the slender spike rush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance.

Its inflorescence consists of multiple tiny flowers arranged in dense, spike-like structures.

Hypolytrum paucistrobiliferum plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetland habitats and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
