Hieracium murorum subsp. infrasericatum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

infrasericatum, commonly known as the lesser spearthistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are usually gray-green in color with a woolly texture. Its flowering heads are yellow and borne on slender stems, producing small, composite flowers.

This subspecies is distinguished by its smaller size and more compact growth compared to other members of the Hieracium genus.
