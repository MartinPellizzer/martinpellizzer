Hahnia aria f. aurea

Hahnia aria f. aurea, commonly known as the golden hahnia, is a unique variant of the hahnia plant characterized by its striking golden foliage.

This succulent belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. The plant features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It is native to South Africa, where it thrives in arid and rocky environments.

Due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
