Hahnia pinnatifida

Hahnia pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hahnia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features linear, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Hahnia pinnatifida is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
