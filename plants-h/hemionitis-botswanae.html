Hemionitis botswanae

Hemionitis botswanae, commonly known as the Botswana rock fern, is a unique and rare plant species native to the arid regions of Botswana.

It belongs to the family Hemionitidaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment. It produces spores rather than seeds, which is a typical feature of ferns.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Hemionitis botswanae is considered a vulnerable species.
