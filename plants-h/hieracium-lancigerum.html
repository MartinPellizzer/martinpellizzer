Hieracium lancigerum

Hieracium lancigerum, commonly known as the spearhead hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough, hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive central disc.

Hieracium lancigerum is also known for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
