Hyptis celebica

Hyptis celebica, commonly known as Java hyptis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of pale blue or violet flowers that bloom throughout the growing season.

Hyptis celebica is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
