Hieracium auriculinum subsp. subteneriusculum

Hieracium auriculinum subsp.

subteneriusculum, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe. It typically features slender, hairy stems that are often prostrate or ascending.

The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a compact cluster at the tip of the stem. Its leaves are narrow, often with a slightly toothed margin, and are covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
