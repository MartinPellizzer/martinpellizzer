Harperia eyreana

Harperia eyreana, commonly known as the Eyre's harperia, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around the stems. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its rugged appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Harperia eyreana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and hardy nature.
