Hydrochorea obliquifoliolata

Hydrochorea obliquifoliolata, commonly known as the zigzag water plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Hydrocharitaceae and is characterized by its floating leaves and flowering stems. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread across water surfaces. Its leaves are narrow and often have a zigzag or undulating edge, giving it its common name.
