Hieracium arvicola subsp. pseudocollinum

Hieracium arvicola subsp.

pseudocollinum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

The plant has a rosette of basal leaves that are hairy and lanceolate in shape. Its flowering stems bear clusters of yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense ring of disk florets.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or grassy slopes at high elevations.
