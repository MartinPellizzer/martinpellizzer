Haworthia reinwardtii var. adelaidensis

Haworthia reinwardtii var. adelaidensis, commonly known as the Adelaidensis Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings, often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for dry, rocky environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
