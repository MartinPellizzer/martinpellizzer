Hieracium cosmiodontum

Hieracium cosmiodontum, commonly known as the broad-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range from yellow to orange in color.

Hieracium cosmiodontum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it both a resilient and sometimes invasive species.
