Hemionitis cayennensis

Hemionitis cayennensis, commonly known as the Cayenne hemionitis, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Hemionitidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hemionitis cayennensis is primarily found in the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.
