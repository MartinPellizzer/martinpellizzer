Helichrysum arenarium subsp. aucheri

Helichrysum arenarium subsp.

aucheri, commonly known as the golden carpet, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to southern Africa. It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer.

The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
