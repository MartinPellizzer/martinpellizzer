Hopea ponga var. ponga

Hopea ponga var. ponga, commonly known as the Pongam tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapindaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant has traditional uses in medicine and also serves as a source of fuel and timber.
