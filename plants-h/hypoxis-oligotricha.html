Hypoxis oligotricha

Hypoxis oligotricha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its star-shaped white flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
