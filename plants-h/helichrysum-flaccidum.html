Helichrysum flaccidum

Helichrysum flaccidum, commonly known as the soft yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves and small, golden-yellow flowers that remain open for a long period, giving it the common name. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas. It is also known for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional medicine.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature.
