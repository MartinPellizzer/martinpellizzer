Hypochaeris schizoglossa

Hypochaeris schizoglossa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that consist of both ray and disk florets. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

Hypochaeris schizoglossa is typically perennial and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
