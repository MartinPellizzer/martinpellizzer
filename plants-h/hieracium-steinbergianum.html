Hieracium steinbergianum

Hieracium steinbergianum, commonly known as the Steinberg's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in early summer, attracting various pollinators.

It is known for its robust growth and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
