Hieracium subbathycephalum

Hieracium subbathycephalum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are generally hairy and lance-shaped, with a rough texture. Its flowering heads are yellow and consist of numerous small florets arranged in a composite flower head.

Hieracium subbathycephalum is often found in mountainous areas and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
