Horsfieldia moluccana var. moluccana

Horsfieldia moluccana var. moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has a pseudobulb structure and produces long, slender leaves that help it thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and come in shades of white, yellow, and pink, making it a popular ornamental species.

It requires high humidity and indirect light to flourish, making it a challenging but rewarding plant for enthusiasts.
