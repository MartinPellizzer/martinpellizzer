Hieracium turcicum

Hieracium turcicum, commonly known as Turkish hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Native to southern Europe and western Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has been introduced to various regions, including North America, where it can become invasive.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and a potential weed in agricultural settings.
