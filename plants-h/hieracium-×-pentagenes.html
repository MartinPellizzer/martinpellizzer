Hieracium × pentagenes

Hieracium × pentagenes, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 20–60 cm, with upright stems and basal leaves that are often lobed or deeply cut. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster, producing a showy appearance.

Hieracium × pentagenes is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, and it can spread aggressively through rhizomes and seeds.
