Helichrysum italicum subsp. ericoideum

Helichrysum italicum subsp.

ericoideum, commonly known as ericoid helichrysum, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe. It features narrow, needle-like leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flower heads that resemble daisies.

The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its aromatic foliage and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice in horticulture.

It is also valued in aromatherapy for its essential oils, which are used in perfumes and skincare products.
