Hasseltia monagensis

Hasseltia monagensis, commonly known as the Natal daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, seed-like fruits.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is primarily found in its natural habitat.
