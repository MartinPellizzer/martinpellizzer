Hohenbergia ridleyi

Hohenbergia ridleyi, commonly known as the "Ridley's hohenbergia," is a species of bromeliad native to Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences that are typically red or orange, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hohenbergia ridleyi is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
