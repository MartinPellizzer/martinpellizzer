Habenaria erichmichelii

Habenaria erichmichelii, commonly known as the Erichmiel's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces delicate, greenish-white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its specialized pollination mechanisms.
