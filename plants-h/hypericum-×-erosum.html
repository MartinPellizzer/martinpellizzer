Hypericum × erosum

Hypericum × erosum, commonly known as common St. John's wort, is a flowering plant in the family Hypericaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features opposite, simple leaves with serrated edges and small, red or orange seed capsules that mature in the fall. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Hypericum × erosum is often found in sunny, well-drained soils in woodland edges, meadows, and disturbed areas.
