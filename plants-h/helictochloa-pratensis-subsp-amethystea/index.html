Helictochloa Pratensis Subsp. Amethystea

Amethyst grass, scientifically known as Helictochloa pratensis subsp. amethystea, is a striking ornamental plant prized for its vivid purple hues. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its delicate, grass-like foliage forms dense clumps that create a soft, shimmering effect in gardens. The plant is particularly popular in rock gardens and alpine settings due to its compact size and drought tolerance. With its unique coloration and ease of care, amethyst grass has become a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts.
