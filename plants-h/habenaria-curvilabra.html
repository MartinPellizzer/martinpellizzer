Habenaria curvilabra

Habenaria curvilabra, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive zigzag pattern on the petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
