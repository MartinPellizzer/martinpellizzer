Hosta minor

Hosta minor, commonly known as the dwarf hosta, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that range in color from light green to deep blue-green, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers on slender stems in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained, moist soil.

Hosta minor is prized for its ornamental foliage and is commonly used in shaded garden settings.
