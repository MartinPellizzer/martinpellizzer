Habenaria linearifolia subsp. schindleri

Habenaria linearifolia subsp.

schindleri, commonly known as the Schindler's narrow-leaf orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense colonies.

Botanically, it belongs to the orchid family Orchidaceae and is distinguished by its unique floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
