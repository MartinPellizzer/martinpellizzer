Habenaria rolfeana

Habenaria rolfeana, commonly known as the Rolf's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-drained soil.

Habenaria rolfeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
