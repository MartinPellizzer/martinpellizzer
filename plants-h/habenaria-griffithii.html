Habenaria griffithii

Habenaria griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually grass-like, and the flowers are clustered in a spike, often with a distinctive coloration.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
