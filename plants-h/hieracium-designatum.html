Hieracium designatum

Hieracium designatum, commonly known as the designated hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-pink flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium designatum is often found in open habitats and can be a common weed in agricultural areas.
