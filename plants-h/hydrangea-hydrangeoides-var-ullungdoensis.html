Hydrangea hydrangeoides var. ullungdoensis

Hydrangea hydrangeoides var. ullungdoensis, commonly known as the Korean hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to Korea.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink or blue depending on soil pH. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically medium to dark green. It blooms in late summer, producing fluffy, cone-like flowers that attract pollinators.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
