Helenium bolanderi

Helenium bolanderi, commonly known as Bolander's sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and large, showy yellow to orange-red ray florets. The plant produces a single, elongated flower head at the top of the stem, surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Helenium bolanderi is a perennial species that blooms in late summer to early fall.
