Helleboraster viridis

Helleboraster viridis, commonly known as the green hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or pale yellow markings. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
