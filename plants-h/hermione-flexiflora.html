Hermione flexiflora

Hermione flexiflora, commonly known as the wispbloom, is a rare flowering plant native to the misty highlands of the eastern continent.

It features delicate, translucent leaves that shimmer with a faint bioluminescent glow at night. The plant's flowers are small and clustered, producing a sweet, ethereal fragrance that attracts nocturnal pollinators. Its roots are known to absorb moisture from the air, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its unique properties, Hermione flexiflora is highly sought after in both botanical and magical research.
