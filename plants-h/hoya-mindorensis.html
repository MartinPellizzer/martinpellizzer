Hoya mindorensis

Hoya mindorensis, commonly known as the Mindanao Wax Plant, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of star-shaped, waxy flowers. It is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
