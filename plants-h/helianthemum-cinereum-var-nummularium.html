Helianthemum cinereum var. nummularium

Helianthemum cinereum var. nummularium, commonly known as silver saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are succulent and adapted to arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact form and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
