Hydrangea belzonii

Hydrangea belzonii, commonly known as the African hydrangea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Hydrangeaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, with broad, dark green leaves. Its flowers, which can be white, blue, or pink depending on soil pH, bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Hydrangea belzonii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
