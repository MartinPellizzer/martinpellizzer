Hieracium hypomaerum

Hieracium hypomaerum, commonly known as the small-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring moist to moderately dry conditions. Its leaves are alternate, often hairy, and have a distinctive heart-shaped base.

The flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, producing small seeds that are dispersed by wind.
