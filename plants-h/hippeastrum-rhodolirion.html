Hippeastrum rhodolirion

Hippeastrum rhodolirion, commonly known as the red-flowered amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically red or pink, with a distinctive white throat. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often evergreen in warmer climates. Its bulbs are underground storage organs that support the plant through periods of dormancy.

Hippeastrum rhodolirion is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
