Helxine cilinode

Helxine cilinode, commonly known as the spiral vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its unique helical growth pattern, where the stems twist around supports in a spiral manner. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy, dark green surface. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to create intricate, natural patterns in gardens.
