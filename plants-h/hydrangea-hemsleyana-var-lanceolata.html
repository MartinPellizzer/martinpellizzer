Hydrangea hemsleyana var. lanceolata

Hydrangea hemsleyana var. lanceolata, commonly known as the Hemsley's hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink or blue, depending on soil pH. The plant has serrated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to change flower color, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.
