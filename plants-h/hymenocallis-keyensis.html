Hymenocallis keyensis

Hymenocallis keyensis, commonly known as the Key Island spider lily, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its striking, white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces a single, erect stem with narrow, linear leaves. It is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

Hymenocallis keyensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
