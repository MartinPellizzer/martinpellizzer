Hermione × bifrons var. biscrenata

Hermione × bifrons var. biscrenata, commonly known as the silverleaf bamboo, is a hybrid plant that exhibits unique botanical features.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which results from a dense covering of fine, reflective hairs on the leaves. The plant has a clumping growth habit, forming compact clusters of stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, with a slightly waxy texture that aids in reducing water loss.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
