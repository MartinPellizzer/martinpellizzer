Hieracium scabripes

Hieracium scabripes, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often in temperate climates. Its leaves are rough and hairy, and the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered invasive in certain regions.
