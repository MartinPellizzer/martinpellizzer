Homalocenchrus angustifolius

Homalocenchrus angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sand mat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Homalocenchrus angustifolius plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and is often found in disturbed or degraded habitats.
