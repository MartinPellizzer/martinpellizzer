Hypoestes talbotiae

Hypoestes talbotiae, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.
