Hieracium plumbagineum

Hieracium plumbagineum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium plumbagineum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, where it spreads through its rhizomes and seeds.
