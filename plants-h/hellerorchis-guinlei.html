Hellerorchis guinlei

Hellerorchis guinlei, commonly known as the Guinleean orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in a basal rosette. Hellerorchis guinlei thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique floral structure makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
