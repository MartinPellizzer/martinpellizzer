Habenaria wacketii

Habenaria wacketii, commonly known as the Wackett's butterfly orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, butterfly-like flowers with a distinctive coloration. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with intricate patterns.

Habenaria wacketii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialty gardens.
