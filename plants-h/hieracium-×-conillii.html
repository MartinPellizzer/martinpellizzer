Hieracium × conillii

Hieracium × conillii, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils and cool climates. Hieracium × conillii is native to the mountainous areas of Europe and is often found in rocky or grassy habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
