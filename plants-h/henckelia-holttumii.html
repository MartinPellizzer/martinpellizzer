Henckelia holttumii

Henckelia holttumii, commonly known as the Taitung orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by insects.

Henckelia holttumii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
