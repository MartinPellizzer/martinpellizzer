Hieracium gombense subsp. gombense

Hieracium gombense subsp.

gombense, commonly known as the Gombes hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It features basal leaves that are usually lanceolate or ovate with hairy surfaces.

The flowers are yellow, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
