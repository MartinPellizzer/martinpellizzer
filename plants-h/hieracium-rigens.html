Hieracium rigens

Hieracium rigens, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often hairy.

Hieracium rigens is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
