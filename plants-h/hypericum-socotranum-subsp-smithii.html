Hypericum socotranum subsp. smithii

Hypericum socotranum subsp.

smithii, commonly known as Socotra golden strobilus, is a unique plant endemic to the Socotra archipelago. It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes species known for their yellow flowers and medicinal properties.

This subspecies is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flowers and woody stems. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky substrates.

The plant's botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and a subject of scientific interest.
