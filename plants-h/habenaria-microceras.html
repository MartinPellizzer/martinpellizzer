Habenaria microceras

Habenaria microceras, commonly known as the small-horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

The species is noted for its small, fragrant blooms that appear in late spring or early summer.
