Hieracium Sparsum Subsp. Porphyriticum

Hieracium sparsum subsp. porphyriticum is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its distinctive purple-tinged leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. This subspecies thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique member of the dandelion family.
