Hibiscus commixtus

Hibiscus commixtus, commonly known as the wild hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. Hibiscus commixtus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and full sunlight.

It is often found in wetlands, along riverbanks, and in coastal areas.
