Hieracium trichobrachium

Hieracium trichobrachium, commonly known as the hairy bracted hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. The plant features basal and cauline leaves that are hairy and lance-shaped, with a rough texture. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, consisting of numerous small disc flowers surrounded by ray florets.

The species is characterized by its distinctive hairy bracts and is part of the Asteraceae family.
