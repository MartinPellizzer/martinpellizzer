Helichrysum scleranthoides

Helichrysum scleranthoides, commonly known as the silver leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its aromatic properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ability to retain its color when dried makes it a valued plant in floral arrangements.
