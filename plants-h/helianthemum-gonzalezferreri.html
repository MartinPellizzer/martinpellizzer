Helianthemum gonzalezferreri

Helianthemum gonzalezferreri, commonly known as the Gonzalez Ferreri rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, fleshy leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Helianthemum gonzalezferreri is adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is relatively drought-tolerant.
