Huperzia hellwigii

Huperzia hellwigii, commonly known as the Hellwig's spikemoss, is a species of fern-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems covered with small, scale-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Huperzia hellwigii is a cryptogamic plant that reproduces via spores rather than seeds.

Its structure and growth habits make it an important component of certain ecosystems, contributing to soil stability and biodiversity.
