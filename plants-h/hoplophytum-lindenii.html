Hoplophytum lindenii

Hoplophytum lindenii, commonly known as the red-stemmed euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that can store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers, typically green or yellow, surrounded by colorful bracts. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
