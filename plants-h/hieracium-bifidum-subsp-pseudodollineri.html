Hieracium bifidum subsp. pseudodollineri

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

pseudodollineri, commonly known as the false doll's-ears, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its distinctive, pale yellow to orange-yellow ray florets and a central disk of tubular flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has rough, hairy leaves that are often lobed or divided, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.
