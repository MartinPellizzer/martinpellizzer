Homalomena batoeensis

Homalomena batoeensis, commonly known as the Batoe's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Homalomena batoeensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
