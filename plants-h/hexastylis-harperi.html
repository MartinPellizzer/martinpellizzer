Hexastylis harperi

Hexastylis harperi, commonly known as the Florida basket fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its clustered, evergreen foliage. The plant features a short, underground rhizome from which multiple fronds emerge. Its leaves are typically narrow and strap-like, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by a spathe, a common feature in the Araceae family.
