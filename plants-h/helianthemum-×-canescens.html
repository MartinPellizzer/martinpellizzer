Helianthemum × canescens

Helianthemum × canescens, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Helianthemum × canescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
