Haworthia triebneriana

Haworthia triebneriana, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or yellow stripes. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
