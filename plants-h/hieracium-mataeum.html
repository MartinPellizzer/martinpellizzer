Hieracium mataeum

Hieracium mataeum, commonly known as the Mataeum hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.
