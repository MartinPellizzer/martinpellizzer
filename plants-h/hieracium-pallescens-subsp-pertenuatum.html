Hieracium pallescens subsp. pertenuatum

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

pertenuatum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy structure.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and often have a fuzzy texture, while the flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a composite head.

It is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
