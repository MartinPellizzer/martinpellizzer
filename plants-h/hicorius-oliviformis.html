Hicorius oliviformis

Hicorius oliviformis, commonly known as the olive-shaped hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Hicorius oliviformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
