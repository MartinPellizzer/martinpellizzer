Hippomarathrum tineoi

Hippomarathrum tineoi, commonly known as the Tineo's thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny environments. The plant features spiny leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
