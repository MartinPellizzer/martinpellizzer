Hieracium atratum subsp. subacutissimum

Hieracium atratum subsp.

subacutissimum, commonly known as the dark hawkweed, is a member of the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and yellow flowers arranged in composite heads.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks. It has a creeping growth habit with rhizomes that allow it to spread vegetatively.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native habitats.
