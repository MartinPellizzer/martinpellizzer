Habenaria clareae

Habenaria clareae, commonly known as the pale green orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on the forest floor or along stream banks. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces clusters of pale green to white flowers. Its flowers are characterized by their narrow, spurred petals and are often fragrant.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
