Hyptis altissima

Hyptis altissima, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
