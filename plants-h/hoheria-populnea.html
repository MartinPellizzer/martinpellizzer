Hoheria populnea

Hoheria populnea, commonly known as the silver fern, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-green foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and forested areas. Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The leaves are evergreen and can be used in traditional Māori medicine.
