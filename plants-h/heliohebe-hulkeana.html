Heliohebe hulkeana

Heliohebe hulkeana, commonly known as the Hulke's heliohebe, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a compact, mat-like form. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Heliohebe hulkeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to form dense ground cover in gardens.
