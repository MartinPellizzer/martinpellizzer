Hermannia ignescens

Hermannia ignescens, commonly known as the fiery torch plant, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that resemble small torches. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Hermannia ignescens thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its vibrant floral display makes it a notable addition to both cultivated and natural landscapes.
