Hieracium apachyglossum

Hieracium apachyglossum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers, which are typically arranged in a single, showy head. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and thrives in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium apachyglossum is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through its rhizomes.
