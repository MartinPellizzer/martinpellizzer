Holboellia latifolia var. acuminata

Holboellia latifolia var. acuminata, commonly known as the broadleaf holboellia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features elongated, broad leaves with a smooth, waxy surface that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often blooming during the rainy season. Its root system is fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soils.

This species is valued in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.
