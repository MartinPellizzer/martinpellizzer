Helichrysum microcephalum

Helichrysum microcephalum, commonly known as the narrow-headed everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant produces small, bright yellow to orange flowers that are often arranged in compact clusters. Its foliage is typically narrow and linear, giving it a grass-like appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
