Haplostachys bryanii

Haplostachys bryanii, commonly known as Bryan's false dragonhead, is a member of the Lamiaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Haplostachys bryanii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
