Helichrysum abietifolium subsp. gracilifolium

Helichrysum abietifolium subsp.

gracilifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
