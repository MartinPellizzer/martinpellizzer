Huernia verekeri subsp. pauciflora

Huernia verekeri subsp.

pauciflora, commonly known as the Cape Town moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features rounded, ribbed stems that are typically green or pale yellow, with a thick, fleshy texture to store water.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
