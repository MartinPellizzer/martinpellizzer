Helichrysum citrispinum var. citrispinum

Helichrysum citrispinum var. citrispinum, commonly known as the lemon button bush, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that resemble tiny lemons, which bloom in late summer. The plant has aromatic, slightly hairy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display make it an attractive addition to gardens.
