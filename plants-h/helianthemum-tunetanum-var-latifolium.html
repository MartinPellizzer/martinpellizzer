Helianthemum tunetanum var. latifolium

Helianthemum tunetanum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and fleshy, aromatic leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and hardy addition to rock gardens and arid landscapes.
