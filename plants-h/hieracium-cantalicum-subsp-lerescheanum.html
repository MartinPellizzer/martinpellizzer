Hieracium cantalicum subsp. lerescheanum

Hieracium cantalicum subsp.

lerescheanum, commonly known as the Leresche's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, with ray florets that are often reflexed. Its leaves are basal and sessile, with a rough, hairy texture.

It is characterized by its holarctic distribution and is often found in regions with temperate climates.
