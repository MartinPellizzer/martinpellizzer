Haxtonia furfuracea

Haxtonia furfuracea, commonly known as the velvetleaf haxtonia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving them a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Haxtonia furfuracea is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
