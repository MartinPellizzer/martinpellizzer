Hazunta

Hazunta, commonly known as the wild thyme.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features small, fragrant purple flowers and needle-like leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. Hazunta thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its essential oils contain compounds with antimicrobial and antioxidant properties.
