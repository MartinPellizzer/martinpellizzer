Hieracium bifidum subsp. hastifrons

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

hastifrons, commonly known as the forked hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads.

The plant produces yellow disk flowers surrounded by ray florets, typical of the Compositae family. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
