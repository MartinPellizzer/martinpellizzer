Hakea glabella var. denticulata

Hakea glabella var. denticulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, glandular hairs. The plant produces cylindrical, woody cones that are typically red or purple in color. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, with a deep root system for water storage.

This variety is often used in native landscaping due to its hardy nature and ornamental appearance.
