Helianthemum virgatum var. marmaricum

Helianthemum virgatum var. marmaricum, commonly known as the marble rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and unique, marble-like leaf patterns.
