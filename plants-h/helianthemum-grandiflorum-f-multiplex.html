Helianthemum grandiflorum f. multiplex

Helianthemum grandiflorum f. multiplex, commonly known as the multiflorous rockrose, is a compact shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and hardy, drought-resistant nature.
