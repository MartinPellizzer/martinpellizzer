Heliotropium incanum

Heliotropium incanum, commonly known as the silverleaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Its flowers are also popular in horticulture for their ornamental value.
