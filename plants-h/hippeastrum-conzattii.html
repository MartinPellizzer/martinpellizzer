Hippeastrum conzattii

Hippeastrum conzattii, commonly known as the Conzatti's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant features strap-shaped leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted for storing water in its native arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum conzattii is a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
