Hieracium bulsanense

Hieracium bulsanense, commonly known as the Bulsan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Korea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. It has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
