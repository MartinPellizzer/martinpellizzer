Helenium polygonatum

Helenium polygonatum, commonly known as the yellow wood orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers with a distinctive orange and yellow coloration. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Helenium polygonatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
