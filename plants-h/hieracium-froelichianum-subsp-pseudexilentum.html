Hieracium froelichianum subsp. pseudexilentum

Hieracium froelichianum subsp.

pseudexilentum, commonly known as the false crocus hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges. It has a taproot system and produces numerous small, elongated achenes as its seeds.
