Hedera turbacensis

Hedera turbacensis, commonly known as the Turkish ivy, is a climbing evergreen vine native to Turkey and the eastern Mediterranean.

It features dark green, lobed leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Hedera turbacensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to climb walls or trees.
