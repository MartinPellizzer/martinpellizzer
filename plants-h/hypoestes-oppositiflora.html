Hypoestes oppositiflora

Hypoestes oppositiflora, commonly known as the "Snow-on-the-Mountain," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bi-colored leaves that often display a combination of white, pink, and green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoestes oppositiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
