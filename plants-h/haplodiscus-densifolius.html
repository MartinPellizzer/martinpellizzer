Haplodiscus densifolius

Haplodiscus densifolius, commonly known as the dense-leaved hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are dense and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.
