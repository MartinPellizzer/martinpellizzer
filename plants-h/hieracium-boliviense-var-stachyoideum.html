Hieracium boliviense var. stachyoideum

Hieracium boliviense var. stachyoideum, commonly known as the Bolivian hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to South America, particularly Bolivia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust stem structure. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces and produces yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native range.
