Habenaria biloba

Habenaria biloba, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swamps. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Habenaria biloba is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
