Hosta decorata f. marginata

Hosta decorata f. marginata, commonly known as the variegated hosta, is a striking cultivar of the Hosta genus native to East Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a striking combination of light green and white margins, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant thrives in partial to full shade, making it an excellent choice for woodland gardens or shaded borders. Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, with no significant flowers, focusing entirely on ornamental leaves.

This variety is relatively low maintenance, requiring well-drained soil and consistent moisture for optimal growth.
