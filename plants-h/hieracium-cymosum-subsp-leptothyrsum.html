Hieracium cymosum subsp. leptothyrsum

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

leptothyrsum, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets and white to pale yellow ray florets.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are lanceolate, hairy, and often have a reddish or purplish hue on the underside.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender, elongated leaves and a more compact inflorescence compared to other members of the species.
