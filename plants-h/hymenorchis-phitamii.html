Hymenorchis phitamii

Hymenorchis phitamii, commonly known as the Phitamii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem that bears several elliptical, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to cream-colored flowers with distinctive, spurred petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Hymenorchis phitamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
