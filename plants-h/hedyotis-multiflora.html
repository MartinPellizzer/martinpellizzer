Hedyotis multiflora

Hedyotis multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered blushing root, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. The plant has slender, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Hedyotis multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
