Hieracium spodiocladum

Hieracium spodiocladum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has hairy, reddish stems and small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a woolly appearance.
