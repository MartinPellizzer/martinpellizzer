Hyptis ovalifolia

Hyptis ovalifolia, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and clusters of blue or purple flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Hyptis ovalifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
