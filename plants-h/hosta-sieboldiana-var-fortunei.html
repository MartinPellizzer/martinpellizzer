Hosta sieboldiana var. fortunei

Hosta sieboldiana var. fortunei, commonly known as the Fortune's Hosta, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a white margin. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and is a popular choice in shady garden settings.
