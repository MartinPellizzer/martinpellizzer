Hieracium vogesiacum subsp. stelliceps

Hieracium vogesiacum subsp.

stelliceps, commonly known as the star-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spiky flower heads that resemble a star.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are alternate, rough, and often have a woolly texture.

It produces small, yellow to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn.
