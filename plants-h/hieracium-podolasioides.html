Hieracium podolasioides

Hieracium podolasioides, commonly known as the Podolasian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are usually alternate and lanceolate in shape.

Hieracium podolasioides is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
