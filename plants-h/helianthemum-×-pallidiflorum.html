Helianthemum × pallidiflorum

Helianthemum × pallidiflorum, commonly known as the paleflower rockrose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compact, evergreen foliage with a grayish-blue hue, adapted to rocky and arid environments. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and hardy nature.
