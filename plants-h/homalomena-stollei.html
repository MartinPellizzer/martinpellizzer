Homalomena stollei

Homalomena stollei, commonly known as the Stolle's arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, colorful inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Homalomena stollei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
