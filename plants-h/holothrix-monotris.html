Holothrix monotris

Holothrix monotris, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, warm environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Holothrix monotris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
