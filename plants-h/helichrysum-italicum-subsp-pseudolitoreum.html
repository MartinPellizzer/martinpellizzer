Helichrysum italicum subsp. pseudolitoreum

Helichrysum italicum subsp.

pseudolitoreum, commonly known as the coastal helichrysum, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellow-orange flower heads that are often retained on the plant long after blooming.

The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.

Its essential oils are used in aromatherapy and natural remedies.
