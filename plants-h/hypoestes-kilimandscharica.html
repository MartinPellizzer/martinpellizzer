Hypoestes kilimandscharica

Hypoestes kilimandscharica, commonly known as the "Kilimanjaro Marvel," is a striking flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, bi-colored flowers that typically display a combination of red and white. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-draining soil with moderate to bright indirect light.
