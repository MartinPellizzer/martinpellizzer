Hypoestes poilanei

Hypoestes poilanei, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and tubular, often hidden beneath the vibrant bracts.

Hypoestes poilanei thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.
