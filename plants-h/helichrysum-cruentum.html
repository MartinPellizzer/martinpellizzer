Helichrysum cruentum

Helichrysum cruentum, commonly known as the red everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flower heads. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically grouped into dense, spherical clusters. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Helichrysum cruentum is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and striking color.
