Hypoestes floribunda var. pubescens

Hypoestes floribunda var. pubescens, commonly known as the silver bush or velvet plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it a soft, textured appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This evergreen shrub is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
