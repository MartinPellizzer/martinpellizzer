Helianthemum rossmaessleri

Helianthemum rossmaessleri, commonly known as the Rossmaessler's rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with fleshy, hairy stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
