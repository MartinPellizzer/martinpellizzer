Hibiscus sturtii

Hibiscus sturtii, commonly known as Sturt's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, yellow flowers with a distinctive red center. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and covered in fine white hairs. Hibiscus sturtii is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is a hardy perennial that is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
