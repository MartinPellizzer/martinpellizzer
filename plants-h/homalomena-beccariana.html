Homalomena beccariana

Homalomena beccariana, commonly known as the Beccarian arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
