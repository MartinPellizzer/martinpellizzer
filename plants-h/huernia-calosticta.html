Huernia calosticta

Huernia calosticta, commonly known as the Speckled Moon Cactus, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its swollen, fleshy stems that resemble a cactus. The plant features a distinctive pattern of light and dark green markings on its stems, which give it its common name. It produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, and it thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Huernia calosticta is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
