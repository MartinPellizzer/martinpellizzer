Hypoestes axillaris

Hypoestes axillaris, commonly known as the Mock Orange, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to white, often resembling the blooms of the true orange tree. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and arranged opposite along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season with proper care.
