Hieracium paleopyrenaicum

Hieracium paleopyrenaicum, commonly known as the pale Pyrenean hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine meadows, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant has basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive lobed shape.

Hieracium paleopyrenaicum is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to the harsh climatic conditions of its mountainous habitat.
