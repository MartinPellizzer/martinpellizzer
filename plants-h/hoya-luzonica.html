Hoya luzonica

Hoya luzonica, commonly known as the "Luzon Wreath" or "Wreath Hoya," is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Hoya luzonica thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
