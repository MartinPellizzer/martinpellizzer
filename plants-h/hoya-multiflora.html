Hoya multiflora

Hoya multiflora, commonly known as the wax plant or prayer plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink in color. Hoya multiflora is a climbing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
