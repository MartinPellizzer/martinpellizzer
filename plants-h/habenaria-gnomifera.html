Habenaria gnomifera

Habenaria gnomifera, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit and usually produces a single, showy flower. The plant features a short, fleshy rhizome and linear, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically pale green or yellow with a distinctive, spurred lip.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
