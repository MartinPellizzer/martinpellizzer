Habenaria transvaalensis

Habenaria transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single or few flowers at the top. Its flowers are usually white or pale green with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
