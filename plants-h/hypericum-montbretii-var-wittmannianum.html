Hypericum montbretii var. wittmannianum

Hypericum montbretii var. wittmannianum, commonly known as the Wittmann's St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features upright stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated margin. It is often found growing in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
