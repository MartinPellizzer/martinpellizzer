Hieracium pseudozetlandicum

Hieracium pseudozetlandicum, commonly known as the Zetland hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces numerous small flowers in a single inflorescence.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
