Hippeastrum tiltilense

Hippeastrum tiltilense, commonly known as the Tiltil narcissus, is a striking bulbous plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive yellow or orange throat. The plant produces large, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Hippeastrum tiltilense is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
