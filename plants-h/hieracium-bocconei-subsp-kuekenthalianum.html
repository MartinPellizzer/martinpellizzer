Hieracium bocconei subsp. kuekenthalianum

Hieracium bocconei subsp.

kuekenthalianum, commonly known as the Kuekenthalianum hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has finely divided, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is often found in moist, sandy soils.
