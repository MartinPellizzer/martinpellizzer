Habenaria holstii

Habenaria holstii, commonly known as the Holst's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces erect, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red.

Habenaria holstii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
