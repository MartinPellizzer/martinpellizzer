Hovenia kiukiangensis

Hovenia kiukiangensis, commonly known as the Chinese date tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are similar in appearance to dates, hence its common name. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its fruit and ornamental value.

Hovenia kiukiangensis is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
