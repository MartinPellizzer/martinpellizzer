Haworthia coarctata f. major

Haworthia coarctata f. major, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, gray-green leaves with distinctive white or light-colored markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces tall, slender flower stems with small, white or yellowish flowers during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
