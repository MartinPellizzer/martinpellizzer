Haumania liebrechtsiana

Haumania liebrechtsiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a perennial herb native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of soft, velvety hairs. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Haumania liebrechtsiana is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
