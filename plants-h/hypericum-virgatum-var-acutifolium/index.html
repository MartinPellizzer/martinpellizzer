Hypericum Virgatum Var. Acutifolium

Hypericum virgatum var. acutifolium is a striking plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive foliage. It is a member of the Hypericaceae family and is commonly found in rocky and sunny habitats across northern regions. The plant features narrow, pointed leaves that give it its name, while its flowers bloom in late summer. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature. This variety is also valued for its potential medicinal properties.
