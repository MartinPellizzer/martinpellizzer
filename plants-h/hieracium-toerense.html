Hieracium toerense

Hieracium toerense, commonly known as the Toerens hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant produces yellow daisy-like flowers with a central disc surrounded by ray florets. Its leaves are typically basal, forming a rosette, and are covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium toerense is often found in dry, sunny habitats and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
