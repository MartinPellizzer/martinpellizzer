Hieracium amideii

Hieracium amideii, commonly known as the Amidei hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. It has basal and stem leaves that are usually hairy and serrated.
