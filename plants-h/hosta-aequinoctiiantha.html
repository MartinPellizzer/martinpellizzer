Hosta aequinoctiiantha

Hosta aequinoctiiantha, commonly known as the equinox hosta, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-white margin. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and subtle floral display.
