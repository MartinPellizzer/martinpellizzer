Hippeastrum dichromanthum

Hippeastrum dichromanthum, commonly known as the red-flowered amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that can range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often used for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum dichromanthum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and showy blooms.
