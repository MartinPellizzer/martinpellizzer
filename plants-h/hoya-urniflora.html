Hoya urniflora

Hoya urniflora, commonly known as the urn-shaped waxplant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to heart-shaped. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink. Hoya urniflora is a popular houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low light conditions and its air-purifying properties.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favored choice among plant enthusiasts.
