Hieracium hodophyes

Hieracium hodophyes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant features yellow flowers with ray florets that are typically shorter than the central disc florets. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium hodophyes is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
