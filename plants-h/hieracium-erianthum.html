Hieracium erianthum

Hieracium erianthum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
