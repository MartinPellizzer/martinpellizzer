Helianthemum Vulgare Var. Nummularium

Helianthemum vulgare var. nummularium is a low-growing evergreen shrub known for its compact, coin-shaped leaves. Native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, it thrives in rocky and sunny environments. This plant is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature. Its small yellow flowers bloom in spring, adding a touch of color to the landscape. It is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a resilient and ornamental plant.
