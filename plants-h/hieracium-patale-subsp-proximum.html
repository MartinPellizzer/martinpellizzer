Hieracium patale subsp. proximum

Hieracium patale subsp.

proximum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates. It has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with toothed margins.

This subspecies is often found in Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in open, sunny habitats.
