Hirculus stellariifolius

Hirculus stellariifolius, commonly known as the starleaf hirculus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves, which are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid regions.

Hirculus stellariifolius is adapted to withstand drought conditions due to its succulent leaves and deep root system.
