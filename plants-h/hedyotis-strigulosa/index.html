Hedyotis Strigulosa

Hedyotis strigulosa is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific islands. It is commonly found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Known for its delicate, fragrant flowers, it is often mistaken for other similar species due to its subtle appearance. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators and providing habitat for small organisms. This plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
