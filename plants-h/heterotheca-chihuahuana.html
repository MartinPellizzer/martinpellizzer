Heterotheca chihuahuana

Heterotheca chihuahuana, commonly known as the Chihuahua coneflower, is a flowering plant native to the Chihuahuan Desert in North America.

It is characterized by its showy yellow flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
