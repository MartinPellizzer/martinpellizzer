Hieracium brachiatum var. flagelliflorum

Hieracium brachiatum var. flagelliflorum, commonly known as the flagelliflorum hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellowish to orange flowers that appear in clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and often have a rough texture. This variety is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators due to its nectar-rich flowers.
