Hibiscus wrayae

Hibiscus wrayae, commonly known as the Wray's hibiscus, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and limestone soils. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Hibiscus wrayae is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and is often found in humid, tropical climates.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
