Helleborine helleborine

Helleborine helleborine, commonly known as the lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to Europe, Asia, and North America.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and its distinctive, spiraled arrangement of flowers that resemble a train of trusses. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a subtle fragrance.

Helleborine helleborine is a perennial herb that reproduces through rhizomes and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
