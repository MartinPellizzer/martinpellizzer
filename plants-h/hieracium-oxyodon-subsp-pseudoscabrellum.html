Hieracium oxyodon subsp. pseudoscabrellum

Hieracium oxyodon subsp.

pseudoscabrellum, commonly known as the rough hawksbeard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, lobed leaves.

This subspecies is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a taproot system and produces achenes as its seed type.

The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
