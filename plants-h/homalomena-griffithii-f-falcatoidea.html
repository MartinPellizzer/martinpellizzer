Homalomena griffithii f. falcatoidea

Homalomena griffithii f. falcatoidea, commonly known as the narrow-leafed caladium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive leaves and inflorescence. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a striking variegation pattern, often in shades of green, white, and pink. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a spadix, typical of the Araceae family.

This ornamental plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its decorative foliage.
