Hypoestes floribunda subsp. distans

Hypoestes floribunda subsp.

distans, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and white.

The leaves are typically velvety and dark green, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
