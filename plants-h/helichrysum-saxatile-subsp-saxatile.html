Helichrysum saxatile subsp. saxatile

Helichrysum saxatile subsp.

saxatile, commonly known as the stone everlasting, is a hardy, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa. It features small, yellow-orange flowers that are often retained on the plant long after blooming, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant has a compact growth habit with silver-gray foliage that is drought-tolerant and sun-loving. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
