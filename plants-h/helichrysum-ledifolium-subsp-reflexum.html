Helichrysum ledifolium subsp. reflexum

Helichrysum ledifolium subsp.

reflexum, commonly known as the silverleaf helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and often have a woolly texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
