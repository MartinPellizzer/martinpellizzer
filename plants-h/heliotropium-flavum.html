Heliotropium flavum

Heliotropium flavum, commonly known as the yellow heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow and sometimes pink. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and usually covered in fine hairs.

Heliotropium flavum is known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
