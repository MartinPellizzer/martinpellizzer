Hypericum nubigenum

Hypericum nubigenum, commonly known as the clouded St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for rock gardens.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
