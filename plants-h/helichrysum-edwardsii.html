Helichrysum edwardsii

Helichrysum edwardsii, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its vibrant yellow to orange flowers that resemble small suns. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in dense, spherical clusters. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and bright color.
