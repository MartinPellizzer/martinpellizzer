Hoya crassior

Hoya crassior, commonly known as the wax plant or giant wax plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asclepiadaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. Hoya crassior produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink.
