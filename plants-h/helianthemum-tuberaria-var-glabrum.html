Helianthemum tuberaria var. glabrum

Helianthemum tuberaria var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, rounded leaves that are generally hairless, giving it a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers in spring, which are typically tubular in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in xeriscaping and as a ground cover in dry climates.
