Helianthemum marminorense

Helianthemum marminorense, commonly known as the Marmenorensis sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a member of the Cariophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. Helianthemum marminorense is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
