Hieracium diversifolium

Hieracium diversifolium, commonly known as the variable hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and can reach heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are usually alternate and have a variety of shapes, contributing to its common name.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.
