Hieracium incisum subsp. hugueninianum

Hieracium incisum subsp.

hugueninianum, commonly known as the Huguenin's spear thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and yellow flowers.

The leaves are typically lobed and often have a rough texture. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands.

This subspecies is distinguished by its distinctive leaf shape and flowering pattern.
