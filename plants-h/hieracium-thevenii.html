Hieracium thevenii

Hieracium thevenii, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

Hieracium thevenii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual appeal.
