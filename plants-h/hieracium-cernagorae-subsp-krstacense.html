Hieracium cernagorae subsp. krstacense

Hieracium cernagorae subsp.

krstacense, commonly known as the Krstacene hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flower heads.

The subspecies is distinguished by its specific morphology, including the shape and size of its leaves and involucral bracts. It typically thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
