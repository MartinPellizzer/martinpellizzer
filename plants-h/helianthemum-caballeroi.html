Helianthemum caballeroi

Helianthemum caballeroi, commonly known as the Canary Islands rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub with succulent, fleshy leaves adapted to arid environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
