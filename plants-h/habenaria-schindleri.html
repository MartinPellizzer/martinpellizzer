Habenaria schindleri

Habenaria schindleri, commonly known as the Schindler's Habenaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
