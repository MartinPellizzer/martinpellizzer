Huperzia compacta

Huperzia compacta, commonly known as the compact huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its small, dense clusters of spore-bearing leaves, which are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant is evergreen and thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are leathery and have a distinctive, compact growth habit.

Huperzia compacta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
