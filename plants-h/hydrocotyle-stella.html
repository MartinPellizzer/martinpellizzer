Hydrocotyle stella

Hydrocotyle stella, commonly known as starleaf pondweed, is a perennial aquatic plant native to North America.

It has small, rounded leaves that are often arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shallow water and is characterized by its floating or submerged leaves. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hydrocotyle stella is often found in ponds, lakes, and slow-moving streams.
