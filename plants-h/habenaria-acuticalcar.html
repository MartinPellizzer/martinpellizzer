Habenaria acuticalcar

Habenaria acuticalcar, commonly known as the sharp-angled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats and has a rhizomatous root system. The plant features a single, erect stem with several oval, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small, pale green to white, and have a distinctive, spurred lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
