Hieracium spissidens

Hieracium spissidens, commonly known as the dense-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are typically hairy and have a serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium spissidens is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
