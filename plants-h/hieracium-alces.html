Hieracium alces

Hieracium alces, commonly known as the moose hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are typically rough and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and often reddish in color.

Hieracium alces is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
