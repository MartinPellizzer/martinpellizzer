Hedysarum cyprium

Hedysarum cyprium, commonly known as Cyprus peavine, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features compound leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its fruit is a legume that contains several seeds.

Hedysarum cyprium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
