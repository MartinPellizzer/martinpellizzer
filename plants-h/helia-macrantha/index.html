Helia Macrantha

Helia macrantha is a striking plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers that attract pollinators. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to certain regions of South America. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and can grow in a variety of soil types. Its blooming period typically occurs during the warmer months, adding color to its natural habitat. Due to its beauty and ecological value, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation efforts.
