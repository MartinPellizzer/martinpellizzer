Helianthemum stipulare

Helianthemum stipulare, commonly known as the stipulate rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 15 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, evergreen leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, woolly hair. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Helianthemum stipulare thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
