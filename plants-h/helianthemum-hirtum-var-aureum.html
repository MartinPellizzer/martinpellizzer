Helianthemum hirtum var. aureum

Helianthemum hirtum var. aureum, commonly known as the golden stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has fleshy, hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
