Heliconia schiedeana var. spissa

Heliconia schiedeana var. spissa, commonly known as the dense heliconia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Heliconiaceae family and is characterized by its large, colorful bracts that resemble flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant appearance. Its leaves are broad and waxy, providing a lush, tropical look, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to moist, humid environments.

This species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
