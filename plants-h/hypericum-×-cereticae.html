Hypericum × cereticae

Hypericum × cereticae, commonly known as the hybrid St. John's wort, is a member of the Hypericaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its bright yellow flowers and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, elliptical berries that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
