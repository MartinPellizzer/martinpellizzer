Hippeastrum santacatarina

Hippeastrum santacatarina, commonly known as the Santa Catarina amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in various colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces strap-shaped leaves that are often broad and dark green. It is typically grown as a decorative plant due to its striking blooms and relatively easy care requirements.

Hippeastrum santacatarina is well-suited for indoor cultivation and can thrive in warm, humid environments.
