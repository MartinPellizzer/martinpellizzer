Hosta sieboldiana var. sieboldiana

Hosta sieboldiana var. sieboldiana, commonly known as Siebold's plantain-leaved hosta, is a popular ornamental plant native to Japan.

It features broad, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces upright, bell-shaped white flowers in late summer, which are borne on slender stems. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its striking foliage and adaptability to a range of garden conditions.
