Hieracium fragilicaule

Hieracium fragilicaule, commonly known as the fragile hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, often hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas. The plant is known for its slender stems and small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is an herbaceous perennial that can be found in a variety of soil types, though it prefers moist and well-drained conditions.
