Hippeastrum ruizlealii

Hippeastrum ruizlealii, commonly known as the Ruizleal's amaryllis, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces striking, strap-like leaves that are broad and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
