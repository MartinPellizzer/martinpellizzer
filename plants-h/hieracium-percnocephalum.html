Hieracium percnocephalum

Hieracium percnocephalum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy, upright stems and basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Hieracium percnocephalum is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
