Hibbertia stichodonta

Hibbertia stichodonta, commonly known as the Stichodonta ribbon flower, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and striking, ribbon-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Hibbertia stichodonta is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
