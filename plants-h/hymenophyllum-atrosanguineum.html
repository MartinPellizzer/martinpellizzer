Hymenophyllum atrosanguineum

Hymenophyllum atrosanguineum, commonly known as the black blood fern, is a species of moss belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length. The plant has a unique appearance with a dark reddish-brown coloration, particularly on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or tree trunks in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
