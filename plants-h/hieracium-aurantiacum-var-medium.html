Hieracium aurantiacum var. medium

Hieracium aurantiacum var. medium, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flower heads, which are typically composed of numerous small tubular florets. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium aurantiacum var. medium is a perennial plant that spreads through rhizomes and can form dense colonies.
