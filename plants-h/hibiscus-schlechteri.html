Hibiscus schlechteri

Hibiscus schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Hibiscus schlechteri is a shrub that can grow up to several meters in height.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
