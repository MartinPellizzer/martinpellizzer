Helleborine wilsonii

Helleborine wilsonii, commonly known as Wilson's helleborine, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces showy, pale green to yellowish flowers with prominent, hood-like sepals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Helleborine wilsonii is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
