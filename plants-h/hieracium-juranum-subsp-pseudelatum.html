Hieracium juranum subsp. pseudelatum

Hieracium juranum subsp.

pseudelatum, commonly known as the Jura hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, characteristic of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is known for its robust growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
