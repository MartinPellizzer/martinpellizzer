Hibiscus celebicus

Hibiscus celebicus, commonly known as the Celebes hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to deep red. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. Hibiscus celebicus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater habitats.

It is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its traditional uses in local cultures.
