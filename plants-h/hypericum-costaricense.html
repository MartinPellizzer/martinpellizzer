Hypericum costaricense

Hypericum costaricense, commonly known as the Costa Rican St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and glossy, dark green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Hypericum costaricense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
