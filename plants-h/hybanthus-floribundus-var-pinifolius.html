Hybanthus floribundus var. pinifolius

Hybanthus floribundus var. pinifolius, commonly known as the needle-leaved hybanthus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and a subject of interest for plant enthusiasts.
