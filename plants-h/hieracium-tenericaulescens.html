Hieracium tenericaulescens

Hieracium tenericaulescens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, while its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.

Hieracium tenericaulescens is often found in open habitats and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens.
