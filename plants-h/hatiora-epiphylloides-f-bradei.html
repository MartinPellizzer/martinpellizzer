Hatiora epiphylloides f. bradei

Hatiora epiphylloides f. bradei, commonly known as the Brade's Christmas cactus, is a unique hybrid cultivar within the Schlumbergera genus.

It is characterized by its compact, branching structure and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant features thick, fleshy stems that are segmented and can be easily propagated through stem cuttings. Its foliage is usually a deep green, and the flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white.

This hybrid is well-suited for indoor cultivation and thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate watering.
