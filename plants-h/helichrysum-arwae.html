Helichrysum arwae

Helichrysum arwae, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even after drying, making it popular in floral arrangements. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Helichrysum arwae is also valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
