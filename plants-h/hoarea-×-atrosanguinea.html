Hoarea × atrosanguinea

Hoarea × atrosanguinea, commonly known as the red-flowered hoya.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family, characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, depending on the variety. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Hoarea × atrosanguinea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique flowering habits.
