Hypericum tomentosum var. viridulum

Hypericum tomentosum var. viridulum, commonly known as the green-haired St. John's wort, is a variant of the common St. John's wort.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine, greenish-white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
