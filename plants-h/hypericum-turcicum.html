Hypericum turcicum

Hypericum turcicum, commonly known as Turkish St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its stems are woody and covered with small, glandular dots.

Hypericum turcicum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
