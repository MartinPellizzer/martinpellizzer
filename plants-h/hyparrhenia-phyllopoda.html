Hyparrhenia phyllopoda

Hyparrhenia phyllopoda, commonly known as the narrow-leaf muhly, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Hyparrhenia phyllopoda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
