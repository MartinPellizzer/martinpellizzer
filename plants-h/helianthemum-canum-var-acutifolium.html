Helianthemum canum var. acutifolium

Helianthemum canum var. acutifolium, commonly known as the sharp-leaved rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with aromatic, gray-green foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a sharp, pointed tip, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
