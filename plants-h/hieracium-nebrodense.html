Hieracium nebrodense

Hieracium nebrodense, commonly known as the Nebrodes hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has basal rosettes of pale green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

Hieracium nebrodense is known for its adaptability to harsh environments and its role in local ecosystems.
