Habenaria eurystoma

Habenaria eurystoma, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive violet or purple coloration. The plant typically features long, strap-like leaves and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and role in local ecosystems.
