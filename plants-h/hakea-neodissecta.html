Hakea neodissecta

Hakea neodissecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often densely arranged along the stems. The plant produces striking, urn-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Hakea neodissecta is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
