Helleborus hunfalvyanus var. atrorubens

Helleborus hunfalvyanus var. atrorubens, commonly known as the Hungarian hellebore, is a rare and ornamental plant native to Hungary.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and striking, deep red to purple flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens. It is noted for its slow growth and long lifespan, often living for several decades.
