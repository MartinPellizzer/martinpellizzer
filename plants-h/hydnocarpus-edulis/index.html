Hydnocarpus Edulis

Hydnocarpus edulis is a unique tropical plant known for its distinctive fruit and medicinal properties. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments within dense forests. The tree's fruit, which resembles a small coconut, contains a hard, woody core and is often used in traditional medicine. Its seeds are valued for their ability to treat various ailments, including skin conditions and inflammation. Despite its benefits, the plant remains under-researched and faces threats from deforestation and habitat loss.
