Habenaria tuanae

Habenaria tuanae, commonly known as the Tuanae orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often in the understory where it can receive indirect sunlight. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

Habenaria tuanae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral structure.
