Hieracium trachyticum

Hieracium trachyticum, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium trachyticum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
