Heliotropium Nuttallii

Heliotropium nuttallii is a flowering plant native to the southwestern united states and northern mexico. it belongs to the family mallow and is known for its distinctive pink or purple flowers. this species thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and desert regions. it is also called the desert heliotrope due to its tendency to follow the sun. its unique appearance and adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanists and gardeners alike.
