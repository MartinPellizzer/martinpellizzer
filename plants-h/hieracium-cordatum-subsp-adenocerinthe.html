Hieracium cordatum subsp. adenocerinthe

Hieracium cordatum subsp.

adenocerinthe, commonly known as the腺毛蒲公英, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically hairy stems.

The plant features heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are often glandular, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family.

It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks.
