Hypericum elodes f. glabratum

Hypericum elodes f. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed St. John's wort, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that are covered in smooth, glabrous skin, distinguishing it from other forms of Hypericum elodes. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with four petals, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
