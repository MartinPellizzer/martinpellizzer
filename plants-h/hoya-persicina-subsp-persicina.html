Hoya persicina subsp. persicina

Hoya persicina subsp.

persicina, commonly known as the sweet potato vine or Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which are borne on clusters. It is a popular houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its attractive foliage. Hoya persicina subsp.

persicina is also known for its adaptability and ease of care, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
