Hieracium Pachymeroides

Hieracium pachymeroides is a striking member of the daisy family known for its vibrant yellow flowers and robust growth. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in a variety of habitats including meadows and disturbed soils. Its thick, hairy leaves and showy blooms make it a notable sight in the wild. Often mistaken for other hawkweeds, it has unique characteristics that set it apart. This plant plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems and attracting pollinators.
