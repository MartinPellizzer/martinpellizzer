Hieracium vulgatum subsp. wainioi

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

wainioi, commonly known as the Wainio's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

This subspecies is noted for its limited distribution and is often found in specific microhabitats.
