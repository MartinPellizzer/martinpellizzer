Haloragis teucrioides var. glabrata

Haloragis teucrioides var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved willowherb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, sandy soils along riverbanks and in wetlands. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairless. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences, with small, pink to purple blooms that appear in spring.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and floral display.
