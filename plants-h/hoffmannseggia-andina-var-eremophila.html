Hoffmannseggia andina var. eremophila

Hoffmannseggia andina var. eremophila, commonly known as the Andean Hoffmannseggia, is a succulent plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and adaptability to arid environments. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with small, often blue-green or grayish leaves that help reduce water loss. This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
