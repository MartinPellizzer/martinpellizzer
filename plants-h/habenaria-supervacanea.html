Habenaria supervacanea

Habenaria supervacanea, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and dense, white to pale pink floral spikes. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are small and arranged in a loose raceme, with three sepals and three petals.

The orchid is notable for its ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.
