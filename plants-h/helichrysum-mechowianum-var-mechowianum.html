Helichrysum mechowianum var. mechowianum

Helichrysum mechowianum var. mechowianum, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features bright yellow, ray-floret flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers, blooming in spring and early summer. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and attractive appearance make it a valuable addition to xeriscapic gardens.
