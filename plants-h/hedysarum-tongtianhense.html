Hedysarum tongtianhense

Hedysarum tongtianhense, commonly known as the Tibetan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Tibetan Plateau.

Hedysarum tongtianhense is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
