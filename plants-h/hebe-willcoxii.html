Hebe willcoxii

Hebe willcoxii, commonly known as the Kauri hebe, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight pubescence.

Hebe willcoxii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness.
