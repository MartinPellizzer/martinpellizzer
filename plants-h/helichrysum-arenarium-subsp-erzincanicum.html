Helichrysum arenarium subsp. erzincanicum

Helichrysum arenarium subsp.

erzincanicum, commonly known as the Erzincan everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Turkey. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit.

The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often mistaken for dandelions. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in dried flower arrangements.
