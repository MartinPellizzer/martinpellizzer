Hieracium klisurae subsp. klisurae

Hieracium klisurae subsp.

klisurae, commonly known as the Klisura Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Romania. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It is distinguished by its hairy stems and small, daisy-like inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
