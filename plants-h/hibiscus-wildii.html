Hibiscus wildii

Hibiscus wildii, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to deep red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. Hibiscus wildii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various climates.

It also has cultural and traditional significance in some African communities.
