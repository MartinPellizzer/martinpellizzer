Helichrysum amblyphyllum

Helichrysum amblyphyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that are often arranged in compact clusters. The plant is known for its aromatic leaves and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ability to retain its color after drying has made it a popular choice for floral arrangements.
