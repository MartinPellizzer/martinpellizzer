Hieracium personatum

Hieracium personatum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture.

Hieracium personatum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common and persistent weed in various habitats.
