Hieracium carolinianum f. virescens

Hieracium carolinianum f. virescens, commonly known as the Carolina Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered with fine hairs.

The inflorescence consists of a single flower head surrounded by leaf-like bracts, which are usually greenish in color.
