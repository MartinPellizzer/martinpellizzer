Hemionitis angustifrondosa

Hemionitis angustifrondosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hemionitis, is a rare and delicate fern native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, linear leaves that give it a grass-like appearance. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to extreme environmental conditions. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are typically found on the underside of the fronds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
