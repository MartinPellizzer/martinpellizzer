Hedysarum sikkimense

Hedysarum sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim peavine, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas, particularly in Nepal, India, and Bhutan.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, temperate forests and mountainous regions. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces yellow to orange pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. Its pods are elongated and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Hedysarum sikkimense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
