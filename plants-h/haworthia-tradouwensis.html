Haworthia tradouwensis

Haworthia tradouwensis, commonly known as the Tradouwensis Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pale green leaves with distinctive white markings and a spiky texture. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
