Hieracium tenuifloccosum

Hieracium tenuifloccosum, commonly known as the slender floccose hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium tenuifloccosum is often found in open habitats and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
