Helleborus atrorubens f. incisus

Helleborus atrorubens f. incisus, commonly known as the dark red hellebore, is a hybrid form of the hellebore genus native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its dark red to almost black flowers with intricate, often notched petals. The plant features large, evergreen, leathery leaves that are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, somewhat jagged appearance. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking winter blooms.
