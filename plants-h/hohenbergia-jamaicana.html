Hohenbergia jamaicana

Hohenbergia jamaicana, commonly known as the Jamaican hohenbergia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a dense, water-holding cup. Hohenbergia jamaicana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
