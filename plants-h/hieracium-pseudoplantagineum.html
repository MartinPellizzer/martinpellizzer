Hieracium pseudoplantagineum

Hieracium pseudoplantagineum, commonly known as the false plantain hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium pseudoplantagineum is often considered a weed due to its rapid spread and ability to thrive in various environmental conditions.
