Hakea pritzelii

Hakea pritzelii, commonly known as the Pritzel's hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical or globose stems and narrow, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, bottlebrush-like inflorescences that are typically pink or red in color. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought and heat.

Hakea pritzelii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-water conditions.
