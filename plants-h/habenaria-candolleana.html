Habenaria candolleana

Habenaria candolleana, commonly known as the Candollean orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
