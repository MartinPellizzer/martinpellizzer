Hymenorebutia cintiensis

Hymenorebutia cintiensis, commonly known as the Cinti's holiday cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its branching stems and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces bright pink or red flowers that bloom during the holiday season, typically from December to February. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hymenorebutia cintiensis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
