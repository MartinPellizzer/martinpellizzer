Herniaria americana

Herniaria americana, commonly known as American carpetweed, is a low-growing annual plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, rounded leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny white flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and garden beds.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in various habitats across the continent.
