Hieracium leptoprenanthes subsp. leptoprenanthes

Hieracium leptoprenanthes subsp.

leptoprenanthes, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disc flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring moist to moderately dry conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is considered a weed in some agricultural settings.
