Hieracium nigrescens subsp. schibleri

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

schibleri, commonly known as the dark-centred hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers with five petals. Its subspecies schibleri is characterized by its dark central disc and shorter, more compact growth habit.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal potential in some species.
