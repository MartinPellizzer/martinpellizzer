Hieracium planchonianum var. canillosum

Hieracium planchonianum var. canillosum, commonly known as the Canillo yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers and is native to the Pyrenees region of Europe. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and sunny exposures. Its leaves are broadly lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
