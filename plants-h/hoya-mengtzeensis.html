Hoya mengtzeensis

Hoya mengtzeensis, commonly known as the Mengtze's Wax Plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, often variegated leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Hoya mengtzeensis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
