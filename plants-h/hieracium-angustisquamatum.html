Hieracium angustisquamatum

Hieracium angustisquamatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, yellowish to reddish flowers that appear in clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and serrated, with a hairy texture. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

This species is often found in temperate climates and is known for its ability to colonize open areas quickly.
