Holostemma brunonianum

Holostemma brunonianum, commonly known as the climbing nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves that are usually pubescent. The plant produces small, white or violet flowers that are clustered in axillary cymes. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Holostemma brunonianum is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
