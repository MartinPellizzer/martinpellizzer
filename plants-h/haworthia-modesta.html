Haworthia modesta

Haworthia modesta, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for succulent gardens and indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
