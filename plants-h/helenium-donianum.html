Helenium donianum

Helenium donianum, commonly known as the Donian's sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, orange to red ray flowers. The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, thriving in moist, sandy soils. It has a distinctive, hollow stem with narrow, grass-like leaves.

Helenium donianum is often found in the southeastern United States, particularly in Florida and Georgia.
