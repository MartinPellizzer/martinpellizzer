Heliotropium nesopelydum

Heliotropium nesopelydum, commonly known as the rock heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branching stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Heliotropium nesopelydum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
