Hypochaeris graminifolia

Hypochaeris graminifolia, commonly known as the slender weed or common groundsel, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and yellow flowers that resemble dandelions. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and gardens. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that release numerous seeds, aiding in its spread.

Hypochaeris graminifolia is a hardy perennial that thrives in temperate climates.
