Hieracium orophilon

Hieracium orophilon, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

Hieracium orophilon is known for its resilience to harsh climatic conditions, making it a common sight in alpine meadows.
