Haworthiopsis reinwardtii

Haworthiopsis reinwardtii, commonly known as the Tiger Tooth Plant, is a species of succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow markings, resembling tiger stripes. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Haworthiopsis reinwardtii is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
