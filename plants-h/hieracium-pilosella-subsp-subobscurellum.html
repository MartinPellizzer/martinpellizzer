Hieracium pilosella subsp. subobscurellum

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

subobscurellum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish-green flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture, and the flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

This subspecies is distinguished by its pale coloration and subtle morphological differences from other Hieracium species.
