Helenium campestre

Helenium campestre, commonly known as the field helenium, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces striking orange to red-orange ray flowers surrounded by yellow disk flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats like meadows and prairies.

Helenium campestre is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
