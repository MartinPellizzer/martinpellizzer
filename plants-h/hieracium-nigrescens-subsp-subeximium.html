Hieracium nigrescens subsp. subeximium

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

subeximium, commonly known as the dark-hued hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and serrated leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in nutrient-poor soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Its subspecific classification highlights its distinct morphological features compared to other hawkweed species.
