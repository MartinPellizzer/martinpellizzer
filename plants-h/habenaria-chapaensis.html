Habenaria chapaensis

Habenaria chapaensis, commonly known as the Chapa orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
