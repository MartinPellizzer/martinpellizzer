Haworthia pygmaea f. crystallina

Haworthia pygmaea f. crystallina, commonly known as the crystalline Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white or translucent markings, giving it a striking, almost glass-like appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
