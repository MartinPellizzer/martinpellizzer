Helianthemum glaucum var. stenophyllum

Helianthemum glaucum var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved helianthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features silvery-gray foliage due to dense pubescence, and its flowers are typically pink or red, appearing in clusters. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in Mediterranean climates.
