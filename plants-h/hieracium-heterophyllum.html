Hieracium heterophyllum

Hieracium heterophyllum, commonly known as the variable hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. It features basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and stems that are often hairy.

Its flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.
