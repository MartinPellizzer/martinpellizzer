Haworthia coarctata var. kraussii

Haworthia coarctata var. kraussii, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of thick, fleshy leaves with distinctive white to pale blue markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant is known for its compact size and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect light and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity among succulent enthusiasts.
