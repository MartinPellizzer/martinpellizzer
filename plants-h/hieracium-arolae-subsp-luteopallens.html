Hieracium arolae subsp. luteopallens

Hieracium arolae subsp.

luteopallens, commonly known as the pale-yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellowish to pale-yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The plant has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Its botanical features make it easily distinguishable from other hawkweed species.
