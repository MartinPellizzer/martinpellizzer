Hieracium subatronitens

Hieracium subatronitens, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer, are surrounded by pale green bracts.

Hieracium subatronitens is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
