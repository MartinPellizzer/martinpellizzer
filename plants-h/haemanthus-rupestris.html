Haemanthus rupestris

Haemanthus rupestris, commonly known as the rock lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is well adapted to arid conditions.

Haemanthus rupestris is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
