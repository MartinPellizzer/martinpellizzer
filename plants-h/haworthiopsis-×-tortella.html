Haworthiopsis × tortella

Haworthiopsis × tortella, commonly known as the Tortella Haworthiopsis, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with prominent white or yellow markings. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for succulent collections and indoor gardening.
