Helichrysum sonderi

Helichrysum sonderi, commonly known as the golden crown, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble small suns. The plant produces papery, persistent bracts that remain after the flowers have faded, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Helichrysum sonderi is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
