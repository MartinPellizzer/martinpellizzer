Hieracium sparsum subsp. secundum

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

secundum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in clusters.

The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves with a serrated edge. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain regions.
