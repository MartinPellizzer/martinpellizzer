Hieracium sparsiflorum subsp. subsparsicrinum

Hieracium sparsiflorum subsp.

subsparsicrinum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can form dense colonies in suitable environments.
