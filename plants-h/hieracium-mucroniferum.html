Hieracium mucroniferum

Hieracium mucroniferum, commonly known as the blunt-tipped hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically clustered in a head, with small, oval-shaped phyllaries surrounding the base. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rough texture and a slightly lobed appearance.

Hieracium mucroniferum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
