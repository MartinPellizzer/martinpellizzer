Hieracium Pilisquamum

Hieracium pilisquamum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the dandelion family and is known for its small, yellow blooms and hairy leaves. This species is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Due to its limited range and vulnerability to habitat loss, it is considered a protected species in several countries. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
