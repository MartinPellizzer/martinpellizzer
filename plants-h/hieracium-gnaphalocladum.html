Hieracium gnaphalocladum

Hieracium gnaphalocladum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium gnaphalocladum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common but sometimes invasive species in certain regions.
