Helianthemum langei

Helianthemum langei, commonly known as the Lange's rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub with fleshy, succulent leaves adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Helianthemum langei is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
