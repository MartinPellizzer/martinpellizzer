Hieracium holleri

Hieracium holleri, commonly known as the Holli’s hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with hairy stems and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape. Its flowers bloom in late summer, producing yellowish-orange inflorescences that attract various pollinators.

Hieracium holleri is often found in meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges, thriving in well-drained soils.
