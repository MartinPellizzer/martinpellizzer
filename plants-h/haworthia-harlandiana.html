Haworthia harlandiana

Haworthia harlandiana, commonly known as the Harland's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in white or pale green markings. The leaves are often adorned with translucent windows, which allow light to pass through and reach the plant's interior tissues. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the growing season.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
