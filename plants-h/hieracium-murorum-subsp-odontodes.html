Hieracium murorum subsp. odontodes

Hieracium murorum subsp.

odontodes, commonly known as the toothed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy leaves.

The subspecies is distinguished by its serrated or toothed leaf margins, which give it its common name. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

This plant is often found in open habitats and is relatively common in regions with suitable conditions.
