Hieracium rivale

Hieracium rivale, commonly known as the water hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a dense, flat-topped cluster. The plant has hairy, rough leaves that are usually alternate and lance-shaped. It thrives in moist environments such as meadows, stream banks, and wetlands.

Hieracium rivale is often found in temperate regions and is known for its adaptability to wet soil conditions.
