Hieracium plumosum

Hieracium plumosum, commonly known as the downy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, giving them a velvety texture.

The plant produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by the wind, contributing to its ability to spread across various regions.
