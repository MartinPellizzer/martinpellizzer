Habenaria triplonema

Habenaria triplonema, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with three distinct lobes, typical of the Habenaria genus. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
