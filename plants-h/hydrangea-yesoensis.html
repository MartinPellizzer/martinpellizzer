Hydrangea yesoensis

Hydrangea yesoensis, commonly known as the Yesso hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from blue to pink depending on soil pH. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically large, ovate, and have a serrated edge.

Hydrangea yesoensis thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.
