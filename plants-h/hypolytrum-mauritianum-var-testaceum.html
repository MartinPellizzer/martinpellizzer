Hypolytrum mauritianum var. testaceum

Hypolytrum mauritianum var. testaceum, commonly known as the red-flowered swamp grass, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its erect, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences bearing reddish-purple flowers. It thrives in wet, marshy habitats and is often found in lowland areas.

Due to its invasive nature, it can outcompete native vegetation in certain environments.
