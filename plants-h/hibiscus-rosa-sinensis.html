Hibiscus rosa-sinensis

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, yellow, and white. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are usually green and have a smooth texture. Hibiscus rosa-sinensis produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
