Hydrangea weberbaueri

Hydrangea weberbaueri, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink or blue, depending on soil pH. The plant has serrated, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Hydrangea weberbaueri is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
