Hevea nitida var. nitida

Hevea nitida var. nitida, commonly known as the slender rubber tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, straight trunk and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is cultivated for its latex, which is used in the production of natural rubber.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
