Hedeoma tenuipes

Hedeoma tenuipes, commonly known as the slenderleaf hedeoma, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, dry fruits known as schizocarps.

Hedeoma tenuipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
