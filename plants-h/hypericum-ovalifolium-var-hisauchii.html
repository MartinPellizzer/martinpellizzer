Hypericum ovalifolium var. hisauchii

Hypericum ovalifolium var. hisauchii, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but should be consumed with caution. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and woodland settings.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
