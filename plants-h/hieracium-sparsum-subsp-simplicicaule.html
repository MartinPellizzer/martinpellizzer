Hieracium sparsum subsp. simplicicaule

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

simplicicaule, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its stems are slender and often hairy, while its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly serrated.

The flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
