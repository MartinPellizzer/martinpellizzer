Hieracium subcaesium subsp. livescens

Hieracium subcaesium subsp.

livescens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring well-drained conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

It is a common weed in agricultural areas and can form dense colonies.
