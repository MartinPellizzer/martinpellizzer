Helichroa uniflora

Helichroa uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered yellow or golden-orange flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces a single flower per stem, which is a distinguishing feature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichroa uniflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
