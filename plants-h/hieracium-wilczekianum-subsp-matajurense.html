Hieracium wilczekianum subsp. matajurense

Hieracium wilczekianum subsp.

matajurense, commonly known as the Matajuren heath. It is a perennial herb native to southern Africa, particularly found in the Eastern Cape and KwaZulu-Natal regions.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer.

The subspecies is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in association with other endemic flora in its native range.
