Hieracium × tomosenze, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which typically feature yellow centers surrounded by white or pale pink ray florets. Hieracium × tomosenze is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides, meadows, and gardens.
