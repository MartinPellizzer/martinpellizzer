Habenaria ukingensis

Habenaria ukingensis, commonly known as the Uking orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizomatous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale pink to white in color. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its unique floral morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
