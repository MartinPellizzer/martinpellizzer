Hindsia ramosissima

Hindsia ramosissima, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often have a velvety texture.

Hindsia ramosissima plays an important ecological role in Hawaiian ecosystems and is also valued for its ornamental beauty.
