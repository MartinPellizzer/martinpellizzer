Helianthemum chamaecistus var. stramineum

Helianthemum chamaecistus var. stramineum, commonly known as the pale thyme-hedera.

This plant is a low-growing shrub characterized by its compact, cushion-like form and silver-gray foliage. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a subtle floral display to its appearance. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.

Its aromatic leaves and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
