Hordeum brachyantherum var. gracilius

Hordeum brachyantherum var. gracilius, commonly known as slender barley, is a grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
