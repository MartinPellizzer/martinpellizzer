Hieracium angustatum subsp. melanostictum

Hieracium angustatum subsp.

melanostictum, commonly known as the black-streaked hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers.

The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive dark green color. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dark-streaked leaves and reduced flowering structures compared to other hawkweed species.
