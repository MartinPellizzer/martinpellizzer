Hypobathrum graveolens

Hypobathrum graveolens, commonly known as the stinking rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, and it is known for its strong, unpleasant odor.
