Hypericum hookerianum var. leschenaultii

Hypericum hookerianum var. leschenaultii, commonly known as the Leschenault's St. John's wort, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
