Hieracium virentisquamum

Hieracium virentisquamum, commonly known as the green-squamated hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of broadly ovate, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces yellowish to orange composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
