Habenaria hebes

Habenaria hebes, commonly known as the small-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and undergrowth. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and the flowers are usually pale green or yellow with a distinct lip.

Habenaria hebes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
