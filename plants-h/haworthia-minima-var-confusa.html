Haworthia minima var. confusa

Haworthia minima var. confusa, commonly known as the compact haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
