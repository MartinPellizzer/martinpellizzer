Hieracium picroides subsp. trichopicris

Hieracium picroides subsp.

trichopicris, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its yellowish to orange-red flowers and hairy stems and leaves.

The subspecies is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils. It has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, dandelion-like flowers.

The plant is typically herbaceous and can grow up to 30 cm in height.
