Hieracium brevifolium subsp. noeanum

Hieracium brevifolium subsp.

noeanum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features upright stems with alternate, narrow, and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow, with a distinctive ray floret structure, and are arranged in a composite head.

It is characterized by its small size and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
