Heterocentron

Heterocentron, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny bracts that surround the small, yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and salt marshes, demonstrating its adaptability to saline environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, with a rough texture.

Heterocentron is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
