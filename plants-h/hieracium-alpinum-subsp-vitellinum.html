Hieracium alpinum subsp. vitellinum

Hieracium alpinum subsp.

vitellinum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Its blooming period occurs in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
