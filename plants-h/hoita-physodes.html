Hoita physodes

Hoita physodes, commonly known as the silver fern, is a striking plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its feathery, silver-green fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the fern family and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are composed of small, overlapping pinnae that create a dense, lush foliage.

Hoita physodes is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and its ability to add a tropical feel to gardens.
