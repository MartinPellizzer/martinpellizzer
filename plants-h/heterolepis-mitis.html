Heterolepis mitis

Heterolepis mitis, commonly known as the slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of small, spikelet-like flowers.

Heterolepis mitis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
