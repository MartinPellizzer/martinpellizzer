Hieracium pilosella subsp. trichocephalum

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

trichocephalum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the genus Hieracium. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.
