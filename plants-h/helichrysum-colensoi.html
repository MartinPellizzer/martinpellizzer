Helichrysum colensoi

Helichrysum colensoi, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of golden-yellow, sun-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its foliage is usually grey-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small, narrow leaves.
