Haemanthus multiflorus

Haemanthus multiflorus, commonly known as the Rainforest Hemerocallis, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with reddish veins. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
