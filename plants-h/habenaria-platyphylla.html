Habenaria platyphylla

Habenaria platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a single, fleshy, broad leaf. Its flowers are usually small, with a distinctive three-lobed lip and a greenish-yellow coloration.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral structure.
