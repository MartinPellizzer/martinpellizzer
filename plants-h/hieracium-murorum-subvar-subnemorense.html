Hieracium murorum subvar. subnemorense

Hieracium murorum subvar. subnemorense, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are yellow, with a single central ray floret and numerous disk florets, blooming from late spring to early autumn.

It is part of the Asteraceae family and is often found in disturbed or nutrient-rich habitats.
