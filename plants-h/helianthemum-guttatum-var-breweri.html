Helianthemum guttatum var. breweri

Helianthemum guttatum var. breweri, commonly known as the Brewer's helenia, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features fleshy, green to blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for arid landscape designs.
