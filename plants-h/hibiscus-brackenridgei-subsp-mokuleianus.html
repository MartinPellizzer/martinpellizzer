Hibiscus brackenridgei subsp. mokuleianus

Hibiscus brackenridgei subsp.

mokuleianus, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a species native to the Hawaiian Islands. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red.

The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often found growing in coastal areas and on volcanic soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in its native habitat.
