Habenaria ridleyana

Habenaria ridleyana, commonly known as the Ridley's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and swampy areas. Its rhizome is fibrous, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.
