Habenaria clavellata

Habenaria clavellata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Habenaria clavellata is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
