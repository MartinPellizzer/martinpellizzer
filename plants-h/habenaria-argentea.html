Habenaria argentea

Habenaria argentea, commonly known as the silver orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery or white pubescence. The plant produces showy, pale yellow or greenish-white flowers with distinctive, elongated sepals and petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
