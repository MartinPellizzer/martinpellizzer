Hypoxis dulcis

Hypoxis dulcis, commonly known as sweet yellow star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with white markings. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces clusters of small, star-shaped blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoxis dulcis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
