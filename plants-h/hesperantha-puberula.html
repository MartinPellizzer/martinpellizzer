Hesperantha puberula

Hesperantha puberula, commonly known as the velvet bell orchid, is a South African orchid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety stems and striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant is native to the Eastern Cape and Western Cape provinces of South Africa, where it grows in rocky or sandy soils. Hesperantha puberula is a perennial herb that blooms during the summer months, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its unique morphology and vibrant floral display make it a popular choice for cultivated gardens and botanical collections.
