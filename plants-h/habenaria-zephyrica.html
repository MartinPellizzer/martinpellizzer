Habenaria zephyrica

Habenaria zephyrica, commonly known as the wind orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest floors and thrives in humid environments. The plant features slender, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. Its flowers are small and delicate, usually appearing in clusters of 3 to 5, with a pale pink or white coloration.

The species is noted for its ability to adapt to various soil types, making it a resilient member of the orchid family.
