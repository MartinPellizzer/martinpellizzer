Hieracium mariae-bornmuelleriae

Hieracium mariae-bornmuelleriae, commonly known as the Marie Bornmueller's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 2,500 meters. Its leaves are usually lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

It is a relatively rare species, often found in specific microhabitats that support its growth.
