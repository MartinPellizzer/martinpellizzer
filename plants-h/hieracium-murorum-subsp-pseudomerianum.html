Hieracium murorum subsp. pseudomerianum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

pseudomerianum, commonly known as the false merian's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, elongated leaves.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas, disturbed soils, and along roadsides. It has a creeping growth habit with rhizomes that allow it to spread vegetatively.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
