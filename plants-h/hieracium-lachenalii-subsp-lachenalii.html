Hieracium lachenalii subsp. lachenalii

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

lachenalii, commonly known as the Lachenal's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and the stems are often reddish in color.

It is part of the dandelion family (Compositae) and is characterized by its ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
