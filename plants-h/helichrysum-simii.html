Helichrysum simii

Helichrysum simii, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.

Helichrysum simii is also valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine.
