Hieracium murorum subsp. bithynicum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

bithynicum, commonly known as the Bithynian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single head.

The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are typically surrounded by pale green or white ray florets.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting pollinators.
