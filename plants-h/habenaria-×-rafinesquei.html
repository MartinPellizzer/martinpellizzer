Habenaria × rafinesquei

Habenaria × rafinesquei, commonly known as the Rafinesque's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant produces showy, greenish-white to pale yellow blossoms with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a hybrid, showcasing the genetic diversity within the Habenaria genus.
