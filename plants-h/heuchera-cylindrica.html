Heuchera cylindrica

Heuchera cylindrica, commonly known as the red stem heuchera, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features striking red stems that are often more prominent than its foliage, which consists of dark green, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the summer months. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Heuchera cylindrica is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shaded landscapes.
