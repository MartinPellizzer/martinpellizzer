Helicia cochinchinensis var. rengetiensis

Helicia cochinchinensis var. rengetiensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese holly or mountain holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and thrives in humid, mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
