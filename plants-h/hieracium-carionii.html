Hieracium carionii

Hieracium carionii, commonly known as the purple meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragweed, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium carionii is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in disturbed habitats.
