Hieracium subcurvatifolium

Hieracium subcurvatifolium, commonly known as the bent-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along roadsides, where it plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
