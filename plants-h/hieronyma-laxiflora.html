Hieronyma laxiflora

Hieronyma laxiflora, commonly known as the Brazilian rosewood or the pink hibiscus.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in subtropical gardens.
