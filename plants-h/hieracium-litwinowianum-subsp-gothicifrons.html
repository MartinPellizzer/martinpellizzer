Hieracium litwinowianum subsp. gothicifrons

Hieracium litwinowianum subsp.

gothicifrons, commonly known as the Gothic-flowered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its elongated, narrowly lance-shaped leaves and distinctive floral morphology. It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showing adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
