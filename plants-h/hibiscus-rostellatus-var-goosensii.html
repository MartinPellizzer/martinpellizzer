Hibiscus rostellatus var. goosensii

Hibiscus rostellatus var. goosensii, commonly known as the Goosens' hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Hibiscus rostellatus var. goosensii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
