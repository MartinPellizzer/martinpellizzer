Hecatonia

Hecatonia, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Nymphaeaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, slender stalks above the water. The plant has broad, floating leaves that help it thrive in aquatic environments. Hecatonia is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Asia and Africa.
