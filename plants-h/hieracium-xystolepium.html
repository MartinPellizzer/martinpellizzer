Hieracium xystolepium

Hieracium xystolepium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright stems and basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is sometimes grown in gardens.
