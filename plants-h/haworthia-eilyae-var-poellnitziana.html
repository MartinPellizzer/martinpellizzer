Haworthia eilyae var. poellnitziana

Haworthia eilyae var. poellnitziana, commonly known as the Poellnitz's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white to pale blue markings, which provide a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the cooler months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
