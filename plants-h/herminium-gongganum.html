Herminium gongganum

Herminium gongganum, commonly known as the Chinese tassel orchid, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer.

Herminium gongganum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
