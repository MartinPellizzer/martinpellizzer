Huperzia dura

Huperzia dura, commonly known as the hardy clubmoss, is a species of fern ally that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and a creeping rhizome system. Native to regions of Asia, including China and Japan, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its spirally arranged leaves and is often found in forest understories.

Huperzia dura is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
