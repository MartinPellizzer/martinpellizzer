Hieracium debilescens

Hieracium debilescens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually yellow or cream-colored, and it produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Hieracium debilescens is a perennial species that can form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
