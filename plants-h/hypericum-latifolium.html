Hypericum latifolium

Hypericum latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its oval, dark green leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Hypericum latifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
