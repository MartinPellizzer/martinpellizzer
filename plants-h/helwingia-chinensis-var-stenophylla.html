Helwingia chinensis var. stenophylla

Helwingia chinensis var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed helwingia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its ornamental value comes from its finely cut foliage and the subtle beauty of its spring blooms.

It is relatively low maintenance and suitable for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
