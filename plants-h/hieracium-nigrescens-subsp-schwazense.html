Hieracium nigrescens subsp. schwazense

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

schwazense, commonly known as the Schwazense Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

The plant features basal rosettes of pale green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single flower per stem, with showy, rayless florets.

It is known for its rapid spread and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
