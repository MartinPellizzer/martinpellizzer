Helianthemum guttatum var. eriocaulon

Helianthemum guttatum var. eriocaulon, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, often in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in dry environments. Its leaves are typically covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
