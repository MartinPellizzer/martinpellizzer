Hieracium orbiculatum

Hieracium orbiculatum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, hairy leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in a dense cluster, typically blooming from June to September. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, roadsides, and disturbed soils.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in some regions.
