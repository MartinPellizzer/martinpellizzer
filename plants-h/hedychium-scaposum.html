Hedychium scaposum

Hedychium scaposum, commonly known as the golden ginger or white ginger, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other ginger species. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces tall, branched stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its most striking feature is the large, showy yellow or white flowers that bloom in clusters.
