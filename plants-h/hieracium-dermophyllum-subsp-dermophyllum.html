Hieracium dermophyllum subsp. dermophyllum

Hieracium dermophyllum subsp.

dermophyllum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Due to its hairy texture and showy flowers, it is often found in open, sunny habitats.
