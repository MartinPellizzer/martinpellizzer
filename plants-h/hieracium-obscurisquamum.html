Hieracium obscurisquamum

Hieracium obscurisquamum, commonly known as the dark-squamate hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium obscurisquamum is often found in low nutrient soils and can be an indicator species for certain ecological conditions.
