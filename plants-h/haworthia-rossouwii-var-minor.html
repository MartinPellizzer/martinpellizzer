Haworthia rossouwii var. minor

Haworthia rossouwii var. minor, commonly known as the Rossouw's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor or rock garden settings.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
