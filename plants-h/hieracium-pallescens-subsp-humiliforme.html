Hieracium pallescens subsp. humiliforme

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

humiliforme, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features slender, upright stems that are usually covered in fine, white hairs.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a fuzzy texture.

It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
