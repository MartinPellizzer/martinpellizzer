Hieracium psilodorum

Hieracium psilodorum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Hieracium psilodorum is often found in temperate climates and is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and seeds.
