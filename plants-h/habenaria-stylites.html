Habenaria stylites

Habenaria stylites, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and dense clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and forest floors. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette at the base of the stem.

The species is native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Korean Peninsula.
