Hesperis hyrcanica

Hesperis hyrcanica, commonly known as the Hyrcanian hesperis, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to temperate climates.
