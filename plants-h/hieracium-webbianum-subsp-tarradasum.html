Hieracium webbianum subsp. tarradasum

Hieracium webbianum subsp.

tarradasum, commonly known as the Tarradasum hawkweed, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a single, upright stem that bears yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale green to white ray florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, pastures, and roadsides. It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is sometimes grown in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
