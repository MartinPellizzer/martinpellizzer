Hatiora rosea

Hatiora rosea, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often used for propagation. Hatiora rosea is a popular indoor plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
