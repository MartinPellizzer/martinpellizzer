Hylotelephium Vulgare

Hylotelephium vulgare is a hardy perennial plant known for its vibrant autumn foliage. It belongs to the family Crassulaceae and is commonly found in rocky and gravelly soils. This plant is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its low maintenance requirements. Its leaves turn brilliant shades of red and orange in the fall, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
