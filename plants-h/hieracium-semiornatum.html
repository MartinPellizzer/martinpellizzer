Hieracium semiornatum

Hieracium semiornatum, commonly known as the lesser yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Hieracium semiornatum is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable habitats.
