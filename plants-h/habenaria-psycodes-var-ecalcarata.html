Habenaria psycodes var. ecalcarata

Habenaria psycodes var. ecalcarata, commonly known as the Spur Orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive spur at the base of the lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
