Hieracium cernagorae subsp. doljaninum

Hieracium cernagorae subsp.

doljaninum, commonly known as the Doljanin's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

Native to specific regions in Europe, this subspecies is adapted to rocky and mountainous habitats. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The plant plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and supporting biodiversity in its native range.
