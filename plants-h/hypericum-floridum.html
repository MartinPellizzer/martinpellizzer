Hypericum floridum

Hypericum floridum, commonly known as the Florida St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly waxy texture.

Hypericum floridum is often found in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in wetlands.
