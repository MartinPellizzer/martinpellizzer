Hedyotis biflora var. graminicola

Hedyotis biflora var. graminicola, commonly known as the grassy marsh woundwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

It typically grows in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in temperate regions. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually lanceolate or ovate in shape. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and are typically white or pale yellow, with four petals.

This variant is characterized by its more grass-like appearance compared to the typical form of Hedyotis biflora.
