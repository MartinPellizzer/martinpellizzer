Hieracium hyparcticoides

Hieracium hyparcticoides, commonly known as the northern hedgehog.

It is a flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae, characterized by its spiky, yellow-orange flowers and hairy stems. Native to northern regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant has small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Hieracium genus, which includes many species of ragweed and hawkweeds.
