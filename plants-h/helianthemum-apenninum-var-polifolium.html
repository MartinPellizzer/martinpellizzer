Helianthemum apenninum var. polifolium

Helianthemum apenninum var. polifolium, commonly known as the Sicilian rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a vibrant touch to rocky and coastal landscapes. The plant has aromatic, needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color, providing a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and dry climates.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural use in Mediterranean-style gardens.
