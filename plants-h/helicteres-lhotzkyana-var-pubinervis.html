Helicteres lhotzkyana var. pubinervis

Helicteres lhotzkyana var. pubinervis, commonly known as the pubescent nerve-leaf helicteres, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with pubescent (hairy) veins, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are woody capsules that contain several seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical forests.
