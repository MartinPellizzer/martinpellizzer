Helichrysum buphthalmoides

Helichrysum buphthalmoides, commonly known as the yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands and shrublands. Helichrysum buphthalmoides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
