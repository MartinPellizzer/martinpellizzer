Hieracium procedens

Hieracium procedens, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Native to Europe, it is a perennial herb that thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators but can also become invasive in certain environments.
