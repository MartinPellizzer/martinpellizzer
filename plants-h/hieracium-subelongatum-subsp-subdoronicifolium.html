Hieracium subelongatum subsp. subdoronicifolium

Hieracium subelongatum subsp.

subdoronicifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.
