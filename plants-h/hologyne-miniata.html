Hologyne miniata

Hologyne miniata, commonly known as the miniature holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves with white veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that persist into winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, Hologyne miniata is often used in landscape design for its year-round visual interest.
