Hieracium rapunculoidiforme

Hieracium rapunculoidiforme, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellowish-orange composite flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this perennial plant thrives in moist, calcareous soils. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks.

The plant's structure includes basal rosettes of leaves and elongated flowering stems that bear numerous small florets.
