Helipterum floribundum var. stuartianum

Helipterum floribundum var. stuartianum, commonly known as Stuart's everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, long-lasting yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
