Hypelichrysum heterotrichum

Hypelichrysum heterotrichum, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, white to pale yellow flowers.

Hypelichrysum heterotrichum is often used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
