Habenaria adenosepala

Habenaria adenosepala, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces upright spikes of white to pale yellow flowers with prominent sepals and petals. Its leaves are broad, lanceolate, and often have a waxy texture, adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Habenaria adenosepala is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
