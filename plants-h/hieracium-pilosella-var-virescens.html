Hieracium pilosella var. virescens

Hieracium pilosella var. virescens, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands. The plant features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, blooming from late spring to early autumn.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads.
