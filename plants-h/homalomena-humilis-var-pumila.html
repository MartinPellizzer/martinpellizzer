Homalomena humilis var. pumila

Homalomena humilis var. pumila, commonly known as the dwarf arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, adding a subtle ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-draining soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for indoor and shaded garden settings.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a versatile and low-maintenance plant for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
