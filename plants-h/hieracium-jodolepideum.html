Hieracium jodolepideum

Hieracium jodolepideum, commonly known as the Jodolepideum hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer.

The leaves are usually basal, alternate, and have a rough, hairy texture.
