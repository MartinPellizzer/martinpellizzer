Hieracium substellatum subsp. simonkaianum

Hieracium substellatum subsp.

simonkaianum, commonly known as the Simonkaian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

This subspecies is distinguished by its smaller flower heads and specific geographic distribution compared to other members of the Hieracium genus.
