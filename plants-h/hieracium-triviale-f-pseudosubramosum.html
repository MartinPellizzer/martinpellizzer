Hieracium triviale f. pseudosubramosum

Hieracium triviale f. pseudosubramosum, commonly known as the false subramosum hawkweed, is a variant of the common hawkweed.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. The plant features yellow composite flowers and has a rosette of basal leaves. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it distinct from other hawkweed species, particularly in its growth form and floral structure.
