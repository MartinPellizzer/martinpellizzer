Habenaria gracillima

Habenaria gracillima, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories and thrives in humid environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. Its flowers are often arranged in a raceme and are known for their subtle fragrance.

Habenaria gracillima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
