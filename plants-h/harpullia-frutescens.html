Harpullia frutescens

Harpullia frutescens, commonly known as the red tamarind tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, usually appearing in yellow or greenish-yellow hues.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to reveal edible seeds surrounded by a fibrous pulp.
