Hieracium murorum subsp. ovalifrons

Hieracium murorum subsp.

ovalifrons, commonly known as the oval-fronded hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are oval-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its robust growth and adaptability to various soil types.
