Helichrysum stellatum

Helichrysum stellatum, commonly known as the star daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Helichrysum stellatum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
