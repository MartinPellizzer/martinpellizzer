Hieracium sinuans

Hieracium sinuans, commonly known as the zigzag hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a zigzag pattern along the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.
