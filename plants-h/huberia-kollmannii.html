Huberia kollmannii

Huberia kollmannii, commonly known as the Kollmann's orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in regions with warm, humid climates. The plant features striking, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a complex structure typical of orchids. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in loose, well-draining soil.

Huberia kollmannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
