Hypericum tarquense

Hypericum tarquense, commonly known as the Tarquense St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering shrubs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, typical of the Hypericaceae family. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient plant, well adapted to its native environment.
