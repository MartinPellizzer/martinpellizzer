Helenium radiatum

Helenium radiatum, commonly known as the radiant helenium, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red ray flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Helenium radiatum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
