Hosta longipes var. alba

Hosta longipes var. alba, commonly known as the White Giant Hosta, is a striking variety of the Hosta genus native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, waxy leaves that are predominantly white to light green in color. The plant produces tall, upright flowering stems that bear showy, lavender-blue flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for shade gardens.
