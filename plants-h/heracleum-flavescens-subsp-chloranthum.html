Heracleum flavescens subsp. chloranthum

Heracleum flavescens subsp.

chloranthum, commonly known as the yellow cowparsnip, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its tall, hollow stems and large, umbrella-like compound leaves.
