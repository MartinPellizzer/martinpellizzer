Habenaria linguicruris

Habenaria linguicruris, commonly known as the tongue-armed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive lip that resembles a tongue. It typically blooms during the summer months and prefers shaded, moist habitats.

Its unique morphology and floral structure make it a notable species within the Orchidaceae family.
