Hieracium Britannicum Var. Subbritannicum

Hieracium britannicum var. subbritannicum is a lesser-known member of the daisy family native to the British Isles. This plant is often mistaken for its more common relatives due to its similar appearance. It thrives in damp, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its small, yellow-centered flowers bloom in early summer. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in local ecosystems.
