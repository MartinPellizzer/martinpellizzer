Hieracium albinum

Hieracium albinum, commonly known as the white hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features basal and stem leaves that are typically lance-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, with ray florets that are often pale yellow or white.

Hieracium albinum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
