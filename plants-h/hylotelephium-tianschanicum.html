Hylotelephium tianschanicum

Hylotelephium tianschanicum, commonly known as the Tianshan crassula, is a succulent plant native to the Tianshan Mountains in Central Asia.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially under stressful conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant.

Hylotelephium tianschanicum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and resilience.
