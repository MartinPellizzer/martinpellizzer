Habenaria mechowii

Habenaria mechowii, commonly known as the Mechow's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with three sepals and three petals, typical of the genus Habenaria. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered in many parts of its range.
