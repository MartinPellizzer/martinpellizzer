Hippeastrum susatanum

Hippeastrum susatanum, commonly known as the Susana's amaryllis, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and have a waxy texture. Its bulbs are fleshy and store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions.

Hippeastrum susatanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
