Humboltia oblonga

Humboltia oblonga, commonly known as the narrowleaf humboldtia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Humboltia oblonga is adapted to withstand drought conditions due to its deep root system.

It is often found in rocky soils and is considered a characteristic species of the Chihuahuan Desert.
