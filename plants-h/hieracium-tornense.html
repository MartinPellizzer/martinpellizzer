Hieracium tornense

Hieracium tornense, commonly known as the Tornese hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium tornense is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
