Hieracium streptochaetum f. floccosius

Hieracium streptochaetum f. floccosius, commonly known as the floccose hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, woolly leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

This variant is noted for its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
