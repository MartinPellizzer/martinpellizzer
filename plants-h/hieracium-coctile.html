Hieracium coctile

Hieracium coctile, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium coctile is often considered a weed due to its aggressive spreading habit.
