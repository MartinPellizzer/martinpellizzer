Habenaria ghiesbreghtiana

Habenaria ghiesbreghtiana, commonly known as the Ghiesbreght's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and Malaysia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring elongated, grass-like leaves and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in summer. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with intricate patterns, and it produces a unique, fragrant scent.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
