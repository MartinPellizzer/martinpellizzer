Habenaria perezii

Habenaria perezii, commonly known as the Peruvian butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and showy, butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale green to white with distinctive purple or brown markings.

Habenaria perezii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
