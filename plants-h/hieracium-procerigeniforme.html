Hieracium procerigeniforme

Hieracium procerigeniforme, commonly known as the tall yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with prominent central disk florets.

It is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
