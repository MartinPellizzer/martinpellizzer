Heterostemma membranifolium

Heterostemma membranifolium, commonly known as the membranous-leaf heterostemma, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery leaves that are often translucent, giving it a unique aesthetic. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
