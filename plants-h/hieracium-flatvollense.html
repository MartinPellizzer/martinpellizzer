Hieracium flatvollense

Hieracium flatvollense, commonly known as the Flatvoll hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and meadows. It has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and is often found in disturbed or nutrient-rich soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and locally endemic species.
