Hypericum henryi subsp. henryi

Hypericum henryi subsp.

henryi, commonly known as the Henry's St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to China. It features bright yellow flowers with prominent red glands, typically blooming in summer.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or woodland habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Hypericaceae family.
