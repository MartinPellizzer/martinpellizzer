Hoya wallichiana

Hoya wallichiana, commonly known as the "Wallich's wax plant," is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of India, Thailand, and the Philippines. Hoya wallichiana is also valued for its air-purifying properties and its ability to add a touch of elegance to indoor spaces.
