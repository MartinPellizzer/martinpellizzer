Horsfieldia iriana

Horsfieldia iriana, commonly known as the Irianese orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Indonesian island of Irian Jaya.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in the humid, shaded environments of tropical rainforests. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, typically featuring white or pale pink petals. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Horsfieldia iriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the biodiversity of its native ecosystem.
