Helianthemum vestitum

Helianthemum vestitum, commonly known as the woolly rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a vibrant touch to rocky or coastal landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sunny, arid environments.

Helianthemum vestitum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh conditions.
