Hippion nanum

Hippion nanum, commonly known as the dwarf hibiscus, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow, typically blooming throughout the warm seasons. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
