Hedysarum polybotrys var. euodontum

Hedysarum polybotrys var. euodontum, commonly known as the pink Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
