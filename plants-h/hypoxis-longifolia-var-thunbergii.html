Hypoxis longifolia var. thunbergii

Hypoxis longifolia var. thunbergii, commonly known as the African Crown Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asphodelaceae and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces a showy yellow flower head with white rays, which resembles a daisy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is noted for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.
