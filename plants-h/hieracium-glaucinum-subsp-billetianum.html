Hieracium glaucinum subsp. billetianum

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

billetianum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of lanceolate, glaucous leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late spring to early summer. It is characterized by its reduced leaf structure and the presence of glandular hairs on its foliage.

This subspecies is part of the genus Hieracium, which includes many species of hawkweeds known for their ecological and horticultural significance.
