Heliconia × nickeriensis

Heliconia × nickeriensis, commonly known as the Nickerie heliconia, is a hybrid plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, colorful bracts that resemble flowers, which serve as a visual attractant for pollinators. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, often with a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
