Haworthia pilifera var. gordoniana

Haworthia pilifera var. gordoniana, commonly known as the Gordon's Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white tubercles and translucent tips, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
