Hieracium perciliatum

Hieracium perciliatum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant features upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family.

Hieracium perciliatum is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in some regions.
