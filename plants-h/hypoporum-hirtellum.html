Hypoporum hirtellum

Hypoporum hirtellum, commonly known as the hairy hypoporum, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky outcrops.

Hypoporum hirtellum is often found in association with other understory plants in humid tropical climates.
