Hypericum daliense

Hypericum daliense, commonly known as the Dali St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has elliptical, dark green leaves. It is often found in rocky or forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Hypericum daliense is also noted for its medicinal properties, though it contains compounds similar to those found in other Hypericum species.
