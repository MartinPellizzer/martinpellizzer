Helianthemum vulgare

Helianthemum vulgare, commonly known as the common rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to August. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in dry environments. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry grasslands.

Helianthemum vulgare is a hardy plant that is well adapted to Mediterranean climates.
