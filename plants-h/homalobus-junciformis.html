Homalobus junciformis

Homalobus junciformis, commonly known as the slender homalobus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in late summer. Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
