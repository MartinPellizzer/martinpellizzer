Habenaria hymenophylla

Habenaria hymenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and swampy areas. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and pale green, often with a slight pinkish hue, and are arranged in a loose spike.

This orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other epiphytic and terrestrial orchids in its habitat.
