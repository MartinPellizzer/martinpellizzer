Hieracium × scribneri

Hieracium × scribneri, commonly known as the Scribner's hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a dense, flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and its foliage is often covered with fine, white pubescence. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variation.
