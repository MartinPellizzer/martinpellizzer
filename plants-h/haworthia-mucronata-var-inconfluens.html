Haworthia mucronata var. inconfluens

Haworthia mucronata var. inconfluens, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow markings. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in arid environments. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall stems during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
