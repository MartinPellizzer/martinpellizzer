Hieracium sylvaticum subsp. caesionigrescens

Hieracium sylvaticum subsp.

caesionigrescens, commonly known as the dark-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, dark green stems and yellow composite flowers.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

This subspecies is distinguished by its dark green coloration and its tendency to form dense colonies.
