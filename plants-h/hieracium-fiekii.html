Hieracium fiekii

Hieracium fiekii, commonly known as the Fiek's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers that attract pollinators. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils. The plant is typically found in grasslands and along streambanks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
