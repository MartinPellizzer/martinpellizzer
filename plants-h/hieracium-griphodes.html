Hieracium griphodes

Hieracium griphodes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium griphodes is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
