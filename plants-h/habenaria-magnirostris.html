Habenaria magnirostris

Habenaria magnirostris, commonly known as the broad-beaked orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces tall, erect stems with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive broad, elongated lip that resembles a beak.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
