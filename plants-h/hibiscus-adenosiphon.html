Hibiscus adenosiphon

Hibiscus adenosiphon, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. Hibiscus adenosiphon thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
