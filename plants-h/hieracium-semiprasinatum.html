Hieracium semiprasinatum

Hieracium semiprasinatum, commonly known as the greenish hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, dark green leaves and yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has hairy stems and produces seeds in a disc-like structure surrounded by ray florets.

Hieracium semiprasinatum is typically perennial and thrives in temperate climates.
