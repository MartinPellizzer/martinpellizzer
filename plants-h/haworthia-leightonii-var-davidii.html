Haworthia leightonii var. davidii

Haworthia leightonii var. davidii, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in white tubercles, giving it a distinctive zebra-like appearance. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect light and is often grown as a decorative indoor plant.

Its slow growth rate makes it an ideal choice for small spaces and rock gardens.
