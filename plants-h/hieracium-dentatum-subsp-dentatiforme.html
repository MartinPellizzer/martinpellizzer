Hieracium dentatum subsp. dentatiforme

Hieracium dentatum subsp.

dentatiforme, commonly known as the toothed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
