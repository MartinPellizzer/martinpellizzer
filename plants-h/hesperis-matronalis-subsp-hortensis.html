Hesperis matronalis subsp. hortensis

Hesperis matronalis subsp.

hortensis, commonly known as the garden honesty, is a flowering plant native to Europe. It produces clusters of white, four-petaled flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and long-lasting seed pods.
