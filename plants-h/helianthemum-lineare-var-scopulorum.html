Helianthemum lineare var. scopulorum

Helianthemum lineare var. scopulorum, commonly known as the rock helianthemum, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fleshy, yellowish-green leaves that are adapted to arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a hardy addition to xeriscaped environments.
