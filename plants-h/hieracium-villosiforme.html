Hieracium villosiforme

Hieracium villosiforme, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium villosiforme is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
