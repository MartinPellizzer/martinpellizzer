Hebejeebie densifolia

Hebejeebie densifolia, commonly known as the denseleaf hebe, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, dark green foliage that is often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a favored plant among gardeners in temperate climates.
