Hieracium ruprechtii subsp. tropojanum

Hieracium ruprechtii subsp.

tropojanum, commonly known as the Tropojan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region. It typically grows in alpine and subalpine meadows, preferring well-drained, rocky soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and bear several flower heads.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and milky sap.
