Hypoestes rosea var. laxiflora

Hypoestes rosea var. laxiflora, commonly known as the "pink shower" or "pink snowflake," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy pink to lilac flowers. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a slightly hairy texture. This variety is prized for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms and is often used in ornamental gardening.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.
