Hieracium baeticum var. praecocifolioides

Hieracium baeticum var. praecocifolioides, commonly known as the early-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, preferring sunny conditions. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

This variant is distinguished by its earlier flowering compared to other hawkweed species.
