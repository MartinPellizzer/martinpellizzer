Harfordia macroptera var. fruticosa

Harfordia macroptera var. fruticosa, commonly known as the red-flowered hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that are typically red or pink in color. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
