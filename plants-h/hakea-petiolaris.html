Hakea petiolaris

Hakea petiolaris, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white bloom. The plant produces cylindrical, yellowish flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. Hakea petiolaris is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

It is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
