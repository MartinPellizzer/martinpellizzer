Hieracium cynodon var. stelluligerum

Hieracium cynodon var. stelluligerum, commonly known as the starry hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and produces showy, yellow to orange composite flowers with a star-like appearance. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often glandular, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in certain environments.
