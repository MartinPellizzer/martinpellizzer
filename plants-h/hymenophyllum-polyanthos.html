Hymenophyllum polyanthos

Hymenophyllum polyanthos, commonly known as the fairy moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, intricate foliage and is typically found in humid, shaded environments. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Hymenophyllum polyanthos plays a vital role in forest ecosystems by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for microorganisms.
