Hordeum scabriusculum

Hordeum scabriusculum, commonly known as rough barley, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its coarse, hairy leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its seeds are small and oval, often used in traditional agricultural practices.

Hordeum scabriusculum is also valued for its role in ecological restoration due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
