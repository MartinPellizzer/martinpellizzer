Hyptis platanifolia

Hyptis platanifolia, commonly known as white hyptis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, white or pale purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats.

Hyptis platanifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
