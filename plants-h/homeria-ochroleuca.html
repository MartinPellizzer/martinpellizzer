Homeria ochroleuca

Homeria ochroleuca, commonly known as the yellow-flowered homeria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and greenish-yellow disk florets. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Homeria ochroleuca is often found in open vegetation and is adapted to withstand coastal conditions such as salt spray and strong winds.
