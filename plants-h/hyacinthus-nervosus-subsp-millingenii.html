Hyacinthus nervosus subsp. millingenii

Hyacinthus nervosus subsp.

millingenii, commonly known as the Millingen hyacinth, is a species native to South Africa. It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers with a distinctive white throat.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces compact, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
