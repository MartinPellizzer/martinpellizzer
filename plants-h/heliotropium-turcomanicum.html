Heliotropium turcomanicum

Heliotropium turcomanicum, commonly known as the Turkestan heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite and often covered in fine hairs.

It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with a deep root system and succulent stems.
