Hieracium colorhizum

Hieracium colorhizum, commonly known as the purple meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
