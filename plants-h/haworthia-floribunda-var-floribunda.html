Haworthia floribunda var. floribunda

Haworthia floribunda var. floribunda, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy leaves with white to yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
