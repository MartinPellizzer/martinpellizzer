Hedysarum arenarium

Hedysarum arenarium, commonly known as sand clover, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, which bloom in early summer.
