Humulus yunnanensis

Humulus yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan hops, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Cannabaceae family and is closely related to hops (Humulus lupulus). The plant is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Yunnan hops are also valued for their potential use in brewing and as a source of bioactive compounds.
