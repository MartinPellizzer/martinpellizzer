Hosta lancifolia var. thunbergiana

Hosta lancifolia var. thunbergiana, commonly known as the Thunbergian arrowleaf hosta, is a shade-loving plant native to Japan.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a silvery-white margin. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is relatively low maintenance.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to garden settings.
