Haworthia arachnoidea var. gigas

Haworthia arachnoidea var. gigas, commonly known as the giant spider plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in a whitish, powdery coating. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in arid environments. It produces tall, flowering stems with small white or yellow flowers during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
