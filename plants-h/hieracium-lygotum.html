Hieracium lygotum

Hieracium lygotum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Hieracium lygotum is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.
