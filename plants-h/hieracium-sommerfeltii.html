Hieracium sommerfeltii

Hieracium sommerfeltii, commonly known as the Somberfield hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium sommerfeltii is notable for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions, making it a hardy species in its native range.
