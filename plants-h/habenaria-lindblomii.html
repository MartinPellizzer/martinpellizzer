Habenaria lindblomii

Habenaria lindblomii, commonly known as the Lindblom's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and mountainous regions. It has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil.

The orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
