Haworthia blackbeardiana var. major

Haworthia blackbeardiana var. major, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings, especially along the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
