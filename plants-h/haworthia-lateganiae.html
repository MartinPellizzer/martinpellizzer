Haworthia lateganiae

Haworthia lateganiae, commonly known as the Lategan's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The leaves are often adorned with white or silver markings, which provide a striking visual contrast. It produces small, white or yellowish flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
