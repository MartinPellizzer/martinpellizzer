Hypericum chejuense

Hypericum chejuense, commonly known as Jeju goldthread, is a flowering plant native to the island of Jeju in South Korea.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
