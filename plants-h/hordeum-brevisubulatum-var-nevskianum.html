Hordeum brevisubulatum var. nevskianum

Hordeum brevisubulatum var. nevskianum, commonly known as the Nevskian wild barley, is a grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, with a bluish-green coloration.

It plays a role in local ecosystems and has been studied for its potential in agricultural and ecological research.
