Hieracium glaucum subsp. graminifolium

Hieracium glaucum subsp.

graminifolium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs.

The flowering stems bear yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets.
