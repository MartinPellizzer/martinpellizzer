Haageocereus multicolorispinus

Haageocereus multicolorispinus, commonly known as the multicolored hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, segmented stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its striking appearance and unique morphology, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and botanical collections.
