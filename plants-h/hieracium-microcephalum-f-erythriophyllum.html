Hieracium microcephalum f. erythriophyllum

Hieracium microcephalum f. erythriophyllum, commonly known as the red-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, yellowish to reddish flowers. The leaves are typically hairy and have a distinctive reddish hue, which gives the plant its common name. It prefers moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

This plant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant foliage.
