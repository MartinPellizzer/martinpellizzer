Hieracium collinum var. latifolium

Hieracium collinum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are broadly ovate with a rough texture, and its yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters.

It is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed habitats and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
