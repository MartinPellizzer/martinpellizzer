Hesperis balansae

Hesperis balansae, commonly known as the Balansae Hesperis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, particularly in Spain and Portugal. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to rocky soils.

Hesperis balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
