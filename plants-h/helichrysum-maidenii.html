Helichrysum maidenii

Helichrysum maidenii, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that resemble miniature suns. The plant produces papery, wind-dispersed seeds, which aid in its propagation across open habitats. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
