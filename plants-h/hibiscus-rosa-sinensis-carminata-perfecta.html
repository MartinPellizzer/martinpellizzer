Hibiscus rosa-sinensis carminata-perfecta

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis carminata-perfecta, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, with a distinctive five-petaled structure. The plant has dark green, lobed leaves that are glossy and aromatic. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis carminata-perfecta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
