Hoffmannia striata

Hoffmannia striata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hoffmannia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. It is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and withstand drought.

Hoffmannia striata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
