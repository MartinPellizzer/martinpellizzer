Hosta sieboldiana var. mira

Hosta sieboldiana var. mira, commonly known as the Mirabilis hosta, is a striking ornamental plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-blue hue on the underside. The plant produces upright, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a delicate touch to the foliage. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in shaded garden settings.
