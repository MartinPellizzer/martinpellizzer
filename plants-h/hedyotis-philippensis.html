Hedyotis philippensis

Hedyotis philippensis, commonly known as Philippine pennywort, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, opposite, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces tiny white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
