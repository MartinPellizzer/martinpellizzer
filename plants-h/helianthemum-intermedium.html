Helianthemum intermedium

Helianthemum intermedium, commonly known as the middle heather, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Helianthemum intermedium is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.
