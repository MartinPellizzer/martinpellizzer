Helenium autumnale var. tubuliflorum

Helenium autumnale var. tubuliflorum, commonly known as the orange hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features bright orange to red-orange ray florets surrounding a yellow disk in its showy composite flower heads. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in clusters, often reaching heights of 1 to 3 feet. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
