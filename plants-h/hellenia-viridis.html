Hellenia viridis

Hellenia viridis, commonly known as green hellenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hellenia viridis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
