Hieracium schmidtii subsp. vranjanum

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

vranjanum, commonly known as Schmidt's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellowish to reddish composite flowers.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. It has a hairy stem and leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
