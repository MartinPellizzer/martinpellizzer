Hemigraphis rhytiphylla

Hemigraphis rhytiphylla, commonly known as the silverleaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.
