Haworthiopsis granulata var. granulata

Haworthiopsis granulata var. granulata, commonly known as the granulate Haworthiopsis, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
