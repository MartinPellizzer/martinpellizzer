Hieracium billetianum

Hieracium billetianum, commonly known as the Billet's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is often found in disturbed or nutrient-rich soils.
