Hypericum elegantissimum

Hypericum elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its botanical features include small, elliptical leaves and a preference for well-drained, sunny habitats.

Hypericum elegantissimum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
