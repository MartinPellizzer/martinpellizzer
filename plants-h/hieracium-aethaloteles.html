Hieracium aethaloteles

Hieracium aethaloteles, commonly known as the pale spearthistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. Hieracium aethaloteles is often considered a weed but plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
