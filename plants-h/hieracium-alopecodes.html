Hieracium alopecodes

Hieracium alopecodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its yellow flowers, which bloom in summer, are surrounded by bracts that give it a distinctive appearance.

Hieracium alopecodes is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
