Helleborine latifolia subsp. platyphylla

Helleborine latifolia subsp.

platyphylla, commonly known as the broad-leaf helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family. It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green and velvety in texture.

The plant produces striking, hooded flowers with a greenish-yellow color, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky crevices.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
