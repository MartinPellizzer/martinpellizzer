Haworthia heidelbergensis var. toonensis

Haworthia heidelbergensis var. toonensis, commonly known as the Heidelberg Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a waxy coating. The leaves are usually green with distinct white or pale markings, which provide camouflage in its natural habitat. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
