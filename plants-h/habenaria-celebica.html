Habenaria celebica

Habenaria celebica, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, grass-like foliage. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and purple coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
