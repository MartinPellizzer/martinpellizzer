Hieracium porimense

Hieracium porimense, commonly known as the Porimense hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single head. The plant is native to Central Europe and thrives in moist, calcareous soils. It is often found in meadows and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
