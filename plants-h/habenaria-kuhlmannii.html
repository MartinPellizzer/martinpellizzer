Habenaria kuhlmannii

Habenaria kuhlmannii, commonly known as the Kuhlmann's butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on the forest floor or on tree trunks. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the surrounding soil, and it produces showy, fragrant blooms during the rainy season.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
