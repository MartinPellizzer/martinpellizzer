Hieracium sparsiramum subsp. sparsiramum

Hieracium sparsiramum subsp.

sparsiramum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are usually lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale yellow to orange ray florets.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in disturbed or nutrient-poor soils.
