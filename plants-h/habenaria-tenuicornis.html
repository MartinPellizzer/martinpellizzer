Habenaria tenuicornis

Habenaria tenuicornis, commonly known as the slender-horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that produces a single, erect stem. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate markings.

The species is characterized by its small, linear leaves and is often found in association with specific mycorrhizal fungi.
