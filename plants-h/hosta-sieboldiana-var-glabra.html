Hosta sieboldiana var. glabra

Hosta sieboldiana var. glabra, commonly known as the Siebold's Hosta, is a species of plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The leaves are smooth and lack the hairy texture found in many other hosta varieties. This plant prefers shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

It produces small, fragrant flowers in the summer, adding a subtle visual appeal to its foliage.
