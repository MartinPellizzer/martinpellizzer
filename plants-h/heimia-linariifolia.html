Heimia linariifolia

Heimia linariifolia, commonly known as the red root or scarlet root, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb with slender, reddish stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
