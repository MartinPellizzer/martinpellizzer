Hieracium commersonii var. hypochaetophorum

Hieracium commersonii var. hypochaetophorum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has basal leaves that are often hairy and can reach up to 30 cm in height.

The inflorescence consists of both ray and disc flowers, which attract various pollinators.
