Hieracium karpinskyanum

Hieracium karpinskyanum, commonly known as the Karpinsky's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a taproot system and produces numerous small, herbaceous stems that bear the inflorescences.
