Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. arnoldianum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

arnoldianum, commonly known as Arnold's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant is native to southern Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. It has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture.

This subspecies is distinguished by its smaller flower heads and specific leaf morphology compared to other hawkweed species.
