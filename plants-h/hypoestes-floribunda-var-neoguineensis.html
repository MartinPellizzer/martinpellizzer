Hypoestes floribunda var. neoguineensis

Hypoestes floribunda var. neoguineensis, commonly known as the Christmas star or flame violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are often surrounded by bright, contrasting bracts. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the year under optimal conditions.
