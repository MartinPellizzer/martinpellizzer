Hieracium thaumatum

Hieracium thaumatum, commonly known as the fairy dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble dandelions. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and leaves that are alternate and lance-shaped.
