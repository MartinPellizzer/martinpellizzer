Hieracium canovillosum

Hieracium canovillosum, commonly known as the Canovillo's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by small, hairy seeds.

Hieracium canovillosum is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain areas.
