Habenaria rumphii var. meraukensis

Habenaria rumphii var. meraukensis, commonly known as the Meraukensis orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers with intricate patterns, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
