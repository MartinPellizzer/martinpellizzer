Hippocratea ovata var. oblongifolia

Hippocratea ovata var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the oblong-leaved hippocratea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its leguminous properties and compound leaves. The plant features elongated, ovate leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in inflorescences, with a distinctive papilionaceous structure.
