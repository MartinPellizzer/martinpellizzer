Hypoestes aculior

Hypoestes aculior, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, purple, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically velvety in texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Hypoestes aculior is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
