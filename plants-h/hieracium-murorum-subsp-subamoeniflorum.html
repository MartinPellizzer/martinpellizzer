Hieracium murorum subsp. subamoeniflorum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

subamoeniflorum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are usually alternate, lance-shaped, and covered with fine hairs.

It is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
