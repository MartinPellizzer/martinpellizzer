Haworthia setata var. subinermis

Haworthia setata var. subinermis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically light green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
