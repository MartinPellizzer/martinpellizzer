Hosta plantaginea f. grandiflora

Hosta plantaginea f. grandiflora, commonly known as the giant-flowered hosta, is a cultivar of the hosta plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves that often have a silvery margin. The plant produces large, showy white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
