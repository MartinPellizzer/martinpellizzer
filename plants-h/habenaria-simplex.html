Habenaria simplex

Habenaria simplex, commonly known as the simple orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in a raceme. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Orchidaceae family.
