Hieracium pichleri subsp. pichleri

Hieracium pichleri subsp.

pichleri, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is a perennial herb with basal rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in several regions.
