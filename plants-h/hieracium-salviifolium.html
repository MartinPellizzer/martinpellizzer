Hieracium salviifolium

Hieracium salviifolium, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium salviifolium is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
