Helichrysum scutellifolium

Helichrysum scutellifolium, commonly known as the golden crown bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flowers that bloom in late summer and autumn. The plant has silvery, woolly leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid native habitat. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Helichrysum scutellifolium is often used in traditional medicine and is also popular in horticulture for its ornamental value.
