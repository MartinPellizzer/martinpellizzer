Hieracium ausserdorferi subsp. inexpertum

Hieracium ausserdorferi subsp.

inexpertum, commonly known as the Austrian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
