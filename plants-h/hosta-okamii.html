Hosta okamii

Hosta okamii, commonly known as the Okami hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 18 inches and produces small, fragrant white flowers in the summer. Its foliage is prized for its ornamental value in shaded gardens.

Hosta okamii prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.
