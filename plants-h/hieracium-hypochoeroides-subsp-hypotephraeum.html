Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. hypotephraeum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

hypotephraeum, commonly known as the small yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features basal leaves that are spoon-shaped and hairy, with a compact rosette structure. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clustered inflorescences, blooming from late spring to early summer.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and wind-pollinated reproductive system.
