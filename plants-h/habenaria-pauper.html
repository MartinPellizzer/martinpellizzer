Habenaria pauper

Habenaria pauper, commonly known as the poor lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and delicate, linear flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or grasslands. Its inflorescence consists of a spiral of small, white or pale pink flowers that are often fragrant.

Habenaria pauper is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
