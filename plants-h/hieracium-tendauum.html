Hieracium tendauum

Hieracium tendauum, commonly known as the Tendau hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets and white ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Hieracium tendauum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is considered a weed in some regions due to its rapid spread.
