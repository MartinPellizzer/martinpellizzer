Hymenocallis Adnata Var. Disticha

Hymenocallis adnata var. disticha is a striking tropical plant known for its elegant, trumpet-shaped flowers. Native to the Caribbean, this variety thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil. Its blooms, which range from white to pale yellow, are often adorned with a dark green or purple throat. The plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care. It is also a favored choice for container gardening and landscape design.
