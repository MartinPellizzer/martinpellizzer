Helianthemum cinereum var. lagascanum

Helianthemum cinereum var. lagascanum, commonly known as the Lagascan rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to rocky or sandy soils. The plant has aromatic, glandular leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
