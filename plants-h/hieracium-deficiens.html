Hieracium deficiens

Hieracium deficiens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, which is typical of the Asteraceae family. It has hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped.

Hieracium deficiens is native to Europe and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
