Hieracium prenanthoides subsp. jaquetianum

Hieracium prenanthoides subsp.

jaquetianum, commonly known as the Jaquet's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellowish flowers in a compact inflorescence.

The plant is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It has finely divided leaves and is known for its ability to form dense colonies.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
