Hesperis gariepina

Hesperis gariepina, commonly known as the Gariep bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in arid regions.

Hesperis gariepina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
