Hypecoum angustilobum

Hypecoum angustilobum, commonly known as narrow-lobed hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically deep purple or violet in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Hypecoum angustilobum is known for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
