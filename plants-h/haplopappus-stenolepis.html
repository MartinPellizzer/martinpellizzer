Haplopappus stenolepis

Haplopappus stenolepis, commonly known as the narrowleaf pappus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has linear, grass-like leaves. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in dense, spherical heads.
