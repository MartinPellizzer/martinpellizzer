Harpephyllum caffrum

Harpephyllum caffrum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features silvery-white, velvety leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, clustered flowers are usually white or pale pink and bloom in spring.

Harpephyllum caffrum is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
