Hyphaene multiformis subsp. odorata

Hyphaene multiformis subsp.

odorata, commonly known as the fragrant palm, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa. It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a fibrous, spiky structure that contains edible seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic appeal.
