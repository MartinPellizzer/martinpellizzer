Hieracium cillense

Hieracium cillense, commonly known as the Cilense Hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellowish to reddish flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a single head.

Hieracium cillense is often found in temperate regions and is considered a minor weed in some agricultural settings.
