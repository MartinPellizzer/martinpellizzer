Helichrysum praecinctum

Helichrysum praecinctum, commonly known as the golden marguerite or silver frost, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

The flowers are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
