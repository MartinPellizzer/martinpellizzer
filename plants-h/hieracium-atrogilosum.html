Hieracium atrogilosum

Hieracium atrogilosum, commonly known as the dark-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium atrogilosum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
