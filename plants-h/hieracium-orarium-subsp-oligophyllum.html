Hieracium orarium subsp. oligophyllum

Hieracium orarium subsp.

oligophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, with a central flowering stem bearing yellow composite flowers. Its leaves are usually lanceolate or linear, and the plant produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
