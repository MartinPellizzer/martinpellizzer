Haworthia rycroftiana

Haworthia rycroftiana, commonly known as the Rycroft's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
