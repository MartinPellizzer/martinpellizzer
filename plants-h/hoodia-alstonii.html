Hoodia alstonii

Hoodia alstonii, commonly known as the desert cabbage, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, spineless stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds.

Hoodia alstonii is also noted for its ability to thrive in extreme heat and drought conditions.
