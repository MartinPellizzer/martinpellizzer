Haworthia limifolia f. major

Haworthia limifolia f. major, commonly known as the rock zebra or zebra plant, is a succulent belonging to the Asphodelaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves with distinctive white or yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
