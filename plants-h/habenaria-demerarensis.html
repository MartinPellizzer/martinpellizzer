Habenaria demerarensis

Habenaria demerarensis, commonly known as the Demerara Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
