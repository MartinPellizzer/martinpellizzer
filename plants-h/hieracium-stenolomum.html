Hieracium stenolomum

Hieracium stenolomum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils in meadows, along roadsides, and in open woodlands.

Hieracium stenolomum is known for its ability to spread through both vegetative growth and seed production.
