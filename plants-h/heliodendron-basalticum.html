Heliodendron basalticum

Heliodendron basalticum, commonly known as the basaltic suntree, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions with basaltic soil.

The plant has a woody stem with a distinctive, rugged texture that adapts to its rocky environment. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern to maximize sunlight absorption.
