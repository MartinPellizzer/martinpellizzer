Hieracium philanthracinum

Hieracium philanthracinum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family (Compositae).

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy stems, typically found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It produces a single flower head that blooms from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium philanthracinum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth habit and ability to spread rapidly.
