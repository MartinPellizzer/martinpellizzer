Hymenophyllum latisorum

Hymenophyllum latisorum, commonly known as the broad-leaf filmy fern, is a small, delicate fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The fronds are usually pale green to silvery in color, with a leathery texture and a distinctive, finely veined appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is known for its ability to grow on rocks or tree trunks, making it a common epiphyte in humid forests.
