Hemerocallis × vomerense

Hemerocallis × vomerense, commonly known as the orange daylily, is a hybrid plant within the genus Hemerocallis.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange flowers that bloom in late summer and last for only one day, typical of the Hemerocallis family. The plant is native to China and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a sturdy stem that supports its showy blooms.
