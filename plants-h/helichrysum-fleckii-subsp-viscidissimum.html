Helichrysum fleckii subsp. viscidissimum

Helichrysum fleckii subsp.

viscidissimum, commonly known as the sticky helichrysum, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a sticky texture, and its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
