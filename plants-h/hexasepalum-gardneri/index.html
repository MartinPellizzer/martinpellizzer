Hexasepalum Gardneri

Hexasepalum gardneri is a rare and fascinating flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It belongs to the orchid family, known for its intricate and often showy blooms. This species is characterized by its unique six-lobed sepals, which give it its scientific name. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Hexasepalum gardneri is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
