Halimium stenophyllum

Halimium stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved honesty, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Halimium stenophyllum is also known for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and is often found in sandy or gravelly habitats.
