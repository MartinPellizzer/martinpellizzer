Hieracium lampreilema

Hieracium lampreilema, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually erect and somewhat hairy.

Hieracium lampreilema is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
