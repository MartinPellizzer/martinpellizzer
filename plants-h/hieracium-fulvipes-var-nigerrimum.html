Hieracium fulvipes var. nigerrimum

Hieracium fulvipes var. nigerrimum, commonly known as the blackstem hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are usually dark green with a rough texture, and the flowers are yellow with a distinctive, flattened shape.

It is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to colonize various environments.
