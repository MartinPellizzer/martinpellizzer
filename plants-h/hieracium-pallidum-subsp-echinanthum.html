Hieracium pallidum subsp. echinanthum

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

echinanthum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
