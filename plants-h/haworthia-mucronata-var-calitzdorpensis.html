Haworthia mucronata var. calitzdorpensis

Haworthia mucronata var. calitzdorpensis, commonly known as the Calitzdorp Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green to blue-gray in color, often with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear small, tubular white or pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
