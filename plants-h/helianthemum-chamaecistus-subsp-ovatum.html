Helianthemum chamaecistus subsp. ovatum

Helianthemum chamaecistus subsp.

ovatum, commonly known as the rock rose or summer rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy leaves adapted to arid environments.

The plant produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
