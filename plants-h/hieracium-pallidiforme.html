Hieracium pallidiforme

Hieracium pallidiforme, commonly known as the pale-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium pallidiforme is often found in open habitats and can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in certain regions.
