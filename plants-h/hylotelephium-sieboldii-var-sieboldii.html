Hylotelephium sieboldii var. sieboldii

Hylotelephium sieboldii var. sieboldii, commonly known as the Japanese stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to Japan.

It features fleshy, upright stems and dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that store water, aiding in its drought tolerance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both garden and landscape designs.
