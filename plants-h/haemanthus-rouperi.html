Haemanthus rouperi

Haemanthus rouperi, commonly known as the red-stemmed haemanthus, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a red or purple tinge along the midrib. The plant produces clusters of bright red flowers during the summer months, which are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms.
