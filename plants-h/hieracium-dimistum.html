Hieracium dimistum

Hieracium dimistum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has basal and alternate leaves that are generally hairy and have a rough texture. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its ability to spread vegetatively, it can become invasive in certain regions.
