Hieracium scopulinum

Hieracium scopulinum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its rosette growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence.

Hieracium scopulinum is adapted to cold climates and is a hardy perennial species.
