Hypericum harlingii

Hypericum harlingii, commonly known as the Harling's St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features woody stems and opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas.

Hypericum harlingii is notable for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
