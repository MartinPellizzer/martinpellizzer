Habenaria balansae

Habenaria balansae, commonly known as the Balansae orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

The flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.
