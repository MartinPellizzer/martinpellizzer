Habenaria myodes subsp. latipetala

Habenaria myodes subsp.

latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petalled marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia. It typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, where it thrives in moist, acidic soils.

The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are often pale green or yellowish in color. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and the flowers are characterized by their broad, spreading petals.

This subspecies is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
