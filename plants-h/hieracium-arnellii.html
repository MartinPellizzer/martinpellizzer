Hieracium arnellii

Hieracium arnellii, commonly known as the Arnold's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are usually basal, forming a rosette, and are covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, making it a notable presence in its native habitats.
