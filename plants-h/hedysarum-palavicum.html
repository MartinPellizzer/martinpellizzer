Hedysarum palavicum

Hedysarum palavicum, commonly known as the Caucasian vetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to temperate climates. Hedysarum palavicum is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, which enhances soil fertility.

It is sometimes cultivated for forage or as an ornamental plant.
