Humea macra

Humea macra, commonly known as the large-flowered humpback, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 cm in diameter. Its flowers typically display a combination of yellow and green hues with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Humea macra thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
