Hieracium hadiomeriforme

Hieracium hadiomeriforme, commonly known as the Hadiomeriforme hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces numerous yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It is a perennial species with a relatively short flowering period during the summer months.
