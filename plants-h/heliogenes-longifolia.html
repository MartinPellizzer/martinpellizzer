Heliogenes longifolia

Heliogenes longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf heliogenes, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sunlight. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often giving the plant a grass-like appearance.

Heliogenes longifolia is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate floral display and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
