Hieracium glanduliferum f. nigritelliforme

Hieracium glanduliferum f. nigritelliforme, commonly known as the black-tipped hawkweed, is a variant of the common hawkweed species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe, particularly found in grassy areas and meadows. The plant features yellow flowers with a distinctive black-tipped appearance on the petals, which sets it apart from other hawkweed varieties. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its overall appearance.

The species is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
