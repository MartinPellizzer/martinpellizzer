Hieracium cryptadenum subsp. cryptadenum

Hieracium cryptadenum subsp.

cryptadenum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface, and its stems are usually hairy as well. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller flower heads and more compact inflorescence compared to other hawkweed species.
