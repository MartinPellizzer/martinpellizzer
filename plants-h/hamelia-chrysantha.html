Hamelia chrysantha

Hamelia chrysantha, commonly known as flame bush, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces vibrant orange-red tubular flowers, which bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Hamelia chrysantha is also known for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
