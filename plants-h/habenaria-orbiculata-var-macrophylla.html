Habenaria orbiculata var. macrophylla

Habenaria orbiculata var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf marsh orchid, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and tall, branched inflorescences. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive three-lobed lip. This orchid thrives in wet, boggy habitats and is often found in swampy areas or along the edges of ponds.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to plant biodiversity.
