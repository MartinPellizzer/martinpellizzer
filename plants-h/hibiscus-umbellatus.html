Hibiscus umbellatus

Hibiscus umbellatus, commonly known as the tropical hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Hibiscus umbellatus thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
