Haplophyllum robustum

Haplophyllum robustum, commonly known as the robust rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Haplophyllum robustum is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
