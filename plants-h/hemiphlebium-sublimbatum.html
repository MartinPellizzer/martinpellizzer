Hemiphlebium sublimbatum

Hemiphlebium sublimbatum, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a perennial evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It features delicate, arching fronds with a silvery-white underside, giving it its characteristic appearance. The plant thrives in cool, shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its elegant foliage.

Hemiphlebium sublimbatum is relatively low maintenance and can be grown in containers or as part of a woodland garden.
