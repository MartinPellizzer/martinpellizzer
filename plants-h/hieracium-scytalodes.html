Hieracium scytalodes

Hieracium scytalodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often heart-shaped or oval in appearance. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grasslands.

It is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, making it somewhat invasive in certain regions.
