Hosta sieboldiana var. aureomarginata

Hosta sieboldiana var. aureomarginata, commonly known as the golden-margin hosta, is a hybrid variety originating from Japan.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a striking yellow margin that contrasts beautifully with its deep green center. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 18 inches, making it suitable for shaded garden spaces. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, thriving in cooler climates.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and is often used in landscape design for its visual appeal.
