Hieracium bjeluschae subsp. barathron

Hieracium bjeluschae subsp.

barathron, commonly known as the Balkan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing adaptability to harsh environments. Its subspecific classification highlights its distinct morphological and genetic traits compared to other members of the Hieracium species.
