Hibiscus rosa-sinensis var. vivicans

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis var. vivicans, commonly known as the Vivid Hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and plenty of sunlight.

This variety is popular for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to container gardening.
