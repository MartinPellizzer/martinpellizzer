Hackelia deflexa var. deflexa

Hackelia deflexa var. deflexa, commonly known as the flexible honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with upright, branching stems that often have a reddish tinge. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in dry, rocky soils.
