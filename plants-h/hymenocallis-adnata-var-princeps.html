Hymenocallis adnata var. princeps

Hymenocallis adnata var. princeps, commonly known as the Prince's Spider Lily, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It features showy, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, narrow, funnel-shaped corolla. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is typically grown as a bulbous perennial.

The flowers are nocturnal, opening at night and closing in the morning, which is a unique botanical characteristic.
