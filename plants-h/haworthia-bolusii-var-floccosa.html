Haworthia bolusii var. floccosa

Haworthia bolusii var. floccosa, commonly known as the woolly Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are covered in soft, white to pale blue tufts of hair, giving it a unique, almost fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative houseplant.
