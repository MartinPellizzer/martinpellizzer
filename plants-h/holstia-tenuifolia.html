Holstia tenuifolia

Holstia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf holstia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It is a perennial herb that produces seeds in small, rounded heads, and it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
