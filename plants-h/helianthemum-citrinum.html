Helianthemum citrinum

Helianthemum citrinum, commonly known as the yellow rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to survive in dry, rocky environments.

Helianthemum citrinum is often found in coastal areas and is well-suited to Mediterranean climates.
