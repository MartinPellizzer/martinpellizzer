Helichrysum gracilifolium

Helichrysum gracilifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its flowers are long-lasting and can be used in floral arrangements, adding both ornamental and practical value.
