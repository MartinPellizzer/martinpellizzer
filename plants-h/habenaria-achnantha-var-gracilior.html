Habenaria achnantha var. gracilior

Habenaria achnantha var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender-lobed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its inflorescence consists of a single spike bearing several flowers, each with three sepals and three petals.

The species is noted for its unique morphology and is often found in humid tropical climates.
