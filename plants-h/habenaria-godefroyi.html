Habenaria godefroyi

Habenaria godefroyi, commonly known as the Godefroy's butterfly orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white in color. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories, and requires high humidity and well-drained soil. Its roots are fibrous, and it produces a single spike of flowers from the base of the plant.

The orchid is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral structure.
