Hieracium saxetanum

Hieracium saxetanum, commonly known as the Saxtane hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, where it forms dense mats with basal leaves. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
