Hieracium faurei

Hieracium faurei, commonly known as the Faure's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of broadly ovate to lanceolate leaves and produces yellow flowers in dense clusters. Native to southern Africa, this species thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats. The plant has a fibrous root system and exhibits a compact growth form.

It is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
