Hieracium viridicaniceps

Hieracium viridicaniceps, commonly known as the green-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features basal and alternate leaves that are typically lance-shaped and hairy. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
