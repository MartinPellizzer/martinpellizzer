Hieracium × sciadophorum

Hieracium × sciadophorum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and its ability to produce numerous yellow to orange flowering heads. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world, often becoming invasive in certain regions. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and natural settings.
