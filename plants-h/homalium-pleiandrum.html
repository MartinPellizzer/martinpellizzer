Homalium pleiandrum

Homalium pleiandrum, commonly known as the red-flowered holly.

It is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. The plant features opposite, simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its most striking feature is the clusters of bright red, fleshy berries that appear in late summer.

Homalium pleiandrum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in warm, humid climates.
