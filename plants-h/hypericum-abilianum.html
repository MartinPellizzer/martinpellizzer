Hypericum abilianum

Hypericum abilianum, commonly known as the flame root or yellow hypericum, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its woody stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and the presence of yellow-orange stamens in its flowers.

Hypericum abilianum is often found in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in open woodlands.
