Hypericum decaisneanum

Hypericum decaisneanum, commonly known as the Decaisne's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs known for their yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. Its flowers are small, five-petaled, and usually yellow or orange, with prominent stamens.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
