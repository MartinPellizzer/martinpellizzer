Helianthemum triflorum

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. Native to southern Europe and North Africa, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
