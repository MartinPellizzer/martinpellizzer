Habenaria reticulata

Habenaria reticulata, commonly known as the netted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, net-like patterns on its petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Habenaria reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
