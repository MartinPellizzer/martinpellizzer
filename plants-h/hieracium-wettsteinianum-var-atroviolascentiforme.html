Hieracium wettsteinianum var. atroviolascentiforme

Hieracium wettsteinianum var. atroviolascentiforme, commonly known as the dark yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of leaves. The plant produces yellow to orange composite flowers, which are typically arranged in a single terminal head. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a hairy surface, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is notable for its dark yellow flower color, which sets it apart from other members of the Hieracium genus.
