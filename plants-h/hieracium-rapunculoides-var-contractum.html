Hieracium rapunculoides var. contractum

Hieracium rapunculoides var. contractum, commonly known as the contracted hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has a hairy stem and leaves with a rough texture.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
