Hieracium punctilliferum

Hieracium punctilliferum, commonly known as the dotted hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs.

Hieracium punctilliferum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it thrives in temperate climates.
