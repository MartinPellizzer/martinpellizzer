Halimus crystallinus

Halimus crystallinus, commonly known as the crystal bush, is a succulent plant native to arid regions.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in translucent, waxy leaves that reflect sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow during the summer months. Its unique structure allows it to store water efficiently, adapting to harsh desert conditions.

Halimus crystallinus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in low-maintenance gardens.
