Helichrysum outeniquense

Helichrysum outeniquense, commonly known as the Cape goldring, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble gold rings. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts to full sun and dry conditions.

Helichrysum outeniquense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
