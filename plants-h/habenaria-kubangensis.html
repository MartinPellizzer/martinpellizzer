Habenaria kubangensis

Habenaria kubangensis, commonly known as the Kubang Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Borneo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, preferring rich, organic soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
