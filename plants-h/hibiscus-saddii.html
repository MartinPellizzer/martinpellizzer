Hibiscus saddii

Hibiscus saddii, commonly known as the Socotra hibiscus, is a unique plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range from pink to red in color. The plant has distinctive, palmate leaves that are lobed and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.

Hibiscus saddii is an important component of the local flora and is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
