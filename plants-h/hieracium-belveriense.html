Hieracium belveriense, commonly known as the Belver's hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to specific regions in Europe. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has slender stems and basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
