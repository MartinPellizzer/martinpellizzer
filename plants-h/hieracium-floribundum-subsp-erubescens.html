Hieracium floribundum subsp. erubescens

Hieracium floribundum subsp.

erubescens, commonly known as the red-centred hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

The plant features upright stems with basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are yellow with a distinctive red center, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
