Hieracium pseudostellipilum

Hieracium pseudostellipilum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has a taproot system and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny areas.
