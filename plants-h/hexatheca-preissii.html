Hexatheca preissii

Hexatheca preissii, commonly known as the Sydney aster, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often in open woodlands or heathlands. Its showy, daisy-like flowers are usually blue to purple in color, and it blooms during the spring and summer months.
