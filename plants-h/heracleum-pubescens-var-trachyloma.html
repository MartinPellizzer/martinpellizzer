Heracleum pubescens var. trachyloma

Heracleum pubescens var. trachyloma, commonly known as the European cow parsnip, is a biennial or perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered with short, stiff hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in meadows.
