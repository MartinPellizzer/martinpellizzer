Hydrangea villosa var. mairei

Hydrangea villosa var. mairei, commonly known as the Maire hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink or blue depending on soil pH. The plant has ovate to elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its ability to produce abundant blooms in the summer months.
