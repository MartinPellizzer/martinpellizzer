Habenaria narcissiflora

Habenaria narcissiflora, commonly known as the narcissus orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, nodding flowers that resemble those of the narcissus flower. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. It has linear to lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive lip.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
