Halia

Halia, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple ray flowers and spiny, leaf-like bracts that surround the central disk. Native to coastal areas of the Mediterranean, it thrives in saline and sandy soils. The plant is often found in maritime environments, where it tolerates salt spray and strong winds.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and coastal landscaping.
