Horsfieldia glabrescens

Horsfieldia glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, fleshy rhizome and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

The orchid is noted for its unique floral structure and is often found in lowland to mid-elevation areas.
