Helanthium zombiense

Helanthium zombiense, commonly known as the zombie weed, is a rare and unusual plant species characterized by its dark, velvety foliage and bioluminescent properties.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near decomposing organic matter. The plant's unique ability to emit a faint blue glow at night has led to its association with eerie, otherworldly landscapes. Its flowers, which resemble small, twisted skulls, bloom only during the full moon.

Despite its ominous appearance, it plays a vital role in nutrient cycling in its ecosystem.
