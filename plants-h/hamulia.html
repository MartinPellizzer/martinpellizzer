Hamulia

Hamulia, commonly known as the goldenrod, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the Americas and includes species such as Hamulia filiformis, which is often found in wetland environments. Hamulia species are also valued for their ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
