Hieracium pellucidum var. pulcherrimum

Hieracium pellucidum var. pulcherrimum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are usually basal, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
