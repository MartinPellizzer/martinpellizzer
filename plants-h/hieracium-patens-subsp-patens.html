Hieracium patens subsp. patens

Hieracium patens subsp.

patens, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of spoon-shaped leaves and yellow flowers arranged in a composite head.

The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread vegetatively, making it a persistent weed in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.
