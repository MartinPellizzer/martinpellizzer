Hydrangea mutabilis

Hydrangea mutabilis, commonly known as the panicled hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that change color from white to pink to red as the season progresses. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade. Its flowers are composed of sterile, showy petals surrounding smaller, fertile flowers.

Hydrangea mutabilis is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
