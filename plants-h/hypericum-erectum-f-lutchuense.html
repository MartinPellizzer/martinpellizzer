Hypericum erectum f. lutchuense

Hypericum erectum f. lutchuense, commonly known as the Lutchuense St. John's Wort, is a botanical variant of the common St. John's Wort.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its erect growth habit and yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, orange-red berries. It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
