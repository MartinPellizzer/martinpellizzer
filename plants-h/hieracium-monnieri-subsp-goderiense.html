Hieracium monnieri subsp. goderiense

Hieracium monnieri subsp.

goderiense, commonly known as the Alpine Groundsel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high elevations.

The plant has basal leaves that are usually lanceolate or ovate, with rough, hairy surfaces. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in harsh, cold environments.
