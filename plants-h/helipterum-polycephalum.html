Helipterum polycephalum

Helipterum polycephalum, commonly known as the clustered helipterum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous annual with hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense heads, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Helipterum polycephalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
