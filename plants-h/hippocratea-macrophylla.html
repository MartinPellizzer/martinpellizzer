Hippocratea macrophylla

Hippocratea macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf hippocratea, is a tropical plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
