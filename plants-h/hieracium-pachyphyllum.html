Hieracium pachyphyllum

Hieracium pachyphyllum, commonly known as the thick-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium pachyphyllum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
