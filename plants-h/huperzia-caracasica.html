Huperzia caracasica

Huperzia caracasica, commonly known as the Caracas huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, linear leaves that are often clustered at the base of the plant. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are located on the underside of the leaves.

Huperzia caracasica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
