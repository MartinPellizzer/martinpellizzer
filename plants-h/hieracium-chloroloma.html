Hieracium chloroloma

Hieracium chloroloma, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. The plant has bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped with hairy surfaces. It is a perennial herb that can grow up to 30 centimeters tall.

Hieracium chloroloma is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
