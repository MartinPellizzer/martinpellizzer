Hyacinthorchis variabilis

Hyacinthorchis variabilis, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Hyacinthorchis variabilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
