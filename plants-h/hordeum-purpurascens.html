Hordeum purpurascens

Hordeum purpurascens, commonly known as purple barley, is a species of grass native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking purple inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its grains are smaller and more compact compared to common barley, and it is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop.

Hordeum purpurascens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
