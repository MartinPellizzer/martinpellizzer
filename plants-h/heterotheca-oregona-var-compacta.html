Heterotheca oregona var. compacta

Heterotheca oregona var. compacta, commonly known as the Oregon fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

The compact growth form of this variety distinguishes it from other forms of Heterotheca oregona.
