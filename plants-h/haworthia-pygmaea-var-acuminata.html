Haworthia pygmaea var. acuminata

Haworthia pygmaea var. acuminata, commonly known as the needle Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green with white or yellow tubercles, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor or rock garden settings.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
