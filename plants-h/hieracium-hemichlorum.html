Hieracium hemichlorum

Hieracium hemichlorum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features upright stems with basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is an herbaceous perennial that thrives in temperate climates and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
