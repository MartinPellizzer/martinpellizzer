Hippeastrum pulchrum

Hippeastrum pulchrum, commonly known as the Splendour Amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum pulchrum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
