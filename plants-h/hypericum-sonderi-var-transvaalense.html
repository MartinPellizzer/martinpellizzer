Hypericum sonderi var. transvaalense

Hypericum sonderi var. transvaalense, commonly known as the Transvaal fireweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Hypericaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, and is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It has small, opposite leaves and produces berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
