Heliotropium ternatum var. fumana

Heliotropium ternatum var. fumana, commonly known as the smoky heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its flowers open in the morning and close in the evening, a behavior known as nyctinasty.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
