Homalomena humilis var. undulata

Homalomena humilis var. undulata, commonly known as the wavy-leaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, wavy-edged leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, adding a delicate ornamental touch. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
