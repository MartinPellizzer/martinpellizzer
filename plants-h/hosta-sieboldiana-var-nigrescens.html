Hosta sieboldiana var. nigrescens

Hosta sieboldiana var. nigrescens, commonly known as the Black Velvet Hosta, is a striking perennial plant native to Japan.

It features broad, dark green leaves that turn deep purple to black in the autumn, adding dramatic visual interest to gardens. The plant produces tall, slender stems with white to pale lavender flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shaded garden beds and woodland settings.
