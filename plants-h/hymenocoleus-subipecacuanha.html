Hymenocoleus subipecacuanha

Hymenocoleus subipecacuanha, commonly known as the slender leafed daisies, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and unique morphology, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
