Hieracium subrecurvum

Hieracium subrecurvum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are usually hairy as well.

Hieracium subrecurvum is an herbaceous perennial that thrives in temperate climates.
