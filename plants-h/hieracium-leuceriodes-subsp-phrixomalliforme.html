Hieracium leuceriodes subsp. phrixomalliforme

Hieracium leuceriodes subsp.

phrixomalliforme, commonly known as the white-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, reaching heights of up to 60 cm.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces yellow flowers in late summer. Its inflorescence is a flat-topped cluster, with small, daisy-like blooms.

It is characterized by its white to pale yellow petals and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
