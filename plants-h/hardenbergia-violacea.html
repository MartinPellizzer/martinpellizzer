Hardenbergia violacea

Hardenbergia violacea, commonly known as the blue-flowered burr legume, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
