Hieracium lachenalii subsp. schellenbergense

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

schellenbergense, commonly known as the Schellenberg's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowering heads and basal rosettes of leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in compact clusters.

This subspecies is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and morphological traits that set it apart from other members of the Hieracium genus.
