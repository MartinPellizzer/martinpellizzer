Habenaria heptadactyla

Habenaria heptadactyla, commonly known as the seven-finger orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has a slender, erect stem with several leaves along its length. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
