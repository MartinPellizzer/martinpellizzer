Habenaria buntingii

Habenaria buntingii, commonly known as the Bunting's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its roots are fibrous, and it produces a single, upright stem with several showy, greenish-yellow flowers.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
