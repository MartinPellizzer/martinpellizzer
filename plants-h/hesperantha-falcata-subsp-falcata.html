Hesperantha falcata subsp. falcata

Hesperantha falcata subsp.

falcata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hesperantha, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight curvature.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
