Hieracium cuneolatoides

Hieracium cuneolatoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in composite heads, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

It is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
