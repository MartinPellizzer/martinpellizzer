Hieracium uistense

Hieracium uistense, commonly known as the Wistense hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellowish to reddish flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has hairy stems and leaves, which help reduce water loss in its often dry environments.

Hieracium uistense is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
