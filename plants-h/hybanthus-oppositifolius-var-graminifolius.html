Hybanthus oppositifolius var. graminifolius

Hybanthus oppositifolius var. graminifolius, commonly known as the slender leaf hybanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its opposite, narrow, grass-like leaves that give it its varietal name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming during the wet season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany and horticulture.
