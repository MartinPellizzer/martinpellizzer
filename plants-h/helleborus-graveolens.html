Helleborus graveolens

Helleborus graveolens, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are basal and palmately lobed. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Helleborus graveolens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom during the holiday season.
