Hypericum yezoanum var. risiriense

Hypericum yezoanum var. risiriense, commonly known as the Yezo hypericum, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features small, rounded leaves that are dark green and glossy, with a dense, bushy growth habit. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are borne in clusters at the ends of the stems. It is characterized by its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
