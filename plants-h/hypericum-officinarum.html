Hypericum officinarum

Hypericum officinarum, commonly known as St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, which are often covered in small, orange or reddish glands. The plant typically grows in sunny, well-drained soils and can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and oval-shaped with a slightly hairy surface.

Hypericum officinarum is known for its medicinal properties, particularly for its use in treating mild to moderate depression.
