Hieracium stenopum

Hieracium stenopum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium stenopum is often found in sunny habitats and can spread through rhizomes or by seed dispersal.
