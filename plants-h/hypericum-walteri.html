Hypericum walteri

Hypericum walteri, commonly known as Walter's St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens.

Botanically, it belongs to the genus Hypericum, which is part of the family Clusiaceae.
