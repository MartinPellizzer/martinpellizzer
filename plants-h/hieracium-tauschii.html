Hieracium tauschii

Hieracium tauschii, commonly known as the Tausch's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, and its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively.
