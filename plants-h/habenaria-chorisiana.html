Habenaria chorisiana

Habenaria chorisiana, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical forests.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
