Herpestis stellarioides var. pumila

Herpestis stellarioides var. pumila, commonly known as the dwarf starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically reaches heights of less than 10 centimeters and features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly toothed margin.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
