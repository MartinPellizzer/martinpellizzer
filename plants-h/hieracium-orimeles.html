Hieracium orimeles

Hieracium orimeles, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils at high altitudes. It features basal rosettes of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers with a distinctive central disc.

Hieracium orimeles is often found in open, sunny environments and is adapted to cold climates.
