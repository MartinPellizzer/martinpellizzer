Hieracium kernerianum

Hieracium kernerianum, commonly known as the Kerner's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas. The plant produces showy yellow to orange composite flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Hieracium kernerianum is an herbaceous perennial that thrives in temperate climates.
