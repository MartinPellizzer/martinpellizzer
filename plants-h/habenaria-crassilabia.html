Habenaria crassilabia

Habenaria crassilabia, commonly known as the broad-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with several leaves and produces clusters of showy, violet to purple flowers. The plant has a complex root system and is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers have a distinctive, broad lip that is often more prominent than the other petals.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
