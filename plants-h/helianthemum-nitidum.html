Helianthemum nitidum

Helianthemum nitidum, commonly known as the smooth rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It features small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for Mediterranean-style landscapes.
