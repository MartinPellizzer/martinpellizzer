Helianthopsis Lanata

Helianthopsis lanata is a unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America. It is known for its hairy, woolly leaves and bright yellow blooms that resemble the sun. This species thrives in harsh, dry environments and is adapted to survive with minimal water. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare and is often overlooked in favor of more common plants.
