Heliotropium monostachyum var. ovatum

Heliotropium monostachyum var. ovatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its densely hairy, ovate leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Its growth habit is usually upright, with multiple stems branching from the base.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
