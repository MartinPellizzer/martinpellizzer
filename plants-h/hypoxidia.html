Hypoxidia

Hypoxidia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family.

These plants are native to various regions of Africa and are characterized by their silver-gray foliage. Hypoxidia species typically have small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. They are often found in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with their succulent leaves.

The genus includes several species, some of which are used in traditional medicine.
