Helianthemum oelandicum var. canum

Helianthemum oelandicum var. canum, commonly known as the rough sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe.

It typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland dunes. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, blooming in early summer. Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture.

This species is well adapted to harsh environments and plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils.
