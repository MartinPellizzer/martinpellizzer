Hieracium oroglaucum

Hieracium oroglaucum, commonly known as alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, thriving in high-altitude environments. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are generally hairy and lance-shaped, adapted to withstand cold and arid conditions.

Hieracium oroglaucum is known for its hardy nature and is often found in meadows and alpine tundra.
