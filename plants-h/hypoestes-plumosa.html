Hypoestes plumosa

Hypoestes plumosa, commonly known as the rainbow plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with slender, branching stems. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often hidden beneath the vibrant bracts.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
