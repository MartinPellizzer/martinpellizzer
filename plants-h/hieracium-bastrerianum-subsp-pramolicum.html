Hieracium bastrerianum subsp. pramolicum

Hieracium bastrerianum subsp.

pramolicum, commonly known as the Pramolic hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in the summer months.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in nutrient-rich soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare species in certain regions of Europe.
