Hieracium bifidum subsp. poliocranellum

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

poliocranellum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and basal rosettes of narrowly lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the Compositae family. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
