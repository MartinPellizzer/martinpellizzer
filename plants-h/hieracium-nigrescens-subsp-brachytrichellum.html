Hieracium nigrescens subsp. brachytrichellum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

brachytrichellum, commonly known as the short-haired hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy foliage.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It features basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered with short, stiff hairs.

The flowers are yellow, arranged in a single head, and are surrounded by bracts that are often glandular and hairy.
