Helleborine × hybrida

Helleborine × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid helleborine, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen rhizomatous growth habit.

It produces showy, often fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant features broad, strap-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is native to parts of Europe and Asia.

Helleborine × hybrida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
