Hypericum hyssopifolium var. callithyrsum

Hypericum hyssopifolium var. callithyrsum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features woody stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
