Hopea nutans

Hopea nutans, commonly known as the slender hopea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture-making.

Hopea nutans plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
