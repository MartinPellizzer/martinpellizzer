Hosta lancifolia f. polycarpellata

Hosta lancifolia f. polycarpellata, commonly known as the lanceleaf hosta with multiple carpels, is a variant of the lanceleaf hosta.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a subtle variegation. The plant is native to eastern Asia and thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-white in color.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to garden conditions.
