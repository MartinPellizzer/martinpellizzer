Hymenostephium mucronatum

Hymenostephium mucronatum, commonly known as the sharp-pointed hymenostephium, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and typically have five petals.
