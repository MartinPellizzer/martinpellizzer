Hydrangea chinensis var. grosseserrata

Hydrangea chinensis var. grosseserrata, commonly known as the serrated leaf hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features large, palmately lobed leaves with serrated edges, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, cone-shaped flower clusters that range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to change flower color, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.
