Haageocereus Pseudomelanostele Subsp. Aureispinus

Haageocereus pseudomelanostele subsp. aureispinus is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of northern Mexico. Known for its vibrant yellow spines and unique growth form, this plant is a subject of interest among botanists and cactus enthusiasts. It thrives in rocky soils and full sun, adapting to extreme desert conditions with remarkable resilience. Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species.
