Hermansia setipes

Hermansia setipes, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Hermansia setipes is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of tropical flora.
