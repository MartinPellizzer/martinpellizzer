Hieracium dertosense subsp. carolipauanum

Hieracium dertosense subsp.

carolipauanum, commonly known as the Carolipauan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. Its leaves are lanceolate, hairy, and have a serrated margin, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and is often found in temperate climates.
