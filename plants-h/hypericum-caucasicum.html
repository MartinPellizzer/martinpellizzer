Hypericum caucasicum

Hypericum caucasicum, commonly known as Caucasian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Hypericum caucasicum is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
