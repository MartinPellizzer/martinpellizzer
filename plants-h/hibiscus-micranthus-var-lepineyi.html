Hibiscus micranthus var. lepineyi

Hibiscus micranthus var. lepineyi, commonly known as the Lepine's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features palmate leaves with a velvety texture and a short, sturdy stem. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hibiscus micranthus var. lepineyi is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
