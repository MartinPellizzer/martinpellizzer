Hieracium altenahrense

Hieracium altenahrense, commonly known as the Altenahr hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat, composite head.

It is often found in areas with well-drained soil and partial sunlight.
