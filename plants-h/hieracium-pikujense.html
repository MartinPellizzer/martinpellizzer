Hieracium pikujense

Hieracium pikujense, commonly known as the Pikujan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of Peru.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky soils, thriving in cool and temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from yellow to orange.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Hieracium pikujense is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
