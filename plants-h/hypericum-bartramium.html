Hypericum bartramium

Hypericum bartramium, commonly known as the Florida St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows in wetlands, swampy areas, and along stream banks. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with orange stamens, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Hypericum bartramium is also known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
