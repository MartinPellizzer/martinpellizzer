Helipterum dregeanum

Helipterum dregeanum, commonly known as the yellow helipterum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow ray flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, where it adapts well to arid conditions.
