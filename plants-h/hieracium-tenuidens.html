Hieracium tenuidens

Hieracium tenuidens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in open, rocky habitats at high altitudes, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Hieracium tenuidens is a perennial species that plays a role in stabilizing soil in its harsh mountainous environment.
