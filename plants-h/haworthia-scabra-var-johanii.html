Haworthia scabra var. johanii

Haworthia scabra var. johanii, commonly known as the Johanii Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features short, thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish-gray hue and prominent white tubercles. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
