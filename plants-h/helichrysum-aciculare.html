Helichrysum aciculare

Helichrysum aciculare, commonly known as the needle helichrysum, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves and small, yellowish flowers that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

Its aromatic foliage and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
