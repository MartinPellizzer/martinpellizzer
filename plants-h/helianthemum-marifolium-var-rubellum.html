Helianthemum marifolium var. rubellum

Helianthemum marifolium var. rubellum, commonly known as the red-flowered rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, evergreen leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a dense, woolly texture. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range from pink to deep red, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens.

Its compact form and vibrant floral display make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
