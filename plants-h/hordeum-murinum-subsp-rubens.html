Hordeum murinum subsp. rubens

Hordeum murinum subsp.

rubens, commonly known as red brome, is a grass species native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and spikelets that are often tinged with red or purple hues.

The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that contribute to its adaptability and spread in various environments.
