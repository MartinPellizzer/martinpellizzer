Hemitelia horrida var. hookeri

Hemitelia horrida var. hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's hemitelia, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its rough, glandular leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal or sandy soils and is adapted to saline environments. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
