Hordeum stenostachys

Hordeum stenostachys, commonly known as the narrow-stalked barley, is a species of grass native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves, typically growing in dry, arid environments. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. It is a member of the Poaceae family and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
