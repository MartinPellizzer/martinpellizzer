Hieracium panaeoliforme

Hieracium panaeoliforme, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are typically basal, with a rough, hairy texture, and the stems are usually upright.

Hieracium panaeoliforme is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
