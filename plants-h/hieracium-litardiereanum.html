Hieracium litardiereanum

Hieracium litardiereanum, commonly known as the Litardiere's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads made up of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer.

The leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
