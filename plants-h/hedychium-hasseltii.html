Hedychium hasseltii

Hedychium hasseltii, commonly known as the flame spike ginger, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, orange-red spadix surrounded by a green, leaf-like bract, which gives it its dramatic appearance. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in summer, making it a popular ornamental species. Hedychium hasseltii thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and landscapes.
