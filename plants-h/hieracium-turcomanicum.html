Hieracium turcomanicum

Hieracium turcomanicum, commonly known as the Turkestan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clustered, yellowish to reddish flower heads. The plant typically has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, which are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas, and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates.

Hieracium turcomanicum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
