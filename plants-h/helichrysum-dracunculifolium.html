Helichrysum dracunculifolium

Helichrysum dracunculifolium, commonly known as the golden crown or dragon's tail, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. The plant produces showy, golden-yellow flower heads that resemble small crowns, which are particularly striking when in bloom. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a versatile addition to various garden settings.
