Hylotelephium angustum var. longipedunculum

Hylotelephium angustum var. longipedunculum, commonly known as the narrowleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a versatile addition to both garden and landscape designs.
