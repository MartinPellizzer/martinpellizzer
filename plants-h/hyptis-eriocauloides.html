Hyptis eriocauloides

Hyptis eriocauloides, commonly known as silverleaf hyptis, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Hyptis eriocauloides is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.
