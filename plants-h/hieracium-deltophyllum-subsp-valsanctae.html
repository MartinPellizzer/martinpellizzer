Hieracium deltophyllum subsp. valsanctae

Hieracium deltophyllum subsp.

valsanctae, commonly known as the Saint Valentine's hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and specific floral morphology. It thrives in rocky and grassy habitats at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic plant species.
