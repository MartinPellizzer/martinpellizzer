Hesperis Leptocarpa

Hesperis leptocarpa is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. Known for its showy, fragrant blooms, it thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky soil. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
