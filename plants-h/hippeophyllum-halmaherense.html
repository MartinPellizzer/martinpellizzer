Hippeophyllum halmaherense

Hippeophyllum halmaherense, commonly known as the Halmahera starling tree, is a tropical plant native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in lowland rainforests. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and restricted geographic distribution.
