Holmskioldia spinescens

Holmskioldia spinescens, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has woody stems with spines, which give it its scientific name. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with each leaflet being ovate to elliptical in shape.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
