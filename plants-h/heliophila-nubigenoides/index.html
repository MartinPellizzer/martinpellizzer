Heliophila Nubigenoides

Heliophila nubigenoides is a rare and enigmatic plant known for its unique ability to thrive in high-altitude environments. This species is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that open only in direct sunlight. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly understood by botanists due to its elusive nature and limited geographic range. Found primarily in isolated mountain regions, it is often mistaken for a hallucinogenic plant by local communities. Its study offers valuable insights into plant adaptation and survival in extreme conditions.
