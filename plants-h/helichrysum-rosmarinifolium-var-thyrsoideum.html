Helichrysum rosmarinifolium var. thyrsoideum

Helichrysum rosmarinifolium var. thyrsoideum, commonly known as the golden everlasting or silver thyme.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their longevity and vibrant color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a versatile plant for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
