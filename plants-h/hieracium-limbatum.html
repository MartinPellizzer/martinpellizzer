Hieracium limbatum

Hieracium limbatum, commonly known as the broad-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features basal rosettes of broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in a composite head. It is characterized by its hairy stems and can grow up to 30 cm in height.

Hieracium limbatum is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates.
