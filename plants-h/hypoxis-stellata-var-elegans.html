Hypoxis stellata var. elegans

Hypoxis stellata var. elegans, commonly known as the star-shaped yellowstar, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late summer, arranged in a star-like pattern, which gives it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and low maintenance requirements.
