Hieracium atratulum

Hieracium atratulum, commonly known as the black hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features dark green, hairy leaves and produces small, purple to brown flowers that bloom in the summer months. Hieracium atratulum is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

Its growth habit and appearance make it a distinctive member of the hawkweed genus.
