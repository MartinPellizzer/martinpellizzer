Helichrysum leontonyx

Helichrysum leontonyx, commonly known as the golden marguerite or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces numerous small, tubular florets arranged in a compact cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Helichrysum leontonyx is also known for its drought tolerance and long-lasting blooms, which can persist for several months.
