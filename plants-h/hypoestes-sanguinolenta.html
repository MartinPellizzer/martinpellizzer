Hypoestes sanguinolenta

Hypoestes sanguinolenta, commonly known as the red-stemmed wonder plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant has succulent-like leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped, and it thrives in well-drained soil with bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its vibrant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Hypoestes sanguinolenta is also valued for its ability to produce offsets, making it easy to propagate.
