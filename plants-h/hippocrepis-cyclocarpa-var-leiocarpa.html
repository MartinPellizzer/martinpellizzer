Hippocrepis cyclocarpa var. leiocarpa

Hippocrepis cyclocarpa var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the white helmet orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive white, helmet-shaped flowers with a prominent, elongated spur. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting specific pollinators such as bees and moths.
