Hoya mindorensis subsp. sarawakensis

Hoya mindorensis subsp.

sarawakensis, commonly known as the Sarawak Hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Borneo. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and clusters of waxy, waxy, white to pale green flowers.

The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
