Hibiscus meyeri subsp. meyeri

Hibiscus meyeri subsp.

meyeri, commonly known as the Meyer hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red.

The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth, glossy surface. Hibiscus meyeri subsp.

meyeri thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
