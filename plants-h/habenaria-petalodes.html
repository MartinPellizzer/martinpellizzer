Habenaria petalodes

Habenaria petalodes, commonly known as the Orchid of the Forest, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant features a complex structure with three sepals and three petals, with the lateral petals often being more prominent. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid, shaded environments where it thrives.

This orchid is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
