Hieracium detonsum transbotnicum

Hieracium detonsum transbotnicum, commonly known as the Transbotnic Hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of serrated, hairy leaves and produces yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disk florets. The plant is native to specific regions of Transbotnia and thrives in moist, calcareous soils. It is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes and has a low-growing habit.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native habitat.
