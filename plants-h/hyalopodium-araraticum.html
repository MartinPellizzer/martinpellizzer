Hyalopodium araraticum

Hyalopodium araraticum, commonly known as the Araraticum hyalopodium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, blade-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is typically found in grasslands and open areas with well-drained soil.
