Habenaria pubipetala

Habenaria pubipetala, commonly known as the hairy lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, hairy sepals and petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and grasslands. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinct lip that is often hairy.

Habenaria pubipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
