Hieracium cincinnellum

Hieracium cincinnellum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish flower heads. The plant typically has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.

Hieracium cincinnellum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
