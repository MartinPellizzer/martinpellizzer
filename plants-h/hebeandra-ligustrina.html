Hebeandra ligustrina

Hebeandra ligustrina, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hebe, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers a shady to partially sunny environment. It is often found in forested areas and along streambanks.

Hebeandra ligustrina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
