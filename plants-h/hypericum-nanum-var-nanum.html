Hypericum nanum var. nanum

Hypericum nanum var. nanum, commonly known as creeping St. John's wort, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in a variety of environmental conditions.
