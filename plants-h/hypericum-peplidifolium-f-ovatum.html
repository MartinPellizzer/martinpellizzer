Hypericum peplidifolium f. ovatum

Hypericum peplidifolium f. ovatum, commonly known as the ovate-stemmed St. John's wort, is a variant of the St. John's wort genus native to the western United States.

This plant is characterized by its ovate-shaped leaves and compact growth habit, making it a desirable ornamental species. It typically features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, preferring dry to moist conditions.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to native plant collections and ecological landscapes.
