Hippochaete × trachyodon

Hippochaete × trachyodon, commonly known as the hybrid dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of yellow ray florets and yellow disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas and has a creeping growth habit with a rosette of basal leaves. Its seeds are small, wind-dispersed capsules that form a pappus for easy aerial transport.

The botanical features of this hybrid make it a common weed in various regions, often found in lawns and along roadsides.
