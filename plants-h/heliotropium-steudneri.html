Heliotropium steudneri

Heliotropium steudneri, commonly known as the dwarf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive scent. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Heliotropium steudneri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
