Hibiscus stenophyllus var. stenophyllus

Hibiscus stenophyllus var. stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hibiscus, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
