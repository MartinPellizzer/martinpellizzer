Hubertia pleiantha

Hubertia pleiantha, commonly known as the yellow hubertia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Hubertia pleiantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
