Hypoxis grandis

Hypoxis grandis, commonly known as the African yellow star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow petals and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates.

Hypoxis grandis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
