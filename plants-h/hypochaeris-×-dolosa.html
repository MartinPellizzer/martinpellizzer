Hypochaeris × dolosa

Hypochaeris × dolosa, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers, which consist of numerous small florets arranged in a disk shape. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields, preferring full sun and well-drained soil. It has basal leaves that are oblanceolate to elliptic in shape, with hairy surfaces.

Hypochaeris × dolosa is often considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
