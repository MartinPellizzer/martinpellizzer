Hypericum breviflorum

Hypericum breviflorum, commonly known as the short-flowered St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in woodland edges and open fields.

Hypericum breviflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
