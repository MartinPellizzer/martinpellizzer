Hieracium juranum subsp. cichoriaceum

Hieracium juranum subsp.

cichoriaceum, commonly known as the Jura hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in the summer.

The plant is native to Central and Eastern Europe, particularly found in meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in its often dry habitat.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
