Helianthemum canum var. piloselloides

Helianthemum canum var. piloselloides, commonly known as the woolly rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, rounded foliage with dense, greyish-white or silver-blue leaves covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in sunny, arid conditions.
