Hibiscus hybridus

Hibiscus hybridus, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, with a prominent central pistil and stamens. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Hibiscus hybridus is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking floral display.
