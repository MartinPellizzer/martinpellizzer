Habenaria Hystrix

Habenaria hystrix is a striking orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia. It is known for its unique, twisted floral structure and vibrant coloration. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its name, meaning "hysteric" in Greek, reflects its unusual appearance. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the orchid world.
