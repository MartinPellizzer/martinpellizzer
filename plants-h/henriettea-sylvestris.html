Henriettea sylvestris

Henriettea sylvestris, commonly known as the forest bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Henriettea sylvestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
