Homocolleticon brownii

Homocolleticon brownii, commonly known as the brown brownscale, is a species of scale insect belonging to the family Coccidae.

It is native to various regions in Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant it infests is typically a member of the genus *Pterocarpus*, such as *Pterocarpus erinaceus*. Its appearance is characterized by a dark brown, waxy coating that covers its body.

This insect is known for its ability to cause significant damage to the host plant, leading to reduced growth and productivity.
