Hieracium doronicifolium subsp. scabiosifolium

Hieracium doronicifolium subsp.

scabiosifolium, commonly known as the scabious-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves with a rough, scabious texture.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often in sunny to partially shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
