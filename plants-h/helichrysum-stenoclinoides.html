Helichrysum stenoclinoides

Helichrysum stenoclinoides, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and showy yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces small, papery bracts that resemble petals, giving it a fluffy appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The flowers are long-lasting and often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant color and durability.
