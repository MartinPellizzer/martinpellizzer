Hedyotis wallichii

Hedyotis wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan carpet grass, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It typically grows in moist, rocky soils at high altitudes, often forming dense mats or cushions. The plant features small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. Hedyotis wallichii is adapted to cold climates and can withstand harsh environmental conditions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in mountainous areas and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
