Homalomena ellipticifolia

Homalomena ellipticifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spadices, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Homalomena ellipticifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and resilience.
