Horsfieldia thorelii

Horsfieldia thorelii, commonly known as the Bornean orchid, is a tropical flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Borneo and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Horsfieldia thorelii is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in horticultural displays.
