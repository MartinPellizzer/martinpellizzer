Haplopappus racemosus var. glomerellus

Haplopappus racemosus var. glomerellus, commonly known as the clustered haplopappus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage.
