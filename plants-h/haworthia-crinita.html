Haworthia crinita

Haworthia crinita, commonly known as the curly Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in white, hair-like tubercles, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Haworthia crinita is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.
