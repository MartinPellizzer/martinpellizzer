Heliotropium dubium

Heliotropium dubium, commonly known as the stinkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and it often grows in disturbed soils or along roadsides. It is an annual herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Heliotropium dubium is also known for its strong, unpleasant odor, which is more pronounced when the flowers are in bloom.
