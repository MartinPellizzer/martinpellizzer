Halimium ocymoides var. algarvense

Halimium ocymoides var. algarvense, commonly known as the Algarve hemicarpha, is a flowering plant native to southern Portugal.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in late spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It is often found in rocky soils and coastal areas.
