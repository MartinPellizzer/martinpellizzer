Hieracium phaedrocheilon

Hieracium phaedrocheilon, commonly known as the purple sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in alpine or subalpine regions. Its leaves are linear and grayish-green, with a rough texture.

Hieracium phaedrocheilon is adapted to cold climates and is often found in high-altitude environments.
