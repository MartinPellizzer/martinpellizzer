Hieracium peroblongum

Hieracium peroblongum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate with hairy surfaces, and its flowering stems are often reddish or purplish in color. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, followed by seeds dispersed by wind.

Hieracium peroblongum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
