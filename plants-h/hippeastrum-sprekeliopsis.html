Hippeastrum sprekeliopsis

Hippeastrum sprekeliopsis, commonly known as the Sprekeliopsis amaryllis, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a bulbous plant characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically bloom in the summer months. The leaves are broad, strap-like, and often appear after the flowers have faded. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant colors and relatively easy care requirements.
