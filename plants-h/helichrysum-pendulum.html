Helichrysum pendulum

Helichrysum pendulum, commonly known as the hanging goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellow-orange flowers that resemble small cups, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has feathery, grey-green leaves and a compact, shrub-like growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its long-lasting flowers make it a favorite in dried floral arrangements.
