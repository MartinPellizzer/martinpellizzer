Hieracium Levicaule Subsp. Lucens

Hieracium levicaule subsp. lucens is a lesser-known member of the sunflower family known for its delicate, bright yellow flowers. This subspecies is often found in grassy areas and open woodlands across temperate regions. Its slender stems and daisy-like appearance make it easy to overlook in the wild. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators. Conservation efforts are growing as awareness of its ecological value increases.
