Hieracium scandinavicum subsp. mesodicum

Hieracium scandinavicum subsp.

mesodicum, commonly known as the Scandinavian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in open habitats such as meadows and forest edges. It has a widespread distribution across Scandinavia and is often found in areas with cool, temperate climates.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a characteristic species of certain ecological zones.
