Halimium × martrei

Halimium × martrei, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, which is often covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to sunny, dry environments.

Halimium × martrei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
