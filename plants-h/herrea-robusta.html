Herrea robusta

Herrea robusta, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Herrea robusta is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
