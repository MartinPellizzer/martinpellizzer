Hippeastrum duarteanum

Hippeastrum duarteanum, commonly known as the Duarte's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces strap-like, evergreen leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy followed by a warm, humid environment to encourage blooming.

This species is popular among horticulturists for its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
