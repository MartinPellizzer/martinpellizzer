Hildaea breviscrobs

Hildaea breviscrobs, commonly known as the short-stemmed hildaea, is a low-growing succulent native to arid regions of South America.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the spring and summer seasons. Its root system is shallow but extensive, enabling it to absorb moisture from the soil efficiently.

Hildaea breviscrobs is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in full sun exposure.
