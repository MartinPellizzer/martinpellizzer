Hieracium compar

Hieracium compar, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray flowers and is often found in meadows and disturbed soils. The plant has a creeping growth habit with hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped. It produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across various habitats.

Hieracium compar is known for its ability to thrive in a wide range of environmental conditions.
