Hypargyrium × decipiens

Hypargyrium × decipiens, commonly known as the false silverleaf.

This hybrid plant is a cross between Hypargyrium and another species, resulting in unique botanical characteristics. It typically features silver-white foliage and is often found in moist, shady environments. The plant has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance.
