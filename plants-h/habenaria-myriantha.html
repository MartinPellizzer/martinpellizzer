Habenaria myriantha

Habenaria myriantha, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial herb, with strap-like leaves and clustered inflorescences. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral fragrance.
