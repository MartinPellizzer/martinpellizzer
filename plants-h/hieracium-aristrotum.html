Hieracium aristrotum

Hieracium aristrotum, commonly known as the purple spearthistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. Its leaves are spiny and arranged in a rosette, while its stems are erect and often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are purple to reddish in color and bloom from late summer to early autumn.
