Hieracium psilophyllum

Hieracium psilophyllum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and yellow composite flowers that consist of both ray and disk florets. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this species thrives in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant has finely divided, serrated leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem.

It is often found in open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
