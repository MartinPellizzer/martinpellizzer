Helenium caespitosum

Helenium caespitosum, commonly known as the yellow helenium, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, ray-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its vibrant yellow blooms appear in late summer to early fall, adding color to gardens and natural habitats.

Helenium caespitosum is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
