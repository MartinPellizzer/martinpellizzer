Habenaria schischmareffiana

Habenaria schischmareffiana, commonly known as the Schischmareff's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and elongated leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with prominent lip sections. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained, humus-rich soil.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
