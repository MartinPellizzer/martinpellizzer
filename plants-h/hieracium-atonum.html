Hieracium atonum

Hieracium atonum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas. It has a basal rosette of leaves and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

The flowers are yellow or orange and bloom from late spring to early autumn.
