Hypoporum compositum

Hypoporum compositum, commonly known as the composite hypoporum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its composite inflorescence, which consists of numerous small flowers arranged in a head-like structure. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often grouped together. It is native to specific regions in southern Africa and thrives in dry, sandy soils.

Due to its unique floral structure and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
