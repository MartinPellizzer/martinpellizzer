Haworthia maraisii var. maraisii

Haworthia maraisii var. maraisii, commonly known as the Marais Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The leaves are thick and water-storing, adapted to arid environments. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
