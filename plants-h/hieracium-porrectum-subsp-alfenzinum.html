Hieracium porrectum subsp. alfenzinum

Hieracium porrectum subsp.

alfenzinum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.

The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, consisting of numerous small florets.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and is often found in high-altitude environments.
