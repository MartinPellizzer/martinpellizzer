Heteropterys bicolor var. elliptica

Heteropterys bicolor var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic-flowered heteropterys, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, lowland forests. The plant is characterized by its elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small and clustered, with two distinct colors—white and pink—on the same inflorescence.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
