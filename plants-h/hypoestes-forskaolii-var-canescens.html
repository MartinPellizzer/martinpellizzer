Hypoestes forskaolii var. canescens

Hypoestes forskaolii var. canescens, commonly known as the silver bush or snowflake plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and striking, showy flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and purple. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, silvery down, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in warm climates.
