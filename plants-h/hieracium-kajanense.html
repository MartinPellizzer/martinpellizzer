Hieracium kajanense

Hieracium kajanense, commonly known as the Kajanense hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette growth habit and produces yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy areas and is native to parts of Central Asia. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

Hieracium kajanense is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
