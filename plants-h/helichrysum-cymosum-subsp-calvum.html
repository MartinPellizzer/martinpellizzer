Helichrysum cymosum subsp. calvum

Helichrysum cymosum subsp.

calvum, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble small cups, hence its common name.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
