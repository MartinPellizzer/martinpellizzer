Haworthia haageana

Haworthia haageana, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves with distinctive white or light green markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
