Hosta lancifolia f. kabitan

Hosta lancifolia f. kabitan, commonly known as the Kabitan Hosta, is a unique cultivar characterized by its striking foliage.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a prominent white margin, creating a bold visual contrast. The plant is native to Korea and is prized for its ornamental value in shaded gardens. It typically grows to a height of 12 to 18 inches and spreads slowly, forming a dense clump.

This variety is relatively low maintenance, thriving in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.
