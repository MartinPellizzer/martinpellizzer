Hosta nakaiana

Hosta nakaiana, commonly known as the Japanese hosta, is a shade-loving perennial plant native to Japan.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery-blue hue on the upper surface. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers in late summer, which are borne on slender stalks. Hosta nakaiana thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for woodland gardens and shaded landscapes.
