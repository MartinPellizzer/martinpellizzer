Haworthia splendens var. hansii

Haworthia splendens var. hansii, commonly known as the Hansii Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf patterns and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
