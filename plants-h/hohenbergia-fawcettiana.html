Hohenbergia fawcettiana

Hohenbergia fawcettiana, commonly known as the Fawcett's hohenbergia, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that resemble flowers. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. It produces inflorescences with small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer.

Hohenbergia fawcettiana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
