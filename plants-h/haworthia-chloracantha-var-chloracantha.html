Haworthia chloracantha var. chloracantha

Haworthia chloracantha var. chloracantha, commonly known as the green Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers on slender stalks during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique look and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
