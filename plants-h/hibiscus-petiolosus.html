Hibiscus petiolosus

Hibiscus petiolosus, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a serrated edge, and its stems are often hairy. Hibiscus petiolosus thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in swampy areas or along riverbanks.

It is also valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in some African communities.
