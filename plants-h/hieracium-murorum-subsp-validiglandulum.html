Hieracium murorum subsp. validiglandulum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

validiglandulum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies due to its adaptability and role in supporting pollinators.
