Hedychium griersonianum

Hedychium griersonianum, commonly known as the giant ginger or golden torch ginger, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, yellow-orange flowers that resemble torches, which bloom in clusters along branched inflorescences. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its lush appearance. Hedychium griersonianum thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and tropical landscapes.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
