Hedbergia longiflora subsp. macrophylla

Hedbergia longiflora subsp.

macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf hedbergia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves.

The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its foliage is notable for its large, heart-shaped leaves, which are often used in ornamental gardening.

This subspecies is valued for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
