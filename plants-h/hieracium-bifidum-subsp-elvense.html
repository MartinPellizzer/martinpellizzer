Hieracium bifidum subsp. elvense

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

elvense, commonly known as the elf's daisy, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flowers with ray florets.

The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are usually basal, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
