Hyptis corymbosa

Hyptis corymbosa, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of blue or purple flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
