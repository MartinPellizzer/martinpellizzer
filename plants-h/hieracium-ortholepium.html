Hieracium ortholepium

Hieracium ortholepium, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium ortholepium is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in certain regions.
