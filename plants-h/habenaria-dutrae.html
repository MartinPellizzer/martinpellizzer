Habenaria dutrae

Habenaria dutrae, commonly known as the Dutra's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive white lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and decaying organic matter.

Habenaria dutrae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
