Hieracium gradile

Hieracium gradile, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium gradile is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in many regions.
