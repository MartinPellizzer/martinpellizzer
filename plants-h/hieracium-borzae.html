Hieracium borzae

Hieracium borzae, commonly known as the Borza Hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. Its leaves are typically basal, hairy, and have a rough texture.

Hieracium borzae is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing resilience to harsh climatic conditions.
