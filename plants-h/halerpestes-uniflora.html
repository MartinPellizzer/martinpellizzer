Halerpestes uniflora

Halerpestes uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and distinctive, single-flowered inflorescences that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are leathery and glossy, providing ornamental value in gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
