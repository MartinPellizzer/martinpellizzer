Hypericum kouytchense

Hypericum kouytchense, commonly known as the Armenian St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Hypericaceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Hypericum kouytchense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
