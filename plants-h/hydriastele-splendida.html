Hydriastele splendida

Hydriastele splendida, commonly known as the splendour star, is a striking member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that resemble stars, which are actually modified leaves. The flowers are small and inconspicuous, emerging from the base of the inflorescence.

Hydriastele splendida thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
