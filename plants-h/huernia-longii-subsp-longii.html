Huernia longii subsp. longii

Huernia longii subsp.

longii, commonly known as the Long's horned lithops, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
