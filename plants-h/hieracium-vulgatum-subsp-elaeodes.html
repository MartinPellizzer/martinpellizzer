Hieracium vulgatum subsp. elaeodes

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

elaeodes, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in open grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm.

The plant features basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to ovate, with rough, hairy surfaces. Its flowering stems bear clusters of yellow to orange disc flowers, surrounded by yellow ray florets.

The plant is characterized by its robust root system and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
