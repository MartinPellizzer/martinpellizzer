Hieracium crocydograptum

Hieracium crocydograptum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble daisies, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium crocydograptum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
