Habenaria uncatiloba

Habenaria uncatiloba, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and delicate, pale greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and swampy areas. Its flowers are usually arranged in a spike and have a distinct, narrow lip that distinguishes it from other orchid species.

Habenaria uncatiloba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
