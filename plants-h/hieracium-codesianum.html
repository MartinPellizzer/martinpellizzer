Hieracium codesianum

Hieracium codesianum, commonly known as the Codesia hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is often found in disturbed habitats.
