Hieracium leucopsilon

Hieracium leucopsilon, commonly known as the white hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant features bright yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a flat, composite head. Its leaves are usually hairy and alternate along the stem.

It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands.
