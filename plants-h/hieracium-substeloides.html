Hieracium substeloides

Hieracium substeloides, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Hieracium substeloides is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in many regions.
