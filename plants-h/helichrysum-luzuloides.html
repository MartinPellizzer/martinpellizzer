Helichrysum luzuloides

Helichrysum luzuloides, commonly known as the silverleaf helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its foliage is covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance.

Helichrysum luzuloides is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
