Hakea psilophylla

Hakea psilophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is an evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Hakea psilophylla is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native landscaping.
