Heterocaryum rigidum

Heterocaryum rigidum, commonly known as the rigid thornbush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rigid, thorny stems and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Heterocaryum rigidum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.
