Helopus polystachyus

Helopus polystachyus, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has a trunk with a smooth, greyish-brown bark and produces long, pendulous inflorescences. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Helopus polystachyus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
