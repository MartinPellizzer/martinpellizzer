Heuchera × rosea

Heuchera × rosea, commonly known as the coral bells hybrid, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often variegated foliage that comes in shades of red, pink, and green. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Heuchera × rosea is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and ease of cultivation.
