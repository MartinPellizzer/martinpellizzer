Heliopsis laevis var. minor

Heliopsis laevis var. minor, commonly known as the narrowleaf heliopsis, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. Its narrow, linear leaves are a distinguishing feature, and it produces small, elongated fruits called achenes.
