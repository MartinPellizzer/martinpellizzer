Hymenorebutia nealeana

Hymenorebutia nealeana, commonly known as the Neale's Hymenorebutia, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, fragrant, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable addition to botanical collections.
