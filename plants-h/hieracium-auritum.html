Hieracium auritum

Hieracium auritum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium auritum is often considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid growth and ability to form dense stands.
