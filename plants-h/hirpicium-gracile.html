Hirpicium gracile

Hirpicium gracile, commonly known as the slender hirpicium, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and along stream banks. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its root system is fibrous and contributes to soil stabilization in its natural habitat.

Hirpicium gracile is often found in association with other woodland flora, thriving in partially shaded conditions with well-drained soil.
