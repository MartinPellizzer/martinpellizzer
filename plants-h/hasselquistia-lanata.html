Hasselquistia lanata

Hasselquistia lanata, commonly known as the woolly hasselquistia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
