Helianthemum halimifolium var. maculatum

Helianthemum halimifolium var. maculatum, commonly known as the spotted rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often speckled with darker markings. The plant has gray-green, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact form and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
