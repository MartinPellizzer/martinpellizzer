Hortensia serrata f. belladonna

Hortensia serrata f. belladonna, commonly known as the Italian belladonna hortensia, is a unique cultivar of the hydrangea genus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from deep pink to red, depending on soil pH. The plant is a deciduous shrub that thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil. Its foliage is dark green and provides a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
