Helianthemum × doumerguei

Helianthemum × doumerguei, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, showy yellow flowers in early summer, which are typically borne on hairy stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its hardy nature and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
