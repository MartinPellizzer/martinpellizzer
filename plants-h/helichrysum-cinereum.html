Helichrysum cinereum

Helichrysum cinereum, commonly known as the silver frost or silver leaf helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its essential oils are also used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
