Hemigraphis turnerifolia

Hemigraphis turnerifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf or purple heart plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It features striking, velvety leaves that range from deep purple to green, often with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant foliage.

Hemigraphis turnerifolia is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its low maintenance requirements.
