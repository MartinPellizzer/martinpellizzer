Hemerocallis Flava Var. Coreana

Hemerocallis flava var. coreana is a striking variety of daylily known for its vibrant yellow flowers. Native to Korea, this plant thrives in a range of soil types and is relatively easy to care for. Its blooms appear in late spring and last for only one day, making each flower a fleeting beauty. The plant is also valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand various environmental conditions. It is a popular choice for gardeners looking to add color and interest to their landscapes.
