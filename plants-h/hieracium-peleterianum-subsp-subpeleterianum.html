Hieracium peleterianum subsp. subpeleterianum

Hieracium peleterianum subsp.

subpeleterianum, commonly known as the lesser spear thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow flower heads.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowering stems are usually unbranched.
