Hieracium calcareum subsp. baldensiforme

Hieracium calcareum subsp.

baldensiforme, commonly known as the limestone hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe. It typically grows in calcareous soils, such as limestone grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in the summer. Its subspecies is distinguished by its slightly elongated and more upright leaves compared to the typical form.

It is often found in open, sunny habitats and is considered a characteristic species of calcareous grasslands.
