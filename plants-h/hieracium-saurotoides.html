Hieracium saurotoides

Hieracium saurotoides, commonly known as the snake's head hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which are composed of numerous small florets. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough, hairy texture.

Hieracium saurotoides is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
