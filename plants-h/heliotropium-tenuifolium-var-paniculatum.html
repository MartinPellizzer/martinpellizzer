Heliotropium tenuifolium var. paniculatum

Heliotropium tenuifolium var. paniculatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period usually occurs during the warm seasons, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
