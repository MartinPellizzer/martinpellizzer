Henslowia minor

Henslowia minor, commonly known as the dwarf henslowia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters and features delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator species for certain forest ecosystems.
