Helenium donianum var. linearifolium

Helenium donianum var. linearifolium, commonly known as the slenderleaf garden everlasting, is a perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, orange-red to reddish-orange ray flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that give it its varietal name, linearifolium. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
