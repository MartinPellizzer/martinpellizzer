Hoya amorosoae

Hoya amorosoae, commonly known as the "Love Plant," is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers. The plant produces fragrant, pinkish-white blooms that typically appear in late winter to early spring. Hoya amorosoae is known for its slow growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
