Hieracium kravarskense

Hieracium kravarskense, commonly known as the Kravarskian hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow daisy-like flowers with ray florets. Native to central and eastern Europe, this plant thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It has a hairy stem and leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges.

The plant is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
