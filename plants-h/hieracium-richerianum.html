Hieracium richerianum

Hieracium richerianum, commonly known as the richier's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and hairy, often reddish stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium richerianum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
