Hieracium elongatum

Hieracium elongatum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium elongatum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
