Heloniopsis orientalis var. yakusimensis

Heloniopsis orientalis var. yakusimensis, commonly known as the Yakusim snowbell, is a rare and striking alpine plant native to the Japanese island of Yakushima.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments at high elevations. Its leaves are narrow and lanceolate, often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
