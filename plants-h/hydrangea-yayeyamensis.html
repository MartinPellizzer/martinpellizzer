Hydrangea yayeyamensis

Hydrangea yayeyamensis, commonly known as the Yaeyama hydrangea, is a rare species native to the Yaeyama Islands of Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flower clusters that range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil to flourish. Its foliage consists of broad, serrated leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized collections.
