Hypericum polygonifolium

Hypericum polygonifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features upright stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
