Habenella petalodes

Habenella petalodes, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple petals and delicate, fragrant blossoms. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth, waxy surface.

Habenella petalodes thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
