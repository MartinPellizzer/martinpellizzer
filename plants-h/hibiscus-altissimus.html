Hibiscus altissimus

Hibiscus altissimus, commonly known as the tall hibiscus, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a flowering plant that typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are usually bright red or yellow in color. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Hibiscus altissimus thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
