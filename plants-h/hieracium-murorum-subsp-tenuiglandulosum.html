Hieracium murorum subsp. tenuiglandulosum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

tenuiglandulosum, commonly known as the slender glandular hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-red flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender stems and small, glandular hairs on the leaves and stems. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

The plant produces small, herbaceous leaves and is often found in temperate climates.
