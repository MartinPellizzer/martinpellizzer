Hypericum edisonianum

Hypericum edisonianum, commonly known as the Edison goldenstar, is a perennial flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Botanically, it belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species known for their medicinal and ornamental value.
