Hypericum somaliense

Hypericum somaliense, commonly known as the Somali St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, typical of the Hypericaceae family. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
