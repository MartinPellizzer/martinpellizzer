Haworthia limifolia var. schuldtiana

Haworthia limifolia var. schuldtiana, commonly known as the Schuldt's narrow-leaf Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-forming leaves that are typically narrow and pointed, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped white or pink flowers on tall, slender stalks during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal watering, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
