Heinsenia diervilleoides

Heinsenia diervilleoides, commonly known as the smooth heath.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to the southeastern United States, characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive texture.

Heinsenia diervilleoides thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and coastal dunes.
