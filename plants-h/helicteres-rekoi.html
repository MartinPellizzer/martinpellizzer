Helicteres rekoi

Helicteres rekoi, commonly known as the velvetleaf senna, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features palmately lobed leaves with a velvety texture, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, attracting pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

The seeds are enclosed in hairy, elongated pods and can remain viable in the soil for several years.
