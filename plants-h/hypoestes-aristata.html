Hypoestes aristata

Hypoestes aristata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hypoestes aristata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
