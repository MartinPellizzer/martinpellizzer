Helianthemum racemosum var. micranthum

Helianthemum racemosum var. micranthum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, upright stems and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, and the plant thrives in rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sunny, well-drained environments.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
