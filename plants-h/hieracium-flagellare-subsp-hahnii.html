Hieracium flagellare subsp. hahnii

Hieracium flagellare subsp.

hahnii, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright stems and yellowish to reddish flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowering period occurs from late spring to early autumn.

This subspecies is notable for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural and natural areas.
