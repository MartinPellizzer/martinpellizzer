Hieracium pallidum subsp. provinciale

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

provinciale, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to brownish flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

Its botanical features make it a common indicator species in certain ecological communities.
