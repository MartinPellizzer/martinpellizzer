Huernia verekeri var. stenensonii

Huernia verekeri var. stenensonii, commonly known as the Stenenson's horned cucumber, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in small, spiky protrusions. The plant produces pale green to yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts and rock gardens.
