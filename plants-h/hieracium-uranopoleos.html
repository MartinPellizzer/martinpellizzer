Hieracium uranopoleos

Hieracium uranopoleos, commonly known as the Uranopole Goldenweed, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Central and Eastern Europe, particularly found in grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant produces yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense, spiny disk of tubular flowers. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

Hieracium uranopoleos is typically found in open habitats and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
