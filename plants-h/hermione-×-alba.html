Hermione × alba

Hermione × alba, commonly known as the white hermione, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Hermione and Alba species.

It is characterized by its delicate, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 feet and prefers well-drained soil in full sun. Its foliage is usually a mix of green and silver, adding visual interest to gardens.

It is relatively low maintenance and attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
