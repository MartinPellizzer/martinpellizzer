Haworthia coarctata f. greenii

Haworthia coarctata f. greenii, commonly known as the Green Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for low-maintenance indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
