Habenaria agapitae

Habenaria agapitae, commonly known as the Agapit's orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant floral structure. The plant typically grows in montane forest environments, where it thrives in shaded, moist conditions. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
