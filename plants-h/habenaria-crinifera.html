Habenaria crinifera

Habenaria crinifera, commonly known as the feather-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and its distinctive, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a unique, crinkled appearance.

Habenaria crinifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
