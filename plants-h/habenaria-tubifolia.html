Habenaria tubifolia

Habenaria tubifolia, commonly known as the tubular orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its unique floral structure and ecological role in its native habitat.
