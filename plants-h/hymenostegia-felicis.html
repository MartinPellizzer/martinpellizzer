Hymenostegia felicis

Hymenostegia felicis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are usually heart-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemose inflorescences. Its fruits are capsules that split open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Hymenostegia felicis is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
