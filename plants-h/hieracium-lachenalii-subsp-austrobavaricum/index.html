Hieracium Lachenalii Subsp. Austrobavaricum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp. austrobavaricum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the dandelion family and is known for its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. This subspecies thrives in rocky and calcareous soils, often found in mountainous regions. Its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
