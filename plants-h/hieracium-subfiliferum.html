Hieracium subfiliferum

Hieracium subfiliferum, commonly known as the slender leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Hieracium subfiliferum is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
