Hosta lancifolia f. okamii

Hosta lancifolia f. okamii, commonly known as the Okamii Hosta, is a cultivar prized for its striking foliage.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to Japan. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and rockeries.
