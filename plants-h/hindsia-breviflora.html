Hindsia breviflora

Hindsia breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered hindsia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Hindsia breviflora thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
