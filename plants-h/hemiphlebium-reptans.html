Hemiphlebium reptans

Hemiphlebium reptans, commonly known as the forest fern, is a perennial plant belonging to the Aspidiaceae family.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine hairs. Hemiphlebium reptans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its lush, evergreen foliage.
