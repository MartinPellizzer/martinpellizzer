Hypoestes verticillaris var. glabra

Hypoestes verticillaris var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth hypoestes, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from pink to purple. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in whorls around the stem. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.

It prefers well-draining soil and moderate light conditions for optimal growth.
