Habenaria germainii

Habenaria germainii, commonly known as the Germain's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in acidic soils. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are pale green to white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that is often tinged with purple.

Habenaria germainii is a relatively rare species, with limited distribution and specific habitat requirements.
