Hieracium angustum

Hieracium angustum, commonly known as narrowleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural areas due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
