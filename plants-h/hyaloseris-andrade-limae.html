Hyaloseris andrade-limae

Hyaloseris andrade-limae, commonly known as the Andrade-Lima hyaloseris, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
