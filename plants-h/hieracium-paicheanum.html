Hieracium paicheanum

Hieracium paicheanum, commonly known as the Paichean hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow composite flowers that resemble dandelions. Native to South America, particularly in regions of Argentina and Chile, it thrives in temperate climates. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in its native environment.

It is often found in grasslands and open areas where it can receive ample sunlight.
