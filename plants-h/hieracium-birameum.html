Hieracium birameum

Hieracium birameum, commonly known as the two-lobed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragweed, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers consist of both ray and disk florets.

Hieracium birameum is often considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive growth.
