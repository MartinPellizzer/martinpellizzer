Hispidella barnardesii

Hispidella barnardesii, commonly known as the Cape mountain daisy, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its hairy stems and leaves help reduce water loss, making it well adapted to arid environments.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
