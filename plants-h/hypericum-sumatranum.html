Hypericum sumatranum

Hypericum sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes various species of flowering shrubs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and has small, rounded leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Hypericum sumatranum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
