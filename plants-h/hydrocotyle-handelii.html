Hydrocotyle handelii

Hydrocotyle handelii, commonly known as the waxy begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.
