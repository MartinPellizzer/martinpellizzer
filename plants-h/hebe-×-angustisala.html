Hebe × angustisala

Hebe × angustisala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hebe, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, which bloom in spring and summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
