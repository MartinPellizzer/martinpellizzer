Heteropterys prunifolia

Heteropterys prunifolia, commonly known as the cherryleaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which resemble those of cherry trees, and its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink.
