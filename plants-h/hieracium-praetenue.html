Hieracium praetenue

Hieracium praetenue, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish composite flowers. Hieracium praetenue is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
