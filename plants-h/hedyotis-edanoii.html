Hedyotis edanoii

Hedyotis edanoii, commonly known as the Japanese pennywort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features small, bright green, oval-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy. The plant produces tiny white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Hedyotis edanoii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
