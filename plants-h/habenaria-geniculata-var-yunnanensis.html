Habenaria geniculata var. yunnanensis

Habenaria geniculata var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan butterfly orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a greenish hue. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
