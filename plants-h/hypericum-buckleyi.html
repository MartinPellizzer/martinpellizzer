Hypericum buckleyi

Hypericum buckleyi, commonly known as Buckley's St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features opposite, narrow leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along streambanks.

Buckley's St. John's wort is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
