Hieracium Schmidtii Subsp. Floccosum

Hieracium schmidtii subsp. floccosum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the dandelion family and is known for its soft, fluffy appearance. This subspecies is characterized by its white to pale yellow flowers and hairy stems. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Its unique features make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
