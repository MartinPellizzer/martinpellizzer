Hesperis aintabica

Hesperis aintabica, commonly known as the Aintab bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Hesperis aintabica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
