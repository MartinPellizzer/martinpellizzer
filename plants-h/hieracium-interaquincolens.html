Hieracium interaquincolens

Hieracium interaquincolens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium interaquincolens is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
