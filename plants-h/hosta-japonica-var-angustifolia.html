Hosta japonica var. angustifolia

Hosta japonica var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Japanese hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are narrower than those of other hosta varieties. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers on slender stems in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is prized for its elegant foliage and adaptability to garden settings.
