Habenaria bolusiana

Habenaria bolusiana, commonly known as the Bolus orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and linear, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often fragrant. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species.
