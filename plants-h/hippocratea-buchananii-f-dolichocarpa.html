Hippocratea buchananii f. dolichocarpa

Hippocratea buchananii f. dolichocarpa, commonly known as the long-stemmed hippocratea, is a unique hybrid plant native to parts of western Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-bearing fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant is distinguished by its elongated, slender stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces distinctive, elongated seed pods.
