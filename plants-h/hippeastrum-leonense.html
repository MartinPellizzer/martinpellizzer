Hippeastrum leonense

Hippeastrum leonense, commonly known as the Leonense amaryllis, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter. The plant produces strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often mistaken for those of other amaryllis species. It blooms in the spring and summer, producing clusters of vibrant red or orange flowers.

The plant is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
