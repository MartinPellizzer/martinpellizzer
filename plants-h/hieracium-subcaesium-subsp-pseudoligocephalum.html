Hieracium subcaesium subsp. pseudoligocephalum

Hieracium subcaesium subsp.

pseudoligocephalum, commonly known as the pale spearhead hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and yellowish to brownish composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and may have a bluish or grayish hue.

This subspecies is notable for its distinct floral structure and is often found in regions with temperate climates.
