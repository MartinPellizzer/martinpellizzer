Habenaria obcordata

Habenaria obcordata, commonly known as the heart-shaped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped flowers, which are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lanceolate and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Habenaria obcordata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
