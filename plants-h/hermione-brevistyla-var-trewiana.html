Hermione brevistyla var. trewiana

Hermione brevistyla var. trewiana, commonly known as the Trewiana honeysuckle, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often prostrate stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that are edible but not widely cultivated.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
