Haworthia doldii

Haworthia doldii, commonly known as the Dold's Haworthia, is a species of succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base, especially when exposed to sunlight. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on short, upright stems during the warmer months.

Haworthia doldii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil to thrive.
