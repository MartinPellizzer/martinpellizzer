Haworthia limifolia var. koelmaniorum

Haworthia limifolia var. koelmaniorum, commonly known as the Koelman's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
