Habenaria brachyceras

Habenaria brachyceras, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a compact, pseudobulbous structure that supports its growth in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are typically pollinated by insects. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
