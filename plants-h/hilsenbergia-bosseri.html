Hilsenbergia bosseri

Hilsenbergia bosseri, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Hilsenbergia bosseri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a favored plant among gardeners.
