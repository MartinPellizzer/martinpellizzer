Haworthia crousii

Haworthia crousii, commonly known as the Crous' Haworthia, is a species of succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of thick, fleshy leaves. The leaves are typically green with white or pale markings, and they are covered in a layer of fine, white hairs. This plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal irrigation.

It is often grown as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
