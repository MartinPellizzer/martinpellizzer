Hieracium dollineri subsp. obscuribracteolatum

Hieracium dollineri subsp.

obscuribracteolatum, commonly known as the dark bracted hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dark, hairy bracts and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It typically thrives in dry, grassy habitats and is often found in open woodlands.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered plant species.
