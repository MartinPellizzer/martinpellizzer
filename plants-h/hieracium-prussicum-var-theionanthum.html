Hieracium prussicum var. theionanthum

Hieracium prussicum var. theionanthum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has basal leaves that are broadly lanceolate and often covered with fine hairs.

Its flowers are arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family, and are surrounded by a ring of phyllaries.
