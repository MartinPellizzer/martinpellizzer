Hieracium pulchriceps

Hieracium pulchriceps, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically borne in clusters at the top of the plant. The plant has a hairy stem and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium pulchriceps is an herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
