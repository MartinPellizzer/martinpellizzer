Humata cromwelliana

Humata cromwelliana, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its segmented stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to bloom during the holiday season.

Humata cromwelliana is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
