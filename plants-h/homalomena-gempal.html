Homalomena gempal

Homalomena gempal, commonly known as the red-veined arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves with striking red veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by spadices that bear berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage.

Homalomena gempal is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
