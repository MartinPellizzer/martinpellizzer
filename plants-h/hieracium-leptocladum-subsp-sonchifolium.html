Hieracium leptocladum subsp. sonchifolium

Hieracium leptocladum subsp.

sonchifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small size and preference for well-drained soils.
