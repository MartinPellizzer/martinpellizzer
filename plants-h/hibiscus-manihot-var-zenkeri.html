Hibiscus manihot var. zenkeri

Hibiscus manihot var. zenkeri, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features large, showy flowers with five deeply lobed petals, typically in shades of red or pink. The plant has heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Hibiscus manihot var. zenkeri is also valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional uses in some cultures.
