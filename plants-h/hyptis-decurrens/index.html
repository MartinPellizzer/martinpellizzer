Hyptis Decurrens

Blue hyptis, scientifically known as Hyptis decurrens, is a hardy perennial plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas. It is characterized by its striking blue-gray foliage and delicate, aromatic flowers that bloom in clusters. This plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is also popular in ornamental gardening. Its ability to thrive in various soil types makes it a versatile addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes. Despite its beauty, Blue hyptis remains relatively underappreciated compared to more common ornamental plants.
