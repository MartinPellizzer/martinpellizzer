Hoya mindorensis subsp. mindorensis

Hoya mindorensis subsp.

mindorensis, commonly known as the Mindanao hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and large, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming during the warmer months. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements. Hoya mindorensis subsp.

mindorensis thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
