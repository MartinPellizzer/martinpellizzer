Hypericum pseudojaponicum

Hypericum pseudojaponicum, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to East Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in summer. It is often found in woodland edges and moist soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
