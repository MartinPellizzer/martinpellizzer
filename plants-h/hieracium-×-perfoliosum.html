Hieracium × perfoliosum

Hieracium × perfoliosum, commonly known as the broad-leaved hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its broad, often lobed leaves and yellow composite flowers that resemble daisies. This plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It is a perennial herb that can form dense colonies, sometimes becoming invasive in certain regions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
