Hieracium canipioides

Hieracium canipioides, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium canipioides is often considered a weed due to its ability to spread rapidly in new environments.
