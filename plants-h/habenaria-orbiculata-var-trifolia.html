Habenaria orbiculata var. trifolia

Habenaria orbiculata var. trifolia, commonly known as the three-leaf orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and trifoliate leaves, which are divided into three distinct lobes. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat preferences, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
