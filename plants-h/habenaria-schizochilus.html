Habenaria schizochilus

Habenaria schizochilus, commonly known as the slit-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive green veins. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically borne on the upper part of the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its elegance.
