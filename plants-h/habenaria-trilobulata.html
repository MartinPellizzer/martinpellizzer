Habenaria trilobulata

Habenaria trilobulata, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with three distinct lobes on the lip, which is a notable botanical feature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

The genus Habenaria is known for its diverse floral structures, and H. trilobulata is no exception.
