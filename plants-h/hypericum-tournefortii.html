Hypericum tournefortii

Hypericum tournefortii, commonly known as the Texas hypericum or flame root.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, and it has woody, often reddish stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum tournefortii is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
