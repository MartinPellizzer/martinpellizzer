Habenaria sparsiflora var. laxiflora

Habenaria sparsiflora var. laxiflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, where it thrives in humid and cool conditions. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a short rhizome and a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of several pale green to white flowers with distinctive spurred petals.

This variety is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
