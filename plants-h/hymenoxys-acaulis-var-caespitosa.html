Hymenoxys acaulis var. caespitosa

Hymenoxys acaulis var. caespitosa, commonly known as the clustered hymenoxys, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that forms dense, low-growing clumps of foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, alternate, and have a rough, hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
