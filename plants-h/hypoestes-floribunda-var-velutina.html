Hypoestes floribunda var. velutina

Hypoestes floribunda var. velutina, commonly known as velvet hypoestes, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of small, bright orange or red flowers, which are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
