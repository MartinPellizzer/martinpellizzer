Helichrysum leptorhizum

Helichrysum leptorhizum, commonly known as the golden everlasting or yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, gray-green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Helichrysum leptorhizum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
