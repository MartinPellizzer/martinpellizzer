Hosta aurea

Hosta aurea, commonly known as the golden hosta, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright yellow to green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta aurea is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
