Heterolophus Sibiricus

Heterolophus sibiricus is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the cold regions of Siberia. It thrives in harsh, arctic-like conditions where few species can survive. This plant is characterized by its unique leaf structure and slow growth rate. Despite its resilience, it remains poorly understood by scientists. Its study offers valuable insights into plant adaptation to extreme climates.
