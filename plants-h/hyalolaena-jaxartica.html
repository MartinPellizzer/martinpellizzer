Hyalolaena jaxartica

Hyalolaena jaxartica, commonly known as the Central Asian grass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitat.

Hyalolaena jaxartica is often found in steppe and desert ecosystems.
