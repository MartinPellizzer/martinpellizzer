Hymenachne bresolinii

Hymenachne bresolinii, commonly known as the Bresolin's hymenachne, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along waterways, where it thrives in moist to aquatic conditions. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands, which can influence local ecosystems.

Hymenachne bresolinii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and improve water quality.
