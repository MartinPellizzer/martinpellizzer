Haworthia pumila var. kingiana

Haworthia pumila var. kingiana, commonly known as the King's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with a distinctive white or pale pinkish bloom at the center. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
