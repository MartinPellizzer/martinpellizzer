Hypericum salsolifolium

Hypericum salsolifolium, commonly known as sea buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange berries, which are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant has small, alternate leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Hypericum salsolifolium is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine.
