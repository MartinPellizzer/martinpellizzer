Hypericum hayatae

Hypericum hayatae, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes approximately 400 species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in summer, and its opposite, linear leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Hypericum hayatae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
