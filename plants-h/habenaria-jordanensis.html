Habenaria jordanensis

Habenaria jordanensis, commonly known as Jordan's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Middle East, particularly Jordan.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers, which are usually pale green or yellowish, bloom in early spring and attract various pollinators.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
