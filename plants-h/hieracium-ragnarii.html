Hieracium ragnarii

Hieracium ragnarii, commonly known as the Ragnar's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, and its leaves are usually hairy and lance-shaped.

Hieracium ragnarii is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
