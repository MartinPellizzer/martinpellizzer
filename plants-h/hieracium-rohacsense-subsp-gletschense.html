Hieracium rohacsense subsp. gletschense

Hieracium rohacsense subsp.

gletschense, commonly known as the Gletsch Hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-colored flowers.

The leaves are typically basal and hairy, with a rough texture. This subspecies is native to alpine regions of Europe, particularly in the Alps and Carpathians.

It thrives in rocky and dry habitats, often found in high-altitude meadows and slopes.
