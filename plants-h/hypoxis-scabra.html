Hypoxis scabra

Hypoxis scabra, commonly known as the African ground orchid, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a fibrous root system and produces basal, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant bears small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Hypoxis scabra is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
