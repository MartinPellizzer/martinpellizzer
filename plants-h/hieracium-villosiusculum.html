Hieracium villosiusculum

Hieracium villosiusculum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
