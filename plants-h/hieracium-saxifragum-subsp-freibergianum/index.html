Hieracium Saxifragum Subsp. Freibergianum

Hieracium saxifragum subsp. freibergianum is a rare and distinctive plant native to specific regions of central europe. it belongs to the asteraceae family and is known for its unique floral structure and coloration. this subspecies is often found in rocky or alpine environments where it thrives in poor soils. its narrow distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces threats that highlight the need for protection.
