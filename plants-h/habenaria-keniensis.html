Habenaria keniensis

Habenaria keniensis, commonly known as the Kenyan marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Kenya.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy habitats, preferring humid and shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous, and it has lance-shaped leaves that help in water retention.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to its wetland ecosystem.
