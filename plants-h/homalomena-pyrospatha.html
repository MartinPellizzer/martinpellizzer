Homalomena pyrospatha

Homalomena pyrospatha, commonly known as the flame-leaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with red, orange, and yellow variegation. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rhizomes are also used in traditional medicine and as a starch source.
