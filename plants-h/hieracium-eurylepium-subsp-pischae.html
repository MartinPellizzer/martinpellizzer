Hieracium eurylepium subsp. pischae

Hieracium eurylepium subsp.

pischae, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow disk flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy slopes at high altitudes. It has a fibrous root system and produces numerous small, inconspicuous seeds.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and sensitive species in its native range.
