Hieracium mougeotii subsp. stelliceps

Hieracium mougeotii subsp.

stelliceps, commonly known as the star-headed hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow with a distinctive star-shaped appearance, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting pollinator populations.
