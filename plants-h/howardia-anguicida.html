Howardia anguicida

Howardia anguicida, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its unique appearance.

Howardia anguicida is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
