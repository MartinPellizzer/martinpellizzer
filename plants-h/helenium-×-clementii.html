Helenium × clementii

Helenium × clementii, commonly known as the New Zealand aster, is a hybrid perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Helenium × clementii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its long-lasting blooms.
