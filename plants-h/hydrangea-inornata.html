Hydrangea inornata

Hydrangea inornata, commonly known as the smooth hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its large, round clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features dark green, oval-shaped leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Hydrangea inornata is also noted for its ability to change flower color based on soil pH, adding versatility to its ornamental value.
