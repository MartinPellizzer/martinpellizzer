Hieracium sylvaticum subsp. heteroschistum

Hieracium sylvaticum subsp.

heteroschistum, commonly known as the woodland hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in woodland and meadow habitats, preferring moist, well-drained soils.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped. Its flowering stems bear yellow composite flowers, which are composed of numerous small florets.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its characteristic composite flower heads.
