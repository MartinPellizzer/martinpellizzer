Haworthia cymbiformis var. compacta

Haworthia cymbiformis var. compacta, commonly known as the compact Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a dense rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale markings. The leaves are short and rounded, giving the plant a compact and rounded appearance. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
