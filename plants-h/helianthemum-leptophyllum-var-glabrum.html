Helianthemum leptophyllum var. glabrum

Helianthemum leptophyllum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are covered in a thin layer of wax, helping it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
