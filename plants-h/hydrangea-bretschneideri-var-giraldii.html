Hydrangea bretschneideri var. giraldii

Hydrangea bretschneideri var. giraldii, commonly known as the mountain hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink and blue, depending on soil pH. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of large, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and thrives in partially shaded, moist environments.
