Hieracium omangii

Hieracium omangii, commonly known as the Omangii hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and often endangered species.
