Hieracium chevallieri

Hieracium chevallieri, commonly known as the Chevalier's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers that attract pollinators. Native to Europe, this perennial plant thrives in grassy areas and disturbed soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and can grow up to 30 centimeters tall.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
