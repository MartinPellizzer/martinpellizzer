Helichrysum suffruticosum

Helichrysum suffruticosum, commonly known as the golden weed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub-like species with yellow-orange flowers that resemble small sunflowers. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces numerous tiny, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy environments.

Helichrysum suffruticosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in some traditional medicinal practices.
