Habenaria pertenuis

Habenaria pertenuis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is known for its delicate appearance.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
