Hatiora cylindrica

Hatiora cylindrica, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular epiphytic plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, fleshy stems that are often adorned with vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is known for its seasonal blooming, typically during the holiday season, which contributes to its cultural significance.

Hatiora cylindrica is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
