Hieracium arvernense

Hieracium arvernense, commonly known as the Arvernes hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typical leaves. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring temperate climates. Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer, and it produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Hieracium arvernense is often found in disturbed soils and can form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
