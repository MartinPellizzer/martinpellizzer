Helichrysum chiliocephalum

Helichrysum chiliocephalum, commonly known as the yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain colorful for an extended period. The plant has small, narrow leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum chiliocephalum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
