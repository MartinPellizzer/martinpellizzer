Hypoxis argentea

Hypoxis argentea, commonly known as silver star, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in a star-like arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking foliage and long blooming period.
