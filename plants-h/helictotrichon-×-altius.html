Helictotrichon × altius

Helictotrichon × altius, commonly known as the tall bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, bluish-green foliage and slender, erect growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and prairies. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of small, spikelet-like flowers.

Helictotrichon × altius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and turfgrass management.
