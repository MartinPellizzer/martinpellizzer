Hieracium villosiceps subsp. trefferianum

Hieracium villosiceps subsp.

trefferianum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a single head. It thrives in moist, grassy habitats and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
