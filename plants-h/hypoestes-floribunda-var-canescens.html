Hypoestes floribunda var. canescens

Hypoestes floribunda var. canescens, commonly known as the woolly hypoestes, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bi-colored leaves that often display a combination of white and green hues. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to add visual interest to gardens.
