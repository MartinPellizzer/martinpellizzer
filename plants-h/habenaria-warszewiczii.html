Habenaria warszewiczii

Habenaria warszewiczii, commonly known as the Warszewicz's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with three sepals and three petals, typically arranged in a spike. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines.

This species thrives in moist, shaded habitats and is often found in forest understories.
