Habenaria arachnoides

Habenaria arachnoides, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance, with slender, hairy stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and swamps. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
