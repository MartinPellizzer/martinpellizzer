Hieracium kennedyi

Hieracium kennedyi, commonly known as Kennedy's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts. This perennial herb typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and it has finely divided, grass-like leaves.

The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in arid conditions and its role in supporting local pollinators.
