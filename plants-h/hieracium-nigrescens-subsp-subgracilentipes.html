Hieracium nigrescens subsp. subgracilentipes

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

subgracilentipes, commonly known as the slender yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender, graceful stems and relatively small, elongated leaves. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant produces small, wind-dispersed seeds, contributing to its ability to spread in open habitats.
