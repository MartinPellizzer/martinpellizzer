Hermione × bifrons var. princeps

Hermione × bifrons var. princeps, commonly known as the hybrid English ivy, is a vigorous climbing plant native to the United Kingdom.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy underside, and it produces small, pale yellow flowers in late summer. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a common garden and ornamental species. It is often used for covering walls, trellises, and as a ground cover due to its dense foliage.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
