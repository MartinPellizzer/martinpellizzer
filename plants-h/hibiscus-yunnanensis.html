Hibiscus yunnanensis

Hibiscus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hibiscus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Hibiscus yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
