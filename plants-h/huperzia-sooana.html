Huperzia sooana

Huperzia sooana, commonly known as the mountain huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is native to various regions in Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Huperzia sooana is often found growing on rocky substrates or in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
