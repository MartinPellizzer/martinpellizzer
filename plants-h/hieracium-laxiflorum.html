Hieracium laxiflorum

Hieracium laxiflorum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically from June to September. The plant has narrow, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.
