Hippeastrum citrinum

Hippeastrum citrinum, commonly known as the yellow hurricane lily, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with orange centers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that grow from a bulbous base, typical of the Amaryllidaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum citrinum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
