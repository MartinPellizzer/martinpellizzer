Hieracium caesium f. subgalbanum

Hieracium caesium f. subgalbanum, commonly known as the lesser yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy, basal leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has a rosette of leaves at the base and produces numerous flowering stems that rise above the foliage.

This subspecies is distinguished by its smaller flower heads and reduced leaf hairs compared to the typical form of H. caesium.
