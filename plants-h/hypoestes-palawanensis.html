Hypoestes palawanensis

Hypoestes palawanensis, commonly known as the Palawan Marvel, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored bracts that resemble flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while the flowers are small and inconspicuous, hidden beneath the colorful bracts.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
