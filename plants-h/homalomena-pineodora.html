Homalomena pineodora

Homalomena pineodora, commonly known as the Pineapple Philodendron, is a tropical evergreen climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a vibrant green color and a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
