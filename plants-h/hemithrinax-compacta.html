Hemithrinax compacta

Hemithrinax compacta, commonly known as the compact palm, is a flowering plant species native to the Caribbean.

It is a small, slow-growing palm with a short trunk and dense, feather-like fronds. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red fruits. It thrives in coastal and limestone soils, often found in dry, sunny environments.

Hemithrinax compacta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
