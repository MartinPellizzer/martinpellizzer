Hieracium dolichellum

Hieracium dolichellum, commonly known as the long-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

Hieracium dolichellum is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed habitats and is often found in areas with moderate to full sunlight.
