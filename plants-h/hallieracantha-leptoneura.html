Hallieracantha leptoneura

Hallieracantha leptoneura, commonly known as the slender-veined rock fig, is a woody shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves with prominent venation. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, spherical fruits. It thrives in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
