Hieracium barbareifolium

Hieracium barbareifolium, commonly known as the noble hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its yellow flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Hieracium barbareifolium is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
