Hippeastrum solandriflorum

Hippeastrum solandriflorum, commonly known as the Solander lily, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces large, showy blooms that typically appear in the summer months. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are evergreen in warmer climates.

Hippeastrum solandriflorum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
