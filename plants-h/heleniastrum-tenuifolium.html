Heleniastrum tenuifolium

Heleniastrum tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf helenea, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
