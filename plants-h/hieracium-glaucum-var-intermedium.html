Hieracium glaucum var. intermedium

Hieracium glaucum var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, with both ray and disk florets. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, whitish down.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its characteristic composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
