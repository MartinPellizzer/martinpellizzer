Hieracium × erythrocephalum

Hieracium × erythrocephalum, commonly known as the red-headed hawkweed, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to orange flower heads, which are typically surrounded by yellow ray florets. The plant produces a basal rosette of leaves that are hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
