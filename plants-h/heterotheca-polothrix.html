Heterotheca polothrix

Heterotheca polothrix, commonly known as the narrowleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the central and eastern United States.

Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough, hairy texture, and it is often found in sandy or gravelly soils.

Heterotheca polothrix is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
