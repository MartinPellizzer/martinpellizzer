Haworthia reinwardtii var. chalwinii

Haworthia reinwardtii var. chalwinii, commonly known as the Chalwin's Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy rosettes of dark green leaves with prominent white or pale markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular white or pink flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
