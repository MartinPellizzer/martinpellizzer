Hieracium pseudorionii subsp. pseudorionii

Hieracium pseudorionii subsp.

pseudorionii, commonly known as the Greek hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and basal rosettes of serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to full sun conditions. It has a compact growth habit with numerous flowering stems that rise from the base.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
