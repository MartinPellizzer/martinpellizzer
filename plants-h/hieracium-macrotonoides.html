Hieracium macrotonoides

Hieracium macrotonoides, commonly known as the large-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium macrotonoides is often found in temperate regions and can sometimes be invasive in non-native habitats.
