Hymenospermum dentatum

Hymenospermum dentatum, commonly known as the toothed hymenospermum, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically followed by distinctive, winged fruits. Its botanical features include a woody stem and tendrils that aid in climbing.

It is often found in tropical and subtropical forest environments.
