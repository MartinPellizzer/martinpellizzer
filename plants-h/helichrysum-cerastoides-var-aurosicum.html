Helichrysum cerastoides var. aurosicum

Helichrysum cerastoides var. aurosicum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, fluffy yellow or golden-orange inflorescences. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. It is drought-tolerant and often used in ornamental gardening for its long-lasting flowers.

The species is also valued for its aromatic properties and traditional medicinal uses.
