Helwingia Chinensis Var. Genuina

Helwingia chinensis var. genuina is a rare and delicate plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It is known for its striking, urn-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. This variety is often referred to as the "Chinese snowbell" due to its appearance and habitat. It thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in shaded forest environments. Its unique beauty and limited distribution make it a prized specimen among plant enthusiasts and botanists.
