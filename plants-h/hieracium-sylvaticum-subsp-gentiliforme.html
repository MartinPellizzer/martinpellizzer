Hieracium sylvaticum subsp. gentiliforme

Hieracium sylvaticum subsp.

gentiliforme, commonly known as the woodland hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers arranged in a composite head.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It has basal leaves that are lanceolate and often covered with fine hairs.

The subspecies gentiliforme is distinguished by its elongated flower heads and slightly different leaf morphology compared to other Hieracium species.
