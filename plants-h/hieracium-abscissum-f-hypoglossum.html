Hieracium abscissum f. hypoglossum

Hieracium abscissum f. hypoglossum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, which distinguishes it from other hawkweed species. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

This plant is often found in temperate regions of Europe and is noted for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
