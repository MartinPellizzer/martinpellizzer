Hieracium angustissimum

Hieracium angustissimum, commonly known as narrowleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium angustissimum is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
