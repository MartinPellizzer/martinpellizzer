Hibiscus leviseminus

Hibiscus leviseminus, commonly known as the slender hibiscus, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hibiscus leviseminus is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive blooms and adaptability to various climates.
