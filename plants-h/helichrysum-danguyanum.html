Helichrysum danguyanum

Helichrysum danguyanum, commonly known as the golden crown, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble a crown, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its aromatic foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum danguyanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
