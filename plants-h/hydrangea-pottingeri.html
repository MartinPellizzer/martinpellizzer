Hydrangea pottingeri

Hydrangea pottingeri, commonly known as the Japanese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from blue to pink depending on soil pH. The plant thrives in partial shade and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth. Its foliage is typically green, turning red or orange in the fall.

Hydrangea pottingeri is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to bloom multiple times throughout the growing season.
