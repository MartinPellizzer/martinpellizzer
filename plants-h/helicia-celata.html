Helicia celata

Helicia celata, commonly known as the Chinese holly or evergreen holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including China, India, and parts of the Himalayas. Helicia celata thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden settings.
