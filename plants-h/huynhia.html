Huynhia

Huynhia, commonly known as the golden bell tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Huynhia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a shade tree.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
