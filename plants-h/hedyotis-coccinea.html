Hedyotis coccinea

Hedyotis coccinea, commonly known as red flame or scarlet bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is a low-growing herb with small, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has thin, wiry stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Hedyotis coccinea is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
