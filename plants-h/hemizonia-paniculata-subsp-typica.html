Hemizonia paniculata subsp. typica

Hemizonia paniculata subsp.

typica, commonly known as the narrow-leafed panicled hemizonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or sandy habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
