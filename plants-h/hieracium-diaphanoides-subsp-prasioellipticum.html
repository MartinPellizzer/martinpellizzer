Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. prasioellipticum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

prasioellipticum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellowish-green leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers.

This subspecies is typically found in meadows and grasslands in temperate regions of Europe. It has a rosette growth habit and produces numerous flowering stems.
