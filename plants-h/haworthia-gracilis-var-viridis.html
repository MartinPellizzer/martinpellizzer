Haworthia gracilis var. viridis

Haworthia gracilis var. viridis, commonly known as the Green Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green color, often with subtle markings or variegation. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
