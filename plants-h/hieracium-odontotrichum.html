Hieracium odontotrichum

Hieracium odontotrichum, commonly known as the toothed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grassy areas and open woodlands. Hieracium odontotrichum is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
