Hoya hernaezii

Hoya hernaezii, commonly known as the "Hernaez's Waxplant," is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the summer months. Hoya hernaezii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its slow growth and minimal care requirements make it an attractive addition to both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
