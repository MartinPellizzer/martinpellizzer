Hesperis reuteriana

Hesperis reuteriana, commonly known as the German bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.

Hesperis reuteriana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
