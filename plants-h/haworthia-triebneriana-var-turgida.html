Haworthia triebneriana var. turgida

Haworthia triebneriana var. turgida, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
