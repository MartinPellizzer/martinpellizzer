Hybanthus concolor

Hybanthus concolor, commonly known as the smooth hybanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Hybanthus.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It produces small, spherical berries that contain the seeds.

Hybanthus concolor is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.
