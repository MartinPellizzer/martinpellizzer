Helosciadium inundatum

Helosciadium inundatum, commonly known as the water helosciadium, is a flowering plant native to wetland environments in Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and slightly succulent, adapted to retain moisture in soggy conditions.
