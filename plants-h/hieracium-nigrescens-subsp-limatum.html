Hieracium nigrescens subsp. limatum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

limatum, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering head that produces showy purple to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils in meadows and along stream banks. It has a taproot system and produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
