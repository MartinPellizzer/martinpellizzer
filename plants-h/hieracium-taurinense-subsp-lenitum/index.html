Hieracium Taurinense Subsp. Lenitum

Hieracium taurinense subsp. lenitum is a rare flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the dandelion family and is known for its delicate yellow flowers. This subspecies is often found in rocky or limestone areas, thriving in harsh environments. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered plant from habitat loss.
