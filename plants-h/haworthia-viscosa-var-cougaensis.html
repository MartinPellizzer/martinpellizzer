Haworthia viscosa var. cougaensis

Haworthia viscosa var. cougaensis, commonly known as the Cougaens Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The leaves are typically green with white or pale markings, and the plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor or rock garden cultivation.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
