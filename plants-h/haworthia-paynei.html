Haworthia paynei

Haworthia paynei, commonly known as the Payne's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
