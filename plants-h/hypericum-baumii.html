Hypericum baumii

Hypericum baumii, commonly known as the golden-stemmed St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in summer. Its stems are woody and often have a reddish or golden hue, contributing to its ornamental value.

Hypericum baumii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
