Holcolemma dispar

Holcolemma dispar, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically red or orange with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often mistaken for grasses or ferns. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Holcolemma dispar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
