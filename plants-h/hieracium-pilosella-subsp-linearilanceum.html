Hieracium pilosella subsp. linearilanceum

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

linearilanceum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate, with a hairy surface, and its flowers are yellow, arranged in a composite head.

It is often found in open, sunny habitats and is considered a characteristic species of certain grassland ecosystems.
