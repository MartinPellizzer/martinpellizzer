Hieracium pseudosevericeps

Hieracium pseudosevericeps, commonly known as the slender spearthistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous tiny florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, exhibiting a tall, spiny stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves. Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species.
