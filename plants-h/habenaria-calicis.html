Habenaria calicis

Habenaria calicis, commonly known as the calyx orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure, which includes a distinctive calyx-like structure around the flower. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually small and have a delicate, fragrant appearance, with colors ranging from white to pale pink.

Habenaria calicis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
