Hieracium paolovicii

Hieracium paolovicii, commonly known as the Paulovici hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to parts of Europe, particularly Romania. The plant typically grows in meadows and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Hieracium paolovicii is often found in areas with moderate sunlight and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
