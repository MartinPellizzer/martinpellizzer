Helichrysum goetzeanum

Helichrysum goetzeanum, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sunlight.

Helichrysum goetzeanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in dried flower arrangements.
