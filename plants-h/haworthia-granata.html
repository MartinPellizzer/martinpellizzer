Haworthia granata

Haworthia granata, commonly known as the red Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or pink markings, especially on the undersides. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Haworthia granata is prized for its compact size and unique aesthetic appeal.
