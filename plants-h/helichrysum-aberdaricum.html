Helichrysum aberdaricum

Helichrysum aberdaricum, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flowers that resemble miniature suns. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions. Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Helichrysum aberdaricum is also valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in herbal remedies.
