Hieracium × latisquamum subsp. diaporphyrum

Hieracium × latisquamum subsp.

diaporphyrum, commonly known as the broad-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of broad, spoon-shaped leaves.
