Helianthemum Variabile Var. Virescens

Helianthemum variabile var. virescens is a striking succulent native to southern Europe and North Africa. It is known for its compact, shrub-like form and vibrant green foliage that often features a bluish tint. This plant thrives in rocky soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in late spring, adding a cheerful touch to its appearance. Despite its delicate look, it is remarkably hardy and drought-tolerant, making it an excellent plant for low-maintenance gardens.
