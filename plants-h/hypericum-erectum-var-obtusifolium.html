Hypericum erectum var. obtusifolium

Hypericum erectum var. obtusifolium, commonly known as blunt-leaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems covered with small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with rounded tips, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, often found in woodland edges and rocky slopes.

This plant is noted for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
