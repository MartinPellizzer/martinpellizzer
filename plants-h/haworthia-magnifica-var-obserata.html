Haworthia magnifica var. obserata

Haworthia magnifica var. obserata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette shape and distinctive white to light green stripes on its fleshy, thick leaves. The plant is noted for its ability to store water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
