Hesperis aprica var. isatidea

Hesperis aprica var. isatidea, commonly known as the yellow horned poppy, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers with a distinctive horn-shaped center, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained soil in full sun.
