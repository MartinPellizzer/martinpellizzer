Hieracium × cryptastrum

Hieracium × cryptastrum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces a rosette of basal leaves that are usually hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its invasive potential, it is considered a noxious weed in many regions.
