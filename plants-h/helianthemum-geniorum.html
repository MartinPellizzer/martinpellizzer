Helianthemum geniorum

Helianthemum geniorum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are small, leathery, and arranged in a dense, compact manner.
