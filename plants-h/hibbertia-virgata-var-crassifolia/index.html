Hibbertia Virgata Var. Crassifolia

Hibbertia virgata var. crassifolia is a striking member of the Dilleniaceae family native to Australia. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive leaf patterns, this plant is a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. It thrives in coastal regions and is often found in sandy soils along the eastern coast of Australia. The variety crassifolia is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
