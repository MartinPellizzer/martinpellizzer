Hohenbergia nudicaulis

Hohenbergia nudicaulis, commonly known as the naked stem hohenbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery bloom, giving it a unique appearance. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale pink or white flowers.

Hohenbergia nudicaulis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
