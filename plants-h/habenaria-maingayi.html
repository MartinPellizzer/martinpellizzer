Habenaria maingayi

Habenaria maingayi, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, moist habitats and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
