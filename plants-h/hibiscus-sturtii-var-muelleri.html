Hibiscus sturtii var. muelleri

Hibiscus sturtii var. muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's Hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in shrub form, with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
