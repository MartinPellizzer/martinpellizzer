Heloniopsis orientalis subsp. breviscapa

Heloniopsis orientalis subsp.

breviscapa, commonly known as the narrow-capped heloniopsis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. It features slender, upright stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to botanical gardens and ecological restoration projects.
