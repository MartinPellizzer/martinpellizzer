Hedona sinensis

Hedona sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Hedona sinensis produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its traditional use in Chinese medicine.
