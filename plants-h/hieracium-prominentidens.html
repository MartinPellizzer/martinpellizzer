Hieracium prominentidens

Hieracium prominentidens, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium prominentidens is an herbaceous perennial that can spread aggressively through its rhizomes, often forming dense colonies.
