Hieracium grovesianum subsp. stenofolium

Hieracium grovesianum subsp.

stenofolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is sometimes considered invasive in certain regions.
