Habenaria dentata var. dentata

Habenaria dentata var. dentata, commonly known as the toothed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and have a distinctive tooth-like appearance on the petals.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
