Hieracium hypoleptolepis

Hieracium hypoleptolepis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium hypoleptolepis is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction, making it a common weed in various habitats.
