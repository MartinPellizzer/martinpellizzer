Helianthemum Apenninum Subsp. Estevei

Helianthemum apenninum subsp. estevei is a rare and striking plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the rockrose family, known for its hardy and drought-resistant traits. This subspecies is distinguished by its vibrant yellow flowers and compact growth habit. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, making it a symbol of resilience in harsh climates. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
