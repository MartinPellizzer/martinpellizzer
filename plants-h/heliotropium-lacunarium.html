Heliotropium lacunarium

Heliotropium lacunarium, commonly known as the slender heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features small, violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling those of the common heliotrope. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Heliotropium lacunarium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
