Hieracium aclados

Hieracium aclados, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a hairy stem and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Hieracium aclados is also noted for its role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
