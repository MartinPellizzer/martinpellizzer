Hieracium transiens subsp. balakderense

Hieracium transiens subsp.

balakderense, commonly known as the Balakderyan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets.

The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to ovate, with rough or hairy surfaces.

The flowers are yellow, with a central disk and surrounding ray florets, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
