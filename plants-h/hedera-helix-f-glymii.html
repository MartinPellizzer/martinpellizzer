Hedera helix f. glymii

Hedera helix f. glymii, commonly known as the Variegated Ivy, is a cultivar of the common ivy plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive variegated leaves, which feature a mix of green, white, and yellow colors. This plant is a climbing vine with dark green stems and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Hedera helix f. glymii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to cover walls or trellises.
