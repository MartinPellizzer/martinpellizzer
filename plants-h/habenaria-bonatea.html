Habenaria bonatea

Habenaria bonatea, commonly known as the Bonatea orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in forest understory environments, preferring moist and shaded conditions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

The orchid produces showy, greenish-yellow blooms that attract various pollinators.
