Hieracium poliocladum

Hieracium poliocladum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium poliocladum is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
