Hieracium vulgatum subsp. lonchodiforme

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

lonchodiforme, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss. It grows in a variety of habitats, including meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.
