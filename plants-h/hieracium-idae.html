Hieracium idae

Hieracium idae, commonly known as the Idaean hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, rough leaves. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, it thrives in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant has a rosette of basal leaves and produces numerous flowering stems.

It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand cold and harsh climatic conditions.
