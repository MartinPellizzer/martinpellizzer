Hieracium rivas-martinezii

Hieracium rivas-martinezii, commonly known as the Rivas-Martinez's hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant has basal leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive shape. It is native to Spain and is adapted to grow in rocky, nutrient-poor soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
