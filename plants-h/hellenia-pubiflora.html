Hellenia pubiflora

Hellenia pubiflora, commonly known as the hairy-flowered helena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers arranged in composite heads. Hellenia pubiflora thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
