Haworthia reinwardtii var. pseudocoarctata

Haworthia reinwardtii var. pseudocoarctata, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
