Hieracium santaniolense

Hieracium santaniolense, commonly known as the Santanion ragwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its yellowish to greenish composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. It has hairy stems and leaves with a serrated edge, and its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in certain areas of southern Europe.
