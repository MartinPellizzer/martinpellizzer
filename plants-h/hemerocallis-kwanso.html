Hemerocallis kwanso

Hemerocallis kwanso, commonly known as the Kwanso daylily, is a perennial flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The flowers typically come in shades of yellow, orange, and red, with a distinctive orange throat. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
