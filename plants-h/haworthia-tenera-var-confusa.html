Haworthia tenera var. confusa

Haworthia tenera var. confusa, commonly known as the compact haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
