Hieracium fulvasterum

Hieracium fulvasterum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Hieracium. Native to Europe, it is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
