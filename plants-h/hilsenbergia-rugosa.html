Hilsenbergia rugosa

Hilsenbergia rugosa, commonly known as the rough hilsenbergia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

Hilsenbergia rugosa is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand mild frost, though it may require protection in colder climates.
