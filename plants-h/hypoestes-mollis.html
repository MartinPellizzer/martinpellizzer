Hypoestes mollis

Hypoestes mollis, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated leaves that display shades of white, pink, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes mollis is popular as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
