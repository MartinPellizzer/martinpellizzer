Hypericum tosaense var. atumense

Hypericum tosaense var. atumense, commonly known as the Tosa Hypericum, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing small, clustered flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are woody and somewhat branched.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
