Hepetis ensifolia

Hepetis ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf hepetis, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually violet or purple in color, arranged in loose clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and wetland restoration projects.
