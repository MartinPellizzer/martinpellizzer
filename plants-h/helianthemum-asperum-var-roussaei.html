Helianthemum asperum var. roussaei

Helianthemum asperum var. roussaei, commonly known as the rough stonecrop, is a low-growing, succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in fine hairs, helping it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in sunny, well-drained areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in garden settings.
