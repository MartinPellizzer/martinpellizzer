Holothrix ledermannii

Holothrix ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's holothrix, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in mountainous regions and has a compact growth habit.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
