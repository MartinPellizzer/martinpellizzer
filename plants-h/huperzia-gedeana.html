Huperzia gedeana

Huperzia gedeana, commonly known as the Florida huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stems and dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and parts of Georgia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as pine flatwoods and wetlands.

Huperzia gedeana is often found growing in association with other epiphytic and terrestrial plants in its habitat.
