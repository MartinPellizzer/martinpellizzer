Haworthia chloracantha var. pulchella

Haworthia chloracantha var. pulchella, commonly known as the "Lamb's Ear Haworthia," is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-blue leaves with a soft, velvety texture and prominent white markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stalks during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
