Haworthia coarctatoides

Haworthia coarctatoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
