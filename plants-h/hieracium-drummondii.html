Hieracium drummondii

Hieracium drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and prairies. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a single head.

Drummond's hawkweed is a perennial species that can form dense colonies in suitable environments.
