Hedera protea

Hedera protea, commonly known as the African ivy, is a climbing plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits containing seeds. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Hedera protea is also valued for its ability to climb and trail, making it suitable for hanging baskets and wall coverings.
