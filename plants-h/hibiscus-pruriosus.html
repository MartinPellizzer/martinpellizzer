Hibiscus pruriosus

Hibiscus pruriosus, commonly known as the itchy hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near rivers or in humid forests.

Hibiscus pruriosus is also noted for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
