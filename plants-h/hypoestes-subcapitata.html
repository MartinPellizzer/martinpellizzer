Hypoestes subcapitata

Hypoestes subcapitata, commonly known as the "Snowflake" plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that resemble snowflakes. The leaves are typically ovate to lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
