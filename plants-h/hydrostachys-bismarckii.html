Hydrostachys bismarckii

Hydrostachys bismarckii, commonly known as the Bismarck grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, narrow leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Hydrostachys bismarckii is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in marginal environments.
