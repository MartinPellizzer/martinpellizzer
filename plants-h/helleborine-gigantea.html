Helleborine gigantea

Helleborine gigantea, commonly known as the giant helleborine, is a species of orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Helleborine gigantea is known for its ability to thrive in cool climates and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
