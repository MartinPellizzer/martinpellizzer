Haworthia chalwinii

Haworthia chalwinii, commonly known as the Chalwin's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a waxy coating. The leaves are usually green with distinctive white or pale markings, and the plant produces small, yellowish flowers on slender stems.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil to thrive.
