Hoya bacunganensis

Hoya bacunganensis, commonly known as the Hoya of Borneo, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asclepiadaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Borneo and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It is a climbing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Hoya bacunganensis is relatively low-maintenance and is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value.
