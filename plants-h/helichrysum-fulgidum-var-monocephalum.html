Helichrysum fulgidum var. monocephalum

Helichrysum fulgidum var. monocephalum, commonly known as the golden marguerite or Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance contribute to its ornamental value in horticulture.
