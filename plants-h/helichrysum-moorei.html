Helichrysum moorei

Helichrysum moorei, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, bract-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Helichrysum moorei is also known for its aromatic foliage and ability to retain color when dried, making it a favored plant for floral arrangements.
