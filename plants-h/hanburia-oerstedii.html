Hanburia oerstedii

Hanburia oerstedii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. Hanburia oerstedii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
