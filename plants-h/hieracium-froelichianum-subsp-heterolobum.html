Hieracium froelichianum subsp. heterolobum

Hieracium froelichianum subsp.

heterolobum, commonly known as the froelichian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowering heads.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. Its flowers are composed of both ray and disk florets, and it produces small, oval-shaped achenes as its seed form.

The subspecies heterolobum is distinguished by its unique leaf shape and floral structure compared to other members of the species.
