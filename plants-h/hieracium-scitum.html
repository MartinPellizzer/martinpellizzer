Hieracium scitum

Hieracium scitum, commonly known as the bent grass, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are typically clustered in dense heads. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Hieracium scitum is often found in temperate regions of Europe and is known for its ability to colonize disturbed or nutrient-rich soils.
