Hieracium echinodes

Hieracium echinodes, commonly known as the spiny hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Hieracium echinodes is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

It is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
