Hicorius ovata

Hicorius ovata, commonly known as the oval leaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hicorius ovata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
