Hieracium pilosella subsp. prenitense

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

prenitense, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky and grassy habitats at high elevations.

The plant has a basal rosette of leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a single head and bloom in the summer months.

It is characterized by its small, glandular leaves and is often found in mountainous areas with poor soil conditions.
