Haworthia reinwardtii f. chalumnensis

Haworthia reinwardtii f. chalumnensis, commonly known as the Chalumna Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks with white or pale pink flowers during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
