Hieracium norvegicum var. diodontum

Hieracium norvegicum var. diodontum, commonly known as the Norwegian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a taproot system and produces numerous flowering stems that bear small, daisy-like blooms.

This variety is distinguished by its finely divided, needle-like leaves and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.
