Hyptis floribunda

Hyptis floribunda, commonly known as Brazilian hyssop, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and aromatic. Its flowers are clustered in whorled inflorescences and range in color from white to purple.

Hyptis floribunda is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
