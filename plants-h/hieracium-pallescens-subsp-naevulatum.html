Hieracium pallescens subsp. naevulatum

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

naevulatum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Hieracium.

The subspecies naevulatum is distinguished by its pale green leaves and the presence of dark spots, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.
