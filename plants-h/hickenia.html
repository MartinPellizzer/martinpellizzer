Hickenia

Hickenia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Rhamnaceae.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, giving it its common name. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are opposite in arrangement and are often covered in fine hairs. Hickenia thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near riverbanks.

Its rapid growth makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes and soil stabilization.
