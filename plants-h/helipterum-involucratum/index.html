Helipterum Involucratum

Helipterum involucratum is a striking member of the Malvaceae family known for its vibrant red flowers and unique leaf structure. Native to Australia, this plant thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. Its clustered flowers resemble small umbrellas, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. The plant's ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it both resilient and adaptable. With its bold colors and ecological importance, Helipterum involucratum stands out as a remarkable native species.
