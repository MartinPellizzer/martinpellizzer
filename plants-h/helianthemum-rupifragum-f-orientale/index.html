Helianthemum Rupifragum F. Orientale

Helianthemum rupifragum f. orientale is a rare and hardy plant native to rocky and arid regions of the Mediterranean. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is known for its compact growth and vibrant yellow flowers. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking low-maintenance ornamental plants. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles.
