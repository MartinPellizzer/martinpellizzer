Hesperis tenella

Hesperis tenella, commonly known as the slender scabious, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbages and mustards. Hesperis tenella is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
