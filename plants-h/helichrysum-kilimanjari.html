Helichrysum kilimanjari

Helichrysum kilimanjari, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It produces small, bright yellow or orange flowers that remain upright and dry, giving it a lifelike appearance. The plant is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and is often found in rocky or alpine environments. It thrives in cool, sunny conditions and is adapted to the harsh climate of its native habitat.

Helichrysum kilimanjari is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
