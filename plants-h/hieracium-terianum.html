Hieracium terianum

Hieracium terianum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a composite head. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. It has a taproot system and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Hieracium terianum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to colonize various environments.
