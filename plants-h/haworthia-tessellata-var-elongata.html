Haworthia tessellata var. elongata

Haworthia tessellata var. elongata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves with distinctive white or light green markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The leaves are typically shorter and more elongated compared to the standard variety, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

This plant is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
