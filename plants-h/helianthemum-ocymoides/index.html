Helianthemum Ocymoides

Helianthemum ocymoides is a small, evergreen shrub native to the rocky landscapes of southern Europe. It is known for its delicate, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and coastal areas. Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking low-maintenance ornamental plants. Despite its modest size, Helianthemum ocymoides holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitats.
