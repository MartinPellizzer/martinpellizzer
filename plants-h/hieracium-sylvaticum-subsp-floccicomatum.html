Hieracium sylvaticum subsp. floccicomatum

Hieracium sylvaticum subsp.

floccicomatum, commonly known as the fluffy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its subspecific name, *floccicomatum*, refers to the fluffy appearance of its leaves and flowers.
