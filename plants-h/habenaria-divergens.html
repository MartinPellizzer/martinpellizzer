Habenaria divergens

Habenaria divergens, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with purple or brown markings.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
