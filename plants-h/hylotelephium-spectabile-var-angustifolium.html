Hylotelephium spectabile var. angustifolium

Hylotelephium spectabile var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stonecrop, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of fleshy, blue to purple flowers. The leaves are narrow, fleshy, and typically gray-green in color, adapted to retain moisture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
