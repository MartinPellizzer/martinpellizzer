Heliotropium tenuifolium var. annuum

Heliotropium tenuifolium var. annuum, commonly known as the slender-leaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often resembling those of the common heliotrope. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with thin, linear leaves and a branching stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
