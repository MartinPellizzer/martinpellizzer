Hieraciodes conyzifolia

Hieraciodes conyzifolia, commonly known as the woolly weed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, clustered yellow flowers. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has a sprawling growth habit and is often found in disturbed soils.
