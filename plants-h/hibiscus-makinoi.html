Hibiscus makinoi

Hibiscus makinoi, commonly known as Makino's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Hibiscus makinoi is a member of the Malvaceae family, which includes other hibiscus species.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
