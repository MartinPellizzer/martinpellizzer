Hieracium iratianum

Hieracium iratianum, commonly known as the Iratian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in sunny, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

It is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in disturbed areas.
