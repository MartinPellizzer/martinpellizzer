Helleborine microphylla f. firmior

Helleborine microphylla f. firmior, commonly known as the firm compact helleborine, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale green to yellowish flowers with three sepals and three petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
