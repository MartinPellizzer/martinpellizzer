Hieracium rosaemontis

Hieracium rosaemontis, commonly known as the mountain rose hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

Hieracium rosaemontis is often found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky slopes, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
