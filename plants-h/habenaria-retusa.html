Habenaria retusa

Habenaria retusa, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to various regions in Asia and the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and a dense, hairy inflorescence that bears numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are arranged in a spiral along the stem, a feature typical of the orchid family.

Habenaria retusa is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
