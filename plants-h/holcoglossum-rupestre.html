Holcoglossum rupestre

Holcoglossum rupestre, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, often in mountainous regions. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with a short rhizome and a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually small, pale green or yellowish, and are arranged in a spike.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
