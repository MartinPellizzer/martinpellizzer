Hieracium divisum subsp. pollichiae

Hieracium divisum subsp.

pollichiae, commonly known as the Pollich's divided hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and is native to Central Europe.

The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are divided into narrow segments, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable habitats.
