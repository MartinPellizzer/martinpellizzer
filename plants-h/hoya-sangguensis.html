Hoya sangguensis

Hoya sangguensis, commonly known as the Sanggu Hoya, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers. The plant produces fragrant, white to pinkish blooms that are typically borne on peduncles. Hoya sangguensis is a climbing or trailing plant that thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
