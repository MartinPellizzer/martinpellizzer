Horsfieldia laevigata var. laevigata

Horsfieldia laevigata var. laevigata, commonly known as the smooth horsfieldia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-shaped, often with a waxy coating.

This orchid prefers shaded, humid environments and is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.
