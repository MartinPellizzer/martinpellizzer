Hieracium × agronomon

Hieracium × agronomon, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems, typically growing in disturbed soils and open areas. The plant produces numerous small, yellow florets arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It is a perennial weed that spreads through both seeds and rhizomes, making it invasive in some regions.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions, contributing to its widespread distribution.
