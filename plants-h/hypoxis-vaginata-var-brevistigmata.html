Hypoxis vaginata var. brevistigmata

Hypoxis vaginata var. brevistigmata, commonly known as the narrow-stigma goldenstar, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with six petals, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
