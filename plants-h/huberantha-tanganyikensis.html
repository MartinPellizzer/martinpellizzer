Huberantha tanganyikensis

Huberantha tanganyikensis, commonly known as the Lake Tanganyika water lily, is a unique aquatic plant native to the Lake Tanganyika region in East Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that provide habitat for various aquatic organisms. The plant has a short, stout stem with roots that anchor it to the lake bed. Its flowers are small and white, typically blooming at night, which attracts specific pollinators.

Due to its restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species within its environment.
