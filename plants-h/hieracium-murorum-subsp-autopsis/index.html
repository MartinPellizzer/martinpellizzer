Hieracium Murorum Subsp. Autopsis

Hieracium murorum subsp. autopsis is a lesser-known member of the daisy family native to mountainous regions of Europe. This subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure. It thrives in rocky and disturbed soils, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant plant. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss and environmental degradation.
