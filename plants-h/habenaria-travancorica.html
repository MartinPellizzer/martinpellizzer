Habenaria travancorica

Habenaria travancorica, commonly known as the Travancore orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
