Helianthemum vulgare var. heterophyllum

Helianthemum vulgare var. heterophyllum, commonly known as the variable rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
