Hypericum olympicum f. minus

Hypericum olympicum f. minus, commonly known as the lesser goldenstar, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It features dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
