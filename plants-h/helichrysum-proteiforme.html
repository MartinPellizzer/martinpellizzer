Helichrysum proteiforme

Helichrysum proteiforme, commonly known as the Cape gold or protea gold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble small sunflowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in full sun.

The flowers are typically short-lived but are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant color and longevity.
