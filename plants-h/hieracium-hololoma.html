Hieracium hololoma

Hieracium hololoma, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a cluster, and the leaves are often hairy and serrated.

Hieracium hololoma is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
