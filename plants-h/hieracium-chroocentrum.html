Hieracium chroocentrum

Hieracium chroocentrum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped or lanceolate. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

It thrives in moist meadows and woodland edges, often forming dense colonies.
