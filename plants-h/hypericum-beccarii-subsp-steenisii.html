Hypericum beccarii subsp. steenisii

Hypericum beccarii subsp.

steenisii, commonly known as the Bornean St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo. It is characterized by its yellow flowers and opposite leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is known for its medicinal properties. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and valuable species in both ecological and pharmacological contexts.
