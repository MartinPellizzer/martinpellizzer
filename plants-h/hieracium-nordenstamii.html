Hieracium nordenstamii

Hieracium nordenstamii, commonly known as the Nordenstam's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a dense cluster.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
