Hieracium ctenodon subsp. tephrosoma

Hieracium ctenodon subsp.

tephrosoma, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in meadows and grasslands.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinct appearance.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is often considered a weed in agricultural areas.
