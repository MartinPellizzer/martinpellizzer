Hypericum assurgens

Hypericum assurgens, commonly known as the yellow St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring woody stems and opposite, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Hypericum assurgens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
