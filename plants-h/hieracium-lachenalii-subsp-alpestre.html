Hieracium lachenalii subsp. alpestre

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

alpestre, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow flowering heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers are usually arranged in a single, terminal head and are surrounded by bracts.

This subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and is often found in mountainous habitats across the Alps.
