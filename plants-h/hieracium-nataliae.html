Hieracium nataliae

Hieracium nataliae, commonly known as Natal's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.

Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its native arid environment.
