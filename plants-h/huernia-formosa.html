Huernia formosa

Huernia formosa, commonly known as the horned African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, which bloom during the warmer months. Its unique appearance and ability to store water make it well-suited for arid environments.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
