Haworthia paradoxa var. jakubii

Haworthia paradoxa var. jakubii, commonly known as the Jakub's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings, often resembling a tiger's stripes. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
