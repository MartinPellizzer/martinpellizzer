Hieracium pallidum subsp. arcicola

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

arcicola, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

The plant features erect, hairy stems and produces yellow flowers in early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads.
