Hieracium orthotrichum

Hieracium orthotrichum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

Hieracium orthotrichum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
