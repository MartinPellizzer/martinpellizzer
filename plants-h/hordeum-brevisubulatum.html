Hordeum brevisubulatum

Hordeum brevisubulatum, commonly known as the short-subulate barley, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it is adapted to cold and arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Hordeum brevisubulatum is often found in association with other high-altitude flora, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
