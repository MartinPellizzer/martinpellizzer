Henrya costata

Henrya costata, commonly known as the Japanese snowbell, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate floral display. The plant has broad, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its pale flowers. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Henrya costata is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
