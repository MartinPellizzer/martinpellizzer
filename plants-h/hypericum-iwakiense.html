Hypericum iwakiense

Hypericum iwakiense, commonly known as the Iwaki St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum iwakiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
