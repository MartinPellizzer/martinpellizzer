Hieracium subrasile

Hieracium subrasile, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers that consist of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and its stems are usually unbranched.

Hieracium subrasile is native to Europe and is considered a perennial species.
