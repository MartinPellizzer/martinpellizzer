Hypericum nakaii var. nanum

Hypericum nakaii var. nanum, commonly known as the dwarf goldthread, is a low-growing perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
