Hibiscus waimeae

Hibiscus waimeae, commonly known as the Waimea hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found growing in coastal areas and rocky habitats. Hibiscus waimeae is a member of the Malvaceae family, which includes other hibiscus species.

It plays an important ecological role in Hawaiian ecosystems and is considered a rare and endangered species.
