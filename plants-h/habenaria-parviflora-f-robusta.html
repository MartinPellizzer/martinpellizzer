Habenaria parviflora f. robusta

Habenaria parviflora f. robusta, commonly known as the robust miniature orchid, is a compact, epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, stout rhizome and produces small, pale green to white flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its compact appearance.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
