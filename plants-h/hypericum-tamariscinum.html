Hypericum tamariscinum

Hypericum tamariscinum, commonly known as the yellow St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Hypericum tamariscinum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its purported healing benefits.
