Hardenbergia lindleyi

Hardenbergia lindleyi, commonly known as the bluebonnet, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and a robust root system that helps it thrive in sandy soils. It is often found in coastal regions and is well adapted to full sun and dry conditions.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
