Hieracium gemellum

Hieracium gemellum, commonly known as the twindevil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and woodland edges. The plant features hairy, upright stems and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers in clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

Hieracium gemellum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
