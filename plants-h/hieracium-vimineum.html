Hieracium vimineum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its wiry, creeping stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are typically alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.
