Hedyotis obscura

Hedyotis obscura, commonly known as the dark knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to various regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, with five petals and prominent stamens. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

Hedyotis obscura is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
