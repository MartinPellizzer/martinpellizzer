Hieracium orthophrixum

Hieracium orthophrixum, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
