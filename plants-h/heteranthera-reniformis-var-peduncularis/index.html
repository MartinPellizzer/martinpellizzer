Heteranthera Reniformis Var. Peduncularis

Heteranthera reniformis var. peduncularis is a striking aquatic plant native to the wetlands of South America. It is known for its unique heart-shaped leaves and vibrant orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. This variety is often favored by aquatic gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care. It thrives in shallow waters and can be easily propagated through rhizomes or seeds. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both natural and artificial pond environments.
