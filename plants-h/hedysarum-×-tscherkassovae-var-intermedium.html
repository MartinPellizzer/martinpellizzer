Hedysarum × tscherkassovae var. intermedium

Hedysarum × tscherkassovae var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate Persian peashrub, is a hybrid species within the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods containing several seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
