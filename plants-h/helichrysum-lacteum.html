Helichrysum lacteum

Helichrysum lacteum, commonly known as the yellow helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant color and long-lasting nature. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are arranged in a compact, bushy form. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

The flowers are also known for their subtle, sweet fragrance, which adds to their ornamental appeal.
