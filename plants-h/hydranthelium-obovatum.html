Hydranthelium obovatum

Hydranthelium obovatum, commonly known as the broad-ovate hydranthelium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Hydranthelaceae.

It is characterized by its obovate leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Hydranthelium obovatum is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
