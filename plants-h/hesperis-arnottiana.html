Hesperis arnottiana

Hesperis arnottiana, commonly known as the Arnott's honesty, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white, four-petaled flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hesperis arnottiana is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
