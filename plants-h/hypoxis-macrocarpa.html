Hypoxis macrocarpa

Hypoxis macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

Hypoxis macrocarpa is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
