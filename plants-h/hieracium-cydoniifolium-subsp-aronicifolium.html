Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp. aronicifolium

Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp.

aronicifolium, commonly known as the aronicifolium hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped with a rough texture and often have a distinctive reddish or purplish hue. The plant produces yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands, wastelands, and along roadsides.
