Helminthia echioides var. mollis

Helminthia echioides var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-hairy helminthia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. The plant has hairy, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and delicate floral display.
