Hyptis conferta

Hyptis conferta, commonly known as silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Hyptis conferta is also valued for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses in treating various ailments.
