Helichrysum tephrodes

Helichrysum tephrodes, commonly known as the silver helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers and silver-gray foliage, which give it a striking appearance. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, also known as the daisy or sunflower family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
