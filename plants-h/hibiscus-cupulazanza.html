Hibiscus cupulazanza

Hibiscus cupulazanza, commonly known as the cupula hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, somewhat aromatic scent. Hibiscus cupulazanza thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its potential medicinal properties.
