Hieracium stoedvarense

Hieracium stoedvarense, commonly known as the Stoevaren yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish flowers and basal rosette of leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils. The plant produces numerous small, dandelion-like flowers that bloom in the summer months.

It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks.
