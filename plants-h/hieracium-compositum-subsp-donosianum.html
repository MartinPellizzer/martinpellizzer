Hieracium compositum subsp. donosianum

Hieracium compositum subsp.

donosianum, commonly known as the Donosian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to ovate, often with hairy surfaces.

Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, producing yellowish to pale orange flowers that attract pollinators.
