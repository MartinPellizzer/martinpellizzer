Habenaria intermedia

Habenaria intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, upright stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow to reddish-brown flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom in late summer or early autumn. It prefers moist, shaded habitats and is often found in forest understories.

The orchid has a complex root system and is known for its ability to form mycorrhizal associations with fungi.
