Helianthus Atrorubens Subsp. Radula

Helianthus atrorubens subsp. radula is a rare and striking plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the sunflower family and is known for its vibrant red and orange flowers. this subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf structure and compact growth habit. it thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
