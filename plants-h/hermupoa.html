Hermupoa

Hermupoa, commonly known as the "Cape umbrella plant," is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a compact, umbrella-like shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Hermupoa is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its striking foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
