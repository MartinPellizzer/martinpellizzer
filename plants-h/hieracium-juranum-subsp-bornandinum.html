Hieracium juranum subsp. bornandinum

Hieracium juranum subsp.

bornandinum, commonly known as the Bornandin hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring well-drained conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
