Hieracium chiamuerae

Hieracium chiamuerae, commonly known as the Chiamuera hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow to orange composite flowers. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in grassy and open habitats. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in dry environments.

It is often found in alpine and subalpine areas, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
