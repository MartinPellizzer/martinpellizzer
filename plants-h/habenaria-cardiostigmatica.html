Habenaria cardiostigmatica

Habenaria cardiostigmatica, commonly known as the heart-stigma orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, upright stem with several ovate, dark green leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive heart-shaped lip, which is a key identifying feature. It typically blooms during the rainy season, showcasing its vibrant colors against the lush forest understory.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its habitat.
