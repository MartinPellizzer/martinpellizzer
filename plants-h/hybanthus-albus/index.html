Hybanthus Albus

Hybanthus albus is a delicate flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and South America. It is known for its small, white, funnel-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the year. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its unique appearance and gentle growth habits make it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
