Heterosamara resinosa

Heterosamara resinosa, commonly known as the resinous heterosamara, is a flowering plant species native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its unique resinous secretions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with both male and female structures, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

The resinous sap of Heterosamara resinosa has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
