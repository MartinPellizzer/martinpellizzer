Hedera helix var. scutifolia

Hedera helix var. scutifolia, commonly known as the shield-leaf ivy, is a variant of the common ivy characterized by its distinctive shield-shaped leaves.

This evergreen climbing plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in temperate climates. Its foliage is typically deep green with a slightly waxy texture, and it produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring. The plant is well-suited for vertical gardening and can be grown in hanging baskets or trained on walls.

It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for both indoor and outdoor settings.
