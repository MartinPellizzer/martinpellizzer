Haloragis capensis

Haloragis capensis, commonly known as the Cape rush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Haloragaceae and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found near coastal areas.

Haloragis capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
