Hieracium armerioides subsp. siphonophorum

Hieracium armerioides subsp.

siphonophorum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers.

Its subspecies status reflects subtle morphological differences from the main species, often noted in its leaf structure and flower arrangement.
