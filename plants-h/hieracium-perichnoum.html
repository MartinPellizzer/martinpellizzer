Hieracium perichnoum

Hieracium perichnoum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers and hairy, basal leaves. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and sandy soils, preferring temperate climates. It has a creeping growth habit with rhizomes that allow it to spread vegetatively.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
