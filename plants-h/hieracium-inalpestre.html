Hieracium inalpestre

Hieracium inalpestre, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine soils, thriving in high-altitude environments. It features lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often found in meadows and grasslands at elevations above 1,500 meters.
