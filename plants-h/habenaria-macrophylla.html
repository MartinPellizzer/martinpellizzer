Habenaria macrophylla

Habenaria macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf sword orchid, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy orchid flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
