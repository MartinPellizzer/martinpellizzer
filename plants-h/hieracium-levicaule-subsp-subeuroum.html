Hieracium levicaule subsp. subeuroum

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

subeuroum, commonly known as the European meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower.

It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.
