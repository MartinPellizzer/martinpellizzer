Hieracium peleterianum subsp. vansoestii

Hieracium peleterianum subsp.

vansoestii, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides. It has a taproot system and produces achenes as its fruits, which are dispersed by wind.

Its botanical features make it an important component of grassland ecosystems.
