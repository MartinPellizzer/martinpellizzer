Homalomena rusdii

Homalomena rusdii, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive leaf structure and inflorescence. The plant features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. Its rhizomes are used for their starchy content, making it valuable in traditional cuisine.
