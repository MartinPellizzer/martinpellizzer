Hoya barbata

Hoya barbata, commonly known as the "snowy hoya," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, giving it a delicate appearance. Hoya barbata is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

It is also noted for its slow growth and ability to produce offsets, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
