Hieracium litwinowianum subsp. ermaniense

Hieracium litwinowianum subsp.

ermaniense, commonly known as the Ermenian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaf arrangement.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head, with ray florets that are usually shorter than the central disk.

It is considered a rare and endemic species, contributing to the biodiversity of its native region.
