Helichrysum kitianum

Helichrysum kitianum, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain open during the day. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its spread. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
