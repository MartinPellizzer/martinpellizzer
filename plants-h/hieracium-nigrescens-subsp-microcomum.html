Hieracium nigrescens subsp. microcomum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

microcomum, commonly known as the small-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats and has small, hairy leaves that are usually less than 1 cm in length. Its flowers are yellow and are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the genus Hieracium.

It is often found in meadows and along stream banks, where it thrives in damp conditions.
