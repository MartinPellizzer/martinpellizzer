Hieracium dialeptum

Hieracium dialeptum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape, often with hairy surfaces. It is native to Europe and is commonly found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

Hieracium dialeptum is a perennial species that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
