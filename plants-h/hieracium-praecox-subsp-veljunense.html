Hieracium praecox subsp. veljunense

Hieracium praecox subsp.

veljunense, commonly known as the Veljunian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange flower heads.

The plant typically features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist meadows and grasslands, often forming dense colonies.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
