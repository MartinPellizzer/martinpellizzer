Haworthia altilinea var. inermis

Haworthia altilinea var. inermis, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinct white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
