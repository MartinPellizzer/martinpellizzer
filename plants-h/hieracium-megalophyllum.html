Hieracium megalophyllum

Hieracium megalophyllum, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Hieracium megalophyllum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.
