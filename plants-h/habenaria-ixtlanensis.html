Habenaria ixtlanensis

Habenaria ixtlanensis, commonly known as the Ixtlan orchid, is a rare species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a unique coloration pattern.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
