Hippuris maritima

Hippuris maritima, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its bright green, fleshy leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. Hippuris maritima plays an important ecological role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining the health of coastal ecosystems.
