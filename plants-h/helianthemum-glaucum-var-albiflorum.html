Helianthemum glaucum var. albiflorum

Helianthemum glaucum var. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe.

It features silver-gray foliage and produces clusters of white flowers in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun exposure. It is drought-tolerant and often found in coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
