Helianthemum sericeum

Helianthemum sericeum, commonly known as silver cushion carnation, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense clusters of small, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has silvery-gray foliage covered in fine, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is cultivated in gardens and used in landscaping.
