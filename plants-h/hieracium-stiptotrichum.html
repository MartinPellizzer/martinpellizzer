Hieracium stiptotrichum

Hieracium stiptotrichum, commonly known as the spiked hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features showy yellow flowers arranged in composite heads. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are hairy and often reddish in color. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

This plant is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
