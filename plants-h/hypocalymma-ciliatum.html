Hypocalymma ciliatum

Hypocalymma ciliatum, commonly known as the silky hypocalymma, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hypocalymma ciliatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
