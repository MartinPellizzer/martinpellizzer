Hypericum richeri var. androsaemifolium

Hypericum richeri var. androsaemifolium, commonly known as the Androsaemifolium St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers with prominent stamens. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
