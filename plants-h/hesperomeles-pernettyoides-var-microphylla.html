Hesperomeles pernettyoides var. microphylla

Hesperomeles pernettyoides var. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf pineapple guava, is a compact shrub native to southern Chile.

It features small, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its fruit is a small, sweet, and aromatic berry with a texture similar to a pineapple.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible fruit, making it a popular choice for gardens and small-scale cultivation.
