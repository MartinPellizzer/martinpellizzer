Hieracium alpinum subsp. nigrosetosum

Hieracium alpinum subsp.

nigrosetosum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dark, bristly hairs, which give it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas, thriving in cool, moist environments.

This plant is often found at elevations above 1,500 meters, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
