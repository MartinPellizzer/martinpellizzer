Helicia erratica var. sinica

Helicia erratica var. sinica, commonly known as the Chinese holly or Chinese helicia, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers are usually white or pale pink.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
