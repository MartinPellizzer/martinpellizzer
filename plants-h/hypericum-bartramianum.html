Hypericum bartramianum

Hypericum bartramianum, commonly known as the Bartram's St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, wooded areas and along stream banks. The plant features bright yellow flowers with orange stamens, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are oval and slightly hairy, and the plant has a woody stem with reddish-brown bark.

Hypericum bartramianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in herbal medicine.
