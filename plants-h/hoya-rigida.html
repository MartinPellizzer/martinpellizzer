Hoya rigida

Hoya rigida, commonly known as the rigid wax plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Hoya rigida is a climbing plant that thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
