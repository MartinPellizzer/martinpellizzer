Hornstedtia reticosa

Hornstedtia reticosa, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its delicate, pale green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. Its intricate network of veins on the leaves gives it its common name.

Hornstedtia reticosa is typically found in shaded, moist habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
