Helichrysum youngii

Helichrysum youngii, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers that resemble small cups. The plant has narrow, silver-green leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its drought tolerance and attractive appearance.

Helichrysum youngii is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
