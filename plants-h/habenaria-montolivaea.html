Habenaria montolivaea

Habenaria montolivaea, commonly known as the Montoliva orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in forested areas with high humidity. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive, spurred lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
