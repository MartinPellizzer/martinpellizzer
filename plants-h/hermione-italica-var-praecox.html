Hermione italica var. praecox

Hermione italica var. praecox, commonly known as early Italian Hermione, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to white, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice among gardeners in temperate climates.
