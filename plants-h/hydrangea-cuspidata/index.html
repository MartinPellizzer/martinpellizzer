Hydrangea Cuspidata

Hydrangea cuspidata is a striking shrub known for its vibrant, cone-shaped blooms that change color with the seasons. Native to eastern Asia, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its unique foliage and seasonal color shifts make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking visual interest. The plant's adaptability allows it to flourish in a variety of climates, enhancing its appeal. With proper care, Hydrangea cuspidata can provide stunning floral displays throughout the growing season.
