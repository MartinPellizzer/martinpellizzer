Horsfieldia nunu

Horsfieldia nunu, commonly known as the Nunu orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often clustered in a raceme. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Horsfieldia nunu is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
