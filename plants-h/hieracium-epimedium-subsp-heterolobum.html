Hieracium epimedium subsp. heterolobum

Hieracium epimedium subsp.

heterolobum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flower heads.

The plant typically grows in moist, grassy areas and has hairy stems and leaves. Its flowers are discoid, with many small tubular florets surrounding a central cluster.

It is often found in meadows and along stream banks, where it thrives in temperate climates.
