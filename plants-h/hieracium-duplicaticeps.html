Hieracium duplicaticeps

Hieracium duplicaticeps, commonly known as the double-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a single inflorescence. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and covered in fine pubescence. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas.

It is an annual or biennial herb that produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.
