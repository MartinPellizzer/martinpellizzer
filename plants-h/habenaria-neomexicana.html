Habenaria neomexicana

Habenaria neomexicana, commonly known as the New Mexico butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southwestern United States.

It typically grows in open, rocky habitats with well-drained soil. The plant features a sympodial growth pattern, with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. Its flowers are showy, with a distinctive butterfly-like appearance, often in shades of white and purple.

It blooms in late spring to early summer, contributing to the biodiversity of its native ecosystem.
