Hieracium alatum subsp. hypocoleum

Hieracium alatum subsp.

hypocoleum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant features a hairy stem with rough, lobed leaves that are often glandular-dotted. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the genus Hieracium.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and woodland edges.
