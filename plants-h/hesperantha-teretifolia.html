Hesperantha teretifolia

Hesperantha teretifolia, commonly known as the slender bell orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with high light exposure. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable environment.
