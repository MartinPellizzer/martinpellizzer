Haworthia nigra

Haworthia nigra, commonly known as the Black Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves. The leaves are thick, fleshy, and often have a dark green to almost black coloration, with white or pale markings. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
