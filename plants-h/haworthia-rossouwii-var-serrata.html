Haworthia rossouwii var. serrata

Haworthia rossouwii var. serrata, commonly known as the serrated Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The leaves are often edged with sharp, serrated teeth, giving it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor settings.
