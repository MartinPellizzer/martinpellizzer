Hieracium subulatidens

Hieracium subulatidens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and wastelands. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters.
