Hieracium glaucinum subsp. guaranum

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

guaranum, commonly known as the Brazilian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.
