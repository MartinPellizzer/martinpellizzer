Hieracium varianiceps

Hieracium varianiceps, commonly known as the variable hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features basal and stem leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Hieracium varianiceps is known for its variability in leaf and flower morphology, which contributes to its common name.
