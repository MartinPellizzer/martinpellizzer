Hebe petriei var. murrellii

Hebe petriei var. murrellii, commonly known as Murrell's hebe, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, evergreen foliage that ranges from dark green to a bluish-gray hue. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple or violet during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
