Humiria balsamifera var. stenocarpa

Humiria balsamifera var. stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited balsam tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, resinous leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule-like fruits that are typically narrow and elongated, as suggested by its varietal name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
