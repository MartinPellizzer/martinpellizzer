Hypericum otaruense

Hypericum otaruense, commonly known as the Otaru St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes numerous species of flowering shrubs. The plant is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and woody stems, typically growing in rocky or coastal environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
