Humboltia acianthera

Humboltia acianthera, commonly known as the white-flowered hummingbird orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Humboltia acianthera thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
