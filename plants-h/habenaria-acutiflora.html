Habenaria acutiflora

Habenaria acutiflora, commonly known as the acute-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Indonesia and the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
