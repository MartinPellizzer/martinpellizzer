Hieracium Purpureibracteum

Hieracium purpureibracteum, commonly known as the purple bract hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers. This plant thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its striking appearance makes it a popular subject in botanical studies and garden landscapes. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in certain regions, posing challenges for ecosystem management.
