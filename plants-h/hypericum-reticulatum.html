Hypericum reticulatum

Hypericum reticulatum, commonly known as the yellow archangel, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and serrated leaves that are often reddish underneath. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Hypericum reticulatum is known for its attractive appearance and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
