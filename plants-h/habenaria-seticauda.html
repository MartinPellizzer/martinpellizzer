Habenaria seticauda

Habenaria seticauda, commonly known as the slender-tailed orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while the flowers are small and resemble those of other orchids.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its beauty.
