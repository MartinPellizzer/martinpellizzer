Hoya clemensiorum

Hoya clemensiorum, commonly known as the Snowy Hoya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tint. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Hoya clemensiorum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
