Hieracium subspeciosum subsp. delasoiei

Hieracium subspeciosum subsp.

delasoiei, commonly known as the Delasoie's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets that are often shorter than the central disk florets.

This subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and floral structure compared to other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
