Hydriastele macrospadix

Hydriastele macrospadix, commonly known as the giant spathiphyllum, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy spadix surrounded by a broad, heart-shaped spathe. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its unique inflorescence structure. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often reaching several feet in length.
