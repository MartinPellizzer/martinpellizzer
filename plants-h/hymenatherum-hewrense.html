Hymenatherum hewrense

Hymenatherum hewrense, commonly known as the Hewren's flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

Hymenatherum hewrense is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife and improving soil fertility.
