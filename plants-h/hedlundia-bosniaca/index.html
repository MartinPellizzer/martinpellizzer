Hedlundia Bosniaca

Hedlundia bosniaca is a rare and delicate plant native to the mountainous regions of Bosnia and Herzegovina. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest floors. This species is known for its small, intricate flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
