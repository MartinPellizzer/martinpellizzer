Hieracium leucoclonum

Hieracium leucoclonum, commonly known as the white hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its bright white flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Hieracium leucoclonum is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
