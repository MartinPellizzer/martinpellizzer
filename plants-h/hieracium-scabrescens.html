Hieracium scabrescens

Hieracium scabrescens, commonly known as rough hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium scabrescens is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
