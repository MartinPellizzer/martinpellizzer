Haworthia Arachnoidea Var. Tenera

Tender haworthia, scientifically known as Haworthia arachnoidea var. tenera, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. Its most distinctive feature is its dense, velvety white hairs that cover its rosette-shaped leaves, giving it a unique and almost otherworldly appearance. This plant is prized for its compact size and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice among indoor gardeners. Despite its delicate look, it is remarkably hardy and can thrive in a variety of light conditions. Its ability to store water in its leaves makes it well-suited for arid environments and indoor spaces with minimal care.
