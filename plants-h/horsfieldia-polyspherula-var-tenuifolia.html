Horsfieldia polyspherula var. tenuifolia

Horsfieldia polyspherula var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticulture.
