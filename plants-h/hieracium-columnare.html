Hieracium columnare

Hieracium columnare, commonly known as the columnar hawkweed, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium columnare is often found in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
