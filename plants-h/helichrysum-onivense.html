Helichrysum onivense

Helichrysum onivense, commonly known as the silver leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that remain intact for a long period after blooming. The plant features silver-gray foliage with small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum onivense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in dried floral arrangements.
