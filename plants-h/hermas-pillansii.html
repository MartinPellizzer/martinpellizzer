Hermas pillansii

Hermas pillansii, commonly known as the Pillans' ericoid, is a unique succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, Hermas pillansii is considered a rare and endangered species.
