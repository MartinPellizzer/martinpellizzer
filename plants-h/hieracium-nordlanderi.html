Hieracium nordlanderi

Hieracium nordlanderi, commonly known as the Nordland hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to northern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges. Its yellow-orange flowers bloom in late summer, and it produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

Hieracium nordlanderi is notable for its robust growth and adaptability to temperate climates.
