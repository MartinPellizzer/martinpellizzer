Henckelia virginea

Henckelia virginea, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered together. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Henckelia virginea is also known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
