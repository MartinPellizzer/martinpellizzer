Habenaria aberrans

Habenaria aberrans, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Habenaria aberrans is known for its variability in flower color and morphology, which contributes to its common name.
