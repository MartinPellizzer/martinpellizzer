Homalomena klossii

Homalomena klossii, commonly known as the Kloss's arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Homalomena klossii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
