Haworthiopsis scabra var. morrisiae

Haworthiopsis scabra var. morrisiae, commonly known as the Morris' Haworthiopsis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a textured, waxy surface. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
