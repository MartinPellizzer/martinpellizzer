Hakea sessilis

Hakea sessilis, commonly known as the coastal hakea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves that are typically grey-green in color. The plant produces striking, spherical yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is well adapted to coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils.

Hakea sessilis is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
