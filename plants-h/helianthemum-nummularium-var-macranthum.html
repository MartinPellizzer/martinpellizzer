Helianthemum nummularium var. macranthum

Helianthemum nummularium var. macranthum, commonly known as the broad-leaved stonecrop, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically rounded and densely packed, forming a rosette structure. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its hardy nature and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant landscapes.
