Hieracium corconticum

Hieracium corconticum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium corconticum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some environments.
