Hieracium cydoniifolium var. calvescens

Hieracium cydoniifolium var. calvescens, commonly known as the strawberry nettle, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides. It has a creeping growth habit with a central stem that branches profusely.

The species is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in certain habitats.
