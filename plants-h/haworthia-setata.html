Haworthia setata

Haworthia setata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in white or pale green tubercles, giving it a distinctive zebra-like appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Haworthia setata is prized for its low maintenance and unique aesthetic, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
