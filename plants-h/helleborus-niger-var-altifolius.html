Helleborus niger var. altifolius

Helleborus niger var. altifolius, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Alps.

It features dark green, palmately lobed leaves and produces striking, waxy white flowers with a pinkish tinge. The plant is noted for its ability to thrive in shaded, moist woodland environments. It is particularly valued for its winter blooming habit, often flowering from late winter to early spring.

The species is also cultivated for its ornamental value and is considered a symbol of purity and protection.
