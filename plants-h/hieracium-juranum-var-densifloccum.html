Hieracium juranum var. densifloccum

Hieracium juranum var. densifloccum, commonly known as the dense flocculent hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are basal, oblanceolate, and covered in fine, white hairs, giving them a soft, fuzzy appearance.

The inflorescence consists of numerous small yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets.
