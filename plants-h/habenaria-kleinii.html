Habenaria kleinii

Habenaria kleinii, commonly known as the Klein's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Habenaria kleinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
