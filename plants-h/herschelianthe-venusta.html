Herschelianthe venusta

Herschelianthe venusta, commonly known as the velvetleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.
