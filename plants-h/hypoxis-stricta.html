Hypoxis stricta

Hypoxis stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf starwort, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. It is adapted to well-drained soils and can tolerate partial shade.

Hypoxis stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
