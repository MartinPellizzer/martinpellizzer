Homalomena engleri

Homalomena engleri, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its dark green, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped flowers that are typically green with a yellow spadix. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Homalomena engleri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
