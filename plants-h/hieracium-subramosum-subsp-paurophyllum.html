Hieracium subramosum subsp. paurophyllum

Hieracium subramosum subsp.

paurophyllum, commonly known as the pale-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its slender stems and basal rosettes of pale green, hairy leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

This subspecies is distinguished by its reduced leaf size and pale coloration compared to other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
