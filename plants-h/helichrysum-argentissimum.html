Helichrysum argentissimum

Helichrysum argentissimum, commonly known as silver everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and delicate appearance.
