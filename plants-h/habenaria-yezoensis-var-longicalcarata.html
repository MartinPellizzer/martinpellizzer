Habenaria yezoensis var. longicalcarata

Habenaria yezoensis var. longicalcarata, commonly known as the Yezo orchid, is a species native to Japan.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its unique floral structure and habitat preferences make it a notable species in botanical studies.
