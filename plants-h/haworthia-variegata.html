Haworthia variegata

Haworthia variegata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a compact, succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often marked with distinct white or yellow stripes resembling a zebra's pattern. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white or yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for low-maintenance indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
