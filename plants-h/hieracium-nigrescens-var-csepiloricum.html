Hieracium nigrescens var. csepiloricum

Hieracium nigrescens var. csepiloricum, commonly known as the Hungarian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow disc flowers surrounded by yellow to orange ray florets. The plant typically grows in dry meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are lanceolate and often covered with fine hairs.

Its flowering period occurs in late summer to early autumn, contributing to its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
