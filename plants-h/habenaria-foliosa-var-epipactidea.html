Habenaria foliosa var. epipactidea

Habenaria foliosa var. epipactidea, commonly known as the Epipactis orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and showy, greenish-yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences and have a distinct lip that is folded under the column.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its delicate appearance.
