Helianthemum thymifolium var. viride

Helianthemum thymifolium var. viride, commonly known as green thyme hedge.

It is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees. Its leaves are aromatic and resemble those of thyme, making it a popular choice for hedges and ground cover.

Botanically, it belongs to the Rosaceae family and is well adapted to poor, rocky soils.
