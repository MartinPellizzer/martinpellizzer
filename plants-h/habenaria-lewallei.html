Habenaria lewallei

Habenaria lewallei, commonly known as the Lewalle's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and delicate appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually small, with a complex arrangement of petals and sepals, and it produces a single spike with multiple blooms.

Habenaria lewallei is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
