Hieracium pseudogrovesianum subsp. opertum

Hieracium pseudogrovesianum subsp.

opertum, commonly known as the opertum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.
