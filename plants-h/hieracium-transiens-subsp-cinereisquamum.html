Hieracium transiens subsp. cinereisquamum

Hieracium transiens subsp.

cinereisquamum, commonly known as the silver-squamate hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant features dark green, hairy leaves with a silver-blue underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. It produces yellow flowers in late summer, which are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

This subspecies is often found in dry meadows and rocky soils, thriving in temperate climates.
