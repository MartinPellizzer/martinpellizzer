Heloniopsis pauciflora var. rubra

Heloniopsis pauciflora var. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered heloniopsis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, sparsely branched stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a desirable ornamental plant in arid or semi-arid climates.
