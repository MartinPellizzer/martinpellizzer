Hieracium filifolium

Hieracium filifolium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often in sunny locations. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and the flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
