Hieracium subepimedium

Hieracium subepimedium, commonly known as the pale meadowhawkweed, is a flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Hieracium subepimedium is often found in temperate regions of Europe and is considered a herbaceous perennial.
