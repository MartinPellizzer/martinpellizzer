Haworthia Pallida Var. Flaccida

Haworthia pallida var. flaccida is a rare and striking succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its compact rosette shape and translucent, waxy leaves that reflect light in a unique way. This variety is often mistaken for its parent species due to its similar appearance but has distinct characteristics that set it apart. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. Due to its beauty and rarity, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts and collectors.
