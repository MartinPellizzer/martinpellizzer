Haworthia magnifica var. atrofusca

Haworthia magnifica var. atrofusca, commonly known as the Black Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are dark green with a striking blackish or purplish hue. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
