Hieracium Laevigatum Subsp. Ficifolium

Hieracium laevigatum subsp. ficifolium is a unique species of hawkweed known for its distinctive leaf shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas. This subspecies has a compact growth habit and produces small, yellow flowers. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits. Understanding its biology helps in its conservation and cultivation.
