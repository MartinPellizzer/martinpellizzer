Hieracium atratum subsp. hypsiphyllum

Hieracium atratum subsp.

hypsiphyllum, commonly known as the dark hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and open woodlands. The plant has a rosette of basal leaves and is often found in moist, nutrient-rich soils.

It is considered a perennial species with a relatively short flowering period in late summer.
