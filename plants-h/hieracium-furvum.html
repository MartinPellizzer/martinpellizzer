Hieracium furvum

Hieracium furvum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has basal leaves that are usually hairy and alternate leaves along the stem.

Hieracium furvum is often found in temperate regions of Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.
