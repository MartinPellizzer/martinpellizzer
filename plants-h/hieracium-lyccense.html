Hieracium lyccense

Hieracium lyccense, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium lyccense is often considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and ability to colonize new areas.
