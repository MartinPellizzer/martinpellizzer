Hypericum uliginosum var. laeve

Hypericum uliginosum var. laeve, commonly known as smooth St. John's wort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and often reddish underneath. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in bogs, marshes, and along stream banks.

This variety is distinguished by its smooth leaf surfaces, setting it apart from other varieties of Hypericum uliginosum.
