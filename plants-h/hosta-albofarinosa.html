Hosta albofarinosa

Hosta albofarinosa, commonly known as the Milky Blue Hosta, is a popular shade-loving plant native to Korea.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are predominantly light green with a distinctive creamy white margin. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 18 inches, forming a dense, compact clump. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the growing season, making it a standout in shady garden settings.

While it does not produce showy flowers, its ornamental value lies primarily in its striking foliage.
