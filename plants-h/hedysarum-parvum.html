Hedysarum parvum

Hedysarum parvum, commonly known as the small-flowered vetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pale yellow to reddish in color. Hedysarum parvum is often found in grasslands, open woodlands, and disturbed soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in ecological restoration projects.
