Helianthemum chatenieri

Helianthemum chatenieri, commonly known as the Catenier's rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. The plant has compact, succulent-like leaves that are gray-green in color, adapted to thrive in rocky and arid environments. It is often found in coastal areas and limestone soils, showcasing its drought tolerance.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
