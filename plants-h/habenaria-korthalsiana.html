Habenaria korthalsiana

Habenaria korthalsiana, commonly known as the Korthals' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. The plant features a pseudobulb-like stem and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
