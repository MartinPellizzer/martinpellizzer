Hosta ibukiensis

Hosta ibukiensis, commonly known as the Ibukiensis Hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-blue hue. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late summer, which are clustered in panicles. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta ibukiensis is a popular ornamental plant in gardens due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
