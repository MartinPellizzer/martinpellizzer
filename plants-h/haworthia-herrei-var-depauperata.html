Haworthia herrei var. depauperata

Haworthia herrei var. depauperata, commonly known as the bare-stemmed Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a short, fibrous stem and triangular, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
