Habenaria kariniae

Habenaria kariniae, commonly known as the Karin's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and specific growth requirements. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with intricate patterns, and it produces a single spike with several blooms.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
