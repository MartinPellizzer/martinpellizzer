Hieracium permodicum

Hieracium permodicum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a hairy texture, and its yellow flowers bloom from June to August.

Hieracium permodicum is often found in open habitats and is relatively common in temperate regions.
