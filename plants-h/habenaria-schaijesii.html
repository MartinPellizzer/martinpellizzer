Habenaria schaijesii

Habenaria schaijesii, commonly known as the Schaijes' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. The plant produces intricate flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a mix of white, green, and yellow hues. It typically thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its range.
