Hoya mcgregorii

Hoya mcgregorii, commonly known as the "Coral Hoya," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its waxy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, coral-pink flowers. The plant is a trailing vine that produces fragrant blooms, often blooming in late winter or early spring. Hoya mcgregorii is relatively easy to care for, thriving in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular among houseplant enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
