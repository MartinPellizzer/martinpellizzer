Helianthemum italicum f. alpestris

Helianthemum italicum f. alpestris, commonly known as the alpine rock rose, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically in clusters along the stems. The plant has compact, succulent leaves that are covered in fine hairs, helping to retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh climatic conditions.
