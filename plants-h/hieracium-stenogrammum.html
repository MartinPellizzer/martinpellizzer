Hieracium stenogrammum

Hieracium stenogrammum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish to reddish flowers in dense clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Hieracium stenogrammum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many temperate regions.
