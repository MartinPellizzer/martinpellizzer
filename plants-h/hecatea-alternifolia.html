Hecatea alternifolia

Hecatea alternifolia, commonly known as the alternate-leaf hecatea, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and yellow. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Hecatea alternifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas and rainforests.
