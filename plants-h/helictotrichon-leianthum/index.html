Helictotrichon Leianthum

Helictotrichon leianthum is a lesser-known grass species native to the western United States. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. This plant is often overlooked due to its modest appearance but plays a vital role in local ecosystems. Its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for small wildlife makes it ecologically significant. Understanding and conserving this species is essential for maintaining biodiversity in its native range.
