Herniaria annua var. virescens

Herniaria annua var. virescens, commonly known as green sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in coastal or disturbed areas. The plant has small, rounded leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. Its flowers are tiny, white or pale pink, and appear in clusters during the summer months.

It is valued in horticulture for its hardiness and ability to stabilize sandy soils.
