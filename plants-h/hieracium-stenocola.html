Hieracium stenocola

Hieracium stenocola, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has a creeping growth habit with flowering stems that rise above the foliage.

Hieracium stenocola is an annual or biennial species, depending on the climate, and is known for its ability to spread through seeds and rhizomes.
