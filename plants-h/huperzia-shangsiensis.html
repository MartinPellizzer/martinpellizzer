Huperzia shangsiensis

Huperzia shangsiensis, commonly known as the Shangsi fern, is a species of clubmoss native to China.

It is characterized by its upright, evergreen fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces spore-bearing cones at the base of its fronds, which are clustered in a distinctive, compact structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions.

Huperzia shangsiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
