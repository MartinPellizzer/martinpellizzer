Hieracium dollianum

Hieracium dollianum, commonly known as the Dolliana hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense mats. It has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture, and its yellow flowers are surrounded by pale green ray florets.

The species is known for its ability to spread rapidly through vegetative reproduction, making it a common weed in many regions.
