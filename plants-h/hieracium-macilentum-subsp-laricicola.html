Hieracium macilentum subsp. laricicola

Hieracium macilentum subsp.

laricicola, commonly known as the alderleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and along stream banks.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear yellow composite flowers, which are arranged in a single terminal head.

It is characterized by its association with alder trees, which contribute to its specific habitat requirements.
