Hieraciodes hierosolymitana

Hieraciodes hierosolymitana, commonly known as the Holy Land hieracium, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and parts of southern Europe.

The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments.
