Heeria elegans

Heeria elegans, commonly known as the velvetleaf or sweetleaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy fruits. Heeria elegans is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
