Homalomena hendersonii

Homalomena hendersonii, commonly known as the Henderson's arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is a common feature in the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Homalomena hendersonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
