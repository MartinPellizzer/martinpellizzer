Helichrysum italicum var. serotinum

Helichrysum italicum var. serotinum, commonly known as autumn helichrysum, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features wiry, branched stems and dense clusters of small, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to autumn. The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves that emit a scent reminiscent of citrus and honey when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a favored plant in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
