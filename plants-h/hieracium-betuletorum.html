Hieracium betuletorum

Hieracium betuletorum, commonly known as the birch hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. Hieracium betuletorum is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
