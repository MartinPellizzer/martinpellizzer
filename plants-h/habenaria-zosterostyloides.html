Habenaria zosterostyloides

Habenaria zosterostyloides, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are linear and often have a distinct midrib, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
