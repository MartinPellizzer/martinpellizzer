Hibiscus verdcourtii

Hibiscus verdcourtii, commonly known as the Congo hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Central and West Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically red or pink in color, with a prominent yellow center.
