Hieracium atratum subsp. pseudocurvatifolium

Hieracium atratum subsp.

pseudocurvatifolium, commonly known as the black hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.
