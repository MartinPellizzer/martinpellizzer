Hieracium separ

Hieracium separ, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its clustered inflorescence of yellow to orange flowers, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and somewhat hairy.

Hieracium separ is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
