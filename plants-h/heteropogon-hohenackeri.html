Heteropogon hohenackeri

Heteropogon hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's grass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, wiry leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, spikelet-like flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland ecosystems.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
