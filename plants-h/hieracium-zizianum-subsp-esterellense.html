Hieracium zizianum subsp. esterellense

Hieracium zizianum subsp.

esterellense, commonly known as the Esterel hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in sunny, open habitats.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, with leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.

This subspecies is part of the genus Hieracium, which includes many other dandelion-like plants.
