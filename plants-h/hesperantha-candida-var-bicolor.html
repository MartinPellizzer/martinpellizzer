Hesperantha candida var. bicolor

Hesperantha candida var. bicolor, commonly known as the white and pink marsh orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, showy flowers with white and pink petals. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its flowering period occurs during the late winter to early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural environment.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native ecosystems.
