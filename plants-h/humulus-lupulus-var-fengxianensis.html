Humulus lupulus var. fengxianensis

Humulus lupulus var. fengxianensis, commonly known as the Fengxian hop, is a variant of the hop plant native to China.

It is characterized by its unique floral structures and aromatic compounds that differ from standard hop varieties. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soils. Its botanical features include compound leaves and cone-like flowers called strobiles, which contain the essential oils.

This variety is of interest in botanical studies due to its distinct chemical profile and potential uses in traditional medicine.
