Haplopappus spinulosus var. turbinellus

Haplopappus spinulosus var. turbinellus, commonly known as the Turbinellus pappus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive bracts. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has a low, sprawling growth habit. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellowish.
