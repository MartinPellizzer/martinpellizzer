Haworthia teres

Haworthia teres, commonly known as the dwarf Haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often marked with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on short stalks during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
