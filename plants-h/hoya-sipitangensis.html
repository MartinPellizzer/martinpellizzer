Hoya sipitangensis

Hoya sipitangensis, commonly known as the Sipitang Waxplant, is a species of flowering plant native to Borneo.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, ovate leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
