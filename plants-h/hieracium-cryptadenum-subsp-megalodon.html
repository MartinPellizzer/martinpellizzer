Hieracium cryptadenum subsp. megalodon

Hieracium cryptadenum subsp.

megalodon, commonly known as the giant hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its robust growth and distinctive, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellowish to orange flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.
