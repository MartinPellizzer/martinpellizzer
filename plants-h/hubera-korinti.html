Hubera korinti

Hubera korinti, commonly known as the Korinti orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the island of Korinth.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its humid environment.

Hubera korinti is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
