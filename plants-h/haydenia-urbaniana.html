Haydenia urbaniana

Haydenia urbaniana, commonly known as the yellow daisy or golden daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Haydenia urbaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
