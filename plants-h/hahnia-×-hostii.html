Hahnia × hostii

Hahnia × hostii, commonly known as the hybrid hahnia, is a perennial plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Hahnia filiformis and Hahnia hostii, two species native to South America. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

However, it is not typically used for agricultural or commercial purposes.
