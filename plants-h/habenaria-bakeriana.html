Habenaria bakeriana

Habenaria bakeriana, commonly known as the Baker's Habenaria, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its unique floral structure and ecological significance.
