Hopea vietnamensis

Hopea vietnamensis, commonly known as Vietnamese hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and is often found in lowland evergreen rainforests. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and has smooth, greyish bark with distinct leaf scars. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a leathery texture and prominent venation.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody capsules that release seeds.
