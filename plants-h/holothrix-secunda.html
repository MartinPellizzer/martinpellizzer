Holothrix secunda

Holothrix secunda, commonly known as the silverleaf holothrix, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Holothrix secunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
