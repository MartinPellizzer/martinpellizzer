Haworthia janvlokii

Haworthia janvlokii, commonly known as the Janvlokii Haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant is known for its ability to store water in its fleshy leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It produces small, white flowers on tall stalks during the growing season.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
