Hymenocallis rotata

Hymenocallis rotata, commonly known as the twisted spider lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Americas, particularly Mexico and Central America. The plant is characterized by its narrow, twisted leaves and showy, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent, thread-like stamens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Hymenocallis rotata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
