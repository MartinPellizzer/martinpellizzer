Hieracium remissifolium

Hieracium remissifolium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium remissifolium is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of habitats, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
