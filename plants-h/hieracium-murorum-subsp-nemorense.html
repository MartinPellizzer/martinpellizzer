Hieracium murorum subsp. nemorense

Hieracium murorum subsp.

nemorense, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, reaching heights of up to 60 cm.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are hairy and have a distinctive yellowish-green color. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, forming a composite head that attracts various pollinators.

It is characterized by its robust growth and adaptability to different soil conditions.
