Hedysarum lydium

Hedysarum lydium, commonly known as Lydian milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical seed pods that contain small, dark seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Hedysarum lydium is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
