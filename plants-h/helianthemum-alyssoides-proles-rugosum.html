Helianthemum alyssoides proles rugosum

Helianthemum alyssoides proles rugosum, commonly known as the rough-stemmed rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, often glandular leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white wax. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
