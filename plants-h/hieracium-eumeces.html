Hieracium eumeces

Hieracium eumeces, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are typically hairy and have a heart-shaped base. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium eumeces is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
