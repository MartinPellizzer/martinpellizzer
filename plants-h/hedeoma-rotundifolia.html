Hedeoma rotundifolia

Hedeoma rotundifolia, commonly known as roundleaf hedeoma, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its round, succulent leaves and clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers. It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in shaded areas.

Hedeoma rotundifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
