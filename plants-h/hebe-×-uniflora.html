Hebe × uniflora

Hebe × uniflora, commonly known as the common hebe, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It typically grows to a height of 30–100 cm and features dense, evergreen foliage with a grey-green to dark green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Hebe × uniflora is relatively low maintenance and resistant to many common pests and diseases.
