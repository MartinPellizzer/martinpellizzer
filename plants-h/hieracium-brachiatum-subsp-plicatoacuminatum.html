Hieracium brachiatum subsp. plicatoacuminatum

Hieracium brachiatum subsp.

plicatoacuminatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

This subspecies is distinguished by its finely pinnatifid leaves and acuminate tips, which contribute to its unique botanical appearance.
