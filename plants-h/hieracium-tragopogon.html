Hieracium tragopogon

Hieracium tragopogon, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

Hieracium tragopogon is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it an aggressive weed in some regions.
