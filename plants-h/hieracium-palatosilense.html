Hieracium palatosilense

Hieracium palatosilense, commonly known as the Silene hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow to orange in color and are arranged in a flat, composite head.

Hieracium palatosilense is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
