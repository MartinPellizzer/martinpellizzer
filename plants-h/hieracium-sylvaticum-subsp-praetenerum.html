Hieracium sylvaticum subsp. praetenerum

Hieracium sylvaticum subsp.

praetenerum, commonly known as the forest hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Its flowering period occurs from June to August, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
