Habenaria brenesii

Habenaria brenesii, commonly known as the Guanacaste orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil, and it has a symbiotic relationship with mycorrhizal fungi.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
