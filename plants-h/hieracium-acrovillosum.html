Hieracium acrovillosum

Hieracium acrovillosum, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often glandular-dotted.

Hieracium acrovillosum is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in some areas.
