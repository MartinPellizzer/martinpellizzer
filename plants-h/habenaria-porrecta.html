Habenaria porrecta

Habenaria porrecta, commonly known as the straight-lipped orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces erect, hairy stems with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a distinctive, elongated lip that is often curved.

Habenaria porrecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
