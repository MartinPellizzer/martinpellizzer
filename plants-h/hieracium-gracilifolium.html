Hieracium gracilifolium

Hieracium gracilifolium, commonly known as slender-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium gracilifolium is often found in open habitats and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens with well-drained soil.
