Habenaria carinata

Habenaria carinata, commonly known as the ridge orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive ridge-like structure on the lip.

Habenaria carinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
