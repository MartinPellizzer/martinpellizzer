Hedyotis stelligera

Hedyotis stelligera, commonly known as the coastal hedyotis, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along the shoreline. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Hedyotis stelligera is adapted to salt spray and wind, making it a hardy species in coastal environments.
