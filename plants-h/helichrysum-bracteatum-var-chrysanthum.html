Helichrysum bracteatum var. chrysanthum

Helichrysum bracteatum var. chrysanthum, commonly known as the golden marguerite or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, silver-green leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its long-lasting flowers make it a favorite in dried flower arrangements.
