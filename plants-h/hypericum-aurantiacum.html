Hypericum aurantiacum

Hypericum aurantiacum, commonly known as the orange St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant features yellow-orange stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Hypericum aurantiacum is often found in woodland edges, open fields, and disturbed sites.
