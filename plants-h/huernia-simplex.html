Huernia simplex

Huernia simplex, commonly known as the horned African violet, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
