Haworthia helmiae

Haworthia helmiae, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are marked with distinct white or yellow stripes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers on tall stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
