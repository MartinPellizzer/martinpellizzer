Hydrostachys goudotiana

Hydrostachys goudotiana, commonly known as the African waterwheel, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique whorled arrangement of leaves that resemble a waterwheel, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy habitats and is often found near water bodies. Its flowering stems produce small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in clusters.

Hydrostachys goudotiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with moist soil conditions.
