Hieracium pseudacrifolium

Hieracium pseudacrifolium, commonly known as the false yarrow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium pseudacrifolium is known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it an invasive species in some regions.
