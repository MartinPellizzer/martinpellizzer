Hiptage cuspidata

Hiptage cuspidata, commonly known as the spurred hiptage, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories.

Hiptage cuspidata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
