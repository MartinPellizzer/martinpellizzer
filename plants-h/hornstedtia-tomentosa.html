Hornstedtia tomentosa

Hornstedtia tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly hornstedtia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. Due to its ornamental value and unique texture, it is often cultivated by gardeners interested in exotic plants.
