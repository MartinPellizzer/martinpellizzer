Hieracium macrotonum subsp. smolandicum

Hieracium macrotonum subsp.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer.
