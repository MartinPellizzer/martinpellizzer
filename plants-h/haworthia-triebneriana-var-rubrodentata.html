Haworthia triebneriana var. rubrodentata

Haworthia triebneriana var. rubrodentata, commonly known as the red-toothed Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or pink markings along the edges and tips. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
