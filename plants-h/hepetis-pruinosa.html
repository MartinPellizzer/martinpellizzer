Hepetis pruinosa

Hepetis pruinosa, commonly known as the silverleaf ironweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Hepetis pruinosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
