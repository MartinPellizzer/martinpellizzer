Hedysarum xizangensis

Hedysarum xizangensis, commonly known as the Tibet milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to cold and arid environments, showcasing resilience to harsh climatic conditions. It plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat due to its deep root system.

Hedysarum xizangensis is of interest to botanists and conservationists due to its unique ecological niche and potential for medicinal use.
