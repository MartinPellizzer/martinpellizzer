Hedychium × moorei

Hedychium × moorei, commonly known as the yellow ginger, is a hybrid ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with tall, leafy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Hedychium × moorei is often cultivated for its striking floral display and is used in gardens and as a cut flower.
