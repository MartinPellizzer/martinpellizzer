Hypericum nakaii subsp. miyabei

Hypericum nakaii subsp.

miyabei, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan. It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their yellow or orange flowers.

This subspecies is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens or borders.
