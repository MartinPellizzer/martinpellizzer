Hypoxis villosa var. obliqua

Hypoxis villosa var. obliqua, commonly known as the woolly yellowstar, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, hairy, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine, white pubescence. The plant produces yellow flowers in late summer, which are borne on slender, leafless stalks. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and bloom.
