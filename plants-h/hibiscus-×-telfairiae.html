Hibiscus × telfairiae

Hibiscus × telfairiae, commonly known as the Telfair's hibiscus, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Hibiscus moscheutos and Hibiscus coccineus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 meters in favorable conditions. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are woody and often reddish in color.

Hibiscus × telfairiae thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.
