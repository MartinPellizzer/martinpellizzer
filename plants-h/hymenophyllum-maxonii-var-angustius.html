Hymenophyllum maxonii var. angustius

Hymenophyllum maxonii var. angustius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf filmy fern, is a member of the Hymenophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds.

It is a small epiphytic fern that typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are usually narrow and have a distinctive, translucent appearance due to their thin, papery structure. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant's unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
