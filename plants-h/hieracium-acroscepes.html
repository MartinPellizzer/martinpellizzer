Hieracium acroscepes

Hieracium acroscepes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers that consist of both ray and disk florets. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas.

This plant is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is often found in disturbed soils.
