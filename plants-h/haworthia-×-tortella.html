Haworthia × tortella

Haworthia × tortella, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, fleshy, triangular leaves with white or yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant typically grows in a rosette formation and is well adapted to arid environments. It requires minimal water and thrives in bright, indirect sunlight.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
