Helichrysum Striatum

Helichrysum striatum is a vibrant and resilient plant known for its striking yellow and gold flowers. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. This plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. Its long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for dried flower arrangements. Despite its delicate appearance, it is surprisingly hardy and easy to care for.
