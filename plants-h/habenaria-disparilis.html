Habenaria disparilis

Habenaria disparilis, commonly known as the slender marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant has a slender, erect stem that bears several oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a faintly colored lip.

It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in swampy or marshy habitats.
