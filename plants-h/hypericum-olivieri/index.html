Hypericum Olivieri

Hypericum olivieri is a rare and resilient plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It thrives in rocky and coastal environments, often found growing on cliffs and slopes. Known for its bright yellow flowers, this species adds a striking visual element to its natural habitat. Its leaves are evergreen and slightly glossy, providing year-round interest. Despite its beauty, Hypericum olivieri remains relatively obscure in botanical circles.
