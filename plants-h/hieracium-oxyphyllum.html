Hieracium oxyphyllum

Hieracium oxyphyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium oxyphyllum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
