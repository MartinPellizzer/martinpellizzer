Hieracium minimidens

Hieracium minimidens, commonly known as the small hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. It produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
