Hemiboea integra

Hemiboea integra, commonly known as the silver leaf balm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually pink or purple in color, blooming in clusters.

Hemiboea integra is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact size.
