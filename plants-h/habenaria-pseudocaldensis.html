Habenaria pseudocaldensis

Habenaria pseudocaldensis, commonly known as the Malayan butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems and produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive butterfly-like shape. The plant typically blooms in the summer months, with flowers ranging in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
