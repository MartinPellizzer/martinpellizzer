Hieracium ruebelianum

Hieracium ruebelianum, commonly known as the Ruebel's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and basal rosettes of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in the summer months. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
