Heliotropium arboreum

Heliotropium arboreum, commonly known as the tree heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy clusters of violet or pink flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, and the plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
