Habenaria luceana

Habenaria luceana, commonly known as the Luce's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, slender stems and striking, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
