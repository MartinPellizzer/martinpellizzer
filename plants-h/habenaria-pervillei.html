Habenaria pervillei

Habenaria pervillei, commonly known as the Perville's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinct lip, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
