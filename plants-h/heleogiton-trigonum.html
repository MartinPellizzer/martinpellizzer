Heleogiton trigonum

Heleogiton trigonum, commonly known as the three-angled leaf vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically three-lobed and arranged in a triangular pattern. Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
