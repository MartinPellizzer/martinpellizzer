Haworthia retusa var. multilineata

Haworthia retusa var. multilineata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy leaves with distinct white to yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
