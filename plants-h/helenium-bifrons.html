Helenium bifrons

Helenium bifrons, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It features striking, bright yellow ray flowers with a central disk, resembling a sunflower in appearance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Helenium bifrons is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
