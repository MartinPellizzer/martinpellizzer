Hyptis atrorubens

Hyptis atrorubens, commonly known as red-stemmed hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hyptis atrorubens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
