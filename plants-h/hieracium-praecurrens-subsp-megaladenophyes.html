Hieracium praecurrens subsp. megaladenophyes

Hieracium praecurrens subsp.

megaladenophyes, commonly known as the giant flowered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and woodland edges. Its most distinguishing feature is its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
