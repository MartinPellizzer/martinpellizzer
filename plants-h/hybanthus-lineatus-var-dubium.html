Hybanthus lineatus var. dubium

Hybanthus lineatus var. dubium, commonly known as the slender hybanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to white in color. The plant grows as an annual herb, often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open fields. The flowers bloom in clusters and are adapted for pollination by small insects.
