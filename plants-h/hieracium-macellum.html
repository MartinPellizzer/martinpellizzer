Hieracium macellum

Hieracium macellum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its stems are hairy.

Hieracium macellum is an annual or biennial plant, producing small, inconspicuous fruits called achenes.
