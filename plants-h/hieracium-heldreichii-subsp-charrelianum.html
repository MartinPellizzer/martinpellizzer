Hieracium heldreichii subsp. charrelianum

Hieracium heldreichii subsp.

charrelianum, commonly known as the Heldreich's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, herbaceous rosettes.
