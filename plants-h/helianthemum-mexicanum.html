Helianthemum mexicanum

Helianthemum mexicanum, commonly known as Mexican stonecress, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are usually fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve water.
