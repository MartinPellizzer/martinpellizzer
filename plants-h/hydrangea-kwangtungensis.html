Hydrangea kwangtungensis

Hydrangea kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant has palmate leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Hydrangea kwangtungensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
