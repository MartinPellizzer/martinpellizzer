Helichrysum reticulatum

Helichrysum reticulatum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum reticulatum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in essential oil production.
