Helichrysum italicum f. intermedium

Helichrysum italicum f. intermedium, commonly known as the everlasting or golden marguerite, is a hybrid plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, yellow-orange flowers that are highly aromatic. The plant is known for its dense foliage and ability to thrive in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its essential oils are valued in aromatherapy and herbal medicine for their calming and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's resilience and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice in horticulture.
