Haworthia magnifica

Haworthia magnifica, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, thick, fleshy leaves that are often marked with contrasting white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces tall, flowering stalks with small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
