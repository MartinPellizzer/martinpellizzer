Hieracium sparsum subsp. mirekii

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

mirekii, commonly known as the Mirek's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has basal leaves that are usually hairy and linear, with a rosette structure.

The subspecies is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and subtle morphological differences from other hawkweed species.
