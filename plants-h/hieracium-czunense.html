Hieracium czunense

Hieracium czunense, commonly known as the Czunenian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are basal, rough, and often hairy, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium czunense is an annual or biennial herb that plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
