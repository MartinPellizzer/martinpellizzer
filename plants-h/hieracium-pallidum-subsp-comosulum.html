Hieracium pallidum subsp. comosulum

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

comosulum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a hairy surface, and the flower heads are composed of small florets arranged in a composite structure.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its daisy-like appearance and wind-pollinated flowers.
