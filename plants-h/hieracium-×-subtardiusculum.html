Hieracium × subtardiusculum

Hieracium × subtardiusculum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flower heads composed of numerous small ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has basal leaves that are often lobed and may have a fuzzy texture.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations.
