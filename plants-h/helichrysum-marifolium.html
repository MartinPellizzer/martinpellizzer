Helichrysum marifolium

Helichrysum marifolium, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum marifolium is also valued for its aromatic foliage and ability to attract beneficial insects.
