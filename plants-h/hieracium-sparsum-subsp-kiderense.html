Hieracium sparsum subsp. kiderense

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

kiderense, commonly known as the Kiderenskaya hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in composite flower heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its range.
