Helichroa linnaeana

Helichroa linnaeana, commonly known as the Linnaean's helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Helichroa linnaeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
