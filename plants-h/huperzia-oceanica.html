Huperzia oceanica

Huperzia oceanica, commonly known as the oceanic huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.

Huperzia oceanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in maintaining soil stability.
