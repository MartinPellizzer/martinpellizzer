Habenaria nuda

Habenaria nuda, commonly known as the naked orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in late summer. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

Habenaria nuda is a terrestrial orchid that plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
