Hieracium tolimense

Hieracium tolimense, commonly known as Tolimense Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring and summer.
