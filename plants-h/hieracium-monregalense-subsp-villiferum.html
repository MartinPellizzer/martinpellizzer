Hieracium monregalense subsp. villiferum

Hieracium monregalense subsp.

villiferum, commonly known as the Catalan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flower heads.

The plant typically features basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape, with rough, hairy surfaces. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas, and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural reserves.
