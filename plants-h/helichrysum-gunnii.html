Helichrysum gunnii

Helichrysum gunnii, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow to orange-colored flowers that remain intact even after flowering, giving it a lifelike appearance. The plant has narrow, grey-green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Helichrysum gunnii is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
