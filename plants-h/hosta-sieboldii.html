Hosta sieboldii

Hosta sieboldii, commonly known as Siebold's hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a silvery-blue hue on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
