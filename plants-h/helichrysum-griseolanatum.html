Helichrysum griseolanatum

Helichrysum griseolanatum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has silver-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Helichrysum griseolanatum is also valued for its ornamental use and has been used in traditional medicine.
