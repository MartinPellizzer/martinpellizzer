Hieracium frolense

Hieracium frolense, commonly known as the Frolens hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant features basal rosettes of lanceolate leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late spring to early summer. It is often found in grassy habitats and disturbed soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological studies and botanical gardens.
