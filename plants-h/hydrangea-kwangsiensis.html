Hydrangea kwangsiensis

Hydrangea kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range from blue to pink depending on soil pH. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a rough texture.

Hydrangea kwangsiensis is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
