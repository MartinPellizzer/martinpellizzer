Hippomarathrum libanotis var. pungens

Hippomarathrum libanotis var. pungens, commonly known as the spiny desert hibiscus, is a shrub native to the arid regions of the Arabian Peninsula.

It features woody stems covered in sharp, spiny projections, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming during the cooler months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to store water in harsh desert conditions.

The species is well-suited for xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in poor soils.
