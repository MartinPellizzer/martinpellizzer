Hieracium ruprechtii subsp. pistoriense

Hieracium ruprechtii subsp.

pistoriense, commonly known as the Pistoriense hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single terminal inflorescence. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with rough, hairy surfaces.

It is characterized by its small stature and is often found in nutrient-poor soils.
