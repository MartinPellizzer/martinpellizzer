Heliotropium lanceolatum

Heliotropium lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and disturbed sites due to its adaptability.

Heliotropium lanceolatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
