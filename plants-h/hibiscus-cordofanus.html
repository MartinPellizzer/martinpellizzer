Hibiscus cordofanus

Hibiscus cordofanus, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range from pink to red in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Hibiscus cordofanus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sunlight for optimal growth.
