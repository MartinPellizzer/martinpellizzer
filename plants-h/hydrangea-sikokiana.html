Hydrangea sikokiana

Hydrangea sikokiana, commonly known as the Ogasawara hydrangea, is a species native to Japan's Ogasawara Islands.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have serrated edges.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
