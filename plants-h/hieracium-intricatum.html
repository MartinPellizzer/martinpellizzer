Hieracium intricatum

Hieracium intricatum, commonly known as the intricate hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. Hieracium intricatum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
