Hieracium × psilophyllum

Hieracium × psilophyllum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a hybrid plant species within the genus Hieracium, which belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and typically produces yellow flowers with ray florets. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in certain habitats. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations, making it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
