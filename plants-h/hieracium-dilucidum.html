Hieracium dilucidum

Hieracium dilucidum, commonly known as the clearweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium dilucidum is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
