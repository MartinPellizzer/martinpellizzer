Hieracium lepidiforme

Hieracium lepidiforme, commonly known as the silky hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium lepidiforme is known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it an invasive species in some regions.
