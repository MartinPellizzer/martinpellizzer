Hakea Spinosa

Hakea spinosa is a hardy shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. Known for its unique appearance and ecological significance, it thrives in sandy soils and salty air. Its spiny leaves and vibrant yellow flowers make it a striking addition to gardens. This plant plays a vital role in supporting local wildlife and stabilizing dunes. Despite its tough exterior, Hakea spinosa is a symbol of resilience and adaptation in harsh environments.
