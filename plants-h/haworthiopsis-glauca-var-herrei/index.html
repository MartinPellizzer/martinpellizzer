Haworthiopsis Glauca Var. Herrei

Haworthiopsis glauca var. herrei is a unique and striking succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its compact, rosette-like growth pattern and its ability to thrive in arid environments. The plant features pale blue-green leaves with distinct white markings that give it a striking appearance. It is a popular choice among collectors for its low maintenance and ornamental value. This variety is also valued for its resilience and adaptability to various growing conditions.
