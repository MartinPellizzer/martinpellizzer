Hieracium vulpinum

Hieracium vulpinum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils.

Hieracium vulpinum is often found in temperate regions across Europe and parts of Asia.
