Hieracium nigrofuscum

Hieracium nigrofuscum, commonly known as the blackish hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features basal rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in a composite head. It is a perennial species with a taproot system, and it spreads primarily through vegetative reproduction.

Due to its adaptability, it is considered a common weed in various habitats.
