Hymenophyllum perrieri

Hymenophyllum perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's filmy fern, is a small, delicate fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its thin, translucent fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spore-producing structures, called soruses, are clustered on the underside of the fronds.

The plant's unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
