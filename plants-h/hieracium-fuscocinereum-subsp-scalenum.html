Hieracium fuscocinereum subsp. scalenum

Hieracium fuscocinereum subsp.

scalenum, commonly known as the brown-capped hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its erect stems and basal rosettes of leaves.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a single head. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks.

Its foliage is often covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
