Hieracium murorum subsp. trunciceps

Hieracium murorum subsp.

trunciceps, commonly known as the short-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are often heart-shaped.

The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, flat-topped clusters, typically blooming from June to August. It is native to Europe and prefers disturbed soils in grasslands and woodland edges.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
