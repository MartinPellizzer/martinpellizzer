Hieracium brevifolium subsp. weldenii

Hieracium brevifolium subsp.

weldenii, commonly known as the Welden's narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a single head, and its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.
