Helichrysum lutescens

Helichrysum lutescens, commonly known as the yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain colorful even when dried, making it popular in floral arrangements. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or sandy environments.

Helichrysum lutescens is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
