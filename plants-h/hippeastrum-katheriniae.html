Hippeastrum katheriniae

Hippeastrum katheriniae, commonly known as the Katherine's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in the spring or early summer. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that emerge from a bulb and can grow up to several feet in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum katheriniae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
