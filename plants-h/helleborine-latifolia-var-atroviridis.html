Helleborine latifolia var. atroviridis

Helleborine latifolia var. atroviridis, commonly known as the dark green helleborine, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves and distinctive, hooded flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant is a terrestrial orchid, often found in moist, shaded woodlands and along stream banks. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces small, dark brown seeds.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
