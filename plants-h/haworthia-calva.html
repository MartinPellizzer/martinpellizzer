Haworthia calva

Haworthia calva, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, pale green leaves with distinctive white or grayish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stalks during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
