Hypericum luristanicum

Hypericum luristanicum, commonly known as the Luristanic St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in summer and are often visited by bees and other pollinators. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum luristanicum is typically found in rocky and dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
