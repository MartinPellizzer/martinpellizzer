Hakea macraeana

Hakea macraeana, commonly known as the Macrae's hakea, is an evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves that are typically grey-green in color. The plant produces striking clusters of red or orange flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species.

Hakea macraeana is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
