Hieracium auriflorum

Hieracium auriflorum, commonly known as the golden hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium auriflorum is known for its hairy leaves and is sometimes considered a weed due to its aggressive growth habits.
