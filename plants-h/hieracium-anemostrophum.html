Hieracium anemostrophum

Hieracium anemostrophum, commonly known as the wind-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow flowers arranged in a single head. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and meadows. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

Hieracium anemostrophum is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.
