Habenaria cultrata

Habenaria cultrata, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green or yellowish. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers have a distinctive, sword-shaped lip.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
