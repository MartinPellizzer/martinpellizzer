Hieracium dilucidiusculum

Hieracium dilucidiusculum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant is distinguished by its finely divided, silver-green leaves and pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.
