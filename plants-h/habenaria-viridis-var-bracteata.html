Habenaria viridis var. bracteata

Habenaria viridis var. bracteata, commonly known as the green orchid or bracteate green orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, upright stem with a single, showy greenish-white flower that has a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, aiding in water retention.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
