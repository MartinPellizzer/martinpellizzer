Hieracium Doratolepium

Hieracium doratolepium is a rare and elusive species of hawkweed found in specific alpine habitats. It is known for its delicate yellow flowers and hairy foliage, which help it survive in harsh environments. This plant is native to a limited geographic range, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Its unique morphology and restricted distribution highlight its ecological significance. Understanding and protecting this species is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
