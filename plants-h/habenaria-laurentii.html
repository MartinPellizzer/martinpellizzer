Habenaria laurentii

Habenaria laurentii, commonly known as the Laurentian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the temperate regions of North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces upright, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish-white, with three sepals and three petals, and a distinctive lip.

Habenaria laurentii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
