Heteropanax chinensis

Heteropanax chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese heteropanax, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with opposite or whorled arrangement. The plant features a straight, cylindrical stem with smooth, grayish bark. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, and it produces dark, spherical berries as fruit.

Heteropanax chinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
