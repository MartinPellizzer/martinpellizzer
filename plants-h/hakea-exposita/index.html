Hakea Exposita

Hakea exposita is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia. It is known for its unique, bottle-shaped stems and vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its architectural form and long-lasting blooms make it a standout addition to native plant collections. Hakea exposita is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and nectar for local wildlife.
