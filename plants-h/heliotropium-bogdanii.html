Heliotropium bogdanii

Heliotropium bogdanii, commonly known as the bogdanii heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, violet to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Heliotropium bogdanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
