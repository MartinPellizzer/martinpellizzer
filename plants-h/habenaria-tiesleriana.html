Habenaria tiesleriana

Habenaria tiesleriana, commonly known as the Tiesler's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich soil. Its flowers are usually fragrant and bloom during the summer months.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
