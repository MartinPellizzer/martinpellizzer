Hieracium levicaule subsp. mecophyllum

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

mecophyllum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellowish to greenish composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its reduced leaf size and a more compact growth habit compared to other members of the species. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for studies on plant adaptation and ecological distribution.
