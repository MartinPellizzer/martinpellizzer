Hieracium Aestivum Subsp. Valdefrondosum

Hieracium aestivum subsp. valdefrondosum is a rare and lesser-known plant species belonging to the dandelion family. It is primarily found in specific regions of southern Europe, where it thrives in open, sunny habitats. This subspecies is distinguished by its delicate yellow flowers and hairy leaves, which help it adapt to its environment. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it faces threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
