Hybridella

Hybridella, commonly known as the hybrid bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that typically range in color from blue to purple. The plant is native to parts of Europe and Asia and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Hybridella is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive blooms and compact growth habit.

It prefers full sun to partial shade and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
