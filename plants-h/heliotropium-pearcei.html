Heliotropium pearcei

Heliotropium pearcei, commonly known as the pink heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep purple, often with a distinctive central blue spot. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Heliotropium pearcei thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.
