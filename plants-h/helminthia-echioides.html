Helminthia echioides

Helminthia echioides, commonly known as the horsebean vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a perennial legume that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. Its seed pods are long and cylindrical, containing several elliptical seeds.

Helminthia echioides is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
