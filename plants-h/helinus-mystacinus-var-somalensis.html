Helinus mystacinus var. somalensis

Helinus mystacinus var. somalensis, commonly known as the Somali helinus, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow ray florets and dark purple disk florets. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, herbaceous stems.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
