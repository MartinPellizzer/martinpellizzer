Hieracium piloselliflorum subsp. stenozon

Hieracium piloselliflorum subsp.

stenozon, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single, elongated flower head with tubular florets.

The species is characterized by its compact growth form and is often found in temperate regions.
