Haworthia maraisii var. livida

Haworthia maraisii var. livida, commonly known as the Marais' Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white tubercles and translucent windows, which are adaptations for photosynthesis in arid conditions. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks with clusters of small, white or pale green flowers during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
