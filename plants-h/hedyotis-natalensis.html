Hedyotis natalensis

Hedyotis natalensis, commonly known as Natal buttonweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Hedyotis natalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
