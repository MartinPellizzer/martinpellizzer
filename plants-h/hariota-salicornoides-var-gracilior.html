Hariota salicornoides var. gracilior

Hariota salicornoides var. gracilior, commonly known as slender glasswort, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It is characterized by its erect, cylindrical stems covered in minute, scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other saline habitats.

Its structure and physiology enable it to tolerate high salt concentrations and arid conditions.
