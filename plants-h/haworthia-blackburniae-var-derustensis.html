Haworthia blackburniae var. derustensis

Haworthia blackburniae var. derustensis, commonly known as the Zulu beauty, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves with a dark green to almost black coloration, often marked with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
