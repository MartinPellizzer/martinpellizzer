Hypericum koshiense

Hypericum koshiense, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Hypericum koshiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
