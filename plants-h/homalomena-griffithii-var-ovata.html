Homalomena griffithii var. ovata

Homalomena griffithii var. ovata, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
