Hieracium × bueckii

Hieracium × bueckii, commonly known as the hybrid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering stems that bear yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, often becoming invasive in some areas. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered a weed in agricultural and natural settings.
