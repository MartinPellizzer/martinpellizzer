Hibbertia ovata var. monadelpha

Hibbertia ovata var. monadelpha, commonly known as the broad-leafed hibbertia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Hibbertia ovata var. monadelpha is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
