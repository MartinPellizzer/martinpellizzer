Hieracium sartorianum

Hieracium sartorianum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of many members of the genus Hieracium. Hieracium sartorianum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
