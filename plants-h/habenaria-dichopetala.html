Habenaria dichopetala

Habenaria dichopetala, commonly known as the two-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers with two distinct lobes on each petal. It prefers shady, moist habitats and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
