Helicia microcarpa

Helicia microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf holly or small-fruited holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is a small evergreen shrub, typically growing to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

The small, yellowish flowers are followed by orange-red berries, which are an important food source for various bird species.
