Helianthemum × lagunae

Helianthemum × lagunae, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and succulent leaves. The plant produces showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Helianthemum × lagunae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
