Haworthia pumila subsp. minima

Haworthia pumila subsp.

minima, commonly known as the miniature Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa. It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive white or pale blueish waxy coating.

The leaves are short and thick, often with subtle markings or patterns that enhance its aesthetic appeal. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its minimal care requirements.
