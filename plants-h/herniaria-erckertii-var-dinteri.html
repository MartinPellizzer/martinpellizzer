Herniaria erckertii var. dinteri

Herniaria erckertii var. dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's Herniaria, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant is adapted to harsh, dry environments, with a succulent-like structure that helps it retain moisture.
