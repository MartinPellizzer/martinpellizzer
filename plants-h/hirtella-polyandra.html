Hirtella polyandra

Hirtella polyandra, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its small, clustered white flowers and smooth, glossy leaves.
