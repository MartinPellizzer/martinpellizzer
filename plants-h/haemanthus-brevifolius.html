Haemanthus brevifolius

Haemanthus brevifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved haemanthus, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, umbellate inflorescence of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Haemanthus brevifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
