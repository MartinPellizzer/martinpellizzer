Helianthemum hercegovinum

Helianthemum hercegovinum, commonly known as the Hercegovean sunflower, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Balkans.

It features dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in mild climates, though it is not frost-tender.
