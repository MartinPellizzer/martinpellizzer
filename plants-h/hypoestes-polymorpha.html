Hypoestes polymorpha

Hypoestes polymorpha, commonly known as the African Violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, purple, and white. The plant has a compact growth habit with rosettes of dark green, waxy leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hypoestes polymorpha is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
