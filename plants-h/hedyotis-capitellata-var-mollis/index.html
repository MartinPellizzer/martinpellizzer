Hedyotis Capitellata Var. Mollis

Hedyotis capitellata var. mollis is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the Rubiaceae family. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its delicate appearance and unique foliage make it a sought-after addition to botanical gardens and greenhouses. Despite its beauty, it remains underdocumented in many horticultural resources. This article aims to shed light on its characteristics, habitat, and cultivation tips.
