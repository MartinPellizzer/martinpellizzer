Hemionitis hynreckii

Hemionitis hynreckii, commonly known as the Hynreck's hemionitis, is a rare and specialized fern native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It thrives in cool, moist, and shaded environments, often found in rocky crevices or forest understories. The plant has distinctive, finely divided fronds that are typically dark green and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, a common feature among ferns.

Due to habitat destruction and limited distribution, Hemionitis hynreckii is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
