Hieracium atratiforme subsp. pseudotranssilvanicum

Hieracium atratiforme subsp.

pseudotranssilvanicum, commonly known as the Transylvanian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowering heads.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, pastures, and woodland edges. It has basal leaves that are usually lanceolate or ovate, and its flowering stems are usually unbranched.
