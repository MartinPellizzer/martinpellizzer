Herminium souliei var. lichiangense

Herminium souliei var. lichiangense, commonly known as the Lichiang orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and delicate, pendulous flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing small, white to pale pink flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
