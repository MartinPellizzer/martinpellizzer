Hieracium tenellifrons

Hieracium tenellifrons, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia. The plant features basal and alternate leaves that are typically lanceolate or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Hieracium tenellifrons is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
