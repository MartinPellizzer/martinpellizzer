Haworthia mirabilis var. maraisii

Haworthia mirabilis var. maraisii, commonly known as the Marais Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with dark green markings. The leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to store water in arid environments. It produces tall, slender flower stalks that bear small, white or pale yellow flowers.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
