Hieracium doronicifolium

Hieracium doronicifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Hieracium doronicifolium is often found in disturbed soils and can spread aggressively through rhizomes.
