Hieracium gymnonotolepis

Hieracium gymnonotolepis, commonly known as the smoothleaf hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its basal rosette of smooth, lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous yellow composite flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has a taproot system and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its flowers are typically short-lived, opening in the morning and closing by midday.
