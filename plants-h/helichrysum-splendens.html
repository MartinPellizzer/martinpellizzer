Helichrysum splendens

Helichrysum splendens, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic, hairy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its long-lasting blooms make it a favorite in dried flower arrangements.
