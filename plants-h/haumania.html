Haumania

Haumania, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and enigmatic plant belonging to the family Haumanieae.

It is characterized by its unique, pale-colored flowers that resemble ghostly apparitions, hence its common name. The plant is native to the high altitudes of the Andes Mountains in South America, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Haumania has a compact growth habit with small, lance-shaped leaves and blooms during the cooler months.

Due to its elusive nature and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
