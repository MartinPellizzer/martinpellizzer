Hoya kuhlii

Hoya kuhlii, commonly known as the Waffle Plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, heart-shaped leaves.
