Hermione italica var. semiplena

Hermione italica var. semiplena, commonly known as the semi-flowered Italian honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color, appearing in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and its growth habit is compact and bushy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and attracting pollinators.

The semi-plena variety is distinguished by its partially filled flowers, giving it a unique appearance compared to other honeysuckle species.
