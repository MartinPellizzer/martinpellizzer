Habenaria digitata

Habenaria digitata, commonly known as the digitate orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often near streams or in undergrowth. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often with a subtle fragrance, and are arranged in a loose raceme.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
