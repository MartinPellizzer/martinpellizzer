Hieracium megaladenium

Hieracium megaladenium, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant features a robust stem that can grow up to 1 meter in height, with serrated, lance-shaped leaves. Its showy yellow flowers appear in late summer, forming dense clusters at the top of the plant.

Due to its invasive nature, it is considered a problematic weed in many regions.
