Hymenocallis pedunculata

Hymenocallis pedunculata, commonly known as the pink hurricane lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves and produces a tall, sturdy stem that supports its blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
