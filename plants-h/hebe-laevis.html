Hebe laevis

Hebe laevis, commonly known as the smooth hebe, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces clusters of blue or violet flowers during the spring and summer months. Hebe laevis typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and long blooming period.
