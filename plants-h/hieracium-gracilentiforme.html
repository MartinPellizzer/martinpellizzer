Hieracium gracilentiforme

Hieracium gracilentiforme, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces numerous yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped and serrated. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium gracilentiforme is a perennial plant that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
