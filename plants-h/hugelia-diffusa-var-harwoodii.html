Hugelia diffusa var. harwoodii

Hugelia diffusa var. harwoodii, commonly known as the Harwood's hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically large and glossy. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Hugelia diffusa var. harwoodii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
