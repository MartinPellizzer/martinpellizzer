Henckelia reptans

Henckelia reptans, commonly known as the creeping henckelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers. Its root system is fibrous, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

Henckelia reptans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
