Haworthia reinwardtii var. peddiensis

Haworthia reinwardtii var. peddiensis, commonly known as the "Pediensis Haworthia," is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves with a distinctive white or pale green coloration and prominent central markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
