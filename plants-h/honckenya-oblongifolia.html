Honckenya oblongifolia

Honckenya oblongifolia, commonly known as the mountain huckleberry, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows at high elevations, often in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and usually elliptical or oblong in shape.

Honckenya oblongifolia is adapted to cold climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
