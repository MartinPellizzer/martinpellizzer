Helichrysum wittei

Helichrysum wittei, commonly known as the golden crown, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble small suns. The plant produces small, papery bracts that give it a distinctive appearance, often mistaken for actual petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum wittei is also valued for its ornamental beauty and drought tolerance.
