Hippeastrum latifolium

Hippeastrum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom atop sturdy stems. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Hippeastrum latifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown both indoors and outdoors in suitable climates.
