Hypericum kiusianum

Hypericum kiusianum, commonly known as the Kiusian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes numerous species of shrubs and herbs known for their yellow flowers and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its flowers, which bloom in summer, are characterized by their vibrant yellow petals and five stamens.

Hypericum kiusianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic benefits.
