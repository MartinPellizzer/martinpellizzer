Hieracium macrocymum

Hieracium macrocymum, commonly known as the large-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium macrocymum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
