Hisingera elliptica

Hisingera elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its elliptical, narrow leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hisingera elliptica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
