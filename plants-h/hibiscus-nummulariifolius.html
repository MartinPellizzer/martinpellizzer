Hibiscus nummulariifolius

Hibiscus nummulariifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its round, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Hibiscus nummulariifolius is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
