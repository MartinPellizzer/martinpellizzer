Habenaria schwackei

Habenaria schwackei, commonly known as the Schwacke's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant produces lateral spikes bearing small, pink or white flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines.

Habenaria schwackei prefers moist, shaded habitats and is often found in forest understories where it thrives in the humid environment.
