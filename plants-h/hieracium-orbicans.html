Hieracium orbicans

Hieracium orbicans, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky flower heads that resemble a globe, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Hieracium orbicans produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.
