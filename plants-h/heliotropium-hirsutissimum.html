Heliotropium hirsutissimum

Heliotropium hirsutissimum, commonly known as the hairy heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with a faint scent. Its hairy stems and leaves are characteristic of the species, and it thrives in moist, sunny environments.

Heliotropium hirsutissimum is often found in disturbed soils and along coastal areas.
