Hieracium × disputabile

Hieracium × disputabile, commonly known as the hybrid dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, exhibiting a robust growth habit. Its flowers develop into fluffy seed heads, which are dispersed by wind, facilitating its spread.

Due to its hybrid origin, it displays a wide range of morphological variations.
