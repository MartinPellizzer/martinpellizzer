Helleborine atropurpurea f. orbicularis

Helleborine atropurpurea f. orbicularis, commonly known as the purple-flowered helleborine, is a perennial orchid with distinctive botanical features.

It has broad, ovate leaves that are often hairy and dark green in color. The plant produces striking purple or violet flowers with a unique, spurred lip. It thrives in woodland environments, preferring moist, shaded conditions.

This variety is characterized by its rounded leaf shape, which distinguishes it from other helleborine species.
