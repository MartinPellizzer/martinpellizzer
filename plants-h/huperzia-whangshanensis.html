Huperzia whangshanensis

Huperzia whangshanensis, commonly known as the Whangshan huperzia, is a rare fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Zhejiang Province. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, leathery, and often sparsely hairy leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
