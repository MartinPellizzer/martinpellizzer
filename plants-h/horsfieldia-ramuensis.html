Horsfieldia ramuensis

Horsfieldia ramuensis, commonly known as the Malay orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact stature and produces small, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, often white or yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
