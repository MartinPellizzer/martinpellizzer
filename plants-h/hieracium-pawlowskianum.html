Hieracium pawlowskianum

Hieracium pawlowskianum, commonly known as the Polish hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium pawlowskianum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
