Hedeoma multiflora

Hedeoma multiflora, commonly known as American burnet, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy and covered in fine, white pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers arranged in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Hedeoma multiflora is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
