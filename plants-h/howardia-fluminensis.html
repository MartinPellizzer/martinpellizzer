Howardia fluminensis

Howardia fluminensis, commonly known as the forest daisy, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Howardia fluminensis is drought-tolerant and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance.
