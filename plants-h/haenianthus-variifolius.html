Haenianthus variifolius

Haenianthus variifolius, commonly known as the variable leaf sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant has alternate, linear to lanceolate leaves that vary in shape and size along the stem, giving it its common name. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime climates.
