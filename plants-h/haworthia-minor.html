Haworthia minor

Haworthia minor, commonly known as the compact Haworthia, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for low-maintenance gardens.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor rock gardens.
