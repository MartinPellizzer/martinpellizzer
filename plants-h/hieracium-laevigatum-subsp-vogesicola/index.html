Hieracium Laevigatum Subsp. Vogesicola

Hieracium laevigatum subsp. vogesicola is a lesser-known member of the daisy family known for its subtle beauty. Found primarily in mountainous regions of Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and acidic soils. Its small, yellowish flowers bloom in early summer, adding a delicate touch to alpine landscapes. Despite its modest appearance, it plays a vital role in supporting local insect populations. This subspecies is a fascinating example of plant adaptation to harsh environments.
