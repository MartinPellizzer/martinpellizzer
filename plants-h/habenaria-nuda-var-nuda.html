Habenaria nuda var. nuda

Habenaria nuda var. nuda, commonly known as the naked lady orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and a single, showy spike of white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing a single, elongated flower with a distinctive, hooded lip. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, often growing in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable climates.
