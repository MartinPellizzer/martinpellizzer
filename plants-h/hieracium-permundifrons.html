Hieracium permundifrons

Hieracium permundifrons, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which are visited by various pollinators. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a hairy texture.

Hieracium permundifrons is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates.
