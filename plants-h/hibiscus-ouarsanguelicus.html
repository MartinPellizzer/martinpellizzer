Hibiscus ouarsanguelicus

Hibiscus ouarsanguelicus, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Hibiscus ouarsanguelicus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
