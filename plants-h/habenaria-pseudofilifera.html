Habenaria pseudofilifera

Habenaria pseudofilifera, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often near streams or in mountainous regions. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinct lip. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its specialized pollination mechanisms.
