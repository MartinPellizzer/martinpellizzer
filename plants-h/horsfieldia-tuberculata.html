Horsfieldia tuberculata

Horsfieldia tuberculata, commonly known as the warty orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit and possesses fleshy, dark green leaves with a warty texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Horsfieldia tuberculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
