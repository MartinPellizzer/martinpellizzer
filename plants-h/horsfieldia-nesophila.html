Horsfieldia nesophila

Horsfieldia nesophila, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees in the rainforest canopy.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
