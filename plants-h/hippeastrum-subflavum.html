Hippeastrum subflavum

Hippeastrum subflavum, commonly known as the yellow hurricane lily, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often spotted with white or green markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Hippeastrum subflavum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
