Hypericum drummondii

Hypericum drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the central and western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
