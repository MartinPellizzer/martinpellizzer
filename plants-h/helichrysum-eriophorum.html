Helichrysum eriophorum

Helichrysum eriophorum, commonly known as the woolly helichrysum, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance gardening.

The plant's aromatic leaves and long-lasting flowers also make it a valuable addition to herbal and ornamental collections.
