Helichrysum formosissimum var. formosissimum

Helichrysum formosissimum var. formosissimum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice in horticulture.
