Hosta plantaginea var. japonica

Hosta plantaginea var. japonica, commonly known as the Japanese hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers on tall, slender stems in the summer months. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
