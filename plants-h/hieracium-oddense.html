Hieracium oddense

Hieracium oddense, commonly known as the Oddens hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium oddense is often found in meadows and along roadsides, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
