Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. ayliesii

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

ayliesii, commonly known as the Aylies' hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is a perennial herb characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and grasslands. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in open habitats with moderate sunlight.
