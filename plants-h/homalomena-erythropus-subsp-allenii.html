Homalomena erythropus subsp. allenii

Homalomena erythropus subsp.

allenii, commonly known as the red-veined arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with prominent red veins.

The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense foliage.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
