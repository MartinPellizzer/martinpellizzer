Hieracium tenuans

Hieracium tenuans, commonly known as slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and the flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Hieracium tenuans is often found in open habitats and can spread aggressively in some regions.
