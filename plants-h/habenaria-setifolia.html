Habenaria setifolia

Habenaria setifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with prominent sepals and petals. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and rhizomatous growth habit.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in areas with high humidity and well-drained soil.
