Hibiscus setinervis

Hibiscus setinervis, commonly known as the serrated nerve hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant features palmate leaves with prominent veins, giving it its common name. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hibiscus setinervis is also known for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types, making it a versatile choice for gardeners.
