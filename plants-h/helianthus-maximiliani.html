Helianthus maximiliani

Helianthus maximiliani, commonly known as the Maximilian sunflower, is a species native to North America.

It is a herbaceous annual plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features large, yellow-orange flower heads with a distinctive central disk surrounded by ray florets. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in prairies and open fields.
