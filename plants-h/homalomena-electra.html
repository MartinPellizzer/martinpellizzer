Homalomena electra

Homalomena electra, commonly known as the golden arrow plant, is a tropical evergreen herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers with a distinctive yellow spadix, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
