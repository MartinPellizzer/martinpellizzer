Heptapleurum Alpinum

Heptapleurum alpinum is a rare and striking plant native to high altitude regions of Asia. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring dense clusters of small, waxy leaves. This evergreen shrub thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows. Its foliage displays a striking coloration that changes with the seasons. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered a valuable and endangered species.
