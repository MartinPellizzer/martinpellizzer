Heteropterys catingarum

Heteropterys catingarum, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red when mature. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in a variety of soil types.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
