Habenaria deanscowaniana

Habenaria deanscowaniana, commonly known as the Dean's Cowan's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and heathlands. Its flowering period occurs during the spring and early summer months.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its unique appearance.
