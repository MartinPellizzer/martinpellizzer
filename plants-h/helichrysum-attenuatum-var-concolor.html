Helichrysum attenuatum var. concolor

Helichrysum attenuatum var. concolor, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a low-growing, perennial shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces numerous small, tubular florets that are typically clustered in dense, spherical heads. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
