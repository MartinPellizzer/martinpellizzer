Hermannia oblongifolia

Hermannia oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hermannia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Hermannia oblongifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
