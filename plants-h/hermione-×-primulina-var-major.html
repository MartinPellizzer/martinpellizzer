Hermione × primulina var. major

Hermione × primulina var. major, commonly known as the giant primrose, is a hybrid plant that exhibits characteristics of both its parent species.

It is a herbaceous perennial known for its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and can grow up to several feet in height.

This variety is particularly noted for its ability to bloom profusely during the spring and early summer months.
