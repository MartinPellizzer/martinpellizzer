Hieracium loennbohmii

Hieracium loennbohmii, commonly known as the Loennberg's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy basal rosettes. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations due to its nectar-rich flowers.
