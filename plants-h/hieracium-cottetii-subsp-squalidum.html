Hieracium cottetii subsp. squalidum

Hieracium cottetii subsp.

squalidum, commonly known as the squalid hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
