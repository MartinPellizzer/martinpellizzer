Hebe gibbsii

Hebe gibbsii, commonly known as the mountain hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces showy clusters of blue to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. Hebe gibbsii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value contribute to its widespread cultivation in temperate climates.
