Hieracium wjazowoense

Hieracium wjazowoense, commonly known as the Wjazowoense hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowering heads composed of numerous small florets. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils. The plant has finely toothed leaves and produces a distinctive, slender flowering stem.

It is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
