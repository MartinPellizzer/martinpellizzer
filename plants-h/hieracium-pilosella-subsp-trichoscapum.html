Hieracium pilosella subsp. trichoscapum

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

trichoscapum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.
