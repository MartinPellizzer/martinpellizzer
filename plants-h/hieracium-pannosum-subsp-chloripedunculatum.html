Hieracium pannosum subsp. chloripedunculatum

Hieracium pannosum subsp.

chloripedunculatum, commonly known as the green-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy, sprawling growth habit and yellow composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its greenish stems and leaves, which are often covered in fine, white hairs. It typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.
