Habenaria longitheca

Habenaria longitheca, commonly known as the long-throated orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive lip that is often brightly colored. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is notable for its intricate floral structure and pollination mechanisms.
