Haworthia mirabilis var. triebneriana

Haworthia mirabilis var. triebneriana, commonly known as the "Triebner's Haworthia," is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
