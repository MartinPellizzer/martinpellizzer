Huperzia desvauxii

Huperzia desvauxii, commonly known as the Desvaux's huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant characterized by its simple, linear leaves and compact growth habit. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant's spore-producing structures are located on the underside of its leaves, typical of many ferns.

Huperzia desvauxii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
