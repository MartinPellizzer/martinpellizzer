Haworthia inconfluens var. lockwoodii

Haworthia inconfluens var. lockwoodii, commonly known as the Lockwoodii Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in well-draining soil. It produces small, star-shaped white flowers on tall stems during the growing season.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
