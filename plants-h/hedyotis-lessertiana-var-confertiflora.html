Hedyotis lessertiana var. confertiflora

Hedyotis lessertiana var. confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered blushing geranium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with small, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
