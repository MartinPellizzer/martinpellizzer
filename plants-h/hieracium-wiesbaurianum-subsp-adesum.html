Hieracium wiesbaurianum subsp. adesum

Hieracium wiesbaurianum subsp.

adesum, commonly known as the Wiesbaur's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers.

The plant typically has basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Central and Eastern Europe and thrives in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
