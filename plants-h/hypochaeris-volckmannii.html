Hypochaeris volckmannii

Hypochaeris volckmannii, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and has hairy stems and leaves. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in the spring and early summer.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
