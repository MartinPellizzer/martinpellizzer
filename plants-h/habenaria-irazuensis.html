Habenaria irazuensis

Habenaria irazuensis, commonly known as the Irazu orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments at higher elevations.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
