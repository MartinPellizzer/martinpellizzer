Hedysarum siliquosum

Hedysarum siliquosum, commonly known as the common sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Hedysarum siliquosum is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
