Hartmannia kunthiana

Hartmannia kunthiana, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are often white or pale pink, arranged in a spike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Hartmannia kunthiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
