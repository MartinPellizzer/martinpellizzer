Hemipilia avisoides

Hemipilia avisoides, commonly known as the bird's nest orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, bird-like flower structure that resembles a nest. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Hemipilia avisoides is considered an endangered species.
