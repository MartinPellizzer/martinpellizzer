Hieracium blatteum

Hieracium blatteum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates. It has finely divided leaves and produces achenes as its seeds.

Hieracium blatteum is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through both seeds and vegetative means.
