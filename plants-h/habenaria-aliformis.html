Habenaria aliformis

Habenaria aliformis, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous root system and erect, linear leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive zigzag pattern on the petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The orchid is valued for its unique floral structure and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
