Hieracium pseudomixtum

Hieracium pseudomixtum, commonly known as the false mixturoid dandelion, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. Native to Europe, it often grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces a distinctive, elongated seed head that disperses seeds via the wind.

It is a perennial species that can form dense colonies in suitable environments.
