Habenaria setigera

Habenaria setigera, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces erect, slender stems with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive zigzag pattern on the petals and sepals.

Habenaria setigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
