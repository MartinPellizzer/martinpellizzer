Hemsleya macrosperma var. macrosperma

Hemsleya macrosperma var. macrosperma, commonly known as the giant hemslay, is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that feature vibrant purple to red flowers, which bloom in terminal clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to semi-shaded environments in its natural habitat.
