Huodendron siamicum

Huodendron siamicum, commonly known as the Siamese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. Huodendron siamicum prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
