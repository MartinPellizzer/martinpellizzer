Hypericum russeggeri

Hypericum russeggeri, commonly known as the Alpine St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous habitats.

Hypericum russeggeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
