Haplopappus racemosus var. prionophyllus

Haplopappus racemosus var. prionophyllus, commonly known as the slender pappus plant, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow.

The plant is known for its unique, elongated pappus structures that aid in wind dispersal of its seeds.
