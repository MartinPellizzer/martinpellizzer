Hieracium caucasicum subsp. hohenackeri

Hieracium caucasicum subsp.

hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has basal and stem leaves that are usually hairy and have a rough texture.

Its flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.
