Haworthia candida

Haworthia candida, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves with white to light green markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
