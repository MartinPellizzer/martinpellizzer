Habenaria minima

Habenaria minima, commonly known as the minute orchid, is a small terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rock crevices. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, pale green or white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a short flowering period.

Habenaria minima is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
