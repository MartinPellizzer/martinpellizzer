Heliotropium antillanum

Heliotropium antillanum, commonly known as the tropical heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, tropical environments. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. Its flowers are often described as having a sweet, vanilla-like scent.

Heliotropium antillanum is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its use in traditional medicine.
