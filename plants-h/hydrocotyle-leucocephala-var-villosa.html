Hydrocotyle leucocephala var. villosa

Hydrocotyle leucocephala var. villosa, commonly known as the silky marsh pennywort, is a low-growing aquatic plant native to wetlands and marshy areas.

It features small, rounded leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. The plant typically has white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its habitat. It thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving water and is often found in temperate regions.

Its presence is an indicator of healthy, undisturbed wetland ecosystems.
