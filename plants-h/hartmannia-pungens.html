Hartmannia pungens

Hartmannia pungens, commonly known as the spiky orchid, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spiky appearance. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Hartmannia pungens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its striking visual appeal.
