Hypochaeris catharinensis

Hypochaeris catharinensis, commonly known as the Brazilian false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, where it thrives in full sun.
