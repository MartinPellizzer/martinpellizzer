Hypericum yakusimense

Hypericum yakusimense, commonly known as the Yakusim goldenstar, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or woodland habitats, preferring well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are woody and often branched.

Hypericum yakusimense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
