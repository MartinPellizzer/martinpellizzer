Hemsleya delavayi var. yalungensis

Hemsleya delavayi var. yalungensis, commonly known as the Yalung bellflower, is a species of flowering plant in the Campanulaceae family.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in specialized horticulture and botanical gardens.
