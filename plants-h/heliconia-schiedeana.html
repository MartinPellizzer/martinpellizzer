Heliconia schiedeana

Heliconia schiedeana, commonly known as the red heliconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Heliconiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble flowers. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces inflorescences with multiple small, clustered flowers. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The red heliconia is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is used in floral arrangements.
