Hieracium gnilagredae

Hieracium gnilagredae, commonly known as the sickleleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. Native to certain regions of Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some environments.
