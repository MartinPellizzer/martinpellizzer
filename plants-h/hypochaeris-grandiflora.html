Hypochaeris grandiflora

Hypochaeris grandiflora, commonly known as the largeleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are basal, alternate, and often hairy, with a distinctive yellow or orange disc flower that attracts pollinators.

Hypochaeris grandiflora is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
