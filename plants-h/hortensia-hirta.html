Hortensia hirta

Hortensia hirta, commonly known as the hairy hortensia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs, giving it its common name.

Hortensia hirta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil conditions.
