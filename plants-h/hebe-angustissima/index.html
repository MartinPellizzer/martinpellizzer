Hebe Angustissima

Hebe angustissima is a striking evergreen shrub native to New Zealand, known for its vibrant blue to violet flowers that bloom in spring. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its narrow, lance-shaped leaves add a unique texture to any landscape. Hardy and relatively low maintenance, it is favored by gardeners for its beauty and resilience. Hebe angustissima is not only ornamental but also supports local wildlife, enhancing biodiversity in gardens.
