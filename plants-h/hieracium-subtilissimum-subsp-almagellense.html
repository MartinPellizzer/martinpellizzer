Hieracium subtilissimum subsp. almagellense

Hieracium subtilissimum subsp.

almagellense, commonly known as the Almagellene hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
