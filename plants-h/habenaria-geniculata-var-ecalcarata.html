Habenaria geniculata var. ecalcarata

Habenaria geniculata var. ecalcarata, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with elongated sepals and a distinctive spur. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Orchidaceae family.
