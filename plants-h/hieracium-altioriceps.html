Hieracium altioriceps

Hieracium altioriceps, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features serrated, hairy leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium altioriceps is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to moderate grazing.
