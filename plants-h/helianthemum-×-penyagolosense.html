Helianthemum × penyagolosense

Helianthemum × penyagolosense, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes where it adds visual interest with its vibrant floral display.
