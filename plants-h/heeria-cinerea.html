Heeria cinerea

Heeria cinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf heeria, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, gray-green foliage that gives it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers during the summer months. Heeria cinerea is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
