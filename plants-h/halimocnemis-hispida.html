Halimocnemis hispida

Halimocnemis hispida, commonly known as the rough halimocnemis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or arid environments. Its adaptability to dry conditions makes it a resilient species in its native habitat.

Halimocnemis hispida is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
