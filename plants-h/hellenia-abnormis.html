Hellenia abnormis

Hellenia abnormis, commonly known as the twisted hellena, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally twisted stems that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Hellenia abnormis is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions.
