Hieracium floribundum var. subfloribundum

Hieracium floribundum var. subfloribundum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped with toothed margins.

Its yellow flowers bloom in summer, and it produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind.
