Hadrolaelia × fidelensis

Hadrolaelia × fidelensis, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Hadrolaelia and Laelia species.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes, particularly in Ecuador. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and linear, lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are typically showy, with a range of colors including white, pink, and purple.

It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
