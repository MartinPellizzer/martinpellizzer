Homalomena hanneae

Homalomena hanneae, commonly known as the Hanne's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with broad, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Homalomena hanneae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
