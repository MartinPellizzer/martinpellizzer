Hieracium murorum subsp. euchloroprasinum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

euchloroprasinum, commonly known as the green-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The leaves are typically narrow, grass-like, and have a distinctive greenish hue, which gives the species its common name. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
