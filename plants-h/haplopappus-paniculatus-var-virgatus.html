Haplopappus paniculatus var. virgatus

Haplopappus paniculatus var. virgatus, commonly known as the slenderleaf goatweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically herbaceous growth habit. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and open fields.
