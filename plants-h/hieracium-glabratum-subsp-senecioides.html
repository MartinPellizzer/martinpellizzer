Hieracium glabratum subsp. senecioides

Hieracium glabratum subsp.

senecioides, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and dandelion-like yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a single head.

It is a flowering plant that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
