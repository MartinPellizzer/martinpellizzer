Herpetophytum Millariae

Herpetophytum millariae is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of eastern Africa. Known for its unique, spiral-shaped leaves that resemble the patterns of reptile skin, it has earned its common name. This species is highly sought after by collectors due to its distinctive appearance and limited natural habitat. It thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity and well-drained soil. Despite its beauty, it remains a challenging plant to cultivate outside its native region.
