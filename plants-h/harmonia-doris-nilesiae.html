Harmonia doris-nilesiae

Harmonia doris-nilesiae, commonly known as the Doris-Nilesiae Orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant blooms that range in color from pale pink to deep magenta. The plant has slender, strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid environments with high light exposure and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered an endangered species and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for conservation purposes.
