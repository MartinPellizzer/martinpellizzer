Hieracium kerneri subsp. issense

Hieracium kerneri subsp.

issense, commonly known as the Italian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellowish to orange-red composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain regions.
