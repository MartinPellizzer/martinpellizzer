Hippeastrum hermokrokos

Hippeastrum hermokrokos, commonly known as the Hermokrokos amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and dark green, emerging from a bulbous underground stem. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hippeastrum hermokrokos is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
