Hieracium erythraeum

Hieracium erythraeum, commonly known as the red hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has hairy, alternate leaves that are often lobed or toothed. It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in dry meadows and rocky areas.

Hieracium erythraeum is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in disturbed soils.
