Hieracium waldsteinii f. oligadenum

Hieracium waldsteinii f. oligadenum, commonly known as the oligadenum hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves and produces yellow flowers in a compact inflorescence. Native to Central and Eastern Europe, this plant thrives in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and grasslands. It is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense colonies.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
