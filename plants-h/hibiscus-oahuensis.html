Hibiscus oahuensis

Hibiscus oahuensis, commonly known as the Oahu hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the island of Oahu in Hawaii.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat. Hibiscus oahuensis is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories.

It plays an important ecological role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining the biodiversity of Hawaiian ecosystems.
