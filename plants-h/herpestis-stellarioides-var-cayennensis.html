Herpestis stellarioides var. cayennensis

Herpestis stellarioides var. cayennensis, commonly known as the Cayenne starwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid, lowland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
