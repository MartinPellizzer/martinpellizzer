Hedera helix var. angularis

Hedera helix var. angularis, commonly known as the angular ivy, is a cultivar of the common ivy plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive, angular leaf shape and vibrant green color. This variety thrives in shaded environments and is often used in hanging baskets or as a trailing plant. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small spaces and containers.

It requires well-draining soil and moderate watering to maintain its health and appearance.
