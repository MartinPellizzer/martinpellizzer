Hederella lysipetala

Hederella lysipetala, commonly known as the small-leaf ivy, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Hederella lysipetala is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
