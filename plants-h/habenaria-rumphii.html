Habenaria rumphii

Habenaria rumphii, commonly known as the Rumphii Pink Violet, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often near streams or in mountainous regions. The plant features a sympodial growth pattern, with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots each year. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown spots.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture for its delicate appearance.
