Hieracium subglaberrimum subsp. draconis

Hieracium subglaberrimum subsp.

draconis, commonly known as dragon's milk weed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a composite head. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
