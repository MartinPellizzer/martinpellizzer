Hoya velutina

Hoya velutina, commonly known as the velvet hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the summer months. Hoya velutina is a trailing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular among houseplant enthusiasts for its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
