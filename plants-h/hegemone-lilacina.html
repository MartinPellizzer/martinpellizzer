Hegemone lilacina

Hegemone lilacina, commonly known as the purpleleaf hedera, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves that often display a purplish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in shaded to partially sunny environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Hegemone lilacina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be used in vertical gardening or as a ground cover.
