Hyptis vestita var. glabriuscula

Hyptis vestita var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the velvetleaf hyptis, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils or near water bodies.

Its aromatic leaves and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental and herbal use.
