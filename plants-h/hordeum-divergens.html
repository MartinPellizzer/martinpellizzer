Hordeum divergens

Hordeum divergens, commonly known as the slender barley, is a species of grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairless stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences, which are typical of the Poaceae family. Hordeum divergens is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

It is not widely cultivated for agricultural purposes but is of interest in ecological and taxonomic studies.
