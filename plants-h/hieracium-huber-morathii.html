Hieracium huber-morathii

Hieracium huber-morathii, commonly known as the Morath's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets that are often shorter than the disk florets.

Hieracium huber-morathii is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and seeds, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
