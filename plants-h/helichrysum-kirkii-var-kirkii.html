Helichrysum kirkii var. kirkii

Helichrysum kirkii var. kirkii, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flower heads that resemble daisies, which are tightly packed and retain their color when dried. The plant has silver-green, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
