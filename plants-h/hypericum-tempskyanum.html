Hypericum tempskyanum

Hypericum tempskyanum, commonly known as the Armenian St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological gardens.
