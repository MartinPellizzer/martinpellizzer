Haworthia longiana

Haworthia longiana, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves with distinctive white or yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
