Hyptis insignis

Hyptis insignis, commonly known as the blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hyptis insignis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
