Hydrolea uniflora

Hydrolea uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower hydrolea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has a creeping growth habit with succulent, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Hydrolea uniflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
