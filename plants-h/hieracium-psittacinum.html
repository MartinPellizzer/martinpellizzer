Hieracium psittacinum

Hieracium psittacinum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.

Hieracium psittacinum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
