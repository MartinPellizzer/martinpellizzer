Helichrysum panaetioides

Helichrysum panaetioides, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers that resemble tiny sunflowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum panaetioides is also valued for its ornamental beauty and drought tolerance.
