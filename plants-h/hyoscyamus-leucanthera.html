Hyoscyamus leucanthera

Hyoscyamus leucanthera, commonly known as the white thornapple, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in temperate climates. The plant features showy, pale yellow or white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are broad and lobed, while its fruits are toxic berries that mature to a dark purple color.

Hyoscyamus leucanthera is known for its medicinal and hallucinogenic properties, though it is also considered a weed in some areas.
