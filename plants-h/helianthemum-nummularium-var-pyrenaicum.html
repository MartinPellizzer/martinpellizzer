Helianthemum nummularium var. pyrenaicum

Helianthemum nummularium var. pyrenaicum, commonly known as the Pyrenean stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent perennial native to the Pyrenees region.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically silver-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate floral display. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun conditions.

This variety is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
