Hesperis laciniata var. hieraciifolia

Hesperis laciniata var. hieraciifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sweet rocket, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with small, four-petaled flowers. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, often preceding the leaves.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
