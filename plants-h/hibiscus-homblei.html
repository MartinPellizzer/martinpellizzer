Hibiscus homblei

Hibiscus homblei, commonly known as the Hainan hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with dark green, broad leaves. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color and bloom in the summer months.

Hibiscus homblei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
