Hieracium mixtum

Hieracium mixtum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, often becoming invasive.

Hieracium mixtum thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.
