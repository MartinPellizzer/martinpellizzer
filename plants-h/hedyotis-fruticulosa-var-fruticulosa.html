Hedyotis fruticulosa var. fruticulosa

Hedyotis fruticulosa var. fruticulosa, commonly known as the small flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and evergreen leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, low-maintenance plants.
