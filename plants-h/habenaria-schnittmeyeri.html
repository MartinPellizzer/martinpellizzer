Habenaria schnittmeyeri

Habenaria schnittmeyeri, commonly known as the Schnittmeyer's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale pink, and are arranged in a loose spike.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
