Hieracium hirtellum

Hieracium hirtellum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium hirtellum is often found in disturbed habitats and can spread aggressively through its rhizomes.
