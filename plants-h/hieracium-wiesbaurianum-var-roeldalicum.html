Hieracium wiesbaurianum var. roeldalicum

Hieracium wiesbaurianum var. roeldalicum, commonly known as the Roeldalicum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has finely divided, lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This variety is native to Europe and is often found in temperate regions.
