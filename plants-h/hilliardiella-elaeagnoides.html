Hilliardiella elaeagnoides

Hilliardiella elaeagnoides, commonly known as the silverleaf shrub, is a woody perennial native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Hilliardiella elaeagnoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to dry conditions.
