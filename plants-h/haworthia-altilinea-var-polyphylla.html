Haworthia altilinea var. polyphylla

Haworthia altilinea var. polyphylla, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or yellowish markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
