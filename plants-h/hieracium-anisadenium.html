Hieracium anisadenium

Hieracium anisadenium, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and has hairy stems and leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or purple, with ray florets that give it a daisy-like appearance.

It is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
