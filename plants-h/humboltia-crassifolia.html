Humboltia crassifolia

Humboltia crassifolia, commonly known as the thick-leaved hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
