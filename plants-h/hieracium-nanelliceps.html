Hieracium nanelliceps

Hieracium nanelliceps, commonly known as the narrow-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium nanelliceps is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
