Hypoxis bampsiana

Hypoxis bampsiana, commonly known as the Bamp's yellowstar, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and grass-like foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional African medicine.

Hypoxis bampsiana is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
