Hieracium scorzonerifolium subsp. scorzonerifolium

Hieracium scorzonerifolium subsp.

scorzonerifolium, commonly known as the scorzonerifolium hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It prefers moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species in some regions.
