Hypericum ciliatum var. montbretii

Hypericum ciliatum var. montbretii, commonly known as the Montbretii St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative plant in horticulture.
