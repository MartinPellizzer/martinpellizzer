Hieracium nigrificatum

Hieracium nigrificatum, commonly known as black devil's bit.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae, characterized by its dark purple to black flowers and hairy stems. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and flies.

It is often found growing in colonies, forming dense mats that can dominate certain habitats.
