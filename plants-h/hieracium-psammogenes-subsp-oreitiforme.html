Hieracium psammogenes subsp. oreitiforme

Hieracium psammogenes subsp.

oreitiforme, commonly known as the sand hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils.

The plant features yellow ray florets and dark brown disk florets, with a generally compact growth habit. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

It is adapted to arid environments and plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils.
