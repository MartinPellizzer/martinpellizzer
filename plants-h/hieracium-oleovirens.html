Hieracium oleovirens

Hieracium oleovirens, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are typically basal, with a rough texture and a hairy surface.

Hieracium oleovirens is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
