Hieracium rigorosum

Hieracium rigorosum, commonly known as the rigid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive leaf structures. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped, rough to the touch, and often have a hairy texture.

The flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped cluster, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.
