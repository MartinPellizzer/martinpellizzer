Hieracium cymosum subsp. regelii

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

regelii, commonly known as Regel's hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which are often covered in fine, white pubescence.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a cymose inflorescence. It is native to Central Asia and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
