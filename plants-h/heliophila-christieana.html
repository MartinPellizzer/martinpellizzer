Heliophila christieana

Heliophila christieana, commonly known as Christie's sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sunlight. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Heliophila christieana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
