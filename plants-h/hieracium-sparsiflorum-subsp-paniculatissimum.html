Hieracium sparsiflorum subsp. paniculatissimum

Hieracium sparsiflorum subsp.

paniculatissimum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs, while its inflorescence consists of a dense, branched arrangement of small flowers.

This subspecies is distinguished by its more open and paniculate flowering structure compared to other members of the species.
