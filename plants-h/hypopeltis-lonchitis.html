Hypopeltis lonchitis

Hypopeltis lonchitis, commonly known as the slender grasshopper plant, is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to thrive in environments with moderate sunlight and well-drained soils.
