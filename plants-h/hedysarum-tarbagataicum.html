Hedysarum tarbagataicum

Hedysarum tarbagataicum, commonly known as the Tarbagatai milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a deep root system, allowing it to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in steppes and rocky areas, contributing to soil stabilization.

Its seeds are adapted for dispersal by wind, aiding in its survival in harsh climates.
