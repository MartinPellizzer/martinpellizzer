Hieracium sparsum subsp. chromolepium

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

chromolepium, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy yellow to orange flowers.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture. Native to parts of Europe and western Asia, it thrives in moist, disturbed soils.

This subspecies is distinguished by its vibrant floral coloration and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks.
