Hibiscus womersleyanus

Hibiscus womersleyanus, commonly known as the Womersley's hibiscus, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces fruit that resembles a capsule, containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist woodland or swampy areas.

Hibiscus womersleyanus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
