Hypericum hyssopifolium var. lydium

Hypericum hyssopifolium var. lydium, commonly known as the Lydian St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom from June to August. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and a strong, spicy aroma. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
