Hypericum cymosum

Hypericum cymosum, commonly known as narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in compact clusters. The plant has slender, woody stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its botanical name reflects its close relation to other members of the Hypericaceae family.
