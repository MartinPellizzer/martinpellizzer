Helichrysum striatum

Helichrysum striatum, commonly known as the strawflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange inflorescences, which resemble small, fluffy flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is known for its long-lasting blooms, making it popular in dried flower arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

Helichrysum striatum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
