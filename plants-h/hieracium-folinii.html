Hieracium folinii

Hieracium folinii, commonly known as the Italian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to southern Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides. It has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

Hieracium folinii is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
