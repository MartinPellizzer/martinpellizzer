Helichrysum purpurascens

Helichrysum purpurascens, commonly known as the purple everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, bright yellow to orange flowers that are often covered in fine, hairy structures. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in a soft, woolly coating.

Helichrysum purpurascens is well-known for its drought tolerance and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and resilience.
