Hieracium poliodermum

Hieracium poliodermum, commonly known as the pale-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are generally pale green and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Hieracium poliodermum is often found in open habitats and can sometimes form dense colonies.
