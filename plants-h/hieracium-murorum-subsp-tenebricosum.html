Hieracium murorum subsp. tenebricosum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

tenebricosum, commonly known as the dark hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disc florets.

The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

This subspecies is distinguished by its darker coloration and slightly different morphology compared to other members of the Hieracium genus.
