Hieracium fragile proles barbulatum

Hieracium fragile proles barbulatum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes, making it somewhat invasive in certain environments.
