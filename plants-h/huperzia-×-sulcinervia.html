Huperzia × sulcinervia

Huperzia × sulcinervia, commonly known as the hybrid rock huperzia, is a fern-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its leathery, scale-like leaves that form dense, cushion-like growths, typically growing on rocky substrates. The plant is native to regions of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its sporangia are clustered in terminal clusters, a feature typical of the genus Huperzia.

Huperzia × sulcinervia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
