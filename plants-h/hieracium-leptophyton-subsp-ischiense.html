Hieracium leptophyton subsp. ischiense

Hieracium leptophyton subsp.

ischiense, commonly known as the Ischian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and hairy, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
