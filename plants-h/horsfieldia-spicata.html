Horsfieldia spicata

Horsfieldia spicata, commonly known as the spike orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Horsfieldia spicata thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests.
