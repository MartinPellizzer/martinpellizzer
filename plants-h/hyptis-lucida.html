Hyptis lucida

Hyptis lucida, commonly known as white hyptis, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Hyptis lucida is also valued for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to treat various ailments.
