Habenaria lacertifera

Habenaria lacertifera, commonly known as the lizard orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to various regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Himalayas.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous areas. The plant features a slender rhizome with several erect, linear leaves. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and are characterized by their distinctive, often yellow or greenish, spurred petals.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
