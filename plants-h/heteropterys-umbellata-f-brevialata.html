Heteropterys umbellata f. brevialata

Heteropterys umbellata f. brevialata, commonly known as the short-leaved umbellate elder, is a variant of the species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and shorter, broader leaves compared to the typical form. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
