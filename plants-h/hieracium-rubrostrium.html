Hieracium rubrostrium

Hieracium rubrostrium, commonly known as the red-streaked hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 40 centimeters. Its leaves are hairy and lance-shaped, while the flowers are yellow with red streaks along the petals.

It is an herbaceous perennial that produces numerous seeds, contributing to its widespread distribution.
