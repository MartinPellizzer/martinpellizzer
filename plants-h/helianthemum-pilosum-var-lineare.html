Helianthemum pilosum var. lineare

Helianthemum pilosum var. lineare, commonly known as the linear thyme.

This perennial shrub is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its low-growing habit and aromatic foliage. The leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a distinct appearance compared to other thyme varieties. It produces small, yellow flowers in the summer, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
