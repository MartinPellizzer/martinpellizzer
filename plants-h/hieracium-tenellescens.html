Hieracium tenellescens

Hieracium tenellescens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow to orange flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically has slender, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often hairy as well. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides, preferring well-drained soils.

Hieracium tenellescens is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.
