Haplopappus retinervius

Haplopappus retinervius, commonly known as the slender pappus plant, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive bracts. The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and are pollinated primarily by insects.
