Hypericum angulosum

Hypericum angulosum, commonly known as angular St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, angular leaves and is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.

The plant's name reflects its distinctive leaf shape and its association with the genus Hypericum.
