Hymenocallis hawkesii

Hymenocallis hawkesii, commonly known as the Hawkes' spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It features large, showy white flowers with prominent yellow stamens that resemble spider legs, giving it its common name. The plant produces strap-like, dark green leaves and has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread in moist, well-drained soils. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
