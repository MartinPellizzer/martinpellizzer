Hieracium praecox subsp. venulosum

Hieracium praecox subsp.

venulosum, commonly known as the early hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow ray florets and a dense, upright growth habit.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, giving the plant a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

This subspecies is notable for its ability to spread through both seed production and vegetative reproduction.
