Hebe Barkeri

Barker's hebe, scientifically known as Hebe barkeri, is a striking perennial shrub native to New Zealand. This plant is celebrated for its vibrant flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions. It belongs to the mint family and is commonly found in forested areas and rocky habitats. Barker's hebe is a popular choice for gardeners due to its low maintenance and ornamental value. Its unique appearance makes it a standout addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
