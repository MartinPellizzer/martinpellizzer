Hibiscus wellbyi

Hibiscus wellbyi, commonly known as the wellby's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Hibiscus wellbyi is a shrub that can grow up to several feet tall, with woody stems and fibrous roots.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
