Hieracium vaudense

Hieracium vaudense, commonly known as the Vaud hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
