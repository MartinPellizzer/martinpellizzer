Hemigenia clotteniana

Hemigenia clotteniana, commonly known as the slender hemigenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often hairy. It produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring.

Hemigenia clotteniana is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
