Hesperis subspinescens

Hesperis subspinescens, commonly known as the prickly honesty, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

Hesperis subspinescens is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy garden plant.
