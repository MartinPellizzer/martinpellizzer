Hypericum assamicum

Hypericum assamicum, commonly known as the Assam goldenseal, is a perennial herb native to the eastern Himalayas.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its medicinal properties have been explored for their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
