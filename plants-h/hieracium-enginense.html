Hieracium enginense

Hieracium enginense, commonly known as the Engin's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in a composite head. Native to specific regions of Europe, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils. The plant is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
