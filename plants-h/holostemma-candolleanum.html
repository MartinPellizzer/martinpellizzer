Holostemma candolleanum

Holostemma candolleanum, commonly known as the white holostemma, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.
