Hieracium escalanteae

Hieracium escalanteae, commonly known as the Escalante hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces yellow to reddish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Hieracium escalanteae is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats.
