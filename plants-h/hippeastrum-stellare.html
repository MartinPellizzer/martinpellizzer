Hippeastrum stellare

Hippeastrum stellare, commonly known as the star hippeastrum, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. Its leaves are broad, strap-like, and often emerge from the base of the plant.

Hippeastrum stellare is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
