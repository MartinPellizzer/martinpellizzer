Hypericum bissellii

Hypericum bissellii, commonly known as narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry meadows and rocky areas.

Hypericum bissellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
