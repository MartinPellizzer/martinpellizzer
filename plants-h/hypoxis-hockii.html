Hypoxis hockii

Hypoxis hockii, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Hypoxis hockii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
