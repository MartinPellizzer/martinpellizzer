Hieracium oligolepium

Hieracium oligolepium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy surface, while its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.

Hieracium oligolepium is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
