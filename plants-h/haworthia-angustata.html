Haworthia angustata

Haworthia angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and ribbed. The leaves are usually gray-green with white markings, and the plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the growing season. This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
