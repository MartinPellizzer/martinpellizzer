Haworthia decipiens var. decipiens

Haworthia decipiens var. decipiens, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves covered in white to yellow tubercles that resemble a zebra's stripes. The leaves are typically bluish-green with a waxy coating, and the plant produces tall, slender flower stems with white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

This plant is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
