Huperzia varia

Huperzia varia, commonly known as the variable huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and distinctive, clustered growth habit. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves with a serrated margin, and it produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptability make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
