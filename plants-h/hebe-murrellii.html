Hebe murrellii

Hebe murrellii, commonly known as the Murrell's hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its erect, compact growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in spring. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
