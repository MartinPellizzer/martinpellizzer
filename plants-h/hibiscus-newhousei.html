Hibiscus newhousei

Hibiscus newhousei, commonly known as the Newhouse hibiscus, is a rare and unique species native to the island of New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically range in color from deep red to orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often found growing in moist, shaded environments. Hibiscus newhousei is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in tropical climates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
