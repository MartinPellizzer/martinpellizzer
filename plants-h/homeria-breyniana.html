Homeria breyniana

Homeria breyniana, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or a small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
