Hieracium pulverosiceps

Hieracium pulverosiceps, commonly known as the powdered milk thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its hairy, yellowish-green leaves and spherical, spiny seed heads. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

The species is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other open habitats.
