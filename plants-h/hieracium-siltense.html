Hieracium siltense

Hieracium siltense, commonly known as the Silt Hill hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium siltense is often found in open habitats and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain areas.
