Helianthemum oelandicum f. montserratense

Helianthemum oelandicum f. montserratense, commonly known as the Catalan rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It is well adapted to harsh environments, displaying drought tolerance and resistance to salt spray.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
