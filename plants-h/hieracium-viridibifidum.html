Hieracium viridibifidum

Hieracium viridibifidum, commonly known as green-veined hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers with ray florets. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture, often with a bluish-green hue. This species is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

It is a perennial plant that blooms from late spring to early summer.
