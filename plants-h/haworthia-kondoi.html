Haworthia kondoi

Haworthia kondoi, commonly known as the Kondoi Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit. The plant features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the warmer months.

Haworthia kondoi is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
