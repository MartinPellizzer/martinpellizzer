Haworthia mucronata var. nortieri

Haworthia mucronata var. nortieri, commonly known as the Nortier's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for dry environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
