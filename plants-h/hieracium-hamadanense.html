Hieracium hamadanense

Hieracium hamadanense, commonly known as the Hamadan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
