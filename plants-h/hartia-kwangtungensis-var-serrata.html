Hartia kwangtungensis var. serrata

Hartia kwangtungensis var. serrata, commonly known as the Guangdong holly.

It is a shrub native to China, characterized by its evergreen leaves with serrated edges. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1-2 meters and has small, clustered white flowers. Its foliage is dense and glossy, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
