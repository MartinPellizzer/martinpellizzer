Hoffmannseggia sandersonii var. lactea

Hoffmannseggia sandersonii var. lactea, commonly known as the white-flowered Hoffmannseggia, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Mexico.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming cactus with pale green stems and white, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features spiny areoles and produces small, ribbed stems that help it conserve water in arid environments.

Its unique floral structure and coloration make it a distinctive and sought-after species among cactus enthusiasts.
