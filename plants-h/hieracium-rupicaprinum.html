Hieracium rupicaprinum

Hieracium rupicaprinum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and bright yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. Native to the alpine regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant has finely divided, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is known for its ability to withstand cold temperatures and is often found in mountainous habitats.
