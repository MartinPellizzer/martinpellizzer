Hopea chinensis

Hopea chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of China, India, and Indonesia. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically greenish-yellow in color, and it produces woody fruits that contain one or two seeds.

The plant is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland evergreen forests.
