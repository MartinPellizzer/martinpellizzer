Hieracium fragile subsp. prasinifolium

Hieracium fragile subsp.

prasinifolium, commonly known as the green-leaved hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its slender stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
