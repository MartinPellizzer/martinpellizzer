Hypericum tenuicaule

Hypericum tenuicaule, commonly known as slender St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine, yellowish hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly waxy texture.

Hypericum tenuicaule is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
