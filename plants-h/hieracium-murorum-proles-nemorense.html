Hieracium murorum proles nemorense

Hieracium murorum proles nemorense, commonly known as the nemorensis hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, erect stems and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats such as roadsides and meadows. The plant has small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable environments.
