Hesperantha leucantha

Hesperantha leucantha, commonly known as the white star lily, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form with narrow, strap-like leaves. Its blooms appear in late spring to early summer, adding a dramatic visual impact to gardens.

Hesperantha leucantha is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded or partially shaded environments.
