Hieracium prilakenense

Hieracium prilakenense, commonly known as the Prilaken hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

It is often found in meadows and along roadsides, contributing to local biodiversity.
