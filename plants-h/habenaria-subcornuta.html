Habenaria subcornuta

Habenaria subcornuta, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive horn-like spur at the base of the lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it a notable species within its native habitats.
