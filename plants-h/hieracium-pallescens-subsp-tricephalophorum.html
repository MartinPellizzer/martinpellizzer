Hieracium pallescens subsp. tricephalophorum

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

tricephalophorum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy, pale yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces achenes as its seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
