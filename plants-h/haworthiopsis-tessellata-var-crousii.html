Haworthiopsis tessellata var. crousii

Haworthiopsis tessellata var. crousii, commonly known as the Crous' Haworthiopsis, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive white or light green pattern resembling a mosaic. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
