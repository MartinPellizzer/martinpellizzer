Habenaria butantanensis

Habenaria butantanensis, commonly known as the Butantan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinct, fragrant scent. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
