Hyptis iodantha

Hyptis iodantha, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
