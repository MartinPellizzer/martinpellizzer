Haworthia reinwardtii var. greenii

Haworthia reinwardtii var. greenii, commonly known as the Green Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often marked with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
