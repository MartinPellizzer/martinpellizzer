Hieracium tenuifolium

Hieracium tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy surface, and its flowers are yellow, often arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
