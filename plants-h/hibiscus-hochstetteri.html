Hibiscus hochstetteri

Hibiscus hochstetteri, commonly known as the Hochstetter's hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to New Zealand.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the stage of bloom. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Hibiscus hochstetteri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued for its ecological role in supporting native pollinators.
