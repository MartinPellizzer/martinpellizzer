Hoitzia ramosissima

Hoitzia ramosissima, commonly known as the hairy leaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed soils.

Hoitzia ramosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
