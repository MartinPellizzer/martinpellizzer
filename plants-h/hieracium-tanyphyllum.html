Hieracium tanyphyllum

Hieracium tanyphyllum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium tanyphyllum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural and natural areas.
