Hydrosme niimurai

Hydrosme niimurai, commonly known as the Japanese water lily, is a species of aquatic plant native to the wetlands of Japan.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide a habitat for various aquatic organisms. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the water, supporting its growth in shallow aquatic environments.

Hydrosme niimurai plays an important role in maintaining the ecological balance of freshwater ecosystems.
