Haworthia icosiphylla

Haworthia icosiphylla, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, fleshy leaves with white or pale green markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
