Hortensia Macrophylla F. Isobue

Hortensia macrophylla f. isobue is a unique and striking variant of the common hortensia known for its large, showy blooms. This particular form exhibits an unusual coloration that sets it apart from typical hortensia varieties. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant floral displays. The plant is prized for its adaptability and ability to produce abundant flowers throughout the growing season. Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after addition to both private and public gardens.
