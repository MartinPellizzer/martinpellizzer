Hieracium salayense subsp. brevifissum

Hieracium salayense subsp.

brevifissum, commonly known as the short-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in grassy and alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are basal and lanceolate, with a rough texture, and it produces yellow flowers in late summer.
