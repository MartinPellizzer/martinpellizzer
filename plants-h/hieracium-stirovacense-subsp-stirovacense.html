Hieracium stirovacense subsp. stirovacense

Hieracium stirovacense subsp.

stirovacense, commonly known as the Stirovacense hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils, often in mountainous areas. It has finely divided, serrated leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
