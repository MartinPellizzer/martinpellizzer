Helenium mattfeldianum

Helenium mattfeldianum, commonly known as the Mattfeld's sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, orange to yellow ray florets surrounding a central disc of tubular flowers. It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and swamps.

Helenium mattfeldianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
