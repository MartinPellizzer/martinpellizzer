Helianthemum Atlanticum

Helianthemum atlanticum is a hardy shrub native to the coastal regions of the Iberian Peninsula. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found along cliffs and sea shores. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a splash of color to its rugged habitat. Its resilience to salt and wind makes it a common sight in coastal landscapes. This plant plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
