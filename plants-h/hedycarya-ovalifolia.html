Hedycarya ovalifolia

Hedycarya ovalifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hedycarya, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers, which are often borne on short pedicels. Hedycarya ovalifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along riverbanks.
