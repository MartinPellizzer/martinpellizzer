Heeria aromatica

Heeria aromatica, commonly known as sweet tea or fragrant heeria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant is often grown for its fragrant foliage, which is used in traditional herbal remedies. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Heeria aromatica is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its medicinal properties.
