Hieracium chaunadenium

Hieracium chaunadenium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, producing a showy display that attracts pollinators.

Hieracium chaunadenium is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it both a common weed and an ecological indicator species.
