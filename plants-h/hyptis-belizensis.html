Hyptis belizensis

Hyptis belizensis, commonly known as the Belize hyptis, is a flowering plant native to Central America, particularly Belize.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hyptis belizensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks.
