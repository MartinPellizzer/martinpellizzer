Hieracium notense

Hieracium notense, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy areas and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

Hieracium notense is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in disturbed habitats.
