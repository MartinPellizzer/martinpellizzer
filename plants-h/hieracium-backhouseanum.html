Hieracium backhouseanum

Hieracium backhouseanum, commonly known as the backhouse's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats. It has small, linear leaves and produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often found in gardens and naturalized areas in temperate regions.
