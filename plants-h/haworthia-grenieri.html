Haworthia grenieri

Haworthia grenieri, commonly known as the Zulu beauty or the mountain haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically light green with distinct white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
