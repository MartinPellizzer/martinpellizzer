Hardenbergia monophylla var. rosea

Hardenbergia monophylla var. rosea, commonly known as the pink burr legume, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny pods and its striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and unique appearance.

This variety is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
