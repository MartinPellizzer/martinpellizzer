Heterotheca grandiflora

Heterotheca grandiflora, commonly known as the blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy, blue to purple composite flower heads that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant has silvery-gray foliage and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
