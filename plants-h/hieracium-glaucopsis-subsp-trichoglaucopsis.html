Hieracium glaucopsis subsp. trichoglaucopsis

Hieracium glaucopsis subsp.

trichoglaucopsis, commonly known as the glaucous hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in a whitish or bluish waxy coating, known as a glaucous bloom.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It is typically found in moist meadows and along stream banks in temperate regions.
