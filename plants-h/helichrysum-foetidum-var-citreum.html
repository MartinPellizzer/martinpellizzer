Helichrysum foetidum var. citreum

Helichrysum foetidum var. citreum, commonly known as the sweet helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers and aromatic, fern-like foliage. The plant is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
