Hieracium angustatum subsp. orsense

Hieracium angustatum subsp.

orsense, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellowish-orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows, pastures, and woodland edges, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate, often with a hairy surface, and it produces numerous tiny seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This subspecies is notable for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its potential use in horticulture.
