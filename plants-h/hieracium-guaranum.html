Hieracium guaranum

Hieracium guaranum, commonly known as the Guarani dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium guaranum is often used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities for its purported health benefits.
