Hydnora solmsiana

Hydnora solmsiana, commonly known as the devil's flower, is a parasitic plant belonging to the Hydnoraceae family.

Its flowers are usually unisexual, with separate male and female structures.

Hydnora solmsiana is native to southern Africa and is considered a rare and specialized species due to its parasitic nature and specific ecological requirements.
