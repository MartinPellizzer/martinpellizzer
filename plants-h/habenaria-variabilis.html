Habenaria variabilis

Habenaria variabilis, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish or yellowish flowers arranged in a raceme, often with a distinct lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its variable morphology across different populations.
