Hieracium glanduliferum f. nigrosericeum

Hieracium glanduliferum f. nigrosericeum, commonly known as the black-hairy hawkweed, is a variant of the common hawkweed species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features dark green, hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Its hairy stems and leaves give it a distinctive appearance, and it is often found in moist or calcareous habitats.
