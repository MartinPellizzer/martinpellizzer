Helleborus hercegovinus f. pauciflorus

Helleborus hercegovinus f. pauciflorus, commonly known as the Hercegovina hellebore, is a rare and unique cultivar of the hellebore genus.

It is characterized by its reduced flower count, with only a few blooms per inflorescence, making it distinct from other hellebore varieties. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves with a waxy texture, and its flowers are typically dark purple or maroon in color. It is native to the Balkan region and thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
