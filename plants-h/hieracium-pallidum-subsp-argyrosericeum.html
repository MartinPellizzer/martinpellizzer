Hieracium pallidum subsp. argyrosericeum

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

argyrosericeum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

The plant has slender, hairy stems and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a whitish or silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
