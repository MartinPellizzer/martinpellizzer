Helianthemum pilosum var. tomentellum

Helianthemum pilosum var. tomentellum, commonly known as the woolly rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, grayish-white hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in spring, which are clustered in compact inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
