Hieracium mucidum

Hieracium mucidum, commonly known as the stinking hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Hieracium mucidum is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
