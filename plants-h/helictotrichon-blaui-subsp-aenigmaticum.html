Helictotrichon blaui subsp. aenigmaticum

Helictotrichon blaui subsp.

aenigmaticum, commonly known as the blue false brome, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, bluish-green leaves.
