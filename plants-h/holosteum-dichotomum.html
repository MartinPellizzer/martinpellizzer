Holosteum dichotomum

Holosteum dichotomum, commonly known as the dichotomous holosteum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy surface. Its small, white flowers are clustered in dense, branched inflorescences, and it blooms during the cooler months.

The plant is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
