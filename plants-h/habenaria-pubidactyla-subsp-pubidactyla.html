Habenaria pubidactyla subsp. pubidactyla

Habenaria pubidactyla subsp.

pubidactyla, commonly known as the pubidactyl orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves.

The flowers are typically greenish-yellow with white and purple markings, arranged in a raceme. It prefers humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

This orchid is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
