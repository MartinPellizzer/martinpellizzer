Habenaria harmsiana

Habenaria harmsiana, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features slender, erect stems with elliptical or ovate leaves that are often narrowly lance-shaped. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale yellow, and are arranged in a loose raceme.

Habenaria harmsiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
