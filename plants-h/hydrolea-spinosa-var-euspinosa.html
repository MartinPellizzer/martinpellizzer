Hydrolea spinosa var. euspinosa

Hydrolea spinosa var. euspinosa, commonly known as the spiny hydrolea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
