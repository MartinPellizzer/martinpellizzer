Habenaria odontopetala

Habenaria odontopetala, commonly known as the toothed petal orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with distinctive tooth-like markings on the petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
