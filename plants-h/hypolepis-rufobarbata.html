Hypolepis rufobarbata

Hypolepis rufobarbata, commonly known as the red-barbed shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
