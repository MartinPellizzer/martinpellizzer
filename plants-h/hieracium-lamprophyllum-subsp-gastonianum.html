Hieracium lamprophyllum subsp. gastonianum

Hieracium lamprophyllum subsp.

gastonianum, commonly known as the silverleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm.

The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, with showy bracts.

It is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
