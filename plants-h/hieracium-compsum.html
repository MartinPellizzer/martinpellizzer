Hieracium compsum

Hieracium compsum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering head composed of yellow disk flowers surrounded by ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes and is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

Due to its invasive potential, it can outcompete native flora in certain environments.
