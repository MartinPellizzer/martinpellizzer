Hibiscus palmatifidus

Hibiscus palmatifidus, commonly known as the broadleaf hibiscus, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It features large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the warm seasons. Hibiscus palmatifidus thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical climates.
