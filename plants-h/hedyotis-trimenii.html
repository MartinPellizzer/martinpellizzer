Hedyotis trimenii

Hedyotis trimenii, commonly known as the mountain geranium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its small white or pink flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Hedyotis trimenii is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing benefits.
