Hieracium stenophyes

Hieracium stenophyes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

It is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive growth.
