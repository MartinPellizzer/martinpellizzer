Helichrysum fowerakeri

Helichrysum fowerakeri, commonly known as the Cape gold flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flowers that resemble miniature suns. The plant produces small, papery bracts that give it a distinctive appearance, often mistaken for actual petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum fowerakeri is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
