Haworthia maraisii var. schuldtiana

Haworthia maraisii var. schuldtiana, commonly known as the Schuldt's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or light green markings. The leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to store water and thrive in arid conditions. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring or summer.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
