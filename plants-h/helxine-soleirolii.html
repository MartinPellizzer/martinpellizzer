Helxine soleirolii

Helxine soleirolii, commonly known as the silver thread plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense clusters of small, needle-like leaves that give it a unique, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, drought-tolerant plant.
