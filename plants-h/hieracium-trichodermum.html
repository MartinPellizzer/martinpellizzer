Hieracium trichodermum

Hieracium trichodermum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are hairy and lance-shaped, while its flowers consist of yellow disk flowers surrounded by white to pale yellow ray florets.

Hieracium trichodermum is often found in open habitats and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
