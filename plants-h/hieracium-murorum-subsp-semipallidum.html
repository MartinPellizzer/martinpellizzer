Hieracium murorum subsp. semipallidum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

semipallidum, commonly known as the pale-centre hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow ray florets.

The leaves are typically lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while the stems are often reddish in color. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands, roadsides, and waste areas.

This subspecies is distinguished by its pale yellow center in the flower head, setting it apart from other hawkweed species.
