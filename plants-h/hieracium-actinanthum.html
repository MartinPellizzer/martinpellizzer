Hieracium actinanthum

Hieracium actinanthum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a single head atop a hairy stem. The plant has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

Hieracium actinanthum is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes, making it an invasive species in some regions.
