Hartogia hirtella

Hartogia hirtella, commonly known as the hairy hartogia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, yellowish flowers. Hartogia hirtella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
