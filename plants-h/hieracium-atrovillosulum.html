Hieracium atrovillosulum

Hieracium atrovillosulum, commonly known as the dark-haired hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright stems and basal rosette of dark green, hairy leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in late summer. It is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
