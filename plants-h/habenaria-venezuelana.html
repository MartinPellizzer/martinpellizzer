Habenaria venezuelana

Habenaria venezuelana, commonly known as the Venezuelan butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Venezuela.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its unique floral structure.
