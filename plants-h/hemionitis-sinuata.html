Hemionitis sinuata

Hemionitis sinuata, commonly known as the mountain holly or Chinese holly, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves with a serrated margin and a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique species within the Hemionitidaceae family.
