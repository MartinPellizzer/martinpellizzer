Helianthus stenophyllus

Helianthus stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sunflower, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features upright stems with narrow, linear leaves and produces vibrant yellow flower heads that bloom in the summer. Its botanical characteristics include showy composite flowers with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular florets.

Helianthus stenophyllus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
