Haworthia wimii

Haworthia wimii, commonly known as the "Wimii Haworthia," is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings, which help in photosynthesis and water retention. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
