Hetaeria goodyeroides

Hetaeria goodyeroides, commonly known as the Goodyera orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of greenish-white or pale yellow blooms. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
