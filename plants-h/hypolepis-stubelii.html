Hypolepis stubelii

Hypolepis stubelii, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a tropical fern native to Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Hypolepis stubelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in many tropical and subtropical regions.
