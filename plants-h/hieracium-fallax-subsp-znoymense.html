Hieracium fallax subsp. znoymense

Hieracium fallax subsp.

znoymense, commonly known as the Znoymenskaya hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has small, alternate leaves that are often covered with fine hairs.

This subspecies is noted for its ecological adaptability and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
