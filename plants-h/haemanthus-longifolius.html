Haemanthus longifolius

Haemanthus longifolius, commonly known as the Natal snowflake, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique flowering display and cold-hardiness.
