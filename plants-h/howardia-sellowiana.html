Howardia sellowiana

Howardia sellowiana, commonly known as the African violet or velvet plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
