Hieracium chaozum

Hieracium chaozum, commonly known as the Chinese hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Hieracium chaozum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some regions.
