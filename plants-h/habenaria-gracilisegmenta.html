Habenaria gracilisegmenta

Habenaria gracilisegmenta, commonly known as the slender-segmented orchid, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of several small, clustered flowers that bloom during the warmer months.

The orchid's rhizome is fibrous, and it reproduces through both seeds and vegetative propagation.
