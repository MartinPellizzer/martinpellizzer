Hieracium anomodontum

Hieracium anomodontum, commonly known as the anomalous hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, rough stems. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium anomodontum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
