Heracleum sphondylium subsp. orsinii

Heracleum sphondylium subsp.

orsinii, commonly known as the lesser cow parsley, is a member of the Apiaceae family. It is a biennial or short-lived perennial herb that typically grows in moist, calcareous soils.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in wet meadows, riverbanks, and other damp habitats.
