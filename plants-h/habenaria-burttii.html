Habenaria burttii

Habenaria burttii, commonly known as the Burtt's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous, and it produces a single spike of flowers during the spring or early summer.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
