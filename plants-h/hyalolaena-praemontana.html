Hyalolaena praemontana

Hyalolaena praemontana, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that are typically pale green or white. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in high-altitude environments. Hyalolaena praemontana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
