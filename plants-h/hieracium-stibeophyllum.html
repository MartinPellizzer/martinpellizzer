Hieracium stibeophyllum

Hieracium stibeophyllum, commonly known as the Stibe hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained, rocky soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and clustered in heads.

Hieracium stibeophyllum is adapted to cold climates and is often found in high-altitude environments.
