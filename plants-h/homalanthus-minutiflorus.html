Homalanthus minutiflorus

Homalanthus minutiflorus, commonly known as the dwarf flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
