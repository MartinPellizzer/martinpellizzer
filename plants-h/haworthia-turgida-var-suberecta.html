Haworthia turgida var. suberecta

Haworthia turgida var. suberecta, commonly known as the compact Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern and are somewhat upright, giving the plant a compact appearance. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the growing season.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
