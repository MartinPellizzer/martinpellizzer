Hieracium pseudotommasinii subsp. krstacense

Hieracium pseudotommasinii subsp.

krstacense, commonly known as the Krstacene hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native range.
