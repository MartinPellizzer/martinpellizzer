Hortensia serrata var. acuminata

Hortensia serrata var. acuminata, commonly known as the serrated hortensia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and clusters of showy, spherical flower heads that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant features finely serrated, ovate leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its blooms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

The flowers are often used in traditional medicine and are also appreciated for their ornamental value.
