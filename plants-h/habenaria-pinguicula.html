Habenaria pinguicula

Habenaria pinguicula, commonly known as the sticky orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. Its leaves are often covered in a sticky substance, which helps in trapping small insects.

This adaptation aids in nutrient acquisition, as the plant is capable of digesting the trapped prey.
