Habenaria bicornis

Habenaria bicornis, commonly known as the two-horned orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive two-lobed lip, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish in color, with a showy, spurred structure.

Habenaria bicornis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
