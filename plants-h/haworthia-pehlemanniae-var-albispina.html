Haworthia pehlemanniae var. albispina

Haworthia pehlemanniae var. albispina, commonly known as the white-spined Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale yellow markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
