Hieracium truncellum

Hieracium truncellum, commonly known as the blunt-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium truncellum is often considered a weed but can be an important food source for certain pollinators.
