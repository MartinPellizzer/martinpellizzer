Helenium flexuosum

Helenium flexuosum, commonly known as the softstemmed sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its showy, orange to red flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Helenium flexuosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
