Heloniopsis breviscapa var. flavida

Heloniopsis breviscapa var. flavida, commonly known as the yellow-flowered heloniopsis, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-gray hue.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in rock gardens and alpine settings.
