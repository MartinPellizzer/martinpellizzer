Heliotropium gillianum

Heliotropium gillianum, commonly known as the Gillian's heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, violet-blue flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.
