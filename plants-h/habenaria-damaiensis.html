Habenaria damaiensis

Habenaria damaiensis, commonly known as the Damaiens Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
