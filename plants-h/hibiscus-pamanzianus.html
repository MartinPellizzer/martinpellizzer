Hibiscus pamanzianus

Hibiscus pamanzianus, commonly known as the Natal hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. Hibiscus pamanzianus is often found in coastal areas and can grow up to several meters in height.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
