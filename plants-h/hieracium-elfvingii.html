Hieracium elfvingii

Hieracium elfvingii, commonly known as the elfving's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium elfvingii is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often found in areas with moderate to high moisture levels.
