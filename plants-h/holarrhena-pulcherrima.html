Holarrhena pulcherrima

Holarrhena pulcherrima, commonly known as the angel's trumpet or sacred lotus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky, resinous substance. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a waxy coating.

Holarrhena pulcherrima is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional medicinal uses in various cultures.
