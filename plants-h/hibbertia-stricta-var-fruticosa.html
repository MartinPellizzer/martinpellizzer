Hibbertia stricta var. fruticosa

Hibbertia stricta var. fruticosa, commonly known as the broad-leafed hibbertia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features woody stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with prominent, recurved petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Hibbertia stricta var. fruticosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
