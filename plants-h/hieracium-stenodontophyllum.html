Hieracium stenodontophyllum

Hieracium stenodontophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-toothed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and wastelands. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

It is a hardy species that can thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
