Hyphaene mangoides

Hyphaene mangoides, commonly known as the African palm or the mangosteen palm, is a tall, slender palm native to tropical Africa.

It features a slender trunk covered with persistent leaf bases, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in length. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has ecological importance in its native habitats.
