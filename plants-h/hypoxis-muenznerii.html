Hypoxis muenznerii

Hypoxis muenznerii, commonly known as the golden cup, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense cluster at the base. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on slender, upright stalks. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
