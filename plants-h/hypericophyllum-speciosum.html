Hypericophyllum speciosum

Hypericophyllum speciosum, commonly known as the showy hypericum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Hypericaceae.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers with prominent red stamens, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

Hypericophyllum speciosum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in temperate climates.
