Hosta lancifolia f. sieboldii

Hosta lancifolia f. sieboldii, commonly known as the Siebold's lancifolia hosta, is a cultivar of the hosta plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a striking blue-green color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and is a popular choice in shady garden settings.
