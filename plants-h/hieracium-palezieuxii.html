Hieracium palezieuxii

Hieracium palezieuxii, commonly known as the palezieux's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are basal and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

Hieracium palezieuxii is noted for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its potential use in traditional herbal medicine.
