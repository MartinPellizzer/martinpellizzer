Habenaria ensifolia var. gigantea

Habenaria ensifolia var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant sword orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, organic soil of its habitat.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
