Hieracium pteropogon subsp. pogonites

Hieracium pteropogon subsp.

pogonites, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in dry conditions.
