Hibiscus microchlaenus var. leptocladus

Hibiscus microchlaenus var. leptocladus, commonly known as the slender-leaved hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink.
