Helipterum floribundum var. tubulipappum

Helipterum floribundum var. tubulipappum, commonly known as the tubular-rayed helipterum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disk florets, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid regions.
