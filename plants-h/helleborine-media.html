Helleborine media

Helleborine media, commonly known as the common helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and its striking, spurred orchid-like flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in moist woodlands and shaded areas. Helleborine media has a rhizomatous root system and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
