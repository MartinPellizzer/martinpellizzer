Hieracium candidissimum

Hieracium candidissimum, commonly known as the white hawk's beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
