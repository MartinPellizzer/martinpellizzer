Hypoxis hygrometrica var. splendida

Hypoxis hygrometrica var. splendida, commonly known as the splendida oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of bright green, lance-shaped leaves with a prominent central vein. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are borne on slender stems above the foliage. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
