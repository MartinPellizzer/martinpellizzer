Hieracium toratum

Hieracium toratum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features upright stems with alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a clustered inflorescence, giving it its common name.

Hieracium toratum is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
