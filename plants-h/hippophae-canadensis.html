Hippophae canadensis

Hippophae canadensis, commonly known as the Canadian sea buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange berries, which are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant has small, oval leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sandy areas.

The shrub is valued for its ecological benefits and has been used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible fruit.
