Homalomena philippinensis

Homalomena philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine arrowroot, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a smooth texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is a common feature in the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
