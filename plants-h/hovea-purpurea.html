Hovea purpurea

Hovea purpurea, commonly known as the purple hovea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive purple flowers. The plant has compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Hovea purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
