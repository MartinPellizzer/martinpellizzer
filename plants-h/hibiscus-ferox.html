Hibiscus ferox

Hibiscus ferox, commonly known as the Cape rose or wild hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Hibiscus ferox is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
