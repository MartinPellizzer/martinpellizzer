Haworthia pilosa var. ciliata

Haworthia pilosa var. ciliata, commonly known as the ciliate Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The leaves are adorned with fine, hair-like structures along the edges, giving it its varietal name "ciliata.". It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
