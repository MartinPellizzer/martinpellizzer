Hieracium pernervosum

Hieracium pernervosum, commonly known as the net-veined hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and its bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has finely divided, serrated leaves and is often found in grassy areas and open habitats. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties in some traditional herbal practices.
