Helichrysum delhayei

Helichrysum delhayei, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, tubular florets surrounded by a ring of narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum delhayei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
