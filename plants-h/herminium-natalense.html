Herminium natalense

Herminium natalense, commonly known as Natal grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features erect, leafy stems that bear small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow and are arranged in dense clusters.

Herminium natalense is often found in grassland and coastal habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
