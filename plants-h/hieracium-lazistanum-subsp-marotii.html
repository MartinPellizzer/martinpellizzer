Hieracium lazistanum subsp. marotii

Hieracium lazistanum subsp.

marotii, commonly known as the Lazistan hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow ray florets, typical of the genus Hieracium.

The plant is native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus, particularly in Georgia and Turkey. It thrives in rocky and grassy habitats at moderate elevations.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
