Heurckia semperflorens

Heurckia semperflorens, commonly known as the ever-blooming geranium, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Heurckia semperflorens is prized for its long blooming season and low maintenance requirements.
