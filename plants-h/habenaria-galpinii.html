Habenaria galpinii

Habenaria galpinii, commonly known as the Galpin's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in open woodland and grassland habitats. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its flowering stems. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with three sepals and three petals, and a distinctive labellum.

It is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.
