Hieracium Hypochoeroides Subsp. Monomeres

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. monomeres is a lesser-known member of the daisy family known for its unique floral structure and ecological significance. This subspecies is characterized by its single, prominent flower head and narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to specific regions of Europe, it thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. Due to its limited distribution and specialized requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in many areas. Understanding and protecting this plant is essential for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
