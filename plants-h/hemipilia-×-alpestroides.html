Hemipilia × alpestroides

Hemipilia × alpestroides, commonly known as the alpine orchid, is a hybrid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in the Himalayas and adjacent areas. Hemipilia × alpestroides prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested habitats.

Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a slender stem topped with a single, showy flower.
