Haplopteris lineata

Haplopteris lineata, commonly known as the slender water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Haplopteraceae and is characterized by its finely divided, linear fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. Its reproductive structures include sori that are closely clustered on the underside of the fronds.

Haplopteris lineata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in aquatic and riparian habitats.
