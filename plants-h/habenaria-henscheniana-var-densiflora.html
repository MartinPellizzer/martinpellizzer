Habenaria henscheniana var. densiflora

Habenaria henscheniana var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and forest understories. The plant features slender, erect stems with a basal rosette of leaves, and its inflorescence bears numerous small, greenish-white flowers. It has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
