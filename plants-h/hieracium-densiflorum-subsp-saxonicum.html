Hieracium densiflorum subsp. saxonicum

Hieracium densiflorum subsp.

saxonicum, commonly known as the Saxony hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features a rosette of basal leaves that are typically covered in dense, glandular hairs.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
