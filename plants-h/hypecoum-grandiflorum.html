Hypecoum grandiflorum

Hypecoum grandiflorum, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a hairy texture.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
