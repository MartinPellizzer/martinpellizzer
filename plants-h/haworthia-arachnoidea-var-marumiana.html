Haworthia arachnoidea var. marumiana

Haworthia arachnoidea var. marumiana, commonly known as the spider haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish-green hue and white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, slender stems with clusters of white flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in dry conditions make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
