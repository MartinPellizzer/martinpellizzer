Hibiscus myriaster

Hibiscus myriaster, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. Hibiscus myriaster thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
