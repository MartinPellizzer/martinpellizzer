Hieracium stenosphyrum

Hieracium stenosphyrum, commonly known as narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium stenosphyrum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its resilience to environmental stress.
