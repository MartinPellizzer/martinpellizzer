Hyphaene semiplaena

Hyphaene semiplaena, commonly known as the African palm or the red palm, is a species of palm native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers followed by edible, sweet fruits that are often consumed by both humans and animals. Hyphaene semiplaena is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in challenging climates.
