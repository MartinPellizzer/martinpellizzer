Helipterum uniflorum

Helipterum uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flower helipterum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features bright yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, showy inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid habitat.

Helipterum uniflorum is often found in sandy or rocky soils in coastal regions.
