Hebeclinium hecatanthum

Hebeclinium hecatanthum, commonly known as the giant hebe, is a striking plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces large, showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Hebeclinium hecatanthum is well-suited for growing in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
