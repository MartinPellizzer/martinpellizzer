Habenaria perbella

Habenaria perbella, commonly known as the Elegant Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically blooms in late summer or early autumn, with flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
