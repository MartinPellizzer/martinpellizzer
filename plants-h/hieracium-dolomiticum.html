Hieracium dolomiticum

Hieracium dolomiticum, commonly known as the Dolomite Hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in calcareous soils, such as those found in limestone areas. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species.
