Habenaria petalodes var. micrantha

Habenaria petalodes var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and streamside areas. Its flowers are usually less than 1 cm in size, and the species is characterized by its compact growth habit.

The orchid has a complex pollination mechanism involving specific insect pollinators.
