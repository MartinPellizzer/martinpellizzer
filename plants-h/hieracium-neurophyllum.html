Hieracium neurophyllum

Hieracium neurophyllum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium neurophyllum is often found in temperate climates and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens.
