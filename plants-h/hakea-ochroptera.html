Hakea ochroptera

Hakea ochroptera, commonly known as the yellow hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features needle-like leaves that are typically dense and covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance and fire resistance.

The botanical characteristics of Hakea ochroptera make it an important component of local ecosystems and a popular choice for native landscaping.
