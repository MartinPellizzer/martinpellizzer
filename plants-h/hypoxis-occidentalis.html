Hypoxis occidentalis

Hypoxis occidentalis, commonly known as the purplestar lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and striking, star-shaped purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open, rocky habitats and thrives in well-drained soils. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

Hypoxis occidentalis is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
