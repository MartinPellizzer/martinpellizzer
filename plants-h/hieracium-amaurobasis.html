Hieracium amaurobasis

Hieracium amaurobasis, commonly known as the blindweed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it thrives in temperate climates. Its flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Hieracium amaurobasis is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in agricultural and natural settings.
