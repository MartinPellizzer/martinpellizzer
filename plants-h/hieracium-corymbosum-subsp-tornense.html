Hieracium corymbosum subsp. tornense

Hieracium corymbosum subsp.

tornense, commonly known as the Tornese hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, flowering heads that are typically yellow or reddish in color.

The plant has finely divided, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
