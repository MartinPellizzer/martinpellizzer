Hosta sieboldii f. atropurpurea

Hosta sieboldii f. atropurpurea, commonly known as the Purple-leafed Siebold's Hosta, is a striking variegated foliage plant native to Japan.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are predominantly dark purple with a contrasting light green margin. The plant produces tall, slender stems topped with bell-shaped, white to pale blue flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained, moist soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in shaded garden landscapes.
