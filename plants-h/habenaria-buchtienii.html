Habenaria buchtienii

Habenaria buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, showy flower that typically blooms in the summer months. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

The orchid's delicate pink or white petals are often adorned with intricate patterns, making it a visually striking species.
