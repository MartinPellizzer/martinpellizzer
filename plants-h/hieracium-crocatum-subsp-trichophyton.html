Hieracium crocatum subsp. trichophyton

Hieracium crocatum subsp.

trichophyton, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its subspecies name *trichophyton*.

The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Its botanical features make it identifiable by its pubescent foliage and distinctive floral structure.
