Hieracium tazense

Hieracium tazense, commonly known as the Tazene hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in coastal and mountainous regions. It produces yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium tazense is known for its low-growing habit and is often found in open, sun-exposed areas.
