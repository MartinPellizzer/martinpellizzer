Habenaria repens var. maxillaris

Habenaria repens var. maxillaris, commonly known as the creeping spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant has thin, rhizomatous stems that spread along the ground, forming dense mats. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and often have a subtle fragrance.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
