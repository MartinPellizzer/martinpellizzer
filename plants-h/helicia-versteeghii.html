Helicia versteeghii

Helicia versteeghii, commonly known as the Malabar hellimba, is a small evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in dense forests.
