Hieracium pilosum subsp. schleicheri

Hieracium pilosum subsp.

schleicheri, commonly known as the Schleicher's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
