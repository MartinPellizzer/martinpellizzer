Habenaria stenostachya subsp. buchneroides

Habenaria stenostachya subsp.

buchneroides, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed marsh orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in wet, boggy habitats and is found in parts of Southeast Asia. It has a rhizomatous root system that helps it survive in waterlogged soils.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ecologically important species in its native environment.
