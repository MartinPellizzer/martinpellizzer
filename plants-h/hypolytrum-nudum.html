Hypolytrum nudum

Hypolytrum nudum, commonly known as the slender false orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces linear, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
