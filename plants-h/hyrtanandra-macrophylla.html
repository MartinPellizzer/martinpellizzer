Hyrtanandra macrophylla

Hyrtanandra macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leafed hyrtanandra, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
