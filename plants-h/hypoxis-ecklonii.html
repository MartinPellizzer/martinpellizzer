Hypoxis ecklonii

Hypoxis ecklonii, commonly known as the yellow star lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a basal rosette of foliage.

Hypoxis ecklonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
