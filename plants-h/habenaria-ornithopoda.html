Habenaria ornithopoda

Habenaria ornithopoda, commonly known as the bird's-beak orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces a single, showy spike of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and swampy areas.

The species is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in lowland and montane regions.
