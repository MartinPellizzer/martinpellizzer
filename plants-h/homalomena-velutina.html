Homalomena velutina

Homalomena velutina, commonly known as the velvet arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.
