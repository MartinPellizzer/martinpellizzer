Hypoxis pallida

Hypoxis pallida, commonly known as the pale starflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually pale green to blue-gray in color. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with a star-shaped pattern, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Hypoxis pallida is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
