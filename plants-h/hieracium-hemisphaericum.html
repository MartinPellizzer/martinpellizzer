Hieracium hemisphaericum

Hieracium hemisphaericum, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky flower heads that resemble a globe, giving it its common name. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands. The leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are upright and often branched.

It produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into distinctive, prickly seed heads.
