Hypolytrum pandanophyllum

Hypolytrum pandanophyllum, commonly known as the pandan-leaf lily, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, which includes many diverse and economically important species. The plant is characterized by its strap-like, fragrant leaves that resemble pandan leaves, hence its common name. It produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
