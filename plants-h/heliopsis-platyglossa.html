Heliopsis platyglossa

Heliopsis platyglossa, commonly known as the flatleaf sunflower, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States, where it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with orange centers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are broad and slightly heart-shaped, with a smooth texture.

Heliopsis platyglossa is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
