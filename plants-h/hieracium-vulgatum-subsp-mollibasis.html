Hieracium vulgatum subsp. mollibasis

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

mollibasis, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single flower head with ray florets and a central disk.

The leaves are usually lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
