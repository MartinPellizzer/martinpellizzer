Hieracium melandryfolium

Hieracium melandryfolium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which help it retain moisture in its native habitats. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks in temperate regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium melandryfolium is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils, making it a common sight in various grassland ecosystems.
