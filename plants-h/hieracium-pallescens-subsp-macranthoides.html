Hieracium pallescens subsp. macranthoides

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

macranthoides, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant typically features yellow to orange disk flowers surrounded by white to pale yellow ray florets. Its leaves are usually lanceolate or ovate, with a rough, hairy texture.

It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadside areas.
