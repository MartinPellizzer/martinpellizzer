Habenaria leucostachys

Habenaria leucostachys, commonly known as the white-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and its clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, delicate blossoms that bloom in late summer.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
