Hieracium fallax var. stoloniferum

Hieracium fallax var. stoloniferum, commonly known as the creeping hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish composite flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are typically basal, with a rough, hairy texture, and the stems are often reddish in color.

It spreads primarily through stolons, forming dense colonies that can dominate grassy areas.
