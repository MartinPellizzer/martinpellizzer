Haworthia gracilis

Haworthia gracilis, commonly known as the narrow leafed Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, grass-like leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, slender flower stems with small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its slow growth and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
