Hieracium nigrescens subsp. finmarkicum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

finmarkicum, commonly known as the Finmark hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern regions of Europe and Siberia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-red composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, nutrient-rich soils in alpine or subalpine environments. Its leaves are usually basal, lanceolate, and covered with fine hairs.

It is a relatively small plant, typically reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
