Hieracium canescens subsp. lindebergii

Hieracium canescens subsp.

lindebergii, commonly known as the Lindeberg's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its hairy, grey-green stems and basal rosettes of lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
