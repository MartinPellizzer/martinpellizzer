Habenaria schweinfurthii

Habenaria schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, hairy lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists.
