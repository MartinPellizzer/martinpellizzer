Haworthia suberecta var. pallidifolia

Haworthia suberecta var. pallidifolia, commonly known as the pale leaf Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, pale green to bluish-gray leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
