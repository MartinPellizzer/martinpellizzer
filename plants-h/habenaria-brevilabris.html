Habenaria brevilabris

Habenaria brevilabris, commonly known as the short-lipped marsh orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes and a rosette of linear, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and white to pale green, often arranged in a loose raceme.

The species is characterized by its short, narrow labellum, which is a distinguishing feature in the genus Habenaria.
