Hieracium pannosum var. pantocekianum

Hieracium pannosum var. pantocekianum, commonly known as the Pantocek's hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.
