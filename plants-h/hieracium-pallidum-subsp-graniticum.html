Hieracium pallidum subsp. graniticum

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

graniticum, commonly known as the granitic hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to rocky and alpine environments. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to pale yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in well-drained soils, often on limestone or granite substrates. It has basal and sometimes stem leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped.

This subspecies is adapted to harsh, nutrient-poor conditions and is often found in mountainous regions across Europe.
