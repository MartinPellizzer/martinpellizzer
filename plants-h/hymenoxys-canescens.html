Hymenoxys canescens

Hymenoxys canescens, commonly known as the white sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Its small, white to pale yellow flowers bloom in spring, and the plant produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by wind.
