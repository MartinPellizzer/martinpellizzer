Hypoxis polystachya

Hypoxis polystachya, commonly known as the star grass or African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers in spring. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped blooms that resemble small suns. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Hypoxis polystachya is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
