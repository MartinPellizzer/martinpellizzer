Hermione italica var. brevis

Hermione italica var. brevis, commonly known as the short-stemmed Hermione, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces delicate, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
