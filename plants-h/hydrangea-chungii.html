Hydrangea chungii

Hydrangea chungii, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, blue, or purple depending on soil pH. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is typically dark green and turns red or orange in the fall.

Hydrangea chungii prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
