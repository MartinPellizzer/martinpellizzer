Hypericum japonicum f. yabei

Hypericum japonicum f. yabei, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its persistent red berries that appear in the fall.
