Habenaria norae

Habenaria norae, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale green to white flowers that are arranged in a loose raceme. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

Habenaria norae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
