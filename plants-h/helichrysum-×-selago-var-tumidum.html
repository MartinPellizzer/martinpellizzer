Helichrysum × selago var. tumidum

Helichrysum × selago var. tumidum, commonly known as the woolly sunburst, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
