Haemanthus nutans

Haemanthus nutans, commonly known as the nodding haemanthus, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, and its most distinctive characteristic is its showy, red-orange flowers that droop downward. The plant produces clusters of tubular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Haemanthus nutans is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in coastal climates.
