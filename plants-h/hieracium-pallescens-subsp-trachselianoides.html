Hieracium pallescens subsp. trachselianoides

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

trachselianoides, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its native range.
