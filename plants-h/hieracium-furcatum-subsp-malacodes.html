Hieracium furcatum subsp. malacodes

Hieracium furcatum subsp.

malacodes, commonly known as the Malacode hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in clusters.

The plant is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Malaysia and Indonesia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.
