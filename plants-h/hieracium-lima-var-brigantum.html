Hieracium lima var. brigantum

Hieracium lima var. brigantum, commonly known as the Brabant Hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single head at the end of each stem. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in dry conditions.

Its flowers are typically visited by various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
