Hieracium insulicola

Hieracium insulicola, commonly known as the island hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Azores and other Mediterranean islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Due to its restricted distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
