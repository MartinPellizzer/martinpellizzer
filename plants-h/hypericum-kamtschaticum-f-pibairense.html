Hypericum kamtschaticum f. pibairense

Hypericum kamtschaticum f. pibairense, commonly known as the Pibairen Hypericum, is a hybrid form of the genus Hypericum native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is appreciated by gardeners interested in native and hybrid shrubs.
