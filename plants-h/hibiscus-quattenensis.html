Hibiscus quattenensis

Hibiscus quattenensis, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and grows as an upright shrub or small tree. Hibiscus quattenensis thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
