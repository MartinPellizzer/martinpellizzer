Hypericum hilaireanum

Hypericum hilaireanum, commonly known as the Hilairean St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky soils and dry habitats, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum hilaireanum is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
