Hieracium nigrescens subsp. euglossum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

euglossum, commonly known as the black-centred hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its dark purple to black central disk and yellowish to brownish ray florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has lance-shaped leaves with rough textures and produces small, clustered flowers.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in temperate regions.
