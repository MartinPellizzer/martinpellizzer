Hordeum nigrum

Hordeum nigrum, commonly known as black barley, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is a herbaceous annual plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with dark purple to black glumes, which give it its common name. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

The seeds are enclosed in spikelets, and the plant is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
