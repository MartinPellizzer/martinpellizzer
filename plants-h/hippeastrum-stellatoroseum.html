Hippeastrum stellatoroseum

Hippeastrum stellatoroseum, commonly known as the starry amaryllis, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble stars, with petals that are often a mix of white and pink. The plant produces large, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens and indoor spaces.
