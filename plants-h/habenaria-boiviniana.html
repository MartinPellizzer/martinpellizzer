Habenaria boiviniana

Habenaria boiviniana, commonly known as the Boivin's orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on rocky substrates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with intricate patterns.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
