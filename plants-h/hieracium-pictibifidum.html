Hieracium pictibifidum

Hieracium pictibifidum, commonly known as the painted devil's bit, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers with distinctive black markings, which give it its common name. Hieracium pictibifidum is native to Europe and is often found in damp meadows and along riverbanks.
