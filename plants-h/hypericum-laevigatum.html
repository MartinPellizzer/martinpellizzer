Hypericum laevigatum

Hypericum laevigatum, commonly known as smooth St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features woody stems with yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically smooth to the touch. It is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Hypericum laevigatum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
