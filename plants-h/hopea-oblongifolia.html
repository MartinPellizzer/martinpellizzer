Hopea oblongifolia

Hopea oblongifolia, commonly known as the oblong-leaf hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody, oval-shaped fruits.

Hopea oblongifolia is often found in lowland tropical forests and is valued for its durable timber.
