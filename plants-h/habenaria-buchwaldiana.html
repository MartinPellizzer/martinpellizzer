Habenaria buchwaldiana

Habenaria buchwaldiana, commonly known as the Buchwald's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and marshy areas. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white with intricate patterns, and it blooms during the spring and summer seasons.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
