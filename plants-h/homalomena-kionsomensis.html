Homalomena kionsomensis

Homalomena kionsomensis, commonly known as the Kionsomensis arrowhead plant, is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves.

Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. It produces striking, spatulate-shaped flowers that are typically green or white with a prominent spadix. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its lush foliage and unique floral structure.

It prefers well-draining soil and consistent moisture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
