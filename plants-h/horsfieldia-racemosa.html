Horsfieldia racemosa

Horsfieldia racemosa, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Horsfieldia racemosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
