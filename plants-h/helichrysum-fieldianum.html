Helichrysum fieldianum

Helichrysum fieldianum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain intact for a long time after blooming. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.

Helichrysum fieldianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
