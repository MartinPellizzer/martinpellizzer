Hyperbaena rotundiuscula

Hyperbaena rotundiuscula, commonly known as the round-leaved hyperbaena, is a small shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, evergreen foliage with rounded, leathery leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
