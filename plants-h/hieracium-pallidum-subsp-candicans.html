Hieracium pallidum subsp. candicans

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

candicans, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.

The subspecies candicans is distinguished by its more erect growth habit and slightly larger flowers compared to other subspecies of Hieracium pallidum.
