Helichrysum congolanum

Helichrysum congolanum, commonly known as the golden marguerite or African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces small, bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Helichrysum congolanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
