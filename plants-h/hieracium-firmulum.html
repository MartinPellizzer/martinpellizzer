Hieracium firmulum

Hieracium firmulum, commonly known as the stiff hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. Native to Europe, it thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its robust and resilient nature.
