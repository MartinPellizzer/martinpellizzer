Habenaria stenorhynchus

Habenaria stenorhynchus, commonly known as the narrow-lobed marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces tall, erect stems with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that is often darker in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
