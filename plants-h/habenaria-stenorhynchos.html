Habenaria stenorhynchos

Habenaria stenorhynchos, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and delicate, featuring a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
