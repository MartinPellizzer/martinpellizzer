Heterotrichum patens

Heterotrichum patens, commonly known as the common feather moss, is a species of moss that belongs to the family Hypnaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, carpet-like growth and slender, branching stems covered in small, ovate leaves. The leaves are typically green and have a smooth, waxy surface, which helps in reducing water loss. This moss thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky outcrops.

It plays an important ecological role in soil stabilization and nutrient cycling in its habitat.
