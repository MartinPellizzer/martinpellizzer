Habenaria schultzei

Habenaria schultzei, commonly known as the Schultze's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with intricate patterns, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
