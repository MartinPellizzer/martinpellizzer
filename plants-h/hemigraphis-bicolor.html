Hemigraphis bicolor

Hemigraphis bicolor, commonly known as the shining iron plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, two-toned leaves that display a mix of green and red or purple hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant foliage.

Hemigraphis bicolor is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
