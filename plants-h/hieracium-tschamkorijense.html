Hieracium tschamkorijense

Hieracium tschamkorijense, commonly known as the Tschamkori hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and rocky slopes, preferring well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native habitat.
