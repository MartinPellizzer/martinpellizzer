Hieracium akhverdovii

Hieracium akhverdovii, commonly known as the Akhverdov's hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the Caucasus region, specifically found in Georgia and Azerbaijan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
