Hamulium alatum

Hamulium alatum, commonly known as the winged bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has distinctive winged stems and leaves with a rough, hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Hamulium alatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
