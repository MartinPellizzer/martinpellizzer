Huonia laevis

Huonia laevis, commonly known as the smooth huonia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of China, Kazakhstan, and Russia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in rocky or forested areas at high elevations.

Huonia laevis is not widely cultivated but is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
