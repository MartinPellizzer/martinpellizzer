Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. uazzanum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

uazzanum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing at high altitudes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
