Heterothalamus densus

Heterothalamus densus, commonly known as the dense heterothallic plant, is a unique species within the fungal kingdom.

It exhibits heterothallism, a reproductive strategy where mating occurs between individuals of opposite mating types. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forest floors. Its dense growth habit contributes to its common name, and it plays a role in nutrient cycling within its ecosystem.

The species is notable for its complex genetic and ecological interactions with other organisms.
