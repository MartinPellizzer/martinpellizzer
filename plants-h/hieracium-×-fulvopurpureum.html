Hieracium × fulvopurpureum

Hieracium × fulvopurpureum, commonly known as the purple-leaf hawkweed, is a hybrid plant species within the genus Hieracium, which belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and green foliage, which distinguishes it from other hawkweed species. The plant typically produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations, making it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
