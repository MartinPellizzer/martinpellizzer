Hypericum loureiroi

Hypericum loureiroi, commonly known as the yellow St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Hypericaceae family.
