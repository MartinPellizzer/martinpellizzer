Hieracium emaceraticeps

Hieracium emaceraticeps, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and yellowish-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem.

Hieracium emaceraticeps is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is native to parts of Europe and Asia.
