Hieracium pseudobifidum subsp. strictifidum

Hieracium pseudobifidum subsp.

strictifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching a height of up to 60 centimeters.

The plant features bright yellow composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, with showy ray florets surrounding disk flowers. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a distinct, deep lobing at the base.

The species is characterized by its robust growth and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
