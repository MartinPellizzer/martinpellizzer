Hyparrhenia micrathera

Hyparrhenia micrathera, commonly known as the African grass or red grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces dense, fluffy inflorescences that are often bright red or orange in color. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of climates. It is widely used in landscaping and as a ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil stabilization and can be found in grasslands and savanna ecosystems.
