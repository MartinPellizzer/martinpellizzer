Habenaria nicholsonii var. debeerstiana

Habenaria nicholsonii var. debeerstiana, commonly known as the De Beer's lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and its distinctive floral arrangement of pinkish-white, fragrant flowers that resemble a tresses of hair. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. It produces small, oval-shaped pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its native habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
