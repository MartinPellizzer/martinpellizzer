Homalomena sengkenyang

Homalomena sengkenyang, commonly known as the Sengkenyang arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a glossy texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in dense forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and water garden settings.
