Habenaria maculosa

Habenaria maculosa, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it produces small, colorful blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
