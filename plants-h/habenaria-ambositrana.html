Habenaria ambositrana

Habenaria ambositrana, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to greenish-white blooms with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the unique ecological conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
