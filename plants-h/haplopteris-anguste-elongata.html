Haplopteris anguste-elongata

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically less than 10 cm in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spores are produced in small, clustered sori on the underside of the fronds.
