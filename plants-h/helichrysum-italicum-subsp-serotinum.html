Helichrysum italicum subsp. serotinum

Helichrysum italicum subsp.

serotinum, commonly known as the late helichrysum, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features erect, branched stems covered in dense, silvery-gray foliage.

The plant produces small, yellowish-orange flowers that are often used in dried floral arrangements due to their long-lasting color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its aromatic leaves and flowers contain essential oils that are valued in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
