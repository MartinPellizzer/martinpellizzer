Hieracium cymosum subsp. confluentinum

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

confluentinum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads.

The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs.
