Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. porrigentiforme

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

porrigentiforme, commonly known as the transparent hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typical sunflower-like appearance.

The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the genus Hieracium.

It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
