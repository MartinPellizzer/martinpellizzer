Hieracium fimreitense

Hieracium fimreitense, commonly known as the Fimreit hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its native range.
