Habenaria correlliana

Habenaria correlliana, commonly known as the Correll's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and pine flatwoods. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its native range.
