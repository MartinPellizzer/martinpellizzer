Hieracium convenicola

Hieracium convenicola, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture, and its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can spread aggressively in certain environments.
