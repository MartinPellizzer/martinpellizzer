Humularia renieri

Humularia renieri, commonly known as the Malabar starling tree, is a tropical plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, which is dispersed by animals.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
