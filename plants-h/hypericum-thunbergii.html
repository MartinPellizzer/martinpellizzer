Hypericum thunbergii

Hypericum thunbergii, commonly known as yellow St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers with prominent orange stamens, blooming from June to August. The plant has opposite, narrow, dark green leaves and a woody stem covered in short, stiff hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Hypericum thunbergii is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in herbal medicine.
