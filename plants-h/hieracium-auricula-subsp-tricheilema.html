Hieracium auricula subsp. tricheilema

Hieracium auricula subsp.

tricheilema, commonly known as the golden hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are hairy and have a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow and typically appear in late spring to early summer.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.
