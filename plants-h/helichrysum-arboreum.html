Helichrysum arboreum

Helichrysum arboreum, commonly known as the golden bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting appearance. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with grey-green, aromatic leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
