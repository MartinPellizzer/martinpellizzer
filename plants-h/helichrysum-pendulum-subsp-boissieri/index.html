Helichrysum Pendulum Subsp. Boissieri

Golden everlasting, scientifically known as Helichrysum pendulum subsp. boissieri, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. Its unique appearance, with bright yellow flowers that retain their color even when dried, makes it a popular choice for floral arrangements and herbal remedies. This resilient species thrives in harsh conditions, showcasing its adaptability to drought and poor soil. The plant's name reflects its enduring beauty, which remains unchanged over time. Its cultural and medicinal significance adds to its appeal among botanists and gardeners alike.
