Heinsia mozambicensis

Heinsia mozambicensis, commonly known as the Mozambique heinsia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Heinsia mozambicensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
