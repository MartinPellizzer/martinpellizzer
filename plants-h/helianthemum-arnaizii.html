Helianthemum arnaizii

Helianthemum arnaizii, commonly known as the Balearic rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, evergreen leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in rocky and coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
