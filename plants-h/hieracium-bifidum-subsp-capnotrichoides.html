Hieracium bifidum subsp. capnotrichoides

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

capnotrichoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It features a rosette of basal leaves that are narrow and often have a rough texture.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Its botanical characteristics make it identifiable by its distinct leaf morphology and flowering structure.
