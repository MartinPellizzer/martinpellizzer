Heuffelia pubescens var. subracemosa

Heuffelia pubescens var. subracemosa, commonly known as the hairy heuffelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
