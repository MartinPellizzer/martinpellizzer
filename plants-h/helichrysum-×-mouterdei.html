Helichrysum × mouterdei

Helichrysum × mouterdei, commonly known as the everlasting daisy, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant is known for its aromatic foliage, which releases a strong, spicy scent when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers are long-lasting when dried, contributing to its common name and its use in floral arrangements.
