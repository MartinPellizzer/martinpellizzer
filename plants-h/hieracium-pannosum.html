Hieracium pannosum

Hieracium pannosum, commonly known as the fuzzy hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features rough, hairy stems and basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a flat-topped cluster, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium pannosum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun.
