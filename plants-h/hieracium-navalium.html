Hieracium navalium

Hieracium navalium, commonly known as the sea hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy, lobed leaves that help it withstand salty air and strong winds. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the shorelines. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts.

Hieracium navalium is often found in maritime environments, thriving in the harsh conditions of coastal habitats.
