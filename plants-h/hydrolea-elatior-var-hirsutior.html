Hydrolea elatior var. hirsutior

Hydrolea elatior var. hirsutior, commonly known as the hairy hydrolea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features elongated, fleshy leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale green or white flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Its botanical structure makes it well adapted to retaining moisture and surviving in low-light conditions.
