Hermas

Hermas, commonly known as the common milkweed, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fluffy flowers that range in color from white to pink. Hermas is known for its milky sap, which contains toxic compounds that deter herbivores.

It plays an important role in supporting the life cycle of the monarch butterfly.
