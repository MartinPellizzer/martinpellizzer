Habenaria ligulata

Habenaria ligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in a raceme, with white to pale yellow petals and sepals.

The species is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
