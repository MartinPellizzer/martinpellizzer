Hepetis Uaupensis

Hepetis uaupensis is a rare flowering plant native to the rainforests of Brazil. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its delicate, colorful blooms. This species is often found in shaded, moist environments within the understory of dense vegetation. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its continued existence in the wild.
