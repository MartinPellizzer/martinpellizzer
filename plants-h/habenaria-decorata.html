Habenaria decorata

Habenaria decorata, commonly known as the Decorated Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The species is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
