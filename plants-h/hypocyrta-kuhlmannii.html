Hypocyrta kuhlmannii

Hypocyrta kuhlmannii, commonly known as the Kuhlmann's rock cress, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

The flowers are small, yellowish-white, and bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
