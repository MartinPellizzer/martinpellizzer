Hieracium phaeopsis

Hieracium phaeopsis, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Hieracium phaeopsis is often found in open habitats and can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies.
