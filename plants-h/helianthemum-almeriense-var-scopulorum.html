Helianthemum almeriense var. scopulorum

Helianthemum almeriense var. scopulorum, commonly known as the Almerian rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Spain.

It features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are adapted to arid environments, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it withstands harsh conditions.

Its compact form and vibrant flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
