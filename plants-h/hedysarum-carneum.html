Hedysarum carneum

Hedysarum carneum, commonly known as the red vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of pink to reddish-purple flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Hedysarum carneum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
