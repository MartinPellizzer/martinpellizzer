Hieracium percomans

Hieracium percomans, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium percomans is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
