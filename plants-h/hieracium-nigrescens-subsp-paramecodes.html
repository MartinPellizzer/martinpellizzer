Hieracium nigrescens subsp. paramecodes

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

paramecodes, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of dark green, hairy leaves.

The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It prefers moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its subspecies status highlights its distinct morphological features compared to other Hieracium species.
