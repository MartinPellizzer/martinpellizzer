Habenaria rhynchocarpa

Habenaria rhynchocarpa, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often in mountainous regions. It has a pseudobulbous structure, which helps it store water and nutrients.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
