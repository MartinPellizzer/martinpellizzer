Huernia leachii

Huernia leachii, commonly known as the horned ice plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically spiny and adapted to reduce water loss.

Huernia leachii is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements in cultivation.
