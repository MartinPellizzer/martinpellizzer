Hieracium luzuleti

Hieracium luzuleti, commonly known as the Luzuleti hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.

Hieracium luzuleti is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments, preferring cooler climates.
