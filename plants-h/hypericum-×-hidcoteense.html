Hypericum × hidcoteense

Hypericum × hidcoteense, commonly known as the Hidcote Hypericum, is a hybrid shrub native to the United Kingdom.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in midsummer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has upright, woody stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is typically found in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
