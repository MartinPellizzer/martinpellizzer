Hemizonia congesta subsp. typica

Hemizonia congesta subsp.

typica, commonly known as the clustered hemizonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
