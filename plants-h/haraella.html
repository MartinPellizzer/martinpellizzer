Haraella

Haraella, commonly known as the haraella plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, yellow, or orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Haraella is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
