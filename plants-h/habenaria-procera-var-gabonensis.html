Habenaria procera var. gabonensis

Habenaria procera var. gabonensis, commonly known as the Gabon tall orchid, is a species native to Central and Southern Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, showy, white or pale pink flower. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, well-drained conditions.
