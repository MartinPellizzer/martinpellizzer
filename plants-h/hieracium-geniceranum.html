Hieracium geniceranum

Hieracium geniceranum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It has a rosette of basal leaves and can produce numerous flowering stems.

Hieracium geniceranum is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its aggressive growth habit.
