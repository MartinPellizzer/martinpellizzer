Haworthiopsis reinwardtii var. tenuis

Haworthiopsis reinwardtii var. tenuis, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically a mix of green and blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
