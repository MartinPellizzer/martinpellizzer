Hemisphaerocarya suffruticosa var. multicaulis

Hemisphaerocarya suffruticosa var. multicaulis, commonly known as the clustered hemisphaerocarya, is a shrub native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, multi-stemmed growth habit with dense foliage. The leaves are typically small, ovate, and have a glossy surface. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
