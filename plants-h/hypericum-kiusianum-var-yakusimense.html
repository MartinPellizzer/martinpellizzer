Hypericum kiusianum var. yakusimense

Hypericum kiusianum var. yakusimense, commonly known as the Yakusimense St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers with prominent orange stamens. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, soft hairs. This variety is often found in rocky or well-drained soils at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 meters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
