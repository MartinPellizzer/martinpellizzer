Hosta atropurpurea f. albiflora

Hosta atropurpurea f. albiflora, commonly known as the White-flowered Purpleleaf Hosta, is a variegated cultivar of the Hosta plant.

It features striking purple foliage with a white variegation pattern that enhances its ornamental appeal. The plant produces large, showy white flowers on tall, sturdy stems during the summer months. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This cultivar is prized for its unique color contrast and is a popular choice in garden landscapes.
