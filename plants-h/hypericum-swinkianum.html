Hypericum swinkianum

Hypericum swinkianum, commonly known as Swink's St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 inches. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are oval-shaped and slightly hairy, with a reddish hue when young.

Hypericum swinkianum thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or woodland edges.
