Hoya scortechinii

Hoya scortechinii, commonly known as the "Coral Hoya," is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, coral-pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant produces thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped, and it thrives in bright, indirect light. Hoya scortechinii is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and moderate watering.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and resilience.
