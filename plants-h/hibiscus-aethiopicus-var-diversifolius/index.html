Hibiscus Aethiopicus Var. Diversifolius

Variegated hibiscus, scientifically known as Hibiscus aethiopicus var. diversifolius, is a strikingly beautiful ornamental plant prized for its vibrant foliage and showy flowers. This tropical species is native to parts of Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates with plenty of sunlight. Its leaves display a unique mix of green, white, and variegated patterns that add visual interest to any garden or indoor space. The plant produces large, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, blooming throughout the growing season. With proper care, it can be a rewarding addition to both outdoor gardens and container plantings.
