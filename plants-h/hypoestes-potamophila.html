Hypoestes potamophila

Hypoestes potamophila, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes potamophila is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
