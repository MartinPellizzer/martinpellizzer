Hieracium pseudodiaphanum

Hieracium pseudodiaphanum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairless leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe, this species is often found in meadows and open woodlands. Its translucent, waxy leaves give it a unique appearance, and it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.

However, it can be invasive in certain regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
