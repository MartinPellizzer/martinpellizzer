Hoffmannia pustulata

Hoffmannia pustulata, commonly known as the spiny pustulate cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in spines and small, tuberculate structures. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soil.

Hoffmannia pustulata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
