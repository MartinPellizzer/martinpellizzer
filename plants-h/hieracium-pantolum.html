Hieracium pantolum

Hieracium pantolum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as meadows, wetlands, and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium pantolum is known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it an aggressive weed in some regions.
