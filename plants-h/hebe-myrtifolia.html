Hebe myrtifolia

Hebe myrtifolia, commonly known as the silver hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet. The plant typically grows in a shrub form, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Hebe myrtifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
