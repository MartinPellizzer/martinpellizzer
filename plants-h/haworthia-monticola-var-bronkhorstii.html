Haworthia monticola var. bronkhorstii

Haworthia monticola var. bronkhorstii, commonly known as the Bronkhorst's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or silver markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
