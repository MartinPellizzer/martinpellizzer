Hosackia oaxacana

Hosackia oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It plays a role in nitrogen fixation, contributing to soil fertility in its native environment.
