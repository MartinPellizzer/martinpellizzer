Hibiscus oculiroseus

Hibiscus oculiroseus, commonly known as the eye-spotted hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, large, and brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Hibiscus oculiroseus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
