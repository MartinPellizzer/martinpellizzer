Haworthia limpida

Haworthia limpida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically a pale green or white color with distinct markings. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Haworthia limpida is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and unique appearance.
