Habenaria marxiana

Habenaria marxiana, commonly known as the Marx's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with several oval, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with three petals and three sepals, typically in shades of white and green. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
