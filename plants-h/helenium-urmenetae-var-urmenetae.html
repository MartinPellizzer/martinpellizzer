Helenium urmenetae var. urmenetae

Helenium urmenetae var. urmenetae, commonly known as the Urmenetae helenium, is a perennial plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy inflorescences. The plant features upright, hollow stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture. Its flowers are typically orange to yellow in hue, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
