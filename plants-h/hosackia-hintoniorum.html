Hosackia hintoniorum

Hosackia hintoniorum, commonly known as Hinton's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, such as sagebrush steppe and grasslands. It has small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
