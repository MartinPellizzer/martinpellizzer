Herminium wangianum

Herminium wangianum, commonly known as the Wangian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with purple markings, and they bloom during the warmer months.

Herminium wangianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
