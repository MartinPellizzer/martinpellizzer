Helianthemum clausonis var. safiense

Helianthemum clausonis var. safiense, commonly known as the Safi rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the rocky coastal regions of North Africa.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact form and long-lasting blooms.
