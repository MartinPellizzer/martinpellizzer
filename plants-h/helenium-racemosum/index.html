Helenium Racemosum

Helenium, scientifically known as Helenium racemosum, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. This perennial plant is celebrated for its vibrant, elongated flower heads that range from yellow to orange and red, adding a burst of color to gardens and natural landscapes. Helenium racemosum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a resilient choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. Its unique appearance and hardy nature have made it a popular subject in botanical studies and a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts. With its historical significance and ecological benefits, Helenium racemosum continues to captivate plant lovers worldwide.
