Haworthia lockwoodii

Haworthia lockwoodii, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
