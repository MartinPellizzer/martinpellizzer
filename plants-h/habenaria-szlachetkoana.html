Habenaria szlachetkoana

Habenaria szlachetkoana, commonly known as the Polish orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Eastern Europe and Central Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soils.

It is a perennial species that blooms in late spring to early summer.
