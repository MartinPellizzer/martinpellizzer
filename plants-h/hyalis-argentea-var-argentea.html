Hyalis argentea var. argentea

Hyalis argentea var. argentea, commonly known as silverleaf hyalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems covered in silvery-gray hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a waxy coating that reflects light, contributing to its silvery sheen. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months.

This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
