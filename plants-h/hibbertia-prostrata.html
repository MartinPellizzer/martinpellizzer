Hibbertia prostrata

Hibbertia prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate hibbertia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its trailing stems and bright yellow flowers. The plant features small, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive reddish stem. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Hibbertia prostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
