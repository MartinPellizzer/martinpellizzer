Hieracium subulosifrons

Hieracium subulosifrons, commonly known as the bristly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Its flowers are typically yellow and bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

Hieracium subulosifrons is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, showcasing its adaptability to different environments.
