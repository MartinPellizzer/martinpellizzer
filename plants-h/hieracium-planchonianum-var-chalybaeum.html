Hieracium planchonianum var. chalybaeum

Hieracium planchonianum var. chalybaeum, commonly known as the Chalybaeum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has a taproot system and produces achenes as its seeds.

This variety is native to Europe and is often found in temperate climates.
