Habenaria peyentsinensis

Habenaria peyentsinensis, commonly known as the Peyentsinensis Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, spurred flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
