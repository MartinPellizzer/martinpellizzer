Hypericum gramineum

Hypericum gramineum, commonly known as St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant in the family Hypericaceae.

It is native to Europe, western Asia, and northern Africa, and it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with prominent stamens, which bloom from June to August. Its leaves are opposite, oval, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum gramineum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported antidepressant properties.
