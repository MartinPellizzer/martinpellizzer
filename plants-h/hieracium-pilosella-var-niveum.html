Hieracium pilosella var. niveum

Hieracium pilosella var. niveum, commonly known as the white hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are hairy and lobed, while the flowers are white with yellow centers, giving it a striking appearance.

It is often found in open habitats and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens.
