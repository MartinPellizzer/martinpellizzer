Hypericum lorentzianum

Hypericum lorentzianum, commonly known as the Lorentzian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has narrow, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for gardens.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to native plant collections and ecological landscapes.
