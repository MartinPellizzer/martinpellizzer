Hibiscus benadirensis

Hibiscus benadirensis, commonly known as the Benadirensis hibiscus, is a species native to specific regions in Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Hibiscus benadirensis is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its traditional uses in local cultures.
