Hieracium pseudodenticuliferum

Hieracium pseudodenticuliferum, commonly known as the false dentedleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges, and the plant can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.
