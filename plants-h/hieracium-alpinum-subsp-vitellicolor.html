Hieracium alpinum subsp. vitellicolor

Hieracium alpinum subsp.

vitellicolor, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-red flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rough, hairy texture, and the stems are often reddish in color.

It is a hardy species adapted to cold climates and poor soil conditions.
