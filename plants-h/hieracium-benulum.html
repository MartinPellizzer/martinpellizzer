Hieracium benulum

Hieracium benulum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom from June to August, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it can grow up to 60 cm tall.

Hieracium benulum is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
