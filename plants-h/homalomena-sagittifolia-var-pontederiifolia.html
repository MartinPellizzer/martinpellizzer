Homalomena sagittifolia var. pontederiifolia

Homalomena sagittifolia var. pontederiifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf caladium, is a tropical plant characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves.

The foliage is typically deep green with vibrant red or purple veins, creating a dramatic visual effect. It is a member of the Araceae family, which is known for its unique inflorescence structure called a spathe. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its ornate leaf patterns and ability to add color to shaded garden spaces.
