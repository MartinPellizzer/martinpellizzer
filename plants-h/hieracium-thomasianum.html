Hieracium thomasianum

Hieracium thomasianum, commonly known as the Thomasian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It produces yellow ray florets surrounded by a cluster of tubular disc florets.

Hieracium thomasianum is often found in coastal and subalpine regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
