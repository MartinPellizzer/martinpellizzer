Hieracium criniferum

Hieracium criniferum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It often grows in meadows and disturbed soils, and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Hieracium criniferum is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
