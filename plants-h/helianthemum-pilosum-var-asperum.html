Helianthemum pilosum var. asperum

Helianthemum pilosum var. asperum, commonly known as the rough rockrose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. Its foliage is covered in stiff, bristly hairs, giving it a distinctive, rugged appearance.

It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or Mediterranean climates.
