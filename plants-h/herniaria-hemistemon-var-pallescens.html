Herniaria hemistemon var. pallescens

Herniaria hemistemon var. pallescens, commonly known as the pale sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments. The plant produces tiny, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its succulent stems and leaves help it withstand drought conditions, making it a hardy species.

It is often found in sandy soils and rocky habitats, contributing to the stabilization of such environments.
