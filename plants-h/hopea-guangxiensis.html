Hopea guangxiensis

Hopea guangxiensis, commonly known as Guangxi hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to southern China, particularly Guangxi, and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and smooth, greyish bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a prominent midrib.

Hopea guangxiensis is valued for its durable wood and is often found in lowland evergreen forests.
