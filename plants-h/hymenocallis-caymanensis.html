Hymenocallis caymanensis

Hymenocallis caymanensis, commonly known as the Cayman Lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Cayman Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Hymenocallis caymanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
