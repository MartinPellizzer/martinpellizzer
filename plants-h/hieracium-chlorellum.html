Hieracium chlorellum

Hieracium chlorellum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are alternate, rough to the touch, and have a distinctive yellowish-green hue.

The flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, producing small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
