Hoitzia coccinea

Hoitzia coccinea, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red, velvety flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Hoitzia coccinea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
