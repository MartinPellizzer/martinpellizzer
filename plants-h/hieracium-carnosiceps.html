Hieracium carnosiceps

Hieracium carnosiceps, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant features dense, woolly hairs on its stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
