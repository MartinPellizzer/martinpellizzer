Helianthemum variabile var. linearifolium

Helianthemum variabile var. linearifolium, commonly known as the linear-leaved rockrose, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub that typically thrives in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are narrow and linear in shape. It is well adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in Mediterranean climates.
