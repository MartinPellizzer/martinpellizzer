Hypolytrum laevigatum

Hypolytrum laevigatum, commonly known as the smooth hypolytrum, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. Hypolytrum laevigatum is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities in wetland gardens.
