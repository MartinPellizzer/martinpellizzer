Haworthia cangoensis

Haworthia cangoensis, commonly known as the Cango mountain Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the summer months.

Haworthia cangoensis is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
