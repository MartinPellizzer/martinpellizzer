Hemizonia corymbosa var. angustifolia

Hemizonia corymbosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hemizonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescences and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks or in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a subtle fragrance.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
