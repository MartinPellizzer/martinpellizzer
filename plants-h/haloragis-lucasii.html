Haloragis lucasii

Haloragis lucasii, commonly known as the Lucas's willowherb, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in damp, shaded environments such as streambanks and wetlands. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pinkish-white flowers clustered in dense spikes. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to tolerate waterlogged soils.
