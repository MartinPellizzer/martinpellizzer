Haworthia venosa subsp. recurva

Haworthia venosa subsp.

recurva, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa. It features distinctive, fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically green with white or pale green markings resembling a zebra's stripes.

The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
