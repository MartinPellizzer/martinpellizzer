Hosta Sieboldii F. Alba

Hosta sieboldii f. alba is a striking variegated plant known for its unique white and green foliage. This hybrid variety of the hosta genus thrives in shaded environments and is prized for its ornamental value. Its leaves display a beautiful contrast of pale white and deep green, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. The plant is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal care once established. It adds a touch of elegance to any shady garden space.
