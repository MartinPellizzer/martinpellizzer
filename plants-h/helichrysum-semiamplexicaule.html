Helichrysum semiamplexicaule

Helichrysum semiamplexicaule, commonly known as the narrow-leaf everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and bright yellow to orange flowers. The leaves are narrow and often clasping, giving it the species name "semiamplexicaule.". It produces small, disk-shaped flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
