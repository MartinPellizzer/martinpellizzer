Hieracium irregularidens

Hieracium irregularidens, commonly known as the irregular hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, showing a preference for temperate climates. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rough texture and a hairy surface.

Hieracium irregularidens is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
