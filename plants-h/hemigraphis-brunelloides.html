Hemigraphis brunelloides

Hemigraphis brunelloides, commonly known as the velvetleaf or red-veined nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, arranged in clusters.
