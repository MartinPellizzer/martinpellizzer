Hibiscus aponeurus

Hibiscus aponeurus, commonly known as the white hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, white, showy flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Hibiscus aponeurus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.
