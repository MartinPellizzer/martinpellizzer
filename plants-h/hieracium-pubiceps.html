Hieracium pubiceps

Hieracium pubiceps, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium pubiceps is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
