Hernandia peltata var. cordata

Hernandia peltata var. cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf hernandia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
