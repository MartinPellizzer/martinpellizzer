Hahnia aria var. rotundifolia

Hahnia aria var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed hahnia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features basal rosettes of broad, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink to purple in color.

This variety is noted for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in nutrient-poor environments.
