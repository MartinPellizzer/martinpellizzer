Homalomena elmeri

Homalomena elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's arrowleaf, is a tropical evergreen perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Homalomena elmeri requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
