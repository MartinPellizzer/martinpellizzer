Hieracium corniculans

Hieracium corniculans, commonly known as the horned hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, often reddish stems. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grasslands. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in disturbed soils.
