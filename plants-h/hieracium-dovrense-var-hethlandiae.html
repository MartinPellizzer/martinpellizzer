Hieracium dovrense var. hethlandiae

Hieracium dovrense var. hethlandiae, commonly known as the Hethland hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its flowers are yellow and occur in small clusters, while its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.
