Helleborus Viridis Var. Cupreus

Helleborus viridis var. cupreus is a rare and striking plant known for its unique foliage and subtle blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, it thrives in cool, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Its leaves are a vibrant green, often edged with a coppery hue that adds to its visual appeal. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that contrast beautifully with its foliage. Due to its limited distribution and specific growing conditions, it is a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts and conservationists.
