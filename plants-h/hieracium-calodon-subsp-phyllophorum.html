Hieracium calodon subsp. phyllophorum

Hieracium calodon subsp.

phyllophorum, commonly known as the leafy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate leaves and flowering heads that produce yellow-orange ray florets.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges. It has a fibrous root system and produces numerous flowering stems that can reach up to 30 cm in height.

The subspecies is distinguished by its more elongated leaves and slightly different flowering structure compared to other Hieracium species.
