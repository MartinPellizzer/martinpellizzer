Hypericum ascyron var. genuinum

Hypericum ascyron var. genuinum, commonly known as the yellow flowering St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy and ornamental plant.
