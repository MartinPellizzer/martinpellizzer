Hypericum matsumurae

Hypericum matsumurae, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Hypericaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in summer. Hypericum matsumurae is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
