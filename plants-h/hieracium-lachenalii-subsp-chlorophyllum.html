Hieracium lachenalii subsp. chlorophyllum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

chlorophyllum, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and flowering heads that produce yellow-orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has a relatively compact stature and is often found in temperate regions of Europe.

Its botanical features make it distinguishable from other hawkweed species, particularly in its leaf structure and flower coloration.
