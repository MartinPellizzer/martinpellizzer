Hedychium coronarium var. flavum

Hedychium coronarium var. flavum, commonly known as the golden ginger, is a member of the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, ornamental stems and large, showy yellow flowers. Hedychium coronarium var. flavum thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade for optimal growth.
