Hieracium asbolopithum

Hieracium asbolopithum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, upright stems. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and grasslands, often forming dense colonies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive growth.
