Habenaria luentensis

Habenaria luentensis, commonly known as the Luentens Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or grasslands. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Habenaria luentensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
