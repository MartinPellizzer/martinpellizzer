Hieracium excellens

Hieracium excellens, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides. The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a single head, surrounded by pale green bracts. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium excellens is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
