Helianthemum grandiflorum subsp. urrielense

Helianthemum grandiflorum subsp.

urrielense, commonly known as the Urrielense rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is a low-growing shrub with woody stems and fleshy, evergreen leaves.

The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to dry and sunny environments.
