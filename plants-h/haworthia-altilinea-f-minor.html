Haworthia altilinea f. minor

Haworthia altilinea f. minor, commonly known as the miniature Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically a pale green to blue-gray in color, often with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
