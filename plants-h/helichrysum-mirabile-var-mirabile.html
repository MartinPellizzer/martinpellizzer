Helichrysum mirabile var. mirabile

Helichrysum mirabile var. mirabile, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The plant is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
