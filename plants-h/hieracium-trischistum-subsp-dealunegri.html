Hieracium trischistum subsp. dealunegri

Hieracium trischistum subsp.

dealunegri, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.
