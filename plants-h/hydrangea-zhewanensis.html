Hydrangea zhewanensis

Hydrangea zhewanensis, commonly known as the Zhewan hydrangea, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the Hydrangeaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences. The plant typically features broad, waxy leaves that are green on the upper surface and lighter in color on the underside. Its flowers can range in color from blue to pink, depending on soil pH, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Hydrangea zhewanensis thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil conditions.
