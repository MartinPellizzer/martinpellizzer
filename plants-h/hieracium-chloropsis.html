Hieracium chloropsis

Hieracium chloropsis, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

Hieracium chloropsis is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
