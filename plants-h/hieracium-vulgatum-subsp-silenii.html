Hieracium vulgatum subsp. silenii

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

silenii, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and can reach up to 15 cm in length.

The flowering period occurs from June to August, and it is often found in open habitats across temperate regions.
