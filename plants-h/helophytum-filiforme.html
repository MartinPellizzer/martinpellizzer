Helophytum filiforme

Helophytum filiforme, commonly known as the slender helophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Helophytum filiforme thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
