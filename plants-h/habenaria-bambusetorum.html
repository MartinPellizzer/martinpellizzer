Habenaria bambusetorum

Habenaria bambusetorum, commonly known as the bamboo orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and compact inflorescence. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and fragrant, usually white or pale pink, and bloom during the rainy season.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
