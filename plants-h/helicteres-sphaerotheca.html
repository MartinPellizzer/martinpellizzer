Helicteres sphaerotheca

Helicteres sphaerotheca, commonly known as the globe hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, globe-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.
