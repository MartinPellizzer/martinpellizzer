Hoita orbicularis

Hoita orbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its orbicular, or nearly round, leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Hoita orbicularis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique leaf shape and delicate blooms make it a distinctive and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
