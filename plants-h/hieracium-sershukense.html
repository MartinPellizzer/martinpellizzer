Hieracium sershukense

Hieracium sershukense, commonly known as the Sershuk hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are hairy and lance-shaped, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium sershukense is often found in rocky or grassy habitats at high elevations.
