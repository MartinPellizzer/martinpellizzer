Haemanthus diadema

Haemanthus diadema, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, clustered white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, creating a star-like appearance. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the white blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Haemanthus diadema is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and seasonal flowering.
