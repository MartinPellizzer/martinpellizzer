Hosta sieboldiana var. amplissima

Hosta sieboldiana var. amplissima, commonly known as the giant siebold hosta, is a striking ornamental plant native to Japan.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, displaying a range of colors from light green to deep blue. The plant thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil. It is a clump-forming perennial that spreads slowly through rhizomes.

Its foliage is the main ornamental feature, though it produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in late summer.
