Heteropterys brunnea

Heteropterys brunnea, commonly known as the blackleaf piper, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, round berries that turn red when mature.

Heteropterys brunnea is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
