Helichroa amoena

Helichroa amoena, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow, daisy-like flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It produces small, pale green fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Helichroa amoena is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
