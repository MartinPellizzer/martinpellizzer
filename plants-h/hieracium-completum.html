Hieracium completum

Hieracium completum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are rough and hairy, and the stems are upright with a reddish hue.

Hieracium completum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is considered a weed in some regions.
