Hosta tokudama f. flavocircinalis

Hosta tokudama f. flavocircinalis, commonly known as the Golden Ring Hosta, is a cultivar prized for its striking foliage.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a vibrant green center and a distinctive golden-yellow margin. The plant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to Japan. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and container planting.
