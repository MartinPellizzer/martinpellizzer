Hieracium suksdorfii

Hieracium suksdorfii, commonly known as the Saksdorf's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
