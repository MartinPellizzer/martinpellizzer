Hypoxis villosa var. scabra

Hypoxis villosa var. scabra, commonly known as the rough hypoxis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, hairy leaves that are typically grayish-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in a dense inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its medicinal properties have made it a subject of interest in traditional and modern herbal practices.
