Hesperantha umbricola

Hesperantha umbricola, commonly known as the shadow lily, is a South African plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as under forest canopies. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Hesperantha umbricola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with partial shade.
