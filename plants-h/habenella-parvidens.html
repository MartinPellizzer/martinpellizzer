Habenella parvidens

Habenella parvidens, commonly known as the small-leaf habenella, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, opposite leaves that are usually less than 2 inches in length. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often greenish-white in color, and appear in clusters.

Habenella parvidens is often found in association with other moisture-loving plants in its native habitat.
