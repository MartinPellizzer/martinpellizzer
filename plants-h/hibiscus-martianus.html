Hibiscus martianus

Hibiscus martianus, commonly known as the Martian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

Hibiscus martianus thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils.
