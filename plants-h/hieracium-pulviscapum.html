Hieracium pulviscapum

Hieracium pulviscapum, commonly known as the dustcap hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium pulviscapum is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to colonize various habitats.
