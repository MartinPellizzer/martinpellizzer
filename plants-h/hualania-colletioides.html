Hualania colletioides

Hualania colletioides, commonly known as the giant hibiscus, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a lush, dense foliage. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Hualania colletioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
