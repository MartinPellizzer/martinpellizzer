Hieracium amitsokense

Hieracium amitsokense, commonly known as the Amitsokense hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It plays a minor role in local ecosystems but is of interest to botanists due to its unique distribution and morphology.
