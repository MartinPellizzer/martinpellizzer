Hieracium sclerocomum

Hieracium sclerocomum, commonly known as the rigid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped. Its flowers are yellow and are arranged in a single head, typical of the genus Hieracium.

It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.
