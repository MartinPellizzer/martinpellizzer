Helictotrichon setaceum

Helictotrichon setaceum, commonly known as the needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Helictotrichon setaceum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
