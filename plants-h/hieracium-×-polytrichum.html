Hieracium × polytrichum

Hieracium × polytrichum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces numerous flowering stems with clustered flower heads.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
