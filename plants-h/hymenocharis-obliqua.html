Hymenocharis obliqua

Hymenocharis obliqua, commonly known as the zigzag bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue. The flowers bloom in spring and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
