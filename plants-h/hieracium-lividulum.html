Hieracium lividulum

Hieracium lividulum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and western Asia, and it thrives in grassy areas and open woodlands. Hieracium lividulum is often found in disturbed soils and can form dense colonies, making it a common weed in agricultural settings.
