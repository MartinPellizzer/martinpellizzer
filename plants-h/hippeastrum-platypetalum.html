Hippeastrum platypetalum

Hippeastrum platypetalum, commonly known as the flat-petaled amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and slightly wavy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to produce flowers.

Hippeastrum platypetalum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
