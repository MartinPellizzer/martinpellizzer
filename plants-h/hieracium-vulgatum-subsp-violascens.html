Hieracium vulgatum subsp. violascens

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

violascens, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers range in color from pale purple to deep violet.

It is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
