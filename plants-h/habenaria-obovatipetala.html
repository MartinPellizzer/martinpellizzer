Habenaria obovatipetala

Habenaria obovatipetala, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and ovate, contributing to its common name.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
