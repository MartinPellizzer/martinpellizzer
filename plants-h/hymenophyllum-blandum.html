Hymenophyllum blandum

Hymenophyllum blandum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a small, epiphytic fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant's leaves are typically narrow and have a silvery or pale green appearance due to the presence of trichomes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of moisture and indirect light.

Hymenophyllum blandum is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks in moist forests.
