Heteromorpha mitis

Heteromorpha mitis, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its compact size and attractive appearance.

Heteromorpha mitis is drought-tolerant and requires minimal care once established.
