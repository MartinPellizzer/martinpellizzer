Hedera helix var. minor-donerailensis

Hedera helix var. minor-donerailensis, commonly known as the Donerailensis ivy, is a compact, trailing variety of English ivy.

It features small, dark green, glossy leaves that are typically heart-shaped with a slightly wavy margin. This variety is well-suited for hanging baskets, ground cover, or as a climbing plant on trellises. It prefers partial to full shade and well-draining soil, making it ideal for shaded garden areas.

The plant is known for its dense growth habit and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor and outdoor environments.
