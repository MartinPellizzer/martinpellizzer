Hedeoma nana

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in cold, moist environments. The plant has a compact growth habit with small, spoon-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences and are usually pink or purple in color.
