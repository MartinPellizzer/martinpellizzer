Hieracium altefurcatum

Hieracium altefurcatum, commonly known as the tall forked hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium altefurcatum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
