Helichrysum junodii

Helichrysum junodii, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum junodii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
