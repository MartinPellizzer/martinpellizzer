Hieracium medelingense

Hieracium medelingense, commonly known as the Medeling yellow hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and the plant is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
