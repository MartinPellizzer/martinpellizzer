Huxleya linifolia

Huxleya linifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf huxleya, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hair, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Huxleya linifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
