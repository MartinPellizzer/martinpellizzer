Hieracium decipiens

Hieracium decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium decipiens is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
