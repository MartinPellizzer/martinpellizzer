Hieracium athoum

Hieracium athoum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, often found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant typically grows to a height of about 20 to 60 centimeters and has basal leaves that are lance-shaped. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates.

The species is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some regions.
