Hieracium bifidum subsp. sivyense

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing adaptability to arid conditions. Its subspecies status highlights its distinct morphological and genetic differences from other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
