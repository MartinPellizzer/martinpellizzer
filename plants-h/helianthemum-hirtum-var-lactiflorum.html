Helianthemum hirtum var. lactiflorum

Helianthemum hirtum var. lactiflorum, commonly known as the milky rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its hairy stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces a milky sap, which is a distinguishing feature of the species. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its hardy nature makes it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.
