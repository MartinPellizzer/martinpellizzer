Hedysarum nagarzense

Hedysarum nagarzense, commonly known as the Nagarkot milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Hedysarum nagarzense is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil in high-altitude environments.
