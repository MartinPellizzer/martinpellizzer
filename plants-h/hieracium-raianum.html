Hieracium raianum

Hieracium raianum, commonly known as the Rhaetian hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant has basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium raianum is often found in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes.
