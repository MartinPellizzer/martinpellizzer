Hieracium scabrum var. hirsutum

Hieracium scabrum var. hirsutum, commonly known as rough hawksbeard, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a rough texture. The plant produces bright yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a single head. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils.
