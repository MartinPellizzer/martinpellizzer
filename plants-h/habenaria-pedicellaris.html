Habenaria pedicellaris

Habenaria pedicellaris, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It features a slender, creeping rhizome and produces a dense, spike-like inflorescence with small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and blooms from late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Its flowers are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem, a characteristic feature of the orchid family.
