Helleborine latifolia f. pseudovarians

Helleborine latifolia f. pseudovarians, commonly known as the broad-leaf helleborine, is a hybrid orchid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of greenish-white or pale yellow flowers. This plant is native to regions in Europe and is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and forest floors. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is typically perennial, with a rhizomatous growth habit.

The plant's botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical study.
