Hortensia nigra

Hortensia nigra, commonly known as black hortensia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Hortensia nigra is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
