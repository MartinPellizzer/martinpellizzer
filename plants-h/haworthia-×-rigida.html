Haworthia × rigida

Haworthia × rigida, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette shape and distinctive, striped leaves that resemble a zebra's pattern. The leaves are fleshy and typically range in color from pale green to bluish-green, with prominent white or pale markings. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
