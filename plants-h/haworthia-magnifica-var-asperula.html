Haworthia magnifica var. asperula

Haworthia magnifica var. asperula, commonly known as the asperula Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with white markings. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in arid environments. It produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
