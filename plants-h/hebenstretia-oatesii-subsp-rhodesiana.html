Hebenstretia oatesii subsp. rhodesiana

Hebenstretia oatesii subsp.

rhodesiana, commonly known as Rhodesian hebenstretia, is a member of the Apocynaceae family. It is a shrub native to southern Africa, particularly found in countries like Zimbabwe and South Africa.

The plant features elliptical to ovate leaves that are usually green with a slightly pubescent surface. Its flowers are typically orange or yellow, with a distinctive corolla that is tubular in shape.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soils with moderate sunlight.
