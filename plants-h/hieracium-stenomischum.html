Hieracium stenomischum

Hieracium stenomischum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium stenomischum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
