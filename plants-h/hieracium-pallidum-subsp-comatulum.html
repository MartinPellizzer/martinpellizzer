Hieracium pallidum subsp. comatulum

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

comatulum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its low-growing habit and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
