Helianthella californica subsp. shastensis

Helianthella californica subsp.

shastensis, commonly known as Shasta helianthella, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its basal rosette of gray-green, woolly leaves.
