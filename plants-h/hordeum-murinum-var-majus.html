Hordeum murinum var. majus

Hordeum murinum var. majus, commonly known as mouse barley, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous pale green to brown florets. Its seeds are small and often dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which help it thrive in various environmental conditions.
