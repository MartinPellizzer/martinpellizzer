Hieracium nudifolium

Hieracium nudifolium, commonly known as the nakedleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow disk flowers surrounded by ray florets. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant is typically found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.

It is a perennial species that produces seeds through wind dispersal.
