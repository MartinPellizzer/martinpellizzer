Hilaria jamesii

Hilaria jamesii, commonly known as blue grama, is a native grass species found primarily in the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and produces small, fluffy seed heads that are distinctive in appearance. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments. It plays an important role in stabilizing soils and supporting wildlife in its native range.

Hilaria jamesii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
