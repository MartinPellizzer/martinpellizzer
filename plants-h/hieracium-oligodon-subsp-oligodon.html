Hieracium oligodon subsp. oligodon

Hieracium oligodon subsp.

oligodon, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and flowering heads that produce small, yellowish to reddish flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring moist to moderately dry conditions. Its flowers are arranged in a single head, and the plant produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

It is often found in open habitats and can form dense colonies in suitable environments.
