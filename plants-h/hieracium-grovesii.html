Hieracium grovesii

Hieracium grovesii, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust stem structure. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands, thriving in temperate climates. Hieracium grovesii is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it both a common weed and a resilient flora species.
