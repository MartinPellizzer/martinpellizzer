Hieracium norvegicum subsp. trothanum

Hieracium norvegicum subsp.

trothanum, commonly known as the Norwegian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

The plant features dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

This subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology and flowering pattern compared to other hawkweed species.
