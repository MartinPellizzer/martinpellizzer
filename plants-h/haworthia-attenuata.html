Haworthia attenuata

Haworthia attenuata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or pale green markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers on slender stalks during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
