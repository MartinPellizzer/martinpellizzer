Hypericum Elongatum Subsp. Microcalycinum

Common st. john's wort, scientifically known as Hypericum elongatum subsp. microcalycinum, is a flowering plant native to parts of europe and western asia. it is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and small, elliptical leaves. this species thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or woodland areas. it has been used traditionally for its purported medicinal properties, though its effects remain debated in modern science. its resilience and beauty make it a notable plant in both ecological and botanical contexts.
