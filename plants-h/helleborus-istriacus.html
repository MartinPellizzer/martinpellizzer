Helleborus istriacus

Helleborus istriacus, commonly known as the Istrian hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces clusters of pale green to white flowers with prominent, waxy petals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Helleborus istriacus is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
