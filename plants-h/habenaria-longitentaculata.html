Habenaria longitentaculata

Habenaria longitentaculata, commonly known as the long-tendril orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and distinctive, pendulous inflorescences. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish with intricate patterns, and they bloom during the wet season. This orchid is often found growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a unique and visually striking addition to orchid collections.
