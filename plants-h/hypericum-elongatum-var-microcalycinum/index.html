Hypericum Elongatum Var. Microcalycinum

Common st. john's wort, scientifically known as Hypericum elongatum var. microcalycinum, is a hardy perennial plant native to parts of europe and western asia. it is recognized for its vibrant yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. this plant is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and rocky areas, where it contributes to the local ecosystem. its historical use in traditional medicine has made it a subject of both scientific interest and cultural significance. despite its medicinal potential, it requires careful cultivation to prevent overharvesting and ensure its survival in the wild.
