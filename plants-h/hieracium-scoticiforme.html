Hieracium scoticiforme

Hieracium scoticiforme, commonly known as Scottish dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous tiny florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are rough and hairy, with a distinctive heart-shaped base.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, producing seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
