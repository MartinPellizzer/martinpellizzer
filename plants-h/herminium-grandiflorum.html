Herminium grandiflorum

Herminium grandiflorum, commonly known as the showy lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to North America.

It features a dense, mat-forming growth habit with a basal rosette of leaves and a tall, flowering stem. The plant produces a showy inflorescence of small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in a loose spike. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

This orchid is often found in wetlands, along stream banks, and in woodland areas.
