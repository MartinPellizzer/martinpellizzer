Hibiscus tricolor

Hibiscus tricolor, commonly known as the tri-color hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tri-colored petals that range from white, yellow, and red. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. Hibiscus tricolor thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.
