Hippeastrum colchicum

Hippeastrum colchicum, commonly known as the Colombian amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, with a distinctive trumpet-shaped form. The plant produces a tall, sturdy stem that can reach up to two meters in height, supporting its vibrant blooms. Its leaves are broad, strap-like, and often appear after the flowers have opened.

Hippeastrum colchicum is a popular ornamental plant due to its dramatic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
