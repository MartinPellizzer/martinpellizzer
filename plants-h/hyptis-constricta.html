Hyptis constricta

Hyptis constricta, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks.
