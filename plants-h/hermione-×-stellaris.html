Hermione × stellaris

Hermione × stellaris, commonly known as the star Hermione, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Hermione and stellaris species.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, often with a contrasting central stripe. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of 1 to 3 feet. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green, providing a nice contrast to its vibrant blooms.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is known for its long blooming period, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking visual interest throughout the growing season.
