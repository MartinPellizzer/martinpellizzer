Habenaria heyneana

Habenaria heyneana, commonly known as the Spotted Pterostylis, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy flower that has a distinctive, spurred lip. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette at the base of the stem.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens.
