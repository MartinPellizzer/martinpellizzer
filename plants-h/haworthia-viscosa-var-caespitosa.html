Haworthia viscosa var. caespitosa

Haworthia viscosa var. caespitosa, commonly known as the compact Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, rosette-shaped clusters of fleshy, pale green leaves with white tubercles and translucent windows. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
