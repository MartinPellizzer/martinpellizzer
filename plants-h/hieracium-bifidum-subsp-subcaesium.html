Hieracium bifidum subsp. subcaesium

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

subcaesium, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in composite flower heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist meadows and grasslands, often forming dense mats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
