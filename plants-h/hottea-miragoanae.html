Hottea miragoanae

Hottea miragoanae, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange petals that resemble flames. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

The leaves are broad and waxy, helping to retain water in its natural habitat.
