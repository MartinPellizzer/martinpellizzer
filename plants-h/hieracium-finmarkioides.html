Hieracium finmarkioides

Hieracium finmarkioides, commonly known as the Finmark's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to northern regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic environments such as peat bogs and mountain tundra. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in dense, flat-topped clusters.

Hieracium finmarkioides is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh, cold climates.
