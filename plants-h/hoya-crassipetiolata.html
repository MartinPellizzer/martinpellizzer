Hoya crassipetiolata

Hoya crassipetiolata, commonly known as the giant hoya or the broad-petioled hoya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Hoya crassipetiolata is a tropical species that thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
