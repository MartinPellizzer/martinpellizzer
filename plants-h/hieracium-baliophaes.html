Hieracium baliophaes

Hieracium baliophaes, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium baliophaes is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many temperate regions.
