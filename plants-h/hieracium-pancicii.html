Hieracium pancicii

Hieracium pancicii, commonly known as the Pancici's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist grasslands and meadows, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a dense cluster.
