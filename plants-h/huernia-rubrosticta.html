Huernia rubrosticta

Huernia rubrosticta, commonly known as the red-streaked African ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Its leaves are typically small and rounded, and it is adapted to arid environments.

Huernia rubrosticta is popular in succulent gardens due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
