Hedyotis elatior var. ensiformis

Hedyotis elatior var. ensiformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bedstraw, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Asia.

It features slender, ascending stems covered with fine, soft hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated margin. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
