Hosta sieboldiana var. glauca

Hosta sieboldiana var. glauca, commonly known as the Blue Dragon Hosta, is a cultivar prized for its striking blue-gray foliage.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are densely packed and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces tall, slender spikes of white to pale lavender flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-draining soil, making it ideal for woodland gardens.

Its unique coloration and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage.
