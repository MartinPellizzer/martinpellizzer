Hieracium sudreanum

Hieracium sudreanum, commonly known as the Sudre's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers. Native to Europe, this plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands. It has finely divided leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that attract various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
