Hieracium glaciellum subsp. subincanum

Hieracium glaciellum subsp.

subincanum, commonly known as the glacier hawkweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe. It thrives in cold, rocky environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions with its compact growth habit.

The plant features hairy, often bluish-green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts.

Due to its specialized habitat, it is considered a rare and sensitive indicator species of alpine ecosystems.
