Hymenocallis americana f. disticha

Hymenocallis americana f. disticha, commonly known as the twin-stemmed spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, recurved spathe and a central spadix, typical of the Amaryllidaceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
