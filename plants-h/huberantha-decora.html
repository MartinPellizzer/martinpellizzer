Huberantha decora

Huberantha decora, commonly known as the decorative orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms.

Huberantha decora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
