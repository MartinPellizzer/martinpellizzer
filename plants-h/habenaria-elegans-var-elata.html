Habenaria elegans var. elata

Habenaria elegans var. elata, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually appear in clusters, displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
