Hypoxis hygrometrica var. hygrometrica

Hypoxis hygrometrica var. hygrometrica, commonly known as the water hypoxis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
