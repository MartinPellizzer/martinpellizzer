Hieracium tossalense

Hieracium tossalense, commonly known as the Tossal hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

Hieracium tossalense is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.
