Helxine scandens

Helxine scandens, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a trailing evergreen perennial that produces small, white, spider-like flowers. The plant has narrow, silver-green leaves that are slightly curled, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Helxine scandens is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
