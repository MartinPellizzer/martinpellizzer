Hypericum hengshanense var. xinlinense

Hypericum hengshanense var. xinlinense, commonly known as the Xinlin St. John's Wort, is a rare and endemic plant species native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has small, opposite leaves and produces clusters of four-petaled flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
