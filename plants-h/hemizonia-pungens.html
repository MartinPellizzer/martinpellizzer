Hemizonia pungens

Hemizonia pungens, commonly known as the spiky leaf cactus, is a unique succulent native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often covered in sharp, spiny structures for protection. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and adapting it to arid environments.

Hemizonia pungens is valued in horticulture for its distinctive appearance and resilience in dry conditions.
