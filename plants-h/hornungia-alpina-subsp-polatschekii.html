Hornungia alpina subsp. polatschekii

Hornungia alpina subsp.

polatschekii, commonly known as the alpine hornungia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to the harsh conditions of its habitat.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
