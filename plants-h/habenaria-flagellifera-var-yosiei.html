Habenaria flagellifera var. yosiei

Habenaria flagellifera var. yosiei, commonly known as the Yosiei's lady's-tongue orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive, lip-like structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, organic soil for optimal growth.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
