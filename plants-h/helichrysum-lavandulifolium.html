Helichrysum lavandulifolium

Helichrysum lavandulifolium, commonly known as silverleaf helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
