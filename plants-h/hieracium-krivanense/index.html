Hieracium Krivanense

Hieracium krivanense is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Croatia. It belongs to the daisy family, Asteraceae, and is known for its striking yellow flowers. This species thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing in open meadows and slopes. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it an important subject for botanical study.
