Habenaria amphorchis

Habenaria amphorchis, commonly known as the orchid with a distinctive floral structure, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a unique lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
