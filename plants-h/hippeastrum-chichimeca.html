Hippeastrum chichimeca

Hippeastrum chichimeca, commonly known as the Chichimeca amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to Mexico.

It produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, with a trumpet-shaped form. The plant features strap-like, lanceolate leaves that are often a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
