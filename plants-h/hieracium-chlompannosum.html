Hieracium chlompannosum

Hieracium chlompannosum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces achenes as its fruit, which are dispersed by wind.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
