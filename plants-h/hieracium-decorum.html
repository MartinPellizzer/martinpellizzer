Hieracium decorum

Hieracium decorum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer, attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Hieracium decorum is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appearance in garden settings.
