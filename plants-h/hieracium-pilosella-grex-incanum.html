Hieracium pilosella grex incanum

Hieracium pilosella grex incanum, commonly known as the silverleaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

The plant is known for its ability to spread rapidly through both seeds and rhizomes, making it an invasive species in some regions.
