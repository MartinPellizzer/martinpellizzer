Hieracium speciosiforme

Hieracium speciosiforme, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium speciosiforme is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
