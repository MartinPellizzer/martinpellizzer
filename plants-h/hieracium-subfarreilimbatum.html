Hieracium subfarreilimbatum

Hieracium subfarreilimbatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in the summer months. Native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, it thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open grasslands. The plant has a fibrous root system and can spread vegetatively through rhizomes.

Its flowers are typically arranged in a single head, with both ray and disk florets.
