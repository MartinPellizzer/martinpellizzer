Hieracium stenophillum

Hieracium stenophillum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium stenophillum is often found in open habitats and can be an indicator of nutrient-rich soils.
