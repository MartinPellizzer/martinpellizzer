Hedera nepalensis var. sinensis

Hedera nepalensis var. sinensis, commonly known as Chinese ivy, is a climbing evergreen vine native to eastern Asia.

It features dark green, glossy, lobed leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by orange-red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Its adaptability makes it a common species in both natural and cultivated settings.
