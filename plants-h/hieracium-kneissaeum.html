Hieracium kneissaeum

Hieracium kneissaeum, commonly known as the Kneissau hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
