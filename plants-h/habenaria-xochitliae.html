Habenaria xochitliae

Habenaria xochitliae, commonly known as the Mexican lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and long, spiraled inflorescences. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
