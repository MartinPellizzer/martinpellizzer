Hieracium umbellatum f. latifolium

Hieracium umbellatum f. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf field hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant features a bushy growth habit with yellow flowers that bloom from June to August. Its leaves are broadly ovate to lanceolate, often with a hairy surface, and the stems are usually reddish in color.

This variant is distinguished by its larger, broader leaves compared to other forms of Hieracium umbellatum.
