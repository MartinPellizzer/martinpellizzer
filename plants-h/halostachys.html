Halostachys

Halostachys, commonly known as saltbush, is a genus of flowering plants in the amaranth family, Amaranthaceae.

These plants are typically shrubs or small trees, characterized by their succulent leaves and ability to thrive in arid or saline environments. They are native to various regions in the Americas, particularly in the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. Halostachys species often have small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, and they produce small, dry fruits.
