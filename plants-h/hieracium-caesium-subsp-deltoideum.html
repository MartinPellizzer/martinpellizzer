Hieracium caesium subsp. deltoideum

Hieracium caesium subsp.

deltoideum, commonly known as the deltoid hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers.

The plant typically features basal leaves that are broadly triangular or deltoid in shape, giving it its subspecific name. It thrives in moist meadows and open woodlands, often forming dense colonies.

The species is known for its hairy stems and involucral leaves that surround the flower heads.
