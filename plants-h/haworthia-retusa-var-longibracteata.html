Haworthia retusa var. longibracteata

Haworthia retusa var. longibracteata, commonly known as the longibracteata Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stalks during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
