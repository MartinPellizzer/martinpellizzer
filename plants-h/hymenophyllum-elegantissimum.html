Hymenophyllum elegantissimum

Hymenophyllum elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant hymenophyllum, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, moss-like fronds that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found on tree trunks or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of tiny, scale-like structures that help reduce water loss.

This fern is notable for its intricate morphology and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
