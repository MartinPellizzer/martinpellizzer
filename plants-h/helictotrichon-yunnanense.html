Helictotrichon yunnanense

Helictotrichon yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and forested areas. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
