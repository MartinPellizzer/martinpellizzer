Heliotropium stigosum var. stellulatum

Heliotropium stigosum var. stellulatum, commonly known as the starry heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the early morning and close by midday. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

The species is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in wet, sunny environments.
