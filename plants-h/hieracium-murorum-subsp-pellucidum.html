Hieracium murorum subsp. pellucidum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

pellucidum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its translucent, waxy leaves that give it a unique appearance. It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats such as roadsides and meadows.
