Habenaria × chevallieriana

Habenaria × chevallieriana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Habenaria uniflora and Habenaria rothschildiana.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Thailand and Indonesia. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
