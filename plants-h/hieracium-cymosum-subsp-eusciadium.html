Hieracium cymosum subsp. eusciadium

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

eusciadium, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

The plant features basal rosettes of broadly lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in cymes. Its flowers are usually 5-lobed and have a distinctive central disc.

The subspecies is characterized by its compact growth habit and is part of the Asteraceae family.
