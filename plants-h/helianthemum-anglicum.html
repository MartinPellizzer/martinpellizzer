Helianthemum anglicum

Helianthemum anglicum, commonly known as English rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to southern and eastern Europe.

It features compact, evergreen foliage with small, rounded leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, yellow or orange flowers in clusters during late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats.

Helianthemum anglicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
