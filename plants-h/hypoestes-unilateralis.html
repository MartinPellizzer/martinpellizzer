Hypoestes unilateralis

Hypoestes unilateralis, commonly known as the Purple Queen, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety purple foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and in ornamental horticulture.
