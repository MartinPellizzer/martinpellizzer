Hieracium polymelinum

Hieracium polymelinum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
