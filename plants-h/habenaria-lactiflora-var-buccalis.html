Habenaria lactiflora var. buccalis

Habenaria lactiflora var. buccalis, commonly known as the milk orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often swollen and hairy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest undergrowth.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
