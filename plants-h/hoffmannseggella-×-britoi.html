Hoffmannseggella × britoi

Hoffmannseggella × britoi, commonly known as the hybrid hawthorn, is a naturally occurring hybrid between *Crataegus monogyna* and *Crataegus laevigata*.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in temperate regions of Europe. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces white or pink flowers in spring. Its fruits are red berries that ripen in autumn, providing food for various wildlife.

The hybrid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.
