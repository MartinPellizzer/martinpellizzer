Homalomena falcata

Homalomena falcata, commonly known as the sword leaf arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking white or pale yellow spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is a common feature in the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Homalomena falcata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
