Henslowia lobbiana

Henslowia lobbiana, commonly known as the slender rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features a slender, upright stem with lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers in late spring. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a unique, compact growth habit.

Henslowia lobbiana is often found in open woodlands and along stream banks, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
