Habenaria yezoensis

Habenaria yezoensis, commonly known as the Yezo orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, pale pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and bloom during the summer months.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated for horticultural purposes.
