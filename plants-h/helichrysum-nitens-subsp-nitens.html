Helichrysum nitens subsp. nitens

Helichrysum nitens subsp.

nitens, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that resemble tiny suns, giving it its common name.

The plant produces papery, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its long-lasting flowers make it a favored plant for floral arrangements and dried flower crafts.
