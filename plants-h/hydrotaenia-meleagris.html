Hydrotaenia meleagris

Hydrotaenia meleagris, commonly known as the spotted water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. The plant features broad, floating leaves with a distinctive spotted pattern on the underside. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale yellow, blooming during the day.

Hydrotaenia meleagris plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various small organisms.
