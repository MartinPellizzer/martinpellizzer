Hippeastrum gameleirense

Hippeastrum gameleirense, commonly known as the Gameleirensis amaryllis, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum gameleirense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
