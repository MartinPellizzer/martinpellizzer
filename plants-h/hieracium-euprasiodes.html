Hieracium euprasiodes

Hieracium euprasiodes, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and the leaves are lance-shaped with rough textures.

It is often found in open habitats and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
