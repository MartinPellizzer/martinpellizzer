Hieracium × rubriforme

Hieracium × rubriforme, commonly known as the red-stemmed hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and reddish stems, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and meadows. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium × rubriforme is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
