Holcoglossum omeiense

Holcoglossum omeiense, commonly known as the Omei Orchid, is a rare and endangered orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant grows in high-altitude forest habitats, often in shaded and moist environments, and relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake. Its conservation status is critical due to habitat loss and over-collection for horticultural trade.

Holcoglossum omeiense plays a unique role in its ecosystem and is of great interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
