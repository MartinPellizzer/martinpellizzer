Hieracium fuligeniellum

Hieracium fuligeniellum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has basal leaves that are often heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

Hieracium fuligeniellum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed due to its aggressive growth habit.
