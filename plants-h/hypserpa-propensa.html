Hypserpa propensa

Hypserpa propensa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Hypserpa propensa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
