Hieracium niveolimbatum

Hieracium niveolimbatum, commonly known as the white-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are broadly ovate with a distinctive white or pale green coloration, and its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

It is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
