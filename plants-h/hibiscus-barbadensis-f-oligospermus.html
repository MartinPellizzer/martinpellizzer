Hibiscus barbadensis f. oligospermus

Hibiscus barbadensis f. oligospermus, commonly known as the scarlet hibiscus, is a cultivated form of the common hibiscus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. It has heart-shaped leaves and produces fruits that are smaller than those of the typical hibiscus.

This variety is often grown for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
