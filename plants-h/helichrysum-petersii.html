Helichrysum petersii

Helichrysum petersii, commonly known as the Cape gold cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum petersii is also known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
