Habenaria stenophylla

Habenaria stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, pale pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
