Hilsenbergia angustifolia

Hilsenbergia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hilsenbergia, is a perennial flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often violet or purple in color, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Hilsenbergia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
