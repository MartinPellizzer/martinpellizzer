Hieracium streptophyllum

Hieracium streptophyllum, commonly known as the zigzag hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are alternate, rough, and often have a zigzagged or wavy margin, giving it its common name.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
