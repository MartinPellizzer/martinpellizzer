Henfreya scandens

Henfreya scandens, commonly known as the climbing henfreya, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial vine that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Henfreya scandens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be used in vertical gardening or as a climbing plant in shaded areas.
