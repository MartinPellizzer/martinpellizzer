Hopea sericea

Hopea sericea, commonly known as the silky hopea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. Hopea sericea is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.

It also plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
