Haworthia retusa var. turgida

Haworthia retusa var. turgida, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinct white or pale green markings. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
