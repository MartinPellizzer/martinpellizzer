Haxtonia stellulata

Haxtonia stellulata, commonly known as the star-stemmed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Haxtonia stellulata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
