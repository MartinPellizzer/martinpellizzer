Hemerocallis × yeldara

Hemerocallis × yeldara, commonly known as the Yeldara Daylily, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Hemerocallis genus.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically blooms for a single day, though some cultivars may have extended flowering periods. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its foliage is broad and grass-like, providing an attractive ground cover in ornamental gardens.
