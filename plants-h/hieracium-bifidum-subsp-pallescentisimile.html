Hieracium bifidum subsp. pallescentisimile

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

pallescentisimile, commonly known as the pale-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and it produces small, oval seeds.

It is characterized by its pale, often bluish-green foliage, which distinguishes it from other hawkweed species.
