Hippocratea tulasnei

Hippocratea tulasnei, commonly known as the Tulasne's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a unique structure that includes a spurred lip, a characteristic feature of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually violet or purple in color, and they bloom during the cooler months of the year.

Hippocratea tulasnei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare and exotic plants.
