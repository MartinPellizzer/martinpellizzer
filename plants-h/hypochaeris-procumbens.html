Hypochaeris procumbens

Hypochaeris procumbens, commonly known as the prostrate weed or yellow weed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces small, yellow composite flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped. It is native to Europe but has been introduced to various parts of the world, often growing in disturbed soils.

Hypochaeris procumbens is known for its rapid spread and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
