Helichrysum robbrechtianum

Helichrysum robbrechtianum, commonly known as the Robbrecht's everlasting.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its showy, long-lasting yellow flowers that resemble small suns. The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It produces cylindrical, hairy flower heads that retain their color for extended periods, making it a popular choice for dried floral arrangements.

Helichrysum robbrechtianum is also valued for its aromatic foliage and drought tolerance.
