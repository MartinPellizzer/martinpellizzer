Hieracium subfarinaceum

Hieracium subfarinaceum, commonly known as the farinaceous hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium subfarinaceum is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
