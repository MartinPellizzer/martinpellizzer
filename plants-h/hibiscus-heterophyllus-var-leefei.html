Hibiscus heterophyllus var. leefei

Hibiscus heterophyllus var. leefei, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and can vary in size depending on the growing conditions. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
