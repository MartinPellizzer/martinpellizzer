Heliocereus aurantiacus

Heliocereus aurantiacus, commonly known as the orange night bloomer, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a climbing cactus that produces striking, fragrant flowers that open at night. The flowers are typically bright orange or red, with a sweet, honey-like scent. The plant has spiny stems and is adapted to arid environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique blooming behavior.
