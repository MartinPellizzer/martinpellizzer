Homalomena longipes

Homalomena longipes, commonly known as the long-lobed arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense foliage in suitable conditions.
