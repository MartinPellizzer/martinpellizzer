Hieracium stenolepium

Hieracium stenolepium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often considered a weed in agricultural areas.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and its tolerance to various environmental conditions.
