Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. jacquiniiforme

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

jacquiniiforme, commonly known as the Jacquiniiform hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a perennial herbaceous plant with a rosette of basal leaves and tall flowering stems.

The flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a single head with ray florets. It has a fibrous root system and prefers well-drained soils in open habitats.

This subspecies is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas.
