Hedychium coccineum var. squarrosum

Hedychium coccineum var. squarrosum, commonly known as the red flame ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant features tall, slender stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and aromatic. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
