Helianthemum lippii var. pedicellatum

Helianthemum lippii var. pedicellatum, commonly known as the pink rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer, adding vibrant color to rocky or coastal habitats. The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in dry environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and long flowering period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
