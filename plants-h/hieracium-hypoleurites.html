Hieracium hypoleurites

Hieracium hypoleurites, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is often considered a weed due to its rapid spread and ability to colonize new areas.
