Hieracium umbellatum subsp. filifolium

Hieracium umbellatum subsp.

filifolium, commonly known as the slenderleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are narrow and filamentous, giving it its subspecific name. Its flowering heads are composed of numerous yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of disk florets.

It is often found in low elevations and is characteristic of temperate climates.
