Hieracium lachenalii subsp. tenellicaule

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

tenellicaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowering heads.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender, often hairy stems and relatively small, daisy-like flowers. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and locally endemic species.
