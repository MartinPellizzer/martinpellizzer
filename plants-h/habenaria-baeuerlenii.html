Habenaria baeuerlenii

Habenaria baeuerlenii, commonly known as the Baeuerlen's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
