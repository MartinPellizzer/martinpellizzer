Hebe stricta var. lata

Hebe stricta var. lata, commonly known as the broadleaf hebe, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features upright, evergreen foliage with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a favored plant in horticultural settings.
