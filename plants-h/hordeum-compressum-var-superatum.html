Hordeum compressum var. superatum

Hordeum compressum var. superatum, commonly known as the slender barleygrass, is a variant of the barley species native to temperate regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a rapid growth rate, making it a resilient species.

It is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
