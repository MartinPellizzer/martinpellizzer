Hieracium pellitum subsp. praetenue

Hieracium pellitum subsp.

praetenue, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has finely divided, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that develop into oval, hairy achenes.

This subspecies is often found in temperate regions and is considered a common weed in agricultural areas.
