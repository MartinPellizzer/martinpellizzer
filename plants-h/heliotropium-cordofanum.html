Heliotropium cordofanum

Heliotropium cordofanum, commonly known as the African heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is an annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually purple or violet in color.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
