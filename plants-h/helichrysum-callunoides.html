Helichrysum callunoides

Helichrysum callunoides, commonly known as the coastal everlasting or silverleaf everlasting, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments. Its aromatic leaves and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Helichrysum callunoides is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
