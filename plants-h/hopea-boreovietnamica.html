Hopea boreovietnamica

Hopea boreovietnamica, commonly known as the Vietnamese hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam and Cambodia, where it thrives in lowland evergreen forests. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and has a straight trunk with a smooth, greyish-brown bark. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptic in shape, with a leathery texture and a prominent midrib.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
