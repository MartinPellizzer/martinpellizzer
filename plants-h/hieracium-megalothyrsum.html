Hieracium megalothyrsum

Hieracium megalothyrsum, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its rosette of broad, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered a potential invasive species in some regions.
