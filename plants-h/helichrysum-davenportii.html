Helichrysum davenportii

Helichrysum davenportii, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble small cups, which give it its common name. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and long-lasting blooms make it a valuable ornamental plant.
