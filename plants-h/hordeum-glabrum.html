Hordeum glabrum

Hordeum glabrum, commonly known as the smooth barley, is a species of grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spikelet-based flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its leaves are narrow and smooth, with a bluish-green hue.

Hordeum glabrum is often found in fields, along roadsides, and in cultivated areas where it can become a weed.
