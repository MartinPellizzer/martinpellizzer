Hieracium tardiusculum subsp. cryptastrum

Hieracium tardiusculum subsp.

cryptastrum, commonly known as the cryptastrum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single flower per stem, with a distinctive involucres that are often reddish-brown.

It is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and is part of the Asteraceae family.
