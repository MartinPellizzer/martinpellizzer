Haemanthus namaquensis

Haemanthus namaquensis, commonly known as the Namaqua star.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The plant produces bright red flowers in the summer months, which are clustered in a star-like formation. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
