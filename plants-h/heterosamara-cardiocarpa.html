Heterosamara cardiocarpa

Heterosamara cardiocarpa, commonly known as the heart-shaped fruit plant, is a unique species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped fruits, which are typically green when mature and develop a woody texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The botanical features of Heterosamara cardiocarpa make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
