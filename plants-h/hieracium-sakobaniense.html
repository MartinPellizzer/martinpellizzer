Hieracium sakobaniense

Hieracium sakobaniense, commonly known as the Sakoban Island groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the island of Sakoban in the Indian Ocean.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets that bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Hieracium sakobaniense is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptation and limited distribution.
