Hieracium levicaule subsp. borszekense

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

borszekense, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and hairy, with yellow flowers arranged in a single head. Its inflorescence consists of ray florets, which are usually yellow, and it is part of the Asteraceae family.

This subspecies is characterized by its slender stem and distinct leaf morphology.
