Haworthia limifolia var. marlothiana

Haworthia limifolia var. marlothiana, commonly known as the Marloth's narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and fleshy, narrow leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the tips. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
