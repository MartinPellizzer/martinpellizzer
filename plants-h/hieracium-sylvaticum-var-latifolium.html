Hieracium sylvaticum var. latifolium

Hieracium sylvaticum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf wood hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant features upright stems with hairy surfaces and broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often lobed. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.
