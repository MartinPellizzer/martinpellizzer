Hypoestes glabrata

Hypoestes glabrata, commonly known as the Indian blanket flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has small, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact size and colorful blooms make it an attractive addition to gardens and landscapes.
