Homalomena speariae

Homalomena speariae, commonly known as the speara lily, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant typically features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
