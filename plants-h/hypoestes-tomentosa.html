Hypoestes tomentosa

Hypoestes tomentosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and delicate blooms.

Hypoestes tomentosa is also used in traditional medicine in some regions for its purported health benefits.
