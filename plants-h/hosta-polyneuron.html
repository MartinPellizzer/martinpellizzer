Hosta polyneuron

Hosta polyneuron, commonly known as the silver sword hosta, is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a silvery sheen, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in late summer, usually in shades of white or pale blue. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for shade gardens.
