Hoya cardiophylla

Hoya cardiophylla, commonly known as the heart-leaf hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the summer months. Hoya cardiophylla thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a favored plant among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts.
