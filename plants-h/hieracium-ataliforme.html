Hieracium ataliforme

Hieracium ataliforme, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium ataliforme is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
