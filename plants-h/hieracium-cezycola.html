Hieracium cezycola

Hieracium cezycola, commonly known as the Ceylon hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including India and Sri Lanka.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in clusters.

Hieracium cezycola is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
