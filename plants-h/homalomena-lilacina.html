Homalomena lilacina

Homalomena lilacina, commonly known as the lilac arrowhead, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. It produces fragrant, lilac-colored bracts that surround the spadix, giving it its common name.

Homalomena lilacina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
