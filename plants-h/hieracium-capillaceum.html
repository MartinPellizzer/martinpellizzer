Hieracium capillaceum

Hieracium capillaceum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium capillaceum is often considered a weed but plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
