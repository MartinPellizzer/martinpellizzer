Hieracium saxifragum subsp. oreinum

Hieracium saxifragum subsp.

oreinum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky and open habitats, thriving in high-altitude environments.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish to orange flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It is adapted to cold climates and has a robust root system to anchor itself in unstable substrates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in alpine flora studies.
