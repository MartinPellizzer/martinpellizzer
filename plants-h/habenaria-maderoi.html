Habenaria maderoi

Habenaria maderoi, commonly known as the Mader's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
