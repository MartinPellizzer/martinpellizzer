Hieracium iseranum subsp. anguinescens

Hieracium iseranum subsp.

anguinescens, commonly known as the angular hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the daisy family. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in grassy habitats.
