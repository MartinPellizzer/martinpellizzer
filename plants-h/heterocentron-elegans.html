Heterocentron elegans

Heterocentron elegans, commonly known as the elegant waterwheel, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiral arrangement of leaves and its small, delicate flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, often floating on the water surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in aquatic ecosystems.
