Hieracium psilotrichum

Hieracium psilotrichum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense heads. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are generally lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The plant is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction, making it a common sight in various habitats.
