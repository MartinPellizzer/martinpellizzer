Hieracium plicatum var. ivigtutense

Hieracium plicatum var. ivigtutense, commonly known as the Ivigtut hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Greenland.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, nutrient-rich soils in Arctic and alpine environments. It has a low stature, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
