Hippeastrum tepicense

Hippeastrum tepicense, commonly known as the Mexican belladonna lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Mexico.

It produces large, showy flowers that are typically orange or red with a white throat, often resembling the shape of a bell. The plant features strap-like, broad leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hippeastrum tepicense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
