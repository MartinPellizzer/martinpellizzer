Haworthia lapis var. rava

Haworthia lapis var. rava, commonly known as the Blue Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth and striking blue-green foliage. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments. It produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
