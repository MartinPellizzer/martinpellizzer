Hypopterygiopsis compta

Hypopterygiopsis compta, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The plant prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are wiry and covered in a fine layer of brownish hairs.

The spores are produced in clusters on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
