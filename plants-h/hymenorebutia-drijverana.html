Hymenorebutia drijverana

Hymenorebutia drijverana, commonly known as the Drijver's crown of thorns, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in spines and its clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and requires minimal water. It is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable addition to botanical collections.
