Holothrix filicornis

Holothrix filicornis, commonly known as the hairy holothrix, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Holothrix filicornis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
