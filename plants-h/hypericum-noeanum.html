Hypericum noeanum

Hypericum noeanum, commonly known as the narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, narrow, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
