Helleborus niger var. humilifolius

Helleborus niger var. humilifolius, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and southern Europe.

It features dark green, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped and emit a mild, sweet scent. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, adding visual interest even when it is not in bloom.
