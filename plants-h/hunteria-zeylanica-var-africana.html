Hunteria zeylanica var. africana

Hunteria zeylanica var. africana, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has a woody stem with smooth, greyish bark and can grow as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

The species is well adapted to warm, humid climates and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
