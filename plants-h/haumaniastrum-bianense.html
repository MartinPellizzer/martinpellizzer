Haumaniastrum bianense

Haumaniastrum bianense, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in the summer, and its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes.
