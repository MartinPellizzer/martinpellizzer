Helianthemum × sulphureum var. cupreum

Helianthemum × sulphureum var. cupreum, commonly known as the coppery rockrose, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
