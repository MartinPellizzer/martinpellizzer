Hippeastrum teyucuarense

Hippeastrum teyucuarense, commonly known as the Teyucuarense amaryllis, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a bulbous plant that produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
