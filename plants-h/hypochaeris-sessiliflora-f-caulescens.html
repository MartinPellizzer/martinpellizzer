Hypochaeris sessiliflora f. caulescens

Hypochaeris sessiliflora f. caulescens, commonly known as the clustered false dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright, often branched stem and basal rosette of leaves. The plant produces numerous small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically oval and covered with fine hairs, and it is often found in disturbed soils or open habitats.
