Hieracium euargyreum

Hieracium euargyreum, commonly known as the golden hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by wind.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
