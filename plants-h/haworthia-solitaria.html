Haworthia solitaria

Haworthia solitaria, commonly known as the solitary Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white to pale blue markings, which help in photosynthesis and water storage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
