Habenaria davidii

Habenaria davidii, commonly known as the David's butterfly orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces a single spike with several flowers, each having three petals and three sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
