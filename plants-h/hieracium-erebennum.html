Hieracium erebennum

Hieracium erebennum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, and it has basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed. Hieracium erebennum typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can spread through rhizomes or seeds.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its aggressive growth habit.
