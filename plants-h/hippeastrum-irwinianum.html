Hippeastrum irwinianum

Hippeastrum irwinianum, commonly known as the red hurricane.

This plant is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil. It is characterized by its striking red flowers with orange centers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often used for propagation.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
