Hieracium maesticolor

Hieracium maesticolor, commonly known as the regal hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are typically arranged in a single, showy head. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are rough and hairy, with a lance-shaped appearance. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the genus Hieracium.
