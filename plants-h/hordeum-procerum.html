Hordeum procerum

Hordeum procerum, commonly known as slender barley, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, spikelet-based flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to harsh climatic conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Hordeum procerum is primarily of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological niche.
