Habenaria leucosantha var. riograndensis

Habenaria leucosantha var. riograndensis, commonly known as the Rio Grande orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually broad and elliptical, providing a lush appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
