Hieracium eximium var. pseudeximium

Hieracium eximium var. pseudeximium, commonly known as the False Showy Hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, often growing in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated but can become invasive in certain environments.
