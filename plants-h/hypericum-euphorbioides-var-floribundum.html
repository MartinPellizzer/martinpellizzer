Hypericum euphorbioides var. floribundum

Hypericum euphorbioides var. floribundum, commonly known as the spreading St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its upright, often sprawling growth habit and is known for its bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its botanical features include opposite leaves and a distinctive yellow floral display.
