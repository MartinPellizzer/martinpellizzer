Hieracium tarnense

Hieracium tarnense, commonly known as the Tarn meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

Hieracium tarnense is known for its hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in its arid environment.
