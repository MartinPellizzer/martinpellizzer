Hieracium viride subsp. triquentinum

Hieracium viride subsp.

triquentinum, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant typically has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and botanical classification.
