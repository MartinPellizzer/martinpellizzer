Himatanthus bracteatus var. revolutus

Himatanthus bracteatus var. revolutus, commonly known as the velvet tassel tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that resemble tassels, which are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable environments.
