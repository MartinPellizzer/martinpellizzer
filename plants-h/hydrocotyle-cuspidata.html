Hydrocotyle cuspidata

Hydrocotyle cuspidata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically shiny and have a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Hydrocotyle cuspidata is often used in aquatic gardens and as a ground cover due to its rapid growth and attractive foliage.
