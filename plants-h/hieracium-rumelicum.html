Hieracium rumelicum

Hieracium rumelicum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in late summer, and it produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Hieracium rumelicum is often found in open habitats and can sometimes form dense colonies.
