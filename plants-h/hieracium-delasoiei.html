Hieracium delasoiei

Hieracium delasoiei, commonly known as the Delasoie's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are basal, rough, and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat, daisy-like cluster.

Hieracium delasoiei is known for its resilience to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly terrain.
