Hieracium scheppigianum subsp. pljesevicae

Hieracium scheppigianum subsp.

pljesevicae, commonly known as the Pljesevica hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has serrated, alternate leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
