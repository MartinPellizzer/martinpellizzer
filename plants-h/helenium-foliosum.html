Helenium foliosum

Helenium foliosum, commonly known as the New Zealand aster, is a perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features showy, daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk and ray florets that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has upright, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Helenium foliosum is well-suited for gardens and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
