Hieracium tubulosum

Hieracium tubulosum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, yellowish flower heads that are surrounded by pale green to white bracts.

Hieracium tubulosum is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable habitats.
