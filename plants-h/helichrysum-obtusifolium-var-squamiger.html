Helichrysum obtusifolium var. squamiger

Helichrysum obtusifolium var. squamiger, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is known for its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
