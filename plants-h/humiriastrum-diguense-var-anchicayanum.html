Humiriastrum diguense var. anchicayanum

Humiriastrum diguense var. anchicayanum, commonly known as the Anchicaya humiriastrum, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous roots. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky soils, adapting to the harsh conditions of its environment. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, while its flowers are small and yellow, contributing to its ornamental value.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
