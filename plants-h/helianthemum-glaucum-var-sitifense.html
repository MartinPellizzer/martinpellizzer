Helianthemum glaucum var. sitifense

Helianthemum glaucum var. sitifense, commonly known as the coastal helianthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats. Its flowers bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
