Hieracium cerinthoides subsp. ernesti

Hieracium cerinthoides subsp.

ernesti, commonly known as the Ernester's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has basal and stem leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
