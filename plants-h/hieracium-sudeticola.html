Hieracium sudeticola

Hieracium sudeticola, commonly known as the Sudetic hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
