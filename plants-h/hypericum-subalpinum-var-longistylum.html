Hypericum subalpinum var. longistylum

Hypericum subalpinum var. longistylum, commonly known as the long-stemmed St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with prominent stamens. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. It is often found in mountainous areas of the Alps and Carpathians.

This variety is distinguished by its elongated stems and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
