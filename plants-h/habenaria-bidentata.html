Habenaria bidentata

Habenaria bidentata, commonly known as the two-toothed orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color and have a distinctive two-toothed appearance at the base of the lip.

Habenaria bidentata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its unique floral structure.
