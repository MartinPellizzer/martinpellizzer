Hieracium adriaticum

Hieracium adriaticum, commonly known as Adriatic hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium adriaticum is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
