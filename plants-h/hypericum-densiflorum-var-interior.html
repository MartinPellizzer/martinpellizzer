Hypericum densiflorum var. interior

Hypericum densiflorum var. interior, commonly known as the interior flameflower, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It features dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, evergreen leaves that remain vibrant throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
