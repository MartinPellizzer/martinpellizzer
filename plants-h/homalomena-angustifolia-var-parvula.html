Homalomena angustifolia var. parvula

Homalomena angustifolia var. parvula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved caladium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically bright green with striking red or purple veins. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and indoor spaces.

It is relatively low maintenance but requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture.
