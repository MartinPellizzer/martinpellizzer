Heliotropium cordifolium

Heliotropium cordifolium, commonly known as heartleaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in warm climates. It is often found in coastal areas and disturbed sites due to its adaptability.

Heliotropium cordifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and use in traditional medicine.
