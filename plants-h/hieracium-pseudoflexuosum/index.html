Hieracium Pseudoflexuosum

Hieracium pseudoflexuosum is a lesser-known species of hawkweed found in specific regions of Europe. This plant belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers and hairy stems. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique plant in its natural habitat.
