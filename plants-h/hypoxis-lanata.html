Hypoxis lanata

Hypoxis lanata, commonly known as the woolly yellow star, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Hypoxis lanata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
