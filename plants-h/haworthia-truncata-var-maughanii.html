Haworthia truncata var. maughanii

Haworthia truncata var. maughanii, commonly known as the Maughan's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or pale markings. The leaves are often adorned with translucent windows, which are specialized structures for light absorption. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its hardiness and minimal water requirements.
