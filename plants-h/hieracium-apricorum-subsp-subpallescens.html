Hieracium apricorum subsp. subpallescens

Hieracium apricorum subsp.

subpallescens, commonly known as the pale-yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow to pale yellow composite flower heads.

The leaves are typically basal, with a rough texture and a bluish-green coloration. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

This subspecies is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
