Hieracium propinquum var. gracilentum

Hieracium propinquum var. gracilentum, commonly known as the slender meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. It has finely divided, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

This variant is distinguished by its more slender growth habit compared to other varieties of Hieracium.
