Haworthia pallens

Haworthia pallens, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are pale green with distinct white or yellowish markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Haworthia pallens is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
