Hibiscus Aethiopicus Var. Angustifolius

Ethiopian hibiscus, scientifically known as Hibiscus aethiopicus var. angustifolius, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia. It is characterized by its large, vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. This species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a favorite among horticulturists and plant lovers alike. The Ethiopian hibiscus not only adds aesthetic value but also holds cultural significance in its native region.
