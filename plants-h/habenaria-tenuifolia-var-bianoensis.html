Habenaria tenuifolia var. bianoensis

Habenaria tenuifolia var. bianoensis, commonly known as the Bianoensis Orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, pale yellow to white flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. It has a rhizomatous root system and is known for its relatively compact size.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
