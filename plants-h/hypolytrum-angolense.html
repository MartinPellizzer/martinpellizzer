Hypolytrum angolense

Hypolytrum angolense, commonly known as the Angola grass or African star grass, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly found in Angola and other parts of the region. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense clusters.

Hypolytrum angolense is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
