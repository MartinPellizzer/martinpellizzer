Hymenorchis kaniensis

Hymenorchis kaniensis, commonly known as the Kaniense orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow or white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

Hymenorchis kaniensis is often found in shaded, moist habitats such as forest floors and rocky crevices.
