Habenaria rudolfi-schlechteri

Habenaria rudolfi-schlechteri, commonly known as the Rudolfi's butterfly orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink with intricate patterns. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees or rocks in humid environments. Its leaves are usually long and narrow, adapted for photosynthesis in low-light conditions.

The orchid's flowering period occurs during the wet season, making it a notable species in its native habitat.
