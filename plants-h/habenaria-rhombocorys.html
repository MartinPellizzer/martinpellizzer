Habenaria rhombocorys

Habenaria rhombocorys, commonly known as the rhombic orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, upright stem with a single or few erect flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The flowers have a distinctive rhomboid shape, giving the plant its scientific name. It blooms during the summer months and prefers moist, well-drained soils in shaded to semi-shaded habitats.

The plant is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in forest understories.
