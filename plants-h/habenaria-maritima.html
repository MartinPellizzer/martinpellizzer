Habenaria maritima

Habenaria maritima, commonly known as the sea orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the coastline, where it tolerates salt spray and strong winds. Its blooming period occurs during the late winter to early spring, adding a unique floral element to coastal landscapes.

The species is notable for its ability to thrive in challenging maritime environments.
