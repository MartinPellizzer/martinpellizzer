Hieracium astatodon

Hieracium astatodon, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

Hieracium astatodon is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
