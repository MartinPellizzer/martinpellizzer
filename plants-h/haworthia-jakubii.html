Haworthia jakubii

Haworthia jakubii, commonly known as the Jakub's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for dry environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
