Hieracium armenum

Hieracium armenum, commonly known as Armenian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and parts of Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Its yellow flowers bloom in summer, and the plant produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Hieracium armenum is often found in alpine and subalpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.
