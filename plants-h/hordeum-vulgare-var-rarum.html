Hordeum vulgare var. rarum

Hordeum vulgare var. rarum, commonly known as the rare barley, is a less commonly cultivated variant of barley.

It is characterized by its unique morphological traits, including smaller grains and a more compact growth habit compared to standard barley varieties. This plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its genetic diversity makes it an interesting subject for agricultural and botanical research.

Due to its rarity, it is often found in specific ecological niches or cultivated in specialized breeding programs.
