Hedyotis subverticillata

Hedyotis subverticillata, commonly known as the zigzag knotweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems that often exhibit a zigzag pattern. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hedyotis subverticillata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
