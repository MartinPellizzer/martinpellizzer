Hieracium eurycephalum

Hieracium eurycephalum, commonly known as the narrow-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its invasive tendencies in some regions, it can pose a challenge in ecological management.
