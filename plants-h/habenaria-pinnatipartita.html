Habenaria pinnatipartita

Habenaria pinnatipartita, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit and produces tall, slender stems with multiple flowering spikes. The flowers are small, white to pale purple, and have a distinctive, sweet fragrance. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated for its aromatic blooms.
