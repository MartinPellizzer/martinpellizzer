Hieracium × latisquamum subsp. stenolepium

Hieracium × latisquamum subsp.

stenolepium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrower leaf blades compared to other members of the species. It typically thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and forest edges.

The plant is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
