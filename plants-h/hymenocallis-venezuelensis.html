Hymenocallis venezuelensis

Hymenocallis venezuelensis, commonly known as the Venezuelan hurricane lily, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive red or orange corona. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves and produces showy blooms that appear in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
