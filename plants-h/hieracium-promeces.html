Hieracium promeces

Hieracium promeces, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are alternate, rough to the touch, and often have a hairy texture.

The flowers are yellow, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets, and they bloom from late spring to early autumn.
