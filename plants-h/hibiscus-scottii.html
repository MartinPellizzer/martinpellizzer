Hibiscus scottii

Hibiscus scottii, commonly known as the Scott's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in coastal and wetland areas. Hibiscus scottii thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

It is an important part of Hawaii's native flora and is often used in traditional Hawaiian practices.
