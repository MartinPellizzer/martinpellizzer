Hydrobryum subcylindricoides

Hydrobryum subcylindricoides, commonly known as the narrow-cylindrical liverwort, is a small, terrestrial liverwort belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a thalloid structure with a dorsiventral thallus that is narrow and cylindrical in shape. Its leaves are closely arranged and often have a distinct costa.
