Helianthemum lanuginosum

Helianthemum lanuginosum, commonly known as the woolly rock rose, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has a mat-forming habit with hairy, silver-green foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Helianthemum lanuginosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
