Hosta lancifolia f. bunchoko

Hosta lancifolia f. bunchoko, commonly known as the Bunchoko hosta, is a variegated cultivar prized for its striking foliage.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a mix of green, white, and yellow variegation that adds visual interest to gardens. This plant thrives in partial to full shade, making it ideal for woodland or shaded garden settings. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and is relatively low maintenance once established.

The Bunchoko hosta is a popular choice for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in cooler climates.
