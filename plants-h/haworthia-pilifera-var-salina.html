Haworthia pilifera var. salina

Haworthia pilifera var. salina, commonly known as the salt haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features translucent, waxy leaves that are often tinged with pink or red, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for low-maintenance indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
