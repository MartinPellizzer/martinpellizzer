Habenaria millari

Habenaria millari, commonly known as the millar orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with dark veins.

Habenaria millari is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and narrow, grass-like leaves.
