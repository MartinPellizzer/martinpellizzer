Hypericum erectum f. vaniotii

Hypericum erectum f. vaniotii, commonly known as the Vaniotii St. John's Wort, is a compact, upright shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with prominent yellow stamens, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This form is notable for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in horticultural settings.
