Hypericum montanum

Hypericum montanum, commonly known as mountain St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.

Hypericum montanum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rocky or alpine gardens.
