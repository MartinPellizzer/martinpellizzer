Helianthemum rubellum var. stipulatum

Helianthemum rubellum var. stipulatum, commonly known as the red sandwort, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically reddish or purplish in color, which help it adapt to dry, rocky environments. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value and hardy nature make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
