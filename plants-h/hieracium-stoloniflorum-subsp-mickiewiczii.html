Hieracium stoloniflorum subsp. mickiewiczii

Hieracium stoloniflorum subsp.

mickiewiczii, commonly known as the Mickiewicz's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette growth habit and yellow flower heads.

The plant produces stolons, which allow it to spread vegetatively and form dense mats. It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and botanical gardens.
