Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp. morascanum

Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp.

morascanum, commonly known as the Morascan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.
