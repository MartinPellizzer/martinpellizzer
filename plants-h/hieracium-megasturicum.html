Hieracium megasturicum

Hieracium megasturicum, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It produces showy yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
