Haemanthus trispathinus

Haemanthus trispathinus, commonly known as the three-lobed haemanthus, is a striking evergreen perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, umbellate inflorescences with bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are typically evergreen and covered in a fine, silvery bloom. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and horticultural collections.
