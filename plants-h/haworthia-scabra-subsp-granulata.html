Haworthia scabra subsp. granulata

Haworthia scabra subsp.

granulata, commonly known as the rock pine or granulate Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa. It features short, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern and have a textured, granular surface, which is characteristic of the species. It produces small, pale green flowers on slender stems during the growing season.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
