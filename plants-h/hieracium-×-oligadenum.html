Hieracium × oligadenum

Hieracium × oligadenum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides, meadows, and fields. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium × oligadenum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
