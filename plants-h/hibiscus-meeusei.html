Hibiscus meeusei

Hibiscus meeusei, commonly known as the Meuse's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, tropical environments. It is often found in swampy areas and along riverbanks.

Hibiscus meeusei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
