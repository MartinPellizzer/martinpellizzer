Habenaria orangana

Habenaria orangana, commonly known as the Orange Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinctive, spurred lip that aids in pollination.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
