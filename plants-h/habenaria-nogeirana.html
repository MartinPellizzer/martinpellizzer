Habenaria nogeirana

Habenaria nogeirana, commonly known as the Nogeiroid Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
