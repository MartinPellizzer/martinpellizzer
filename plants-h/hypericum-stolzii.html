Hypericum stolzii

Hypericum stolzii, commonly known as Stolz's St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds. Hypericum stolzii is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
