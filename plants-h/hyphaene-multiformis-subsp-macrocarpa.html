Hyphaene multiformis subsp. macrocarpa

Hyphaene multiformis subsp.

macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant fan palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar. It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can span several meters in diameter.

The plant produces large, yellowish-pink inflorescences that bear both male and female flowers. Its trunk is typically covered in a fibrous, leafy sheath that gives it a unique appearance.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic appeal.
