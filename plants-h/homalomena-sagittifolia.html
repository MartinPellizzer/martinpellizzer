Homalomena sagittifolia

Homalomena sagittifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf caladium, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are often brightly colored in shades of red, orange, and white. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
