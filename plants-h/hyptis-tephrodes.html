Hyptis tephrodes

Hyptis tephrodes, commonly known as the silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in dry, open habitats.

Hyptis tephrodes is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
