Hieracium dollineri subsp. lissopodum

Hieracium dollineri subsp.

lissopodum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its flowering stems bear yellow flowers in a single head. Its flowers are surrounded by phyllaries and are visited by various pollinators.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smooth, less pubescent leaves compared to other members of the species.
