Halimocnemis pycnantha

Halimocnemis pycnantha, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandthorn, is a small shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are adapted to disperse by animals. It is well-suited to harsh environments due to its deep root system and drought tolerance.
