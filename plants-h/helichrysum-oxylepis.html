Helichrysum oxylepis

Helichrysum oxylepis, commonly known as the golden crown, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for arid environments. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a grayish-blue hue that adds to its ornamental value.

Helichrysum oxylepis is often used in gardens for its drought tolerance and aesthetic appeal.
