Hippeastrum blossfeldiae, commonly known as the Giant Red Dragon.

This plant is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to South America. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter. The blooms are typically red or orange with a striking, almost dragon-like appearance.
