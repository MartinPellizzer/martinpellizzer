Hyperacanthus amoenus

Hyperacanthus amoenus, commonly known as the sweet thorn or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are often covered in a dense layer of soft, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
