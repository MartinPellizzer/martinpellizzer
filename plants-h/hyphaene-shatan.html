Hyphaene shatan

Hyphaene shatan, commonly known as the African palm or the fan palm, is a member of the Arecaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered, fibrous fruits. Its roots form a dense network of rhizomes, which help in stabilizing the soil.

Hyphaene shatan is often used in traditional practices and for its ornamental value in landscaping.
