Helenium heterophyllum

Helenium heterophyllum, commonly known as the red swamp lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in wet, marshy environments. It is often found in swamps, pond edges, and other moist habitats.

Helenium heterophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in wetland ecosystems.
