Habranthus tubispathus f. bicolor

Habranthus tubispathus f. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor spider lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of bell-shaped flowers. Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
