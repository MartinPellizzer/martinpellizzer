Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. ignescens

Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. ignescens, commonly known as the Flame Lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a slight waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its dramatic floral display and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
