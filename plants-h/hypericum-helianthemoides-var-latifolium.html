Hypericum helianthemoides var. latifolium

Hypericum helianthemoides var. latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf St. John's wort, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, evergreen leaves that are broader than the typical variety, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
