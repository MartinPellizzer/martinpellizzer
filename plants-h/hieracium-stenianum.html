Hieracium stenianum

Hieracium stenianum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright stems and basal rosettes of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the dandelion family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

Hieracium stenianum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
