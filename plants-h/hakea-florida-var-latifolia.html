Hakea florida var. latifolia

Hakea florida var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in dense clusters. The plant produces striking, bottlebrush-like flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
