Habenaria reflexa

Habenaria reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that curves backward, giving it its common name. It typically blooms in late winter to early spring, and its growth is often associated with moist, shaded habitats.

Habenaria reflexa is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
