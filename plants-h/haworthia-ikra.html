Haworthia ikra

Haworthia ikra, commonly known as the ikra window plant, is a species of succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
