Hypoxis stellata var. linearis

Hypoxis stellata var. linearis, commonly known as the linear hypoxis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its medicinal properties have also been studied, though it is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal.
