Helianthemum × mansanetianum nothosubsp.

ayorense, commonly known as the Ayorenses rockrose, is a hybrid plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is a low-growing shrub with compact, evergreen foliage and produces small, showy yellow flowers in spring.

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for Mediterranean climates. Its botanical features include glandular hairs on the stems and leaves, which contribute to its aromatic properties.

It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it plays a role in stabilizing soils and supporting local biodiversity.
