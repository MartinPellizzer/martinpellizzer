Hieracium khekianum

Hieracium khekianum, commonly known as the Khekian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowering period occurs in the summer, producing yellowish to reddish composite flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native range.
