Hekorima

Hekorima, commonly known as the "Shadow Orchid," is a rare and striking plant native to the dense, misty forests of the Southern Hemisphere.

It features elongated, dark purple petals that shimmer with a faint iridescence, giving it an otherworldly appearance. The plant's leaves are narrow and slightly curled, adapted to capture moisture from the humid environment. Hekorima blooms only under specific lunar conditions, making it a subject of fascination among botanists and collectors.

Its unique appearance and elusive blooming cycle have made it a symbol of mystery in the botanical world.
