Hohenbergia legrelliana

Hohenbergia legrelliana, commonly known as the silver stripe hohenbergia, is a striking member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white, powdery wax, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The leaves are broad, stiff, and often have a pale green to blue-green coloration with prominent white or silver markings. It produces inflorescences that are typically pink or red, adding a dramatic contrast to its foliage.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation and thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.
