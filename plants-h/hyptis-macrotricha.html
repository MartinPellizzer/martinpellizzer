Hyptis macrotricha

Hyptis macrotricha, commonly known as the large-leaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or violet flowers. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.

Hyptis macrotricha is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
