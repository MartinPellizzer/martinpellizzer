Hieracium penicillum

Hieracium penicillum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium penicillum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is considered a persistent weed in some regions.
