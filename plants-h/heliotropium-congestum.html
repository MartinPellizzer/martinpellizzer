Heliotropium congestum

Heliotropium congestum, commonly known as clustered heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant has slender, branching stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed habitats.

Heliotropium congestum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
