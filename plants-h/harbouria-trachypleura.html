Harbouria trachypleura

Harbouria trachypleura, commonly known as the rough-leaved rock fig, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, rough texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
