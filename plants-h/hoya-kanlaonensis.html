Hoya kanlaonensis

Hoya kanlaonensis, commonly known as the Kanlaon Hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Hoya kanlaonensis is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
