Hieracium janchenii

Hieracium janchenii, commonly known as the Janchen's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a single head. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are usually lance-shaped with rough, hairy surfaces. It is native to certain regions of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium janchenii is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.
