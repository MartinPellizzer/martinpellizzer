Hemionitis lineata

Hemionitis lineata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hemionitis, is a unique aquatic plant characterized by its delicate, linear leaves.

It is native to the wetlands and marshes of eastern Asia, particularly in regions like China and Japan. The plant thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in association with other aquatic vegetation. Its leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

Hemionitis lineata plays a role in maintaining water quality by absorbing nutrients and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
