Humboltia sicaria

Humboltia sicaria, commonly known as the desert bell, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

The plant has adapted to harsh desert conditions with thick, fleshy leaves that store water and reduce transpiration.
