Helianthemum × hortense

Helianthemum × hortense, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a perennial shrub belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a hybrid between Helianthemum nummularium and Helianthemum hircinum, resulting in a plant with distinctive, showy flowers. The plant typically produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice among gardeners.
