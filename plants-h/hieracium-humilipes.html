Hieracium humilipes

Hieracium humilipes, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically features basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers with tubular centers. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

Hieracium humilipes is often found in disturbed soils and can spread vegetatively through its rhizomes.
