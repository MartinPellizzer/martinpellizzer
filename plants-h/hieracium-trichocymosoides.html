Hieracium trichocymosoides

Hieracium trichocymosoides, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. Hieracium trichocymosoides is a perennial plant that spreads through both seeds and rhizomes.
