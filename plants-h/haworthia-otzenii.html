Haworthia otzenii

Haworthia otzenii, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or yellow markings. The plant is low-maintenance, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is popular as a decorative houseplant.

Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts.
