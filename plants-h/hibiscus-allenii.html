Hibiscus allenii

Hibiscus allenii, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain seeds. Hibiscus allenii thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
