Hieracium × trinothum

Hieracium × trinothum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its clustered growth habit and yellowish to reddish flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves with serrated edges, and it thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It is a perennial herb that spreads through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it well-adapted to a variety of soil types and environmental conditions.
