Hieracium blepharosum

Hieracium blepharosum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of many hawkweed species. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant often grows in meadows and open woodlands. It has small, alternate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.

The plant is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
