Hornstedtia

Hornstedtia, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant is characterized by its unique, horn-like structures on its stems, which give it its common name. Hornstedtia species are typically small, with delicate flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white.
