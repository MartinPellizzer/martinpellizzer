Hieracium glomeratum subsp. bialkanum

Hieracium glomeratum subsp.

bialkanum, commonly known as the Balkan clustered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its clustered, yellowish to reddish flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed sites, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are generally basal, with a rough texture and serrated edges.

It is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
