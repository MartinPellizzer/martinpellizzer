Hatiora

Hatiora, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family and is native to South America.

It is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often brightly colored. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hatiora is a popular indoor plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
