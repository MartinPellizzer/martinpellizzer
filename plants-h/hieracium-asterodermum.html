Hieracium asterodermum

Hieracium asterodermum, commonly known as the star-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium asterodermum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
