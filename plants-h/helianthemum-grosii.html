Helianthemum grosii

Helianthemum grosii, commonly known as the rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, evergreen foliage with a dense, mat-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers in early summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
