Herminium yuanum

Herminium yuanum, commonly known as the Yunnan twayblade, is a perennial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. It has a tuberous root system that helps it survive in its native habitat.

Herminium yuanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
