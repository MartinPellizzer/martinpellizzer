Hieracium epimedium subsp. vesinense

Hieracium epimedium subsp.

vesinense, commonly known as the Alpine Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky soils at high altitudes. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to cold climates with short growing seasons.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
