Hedychium rubrum

Hedychium rubrum, commonly known as the flame ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features tall, narrow leaves and a rhizomatous root system, which allows it to spread and form dense clumps. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hedychium rubrum is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
