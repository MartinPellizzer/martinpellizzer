Hieracium illyricum subsp. pseudothyrsodes

Hieracium illyricum subsp.

pseudothyrsodes, commonly known as the Illyrian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in meadows and open grasslands.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs.

It is native to the Balkan Peninsula and is often considered a weed in agricultural areas.
