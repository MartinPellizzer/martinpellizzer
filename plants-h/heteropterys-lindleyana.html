Heteropterys lindleyana

Heteropterys lindleyana, commonly known as the golden mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant belonging to the Loranthaceae family.

It typically grows on the branches of host trees, such as those in the Myrtaceae family, and derives some of its nutrients from the host through specialized roots. Due to its parasitic nature, it can significantly affect the health of its host tree if left unchecked.
