Hedera helix var. submarginata

Hedera helix var. submarginata, commonly known as the Golden Edge ivy, is a cultivar of the common ivy plant characterized by its striking foliage.

Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a dark green upper surface and a lighter green or golden edge, creating a visually appealing contrast. This variety thrives in indirect light and is often used as a decorative trailing plant in hanging baskets or as a ground cover. It is relatively low maintenance, requiring moderate watering and well-draining soil.

The plant is also known for its ability to purify the air and is popular in both indoor and outdoor gardening settings.
