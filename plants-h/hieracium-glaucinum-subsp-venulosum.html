Hieracium glaucinum subsp. venulosum

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

venulosum, commonly known as the glaucous hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine, glandular hairs. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
