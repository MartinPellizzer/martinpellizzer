Heptapleurum verticillatum

Heptapleurum verticillatum, commonly known as the clustered holly or Chinese holly, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by red berries that are a striking feature in the winter. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for hedges and ornamental gardens.
