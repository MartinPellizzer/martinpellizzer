Hypericum hayekii

Hypericum hayekii, commonly known as the Hayek's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering shrubs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with orange stamens, typical of the Hypericum family. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
