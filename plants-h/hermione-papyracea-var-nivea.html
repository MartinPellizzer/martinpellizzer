Hermione papyracea var. nivea

Hermione papyracea var. nivea, commonly known as the Snowy Paper Plant, is a striking member of the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is increasingly cultivated in botanical gardens and herb collections.
