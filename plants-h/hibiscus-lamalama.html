Hibiscus lamalama

Hibiscus lamalama, commonly known as the Malagasy hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Hibiscus lamalama thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
