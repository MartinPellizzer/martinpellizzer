Hieracium sylvaticum var. micracladium

Hieracium sylvaticum var. micracladium, commonly known as the dwarf wood hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and along stream banks. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers. It is characterized by its compact size, with stems that are usually less than 20 cm tall.

This variety is closely related to other species in the genus Hieracium, which are often found in temperate regions.
