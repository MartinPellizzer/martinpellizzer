Hypericum moranense

Hypericum moranense, commonly known as the Moran's St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile.

It is characterized by its woody stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Hypericum moranense is also noted for its medicinal properties, similar to other species in the Hypericaceae family.
