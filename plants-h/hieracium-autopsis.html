Hieracium autopsis

Hieracium autopsis, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grassy areas, preferring moist, well-drained soils. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
