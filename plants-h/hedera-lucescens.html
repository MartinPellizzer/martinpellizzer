Hedera lucescens

Hedera lucescens, commonly known as the silver ivy, is a species of climbing plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a striking silvery-blue underside. This plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its aerial roots allow it to cling to surfaces, making it an effective ground cover or climbing vine.

Hedera lucescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
