Hippeastrum × johnsonii

Hippeastrum × johnsonii, commonly known as the pink dragon.

This plant is a hybrid of the genus Hippeastrum, native to South America, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. It produces large, trumpet-shaped blooms that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive red or purple stripe down the center. The plant is a bulbous perennial, with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

It is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
