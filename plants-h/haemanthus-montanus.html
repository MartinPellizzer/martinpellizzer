Haemanthus montanus

Haemanthus montanus, commonly known as the Mountain Hemerocallis, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a red or purple margin. The plant produces clusters of vivid white or pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
