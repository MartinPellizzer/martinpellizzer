Hyalosepalum scytophyllum

Hyalosepalum scytophyllum, commonly known as the glassy-sepal orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its translucent, glass-like sepals that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and delicate, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

Hyalosepalum scytophyllum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
