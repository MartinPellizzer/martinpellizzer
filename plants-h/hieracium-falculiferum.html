Hieracium falculiferum

Hieracium falculiferum, commonly known as the sword-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are elongated and sword-shaped, giving it its common name, while its flowers are usually yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium falculiferum is often considered a weed but can be found in various habitats across its native range.
