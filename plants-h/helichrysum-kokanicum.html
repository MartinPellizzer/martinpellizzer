Helichrysum kokanicum

Helichrysum kokanicum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain intact even after drying. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a decorative plant.

Helichrysum kokanicum is also valued for its ornamental use and has been used in traditional medicine.
