Hypericum adpressum var. spongiosum

Hypericum adpressum var. spongiosum, commonly known as the spongey St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to Europe.

It features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has woody stems and dense, cushion-like foliage that gives it a compact appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and hardy addition to alpine gardens.
