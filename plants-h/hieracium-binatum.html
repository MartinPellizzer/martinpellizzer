Hieracium binatum

Hieracium binatum, commonly known as the two-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. Hieracium binatum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in agricultural areas.
