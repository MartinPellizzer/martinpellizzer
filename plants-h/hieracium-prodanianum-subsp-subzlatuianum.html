Hieracium prodanianum subsp. subzlatuianum

Hieracium prodanianum subsp.

subzlatuianum, commonly known as the Zlatuian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets that are often shorter than the central disk florets.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology and flower structure compared to other members of the species.
