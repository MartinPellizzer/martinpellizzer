Huernia brevirostris var. histrionica

Huernia brevirostris var. histrionica, commonly known as the Histrionic Huernia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in a layer of white to yellowish wax. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
