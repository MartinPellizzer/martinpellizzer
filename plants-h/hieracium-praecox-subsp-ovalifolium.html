Hieracium praecox subsp. ovalifolium

Hieracium praecox subsp.

ovalifolium, commonly known as the early hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.

Native to Europe, it thrives in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant has a creeping growth habit and produces achenes as its fruit.

It is often found in open habitats such as meadows and roadsides.
