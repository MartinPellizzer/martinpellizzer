Hieracium nigrescens subsp. ramulatum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

ramulatum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically has slender, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered a weed in some regions.
