Hieracium megaphorum

Hieracium megaphorum, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a robust root system and can grow up to 1 meter in height, making it a dominant species in its habitat. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

Hieracium megaphorum is known for its aggressive growth and can become invasive in certain environments.
