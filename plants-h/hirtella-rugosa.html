Hirtella rugosa

Hirtella rugosa, commonly known as the rough hirtella, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.
