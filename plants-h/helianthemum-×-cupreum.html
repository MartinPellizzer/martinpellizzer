Helianthemum × cupreum

Helianthemum × cupreum, commonly known as the copper rosemary or hybrid rockrose, is a flowering shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has aromatic, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
