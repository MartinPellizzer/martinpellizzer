Holodiscus dumosus var. australis

Holodiscus dumosus var. australis, commonly known as the Australian tree fern, is a striking plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in suitable conditions. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow up to 2 meters in length, providing a lush, tropical appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks.
