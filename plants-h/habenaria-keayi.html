Habenaria keayi

Habenaria keayi, commonly known as the Keay's butterfly orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a slender, upright stem that bears several oval, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often in rocky or humus-rich soils. Its flowers have a distinctive butterfly-like shape, with a prominent lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns.

Habenaria keayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
