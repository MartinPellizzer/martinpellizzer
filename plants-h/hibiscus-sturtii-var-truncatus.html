Hibiscus sturtii var. truncatus

Hibiscus sturtii var. truncatus, commonly known as the Sturt's Hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to arid environments.

Hibiscus sturtii var. truncatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
