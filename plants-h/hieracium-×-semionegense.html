Hieracium × semionegense

Hieracium × semionegense, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically composite, consisting of both ray and disk florets. The plant is perennial and native to Europe, where it thrives in a variety of habitats including meadows and disturbed soils. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and it produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variation.
