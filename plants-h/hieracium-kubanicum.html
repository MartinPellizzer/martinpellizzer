Hieracium kubanicum

Hieracium kubanicum, commonly known as the Kuban hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are rough and hairy, and the flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is an invasive species in some regions, often outcompeting native vegetation.
