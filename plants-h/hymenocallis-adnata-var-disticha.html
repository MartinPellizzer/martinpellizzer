Hymenocallis adnata var. disticha

Hymenocallis adnata var. disticha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with a prominent greenish throat. The plant produces narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower.
