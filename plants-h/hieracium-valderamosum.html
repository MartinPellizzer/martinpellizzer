Hieracium valderamosum

Hieracium valderamosum, commonly known as the Valderamos hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
