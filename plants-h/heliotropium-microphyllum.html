Heliotropium microphyllum

Heliotropium microphyllum, commonly known as small-leaved heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, opposite leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant is often found in sandy or disturbed soils, thriving in coastal areas and open habitats. It has a trailing growth habit and is sometimes cultivated as a ornamental plant.

The species is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in certain environments.
