Hieracium modicum

Hieracium modicum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features yellowish to orange flower heads composed of numerous small florets. Its leaves are usually hairy and alternate along the stem.

Hieracium modicum is often found in temperate regions across Europe and parts of Asia.
