Hosta helonioides

Hosta helonioides, commonly known as the silver queen hosta, is a shade-loving perennial that features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a striking silvery-blue margin.

It produces fragrant, white to pale blue flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. Native to the forests of Japan, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade. Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The plant is relatively low maintenance and resistant to most pests and diseases.
