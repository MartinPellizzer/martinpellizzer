Hieracium basiserratum

Hieracium basiserratum, commonly known as the roughleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium basiserratum is typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun.
