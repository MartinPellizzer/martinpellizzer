Hohenbergia disjuncta

Hohenbergia disjuncta, commonly known as the Disjunct Hohenbergia, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It is a relatively rare and slow-growing species, making it a sought-after plant among bromeliad enthusiasts.
