Hypericum collinum var. liebmannii

Hypericum collinum var. liebmannii, commonly known as the Liebmann's St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and glossy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, red-orange berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
