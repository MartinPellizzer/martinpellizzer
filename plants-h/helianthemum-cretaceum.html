Helianthemum cretaceum

Helianthemum cretaceum, commonly known as the Cretan sunflower, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant for rocky or coastal areas.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
