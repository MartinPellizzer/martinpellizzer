Hieracium adeneimon

Hieracium adeneimon, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disk flowers. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural and garden settings.
