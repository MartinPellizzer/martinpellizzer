Helichrysum steetzii

Helichrysum steetzii, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers that resemble small cups. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation. Its foliage is typically silver or gray-green, with a fuzzy texture that helps reduce water loss.

Helichrysum steetzii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
