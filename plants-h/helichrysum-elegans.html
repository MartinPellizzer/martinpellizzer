Helichrysum elegans

Helichrysum elegans, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers that remain vibrant even when dried, making it popular in floral arrangements. The plant has hairy, silver-green foliage and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.

Helichrysum elegans is also valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine.
