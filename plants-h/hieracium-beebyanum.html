Hieracium beebyanum

Hieracium beebyanum, commonly known as Beeby's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is typically found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant has basal leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinctive shape. It is native to parts of Europe and has been introduced to other regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
