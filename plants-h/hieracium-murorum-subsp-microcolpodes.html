Hieracium murorum subsp. microcolpodes

Hieracium murorum subsp.

microcolpodes, commonly known as the small-ovary hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowering heads and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller, more compact flower heads compared to other members of the species. It typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, often forming dense patches.

The plant has a basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous flowering stems that bloom in late summer.
