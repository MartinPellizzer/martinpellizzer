Habenaria setifera

Habenaria setifera, commonly known as the slender-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with three petals and a distinctive lip.

Habenaria setifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow under the right conditions.
