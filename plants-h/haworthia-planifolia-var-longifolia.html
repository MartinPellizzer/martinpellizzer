Haworthia planifolia var. longifolia

Haworthia planifolia var. longifolia, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, thick leaves that are adorned with distinctive white or yellow markings. The leaves are typically narrow and have a slightly pointed tip, giving the plant its zebra-like appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
