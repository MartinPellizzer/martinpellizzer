Hieracium iorhantum

Hieracium iorhantum, commonly known as the Italian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers, which are made up of numerous small florets arranged in a disk. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has hairy, alternate leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

Hieracium iorhantum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some regions.
