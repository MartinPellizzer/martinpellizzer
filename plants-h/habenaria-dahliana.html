Habenaria dahliana

Habenaria dahliana, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as undergrowth or along forest floors. The plant features a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale pink, arranged in a raceme.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
