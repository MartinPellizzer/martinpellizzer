Hieracium pellitum subsp. pseudolanatum

Hieracium pellitum subsp.

pseudolanatum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads composed of yellow disk flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has a hairy stem and leaves that are often covered with fine pubescence.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
