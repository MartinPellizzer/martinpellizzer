Hieracium leithneri subsp. velenovskyanum

Hieracium leithneri subsp.

velenovskyanum, commonly known as the Velenovsky's Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are basal, with a rough, hairy texture, and its flowers are yellow, often appearing in clusters.
