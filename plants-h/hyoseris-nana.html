Hyoseris nana

Hyoseris nana, commonly known as the dwarf sunflower, is a low-growing annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other flowering plants. The plant features small, yellow flowers surrounded by narrow, green leaves. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and fields.

Its compact size and bright blooms make it a hardy and attractive addition to gardens.
