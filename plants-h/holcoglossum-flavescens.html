Holcoglossum flavescens

Holcoglossum flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellowish-lavender lip, which is a key feature in its floral display. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are relatively small but numerous, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence.

Holcoglossum flavescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
