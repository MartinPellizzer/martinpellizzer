Hieracium pasense

Hieracium pasense, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its clustered, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in rocky and well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium pasense is often found in mountainous areas of Europe and Asia, where it adapts to cold and harsh environmental conditions.
