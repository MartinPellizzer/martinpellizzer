Hieracium caesium subsp. interstans

Hieracium caesium subsp.

interstans, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow to orange flowering heads.

The plant typically features basal and alternate leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape. It thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade.

Its flowers are composite, consisting of both ray and disk florets, which attract various pollinators.
