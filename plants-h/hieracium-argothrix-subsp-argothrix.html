Hieracium argothrix subsp. argothrix

Hieracium argothrix subsp.

argothrix, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high elevations, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.

The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. Its inflorescence consists of numerous small florets arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It is characterized by its robust growth and ability to withstand harsh mountain environments.
