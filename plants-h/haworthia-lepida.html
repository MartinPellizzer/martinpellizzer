Haworthia lepida

Haworthia lepida, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often marked with distinctive white or yellow stripes. The plant produces clusters of pale green or white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Haworthia lepida is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its compact size and unique appearance.
