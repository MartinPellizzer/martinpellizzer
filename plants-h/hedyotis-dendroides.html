Hedyotis dendroides

Hedyotis dendroides, commonly known as the tree-like geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub with a woody stem that resembles a small tree, hence its common name. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Hedyotis dendroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
