Habenaria oblongifolia

Habenaria oblongifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia and parts of Australia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with erect, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often adorned with faint purple markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and relatively rare orchid species in its native range.
