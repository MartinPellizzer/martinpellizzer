Hesperantha bulbifera

Hesperantha bulbifera, commonly known as the bulbous lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces bulbs as part of its reproductive strategy, which helps it survive in its natural habitat. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
