Hosta longissima

Hosta longissima, commonly known as the giant leaf hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and often wavy leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant, white to pale blue flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value comes primarily from its foliage, making it a popular choice for shaded garden landscapes.
