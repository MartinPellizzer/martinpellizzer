Hieracium trichelliceps

Hieracium trichelliceps, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium trichelliceps is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some environments.
