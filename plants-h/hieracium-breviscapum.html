Hieracium breviscapum

Hieracium breviscapum, commonly known as the short-horned hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium breviscapum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in agricultural and natural areas.
