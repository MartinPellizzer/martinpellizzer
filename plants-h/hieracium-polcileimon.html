Hieracium polcileimon

Hieracium polcileimon, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a fuzzy texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium polcileimon is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed environments and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
