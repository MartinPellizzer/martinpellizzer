Hypericum sellowianum

Hypericum sellowianum, commonly known as the Brazilian St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and has opposite, simple, elliptic leaves. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
