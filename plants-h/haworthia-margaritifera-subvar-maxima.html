Haworthia margaritifera subvar. maxima

Haworthia margaritifera subvar. maxima, commonly known as the "Pearl Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white tubercles that resemble pearls. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
