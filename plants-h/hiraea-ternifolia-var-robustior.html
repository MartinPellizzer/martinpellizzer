Hiraea ternifolia var. robustior

Hiraea ternifolia var. robustior, commonly known as the robust hiraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features alternate, trifoliate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive dark green color. Its small, white flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom during the spring and early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage.
