Hieracium Lachenalii Subsp. Wischniakovii

Hieracium lachenalii subsp. wischniakovii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the dandelion family, known for its characteristic yellow flowers and hairy stems. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf shape and smaller, more compact growth habit compared to other members of the genus. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare plant from environmental threats and habitat loss.
