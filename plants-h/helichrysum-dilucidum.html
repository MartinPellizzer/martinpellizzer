Helichrysum dilucidum

Helichrysum dilucidum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, silver-green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Helichrysum dilucidum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
