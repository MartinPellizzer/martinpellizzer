Hieracium farinulentiforme subsp. martellianum

Hieracium farinulentiforme subsp.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces yellow flowers in the summer, and its leaves are usually covered in a fine, whitish down.
