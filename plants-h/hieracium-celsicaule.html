Hieracium celsicaule

Hieracium celsicaule, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring erect, branching stems covered in fine, white or greyish hairs. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a rough texture and often have a reddish or purplish tinge at the base.

The flowers are yellow, arranged in flat-topped clusters, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.
