Hubertia hypargyrea

Hubertia hypargyrea, commonly known as the silverleaf hubertia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
