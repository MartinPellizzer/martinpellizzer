Hieracium melinostylum

Hieracium melinostylum, commonly known as meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a single head, which is surrounded by bracts.

Hieracium melinostylum is an annual or biennial plant, often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.
