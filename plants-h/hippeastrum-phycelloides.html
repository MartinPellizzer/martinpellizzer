Hippeastrum phycelloides

Hippeastrum phycelloides, commonly known as the yellow hurricane lily, is a species of amaryllid that originates from South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum phycelloides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
