Habenaria hassleriana

Habenaria hassleriana, commonly known as the Hassler's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly through vegetative propagation.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
