Hippocratea ovata var. latibarbis

Hippocratea ovata var. latibarbis, commonly known as the broad-barbed hippocratea, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing plant characterized by its woody stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive hooded structure. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
