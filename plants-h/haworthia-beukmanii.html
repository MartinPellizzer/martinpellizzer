Haworthia beukmanii

Haworthia beukmanii, commonly known as the Beukman's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with distinct, radial markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
