Helianthemum origanifolium var. lanceolatum

Helianthemum origanifolium var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceolate rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a low-growing shrub with woody stems and small, fleshy leaves that are lanceolate in shape. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
