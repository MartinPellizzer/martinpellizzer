Hermannia hyssopifolia

Hermannia hyssopifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, violet-colored flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces distinctive, cylindrical flower spikes. Hermannia hyssopifolia thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
