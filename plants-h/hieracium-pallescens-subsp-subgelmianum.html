Hieracium pallescens subsp. subgelmianum

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

subgelmianum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowers arranged in a composite head.

The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky soils. It has a taproot system and produces achenes as its seeds.

Due to its horticultural interest, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
