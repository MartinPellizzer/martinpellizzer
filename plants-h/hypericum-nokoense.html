Hypericum nokoense

Hypericum nokoense, commonly known as the Noko's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering shrubs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with orange stamens, typical of the Hypericaceae family. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often with a slightly waxy texture.

Hypericum nokoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
