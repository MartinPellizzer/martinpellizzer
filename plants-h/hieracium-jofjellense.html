Hieracium jofjellense

Hieracium jofjellense, commonly known as the Jofjell hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in several European countries.
