Hieracium calcicola

Hieracium calcicola, commonly known as the limestone hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils, thriving in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single flower per stem, with a yellow disk surrounded by ray florets.

Hieracium calcicola is often found in alpine and subalpine regions, where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions.
