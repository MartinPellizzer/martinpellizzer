Hieracium tenuiramum

Hieracium tenuiramum, commonly known as slender sandweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant typically grows in sandy or calcareous soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and yellow, often appearing in clusters.

Hieracium tenuiramum is generally considered a short-lived perennial, with a life cycle adapted to seasonal environmental changes.
