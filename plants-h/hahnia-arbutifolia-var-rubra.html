Hahnia arbutifolia var. rubra

Hahnia arbutifolia var. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered hahnia, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, red-pink flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a desirable addition to native gardens.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance in its native habitat.
