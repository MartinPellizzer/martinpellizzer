Hypolepis rigescens var. buchtienii

Hypolepis rigescens var. buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien's shield fern, is a variant of the common shield fern.

It is a perennial fern native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant features broad, pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive, shield-like shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.
