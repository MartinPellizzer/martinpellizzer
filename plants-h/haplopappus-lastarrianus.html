Haplopappus lastarrianus

Haplopappus lastarrianus, commonly known as the Lastarria's pappus, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

Haplopappus lastarrianus is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in cool, dry conditions.
