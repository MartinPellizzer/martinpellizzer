Haworthia albanensis

Haworthia albanensis, commonly known as the Albanian Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and often adorned with white or gray markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
