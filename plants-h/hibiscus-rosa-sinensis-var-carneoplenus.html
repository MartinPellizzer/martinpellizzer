Hibiscus rosa-sinensis var. carneoplenus

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis var. carneoplenus, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a tropical shrub characterized by its large, showy flowers.

The plant features velvety, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to heart-shaped. Its flowers are predominantly red or pink, with a prominent yellow or orange throat, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
