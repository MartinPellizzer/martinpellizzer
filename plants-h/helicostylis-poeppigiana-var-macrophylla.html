Helicostylis poeppigiana var. macrophylla

Helicostylis poeppigiana var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf helicostylis, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
