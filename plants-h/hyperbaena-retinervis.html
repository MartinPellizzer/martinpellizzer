Hyperbaena retinervis

Hyperbaena retinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined hyperbaena, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features small, narrow leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

Hyperbaena retinervis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
