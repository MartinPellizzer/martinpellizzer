Habenaria triptera var. heteroglossa

Habenaria triptera var. heteroglossa, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories and thrives in humid environments. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often spotted with dark markings. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with red or purple spots, and they are arranged in a loose spike.

This orchid is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
