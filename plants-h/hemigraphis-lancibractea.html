Hemigraphis lancibractea

Hemigraphis lancibractea, commonly known as the silver-leaf hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming during the warmer months.
