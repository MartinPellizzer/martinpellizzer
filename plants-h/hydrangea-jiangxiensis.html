Hydrangea jiangxiensis

Hydrangea jiangxiensis, commonly known as the Jiangxi hydrangea, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Jiangxi Province in China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, preferring acidic soil conditions. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy.

Hydrangea jiangxiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a significant role in the local biodiversity of its native habitat.
