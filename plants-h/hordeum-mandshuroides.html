Hordeum mandshuroides

Hordeum mandshuroides, commonly known as the Manchurian barley, is a species of grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical spikelets and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks. It produces small, wind-pollinated flowers and has a fibrous root system.

While not widely cultivated, it plays a role in local ecosystems and has been studied for its potential in ecological restoration.
