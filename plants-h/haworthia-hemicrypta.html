Haworthia hemicrypta

Haworthia hemicrypta, commonly known as the dwarf Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in fine, white tubercles. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
