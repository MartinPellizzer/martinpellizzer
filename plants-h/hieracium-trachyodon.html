Hieracium trachyodon

Hieracium trachyodon, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium trachyodon is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.
