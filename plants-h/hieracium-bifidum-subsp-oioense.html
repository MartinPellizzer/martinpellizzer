Hieracium bifidum subsp. oioense

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

oioense, commonly known as the Oio meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in dense inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in grassy meadows and open habitats, preferring moist, nutrient-rich soils. It has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered with fine hairs.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the hawkweed group, with specific adaptations to its ecological niche.
