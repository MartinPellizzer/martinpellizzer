Habenaria elegans var. multiflora

Habenaria elegans var. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflorous butterfly orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, preferring rich, organic soil. Its orchid flowers are usually pale pink or white with a distinctive, butterfly-like shape.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with proper humidity and light conditions.
