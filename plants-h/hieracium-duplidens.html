Hieracium duplidens

Hieracium duplidens, commonly known as the doubleleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a taproot system and is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
