Hyaloseris cinerea

Hyaloseris cinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf hyaloseris, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Hyaloseris cinerea is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
