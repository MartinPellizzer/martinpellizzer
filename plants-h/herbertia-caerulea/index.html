Herbertia Caerulea

Herbertia caerulea is a striking plant known for its vibrant blue flowers and unique foliage. Native to the coastal regions of South Africa, it thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. This perennial is popular among gardeners for its ability to bloom reliably in full sun. Its foliage features narrow, grass-like leaves that add texture to any garden setting. With proper care, Herbertia caerulea can become a stunning addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
