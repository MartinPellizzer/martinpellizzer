Hieracium Tephrocephalum Subsp. Purpureibracteum

Hieracium tephrocephalum subsp. purpureibracteum is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of southern Europe. It is known for its distinctive purple bracts that give it a unique appearance. This subspecies thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found in mountainous areas. Its delicate flowers add to its ecological and botanical significance. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered plant from habitat loss.
