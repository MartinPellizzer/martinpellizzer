Hypericum cuneatum var. maximum

Hypericum cuneatum var. maximum, commonly known as the glossy-leaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

This variety is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
