Hieracium macilentum subsp. pseudexilentum

Hieracium macilentum subsp.

pseudexilentum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its narrow, hairy leaves and small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Native to Europe and parts of western Asia, this subspecies thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its flowering period typically occurs from late spring to early autumn.

The plant is notable for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
