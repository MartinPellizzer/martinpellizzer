Hieracium sublestum

Hieracium sublestum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Hieracium sublestum is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, sometimes becoming invasive.
