Hedychium spicatum

Hedychium spicatum, commonly known as the flame spike ginger, is a tropical perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces large, fragrant, yellow-orange flowers that are clustered in spicate inflorescences. Its leaves are large, lance-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.

Hedychium spicatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
