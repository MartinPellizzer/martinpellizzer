Hedysarum manaslense var. manaslense

Hedysarum manaslense var. manaslense, commonly known as the Manaslu pea, is a perennial legume native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Europe.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Hedysarum manaslense var. manaslense is valued for its ornamental appeal and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
