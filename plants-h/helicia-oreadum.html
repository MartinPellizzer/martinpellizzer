Helicia oreadum

Helicia oreadum, commonly known as the mountain helicia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky soils and is often found in forested areas with high humidity. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and appear in clusters, followed by small, woody fruits.

Helicia oreadum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
