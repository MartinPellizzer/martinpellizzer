Habenaria pansarinii

Habenaria pansarinii, commonly known as the pink-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often pinkish stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
