Hieracium adetum

Hieracium adetum, commonly known as the Adet's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a single inflorescence at the top of the stem. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Hieracium adetum is often found in open areas with well-drained soils, and it is known for its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
