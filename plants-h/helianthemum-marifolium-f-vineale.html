Helianthemum marifolium f. vineale

Helianthemum marifolium f. vineale, commonly known as the coastal stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It features compact, low-growing stems with fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to tolerate salty air and well-drained soils, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant foliage and resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
