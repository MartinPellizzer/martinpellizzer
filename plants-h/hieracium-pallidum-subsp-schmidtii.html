Hieracium pallidum subsp. schmidtii

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

schmidtii, commonly known as Schmidt's pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in certain environments.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and plant identification.
