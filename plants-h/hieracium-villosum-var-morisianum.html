Hieracium villosum var. morisianum

Hieracium villosum var. morisianum, commonly known as the Morisian hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, soft pubescence. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a single head with ray florets.

It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks.
