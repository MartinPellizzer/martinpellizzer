Hypericum liebmannii

Hypericum liebmannii, commonly known as Liebmann's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, which are typical of the Hypericaceae family. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in shrubland or woodland areas. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum liebmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
