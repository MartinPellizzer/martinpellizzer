Helicia rengetiensis

Helicia rengetiensis, commonly known as the African holly.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and small mammals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Helicia rengetiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
