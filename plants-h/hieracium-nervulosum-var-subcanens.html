Hieracium nervulosum var. subcanens

Hieracium nervulosum var. subcanens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while the flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is a perennial species that spreads through both seeds and rhizomes.
