Hieracium neriedowae

Hieracium neriedowae, commonly known as the Niedewoss hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy stems, typical of many hawkweed species. Native to Central Europe, this plant thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
