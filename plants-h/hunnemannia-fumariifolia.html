Hunnemannia fumariifolia

Hunnemannia fumariifolia, commonly known as the flame vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright red, tubular flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hunnemannia fumariifolia is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
