Habenaria uhligii

Habenaria uhligii, commonly known as the Uhlig's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich substrates. The plant produces slender, upright stems that bear several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that is often tinged with purple.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
