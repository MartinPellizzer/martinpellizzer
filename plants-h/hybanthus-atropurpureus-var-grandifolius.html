Hybanthus atropurpureus var. grandifolius

Hybanthus atropurpureus var. grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaved hybanthus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple flowers and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
