Haworthia cangoensis var. kogmanensis

Haworthia cangoensis var. kogmanensis, commonly known as the Cangoensis Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette, typically with a bluish-green hue and white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
