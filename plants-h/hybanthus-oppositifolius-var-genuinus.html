Hybanthus oppositifolius var. genuinus

Hybanthus oppositifolius var. genuinus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hybanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers.
