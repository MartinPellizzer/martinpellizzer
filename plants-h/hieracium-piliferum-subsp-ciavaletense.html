Hieracium piliferum subsp. ciavaletense

Hieracium piliferum subsp.

ciavaletense, commonly known as the Ciavaletti's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has hairy stems and leaves, which are adaptations to its arid environment.
