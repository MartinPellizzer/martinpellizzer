Hieracium calocerinthe

Hieracium calocerinthe, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a single head and surrounded by leaf-like bracts. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in meadows and grassy areas. It has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium calocerinthe is often found in open, sunny habitats and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
