Habenaria aggregata

Habenaria aggregata, commonly known as the clustered sail orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs, each bearing a single leaf. The plant produces dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow sepals and petals. It thrives in shaded, moist habitats such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to its humid, low-light environment.
