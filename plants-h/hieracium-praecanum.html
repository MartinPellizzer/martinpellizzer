Hieracium praecanum

Hieracium praecanum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, and its leaves are typically lance-shaped with a rough texture. It is characterized by its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.

The species is also noted for its chemical composition, which includes compounds with potential medicinal properties.
