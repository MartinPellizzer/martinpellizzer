Hieracium madarodes

Hieracium madarodes, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish to reddish flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

Hieracium madarodes is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often found in temperate regions across Europe.
