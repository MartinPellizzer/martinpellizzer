Hymenophyllopsis ctenitoides

Hymenophyllopsis ctenitoides, commonly known as the feather-leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Hymenophyllaceae family, characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its humid habitat. It typically grows on rocks or tree trunks in shaded, moist environments.

Its unique appearance and specialized adaptations make it a distinctive species within its ecological niche.
