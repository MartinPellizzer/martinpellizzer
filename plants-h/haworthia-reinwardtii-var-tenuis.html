Haworthia reinwardtii var. tenuis

Haworthia reinwardtii var. tenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
