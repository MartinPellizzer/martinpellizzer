Hieracium commersonii

Hieracium commersonii, commonly known as Commerson's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain regions.
