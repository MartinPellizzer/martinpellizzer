Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. acuminatum

Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. acuminatum, commonly known as the Acuminated Dragon Flower, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces a cluster of strap-like leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted for storing water in its native arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
