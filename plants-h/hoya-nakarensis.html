Hoya nakarensis

Hoya nakarensis, commonly known as the Snowy Wreath Hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing or trailing plant with thick, fleshy stems and waxy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Hoya nakarensis is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil.

It is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
