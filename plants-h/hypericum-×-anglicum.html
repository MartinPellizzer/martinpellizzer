Hypericum × anglicum

Hypericum × anglicum, commonly known as St. John's wort, is a flowering plant in the family Hypericaceae.

It is characterized by its yellow, five-petaled flowers and opposite, simple leaves with serrated edges. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been naturalized in North America. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used for treating mild depression and anxiety.
