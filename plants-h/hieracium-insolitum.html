Hieracium insolitum

Hieracium insolitum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has basal leaves that are broadly ovate with serrated edges, and its stems are hairy and often reddish in color. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Hieracium insolitum is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny areas where it can spread rapidly.
