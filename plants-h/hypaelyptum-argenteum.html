Hypaelyptum argenteum

Hypaelyptum argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant features strap-shaped, silver-gray leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypaelyptum argenteum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
