Helianthemum nummularium var. oblongifolium

Helianthemum nummularium var. oblongifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sunflower saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in sunny, well-drained environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
