Hosta praecox

Hosta praecox, commonly known as the early hosta, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow margins. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers on slender stems in early summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta praecox is valued for its ornamental foliage and is a popular choice for shady garden landscapes.
