Hebecarpa caracasana

Hebecarpa caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It produces capsules as its fruit, which contain several seeds.

Hebecarpa caracasana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
