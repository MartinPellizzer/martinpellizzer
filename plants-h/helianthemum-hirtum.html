Helianthemum hirtum

Helianthemum hirtum, commonly known as the hairy rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions. Its flowers bloom in spring and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

Helianthemum hirtum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate coastal and arid environments.
