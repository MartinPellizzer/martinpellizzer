Habenaria harroldii

Habenaria harroldii, commonly known as the Harrold's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. The plant produces a single, erect stem bearing several showy, fragrant flowers with white petals and a deep purple lip.
