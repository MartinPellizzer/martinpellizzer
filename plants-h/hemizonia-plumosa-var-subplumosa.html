Hemizonia plumosa var. subplumosa

Hemizonia plumosa var. subplumosa, commonly known as the silky feather grass, is a low-growing, ornamental grass native to South America.

It features delicate, feathery plumes that give it a soft, flowing appearance, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its unique texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add visual interest to gardens throughout the growing season.
