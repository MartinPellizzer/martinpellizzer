Hippeastrum alberti

Hippeastrum alberti, commonly known as the Albert's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a contrasting stripe or band. The plant produces large, fleshy leaves that are typically broad and strap-like. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

Hippeastrum alberti is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
