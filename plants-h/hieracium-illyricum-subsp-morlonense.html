Hieracium illyricum subsp. morlonense

Hieracium illyricum subsp.

morlonense, commonly known as the Morlon's Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and grasslands. It has slender, hairy stems that bear alternate, lance-shaped leaves.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller flower heads and specific geographic distribution within its native range.
