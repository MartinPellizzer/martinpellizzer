Helichrysum marginatum

Helichrysum marginatum, commonly known as the Cape marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant has feathery, aromatic leaves and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ability to produce long-lasting blooms makes it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological gardens.
