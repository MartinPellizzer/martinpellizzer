Hieracium hypsilepis

Hieracium hypsilepis, commonly known as the tall spearthistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. It has spiny, upright stems and leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
