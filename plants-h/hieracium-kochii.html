Hieracium kochii

Hieracium kochii, commonly known as Koch's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Hieracium kochii is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in many habitats.
