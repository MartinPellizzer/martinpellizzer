Helichrysum melanophthalmum

Helichrysum melanophthalmum, commonly known as the black-eyed helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers with dark centers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, compact flower heads.

Helichrysum melanophthalmum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
