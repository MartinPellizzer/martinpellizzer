Heteropterys beecheyana f. tomentosa

Heteropterys beecheyana f. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly heteropterys, is a rare and striking plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The leaves are typically elliptical with a leathery texture and are covered in fine, white hairs. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.
