Hypericum rotundatum

Hypericum rotundatum, commonly known as round-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its stems are woody and can be branched, with a reddish-brown color.

Hypericum rotundatum is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
