Hippomarathrum amplifolium

Hippomarathrum amplifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf hippomarathrum, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The showy yellow flowers appear in the summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
