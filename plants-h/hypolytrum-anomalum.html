Hypolytrum anomalum

Hypolytrum anomalum, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag or twisted stem, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a prominent midrib, and it produces small, pale blue or purple flowers.

Hypolytrum anomalum is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique appearance and hardy nature.
