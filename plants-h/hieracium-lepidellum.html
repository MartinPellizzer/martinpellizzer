Hieracium lepidellum

Hieracium lepidellum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium lepidellum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
