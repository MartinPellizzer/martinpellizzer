Helianthemum apenninum f. roseum

Helianthemum apenninum f. roseum, commonly known as the pink rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, evergreen foliage with a dense, mat-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, exhibiting drought tolerance once established.
