Hieracium calcareum subsp. baldense

Hieracium calcareum subsp.

baldense, commonly known as the limestone hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe. It typically grows in calcareous soils, such as those found in grasslands, meadows, and rocky areas.

The plant features erect stems with basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets, and it blooms from June to August.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its adaptability to alkaline environments.
