Haworthia decipiens var. pringlei

Haworthia decipiens var. pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit and features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often tinged with red or pink at the edges. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
