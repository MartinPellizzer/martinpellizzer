Huernia macrocarpa f. schimperi

Huernia macrocarpa f. schimperi, commonly known as the Schimper's moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in a layer of white, waxy material. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for collectors and cactus enthusiasts.
