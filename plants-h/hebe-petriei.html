Hebe petriei

Hebe petriei, commonly known as the Waitaha hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the plantain family (Plantaginaceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. Hebe petriei thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the summer months.
