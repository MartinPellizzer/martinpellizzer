Hypericum caracasanum subsp. caracasanum

Hypericum caracasanum subsp.

caracasanum, commonly known as the Carpathian St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains. It features erect, branching stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
