Haworthia ligulata

Haworthia ligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on thin stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
