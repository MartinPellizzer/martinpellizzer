Hippeastrum multiflorum

Hippeastrum multiflorum, commonly known as the Chinese Amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces multiple blooms on a single stem, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its leaves are broad, strap-like, and can grow up to several feet in length.

Hippeastrum multiflorum thrives in warm, bright environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
