Habenaria megistosolen

Habenaria megistosolen, commonly known as the broad-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually pale green to white with a distinctive, hairy lip.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
