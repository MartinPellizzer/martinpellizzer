Horsfieldia lancifolia

Horsfieldia lancifolia, commonly known as the lanceleaf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees in humid, tropical forest environments. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Horsfieldia lancifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
