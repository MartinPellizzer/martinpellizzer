Hieracium × peczoryense

Hieracium × peczoryense, commonly known as the Peczorynskya hawkweed, is a hybrid plant species within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in moist, disturbed habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations depending on its geographic location.
