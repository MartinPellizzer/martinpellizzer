Hieracium pseudogothicum

Hieracium pseudogothicum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Hieracium pseudogothicum is a perennial species that can spread aggressively through rhizomes, making it a potential weed in certain environments.
