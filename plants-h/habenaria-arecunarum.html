Habenaria arecunarum

Habenaria arecunarum, commonly known as the tropical orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with long, slender stems and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil, and it often grows in dense, humid forests.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
