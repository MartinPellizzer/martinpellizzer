Homalocenchrus oryzoides

Homalocenchrus oryzoides, commonly known as the ricefield grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown florets. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along waterways.

Due to its rapid growth and adaptability, it can become a weed in rice paddies and other cultivated areas.
