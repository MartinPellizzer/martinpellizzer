Hymenophyllum malingii

Hymenophyllum malingii, commonly known as the silver fern, is a small, delicate fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silvery-green fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which contribute to its reflective, almost metallic sheen.

Hymenophyllum malingii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
