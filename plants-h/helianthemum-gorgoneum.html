Helianthemum gorgoneum

Helianthemum gorgoneum, commonly known as the Gorgoneum rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are small, fleshy, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

The species is adapted to rocky and coastal environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh climates.
