Howardia ehrenbergiana

Howardia ehrenbergiana, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to produce flowers during the holiday season adds to its ornamental value.
