Hedyotis Glomerata

Hedyotis glomerata is a small, herbaceous plant native to various regions across Asia. It is commonly found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Known for its clusters of tiny, white flowers, it adds a delicate beauty to its natural habitat. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. This plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
