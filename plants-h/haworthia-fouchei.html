Haworthia fouchei

Haworthia fouchei, commonly known as the Fouché’s Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
