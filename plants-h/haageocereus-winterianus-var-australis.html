Haageocereus winterianus var. australis

Haageocereus winterianus var. australis, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a hybrid plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, branching stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant typically features bright red, pink, or orange flowers that are borne on the ends of the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and seasonal blooming habit.
