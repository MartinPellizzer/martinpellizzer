Hypericum formosanum var. nakamurae

Hypericum formosanum var. nakamurae, commonly known as the Formosan St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes numerous species known for their yellow flowers and medicinal properties. The plant typically features bright yellow flowers with orange stamens, blooming in summer. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins or open areas.

Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered with fine hairs.
