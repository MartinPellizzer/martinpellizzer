Haworthia nitida

Haworthia nitida, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, fleshy, blue-green leaves with white or yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
