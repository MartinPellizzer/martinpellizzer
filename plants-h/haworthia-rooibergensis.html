Haworthia rooibergensis

Haworthia rooibergensis, commonly known as the Rooiberg Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, white flowers during the cooler months, usually in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor and rock garden displays.
