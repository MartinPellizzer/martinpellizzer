Hieracium expallidiforme var. oligasterum

Hieracium expallidiforme var. oligasterum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
