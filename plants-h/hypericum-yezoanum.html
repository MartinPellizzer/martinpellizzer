Hypericum yezoanum

Hypericum yezoanum, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.
