Hieracium angustatum subsp. coarctatum

Hieracium angustatum subsp.

coarctatum, commonly known as narrowleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy surface, and it produces yellow flowers in clusters.
