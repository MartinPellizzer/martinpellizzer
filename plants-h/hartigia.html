Hartigia

Hartigia, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple petals and spiky yellow centers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Hartigia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and landscapes.

It also attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
