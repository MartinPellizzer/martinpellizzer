Habenaria pectinata var. gigantea

Habenaria pectinata var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant lady's mantle, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, overlapping leaves that form a rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers arranged in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and conservation purposes.
