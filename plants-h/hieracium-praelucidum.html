Hieracium praelucidum

Hieracium praelucidum, commonly known as the clearleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium praelucidum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
