Homeria breyniana var. aurantiaca

Homeria breyniana var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the yellow bell orchid, is a striking South African orchid species characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Eastern Cape province. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces pendulous, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
