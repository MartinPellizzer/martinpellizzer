Hannonia hesperidum

Hannonia hesperidum, commonly known as the western hannonia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
