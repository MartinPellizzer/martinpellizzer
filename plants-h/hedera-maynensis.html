Hedera maynensis

Hedera maynensis, commonly known as the Mayan ivy, is a climbing evergreen vine native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
