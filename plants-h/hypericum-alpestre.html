Hypericum alpestre

Hypericum alpestre, commonly known as alpine St. John's wort, is a hardy shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with slender, woody stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late summer, which are typically clustered in small groups. Hypericum alpestre is often found in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

It is a member of the Hypericaceae family and is known for its ability to tolerate cold and harsh climatic conditions.
