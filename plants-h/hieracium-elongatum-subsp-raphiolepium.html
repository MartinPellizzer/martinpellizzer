Hieracium elongatum subsp. raphiolepium

Hieracium elongatum subsp.

raphiolepium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in grasslands and open areas, often forming dense colonies.

The plant features elongated, hairy stems and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and its role in local ecosystems as a food source for various insects.
