Heterodendrum oleifolium var. europhyllum

Heterodendrum oleifolium var. europhyllum, commonly known as the European olive tree, is a variant of the olive tree native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically glossy and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by the development of drupes. Its botanical features include a deep root system and a tendency to grow in a rounded, bushy form.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in traditional horticulture.
