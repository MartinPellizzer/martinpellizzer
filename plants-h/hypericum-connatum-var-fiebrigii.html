Hypericum connatum var. fiebrigii

Hypericum connatum var. fiebrigii, commonly known as the Fiebrig's St. John's wort, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for both aesthetic and therapeutic purposes.
