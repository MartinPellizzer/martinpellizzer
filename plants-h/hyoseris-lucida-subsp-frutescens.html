Hyoseris lucida subsp. frutescens

Hyoseris lucida subsp.

frutescens, commonly known as the slender yellowhead, is a flowering plant native to parts of North America. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its yellow-orange flowers bloom in the summer, and the leaves are usually alternate and linear to lanceolate.

It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 cm.
