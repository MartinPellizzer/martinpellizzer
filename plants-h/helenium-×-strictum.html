Helenium × strictum

Helenium × strictum, commonly known as the orange hawkweed, is a hybrid perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, cone-shaped flower heads that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with narrow, grass-like leaves and sturdy, upright stems. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early fall, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Helenium × strictum is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ornamental value.
