Hieracium subvaldepilosum

Hieracium subvaldepilosum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
