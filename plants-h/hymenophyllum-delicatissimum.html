Hymenophyllum delicatissimum

Hymenophyllum delicatissimum, commonly known as the delicate filmy fern, is a member of the Hymenophyllaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, lace-like fronds.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in moist, shaded environments. The plant's fronds are delicate and translucent, with a unique structure that allows for efficient water retention. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in humid forests.

Due to its fragile nature, it requires specific conditions to flourish and is often used in botanical gardens for its aesthetic and scientific value.
