Habenaria carvalhoi

Habenaria carvalhoi, commonly known as the Carvalho's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown patterns.

Habenaria carvalhoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
