Hydrangea obovatifolia

Hydrangea obovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf hydrangea, is a species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, ovate to broadly ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy inflorescences of white or pink flowers, which can change color depending on soil pH. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, adding visual interest even when not in bloom.
