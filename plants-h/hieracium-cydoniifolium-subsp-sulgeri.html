Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp. sulgeri

Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp.

sulgeri, commonly known as the Sulger's cow parsley, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features slender, hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery underside. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
