Haemanthus mildbraedii

Haemanthus mildbraedii, commonly known as the Natal grassbladder, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, and it produces clusters of bright red flowers during the summer months. The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice among gardeners interested in ornamental plants.

The Natal grassbladder is also valued for its ability to thrive in coastal and semi-arid environments.
