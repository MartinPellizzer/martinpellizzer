Hibiscus macrogonus

Hibiscus macrogonus, commonly known as the large-ovary hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Hibiscus macrogonus thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
