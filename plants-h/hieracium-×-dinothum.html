Hieracium × dinothum

Hieracium × dinothum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a single head and surrounded by pale green leaves. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has a creeping growth habit with rhizomes that allow it to spread vegetatively.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
