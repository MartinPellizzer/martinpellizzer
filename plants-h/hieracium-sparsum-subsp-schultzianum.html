Hieracium sparsum subsp. schultzianum

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

schultzianum, commonly known as the Schultze's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering stems that bear yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has finely divided, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, herbaceous flower heads.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
