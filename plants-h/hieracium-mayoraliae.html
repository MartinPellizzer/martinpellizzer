Hieracium mayoraliae

Hieracium mayoraliae, commonly known as the Mayoral's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish composite flowers and is typically found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
