Hieracium kulkowianum

Hieracium kulkowianum, commonly known as Kulkowian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium kulkowianum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
