Heeria uelensis var. isotricha

Heeria uelensis var. isotricha, commonly known as the velvetleaf heeria, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring.

The species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
