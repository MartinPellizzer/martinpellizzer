Hieracium Lachenalii Subsp. Aurulentum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp. aurulentum is a rare and intriguing member of the daisy family. It is native to specific regions in southern Europe and is known for its distinctive yellow-orange flowers. This subspecies thrives in rocky and coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its natural habitat.
