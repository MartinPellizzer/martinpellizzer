Hypericum empetrifolium var. oliganthum

Hypericum empetrifolium var. oliganthum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. It is part of the Hypericaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and alpine displays.
