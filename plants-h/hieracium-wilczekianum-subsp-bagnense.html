Hieracium wilczekianum subsp. bagnense

Hieracium wilczekianum subsp.

bagnense, commonly known as the Bagnes Hill Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a single head, with narrow, grass-like leaves.
