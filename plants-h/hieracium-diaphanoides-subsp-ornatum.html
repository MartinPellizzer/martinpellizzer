Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. ornatum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

ornatum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in temperate regions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species in parts of its range.
