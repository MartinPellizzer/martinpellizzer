Holostylon strictipes

Holostylon strictipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved holostylon, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers in clusters. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.

Holostylon strictipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
