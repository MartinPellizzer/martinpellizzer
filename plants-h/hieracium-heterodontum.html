Hieracium heterodontum

Hieracium heterodontum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are usually hairy and serrated, while its flowers consist of both ray and disk florets.

Hieracium heterodontum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some regions.
