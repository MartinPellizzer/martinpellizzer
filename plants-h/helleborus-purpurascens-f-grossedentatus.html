Helleborus purpurascens f. grossedentatus

Helleborus purpurascens f. grossedentatus, commonly known as the giant-flowered hellebore, is a hybrid form of the Christmas rose.

It is characterized by its large, showy, purple-pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. It prefers well-drained, moist soil and partial to full shade.

Its foliage consists of large, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that remain evergreen throughout the year.
