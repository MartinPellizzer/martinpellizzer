Habenaria bilabrella

Habenaria bilabrella, commonly known as the two-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pale green to yellowish flowers with a distinctive two-lipped structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid air and soil.

The orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
