Heptapleurum brassii

Heptapleurum brassii, commonly known as the African nettle, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along stream banks.

Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
