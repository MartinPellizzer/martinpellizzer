Hibbertia ericifolia subsp. ericifolia

Hibbertia ericifolia subsp.

ericifolia, commonly known as the Ericaceous Hibbertia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in heathlands and dry forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native gardens.
