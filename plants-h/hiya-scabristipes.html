Hiya scabristipes

Hiya scabristipes, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa, characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The flowers typically bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white, adding a vibrant touch to any space.
