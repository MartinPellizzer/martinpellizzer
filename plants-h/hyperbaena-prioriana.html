Hyperbaena prioriana

Hyperbaena prioriana, commonly known as the African rock fig, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often consumed by local wildlife and humans. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its hardiness, it is sometimes used in xeriscaping and as a ornamental plant in dry regions.
