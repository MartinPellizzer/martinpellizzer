Hypericum petiolatum var. tubulosum

Hypericum petiolatum var. tubulosum, commonly known as the slender St. John's wort, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features upright, branching stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom from summer to fall. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are composed of four petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Hypericaceae family.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
