Haworthia Attenuata

Haworthia attenuata is a popular succulent plant known for its unique appearance and easy care requirements. It features rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that often display striking white or translucent markings. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight. Its compact size makes it an ideal choice for indoor gardens and rockeries. With minimal watering needs, it is a favorite among both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
