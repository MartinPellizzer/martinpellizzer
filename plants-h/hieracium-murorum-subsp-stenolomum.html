Hieracium murorum subsp. stenolomum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

stenolomum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.

The plant has upright stems with opposite, lanceolate leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.
