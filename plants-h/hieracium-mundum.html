Hieracium mundum

Hieracium mundum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically clustered in a single head at the end of each stem. The plant has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium mundum is an annual or biennial plant that thrives in temperate climates.
