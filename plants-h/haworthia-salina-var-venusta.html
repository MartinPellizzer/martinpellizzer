Haworthia salina var. venusta

Haworthia salina var. venusta, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, fleshy, triangular leaves with white to yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant grows in a rosette form and is well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, greenish-white flowers on tall stems during the summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
