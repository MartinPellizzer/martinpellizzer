Hieracium × naszodense

Hieracium × naszodense, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and basal rosettes of lanceolate leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that consist of numerous small florets arranged in a disc. It is native to Central and Eastern Europe and thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations.
