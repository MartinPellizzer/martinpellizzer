Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp. megalocladum

Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp.

megalocladum, commonly known as the giant-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its robust growth and large, serrated leaves.
