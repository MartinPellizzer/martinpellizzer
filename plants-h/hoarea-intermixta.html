Hoarea intermixta

Hoarea intermixta, commonly known as the mixed-leaved hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent, thick, and fleshy stems and leaves, which are adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its leaves are often variegated with contrasting colors, adding to its ornamental value.

Hoarea intermixta is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements and aesthetic appeal.
