Hermione decora

Hermione decora, commonly known as the decorative hermione, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
