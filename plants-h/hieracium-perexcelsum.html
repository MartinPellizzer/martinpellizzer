Hieracium perexcelsum

Hieracium perexcelsum, commonly known as the greater hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa, and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, and its leaves are typically lance-shaped with rough textures. It is a perennial herb that can grow up to 60 cm tall, and it has a robust root system that helps it thrive in various soil conditions.

Hieracium perexcelsum is also known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it an invasive species in some regions.
