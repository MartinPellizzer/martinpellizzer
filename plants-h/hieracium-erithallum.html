Hieracium erithallum

Hieracium erithallum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium erithallum is also known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in agricultural and natural settings.
