Hypericum australe

Hypericum australe, commonly known as the Australian St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, which are typical of the Hypericaceae family. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, showing a preference for well-drained environments. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum australe is often used in native landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
