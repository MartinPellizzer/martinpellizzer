Helichrysum gymnocomum

Helichrysum gymnocomum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers that resemble tiny suns. The plant is known for its aromatic foliage and is often used in dried flower arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum gymnocomum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
