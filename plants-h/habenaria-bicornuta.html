Habenaria bicornuta

Habenaria bicornuta, commonly known as the two-horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers feature two prominent, horn-like projections on the lip, which give it its common name.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
