Habenaria maxillaris

Habenaria maxillaris, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on the forest floor or along stream banks. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
