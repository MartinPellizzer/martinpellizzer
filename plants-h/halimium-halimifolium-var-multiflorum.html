Halimium halimifolium var. multiflorum

Halimium halimifolium var. multiflorum, commonly known as the many-flowered rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a perennial shrub that thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its foliage consists of small, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand drought and harsh environmental conditions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping.
