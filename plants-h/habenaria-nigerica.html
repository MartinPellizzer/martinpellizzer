Habenaria nigerica

Habenaria nigerica, commonly known as the black orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which are striking and often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

The species is part of the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its unique floral structure and coloration.
