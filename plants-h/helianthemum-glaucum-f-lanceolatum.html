Helianthemum glaucum f. lanceolatum

Helianthemum glaucum f. lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceolate rockrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a hardy ornamental choice.
