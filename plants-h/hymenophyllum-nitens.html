Hymenophyllum nitens

Hymenophyllum nitens, commonly known as the silver moss, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves that are covered in a fine layer of silvery scales, giving it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are usually less than 10 centimeters in length and have a leathery texture.

Hymenophyllum nitens plays an important ecological role in maintaining soil moisture and providing habitat for microorganisms.
