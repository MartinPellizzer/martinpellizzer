Habenaria pectinata var. limprichtii

Habenaria pectinata var. limprichtii, commonly known as the Limpricht's butterfly orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

This terrestrial orchid typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on the forest floor or in rocky crevices. Its flowers are characterized by their delicate, butterfly-like appearance with pale green to white petals and sepals. The plant produces a single, slender stem that bears several small, fragrant blooms.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings due to its specific care requirements.
