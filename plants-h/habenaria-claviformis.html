Habenaria claviformis

Habenaria claviformis, commonly known as the club-shaped orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive club-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, or green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade in its natural habitat.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
