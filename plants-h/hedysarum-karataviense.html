Hedysarum karataviense

Hedysarum karataviense, commonly known as the Karatav Valley milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces slender, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Hedysarum karataviense plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential in ecological restoration projects.
