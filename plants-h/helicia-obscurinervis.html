Helicia obscurinervis

Helicia obscurinervis, commonly known as the dark-veined helicia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically elliptical with dark veins, giving it its common name.
