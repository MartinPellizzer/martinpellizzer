Hippeastrum primulinum

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Hippeastrum primulinum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
