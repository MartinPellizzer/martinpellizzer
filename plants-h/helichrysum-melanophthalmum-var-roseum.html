Helichrysum melanophthalmum var. roseum

Helichrysum melanophthalmum var. roseum, commonly known as the roseum helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. This species is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often used in rock gardens or as a decorative plant.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
