Hieracium obscuripes

Hieracium obscuripes, commonly known as the dark hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy areas and has hairy stems and leaves. Its flowers are yellow and are surrounded by a ring of pale green leaves, which help in distinguishing it from other hawkweed species.

It is an herbaceous perennial that can form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
