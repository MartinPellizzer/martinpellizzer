Helenium somalense

Helenium somalense, commonly known as the Somali sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Somalia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright orange to red ray florets and yellow disk florets. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. Its structure includes a tall, branching stem with lance-shaped leaves, and it produces numerous small, composite flowers that attract pollinators.

Helenium somalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
