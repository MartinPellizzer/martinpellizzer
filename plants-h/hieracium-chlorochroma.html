Hieracium chlorochroma

Hieracium chlorochroma, commonly known as the greenish hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to greenish-yellow flowers and hairy stems. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, dandelion-like seeds.

It is often considered a weed but has ecological value in supporting pollinators.
