Hypericum minutiflorum

Hypericum minutiflorum, commonly known as the lesser St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hypericum minutiflorum is part of the genus Hypericum, which includes many species known for their medicinal and ornamental value.
