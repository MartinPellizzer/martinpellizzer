Hieracium × leptosoma

Hieracium × leptosoma, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It is a hybrid species, resulting from the cross between Hieracium praeustum and Hieracium piloselloides.

Its botanical features make it an important component of certain ecological communities.
