Hieracium streptochaetum var. epilosum

Hieracium streptochaetum var. epilosum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble clusters of small flowers. The plant is distinguished by its dense, woolly covering on the stems and leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

Its flowers bloom in the summer, producing yellow or orange disc flowers surrounded by pale green to white ray flowers.
