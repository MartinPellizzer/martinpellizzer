Hieracium guttatifrons

Hieracium guttatifrons, commonly known as the spotted hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and may bear white or yellow spots.

Hieracium guttatifrons is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can spread aggressively through rhizomes.
