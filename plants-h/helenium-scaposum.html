Helenium scaposum

Helenium scaposum, commonly known as the slender swamp gromwell, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with ray florets that give it a daisy-like appearance.

Helenium scaposum thrives in moist, wet environments such as marshes and swamps.
