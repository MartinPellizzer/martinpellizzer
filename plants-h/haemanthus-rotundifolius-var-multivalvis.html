Haemanthus rotundifolius var. multivalvis

Haemanthus rotundifolius var. multivalvis, commonly known as the red-stemmed haemanthus, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its clustered, strap-like leaves that are dark green with a reddish hue on the underside. The plant produces striking red or orange flower spikes during the winter months, which are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms make it a valuable addition to botanical collections.
