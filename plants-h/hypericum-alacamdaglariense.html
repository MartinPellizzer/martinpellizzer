Hypericum alacamdaglariense

Hypericum alacamdaglariense, commonly known as the Alacaglar Hypericum, is a shrub native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is found in mountainous regions. It has small, opposite leaves and produces bright orange-yellow flowers during the summer months.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
