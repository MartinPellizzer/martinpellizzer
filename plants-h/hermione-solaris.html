Hermione solaris

Hermione solaris, commonly known as the Sunspark Vervain, is a striking flowering plant native to the sun-drenched regions of the Mediterranean.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, silvery-gray hairs that reflect sunlight, giving it a shimmering appearance. Its flowers are typically bright yellow or orange, resembling small sunbursts, and bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies make it a valuable addition to any garden.
