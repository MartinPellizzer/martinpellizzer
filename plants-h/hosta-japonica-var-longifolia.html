Hosta japonica var. longifolia

Hosta japonica var. longifolia, commonly known as the Japanese hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a silvery underside. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often used in shaded garden settings.
