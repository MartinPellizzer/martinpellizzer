Habenaria janellehayneana

Habenaria janellehayneana, commonly known as Janelle Hayne's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms during the wet season, producing clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white petals with a distinct lip. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
