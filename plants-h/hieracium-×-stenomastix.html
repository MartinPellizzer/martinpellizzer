Hieracium × stenomastix

Hieracium × stenomastix, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and rosette growth habit, often found in meadows and disturbed soils. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture. It produces small, disk-like flowers that are clustered in a composite head.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits variability in morphology and is often found in regions with diverse environmental conditions.
