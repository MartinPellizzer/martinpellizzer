Haworthia eilyae

Haworthia eilyae, commonly known as the "Eilyae Haworthia," is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pinkish markings, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
