Hieracium invenustum

Hieracium invenustum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and wetlands. It has basal leaves that are broadly lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium invenustum is an invasive species in some regions, outcompeting native plants and altering local ecosystems.
