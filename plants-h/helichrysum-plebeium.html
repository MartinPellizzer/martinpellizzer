Helichrysum plebeium

Helichrysum plebeium, commonly known as the common everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, yellowish to golden flowers. The plant produces numerous tiny, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving it a fluffy appearance. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in dry, sandy soils.

Helichrysum plebeium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting flowers.
