Haworthia semiglabrata

Haworthia semiglabrata, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white tubercles and a central spiky structure. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stalks during the warmer months. It prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
