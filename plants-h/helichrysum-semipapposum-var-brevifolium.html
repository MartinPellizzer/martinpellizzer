Helichrysum semipapposum var. brevifolium

Helichrysum semipapposum var. brevifolium, commonly known as the Cape gold flower, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, bright yellow to orange flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting color. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological gardening.
