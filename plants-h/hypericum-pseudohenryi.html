Hypericum pseudohenryi

Hypericum pseudohenryi, commonly known as the narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species known for their yellow flowers and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in the summer months.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
