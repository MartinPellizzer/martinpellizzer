Hyptis graveolens

Hyptis graveolens, commonly known as blue hyssop, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular blue or purple flowers. Its leaves are often aromatic and can be used in traditional medicine.

Hyptis graveolens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
