Hypericum choji-suzukii

Hypericum choji-suzukii, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes numerous species known for their yellow flowers and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are alternate, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers, which bloom in summer, are bright yellow and often have orange or red markings.
