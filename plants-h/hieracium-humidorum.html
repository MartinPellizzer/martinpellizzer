Hieracium humidorum

Hieracium humidorum, commonly known as the damp meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats such as meadows and marshes. It produces yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium humidorum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in areas with moderate to high humidity.
