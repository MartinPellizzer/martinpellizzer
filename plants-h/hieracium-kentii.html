Hieracium kentii

Hieracium kentii, commonly known as Kent's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Kent's hawkweed is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
