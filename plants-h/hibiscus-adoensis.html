Hibiscus adoensis

Hibiscus adoensis, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to deep red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Hibiscus adoensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its rapid growth and ability to adapt to various conditions make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
