Hieracium oenotherifolium

Hieracium oenotherifolium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaf arrangement. The plant features basal rosettes of spoon-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its showy yellow flowers bloom in summer, forming a cluster at the top of the stem.

Hieracium oenotherifolium is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
