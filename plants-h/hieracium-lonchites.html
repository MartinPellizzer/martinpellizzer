Hieracium lonchites

Hieracium lonchites, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, yellowish to reddish flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs.

Hieracium lonchites is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
