Habenaria cruciformis

Habenaria cruciformis, commonly known as the Cruciform Orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive cruciform (cross-shaped) flower structure, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with a prominent lip that is often marked with dark spots.

Habenaria cruciformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
