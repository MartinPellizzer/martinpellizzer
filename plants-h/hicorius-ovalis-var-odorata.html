Hicorius ovalis var. odorata

Hicorius ovalis var. odorata, commonly known as the fragrant oval hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in vibrant hues of red, pink, and white. The plant produces a distinctive, sweet fragrance, which is most noticeable in the evening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and aromatic qualities.
