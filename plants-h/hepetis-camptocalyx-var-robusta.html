Hepetis camptocalyx var. robusta

Hepetis camptocalyx var. robusta, commonly known as the robust hepetis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and have a slightly aromatic scent. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale blue or violet in color, arranged in loose clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
