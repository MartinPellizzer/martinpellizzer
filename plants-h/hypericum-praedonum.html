Hypericum praedonum

Hypericum praedonum, commonly known as the yellow crowfoot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or streambanks.

Hypericum praedonum is also known for its medicinal properties, though it contains toxic compounds that require caution in use.
