Holothrix exilis

Holothrix exilis, commonly known as the slender holothrix, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Holothrix exilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to arid conditions.
