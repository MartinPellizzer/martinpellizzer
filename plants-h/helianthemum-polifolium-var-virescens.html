Helianthemum polifolium var. virescens

Helianthemum polifolium var. virescens, commonly known as the greenleaf rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to rocky or coastal landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for Mediterranean gardens.
