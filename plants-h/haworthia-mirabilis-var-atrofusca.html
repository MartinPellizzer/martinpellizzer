Haworthia mirabilis var. atrofusca

Haworthia mirabilis var. atrofusca, commonly known as the black Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a striking purplish-black hue, especially under bright light. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
