Helichrysum Fulvescens Var. Patulum

Helichrysum fulvescens var. patulum is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its vibrant yellow to orange flowers that retain their color even after drying. This variety of helichrysum is native to southern Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rockeries, borders, and container planting. The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses in treating respiratory and skin conditions. Despite its delicate appearance, it is remarkably hardy and adaptable to various growing conditions.
