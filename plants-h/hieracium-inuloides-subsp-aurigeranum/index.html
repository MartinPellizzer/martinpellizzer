Hieracium Inuloides Subsp. Aurigeranum

Hieracium inuloides subsp. aurigeranum is a unique plant species found in specific regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads. This subspecies is distinguished by its vibrant yellow flowers and hairy leaves. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly areas. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable part of local flora.
