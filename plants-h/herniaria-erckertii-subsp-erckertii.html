Herniaria erckertii subsp. erckertii

Herniaria erckertii subsp.

erckertii, commonly known as the erckert's bladderwort, is a small, mat-forming plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family. It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with tiny, rounded leaves that often appear to be fused at the base.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It is typically found in dry, calcareous soils, often in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is adapted to thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
