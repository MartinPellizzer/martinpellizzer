Hieracium murorum subsp. petersii

Hieracium murorum subsp.

petersii, commonly known as the Peters' hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single inflorescence. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive lobed shape.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.
