Hibiscus skeneae

Hibiscus skeneae, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while the stems are woody and often covered in fine hairs.

Hibiscus skeneae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
