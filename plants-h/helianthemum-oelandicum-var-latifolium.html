Helianthemum oelandicum var. latifolium

Helianthemum oelandicum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, characterized by its low, spreading growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

It is often found in limestone soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
