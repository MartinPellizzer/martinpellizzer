Habenaria laotica

Habenaria laotica, commonly known as the Laotian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Botanically, it exhibits a complex structure with bilateral symmetry and specialized pollination mechanisms.
