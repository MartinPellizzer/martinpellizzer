Hieracium bifidum subsp. gigantodontum

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

gigantodontum, commonly known as the giant toothed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, toothed leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a rosette growth form with basal leaves and can reach heights of up to 30 cm.

Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
