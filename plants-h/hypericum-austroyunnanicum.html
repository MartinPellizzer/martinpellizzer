Hypericum austroyunnanicum

Hypericum austroyunnanicum, commonly known as the Austro-Yunnan hypericum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers, which are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
