Hypochaeris carpathica

Hypochaeris carpathica, commonly known as the Carpathian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Hypochaeris carpathica is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
