Hypericum sagittifolium

Hypericum sagittifolium, commonly known as the arrowleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features narrow, arrow-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is part of the Hypericaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology. It thrives in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in shaded or partially shaded areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and traditional herbal practices.
