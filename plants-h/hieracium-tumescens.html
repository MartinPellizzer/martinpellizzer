Hieracium tumescens

Hieracium tumescens, commonly known as the swollen hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium tumescens is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in agricultural areas.
