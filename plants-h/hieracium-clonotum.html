Hieracium clonotum

Hieracium clonotum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are yellow and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium clonotum spreads through rhizomes and is often found in colonies, making it a common sight in open, sunny habitats.
