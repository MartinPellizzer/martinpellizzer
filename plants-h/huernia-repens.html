Huernia repens

Huernia repens, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the summer months. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Huernia repens is a low-growing plant that forms dense mats, making it an attractive and easy-to-care-for houseplant.
