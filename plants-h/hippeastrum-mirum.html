Hippeastrum mirum

Hippeastrum mirum, commonly known as the Marvelous Amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter. The plant produces strikingly colorful blooms in shades of red, white, and pink, often with a white or yellow throat. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are evergreen in warmer climates.

Hippeastrum mirum is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
