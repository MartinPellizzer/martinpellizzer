Habenaria osmastonii

Habenaria osmastonii, commonly known as the Osmaston's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral structure.
