Haageocereus tenuispinus

Haageocereus tenuispinus, commonly known as the narrow-spined hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in small, sharp spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for desert gardens.
