Hieracium anantozum

Hieracium anantozum, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, with basal leaves that are heart-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped cluster, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium anantozum is known for its aggressive growth and ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
