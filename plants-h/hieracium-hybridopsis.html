Hieracium hybridopsis

Hieracium hybridopsis, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring temperate climates. It has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
