Hieracium procerum

Hieracium procerum, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium procerum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
