Habenaria sanfordiana

Habenaria sanfordiana, commonly known as Sanford's orchid, is a species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and typically produces a single, showy flower per stem. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single leaf and a distinctive, pale yellow to greenish-white flower. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
