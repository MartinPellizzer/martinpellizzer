Hippeastrum paranaense

Hippeastrum paranaense, commonly known as the Parana lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Hippeastrum paranaense thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.

Its botanical features make it both visually appealing and relatively easy to care for.
