Hypericum canadense f. minimum

Hypericum canadense f. minimum, commonly known as the dwarf St. John's wort, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to North America.

It features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
