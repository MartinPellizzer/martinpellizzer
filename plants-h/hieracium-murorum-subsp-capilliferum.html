Hieracium murorum subsp. capilliferum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

capilliferum, commonly known as the slender spearthistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and yellow composite flowers.

The leaves are typically lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, which help reduce water loss. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

This subspecies is distinguished by its slender, erect growth habit and is considered a minor weed in agricultural settings.
