Hypoxis engleriana var. scottii

Hypoxis engleriana var. scottii, commonly known as the Scott's yellow star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in summer, which are clustered in a dense inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
