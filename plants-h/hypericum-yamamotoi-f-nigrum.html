Hypericum yamamotoi f. nigrum

Hypericum yamamotoi f. nigrum, commonly known as the black-stemmed St. John's wort, is a rare and unique cultivar of the Hypericum genus.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black stems and leaves, which contrast with its bright yellow flowers. This variety is native to Japan and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, exhibiting a compact growth habit.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after specimen for horticultural enthusiasts and botanists alike.
