Hieracium orthocladum

Hieracium orthocladum, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can form dense colonies in suitable environments.
