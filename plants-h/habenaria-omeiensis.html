Habenaria omeiensis

Habenaria omeiensis, commonly known as the Omei orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the spring or early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
