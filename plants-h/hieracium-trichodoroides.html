Hieracium trichodoroides

Hieracium trichodoroides, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
