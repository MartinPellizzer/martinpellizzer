Hieracium fagopalentinum

Hieracium fagopalentinum, commonly known as the wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. Hieracium fagopalentinum is also known for its edible leaves, which have a tangy, sour flavor.
