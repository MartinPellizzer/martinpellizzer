Hieracium × mrazii

Hieracium × mrazii, commonly known as the Mrazii hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the daisy family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers and is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. The plant has narrow, upright stems and basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in certain areas.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
