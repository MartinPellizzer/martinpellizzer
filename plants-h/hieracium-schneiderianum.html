Hieracium schneiderianum

Hieracium schneiderianum, commonly known as Schneider's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces showy yellow flowers in late summer. Native to Central and Eastern Europe, it thrives in moist meadows and grassy areas. The plant is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through its rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and botanical gardens.
