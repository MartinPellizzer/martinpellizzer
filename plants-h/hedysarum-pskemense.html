Hedysarum pskemense

Hedysarum pskemense, commonly known as the Pskem honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Hedysarum pskemense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting local insect populations.
