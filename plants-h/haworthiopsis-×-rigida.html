Haworthiopsis × rigida

Haworthiopsis × rigida, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of thick, fleshy leaves with distinctive light and dark green stripes, resembling a zebra's pattern. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect light with minimal watering.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
