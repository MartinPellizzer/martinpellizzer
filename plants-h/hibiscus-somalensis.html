Hibiscus somalensis

Hibiscus somalensis, commonly known as the Somali hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often found in coastal areas and can tolerate saline soils.

Hibiscus somalensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in some regions.
