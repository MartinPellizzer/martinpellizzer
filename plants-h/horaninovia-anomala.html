Horaninovia anomala

Horaninovia anomala, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It features striking silver-gray foliage with intricate patterns, and its flowers are typically pale blue or white, with a subtle fragrance. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is noted for its slow growth and is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation programs.
