Hieracium sernanderianum

Hieracium sernanderianum, commonly known as the Sernander's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

Hieracium sernanderianum is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early summer.
