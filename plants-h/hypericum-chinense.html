Hypericum chinense

Hypericum chinense, commonly known as Chinese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Hypericum chinense is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats.
