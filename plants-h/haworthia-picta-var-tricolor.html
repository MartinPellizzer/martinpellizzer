Haworthia picta var. tricolor

Haworthia picta var. tricolor, commonly known as the Tricolor Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The leaves are often adorned with intricate patterns that resemble a tricolor design, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

This plant is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
