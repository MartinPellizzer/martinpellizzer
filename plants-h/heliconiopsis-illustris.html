Heliconiopsis illustris

Heliconiopsis illustris, commonly known as the red heliconiopsis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Heliconiaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy inflorescences. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish tinge. Its flowers are typically red or orange, arranged in a spiral pattern on a tall, branching stem.

Heliconiopsis illustris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
