Hieracium prenanthoides subsp. divaricatulum

Hieracium prenanthoides subsp.

divaricatulum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant typically has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, contributing to the ecological diversity of its native environments.
