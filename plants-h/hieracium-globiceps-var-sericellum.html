Hieracium globiceps var. sericellum

Hieracium globiceps var. sericellum, commonly known as the silky globular hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and spherical, yellow flower heads that resemble small dandelions. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts.
