Hunteria corymbosa var. salicifolia

Hunteria corymbosa var. salicifolia, commonly known as the silky hunteria, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, willow-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. Its growth habit is upright, with branches that often droop slightly, creating a graceful form.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
