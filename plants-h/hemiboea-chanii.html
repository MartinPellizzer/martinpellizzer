Hemiboea chanii

Hemiboea chanii, commonly known as the red-flowered hemiboea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in a fine pubescence.

Hemiboea chanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
