Haastia sinclairii var. fulvida

Haastia sinclairii var. fulvida, commonly known as the golden Haastia, is a striking member of the Bromeliaceae family native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spiky leaves that range from bright yellow to orange, giving it a fiery appearance.
