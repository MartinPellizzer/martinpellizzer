Hieracium goritianum

Hieracium goritianum, commonly known as the Goritian hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in grassy habitats and has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Hieracium goritianum is often found in mountainous areas and is adapted to temperate climates.
