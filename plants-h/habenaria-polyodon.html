Habenaria polyodon

Habenaria polyodon, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped inflorescence and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers. It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in humid, tropical climates.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in some regions.
