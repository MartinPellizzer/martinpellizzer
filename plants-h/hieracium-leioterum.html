Hieracium leioterum

Hieracium leioterum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hieracium leioterum is often considered an invasive species in regions outside its native range.
