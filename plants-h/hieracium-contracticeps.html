Hieracium contracticeps

Hieracium contracticeps, commonly known as the contracted hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, yellowish-green composite flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Hieracium contracticeps is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
