Hybanthus costaricensis

Hybanthus costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican hybanthus, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Hybanthusaceae and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has opposite, elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique floral structure makes it an interesting subject for botanical study.
