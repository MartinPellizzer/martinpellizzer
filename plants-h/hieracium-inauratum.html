Hieracium inauratum

Hieracium inauratum, commonly known as the golden hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium inauratum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
