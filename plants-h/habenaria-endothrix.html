Habenaria endothrix

Habenaria endothrix, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine layer of pubescence.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is notable for its delicate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
