Hosta caput-avis

Hosta caput-avis, commonly known as the "blue bird hosta," is a striking perennial plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that range in color from bright blue-green to deep blue, depending on light exposure. The plant produces tall, upright flower spikes that bear fragrant, white to pale lavender blooms in late summer. Hosta caput-avis thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and container planting.
