Hieracium fullyanum

Hieracium fullyanum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are yellow, often arranged in a flat-topped cluster. It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Hieracium fullyanum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
