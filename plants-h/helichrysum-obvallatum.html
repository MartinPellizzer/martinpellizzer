Helichrysum obvallatum

Helichrysum obvallatum, commonly known as the golden ball or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that produces small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in spherical clusters. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Its foliage is aromatic and may be used in traditional medicine.
