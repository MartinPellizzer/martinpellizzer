Heterotis decumbens

Heterotis decumbens, commonly known as the African butterbean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that grows along the ground, with sprawling stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bean-like pods containing edible seeds. It is valued for its nutritional seeds, which are rich in protein and used in local cuisines.

The plant also has traditional medicinal uses in some African cultures.
