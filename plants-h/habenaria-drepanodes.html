Habenaria drepanodes

Habenaria drepanodes, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that produce multiple stems each year. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive zigzag pattern on the petals and sepals.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
