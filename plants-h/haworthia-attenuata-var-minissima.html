Haworthia attenuata var. minissima

Haworthia attenuata var. minissima, commonly known as the miniature Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically a pale green or bluish-green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
