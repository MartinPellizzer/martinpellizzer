Hieracium rueblerianum

Hieracium rueblerianum, commonly known as the Ruebler's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium rueblerianum is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes, forming colonies in suitable habitats.
