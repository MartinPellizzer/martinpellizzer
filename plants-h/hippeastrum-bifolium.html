Hippeastrum bifolium

Hippeastrum bifolium, commonly known as the two-leaf amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that can be red, orange, or white, often with a contrasting stripe. The plant has two broad, lance-shaped leaves that emerge from the base of the bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to bloom.

Hippeastrum bifolium is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
