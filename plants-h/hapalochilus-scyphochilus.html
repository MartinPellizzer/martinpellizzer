Hapalochilus scyphochilus

Hapalochilus scyphochilus, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage and its showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Hapalochilus scyphochilus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
