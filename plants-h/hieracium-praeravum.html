Hieracium praeravum

Hieracium praeravum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium praeravum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is considered a persistent weed in some regions.
