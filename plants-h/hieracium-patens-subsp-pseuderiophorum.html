Hieracium patens subsp. pseuderiophorum

Hieracium patens subsp.

pseuderiophorum, commonly known as the slender spearthistle, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright, sparsely haired stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
