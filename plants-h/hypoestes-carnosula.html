Hypoestes carnosula

Hypoestes carnosula, commonly known as the "Magic Bean Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble small flowers. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked in favor of its vibrant foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Hypoestes carnosula is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
