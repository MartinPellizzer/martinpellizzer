Habenaria listeroides

Habenaria listeroides, commonly known as the Lister's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia, including China, India, and Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores water, which is characteristic of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that is often more brightly colored.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
