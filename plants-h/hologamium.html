Hologamium

Hologamium, commonly known as the spectral bloom, is a rare flowering plant native to the misty highlands of the Andes.

Its most distinctive feature is its translucent, iridescent petals that shimmer with a bioluminescent glow at night. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific mineral-rich soil to flourish. Hologamium's unique phototropic properties allow it to absorb and reflect light in unusual ways.

Due to its rarity and ethereal beauty, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
