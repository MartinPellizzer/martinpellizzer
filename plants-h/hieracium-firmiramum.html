Hieracium firmiramum

Hieracium firmiramum, commonly known as the rigid hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, it thrives in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become a weed in cultivated areas.
