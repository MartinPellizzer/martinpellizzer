Habenaria staminodiata

Habenaria staminodiata, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structures. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
