Hieracium sylvaticum subsp. subtenuiflorum

Hieracium sylvaticum subsp.

subtenuiflorum, commonly known as the slenderflower wood hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems rise above the leaves, bearing small, yellowish flowers arranged in a single inflorescence.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous root system.
