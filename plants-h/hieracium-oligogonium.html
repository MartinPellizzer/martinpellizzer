Hieracium oligogonium

Hieracium oligogonium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium oligogonium is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense colonies.
