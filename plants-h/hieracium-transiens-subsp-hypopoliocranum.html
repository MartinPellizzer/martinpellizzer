Hieracium transiens subsp. hypopoliocranum

Hieracium transiens subsp.

hypopoliocranum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs, and it produces small, dandelion-like seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This subspecies is notable for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its potential use in ecological restoration projects.
