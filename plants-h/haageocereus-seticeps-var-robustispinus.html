Haageocereus seticeps var. robustispinus

Haageocereus seticeps var. robustispinus, commonly known as the robust spiny cactus, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in dense, spiny areoles, which provide protection against herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid landscapes.
