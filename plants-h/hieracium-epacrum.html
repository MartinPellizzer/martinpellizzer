Hieracium epacrum

Hieracium epacrum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the dandelion family (Compositae).

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosette of leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in grassy meadows and disturbed soils. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often glandular, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is an annual or biennial herb that plays a role in pollinator habitats.
