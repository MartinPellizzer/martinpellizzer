Hieracium sparsum subsp. concinnidens

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

concinnidens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and basal rosettes of narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a single head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
