Hydrangea rehderiana

Hydrangea rehderiana, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from blue to pink depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The flowers, which bloom in late summer, are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
