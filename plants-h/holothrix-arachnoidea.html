Holothrix arachnoidea

Holothrix arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider's web plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded rosette of fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are covered in fine, hair-like structures resembling spider webs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
