Hippeastrum albispiritum

Hippeastrum albispiritum, commonly known as the white spirit amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that emerge from a central bulb and can grow up to several feet in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum albispiritum is popular as a ornamental plant due to its dramatic floral display and relatively easy care requirements.
