Hibiscus rosiflorus

Hibiscus rosiflorus, commonly known as the rock hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

Hibiscus rosiflorus is also notable for its ability to bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions.
