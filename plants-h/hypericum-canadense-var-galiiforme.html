Hypericum canadense var. galiiforme

Hypericum canadense var. galiiforme, commonly known as Canadian St. John's Wort, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and stems that are often reddish or purple in color. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. It has opposite, linear to lanceolate leaves and produces small, yellow-orange flowers in late spring to early summer.

The species is part of the genus Hypericum, which includes many plants known for their medicinal properties.
