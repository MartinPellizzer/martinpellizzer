Habenaria renziana

Habenaria renziana, commonly known as the Zanzibar orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the coastal regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with purple markings. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often near the coast or in forested areas. Its roots are fibrous, and it has linear, grass-like leaves that help it thrive in moist conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
