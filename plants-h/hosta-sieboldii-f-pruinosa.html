Hosta sieboldii f. pruinosa

Hosta sieboldii f. pruinosa, commonly known as the Prunose Hosta, is a varietal form of the species Hosta sieboldii.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy, and often bluish-green leaves that are covered with a fine, powdery coating, giving them a distinctive pruinose appearance. The plant is native to Japan and is valued for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowers, which are small and inconspicuous. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for shade-loving gardeners.
