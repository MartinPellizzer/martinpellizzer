Hieracium murorum subsp. firmifrons

Hieracium murorum subsp.

firmifrons, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has linear to lanceolate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a common sight in meadows and along roadsides.
