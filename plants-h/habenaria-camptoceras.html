Habenaria camptoceras

Habenaria camptoceras, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Habenaria camptoceras is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
