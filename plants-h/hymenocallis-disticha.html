Hymenocallis disticha

Hymenocallis disticha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a prominent yellow or orange stripe down the center. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are arranged in a fan shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
