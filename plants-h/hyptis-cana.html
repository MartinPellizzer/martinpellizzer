Hyptis cana

Hyptis cana, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Hyptis cana is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
