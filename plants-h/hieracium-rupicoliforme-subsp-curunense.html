Hieracium rupicoliforme subsp. curunense

Hieracium rupicoliforme subsp.

curunense, commonly known as the mountain hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and Eastern Europe. It typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers with ray florets. Its leaves are usually lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

This subspecies is notable for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
