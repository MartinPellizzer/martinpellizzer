Haworthia clariperla

Haworthia clariperla, commonly known as the white-flowered Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white markings and a distinctive white bloom at the center. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
