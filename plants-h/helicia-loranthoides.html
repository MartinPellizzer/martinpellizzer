Helicia loranthoides

Helicia loranthoides, commonly known as the parasitic honeycomb tree, is a hemiparasitic plant belonging to the family Theaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its woody structure and small, clustered flowers. It plays a role in local ecosystems by influencing host tree growth and providing habitat for various insects.
