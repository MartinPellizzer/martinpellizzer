Hieracium murorum subsp. macropterum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

macropterum, commonly known as the large-winged hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its larger, more robust leaves and notably elongated, winged bracts. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

This plant is often considered a weed but plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
