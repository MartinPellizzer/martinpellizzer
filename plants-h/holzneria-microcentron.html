Holzneria microcentron

Holzneria microcentron, commonly known as the dwarf star grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South America.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact appearance. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow, adding a delicate touch to its foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
