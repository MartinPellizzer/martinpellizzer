Hatiora gaertneri f. serrata

Hatiora gaertneri f. serrata, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a hybrid species belonging to the Schlumbergera genus.

It is characterized by its segmented, succulent stems that bear vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is native to Brazil and thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to bloom during the holiday season.

Its unique morphology and seasonal flowering make it a distinctive addition to botanical collections.
