Hemigraphis reptans var. glaucescens

Hemigraphis reptans var. glaucescens, commonly known as the silver moss plant, is a compact, low-growing herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with leaves that have a distinctive silver or bluish-green coloration due to a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink, though they are not the main ornamental feature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in landscape design for its ornamental value and ability to form dense ground covers.

Its foliage is also popular in indoor gardening for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
