Hieronymiella

It consists of a single species, Hieronymiella thunbergii, which is native to eastern Asia, particularly Japan. The plant is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and a tall, slender flowering stem that produces small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
