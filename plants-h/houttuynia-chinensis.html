Houttuynia chinensis

Houttuynia chinensis, commonly known as the Indian nettle or snake plant, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white and yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant cultivation.
