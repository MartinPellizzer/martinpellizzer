Hieracium alupkanum

Hieracium alupkanum, commonly known as the Alupkan hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disk florets. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in rocky and alpine environments. The plant has a low-growing habit and is often found in open, sunny habitats at high elevations.

It is a perennial species with a fibrous root system adapted to harsh, cold climates.
