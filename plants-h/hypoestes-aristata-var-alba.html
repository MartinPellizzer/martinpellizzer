Hypoestes aristata var. alba

Hypoestes aristata var. alba, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is popular in gardens and as a potted plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
