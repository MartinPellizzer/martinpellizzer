Hymenophyllum applanatum

It is characterized by its small, closely spaced leaves that are typically less than 1 millimeter in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or tree trunks in temperate regions. Its leaves are usually flat and have a smooth, glossy surface.
