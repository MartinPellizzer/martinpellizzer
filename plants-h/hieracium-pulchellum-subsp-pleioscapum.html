Hieracium pulchellum subsp. pleioscapum

Hieracium pulchellum subsp.

pleioscapum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has basal and stem leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture.

Its flowers are arranged in a dense cluster, making it easily recognizable in its native environment.
