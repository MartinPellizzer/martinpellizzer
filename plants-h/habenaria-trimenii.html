Habenaria trimenii

Habenaria trimenii, commonly known as the Trimonti's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are small and clustered, with three sepals and three petals, and it produces a unique, fragrant scent.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
