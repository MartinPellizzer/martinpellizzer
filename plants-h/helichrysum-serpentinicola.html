Helichrysum serpentinicola

Helichrysum serpentinicola, commonly known as the serpentine helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils, often found on serpentine substrates, which are rich in minerals like magnesium and iron. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
