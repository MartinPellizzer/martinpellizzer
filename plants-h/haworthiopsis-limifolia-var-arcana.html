Haworthiopsis limifolia var. arcana

Haworthiopsis limifolia var. arcana, commonly known as the narrow-leafed haworthiopsis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure and fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
