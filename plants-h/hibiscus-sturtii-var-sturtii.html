Hibiscus sturtii var. sturtii

Hibiscus sturtii var. sturtii, commonly known as Sturt's desert rose, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Australia.

It features distinctive, velvety, pink to red flowers that bloom during the cooler months. The plant has woody stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a hardy plant for dry climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in semi-arid environments.
