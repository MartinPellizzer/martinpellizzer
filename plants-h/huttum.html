Huttum

Huttum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety, waxy leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom from the center of the leaves. Huttum prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
