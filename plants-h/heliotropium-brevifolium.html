Heliotropium brevifolium

Heliotropium brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Heliotropium brevifolium is a herbaceous perennial that blooms throughout the growing season.
