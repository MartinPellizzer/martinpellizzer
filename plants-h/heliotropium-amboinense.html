Heliotropium amboinense

Heliotropium amboinense, commonly known as the sacred heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant is often found in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are typically opposite and ovate, with a slightly hairy surface.

Heliotropium amboinense is also noted for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
