Hieracium viride subsp. caerulaceum

Hieracium viride subsp.

caerulaceum, commonly known as blue hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features bright blue to violet flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically basal, with a rough texture and a bluish-green color.

It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
