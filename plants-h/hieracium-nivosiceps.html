Hieracium nivosiceps

Hieracium nivosiceps, commonly known as the snow-haired hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in moist meadows, woodlands, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are covered in dense, white to grayish hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hieracium nivosiceps is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads.
