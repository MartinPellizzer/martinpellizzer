Haworthia deltoidea

Haworthia deltoidea, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, triangular leaves with white or yellow tubercles that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant is known for its compact, rosette-like growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soil. It requires minimal water and prefers bright, indirect light.

Haworthia deltoidea is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
