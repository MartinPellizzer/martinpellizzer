Hypericum thasium

Hypericum thasium, commonly known as the Thasian St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, which are typical features of the Hypericaceae family. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hypericum thasium is known for its medicinal properties, similar to other members of its genus.
