Hemiboea follicularis var. retroflexa

Hemiboea follicularis var. retroflexa, commonly known as the Cape coral plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which attract pollinators such as birds and insects.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
