Haworthia albispina

Haworthia albispina, commonly known as the white-spined Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on short stalks during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
