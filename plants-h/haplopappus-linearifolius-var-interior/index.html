Haplopappus Linearifolius Var. Interior

Haplopappus linearifolius var. interior is a unique and resilient plant native to the arid regions of North America. It thrives in harsh environments with limited water and extreme temperatures. This variety is known for its distinctive linear leaves and compact growth habit. Its ability to survive in tough conditions makes it an important species for ecological studies. Despite its hardiness, it remains relatively obscure in popular horticultural circles.
