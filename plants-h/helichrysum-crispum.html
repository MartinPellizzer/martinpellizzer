Helichrysum crispum

Helichrysum crispum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is perennial and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a border plant.

The flowers are known for their long-lasting dried appearance, making them popular in floral arrangements.
