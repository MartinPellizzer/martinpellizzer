Hoya dalrympliana

Hoya dalrympliana, commonly known as the "Wax Plant" or "Kiss Me" plant, is a popular species within the Hoya genus.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, or white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and glossy, with a velvety texture. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is a trailing plant that can be grown as a hanging plant or trained to climb, making it a versatile choice for indoor gardening.
