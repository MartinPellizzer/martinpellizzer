Hieracium praecox var. serratense

Hieracium praecox var. serratense, commonly known as the serrated early hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils. It has rough, serrated leaf margins, which give it its common name.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
