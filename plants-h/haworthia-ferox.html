Haworthia ferox

Haworthia ferox, commonly known as the fierce Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The leaves are covered in a fine layer of wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
