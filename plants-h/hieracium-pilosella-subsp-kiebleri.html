Hieracium pilosella subsp. kiebleri

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has hairy stems and leaves, with small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
