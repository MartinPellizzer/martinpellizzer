Helichrysum yurterianum

Helichrysum yurterianum, commonly known as the Yurterian everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and showy yellow or golden flowers. The plant produces small, bract-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

The flowers are long-lasting and retain their color when dried, making the plant popular in floral arrangements.
