Hieracium lissoterum

Hieracium lissoterum, commonly known as the smoothleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and slightly hairy. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

Hieracium lissoterum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it is considered a weed in some agricultural areas.
