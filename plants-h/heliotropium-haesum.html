Heliotropium haesum

Heliotropium haesum, commonly known as the blue heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a faint, sweet fragrance. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and usually covered in fine hairs.

Heliotropium haesum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.
