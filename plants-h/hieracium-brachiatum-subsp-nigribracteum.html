Hieracium brachiatum subsp. nigribracteum

Hieracium brachiatum subsp.

nigribracteum, commonly known as the black bract hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and distinctive, dark-colored bracts that enclose the flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has hairy stems and basal leaves that are often lobed or serrated.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is considered a minor weed in some regions.
