Hypolepis repens var. colorata

Hypolepis repens var. colorata, commonly known as the variegated water fern, is a variant of the water fern species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, frond-like leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and sometimes pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in humid forests. Its rhizomes spread along the ground, allowing it to form dense mats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in aquatic gardens and as a houseplant.
