Habenaria protearum

Habenaria protearum, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in coastal areas. Its flowers are usually pale pink or white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Habenaria protearum is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
