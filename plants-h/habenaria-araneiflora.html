Habenaria araneiflora

Habenaria araneiflora, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like floral structure, which gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, pale green to yellowish flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, thriving in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject in both horticulture and botanical research.
