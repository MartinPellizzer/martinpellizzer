Hieracium rupicola subsp. lasiophyllum

Hieracium rupicola subsp.

lasiophyllum, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in rocky and gravelly soils, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.
