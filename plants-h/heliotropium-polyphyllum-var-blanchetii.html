Heliotropium polyphyllum var. blanchetii

Heliotropium polyphyllum var. blanchetii, commonly known as the Blanchet's heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, tropical environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
