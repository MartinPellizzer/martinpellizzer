Hemizonia citriodora

Hemizonia citriodora, commonly known as lemon-scented honeysuckle, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lemon-scented leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces yellow-orange berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Hemizonia citriodora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
