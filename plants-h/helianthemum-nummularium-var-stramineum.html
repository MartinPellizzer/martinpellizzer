Helianthemum nummularium var. stramineum

Helianthemum nummularium var. stramineum, commonly known as the silver cushion carnation, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are rounded, silver-gray in color, and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
