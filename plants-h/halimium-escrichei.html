Halimium escrichei

Halimium escrichei, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically small, gray-green, and covered in a fine layer of hair.

Halimium escrichei is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
