Haworthia albicans var. virescens

Haworthia albicans var. virescens, commonly known as the green Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green color, often with white or yellowish markings. The plant is known for its ability to store water in its fleshy leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the summer months.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
