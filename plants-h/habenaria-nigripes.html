Habenaria nigripes

Habenaria nigripes, commonly known as the black-veined orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with dark purple or black veins. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered in some regions.
