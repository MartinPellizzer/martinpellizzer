Hieracium scheppigianum subsp. volujakense

Hieracium scheppigianum subsp.

volujakense, commonly known as the Volujakense Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

Native to Central Europe, it thrives in moist meadows and grasslands. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are typically arranged in a single head.

It is considered a minor weed but has ecological significance in its native habitat.
