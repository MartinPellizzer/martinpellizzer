Herschelia purpurascens

Herschelia purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking violet to purple blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

The flowers are borne on slender stems and are known for their intricate, fragrant structure.
