Habenaria longissima

Habenaria longissima, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its growth habit and floral structure make it a notable species within the Orchidaceae family.
