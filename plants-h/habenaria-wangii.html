Habenaria wangii

Habenaria wangii, commonly known as the Wang's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous root system and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are often short-lived, blooming for only a few days before wilting.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
