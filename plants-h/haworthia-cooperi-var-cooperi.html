Haworthia cooperi var. cooperi

Haworthia cooperi var. cooperi, commonly known as the cooper's haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green with white markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, making it well-suited for arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
