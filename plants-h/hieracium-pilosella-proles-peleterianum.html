Hieracium pilosella proles peleterianum

Hieracium pilosella proles peleterianum, commonly known as the velvetleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and yellow flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is often found in regions of Europe and Asia.
