Hypoestes loniceroides

Hypoestes loniceroides, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, blue-violet bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypoestes loniceroides thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
