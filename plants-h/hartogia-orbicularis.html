Hartogia orbicularis

Hartogia orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, orbicular-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has strap-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in well-drained soil. Hartogia orbicularis is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its unique floral structure makes it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
