Helichrysum dinteri

Helichrysum dinteri, commonly known as the Cape gold flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flower heads. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Helichrysum dinteri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
