Hieracium × caloscias

Hieracium × caloscias, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers, which are typically arranged in a single, showy head. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in wetlands.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
