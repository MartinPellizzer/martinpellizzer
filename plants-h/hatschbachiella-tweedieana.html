Hatschbachiella tweedieana

Hatschbachiella tweedieana, commonly known as the Tweedie's daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it blooms throughout the warmer months.
