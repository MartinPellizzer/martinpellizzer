Haworthia inconfluens

Haworthia inconfluens, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves with distinctive white or yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
