Habenaria dilatata var. media

Habenaria dilatata var. media, commonly known as the broad-leafed marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often in shaded environments with rich, organic soil. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with faint purple markings, and they are arranged in a loose raceme.

This orchid is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in moist forest understories.
