Hesperis runawurensis

Hesperis runawurensis, commonly known as the Tibetan Hesperis, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Tibet.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hair, adapted to the cold, high-altitude environment.
