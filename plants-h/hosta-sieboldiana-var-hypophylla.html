Hosta sieboldiana var. hypophylla

Hosta sieboldiana var. hypophylla, commonly known as the Siebold's Hosta, is a cultivar prized for its unique foliage.

It features broad, dark green leaves with a striking white to pale green variegation pattern. The plant is native to Japan and is well-suited for shaded garden environments. It produces small, pale greenish-yellow flowers in late summer.

This variety is particularly noted for its compact growth habit and ornamental leaf coloration.
