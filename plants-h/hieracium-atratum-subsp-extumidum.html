Hieracium atratum subsp. extumidum

Hieracium atratum subsp.

extumidum, commonly known as the dark hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and yellowish to reddish flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. It has a rosette growth form with basal leaves and produces a single flowering stem.

The subspecies is distinguished by its elongated, glandular leaves and the presence of a central stem with bracts.
