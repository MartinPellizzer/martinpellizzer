Hymenanthera novae-zelandiae

Hymenanthera novae-zelandiae, commonly known as the New Zealand honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring slender, often spiny stems. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a rough texture with a serrated margin.

The plant is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in coastal and forested areas.
