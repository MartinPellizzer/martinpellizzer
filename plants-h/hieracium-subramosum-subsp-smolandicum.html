Hieracium subramosum subsp. smolandicum

Hieracium subramosum subsp.

smolandicum, commonly known as the S Norwegian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides, meadows, and forest edges.

The plant has basal leaves that are broadly ovate to lanceolate, with hairy surfaces. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and are borne on slender stems.

It is characterized by its finely divided leaves and is often found in moist, temperate climates.
