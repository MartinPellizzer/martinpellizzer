Helianthemum oelandicum subsp. levigatum

Helianthemum oelandicum subsp.

levigatum, commonly known as the smooth rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe. It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, typically from May to June.

The plant has dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
