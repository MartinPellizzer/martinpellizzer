Hatiora salicornoides var. stricta

Hatiora salicornoides var. stricta, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often brightly colored and used for decorative purposes. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and orange, typically during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and long blooming period.
