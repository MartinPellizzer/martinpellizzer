Helichrysum salviifolium var. amboaboryense

Helichrysum salviifolium var. amboaboryense, commonly known as the golden everlasting or Amboaborye everlasting, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that are often used in floral arrangements. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

The variety amboaboryense is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and unique floral morphology.
