Hieracium dalecarlicum

Hieracium dalecarlicum, commonly known as the Swedish hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, numerous florets that form a composite flower head.

Hieracium dalecarlicum is often found in temperate regions and is considered a perennial herb.
