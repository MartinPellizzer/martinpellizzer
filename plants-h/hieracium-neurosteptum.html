Hieracium neurosteptum

Hieracium neurosteptum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy surface, and its yellow flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Hieracium neurosteptum is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various ailments.
