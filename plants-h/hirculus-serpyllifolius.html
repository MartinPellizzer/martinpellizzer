Hirculus serpyllifolius

Hirculus serpyllifolius, commonly known as the common rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with woody stems and small, evergreen leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is drought-tolerant.

Hirculus serpyllifolius is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
