Helenium laciniatum

Helenium laciniatum, commonly known as the cutleaf hedgerow, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, orange to red-orange ray florets. Helenium laciniatum is valued for its ornamental value and its role in supporting pollinators in wetland ecosystems.
