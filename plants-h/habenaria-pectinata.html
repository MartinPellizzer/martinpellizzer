Habenaria pectinata

Habenaria pectinata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Habenaria pectinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
