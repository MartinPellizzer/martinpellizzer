Hohenbergia Catingae Var. Horrida

Hohenbergia catingae var. horrida is a unique and striking plant native to Brazil's arid regions. It is known for its vibrant, spiky leaves that give it a distinctive, almost intimidating appearance. This variety thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for succulent enthusiasts. Its foliage often displays shades of green, red, and purple, adding visual interest to any collection. Despite its rugged look, it is a relatively easy plant to care for, offering both beauty and resilience.
