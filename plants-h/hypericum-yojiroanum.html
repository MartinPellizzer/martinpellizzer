Hypericum yojiroanum

Hypericum yojiroanum, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its botanical features include small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy surface and a distinctive, aromatic scent.

Hypericum yojiroanum is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks, thriving in moist, well-drained soil.
