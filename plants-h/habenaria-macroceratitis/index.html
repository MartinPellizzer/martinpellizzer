Habenaria Macroceratitis

Habenaria macroceratitis is a rare orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking floral structure and unique pollination mechanisms. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its conservation status is threatened due to habitat loss and over-collection. Studying Habenaria macroceratitis offers insights into orchid evolution and ecological adaptation.
