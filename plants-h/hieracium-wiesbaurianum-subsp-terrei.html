Hieracium wiesbaurianum subsp. terrei

Hieracium wiesbaurianum subsp.

terrei, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically has hairy stems and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species in certain regions.
