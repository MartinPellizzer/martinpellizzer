Habenaria armatissima

Habenaria armatissima, commonly known as the armed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hairy leaves and elongated, slender stems. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, typically in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
