Hydrangea yesoensis f. alba

Hydrangea yesoensis f. alba, commonly known as the white form of the Yesso hydrangea, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Hydrangeaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
