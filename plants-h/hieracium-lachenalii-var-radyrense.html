Hieracium lachenalii var. radyrense

Hieracium lachenalii var. radyrense, commonly known as the Radyrense hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous flowering heads arranged in a flat-topped cluster.
