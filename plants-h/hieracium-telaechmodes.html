Hieracium telaechmodes

Hieracium telaechmodes, commonly known as the Mexican hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
