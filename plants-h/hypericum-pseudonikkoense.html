Hypericum pseudonikkoense

Hypericum pseudonikkoense, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
