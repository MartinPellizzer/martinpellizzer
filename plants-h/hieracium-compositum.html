Hieracium compositum

Hieracium compositum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a single, upright stem that bears yellow flowers in a composite head. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium compositum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in many regions.
