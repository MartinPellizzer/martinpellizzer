Hieracium cernuiforme

Hieracium cernuiforme, commonly known as the bent-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features upright stems covered in fine, hairy hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a distinctive downward curve. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in dense inflorescences, resembling a cluster of small daisies.

Hieracium cernuiforme is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
