Heterostemma piperifolium var. cordatum

Heterostemma piperifolium var. cordatum, commonly known as the heart-leaf heterostemma, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
