Hieracium vitreicuspis

Hieracium vitreicuspis, commonly known as the glassy thimbleweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typical daisy-like appearance. The plant features finely divided, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, giving them a glassy appearance. Its flowers are small and yellow, typically appearing in late summer to early autumn.

Hieracium vitreicuspis is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.
