Hedysarum deltoideum

Hedysarum deltoideum, commonly known as the deltoid heddewar, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh, alpine environments.

Hedysarum deltoideum plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest in ecological restoration projects.
