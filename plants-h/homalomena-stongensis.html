Homalomena stongensis

Homalomena stongensis, commonly known as the Stong's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spatulate leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its flowers, which are typically yellow with a spadix, are followed by berries that contain the seeds.
