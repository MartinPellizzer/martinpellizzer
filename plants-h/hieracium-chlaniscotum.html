Hieracium chlaniscotum

Hieracium chlaniscotum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is found in temperate regions. It has finely divided leaves and is often mistaken for other hawkweed species due to its similar appearance.

Hieracium chlaniscotum is considered an invasive species in some areas, outcompeting native vegetation.
