Hierochloe pallida

Hierochloe pallida, commonly known as slender hairgrass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hierochloe pallida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
