Hieracium arvicola subsp. molendianum

Hieracium arvicola subsp.

molendianum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky and grassy habitats at high altitudes.

The plant has a rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. It is characterized by its hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and sensitive species.
