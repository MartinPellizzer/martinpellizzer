Hieracium bifidum subsp. stenolepidimorphum

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

stenolepidimorphum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture, with a distinctive bipinnate venation pattern. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

This subspecies is notable for its smaller, more compact leaves compared to other hawkweed species.
