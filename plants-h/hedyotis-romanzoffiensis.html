Hedyotis romanzoffiensis

Hedyotis romanzoffiensis, commonly known as the Zambian geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Hedyotis romanzoffiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
