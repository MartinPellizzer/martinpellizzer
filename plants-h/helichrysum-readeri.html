Helichrysum readeri

Helichrysum readeri, commonly known as the golden marguerite or yellow helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant is known for its aromatic foliage, which emits a subtle, spicy scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum readeri is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
