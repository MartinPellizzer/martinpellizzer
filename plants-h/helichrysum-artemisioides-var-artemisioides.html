Helichrysum artemisioides var. artemisioides

Helichrysum artemisioides var. artemisioides, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces papery, dried flowers that retain their color for extended periods, making it popular in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a favored choice for gardens and as a cut flower.
