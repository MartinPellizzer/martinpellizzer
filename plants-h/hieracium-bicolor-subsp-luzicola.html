Hieracium bicolor subsp. luzicola

Hieracium bicolor subsp.

luzicola, commonly known as the Luzicola hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges. It has basal leaves that are lanceolate and often covered with fine hairs.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the hawkweed group, with specific adaptations to its native habitat.
