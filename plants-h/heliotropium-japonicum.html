Heliotropium japonicum

Heliotropium japonicum, commonly known as Japanese heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
