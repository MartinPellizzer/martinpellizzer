Haemanthus zebrinus

Haemanthus zebrinus, commonly known as the Zebra lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant, sword-shaped leaves with distinctive white and green stripes, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in summer, adding a dramatic visual impact. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
