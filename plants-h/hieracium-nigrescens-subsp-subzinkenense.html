Hieracium nigrescens subsp. subzinkenense

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, bracteate flower heads.
