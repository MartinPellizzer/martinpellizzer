Hieracium sparsum subsp. tomiasae

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

tomiasae, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is a perennial species that spreads through both seeds and rhizomes.
