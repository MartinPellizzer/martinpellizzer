Hypericum multiflorum

Hypericum multiflorum, commonly known as the many-flowered St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum multiflorum is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
