Hieracium isonomoum

Hieracium isonomoum, commonly known as the variable hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, exhibiting a range of morphological variations. It has hairy stems and basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

Hieracium isonomoum is known for its ability to hybridize with other hawkweed species, contributing to its variability in appearance.
