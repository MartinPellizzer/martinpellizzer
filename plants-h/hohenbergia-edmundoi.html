Hohenbergia edmundoi

Hohenbergia edmundoi, commonly known as the Edmond's hohenbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit. The plant features vibrant, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy bloom, giving it a striking appearance. Its flowers, which are typically yellow or orange, emerge from the center of the rosette.

Hohenbergia edmundoi is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
