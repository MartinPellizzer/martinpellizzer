Hydnocarpus sumatranus var. sumatranus

Hydnocarpus sumatranus var. sumatranus, commonly known as the Sumatran ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant produces a distinctive type of fruit known as a hydnoid, which has a unique, sponge-like structure. Its leaves are typically compound, with elongated leaflets, and it flowers in clusters.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional remedies.
