Haworthia angolensis

Haworthia angolensis, commonly known as the Angolan Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
