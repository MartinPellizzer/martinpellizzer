Howardia fimbriata

Howardia fimbriata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped stems that are covered in small, triangular leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Howardia fimbriata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
