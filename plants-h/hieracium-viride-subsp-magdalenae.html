Hieracium viride subsp. magdalenae

Hieracium viride subsp.

magdalenae, commonly known as the Magdalen's green hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright green, hairy leaves and yellow daisy-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded conditions. It has a rosette growth habit with basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly smaller flowers and more compact growth compared to other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
