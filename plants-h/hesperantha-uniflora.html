Hesperantha uniflora

Hesperantha uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower orchid, is a species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and feature a unique, spurred shape.

Hesperantha uniflora is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
