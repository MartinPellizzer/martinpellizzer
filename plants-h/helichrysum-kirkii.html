Helichrysum kirkii

Helichrysum kirkii, commonly known as the golden crown, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even after drying. The plant is evergreen, with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum kirkii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
