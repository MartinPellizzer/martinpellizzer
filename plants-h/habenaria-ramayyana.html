Habenaria ramayyana

Habenaria ramayyana, commonly known as the Ramayana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous, and it has linear, grass-like leaves.

The flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with intricate patterns, making it a visually distinctive species.
