Hieracium nylanderianum

Hieracium nylanderianum, commonly known as the Nylander's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Nylander's hawkweed is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
