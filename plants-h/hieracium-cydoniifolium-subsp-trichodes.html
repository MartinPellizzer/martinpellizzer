Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp. trichodes

Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp.

trichodes, commonly known as the honeycomb hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to central and southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain regions.
