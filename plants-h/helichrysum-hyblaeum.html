Helichrysum hyblaeum

Helichrysum hyblaeum, commonly known as the Sicilian everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, bright yellow to orange flowers that remain open for a long period. The plant produces compact, bushy growth with narrow, gray-green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Helichrysum hyblaeum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
