Horsfieldia tuberculata var. tuberculata

Horsfieldia tuberculata var. tuberculata, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a tropical epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its unique tuberculate (bumpy) stems are a distinguishing characteristic.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
