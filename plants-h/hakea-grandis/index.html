Hakea Grandis

Hakea grandis is a striking evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia. It is known for its unique, bottle-shaped leaves and vibrant, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in spring. This plant is often found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for gardens and restoration projects. Hakea grandis not only adds visual appeal but also supports local wildlife and biodiversity.
