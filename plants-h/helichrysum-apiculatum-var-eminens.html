Helichrysum apiculatum var. eminens

Helichrysum apiculatum var. eminens, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is drought-tolerant and often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms.

The flowers are also known for their aromatic properties and are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
