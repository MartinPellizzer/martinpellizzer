Hydrangea xanthoneura var. sikangensis

Hydrangea xanthoneura var. sikangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hydrangea, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Hydrangeaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy bracts that can range in color from white to pink depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal. It produces small, clustered flowers that are often hidden beneath the colorful bracts.

This variety is relatively rare and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
