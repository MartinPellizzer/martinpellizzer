Hieracium malacotrichum

Hieracium malacotrichum, commonly known as the sticky hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

Hieracium malacotrichum is often found in grassy areas and can form dense colonies, making it a notable weed in some regions.
