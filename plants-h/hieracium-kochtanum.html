Hieracium kochtanum

Hieracium kochtanum, commonly known as the Kochtanum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It produces small, yellow to orange flower heads that are surrounded by pale green bracts.

Hieracium kochtanum is often found in temperate climates and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens with well-drained soil.
