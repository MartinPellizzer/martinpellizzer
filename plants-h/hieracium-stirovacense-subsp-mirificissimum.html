Hieracium stirovacense subsp. mirificissimum

Hieracium stirovacense subsp.

mirificissimum, commonly known as the Stirovacan hawkweed, is a rare flowering plant native to the Balkan region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its unique floral morphology and leaf structure. It thrives in calcareous soils and is often found in mountainous areas.
