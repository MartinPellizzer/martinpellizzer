Hieracium onychodontum

Hieracium onychodontum, commonly known as the mouse-ear hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium onychodontum is often found in clusters and can spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
