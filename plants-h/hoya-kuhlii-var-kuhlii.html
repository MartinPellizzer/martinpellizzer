Hoya kuhlii var. kuhlii

Hoya kuhlii var. kuhlii, commonly known as the Kuhlii Waffle Plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Hoya kuhlii var. kuhlii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
