Helleborine viridans

Helleborine viridans, commonly known as the green helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. It has a complex root system and can form dense colonies in suitable habitats.

Helleborine viridans is also known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
