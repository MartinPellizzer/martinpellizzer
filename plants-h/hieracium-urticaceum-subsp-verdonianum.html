Hieracium urticaceum subsp. verdonianum

Hieracium urticaceum subsp.

verdonianum, commonly known as the Verdonian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller leaves and shorter peduncles compared to other members of the species.
