Haworthia cyanea

Haworthia cyanea, commonly known as the blue haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with distinct white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Haworthia cyanea is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
