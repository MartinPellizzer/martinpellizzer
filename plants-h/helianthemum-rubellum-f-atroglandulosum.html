Helianthemum rubellum f. atroglandulosum

Helianthemum rubellum f. atroglandulosum, commonly known as the dark-glandular rockrose, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring, typically from May to June. The plant has glandular-pubescent stems and leaves, which are covered in fine, sticky hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or gravelly soils and prefers full sun.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
