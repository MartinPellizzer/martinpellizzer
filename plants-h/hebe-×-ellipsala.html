Hebe × ellipsala

Hebe × ellipsala, commonly known as the compact hebe, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, rounded growth with small, narrow leaves that are typically a dark green color. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, which bloom in spring and summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact form and drought tolerance.

Hebe × ellipsala is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its low maintenance requirements.
