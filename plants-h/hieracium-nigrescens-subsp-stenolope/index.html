Hieracium Nigrescens Subsp. Stenolope

Hieracium nigrescens subsp. stenolope is a lesser-known member of the daisy family known for its striking appearance and ecological significance. Found primarily in mountainous regions of Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and alpine habitats. Its dark purple flowers and narrow leaves make it easily distinguishable from other hawkweeds. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked by gardeners and botanists alike. Understanding this plant offers insights into the biodiversity of high-altitude ecosystems.
