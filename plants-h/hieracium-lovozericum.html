Hieracium lovozericum

Hieracium lovozericum, commonly known as the Lovozeric Hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces bright yellow composite flowers. Native to the Arctic and subarctic regions, this plant thrives in harsh, alpine environments. It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to cold climates with short growing seasons.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
