Hedysarum polybotrys var. polybotrys

Hedysarum polybotrys var. polybotrys, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds, which are often used in traditional Chinese cuisine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
