Hoya acuta var. citrina

Hoya acuta var. citrina, commonly known as the Lemon Hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant yellow-green color. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Hoya acuta var. citrina is also valued for its ability to purify the air and its aesthetic appeal.
