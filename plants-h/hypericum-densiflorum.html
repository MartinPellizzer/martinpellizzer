Hypericum densiflorum

Hypericum densiflorum, commonly known as denseflower St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which are characteristic of the Hypericum genus. These flowers are borne on dense, branched inflorescences, giving the plant its common name.

The leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a slightly glandular surface.
