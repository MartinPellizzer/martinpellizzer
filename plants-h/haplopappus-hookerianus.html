Haplopappus hookerianus

Haplopappus hookerianus, commonly known as Hooker's pappus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in dense heads, and the leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs.
