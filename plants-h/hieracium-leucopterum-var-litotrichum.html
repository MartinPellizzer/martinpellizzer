Hieracium leucopterum var. litotrichum

Hieracium leucopterum var. litotrichum, commonly known as the white-rayed hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, with white ray florets that give it its common name. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
