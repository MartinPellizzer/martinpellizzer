Hieracium aorense

Hieracium aorense, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies. Its yellow flowers, which bloom in summer, are surrounded by bracts that give it a distinctive appearance.

Hieracium aorense is known for its rapid spread and can become invasive in certain regions.
