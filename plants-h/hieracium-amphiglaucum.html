Hieracium amphiglaucum

Hieracium amphiglaucum, commonly known as the silverleaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. The flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
