Hyoseris knochei

Hyoseris knochei, commonly known as the Knoche's dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are followed by distinctive, pappus-equipped seeds that aid in wind dispersal.

Hyoseris knochei is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
