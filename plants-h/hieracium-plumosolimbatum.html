Hieracium plumosolimbatum

Hieracium plumosolimbatum, commonly known as the silky hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and basal leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
