Hornstedtia macrocheilos

Hornstedtia macrocheilos, commonly known as the large-lipped hornstedtia, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its showy, spurred flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps in water retention.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
