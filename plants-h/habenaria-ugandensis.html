Habenaria ugandensis

Habenaria ugandensis, commonly known as the Uganda butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its roots are fibrous, and it has strap-like leaves that help in water retention.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral structure.
