Hieracium erioleucum subsp. stenopodum

Hieracium erioleucum subsp.

stenopodum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces a flowering stem with numerous small florets.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the hawkweed genus.
