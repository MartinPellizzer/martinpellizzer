Hermannia abrotanoides

Hermannia abrotanoides, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Hermannia abrotanoides is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
