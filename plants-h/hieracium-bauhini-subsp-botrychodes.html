Hieracium bauhini subsp. botrychodes

Hieracium bauhini subsp.

botrychodes, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, often reddish stems and basal rosettes of leaves.
