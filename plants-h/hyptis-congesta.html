Hyptis congesta

Hyptis congesta, commonly known as Brazilian hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. Its leaves are often aromatic and have a minty scent, which is typical of the mint family.

Hyptis congesta is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
