Hieracium exilentiforme

Hieracium exilentiforme, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of narrowly lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
