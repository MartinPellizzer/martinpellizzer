Hymenocardia Acida Var. Mollis

Coral tree, scientifically known as Hymenocardia acida var. mollis, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. It is renowned for its vibrant coral-like flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal regions and lowland forests. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and water conditions to flourish.
