Haworthia emeralda

Haworthia emeralda, commonly known as the Emerald Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pink markings. The plant produces clusters of white flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
