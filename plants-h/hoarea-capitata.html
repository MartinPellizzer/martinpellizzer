Hoarea capitata

Hoarea capitata, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that resemble small, clustered tulips. Hoarea capitata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
