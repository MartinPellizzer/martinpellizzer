Hubertia multifoliosa

Hubertia multifoliosa, commonly known as the multileaf hubertia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and flowering make it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscapes.
