Helenium hookeri

Helenium hookeri, commonly known as the yellow helenium, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet, featuring narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, orange to yellow ray flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy or loamy soils.

Helenium hookeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in garden settings.
