Habenaria satsumana

Habenaria satsumana, commonly known as the Satsuma orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with prominent purple markings. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
