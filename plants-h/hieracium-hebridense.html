Hieracium hebridense

Hieracium hebridense, commonly known as the Hebridean hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas. It features bright yellow flowers that bloom from June to September.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
