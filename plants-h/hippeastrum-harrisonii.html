Hippeastrum harrisonii

Hippeastrum harrisonii, commonly known as the Harrison's amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that emerge from a bulbous base, typical of many amaryllis species. Its flowers are borne on tall, sturdy stems and can reach up to 3 feet in height.

Hippeastrum harrisonii is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
