Hoya bandongii

Hoya bandongii, commonly known as the "Bandung Wax Plant," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale pink blooms that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.
