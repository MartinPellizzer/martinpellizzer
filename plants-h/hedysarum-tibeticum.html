Hedysarum tibeticum

Hedysarum tibeticum, commonly known as the Tibetan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or orange flowers. The plant is adapted to cold and arid environments, often growing in rocky or alpine conditions. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems in its native habitat.

Hedysarum tibeticum is also of interest for its potential in ecological restoration projects.
