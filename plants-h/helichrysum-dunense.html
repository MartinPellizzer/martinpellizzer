Helichrysum dunense

Helichrysum dunense, commonly known as the yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even when dried. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its small, papery bracts give it a unique appearance, and it is often used in floral arrangements due to its longevity.

Helichrysum dunense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native habitats.
