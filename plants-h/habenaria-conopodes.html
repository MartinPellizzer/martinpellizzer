Habenaria conopodes

Habenaria conopodes, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, pale yellow or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along streams. Its inflorescence consists of a single spike bearing several small, clustered flowers.

Botanically, it is classified within the Orchidaceae family and is notable for its unique floral structure and growth habits.
