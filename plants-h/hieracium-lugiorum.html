Hieracium lugiorum

Hieracium lugiorum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, alternate leaves. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, disturbed soils such as meadows and roadside areas. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous small, daisy-like flower heads.

It is often found in open habitats and is considered a common weed in some regions.
