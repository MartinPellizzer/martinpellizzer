Helianthemum origanifolium var. valentinum

Helianthemum origanifolium var. valentinum, commonly known as the Valentine's Rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in spring. The plant has gray-green, aromatic foliage that is similar to oregano, giving it a distinctive scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
