Hieracium sommerfeltii subsp. zobelianum

Hieracium sommerfeltii subsp.

zobelianum, commonly known as the Zobelian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine soils, preferring well-drained environments. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.
