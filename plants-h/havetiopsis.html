Havetiopsis

Havetiopsis, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which gives it a unique appearance in gardens. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a versatile choice for ornamental landscaping.
