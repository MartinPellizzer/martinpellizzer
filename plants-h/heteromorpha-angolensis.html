Heteromorpha angolensis

Heteromorpha angolensis, commonly known as the Angola starfish plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the growing season, typically in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
