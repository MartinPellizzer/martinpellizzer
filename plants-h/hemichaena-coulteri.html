Hemichaena coulteri

Hemichaena coulteri, commonly known as Coulter's hemichaena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Hemichaena coulteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
