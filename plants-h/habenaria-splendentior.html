Habenaria splendentior

Habenaria splendentior, commonly known as the Splendid Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors such as pink, white, and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil, and it produces intricate, fragrant blooms during the warm season.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
