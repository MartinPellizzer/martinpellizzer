Houttuynia cordata var. variegata

Houttuynia cordata var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated heartleaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with striking variegation of white, yellow, and green, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in garden borders or as a ground cover.

Its foliage is aromatic and can be used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
