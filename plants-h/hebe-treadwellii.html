Hebe treadwellii

Hebe treadwellii, commonly known as the Treadwell hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant typically features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of violet or blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
