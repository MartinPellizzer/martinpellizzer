Haynea

Haynea, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and striking blue or purple flower heads. Native to coastal areas of the Northern Hemisphere, it thrives in salty, well-drained soils. The plant is often found in dunes and salt marshes, where it plays a role in stabilizing sandy environments.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in maritime ecosystems.
