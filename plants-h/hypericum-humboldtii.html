Hypericum humboldtii

Hypericum humboldtii, commonly known as the Humboldt goldenstar, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils. It has simple, opposite leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Hypericum humboldtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained conditions.
