Hieracium nipholasium

Hieracium nipholasium, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clustered heads. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring temperate climates. It has a taproot system and is often found in regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
