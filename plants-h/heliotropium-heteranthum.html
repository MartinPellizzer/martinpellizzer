Heliotropium heteranthum

Heliotropium heteranthum, commonly known as the tropical heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or herbaceous plant characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, violet or purple flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Heliotropium heteranthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
