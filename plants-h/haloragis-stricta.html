Haloragis stricta

Haloragis stricta, commonly known as the slender waterplantain, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or damp environments such as marshes and stream banks. The plant has slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense spikes and bloom during the spring and summer months.

Haloragis stricta plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic invertebrates.
