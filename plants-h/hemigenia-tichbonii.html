Hemigenia tichbonii

Hemigenia tichbonii, commonly known as the Tichbon's honeysuckle, is a small shrub native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in spring. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and heathland environments.

Hemigenia tichbonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
