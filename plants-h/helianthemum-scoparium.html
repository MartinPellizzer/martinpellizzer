Helianthemum scoparium

Helianthemum scoparium, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with dense, evergreen foliage that thrives in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry climates and full sun exposure, making it a hardy perennial.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
