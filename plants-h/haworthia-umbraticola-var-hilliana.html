Haworthia umbraticola var. hilliana

Haworthia umbraticola var. hilliana, commonly known as the Hill's shade haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color with white or pale markings. The plant is well adapted to low light conditions and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. It produces small, white flowers on slender stalks during the growing season.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
