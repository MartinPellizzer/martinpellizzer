Helichrysum moeserianum

Helichrysum moeserianum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that are densely packed and remain vibrant for extended periods. The plant has silver-gray foliage with hairy leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
