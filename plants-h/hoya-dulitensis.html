Hoya dulitensis

Hoya dulitensis, commonly known as the Maluku star, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asclepiadaceae.

It is native to Indonesia and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, star-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-like flowers that are often fragrant. Hoya dulitensis is a succulent plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
