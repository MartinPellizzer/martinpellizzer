Habenaria leucostachys var. viridis

Habenaria leucostachys var. viridis, commonly known as the green-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
