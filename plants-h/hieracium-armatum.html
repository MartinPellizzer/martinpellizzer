Hieracium armatum

Hieracium armatum, commonly known as the armed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can reach heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are hairy and often have a serrated edge, while the stems are covered in stiff, bristly hairs.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
