Hippeastrum moelleri

Hippeastrum moelleri, commonly known as the red-flowered amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with contrasting veins. The plant produces a tall, erect stem with broad, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It is a bulbous plant that thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight.

Hippeastrum moelleri is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
