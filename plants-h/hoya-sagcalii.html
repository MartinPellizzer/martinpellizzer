Hoya sagcalii

Hoya sagcalii, commonly known as the silver star hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with silver and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, waxy flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Hoya sagcalii is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
