Hieracium subulatum

Hieracium subulatum, commonly known as the needleleaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium subulatum is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
