Helichrysum whitei

Helichrysum whitei, commonly known as the Cape gold or white helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, with a fuzzy texture.

Helichrysum whitei is well adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its drought tolerance and attractive appearance.
