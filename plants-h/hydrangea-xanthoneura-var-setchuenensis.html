Hydrangea xanthoneura var. setchuenensis

Hydrangea xanthoneura var. setchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan hydrangea, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink and blue, depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with dark green, ovate leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
