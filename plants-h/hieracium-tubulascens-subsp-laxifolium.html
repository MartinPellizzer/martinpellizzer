Hieracium tubulascens subsp. laxifolium

Hieracium tubulascens subsp.

laxifolium, commonly known as the slenderleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a serrated edge. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it often grows in meadows and disturbed soils.

This subspecies is distinguished by its relatively slender foliage compared to other hawkweed species.
