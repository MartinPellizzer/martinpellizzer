Homalomena intermedia

Homalomena intermedia, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental and edible plants. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its striking foliage.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread readily in suitable conditions.
