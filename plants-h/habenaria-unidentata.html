Habenaria unidentata

Habenaria unidentata, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to various regions in Africa, including South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and dense, dark green leaves. The plant produces a tall, cylindrical spike of small, white to pale pink flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands.

The orchid is notable for its delicate appearance and is often found growing on rocks or in soil.
