Hydrolea ovata var. parvifolia

Hydrolea ovata var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved hydrolea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, arranged in dense clusters.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
