Habenaria gilbertii

Habenaria gilbertii, commonly known as Gilbert's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of small, pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is often found in coastal regions and is considered a rare and protected species in some areas.
