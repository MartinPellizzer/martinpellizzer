Hieracium acromanum

Hieracium acromanum, commonly known as the acromantic hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has basal and stem leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped.

Hieracium acromanum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
