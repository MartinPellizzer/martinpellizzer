Hieracium propinquum

Hieracium propinquum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It has a creeping growth habit with rhizomes that allow it to spread vegetatively.

Hieracium propinquum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to colonize various habitats.
