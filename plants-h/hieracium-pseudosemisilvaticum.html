Hieracium pseudosemisilvaticum

Hieracium pseudosemisilvaticum, commonly known as the false wood sorrel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland areas and has a creeping growth habit. It produces achenes as its seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
