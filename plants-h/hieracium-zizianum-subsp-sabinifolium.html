Hieracium zizianum subsp. sabinifolium

Hieracium zizianum subsp.

sabinifolium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters.

The plant features narrow, elongated leaves and produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster. Its flowering period occurs from late spring to early summer, and it is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and seeds.

The species is characterized by its distinctive, finely divided foliage and its role in local ecosystems.
