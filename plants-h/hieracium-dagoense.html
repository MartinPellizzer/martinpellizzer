Hieracium dagoense

Hieracium dagoense, commonly known as the Dago hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom from spring to early summer.

Hieracium dagoense is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
