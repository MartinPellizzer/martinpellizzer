Hieracium macroradium

Hieracium macroradium, commonly known as the large-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to lanceolate, with rough, hairy surfaces. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and bear numerous yellow flowers in clusters.

Hieracium macroradium is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
