Hieracium serenaiae

Hieracium serenaiae, commonly known as the Serenae Hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming basal leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a relatively short stature, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare species in certain regions of Europe.
