Heteropanax nitentifolius

Heteropanax nitentifolius, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are typically divided into five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by fruiting bodies that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also noted for its ability to tolerate a range of soil types and its relatively fast growth rate.
