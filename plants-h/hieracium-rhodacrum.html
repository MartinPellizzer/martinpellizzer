Hieracium rhodacrum

Hieracium rhodacrum, commonly known as the red-hair hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a reddish tinge.

Hieracium rhodacrum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
