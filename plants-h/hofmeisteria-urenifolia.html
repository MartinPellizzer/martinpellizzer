Hofmeisteria urenifolia

Hofmeisteria urenifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf hofmeisteria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name.

Hofmeisteria urenifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
