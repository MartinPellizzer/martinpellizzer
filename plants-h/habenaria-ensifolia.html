Habenaria ensifolia

Habenaria ensifolia, commonly known as the sword-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its linear, grass-like leaves and produces spikes of fragrant, often yellow or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and unique floral structure.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ease of cultivation.
