Hieracium scorzonerifolium proles chlorifolium

Hieracium scorzonerifolium proles chlorifolium, commonly known as the green-leafed hawkweed, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering head composed of yellow disk flowers surrounded by ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is found in meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
