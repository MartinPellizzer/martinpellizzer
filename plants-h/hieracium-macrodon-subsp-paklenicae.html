Hieracium macrodon subsp. paklenicae

Hieracium macrodon subsp.

paklenicae, commonly known as the Paklenica hawkweed, is a rare endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Croatia. It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and sunny exposure. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered in fine pubescence, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
