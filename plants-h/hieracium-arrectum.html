Hieracium arrectum

Hieracium arrectum, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are sturdy and often branched.

Hieracium arrectum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many temperate regions.
