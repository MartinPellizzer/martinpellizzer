Hieracium bupleuroides subsp. malacosericeum

Hieracium bupleuroides subsp.

malacosericeum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers.

The plant typically has basal leaves that are lanceolate and hairy, while the upper leaves are smaller and sessile. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, pastures, and woodland edges.

Its flowering period occurs from late spring to early summer, and it is often found in moist or calcareous soils.
