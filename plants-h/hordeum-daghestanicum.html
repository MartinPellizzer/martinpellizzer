Hordeum daghestanicum

Hordeum daghestanicum, commonly known as the Daghestan barley, is a species of grass native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spikelet-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions. Its seeds are small and oval, with a tough outer husk.

Hordeum daghestanicum is of interest to botanists and agricultural researchers due to its potential for cultivation in arid regions.
