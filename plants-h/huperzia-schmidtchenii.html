Huperzia schmidtchenii

Huperzia schmidtchenii, commonly known as the Schmidtchen's huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and the presence of sporangia on the underside of its leaves. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizome system is robust, allowing it to anchor firmly in soil and absorb nutrients efficiently.

The plant's unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
