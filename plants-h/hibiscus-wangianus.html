Hibiscus wangianus

Hibiscus wangianus, commonly known as the Wangian hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to New Guinea.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 meters and has palmately lobed leaves. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are often visited by birds and insects.

Hibiscus wangianus thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in rainforest understories.
