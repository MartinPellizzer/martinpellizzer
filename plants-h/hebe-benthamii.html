Hebe benthamii

Hebe benthamii, commonly known as the Canterbury knobby hebe, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.

Hebe benthamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
