Helianthemum tenuicaule

Helianthemum tenuicaule, commonly known as the slender rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula and northern Africa.

It features small, yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compact, evergreen foliage with silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Helianthemum tenuicaule is drought-tolerant and well adapted to Mediterranean climates.
