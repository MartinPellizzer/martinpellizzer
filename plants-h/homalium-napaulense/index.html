Homalium Napaulense

Homalium napaulense is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Indian subcontinent. It is known for its striking, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is commonly found in forest understories. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners and horticulturists. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively underappreciated in global botanical circles.
