Helichrysum thorbeckei

Helichrysum thorbeckei, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers. The plant produces small, papery bracts that give it a unique, cup-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Helichrysum thorbeckei is also noted for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
