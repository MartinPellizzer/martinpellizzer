Hieracium curtispicans

Hieracium curtispicans, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grasslands and open habitats. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a rough texture, which helps it thrive in various environmental conditions.

It is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
