Haworthia marginata var. virescens

Haworthia marginata var. virescens, commonly known as the Green Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red at the base, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and moderate sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
