Haworthia gracilis var. minor

Haworthia gracilis var. minor, commonly known as the miniature Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, bluish-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with white or pale markings. The plant produces short, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Due to its small size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
