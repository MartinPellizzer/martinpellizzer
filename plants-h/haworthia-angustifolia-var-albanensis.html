Haworthia angustifolia var. albanensis

Haworthia angustifolia var. albanensis, commonly known as the Albanian narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
