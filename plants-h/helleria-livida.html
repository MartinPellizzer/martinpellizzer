Helleria livida

Helleria livida, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Helleria livida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
