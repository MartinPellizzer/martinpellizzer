Hosta kikutii var. yakusimensis

Hosta kikutii var. yakusimensis, commonly known as the Yakusimensis Hosta, is a striking perennial plant native to Japan.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are a deep, dark green with a subtle metallic sheen. The foliage often has a waxy texture and can exhibit slight variegation in some cultivars. This plant thrives in shady, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes primarily from its lush, textured foliage rather than its flowers.
