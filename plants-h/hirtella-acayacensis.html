Hirtella acayacensis

Hirtella acayacensis, commonly known as the Brazilian hirtella, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest, specifically found in Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, short hairs. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Hirtella acayacensis is typically found in humid, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests.
