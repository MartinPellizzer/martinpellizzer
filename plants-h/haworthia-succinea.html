Haworthia succinea

Haworthia succinea, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy, triangular leaves with white to yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant typically produces small, white or pale green flowers on slender stalks during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
