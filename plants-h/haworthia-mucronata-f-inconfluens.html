Haworthia mucronata f. inconfluens

Haworthia mucronata f. inconfluens, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically a pale green to bluish-green color. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and unique foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
