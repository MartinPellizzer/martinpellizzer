Heracleum australe f. wendtioides

Heracleum australe f. wendtioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cowparade, is a variant of the broad-leaved cowparade, characterized by its narrower leaves.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Australia, typically found in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in umbels, which bloom in spring. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is notable for its adaptability to arid conditions and its role in local ecosystems.
