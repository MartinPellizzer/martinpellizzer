Homalomena hottae

Homalomena hottae, commonly known as the Hottae arrowleaf.

It is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its unique inflorescence structure called a spadix, surrounded by a spathe. Homalomena hottae prefers bright, indirect light and thrives in humid environments with well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
