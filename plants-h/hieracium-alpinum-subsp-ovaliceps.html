Hieracium alpinum subsp. ovaliceps

Hieracium alpinum subsp.

ovaliceps, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer.

The plant is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has a taproot system and is adapted to cold, harsh environments.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient species within its ecological niche.
