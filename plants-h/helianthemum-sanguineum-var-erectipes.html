Helianthemum sanguineum var. erectipes

Helianthemum sanguineum var. erectipes, commonly known as the upright rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, evergreen leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red or pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and hardy addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
