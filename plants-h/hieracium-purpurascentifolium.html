Hieracium purpurascentifolium

Hieracium purpurascentifolium, commonly known as the purple-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are broadly ovate with a distinctive purple-tinged underside, and the flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is an annual or biennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
