Helichrysum keniense

Helichrysum keniense, commonly known as Kenyan immortelle, is a flowering plant native to Kenya.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange, sunflower-like blooms. The plant produces small, hairy leaves and has a compact growth habit. It thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Helichrysum keniense is known for its drought tolerance and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
