Halimus portulacastrum

Halimus portulacastrum, commonly known as the desert purslane, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant has a robust root system that helps it survive in dry conditions. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Its ability to store water makes it well-adapted to harsh desert environments.
