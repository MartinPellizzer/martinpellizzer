Hypericum jankae

Hypericum jankae, commonly known as the Janka's St. John's Wort, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Europe.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of flowering plants commonly referred to as St. John's Wort. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and opposite, simple leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Hypericum jankae is noted for its ornamental value and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
