Hirculus ranunculoides

Hirculus ranunculoides, commonly known as the goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has a creeping habit with hairy stems. Its seeds are encased in a pod that splits open when mature, dispersing the seeds by wind.

Hirculus ranunculoides is also known for its historical use in traditional medicine.
