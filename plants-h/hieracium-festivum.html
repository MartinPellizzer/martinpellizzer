Hieracium festivum

Hieracium festivum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, with ray florets that resemble petals. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium festivum is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
