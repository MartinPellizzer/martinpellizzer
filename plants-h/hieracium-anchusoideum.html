Hieracium anchusoideum

Hieracium anchusoideum, commonly known as the Anchusoideum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lanceolate and hairy, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
