Hieracium ramosissimum subsp. garibaldianum

Hieracium ramosissimum subsp.

garibaldianum, commonly known as the Garibaldian Hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer.
