Hieracium leviculum

Hieracium leviculum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It is characterized by its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in disturbed habitats.
