Hoya corollimarginata

Hoya corollimarginata, commonly known as the "Coral Margin Hoya," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a reddish or pinkish margin. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink, often with a subtle fragrance. Hoya corollimarginata is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

It is also noted for its slow growth and ability to develop aerial roots, making it a unique addition to any indoor garden.
