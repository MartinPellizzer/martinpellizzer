Hopea griffithii var. pedicellata

Hopea griffithii var. pedicellata, commonly known as the pedicellate hopea, is a species of evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland tropical forests.
