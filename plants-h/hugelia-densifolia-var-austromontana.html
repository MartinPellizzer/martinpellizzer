Hugelia densifolia var. austromontana

Hugelia densifolia var. austromontana, commonly known as the Australian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adaptable to a variety of light conditions, making it a popular ornamental plant.
