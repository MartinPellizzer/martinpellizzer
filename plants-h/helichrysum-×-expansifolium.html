Helichrysum × expansifolium

Helichrysum × expansifolium, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant nature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
