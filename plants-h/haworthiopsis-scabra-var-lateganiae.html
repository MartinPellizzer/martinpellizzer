Haworthiopsis scabra var. lateganiae

Haworthiopsis scabra var. lateganiae, commonly known as the Lategan's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically a mix of green and grayish-blue, with prominent white markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a potted plant due to its drought tolerance and minimal care requirements.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
