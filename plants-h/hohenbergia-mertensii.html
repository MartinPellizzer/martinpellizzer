Hohenbergia mertensii

Hohenbergia mertensii, commonly known as the silver stripe bromeliad, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, silver-striped leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette. Hohenbergia mertensii is a compact, evergreen perennial that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is relatively low maintenance and popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental appeal.
