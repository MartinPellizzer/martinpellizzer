Hyptis heterophylla

Hyptis heterophylla, commonly known as the variable hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves vary in shape and size, which contributes to its common name.

Hyptis heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
