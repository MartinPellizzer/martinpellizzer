Hieracium kingshousense

Hieracium kingshousense, commonly known as the Kingshouse hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and waste areas. It has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom from June to August.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species in the UK.
