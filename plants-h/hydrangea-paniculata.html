Hydrangea paniculata

Hydrangea paniculata, commonly known as the smooth hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, cone-shaped flower clusters that can range in color from white to pink, red, or blue depending on soil pH. The plant produces showy inflorescences that develop from clusters of small, white flowers. Its foliage is typically dark green, and it thrives in partial to full sun with moist, well-drained soil.

Hydrangea paniculata is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
