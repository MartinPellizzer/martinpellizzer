Hohenbergia penduliflora

Hohenbergia penduliflora, commonly known as the pendant-flowered hohenbergia, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish or bronze hue. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing small, colorful flowers, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hohenbergia penduliflora is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
