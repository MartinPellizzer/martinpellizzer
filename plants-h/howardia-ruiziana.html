Howardia ruiziana

Howardia ruiziana, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, striped leaves that resemble a zebra's coat, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is known for its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Howardia ruiziana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
