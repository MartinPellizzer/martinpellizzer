Helichrysum nitens

Helichrysum nitens, commonly known as the silver leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers and silvery-gray foliage. The plant is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
