Hedysarum stenorhizum

Hedysarum stenorhizum, commonly known as narrow-rooted milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces yellow to reddish-purple pea-like flowers. Its fruit is a legume containing several elliptical seeds.

Hedysarum stenorhizum is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
