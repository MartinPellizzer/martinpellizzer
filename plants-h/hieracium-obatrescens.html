Hieracium obatrescens

Hieracium obatrescens, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a rough texture. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium obatrescens is typically perennial, with a robust root system that allows it to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
