Habenaria cerea var. polyantha

Habenaria cerea var. polyantha, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette, while the inflorescence consists of a raceme of fragrant, spurred flowers.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
