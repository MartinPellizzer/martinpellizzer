Habenaria leprieurii

Habenaria leprieurii, commonly known as the Leprieur's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, white or pale yellow blooms with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
