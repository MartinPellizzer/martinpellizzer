Hypericum beamanii

Hypericum beamanii, commonly known as the Beaman's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Hypericum beamanii is often found in rocky soils and dry habitats, thriving in full sun.
