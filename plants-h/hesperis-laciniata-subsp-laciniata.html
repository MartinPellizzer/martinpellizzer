Hesperis laciniata subsp. laciniata

Hesperis laciniata subsp.

laciniata, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Hesperis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a biennial or short-lived perennial that produces striking white or pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer.

The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically narrow and linear. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and prolonged blooming period.
