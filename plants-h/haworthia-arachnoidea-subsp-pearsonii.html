Haworthia arachnoidea subsp. pearsonii

Haworthia arachnoidea subsp.

pearsonii, commonly known as the pearson's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa. It features dense rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white to pale blue markings.

The leaves are covered in fine, hair-like structures that give it a distinctive, almost spider-like appearance. It produces small, star-shaped white flowers on slender stems during the growing season.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
