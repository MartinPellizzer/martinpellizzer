Hyalea pergamacea

Hyalea pergamacea, commonly known as the parchment moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its thin, flat, and often translucent leaves that resemble parchment, hence its common name. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate regions. Hyalea pergamacea is often found growing on rocks or soil in forest floors.

Its simple structure and unique appearance make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
