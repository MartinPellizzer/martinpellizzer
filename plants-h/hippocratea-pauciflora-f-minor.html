Hippocratea pauciflora f. minor

Hippocratea pauciflora f. minor, commonly known as the lesser few-flowered hippocratea, is a rare and unique variant of the hippocratea genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and reduced floral display compared to the typical form. The plant features small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, often with a subtle fragrance. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
