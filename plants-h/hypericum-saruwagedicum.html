Hypericum saruwagedicum

Hypericum saruwagedicum, commonly known as the Saruwagedic St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in summer and are often accompanied by orange-red stamens. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and oval-shaped with a slightly serrated margin.

The plant is adapted to humid, shaded environments and is often found growing in dense undergrowth.
