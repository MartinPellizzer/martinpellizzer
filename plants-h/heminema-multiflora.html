Heminema multiflora

Heminema multiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered heminema, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Heminema multiflora is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
