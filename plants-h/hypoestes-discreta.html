Hypoestes discreta

Hypoestes discreta, commonly known as the Indian Violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated leaves that display shades of pink, red, and white. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Hypoestes discreta is known for its ease of care and ability to bloom throughout the year under proper conditions.
