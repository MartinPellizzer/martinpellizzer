Habenaria propinquior

Habenaria propinquior, commonly known as the common pink orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in a loose cluster, and have a distinctive, spurred lip.

Habenaria propinquior is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its attractive blooms.
