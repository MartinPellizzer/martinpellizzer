Hebe cassinioides

Hebe cassinioides, commonly known as the silver hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and spikes of blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils, often in coastal or alpine regions. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.
