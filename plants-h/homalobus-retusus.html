Homalobus retusus

Homalobus retusus, commonly known as the smoothleaf thornapple, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, violet to purple flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
