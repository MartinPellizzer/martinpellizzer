Hadrolaelia jongheana

Hadrolaelia jongheana, commonly known as the Congo orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces a single spike with several flowers, each featuring intricate, fragrant petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and habitat destruction, it is considered an endangered species.
