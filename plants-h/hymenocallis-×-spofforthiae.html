Hymenocallis × spofforthiae

Hymenocallis × spofforthiae, commonly known as the Spofforth's spider lily, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with strap-like, grassy leaves emerging from a basal rosette. Native to South America, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental plant is prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
