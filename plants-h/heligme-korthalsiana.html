Heligme korthalsiana

Heligme korthalsiana, commonly known as the Korthals' helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
