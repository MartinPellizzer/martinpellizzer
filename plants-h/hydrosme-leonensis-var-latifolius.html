Hydrosme leonensis var. latifolius

Hydrosme leonensis var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf hydrosme, is a perennial aquatic plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in shallow, freshwater environments with moderate sunlight.

Its presence is often indicative of healthy wetland ecosystems.
