Hymenolobus divaricatus

Hymenolobus divaricatus, commonly known as the African orchid or the grass orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands across sub-Saharan Africa. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

Hymenolobus divaricatus is notable for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor environments.
