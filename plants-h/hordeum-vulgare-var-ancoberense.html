Hordeum vulgare var. ancoberense

Hordeum vulgare var. ancoberense, commonly known as Ancober barley, is a variant of barley characterized by its unique botanical features.

It typically exhibits shorter culms and a more compact growth habit compared to other barley varieties. The plant produces spikelets that are often more densely arranged, contributing to its distinct appearance. Its leaves are usually narrower and more rigid, with a slightly bluish-green hue.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to specific environmental conditions, making it of interest in agricultural and ecological studies.
