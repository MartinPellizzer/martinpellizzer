Hibiscus Flaviflorus

Hibiscus flaviflorus is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. Known for its vibrant yellow blooms and lush green foliage, it adds a splash of color to any garden or landscape. This species belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cotton and okra. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to thrive in non-native environments.
