Hieracium syncolanthum

Hieracium syncolanthum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a composite head made up of numerous small florets. The plant has basal leaves that are broadly oval and often covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

Hieracium syncolanthum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
