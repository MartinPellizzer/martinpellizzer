Hyptis alopecuroides

Hyptis alopecuroides, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue or purple and are clustered in whorled inflorescences.

Hyptis alopecuroides is often used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and fragrance.
