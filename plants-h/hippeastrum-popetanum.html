Hippeastrum popetanum

Hippeastrum popetanum, commonly known as the Popetan amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in late winter or early spring. The plant features strap-like, linear leaves that are broad at the base and taper toward the tips. Its bulbous stem is fleshy and stores water, helping it survive in dry conditions.

Hippeastrum popetanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant and long-lasting blooms.
