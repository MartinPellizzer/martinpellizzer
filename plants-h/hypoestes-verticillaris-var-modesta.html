Hypoestes verticillaris var. modesta

Hypoestes verticillaris var. modesta, commonly known as the "Cape Myrtle," is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow centers. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
