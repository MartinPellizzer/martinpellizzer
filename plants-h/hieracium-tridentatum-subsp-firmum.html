Hieracium tridentatum subsp. firmum

Hieracium tridentatum subsp.

firmum, commonly known as the stiff thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features spiny, lobed leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Its botanical characteristics include a robust stem and a distinctive, spiky appearance.

It is often found in dry, sunny environments and is part of the Asteraceae family.
