Habenaria friesii

Habenaria friesii, commonly known as the Fries' orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
