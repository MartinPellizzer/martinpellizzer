Hemicarpha micrantha var. minor

Hemicarpha micrantha var. minor, commonly known as the small-flowered hemicarpha, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas, often in regions with a Mediterranean climate. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences with spikelets. Its flowering period usually occurs during the warmer months, and it is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
