Heliotropium krauseanum subsp. krauseanum

Heliotropium krauseanum subsp.

krauseanum, commonly known as the tropical heliotrope, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly succulent texture.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or violet, blooming in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and gardens.

Its rapid growth and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
