Hieracium leucopodum

Hieracium leucopodum, commonly known as the white hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its white to pale yellow flowers are surrounded by bracts that give it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in disturbed habitats and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
