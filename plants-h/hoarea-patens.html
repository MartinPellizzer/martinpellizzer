Hoarea patens

Hoarea patens, commonly known as the desert bell, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has a compact growth habit with silver-gray foliage that helps it conserve water. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well adapted to harsh desert environments.

Hoarea patens is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
