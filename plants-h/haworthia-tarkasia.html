Haworthia tarkasia

Haworthia tarkasia, commonly known as the Tarkasia Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

Due to its low maintenance and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
