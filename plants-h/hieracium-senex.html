Hieracium senex

Hieracium senex, commonly known as the old man's beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its long, drooping yellow flowers that resemble a beard, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It has rough, hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges.

Hieracium senex is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
