Hibiscus ferox var. metensis

Hibiscus ferox var. metensis, commonly known as the Metensis hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features large, showy flowers with five petals that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has rough, hairy leaves and produces capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
