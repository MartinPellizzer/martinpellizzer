Hyptis minutifolia

Hyptis minutifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of pale blue or purple flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Hyptis minutifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
