Hypoestes mollior

Hypoestes mollior, commonly known as the "Blue Mistflower," is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and striking clusters of small, tubular blue to purple flowers. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped blooms that are often tinged with white, creating a mist-like appearance. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Hypoestes mollior thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
