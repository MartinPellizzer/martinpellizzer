Hieracium inuliflorum subsp. inulifloroides

Hieracium inuliflorum subsp.

inulifloroides, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can spread vegetatively through its rhizomes.
