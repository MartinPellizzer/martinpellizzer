Hardenbergia monophylla f. longiracemosa

Hardenbergia monophylla f. longiracemosa, commonly known as the long raceme larkspur, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is notable for its long, racemose inflorescences, which give it its specific epithet.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
