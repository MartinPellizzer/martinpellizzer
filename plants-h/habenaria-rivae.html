Habenaria rivae

Habenaria rivae, commonly known as the river orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks. The plant has a slender, erect stem with several leaves arranged spirally. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale green, with three sepals and three petals.

Habenaria rivae is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
