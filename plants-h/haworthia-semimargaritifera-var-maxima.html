Haworthia semimargaritifera var. maxima

Haworthia semimargaritifera var. maxima, commonly known as the Giant Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant forms a dense rosette and can grow up to 30 cm in height, making it one of the larger species in the Haworthia genus. Its leaves are covered in a layer of fine, white hairs that help reduce water loss.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.
