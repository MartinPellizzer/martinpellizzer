Hieracium cinerascens

Hieracium cinerascens, commonly known as the ash-gray hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
