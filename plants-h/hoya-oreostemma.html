Hoya oreostemma

Hoya oreostemma, commonly known as the snowflake hoya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Hoya oreostemma thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid conditions.
