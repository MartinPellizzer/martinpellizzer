Holodiscus discolor var. ariifolius

Holodiscus discolor var. ariifolius, commonly known as the golden-leafed honeysuckle, is a variant of the honeysuckle plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking yellow foliage, which contrasts with its green stems and fragrant, tubular flowers. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to horticultural collections.
