Helichrysum ambiguum

Helichrysum ambiguum, commonly known as the yellow oxeye, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its small, papery bracts give it a distinctive appearance, and it is often used in traditional medicine.

Helichrysum ambiguum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
