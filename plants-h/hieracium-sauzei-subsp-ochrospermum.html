Hieracium sauzei subsp. ochrospermum

Hieracium sauzei subsp.

ochrospermum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and slender, branching stems that bear yellow to orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are composed of ray florets and disc florets, typical of the Asteraceae family.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
