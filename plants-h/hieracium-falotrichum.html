Hieracium falotrichum

Hieracium falotrichum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the dandelion family (Compositae) and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Hieracium falotrichum is often found in temperate climates and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
