Hypogynium arrectum

Hypogynium arrectum, commonly known as the upright hypogynium, is a rare flowering plant characterized by its unique floral structure.

It possesses a distinctive hypogynous flower arrangement, where the floral parts are attached below the ovary. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodland floors or stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, textured surface.

The flowers are usually small and white or pale green, with prominent stamens and a single pistil.
