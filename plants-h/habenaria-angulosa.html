Habenaria angulosa

Habenaria angulosa, commonly known as the angular orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often fragrant. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Habenaria angulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
