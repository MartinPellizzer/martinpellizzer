Haworthia batesiana var. reddii

Haworthia batesiana var. reddii, commonly known as the Redii Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves with prominent white markings and a red or pinkish hue along the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
