Huernia penzigii

Huernia penzigii, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, rounded stem with distinctive ridges and a waxy, pale green to blue-green color. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are small, fleshy, and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Huernia penzigii is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.
