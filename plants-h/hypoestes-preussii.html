Hypoestes preussii

Hypoestes preussii, commonly known as the "Magic Beanplant," is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble small flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or green flowers that are followed by bean-like seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes preussii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
