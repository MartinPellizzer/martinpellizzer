Hymenorebutia pusilla f. flaviflora

Hymenorebutia pusilla f. flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered miniature cactus, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are predominantly yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Its foliage is covered in fine, white hairs, providing some protection against intense sunlight and moisture loss.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
