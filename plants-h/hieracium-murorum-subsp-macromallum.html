Hieracium murorum subsp. macromallum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

macromallum, commonly known as the large-flowered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its robust growth and showy yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are basal and lanceolate, with a rough texture, while its stems are upright and hairy.

The subspecies is distinguished by its larger flower heads and more robust stature compared to other hawkweed species.
