Hieracium trichocymosum

Hieracium trichocymosum, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are rough to the touch, with a hairy texture, and its flowers are yellow with dark central markings.

Hieracium trichocymosum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many temperate regions.
