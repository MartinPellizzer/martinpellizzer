Hymenocallis Disticha

Hymenocallis disticha is a striking member of the amaryllis family known for its elegant, bell-shaped flowers. Native to tropical regions of the Americas, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that add a lush, green presence to any garden. The flowers, which bloom in clusters, come in shades of white and yellow, creating a dramatic visual display. This plant is popular among gardeners for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
