Hypericum liottardii

Hypericum liottardii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has opposite, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Hypericaceae family.
