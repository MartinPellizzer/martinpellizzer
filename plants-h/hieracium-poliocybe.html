Hieracium poliocybe

Hieracium poliocybe, commonly known as the pale-spored hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dandelion-like yellow flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

The pale color of its spores is a distinguishing feature in its reproductive structure.
