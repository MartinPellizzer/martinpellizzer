Hypericum musadoganii

Hypericum musadoganii, commonly known as the Musadoganii St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum musadoganii is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
