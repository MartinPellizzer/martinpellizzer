Hieracium guatemalense

Hieracium guatemalense, commonly known as Guatemalan hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are generally lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
