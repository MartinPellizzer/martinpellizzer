Heliconia schiedeana f. glabrifolia

Heliconia schiedeana f. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved heliconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Heliconiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble flowers. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are smooth and glossy, giving it its common name. It produces inflorescences with multiple small, clustered flowers that are often pollinated by hummingbirds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
