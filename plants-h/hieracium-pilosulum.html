Hieracium pilosulum

Hieracium pilosulum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant features yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically hairy and alternate along the stem, contributing to its common name.

Hieracium pilosulum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.
