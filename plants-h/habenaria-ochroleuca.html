Habenaria ochroleuca

Habenaria ochroleuca, commonly known as the yellow-streaked orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, while its flowers are pale yellow with distinctive green markings.

It is a perennial herb that produces a single spike of flowers, often with a pleasant, spicy scent.
