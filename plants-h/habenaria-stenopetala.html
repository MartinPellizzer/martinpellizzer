Habenaria stenopetala

Habenaria stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are often fragrant. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
