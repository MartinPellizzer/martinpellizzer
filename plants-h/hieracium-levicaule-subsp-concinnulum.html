Hieracium levicaule subsp. concinnulum

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

concinnulum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in grassy meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed soils, preferring well-drained conditions.

The plant features slender, erect stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a single head, and are surrounded by pale green bracts.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ability to produce a large number of seeds.
