Hieracium erythrocarpum subsp. stribrnyanum

Hieracium erythrocarpum subsp.

stribrnyanum, commonly known as the Stribrnyanum hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its erect growth habit and yellow to orange flowers arranged in composite heads.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs. This subspecies is native to Central Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological preferences.
