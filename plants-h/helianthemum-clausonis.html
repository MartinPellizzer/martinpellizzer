Helianthemum clausonis

Helianthemum clausonis, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Helianthemum clausonis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
