Helichrysum ruandense

Helichrysum ruandense, commonly known as the Ruanda everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Eastern African region, particularly Rwanda and surrounding countries. The plant is characterized by its dense, yellow-orange flowers that resemble small suns, which give it its common name. Its foliage is typically covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Helichrysum ruandense is often found in rocky or grassy areas and is adapted to the cool, moist climate of its native habitat.
