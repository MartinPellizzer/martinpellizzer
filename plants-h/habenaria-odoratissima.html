Habenaria odoratissima

Habenaria odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet flag orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant white or pale pink flowers that emit a strong, sweet scent, especially at night. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and features long, narrow leaves that form a dense rosette. Its flowers are typically arranged in a spike and can bloom multiple times throughout the year.

The species is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its unique aromatic properties.
