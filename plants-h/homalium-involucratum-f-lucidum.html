Homalium involucratum f. lucidum

Homalium involucratum f. lucidum, commonly known as the shining or glossy homalium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, flat seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
