Hieracium anchusifolium

Hieracium anchusifolium, commonly known as the anchusleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has hairy, upright stems and produces yellow flowers in composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
