Heterospathe phillipsii

Heterospathe phillipsii, commonly known as the Phillips' arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy surface. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in marshes and ponds. Its roots are fibrous and help stabilize the substrate, while its stems can grow up to several feet in length.

Heterospathe phillipsii is valued in ornamental aquatic gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for small aquatic life.
