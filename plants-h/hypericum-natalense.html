Hypericum natalense

Hypericum natalense, commonly known as Natal St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and dense, woody stems. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in sunny conditions. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum natalense is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
