Hieracium cineraria

Hieracium cineraria, commonly known as cineraria hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows and along roadsides. It has a rosette growth habit with basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and has been cultivated for its attractive appearance.
