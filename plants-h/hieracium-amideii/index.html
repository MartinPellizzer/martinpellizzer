Hieracium Amideii

Hieracium amideii is a lesser-known species of hawkweed found in specific regions of Europe. It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and hairy leaves. This plant thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Due to habitat loss, it faces threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique flora.
