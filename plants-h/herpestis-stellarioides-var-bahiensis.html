Herpestis stellarioides var. bahiensis

Herpestis stellarioides var. bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia starwort, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while the stems are erect and branching.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its delicate appearance.
