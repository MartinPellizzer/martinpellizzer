Haworthia tortuosa var. major

Haworthia tortuosa var. major, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, translucent tubercles that resemble the stripes of a zebra, giving it its common name. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. It typically grows in a rosette form, with leaves that are pale green to blue-gray in color.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
