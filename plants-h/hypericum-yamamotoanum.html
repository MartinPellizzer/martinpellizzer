Hypericum yamamotoanum

Hypericum yamamotoanum, commonly known as the Yamamoto's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, typically growing in shrub form. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for both ecological and horticultural studies.
