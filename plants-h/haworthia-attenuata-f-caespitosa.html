Haworthia attenuata f. caespitosa

Haworthia attenuata f. caespitosa, commonly known as the compact Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, rosette-shaped clusters of fleshy, dark green leaves with white tubercles and translucent windows. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
