Hieracium megatrichum

Hieracium megatrichum, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers. Hieracium megatrichum is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in certain regions.
