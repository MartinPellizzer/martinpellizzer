Helianthemum lucidum

Helianthemum lucidum, commonly known as the glossy rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow, typically blooming in spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are covered in a waxy coating, helping it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
