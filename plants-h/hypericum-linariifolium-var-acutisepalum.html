Hypericum linariifolium var. acutisepalum

Hypericum linariifolium var. acutisepalum, commonly known as the sharp-sepal St. John's wort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Hypericaceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers with five acute sepals and numerous stamens. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, and it is often found in temperate regions.
