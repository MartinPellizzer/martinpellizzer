Hieracium sillrense

Hieracium sillrense, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually unbranched and somewhat woody at the base.

Hieracium sillrense is often found in temperate regions of Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.
