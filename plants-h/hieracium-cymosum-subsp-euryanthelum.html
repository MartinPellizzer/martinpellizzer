Hieracium cymosum subsp. euryanthelum

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

euryanthelum, commonly known as the broad-flowered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single, flat-topped head of numerous small florets.

It is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed habitats and is often found in open, sunny environments.
