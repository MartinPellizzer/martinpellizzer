Hedyotis elongata

Hedyotis elongata, commonly known as the slender dotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves arranged alternately along its stem. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, clustered in umbels, and bloom during the warmer months.

Hedyotis elongata is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
