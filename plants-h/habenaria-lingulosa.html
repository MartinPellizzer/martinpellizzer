Habenaria lingulosa

Habenaria lingulosa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single spike of flowers.

Habenaria lingulosa is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
