Hieracium murorum subsp. liljeholmii

Hieracium murorum subsp.

liljeholmii, commonly known as the Swedish sandweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with hairy stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer.

The subspecies is native to parts of Scandinavia, particularly Sweden and Norway, where it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid habitat.
