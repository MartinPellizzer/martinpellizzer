Hymenodium crinitum

Hymenodium crinitum, commonly known as the featherleaf hakea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, feathery leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. It is typically found in heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments.

Hymenodium crinitum is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
