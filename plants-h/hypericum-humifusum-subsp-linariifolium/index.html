Hypericum Humifusum Subsp. Linariifolium

Hypericum humifusum subsp. linariifolium is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is known for its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This subspecies is closely related to other hypericums but is distinguished by its narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its medicinal properties and ornamental value have made it a subject of both botanical and horticultural interest.
