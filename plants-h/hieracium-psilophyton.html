Hieracium psilophyton

Hieracium psilophyton, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and clusters of small, yellowish to brownish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is found in various parts of Europe and Asia. It has a taproot system and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.

The leaves are often hairy and have a rough texture, which helps in reducing water loss in its habitat.
