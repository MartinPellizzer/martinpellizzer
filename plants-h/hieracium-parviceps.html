Hieracium parviceps

Hieracium parviceps, commonly known as the small-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 20 to 60 centimeters, with basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, forming a flat-topped inflorescence.

The plant has a fibrous root system and is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils.
