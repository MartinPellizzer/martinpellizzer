Hieracium lychnioides

Hieracium lychnioides, commonly known as the flameless candleplant, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its bright yellow, dandelion-like flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling small candles. Native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, this plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The leaves are usually deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are upright and somewhat hairy.

It is a perennial plant that thrives in sunny conditions and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
