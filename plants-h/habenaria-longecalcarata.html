Habenaria longecalcarata

Habenaria longecalcarata, commonly known as the long-spurred orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive long spur, which is a feature typical of many orchid species. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and stream sides.

Botanically, it belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
