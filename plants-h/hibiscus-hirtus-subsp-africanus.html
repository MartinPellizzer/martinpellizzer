Hibiscus hirtus subsp. africanus

Hibiscus hirtus subsp.

africanus, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Africa. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red.

The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, which gives it its scientific name. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils. Hibiscus hirtus subsp.

africanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
