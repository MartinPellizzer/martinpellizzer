Hebenstretia erinoides

Hebenstretia erinoides, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems that bear opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of blue, violet, and white, often with a contrasting yellow throat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hebenstretia erinoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
