Homalium Involucratum F. Lucidum

Homalium involucratum f. lucidum is a rare and striking plant known for its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique growth habit. Its compact size makes it suitable for indoor gardening and small spaces.
