Hieracium micantiforme

Hieracium micantiforme, commonly known as the silverleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are silver- or gray-green in color, covered in fine hairs that give it a distinctive appearance.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
