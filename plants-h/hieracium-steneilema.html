Hieracium steneilema

Hieracium steneilema, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring well-drained conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, while the flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Hieracium steneilema is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
