Habenaria clovisii

Habenaria clovisii, commonly known as the Clovis orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded, woodland environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
