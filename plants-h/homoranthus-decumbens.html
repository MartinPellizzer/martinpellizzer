Homoranthus decumbens

Homoranthus decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate honeysuckle, is a low-growing shrub native to Australia.

It features slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.
