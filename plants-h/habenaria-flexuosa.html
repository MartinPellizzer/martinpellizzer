Habenaria flexuosa

Habenaria flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often zigzagging stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, typically in late spring or early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and growth habit make it a distinctive species within the Orchidaceae family.
