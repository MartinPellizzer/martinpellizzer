Hieracium tanymecolepis

Hieracium tanymecolepis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are narrow and often have a rough texture, while the stems are hairy. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas.

This plant is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some regions.
