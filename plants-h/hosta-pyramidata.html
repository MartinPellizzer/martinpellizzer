Hosta pyramidata

Hosta pyramidata, commonly known as the pyramid hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves that form a dense, upright rosette. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale lavender flowers on tall, slender stems in late summer. Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for shade gardens.

Hosta pyramidata prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.
