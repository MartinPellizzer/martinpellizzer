Haworthia minima var. poellnitziana

Haworthia minima var. poellnitziana, commonly known as the "Little Zebra" or "Mini Zebra," is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, rosette-shaped leaves with distinctive white to light green markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant is known for its compact size, typically reaching only a few inches in height, making it ideal for small spaces or containers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
