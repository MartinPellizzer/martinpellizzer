Haworthia greenii f. minor

Haworthia greenii f. minor, commonly known as the miniature Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
