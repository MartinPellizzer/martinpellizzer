Hypertelis suffruticosa

Hypertelis suffruticosa, commonly known as the African tea or bush tea, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Hypertelis suffruticosa is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
