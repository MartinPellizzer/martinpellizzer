Hosta sieboldii var. intermedia

Hosta sieboldii var. intermedia, commonly known as the Siebold's Hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and yellow margins. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers in late summer. It prefers partial to full shade and well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various garden conditions.
