Hieracium fragile subsp. verlotii

Hieracium fragile subsp.

verlotii, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with basal leaves that are linear to oblanceolate in shape.

The plant produces yellow flowers in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its delicate appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and threatened species in many regions.
