Hibiscus diversifolius var. witteanus

Hibiscus diversifolius var. witteanus, commonly known as the Wittean hibiscus, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hibiscus diversifolius var. witteanus is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and unique foliage, making it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
