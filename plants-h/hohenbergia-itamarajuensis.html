Hohenbergia itamarajuensis

Hohenbergia itamarajuensis, commonly known as the Itamaraju bromeliad, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant features vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that can range in color from red to orange, adding visual interest. Its foliage is often covered in fine, white or silver hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
