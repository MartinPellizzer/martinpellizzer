Haworthia decipiens var. incrassa

Haworthia decipiens var. incrassa, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor or rock garden settings.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
