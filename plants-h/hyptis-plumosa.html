Hyptis plumosa

Hyptis plumosa, commonly known as silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Hyptis plumosa is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
