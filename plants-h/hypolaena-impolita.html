Hypolaena impolita

Hypolaena impolita, commonly known as the rough hypolaena, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers surrounded by a spadix, typical of the arum family. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Hypolaena impolita is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and marshy areas.
