Hieracium basilimbatum

Hieracium basilimbatum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, with a distinctive ray floret structure. Its leaves are basal and hairy, often with a rough texture.

Hieracium basilimbatum is known for its aggressive growth and can become invasive in certain regions.
