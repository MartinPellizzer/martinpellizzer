Hildewintera aureispina

Hildewintera aureispina, commonly known as the golden spined hildewintera, is a unique and rare plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow spines, which are a distinguishing feature of the species. The plant has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with dense foliage that thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.
