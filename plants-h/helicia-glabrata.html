Helicia glabrata

Helicia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth helicia, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves that are usually glabrous or only slightly hairy. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense heads, often appearing in late winter to early spring.

Helicia glabrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
