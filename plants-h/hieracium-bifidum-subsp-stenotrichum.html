Hieracium bifidum subsp. stenotrichum

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

stenotrichum, commonly known as the narrow-haired hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy rosettes.

The plant features finely divided, hairy leaves that are often grayish-green in color. Its flowers are yellow and typically appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
