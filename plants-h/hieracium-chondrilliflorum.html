Hieracium chondrilliflorum

Hieracium chondrilliflorum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has a basal rosette of leaves and upright flowering stems with numerous small, daisy-like flower heads.

The species is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.
