Hypolytrum laxum

Hypolytrum laxum, commonly known as the slender hypolytrum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its delicate structure and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and less-studied species within the orchid family.
