Haworthia mucronata var. mucronata

Haworthia mucronata var. mucronata, commonly known as the sharp-tipped Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or pale green markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
