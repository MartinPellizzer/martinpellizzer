Hieracium ivdelense

Hieracium ivdelense, commonly known as the Ivdel hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers and hairy, upright stems. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring moist and well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium ivdelense is native to certain regions of Europe and is noted for its ornamental value in horticulture.
