Hieracium felinum

Hieracium felinum, commonly known as the cat's paw.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its daisy-like flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. Its leaves are often described as resembling a cat's paw, giving it its common name.

Hieracium felinum is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in woodland edges.
