Hyptis eriocalyx

Hyptis eriocalyx, commonly known as silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and aromatic properties. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Hyptis eriocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
