Hippeastrum davidii

Hippeastrum davidii, commonly known as the David's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, white, and pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a bulbous stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum davidii is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
