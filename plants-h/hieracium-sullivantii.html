Hieracium sullivantii

Hieracium sullivantii, commonly known as Sullivant's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the central United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as prairies and roadsides. It produces small yellow to orange flowers and has hairy, upright stems.

Hieracium sullivantii is often found in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to full sun conditions.
