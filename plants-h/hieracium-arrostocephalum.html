Hieracium arrostocephalum

Hieracium arrostocephalum, commonly known as the fiery-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its bright yellow to orange flower heads are composed of numerous small florets arranged in a radiate pattern.

Hieracium arrostocephalum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
