Hieracium herjedalicum

Hieracium herjedalicum, commonly known as the Herjedalicum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

The leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
