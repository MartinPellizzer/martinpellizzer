Huperzia filiformis

Huperzia filiformis, commonly known as the slender huperzia, is a species of moss-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, often linear leaves that are typically less than 1 cm in length. This plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Huperzia filiformis is noted for its ability to form dense mats, contributing to the structure and biodiversity of its habitat.
