Himalrandia tetrasperma

Himalrandia tetrasperma, commonly known as the Himalayan mountain sorrel, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

Himalrandia tetrasperma is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
