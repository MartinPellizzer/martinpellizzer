Hypericum mutilum var. densiflorum

Hypericum mutilum var. densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in wetland or disturbed habitats. It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is known for its medicinal properties, though it contains compounds that can be toxic in high doses.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
