Hieracium michelii

Hieracium michelii, commonly known as Michel's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are generally hairy and lance-shaped, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
