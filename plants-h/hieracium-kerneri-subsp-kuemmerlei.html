Hieracium kerneri subsp. kuemmerlei

Hieracium kerneri subsp.

kuemmerlei, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy slopes at high altitudes, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rough texture and serrated edges, while its stems are often hairy.

This subspecies is notable for its adaptability to harsh mountainous environments and its role in local ecosystems.
