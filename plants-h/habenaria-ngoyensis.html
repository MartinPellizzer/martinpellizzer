Habenaria ngoyensis

Habenaria ngoyensis, commonly known as the Ngoye's butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink with intricate patterns. The plant grows in shaded, moist forest understories, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the substrate.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
