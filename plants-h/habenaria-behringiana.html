Habenaria behringiana

Habenaria behringiana, commonly known as the Bering's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soil conditions.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and resilience.
