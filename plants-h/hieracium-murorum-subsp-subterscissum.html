Hieracium murorum subsp. subterscissum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

subterscissum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer.

This subspecies is distinguished by its pale coloration and compact growth habit compared to other hawkweed species.
