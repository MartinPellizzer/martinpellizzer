Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. mollardianum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

mollardianum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm.

The plant features basal and alternate leaves that are broadly lanceolate with rough, hairy surfaces. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets and a central disk, and it blooms from June to August.

It is characterized by its robust growth and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
