Hieracium Zizianum Subsp. Sabinifolium

Hieracium zizianum subsp. sabinifolium is a lesser-known member of the dandelion family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments across parts of Europe and Asia. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in local ecosystems.
