Hieracium rigidum var. calcaricola

Hieracium rigidum var. calcaricola, commonly known as the rigid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in dry, calcareous soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. It has upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
