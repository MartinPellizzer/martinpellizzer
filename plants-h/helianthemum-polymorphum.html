Helianthemum polymorphum

Helianthemum polymorphum, commonly known as the variable rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, fleshy leaves and produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
