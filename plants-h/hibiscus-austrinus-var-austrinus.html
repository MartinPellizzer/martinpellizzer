Hibiscus austrinus var. austrinus

Hibiscus austrinus var. austrinus, commonly known as the Australian hibiscus, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
