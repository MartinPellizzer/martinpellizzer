Heteranthus macer

Heteranthus macer, commonly known as the yellow starburst, is a flowering plant native to the Cape region of South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a star-shaped appearance, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Heteranthus macer is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and unique floral structure.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
