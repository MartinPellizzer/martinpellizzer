Helichrysum evansii

Helichrysum evansii, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow to orange flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils. It produces small, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Helichrysum evansii is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
