Heterotheca deltoidea

Heterotheca deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as prairies, roadsides, and disturbed soils. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer.

Heterotheca deltoidea is often found in dry, sunny environments and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.
