Hieracium detruncatum

Hieracium detruncatum, commonly known as the short-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium detruncatum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its resilience to environmental stress.
