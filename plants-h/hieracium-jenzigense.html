Hieracium jenzigense

Hieracium jenzigense, commonly known as the Jenzig's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically features basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and forest edges.

Hieracium jenzigense is native to parts of Europe and is often found in temperate regions.
