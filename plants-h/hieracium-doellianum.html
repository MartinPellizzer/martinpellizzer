Hieracium doellianum

Hieracium doellianum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features basal and stem leaves that are typically lance-shaped with hairy surfaces. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, producing bright yellow flowers.

Hieracium doellianum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can spread aggressively through rhizomes.
