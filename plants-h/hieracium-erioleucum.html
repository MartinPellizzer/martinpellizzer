Hieracium erioleucum

Hieracium erioleucum, commonly known as the white-rayed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its flowers consist of white ray florets surrounding a yellow disk, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

Hieracium erioleucum is often found in open habitats and can sometimes be considered a weed in agricultural areas.
