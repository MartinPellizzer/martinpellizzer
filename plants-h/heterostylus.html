Heterostylus

Heterostylus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with ray florets and disc florets. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa. Its botanical features include a woody base and a rosette of leaves.

Heterostylus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
