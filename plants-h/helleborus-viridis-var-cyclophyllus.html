Helleborus viridis var. cyclophyllus

Helleborus viridis var. cyclophyllus, commonly known as the green hellebore, is a variant of the evergreen hellebore native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that range from green to yellowish-white. The plant is known for its winter blooming habit, often flowering from late winter to early spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
