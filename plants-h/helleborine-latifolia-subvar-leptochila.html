Helleborine latifolia subvar. leptochila

Helleborine latifolia subvar. leptochila, commonly known as the narrow-leaved helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly folded along the midrib. The plant produces striking, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip that is usually darker in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This orchid is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral structure.
