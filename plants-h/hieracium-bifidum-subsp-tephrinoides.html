Hieracium bifidum subsp. tephrinoides

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

tephrinoides, commonly known as the Tephrinoid dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has a taproot system and produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.
