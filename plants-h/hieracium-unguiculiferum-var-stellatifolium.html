Hieracium unguiculiferum var. stellatifolium

Hieracium unguiculiferum var. stellatifolium, commonly known as the star-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, and it is often found in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the inflorescence features bright yellow flowers arranged in a star-like pattern.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
