Habenaria jaegeri

Habenaria jaegeri, commonly known as the Jaeger's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few erect, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a distinctive greenish or brownish hue.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities.
