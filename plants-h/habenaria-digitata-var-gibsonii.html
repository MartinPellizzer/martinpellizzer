Habenaria digitata var. gibsonii

Habenaria digitata var. gibsonii, commonly known as the Gibson's lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It features a slender, hairy rhizome and produces a dense, dark green to brownish violet spike of small, fragrant flowers. The flowers are arranged in a loose spiral and have three narrow sepals and three petals, with the lateral petals slightly longer than the dorsal one. It typically blooms in late winter to early spring, and the plant prefers moist, shaded habitats.

This orchid is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate appearance.
