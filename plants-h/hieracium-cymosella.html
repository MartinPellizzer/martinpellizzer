Hieracium cymosella

Hieracium cymosella, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are generally basal, rough to the touch, and have a distinctive, hairy texture.

Hieracium cymosella is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including meadows and woodland edges.
