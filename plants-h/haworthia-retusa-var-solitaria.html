Haworthia retusa var. solitaria

Haworthia retusa var. solitaria, commonly known as the solitary Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale blue tubercles. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
