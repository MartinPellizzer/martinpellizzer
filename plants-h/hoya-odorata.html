Hoya odorata

Hoya odorata, commonly known as the sweet scented wax plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a trailing evergreen vine characterized by its waxy, elliptical leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Hoya odorata thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

It is popular among houseplants due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
