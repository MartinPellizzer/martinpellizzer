Hypoxis curculigoides

Hypoxis curculigoides, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a dense rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a low-growing mound. The plant produces yellow flowers in late summer, which are arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Hypoxis curculigoides is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in traditional medicine.
