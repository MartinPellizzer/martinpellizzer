Hermione luna

Hermione luna, commonly known as the moonflower, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, white, fragrant blooms that open at night. The flowers have a unique ability to change color slightly depending on the temperature and humidity. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical name is Cestrothion lunaria, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
