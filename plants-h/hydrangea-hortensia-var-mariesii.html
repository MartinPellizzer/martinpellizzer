Hydrangea hortensia var. mariesii

Hydrangea hortensia var. mariesii, commonly known as the Mariei hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flower clusters that range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant produces sterile flowers, which contribute to its ornamental value. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ability to change flower color and its compact, bushy form.
