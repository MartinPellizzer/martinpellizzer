Hypoestes tenuis

Hypoestes tenuis, commonly known as the "Blue Mist" plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, blue-violet bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, with slender, branching stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Hypoestes tenuis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
