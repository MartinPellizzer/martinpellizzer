Helichrysum parviflorum var. longifolium

Helichrysum parviflorum var. longifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features small, yellow to golden-orange flowers that bloom in dense, compact clusters. The plant has slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its longevity and ability to retain color when dried make it a popular choice for floral arrangements.
