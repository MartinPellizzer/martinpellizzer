Hypericum erectum proles vaniotii

Hypericum erectum proles vaniotii, commonly known as the Vaniotti's St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers with prominent stamens. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adapted to well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
