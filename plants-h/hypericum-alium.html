Hypericum alium

Hypericum alium, commonly known as the lesser St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, linear to lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. Its botanical characteristics include yellow-orange floral buds that open to five-petaled flowers.

Hypericum alium is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
