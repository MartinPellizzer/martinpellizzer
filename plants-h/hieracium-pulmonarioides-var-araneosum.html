Hieracium pulmonarioides var. araneosum

Hieracium pulmonarioides var. araneosum, commonly known as the spider's wheel, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a single head, which are surrounded by a dense ring of bracts. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its common name reflects the intricate, web-like appearance of its leaf arrangement.
