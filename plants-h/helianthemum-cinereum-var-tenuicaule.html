Helianthemum cinereum var. tenuicaule

Helianthemum cinereum var. tenuicaule, commonly known as the slender sandthrift, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
