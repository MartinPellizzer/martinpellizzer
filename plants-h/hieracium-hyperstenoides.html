Hieracium hyperstenoides

Hieracium hyperstenoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and basal rosettes of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flower heads composed of numerous small disc flowers surrounded by pale yellow ray florets. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
