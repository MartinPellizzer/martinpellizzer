Hoya kuhlii var. hasseltii

Hoya kuhlii var. hasseltii, commonly known as the "Hoya Kuhlii Variegated," is a compact, trailing evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves that often display a striking variegation of white and green. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hoya kuhlii var. hasseltii is popular among indoor gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
