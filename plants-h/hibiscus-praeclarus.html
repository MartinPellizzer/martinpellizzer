Hibiscus praeclarus

Hibiscus praeclarus, commonly known as the splendid hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Hibiscus praeclarus thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
