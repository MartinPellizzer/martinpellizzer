Homalomena carrii

Homalomena carrii, commonly known as the Carrii arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Homalomena carrii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
