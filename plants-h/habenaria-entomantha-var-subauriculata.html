Habenaria entomantha var. subauriculata

Habenaria entomantha var. subauriculata, commonly known as the auriculate helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive hood-like labellum. It grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth.

This variant is distinguished by its auriculate (ear-shaped) lobes on the petals and sepals.
