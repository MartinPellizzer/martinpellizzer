Hieracium glaucogenes

Hieracium glaucogenes, commonly known as the glaucous hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, often blue-green leaves and yellow ray flowers that resemble daisies. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in moist, meadow-like habitats. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds, aiding in its spread across various regions.

The plant's distinctive coloration and structure make it easily identifiable within its native range.
