Helicilla

Helicilla, commonly known as the spiral grass, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Poaceae.

These plants are characterized by their unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives them their common name. Helicilla species are typically found in temperate regions and thrive in moist, well-drained soils. They are often used in ornamental gardening due to their attractive, grass-like appearance.

The genus includes several species, each with variations in leaf color and growth habit.
