Hieracium heterogenes

Hieracium heterogenes, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats across Europe and parts of Asia. It is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
