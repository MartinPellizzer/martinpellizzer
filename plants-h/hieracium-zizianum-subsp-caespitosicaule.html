Hieracium zizianum subsp. caespitosicaule

Hieracium zizianum subsp.

caespitosicaule, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry meadows and grasslands.

The plant features dense, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its subspecies is distinguished by its compact, tufted growth habit and hairy stems.
