Haworthia retusa f. geraldii

Haworthia retusa f. geraldii, commonly known as the Geraldii Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or translucent markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
