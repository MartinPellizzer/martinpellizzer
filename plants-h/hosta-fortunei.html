Hosta fortunei

Hosta fortunei, commonly known as the Fortune's plantain, is a shade-tolerant perennial native to China.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a subtle metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers on slender stems in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.

Hosta fortunei is relatively low maintenance and suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.
