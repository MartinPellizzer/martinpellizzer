Hexacadica

Hexacadica, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical regions.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom on long, slender stalks rising from water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. Its roots anchor it in muddy substrates, while its stems provide support for the blossoms.

Hexacadica is revered in many cultures for its symbolic and medicinal significance.
