Hedysarum hyrcanum var. viridissimum

Hedysarum hyrcanum var. viridissimum, commonly known as the greenleaf sweet clover, is a perennial leguminous plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a robust root system and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.

Its foliage and flowers are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
