Heracleum pumilum

Heracleum pumilum, commonly known as small cow parsnip, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other members like carrots and parsley. The plant features a thick, hairy stem and large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. Its flowers are small and yellowish-green, arranged in umbrella-like clusters called umbels.
