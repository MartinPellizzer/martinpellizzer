Haworthia mirabilis

Haworthia mirabilis, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces tall, spiky flower stalks with small, star-shaped white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
