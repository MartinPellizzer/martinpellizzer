Hillia illustris

Hillia illustris, commonly known as the showy hillia, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that are often used in ornamental horticulture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish.

Hillia illustris is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
