Hieracium perpilosum

Hieracium perpilosum, commonly known as the silky hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers that are surrounded by a series of small, petal-like bracts. Its leaves are typically hairy and lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium perpilosum is often found in meadows and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
