Habenaria vanoverberghii

Habenaria vanoverberghii, commonly known as the Vanoverbergh's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are typical of the Orchidaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
