Helianthocereus orurensis var. albiflorus

Helianthocereus orurensis var. albiflorus, commonly known as the white-flowered night blooming cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with ribbed stems and produces large, showy white flowers that open at night. The plant thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil. Its flowers emit a sweet fragrance, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as bats and moths.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and unique nocturnal blooming habit.
