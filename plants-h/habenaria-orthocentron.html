Habenaria orthocentron

Habenaria orthocentron, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
