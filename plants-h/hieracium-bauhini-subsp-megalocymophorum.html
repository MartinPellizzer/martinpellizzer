Hieracium bauhini subsp. megalocymophorum

Hieracium bauhini subsp.

megalocymophorum, commonly known as the giant meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.
