Haworthia arachnoidea var. xiphiophylla

Haworthia arachnoidea var. xiphiophylla, commonly known as the spider haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth and fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white tubercles that resemble spider legs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
