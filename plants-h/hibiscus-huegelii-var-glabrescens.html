Hibiscus huegelii var. glabrescens

Hibiscus huegelii var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf hibiscus, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are generally smooth and glossy. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming from summer to fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
