Hypericum polyanthemum

Hypericum polyanthemum, commonly known as the American St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Hypericum polyanthemum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
