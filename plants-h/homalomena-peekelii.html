Homalomena peekelii

Homalomena peekelii, commonly known as the Peckel's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a glossy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
