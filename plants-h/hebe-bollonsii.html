Hebe bollonsii

Hebe bollonsii, commonly known as the Southern Alps hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the plantain family (Plantaginaceae) and is characterized by its compact growth habit and evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. Hebe bollonsii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its cultivation in gardens across temperate regions.
