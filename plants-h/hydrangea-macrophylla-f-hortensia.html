Hydrangea macrophylla f. hortensia

Hydrangea macrophylla f. hortensia, commonly known as the panicle hydrangea, is a cultivar renowned for its large, showy flower clusters.

It belongs to the Hydrangeaceae family and is native to Japan. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its inflorescences, which can range from white to pink or blue depending on soil pH, are composed of numerous small flowers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
