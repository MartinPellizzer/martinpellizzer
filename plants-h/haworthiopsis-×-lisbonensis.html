Haworthiopsis × lisbonensis

Haworthiopsis × lisbonensis, commonly known as the Lisbon Hybrid, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in arid landscapes.
