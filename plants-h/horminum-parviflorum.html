Horminum parviflorum

Horminum parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered horminum, is a herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Europe.

It features delicate, small white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
