Habenaria secunda

Habenaria secunda, commonly known as the second lady orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Habenaria secunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
