Hieracium × caralti

Hieracium × caralti, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a composite plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Hieracium pilosellum and Hieracium auripigmentum, resulting in a plant with intermediate characteristics. The species typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It produces yellow to orange flowering heads surrounded by pale green to white ray florets.

The plant is often found in temperate regions of Europe and has a creeping growth habit with hairy stems and leaves.
