Hieracium caesium subsp. mesotephrodes

Hieracium caesium subsp.

mesotephrodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers.

The plant typically has basal leaves that are oblanceolate to elliptic in shape, with hairy surfaces. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas.

Its flowering period occurs from late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
