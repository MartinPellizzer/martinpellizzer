Hakea lambertii

Hakea lambertii, commonly known as the Lambert's hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces woody, cone-like seed pods that persist on the plant for several years. Hakea lambertii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought tolerance through its thick, waxy leaves.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
