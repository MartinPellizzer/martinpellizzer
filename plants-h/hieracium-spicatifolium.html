Hieracium spicatifolium

Hieracium spicatifolium, commonly known as the spicate hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium spicatifolium is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
