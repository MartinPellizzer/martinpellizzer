Hesperantha latifolia

Hesperantha latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf hesperantha, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hesperantha latifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
