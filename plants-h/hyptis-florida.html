Hyptis florida

Hyptis florida, commonly known as the silverleaf hyptis, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hyptis florida is also valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
