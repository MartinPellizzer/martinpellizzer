Herminium josephi

Herminium josephi, commonly known as the Joseph's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe, particularly in countries like Italy, France, and Spain. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Herminium josephi is often found in moist, calcareous soils and thrives in shaded, woodland environments.
