Hibiscus rosa-sinensis colleri

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis colleri, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis colleri is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
