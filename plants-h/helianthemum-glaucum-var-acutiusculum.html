Helianthemum glaucum var. acutiusculum

Helianthemum glaucum var. acutiusculum, commonly known as the sharp-leaved sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with succulent, blue-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact form and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for coastal and arid landscapes.
