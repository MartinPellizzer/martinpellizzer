Hosta longipes var. longipes

Hosta longipes var. longipes, commonly known as the giant hosta, is a variety of the hosta plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture and can have a slight variegation in some cultivars. This plant thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated for its striking foliage.

It is a popular choice for gardeners looking to add a dramatic, textural element to their landscape.
