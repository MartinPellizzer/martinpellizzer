Hieracium × macromastix

Hieracium × macromastix, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers and robust, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It has a creeping growth habit with extensive root systems that allow it to spread rapidly.

Due to its invasive nature, it is often considered a noxious weed in many regions.
