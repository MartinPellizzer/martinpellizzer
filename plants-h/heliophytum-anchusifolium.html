Heliophytum anchusifolium

Heliophytum anchusifolium, commonly known as the rock rose, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
