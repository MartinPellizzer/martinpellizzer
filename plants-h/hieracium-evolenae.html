Hieracium evolenae

Hieracium evolenae, commonly known as the Alaskan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in cold climates. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

Hieracium evolenae is known for its hardy nature and ability to survive in harsh environmental conditions.
