Hieracium euwiesbaurianiforme

Hieracium euwiesbaurianiforme, commonly known as the European wood sorrel, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that resemble those of the common sorrel. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has small, hairy stems and is often found in temperate regions of Europe.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
