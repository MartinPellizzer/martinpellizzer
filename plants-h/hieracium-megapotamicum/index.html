Hieracium Megapotamicum

Hieracium megapotamicum is a rare flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America. It belongs to the sunflower family and is known for its striking yellow blooms. This species thrives in harsh environments, often found in rocky soils and mountainous regions. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and faces threats from habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful plant.
