Halimium umbellatum var. viscosum

Halimium umbellatum var. viscosum, commonly known as sticky rockrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its succulent, hairy leaves and showy, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.
