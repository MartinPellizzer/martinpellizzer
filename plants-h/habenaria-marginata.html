Habenaria marginata

Habenaria marginata, commonly known as the marginate orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with several oval, dark green leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellowish lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Its floral structure is characteristic of the Orchidaceae family, with specialized pollination mechanisms.
