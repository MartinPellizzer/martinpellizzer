Hieracium murlainzii

Hieracium murlainzii, commonly known as the Murlainz hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium murlainzii is often found in moist soils and can thrive in partial shade to full sun.
