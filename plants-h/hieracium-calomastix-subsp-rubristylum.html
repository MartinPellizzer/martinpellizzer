Hieracium calomastix subsp. rubristylum

Hieracium calomastix subsp.

rubristylum, commonly known as the red-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges.

Its subspecies status highlights its distinct morphological features, particularly its reddish stem coloration.
