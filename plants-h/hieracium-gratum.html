Hieracium gratum

Hieracium gratum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and woodland edges.

It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
