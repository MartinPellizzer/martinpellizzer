Hieracium lindebergii

Hieracium lindebergii, commonly known as Lindberg's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually basal, forming a rosette and are covered in fine hairs.
