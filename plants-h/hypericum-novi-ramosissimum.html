Hypericum novi-ramosissimum

Hypericum novi-ramosissimum, commonly known as the New Zealand St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and oval-shaped, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Hypericum novi-ramosissimum is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in coastal and forested areas.
