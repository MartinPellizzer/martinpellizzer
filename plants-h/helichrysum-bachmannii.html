Helichrysum bachmannii

Helichrysum bachmannii, commonly known as the yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even after drying. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically clustered in dense, rounded heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Helichrysum bachmannii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and use in floral arrangements.
