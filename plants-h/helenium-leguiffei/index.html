Helenium Leguiffei

Helenium leguiffei is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant orange and yellow flowers. Native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States, this plant thrives in rocky soils and full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native gardens. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its limited distribution and subtle foliage. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and ecological significance of Helenium leguiffei.
