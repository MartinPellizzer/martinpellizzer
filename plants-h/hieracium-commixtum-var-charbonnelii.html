Hieracium commixtum var. charbonnelii

Hieracium commixtum var. charbonnelii, commonly known as the Charbonnel's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates. It has finely divided leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
