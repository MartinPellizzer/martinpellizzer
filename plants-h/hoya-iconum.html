Hoya iconum

Hoya iconum, commonly known as the "Starburst Waffleplant," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy, and often variegated leaves that resemble a waffle pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in summer. Hoya iconum is a trailing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
