Hypericum clarense

Hypericum clarense, commonly known as the Clearwater St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, orange-red fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Hypericum clarense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
