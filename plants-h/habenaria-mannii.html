Habenaria mannii

Habenaria mannii, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often among leaf litter or in rocky crevices. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a single, fleshy pseudobulb. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Habenaria mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
