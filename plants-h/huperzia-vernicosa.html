Huperzia vernicosa

Huperzia vernicosa, commonly known as the glossy huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are typically dark green and covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered in strobila, a distinctive feature of the Huperziaceae family. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
