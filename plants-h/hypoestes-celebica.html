Hypoestes celebica

Hypoestes celebica, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering shrub native to Madagascar.

It features striking, colorful, velvety bracts that resemble small flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes celebica is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
