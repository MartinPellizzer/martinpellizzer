Hypericum erectum var. erectum

Hypericum erectum var. erectum, commonly known as upright St. John's wort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that are often covered in yellow-orange glandular dots. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a slightly rough texture.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
