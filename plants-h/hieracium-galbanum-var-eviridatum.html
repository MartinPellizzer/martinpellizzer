Hieracium galbanum var. eviridatum

Hieracium galbanum var. eviridatum, commonly known as the greenleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its leaves and flowers contain compounds that have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.
