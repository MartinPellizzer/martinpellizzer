Hypericum × cyathiflorum

Hypericum × cyathiflorum, commonly known as the golden st. john's wort, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, typically in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
