Hieracium Tenuissimum

Common hieracium, scientifically known as Hieracium tenuissimum, is a small, delicate flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is often found growing in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in open, sunny environments. With its slender stems and tiny, daisy-like flowers, it is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting pollinators and maintaining ecological balance. This unassuming plant is a reminder of the beauty and diversity found in the natural world.
