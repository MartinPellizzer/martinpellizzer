Helianthemum × crespoi

Helianthemum × crespoi, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, evergreen leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
