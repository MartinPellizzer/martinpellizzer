Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. ventosicum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

ventosicum, commonly known as the windgrass hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides, meadows, and grasslands.

The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. It has a taproot system and is often found in soils with moderate to high moisture content.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common weed in agricultural and urban environments.
