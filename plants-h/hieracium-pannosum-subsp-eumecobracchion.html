Hieracium pannosum subsp. eumecobracchion

Hieracium pannosum subsp.

eumecobracchion, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous flowering stems.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
