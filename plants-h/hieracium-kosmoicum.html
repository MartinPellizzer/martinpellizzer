Hieracium kosmoicum

Hieracium kosmoicum, commonly known as the Kosmos hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow-orange flowers with prominent central disks. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing at high elevations.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
