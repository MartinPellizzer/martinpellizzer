Hoplophyllum spinosum

Hoplophyllum spinosum, commonly known as the spiny hoplophyllum, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its spiny stems and opposite leaves. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish in color.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in dry, sandy soils.
