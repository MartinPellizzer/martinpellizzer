Habenaria steudneri

Habenaria steudneri, commonly known as the steudner's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its inflorescence bears a cluster of small, colorful flowers.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
