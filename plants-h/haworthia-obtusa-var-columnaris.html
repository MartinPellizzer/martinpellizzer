Haworthia obtusa var. columnaris

Haworthia obtusa var. columnaris, commonly known as the columnar haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves that are adorned with white to pale blue markings. The leaves are somewhat elongated and cylindrical, giving the plant its distinctive columnar appearance. It produces small, greenish-white flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
