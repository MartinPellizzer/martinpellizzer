Heliophytum phylicoides

Heliophytum phylicoides, commonly known as the golden sunplant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sunlight. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Heliophytum phylicoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
