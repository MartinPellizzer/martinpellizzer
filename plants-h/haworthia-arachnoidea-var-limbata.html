Haworthia arachnoidea var. limbata

Haworthia arachnoidea var. limbata, commonly known as the spider haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The leaves are covered in fine, hair-like structures that give the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
