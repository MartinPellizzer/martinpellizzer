Holostylis reniformis f. minor

Holostylis reniformis f. minor, commonly known as the small-leafed holostylis, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive reniform (kidney-shaped) leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments, often found in dense rainforests or along riverbanks. Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental appeal and the challenge of cultivating it successfully outside its native habitat.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
