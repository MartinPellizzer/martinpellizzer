Helianthemum mauritii

Helianthemum mauritii, commonly known as the Mauritius rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the island of Mauritius.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to its rocky or coastal habitats. The plant has fleshy, aromatic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention in its arid environment. It is well adapted to coastal conditions, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils.

Helianthemum mauritii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
