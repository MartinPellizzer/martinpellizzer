Hieracium praesomatum

Hieracium praesomatum, commonly known as the false hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and woodland edges. It has hairy stems and leaves, which help in reducing water loss.

Hieracium praesomatum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
