Heynea trifolia var. ternata

Heynea trifolia var. ternata, commonly known as the slender leaf heynea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically arranged in a whorled pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in cyathia, a feature typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions.

The species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
