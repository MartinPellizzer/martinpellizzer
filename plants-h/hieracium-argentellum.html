Hieracium argentellum

Hieracium argentellum, commonly known as silver hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features silver-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade.

Silver hawkweed is known for its showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
