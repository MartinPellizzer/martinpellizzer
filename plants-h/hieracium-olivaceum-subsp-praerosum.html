Hieracium olivaceum subsp. praerosum

Hieracium olivaceum subsp.

praerosum, commonly known as the olive hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value and invasive tendencies in some regions, it is sometimes considered a weed.
