Helichrysum pendulum var. compactum

Helichrysum pendulum var. compactum, commonly known as the compactum golden marguerite, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. The plant has narrow, slightly hairy leaves that are arranged in a compact, bushy form. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its compact size and ornamental value.

The foliage remains attractive year-round, making it a versatile choice for ornamental gardening.
