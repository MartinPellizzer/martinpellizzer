Haworthia emelyae

Haworthia emelyae, commonly known as the Emelyae Haworthia, is a species of succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a waxy coating. The leaves are usually green with white or pale markings, and the plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Haworthia emelyae is prized for its low maintenance and unique appearance, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
