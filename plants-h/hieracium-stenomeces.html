Hieracium stenomeces

Hieracium stenomeces, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, with a distinctive basal rosette structure.

The flowers are yellow, with ray florets that bloom in the summer months.
