Haworthia divergens

Haworthia divergens, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are adorned with distinctive white and green stripes. The plant is a member of the Asphodelaceae family and is known for its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Haworthia divergens is also appreciated for its compact size and ornamental appeal.
