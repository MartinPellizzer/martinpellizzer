Hydrangea robusta var. griffithii

Hydrangea robusta var. griffithii, commonly known as the Griffithii hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink or blue depending on soil pH. The plant has serrated, ovate leaves that are dark green and turn red in the fall. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to different garden conditions.
