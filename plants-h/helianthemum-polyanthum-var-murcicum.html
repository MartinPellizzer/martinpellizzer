Helianthemum polyanthum var. murcicum

Helianthemum polyanthum var. murcicum, commonly known as the Murcian rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are gray-green in color, adapted to arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in Mediterranean climates.
