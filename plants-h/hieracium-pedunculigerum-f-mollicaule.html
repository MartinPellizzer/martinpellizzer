Hieracium pedunculigerum f. mollicaule

Hieracium pedunculigerum f. mollicaule, commonly known as the soft-hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has a low-growing habit and produces numerous small, dandelion-like flowers.

The foliage is typically covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
