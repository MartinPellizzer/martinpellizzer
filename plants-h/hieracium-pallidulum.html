Hieracium pallidulum

Hieracium pallidulum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal and stem leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium pallidulum is a perennial species native to Europe and parts of Asia.
