Hakea florida

Hakea florida, commonly known as the coastal hakea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical flower spikes that bloom in spring, producing showy pink or red flowers. The plant has woody stems and narrow, leathery leaves that are adapted to arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Hakea florida is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
