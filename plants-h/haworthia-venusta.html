Haworthia venusta

Haworthia venusta, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with distinctive white or yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces tall, slender flower stems with tubular white or yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
