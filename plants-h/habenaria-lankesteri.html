Habenaria lankesteri

Habenaria lankesteri, commonly known as the Lankester's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
