Hedyotis nigricans

Hedyotis nigricans, commonly known as black willow or black geranium, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white to pink flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Hedyotis nigricans is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
