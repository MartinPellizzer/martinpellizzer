Habenaria paucifolia

Habenaria paucifolia, commonly known as the few-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in forest understories and thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a short rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base of the stem.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and limited number of flowers.
