Hieracium lamyi var. albipes

Hieracium lamyi var. albipes, commonly known as the whitefoot buttercup, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with white markings. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges.

The varietal name 'albipes' refers to its distinctive white-veined leaves and white-tipped petals.
