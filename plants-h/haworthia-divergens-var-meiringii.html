Haworthia divergens var. meiringii

Haworthia divergens var. meiringii, commonly known as the Meiring's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
