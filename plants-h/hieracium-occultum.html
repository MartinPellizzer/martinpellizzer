Hieracium occultum

Hieracium occultum, commonly known as the ghost dandelion, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its delicate, pale yellow flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.
