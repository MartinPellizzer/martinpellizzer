Habenaria guanchezii

Habenaria guanchezii, commonly known as the Canary Islands orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, terrestrial growth habit. The plant typically features small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas.
