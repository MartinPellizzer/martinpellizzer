Hydrangea liukiuensis

Hydrangea liukiuensis, commonly known as the Ryukyu hydrangea, is a species native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences composed of both sterile and fertile flowers, which can range in color from white to pink and blue depending on soil pH. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy.

The plant prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded woodland environments.
