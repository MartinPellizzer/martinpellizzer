Helopus mollis

Helopus mollis, commonly known as the silky hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Helopus mollis is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
