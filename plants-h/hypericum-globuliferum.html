Hypericum globuliferum

Hypericum globuliferum, commonly known as the globular St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
