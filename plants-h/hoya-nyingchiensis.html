Hoya nyingchiensis

Hoya nyingchiensis, commonly known as the Nyingchi Waffle Plant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its unique foliage and blooming habits make it a sought-after ornamental plant.

Hoya nyingchiensis is also noted for its slow growth rate and preference for well-draining soil.
