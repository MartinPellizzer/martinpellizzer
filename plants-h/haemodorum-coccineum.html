Haemodorum coccineum

Haemodorum coccineum, commonly known as the crimson lily or red lily, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Haemodoraceae, which includes species known for their striking, often red or orange flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, zigzagging stems with showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Haemodorum coccineum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
