Hexatheca longipedunculata

Hexatheca longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-stemmed hexatheca, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its slender, elongated peduncles that bear small, white or pale pink flowers. The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
