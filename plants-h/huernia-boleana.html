Huernia boleana

Huernia boleana, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent belonging to the Asphodelaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in shades of yellow, orange, or red, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
