Habenaria bismarckiensis

Habenaria bismarckiensis, commonly known as the Bismarck orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellowish throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in some areas.
