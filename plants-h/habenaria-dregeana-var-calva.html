Habenaria dregeana var. calva

Habenaria dregeana var. calva, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, upright stem and a few broad, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a dense spike, which are often fragrant. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in forested or rocky habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique floral display.
