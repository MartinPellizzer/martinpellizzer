Haworthia mucronata f. acuminata

Haworthia mucronata f. acuminata, commonly known as the sharp-tipped Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The leaves are pointed, giving the plant its common name, and are covered in a fine layer of white wax. It produces small, star-shaped white flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
