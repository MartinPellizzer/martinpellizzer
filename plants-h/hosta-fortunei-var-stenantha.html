Hosta fortunei var. stenantha

Hosta fortunei var. stenantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hosta, is a perennial plant native to China.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in late summer, usually in shades of blue or white. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to a range of garden conditions.
