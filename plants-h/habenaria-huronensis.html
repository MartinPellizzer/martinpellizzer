Habenaria huronensis

Habenaria huronensis, commonly known as Huron's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the northeastern United States and southeastern Canada.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial shade. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green to yellow in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

The species is notable for its delicate appearance and ecological significance in its native habitat.
