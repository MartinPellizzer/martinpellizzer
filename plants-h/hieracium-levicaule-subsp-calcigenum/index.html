Hieracium Levicaule Subsp. Calcigenum

Hieracium levicaule subsp. calcigenum is a rare and delicate plant native to specific alpine regions in Europe. It thrives in rocky, calcareous soils where few other species can survive. This subspecies is characterized by its small, pale yellow flowers and compact growth habit. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and human disturbance. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
