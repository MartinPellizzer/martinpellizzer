Heterotheca sessiliflora subsp. bolanderi

Heterotheca sessiliflora subsp.

bolanderi, commonly known as Bolander's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue to violet ray florets and yellow disk florets.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has sessile, linear leaves and produces small, compact flower heads.

This subspecies is often found in California and is adapted to Mediterranean climates with dry summers and wet winters.
