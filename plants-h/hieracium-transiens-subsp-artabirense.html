Hieracium transiens subsp. artabirense

Hieracium transiens subsp.

artabirense, commonly known as the Artabirian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus. It typically grows in rocky and alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in the summer months. Its flowers are typically arranged in a single inflorescence at the end of a flowering stem.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
