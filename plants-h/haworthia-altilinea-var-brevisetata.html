Haworthia altilinea var. brevisetata

Haworthia altilinea var. brevisetata, commonly known as the compact Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant grows in a compact rosette form, making it well-suited for small spaces. It requires bright, indirect light and minimal watering to thrive.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
