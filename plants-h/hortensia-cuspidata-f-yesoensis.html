Hortensia cuspidata f. yesoensis

Hortensia cuspidata f. yesoensis, commonly known as the Yesso hortensia, is a unique cultivar of the hydrangea plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flower clusters that typically range in color from blue to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant thrives in partial shade and requires moist, well-draining soil for optimal growth. Its foliage is dark green and remains attractive throughout the growing season.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
