Haemadictyon tomentellum

Haemadictyon tomentellum, commonly known as the hairy heartleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of southern Africa. The plant features heart-shaped leaves covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of pink or purple.

It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in marshy or swampy habitats.
