Helenium alatum

Helenium alatum, commonly known as the sunset torch, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the eastern and central United States and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, orange to red-orange ray flowers and a central disk of yellow florets, which bloom in late summer to early fall. Its feathery foliage adds a decorative touch, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Helenium alatum is relatively low maintenance and resistant to most pests and diseases.
