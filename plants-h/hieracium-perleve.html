Hieracium perleve

Hieracium perleve, commonly known as the golden hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy, upright stems. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, oblong seeds that are dispersed by wind.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and competitive nature.
