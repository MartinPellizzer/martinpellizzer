Hieracium doronicifolium grex adenocalathium

Hieracium doronicifolium grex adenocalathium, commonly known as the Adenocalathium hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant features yellow flowers with a distinctive tubular structure, surrounded by pale green to white ray florets. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges, and it often thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

This species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
