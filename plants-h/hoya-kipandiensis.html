Hoya kipandiensis

Hoya kipandiensis, commonly known as the Kipandi Wax Plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and clusters of star-shaped, waxy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves with a glossy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hoya kipandiensis is popular among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
