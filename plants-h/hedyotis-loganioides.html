Hedyotis loganioides

Hedyotis loganioides, commonly known as the small-leaved bedstraw, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, dark berries as fruit.

Hedyotis loganioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
