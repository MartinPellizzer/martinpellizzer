Helichrysum bailundense

Helichrysum bailundense, commonly known as the Natal goldenrods, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in summer, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice among gardeners.
