Haworthia gordoniana

Haworthia gordoniana, commonly known as the Gordon's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

Haworthia gordoniana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
