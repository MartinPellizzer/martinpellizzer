Hieracium dovrense var. groenlandicum

Hieracium dovrense var. groenlandicum, commonly known as Greenland hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It typically grows in open, rocky, or sandy habitats, often in tundra or alpine environments. The plant has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, and its flowers are yellow, arranged in a composite head. It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This species is adapted to cold climates and has a low-growing habit to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
