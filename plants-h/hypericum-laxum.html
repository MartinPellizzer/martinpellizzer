Hypericum laxum

Hypericum laxum, commonly known as the lax st. john's wort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum laxum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
