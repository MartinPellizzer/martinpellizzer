Hieracium incisum subsp. ovale

Hieracium incisum subsp.

ovale, commonly known as the oval milkweed or oval hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.

The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to elliptic in shape, often with toothed margins. Its flowers are yellow, with a distinctive central disk surrounded by ray florets.

It is characterized by a milky sap, which is a common feature among plants in the Asteraceae family.
