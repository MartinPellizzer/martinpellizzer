Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. paikoanum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

paikoanum, commonly known as the Paikoanum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. It has small, elongated bracts and is characterized by its distinct floral structure.

This subspecies is part of the Asteraceae family and is adapted to the unique ecological conditions of its native environment.
