Heuchera suksdorfii f. ribesoides

Heuchera suksdorfii f. ribesoides, commonly known as the ribesoides heuchera, is a unique cultivar of the heuchera genus native to North America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking foliage, often featuring deep green, red, or purple hues. The plant typically produces small, white to pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a variety of garden conditions.
