Hieracium commersonii var. genuinum

Hieracium commersonii var. genuinum, commonly known as the Commerson's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect stems and basal rosettes of leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
