Haworthia nortieri var. devriesii

Haworthia nortieri var. devriesii, commonly known as the "Devriesii Haworthia," is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with white or yellowish markings. The leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
