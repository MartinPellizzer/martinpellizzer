Hieracium androsaceum

Hieracium androsaceum, commonly known as the Androsace hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are usually basal, with a hairy texture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many parts of its range.
