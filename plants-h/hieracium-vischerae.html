Hieracium vischerae

Hieracium vischerae, commonly known as the Vischer's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads made up of numerous small florets. The plant typically features a rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and grasslands.
