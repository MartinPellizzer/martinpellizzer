Helichrysum argyrocaule

Helichrysum argyrocaule, commonly known as silver-stemmed everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray stems and small, yellowish-orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and long-lasting blooms make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
