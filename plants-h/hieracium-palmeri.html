Hieracium palmeri

Hieracium palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, such as grasslands and mountain slopes. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Its yellow flowers bloom in summer, and it is often found in regions with low precipitation.
