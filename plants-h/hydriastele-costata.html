Hydriastele costata

Hydriastele costata, commonly known as the ridge-stemmed orchid, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive ribbed or ridged stems, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Hydriastele costata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
