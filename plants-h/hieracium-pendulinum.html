Hieracium pendulinum

Hieracium pendulinum, commonly known as the hanging hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its drooping, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium pendulinum is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.
