Hieracium murorum subsp. chloroprasinum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

chloroprasinum, commonly known as the green-centred hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to greenish-yellow flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its greenish central disk and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils.

This plant is often found in temperate regions and is relatively low-growing, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
