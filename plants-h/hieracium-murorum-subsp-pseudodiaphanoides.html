Hieracium murorum subsp. pseudodiaphanoides

Hieracium murorum subsp.

pseudodiaphanoides, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its translucent, thin leaves and a more delicate appearance compared to other hawkweed species. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered plant in many regions.
