Hypoxis tabularis

Hypoxis tabularis, commonly known as the African star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Hypoxis tabularis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
