Habenaria stenopetala var. polytricha

Habenaria stenopetala var. polytricha, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, preferring rich, organic soil. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and the inflorescence consists of a raceme of fragrant, tubular flowers.

This orchid is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
