Hieracium longifidum

Hieracium longifidum, commonly known as the longleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium longifidum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
