Hypericum conjungens

Hypericum conjungens, commonly known as the yellow hypericum, is a shrub native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Hypericum conjungens is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
