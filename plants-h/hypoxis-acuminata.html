Hypoxis acuminata

Hypoxis acuminata, commonly known as the white star lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. It is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional medicine.

Hypoxis acuminata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and easy maintenance in garden settings.
