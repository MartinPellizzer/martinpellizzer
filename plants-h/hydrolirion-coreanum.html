Hydrolirion coreanum

Hydrolirion coreanum, commonly known as the Korean water lily, is a perennial aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, supported by long, slender petioles. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinct, fragrant scent that attracts pollinators. The plant thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in marshes and ponds.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen to surrounding water bodies.
