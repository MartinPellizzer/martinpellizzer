Helenium linearifolia

Helenium linearifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features tall, slender stems that bear bright orange to red flower heads, which are composed of ray florets surrounding a central disk. The leaves are linear and alternate, often with a slight hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
