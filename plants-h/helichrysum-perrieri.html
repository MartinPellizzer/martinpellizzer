Helichrysum perrieri

Helichrysum perrieri, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, bright yellow to orange flowers that are often mistaken for sunflowers due to their radiant appearance. The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Helichrysum perrieri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
