Herminium albomarginatum

Herminium albomarginatum, commonly known as the white-margin orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers with distinctive white margins, which bloom in late summer. It prefers moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows.

The species is notable for its delicate appearance and ecological significance in its native range.
