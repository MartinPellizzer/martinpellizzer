Haworthia virescens var. minor

Haworthia virescens var. minor, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its attractive appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
