Hieracium nigrescens subsp. stenomischum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

stenomischum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat, disk-like cluster.

It is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
