Habenaria thorelii

Habenaria thorelii, commonly known as the Thorell's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers with a distinct, spurred lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
