Hieracium lyperodes

Hieracium lyperodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often lance-shaped, with the flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Hieracium lyperodes is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
