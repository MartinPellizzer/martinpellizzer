Hieracium valdepilosum subsp. loschniggii

Hieracium valdepilosum subsp.

loschniggii, commonly known as the Loschnigg's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a single head, typical of the genus Hieracium. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
