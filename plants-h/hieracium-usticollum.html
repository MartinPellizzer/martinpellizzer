Hieracium usticollum

Hieracium usticollum, commonly known as the rough hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. Its leaves are rough and hairy, and the flowers are yellow with a central disk.
