Hibiscus hilarianus

Hibiscus hilarianus, commonly known as the silky hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a soft, silky texture, which gives it its common name. Hibiscus hilarianus thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
