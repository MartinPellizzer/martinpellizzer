Hypoestes floribunda

Hypoestes floribunda, commonly known as the Marvelous Marvelberry, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged blooming period, which can last from spring to autumn.
