Hieracium paznaunicum

Hieracium paznaunicum, commonly known as the Paznaun hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Alpine regions of Central Europe, particularly in Austria and Switzerland. The plant is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium paznaunicum is often found in meadows and alpine grasslands, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
