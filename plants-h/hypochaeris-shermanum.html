Hypochaeris shermanum

Hypochaeris shermanum, commonly known as Sherman's weed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to lanceolate in shape, often with serrated margins. Its flowers are yellow and daisy-like, consisting of ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular florets.

Hypochaeris shermanum is native to North America and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields.
