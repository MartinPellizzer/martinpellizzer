Hamelia patens var. quinifolia

Hamelia patens var. quinifolia, commonly known as the yellow flamebush, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
