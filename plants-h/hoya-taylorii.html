Hoya taylorii

Hoya taylorii, commonly known as the Taylor's wax plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asclepiadaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia, and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers. Hoya taylorii is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
