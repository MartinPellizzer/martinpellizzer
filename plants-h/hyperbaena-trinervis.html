Hyperbaena trinervis

Hyperbaena trinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined hyperbaena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with three distinct veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
