Henriettella ramiflora

Henriettella ramiflora, commonly known as the branched-flowered heath, is a perennial shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

Henriettella ramiflora is well adapted to coastal conditions, showing resilience to salt spray and strong winds.
