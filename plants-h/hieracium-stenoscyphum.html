Hieracium stenoscyphum

Hieracium stenoscyphum, commonly known as narrow-cup buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers with five petals and a central disk. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges.

Hieracium stenoscyphum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
