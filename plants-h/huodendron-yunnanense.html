Huodendron yunnanense

Huodendron yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan firethorn, is a shrub native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its thorny stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible but have a tart flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
