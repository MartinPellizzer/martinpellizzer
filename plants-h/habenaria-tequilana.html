Habenaria tequilana

Habenaria tequilana, commonly known as the Tequila orchid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in tropical or subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, while its inflorescence consists of clusters of small, colorful blooms.

Habenaria tequilana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
