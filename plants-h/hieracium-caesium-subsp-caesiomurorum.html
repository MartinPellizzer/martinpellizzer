Hieracium caesium subsp. caesiomurorum

Hieracium caesium subsp.

caesiomurorum, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its striking purple to reddish flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
