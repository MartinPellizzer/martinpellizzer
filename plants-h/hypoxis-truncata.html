Hypoxis truncata

Hypoxis truncata, commonly known as the African ground orchid, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period typically occurs in late winter to early spring.

Hypoxis truncata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
