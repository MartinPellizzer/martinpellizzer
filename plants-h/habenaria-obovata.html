Habenaria obovata

Habenaria obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its obovate, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, with a distinctive spurred lip. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject for both horticultural cultivation and botanical study.
