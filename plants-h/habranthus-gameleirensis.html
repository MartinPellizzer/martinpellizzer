Habranthus gameleirensis

Habranthus gameleirensis, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
