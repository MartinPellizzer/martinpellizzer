Hieracium eriophyllum

Hieracium eriophyllum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium eriophyllum is often considered a weed due to its ability to spread rapidly and colonize new areas.
