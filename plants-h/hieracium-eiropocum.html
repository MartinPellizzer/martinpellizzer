Hieracium eiropocum

Hieracium eiropocum, commonly known as the European hedgehog.

This plant is a perennial species native to Europe, characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It often thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows and along roadsides.
