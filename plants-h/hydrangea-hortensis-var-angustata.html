Hydrangea hortensis var. angustata

Hydrangea hortensis var. angustata, commonly known as the narrowleaf hydrangea, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features narrow, elongated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces cone-shaped flower clusters that range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. It thrives in partial shade and requires moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to garden settings.
