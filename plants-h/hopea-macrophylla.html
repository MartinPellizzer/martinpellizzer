Hopea macrophylla

Hopea macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf hopea, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by woody pods containing flat, winged seeds. It is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Hopea macrophylla plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitats.
