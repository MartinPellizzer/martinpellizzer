Homalanthus populneus var. laevis

Homalanthus populneus var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, often greyish bark and clusters of bright yellow, flame-like flowers that appear in summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and urban landscapes.
