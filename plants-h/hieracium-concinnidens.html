Hieracium concinnidens

Hieracium concinnidens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium concinnidens is often found in temperate climates and is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and seeds.
