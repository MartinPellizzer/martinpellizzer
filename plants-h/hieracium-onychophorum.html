Hieracium onychophorum

Hieracium onychophorum, commonly known as the purple meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces showy purple to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
