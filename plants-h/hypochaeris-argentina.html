Hypochaeris argentina

Hypochaeris argentina, commonly known as the Argentine weed or lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands, roadsides, and agricultural fields. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer, followed by seeds dispersed by wind.

Hypochaeris argentina is often considered a weed but has some ornamental value due to its bright flowers.
