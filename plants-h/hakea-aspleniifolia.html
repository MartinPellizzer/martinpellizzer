Hakea aspleniifolia

Hakea aspleniifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It is adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Hakea aspleniifolia is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
