Horsfieldia nervosa

Horsfieldia nervosa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescence and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a distinct veining pattern.

Horsfieldia nervosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
