Hieracium igneum

Hieracium igneum, commonly known as the flame hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange-red flowers, which are typically clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy, upright stems and basal leaves that are usually lobed or toothed. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and often invasive species in the regions where it is found.
