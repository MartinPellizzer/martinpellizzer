Hypericum caracasanum

Hypericum caracasanum, commonly known as the Carpathian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with orange stamens, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum caracasanum is known for its medicinal properties, similar to other members of the Hypericaceae family.
