Hypochaeris lechleri

Hypochaeris lechleri, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer, followed by small, oval-shaped seeds.

Hypochaeris lechleri is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
