Huberia corymbosa

Huberia corymbosa, commonly known as the African orchid or African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically features broad, waxy leaves and produces inflorescences of small, colorful blooms. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Huberia corymbosa is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
