Heroion

Heroion, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to pink and deep red. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and marshes. Its leaves are broad and floating, with a waxy surface that helps repel water.

The Heroion holds cultural and spiritual significance in various ancient civilizations.
