Hieracium peralbidum

Hieracium peralbidum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium peralbidum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and has a somewhat invasive growth habit in certain regions.
