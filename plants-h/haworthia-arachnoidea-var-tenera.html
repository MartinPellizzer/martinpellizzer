Haworthia arachnoidea var. tenera

Haworthia arachnoidea var. tenera, commonly known as the woolly Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a dense rosette of fleshy, bluish-green leaves covered in fine, white tubercles that give it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers on tall, slender stalks during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
