Heliophytum nicotianifolium

Heliophytum nicotianifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf heliophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are adapted to store water, making it well-suited for arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive choice for rock gardens or container gardening.

Heliophytum nicotianifolium thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water once established.
