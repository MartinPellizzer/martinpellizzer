Hieracium leucotrachelum

Hieracium leucotrachelum, commonly known as the white-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellowish to brownish composite flower heads. The plant features basal and alternate leaves that are typically hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its invasive tendencies, it can outcompete native vegetation in certain regions.
