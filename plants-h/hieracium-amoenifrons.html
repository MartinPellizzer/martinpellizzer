Hieracium amoenifrons

Hieracium amoenifrons, commonly known as the pretty hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are typically arranged in a dense, flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

It is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
