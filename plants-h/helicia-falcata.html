Helicia falcata

Helicia falcata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed holly.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, often appearing in late spring to early summer.

Helicia falcata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens for its dense foliage and seasonal blooms.
