Hieracium tubulascens subsp. fragilicolium

Hieracium tubulascens subsp.

fragilicolium, commonly known as the fragile-stem hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to brownish flower heads.

The plant typically features slender, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape. It produces small, tubular flowers arranged in a composite head, which are visited by various pollinators.

This subspecies is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.
