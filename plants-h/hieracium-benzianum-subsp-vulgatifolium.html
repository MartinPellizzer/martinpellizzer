Hieracium benzianum subsp. vulgatifolium

Hieracium benzianum subsp.

vulgatifolium, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 50 cm. Its leaves are hairy and lance-shaped, while its yellow flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It is an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
