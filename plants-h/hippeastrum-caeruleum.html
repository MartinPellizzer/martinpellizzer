Hippeastrum caeruleum

Hippeastrum caeruleum, commonly known as the blue hurricane lily, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue and purple, with a striking red or orange center. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous base, which stores nutrients for the plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Hippeastrum caeruleum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
