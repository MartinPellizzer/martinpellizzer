Hieracium Arrosum

Hieracium arrosum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers. This plant often grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in sunny environments. Its hairy leaves and bright blooms make it easily recognizable in the wild. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in certain regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
