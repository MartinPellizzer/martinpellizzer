Hieracium albulae

Hieracium albulae, commonly known as the white hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its fluffy, white to pale yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and its stems are usually unbranched.

Hieracium albulae is a perennial species that is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
