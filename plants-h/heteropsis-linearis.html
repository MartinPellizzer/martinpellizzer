Heteropsis linearis

Heteropsis linearis, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or rocky areas. Its root system is adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soils, and it reproduces through seeds and rhizomes.

Heteropsis linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
