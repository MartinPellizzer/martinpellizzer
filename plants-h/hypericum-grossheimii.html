Hypericum grossheimii

Hypericum grossheimii, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, which are typical features of the Hypericaceae family. The plant produces small, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum grossheimii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
