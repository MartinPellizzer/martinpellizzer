Hypericum delavayi

Hypericum delavayi, commonly known as the Delavay's St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Hypericum delavayi is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
