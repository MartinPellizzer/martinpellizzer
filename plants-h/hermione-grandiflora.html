Hermione grandiflora

Hermione grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora Hermione, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a glossy surface that reflects light.

The flowers bloom in clusters, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
