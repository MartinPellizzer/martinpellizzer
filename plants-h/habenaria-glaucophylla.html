Habenaria glaucophylla

Habenaria glaucophylla, commonly known as the blue-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically a bluish-green color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
