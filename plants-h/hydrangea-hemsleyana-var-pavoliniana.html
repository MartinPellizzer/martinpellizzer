Hydrangea hemsleyana var. pavoliniana

Hydrangea hemsleyana var. pavoliniana, commonly known as the Hemsley hydrangea, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that typically range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant prefers acidic soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens. Its foliage consists of large, serrated leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained environments.
