Helichrysum monticola

Helichrysum monticola, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a grayish or silver-green hue.

Helichrysum monticola is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.
