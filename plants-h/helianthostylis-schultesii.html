Helianthostylis schultesii

Helianthostylis schultesii, commonly known as the Schultesi's sunflower.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, and belongs to the Asteraceae family. The plant features showy yellow flowers that resemble those of the sunflower, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. It typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.
