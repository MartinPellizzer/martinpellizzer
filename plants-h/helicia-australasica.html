Helicia australasica

Helicia australasica, commonly known as the Australian holly.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Australia, characterized by its dense, leathery foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange-red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are typically oval to elliptic in shape, with a glossy surface and a serrated margin.

Helicia australasica is often found in coastal and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.
