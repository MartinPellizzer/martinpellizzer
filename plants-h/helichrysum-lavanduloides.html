Helichrysum lavanduloides

Helichrysum lavanduloides, commonly known as the silver leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that resemble those of lavender. The plant has silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance and helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Helichrysum lavanduloides is also valued for its ornamental use and is often used in dried flower arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
