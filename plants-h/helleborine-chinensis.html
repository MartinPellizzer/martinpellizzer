Helleborine chinensis

Helleborine chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect stem and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in spikes. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Helleborine chinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
