Hoffmannseggia miranda

Hoffmannseggia miranda, commonly known as the Miranda's Hoffmannseggia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. This species is characterized by its cylindrical stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant exhibits a succulent structure, allowing it to store water in its thick, fleshy stems.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh native environment.
