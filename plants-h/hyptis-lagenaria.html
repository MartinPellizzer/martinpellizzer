Hyptis lagenaria

Hyptis lagenaria, commonly known as the silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hyptis lagenaria is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
