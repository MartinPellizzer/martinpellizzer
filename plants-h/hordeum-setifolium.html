Hordeum setifolium

Hordeum setifolium, commonly known as slender barley, is a species of grass native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is characterized by its fine, silky hairs along the leaf blades and stems, which give it a distinctive appearance. This plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to beige flowers.

Hordeum setifolium is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
