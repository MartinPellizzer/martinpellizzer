Homalomena robusta

Homalomena robusta, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is native to Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
