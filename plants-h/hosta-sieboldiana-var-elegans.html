Hosta sieboldiana var. elegans

Hosta sieboldiana var. elegans, commonly known as the Siebold's Hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-blue sheen. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late summer, which are clustered in loose racemes. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various garden conditions.
