Hedythyrsus katangensis var. ternifolius

Hedythyrsus katangensis var. ternifolius, commonly known as the Katanga hedythyrsus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and bears small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The leaves are usually simple and alternate, with a rough texture and a somewhat leathery feel. This species is often found in moist, wooded areas and is adapted to the humid climate of its native habitat.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany and ecological research.
