Hieracium villosum subsp. scorzonerifolium

Hieracium villosum subsp.

scorzonerifolium, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, lobed leaves and yellow to orange flower heads.

The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats and has a rosette growth form. Its flowers are composed of ray florets and tubular florets, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along riverbanks.
