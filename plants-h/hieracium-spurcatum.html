Hieracium spurcatum

Hieracium spurcatum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it is often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

It is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes and can form dense colonies.
