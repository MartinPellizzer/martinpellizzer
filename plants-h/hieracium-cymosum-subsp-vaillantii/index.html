Hieracium Cymosum Subsp. Vaillantii

Hieracium cymosum subsp. vaillantii is a lesser-known member of the daisy family known for its delicate yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. This subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable plant. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this species from habitat loss and environmental changes.
