Hordeum vulgare var. deficiens

Hordeum vulgare var. deficiens, commonly known as the deficient barley, is a variant of the common barley species.

It is characterized by its reduced number of spikelets and shorter grains compared to typical barley. This variety is often found in specific ecological niches, such as disturbed soils or areas with limited nutrients. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for genetic and agricultural research.

The plant typically grows in temperate regions and has a short growing season.
