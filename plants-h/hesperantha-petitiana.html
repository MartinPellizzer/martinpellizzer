Hesperantha petitiana

Hesperantha petitiana, commonly known as the Cape bell orchid, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

Hesperantha petitiana is a perennial herb that is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact growth habit.
