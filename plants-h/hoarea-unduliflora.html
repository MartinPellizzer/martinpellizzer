Hoarea unduliflora

Hoarea unduliflora, commonly known as the undulating-flowered heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features small, leathery leaves and produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Hoarea unduliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
