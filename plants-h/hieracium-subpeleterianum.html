Hieracium subpeleterianum

Hieracium subpeleterianum, commonly known as the subpeleterian hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and hairy stems. Native to Central Asia, it thrives in grassy and open habitats. The plant has narrow, serrated leaves and produces small, elongated achenes as its seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
