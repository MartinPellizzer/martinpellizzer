Hesperantha brevicaulis

Hesperantha brevicaulis, commonly known as the short-stemmed star orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Hesperantha brevicaulis thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among horticulturists and botanists.
