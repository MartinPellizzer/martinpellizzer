Hippeastrum estense

Hippeastrum estense, commonly known as the Estense amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces a tall, sturdy stem with broad, strap-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters and are known for their strong, spicy fragrance.

Hippeastrum estense is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
