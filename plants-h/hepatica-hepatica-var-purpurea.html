Hepatica hepatica var. purpurea

Hepatica hepatica var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple hepatica, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its early spring blooms. The plant features distinctive, palmate leaves that are often hairy and have a waxy texture. Its flowers are typically blue or purple, with five to seven petals and a yellow center.
