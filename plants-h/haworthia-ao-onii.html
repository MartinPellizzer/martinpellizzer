Haworthia ao-onii

Haworthia ao-onii, commonly known as the "Lucky Cat" or "Cute Cat," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with distinct, waxy white markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
