Hermannia woodii

Hermannia woodii, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine white pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically blooming from spring to autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers indirect light, making it a popular houseplant.
