Haworthia bella

Haworthia bella, commonly known as the "Cape Beauty," is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a blue-green hue and prominent white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
