Habenaria backeri

Habenaria backeri, commonly known as the Malayan butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a distinctive butterfly-like shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Habenaria backeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
