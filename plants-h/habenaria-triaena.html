Habenaria triaena

Habenaria triaena, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green with a yellowish throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The orchid is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
