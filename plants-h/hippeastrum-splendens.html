Hippeastrum splendens

Hippeastrum splendens, commonly known as the Splendour amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of red, orange, and white. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous base, which stores nutrients for flowering. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hippeastrum splendens is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
