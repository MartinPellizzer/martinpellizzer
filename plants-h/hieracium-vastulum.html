Hieracium vastulum

Hieracium vastulum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow flowers in a dense cluster at the top of the stem. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate regions.
