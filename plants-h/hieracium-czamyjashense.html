Hieracium czamyjashense

Hieracium czamyjashense, commonly known as the Czamyjashan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, adapting to harsh climatic conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
