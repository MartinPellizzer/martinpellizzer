Hypsophila dielsiana

Hypsophila dielsiana, commonly known as the pink fairy wand, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in horticultural settings.
