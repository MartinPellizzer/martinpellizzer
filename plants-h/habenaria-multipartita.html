Habenaria multipartita

Habenaria multipartita, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has a slender, erect stem with several nodes, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette. The flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
