Helianthemum hirtum var. villosissimum

Helianthemum hirtum var. villosissimum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves covered in dense white or grayish hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in gardens.
