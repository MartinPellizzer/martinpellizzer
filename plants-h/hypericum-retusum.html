Hypericum retusum

Hypericum retusum, commonly known as rough St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has woody stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Hypericum retusum is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
