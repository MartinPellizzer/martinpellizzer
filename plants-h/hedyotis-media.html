Hedyotis media

Hedyotis media, commonly known as smallleaf knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It features small, opposite, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which are usually borne in the axils of the leaves. Hedyotis media is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common species in the Rubiaceae family.
