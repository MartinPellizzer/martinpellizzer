Hoya plicata

Hoya plicata, commonly known as the pleated wax plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, pleated leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, often blooming during the summer months. Hoya plicata is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
