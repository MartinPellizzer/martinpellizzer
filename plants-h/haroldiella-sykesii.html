Haroldiella sykesii

Haroldiella sykesii, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue with a yellow center. The plant grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with moderate to high sunlight exposure. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette.

Haroldiella sykesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
