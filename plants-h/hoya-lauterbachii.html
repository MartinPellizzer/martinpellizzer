Hoya lauterbachii

Hoya lauterbachii, commonly known as the "Coral Hoya," is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, resembling coral in appearance. Hoya lauterbachii thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with high humidity.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and ornamental appeal.
