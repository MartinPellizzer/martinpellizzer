Hieracium adeniscodes

Hieracium adeniscodes, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are usually covered in dense, white or grayish hairs.

Hieracium adeniscodes is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
