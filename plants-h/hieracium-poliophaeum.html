Hieracium poliophaeum

Hieracium poliophaeum, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium poliophaeum is also known for its ability to thrive in disturbed habitats, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
