Heuchera barbarossa

Heuchera barbarossa, commonly known as the Black Dragon heuchera, is a striking ornamental plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its deep, dark green to almost black foliage that contrasts beautifully with its vibrant red and orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 18 inches, with a spread of about 12 to 18 inches, making it a compact and visually appealing choice for gardens. It thrives in partial shade to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Heuchera barbarossa is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often used in shaded garden beds or as a border plant.
