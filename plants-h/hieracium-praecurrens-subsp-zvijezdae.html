Hieracium praecurrens subsp. zvijezdae

Hieracium praecurrens subsp.

zvijezdae, commonly known as the Croatian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkans. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has lance-shaped leaves with rough textures and produces numerous small, fluffy seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in parts of its native range.
