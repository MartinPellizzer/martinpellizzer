Havilandia robusta

Havilandia robusta, commonly known as the hardy hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It features large, showy flowers with five petals, typically in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant has rough, hairy leaves and a woody stem, which contribute to its robust appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and adaptable to various growing conditions.
