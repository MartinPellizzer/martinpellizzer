Helietta barrancae

Helietta barrancae, commonly known as the barranca heath, is a rare plant species native to the coastal areas of southern California and northern Baja California.

The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to the harsh coastal climate with high salinity and strong winds.
