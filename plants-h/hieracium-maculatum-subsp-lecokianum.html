Hieracium maculatum subsp. lecokianum

Hieracium maculatum subsp.

lecokianum, commonly known as the Lecokian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellowish-green flowers and is native to Central and Eastern Europe.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, showing a preference for sunny locations. It has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture, and its flowers are usually arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

This subspecies is often found in regions with temperate climates and is relatively hardy in various soil types.
