Hybanthus communis var. circaeoides

Hybanthus communis var. circaeoides, commonly known as the slender hybanthus, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in umbels, often appearing in the axils of the leaves.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
