Hieracium patens subsp. hecatadenum

Hieracium patens subsp.

hecatadenum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The plant has basal and upright stems, with leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting pollinators.
