Haworthiopsis pungens

Haworthiopsis pungens, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces tiny, white flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by small, red berries. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
