Hoya lagunaensis

Hoya lagunaensis, commonly known as the Laguna Wisteria, is a climbing epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features waxy, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or lavender, often with a slight fragrance. Hoya lagunaensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
