Huperzia vanikorensis

Huperzia vanikorensis, commonly known as the Vanikoro huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette formation. The plant is native to the Pacific islands, particularly Vanikoro in the Solomon Islands. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Huperzia vanikorensis is notable for its primitive morphology and is of interest to botanists studying ancient plant lineages.
