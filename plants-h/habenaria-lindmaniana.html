Habenaria lindmaniana

Habenaria lindmaniana, commonly known as the Lindman's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
