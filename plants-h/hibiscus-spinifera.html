Hibiscus spinifera

Hibiscus spinifera, commonly known as the spiny hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant features serrated, spiny leaves that provide a distinctive appearance. Hibiscus spinifera thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
