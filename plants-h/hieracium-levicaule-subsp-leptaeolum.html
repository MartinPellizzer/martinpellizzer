Hieracium levicaule subsp. leptaeolum

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

leptaeolum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a hairy texture, which helps in reducing water loss in arid environments. This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
