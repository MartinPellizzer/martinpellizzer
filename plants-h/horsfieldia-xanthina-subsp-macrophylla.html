Horsfieldia xanthina subsp. macrophylla

Horsfieldia xanthina subsp.

macrophylla, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers.

The subspecies macrophylla is distinguished by its notably large leaves, which can reach several feet in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in lowland rainforests.

This plant is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in its native habitats.
