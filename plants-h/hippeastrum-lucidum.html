Hippeastrum lucidum

Hippeastrum lucidum, commonly known as the shining amaryllis, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and lance-shaped. It is a bulbous plant that thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Hippeastrum lucidum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
