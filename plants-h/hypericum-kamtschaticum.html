Hypericum kamtschaticum

Hypericum kamtschaticum, commonly known as the Kamtschaticum St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features upright stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous areas.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.
