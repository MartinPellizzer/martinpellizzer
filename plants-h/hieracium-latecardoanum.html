Hieracium latecardoanum

Hieracium latecardoanum, commonly known as the latecardo hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and tall, branching stems that bear yellowish to reddish flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are arranged in dense clusters.

Hieracium latecardoanum is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in certain environments.
