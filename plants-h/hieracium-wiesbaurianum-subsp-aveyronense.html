Hieracium wiesbaurianum subsp. aveyronense

Hieracium wiesbaurianum subsp.

aveyronense, commonly known as the Aveyron hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern France. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, clustered flowers in the summer months.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and localized species within its native range.
