Helianthemum umbellatum

Helianthemum umbellatum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are adapted to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats due to its drought tolerance.

Helianthemum umbellatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping for its hardy nature and vibrant blooms.
