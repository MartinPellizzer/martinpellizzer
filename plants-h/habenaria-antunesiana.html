Habenaria antunesiana

Habenaria antunesiana, commonly known as the Antunes' orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces a single raceme of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to the humid climate of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
