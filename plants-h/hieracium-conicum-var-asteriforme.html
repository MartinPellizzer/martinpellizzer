Hieracium conicum var. asteriforme

Hieracium conicum var. asteriforme, commonly known as the star-shaped hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in meadows and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture.

This variant is distinguished by its unique star-shaped flower heads, which set it apart from other hawkweed species.
