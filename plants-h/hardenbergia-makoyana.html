Hardenbergia makoyana

Hardenbergia makoyana, commonly known as the purple wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
