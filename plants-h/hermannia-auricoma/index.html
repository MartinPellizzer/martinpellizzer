Hermannia Auricoma

Hermannia auricoma is a strikingly unique plant native to the tropical regions of Africa. It is known for its vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic flair to its appearance. This species belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its distinctive ear-shaped petals give it the common name "ear-pot" or "ear-cup." Despite its beauty, Hermannia auricoma remains a relatively obscure plant in the world of botany.
