Hieracium nudicaule

Hieracium nudicaule, commonly known as the naked-stem hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium nudicaule is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
