Hypoestes pubescens

Hypoestes pubescens, commonly known as the "Shiny Leaf" or "Cape Myrtle," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, clustered in dense inflorescences.

Hypoestes pubescens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
