Habenaria integra

Habenaria integra, commonly known as the orchid with the unique floral structure, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to various regions in Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that produce multiple shoots and leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Habenaria integra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
