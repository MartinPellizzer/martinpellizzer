Hedyosmum laciniatum

Hedyosmum laciniatum, commonly known as the laceleaf hedyosmum, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, deeply lobed leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-yellow in color, and it produces small, fleshy fruits.

Hedyosmum laciniatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage.
