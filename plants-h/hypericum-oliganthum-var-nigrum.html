Hypericum oliganthum var. nigrum

Hypericum oliganthum var. nigrum, commonly known as black-stemmed St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, almost black stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is part of the genus Hypericum, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a decorative plant in smaller spaces.
