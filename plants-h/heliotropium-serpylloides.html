Heliotropium serpylloides

Heliotropium serpylloides, commonly known as the slender heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often resembling those of the common heliotrope. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered with fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings.
