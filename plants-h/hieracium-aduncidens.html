Hieracium aduncidens

Hieracium aduncidens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its invasive tendencies, it can sometimes outcompete native flora in certain regions.
