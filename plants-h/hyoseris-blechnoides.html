Hyoseris blechnoides

Hyoseris blechnoides, commonly known as the fern-like sunflower, is a herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fern-like leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is composed of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that resemble those of a fern.

The flowers are arranged in a composite head, typical of the sunflower family, and bloom in late summer.
