Hoya Obovata Var. Kerrii

Hoya obovata var. kerrii is a unique and striking plant known for its waxy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, star-like flowers. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight. This varietal is prized by plant enthusiasts for its ease of care and ornamental beauty. Its foliage often displays a striking variegation of green and cream, adding visual interest to any indoor space. Whether grown as a houseplant or in a greenhouse, Hoya obovata var. kerrii is a rewarding addition to any plant collection.
