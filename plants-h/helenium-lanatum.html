Helenium lanatum

Helenium lanatum, commonly known as the woolly helenium, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, orange to yellow ray flowers. The plant features soft, fuzzy foliage that gives it its common name, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in prairies and open woodlands, where it blooms in late summer to early fall.

Helenium lanatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
