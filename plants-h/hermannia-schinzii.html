Hermannia schinzii

Hermannia schinzii, commonly known as the dwarf iron plant, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a glossy texture and produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hermannia schinzii is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
