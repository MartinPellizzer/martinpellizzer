Hieracium stenosemum

Hieracium stenosemum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
