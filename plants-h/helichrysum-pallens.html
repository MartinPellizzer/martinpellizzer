Helichrysum pallens

Helichrysum pallens, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its long-lasting flowers and attractive foliage.
