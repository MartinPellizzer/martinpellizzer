Hieracium Lachenalii Subsp. Lortetiae

Hieracium lachenalii subsp. lortetiae is a rare and delicate plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the dandelion family and is known for its small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. This subspecies is often found in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in harsh environments. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species by conservationists. Its unique characteristics make it an important subject for botanical study and ecological preservation.
