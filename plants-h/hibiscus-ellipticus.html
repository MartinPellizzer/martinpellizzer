Hibiscus ellipticus

Hibiscus ellipticus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hibiscus, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped, elliptic leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom during the summer months. Hibiscus ellipticus thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
