Hakea wittweri

Hakea wittweri, commonly known as the red hakea, is an evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves that are typically red or reddish-brown in color. The plant produces cylindrical, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. Hakea wittweri thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
