Haworthia borealis

Haworthia borealis, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often marked with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
