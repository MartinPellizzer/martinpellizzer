Hieracium colpodes

Hieracium colpodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early autumn. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium colpodes is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting pollinators.
