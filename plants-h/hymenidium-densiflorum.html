Hymenidium densiflorum

Hymenidium densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered hymenidium, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

Hymenidium densiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
