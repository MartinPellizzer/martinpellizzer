Haworthia bolusii var. blackbeardiana

Haworthia bolusii var. blackbeardiana, commonly known as Blackbeard Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, dark green leaves with a waxy coating and prominent white to light green markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped white flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
