Homalomena confusa

Homalomena confusa, commonly known as the purple-leafed arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and showy inflorescences. The plant features large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with purple and green hues. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in a spadix, surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Homalomena confusa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
