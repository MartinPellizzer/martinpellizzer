Helianthemum pannosum subsp. pannosum

Helianthemum pannosum subsp.

pannosum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia. It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has dense, hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suited for rocky or coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
