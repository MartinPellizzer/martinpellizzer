Habenaria radicans

Habenaria radicans, commonly known as the hanging orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, hanging stems that bear clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure and adaptability to humid conditions.
