Hypericum perforatum var. pellucidum

Hypericum perforatum var. pellucidum, commonly known as the translucent St. John's wort, is a variant of the common St. John's wort.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. The leaves are characterized by their translucent yellow spots, which are visible through the translucent leaf blades. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and rocky areas.
