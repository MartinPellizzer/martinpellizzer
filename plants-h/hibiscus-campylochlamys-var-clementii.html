Hibiscus campylochlamys var. clementii

Hibiscus campylochlamys var. clementii, commonly known as the Clementii hibiscus, is a rare and distinctive variant of the hibiscus genus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal or tropical regions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticultural enthusiasts alike.
