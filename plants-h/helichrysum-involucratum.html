Helichrysum involucratum

Helichrysum involucratum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
