Hieracium collinderi

Hieracium collinderi, commonly known as Collinder's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small yellow to orange flowers, which are arranged in dense clusters.

Hieracium collinderi is known for its hardy nature and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
