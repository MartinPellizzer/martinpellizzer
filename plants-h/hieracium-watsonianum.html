Hieracium watsonianum

Hieracium watsonianum, commonly known as Watson's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Watson's hawkweed is often found in disturbed soils and can form dense colonies.
