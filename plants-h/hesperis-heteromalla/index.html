Hesperis Heteromalla

Hesperis heteromalla is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is known for its striking white to pale pink blooms that appear in late spring. This species is part of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many familiar garden plants. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now classified as critically endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this beautiful and rare species.
