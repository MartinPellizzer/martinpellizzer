Hieracium scabriusculum var. scabrum

Hieracium scabriusculum var. scabrum, commonly known as rough hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive tactile texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other open habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is considered a common weed in many regions.
