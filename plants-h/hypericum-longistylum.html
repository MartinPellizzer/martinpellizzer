Hypericum longistylum

Hypericum longistylum, commonly known as the long-stemmed St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has opposite, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along roadsides.

Hypericum longistylum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
