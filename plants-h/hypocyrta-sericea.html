Hypocyrta sericea

Hypocyrta sericea, commonly known as the silky hypocrysta, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic properties.
