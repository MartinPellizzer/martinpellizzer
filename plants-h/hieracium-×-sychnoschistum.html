Hieracium × sychnoschistum

Hieracium × sychnoschistum, commonly known as the hybrid meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate leaves and produces numerous yellow composite flowers in the summer months. The plant is typically found in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has a taproot system and exhibits asexual reproduction through rhizomes, allowing it to spread readily in suitable environments.

Due to its hybrid nature, it displays a range of morphological variations, making it challenging to distinguish from its parent species.
