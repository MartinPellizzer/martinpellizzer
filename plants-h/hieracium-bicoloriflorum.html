Hieracium bicoloriflorum

Hieracium bicoloriflorum, commonly known as the two-colored hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

Hieracium bicoloriflorum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
