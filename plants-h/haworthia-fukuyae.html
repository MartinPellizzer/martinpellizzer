Haworthia fukuyae

Haworthia fukuyae, commonly known as the Fukuyae Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks bearing white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
