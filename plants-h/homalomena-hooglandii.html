Homalomena hooglandii

Homalomena hooglandii, commonly known as the Bornean arrowroot.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. It produces small, pale green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by red berries containing seeds.

Homalomena hooglandii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
