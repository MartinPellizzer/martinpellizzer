Hieracium obrhombiare

Hieracium obrhombiare, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering stems that bear numerous yellow to orange disk flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides. It has a fibrous root system and produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
