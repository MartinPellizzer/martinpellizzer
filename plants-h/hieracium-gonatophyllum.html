Hieracium gonatophyllum

Hieracium gonatophyllum, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium gonatophyllum is typically found in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, and it can sometimes be invasive in certain regions.
