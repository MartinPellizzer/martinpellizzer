Hieracium magyaricum subsp. megalomastix

Hieracium magyaricum subsp.

megalomastix, commonly known as the Hungarian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flower heads.

The subspecies is distinguished by its large, swollen flower heads and robust growth habit. It typically thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and botanical gardens.
