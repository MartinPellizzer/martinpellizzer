Halophila ovalis var. minor

Halophila ovalis var. minor, commonly known as the lesser sea whiskgrass, is a marine flowering plant belonging to the Alismataceae family.

It is a submerged aquatic plant that thrives in shallow, brackish, and coastal waters. The plant is often found in estuaries and tidal zones where it tolerates varying salinity levels.
