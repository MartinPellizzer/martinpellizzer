Helenium candidum

Helenium candidum, commonly known as white sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces large, showy white to pale yellow flower heads that bloom in late summer. Helenium candidum is often found in moist meadows, wetlands, and along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
