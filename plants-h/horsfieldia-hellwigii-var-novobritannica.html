Horsfieldia hellwigii var. novobritannica

Horsfieldia hellwigii var. novobritannica, commonly known as the New Britain orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly New Britain in the Solomon Islands. The plant is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and its role in the biodiversity of its native habitat.
