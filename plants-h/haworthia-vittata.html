Haworthia vittata

Haworthia vittata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are often marked with white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments. It prefers bright, indirect sunlight and is known for its low maintenance requirements.

Haworthia vittata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
