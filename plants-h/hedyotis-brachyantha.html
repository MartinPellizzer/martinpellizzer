Hedyotis brachyantha

Hedyotis brachyantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, greenish-yellow flowers clustered in umbels, which bloom during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hedyotis brachyantha is often found in sandy or rocky soils in coastal and inland regions.
