Habenaria poissoniana

Habenaria poissoniana, commonly known as the Poisson's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
