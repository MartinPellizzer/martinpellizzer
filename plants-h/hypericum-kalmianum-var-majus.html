Hypericum kalmianum var. majus

Hypericum kalmianum var. majus, commonly known as the Kalm's St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a reddish hue when mature.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
