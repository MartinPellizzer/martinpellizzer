Habenaria kassneriana

Habenaria kassneriana, commonly known as the Kassner's orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
