Hedysarum ferganense var. minjanense

Hedysarum ferganense var. minjanense, commonly known as the Fergana milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.

Its seeds are often used as forage for livestock.
