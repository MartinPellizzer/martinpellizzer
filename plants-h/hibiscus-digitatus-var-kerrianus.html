Hibiscus digitatus var. kerrianus

Hibiscus digitatus var. kerrianus, commonly known as the Kerrian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with a prominent central stigma. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Hibiscus digitatus var. kerrianus is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
