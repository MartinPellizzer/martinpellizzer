Hydrangea linkweiensis

Hydrangea linkweiensis, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth texture and dark green coloration.

Hydrangea linkweiensis thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
