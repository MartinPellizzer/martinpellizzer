Hypericum stenoclados

Hypericum stenoclados, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
