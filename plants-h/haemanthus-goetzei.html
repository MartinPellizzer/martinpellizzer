Haemanthus goetzei

Haemanthus goetzei, commonly known as the red-stemmed haemanthus, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking red stems and leaves with a distinctive blue-green hue. The plant produces umbellate inflorescences with white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
