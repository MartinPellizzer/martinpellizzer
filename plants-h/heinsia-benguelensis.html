Heinsia benguelensis

Heinsia benguelensis, commonly known as the Benguela heinsia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Heinsia benguelensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
