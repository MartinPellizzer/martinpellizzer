Hypericum ardasenovii

Hypericum ardasenovii, commonly known as the Armenian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a low-growing shrub with yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features woody stems and alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to mountainous environments.

Hypericum ardasenovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
