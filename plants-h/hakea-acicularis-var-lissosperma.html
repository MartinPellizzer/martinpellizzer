Hakea acicularis var. lissosperma

Hakea acicularis var. lissosperma, commonly known as the smooth-fruited hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are densely arranged along the stems. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are woody follicles that release seeds when dry.

This variety is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
