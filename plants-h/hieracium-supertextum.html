Hieracium supertextum

Hieracium supertextum, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a purple hue at the base.

It is often considered a weed but plays a role in supporting pollinators and soil stability.
