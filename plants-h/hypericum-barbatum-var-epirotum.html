Hypericum barbatum var. epirotum

Hypericum barbatum var. epirotum, commonly known as the Epirotic St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its medicinal properties are similar to other Hypericum species, though it is less commonly used in modern herbal medicine.
