Hieracium pilosella subsp. inalpestriforme

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

inalpestriforme, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in composite inflorescences, typical of the dandelion family. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing in open, sunny habitats.
