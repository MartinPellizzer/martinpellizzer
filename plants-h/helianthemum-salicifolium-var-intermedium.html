Helianthemum salicifolium var. intermedium

Helianthemum salicifolium var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate willow-leafed sunflower, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, willow-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color and covered in fine white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is noted for its hardy nature and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
