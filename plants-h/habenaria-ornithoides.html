Habenaria ornithoides

Habenaria ornithoides, commonly known as the bird's nest orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, bird-like appearance, with a tall stem and a single, showy flower that resembles a bird's nest. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. Its flowers are often pale green or yellow, with intricate patterns that mimic the texture of a bird's nest.

Habenaria ornithoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
