Hieracium × melanistum

Hieracium × melanistum, commonly known as the black hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It is a perennial herb that spreads through both seeds and rhizomes, making it somewhat invasive in certain regions.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies due to its adaptability and hybrid nature.
