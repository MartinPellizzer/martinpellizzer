Hyphaene natalensis

Hyphaene natalensis, commonly known as the Natal date palm, is a species of palm native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible dates, similar to those of the date palm. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

The Natal date palm is also valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand drought conditions.
