Huernia Brevirostris Subsp. Intermedia

Huernia brevirostris subsp. intermedia is a unique succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its distinctive, tubular-shaped stems and vibrant, star-like flowers. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to survive. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections. Despite its tough exterior, it offers a striking visual appeal that adds charm to any landscape.
