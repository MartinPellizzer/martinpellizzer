Heuchera micrantha var. glaberrima

Heuchera micrantha var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth heuchera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped, smooth, and often variegated leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale pink during the summer months. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often used in woodland gardens.
