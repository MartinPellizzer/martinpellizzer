Hedera pedunculata

Hedera pedunculata, commonly known as the climbing ivy, is a species of flowering plant in the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. Hedera pedunculata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is relatively easy to care for in gardens and indoor settings.
