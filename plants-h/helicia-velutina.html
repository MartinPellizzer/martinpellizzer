Helicia velutina

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are glossy and covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow and appear in clusters.
