Hieracium trebevicianum subsp. deanum

Hieracium trebevicianum subsp.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate to ovate, with serrated margins, and it produces yellow flowers in clusters.
