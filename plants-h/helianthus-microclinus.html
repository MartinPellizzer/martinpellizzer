Helianthus microclinus

Helianthus microclinus, commonly known as the narrowleaf sunflower, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow, daisy-like flowers with a central disk. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a hardy species in dry climates.

The narrowleaf sunflower is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
