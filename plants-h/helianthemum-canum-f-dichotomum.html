Helianthemum canum f. dichotomum

Helianthemum canum f. dichotomum, commonly known as the dichotomous rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for horticultural use.
