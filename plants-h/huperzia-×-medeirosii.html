Huperzia × medeirosii

Huperzia × medeirosii, commonly known as the hybrid rock huperzia, is a fern-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Huperzia selago and Huperzia phlebophylla, characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant features simple, linear leaves that are often arranged in two rows, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in rocky or forested areas.

Huperzia × medeirosii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
