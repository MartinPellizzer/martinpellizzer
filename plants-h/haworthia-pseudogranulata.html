Haworthia pseudogranulata

Haworthia pseudogranulata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with distinctive white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
