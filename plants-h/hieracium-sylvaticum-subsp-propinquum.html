Hieracium sylvaticum subsp. propinquum

Hieracium sylvaticum subsp.

propinquum, commonly known as the wood hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in moist woodland habitats, often found in shaded areas with rich, loamy soil.

The plant features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, consisting of numerous small florets.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its ability to thrive in semi-shaded environments.
