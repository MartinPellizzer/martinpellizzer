Hieracium chrysostyloides

Hieracium chrysostyloides, commonly known as the golden-stem hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium chrysostyloides is often found in sunny, open habitats and can be invasive in certain regions.
