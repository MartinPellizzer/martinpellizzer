Helichrysum excisum

Helichrysum excisum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, papery bracts that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice in horticulture.
