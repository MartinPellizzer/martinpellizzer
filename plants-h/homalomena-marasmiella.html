Homalomena marasmiella

Homalomena marasmiella, commonly known as the Marasmiella arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
