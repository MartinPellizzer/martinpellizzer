Habenaria mesophylla

Habenaria mesophylla, commonly known as the pale leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
