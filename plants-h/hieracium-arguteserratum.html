Hieracium arguteserratum

Hieracium arguteserratum, commonly known as the serrated hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grasslands.

It is often considered a weed but has ecological value in supporting pollinators.
