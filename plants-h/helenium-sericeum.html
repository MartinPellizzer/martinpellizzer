Helenium sericeum

Helenium sericeum, commonly known as the silky helenium, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, orange to red ray flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and prairies. Its foliage is covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Helenium sericeum is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
