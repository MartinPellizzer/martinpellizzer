Hieracium vulgatum subsp. jaccardii

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

The plant produces yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and its leaves are usually hairy and lance-shaped. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
