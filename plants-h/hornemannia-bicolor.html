Hornemannia bicolor

Hornemannia bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored hornemannia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue or purple and are arranged in whorls along the stems.

Hornemannia bicolor thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
