Hibiscus stenophyllus

Hibiscus stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Hibiscus stenophyllus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
