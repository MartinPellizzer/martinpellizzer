Habenaria nivea

Habenaria nivea, commonly known as the white-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white flowers with distinctive veining. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Habenaria nivea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
