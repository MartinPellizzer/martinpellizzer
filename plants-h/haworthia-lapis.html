Haworthia lapis

Haworthia lapis, commonly known as the blue haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
