Hyospathe elegans subsp. costaricensis

Hyospathe elegans subsp.

costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican elegant hyospathe, is a member of the Araceae family. It is native to Central America, particularly found in Costa Rica and Panama.

The plant is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
