Hieracium macrolepium

Hieracium macrolepium, commonly known as the large-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas. The plant features yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and its leaves are usually broad and lobed. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragweed.

Hieracium macrolepium is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
