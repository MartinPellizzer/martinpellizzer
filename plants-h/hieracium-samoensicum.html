Hieracium samoensicum

Hieracium samoensicum, commonly known as the Samoan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the island of Samoa in the South Pacific.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. It has serrated leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom from late winter to early spring.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
