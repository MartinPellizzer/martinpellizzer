Hieracium cymosum subsp. leptadenium

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

leptadenium, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The plant typically has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands, often forming dense colonies.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller, more elongated leaves compared to other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
