Hieracium rigidum subsp. glareosum

Hieracium rigidum subsp.

glareosum, commonly known as the rigid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and wastelands. Its leaves are stiff and hairy, with a distinctive yellowish-green color, and its flowers are yellow with prominent central disc florets.

It is a common weed that can spread rapidly in suitable environments.
