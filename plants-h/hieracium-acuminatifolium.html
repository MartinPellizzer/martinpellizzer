Hieracium acuminatifolium

Hieracium acuminatifolium, commonly known as the slender spearthistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Due to its hardy nature, it is considered a common weed in some regions.
