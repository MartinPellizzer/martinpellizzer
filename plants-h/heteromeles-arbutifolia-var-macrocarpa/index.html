Heteromeles Arbutifolia Var. Macrocarpa

Coffeeberry, scientifically known as Heteromeles arbutifolia var. macrocarpa, is a striking evergreen shrub native to coastal regions of Southern California. It is prized for its vibrant red berries that persist through winter, adding visual interest to landscapes. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits have made it a popular choice for native plant enthusiasts. Despite its ornamental appeal, the Coffeeberry also plays an important role in supporting local wildlife.
