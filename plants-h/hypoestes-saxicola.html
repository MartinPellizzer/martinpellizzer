Hypoestes saxicola

Hypoestes saxicola, commonly known as the rock hypoestes, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its foliage is often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hypoestes saxicola is a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping due to its resilience and ornamental value.
