Hypericum struthiolifolium var. congestiflorum

Hypericum struthiolifolium var. congestiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in dense clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species within the Hypericaceae family.
