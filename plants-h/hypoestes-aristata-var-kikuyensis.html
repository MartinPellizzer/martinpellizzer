Hypoestes aristata var. kikuyensis

Hypoestes aristata var. kikuyensis, commonly known as the Kikuyu Hypoestes, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals. The plant typically grows as a shrub with lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
