Hieracium volujakense

Hieracium volujakense, commonly known as the Volujakense hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium volujakense is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
