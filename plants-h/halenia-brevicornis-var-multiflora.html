Halenia brevicornis var. multiflora

Halenia brevicornis var. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora halenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, while its stems are often hairy.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
