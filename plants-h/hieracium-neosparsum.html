Hieracium neosparsum

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and riverbanks. It has hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves, and it produces small, spiky seed heads that aid in wind dispersal.
