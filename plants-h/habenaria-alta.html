Habenaria alta

Habenaria alta, commonly known as the high orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that often have a striking purple or pink hue. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and are pollinated by insects.

Habenaria alta is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
