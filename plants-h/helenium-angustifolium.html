Helenium angustifolium

Helenium angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hedysarum, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its striking red, orange, or yellow ray flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and meadows. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are arranged alternately along the stem.

Helenium angustifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
