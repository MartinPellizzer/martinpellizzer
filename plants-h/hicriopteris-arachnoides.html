Hicriopteris arachnoides

Hicriopteris arachnoides, commonly known as the silky holly fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a delicate appearance. The fronds are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs, which contribute to its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
