Hartia obovata

Hartia obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate hartia, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

Hartia obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its compact growth habit and resilience.
