Hirculus lychnitis

Hirculus lychnitis, commonly known as the candleflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescences and showy, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is found in temperate regions. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Hirculus lychnitis is often considered a herbaceous perennial, thriving in partial shade to full sun.
