Heteromorpha laxiflora var. angustifolia

Heteromorpha laxiflora var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heteromorpha, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, often branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Geraniaceae family.
