Hibiscus fluminis-aprili

Hibiscus fluminis-aprili, commonly known as the April river hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in the spring. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces vibrant pink or red petals. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Hibiscus fluminis-aprili is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
