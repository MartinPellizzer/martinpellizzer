Hippeastrum oconequense

Hippeastrum oconequense, commonly known as the Oconequense amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America, specifically Colombia.

It produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, with a prominent central crest. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are often dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of cool dormancy to bloom properly.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
