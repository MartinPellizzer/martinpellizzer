Hypericum leichtlinii

Hypericum leichtlinii, commonly known as the alpine St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, with small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.
