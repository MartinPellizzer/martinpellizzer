Hedyotis minuta

Hedyotis minuta, commonly known as the small bladdernut, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tiny, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually white or pale yellow.

Hedyotis minuta is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
