Hedysarum thunbergianum

Hedysarum thunbergianum, commonly known as Thunberg's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Hedysarum thunbergianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
