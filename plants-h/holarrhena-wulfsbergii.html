Holarrhena wulfsbergii

Holarrhena wulfsbergii, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season.
