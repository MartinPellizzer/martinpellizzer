Hibiscus schweinfurthii

Hibiscus schweinfurthii, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in hot, dry environments and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Hibiscus schweinfurthii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
