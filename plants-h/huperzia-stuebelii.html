Huperzia stuebelii

Huperzia stuebelii, commonly known as the Stuebel's spikemoss, is a species of fern ally belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its sporophytes produce spores in capsules that are clustered at the tips of the stems.

Huperzia stuebelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its unique appearance.
