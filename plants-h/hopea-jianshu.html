Hopea jianshu

Hopea jianshu, commonly known as the Jianshu Hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Vietnam, and Myanmar. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and a prominent midrib. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in yellow or greenish-yellow hues.

Hopea jianshu is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
