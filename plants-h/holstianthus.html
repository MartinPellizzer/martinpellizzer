Holstianthus

Holstianthus, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is native to the Americas but has been introduced to other tropical regions.

Holstianthus is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
