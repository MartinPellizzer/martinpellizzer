Habenaria corydophora

Habenaria corydophora, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and grasslands. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

Habenaria corydophora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
