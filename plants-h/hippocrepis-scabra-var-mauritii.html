Hippocrepis scabra var. mauritii

Hippocrepis scabra var. mauritii, commonly known as the Mauritius rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or limestone areas. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its delicate floral display.
