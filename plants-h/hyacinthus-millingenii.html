Hyacinthus millingenii

Hyacinthus millingenii, commonly known as the Cape hyacinth, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of violet, blue, and white. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Hyacinthus millingenii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
