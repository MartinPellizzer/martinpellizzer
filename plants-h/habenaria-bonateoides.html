Habenaria bonateoides

Habenaria bonateoides, commonly known as the Bonate's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa, particularly Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
