Hieracium glomerosum

Hieracium glomerosum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium glomerosum is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable habitats.
