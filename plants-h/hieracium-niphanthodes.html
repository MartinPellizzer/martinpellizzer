Hieracium niphanthodes

Hieracium niphanthodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium niphanthodes is often found in temperate regions and can sometimes become invasive in non-native habitats.
