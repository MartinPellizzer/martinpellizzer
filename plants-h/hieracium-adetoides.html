Hieracium adetoides

Hieracium adetoides, commonly known as the Adet's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are typically visited by various pollinators.

Hieracium adetoides is a perennial plant that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
