Hydrangea minamitanii

Hydrangea minamitanii, commonly known as the Japanese hydrangea, is a species native to Japan.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth texture and dark green color.

Hydrangea minamitanii thrives in partial shade and well-drained, acidic soil.
