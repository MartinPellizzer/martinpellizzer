Habenaria polychlamys

Habenaria polychlamys, commonly known as the showy helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, helmet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a contrasting lip. The plant has a slender, upright stem and produces several to many flowers along its length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The orchid's intricate floral structure is adapted for specific pollination by insects, making it an important species in its ecosystem.
