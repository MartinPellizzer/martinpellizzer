Hemiorchis burmanica

Hemiorchis burmanica, commonly known as the Burmese orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a complex root system and thrives in humid, tropical environments. It is often found in forest understories where it receives partial shade.

Hemiorchis burmanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
