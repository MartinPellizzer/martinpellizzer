Hieracium ctenodontoides

Hieracium ctenodontoides, commonly known as the tufted hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically hairy stems. The plant features basal rosettes of leaves that are typically lobed or toothed, with a distinctive central stem that bears flowering heads. Hieracium ctenodontoides is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
