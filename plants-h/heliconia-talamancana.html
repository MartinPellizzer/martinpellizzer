Heliconia talamancana

Heliconia talamancana, commonly known as the Talamancan heliconia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Heliconiaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spathate flowers that enclose the inflorescence. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and as decorative elements. Its striking appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Heliconia talamancana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
