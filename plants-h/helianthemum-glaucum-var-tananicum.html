Helianthemum glaucum var. tananicum

Helianthemum glaucum var. tananicum, commonly known as the Tananian rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has greyish-blue foliage that is often covered in a powdery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and hardy nature make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
