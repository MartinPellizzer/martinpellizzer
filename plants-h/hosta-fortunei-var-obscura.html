Hosta fortunei var. obscura

Hosta fortunei var. obscura, commonly known as the Black Beauty Hosta, is a shade-loving plant native to Korea.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, velvety leaves that are broad and heart-shaped. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers on tall, slender stems in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its striking foliage and is a popular choice in shady garden landscapes.
