Hedysarum elbursense

Hedysarum elbursense, commonly known as the Elburz milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its root system has the ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitat.

Hedysarum elbursense is of interest to botanists and ecologists due to its ecological significance and potential for use in restoration projects.
