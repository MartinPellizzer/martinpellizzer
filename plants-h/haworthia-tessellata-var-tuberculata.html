Haworthia tessellata var. tuberculata

Haworthia tessellata var. tuberculata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves with distinctive white or light green markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The leaves are typically a dark green color, providing a striking contrast to the lighter patterns. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
