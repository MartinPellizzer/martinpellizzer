Herbertia amoena

Herbertia amoena, commonly known as the silver spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves and clusters of small, showy blue or violet flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its foliage is often used in horticultural displays for its ornamental value.

Herbertia amoena is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
