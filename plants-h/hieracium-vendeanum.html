Hieracium vendeanum

Hieracium vendeanum, commonly known as the Vendean hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to western Europe, particularly France.

It belongs to the dandelion family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and waste areas. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rough texture and a distinctive lobed shape.

Hieracium vendeanum is often found in moist environments and can form dense colonies in suitable conditions.
