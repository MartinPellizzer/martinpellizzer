Hieracium acebedoanum

Hieracium acebedoanum, commonly known as the white-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine white down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
