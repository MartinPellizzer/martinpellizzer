Hieracium Onosmoides Subsp. Orthoglossum

Hieracium onosmoides subsp. orthoglossum is a rare and fascinating member of the daisy family. Found primarily in the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, this subspecies thrives in rocky and alpine habitats. Its distinctive yellow flowers and unique leaf structure set it apart from other plants in its genus. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly understood due to its limited distribution and elusive nature. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss and environmental changes.
