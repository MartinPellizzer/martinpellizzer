Haplopappus insecticruris

Haplopappus insecticruris, commonly known as the narrow-pappus grasshopperweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often in sandy or gravelly soils. It has small, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
