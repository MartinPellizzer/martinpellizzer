Hordeum pilosum

Hordeum pilosum, commonly known as silky barley, is a species of grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically green or pale in color. Hordeum pilosum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
