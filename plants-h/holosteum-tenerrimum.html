Holosteum tenerrimum

Holosteum tenerrimum, commonly known as the slender holosteum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Holosteum tenerrimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in xeriscapic gardens.
