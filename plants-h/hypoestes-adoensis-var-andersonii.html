Hypoestes adoensis var. andersonii

Hypoestes adoensis var. andersonii, commonly known as the Anderson's Marvelous Begonia, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or silver patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its compact size and showy blooms, it is widely appreciated as a decorative plant in gardens and homes.
