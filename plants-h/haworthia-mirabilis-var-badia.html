Haworthia mirabilis var. badia

Haworthia mirabilis var. badia, commonly known as the Baviaanskloof Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or pale markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, allowing the plant to thrive in arid environments. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
