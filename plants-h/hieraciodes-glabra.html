Hieraciodes glabra

Hieraciodes glabra, commonly known as smooth hieracium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features upright stems that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. Its flowers are yellow, with five petals and are arranged in a cluster at the ends of the stems.

Hieraciodes glabra is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and is often found in disturbed or marginal habitats.
