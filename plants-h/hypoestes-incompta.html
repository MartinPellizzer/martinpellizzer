Hypoestes incompta

Hypoestes incompta, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, colorful, and often contrasting bracts that resemble small flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes incompta is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
