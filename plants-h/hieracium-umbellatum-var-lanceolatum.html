Hieracium umbellatum var. lanceolatum

Hieracium umbellatum var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of lanceolate leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

The species is known for its rapid spread and can become invasive in certain environments.
