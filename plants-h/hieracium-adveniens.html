Hieracium adveniens

Hieracium adveniens, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium adveniens is an invasive species in many regions, often outcompeting native vegetation.
