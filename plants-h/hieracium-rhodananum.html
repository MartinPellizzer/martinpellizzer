Hieracium rhodananum

Hieracium rhodananum, commonly known as the red-centred hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive red central disk. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed sites, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium rhodananum is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
