Harpullia petiolaris subsp. petiolaris

Harpullia petiolaris subsp.

petiolaris, commonly known as the slender harpullia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, fleshy, orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
