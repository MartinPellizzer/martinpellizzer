Hieracium turritifolium

Hieracium turritifolium, commonly known as the tower-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy habitats and disturbed soils. The plant is characterized by its tall, sparsely hairy stems and leaves that are elongated and often arranged in a rosette. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
