Hopea griffithii

Hopea griffithii, commonly known as the Malayan ironwood, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The tree is characterized by its dense, heavy wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. It typically grows in lowland and hill dipterocarp forests, often reaching heights of up to 40 meters.

The plant has a long lifespan and plays an important ecological role in its native habitats.
