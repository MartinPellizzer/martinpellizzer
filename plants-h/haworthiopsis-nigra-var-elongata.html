Haworthiopsis nigra var. elongata

Haworthiopsis nigra var. elongata, commonly known as the Longleaf Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are elongated and often have a slight blue or grayish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
