Hypoxis ramosa

Hypoxis ramosa, commonly known as the slender star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in open, sunny habitats. It is adapted to dry conditions and has a fibrous root system that helps it survive in arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
