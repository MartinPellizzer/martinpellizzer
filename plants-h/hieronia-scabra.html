Hieronia scabra

Hieronia scabra, commonly known as the rough hieronyma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems covered in fine, rough hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are linear to lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hieronia scabra is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
