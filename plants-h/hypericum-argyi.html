Hypericum argyi

Hypericum argyi, commonly known as American St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has opposite, simple, and elliptic leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or open fields.

Hypericum argyi is a member of the Hypericaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties.
