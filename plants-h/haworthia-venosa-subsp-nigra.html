Haworthia venosa subsp. nigra

Haworthia venosa subsp.

nigra, commonly known as the Black Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with dark, almost black, markings.

The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its fleshy leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on tall stems during the growing season.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
