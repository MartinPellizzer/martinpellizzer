Hibiscus microsiphon

Hibiscus microsiphon, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds. Hibiscus microsiphon thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
