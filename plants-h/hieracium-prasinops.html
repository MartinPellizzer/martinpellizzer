Hieracium prasinops

Hieracium prasinops, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are usually hairy as well.

Hieracium prasinops is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
