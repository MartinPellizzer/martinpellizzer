Hieracium vasconicum subsp. dolosum

Hieracium vasconicum subsp.

dolosum, commonly known as the Pyrenean hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its elongated, slender leaves and its ability to thrive in alpine and subalpine habitats. It is often found in rocky soils and open meadows at high elevations.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
