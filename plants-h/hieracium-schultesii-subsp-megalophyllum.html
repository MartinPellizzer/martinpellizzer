Hieracium schultesii subsp. megalophyllum

Hieracium schultesii subsp.

megalophyllum, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has rough, hairy leaves and can grow up to 1 meter in height. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Due to its invasive tendencies, it is considered a weed in some regions.
