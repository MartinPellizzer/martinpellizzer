Hieracium orodoxum

Hieracium orodoxum, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium orodoxum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
