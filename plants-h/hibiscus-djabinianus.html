Hibiscus djabinianus

Hibiscus djabinianus, commonly known as the Djabini hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a distinct, aromatic scent. Hibiscus djabinianus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
