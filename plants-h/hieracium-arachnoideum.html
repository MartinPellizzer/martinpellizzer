Hieracium arachnoideum

Hieracium arachnoideum, commonly known as the spider's web hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant is known for its hairy, bristly stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, rugged appearance. It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its flowers, which bloom in the summer, are yellow and resemble dandelions, attracting various pollinators.
