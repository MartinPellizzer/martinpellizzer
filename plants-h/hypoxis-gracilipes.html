Hypoxis gracilipes

Hypoxis gracilipes, commonly known as the slender star grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to yellowish flowers in clusters. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions.

Hypoxis gracilipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
