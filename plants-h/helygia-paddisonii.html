Helygia paddisonii

Helygia paddisonii, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms that appear in late spring. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish.
