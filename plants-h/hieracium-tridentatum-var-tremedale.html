Hieracium tridentatum var. tremedale

Hieracium tridentatum var. tremedale, commonly known as the Tremedale hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and hairy, rough leaves. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has a creeping growth habit with a central stem that produces multiple flowering shoots.

The varietal name 'tremedale' refers to its native habitat in the Tremedale area of Cornwall.
