It is native to South Africa, where it typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features a compact, shrub-like form with glossy, dark green leaves that are often lobed or serrated. Its flowers are small, clustered, and usually have a distinctive yellow or orange hue.

Haxtonia foliosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
