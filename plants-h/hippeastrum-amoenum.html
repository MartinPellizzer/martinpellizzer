Hippeastrum amoenum

Hippeastrum amoenum, commonly known as the Brazilian belladonna lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Brazil.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a contrasting throat. The plant produces large, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green and strap-like in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hippeastrum amoenum is a popular ornamental plant due to its dramatic blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
