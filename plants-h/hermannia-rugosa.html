Hermannia rugosa

Hermannia rugosa, commonly known as the woolly hermannia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

Hermannia rugosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique texture.
