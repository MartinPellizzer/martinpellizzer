Hellenia scabra

Hellenia scabra, commonly known as the rough hellena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture, and the flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a dense cluster.

Hellenia scabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
