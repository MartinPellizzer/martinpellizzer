Helichrysum abietinum

Helichrysum abietinum, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is known for its aromatic foliage, which emits a subtle, resinous scent. Its flowers are often used in dried flower arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and vibrant color.

Helichrysum abietinum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rocky or sandy environments.
