Haworthia britteniana

Haworthia britteniana, commonly known as the Britten's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale markings, which help in photosynthesis and water storage. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
