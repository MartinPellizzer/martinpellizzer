Hancea micrantha

Hancea micrantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and has lance-shaped leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance.

Hancea micrantha is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
