Haworthia coarctata var. haworthii

Haworthia coarctata var. haworthii, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves with distinctive white to pale yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
