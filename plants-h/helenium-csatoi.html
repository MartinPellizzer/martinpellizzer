Helenium csatoi

Helenium csatoi, commonly known as the Chinese sneezeweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, hairy stems. The plant features showy, orange to red-orange ray florets surrounding a central disk of yellow tubular flowers. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

Helenium csatoi typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
