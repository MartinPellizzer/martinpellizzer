Hebenstretia macra

Hebenstretia macra, commonly known as the giant hebenstretia, is a striking herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and large, showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Hebenstretia macra thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long flowering period and ability to attract pollinators.
