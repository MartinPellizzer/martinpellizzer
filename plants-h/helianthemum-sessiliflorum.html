Helianthemum sessiliflorum

Helianthemum sessiliflorum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compact, succulent-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
