Habenaria rautaneniana

Habenaria rautaneniana, commonly known as the Rautanen's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its unique floral structure and adaptability to humid climates.
