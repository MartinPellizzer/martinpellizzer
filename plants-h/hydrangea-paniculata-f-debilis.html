Hydrangea paniculata f. debilis

Hydrangea paniculata f. debilis, commonly known as the smooth hydrangea, is a compact, deciduous shrub that features showy, cone-shaped flower clusters.

It produces large, white or pinkish-white inflorescences that can transition to red or purple as the season progresses. The plant is characterized by its relatively small, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens with limited sunlight.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
