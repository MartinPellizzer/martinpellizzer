Horsfieldia longiflora

Horsfieldia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, pendulous inflorescence bearing numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Horsfieldia longiflora is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting biodiversity in its native habitats.
