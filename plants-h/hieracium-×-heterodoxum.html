Hieracium × heterodoxum

Hieracium × heterodoxum, commonly known as the hybrid hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It produces numerous small, herbaceous leaves and has a fibrous root system.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations, making it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
