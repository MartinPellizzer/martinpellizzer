Hieracium rhamphodon

Hieracium rhamphodon, commonly known as the beak-horn hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium rhamphodon is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
