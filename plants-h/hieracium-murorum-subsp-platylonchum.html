Hieracium murorum subsp. platylonchum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

platylonchum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers.

The plant typically grows in grassy meadows and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are basal, rough, and hairy, with a distinctive yellowish-green color.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, producing small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
