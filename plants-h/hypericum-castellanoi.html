Hypericum castellanoi

Hypericum castellanoi, commonly known as the Castellano St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers with prominent stamens. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and valuable addition to Mediterranean flora collections.
