Hypericum socotranum

Hypericum socotranum, commonly known as Socotra's St. John's Wort, is a unique plant native to the Arabian island of Socotra.

It is a shrub characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and bright red seed pods. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Hypericum socotranum is notable for its ecological importance and has been the subject of botanical research due to its unique adaptations.
