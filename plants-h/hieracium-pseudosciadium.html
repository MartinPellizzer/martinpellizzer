Hieracium pseudosciadium

Hieracium pseudosciadium, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk and surrounding ray florets.

Hieracium pseudosciadium is often found in temperate climates and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
