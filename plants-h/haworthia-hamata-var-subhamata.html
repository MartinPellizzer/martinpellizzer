Haworthia hamata var. subhamata

Haworthia hamata var. subhamata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves with distinctive white to pale green markings that resemble zebra stripes. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant appearance. It is drought-tolerant and prefers warm, dry conditions with minimal fertilizer.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
