Hedyotis veronicifolia

Hedyotis veronicifolia, commonly known as the lady's mantle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has oval, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Hedyotis veronicifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
