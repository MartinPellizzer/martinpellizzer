Hieracium oligozum

Hieracium oligozum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a cluster of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are usually arranged in a compact head.

Hieracium oligozum is often found in Europe and parts of Asia, where it plays a role in pollinator habitats.
