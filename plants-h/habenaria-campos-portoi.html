Habenaria campos-portoi

Habenaria campos-portoi, commonly known as the Campos-Portoi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and showy, spurred flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer, producing pink to purple flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in grasslands and forest edges.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
