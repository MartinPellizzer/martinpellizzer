Habenaria thailandica

Habenaria thailandica, commonly known as the Thai butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, preferring rich, well-drained soil. Its inflorescence consists of several showy, pale pink to white flowers with a distinctive lip.

Habenaria thailandica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
