Haplopappus palmeri

Haplopappus palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's pappus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, yellowish to brownish flowers arranged in a flat, disc-like structure. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a hairy texture.

Haplopappus palmeri is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
