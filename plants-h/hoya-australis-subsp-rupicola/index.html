Hoya Australis Subsp. Rupicola

Hoya australis subsp. rupicola is a unique and striking plant native to coastal regions of Australia. It is known for its waxy, spider-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This subspecies thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Its ability to grow on rocky surfaces makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and terrariums. With its easy care and ornamental value, it is a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
