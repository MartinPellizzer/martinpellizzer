Helicostylis elegans

Helicostylis elegans, commonly known as the elegant helicostylis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family.

Helicostylis elegans is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
