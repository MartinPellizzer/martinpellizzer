Hoffmannia pusilla

Hoffmannia pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf hoffmannia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features compact, rosette-like foliage with narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color.

Hoffmannia pusilla is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
