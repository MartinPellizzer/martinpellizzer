Helichrysum hedbergianum

Helichrysum hedbergianum, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Helichrysum hedbergianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
