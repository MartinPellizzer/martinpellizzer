Hieracium fluididens

Hieracium fluididens, commonly known as the water buttercup, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist or wet habitats such as marshes and stream banks. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts.

Hieracium fluididens is known for its ability to thrive in waterlogged soils, making it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
