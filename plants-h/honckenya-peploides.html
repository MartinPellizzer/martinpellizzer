Honckenya peploides

Honckenya peploides, commonly known as the silky sandmat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
