Habenaria × canbyi

Habenaria × canbyi, commonly known as the Canby's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its inflorescence consists of multiple showy, spurred flowers.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
