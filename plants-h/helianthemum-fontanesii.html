Helianthemum fontanesii

Helianthemum fontanesii, commonly known as the rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, blooming in spring and summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helianthemum fontanesii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
