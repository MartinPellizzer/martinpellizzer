Habenaria notabilis

Habenaria notabilis, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white with a yellow lip. The plant produces a tall, slender stem that bears multiple clusters of flowers, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The botanical features of Habenaria notabilis make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
