Hieracium handelianum

Hieracium handelianum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, and its leaves are typically rough and hairy. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium handelianum is also known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
