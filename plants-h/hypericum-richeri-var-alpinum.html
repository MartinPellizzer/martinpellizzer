Hypericum richeri var. alpinum

Hypericum richeri var. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in well-drained conditions.

This variety is a hardy plant that thrives in full sun and is resistant to pests and diseases.
