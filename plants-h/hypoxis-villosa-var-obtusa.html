Hypoxis villosa var. obtusa

Hypoxis villosa var. obtusa, commonly known as the woolly yellowstar, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a short, upright stem and produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
