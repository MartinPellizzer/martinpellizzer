Hieracium gussembauerianum

Hieracium gussembauerianum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and Eastern Europe.

Hieracium gussembauerianum is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions, making it a common sight in alpine meadows.
