Habenaria pilosa

Habenaria pilosa, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive hairy texture on its stems and leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Habenaria pilosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
