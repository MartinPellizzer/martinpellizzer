Hypobathrum lagoense

Hypobathrum lagoense, commonly known as the Lake Lago sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in swampy or marshy areas near water bodies. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it plays a role in stabilizing soil in its wetland habitat.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
