Henckelia pectinata

Henckelia pectinata, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsules that contain numerous small, black seeds.

Henckelia pectinata is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
