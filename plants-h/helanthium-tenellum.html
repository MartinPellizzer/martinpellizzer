Helanthium tenellum

Helanthium tenellum, commonly known as the slender helanthium, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters and features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, often in early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Helanthium tenellum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
