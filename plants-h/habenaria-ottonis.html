Habenaria ottonis

Habenaria ottonis, commonly known as the Otto's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, with flowers that are often pink or purple in hue. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Habenaria ottonis is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high light exposure for optimal growth.
