Hieracium orphnodes

Hieracium orphnodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are erect and often branched.

Hieracium orphnodes is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in many regions.
