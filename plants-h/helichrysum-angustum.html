Helichrysum angustum

Helichrysum angustum, commonly known as narrow goldenrod, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features wiry, branching stems covered in small, narrow, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, golden-yellow flowers that resemble tiny sunflowers, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Helichrysum angustum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
