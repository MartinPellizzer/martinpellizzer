Habenaria micholitziana

Habenaria micholitziana, commonly known as the Micholitz's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in humid, tropical regions with well-drained soil.
