Hermione deflexicaulis

Hermione deflexicaulis, commonly known as the drooping stem plant, is a species characterized by its unique growth habit.

It features elongated, flexible stems that often curve downward, giving it a graceful appearance. The plant typically has small, lance-shaped leaves arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are usually clustered in inflorescences and may vary in color depending on the species.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
