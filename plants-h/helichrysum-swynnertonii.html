Helichrysum swynnertonii

Helichrysum swynnertonii, commonly known as the Swynnerton's golden daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its foliage is usually gray-green with hairy leaves, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Helichrysum swynnertonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought-resistant properties.
