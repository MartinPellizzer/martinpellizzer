Habenaria sumatrana var. major

Habenaria sumatrana var. major, commonly known as the Sumatran orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives on decaying organic matter. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient absorption.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
