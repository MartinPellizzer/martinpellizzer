Helicteres parviflora

Helicteres parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered helicteres, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, often with a distinctive, hairy appearance. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
