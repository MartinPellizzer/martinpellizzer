Helianthemum asperum var. latifolium

Helianthemum asperum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has rough, hairy leaves that are typically broad and ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid climates.
