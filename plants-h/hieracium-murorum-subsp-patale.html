Hieracium murorum subsp. patale

Hieracium murorum subsp.

patale, commonly known as the pale meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and roadsides.

The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. Its flowers are yellow, with a distinctive central disc and ray florets, forming a composite flower head.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in moist to moderately dry soils.
