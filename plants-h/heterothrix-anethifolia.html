Heterothrix anethifolia

Heterothrix anethifolia, commonly known as the anise-leaved heterothrix, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, aromatic leaves that resemble those of anise, giving it its common name. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, typical of the daisy family.

Heterothrix anethifolia is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.
