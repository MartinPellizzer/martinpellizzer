Haworthia unicolor var. venteri

Haworthia unicolor var. venteri, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are pale green with distinct dark markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on short, erect stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
