Homalomena clandestina

Homalomena clandestina, commonly known as the hidden arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
