Hippocrepis squamata var. toletana

Hippocrepis squamata var. toletana, commonly known as the Toletan rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant features small, scale-like leaves that are densely arranged along the stems. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate dry conditions.
