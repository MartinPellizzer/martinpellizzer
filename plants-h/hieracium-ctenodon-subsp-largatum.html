Hieracium ctenodon subsp. largatum

Hieracium ctenodon subsp.

largatum, commonly known as the large-haired hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The plant typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped with hairy surfaces, and the stems are usually erect and covered in fine hairs.

It is often found in open habitats where it can compete effectively with other vegetation.
