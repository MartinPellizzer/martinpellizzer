Hyparrhenia hirta var. longiaristata

Hyparrhenia hirta var. longiaristata, commonly known as the long-awned spiny grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in open woodlands, savannas, and disturbed soils, exhibiting a clump-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with hairy surfaces and elongated, spiny awns on its inflorescences. Its botanical structure is adapted to withstand drought and grazing, making it resilient in harsh environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in degraded areas.
