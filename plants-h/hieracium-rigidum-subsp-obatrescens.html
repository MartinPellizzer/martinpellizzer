Hieracium rigidum subsp. obatrescens

Hieracium rigidum subsp.

obatrescens, commonly known as the rigid hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm.

The plant features upright stems with hairy leaves that are lanceolate in shape. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a single head, surrounded by pale green involucral bracts.

It is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
