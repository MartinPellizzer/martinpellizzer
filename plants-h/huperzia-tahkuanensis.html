Huperzia tahkuanensis

Huperzia tahkuanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette-like pattern. The plant is native to China, particularly in the Yunnan Province, where it thrives in moist, shaded forest environments. Huperzia tahkuanensis is known for its ability to form dense mats, which helps in soil stabilization and moisture retention.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important component of its native habitat.
