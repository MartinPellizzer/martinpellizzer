Hieracium racemosum

Hieracium racemosum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, giving the plant its common name.

Hieracium racemosum is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
