Heterospathe dransfieldii

Heterospathe dransfieldii, commonly known as the Dransfield's arrowleaf, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves, which are often glossy and have a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and restricted distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
