Hieracium sylvaticum subsp. tenuiflorum

Hieracium sylvaticum subsp.

tenuiflorum, commonly known as slenderflower hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe. It typically grows in moist meadows and woodlands, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters.

The plant features basal rosettes of lanceolate leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single, terminal head. Its flowers are surrounded by phyllaries and are visited by various pollinators.

It is known for its ability to thrive in shaded environments while still maintaining its characteristic floral structure.
