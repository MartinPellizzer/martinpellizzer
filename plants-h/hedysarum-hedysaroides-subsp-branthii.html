Hedysarum hedysaroides subsp. branthii

Hedysarum hedysaroides subsp.

branthii, commonly known as the Branthii milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow to reddish flowers in racemes.

The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing deep root systems for water retention. It belongs to the legume family Fabaceae and is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
