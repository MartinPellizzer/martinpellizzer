Hieracium subcanofloccosum

Hieracium subcanofloccosum, commonly known as the pale floccose hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to pale green flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has basal leaves that are usually oblanceolate or elliptic in shape, with a hairy texture. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
