Hasseanthus variegatus var. elongatus

Hasseanthus variegatus var. elongatus, commonly known as the variegated elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and yellow patterns. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its foliage can reach up to 2 feet in diameter, making it a striking addition to indoor or outdoor gardens.

It is relatively low maintenance but benefits from regular watering and well-draining soil.
