Heuchera micrantha var. nuttallii

Heuchera micrantha var. nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's alumroot, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features basal leaves that are kidney-shaped with a velvety texture and deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers in early spring, which are borne on erect, leafless stems. It thrives in shady, moist woodland habitats and has a compact growth habit.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and early blooming period.
