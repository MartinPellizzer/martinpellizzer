Huernia striata

Huernia striata, commonly known as the horned African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often ribbed and covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
