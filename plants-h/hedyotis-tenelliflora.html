Hedyotis tenelliflora

Hedyotis tenelliflora, commonly known as the small-flowered bedstraw, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has opposite, slender leaves and produces small, white or greenish-white flowers in clusters. Its fruits are small, dry capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

Hedyotis tenelliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
