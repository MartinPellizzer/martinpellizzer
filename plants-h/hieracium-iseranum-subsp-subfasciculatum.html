Hieracium iseranum subsp. subfasciculatum

Hieracium iseranum subsp.

subfasciculatum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

The plant features basal leaves that are broadly lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the upper leaves are narrower and more upright. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a dense, clustered inflorescence, and the plant produces small, oval seeds.

It is characterized by its robust growth and is often found in disturbed habitats.
