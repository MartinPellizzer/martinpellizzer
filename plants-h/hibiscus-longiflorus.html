Hibiscus longiflorus

Hibiscus longiflorus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a prominent yellow throat. The plant has heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. Hibiscus longiflorus thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
