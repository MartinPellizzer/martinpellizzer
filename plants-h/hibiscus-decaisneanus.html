Hibiscus decaisneanus

Hibiscus decaisneanus, commonly known as the Congo hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Central and West Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and is often found in moist, tropical environments. Hibiscus decaisneanus is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
