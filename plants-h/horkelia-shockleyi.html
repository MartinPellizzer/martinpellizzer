Horkelia shockleyi

Horkelia shockleyi, commonly known as Shockley's horkelia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Horkelia shockleyi is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
