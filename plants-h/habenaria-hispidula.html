Habenaria hispidula

Habenaria hispidula, commonly known as the hairy Habenaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often in areas with rich organic soil. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white with a distinct yellowish lip, and they bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Habenaria hispidula is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
