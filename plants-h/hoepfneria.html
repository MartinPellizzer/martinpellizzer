Hoepfneria

Hoepfneria, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Hoepfneria is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees without deriving nutrients from them.
