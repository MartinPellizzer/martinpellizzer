Hibiscus ficulneus var. alboruber

Hibiscus ficulneus var. alboruber, commonly known as the red-and-white hibiscus, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that display a striking contrast of white and red petals. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green.

Hibiscus ficulneus var. alboruber thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
