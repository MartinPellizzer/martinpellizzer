Helianthemum apenninum var. croceum

Helianthemum apenninum var. croceum, commonly known as the Apennine sandwort, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy environments.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
