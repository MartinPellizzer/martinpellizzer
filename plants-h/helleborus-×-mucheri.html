Helleborus × mucheri

Helleborus × mucheri, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking, waxy, dark green leaves and showy, cup-shaped flowers that range from white to pink. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Helleborus × mucheri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
