Haworthia viscosa subsp. derekii-clarkii

Haworthia viscosa subsp.

derekii-clarkii, commonly known as the Derekii-Clarkii Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings, often arranged in a rosette formation.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

This subspecies is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
