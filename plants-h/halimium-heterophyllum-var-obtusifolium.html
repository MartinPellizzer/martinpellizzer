Halimium heterophyllum var. obtusifolium

Halimium heterophyllum var. obtusifolium, commonly known as the blunt-leaved rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair, which helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, rocky environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh climates.
