Hieracium breunium

Hieracium breunium, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are bright purple and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Hieracium breunium is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
