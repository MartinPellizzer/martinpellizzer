Hieracium amoeniceps

Hieracium amoeniceps, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disc and surrounding ray florets. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium amoeniceps is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
