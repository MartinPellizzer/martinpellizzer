Hedyotis longifolia var. tenuifolia

Hedyotis longifolia var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender leaf bluet, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streambanks. The plant features small, bright blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, giving it a delicate appearance.

It is often found in the understory of tropical and subtropical forests, where it contributes to the biodiversity of the ecosystem.
