Henckelia nana

Henckelia nana, commonly known as the dwarf henckelia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, with compact, rosette-like foliage. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It produces delicate, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

Henckelia nana is well adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.
