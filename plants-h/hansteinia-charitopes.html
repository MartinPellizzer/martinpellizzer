Hansteinia charitopes

Hansteinia charitopes, commonly known as the silver star fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly, forming dense mats on forest floors.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays due to its elegant appearance.
