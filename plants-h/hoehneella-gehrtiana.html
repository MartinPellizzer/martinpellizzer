Hoehneella gehrtiana

Hoehneella gehrtiana, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often growing in a rosette formation.

Hoehneella gehrtiana prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found in rainforests.
