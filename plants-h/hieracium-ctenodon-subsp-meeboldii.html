Hieracium ctenodon subsp. meeboldii

Hieracium ctenodon subsp.

meeboldii, commonly known as the meebold's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat, daisy-like inflorescence.

It is generally small in size, with a height ranging from 10 to 30 centimeters.
