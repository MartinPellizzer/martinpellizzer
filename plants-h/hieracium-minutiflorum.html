Hieracium minutiflorum

Hieracium minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with bright yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. The plant has basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Its small, daisy-like flowers are composed of ray florets surrounding a central disk, typical of the Asteraceae family.
