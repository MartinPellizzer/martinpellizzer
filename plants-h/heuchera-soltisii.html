Heuchera soltisii

Heuchera soltisii, commonly known as the Soltisii alumroot, is a perennial herb native to the United States.

It is characterized by its ornamental foliage, which features deep red, purple, and bronze hues. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers in the spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Heuchera soltisii is valued for its hardiness and ability to add color to shaded landscapes.
