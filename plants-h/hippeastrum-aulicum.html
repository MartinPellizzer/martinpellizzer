Hippeastrum aulicum

Hippeastrum aulicum, commonly known as the Brazilian firecracker lily, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant produces large, showy blooms that typically appear in late summer or early autumn. It has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a fan shape.

Hippeastrum aulicum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
