Huperzia ulixis

Huperzia ulixis, commonly known as the tropical clubmoss, is a species of fern-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, linear leaves and a distinctive, branching sporophyte structure. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Huperzia ulixis reproduces via spores rather than seeds, and its life cycle includes both a gametophytic and sporophytic phase.

It is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological role.
