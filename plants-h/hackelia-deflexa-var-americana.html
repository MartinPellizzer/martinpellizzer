Hackelia deflexa var. americana

Hackelia deflexa var. americana, commonly known as American bluecurls, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, blue to purple tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves with toothed margins. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
