Hieracium prenanthoides subsp. lissocorium

Hieracium prenanthoides subsp.

lissocorium, commonly known as the smoothleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellowish flower heads.

The leaves are typically smooth and lance-shaped, with a distinctive hairy underside. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
