Hoya persicinicoronaria

Hoya persicinicoronaria, commonly known as the rose-lace hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of white and pink. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It is a climbing epiphyte, often found growing on trees in humid, tropical environments.

Hoya persicinicoronaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
