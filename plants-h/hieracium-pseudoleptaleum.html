Hieracium pseudoleptaleum

Hieracium pseudoleptaleum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Hieracium pseudoleptaleum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
