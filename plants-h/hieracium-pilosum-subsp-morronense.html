Hieracium pilosum subsp. morronense

Hieracium pilosum subsp.

morronense, commonly known as the Morron hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features basal rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clustered inflorescences. It is characterized by its finely divided leaves and a lack of a central stem.

This subspecies is often found in Mediterranean climates and is part of the dandelion family (Asteraceae).
