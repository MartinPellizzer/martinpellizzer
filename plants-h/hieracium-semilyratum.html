Hieracium semilyratum

Hieracium semilyratum, commonly known as the Semily rat's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It features a basal rosette of leaves and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer, contributing to its role in local pollinator ecosystems.
