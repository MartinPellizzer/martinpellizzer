Hieracium chaboissaei

Hieracium chaboissaei, commonly known as the Corsican hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It features yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
