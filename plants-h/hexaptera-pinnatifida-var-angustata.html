Hexaptera pinnatifida var. angustata

Hexaptera pinnatifida var. angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hexaptera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, pinnately compound leaves with a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its foliage is used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
