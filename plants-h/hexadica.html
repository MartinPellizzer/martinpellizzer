Hexadica

Hexadica, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Hexadica is known for its rapid growth and is often considered an invasive species in certain areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable plant in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
