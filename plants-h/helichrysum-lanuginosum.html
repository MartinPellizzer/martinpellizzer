Helichrysum lanuginosum

Helichrysum lanuginosum, commonly known as the woolly helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of small, golden-yellow flowers. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
