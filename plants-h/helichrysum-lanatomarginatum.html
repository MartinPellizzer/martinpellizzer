Helichrysum lanatomarginatum

Helichrysum lanatomarginatum, commonly known as the yellow star flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow, star-shaped flowers that resemble miniature suns. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
