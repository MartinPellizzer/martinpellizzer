Habenaria thomana

Habenaria thomana, commonly known as the Thom's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy coating.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
