Hoarea nummulariifolia

Hoarea nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, round, silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, fuzzy hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
