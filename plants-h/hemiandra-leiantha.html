Hemiandra leiantha

Hemiandra leiantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hemiandra, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is typically found in sandy soils in coastal areas and is adapted to arid conditions.
