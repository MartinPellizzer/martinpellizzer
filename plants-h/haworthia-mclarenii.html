Haworthia mclarenii

Haworthia mclarenii, commonly known as the McGregor's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The leaves are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.
