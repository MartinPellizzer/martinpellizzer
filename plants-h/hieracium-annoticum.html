Hieracium annoticum

Hieracium annoticum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

Hieracium annoticum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in disturbed soils.
