Hieracium sychnodon

Hieracium sychnodon, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, rough to the touch, and have a distinctive serrated edge.
