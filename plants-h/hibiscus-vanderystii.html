Hibiscus vanderystii

Hibiscus vanderystii, commonly known as the Vanderyst's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and thrives in warm, humid climates. Hibiscus vanderystii is often found in coastal areas and can grow up to several meters in height.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and ecological role in its native habitat.
