Habenaria arnhemica

Habenaria arnhemica, commonly known as the Arnhem orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Northern Territory of Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant thrives in wet, shaded environments, often found in rainforests or near water sources. It is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and ecological significance in its habitat.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
