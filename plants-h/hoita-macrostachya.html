Hoita macrostachya

Hoita macrostachya, commonly known as the giant bamboo or giant lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by elongated seed pods. Hoita macrostachya is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for erosion control in certain landscapes.
