Habenaria stenodon

Habenaria stenodon, commonly known as the narrow-lobed marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps, where it thrives in moist, acidic soils. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette at the base of the stem.

The species is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in swampy or boggy environments.
