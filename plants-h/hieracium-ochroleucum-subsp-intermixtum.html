Hieracium ochroleucum subsp. intermixtum

Hieracium ochroleucum subsp.

intermixtum, commonly known as the mixed yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and basal rosettes of leaves.

The subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine pubescence.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
