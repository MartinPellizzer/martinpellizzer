Hieracium Racemosum Subsp. Crinitum

Hieracium racemosum subsp. crinitum is a striking member of the daisy family known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. This subspecies features bright yellow flowers surrounded by pale green ray florets, creating a visually appealing contrast. Native to parts of Europe and western Asia, it thrives in grassy areas and open woodlands. Its hairy stems and leaves give it a unique texture that aids in identification. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and underappreciated plant in the wild.
