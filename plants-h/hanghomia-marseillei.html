Hanghomia marseillei

Hanghomia marseillei, commonly known as the Marseille hanging plant, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and bright green coloration. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires support for its climbing growth habit.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
