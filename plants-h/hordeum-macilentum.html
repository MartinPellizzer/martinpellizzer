Hordeum macilentum

Hordeum macilentum, commonly known as slender barley, is a species of grass native to Eurasia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale yellow to brown flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Hordeum macilentum is not typically cultivated for grain but is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
