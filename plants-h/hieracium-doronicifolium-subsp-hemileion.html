Hieracium doronicifolium subsp. hemileion

Hieracium doronicifolium subsp.

hemileion, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies.

The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and often have a hairy texture, while the upper leaves are narrower and more upright. It produces yellow ray flowers surrounded by numerous disk flowers, which are typically tubular in shape.

This subspecies is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
