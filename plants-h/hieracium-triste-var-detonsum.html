Hieracium triste var. detonsum

Hieracium triste var. detonsum, commonly known as the weeping hawkweed, is a variant of the common hawkweed species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features upright stems that bear clusters of small, yellowish to reddish flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in wetlands.
