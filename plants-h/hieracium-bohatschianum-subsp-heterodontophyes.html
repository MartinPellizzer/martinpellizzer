Hieracium bohatschianum subsp. heterodontophyes

Hieracium bohatschianum subsp.

heterodontophyes, commonly known as the Bohatschian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has finely divided, grass-like leaves and produces numerous small florets arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.
