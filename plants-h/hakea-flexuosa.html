Hakea flexuosa

Hakea flexuosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hakea, is a shrub native to southwestern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of whitish wax. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Hakea flexuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
