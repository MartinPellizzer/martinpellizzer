Hedyotis sieberi

Hedyotis sieberi, commonly known as the small red root or red root grass, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features small, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stems.

Hedyotis sieberi is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
