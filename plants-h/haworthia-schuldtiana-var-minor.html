Haworthia schuldtiana var. minor

Haworthia schuldtiana var. minor, commonly known as the miniature Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
