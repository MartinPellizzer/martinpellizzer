Hoya rima

Hoya rima, commonly known as the "Rima Wreath," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
