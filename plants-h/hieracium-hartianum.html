Hieracium hartianum

Hieracium hartianum, commonly known as Hart's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded conditions. Its leaves are hairy and lance-shaped, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hart's hawkweed is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
