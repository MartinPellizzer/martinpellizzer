Hibiscus hornei

Hibiscus hornei, commonly known as the horned hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny calyx that gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Hibiscus hornei typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
