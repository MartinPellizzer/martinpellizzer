Hieracium brevifolium subsp. bergameum

Hieracium brevifolium subsp.

bergameum, commonly known as Bergamian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant features small, yellowish to reddish composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are usually basal, lanceolate, and covered with fine hairs.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in Mediterranean climates.
