Hyptis cuniloides

Hyptis cuniloides, commonly known as Brazilian hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its gray-green, hairy leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Hyptis cuniloides is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
