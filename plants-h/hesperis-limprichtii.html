Hesperis limprichtii

Hesperis limprichtii, commonly known as the Limpricht's honesty, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant produces small, white, four-petaled flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are elongated and slightly hairy, typical of the family.

Hesperis limprichtii is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.
