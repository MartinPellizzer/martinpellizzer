Hellerochloa livida

Hellerochloa livida, commonly known as the slender grass or silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its botanical features make it suitable for use in native gardens and as a low-maintenance landscape plant.
