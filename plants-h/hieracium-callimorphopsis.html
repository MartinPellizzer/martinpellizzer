Hieracium callimorphopsis

Hieracium callimorphopsis, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It is often found in open habitats and can spread readily through its extensive root system.
