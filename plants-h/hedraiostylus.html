Hedraiostylus

Hedraiostylus, commonly known as the star-stemmed orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including countries like India, China, and Indonesia. Hedraiostylus species are characterized by their striking, often fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. They have slender, erect stems and are often found growing in shaded, moist environments.
