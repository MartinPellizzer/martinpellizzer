Hypericum cordatum subsp. kleinii

Hypericum cordatum subsp.

kleinii, commonly known as the narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers with four petals, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy garden plant.

Its medicinal properties, similar to other Hypericum species, have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.
