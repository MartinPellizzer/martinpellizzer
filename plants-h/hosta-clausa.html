Hosta clausa

Hosta clausa, commonly known as the closed hosta, is a perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact foliage. The leaves are broad, ovate, and often have a waxy texture with a deep green color. It produces small, fragrant flowers in late summer, typically in shades of white or pale yellow.

This plant prefers partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.
