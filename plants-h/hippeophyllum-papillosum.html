Hippeophyllum papillosum

Hippeophyllum papillosum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm climate.

Hippeophyllum papillosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
