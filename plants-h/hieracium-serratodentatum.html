Hieracium serratodentatum

Hieracium serratodentatum, commonly known as the serrated-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in clusters, contributing to its role in pollination and ecological interactions.
