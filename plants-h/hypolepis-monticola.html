Hypolepis monticola

Hypolepis monticola, commonly known as the mountain fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Davalliaceae.

It is characterized by its fronds that are typically divided into numerous pinnae, giving it a delicate, lacy appearance. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of many fern species.
