Henrya brevifolia

Henrya brevifolia, commonly known as the dwarf henrya, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compact, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and evergreen foliage.
