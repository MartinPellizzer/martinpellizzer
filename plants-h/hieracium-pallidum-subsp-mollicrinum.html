Hieracium pallidum subsp. mollicrinum

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

mollicrinum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies mollicrinum is distinguished by its soft, hairy leaves and shorter flower stems compared to other varieties. It typically grows in grassy areas and is often found in disturbed soils.

This plant is also known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
