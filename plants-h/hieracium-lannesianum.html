Hieracium lannesianum

Hieracium lannesianum, commonly known as the Lannese hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium lannesianum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
