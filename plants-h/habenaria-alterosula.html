Habenaria alterosula

Habenaria alterosula, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear to lanceolate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale green flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
