Hesperantha rosea

Hesperantha rosea, commonly known as the pink star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep magenta. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with high sunlight exposure. Its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences, and the leaves are narrow and grass-like.

Hesperantha rosea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
