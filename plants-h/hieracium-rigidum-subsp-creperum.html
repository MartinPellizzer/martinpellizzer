Hieracium rigidum subsp. creperum

Hieracium rigidum subsp.

creperum, commonly known as the rigid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm.

The plant features upright stems with rough, hairy surfaces and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, consisting of numerous small, daisy-like blooms.

It is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
