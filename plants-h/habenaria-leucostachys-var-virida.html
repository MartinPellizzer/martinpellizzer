Habenaria leucostachys var. virida

Habenaria leucostachys var. virida, commonly known as the green-stemmed butterfly orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are typical of the Orchidaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
