Hieracium cymosoides

Hieracium cymosoides, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are basal and lance-shaped, with a rough texture, and the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is often found in temperate regions and is considered a weed in some agricultural settings.
