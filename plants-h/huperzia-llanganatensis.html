Huperzia llanganatensis

Huperzia llanganatensis, commonly known as the Llanganatensis spikemoss, is a species of moss plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, spiky appearance and is native to the Andean region of South America. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its sporophytes are elongated and often have a distinct, segmented structure.

Huperzia llanganatensis plays a role in maintaining soil stability and moisture retention in its natural habitat.
