Hieracium acroleucum var. daedalolepium

Hieracium acroleucum var. daedalolepium, commonly known as the Daedal yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with ray florets.

It is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
