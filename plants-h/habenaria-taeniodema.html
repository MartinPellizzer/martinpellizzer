Habenaria taeniodema

Habenaria taeniodema, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with distinctive purple spots. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soils.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral appearance.
