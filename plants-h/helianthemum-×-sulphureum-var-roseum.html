Helianthemum × sulphureum var. roseum

Helianthemum × sulphureum var. roseum, commonly known as the sulphur yellow rockrose, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal environments. Its foliage is typically covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh climates.
