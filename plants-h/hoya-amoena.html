Hoya amoena

Hoya amoena, commonly known as the sweetheart plant or waffle plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its waxy, fleshy leaves and clusters of fragrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink.
