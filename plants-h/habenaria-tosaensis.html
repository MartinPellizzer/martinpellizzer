Habenaria tosaensis

Habenaria tosaensis, commonly known as the Tosa Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single spike of flowers during the summer months.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
