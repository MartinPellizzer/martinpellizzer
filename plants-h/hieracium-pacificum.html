Hieracium pacificum

Hieracium pacificum, commonly known as the Pacific hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to western North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It produces yellow to orange flower heads that are surrounded by pale green to white ray florets.

The leaves are usually basal, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.
