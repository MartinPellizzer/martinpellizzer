Hopea glaucescens

Hopea glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-green hopea, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its smooth, bluish-green bark. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and has lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces woody fruits containing one to three seeds.

Hopea glaucescens is valued for its durable wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.
