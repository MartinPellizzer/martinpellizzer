Helipterum elegantissimum

Helipterum elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its showy, daisy-like blooms. The plant produces yellow flowers with white petals, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

The species is known for its long-lasting flowers, which remain intact even when dried.
