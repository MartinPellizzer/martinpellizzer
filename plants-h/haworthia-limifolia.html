Haworthia limifolia

Haworthia limifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a species of succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and fleshy. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact size and unique foliage make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
