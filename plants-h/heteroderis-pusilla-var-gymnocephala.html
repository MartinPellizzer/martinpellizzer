Heteroderis pusilla var. gymnocephala

Heteroderis pusilla var. gymnocephala, commonly known as the naked-headed pine root knot nematode, is a species of microscopic roundworm belonging to the family Tylenchidae.

It is primarily found in forested regions and is known for its parasitic lifestyle, infecting the roots of various coniferous trees. This species plays a significant role in the ecological dynamics of forest ecosystems by influencing plant health and soil biodiversity.
