Homalomena pontederifolia

Homalomena pontederifolia, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white or pale yellow, which bloom above the water surface. It thrives in shallow, still water and is often used in water gardens and ponds.

Its rapid growth and ability to filter water make it a popular choice for aquatic landscaping.
