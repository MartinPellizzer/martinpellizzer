Heleochloa schoenoides

Heleochloa schoenoides, commonly known as the silver hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. Heleochloa schoenoides plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates in its native ecosystems.
