Halimium spartioides

Halimium spartioides, commonly known as the rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dense, silver-gray foliage that is adapted to withstand hot, dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Halimium spartioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in gardens.
