Habenaria decumbens

Habenaria decumbens, commonly known as the spreading lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a horizontal rhizome with numerous small, linear leaves. Its flowers are arranged in a dense, spiraled inflorescence, often with pale pink or white petals.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
