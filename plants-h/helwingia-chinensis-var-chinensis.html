Helwingia chinensis var. chinensis

Helwingia chinensis var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese helwingia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Saxifragaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in spring, typically white or pink, followed by elliptical, winged fruits. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
