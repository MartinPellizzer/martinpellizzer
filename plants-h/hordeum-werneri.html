Hordeum werneri

Hordeum werneri, commonly known as Werneri's barley, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with two to three florets per spikelet. Hordeum werneri is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils.

It plays a role in local ecosystems and has been studied for its potential in sustainable agriculture.
