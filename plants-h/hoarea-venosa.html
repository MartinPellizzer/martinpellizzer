Hoarea venosa

Hoarea venosa, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a succulent perennial that features a rosette of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Hoarea venosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
