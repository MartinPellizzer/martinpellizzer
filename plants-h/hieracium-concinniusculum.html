Hieracium concinniusculum

Hieracium concinniusculum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers. The plant typically has finely divided, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in open, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species in certain regions.
