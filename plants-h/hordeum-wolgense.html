Hordeum wolgense

Hordeum wolgense, commonly known as the Wolgian barley, is a species of grass native to the temperate regions of Eurasia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green color.

Hordeum wolgense is primarily of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological adaptability and potential for agricultural use.
