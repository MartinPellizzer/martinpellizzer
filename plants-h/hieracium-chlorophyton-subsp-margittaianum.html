Hieracium chlorophyton subsp. margittaianum

Hieracium chlorophyton subsp.

margittaianum, commonly known as the Margittaian hawkweed, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and yellow flower heads composed of numerous small florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has a hairy stem and is often found in temperate regions of Europe.
