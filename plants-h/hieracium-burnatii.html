Hieracium burnatii

Hieracium burnatii, commonly known as the Burnatii hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers with ray florets that are often shorter than the central disk florets.

Hieracium burnatii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
