Habenaria × suzannae

Habenaria × suzannae, commonly known as the hybrid lady's tresses, is a rare orchid species resulting from the cross between Habenaria uniflora and Habenaria radiata.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and a floral arrangement of pale pink to white, fragrant flowers that resemble a tresses of hair. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. It is native to regions in Southeast Asia, including parts of India and Indonesia.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
