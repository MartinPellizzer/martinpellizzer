Habenaria gnoma

Habenaria gnoma, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Habenaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. The plant features a rhizomatous structure with fleshy roots and possesses a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate appearance.
