Hypoestes verticillaris var. forskaolii

Hypoestes verticillaris var. forskaolii, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble small flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
