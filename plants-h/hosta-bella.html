Hosta bella

Hosta bella, commonly known as the White Beauty hosta, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a white margin. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in late summer, usually in shades of blue or purple. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta bella is prized for its ornamental foliage and is a popular choice for shady garden landscapes.
