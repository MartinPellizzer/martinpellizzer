Hyptis unilateralis

Hyptis unilateralis, commonly known as the silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or purple, arranged in dense clusters.

Hyptis unilateralis is often used in traditional medicine and is also cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and aromatic properties.
