Homalomena borneensis

Homalomena borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean arrowhead plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Borneo and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Homalomena borneensis is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in its native habitat.
