Hedysarum ignescens

Hedysarum ignescens, commonly known as the burning bush, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is notable for its vibrant orange to red foliage, which becomes more intense in the autumn. Hedysarum ignescens thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
