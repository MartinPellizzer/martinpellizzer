Hieracium murorum subsp. stenocranum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

stenocranum, commonly known as the narrow-capped hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems, with basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

The flowers are yellow and arranged in a compact head, often with a narrow, elongated ligulate floret. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in grasslands and along stream banks.

This subspecies is notable for its distinct floral morphology and ecological adaptability.
