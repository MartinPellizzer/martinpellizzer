Helianthemum asperum var. angustifolium

Helianthemum asperum var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in dense clusters. The plant has aromatic, glandular leaves that are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
