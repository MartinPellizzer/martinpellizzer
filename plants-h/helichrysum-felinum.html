Helichrysum felinum

Helichrysum felinum, commonly known as the cat's paw plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers and distinctive, palmate leaves that resemble a cat's paw. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic, resinous scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum felinum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in traditional medicine.
