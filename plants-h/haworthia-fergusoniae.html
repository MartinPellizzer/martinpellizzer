Haworthia fergusoniae

Haworthia fergusoniae, commonly known as the Fergusson's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a waxy coating. The leaves are usually green with distinctive white or pale markings, and they often have a translucent window at the tip. This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

It is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
