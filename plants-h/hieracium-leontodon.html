Hieracium leontodon

Hieracium leontodon, commonly known as the lion's tooth hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers consist of yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular florets.

Hieracium leontodon is often found in temperate climates and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
