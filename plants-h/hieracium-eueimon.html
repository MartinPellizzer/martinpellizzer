Hieracium eueimon

Hieracium eueimon, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has a taproot system and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.

Hieracium eueimon is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
