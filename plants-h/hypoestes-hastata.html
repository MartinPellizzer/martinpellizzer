Hypoestes hastata

Hypoestes hastata, commonly known as the "Blue Mist Flower," is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features striking, cascading clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoestes hastata is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
