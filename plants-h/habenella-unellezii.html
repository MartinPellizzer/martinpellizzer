Habenella unellezii

Habenella unellezii, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are broad and oval, with a smooth texture and a deep green color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
