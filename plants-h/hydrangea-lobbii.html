Hydrangea lobbii

Hydrangea lobbii, commonly known as the mountain hydrangea, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth texture, while its stems are often covered in fine hairs.

Hydrangea lobbii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with specific soil conditions.
