Haworthia decipiens var. minor

Haworthia decipiens var. minor, commonly known as the miniature Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
