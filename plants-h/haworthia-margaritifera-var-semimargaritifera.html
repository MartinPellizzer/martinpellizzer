Haworthia margaritifera var. semimargaritifera

Haworthia margaritifera var. semimargaritifera, commonly known as the "Pearl Haworthia," is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically green with white or translucent tubercles. The leaves are often adorned with small, bead-like bumps that give the plant its characteristic appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique look and relatively easy care requirements.
