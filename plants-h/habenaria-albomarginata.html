Habenaria albomarginata

Habenaria albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically white with a yellowish margin, arranged in a dense spike. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This species is notable for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
