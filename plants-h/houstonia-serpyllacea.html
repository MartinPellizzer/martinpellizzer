Houstonia serpyllacea

Houstonia serpyllacea, commonly known as carpet bugleweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, dark blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy, spreading stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Houstonia serpyllacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to native pollinators.
