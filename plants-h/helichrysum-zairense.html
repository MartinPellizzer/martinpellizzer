Helichrysum zairense

Helichrysum zairense, commonly known as the Zaire everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, tightly packed flower heads that resemble tiny suns, giving it its common name. It is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and vibrant color.

Helichrysum zairense thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
