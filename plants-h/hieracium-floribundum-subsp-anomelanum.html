Hieracium floribundum subsp. anomelanum

Hieracium floribundum subsp.

anomelanum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The flowering stems are usually shorter than the leaves and bear clusters of small, bright yellow flowers.
