Hydrocotyle Hookeri Subsp. Chinensis

Hydrocotyle hookeri subsp. chinensis is a rare aquatic plant native to parts of eastern Asia. It thrives in shallow, still waters and is known for its delicate, floating leaves. This subspecies is often mistaken for other Hydrocotyle species due to its similar appearance. Its unique ecological role supports a variety of waterborne life. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile plant from habitat loss.
