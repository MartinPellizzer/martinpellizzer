Hieracium × rubrum

Hieracium × rubrum, commonly known as the red devil's bit, is a hybrid species within the dandelion family (Compositae).

It is characterized by its bright red flowers, which are typically borne on long, slender stems. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world, including North America. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium × rubrum is an annual or biennial plant, depending on the climate, and it produces a large number of seeds that are easily dispersed by the wind.
