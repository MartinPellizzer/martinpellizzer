Hieracium oreoxerum

Hieracium oreoxerum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces yellow flowers in late summer. It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

Hieracium oreoxerum is adapted to cold climates and is often found at high altitudes where competition from other plants is minimal.
