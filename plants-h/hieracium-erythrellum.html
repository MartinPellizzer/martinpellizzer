Hieracium erythrellum

Hieracium erythrellum, commonly known as the red hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically features hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain regions.
