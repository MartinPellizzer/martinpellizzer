Hedyotis stellarioides

Hedyotis stellarioides, commonly known as star-like or small starwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hedyotis stellarioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
