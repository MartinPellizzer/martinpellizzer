Hieracium botniense

Hieracium botniense, commonly known as the Botnian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Its flowers are yellow, with a central disc surrounded by ray florets, and it blooms from June to August.

Hieracium botniense is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is considered a minor weed in some agricultural settings.
