Hoya fitoensis

Hoya fitoensis, commonly known as the Fitoensis Woundwort, is a rare and intriguing species within the Hoya genus.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features thick, fleshy stems and produces clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and it is known for its ability to store water, making it somewhat drought-tolerant.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, Hoya fitoensis is often sought after by collectors and botanists alike.
