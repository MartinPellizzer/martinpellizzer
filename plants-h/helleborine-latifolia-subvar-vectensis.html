Helleborine latifolia subvar. vectensis

Helleborine latifolia subvar. vectensis, commonly known as the broadleaf helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and the Mediterranean.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, elliptical seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Helleborine latifolia subvar. vectensis is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
