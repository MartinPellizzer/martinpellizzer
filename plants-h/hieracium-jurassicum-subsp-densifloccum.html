Hieracium jurassicum subsp. densifloccum

Hieracium jurassicum subsp.

densifloccum, commonly known as the Jurassic hawkweed, is a member of the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dense, fluffy flower heads that are typically yellow to brown in color.

The plant has basal and upright stems with hairy leaves that are usually lance-shaped. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and localized species.
