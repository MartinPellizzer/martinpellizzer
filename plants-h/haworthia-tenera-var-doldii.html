Haworthia tenera var. doldii

Haworthia tenera var. doldii, commonly known as the Dold's Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
