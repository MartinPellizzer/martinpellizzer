Haworthia viscosa var. pseudotortuosa

Haworthia viscosa var. pseudotortuosa, commonly known as the tortuous Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow tubercles. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
