Haworthia subfasciata

Haworthia subfasciata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves with distinctive white or yellowish stripes, resembling a zebra's pattern. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall stems during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for low-maintenance indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
