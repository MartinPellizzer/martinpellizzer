Hieracium varianellum

Hieracium varianellum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow or orange composite flowers that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually upright and branched.

Hieracium varianellum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural and natural areas.
