Hermione tazetta

Hermione tazetta, commonly known as the paperwhite narcissus, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces clusters of fragrant, white, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often grown as a houseplant or in gardens for its ornamental value. It prefers cool, bright conditions and well-draining soil.

The flowers are also used in floral arrangements and have a mild, sweet fragrance.
