Hieracium corruscans

Hieracium corruscans, commonly known as the shining hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.

Hieracium corruscans is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
