Hieracium subramosum subsp. pimelophyllum

Hieracium subramosum subsp.

pimelophyllum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowering period occurs in late summer, producing yellow flowers that are often clustered in a single head.
