Hartia brevicalyx

Hartia brevicalyx, commonly known as the broad-leaved hartia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its foliage is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
