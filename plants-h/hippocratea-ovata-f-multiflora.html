Hippocratea ovata f. multiflora

Hippocratea ovata f. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora form of the ovate hippocratea, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the Hippocrateaceae family.
