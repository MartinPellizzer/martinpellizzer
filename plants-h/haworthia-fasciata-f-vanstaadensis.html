Haworthia fasciata f. vanstaadensis

Haworthia fasciata f. vanstaadensis, commonly known as the Van Stadens Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or pale yellow markings. The leaves are covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
