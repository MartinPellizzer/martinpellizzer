Helleborine ovalis

Helleborine ovalis, commonly known as the oval helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with three sepals and three petals, which are often adorned with purple or reddish markings. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, it thrives in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky areas.

Helleborine ovalis is known for its ability to grow in poor soils and is often found in association with other orchids in its habitat.
