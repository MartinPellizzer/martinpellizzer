Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. extenuatum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are linear to lance-shaped, with a hairy surface, and it produces yellow flowers in clusters.
