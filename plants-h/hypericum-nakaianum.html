Hypericum nakaianum

Hypericum nakaianum, commonly known as the Nakaian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum nakaianum is often found in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils.
