Hibiscus puniceus

Hibiscus puniceus, commonly known as the scarlet hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking red, showy flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Hibiscus puniceus thrives in full sunlight and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and vibrant blooms make it a favorite in tropical gardens.
