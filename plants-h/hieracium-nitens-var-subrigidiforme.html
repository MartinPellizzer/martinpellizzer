Hieracium nitens var. subrigidiforme

Hieracium nitens var. subrigidiforme, commonly known as the rigid-hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright stems and yellow composite flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in dense, stiff hairs, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense colonies, making it a common weed in disturbed areas.
