Hopea beccariana

Hopea beccariana, commonly known as the beccariana hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the tropical rainforests of countries like Malaysia, Indonesia, and Thailand. The tree can grow up to 40 meters tall and is characterized by its buttressed roots and thick, flaky bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, egg-shaped fruits containing a single seed.
