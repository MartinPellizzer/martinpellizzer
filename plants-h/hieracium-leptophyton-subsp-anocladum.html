Hieracium leptophyton subsp. anocladum

Hieracium leptophyton subsp.

anocladum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters.
