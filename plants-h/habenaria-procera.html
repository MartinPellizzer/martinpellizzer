Habenaria procera

Habenaria procera, commonly known as the tall orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a complex inflorescence with multiple flowers arranged along the stem. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for anchoring in moist, shaded environments.

Habenaria procera is often found in tropical rainforests and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
