Hieracium souliei

Hieracium souliei, commonly known as the Soulie's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous root system. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually basal, rough, and hairy, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat, composite head.

It is relatively small in stature, typically reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
