Hieracium parikkalense

Hieracium parikkalense, commonly known as the Parikkal hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like India and Nepal. The plant features small, yellowish to reddish composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are typically basal, with a rough, hairy texture and a serrated margin.

Hieracium parikkalense is often found in grassy slopes and rocky habitats, thriving in temperate to subtropical climates.
