Helichrysum declinatum

Helichrysum declinatum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rocky or well-drained soils. Its fluffy, long-lasting flowers are popular in floral arrangements.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
