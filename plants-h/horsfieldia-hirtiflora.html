Horsfieldia hirtiflora

Horsfieldia hirtiflora, commonly known as the hairy-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration that ranges from yellow to orange. Its leaves are typically elongated and covered in a fine, hairy texture, contributing to its common name.

Horsfieldia hirtiflora thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the canopies of tropical rainforests.
