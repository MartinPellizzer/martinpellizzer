Hosta kikutii var. scabrinervia

Hosta kikutii var. scabrinervia, commonly known as the scabrous-veined hosta, is a variety of the hosta plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a slight blue or grayish hue. The leaves have a distinctive scabrous texture, with prominent, rough veins that give it its common name. It produces small, fragrant flowers in late summer, typically in shades of white or pale yellow.

This variety is well-suited for shaded gardens and is prized for its unique foliage appearance.
