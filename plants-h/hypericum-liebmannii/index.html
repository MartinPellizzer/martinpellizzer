Hypericum Liebmannii

St. john's wort, scientifically known as Hypericum liebmannii, is a flowering plant native to southern africa. it is recognized for its bright yellow flowers and medicinal properties that have been used for centuries. this species thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. its name is derived from the traditional belief that it was used to ward off evil spirits during the summer solstice. the plant has gained attention in modern times for its potential therapeutic benefits.
