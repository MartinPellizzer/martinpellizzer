Heterothrix longifolia

Heterothrix longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved heterothrix, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are often elongated and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting addition to native plant collections.
