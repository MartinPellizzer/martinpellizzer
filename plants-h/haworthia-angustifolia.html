Haworthia angustifolia

Haworthia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, grass-like leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
