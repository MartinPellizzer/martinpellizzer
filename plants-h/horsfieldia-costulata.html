Horsfieldia costulata

Horsfieldia costulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often pendulous stems and narrow, strap-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are typically fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates.

Horsfieldia costulata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
