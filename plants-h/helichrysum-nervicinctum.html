Helichrysum nervicinctum

Helichrysum nervicinctum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, golden-yellow flowers that are often arranged in dense, spherical clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum nervicinctum is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
