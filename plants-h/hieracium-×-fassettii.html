Hieracium × fassettii

Hieracium × fassettii, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a hybrid species within the genus Hieracium, which belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands. It has a creeping growth habit with rhizomes that allow it to spread readily.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is often considered a weed in agricultural and natural areas.
