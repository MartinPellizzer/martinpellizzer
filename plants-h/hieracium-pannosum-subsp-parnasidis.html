Hieracium pannosum subsp. parnasidis

Hieracium pannosum subsp.

parnasidis, commonly known as the Parnassian ragwort, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae. It is native to the alpine regions of Europe, particularly in the Alps and Carpathian Mountains.

The plant is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. It thrives in rocky, nutrient-poor soils at high altitudes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many parts of its range.
