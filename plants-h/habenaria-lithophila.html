Habenaria lithophila

Habenaria lithophila, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on rock surfaces or in rocky soils, showcasing its lithophytic habit. The plant features a slender, upright stem with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a distinct three-lobed lip, typical of the Orchidaceae family.

It blooms during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental value to its natural habitat.
