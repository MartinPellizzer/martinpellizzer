Hedypnois zacintha

Hedypnois zacintha, commonly known as the Zanzibar lily, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in humid, coastal environments and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Hedypnois zacintha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
