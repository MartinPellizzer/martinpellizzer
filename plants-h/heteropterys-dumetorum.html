Heteropterys dumetorum

Heteropterys dumetorum, commonly known as the dwarf piper, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their aromatic properties. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 meter and features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough, hairy texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in terminal spikes, and it produces small, round berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.
