Haworthia violacea

Haworthia violacea, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or pinkish markings. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Haworthia violacea is prized for its compact size and unique appearance, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
