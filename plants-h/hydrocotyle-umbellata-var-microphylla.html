Hydrocotyle umbellata var. microphylla

Hydrocotyle umbellata var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved water pennywort, is a perennial aquatic plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds, streams, and ditches.
