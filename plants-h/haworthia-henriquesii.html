Haworthia henriquesii

Haworthia henriquesii, commonly known as the Henriques' Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
