Habenaria gymnadenia

Habenaria gymnadenia, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the summer months.

Botanically, it is characterized by its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
