Helichrysum diversifolium

Helichrysum diversifolium, commonly known as the everlasting dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and silver-green, fuzzy leaves. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
