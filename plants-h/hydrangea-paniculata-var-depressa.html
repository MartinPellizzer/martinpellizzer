Hydrangea paniculata var. depressa

Hydrangea paniculata var. depressa, commonly known as the smooth hydrangea, is a compact shrub native to eastern North America.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink or blue depending on soil pH. The plant produces showy, cone-shaped flower clusters that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are large, ovate, and often have a serrated edge.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
