Hieracium subviolascentiforme

Hieracium subviolascentiforme, commonly known as the slender yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy, upright stems. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadside areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

It is a perennial species that can form dense colonies in suitable environments.
