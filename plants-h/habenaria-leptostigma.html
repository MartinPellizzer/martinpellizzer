Habenaria leptostigma

Habenaria leptostigma, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
