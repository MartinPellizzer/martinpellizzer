Herpestis repens var. multistriata

Herpestis repens var. multistriata, commonly known as the striped morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves with a distinct veining pattern.
