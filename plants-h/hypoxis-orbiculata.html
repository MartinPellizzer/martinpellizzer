Hypoxis orbiculata

Hypoxis orbiculata, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with six petals. The plant has lance-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoxis orbiculata is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
