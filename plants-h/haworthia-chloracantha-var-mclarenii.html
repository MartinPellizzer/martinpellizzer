Haworthia chloracantha var. mclarenii

Haworthia chloracantha var. mclarenii, commonly known as the McLaren's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with prominent white markings. The leaves are covered in a fine, waxy coating that helps reduce water loss, making it well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
