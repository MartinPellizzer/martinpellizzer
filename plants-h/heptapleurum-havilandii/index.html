Heptapleurum Havilandii

Heptapleurum havilandii is a rare and striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China. Known for its dense, leathery foliage and unique, clustered growth habit, this plant is a favorite among collectors and botanists alike. It thrives in cool, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the horticultural world due to its limited availability and specific care requirements. This plant offers a glimpse into the diverse and often overlooked flora of its native habitat.
