Haworthia blackbeardiana

Haworthia blackbeardiana, commonly known as the Blackbeard Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves with a distinctive black or dark purple marginal band, giving it its common name. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

Haworthia blackbeardiana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
