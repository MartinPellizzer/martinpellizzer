Hydrangea hortensis var. lindleyana

Hydrangea hortensis var. lindleyana, commonly known as the lacecap hydrangea, is a cultivar prized for its unique floral display.

It features dense, showy clusters of sterile, lace-like flowers surrounded by fewer fertile blooms. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with dark green, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the growing season, adding ornamental value beyond its flowering period.
