Hieracium asbolocephalum

Hieracium asbolocephalum, commonly known as the asbolocephalous hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flower heads consist of numerous small florets arranged in a disk pattern.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
