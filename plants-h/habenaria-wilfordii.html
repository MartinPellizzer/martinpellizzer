Habenaria wilfordii

Habenaria wilfordii, commonly known as the Wilford's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and prefers well-drained, humus-rich soil. The plant produces tall, slender stems with several showy, white to pale pink flowers that have a distinctive lip. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom in late summer to early autumn.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
