Hordeum muticum subvar. tenuispicatum

Hordeum muticum subvar. tenuispicatum, commonly known as slender spike grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, ditches, and riverbanks. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often hairless or sparsely hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a single, narrow spike that bears small, spikelet-like flowers.

The leaves are linear and usually have a smooth or slightly rough surface, contributing to its adaptability in wetland habitats.
