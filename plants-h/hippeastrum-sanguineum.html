Hippeastrum sanguineum

Hippeastrum sanguineum, commonly known as the red hurricane lily, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces striking red flowers with a yellow center, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are broad at the base and taper towards the tip. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and relatively easy care requirements.
