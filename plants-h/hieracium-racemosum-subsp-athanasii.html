Hieracium racemosum subsp. athanasii

Hieracium racemosum subsp.

athanasii, commonly known as the Athanasius's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has alternate, linear to lanceolate leaves and produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Hieracium genus.
