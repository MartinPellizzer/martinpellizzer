Habenaria promensis

Habenaria promensis, commonly known as the Prominent Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a white or pale pink coloration with contrasting purple or brown markings. The plant grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of several flowers arranged in a spike, and the leaves are usually elongated and lance-shaped.

Habenaria promensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
