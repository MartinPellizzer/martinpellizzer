Hypericum nummularioides var. dshessaricum

Hypericum nummularioides var. dshessaricum, commonly known as the Dshessaric golden-stemmed St. John's wort, is a low-growing shrub native to parts of Central Asia.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically a bright green color, and its flowers are yellow with prominent orange-red glands. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
