Hieracium fuscoviridiceps

Hieracium fuscoviridiceps, commonly known as the dark green hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with a hairy surface, and the stems are upright with a slightly woolly texture.

Hieracium fuscoviridiceps is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
