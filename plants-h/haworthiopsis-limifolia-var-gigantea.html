Haworthiopsis limifolia var. gigantea

Haworthiopsis limifolia var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often lined with white or pale markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
