Helichrysum ciliatum var. sieberi

Helichrysum ciliatum var. sieberi, commonly known as the silky helichrysum, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellowish-orange flowers that are often arranged in dense, compact clusters. The plant has slender, hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves with a silvery-green appearance. It is well adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its drought tolerance and attractive foliage.
