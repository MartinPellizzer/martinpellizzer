Hieracium orteganum

Hieracium orteganum, commonly known as the Ortega hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single head at the end of each stem. The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has a short flowering period in late spring to early summer.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
