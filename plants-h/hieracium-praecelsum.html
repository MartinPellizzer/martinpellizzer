Hieracium praecelsum

Hieracium praecelsum, commonly known as the noble hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium praecelsum is also known for its ornamental value and has been introduced to other regions as a garden plant.
