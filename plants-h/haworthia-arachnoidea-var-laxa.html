Haworthia arachnoidea var. laxa

Haworthia arachnoidea var. laxa, commonly known as the spider haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green with white or translucent tubercles, giving it a unique, almost otherworldly appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
