Helenium autumnale var. montanum

Helenium autumnale var. montanum, commonly known as the mountain sneezeweed, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red ray flowers and yellow disk flowers, which bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky slopes. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Helenium autumnale var. montanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
