Habenaria thurberi

Habenaria thurberi, commonly known as Thurber's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and several leaves. The plant produces a single, slender stem that bears a few flowers with distinctive white to pale pink petals and a yellowish lip. Its flowers are typically 1 to 2 inches long and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded, moist woodlands.
