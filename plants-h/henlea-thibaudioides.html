Henlea thibaudioides

Henlea thibaudioides, commonly known as the African orchid.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces showy, fragrant orchid-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
