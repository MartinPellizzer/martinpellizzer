Haplopappus canus

Haplopappus canus, commonly known as the white goatweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. Haplopappus canus is often found in disturbed areas and is adapted to arid environments.
