Hyptis Durifolia

Blue hyptis, scientifically known as Hyptis durifolia, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas. It is characterized by its vibrant blue and silver foliage, which gives it a unique and eye-catching appearance. This herbaceous plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to semi-shaded environments. Its aromatic leaves and small white flowers make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes. Due to its adaptability and visual appeal, blue hyptis has gained recognition among gardeners and herbalists alike.
