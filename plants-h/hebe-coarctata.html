Hebe coarctata

Hebe coarctata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright, bushy growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, typically blooming in spring and summer. Hebe coarctata is well-suited for rock gardens and coastal gardens due to its tolerance of well-drained soils and full sun.
