Hieracium dolosum

Hieracium dolosum, commonly known as the deceptive hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a cluster.

Hieracium dolosum is known for its ability to spread rapidly, often becoming a weed in agricultural areas.
