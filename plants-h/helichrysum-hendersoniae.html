Helichrysum hendersoniae

Helichrysum hendersoniae, commonly known as the Henderson's everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain open for several days. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spherical seed heads that persist after flowering. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is cultivated in gardens and used in floral arrangements.
