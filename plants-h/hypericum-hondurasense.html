Hypericum hondurasense

Hypericum hondurasense, commonly known as the Honduras goldenseal, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hypericum hondurasense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
