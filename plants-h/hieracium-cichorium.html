Hieracium cichorium

Hieracium cichorium, commonly known as the Italian spear thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features a robust, upright stem covered with coarse, spiny hairs. The leaves are deeply lobed, with a bluish-green hue and a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow and form a dense, spherical inflorescence at the top of the plant.

This species is often found in dry, rocky soils and is known for its medicinal properties.
