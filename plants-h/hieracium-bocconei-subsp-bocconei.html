Hieracium bocconei subsp. bocconei

Hieracium bocconei subsp.

bocconei, commonly known as the Boccone's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Italy. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
