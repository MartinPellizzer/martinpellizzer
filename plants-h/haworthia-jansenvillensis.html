Haworthia jansenvillensis

Haworthia jansenvillensis, commonly known as the Jansenville Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features short, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
