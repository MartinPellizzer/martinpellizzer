Heterotropa Variegata

Variegated heterotropa, scientifically known as Heterotropa variegata, is a rare and fascinating member of the Rubiaceae family. This plant is native to tropical regions and is characterized by its unique leaf patterns and vibrant colors. Despite its beauty, it remains understudied due to its limited distribution and elusive nature. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in dense undergrowth. Its ecological importance and ornamental value make it a subject of growing interest among botanists and plant enthusiasts.
