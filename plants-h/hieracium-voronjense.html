Hieracium voronjense

Hieracium voronjense, commonly known as Voronovsky's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in alpine and subalpine habitats. The plant has rough, hairy leaves and produces small, inconspicuous florets arranged in a composite head.

It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
