Hardenbergia bimaculata

Hardenbergia bimaculata, commonly known as the red billygoat weed, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive red and green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural areas.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important species in soil regeneration.
