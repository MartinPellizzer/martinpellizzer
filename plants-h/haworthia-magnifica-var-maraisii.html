Haworthia magnifica var. maraisii

Haworthia magnifica var. maraisii, commonly known as the Maraisii Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, pale green to blue-gray leaves with white or pale green markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring or summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
