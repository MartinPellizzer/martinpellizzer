Hieracium glabratum subsp. trichoneurum

Hieracium glabratum subsp.

trichoneurum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. It has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
