Holostemma ada-kodien

Holostemma ada-kodien, commonly known as the white sand orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to poor soil conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
