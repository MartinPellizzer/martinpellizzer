Helleborus Hunfalvyanus Var. Cupreus

Helleborus hunfalvyanus var. cupreus is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Hungary. Its unique foliage features a deep green with a subtle coppery hue that adds to its visual appeal. The plant produces elegant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale pink during late winter and early spring. Despite its beauty, it is a challenging plant to cultivate due to its specific environmental needs. This variety is highly valued by botanists and gardeners alike for its ornamental qualities and ecological significance.
