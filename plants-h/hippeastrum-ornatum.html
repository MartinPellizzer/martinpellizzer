Hippeastrum ornatum

Hippeastrum ornatum, commonly known as the Amazonian belladonna lily, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, with a distinctive bell-shaped corona. The plant produces bulbous rhizomes that store nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often reaching several feet in length, and it prefers well-draining soil.

Hippeastrum ornatum is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
