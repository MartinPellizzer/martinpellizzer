Hernandia rostrata

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite, and often elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in cyathia, a feature typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain seeds, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
