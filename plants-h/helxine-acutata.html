Helxine acutata

Helxine acutata, commonly known as the creeping Jenny, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Helxine acutata is often used in ground covers due to its spreading habit and ornamental value.
