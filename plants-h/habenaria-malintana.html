Habenaria malintana

Habenaria malintana, commonly known as the Malintan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
