Hieracium corteyanum

Hieracium corteyanum, commonly known as the Corteyan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from yellow to orange.

Hieracium corteyanum is often found in meadows and rocky slopes at high elevations.
