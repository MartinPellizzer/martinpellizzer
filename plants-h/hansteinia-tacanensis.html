Hansteinia tacanensis

Hansteinia tacanensis, commonly known as the Tacana grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. This plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Hansteinia tacanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.
