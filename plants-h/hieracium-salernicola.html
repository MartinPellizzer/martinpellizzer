Hieracium salernicola

Hieracium salernicola, commonly known as the Salerno hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe, particularly Italy.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium salernicola is often found in areas with mild climates and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
