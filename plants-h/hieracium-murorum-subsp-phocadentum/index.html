Hieracium Murorum Subsp. Phocadentum

Hieracium murorum subsp. phocadentum is a lesser-known member of the dandelion family found in specific regions of Europe. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf shape and flower structure, making it a subject of interest for botanists. It thrives in rocky and coastal areas, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable plant. Studying this species offers insights into plant resilience and ecological diversity.
