Habenaria holotricha

Habenaria holotricha, commonly known as the hairy-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and erect, often branched stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow with dark markings. Its leaves are usually linear to ovate and arranged in a basal rosette, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

The species is notable for its intricate floral structure and is often found in forest understories.
