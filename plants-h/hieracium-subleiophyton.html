Hieracium subleiophyton

Hieracium subleiophyton, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
