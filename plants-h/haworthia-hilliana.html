Haworthia hilliana

Haworthia hilliana, commonly known as the Zulu candle, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, gray-green leaves with white markings and a distinctive, candle-like appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
