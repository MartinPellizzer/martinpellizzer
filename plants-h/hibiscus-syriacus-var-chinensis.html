Hibiscus syriacus var. chinensis

Hibiscus syriacus var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese rose or summer hibiscus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a rough texture. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.

Hibiscus syriacus var. chinensis prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.
