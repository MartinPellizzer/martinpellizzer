Heliotropium mexicanum

Heliotropium mexicanum, commonly known as Mexican heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with a distinctive violet hue. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and gardens.
