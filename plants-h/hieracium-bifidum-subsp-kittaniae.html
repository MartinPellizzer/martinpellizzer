Hieracium bifidum subsp. kittaniae

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

kittaniae, commonly known as the Kittan's hawkweed, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as alpine meadows and rocky slopes. It has distinctive yellow flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
