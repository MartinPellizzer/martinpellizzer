Humata huahinensis

Humata huahinensis, commonly known as the Thai starflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Humata huahinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
