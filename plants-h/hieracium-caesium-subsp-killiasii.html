Hieracium caesium subsp. killiasii

Hieracium caesium subsp.

killiasii, commonly known as the Killias' hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowering period occurs from late spring to early summer.
