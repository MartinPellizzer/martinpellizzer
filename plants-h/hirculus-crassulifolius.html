Hirculus crassulifolius

Hirculus crassulifolius, commonly known as the thick-leaved hirculus, is a succulent plant native to arid regions.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the spring. Its growth habit is typically compact, forming a rosette structure.

Hirculus crassulifolius is valued in horticulture for its resilience and ornamental appearance.
