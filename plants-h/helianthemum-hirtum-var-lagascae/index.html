Helianthemum Hirtum Var. Lagascae

Helianthemum hirtum var. lagascae is a small, evergreen shrub native to the rocky hills of southern Spain. It is known for its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This variety is a hardy plant that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Despite its modest size, it holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitat.
