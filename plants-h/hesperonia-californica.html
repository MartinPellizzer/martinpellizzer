Hesperonia californica

Hesperonia californica, commonly known as the California hesperon, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, seed-containing capsules as its fruit.

Hesperonia californica is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats.
