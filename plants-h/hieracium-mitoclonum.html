Hieracium mitoclonum

Hieracium mitoclonum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has basal leaves that are often hairy and alternate along the stem. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium mitoclonum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative means, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
