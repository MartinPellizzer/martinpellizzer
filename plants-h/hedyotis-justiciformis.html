Hedyotis justiciformis

Hedyotis justiciformis, commonly known as the blue-flowered geranium, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems and produces small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often found in shaded or semi-shaded habitats, such as forest floors or rocky areas.

Hedyotis justiciformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
