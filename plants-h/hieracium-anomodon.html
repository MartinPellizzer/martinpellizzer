Hieracium anomodon

Hieracium anomodon, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a composite head, which is typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are usually alternate and have a rough, hairy texture.

Hieracium anomodon is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to colonize various environments.
