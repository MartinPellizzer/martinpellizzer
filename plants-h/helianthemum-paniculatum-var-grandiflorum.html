Helianthemum paniculatum var. grandiflorum

Helianthemum paniculatum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered rockrose, is a shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, evergreen leaves that are typically gray-green in color and covered in a fine layer of whitish hair. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers in spring, which are borne on long, branched stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
