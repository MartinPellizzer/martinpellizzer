Hypericum gladiatum

Hypericum gladiatum, commonly known as the swordleaf St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species known for their medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
