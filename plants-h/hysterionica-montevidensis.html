Hysterionica montevidensis

Hysterionica montevidensis, commonly known as the Montevidense hysteric, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Uruguay and parts of Argentina.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
