Hieracium stuposum

Hieracium stuposum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring moist to moderately dry conditions. It has basal leaves that are often hairy and can form a rosette, while its stems are usually erect.

Hieracium stuposum is known for its ability to thrive in various habitats and is often found in open, sunny areas.
