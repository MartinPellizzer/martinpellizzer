Haworthiopsis mcmurtryi

Haworthiopsis mcmurtryi, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

Due to its low maintenance and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
