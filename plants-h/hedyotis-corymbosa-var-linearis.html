Hedyotis corymbosa var. linearis

Hedyotis corymbosa var. linearis, commonly known as the slender leaf blushing flower, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. The leaves are narrow and linear, giving the plant its varietal name, and they are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
