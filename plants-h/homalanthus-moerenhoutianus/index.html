Homalanthus Moerenhoutianus

Homalanthus moerenhoutianus is a unique plant native to the wetlands of southern Africa. It belongs to the spurge family, known for its milky sap and often toxic properties. This species is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and palmate leaves that resemble a hand. It thrives in moist environments and is often found near water sources. Despite its beauty, it requires careful handling due to its potentially harmful characteristics.
