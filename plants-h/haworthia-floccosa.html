Haworthia floccosa

Haworthia floccosa, commonly known as the woolly window plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in white, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Haworthia floccosa is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and unique visual appeal.
