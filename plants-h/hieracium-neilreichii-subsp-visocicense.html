Hieracium neilreichii subsp. visocicense

Hieracium neilreichii subsp.

visocicense, commonly known as the Visocinian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is noted for its limited distribution and is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
