Haworthia triebneriana var. lanceolata

Haworthia triebneriana var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or translucent markings, giving it a striking, almost glass-like appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
