Habenaria grandis

Habenaria grandis, commonly known as the large-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces a single spike of flowers that can reach up to 30 cm in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
