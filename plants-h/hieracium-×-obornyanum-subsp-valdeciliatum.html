Hieracium × obornyanum subsp. valdeciliatum

Hieracium × obornyanum subsp.

valdeciliatum, commonly known as the Valdeciliatum hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and hairy stems, typical of many hawkweed species.

The subspecies is native to parts of Central and Eastern Europe, where it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. It exhibits a compact growth habit with basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

Botanically, it is notable for its ability to hybridize with other Hieracium species, contributing to its genetic diversity.
