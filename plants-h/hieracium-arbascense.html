Hieracium arbascense

Hieracium arbascense, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium arbascense is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
