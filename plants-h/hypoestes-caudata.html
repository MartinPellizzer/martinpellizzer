Hypoestes caudata

Hypoestes caudata, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features striking, bicolor leaves with a white or pink margin and a green center, making it visually distinctive. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes caudata is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.
