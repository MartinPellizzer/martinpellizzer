Helichrysum raynalianum

Helichrysum raynalianum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that retain their color even after drying. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Helichrysum raynalianum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
