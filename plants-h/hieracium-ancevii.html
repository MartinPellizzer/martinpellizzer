Hieracium ancevii

Hieracium ancevii, commonly known as the Corsican hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Corsica and Sardinia.

Hieracium ancevii is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
