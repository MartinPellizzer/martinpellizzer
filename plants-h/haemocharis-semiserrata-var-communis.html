Haemocharis semiserrata var. communis

Haemocharis semiserrata var. communis, commonly known as the pink shower plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to South America.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive, velvety texture. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers emit a sweet fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
