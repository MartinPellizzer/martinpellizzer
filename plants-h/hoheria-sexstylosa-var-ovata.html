Hoheria sexstylosa var. ovata

Hoheria sexstylosa var. ovata, commonly known as the broad-leaved hoheria, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers in spring, which attract nectar-feeding birds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
