Hieracium pellitum subsp. pellitum

Hieracium pellitum subsp.

pellitum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and is typically found in disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds and is adapted to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is considered an invasive species in some regions.
