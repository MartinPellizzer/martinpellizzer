Helichrysum lucidum

Helichrysum lucidum, commonly known as the golden marguerite or everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers and its ability to retain its color when dried, making it popular in floral arrangements. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branched.

Helichrysum lucidum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
