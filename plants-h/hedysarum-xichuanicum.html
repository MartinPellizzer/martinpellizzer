Hedysarum xichuanicum

Hedysarum xichuanicum, commonly known as the Xichuan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

Its seeds are often dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread in suitable environments.
