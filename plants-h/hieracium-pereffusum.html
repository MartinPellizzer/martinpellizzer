Hieracium pereffusum

Hieracium pereffusum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium pereffusum is often considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and ability to outcompete native vegetation.
