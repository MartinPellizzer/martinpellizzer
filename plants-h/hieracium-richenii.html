Hieracium richenii

Hieracium richenii, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
