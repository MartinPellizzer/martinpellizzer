Homalium foetidum

Homalium foetidum, commonly known as the stinkwood or foul-smelling holly, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves and small, clustered flowers. The tree is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its durable wood.
