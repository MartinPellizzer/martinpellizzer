Hardenbergia macrophylla

Hardenbergia macrophylla, commonly known as the bluebonnet, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a spreading habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and birds.
