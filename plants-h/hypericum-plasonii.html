Hypericum plasonii

Hypericum plasonii, commonly known as the yellow St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly rough texture.

Hypericum plasonii is often found in open woodland areas and along riverbanks.
