Humularia welwitschii var. welwitschii

Humularia welwitschii var. welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's humularia, is a unique plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and distinctive, fleshy, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are small, ovate, and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unusual appearance.
