Hieracium megalomastix

Hieracium megalomastix, commonly known as the giant hawksbeard, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its large, yellow composite flowers and robust, hairy stems. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this species thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows and disturbed soils. The plant has rough, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is known for its showy blooms and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
