Hieracium macrum

Hieracium macrum, commonly known as the rough hawksbeard, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features yellow composite flowers with ray florets and a central disk, surrounded by rough, hairy leaves. It can reach heights of up to 1 meter and has a robust root system.

Hieracium macrum is often found in sunny habitats and is adapted to a variety of soil conditions.
