Haworthia reinwardtii var. triebneri

Haworthia reinwardtii var. triebneri, commonly known as the triangular Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that form a rosette, often with a bluish-green hue and prominent white to yellowish markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
