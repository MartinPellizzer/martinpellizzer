Hermannia litoralis

Hermannia litoralis, commonly known as the coastal Hermannia, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and succulent leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to saline and well-drained environments.

Hermannia litoralis is often found in dune ecosystems and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological resilience.
