Helicia formosana var. oblanceolata

Helicia formosana var. oblanceolata, commonly known as the Formosan holly.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae, native to Taiwan. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with lanceolate to oblanceolate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, followed by orange-red berries.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
