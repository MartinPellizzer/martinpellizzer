Hieracium leptophytomorphum

Hieracium leptophytomorphum, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring well-drained conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare species in some regions.
