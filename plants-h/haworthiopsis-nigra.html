Haworthiopsis nigra

Haworthiopsis nigra, commonly known as the Black Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a bluish tinge, often marked by white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a potted plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
