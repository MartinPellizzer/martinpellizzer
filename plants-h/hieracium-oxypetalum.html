Hieracium oxypetalum

Hieracium oxypetalum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe, particularly in regions such as France, Italy, and the Iberian Peninsula. Hieracium oxypetalum is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in woodland edges.
