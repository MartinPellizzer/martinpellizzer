Hiptage candicans var. lanata

Hiptage candicans var. lanata, commonly known as the woolly hiptage, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, woolly leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical climates.
