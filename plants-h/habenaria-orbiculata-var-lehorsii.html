Habenaria orbiculata var. lehorsii

Habenaria orbiculata var. lehorsii, commonly known as the Lehorsii orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
