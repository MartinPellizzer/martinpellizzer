Heliconia weberbaueri

Heliconia weberbaueri, commonly known as the purple heliconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its striking, boat-shaped inflorescence. The plant features long, narrow leaves that form a rosette pattern, and its flowers are typically orange or reddish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
