Hieracium nigrescens subsp. glabridens

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

glabridens, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are usually hairy but can be smooth in this subspecies, and its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

It is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
