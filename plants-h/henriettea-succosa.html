Henriettea succosa

Henriettea succosa, commonly known as the succulent heath, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Province of South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves and produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade. Henriettea succosa is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it adapts well to harsh environmental conditions.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
