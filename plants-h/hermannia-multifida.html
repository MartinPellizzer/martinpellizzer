Hermannia multifida

Hermannia multifida, commonly known as the spurred gentian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that are often adorned with spurred petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its leaves are palmately lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hermannia multifida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
