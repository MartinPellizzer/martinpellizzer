Helichrysum grayi

Helichrysum grayi, commonly known as the gray helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often arranged in clusters, giving it a fluffy appearance. Its foliage is typically gray-green, with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Helichrysum grayi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
