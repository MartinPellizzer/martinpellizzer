Hieracium chaetophyton

Hieracium chaetophyton, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are arranged in a head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
