Hakea Sulcata Var. Gilbertii

Hakea sulcata var. gilbertii is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of Australia. It is known for its unique, spiny foliage and vibrant, urn-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter. This variety is a hybrid form of Hakea sulcata, often found in coastal and sandy soils. Its hardy nature makes it well-suited for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles.
