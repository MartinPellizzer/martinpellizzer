Hieracium pannucium

Hieracium pannucium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are broadly lance-shaped with a rough texture, and its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Hieracium pannucium is known for its bright yellow color and is often used in ornamental gardening.
