Hieracium pannosum subsp. reductum

Hieracium pannosum subsp.

reductum, commonly known as the reduced hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
