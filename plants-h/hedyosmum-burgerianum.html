Hedyosmum burgerianum

Hedyosmum burgerianum, commonly known as the Brazilian star anise, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of star anise. Hedyosmum burgerianum thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
