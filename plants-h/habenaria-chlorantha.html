Habenaria chlorantha

Habenaria chlorantha, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in a spike. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and relatively rare orchid species within its native range.
