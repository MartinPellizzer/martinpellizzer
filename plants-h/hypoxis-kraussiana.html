Hypoxis kraussiana

Hypoxis kraussiana, commonly known as the golden star or star lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces bright yellow flowers arranged in a star-like pattern. The plant has lance-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoxis kraussiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
