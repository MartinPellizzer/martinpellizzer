Hydrangea longialata

Hydrangea longialata, commonly known as the panicled hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that consist of sterile flowers surrounding fertile ones, forming a cone-like shape. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters and features dark green, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers can change color depending on soil pH, ranging from blue to pink.

Hydrangea longialata thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.
