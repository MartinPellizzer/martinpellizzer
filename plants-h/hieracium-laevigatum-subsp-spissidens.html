Hieracium laevigatum subsp. spissidens

Hieracium laevigatum subsp.

spissidens, commonly known as the denseleaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, hairy leaves and bright yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has a rosette growth form with basal leaves that are often covered in fine, stiff hairs.
