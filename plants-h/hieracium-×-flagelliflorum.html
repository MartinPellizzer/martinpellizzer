Hieracium × flagelliflorum

Hieracium × flagelliflorum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations.
