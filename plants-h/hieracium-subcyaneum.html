Hieracium subcyaneum

Hieracium subcyaneum, commonly known as the blue hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring moist to moderately dry conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range from pale blue to violet.

Hieracium subcyaneum is often found in open habitats and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
