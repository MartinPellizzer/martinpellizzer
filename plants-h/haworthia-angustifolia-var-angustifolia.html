Haworthia angustifolia var. angustifolia

Haworthia angustifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
