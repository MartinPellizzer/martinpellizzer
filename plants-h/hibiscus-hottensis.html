Hibiscus hottensis

Hibiscus hottensis, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.

Hibiscus hottensis thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
