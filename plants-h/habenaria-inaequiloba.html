Habenaria inaequiloba

Habenaria inaequiloba, commonly known as the unequal-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive lip that is unequal in size, giving it its specific epithet. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
