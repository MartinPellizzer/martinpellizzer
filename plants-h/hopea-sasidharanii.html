Hopea sasidharanii

It is native to the Western Ghats of India, where it thrives in moist, tropical climates. The tree is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and elongated, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. This species is valued for its hard, durable wood and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
