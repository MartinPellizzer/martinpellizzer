Hypoestes floribunda var. suaveolens

Hypoestes floribunda var. suaveolens, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that resemble snowflakes. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture and a pale green color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
