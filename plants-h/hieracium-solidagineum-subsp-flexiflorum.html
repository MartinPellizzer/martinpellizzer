Hieracium solidagineum subsp. flexiflorum

Hieracium solidagineum subsp.

flexiflorum, commonly known as the flexible hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain environments.
