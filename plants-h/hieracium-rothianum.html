Hieracium rothianum

Hieracium rothianum, commonly known as the Rothian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features upright stems with basal leaves that are lanceolate and hairy. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the daisy family.

Hieracium rothianum is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its aggressive growth habit.
