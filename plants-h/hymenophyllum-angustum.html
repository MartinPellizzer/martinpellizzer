Hymenophyllum angustum

Hymenophyllum angustum, commonly known as the narrow fern, is a small, epiphytic fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or rocks. Its spores are produced in small, club-shaped sporangia located on the underside of its leaves.

The botanical features of Hymenophyllum angustum make it a unique and specialized member of the fern family.
