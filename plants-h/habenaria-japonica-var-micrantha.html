Habenaria japonica var. micrantha

Habenaria japonica var. micrantha, commonly known as the Japanese butterfly orchid, is a species native to East Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale green or white, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or reddish veins. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
