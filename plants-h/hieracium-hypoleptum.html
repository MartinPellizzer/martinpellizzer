Hieracium hypoleptum

Hieracium hypoleptum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies. Its leaves are usually alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium hypoleptum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in many regions.
