Hieracium catenatum

Hieracium catenatum, commonly known as the chain hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its flowering stems bear clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Hieracium catenatum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can spread aggressively through rhizomes.
