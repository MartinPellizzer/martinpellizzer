Hieracium comatum

Hieracium comatum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, surrounded by pale green bracts. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters.

Hieracium comatum is often found in open habitats and can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies.
