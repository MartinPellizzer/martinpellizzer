Habenaria galeandra var. annamica

Habenaria galeandra var. annamica, commonly known as the Annamite orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically features linear to lanceolate leaves and produces inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
