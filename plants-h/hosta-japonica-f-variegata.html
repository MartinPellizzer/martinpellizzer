Hosta japonica f. variegata

Hosta japonica f. variegata, commonly known as the Japanese hosta variegata.

This cultivar is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, featuring a mix of green and white or yellow patterns on its broad, heart-shaped leaves. It is a compact, shade-tolerant plant that thrives in moist, well-drained soil. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in late summer, though they are less showy than the foliage.

It is a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
