Halimium podanisium

Halimium podanisium, commonly known as the pink sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy pink or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.

Halimium podanisium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
