Hieracium villanuense

Hieracium villanuense, commonly known as the Villanueva hawkweed, is a flowering plant species native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It features basal leaves that are often hairy and alternate along the stem.

Its flowers are yellow and bloom from late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
