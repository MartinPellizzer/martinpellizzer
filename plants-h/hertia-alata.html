Hertia alata

Hertia alata, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Hertia alata is known for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
