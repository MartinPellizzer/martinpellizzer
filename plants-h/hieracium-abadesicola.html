Hieracium abadesicola

Hieracium abadesicola, commonly known as the Abadesicola hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its flowers are small and yellow, often appearing in clusters, and it produces achenes as its reproductive structures.
