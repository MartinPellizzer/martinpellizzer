Habenaria carolinensis

Habenaria carolinensis, commonly known as the Carolina orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and forest floors. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy orchid-like flower that is usually pink or white. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
