Haworthia viscosa f. beanii

Haworthia viscosa f. beanii, commonly known as the Bean's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
