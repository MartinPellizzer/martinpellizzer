Hieracium algidiforme

Hieracium algidiforme, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. Hieracium algidiforme is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions, making it a common sight in mountainous landscapes.
