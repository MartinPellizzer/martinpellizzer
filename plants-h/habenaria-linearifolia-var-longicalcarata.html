Habenaria linearifolia var. longicalcarata

Habenaria linearifolia var. longicalcarata, commonly known as the long-spurred marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, preferring moist, acidic soils. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single or few flowering stems that bear clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its flowers have distinctive elongated spurs, which are a key identifying feature.

The orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in swampy or boggy environments.
