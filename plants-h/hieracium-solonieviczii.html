Hieracium solonieviczii

Hieracium solonieviczii, commonly known as the Solonievicz's hawkweed, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flower heads, which consist of numerous small florets. The plant typically has basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate or linear in shape, with rough or hairy surfaces. It is native to specific regions in Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
