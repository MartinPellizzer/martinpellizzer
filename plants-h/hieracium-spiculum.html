Hieracium spiculum

Hieracium spiculum, commonly known as the spiked hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal rosettes of narrow, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a spiky inflorescence. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils.

Hieracium spiculum is an annual or biennial plant that thrives in temperate climates.
