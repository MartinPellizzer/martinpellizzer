Hieracium pyrenaicum subsp. burserianum

Hieracium pyrenaicum subsp.

burserianum, commonly known as the Pyrenean hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range. It is a perennial herb with basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers arranged in a head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in alpine and subalpine meadows, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
