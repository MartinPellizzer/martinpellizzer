Hoya macrophylla

Hoya macrophylla, commonly known as the wax plant or giant wax plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink in color. Hoya macrophylla is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favored choice among plant enthusiasts.
