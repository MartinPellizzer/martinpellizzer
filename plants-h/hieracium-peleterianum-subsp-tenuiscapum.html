Hieracium peleterianum subsp. tenuiscapum

Hieracium peleterianum subsp.

tenuiscapum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.

The plant features upright stems that are often hairy, with basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and are produced in late summer.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
