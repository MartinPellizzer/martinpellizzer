Hieracium trebevicianum

Hieracium trebevicianum, commonly known as the Trebevician hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has basal leaves that are usually lanceolate and covered in fine hairs.

Its flowers bloom in the summer, producing yellowish to orange disc flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets.
