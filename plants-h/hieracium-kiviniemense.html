Hieracium kiviniemense

Hieracium kiviniemense, commonly known as the Kivinieme hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It has narrow, serrated leaves and produces small, herbaceous shoots.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare species in some regions.
