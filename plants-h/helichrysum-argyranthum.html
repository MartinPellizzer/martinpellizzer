Helichrysum argyranthum

Helichrysum argyranthum, commonly known as the silver leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that retain their color and form even when dried. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
