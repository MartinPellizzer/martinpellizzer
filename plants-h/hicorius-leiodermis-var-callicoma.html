Hicorius leiodermis var. callicoma

Hicorius leiodermis var. callicoma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hibiscus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with opposite, lanceolate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The flowers are showy, with five petals that range in color from white to pink, and they bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
