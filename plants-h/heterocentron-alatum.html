Heterocentron alatum

Heterocentron alatum, commonly known as the winged waterwheel, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally arranged leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshy areas near water bodies.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
