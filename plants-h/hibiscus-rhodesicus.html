Hibiscus rhodesicus

Hibiscus rhodesicus, commonly known as the Rhodes hibiscus, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in coastal and sandy soils. Hibiscus rhodesicus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
