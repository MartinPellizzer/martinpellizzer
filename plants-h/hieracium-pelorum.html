Hieracium pelorum

Hieracium pelorum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring well-drained soils. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are hairy and have a distinctive lobed shape.

Hieracium pelorum is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.
