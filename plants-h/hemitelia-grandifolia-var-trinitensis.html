Hemitelia grandifolia var. trinitensis

Hemitelia grandifolia var. trinitensis, commonly known as the Trinidad nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Trinidad and Tobago.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, violet-blue flowers that bloom at night. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
