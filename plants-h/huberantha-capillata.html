Huberantha capillata

Huberantha capillata, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with delicate, strap-like leaves that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticulture.
