Helichrysum lecomtei

Helichrysum lecomtei, commonly known as the Lecomte's everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow to orange-colored composite flowers that resemble daisies. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Helichrysum lecomtei is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
