Hieracium praosomatum

Hieracium praosomatum, commonly known as the early yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flower heads composed of numerous small florets, which bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its stems are upright and branching.

Hieracium praosomatum is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.
