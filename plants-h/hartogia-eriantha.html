Hartogia eriantha

Hartogia eriantha, commonly known as the Cape sugarbush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers. The plant has elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. It thrives in sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates.

Hartogia eriantha is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine.
