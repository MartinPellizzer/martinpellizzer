Hypericum notiale

Hypericum notiale, commonly known as the yellowSt John's wort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, which are typical features of the Hypericaceae family. The plant typically grows in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or rocky habitats. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum notiale is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
