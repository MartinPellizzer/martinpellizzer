Hydrangea chinensis var. chinensis

Hydrangea chinensis var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink, blue, or purple depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and produces opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

The flowers bloom in summer and are often used in ornamental gardening for their vibrant colors and long-lasting display.
