Hugelia brauntonii

Hugelia brauntonii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of purple, pink, and white. Hugelia brauntonii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
