Haworthia cooperi

Haworthia cooperi, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often marked with distinctive white or yellow stripes. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for collectors and garden enthusiasts.
