Habenaria unguiculata

Habenaria unguiculata, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated flowers that resemble horns, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its orchidaceous structure includes a slender stem and lance-shaped leaves.

The flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a contrasting purple or brown lip, and they bloom during the warmer months of the year.
