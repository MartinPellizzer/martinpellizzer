Helleborus purpurascens f. nanus

Helleborus purpurascens f. nanus, commonly known as the dwarf Christmas rose, is a compact, evergreen perennial with dark green, leathery foliage.

It produces striking, nodding flowers in shades of pink and white, typically appearing in late winter to early spring. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom during the holiday season.

Due to its slow growth and small stature, it is ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.
