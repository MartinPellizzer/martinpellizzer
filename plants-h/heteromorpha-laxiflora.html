Heteromorpha laxiflora

Heteromorpha laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered heteromorpha, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers. Its flowers are typically pink to purple in color and are borne on slender stems.

Heteromorpha laxiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
