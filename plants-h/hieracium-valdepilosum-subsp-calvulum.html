Hieracium valdepilosum subsp. calvulum

Hieracium valdepilosum subsp.

calvulum, commonly known as the smooth hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright stems and yellow composite flowers.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, short hairs. This subspecies is distinguished by its reduced leaf pubescence compared to other members of the species.

It thrives in meadows and grasslands, often forming dense colonies.
