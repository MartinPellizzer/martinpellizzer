Hieracium syncomistum

Hieracium syncomistum, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture, and its yellow flowers bloom in summer.

Hieracium syncomistum is often found in clusters and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
