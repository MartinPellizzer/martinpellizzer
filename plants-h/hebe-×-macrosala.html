Hebe × macrosala

Hebe × macrosala, commonly known as the silver fern, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple or blue during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value and hardy nature make it a favored plant in horticulture.
