Hemizygia thorncroftii

Hemizygia thorncroftii, commonly known as the Thorncroft's hemizygia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the cooler months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
