Hygrophila undulata var. obovatifolia

Hygrophila undulata var. obovatifolia, commonly known as the wavy hygrophila, is a species of flowering plant in the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its elongated, wavy leaves that are typically obovate in shape. It thrives in moist environments, often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.
