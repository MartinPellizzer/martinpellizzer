Hieracium svenonianum

Hieracium svenonianum, commonly known as the Svenonian hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous yellow to orange flower heads that bloom from late spring to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
