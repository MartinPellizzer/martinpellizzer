Hieracium trichocolorhizum

Hieracium trichocolorhizum, commonly known as the trichocolorhizum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive coloration, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Due to its ornamental qualities, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
