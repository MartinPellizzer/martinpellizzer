Hieracium jodolepis

Hieracium jodolepis, commonly known as the Jodolepis hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
