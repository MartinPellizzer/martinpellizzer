Huberodendron ingens

Huberodendron ingens, commonly known as the giant umbrella tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like canopy formed by its dense, spreading branches. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, waxy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, typically appearing in shades of yellow or green.

Huberodendron ingens is well adapted to the humid, tropical climate of its native habitat, thriving in well-drained soils and partial shade.
