Hieracium stenoglossodes

Hieracium stenoglossodes, commonly known as the narrow-lipped hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering head composed of numerous yellow ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges across Europe. It has a fibrous root system and produces seeds through wind dispersal.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the hawkweed genus, with variations in leaf shape and flower structure.
