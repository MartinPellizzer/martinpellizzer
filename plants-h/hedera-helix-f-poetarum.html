Hedera helix f. poetarum

Hedera helix f. poetarum, commonly known as the poet's ivy, is a cultivar of the common ivy plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive, waxy, and often variegated leaves that display shades of white, yellow, and green. This form thrives in shady to partially sunny conditions and is well-suited for climbing or trailing in gardens and indoor spaces. Its foliage remains evergreen, providing year-round visual interest.

The plant is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for both ornamental and decorative purposes.
