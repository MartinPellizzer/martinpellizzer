Haworthia pilosa var. lachnosa

Haworthia pilosa var. lachnosa, commonly known as the woolly Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine, woolly hairs, giving it its common name. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
