Hermione × bifrons var. compressa

Hermione × bifrons var. compressa, commonly known as the compact honeysuckle, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a unique combination of traits from its parent species.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit, making it well-suited for smaller garden spaces. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is often dark green and glossy, providing an attractive visual element throughout the growing season.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil conditions.
