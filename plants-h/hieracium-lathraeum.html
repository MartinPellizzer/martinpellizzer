Hieracium lathraeum

Hieracium lathraeum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, with a stem that is usually unbranched.

Hieracium lathraeum is an annual or biennial plant, often found in temperate regions across Europe and parts of Asia.
