Hieracium nubiceps

Hieracium nubiceps, commonly known as the cloud-capped hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadside areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and often reddish in color.

Hieracium nubiceps is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
