Habenaria marginata var. flavescens

Habenaria marginata var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-stained orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with a few leaves and produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green to pale orange flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, and its flowers have a distinctive, elongated lip that is often marked with contrasting coloration. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
