Hieracium blyttianoides

Hieracium blyttianoides, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Hieracium blyttianoides is a perennial herb that thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
