Hieracium epimedium subsp. hartzianum

Hieracium epimedium subsp.

hartzianum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, preferring partial shade. It produces achenes as its seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
