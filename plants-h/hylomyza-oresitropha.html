Hylomyza oresitropha

Hylomyza oresitropha, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
