Haloragis citriodora

Haloragis citriodora, commonly known as lemon scented everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for dry gardens.

Haloragis citriodora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
