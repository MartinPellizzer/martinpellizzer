Heterogonium cyatheifolium

Heterogonium cyatheifolium, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its large, tree-like structure.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. The plant features a tall, unbranched trunk covered in a fibrous, spongy material called the rachis. Its fronds are typically large, feather-like, and can grow up to several meters in length.

Heterogonium cyatheifolium plays an important ecological role in tropical rainforests, providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
