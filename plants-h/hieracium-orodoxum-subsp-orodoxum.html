Hieracium orodoxum subsp. orodoxum

Hieracium orodoxum subsp.

orodoxum, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.
