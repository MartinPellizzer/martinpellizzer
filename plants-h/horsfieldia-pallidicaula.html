Horsfieldia pallidicaula

Horsfieldia pallidicaula, commonly known as the pale-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid tropical environments. The plant features slender, fleshy roots and produces delicate, pale yellow or greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are usually ovate and covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Horsfieldia pallidicaula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
