Helleborine yunnanensis

Helleborine yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan helleborine, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with yellow petals and a purple throat, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions.

Helleborine yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
