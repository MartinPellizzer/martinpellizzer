Hohenbergia conquistensis

Hohenbergia conquistensis, commonly known as the Brazilian pineapple plant, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features rosettes of broad, waxy, and often brightly colored leaves that can range from green to red or purple. The plant produces inflorescences with showy, spadix-like flowers that are typically white or pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
