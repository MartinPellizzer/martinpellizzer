Heliotropium eremogenum

Heliotropium eremogenum, commonly known as the desert heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its small, showy violet or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy, succulent leaves that help reduce water loss in dry environments. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in desert scrub and coastal dunes.

Heliotropium eremogenum is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low precipitation, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
