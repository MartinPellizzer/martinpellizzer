Hypochaeris stenocephala var. taraxacoides

Hypochaeris stenocephala var. taraxacoides, commonly known as the narrow-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow composite flowers with ray florets. It is characterized by its small, elongated flower heads and is often found in temperate regions.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in some areas.
