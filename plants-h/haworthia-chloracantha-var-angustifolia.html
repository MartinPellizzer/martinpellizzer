Haworthia chloracantha var. angustifolia

Haworthia chloracantha var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a waxy coating, often marked by white or pale green transverse stripes. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
