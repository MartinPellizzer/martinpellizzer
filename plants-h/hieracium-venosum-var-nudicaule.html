Hieracium venosum var. nudicaule

Hieracium venosum var. nudicaule, commonly known as the naked-stem hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. Its leaves are generally lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a cluster.

It is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
