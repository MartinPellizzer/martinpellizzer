Hieracium × rubellum

Hieracium × rubellum, commonly known as the red hawkweed, is a hybrid plant species within the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its tall, reddish stems and clusters of small, yellowish to reddish-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open areas, such as roadsides and meadows. Its leaves are usually hairy and alternate along the stem, with a distinctive lobed shape.

Hieracium × rubellum is often found in temperate regions of Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.
