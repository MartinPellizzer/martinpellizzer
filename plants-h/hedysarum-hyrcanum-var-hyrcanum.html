Hedysarum hyrcanum var. hyrcanum

Hedysarum hyrcanum var. hyrcanum, commonly known as the Hyrcanian milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It features compound leaves with trifoliate or pinnate structures and produces small, yellow to orange flowers in raceme clusters. The plant is characterized by its stout, hairy stems and is typically found in dry, rocky habitats. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Hedysarum hyrcanum var. hyrcanum is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in soil enrichment.
