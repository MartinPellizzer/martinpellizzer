Humboltia perpusilla

Humboltia perpusilla, commonly known as the dwarf humboldtia, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves and produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is well adapted to coastal environments and thrives in sandy, well-drained soils.

Humboltia perpusilla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
