Haworthia tessellata

Haworthia tessellata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or grayish markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant grows in a rosette formation and is well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall, slender stalks during the growing season.

Haworthia tessellata is popular as a low-maintenance indoor plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
