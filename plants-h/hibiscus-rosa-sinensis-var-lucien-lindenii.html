Hibiscus rosa-sinensis var. lucien-lindenii

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis var. lucien-lindenii, commonly known as the Lucien Lindenii hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely on the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and plenty of sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
