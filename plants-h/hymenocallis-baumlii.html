Hymenocallis baumlii

Hymenocallis baumlii, commonly known as the Baumli's spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with prominent yellow stamens. The plant features strap-shaped, evergreen leaves that are broad and lanceolate. It produces bulbous rhizomes that store nutrients, supporting its growth in humid, shaded environments.

Hymenocallis baumlii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
