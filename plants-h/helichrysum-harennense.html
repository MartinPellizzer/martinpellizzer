Helichrysum harennense

Helichrysum harennense, commonly known as the Cape goldring, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, papery bracts that resemble small rings. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant appearance.

Helichrysum harennense is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
