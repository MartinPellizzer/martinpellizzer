Hypoestes corymbosa

Hypoestes corymbosa, commonly known as the "blue mist shrub," is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of blue, pink, and white. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. This plant is well-suited for container gardening and thrives in bright, indirect light.

It is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
