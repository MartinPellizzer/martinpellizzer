Hieracium Bupleuroides Subsp. Bupleuroidiforme

Hieracium bupleuroides subsp. bupleuroidiforme is a lesser-known member of the daisy family found primarily in northern regions. This plant is distinguished by its slender stems and delicate, blue-green leaves. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream sides. Though not widely cultivated, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Its subtle beauty makes it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
