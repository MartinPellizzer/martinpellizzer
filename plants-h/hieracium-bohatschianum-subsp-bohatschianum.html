Hieracium bohatschianum subsp. bohatschianum

Hieracium bohatschianum subsp.

bohatschianum, commonly known as the Bohatschian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has a fibrous root system and produces numerous short, flowering stems.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
