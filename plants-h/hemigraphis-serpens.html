Hemigraphis serpens

Hemigraphis serpens, commonly known as shining iron plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink, clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.

Hemigraphis serpens is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
