Hovea densivellosa

Hovea densivellosa, commonly known as the silky hovea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is known for its dense, silky pubescence, which gives it its common name. It typically grows in sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.
