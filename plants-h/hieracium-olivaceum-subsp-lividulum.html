Hieracium olivaceum subsp. lividulum

Hieracium olivaceum subsp.

lividulum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow to orange-yellow flowers and hairy, usually glandular leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate regions.

Due to its hairy foliage and showy blooms, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
