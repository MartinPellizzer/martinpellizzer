Helianthemum obtusatum

Helianthemum obtusatum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features dense, woolly foliage covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals and numerous stamens, blooming in spring.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
