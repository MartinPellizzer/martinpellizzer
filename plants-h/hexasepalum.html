Hexasepalum, commonly known as the six-sepal plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive floral structure, featuring six sepals that are often brightly colored and showy. Hexasepalum species are native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Asia and Africa.
