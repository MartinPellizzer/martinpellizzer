Hieracium patens subsp. eynense

Hieracium patens subsp.

eynense, commonly known as the Eynense hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are lanceolate and hairy, with a height ranging from 10 to 30 centimeters.

Its flowers are yellow, with a central disk and surrounding ray florets, and it blooms from June to August.
