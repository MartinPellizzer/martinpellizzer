Harungana robynsii

Harungana robynsii, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of white, fluffy flowers that appear in winter, giving it its common name. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. It produces small, round, yellowish fruits that are edible for some animals but not typically for humans.

Harungana robynsii is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
