Habenaria horsfieldiana

Habenaria horsfieldiana, commonly known as the Horsfield's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation efforts.
