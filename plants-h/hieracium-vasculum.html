Hieracium vasculum

Hieracium vasculum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium vasculum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it both a common weed and a resilient plant in various habitats.
