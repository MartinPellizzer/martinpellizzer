Hieracium tenuivillosum

Hieracium tenuivillosum, commonly known as slender hairgrass, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy areas and has fine, hairy leaves that give it its common name. Its small yellow flowers bloom in the summer, attracting various pollinators.

It is a perennial species that often forms dense colonies in its native habitat.
