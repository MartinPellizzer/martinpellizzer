Hieracium bupleuroides var. schenkii

Hieracium bupleuroides var. schenkii, commonly known as the Schenk's yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, slender flowering stems that bear numerous yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets.

This variant is distinguished by its compact growth habit and specific floral morphology compared to other members of the Hieracium genus.
