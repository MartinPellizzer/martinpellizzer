Helichrysum basedowii

Helichrysum basedowii, commonly known as the Cape goldfluff, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, yellow to orange flower heads that resemble fluffy balls. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically clustered together in a spherical shape. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The foliage is usually silver or gray-green, adding visual interest even when the plant is not in bloom.
