Hieracium comperianum

Hieracium comperianum, commonly known as the Comper's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a single, tall flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale yellow ray florets.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
