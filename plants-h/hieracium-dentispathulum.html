Hieracium dentispathulum

Hieracium dentispathulum, commonly known as the serrated hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring moist and well-drained conditions. It is often found in open habitats and can spread readily through its rhizomes.
