Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. canibifidum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

canibifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of yellow flowers.

The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and horticultural practices.
