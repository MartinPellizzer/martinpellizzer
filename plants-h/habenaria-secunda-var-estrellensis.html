Habenaria secunda var. estrellensis

Habenaria secunda var. estrellensis, commonly known as the Estrellan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as pine flatwoods and wetlands. The plant features a slender rhizome with several erect, linear leaves. Its flowers are small, white to pale green, and arranged in a loose raceme.

It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its native range.
