Hypocistis americana

Hypocistis americana, commonly known as the American swallowtail butterfly, is a species of butterfly native to North America.

Its larvae feed on plants in the carrot family, such as parsley and dill. The adult butterfly has distinctive black and yellow wing patterns with a long, slender body. It plays an important role in pollination and is often found in gardens and meadows.
