Haworthiopsis nigra var. diversifolia

Haworthiopsis nigra var. diversifolia, commonly known as the Variegated Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically a mix of green, white, and pink, creating a striking variegation pattern. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
