Haworthia reinwardtii var. diminuta

Haworthia reinwardtii var. diminuta, commonly known as the miniature Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, giving it a striking, almost gem-like appearance. The plant grows slowly and forms dense rosettes, often reaching only a few inches in height. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
