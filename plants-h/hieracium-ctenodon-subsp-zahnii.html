Hieracium ctenodon subsp. zahnii

Hieracium ctenodon subsp.

zahnii, commonly known as the Zahn's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by wind.
