Haworthia picta var. comptoniana

Haworthia picta var. comptoniana, commonly known as the Compton's Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves with distinctive white or pale yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
