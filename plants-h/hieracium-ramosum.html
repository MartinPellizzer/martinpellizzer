Hieracium ramosum

Hieracium ramosum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, and it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Hieracium ramosum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.
