Hypericum humifusum var. australe

Hypericum humifusum var. australe, commonly known as the Australian St. John's Wort, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to Australia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has woody stems that remain close to the ground, and its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

This variety is known for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
