Hypoxis arenosa

Hypoxis arenosa, commonly known as the sand hypoxis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Hyacinthaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers in a dense cluster at the top of the stem. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
