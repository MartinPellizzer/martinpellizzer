Hieracium viride subsp. erythrellum

Hieracium viride subsp.

erythrellum, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange disk flowers surrounded by pale green to white ray florets.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.
