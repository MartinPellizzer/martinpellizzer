Hieracium krameri

Hieracium krameri, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragweed, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium krameri is often considered a weed but can play a role in supporting local pollinators.
