Hymenophyllum polyanthos var. blumeanum

Hymenophyllum polyanthos var. blumeanum, commonly known as the Blumeanum Hymenophyllum, is a species of moss belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with closely spaced, lanceolate leaflets that are often covered in a dense layer of rhizoids. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or tree trunks in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
