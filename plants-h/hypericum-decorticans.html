Hypericum decorticans

Hypericum decorticans, commonly known as the slender St. John's wort, is a low-growing shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, woody stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Hypericum decorticans is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
