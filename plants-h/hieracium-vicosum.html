Hieracium vicosum

Hieracium vicosum, commonly known as the Vicos hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and typically showy blooms. The plant features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage, and its flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters. Hieracium vicosum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is noted for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
