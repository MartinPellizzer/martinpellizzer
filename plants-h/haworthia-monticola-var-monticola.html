Haworthia monticola var. monticola

Haworthia monticola var. monticola, commonly known as the mountain haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy rosettes of thick, pale green leaves with white to yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, erect flower stalks bearing small, white or pale purple flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
