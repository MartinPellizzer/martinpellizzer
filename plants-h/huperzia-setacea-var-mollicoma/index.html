Huperzia Setacea Var. Mollicoma

Huperzia setacea var. mollicoma is a unique variant of the clubmoss plant known for its distinctive, soft, and velvety foliage. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions with high humidity. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in dense, spiral patterns along the stems. Despite its small size, this plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitats.
