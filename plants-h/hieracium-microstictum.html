Hieracium microstictum

Hieracium microstictum, commonly known as the small-streaked hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, typical of many hawkweed species. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium microstictum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
