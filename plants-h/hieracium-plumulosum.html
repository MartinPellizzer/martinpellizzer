Hieracium plumulosum

Hieracium plumulosum, commonly known as the downy hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and can reach heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are hairy and lobed, and the flowers are yellow with a distinctive, spiky central disk.

It is often found in open habitats and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
