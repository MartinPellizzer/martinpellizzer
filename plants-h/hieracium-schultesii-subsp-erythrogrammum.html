Hieracium schultesii subsp. erythrogrammum

Hieracium schultesii subsp.

erythrogrammum, commonly known as the red-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features basal and cauline leaves that are usually lanceolate to oblanceolate in shape. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a composite head, and are accompanied by bracts that are often reddish in color.

This subspecies is characterized by its distinctive red stems and is part of the Asteraceae family.
