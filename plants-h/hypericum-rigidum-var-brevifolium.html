Hypericum rigidum var. brevifolium

Hypericum rigidum var. brevifolium, commonly known as the compact St. John's wort, is a compact shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, dark green leaves and produces bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant is characterized by its dense growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to many landscapes.

Its medicinal properties, similar to other Hypericum species, have been historically noted for their potential therapeutic uses.
