Habenaria orbiculata var. menziesii

Habenaria orbiculata var. menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies' orchid, is a species native to Australia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a loose spike, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
