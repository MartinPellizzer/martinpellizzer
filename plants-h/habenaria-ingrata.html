Habenaria ingrata

Habenaria ingrata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale green to white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and often arranged in two rows along the stem.

Habenaria ingrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
