Hyoscyamus pungens

Hyoscyamus pungens, commonly known as the horned toad plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that contain toxic seeds, making it potentially harmful to animals and humans. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with caution.
