Hieracium demorsum

Hieracium demorsum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium demorsum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting pollinators.
