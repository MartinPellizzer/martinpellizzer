Hieracium stenosoma

Hieracium stenosoma, commonly known as narrowleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, flat-topped clusters, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.
