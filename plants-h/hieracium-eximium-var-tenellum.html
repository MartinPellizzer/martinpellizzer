Hieracium eximium var. tenellum

Hieracium eximium var. tenellum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. It has slender, upright stems with alternate leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy.

Its flowers are yellow and bloom from late spring to early autumn, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
