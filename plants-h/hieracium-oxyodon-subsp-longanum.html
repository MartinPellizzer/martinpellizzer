Hieracium oxyodon subsp. longanum

Hieracium oxyodon subsp.

longanum, commonly known as the long-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
