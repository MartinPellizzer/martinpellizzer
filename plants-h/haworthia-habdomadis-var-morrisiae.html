Haworthia habdomadis var. morrisiae

Haworthia habdomadis var. morrisiae, commonly known as the Morris's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dark green, fleshy leaves with prominent white or pale markings, often resembling a star pattern. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
