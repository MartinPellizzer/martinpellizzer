Hemidictyum limbatum

Hemidictyum limbatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water clover, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater habitats such as ponds and streams. The plant has long, slender leaves that are arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems. Its small, white flowers appear in clusters and are often submerged or floating.

Hemidictyum limbatum plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
