Habenaria microstylina

Habenaria microstylina, commonly known as the miniature-styled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it prefers shaded, humid environments.

The orchid's unique floral structure includes a short, narrow column and a distinctive lip, which are key features in its classification.
