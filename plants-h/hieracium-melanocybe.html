Hieracium melanocybe

Hieracium melanocybe, commonly known as the black-centered hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and dark central disk, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has hairy leaves and stems, and its flowers are usually arranged in a single head.

Hieracium melanocybe is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
