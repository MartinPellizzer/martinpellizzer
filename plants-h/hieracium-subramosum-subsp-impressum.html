Hieracium subramosum subsp. impressum

Hieracium subramosum subsp.

impressum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and serrated leaves.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and often have a distinct basal rosette formation.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.
