Homalolepis glabra

Homalolepis glabra, commonly known as the smooth sword fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright fronds that are covered in smooth, glabrous surfaces. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The botanical features of Homalolepis glabra make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
