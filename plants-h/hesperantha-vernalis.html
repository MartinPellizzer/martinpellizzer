Hesperantha vernalis

Hesperantha vernalis, commonly known as the spring bell orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained conditions. It is a perennial herb with slender stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Hesperantha vernalis blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a notable addition to gardens during the cooler months.
