Hieracium sphenophyllum

Hieracium sphenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in meadows and open woodlands. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Hieracium sphenophyllum is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
