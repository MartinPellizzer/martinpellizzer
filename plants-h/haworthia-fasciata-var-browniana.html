Haworthia fasciata var. browniana

Haworthia fasciata var. browniana, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellowish stripes, resembling a zebra's pattern. The leaves are covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on short, slender stalks during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
