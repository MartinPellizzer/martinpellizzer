Helianthemum marifolium var. canum

Helianthemum marifolium var. canum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, hairy foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its compact growth habit makes it well-suited for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.
