Helichrysum scaettai

Helichrysum scaettai, commonly known as the Sicilian everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Sicily, and belongs to the Asteraceae family. The plant is characterized by its dense, fluffy yellow to orange inflorescences that resemble small daisies. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to the dry, sunny climate of its native habitat.

Helichrysum scaettai is often used in dried floral arrangements due to its long-lasting, vibrant appearance.
