Homalomena palawanensis

Homalomena palawanensis, commonly known as the Palawan arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps.

The botanical features of Homalomena palawanensis make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
