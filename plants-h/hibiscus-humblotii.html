Hibiscus humblotii

Hibiscus humblotii, commonly known as the Malagasy hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Hibiscus humblotii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
