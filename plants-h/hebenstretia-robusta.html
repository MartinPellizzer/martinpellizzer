Hebenstretia robusta

Hebenstretia robusta, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple or pink, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
