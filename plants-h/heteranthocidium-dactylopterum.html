Heteranthocidium dactylopterum

Heteranthocidium dactylopterum, commonly known as the featherleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers that develop into parachute-like seeds. Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
