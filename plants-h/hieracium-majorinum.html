Hieracium majorinum

Hieracium majorinum, commonly known as the Greater Hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, rough stems. Native to Europe, it thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows and woodland edges. The plant produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

Its leaves are typically lobed and have a distinctive, rough texture.
