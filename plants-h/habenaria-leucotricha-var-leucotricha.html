Habenaria leucotricha var. leucotricha

Habenaria leucotricha var. leucotricha, commonly known as the white-tailed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
