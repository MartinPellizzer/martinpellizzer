Heleniastrum varium

Heleniastrum varium, commonly known as the variable heleniastrum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has alternate, narrow, and often hairy leaves that are usually covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of streams.

Heleniastrum varium is native to parts of Asia and is known for its ornamental value in garden settings.
