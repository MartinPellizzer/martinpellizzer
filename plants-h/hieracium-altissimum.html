Hieracium altissimum

Hieracium altissimum, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium altissimum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to colonize various habitats.
