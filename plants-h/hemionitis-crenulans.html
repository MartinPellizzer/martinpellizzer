Hemionitis crenulans

Hemionitis crenulans, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a unique fern species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a compact growth habit with small, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Hemionitis crenulans thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
