Hieracium sylvaticum subsp. dentifolium

Hieracium sylvaticum subsp.

dentifolium, commonly known as the serrated hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that have serrated edges, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is often found in association with other wildflowers in temperate regions.
