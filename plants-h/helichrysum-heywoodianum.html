Helichrysum heywoodianum

Helichrysum heywoodianum, commonly known as the everlasting daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain dry and retain their color for a long time, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
