Habenaria haullevilleana

Habenaria haullevilleana, commonly known as the Haulleville orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as pine flatwoods and wetlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elliptical, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small, white to pale pink, and often have a slight purple tinge.

It is a perennial species that blooms in late spring to early summer.
