Heliotropium minutiflorum

Heliotropium minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its flowers have a strong, sweet scent that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Heliotropium minutiflorum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
