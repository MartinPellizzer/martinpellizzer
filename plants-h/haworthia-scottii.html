Haworthia scottii

Haworthia scottii, commonly known as the Scott's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish or grayish hue and distinct white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the spring or summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Haworthia scottii is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of care.
