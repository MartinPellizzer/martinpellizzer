Helianthemum polifolium var. strictum

Helianthemum polifolium var. strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sunflower of the rock rose family, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, upright growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically narrow and densely arranged. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a vibrant splash of color to rocky or coastal environments. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh, sunny conditions.
