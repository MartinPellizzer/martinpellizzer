Hieracium sagittatum subsp. rubiginosum

Hieracium sagittatum subsp.

rubiginosum, commonly known as the red-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its reddish stems and leaves with a rough, hairy texture. It typically thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.
