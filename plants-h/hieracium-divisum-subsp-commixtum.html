Hieracium divisum subsp. commixtum

Hieracium divisum subsp.

commixtum, commonly known as the mixed yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its stems are hairy.

It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
