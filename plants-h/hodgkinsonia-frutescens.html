Hodgkinsonia frutescens

Hodgkinsonia frutescens, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, typically pink, purple, and white. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Hodgkinsonia frutescens is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
