Haworthia pearsonii

Haworthia pearsonii, commonly known as the Pearson's Haworthia, is a species of succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
