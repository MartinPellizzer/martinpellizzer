Habenaria fusifera

Habenaria fusifera, commonly known as the lance-shaped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a yellow-green lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
