Hypericum aviculariifolium

Hypericum aviculariifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb with upright, branched stems that bear small, yellow flowers. The leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

The plant is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
