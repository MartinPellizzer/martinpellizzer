Helipterum eximium

Helipterum eximium, commonly known as the showy everlasting.

This perennial herb is native to Australia and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy, daisy-like flowers. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its flowers are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
