Hermione polyantha

Hermione polyantha, commonly known as the miniature rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink, white, and red throughout the growing season. The plant has a bushy growth habit with dark green, glossy leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its colorful blooms. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.

Hermione polyantha prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth and flowering.
