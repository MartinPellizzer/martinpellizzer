Hypericum virgatum

Hypericum virgatum, commonly known as St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its stems are usually reddish-brown and covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum virgatum is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
