Hebeclinium hygrohylaeum

Hebeclinium hygrohylaeum, commonly known as the moisture-loving hebe, is a unique plant species characterized by its preference for humid environments.

It typically thrives in shaded, moist areas such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are small, clustered, and usually display shades of blue or purple, adding a subtle yet striking visual element.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized flora species.
