Hieracium calophyllomorphum

Hieracium calophyllomorphum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, where it thrives in meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are typically covered in fine hairs, and the flowers have tubular corollas with five lobes.

Hieracium calophyllomorphum is often found in sunny, well-drained soils and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
