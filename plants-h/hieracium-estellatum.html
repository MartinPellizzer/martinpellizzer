Hieracium estellatum

Hieracium estellatum, commonly known as Estelle's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium estellatum is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
