Hippeastrum bellum

Hippeastrum bellum, commonly known as the Bellum amaryllis, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces large, showy blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a contrasting white or pink center. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

Hippeastrum bellum is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
