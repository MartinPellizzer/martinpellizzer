Haworthia aranea

Haworthia aranea, commonly known as the netted haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with distinctive white or pale green markings that resemble a spider's web, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Haworthia aranea is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
