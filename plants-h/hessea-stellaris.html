Hessea stellaris

Hessea stellaris, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, bright yellow petals that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Hessea stellaris has small, needle-like leaves and produces a cluster of flowers at the end of each stem.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine by some indigenous communities.
