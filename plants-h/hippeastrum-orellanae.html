Hippeastrum orellanae

Hippeastrum orellanae, commonly known as the Orellana's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the warmer months. The plant features strap-like leaves that emerge from a bulb and are often used in ornamental gardening. Its flowers are borne on tall, slender stems and can reach up to 3 feet in height.

Hippeastrum orellanae is valued for its vibrant blooms and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
