Hylotelephium uralense

Hylotelephium uralense, commonly known as the Armenian carpet, is a perennial succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
