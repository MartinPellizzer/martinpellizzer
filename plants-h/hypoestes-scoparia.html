Hypoestes scoparia

Hypoestes scoparia, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoestes scoparia is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
