Hieracium Picroides Subsp. Grimsulanum

Hieracium picroides subsp. grimsulanum is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the daisy family, asteraceae, and is known for its distinctive yellow-orange flowers. This subspecies is characterized by its compact growth and hairy leaves, which help it survive in harsh alpine environments. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique and delicate plant from further decline.
