Habenaria combusta

Habenaria combusta, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It features a slender, upright stem with several lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, red-orange flowers with a distinctive yellow lip, which are borne on a branched inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and is often found on tree trunks or rocks.

The species is notable for its vibrant coloration and unique floral structure, making it a popular subject in botanical studies.
