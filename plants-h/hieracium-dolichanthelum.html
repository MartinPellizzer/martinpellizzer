Hieracium dolichanthelum

Hieracium dolichanthelum, commonly known as the long-flowered hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems, typical of many hawkweed species. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it often grows in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces numerous small, yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
