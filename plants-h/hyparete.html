Hyparete

Hyparete, commonly known as the mountain lily, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer.

Hyparete is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in alpine meadows.
