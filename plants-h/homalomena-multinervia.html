Homalomena multinervia

Homalomena multinervia, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically borne on a spadix surrounded by a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.

Homalomena multinervia is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
