Hyperocarpa filiformis

Hyperocarpa filiformis, commonly known as the slender pod plant, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated pods and small, delicate leaves. Native to certain regions of South America, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is often found in moist, well-drained soils and can grow in both full sun and partial shade.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
