Habenaria polytrichia

Habenaria polytrichia, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and dense, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellowish or greenish tint. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
