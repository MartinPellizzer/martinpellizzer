Habenaria brachyceratitis

Habenaria brachyceratitis, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a loose spike. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

This orchid is notable for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
