Horsfieldia hellwigii var. hellwigii

Horsfieldia hellwigii var. hellwigii, commonly known as the Horsfield's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellowish throat. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests.
