Hyoscyamus leptocalyx

Hyoscyamus leptocalyx, commonly known as the slender-flowered henbane, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces showy, yellowish to orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Hyoscyamus leptocalyx is known for its toxic properties, containing tropane alkaloids such as scopolamine and hyoscyamine.
