Hieracium pallescens subsp. sonklarianum

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

sonklarianum, commonly known as the Sonklara hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring well-drained conditions. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered with fine hairs, while its yellow flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
