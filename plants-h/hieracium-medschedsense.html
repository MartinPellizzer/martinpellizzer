Hieracium medschedsense

Hieracium medschedsense, commonly known as the Medschedsen's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange-pink flowers and is typically found in grassy or open habitats. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to parts of Europe and has been introduced to other regions.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
