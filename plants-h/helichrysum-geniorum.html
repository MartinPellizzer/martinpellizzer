Helichrysum geniorum

Helichrysum geniorum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seed heads that aid in wind pollination. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum geniorum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
