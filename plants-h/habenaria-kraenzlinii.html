Habenaria kraenzlinii

Habenaria kraenzlinii, commonly known as the Kraenzlin's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in shades of pink or purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged along the stem, and it prefers shady, moist habitats. It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in humid environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
