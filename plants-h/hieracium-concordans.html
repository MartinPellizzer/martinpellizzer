Hieracium concordans

Hieracium concordans, commonly known as the concord hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are hairy and alternate, with a serrated edge, and it has a robust root system.

Hieracium concordans is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
