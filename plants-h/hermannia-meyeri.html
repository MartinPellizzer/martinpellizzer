Hermannia meyeri

Hermannia meyeri, commonly known as the dwarf red hot poker, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, cylindrical clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
