Haworthia globosiflora

Haworthia globosiflora, commonly known as the globular haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
