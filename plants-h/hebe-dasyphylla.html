Hebe dasyphylla

Hebe dasyphylla, commonly known as the silver hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. Hebe dasyphylla thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value contribute to its widespread cultivation in temperate climates.
