Hypericum hyssopifolium proles elongatum

Hypericum hyssopifolium proles elongatum, commonly known as the elongated leaves St. John's wort, is a variant of the St. John's wort species characterized by its elongated leaves.

It is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia, typically found in woodland margins and grassy areas. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite and lanceolate, with a slightly hairy surface.

It is known for its medicinal properties, similar to other Hypericum species, though its specific uses are less documented.
