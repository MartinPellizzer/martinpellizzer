Habenaria ovariophora

Habenaria ovariophora, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features erect, hairy stems that bear several oval-shaped leaves with a prominent midrib. The plant produces pendulous, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip that is often hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a visually striking and ecologically significant species within its habitat.
