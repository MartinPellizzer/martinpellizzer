Hieracium murorum var. hyparcticum

Hieracium murorum var. hyparcticum, commonly known as the northern hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. This variety is native to northern regions of Europe and is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils. It produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal, aiding in its spread.

The plant is typically low-growing, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
