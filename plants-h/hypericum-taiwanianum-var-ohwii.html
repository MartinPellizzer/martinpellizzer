Hypericum taiwanianum var. ohwii

Hypericum taiwanianum var. ohwii, commonly known as the Taiwan Hypericum, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers with prominent orange stamens. The plant typically grows in shrub form, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and covered with fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.
