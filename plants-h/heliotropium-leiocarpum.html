Heliotropium leiocarpum

Heliotropium leiocarpum, commonly known as the silky heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm, featuring slender stems and hairy leaves. The plant produces small, violet or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving it a soft, fluffy appearance. It is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Heliotropium leiocarpum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and flowers.
