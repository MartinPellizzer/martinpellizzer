Habenaria socialis

Habenaria socialis, commonly known as the social orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance and delicate fragrance make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
