Hypericum formosissimum

Hypericum formosissimum, commonly known as the Chinese St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in terminal clusters. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
