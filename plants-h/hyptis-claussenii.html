Hyptis claussenii

Hyptis claussenii, commonly known as the silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in the summer. Hyptis claussenii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its aromatic properties make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
