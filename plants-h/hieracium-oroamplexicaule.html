Hieracium oroamplexicaule

Hieracium oroamplexicaule, commonly known as the golden hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, often lobed leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has a creeping growth habit with a central stem that produces numerous flowering stems.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas, where it spreads through both seeds and rhizomes.
