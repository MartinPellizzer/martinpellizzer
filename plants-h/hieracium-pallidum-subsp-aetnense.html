Hieracium pallidum subsp. aetnense

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

aetnense, commonly known as the Aetna hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the volcanic regions of Sicily. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to pale green flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

This subspecies is notable for its ecological adaptations to high-altitude and volcanic substrates.
