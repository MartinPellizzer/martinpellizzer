Hieracium subtenellescens

Hieracium subtenellescens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium subtenellescens is often found in disturbed areas and can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in certain regions.
