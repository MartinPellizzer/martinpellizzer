Hedyosmum macrophyllum

Hedyosmum macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant hedyosmum, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Hedyosmum macrophyllum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and pleasant floral scent.
