Helichrysum forskahlii var. compactum

Helichrysum forskahlii var. compactum, commonly known as the compact everlasting, is a dwarf shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, yellow-orange flower heads that resemble tiny sunflowers. The plant produces papery, dry flower heads that persist throughout the year, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
