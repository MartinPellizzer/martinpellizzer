Hemigraphis elegans var. crenata

Hemigraphis elegans var. crenata, commonly known as silver moss or velvet plant, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a distinctive silvery underside, giving it a shimmering appearance. The leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape and often have a slightly wavy margin. It produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that appear in clusters.

This plant is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
