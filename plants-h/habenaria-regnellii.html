Habenaria regnellii

Habenaria regnellii, commonly known as the regnell's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with three petals and three sepals, typically arranged in a spike. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Habenaria regnellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
