Hintonia latiflora var. leiantha

Hintonia latiflora var. leiantha, commonly known as the broadleaf hintonia, is a species native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a distinct, fragrant aroma. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, blooming in late spring.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to native pollinators.
