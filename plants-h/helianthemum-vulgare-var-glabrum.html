Helianthemum vulgare var. glabrum

Helianthemum vulgare var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in dry environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
