Hedysarum flavum

Hedysarum flavum, commonly known as yellow peavine, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial legume that produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. The plant has trifoliate leaves with three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Hedysarum flavum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
