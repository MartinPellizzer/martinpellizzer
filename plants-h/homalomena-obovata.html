Homalomena obovata

Homalomena obovata, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and glossy. The plant produces striking, pendant-like white flowers with a purple-tinged base. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps over time.
