Helichrysum itomampyense

Helichrysum itomampyense, commonly known as the golden crown bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shrubland and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture.

Helichrysum itomampyense is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
