Hartogia barosmifolia

Hartogia barosmifolia, commonly known as the Barosmi leaf plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by orange-red fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it valuable for both ecological and medicinal purposes.
