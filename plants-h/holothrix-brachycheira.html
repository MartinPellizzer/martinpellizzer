Holothrix brachycheira

Holothrix brachycheira, commonly known as the narrow-leaved holothrix, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear small, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in compact clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

Holothrix brachycheira is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
