Humiria floribunda var. parvifolia

Humiria floribunda var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved humiria, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The leaves are typically small, ovate, and have a glossy surface, while the flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
