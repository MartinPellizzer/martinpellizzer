Hieracium crocatum subsp. brachybrachion

Hieracium crocatum subsp.

brachybrachion, commonly known as the short-bract hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat head.

This subspecies is distinguished by its shorter bracts compared to other members of the species.
