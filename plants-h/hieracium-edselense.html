Hieracium edselense

Hieracium edselense, commonly known as the Edsel's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It features yellow flowers with ray florets and a basal rosette of leaves.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species in parts of its native range.
