Helichrysum papillosum

Helichrysum papillosum, commonly known as the Cape marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant has hairy, gray-green leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Helichrysum papillosum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
