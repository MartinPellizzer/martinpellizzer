Hieracium faurei subsp. macracladium

Hieracium faurei subsp.

macracladium, commonly known as the Macracladium hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. Its flowering period occurs in late summer, producing yellowish to reddish flowers that attract various pollinators.
