Hibiscus lampadius

Hibiscus lampadius, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. Hibiscus lampadius thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to bloom throughout the growing season contribute to its widespread cultivation.
