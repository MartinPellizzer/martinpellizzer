Hyoseris × pendula

Hyoseris × pendula, commonly known as the hanging yellow dandelion, is a hybrid species belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as meadows and stream banks. Its flowers are followed by fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds, aiding in its propagation.

Hyoseris × pendula is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
