Hieracium subgouanii, commonly known as the subgouan's hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Hieracium subgouanii is native to parts of Europe and is considered a minor weed in some agricultural settings.
