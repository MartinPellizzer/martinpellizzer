Hypoxis argentea var. argentea

Hypoxis argentea var. argentea, commonly known as silver groundsel, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, fibrous rootstock and produces a rosette of broad, silver-gray, hairy leaves. The plant blooms with yellow flowers arranged in a dense, star-shaped inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value is due to its striking foliage and delicate flowers.
