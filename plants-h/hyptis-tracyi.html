Hyptis tracyi

Hyptis tracyi, commonly known as Tracy's hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale purple, arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the branches.

Hyptis tracyi is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
