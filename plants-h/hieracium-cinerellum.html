Hieracium cinerellum

Hieracium cinerellum, commonly known as the cinerary weed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves. The plant produces yellow daisy-like flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium cinerellum is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
