Hieracium levicaule subsp. pertinacifolium

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

pertinacifolium, commonly known as the robustleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow disk flowers with surrounding ray florets, typical of the genus Hieracium. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its subspecies name, *pertinacifolium*, refers to its tough, persistent foliage.
