Hyoscyamus niger var. chinensis

Hyoscyamus niger var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese henbane, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its erect stem, alternate leaves, and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, dark brown berries that contain toxic alkaloids, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
