Hieracium Wilczekianum Subsp. Intybelloides

Hieracium wilczekianum subsp. intybelloides is a rare flowering plant native to a small region in central europe. it belongs to the daisy family and is known for its distinctive yellow flowers. this subspecies is particularly rare and has a limited geographic distribution. conservation efforts are crucial to protect its fragile habitat from human encroachment. its beauty and scarcity make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
