Hieracium austrovallesiacum

Hieracium austrovallesiacum, commonly known as the southern valley hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and typical sunflower-like appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas. Hieracium austrovallesiacum is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a common weed in agricultural areas.
