Huernia barbata var. crispa

Huernia barbata var. crispa, commonly known as the curly horned ice plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its unique, curling leaves give it a distinctive appearance, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
