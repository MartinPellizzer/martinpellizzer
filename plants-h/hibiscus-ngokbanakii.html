Hibiscus ngokbanakii

Hibiscus ngokbanakii, commonly known as the Ngokbanakii hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Hibiscus ngokbanakii thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
