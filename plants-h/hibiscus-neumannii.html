Hibiscus neumannii

Hibiscus neumannii, commonly known as the Neumann's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Hibiscus neumannii is a shrub or small tree, growing up to several meters in height.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
