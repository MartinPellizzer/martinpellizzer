Hypericum lianzhouense

Hypericum lianzhouense, commonly known as the Lianzhou St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite and elliptic, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum lianzhouense is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive floral display.
