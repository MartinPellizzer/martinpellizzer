Hieracium subspeciosum subsp. tomense

Hieracium subspeciosum subsp.

tomense, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed sites, reaching heights of up to 60 cm.

The plant features basal and cauline leaves that are lanceolate to ovate, with hairy surfaces. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a composite head, and are borne on long, slender peduncles.

It is characterized by its showy appearance and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
