Heterotis sylvestris

Heterotis sylvestris, commonly known as the African water spinach, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its edible, arrow-shaped leaves and hollow, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in moist, swampy environments and is often found near rivers and wetlands. Its leaves are used in various traditional cuisines, and it is valued for its nutritional content.

Heterotis sylvestris is also known for its rapid growth rate and ability to tolerate a wide range of environmental conditions.
