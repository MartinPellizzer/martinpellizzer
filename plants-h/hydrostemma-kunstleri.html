Hydrostemma kunstleri

Hydrostemma kunstleri, commonly known as the Kunstler's hydrostemma, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Hydrostemma kunstleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
