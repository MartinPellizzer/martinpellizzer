Habenaria pabstii

Habenaria pabstii, commonly known as the Pabst's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that allow it to spread gradually over time. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

This orchid is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in forest understories or on rocky substrates.
