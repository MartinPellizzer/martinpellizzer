Helianthemum vulgare f. scopolii

Helianthemum vulgare f. scopolii, commonly known as the Scopolii rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dense, needle-like leaves that are gray-green in color, providing a striking contrast to its bright yellow blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in mild climates.
