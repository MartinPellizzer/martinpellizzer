Heliotropium tenuifolium var. distantiflorum

Heliotropium tenuifolium var. distantiflorum, commonly known as the slender-leaved heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This variant is distinguished from other heliotrope species by its unique flower arrangement and leaf morphology.
