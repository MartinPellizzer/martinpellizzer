Hardenbergia ovata var. rosea

Hardenbergia ovata var. rosea, commonly known as the pink burr legume, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny pods and small, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and erosion control.

The variety 'rosea' is distinguished by its more intense pink flower color compared to the typical form.
