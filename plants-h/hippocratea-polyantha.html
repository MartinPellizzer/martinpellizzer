Hippocratea polyantha

Hippocratea polyantha, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has long, slender leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Hippocratea polyantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique and elegant floral display.
