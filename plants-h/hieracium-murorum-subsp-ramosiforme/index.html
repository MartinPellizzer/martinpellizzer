Hieracium Murorum Subsp. Ramosiforme

Hieracium murorum subsp. ramosiforme is a lesser-known member of the dandelion family known for its delicate yet resilient nature. Found primarily in rocky and arid environments, this subspecies thrives in conditions where few plants can survive. Its finely divided leaves and small yellow flowers make it a subtle yet distinctive addition to the flora of its native regions. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local insect populations. This plant serves as a reminder of the hidden beauty and ecological significance of understudied species.
