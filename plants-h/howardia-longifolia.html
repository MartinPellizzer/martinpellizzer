Howardia longifolia

Howardia longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Howardia longifolia is prized for its compact size and ornamental appeal in rock gardens and indoor collections.
