Hypericum kushakuense

Hypericum kushakuense, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, which are typical of many Hypericum species. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in moist or wet habitats.

The leaves are usually opposite and have a slightly succulent texture, contributing to its adaptability in various environments.
