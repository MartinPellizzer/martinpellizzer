Haplopappus venetus

Haplopappus venetus, commonly known as the slender pappus plant, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Haplopappus venetus is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats, contributing to the ecological diversity of these regions.
