Helichrysum lejolyanum

Helichrysum lejolyanum, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in full sun and well-drained conditions.

Helichrysum lejolyanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
