Hieracium chrysoprasium

Hieracium chrysoprasium, commonly known as the golden hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and western Asia and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Hieracium chrysoprasium is often found in disturbed habitats and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
