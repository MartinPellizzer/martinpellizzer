Hieracium rectum subsp. costae

Hieracium rectum subsp.

costae, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are typically narrow and serrated, with a distinctive ribbed or veined structure. Native to Europe, it often grows in meadows and grassy areas.

This subspecies is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
