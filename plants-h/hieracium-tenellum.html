Hieracium tenellum

Hieracium tenellum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features upright stems with basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and borne in clusters, often with a distinctive central disk.

Hieracium tenellum is an annual or biennial herb that can spread aggressively through its rhizomes.
