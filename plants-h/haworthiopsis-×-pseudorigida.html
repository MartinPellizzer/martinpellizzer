Haworthiopsis × pseudorigida

Haworthiopsis × pseudorigida, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern with thick, fleshy leaves that are often marked with distinct white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is closely related to species within the Haworthiopsis genus. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal watering, making it well-suited for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
