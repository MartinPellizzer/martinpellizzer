Hypericum petiolulatum subsp. yunnanense

Hypericum petiolulatum subsp.

yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to China. It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August.

The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
