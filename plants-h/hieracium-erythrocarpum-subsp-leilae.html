Hieracium erythrocarpum subsp. leilae

Hieracium erythrocarpum subsp.

leilae, commonly known as the Leilae's red-fruited hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers and distinctive, hairy, reddish-orange fruits.
