Hieracium herzogianum

Hieracium herzogianum, commonly known as Herzog's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous yellow to orange composite flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this species thrives in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which help protect it from harsh environmental conditions.

It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude climates.
