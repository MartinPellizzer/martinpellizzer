Hieracium suecicum subsp. fennicum

Hieracium suecicum subsp.

fennicum, commonly known as the Scandinavian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows, pastures, and wetlands.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. It has hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive basal rosette structure.

Botanically, it is characterized by its composite flower heads and is part of the Asteraceae family.
