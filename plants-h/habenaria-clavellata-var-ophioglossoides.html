Habenaria clavellata var. ophioglossoides

Habenaria clavellata var. ophioglossoides, commonly known as the orchid with the distinctive club-shaped spur, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a unique, elongated spur that houses the nectar.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
