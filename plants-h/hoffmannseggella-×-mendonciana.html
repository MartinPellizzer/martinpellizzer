Hoffmannseggella × mendonciana

Hoffmannseggella × mendonciana, commonly known as the hybrid hawthorn, is a naturally occurring hybrid between *Crataegus monogyna* and *Crataegus laevigata*.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in temperate regions of Europe. The plant features small, white or pink flowers in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with serrated edges, and it produces red, berry-like fruits in the fall.

The hybrid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
