Hieracium Chondrillifolium Subsp. Delasoiei

Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp. delasoiei is a rare and lesser-known member of the dandelion family. Found primarily in specific alpine regions of Europe, this subspecies thrives in harsh, high-altitude environments. Its distinctive yellow flowers and finely divided leaves set it apart from other members of the genus. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied due to its limited distribution and elusive nature. This article explores the unique characteristics, habitat, and ecological significance of this fascinating plant.
