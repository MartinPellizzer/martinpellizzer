Hermione viridiflora var. integra

Hermione viridiflora var. integra, commonly known as the Emerald Leaf Fern, is a striking evergreen fern characterized by its lush, dark green, lance-shaped fronds.

The plant features a dense, upright growth habit with intricate, feathery foliage that gives it a lush, tropical appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas or indoor environments. The fronds are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, with each leaf emerging from a central point.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for both horticultural and indoor gardening.
