Hieracium vladeasae

Hieracium vladeasae, commonly known as the Vladeasa hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Romania.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a low-growing structure with hairy stems and basal leaves.
