Habenaria ovoidea

Habenaria ovoidea, commonly known as the egg-shaped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, egg-shaped pseudobulbs and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
