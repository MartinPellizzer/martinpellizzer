Hieracium leuciscum

Hieracium leuciscum, commonly known as the white hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a central disc surrounded by ray florets.

Hieracium leuciscum is often found in open habitats and can be an indicator of nutrient-rich soils.
