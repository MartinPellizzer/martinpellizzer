Haworthia triebneriana var. diversicolor

Haworthia triebneriana var. diversicolor, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or yellow stripes. The leaves are typically a pale green to blue-gray color, providing a striking contrast to the markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
