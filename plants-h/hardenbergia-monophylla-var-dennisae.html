Hardenbergia monophylla var. dennisae

Hardenbergia monophylla var. dennisae, commonly known as the pink burr legume, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, pod-like fruits that turn pink as they mature. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
