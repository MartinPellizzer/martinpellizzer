Helianthemum bicolor

Helianthemum bicolor, commonly known as the pink rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with evergreen foliage and produces showy, pink or white flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
