Hypericum × sparsiflorum

Hypericum × sparsiflorum, commonly known as the sparseflower St. John's wort, is a hybrid shrub native to the United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers with five petals. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Hypericum × sparsiflorum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
