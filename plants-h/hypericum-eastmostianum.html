Hypericum eastmostianum

Hypericum eastmostianum, commonly known as Eastmost's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, woodland habitats, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with orange stamens, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum eastmostianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
