Hieracium Nigrescens Subsp. Limatum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp. limatum is a lesser-known member of the dandelion family known for its unique appearance and ecological significance. This subspecies is characterized by its dark green leaves and striking purple to black flowers that bloom in late summer. Found primarily in moist, shaded environments, it thrives in areas with rich, organic soil. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its subtle presence in natural habitats. Understanding this plant helps highlight the diversity within the Hieracium genus and the importance of preserving such species.
