Hieracium vallisiacum

Hieracium vallisiacum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish-green flowers and is typically found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous flowering stems that bear small, clustered flowers. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
