Hebe Formosa

Hebe formosa is a striking evergreen shrub native to New Zealand, known for its vibrant flowers and compact growth. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped blooms in shades of pink, purple, and white during spring and summer. Its adaptability and ornamental value have made it a favored plant among horticulturists. Hebe formosa is not only visually appealing but also easy to care for, offering beauty with minimal maintenance.
