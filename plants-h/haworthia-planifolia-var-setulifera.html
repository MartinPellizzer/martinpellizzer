Haworthia planifolia var. setulifera

Haworthia planifolia var. setulifera, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellow markings. The leaves are often adorned with fine, bristly hairs, giving it a unique textured appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
