Habenaria carnea var. concolor

Habenaria carnea var. concolor, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, hair-like inflorescence that often displays a pink or white hue. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and forest floors. Its flowers are small and arranged in a spiral, with three sepals and three petals.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique and delicate appearance.
