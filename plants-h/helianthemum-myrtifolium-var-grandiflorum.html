Helianthemum myrtifolium var. grandiflorum

Helianthemum myrtifolium var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered rock rose, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its hardy nature and attractive blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
