Hemilophia cardiocarpa

Hemilophia cardiocarpa, commonly known as the heart-shaped leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
