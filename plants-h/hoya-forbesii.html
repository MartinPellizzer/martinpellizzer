Hoya forbesii

Hoya forbesii, commonly known as the "Chinese everlasting," is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and clusters of star-shaped, waxy flowers. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
