Helianthemum globulariifolium var. minor

Helianthemum globulariifolium var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf globular rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense, compact growth with small, rounded leaves that are typically covered in fine white hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in small, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and ornamental plant for Mediterranean climates.
