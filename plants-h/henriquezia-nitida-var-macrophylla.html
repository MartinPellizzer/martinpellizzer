Henriquezia nitida var. macrophylla

Henriquezia nitida var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf henriquezia, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

Due to its unique foliage and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in subtropical gardens.
