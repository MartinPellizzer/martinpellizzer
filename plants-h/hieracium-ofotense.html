Hieracium ofotense

Hieracium ofotense, commonly known as the Otter Island hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and riverbanks. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs.
