Haworthiopsis × broteroana

Haworthiopsis × broteroana, commonly known as the Broter’s Haworthia, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings, which help it store water. It produces small, white or yellow flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
