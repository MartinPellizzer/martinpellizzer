Hohenbergia laxiflora

Hohenbergia laxiflora, commonly known as the loose-flowered hohenbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among bromeliad enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
