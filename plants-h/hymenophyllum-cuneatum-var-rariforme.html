Hymenophyllum cuneatum var. rariforme

Hymenophyllum cuneatum var. rariforme, commonly known as the rare-form fern, is a delicate and epiphytic fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Hymenophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate fronds with a velvety texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or rocks in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinctive, finely divided structure.

This varietal form is noted for its unique leaf morphology, which sets it apart from other members of the Hymenophyllum genus.
