Hexachlamys tapiraguaiensis

Hexachlamys tapiraguaiensis, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit tree, is a member of the Meliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped fruits that resemble small, yellow-orange starfruits. The plant has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and produces small, yellow flowers that develop into the unique fruit. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and potential economic uses.
