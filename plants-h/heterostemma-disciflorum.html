Heterostemma disciflorum

Heterostemma disciflorum, commonly known as the disc-flowered heterostemma, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence, which consists of yellow disk flowers surrounded by white to pale blue ray florets. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Heterostemma disciflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
