Hordeum lenkoranicum

Hordeum lenkoranicum, commonly known as the Central Asian barley, is a wild grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Hordeum lenkoranicum is of interest to botanists due to its potential for agricultural use and ecological resilience.
