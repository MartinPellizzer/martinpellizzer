Hieracium micosum

Hieracium micosum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers consist of yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Hieracium micosum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and seeds, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
