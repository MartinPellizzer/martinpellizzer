Hieracium nigrescens subsp. polymelinum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

polymelinum, commonly known as the dark-centred hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, with flowering stems that bear yellow composite flowers. Its flowers consist of a central disk surrounded by ray florets, and the plant produces numerous small, oval seeds.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black central disk, which gives it its common name.
