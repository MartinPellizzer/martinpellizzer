Hieracium scheppigianum

Hieracium scheppigianum, commonly known as the Scheppigian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. Its yellow flowers bloom in the summer, and it is known for its robust growth habit.

Hieracium scheppigianum is often found in open habitats and can form dense colonies.
