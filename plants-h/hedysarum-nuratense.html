Hedysarum nuratense

Hedysarum nuratense, commonly known as the Turkestan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features compound leaves and produces yellow or orange pea-like flowers. Its pods contain small, edible seeds that are sometimes used in local cuisines.

Hedysarum nuratense is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
