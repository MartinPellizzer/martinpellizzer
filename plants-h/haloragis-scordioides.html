Haloragis scordioides

Haloragis scordioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water-starflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland or swampy habitats. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, star-shaped white or pale blue flowers. It is adapted to waterlogged soils and can tolerate periodic flooding.
