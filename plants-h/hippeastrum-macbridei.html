Hippeastrum macbridei

Hippeastrum macbridei, commonly known as the MacBride's amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hippeastrum macbridei is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
