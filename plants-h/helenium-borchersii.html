Helenium borchersii

Helenium borchersii, commonly known as the Borchers' sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to the central United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in prairies and open woods, preferring well-drained soils and full sun. Its showy yellow to orange flowers bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

Helenium borchersii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
