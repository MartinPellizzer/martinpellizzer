Hosta undulata

Hosta undulata, commonly known as wavyleaf plantain, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features broad, wavy-edged leaves that are typically a deep green with a lighter midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers on slender stems in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta undulata is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often used in shaded garden settings.
