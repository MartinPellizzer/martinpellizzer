Hedyotis axillaris

Hedyotis axillaris, commonly known as the lesser flame vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a climbing herb with slender stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with slightly hairy surfaces, and its flowers are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.

Hedyotis axillaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
