Horridocactus paucicostatus subsp. echinus

Horridocactus paucicostatus subsp.

echinus, commonly known as the spiny hedgehog cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico. It features a cylindrical stem with prominent, spine-like tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its rugged structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and cactus enthusiasts.
