Hypecoum tetragonum

Hypecoum tetragonum, commonly known as the square-stemmed hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features upright, quadrangular stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Hypecoum tetragonum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering display.
