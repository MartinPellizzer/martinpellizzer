Hosta capitata

Hosta capitata, commonly known as the clustered hosta, is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a white margin. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in late summer, consisting of white to pale yellow petals. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in garden landscaping for its ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for shade gardens and woodland settings.
