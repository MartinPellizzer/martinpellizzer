Hedyotis delessertiana

Hedyotis delessertiana, commonly known as the scarlet bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, bright red flowers in clusters. The plant has slender, creeping stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and ecologically valuable species in native plant gardens.
