Heuffelia pubescens var. pallida

Heuffelia pubescens var. pallida, commonly known as the pale heuffelia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers. Native to tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and covered in fine pubescence.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
