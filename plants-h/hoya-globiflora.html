Hoya globiflora

Hoya globiflora, commonly known as the globeflower hoya, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asclepiadaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. Hoya globiflora is also valued for its adaptability and ease of care, making it popular among indoor gardeners.
