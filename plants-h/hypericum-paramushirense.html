Hypericum paramushirense

Hypericum paramushirense, commonly known as the Paramushire St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Hypericaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
