Habenaria libeniana

Habenaria libeniana, commonly known as the Liben orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories or rocky areas. Its flowering period occurs during the rainy season, and it is known for its unique floral structure that includes a labellum with intricate patterns.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
