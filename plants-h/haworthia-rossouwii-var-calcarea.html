Haworthia rossouwii var. calcarea

Haworthia rossouwii var. calcarea, commonly known as the rock haworthia, is a compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette, often covered in a white or grayish waxy coating. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
