Horsfieldia brachiata var. sumatrana

Horsfieldia brachiata var. sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
