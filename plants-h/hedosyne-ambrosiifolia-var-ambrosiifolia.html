Hedosyne ambrosiifolia var. ambrosiifolia

Hedosyne ambrosiifolia var. ambrosiifolia, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical plant characterized by its large, showy flowers and broad, heart-shaped leaves.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates with abundant water. The plant's botanical features include a woody stem, spirally arranged leaves, and a distinctive floral structure with multiple stamens. Its flowers typically bloom in shades of pink, red, or white, and they emit a subtle fragrance.

The species is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and holds cultural significance in various traditions.
