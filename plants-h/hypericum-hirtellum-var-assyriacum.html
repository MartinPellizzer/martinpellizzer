Hypericum hirtellum var. assyriacum

Hypericum hirtellum var. assyriacum, commonly known as the Assyrian St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its bright flowers and compact growth habit.
