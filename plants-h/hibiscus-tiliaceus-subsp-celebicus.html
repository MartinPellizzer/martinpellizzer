Hibiscus tiliaceus subsp. celebicus

Hibiscus tiliaceus subsp.

celebicus, commonly known as the Celebes hibiscus, is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green.

The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink or red, depending on the variety. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in mangrove swamps or along sandy shores.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
