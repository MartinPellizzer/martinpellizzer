Helianthemum × Sulphureum Var. Masguindalii

Helianthemum × sulphureum var. masguindalii is a striking plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and compact growth habit. It belongs to the rockrose family and is native to the Iberian Peninsula. This variety is prized for its ability to thrive in rocky and sunny environments. Its unique coloration and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and underappreciated species in horticultural circles.
