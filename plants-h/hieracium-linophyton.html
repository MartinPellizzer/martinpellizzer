Hieracium linophyton

Hieracium linophyton, commonly known as the silky hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
