Helictotrichon phaneroneuron

Helictotrichon phaneroneuron, commonly known as the tall bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear blue to purple flowers, which are typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and prairie habitats.
