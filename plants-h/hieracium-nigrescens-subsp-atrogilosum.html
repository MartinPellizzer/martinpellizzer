Hieracium nigrescens subsp. atrogilosum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

atrogilosum, commonly known as the dark-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dark green, hairy stems and small, yellowish to reddish flowers.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, often found in meadows and along roadsides.

This subspecies is notable for its horticultural value and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
