Haworthia hurlingii var. ambigua

Haworthia hurlingii var. ambigua, commonly known as the Hurling's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green to bluish-gray, often with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
