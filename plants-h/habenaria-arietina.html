Habenaria arietina

Habenaria arietina, commonly known as the Red-tipped Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red-tipped leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers arranged in a spike, with petals that are often white or pale pink.

The orchid's botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ornamental purposes.
