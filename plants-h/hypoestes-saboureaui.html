Hypoestes saboureaui

Hypoestes saboureaui, commonly known as the "Snowy Blush" or "Cape Myrtle," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white, often with a contrasting red or purple base. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Hypoestes saboureaui is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
