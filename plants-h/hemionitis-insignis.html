Hemionitis insignis

Hemionitis insignis, commonly known as the Chinese water fern, is a unique aquatic fern native to Asia.

It thrives in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has finely divided, feather-like fronds that are adapted for aquatic life. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Hemionitis insignis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in aquatic habitats.
