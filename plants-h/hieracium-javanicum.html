Hieracium javanicum

Hieracium javanicum, commonly known as the Java hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium javanicum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
