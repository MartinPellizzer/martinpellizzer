Huernia laevis

Huernia laevis, commonly known as the smooth horned orchid, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and spiky, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a unique appearance with its swollen, horn-like structures along the stems. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Huernia laevis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to store water in its stems.
