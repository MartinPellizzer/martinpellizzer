Hedysarum nepalense

Hedysarum nepalense, commonly known as the Nepal milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant features compound leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has a shallow root system.

Hedysarum nepalense is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.
