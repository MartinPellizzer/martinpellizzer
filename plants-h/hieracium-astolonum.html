Hieracium astolonum

Hieracium astolonum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish-orange flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

Hieracium astolonum is an annual or biennial plant that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
