Helminthostachys zeylanica var. brachyspicae

Helminthostachys zeylanica var. brachyspicae, commonly known as the small-spurred helminthostachys, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, fragrant flowers with short, spurred petals. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring or early summer.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
