Hyptis muricata var. inodora

Hyptis muricata var. inodora, commonly known as the velvetleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are hairy and have a distinctive velvety texture, contributing to its common name.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks.
