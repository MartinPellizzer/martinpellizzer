Hippeastrum canastrense

Hippeastrum canastrense, commonly known as the Canastrense amaryllis, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces strap-like, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and grow from a bulbous stem. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum canastrense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
