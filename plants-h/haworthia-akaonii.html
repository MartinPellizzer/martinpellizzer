Haworthia akaonii

Haworthia akaonii, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically grayish-green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
