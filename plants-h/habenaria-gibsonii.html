Habenaria gibsonii

Habenaria gibsonii, commonly known as the Gibson's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer months, producing clusters of white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
