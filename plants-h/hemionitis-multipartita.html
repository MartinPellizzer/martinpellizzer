Hemionitis multipartita

Hemionitis multipartita, commonly known as the basket fern, is a unique plant belonging to the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fan-shaped fronds that resemble a basket, hence its common name. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome with numerous branches that give rise to the fronds. Its leaves are typically bright green and have a leathery texture.
