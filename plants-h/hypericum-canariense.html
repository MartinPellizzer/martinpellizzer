Hypericum canariense

Hypericum canariense, commonly known as Canary goldenstar, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous growth habit. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in terminal clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum canariense is often found in open, sunny habitats such as coastal areas and rocky slopes.
