Haworthia decipiens var. exilis

Haworthia decipiens var. exilis, commonly known as the miniature Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically a pale green to bluish-green color, often with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
