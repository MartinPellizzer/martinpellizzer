Horsfieldia moluccana var. pubescens

Horsfieldia moluccana var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy horsfieldia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts interested in rare orchids.
