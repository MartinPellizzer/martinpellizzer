Hebenstretia glandulosa

Hebenstretia glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular hebenstretia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets and white to pale blue ray florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually linear or lance-shaped with glandular dots, giving it its scientific name.

Hebenstretia glandulosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
