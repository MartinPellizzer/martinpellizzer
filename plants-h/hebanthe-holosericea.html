Hebanthe holosericea

Hebanthe holosericea, commonly known as the silverleaf hebanthe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
