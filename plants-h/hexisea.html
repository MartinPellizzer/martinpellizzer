Hexisea

Hexisea, commonly known as the sea hibiscus, is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

These plants are typically found in coastal areas and are adapted to saline environments. They have showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The leaves are usually palmately lobed and often covered in fine hairs.
