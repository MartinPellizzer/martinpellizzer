Haageocereus platinospinus var. pluriflorus

Haageocereus platinospinus var. pluriflorus, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a columnar cactus native to Mexico.

It features a tall, branching stem covered in dense, white to silver spines. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for arid gardens and rockeries.
