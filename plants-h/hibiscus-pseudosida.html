Hibiscus pseudosida

Hibiscus pseudosida, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually narrow and pointed, with a smooth texture. Hibiscus pseudosida thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in moist forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
