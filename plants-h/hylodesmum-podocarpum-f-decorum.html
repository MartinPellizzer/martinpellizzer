Hylodesmum podocarpum f. decorum

Hylodesmum podocarpum f. decorum, commonly known as the ornamental podocarp, is a unique variant of the podocarp species.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Podocarpaceae, characterized by its evergreen foliage and conifer-like appearance. The plant exhibits a distinct ornamental form, with its dense, compact growth and attractive foliage making it a popular choice for horticultural use. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
