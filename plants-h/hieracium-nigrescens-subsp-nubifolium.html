Hieracium nigrescens subsp. nubifolium

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

nubifolium, commonly known as the cloudleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves that are deeply lobed and often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, flat-topped clusters, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are usually gray-green in color and can become more silvery as the plant matures.

It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along riverbanks.
