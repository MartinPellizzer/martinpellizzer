Hypericum lalandii var. macropetalum

Hypericum lalandii var. macropetalum, commonly known as the large-petaled St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering shrubs. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with large, showy petals that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine, soft hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
