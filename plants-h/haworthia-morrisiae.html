Haworthia morrisiae

Haworthia morrisiae, commonly known as the Morrisia Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
