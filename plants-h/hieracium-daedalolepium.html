Hieracium daedalolepium

Hieracium daedalolepium, commonly known as the intricate hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often branched inflorescence and yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is found in temperate regions of Europe. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.
