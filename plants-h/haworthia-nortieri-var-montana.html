Haworthia nortieri var. montana

Haworthia nortieri var. montana, commonly known as the mountain haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette, often with white or pale markings that give it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
