Hypodematium crenatum subsp. crenatum

Hypodematium crenatum subsp.

crenatum, commonly known as the crested hypodematium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats, often in areas with well-drained soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of lanceolate, hairy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in a dense cluster at the top of the stem.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
