Habenaria pelorioides

Habenaria pelorioides, commonly known as the Pelorioides orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a raceme, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
