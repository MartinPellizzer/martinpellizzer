Hieracium gothiciforme

Hieracium gothiciforme, commonly known as the Gothic dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this species thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces a distinctive, elongated seed head that aids in wind dispersal.

Its common name reflects its intricate, almost Gothic-like flower structure.
