Huernia hystrix subsp. parvula

Huernia hystrix subsp.

parvula, commonly known as the dwarf horned ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa. It features a compact, rounded stem covered in dense, white to pale green tubercles.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
