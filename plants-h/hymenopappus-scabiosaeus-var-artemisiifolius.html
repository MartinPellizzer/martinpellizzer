Hymenopappus scabiosaeus var. artemisiifolius

Hymenopappus scabiosaeus var. artemisiifolius, commonly known as the Artemisia-leaved Hymenopappus, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves with a rough texture, resembling those of Artemisia species. Its yellowish to brownish flowers are arranged in dense clusters, typical of the family.

It thrives in arid and rocky habitats, often found in desert environments.
