Habenaria rendlei

Habenaria rendlei, commonly known as the purple-petaled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with darker pigments. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and its role in local ecosystems.
