Hypericum erythreae

Hypericum erythreae, commonly known as the red-stemmed St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Hypericaceae family and is known for its small, elliptical leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the genus Hypericum.
