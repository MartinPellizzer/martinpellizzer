Habenaria delessertiana

Habenaria delessertiana, commonly known as the Delessert's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green or yellowish in color. Its flowers have a distinctive, intricate structure with three sepals and three petals, with the lateral petals often forming a hood-like shape.

The orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
