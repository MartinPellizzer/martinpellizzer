Hieracium grovesianum subsp. rivasolanum

Hieracium grovesianum subsp.

rivasolanum, commonly known as the Rivasolanum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single flower per stem, with ray florets that are often shorter than the central disk.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its distinctive botanical appearance.
