Hemerocallis middendorffii

Hemerocallis middendorffii, commonly known as the Siberian daylily, is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange, typically in late summer. The plant has broad, sword-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Hemerocallis middendorffii is valued for its ornamental beauty and resilience in temperate climates.
