Hibiscus reekmansii

Hibiscus reekmansii, commonly known as the Reekmansii hibiscus, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Hibiscus reekmansii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
