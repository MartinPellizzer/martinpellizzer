Helichrysum apiculatum var. typicum

Helichrysum apiculatum var. typicum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its densely packed, bright yellow to golden-orange flowers that are tightly clustered in heads. The plant produces small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its flowers are long-lasting when dried, making it a popular choice for floral arrangements.
