Helictotrichon sumatrense

Helictotrichon sumatrense, commonly known as the Sumatran feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves that give it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are often wind-pollinated.

Helictotrichon sumatrense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.
