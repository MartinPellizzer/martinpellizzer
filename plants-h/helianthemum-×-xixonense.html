Helianthemum × xixonense

Helianthemum × xixonense, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a dense, mat-like formation. It is well adapted to rocky and sunny environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

This species is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
