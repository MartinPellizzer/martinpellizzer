Helenium microcephalum var. ooclinium

Helenium microcephalum var. ooclinium, commonly known as the dwarf orange hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright orange to red flowers in late summer. The plant has a compact growth habit, typically reaching heights of less than 12 inches. Its botanical characteristics include showy, disk-like flowers with yellow centers and ray florets.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
