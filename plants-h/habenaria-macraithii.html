Habenaria macraithii

Habenaria macraithii, commonly known as the Macraith's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration, often featuring a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
