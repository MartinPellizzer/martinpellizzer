Holothrix tridentata

Holothrix tridentata, commonly known as the three-toothed holothrix, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Holothrix tridentata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
