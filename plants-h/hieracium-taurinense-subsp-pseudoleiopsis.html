Hieracium taurinense subsp. pseudoleiopsis

Hieracium taurinense subsp.

pseudoleiopsis, commonly known as the Taurine Hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, basal leaves.

This subspecies is native to southern Europe, particularly the regions around the Taurus Mountains. It typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils.
