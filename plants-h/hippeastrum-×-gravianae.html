Hippeastrum × gravianae

Hippeastrum × gravianae, commonly known as the red dragon lily, is a hybrid plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces fleshy, bulbous underground stems called bulbs, which store nutrients and water. Its leaves are broad, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture.

Hippeastrum × gravianae is cultivated for its ornamental value and is often grown as a houseplant or in gardens.
