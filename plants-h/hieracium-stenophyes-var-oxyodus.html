Hieracium stenophyes var. oxyodus

Hieracium stenophyes var. oxyodus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typical of the dandelion family. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is occasionally grown in gardens.
