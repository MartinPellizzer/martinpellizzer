Hydrocotyle latisecta

Hydrocotyle latisecta, commonly known as the broad-leaved water arum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often divided into broad, lobed segments, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in moist soil conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, often surrounded by a spathe-like structure.

The botanical characteristics of Hydrocotyle latisecta make it an attractive and adaptable species for wetland restoration and ornamental gardening.
