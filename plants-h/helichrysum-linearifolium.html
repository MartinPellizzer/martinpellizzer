Helichrysum linearifolium

Helichrysum linearifolium, commonly known as the linearleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature.

Helichrysum linearifolium is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
