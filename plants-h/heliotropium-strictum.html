Heliotropium strictum

Heliotropium strictum, commonly known as the narrowleaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect, often hairy stems and opposite leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually violet or purple in color, which bloom in the morning and close in the afternoon. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and coastal regions.

Heliotropium strictum is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
