Hieracium chloromuticum

Hieracium chloromuticum, commonly known as the green-changing hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its yellowish to greenish flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Hieracium chloromuticum is known for its ability to adapt to various environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many regions.
