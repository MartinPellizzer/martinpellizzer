Hymenidium corydalifolium

Hymenidium corydalifolium, commonly known as the Chinese cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces cones rather than flowers, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants. It is native to southern China and is adapted to thrive in warm, humid climates.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
