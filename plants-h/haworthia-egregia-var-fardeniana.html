Haworthia egregia var. fardeniana

Haworthia egregia var. fardeniana, commonly known as the Farden's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with white or pale markings, often resembling a star pattern. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
