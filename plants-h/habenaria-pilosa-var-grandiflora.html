Habenaria pilosa var. grandiflora

Habenaria pilosa var. grandiflora, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
