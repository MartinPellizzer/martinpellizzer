Hoya obscura subsp. obscura

Hoya obscura subsp.

obscura, commonly known as the dark hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink.

The plant is a succulent, capable of storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves, which helps it survive in dry conditions. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant among hoya enthusiasts.
