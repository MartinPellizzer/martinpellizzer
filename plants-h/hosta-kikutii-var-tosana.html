Hosta kikutii var. tosana

Hosta kikutii var. tosana, commonly known as the Tosan hosta, is a variant of the Japanese hosta species.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-white margin. The plant is native to Japan and is prized for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and suitable for woodland gardens or as a ground cover.
