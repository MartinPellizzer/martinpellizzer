Holothrix burchellii

Holothrix burchellii, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces striking white or pale blue flowers with a distinctive star-like shape. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in open grasslands and along rocky outcrops.

Holothrix burchellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
