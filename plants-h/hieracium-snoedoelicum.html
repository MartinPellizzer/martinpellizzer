Hieracium snoedoelicum

Hieracium snoedoelicum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium snoedoelicum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
