Howardia bridgesii

Howardia bridgesii, commonly known as the Zulu velvet plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, velvety foliage that ranges in color from deep green to reddish-purple, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

Howardia bridgesii is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
