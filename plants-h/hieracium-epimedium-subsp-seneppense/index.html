Hieracium Epimedium Subsp. Seneppense

Hieracium epimedium subsp. seneppense is a lesser-known member of the sunflower family known for its delicate yellow flowers and hairy leaves. Found primarily in the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and alpine environments. It is often mistaken for other species in the Hieracium genus due to its similar appearance. Despite its beauty, it remains a rare and underappreciated species in botanical circles. Its unique characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
