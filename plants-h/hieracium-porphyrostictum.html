Hieracium porphyrostictum

Hieracium porphyrostictum, commonly known as the purple-streaked hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which are typically grayish-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, which bloom in the summer months. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
