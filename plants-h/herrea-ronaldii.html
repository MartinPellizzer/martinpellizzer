Herrea ronaldii

Herrea ronaldii, commonly known as the Ronaldii dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, typical of the family. Herrea ronaldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
