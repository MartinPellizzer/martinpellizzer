Hicorius cordiformis var. elongata

Hicorius cordiformis var. elongata, commonly known as the heart-shaped leaf euphorbia, is a perennial plant characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to specific regions in Africa. The plant features a thick, fleshy stem that often grows upright, with leaves that are broad and lobed. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing seeds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
