Hypericum calycinum f. luteum

Hypericum calycinum f. luteum, commonly known as the yellow St. John's wort, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
