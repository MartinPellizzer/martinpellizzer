Herrania breviligulata

Herrania breviligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Herrania, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens in regions with suitable climates.
