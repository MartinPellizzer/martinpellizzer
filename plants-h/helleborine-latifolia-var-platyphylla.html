Helleborine latifolia var. platyphylla

Helleborine latifolia var. platyphylla, commonly known as the broad-leaved helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often densely covered with a waxy cuticle. The plant produces striking, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip, typical of orchids. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral structure.
