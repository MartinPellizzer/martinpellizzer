Hieracium corsicum

Hieracium corsicum, commonly known as the Corsican hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Corsica, Sardinia, and southern Italy. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It features bright yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, with hairy stems and leaves.

Hieracium corsicum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
