Haworthia mucronata subsp. lockwoodii

Haworthia mucronata subsp.

lockwoodii, commonly known as the Lockwood's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings, arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
