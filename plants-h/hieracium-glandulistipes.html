Hieracium glandulistipes

Hieracium glandulistipes, commonly known as the glandular-stem hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its stems are hairy and glandular, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
