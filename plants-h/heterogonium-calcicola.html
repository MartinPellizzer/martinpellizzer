Heterogonium calcicola

Heterogonium calcicola, commonly known as the calcicola heterogonium, is a rare and specialized alga found in specific calcareous environments.

It thrives in alkaline waters, often in freshwater springs or moist rock surfaces where calcium levels are high. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with a filamentous structure and distinct reproductive organs. Its growth is closely tied to the chemical composition of its habitat, making it an indicator species for certain ecological conditions.
