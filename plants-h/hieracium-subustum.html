Hieracium subustum

Hieracium subustum, commonly known as the bent-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish-orange composite flowers. The plant has heart-shaped or oval leaves that are often hairy and slightly toothed. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium subustum is an herbaceous perennial that produces achenes as its seed type.
