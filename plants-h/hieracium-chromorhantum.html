Hieracium chromorhantum

Hieracium chromorhantum, commonly known as the chromatic hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including yellow, red, and orange. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rough or hairy texture, and the stems are erect and often hairy as well.

Hieracium chromorhantum is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
