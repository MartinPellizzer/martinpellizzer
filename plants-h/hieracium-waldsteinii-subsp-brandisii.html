Hieracium waldsteinii subsp. brandisii

Hieracium waldsteinii subsp.

brandisii, commonly known as Brandis' hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its clustered, yellowish to orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and forest edges, preferring well-drained soils. It has slender, hairy stems and basal leaves that are often heart-shaped.
