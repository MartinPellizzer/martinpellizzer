Hypericum reptans subsp. ogisui

Hypericum reptans subsp.

ogisui, commonly known as the Japanese st. john's wort, is a low-growing shrub native to Japan. It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has narrow, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, ornamental plant.
