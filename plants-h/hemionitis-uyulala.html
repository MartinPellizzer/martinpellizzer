Hemionitis uyulala

Hemionitis uyulala, commonly known as the narrow-leafed water fern, is a unique aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in shallow, freshwater environments, often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its rhizomes are submerged, anchoring the plant while absorbing nutrients from the water.

Hemionitis uyulala plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms and contributing to water filtration.
