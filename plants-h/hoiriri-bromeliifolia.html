Hoiriri bromeliifolia

Hoiriri bromeliifolia, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, silver-blue leaves that form a central cup. The plant produces striking red flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette, adding a dramatic visual contrast. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is also considered a symbol of the Hawaiian ecosystem.
