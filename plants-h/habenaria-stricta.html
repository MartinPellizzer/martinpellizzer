Habenaria stricta

Habenaria stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features slender, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green in color. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in a spike, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Habenaria stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
