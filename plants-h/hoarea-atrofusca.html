Hoarea atrofusca

Hoarea atrofusca, commonly known as the black-leaved hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that range in color from dark green to almost black. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It is a succulent, meaning it stores water in its leaves and stems, making it drought-tolerant.

Hoarea atrofusca is often grown as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
