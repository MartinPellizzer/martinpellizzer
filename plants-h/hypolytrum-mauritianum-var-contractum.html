Hypolytrum mauritianum var. contractum

Hypolytrum mauritianum var. contractum, commonly known as the Mauritius rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its inflorescence consists of clustered flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green.

This variety is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological role and potential for conservation efforts.
