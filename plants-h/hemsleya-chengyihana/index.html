Hemsleya Chengyihana

Hemsleya chengyihana is a rare and striking plant native to China's Yunnan province. Known for its vibrant purple flowers and delicate foliage, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. First discovered in the early 2000s, this species remains poorly understood due to its limited distribution. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique and endangered plant from habitat loss.
