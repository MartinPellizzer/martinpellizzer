Habenaria mairei

Habenaria mairei, commonly known as the Maire's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with intricate patterns, and it has a sympodial growth habit.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
