Hosta plantaginea var. grandiflora

Hosta plantaginea var. grandiflora, commonly known as the giant leaf hosta, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant, white to pale blue flowers on tall, sturdy stems. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to provide shade and texture in woodland settings.
