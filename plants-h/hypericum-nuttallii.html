Hypericum nuttallii

Hypericum nuttallii, commonly known as the Nuttall's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum nuttallii is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
