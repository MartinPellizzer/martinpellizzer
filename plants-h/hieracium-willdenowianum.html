Hieracium willdenowianum

Hieracium willdenowianum, commonly known as the Willdenow's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, often reddish stems. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
