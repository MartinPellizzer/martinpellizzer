Hieracium vulgatum var. medianum

Hieracium vulgatum var. medianum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

The species is known for its rapid spread and can become invasive in certain regions.
