Hypericum moesicum

Hypericum moesicum, commonly known as the yellow St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, which are typical of the Hypericaceae family. The plant typically grows in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly serrated margin.

Hypericum moesicum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
