Huperzia setacea var. mollicoma

Huperzia setacea var. mollicoma, commonly known as the soft moss huperzia, is a species of fern-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including countries such as China, Japan, and Indonesia.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings for its ability to form lush, carpet-like foliage.
