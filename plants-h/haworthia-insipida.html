Haworthia insipida

Haworthia insipida, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Haworthia insipida is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
