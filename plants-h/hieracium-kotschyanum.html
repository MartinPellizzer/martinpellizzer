Hieracium kotschyanum

Hieracium kotschyanum, commonly known as the Kotschyan's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped with hairy surfaces.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
