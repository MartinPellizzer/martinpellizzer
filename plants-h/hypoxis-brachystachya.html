Hypoxis brachystachya

Hypoxis brachystachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens across temperate regions.
