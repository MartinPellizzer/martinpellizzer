Hieracium burkartii

Hieracium burkartii, commonly known as Burkart's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in clusters, often resembling dandelions.

It is noted for its robust growth and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
