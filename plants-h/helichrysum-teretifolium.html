Helichrysum teretifolium

Helichrysum teretifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting.

It is a perennial herb native to South Africa, characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature. The plant features slender, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The genus Helichrysum is known for its aromatic properties, and H. teretifolium is no exception, emitting a subtle, pleasant scent.
