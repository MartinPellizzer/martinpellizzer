Hydrangea heteromalla var. parviflora

Hydrangea heteromalla var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered smooth hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features large, round, cone-shaped flower clusters that range from white to pink depending on soil pH. The plant produces showy, sterile flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a smooth texture and dark green color.

It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
