Hieracium leucozum

Hieracium leucozum, commonly known as the white hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
