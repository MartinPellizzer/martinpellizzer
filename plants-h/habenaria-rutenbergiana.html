Habenaria rutenbergiana

Habenaria rutenbergiana, commonly known as the Rutenberg's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
