Hieracium jurassicum subsp. decrescentifolium

Hieracium jurassicum subsp.

decrescentifolium, commonly known as the Jurassic hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions in Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The subspecies is distinguished by its decrescent leaves, which become progressively smaller as they ascend the stem. It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant produces yellowish to orange flower heads, which are surrounded by pale green bracts.
