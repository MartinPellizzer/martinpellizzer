Hieracium crepidispermum f. minoriceps

Hieracium crepidispermum f. minoriceps, commonly known as the small-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. Its leaves are basal and lanceolate, with hairy surfaces.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.
