Herminium motuoense

Herminium motuoense, commonly known as the Motuo orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple and white flowers, which bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often near streams or in mountainous regions. Its rhizome is fibrous, and it produces a single, tall flowering stem with several showy flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Herminium motuoense is considered endangered and is of great interest to botanists and conservationists.
