Helichrysum rigidulum

Helichrysum rigidulum, commonly known as the rigid everlasting.

It is a perennial shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, wiry stems and small, yellow-orange flowers that resemble tiny sunflowers. The plant is known for its aromatic foliage, which emits a pleasant, spicy scent. Its flowers are typically tubular and are borne in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.

Helichrysum rigidulum is often used in gardens for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
