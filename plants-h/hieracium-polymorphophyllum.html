Hieracium polymorphophyllum

Hieracium polymorphophyllum, commonly known as the variable hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is characterized by its variable leaf shapes and sizes. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Due to its adaptability, it is considered an invasive species in some regions outside its native range.
