Hieracium megatrichum var. svevorum

Hieracium megatrichum var. svevorum, commonly known as the Svevian hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

It is known for its aggressive growth and can become invasive in certain environments.
