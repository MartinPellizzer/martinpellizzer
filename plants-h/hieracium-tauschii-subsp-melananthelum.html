Hieracium tauschii subsp. melananthelum

Hieracium tauschii subsp.

melananthelum, commonly known as the dark-flowered hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has basal and upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.
