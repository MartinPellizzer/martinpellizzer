Hieracium × flammeum

Hieracium × flammeum, commonly known as the flame hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are typically arranged in a composite head. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it often thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
