Hieracium congestum

Hieracium congestum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its flowers are yellow and appear in late summer, producing small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Hieracium congestum is often found in association with other flowering plants in its native environment.
