Helogyne urenifolia

Helogyne urenifolia, commonly known as the golden spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often a vibrant green, with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Helogyne urenifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
