Hibiscus beckleri

Hibiscus beckleri, commonly known as the Sierra Madre hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found growing in shaded, moist environments. Hibiscus beckleri is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 3 meters.

It plays an important role in its native ecosystem, providing nectar for various pollinators.
