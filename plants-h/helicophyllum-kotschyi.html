Helicophyllum kotschyi

Helicophyllum kotschyi, commonly known as the spiral-leaved lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Helicophyllum kotschyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
