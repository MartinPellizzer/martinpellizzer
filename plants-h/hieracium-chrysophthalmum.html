Hieracium chrysophthalmum

Hieracium chrysophthalmum, commonly known as the golden-eyed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium chrysophthalmum is often found in temperate regions and can be an indicator plant for certain soil types.
