Haworthia altilinea f. inconfluens

Haworthia altilinea f. inconfluens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and pointed, with a distinctive pale green to bluish-gray coloration. The leaves are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
