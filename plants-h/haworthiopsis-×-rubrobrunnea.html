Haworthiopsis × rubrobrunnea

Haworthiopsis × rubrobrunnea, commonly known as the firecrest or red-brown haworthia, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a mix of green, red, and brown hues. The leaves are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
