Hypericum cambessedesii

Hypericum cambessedesii, commonly known as the Corsican St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering shrubs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with orange stamens, which bloom in summer. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native habitat.
