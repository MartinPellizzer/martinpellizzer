Harungana madagascariensis

Harungana madagascariensis, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in winter, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has smooth, grey-green bark and elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique floral display.

Harungana madagascariensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
