Hedychium villosum var. kachinense

Hedychium villosum var. kachinense, commonly known as the Kachin ginger lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are broad, ovate, and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving the plant a velvety texture. It prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
