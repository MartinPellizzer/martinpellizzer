Hesperanthemum aciculare

Hesperanthemum aciculare, commonly known as the needleleaf hesperanthemum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

Hesperanthemum aciculare is well adapted to arid environments and is often grown as an ornamental plant in rock gardens.
