Hieracium pseudocirritum subsp. hypochoeridiforme

Hieracium pseudocirritum subsp.

hypochoeridiforme, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and often covered with fine hairs, while the upper leaves are smaller and more upright. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and is often found in moist to moderately dry soils.
