Hypoestes umbellata

Hypoestes umbellata, commonly known as the "Magic Bells" or "Rainbow Flower," is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

Hypoestes umbellata prefers well-draining soil and indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
