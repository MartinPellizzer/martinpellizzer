Hieracium eriphalium

Hieracium eriphalium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has a hairy stem and lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish-brown underneath. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.

Hieracium eriphalium is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
