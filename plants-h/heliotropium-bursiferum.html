Heliotropium bursiferum

Heliotropium bursiferum, commonly known as the burr heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often opposite in arrangement. Its fruit is a distinctive burr-like structure that aids in seed dispersal.

Heliotropium bursiferum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in coastal areas.
