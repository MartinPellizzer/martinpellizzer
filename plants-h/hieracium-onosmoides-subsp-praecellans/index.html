Hieracium Onosmoides Subsp. Praecellans

Hieracium onosmoides subsp. praecellans is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the dandelion family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. This subspecies is distinguished by its vibrant yellow flowers and unique leaf structure. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Due to habitat loss, it faces threats and is considered a species of conservation concern.
