Hartogiella schinoides

Hartogiella schinoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved boxwood, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Hartogiella schinoides is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
