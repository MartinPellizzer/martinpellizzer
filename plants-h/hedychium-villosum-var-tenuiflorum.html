Hedychium villosum var. tenuiflorum

Hedychium villosum var. tenuiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered ginger, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is most notable for its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The variety tenuiflorum is distinguished by its narrower flowers compared to other Hedychium species.
