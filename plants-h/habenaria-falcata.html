Habenaria falcata

Habenaria falcata, commonly known as the sickle orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and distinctive, elongated flower spikes. The flowers are typically pale yellow to greenish-white with a faintly colored lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories.

This orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
