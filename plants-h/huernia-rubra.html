Huernia rubra

Huernia rubra, commonly known as the red horned orchid, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its unique, fleshy, spine-tipped stems that resemble horns. The plant produces small, fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Huernia rubra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
