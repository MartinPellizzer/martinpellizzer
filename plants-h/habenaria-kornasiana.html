Habenaria kornasiana

Habenaria kornasiana, commonly known as the Kornasian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
