Hieracium hymenopterum

Hieracium hymenopterum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.
