Hieracium subspeciosum subsp. gymnopsis

Hieracium subspeciosum subsp.

gymnopsis, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in composite inflorescences.

It is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
