Hedychium coccineum var. flavum

Hedychium coccineum var. flavum, commonly known as the yellow flame ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy orange-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are evergreen and provide a lush backdrop for its blossoms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Hedychium coccineum var. flavum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in tropical and subtropical landscapes.
