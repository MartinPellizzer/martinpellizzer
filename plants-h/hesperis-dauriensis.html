Hesperis dauriensis

Hesperis dauriensis, commonly known as Daurian honesty, is a flowering plant native to the mountains of East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are pinnately divided, and the plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters.

Hesperis dauriensis is often found in moist meadows and forest edges, thriving in temperate climates.
