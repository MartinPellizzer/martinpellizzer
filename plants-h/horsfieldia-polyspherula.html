Horsfieldia polyspherula

Horsfieldia polyspherula, commonly known as the small-flowered orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often arranged in a raceme. Its roots are adapted for nutrient absorption from the air and decaying organic matter in its natural habitat.

Horsfieldia polyspherula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
