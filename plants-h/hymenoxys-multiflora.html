Hymenoxys multiflora

Hymenoxys multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered hymenoxys, is a herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It produces numerous small, yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Hymenoxys multiflora is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils and open woodlands.
