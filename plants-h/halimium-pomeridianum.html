Halimium pomeridianum

Halimium pomeridianum, commonly known as the sunset rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in the late afternoon, giving it its common name. Its leaves are small, oval, and covered in a fine layer of white, waxy hairs.

Halimium pomeridianum is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in sunny, well-drained habitats.
