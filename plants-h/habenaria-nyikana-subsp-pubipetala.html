Habenaria nyikana subsp. pubipetala

Habenaria nyikana subsp.

pubipetala, commonly known as the pubescent orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family. It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and features lanceolate, pubescent leaves.

The inflorescence consists of several showy, greenish-yellow flowers with distinct sepals and petals. It typically blooms in late winter to early spring, and the species is found in moist, shaded habitats.

This subspecies is notable for its unique floral structure and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
