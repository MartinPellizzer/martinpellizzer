Hieracium pseudopsarodes

Hieracium pseudopsarodes, commonly known as the false hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features showy, yellow to orange composite flowers that attract pollinators. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the stems are often reddish in color.

Hieracium pseudopsarodes is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates.
