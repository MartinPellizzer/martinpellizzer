Hieracium oxytorum

Hieracium oxytorum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, with a flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers. Its inflorescence consists of a single flower head surrounded by a ring of bracts, and it produces small, elongated achenes as its seeds.

Hieracium oxytorum is often found in open habitats and is characterized by its distinctive foliage and yellow floral display.
