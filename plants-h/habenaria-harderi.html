Habenaria harderi

Habenaria harderi, commonly known as the hardy lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and long, cylindrical spikes of white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem, a feature typical of the Orchidaceae family.

Habenaria harderi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
