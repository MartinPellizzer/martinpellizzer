Herminium chiwui

Herminium chiwui, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to China.

It is a perennial herb with a tall, slender stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is considered a valuable species in botanical collections.
