Haworthia dodsoniana

Haworthia dodsoniana, commonly known as the Dodson's Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with distinctive white or silver markings. The plant produces tall, spiky flower stalks that bear small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are covered in a fine layer of wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
