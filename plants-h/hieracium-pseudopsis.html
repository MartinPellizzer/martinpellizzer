Hieracium pseudopsis

Hieracium pseudopsis, commonly known as the false hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium pseudopsis is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to spread rapidly.
