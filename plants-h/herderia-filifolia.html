Herderia filifolia

Herderia filifolia, commonly known as the silky filifolium, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, thread-like leaves that give it its common name, and it produces small, pale yellow flowers in the spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its delicate appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Herderia filifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
