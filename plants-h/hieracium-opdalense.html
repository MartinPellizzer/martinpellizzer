Hieracium opdalense

Hieracium opdalense, commonly known as the Opdal hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to northern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks. It produces yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium opdalense is noted for its low-growing habit and its ability to thrive in damp environments.
