Hieracium murorum subsp. cuneolatum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

cuneolatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

The plant features basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to narrowly elliptic in shape, with hairy surfaces. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a single inflorescence and bloom from June to August.

The subspecies is characterized by its distinct leaf morphology and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
