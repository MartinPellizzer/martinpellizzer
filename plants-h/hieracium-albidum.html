Hieracium albidum

Hieracium albidum, commonly known as the white hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it produces a milky sap when damaged.

Hieracium albidum is often found in open habitats and is adapted to temperate climates.
