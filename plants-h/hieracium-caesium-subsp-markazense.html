Hieracium caesium subsp. markazense

Hieracium caesium subsp.

markazense, commonly known as the Caucasian hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-colored composite flowers.

The plant typically features basal and cauline leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native range.
