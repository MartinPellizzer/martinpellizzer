Hieracium ovulatum

Hieracium ovulatum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has a basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous flowering stems that rise above the foliage.

Hieracium ovulatum is a perennial plant that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
