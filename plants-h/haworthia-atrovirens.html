Haworthia atrovirens

Haworthia atrovirens, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped body with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Haworthia atrovirens is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
