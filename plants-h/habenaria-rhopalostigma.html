Habenaria rhopalostigma

Habenaria rhopalostigma, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizomatous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
