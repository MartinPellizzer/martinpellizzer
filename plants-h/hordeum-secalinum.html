Hordeum secalinum

Hordeum secalinum, commonly known as barley grass, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in temperate climates and is characterized by its flat, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. Hordeum secalinum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

It is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a nutritional supplement due to its high concentration of nutrients.
