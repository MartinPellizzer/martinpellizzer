Hypericum pibairense

Hypericum pibairense, commonly known as the Pibairensis St. John's Wort, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in forested areas and is known for its medicinal properties, though it is less commonly cultivated than other St. John's wort species.

Its botanical features include opposite leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer.
