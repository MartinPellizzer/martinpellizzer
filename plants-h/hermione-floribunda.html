Hermione floribunda

Hermione floribunda, commonly known as the showy bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
