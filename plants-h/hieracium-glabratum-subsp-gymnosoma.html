Hieracium glabratum subsp. gymnosoma

Hieracium glabratum subsp.

gymnosoma, commonly known as the smooth hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually hairless and have a slightly rough texture, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is considered a common weed in disturbed habitats and has a widespread distribution across its native range.
