Hibiscus syriacus var. variegatus

Hibiscus syriacus var. variegatus, commonly known as the variegated Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in late summer. The leaves are ovate with a distinctive variegated pattern of white and green, adding visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

This plant is relatively low maintenance and resistant to most pests and diseases.
