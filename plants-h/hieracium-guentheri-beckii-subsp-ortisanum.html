Hieracium guentheri-beckii subsp. ortisanum

Hieracium guentheri-beckii subsp.

ortisanum, commonly known as the ortisan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads that produce yellowish to reddish flowers.
