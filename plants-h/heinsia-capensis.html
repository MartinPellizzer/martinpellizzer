Heinsia capensis

Heinsia capensis, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Heinsia capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping and garden design.
