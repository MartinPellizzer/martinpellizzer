Helichrysum flanaganii

Helichrysum flanaganii, commonly known as the Flanagan's everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often mistaken for daisies. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Helichrysum flanaganii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting flowers.
