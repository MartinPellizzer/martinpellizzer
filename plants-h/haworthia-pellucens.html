Haworthia pellucens

Haworthia pellucens, commonly known as the glass plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense rosette of fleshy, translucent leaves that resemble glass, giving it its common name. The leaves are typically blue-green in color with white to pale green markings. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
