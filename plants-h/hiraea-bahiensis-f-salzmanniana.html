Hiraea bahiensis f. salzmanniana

Hiraea bahiensis f. salzmanniana, commonly known as the Salzmann's hiraea, is a rare and striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, colorful inflorescences that resemble small, clustered lanterns.
