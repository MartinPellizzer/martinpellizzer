Habenaria rechingeri

Habenaria rechingeri, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green to reddish-brown rhizomes and its long, spirally arranged leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky areas.

Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it a distinctive member of the Orchidaceae family.
