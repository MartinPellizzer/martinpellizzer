Hieracium glaucinum subsp. chensianum

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

chensianum, commonly known as Chen's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia, including China. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of both ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.
