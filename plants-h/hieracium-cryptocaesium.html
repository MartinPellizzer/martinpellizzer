Hieracium cryptocaesium

Hieracium cryptocaesium, commonly known as the cryptic hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in several European countries.
