Hieracium incisum subsp. neilreichii

Hieracium incisum subsp.

neilreichii, commonly known as the Neilreich's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower.
