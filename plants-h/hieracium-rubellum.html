Hieracium rubellum

Hieracium rubellum, commonly known as the red hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

The flowers are arranged in a dense inflorescence, making it a noticeable presence in its natural habitat.
