Haworthia limifolia var. glaucophylla

Haworthia limifolia var. glaucophylla, commonly known as the blue-leafed Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish or pale green hue. The leaves are typically adorned with white or pale markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
