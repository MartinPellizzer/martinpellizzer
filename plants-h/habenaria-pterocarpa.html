Habenaria pterocarpa

Habenaria pterocarpa, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features long, strap-like leaves and has a complex root system adapted to its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The orchid's flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, contributing to its ecological significance in its native range.
