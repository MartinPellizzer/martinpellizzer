Hebeandra celastroides

Hebeandra celastroides, commonly known as the New Zealand hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
