Helichrysum libanotis

Helichrysum libanotis, commonly known as the Lebanon everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, yellow to golden-orange flowers that are often arranged in dense, compact clusters. The plant has silver-gray foliage with narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum libanotis is also valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
