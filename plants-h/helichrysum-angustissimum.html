Helichrysum angustissimum

Helichrysum angustissimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant is known for its aromatic foliage and is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its dried flowers are used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting appearance.
