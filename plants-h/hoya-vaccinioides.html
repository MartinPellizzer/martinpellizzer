Hoya vaccinioides

Hoya vaccinioides, commonly known as the "Chinese wax plant," is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped, white flowers with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hoya vaccinioides is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
