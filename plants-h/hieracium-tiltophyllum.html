Hieracium tiltophyllum

Hieracium tiltophyllum, commonly known as the Tiltophylus hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a single, upright stem that bears yellowish to brownish composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has fibrous roots and produces achenes as its seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare species in certain regions.
