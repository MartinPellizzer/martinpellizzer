Heptoneurum

Heptoneurum, commonly known as the bluebell of the genus, is a member of the Ericaceae family.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and arranged in a compact, cushion-like structure.

Heptoneurum thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.
