Hieracium pallescens subsp. pseudeversianum

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

pseudeversianum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are usually grey-green in color, with a rough, hairy texture. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, characteristic of the Asteraceae family.

It is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
