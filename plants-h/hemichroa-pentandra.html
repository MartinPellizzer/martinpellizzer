Hemichroa pentandra

Hemichroa pentandra, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a shrub or small tree, typically growing up to 3 meters in height. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.
