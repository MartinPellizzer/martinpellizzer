Hieracium camkorijense subsp. alatavicum

Hieracium camkorijense subsp.

alatavicum, commonly known as the Alatavian hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes.

The plant has rough, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clustered inflorescences. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
