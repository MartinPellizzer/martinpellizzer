Huberantha nitidissima

Huberantha nitidissima, commonly known as the glossy leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, waxy leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are glossy and elongated, while its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with a distinctive lip petal.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
