Holosteum hirsutum

Holosteum hirsutum, commonly known as the hairy holosteum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland habitats. The plant features distinctive hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, often with a greenish-yellow color.

Holosteum hirsutum is known for its ability to thrive in dry conditions, making it a hardy species in arid environments.
