Haloragis heterophylla var. ceratophylla

Haloragis heterophylla var. ceratophylla, commonly known as the pink mistflower, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy, pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually alternate and may vary in shape depending on light conditions.

The species is part of the Haloragaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
