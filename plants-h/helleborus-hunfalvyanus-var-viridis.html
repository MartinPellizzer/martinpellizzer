Helleborus hunfalvyanus var. viridis

Helleborus hunfalvyanus var. viridis, commonly known as the green-flowered hellebore, is a perennial plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces striking, waxy flowers in shades of green, white, and pale yellow, often with a distinctive central green spot. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
