Holothrix parviflora

Holothrix parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered holothrix, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems and small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Holothrix parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
