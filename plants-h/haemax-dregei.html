Haemax dregei

Haemax dregei, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white, snow-like flowers that bloom in winter. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens. Its aromatic leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory properties.

Haemax dregei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
