Hymenoxys chrysanthemoides subsp. osterhoutii

Hymenoxys chrysanthemoides subsp.

osterhoutii, commonly known as the Osterhout's hymenoxys, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant produces yellow to orange ray flowers and dark brown disk flowers, which are typical of the genus Hymenoxys. It typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
