Hypericum androsaemum var. dilatatum

Hypericum androsaemum var. dilatatum, commonly known as the broadleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, branched stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom from June to August. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a hardy plant suitable for gardens and natural habitats.

This variant is distinguished by its broader leaves compared to the typical varietal form.
