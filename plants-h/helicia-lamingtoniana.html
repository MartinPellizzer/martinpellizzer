Helicia lamingtoniana

Helicia lamingtoniana, commonly known as the Lamington helenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its fruits are bright red berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds.

Helicia lamingtoniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
