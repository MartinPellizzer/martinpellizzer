Habranthus saipinensis

Habranthus saipinensis, commonly known as the Saipan starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Northern Mariana Islands.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its star-shaped, showy blue to violet flowers. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
