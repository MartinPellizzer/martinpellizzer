Habenaria richardii

Habenaria richardii, commonly known as the Richard's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that allow it to spread gradually over time. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
