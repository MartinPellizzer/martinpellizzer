Habenaria longa

Habenaria longa, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown spots. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Habenaria longa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
