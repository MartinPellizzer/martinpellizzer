Hieracium sabaudum subsp. seguieri

Hieracium sabaudum subsp.

seguieri, commonly known as the Seguier's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different morphology compared to other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
