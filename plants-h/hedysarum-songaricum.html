Hedysarum songaricum

Hedysarum songaricum, commonly known as the Songarica vetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces elongated pods containing small, dark seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in steppe and grassland habitats.

Hedysarum songaricum is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
