Helianthemum nummularium subsp. ovatum

Helianthemum nummularium subsp.

ovatum, commonly known as the silver carpet cushion plant, is a low-growing, mat-forming shrub native to southern Europe. It features dense clusters of small, rounded, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces yellow flowers in early summer, which are typically borne on short, hairy stalks. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
