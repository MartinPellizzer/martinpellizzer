Henslowia rubiflora

Henslowia rubiflora, commonly known as the red-flowered henslowia, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family, native to the tropical regions of South America.

This herbaceous plant is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically ovate and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms. Henslowia rubiflora thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
