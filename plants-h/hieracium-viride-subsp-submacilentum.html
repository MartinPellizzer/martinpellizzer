Hieracium viride subsp. submacilentum

Hieracium viride subsp.

submacilentum, commonly known as green hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are often reddish in color. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

The plant is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in disturbed soils.
