Hakea armigera

Hakea armigera, commonly known as the red hakea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, needle-like leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Hakea armigera is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
