Holmskioldia mira

Holmskioldia mira, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, round, green berries that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a notable addition to tropical and subtropical landscapes.
