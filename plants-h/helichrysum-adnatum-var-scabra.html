Helichrysum adnatum var. scabra

Helichrysum adnatum var. scabra, commonly known as the rough helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry stems and small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are typically narrow and covered with fine, stiff hairs, giving the plant a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
