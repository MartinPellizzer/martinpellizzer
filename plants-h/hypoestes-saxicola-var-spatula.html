Hypoestes saxicola var. spatula

Hypoestes saxicola var. spatula, commonly known as the spatulate hypoestes, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
