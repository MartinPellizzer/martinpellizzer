Heinsia lindenioides

Heinsia lindenioides, commonly known as the silverleaf heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, leathery leaves that give it a distinctive silver appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Heinsia lindenioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and unique foliage.
