Hosta tortifrons

Hosta tortifrons, commonly known as the warty leaf hosta, is a species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its broad, wavy, and textured leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers on slender stalks during the summer months. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shade gardens.

Its unique foliage makes it a standout plant in the hosta family.
