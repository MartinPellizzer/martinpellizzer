Hieracium digestum

Hieracium digestum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering stems that bear yellow flowers in a single head. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and its flowers are typically surrounded by pale green or yellowish involucres.

Hieracium digestum is an annual or biennial plant, and it can sometimes be invasive in certain regions.
