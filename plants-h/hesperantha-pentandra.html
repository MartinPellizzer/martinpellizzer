Hesperantha pentandra

Hesperantha pentandra, commonly known as the purple bell orchid, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal or mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Hesperantha pentandra is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
