Haworthia reticulata var. reticulata

Haworthia reticulata var. reticulata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit and its distinctive, dark green leaves with white to pale yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
