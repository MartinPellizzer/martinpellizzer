Huernia asirensis

Huernia asirensis, commonly known as the Asirensis Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
