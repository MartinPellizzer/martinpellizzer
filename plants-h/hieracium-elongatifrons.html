Hieracium elongatifrons

Hieracium elongatifrons, commonly known as the elongated thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, covered with dense, spiny leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in composite flower heads, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas.

Hieracium elongatifrons is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
