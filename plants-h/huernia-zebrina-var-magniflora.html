Huernia zebrina var. magniflora

Huernia zebrina var. magniflora, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a cylindrical stem with distinctive zebra-like striping, which is a key botanical characteristic. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is adapted to arid environments, with thick, fleshy stems that store water.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements in cultivation.
