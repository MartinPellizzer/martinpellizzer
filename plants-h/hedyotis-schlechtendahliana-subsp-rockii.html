Hedyotis schlechtendahliana subsp. rockii

Hedyotis schlechtendahliana subsp.

rockii, commonly known as Rock's smallleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is a perennial herb with slender, ascending stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale pink flowers, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
