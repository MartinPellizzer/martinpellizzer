Hypertelis verrucosa

Hypertelis verrucosa, commonly known as the roughleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

Hypertelis verrucosa is well-suited to sandy soils and can thrive in low-water conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
