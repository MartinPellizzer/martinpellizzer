Hieracium uluthrix

Hieracium uluthrix, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are often lobed and can form dense colonies.

Hieracium uluthrix is an invasive species in some regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
