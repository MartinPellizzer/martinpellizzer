Hippeastrum anomalum

Hippeastrum anomalum, commonly known as the red-flowered amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and glossy. It is a bulbous plant that thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

Hippeastrum anomalum is popular as a decorative plant due to its dramatic floral display.
