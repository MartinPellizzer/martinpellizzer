Helianthemum montanum subsp. viride

Helianthemum montanum subsp.

viride, commonly known as the green rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically green, adapted to survive in dry, rocky environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact form and vibrant flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
