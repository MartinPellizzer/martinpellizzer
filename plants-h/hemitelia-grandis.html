Hemitelia grandis

Hemitelia grandis, commonly known as the giant hemitelia, is a striking plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine that can grow to impressive lengths, often reaching several meters in height. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. Its flowers are showy, with bright yellow petals that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Hemitelia grandis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its traditional uses in local medicine.
