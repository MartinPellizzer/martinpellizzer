Huernia × albomaculata

Huernia × albomaculata, commonly known as the variegated horned orchid, is a hybrid succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its unique, horn-like tubercles and fleshy, ribbed stems. The plant produces striking yellow or orange flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically green with white or cream markings, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
