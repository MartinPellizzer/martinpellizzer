Hieracium neosyllectum

Hieracium neosyllectum, commonly known as the New Zealand hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and milky sap. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, showcasing a rosette of basal leaves. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat, disc-like structure, with ray florets that are often absent or reduced.

Hieracium neosyllectum is known for its invasive potential in certain regions outside its native range.
