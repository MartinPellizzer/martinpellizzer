Hieracium obtusifrons

Hieracium obtusifrons, commonly known as the blunt-fronded hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces numerous small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Its growth habit is typically prostrate or spreading, with stems that root at the nodes.
