Hoya pubicenta

Hoya pubicenta, commonly known as the coral hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing plant with thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Hoya pubicenta is known for its ability to thrive in low light conditions and requires well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
