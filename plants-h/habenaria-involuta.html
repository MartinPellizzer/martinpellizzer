Habenaria involuta

Habenaria involuta, commonly known as the twisted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single or few leaves, and its flowers are typically arranged in a loose spike. The flowers are usually pale green to white with a distinctive, twisted labellum that gives the species its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
