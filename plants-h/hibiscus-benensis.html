Hibiscus benensis

Hibiscus benensis, commonly known as the Benin hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Hibiscus benensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in local cultures.
