Hieracium argentimontanum

Hieracium argentimontanum, commonly known as silver mountain hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
