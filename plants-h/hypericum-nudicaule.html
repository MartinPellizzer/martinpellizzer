Hypericum nudicaule

Hypericum nudicaule, commonly known as the lesser St. John's wort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, yellow, four-petaled flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and rocky areas.

Hypericum nudicaule is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
