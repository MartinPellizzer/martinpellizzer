Hedychloe

Hedychloe, commonly known as sweet grass, is a genus of flowering plants in the grass family.

It is characterized by its fine, silky foliage and delicate inflorescences. Hedychloe species are often found in moist, temperate climates and are valued for their ornamental beauty. The plant is also noted for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine and rituals.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for use in lawns and as a ground cover.
