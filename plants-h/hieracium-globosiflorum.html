Hieracium globosiflorum

Hieracium globosiflorum, commonly known as the globehead hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its compact, globular inflorescence composed of numerous small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often glandular, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
