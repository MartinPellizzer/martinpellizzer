Hieracium cerinthiforme var. hartii

Hieracium cerinthiforme var. hartii, commonly known as Hart's yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in summer, and the leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy texture.

It is often found in disturbed habitats and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some regions.
