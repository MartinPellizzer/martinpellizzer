Hypochaeris clarionoides

Hypochaeris clarionoides, commonly known as the Clarion yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields, and it thrives in full sun. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and early summer, followed by seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Hypochaeris clarionoides is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth habit and ability to colonize various habitats.
