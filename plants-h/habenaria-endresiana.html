Habenaria endresiana

Habenaria endresiana, commonly known as the Endres' orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green to yellowish flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown speckles. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.

Habenaria endresiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
