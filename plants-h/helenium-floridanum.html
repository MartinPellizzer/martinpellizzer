Helenium floridanum

Helenium floridanum, commonly known as the Florida sneezeweed, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright orange to yellow ray flowers and dark brown disk flowers, which bloom from late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds, thriving in moist to wet soil conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight hairy texture.

Helenium floridanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
