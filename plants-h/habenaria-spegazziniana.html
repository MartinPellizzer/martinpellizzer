Habenaria spegazziniana

Habenaria spegazziniana, commonly known as the Spegazzini's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America, particularly Argentina and Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. Its roots are fibrous, and it has long, strap-like leaves that help in photosynthesis.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts.
