Hieracium macrolepis

Hieracium macrolepis, commonly known as the large-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium macrolepis is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
