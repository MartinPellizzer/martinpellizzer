Hedera jackiana

Hedera jackiana, commonly known as the Chinese ivy, is a climbing evergreen vine native to China.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are typically heart-shaped or oval in form. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded environments and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Hedera jackiana is relatively low maintenance and can be grown in containers or as a ground cover.
