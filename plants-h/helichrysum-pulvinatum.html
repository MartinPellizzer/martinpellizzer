Helichrysum pulvinatum

Helichrysum pulvinatum, commonly known as the golden bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its native arid environment.

Helichrysum pulvinatum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
