Hosta yingeri

Hosta yingeri, commonly known as the Yinger's plantain, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, medium-green leaves that are broad and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, upright stems. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta yingeri is valued for its ornamental foliage and is a popular choice for woodland gardens.
