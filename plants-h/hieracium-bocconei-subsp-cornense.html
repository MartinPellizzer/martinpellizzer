Hieracium bocconei subsp. cornense

Hieracium bocconei subsp.

cornense, commonly known as Cornish hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the British Isles. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
