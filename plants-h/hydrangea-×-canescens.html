Hydrangea × canescens

Hydrangea × canescens, commonly known as the panicle hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its cone-shaped flower clusters, which range in color from white to pink or red depending on soil pH. The plant produces showy inflorescences that develop from clusters of small, sterile flowers. Its leaves are typically dark green and turn red or orange in the fall.

Hydrangea × canescens is well-suited for partial shade and thrives in moist, well-drained soil.
