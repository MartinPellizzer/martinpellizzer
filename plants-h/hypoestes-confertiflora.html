Hypoestes confertiflora

Hypoestes confertiflora, commonly known as the "Magic Bean Plant," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its striking, colorful bracts that resemble small flowers. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are often overshadowed by the vibrant, showy bracts. Its leaves are typically ovate to lance-shaped and have a smooth texture.

Hypoestes confertiflora is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
