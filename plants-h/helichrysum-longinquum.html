Helichrysum longinquum

Helichrysum longinquum, commonly known as the everlasting helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow to orange-colored flowers that resemble daisies. The plant is known for its long-lasting blooms, which retain their color and form even when dried, making it popular in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its foliage is typically silver or gray-green, adding visual interest to gardens and landscapes.
