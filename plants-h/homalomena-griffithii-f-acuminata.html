Homalomena griffithii f. acuminata

Homalomena griffithii f. acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata varietal of the arrowhead plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in spadices, a characteristic feature of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
