Helianthemum nummularium f. alboplenum

Helianthemum nummularium f. alboplenum, commonly known as the white-leaved rockrose, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to southern Europe.

It features rounded, silver-gray leaves that are densely packed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early summer, which are typically borne on short stems. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is drought-tolerant.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in sunny, arid conditions.
