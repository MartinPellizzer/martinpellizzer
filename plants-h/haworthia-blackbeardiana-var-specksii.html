Haworthia blackbeardiana var. specksii

Haworthia blackbeardiana var. specksii, commonly known as the Blackbeard Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with striking white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
