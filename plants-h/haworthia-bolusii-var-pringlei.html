Haworthia bolusii var. pringlei

Haworthia bolusii var. pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped foliage. The leaves are typically fleshy and green, often marked with distinctive white or pale markings. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care in both indoor and outdoor settings.
