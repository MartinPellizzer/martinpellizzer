Hieracium neobenascanum

Hieracium neobenascanum, commonly known as the New Zealand hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous root system. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense colonies, making it an invasive species in some regions.
