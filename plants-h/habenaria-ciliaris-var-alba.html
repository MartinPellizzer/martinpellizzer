Habenaria ciliaris var. alba

Habenaria ciliaris var. alba, commonly known as the white-tipped orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a slender rhizome and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces tall, erect stems bearing numerous small, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
