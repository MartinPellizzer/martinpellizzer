Hieracium bubanianum

Hieracium bubanianum, commonly known as the Buban's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.

Hieracium bubanianum is often found in disturbed habitats and can spread vegetatively through rhizomes.
