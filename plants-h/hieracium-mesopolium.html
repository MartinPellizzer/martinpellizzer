Hieracium mesopolium

Hieracium mesopolium, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, and its stems are upright with a few branches.

Hieracium mesopolium is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
