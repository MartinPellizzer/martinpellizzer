Hieracium hastifrons

Hieracium hastifrons, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

It is often considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive growth.
