Hieraciodes pinnatifida

Hieraciodes pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hieraciodes, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, opposite leaves that are pinnately divided. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It is native to parts of Australia and thrives in dry, sunny environments.
