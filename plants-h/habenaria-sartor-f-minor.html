Habenaria sartor f. minor

Habenaria sartor f. minor, commonly known as the lesser sartor orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces a single spike of flowers with three to five blooms.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
