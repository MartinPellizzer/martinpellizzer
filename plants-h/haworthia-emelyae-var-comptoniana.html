Haworthia emelyae var. comptoniana

Haworthia emelyae var. comptoniana, commonly known as the Compton's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with distinctive white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
