Hieracium nigrescens subsp. omangii

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

omangii, commonly known as the Omang's hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of South America. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellowish to reddish flowers in clusters. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found in areas with poor soil and harsh climatic conditions.

Due to its restricted distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its range.
