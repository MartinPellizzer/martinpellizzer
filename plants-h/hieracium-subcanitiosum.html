Hieracium subcanitiosum

Hieracium subcanitiosum, commonly known as the white-haired hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and basal leaves that are often grayish-white in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
