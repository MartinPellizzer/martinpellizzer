Helichrysum floccosum

Helichrysum floccosum, commonly known as the woolly helichrysum, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features soft, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy, gray-green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its drought tolerance and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
