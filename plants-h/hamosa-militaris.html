Hamosa militaris

Hamosa militaris, commonly known as the soldier's shield, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, shield-shaped leaves and clusters of delicate white or pale blue flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its historical use in traditional medicine is noted for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.

Hamosa militaris is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid conditions.
