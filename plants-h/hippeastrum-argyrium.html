Hippeastrum argyrium

Hippeastrum argyrium, commonly known as the silver hippeastrum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically displaying white or pale pink petals. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow stamens that contrast beautifully with its vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum argyrium is a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its dramatic floral display and relatively easy care requirements.
