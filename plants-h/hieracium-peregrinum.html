Hieracium peregrinum

Hieracium peregrinum, commonly known as the wandering soldier, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy, alternate leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant is often found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands.

It is an annual or biennial herb that produces numerous small seeds, facilitating its widespread distribution.
