Haworthia crystallina

Haworthia crystallina, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white or yellow tubercles that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
