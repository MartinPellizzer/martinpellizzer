Hereroa fimbriata

Hereroa fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fringed petals that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Hereroa fimbriata belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along riverbanks.
