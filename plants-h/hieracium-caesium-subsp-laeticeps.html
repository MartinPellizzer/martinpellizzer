Hieracium caesium subsp. laeticeps

Hieracium caesium subsp.

laeticeps, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces achenes as its fruits.

Its botanical features make it a common sight in temperate regions.
