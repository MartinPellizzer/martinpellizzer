Hieracium dentatum subsp. pseudoporrectum

Hieracium dentatum subsp.

pseudoporrectum, commonly known as the slender spearthistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and southern Europe. It is characterized by its upright stems and lanceolate leaves with serrated edges.

The plant produces yellow flowers in composite flower heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Due to its prickly foliage, it is often considered a weed in agricultural areas.
