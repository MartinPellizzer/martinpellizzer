Houttuynia emeiensis

Houttuynia emeiensis, commonly known as the Emei mountain pepper plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Sarcodactylaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats.

Houttuynia emeiensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
