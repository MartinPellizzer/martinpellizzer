Hieracium laurae

Hieracium laurae, commonly known as Laura's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are yellow to orange in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Hieracium laurae is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many temperate regions.
