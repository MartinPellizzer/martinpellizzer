Hieracium amplexicaule subsp. leptocladum

Hieracium amplexicaule subsp.

leptocladum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks, preferring well-drained soils. It features upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy.

Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head, surrounded by a ring of phyllaries.
