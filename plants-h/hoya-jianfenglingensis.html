Hoya jianfenglingensis

Hoya jianfenglingensis, commonly known as the Jianfengling hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its waxy, velvety leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale pink blooms that typically appear in summer. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.

Hoya jianfenglingensis thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
