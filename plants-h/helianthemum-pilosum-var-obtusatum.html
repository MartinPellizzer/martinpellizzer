Helianthemum pilosum var. obtusatum

Helianthemum pilosum var. obtusatum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
