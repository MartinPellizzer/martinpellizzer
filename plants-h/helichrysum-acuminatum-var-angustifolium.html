Helichrysum acuminatum var. angustifolium

Helichrysum acuminatum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers and narrow, linear leaves. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its foliage is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting nature.

It produces small, fluffy seed heads that aid in wind pollination.
