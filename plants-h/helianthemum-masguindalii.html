Helianthemum masguindalii

Helianthemum masguindalii, commonly known as the Massanet's rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding vibrant color to rocky or coastal landscapes. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand dry and sunny conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal gardens.
