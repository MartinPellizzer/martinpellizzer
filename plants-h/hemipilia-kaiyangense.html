Hemipilia kaiyangense

Hemipilia kaiyangense, commonly known as the Kaiyang orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
