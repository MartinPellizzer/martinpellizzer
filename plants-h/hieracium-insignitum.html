Hieracium insignitum

Hieracium insignitum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium insignitum is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain regions.
