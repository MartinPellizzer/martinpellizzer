Herya

Herya, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. Herya is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and is relatively easy to care for with proper watering and light conditions.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for indoor gardening and container planting.
