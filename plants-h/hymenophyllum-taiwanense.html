Hymenophyllum taiwanense

Hymenophyllum taiwanense, commonly known as the Taiwan shield fern, is a small, delicate fern native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, scale-like leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in forest undergrowth. Its spores are released from specialized structures called sporangia, which are clustered on the underside of its leaves.

Hymenophyllum taiwanense is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its ability to thrive in moist, cool conditions.
