Hypericum cardonae

Hypericum cardonae, commonly known as Canary Island St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are opposite, oval, and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, red-orange fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal habitats.

Hypericum cardonae is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional medicinal uses.
