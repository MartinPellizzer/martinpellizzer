Hieracium lachenalii subsp. obscuriceps

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

obscuriceps, commonly known as the dark-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, with both ray and disc florets. Its leaves are typically lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and often reddish in color.

This subspecies is distinguished by its dark, almost black, flower heads, which give it its common name.
