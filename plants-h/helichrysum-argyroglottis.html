Helichrysum argyroglottis

Helichrysum argyroglottis, commonly known as silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
