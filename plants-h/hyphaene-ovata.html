Hyphaene ovata

Hyphaene ovata, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a species of palm native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves and a tall, slender trunk that can reach heights of up to 15 meters. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in sandy or dry soils. Its fruit produces a nutritious seed that is often consumed by both humans and animals.
