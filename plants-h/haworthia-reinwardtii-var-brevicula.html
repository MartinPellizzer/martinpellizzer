Haworthia reinwardtii var. brevicula

Haworthia reinwardtii var. brevicula, commonly known as the miniature Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pinkish markings. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
