Hieracium glabellum

Hieracium glabellum, commonly known as the smooth hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has smooth, hairless stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

Hieracium glabellum is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
