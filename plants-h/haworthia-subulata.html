Haworthia subulata

Haworthia subulata, commonly known as the needle Haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of sharp, spiky, needle-like leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
