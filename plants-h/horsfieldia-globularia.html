Horsfieldia globularia

Horsfieldia globularia, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, rounded flower spike that resembles a small globe, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Horsfieldia globularia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
