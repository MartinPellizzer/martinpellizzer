Hibiscus malangensis

Hibiscus malangensis, commonly known as the Malang hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. Hibiscus malangensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
