Helichrysum citrispinum var. armatum

Helichrysum citrispinum var. armatum, commonly known as the lemon thyme helichrysum, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features aromatic, silver-gray leaves with a lemony scent, and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its foliage is also valued for its fragrance and potential medicinal properties.
