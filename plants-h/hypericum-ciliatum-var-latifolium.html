Hypericum ciliatum var. latifolium

Hypericum ciliatum var. latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in the understory of forests. It has a woody stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
