Hieracium tonsiceps

Hieracium tonsiceps, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Native to Europe, it is often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the hawkweed genus.
