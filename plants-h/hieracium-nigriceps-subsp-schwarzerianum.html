Hieracium nigriceps subsp. schwarzerianum

Hieracium nigriceps subsp.

schwarzerianum, commonly known as the black-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dark purple to black flower heads, which are surrounded by yellow ray florets.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and grasslands. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces and a robust root system.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in open habitats with adequate sunlight.
