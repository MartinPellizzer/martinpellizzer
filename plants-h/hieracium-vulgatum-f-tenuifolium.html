Hieracium vulgatum f. tenuifolium

Hieracium vulgatum f. tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and bright yellow composite flowers. This perennial herb typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe. The plant has small, hairy stems and produces achenes as its fruits.

It is often found in open habitats and is relatively common in temperate regions.
