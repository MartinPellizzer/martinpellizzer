Helianthemum Oelandicum Subsp. Orientale

Helianthemum oelandicum subsp. orientale is a hardy evergreen shrub known for its vibrant yellow flowers and compact growth habit. Native to the rocky coastal regions of southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. This plant is prized for its drought tolerance and ability to flourish in challenging environments. Its small, fragrant blooms add a striking touch to rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Despite its modest size, it holds a special place in botanical gardens and among horticultural enthusiasts.
