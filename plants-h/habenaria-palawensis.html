Habenaria palawensis

Habenaria palawensis, commonly known as the Palawan butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient absorption.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
