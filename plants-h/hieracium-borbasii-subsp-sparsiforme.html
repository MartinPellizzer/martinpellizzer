Hieracium borbasii subsp. sparsiforme

Hieracium borbasii subsp.

sparsiforme, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and narrow, hairy leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces a single flowering stem.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
