Hieracium florentinum subsp. bellovense

Hieracium florentinum subsp.

bellovense, commonly known as the Bellovense hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils.

The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single terminal head. It has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture.

This subspecies is characterized by its distinct floral structure and is part of the Asteraceae family.
