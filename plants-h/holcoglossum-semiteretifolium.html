Holcoglossum semiteretifolium

Holcoglossum semiteretifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed holcoglossum, is a rare and unique orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and produces striking, violet-colored flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil. Its conservation status is of concern due to habitat loss and over-collection.

Holcoglossum semiteretifolium is of particular interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts due to its specialized morphology and limited distribution.
