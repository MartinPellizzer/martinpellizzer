Hibiscus borbonicus

Hibiscus borbonicus, commonly known as the Madagascar hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Hibiscus borbonicus is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.
