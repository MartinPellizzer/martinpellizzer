Hortsmania

Hortsmania, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that come in a variety of hues. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties. Hortsmania thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and medicinal value make it a popular choice among horticultural enthusiasts.
