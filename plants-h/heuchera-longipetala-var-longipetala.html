Heuchera longipetala var. longipetala

Heuchera longipetala var. longipetala, commonly known as the long-petaled coral bells, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features upright, branching stems covered with broad, heart-shaped leaves that range in color from deep burgundy to green. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white during late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental foliage and delicate blooms make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
