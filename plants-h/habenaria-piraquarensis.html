Habenaria piraquarensis

Habenaria piraquarensis, commonly known as the Piraquarensis orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to specific regions in South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along stream banks. Its floral structure includes a single, showy bloom with a distinctive lip, a feature common in many orchid species.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant plant.
