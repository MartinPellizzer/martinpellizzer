Homalomena miqueliana var. truella

Homalomena miqueliana var. truella, commonly known as the Truella's ginger-leaf arum, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface and prominent veins. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its growth habit includes rhizomatous stems that can spread horizontally, making it suitable for tropical gardens or indoor cultivation.
