Heteropappus oldhamii

Heteropappus oldhamii, commonly known as the Oldham's heteropappus, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces both ray and disk flowers, with the ray flowers typically being yellow and the disk flowers reddish-brown. It has a low-growing habit with hairy, lance-shaped leaves.

Heteropappus oldhamii is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental potential in gardens.
