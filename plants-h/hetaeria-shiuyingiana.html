Hetaeria shiuyingiana

Hetaeria shiuyingiana, commonly known as the Chinese lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its elegant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and has linear, grass-like leaves. Its bulbs are the primary means of propagation, and it blooms during the summer months.

Hetaeria shiuyingiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
