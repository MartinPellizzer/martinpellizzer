Hieracium pyrrhanthes subsp. detonaticum

Hieracium pyrrhanthes subsp.

detonaticum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and dark green, hairy leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It produces a basal rosette of leaves with a flowering stem that bears a composite flower head.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in temperate regions.
