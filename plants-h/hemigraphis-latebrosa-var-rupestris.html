Hemigraphis latebrosa var. rupestris

Hemigraphis latebrosa var. rupestris, commonly known as the rock hemigraphis, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside, creating a striking visual contrast. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink or white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its ornamental foliage and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
