Halimium brasiliense f. latifolium

Halimium brasiliense f. latifolium, commonly known as the Brazilian rock rose, is a compact shrub native to Brazil.

It features small, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to its rugged appearance. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are adapted to arid environments, showcasing its succulent characteristics. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a desirable plant for drought-tolerant landscaping.
