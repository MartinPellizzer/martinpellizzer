Hieracium macrotonum

Hieracium macrotonum, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with large, heart-shaped basal leaves and hairy stems. Its flowers are yellow and bloom from late spring to early autumn, attracting various pollinators.

Hieracium macrotonum is known for its aggressive growth and can become invasive in certain regions.
