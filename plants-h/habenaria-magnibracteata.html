Habenaria magnibracteata

Habenaria magnibracteata, commonly known as the large-bract orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy orchid flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has broad, linear leaves and produces numerous spikelets that bear small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
