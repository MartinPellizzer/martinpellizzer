Habenaria montana

Habenaria montana, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in forested areas with high humidity and partial shade. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor.

Habenaria montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
