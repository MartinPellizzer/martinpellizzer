Hieracium comptellum

Hieracium comptellum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium comptellum is typically perennial and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
