Hakea Rubra

Hakea rubra is a striking evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia. It is known for its vibrant red flowers and its ability to thrive in dry, coastal conditions. This plant is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes where it plays an important ecological role. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Hakea rubra is also valued for its resilience and adaptability to harsh environments.
