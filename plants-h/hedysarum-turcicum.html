Hedysarum turcicum

Hedysarum turcicum, commonly known as Turkish vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compound leaves with three to seven elliptical leaflets and produces yellow to orange pea-like flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in open woodlands and rocky areas.

Hedysarum turcicum is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and use in ecological restoration projects.
