Helianthemum ruficomum var. bergevinii

Helianthemum ruficomum var. bergevinii, commonly known as the Bergevin rosemary, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine, fuzzy hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
