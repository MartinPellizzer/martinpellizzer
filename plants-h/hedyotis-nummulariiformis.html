Hedyotis nummulariiformis

Hedyotis nummulariiformis, commonly known as the silverleaf geranium, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, rounded, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hedyotis nummulariiformis is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
