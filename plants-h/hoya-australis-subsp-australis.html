Hoya australis subsp. australis

Hoya australis subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian Waffle Plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia. It is characterized by its thick, waxy, and often ribbed leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is a climbing or trailing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. Hoya australis subsp.

australis is relatively low maintenance and is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
