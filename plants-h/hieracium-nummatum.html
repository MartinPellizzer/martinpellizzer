Hieracium nummatum

Hieracium nummatum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium nummatum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
