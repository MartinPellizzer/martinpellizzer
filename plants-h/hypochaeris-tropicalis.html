Hypochaeris tropicalis

Hypochaeris tropicalis, commonly known as the tropical weed or golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite, hairy leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is typically found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in certain regions.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it both a useful forage plant and a potential weed.
