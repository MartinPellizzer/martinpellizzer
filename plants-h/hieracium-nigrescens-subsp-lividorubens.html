Hieracium nigrescens subsp. lividorubens

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

lividorubens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems that turn reddish-purple as the plant matures.

The leaves are lance-shaped, with a rough texture and often bear glandular dots. Its flowers are typically yellow, arranged in a flat-topped cluster, and bloom in late summer.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.
