Helichrysum argyrolepis

Helichrysum argyrolepis, commonly known as silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, yellowish flowers that resemble daisies. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates. Its name reflects the metallic sheen of its leaves and the long-lasting nature of its flowers, which retain their color when dried.

Helichrysum argyrolepis is often used in ornamental gardening and traditional medicine.
