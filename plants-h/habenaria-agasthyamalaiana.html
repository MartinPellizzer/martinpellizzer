Habenaria agasthyamalaiana

Habenaria agasthyamalaiana, commonly known as the Agasthyamala orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically blooms during the monsoon season, showcasing its adaptability to the humid tropical climate. It grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky substrates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
