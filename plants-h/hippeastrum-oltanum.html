Hippeastrum oltanum

Hippeastrum oltanum, commonly known as the Oltanum amaryllis, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a bulbous stem with broad, strap-like leaves that are often a deep green color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hippeastrum oltanum is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
