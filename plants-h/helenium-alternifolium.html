Helenium alternifolium

Helenium alternifolium, commonly known as the common sneezeweed, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, orange to red flower heads. The plant features narrow, alternate leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Helenium alternifolium is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Its flowers bloom in late summer to early fall, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
