Holarrhena crassifolia

Holarrhena crassifolia, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated pods containing flat, brown seeds. It is often found in dry, open woodlands and is valued for its durable wood.
