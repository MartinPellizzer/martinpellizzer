Hieracium oroelense

Hieracium oroelense, commonly known as the Oroel hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous tiny florets. Hieracium oroelense is notable for its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
