Hieracium caussile

Hieracium caussile, commonly known as the Causse hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and stems that bear yellow or orange flowers. The plant is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, thriving in sunny, dry environments. It has hairy leaves and stems, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Hieracium caussile is often found in meadows, rocky areas, and along roadsides.
