Hypoxis graminea

Hypoxis graminea, commonly known as the African yellowstar, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoxis graminea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
