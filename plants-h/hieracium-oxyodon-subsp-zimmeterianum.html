Hieracium oxyodon subsp. zimmeterianum

Hieracium oxyodon subsp.

zimmeterianum, commonly known as the Zimmeter's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges.
