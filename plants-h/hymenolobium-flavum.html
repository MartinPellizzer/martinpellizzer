Hymenolobium flavum

Hymenolobium flavum, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its showy, yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Hymenolobium flavum thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
