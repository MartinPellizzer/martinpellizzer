Hagenia abyssinica

Hagenia abyssinica, commonly known as the African firethorn, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, red berries that are edible for humans and animals. It thrives in high-altitude regions with well-drained soils and is often found in montane forests.

Hagenia abyssinica is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
