Hieracium Murorum Subsp. Obtextum

Hieracium murorum subsp. obtextum is a lesser-known member of the daisy family native to rocky and arid environments. This subspecies is distinguished by its dense, woolly foliage and compact growth habit. It thrives in poor soils and is often found in mountainous regions across Europe. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators. Its resilience and adaptability make it a fascinating subject for ecological study.
