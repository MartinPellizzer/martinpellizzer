Haworthia retusa var. heidelbergensis

Haworthia retusa var. heidelbergensis, commonly known as the Heidelberg Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, pale green leaves with prominent white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
