Haworthia tuberculata var. acuminata

Haworthia tuberculata var. acuminata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellow tubercles, giving it a distinctive zebra-like appearance. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It produces small, white or greenish flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
