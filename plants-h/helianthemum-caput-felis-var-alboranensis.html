Helianthemum caput-felis var. alboranensis

Helianthemum caput-felis var. alboranensis, commonly known as the Alboran Island rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Alboran Sea region.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy leaves adapted to arid conditions. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

This variety is notable for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
