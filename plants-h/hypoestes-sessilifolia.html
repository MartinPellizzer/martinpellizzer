Hypoestes sessilifolia

Hypoestes sessilifolia, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hypoestes sessilifolia is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
