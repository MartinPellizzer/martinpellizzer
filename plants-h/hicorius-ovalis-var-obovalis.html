Hicorius ovalis var. obovalis

Hicorius ovalis var. obovalis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hibiscus, is a perennial flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with silver on the underside. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
