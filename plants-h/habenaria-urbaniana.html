Habenaria urbaniana

Habenaria urbaniana, commonly known as the Urban's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pink or purple with intricate patterns, and it has linear, grass-like leaves.

Habenaria urbaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
