Hieracium Wiesbaurianum Subsp. Epiphaeum

Hieracium wiesbaurianum subsp. epiphaeum is a rare and distinctive member of the daisy family found in specific alpine habitats. It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and hairy leaves that help it survive in harsh environments. This subspecies is often overlooked due to its small size and subtle appearance. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect its dwindling populations from habitat loss and climate change. Understanding and preserving this plant is essential for maintaining biodiversity in mountain ecosystems.
