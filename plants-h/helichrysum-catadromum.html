Helichrysum catadromum

Helichrysum catadromum, commonly known as the yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain open throughout the day. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum catadromum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
