Hypericum geminiflorum subsp. simplicistylum

Hypericum geminiflorum subsp.

simplicistylum, commonly known as the simple-styled goldenseal, is a member of the Hypericaceae family. It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers.

The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is native to specific regions in North America, particularly in the southeastern United States.

Its botanical features make it a unique and relatively rare species within the genus Hypericum.
