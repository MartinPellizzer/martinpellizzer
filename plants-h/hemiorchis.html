Hemiorchis

Hemiorchis, commonly known as the orchid mantis, is a unique plant that combines features of both orchids and insects.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers that are often brightly colored and fragrant. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its stems are wiry and somewhat insect-like in appearance.

Hemiorchis is primarily grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and unusual morphology.
