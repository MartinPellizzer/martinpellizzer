Hydrangea oerstedii

Hydrangea oerstedii, commonly known as the mountain hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink or blue, depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage consists of large, ovate leaves that are dark green in color.

Hydrangea oerstedii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its seasonal blooms.
