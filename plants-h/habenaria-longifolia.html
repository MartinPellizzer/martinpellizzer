Habenaria longifolia

Habenaria longifolia, commonly known as the long-flowered orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is a herbaceous plant with erect, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It blooms during the rainy season, producing clusters of fragrant, showy flowers.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
