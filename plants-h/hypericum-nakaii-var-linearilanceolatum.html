Hypericum nakaii var. linearilanceolatum

Hypericum nakaii var. linearilanceolatum, commonly known as the narrow-lanceleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers with prominent stamens, typical of the Hypericaceae family. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
