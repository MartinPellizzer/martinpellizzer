Habenaria paxamorque

Habenaria paxamorque, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in a loose raceme. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
