Haworthia isabellae var. ligulata

Haworthia isabellae var. ligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically pale green to bluish-green in color. The leaves are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its low maintenance and unique appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
