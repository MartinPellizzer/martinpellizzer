Helleborus × sternii

Helleborus × sternii, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a hybrid evergreen perennial plant native to the Alps.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that remain throughout the year, and its flowers are typically white or pale pink, resembling roses. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped blooms in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Helleborus × sternii is also valued for its ability to bloom reliably in colder climates.
