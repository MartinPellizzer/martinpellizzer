Hordeum compressum

Hordeum compressum, commonly known as slender barley, is a species of grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny grains, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in moist, temperate environments and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
