Haemanthus arnoldianus

Haemanthus arnoldianus, commonly known as the red-stemmed haemanthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice among gardeners.
