Habenaria drepanopetala

Habenaria drepanopetala, commonly known as the sickle-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, sickle-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
