Herrea blanda

Herrea blanda, commonly known as the smoothleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Herrea blanda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
