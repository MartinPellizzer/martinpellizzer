Haworthia notabilis var. diversicolor

Haworthia notabilis var. diversicolor, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette shape and distinctive markings of white and green stripes on its fleshy, triangular leaves. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect light and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
