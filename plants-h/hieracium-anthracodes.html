Hieracium anthracodes

Hieracium anthracodes, commonly known as the black knapweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flower heads, which are surrounded by narrow, spiny bracts. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium anthracodes is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
