Helichrysum odoratissimum

Helichrysum odoratissimum, commonly known as the sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, golden-yellow flowers that retain their color even after drying, making it popular in floral arrangements. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Its aromatic foliage and long-lasting blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and herbal collections.

Helichrysum odoratissimum is also used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.
