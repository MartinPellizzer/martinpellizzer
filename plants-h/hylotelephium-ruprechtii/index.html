Hylotelephium Ruprechtii

Hylotelephium ruprechtii is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Europe and western Asia. Known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall, it adds a bold splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms make it both ornamental and easy to maintain. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked for its ecological benefits, supporting pollinators and adding biodiversity to the ecosystem.
