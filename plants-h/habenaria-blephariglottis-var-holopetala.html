Habenaria blephariglottis var. holopetala

Habenaria blephariglottis var. holopetala, commonly known as the broad-leafed helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, helmet-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes and pseudobulbs that store nutrients. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
