Haworthia dekenahii

Haworthia dekenahii, commonly known as the Deken's Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white to pale yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
