Heteropogon allionii

Heteropogon allionii, commonly known as the Allion's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Heteropogon allionii is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
