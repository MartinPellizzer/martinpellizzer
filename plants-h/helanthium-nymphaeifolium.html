Helanthium nymphaeifolium

Helanthium nymphaeifolium, commonly known as water shield, is a aquatic plant native to North America.

It has distinctive arrow-shaped leaves that are floating or submerged, depending on the water depth. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its roots form dense mats that help stabilize sediments in wetland environments.

Helanthium nymphaeifolium plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various invertebrates and fish.
