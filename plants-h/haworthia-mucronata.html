Haworthia mucronata

Haworthia mucronata, commonly known as the sharp-tipped Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched stems with small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
