Hermannia filifolia var. obusta

Hermannia filifolia var. obusta, commonly known as the robust filifoliate hermannia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in dense, terminal clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
