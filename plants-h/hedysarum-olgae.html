Hedysarum olgae

Hedysarum olgae, commonly known as the Olgan milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain several small, round seeds. Hedysarum olgae is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or gravelly soils.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
