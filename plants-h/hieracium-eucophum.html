Hieracium eucophum

Hieracium eucophum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a hairy texture, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Hieracium eucophum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
