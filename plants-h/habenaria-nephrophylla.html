Habenaria nephrophylla

Habenaria nephrophylla, commonly known as the kidney-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique kidney-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Habenaria nephrophylla is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
