Hymenocallis nutans

Hymenocallis nutans, commonly known as the nodding spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that droop downward, resembling spider-like blooms. The plant produces strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Hymenocallis nutans is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
