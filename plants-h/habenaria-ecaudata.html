Habenaria ecaudata

Habenaria ecaudata, commonly known as the tailless orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale green or white flowers that are arranged in a loose raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Habenaria ecaudata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
