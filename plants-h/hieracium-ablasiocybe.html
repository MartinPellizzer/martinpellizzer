Hieracium ablasiocybe

Hieracium ablasiocybe, commonly known as the blushing hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is found in temperate regions. It has a creeping growth habit with rhizomes that allow it to spread vegetatively.

Due to its invasive tendencies, it can outcompete native vegetation in certain ecosystems.
