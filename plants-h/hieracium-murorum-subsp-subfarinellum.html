Hieracium murorum subsp. subfarinellum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

subfarinellum, commonly known as the farinellum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a slightly rough texture.

It is found primarily in Europe and parts of western Asia.
