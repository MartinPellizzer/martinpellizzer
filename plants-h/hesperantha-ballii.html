Hesperantha ballii

Hesperantha ballii, commonly known as the Ball's African bell orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its elegant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Hesperantha ballii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
