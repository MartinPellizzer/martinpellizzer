Habenaria chloroceras

Habenaria chloroceras, commonly known as the green-veined orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves with prominent green veins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
