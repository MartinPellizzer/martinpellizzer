Homalomena griffithii var. sumatrana

Homalomena griffithii var. sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran arrowhead plant, is a tropical evergreen perennial belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Native to the Indonesian island of Sumatra, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
