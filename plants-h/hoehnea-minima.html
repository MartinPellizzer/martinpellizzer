Hoehnea minima

Hoehnea minima, commonly known as the small hoehnea, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Hoehnea minima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and blooms.
