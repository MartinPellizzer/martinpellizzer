Hoya gracilipes

Hoya gracilipes, commonly known as the spider plant hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The leaves are typically elliptical and have a smooth, glossy texture. This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental appeal.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
