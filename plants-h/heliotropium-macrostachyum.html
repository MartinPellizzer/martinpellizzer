Heliotropium macrostachyum

Heliotropium macrostachyum, commonly known as the giant heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or toothed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.

Its flowers emit a sweet, honey-like fragrance, attracting various pollinators.
