Hovea lanigera

Hovea lanigera, commonly known as the woolly hovea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the warmer months. Hovea lanigera is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable plant in native Australian flora.
