Hedyotis hermanniana

Hedyotis hermanniana, commonly known as the Hermann's geranium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, bright green, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Hedyotis hermanniana is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
