Hyoseris radiatifolia

Hyoseris radiatifolia, commonly known as the radiate leaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk, typical of the family. Its leaves are radiately arranged, giving it a distinctive appearance.
