Hieracium juranum subsp. stelliceps

Hieracium juranum subsp.

stelliceps, commonly known as the star-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a cluster of small flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are hairy and alternate, while the flowers are yellow with a distinctive star-like appearance.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
