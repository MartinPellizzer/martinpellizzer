Hieraciodes silhetensis

Hieraciodes silhetensis, commonly known as Silhet hill aster, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region of Bangladesh.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of tubular and ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes. It features a slender stem with opposite, lanceolate leaves that are often hairy.

Its showy yellow flowers bloom in the summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
