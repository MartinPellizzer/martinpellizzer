Helichrysum lentii

Helichrysum lentii, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, papery bracts that resemble small flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its foliage is aromatic and often used in herbal remedies.

Helichrysum lentii is also popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to retain color when dried.
