Hypolepis Repens

Hypolepis repens is a unique fern species known for its creeping growth habit and delicate fronds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions. This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to carpet the ground with lush, green foliage. Its rhizomatous structure allows it to spread easily, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Despite its beauty, Hypolepis repens remains relatively underappreciated in the broader horticultural community.
