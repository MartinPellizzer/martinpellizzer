Hedyotis orbiculata

Hedyotis orbiculata, commonly known as the globe moss or red root, is a small herbaceous plant native to various regions in Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are often reddish in color, and it produces tiny, white or greenish flowers clustered in umbels. Its roots are characterized by their red pigmentation, which gives the plant its common name.

Hedyotis orbiculata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
