Hieracium piliferum subsp. salanfense

Hieracium piliferum subsp.

salanfense, commonly known as the Salanfense hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is often found in the Pyrenees and surrounding regions, where it thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.
