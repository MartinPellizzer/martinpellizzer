Haworthiopsis scabra var. johanii

Haworthiopsis scabra var. johanii, commonly known as the Johanii Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of narrow, fleshy, blue-gray leaves with white markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces upright, branched inflorescences with small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
