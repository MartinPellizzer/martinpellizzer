Habenaria helferi

Habenaria helferi, commonly known as the Helfer's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its roots are often rhizomatous, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
