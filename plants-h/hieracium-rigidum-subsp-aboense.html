Hieracium rigidum subsp. aboense

Hieracium rigidum subsp.

aboense, commonly known as the Aboen hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and basal rosettes of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clustered inflorescences, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in nutrient-poor, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous habitats.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species in many areas.
