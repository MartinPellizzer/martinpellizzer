Hieracium pellitum subsp. brachysericum

Hieracium pellitum subsp.

brachysericum, commonly known as the short-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and is often found in disturbed or marginal areas.

Its botanical features make it a common yet ecologically significant species in its native range.
