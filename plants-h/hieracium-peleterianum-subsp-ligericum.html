Hieracium peleterianum subsp. ligericum

Hieracium peleterianum subsp.

ligericum, commonly known as the Ligericum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers with ray florets.

Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has a fibrous root system and is often found in temperate climates.

It is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive growth.
