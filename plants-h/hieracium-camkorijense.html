Hieracium camkorijense

Hieracium camkorijense, commonly known as the Camkorijen hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It features basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
