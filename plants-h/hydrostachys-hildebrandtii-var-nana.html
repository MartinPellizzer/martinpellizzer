Hydrostachys hildebrandtii var. nana

Hydrostachys hildebrandtii var. nana, commonly known as dwarf hydrostachys, is a compact, mat-forming succulent native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a dense, woolly coating. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its slow growth rate makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or as a ground cover in xeriscaped landscapes.
