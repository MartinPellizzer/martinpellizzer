Hieracium stictum

Hieracium stictum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium stictum is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its aggressive growth habit.
