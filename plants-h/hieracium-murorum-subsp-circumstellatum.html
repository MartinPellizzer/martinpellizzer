Hieracium murorum subsp. circumstellatum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

circumstellatum, commonly known as the starry hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in clusters.

The subspecies is distinguished by its finely divided, hairy leaves and the presence of small, star-shaped florets. It typically thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.

This plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.
