Hieracium tossianum

Hieracium tossianum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Hieracium tossianum is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to spread vegetatively.
