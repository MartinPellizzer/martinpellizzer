Hemerocallis dumortieri var. exaltata

Hemerocallis dumortieri var. exaltata, commonly known as the tall daylily, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces strap-like, dark green leaves that are narrow and linear. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Hemerocallis dumortieri var. exaltata is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
