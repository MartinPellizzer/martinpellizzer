Haworthia bolusii var. semiviva

Haworthia bolusii var. semiviva, commonly known as the dwarf Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white tubercles and a central spine. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
