Hieracium nigrescens subsp. elfstrandii

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

elfstrandii, commonly known as the Elfstrand hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rough texture and lobed margins.

This subspecies is native to southern Europe and is often found in regions with well-drained soils.
