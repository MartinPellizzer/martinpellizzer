Hieracium glaucum subsp. legitimum

Hieracium glaucum subsp.

legitimum, commonly known as the blue hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

The plant features bright yellow flower heads with ray florets that are often tinged with blue or purple. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower structure.
