Haworthia arabesqua

Haworthia arabesqua, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves with distinctive white or yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect light and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for succulent collections and decorative plantings.
