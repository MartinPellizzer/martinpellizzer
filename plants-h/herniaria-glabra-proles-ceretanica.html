Herniaria glabra proles ceretanica

Herniaria glabra proles ceretanica, commonly known as the smooth herniaria, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of tiny, white flowers.
