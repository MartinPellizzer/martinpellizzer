Heliconia Revoluta

Heliconia revoluta is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, curling bracts that resemble colorful flowers. Native to the wetlands and coastal areas of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, warm climates. This plant is a popular choice for ornamental gardens due to its unique appearance and ability to attract birds and insects. Its foliage is broad and lush, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy inflorescences. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and soil conditions to flourish.
