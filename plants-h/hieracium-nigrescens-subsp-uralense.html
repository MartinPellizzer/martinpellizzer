Hieracium nigrescens subsp. uralense

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

uralense, commonly known as the Ural hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It typically grows in dry meadows, rocky slopes, and steppe environments.

The plant has basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in the summer. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a rough texture, while the stems are upright and branching.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in harsh, arid conditions and is often found in mountainous regions.
