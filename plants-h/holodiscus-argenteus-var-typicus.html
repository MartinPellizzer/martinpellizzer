Holodiscus argenteus var. typicus

Holodiscus argenteus var. typicus, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The leaves are ovate, with a silvery-white underside that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.

The plant is also valued for its adaptability and resistance to pests and diseases.
