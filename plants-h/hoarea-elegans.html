Hoarea elegans

Hoarea elegans, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive and elegant appearance. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to provide shade in landscapes.
