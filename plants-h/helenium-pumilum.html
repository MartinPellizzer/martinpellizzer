Helenium pumilum

Helenium pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf sunflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, slender stems that bear bright orange to red ray flowers surrounded by yellow disk flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and blooms from late summer into early fall. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Helenium pumilum is well adapted to moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.
