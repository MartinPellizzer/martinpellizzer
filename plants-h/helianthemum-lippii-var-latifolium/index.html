Helianthemum Lippii Var. Latifolium

Broadleaf helianthemum, scientifically known as Helianthemum lippii var. latifolium, is a hardy evergreen shrub native to the rocky hills of southern Europe. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its broad, leathery leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers add both texture and visual interest to any garden. This plant is not only ornamental but also drought-resistant, requiring minimal maintenance once established. With its resilience and beauty, Broadleaf helianthemum is a versatile addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
