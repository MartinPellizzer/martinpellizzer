Hyperanthera pterygosperma

Hyperanthera pterygosperma, commonly known as the winged seed grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense clusters of small, spikelet-like flowers. The plant produces distinctive winged seeds, which aid in its dispersal by wind. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and horticultural applications.
