Hibiscus kokio subsp. kokio

Hibiscus kokio subsp.

kokio, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red.

The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a waxy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Hibiscus kokio subsp.

kokio is also valued for its cultural significance in Hawaiian traditions.
