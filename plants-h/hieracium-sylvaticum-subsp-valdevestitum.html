Hieracium sylvaticum subsp. valdevestitum

Hieracium sylvaticum subsp.

valdevestitum, commonly known as the woodland hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It features basal rosettes of serrated leaves and produces yellow flowers in the summer.
