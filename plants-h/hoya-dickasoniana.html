Hoya dickasoniana

Hoya dickasoniana, commonly known as the velvet tangerine hoya, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are usually a deep green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Hoya dickasoniana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
