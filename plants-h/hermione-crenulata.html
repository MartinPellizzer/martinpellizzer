Hermione crenulata

Hermione crenulata, commonly known as the silver leaf fern, is a striking evergreen plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features gracefully arching fronds that are covered in a silvery, waxy coating, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
