Hieracium erectiramum

Hieracium erectiramum, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
