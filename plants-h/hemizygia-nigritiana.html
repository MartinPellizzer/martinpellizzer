Hemizygia nigritiana

Hemizygia nigritiana, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round berries.

Hemizygia nigritiana is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
