Hieracium caesium var. imitans

Hieracium caesium var. imitans, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in meadows and disturbed soils.

Due to its invasive tendencies, it can outcompete native vegetation in certain regions.
