Hosta miquelii

Hosta miquelii, commonly known as Miquel's plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale blue, which appear on slender stalks in late summer. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
