Hieracium macilentum

Hieracium macilentum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. It produces yellow flowers with a central disk and surrounding ray florets, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Hieracium macilentum is often found in open habitats and can form dense colonies, making it a common sight in natural and semi-natural environments.
