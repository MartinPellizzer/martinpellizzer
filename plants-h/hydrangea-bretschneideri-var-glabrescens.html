Hydrangea bretschneideri var. glabrescens

Hydrangea bretschneideri var. glabrescens, commonly known as the panicle hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pink flowers that appear in late summer. The plant produces showy panicles that can range from 10 to 30 centimeters in length. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to the blooms.

This variety is known for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil conditions.
