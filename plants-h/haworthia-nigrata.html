Haworthia nigrata

Haworthia nigrata, commonly known as the Black Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a dense rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white or pale markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
