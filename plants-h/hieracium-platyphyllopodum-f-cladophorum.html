Hieracium platyphyllopodum f. cladophorum

Hieracium platyphyllopodum f. cladophorum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette growth habit and clustered inflorescence. The plant features basal leaves that are broadly lanceolate and covered with dense white hairs. Its yellow disk flowers are surrounded by pale yellow to brown ray florets, and it produces small, oval achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

This plant thrives in sunny, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.
