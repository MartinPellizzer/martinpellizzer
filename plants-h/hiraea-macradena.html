Hiraea macradena

Hiraea macradena, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that allow it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Hiraea macradena thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
