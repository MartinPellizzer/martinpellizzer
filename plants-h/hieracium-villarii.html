Hieracium villarii

Hieracium villarii, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. Hieracium villarii is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions, making it a common sight in mountainous landscapes.
