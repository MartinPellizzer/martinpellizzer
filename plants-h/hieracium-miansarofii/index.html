Hieracium Miansarofii

Hieracium miansarofii is a rare and intriguing species of hawkweed native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. This plant is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to harsh climates. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Studying Hieracium miansarofii offers insights into ecological resilience and the importance of preserving biodiversity in fragile ecosystems.
