Hieracium kluchoricum

Hieracium kluchoricum, commonly known as the Kluchorica hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers with ray florets. The plant has rough, hairy leaves that are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. It is native to certain regions of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some areas.
