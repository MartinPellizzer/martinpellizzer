Hieracium albiflorum f. lyallii

Hieracium albiflorum f. lyallii, commonly known as the Lyall's white hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish-white flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous flowers arranged in a composite head.
