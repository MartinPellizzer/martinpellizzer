Hesperantha linearis

Hesperantha linearis, commonly known as the linear lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Hesperantha linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
