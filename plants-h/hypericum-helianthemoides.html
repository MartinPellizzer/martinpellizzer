Hypericum helianthemoides

Hypericum helianthemoides, commonly known as the yellow hypericum or sun hypericum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum helianthemoides is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.
