Hypericum prietoi

Hypericum prietoi, commonly known as the Mexican St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes numerous species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, typical of many Hypericum species. It thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats and is often found in rocky or disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
