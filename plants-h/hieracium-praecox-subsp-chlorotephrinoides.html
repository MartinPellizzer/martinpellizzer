Hieracium praecox subsp. chlorotephrinoides

Hieracium praecox subsp.

chlorotephrinoides, commonly known as the green-topped hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange composite flowers and hairy, basal leaves.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has a rosette growth habit and produces small, persistent seeds.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
