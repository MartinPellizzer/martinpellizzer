Heterostemon mimosoides var. pacimoniensis

Heterostemon mimosoides var. pacimoniensis, commonly known as the Pacific mimosa, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. Its flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in dense clusters, and it produces a distinctive, flat, pod-like fruit.
