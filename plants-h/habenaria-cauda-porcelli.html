Habenaria cauda-porcelli

Habenaria cauda-porcelli, commonly known as the Porcelline Habenaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive spurred lip that attracts pollinators.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
