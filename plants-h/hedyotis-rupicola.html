Hedyotis rupicola

Hedyotis rupicola, commonly known as the rocky pennywort, is a small herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in mountainous or coastal regions. The plant features delicate, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its small white or pale yellow flowers bloom in clusters, adding a subtle ornamental value.

Hedyotis rupicola is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky outcrops or along coastal cliffs.
