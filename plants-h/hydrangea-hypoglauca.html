Hydrangea hypoglauca

Hydrangea hypoglauca, commonly known as the pale blue hydrangea, is a species native to East Asia, particularly China and Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that typically range in color from white to pale blue, depending on soil pH. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is dark green and often has a slightly hairy texture.

Hydrangea hypoglauca is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
