Hysterionica nidorelloides

Hysterionica nidorelloides, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling small lanterns. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped bracts that are typically more prominent than the actual flowers. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shady environments.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
