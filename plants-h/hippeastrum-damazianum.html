Hippeastrum damazianum

Hippeastrum damazianum, commonly known as the Damazian amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South America, specifically Brazil.

It produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive spotted pattern on the petals. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum damazianum is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
