Hypericum × carpaticum

Hypericum × carpaticum, commonly known as the hybrid St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming from June to August. The plant has woody stems and alternate leaves that are oval to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its medicinal properties have historically been used for their anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.
