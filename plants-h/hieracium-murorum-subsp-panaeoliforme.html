Hieracium murorum subsp. panaeoliforme

Hieracium murorum subsp.

panaeoliforme, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in meadows and grasslands.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a rough texture.
