Habenaria fargesii

Habenaria fargesii, commonly known as the Farges' orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizome is fibrous, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often spotted or streaked.
