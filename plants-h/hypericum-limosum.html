Hypericum limosum

Hypericum limosum, commonly known as swamp St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to wetlands and moist soils in North America.

It features woody stems covered in small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is adapted to waterlogged conditions and plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
