Hieracium pyrrhocranum

Hieracium pyrrhocranum, commonly known as the red-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a tall, flowering stem that bears yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are typically visited by various pollinators.

Hieracium pyrrhocranum is an herbaceous perennial that can form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
