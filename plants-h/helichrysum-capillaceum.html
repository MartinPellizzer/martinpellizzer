Helichrysum capillaceum

Helichrysum capillaceum, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a compact, low-growing habit.

Helichrysum capillaceum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
