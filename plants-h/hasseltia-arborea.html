Hasseltia arborea

Hasseltia arborea, commonly known as the tree hakea, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and dense, leathery foliage. The plant produces striking, cone-like inflorescences that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates with dry summers.
