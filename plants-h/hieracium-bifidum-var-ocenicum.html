Hieracium bifidum var. ocenicum

Hieracium bifidum var. ocenicum, commonly known as the coastal hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dunes or along the edges of coastal grasslands. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its ability to tolerate salty and windy conditions.

Its presence is often an indicator of stable coastal ecosystems.
