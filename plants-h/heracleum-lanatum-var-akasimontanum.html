Heracleum lanatum var. akasimontanum

Heracleum lanatum var. akasimontanum, commonly known as the Akasimon tansy fern, is a variant of the hogweed species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Its foliage is covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance.
