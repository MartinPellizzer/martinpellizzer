Hieracium subconfertum

Hieracium subconfertum, commonly known as the densehead hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, hairy stems. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium subconfertum is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun.
