Helichrysum rosmarinum

Helichrysum rosmarinum, commonly known as Cape rosemary, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow to orange flowers that resemble tiny daisies. The plant produces fibrous roots and has a bushy growth habit with narrow, aromatic leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Helichrysum rosmarinum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine.
