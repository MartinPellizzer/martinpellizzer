Horsfieldia macrocoma var. rufirachis

Horsfieldia macrocoma var. rufirachis, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers that are typically pale yellow or orange with a red or pink throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in primary rainforests.

The varietal name *rufirachis* refers to its distinctive reddish stems, which are a notable feature in its botanical description.
