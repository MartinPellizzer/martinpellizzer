Helianthemum oelandicum var. alpestre

Helianthemum oelandicum var. alpestre, commonly known as the alpine rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh, alpine conditions. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in sunny, open habitats.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
