Habenaria aitchisonii var. josephi

Habenaria aitchisonii var. josephi, commonly known as the Aitchison's Habenaria, is a rare orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with intricate markings that resemble a spider's web, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth where it can receive indirect light. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and the plant produces a single spike of flowers during the warm season.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its rarity and unique appearance.
