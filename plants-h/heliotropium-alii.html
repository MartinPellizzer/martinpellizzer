Heliotropium alii

Heliotropium alii, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple-pink bracts that surround small, white flowers. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with green and white patterns. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Heliotropium alii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
