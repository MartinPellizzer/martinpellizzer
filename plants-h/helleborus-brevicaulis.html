Helleborus brevicaulis

Helleborus brevicaulis, commonly known as the short-stalked hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are broadly ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces clusters of nodding, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, waxy appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Helleborus brevicaulis is known for its resilience and ability to bloom even in cold climates.
