Habenaria palpensis

Habenaria palpensis, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. Its rhizome is fibrous, and it produces a single flower per stem, which is usually borne on a slender stalk.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its beauty.
