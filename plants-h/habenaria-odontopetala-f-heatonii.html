Habenaria odontopetala f. heatonii

Habenaria odontopetala f. heatonii, commonly known as the Heatson's orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while the inflorescence bears numerous small, white or pale yellow flowers.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
