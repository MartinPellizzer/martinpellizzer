Hedyotis capituligera

Hedyotis capituligera, commonly known as the yellow false pennywort, is a small herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.

Its flowering stems are often branched and bear numerous tiny flowers, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
