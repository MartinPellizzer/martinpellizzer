Hippocrepis pusilla

Hippocrepis pusilla, commonly known as the small helmet orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that resemble miniature helmets. The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and is often found in grasslands or open woodlands. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish in color, with a distinctive hood-like structure.
