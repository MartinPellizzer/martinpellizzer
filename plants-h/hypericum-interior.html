Hypericum interior

Hypericum interior, commonly known as the narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Hypericum interior is often found in woodland edges, open woods, and along roadsides.
