Hessea pallida

Hessea pallida, commonly known as the pale hessea, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

Hessea pallida is adapted to arid environments and is often found in dune ecosystems.
