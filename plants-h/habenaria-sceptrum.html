Habenaria sceptrum

Habenaria sceptrum, commonly known as the sacred orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its flowers have a distinctive, spurred lip that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

The botanical features of Habenaria sceptrum make it a notable subject in both horticulture and ecological studies.
