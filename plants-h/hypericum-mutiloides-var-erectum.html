Hypericum mutiloides var. erectum

Hypericum mutiloides var. erectum, commonly known as the erect yellowstar.

It is a member of the Hypericaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and bloom in late spring to early summer.
