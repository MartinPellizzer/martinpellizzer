Hieracium pammelanum

Hieracium pammelanum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs.

Hieracium pammelanum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in many regions.
