Habenaria flagellifera

Habenaria flagellifera, commonly known as the flagellate orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
