Hypericum senanense subsp. mutiloides

Hypericum senanense subsp.

mutiloides, commonly known as the Senanese St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit with opposite, simple leaves.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in the summer months. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
