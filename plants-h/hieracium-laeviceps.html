Hieracium laeviceps

Hieracium laeviceps, commonly known as the smooth-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium laeviceps is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
