Homalomena lucidula

Homalomena lucidula, commonly known as the golden arrow plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Homalomena lucidula is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
