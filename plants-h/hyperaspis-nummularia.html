Hyperaspis nummularia

Hyperaspis nummularia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are thick and waxy, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
