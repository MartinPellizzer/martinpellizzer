Hieracium monophyton

Hieracium monophyton, commonly known as the single-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It features a rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.

Hieracium monophyton is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain regions.
