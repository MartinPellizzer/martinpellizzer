Hieracium mallopoderes

Hieracium mallopoderes, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are alternate, rough to the touch, and often have a lobed or serrated edge.

Hieracium mallopoderes is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes and can form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
