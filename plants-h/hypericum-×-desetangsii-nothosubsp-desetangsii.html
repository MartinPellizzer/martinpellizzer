Hypericum × desetangsii nothosubsp. desetangsii

Hypericum × desetangsii nothosubsp.

desetangsii, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed St. John's wort, is a hybrid shrub native to southern China. It features bright yellow flowers with five petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Hypericaceae family.

The plant has woody stems that are often covered in glandular dots, which can exude a sticky resin. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
