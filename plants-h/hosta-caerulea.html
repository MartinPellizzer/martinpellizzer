Hosta caerulea

Hosta caerulea, commonly known as Blue Hosta, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are typically a deep blue-green color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta caerulea is popular in gardens for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
