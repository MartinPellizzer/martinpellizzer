Heliotropium tenuifolium

Heliotropium tenuifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. The plant has thin, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for xeriscaping.

Heliotropium tenuifolium is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
