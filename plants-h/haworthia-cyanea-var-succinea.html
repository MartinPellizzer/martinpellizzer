Haworthia cyanea var. succinea

Haworthia cyanea var. succinea, commonly known as the blue haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy rosettes of pale blue to greyish-green leaves with prominent white tubercles. The plant produces tall, spindly stems with clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
