Habenaria ndiana

Habenaria ndiana, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizome and pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is adapted to the unique flora of the island.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
