Hieracium Valdepilosum Subsp. Subalpinum

Hieracium valdepilosum subsp. subalpinum is a rare alpine plant found in high elevation regions of Europe. It belongs to the daisy family, asteraceae, and is known for its delicate yellow flowers. This subspecies thrives in rocky and meadow habitats where conditions are harsh and competition is low. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting its habitat is crucial for preserving its unique ecological role.
