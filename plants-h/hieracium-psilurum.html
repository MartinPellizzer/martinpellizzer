Hieracium psilurum

Hieracium psilurum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Hieracium psilurum is often found in disturbed habitats and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some regions.
