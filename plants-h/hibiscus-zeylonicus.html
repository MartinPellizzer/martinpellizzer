Hibiscus zeylonicus

Hibiscus zeylonicus, commonly known as the Ceylon hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually green and slightly hairy. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found near water sources.

Hibiscus zeylonicus is also valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional uses in local cultures.
