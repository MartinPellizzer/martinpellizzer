Hypericum orientale var. tournefortii

Hypericum orientale var. tournefortii, commonly known as the Turkish St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It is often found in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
