Homalomena vivens

Homalomena vivens, commonly known as the red-veined arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves with striking red veins. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its foliage is prized for its ornamental value and its ability to purify indoor air.
