Haworthia diaphana

Haworthia diaphana, commonly known as the translucent Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are pale green with translucent, waxy areas. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in its arid native habitat. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
