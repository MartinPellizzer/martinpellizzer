Hieracium vulgatum subsp. megalogonium

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

megalogonium, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its robust growth and showy yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowering stems can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

It is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive nature.
