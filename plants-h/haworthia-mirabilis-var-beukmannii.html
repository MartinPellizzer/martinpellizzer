Haworthia mirabilis var. beukmannii

Haworthia mirabilis var. beukmannii, commonly known as the Beukmann's Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The leaves are covered in fine, translucent hairs, giving them a unique, almost glass-like appearance. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its striking appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
