Hieracium peradnatum

Hieracium peradnatum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

Hieracium peradnatum is often considered an invasive species in certain regions due to its rapid spread and ability to outcompete native vegetation.
