Hieracium albomurorum

Hieracium albomurorum, commonly known as the white-haired hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, often silver-gray leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has a creeping growth habit with erect flowering stems.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny areas where it can spread rapidly.
