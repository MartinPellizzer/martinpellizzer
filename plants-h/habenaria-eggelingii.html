Habenaria eggelingii

Habenaria eggelingii, commonly known as the Eggeling's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip that is often marked with purple or brown veins. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
