Hemionitis rivalis

Hemionitis rivalis, commonly known as the water starwort, is a small aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It has a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that float on the water surface. The plant produces tiny, greenish flowers that are typically submerged or floating. It is often found in still or slow-moving water, such as ponds and ditches.
