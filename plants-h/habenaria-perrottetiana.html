Habenaria perrottetiana

Habenaria perrottetiana, commonly known as the Perrottet's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has a pseudobulb structure, which helps it store water and nutrients, adapting it to its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
