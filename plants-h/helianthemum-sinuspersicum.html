Helianthemum sinuspersicum

Helianthemum sinuspersicum, commonly known as the Sinus Persicum rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, evergreen leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and Mediterranean climates.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
