Hesperantha rivulicola

Hesperantha rivulicola, commonly known as the river lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive white throat. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments such as rivers and lakes. Its leaves are long and ribbon-like, often floating on the water surface.

Hesperantha rivulicola plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
