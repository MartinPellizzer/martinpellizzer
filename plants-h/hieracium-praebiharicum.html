Hieracium praebiharicum

Hieracium praebiharicum, commonly known as the early hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. Native to Europe, it typically grows in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces achenes as its seed type, which are dispersed by wind.

It is often found in disturbed soils and thrives in temperate climates.
