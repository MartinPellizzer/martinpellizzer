Hypoestes aristata var. staudtii

Hypoestes aristata var. staudtii, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features striking, velvety, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and is often used in container gardening or as a border plant.
