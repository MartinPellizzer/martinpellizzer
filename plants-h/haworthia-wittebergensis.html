Haworthia wittebergensis

Haworthia wittebergensis, commonly known as the Witteberg Haworthia, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with distinctive white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white flowers during the warmer months, usually in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
