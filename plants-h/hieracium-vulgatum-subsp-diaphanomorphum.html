Hieracium vulgatum subsp. diaphanomorphum

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

diaphanomorphum, commonly known as the transparent-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The subspecies is distinguished by its thin, translucent leaves that give it its common name. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn.
