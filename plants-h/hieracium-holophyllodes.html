Hieracium holophyllodes

Hieracium holophyllodes, commonly known as the white hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are basal and lobed, while the flowers are yellow with white ray florets.
