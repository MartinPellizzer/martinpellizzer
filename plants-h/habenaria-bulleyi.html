Habenaria bulleyi

Habenaria bulleyi, commonly known as the Bulley's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest floors, often in rich, organic soil. The plant produces intricate, spurred orchid flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

It is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific ecological conditions for successful growth and reproduction.
