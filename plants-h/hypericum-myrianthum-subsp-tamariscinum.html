Hypericum myrianthum subsp. tamariscinum

Hypericum myrianthum subsp.

tamariscinum, commonly known as the tamarisk hypericum, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and coastal areas.

The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are usually narrow and slightly serrated. It is part of the genus Hypericum, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value.

The subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth form and specific floral characteristics.
