Hieracium stenodontum

Hieracium stenodontum, commonly known as the narrow-toothed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and its numerous yellow flowers arranged in a composite head. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with serrated margins, and the flowers bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium stenodontum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates.
