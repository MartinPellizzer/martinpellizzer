Hydrocotyle bonariensis var. multiflora

Hydrocotyle bonariensis var. multiflora, commonly known as the water pennywort, is a semi-aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, round, floating leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in diameter. The plant produces clusters of tiny white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its roots are fibrous and help stabilize the soil in aquatic environments.

It is often found in slow-moving freshwater habitats such as ponds and streams.
