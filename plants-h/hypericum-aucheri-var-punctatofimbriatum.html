Hypericum aucheri var. punctatofimbriatum

Hypericum aucheri var. punctatofimbriatum, commonly known as the spotted edge St. John's wort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features upright, branching stems covered in dense, yellowish-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers with orange stamens, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically oval with a distinctive white or pale green marginal band, giving it a striking appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
