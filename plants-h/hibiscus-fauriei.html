Hibiscus fauriei

Hibiscus fauriei, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and can grow up to 3 meters in height. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Hibiscus fauriei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
