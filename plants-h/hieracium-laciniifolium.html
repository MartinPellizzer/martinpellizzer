Hieracium laciniifolium

Hieracium laciniifolium, commonly known as the finely lobed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features basal and upright stems, with narrow, deeply lobed leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family.

It is an annual or biennial plant that thrives in temperate climates.
