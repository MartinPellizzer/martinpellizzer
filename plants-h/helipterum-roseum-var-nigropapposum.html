Helipterum roseum var. nigropapposum

Helipterum roseum var. nigropapposum, commonly known as the black pappus helipterum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are usually hairy and somewhat woody.

The distinctive black bracts beneath the flower heads give it its common name and contribute to its ornamental appeal.
