Hieracium nigrescens subsp. stenolope

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

stenolope, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed sites, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a rough, hairy texture, and its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
