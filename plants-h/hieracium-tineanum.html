Hieracium tineanum

Hieracium tineanum, commonly known as the Tinean hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium tineanum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
