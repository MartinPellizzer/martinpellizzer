Hieracium Flocciparum

Hieracium flocciparum is a rare and unique species of dandelion found primarily in specific regions of Europe. This plant is characterized by its fluffy, white flower heads that resemble clouds, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this delicate and ecologically important plant.
