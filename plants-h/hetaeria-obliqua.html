Hetaeria obliqua

Hetaeria obliqua, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a loose spike. Hetaeria obliqua thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and relatively rare orchid species within its native range.
