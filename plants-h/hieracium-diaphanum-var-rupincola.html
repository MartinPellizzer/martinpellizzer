Hieracium diaphanum var. rupincola

Hieracium diaphanum var. rupincola, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellowish flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its delicate foliage and unique floral structure, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
