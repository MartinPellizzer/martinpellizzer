Hieracium cephalochnoum

Hieracium cephalochnoum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early autumn. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stem is usually hairy and upright. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and open woodlands.

Hieracium cephalochnoum is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
