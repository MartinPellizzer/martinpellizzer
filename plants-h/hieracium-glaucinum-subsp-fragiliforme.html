Hieracium glaucinum subsp. fragiliforme

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

fragiliforme, commonly known as the fragiliform yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and basal rosettes of lanceolate, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in composite inflorescences, which are typical of the daisy family. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat preferences, it is considered a rare and localized species in certain regions.
