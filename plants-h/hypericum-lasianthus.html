Hypericum lasianthus

Hypericum lasianthus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Hypericum lasianthus is often found in rocky soils and dry habitats, thriving in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
