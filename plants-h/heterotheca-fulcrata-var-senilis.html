Heterotheca fulcrata var. senilis

Heterotheca fulcrata var. senilis, commonly known as the silverleaf ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the central and eastern United States.

It features silver-gray, woolly foliage and produces conical, yellow-orange flower heads that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and woodland edges. It has a robust root system and can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and ecologically valuable plant in native landscapes.
