Hieracium brachybrachion

Hieracium brachybrachion, commonly known as the short-bract hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Hieracium brachybrachion is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates.
