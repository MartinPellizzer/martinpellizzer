Hypericum maguirei

Hypericum maguirei, commonly known as the Maguire's St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Hypericum maguirei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
