Hieracium viridissimum

Hieracium viridissimum, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright green, hairy stems and leaves, which are often covered in fine, glandular hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is typically found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium viridissimum is a perennial species that can form dense stands, often competing with other vegetation in its habitat.
