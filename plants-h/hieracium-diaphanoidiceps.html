Hieracium diaphanoidiceps

Hieracium diaphanoidiceps, commonly known as the transparent-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that give it a unique appearance. Native to Europe, this species typically grows in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is often found in open, sunny habitats and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens.
