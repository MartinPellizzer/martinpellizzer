Habenaria ayangannensis

Habenaria ayangannensis, commonly known as the Ayangann orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the rainforests of New Guinea.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white blooms, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its restricted habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
