Hieracium murorum var. pallescens

Hieracium murorum var. pallescens, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

This variant is distinguished by its pale greenish-yellow flowers and reduced leaf size compared to other Hieracium species.
