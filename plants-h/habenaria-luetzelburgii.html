Habenaria luetzelburgii

Habenaria luetzelburgii, commonly known as the Luetzelburg's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forests or along streams. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single spike of small, fragrant blooms.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
