Huberantha gracilis

Huberantha gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly across the forest floor.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
