Howardia inflata

Howardia inflata, commonly known as the flame plant or fireplant, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, orange, and yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a dramatic visual display. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
