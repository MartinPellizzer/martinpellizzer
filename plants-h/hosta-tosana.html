Hosta tosana

Hosta tosana, commonly known as the Tosana Hosta, is a striking perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery margin, creating a dramatic visual effect. The plant produces tall, upright spikes of white to pale blue flowers in late summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta tosana is relatively low maintenance and is often used in shady garden beds for its ornamental foliage and flowering display.
