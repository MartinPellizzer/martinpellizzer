Habenaria jaguariahyvae

Habenaria jaguariahyvae, commonly known as the jaguariahyva orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
