Heterothrix pycnantha

Heterothrix pycnantha, commonly known as the goldenbush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, needle-like leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems. Its botanical features include hairy stems and small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
