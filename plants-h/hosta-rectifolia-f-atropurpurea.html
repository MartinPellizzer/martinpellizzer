Hosta rectifolia f. atropurpurea

Hosta rectifolia f. atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple-leafed straight-leaved hosta, is a varietal form of Hosta rectifolia, characterized by its striking purple foliage.

This plant is native to eastern Asia and thrives in shady, moist environments. Its leaves are elongated, upright, and have a smooth texture, with a deep purple hue that intensifies in cooler weather. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers on slender stems in late summer.

It is a popular ornamental plant in gardens for its vibrant color and ease of cultivation.
