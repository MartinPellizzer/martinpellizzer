Hippeastrum glaucum

Hippeastrum glaucum, commonly known as the blue squill, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, often with a distinctive red or purple stripe down the center. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hippeastrum glaucum is a popular ornamental plant due to its dramatic blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
