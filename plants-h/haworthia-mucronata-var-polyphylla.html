Haworthia mucronata var. polyphylla

Haworthia mucronata var. polyphylla, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and its distinctive, zebra-like markings on its fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
