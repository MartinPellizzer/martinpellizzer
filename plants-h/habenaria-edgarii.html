Habenaria edgarii

Habenaria edgarii, commonly known as the Edgari's orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
