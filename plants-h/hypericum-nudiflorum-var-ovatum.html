Hypericum nudiflorum var. ovatum

Hypericum nudiflorum var. ovatum, commonly known as the naked-flowered St. John's wort, is a low-growing shrub native to the western United States.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that appear before the leaves in early summer. The plant has slender, woody stems and is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and open woodlands. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
