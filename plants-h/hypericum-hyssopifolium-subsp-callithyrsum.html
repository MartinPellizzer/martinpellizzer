Hypericum hyssopifolium subsp. callithyrsum

Hypericum hyssopifolium subsp.

callithyrsum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia. It features slender, upright stems covered in small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
