Hieracium britannicum var. stenolepiforme

Hieracium britannicum var. stenolepiforme, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Britain.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, hairy leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clustered inflorescences, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in parts of its native range.
