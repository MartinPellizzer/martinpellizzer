Haworthia triebneriana var. depauperata

Haworthia triebneriana var. depauperata, commonly known as the "Tribute Haworthia," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
