Haworthia magnifica var. paradoxa

Haworthia magnifica var. paradoxa, commonly known as the Paradoxa Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically light green with distinctive white or pale markings. The plant is known for its slow growth and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect light and is often cultivated for its unique appearance and ease of care.

Its foliage can develop a reddish tinge when exposed to full sun, adding to its ornamental value.
