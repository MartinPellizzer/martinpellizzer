Hieracium wiesbaurianum subsp. strizakense

Hieracium wiesbaurianum subsp.

strizakense, commonly known as the Strizak's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow composite flowers, which are typically arranged in a single terminal head.

The leaves are usually basal, with a rough texture and a serrated margin. This subspecies is native to Central Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators due to its nectar-rich flowers.
