Hieracium praehirtum

Hieracium praehirtum, commonly known as the early spearthistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features a basal rosette of spiny leaves and produces yellow or reddish flowers in a composite head. It is typically herbaceous, with a height ranging from 30 to 100 centimeters.

Due to its prickly foliage, it is often considered a weed in agricultural areas.
