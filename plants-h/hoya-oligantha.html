Hoya oligantha

Hoya oligantha, commonly known as the spider plant hoya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.

Hoya oligantha is also known for its slow growth and ability to develop aerial roots, making it an interesting subject for botanical study.
