Haworthia radula var. laevior

Haworthia radula var. laevior, commonly known as the smooth-leafed Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are smooth and often have a waxy coating, distinguishing it from other varieties. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
