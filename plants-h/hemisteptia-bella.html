Hemisteptia bella

Hemisteptia bella, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Hemisteptia bella thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open habitats.
