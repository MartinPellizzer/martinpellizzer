Hieracium phaleratum

Hieracium phaleratum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. Hieracium phaleratum is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
