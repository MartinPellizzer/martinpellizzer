Horsfieldia novoguineensis var. mosleyana

Horsfieldia novoguineensis var. mosleyana, commonly known as the Mosley's Horsfield's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of New Guinea.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy floral structure. The plant typically produces pendulous, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the undergrowth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique beauty.
