Hydromystria laevigata

Hydromystria laevigata, commonly known as the smoothleaf hydromystria, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its roots are known to have medicinal properties used in traditional medicine.

The species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
