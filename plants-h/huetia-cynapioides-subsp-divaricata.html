Huetia cynapioides subsp. divaricata

Huetia cynapioides subsp.

divaricata, commonly known as the purple passion vine, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean. It is characterized by its striking purple and violet flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found growing in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
