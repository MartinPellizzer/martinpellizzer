Hieracium nigelloides

Hieracium nigelloides, commonly known as the blackseed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many regions.
