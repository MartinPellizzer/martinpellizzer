Huernia × transmutata

Huernia × transmutata, commonly known as the hybrid moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is a hybrid between Huernia and other related species, resulting in a unique combination of features. The plant has fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, and it produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
