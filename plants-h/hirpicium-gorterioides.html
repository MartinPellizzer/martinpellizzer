Hirpicium gorterioides

Hirpicium gorterioides, commonly known as the Gorter's hirpicium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces both ray and disk flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
