Hieracium glomeratum subsp. kunzii

Hieracium glomeratum subsp.

kunzii, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant produces dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed environments.
