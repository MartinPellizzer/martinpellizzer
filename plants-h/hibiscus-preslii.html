Hibiscus preslii

Hibiscus preslii, commonly known as the Preslii hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with palmate leaves that are deeply lobed. Hibiscus preslii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
