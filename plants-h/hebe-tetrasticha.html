Hebe tetrasticha

Hebe tetrasticha, commonly known as the mountain hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, which bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Hebe tetrasticha is well adapted to rocky and coastal environments, making it a hardy and attractive garden plant.
