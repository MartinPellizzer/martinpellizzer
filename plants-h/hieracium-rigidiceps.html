Hieracium rigidiceps

Hieracium rigidiceps, commonly known as the rigid-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant has upright, hairy stems and produces yellow flowers in dense, flat-topped clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium rigidiceps is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
