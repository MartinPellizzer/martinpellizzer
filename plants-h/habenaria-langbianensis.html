Habenaria langbianensis

Habenaria langbianensis, commonly known as the Langbian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color, with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
