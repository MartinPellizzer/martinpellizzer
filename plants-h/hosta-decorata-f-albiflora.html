Hosta decorata f. albiflora

Hosta decorata f. albiflora, commonly known as the Whiteflower Hosta, is a cultivar of the Hosta plant native to East Asia.

It features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow margins. The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers on tall, upright stems in the summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display in garden settings.
