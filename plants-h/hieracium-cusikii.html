Hieracium cusikii

Hieracium cusikii, commonly known as the Cusik's hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and yellow flower heads composed of numerous small florets. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often growing in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
