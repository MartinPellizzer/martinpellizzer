Habranthus andersonii var. texanus

Habranthus andersonii var. texanus, commonly known as the Texas starflower, is a rare and endemic species native to the Chihuahuan Desert in Texas and parts of New Mexico.

It is a perennial herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments with limited water availability.
