Hieracium bifidum subsp. caesioprasinum

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

caesioprasinum, commonly known as the greenish hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates.

The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and often have a hairy surface. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and arranged in a single head, with ray florets that are usually greenish in color.

It is part of the Asteraceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
