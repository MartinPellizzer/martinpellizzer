Hieracium purkynei

Hieracium purkynei, commonly known as the Czech dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers that develop into fluffy seed heads, aiding in wind pollination.

Hieracium purkynei is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
