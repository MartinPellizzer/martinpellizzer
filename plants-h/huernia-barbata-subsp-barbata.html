Huernia barbata subsp. barbata

Huernia barbata subsp.

barbata, commonly known as the horned ice plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa. It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in small, spiky projections.

The plant produces clusters of yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
