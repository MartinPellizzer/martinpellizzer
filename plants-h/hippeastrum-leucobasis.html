Hippeastrum leucobasis

Hippeastrum leucobasis, commonly known as the white hurricane lily, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces striking white flowers with a distinctive red stripe down the center, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a fleshy underground bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
