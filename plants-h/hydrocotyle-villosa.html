Hydrocotyle villosa

Hydrocotyle villosa, commonly known as silky water pennywort, is a perennial aquatic plant native to North America.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. Its flowers are white and clustered in umbels, adding to its ornamental value.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
