Huernia brevirostris subsp. intermedia

Huernia brevirostris subsp.

intermedia, commonly known as the Narrow-beaked Huernia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in a layer of white, powdery wax.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
