Hippocrepis multisiliquosa subsp. ciliata

Hippocrepis multisiliquosa subsp.

ciliata, commonly known as the ciliated sweet violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its violet-colored flowers with distinctive purple markings.
