Hyphear koumensis

Hyphear koumensis, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, preferring cool and humid conditions. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the thin soil of its mountainous habitat.

Hyphear koumensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
