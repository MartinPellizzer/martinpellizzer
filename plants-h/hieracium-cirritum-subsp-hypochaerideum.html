Hieracium cirritum subsp. hypochaerideum

Hieracium cirritum subsp.

hypochaerideum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale yellow to orange ray florets.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has finely divided, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is distinguished by its smaller, more compact inflorescence compared to other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
