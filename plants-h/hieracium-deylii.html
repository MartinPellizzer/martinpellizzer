Hieracium deylii

Hieracium deylii, commonly known as Deylii Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium deylii is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
