Hexapterella

Hexapterella, commonly known as the golden star, is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

It typically features showy yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. The plant is native to parts of Australia and is often found in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are usually alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Hexapterella is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
