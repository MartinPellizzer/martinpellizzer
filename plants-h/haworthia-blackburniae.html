Haworthia blackburniae

Haworthia blackburniae, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
