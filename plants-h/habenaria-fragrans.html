Habenaria fragrans

Habenaria fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to various regions in Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
