Hippeastrum smilakrokos

Hippeastrum smilakrokos, commonly known as the Smilakrokos amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces a large, showy inflorescence that typically blooms in the spring or early summer. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a rosette, and the bulbous underground stem stores nutrients for future growth.
