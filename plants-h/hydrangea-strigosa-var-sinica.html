Hydrangea strigosa var. sinica

Hydrangea strigosa var. sinica, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough, strigose texture, giving it its species name. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
