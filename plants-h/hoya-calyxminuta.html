Hoya calyxminuta

Hoya calyxminuta, commonly known as the miniature waxy hoya, is a compact, trailing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Hoya calyxminuta is a succulent, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for indoor cultivation.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
