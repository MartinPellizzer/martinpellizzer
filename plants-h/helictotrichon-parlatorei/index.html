Helictotrichon Parlatorei

Helictotrichon parlatorei is a rare and striking grass species native to the mountainous regions of southern Italy. Known for its unique appearance and ecological significance, it thrives in high-altitude environments where few plants can survive. This plant has become a symbol of biodiversity in its native habitat and is often overlooked due to its delicate form. Its conservation status is a growing concern as climate change threatens its survival. Studying Helictotrichon parlatorei offers valuable insights into the resilience of alpine ecosystems.
