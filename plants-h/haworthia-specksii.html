Haworthia specksii

Haworthia specksii, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tubular leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
