Hieracium glaucomorphum

Hieracium glaucomorphum, commonly known as the blue-green hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually alternate, rough to the touch, and have a distinctive blue-green hue, giving it its common name.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a flat-topped cluster, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.
