Hieracium eulacistum

Hieracium eulacistum, commonly known as the rough hawksbeard, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed soils. The plant features yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk, with leaves that are rough and hairy to the touch. It produces small, elongated achenes that are dispersed by wind.

Hieracium eulacistum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
