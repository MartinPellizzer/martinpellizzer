Habenaria Unalaschensis

Habenaria orchid, scientifically known as Habenaria unalaschensis, is a rare and elusive species native to the mountainous regions of western North America. This delicate orchid thrives in high-altitude habitats, often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes where conditions are harsh and competition is minimal. Its unique appearance, featuring striking white and green petals, makes it a standout among the diverse orchid family. Despite its beauty, the Habenaria unalaschensis remains poorly understood due to its limited distribution and challenging environment. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare plant from threats such as habitat loss and climate change.
