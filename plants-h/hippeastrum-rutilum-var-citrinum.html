Hippeastrum rutilum var. citrinum

Hippeastrum rutilum var. citrinum, commonly known as the Golden Trumpet lily, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces striking, fleshy leaves that are often broad and strap-like. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
