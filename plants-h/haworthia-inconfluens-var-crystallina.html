Haworthia inconfluens var. crystallina

Haworthia inconfluens var. crystallina, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, translucent, window-like leaves with white or pale green markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the growing season.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
