Hylodesmum podocarpum var. japonicum

Hylodesmum podocarpum var. japonicum, commonly known as Japanese rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, providing a textured appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to coastal environments.
