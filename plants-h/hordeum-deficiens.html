Hordeum deficiens

Hordeum deficiens, commonly known as the deficient barley, is a wild grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, spikelet-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Hordeum deficiens has narrow leaves with a rough texture and produces small, pale grains.

It is of interest to botanists due to its genetic diversity and potential for agricultural research.
