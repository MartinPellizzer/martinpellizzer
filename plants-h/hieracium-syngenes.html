Hieracium syngenes

Hieracium syngenes, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single head. The plant is typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often glandular, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium syngenes is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
