Hieracium crispiceps

Hieracium crispiceps, commonly known as the curly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers that are surrounded by a ring of pale green to white ray florets. Its leaves are typically hairy and have a slightly crinkled appearance, contributing to its common name.

Hieracium crispiceps is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
