Heptapleurum brassaiellum

Heptapleurum brassaiellum, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its opposite, simple, and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, white, clustered flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The berries are edible and have a sweet, slightly tart flavor, making it a valuable plant in local cuisines.
