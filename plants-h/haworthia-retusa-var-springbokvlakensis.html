Haworthia retusa var. springbokvlakensis

Haworthia retusa var. springbokvlakensis, commonly known as the Springbokvlakte Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings, forming a rosette shape. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
