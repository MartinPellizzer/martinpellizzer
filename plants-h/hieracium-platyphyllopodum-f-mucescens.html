Hieracium platyphyllopodum f. mucescens

Hieracium platyphyllopodum f. mucescens, commonly known as the mossy hawkweed, is a rare botanical variant of the hawkweed genus.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and pale green to almost white foliage. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or damp meadows. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its preferred habitat.

This form is notable for its unique appearance and ecological niche within its native range.
