Habenaria amanoana

Habenaria amanoana, commonly known as the Amano's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are typically fragrant. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Habenaria amanoana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
