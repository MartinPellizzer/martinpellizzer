Hieracium sparsum subsp. cholomonense

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

cholomonense, commonly known as the Cholomonese hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species in parts of its native range.
