Helichrysum psiadiifolium

Helichrysum psiadiifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its small, golden-yellow flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its unique leaf shape and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice among horticultural enthusiasts.
