Hieracium nitidiceps

Hieracium nitidiceps, commonly known as the shining hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. Native to Europe, it thrives in grassy habitats and open areas. The plant has a creeping rootstock and produces numerous flowering stems.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and garden cultivation.
