Hieracium leptopicris

Hieracium leptopicris, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, and it has a creeping rhizome that helps it spread vegetatively.

Hieracium leptopicris is typically herbaceous, with a short lifespan and a preference for temperate climates.
