Hieracium inflectens

Hieracium inflectens, commonly known as the bent-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium inflectens is also known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
