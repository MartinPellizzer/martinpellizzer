Hieracium Deltophylloides

Hieracium deltophylloides is a striking member of the dandelion family known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique leaf structure. This plant is native to parts of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in open, sunny environments. Its leaves are deeply lobed, giving it a distinct appearance that sets it apart from other members of its genus. Despite its beauty, it is often mistaken for common dandelions due to its similar flower shape. This plant plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance in its habitat.
