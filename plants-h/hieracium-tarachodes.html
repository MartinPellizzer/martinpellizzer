Hieracium tarachodes

Hieracium tarachodes, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from June to August. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
