Hieracium clomacotes

Hieracium clomacotes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically discoid and arranged in a composite head. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape, often with hairy surfaces. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium clomacotes is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
