Hypericophyllum nyassicum

Hypericophyllum nyassicum, commonly known as the Nyassian hypericum, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of East Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae, which includes species known for their medicinal properties. The plant features woody stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.
