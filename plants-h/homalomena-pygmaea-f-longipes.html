Homalomena pygmaea f. longipes

Homalomena pygmaea f. longipes, commonly known as the slender-leafed arrowhead plant, is a compact, evergreen herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are narrower and longer than those of the typical species, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers with three petals and three sepals, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor collections.
