Hieracium chlorophyton subsp. putnokense

Hieracium chlorophyton subsp.

putnokense, commonly known as the Putnok yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are basal, hairy, and have a rough texture, while its stems are upright and often branched.

It is considered a weed in agricultural areas due to its rapid growth and ability to spread vegetatively.
