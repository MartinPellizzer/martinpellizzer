Helichrysum transvalense

Helichrysum transvalense, commonly known as the Transvaal daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble small sunflowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Helichrysum transvalense is also known for its ability to produce long-lasting flowers that retain their color even when dried.
