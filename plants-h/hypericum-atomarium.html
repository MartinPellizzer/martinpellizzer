Hypericum atomarium

Hypericum atomarium, commonly known as the narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its botanical characteristics include glandular dots on the stems and leaves, which are typical of the Hypericaceae family.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in woodland or open areas.
