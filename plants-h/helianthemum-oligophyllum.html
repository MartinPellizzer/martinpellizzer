Helianthemum oligophyllum

Helianthemum oligophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is considered a hardy plant suitable for xeriscaping.
