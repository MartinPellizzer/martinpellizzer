Hymenocallis moritziana var. major

Hymenocallis moritziana var. major, commonly known as the giant yellow hurricane lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces strap-like leaves and has a bulbous root system typical of the Amaryllis family. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
