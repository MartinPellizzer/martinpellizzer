Hieracium carnosum subsp.

austrohercynicum, commonly known as the Austrian hedgehog. It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe, characterized by its hairy, spiny leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and forest edges. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its distinctive, thistle-like appearance.
