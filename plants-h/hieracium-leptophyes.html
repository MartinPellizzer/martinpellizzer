Hieracium leptophyes

Hieracium leptophyes, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and bright yellow composite flowers that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium leptophyes is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
