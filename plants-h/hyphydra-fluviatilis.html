Hyphydra fluviatilis

Hyphydra fluviatilis, commonly known as the river hydra, is a unique aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It possesses delicate, floating leaves that are often covered in a waxy layer to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its root system is adapted for anchoring in muddy or sandy substrates.

Hyphydra fluviatilis plays a vital role in maintaining water quality by absorbing excess nutrients.
