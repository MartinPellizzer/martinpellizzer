Hicorius glabra var. odorata

Hicorius glabra var. odorata, commonly known as the fragrant smooth hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Its fragrance is particularly noticeable in the evening, attracting pollinators such as moths and bees.
