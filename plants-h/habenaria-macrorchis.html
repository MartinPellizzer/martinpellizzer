Habenaria macrorchis

Habenaria macrorchis, commonly known as the large-ovary orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream sides. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker and more textured.

Habenaria macrorchis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
