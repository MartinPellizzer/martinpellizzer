Heliotropium ternatum

Heliotropium ternatum, commonly known as the small heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing herb with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Heliotropium ternatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
