Hieracium murorum f. vulgatum

Hieracium murorum f. vulgatum, commonly known as the common groundsel, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy, rough stems. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are usually alternate, with a serrated margin and a rough texture.
