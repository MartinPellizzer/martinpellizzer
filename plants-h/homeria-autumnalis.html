Homeria autumnalis

Homeria autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn homeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Homeria autumnalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its seasonal blooms.
