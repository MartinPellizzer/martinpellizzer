Harveya sulphurea

Harveya sulphurea, commonly known as the sulphur harveya, is a striking member of the Ericaceae family native to the mountainous regions of South America.

This evergreen shrub is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant features dense, leathery foliage that provides a striking contrast to its bright floral displays. Harveya sulphurea thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires a cool, humid climate to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for rock gardens and shaded borders in botanical gardens.
