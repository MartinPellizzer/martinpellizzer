Haworthia splendens

Haworthia splendens, commonly known as the splendour Haworthia, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
