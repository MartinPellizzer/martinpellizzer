Hieracium pseudomonomeres

Hieracium pseudomonomeres, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy, alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

Due to its invasive tendencies, it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
