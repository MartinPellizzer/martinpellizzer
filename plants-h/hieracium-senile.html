Hieracium senile

Hieracium senile, commonly known as the Goatsbeard.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its tall, branched stems and large, feathery leaves. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Hieracium senile is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
