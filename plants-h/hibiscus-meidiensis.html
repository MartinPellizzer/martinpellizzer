Hibiscus meidiensis

Hibiscus meidiensis, commonly known as the Mei Island hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft hair. Hibiscus meidiensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its traditional uses in local cultures.
