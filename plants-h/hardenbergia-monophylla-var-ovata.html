Hardenbergia monophylla var. ovata

Hardenbergia monophylla var. ovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rounded, spiny pods that enclose its seeds. The plant features compound leaves with broad, ovate leaflets that give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
