Habenaria parvipetala

Habenaria parvipetala, commonly known as the small-petaled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with small petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. Its roots are often mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient absorption.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
