Hieracium argyreum

Hieracium argyreum, commonly known as the silverleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features silver-white hairs on its leaves, which give it its common name and help reduce water loss through transpiration. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

Hieracium argyreum is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, where it can thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
