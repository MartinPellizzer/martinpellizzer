Hedyotis Rigida

Hedyotis rigida is a small, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a resilient, low-maintenance plant. Hedyotis rigida not only adds beauty to landscapes but also plays a role in supporting local wildlife.
