Hieracium mollisetum

Hieracium mollisetum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped with hairy surfaces.

Hieracium mollisetum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
