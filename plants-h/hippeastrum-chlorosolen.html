Hippeastrum chlorosolen

Hippeastrum chlorosolen, commonly known as the Green Trumpet.

This plant is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to South America. It is characterized by its striking green flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often emerging from a bulbous stem.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.
