Hydrotriche hottoniiflora

Hydrotriche hottoniiflora, commonly known as the Hotton's bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to the Chihuahuan Desert in North America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments.
