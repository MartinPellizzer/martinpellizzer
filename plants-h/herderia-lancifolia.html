Herderia lancifolia

Herderia lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved herderia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features linear, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Herderia lancifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
