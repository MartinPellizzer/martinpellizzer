Hieracium perthense

Hieracium perthense, commonly known as the Perth dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Western Australia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny, coastal environments. Its yellow flowers bloom in spring, and the plant is known for its resilience in harsh maritime conditions.

Hieracium perthense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
