Hakea behrii

Hakea behrii, commonly known as the pink hakea, is a shrub native to southwestern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white wax. Hakea behrii is adapted to dry, sandy soils and can thrive in full sun.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
