Hibbertia glomerata var. canescens

Hibbertia glomerata var. canescens, commonly known as the hairy hibbertia, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features slender, hairy stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically elliptical and covered in fine, white hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various garden conditions.
