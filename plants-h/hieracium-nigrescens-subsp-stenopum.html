Hieracium nigrescens subsp. stenopum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

stenopum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and often have a rough, hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow and bloom in summer.

It is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
