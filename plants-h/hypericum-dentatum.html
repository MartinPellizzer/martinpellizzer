Hypericum dentatum

Hypericum dentatum, commonly known as the toothed St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring upright stems covered in small, yellow-orange flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges, giving it its common name. It blooms from June to August, producing clusters of four-petaled flowers.

The plant is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
