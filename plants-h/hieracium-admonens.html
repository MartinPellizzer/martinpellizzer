Hieracium admonens

Hieracium admonens, commonly known as the warning hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its yellow-orange flowers are surrounded by pale green to white ray florets, and it produces numerous small, oval seeds.

Hieracium admonens is noted for its ability to spread rapidly, often forming dense colonies that can dominate local vegetation.
