Hieracium reinungense

Hieracium reinungense, commonly known as the Reinungense hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Central and Eastern Europe and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium reinungense is an herbaceous perennial that produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in a composite head.
