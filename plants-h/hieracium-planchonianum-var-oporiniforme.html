Hieracium planchonianum var. oporiniforme

Hieracium planchonianum var. oporiniforme, commonly known as the Oporniiform Meadow Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and can reach up to 20 cm in length.

The flowers are yellow and appear in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
