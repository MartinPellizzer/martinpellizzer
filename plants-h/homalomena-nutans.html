Homalomena nutans

Homalomena nutans, commonly known as the hanging arrow plant, is a tropical evergreen perennial belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used as an ornamental plant in tropical gardens.

Homalomena nutans is also valued for its air-purifying properties and is relatively easy to care for in indoor settings.
