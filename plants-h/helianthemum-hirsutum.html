Helianthemum hirsutum

Helianthemum hirsutum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features dense, hairy foliage that gives it its common name, and its flowers are usually yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Helianthemum hirsutum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate harsh conditions.
