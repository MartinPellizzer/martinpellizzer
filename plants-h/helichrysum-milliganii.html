Helichrysum milliganii

Helichrysum milliganii, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, fluffy yellow or golden inflorescences that resemble small daisies. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and vibrant color.

Helichrysum milliganii is also valued for its aromatic foliage and ornamental appeal in gardens.
