Herorchis × sciathia

Herorchis × sciathia, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring cross between Herorchis speciosus and Herorchis scitula.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display a blend of colors from both parent species. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding substrate.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
