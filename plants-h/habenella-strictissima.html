Habenella strictissima

Habenella strictissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or dry soils, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, contributing to its drought-resistant characteristics.

Habenella strictissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
