Helianthemum Cinereum Subsp. Hieronymi

Helianthemum cinereum subsp. hieronymi is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the rockrose family, known for its hardy and often drought-resistant characteristics. This subspecies is distinguished by its small, pale yellow flowers and compact growth habit. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and coastal regions. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
