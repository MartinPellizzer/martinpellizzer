Hypericinea lucida

Hypericinea lucida, commonly known as the shining St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, which are typical of the Hypericaceae family. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across various habitats. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericinea lucida thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands or rocky areas.
