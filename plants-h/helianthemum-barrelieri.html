Helianthemum barrelieri

Helianthemum barrelieri, commonly known as the Barrelier's rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage of small, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardening.
