Haworthia jansenvillensis var. regina

Haworthia jansenvillensis var. regina, commonly known as the Regina Haworthia, is a compact, dwarf succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale markings, which provide a striking visual contrast. The plant produces tall, slender flower stems with small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
