Hypericum gaitii

Hypericum gaitii, commonly known as the Gait's St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rocky or dry garden environments.

Hypericum gaitii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
