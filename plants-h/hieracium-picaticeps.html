Hieracium picaticeps

Hieracium picaticeps, commonly known as the purple meadowhawk, is a flowering plant belonging to the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, purple to reddish flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it plays a role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
