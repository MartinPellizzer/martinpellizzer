Hellenocarum multiflorum subsp. strictum

Hellenocarum multiflorum subsp.

strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved helichrysum, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, silver-green foliage.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for Mediterranean-style gardens.
