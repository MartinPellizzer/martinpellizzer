Haworthia coarctata f. chalwinii

Haworthia coarctata f. chalwinii, commonly known as the Chalwin's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, triangular leaves with a distinctive white or pale green coloration and prominent central markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
