Hieracium glaucinum proles fraternum

Hieracium glaucinum proles fraternum, commonly known as the gray hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clustered inflorescences. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas, meadows, and along roadsides. It has a taproot system and is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
