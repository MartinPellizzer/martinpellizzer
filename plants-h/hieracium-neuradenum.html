Hieracium neuradenum

Hieracium neuradenum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
