Hymenodictyon parvifolium subsp. scabrum

Hymenodictyon parvifolium subsp.

scabrum, commonly known as the roughleaf hymenodictyon, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are covered in a rough, scabrous texture.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

It is often found in dry, disturbed habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
