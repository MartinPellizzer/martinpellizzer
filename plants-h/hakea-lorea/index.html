Hakea Lorea

Hakea lorea is a striking evergreen shrub native to western australia known for its unique floral display and hardy nature. it features narrow, needle-like leaves and produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. this plant is well adapted to dry climates and thrives in full sun conditions. it is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance. hakea lorea adds both beauty and resilience to any landscape.
