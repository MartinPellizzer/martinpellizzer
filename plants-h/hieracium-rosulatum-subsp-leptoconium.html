Hieracium rosulatum subsp. leptoconium

Hieracium rosulatum subsp.

leptoconium, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has a hairy stem and lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is known for its adaptability to various soil types.
