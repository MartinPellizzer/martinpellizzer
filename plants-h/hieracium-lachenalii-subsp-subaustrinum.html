Hieracium lachenalii subsp. subaustrinum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

subaustrinum, commonly known as the southern hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

This subspecies is distinguished by its slightly smaller stature and more compact growth compared to other members of the species.
