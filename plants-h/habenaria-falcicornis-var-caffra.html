Habenaria falcicornis var. caffra

Habenaria falcicornis var. caffra, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, horizontal rhizome and produces a single, erect stem bearing several lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow to brownish flowers with a distinctive, hooded labellum. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands and along coastal areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under suitable conditions.
