Hieracium leptophyton subsp. purpureibracteum

Hieracium leptophyton subsp.

purpureibracteum, commonly known as the purple bract hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy habitats.

Its foliage is usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
