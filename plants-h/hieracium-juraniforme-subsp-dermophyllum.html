Hieracium juraniforme subsp. dermophyllum

Hieracium juraniforme subsp.

dermophyllum, commonly known as the Juranic hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Alps and surrounding regions. It typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high altitudes.

The plant has bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are usually hairy and lance-shaped. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in many areas.
