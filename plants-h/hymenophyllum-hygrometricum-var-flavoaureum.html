Hymenophyllum hygrometricum var. flavoaureum

Hymenophyllum hygrometricum var. flavoaureum, commonly known as the golden-haired filmy fern, is a delicate and epiphytic fern belonging to the Hymenophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, translucent, and intricately veined fronds that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its unique morphology allows it to absorb moisture from the air, making it well adapted to humid conditions.

The golden hue of its fronds is a distinguishing feature, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
