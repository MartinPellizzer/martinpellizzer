Hypericum Orientale Var. Jaubertii

Hypericum orientale var. jaubertii is a rare and striking variant of the common St. John's wort. Native to the mountainous regions of Asia, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. This plant is known for its vibrant yellow flowers and medicinal properties. Its unique characteristics make it a sought-after species among botanists and gardeners. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural community.
