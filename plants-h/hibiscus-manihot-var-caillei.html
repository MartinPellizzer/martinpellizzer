Hibiscus manihot var. caillei

Hibiscus manihot var. caillei, commonly known as the Caillei hibiscus, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and glossy. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with five deeply lobed petals, often in shades of yellow, orange, or red. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
