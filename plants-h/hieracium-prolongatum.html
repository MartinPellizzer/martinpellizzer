Hieracium prolongatum

Hieracium prolongatum, commonly known as the long-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium prolongatum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
