Hieracium tolosanum

Hieracium tolosanum, commonly known as the Tolosan hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

Hieracium tolosanum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting local pollinators.
