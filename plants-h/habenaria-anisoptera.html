Habenaria anisoptera

Habenaria anisoptera, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a greenish-yellow hue and distinctive dark spots. It typically blooms in late spring to early summer, often in moist woodland habitats.

Habenaria anisoptera is native to parts of Africa and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
