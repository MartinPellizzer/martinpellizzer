Hypodiscus zeyheri

Hypodiscus zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's kalahari, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
