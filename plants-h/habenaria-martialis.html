Habenaria martialis

Habenaria martialis, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Habenaria martialis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
