Helianthemum vivianii

Helianthemum vivianii, commonly known as the Vivian's rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helianthemum vivianii is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
