Hieracium dicella

Hieracium dicella, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium dicella is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable habitats.
