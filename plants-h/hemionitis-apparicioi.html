Hemionitis apparicioi

Hemionitis apparicioi, commonly known as the ghost fern, is a rare and delicate plant native to the arid regions of central Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, pale green fronds that resemble ghostly shapes, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to extreme environmental conditions. It reproduces through spores and is often found in crevices or shaded areas within its habitat.
