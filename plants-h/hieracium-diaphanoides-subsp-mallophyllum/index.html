Hieracium Diaphanoides Subsp. Mallophyllum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. mallophyllum is a rare and delicate plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the daisy family, known for its diverse and often colorful members. This subspecies is distinguished by its translucent, almost glass-like leaves that give it a unique appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodlands or along stream banks. Due to its limited habitat and fragility, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
