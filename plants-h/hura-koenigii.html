Hura koenigii

Hura koenigii, commonly known as the flame of the forest, is a deciduous tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its bark is smooth and grayish, while its wood is hard and durable.

Hura koenigii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
