Hypochaeris × minima

Hypochaeris × minima, commonly known as the lesser mouse-ear cress, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is a hybrid species that typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and meadows. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with four petals, and its leaves are usually hairy and lobed. It is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in temperate regions.
