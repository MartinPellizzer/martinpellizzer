Herniaria euxina

Herniaria euxina, commonly known as the European bladderwort, is a small, low-growing plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are often lobed or notched, and it produces small, white or pink flowers.
