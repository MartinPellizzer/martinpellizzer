Haworthiopsis × revendettii

Haworthiopsis × revendettii, commonly known as the Rainbow Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to blue, red, and purple. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
