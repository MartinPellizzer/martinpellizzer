Hypericum michauxii

Hypericum michauxii, commonly known as Michaux's St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a reddish or purplish hue.

Hypericum michauxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
