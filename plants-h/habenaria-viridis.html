Habenaria viridis

Habenaria viridis, commonly known as the green orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to various regions in Asia and Australia.

It features slender, erect stems that bear several lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of greenish-yellow flowers with three petals and three sepals, which are often tinged with white or brown. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest floors or along stream banks.

The orchid is noted for its unique coloration and is appreciated in horticulture for its ornamental value.
