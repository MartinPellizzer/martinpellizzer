Hewardia dolosa

Hewardia dolosa, commonly known as the dwarf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Hewardia dolosa is popular in water gardens and as a container plant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
