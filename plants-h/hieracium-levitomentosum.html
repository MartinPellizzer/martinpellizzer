Hieracium levitomentosum

Hieracium levitomentosum, commonly known as the silky hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, surrounded by a ring of pale green to white ray florets. Its leaves are hairy and often covered in a fine, silken down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hieracium levitomentosum is typically found in moist, grassy areas and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
