Hibiscus lunariifolius var. dongolensis

Hibiscus lunariifolius var. dongolensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian hibiscus, is a species native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink to red in color. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in well-drained soils.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
