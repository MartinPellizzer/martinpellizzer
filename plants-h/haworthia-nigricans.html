Haworthia nigricans

Haworthia nigricans, commonly known as the black Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct markings and a dark, almost black coloration on the undersides. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
