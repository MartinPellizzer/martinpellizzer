Hypocrypta pulchra

Hypocrypta pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful crypton, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Hypocrypta pulchra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
