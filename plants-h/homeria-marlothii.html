Homeria marlothii

Homeria marlothii, commonly known as the marlothii homeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in the wild.
