Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. cinerascentiforme

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

cinerascentiforme, commonly known as the cinerascent hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal potential in traditional herbal practices.
