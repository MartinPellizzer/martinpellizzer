Hemionitis latiloba

Hemionitis latiloba, commonly known as the broad-leafed hemionitis, is a unique aquatic plant native to the wetlands of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that are often submerged or floating on the water's surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Hemionitis latiloba thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.
