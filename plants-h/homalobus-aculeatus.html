Homalobus aculeatus

Homalobus aculeatus, commonly known as the spiny hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often with serrated edges.

Homalobus aculeatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
