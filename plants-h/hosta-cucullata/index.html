Hosta Cucullata

Crown shield hosta, scientifically known as Hosta cucullata, is a striking perennial plant native to eastern Asia. It is prized for its unique, shield-shaped leaves that form a dense, compact mound. The foliage ranges from deep green to variegated patterns, adding visual interest to any garden. This hardy plant thrives in shady to partially sunny conditions and is relatively low maintenance. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
