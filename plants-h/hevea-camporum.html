Hevea camporum

Hevea camporum, commonly known as the yellow rubber tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and large, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. The plant is known for producing latex, which is harvested from its bark for various industrial uses. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting biodiversity in its native habitat.
