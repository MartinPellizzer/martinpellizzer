Hedyotis degeneri var. coprosmifolia

Hedyotis degeneri var. coprosmifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf strobilanthes, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its small, opposite leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in dense inflorescences. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in horticulture and botanical research.
