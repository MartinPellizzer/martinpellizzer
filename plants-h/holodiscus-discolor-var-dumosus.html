Holodiscus discolor var. dumosus

Holodiscus discolor var. dumosus, commonly known as the shrub honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that turn red in the fall. Its berries are small, dark blue, and edible, though often not palatable to humans.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in moist woodlands and streambanks.
