Hieracium nigrisquamum

Hieracium nigrisquamum, commonly known as the black-squamate hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant is recognized by its dark, scaly leaves and yellow ray florets. It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.
