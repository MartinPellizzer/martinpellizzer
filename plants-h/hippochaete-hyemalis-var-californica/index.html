Hippochaete Hyemalis Var. Californica

Hippochaete hyemalis var. californica is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California. It is known for its striking purple-blue blossoms that bloom in early spring. This variety thrives in moist, sandy soils and requires a cool climate to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a prized addition to botanical gardens and conservation efforts. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and needs careful protection.
