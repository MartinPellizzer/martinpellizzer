Howardia reniformis

Howardia reniformis, commonly known as the African violet or velvet plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features striking, velvety blue or purple leaves that are oval in shape and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, typically blooming in spring or summer. It prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a versatile addition to gardens and greenhouses.
