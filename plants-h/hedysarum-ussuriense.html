Hedysarum ussuriense

Hedysarum ussuriense, commonly known as Ussuri vetch, is a perennial legume native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces slender, cylindrical pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Hedysarum ussuriense is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
