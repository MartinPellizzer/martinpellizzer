Hisingera rumea

Hisingera rumea, commonly known as the red-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hisingera rumea is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
