Helixanthera subcylindrica

Helixanthera subcylindrica, commonly known as the slender spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique spiral arrangement of its leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
