Hieracium respondens

Hieracium respondens, commonly known as the responding hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Hieracium respondens is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in agricultural and natural settings.
