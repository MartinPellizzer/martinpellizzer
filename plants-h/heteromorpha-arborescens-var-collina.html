Heteromorpha arborescens var. collina

Heteromorpha arborescens var. collina, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
