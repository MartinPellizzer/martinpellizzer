Helianthemum marifolium var. grandiflorum

Helianthemum marifolium var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color, providing a drought-tolerant adaptation. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
