Holosteum heuffelii

Holosteum heuffelii, commonly known as the African waterwheel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive whorled arrangement of leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in wet, marshy environments and is often found near water bodies. It has a creeping stem that can root at the nodes, allowing it to spread rapidly in suitable conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
