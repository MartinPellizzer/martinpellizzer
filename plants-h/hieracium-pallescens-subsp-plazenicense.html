Hieracium pallescens subsp. plazenicense

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

plazenicense, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils.

The plant has a rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are arranged in a single capitulum, typical of the Asteraceae family.

The subspecies is distinguished by its pale yellow flowers and slightly smaller leaves compared to other members of the species.
