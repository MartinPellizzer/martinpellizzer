Hosta undulata var. univittata

Hosta undulata var. univittata, commonly known as the wavy-leaf hosta, is a cultivar prized for its striking, undulating foliage.

Its leaves are typically broad, with a wavy margin that gives it a unique, almost ruffled appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers on tall, slender stems in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is popular in shaded garden beds and is known for its ornamental value and hardiness.
