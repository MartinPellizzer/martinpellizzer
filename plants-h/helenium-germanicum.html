Helenium germanicum

Helenium germanicum, commonly known as the German sneezeweed, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe, characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and bright orange to yellow ray flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Helenium germanicum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
