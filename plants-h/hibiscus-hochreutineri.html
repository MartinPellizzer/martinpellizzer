Hibiscus hochreutineri

Hibiscus hochreutineri, commonly known as the Hochreuter's hibiscus, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow to orange. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.
