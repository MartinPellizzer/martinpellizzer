Haworthia uitewaaliana

Haworthia uitewaaliana, commonly known as the Cape Town beauty, is a compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
