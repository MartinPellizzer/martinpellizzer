Hippocrepis comosa var. prostrata

Hippocrepis comosa var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate helmet orchid, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, creeping stems that lie close to the ground, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, hood-shaped flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils.
