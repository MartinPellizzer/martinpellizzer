Hedysarum lipskyi

Hedysarum lipskyi, commonly known as the Tien Shan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in mountainous regions, thriving in dry, rocky soils. The plant features compound leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by wind.

Hedysarum lipskyi is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
