Habenaria rosulifolia

Habenaria rosulifolia, commonly known as the Rosulate Orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
