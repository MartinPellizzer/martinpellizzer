Hoya trukensis

Hoya trukensis, commonly known as the Truk hoya, is a flowering plant native to the Caroline Islands.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, elliptical leaves. It is relatively low maintenance and popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value.
