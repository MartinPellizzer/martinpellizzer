Herorchis coriophora

Herorchis coriophora, commonly known as the small yellow orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has a slender, erect stem with a single, showy yellow flower that blooms in late spring. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

The orchid is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in sunny, well-drained environments.
