Hoarea corydaliflora

Hoarea corydaliflora, commonly known as the mountain hellebore, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are typically heart-shaped and have a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. It is adapted to cold, mountainous environments and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been historically used in traditional medicine.
