Hymenophyllum cordobense

Hymenophyllum cordobense, commonly known as the Cordoba filmy fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to South America.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are thin and translucent, with a unique structure that allows for efficient water retention.

Hymenophyllum cordobense is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
