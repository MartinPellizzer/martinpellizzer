Hedera helix var. aureovariegata

Hedera helix var. aureovariegata, commonly known as the golden variegated ivy, is a popular climbing plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking yellow and green variegation pattern. The plant thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor environments. Its trailing growth habit makes it ideal for hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

The foliage remains lush throughout the year, adding ornamental value to gardens and interiors.
