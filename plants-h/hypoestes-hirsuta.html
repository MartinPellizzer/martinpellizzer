Hypoestes hirsuta

Hypoestes hirsuta, commonly known as the Mexican snowflake, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bi-color foliage with white and green patterns. Hypoestes hirsuta is often grown as a houseplant or in gardens for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
