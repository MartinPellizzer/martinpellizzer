Hydrocotyle Javanica Var. Podantha

Hydrocotyle javanica var. podantha is a fascinating aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Asia. It thrives in shallow waters and is often found in wetlands, ponds, and stream edges. This variant is known for its distinctive, floating leaves that create a lush green cover on the water surface. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental water gardens. Despite its beauty, it can also be invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
