Hieracium platyterum

Hieracium platyterum, commonly known as the flat-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are broadly ovate with a hairy surface, and the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium platyterum is often considered a weed due to its rapid spread and ability to thrive in various environments.
