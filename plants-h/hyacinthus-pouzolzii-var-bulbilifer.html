Hyacinthus pouzolzii var. bulbilifer

Hyacinthus pouzolzii var. bulbilifer, commonly known as the bulbilifer hyacinth, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant produces bulbils along its stems, which are small plantlets that can be used for propagation. Its foliage consists of strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous base.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in well-drained soil.
