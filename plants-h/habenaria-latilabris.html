Habenaria latilabris

Habenaria latilabris, commonly known as the broad-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive broad lip that is often yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
