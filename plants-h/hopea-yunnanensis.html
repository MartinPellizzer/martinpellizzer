Hopea yunnanensis

Hopea yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Myanmar, and Thailand. This evergreen tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

Hopea yunnanensis is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in tropical lowland forests.
