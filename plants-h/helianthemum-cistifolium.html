Helianthemum cistifolium

Helianthemum cistifolium, commonly known as the rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
