Hieracium sigalodes

Hieracium sigalodes, commonly known as the sigalodes hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish to reddish flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. Its leaves are usually alternate, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Hieracium sigalodes is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various regions.
