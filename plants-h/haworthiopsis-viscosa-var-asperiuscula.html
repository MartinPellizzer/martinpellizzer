Haworthiopsis viscosa var. asperiuscula

Haworthiopsis viscosa var. asperiuscula, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, ridged, and often variegated leaves that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
