Habenaria plectromaniaca

Habenaria plectromaniaca, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and grasslands. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent lip, and it produces small, rounded pseudobulbs.

The species is notable for its unique morphology and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
