Hoya pendula var. neelgherrensis

Hoya pendula var. neelgherrensis, commonly known as the Neelgherrensis Wisteria, is a climbing epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features long, trailing stems that can reach several meters in length, often adorned with clusters of small, waxy, star-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. This species is known for its ability to thrive in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice among indoor gardeners due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
