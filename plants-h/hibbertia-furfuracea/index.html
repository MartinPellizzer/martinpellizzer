Hibbertia Furfuracea

Hibbertia furfuracea is a unique and striking plant native to Australia's arid regions. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive, woolly foliage, it stands out in its natural habitat. This species thrives in harsh conditions, showcasing remarkable adaptability to dry and nutrient-poor soils. Its appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscaping. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticultural circles.
