Hieracium involutum

Hieracium involutum, commonly known as the curled hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are rough and hairy, with a rosette growth habit. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family.
