Hieracium amoeniflorum

Hieracium amoeniflorum, commonly known as the pretty flowered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer.

Hieracium amoeniflorum is often found in open habitats and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
