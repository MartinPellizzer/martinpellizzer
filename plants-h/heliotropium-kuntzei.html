Heliotropium kuntzei

Heliotropium kuntzei, commonly known as the purple heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often resembling those of the true heliotropes. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate to lanceolate in shape, and covered with fine hairs.

Heliotropium kuntzei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
