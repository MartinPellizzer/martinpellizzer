Hieracium hausmannii

Hieracium hausmannii, commonly known as Hausmann's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the alpine regions of Central Europe, particularly in the Alps and Carpathians. The plant is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Hieracium hausmannii is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes.
