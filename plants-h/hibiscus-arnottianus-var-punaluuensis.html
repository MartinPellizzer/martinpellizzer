Hibiscus arnottianus var. punaluuensis

Hibiscus arnottianus var. punaluuensis, commonly known as the Punaluu hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Hawaii.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is well adapted to the tropical climate. It has heart-shaped leaves and produces fruit that contains seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
