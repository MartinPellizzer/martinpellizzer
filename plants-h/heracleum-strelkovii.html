Heracleum strelkovii

Heracleum strelkovii, commonly known as Siberian cowparley, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its tall, hollow stems and large, feather-like leaves. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
