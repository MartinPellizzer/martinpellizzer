Hippeastrum equestre var. glabrifolium

Hippeastrum equestre var. glabrifolium, commonly known as the smooth-leaved amaryllis, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces strap-like, smooth leaves that are typically broad and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is popular for its ease of care and ability to bloom reliably indoors or outdoors in suitable climates.
