Haworthia blackburniae subsp. graminifolia

Haworthia blackburniae subsp.

graminifolia, commonly known as the grass-leafed Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or pale markings.

The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
