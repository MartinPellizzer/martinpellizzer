Haworthiopsis limifolia var. striata

Haworthiopsis limifolia var. striata, commonly known as the Striped Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of thick, fleshy, light green leaves with distinctive white to pale green stripes. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
