Hypericum altissimum

Hypericum altissimum, commonly known as tall St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and rocky areas.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
