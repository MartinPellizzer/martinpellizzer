Hymenocallis ruizii

Hymenocallis ruizii, commonly known as the Ruiz's spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a bulbous base, typical of many amaryllid species. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hymenocallis ruizii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
