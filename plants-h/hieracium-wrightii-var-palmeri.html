Hieracium wrightii var. palmeri

Hieracium wrightii var. palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in desert environments. It has small, alternate leaves and produces seeds in a composite flower head.

This variant is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and morphological traits.
