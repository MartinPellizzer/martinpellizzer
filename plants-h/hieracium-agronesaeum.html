Hieracium agronesaeum

Hieracium agronesaeum, commonly known as the Agronease hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous tiny florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sunlight. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
