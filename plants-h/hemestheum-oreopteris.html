Hemestheum oreopteris

Hemestheum oreopteris, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a unique and rare plant species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, golden-yellow fronds that contrast beautifully with its dark green base. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are divided into small, feather-like segments, giving it a delicate and ornamental appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
