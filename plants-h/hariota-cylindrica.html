Hariota cylindrica

Hariota cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical hariota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, cylindrical stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.

Hariota cylindrica is also known for its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
