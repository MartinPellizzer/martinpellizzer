Hebe olsenii

Hebe olsenii, commonly known as the silver hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces spikes of blue or purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Hebe olsenii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
