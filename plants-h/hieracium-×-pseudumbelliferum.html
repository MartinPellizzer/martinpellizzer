Hieracium × pseudumbelliferum

Hieracium × pseudumbelliferum, commonly known as the false umbrella thistle, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its robust growth and distinctive, often spiny, composite flower heads that resemble umbrellas. The plant typically features dark green, rough, and hairy leaves that are alternate and often lobed. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
