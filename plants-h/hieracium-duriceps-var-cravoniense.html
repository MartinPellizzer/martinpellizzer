Hieracium duriceps var. cravoniense

Hieracium duriceps var. cravoniense, commonly known as the Cravonic Hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of stiff, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. This variety is native to Central Europe and thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant has a taproot system and is known for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and garden cultivation.
