Homalomena yohorensis

Homalomena yohorensis, commonly known as the Yohorensis Arrowhead Plant, is a tropical evergreen herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
