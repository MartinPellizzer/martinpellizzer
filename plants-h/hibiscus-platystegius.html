Hibiscus platystegius

Hibiscus platystegius, commonly known as the broad-stemmed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually hairy, and its stems are thick and robust. Hibiscus platystegius thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
