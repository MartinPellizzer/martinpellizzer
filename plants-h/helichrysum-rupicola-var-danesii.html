Helichrysum rupicola var. danesii

Helichrysum rupicola var. danesii, commonly known as the Cape goldball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of golden-yellow, sun-like flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is known for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
