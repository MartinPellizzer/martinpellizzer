Helianthemum italicum var. micranthum

Helianthemum italicum var. micranthum, commonly known as the Sicilian rock rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has aromatic, glandular leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardening.
