Habenaria dregeana

Habenaria dregeana, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in coastal areas. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Habenaria dregeana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
