Huernia zebrina subsp. zebrina

Huernia zebrina subsp.

zebrina, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its distinctive, ribbed stems that are covered in white to pale green tubercles.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
