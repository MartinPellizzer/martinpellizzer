Hugonia reygaertii

Hugonia reygaertii, commonly known as the dwarf arrowhead, is a small aquatic plant native to the wetlands of southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically submerged or floating, with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emerging from the water surface. It thrives in shallow, still water and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of ponds.

Hugonia reygaertii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in aquatic ecosystems.
