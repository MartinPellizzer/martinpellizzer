Hieracium etheirodes

Hieracium etheirodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped.

Hieracium etheirodes is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
