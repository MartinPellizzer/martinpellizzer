Haworthiopsis bruynsii

Haworthiopsis bruynsii, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, narrow, grass-like leaves that are often marked with distinct white or yellow markings, resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for low-maintenance gardens.

Haworthiopsis bruynsii is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care.
