Herniaria × urrutiae

Herniaria × urrutiae, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a whitish, powdery coating called a pruina. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and garden design.
