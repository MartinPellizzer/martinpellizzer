Hippeastrum × hendersonii

Hippeastrum × hendersonii, commonly known as the red hurricane.

This ornamental plant is a hybrid of the amaryllis genus, characterized by its striking red flowers with orange markings. It is a bulbous plant that thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil. The flowers typically bloom in the spring or early summer, adding vibrant color to gardens.

Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that emerge from the base of the bulb.
