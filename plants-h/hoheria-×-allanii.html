Hoheria × allanii

Hoheria × allanii, commonly known as the silver tree, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of fragrant, bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it well-suited for coastal gardens. Its unique appearance and adaptability have made it a popular ornamental plant in temperate climates.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract native pollinators and birds.
