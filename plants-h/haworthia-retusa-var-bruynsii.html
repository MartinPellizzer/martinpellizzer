Haworthia retusa var. bruynsii

Haworthia retusa var. bruynsii, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, pale green to blue-gray leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
