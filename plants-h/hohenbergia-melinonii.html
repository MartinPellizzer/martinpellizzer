Hohenbergia melinonii

Hohenbergia melinonii, commonly known as the silver stripe bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to Brazil.

It features rosettes of narrow, silver-striped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant red flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Hohenbergia melinonii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
