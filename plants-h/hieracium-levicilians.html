Hieracium levicilians

Hieracium levicilians, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. It has a creeping growth habit with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves.

The flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster, and the plant is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes.
