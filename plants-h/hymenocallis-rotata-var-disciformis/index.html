Hymenocallis Rotata Var. Disciformis

Coral pink spider lily, scientifically known as Hymenocallis rotata var. disciformis, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. This rare and delicate species is celebrated for its vivid coral-pink blooms that resemble the intricate patterns of a spider's web. With its slender stems and large, showy flowers, it adds a unique touch to gardens and natural landscapes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a sought-after addition for botanical enthusiasts. Due to its limited distribution and conservation status, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens and conservation programs.
