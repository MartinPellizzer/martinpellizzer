Harmonia nutans

Harmonia nutans, commonly known as the hanging bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that hang downward, typically in shades of blue or purple. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Harmonia nutans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, cascading blooms.
