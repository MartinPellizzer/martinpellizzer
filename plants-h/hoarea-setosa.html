Hoarea setosa

Hoarea setosa, commonly known as the silky hairgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features fine, silky hairs along its leaf blades, giving it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands or along streambanks. Its flowers are small and clustered in spires, with pale green to brown hues.

Hoarea setosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
