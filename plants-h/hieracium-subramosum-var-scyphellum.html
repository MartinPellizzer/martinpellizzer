Hieracium subramosum var. scyphellum

Hieracium subramosum var. scyphellum, commonly known as the scyphellum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy structure. The plant typically features upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the genus Hieracium.

It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
