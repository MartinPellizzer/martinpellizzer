Helichrysum benguellense

Helichrysum benguellense, commonly known as the Benguella everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-orange flowers that are often arranged in tight, spherical clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a slight hairy texture.

Helichrysum benguellense is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
