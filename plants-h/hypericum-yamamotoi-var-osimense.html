Hypericum yamamotoi var. osimense

Hypericum yamamotoi var. osimense, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
