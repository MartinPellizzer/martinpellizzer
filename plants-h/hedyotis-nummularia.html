Hedyotis nummularia

Hedyotis nummularia, commonly known as pennywort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions of Asia and Europe.

It features small, round, waxy leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in diameter, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny white or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Hedyotis nummularia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
