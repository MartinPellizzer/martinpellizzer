Hakea concinna

Hakea concinna, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that appear in late summer to early autumn. Hakea concinna is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and nectar for native wildlife.
