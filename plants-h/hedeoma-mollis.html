Hedeoma mollis

Hedeoma mollis, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features showy, blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Hedeoma mollis is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
