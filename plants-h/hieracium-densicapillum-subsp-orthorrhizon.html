Hieracium densicapillum subsp. orthorrhizon

Hieracium densicapillum subsp.

orthorrhizon, commonly known as the dense-capped hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has a creeping rootstock and produces numerous flowering stems.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the hawkweed complex.
