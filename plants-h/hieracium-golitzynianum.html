Hieracium golitzynianum

Hieracium golitzynianum, commonly known as the Golitzyn's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branched.

The flowers are yellow, often appearing in clusters, and bloom from late spring to early summer.
