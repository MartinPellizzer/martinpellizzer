Hieracium chrysanthum

Hieracium chrysanthum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.

Hieracium chrysanthum is known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
