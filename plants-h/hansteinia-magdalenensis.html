Hansteinia magdalenensis

Hansteinia magdalenensis, commonly known as the Magdalena pineapple.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the Andean region of South America. The plant features rosettes of fleshy, spathate leaves that resemble a pineapple. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, white to yellowish flowers.

Hansteinia magdalenensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
