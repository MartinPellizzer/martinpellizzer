Homalomena oblanceolata

Homalomena oblanceolata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking, orange-red spadices that are surrounded by long, narrow bracts. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Homalomena oblanceolata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
