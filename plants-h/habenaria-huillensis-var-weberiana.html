Habenaria huillensis var. weberiana

Habenaria huillensis var. weberiana, commonly known as the Weber's marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean region of South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and grassy slopes. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces erect, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow with purple markings, and are arranged in a dense spike.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
