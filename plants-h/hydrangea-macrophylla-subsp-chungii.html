Hydrangea macrophylla subsp. chungii

Hydrangea macrophylla subsp.

chungii, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a shrub native to eastern Asia. It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from blue to pink depending on soil pH.

The plant features broad, ovate leaves with a serrated margin and a glossy upper surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
