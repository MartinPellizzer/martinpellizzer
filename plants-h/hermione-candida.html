Hermione candida

Hermione candida, commonly known as the white moonflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, white, fragrant blooms that open at night. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its unique nocturnal blooming habit makes it a popular choice for evening gardens.
