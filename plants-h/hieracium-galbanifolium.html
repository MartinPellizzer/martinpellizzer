Hieracium galbanifolium

Hieracium galbanifolium, commonly known as the galbanifolium hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant contains sesquiterpene lactones, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

It is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects.
