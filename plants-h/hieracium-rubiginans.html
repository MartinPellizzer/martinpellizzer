Hieracium rubiginans

Hieracium rubiginans, commonly known as the rusted hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it typically grows in disturbed soils such as roadsides and fields. The plant has yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, and its leaves are usually hairy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It is characterized by its rust-colored stems and is often found in moist or slightly shaded environments.

Hieracium rubiginans is considered a weed in some regions due to its aggressive growth habit.
