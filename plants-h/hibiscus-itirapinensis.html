Hibiscus itirapinensis

Hibiscus itirapinensis, commonly known as the Itirapina hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Hibiscus itirapinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
