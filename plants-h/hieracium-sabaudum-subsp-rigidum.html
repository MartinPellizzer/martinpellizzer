Hieracium sabaudum subsp. rigidum

Hieracium sabaudum subsp.

rigidum, commonly known as the rigid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright, rigid stems and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky soils.

Its subspecies status reflects subtle morphological differences from other Hieracium species.
