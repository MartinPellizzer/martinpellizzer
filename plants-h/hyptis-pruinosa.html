Hyptis pruinosa

Hyptis pruinosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green foliage and small, clustered flowers that range from white to purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hyptis pruinosa is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
