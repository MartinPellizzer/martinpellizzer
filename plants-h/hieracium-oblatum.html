Hieracium oblatum

Hieracium oblatum, commonly known as the flathead hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage. The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium oblatum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
