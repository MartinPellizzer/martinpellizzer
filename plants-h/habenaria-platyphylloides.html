Habenaria platyphylloides

Habenaria platyphylloides, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. Its leaves are broad and flat, often arranged in a rosette pattern, and it has a short, hairy stem.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
