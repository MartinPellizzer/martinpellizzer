Hemigraphis alternata

Hemigraphis alternata, commonly known as the shining iron plant, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that exhibit a metallic sheen, often with contrasting colors on each side. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.

Hemigraphis alternata is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
