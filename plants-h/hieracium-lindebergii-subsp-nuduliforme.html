Hieracium lindebergii subsp. nuduliforme

Hieracium lindebergii subsp.

nuduliforme, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish at the base.
