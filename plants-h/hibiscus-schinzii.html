Hibiscus schinzii

Hibiscus schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to red, with a prominent central disk. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and have a rough texture. Hibiscus schinzii grows as an upright shrub, often reaching heights of up to 2 meters.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
