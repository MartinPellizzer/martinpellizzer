Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. himatiophidum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

himatiophidum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has a creeping growth habit and produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
