Habenaria leucosantha var. berroana

Habenaria leucosantha var. berroana, commonly known as the Berroan orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly found in Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as rainforests, where it thrives on decaying organic matter. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, while its inflorescence bears clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
