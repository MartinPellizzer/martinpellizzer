Holodiscus orizabae

Holodiscus orizabae, commonly known as the Mexican water lily, is a semi-aquatic plant native to the highlands of Mexico.

It has broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the early morning. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the water, and it plays a role in maintaining water quality.

Holodiscus orizabae is valued both for its ecological benefits and ornamental appeal.
