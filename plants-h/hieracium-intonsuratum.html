Hieracium intonsuratum

Hieracium intonsuratum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers have prominent disc florets surrounded by ray florets.

Hieracium intonsuratum is often found in Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in open, sunny habitats.
