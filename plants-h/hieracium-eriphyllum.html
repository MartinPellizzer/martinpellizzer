Hieracium eriphyllum

Hieracium eriphyllum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are covered in dense, white or grayish hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

The flowers are usually yellow or orange and bloom in the summer months.
