Helwingia japonica var. zhejiangensis

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces delicate, pendulous inflorescences bearing white or pale yellow flowers in early spring.
