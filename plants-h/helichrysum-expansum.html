Helichrysum expansum

Helichrysum expansum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has silvery-green, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Helichrysum expansum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
