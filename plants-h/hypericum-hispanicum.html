Hypericum hispanicum

Hypericum hispanicum, commonly known as Spanish St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom from June to August. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum hispanicum is often found in rocky soils and open habitats, such as cliffs and woodland edges.
