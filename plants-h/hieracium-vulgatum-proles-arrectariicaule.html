Hieracium vulgatum proles arrectariicaule

Hieracium vulgatum proles arrectariicaule, commonly known as the upright common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically growing in meadows and grasslands.

This species is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
