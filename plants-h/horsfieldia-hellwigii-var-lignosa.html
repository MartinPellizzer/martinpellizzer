Horsfieldia hellwigii var. lignosa

Horsfieldia hellwigii var. lignosa, commonly known as the hardy orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its clustered growth habit and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining substrates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
