Hieracium levicaule subsp. pseudopallescens

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

pseudopallescens, commonly known as the pale-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and pale green leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

The plant produces small, yellow flower heads arranged in a composite inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

This subspecies is distinguished by its slightly paler leaf coloration compared to other members of the *Hieracium levicaule* complex.
