Hieracium kuusamoense

Hieracium kuusamoense, commonly known as the Kuusamo hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to northern Finland.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, nutrient-rich soils in forested areas. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, elongated seed heads.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
