Hieracium stoloniflorum subsp. schurianum

Hieracium stoloniflorum subsp.

schurianum, commonly known as the Schurian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks.

The plant features basal rosettes of serrated leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. It has stolons that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological studies and botanical gardens.
