Hieracium mixopolium

Hieracium mixopolium, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies.

Hieracium mixopolium is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
