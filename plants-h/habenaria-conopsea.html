Habenaria conopsea

Habenaria conopsea, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Habenaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its unique inflorescence that bears small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized mycorrhizal associations that aid in nutrient uptake.

The species is often found in rocky or clay soils and is adapted to thrive in humid climates.
