Habenaria decaptera

Habenaria decaptera, commonly known as the Broad-leafed Pterostylis, is an orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and a single, showy flower with a distinctive, spurred lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and heathlands. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its terrestrial habit.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
