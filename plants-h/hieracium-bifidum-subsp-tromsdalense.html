Hieracium bifidum subsp. tromsdalense

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

tromsdalense, commonly known as the Tromsdalen hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers with ray florets.

This subspecies is native to northern Europe and is often found in dry, sandy soils. It has a low-growing habit and is typically found in open habitats such as heaths and grasslands.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
