Hieracium oxyodon subsp. muretii

Hieracium oxyodon subsp.

muretii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and reduced leaf hairs compared to other hawkweed species. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun conditions.
