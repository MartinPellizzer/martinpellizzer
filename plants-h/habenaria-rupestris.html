Habenaria rupestris

Habenaria rupestris, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or shaded environments, often on limestone or sandstone substrates. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several ovate to lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Habenaria rupestris is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
