Holothrix glaberrima

Holothrix glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth holothrix, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit with silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
