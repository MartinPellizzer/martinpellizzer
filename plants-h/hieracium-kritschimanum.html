Hieracium kritschimanum

Hieracium kritschimanum, commonly known as the Kritschimanum hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along rocky outcrops.
