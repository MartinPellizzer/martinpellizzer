Huberia huberioides

Huberia huberioides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Huberia huberioides is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
