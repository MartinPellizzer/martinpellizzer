Habenaria sutepensis

Habenaria sutepensis, commonly known as the Sutep Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems.
