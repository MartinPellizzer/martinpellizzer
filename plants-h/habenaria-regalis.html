Habenaria regalis

Habenaria regalis, commonly known as the royal orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial herb, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features include long, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Habenaria regalis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
