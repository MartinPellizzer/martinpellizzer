Hemiphlebium hookeri

Hemiphlebium hookeri, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Himalayas.

It features delicate, lacy fronds that give it a distinctive appearance, with a light green coloration and a soft, feathery texture. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens. Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, providing visual interest in both summer and winter.

Hemiphlebium hookeri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticultural settings for its elegant, airy look.
