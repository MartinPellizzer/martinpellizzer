Hymenorebutia tiegeliana var. dimorphipetala

Hymenorebutia tiegeliana var. dimorphipetala, commonly known as the "Fairy Cactus," is a compact, low-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with dense, woolly spines and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and red. The plant is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its compact size and drought tolerance. It blooms in late spring to early summer, adding a striking visual element to the garden.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
