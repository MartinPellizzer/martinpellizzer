Hieracium subsquarrosulum

Hieracium subsquarrosulum, commonly known as the squarrose hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in summer, while the leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is often found in open habitats and is considered a minor weed in some agricultural areas.
