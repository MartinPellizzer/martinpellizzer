Heuchera × tiarelloides

Heuchera × tiarelloides, commonly known as the hybrid heuchera or tiarelloides heuchera, is a decorative perennial plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage that ranges from deep purples to bright reds and greens, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 18 inches and features upright stems with heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous white or pink flowers in the spring, which are followed by red berries in the fall.

This hybrid is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in partial to full shade.
