Hypericum canadense var. majus

Hypericum canadense var. majus, commonly known as Canadian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and narrow, opposite leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
