Hieracium miramarense

Hieracium miramarense, commonly known as the Miramar hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Uruguay.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
