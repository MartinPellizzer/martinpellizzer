Hopea foxworthyi

Hopea foxworthyi, commonly known as the Philippine hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Hopea foxworthyi is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.
