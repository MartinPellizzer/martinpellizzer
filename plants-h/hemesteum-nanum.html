Hemesteum nanum

Hemesteum nanum, commonly known as the dwarf hemesteum, is a small, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features compact, rounded growth with dense foliage of dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale pink, during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a compact, decorative plant.
