Huernia brevirostris var. longula

Huernia brevirostris var. longula, commonly known as the Long-stemmed Horned Fig, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, ribbed stems that are often tinged with red or purple hues, especially when exposed to bright sunlight. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
