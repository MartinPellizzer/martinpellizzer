Hubera decora

Hubera decora, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a striking orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It features a unique, showy flower with a pouch-like structure that resembles a slipper, often in shades of pink or white. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Hubera decora is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive floral display.
