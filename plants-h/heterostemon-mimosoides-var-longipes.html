Heterostemon mimosoides var. longipes

Heterostemon mimosoides var. longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed silky mimosoid, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and elongated, slender stems that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often with a silvery or greyish underside that reflects light.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
