Helichrysum milanjiense

Helichrysum milanjiense, commonly known as the Milanji golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain open throughout the day. The plant produces small, papery bracts that give it a distinctive, ever-lasting appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and used in floral arrangements.
