Heeria arenophila

Heeria arenophila, commonly known as the sand heeria, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is well adapted to hot, dry climates. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover in xerophytic landscapes.

Heeria arenophila is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging environmental conditions.
