Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. dalmaticum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

dalmaticum, commonly known as the Dalmatian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The plant typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and rocky soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs, while its stems are erect and sometimes branched.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
