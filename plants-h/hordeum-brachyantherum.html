Hordeum brachyantherum

Hordeum brachyantherum, commonly known as the slender wild barley, is a species of grass native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields, roadsides, and waste areas. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green coloration.

Hordeum brachyantherum plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
