Hymenorebutia tiegeliana

Hymenorebutia tiegeliana, commonly known as the Tiegeli's crown of thorns, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
