Haworthiopsis reinwardtii var. reinwardtii

Haworthiopsis reinwardtii var. reinwardtii, commonly known as the Reinwardt's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or pale markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements in cultivation.
