Hedysarum jinchuanense

Hedysarum jinchuanense, commonly known as the Jinchuan milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in mountainous regions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential in ecological restoration projects.

Its seeds are also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
