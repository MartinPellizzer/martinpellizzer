Haworthia marginata

Haworthia marginata, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellow margins. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
