Hieracium expallescens

Hieracium expallescens, commonly known as the white hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are hairy and often reddish in color.

Hieracium expallescens is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many temperate regions.
