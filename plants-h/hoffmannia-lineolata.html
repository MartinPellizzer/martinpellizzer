Hoffmannia lineolata

Hoffmannia lineolata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features a branching stem with a distinctive zigzag pattern, which helps it conserve water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged alternately along the stems, contributing to its unique appearance.

Hoffmannia lineolata is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
