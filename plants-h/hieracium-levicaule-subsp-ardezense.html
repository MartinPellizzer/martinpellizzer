Hieracium levicaule subsp. ardezense

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

ardezense, commonly known as the Ardezense hawkweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellowish to brownish flower heads.

The plant typically thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine, soft hairs.

It is considered a minor weed but has ecological significance in its native range.
