Hieracium commersonii subvar. hypofloccum

Hieracium commersonii subvar. hypofloccum, commonly known as the Commerson's hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its flowers are composed of both ray and disk florets, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

It is often found in regions with temperate climates and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.
