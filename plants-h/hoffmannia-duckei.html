Hoffmannia duckei

Hoffmannia duckei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, often with a contrasting throat. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hoffmannia duckei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
