Hieracium tubulascens subsp. aestivae

Hieracium tubulascens subsp.

aestivae, commonly known as the summer hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

The plant features basal leaves that are broadly lanceolate and covered with glandular dots, while its flowering stems bear yellow composite flowers. It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The species is characterized by its robust growth and adaptability to various soil types.
