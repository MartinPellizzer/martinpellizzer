Hymenocallis nayaritiana

Hymenocallis nayaritiana, commonly known as the Nayarit spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Mexico.

It features large, showy white flowers with a prominent yellow throat, resembling spider-like blooms. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a tall, slender stem that supports its floral display. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hymenocallis nayaritiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
