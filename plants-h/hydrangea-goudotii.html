Hydrangea goudotii

Hydrangea goudotii, commonly known as the Goudot's hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features opposite, ovate leaves with a serrated margin. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The flowers are produced in late summer and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
