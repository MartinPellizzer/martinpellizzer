Helenium micranthum

Helenium micranthum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered sneezeweed, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America, particularly found in wetlands and moist soils. The plant features showy, orange to red-orange ray flowers surrounded by yellow disk flowers, which attract pollinators. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Helenium micranthum thrives in full sun to partial shade and is valued for its ornamental appeal in wetland restoration projects.
