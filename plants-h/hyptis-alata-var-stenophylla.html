Hyptis alata var. stenophylla

Hyptis alata var. stenophylla, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or violet flowers. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Hyptis alata var. stenophylla is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
