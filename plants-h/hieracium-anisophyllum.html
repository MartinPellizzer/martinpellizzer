Hieracium anisophyllum

Hieracium anisophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium anisophyllum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
