Haworthia retusa var. dekenahii

Haworthia retusa var. dekenahii, commonly known as the Deken's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a dense rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves with prominent white tubercles and translucent windows, which aid in photosynthesis. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
