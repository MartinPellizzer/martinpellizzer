Hieracium ornatilorum

Hieracium ornatilorum, commonly known as the ornate hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Hieracium. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its invasive nature in some regions, it can pose a challenge in ecological management.
