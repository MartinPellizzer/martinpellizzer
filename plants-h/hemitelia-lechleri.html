Hemitelia lechleri

Hemitelia lechleri, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and produces a milky sap known as latex. The plant is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and can grow up to 20 meters in height. Its latex has been historically used for producing natural rubber and has various traditional medicinal applications.
