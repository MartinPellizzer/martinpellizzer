Helwingia himalaica var. stenophylla

Helwingia himalaica var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf helwingia, is a species native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and the presence of stipules along the leaf stems.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and floral display.
