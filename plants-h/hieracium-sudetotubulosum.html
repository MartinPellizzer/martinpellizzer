Hieracium sudetotubulosum

Hieracium sudetotubulosum, commonly known as the Sudetic tubular hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of both ray and disc florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring moderate to moist conditions. Its leaves are basal, rough, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowering stems bear yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

It is often found in the mountainous regions of the Czech Republic and adjacent areas.
