Hieracium filistramineum

Hieracium filistramineum, commonly known as the Filistrum hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellowish to orange flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are usually alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It is considered a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates.
