Habenaria macroceratitis var. brevicalcarata

Habenaria macroceratitis var. brevicalcarata, commonly known as the short-spurred marsh orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish with prominent, short-spurred petals. It thrives in wetland and swampy habitats, often growing in dense colonies.

The plant is notable for its specialized pollination mechanisms and ecological importance in its native ecosystems.
