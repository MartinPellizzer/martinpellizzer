Hakea lucens

Hakea lucens, commonly known as the candle bush, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features striking, candle-like yellow flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant once established.

Hakea lucens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
