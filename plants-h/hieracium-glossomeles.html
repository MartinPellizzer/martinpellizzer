Hieracium glossomeles

Hieracium glossomeles, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a single head atop a hairy stem. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and often covered with fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.

Hieracium glossomeles is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.
