Helianthemum alpestre var. melanothrix

Helianthemum alpestre var. melanothrix, commonly known as the black-stemmed rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves and stems that are often tinged with purple or black, giving it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky gardens or alpine settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
