Hieracium fagersaetense

Hieracium fagersaetense, commonly known as the Fagerset's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has finely toothed leaves and produces numerous small, seed-bearing flowers.

It is often found in temperate regions and is considered a minor weed in some agricultural settings.
