Hieracium altocourelense

Hieracium altocourelense, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe and is characterized by its upright growth habit and flowering heads. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a flat-topped arrangement, and its leaves are lance-shaped with rough, hairy surfaces. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and slopes.

Hieracium altocourelense is adapted to cold climates and is a common component of high-altitude vegetation.
