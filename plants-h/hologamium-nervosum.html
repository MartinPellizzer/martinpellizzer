Hologamium nervosum

Hologamium nervosum, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white bloom, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid, shaded environments where it thrives.

Hologamium nervosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in home gardens.
