Haworthia mirabilis var. mundula

Haworthia mirabilis var. mundula, commonly known as the Moonshine Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
