Hieracium gelmianum

Hieracium gelmianum, commonly known as the Gelman's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium gelmianum is often found in disturbed areas and can spread readily through its rhizomes.
