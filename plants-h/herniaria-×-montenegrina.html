Herniaria × montenegrina

Herniaria × montenegrina, commonly known as the Montenegro herniaria, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and small, rounded leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. It is native to the Balkan region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
