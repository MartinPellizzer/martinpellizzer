Haworthia fasciata f. browniana

Haworthia fasciata f. browniana, commonly known as the Brownian Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with distinct brown or reddish markings. The leaves are often covered in a fine layer of white to grayish hair, giving it a unique, textured appearance. It produces small, star-shaped flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
