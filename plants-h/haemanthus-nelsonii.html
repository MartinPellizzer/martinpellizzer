Haemanthus nelsonii

Haemanthus nelsonii, commonly known as the Natal star.

It is a bulbous plant native to South Africa, characterized by its striking rosette of bright green, sword-shaped leaves. During the flowering season, it produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and requires minimal water.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
