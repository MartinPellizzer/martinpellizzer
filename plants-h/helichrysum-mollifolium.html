Helichrysum mollifolium

Helichrysum mollifolium, commonly known as the soft-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Helichrysum mollifolium is also valued for its use in herbal remedies and as a source of essential oils.
