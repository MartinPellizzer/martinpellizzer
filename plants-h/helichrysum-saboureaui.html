Helichrysum saboureaui

Helichrysum saboureaui, commonly known as the Cape gold flower, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features compact, aromatic foliage and produces clusters of bright yellow, sun-like flowers that resemble miniature suns. The plant is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy choice for coastal gardens. Its flowers are long-lasting when dried, often used in floral arrangements.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
