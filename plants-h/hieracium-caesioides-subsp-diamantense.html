Hieracium caesioides subsp. diamantense

Hieracium caesioides subsp.

diamantense, commonly known as the diamondweed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are hairy and have a distinctive diamond-shaped pattern, giving it its common name.

It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 cm.
