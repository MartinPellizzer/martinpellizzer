Hydrolea depauperata

Hydrolea depauperata, commonly known as the slender hydrolea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale yellow to greenish flowers. It is often found in coastal and inland areas with a Mediterranean climate.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
