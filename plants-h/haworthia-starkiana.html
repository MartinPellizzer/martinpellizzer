Haworthia starkiana

Haworthia starkiana, commonly known as the Stark's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green to bluish-green in color. The leaves are adorned with white to pale blue tubercles, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the cooler months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
