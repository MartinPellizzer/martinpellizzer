Hypochaeris Tenerifolia Var. Glabrescens

Hypochaeris tenerifolia var. glabrescens is a rare and unique plant native to the Canary Islands. It is known for its delicate yellow flowers and compact growth habit. This variant stands out due to its reduced hair coverage compared to other members of the species. It thrives in rocky and coastal environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare botanical gem.
