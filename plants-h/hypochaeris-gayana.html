Hypochaeris gayana

Hypochaeris gayana, commonly known as the yellow weed or golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

Hypochaeris gayana produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and it has a low-growing, herbaceous habit.
