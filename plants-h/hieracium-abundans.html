Hieracium abundans

Hieracium abundans, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a composite head composed of numerous small florets. The plant has basal leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive heart-shaped base. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, roadsides, and disturbed soils, and is native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium abundans is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
