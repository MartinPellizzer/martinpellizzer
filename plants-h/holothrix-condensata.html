Holothrix condensata

Holothrix condensata, commonly known as the dense thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, spiny growth habit and is typically found in rocky or dry soils. The plant produces small, purple or violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a somewhat succulent texture, adapted to conserve moisture.

Holothrix condensata is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
