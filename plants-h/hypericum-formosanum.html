Hypericum formosanum

Hypericum formosanum, commonly known as the Formosan St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and forest margins. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum formosanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
