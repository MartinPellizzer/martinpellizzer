Helichrysum monizii

Helichrysum monizii, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is part of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, papery bracts that give it a distinctive appearance, often mistaken for actual petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum monizii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
