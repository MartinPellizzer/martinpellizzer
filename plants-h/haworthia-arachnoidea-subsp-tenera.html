Haworthia arachnoidea subsp. tenera

Haworthia arachnoidea subsp.

tenera, commonly known as the spider plant or zebra plant, is a succulent native to South Africa. It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or yellow markings.

The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
