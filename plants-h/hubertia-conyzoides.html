Hubertia conyzoides

Hubertia conyzoides, commonly known as the desert daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, characterized by its fleshy, water-storing stems and leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its botanical features include a compact growth habit and a high tolerance for drought, making it well adapted to harsh conditions.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
