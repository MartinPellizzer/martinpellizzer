Hoya alexicaca

Hoya alexicaca, commonly known as the "Crying Lady" or "Wax Plant," is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces nectar-rich flowers that attract pollinators such as birds and insects. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy texture.

Hoya alexicaca thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
