Hoya lucyae

Hoya lucyae, commonly known as the Lucy's Waxplant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers. Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favored choice among indoor gardeners.
