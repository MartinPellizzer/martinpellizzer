Hieracium trollii

Hieracium trollii, commonly known as the Troll's Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are basal, rough, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Hieracium trollii is known for its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
