Helichrysum flammeiceps

Helichrysum flammeiceps, commonly known as the flame everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its bright yellow-orange flowers that resemble flames. The plant produces small, papery bracts that remain colorful even after the flowers have faded. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
