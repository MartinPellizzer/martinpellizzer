Hordeum hibernaculum

Hordeum hibernaculum, commonly known as winter barley, is a species of grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the production of small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Its seeds are used in traditional brewing and as a forage crop.

Hordeum hibernaculum is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and maintaining soil stability.
