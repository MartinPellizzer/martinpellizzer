Haworthia multifolia var. breueri

Haworthia multifolia var. breueri, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green with distinct white or pale markings, resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for succulent enthusiasts.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
