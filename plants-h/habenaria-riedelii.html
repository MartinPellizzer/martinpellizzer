Habenaria riedelii

Habenaria riedelii, commonly known as the Riedel's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. This terrestrial orchid typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip, which is a characteristic feature of many orchid species.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator species for undisturbed forest ecosystems.
