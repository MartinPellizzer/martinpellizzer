Hieracium muonioense

Hieracium muonioense, commonly known as the Muonio hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to northern Europe, particularly Finland and Sweden.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils in boreal and subarctic regions. It has hairy, basal leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.
