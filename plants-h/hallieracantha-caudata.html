Hallieracantha caudata

Hallieracantha caudata, commonly known as the long-spurred bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, with elongated spurs at the base of each petal. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hallieracantha caudata is valued in gardens for its ornamental flowers and drought tolerance.
