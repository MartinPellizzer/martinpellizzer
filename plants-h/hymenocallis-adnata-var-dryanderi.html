Hymenocallis adnata var. dryanderi

Hymenocallis adnata var. dryanderi, commonly known as the dryander's spider lily, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It features striking, white, bell-shaped flowers with a distinctive red stripe down the center. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and has a bulbous root system typical of the Amaryllis family. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
