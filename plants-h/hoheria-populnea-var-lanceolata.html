Hoheria populnea var. lanceolata

Hoheria populnea var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate hoheria, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
