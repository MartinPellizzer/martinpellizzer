Hydrocotyle sibthorpioides var. calcicola

Hydrocotyle sibthorpioides var. calcicola, commonly known as the limestone spurge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It typically grows in rocky, calcareous soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant features small, rounded leaves with a waxy coating, and it produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to dry conditions and has a shallow root system that helps it thrive in well-drained environments.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with specific soil requirements.
