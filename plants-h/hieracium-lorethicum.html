Hieracium lorethicum

Hieracium lorethicum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers, which are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

Hieracium lorethicum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
