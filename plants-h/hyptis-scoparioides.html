Hyptis scoparioides

Hyptis scoparioides, commonly known as the silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hyptis scoparioides is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
