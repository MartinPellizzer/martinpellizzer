Hibiscus boninensis

Hibiscus boninensis, commonly known as the Bonin hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Bonin Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a prominent central vein. Hibiscus boninensis thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in coastal or forested areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
