Hopea laevis

Hopea laevis, commonly known as the smooth hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The tree is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and elliptical leaves that are typically glabrous. It produces small, yellowish flowers and is often found in lowland dipterocarp forests.

Hopea laevis is valued for its timber, which is used in construction and furniture making.
