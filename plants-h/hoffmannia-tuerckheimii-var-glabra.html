Hoffmannia tuerckheimii var. glabra

Hoffmannia tuerckheimii var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth Hoffmannia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its native range.
