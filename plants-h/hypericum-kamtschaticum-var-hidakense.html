Hypericum kamtschaticum var. hidakense

Hypericum kamtschaticum var. hidakense, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
