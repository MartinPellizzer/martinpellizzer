Haworthia sessiliflora

Haworthia sessiliflora, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Haworthia sessiliflora is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its compact size and unique appearance.
