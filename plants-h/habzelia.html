Habzelia

Habzelia, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Habzelia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
