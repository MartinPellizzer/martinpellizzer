Hieracium florentinum subsp. subflorentinum

Hieracium florentinum subsp.

subflorentinum, commonly known as the Florentine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and flowering stems that bear yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has finely divided, linear leaves and produces numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.

This subspecies is native to southern Europe and is often found in Mediterranean regions.
