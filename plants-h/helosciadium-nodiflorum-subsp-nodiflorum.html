Helosciadium nodiflorum subsp. nodiflorum

Helosciadium nodiflorum subsp.

nodiflorum, commonly known as the nodiflorous helosciadium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

The plant features slender, branching stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, white to pale yellow, and arranged in loose clusters, blooming from late spring to early autumn.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
