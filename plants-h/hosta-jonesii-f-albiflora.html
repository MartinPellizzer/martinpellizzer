Hosta jonesii f. albiflora

Hosta jonesii f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered Jones hosta, is a cultivar of the Hosta plantain lily.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves that are often streaked with lighter green, creating a striking visual contrast. The plant produces showy, white flowers that bloom in late summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its unique foliage and elegant floral display.
