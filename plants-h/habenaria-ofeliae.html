Habenaria ofeliae

Habenaria ofeliae, commonly known as the Ofelia's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
