Hieracium krasanii subsp. pikujense

Hieracium krasanii subsp.

pikujense, commonly known as the Krasan hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Carpathian region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at moderate elevations. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in the summer.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
