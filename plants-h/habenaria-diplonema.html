Habenaria diplonema

Habenaria diplonema, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive pink and white coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
