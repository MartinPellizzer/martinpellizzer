Hedera helix var. corrugata

Hedera helix var. corrugata, commonly known as the corrugated ivy, is a variant of the common ivy characterized by its uniquely textured leaves.

The foliage features a distinctive corrugated or ridged pattern, giving it a striking visual appearance. It is a climbing or trailing evergreen vine that thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its adaptability to various soil types and moisture levels makes it a versatile choice for landscaping.
