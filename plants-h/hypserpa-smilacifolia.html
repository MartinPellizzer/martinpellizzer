Hypserpa smilacifolia

Hypserpa smilacifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf mistletoe, is a parasitic plant that grows on various host trees, primarily in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ecological role and ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
