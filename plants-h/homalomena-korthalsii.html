Homalomena korthalsii

Homalomena korthalsii, commonly known as the Korthals' arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are glossy and often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Homalomena korthalsii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
