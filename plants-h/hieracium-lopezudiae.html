Hieracium lopezudiae

Hieracium lopezudiae, commonly known as Lopez's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, such as slopes and gravelly soils, and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Lopez's hawkweed is often found in the Sierra Nevada and other mountainous regions of California.
