Hemsleya emeiensis

Hemsleya emeiensis, commonly known as the Emei Mountain Begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, ornate leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically red or pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with high elevation.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
