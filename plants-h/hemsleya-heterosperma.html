Hemsleya heterosperma

Hemsleya heterosperma, commonly known as the pink-flowered gesneriad, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to violet. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often with fleshy, succulent-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Hemsleya heterosperma is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
