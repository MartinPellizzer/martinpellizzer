Hemizonia congesta subsp. lutescens

Hemizonia congesta subsp.

lutescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered hemizonia, is a flowering plant native to South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features showy yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk, which is typical of the family. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a nectar source for various pollinators.
