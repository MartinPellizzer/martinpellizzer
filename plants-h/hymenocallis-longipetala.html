Hymenocallis longipetala

Hymenocallis longipetala, commonly known as the long-petaled hurricane lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a yellow throat, which bloom in the summer months. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves and produces a tall, slender stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Hymenocallis longipetala is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
