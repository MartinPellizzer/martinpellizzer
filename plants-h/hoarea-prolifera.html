Hoarea prolifera

Hoarea prolifera, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, long-lasting blooms. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Hoarea prolifera is known for its ability to produce numerous offspring through vegetative propagation.
