Hieracium oligophorum

Hieracium oligophorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, and it often grows in grassy areas and meadows. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
