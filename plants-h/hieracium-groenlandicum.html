Hieracium groenlandicum

Hieracium groenlandicum, commonly known as Greenland hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environments. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragweed.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens as an ornamental plant.
