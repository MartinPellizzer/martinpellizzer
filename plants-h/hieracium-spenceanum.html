Hieracium spenceanum

Hieracium spenceanum, commonly known as Spence's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Spence's hawkweed is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
