Hyptis leptostachys

Hyptis leptostachys, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. Its leaves are often fragrant and can be used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Hyptis leptostachys thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or disturbed areas.
