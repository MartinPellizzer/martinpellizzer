Hieracium issatchenkoi

Hieracium issatchenkoi, commonly known as the Issatchenk's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer, and it produces small, elongated achenes as its seeds.

Hieracium issatchenkoi is often found in high-altitude meadows and is adapted to cold, harsh climates.
