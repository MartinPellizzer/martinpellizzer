Hereroa uncipetala

Hereroa uncipetala, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Hereroa uncipetala is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper conditions.
