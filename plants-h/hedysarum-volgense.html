Hedysarum volgense

Hedysarum volgense, commonly known as the Volga milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in steppe and semi-desert environments.

Hedysarum volgense is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
