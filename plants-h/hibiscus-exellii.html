Hibiscus exellii

Hibiscus exellii, commonly known as the Cape rose or Exell's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate and have a rough texture, while its flowers bloom in late summer to autumn.

Hibiscus exellii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
