Helipterum manglesii var. maculatum

Helipterum manglesii var. maculatum, commonly known as the spotted everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands.

The leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, and the plant is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
