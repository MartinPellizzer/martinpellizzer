Hieracium transiens subsp. microtricholepium

Hieracium transiens subsp.

microtricholepium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its finely hairy leaves and stems, which give it its specific epithet "microtricholepium.". It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.

This plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.
