Hieracium polygrammum

Hieracium polygrammum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, with hairy stems and basal leaves. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Hieracium polygrammum is part of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
