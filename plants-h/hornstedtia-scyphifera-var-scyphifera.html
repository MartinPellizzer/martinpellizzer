Hornstedtia scyphifera var. scyphifera

Hornstedtia scyphifera var. scyphifera, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has a short, succulent stem and is known for its ability to thrive in coastal environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, powdery coating.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
