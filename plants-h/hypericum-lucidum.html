Hypericum lucidum

Hypericum lucidum, commonly known as glossy St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy green leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is part of the genus Hypericum, which includes many species known for their medicinal properties.

The plant's name reflects its shiny leaves and its association with the traditional St. John's wort family.
