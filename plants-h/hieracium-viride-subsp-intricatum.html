Hieracium viride subsp. intricatum

Hieracium viride subsp.

intricatum, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has a creeping growth habit and produces small, hairy stems that help it spread across the ground.

The subspecies intricatum is distinguished by its intricate leaf patterns and compact growth form.
