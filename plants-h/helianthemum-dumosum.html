Helianthemum dumosum

Helianthemum dumosum, commonly known as the dwarf sunflower or rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of around 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features dense, compact foliage with small, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with five petals and numerous stamens, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.
