Henriettella reticulata

Henriettella reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds with intricate reticulate venation patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests or rocky crevices. Its rhizome is slender and creeping, allowing it to spread gradually across the substrate.

Henriettella reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
