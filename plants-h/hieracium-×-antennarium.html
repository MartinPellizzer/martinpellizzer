Hieracium × antennarium

Hieracium × antennarium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically discoid and surrounded by ray florets. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world, often becoming invasive in certain regions. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Its vegetative propagation through rhizomes contributes to its ability to spread rapidly and form dense stands.
