Helenium autumnale var. canaliculatum

Helenium autumnale var. canaliculatum, commonly known as the canaliculate sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red-orange ray flowers and yellow disk flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for gardens and naturalized areas.

The plant is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
