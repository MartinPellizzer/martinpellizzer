Hypserpa reticulata

Hypserpa reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate net-like veining on the petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

Hypserpa reticulata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
