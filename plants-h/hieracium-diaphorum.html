Hieracium diaphorum

Hieracium diaphorum, commonly known as the ghostweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium diaphorum is often considered a weed due to its ability to spread rapidly in suitable habitats.
