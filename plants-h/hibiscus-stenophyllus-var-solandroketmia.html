Hibiscus stenophyllus var. solandroketmia

Hibiscus stenophyllus var. solandroketmia, commonly known as the Solandroketmia hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a contrasting red throat. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are velvety to the touch. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
