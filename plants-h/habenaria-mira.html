Habenaria mira

Habenaria mira, commonly known as the fairy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are grass-like, and it produces small, clustered blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Habenaria mira is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
