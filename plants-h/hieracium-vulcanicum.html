Hieracium vulcanicum

Hieracium vulcanicum, commonly known as the volcanic hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to volcanic regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, often reddish stems. The plant thrives in nutrient-rich, disturbed soils, often found near volcanic activity. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Hieracium vulcanicum is typically perennial and can grow up to 60 cm tall, with serrated leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.
