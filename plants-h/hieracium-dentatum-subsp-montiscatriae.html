Hieracium dentatum subsp. montiscatriae

Hieracium dentatum subsp.

montiscatriae, commonly known as the Pyrenean hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers arranged in composite heads.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges, and the plant is often found in alpine and subalpine regions. It is native to the Pyrenees and other mountainous areas of Europe.

This subspecies is adapted to rocky and dry habitats, showing resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
