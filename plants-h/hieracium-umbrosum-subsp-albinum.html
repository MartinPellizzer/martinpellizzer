Hieracium umbrosum subsp. albinum

Hieracium umbrosum subsp.

albinum, commonly known as the white hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
