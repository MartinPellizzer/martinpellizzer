Hieracium viride subsp. trachyticum

Hieracium viride subsp.

trachyticum, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow to orange inflorescences.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

This subspecies is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
