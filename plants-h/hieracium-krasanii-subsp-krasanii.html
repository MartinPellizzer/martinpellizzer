Hieracium krasanii subsp. krasanii

Hieracium krasanii subsp.

krasanii, commonly known as the Krasan's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It typically grows in dry meadows and rocky soils, exhibiting a compact growth habit with basal leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in dense inflorescences, which are composed of numerous small, daisy-like florets. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stem is upright and often branched.

This subspecies is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
