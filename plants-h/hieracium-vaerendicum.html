Hieracium vaerendicum

Hieracium vaerendicum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or serrated. Native to Europe, it is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

It is an invasive species in some regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
