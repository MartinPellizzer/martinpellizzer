Hieracium coleoides

Hieracium coleoides, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, rough-textured leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, disturbed soils such as meadows and roadside areas. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in temperate regions.
