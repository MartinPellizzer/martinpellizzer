Hemerocallis forrestii

Hemerocallis forrestii, commonly known as the Forrest daylily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Hemerocallidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces bright yellow to orange blooms that typically last only one day, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Hemerocallis forrestii is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
