Hieracium scoticum

Hieracium scoticum, commonly known as Scottish hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and road sides. It has hairy, rough leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom from June to August.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
