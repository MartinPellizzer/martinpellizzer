Hieracium chlorophilum

Hieracium chlorophilum, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium chlorophilum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
