Hoya ischnopus

Hoya ischnopus, commonly known as the silver star hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Hoya ischnopus is a trailing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

It is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
