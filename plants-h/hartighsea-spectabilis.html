Hartighsea spectabilis

Hartighsea spectabilis, commonly known as the spectral flamebush, is a strikingly ornamental shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from deep red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Additionally, its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable species in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
