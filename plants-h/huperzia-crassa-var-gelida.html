Huperzia crassa var. gelida

Huperzia crassa var. gelida, commonly known as the frosty huperzia, is a perennial fern native to the cold regions of northern Asia.

It belongs to the family Huperziaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant has thick, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh, alpine conditions. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes.

Its unique morphology and resilience make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
