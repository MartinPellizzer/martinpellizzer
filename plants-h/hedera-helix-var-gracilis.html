Hedera helix var. gracilis

Hedera helix var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender ivy, is a variety of English ivy characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves.

It is a climbing evergreen vine native to Europe and thrives in shaded, moist environments. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, often with a lighter green underside. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping or as a houseplant.

It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by dark blue berries, though these are toxic to humans and animals.
