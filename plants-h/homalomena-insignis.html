Homalomena insignis

Homalomena insignis, commonly known as the red lily or red spider-orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with red veins. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically red or orange with a yellow throat, resembling orchids. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Homalomena insignis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
