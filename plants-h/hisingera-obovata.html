Hisingera obovata

Hisingera obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has broad, ovate-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Hisingera obovata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
