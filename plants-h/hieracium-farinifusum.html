Hieracium farinifusum

Hieracium farinifusum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems, which give it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium farinifusum is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
