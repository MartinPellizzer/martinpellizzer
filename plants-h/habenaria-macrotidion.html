Habenaria macrotidion

Habenaria macrotidion, commonly known as the large-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has erect, branched stems and linear to lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
