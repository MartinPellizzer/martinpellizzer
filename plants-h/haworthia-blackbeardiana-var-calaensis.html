Haworthia blackbeardiana var. calaensis

Haworthia blackbeardiana var. calaensis, commonly known as the Blackbeard Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal watering to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
