Habenaria stocksii

Habenaria stocksii, commonly known as the Stocks' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features a slender rhizome with a dense cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns. Its blossoms are borne on tall, erect stems and often bloom in late summer or early autumn.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
