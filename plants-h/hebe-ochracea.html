Hebe ochracea

Hebe ochracea, commonly known as the ochre hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. It is also valued for its ability to attract native pollinators such as bees and birds.
