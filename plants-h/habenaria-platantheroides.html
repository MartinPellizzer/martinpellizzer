Habenaria platantheroides

Habenaria platantheroides, commonly known as the slender marsh helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive, hood-like structure that protects the reproductive organs.

It produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.
