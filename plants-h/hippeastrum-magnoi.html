Hippeastrum magnoi

Hippeastrum magnoi, commonly known as the Giant Amazonian Aroid, is a striking tropical plant native to the Amazon basin.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Hippeastrum magnoi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
