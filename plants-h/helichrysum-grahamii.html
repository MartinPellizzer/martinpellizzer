Helichrysum grahamii

Helichrysum grahamii, commonly known as the Graham's everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, yellowish to orange flowers that are often arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance.

Helichrysum grahamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in dried flower arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
