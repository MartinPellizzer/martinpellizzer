Hypochaeris radicata subsp. radicata

Hypochaeris radicata subsp.

radicata, commonly known as common sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, spiny fruits. It is native to Europe but has been naturalized in many parts of the world. Hypochaeris radicata subsp.

radicata is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
