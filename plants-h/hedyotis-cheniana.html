Hedyotis cheniana

Hedyotis cheniana, commonly known as the Chinese spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial that typically reaches heights of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hedyotis cheniana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks.
