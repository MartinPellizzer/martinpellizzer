Hydrangea paniculata var. laeta

Hydrangea paniculata var. laeta, commonly known as the smooth hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil. Its foliage is typically dark green, and it produces small, pale yellow flowers in the spring.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
