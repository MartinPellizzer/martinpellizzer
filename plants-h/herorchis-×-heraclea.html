Herorchis × heraclea

Herorchis × heraclea, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Herorchis and Heraclea species.

It is a herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America. The plant features showy, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
