Hylotelephium sieboldii var. chinense

Hylotelephium sieboldii var. chinense, commonly known as the Chinese stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to East Asia.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces showy clusters of pink or red flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, making it a hardy species for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
