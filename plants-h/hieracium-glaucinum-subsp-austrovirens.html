Hieracium glaucinum subsp. austrovirens

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

austrovirens, commonly known as the Austrian yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellowish-green leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

This subspecies is native to Central and Eastern Europe, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a creeping growth habit with numerous flowering stems.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
