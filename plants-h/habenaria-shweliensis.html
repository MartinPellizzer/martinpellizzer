Habenaria shweliensis

Habenaria shweliensis, commonly known as the Shweli orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and help anchor it to the soil while absorbing nutrients.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
