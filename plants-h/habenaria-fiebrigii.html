Habenaria fiebrigii

Habenaria fiebrigii, commonly known as the Fiebrig's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually arranged in a loose raceme and bloom during the spring or early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
