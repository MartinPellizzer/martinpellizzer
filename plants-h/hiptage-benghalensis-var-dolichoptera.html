Hiptage benghalensis var. dolichoptera

Hiptage benghalensis var. dolichoptera, commonly known as the long-flowered hiptage, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its flowering stems are often elongated and bear multiple inflorescences.

The species is valued in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
