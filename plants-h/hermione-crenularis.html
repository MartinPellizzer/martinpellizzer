Hermione crenularis

Hermione crenularis, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, surrounded by narrow, silver-gray leaves. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its foliage and flowers provide a delicate, elegant appearance throughout the growing season.
