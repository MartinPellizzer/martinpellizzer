Hieracium chibinicola

Hieracium chibinicola, commonly known as the Chibinica Hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the Chibin Mountains of Kazakhstan. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it thrives in well-drained soils. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
