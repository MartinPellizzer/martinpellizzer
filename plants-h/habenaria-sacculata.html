Habenaria sacculata

Habenaria sacculata, commonly known as the sacculate orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually linear and arranged in two ranks, and it produces a single raceme of flowers.

The orchid thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
