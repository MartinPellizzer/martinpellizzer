Hieracium basifolium subsp. kemiticum

Hieracium basifolium subsp.

kemiticum, commonly known as the Kemitic groundsel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments.

The plant features basal leaves that are usually broad and lobed, with hairy surfaces. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
