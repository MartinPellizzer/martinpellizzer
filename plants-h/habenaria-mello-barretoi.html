Habenaria mello-barretoi

Habenaria mello-barretoi, commonly known as the Mello-Barreto's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
