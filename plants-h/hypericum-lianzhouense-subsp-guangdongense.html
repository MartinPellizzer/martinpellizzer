Hypericum lianzhouense subsp. guangdongense

Hypericum lianzhouense subsp.

guangdongense, commonly known as the Guangdong St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant species native to southern China. It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
