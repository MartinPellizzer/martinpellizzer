Hebe dieffenbachii

Hebe dieffenbachii, commonly known as the mountain hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom in spring and early summer. Hebe dieffenbachii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value contribute to its widespread cultivation in temperate climates.
