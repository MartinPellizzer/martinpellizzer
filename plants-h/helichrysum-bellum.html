Helichrysum bellum

Helichrysum bellum, commonly known as the golden crown or common helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow to golden-orange flowers that resemble tiny suns. The plant has wiry, hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum bellum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
