Hieracium pseudomurorum

Hieracium pseudomurorum, commonly known as the false mouse-ear hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium pseudomurorum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to spread rapidly.
