Hosta montana var. liliiflora

Hosta montana var. liliiflora, commonly known as the Mountain Hosta, is a cultivar of the Hosta plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy, and often variegated leaves that range in color from light green to white and deep green. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple, which appear in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in woodland settings.
