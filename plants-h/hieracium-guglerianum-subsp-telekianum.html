Hieracium guglerianum subsp. telekianum

Hieracium guglerianum subsp.

telekianum, commonly known as the Telekianum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has finely divided leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and localized species within its native range.
