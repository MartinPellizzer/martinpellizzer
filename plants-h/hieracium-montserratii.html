Hieracium montserratii

Hieracium montserratii, commonly known as theMontserrat hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Pyrenees Mountains.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in rocky and well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a compact cluster.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to soil stabilization in mountainous regions.
