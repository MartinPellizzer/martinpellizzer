Hieracium panneiceps

Hieracium panneiceps, commonly known as the purple spearhead hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows, where it can thrive in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with purple veins.

Hieracium panneiceps is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain regions.
