Hylotelephium stepposum

Hylotelephium stepposum, commonly known as the rock rose or stonecrop, is a perennial succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Hylotelephium stepposum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
