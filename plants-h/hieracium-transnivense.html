Hieracium transnivense

Hieracium transnivense, commonly known as the snow hieracium, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which help protect it from cold temperatures. It thrives in rocky and gravelly soils at high altitudes.

Due to its specialized habitat, it is considered a rare and sensitive species.
