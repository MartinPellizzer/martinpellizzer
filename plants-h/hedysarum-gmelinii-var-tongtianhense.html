Hedysarum gmelinii var. tongtianhense

Hedysarum gmelinii var. tongtianhense, commonly known as the Tibetan milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the Tibetan Plateau.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments and is adapted to cold, arid conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

Its seeds are edible and have been traditionally used in local cuisines.
