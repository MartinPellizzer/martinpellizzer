Hibiscus ovalifolius

Hibiscus ovalifolius, commonly known as the oval-leaf hibiscus, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hibiscus ovalifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
