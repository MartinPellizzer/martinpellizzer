Hypericum perryongii

Hypericum perryongii, commonly known as the Korean St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant belonging to the genus Hypericum in the family Clusiaceae.

It is native to regions of East Asia, particularly Korea, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.
