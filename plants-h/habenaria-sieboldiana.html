Habenaria sieboldiana

Habenaria sieboldiana, commonly known as the Siebold's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with white and purple markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are often covered in a dense, fibrous sheath, which aids in water retention.

The orchid is noted for its fragrant blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
