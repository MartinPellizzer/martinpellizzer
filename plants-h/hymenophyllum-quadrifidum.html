Hymenophyllum quadrifidum

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance and is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Hymenophyllum quadrifidum plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms in its ecological niche.
