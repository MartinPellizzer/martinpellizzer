Hohenbergia catingae var. catingae

Hohenbergia catingae var. catingae, commonly known as the Catinga Hohenbergia, is a unique bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped inflorescences with bright red bracts that contrast beautifully with its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
