Huperzia stemmermanniae

Huperzia stemmermanniae, commonly known as the stemmermann's huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and dense clusters of spore-bearing leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices, where it can thrive in cool and humid conditions. Its fronds are usually short and stiff, with a leathery texture, and they often exhibit a dark green coloration.

Huperzia stemmermanniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance and ecological significance.
