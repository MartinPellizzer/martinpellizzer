Helichrysum alatum

Helichrysum alatum, commonly known as the silver leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its aromatic leaves and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Helichrysum alatum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
