Haemaria argyroneura

Haemaria argyroneura, commonly known as the silver-edged nettle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes other members commonly known as nettles. The plant is characterized by its finely toothed leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs and have a silvery edge, which gives it its common name.
