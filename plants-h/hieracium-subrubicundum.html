Hieracium subrubicundum

Hieracium subrubicundum, commonly known as the red-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, reddish stems and yellow daisy-like flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, clustered flower heads.

The plant is often found in sunny, open habitats and is known for its ornamental appeal.
