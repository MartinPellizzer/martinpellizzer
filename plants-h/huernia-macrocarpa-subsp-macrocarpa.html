Huernia macrocarpa subsp. macrocarpa

Huernia macrocarpa subsp.

macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant horned cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that can grow up to 1 meter in length.

The plant produces large, fleshy, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for collectors and desert gardeners.
