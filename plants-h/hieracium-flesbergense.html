Hieracium flesbergense

Hieracium flesbergense, commonly known as the Flesberg hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats and features bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.

Hieracium flesbergense is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
