Hemipilia tibetica

Hemipilia tibetica, commonly known as the Tibetan hemipilia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau and adjacent areas in China. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged spirally along the stem.

Hemipilia tibetica is adapted to cold, arid environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
