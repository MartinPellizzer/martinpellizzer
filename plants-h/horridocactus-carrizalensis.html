Horridocactus carrizalensis

Horridocactus carrizalensis, commonly known as the Carrizalensis cactus, is a species native to the Sonoran Desert in northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines and a distinctive yellowish-green color. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
