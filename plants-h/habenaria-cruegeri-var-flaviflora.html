Habenaria cruegeri var. flaviflora

Habenaria cruegeri var. flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with erect, linear leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
