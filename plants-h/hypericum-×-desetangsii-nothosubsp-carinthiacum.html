Hypericum × desetangsii nothosubsp. carinthiacum

Hypericum × desetangsii nothosubsp.

carinthiacum, commonly known as the Carinthian St. John's wort, is a hybrid plant native to the Alps. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in summer and are arranged in clusters.

The plant has woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
