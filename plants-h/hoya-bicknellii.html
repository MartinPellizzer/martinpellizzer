Hoya bicknellii

Hoya bicknellii, commonly known as the Bicknell's waxplant, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, round leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It is a tropical vine that thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Hoya bicknellii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
