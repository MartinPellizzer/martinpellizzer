Hieracium perluteum

Hieracium perluteum, commonly known as the white hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its flowers are yellow to orange in color, with white ray florets that give it a distinctive appearance.

The leaves are hairy and lance-shaped, often with a rough texture.
