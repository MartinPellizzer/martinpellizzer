Homalomena burkilliana

Homalomena burkilliana, commonly known as Burkill's arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. Homalomena burkilliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
