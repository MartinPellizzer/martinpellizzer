Hieracium phaeopsarum

Hieracium phaeopsarum, commonly known as the pale-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium phaeopsarum is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
