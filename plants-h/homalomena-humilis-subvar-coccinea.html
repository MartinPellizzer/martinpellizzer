Homalomena humilis subvar. coccinea

Homalomena humilis subvar. coccinea, commonly known as the red-veined arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with striking red veins against a dark green background. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically borne on a spadix surrounded by a spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.
