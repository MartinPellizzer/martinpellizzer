Hoya salmonea

Hoya salmonea, commonly known as the salmon hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its waxy, ovate leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces showy, salmon-pink to red blooms that appear in late summer to early autumn. It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
