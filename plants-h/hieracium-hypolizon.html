Hieracium hypolizon

Hieracium hypolizon, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, which is typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

Hieracium hypolizon is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
