Hippeastrum australe

Hippeastrum australe, commonly known as the Australian amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces striking, strap-like leaves that are broad and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum australe is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home garden.
