Hieracium subunctiusculum

Hieracium subunctiusculum, commonly known as the small yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish to greenish composite flowers and hairy stems. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, disturbed soils such as meadows and roadsides. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is often found in temperate climates and is a common weed in agricultural areas.
