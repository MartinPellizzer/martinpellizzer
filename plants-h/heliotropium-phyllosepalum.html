Heliotropium phyllosepalum

Heliotropium phyllosepalum, commonly known as the silverleaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, violet or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and easy care requirements make it a desirable addition to gardens and landscapes.
