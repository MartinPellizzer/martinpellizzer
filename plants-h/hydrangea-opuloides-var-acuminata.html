Hydrangea opuloides var. acuminata

Hydrangea opuloides var. acuminata, commonly known as the Acuminata hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from blue to pink depending on soil pH. The plant has ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and landscape design.
