Hypericum ascyron var. adamii

Hypericum ascyron var. adamii, commonly known as the yellow hypericum or Adam's hypericum, is a perennial shrub native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long flowering period and ability to attract pollinators.
