Handroanthus piutinga

Handroanthus piutinga, commonly known as the pink trumpet tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink or red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a deciduous tree or shrub, often growing in humid lowland areas. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
