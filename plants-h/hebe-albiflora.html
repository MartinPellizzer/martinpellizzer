Hebe albiflora

Hebe albiflora, commonly known as the silverflower hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine silvery-white down. Its flowers are small, white, and tubular, appearing in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Hebe albiflora is well adapted to coastal environments and thrives in well-drained soils.
