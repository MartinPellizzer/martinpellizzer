Hippeastrum Carinatum

Hippeastrum carinatum is a striking member of the amaryllis family known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers. Native to South America, this plant thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its unique, wavy petals and bold colors make it a popular choice for gardeners and floral enthusiasts. Easy to care for, it requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight to flourish. This plant adds a touch of exotic beauty to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
