Hieracium radiosum

Hieracium radiosum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, including North America. It typically grows in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun.

Hieracium radiosum is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
