Haworthia tisleyi

Haworthia tisleyi, commonly known as the Tisley's Haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Haworthia tisleyi is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
