Hymenocallis adnata var. acutifolia

Hymenocallis adnata var. acutifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a prominent green throat. The plant has strap-like leaves that are narrow and pointed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
