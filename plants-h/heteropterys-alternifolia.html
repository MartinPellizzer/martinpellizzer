Heteropterys alternifolia

Heteropterys alternifolia, commonly known as the zigzag tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves that often have a zigzag or undulating margin. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in terminal panicles, followed by edible, fleshy berries. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitats.
