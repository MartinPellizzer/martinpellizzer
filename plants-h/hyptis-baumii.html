Hyptis baumii

Hyptis baumii, commonly known as the Brazilian hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its gray-green foliage and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Hyptis baumii thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in open woodlands and disturbed areas.
