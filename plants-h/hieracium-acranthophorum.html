Hieracium acranthophorum

Hieracium acranthophorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and have a serrated edge. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

It is often found in moist environments and can form dense colonies in suitable conditions.
