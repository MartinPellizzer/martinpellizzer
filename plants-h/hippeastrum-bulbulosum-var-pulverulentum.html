Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. pulverulentum

Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. pulverulentum, commonly known as the dusty bulbous amaryllis, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant features a bulbous stem and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its dramatic blooms and relatively easy care requirements, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
