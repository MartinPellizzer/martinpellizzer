Hibiscus furcellatus var. azuensis

Hibiscus furcellatus var. azuensis, commonly known as the Azuensian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Hibiscus furcellatus var. azuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
