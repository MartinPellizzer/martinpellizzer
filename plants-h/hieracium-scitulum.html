Hieracium scitulum

Hieracium scitulum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium scitulum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to colonize various habitats.
