Haworthia nigra var. diversifolia

Haworthia nigra var. diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with striking white or yellow variegation. The leaves are often adorned with translucent windows, allowing light to penetrate to the plant's core. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

This plant is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
