Habenaria parvifolia

Habenaria parvifolia, commonly known as the small-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a loose raceme. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are linear and often covered with a fine pubescence.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
