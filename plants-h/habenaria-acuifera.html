Habenaria acuifera

Habenaria acuifera, commonly known as the sharp-leaved orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
