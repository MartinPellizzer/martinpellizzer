Hyoscyamus senecionis var. multifidus

Hyoscyamus senecionis var. multifidus, commonly known as the multifid horsebean, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils, and is often found in the western United States.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
