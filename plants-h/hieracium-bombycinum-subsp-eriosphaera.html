Hieracium bombycinum subsp. eriosphaera

Hieracium bombycinum subsp.

eriosphaera, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It is characterized by its hairy, dark green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has a taproot system and produces numerous flowering stems.

Its subspecies name, *eriosphaera*, refers to the woolly appearance of its flower heads.
