Helicophyllum rauwolffii

Helicophyllum rauwolffii, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a unique member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is native to regions of southern Africa and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.
