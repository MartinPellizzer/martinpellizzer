Hieracium florentinum subsp. obscurum

Hieracium florentinum subsp.

obscurum, commonly known as the dark hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers.

The subspecies obscurum is distinguished by its darker, more subdued flower color compared to other varieties of Hieracium. It typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, preferring partial shade.

The plant has finely divided leaves and produces numerous small, elongated flowers that bloom in the summer months.
