Hieracium elegantulum

Hieracium elegantulum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant has upright stems with lance-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers in clusters. It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.

Due to its low-growing habit, it can be challenging to control once established.
