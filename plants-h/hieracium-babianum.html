Hieracium babianum

Hieracium babianum, commonly known as the Babian Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium babianum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in its native habitat.
