Hydrostachys lukungensis

Hydrostachys lukungensis, commonly known as the Lukungensis waterwheel, is a unique aquatic plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of narrow, ribbon-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as lakes and rivers, where it can anchor itself to submerged surfaces. Its reproductive structures are small and inconspicuous, contributing to its limited distribution.

Due to its specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
