Himatanthus stenophyllus

Himatanthus stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its spadix and spathe structure. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered around a central spadix, typical of the genus Himatanthus. Its leaves are narrow and arrow-shaped, giving it its common name.
