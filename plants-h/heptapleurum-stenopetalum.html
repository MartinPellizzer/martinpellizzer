Heptapleurum stenopetalum

Heptapleurum stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled heptapleurum, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
