Haworthia cyanea var. amethysta

Haworthia cyanea var. amethysta, commonly known as the amethyst haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white or pale blue markings. The leaves are often tinged with a striking purple or amethyst hue, especially in bright light. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white to pale purple flowers.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
