Hieracium sommerfeltii subsp. clusii

Hieracium sommerfeltii subsp.

clusii, commonly known as the Clusius's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has basal leaves that are usually lanceolate and covered in fine hairs.

Its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the genus Hieracium.
