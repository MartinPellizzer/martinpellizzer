Homalomena nieuwenhuisii

Homalomena nieuwenhuisii, commonly known as the Nieuwenhuis' arrowleaf.

It is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often deeply veined. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It prefers warm, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Its ornamental value is due to its striking foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
