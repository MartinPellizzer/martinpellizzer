Hesperidopsis circinatus

Hesperidopsis circinatus, commonly known as the curled leaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
