Habenaria stylites subsp. stylites

Habenaria stylites subsp.

stylites, commonly known as the slender spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia. It typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often near streams or in mountainous regions.

The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single spike of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are elongated and often clustered at the base of the stem.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
