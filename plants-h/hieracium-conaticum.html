Hieracium conaticum

Hieracium conaticum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium conaticum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
