Hypoxis luzuloides

Hypoxis luzuloides, commonly known as the blue star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a dense cluster at the top of the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoxis luzuloides is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
