Humulus neomexicanus

Humulus neomexicanus, commonly known as New Mexico hops, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Cannabaceae family and is closely related to hops and cannabis. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically pollinated by wind. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

This species is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
