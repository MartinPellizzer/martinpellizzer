Hieracium cernuiforme subsp. atriceps

Hieracium cernuiforme subsp.

atriceps, commonly known as the black-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flower heads.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dark, almost black, central disc florets, which contrast with the lighter outer rays. Native to Europe, it often grows in meadows and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
