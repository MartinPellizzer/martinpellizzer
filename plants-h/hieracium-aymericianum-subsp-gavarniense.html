Hieracium aymericianum subsp. gavarniense

Hieracium aymericianum subsp.

gavarniense, commonly known as the Gavarni hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, bract-like structures around its flower heads.
