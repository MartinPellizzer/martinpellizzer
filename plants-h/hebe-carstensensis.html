Hebe carstensensis

Hebe carstensensis, commonly known as the Stewart Island hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows in the temperate forests of Stewart Island. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Hebe carstensensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
