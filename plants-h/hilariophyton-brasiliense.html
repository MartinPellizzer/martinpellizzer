Hilariophyton brasiliense

Hilariophyton brasiliense, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hilariophyton brasiliense is also valued for its ability to provide shade and support local wildlife.
