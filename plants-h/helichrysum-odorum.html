Helichrysum odorum

Helichrysum odorum, commonly known as the sweet everlasting or golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and showy yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in dense, flat clusters. Its aromatic foliage and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Helichrysum odorum is also valued for its use in essential oils and traditional medicine.
