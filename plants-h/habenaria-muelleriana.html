Habenaria muelleriana

Habenaria muelleriana, commonly known as the Mueller's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. It typically blooms during the warmer months, often in spring or early summer.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses, though it requires specific conditions to thrive.
