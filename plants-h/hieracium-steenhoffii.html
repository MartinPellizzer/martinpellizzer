Hieracium steenhoffii

Hieracium steenhoffii, commonly known as the Steenhoff's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium steenhoffii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
