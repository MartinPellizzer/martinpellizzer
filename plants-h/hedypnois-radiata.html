Hedypnois radiata

Hedypnois radiata, commonly known as the radiant hedypnois, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescences that produce small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale yellow.
