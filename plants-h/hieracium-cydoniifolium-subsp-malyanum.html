Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp. malyanum

Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp.

malyanum, commonly known as the Malyan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Central Asia. It typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape, with hairy surfaces. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a composite head, and bloom from late summer to early autumn.

It is characterized by its robust growth and is often found in dry, sunny environments.
