Hypericum songaricum

Hypericum songaricum, commonly known as the Songaric St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
