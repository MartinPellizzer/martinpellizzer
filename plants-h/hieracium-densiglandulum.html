Hieracium densiglandulum

Hieracium densiglandulum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its yellow flowers bloom from June to September, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense colonies.

It has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in certain ecosystems.
