Hypericum lalandii var. valderamosum

Hypericum lalandii var. valderamosum, commonly known as the Valderamos hypericum, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils, often in mountainous regions. It features small, elliptical leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
