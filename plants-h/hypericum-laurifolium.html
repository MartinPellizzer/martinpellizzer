Hypericum laurifolium

Hypericum laurifolium, commonly known as the evergreen St. John's wort, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are elliptical and glossy, giving it a lush, evergreen appearance. The plant is characterized by its aromatic foliage and is often found in rocky or dry soils. It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is known for its medicinal properties.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is cultivated in gardens and used in traditional medicine.
