Haussmannianthes jucunda

Haussmannianthes jucunda, commonly known as the Happy Tree, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with glossy, dark green leaves. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and a fibrous root system that helps it thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

It is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
