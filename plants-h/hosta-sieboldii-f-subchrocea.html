Hosta sieboldii f. subchrocea

Hosta sieboldii f. subchrocea, commonly known as the Siebold's Hosta variegated form, is a cultivar of the Hosta genus native to East Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a striking variegation pattern of white and yellow margins. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often grown as an ornamental foliage plant. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for shaded garden settings.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, thriving in cooler climates.
