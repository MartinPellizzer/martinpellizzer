Habenaria bractescens

Habenaria bractescens, commonly known as the bracteate orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
