Hydrolea spinosa f. purpurascens

Hydrolea spinosa f. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered hydrolea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright, spiny stems and striking purple to pink flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and attractive foliage.
