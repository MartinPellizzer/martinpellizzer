Hieracium aphyllum subsp. neosingulare

Hieracium aphyllum subsp.

neosingulare, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its reduced leaf structure and elongated stem. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species in some regions.
