Hemitelia caudiculata

Hemitelia caudiculata, commonly known as the slender-stemmed hemitelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody, cylindrical stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps reduce water loss.

Hemitelia caudiculata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
