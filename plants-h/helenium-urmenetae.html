Helenium urmenetae

Helenium urmenetae, commonly known as the red swamp tarplant, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking red to orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps, and prefers moist, sandy soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Helenium urmenetae is an important species for wetland ecosystems, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
