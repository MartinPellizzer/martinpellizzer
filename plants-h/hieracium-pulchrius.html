Hieracium pulchrius

Hieracium pulchrius, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a single, showy head. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Native to Europe, it is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
