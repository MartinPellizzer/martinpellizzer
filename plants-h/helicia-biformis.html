Helicia biformis

Helicia biformis, commonly known as the two-lobed helicia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. Helicia biformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
