Hereroa crassa

Hereroa crassa, commonly known as the thick-leaved ericoid, is a shrub native to the eastern regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, often producing a subtle fragrance.

Hereroa crassa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal and sandy environments.
