Helianthemum ocymoides

Helianthemum ocymoides, commonly known as the rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has dense, rounded foliage with small, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand dry, rocky conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
