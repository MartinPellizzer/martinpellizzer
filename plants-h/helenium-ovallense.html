Helenium ovallense

Helenium ovallense, commonly known as the ovalleaf arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. Its flowers are typically yellow to orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Helenium ovallense thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
