Hieracium gramosicum

Hieracium gramosicum, commonly known as meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant has upright stems with lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which help reduce water loss.

Hieracium gramosicum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
