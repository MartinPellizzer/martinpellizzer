Habenaria gerrardii

Habenaria gerrardii, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Habenaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces a single spike of flowers at the top of the stem.

Habenaria gerrardii is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
