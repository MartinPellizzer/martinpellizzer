Hemigenia saligna

Hemigenia saligna, commonly known as the slender hemigenia, is a small shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

Hemigenia saligna is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as rainforests and along streamlines.
