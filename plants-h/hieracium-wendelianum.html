Hieracium wendelianum

Hieracium wendelianum, commonly known as Wendel's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Wendel's hawkweed is often found in temperate climates and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
