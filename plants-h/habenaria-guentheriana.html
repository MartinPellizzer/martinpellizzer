Habenaria guentheriana

Habenaria guentheriana, commonly known as the Guenther's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces small, oval-shaped pseudobulbs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
