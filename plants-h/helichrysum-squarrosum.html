Helichrysum squarrosum

Helichrysum squarrosum, commonly known as the woolly everlasting, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, woolly foliage and small, golden-yellow flowers that resemble daisies. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its aromatic leaves and long-lasting blooms make it both ornamental and useful in traditional medicine.

Helichrysum squarrosum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
