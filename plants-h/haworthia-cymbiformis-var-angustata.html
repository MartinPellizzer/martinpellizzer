Haworthia cymbiformis var. angustata

Haworthia cymbiformis var. angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green with white or pale markings. The leaves are narrow and often have translucent windows, which help in photosynthesis. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and compact size.
