Habenaria bacata

Habenaria bacata, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, branched inflorescence that bears numerous small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

The orchid is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
