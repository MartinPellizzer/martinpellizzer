Heliotropium luzonicum subsp. angustissimum

Heliotropium luzonicum subsp.

angustissimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines. It is characterized by its small, pinkish to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season.
