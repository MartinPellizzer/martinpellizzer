Hieracium ustulatum var. nigritellum

Hieracium ustulatum var. nigritellum, commonly known as the black-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant features dark green, hairy leaves that are often tinged with purple or black, giving it its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
