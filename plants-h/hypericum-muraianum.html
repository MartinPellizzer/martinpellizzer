Hypericum muraianum

Hypericum muraianum, commonly known as the Indian St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from June to September. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while its stems are often reddish in color.

Hypericum muraianum is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.
