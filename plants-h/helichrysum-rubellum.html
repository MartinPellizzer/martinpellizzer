Helichrysum rubellum

Helichrysum rubellum, commonly known as the red everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and vibrant red-orange flowers. The plant produces small, tightly packed flower heads that resemble tiny globes, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Helichrysum rubellum is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
