Hypericum kotoliani

Hypericum kotoliani, commonly known as the Kashmir goldenstar, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude regions, often found in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a vibrant splash of color to its natural habitat. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum kotoliani is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
