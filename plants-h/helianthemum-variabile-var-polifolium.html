Helianthemum variabile var. polifolium

Helianthemum variabile var. polifolium, commonly known as the variable rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
