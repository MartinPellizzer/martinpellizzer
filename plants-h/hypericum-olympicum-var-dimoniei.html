Hypericum olympicum var. dimoniei

Hypericum olympicum var. dimoniei, commonly known as the Olympic goldenstar, is a compact shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has narrow, upright leaves and a dense, bushy growth habit. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant collections and ecological landscapes.
