Helipterum jessenii

Helipterum jessenii, commonly known as the Jessen's everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces numerous small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.

Helipterum jessenii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
