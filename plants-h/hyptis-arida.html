Hyptis arida

Hyptis arida, commonly known as sand hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, gray-green leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or violet flowers. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Hyptis arida is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
