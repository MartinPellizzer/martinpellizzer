Helianthemum marifolium var. leucothrix

Helianthemum marifolium var. leucothrix, commonly known as the white-flowered rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate floral display to rocky or coastal habitats. The plant has compact, silver-gray foliage that is drought-tolerant and well-suited to Mediterranean climates. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a valued addition to native plant collections.
