Huperzia ledermannii

Huperzia ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hypnaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, sparsely branched stems and small, ovate to elliptic leaves. The plant produces sporangia in clusters on the upper surface of the leaves, which are typically found in moist, shaded environments. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, tropical climates.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
