Hieracium adnatifrons

Hieracium adnatifrons, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, and its leaves are narrow and hairy. It is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense patches in its habitat.

The species is characterized by its lack of leaf stalks and its distinctive, elongated flower heads.
