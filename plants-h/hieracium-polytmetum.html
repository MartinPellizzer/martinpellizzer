Hieracium polytmetum

Hieracium polytmetum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange-red flowers and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branched.

Hieracium polytmetum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in many regions.
