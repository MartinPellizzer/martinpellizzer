Hieracium scorzonerifolium subsp. antaleonis

Hieracium scorzonerifolium subsp.

antaleonis, commonly known as the Antaleonis hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape, with rough, hairy surfaces. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and it produces small, hairy achenes as seeds.

Botanically, it is distinguished by its specific leaf morphology and floral structure, which align with its classification within the genus Hieracium.
