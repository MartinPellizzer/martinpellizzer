Habenaria ternatea

Habenaria ternatea, commonly known as the Ternate orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that often has a contrasting color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
