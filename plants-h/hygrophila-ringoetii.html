Hygrophila ringoetii

Hygrophila ringoetii, commonly known as the water hyssop, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

Its small, fragrant flowers are typically blue or purple and are arranged in whorled clusters along the stems.

Hygrophila ringoetii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in aquatic gardens or as a water purifier.
