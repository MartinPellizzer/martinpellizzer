Hartia robusta

Hartia robusta, commonly known as the robust hartia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It features erect, branching stems that are often covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

Hartia robusta is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
