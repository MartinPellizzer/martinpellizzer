Hieracium balkanum

Hieracium balkanum, commonly known as the Balkan hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.
