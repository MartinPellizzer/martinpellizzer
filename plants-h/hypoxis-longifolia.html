Hypoxis longifolia

Hypoxis longifolia, commonly known as the African star of Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoxis longifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
