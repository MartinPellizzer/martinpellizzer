Hoya planiflora

Hoya planiflora, commonly known as the flat-leaf waxplant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Hoya planiflora is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
