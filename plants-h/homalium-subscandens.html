Homalium subscandens

Homalium subscandens, commonly known as the creeping hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its climbing or trailing growth habit. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
