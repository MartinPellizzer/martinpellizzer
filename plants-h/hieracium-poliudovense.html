Hieracium poliudovense

Hieracium poliudovense, commonly known as the Poludovenskaya hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy, upright stems. Native to eastern Europe, it thrives in meadows and open woodlands. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it produces small, elongated achenes as its seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can be an invasive species in certain regions.
