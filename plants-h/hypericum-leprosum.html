Hypericum leprosum

Hypericum leprosum, commonly known as the yellow St. John's wort, is a shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Hypericum leprosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
