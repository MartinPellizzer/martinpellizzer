Hieracium semiciliatum

Hieracium semiciliatum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its flowers are yellow and bloom from late spring to early autumn, attracting various pollinators.

The leaves are alternate, rough to the touch, and often have a hairy texture.
