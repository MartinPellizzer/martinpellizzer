Hedysarum tuberosum var. speciosum

Hedysarum tuberosum var. speciosum, commonly known as the showy vetch, is a perennial legume native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces edible tubers that are rich in nutrients, making it valuable for both horticultural and agricultural purposes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and nutritional properties, it is cultivated in some regions for ornamental gardening and forage.
