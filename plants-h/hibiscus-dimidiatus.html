Hibiscus dimidiatus

Hibiscus dimidiatus, commonly known as the double-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, double-layered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically medium to large in size. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Hibiscus dimidiatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
