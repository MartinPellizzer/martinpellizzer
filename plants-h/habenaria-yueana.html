Habenaria yueana

Habenaria yueana, commonly known as the Yue's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its roots are fibrous, and it has linear, grass-like leaves.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty.
