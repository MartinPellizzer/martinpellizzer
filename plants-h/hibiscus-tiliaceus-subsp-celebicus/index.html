Hibiscus Tiliaceus Subsp. Celebicus

Celebes hibiscus, scientifically known as Hibiscus tiliaceus subsp. celebicus, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This subspecies is distinguished by its large, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in coastal and mangrove environments, often found along the shores of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant plays an important role in local ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species. Its ornamental value has also made it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in tropical gardens.
