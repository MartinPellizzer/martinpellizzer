Heylandia cordifolia

Heylandia cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf daphne, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant features elliptical, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its showy, fragrant flowers are typically pink or white and bloom in spring.
