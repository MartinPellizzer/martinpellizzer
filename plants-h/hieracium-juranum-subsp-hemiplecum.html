Hieracium juranum subsp. hemiplecum

Hieracium juranum subsp.

hemiplecum, commonly known as the Jura hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It typically grows in grassy meadows and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant has yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and its leaves are usually alternate and lanceolate. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which help reduce water loss in its often dry habitats.

This subspecies is part of the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
