Hypericum kanae

Hypericum kanae, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is known for its medicinal properties.

Hypericum kanae is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.
