Helichrysum cotula

Helichrysum cotula, commonly known as dwarf everlasting or yellow ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, yellow or orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant produces small, herbaceous leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Helichrysum cotula is known for its ability to survive in harsh environments due to its drought-resistant properties.
