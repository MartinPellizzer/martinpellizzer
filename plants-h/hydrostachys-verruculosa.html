Hydrostachys verruculosa

Hydrostachys verruculosa, commonly known as the woolly stachys, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, sandy soils along riverbanks and wetlands. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that gives it its common name, and its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple in color. It is known for its ability to thrive in wet environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Hydrostachys verruculosa is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
