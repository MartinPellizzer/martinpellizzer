Hemigraphis discolor

Hemigraphis discolor, commonly known as the shining iron plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage that ranges from deep red to purple, with some varieties displaying a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance.

Hemigraphis discolor is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
