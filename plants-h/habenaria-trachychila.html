Habenaria trachychila

Habenaria trachychila, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It features a slender, hairy stem with a single, narrow leaf and produces a dense spike of small, pale green to brownish flowers. The plant prefers moist, shaded habitats and is often found in forest understories. Its flowers are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem, a characteristic feature of the orchid family.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
