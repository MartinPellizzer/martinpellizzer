Hibiscus baumii

Hibiscus baumii, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a prominent yellow center. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Hibiscus baumii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
