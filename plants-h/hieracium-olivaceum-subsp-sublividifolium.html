Hieracium olivaceum subsp. sublividifolium

Hieracium olivaceum subsp.

sublividifolium, commonly known as the olive-centred hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers.

The leaves are typically lanceolate and have a distinctive bluish-green hue on the underside. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
