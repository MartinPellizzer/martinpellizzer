Helichrysum multirosulatum

Helichrysum multirosulatum, commonly known as the golden crown, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, with a slightly hairy texture.

Helichrysum multirosulatum is well adapted to arid conditions, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
