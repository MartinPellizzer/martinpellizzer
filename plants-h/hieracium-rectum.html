Hieracium rectum

Hieracium rectum, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and yellow composite flowers that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Hieracium rectum is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.
