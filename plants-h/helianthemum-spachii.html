Helianthemum spachii

Helianthemum spachii, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Helianthemum spachii is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, making it a hardy plant in coastal and mountainous areas.
