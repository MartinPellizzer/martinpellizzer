Hypericum nakamurae

Hypericum nakamurae, commonly known as Nakamura's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs known for their yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers, which bloom in summer, are characterized by their five-petaled structure and bright yellow-orange color.
