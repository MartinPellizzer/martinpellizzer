Hoya samarensis

Hoya samarensis, commonly known as the Samarensis Wisteria, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its waxy, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces fragrant, pale yellow to white blooms that typically appear during the summer months. Hoya samarensis is a climbing plant that can grow up to several meters in length, making it suitable for trellises or as a hanging plant.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice among indoor and outdoor gardeners.
