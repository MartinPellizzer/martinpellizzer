Haworthiopsis reinwardtii f. olivacea

Haworthiopsis reinwardtii f. olivacea, commonly known as the Olive Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically a pale green to bluish-gray in color, with distinct white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect light with minimal watering.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
