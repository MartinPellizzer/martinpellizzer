Haplopappus blephariphyllus

Haplopappus blephariphyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed goatweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
