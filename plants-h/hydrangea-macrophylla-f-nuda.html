Hydrangea macrophylla f. nuda

Hydrangea macrophylla f. nuda, commonly known as the smooth hydrangea, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, rounded, leafy bracts that are typically green to white in color. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage remains lush and dense throughout the growing season.

It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
