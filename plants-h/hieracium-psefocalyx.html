Hieracium psefocalyx

Hieracium psefocalyx, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium psefocalyx is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
