Haworthia reinwardtii var. chalumnensis

Haworthia reinwardtii var. chalumnensis, commonly known as the Chalumna Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The leaves are often adorned with translucent windows, allowing light to penetrate for photosynthesis. It produces small, star-shaped white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
