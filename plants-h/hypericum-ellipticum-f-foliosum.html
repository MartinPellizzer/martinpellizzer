Hypericum ellipticum f. foliosum

Hypericum ellipticum f. foliosum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf St. John's wort, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that are densely packed along the stems, giving it a lush appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its foliage and flowers contribute to its ornamental value, while its historical use in herbal medicine adds to its botanical significance.
