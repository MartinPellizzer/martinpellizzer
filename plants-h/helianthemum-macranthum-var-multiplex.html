Helianthemum macranthum var. multiplex

Helianthemum macranthum var. multiplex, commonly known as the clustered rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and attractive flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
