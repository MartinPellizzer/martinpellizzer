Hieracium pilosella subsp. inalpestriforne

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

inalpestriforne, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to mountainous regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

The plant features basal leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped, with a rosette growth form. Its flowers are yellow, with a distinctive central disc surrounded by ray florets, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.

It is often found in open, sunny environments at high altitudes.
