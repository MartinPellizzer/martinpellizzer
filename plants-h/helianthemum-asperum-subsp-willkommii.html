Helianthemum asperum subsp. willkommii

Helianthemum asperum subsp.

willkommii, commonly known as the rough sandwort, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has hairy, gray-green leaves that are typically arranged in a dense, compact form. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover in arid landscapes.
