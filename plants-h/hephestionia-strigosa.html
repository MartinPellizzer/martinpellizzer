Hephestionia strigosa

Hephestionia strigosa, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a unique orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth. Its growth habit is terrestrial, with rhizomes that allow it to spread slowly through the soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
