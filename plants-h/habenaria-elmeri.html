Habenaria elmeri

Habenaria elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, yellow, or green. The plant prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Habenaria elmeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
