Heliconia terciopela

Heliconia terciopela, commonly known as the velvet heliconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

Heliconia terciopela is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
