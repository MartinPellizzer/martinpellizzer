Haworthia oculata

Haworthia oculata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves with distinctive white or yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for succulent gardens.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
