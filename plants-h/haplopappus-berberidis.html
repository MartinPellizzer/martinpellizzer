Haplopappus berberidis

Haplopappus berberidis, commonly known as the yellow globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Haplopappus berberidis has a fibrous root system and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
