Helianthemum marifolium f. ibericum

Helianthemum marifolium f. ibericum, commonly known as the Iberian rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy leaves adapted to arid conditions. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding color to rocky and coastal landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a hardy choice for Mediterranean climates.
