Hannoa undulata

Hannoa undulata, commonly known as the wavy leaf fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, undulating fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually pinnately compound, with a soft, feathery appearance.

Hannoa undulata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
