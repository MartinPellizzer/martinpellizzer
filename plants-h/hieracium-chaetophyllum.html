Hieracium chaetophyllum

Hieracium chaetophyllum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Hieracium chaetophyllum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its resilience to environmental stress.
