Hieracium dubium subsp. sysolskiense

Hieracium dubium subsp.

sysolskiense, commonly known as the Solskiski hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring well-drained conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped with hairy surfaces, and it produces achenes as its seed type.

It is a perennial herb that plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food for pollinators.
