Hieracium isolanum

Hieracium isolanum, commonly known as the Isolan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy slopes, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium isolanum is often found in mountainous areas and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
