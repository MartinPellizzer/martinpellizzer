Hieracium bupleuroides subsp. bupleuroides

Hieracium bupleuroides subsp.

bupleuroides, commonly known as the blueweed or blue hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of blue-green leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer.
