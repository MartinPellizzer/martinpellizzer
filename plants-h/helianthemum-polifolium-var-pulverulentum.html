Helianthemum polifolium var. pulverulentum

Helianthemum polifolium var. pulverulentum, commonly known as the dusty rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, mat-like growth habit with silver-gray foliage that reflects light. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
