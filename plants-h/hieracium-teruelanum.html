Hieracium teruelanum

Hieracium teruelanum, commonly known as the Teruel hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
