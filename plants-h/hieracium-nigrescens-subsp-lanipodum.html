Hieracium nigrescens subsp. lanipodum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

lanipodum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dense, woolly covering on the stems and leaves, which gives it its common name. It typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils.

This plant is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
