Harveya macrantha

Harveya macrantha, commonly known as the giant harveya, is a striking member of the Proteaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often borne in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are usually broad and leathery, adapted to conserve water in its native arid environment.

Harveya macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil and full sun.
