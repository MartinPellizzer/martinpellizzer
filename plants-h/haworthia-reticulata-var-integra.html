Haworthia reticulata var. integra

Haworthia reticulata var. integra, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped foliage with a distinctive zebra-like pattern of light and dark green stripes. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, making it well-suited for arid environments. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the growing season.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
