Habenaria amesiana

Habenaria amesiana, commonly known as the Ames's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with distinctive spurred petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in pine flatwoods or wetlands.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
