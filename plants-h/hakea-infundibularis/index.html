Hakea Infundibularis

Hakea infundibularis is a unique and striking plant native to Australia. It is known for its distinctive bottle-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red and yellow. This species thrives in coastal and sandy environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its unusual appearance has made it a popular choice for horticultural collections. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to thrive in non-native settings.
