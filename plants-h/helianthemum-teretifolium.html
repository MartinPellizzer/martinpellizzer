Helianthemum teretifolium

Helianthemum teretifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact appearance. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous areas.

Helianthemum teretifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
