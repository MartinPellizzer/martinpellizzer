Helianthemum viscarium var. hispidulum

Helianthemum viscarium var. hispidulum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has gray-green, fuzzy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
