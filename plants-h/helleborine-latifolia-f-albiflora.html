Helleborine latifolia f. albiflora

Helleborine latifolia f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered broad-leaved helleborine, is a variant of the helleborine orchid.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of pale to white flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. Its flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer.

This orchid is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
