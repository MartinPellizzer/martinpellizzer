Helianthemum canum var. canescens

Helianthemum canum var. canescens, commonly known as the silver sandmat, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has silvery-gray foliage covered in fine, fuzzy hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for Mediterranean-style gardens.
