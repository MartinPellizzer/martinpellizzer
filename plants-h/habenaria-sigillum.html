Habenaria sigillum

Habenaria sigillum, commonly known as the sigil orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically white or pale green with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the lip.

Habenaria sigillum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
