Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. ladikense

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive central disc.
