Hieracium cyathiceps

Hieracium cyathiceps, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grasslands.

It is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
