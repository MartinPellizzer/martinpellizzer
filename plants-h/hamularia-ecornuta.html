Hamularia ecornuta

Hamularia ecornuta, commonly known as the horned bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, elongated fruits that contain the seeds.

Hamularia ecornuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
