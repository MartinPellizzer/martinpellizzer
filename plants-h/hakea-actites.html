Hakea actites

Hakea actites, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hakea, is an evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces striking, cone-shaped flower spikes that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the species. Hakea actites thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
