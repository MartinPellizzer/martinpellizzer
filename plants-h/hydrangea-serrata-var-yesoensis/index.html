Hydrangea Serrata Var. Yesoensis

Hydrangea serrata var. yesoensis is a rare and beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan. Known for its delicate, lacecap blooms and vibrant green foliage, this variety is a prized addition to gardens and botanical collections. Its flowers shift in color depending on soil pH, creating a stunning display of blue, pink, or white. The plant thrives in cool, humid climates and requires partial shade to flourish. Due to its limited natural range, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens to preserve its unique beauty.
