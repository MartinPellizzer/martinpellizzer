Habenaria silvicola

Habenaria silvicola, commonly known as the forest orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its delicate, ephemeral blooms.
