Hieracium omasvarrincola

Hieracium omasvarrincola, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow ray florets and a basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides. It has a creeping rootstock that allows it to spread vegetatively, forming dense colonies.

The flowers bloom in early summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and flies.
