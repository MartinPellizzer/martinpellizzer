Hieracium yxnerumense

Hieracium yxnerumense, commonly known as the Yxneron meadowhawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is native to Central Europe and is typically found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and meadows. The plant features yellow composite flowers with ray florets surrounding a central disc of tubular florets. Its leaves are usually basal, forming a rosette, and are covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium yxnerumense is often considered a weed in agricultural areas but plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
