Hieracium psilosum

Hieracium psilosum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium psilosum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
