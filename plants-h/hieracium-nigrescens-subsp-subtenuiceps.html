Hieracium nigrescens subsp. subtenuiceps

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

subtenuiceps, commonly known as the slender-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers arranged in a head, with small, tubular florets surrounding a central disk. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
