Hieracium pallescens subsp. knautiifolium

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

knautiifolium, commonly known as the knautia-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow disc flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets. The leaves are lanceolate to oblanceolate, often with a hairy surface, and the plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed sites.

It is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of up to 30 cm, and is often found in moist or calcareous soils.
