Haenelia nana

Haenelia nana, commonly known as the dwarf sea holly, is a small flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and silvery foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its compact stature and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Haenelia nana is also valued for its ability to tolerate salt spray and harsh maritime environments.
