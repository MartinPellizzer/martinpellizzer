Hippeastrum africanum

Hippeastrum africanum, commonly known as the African amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often used for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to bloom successfully.

Hippeastrum africanum is a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
