Hakea Concinna

Hakea concinna is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It is known for its unique, bottle-shaped stems and vibrant, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in sandy soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its showy yellow flowers bloom in spring, attracting a variety of pollinators. Hakea concinna is not only an ornamental plant but also plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
