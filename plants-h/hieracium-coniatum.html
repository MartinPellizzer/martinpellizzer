Hieracium coniatum

Hieracium coniatum, commonly known as the Alpine Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Hieracium coniatum is also notable for its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
