Hypericum tenellum

Hypericum tenellum, commonly known as the lesser St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically features wiry stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and rocky areas.

Hypericum tenellum is part of the genus Hypericum, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental value.
