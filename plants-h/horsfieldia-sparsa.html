Horsfieldia sparsa

Horsfieldia sparsa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a raceme. Horsfieldia sparsa thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
