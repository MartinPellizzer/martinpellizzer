Hiraea orbiculata

Hiraea orbiculata, commonly known as the globe hiraea, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Hiraea orbiculata is considered a valuable species in botanical studies.
