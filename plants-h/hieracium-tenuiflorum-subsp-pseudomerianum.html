Hieracium tenuiflorum subsp. pseudomerianum

Hieracium tenuiflorum subsp.

pseudomerianum, commonly known as the slender flower hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish-orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soil. It has a taproot system and produces numerous flowering stems that bloom from late spring to early summer.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
