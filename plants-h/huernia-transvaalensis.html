Huernia transvaalensis

Huernia transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal horned cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that are often covered in white or yellowish tubercles. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
