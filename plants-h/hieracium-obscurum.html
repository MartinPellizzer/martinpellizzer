Hieracium obscurum

Hieracium obscurum, commonly known as the dark hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges. The plant features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, with a rosette growth habit. Its flowers are yellow and form dense clusters, resembling a hawk's eye, hence its common name.

Hieracium obscurum is characterized by its hairy stems and is often found in moist or shaded environments.
