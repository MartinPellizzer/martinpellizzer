Helianthemum × mansanetianum

Helianthemum × mansanetianum, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It produces small, rounded capsules that contain numerous seeds, facilitating its propagation.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
