Hieracium prasinum

Hieracium prasinum, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Hieracium. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium prasinum is an herbaceous perennial that thrives in temperate climates.
