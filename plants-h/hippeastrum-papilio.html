Hippeastrum papilio

Hippeastrum papilio, commonly known as the pink hurricane lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red, often with a distinctive white throat. The plant produces sword-shaped leaves that are broad and lanceolate, emerging from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum papilio is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
