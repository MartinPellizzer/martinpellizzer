Hibiscus barbosae

Hibiscus barbosae, commonly known as the Barbosa hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found growing in coastal areas or near water sources. Hibiscus barbosae is a perennial shrub that thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and botanical collections.
