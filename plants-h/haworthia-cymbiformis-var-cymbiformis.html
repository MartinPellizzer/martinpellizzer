Haworthia cymbiformis var. cymbiformis

Haworthia cymbiformis var. cymbiformis, commonly known as the cymbiform Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers on short stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for dry environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
