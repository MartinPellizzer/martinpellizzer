Hieracium evernium

Hieracium evernium, commonly known as the noble hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive central disc.

Hieracium evernium is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
