Habenaria novaesii

Habenaria novaesii, commonly known as the New Guinea marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in wet, swampy habitats and thrives in humid, tropical climates. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with a slight pinkish tinge, and they bloom during the rainy season.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
