Hieracium vapenicanum

Hieracium vapenicanum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and hairy stems, which help it retain moisture in dry environments. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its roots form a dense network that aids in soil stabilization.

The plant is also known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in agricultural areas.
