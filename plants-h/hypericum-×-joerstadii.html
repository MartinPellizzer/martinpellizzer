Hypericum × joerstadii

Hypericum × joerstadii, commonly known as the hybrid St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Botanically, it is a hybrid between Hypericum perforatum (common St. John's wort) and Hypericum patulum (woolly St. John's wort).
