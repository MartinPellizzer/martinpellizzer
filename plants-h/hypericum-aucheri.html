Hypericum aucheri

Hypericum aucheri, commonly known as the orange hypericum, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It features bright orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
