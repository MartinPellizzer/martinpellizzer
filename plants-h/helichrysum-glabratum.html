Helichrysum glabratum

Helichrysum glabratum, commonly known as the woolly everlasting or woolly helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble small sunflowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its flowers are long-lasting and often used in dried floral arrangements.

Helichrysum glabratum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
