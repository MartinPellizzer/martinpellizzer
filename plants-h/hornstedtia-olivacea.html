Hornstedtia olivacea

Hornstedtia olivacea, commonly known as the oliveleaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy blue or purple flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Hornstedtia olivacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
