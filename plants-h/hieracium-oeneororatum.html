Hieracium oeneororatum

Hieracium oeneororatum, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from white to purple.

Hieracium oeneororatum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
