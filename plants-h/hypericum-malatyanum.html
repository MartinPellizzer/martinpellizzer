Hypericum malatyanum

Hypericum malatyanum, commonly known as the Malatya St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats and is characterized by its yellow flowers and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is part of the Hypericaceae family, which includes other species of St. John's Wort.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
