Hieracium neotremedale

Hieracium neotremedale, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it forms dense mats. Its leaves are basal, rough, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Hieracium neotremedale is adapted to cold climates and can thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
