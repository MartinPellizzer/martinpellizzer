Hypochaeris magellanica

Hypochaeris magellanica, commonly known as the Magellan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers with five petals. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Hypochaeris magellanica is known for its ability to spread rapidly through seeds and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
