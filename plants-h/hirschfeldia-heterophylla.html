Hirschfeldia heterophylla

Hirschfeldia heterophylla, commonly known as the desert daisy, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water in harsh environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom during the cooler months. It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperature fluctuations.

Hirschfeldia heterophylla plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing nectar for pollinators.
