Helichrysum errerae var. messerii

Helichrysum errerae var. messerii, commonly known as the Cape gold flower, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers, blooming in late winter to early spring. The plant has aromatic, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and long-lasting blooms make it a valuable addition to xeriscaping efforts.
