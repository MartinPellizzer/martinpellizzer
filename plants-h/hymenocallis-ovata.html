Hymenocallis ovata

Hymenocallis ovata, commonly known as the white hurricane lily, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hymenocallis ovata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
