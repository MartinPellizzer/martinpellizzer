Haworthiopsis × major

Haworthiopsis × major, commonly known as the Giant Haworthia, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or grayish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
