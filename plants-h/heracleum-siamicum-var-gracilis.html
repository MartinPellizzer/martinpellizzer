Heracleum siamicum var. gracilis

Heracleum siamicum var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender cowparade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its slender stems and finely divided leaves contribute to its ornamental appeal.

It is often cultivated for its delicate appearance and ecological value to pollinators.
