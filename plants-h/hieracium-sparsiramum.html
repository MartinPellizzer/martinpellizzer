Hieracium sparsiramum

Hieracium sparsiramum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium sparsiramum is often found in open habitats and is considered a pioneer species in ecological succession.
