Hibiscus thespesianus

Hibiscus thespesianus, commonly known as the Christmas hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming during the winter months. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hibiscus thespesianus is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
