Haworthia bijliana var. joubertii

Haworthia bijliana var. joubertii, commonly known as the Joubert's Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, light green leaves with distinctive white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
