Helianthemum duguei

Helianthemum duguei, commonly known as the Canary Island snowflake, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to rocky coastal landscapes. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Helianthemum duguei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
