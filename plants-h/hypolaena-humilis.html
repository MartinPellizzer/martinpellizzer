Hypolaena humilis

Hypolaena humilis, commonly known as the dwarf hypolaena, is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Australia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as sandy soils and rocky areas. The plant has simple, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in terminal spikes. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in well-drained soils.
