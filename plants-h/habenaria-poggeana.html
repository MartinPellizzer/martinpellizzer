Habenaria poggeana

Habenaria poggeana, commonly known as the Pogge's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
