Hibiscus nanuzae

Hibiscus nanuzae, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the island of Oahu in Hawaii.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually green and slightly hairy. Hibiscus nanuzae thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and forests.

It plays an important role in the local ecosystem and is also valued for its ornamental beauty.
