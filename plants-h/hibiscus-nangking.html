Hibiscus nangking

Hibiscus nangking, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms.

Hibiscus nangking is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
