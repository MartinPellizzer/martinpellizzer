Hypoestes calycina

Hypoestes calycina, commonly known as the "Snowflake" or "Shinyleaf" plant, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from white to pink and red, often with contrasting veining. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes calycina is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its vibrant floral display.
