Heracleum circassicum

Heracleum circassicum, commonly known as the Caucasian cow parsley, is a tall biennial plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its large, umbrella-like inflorescence. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
