Helianthemum geniorum var. grandiflorum

Helianthemum geniorum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the grandiflorum stonecress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a rough, slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small, yellowish-white, and clustered in dense, compact inflorescences.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
