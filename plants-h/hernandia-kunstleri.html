Hernandia kunstleri

Hernandia kunstleri, commonly known as the ornamental hernandia, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
