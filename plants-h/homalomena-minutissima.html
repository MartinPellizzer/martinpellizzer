Homalomena minutissima

Homalomena minutissima, commonly known as the miniature arrowhead plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its small, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically less than 5 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by a spathe, a feature common in the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.

Homalomena minutissima is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate watering and well-draining soil.
