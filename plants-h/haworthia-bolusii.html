Haworthia bolusii

Haworthia bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green to blue-gray in color, often with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
