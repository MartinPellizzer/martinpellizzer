Helianthemum cinereum f. leucanthum

Helianthemum cinereum f. leucanthum, commonly known as the white-flowered rock rose, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color, providing a striking contrast to its white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun, making it well-suited for coastal or Mediterranean climates. Its flowers bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch of color to gardens and natural landscapes.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
