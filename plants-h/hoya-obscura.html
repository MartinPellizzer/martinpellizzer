Hoya obscura

Hoya obscura, commonly known as the moonlight hoya, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and clusters of star-shaped, waxy flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant blooms that are typically white or pale pink, often appearing at night. Hoya obscura is a climbing or trailing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
