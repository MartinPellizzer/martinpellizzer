Hieracium rupicola subsp. cossonianum

Hieracium rupicola subsp.

cossonianum, commonly known as the Cossonian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

This subspecies is noted for its ecological importance in supporting local pollinators and its role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
