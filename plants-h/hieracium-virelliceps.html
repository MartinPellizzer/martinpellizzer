Hieracium virelliceps

Hieracium virelliceps, commonly known as the green-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine, greenish hairs, giving it its common name.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
