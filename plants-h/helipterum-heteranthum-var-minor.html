Helipterum heteranthum var. minor

Helipterum heteranthum var. minor, commonly known as the small-flowered helipterum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically pink or red, with five petals and stamens that are often longer than the petals. It grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in open habitats such as roadsides and disturbed areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
