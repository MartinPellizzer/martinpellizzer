Helichrysum × Lamarckii

Helichrysum × lamarckii is a striking evergreen shrub known for its vibrant, long-lasting yellow flowers. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its unique foliage and colorful blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. The species is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance. With its cheerful appearance and easy care requirements, it's a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
