Hypericum turfosum

Hypericum turfosum, commonly known as marsh St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to moist, temperate regions.

It typically features sprawling stems covered in small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in wet or boggy soils and is often found in meadows or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-adapted to wetland environments.
