Hieracium subdecolorans subsp. elegantulum

Hieracium subdecolorans subsp.

elegantulum, commonly known as the elegant yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August.

The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in temperate regions.
