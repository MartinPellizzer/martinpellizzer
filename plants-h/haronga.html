Haronga

Haronga, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors. Haronga thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and ease of care.
