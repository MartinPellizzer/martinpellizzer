Hibiscus owariensis

Hibiscus owariensis, commonly known as the Owari hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. Hibiscus owariensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
