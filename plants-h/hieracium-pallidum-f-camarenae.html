Hieracium pallidum f. camarenae

Hieracium pallidum f. camarenae, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a botanical variant of the hawkweed genus Hieracium.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its yellowish to pale green flowers and hairy stems. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in ecological and taxonomic studies.
