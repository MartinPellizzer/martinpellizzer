Hieracium stenophyton

Hieracium stenophyton, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, exhibiting a rosette of basal leaves. Hieracium stenophyton is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
