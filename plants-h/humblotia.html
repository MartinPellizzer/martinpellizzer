Humblotia

Humblotia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Humblotia is known for its rapid growth and is often considered an invasive species in certain areas.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in wetlands and along riverbanks.
