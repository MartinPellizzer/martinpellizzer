Hemipilia pinguicula

Hemipilia pinguicula, commonly known as the sticky orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

This herbaceous plant is characterized by its small, green, sticky leaves that help in trapping small insects. It produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its unique adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
