Haloragis aenea

Haloragis aenea, commonly known as the silverleaf thyme.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand, characterized by its low-growing habit and silver-gray foliage. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming from late winter to early spring. Haloragis aenea thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
