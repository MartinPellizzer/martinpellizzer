Hypericum grandifolium

Hypericum grandifolium, commonly known as large-leaf St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Hypericum grandifolium is also known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
