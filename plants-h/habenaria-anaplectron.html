Habenaria anaplectron

Habenaria anaplectron, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soils.

The species is notable for its small, clustered inflorescences and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
