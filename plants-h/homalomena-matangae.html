Homalomena matangae

Homalomena matangae, commonly known as the Matangae arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with cream or yellow markings. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense foliage in suitable conditions.
