Hoya orbiculata

Hoya orbiculata, commonly known as the wax plant or the prayer plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asclepiadaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns.
