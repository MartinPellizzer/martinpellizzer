Huberantha floribunda

Huberantha floribunda, commonly known as the fragrant snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a mild, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and seasonal blooming cycle.
