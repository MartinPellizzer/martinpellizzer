Heterospathe sibuyanensis

Heterospathe sibuyanensis, commonly known as the Sibuyan fern, is a species native to the Philippines, specifically the island of Sibuyan.

It is a member of the Davalliaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil. Its botanical features include bipinnate leaves with a distinctive arrangement of pinnae and spores that are produced on the underside of the fronds.

This fern is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
