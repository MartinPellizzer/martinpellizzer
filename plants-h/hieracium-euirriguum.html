Hieracium euirriguum

Hieracium euirriguum, commonly known as the European wood sorrel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces achenes as its fruit type.

It is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
