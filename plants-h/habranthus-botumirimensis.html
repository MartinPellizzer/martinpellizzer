Habranthus botumirimensis

Habranthus botumirimensis, commonly known as the Botumirim grass, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with narrow, grass-like leaves. It is typically found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
