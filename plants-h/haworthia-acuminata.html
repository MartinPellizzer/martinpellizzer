Haworthia acuminata

Haworthia acuminata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinct white or yellow stripes. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
