Hieracium hystrix

Hieracium hystrix, commonly known as the prickly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, surrounded by bracts that give it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are typically rough and hairy, with some varieties having spiny tips.

Hieracium hystrix is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
