Hypertelis namaquensis

Hypertelis namaquensis, commonly known as the Namibian starfish plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, star-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Hypertelis namaquensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh conditions.
