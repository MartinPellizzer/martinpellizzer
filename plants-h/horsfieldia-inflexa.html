Horsfieldia inflexa

Horsfieldia inflexa, commonly known as the Horsfield's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces inflorescences with several fragrant, yellowish-green to white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Horsfieldia inflexa is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
