Hypocalymma angustifolium subsp. angustifolium

Hypocalymma angustifolium subsp.

angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hypocalymma, is a perennial shrub native to Australia. It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in native habitats.
