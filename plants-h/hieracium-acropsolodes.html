Hieracium acropsolodes

Hieracium acropsolodes, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk florets. Hieracium acropsolodes is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in a variety of soil types.
