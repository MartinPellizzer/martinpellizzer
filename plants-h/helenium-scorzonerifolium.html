Helenium scorzonerifolium

Helenium scorzonerifolium, commonly known as the purplehead hedgerow, is a perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blooms. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking purple to red flower heads that attract pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Helenium scorzonerifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
