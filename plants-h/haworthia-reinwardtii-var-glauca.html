Haworthia reinwardtii var. glauca

Haworthia reinwardtii var. glauca, commonly known as the Blue Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white to pale blue markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact size and unique coloration make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
