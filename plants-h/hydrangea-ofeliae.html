Hydrangea ofeliae

Hydrangea ofeliae, commonly known as the mountain hydrangea, is a species native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink and blue depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage consists of large, serrated leaves that are often a deep green.

Hydrangea ofeliae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
