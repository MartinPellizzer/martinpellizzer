Hovea nitida

Hovea nitida, commonly known as the slender hovea, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with elliptical leaflets and produces small, pale yellow flowers in clusters. Hovea nitida is often found in heathlands and coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
