Hatiora salicornoides f. cylindrica

Hatiora salicornoides f. cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical holiday cactus, is a unique hybrid plant that exhibits distinct botanical characteristics.

It is a compact, branching plant with thick, fleshy stems that are typically cylindrical in shape, giving it its common name. The leaves are usually reduced to small, scale-like structures, and the plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, or white. It is native to Brazil and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value.

This cultivar is known for its ability to bloom during the holiday season, making it a popular choice for festive displays.
