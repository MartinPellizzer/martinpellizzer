Hieracium submedianum

Hieracium submedianum, commonly known as the submedian hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Hieracium submedianum is often found in open habitats and can form dense colonies, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
