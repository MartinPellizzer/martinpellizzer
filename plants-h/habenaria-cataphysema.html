Habenaria cataphysema

Habenaria cataphysema, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet and green floral patterns, which are often adorned with intricate veining. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, where it thrives on the forest floor. Its flowers are usually borne on slender stems and are known for their delicate appearance.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticultural settings.
