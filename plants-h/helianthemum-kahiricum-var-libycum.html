Helianthemum kahiricum var. libycum

Helianthemum kahiricum var. libycum, commonly known as the Libyan rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy leaves adapted to conserve water in dry environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens as an ornamental plant.
