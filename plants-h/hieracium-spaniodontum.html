Hieracium spaniodontum

Hieracium spaniodontum, commonly known as the Spanish thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Compositae).

It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, typically growing in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands. The plant features a hairy, upright stem with basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, which is surrounded by bracts.

The plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
