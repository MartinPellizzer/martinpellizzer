Haworthia pygmaea var. esterhuizenii

Haworthia pygmaea var. esterhuizenii, commonly known as the Esterhuizenii Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish-gray hue and white or pale markings. The plant produces tall, slender stems with clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
