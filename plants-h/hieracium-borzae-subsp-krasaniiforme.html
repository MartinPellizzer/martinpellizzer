Hieracium borzae subsp. krasaniiforme

Hieracium borzae subsp.

krasaniiforme, commonly known as the Krasaniiforme hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are generally lance-shaped with a rough texture.
