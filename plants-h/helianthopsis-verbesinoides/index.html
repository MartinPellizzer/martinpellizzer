Helianthopsis Verbesinoides

Helianthopsis verbesinoides is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant yellow blooms. Native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States, this plant thrives in harsh, sunny environments. Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice among desert gardeners. With its compact growth habit and showy flowers, it adds both beauty and ecological value to its habitat. This plant is not only a visual delight but also plays an important role in supporting local pollinators.
