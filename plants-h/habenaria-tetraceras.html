Habenaria tetraceras

Habenaria tetraceras, commonly known as the four-armed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, violet-blue flowers arranged in a dense spike, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Habenaria tetraceras is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
