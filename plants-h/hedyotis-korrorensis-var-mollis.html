Hedyotis korrorensis var. mollis

Hedyotis korrorensis var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-leaved bluishweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has smooth, glabrous leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of native New Zealand ecosystems.
