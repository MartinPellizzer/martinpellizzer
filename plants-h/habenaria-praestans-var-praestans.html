Habenaria praestans var. praestans

Habenaria praestans var. praestans, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces tall, erect stems bearing numerous flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
