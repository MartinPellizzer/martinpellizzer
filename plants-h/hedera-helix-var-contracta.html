Hedera helix var. contracta

Hedera helix var. contracta, commonly known as the compact ivy, is a cultivar of the common ivy characterized by its dense, upright growth habit.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are smaller and more compact compared to typical ivy varieties. This plant is well-suited for hanging baskets, containers, or as a trailing plant in shaded areas. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Its botanical appeal lies in its controlled growth and ornamental foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
