Homeria flexuosa

Homeria flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible homeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, often branching stems that can twist and curl. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a slight sheen.

Homeria flexuosa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
