Hyalea tadshicorum

Hyalea tadshicorum, commonly known as the Tadshiko glasswing, is a species of plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent wings that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments where it has adapted to extreme temperature fluctuations. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, allowing it to store water efficiently.

Hyalea tadshicorum plays a minor role in its ecosystem, primarily as a food source for local insect populations.
