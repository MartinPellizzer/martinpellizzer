Huerniopsis atrosanguinea

Huerniopsis atrosanguinea, commonly known as the black blood orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to blackish stems and leaves, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers with a unique coloration that ranges from deep purple to nearly black. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species.
