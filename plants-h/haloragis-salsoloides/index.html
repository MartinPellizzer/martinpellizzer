Haloragis Salsoloides

Haloragis salsoloides is a hardy plant species known for its resilience in harsh environments. It thrives in saline and arid conditions, making it a common sight in coastal and desert regions. This plant has a low-growing structure with small, succulent leaves that help it conserve water. Its flowers are typically pale green or yellow, adding subtle beauty to its rugged habitat. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
