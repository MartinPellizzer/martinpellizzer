Hypericum quadrangulum subsp. desetangsii

Hypericum quadrangulum subsp.

desetangsii, commonly known as the yellow-flowered St. John's wort, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its quadrangular stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins.

The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers clustered in dense inflorescences, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or open habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
