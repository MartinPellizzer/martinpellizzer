Hieracium macilentum subsp. fariniceps

Hieracium macilentum subsp.

fariniceps, commonly known as the fariniceps hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance.
