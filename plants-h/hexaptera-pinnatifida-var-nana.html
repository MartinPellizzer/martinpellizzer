Hexaptera pinnatifida var. nana

Hexaptera pinnatifida var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf fan-leaf hexaptera, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, pinnately compound leaves with a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
