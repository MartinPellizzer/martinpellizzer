Helenium pinetorum

Helenium pinetorum, commonly known as the pine forest sneaker, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and clusters of bright orange to red ray flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple.

Helenium pinetorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial to full sun.
