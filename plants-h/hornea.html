Hornea

Hornea, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, bright red or orange flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has a smooth, greyish bark and can grow as a tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hornea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
