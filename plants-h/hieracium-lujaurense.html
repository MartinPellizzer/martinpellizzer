Hieracium lujaurense

Hieracium lujaurense, commonly known as the Lujarun Daisy, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are usually basal, forming a rosette, and are covered in fine hairs.

The flowers bloom in the summer, adding a vibrant touch of yellow to its natural habitat.
