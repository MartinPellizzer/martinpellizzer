Haworthia hisui

Haworthia hisui, commonly known as the silver leaf Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
