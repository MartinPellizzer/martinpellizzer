Helinus ovatus var. rotundifolius

Helinus ovatus var. rotundifolius, commonly known as the rounded-leaf helinus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are rounded in shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and collections.
