Hortensia macrophylla f. isobue

Hortensia macrophylla f. isobue, commonly known as the isobue hydrangea, is a compact, flowering shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that range in color from blue to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique flower color variation and lush foliage contribute to its ornamental value.
