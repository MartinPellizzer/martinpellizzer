Hedysarum pulchrum

Hedysarum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful vetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Hedysarum pulchrum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
