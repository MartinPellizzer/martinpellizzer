Hosta sieboldii f. lancifolia

Hosta sieboldii f. lancifolia, commonly known as the lancifolia hosta, is a hybrid variety of the hosta plant.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery white margin. The plant is native to Japan and is prized for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires moist, well-drained soil.

This cultivar is often used in shaded garden landscapes for its striking leaf color and texture.
