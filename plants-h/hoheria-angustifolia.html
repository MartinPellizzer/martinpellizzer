Hoheria angustifolia

Hoheria angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hibiscus, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Hoheria angustifolia is also valued for its ability to attract native pollinators such as bees and birds.
