Herniaria polymorpha var. glabrescens

Herniaria polymorpha var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to arid and coastal regions.

It typically features small, fleshy, greenish-yellow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tiny white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

This variety is characterized by its smooth, hairless appearance, distinguishing it from other varieties of Herniaria.
