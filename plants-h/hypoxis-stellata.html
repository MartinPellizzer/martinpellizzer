Hypoxis stellata

Hypoxis stellata, commonly known as starry hypoxis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with a star-like appearance. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial herb that is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Hypoxis stellata is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
