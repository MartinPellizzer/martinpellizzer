Hieracium subdecolorans subsp. rubens

Hieracium subdecolorans subsp.

rubens, commonly known as the red hawk's-beard, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae). It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flower heads, which are surrounded by pale green to reddish bracts.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped with rough, hairy surfaces, and the stems are often reddish in color.

It is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of up to 30 cm, and is known for its attractive, daisy-like appearance.
