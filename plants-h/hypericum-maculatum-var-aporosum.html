Hypericum maculatum var. aporosum

Hypericum maculatum var. aporosum, commonly known as the spotted yellowstone hypericum, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its yellow, orange, and red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
