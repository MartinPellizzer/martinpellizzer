Haworthia retusa var. comptoniana

Haworthia retusa var. comptoniana, commonly known as the Compton's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor succulent collections.
