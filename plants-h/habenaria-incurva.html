Habenaria incurva

Habenaria incurva, commonly known as the bent-stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often curved stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are typically fragrant. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Habenaria incurva is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
