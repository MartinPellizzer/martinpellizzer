Helia frigida

Helia frigida, commonly known as the cold-loving helia, is a hardy plant that thrives in cooler climates.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-green foliage and small, star-shaped flowers. This plant is native to high-altitude regions and prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure. It is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant due to its ornamental value.

Helia frigida is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests and diseases.
