Heteropterys lindleyana f. rotunda

Heteropterys lindleyana f. rotunda, commonly known as the round-leaf heteropterys, is a compact shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often rounded at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months. Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round interest in gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to shaded environments.
