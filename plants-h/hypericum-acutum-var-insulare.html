Hypericum acutum var. insulare

Hypericum acutum var. insulare, commonly known as the island St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers with four petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.
