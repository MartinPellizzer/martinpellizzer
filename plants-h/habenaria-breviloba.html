Habenaria breviloba

Habenaria breviloba, commonly known as the small-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several leaves arranged along its length. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often with a faintly colored lip that distinguishes it from other orchid species.

It is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
