Hieracium sciadophorum subsp. amblylepium

Hieracium sciadophorum subsp.

amblylepium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along riverbanks.

The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, upright leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single flower head surrounded by pale green to white ray florets.

This subspecies is characterized by its relatively small size and distinctive leaf morphology.
