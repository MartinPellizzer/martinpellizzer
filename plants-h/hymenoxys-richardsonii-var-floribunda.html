Hymenoxys richardsonii var. floribunda

Hymenoxys richardsonii var. floribunda, commonly known as the showy hymenoxys, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
