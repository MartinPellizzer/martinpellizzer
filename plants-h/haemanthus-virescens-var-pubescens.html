Haemanthus virescens var. pubescens

Haemanthus virescens var. pubescens, commonly known as the silver bush or silverleaf haemanthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative plant in coastal regions.
