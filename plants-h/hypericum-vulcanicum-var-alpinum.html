Hypericum vulcanicum var. alpinum

Hypericum vulcanicum var. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, mountainous climates. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in alpine gardens.
