Hoya recurvula subsp. bokorensis

Hoya recurvula subsp.

bokorensis, commonly known as the Bokorensis Wristwort, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, dark green leaves that often have a slight curl.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements. Hoya recurvula subsp.

bokorensis thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
