Hypericum strictum

Hypericum strictum, commonly known as upright St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used for their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.
