Heliotropium europaeum var. ellipticum

Heliotropium europaeum var. ellipticum, commonly known as the European heliotrope, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs and produces clusters of small, pink to violet flowers. The leaves are elliptic in shape, typically with a pointed tip and a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often grown for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
