Hypericum canariense var. platysepalum

Hypericum canariense var. platysepalum, commonly known as the Canary Islands St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes various species of flowering shrubs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, typical of many Hypericum species. Its leaves are opposite and often have a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.
