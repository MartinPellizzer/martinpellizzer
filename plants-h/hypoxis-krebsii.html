Hypoxis krebsii

Hypoxis krebsii, commonly known as the golden star, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces bright yellow flowers in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking floral display and ease of cultivation.
