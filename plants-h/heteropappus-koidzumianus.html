Heteropappus koidzumianus

Heteropappus koidzumianus, commonly known as the Koidzumi's heteropappus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence, which consists of yellow disk flowers surrounded by white to pale pink ray florets. The plant produces achenes with pappus hairs, which aid in wind dispersal. Native to Central Asia, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
