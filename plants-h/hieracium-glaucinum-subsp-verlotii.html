Hieracium glaucinum subsp. verlotii

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

verlotii, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is characterized by its rosette of silver-green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in the summer.

The plant thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing at high altitudes. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species in many regions.
