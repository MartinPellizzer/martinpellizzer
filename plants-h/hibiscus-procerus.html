Hibiscus procerus

Hibiscus procerus, commonly known as the slender hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hibiscus procerus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
