Hieracium paupericeps

Hieracium paupericeps, commonly known as the poorhead hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and basal rosettes of narrow, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a single, terminal head. It is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Hieracium paupericeps is typically short-lived, with a lifespan of one to two years.
