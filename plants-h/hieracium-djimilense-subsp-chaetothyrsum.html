Hieracium djimilense subsp. chaetothyrsum

Hieracium djimilense subsp.

chaetothyrsum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Africa. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of yellowish to reddish flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its hairy stems and leaves, which provide a distinctive appearance. It typically thrives in rocky or grassy habitats at high elevations.
