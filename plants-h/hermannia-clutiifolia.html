Hermannia clutiifolia

Hermannia clutiifolia, commonly known as the red-eyed wonder, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous white flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture.

Hermannia clutiifolia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
