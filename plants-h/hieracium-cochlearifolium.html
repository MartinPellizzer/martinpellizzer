Hieracium cochlearifolium

Hieracium cochlearifolium, commonly known as the spiral-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It is an annual or biennial plant, often found in temperate regions.
