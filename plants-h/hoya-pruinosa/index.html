Hoya Pruinosa

Hoya pruinosa is a unique and captivating plant known for its striking waxy leaves and delicate flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight. This species is prized by plant enthusiasts for its ability to produce clusters of star-shaped blooms. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, powdery coating that gives it a distinctive appearance. With proper care, Hoya pruinosa can become a stunning addition to any indoor garden.
