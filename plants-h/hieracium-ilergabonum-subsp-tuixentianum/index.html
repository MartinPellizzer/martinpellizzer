Hieracium Ilergabonum Subsp. Tuixentianum

Hieracium ilergabonum subsp. tuixentianum is a rare and lesser-known species of hawkweed found in specific regions of Africa. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers. This subspecies is notable for its limited distribution and ecological niche, making it a subject of interest for botanists. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces threats to its survival. Understanding and preserving this plant is crucial for biodiversity conservation in its native region.
