Hypericum japonicum f. microphyllum

Hypericum japonicum f. microphyllum, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort.

This compact variant of Hypericum japonicum is characterized by its small, densely packed leaves and yellow-orange flowers. It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. The plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
