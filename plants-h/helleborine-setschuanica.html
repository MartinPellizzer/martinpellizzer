Helleborine setschuanica

Helleborine setschuanica, commonly known as the Chinese helleborine, is a species of orchid native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Japan.

It is a perennial herb with a rhizomatous root system, often growing in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces striking, hooded flowers with greenish-yellow petals and a distinctive spur. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, typically arranged in a basal rosette.

Helleborine setschuanica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in woodland gardens.
