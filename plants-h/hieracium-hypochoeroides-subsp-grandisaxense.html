Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. grandisaxense

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

grandisaxense, commonly known as the large rock hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of dark green, hairy leaves that are often lobed or toothed. Its flowering stems bear yellow daisy-like flowers with ray florets and a central disk of tubular flowers.

It is characterized by its robust growth and adaptability to harsh, arid conditions.
