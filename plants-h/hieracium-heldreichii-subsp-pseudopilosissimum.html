Hieracium heldreichii subsp. pseudopilosissimum

Hieracium heldreichii subsp.

pseudopilosissimum, commonly known as the Heldreich's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright stems and basal rosettes of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the genus Hieracium. It thrives in rocky and calcareous soils, often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
