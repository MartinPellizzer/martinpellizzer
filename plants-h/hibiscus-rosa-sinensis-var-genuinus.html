Hibiscus rosa-sinensis var. genuinus

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis var. genuinus, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink and white. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces capsules that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
