Homalomena distans

Homalomena distans, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowroot or giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to Southeast Asia.

Homalomena distans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional medicine.
