Hypericum nagasawae var. nokoense

Hypericum nagasawae var. nokoense, commonly known as the Nokoense St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It produces small, elliptical leaves and has a compact growth habit.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
