Homalomena adiensis

Homalomena adiensis, commonly known as the red-veined arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with prominent red veins. The plant produces striking, spadix-bearing inflorescences that are often surrounded by a white or green spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.

Homalomena adiensis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
