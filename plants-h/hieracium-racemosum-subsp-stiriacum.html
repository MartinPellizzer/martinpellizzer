Hieracium racemosum subsp. stiriacum

Hieracium racemosum subsp.

stiriacum, commonly known as the Stiria hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

It is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
