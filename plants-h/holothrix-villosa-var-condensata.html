Holothrix villosa var. condensata

Holothrix villosa var. condensata, commonly known as the dense hairy holothrix, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, velvety foliage with soft, hairy textures that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often clustered at the base, while its flowering stems rise above the foliage.

The flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and typically have a pale greenish hue with yellow anthers.
