Hieracium stenochlorolepis

Hieracium stenochlorolepis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically hairy stems. The plant features alternate, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which attract various pollinators.

Hieracium stenochlorolepis is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
