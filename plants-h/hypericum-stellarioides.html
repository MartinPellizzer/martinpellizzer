Hypericum stellarioides

Hypericum stellarioides, commonly known as star-like St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with opposite, oval to lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, star-like inflorescences. It typically blooms in late spring to early summer and is often found in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and meadows.

Hypericum stellarioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
