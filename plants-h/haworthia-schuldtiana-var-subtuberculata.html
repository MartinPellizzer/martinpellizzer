Haworthia schuldtiana var. subtuberculata

Haworthia schuldtiana var. subtuberculata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a distinctive white or pale green coloration. The leaves are typically adorned with white tubercles, giving it a zebra-like appearance. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation and requires minimal water and bright, indirect light.
