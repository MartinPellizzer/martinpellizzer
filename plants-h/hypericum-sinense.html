Hypericum sinense

Hypericum sinense, commonly known as Chinese St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, opposite leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky areas.

Hypericum sinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
