Hoya obovata

Hoya obovata, commonly known as the wax plant or the sweetheart plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asclepiadaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of India, Thailand, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically ovate or obovate in shape. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink, often with a subtle fragrance.

Hoya obovata is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.
