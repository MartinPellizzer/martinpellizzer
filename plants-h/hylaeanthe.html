Hylaeanthe

Hylaeanthe, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in humid environments.

Hylaeanthe blooms during the winter months, making it a popular ornamental plant in colder regions.
