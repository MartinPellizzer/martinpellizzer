Heteropogon contortus var. polystachyos

Heteropogon contortus var. polystachyos, commonly known as the twisted grass or narrow-leaved grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive twisted appearance in its inflorescence. The plant produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers, which are usually pale green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies and horticultural applications.
