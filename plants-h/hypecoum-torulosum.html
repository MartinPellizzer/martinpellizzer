Hypecoum torulosum

Hypecoum torulosum, commonly known as the slender bladderpod, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, branching stems and produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a dry, dehiscent pod that releases seeds when mature, aiding in its dispersal.
