Hieracium lamprophyllum subsp. cezycola

Hieracium lamprophyllum subsp.

cezycola, commonly known as the silverleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant features silver-white hairs on its leaves and stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring well-drained soils.
