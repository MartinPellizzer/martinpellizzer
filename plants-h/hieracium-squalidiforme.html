Hieracium squalidiforme

Hieracium squalidiforme, commonly known as the squalid yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium squalidiforme is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
