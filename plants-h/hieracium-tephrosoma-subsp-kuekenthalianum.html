Hieracium tephrosoma subsp. kuekenthalianum

Hieracium tephrosoma subsp.

kuekenthalianum, commonly known as the Kuekenthalian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in the summer months.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges. It has finely divided, serrated leaves and is often found in temperate regions of Europe.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in several countries.
