Hieracium molestatum

Hieracium molestatum, commonly known as the torment weed, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in disturbed soils such as roadsides and waste areas. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a composite head. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered a weed in many regions.
