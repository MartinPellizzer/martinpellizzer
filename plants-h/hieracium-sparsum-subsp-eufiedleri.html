Hieracium sparsum subsp. eufiedleri

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

eufiedleri, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

The plant has a basal rosette of leaves that are usually hairy and lanceolate in shape. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early autumn.

The species is characterized by its slender stems and is often found in well-drained soils.
