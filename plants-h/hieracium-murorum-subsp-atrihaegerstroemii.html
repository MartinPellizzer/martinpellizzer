Hieracium murorum subsp. atrihaegerstroemii

Hieracium murorum subsp.

atrihaegerstroemii, commonly known as the black-haegerstroem hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-red flower heads.
