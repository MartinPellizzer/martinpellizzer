Hieracium djimilense subsp. balkanum

Hieracium djimilense subsp.

balkanum, commonly known as the Balkan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering stems that bear yellow composite flowers.

The plant is native to the Balkan Peninsula and thrives in grassy slopes and open woodlands. It has small, hairy leaves and produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and localized species in its native habitat.
