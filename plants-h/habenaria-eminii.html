Habenaria eminii

Habenaria eminii, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and a dense spike of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem, a feature common in the orchid family.

Habenaria eminii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its beauty.
