Hedyosmum callososerratum

Hedyosmum callososerratum, commonly known as the rough-leaved hedyosmum, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, rough-textured leaves with serrated edges, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
