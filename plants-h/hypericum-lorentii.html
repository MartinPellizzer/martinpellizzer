Hypericum lorentii

Hypericum lorentii, commonly known as the Lorentz St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow floral coloration.
