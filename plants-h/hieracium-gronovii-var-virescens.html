Hieracium gronovii var. virescens

Hieracium gronovii var. virescens, commonly known as the greenish hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of yellow flowers with ray florets. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a hairy texture, while the stems are often reddish in color. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas, often forming dense colonies.

This variant is distinguished by its relatively lighter green foliage compared to other varieties of Hieracium.
