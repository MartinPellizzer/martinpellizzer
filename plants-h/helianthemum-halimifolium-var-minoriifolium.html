Helianthemum halimifolium var. minoriifolium

Helianthemum halimifolium var. minoriifolium, commonly known as the lesser rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has compact, evergreen leaves that are gray-green in color and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
