Haworthia minima

Haworthia minima, commonly known as the baby zebra plant, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
