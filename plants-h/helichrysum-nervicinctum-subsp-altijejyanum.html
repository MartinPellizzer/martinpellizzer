Helichrysum nervicinctum subsp. altijejyanum

Helichrysum nervicinctum subsp.

altijejyanum, commonly known as the Altijejyan goldenrod, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, yellowish-gold flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers, which retain their color even when dried.
