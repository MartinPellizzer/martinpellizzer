Hieracium epitholotum

Hieracium epitholotum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium epitholotum is often considered a weed due to its rapid spread and ability to colonize various environments.
