Hedysarum obtusum

Hedysarum obtusum, commonly known as the bluntleaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers in late spring. Hedysarum obtusum is part of the legume family, Fabaceae, and has the ability to fix nitrogen through its root nodules.

It is often used in ecological restoration due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soils.
