Hieracium shuttleworthianum

Hieracium shuttleworthianum, commonly known as the Shuttleworth's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
