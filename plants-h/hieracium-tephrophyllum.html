Hieracium tephrophyllum

Hieracium tephrophyllum, commonly known as the tephrophyllous hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is found in temperate regions of Europe. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium tephrophyllum is a perennial species that thrives in open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
