Hibbertia scandens var. denticulata

Hibbertia scandens var. denticulata, commonly known as the serrated hibbertia, is a climbing shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features wiry, thornless stems that twine around supports, and its leaves are elliptical with finely serrated margins. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers with prominent red stamens, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and dry forest habitats.

Hibbertia scandens var. denticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
