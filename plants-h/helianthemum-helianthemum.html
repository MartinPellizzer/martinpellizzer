Helianthemum helianthemum

Helianthemum helianthemum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow, typically blooming in spring and early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are adapted to dry conditions, making it drought-tolerant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
