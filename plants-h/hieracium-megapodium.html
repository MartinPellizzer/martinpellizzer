Hieracium megapodium

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers and robust, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough, hairy texture.
