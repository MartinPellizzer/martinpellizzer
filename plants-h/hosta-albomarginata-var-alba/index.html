Hosta Albomarginata Var. Alba

Hosta albomarginata var. alba is a striking variant of the hosta plant known for its unique white-edged leaves. This cultivar thrives in shady, moist environments and is a popular choice for gardeners seeking visual interest. Its foliage displays a delicate balance of green and white, creating a soft, elegant appearance. Easy to care for, it adds a touch of refinement to any garden setting. Whether used as a focal point or part of a larger planting scheme, it remains a beloved addition to the plant world.
