Haworthia mucronata var. rycroftiana

Haworthia mucronata var. rycroftiana, commonly known as the Rycroft's Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, typically with a pale green to blue-gray hue. The leaves are adorned with white to pale blue tubercles and have a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.
