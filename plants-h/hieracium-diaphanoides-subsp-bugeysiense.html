Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. bugeysiense

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

bugeysiense, commonly known as the Bugeysian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments.

The plant features a rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves and produces yellow composite flowers. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads.
