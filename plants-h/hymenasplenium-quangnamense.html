Hymenasplenium quangnamense

Hymenasplenium quangnamense, commonly known as the Quangnam spleenwort, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth form and is typically found in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a dark green to reddish-brown stem with closely spaced, lanceolate leaves that are often curled when dry. Its reproductive structures, including the calyptra and peristome, are well-developed and typical of mosses in the Pottiaceae family.

Hymenasplenium quangnamense is primarily distributed in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia.
