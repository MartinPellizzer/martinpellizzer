Hieracium elisaeanum

Hieracium elisaeanum, commonly known as Elisaean hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe, this species is often found in meadows and grasslands. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in dry conditions.

It is considered a perennial herb, typically growing to a height of about 20 to 40 centimeters.
