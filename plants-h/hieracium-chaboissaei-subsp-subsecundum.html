Hieracium chaboissaei subsp. subsecundum

Hieracium chaboissaei subsp.

subsecundum, commonly known as the Algerian hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has hairy stems and leaves. Its flowers are yellow and are arranged in a cluster at the ends of the stems.

It is a relatively small plant, rarely exceeding 30 cm in height.
