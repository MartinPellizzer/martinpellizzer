Hieracium macelophyllum

Hieracium macelophyllum, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium macelophyllum is often found in open habitats and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
