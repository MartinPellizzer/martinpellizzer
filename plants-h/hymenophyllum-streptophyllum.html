Hymenophyllum streptophyllum

Hymenophyllum streptophyllum, commonly known as the zigzag moss, is a small, epiphytic fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its delicate, creeping growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that often form a zigzag pattern. The leaves are typically green to reddish in color and covered with a dense layer of rhizoids. This plant belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is known for its intricate, feathery fronds.

It plays an important ecological role in tropical and subtropical forests by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for microorganisms.
