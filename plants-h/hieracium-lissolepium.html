Hieracium lissolepium

Hieracium lissolepium, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has linear, narrow leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Native to Europe, it is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

It is a perennial species that can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable habitats.
