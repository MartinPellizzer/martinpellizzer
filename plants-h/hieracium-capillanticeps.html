Hieracium capillanticeps

Hieracium capillanticeps, commonly known as the slender-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its daisy-like composite flowers, which consist of a central disc surrounded by ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine, hair-like structures, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hieracium capillanticeps is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
