Hieracium pseudocanipes

Hieracium pseudocanipes, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grasslands.

It is considered a perennial herb and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
