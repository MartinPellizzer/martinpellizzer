Holostigma bottae

Holostigma bottae, commonly known as the pink-flowered rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many species with small, cross-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in high-altitude environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, elongated seed pods.
