Hieracium pilipes subsp. subdimorphum

Hieracium pilipes subsp.

subdimorphum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped with hairy surfaces, and the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

This subspecies is distinguished by its smaller flower heads and shorter peduncles compared to other members of the species.
