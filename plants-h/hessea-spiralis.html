Hessea spiralis

Hessea spiralis, commonly known as the spiral lily, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. Hessea spiralis thrives in well-drained sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often found in coastal dunes and is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.
