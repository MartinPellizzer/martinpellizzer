Hypoxis petitiana

Hypoxis petitiana, commonly known as the small yellow star, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Hypoxis, which includes various species of ground-hugging plants. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Hypoxis petitiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
