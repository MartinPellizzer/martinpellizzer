Hieracium lanseanum subsp. ventenatii

Hieracium lanseanum subsp.

ventenatii, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape, and its stems are often reddish in color.

This subspecies is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
