Hieracium trichadenocaesium

Hieracium trichadenocaesium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, with stems that are often reddish in color.

Hieracium trichadenocaesium is a perennial species native to Europe and parts of Asia.
