Helichrysum cryptomerioides

Helichrysum cryptomerioides, commonly known as the golden marguerite or African daisy, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has aromatic, gray-green foliage that is both drought-tolerant and heat-resistant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
