Hypericum peplidifolium var. robustum

Hypericum peplidifolium var. robustum, commonly known as the robust hypericum, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional remedies.
