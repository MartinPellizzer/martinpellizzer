Hieracium homoglossum

Hieracium homoglossum, commonly known as the smooth meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves and its showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate climates.

Hieracium homoglossum is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense patches in its habitat.
