Hieracium sparsum subsp. tubulatum

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

tubulatum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clustered heads. Its flowering period occurs from late spring to early autumn, and it is often found in disturbed habitats.

The species is characterized by its slender stems and is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
