Hedysarum vicioides f. japonicum

Hedysarum vicioides f. japonicum, commonly known as Japanese vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow to orange flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is native to East Asia, particularly Japan, and is adapted to temperate climates. It has a deep root system that helps it thrive in well-drained soils.

This species is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen and stabilize soil.
