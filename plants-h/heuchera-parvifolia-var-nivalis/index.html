Heuchera Parvifolia Var. Nivalis

Heuchera parvifolia var. nivalis is a striking plant known for its vibrant foliage and compact growth. Native to the mountainous regions of North America, it thrives in cool, shaded environments. Its leaves display a range of colors from deep burgundy to soft pink, making it a popular choice for gardeners. This variety is particularly valued for its ability to add texture and visual interest to woodland gardens. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and moisture conditions to flourish.
