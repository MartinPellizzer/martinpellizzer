Homalomena humilis

Homalomena humilis, commonly known as the dwarf arrowhead plant, is a tropical evergreen herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spadices, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Homalomena humilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
