Hyacinthus haynei

Hyacinthus haynei, commonly known as the Hayne's hyacinth, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It produces striking clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.

Hyacinthus haynei is also noted for its hardiness and ability to naturalize over time.
