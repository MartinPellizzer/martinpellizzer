Hieracium virosum var. latifolium

Hieracium virosum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in the summer.
