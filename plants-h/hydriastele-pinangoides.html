Hydriastele pinangoides

Hydriastele pinangoides, commonly known as the pinang leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble the leaves of the pinang tree. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant's botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
