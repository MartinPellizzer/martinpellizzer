Habenaria trichoglossa

Habenaria trichoglossa, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped flower spikes that give it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial orchid, often found in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a prominent lip that aids in pollination.

The genus Habenaria is known for its diverse floral structures, and H. trichoglossa is no exception, showcasing intricate adaptations for its ecological niche.
