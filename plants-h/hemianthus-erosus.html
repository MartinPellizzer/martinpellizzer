Hemianthus erosus

Hemianthus erosus, commonly known as the African fanwort, is a small aquatic plant native to parts of Africa.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with yellow stamens that are prominent.

Hemianthus erosus is often used in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
