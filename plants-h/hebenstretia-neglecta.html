Hebenstretia neglecta

Hebenstretia neglecta, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features erect stems covered in fine hairs and produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Hebenstretia neglecta thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in arid regions.
