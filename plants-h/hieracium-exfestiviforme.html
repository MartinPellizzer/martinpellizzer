Hieracium exfestiviforme

Hieracium exfestiviforme, commonly known as the festive dandelion, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, followed by white, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture and often have a milky sap.

Hieracium exfestiviforme is a perennial herb that grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters.
