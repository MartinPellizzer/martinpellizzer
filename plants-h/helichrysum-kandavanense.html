Helichrysum kandavanense

Helichrysum kandavanense, commonly known as the Kandavan everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish to orange-colored flowers that are typically clustered in dense, spherical heads. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Helichrysum kandavanense is well adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
