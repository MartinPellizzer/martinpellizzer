Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. apertorum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

apertorum, commonly known as the open-centred hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The subspecies is distinguished by its open, rounded flower heads and is often found in southern Europe.
