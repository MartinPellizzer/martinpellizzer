Haplolobus pachypodus

Haplolobus pachypodus, commonly known as the thick-rooted lobelia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

Haplolobus pachypodus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
