Himatanthus drasticus

Himatanthus drasticus, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a distinctive appearance, featuring a short, thick stem and showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, hard seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant blooms and drought tolerance.
