Helianthemum acutiflorum

Helianthemum acutiflorum, commonly known as the sharpflower rockrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, upright leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to Mediterranean climates.
