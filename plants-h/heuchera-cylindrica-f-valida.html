Heuchera cylindrica f. valida

Heuchera cylindrica f. valida, commonly known as the red veined heuchera, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It features striking foliage with deep green leaves that have prominent red veins, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its vibrant foliage and low maintenance requirements in garden settings.
