Habenaria sikkimensis

Habenaria sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern Himalayas.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and often have a subtle fragrance.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and ecological adaptations.
