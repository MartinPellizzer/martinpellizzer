Haageocereus dichromus var. pallidior

Haageocereus dichromus var. pallidior, commonly known as the pale night bloomer, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems and is known for its striking white flowers that open at night. The plant has a spiny exterior, with areoles that bear both spines and flowers. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and nocturnal blooming habit.
