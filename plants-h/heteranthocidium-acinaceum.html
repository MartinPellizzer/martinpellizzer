Heteranthocidium acinaceum

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, pale blue to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are opposite and lance-shaped. It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and is found in parts of Europe and Asia.
