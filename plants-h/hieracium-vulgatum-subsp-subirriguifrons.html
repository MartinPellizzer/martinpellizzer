Hieracium vulgatum subsp. subirriguifrons

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

subirriguifrons, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to lanceolate, with a rough, hairy texture. Its yellow flowers are clustered in dense heads, often with a reddish or purplish tinge at the base.

It is characterized by its robust growth habit and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
