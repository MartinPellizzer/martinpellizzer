Helichrysum velatum

Helichrysum velatum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its densely hairy, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces papery, maturing to golden-yellow seed heads, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum velatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
