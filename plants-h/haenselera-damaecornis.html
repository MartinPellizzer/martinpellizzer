Haenselera damaecornis

Haenselera damaecornis, commonly known as the velvetleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are typically dark green and heart-shaped. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a prominent central stamen. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist woodland or forested areas.

Haenselera damaecornis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
