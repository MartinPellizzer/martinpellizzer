Hieracium coloripes

Hieracium coloripes, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers, which are typically arranged in a single, showy head. The plant has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium coloripes is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
