Hieracium floribundum var. subcochleatum

Hieracium floribundum var. subcochleatum, commonly known as the broad-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming basal leaves that are broadly ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces yellow composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family.
