Hypericum rochelii

Hypericum rochelii, commonly known as the Rochel's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and woody stems, often growing in rocky or sandy soils. It is typically found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
