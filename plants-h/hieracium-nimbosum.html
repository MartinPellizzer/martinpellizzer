Hieracium nimbosum

Hieracium nimbosum, commonly known as the clouded hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium nimbosum is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
