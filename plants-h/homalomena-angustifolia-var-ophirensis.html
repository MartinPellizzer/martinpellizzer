Homalomena angustifolia var. ophirensis

Homalomena angustifolia var. ophirensis, commonly known as the Ophirensis Caladium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is closely related to other caladium species. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor cultivation.
