Helichrysum schlechteri

Helichrysum schlechteri, commonly known as the Cape golden bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble sunflowers. The plant has narrow, grey-green leaves and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its drought tolerance and attractive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum schlechteri is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
