Habenaria arvensis

Habenaria arvensis, commonly known as the field orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, often in disturbed soils. The plant has a slender, erect stem that bears a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip. Its botanical features include a complex inflorescence and specialized pollination mechanisms.

It is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
