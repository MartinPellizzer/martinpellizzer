Habenaria pubescens

Habenaria pubescens, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking orchid-like flowers with pale yellow to greenish-white petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
