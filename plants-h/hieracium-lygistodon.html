Hieracium lygistodon

Hieracium lygistodon, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grassy areas, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium lygistodon is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
