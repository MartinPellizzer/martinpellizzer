Hieracium armerioides subsp. emossonicum

Hieracium armerioides subsp.

emossonicum, commonly known as the Emosson hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowering period occurs in the summer months.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
