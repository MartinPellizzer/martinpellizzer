Helichroa alba

Helichroa alba, commonly known as the white helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, white, daisy-like flowers. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and produces numerous tiny, clustered blooms. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Helichroa alba is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
