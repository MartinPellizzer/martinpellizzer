Hieracium exilicaule

Hieracium exilicaule, commonly known as slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium exilicaule is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to colonize new areas.
