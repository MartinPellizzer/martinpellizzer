Hieracium Didieri

Hieracium didieri is a lesser-known species of hawkweed native to specific regions of Europe. It is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant thrives in moist, grassy habitats and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are essential to protect its delicate ecosystems and ensure its survival.
