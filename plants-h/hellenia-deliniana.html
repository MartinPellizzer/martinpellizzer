Hellenia deliniana

Hellenia deliniana, commonly known as the slender hellenia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

Hellenia deliniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
