Hoya endauensis

Hoya endauensis, commonly known as the Endau Hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Malaysia.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Hoya endauensis is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
