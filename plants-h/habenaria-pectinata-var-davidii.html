Habenaria pectinata var. davidii

Habenaria pectinata var. davidii, commonly known as the David's Butterfly Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a slender, upright stem with several racemes of small, fragrant blooms. Its leaves are strap-shaped and often have a waxy texture.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
