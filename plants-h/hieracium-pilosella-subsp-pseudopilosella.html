Hieracium pilosella subsp. pseudopilosella

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

pseudopilosella, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

The plant features basal rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in dense, flat-topped clusters. Its leaves are covered with fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
