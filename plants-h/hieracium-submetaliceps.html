Hieracium submetaliceps

Hieracium submetaliceps, commonly known as the submetalic-headed hawkweed, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in composite flower heads. Its leaves are typically alternate, with a serrated margin and a rough texture.

Native to Europe, it is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
