Hymenoxys glabra

Hymenoxys glabra, commonly known as the smooth hymenoxys, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, displaying bright yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. Hymenoxys glabra is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a hardy plant in dry climates.
