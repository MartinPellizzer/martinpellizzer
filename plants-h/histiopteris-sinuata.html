Histiopteris sinuata

Histiopteris sinuata, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that have a zigzag or undulating pattern along their length. The plant has a creeping rhizome that produces clusters of fiddlehead-like fronds. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture.

Histiopteris sinuata thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
