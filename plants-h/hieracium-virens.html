Hieracium virens

Hieracium virens, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium virens is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
