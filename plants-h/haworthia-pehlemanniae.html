Haworthia pehlemanniae

Haworthia pehlemanniae, commonly known as the Pehlemann's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
