Hieracium hostianum

Hieracium hostianum, commonly known as the European meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and basal rosettes of leaves. The plant produces yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium hostianum is typically a perennial herb that can thrive in a variety of soil types.
