Hermione perlutea

Hermione perlutea, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a striking plant characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble lace.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a creeping rhizome system that allows it to spread and form dense mats. Its leaves are typically bioluminescent under certain conditions, adding a unique visual appeal.

Hermione perlutea thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires consistent humidity to flourish.
