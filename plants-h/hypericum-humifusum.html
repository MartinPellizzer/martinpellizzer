Hypericum humifusum

Hypericum humifusum, commonly known as yellow st. john's wort, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to North America.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has woody stems that lie close to the ground, with small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Hypericum humifusum is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and disturbed areas.
