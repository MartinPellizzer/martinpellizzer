Hieracium phrygionium

Hieracium phrygionium, commonly known as the French dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a basal rosette of heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces bright yellow flowers that develop into fluffy white seed heads. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium phrygionium is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
