Humata polypodioides

Humata polypodioides, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome is creeping and produces numerous small, spore-bearing leaves.

The structure of its fronds and spore clusters reflects its evolutionary adaptation to humid, low-light conditions.
