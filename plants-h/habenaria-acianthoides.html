Habenaria acianthoides

Habenaria acianthoides, commonly known as the orchid with violet-like flowers, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, violet-colored flowers with a distinct lip that often has a contrasting color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
