Haageocereus australis f. subtilispinus

Haageocereus australis f. subtilispinus, commonly known as the fine-spined desert cactus, is a rare and specialized cactus native to arid regions of South America.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its otherwise muted foliage.
