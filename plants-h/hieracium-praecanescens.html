Hieracium praecanescens

Hieracium praecanescens, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium praecanescens is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
