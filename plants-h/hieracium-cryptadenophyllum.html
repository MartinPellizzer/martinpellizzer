Hieracium cryptadenophyllum

Hieracium cryptadenophyllum, commonly known as the cryptadenophyllum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow disk flowers with numerous ray florets. This species is native to Europe and is often found in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges. It has a compact growth habit and is typically less than 30 cm in height.

The plant is known for its distinctive flowering structure and is of interest to botanists and gardeners alike.
