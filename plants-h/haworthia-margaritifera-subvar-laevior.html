Haworthia margaritifera subvar. laevior

Haworthia margaritifera subvar. laevior, commonly known as the smooth-leaved haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically smooth and glossy, with a pale green to bluish-green coloration. The leaves are often adorned with small, white tubercles or bumps, giving the plant a unique appearance. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
