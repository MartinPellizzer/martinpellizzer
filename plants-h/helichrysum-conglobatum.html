Helichrysum conglobatum

Helichrysum conglobatum, commonly known as the golden conglobate everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, golden-yellow flowers that are tightly packed into dense, spherical clusters. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs.

Helichrysum conglobatum is often used in dried flower arrangements due to its long-lasting, vibrant appearance.
