Hieracium bladonii

Hieracium bladonii, commonly known as Bladon's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowering period occurs in late summer, producing yellow flowers that attract pollinators.

Hieracium bladonii is often found in open habitats and is relatively common in its native range.
