Hosta rohdeifolia

Hosta rohdeifolia, commonly known as the silver queen hosta, is a popular ornamental plant native to eastern Asia.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a silvery-blue hue, often marked with creamy white veins. The plant produces upright, bell-shaped flowers in late summer, ranging from white to pale lavender. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta rohdeifolia is valued for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to garden conditions.
