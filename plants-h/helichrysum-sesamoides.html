Helichrysum sesamoides

Helichrysum sesamoides, commonly known as the everlasting daisy or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers and its ability to retain its color for extended periods after drying, making it popular in floral arrangements. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic foliage and resilience to drought. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or sandy environments.

Its common name reflects its long-lasting blooms, which are used in both ornamental and traditional medicinal applications.
