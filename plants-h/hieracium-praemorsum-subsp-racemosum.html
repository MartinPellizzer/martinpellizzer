Hieracium praemorsum subsp. racemosum

Hieracium praemorsum subsp.

racemosum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers arranged in a dense, racemose inflorescence, which consists of numerous small florets. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
