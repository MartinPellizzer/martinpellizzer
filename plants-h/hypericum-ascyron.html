Hypericum ascyron

Hypericum ascyron, commonly known as the golden st. john's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant features woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Hypericum ascyron is also valued for its medicinal properties, though it contains compounds that can interact with certain medications.
