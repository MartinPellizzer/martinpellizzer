Hemerocallis × traubiana

Hemerocallis × traubiana, commonly known as the Traubiana Daylily, is a hybrid ornamental plant in the genus Hemerocallis.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is native to China and has been widely cultivated for its hardiness and vibrant floral display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of soil conditions.

The foliage is broad and grass-like, providing a lush backdrop to its striking blooms.
