Hieracium grimsulanum

Hieracium grimsulanum, commonly known as the Grimsul yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous root system. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It produces yellow flowers with ray florets, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped and hairy.

Hieracium grimsulanum is often found in alpine and subalpine environments, where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions.
