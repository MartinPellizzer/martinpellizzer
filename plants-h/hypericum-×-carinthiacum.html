Hypericum × carinthiacum

Hypericum × carinthiacum, commonly known as the Carpathian St. John's Wort, is a hybrid shrub native to Central Europe.

It features upright, branching stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
