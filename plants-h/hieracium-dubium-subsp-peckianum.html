Hieracium dubium subsp. peckianum

Hieracium dubium subsp.

peckianum, commonly known as the Peck's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
