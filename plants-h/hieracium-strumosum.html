Hieracium strumosum

Hieracium strumosum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and its clusters of yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and marshes. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often reddish or purplish in color.

Hieracium strumosum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in disturbed or wetland environments.
