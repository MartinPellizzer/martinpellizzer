Helie

Helie, commonly known as the helie plant, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender stems and compound leaves that often have a fern-like appearance. The flowers are small and clustered in umbels, which is a typical feature of the Apiaceae family. Helie species are typically found in temperate regions and thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

They are sometimes cultivated for their ornamental value and have been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
