Hieracium stictophylloides

Hieracium stictophylloides, commonly known as the pale leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically has hairy, rough stems and heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and it produces small, oval-shaped achenes as fruits.

Hieracium stictophylloides is often found in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun.
