Hieracium covaledanum

Hieracium covaledanum, commonly known as the Covaledan hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features bright yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and often branching.

Hieracium covaledanum is native to parts of Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.
