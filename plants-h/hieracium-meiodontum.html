Hieracium meiodontum

Hieracium meiodontum, commonly known as the mid-tooth hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves. Native to Europe, it thrives in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
