Hedysarum scalpe

Hedysarum scalpe, commonly known as the scarlet trefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each consisting of three small, heart-shaped leaflets. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that are clustered in racemes, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Hedysarum scalpe is native to regions of Europe and Asia, often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.
