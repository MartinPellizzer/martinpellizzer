Helianthemum paniculatum var. rotundifolium

Helianthemum paniculatum var. rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leaved rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a low-growing shrub with succulent, fleshy leaves that are rounded in shape. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
