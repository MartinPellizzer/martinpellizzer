Homalium eburneum

Homalium eburneum, commonly known as the ivory wood or white wood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, light-colored bark and lance-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
