Haworthia chloracantha var. monticola

Haworthia chloracantha var. monticola, commonly known as the mountain haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of thick, fleshy, pale green leaves with white to yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, slender stems with small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
