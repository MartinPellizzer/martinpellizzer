Hieracium × manasae

Hieracium × manasae, commonly known as the Manasas hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations, making it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
