Helianthemum oelandicum var. boreale

Helianthemum oelandicum var. boreale, commonly known as the northern rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Arctic and subarctic regions of Europe and North America.

It features dense clusters of small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh, cold climates. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine and tundra habitats.

Its hardy nature makes it a resilient plant in extreme environments.
