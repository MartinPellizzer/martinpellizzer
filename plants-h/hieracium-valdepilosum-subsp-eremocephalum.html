Hieracium valdepilosum subsp. eremocephalum

Hieracium valdepilosum subsp.

eremocephalum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
