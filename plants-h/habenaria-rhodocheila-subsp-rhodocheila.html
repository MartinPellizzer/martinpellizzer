Habenaria rhodocheila subsp. rhodocheila

Habenaria rhodocheila subsp.

rhodocheila, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia. It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and its distinctive, spiral arrangement of pink or white flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. It produces small, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in variable conditions.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive floral display.
