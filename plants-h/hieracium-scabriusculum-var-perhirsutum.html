Hieracium scabriusculum var. perhirsutum

Hieracium scabriusculum var. perhirsutum, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features hairy, upright stems and basal leaves that are often covered in fine, stiff hairs. Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer, producing numerous small, tubular florets.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and woodland edges.
