Heteropappus altaicus var. millefolius

Heteropappus altaicus var. millefolius, commonly known as the yarrow-like composite, is a flowering plant native to the steppes of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant features slender, branching stems and finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in flat-topped clusters, typical of the family.

It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.
