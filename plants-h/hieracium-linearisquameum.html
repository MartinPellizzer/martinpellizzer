Hieracium linearisquameum

Hieracium linearisquameum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat, disc-like structure.

It is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
