Hippeastrum menesesii

Hippeastrum menesesii, commonly known as the Menesesii amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces a tall, sturdy stem with narrow, strap-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

Hippeastrum menesesii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
