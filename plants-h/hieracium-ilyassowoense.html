Hieracium ilyassowoense

Hieracium ilyassowoense, commonly known as the Ilyassowian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as steppes and rocky soils. Its leaves are basal, rough, and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

Hieracium ilyassowoense is valued for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a source of bioactive compounds.
