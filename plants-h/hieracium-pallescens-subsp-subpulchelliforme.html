Hieracium pallescens subsp. subpulchelliforme

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

subpulchelliforme, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

This subspecies is known for its moderate growth and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
