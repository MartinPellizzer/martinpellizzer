Hieracium scorzonera

Hieracium scorzonera, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish-green flowers and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its stems are hairy.

Hieracium scorzonera is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and environmental conditions.
