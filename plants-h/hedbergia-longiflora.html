Hedbergia longiflora

Hedbergia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered hedbergia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple, which bloom in clusters. Hedbergia longiflora is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and green spaces.
