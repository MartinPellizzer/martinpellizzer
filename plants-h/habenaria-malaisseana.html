Habenaria malaisseana

Habenaria malaisseana, commonly known as the Malacca orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a slender rhizome and produces a single, tall inflorescence bearing several showy, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its flowers have a distinctive three-lobed lip and are pollinated by insects.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
