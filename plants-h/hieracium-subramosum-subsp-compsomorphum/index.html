Hieracium Subramosum Subsp. Compsomorphum

Hieracium subramosum subsp. compsomorphum is a rare and lesser-known plant species belonging to the dandelion family. It is native to specific regions in southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. This subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and unique leaf and flower structures. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
