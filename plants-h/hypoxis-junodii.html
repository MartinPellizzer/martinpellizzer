Hypoxis junodii

Hypoxis junodii, commonly known as the Lesotho starflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Hypoxis junodii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
