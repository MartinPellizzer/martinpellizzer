Hieracium scaberulum

Hieracium scaberulum, commonly known as rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and basal rosettes of broad, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in a compact cluster, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas, where it can form dense colonies.
