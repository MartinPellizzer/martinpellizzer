Hieracium translucens

Hieracium translucens, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive central disc.

Hieracium translucens is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
