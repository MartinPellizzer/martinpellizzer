Hypericum perforatum f. lucidum

Hypericum perforatum f. lucidum, commonly known as the glossy St. John's wort, is a variant of the common St. John's wort.

It is characterized by its distinctive glossy, dark green leaves that are often marked with translucent dots. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in summer. It is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and woodland edges.

This form is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
