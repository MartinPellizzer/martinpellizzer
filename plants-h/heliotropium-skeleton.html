Heliotropium skeleton

Heliotropium skeleton, commonly known as the skeletonweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, branching structure and small, purple or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. Its leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a hairy texture.

It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in arid or semi-arid environments.
