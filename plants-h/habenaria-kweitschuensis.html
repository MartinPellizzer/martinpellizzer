Habenaria kweitschuensis

Habenaria kweitschuensis, commonly known as the Kweitschuan marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wet meadows. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elongated, narrow leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
