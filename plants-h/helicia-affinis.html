Helicia affinis

Helicia affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed helenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and glossy, with a leathery texture.
