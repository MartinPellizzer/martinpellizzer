Hedychium brevicaule

Hedychium brevicaule, commonly known as the dwarf ginger lily, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, sword-shaped leaves and grows as an herbaceous perennial. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hedychium brevicaule is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
