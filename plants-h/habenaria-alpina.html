Habenaria alpina

Habenaria alpina, commonly known as the alpine butterfly orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to mountainous regions of Asia and North America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are pale green to yellowish in color.

Habenaria alpina is often found in high-altitude meadows and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
