Haworthiopsis woolleyi

Haworthiopsis woolleyi, commonly known as the Blue Baby Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, with white markings. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.
