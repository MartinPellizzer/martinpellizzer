Hesperaster decapetalus

Hesperaster decapetalus, commonly known as the ten-petaled starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are composed of ten petals, which is a distinguishing feature of its species name.

Hesperaster decapetalus is often found in rocky soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.
