Habenaria hexaptera

Habenaria hexaptera, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive three-lobed lip and a sweet, spicy fragrance.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique appearance and aromatic qualities.
