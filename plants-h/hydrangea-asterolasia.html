Hydrangea asterolasia

Hydrangea asterolasia, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features large, showy inflorescences composed of both sterile and fertile flowers, which can range in color from white to pink and blue depending on soil pH. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
