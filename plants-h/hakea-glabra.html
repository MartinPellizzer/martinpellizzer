Hakea glabra

Hakea glabra, commonly known as the smooth hakea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, often greyish bark and dense, needle-like leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. Hakea glabra is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

It is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
