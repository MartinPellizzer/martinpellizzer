Helichrysum orbiculare

Helichrysum orbiculare, commonly known as the golden helmet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of yellow flowers that resemble small helmets, hence its common name. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its aromatic, resinous scent. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Helichrysum orbiculare is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
