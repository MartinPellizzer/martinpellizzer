Habenaria bonatea var. boltonii

Habenaria bonatea var. boltonii, commonly known as the Bolton's lady's-tongue, is a species of orchid native to parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are strap-like and often clustered at the base of the plant.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral structure.
