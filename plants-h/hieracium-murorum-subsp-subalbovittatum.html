Hieracium murorum subsp. subalbovittatum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

subalbovittatum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to alpine and subalpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in open, rocky, or grassy habitats at high altitudes.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in the summer. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive white or pale green border.

It is part of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, showy inflorescences.
