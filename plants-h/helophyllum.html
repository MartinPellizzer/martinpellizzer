Helophyllum

Helophyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate, almost fern-like appearance. Helophyllum species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to their ability to stabilize soil in wetland areas.
