Howardia eriantha

Howardia eriantha, commonly known as the woolly mooncactus, is a unique plant belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant is characterized by its swollen, bulbous stem that resembles a cactus and its delicate, velvety leaves that give it a soft appearance. It produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its textured foliage.

Due to its unusual appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
