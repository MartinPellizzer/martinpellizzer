Hypsela reniformis

Hypsela reniformis, commonly known as the kidney-shaped leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive reniform (kidney-shaped) leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is valued both for ornamental purposes and ecological benefits.
