Hovenia dulcis var. glabra

Hovenia dulcis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth hovenia, is a shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are typically alternate and ovate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by small, fleshy fruits that are edible. The fruits have a sweet, wine-like flavor and are often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
