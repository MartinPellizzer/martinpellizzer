Hieracium pseudopilosellinum

Hieracium pseudopilosellinum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has finely divided, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
