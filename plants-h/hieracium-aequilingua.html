Hieracium aequilingua

Hieracium aequilingua, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium aequilingua is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
