Habenaria caldensis

Habenaria caldensis, commonly known as the Calden's butterfly orchid, is a species of orchid native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or rocky areas. Its floral structure includes a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

Habenaria caldensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
