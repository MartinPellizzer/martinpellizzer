Hoheria equitum

Hoheria equitum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a unique and striking plant native to New Zealand.

It is a small, woody shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The leaves are narrow and leathery, with a distinctive silvery-white coating that reflects light. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

The plant is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
