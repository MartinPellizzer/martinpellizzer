Hieracium heerii

Hieracium heerii, commonly known as the Herder's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. It has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in alpine and subalpine regions.

Hieracium heerii is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor environments.
