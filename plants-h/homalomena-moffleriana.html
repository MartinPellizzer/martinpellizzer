Homalomena moffleriana

Homalomena moffleriana, commonly known as the red-flowered arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. Its most notable feature is the vibrant red spathe that surrounds the yellow spadix, creating a visually captivating display.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.
