Hippeastrum glaucescens

Hippeastrum glaucescens, commonly known as the blue hurricane lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, often with a bluish tinge. The plant produces strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a fleshy underground bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hippeastrum glaucescens is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
