Hieracium lonchodes

Hieracium lonchodes, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. Hieracium lonchodes is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
