Helenium nuttallii

Helenium nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to the central and eastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy yellow-orange blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its stems are upright and hairy.

Helenium nuttallii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
