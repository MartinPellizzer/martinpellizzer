Huodendron chunianum

Huodendron chunianum, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental tree in gardens and parks.

Its botanical features make it both visually appealing and ecologically valuable.
