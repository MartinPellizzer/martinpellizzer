Haworthia pallidifolia

Haworthia pallidifolia, commonly known as the pale leaf Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, pale green leaves that are often covered in a white or grayish waxy coating. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
