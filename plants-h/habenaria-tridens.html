Habenaria tridens

Habenaria tridens, commonly known as the three-toothed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale green with three prominent teeth on the lip, giving it its common name.

Habenaria tridens thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
