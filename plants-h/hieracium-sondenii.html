Hieracium sondenii

Hieracium sondenii, commonly known as the Sondens' hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and cool climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer.

Hieracium sondenii is often found in rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
