Huodendron decorticatum

Huodendron decorticatum, commonly known as the Chinese sandalwood, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its fragrant, aromatic wood. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Due to its valuable timber and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
