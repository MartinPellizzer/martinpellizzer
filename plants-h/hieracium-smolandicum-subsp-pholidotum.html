Hieracium smolandicum subsp. pholidotum

Hieracium smolandicum subsp.

pholidotum, commonly known as the Svalbard hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Arctic and alpine regions. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette growth habit and yellow composite flowers.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically grouped in a single head. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or barren habitats.
