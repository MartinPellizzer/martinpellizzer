Hypericum qinlingense

Hypericum qinlingense, commonly known as Qinling hypericum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils and sunny exposures. It has small, opposite leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
