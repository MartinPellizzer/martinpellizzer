Heterotaenia alpestris

Heterotaenia alpestris, commonly known as the alpine heterotaenia, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine meadows. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of delicate, white or pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to cold climates and has a short growing season.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
