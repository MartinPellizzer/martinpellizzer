Hippeastrum jamesonii

Hippeastrum jamesonii, commonly known as the Jameson's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to pink and red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

Hippeastrum jamesonii is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
