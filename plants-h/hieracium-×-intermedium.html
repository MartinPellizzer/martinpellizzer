Hieracium × intermedium

Hieracium × intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate hawkweed, is a hybrid species within the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its composite flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk flowers, typical of the dandelion family. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows, and it thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges, and the stems are often hairy.

Hieracium × intermedium is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
