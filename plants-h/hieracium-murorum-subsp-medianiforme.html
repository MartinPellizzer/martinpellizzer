Hieracium murorum subsp. medianiforme

Hieracium murorum subsp.

medianiforme, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. It has a taproot system and can spread vegetatively through rhizomes.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in various parts of Europe.
