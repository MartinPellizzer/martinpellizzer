Habenaria balimensis

Habenaria balimensis, commonly known as the Balimenes orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the island of Bali in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
