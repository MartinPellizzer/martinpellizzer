Hieracium polyauxes

Hieracium polyauxes, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium polyauxes is known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
