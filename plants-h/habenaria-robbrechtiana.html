Habenaria robbrechtiana

Habenaria robbrechtiana, commonly known as the Robbrecht's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and pine flatwoods. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that produce multiple leaves and flowering stems. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red spots.

The species is characterized by its intricate floral structure and is part of the Orchidaceae family.
