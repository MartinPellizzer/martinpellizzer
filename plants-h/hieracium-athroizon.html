Hieracium athroizon

Hieracium athroizon, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Its hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves are notable botanical features.
