Hieracium kuroksarense

Hieracium kuroksarense, commonly known as the Kuroksare hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes with limited soil nutrients. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
