Hieracium aronicifolium var. litigiosum

Hieracium aronicifolium var. litigiosum, commonly known as the purple sandwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, purple or pinkish flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
