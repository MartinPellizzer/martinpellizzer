Huernia duodecimfida

Huernia duodecimfida, commonly known as the twelve-ribbed African ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are often reduced to spines, which help minimize water loss.

Huernia duodecimfida is popular among cactus and succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of care.
