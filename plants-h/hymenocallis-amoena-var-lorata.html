Hymenocallis amoena var. lorata

Hymenocallis amoena var. lorata, commonly known as the pink hurricane lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from pink to red, often with a contrasting yellow throat. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are narrow and pointed. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
