Hypoxis stenophylla

Hypoxis stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf yellowstar, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with white markings. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in fynbos vegetation. It has small, fibrous roots that help it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Hypoxis stenophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
