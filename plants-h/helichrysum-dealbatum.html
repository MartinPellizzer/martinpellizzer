Helichrysum dealbatum

Helichrysum dealbatum, commonly known as the woolly sunburst, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that are often covered in fine, hairy bracts, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant is characterized by its aromatic foliage, which emits a subtle, pleasant scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
