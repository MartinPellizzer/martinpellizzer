Helianthemum eriocaulon

Helianthemum eriocaulon, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has dense, hairy foliage that helps it retain moisture and resist drought. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
