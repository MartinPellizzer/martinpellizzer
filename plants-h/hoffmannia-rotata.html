Hoffmannia rotata

Hoffmannia rotata, commonly known as the blue haworthia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with white or grayish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Hoffmannia rotata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in both indoor and outdoor settings.
