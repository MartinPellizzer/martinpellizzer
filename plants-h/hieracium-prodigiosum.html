Hieracium prodigiosum

Hieracium prodigiosum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer, surrounded by pale green bracts. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils.

Hieracium prodigiosum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
