Hypoxis lata

Hypoxis lata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Hypoxis lata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
