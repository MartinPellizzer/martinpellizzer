Hyphaene bussei

Hyphaene bussei, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a robust, fan-shaped palm native to the savannas of sub-Saharan Africa.

It features a tall, slender trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown bark and large, compound leaves that radiate from the top. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered, spiny fruits containing edible seeds. Hyphaene bussei is adapted to arid conditions, with deep roots that help it survive in dry environments.

It plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
