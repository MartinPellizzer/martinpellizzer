Haworthia Cooperi Var. Tenera

Haworthia cooperi var. tenera is a unique succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water to flourish. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Due to its slow growth and ease of care, it is favored by both beginners and experienced plant enthusiasts.
