Hakea pandanicarpa subsp. pandanicarpa

Hakea pandanicarpa subsp.

pandanicarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in winter.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance. Its root system is deep, allowing it to access water from lower soil layers.

It plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.
