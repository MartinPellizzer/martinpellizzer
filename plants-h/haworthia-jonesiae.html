Haworthia jonesiae

Haworthia jonesiae, commonly known as the Jonesia Haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with distinctive white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
