Helenium longiaristatum

Helenium longiaristatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf helenium, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, ray-like florets. Its leaves are linear to lance-shaped, with a distinctively narrow profile, and it produces yellow to orange flowers in the late summer to early fall.

Helenium longiaristatum is often found in moist, woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
