Halimium scoparium

Halimium scoparium, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Halimium scoparium thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.
