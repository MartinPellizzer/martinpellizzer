Hyptis brevipes var. remotidens

Hyptis brevipes var. remotidens, commonly known as the slender hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows as a low shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pale blue or violet and are arranged in dense clusters.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
