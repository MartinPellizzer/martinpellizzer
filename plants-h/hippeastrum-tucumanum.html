Hippeastrum tucumanum

Hippeastrum tucumanum, commonly known as the Tucuman amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant features strap-shaped, evergreen leaves that are broad and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum tucumanum is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
