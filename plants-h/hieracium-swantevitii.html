Hieracium swantevitii

Hieracium swantevitii, commonly known as the Swantevit's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and cool climates. Hieracium swantevitii is known for its ecological importance in stabilizing soil in high-altitude ecosystems.
