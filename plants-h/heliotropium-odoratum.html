Heliotropium odoratum

Heliotropium odoratum, commonly known as sweet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
