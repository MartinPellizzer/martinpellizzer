Homalium gnyenziense

Homalium gnyenziense, commonly known as the Yunnan beautyberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan province, and is characterized by its attractive, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a velvety texture.

The fruit, which is edible, is a small, dark purple berry that ripens in the fall.
