Habenaria longispica

Habenaria longispica, commonly known as the long-spurred orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with long, slender spurs that give it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is notable for its intricate floral structure and adaptability to tropical conditions.
