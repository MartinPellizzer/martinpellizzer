Habenaria eatoniana

Habenaria eatoniana, commonly known as the Eaton's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and swamps. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive hooded labellum.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
