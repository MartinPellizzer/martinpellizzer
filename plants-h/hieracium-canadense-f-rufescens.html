Hieracium canadense f. rufescens

Hieracium canadense f. rufescens, commonly known as the Canadian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, erect stems and yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and disturbed soils. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with rough textures, and the subspecies rufescens is distinguished by its reddish tinge in the flower heads.

It is often found in the northeastern United States and parts of Canada.
