Helinus lanceolatus var. lanceolatus

Helinus lanceolatus var. lanceolatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf helinus, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or sandy gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and seasonal floral display.
