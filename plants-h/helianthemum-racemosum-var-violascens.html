Helianthemum racemosum var. violascens

Helianthemum racemosum var. violascens, commonly known as the purple sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, often with a slight violet hue. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to thrive in rocky or sandy soils.

The species is well-suited to dry, sunny environments and is often found in coastal areas or limestone habitats.
