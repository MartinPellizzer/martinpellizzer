Haworthia rubriflora

Haworthia rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces striking red or pink flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
