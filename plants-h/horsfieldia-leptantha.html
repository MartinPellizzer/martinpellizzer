Horsfieldia leptantha

Horsfieldia leptantha, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as rainforests, where it thrives on the forest floor or as an epiphyte on tree trunks. Horsfieldia leptantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
