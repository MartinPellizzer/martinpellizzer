Haworthia zantneriana

Haworthia zantneriana, commonly known as the Zantner's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
