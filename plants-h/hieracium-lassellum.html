Hieracium lassellum

Hieracium lassellum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as meadows and roadsides, and it produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it has a fibrous root system.

Hieracium lassellum is often found in temperate climates and is considered a common weed in some regions.
