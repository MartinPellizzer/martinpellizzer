Habenaria integrilabris

Habenaria integrilabris, commonly known as the smooth-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, smooth lip. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and bloom during the summer months.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
