Hippocrepis confusa subvar. leiocarpa

Hippocrepis confusa subvar. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-flowered helmet orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive helmet-shaped flowers, which are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in calcareous grasslands and rocky habitats. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and the flowers are borne on slender stems.

This subvariety is noted for its subtle appearance and is often overlooked due to its inconspicuous foliage.
