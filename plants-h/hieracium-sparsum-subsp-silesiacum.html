Hieracium sparsum subsp. silesiacum

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

silesiacum, commonly known as the Silesian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

It is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
