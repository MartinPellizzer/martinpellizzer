Hoya lipoensis

Hoya lipoensis, commonly known as the Lipoensis Wisteria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, green, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. Hoya lipoensis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
