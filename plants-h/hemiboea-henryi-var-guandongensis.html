Hemiboea henryi var. guandongensis

Hemiboea henryi var. guandongensis, commonly known as the Guangdong hemiboea, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, tubular flowers. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
