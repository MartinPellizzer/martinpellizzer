Habenaria hamigera

Habenaria hamigera, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its flowers are usually yellow or golden in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker and more prominent.

Habenaria hamigera is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
