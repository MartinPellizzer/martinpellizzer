Hieracium glabratum var. ampezzanum

Hieracium glabratum var. ampezzanum, commonly known as the Ampezzan hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish-green composite flowers and basal rosettes of leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, often in mountainous areas. It has a low-growing habit and is adapted to cold climates with short growing seasons.
