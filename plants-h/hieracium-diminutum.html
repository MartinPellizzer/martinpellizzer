Hieracium diminutum

Hieracium diminutum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually hairy as well. It produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it often thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.
