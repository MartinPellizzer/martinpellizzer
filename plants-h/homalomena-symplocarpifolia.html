Homalomena symplocarpifolia

Homalomena symplocarpifolia, commonly known as the purple-leafed arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display a vibrant purple or reddish hue. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative houseplant in many regions.
