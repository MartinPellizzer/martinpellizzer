Hakea beadleana

Hakea beadleana, commonly known as the red hakea, is a shrub native to southwestern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, cone-shaped inflorescences that are often red or orange in color. The plant has thick, leathery leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered at the base of the cones.

Hakea beadleana is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.
