Hieracium pseudoilerdense

Hieracium pseudoilerdense, commonly known as the Ilerdene hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pale green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant has small, rough, and often glandular leaves, which help reduce water loss.

Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
