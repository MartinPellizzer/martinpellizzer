Heliotropium tenuifolium var. speciosum

Heliotropium tenuifolium var. speciosum, commonly known as the slender-leaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often resembling those of the common heliotrope. The plant has slender, hairy leaves that are usually opposite or alternate in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
