Habenaria uncata

Habenaria uncata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil, and it is known for its ability to thrive in humid climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
