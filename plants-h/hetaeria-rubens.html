Hetaeria rubens

Hetaeria rubens, commonly known as the red-flowered hetaeria, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and aromatic leaves, which emit a distinctive scent. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are tubular and clustered in whorls, making it visually distinctive.
