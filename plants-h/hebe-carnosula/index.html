Hebe Carnosula

Hebe carnosula is a small evergreen shrub native to New Zealand, known for its compact growth and vibrant foliage. It features thick, fleshy leaves that give it a unique appearance, often used in rock gardens and containers. This plant is prized for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions. Its flowers, though small, add a subtle charm to the garden during the spring season. Hebe carnosula is a hardy and versatile plant that offers both ornamental value and low maintenance care.
