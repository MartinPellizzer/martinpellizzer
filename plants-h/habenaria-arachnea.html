Habenaria arachnea

Habenaria arachnea, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals that resemble spider legs. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it can receive indirect sunlight. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Habenaria arachnea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare and exotic plants.
