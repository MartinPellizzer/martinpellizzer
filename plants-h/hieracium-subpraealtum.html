Hieracium subpraealtum

Hieracium subpraealtum, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.

Hieracium subpraealtum is known for its aggressive growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
