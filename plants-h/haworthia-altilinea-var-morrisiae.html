Haworthia altilinea var. morrisiae

Haworthia altilinea var. morrisiae, commonly known as the Morris' Haworthia, is a compact, clumping succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
