Hieracium mauritianum

Hieracium mauritianum, commonly known as the Mauritius hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, often forming dense mats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium mauritianum is known for its ornamental value and has been introduced to other regions as a garden plant.
