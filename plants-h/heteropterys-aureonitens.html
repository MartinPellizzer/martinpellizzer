Heteropterys aureonitens

Heteropterys aureonitens, commonly known as the golden-leafed evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are typically yellow or golden in color, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
