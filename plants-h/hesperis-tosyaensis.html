Hesperis tosyaensis

Hesperis tosyaensis, commonly known as the Tuscan Hesperis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with four-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
