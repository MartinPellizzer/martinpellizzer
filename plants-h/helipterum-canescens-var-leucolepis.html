Helipterum canescens var. leucolepis

Helipterum canescens var. leucolepis, commonly known as the white-rayed everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to Australia, characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The species features pale blue to white petals with yellow centers, and its leaves are typically gray-green and hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in sandy or rocky habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its long-lasting blooms.
