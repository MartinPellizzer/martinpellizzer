Hypericum patulum var. oblongifolium

Hypericum patulum var. oblongifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf St. John's wort, is a low-growing shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in clusters along the stems. The leaves are elliptical in shape, with a smooth texture and a slightly succulent appearance. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

It is noted for its hardy nature and ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
