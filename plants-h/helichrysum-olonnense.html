Helichrysum olonnense

Helichrysum olonnense, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers. The plant produces small, papery bracts that persist after the flowers have faded, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum olonnense is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
