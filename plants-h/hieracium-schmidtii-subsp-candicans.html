Hieracium schmidtii subsp. candicans

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

candicans, commonly known as the shining hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are arranged in a composite head.

This subspecies is distinguished by its bright yellow ray florets and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
