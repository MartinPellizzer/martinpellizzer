Habenaria bangii

Habenaria bangii, commonly known as the Bornean butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that resembles a butterfly.

Habenaria bangii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
