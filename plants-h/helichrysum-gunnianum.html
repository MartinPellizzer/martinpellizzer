Helichrysum gunnianum

Helichrysum gunnianum, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often arranged in dense clusters. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white bloom.

Helichrysum gunnianum is well adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
