Hymenophyllum liukiuense

Hymenophyllum liukiuense, commonly known as the Liukiu fern, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the Hymenophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, moss-like fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant has a unique, intricate structure with closely spaced, hair-like leaves that give it a velvety appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found on rock surfaces or in forest understories.

Hymenophyllum liukiuense is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Japan and the Philippines.
