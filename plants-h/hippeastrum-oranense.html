Hippeastrum oranense

Hippeastrum oranense, commonly known as the Orange Flame or Orange Flame Lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant produces striking orange-red flowers with a white throat, often with a yellow or red center. It has strap-like, broad leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture.

Hippeastrum oranense is valued for its vibrant blooms and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens with well-draining soil.
