Hieracium stenolepis var. anguinum

Hieracium stenolepis var. anguinum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

The variety anguinum is distinguished by its narrow leaves and specific geographic distribution.
