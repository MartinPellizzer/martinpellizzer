Heliconia micholitzii

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant, spathiflorous inflorescences that resemble bird beaks. Heliconia micholitzii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests and coastal areas.
