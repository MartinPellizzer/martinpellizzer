Hieracium vulgatum var. subfasciculare

Hieracium vulgatum var. subfasciculare, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. It has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

This variety is distinguished by its clustered flowering heads, which give it its common name.
