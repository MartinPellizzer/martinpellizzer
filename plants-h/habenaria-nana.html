Habenaria nana

Habenaria nana, commonly known as the dwarf lady's tresses, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in acidic soil conditions. The plant features a dense, mat-like growth habit with slender, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are arranged in a loose, spiraled cluster and are usually white or pale pink in color.

Habenaria nana is a relatively rare species, often found in specific microhabitats within its native range.
