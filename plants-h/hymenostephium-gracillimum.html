Hymenostephium gracillimum

Hymenostephium gracillimum, commonly known as the slender hymenostephium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in the region of South Africa. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, contributing to its overall delicate appearance.

Hymenostephium gracillimum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
