Habenaria jimenezii

Habenaria jimenezii, commonly known as the Jimenez's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces spikes of small, colorful blooms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
