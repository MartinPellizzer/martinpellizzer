Hypericum schimperi var. angustisepalum

Hypericum schimperi var. angustisepalum, commonly known as the narrow-sepal St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs known for their yellow flowers. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers with five petals and distinctive, narrow sepals. It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing adaptability to harsh environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
