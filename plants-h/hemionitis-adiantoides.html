Hemionitis adiantoides

Hemionitis adiantoides, commonly known as the water fern, is a small, aquatic plant native to the wetlands of eastern Asia.

The plant thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving water, often forming dense mats on the surface. Its rhizomes are submerged, anchoring the plant and absorbing nutrients from the water.
