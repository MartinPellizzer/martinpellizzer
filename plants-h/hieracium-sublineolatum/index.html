Hieracium Sublineolatum

Hieracium sublineolatum is a lesser-known member of the dandelion family known for its delicate yellow flowers and unique leaf patterns. This plant is often found in moist, disturbed soils across northern Europe and parts of Asia. It thrives in open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges, where it can receive ample sunlight. Its small size and subtle appearance make it easy to overlook, yet it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Despite its modest looks, Hieracium sublineolatum is a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
