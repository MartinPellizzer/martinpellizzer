Hieracium collinum var. angustifolium

Hieracium collinum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky slopes. The plant features upright stems covered with stiff, hairy leaves that are narrow and lance-shaped. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster, producing small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

It is part of the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its low-growing, spreading habit.
