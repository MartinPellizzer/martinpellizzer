Helichrysum cylindricum

Helichrysum cylindricum, commonly known as the cylindrical everlasting flower, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, yellow to golden-orange flowers that are tightly clustered in cylindrical shapes, giving it its common name. The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to retain its color after drying makes it a favored plant for floral arrangements.
