Hibiscus grewioides

Hibiscus grewioides, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and grows as an herbaceous perennial or shrub. Hibiscus grewioides thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.
