Habenaria quinquefila

Habenaria quinquefila, commonly known as the five-threaded orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizomatous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

The orchid's unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
