Hippocrepis humilis

Hippocrepis humilis, commonly known as the small helmet orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

The plant grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, fragrant blossoms during the spring season.
