Hatiora salicornoides f. villigera

Hatiora salicornoides f. villigera, commonly known as the Variegated Christmas Cactus, is a hybrid epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It features strikingly variegated foliage with a mix of green, white, and pink hues, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This cultivar is popular for its ornamental value and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
