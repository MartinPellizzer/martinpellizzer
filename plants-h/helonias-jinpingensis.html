Helonias jinpingensis

Helonias jinpingensis, commonly known as the Jinping marsh orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers and is typically found in wetland habitats. The plant has a short, erect stem with a few leaves and produces small, clustered blooms. It is adapted to moist environments and requires specific conditions for successful growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species.
