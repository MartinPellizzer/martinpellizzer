Hieracium sosvaense

Hieracium sosvaense, commonly known as Sosva's hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically hairy stems. The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow flowers in the summer months. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
