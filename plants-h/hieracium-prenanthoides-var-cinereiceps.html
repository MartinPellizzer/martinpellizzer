Hieracium prenanthoides var. cinereiceps

Hieracium prenanthoides var. cinereiceps, commonly known as the cinereiceps hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often found in disturbed habitats and can form dense colonies in suitable conditions.
