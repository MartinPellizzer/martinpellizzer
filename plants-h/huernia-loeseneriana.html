Huernia loeseneriana

Huernia loeseneriana, commonly known as the Lion's Tail, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and spiky, leaf-like structures that resemble a lion's tail. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Huernia loeseneriana is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
