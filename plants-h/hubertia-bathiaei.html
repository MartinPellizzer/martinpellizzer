Hubertia bathiaei

Hubertia bathiaei, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often with a distinctive black center. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in its harsh environment. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds.

Hubertia bathiaei is well adapted to drought conditions and thrives in full sun.
