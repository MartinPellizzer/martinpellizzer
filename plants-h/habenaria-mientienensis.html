Habenaria mientienensis

Habenaria mientienensis, commonly known as the Mientien orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its floral structure includes three sepals and three petals, with the latter often being showy and brightly colored.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
