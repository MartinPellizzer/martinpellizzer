Habenaria psycodes var. grandiflora

Habenaria psycodes var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as swamps and marshes.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Orchidaceae family, valued for both its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
