Hieracium capnostylum

Hieracium capnostylum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Hieracium capnostylum is known for its rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.
