Habenaria trimeropetala var. itatingensis

Habenaria trimeropetala var. itatingensis, commonly known as the Itatinga orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive greenish tint. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
