Haworthia greenii

Haworthia greenii, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers on slender stalks during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
