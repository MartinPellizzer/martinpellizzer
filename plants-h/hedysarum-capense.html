Hedysarum capense

Hedysarum capense, commonly known as the Cape peavine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial legume that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features compound leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers in early spring. Its seed pods are distinctive, containing several small, bean-like seeds.

Hedysarum capense is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in poor soils.
