Horridocactus grandiflorus

Horridocactus grandiflorus, commonly known as the giant hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the deserts of northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in arid gardens and xeriscapes.
