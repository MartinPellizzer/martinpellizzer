Hugelia lanata

Hugelia lanata, commonly known as the woolly hugelia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, woolly leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hugelia lanata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
