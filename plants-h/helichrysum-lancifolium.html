Helichrysum lancifolium

Helichrysum lancifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved immortelle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic, resinous scent. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Helichrysum lancifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
