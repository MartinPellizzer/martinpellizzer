Hypoxis hirsuta var. leptocarpa

Hypoxis hirsuta var. leptocarpa, commonly known as the slender-stemmed yellowstar, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of golden-yellow flowers in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its striking foliage and blooms.
