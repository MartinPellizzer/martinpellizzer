Habenaria yookuaaensis

Habenaria yookuaaensis, commonly known as the Yookuaa orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces showy, often purple or pinkish flowers.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
