Hieracium dysonymum

Hieracium dysonymum, commonly known as the Dyson's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium dysonymum is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
