Hypericum kamtschaticum var. pibairense

Hypericum kamtschaticum var. pibairense, commonly known as the Pibairensis St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland edges.

Its medicinal properties, similar to other Hypericum species, have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.
