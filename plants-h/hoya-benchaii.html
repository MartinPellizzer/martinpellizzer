Hoya benchaii

Hoya benchaii, commonly known as the "Coral Hoya," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in the summer months. Hoya benchaii is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive appearance.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
