Helichrysum bracteatum var. chrysanthemum

Helichrysum bracteatum var. chrysanthemum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often used in ornamental gardening for its visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its flowers are also known for their medicinal properties and are sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
