Habenaria paulensis

Habenaria paulensis, commonly known as the Paul's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to white with intricate patterns, and it produces small, round pseudobulbs.

Habenaria paulensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
