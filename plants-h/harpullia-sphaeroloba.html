Harpullia sphaeroloba

Harpullia sphaeroloba, commonly known as the red-fruited hollies, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically white or greenish-white. The plant produces bright red, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
