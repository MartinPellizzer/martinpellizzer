Hystrix patula

Hystrix patula, commonly known as the white hystrix, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Hystrix patula is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
