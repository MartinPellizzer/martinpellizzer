Hieracium capnostylum var. amaurostylum

Hieracium capnostylum var. amaurostylum, commonly known as the dark-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of dark green, hairy leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
