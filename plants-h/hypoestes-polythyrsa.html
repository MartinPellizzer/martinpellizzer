Hypoestes polythyrsa

Hypoestes polythyrsa, commonly known as the "Magic Blue" or "Snowflake" plant, is a flowering species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, bi-colored leaves that often feature a blue or purple hue on the upper surface and a lighter green underside. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant.
