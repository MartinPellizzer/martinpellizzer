Henckelia crenata

Henckelia crenata, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows at high altitudes, often in cloud forests. The plant features showy, five-petaled flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are ovate with wavy edges and are often covered in fine hairs.
