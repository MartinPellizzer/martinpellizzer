Hieracium armerioides subsp. nigritellum

Hieracium armerioides subsp.

nigritellum, commonly known as the black-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.

The plant features dark green, basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, blooming from late spring to early summer.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
