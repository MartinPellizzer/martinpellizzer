Habenaria siamensis

Habenaria siamensis, commonly known as the Siam orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to white with a yellow lip, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
