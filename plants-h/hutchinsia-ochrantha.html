Hutchinsia ochrantha

Hutchinsia ochrantha, commonly known as the red-flowered hutchinsia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Christmas Island in the Indian Ocean.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its striking red flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It has small, narrow leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.
