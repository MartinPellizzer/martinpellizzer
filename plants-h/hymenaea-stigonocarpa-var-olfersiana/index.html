Hymenaea Stigonocarpa Var. Olfersiana

Hymenaea stigonocarpa var. olfersiana is a rare and striking tree native to parts of South America. Known for its massive, buttressed roots and distinctive, flame-colored flowers, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This variant of the Hymenaea genus is particularly noted for its unique seed pods and medicinal properties. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively understudied species in the plant world.
