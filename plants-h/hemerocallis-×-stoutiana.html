Hemerocallis × stoutiana

Hemerocallis × stoutiana, commonly known as the Stoutiana Daylily, is a hybrid dayflower native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It has lance-shaped, dark green foliage and is known for its ability to bloom multiple times throughout the growing season.

Hemerocallis × stoutiana is a hardy perennial that is well-suited for gardens and landscapes due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
