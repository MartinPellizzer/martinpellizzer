Hymenocallis bolivariana

Hymenocallis bolivariana, commonly known as the Bolivian spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, funnel-shaped flowers. Hymenocallis bolivariana is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
