Hieracium sabaudum var. ovatifolium

Hieracium sabaudum var. ovatifolium, commonly known as the Sabaudian hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clustered heads, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
