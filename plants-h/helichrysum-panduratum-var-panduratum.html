Helichrysum panduratum var. panduratum

Helichrysum panduratum var. panduratum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has wiry, gray-green foliage and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

The species is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
