Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. bipolitanum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

bipolitanum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dandelion-like yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and road sides. It has finely divided, grass-like leaves and produces a large number of small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
