Hieracium juraniforme subsp. ortiganum

Hieracium juraniforme subsp.

ortiganum, commonly known as the Ortiga hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish at the base. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural areas.
