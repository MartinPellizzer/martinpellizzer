Herminium orbiculare

Herminium orbiculare, commonly known as the round-leaf twayblade, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial herb with a short, underground rhizome and a single, erect stem. The plant produces a single, rounded leaf that is often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and arranged in a loose spike, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

This plant is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
