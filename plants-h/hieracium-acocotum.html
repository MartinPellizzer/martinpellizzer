Hieracium acocotum

Hieracium acocotum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium acocotum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth habit and ability to colonize new areas quickly.
