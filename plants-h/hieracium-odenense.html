Hieracium odenense

Hieracium odenense, commonly known as the Oden's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium odenense is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its bright, cheerful blooms.
