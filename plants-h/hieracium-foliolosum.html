Hieracium foliolosum

Hieracium foliolosum, commonly known as the leafy hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features basal leaves that are lobed and hairy, with flowering stems that bear yellow ray flowers and numerous disc flowers. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is sometimes grown in gardens.
