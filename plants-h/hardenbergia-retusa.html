Hardenbergia retusa

Hardenbergia retusa, commonly known as the sweet pea bush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, with compound leaves and clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions of Australia.

Hardenbergia retusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
