Hieracium schustleri

Hieracium schustleri, commonly known as the Schuster's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellowish to reddish composite flowers. The plant typically has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Central Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
