Helichrysum bullatum

Helichrysum bullatum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even when dried. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers that are clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum bullatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in traditional medicine.
