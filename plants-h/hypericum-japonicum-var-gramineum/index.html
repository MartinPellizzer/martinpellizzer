Hypericum Japonicum Var. Gramineum

Hypericum japonicum var. gramineum is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and grass-like foliage. Native to Japan, this variety of hypericum has gained popularity among gardeners for its ornamental value and hardy nature. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is relatively easy to care for, making it a great choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. The plant's compact growth habit allows it to be used in rock gardens, borders, or as a ground cover. With its cheerful appearance and resilience, it adds a touch of brightness to any garden space.
