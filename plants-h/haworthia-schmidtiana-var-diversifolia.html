Haworthia schmidtiana var. diversifolia

Haworthia schmidtiana var. diversifolia, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct white or pale markings, resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
