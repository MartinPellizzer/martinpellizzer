Hieracium halfdanii

Hieracium halfdanii, commonly known as the Halfdan's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Hieracium halfdanii is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
