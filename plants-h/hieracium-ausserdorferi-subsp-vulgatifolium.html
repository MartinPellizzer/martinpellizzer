Hieracium ausserdorferi subsp. vulgatifolium

Hieracium ausserdorferi subsp.

vulgatifolium, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, rough leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has hairy stems and is often found in open habitats with moderate moisture.

It is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive growth.
