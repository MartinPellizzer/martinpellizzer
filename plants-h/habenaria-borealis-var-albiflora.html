Habenaria borealis var. albiflora

Habenaria borealis var. albiflora, commonly known as the northern marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to northern regions of North America.

It typically grows in wet, acidic habitats such as bogs and marshes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are white or pale pink, with a distinctive hooded lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks.

This orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other wetland flora.
