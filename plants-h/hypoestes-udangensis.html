Hypoestes udangensis

Hypoestes udangensis, commonly known as the red-veined stromanthe, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green veins. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
