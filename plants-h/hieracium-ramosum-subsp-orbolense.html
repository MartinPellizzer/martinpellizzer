Hieracium ramosum subsp. orbolense

Hieracium ramosum subsp.

orbolense, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in grassy habitats, meadows, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its flowering period occurs from late spring to early summer, and it is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves.

The species is often found in disturbed sites and is relatively hardy, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
