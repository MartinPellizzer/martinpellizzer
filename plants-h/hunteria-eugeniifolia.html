Hunteria eugeniifolia

Hunteria eugeniifolia, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small white flowers. The plant has smooth, grey-green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain a single seed.

Hunteria eugeniifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
