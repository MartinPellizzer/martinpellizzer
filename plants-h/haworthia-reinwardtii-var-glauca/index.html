Haworthia Reinwardtii Var. Glauca

Haworthia reinwardtii var. glauca is a compact, low-maintenance succulent native to southern Africa. Its unique appearance features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white to yellowish markings. This variety is prized for its striking visual appeal and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light. It requires minimal water and is ideal for indoor gardening. Its resilience and aesthetic value make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
