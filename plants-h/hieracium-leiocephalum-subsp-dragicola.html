Hieracium leiocephalum subsp. dragicola

Hieracium leiocephalum subsp.

dragicola, commonly known as the Dragicola hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering heads are composed of numerous yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disk florets.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in coastal and arid environments.
