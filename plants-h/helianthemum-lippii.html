Helianthemum lippii

Helianthemum lippii, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Helianthemum lippii is well adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
