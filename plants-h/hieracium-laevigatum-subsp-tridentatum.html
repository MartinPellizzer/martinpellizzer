Hieracium laevigatum subsp. tridentatum

Hieracium laevigatum subsp.

tridentatum, commonly known as the three-toothed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
