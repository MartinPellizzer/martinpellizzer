Hippeastrum heuserianum

Hippeastrum heuserianum, commonly known as the Easter lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers with white petals and a prominent yellow stamen, often blooming in late spring or early summer. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that grow from a bulbous base, and it thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and fragrance.

Hippeastrum heuserianum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
