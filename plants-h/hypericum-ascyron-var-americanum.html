Hypericum ascyron var. americanum

Hypericum ascyron var. americanum, commonly known as American St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green on top and whitish underneath. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in woodland edges or open fields.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
