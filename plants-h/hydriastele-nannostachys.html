Hydriastele nannostachys

Hydriastele nannostachys, commonly known as the small-stemmed hydriastele, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a terrestrial bromeliad characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that collect water, providing a habitat for small organisms. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in a spadix-like structure.

The species is noted for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
