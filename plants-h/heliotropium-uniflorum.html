Heliotropium uniflorum

Heliotropium uniflorum, commonly known as the one-flower heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with a distinctive violet hue. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Heliotropium uniflorum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or wetland habitats.
