Haworthia eilyae var. zantneriana

Haworthia eilyae var. zantneriana, commonly known as the Zantner's Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
