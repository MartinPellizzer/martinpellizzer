Hieracium pseudobifidum subsp. strungae

Hieracium pseudobifidum subsp.

strungae, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant typically has narrow, serrated leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in the summer. It thrives in grassy areas and can sometimes be found in disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
