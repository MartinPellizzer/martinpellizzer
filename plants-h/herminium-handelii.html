Herminium handelii

Herminium handelii, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces a single spike of flowers during the summer months.

Herminium handelii is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
