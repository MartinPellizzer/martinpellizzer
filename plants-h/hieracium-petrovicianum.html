Hieracium petrovicianum

Hieracium petrovicianum, commonly known as the Petrovich hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are basal, rough, and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

It is considered a weed in some regions due to its aggressive spreading habit.
