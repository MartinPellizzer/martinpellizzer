Haworthia arachnoidea var. joubertii

Haworthia arachnoidea var. joubertii, commonly known as the spider haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure and its distinctive, translucent, spider-like markings on the leaves. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on slender stems during the growing season.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
