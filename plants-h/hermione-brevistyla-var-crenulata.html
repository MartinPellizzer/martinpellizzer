Hermione brevistyla var. crenulata

Hermione brevistyla var. crenulata, commonly known as the curly-stemmed Hermione, is a unique variant of the Hermione brevistyla species.

It is characterized by its short, compact growth habit and distinctive, wavy-edged leaves. The plant typically features small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink to white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
