Haplolobus celebicus

Haplolobus celebicus, commonly known as the Celebes lily, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces a tall, slender stem that bears a single, large bloom. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
