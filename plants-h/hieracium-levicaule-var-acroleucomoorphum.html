Hieracium levicaule var. acroleucomoorphum

Hieracium levicaule var. acroleucomoorphum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single terminal capitulum. Its flowers are surrounded by phyllaries, which are leaf-like structures that protect the flower head.

The species is characterized by its slender stems and is often found in temperate climates.
