Hosta atropurpurea

Hosta atropurpurea, commonly known as the purpleleaf plantain, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a striking purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers on tall, slender stems in late summer. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
