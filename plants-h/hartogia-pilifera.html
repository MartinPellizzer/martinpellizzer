Hartogia pilifera

Hartogia pilifera, commonly known as the featherleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces yellow, dandelion-like flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. Hartogia pilifera is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

It is an important plant in local ecosystems, providing food for various insects and small animals.
