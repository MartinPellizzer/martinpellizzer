Hibiscus katangensis

Hibiscus katangensis, commonly known as the Katanga hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Central Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Hibiscus katangensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in swampy or riverine habitats.

It plays an important role in local ecosystems and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
