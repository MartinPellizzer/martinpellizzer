Huperzia bradeorum

Huperzia bradeorum, commonly known as the Brade's spikemoss, is a species of spikemoss belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is a small, evergreen herbaceous plant characterized by its creeping rhizomes and simple, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Huperzia bradeorum is notable for its ability to thrive in acidic soils and its role in maintaining soil stability in its native habitats.

Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are clustered in cone-like formations at the tips of its stems.
