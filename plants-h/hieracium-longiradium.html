Hieracium longiradium

Hieracium longiradium, commonly known as the long-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along riverbanks.
