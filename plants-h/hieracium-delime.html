Hieracium delime

Hieracium delime, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in dry conditions.

Hieracium delime is often found in open habitats and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
