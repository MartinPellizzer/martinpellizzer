Hieracium lachenalii subsp. bathylepium

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

bathylepium, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is a perennial herb with a rosette of basal leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers.

The plant has a hairy stem and leaves that are typically lance-shaped and serrated. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to cold, harsh environments.
