Habenaria mearnsii

Habenaria mearnsii, commonly known as the Mearns' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and wetlands. It has rhizomatous growth and produces showy, fragrant blooms during the spring and early summer.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
