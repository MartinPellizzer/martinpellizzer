Hieracium stellatifrons

Hieracium stellatifrons, commonly known as the star-faced hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like composite flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.

Hieracium stellatifrons is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
