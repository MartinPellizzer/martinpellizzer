Hieracium marchesettianum subsp. huljakianum

Hieracium marchesettianum subsp.

huljakianum, commonly known as the Huljakianum hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering stems that bear yellow composite flowers.
