Homalomena producta

Homalomena producta, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers with a pleasant fragrance, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect light, making it a popular houseplant.

Its ornamental value and ease of care contribute to its widespread cultivation.
