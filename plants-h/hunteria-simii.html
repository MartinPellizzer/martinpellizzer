Hunteria simii

Hunteria simii, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often brightly colored, ranging from yellow to orange, and are known for their intricate petal structure.

Hunteria simii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
