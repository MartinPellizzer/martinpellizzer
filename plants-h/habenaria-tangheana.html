Habenaria tangheana

Habenaria tangheana, commonly known as the Tanghean orchid, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has linear to lanceolate leaves and blooms in clusters of several flowers. Its flowers are usually pink or purple with a distinctive lip that is often marked with darker pigments.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
