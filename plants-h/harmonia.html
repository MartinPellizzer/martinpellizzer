Harmonia

Harmonia, commonly known as the harmonia orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and purple. The plant typically has strap-like leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Harmonia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
