Hieracium balbisianum subsp. schwimmerianum

Hieracium balbisianum subsp.

schwimmerianum, commonly known as Schwimmer's hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a single head.

This subspecies is noted for its limited distribution and is considered a rare plant in some regions.
