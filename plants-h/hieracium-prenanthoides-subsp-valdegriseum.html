Hieracium prenanthoides subsp. valdegriseum

Hieracium prenanthoides subsp.

valdegriseum, commonly known as the Valdegriseum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are usually basal and lance-shaped.
