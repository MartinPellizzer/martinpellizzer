Hederella brassii

Hederella brassii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Hederella brassii is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under optimal conditions.
