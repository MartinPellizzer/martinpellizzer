Hyptis besckeana

Hyptis besckeana, commonly known as the Brazilian hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its grayish-green leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hyptis besckeana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
