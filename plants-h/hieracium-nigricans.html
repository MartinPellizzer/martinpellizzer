Hieracium nigricans

Hieracium nigricans, commonly known as the black hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are usually hairy and lance-shaped. Hieracium nigricans is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators but can also be invasive in some regions.
