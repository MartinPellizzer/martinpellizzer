Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. subcinereum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

subcinereum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are typically lanceolate and covered with fine hairs. Its flowering heads are composite, consisting of yellow disc flowers surrounded by pale green to white ray florets.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaf arrangement.
