Hieracium krizsnae

Hieracium krizsnae, commonly known as the Krizsna's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-red flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically has basal and stem leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
