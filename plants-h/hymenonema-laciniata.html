Hymenonema laciniata

Hymenonema laciniata, commonly known as the lace-leaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely dissected leaves that give it a lace-like appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Hymenonema laciniata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
