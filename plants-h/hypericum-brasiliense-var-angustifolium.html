Hypericum brasiliense var. angustifolium

Hypericum brasiliense var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers with four petals. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

This plant is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
