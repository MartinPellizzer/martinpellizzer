Hoffmannia liesneriana

Hoffmannia liesneriana, commonly known as the Malabar hoffmannia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the monsoon season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture.

Hoffmannia liesneriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
