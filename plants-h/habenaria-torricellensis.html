Habenaria torricellensis

Habenaria torricellensis, commonly known as the Torricelli's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a greenish tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
