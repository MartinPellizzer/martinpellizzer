Hieracium laniceps

Hieracium laniceps, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dense, white to greyish hairs on its stems and leaves, giving it a woolly appearance. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a cluster at the top of the stem.

Hieracium laniceps is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it plays a role in supporting pollinators.
