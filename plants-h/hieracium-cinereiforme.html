Hieracium cinereiforme

Hieracium cinereiforme, commonly known as the ash-gray hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant features upright stems with hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Its scientific name reflects its ash-gray appearance, particularly in its leaves and stems.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
