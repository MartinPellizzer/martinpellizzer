Hieracium onosmoides subsp. crinigerum

Hieracium onosmoides subsp.

crinigerum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

The plant features yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, with both ray and disk florets. Its leaves are typically rough and hairy, contributing to its common name.

It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.
