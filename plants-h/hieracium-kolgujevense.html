Hieracium kolgujevense

Hieracium kolgujevense, commonly known as the Kolgujev sandweed, is a perennial herb native to the arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in cold climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.
