Hieracium savonicum

Hieracium savonicum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as fields and roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces numerous flowering stems with clustered flowers.

Hieracium savonicum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various regions.
