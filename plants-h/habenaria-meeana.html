Habenaria meeana

Habenaria meeana, commonly known as the Meeana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
