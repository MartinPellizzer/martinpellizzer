Hieracium kinrossense

Hieracium kinrossense, commonly known as the Kinross hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Scotland.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It has basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native habitat.
