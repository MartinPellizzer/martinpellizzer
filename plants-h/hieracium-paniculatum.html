Hieracium paniculatum

Hieracium paniculatum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are typically arranged in a panicle-like inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually alternate and lanceolate in shape. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, often becoming invasive.

Hieracium paniculatum thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, roadsides, and disturbed soils.
