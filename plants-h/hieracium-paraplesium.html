Hieracium paraplesium

Hieracium paraplesium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish to reddish flowers. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are typically lance-shaped with hairy surfaces. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, ditches, and along roadsides.

Hieracium paraplesium is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is considered a weed in some agricultural settings.
