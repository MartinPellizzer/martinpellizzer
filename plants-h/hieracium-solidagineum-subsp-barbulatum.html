Hieracium solidagineum subsp. barbulatum

Hieracium solidagineum subsp.

barbulatum, commonly known as the downy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
