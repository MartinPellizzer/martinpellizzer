Hieracium perlatescens

Hieracium perlatescens, commonly known as the silverleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy foliage. The plant features yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in summer, often arranged in clusters at the ends of stems. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Hieracium perlatescens is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in disturbed soils.
