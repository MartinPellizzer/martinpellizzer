Haplanthus laxiflorus var. laxiflorus

Haplanthus laxiflorus var. laxiflorus, commonly known as the slender-flowered haplanthus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers well-drained conditions. It is a perennial species that thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
