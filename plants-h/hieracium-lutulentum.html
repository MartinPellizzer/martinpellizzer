Hieracium lutulentum

Hieracium lutulentum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, often reddish stems. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has basal leaves that are broadly oval with toothed margins.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas, where it spreads readily through its rhizomes.
