Helianthemum glaucum var. longepedicellatum

Helianthemum glaucum var. longepedicellatum, commonly known as the long pedicel rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has silver-green foliage with a fuzzy texture, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.
