Hosta leptophylla

Hosta leptophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hosta, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery margin. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale blue during the summer months. It prefers partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta leptophylla is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often used in shaded garden settings.
