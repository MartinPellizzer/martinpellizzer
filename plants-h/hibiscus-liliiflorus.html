Hibiscus liliiflorus

Hibiscus liliiflorus, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a prominent yellow throat. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves and is typically found growing in moist, shaded areas. Hibiscus liliiflorus is an evergreen shrub that can reach heights of up to 6 feet.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Hawaiian culture.
