Hieracium eggenense

Hieracium eggenense, commonly known as the Eggenberg hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring sunny and well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets that are shorter than the central disc florets.

Hieracium eggenense is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
