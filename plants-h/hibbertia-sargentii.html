Hibbertia sargentii

Hibbertia sargentii, commonly known as the Sargent's hibbertia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal or heathland environments. The plant features showy yellow flowers with prominent red or orange markings, which attract pollinators like birds and insects. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, and it has a compact growth habit.

Hibbertia sargentii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
