Haworthia angustifolia var. baylissii

Haworthia angustifolia var. baylissii, commonly known as the Bayliss's Haworthia, is a compact, leafy succulent native to South Africa.

It features short, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique look and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
