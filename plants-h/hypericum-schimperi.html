Hypericum schimperi

Hypericum schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, typical of the Hypericaceae family. It thrives in rocky and mountainous regions, often growing at high altitudes.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of plant adaptations to arid and high-altitude environments.
