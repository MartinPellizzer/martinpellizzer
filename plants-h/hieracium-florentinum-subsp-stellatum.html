Hieracium florentinum subsp. stellatum

Hieracium florentinum subsp.

stellatum, commonly known as the star dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow ray flowers and a distinctive, globe-shaped receptacle.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and meadows. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

The species is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it often thrives in sunny, well-drained soils.
