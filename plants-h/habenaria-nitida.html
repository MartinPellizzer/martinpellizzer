Habenaria nitida

Habenaria nitida, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and white, often arranged in a spike, and are known for their delicate appearance.

Habenaria nitida is prized for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
