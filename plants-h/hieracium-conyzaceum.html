Hieracium conyzaceum

Hieracium conyzaceum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium conyzaceum is often considered a weed due to its rapid spread and ability to thrive in various environmental conditions.
