Haworthia caespitosa

Haworthia caespitosa, commonly known as the compact Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers on tall stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
