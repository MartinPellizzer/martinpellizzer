Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. sciagraptum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

sciagraptum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in open woodland or grassland habitats. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.
