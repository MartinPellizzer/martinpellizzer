Hieracium pietrae

Hieracium pietrae, commonly known as the rock hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and is often found growing on rocky soils and in open habitats. The plant produces yellow to orange composite flowers that are typically arranged in a single head. It has basal leaves that are lobed and covered in fine hairs, and its stems are usually hairy as well.

Hieracium pietrae is a perennial plant that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
