Hieracium rhacophyllum

Hieracium rhacophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium rhacophyllum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
