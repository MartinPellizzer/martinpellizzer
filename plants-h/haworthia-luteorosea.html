Haworthia luteorosea

Haworthia luteorosea, commonly known as the yellow rose haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a distinctive yellowish or pinkish tinge along the edges. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
