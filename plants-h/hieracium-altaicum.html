Hieracium altaicum

Hieracium altaicum, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and the leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy texture.

Hieracium altaicum is known for its aggressive growth and can become invasive in certain regions.
