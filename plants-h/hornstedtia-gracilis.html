Hornstedtia gracilis

Hornstedtia gracilis, commonly known as the slender hornstedtia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand saline and arid conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
