Hibiscus meyeri

Hibiscus meyeri, commonly known as the Meyer hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and tropical appearance.

Hibiscus meyeri prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
