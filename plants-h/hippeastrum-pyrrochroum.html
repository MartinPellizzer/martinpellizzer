Hippeastrum pyrrochroum

Hippeastrum pyrrochroum, commonly known as the red-veined amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically red or orange with contrasting white veins. The plant produces large, fleshy leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum pyrrochroum is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
