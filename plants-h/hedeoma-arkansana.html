Hedeoma arkansana

Hedeoma arkansana, commonly known as Arkansas hedeoma, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent. Its small, white flowers bloom in summer, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Hedeoma arkansana is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
