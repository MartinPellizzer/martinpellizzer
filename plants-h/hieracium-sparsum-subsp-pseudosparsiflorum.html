Hieracium sparsum subsp. pseudosparsiflorum

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

pseudosparsiflorum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and are produced in late spring to early summer.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
