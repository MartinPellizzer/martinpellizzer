Helianthemum cinereum var. rubellum

Helianthemum cinereum var. rubellum, commonly known as the crimson stonecrop, is a low-growing evergreen succulent native to southern Europe.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically red or pinkish in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
