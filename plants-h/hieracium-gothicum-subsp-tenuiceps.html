Hieracium gothicum subsp. tenuiceps

Hieracium gothicum subsp.

tenuiceps, commonly known as the slender-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 50 centimeters.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear a single, showy yellow flower head composed of numerous small florets.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender peduncles and relatively small, compact flower heads compared to other hawkweed species.
