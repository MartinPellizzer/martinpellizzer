Helicostylis podogyne

Helicostylis podogyne, commonly known as the red-flowered helicostylis, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking red spadix and a broad, heart-shaped spathe. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of small, inconspicuous flowers clustered around the spadix.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
