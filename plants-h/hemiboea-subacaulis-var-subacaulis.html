Hemiboea subacaulis var. subacaulis

Hemiboea subacaulis var. subacaulis, commonly known as the slender hemiboea, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or undergrowth. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with five petals and a distinctive spurred structure.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in semi-shaded conditions.
