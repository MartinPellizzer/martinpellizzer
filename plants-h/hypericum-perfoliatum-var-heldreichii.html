Hypericum perfoliatum var. heldreichii

Hypericum perfoliatum var. heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its stems are woody and can become somewhat branched as the plant matures.

This variety is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is well adapted to sunny, open habitats.
