Hibbertia Bracteata

Hibbertia bracteata is a striking member of the Dilleniaceae family native to Australia. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique leaf structure, this plant captivates botanists and gardeners alike. It thrives in coastal regions and sandy soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant's ecological importance includes supporting local pollinators and contributing to biodiversity. Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after addition to native gardens and conservation efforts.
