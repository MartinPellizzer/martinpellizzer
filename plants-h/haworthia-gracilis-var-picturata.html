Haworthia gracilis var. picturata

Haworthia gracilis var. picturata, commonly known as the picture window Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features translucent, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or pink at the tips, giving it a striking visual appearance. The leaves are adorned with intricate patterns of white and green markings, resembling a window or a picture frame. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

This plant is prized for its unique aesthetics and low maintenance requirements.
