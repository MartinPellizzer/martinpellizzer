Helicia pallescens

Helicia pallescens, commonly known as the white-flowered helicia, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Theaceae, which includes plants like camellias and tea. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy on top and pale underneath. The fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.
