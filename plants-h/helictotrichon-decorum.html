Helictotrichon decorum

Helictotrichon decorum, commonly known as the bluegrass or fine leaf bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green blades and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and prairies. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Its flowers are small, spike-like clusters that produce light blue to purple seeds.
