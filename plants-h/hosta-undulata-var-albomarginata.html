Hosta undulata var. albomarginata

Hosta undulata var. albomarginata, commonly known as the white-edged wavyleaf plant, is a cultivar of the hosta genus native to Asia.

It features broad, wavy leaves that are typically a deep green with a distinctive white or cream-colored margin. The plant thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

It is valued in gardens for its unique leaf patterns and ability to add texture to shaded landscapes.
