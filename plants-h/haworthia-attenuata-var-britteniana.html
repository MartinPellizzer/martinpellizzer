Haworthia attenuata var. britteniana

Haworthia attenuata var. britteniana, commonly known as the Britten's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features short, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette, often with a bluish-green hue and white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
