Hypericum kalmii

Hypericum kalmii, commonly known as Kalm's St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and opposite, oval-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and can reach a height of up to 1.5 meters. It produces small, red-orange berries that are an important food source for birds and small mammals.

Hypericum kalmii is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in herbal medicine.
