Hippeastrum colonum

Hippeastrum colonum, commonly known as the Colombian amaryllis, is a species native to South America, particularly Colombia.

It is a bulbous plant that produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from the base of the bulb. Its flowers typically bloom in late spring or early summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens.

Hippeastrum colonum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
