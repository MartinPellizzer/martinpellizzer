Hieracium trichellum

Hieracium trichellum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers with ray florets. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

This plant is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
