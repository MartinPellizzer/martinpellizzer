Hieracium scardicum subsp. hypopannosum

Hieracium scardicum subsp.

hypopannosum, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species in certain regions.
