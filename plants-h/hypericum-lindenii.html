Hypericum lindenii

Hypericum lindenii, commonly known as the Lindley's St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. Hypericum lindenii is often found in moist, open habitats such as woodland edges and riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the Hypericaceae family.
