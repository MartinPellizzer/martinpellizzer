Hieracium pallescens subsp. jugicola

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

jugicola, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, preferring well-drained conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine pubescence, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is a member of the genus Hieracium, which includes many species of hawkweeds known for their ecological significance in various habitats.
