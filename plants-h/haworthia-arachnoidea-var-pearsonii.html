Haworthia arachnoidea var. pearsonii

Haworthia arachnoidea var. pearsonii, commonly known as the spider plant, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating, giving it a unique appearance. The leaves are typically bluish-green with white markings and can grow up to 15 centimeters in length. It produces small, white or pink flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
