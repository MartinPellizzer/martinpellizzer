Hyparrhenia Modica

Hyparrhenia modica is a hardy grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and withstand drought. This plant is often used for erosion control and as a forage crop for livestock. Its dense growth habit makes it an effective ground cover. Despite its simplicity, Hyparrhenia modica plays a vital role in sustaining ecosystems and supporting biodiversity.
