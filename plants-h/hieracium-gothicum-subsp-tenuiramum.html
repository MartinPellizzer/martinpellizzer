Hieracium gothicum subsp. tenuiramum

Hieracium gothicum subsp.

tenuiramum, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and road sides. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and can be slightly serrated.

This subspecies is distinguished by its relatively slender stems and smaller flower heads compared to other hawkweed species.
