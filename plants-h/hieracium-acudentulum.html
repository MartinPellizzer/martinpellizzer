Hieracium acudentulum

Hieracium acudentulum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense mats due to its rhizomatous growth habit. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with prominent central disc florets.

Hieracium acudentulum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
