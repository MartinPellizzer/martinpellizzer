Hibiscus campylosiphon

Hibiscus campylosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in warm, sunny environments. Hibiscus campylosiphon is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its blooming season typically occurs during the summer months.
