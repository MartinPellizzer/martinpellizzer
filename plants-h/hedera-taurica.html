Hedera taurica

Hedera taurica, commonly known as the Crimean ivy, is a species of climbing plant native to the Crimean Peninsula.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and dark green, lobed leaves. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a decorative, evergreen climber.
