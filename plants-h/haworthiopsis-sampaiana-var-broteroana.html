Haworthiopsis sampaiana var. broteroana

Haworthiopsis sampaiana var. broteroana, commonly known as the Brotero's Haworthiopsis, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white to yellowish markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
