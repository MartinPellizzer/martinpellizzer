Hieracium bifidum subsp. bifidipraecox

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

bifidipraecox, commonly known as the early bifid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its erect stems and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typically blooming from late spring to early summer. It prefers moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks.

Its subspecies status reflects subtle morphological differences from other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
