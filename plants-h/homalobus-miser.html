Homalobus miser

Homalobus miser, commonly known as the weeping bellflower, is a member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its drooping, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture.
