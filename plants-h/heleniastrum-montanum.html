Heleniastrum montanum

Heleniastrum montanum, commonly known as mountain helene, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils at high elevations. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit adapted to harsh alpine conditions.
