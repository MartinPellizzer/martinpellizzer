Hieracium murorum subsp. euthysanum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

euthysanum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves with rough surfaces and produces seeds in small, dry fruits.

This subspecies is often found in temperate regions across Europe and parts of Asia.
