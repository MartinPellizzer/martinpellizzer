Hieracium vulgatum proles diaphanum

Hieracium vulgatum proles diaphanum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and open woodlands. Its translucent, waxy leaves are a distinguishing feature, giving it a unique appearance.

It is typically not cultivated for ornamental purposes but is occasionally found in wildflower gardens.
