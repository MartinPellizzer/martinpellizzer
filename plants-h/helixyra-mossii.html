Helixyra mossii

Helixyra mossii, commonly known as the spiral moss, is a unique species of moss characterized by its intricate, spiral-shaped growth patterns.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors or along rocky outcrops. The plant's structure consists of tightly coiled stems that form a dense, cushion-like mat. Its leaves are small, ovate, and covered in a layer of fine, translucent cells that aid in water retention.

Helixyra mossii plays a vital role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for microorganisms in its native ecosystems.
