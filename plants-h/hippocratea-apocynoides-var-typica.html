Hippocratea apocynoides var. typica

Hippocratea apocynoides var. typica, commonly known as the typical hippocratea, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae, which includes many milkweed species. The plant is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It produces a milky sap that can be toxic, a common trait among members of its family.
