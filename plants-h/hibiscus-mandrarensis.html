Hibiscus mandrarensis

Hibiscus mandrarensis, commonly known as the Madagascar hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Hibiscus mandrarensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
