Hieracium rigidum

Hieracium rigidum, commonly known as rigid hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant has erect, hairy stems and basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and appear in late spring to early summer, followed by the production of small, oval seeds.

Hieracium rigidum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
