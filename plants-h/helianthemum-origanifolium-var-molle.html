Helianthemum origanifolium var. molle

Helianthemum origanifolium var. molle, commonly known as the soft thyme-helianthemum, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has aromatic, woolly leaves that are closely clustered, giving it a compact appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both horticultural and ecological settings.
