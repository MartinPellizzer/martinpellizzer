Herschelianthe chimanimaniensis

Herschelianthe chimanimaniensis, commonly known as the Chimanimani daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Chimanimani Mountains in eastern Zimbabwe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its compact growth habit and is often found in open grassland habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
