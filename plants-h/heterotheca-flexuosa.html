Heterotheca flexuosa

Heterotheca flexuosa, commonly known as the curlycup gumweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, fuzzy leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. Its leaves are typically curled or folded, giving it its common name.
