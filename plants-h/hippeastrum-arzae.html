Hippeastrum arzae

Hippeastrum arzae, commonly known as the Colombian tiger flower, is a striking bulbous plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns resembling a tiger's stripes. The plant features strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that emerge from a bulbous base and are typically evergreen. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum arzae is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
