Hieracium murorum subsp. oligolepium

Hieracium murorum subsp.

oligolepium, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright stems and clustered, yellowish to reddish flower heads.

The leaves are typically basal, with a rough texture and a distinctive heart-shaped base. It prefers moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

This subspecies is notable for its small, leathery leaves and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
