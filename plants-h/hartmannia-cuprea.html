Hartmannia cuprea

Hartmannia cuprea, commonly known as the copper orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking copper-colored petals and is often found growing in shaded, humid environments. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters.

Hartmannia cuprea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique coloration.
