Hypoderris seemannii

Hypoderris seemannii, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, which attract pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adapted to well-drained soils.

The leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
