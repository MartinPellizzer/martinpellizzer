Hieracium glaucinum subsp. badalii

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

badalii, commonly known as the Badalii hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. It has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in mountainous regions.
