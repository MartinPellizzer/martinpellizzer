Houttuynia cordata f. viridis

Houttuynia cordata f. viridis, commonly known as the variegated snake plant, is a hybrid form of the common houttuynia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive yellow and white variegation. This plant is native to East Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.

The variegation in its leaves is due to genetic factors that affect chlorophyll distribution.
