Hemerocallis fulva var. kwanso

Hemerocallis fulva var. kwanso, commonly known as the orange daylily, is a variant of the daylily species native to Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically bright green and slightly waxy in texture. It is a perennial herbaceous plant that thrives in full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its showy blooms and hardy nature, making it a popular choice in gardens.
