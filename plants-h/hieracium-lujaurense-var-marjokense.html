Hieracium lujaurense var. marjokense

Hieracium lujaurense var. marjokense, commonly known as the Lujauran hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous root system. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces yellowish to reddish flower heads composed of numerous small florets.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with rough textures and are covered in fine hairs.
