Haworthia breueri

Haworthia breueri, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
