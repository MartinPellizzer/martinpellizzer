Hippocrepis comosa var. lignescens

Hippocrepis comosa var. lignescens, commonly known as the shrub violets, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its woody stems and clustered, violet-colored flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
