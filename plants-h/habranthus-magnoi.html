Habranthus magnoi

Habranthus magnoi, commonly known as the giant spider lily, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with prominent, curling petals. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
