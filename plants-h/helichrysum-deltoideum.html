Helichrysum deltoideum

Helichrysum deltoideum, commonly known as the deltoid everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its production of small, yellow to orange-colored flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is known for its long-lasting, dried flowers, which are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
