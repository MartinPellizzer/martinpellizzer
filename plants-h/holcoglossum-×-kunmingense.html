Holcoglossum × kunmingense

Holcoglossum × kunmingense, commonly known as the Kunming hybrid vanda, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found on trees in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant produces striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are showy, with a complex structure featuring multiple petals and sepals, often in shades of white and yellow.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique floral display.
