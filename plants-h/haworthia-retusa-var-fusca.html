Haworthia retusa var. fusca

Haworthia retusa var. fusca, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy, grass-like leaves with distinctive dark green stripes and a pale green center. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
