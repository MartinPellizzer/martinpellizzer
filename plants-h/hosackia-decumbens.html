Hosackia decumbens

Hosackia decumbens, commonly known as the prairie clover, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open habitats such as prairies and grasslands. The plant features small, pea-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the species. Its leaves are alternate and compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.
