Hedyotis fruticulosa var. atroglomerata

Hedyotis fruticulosa var. atroglomerata, commonly known as blackstem jewelweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems that often display a dark purplish or blackish hue. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

This variant is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks.
