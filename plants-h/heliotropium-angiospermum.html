Heliotropium angiospermum

Heliotropium angiospermum, commonly known as the tropical heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, violet to purple flowers that bloom in dense clusters at the ends of its stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Heliotropium angiospermum is known for its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
