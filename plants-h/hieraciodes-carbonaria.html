Hieraciodes carbonaria

Hieraciodes carbonaria, commonly known as the black thistle.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. The plant features a robust stem that is often covered in coarse, prickly hairs. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in a dense layer of white, silky hairs.

The flowers are typically purple or violet, arranged in a dense, spherical cluster.
