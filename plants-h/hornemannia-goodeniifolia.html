Hornemannia goodeniifolia

Hornemannia goodeniifolia, commonly known as the Goodeniopsis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink and purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
