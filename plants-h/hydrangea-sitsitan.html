Hydrangea sitsitan

Hydrangea sitsitan, commonly known as the mountain hydrangea, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink and blue, depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a glossy green surface and serrated edges.

Hydrangea sitsitan thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
