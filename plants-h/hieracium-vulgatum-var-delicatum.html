Hieracium vulgatum var. delicatum

Hieracium vulgatum var. delicatum, commonly known as the slender wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are heart-shaped and often have a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

This variant is distinguished by its more delicate foliage compared to other varieties within the Hieracium genus.
