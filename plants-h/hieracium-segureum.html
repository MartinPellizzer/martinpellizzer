Hieracium segureum

Hieracium segureum, commonly known as the Segureum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium segureum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to spread rapidly.
