Hieracium czywczynae

Hieracium czywczynae, commonly known as the Czywczyn Hill Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has finely divided, linear leaves and produces small, dandelion-like flowers that bloom in late summer.
