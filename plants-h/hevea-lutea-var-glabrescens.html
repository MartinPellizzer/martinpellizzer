Hevea lutea var. glabrescens

Hevea lutea var. glabrescens, commonly known as the yellow rubber tree, is a variant of the rubber tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and yellowish-green leaves. The plant is known for its latex production, which is used in the rubber industry. It typically grows in tropical climates with high humidity and warm temperatures.

This variety is often cultivated for both its economic value and ornamental appeal.
