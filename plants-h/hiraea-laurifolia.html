Hiraea laurifolia

Hiraea laurifolia, commonly known as the glossy laurel, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Hiraea laurifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
