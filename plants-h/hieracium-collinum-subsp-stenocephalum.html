Hieracium collinum subsp. stenocephalum

Hieracium collinum subsp.

stenocephalum, commonly known as the narrow-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features upright stems with basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often have hairy surfaces. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, characteristic of the Asteraceae family.

It has a taproot system and is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
