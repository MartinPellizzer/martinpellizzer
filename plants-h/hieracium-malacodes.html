Hieracium malacodes

Hieracium malacodes, commonly known as the black knapweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Hieracium malacodes is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
