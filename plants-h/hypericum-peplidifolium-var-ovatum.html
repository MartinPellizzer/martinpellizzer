Hypericum peplidifolium var. ovatum

Hypericum peplidifolium var. ovatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, branched stems covered in small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
