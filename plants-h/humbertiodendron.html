Humbertiodendron

Humbertiodendron, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae.

The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces long, slender pods that contain seeds. Humbertiodendron thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and visually striking addition to gardens and landscapes.
