Habenaria austrosinensis

Habenaria austrosinensis, commonly known as the southern China orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich substrates. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip, characteristic of the Orchidaceae family. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated for horticultural purposes.
