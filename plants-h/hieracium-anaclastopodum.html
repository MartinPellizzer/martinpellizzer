Hieracium anaclastopodum

Hieracium anaclastopodum, commonly known as the broken stem hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. It has a weak stem that tends to break easily, giving it its common name.

The species is generally short-lived, with a lifespan of one to two years.
