Hieracium scorzonerifolium

Hieracium scorzonerifolium, commonly known as the Scorzonerifolium hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it somewhat invasive in certain regions.
