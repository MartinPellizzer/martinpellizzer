Hieracium longifolium var. mougeotii

Hieracium longifolium var. mougeotii, commonly known as the longleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are elongated and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and borne in a single terminal head, characteristic of the Asteraceae family.

It is often found in sandy or gravelly soils and is relatively drought-tolerant.
