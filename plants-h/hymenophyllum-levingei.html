Hymenophyllum levingei

Hymenophyllum levingei, commonly known as the silver moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery appearance and bright green to silvery-gray foliage. This epiphytic plant typically grows on rocks, tree trunks, or soil in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a spiral, with a distinct midrib.

Hymenophyllum levingei is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it contributes to the biodiversity of forest ecosystems.
