Hyoscyamus betifolius

Hyoscyamus betifolius, commonly known as the betle leaf hyoscyamus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and hairy leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, followed by dark, spherical berries. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported medicinal properties.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
