Hieracium subuliceps

Hieracium subuliceps, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to orange flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

Hieracium subuliceps is typically perennial and has a fibrous root system that helps it thrive in various environmental conditions.
