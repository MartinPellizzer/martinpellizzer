Hieracium alpinum var. foliosum

Hieracium alpinum var. foliosum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow flower heads that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has small, hairy leaves and is part of the Asteraceae family.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often found in mountainous habitats where other plants struggle to survive.
