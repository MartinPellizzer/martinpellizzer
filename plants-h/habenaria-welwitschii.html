Habenaria welwitschii

Habenaria welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant features linear to lanceolate leaves and has a complex inflorescence with multiple flowers arranged in a raceme. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker and more intricate.

Habenaria welwitschii is adapted to grow in sandy or rocky soils and requires well-drained conditions for optimal growth.
