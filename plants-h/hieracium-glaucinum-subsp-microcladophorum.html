Hieracium glaucinum subsp. microcladophorum

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

microcladophorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered yellow flowers.

The leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, whitish down, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

This subspecies is often found in temperate regions of Europe and is adapted to a variety of soil types.
