Hakea nervosa

Hakea nervosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bottle-shaped, woody fruits that remain on the plant for several years. It is adapted to arid conditions and has deep root systems to access water.

Hakea nervosa is valued in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry environments.
