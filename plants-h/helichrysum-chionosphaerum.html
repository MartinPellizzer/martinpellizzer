Helichrysum chionosphaerum

Helichrysum chionosphaerum, commonly known as the snowball helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, yellow to golden flowers that resemble a snowball, hence its common name. The plant has narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum chionosphaerum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
