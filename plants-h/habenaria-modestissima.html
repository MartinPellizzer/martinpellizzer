Habenaria modestissima

Habenaria modestissima, commonly known as the modest lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. The plant features a slender stem with numerous small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in a loose, spiral pattern. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often emerging from a rhizome.

It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in humid, temperate climates.
