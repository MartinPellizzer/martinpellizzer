Hieracium umbrosum subsp. vippetinum

Hieracium umbrosum subsp.

vippetinum, commonly known as the shadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and meadows.

The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to lanceolate in shape, with hairy surfaces. Its flowers are yellow and borne in loose clusters, characteristic of the hawkweed genus.

The subspecies is distinguished by its specific leaf morphology and habitat preferences.
