Huernia guttata

Huernia guttata, commonly known as the guttate huernia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
