Helicia teysmanniana

Helicia teysmanniana, commonly known as the Malayan holly or teysmann's holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia, where it thrives in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with dark green leaves that are leathery in texture. Helicia teysmanniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
