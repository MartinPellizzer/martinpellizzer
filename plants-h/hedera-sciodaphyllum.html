Hedera sciodaphyllum

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. This climbing plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. It produces small, white flowers followed by orange-red berries that are toxic to humans and animals.
