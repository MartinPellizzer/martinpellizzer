Habenaria sprucei

Habenaria sprucei, commonly known as the Spruce's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Habenaria sprucei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
