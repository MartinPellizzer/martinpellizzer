Hieracium curtipedunculum

Hieracium curtipedunculum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

Hieracium curtipedunculum is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable habitats.
