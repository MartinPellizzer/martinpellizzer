Helichrysum lineatum

Helichrysum lineatum, commonly known as the everlasting or crown of thorns, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, yellowish-orange flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature. The plant has silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, and its leaves are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum lineatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various climates.
