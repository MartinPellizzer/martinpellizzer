Hallieracantha lanceolata

Hallieracantha lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in dense heads. The plant has linear, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hallieracantha lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in xeriscaped gardens.
