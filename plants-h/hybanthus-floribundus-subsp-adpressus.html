Hybanthus floribundus subsp. adpressus

Hybanthus floribundus subsp.

adpressus, commonly known as the dense-flowered hybanthus, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
