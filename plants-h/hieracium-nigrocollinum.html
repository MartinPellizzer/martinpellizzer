Hieracium nigrocollinum

Hieracium nigrocollinum, commonly known as the black-hooded hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers with a distinctive blackish or dark brown involucral bracts. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
