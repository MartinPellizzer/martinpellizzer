Hieracium juncinescens

Hieracium juncinescens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and wetlands. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single, elongated flower head with ray florets.

Hieracium juncinescens is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its resilience to environmental stress.
