Hieracium epicrocifolium

Hieracium epicrocifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Hieracium epicrocifolium is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate climates.
