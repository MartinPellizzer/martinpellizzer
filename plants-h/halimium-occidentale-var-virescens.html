Halimium occidentale var. virescens

Halimium occidentale var. virescens, commonly known as the green-flowered honesty, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, smooth seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its foliage is generally gray-green, with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid conditions.
