Hieracium atratum subsp. subellipticum

Hieracium atratum subsp.

subellipticum, commonly known as the dark hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal, aiding in its propagation.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and botanical gardens.
