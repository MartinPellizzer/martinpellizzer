Huernia keniensis var. grandiflora

Huernia keniensis var. grandiflora, commonly known as the Kenyan horned cactus, is a succulent native to Kenya.

It features a branching stem covered in tubercles, with spines and fleshy, ribbed structures that help store water. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
