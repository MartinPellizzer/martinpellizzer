Helianthemum × kerneri

Helianthemum × kerneri, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dense, evergreen foliage with small, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wool. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
