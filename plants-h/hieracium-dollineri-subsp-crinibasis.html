Hieracium dollineri subsp. crinibasis

Hieracium dollineri subsp.

crinibasis, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are often hairy and have a distinctive basal rosette structure.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
