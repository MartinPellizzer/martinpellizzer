Hieracium chlorops

Hieracium chlorops, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and disturbed soils. The plant has a creeping growth habit with a central stem that branches out.

It is often found in moist environments and is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes.
