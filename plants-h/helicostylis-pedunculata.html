Helicostylis pedunculata

Helicostylis pedunculata, commonly known as the red-flowered helicostylis, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually appealing species. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Helicostylis pedunculata is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
