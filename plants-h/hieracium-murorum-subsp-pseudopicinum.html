Hieracium murorum subsp. pseudopicinum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

pseudopicinum, commonly known as the false picrin. It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

The plant features basal leaves that are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in a loose head, characteristic of the dandelion family.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
