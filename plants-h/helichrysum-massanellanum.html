Helichrysum massanellanum

Helichrysum massanellanum, commonly known as the Cape gold flower, is a South African native plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, golden-yellow flower heads that resemble small sunflowers, which are composed of many tiny tubular florets. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Helichrysum massanellanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
