Hatiora salicornoides var. villigera

Hatiora salicornoides var. villigera, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a hybrid plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often adorned with vibrant flowers. The plant typically produces bright red, orange, or yellow blooms during the holiday season, which is why it is popular as a festive decoration. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its unique appearance and seasonal flowering, it is a favored ornamental plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
