Hymenocardia mollis var. glabra

Hymenocardia mollis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth hymenocardia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves that are smooth and glossy. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually yellow or white in color.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
