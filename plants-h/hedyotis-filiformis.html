Hedyotis filiformis

Hedyotis filiformis, commonly known as slender mistflower, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features small, white or greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Hedyotis filiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
