Hebe cheesemanii

Hebe cheesemanii, commonly known as the Cheeseman's hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces showy spikes of blue to violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
