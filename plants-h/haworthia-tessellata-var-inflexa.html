Haworthia tessellata var. inflexa

Haworthia tessellata var. inflexa, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or light-colored markings. The leaves are fleshy and thick, adapted to store water in arid environments. It produces small, pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
