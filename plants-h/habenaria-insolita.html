Habenaria insolita

Habenaria insolita, commonly known as the solitary orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a slender, upright stem and a single, showy flower. The plant has a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces a delicate, greenish-yellow flower with a distinct lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its unique morphology and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
