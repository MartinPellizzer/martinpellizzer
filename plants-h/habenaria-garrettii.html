Habenaria garrettii

Habenaria garrettii, commonly known as the Garrett's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces erect, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with dark veins.

Habenaria garrettii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
