Hieracium elaticaule

Hieracium elaticaule, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium elaticaule is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is considered a weed in some agricultural settings.
