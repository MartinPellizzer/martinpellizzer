Hypericum elenorae

Hypericum elenorae, commonly known as the Elenora's St. John's Wort, is a species of flowering plant in the family Hypericaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its erect stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
