Hieracium tjumentzevii

Hieracium tjumentzevii, commonly known as the Tjumentzev's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange flower heads, which are composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in rocky and well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium tjumentzevii is a perennial species, often found in mountainous areas of Central Asia.
