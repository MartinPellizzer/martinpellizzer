Hieracium molliusculum

Hieracium molliusculum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and basal rosettes of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium molliusculum is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
