Helianthemum Apenninum Subsp. Masguindalii

Helianthemum apenninum subsp. masguindalii is a rare and intriguing plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the rockrose family, known for its hardy and drought-resistant characteristics. This subspecies is particularly adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in harsh conditions. Its small yellow flowers bloom in early spring, adding a touch of color to its natural habitat. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
