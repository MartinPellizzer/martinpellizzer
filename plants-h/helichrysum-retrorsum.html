Helichrysum retrorsum

Helichrysum retrorsum, commonly known as the golden everlasting or silver frost, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic, resinous scent. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Helichrysum retrorsum is also prized for its ability to retain color when dried, making it a popular choice for floral arrangements.
