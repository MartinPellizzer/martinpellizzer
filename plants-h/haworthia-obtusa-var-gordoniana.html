Haworthia obtusa var. gordoniana

Haworthia obtusa var. gordoniana, commonly known as the Gordon's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings, which help it store water and reflect sunlight. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall, erect stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
