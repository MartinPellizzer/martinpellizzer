Hebe colensoi

Hebe colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's hebe, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in spring. Hebe colensoi thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its evergreen leaves and long flowering period.
