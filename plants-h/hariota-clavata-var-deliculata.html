Hariota clavata var. deliculata

Hariota clavata var. deliculata, commonly known as the delicate clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
