Hieracium petrarchanum

Hieracium petrarchanum, commonly known as Petrarch's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are rough and hairy, with a distinctive lobed shape, and it produces yellow flowers in summer.

Petrarch's hawkweed is often found in alpine and subalpine regions, where it thrives in cooler climates.
