Habenaria cumingii

Habenaria cumingii, commonly known as the Cuming's butterfly orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. The plant produces elongated pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often borne on thin, erect stems and are pollinated by specific insect species.

Habenaria cumingii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
