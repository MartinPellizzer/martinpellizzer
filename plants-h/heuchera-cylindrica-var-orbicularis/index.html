Heuchera Cylindrica Var. Orbicularis

Heuchera cylindrica var. orbicularis is a striking ornamental plant known for its vibrant foliage and compact growth habit. This variety of heuchera features rounded, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance beauty. The plant's unique leaf shape and color variations add visual interest to any landscape. Its versatility and aesthetic appeal have made it a favorite in both residential and commercial gardening settings.
