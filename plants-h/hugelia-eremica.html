Hugelia eremica

Hugelia eremica, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.

Hugelia eremica is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
