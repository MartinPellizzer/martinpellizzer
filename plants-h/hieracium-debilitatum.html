Hieracium debilitatum

Hieracium debilitatum, commonly known as the weak hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. It is characterized by its weak, sprawling growth habit and is often found in disturbed soils.

Hieracium debilitatum is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.
