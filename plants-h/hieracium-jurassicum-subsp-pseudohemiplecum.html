Hieracium jurassicum subsp. pseudohemiplecum

Hieracium jurassicum subsp.

pseudohemiplecum, commonly known as the Jurassic hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers appear in late summer.
