Hieracium doliariceps

Hieracium doliariceps, commonly known as the globe hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its most distinctive feature is the spherical, yellowish flower head that resembles a small globe.

Hieracium doliariceps is often found in disturbed habitats and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
