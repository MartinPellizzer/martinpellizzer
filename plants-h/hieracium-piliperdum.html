Hieracium piliperdum

Hieracium piliperdum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its bright yellow flowers consist of both ray and disk florets, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Hieracium piliperdum is often found in open habitats and can sometimes form dense colonies.
