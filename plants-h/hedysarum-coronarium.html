Hedysarum coronarium

Hedysarum coronarium, commonly known as crown vetch, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant has a robust root system that helps prevent soil erosion, making it a popular choice for landscaping and ecological restoration. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Hedysarum coronarium is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
