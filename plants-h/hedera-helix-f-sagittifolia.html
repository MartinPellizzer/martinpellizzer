Hedera helix f. sagittifolia

Hedera helix f. sagittifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf ivy, is a compact, trailing evergreen vine characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves.

It is a cultivar of the common ivy, Hedera helix, and is prized for its ornamental foliage. The leaves are typically a vibrant green, with a slightly glossy texture and a narrow, pointed shape. It thrives in shady to partially shaded conditions and is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and adaptable to various soil types, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
