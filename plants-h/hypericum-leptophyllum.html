Hypericum leptophyllum

Hypericum leptophyllum, commonly known as the slender St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, usually from July to August. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum leptophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
