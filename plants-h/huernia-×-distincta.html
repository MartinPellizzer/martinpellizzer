Huernia × distincta

Huernia × distincta, commonly known as the hybrid moon cactus, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Huernia truncata and Huernia muscosa.

It is characterized by its unique, fleshy, ribbed stems that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or yellow. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is a succulent, adapted to arid environments, with thick, water-storing tissues.

This hybrid is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
