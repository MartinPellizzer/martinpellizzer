Hieracium isatifolium subsp. orthophyllum

Hieracium isatifolium subsp.

orthophyllum, commonly known as the orthophyllum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, exhibiting a rosette of basal leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while the stems are hairy.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
