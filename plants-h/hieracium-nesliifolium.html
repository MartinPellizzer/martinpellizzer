Hieracium nesliifolium

Hieracium nesliifolium, commonly known as the Turkish dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Turkey and the Balkans, and it thrives in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are hairy and alternate, with a distinctive serrated edge.

Hieracium nesliifolium is often found in open habitats and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
