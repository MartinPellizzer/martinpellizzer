Heynea velutina

Heynea velutina, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark-brown bark and large, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing smooth, oval seeds. The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soils.
