Hieracium bifidum subsp. semiprolixum

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

semiprolixum, commonly known as the slender spearthistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features upright stems with rough, hairy surfaces and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often lobed. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a dense, globular head, and are typically visited by various pollinators.

This subspecies is distinguished by its shorter, more slender leaves compared to other members of the Hieracium genus.
