Habenaria miersiana var. yunnanensis

Habenaria miersiana var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, butterfly-shaped flowers in shades of white and purple. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, and its flowers are often fragrant. It prefers well-drained, humus-rich soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral structure.
