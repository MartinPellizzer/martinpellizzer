Hoya loyceandrewsiana

Hoya loyceandrewsiana, commonly known as the Loyce Andrews Hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of star-shaped, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental value.

Hoya loyceandrewsiana thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
