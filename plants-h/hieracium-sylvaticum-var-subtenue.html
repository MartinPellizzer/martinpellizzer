Hieracium sylvaticum var. subtenue

Hieracium sylvaticum var. subtenue, commonly known as the slender wood hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while the flowers are yellow with ray florets.
