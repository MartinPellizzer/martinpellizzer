Hypoestes forskaolii

Hypoestes forskaolii, commonly known as the "Marvelous Blue" or "Carnival Plant," is a flowering shrub native to Madagascar.

It features striking, colorful, and often variegated leaves that display shades of red, orange, and white, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a favorite among gardeners seeking visual interest in both indoor and outdoor settings.
