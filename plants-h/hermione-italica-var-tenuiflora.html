Hermione italica var. tenuiflora

Hermione italica var. tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered Hermione, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features delicate, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue to violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a compact, flowering plant.
