Hedera elegantissima

Hedera elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant ivy, is a species of climbing plant native to southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. Its berries are dark blue and contain seeds that are dispersed by birds.

Hedera elegantissima is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
