Hieracium oreades

Hieracium oreades, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Hieracium oreades is often found in meadows and grasslands at high elevations.
