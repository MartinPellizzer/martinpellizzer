Hieracium macroscelum

Hieracium macroscelum, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring rough, hairy stems and deeply lobed leaves. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, attracting various pollinators.

Hieracium macroscelum is known for its robust growth and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
