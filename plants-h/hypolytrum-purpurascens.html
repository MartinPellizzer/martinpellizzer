Hypolytrum purpurascens

Hypolytrum purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with purple or pinkish flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight sheen, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Hypolytrum purpurascens is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive floral display.
