Hibiscus isalensis

Hibiscus isalensis, commonly known as the Isalensis hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent. Hibiscus isalensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking blooms and adaptability to various climates.
