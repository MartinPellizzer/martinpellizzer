Haworthia variegata var. modesta

Haworthia variegata var. modesta, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, fleshy leaves with white to pale green markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant is characterized by its slow growth rate and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect light and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
