Hypericum linariifolium

Hypericum linariifolium, commonly known as the slender St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its stems are often prostrate or ascending, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Hypericum linariifolium is part of the genus Hypericum, which includes many species known for their medicinal properties.
