Hevea

Hevea, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is renowned for its latex-producing properties, which are harvested to make natural rubber. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and has large, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically growing in clusters.

The fruit, called a hevea berry, is about the size of a grape and contains a single seed.
