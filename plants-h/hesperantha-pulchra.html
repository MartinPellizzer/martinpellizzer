Hesperantha pulchra

Hesperantha pulchra, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and white flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hesperantha pulchra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
