Hieracium virosum var. oblongifolium

Hieracium virosum var. oblongifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb with a rosette of basal leaves that are typically broad and oval in shape. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a single stem, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, and it has a deep root system that helps it thrive in disturbed soils.

It is often found in meadows, pastures, and along roadsides.
