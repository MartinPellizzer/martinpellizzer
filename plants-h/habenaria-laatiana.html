Habenaria laatiana

Habenaria laatiana, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, often pale green or white flowers that bloom during the wet season. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
