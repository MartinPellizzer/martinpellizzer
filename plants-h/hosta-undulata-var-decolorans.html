Hosta undulata var. decolorans

Hosta undulata var. decolorans, commonly known as the variegated wavyleaf plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin and a variegated pattern of light green and white. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers in late summer, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for shaded garden beds and container planting.
