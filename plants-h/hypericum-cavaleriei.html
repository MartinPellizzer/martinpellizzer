Hypericum cavaleriei

Hypericum cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers with orange stamens, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a reddish or bronze hue.

Hypericum cavaleriei is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
