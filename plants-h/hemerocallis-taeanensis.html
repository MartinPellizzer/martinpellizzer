Hemerocallis taeanensis

Hemerocallis taeanensis, commonly known as the Taean daylily, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Korea.

It belongs to the Hemerocallis genus, which includes many daylily species known for their showy, short-lived blooms. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers that last only one day. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value and resilience have made it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
