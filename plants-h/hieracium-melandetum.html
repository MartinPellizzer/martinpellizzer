Hieracium melandetum

Hieracium melandetum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has hairy stems and produces achenes as its fruit, which are dispersed by wind.

Hieracium melandetum is typically perennial and can form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
