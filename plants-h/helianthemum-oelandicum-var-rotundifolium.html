Helianthemum oelandicum var. rotundifolium

Helianthemum oelandicum var. rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leaved rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has compact, fleshy leaves that are rounded in shape, adapted to retain moisture in dry conditions. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or coastal mountainous regions.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
