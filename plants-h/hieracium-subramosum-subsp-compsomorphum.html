Hieracium subramosum subsp. compsomorphum

Hieracium subramosum subsp.

compsomorphum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

The plant has slender, upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow and borne in clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It produces small, dry fruits called achene, which are dispersed by wind.
