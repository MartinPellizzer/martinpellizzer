Helichrysum oxybelium

Helichrysum oxybelium, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow to golden-orange flowers that remain intact for a long time after blooming. The plant has feathery, aromatic leaves and is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting nature. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its dried flowers are also used in herbal medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
