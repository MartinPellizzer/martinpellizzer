Homalomena asmae

Homalomena asmae, commonly known as the Asmae arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Homalomena asmae is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
