Hieracium sylvaticum subsp. circumstellatum

Hieracium sylvaticum subsp.

circumstellatum, commonly known as the wood hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and flowering heads.

The plant produces yellow disc flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets. It typically grows in moist, shaded woodland habitats.

Its botanical features include hairy stems and leaves with serrated edges.
