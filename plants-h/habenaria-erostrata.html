Habenaria erostrata

Habenaria erostrata, commonly known as the streaked orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, pale yellow or greenish-white flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, though it is not widely cultivated.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitats.
