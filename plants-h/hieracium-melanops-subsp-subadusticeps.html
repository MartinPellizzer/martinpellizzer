Hieracium melanops subsp. subadusticeps

Hieracium melanops subsp.

subadusticeps, commonly known as the dark-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

The plant features dark green, hairy leaves and yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

The subspecies is distinguished by its darker stem coloration and slightly different leaf morphology compared to other members of the species.
