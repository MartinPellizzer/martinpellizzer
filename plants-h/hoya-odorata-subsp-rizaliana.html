Hoya odorata subsp. rizaliana

Hoya odorata subsp.

rizaliana, commonly known as the Rizal hoya, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Philippines. It features thick, fleshy stems and produces clusters of waxy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

The leaves are heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among succulent and houseplant enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
