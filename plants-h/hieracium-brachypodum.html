Hieracium brachypodum

Hieracium brachypodum, commonly known as the shortfoot hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium brachypodum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
