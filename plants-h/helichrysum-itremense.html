Helichrysum itremense

Helichrysum itremense, commonly known as the golden marguerite or African daisy, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features wiry, branched stems and is characterized by its clusters of small, bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant has finely divided, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Helichrysum itremense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
