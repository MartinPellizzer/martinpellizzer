Habenaria malacophylla var. malacophylla

Habenaria malacophylla var. malacophylla, commonly known as the Malacophylla Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil, and it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with proper humidity and light conditions.
