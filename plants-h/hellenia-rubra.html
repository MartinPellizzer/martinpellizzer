Hellenia rubra

Hellenia rubra, commonly known as the red helena, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branched.

Hellenia rubra is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also used in traditional medicine.
