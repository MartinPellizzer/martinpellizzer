Helleborine varians

Helleborine varians, commonly known as the variable helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and possesses lanceolate, often hairy leaves. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow to white flowers with three sepals and three petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is native to Europe and prefers moist, shaded woodland habitats.

Helleborine varians is also known for its ability to thrive in shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
