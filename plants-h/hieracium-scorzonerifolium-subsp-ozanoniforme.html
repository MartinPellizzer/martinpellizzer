Hieracium scorzonerifolium subsp. ozanoniforme

Hieracium scorzonerifolium subsp.

ozanoniforme, commonly known as the ozanoniform hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of yellow disc flowers surrounded by ray florets, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive leaf shape and is often found in regions with Mediterranean climates.
