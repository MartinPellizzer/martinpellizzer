Hippeastrum puniceum f. roezlii

Hippeastrum puniceum f. roezlii, commonly known as the Red Flame Hippeastrum, is a hybrid form of the genus Hippeastrum, which includes amaryllis plants.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers with a distinctive pinkish hue, showcasing a unique coloration pattern. The plant produces large, showy blooms that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This cultivar is valued for its vibrant colors and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings.
