Huernia sudanensis

Huernia sudanensis, commonly known as the Sudanese moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a swollen, fleshy stem that resembles a cactus, with a distinctive, spiky appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
