Hypericum Densiflorum Var. Lobocarpum

Hypericum densiflorum var. lobocarpum is a rare and fascinating plant native to specific regions of the southeastern United States. It is known for its dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This variant of Hypericum is often found in moist, woodland environments where it thrives in partial shade. Its unique foliage and floral structure make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles due to its limited distribution and cultivation challenges.
