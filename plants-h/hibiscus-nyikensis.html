Hibiscus nyikensis

Hibiscus nyikensis, commonly known as the Nyikensis hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent. Hibiscus nyikensis thrives in humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
