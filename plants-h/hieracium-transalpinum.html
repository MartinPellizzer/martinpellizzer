Hieracium transalpinum

Hieracium transalpinum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers and is native to the mountainous regions of Europe. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces a single stem that bears the flowering heads.

Hieracium transalpinum is often found in meadows and rocky areas at high altitudes.
