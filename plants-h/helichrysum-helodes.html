Helichrysum helodes

Helichrysum helodes, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellow-orange flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant is evergreen, with gray-green, silver-velvet leaves that are aromatic when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum helodes is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
