Holostigma heterophyllum

Holostigma heterophyllum, commonly known as the variable holostigma, is a member of the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage, which features alternate, palmately lobed leaves with a velvety texture. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its unique appearance.
