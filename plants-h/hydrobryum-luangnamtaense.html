Hydrobryum luangnamtaense

Hydrobryum luangnamtaense, commonly known as the Luang Nam Ta moss, is a rare and specialized liverwort species native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a simple thalloid structure, which is characteristic of many liverworts, and it reproduces via spores rather than seeds. It plays a vital role in its ecosystem by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for microorganisms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research.
