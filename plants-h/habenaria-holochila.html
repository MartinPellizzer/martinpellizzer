Habenaria holochila

Habenaria holochila, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, upright stem that bears a single, showy flower. The flower is typically pale pink or white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
