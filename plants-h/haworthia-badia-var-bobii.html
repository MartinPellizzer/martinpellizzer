Haworthia badia var. bobii

Haworthia badia var. bobii, commonly known as the Bobii Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white to pale yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular white flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
