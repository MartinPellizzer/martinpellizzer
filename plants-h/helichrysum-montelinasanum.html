Helichrysum montelinasanum

Helichrysum montelinasanum, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble small suns. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically clustered in compact, rounded heads. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Helichrysum montelinasanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
