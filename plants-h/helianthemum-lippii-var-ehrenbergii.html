Helianthemum lippii var. ehrenbergii

Helianthemum lippii var. ehrenbergii, commonly known as the Ehrenberg's rock rose, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
