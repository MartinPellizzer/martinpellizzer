Haworthia sordida

Haworthia sordida, commonly known as the purple haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive purple or reddish tinge on the upper surface. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
