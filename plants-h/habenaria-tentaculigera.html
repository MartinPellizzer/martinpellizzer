Habenaria tentaculigera

Habenaria tentaculigera, commonly known as the tentacle orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent, thread-like structures resembling tentacles. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and marshy areas.

Its unique floral morphology and habitat preferences make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species within the orchid family.
