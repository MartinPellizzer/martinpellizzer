Haworthia tuberculata var. sublaevis

Haworthia tuberculata var. sublaevis, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The leaves are covered in small, raised tubercles, giving the plant its scientific name. It produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This variety is highly sought after for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
