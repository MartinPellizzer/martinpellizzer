Hieracium eupetalum

Hieracium eupetalum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a central yellow disk, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

Hieracium eupetalum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
