Hymenocallis × deflexa

Hymenocallis × deflexa, commonly known as the flexed ginger lily, is a hybrid plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its elegant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb, with strap-like leaves that are broad and lanceolate. Native to the tropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
