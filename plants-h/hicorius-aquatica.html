Hicorius aquatica

Hicorius aquatica, commonly known as water hibiscus, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with five petals and a prominent central column. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soil. Hicorius aquatica is often found near water bodies such as ponds, marshes, and streams.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
