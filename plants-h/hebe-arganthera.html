Hebe arganthera

Hebe arganthera, commonly known as the silver hebe, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Hebe arganthera is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
