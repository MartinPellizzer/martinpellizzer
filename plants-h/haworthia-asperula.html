Haworthia asperula

Haworthia asperula, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in small, white tubercles, giving it a distinctive zebra-like appearance. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect light and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Haworthia asperula is also known for its ability to produce small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange.
