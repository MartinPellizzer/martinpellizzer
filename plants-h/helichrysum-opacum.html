Helichrysum opacum

Helichrysum opacum, commonly known as the silver helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers are often used in dried floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature.
