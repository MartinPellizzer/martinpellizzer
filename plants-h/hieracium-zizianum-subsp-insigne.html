Hieracium zizianum subsp. insigne

Hieracium zizianum subsp.

insigne, commonly known as the noble hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed sites, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
