Hieracium murorum subsp. nastophyllum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

nastophyllum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils.

The plant features basal rosettes of lanceolate, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single head. Its flowering period occurs from late spring to early summer.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller, more compact inflorescence compared to other Hieracium species.
