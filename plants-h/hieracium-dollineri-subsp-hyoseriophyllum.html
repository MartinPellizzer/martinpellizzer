Hieracium dollineri subsp. hyoseriophyllum

Hieracium dollineri subsp.

hyoseriophyllum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky or grassy slopes at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single, short flowering stem with several flower heads.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which help protect it from harsh environmental conditions.
