Hedyotis cordifolia

Hedyotis cordifolia, commonly known as heartleaf or redroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Hedyotis cordifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
