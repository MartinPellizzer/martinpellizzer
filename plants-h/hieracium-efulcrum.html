Hieracium efulcrum

Hieracium efulcrum, commonly known as the bent-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Hieracium efulcrum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
