Haworthia schuldtiana var. robertsonensis

Haworthia schuldtiana var. robertsonensis, commonly known as the Robertson's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The leaves are arranged in a tight spiral, giving the plant a compact and elegant appearance. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the warmer months.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
