Hieracium meconopsis

Hieracium meconopsis, commonly known as the meconopsis hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, thriving in temperate climates. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Hieracium meconopsis is often found in open habitats and is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
