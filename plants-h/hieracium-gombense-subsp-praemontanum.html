Hieracium gombense subsp. praemontanum

Hieracium gombense subsp.

praemontanum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

This subspecies is typically found in alpine and subalpine meadows at high altitudes. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces numerous small, discoid flowers in late summer.

The plant is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
