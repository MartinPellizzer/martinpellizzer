Habenaria jamaicensis

Habenaria jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean region, particularly Jamaica.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes and pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
