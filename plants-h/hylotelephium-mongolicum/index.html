Hylotelephium Mongolicum

Hylotelephium mongolicum is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Mongolia. It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. This species belongs to the Crassulaceae family, which includes many succulent plants adapted to harsh environments. Its fleshy leaves and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Despite its beauty, Hylotelephium mongolicum remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
