Henckelia argentea

Henckelia argentea, commonly known as silverleaf tree or silverwood, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a waxy texture that reflects light. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
