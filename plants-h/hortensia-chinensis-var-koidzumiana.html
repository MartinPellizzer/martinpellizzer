Hortensia chinensis var. koidzumiana

Hortensia chinensis var. koidzumiana, commonly known as the Koidzumi hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that range from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant produces sterile flowers, which are often the primary ornamental feature. It prefers partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or woodland settings.

Its foliage turns vibrant red in the fall, adding seasonal interest to the landscape.
