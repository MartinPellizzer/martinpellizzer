Horsfordia rotundifolia

Horsfordia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed horsfordia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rounded, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by woody, spherical fruits. Horsfordia rotundifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ornamental plant in horticulture.
