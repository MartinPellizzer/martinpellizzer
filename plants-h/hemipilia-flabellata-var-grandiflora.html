Hemipilia flabellata var. grandiflora

Hemipilia flabellata var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered hemipilia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant features slender stems that produce clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white blossoms. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
