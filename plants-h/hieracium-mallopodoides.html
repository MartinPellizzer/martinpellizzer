Hieracium mallopodoides

Hieracium mallopodoides, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium mallopodoides is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in some regions.
