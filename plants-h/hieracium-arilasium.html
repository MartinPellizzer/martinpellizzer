Hieracium arilasium

Hieracium arilasium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has basal and upright leaves that are usually serrated and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium arilasium is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
