Helipterum exiguum

Helipterum exiguum, commonly known as the narrowleaf helipterum, is a small, herbaceous annual plant native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is well adapted to drought conditions, with a short growth cycle to complete its life cycle quickly.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient species in its natural habitat.
