Heliotropium selleanum

Heliotropium selleanum, commonly known as the Cape heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and sandy habitats.

Heliotropium selleanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
