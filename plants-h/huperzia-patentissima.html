Huperzia patentissima

Huperzia patentissima, commonly known as the rock huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen, needle-like leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant typically grows in rocky or shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its rhizome is thick and woody, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

Huperzia patentissima is notable for its ability to survive in harsh conditions, making it a hardy and resilient plant.
