Hieracium pospichalii

Hieracium pospichalii, commonly known as the Pospichal's hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Central and Eastern Europe and is characterized by its erect stems and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, composite flower heads.
