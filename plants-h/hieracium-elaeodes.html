Hieracium elaeodes

Hieracium elaeodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and meadows. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
