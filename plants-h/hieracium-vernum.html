Hieracium vernum

Hieracium vernum, commonly known as spring hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and woodland edges, preferring moist, well-drained soil. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in early spring, often before the leaves fully develop.

The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and it is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
