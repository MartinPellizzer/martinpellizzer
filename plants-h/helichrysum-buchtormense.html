Helichrysum buchtormense

Helichrysum buchtormense, commonly known as the Buchtorm yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even after drying. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a decorative plant.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and striking color.
