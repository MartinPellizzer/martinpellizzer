Hymenocallis tenuiflora

Hymenocallis tenuiflora, commonly known as the narrow-flowered hurricane lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves and produces striking, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. Its bulbs are adapted for storage and can remain dormant during dry periods.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
