Hypoxis uniflorata

Hypoxis uniflorata, commonly known as the moonflower or yellow star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces a single, showy yellow flower with a distinctive star-shaped pattern, which blooms at night. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Hypoxis uniflorata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
