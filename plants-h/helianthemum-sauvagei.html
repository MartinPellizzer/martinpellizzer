Helianthemum sauvagei

Helianthemum sauvagei, commonly known as the sauvage rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in a layer of fine, white hairs.

Helianthemum sauvagei thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
