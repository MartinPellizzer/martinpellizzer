Hieracium rupicola subsp. aldeanum

Hieracium rupicola subsp.

aldeanum, commonly known as the aldeanum hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or subalpine regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it is native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

This subspecies is adapted to harsh environments and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
