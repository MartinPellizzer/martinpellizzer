Helichrysum stramineum

Helichrysum stramineum, commonly known as the straw helichrysum, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that resemble sunflowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its aromatic foliage and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Helichrysum stramineum is also valued for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.
