Helichrysum lamarckii

Helichrysum lamarckii, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers and hairy, silver-green foliage. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum lamarckii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
