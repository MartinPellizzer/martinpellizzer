Hydrangea Serrata F. Buergeri

Japanese hydrangea, scientifically known as Hydrangea serrata f. buergeri, is a popular ornamental shrub native to Japan. This variety is prized for its vibrant, changing flower colors that depend on soil pH, ranging from blue to pink. With its lush foliage and delicate blooms, it adds both beauty and texture to gardens. The plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for landscapers and garden enthusiasts. Its historical significance in Japanese culture further enhances its appeal as a cultivated plant.
