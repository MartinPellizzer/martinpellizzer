Hypericum glomeratum

Hypericum glomeratum, commonly known as clustered St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in dense clusters, which bloom from June to August. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered with fine hairs.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
