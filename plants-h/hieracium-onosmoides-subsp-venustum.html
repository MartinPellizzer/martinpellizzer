Hieracium onosmoides subsp. venustum

Hieracium onosmoides subsp.

venustum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies.
