Haworthia arachnoidea var. calitzdorpensis

Haworthia arachnoidea var. calitzdorpensis, commonly known as the Calitzdorp Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
