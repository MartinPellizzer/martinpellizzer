Hieracium carpathicum subsp. macrellum

Hieracium carpathicum subsp.

macrellum, commonly known as the Carpathian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a tall flowering stem.

The plant produces yellow disk flowers surrounded by ray florets, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and forest edges.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
