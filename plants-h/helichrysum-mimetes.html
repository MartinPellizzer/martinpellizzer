Helichrysum mimetes

Helichrysum mimetes, commonly known as the mimic helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-orange flowers that are often clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy texture.

Helichrysum mimetes is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
