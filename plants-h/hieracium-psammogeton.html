Hieracium psammogeton

Hieracium psammogeton, commonly known as the sand yarrow, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as coastal areas and disturbed ground. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Hieracium psammogeton is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
