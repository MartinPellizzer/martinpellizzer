Haworthia gigas

Haworthia gigas, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped foliage with dark green leaves marked by white or yellowish stripes. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, star-shaped white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Haworthia gigas is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
