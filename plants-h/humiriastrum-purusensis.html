Humiriastrum purusensis

Humiriastrum purusensis, commonly known as the Amazonian candelabra, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its branched, candelabra-like structure. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow or white. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to the humid tropical environment.
