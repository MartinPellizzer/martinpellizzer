Hedyotis trichoglossa

Hedyotis trichoglossa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pennywort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. Its small, yellowish-white flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom during the summer months.

Hedyotis trichoglossa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
