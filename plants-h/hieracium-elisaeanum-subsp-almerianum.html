Hieracium elisaeanum subsp. almerianum

Hieracium elisaeanum subsp.

almerianum, commonly known as the Almerian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands. It is native to southern Europe, particularly Spain and Portugal.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
