Hieracium mankholmense

Hieracium mankholmense, commonly known as Mankholm hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to northern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom from June to August.
