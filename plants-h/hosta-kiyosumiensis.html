Hosta kiyosumiensis

Hosta kiyosumiensis, commonly known as the Kiyosumiensis plant, is a species of plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-white margin. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow during the summer months. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires well-drained, moist soil.

Hosta kiyosumiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
