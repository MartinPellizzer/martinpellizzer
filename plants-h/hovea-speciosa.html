Hovea speciosa

Hovea speciosa, commonly known as the showy hovea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with feather-like arrangements, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Hovea speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
