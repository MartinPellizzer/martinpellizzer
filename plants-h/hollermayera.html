Hollermayera

Hollermayera, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique, delicate appearance, with long, strap-like leaves and a single, showy flower that resembles a ghostly figure. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, Hollermayera is considered a conservation concern.
