Hieracium aeolocephalum

Hieracium aeolocephalum, commonly known as the Aeolian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers, which are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It has hairy, rough stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often glandular-dotted.

Hieracium aeolocephalum is native to Europe and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
