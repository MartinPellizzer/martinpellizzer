Hieracium murorum subsp. suevicola

Hieracium murorum subsp.

suevicola, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in rocky and dry soils.

The plant features basal rosettes of lanceolate leaves and produces yellow composite flowers with ray florets. It is characterized by its hairy stems and is often found in mountainous areas across the Alps.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
