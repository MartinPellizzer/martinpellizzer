Hedyotis centranthoides var. laevis

Hedyotis centranthoides var. laevis, commonly known as smooth false pennywort, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, opposite, ovate leaves with smooth margins, and it produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a useful species for ecological studies and wetland restoration projects.
