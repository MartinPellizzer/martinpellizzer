Himalrandia densa

Himalrandia densa, commonly known as the dense Himalayan phlox, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant clusters of pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it forms low, mat-like structures. Himalrandia densa has small, dark green, ovate leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil and full sun.
