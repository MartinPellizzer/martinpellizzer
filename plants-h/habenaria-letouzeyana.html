Habenaria letouzeyana

Habenaria letouzeyana, commonly known as the Letouzey's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender rhizome with a single, erect stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a distinctive three-lobed lip.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
