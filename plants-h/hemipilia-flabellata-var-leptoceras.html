Hemipilia flabellata var. leptoceras

Hemipilia flabellata var. leptoceras, commonly known as the slender-lobed hemipilia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its floral structure includes a single, nodding spike with pale yellow to greenish-white petals.

Hemipilia flabellata var. leptoceras is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
