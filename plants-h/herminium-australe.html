Herminium australe

Herminium australe, commonly known as the Australian lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and a striking inflorescence of pink to purple flowers that resemble a tresses of hair. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and heathlands. It produces small, tuberous roots that help it survive in its native habitat.

Herminium australe is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
