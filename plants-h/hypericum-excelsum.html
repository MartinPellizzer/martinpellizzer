Hypericum excelsum

Hypericum excelsum, commonly known as the golden st. john's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used for their anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.
