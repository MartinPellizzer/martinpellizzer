Hieracium lachenalii subsp. atrovirescens

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

atrovirescens, commonly known as the dark-green hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its robust, hairy stems and basal rosettes of dark green, lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, flat-topped clusters, typical of the genus Hieracium. It thrives in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
