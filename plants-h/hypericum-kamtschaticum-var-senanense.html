Hypericum kamtschaticum var. senanense

Hypericum kamtschaticum var. senanense, commonly known as the Siberian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Russian Far East and parts of northeastern China.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as stream banks and forest edges. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
