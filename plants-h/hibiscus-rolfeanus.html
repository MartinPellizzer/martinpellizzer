Hibiscus rolfeanus

Hibiscus rolfeanus, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. Hibiscus rolfeanus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
