Haworthia pubescens var. livida

Haworthia pubescens var. livida, commonly known as the silver leaf Haworthia, is a compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically gray-green with white or silver markings. The leaves are densely covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive, almost frosty appearance. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the growing season.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
