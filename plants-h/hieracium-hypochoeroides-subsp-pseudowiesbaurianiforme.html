Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. pseudowiesbaurianiforme

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

pseudowiesbaurianiforme, commonly known as the Wiesbaur's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has smooth, hairy, or glandular leaves that are often lobed or toothed.
