Hoya imperialis var. rauschii

Hoya imperialis var. rauschii, commonly known as the Rauschii Hoya, is a hybrid plant in the family Asclepiadaceae, known for its striking, waxy leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. It produces fragrant, coral-colored flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Hoya imperialis var. rauschii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.
