Helictotrichon uniyalii

Helictotrichon uniyalii, commonly known as the Kashmir Himalayan grass, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
