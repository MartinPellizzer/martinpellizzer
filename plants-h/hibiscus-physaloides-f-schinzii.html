Hibiscus physaloides f. schinzii

Hibiscus physaloides f. schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's hibiscus, is a variant of the hibiscus species native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a robust stem that can grow quite tall. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

This hibiscus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
