Helosciadium nodiflorum var. intermedium

Helosciadium nodiflorum var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate hedge woundwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and borne in loose clusters along the stems.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and its potential use in ecological restoration projects.
