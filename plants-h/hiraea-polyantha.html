Hiraea polyantha

Hiraea polyantha, commonly known as the clustered hiraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. The plant features a compact, bushy growth habit with small, clustered leaves that are often hairy in texture. Its flowers are small and white or pale pink, arranged in dense clusters, giving it a delicate appearance.

Hiraea polyantha is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and subtle floral display.
