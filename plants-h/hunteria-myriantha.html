Hunteria myriantha

Hunteria myriantha, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that resemble snowballs. The plant has simple, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The flowers bloom in winter, making it a valuable ornamental plant in regions with mild winters.
