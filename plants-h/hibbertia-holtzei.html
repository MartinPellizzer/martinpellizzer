Hibbertia holtzei

Hibbertia holtzei, commonly known as the Woolly Sunburst, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring wiry, hairy stems and narrow, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with distinctive, recurved petals that bloom in spring. Hibbertia holtzei is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
