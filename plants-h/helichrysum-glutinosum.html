Helichrysum glutinosum

Helichrysum glutinosum, commonly known as sticky helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange flowers and hairy, sticky leaves that give it its common name. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a notable species in horticulture.
