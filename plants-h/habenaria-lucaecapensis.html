Habenaria lucaecapensis

Habenaria lucaecapensis, commonly known as the Lucaecapensis orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to specific regions in Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves, adapted to its natural habitat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
