Hieracium olenophorum

Hieracium olenophorum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragweed, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring moist, well-drained soils. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped with a rough texture.

Hieracium olenophorum is often found in open habitats and can sometimes form dense colonies.
