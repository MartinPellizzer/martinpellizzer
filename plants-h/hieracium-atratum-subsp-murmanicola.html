Hieracium atratum subsp. murmanicola

Hieracium atratum subsp.

murmanicola, commonly known as the Murmanian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe and parts of Russia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. It has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

Its subspecific classification highlights its distinct ecological and morphological traits compared to other hawkweed species.
