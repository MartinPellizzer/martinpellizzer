Hyptis pinheiroi

Hyptis pinheiroi, commonly known as the Brazilian hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hyptis pinheiroi is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects.
