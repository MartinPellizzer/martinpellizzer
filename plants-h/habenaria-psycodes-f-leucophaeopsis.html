Habenaria psycodes f. leucophaeopsis

Habenaria psycodes f. leucophaeopsis, commonly known as the white-veined orchid, is a hybrid orchid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive white veins on its pale green to white flowers, which contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in tropical and subtropical regions. Its flowers are usually small to medium-sized and arranged in a loose inflorescence.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique visual characteristics.
