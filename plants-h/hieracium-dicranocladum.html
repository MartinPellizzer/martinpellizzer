Hieracium dicranocladum

Hieracium dicranocladum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of hairy, basal leaves and produces yellow composite flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils. The plant is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks.

Its distinctive foliage and floral structure make it a notable species in botanical studies.
