Hemipilia kwangsiensis

Hemipilia kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi hemipilia, is a rare orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within mountainous regions. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a basal rosette, while its inflorescence bears several showy, white to pale pink blossoms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
