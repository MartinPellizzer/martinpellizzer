Habenaria orbiculata

Habenaria orbiculata, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves and its striking, showy flowers that resemble small globes. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the summer months.

Habenaria orbiculata is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological significance in its native habitats.
