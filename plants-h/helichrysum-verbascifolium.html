Helichrysum verbascifolium

Helichrysum verbascifolium, commonly known as the woolly helichrysum, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage and small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its aromatic leaves and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
