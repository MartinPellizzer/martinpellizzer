Hieracium notoscioides

Hieracium notoscioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and woodland edges. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various environments.
