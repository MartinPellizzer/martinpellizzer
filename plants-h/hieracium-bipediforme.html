Hieracium bipediforme

Hieracium bipediforme, commonly known as the two-footed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium bipediforme is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
