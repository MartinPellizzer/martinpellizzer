Helianthemum tuberaria var. lanatum

Helianthemum tuberaria var. lanatum, commonly known as the woolly rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
