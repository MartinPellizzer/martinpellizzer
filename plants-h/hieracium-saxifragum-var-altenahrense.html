Hieracium saxifragum var. altenahrense

Hieracium saxifragum var. altenahrense, commonly known as the Altenahr hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in many areas.
