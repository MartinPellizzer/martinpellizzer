Hieracium × xanthoporphyrum

Hieracium × xanthoporphyrum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become a weed in agricultural and natural settings.
