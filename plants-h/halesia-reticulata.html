Halesia reticulata

Halesia reticulata, commonly known as the Chinese halesia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Halesia reticulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
