Hieracium murrianum subsp. rhoeadifolium

Hieracium murrianum subsp.

rhoeadifolium, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring.
