Hintonia pulchra

Hintonia pulchra, commonly known as the velvetleaf hintonia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are deep green in color. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Hintonia pulchra is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
