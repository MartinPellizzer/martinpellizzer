Huperzia kunmingensis

Huperzia kunmingensis, commonly known as the Kunming huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is native to China, particularly in Yunnan Province, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Huperzia kunmingensis is known for its ability to retain water, which helps it survive in its natural habitat.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
