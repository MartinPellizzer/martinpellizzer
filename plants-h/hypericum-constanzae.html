Hypericum constanzae

Hypericum constanzae, commonly known as the Constantza St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering shrubs. The plant is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and woody stems, typical of the Hypericaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
