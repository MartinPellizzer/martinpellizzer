Hippeastrum evansiae

Hippeastrum evansiae, commonly known as the Evans' amaryllis, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and fleshy, typical of the Amaryllidaceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Hippeastrum evansiae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.
