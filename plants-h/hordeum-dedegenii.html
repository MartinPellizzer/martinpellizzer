Hordeum dedegenii

Hordeum dedegenii, commonly known as the Tibetan barley, is a wild species of barley native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau.

It is a herbaceous plant with slender, grass-like leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, dry conditions. Its seeds are small and hardy, adapted to survive in harsh climates.

Hordeum dedegenii is of interest to botanists and researchers due to its genetic diversity and potential for agricultural use in extreme environments.
