Habenaria christianii

Habenaria christianii, commonly known as the Christian's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral structure.
