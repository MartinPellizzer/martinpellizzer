Helianthemum × petrerense

Helianthemum × petrerense, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a succulent shrub native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Cistaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, often hairy leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
