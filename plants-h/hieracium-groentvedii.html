Hieracium groentvedii

Hieracium groentvedii, commonly known as the Dutch cowweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in various parts of Europe. It has finely divided, grass-like leaves and is often seen in meadows and along roadsides.

Hieracium groentvedii is an annual or biennial plant that plays a role in supporting pollinators.
