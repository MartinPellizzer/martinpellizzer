Hardenbergia bimaculata var. longiracemosa

Hardenbergia bimaculata var. longiracemosa, commonly known as the red-flowered waratah, is a striking perennial shrub native to Australia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces showy, red or pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, forming dense, racemose inflorescences. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem and a preference for well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a range of environmental conditions.
