Hydrocotyle Burmanica Subsp. Chinensis

Chinese pennywort, scientifically known as Hydrocotyle burmanica subsp. chinensis, is a versatile aquatic and semi-aquatic plant native to parts of Asia. It is commonly found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds and streams. This plant is valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions and its ornamental appeal. Its heart-shaped leaves and delicate white flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners and aquatic enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it can also be invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
