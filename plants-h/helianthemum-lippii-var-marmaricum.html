Helianthemum lippii var. marmaricum

Helianthemum lippii var. marmaricum, commonly known as the white rock rose, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a delicate touch to rocky or coastal landscapes. The plant has aromatic, fuzzy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, providing a textured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its hardy nature and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
