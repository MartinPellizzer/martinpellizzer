Hieracium episcoticeps

Hieracium episcoticeps, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow composite flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium episcoticeps is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
