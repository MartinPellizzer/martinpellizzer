Hieracium valoddae subsp. austroitalicum

Hieracium valoddae subsp.

austroitalicum, commonly known as the Southern Italian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides.

Native to southern Europe, it is often found in the Mediterranean region.
