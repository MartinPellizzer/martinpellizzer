Habenaria pseudoplatycoryne

Habenaria pseudoplatycoryne, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, upright stem and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its delicate structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
