Hieracium uechtritzianum

Hieracium uechtritzianum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

Hieracium uechtritzianum is often found in mountainous areas and is adapted to survive in harsh, windy environments.
