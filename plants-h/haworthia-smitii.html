Haworthia smitii

Haworthia smitii, commonly known as the Smit's Haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks bearing white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Haworthia smitii is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
