Hypoxis biflora

Hypoxis biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered yellowstar, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.

Hypoxis biflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
