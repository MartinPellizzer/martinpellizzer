Heptapleurum elatum

Heptapleurum elatum, commonly known as the broadleaf stonecrop, is a member of the Crassulaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, succulent leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative plant in humid climates.
