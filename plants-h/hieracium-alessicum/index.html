Hieracium Alessicum

Hieracium alessicum is a species of hawkweed known for its delicate yellow flowers and hairy stems. Native to parts of Europe and western Asia, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas. This plant is often mistaken for other hawkweed species due to its similar appearance. It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance. Despite its modest size, Hieracium alessicum holds significance in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
