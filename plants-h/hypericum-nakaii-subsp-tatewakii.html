Hypericum nakaii subsp. tatewakii

Hypericum nakaii subsp.

tatewakii, commonly known as Nakai's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia. It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and narrow, linear leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses.
