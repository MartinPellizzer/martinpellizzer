Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp. pseudosubspeciosum

Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp.

pseudosubspeciosum, commonly known as the false showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow disc flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has basal and upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is distinguished by its slightly smaller flowers and shorter peduncles compared to other members of the species.
