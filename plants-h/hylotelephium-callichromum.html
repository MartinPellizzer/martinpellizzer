Hylotelephium callichromum

Hylotelephium callichromum, commonly known as the Armenian carpet cushion, is a succulent perennial plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features dense, fleshy rosettes of leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of pink to red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
