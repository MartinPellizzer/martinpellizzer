Hedyotis Laevigata

Hedyotis laevigata is a small, flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific. It is known for its delicate, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are typically oval and smooth, giving it a distinctive appearance. Despite its modest size, Hedyotis laevigata plays an important role in local ecosystems.
