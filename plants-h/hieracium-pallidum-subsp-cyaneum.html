Hieracium pallidum subsp. cyaneum

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

cyaneum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy basal rosettes.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. Its flowers are yellow with a pale blue or greenish hue, and it produces small, elongated achenes as its seeds.

It is often found in temperate climates and is considered a common weed in certain regions.
