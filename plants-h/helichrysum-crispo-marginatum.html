Helichrysum crispo-marginatum

Helichrysum crispo-marginatum, commonly known as the crinkled margined everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically gray-green with a fuzzy texture and have a distinctive crinkled margin. This species is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in Mediterranean climates.
