Hemigenia eutaxioides

Hemigenia eutaxioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hemigenia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and white. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Hemigenia eutaxioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
