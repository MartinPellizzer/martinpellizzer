Habenaria liguliglossa

Habenaria liguliglossa, commonly known as the narrow-lipped twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Indian subcontinent.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring a slender, leafless stem and small, pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It has a short-lived rhizome and produces its flowers in late spring or early summer.

Due to its delicate structure and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
