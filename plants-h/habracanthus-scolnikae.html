Habracanthus scolnikae

Habracanthus scolnikae, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal starfish, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to the subtropical climate of its native region. Its unique appearance, with its star-shaped blooms, makes it a notable species in botanical gardens.
