Hewardia tasmanica

Hewardia tasmanica, commonly known as the Tasmanian hewardia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Tasmania.

It features upright, narrow leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with some varieties displaying striking variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers during the spring and early summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, decorative plants.
