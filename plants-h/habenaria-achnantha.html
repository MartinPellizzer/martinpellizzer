Habenaria achnantha

Habenaria achnantha, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodland areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Habenaria achnantha is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral appearance.
