Haworthia reinwardtii var. major

Haworthia reinwardtii var. major, commonly known as the giant Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant forms a rosette shape, with leaves that can grow up to 10 inches in length. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the summer months.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
