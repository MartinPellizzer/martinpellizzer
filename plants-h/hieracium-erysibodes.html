Hieracium erysibodes

Hieracium erysibodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright stem and basal rosette of leaves, which are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium erysibodes is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
