Hypoxis nyasica

Hypoxis nyasica, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellowish-white flowers in umbels. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in moist, grassy habitats. It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is valued for its ornamental appearance.

Hypoxis nyasica is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
