Hieracium cololeptum

Hieracium cololeptum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in the summer, and the plant has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture.

Hieracium cololeptum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
