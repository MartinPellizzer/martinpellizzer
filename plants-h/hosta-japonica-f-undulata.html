Hosta japonica f. undulata

Hosta japonica f. undulata, commonly known as the wavy-leaf Japanese hosta, is a cultivar prized for its ornamental foliage.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are wavy-edged, giving it a distinctive, undulating appearance. The plant is native to Japan and thrives in shady, moist environments. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in early summer.

This variety is well-suited for use in shaded garden beds or as a container plant.
