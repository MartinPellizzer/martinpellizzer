Hedyotis cephalantha

Hedyotis cephalantha, commonly known as the rock rose or mountain geranium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or subalpine habitats. Hedyotis cephalantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil and full sun.
