Hullettia griffithiana

Hullettia griffithiana, commonly known as the Kashmir rose or Himalayan rose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of northern India and Nepal.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, single or clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is often found in rocky or alpine environments. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

Hullettia griffithiana is also noted for its resilience to cold climates and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
