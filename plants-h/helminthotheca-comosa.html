Helminthotheca comosa

Helminthotheca comosa, commonly known as the hairy sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Europe and Asia.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer.
