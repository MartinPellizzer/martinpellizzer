Helichrysum aureum var. monocephalum

Helichrysum aureum var. monocephalum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even when dried, making it popular in floral arrangements. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine, silky hairs.

It is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
