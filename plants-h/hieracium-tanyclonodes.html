Hieracium tanyclonodes

Hieracium tanyclonodes, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and can form dense colonies.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered a potential invasive species in some regions.
