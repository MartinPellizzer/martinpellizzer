Hariota knightii var. tenuispinis

Hariota knightii var. tenuispinis, commonly known as the slender-needle hariota, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, cylindrical stems covered in fine, spiky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
