Helwingia himalaica var. prunifolia

Helwingia himalaica var. prunifolia, commonly known as the Himalayan helwingia, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high elevations.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in plant morphology and ecology.
