Hypericum aucheri var. aegeum

Hypericum aucheri var. aegeum, commonly known as the Aegean St. John's Wort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Aegean region of Greece.

It features dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has woody stems that are often covered in a layer of fine, white hairs. Its leaves are oval-shaped, with a slightly serrated edge and a dark green color.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
