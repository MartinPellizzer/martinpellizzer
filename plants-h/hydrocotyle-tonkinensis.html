Hydrocotyle tonkinensis

Hydrocotyle tonkinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese pennywort, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features floating leaves that are typically heart-shaped with a waxy surface, allowing it to thrive in shallow, still waters. The plant produces small, white flowers that are often clustered together, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It is often used in water gardens and pond ecosystems for its ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.

Hydrocotyle tonkinensis is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
