Hieracium lazicum

Hieracium lazicum, commonly known as Lazic hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It features pale yellow to orange flowers and has hairy stems and leaves.

Hieracium lazicum is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates.
