Hieracium nigrescens subsp. cirrostylum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

cirrostylum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of yellow flowers.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are small and arranged in a composite head. This subspecies is distinguished by its pale yellow flowers and the presence of a short, bristly stem.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
