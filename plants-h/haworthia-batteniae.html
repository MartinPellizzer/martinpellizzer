Haworthia batteniae

Haworthia batteniae, commonly known as the Batten's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves with white markings and translucent windows, which are adaptations for capturing and storing water. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
