Hermione × bifrons var. primulina

Hermione × bifrons var. primulina, commonly known as the Primrose Trumpet, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from its parent species.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 12 inches. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring through early summer.
