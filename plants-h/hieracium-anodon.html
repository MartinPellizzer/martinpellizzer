Hieracium anodon

Hieracium anodon, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to Europe, including parts of the United Kingdom. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are often heart-shaped and can reach up to 20 cm in length.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed environments, making it a common weed in agricultural and urban settings.
