Hieracium interjacens

Hieracium interjacens, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and hairy, alternate leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal, aiding in its spread.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its aggressive growth habits.
