Hordeum coeleste var. barbatum

Hordeum coeleste var. barbatum, commonly known as the blue barley or sky barley, is a variant of the barley species characterized by its striking blue hue in its florets.

This plant is native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its botanical features include slender stalks and compact inflorescences, with the distinctive blue coloration resulting from the presence of anthocyanin pigments. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it contributes to the ecological diversity of these regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
