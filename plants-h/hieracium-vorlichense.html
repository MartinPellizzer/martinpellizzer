Hieracium vorlichense

Hieracium vorlichense, commonly known as Vorlich's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges.

Hieracium vorlichense is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
