Hedysarum timoriense

Hedysarum timoriense, commonly known as Timor milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the island of Timor in Indonesia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, which benefits soil fertility.

Hedysarum timoriense is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
