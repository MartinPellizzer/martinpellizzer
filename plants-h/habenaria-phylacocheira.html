Habenaria phylacocheira

Habenaria phylacocheira, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and long, slender flowering stems that bear clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and grasslands. It has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
