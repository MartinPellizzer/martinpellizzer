Habenaria denticulata

Habenaria denticulata, commonly known as the toothed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in tropical and subtropical regions.

Habenaria denticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
