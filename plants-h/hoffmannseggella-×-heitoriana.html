Hoffmannseggella × heitoriana

Hoffmannseggella × heitoriana, commonly known as the hybrid hawthorn, is a naturally occurring hybrid between *Crataegus monogyna* and *Crataegus laevigata*.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in temperate regions of Europe. The plant features thorny branches, ovate to elliptical leaves, and clusters of small, white or pink flowers in spring. It produces red, fleshy berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in hedgerows and gardens.
