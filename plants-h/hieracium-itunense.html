Hieracium itunense

Hieracium itunense, commonly known as the Itunense hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
