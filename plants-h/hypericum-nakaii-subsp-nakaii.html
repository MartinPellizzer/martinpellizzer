Hypericum nakaii subsp. nakaii

Hypericum nakaii subsp.

nakaii, commonly known as the Nakai's St. John's Wort, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its showy yellow flowers, which bloom in summer, are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

The species is characterized by its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
