Hieracium valdefoliosum

Hieracium valdefoliosum, commonly known as the Valdese hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
