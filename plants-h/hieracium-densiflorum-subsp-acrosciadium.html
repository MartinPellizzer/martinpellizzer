Hieracium densiflorum subsp. acrosciadium

Hieracium densiflorum subsp.

acrosciadium, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, producing small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

The species is known for its dense, flowering clusters and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
