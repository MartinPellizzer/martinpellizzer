Hieracium erythrocarpum subsp. stellidorsum

Hieracium erythrocarpum subsp.

stellidorsum, commonly known as the red-fruited hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow to orange flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its distinctive red or reddish-brown fruits, which are spherical and covered in fine hairs. It typically thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides.

The plant is often found in temperate climates and is adapted to well-drained soils.
