Habenaria idroboi

Habenaria idroboi, commonly known as the Idrobo orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. It has a pseudobulb structure and produces linear, grass-like leaves.

Its flowering period occurs during the wet season, contributing to its ecological significance in its native habitat.
