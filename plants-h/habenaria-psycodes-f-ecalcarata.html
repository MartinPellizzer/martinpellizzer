Habenaria psycodes f. ecalcarata

Habenaria psycodes f. ecalcarata, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a botanical variant of the species Habenaria psycodes.

It is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals, which are a notable feature in its floral morphology. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to regions in Southeast Asia. It typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest understories.

Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.
