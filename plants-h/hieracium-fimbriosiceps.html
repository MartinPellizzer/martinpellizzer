Hieracium fimbriosiceps

Hieracium fimbriosiceps, commonly known as the fringed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas.

It is an annual or biennial plant, often found in disturbed soils.
