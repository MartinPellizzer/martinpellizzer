Hieracium psiloloma

Hieracium psiloloma, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, blooming from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium psiloloma is known for its creeping growth habit and ability to form dense mats.
