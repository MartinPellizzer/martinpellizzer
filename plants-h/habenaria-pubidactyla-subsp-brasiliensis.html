Habenaria pubidactyla subsp. brasiliensis

Habenaria pubidactyla subsp.

brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil. It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent, hairy sepals and petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and relatively rare orchid within its native range.
