Hypochaeris maculata var. koritnicensis

Hypochaeris maculata var. koritnicensis, commonly known as the Koritnic's hairy cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive hairy texture.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural areas.
