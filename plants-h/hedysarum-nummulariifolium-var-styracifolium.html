Hedysarum nummulariifolium var. styracifolium

Hedysarum nummulariifolium var. styracifolium, commonly known as the styracifolium form of the shieldleaf vetch, is a perennial legume native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, shield-shaped leaves that are typically silver-white in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
