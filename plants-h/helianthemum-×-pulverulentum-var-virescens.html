Helianthemum × pulverulentum var. virescens

Helianthemum × pulverulentum var. virescens, commonly known as the green-flowered rockrose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, evergreen foliage that ranges from dark green to gray-green. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive greenish tinge in this variety. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardening.
