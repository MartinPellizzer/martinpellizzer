Hellenia rufa

Hellenia rufa, commonly known as the red-flowered helichrysum, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, bright red flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Hellenia rufa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
