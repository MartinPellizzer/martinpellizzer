Helianthemum salicifolium var. securitium

Helianthemum salicifolium var. securitium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved helianthemum, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense, compact growth with small, leathery leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in sunny, coastal or mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
