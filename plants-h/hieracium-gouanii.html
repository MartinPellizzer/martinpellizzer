Hieracium gouanii

Hieracium gouanii, commonly known as Gouan's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its clustered, yellowish flowers and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, exhibiting a low-growing, spreading habit. Its flowering period occurs in the summer months, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Hieracium gouanii is often found in open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
