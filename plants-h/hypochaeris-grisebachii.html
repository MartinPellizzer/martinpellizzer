Hypochaeris grisebachii

Hypochaeris grisebachii, commonly known as the grayleaf false dandelion, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its basal rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Hypochaeris grisebachii is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
