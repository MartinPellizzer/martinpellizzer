Hedysarum mackenziei f. niveum

Hedysarum mackenziei f. niveum, commonly known as the snow-flowered prickly poppy, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

The plant has a robust, branched stem covered in stiff, spiny hairs, which help protect it from herbivores. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small, ovate leaflets, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
