Heterospathe woodfordiana

Heterospathe woodfordiana, commonly known as the Woodford's heterospathe, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that often display a variegated pattern of green and white. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Botanically, it belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental and medicinal plants.
