Habenaria purpurascens

Habenaria purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish with purple or red markings, and they are arranged in a loose raceme.

The leaves are linear to ovate, often with a waxy coating that helps retain moisture.
