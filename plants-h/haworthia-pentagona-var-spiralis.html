Haworthia pentagona var. spiralis

Haworthia pentagona var. spiralis, commonly known as the spiral Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The leaves have a unique spiral pattern along their edges, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its elegant, low-maintenance form.
