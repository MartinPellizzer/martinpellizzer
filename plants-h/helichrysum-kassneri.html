Helichrysum kassneri

Helichrysum kassneri, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like inflorescences that resemble miniature suns, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant color and drought tolerance.

Helichrysum kassneri is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
