Habenaria spiraloides

Habenaria spiraloides, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, spiral-shaped inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and habitat specificity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
