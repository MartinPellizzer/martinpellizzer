Hyacinthella azurea

Hyacinthella azurea, commonly known as the Azure Hyacinth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, intensely blue flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Hyacinthella azurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, delicate blooms.
