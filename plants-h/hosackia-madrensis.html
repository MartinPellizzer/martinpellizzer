Hosackia madrensis

Hosackia madrensis, commonly known as the Madrean prairie clover, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features slender stems with small, clustered leaves and produces delicate, pink to purple flowers. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought conditions.

Hosackia madrensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing nectar for pollinators in its native habitat.
