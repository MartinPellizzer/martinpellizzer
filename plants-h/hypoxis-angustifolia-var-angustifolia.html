Hypoxis angustifolia var. angustifolia

Hypoxis angustifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved yellowstar, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with reflexed petals. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, where it contributes to the local flora.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant collections.
