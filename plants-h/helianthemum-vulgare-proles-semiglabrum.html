Helianthemum vulgare proles semiglabrum

Helianthemum vulgare proles semiglabrum, commonly known as the smooth rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically gray-green in color and covered with fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for landscaping.
