Hieracium olliceps

Hieracium olliceps, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are covered in dense, white hairs, giving it a woolly appearance, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium olliceps is often found in open habitats and can sometimes be invasive in certain regions.
