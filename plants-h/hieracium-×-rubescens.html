Hieracium × rubescens

Hieracium × rubescens, commonly known as the red hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to orange flowers, which bloom in clusters and are typically visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has rough, hairy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white or yellowish down. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas across Europe and parts of Asia.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
