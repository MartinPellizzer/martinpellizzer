Hicorius alba

Hicorius alba, commonly known as white hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy white petals and yellow stamens, which give it a striking appearance. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Hicorius alba is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in some cultures.
