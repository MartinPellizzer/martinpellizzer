Habenaria henryi

Habenaria henryi, commonly known as the Henry's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that allow it to spread gradually over time. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it prefers moist, shaded environments.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
