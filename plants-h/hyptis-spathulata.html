Hyptis spathulata

Hyptis spathulata, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its gray-green leaves and spikes of blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Hyptis spathulata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
