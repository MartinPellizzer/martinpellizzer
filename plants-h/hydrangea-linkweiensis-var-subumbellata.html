Hydrangea linkweiensis var. subumbellata

Hydrangea linkweiensis var. subumbellata, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features large, showy inflorescences that range from white to pink or blue depending on soil pH. The plant has opposite, serrated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
