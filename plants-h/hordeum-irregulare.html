Hordeum irregulare

Hordeum irregulare, commonly known as the irregular barley, is a wild grass species native to Eurasia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and spikelet-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its botanical features include narrow leaves and small, wind-pollinated flowers.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other open habitats.
