Horsfieldia perangusta

Horsfieldia perangusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features elliptical or lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

Horsfieldia perangusta thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
