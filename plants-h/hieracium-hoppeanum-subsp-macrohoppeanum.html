Hieracium hoppeanum subsp. macrohoppeanum

Hieracium hoppeanum subsp.

macrohoppeanum, commonly known as the large-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Compositae. It is characterized by its robust growth habit and large, broad leaves that are typically hairy.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the daisy family. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

This subspecies is distinguished by its larger leaf size compared to other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
