Heterostemma ficoides

Heterostemma ficoides, commonly known as the false fig tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in panicles, while the fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
