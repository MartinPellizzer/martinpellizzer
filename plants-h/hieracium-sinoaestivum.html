Hieracium sinoaestivum

Hieracium sinoaestivum, commonly known as the Chinese early summer hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and tall flowering stems. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting various pollinators. It typically grows in moist meadows and along streambanks, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
