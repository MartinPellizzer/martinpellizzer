Homalomena sebessiana

Homalomena sebessiana, commonly known as the Sebessi arrowleaf.

It is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family, characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is popular in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
