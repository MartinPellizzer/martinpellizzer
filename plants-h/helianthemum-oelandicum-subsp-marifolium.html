Helianthemum oelandicum subsp. marifolium

Helianthemum oelandicum subsp.

marifolium, commonly known as the Oeland carnation, is a flowering plant native to southern Scandinavia. It is a low-growing shrub with woody stems and fleshy, succulent leaves.

The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or maritime environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh climates.
