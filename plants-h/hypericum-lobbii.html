Hypericum lobbii

Hypericum lobbii, commonly known as the African St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are simple and opposite. Hypericum lobbii is notable for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses in treating skin conditions and inflammation.

It is also valued in horticulture for its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
