Hieracium vogesiacum subsp. angliciforme

Hieracium vogesiacum subsp.

angliciforme, commonly known as the English hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The plant typically grows in moist grasslands, meadows, and woodland edges, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped or kidney-shaped with hairy surfaces.

The subspecies angliciforme is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology compared to other subspecies of Hieracium vogesiacum.
