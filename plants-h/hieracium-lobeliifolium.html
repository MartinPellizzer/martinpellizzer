Hieracium lobeliifolium

Hieracium lobeliifolium, commonly known as the lobeliifolium hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the eastern United States and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves. The plant produces yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
