Hieracium oligocephalum

Hieracium oligocephalum, commonly known as the narrow-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blooms. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

Hieracium oligocephalum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
