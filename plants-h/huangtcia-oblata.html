Huangtcia oblata

Huangtcia oblata, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Huangtcia oblata is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
