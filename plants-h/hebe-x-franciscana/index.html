Hebe × Franciscana

Hebe × franciscana is a hybrid shrub known for its vibrant floral displays and adaptability to various climates. Native to New Zealand, it combines the best traits of its parent species, Hebe and franciscana. This plant is popular among gardeners for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements. Its clusters of tubular flowers come in shades of blue, purple, and white, adding visual interest to any landscape. Whether grown in pots or in the ground, Hebe × franciscana is a versatile choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
