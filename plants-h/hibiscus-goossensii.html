Hibiscus goossensii

Hibiscus goossensii, commonly known as the Cook Islands hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in tropical climates with high humidity. It is often found growing in coastal areas and moist lowland forests.

Hibiscus goossensii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional practices by indigenous communities.
