Hieracium centaureifolium

Hieracium centaureifolium, commonly known as the centaureifolium hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has a rosette of basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a single head, surrounded by pale green bracts.

It is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
