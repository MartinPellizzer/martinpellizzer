Habenaria nicholsonii

Habenaria nicholsonii, commonly known as the Nicholson's butterfly orchid, is a species native to the wet tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically pale pink to white in color. The plant produces a tall, slender stem with several clustered flowers, and its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

The orchid is known for its fragrant blooms, which attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
