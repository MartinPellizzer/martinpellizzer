Hieracium obscuriforme

Hieracium obscuriforme, commonly known as the dark-form fern.

This perennial herb is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in grasslands and rocky areas. It features a basal rosette of narrow, dark green leaves with a hairy surface. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, arranged in a loose cluster at the end of leafless stems.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the dandelion family, Asteraceae.
