Helictochloa blaui subsp. aenigmatica

Helictochloa blaui subsp.

aenigmatica, commonly known as the Bluegrass Needlegrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States. It is characterized by its fine, grass-like blades and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats.
