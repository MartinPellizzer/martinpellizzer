Heteropogon polystachyos

Heteropogon polystachyos, commonly known as the tall feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, silver-green foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers that are typically pink to purplish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields, along roadsides, and in disturbed areas.

Heteropogon polystachyos is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping and restoration projects.
