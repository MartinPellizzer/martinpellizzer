Habenaria exaltata

Habenaria exaltata, commonly known as the tall orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it produces showy, pendulous blooms that attract pollinators.

Habenaria exaltata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
