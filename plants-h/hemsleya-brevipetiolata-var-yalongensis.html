Hemsleya brevipetiolata var. yalongensis

Hemsleya brevipetiolata var. yalongensis, commonly known as the Yalong arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to China.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
