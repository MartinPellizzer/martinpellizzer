Hieracium bifidum subsp. bolognense

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

bolognense, commonly known as the Bologna hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. It has narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that attract pollinators.
