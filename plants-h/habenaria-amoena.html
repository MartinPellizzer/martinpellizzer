Habenaria amoena

Habenaria amoena, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged along the stem, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Habenaria amoena is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
