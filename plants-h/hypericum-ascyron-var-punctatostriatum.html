Hypericum ascyron var. punctatostriatum

Hypericum ascyron var. punctatostriatum, commonly known as the spotted-striated St. John's wort, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive pattern of dark green spots and stripes. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in garden settings.
