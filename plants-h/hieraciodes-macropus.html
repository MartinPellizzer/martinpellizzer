Hieraciodes macropus

Hieraciodes macropus, commonly known as the large-flowered hieraciodes, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, daisy-like blooms with yellow centers and white petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
