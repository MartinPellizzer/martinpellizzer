Hypoxis monophylla

Hypoxis monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf starflower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a single, waxy, grass-like leaf that persists throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, yellow, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoxis monophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
