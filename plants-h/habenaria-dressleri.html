Habenaria dressleri

Habenaria dressleri, commonly known as the Dressler's Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in some regions, making conservation efforts essential for its survival.
