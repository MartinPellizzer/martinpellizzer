Hieracium gorodkovianum

Hieracium gorodkovianum, commonly known as the Gorodkovian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
