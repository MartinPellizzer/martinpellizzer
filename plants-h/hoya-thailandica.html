Hoya thailandica

Hoya thailandica, commonly known as the Thai Wax Plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Thailand.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers. The plant is a succulent, capable of storing water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant. Hoya thailandica produces fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
