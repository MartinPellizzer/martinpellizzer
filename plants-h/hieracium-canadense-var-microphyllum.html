Hieracium canadense var. microphyllum

Hieracium canadense var. microphyllum, commonly known as Canadian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its clustered, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has basal rosettes of narrow, spoon-shaped leaves and produces slender, branching stems. It thrives in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks.

Its small, alternate leaves and distinctive flower heads make it identifiable within the genus Hieracium.
