Hieracium arcticum

Hieracium arcticum, commonly known as Arctic hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

Hieracium arcticum is often found in tundra and mountainous areas where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
