Hedyotis fluviatilis var. hathewayi

Hedyotis fluviatilis var. hathewayi, commonly known as the river pennywort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually rounded or ovate in shape. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

It is often found in aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats, contributing to the ecological diversity of its native regions.
