Heptapleurum elatum var. griffithii

Heptapleurum elatum var. griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's heptapleurum, is a variant of the heptapleurum plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical and subtropical forests. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, while its flowers are small and inconspicuous.
