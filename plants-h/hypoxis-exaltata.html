Hypoxis exaltata

Hypoxis exaltata, commonly known as the star lily or African lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Hypoxis exaltata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
