Hieracium sublaeticeps

Hieracium sublaeticeps, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a basal rosette formation. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

This species is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas, showing adaptability to various environmental conditions.
