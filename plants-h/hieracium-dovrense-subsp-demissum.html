Hieracium dovrense subsp. demissum

Hieracium dovrense subsp.

demissum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often glandular, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
