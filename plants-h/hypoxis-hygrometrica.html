Hypoxis hygrometrica

Hypoxis hygrometrica, commonly known as the rain lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky areas.

Hypoxis hygrometrica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
