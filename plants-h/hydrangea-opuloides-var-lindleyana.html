Hydrangea opuloides var. lindleyana

Hydrangea opuloides var. lindleyana, commonly known as the Lindley's hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features large, showy inflorescences that range from white to pink or blue, depending on soil pH. The plant has ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically green with a slight pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
