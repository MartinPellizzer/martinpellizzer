Hieracium plumbeum var. submetaliceps

Hieracium plumbeum var. submetaliceps, commonly known as the silver-haired hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates. Its foliage is covered in fine, soft hairs that give it a distinctive silvery appearance.

The variety submetaliceps is distinguished by its elongated, slender leaves and a more pronounced silver hue compared to the typical form.
