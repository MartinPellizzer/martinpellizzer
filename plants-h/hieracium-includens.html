Hieracium includens

Hieracium includens, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish to reddish flowers. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

Hieracium includens is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
