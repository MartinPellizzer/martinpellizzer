Hornstedtia metriocheilos

Hornstedtia metriocheilos, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
