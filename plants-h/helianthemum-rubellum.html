Helianthemum rubellum

Helianthemum rubellum, commonly known as the red rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety. The plant has silvery-gray foliage with small, glandular hairs that help reduce water loss in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Helianthemum rubellum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
