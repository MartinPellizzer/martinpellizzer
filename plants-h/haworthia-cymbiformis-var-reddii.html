Haworthia cymbiformis var. reddii

Haworthia cymbiformis var. reddii, commonly known as the Redden's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and adorned with white to pale blue markings. The leaves are often tinged with red or pink, especially under bright light conditions. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
