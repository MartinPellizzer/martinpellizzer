Huperzia obscure-denticulata

Huperzia obscure-denticulata, commonly known as the obscure-denticulate huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its intricate, finely toothed leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are usually long and narrow, with a leathery texture that helps it retain moisture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
