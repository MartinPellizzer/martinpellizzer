Humirianthera ampla

Humirianthera ampla, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers with vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding environment.

Humirianthera ampla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings due to its specific care requirements.
