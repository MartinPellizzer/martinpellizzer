Helleborine latifolia f. lancifolia

Helleborine latifolia f. lancifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved helleborine, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant produces striking, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent spurred petals, typically blooming in spring. Its foliage is broad and often has a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This cultivar is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
