Hieracium stenolegnodes

Hieracium stenolegnodes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, which are surrounded by leaf-like bracts. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium stenolegnodes is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
