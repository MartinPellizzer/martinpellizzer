Hieracium × frickii

Hieracium × frickii, commonly known as the hybrid hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates. It is a sterile hybrid, resulting from the cross between two species of hawkweed, which contributes to its unique botanical features.

Its morphology and genetic makeup make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
