Hieracium lobotocum

Hieracium lobotocum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium lobotocum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in many regions.
