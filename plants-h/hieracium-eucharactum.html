Hieracium eucharactum

Hieracium eucharactum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, upright stems. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It produces numerous small, disc-shaped flowers that attract pollinators like bees and flies.
