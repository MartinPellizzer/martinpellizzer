Hyaloseris boliviensis

Hyaloseris boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian hyaloseris, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cluster. It is typically found in high-altitude grasslands and rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
