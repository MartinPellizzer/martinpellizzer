Hieracium zizianum subsp. sclerotrichum

Hieracium zizianum subsp.

sclerotrichum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and tall, branched flowering stems.

The plant produces yellow flowers in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are covered in dense, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.
