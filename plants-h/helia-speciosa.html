Helia speciosa

Helia speciosa, commonly known as the showy helia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Helia speciosa thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.
