Hibbertia fractiflexa subsp. fractiflexa

Hibbertia fractiflexa subsp.

fractiflexa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hibbertia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers with prominent, recurved petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open forests and heathlands. Hibbertia fractiflexa subsp.
