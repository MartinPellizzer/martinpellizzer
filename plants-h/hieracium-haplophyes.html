Hieracium haplophyes

Hieracium haplophyes, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems, which help in reducing water loss in arid environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium haplophyes is native to regions of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.
