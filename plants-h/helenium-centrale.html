Helenium centrale

Helenium centrale, commonly known as the yellow helenium, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring lance-shaped leaves and showy, ray-like petals. Helenium centrale thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental and ecological value.
