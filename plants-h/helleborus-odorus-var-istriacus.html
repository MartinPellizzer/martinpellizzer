Helleborus odorus var. istriacus

Helleborus odorus var. istriacus, commonly known as the Istrian hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Adriatic region.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces striking, nodding flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
