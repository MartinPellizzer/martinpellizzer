Hieronymiella clidanthoides

Hieronymiella clidanthoides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has a robust root system and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, providing drought resistance.

It is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
