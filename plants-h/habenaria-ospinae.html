Habenaria ospinae

Habenaria ospinae, commonly known as the Ospina's butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically pale pink or white with intricate patterns. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often arranged in a basal rosette.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
