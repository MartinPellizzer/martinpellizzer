Hieracium canaliculatum

Hieracium canaliculatum, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
