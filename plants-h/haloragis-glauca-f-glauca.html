Haloragis glauca f. glauca

Haloragis glauca f. glauca, commonly known as the blue star, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, star-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
