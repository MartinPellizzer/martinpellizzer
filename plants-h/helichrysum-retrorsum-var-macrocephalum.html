Helichrysum retrorsum var. macrocephalum

Helichrysum retrorsum var. macrocephalum, commonly known as the golden marguerite or Cape marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of yellow-orange flowers that resemble tiny sunflowers. The plant has silver-gray, woolly leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
