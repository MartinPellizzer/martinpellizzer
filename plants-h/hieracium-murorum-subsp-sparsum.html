Hieracium murorum subsp. sparsum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

sparsum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and basal rosettes of lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a dense, flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the Compositae family. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands.
