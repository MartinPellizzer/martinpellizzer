Haworthia regalis

Haworthia regalis, commonly known as the royal haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
