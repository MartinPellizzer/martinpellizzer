Haworthia brevis

Haworthia brevis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features short, thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
