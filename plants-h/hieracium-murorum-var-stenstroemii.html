Hieracium murorum var. stenstroemii

Hieracium murorum var. stenstroemii, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are often reddish in color. It thrives in moist, grassy habitats and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

This variant is distinguished by its more compact growth and narrower stems compared to other hawkweed species.
