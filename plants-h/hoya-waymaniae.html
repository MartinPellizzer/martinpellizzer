Hoya waymaniae

Hoya waymaniae, commonly known as the "Coral Hoya," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Hoya waymaniae is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
