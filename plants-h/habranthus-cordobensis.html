Habranthus cordobensis

Habranthus cordobensis, commonly known as the Cordoba grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly Argentina. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
