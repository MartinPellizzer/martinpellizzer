Haloragis jerosoides

Haloragis jerosoides, commonly known as the Jerosa grass.

It is a flowering plant native to Australia, typically found in coastal and sandy habitats. The plant features wiry, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It is adapted to saline and arid conditions, making it resilient to harsh environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of coastal ecosystems.
