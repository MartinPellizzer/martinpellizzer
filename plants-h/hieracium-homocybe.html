Hieracium homocybe

Hieracium homocybe, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Hieracium. This plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in meadows and along roadsides.
