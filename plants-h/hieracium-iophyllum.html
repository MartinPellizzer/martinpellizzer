Hieracium iophyllum

Hieracium iophyllum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and grasslands. It has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium iophyllum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
