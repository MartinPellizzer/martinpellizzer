Hechtia pretiosa

Hechtia pretiosa, commonly known as the showy hechtia, is a striking plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescences that range from red to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its rosette-shaped leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in arid conditions.

Hechtia pretiosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant.
