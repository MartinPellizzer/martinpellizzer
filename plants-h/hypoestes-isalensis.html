Hypoestes isalensis

Hypoestes isalensis, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a strikingly colorful flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

Hypoestes isalensis thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
