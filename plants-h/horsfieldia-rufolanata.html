Horsfieldia rufolanata

Horsfieldia rufolanata, commonly known as the red-lanate orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Horsfieldia rufolanata is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
