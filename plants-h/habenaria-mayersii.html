Habenaria mayersii

Habenaria mayersii, commonly known as the Meyer's butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich soil. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding substrate.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
