Hieracium vagum f. croceostylum

Hieracium vagum f. croceostylum, commonly known as the yellow-hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the hawkweed genus. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It produces small, elongated leaves and has a creeping growth habit.

The inflorescence consists of ray flowers that are yellow in color, giving the plant its distinctive appearance.
