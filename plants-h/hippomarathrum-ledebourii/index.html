Hippomarathrum Ledebourii

Hippomarathrum ledebourii is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of southern California and northern Baja California. Known for its unique appearance, it features vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This species is often found in rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in harsh, dry conditions. Due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a threatened species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable plant for future generations.
