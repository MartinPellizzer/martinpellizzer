Haworthia marlothiana

Haworthia marlothiana, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for dry climates.

Haworthia marlothiana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
