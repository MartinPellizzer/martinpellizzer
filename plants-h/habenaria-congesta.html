Habenaria congesta

Habenaria congesta, commonly known as the clustered helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, greenish-brown flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
