Hieracium elongaticeps

Hieracium elongaticeps, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its elongated, upright stems and yellowish to reddish flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium elongaticeps is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
