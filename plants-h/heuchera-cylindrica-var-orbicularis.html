Heuchera cylindrica var. orbicularis

Heuchera cylindrica var. orbicularis, commonly known as the Roundleaf alumroot, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that range in color from deep burgundy to silver-green, depending on the variety. The plant produces upright, cylindrical flower spikes in shades of pink or white during late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental foliage and delicate flowers make it a valuable addition to shade gardens.
