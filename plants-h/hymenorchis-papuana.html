Hymenorchis papuana

Hymenorchis papuana, commonly known as the Papuan twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. The plant features a slender, grass-like stem with a single, elongated leaf. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish or white in color, and are arranged in a loose spike.

Hymenorchis papuana is native to New Guinea and is characterized by its unique floral structure and limited distribution.
