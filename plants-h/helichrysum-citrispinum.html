Helichrysum citrispinum

Helichrysum citrispinum, commonly known as the lemon goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum citrispinum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
