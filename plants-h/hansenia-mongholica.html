Hansenia mongholica

Hansenia mongholica, commonly known as the Mongolian bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the grasslands of Mongolia and northern China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Hansenia mongholica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
