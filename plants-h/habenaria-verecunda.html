Habenaria verecunda

Habenaria verecunda, commonly known as the veiled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a distinctive veined pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
