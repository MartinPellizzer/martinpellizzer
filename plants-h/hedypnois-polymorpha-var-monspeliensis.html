Hedypnois polymorpha var. monspeliensis

Hedypnois polymorpha var. monspeliensis, commonly known as the Mediterranean stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its resilience and ornamental value contribute to its widespread cultivation in Mediterranean climates.
