Hieracium riumarense

Hieracium riumarense, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosettes of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in a single head, which are surrounded by bracts. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

Hieracium riumarense is adapted to cold climates and can tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
