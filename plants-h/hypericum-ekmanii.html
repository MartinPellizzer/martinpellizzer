Hypericum ekmanii

Hypericum ekmanii, commonly known as the Ekman's St. John's Wort, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
