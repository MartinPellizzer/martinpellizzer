Hypericum vesiculosum

Hypericum vesiculosum, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer.

It is characterized by its yellow stems and is often found in woodland edges and dry meadows.
