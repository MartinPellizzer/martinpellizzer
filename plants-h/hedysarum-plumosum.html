Hedysarum plumosum

Hedysarum plumosum, commonly known as the silky vetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is a perennial herb that produces delicate, feathery leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and rocky habitats. Its flowers are typically visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Hedysarum plumosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
