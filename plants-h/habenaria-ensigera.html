Habenaria ensigera

Habenaria ensigera, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive purple or brown spotting on the petals. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Habenaria ensigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
