Hoya soidaoensis

Hoya soidaoensis, commonly known as the Dragon's Breath Hoya, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hoya soidaoensis is popular among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
