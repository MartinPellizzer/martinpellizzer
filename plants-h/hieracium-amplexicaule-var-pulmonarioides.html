Hieracium amplexicaule var. pulmonarioides

Hieracium amplexicaule var. pulmonarioides, commonly known as the lungwort hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, lobed leaves that often have a bluish-green hue. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
