Hypoestes aristata var. insularis

Hypoestes aristata var. insularis, commonly known as the flame violet, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features striking, velvety, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which are borne on short, upright stalks. The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
