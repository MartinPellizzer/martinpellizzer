Hypericum beanii

Hypericum beanii, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features yellow flowers with four petals, often appearing in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum beanii is part of the genus Hypericum, which includes many species known for their medicinal properties.
