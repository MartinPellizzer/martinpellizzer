Habenaria cirrhifera

Habenaria cirrhifera, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, hair-like roots and its tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a distinctive, fragrant scent.

Habenaria cirrhifera is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
