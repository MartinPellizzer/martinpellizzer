Hieracium radians

Hieracium radians, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, including North America. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become a weed in agricultural and natural areas.
