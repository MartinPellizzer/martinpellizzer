Huernia engleri

Huernia engleri, commonly known as the horned African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often ribbed and covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its stems and leaves.

Huernia engleri is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
