Helichrysum mossamedense

Helichrysum mossamedense, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers, which retain their color even when dried.
