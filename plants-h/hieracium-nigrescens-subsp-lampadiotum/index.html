Hieracium Nigrescens Subsp. Lampadiotum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp. lampadiotum is a lesser-known member of the daisy family known for its unique appearance and ecological significance. This subspecies is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers and hairy foliage, which help it thrive in shaded, moist environments. Native to specific regions of Europe, it is often found in woodlands and along stream banks. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and rarely cultivated. Understanding its habitat and biology is key to its conservation and appreciation.
