Hieracium subgalbanum

Hieracium subgalbanum, commonly known as the subgalbanum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. It features basal leaves that are usually lanceolate and covered in fine hairs.

Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat, disc-like structure typical of the Asteraceae family.
