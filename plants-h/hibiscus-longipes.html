Hibiscus longipes

Hibiscus longipes, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces long, slender pods that contain seeds. Hibiscus longipes thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
