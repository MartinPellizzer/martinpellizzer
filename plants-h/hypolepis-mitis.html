Hypolepis mitis

Hypolepis mitis, commonly known as the silver fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate fronds that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and swampy areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The silver fern is also popular as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
