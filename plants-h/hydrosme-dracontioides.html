Hydrosme dracontioides

Hydrosme dracontioides, commonly known as the dragon's tongue fern, is a unique tropical plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated fronds that resemble the shape of a dragon's tongue, hence its common name. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Botanically, it is notable for its intricate vascular system and ability to retain moisture, adapting well to moist, low-light conditions.
