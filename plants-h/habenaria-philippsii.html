Habenaria philippsii

Habenaria philippsii, commonly known as the Philippine butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often in mountainous regions. Its pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to survive in humid, tropical climates.

The flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that resemble butterfly wings.
