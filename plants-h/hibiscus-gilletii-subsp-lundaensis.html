Hibiscus gilletii subsp. lundaensis

Hibiscus gilletii subsp.

lundaensis, commonly known as the Lunda hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to central Africa. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red.

The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, and it is often found in forested areas or along riverbanks. Hibiscus gilletii subsp.

lundaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its beauty.
