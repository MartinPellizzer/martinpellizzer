Hibiscus henriquesii

Hibiscus henriquesii, commonly known as the Henriques hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces fruit that is a capsule containing numerous seeds.

Hibiscus henriquesii thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
