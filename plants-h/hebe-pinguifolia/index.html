Hebe Pinguifolia

Hebe pinguifolia is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of New Zealand. Known for its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of vibrant blue or purple flowers, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and hardy nature. It prefers partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings. Despite its hardiness, it requires protection from harsh winds and extreme heat.
