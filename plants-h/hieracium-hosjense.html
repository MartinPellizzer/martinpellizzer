Hieracium hosjense

Hieracium hosjense, commonly known as the Hosjens hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium hosjense is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
