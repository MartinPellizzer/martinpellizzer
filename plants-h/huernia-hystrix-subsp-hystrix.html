Huernia hystrix subsp. hystrix

Huernia hystrix subsp.

hystrix, commonly known as the spiny moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its swollen, ribbed stems that store water, and its fleshy, spiny leaves that reduce water loss.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
