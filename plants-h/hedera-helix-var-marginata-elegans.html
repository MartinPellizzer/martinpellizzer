Hedera helix var. marginata-elegans

Hedera helix var. marginata-elegans, commonly known as the Variegated Ivy, is a cultivar of the common ivy plant characterized by its striking variegated foliage.

The leaves are typically a mix of white, cream, and green, creating a visually appealing contrast. It is a climbing plant with tendrils that allow it to attach to surfaces and grow vertically. The plant thrives in partial shade to full shade and prefers well-draining soil.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to cover walls or trellises.
