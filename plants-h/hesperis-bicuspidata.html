Hesperis bicuspidata

Hesperis bicuspidata, commonly known as the two-lobed honesty, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many mustard plants. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hesperis bicuspidata is valued in gardens for its delicate appearance and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
