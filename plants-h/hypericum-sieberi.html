Hypericum sieberi

Hypericum sieberi, commonly known as the Sieber's St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features yellow flowers with five petals and prominent yellow stamens, blooming in late summer. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves and a woody stem, typical of the Hypericaceae family. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.
