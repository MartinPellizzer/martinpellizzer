Habenaria hosokawae

Habenaria hosokawae, commonly known as the Hosokawa's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
