Heterodon fragarioides

Heterodon fragarioides, commonly known as the strawberry geranium, is a flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that resemble strawberries, hence its common name. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in various growing conditions.
