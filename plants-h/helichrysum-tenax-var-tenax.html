Helichrysum tenax var. tenax

Helichrysum tenax var. tenax, commonly known as the everlasting or golden everlasting, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its production of small, bright yellow to golden-orange flowers. The plant is known for its ability to retain its color and form when dried, making it popular in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

The leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, with a slightly hairy texture.
