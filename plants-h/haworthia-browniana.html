Haworthia browniana

Haworthia browniana, commonly known as the Brown's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, allowing the plant to survive in arid conditions. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall stems during the warmer months.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
