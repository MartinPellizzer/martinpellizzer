Hieracium magnum

Hieracium magnum, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and fields. It has hairy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color.

Hieracium magnum is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in certain environments.
