Haemanthus sacculus

Haemanthus sacculus, commonly known as the red-stemmed haemanthus, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a distinctive red or purple stem with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
