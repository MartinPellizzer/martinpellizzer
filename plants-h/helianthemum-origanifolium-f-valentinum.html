Helianthemum origanifolium f. valentinum

Helianthemum origanifolium f. valentinum, commonly known as the Valentine's Rockrose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
