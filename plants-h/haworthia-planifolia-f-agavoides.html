Haworthia planifolia f. agavoides

Haworthia planifolia f. agavoides, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with distinctive white or pale yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
