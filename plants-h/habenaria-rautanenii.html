Habenaria rautanenii

Habenaria rautanenii, commonly known as the Rautanen's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with three sepals and three petals, and it produces small, round seed pods.

Habenaria rautanenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
