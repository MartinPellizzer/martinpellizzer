Hieracium rodnense

Hieracium rodnense, commonly known as the Rodnian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats and has a creeping growth habit. Its yellowish flowers bloom in late summer, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

Hieracium rodnense is often found in mountainous areas and is adapted to cooler, temperate climates.
