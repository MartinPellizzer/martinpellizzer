Hordeum pseudomurinum

Hordeum pseudomurinum, commonly known as false barley, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial species native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Europe. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Hordeum pseudomurinum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in coastal areas.
