Hibiscus rosa-sinensis aurantia-pleno

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis aurantia-pleno, commonly known as the orange-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically a vibrant orange or red in color. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It blooms throughout the year in warm climates, producing intricate flowers with five petals and a prominent central pistil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
