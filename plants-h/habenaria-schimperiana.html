Habenaria schimperiana

Habenaria schimperiana, commonly known as the Schimper's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
