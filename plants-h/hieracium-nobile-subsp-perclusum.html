Hieracium nobile subsp. perclusum

Hieracium nobile subsp.

perclusum, commonly known as the noble hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant features basal and alternate leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape, with serrated margins. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
