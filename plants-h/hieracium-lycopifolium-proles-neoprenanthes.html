Hieracium lycopifolium proles neoprenanthes

Hieracium lycopifolium proles neoprenanthes, commonly known as the creeping hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with dark green, hairy leaves that are often lobed or toothed. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and wetlands.
