Hieracium tremulum

Hieracium tremulum, commonly known as the trembling hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, serrated leaves. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant's common name refers to the trembling motion of its leaves when touched.

It is often considered a weed but plays a role in supporting pollinators.
