Haworthia pumila var. smitii

Haworthia pumila var. smitii, commonly known as the Smit's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
