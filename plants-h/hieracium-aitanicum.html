Hieracium aitanicum

Hieracium aitanicum, commonly known as the Aitanic Hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-orange composite flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium aitanicum is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
