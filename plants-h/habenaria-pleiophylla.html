Habenaria pleiophylla

Habenaria pleiophylla, commonly known as the many-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and numerous, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow or greenish-white flowers, which are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Habenaria pleiophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
