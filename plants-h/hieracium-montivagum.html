Hieracium montivagum

Hieracium montivagum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosettes of lanceolate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a single head, typical of the daisy family. It is often found in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in rocky and well-drained soils.

Hieracium montivagum is adapted to cold climates and is often seen in mountainous areas across Europe and parts of Asia.
