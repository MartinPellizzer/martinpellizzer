Hieracium Levicaule Subsp. Subsimile

Hieracium levicaule subsp. subsimile is a lesser-known member of the hawkweed genus found in northern regions of europe. it is characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. this plant often grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. due to its similarity to other hawkweed species, it is frequently misidentified. its ecological role and conservation status remain understudied, making it an interesting subject for further botanical research.
