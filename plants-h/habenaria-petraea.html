Habenaria petraea

Habenaria petraea, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to various regions in Asia and Africa.

It typically grows on rocky substrates and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system adapted to nutrient-poor environments, and its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its ability to thrive in challenging, rocky habitats.

The species is often found in mountainous areas and is valued for its unique floral structure and ecological significance.
