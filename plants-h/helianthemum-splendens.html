Helianthemum splendens

Helianthemum splendens, commonly known as the splendour rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with dense, evergreen foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas. Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Helianthemum splendens is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
