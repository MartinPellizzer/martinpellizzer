Hieracium subglomeratulum

Hieracium subglomeratulum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its basal leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while the stem is usually sparingly hairy.

The flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, with both ray and disk florets.
