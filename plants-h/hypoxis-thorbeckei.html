Hypoxis thorbeckei

Hypoxis thorbeckei, commonly known as the African starwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its star-shaped flowers and grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Hypoxis thorbeckei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
