Hieracium cynoglossoides var. ursinum

Hieracium cynoglossoides var. ursinum, commonly known as the brown yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish to brownish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.
