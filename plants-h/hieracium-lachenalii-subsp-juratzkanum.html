Hieracium lachenalii subsp. juratzkanum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

juratzkanum, commonly known as the Juratzkanum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes.

The plant features bright yellow composite flowers arranged in a head, with both ray and disc florets. Its leaves are usually basal, lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is characterized by its small size and adaptability to arid environments.
