Hypoxis minor

Hypoxis minor, commonly known as the star-shaped starwort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine.

Hypoxis minor is also valued as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
