Hieracium diversidens

Hieracium diversidens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces numerous small, clustered flowers. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium diversidens is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
