Hemiboea purpureotincta

Hemiboea purpureotincta, commonly known as the purple-stained hemiboea, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces vibrant purple to pink flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine, velvety texture.
