Hieracium mallocephalum

Hieracium mallocephalum, commonly known as the broad-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium mallocephalum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
