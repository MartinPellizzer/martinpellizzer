Haworthia schuldtiana var. sublaevis

Haworthia schuldtiana var. sublaevis, commonly known as the White Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, pale green to white leaves that are often tinged with red or pink at the tips. The plant produces small, pale green flowers on slender stems during the spring or summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
