Holarrhena glaberrima

Holarrhena glaberrima, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to the savannas of sub-Saharan Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing elliptical seeds.
