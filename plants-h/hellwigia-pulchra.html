Hellwigia pulchra

Hellwigia pulchra, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a striking tropical perennial native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple to red-colored stems, which are covered in a waxy, powdery coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by winged seeds. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Hellwigia pulchra thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
