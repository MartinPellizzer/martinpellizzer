Hieracium exaltans

Hieracium exaltans, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium exaltans is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it an invasive species in some regions.
