Hieracium stellipilum

Hieracium stellipilum, commonly known as the star-hair hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically hairy foliage. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are usually covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowering stems bear clusters of small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer.

Hieracium stellipilum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
