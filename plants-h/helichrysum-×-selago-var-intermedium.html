Helichrysum × selago var. intermedium

Helichrysum × selago var. intermedium, commonly known as the everlasting or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces papery, dry flower heads that persist through winter, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
