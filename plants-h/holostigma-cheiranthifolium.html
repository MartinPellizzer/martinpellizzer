Holostigma cheiranthifolium

Holostigma cheiranthifolium, commonly known as the rock cress, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with small, four-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh, arid environments. Its leaves are basal, pinnately divided, and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.
