Hypericum × intermedium

Hypericum × intermedium, commonly known as the hybrid St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features woody stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually yellow-green in color. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are followed by orange-red capsules containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used historically for its purported antidepressant effects.
