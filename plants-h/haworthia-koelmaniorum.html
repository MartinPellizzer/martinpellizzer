Haworthia koelmaniorum

Haworthia koelmaniorum, commonly known as the Koelman's Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette, often with a bluish or grayish hue and white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall, leafless stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
