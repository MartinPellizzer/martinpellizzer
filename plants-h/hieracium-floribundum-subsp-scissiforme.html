Hieracium floribundum subsp. scissiforme

Hieracium floribundum subsp.

scissiforme, commonly known as the cutleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
