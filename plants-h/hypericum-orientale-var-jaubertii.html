Hypericum orientale var. jaubertii

Hypericum orientale var. jaubertii, commonly known as the Eastern St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to parts of Asia and the Middle East.

It features bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
