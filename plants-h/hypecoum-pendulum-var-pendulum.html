Hypecoum pendulum var. pendulum

Hypecoum pendulum var. pendulum, commonly known as the hanging water spinach, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions.

It features striking, pendulous inflorescences that resemble delicate, drooping flowers. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and water feature landscapes.
