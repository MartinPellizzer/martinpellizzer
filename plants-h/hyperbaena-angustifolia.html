Hyperbaena angustifolia

Hyperbaena angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf hyperbaena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Hyperbaena angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
