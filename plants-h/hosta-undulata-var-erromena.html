Hosta undulata var. erromena

Hosta undulata var. erromena, commonly known as the Erromena wavyleaf plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to Japan.

It features broad, wavy-edged leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-blue hue on the underside. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its unique foliage and is a popular choice for shade gardens.
