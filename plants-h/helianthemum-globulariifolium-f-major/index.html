Helianthemum Globulariifolium F. Major

Helianthemum globulariifolium f. major is a rare and striking plant native to the rocky landscapes of southern Europe. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is known for its compact, spherical growth habit. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the summer months, adding color to its arid environment. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. This fascinating species highlights the adaptability of flora in extreme conditions.
