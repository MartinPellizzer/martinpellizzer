Hyphaene crinita

Hyphaene crinita, commonly known as the date palm, is a tall, slender tree native to southern Africa.

It has long, pinnate leaves that can reach up to 6 meters in length. The plant produces large, clustered inflorescences that eventually develop into edible dates. Its trunk is fibrous and covered with a thick layer of leaf sheaths.

Hyphaene crinita is well adapted to arid environments and is often used for its fruit and ornamental value.
