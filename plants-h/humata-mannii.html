Humata mannii

Humata mannii, commonly known as the Malagasy sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with upright stems and hairy leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in dry, open habitats.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the flora of Madagascar.
