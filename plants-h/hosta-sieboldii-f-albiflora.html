Hosta sieboldii f. albiflora

Hosta sieboldii f. albiflora, commonly known as the Whiteflower Siebold Hosta, is a cultivar characterized by its striking white to pale yellow flowers.

It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to Japan. The plant features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens.

The flowers appear in late summer and are borne on tall, slender stems.
