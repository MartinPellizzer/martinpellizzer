Hypericum balcanicum

Hypericum balcanicum, commonly known as the Balkan St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or grassy areas.

Hypericum balcanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
