Hermione × tripartita

Hermione × tripartita, commonly known as the hybrid ginger plant, is a cross between two species of Zingiberaceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and produces clusters of fragrant blooms. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a smooth, glossy texture.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
