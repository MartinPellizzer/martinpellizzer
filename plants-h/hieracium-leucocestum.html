Hieracium leucocestum

Hieracium leucocestum, commonly known as the white-centred hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a white central disk, giving it its common name. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
