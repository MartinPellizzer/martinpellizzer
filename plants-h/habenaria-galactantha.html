Habenaria galactantha

Habenaria galactantha, commonly known as the white-tongued orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces striking white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
