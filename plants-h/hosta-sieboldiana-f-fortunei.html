Hosta sieboldiana f. fortunei

Hosta sieboldiana f. fortunei, commonly known as the Fortune's Siebold's Hosta, is a variegated cultivar of the Hosta genus.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a striking combination of light green and white variegation. The plant is native to East Asia and thrives in shady, moist environments. It produces small, fragrant white flowers in late summer.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various garden conditions.
