Hieracium euryodon

Hieracium euryodon, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has basal leaves that are typically lobed or toothed, and its stems are usually hairy. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny areas.
