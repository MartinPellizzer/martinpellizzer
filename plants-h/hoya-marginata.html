Hoya marginata, commonly known as the wax plant or sweetheart plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers. Hoya marginata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
