Hieracium cymosum subsp. dingleri

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

dingleri, commonly known as the Dingler's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant features basal rosettes of lanceolate leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late spring to summer. It has a fibrous root system and is characterized by its compact growth habit.

Botanically, it is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads.
