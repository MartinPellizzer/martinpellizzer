Hypecoum pendulum subsp. ponticum

Hypecoum pendulum subsp.

ponticum, commonly known as the Pontic sweet pea, is a herbaceous perennial native to southeastern Europe. It features slender, climbing stems that produce clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers.

The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, often found in disturbed habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
