Homalomena coerulescens var. pontederifolia

Homalomena coerulescens var. pontederifolia, commonly known as the blue spider-orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green foliage and delicate, spider-like flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It prefers warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
