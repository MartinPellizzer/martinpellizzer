Hypericum canariense var. salicifolium

Hypericum canariense var. salicifolium, commonly known as the Canary Island St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with prominent stamens, typical of the Hypericaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
