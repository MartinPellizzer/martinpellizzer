Hieracium prionocerinthe

Hieracium prionocerinthe, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, with a rosette growth habit.

The flowers are arranged in a single head, and the plant is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and seeds.
