Horkelia californica subsp. dissita

Horkelia californica subsp.

dissita, commonly known as the Sierra horkelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a low-growing perennial herb with hairy stems and basal leaves.

The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in a loose cluster. It thrives in rocky soils and open habitats, often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to the harsh conditions of its native environment.
