Hippeastrum flavissimum

Hippeastrum flavissimum, commonly known as the yellow hurricane lily, is a species of amaryllid that originates from the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are broad and sturdy, providing a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms. Its bulbs are typically large and store energy for the flowering cycle.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as potted plants.
