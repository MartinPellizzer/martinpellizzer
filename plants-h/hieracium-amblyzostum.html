Hieracium amblyzostum

Hieracium amblyzostum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are hairy and lobed, while its flowers consist of both ray and disk florets, often in shades of yellow or orange.

It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
