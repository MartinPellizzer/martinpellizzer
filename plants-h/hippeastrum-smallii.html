Hippeastrum smallii

Hippeastrum smallii, commonly known as the smallii amaryllis, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are typically broad and glossy. It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hippeastrum smallii thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.
