Habenaria gracilis

Habenaria gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are elongated and often clustered at the base of the stem.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its intricate floral structure and adaptability to humid climates.
