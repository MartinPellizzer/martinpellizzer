Habenaria hookeriana

Habenaria hookeriana, commonly known as Hooker's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its inflorescence is usually a raceme of small, showy blooms.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
