Habenaria longicalcarata

Habenaria longicalcarata, commonly known as the long-spurred orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with elongated spurs, which are a distinctive botanical feature. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral structure.
