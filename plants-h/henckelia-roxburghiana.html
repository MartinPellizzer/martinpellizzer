Henckelia roxburghiana

Henckelia roxburghiana, commonly known as the Himalayan rose.

It is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas, characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
