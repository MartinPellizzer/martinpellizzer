Hieracium nigrescens subsp. eucalpideum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

eucalpideum, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy, basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has small, oval leaves with a rough texture and is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils.

The subspecies eucalpideum is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology compared to other members of the species.
