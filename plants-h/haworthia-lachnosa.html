Haworthia lachnosa

Haworthia lachnosa, commonly known as the woolly Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped foliage that is covered in dense, white, woolly hairs. The leaves are typically green with a bluish tinge and have a textured surface. It produces small, white or yellowish flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
