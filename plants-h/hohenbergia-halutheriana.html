Hohenbergia halutheriana

Hohenbergia halutheriana, commonly known as the silver stripe hohenbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white or silver hair, giving it a frosty appearance. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright red or orange flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hohenbergia halutheriana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
