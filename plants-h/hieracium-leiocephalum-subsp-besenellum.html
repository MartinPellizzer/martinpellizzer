Hieracium leiocephalum subsp. besenellum

Hieracium leiocephalum subsp.

besenellum, commonly known as the narrow-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers.

The plant typically features basal leaves that are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, composite flowers arranged in a head, with both ray and disc florets.

This subspecies is often found in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.
