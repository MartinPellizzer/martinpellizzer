Hypericum × arnoldianum

Hypericum × arnoldianum, commonly known as the golden st. john's wort, is a hybrid shrub native to the United Kingdom.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes various species of flowering plants in the family Clusiaceae. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August.
