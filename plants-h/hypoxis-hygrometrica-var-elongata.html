Hypoxis hygrometrica var. elongata

Hypoxis hygrometrica var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated rain lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its grass-like leaves and clusters of yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. It is often found in forested areas and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
