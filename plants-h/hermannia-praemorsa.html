Hermannia praemorsa

Hermannia praemorsa, commonly known as the bloodroot, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has a tuberous root system. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, and it is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Hermannia praemorsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
