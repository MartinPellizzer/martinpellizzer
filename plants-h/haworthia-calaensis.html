Haworthia calaensis

Haworthia calaensis, commonly known as the Cape Town Haworthia, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, grass-like leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
