Habenaria ankylocentron

Habenaria ankylocentron, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces spikes of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive zigzag pattern on the petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
