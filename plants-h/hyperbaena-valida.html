Hyperbaena valida

Hyperbaena valida, commonly known as the golden bush, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Hyperbaena valida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
