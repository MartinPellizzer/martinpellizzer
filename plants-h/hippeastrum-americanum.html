Hippeastrum americanum

Hippeastrum americanum, commonly known as the Brazilian peace lily, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and pointed at the tips. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hippeastrum americanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular houseplant.
