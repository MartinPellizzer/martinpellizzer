Heteropogon grandiflorus

Heteropogon grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered grass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and coastal areas.

Heteropogon grandiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
