Helicteres furfuracea

Helicteres furfuracea, commonly known as the hairy helicteres, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by follicles containing numerous seeds.
