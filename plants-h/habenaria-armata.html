Habenaria armata

Habenaria armata, commonly known as the armed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Habenaria armata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
