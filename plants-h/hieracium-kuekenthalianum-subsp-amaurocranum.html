Hieracium kuekenthalianum subsp. amaurocranum

Hieracium kuekenthalianum subsp.

amaurocranum, commonly known as the dark-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its dark green, often hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads.
