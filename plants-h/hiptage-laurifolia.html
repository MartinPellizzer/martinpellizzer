Hiptage laurifolia

Hiptage laurifolia, commonly known as the flame of the forest, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant has lance-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green and emit a pleasant fragrance. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Hiptage laurifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
