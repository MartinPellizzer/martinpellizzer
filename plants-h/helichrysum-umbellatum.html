Helichrysum umbellatum

Helichrysum umbellatum, commonly known as the African daisies, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are often arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is typically found in open grasslands and rocky areas. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

The flowers are known for their long-lasting appearance, making it a popular choice for dried floral arrangements.
