Habenaria hennigiana

Habenaria hennigiana, commonly known as the Hennig's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with prominent purple or brown markings, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its ecological adaptations and potential for hybridization with other orchid species.
