Haworthia pumila subsp. attenuata

Haworthia pumila subsp.

attenuata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or translucent markings.

The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
