Hypericum neapolitanum var. confertum

Hypericum neapolitanum var. confertum, commonly known as the Naples St. John's Wort, is a compact shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typical of the Hypericaceae family. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves and produces orange-red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowering display and hardy nature.
