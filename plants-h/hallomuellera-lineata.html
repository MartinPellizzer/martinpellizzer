Hallomuellera lineata

Hallomuellera lineata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils in open woodlands and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged alternately along the stem.
