Hypericum ekeri

Hypericum ekeri, commonly known as the Ekeri St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features woody stems and small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its bright floral display and hardy nature.
