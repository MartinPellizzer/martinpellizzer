Hieracium pusilliceps

Hieracium pusilliceps, commonly known as the small-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium pusilliceps is often considered a weed due to its rapid spread and ability to colonize various habitats.
