Hieracium juranum subsp. elegantissimum

Hieracium juranum subsp.

elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single inflorescence at the end of each stem.

The plant is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It has lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces and exhibits a compact growth habit.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
