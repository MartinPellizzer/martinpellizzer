Heterotheca utahensis

Heterotheca utahensis, commonly known as Utah tansy, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has hairy, linear leaves. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Utah tansy is often found in arid regions and is adapted to survive in harsh environmental conditions.
