Hesperis odorata

Hesperis odorata, commonly known as the sweet rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces showy clusters of bright pink or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hesperis odorata is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant, spicy fragrance.
