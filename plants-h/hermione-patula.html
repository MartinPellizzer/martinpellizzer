Hermione patula

Hermione patula, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features striking silver-white leaves and bright yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
