Helictotrichon filifolium subsp. arundanum

Helictotrichon filifolium subsp.

arundanum, commonly known as the slender bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America. It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a fine, silky texture.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland habitats.
