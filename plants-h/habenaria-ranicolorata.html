Habenaria ranicolorata

Habenaria ranicolorata, commonly known as the pink-streaked orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs covered in a fibrous sheath. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with distinctive streaks or spots. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in two rows along the stem.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
