Hieracium lachenalii subsp. irriguum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

irriguum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. It has small, linear leaves and produces a compact inflorescence with numerous small florets.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
