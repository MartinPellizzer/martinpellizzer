Hopea linggensis

Hopea linggensis, commonly known as the Borneo ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the island of Borneo.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree can grow up to 40 meters tall, with a straight trunk and broad, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small and yellowish, and it produces woody fruits that contain two to four seeds.

Hopea linggensis plays an important ecological role in Borneo's rainforests, providing habitat and contributing to the region's biodiversity.
