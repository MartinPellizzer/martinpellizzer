Hohenbergia ramageana

Hohenbergia ramageana, commonly known as the Ramage's hohenbergia, is a compact bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features rosettes of narrow, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings. The plant produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences with red or orange bracts. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.
