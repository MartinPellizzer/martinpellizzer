Hieracium polyphyllum

Hieracium polyphyllum, commonly known as the many-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of western Asia.

Hieracium polyphyllum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
