Hydrobryopsis

Hydrobryopsis, commonly known as the water gemmule, is a type of moss that belongs to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth form and ability to survive in moist, shaded environments. Its simple morphology and ecological adaptability make it a common component of many moist, temperate habitats.
