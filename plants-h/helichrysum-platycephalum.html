Helichrysum platycephalum

Helichrysum platycephalum, commonly known as the flat-headed everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact inflorescences that resemble small, flat heads, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers that are highly ornamental and often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum platycephalum is also valued for its aromatic foliage and drought tolerance.
