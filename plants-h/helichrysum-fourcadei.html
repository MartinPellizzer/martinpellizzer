Helichrysum fourcadei

Helichrysum fourcadei, commonly known as the Cape goldflax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that resemble small sunflowers. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms that retain their color even when dried.
