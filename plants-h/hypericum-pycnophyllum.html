Hypericum pycnophyllum

Hypericum pycnophyllum, commonly known as the denseleaf St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, bright yellow flowers. The plant features opposite, narrow, and often hairy leaves that contribute to its dense foliage. It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
