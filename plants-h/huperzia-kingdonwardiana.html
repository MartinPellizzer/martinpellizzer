Huperzia kingdonwardiana

Huperzia kingdonwardiana, commonly known as the Kingdonward's huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and distinctive, elongated sporophylls. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant's rhizome is thick and often branched, supporting its upright growth habit.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies.
