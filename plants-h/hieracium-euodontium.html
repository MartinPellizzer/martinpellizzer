Hieracium euodontium

Hieracium euodontium, commonly known as the white hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows and grasslands. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of smaller, inconspicuous disk florets. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough, hairy texture.

Hieracium euodontium is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
