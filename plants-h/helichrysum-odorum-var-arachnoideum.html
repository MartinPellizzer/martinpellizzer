Helichrysum odorum var. arachnoideum

Helichrysum odorum var. arachnoideum, commonly known as the spider bush, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that resembles spider webs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its aromatic leaves and drought tolerance make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
