Hidalgoa wercklei var. colombiana

Hidalgoa wercklei var. colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian hidalgoa, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and is adapted to the cool, moist climates of its native habitat. This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
