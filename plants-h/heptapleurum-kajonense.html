Heptapleurum kajonense

Heptapleurum kajonense, commonly known as Kajonese alder.

It is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
