Hieracium cirritum subsp. pravum

Hieracium cirritum subsp.

pravum, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright purple to reddish flowers and is native to parts of Europe and Asia.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It has basal leaves that are often hairy and can reach up to 30 cm in height.

The subspecies pravum is distinguished by its more robust growth and distinct flower coloration compared to other subspecies of Hieracium cirritum.
