Holothrix squamulosa var. glabrata

Holothrix squamulosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth leaf holothrix, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in small, scale-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique foliage and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
