Hieracium × hauense

Hieracium × hauense, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and flowering heads that produce yellow ray florets. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides. It is native to Europe but has been introduced to other regions, including North America.

Hieracium × hauense is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in agricultural and natural areas.
