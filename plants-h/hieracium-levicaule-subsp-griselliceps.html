Hieracium levicaule subsp. griselliceps

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

griselliceps, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has finely divided, grass-like leaves and produces achenes as its seeds.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in temperate regions.
