Hieracium asymmetricum

Hieracium asymmetricum, commonly known as the asymmetric hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium asymmetricum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
