Haplopappus carthamoides subsp. rigidus

Haplopappus carthamoides subsp.

rigidus, commonly known as rigid goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous root system.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, upright leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense, disc-like clusters.

The subspecies is distinguished by its stiff, rigid leaves and compact growth form.
