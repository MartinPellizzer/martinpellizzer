Hymenachne condensata

Hymenachne condensata, commonly known as the dense hairgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Hymenachne condensata plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
