Haworthia intermedia var. notabilis

Haworthia intermedia var. notabilis, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves with distinctive white to yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
