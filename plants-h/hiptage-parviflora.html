Hiptage parviflora

Hiptage parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered hiptage, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.

Hiptage parviflora is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
