Hieracium prasinochroum

Hieracium prasinochroum, commonly known as the green-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands, often forming dense colonies.

Hieracium prasinochroum is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through its rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
