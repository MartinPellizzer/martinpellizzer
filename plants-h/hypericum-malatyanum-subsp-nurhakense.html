Hypericum malatyanum subsp. nurhakense

Hypericum malatyanum subsp.

nurhakense, commonly known as the Nurhak yellowcrown, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Turkey. It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of flowering plants.

The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers with orange centers, which bloom in summer. It typically grows in rocky and dry habitats, showing a preference for well-drained soils.

Its foliage is dense and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
