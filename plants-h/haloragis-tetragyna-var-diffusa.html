Haloragis tetragyna var. diffusa

Haloragis tetragyna var. diffusa, commonly known as the coastal greenweed, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its succulent, often hairy stems and small, opposite leaves.
