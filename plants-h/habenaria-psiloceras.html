Habenaria psiloceras

Habenaria psiloceras, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple and white flower spikes. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that aids in pollination.

The botanical features of Habenaria psiloceras make it a notable species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
