Hosta lancifolia f. kifukurin

Hosta lancifolia f. kifukurin, commonly known as the Kifukurin hosta, is a varietal form of the lanceleaf hosta, characterized by its striking foliage.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-white margin, creating a dramatic visual contrast. The plant is native to Japan and is valued for its ornamental qualities in shaded garden environments. It grows to a height of about 30 to 40 centimeters and spreads slowly, forming a dense clump over time.

The leaves remain evergreen throughout the year, providing year-round interest in the garden.
