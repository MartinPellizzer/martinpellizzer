Habenaria loerzingii

Habenaria loerzingii, commonly known as the Loerzing's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces showy, spurred flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
