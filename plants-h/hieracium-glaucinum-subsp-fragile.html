Hieracium glaucinum subsp. fragile

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

fragile, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with slender stems and small, yellowish-green flowers.

The plant typically thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is often found in temperate regions of Europe and is adapted to a variety of soil types.
