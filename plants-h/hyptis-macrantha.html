Hyptis macrantha

Hyptis macrantha, commonly known as the purple hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and spikes of violet or purple flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Hyptis macrantha is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
