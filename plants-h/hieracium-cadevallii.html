Hieracium cadevallii

Hieracium cadevallii, commonly known as the Catalan hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces numerous small, daisy-like flowers.

Hieracium cadevallii is often found in areas with well-drained soil and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.
