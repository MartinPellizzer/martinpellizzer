Hypolytrum ensifolium

Hypolytrum ensifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf hypolytrum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically sword-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale yellow, arranged in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hypolytrum ensifolium is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and easy maintenance.
