Hordeum distichon convar. zeocriton

Hordeum distichon convar. zeocriton, commonly known as the slender barley, is a grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and compact inflorescences. The plant typically grows in low vegetation, often in disturbed or rocky soils. Its seeds are small and hardy, allowing it to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.

Botanically, it is notable for its adaptability and role in stabilizing soil in dry ecosystems.
