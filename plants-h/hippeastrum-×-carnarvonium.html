Hippeastrum × carnarvonium

Hippeastrum × carnarvonium, commonly known as the Carnarvon amaryllis, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces strap-like, evergreen leaves that are broad and stiff, forming a dense rosette. Its bulbous stem stores nutrients and water, supporting the plant's growth and flowering cycle.

Native to South America, it is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking floral display.
