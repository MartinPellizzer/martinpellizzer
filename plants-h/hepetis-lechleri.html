Hepetis lechleri

Hepetis lechleri, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is valued for its latex, which is harvested for producing natural rubber. The plant has large, broad leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in panicles, and it produces small, round fruits.

The rubber tree plays an important ecological role in its native habitat and is also economically significant for local communities.
