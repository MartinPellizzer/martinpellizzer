Habenaria diceras

Habenaria diceras, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped flower spikes that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

Habenaria diceras is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
