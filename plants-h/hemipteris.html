Hemipteris

Hemipteris, commonly known as the common spurge, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

The plants typically have milky sap, which can be toxic and cause skin irritation. Hemipteris species are often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and meadows.
