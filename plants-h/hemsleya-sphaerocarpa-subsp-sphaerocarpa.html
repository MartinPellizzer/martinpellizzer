Hemsleya sphaerocarpa subsp. sphaerocarpa

Hemsleya sphaerocarpa subsp.

sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globular orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae. It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often arranged in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous areas with rich, well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in specialty gardens.
