Homeria tenuis

Homeria tenuis, commonly known as the narrowleaf homeria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South Africa, where it typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
