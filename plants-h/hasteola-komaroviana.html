Hasteola komaroviana

Hasteola komaroviana, commonly known as Komarov's grass, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.
