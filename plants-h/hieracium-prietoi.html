Hieracium prietoi

Hieracium prietoi, commonly known as the Priet's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow composite flowers that are typically borne in dense clusters. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in grassy habitats and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
