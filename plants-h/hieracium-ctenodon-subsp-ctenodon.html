Hieracium ctenodon subsp. ctenodon

Hieracium ctenodon subsp.

ctenodon, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

The plant features dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It has a taproot system and is characterized by its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a food source for certain insects and its potential use in ecological restoration projects.
