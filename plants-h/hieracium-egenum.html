Hieracium egenum

Hieracium egenum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

Hieracium egenum is known for its ability to thrive in harsh, cold environments due to its low-growing habit and frost-resistant physiology.
