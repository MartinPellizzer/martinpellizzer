Hexostemon virgatus

Hexostemon virgatus, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, silvery-green foliage that gives it its common name. The plant has a dense, rounded canopy and produces small, pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Hexostemon virgatus thrives in warm, humid climates and is well-suited for tropical and subtropical gardens.
