Habenaria pentaglossa

Habenaria pentaglossa, commonly known as the five-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, nodding flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with purple or brown markings, and it produces small, rounded pseudobulbs.

Habenaria pentaglossa is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
