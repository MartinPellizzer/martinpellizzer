Hydrangea chinensis var. lobbii

Hydrangea chinensis var. lobbii, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea or the mountain hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink or blue, depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

Hydrangea chinensis var. lobbii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
