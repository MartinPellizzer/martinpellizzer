Hieracium subelegans

Hieracium subelegans, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal rosettes of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium subelegans is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
