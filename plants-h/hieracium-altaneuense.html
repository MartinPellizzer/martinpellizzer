Hieracium altaneuense

Hieracium altaneuense, commonly known as the Altaneuense hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-pink composite flowers. The plant typically features basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium altaneuense is native to southern Europe and is often found in regions with calcareous soils.
