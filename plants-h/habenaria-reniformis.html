Habenaria reniformis

Habenaria reniformis, commonly known as the kidney orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped flowers, which are typically greenish-yellow with purple or brown markings. The plant has a slender, upright stem and produces small, clustered flowers along its length. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a smooth texture and a waxy appearance.

Habenaria reniformis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
