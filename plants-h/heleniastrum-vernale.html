Heleniastrum vernale

Heleniastrum vernale, commonly known as the spring helene, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, small yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks.

Due to its early blooming habit, it plays an important role in providing nectar for early pollinators.
