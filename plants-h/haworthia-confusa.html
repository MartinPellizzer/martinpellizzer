Haworthia confusa

Haworthia confusa, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, fleshy, triangular leaves with white, vertical markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant typically grows up to 10 centimeters in height and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect sunlight and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Haworthia confusa is prized for its compact size and unique appearance, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
