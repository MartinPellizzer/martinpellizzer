Haworthia umbraticola var. obesa

Haworthia umbraticola var. obesa, commonly known as the Fat Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges, especially in full sun. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
