Helicia darlingiana

Helicia darlingiana, commonly known as the Darling's holly.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white, fragrant flowers in spring. It is well adapted to coastal and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its ecological role in local ecosystems.
