Hirculus diversifolius

Hirculus diversifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a creeping rhizome system that allows it to spread across the forest floor. The plant produces feathery, bipinnate fronds that are typically gray-green in color due to a dense covering of trichomes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Hirculus diversifolius is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
