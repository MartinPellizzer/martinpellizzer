Hedera helix var. argenteovariegata

Hedera helix var. argenteovariegata, commonly known as the silver-veined ivy, is a variegated cultivar of the common ivy.

It features striking foliage with a mix of light green and white or silver markings on its heart-shaped leaves. The plant is a climbing or trailing evergreen that thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. It has small, dark green flowers that appear in late summer, followed by black berries.

This variety is popular for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor and outdoor settings.
