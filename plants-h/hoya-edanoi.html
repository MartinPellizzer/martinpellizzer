Hoya edanoi

Hoya edanoi, commonly known as the "Lunar Hoya," is a member of the Apocynaceae family, native to the Philippines.

It is a trailing plant with thick, fleshy stems that produce waxy, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. The leaves are ovate, glossy, and often have a slight waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental foliage and unique flowering display.
