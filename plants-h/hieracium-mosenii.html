Hieracium mosenii

Hieracium mosenii, commonly known as Mosen's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disc florets. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a distinctive central disc.

Hieracium mosenii is often found in meadows and open slopes at high altitudes.
