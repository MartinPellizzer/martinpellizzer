Huttonaea oreophila var. grandiflora

Huttonaea oreophila var. grandiflora, commonly known as the mountain huttonaea, is a flowering plant native to specific high-altitude regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, yellow to orange ray flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.
