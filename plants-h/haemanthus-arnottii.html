Haemanthus arnottii

Haemanthus arnottii, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright spikes of bright orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is known for its showy, long-lasting blooms.

Haemanthus arnottii is relatively low maintenance and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.
