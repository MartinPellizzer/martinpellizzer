Habenaria perpulchra

Habenaria perpulchra, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually linear and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
