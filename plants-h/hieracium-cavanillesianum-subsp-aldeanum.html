Hieracium cavanillesianum subsp. aldeanum

Hieracium cavanillesianum subsp.

aldeanum, commonly known as the Aldeanum hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is generally not invasive and plays a minor role in local ecosystems.
