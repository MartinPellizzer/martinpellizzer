Hieracium subargenteum

Hieracium subargenteum, commonly known as silver hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-white hairs on its leaves and stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its growth habit is typically low and spreading.

Silver hawkweed is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, where it can form dense colonies.
