Homalomena hypsiantha

Homalomena hypsiantha, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking orange-red spathes that enclose its yellow-orange flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
