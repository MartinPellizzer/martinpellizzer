Hemerocallis × stoutara

Hemerocallis × stoutara, commonly known as the Stoutara Daylily, is a hybrid cultivar of the daylily genus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is a perennial herbaceous species with rhizomatous growth, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun. It produces multiple blooms over a short period, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant summer color.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming dense foliage that supports the flowering stems.
