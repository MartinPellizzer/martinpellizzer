Haworthiopsis granulata

Haworthiopsis granulata, commonly known as the Granite Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
