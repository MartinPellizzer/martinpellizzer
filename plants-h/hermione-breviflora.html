Hermione breviflora

Hermione breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered Hermione, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a compact rosette of narrow, silvery-gray leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, pale blue or violet flowers during the cooler months, typically from late autumn to early spring. It is well adapted to rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is often cultivated in xeriscapes and rock gardens.
