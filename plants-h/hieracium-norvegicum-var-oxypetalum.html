Hieracium norvegicum var. oxypetalum

Hieracium norvegicum var. oxypetalum, commonly known as the Norwegian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to northern Europe.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands, often in regions with cooler climates. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variant is distinguished by its slightly longer petals compared to other varieties within the Hieracium genus.
