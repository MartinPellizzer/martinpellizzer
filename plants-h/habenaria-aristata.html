Habenaria aristata

Habenaria aristata, commonly known as the noble orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Habenaria aristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
