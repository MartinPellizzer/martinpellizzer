Haworthia longibracteata

Haworthia longibracteata, commonly known as the Longibract Haworthia, is a compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with white or translucent markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
