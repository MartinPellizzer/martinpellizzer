Hippocratea ovata var. parviflora

Hippocratea ovata var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered hippocratea, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
