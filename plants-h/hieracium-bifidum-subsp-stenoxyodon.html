Hieracium bifidum subsp. stenoxyodon

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

stenoxyodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrow, linear leaves and reduced flower heads compared to other hawkweed species. It typically thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and botanical gardens.
