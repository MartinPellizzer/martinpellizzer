Haworthiopsis reinwardtii var. greenii

Haworthiopsis reinwardtii var. greenii, commonly known as the Green Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red at the base. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
