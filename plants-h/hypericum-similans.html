Hypericum similans

Hypericum similans, commonly known as the similar St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in woodland edges and open fields.

Hypericum similans is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
