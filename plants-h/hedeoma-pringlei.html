Hedeoma pringlei

Hedeoma pringlei, commonly known as Pringle's hedeoma, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense spikes and bloom in shades of purple or pink.

Hedeoma pringlei is known for its ecological importance, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
