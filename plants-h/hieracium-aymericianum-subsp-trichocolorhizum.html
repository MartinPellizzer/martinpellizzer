Hieracium aymericianum subsp. trichocolorhizum

Hieracium aymericianum subsp.

trichocolorhizum, commonly known as the trichocolorhizum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

The plant is native to Central and Southern Europe and thrives in moist, calcareous soils. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
