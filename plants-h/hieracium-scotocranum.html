Hieracium scotocranum

Hieracium scotocranum, commonly known as the Scottish crane's-bill, is a perennial herb native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges and heathlands. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

The leaves are basal, with a rough texture and a distinctive lobed shape.
