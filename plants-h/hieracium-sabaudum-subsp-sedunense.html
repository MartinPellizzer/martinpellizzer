Hieracium sabaudum subsp. sedunense

Hieracium sabaudum subsp.

sedunense, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. It has a fibrous root system and produces numerous small, hairy stems.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to cold, harsh environments.
