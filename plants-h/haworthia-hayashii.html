Haworthia hayashii

Haworthia hayashii, commonly known as the Hayashii Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. Its foliage is typically green with distinct markings, and it produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the warmer months.

Haworthia hayashii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive appearance.
