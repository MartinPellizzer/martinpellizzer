Hieracium conocerinthe

Hieracium conocerinthe, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early autumn. Hieracium conocerinthe is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
