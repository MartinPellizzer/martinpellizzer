Hedysarum jaxartucirdes

Hedysarum jaxartucirdes, commonly known as the Jaxartus peavine, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, with small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It is often found in mountainous regions and has adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
