Hieracium mirabile

Hieracium mirabile, commonly known as the noble hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium mirabile is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
