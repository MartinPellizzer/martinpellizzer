Hieracium carpathicum subsp. orphneilema

Hieracium carpathicum subsp.

orphneilema, commonly known as the Carpathian hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Carpathian Mountains. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and forest edges, exhibiting a rosette of basal leaves with hairy surfaces. Its flowering period occurs in late summer, producing yellow flowers that attract various pollinators.

The subspecies is distinguished by its specific leaf morphology and habitat preferences within the Carpathian region.
