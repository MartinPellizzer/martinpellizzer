Hedychium × wilkeanum

Hedychium × wilkeanum, commonly known as the yellow ginger or golden torch ginger, is a hybrid plant in the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and can grow up to 2-3 meters in height. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Hedychium × wilkeanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
