Houstonia × gracilenta

Houstonia × gracilenta, commonly known as the slender blue-eyed grass, is a hybrid plant in the genus Houstonia, which belongs to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance with small, blue to violet flowers. This species is native to the southeastern United States, where it thrives in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and meadows. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 centimeters and produces numerous slender, grass-like stems.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and relatively rare ornamental plant in horticultural collections.
