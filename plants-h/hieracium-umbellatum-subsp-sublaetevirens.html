Hieracium umbellatum subsp. sublaetevirens

Hieracium umbellatum subsp.

sublaetevirens, commonly known as the pale-green hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its lighter green foliage compared to other hawkweed varieties. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

The plant is often found in open habitats where it can spread via its rhizomatous root system.
