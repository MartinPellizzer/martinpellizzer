Haworthia ramosa

Haworthia ramosa, commonly known as the Rosy Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Haworthia ramosa is prized for its compact size and unique appearance, often used in rock gardens or as indoor plants.
