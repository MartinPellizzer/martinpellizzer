Haworthia chloracantha var. variegata

Haworthia chloracantha var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish variegation. The leaves are fleshy and covered in a layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the warmer months.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
