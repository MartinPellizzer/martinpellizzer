Hieracium umbellatum subsp. turfosum

Hieracium umbellatum subsp.

turfosum, commonly known as the turf meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, grassy habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

The plant features upright stems that bear clusters of small, yellowish to orange-yellow flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in nutrient-rich, moist soils and is often found in association with other meadow plants.
