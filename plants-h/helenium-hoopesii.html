Helenium hoopesii

Helenium hoopesii, commonly known as the Hoopes' sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and prairies. Its yellow-orange ray flowers and dark brown disk flowers make it visually striking.

Helenium hoopesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
