Helichrysum fontanesii

Helichrysum fontanesii, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is usually gray-green, and the flowers are often long-lasting, making it a popular choice for dried floral arrangements.

Helichrysum fontanesii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
