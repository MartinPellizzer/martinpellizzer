Hypericum kamtschaticum var. hondoense

Hypericum kamtschaticum var. hondoense, commonly known as the Hondo hypericum, is a shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its dense clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streamside areas. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
