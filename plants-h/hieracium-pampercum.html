Hieracium pampercum

Hieracium pampercum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe, it is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
