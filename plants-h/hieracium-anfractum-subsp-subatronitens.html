Hieracium anfractum subsp. subatronitens

Hieracium anfractum subsp.

subatronitens, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.
