Hieracium inuloides subsp. subconicum

Hieracium inuloides subsp.

subconicum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

The subspecies is distinguished by its shorter stature and more compact growth compared to other hawkweed species.
