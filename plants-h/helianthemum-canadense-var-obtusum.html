Helianthemum canadense var. obtusum

Helianthemum canadense var. obtusum, commonly known as the Canadian sandverbena, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to North America.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are rounded and slightly hairy, adapted to thrive in sandy or rocky soils. It is often found in open habitats such as dunes, prairies, and coastal areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments.
