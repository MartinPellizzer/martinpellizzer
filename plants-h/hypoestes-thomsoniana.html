Hypoestes thomsoniana

Hypoestes thomsoniana, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hypoestes thomsoniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking visual appeal.
