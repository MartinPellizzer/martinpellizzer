Hedyotis nicobarensis

Hedyotis nicobarensis, commonly known as the Nicobar bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to the Nicobar Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

Hedyotis nicobarensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
