Howardia cordigera

Howardia cordigera, commonly known as the flame tree or fire tree, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a velvety texture.

Howardia cordigera thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
