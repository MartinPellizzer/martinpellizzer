Herorchis robusta

Herorchis robusta, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and purple. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Herorchis robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
