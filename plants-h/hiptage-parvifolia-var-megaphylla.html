Hiptage parvifolia var. megaphylla

Hiptage parvifolia var. megaphylla, commonly known as the giant-leaf hiptage, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers with a distinctive purple calyx. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
