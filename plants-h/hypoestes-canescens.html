Hypoestes canescens

Hypoestes canescens, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, which are usually pale green or yellow, contrasting with the vibrant bracts.

Hypoestes canescens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
