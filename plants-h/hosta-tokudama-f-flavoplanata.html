Hosta tokudama f. flavoplanata

Hosta tokudama f. flavoplanata, commonly known as the golden shield hosta, is a cultivar prized for its striking foliage.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are predominantly golden yellow with a slight greenish tint. The plant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to Japan. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its vibrant, variegated leaves rather than its flowers.
