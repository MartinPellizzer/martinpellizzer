Homalomena havilandii

Homalomena havilandii, commonly known as the Haviland's arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are usually a vibrant green. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a contrasting spadix. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil.

Homalomena havilandii is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
