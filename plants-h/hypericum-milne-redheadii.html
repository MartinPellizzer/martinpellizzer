Hypericum milne-redheadii

Hypericum milne-redheadii, commonly known as Milne-Redhead's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include glandular dots on the stems and leaves, which are typical of the Hypericaceae family.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
