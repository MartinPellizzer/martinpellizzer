Hieracium tanytrichum

Hieracium tanytrichum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate climates.
