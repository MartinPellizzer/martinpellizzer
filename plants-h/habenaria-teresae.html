Habenaria teresae

Habenaria teresae, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
