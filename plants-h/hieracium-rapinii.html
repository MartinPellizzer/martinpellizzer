Hieracium rapinii

Hieracium rapinii, commonly known as the Alpine Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

Hieracium rapinii is often found in high-altitude habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
