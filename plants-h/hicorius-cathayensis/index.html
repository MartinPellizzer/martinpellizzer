Hicorius Cathayensis

Hicorius cathayensis is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. Known for its delicate, fern-like leaves and striking, pale yellow flowers, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This species is often found in shaded forest floors, where it relies on specific soil conditions to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical wonder.
