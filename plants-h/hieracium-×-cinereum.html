Hieracium × cinereum

Hieracium × cinereum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a hybrid plant species within the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that typically bloom in the summer months. The plant has a sprawling growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats including meadows and roadsides.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered a weed in many regions.
