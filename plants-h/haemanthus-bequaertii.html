Haemanthus bequaertii

Haemanthus bequaertii, commonly known as the red-stemmed haemanthus, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features distinctive red or purple stems that contrast with its bright green leaves. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a desirable addition to both gardens and collections.
