Hypolepis blepharochlaena

Hypolepis blepharochlaena, commonly known as the lace fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that give it a lacy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in some regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
