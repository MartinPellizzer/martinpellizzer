Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. simsianum

Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. simsianum, commonly known as the Sim's Giant Amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
