Helianthemum ledifolium var. macrocarpum

Helianthemum ledifolium var. macrocarpum, commonly known as the large-fruited rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a white, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.
