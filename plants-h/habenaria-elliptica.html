Habenaria elliptica

Habenaria elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and usually greenish-white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Habenaria elliptica is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
