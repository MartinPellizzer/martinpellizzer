Hypoestes pubiflora

Hypoestes pubiflora, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Hypoestes pubiflora is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
