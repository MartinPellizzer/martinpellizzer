Hypericum garrettii var. ovatum

Hypericum garrettii var. ovatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a low-growing shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, woody stems and elliptical leaves that are typically less than an inch in length. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
