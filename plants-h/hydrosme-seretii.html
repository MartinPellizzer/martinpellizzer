Hydrosme seretii

Hydrosme seretii, commonly known as the water lily of Seret, is a rare aquatic plant found in the marshy regions of Southeast Asia.

It has broad, waxy leaves that float on the water's surface, with a distinctive white and pink flower that blooms during the warmer months. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often near the edges of ponds and wetlands. Its root system helps stabilize the soil, preventing erosion in aquatic habitats.
