Helianthemum nudicaule

Helianthemum nudicaule, commonly known as the naked-stemmed rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has thin, hairless stems and is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal and mountainous areas where it thrives in full sun.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a hardy, ornamental plant suitable for rock gardens.
