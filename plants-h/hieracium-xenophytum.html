Hieracium xenophytum

Hieracium xenophytum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium xenophytum is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
