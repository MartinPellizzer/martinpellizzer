Haworthia mirabilis var. splendens

Haworthia mirabilis var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendens Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish-green hue and distinctive white or pale markings. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
