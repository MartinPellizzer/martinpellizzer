Haworthia maraisii var. toonensis

Haworthia maraisii var. toonensis, commonly known as the Maraisii Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings, often resembling a star pattern. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers on short, upright stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
