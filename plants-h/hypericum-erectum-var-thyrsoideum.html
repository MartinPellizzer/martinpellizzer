Hypericum erectum var. thyrsoideum

Hypericum erectum var. thyrsoideum, commonly known as upright St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and slightly serrated leaves that are typically green with a slight waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
