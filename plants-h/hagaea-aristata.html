Hagaea aristata

Hagaea aristata, commonly known as the desert holly, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of North America.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-gray leaves that store water, aiding its survival in dry environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for desert gardens.
