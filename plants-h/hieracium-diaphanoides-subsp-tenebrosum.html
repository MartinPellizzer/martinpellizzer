Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. tenebrosum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

tenebrosum, commonly known as the dark-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and occur in small clusters, blooming from late spring to early summer.

It is characterized by its delicate appearance and preference for cool, humid conditions.
