Hieracium murorum subsp. oligopolium

Hieracium murorum subsp.

oligopolium, commonly known as the lesser yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has basal and upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
