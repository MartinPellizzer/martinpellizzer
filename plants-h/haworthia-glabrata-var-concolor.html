Haworthia glabrata var. concolor

Haworthia glabrata var. concolor, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, light green leaves with distinctive dark green, vertical stripes. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
