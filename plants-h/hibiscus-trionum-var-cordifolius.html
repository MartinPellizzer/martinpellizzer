Hibiscus trionum var. cordifolius

Hibiscus trionum var. cordifolius, commonly known as heartleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically light green and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
