Helianthemum desiderii

Helianthemum desiderii, commonly known as the Desideri's rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fleshy leaves and produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant. It is often found in coastal and mountainous areas, where it withstands harsh environmental conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
