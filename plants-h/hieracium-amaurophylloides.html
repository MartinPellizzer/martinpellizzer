Hieracium amaurophylloides

Hieracium amaurophylloides, commonly known as the dark-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

The plant features dark green, broad leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
