Haworthia graminifolia var. derustensis

Haworthia graminifolia var. derustensis, commonly known as the dwarf Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish-gray hue and white or yellowish markings. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
