Homalomena tirtae

Homalomena tirtae, commonly known as the Tirta arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves with a waxy surface, which help it thrive in humid environments. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden within the spathe.

Homalomena tirtae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
