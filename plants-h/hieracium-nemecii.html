Hieracium nemecii

Hieracium nemecii, commonly known as the Czech hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded conditions. Its flowers are yellow with ray florets, and it produces achenes as its seeds.

Hieracium nemecii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
