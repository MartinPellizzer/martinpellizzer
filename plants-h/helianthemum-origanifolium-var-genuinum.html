Helianthemum origanifolium var. genuinum

Helianthemum origanifolium var. genuinum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub with compact, evergreen foliage that has a strong aromatic scent reminiscent of oregano. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
