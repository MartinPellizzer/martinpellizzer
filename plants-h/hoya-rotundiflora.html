Hoya rotundiflora

Hoya rotundiflora, commonly known as the rounded-leaf wax plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically round or oval in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Hoya rotundiflora is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

It is also known for its slow growth and ease of care, making it ideal for beginners.
