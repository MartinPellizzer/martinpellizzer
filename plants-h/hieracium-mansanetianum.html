Hieracium mansanetianum

Hieracium mansanetianum, commonly known as the Manzanet's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It is known for its robust growth and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
