Haworthia × janseana

Haworthia × janseana, commonly known as the Zulu candle, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered with a waxy coating. The leaves are often adorned with white or pale blue markings, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its compact size and ornamental value.
