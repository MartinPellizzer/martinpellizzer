Haworthia magnifica var. meiringii

Haworthia magnifica var. meiringii, commonly known as the Meiring's Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
