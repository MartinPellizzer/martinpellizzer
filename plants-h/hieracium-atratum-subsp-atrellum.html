Hieracium atratum subsp. atrellum

Hieracium atratum subsp.

atrellum, commonly known as the black hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It produces small, achene-like seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
