Hakea Metamorpha

Blackboy, scientifically known as Hakea metamorpha, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. Its unique appearance, featuring needle-like leaves and vibrant red or orange flowers, makes it a standout in both natural and cultivated settings. This plant is well adapted to arid and coastal environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils. Despite its hardiness, it requires careful watering to avoid root rot, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens. With its ecological importance and ornamental value, the Blackboy remains a fascinating subject for botanists and gardeners alike.
