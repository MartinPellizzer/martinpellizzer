Hieracium sagittipotens

Hieracium sagittipotens, commonly known as the arrowhead hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive leaves that are lanceolate and often hairy. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium sagittipotens is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its resilience to moderate grazing.
