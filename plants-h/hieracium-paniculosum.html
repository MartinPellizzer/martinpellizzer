Hieracium paniculosum

Hieracium paniculosum, commonly known as the panicled hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers arranged in a panicle inflorescence. The plant has basal and cauline leaves that are typically hairy and have a rough texture. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium paniculosum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
