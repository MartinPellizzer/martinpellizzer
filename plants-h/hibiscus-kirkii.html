Hibiscus kirkii

Hibiscus kirkii, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and grows as an upright shrub or small tree. Hibiscus kirkii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in some cultures.
