Hieracium deplumatum

Hieracium deplumatum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Hieracium deplumatum is often found in temperate regions and can sometimes be invasive in certain areas.
