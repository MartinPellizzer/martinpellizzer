Handroanthus odontodiscus var. violascens

Handroanthus odontodiscus var. violascens, commonly known as the violet flame tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its rapid growth and showy floral display.
