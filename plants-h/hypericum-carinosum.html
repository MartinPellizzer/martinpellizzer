Hypericum carinosum

Hypericum carinosum, commonly known as the scarlet goldthread, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial with woody stems and dense clusters of bright orange-yellow flowers. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Hypericum carinosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
