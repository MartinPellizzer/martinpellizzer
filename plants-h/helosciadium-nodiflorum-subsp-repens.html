Helosciadium nodiflorum subsp. repens

Helosciadium nodiflorum subsp.

repens, commonly known as creeping helosciadium, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July.

The plant has creeping stems that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread along the ground. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas or along stream banks.
