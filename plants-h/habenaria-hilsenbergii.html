Habenaria hilsenbergii

Habenaria hilsenbergii, commonly known as the Hilsenberg's orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a slender, erect stem with few leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white to pale yellow flowers. Its root system is fibrous, and it often forms symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi.

The orchid's botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
