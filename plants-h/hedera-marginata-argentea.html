Hedera marginata-argentea

Hedera marginata-argentea, commonly known as silver ivy, is a trailing evergreen vine native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver-white margin, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are not particularly showy, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

Its adaptability and ornamental foliage make it a favored plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
