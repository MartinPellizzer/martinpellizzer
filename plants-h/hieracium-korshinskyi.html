Hieracium korshinskyi

Hieracium korshinskyi, commonly known as the Korshinsky's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Hieracium korshinskyi is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
