Hieracium bifidum subsp. adlasiocybe

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

adlasiocybe, commonly known as the Adlasio cybe hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges.
