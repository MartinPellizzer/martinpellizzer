Hibiscus Arnottianus Subsp. Punaluuensis

Hibiscus arnottianus subsp. punaluuensis is a rare and striking plant native to the Hawaiian Islands. It is known for its large, showy flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of red and pink. This subspecies thrives in the unique microclimates of the Puna District on the Big Island. Its ecological importance makes it a key species for conservation efforts in Hawaii. Due to habitat loss, it is now critically endangered and requires careful protection.
