Hemigenia wandooana

Hemigenia wandooana, commonly known as the Wandooan hemigenia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Hemigenia wandooana is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
