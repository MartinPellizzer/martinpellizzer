Habenaria sigillum var. cruenta

Habenaria sigillum var. cruenta, commonly known as the Crimson Habenaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, grass-like stem with a single, showy flower that is typically bright red or orange-red in color. The plant has a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in seasonal habitats. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
