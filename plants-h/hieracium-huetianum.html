Hieracium huetianum

Hieracium huetianum, commonly known as the Huetian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are usually serrated and lance-shaped.

Hieracium huetianum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
