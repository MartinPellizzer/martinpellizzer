Helichrysum seineri

Helichrysum seineri, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its golden-yellow, papery flowers that resemble tiny cups. The plant is evergreen, with narrow, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and long-lasting blooms make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
