Hibiscus sidoides

Hibiscus sidoides, commonly known as the African rose or red hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hibiscus sidoides is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
