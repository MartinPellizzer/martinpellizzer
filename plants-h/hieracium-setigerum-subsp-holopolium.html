Hieracium setigerum subsp. holopolium

Hieracium setigerum subsp.

holopolium, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

The subspecies holopolium is distinguished by its fully hairy stems and leaves, setting it apart from other subspecies of Hieracium setigerum.
