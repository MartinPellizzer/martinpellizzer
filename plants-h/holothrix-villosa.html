Holothrix villosa

Holothrix villosa, commonly known as the hairy holothrix, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, woolly foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both gardens and botanical collections.
