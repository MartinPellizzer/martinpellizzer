Huernia penzigii var. schweinfurthii

Huernia penzigii var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are typically green or yellowish with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
