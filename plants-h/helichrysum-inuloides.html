Helichrysum inuloides

Helichrysum inuloides, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces bright yellow to orange flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Helichrysum inuloides thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
