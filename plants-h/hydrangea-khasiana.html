Hydrangea khasiana

Hydrangea khasiana, commonly known as the Khasi hydrangea, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern regions of India.

It belongs to the Hydrangeaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and thrives in temperate climates. Its foliage is broad and glossy, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Hydrangea khasiana is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
