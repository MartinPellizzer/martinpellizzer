Hieracium eucalyptum

Hieracium eucalyptum, commonly known as the eucalyptus hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. It has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium eucalyptum is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.
