Hieracium atropictum

Hieracium atropictum, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium atropictum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed due to its aggressive growth habit.
