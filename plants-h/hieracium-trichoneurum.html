Hieracium trichoneurum

Hieracium trichoneurum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Hieracium trichoneurum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
