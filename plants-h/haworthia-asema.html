Haworthia asema

Haworthia asema, commonly known as the Asema Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically pale green with prominent white or pale blue markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

Haworthia asema is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
