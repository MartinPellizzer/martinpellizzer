Hypoxis costata

Hypoxis costata, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive white to pale blue coloration. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Hypoxis costata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
