Hovea linearis

Hovea linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hovea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant yellow flowers. It is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.

Hovea linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
