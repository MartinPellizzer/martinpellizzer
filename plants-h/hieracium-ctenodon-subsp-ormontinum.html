Hieracium ctenodon subsp. ormontinum

Hieracium ctenodon subsp.

ormontinum, commonly known as the Ormontin hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming basal leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has a fibrous root system and produces numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
