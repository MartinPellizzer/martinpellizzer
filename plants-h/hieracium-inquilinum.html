Hieracium inquilinum

Hieracium inquilinum, commonly known as the mossy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with hairy stems and basal leaves that often form a rosette. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
