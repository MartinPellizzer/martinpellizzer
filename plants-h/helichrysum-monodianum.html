Helichrysum monodianum

Helichrysum monodianum, commonly known as the Cape goldball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow or golden-orange flowers. The plant produces small, densely packed flower heads that resemble a ball, giving it its common name. It is often found in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

Helichrysum monodianum is also noted for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
