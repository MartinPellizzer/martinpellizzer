Hydrangea mandarinorum

Hydrangea mandarinorum, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, blue, or purple depending on soil pH. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a smooth, dark green upper surface. It typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and thrives in partial shade with moist, well-drained soil.

Hydrangea mandarinorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes.
