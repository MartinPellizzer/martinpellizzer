Hieracium chondrilloides subsp. morascanum

Hieracium chondrilloides subsp.

morascanum, commonly known as the Morascan hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flower heads.

The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species.
