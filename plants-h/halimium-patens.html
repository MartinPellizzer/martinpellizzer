Halimium patens

Halimium patens, commonly known as the spreading sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal and arid environments. The plant features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

Halimium patens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in harsh landscapes.
