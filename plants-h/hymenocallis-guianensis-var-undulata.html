Hymenocallis guianensis var. undulata

Hymenocallis guianensis var. undulata, commonly known as the undulating spider lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow-green stripe down the center. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements make it a favored plant among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
