Hovea trisperma var. grandiflora

Hovea trisperma var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered hovea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open forests or woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
