Haworthia mirabilis var. scabra

Haworthia mirabilis var. scabra, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct white or gray markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant grows in a rosette form and is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves. It produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
