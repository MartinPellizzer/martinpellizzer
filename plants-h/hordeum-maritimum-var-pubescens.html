Hordeum maritimum var. pubescens

Hordeum maritimum var. pubescens, commonly known as sea barley, is a coastal plant adapted to saline and sandy environments.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spikelet-like inflorescences with pale or brownish flowers. It thrives in maritime climates and is often found along shorelines and dunes.

Its hairy leaves and salt tolerance make it a resilient species in harsh coastal conditions.
