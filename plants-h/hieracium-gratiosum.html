Hieracium gratiosum

Hieracium gratiosum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, where it typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn, and it has hairy, rough-textured leaves. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.

Hieracium gratiosum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
