Hypericum paludosum

Hypericum paludosum, commonly known as swamp St. John's wort, is a perennial herb native to wetland habitats in North America.

It features erect, branched stems that are often covered in fine, glandular hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Hypericum paludosum thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in bogs, marshes, and along stream banks.
