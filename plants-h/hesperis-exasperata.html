Hesperis exasperata

Hesperis exasperata, commonly known as the showy bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Hesperis exasperata is also known for its short growing season and early blooming period.
