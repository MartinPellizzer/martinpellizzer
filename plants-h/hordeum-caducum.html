Hordeum caducum

Hordeum caducum, commonly known as the slender barley, is a species of grass native to Eurasia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as marshes and wet meadows. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Hordeum caducum plays a role in wetland ecosystems by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
