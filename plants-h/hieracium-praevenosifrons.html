Hieracium praevenosifrons

Hieracium praevenosifrons, commonly known as the narrow-veined hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is often found in open habitats and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
