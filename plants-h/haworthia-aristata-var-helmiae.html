Haworthia aristata var. helmiae

Haworthia aristata var. helmiae, commonly known as the helmeted Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or pale markings. The leaves are covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
