Hieracium schmidtii var. tremedale

Hieracium schmidtii var. tremedale, commonly known as the Tremedale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of southern South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, typically from October to December. Its leaves are generally basal, with a rough, hairy texture, and the plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
