Hieracium × stoloniferum nothovar. cayouetteanum

Hieracium × stoloniferum nothovar. cayouetteanum, commonly known as the Cayouette's hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves with a serrated edge, and it produces numerous stolons for vegetative propagation. It is native to regions of North America and is often found in disturbed soils such as roadsides and fields.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variability.
