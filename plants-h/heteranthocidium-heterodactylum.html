Heteranthocidium heterodactylum

Heteranthocidium heterodactylum, commonly known as the variable-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring different sizes and shapes of petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often epiphytic on trees. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

The genus Heteranthocidium is notable for its morphological diversity and adaptability to various tropical habitats.
