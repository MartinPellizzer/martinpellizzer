Heliotropium foliosum

Heliotropium foliosum, commonly known as the leafy heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple, often resembling those of the common heliotrope. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous annual, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Heliotropium foliosum is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often found in open areas such as roadsides and fields.
