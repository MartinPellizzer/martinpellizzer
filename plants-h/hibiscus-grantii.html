Hibiscus grantii

Hibiscus grantii, commonly known as the Grant's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Hibiscus grantii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant colors and unique foliage.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
