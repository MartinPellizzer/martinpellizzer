Hieracium sparsum subsp. subsimplex

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

subsimplex, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically has slender, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often have serrated edges. It is native to parts of Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in certain habitats.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
