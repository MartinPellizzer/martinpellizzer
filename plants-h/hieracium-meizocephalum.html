Hieracium meizocephalum

Hieracium meizocephalum, commonly known as the large-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its robust growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Hieracium meizocephalum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
