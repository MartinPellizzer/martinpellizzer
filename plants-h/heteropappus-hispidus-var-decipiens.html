Heteropappus hispidus var. decipiens

Heteropappus hispidus var. decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.
