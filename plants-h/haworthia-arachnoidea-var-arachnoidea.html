Haworthia arachnoidea var. arachnoidea

Haworthia arachnoidea var. arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The leaves are covered in fine, hair-like tubercles that give the plant its distinctive appearance. It produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and container cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
