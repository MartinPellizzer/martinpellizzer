Holothrix schimperi

Holothrix schimperi, commonly known as the African holothrix, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features elongated, hairy stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Holothrix schimperi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
