Homalomena striatieopetiolata

Homalomena striatieopetiolata, commonly known as the streaked arrowhead plant, is a tropical evergreen perennial belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves with distinctive white or yellow streaks running along the veins. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
