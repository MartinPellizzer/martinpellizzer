Haworthia keithii

Haworthia keithii, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or yellow stripes. The leaves are typically blue-green in color, providing a striking visual contrast to the lighter markings. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for dry environments.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
