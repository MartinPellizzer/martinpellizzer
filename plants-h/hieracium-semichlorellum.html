Hieracium semichlorellum

Hieracium semichlorellum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and leaves that are usually covered in fine, soft pubescence.

Hieracium semichlorellum is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is sometimes considered invasive in certain regions.
