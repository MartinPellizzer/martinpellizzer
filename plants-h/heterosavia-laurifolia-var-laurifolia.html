Heterosavia laurifolia var. laurifolia

Heterosavia laurifolia var. laurifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawthorn, is a shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural use.
