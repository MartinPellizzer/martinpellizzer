Hedyotis trimenii var. orbicularifolia

Hedyotis trimenii var. orbicularifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed dotweed, is a perennial herb native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features small, opposite leaves that are typically rounded or ovate in shape, giving it a distinct appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its flowering stems are usually slender and hairy, and the plant often grows in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
