Hieracium penninicola

Hieracium penninicola, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer, are surrounded by pale green ray florets.

Hieracium penninicola is often found in open meadows and scree slopes, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil in harsh conditions.
