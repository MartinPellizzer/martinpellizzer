Haworthia attenuata var. argyrostigma

Haworthia attenuata var. argyrostigma, commonly known as the Silver Cross Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with distinct silver or white markings. The leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
