Heliotropium noeanum

Heliotropium noeanum, commonly known as the African heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with hairy surfaces, and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually purple or pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
