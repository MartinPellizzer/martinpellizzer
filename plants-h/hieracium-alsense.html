Hieracium alsense

Hieracium alsense, commonly known as the Alsace hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in early summer.

Hieracium alsense is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
