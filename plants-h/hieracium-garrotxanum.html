Hieracium garrotxanum

Hieracium garrotxanum, commonly known as the Garrotxa hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are usually hairy and lance-shaped, with a basal rosette structure.
