Hieracium nigriusculum

Hieracium nigriusculum, commonly known as the small black hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny areas.

The species is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
