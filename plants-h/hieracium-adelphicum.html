Hieracium adelphicum

Hieracium adelphicum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has basal leaves that are often hairy and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Hieracium adelphicum is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in agricultural areas.
