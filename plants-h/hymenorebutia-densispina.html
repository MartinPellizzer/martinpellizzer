Hymenorebutia densispina

Hymenorebutia densispina, commonly known as the dense-spined pincushion cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny areoles and cylindrical stems. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered at the tips of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure, making it suitable for arid climates.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
