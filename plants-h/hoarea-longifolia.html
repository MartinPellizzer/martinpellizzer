Hoarea longifolia

Hoarea longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved hoarea, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its small, white to pale blue flowers appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Hoarea longifolia is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
