Hymenodictyon floribundum var. ziamaeanum

Hymenodictyon floribundum var. ziamaeanum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers are typically followed by small, woody pods that contain several seeds.
