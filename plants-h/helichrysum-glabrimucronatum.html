Helichrysum glabrimucronatum

Helichrysum glabrimucronatum, commonly known as the smooth-tipped everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in dry, rocky environments. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often arranged in dense, compact clusters. Its foliage is typically narrow and linear, with a soft, velvety texture.

The species is well-known for its ornamental value and is often used in dried flower arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
