Hellenocarum depressum

Hellenocarum depressum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Hellenocarum depressum is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
