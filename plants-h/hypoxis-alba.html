Hypoxis alba

Hypoxis alba, commonly known as the white star lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking white, star-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. Hypoxis alba is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
