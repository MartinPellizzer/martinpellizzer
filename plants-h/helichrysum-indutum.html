Helichrysum indutum

Helichrysum indutum, commonly known as the African frost.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and vibrant yellow-orange flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Helichrysum indutum is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
