Hieracium integratum subsp. munduliforme

Hieracium integratum subsp.

munduliforme, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a fibrous root system and produces numerous flowering stems.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native range.
