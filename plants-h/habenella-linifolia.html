Habenella linifolia

Habenella linifolia, commonly known as the silky leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts well to dry conditions.

The botanical features of Habenella linifolia make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
