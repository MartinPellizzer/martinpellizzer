Hieracium subspeciosum subsp. melananthum

Hieracium subspeciosum subsp.

melananthum, commonly known as the dark-flowered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers and basal rosettes of leaves.

The subspecies is distinguished by its darker flower coloration compared to other subspecies of *H. subspeciosum*. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant has finely divided leaves and produces numerous small florets arranged in a composite head.
