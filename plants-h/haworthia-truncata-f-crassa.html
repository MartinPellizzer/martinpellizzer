Haworthia truncata f. crassa

Haworthia truncata f. crassa, commonly known as the thick-leaved Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The leaves are often adorned with white or pale green markings, which provide a striking visual contrast. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
