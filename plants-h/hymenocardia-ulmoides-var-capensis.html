Hymenocardia ulmoides var. capensis

Hymenocardia ulmoides var. capensis, commonly known as the Cape hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit, with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in coastal and sandy soils, often found in coastal dunes and rocky areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
