Hesperozygis myrtoides

Hesperozygis myrtoides, commonly known as the pink fairy martin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small shrub that produces clusters of pink to purple flowers, which bloom in the spring. The plant has aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.
