Haworthia margaritifera subvar. major

Haworthia margaritifera subvar. major, commonly known as the giant window leaf, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or translucent markings. The plant is characterized by its compact rosette growth habit and is well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
