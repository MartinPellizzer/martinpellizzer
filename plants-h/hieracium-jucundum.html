Hieracium jucundum

Hieracium jucundum, commonly known as the cheerful hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Hieracium. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas, often in temperate climates. Hieracium jucundum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common sight in many natural habitats.
