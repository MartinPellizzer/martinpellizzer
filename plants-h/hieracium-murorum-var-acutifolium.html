Hieracium murorum var. acutifolium

Hieracium murorum var. acutifolium, commonly known as the sharp-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers in clustered heads. The leaves are typically narrow and pointed, giving it its common name. It is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

This plant is perennial and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
