Hafotra

Hafotra, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Tanzania and surrounding regions.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, waxy leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. Hafotra thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
