Hieracium caesium subsp. brandelii

Hieracium caesium subsp.

brandelii, commonly known as the Brandel's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. It features bright yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer.
