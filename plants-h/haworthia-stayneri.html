Haworthia stayneri

Haworthia stayneri, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks that bear white or pale pink flowers during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
