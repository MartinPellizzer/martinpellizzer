Hieracium murorum subsp. pseudofestiviforme

Hieracium murorum subsp.

pseudofestiviforme, commonly known as the lesser spearthistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands.

The plant has spiny, lobed leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

This subspecies is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a minor weed in some agricultural areas.
