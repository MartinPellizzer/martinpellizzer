Hypericum densiflorum var. densiflorum

Hypericum densiflorum var. densiflorum, commonly known as denseflower St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has woody stems and opposite, elliptic leaves with smooth margins. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ornamental gardening and ecological habitats.
