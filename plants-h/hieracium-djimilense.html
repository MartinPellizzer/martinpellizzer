Hieracium djimilense

Hieracium djimilense, commonly known as the Djimilense hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.
