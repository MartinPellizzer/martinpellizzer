Heliotropium cyrtostachyum

Heliotropium cyrtostachyum, commonly known as the tropical heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for ornamental gardening.

Heliotropium cyrtostachyum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
