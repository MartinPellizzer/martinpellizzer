Helichrysum simulans

Helichrysum simulans, commonly known as the golden marguerite or everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers and silver-green foliage. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.

Helichrysum simulans thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
