Hieracium jaliscense

Hieracium jaliscense, commonly known as the Jalisco hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium jaliscense is often found in the state of Jalisco, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
