Helicteres prostrata

Helicteres prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate helicteres, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its slender, spreading stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
