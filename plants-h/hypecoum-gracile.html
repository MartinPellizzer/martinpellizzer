Hypecoum gracile

Hypecoum gracile, commonly known as slender milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Hypecoum gracile is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
