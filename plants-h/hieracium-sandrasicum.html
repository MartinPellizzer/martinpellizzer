Hieracium sandrasicum

Hieracium sandrasicum, commonly known as the sandrasicum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

The plant typically grows in sandy or dry soils, often in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium sandrasicum is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor environments, making it a resilient and adaptable species.
