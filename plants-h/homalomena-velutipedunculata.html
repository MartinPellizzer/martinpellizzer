Homalomena velutipedunculata

Homalomena velutipedunculata, commonly known as the velvet-stemmed arrowhead, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a common feature in the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant in horticulture.
