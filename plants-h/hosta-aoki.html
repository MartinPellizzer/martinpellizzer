Hosta aoki

Hosta aoki, commonly known as the Japanese hosta, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-white margin. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta aoki is a popular ornamental plant in gardens due to its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
