Hieracium megalocerinthe

Hieracium megalocerinthe, commonly known as the giant meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its robust growth and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are often hairy.

Hieracium megalocerinthe is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain areas.
