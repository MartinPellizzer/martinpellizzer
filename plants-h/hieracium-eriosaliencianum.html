Hieracium eriosaliencianum

Hieracium eriosaliencianum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in grassy areas and meadows. The plant has basal leaves that are usually hairy and narrow, while the upper leaves are more lance-shaped. Its flowers are yellow and form a composite head, typical of the daisy family.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid spread and adaptability.
