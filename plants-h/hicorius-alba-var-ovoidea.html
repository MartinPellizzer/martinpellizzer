Hicorius alba var. ovoidea

Hicorius alba var. ovoidea, commonly known as the white-flowered ovoid hibiscus, is a variant of the hibiscus species characterized by its pale white petals and ovoid-shaped flowers.

It is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. Its flowers bloom in summer, producing a soft, delicate appearance that adds ornamental value to gardens.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral form.
