Hieracium subramosum subsp. orcadense

Hieracium subramosum subsp.

orcadense, commonly known as Orkney hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the British Isles. It typically grows in moist, calcareous grasslands and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant has basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while the upper leaves are smaller and more linear. Its flowers are arranged in a single head, with ray florets that are usually yellow and tubular florets that are often reddish.

It is a relatively rare species, often found in specific habitats on the Orkney Islands.
