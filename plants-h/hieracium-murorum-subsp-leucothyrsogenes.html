Hieracium murorum subsp. leucothyrsogenes

Hieracium murorum subsp.

leucothyrsogenes, commonly known as the white thyrse hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange composite flowers, which are arranged in a conical shape known as a thyrse.

The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is known for its distinctive floral structure and is found primarily in central and eastern Europe.
