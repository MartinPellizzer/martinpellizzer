Hieracium benzianum subsp. ausserdorferi

Hieracium benzianum subsp.

ausserdorferi, commonly known as the Austrian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom from June to August.
