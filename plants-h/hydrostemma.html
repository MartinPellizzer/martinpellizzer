Hydrostemma

Hydrostemma, commonly known as the waterstem, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers that often have a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showing its adaptability to waterlogged soils. Its root system is fibrous and contributes to its ability to thrive in such conditions.

Hydrostemma species are valued for their medicinal properties and are sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
