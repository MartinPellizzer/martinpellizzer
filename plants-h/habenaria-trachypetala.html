Habenaria trachypetala

Habenaria trachypetala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish or yellowish flowers clustered in racemes. Its flowering period usually occurs in late spring to early summer.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in swampy or boggy environments.
