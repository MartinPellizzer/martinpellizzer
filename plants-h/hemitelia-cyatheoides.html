Hemitelia cyatheoides

Hemitelia cyatheoides, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant has a thick, fibrous trunk that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in humid, shaded environments. Its reproductive structures include spore-bearing cones, which are typically found on the underside of the fronds.

Hemitelia cyatheoides plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for various species.
