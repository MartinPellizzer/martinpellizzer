Hieracium elegantidens

Hieracium elegantidens, commonly known as the elegant hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features bright yellow flowers with ray florets surrounding a central disc, and it typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium elegantidens is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
