Hieracium lopezianum

Hieracium lopezianum, commonly known as Lopez's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually hairy and have a rough texture, which helps it retain moisture in its arid environment.
