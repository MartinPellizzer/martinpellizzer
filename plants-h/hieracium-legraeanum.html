Hieracium legraeanum

Hieracium legraeanum, commonly known as the Lecanora hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.
