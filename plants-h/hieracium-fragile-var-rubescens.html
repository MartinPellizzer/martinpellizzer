Hieracium fragile var. rubescens

Hieracium fragile var. rubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and grasslands. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and often have a reddish tinge.

This variant is distinguished by its vivid red coloration, which is more pronounced in its stems and leaves compared to other varieties of Hieracium.
