Heliotropium albrechtii

Heliotropium albrechtii, commonly known as the African heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Heliotropium albrechtii is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and is often found in disturbed habitats.
