Helichrysum mannii

Helichrysum mannii, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to golden-orange flower heads. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in a compact cluster. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Helichrysum mannii is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
