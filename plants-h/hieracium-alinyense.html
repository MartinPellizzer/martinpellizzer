Hieracium alinyense

Hieracium alinyense, commonly known as the Alinyen hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are usually yellow or orange in color.

Hieracium alinyense is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions, making it a notable species in high-altitude flora.
