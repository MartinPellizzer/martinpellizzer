Hymenocallis bonplandii

Hymenocallis bonplandii, commonly known as the Bonplandian spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, spidery appearance, often with a red or orange stripe down the center. The plant produces strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically green and covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hymenocallis bonplandii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
