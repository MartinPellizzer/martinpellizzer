Habenaria pseudoculicina

Habenaria pseudoculicina, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and dense, grass-like leaves. The plant produces a spike of small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in a loose raceme. It typically blooms in late spring to early summer, and its flowers have a delicate, sweet scent.

This orchid is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and along stream banks.
