Helenium helenium

Helenium helenium, commonly known as the American lion's ear, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features striking, bright orange to red ray flowers that resemble a lion's mane, surrounded by yellow disk flowers. Helenium helenium thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and wildflower landscapes.
