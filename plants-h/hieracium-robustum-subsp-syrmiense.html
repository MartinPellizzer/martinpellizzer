Hieracium robustum subsp. syrmiense

Hieracium robustum subsp.

syrmiense, commonly known as the Sirmium hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and forest edges.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are hairy and have a rough texture. Its flowering stems bear yellow flowers arranged in a single, terminal capitulum.

It is characterized by its robust growth and is often found in regions with temperate climates.
