Hura crepitans var. orbicularis

Hura crepitans var. orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular hura, is a tropical plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, orbicular (rounded) leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds with tufted hairs.

The species is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
