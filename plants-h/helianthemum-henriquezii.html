Helianthemum henriquezii

Helianthemum henriquezii, commonly known as the Canary Islands rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in rocky and coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Helianthemum henriquezii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native ecosystem.
