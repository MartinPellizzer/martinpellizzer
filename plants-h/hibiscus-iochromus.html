Hibiscus iochromus

Hibiscus iochromus, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Hibiscus iochromus thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
