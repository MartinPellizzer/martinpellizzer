Hieracium lillense

Hieracium lillense, commonly known as the Lilac Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium lillense is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
