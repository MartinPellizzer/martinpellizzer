Hieracium triviale f. subravusculum

Hieracium triviale f. subravusculum, commonly known as the small yellow hawkweed, is a variant of the common hawkweed.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Its subspecies, subravusculum, is distinguished by its smaller, more compact inflorescence compared to the typical form.
