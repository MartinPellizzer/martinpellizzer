Honckenya ficifolia

Honckenya ficifolia, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the silver sword, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green, sword-shaped leaves that are often speckled with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and air-purifying properties.

Honckenya ficifolia prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
