Hieracium brentanum

Hieracium brentanum, commonly known as the Brentanum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium brentanum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun conditions.
