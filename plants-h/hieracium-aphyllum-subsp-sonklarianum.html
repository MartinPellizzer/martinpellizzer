Hieracium aphyllum subsp. sonklarianum

Hieracium aphyllum subsp.

sonklarianum, commonly known as the Sonklarian hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellowish to reddish flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. It has a taproot system and is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species in parts of its range.
