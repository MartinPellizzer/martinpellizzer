Hieracium vulgatum f. subravusculum

Hieracium vulgatum f. subravusculum, commonly known as the small-leaved hawkweed, is a botanical variant of the common hawkweed.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are smaller and more densely arranged compared to the typical form, and it produces yellow-orange composite flowers.

This subspecies is noted for its adaptability and is often found in temperate regions across Europe.
