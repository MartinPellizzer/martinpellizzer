Hieracium bifidum subsp. oreitiforme

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

oreitiforme, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, often reddish stems.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its hardy nature.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in alpine environments and is adapted to cold, harsh climates.
