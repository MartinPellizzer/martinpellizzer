Helinus arabicus

Helinus arabicus, commonly known as the Arabian helinus, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Helinus arabicus is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
