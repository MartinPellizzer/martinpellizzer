Helicia lobata

Helicia lobata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed helenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical forests.
