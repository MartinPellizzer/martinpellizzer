Helichrysum stoechas var. argentatum

Helichrysum stoechas var. argentatum, commonly known as the silver helmet lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and vibrant yellow-orange flowers that resemble small helmets. The plant is a perennial shrub that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
