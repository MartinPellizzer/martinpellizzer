Hieracium transiens subsp. stellidorsum

Hieracium transiens subsp.

stellidorsum, commonly known as the starry hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features upright stems with hairy leaves and produces showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a pioneer species in ecological succession.
