Helianthus annuus var. pumilus

Helianthus annuus var. pumilus, commonly known as the dwarf sunflower, is a compact variety of the sunflower species.

It features small, rounded leaves and produces yellow flower heads with a central disk. This variant is typically shorter than the standard sunflower, making it ideal for smaller gardens or containers. It thrives in full sunlight and well-drained soil, requiring moderate water.

The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and can attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
