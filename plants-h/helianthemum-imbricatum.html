Helianthemum imbricatum

Helianthemum imbricatum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are typically greyish-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
