Hedysarum tschuense

Hedysarum tschuense, commonly known as the Tschuenese milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has been used traditionally for forage and in some medicinal practices.

Its root system contributes to soil stabilization, making it ecologically significant in its native habitats.
