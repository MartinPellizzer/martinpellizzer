Hieracium cilicicum

Hieracium cilicicum, commonly known as the Cilician hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium cilicicum is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to thrive in temperate climates.
