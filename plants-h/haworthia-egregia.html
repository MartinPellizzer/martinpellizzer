Haworthia egregia

Haworthia egregia, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped foliage. The leaves are typically bluish-green with distinct white or yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
