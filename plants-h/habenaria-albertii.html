Habenaria albertii

Habenaria albertii, commonly known as the Albert's orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features a single or few erect spikes bearing several flowers, each with a three-lobed lip. Its roots are often tuberous, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Habenaria albertii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
