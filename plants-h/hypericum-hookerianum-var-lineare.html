Hypericum hookerianum var. lineare

Hypericum hookerianum var. lineare, commonly known as the linear St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along its stems. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers with four petals, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
