Hieracium interrogans

Hieracium interrogans, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy, often reddish stems. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has alternate, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges.

Hieracium interrogans is a perennial herb that can spread vegetatively through rhizomes.
