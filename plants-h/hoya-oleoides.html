Hoya oleoides

Hoya oleoides, commonly known as the "stinking hoya," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It is a climbing or trailing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Hoya oleoides is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
