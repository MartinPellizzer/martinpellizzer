Hieracioides

Hieracioides is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Asteraceae, known for its diverse species found primarily in tropical and subtropical regions. These plants are characterized by their composite flower heads and often exhibit a variety of leaf shapes and growth habits. Many species within this genus are valued for their ornamental appeal and are commonly cultivated in gardens. They also play important ecological roles by providing food and habitat for various pollinators. Despite their beauty and utility, Hieracioides remains relatively understudied compared to more widely recognized members of the Asteraceae family.
