Hieracium vulgatum subsp. tenellum

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

tenellum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

The plant features basal leaves that are broadly lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, with flowering stems that bear yellow composite flowers. Its inflorescence consists of a single flower head composed of numerous small florets, arranged in a radiate pattern.

The species is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
