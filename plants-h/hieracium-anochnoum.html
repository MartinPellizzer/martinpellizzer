Hieracium anochnoum

Hieracium anochnoum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers, which are typically clustered in dense, flat-topped inflorescences. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.

Hieracium anochnoum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
