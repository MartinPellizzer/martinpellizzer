Hardenbergia violacea f. rosea

Hardenbergia violacea f. rosea, commonly known as the pink burr legume, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its distinctive pink to purple flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces cylindrical, woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

The varietal form 'rosea' is distinguished by its more intensely colored flowers compared to the typical species.
