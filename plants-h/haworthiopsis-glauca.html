Haworthiopsis glauca

Haworthiopsis glauca, commonly known as the Blue Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are fleshy and covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on short, slender stems during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
