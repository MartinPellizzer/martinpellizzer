Hosta kiyosumiensis var. petrophila

Hosta kiyosumiensis var. petrophila, commonly known as the rock hosta, is a compact, rhizomatous perennial native to Japan.

It features narrow, dark green leaves with a metallic sheen that can turn bronze in winter. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers on slender stems in late summer. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is well-suited for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a prized addition to botanical collections.
