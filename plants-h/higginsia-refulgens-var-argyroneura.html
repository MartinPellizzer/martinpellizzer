Higginsia refulgens var. argyroneura

Higginsia refulgens var. argyroneura, commonly known as the silverleaf Higginsia, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It features slender, upright stems covered in dense, silvery-white hairs that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a soft, fuzzy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
