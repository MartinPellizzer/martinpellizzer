Hieracium giresunense

Hieracium giresunense, commonly known as Giresun hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow, often arranged in a single head.

Hieracium giresunense is valued for its potential in traditional medicine and ecological resilience.
