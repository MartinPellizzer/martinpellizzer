Hieracium umbellatum subsp. canadense

Hieracium umbellatum subsp.

canadense, commonly known as the Canadian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its clustered inflorescence of yellow to orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has basal and upright stems with rough, hairy leaves.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller flower heads and shorter peduncles compared to other members of the species.
