Hieracium Subandurense

Hieracium subandurense is a rare flowering plant native to the alpine regions of southern Europe. It is known for its delicate yellow flowers and hairy stems that help it survive in harsh mountain climates. This species is often found in rocky soils and high-altitude meadows where competition for resources is minimal. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful plant.
