Hieracium lustratum

Hieracium lustratum, commonly known as the shining hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium lustratum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some regions.
