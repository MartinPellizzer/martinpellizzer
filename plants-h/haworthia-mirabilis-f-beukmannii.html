Haworthia mirabilis f. beukmannii

Haworthia mirabilis f. beukmannii, commonly known as the Beukmann's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves with white to pale blue tubercles and a striking central spine. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
