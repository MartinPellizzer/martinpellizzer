Helichrysum roseum var. typicum

Helichrysum roseum var. typicum, commonly known as the rose-honeycomb plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, yellowish to orange-colored flowers that resemble honeycombs. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often grown as a ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and long-lasting blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The species is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
