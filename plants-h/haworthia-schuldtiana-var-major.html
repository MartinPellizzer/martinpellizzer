Haworthia schuldtiana var. major

Haworthia schuldtiana var. major, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green with white or silver markings, resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, pale green flowers on short, upright stems during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
