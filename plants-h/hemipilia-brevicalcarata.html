Hemipilia brevicalcarata

Hemipilia brevicalcarata, commonly known as the short-spurred hemipilia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers with elongated spurs, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its overall appearance.

Hemipilia brevicalcarata is often found in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
