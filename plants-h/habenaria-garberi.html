Habenaria garberi

Habenaria garberi, commonly known as the Garber's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with yellow markings. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces small, clustered flowers during the spring and early summer.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts interested in native flora.
