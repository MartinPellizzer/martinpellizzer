Helenium leguiffei

Helenium leguiffei, commonly known as the Mexican sunset flower, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its striking orange to red flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Helenium leguiffei is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice in garden landscapes.
