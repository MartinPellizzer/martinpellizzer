Hibiscus normanii

Hibiscus normanii, commonly known as the Norman's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces fruits that are capsule-like in structure. Hibiscus normanii thrives in tropical climates and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
