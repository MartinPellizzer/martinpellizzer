Huernia volkartii var. volkartii

Huernia volkartii var. volkartii, commonly known as the Volcark's horned cucumber, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in a layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are usually reduced to small, scale-like structures, giving it a unique appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
