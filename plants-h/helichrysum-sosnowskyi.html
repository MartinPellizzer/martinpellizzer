Helichrysum sosnowskyi

Helichrysum sosnowskyi, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often grown as an ornamental due to its vibrant color and compact growth habit. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Helichrysum sosnowskyi is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
