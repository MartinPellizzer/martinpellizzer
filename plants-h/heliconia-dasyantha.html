Heliconia dasyantha

Heliconia dasyantha, commonly known as the red heliconia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, brightly colored bracts that resemble flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces long, slender inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant appearance. Heliconia dasyantha thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
