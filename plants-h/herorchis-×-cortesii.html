Herorchis × cortesii

Herorchis × cortesii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Herorchis mira and Herorchis speciosa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of purple, white, and green hues. The plant is native to the humid forests of Central America, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

This orchid is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
