Helicia glabrescens

Helicia glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth helicia, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Helicia glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
