Helichrysum rotundatum

Helichrysum rotundatum, commonly known as the African goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flowers that resemble small cups. The plant has rounded, hairy leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Helichrysum rotundatum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
