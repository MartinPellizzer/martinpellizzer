Hieracium alpinum subsp. villosiflorum

Hieracium alpinum subsp.

villosiflorum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is characterized by its hairy, yellowish-green stems and leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets surrounded by white to pale yellow ray florets. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, often in mountainous environments.

Due to its adaptability to harsh conditions, it is considered a hardy species within the genus Hieracium.
