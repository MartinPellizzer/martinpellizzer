Hypericum orientale var. teberdinum

Hypericum orientale var. teberdinum, commonly known as the Turkish St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
