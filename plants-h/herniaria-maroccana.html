Herniaria maroccana

Herniaria maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan bladder campion, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fleshy, bladder-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Herniaria maroccana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
