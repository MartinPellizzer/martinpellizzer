Haworthia schuldtiana var. whitesloaneana

Haworthia schuldtiana var. whitesloaneana, commonly known as the Whitesloane's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white to pale yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
