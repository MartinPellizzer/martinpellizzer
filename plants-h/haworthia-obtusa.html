Haworthia obtusa

Haworthia obtusa, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped body with fleshy, triangular leaves that are often marked with white or yellow stripes. The leaves are typically a deep green color, providing a striking contrast to the lighter markings. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
