Hieracium jurassicum subsp. obesum

Hieracium jurassicum subsp.

obesum, commonly known as the Jurassic hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy rosettes.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are broadly lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

It is notable for its historical significance in botanical studies due to its distinctive morphology and limited distribution.
