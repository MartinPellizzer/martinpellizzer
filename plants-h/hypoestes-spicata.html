Hypoestes spicata

Hypoestes spicata, commonly known as the Indian blanket flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features striking, bi-colored flowers that range from red, orange, and yellow, often with a contrasting central stripe. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and native plant gardens.
