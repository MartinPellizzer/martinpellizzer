Hedyotis schlechtendahliana var. reticulata

Hedyotis schlechtendahliana var. reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined blushing geranium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, bright red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are usually ovate with a reticulate venation pattern, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
