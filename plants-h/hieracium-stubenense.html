Hieracium stubenense

Hieracium stubenense, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central dark disc.

Hieracium stubenense is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
