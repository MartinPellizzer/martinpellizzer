Hypericum × moserianum f. tricolor

Hypericum × moserianum f. tricolor, commonly known as the tricolor St. John's wort, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe.

It is characterized by its striking tri-color foliage, with leaves displaying shades of green, gold, and red. The plant produces small, yellowish-orange flowers in late summer, which are followed by red or orange seed capsules. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ecological and horticultural landscapes.
