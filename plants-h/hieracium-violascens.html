Hieracium violascens

Hieracium violascens, commonly known as the violet hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has a rosette of basal leaves and a hairy stem that supports its flowering heads.
