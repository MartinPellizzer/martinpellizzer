Hippeastrum longipes

Hippeastrum longipes, commonly known as the longipes amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Hippeastrum longipes is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
