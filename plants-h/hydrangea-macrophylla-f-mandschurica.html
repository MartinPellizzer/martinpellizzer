Hydrangea macrophylla f. mandschurica

Hydrangea macrophylla f. mandschurica, commonly known as the Mandschurian hydrangea, is a flowering shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from blue to pink depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features broad, ovate leaves with a smooth texture. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ability to change flower color, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.
