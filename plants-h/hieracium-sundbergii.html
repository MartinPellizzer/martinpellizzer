Hieracium sundbergii

Hieracium sundbergii, commonly known as Sundberg's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and basal rosettes of leaves. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and grasslands, often in moist or calcareous soils. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous small, inconspicuous florets in a composite flower head.

It is typically found in temperate regions and is considered a herbaceous perennial.
