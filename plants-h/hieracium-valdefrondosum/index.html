Hieracium Valdefrondosum

Hieracium valdefrondosum is a rare and lesser-known species of hawkweed found primarily in specific regions of Europe. This plant belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its delicate yellow flowers and hairy stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered in many areas. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and ecologically important plant.
