Hydrangea paniculata var. vegeta

Hydrangea paniculata var. vegeta, commonly known as the smooth hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink, red, or blue depending on soil pH. The plant produces cone-like flower clusters that develop from green to white or pink as they mature. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
