Hippeastrum microstigmum

Hippeastrum microstigmum, commonly known as the dwarf red hurricane lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a yellowish throat. The plant produces narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
