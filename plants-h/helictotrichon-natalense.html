Helictotrichon natalense

Helictotrichon natalense, commonly known as Natal grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. It produces small, inconspicuous blue or purple flowers in late summer.

Natal grass is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
