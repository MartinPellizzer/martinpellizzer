Hieracium murorum subsp. fennoorbicans

Hieracium murorum subsp.

fennoorbicans, commonly known as the northern hedge hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to northern Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils, often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive lobed or serrated edge.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ability to thrive in diverse environmental conditions.
