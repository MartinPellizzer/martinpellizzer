Hieraciodes reuteriana

Hieraciodes reuteriana, commonly known as the blue-eyed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue petals and yellow center. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
