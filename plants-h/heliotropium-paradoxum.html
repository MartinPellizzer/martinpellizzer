Heliotropium paradoxum

Heliotropium paradoxum, commonly known as the purple false nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its small, showy purple or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Heliotropium paradoxum is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
