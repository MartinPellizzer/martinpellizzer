Hosta plantaginea

Hosta plantaginea, commonly known as the silver queen hosta, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features broad, waxy, blue-green leaves that are slightly undulating and have a silvery-white margin. The plant produces tall, spike-like inflorescences with small, pale blue to white flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-draining soil.

Hosta plantaginea is prized for its ornamental foliage and is a popular choice for shaded garden landscapes.
