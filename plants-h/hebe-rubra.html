Hebe rubra

Hebe rubra, commonly known as the crimson hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. Hebe rubra thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage is often dark green and evergreen, providing year-round interest in the garden.
