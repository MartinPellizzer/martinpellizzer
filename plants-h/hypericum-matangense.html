Hypericum matangense

Hypericum matangense, commonly known as the Matang St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, typical of the Hypericaceae family. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum matangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
