Helichrysum rosmarinifolium

Helichrysum rosmarinifolium, commonly known as Cape rosemary, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that resemble those of rosemary, and it produces small, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. It is also valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in herbal remedies.

Helichrysum rosmarinifolium is a hardy perennial that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
