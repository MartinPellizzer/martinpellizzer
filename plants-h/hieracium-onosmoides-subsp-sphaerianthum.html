Hieracium onosmoides subsp. sphaerianthum

Hieracium onosmoides subsp.

sphaerianthum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations.

The plant has a rosette of basal leaves that are usually hairy and have a rough texture. Its flowering stems bear yellow disk flowers surrounded by ray florets, which are characteristic of the Asteraceae family.

It is well adapted to cold climates and has a slow growth rate, making it a resilient plant in harsh environments.
