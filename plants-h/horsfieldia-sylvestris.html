Horsfieldia sylvestris

Horsfieldia sylvestris, commonly known as the wild orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the forests of Malaysia, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and montane rainforests.

Horsfieldia sylvestris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
