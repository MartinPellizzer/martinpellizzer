Hieracium vernum var. rubescens

Hieracium vernum var. rubescens, commonly known as the red-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often accompanied by a distinctive reddish stem and leaves. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its common name.

The species is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
