Hieracium stenobracteum

Hieracium stenobracteum, commonly known as the narrow-bract hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
