Hylotelephium pseudospectabile

Hylotelephium pseudospectabile, commonly known as the showy stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces striking pink to red flowers in the summer months. The plant is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
