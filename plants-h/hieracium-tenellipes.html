Hieracium tenellipes

Hieracium tenellipes, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.
