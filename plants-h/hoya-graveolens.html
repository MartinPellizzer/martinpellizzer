Hoya graveolens

Hoya graveolens, commonly known as the sweet sarsaparilla, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its vines can grow quite long, making it suitable for trellises or hanging baskets.

Hoya graveolens is popular among houseplant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
