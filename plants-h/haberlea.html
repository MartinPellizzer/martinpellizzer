Haberlea

Haberlea, commonly known as the rainbow plant, is a genus of succulent plants belonging to the Aizoaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to store water in its fleshy leaves. Haberlea species often produce vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red.

These plants are popular among succulent enthusiasts due to their unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
