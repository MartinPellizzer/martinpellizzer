Hedychium × natmataungense

Hedychium × natmataungense, commonly known as the Natmataung ginger lily, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of Myanmar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy yellow flowers with a strong, fragrant scent. The plant produces broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas at higher elevations.

Hedychium × natmataungense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its striking floral display.
