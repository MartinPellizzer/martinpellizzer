Hieracium expressum

Hieracium expressum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow or orange composite flowers, which are typically arranged in a single, showy head. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and grows primarily in meadows and open woodlands. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Hieracium expressum is often found in disturbed areas and can spread aggressively through its rhizomes.
