Haplopappus reflexus

Haplopappus reflexus, commonly known as the bent pappus, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are yellow and produce achenes with pappus hairs for wind dispersal.

Haplopappus reflexus is often found in prairies and open woodlands.
