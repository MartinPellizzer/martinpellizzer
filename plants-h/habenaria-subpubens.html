Habenaria subpubens

Habenaria subpubens, commonly known as the pubescent orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. Its roots are often rhizomatous, allowing it to spread and form colonies.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
