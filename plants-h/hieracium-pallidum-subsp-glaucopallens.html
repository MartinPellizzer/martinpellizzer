Hieracium pallidum subsp. glaucopallens

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

glaucopallens, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The subspecies is distinguished by its pale greenish-yellow flowers and hairy stems.
