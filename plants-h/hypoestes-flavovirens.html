Hypoestes flavovirens

Hypoestes flavovirens, commonly known as the "Purple Heart" plant, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple foliage and clusters of small, white or yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light. Its leaves are often variegated with shades of green and purple, adding visual interest to indoor gardens.

Hypoestes flavovirens is also appreciated for its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements.
