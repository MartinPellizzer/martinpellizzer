Hoya gildingii

Hoya gildingii, commonly known as the golden hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its waxy, dark green leaves and clusters of star-shaped white flowers. The plant produces nectar-rich flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its unique foliage and blooming habit make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
