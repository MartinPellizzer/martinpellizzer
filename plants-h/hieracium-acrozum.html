Hieracium acrozum

Hieracium acrozum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

Hieracium acrozum is often found in high-altitude ecosystems and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
