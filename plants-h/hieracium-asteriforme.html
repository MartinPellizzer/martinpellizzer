Hieracium asteriforme

Hieracium asteriforme, commonly known as the star-like hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Hieracium asteriforme is a perennial species that spreads through both seeds and rhizomes.
