Hedyotis simplicissima

Hedyotis simplicissima, commonly known as the simple bladdernut, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant has small, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are tiny, white or greenish, and clustered in terminal racemes.

The fruit is a three-lobed capsule that releases tiny seeds when mature.
