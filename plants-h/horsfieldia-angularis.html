Horsfieldia angularis

Horsfieldia angularis, commonly known as the angular orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, angular-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Horsfieldia angularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
