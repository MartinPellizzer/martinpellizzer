Haageocereus setosus var. longicomus

Haageocereus setosus var. longicomus, commonly known as the long-tailed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its ribbed stems and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically clustered at the ends of the stems. Its varietal name, *longicomus*, refers to its long, slender spines.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
