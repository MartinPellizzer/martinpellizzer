Houstonia minor

Houstonia minor, commonly known as blue-eyed Mary, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features small, delicate flowers that are typically blue or violet, with white centers, blooming from late spring to early summer. The plant has wiry, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded woodlands or along stream banks.
