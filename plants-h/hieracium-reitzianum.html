Hieracium reitzianum

Hieracium reitzianum, commonly known as Reitz's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils. It has basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
