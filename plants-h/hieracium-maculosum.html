Hieracium maculosum

Hieracium maculosum, commonly known as the spotted hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, often spotted leaves. Native to Europe, it has been introduced to other parts of the world, including North America. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can form dense mats in meadows and roadsides.

It is an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive growth.
