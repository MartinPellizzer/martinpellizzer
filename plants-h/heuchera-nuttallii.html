Heuchera nuttallii

Heuchera nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's coral bells, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features striking foliage with variegated leaves that range from green to red, bronze, and purple, depending on the variety. The plant produces upright clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Heuchera nuttallii is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to add color to shaded landscapes.
