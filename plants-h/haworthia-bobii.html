Haworthia bobii

Haworthia bobii, commonly known as the Bobii Haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, green leaves with white or yellowish markings, often resembling a star pattern. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks that bear clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
