Habenaria subaequalis

Habenaria subaequalis, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base of the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
