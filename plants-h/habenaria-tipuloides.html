Habenaria tipuloides

Habenaria tipuloides, commonly known as the mosquito orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant has a slender, upright stem with several oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often resemble mosquitoes, hence its common name.

It belongs to the orchid family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique floral structure and rhizomatous growth habit.
