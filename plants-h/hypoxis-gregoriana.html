Hypoxis gregoriana

Hypoxis gregoriana, commonly known as the African Crown Orchid, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of broad, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of white or pale yellow flowers resembling orchids. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
