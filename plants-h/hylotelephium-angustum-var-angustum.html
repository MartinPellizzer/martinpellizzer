Hylotelephium angustum var. angustum

Hylotelephium angustum var. angustum, commonly known as the narrowleaf stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value contribute to its popularity among gardeners.
