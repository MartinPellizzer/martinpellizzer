Hieracium lachnodes

Hieracium lachnodes, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically has basal and stem leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Hieracium lachnodes is a perennial species native to Europe and parts of Asia.
