Hieracium sonchoides subsp. heterocerinthe

Hieracium sonchoides subsp.

heterocerinthe, commonly known as the heterocerine hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Its subspecies status reflects subtle differences in morphology, particularly in the color and structure of its flower heads.
