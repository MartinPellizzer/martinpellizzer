Hippeastrum solisii

Hippeastrum solisii, commonly known as Sol's amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It is a bulbous plant that thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

Hippeastrum solisii is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
