Hedypnois polymorpha var. mauritiana

Hedypnois polymorpha var. mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius starfish plant, is a unique succulent native to the island of Mauritius.

It features a compact, low-growing rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in late winter or early spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
