Hieracium kebeshense

Hieracium kebeshense, commonly known as the Kebesh hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Carpathian Basin.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its yellow flower heads consist of numerous small florets, and it produces achenes as its seeds.
