Hibiscus chittle-benda

Hibiscus chittle-benda, commonly known as the Natal hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Hibiscus chittle-benda thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
