Horsfieldia glabra var. oviflora

Horsfieldia glabra var. oviflora, commonly known as the ovate-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and broad, ovate-shaped leaves. The plant produces pendulous, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in lowland and hill forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
