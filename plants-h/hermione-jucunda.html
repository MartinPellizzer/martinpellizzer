Hermione jucunda

Hermione jucunda, commonly known as the joyous bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features upright stems with clusters of bell-shaped, bright blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its cheerful color and long blooming period.
