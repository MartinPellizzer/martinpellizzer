Hieracium pulverulentum

Hieracium pulverulentum, commonly known as the dusty hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a dusty appearance.

The flowers are yellow and produce small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
