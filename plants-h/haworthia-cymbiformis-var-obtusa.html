Haworthia cymbiformis var. obtusa

Haworthia cymbiformis var. obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leafed Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
