Hieracium praecox subsp. chensianum

Hieracium praecox subsp.

chensianum, commonly known as the Chinese early hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in meadows and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, clustered flowers in the summer months.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
