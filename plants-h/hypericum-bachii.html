Hypericum bachii

Hypericum bachii, commonly known as the Bach's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering shrubs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, typical of the Hypericaceae family. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
