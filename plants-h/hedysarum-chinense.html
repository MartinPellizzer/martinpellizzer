Hedysarum chinense

Hedysarum chinense, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces slender, cylindrical pods that contain small, dark seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Hedysarum chinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
