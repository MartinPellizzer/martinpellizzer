Humboltia longicaulis

Humboltia longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed humboldtia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, covered with soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Humboltia longicaulis is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
