Hieracium pallescens subsp. visocicense

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

visocicense, commonly known as the Visocinian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. It typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, often on rocky soils and open slopes.

The plant has basal and stem leaves that are typically hairy and have a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow, with a composite structure typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, inconspicuous flower heads.
