Heliotropium ditrichum

Heliotropium ditrichum, commonly known as the silverleaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing herb with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and delicate flowers make it a visually appealing addition to gardens and landscapes.
