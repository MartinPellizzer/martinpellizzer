Habenaria microcorys

Habenaria microcorys, commonly known as the small-corned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. The plant has a slender, upright stem with few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the soil.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and specific ecological requirements.
