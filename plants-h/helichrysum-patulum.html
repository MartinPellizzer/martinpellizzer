Helichrysum patulum

Helichrysum patulum, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, dandelion-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, open habitats. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Helichrysum patulum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
