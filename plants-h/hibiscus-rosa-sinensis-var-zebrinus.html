Hibiscus rosa-sinensis var. zebrinus

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis var. zebrinus, commonly known as the Zebra Hibiscus, is a tropical shrub characterized by its striking, multicolored foliage.

Its leaves are broad, ovate, and often feature a distinctive zebra-like pattern of dark green and cream or white stripes. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically with a prominent yellow or orange center. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is popular in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant appearance and adaptability to container gardening.
