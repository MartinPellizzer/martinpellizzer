Hieracium thlaspidifolium

Hieracium thlaspidifolium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium thlaspidifolium is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
