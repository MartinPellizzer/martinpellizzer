Hieracium friesii var. stewartii

Hieracium friesii var. stewartii, commonly known as Stewart's common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, including North America.

Due to its hardy nature and adaptability, it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
