Homalomena lunduensis

Homalomena lunduensis, commonly known as the Lundu arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in some regions.
