Hypericum balearicum var. ochroleucum

Hypericum balearicum var. ochroleucum, commonly known as the Balearic St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces orange-red berries that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native habitat.
