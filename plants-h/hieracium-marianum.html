Hieracium marianum

Hieracium marianum, commonly known as Mary's thimble, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium marianum is often considered a weed but has historical uses in folk medicine.
