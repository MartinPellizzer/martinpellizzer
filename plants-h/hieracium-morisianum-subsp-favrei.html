Hieracium morisianum subsp. favrei

Hieracium morisianum subsp.

favrei, commonly known as the Morisian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
