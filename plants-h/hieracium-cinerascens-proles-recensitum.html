Hieracium cinerascens proles recensitum

Hieracium cinerascens proles recensitum, commonly known as the cinerascens hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to white in color. The plant features basal and alternate leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its invasive tendencies, it can outcompete native flora in certain regions.
