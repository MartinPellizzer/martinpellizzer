Hieracium asyngamicum

Hieracium asyngamicum, commonly known as the smooth hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that consist of both ray and disk florets. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a slightly rough texture. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

This species is often found in open habitats and is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and seeds.
