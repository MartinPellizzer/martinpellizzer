Herminium mannii

Herminium mannii, commonly known as the African lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and grasslands. It is characterized by its intricate root system and ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

Herminium mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
