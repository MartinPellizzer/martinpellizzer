Huernia whitesloaneana

Huernia whitesloaneana, commonly known as the White Sloane's Husk or Sloane's moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a waxy, white coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
