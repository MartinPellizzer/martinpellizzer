Hedyotis kamputensis

Hedyotis kamputensis, commonly known as the Kampti trailing geranium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with red or purple markings. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptic, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Hedyotis kamputensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
