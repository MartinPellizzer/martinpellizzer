Hieracium megalomeres

Hieracium megalomeres, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant typically grows up to 1 meter in height, featuring basal rosettes of broad, hairy leaves. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single terminal head, often surrounded by bracts.

Hieracium megalomeres is known for its aggressive growth and has been classified as an invasive species in some regions.
