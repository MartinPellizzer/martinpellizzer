Helichrysum pullulum

Helichrysum pullulum, commonly known as the yellow star flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that resemble small suns. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, with a slight aromatic scent.

Helichrysum pullulum is also known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
