Habenaria spithamaea

Habenaria spithamaea, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
