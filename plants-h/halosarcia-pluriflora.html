Halosarcia pluriflora

Halosarcia pluriflora, commonly known as the coastal hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red.
