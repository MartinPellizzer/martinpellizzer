Hieracium gomizii

Hieracium gomizii, commonly known as the Gomizii hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and Eastern Europe, where it thrives in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically hairy and have a rough texture, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Hieracium gomizii is adapted to harsh climatic conditions and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
