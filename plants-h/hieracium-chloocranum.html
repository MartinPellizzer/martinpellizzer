Hieracium chloocranum

Hieracium chloocranum, commonly known as the green-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a greenish base.

Hieracium chloocranum is often found in temperate climates and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
