Haloragis acutangula f. inflata

Haloragis acutangula f. inflata, commonly known as the inflated spike rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Haloragaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescence that is often inflated, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in shallow water or damp soil. Its leaves are grass-like and arranged in a tight spiral, while its flowers are small and clustered in spike-like structures.

This variant is notable for its adaptability to aquatic conditions and is often found in temperate regions.
