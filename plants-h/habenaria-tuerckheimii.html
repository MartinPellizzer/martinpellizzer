Habenaria tuerckheimii

Habenaria tuerckheimii, commonly known as the Tuerckheim's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Indonesia. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
