Hieracium cincinnatum subsp. virosiforme

Hieracium cincinnatum subsp.

virosiforme, commonly known as the toxic dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, lobed leaves.
