Haworthia limifolia var. diploidea

Haworthia limifolia var. diploidea, commonly known as the Cape mountain haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with white or pale blue markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, making it well-suited for arid environments. It produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
