Heteropanax fragrans var. serrata

Heteropanax fragrans var. serrata, commonly known as the serrated fragrant heteropanax, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically alternate and have serrated margins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
