Hieracium oppletiforme

Hieracium oppletiforme, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is an erect perennial that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.
