Haworthia cooperi var. picturata

Haworthia cooperi var. picturata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, translucent tubular leaves with dark green markings that resemble zebra stripes, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
