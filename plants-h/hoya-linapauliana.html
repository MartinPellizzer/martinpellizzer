Hoya linapauliana

Hoya linapauliana, commonly known as the Linapauliana Waffle Plant, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.

Hoya linapauliana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
