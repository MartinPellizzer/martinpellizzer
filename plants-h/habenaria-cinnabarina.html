Habenaria cinnabarina

Habenaria cinnabarina, commonly known as the red-tipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-tipped petals and a slender, erect stem. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and bloom during the summer months.

Habenaria cinnabarina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
