Hypericum taisanense

Hypericum taisanense, commonly known as the Taisan goldthread, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas.

The leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
