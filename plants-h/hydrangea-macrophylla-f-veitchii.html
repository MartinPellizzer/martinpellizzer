Hydrangea macrophylla f. veitchii

Hydrangea macrophylla f. veitchii, commonly known as the Veitchii hydrangea, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from blue to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant produces broad, serrated leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in partial shade and requires moist, well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
