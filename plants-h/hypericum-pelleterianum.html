Hypericum pelleterianum

Hypericum pelleterianum, commonly known as the evergreen St. John's wort, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves that remain on the stems throughout the year, contributing to its evergreen nature. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its distinctive red, woody stems and medicinal properties.
