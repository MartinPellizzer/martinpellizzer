Hieracium Jordanii Subsp. Eriosphaera

Hieracium jordanii subsp. eriosphaera is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of the Middle East. It belongs to the dandelion family, known for its distinctive composite flowers and milky sap. This subspecies is characterized by its dense, woolly covering on its stems and leaves. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and endangered plant species.
