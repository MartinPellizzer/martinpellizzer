Hemionitis alismifolia

Hemionitis alismifolia, commonly known as the water fern, is a rare aquatic plant native to the wetlands of eastern Asia.

The plant thrives in shallow, freshwater environments with abundant sunlight and nutrient-rich water. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are located on the underside of the fronds and produce spores for propagation.
