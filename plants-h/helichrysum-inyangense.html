Helichrysum inyangense

Helichrysum inyangense, commonly known as the yellow star flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and is often found in grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are composed of ray florets surrounded by a central disk.

Helichrysum inyangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
