Hibiscus hispidissimus

Hibiscus hispidissimus, commonly known as the rough hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Hibiscus hispidissimus thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
