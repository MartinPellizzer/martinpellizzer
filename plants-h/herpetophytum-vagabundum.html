Herpetophytum vagabundum

Herpetophytum vagabundum, commonly known as the wandering orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial herb with slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of small, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters.
