Hieracium polysteleum

Hieracium polysteleum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and its leaves are typically lance-shaped with hairy surfaces. It is characterized by its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes and stolons, making it an invasive species in some regions.

Hieracium polysteleum is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny, well-drained areas.
