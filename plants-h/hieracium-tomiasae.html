Hieracium tomiasae

Hieracium tomiasae, commonly known as the Tomiasa's hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, particularly in the Alps. The plant is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in summer. It thrives in rocky and gravelly soils, often growing in alpine meadows and open slopes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
