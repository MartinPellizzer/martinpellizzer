Haworthia integra

Haworthia integra, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern with thick, fleshy leaves that have distinctive white to yellowish markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for low-maintenance gardening.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor collections.
