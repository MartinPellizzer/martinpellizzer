Habenaria stylites subsp. johnsonii

Habenaria stylites subsp.

johnsonii, commonly known as the Johnson's stylite orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a loose inflorescence. It prefers moist, shaded habitats and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and specialized orchid species.
