Habenaria dusenii

Habenaria dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as forest understories.

Habenaria dusenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
