Hieracium gyioleptum

Hieracium gyioleptum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium gyioleptum is a perennial species that can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable environments.
