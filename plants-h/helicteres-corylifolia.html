Helicteres corylifolia

Helicteres corylifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf cowtree, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
