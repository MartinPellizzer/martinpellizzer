Huberia limae

Huberia limae, commonly known as the Peruvian lily, is a striking plant native to the Andean regions of Peru.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in cool, moist conditions.

Huberia limae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable climates.
