Hymenophyllum reinwardtii

Hymenophyllum reinwardtii, commonly known as the reinwardt's filmy fern, is a small epiphytic fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds and are protected by a membranous cover.

The plant's unique morphology and ecological preferences make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
