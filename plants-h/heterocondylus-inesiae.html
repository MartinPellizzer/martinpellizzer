Heterocondylus inesiae

Heterocondylus inesiae, commonly known as the Inesia orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces small, clustered blooms in shades of white and pink.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
