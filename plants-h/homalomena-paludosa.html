Homalomena paludosa

Homalomena paludosa, commonly known as the swamp arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet, marshy environments. The plant features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating in water. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread across wetland areas.

Homalomena paludosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in aquatic conditions.
