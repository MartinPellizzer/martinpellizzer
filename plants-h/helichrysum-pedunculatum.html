Helichrysum pedunculatum

Helichrysum pedunculatum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble small suns. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Helichrysum pedunculatum is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
