Hieracium lecithodes

Hieracium lecithodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium lecithodes is often found in open habitats and can form dense colonies, sometimes becoming invasive in certain regions.
