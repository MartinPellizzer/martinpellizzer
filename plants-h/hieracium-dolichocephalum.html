Hieracium dolichocephalum

Hieracium dolichocephalum, commonly known as the long-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, with ray florets that are longer than the disk florets. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

Hieracium dolichocephalum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate climates.
