Hieracium phaliotrichum

Hieracium phaliotrichum, commonly known as the purple meadowhawk, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the variety. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
