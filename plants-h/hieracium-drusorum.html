Hieracium drusorum

Hieracium drusorum, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features upright stems with rough, hairy surfaces and produces yellow flowers in composite inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive rough texture.

Hieracium drusorum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
