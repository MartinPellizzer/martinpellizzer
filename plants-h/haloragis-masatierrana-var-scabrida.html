Haloragis masatierrana var. scabrida

Haloragis masatierrana var. scabrida, commonly known as the rough haloragis, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are covered in a rough, sandpaper-like texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental foliage make it a valuable addition to xeriscapic gardens.
