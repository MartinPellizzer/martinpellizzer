Hieracium Lachenalii Subsp. Garckeanum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp. garckeanum is a rare and elusive plant found in specific alpine habitats. It belongs to the dandelion family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and hairy leaves. This subspecies is native to southern Europe and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Due to its limited distribution and fragile ecosystem, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique botanical species from extinction.
