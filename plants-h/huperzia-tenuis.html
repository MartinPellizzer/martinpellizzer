Huperzia tenuis

Huperzia tenuis, commonly known as the slender huperzia, is a species of clubmoss belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, linear leaves and a compact, low-growing habit. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a distinctive sporophyte structure with spore-producing cones at the tips of its stems.

Its morphology reflects adaptations to its woodland and forest floor habitats.
