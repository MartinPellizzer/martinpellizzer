Hypericum sonderi

Hypericum sonderi, commonly known as the Sonder's St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, opposite leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hypericum sonderi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
