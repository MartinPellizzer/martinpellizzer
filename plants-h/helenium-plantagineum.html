Helenium plantagineum

Helenium plantagineum, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead, is a perennial flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in a compact, upright form. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Helenium plantagineum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a nectar source for pollinators.
