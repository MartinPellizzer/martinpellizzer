Hibiscus marenitensis

Hibiscus marenitensis, commonly known as the Marenitensis hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in humid, shaded environments. Hibiscus marenitensis belongs to the Malvaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of moisture.
