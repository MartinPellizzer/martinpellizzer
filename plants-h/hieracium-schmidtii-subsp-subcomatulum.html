Hieracium schmidtii subsp. subcomatulum

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

subcomatulum, commonly known as the Schmidt's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dense, woolly foliage and shorter, more compact growth compared to other members of the species. It typically thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
