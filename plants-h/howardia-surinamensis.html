Howardia surinamensis

Howardia surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Suriname and Guyana. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Howardia surinamensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and unique appearance.
