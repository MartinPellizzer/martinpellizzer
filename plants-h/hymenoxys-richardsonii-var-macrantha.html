Hymenoxys richardsonii var. macrantha

Hymenoxys richardsonii var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered hymenoxys, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically showy blooms. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces tall, branched stems that bear numerous yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
