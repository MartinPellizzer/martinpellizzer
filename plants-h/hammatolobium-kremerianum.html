Hammatolobium kremerianum

Hammatolobium kremerianum, commonly known as the desert milkweed, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant has hairy foliage and produces capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It is adapted to survive in dry, sandy soils and can tolerate high temperatures and limited water availability.
