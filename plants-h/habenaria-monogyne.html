Habenaria monogyne

Habenaria monogyne, commonly known as the single-stamen orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and swamps. Its flowers have a single stamen, which is a distinctive botanical feature.

Habenaria monogyne is a terrestrial orchid that relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.
