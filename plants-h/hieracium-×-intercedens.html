Hieracium × intercedens

Hieracium × intercedens, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to various parts of the world, often growing in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Hieracium × intercedens is an annual or biennial plant that can spread rapidly through its extensive root system.
