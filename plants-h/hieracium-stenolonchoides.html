Hieracium stenolonchoides

Hieracium stenolonchoides, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of leaves, with flowering stems that bear yellowish to reddish composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and often covered with fine hairs, while its flowering heads consist of numerous small florets.

Hieracium stenolonchoides is native to parts of Europe and is considered a minor weed in agricultural settings.
