Haemanthus arabicus

Haemanthus arabicus, commonly known as the red-stemmed haemanthus, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen rosette of dark green, strap-like leaves that form a dense, compact shape. The plant produces striking red or orange tubular flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a desirable ornamental plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
