Hieracium sparsum subsp. telekianum

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

telekianum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads that produce yellow disk flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in high-altitude meadows and slopes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and localized species.
