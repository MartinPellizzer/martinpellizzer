Helichrysum milleri

Helichrysum milleri, commonly known as the golden crown, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flowers that resemble a crown. The plant is evergreen, with small, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum milleri is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
