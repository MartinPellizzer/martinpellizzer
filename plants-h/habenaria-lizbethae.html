Habenaria lizbethae

Habenaria lizbethae, commonly known as Lizbeth's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with purple markings, and it produces small, greenish-brown seed pods.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
