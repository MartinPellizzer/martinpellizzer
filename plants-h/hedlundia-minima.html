Hedlundia minima

Hedlundia minima, commonly known as the dwarf hedlundia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Hedlundia minima is often found in dune environments and is valued for its resilience and ornamental appeal.
