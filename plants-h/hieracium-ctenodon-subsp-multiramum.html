Hieracium ctenodon subsp. multiramum

Hieracium ctenodon subsp.

multiramum, commonly known as the many-branched hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe, particularly in regions such as France and Italy.

The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are typically basal and lobed, with a rough texture.

It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
