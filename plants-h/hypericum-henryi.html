Hypericum henryi

Hypericum henryi, commonly known as the Chinese St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has woody stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Hypericum henryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
