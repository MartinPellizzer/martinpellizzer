Helleborine latifolia f. przemysliensis

Helleborine latifolia f. przemysliensis, commonly known as the Przemyslensis Helleborine, is a rare orchid species native to specific regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and unique floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Its flowers, which are usually greenish-yellow with purple markings, bloom in late spring to early summer.

This orchid is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal.
