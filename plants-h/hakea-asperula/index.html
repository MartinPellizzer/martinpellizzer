Hakea Asperula

Hakea asperula is a unique shrub native to Australia known for its distinctive appearance and ecological importance. This plant features spiny leaves and clusters of bright red flowers that attract native pollinators. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal regions. Hakea asperula is valued for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh conditions. Its role in supporting local wildlife makes it an important species in Australian ecosystems.
