Haworthia fluffa

Haworthia fluffa, commonly known as the Woolly Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, white to pale green tubercles that give it a fluffy appearance, and its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Haworthia fluffa is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
