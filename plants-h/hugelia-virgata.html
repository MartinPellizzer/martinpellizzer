Hugelia virgata

Hugelia virgata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in shrub form, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Hugelia virgata thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in arid regions.
