Hieracium columniforme

Hieracium columniforme, commonly known as the columnar hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features upright, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

Hieracium columniforme is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many regions.
