Holoschoenus panormitanus

Holoschoenus panormitanus, commonly known as the Panormitanus grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green to brown in color. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, with a smooth surface and a prominent midrib.
