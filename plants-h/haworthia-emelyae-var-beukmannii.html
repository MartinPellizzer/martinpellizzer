Haworthia emelyae var. beukmannii

Haworthia emelyae var. beukmannii, commonly known as the Beukmann's Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that form a rosette, typically ranging in color from pale green to bluish-gray with distinct white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
