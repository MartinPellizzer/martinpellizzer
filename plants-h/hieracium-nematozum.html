Hieracium nematozum

Hieracium nematozum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies.

Hieracium nematozum is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
