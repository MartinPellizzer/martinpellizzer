Habenaria physuriformis

Habenaria physuriformis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and delicate, pale green or white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. Its inflorescence consists of a single raceme bearing several small, fragrant flowers.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
