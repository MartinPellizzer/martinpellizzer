Hieracium stewartii

Hieracium stewartii, commonly known as Stewart's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It produces yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer.

Hieracium stewartii is known for its low-growing habit and is often found in open, sunny areas at high elevations.
