Hydrolea inermis

Hydrolea inermis, commonly known as the slender hydrolea, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Hydrolea inermis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
