Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. fulgidum

Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. fulgidum, commonly known as the golden hurricane lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep yellow, often with a red or orange throat. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are broad and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for gardens and container planting.
