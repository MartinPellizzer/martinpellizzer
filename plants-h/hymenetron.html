Hymenetron

Hymenetron, commonly known as the silky oak, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces showy, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which attract a variety of pollinators. Its bark is smooth and light gray, often exuding a milky sap when damaged.

Hymenetron is native to Australia and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
