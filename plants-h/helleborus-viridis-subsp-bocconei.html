Helleborus viridis subsp. bocconei

Helleborus viridis subsp.

bocconei, commonly known as the green hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features dark green, waxy leaves that are broadly ovate to elliptical in shape.

The plant produces clusters of pale green to yellowish flowers with showy, petal-like bracts. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in horticulture.
