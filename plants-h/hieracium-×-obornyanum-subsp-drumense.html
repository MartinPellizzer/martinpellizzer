Hieracium × obornyanum subsp. drumense

Hieracium × obornyanum subsp.

drumense, commonly known as the Drummond's hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, typical of many hawkweed species.

This subspecies is native to parts of North America, particularly in the western United States. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it identifiable by its composite flower heads and robust growth habit.
