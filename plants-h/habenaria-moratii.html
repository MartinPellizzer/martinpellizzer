Habenaria moratii

Habenaria moratii, commonly known as the Moratii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinct lip petal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
