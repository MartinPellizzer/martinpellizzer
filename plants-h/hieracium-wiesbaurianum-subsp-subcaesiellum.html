Hieracium wiesbaurianum subsp. subcaesiellum

Hieracium wiesbaurianum subsp.

subcaesiellum, commonly known as the pale-yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to pale orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded conditions. Its leaves are basal, lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

The subspecies is distinguished by its relatively smaller flower heads and pale coloration compared to other members of the Hieracium genus.
