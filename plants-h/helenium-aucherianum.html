Helenium aucherianum

Helenium aucherianum, commonly known as the American snefron, is a striking perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red ray florets and yellow disk flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower landscapes.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
