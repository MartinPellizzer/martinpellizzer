Hemsleya dolichocarpa

Hemsleya dolichocarpa, commonly known as the long-stemmed hemsleya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.
