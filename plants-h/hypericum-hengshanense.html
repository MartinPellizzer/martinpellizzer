Hypericum hengshanense

Hypericum hengshanense, commonly known as the Hengshan St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, reddish-brown fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
