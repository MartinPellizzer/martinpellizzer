Habranthus punctatus

Habranthus punctatus, commonly known as the spotted bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple. Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a desirable ornamental plant.
