Hibiscus populneus

Hibiscus populneus, commonly known as the white hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hibiscus populneus thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
