Hymenoxys californica var. coronaria

Hymenoxys californica var. coronaria, commonly known as the California crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
