Hieracium globulariiforme

Hieracium globulariiforme, commonly known as the globe hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, globular inflorescence composed of numerous small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are upright and often branched.

The species is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
