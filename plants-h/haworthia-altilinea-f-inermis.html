Haworthia altilinea f. inermis

Haworthia altilinea f. inermis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its compact size and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
