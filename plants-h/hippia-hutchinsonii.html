Hippia hutchinsonii

Hippia hutchinsonii, commonly known as the Hutchinson's rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and public landscapes.
