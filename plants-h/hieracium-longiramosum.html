Hieracium longiramosum

Hieracium longiramosum, commonly known as the long-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disc florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in certain environments.
