Hieracium prenanthoides subsp. bupleurifolioides

Hieracium prenanthoides subsp.

bupleurifolioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender stems and yellow flowers.

The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy surface. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

This subspecies is often found in disturbed soils and can form dense colonies.
