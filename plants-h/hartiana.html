Hartiana

Hartiana, commonly known as the Hartiana orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its elegant, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically features strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Hartiana species are native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia.

They are appreciated by horticulturists for their ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
