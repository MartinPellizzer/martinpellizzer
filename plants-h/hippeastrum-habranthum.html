Hippeastrum habranthum

Hippeastrum habranthum, commonly known as the Brazilian belladonna lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically orange or red with a distinctive white or pink interior. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum habranthum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
