Heligme

Heligme, commonly known as the blue helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, blue or purple flower heads that resemble daisies, which are often used in floral arrangements. Heligme is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its aromatic foliage and vibrant flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
