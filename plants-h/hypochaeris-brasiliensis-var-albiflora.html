Hypochaeris brasiliensis var. albiflora

Hypochaeris brasiliensis var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered buttercup, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow or white flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its dissemination.

The leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.
