Habenaria hydrophila

Habenaria hydrophila, commonly known as the water orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors or near water sources. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that produces multiple stems each year. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, arranged in a raceme, and are known for their delicate, orchid-like appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
