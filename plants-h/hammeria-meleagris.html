Hammeria meleagris

Hammeria meleagris, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces violet or purple blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Hammeria meleagris is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
