Hieracium nigelliceps

Hieracium nigelliceps, commonly known as the noble hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking yellow to orange composite flower heads, which are composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and may have a hairy texture.

Hieracium nigelliceps is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
