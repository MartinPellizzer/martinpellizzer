Haemanthus humilis

Haemanthus humilis, commonly known as the rainforest snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a short, thick rhizome and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Haemanthus humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
