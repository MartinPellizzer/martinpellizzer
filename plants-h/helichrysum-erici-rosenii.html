Helichrysum erici-rosenii

Helichrysum erici-rosenii, commonly known as the silverleaf helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant has hairy, silver-gray leaves that give it its common name and help reduce water loss in arid conditions. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.

Helichrysum erici-rosenii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
