Haworthiopsis × kewensis

Haworthiopsis × kewensis, commonly known as the Kew haworthia, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
