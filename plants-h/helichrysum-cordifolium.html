Helichrysum cordifolium

Helichrysum cordifolium, commonly known as the heart-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Helichrysum cordifolium is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
