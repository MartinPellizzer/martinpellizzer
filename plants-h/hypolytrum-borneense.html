Hypolytrum borneense

Hypolytrum borneense, commonly known as the Bornean yellow lily, is a flowering plant native to the island of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which are often clustered in inflorescences. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
