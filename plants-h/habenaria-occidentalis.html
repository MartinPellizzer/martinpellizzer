Habenaria occidentalis

Habenaria occidentalis, commonly known as the Western Habenaria, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of North America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces tall, erect stems with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate moisture and light conditions.
