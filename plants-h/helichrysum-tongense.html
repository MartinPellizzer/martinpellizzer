Helichrysum tongense

Helichrysum tongense, commonly known as the African immortelle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that retain their color when dried. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils. Its leaves are linear and aromatic, often used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory properties.

Helichrysum tongense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
