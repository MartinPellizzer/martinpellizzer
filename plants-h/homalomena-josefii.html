Homalomena josefii

Homalomena josefii, commonly known as the Josef's arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped flowers that are typically green with a white or yellow spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Homalomena josefii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
