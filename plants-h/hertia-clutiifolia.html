Hertia clutiifolia

Hertia clutiifolia, commonly known as the coastal hertia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas, where it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes and are usually pink or purple in color.

Hertia clutiifolia is well adapted to salt spray and wind, making it a hardy species suitable for coastal gardens.
