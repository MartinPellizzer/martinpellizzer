Hypericum yezoense

Hypericum yezoense, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan and parts of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its yellow, five-petaled flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum yezoense is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.
