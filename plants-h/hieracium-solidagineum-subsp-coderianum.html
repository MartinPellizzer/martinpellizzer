Hieracium solidagineum subsp. coderianum

Hieracium solidagineum subsp.

coderianum, commonly known as the Coderianum hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically features basal leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, riverbanks, and woodland edges.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
