Hieracium cymosum subsp. meizocephalum

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

meizocephalum, commonly known as the meizocephalum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are usually hairy and have a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow and borne in a single head, which is often surrounded by bracts.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is part of the Asteraceae family.
