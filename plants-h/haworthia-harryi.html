Haworthia harryi

Haworthia harryi, commonly known as the Harry's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves with distinctive white tubercles and translucent windows. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Haworthia harryi is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of care.
