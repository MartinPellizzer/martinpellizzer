Habenaria tortilis

Habenaria tortilis, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
