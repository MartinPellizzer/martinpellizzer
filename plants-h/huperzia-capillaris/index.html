Huperzia Capillaris

Huperzia capillaris is a rare and ancient fern-like plant found in specific forest habitats. It belongs to the family Huperziaceae and is known for its unique, needle-like leaves. This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often seen growing on rocky substrates. Its delicate structure and slow growth make it a fascinating subject for botanical study. Due to habitat loss, Huperzia capillaris is considered a vulnerable species in many regions.
