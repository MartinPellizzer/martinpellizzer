Hieracium praecox subsp. fallens

Hieracium praecox subsp.

fallens, commonly known as the early hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom in early summer. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

This subspecies is distinguished by its shorter stature and earlier flowering time compared to other members of the species.
