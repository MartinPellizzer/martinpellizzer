Hedera × soroksarensis

Hedera × soroksarensis, commonly known as the Soroksa ivy, is a hybrid species of ivy that combines traits from different Hedera species.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are typically heart-shaped with a notched or lobed margin. This plant is a vigorous climber, capable of attaching itself to various surfaces using its aerial roots. It prefers cool, moist environments and can thrive in partial to full shade.

Hedera × soroksarensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to different garden settings.
