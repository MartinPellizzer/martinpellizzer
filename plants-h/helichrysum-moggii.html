Helichrysum moggii

Helichrysum moggii, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, yellow to golden-orange flowers. The plant is known for its aromatic foliage and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Its long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for dried floral arrangements.
