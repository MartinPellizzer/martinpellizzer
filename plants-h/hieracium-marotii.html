Hieracium marotii

Hieracium marotii, commonly known as the Italian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and Southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium marotii is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
