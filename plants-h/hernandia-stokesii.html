Hernandia stokesii

Hernandia stokesii, commonly known as Stokes' hernandia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests.
