Hieracium acropoecilum

Hieracium acropoecilum, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its clustered growth habit and distinctive purple to pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy areas and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Hieracium acropoecilum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
