Hieracium poliotrachelum

Hieracium poliotrachelum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped with hairy surfaces.

Hieracium poliotrachelum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
