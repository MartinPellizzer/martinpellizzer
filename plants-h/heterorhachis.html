Heterorhachis

Heterorhachis, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped flowers. The plants typically grow as epiphytes or lithophytes, often found on trees or rocks in humid environments. Heterorhachis species have narrow, linear leaves and produce small, fragrant blooms in various colors.

These orchids are valued for their ornamental appeal and are often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
