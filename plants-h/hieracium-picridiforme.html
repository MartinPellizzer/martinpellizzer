Hieracium picridiforme

Hieracium picridiforme, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium picridiforme is often found in open habitats and can spread aggressively through rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
