Hypolytrum polystachyum var. depauperatum

Hypolytrum polystachyum var. depauperatum, commonly known as the slender spike rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, cylindrical stems and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, such as along stream banks or in swampy areas. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight sheen, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is relatively low-maintenance and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil in wet environments.
