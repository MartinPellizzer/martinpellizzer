Hiraea elliptica

Hiraea elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hiraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features elliptical, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Hiraea elliptica is adapted to arid environments and is often found in open woodlands or along riverbanks.
