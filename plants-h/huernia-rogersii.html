Huernia rogersii

Huernia rogersii, commonly known as the Rogers' horned cucumber, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its unique, ribbed, and often spiny stems. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments.

Huernia rogersii is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
