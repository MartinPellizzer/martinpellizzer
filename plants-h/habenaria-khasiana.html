Habenaria khasiana

Habenaria khasiana, commonly known as the Khasi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the northeastern regions of India.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
