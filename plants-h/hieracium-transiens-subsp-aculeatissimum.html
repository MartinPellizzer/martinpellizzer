Hieracium transiens subsp. aculeatissimum

Hieracium transiens subsp.

aculeatissimum, commonly known as the prickly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its densely hairy, often prickly stems and leaves. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides.

The plant is an annual or biennial herb, with a short life cycle adapted to seasonal environments.
