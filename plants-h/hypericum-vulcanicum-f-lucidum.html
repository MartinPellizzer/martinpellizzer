Hypericum vulcanicum f. lucidum

Hypericum vulcanicum f. lucidum, commonly known as the glossy St. John's wort, is a rare and striking variant of the St. John's wort genus.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Native to specific regions in Italy, it thrives in rocky, sun-exposed environments. The plant is notable for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in horticulture.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and limited distribution.
