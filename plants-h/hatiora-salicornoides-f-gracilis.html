Hatiora salicornoides f. gracilis

Hatiora salicornoides f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender Christmas cactus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, branching structure and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in winter. The leaves are typically short and rounded, forming dense clusters along the stems. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor environments with moderate light and humidity.

It requires well-draining soil and careful watering to prevent root rot.
