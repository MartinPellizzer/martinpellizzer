Hibiscus rosiflorus var. plenus

Hibiscus rosiflorus var. plenus, commonly known as the full-flowered rosy hibiscus, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a prominent central pistil. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are velvety to the touch. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Hibiscus rosiflorus var. plenus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
