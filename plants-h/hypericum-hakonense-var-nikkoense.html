Hypericum hakonense var. nikkoense

Hypericum hakonense var. nikkoense, commonly known as Nikko yellow root, is a low-growing shrub native to Japan.

It features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to its habitat. The plant has woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
