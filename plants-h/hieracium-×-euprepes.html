Hieracium × euprepes

Hieracium × euprepes, commonly known as the meadowhawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its composite flowers, which consist of both ray and disk florets, typically displaying shades of yellow to orange. The plant produces achenes, which are small, dry fruits that disperse via wind, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

Hieracium × euprepes is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
