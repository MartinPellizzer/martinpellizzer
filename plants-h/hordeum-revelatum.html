Hordeum revelatum

Hordeum revelatum, commonly known as the red barley, is a species of wild barley native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its slender stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces pale yellow to reddish flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Hordeum revelatum is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, and it plays a role in ecological succession and soil stabilization.
