Hieracium zizianum subsp. subfarinosum

Hieracium zizianum subsp.

subfarinosum, commonly known as the farinose hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, often glandular stems and leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are typically lanceolate and covered with fine, whitish to grayish pubescence.

This subspecies is often found in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes.
