Haworthiopsis venosa var. woolleyi

Haworthiopsis venosa var. woolleyi, commonly known as the Woolley's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks that bear small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or container planting due to its drought tolerance and minimal care requirements.

Its unique appearance and ease of cultivation make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
