Hirpicium echinus

Hirpicium echinus, commonly known as the spiny hirpicium, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, spherical fruiting bodies that resemble small, rough spheres. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense, spherical clusters.

Hirpicium echinus is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is often found in disturbed soils.
