Hieracium Murorum Subsp. Crepidiflorum

Hieracium murorum subsp. crepidiflorum is a lesser-known member of the daisy family known for its delicate yellow flowers and hairy foliage. It thrives in rocky and disturbed soils, often found in alpine and subalpine regions across Europe. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique flower shape and smaller, more compact growth compared to other Hieracium species. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and rarely cultivated. Understanding this plant offers insight into the resilience and diversity of wild flora in challenging environments.
