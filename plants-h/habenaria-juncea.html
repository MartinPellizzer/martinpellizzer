Habenaria juncea

Habenaria juncea, commonly known as the slender marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to various regions in Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often near water bodies or in swampy areas. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single or few flowers that are usually greenish-white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are characterized by their delicate, spurred structure typical of the orchid family.

Habenaria juncea is noted for its ability to thrive in humid environments and is often found in association with other moisture-loving flora.
