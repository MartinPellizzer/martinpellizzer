Hutchinsia alba

Hutchinsia alba, commonly known as the white hutchinsia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. Its flowers are small and white, often arranged in a dense cluster.

Hutchinsia alba is known for its resilience to coastal conditions and is often found in dune environments.
