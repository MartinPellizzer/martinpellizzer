Hibiscus byrnesii

Hibiscus byrnesii, commonly known as the Byrnes hibiscus, is a species of flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with five petals and a prominent yellow or orange center. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland habitats. Hibiscus byrnesii is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height, featuring broad, heart-shaped leaves.

It plays an important role in the local ecosystem and is also valued for its ornamental qualities.
