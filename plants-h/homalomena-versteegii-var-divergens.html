Homalomena versteegii var. divergens

Homalomena versteegii var. divergens, commonly known as the Divergent Arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shaded garden areas and indoor cultivation.
