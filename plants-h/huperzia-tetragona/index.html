Huperzia Tetragona

Huperzia tetragona is a unique fern-like plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America. It is known for its distinctive appearance, featuring narrow, spirally arranged leaves that give it a delicate, lace-like look. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its simple structure and striking form make it a popular choice for terrariums and indoor gardening. Despite its beauty, Huperzia tetragona remains relatively unknown to the general public.
