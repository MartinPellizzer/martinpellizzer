Hohenbergia castellanosii

Hohenbergia castellanosii, commonly known as the Castellanos' hohenbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Ecuador and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit. The plant features dense, fleshy, sword-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It produces showy, inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements in cultivation.
