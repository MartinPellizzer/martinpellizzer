Hieracium elassonanthelum

Hieracium elassonanthelum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in meadows and grasslands. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, often with a hairy surface.

Hieracium elassonanthelum is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
