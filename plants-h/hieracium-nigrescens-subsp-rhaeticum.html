Hieracium nigrescens subsp. rhaeticum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

rhaeticum, commonly known as the Rhaetian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in a dense, terminal cluster.

The plant is native to the Alps and is typically found in rocky or grassy slopes. It has a low-growing habit and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Hieracium genus.
