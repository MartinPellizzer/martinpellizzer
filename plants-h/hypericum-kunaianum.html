Hypericum kunaianum

Hypericum kunaianum, commonly known as Kunaian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

Hypericum kunaianum is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance.
