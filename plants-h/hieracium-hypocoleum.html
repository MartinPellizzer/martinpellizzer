Hieracium hypocoleum

Hieracium hypocoleum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium hypocoleum is often found in open habitats and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
