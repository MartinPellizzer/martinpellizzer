Heteromorpha arborescens var. platyphylla

Heteromorpha arborescens var. platyphylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed heteromorpha, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a distinctive yellow or greenish hue.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid environments.
