Helianthemum hadedense

Helianthemum hadedense, commonly known as the Algerian rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in coastal and mountainous areas. Its compact size and drought tolerance make it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helianthemum hadedense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
