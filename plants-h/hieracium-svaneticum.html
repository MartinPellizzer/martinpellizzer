Hieracium svaneticum

Hieracium svaneticum, commonly known as the Svanetian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are basal and often have a rough, hairy texture.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
