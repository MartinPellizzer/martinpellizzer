Hieracium reticulatum var. reayense

Hieracium reticulatum var. reayense, commonly known as the Reay's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It has finely divided, serrated leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
