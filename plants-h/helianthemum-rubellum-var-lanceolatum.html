Helianthemum rubellum var. lanceolatum

Helianthemum rubellum var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the red sandwort, is a low-growing succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a desirable ornamental plant in arid landscapes.
