Hieracium Subspeciosum Subsp. Delasoiei

Hieracium subspeciosum subsp. delasoiei is a rare and lesser-known species of hawkweed found primarily in specific regions of Europe. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique floral structure and coloration, setting it apart from other members of the Hieracium genus. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable plant in its native range. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this species from threats such as habitat loss and climate change. Understanding and documenting its ecology is crucial for its long-term survival.
