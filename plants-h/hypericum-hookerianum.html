Hypericum hookerianum

Hypericum hookerianum, commonly known as the Hooker's St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with prominent orange stamens, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are oval and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hypericum hookerianum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
