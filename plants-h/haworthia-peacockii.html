Haworthia peacockii

Haworthia peacockii, commonly known as the peacock plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped foliage. The leaves are thick and fleshy, often featuring intricate patterns of white and green markings. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
