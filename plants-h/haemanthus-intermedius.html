Haemanthus intermedius

Haemanthus intermedius, commonly known as the Natal star, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking red and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has a rosette of strap-shaped leaves that are typically green with red veins. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a standout in botanical collections.
