Habenaria sobraliana

Habenaria sobraliana, commonly known as the Sobralian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with three petals and a distinctive lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
