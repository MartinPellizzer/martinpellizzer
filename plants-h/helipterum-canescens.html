Helipterum canescens

Helipterum canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy purple or pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its flowers are composite, consisting of both ray and disk florets, which attract various pollinators.

Helipterum canescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in garden settings.
