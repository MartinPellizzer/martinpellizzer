Helichrysum × Ambiguum

Helichrysum × ambiguum is a captivating plant known for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Native to South Africa, this species is a hybrid that showcases the beauty of natural plant evolution. Its feathery foliage and striking blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it relatively easy to care for. With its unique appearance and hardy nature, Helichrysum × ambiguum is a standout addition to any botanical collection.
