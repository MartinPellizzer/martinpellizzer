Hibiscus endlicheri

Hibiscus endlicheri, commonly known as the white hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy white petals and prominent yellow stamen, which give it a striking appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a glossy surface.

Hibiscus endlicheri thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
