Helichrysum adscendens

Helichrysum adscendens, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even when dried. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, with narrow, linear leaves and a branched structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Helichrysum adscendens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
