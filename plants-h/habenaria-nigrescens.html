Habenaria nigrescens

Habenaria nigrescens, commonly known as the blackening orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers that gradually fade to a lighter hue as they mature. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are often mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
