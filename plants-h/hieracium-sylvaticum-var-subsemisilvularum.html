Hieracium sylvaticum var. subsemisilvularum

Hieracium sylvaticum var. subsemisilvularum, commonly known as the woodland hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and along stream banks. It has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, clustered flowers in the summer.

This variety is often found in forested areas where it thrives in partial to full shade.
