Habenaria mandarinorum

Habenaria mandarinorum, commonly known as the Mandarin Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact rhizome with a single, upright stem that bears several oval, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often resembling a mandarin orange, hence its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The flowers are typically arranged in a spike and bloom during the summer months.
