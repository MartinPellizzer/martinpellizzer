Helichrysum graniticola

Helichrysum graniticola, commonly known as the granite helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils, often found in mountainous regions. Its drought tolerance makes it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
