Horsfieldia sucosa subsp. bifissa

Horsfieldia sucosa subsp.

bifissa, commonly known as the hairy horsfieldia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, hairy structures.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
