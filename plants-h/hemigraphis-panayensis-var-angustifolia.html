Hemigraphis panayensis var. angustifolia

Hemigraphis panayensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf iron plant, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with distinctive, velvety, dark green leaves that often have a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and terrariums.
