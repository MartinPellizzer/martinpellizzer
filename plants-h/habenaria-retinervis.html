Habenaria retinervis

Habenaria retinervis, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with intricate net-like venation on the petals. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
