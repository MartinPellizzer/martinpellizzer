Hippeastrum × splendens

Hippeastrum × splendens, commonly known as the red dragon or Amazon lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is a hybrid species within the Amaryllidaceae family, characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces bulbous rhizomes that store nutrients, allowing it to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that emerge from the base of the plant.

Hippeastrum × splendens is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is popular in gardens and as a potted plant.
