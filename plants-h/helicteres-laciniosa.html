Helicteres laciniosa

Helicteres laciniosa, commonly known as the silky hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

The silky hibiscus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
