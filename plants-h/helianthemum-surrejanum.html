Helianthemum surrejanum

Helianthemum surrejanum, commonly known as the Southern rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, fleshy leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in harsh environmental conditions.
