Hibiscus farragei

Hibiscus farragei, commonly known as the Cape hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Hibiscus farragei thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
