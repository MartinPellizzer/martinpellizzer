Hypodiscus argenteus

Hypodiscus argenteus, commonly known as the silverleaf hypodiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, silvery-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or shrubland habitats.

Hypodiscus argenteus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
