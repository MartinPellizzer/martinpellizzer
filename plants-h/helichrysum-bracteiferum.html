Helichrysum bracteiferum

Helichrysum bracteiferum, commonly known as the golden crown, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble a crown, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum bracteiferum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
