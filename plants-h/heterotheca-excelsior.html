Heterotheca excelsior

Heterotheca excelsior, commonly known as the tall ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and prairies. Its showy purple to pink flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Heterotheca excelsior is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
