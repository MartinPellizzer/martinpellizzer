Hedychium panzhuum

Hedychium panzhuum, commonly known as the Panzhuu ginger lily, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces fragrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by broad, green bracts. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Hedychium panzhuum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
