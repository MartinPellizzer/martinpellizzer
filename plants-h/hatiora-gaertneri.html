Hatiora gaertneri

Hatiora gaertneri, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its segmented, branching stems that resemble those of true cacti. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hatiora gaertneri is popular as a decorative plant due to its long blooming period and ease of care.
