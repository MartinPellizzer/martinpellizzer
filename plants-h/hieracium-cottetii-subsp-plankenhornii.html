Hieracium cottetii subsp. plankenhornii

Hieracium cottetii subsp.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are generally basal, with a rough, hairy texture, and its flowers are yellow, often appearing in clusters.
