Horsfieldia discolor

Horsfieldia discolor, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, often arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Horsfieldia discolor is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
