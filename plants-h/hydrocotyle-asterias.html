Hydrocotyle asterias

Hydrocotyle asterias, commonly known as the star-shaped water pennywort, is a perennial aquatic plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features floating leaves that are round and often have a star-like pattern of veins, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. Its small white flowers appear in clusters and bloom from late spring to early autumn.
