Heterothalamus tenellus var. robustior

Heterothalamus tenellus var. robustior, commonly known as the robust moss, is a variant of the heterothallic moss species.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, glossy foliage. The plant exhibits a distinct dimorphism in its sexual structures, with separate male and female gametophytes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found on soil or rock surfaces.

Its botanical significance lies in its role in ecological succession and as an indicator of environmental conditions.
