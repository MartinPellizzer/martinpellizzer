Hibbertia stricta var. calycina

Hibbertia stricta var. calycina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hibbertia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with red markings, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in native plant collections.
