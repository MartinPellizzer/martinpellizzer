Hanburia spectabilis

Hanburia spectabilis, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and a distinctive, fragrant scent that attracts pollinators. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, thriving on tree bark or rocky surfaces. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often displaying a unique combination of colors and patterns.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts worldwide.
