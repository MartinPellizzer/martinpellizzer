Habenaria dussii

Habenaria dussii, commonly known as the Dussi's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on the forest floor or in rocky crevices. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive violet or purple coloration. Its inflorescence is usually a raceme of elongated, spurred flowers.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
