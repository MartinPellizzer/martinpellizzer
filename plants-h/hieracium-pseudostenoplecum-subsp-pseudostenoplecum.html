Hieracium pseudostenoplecum subsp. pseudostenoplecum

Hieracium pseudostenoplecum subsp.

pseudostenoplecum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a single head. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate, often with a hairy surface, and it thrives in moist, calcareous soils.

It is often found in meadows, pastures, and along streambanks.
