Hymenoxys argentea var. ivesiana

Hymenoxys argentea var. ivesiana, commonly known as silverleaf hymenoxys, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence covering the leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with prominent white stamens, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
