Hybochilus huebneri

Hybochilus huebneri, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green petals that resemble a star. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers bloom during the warmer months, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
