Helichrysum margaritaceum

Helichrysum margaritaceum, commonly known as the Cape marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that are often used in dried floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance contribute to its widespread cultivation.
