Hibiscus hiernianus

Hibiscus hiernianus, commonly known as the Sierra Leone hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Hibiscus hiernianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
