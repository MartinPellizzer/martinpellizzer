Hieracium prenanthoides subsp. hoegeri

Hieracium prenanthoides subsp.

hoegeri, commonly known as the Hoeger's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and meadows. It has slender, upright stems with basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped.
