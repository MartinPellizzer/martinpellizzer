Heteropterys leschenaultiana f. ovata

Heteropterys leschenaultiana f. ovata, commonly known as the ovate-leaf heteropterys, is a compact shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features elliptical, ovate-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, often with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its foliage is evergreen, providing dense coverage and making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to well-drained soils in temperate climates.
