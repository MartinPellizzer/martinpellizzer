Heterochiton fontanesii subsp. almeriana

Heterochiton fontanesii subsp.

almeriana, commonly known as the Almerian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe, particularly Spain. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, often blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
