Hornera pruriens

Hornera pruriens, commonly known as the itching bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers and rough, often hairy foliage. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, with various parts used traditionally in herbal remedies.
