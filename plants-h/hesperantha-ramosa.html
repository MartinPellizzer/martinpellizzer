Hesperantha ramosa

Hesperantha ramosa, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Hesperantha ramosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
