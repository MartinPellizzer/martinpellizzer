Hieracium nigrescens subsp. curvatipes

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

curvatipes, commonly known as the bent-stem hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has serrated leaves and produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and has been introduced to other regions.
