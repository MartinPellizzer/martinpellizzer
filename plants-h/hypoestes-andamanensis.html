Hypoestes andamanensis

Hypoestes andamanensis, commonly known as the Andaman Islands hypostemon, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Hypoestes andamanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
