Hippocrepis comosa var. alpina

Hippocrepis comosa var. alpina, commonly known as the Alpine helmet orchid, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique helmet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pale pink or white with a distinctive hooded structure.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often found in mountainous habitats across the Alps.
