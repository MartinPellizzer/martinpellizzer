Hieracium juranum subsp. subepimedium

Hieracium juranum subsp.

subepimedium, commonly known as the Juranian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, reaching heights of up to 40 centimeters.

The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate with hairy surfaces and flowering stems that bear yellow composite flowers. Its flowers are composed of ray florets and disc florets, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
