Hieracium manubricatum

Hieracium manubricatum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually upright and branching.

Hieracium manubricatum is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragweed.
