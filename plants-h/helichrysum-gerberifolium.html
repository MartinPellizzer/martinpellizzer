Helichrysum gerberifolium

Helichrysum gerberifolium, commonly known as the golden everlasting or the Cape gold flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even when dried. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions. It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Helichrysum gerberifolium is well-suited for rock gardens and coastal areas due to its drought tolerance and hardy nature.
