Homalomena baangongensis

Homalomena baangongensis, commonly known as the Baangong arrowleaf.

It is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family, characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix and a spathe, typical of the arum family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
