Helleborus monanthos

Helleborus monanthos, commonly known as the naked-stem hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are deeply lobed and often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, nodding flowers that are typically purple or white, with a single bloom per stem. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Helleborus monanthos is valued for its ornamental appeal and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
