Helianthemum apenninum var. oblongifolium

Helianthemum apenninum var. oblongifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with evergreen foliage, featuring small, leathery leaves that are typically oblong in shape. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural use in arid and semi-arid climates.
