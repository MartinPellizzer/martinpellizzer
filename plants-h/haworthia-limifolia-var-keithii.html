Haworthia limifolia var. keithii

Haworthia limifolia var. keithii, commonly known as the Keith's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically a bluish-green color with white or pale markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, making it well-suited for arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its attractive appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
