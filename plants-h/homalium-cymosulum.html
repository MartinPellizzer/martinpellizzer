Homalium cymosulum

Homalium cymosulum, commonly known as the mountain huito, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by fleshy, orange-red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats and is often found in cloud forests.
