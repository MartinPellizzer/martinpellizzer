Habenaria longicornu

Habenaria longicornu, commonly known as the long-horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with elongated petals and a distinctive, club-shaped lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
