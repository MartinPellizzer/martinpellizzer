Homalomena tenuispadix

Homalomena tenuispadix, commonly known as the narrow-spadix arrowhead plant, is a tropical evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or green, followed by berries that turn red when mature.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
