Hypericum llanganaticum

Hypericum llanganaticum, commonly known as the Mexican St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum llanganaticum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
