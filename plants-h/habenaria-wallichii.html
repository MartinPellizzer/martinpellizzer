Habenaria wallichii

Habenaria wallichii, commonly known as the Wall's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Himalayas and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and habitat specificity, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
