Heliconia osaensis var. rubescens

Heliconia osaensis var. rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered heliconia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Heliconiaceae family and is characterized by its large, brightly colored inflorescences. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves that are often arranged in a fan shape. Its flowers are typically clustered in a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded environments.
