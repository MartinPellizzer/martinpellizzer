Hibiscus barbadensis var. klotzschianus

Hibiscus barbadensis var. klotzschianus, commonly known as the Klotzschianus hibiscus, is a variant of the common hibiscus native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a prominent central vein. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking floral display.
