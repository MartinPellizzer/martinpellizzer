Haworthia coarctata var. adelaidensis

Haworthia coarctata var. adelaidensis, commonly known as the Adelaidensis Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with white or pinkish markings. The leaves are often adorned with translucent windows, which help in photosynthesis and water conservation. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its drought tolerance and minimal care requirements.
