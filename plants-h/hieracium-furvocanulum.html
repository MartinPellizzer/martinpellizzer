Hieracium furvocanulum

Hieracium furvocanulum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has basal and alternate leaves that are typically hairy and lance-shaped. It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium furvocanulum is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
