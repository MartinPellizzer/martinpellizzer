Hieracium faucium

Hieracium faucium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Hieracium faucium is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
