Hoya heuschkeliana subsp. heuschkeliana

Hoya heuschkeliana subsp.

heuschkeliana, commonly known as the Heuschkeliana String of Hearts, is a tropical vine belonging to the Apocynaceae family. It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink.

The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is a popular houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.

Its unique appearance and air-purifying qualities make it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
