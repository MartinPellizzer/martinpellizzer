Hieracium euphyllotum

Hieracium euphyllotum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

The species is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate climates.
