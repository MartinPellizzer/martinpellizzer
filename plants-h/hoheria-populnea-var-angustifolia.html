Hoheria populnea var. angustifolia

Hoheria populnea var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flaxwood, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, bottle-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. These flowers are often followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.
