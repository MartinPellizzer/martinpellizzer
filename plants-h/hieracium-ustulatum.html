Hieracium ustulatum

Hieracium ustulatum, commonly known as the burnet hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems, which help it retain moisture in dry environments. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It has small, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium ustulatum is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
