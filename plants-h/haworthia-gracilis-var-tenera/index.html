Haworthia Gracilis Var. Tenera

Haworthia tenera, scientifically known as Haworthia gracilis var. tenera, is a compact, low-maintenance succulent native to South Africa. This plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or pink under direct sunlight. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. Despite its small size, Haworthia tenera adds a touch of elegance to any space.
