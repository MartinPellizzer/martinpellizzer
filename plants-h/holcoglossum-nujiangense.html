Holcoglossum nujiangense

Holcoglossum nujiangense, commonly known as the Nujiang orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors including white, pink, and purple.

Holcoglossum nujiangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
