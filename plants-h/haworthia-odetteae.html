Haworthia odetteae

Haworthia odetteae, commonly known as the Odette's Haworthia, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or yellow markings. The plant is known for its compact size and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water. It produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

Haworthia odetteae is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
