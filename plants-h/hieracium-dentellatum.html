Hieracium dentellatum

Hieracium dentellatum, commonly known as the toothed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges, and the flowers are yellow with ray florets.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
