Hibiscus negundo

Hibiscus negundo, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus or the yellow hibiscus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and can grow as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hibiscus negundo is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
