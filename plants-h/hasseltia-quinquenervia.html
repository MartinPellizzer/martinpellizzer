Hasseltia quinquenervia

Hasseltia quinquenervia, commonly known as the five-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in rocky or sandy soils in the fynbos biome.
