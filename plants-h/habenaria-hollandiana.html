Habenaria hollandiana

Habenaria hollandiana, commonly known as the Dutch sword orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulbous stem with oval, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
