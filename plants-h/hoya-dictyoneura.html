Hoya dictyoneura

Hoya dictyoneura, commonly known as the "Coral Hoya," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with subtle veins. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Hoya dictyoneura is a vine that thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

It is popular among houseplant enthusiasts for its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
