Heterotheca oregona var. scaberrima

Heterotheca oregona var. scaberrima, commonly known as the roughleaf goldenweed, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant features rough, hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its yellow flowers bloom in the summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
