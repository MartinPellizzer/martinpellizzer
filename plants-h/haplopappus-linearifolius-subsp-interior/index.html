Haplopappus Linearifolius Subsp. Interior

Haplopappus linearifolius subsp. interior is a rare and delicate plant native to the arid regions of western North America. It thrives in harsh desert environments where few other species can survive. This subspecies is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, pale flowers that bloom in the summer. Due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to habitat loss, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and fragile species.
