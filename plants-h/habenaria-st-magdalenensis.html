Habenaria st-magdalenensis

Habenaria st-magdalenensis, commonly known as the Saint Magdalene orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in rich, organic soils. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces erect, branching stems with lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white with yellow markings, and are arranged in a dense spike.

It is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific habitat conditions for successful growth.
