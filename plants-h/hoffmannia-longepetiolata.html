Hoffmannia longepetiolata

Hoffmannia longepetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled hoffmannia, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated petioles, which give it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments.

Its foliage is often lance-shaped with a smooth texture, making it a desirable ornamental plant.
