Habenaria ciliaris

Habenaria ciliaris, commonly known as the fringed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and often have a hairy texture, while its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral appearance.
