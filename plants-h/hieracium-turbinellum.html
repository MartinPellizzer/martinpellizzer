Hieracium turbinellum

Hieracium turbinellum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and sometimes reddish in color.

Hieracium turbinellum is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
