Habenaria bequaertii

Habenaria bequaertii, commonly known as the Bequaert's orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
