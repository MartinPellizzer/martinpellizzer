Hypericum aucheri f. minutum

Hypericum aucheri f. minutum, commonly known as the dwarf St. John's wort, is a compact, low-growing perennial shrub native to Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has dark green, narrow leaves that are densely packed along its stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
