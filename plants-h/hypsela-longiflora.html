Hypsela longiflora

Hypsela longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered hypsela, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

Hypsela longiflora is often found growing in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
