Hieracium sparsum subsp. maricae

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

maricae, commonly known as the Maricae hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, often in regions with a temperate climate. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
