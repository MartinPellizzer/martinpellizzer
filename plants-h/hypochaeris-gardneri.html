Hypochaeris gardneri

Hypochaeris gardneri, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and disturbed soils. It has alternate leaves that are often hairy and can vary in shape.

Hypochaeris gardneri is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
