Hieracium neomelanotrichum

Hieracium neomelanotrichum, commonly known as the black-hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, dark green leaves and yellowish-orange composite flowers. Native to Europe, it thrives in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, wind-dispersed seeds, aiding in its spread.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and weed management.
