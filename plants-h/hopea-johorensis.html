Hopea johorensis

Hopea johorensis, commonly known as the Johore hopea, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense canopy. The tree produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its bark is thick and fissured, and it is often found in lowland rainforests.

Hopea johorensis is valued for its hard, durable wood and plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitats.
