Hieracium subrectum

Hieracium subrectum, commonly known as the upright hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has serrated leaves and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates.

Hieracium subrectum is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
