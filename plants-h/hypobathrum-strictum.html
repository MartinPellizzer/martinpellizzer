Hypobathrum strictum

Hypobathrum strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lily, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

Hypobathrum strictum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
