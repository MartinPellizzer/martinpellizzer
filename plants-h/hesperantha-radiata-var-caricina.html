Hesperantha radiata var. caricina

Hesperantha radiata var. caricina, commonly known as the Cape violet, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its appeal in horticultural settings.
