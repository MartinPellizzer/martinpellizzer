Helichrysum errerae var. errerae

Helichrysum errerae var. errerae, commonly known as the golden marguerite or African marguerite, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features delicate, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, silvery-green leaves that are slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
