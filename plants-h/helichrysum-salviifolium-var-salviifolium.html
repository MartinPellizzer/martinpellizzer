Helichrysum salviifolium var. salviifolium

Helichrysum salviifolium var. salviifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
