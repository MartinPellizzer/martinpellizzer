Hieracium undulifolium

Hieracium undulifolium, commonly known as the wavy-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
