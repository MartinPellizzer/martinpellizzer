Helianthemum hyssopifolium var. cupreum

Helianthemum hyssopifolium var. cupreum, commonly known as the coppery rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub with grey-green, succulent leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for Mediterranean climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
