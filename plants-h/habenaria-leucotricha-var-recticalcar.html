Habenaria leucotricha var. recticalcar

Habenaria leucotricha var. recticalcar, commonly known as the white-haired orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
