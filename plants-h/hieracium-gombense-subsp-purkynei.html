Hieracium gombense subsp. purkynei

Hieracium gombense subsp.

purkynei, commonly known as the Gomboczky's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flower heads.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it produces small, hairy leaves. It has a compact growth habit with a rosette of basal leaves and flowering stems that bear composite flowers.
