Haworthia Flaccida

Haworthia flaccida is a unique succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its compact rosette shape and striking translucent leaves. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water. Its ability to store water makes it ideal for low-maintenance gardening. Haworthia flaccida is a popular choice for indoor and rock garden displays.
