Hohenbergia abbreviata

Hohenbergia abbreviata, commonly known as the compact hohenbergia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white woolly hairs. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright orange or red flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is a compact, low-maintenance plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Hohenbergia abbreviata is popular among succulent and bromeliad enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of care.
