Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. poeciloprasum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

poeciloprasum, commonly known as the purple meadowhawk, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features showy, daisy-like yellow flowers with purple centers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and dark green in color.

This subspecies is often found in temperate regions of Europe and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
