Holboellia marmorata

Holboellia marmorata, commonly known as the marble-stemmed orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, ornamental appearance. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Holboellia marmorata thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Its unique marbled stem pattern makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
