Heinsia zanzibarica

Heinsia zanzibarica, commonly known as Zanzibar heinsia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Zanzibar in Tanzania.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in dry, sunny environments. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Heinsia zanzibarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
