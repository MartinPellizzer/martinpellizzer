Habenaria flabelliformis

Habenaria flabelliformis, commonly known as the fan-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with long, narrow leaves that are often folded or rolled. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green or yellowish flowers with three petals and a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
