Heliotropium applanatum

Heliotropium applanatum, commonly known as the flatleaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
