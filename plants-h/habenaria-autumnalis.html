Habenaria autumnalis

Habenaria autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and along stream banks. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow with purple markings, and are arranged in a loose spike.

It blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding a subtle ornamental value to its natural environment.
