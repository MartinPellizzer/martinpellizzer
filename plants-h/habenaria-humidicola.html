Habenaria humidicola

Habenaria humidicola, commonly known as the swamp orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to wetland and swampy habitats in Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments with rich, organic soil. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding wetland environment.

Habenaria humidicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in maintaining wetland biodiversity.
