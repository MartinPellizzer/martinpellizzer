Hymenocallis Americana F. Disticha

Hymenocallis americana f. disticha is a striking plant known for its elegant, double-flowered blooms. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to the southern United States. This cultivar features long, narrow leaves and clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
