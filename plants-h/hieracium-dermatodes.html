Hieracium dermatodes

Hieracium dermatodes, commonly known as the scaly hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium dermatodes is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and can become invasive in certain regions.
