Helianthemum roseum

Helianthemum roseum, commonly known as the pink rockrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub with woody stems and small, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Helianthemum roseum is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in Mediterranean climates.
