Helianthemum canum var. origanifolium

Helianthemum canum var. origanifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandthorn, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has aromatic, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional herbal remedies. Its succulent stems allow it to thrive in arid and rocky environments.

It is well adapted to full sun and requires minimal water once established.
