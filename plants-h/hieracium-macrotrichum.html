Hieracium macrotrichum

Hieracium macrotrichum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium macrotrichum is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
