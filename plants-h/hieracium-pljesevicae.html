Hieracium pljesevicae

Hieracium pljesevicae, commonly known as the Pleszewicz's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellowish to orange flowers in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It is native to certain regions of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in some areas.
