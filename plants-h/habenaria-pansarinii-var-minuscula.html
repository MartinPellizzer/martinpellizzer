Habenaria pansarinii var. minuscula

Habenaria pansarinii var. minuscula, commonly known as the small-flowered marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often in shaded areas with rich, moist soil. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it is known for its delicate appearance.

This variety is relatively rare and is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and habitat preferences.
