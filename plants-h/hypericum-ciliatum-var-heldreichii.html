Hypericum ciliatum var. heldreichii

Hypericum ciliatum var. heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features small, yellow flowers with prominent yellow stamens, typical of the Hypericaceae family. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or woodland habitats.

Its medicinal properties have been historically valued, though it is less commonly cultivated than other Hypericum species.
