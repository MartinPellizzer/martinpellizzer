Habenaria tenuis

Habenaria tenuis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are usually arranged in a loose raceme and are pollinated by insects.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
