Hicorius ovata var. pubescens

Hicorius ovata var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy oval hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety, pubescent texture on the underside. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
