Hydrocotyle spananthe

Hydrocotyle spananthe, commonly known as the white water shield, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its spreading growth habit and distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its small white flowers are borne in clusters and bloom during the summer months.
