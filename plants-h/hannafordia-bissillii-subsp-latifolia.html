Hannafordia bissillii subsp. latifolia

Hannafordia bissillii subsp.

latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf Hannafordia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
