Hicorius × ludoviciana

Hicorius × ludoviciana, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in late summer to early fall. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Hicorius × ludoviciana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
