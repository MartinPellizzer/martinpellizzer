Hieracium synozum

Hieracium synozum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Hieracium synozum is often found in Europe and parts of Asia, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
