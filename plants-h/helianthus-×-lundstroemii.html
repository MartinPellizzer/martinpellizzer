Helianthus × lundstroemii

Helianthus × lundstroemii, commonly known as the Lundsberg sunflower, is a hybrid species within the sunflower genus.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow to orange flower heads and robust, upright growth habit. Native to the southwestern United States, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces both ray and disk flowers, typical of the genus Helianthus.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
