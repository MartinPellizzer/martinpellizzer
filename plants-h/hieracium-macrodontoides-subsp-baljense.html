Hieracium macrodontoides subsp. baljense

Hieracium macrodontoides subsp.

baljense, commonly known as the Baljens' hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has small, hairy leaves and produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

This subspecies is distinguished by its specific morphology and geographic distribution within the Hieracium genus.
