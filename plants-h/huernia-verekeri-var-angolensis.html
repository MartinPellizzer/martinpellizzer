Huernia verekeri var. angolensis

Huernia verekeri var. angolensis, commonly known as the Angolan horned cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, branching stem covered in small, rounded tubercles that give it a distinctive, spiky appearance. The plant produces pale yellow to orange flowers during the warmer months, typically in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
