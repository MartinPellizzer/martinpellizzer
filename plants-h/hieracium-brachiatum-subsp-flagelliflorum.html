Hieracium brachiatum subsp. flagelliflorum

Hieracium brachiatum subsp.

flagelliflorum, commonly known as the flagelliflorous hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
