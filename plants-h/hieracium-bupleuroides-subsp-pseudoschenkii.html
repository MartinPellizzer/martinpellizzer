Hieracium bupleuroides subsp. pseudoschenkii

Hieracium bupleuroides subsp.

pseudoschenkii, commonly known as the false bupleurum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It typically grows in open grasslands and meadows, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and is characterized by its distinctive inflorescence structure.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
