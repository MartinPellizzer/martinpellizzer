Hieracium kintrawense

Hieracium kintrawense, commonly known as the Kintraw hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Scottish Highlands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats. Its flowers are yellow and bloom from June to August, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
