Hostia foetida

Hostia foetida, commonly known as the stinking sprout, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has a distinctive, often unpleasant odor, which is most noticeable when the flowers are in bloom. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understories.

Due to its unique fragrance and appearance, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
