Hesperis fenestralis

Hesperis fenestralis, commonly known as the fenestrate hesperis, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hesperis fenestralis is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in regions with temperate climates.
