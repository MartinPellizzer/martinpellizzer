Hibiscus upingtoniae

Hibiscus upingtoniae, commonly known as the Upington's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils. It has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Hibiscus upingtoniae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
