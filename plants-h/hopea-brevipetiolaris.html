Hopea brevipetiolaris

Hopea brevipetiolaris, commonly known as the short-peduncle hopea, is a tropical tree species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, small, and clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests and is known for its hard, durable wood. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth surface.

Hopea brevipetiolaris plays a significant role in the ecological systems of its native habitats.
