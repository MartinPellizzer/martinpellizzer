Helianthemum origanifolium var. serrae

Helianthemum origanifolium var. serrae, commonly known as the serrated rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, fleshy leaves that are serrated along the edges. The plant produces yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardening.
