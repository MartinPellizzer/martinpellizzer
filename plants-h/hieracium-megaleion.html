Hieracium megaleion

Hieracium megaleion, commonly known as the large-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features broad, serrated leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered a weed in some regions.
