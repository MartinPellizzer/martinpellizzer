Hydrangea tiliifolia

Hydrangea tiliifolia, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Hydrangea tiliifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
