Homalomena plicata

Homalomena plicata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are typically a deep green with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in an inflorescence, though it is more prized for its ornamental foliage than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Homalomena plicata is a popular houseplant due to its adaptability and ability to purify indoor air.
