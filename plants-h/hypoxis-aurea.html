Hypoxis aurea

Hypoxis aurea, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, tuberous rhizome and produces bright yellow flowers with a star-like shape. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoxis aurea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
