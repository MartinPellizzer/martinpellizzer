Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. pseudostorliense

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

pseudostorliense, commonly known as the transparent hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers and hairy, often lobed leaves.

This subspecies is native to Central and Eastern Europe and thrives in moist, calcareous soils. It typically grows in meadows, pastures, and along riverbanks.

The plant is noted for its distinctive, translucent appearance, which gives it its common name.
