Habenaria unalascensis var. maritima

Habenaria unalascensis var. maritima, commonly known as the coastal marsh orchid, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Alaska.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in wet, marshy habitats along the shores. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

It plays an important role in the local ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators and stabilizing coastal soils.
