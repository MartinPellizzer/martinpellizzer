Hieracium aurantiacum subsp. subkajanense

Hieracium aurantiacum subsp.

subkajanense, commonly known as the Kajan yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and is native to Central Asia.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture, and its flowers bloom in late summer.

This subspecies is often found in regions with temperate climates and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
