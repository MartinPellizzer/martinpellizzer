Haemanthus orchidifolius

Haemanthus orchidifolius, commonly known as the red-stemmed haemanthus, is a striking evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features strap-like, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish tinge along the midrib. The plant produces dense clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable ornamental plant in horticulture.
