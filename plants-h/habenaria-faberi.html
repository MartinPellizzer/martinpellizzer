Habenaria faberi

Habenaria faberi, commonly known as the Chinese spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals and a distinctive, narrow spur. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a subtle fragrance.

Habenaria faberi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
