Hieracium ramosum subsp. leiocranum

Hieracium ramosum subsp.

leiocranum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the flowers have five petals and numerous stamens.

This subspecies is characterized by its slender stems and is often found in temperate climates.
