Habenaria spiralis

Habenaria spiralis, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its roots are fibrous, and it often forms dense colonies in its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
