Hippochaete laevigata var. eatonii

Hippochaete laevigata var. eatonii, commonly known as the Eaton's bushveld daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in a circular pattern, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
