Hypericum bolivianum

Hypericum bolivianum, commonly known as the Bolivian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, often found in mountainous regions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate to subtropical climates.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
