Hypoestes longifolia

Hypoestes longifolia, commonly known as the "Snowflake plant," is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble snowflakes. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It produces clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hypoestes longifolia is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
