Hieracium konshakovskianum

Hieracium konshakovskianum, commonly known as the Konshakovskian hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to harsh alpine conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
