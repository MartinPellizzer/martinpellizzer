Habenaria × wintonii

Habenaria × wintonii, commonly known as the Winton's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland or swampy habitats, thriving in moist, acidic soils. Its inflorescence consists of multiple showy, spurred flowers that vary in color from white to pink.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts, though it remains relatively rare in the wild.
