Hieracium pulchrum

Hieracium pulchrum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it produces achenes as its fruit.

Hieracium pulchrum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
