Hieracium sagittatum subsp. expallidiceps

Hieracium sagittatum subsp.

expallidiceps, commonly known as the slender spear-thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are arranged in a composite head, with both ray and disk florets, and it produces achenes as its seeds.

The subspecies is distinguished by its pale yellow flowers and slender stem compared to other Hieracium species.
