Helianthemum rubellum var. giganteum

Helianthemum rubellum var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
