Hydrangea Serrata F. Cuspidata

Hydrangea serrata f. cuspidata is a striking variation of the hydrangea known for its vibrant flower clusters and unique foliage. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value. Its serrated leaves and cone-shaped flowers add a distinctive touch to any garden setting. The plant's ability to change flower color based on soil pH makes it a fascinating subject for both horticulture and botany. With proper care, it can bloom beautifully throughout the growing season.
