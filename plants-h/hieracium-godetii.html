Hieracium godetii

Hieracium godetii, commonly known as the Godet's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers in clusters. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and leaves, which help in reducing water loss.

Hieracium godetii is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates.
