Hypericum suzukianum

Hypericum suzukianum, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments. It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is known for its medicinal properties.

Its small, clustered flowers bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
