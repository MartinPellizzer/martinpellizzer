Hypericum strictum var. reductum

Hypericum strictum var. reductum, commonly known as the narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its small, yellow flowers bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
