Helogyne apaloidea

Helogyne apaloidea, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. Helogyne apaloidea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with partial shade.
