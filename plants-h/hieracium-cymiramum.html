Hieracium cymiramum

Hieracium cymiramum, commonly known as the Cymiramum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium cymiramum is often found in disturbed habitats and can sometimes be invasive in certain regions.
