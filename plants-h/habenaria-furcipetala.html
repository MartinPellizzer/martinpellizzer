Habenaria furcipetala

Habenaria furcipetala, commonly known as the toothed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a distinctive tooth-like structure at the base of the petals.

It is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
