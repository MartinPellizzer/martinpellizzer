Hieracium arvicola subsp. javorinense

Hieracium arvicola subsp.

javorinense, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has small, linear leaves and produces numerous small florets arranged in a composite head.

This subspecies is adapted to cold climates and is often found in mountainous areas across the Alps and Carpathians.
