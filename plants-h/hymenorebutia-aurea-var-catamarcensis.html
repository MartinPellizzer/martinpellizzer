Hymenorebutia aurea var. catamarcensis

Hymenorebutia aurea var. catamarcensis, commonly known as the Catamarcensis Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Argentina.

It features a dense, spiny exterior with short, cylindrical spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are clustered at the top of the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
