Houlletia stapeliiflora

Houlletia stapeliiflora, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, enabling it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically spine-tipped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Houlletia stapeliiflora is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
