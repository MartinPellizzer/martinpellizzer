Haworthia coarctata var. tenuis

Haworthia coarctata var. tenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically a pale green to bluish-gray, with prominent white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
