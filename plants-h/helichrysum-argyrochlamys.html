Helichrysum argyrochlamys

Helichrysum argyrochlamys, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces papery, dry flower heads that persist through winter, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice in horticulture.
