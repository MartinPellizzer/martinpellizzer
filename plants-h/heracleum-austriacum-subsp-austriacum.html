Heracleum austriacum subsp. austriacum

Heracleum austriacum subsp.

austriacum, commonly known as the Austrian cow parsley, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants with characteristic umbel-shaped flowers.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
