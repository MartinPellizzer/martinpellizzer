Hieracium morisianum subsp. melanostylum

Hieracium morisianum subsp.

melanostylum, commonly known as the black-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and yellow flower heads that consist of numerous small florets.

The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It produces a basal rosette of leaves with flowering stems that rise above them.

The subspecies is distinguished by its darker stem coloration compared to other members of the species.
