Hieracium flagellare subsp. besnikense

Hieracium flagellare subsp.

besnikense, commonly known as the Besnik's hairy hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of the Balkans. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is a relatively rare subspecies, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitats.
