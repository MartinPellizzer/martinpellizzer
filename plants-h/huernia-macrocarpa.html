Huernia macrocarpa

Huernia macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant horned lithops, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
