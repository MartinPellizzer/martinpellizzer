Helianthemum crassifolium subsp. djeniense

Helianthemum crassifolium subsp.

djeniense, commonly known as the Djeniense rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Africa. It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are adapted to arid conditions, allowing it to store water efficiently. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
