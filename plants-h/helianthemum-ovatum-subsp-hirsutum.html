Helianthemum ovatum subsp. hirsutum

Helianthemum ovatum subsp.

hirsutum, commonly known as the hairy rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It features small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to June.

The plant has dense, hairy foliage that helps it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
