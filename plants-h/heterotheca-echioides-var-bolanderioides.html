Heterotheca echioides var. bolanderioides

Heterotheca echioides var. bolanderioides, commonly known as Bolander's horseweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically herbaceous growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow ray flowers surrounded by tubular florets. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
