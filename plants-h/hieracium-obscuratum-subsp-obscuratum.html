Hieracium obscuratum subsp. obscuratum

Hieracium obscuratum subsp.

obscuratum, commonly known as the dark hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellowish to reddish flowers in clusters. It is characterized by its hairy stems and is part of the Asteraceae family.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species in certain regions.
