Hoffmannia rexmontis

Hoffmannia rexmontis, commonly known as the mountain king's hoffmannia, is a striking perennial plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from deep red to purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery coating, which helps reflect excess sunlight.

Hoffmannia rexmontis is valued in botanical gardens for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native ecosystems.
