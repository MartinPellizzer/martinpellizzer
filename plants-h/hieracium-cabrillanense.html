Hieracium cabrillanense

Hieracium cabrillanense, commonly known as the Cabrillanense hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers and hairy, often reddish stems. Native to the Iberian Peninsula, it typically grows in rocky or open habitats. The plant has small, alternate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
