Helenium californicum

Helenium californicum, commonly known as the California sneezeweed, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant orange to yellow ray flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.

Helenium californicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
