Habenaria tentaculata

Habenaria tentaculata, commonly known as the tentacle orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swamps. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The orchid is known for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
