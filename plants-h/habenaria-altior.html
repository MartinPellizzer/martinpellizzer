Habenaria altior

Habenaria altior, commonly known as the tall marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that is often spotted or streaked.

Habenaria altior produces small, elongated capsules as its fruit, which contain the seeds typical of the orchid family.
