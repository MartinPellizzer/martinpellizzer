Hunteria Zeylanica

Hunteria zeylanica is a rare and striking plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is known for its large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. This species thrives in tropical climates and requires specific environmental conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered in the wild. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after plant among horticulturists and botanists alike.
