Haworthia mucronata var. morrisiae

Haworthia mucronata var. morrisiae, commonly known as the Morris's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The leaves are often adorned with small, pointed teeth along the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
