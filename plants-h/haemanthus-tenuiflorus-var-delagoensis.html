Haemanthus tenuiflorus var. delagoensis

Haemanthus tenuiflorus var. delagoensis, commonly known as the Delagoa star lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a cluster at the top of its narrow, grass-like foliage. The plant produces underground bulbs that store nutrients, allowing it to survive dry periods. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
