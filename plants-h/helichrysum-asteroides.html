Helichrysum asteroides

Helichrysum asteroides, commonly known as the everlasting daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, fluffy yellow or orange flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically surrounded by a ring of narrow, linear leaves. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and vibrant color.
