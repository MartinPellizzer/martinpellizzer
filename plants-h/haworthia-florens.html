Haworthia florens

Haworthia florens, commonly known as the Zulu bulb or the Zulu cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with white markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves.

Haworthia florens is prized for its low maintenance and unique appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
