Hieracium spectandrum

Hieracium spectandrum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, and it has a robust root system.

Hieracium spectandrum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to spread rapidly.
