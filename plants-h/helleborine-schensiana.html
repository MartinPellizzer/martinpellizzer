Helleborine schensiana

Helleborine schensiana, commonly known as the Schensian helleborine, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant is a terrestrial orchid, typically growing in shaded forest environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, nutrient-rich soils.

The flowers are often pollinated by flies and beetles, which are attracted to their scent and color.
