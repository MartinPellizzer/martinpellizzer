Hieracium ramosum subsp. sendtneri

Hieracium ramosum subsp.

sendtneri, commonly known as the Sendtner's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, typical of the genus Hieracium. It is native to Central and Eastern Europe and thrives in meadows and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it distinguishable from other hawkweed species, particularly in its leaf structure and flower morphology.
