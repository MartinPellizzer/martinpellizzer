Hieracium pallidum subsp. rupicola

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

rupicola, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to rocky and alpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as meadows and slopes.

The plant features basal rosettes of gray-green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Its inflorescence consists of several disc flowers surrounded by ray florets.

It is adapted to harsh environments through its compact growth form and hairy foliage.
