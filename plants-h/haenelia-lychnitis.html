Haenelia lychnitis

Haenelia lychnitis, commonly known as the nightshade or lantern flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

Haenelia lychnitis is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
