Hypoxis sericea

Hypoxis sericea, commonly known as silver star, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem with a rosette of narrow, silvery-haired leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are clustered in a star-like arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens across the United States.
