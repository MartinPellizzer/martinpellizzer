Habenaria hermannjosef-rothii

Habenaria hermannjosef-rothii, commonly known as the Hermannjosef Roth's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are arranged in a raceme, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is relatively rare and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
