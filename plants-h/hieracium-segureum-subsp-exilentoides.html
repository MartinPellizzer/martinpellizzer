Hieracium segureum subsp. exilentoides

Hieracium segureum subsp.

exilentoides, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowering period occurs in the summer months.
