Hibiscus cupreus

Hibiscus cupreus, commonly known as the copperleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking copper-colored leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Hibiscus cupreus thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
