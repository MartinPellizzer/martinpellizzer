Hieracium subfusciforme

Hieracium subfusciforme, commonly known as the dark-leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and dark green in color. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the genus Hieracium.

It is a perennial plant that produces seeds through achenes, which are dispersed by wind.
