Hieracium anthracinum

Hieracium anthracinum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows and disturbed soils.

Hieracium anthracinum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
