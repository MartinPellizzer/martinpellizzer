Hypericum olympicum f. macrocalyx

Hypericum olympicum f. macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered St. John's wort, is a botanical variant of the St. John's wort species.

It is characterized by its notably larger, showy yellow flowers compared to the typical form. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Europe, particularly the Alps. It thrives in well-drained, sunny soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Its botanical features make it a desirable ornamental plant in gardens and botanical collections.
