Haworthia retusa var. acuminata

Haworthia retusa var. acuminata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are often marked with white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments. It prefers bright, indirect sunlight and can tolerate low light conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
