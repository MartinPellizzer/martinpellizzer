Hatiora salicornoides f. bambusoides

Hatiora salicornoides f. bambusoides, commonly known as the bamboo-like zebrina, is a unique hybrid plant that exhibits characteristics of both the parent species and its bamboo-like form.

The plant features elongated, grass-like leaves that give it a distinctive bamboo appearance, while its stems are often woody and branching. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
