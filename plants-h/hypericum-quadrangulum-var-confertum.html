Hypericum quadrangulum var. confertum

Hypericum quadrangulum var. confertum, commonly known as the dense-stamened St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has four-sided, woody stems and alternate, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in dry, rocky soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ecologically valuable species in its native habitat.
