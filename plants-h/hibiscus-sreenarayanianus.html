Hibiscus sreenarayanianus

Hibiscus sreenarayanianus, commonly known as the Sreenarayan Hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
