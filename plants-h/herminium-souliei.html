Herminium souliei

Herminium souliei, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species native to China and belongs to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a perennial herb with slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, orchid-like flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are often clustered in racemes and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Herminium souliei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
