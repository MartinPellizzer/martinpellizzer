Hisingera grandifolia

Hisingera grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaved hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the year. Hisingera grandifolia thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
