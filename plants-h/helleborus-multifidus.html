Helleborus multifidus

Helleborus multifidus, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Southern Europe.

It features dark green, velvety leaves that are deeply lobed and often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, bowl-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Helleborus multifidus is known for its hardy nature and ability to bloom even in cold conditions, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
