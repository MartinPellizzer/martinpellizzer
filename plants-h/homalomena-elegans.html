Homalomena elegans

Homalomena elegans, commonly known as the elegant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its foliage is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
