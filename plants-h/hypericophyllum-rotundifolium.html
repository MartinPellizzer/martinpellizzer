Hypericophyllum rotundifolium

Hypericophyllum rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leafed St. John's wort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Hypericaceae.

It is characterized by its rounded, fleshy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to certain regions of North America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.
