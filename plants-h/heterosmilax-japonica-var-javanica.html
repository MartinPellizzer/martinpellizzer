Heterosmilax japonica var. javanica

Heterosmilax japonica var. javanica, commonly known as the Java arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often glossy and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
