Habenaria calva

Habenaria calva, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white to pale pink, and arranged in a spike, often with a distinct lip.

Habenaria calva is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
