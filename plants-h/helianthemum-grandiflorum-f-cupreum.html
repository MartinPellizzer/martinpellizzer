Helianthemum grandiflorum f. cupreum

Helianthemum grandiflorum f. cupreum, commonly known as the coppery rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and gray-green foliage. The plant produces showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its compact size and drought tolerance make it well-suited for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The varietal form 'cupreum' is distinguished by its coppery tones in the leaves and flowers, adding a unique visual appeal.
