Hoya papaschonii

Hoya papaschonii, commonly known as the Papaschonii Wreath, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped, with a deep green color and a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in late spring or early summer. Hoya papaschonii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
