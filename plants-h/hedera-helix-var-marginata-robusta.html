Hedera helix var. marginata-robusta

Hedera helix var. marginata-robusta, commonly known as the robust marginated ivy, is a variant of the common ivy characterized by its distinctive variegated foliage.

The leaves are typically broad and have a serrated margin, with a striking combination of green, white, and sometimes yellow or cream-colored patterns. This plant is a vigorous climber, often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to cover walls or trellises. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-draining, fertile soil.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor landscaping.
