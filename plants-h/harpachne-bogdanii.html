Harpachne bogdanii

Harpachne bogdanii, commonly known as the Bogdan's harpachne, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions, making it a hardy species.

Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
