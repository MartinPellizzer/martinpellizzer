Hieracium kerneri

Hieracium kerneri, commonly known as the Kern's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. Hieracium kerneri is often found in open meadows and slopes, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
