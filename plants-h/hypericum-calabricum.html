Hypericum calabricum

Hypericum calabricum, commonly known as the Sicilian St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional remedies.
