Haworthia limifolia var. striata

Haworthia limifolia var. striata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, gray-green leaves with distinctive white or pale stripes, giving it its characteristic zebra-like appearance. The plant grows in a rosette form, with leaves that are thick and somewhat triangular in cross-section. It produces small, white flowers on short, erect stems during the summer months.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
