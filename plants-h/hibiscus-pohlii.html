Hibiscus pohlii

Hibiscus pohlii, commonly known as the Pohli's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Hibiscus pohlii is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
