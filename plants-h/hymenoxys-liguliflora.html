Hymenoxys liguliflora

Hymenoxys liguliflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hymenoxys, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow disk flowers surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Hymenoxys liguliflora is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
