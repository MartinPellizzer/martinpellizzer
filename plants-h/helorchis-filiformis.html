Helorchis filiformis

Helorchis filiformis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with few leaves and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. Its roots are often clustered and adapted for absorbing moisture from the soil.

Helorchis filiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
