Heimerlia brunoniana

Heimerlia brunoniana, commonly known as the Brunoniana heather, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

Heimerlia brunoniana thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or forested habitats.
