Hibiscus donii

Hibiscus donii, commonly known as the Philippine hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found growing in moist, shaded environments.
