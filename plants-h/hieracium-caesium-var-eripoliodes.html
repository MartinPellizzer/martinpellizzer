Hieracium caesium var. eripoliodes

Hieracium caesium var. eripoliodes, commonly known as the ericoid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and typical sunflower-like appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed sites. It is noted for its ornamental value and has been introduced to other regions as a garden plant.
