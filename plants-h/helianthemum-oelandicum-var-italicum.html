Helianthemum oelandicum var. italicum

Helianthemum oelandicum var. italicum, commonly known as the Italian rock rose, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has succulent, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for Mediterranean-style landscaping.
