Habenaria foliosissima

Habenaria foliosissima, commonly known as the lace orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like petals and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often near streams or in forest undergrowth. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a spike.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and unique foliage structure.
