Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. pelidnopolium

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

pelidnopolium, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small yellow flowers arranged in composite inflorescences.

This subspecies is native to southern Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has a rosette growth habit and is known for its delicate, translucent appearance.

The plant is adapted to temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soils.
