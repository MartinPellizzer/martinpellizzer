Hieracium murorum subsp. leptotes

Hieracium murorum subsp.

leptotes, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats such as roadsides and meadows.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and botanical gardens.
