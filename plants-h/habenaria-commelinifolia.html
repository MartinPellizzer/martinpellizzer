Habenaria commelinifolia

Habenaria commelinifolia, commonly known as the Commelinifolia orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. It has linear to lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant blooms during the rainy season.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to its humid, tropical habitat.
