Heteromorpha arborescens var. arborescens

Heteromorpha arborescens var. arborescens, commonly known as the tree hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a woody shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, featuring broad, palmate leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the warm season. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, aiding in its propagation.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to thrive in humid, sunny environments.
