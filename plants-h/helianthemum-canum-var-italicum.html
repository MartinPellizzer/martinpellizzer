Helianthemum canum var. italicum

Helianthemum canum var. italicum, commonly known as the Italian rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has aromatic, gray-green foliage that is often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners in mild climates.
