Helenium polyphyllum

Helenium polyphyllum, commonly known as the New Zealand fireweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand.

It features showy, orange-red ray florets surrounded by yellow disk florets, creating a striking floral display. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Helenium polyphyllum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
