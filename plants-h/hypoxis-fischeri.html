Hypoxis fischeri

Hypoxis fischeri, commonly known as Fischer's starry grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Hypoxis, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and has a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a cluster of bright yellow flowers arranged in a star pattern.

Hypoxis fischeri is noted for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
