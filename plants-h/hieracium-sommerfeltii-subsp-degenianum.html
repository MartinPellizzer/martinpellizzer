Hieracium sommerfeltii subsp. degenianum

Hieracium sommerfeltii subsp.

degenianum, commonly known as the Degen's Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers and basal rosettes of leaves.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands. It has hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
