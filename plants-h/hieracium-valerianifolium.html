Hieracium valerianifolium

Hieracium valerianifolium, commonly known as the Valerian-leaved Hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy appearance.

Hieracium valerianifolium is known for its ability to spread rapidly, sometimes becoming invasive in certain regions.
