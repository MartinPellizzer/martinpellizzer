Hymenophyllum siliquosum

Hymenophyllum siliquosum, commonly known as the silquose filmy fern, is a delicate, epiphytic fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds with a translucent, almost paper-like appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found on tree trunks or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

The plant's unique morphology and habitat requirements make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
