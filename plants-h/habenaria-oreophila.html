Habenaria oreophila

Habenaria oreophila, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Habenaria oreophila is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
