Haworthia batesiana

Haworthia batesiana, commonly known as the Zulu Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with white or light-colored markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xerophytic gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor succulent collections.
