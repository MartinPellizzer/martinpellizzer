Habenaria fimbriata f. mentotonsa

Habenaria fimbriata f. mentotonsa, commonly known as the Mentotonsa Sword Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it can receive indirect sunlight. Its flowers, which bloom in spring, are usually pale green to white with intricate patterns, making it a visually distinctive species.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered orchid in its native range.
