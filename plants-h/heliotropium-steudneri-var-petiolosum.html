Heliotropium steudneri var. petiolosum

Heliotropium steudneri var. petiolosum, commonly known as the petioled heliotropium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with prominent petioles, and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in terminal spikes. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
