Habenaria graminea

Habenaria graminea, commonly known as the grass orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as rainforests and marshy areas. It has a rhizomatous root system that helps it thrive in moist soils.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is often found in protected or conservation areas.
