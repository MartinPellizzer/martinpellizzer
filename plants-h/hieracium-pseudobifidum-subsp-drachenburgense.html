Hieracium pseudobifidum subsp. drachenburgense

Hieracium pseudobifidum subsp.

drachenburgense, commonly known as the Dragon's Burg Daisy, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow composite flowers with ray florets. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in calcareous soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
