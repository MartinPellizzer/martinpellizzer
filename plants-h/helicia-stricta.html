Helicia stricta

Helicia stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf helenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Theaceae, which includes plants such as camellias and tea. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters, with slender, upright stems and small, leathery leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters and blooming in spring.
