Heteropterys nervosa var. nervosa

Heteropterys nervosa var. nervosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and a velvety texture on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in terminal panicles. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along streambanks.
