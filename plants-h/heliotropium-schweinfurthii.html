Heliotropium schweinfurthii

Heliotropium schweinfurthii, commonly known as the desert heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or subshrub with small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Heliotropium schweinfurthii is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.
