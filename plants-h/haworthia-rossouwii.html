Haworthia rossouwii

Haworthia rossouwii, commonly known as the Rossouw's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth pattern. The plant features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. It produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the summer months.

Haworthia rossouwii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
