Hubertia alleizettei var. taxifolia

Hubertia alleizettei var. taxifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hubertia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a decorative succulent.
