Haworthia pringlei

Haworthia pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
