Hemionitis albomarginata

Hemionitis albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin fern, is a rare and delicate plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

The plant belongs to the family Hemionitidaceae and is known for its unique morphology and limited distribution. It thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in shaded, rocky habitats.
