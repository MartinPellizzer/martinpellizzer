Hieracium Pikujense

Hieracium pikujense is a rare and lesser-known species of hawkweed found in specific alpine regions. This plant is notable for its delicate yellow flowers and compact growth habit. It thrives in high-altitude environments where few other plants can survive. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Studying Hieracium pikujense offers insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in extreme habitats.
