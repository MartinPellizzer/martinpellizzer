Hieracium leptophyton subsp. discolor

Hieracium leptophyton subsp.

discolor, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are typically basal, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

This subspecies is distinguished by its pale coloration compared to other members of the Hieracium genus.
