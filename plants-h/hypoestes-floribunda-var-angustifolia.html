Hypoestes floribunda var. angustifolia

Hypoestes floribunda var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Hypoestes, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, bi-color leaves that often display striking red and green patterns. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage, which remains attractive throughout the growing season.
