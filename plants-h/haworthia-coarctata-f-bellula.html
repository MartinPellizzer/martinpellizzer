Haworthia coarctata f. bellula

Haworthia coarctata f. bellula, commonly known as the Bell's Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically tinged with red or pink at the tips, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped white or pale green flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
