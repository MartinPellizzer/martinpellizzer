Hydnocarpus clemensiorum

Hydnocarpus clemensiorum, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and resistance to decay.
