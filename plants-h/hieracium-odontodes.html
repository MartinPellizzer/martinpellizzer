Hieracium odontodes

Hieracium odontodes, commonly known as the toothed hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stem is usually unbranched and somewhat hairy. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early summer.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
