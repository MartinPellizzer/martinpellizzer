Helenium tubuliflorum

Helenium tubuliflorum, commonly known as the orange hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant features bright orange to yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Helenium tubuliflorum is typically found in dry, open habitats like meadows and grasslands.
