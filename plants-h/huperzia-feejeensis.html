Huperzia feejeensis

Huperzia feejeensis, commonly known as the Feejeen's spikemoss, is a tropical fern ally native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Huperziaceae and is characterized by its upright, evergreen stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its sporophytes produce spores on the underside of its leaves, which are dispersed by wind.

Huperzia feejeensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
