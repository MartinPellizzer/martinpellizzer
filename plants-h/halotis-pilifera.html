Halotis pilifera

Halotis pilifera, commonly known as the silky sea hare, is a species of sea slug found in tropical and subtropical marine environments.

The plant-like appearance of Halotis pilifera is due to its ability to absorb nutrients through its skin, a feature common among many opisthobranch mollusks. It thrives in shallow coastal waters and is often found in areas with abundant algae.
