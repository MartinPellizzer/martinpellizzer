Hieracium murorramondii

Hieracium murorramondii, commonly known as the Murorramondi hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Europe.

Hieracium murorramondii is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
