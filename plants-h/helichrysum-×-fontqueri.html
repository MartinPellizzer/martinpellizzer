Helichrysum × fontqueri

Helichrysum × fontqueri, commonly known as the African daisy, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even when dried. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
