Haworthia leightonii

Haworthia leightonii, commonly known as the Lemon Surprise, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with prominent white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
