Helicia balansae

Helicia balansae, commonly known as the Balansae holly, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Theaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are glossy and leathery in texture. Helicia balansae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is valued in horticulture for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
