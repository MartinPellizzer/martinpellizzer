Hippeastrum breviflorum

Hippeastrum breviflorum, commonly known as the dwarf Amazon lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact size and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and pointed, growing from a bulbous base. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Hippeastrum breviflorum is known for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
