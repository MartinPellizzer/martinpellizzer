Hieracium coazii

Hieracium coazii, commonly known as the Coazii hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of many hawkweed species. Native to Europe, this plant thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows and woodland edges. It has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
