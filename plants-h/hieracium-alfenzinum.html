Hieracium alfenzinum

Hieracium alfenzinum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers with ray florets, and its leaves are usually basal and hairy.

Hieracium alfenzinum is often found in open meadows and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
