Hedysarum yinshanicum

Hedysarum yinshanicum, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow to orange flowers in late spring. The plant has a robust root system that helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Hedysarum yinshanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in arid landscapes.
