Hieracium tephrodes subsp. cinerosiforme

Hieracium tephrodes subsp.

cinerosiforme, commonly known as the ash-gray hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green leaves and yellow flowers arranged in a composite head.
