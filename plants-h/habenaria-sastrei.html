Habenaria sastrei

Habenaria sastrei, commonly known as the Sastri's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often fragrant. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
