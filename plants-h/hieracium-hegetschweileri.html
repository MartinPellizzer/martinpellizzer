Hieracium hegetschweileri

Hieracium hegetschweileri, commonly known as the Hegetschweiler's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
