Hypoxis decumbens var. mexicana

Hypoxis decumbens var. mexicana, commonly known as Mexican star grass, is a perennial herb native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It features a low-growing, spreading habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and is often found in shaded, moist environments.
