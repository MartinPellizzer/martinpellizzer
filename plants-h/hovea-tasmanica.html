Hovea tasmanica

Hovea tasmanica, commonly known as the Tasmanian hovea, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which bloom during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hovea tasmanica is adapted to the temperate climate of Tasmania and thrives in well-drained soils.
