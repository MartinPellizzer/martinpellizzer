Haworthia venetia

Haworthia venetia, commonly known as the Venetia Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically a deep green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
