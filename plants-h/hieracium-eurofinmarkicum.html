Hieracium eurofinmarkicum

Hieracium eurofinmarkicum, commonly known as the Eurofinmark hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to northern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
