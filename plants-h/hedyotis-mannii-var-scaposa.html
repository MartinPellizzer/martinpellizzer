Hedyotis mannii var. scaposa

Hedyotis mannii var. scaposa, commonly known as the slender mistflower, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in terminal racemes, and the leaves are opposite, ovate, and serrated.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.
