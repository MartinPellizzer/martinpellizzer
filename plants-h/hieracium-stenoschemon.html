Hieracium stenoschemon

Hieracium stenoschemon, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium stenoschemon is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and can form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
