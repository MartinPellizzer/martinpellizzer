Habenaria rhopaloceras

Habenaria rhopaloceras, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, upright stem and linear leaves. The plant produces striking, spurred orchid flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
