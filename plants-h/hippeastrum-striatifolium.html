Hippeastrum striatifolium

Hippeastrum striatifolium, commonly known as the Zebra lily, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to South America.

Hippeastrum striatifolium is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
