Halocharis lachnantha

Halocharis lachnantha, commonly known as the silverleaf halocharis, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Halocharis lachnantha is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
