Habenaria talaensis

Habenaria talaensis, commonly known as the Talaen Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short rhizome and a single, lanceolate leaf. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a pale green to yellowish perianth, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in moist, shaded forest environments and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
