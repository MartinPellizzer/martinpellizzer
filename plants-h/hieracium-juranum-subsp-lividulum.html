Hieracium juranum subsp. lividulum

Hieracium juranum subsp.

lividulum, commonly known as the purple meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in sunny, well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

The subspecies is distinguished by its vivid flower color and specific geographic distribution.
