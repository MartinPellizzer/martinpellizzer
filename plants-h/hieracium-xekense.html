Hieracium xekense

Hieracium xekense, commonly known as the Xekense hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and elongated stems that bear small, yellowish to reddish flowers. Native to specific regions in Turkey, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit, with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

Its flowers are typically tubular and are visited by various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
