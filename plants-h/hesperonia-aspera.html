Hesperonia aspera

Hesperonia aspera, commonly known as the rough hesperonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
