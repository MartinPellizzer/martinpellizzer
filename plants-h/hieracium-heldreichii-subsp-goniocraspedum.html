Hieracium heldreichii subsp. goniocraspedum

Hieracium heldreichii subsp.

goniocraspedum, commonly known as the Heldreich's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant typically has hairy stems and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
