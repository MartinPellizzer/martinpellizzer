Helictotrichon tentoense

It is native to the arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. This perennial grass is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.
