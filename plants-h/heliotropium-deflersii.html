Heliotropium deflersii

Heliotropium deflersii, commonly known as the desert heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces clusters of violet or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are typically tubular with five petals and are pollinated by insects such as bees and butterflies.
