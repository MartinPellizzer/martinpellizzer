Hieracium praecox var. pinicola

Hieracium praecox var. pinicola, commonly known as the early hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting various pollinators.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
