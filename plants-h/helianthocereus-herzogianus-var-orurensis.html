Helianthocereus herzogianus var. orurensis

Helianthocereus herzogianus var. orurensis, commonly known as the yellow night-blooming cereus, is a cactus species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and nocturnal flowers that open at night. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers with a strong fragrance, which attract pollinators such as moths. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique blooming habit.
