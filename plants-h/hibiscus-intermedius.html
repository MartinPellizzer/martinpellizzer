Hibiscus intermedius

Hibiscus intermedius, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Hibiscus intermedius thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking quick landscaping results.
