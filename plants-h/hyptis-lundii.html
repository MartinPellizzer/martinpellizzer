Hyptis lundii

Hyptis lundii, commonly known as silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and blue. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Hyptis lundii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
