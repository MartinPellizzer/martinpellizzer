Hieracium bifidum subsp. exfestiviforme

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

exfestiviforme, commonly known as the festive dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and a basal rosette structure.

Its name reflects its striking, showy inflorescence that resembles a miniature dandelion.
