Huperzia durissima

Huperzia durissima, commonly known as the hardy spikemoss, is a species of moss-like plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, spore-bearing stems and simple, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of tiny, scale-like structures. This plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms.

Huperzia durissima is relatively hardy and can tolerate a range of environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
