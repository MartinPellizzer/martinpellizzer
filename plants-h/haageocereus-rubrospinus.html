Haageocereus rubrospinus

Haageocereus rubrospinus, commonly known as the red-spined cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in reddish spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and cactus collections.
