Hieracium cottetii

Hieracium cottetii, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in the summer, adding a splash of color to mountainous landscapes.

Hieracium cottetii is often found in meadows and on slopes, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
