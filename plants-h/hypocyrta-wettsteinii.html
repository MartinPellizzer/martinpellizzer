Hypocyrta wettsteinii

Hypocyrta wettsteinii, commonly known as the Wettstein's spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, a characteristic feature of the spurge family.

The plant produces a latex sap that is milky and can cause skin irritation.
