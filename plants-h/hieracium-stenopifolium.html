Hieracium stenopifolium

Hieracium stenopifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring well-drained conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
