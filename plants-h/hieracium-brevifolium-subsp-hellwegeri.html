Hieracium brevifolium subsp. hellwegeri

Hieracium brevifolium subsp.

hellwegeri, commonly known as the Hellweger's short-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow flower heads.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous ray florets.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
