Huernia hislopii subsp. robusta

Huernia hislopii subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the robust Huernea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal watering, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus and succulent collections.
