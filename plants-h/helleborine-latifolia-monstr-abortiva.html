Helleborine latifolia monstr. abortiva

Helleborine latifolia monstr.

abortiva, commonly known as the broad-leaved helleborine, is a species of orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae. It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with purple markings.

Native to parts of Europe and Asia, this plant thrives in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. The species is known for its unique floral structure and its ability to grow in poor soil conditions.

It is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
