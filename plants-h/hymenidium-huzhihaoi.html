Hymenidium huzhihaoi

Hymenidium huzhihaoi, commonly known as the Huzhihao's hymenidium, is a rare and lesser-known member of the Hymenidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique leaf morphology, featuring narrow, elongated leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with pale yellow petals that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions of China.
