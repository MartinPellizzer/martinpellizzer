Hereroa pallens

Hereroa pallens, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
