Hesperis schaffneri

Hesperis schaffneri, commonly known as Schaffner's honesty, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features small, white flowers with four petals and heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Hesperis schaffneri is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
