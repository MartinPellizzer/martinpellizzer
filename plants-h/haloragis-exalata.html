Haloragis exalata

Haloragis exalata, commonly known as the slender water-starburry, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Haloragaceae family and is typically found in wetland environments. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, sunny conditions.

Haloragis exalata is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic habitats.
