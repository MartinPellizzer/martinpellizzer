Hoffmannia tonduzii

Hoffmannia tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduz's hoffmannia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds in its capitula. It is typically found in rocky or coastal habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
