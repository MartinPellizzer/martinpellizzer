Habenaria japonica var. minor

Habenaria japonica var. minor, commonly known as the Japanese marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan.

It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate moisture conditions.
