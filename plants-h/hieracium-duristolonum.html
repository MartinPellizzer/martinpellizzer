Hieracium duristolonum

Hieracium duristolonum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in waste areas.

It is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes and is sometimes considered invasive in certain regions.
