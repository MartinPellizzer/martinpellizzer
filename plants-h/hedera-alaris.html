Hedera alaris

Hedera alaris, commonly known as the silver ivy, is a climbing evergreen vine native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits. It thrives in shaded environments and is often used as a decorative ground cover or hanging plant.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
