Haworthia ubomboensis

Haworthia ubomboensis, commonly known as the Ubombo Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, white or yellow flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
