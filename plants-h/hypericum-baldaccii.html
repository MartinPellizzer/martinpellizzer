Hypericum baldaccii

Hypericum baldaccii, commonly known as the Baldaccii St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves with a slightly rough texture, and its stems are often reddish in color. It is part of the Hypericaceae family, which includes other species of St. John's Wort known for their medicinal properties.

Hypericum baldaccii is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
