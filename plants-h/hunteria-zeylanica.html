Hunteria zeylanica

Hunteria zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale purple, which bloom at night. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a yellow or orange color.
