Halgania cyanea

Halgania cyanea, commonly known as the blue hibiscus, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue-violet flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch and emit a mild fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Halgania cyanea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and unique appearance.
