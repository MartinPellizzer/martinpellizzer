Hiya brooksiae

Hiya brooksiae, commonly known as the brooksia or brooksia vine, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial vine that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and wetlands. The plant produces small, fragrant white or greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
