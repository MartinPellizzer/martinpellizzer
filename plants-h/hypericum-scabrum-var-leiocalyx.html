Hypericum scabrum var. leiocalyx

Hypericum scabrum var. leiocalyx, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in clusters along the stems. The plant has rough, woody stems and elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or woodland habitats.

This variety is distinguished by its smoother stems compared to the typical form of Hypericum scabrum.
