Hypericum humifusum var. obtusisepalum

Hypericum humifusum var. obtusisepalum, commonly known as the blunt-sepal St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to North America.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has woody stems that remain close to the ground, and its leaves are oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is distinguished by its blunt, rounded sepals, which give it its specific epithet.
