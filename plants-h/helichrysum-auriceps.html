Helichrysum auriceps

Helichrysum auriceps, commonly known as the golden-capped helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers with a distinctive golden cap on each floret, which gives it its common name. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
