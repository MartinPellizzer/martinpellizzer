Hymenocyclus luteus

Hymenocyclus luteus, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow petals and a central spathaceous structure. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are pollinated primarily by bees and other insects attracted by its nectar.

The botanical features of Hymenocyclus luteus make it a notable species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
