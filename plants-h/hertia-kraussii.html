Hertia kraussii

Hertia kraussii, commonly known as the Cape buttercup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Hertia kraussii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its hardy, drought-resistant nature.
