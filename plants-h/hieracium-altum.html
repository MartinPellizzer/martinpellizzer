Hieracium altum

Hieracium altum, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are usually hairy as well.

Hieracium altum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it plays a role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
