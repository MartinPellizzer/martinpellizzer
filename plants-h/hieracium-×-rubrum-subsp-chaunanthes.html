Hieracium × rubrum subsp. chaunanthes

Hieracium × rubrum subsp.

chaunanthes, commonly known as the red hawkweed, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.

The plant features basal rosettes of lance-shaped, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. It is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits variability in morphology and is often found in areas with diverse soil types.
