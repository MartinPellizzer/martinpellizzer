Hieracium × variifurcum

Hieracium × variifurcum, commonly known as the variable hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the hawkweed genus. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has variable leaf shapes, which contribute to its common name.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological traits that can vary significantly between individuals.
