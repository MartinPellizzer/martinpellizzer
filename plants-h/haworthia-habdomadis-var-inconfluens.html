Haworthia habdomadis var. inconfluens

Haworthia habdomadis var. inconfluens, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green with white or grayish markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, allowing the plant to thrive in arid conditions. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
