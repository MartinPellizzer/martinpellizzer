Helenium linifolium

Helenium linifolium, commonly known as the slender yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and bright yellow ray flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and arranged alternately along the stem.

Helenium linifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
