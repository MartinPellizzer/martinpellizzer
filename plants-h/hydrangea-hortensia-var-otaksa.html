Hydrangea hortensia var. otaksa

Hydrangea hortensia var. otaksa, commonly known as the Otaksa hydrangea, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and blue, depending on soil pH. The plant produces dense clusters of small, sterile flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to the blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to a variety of garden conditions.
