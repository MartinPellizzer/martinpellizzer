Hieracium candanchuanum

Hieracium candanchuanum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, hairy stems and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are typically alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium candanchuanum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, where it spreads through rhizomes and seeds.
