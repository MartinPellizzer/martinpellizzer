Hippeastrum purpurellum

Hippeastrum purpurellum, commonly known as the purple hurricane lily, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and purple, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features strap-like, linear leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hippeastrum purpurellum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
