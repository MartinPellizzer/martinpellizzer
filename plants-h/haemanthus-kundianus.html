Haemanthus kundianus

Haemanthus kundianus, commonly known as the red-stemmed haemanthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and sword-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a valuable addition to both cultivated and natural landscapes.
