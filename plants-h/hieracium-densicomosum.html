Hieracium densicomosum

Hieracium densicomosum, commonly known as the dense-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium densicomosum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in many regions.
