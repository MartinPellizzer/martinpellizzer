Hicorius ovalis var. mollis

Hicorius ovalis var. mollis, commonly known as the soft oval hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features large, showy flowers with five broad, waxy petals that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, soft pubescence, giving the plant its varietal name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
