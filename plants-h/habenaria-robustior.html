Habenaria robustior

Habenaria robustior, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to various regions in Southeast Asia, including Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on the forest floor or along stream banks. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that is adorned with intricate patterns.

The species is noted for its robust growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
