Helicia zeylanica

Helicia zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon holly, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its dense, glossy green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
