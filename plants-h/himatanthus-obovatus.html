Himatanthus obovatus

Himatanthus obovatus, commonly known as the African starwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant is evergreen and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.
