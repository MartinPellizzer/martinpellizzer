Habenaria filicornis var. chlorotica

Habenaria filicornis var. chlorotica, commonly known as the green-flowered helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip, which is a key feature of the species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories.

This variant is noted for its unique coloration and is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal.
