Hemigraphis rosmarinifolia

Hemigraphis rosmarinifolia, commonly known as Christmas moss, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, moss-like foliage that gives it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.
