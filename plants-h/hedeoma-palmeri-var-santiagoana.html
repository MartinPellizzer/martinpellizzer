Hedeoma palmeri var. santiagoana

Hedeoma palmeri var. santiagoana, commonly known as Santiago's hedeoma, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a dense cluster, typical of the genus Hedeoma.
