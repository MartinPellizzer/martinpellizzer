Hebe magellanica

Hebe magellanica, commonly known as Magellan hebe, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in a shrub form, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Hebe magellanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
