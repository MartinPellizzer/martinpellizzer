Huernia longii

Huernia longii, commonly known as the longii horned cucumber, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and spiny, leaf-like structures that resemble horns, giving it its common name. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Huernia longii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
