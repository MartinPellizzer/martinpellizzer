Hypericum tomentosum var. genuinum

Hypericum tomentosum var. genuinum, commonly known as the woolly St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to Europe.

It features dense, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers with orange stamens, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties have made it historically valued in herbal remedies.
