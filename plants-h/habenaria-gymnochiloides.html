Habenaria gymnochiloides

Habenaria gymnochiloides, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a loose raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many parts of its range.
