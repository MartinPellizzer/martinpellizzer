Hyptis pseudolantana

Hyptis pseudolantana, commonly known as false lantana, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to violet. Its leaves are often grayish-green and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hyptis pseudolantana is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
