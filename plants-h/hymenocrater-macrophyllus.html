Hymenocrater macrophyllus

Hymenocrater macrophyllus, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a striking member of the Blechnaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant features a robust, creeping rhizome that supports its extensive growth habit. Its leaves are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive, elongated shape.

Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
