Homalomena paliformis

Homalomena paliformis, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically enclosed within a spadix and surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

Homalomena paliformis is relatively low maintenance and can be grown indoors or outdoors in suitable climates.
