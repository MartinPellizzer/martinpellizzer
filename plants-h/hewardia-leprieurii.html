Hewardia leprieurii

Hewardia leprieurii, commonly known as the upright snake plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish tint along the edges. The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its fleshy leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers on slender stalks under optimal conditions.

Hewardia leprieurii is valued for its low maintenance and air-purifying qualities.
