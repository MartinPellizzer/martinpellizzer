Hieracium euexum

Hieracium euexum, commonly known as the European meadowhawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dandelion-like yellow flowers and hairy stems, which help in reducing water loss. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands across Europe. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its root system is fibrous, aiding in its survival in various soil types.

Hieracium euexum plays a role in supporting pollinators and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
