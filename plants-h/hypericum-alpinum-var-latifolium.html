Hypericum alpinum var. latifolium

Hypericum alpinum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf fireweed, is a perennial shrub native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with prominent stamens, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
