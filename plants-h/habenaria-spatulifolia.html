Habenaria spatulifolia

Habenaria spatulifolia, commonly known as the spoon-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features lanceolate to spoon-shaped leaves that are often glaucous in appearance. The plant produces racemes of small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive spur. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and attractive species within the Orchidaceae family.
