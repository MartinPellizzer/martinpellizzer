Holstia sessiliflora

Holstia sessiliflora, commonly known as the sessile-flowered holstia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its flowers are small and clustered, with yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale green to brown ray florets.

Holstia sessiliflora is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
