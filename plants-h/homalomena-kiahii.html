Homalomena kiahii

Homalomena kiahii, commonly known as the Kiahii arrowleaf.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant features a short, stout rhizome and produces striking inflorescences with a spadix and a spathe. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.
