Hedera Helix F. Arborescens

Hedera helix f. arborescens is a unique variety of ivy known for its striking, cascading growth habit. This form of the common English ivy features broad, leathery leaves that often display a variegated pattern of white and green. It is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments. While it shares the climbing traits of its parent species, it exhibits a more shrub-like appearance. This plant is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening due to its versatility and aesthetic appeal.
