Habenaria ctenophora

Habenaria ctenophora, commonly known as the feather-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique appearance and fragrance.
