Hymenorebutia torreana

Hymenorebutia torreana, commonly known as the Torreana Crown of Thorns, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are clustered at the ends of the stems. It is a low-growing plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
