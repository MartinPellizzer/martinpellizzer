Hermannia grosseserrata

Hermannia grosseserrata, commonly known as the serrated hermannia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often in shades of pink or white.

Hermannia grosseserrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
