Hieracium adenophyton

Hieracium adenophyton, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium adenophyton is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
