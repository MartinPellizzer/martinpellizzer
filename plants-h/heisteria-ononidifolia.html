Heisteria ononidifolia

Heisteria ononidifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf heister, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing, evergreen shrub characterized by its gray-green, leathery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
