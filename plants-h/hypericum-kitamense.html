Hypericum kitamense

Hypericum kitamense, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and upright growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum kitamense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
