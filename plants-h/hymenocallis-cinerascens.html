Hymenocallis cinerascens

Hymenocallis cinerascens, commonly known as the ash-gray hurricane lily, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces large, showy blooms that typically appear in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a waxy texture.

Hymenocallis cinerascens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
