Hebenstretia lanceolata

Hebenstretia lanceolata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of blue, violet, and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and velvety to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light conditions.

Hebenstretia lanceolata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
