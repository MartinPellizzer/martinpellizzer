Hypericum senanense

Hypericum senanense, commonly known as the Senanense St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in small, glandular dots.

Hypericum senanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
