Hieracium brennerianum

Hieracium brennerianum, commonly known as the Brenner's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, thriving in temperate climates. It produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium brennerianum is often found in open habitats and can sometimes form dense colonies.
