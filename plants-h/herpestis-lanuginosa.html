Herpestis lanuginosa

Herpestis lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, often containing several seeds.

Herpestis lanuginosa is typically found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
