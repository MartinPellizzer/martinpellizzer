Helianthemum canum f. alpinum

Helianthemum canum f. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine helianthemum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, succulent leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens with full sun exposure.
