Hieracium Holmiense

Hieracium holmiense is a rare and intriguing species of hawkweed native to the mountainous regions of Europe. This plant is known for its delicate yellow flowers and hairy stems, which help it survive in harsh alpine environments. It thrives in rocky soils and high-altitude areas where few other plants can grow. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from climate change and habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
