Helichrysum longifolium

Helichrysum longifolium, commonly known as the everlasting or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers and narrow, linear leaves. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for dried floral arrangements.
