Habenaria megaceras

Habenaria megaceras, commonly known as the giant horned orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that resemble horns, hence its common name. The plant produces intricate, fragrant blooms with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its complex floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
