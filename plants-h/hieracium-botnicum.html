Hieracium botnicum

Hieracium botnicum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium botnicum is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
