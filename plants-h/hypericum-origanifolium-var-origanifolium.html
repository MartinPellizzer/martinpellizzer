Hypericum origanifolium var. origanifolium

Hypericum origanifolium var. origanifolium, commonly known as the orange hypericum, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and long-lasting blooms.
