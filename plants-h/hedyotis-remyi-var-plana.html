Hedyotis remyi var. plana

Hedyotis remyi var. plana, commonly known as the flat-leaved dotseed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, opposite leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and bear the inflorescences at the tips.

Hedyotis remyi var. plana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
