Hoya billardierei

Hoya billardierei, commonly known as the laceleaf hoya, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, ovate leaves that are often marked with intricate patterns and a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hoya billardierei is popular among plant enthusiasts for its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
