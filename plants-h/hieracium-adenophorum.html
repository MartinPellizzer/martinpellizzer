Hieracium adenophorum

Hieracium adenophorum, commonly known as the腺花蒲公英, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of many dandelion-like species. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in meadows and grassy areas. It produces a large number of small seeds that are dispersed by the wind, a common reproductive strategy in the Asteraceae family.

The leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
