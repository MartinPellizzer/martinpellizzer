Hieracium heterospermum subsp. provinciale

Hieracium heterospermum subsp.

provinciale, commonly known as the provincial hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in grassy areas and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies.

Due to its invasive tendencies, it is considered a weed in some regions.
