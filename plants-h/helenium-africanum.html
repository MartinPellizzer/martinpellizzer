Helenium africanum

Helenium africanum, commonly known as the African sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces bright orange to red ray flowers, which are typically arranged in a single row, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental value.

Helenium africanum is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
