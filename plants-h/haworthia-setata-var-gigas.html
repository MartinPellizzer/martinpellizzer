Haworthia setata var. gigas

Haworthia setata var. gigas, commonly known as the giant Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically thick and fleshy, with a distinctive white or pale green coloration. The leaves are adorned with white tubercles and often have a translucent or waxy texture. This variety is known for its compact size and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
