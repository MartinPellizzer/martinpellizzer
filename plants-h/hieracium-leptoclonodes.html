Hieracium leptoclonodes

Hieracium leptoclonodes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves. The plant produces yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disc florets, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium leptoclonodes is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative propagation.
