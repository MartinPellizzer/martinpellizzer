Hieracium praemorsum

Hieracium praemorsum, commonly known as the death's-head hawkmoth plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers that are tubular in shape, with prominent purple markings. It produces achenes as its fruit, which are dispersed by wind.
