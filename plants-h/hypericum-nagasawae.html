Hypericum nagasawae

Hypericum nagasawae, commonly known as the Nagaoka St. John's Wort, is a species of flowering plant in the family Hypericaceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It has small, opposite leaves and produces small, red-orange fruits that contain seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
