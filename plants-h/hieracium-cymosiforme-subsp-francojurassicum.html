Hieracium cymosiforme subsp. francojurassicum

Hieracium cymosiforme subsp.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.
