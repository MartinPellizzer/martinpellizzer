Hyptis propinqua

Hyptis propinqua, commonly known as silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. Hyptis propinqua is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
