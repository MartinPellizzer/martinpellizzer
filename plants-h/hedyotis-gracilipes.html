Hedyotis gracilipes

Hedyotis gracilipes, commonly known as slender leaf bladdernut, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are usually white or pale yellow.

Hedyotis gracilipes is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
