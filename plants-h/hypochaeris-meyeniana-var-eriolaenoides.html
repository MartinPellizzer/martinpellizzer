Hypochaeris meyeniana var. eriolaenoides

Hypochaeris meyeniana var. eriolaenoides, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in open areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer.

It is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural and natural settings.
