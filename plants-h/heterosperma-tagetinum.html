Heterosperma tagetinum

Heterosperma tagetinum, commonly known as the golden water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, floating leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy surface. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its roots are anchored in the mud at the bottom of water bodies.

Heterosperma tagetinum plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
