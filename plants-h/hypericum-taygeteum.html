Hypericum taygeteum

Hypericum taygeteum, commonly known as the Greek burnet, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has oval, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is often found in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun.

Hypericum taygeteum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
