Hypericum canescens

Hypericum canescens, commonly known as pale St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features yellow flowers with four petals, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval, and covered in fine, white hairs.

Hypericum canescens is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
