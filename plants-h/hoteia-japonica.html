Hoteia japonica

Hoteia japonica, commonly known as the Japanese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to deep red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hoteia japonica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
