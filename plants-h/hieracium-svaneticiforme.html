Hieracium svaneticiforme

Hieracium svaneticiforme, commonly known as the Svaneti hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from yellow to red.

Hieracium svaneticiforme is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
