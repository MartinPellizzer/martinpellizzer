Habenaria bimaculata

Habenaria bimaculata, commonly known as the two-spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with two distinct dark spots on each petal. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are often mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
