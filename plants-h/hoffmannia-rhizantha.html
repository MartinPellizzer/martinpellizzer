Hoffmannia rhizantha

Hoffmannia rhizantha, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. Hoffmannia rhizantha is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
