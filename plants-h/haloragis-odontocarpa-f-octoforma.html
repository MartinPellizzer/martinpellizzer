Haloragis odontocarpa f. octoforma

Haloragis odontocarpa f. octoforma, commonly known as the eight-leafed waterwheel, is a member of the Haloragaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland or aquatic environments. The plant is characterized by its distinctive whorled arrangement of eight leaves around each node. Its small, green flowers are usually clustered in dense inflorescences.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
