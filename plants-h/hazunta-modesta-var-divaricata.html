Hazunta modesta var. divaricata

Hazunta modesta var. divaricata, commonly known as the slender hazunta, is a unique variant of the hazunta modesta species.

It is characterized by its slender, branching structure and smaller leaves compared to the typical form. This plant is native to the humid lowland forests of Southeast Asia. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Its flowers are small and pale green, often overlooked but essential for its reproductive cycle.
