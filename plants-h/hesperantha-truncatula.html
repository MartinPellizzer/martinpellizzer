Hesperantha truncatula

Hesperantha truncatula, commonly known as the dwarf African bell orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a unique, spurred lip. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
