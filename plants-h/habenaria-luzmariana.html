Habenaria luzmariana

Habenaria luzmariana, commonly known as the Luzmarana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with three petals and a distinctive lip. It typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, preferring rich, well-drained soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
