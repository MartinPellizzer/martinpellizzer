Hypoestes sinica

Hypoestes sinica, commonly known as the Chinese ice plant, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals. The plant typically grows as a low, spreading herb with fleshy leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoestes sinica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
