Haworthia Arachnoidea Subsp. Tenera

Spider haworthia, scientifically known as Haworthia arachnoidea subsp. tenera, is a unique and striking succulent native to southern Africa. Its name derives from the spider-like appearance of its spiky, translucent leaves that resemble delicate webbing. This plant is prized for its compact size, ease of care, and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. With its rosette shape and subtle color variations, it adds a touch of elegance to any indoor or outdoor garden. It is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
