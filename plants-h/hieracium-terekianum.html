Hieracium terekianum

Hieracium terekianum, commonly known as Terekian's hawkweed, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a single, upright flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant has small, rough, and hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.

It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to cold climates with short growing seasons.
