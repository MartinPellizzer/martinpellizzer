Hieracium jarzabczynum

Hieracium jarzabczynum, commonly known as the Jarzabczynski's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
