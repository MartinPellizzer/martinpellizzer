Hedyotis tillaeacea

Hedyotis tillaeacea, commonly known as the mountain buttonweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It typically features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Hedyotis tillaeacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
