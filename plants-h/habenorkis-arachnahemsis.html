Habenorkis arachnahemsis

Habenorkis arachnahemsis, commonly known as the spiderweb orchid, is a rare and exotic flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the spring season.
