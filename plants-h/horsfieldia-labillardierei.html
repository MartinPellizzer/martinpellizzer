Horsfieldia labillardierei

Horsfieldia labillardierei, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Malaysia, Indonesia, and Thailand. The plant is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces small, dark brown seeds.

Horsfieldia labillardierei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
