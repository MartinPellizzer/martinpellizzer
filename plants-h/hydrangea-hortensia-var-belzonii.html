Hydrangea hortensia var. belzonii

Hydrangea hortensia var. belzonii, commonly known as the Belzonii hydrangea, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that range from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant produces sterile flowers, which are actually clusters of small, individual blooms. It thrives in partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom reliably in garden settings.
