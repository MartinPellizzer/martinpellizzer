Helicteres acuminata

Helicteres acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata sycamore, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its palmate leaves with five to seven lobes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
