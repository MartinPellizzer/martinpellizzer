Hydrocotyle mollissima

Hydrocotyle mollissima, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Hydrocotyle mollissima is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
