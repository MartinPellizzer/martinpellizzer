Hippeastrum equestre var. semiplenum

Hippeastrum equestre var. semiplenum, commonly known as the Horseman's Aroid, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive red or purple stripe down the center. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It is a tropical plant that thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its hybrid nature, it is often cultivated for its striking floral display in gardens and as a houseplant.
