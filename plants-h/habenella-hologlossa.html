Habenella hologlossa

Habenella hologlossa, commonly known as the velvet leaf, is a unique plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage that gives it its common name. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
