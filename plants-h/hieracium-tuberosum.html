Hieracium tuberosum

Hieracium tuberosum, commonly known as the tuberous hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves and produces yellow or orange composite flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has tuberous roots that allow it to survive in harsh conditions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
