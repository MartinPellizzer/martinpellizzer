Hermione cypri var. semiplena

Hermione cypri var. semiplena, commonly known as the semi-double cypripedium, is a unique variant of the cypripedium orchid.

It is characterized by its semi-double petals, which give it a more elaborate and showy appearance compared to the typical single-petaled form. The plant features a striking, showy flower with a prominent lip that is often a different color from the rest of the petals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is native to certain regions of Asia.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
