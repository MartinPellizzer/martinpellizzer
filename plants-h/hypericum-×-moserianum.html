Hypericum × moserianum

Hypericum × moserianum, commonly known as the golden-stemmed St. John's wort, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has slender, woody stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for borders and rock gardens.

Its medicinal properties have also been studied, though it contains compounds that can be harmful if misused.
