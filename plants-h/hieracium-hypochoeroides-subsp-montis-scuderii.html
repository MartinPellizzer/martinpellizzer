Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. montis-scuderii

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

montis-scuderii, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and bear several flower heads.

It is characterized by its compact growth form and is often found in high-altitude environments.
