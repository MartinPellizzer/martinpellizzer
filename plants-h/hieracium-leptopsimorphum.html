Hieracium leptopsimorphum

Hieracium leptopsimorphum, commonly known as the slenderleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow ray flowers with numerous disc florets. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats such as meadows and roadsides. The plant has a taproot system and is often found in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the genus Hieracium.
