Hibbertia hibbertioides var. pedunculata

Hibbertia hibbertioides var. pedunculata, commonly known as the mountain hibbertia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with prominent red or orange markings. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hibbertia hibbertioides var. pedunculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
