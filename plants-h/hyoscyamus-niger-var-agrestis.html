Hyoscyamus niger var. agrestis

Hyoscyamus niger var. agrestis, commonly known as deadly nightshade or black henbane, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and purple or violet flowers. The plant produces small, dark berries that contain toxic alkaloids, making it highly poisonous to humans and animals. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, along roadsides, and in waste areas.

Due to its toxic properties, it is considered a noxious weed in many regions.
