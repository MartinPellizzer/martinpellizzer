Haplopappus multicaulis

Haplopappus multicaulis, commonly known as the slender pappus plant, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The plant is known for its adaptability to arid environments and is often used in native plant landscaping.
