Hieracium canum subsp. leptobium

Hieracium canum subsp.

leptobium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in grassy areas and is often found in disturbed soils.
