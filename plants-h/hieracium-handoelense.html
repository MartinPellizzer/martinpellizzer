Hieracium handoelense

Hieracium handoelense, commonly known as the Handoel hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, and its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.

Hieracium handoelense is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
