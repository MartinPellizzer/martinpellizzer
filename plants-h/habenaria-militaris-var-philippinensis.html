Habenaria militaris var. philippinensis

Habenaria militaris var. philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine sword orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in montane forests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
