Hyparrhenia yunnanensis

Hyparrhenia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Hyparrhenia yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
