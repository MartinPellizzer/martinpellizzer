Habenaria elegans

Habenaria elegans, commonly known as the elegant twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring a slender, grass-like stem and a single, showy flower. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and woodlands. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette, and it produces a single, erect flower with a spurred lip.

Habenaria elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
