Hieracium transiens subsp. albellipes

Hieracium transiens subsp.

albellipes, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender stems and smaller, more elongated leaves compared to other Hieracium species.
