Habenaria leucophaea var. praeclara

Habenaria leucophaea var. praeclara, commonly known as the White-veined Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, upright stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are pale green with distinct white veins. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green to white flowers with three sepals and three petals, often with a lip that is slightly broader than the others. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is prized for its delicate appearance and is cultivated for ornamental purposes.
