Habenaria perfoliata

Habenaria perfoliata, commonly known as the perforate orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves that have a distinctive perforation near the base. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with a single prominent lip. It thrives in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and mountainous regions.

Its unique leaf structure and delicate blooms make it a notable species in orchidology.
