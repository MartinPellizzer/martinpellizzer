Hieracium abradenium

Hieracium abradenium, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Compositae).

It is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a single head. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

Hieracium abradenium is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
