Hieracium tommasinii subsp. adenothyrsum

Hieracium tommasinii subsp.

adenothyrsum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish flowers.

The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in rocky or alpine environments, often growing at high altitudes.
