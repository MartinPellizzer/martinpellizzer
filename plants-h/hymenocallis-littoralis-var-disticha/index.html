Hymenocallis Littoralis Var. Disticha

Hymenocallis littoralis var. disticha is a striking tropical plant known for its elegant white flowers and broad, strap-like leaves. Native to coastal regions of the southeastern United States, it thrives in sandy, well-drained soils near the shore. This variety is a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience in challenging environments. Its unique appearance makes it a standout addition to landscapes or containers. With proper care, it can bloom reliably year after year, adding a touch of subtropical charm to any garden.
