Heliotropium turcicum

Heliotropium turcicum, commonly known as Turkish heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, violet or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Heliotropium turcicum is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and adaptable species.
