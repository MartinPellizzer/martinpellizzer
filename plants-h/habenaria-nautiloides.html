Habenaria nautiloides

Habenaria nautiloides, commonly known as the nautiloid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots and leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in a raceme, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral structure.
