Habenaria humilior var. brevicalcarata

Habenaria humilior var. brevicalcarata, commonly known as the short-spurred orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish with prominent, short spurs. It thrives in shaded, moist habitats such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

This variety is notable for its delicate appearance and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
