Hornstedtia sphaerocephala

Hornstedtia sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe-headed orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South America.

This perennial herbaceous plant is characterized by its compact, globular inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive hood-like structure covering the reproductive organs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests.

The plant's unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species in botanical studies.
