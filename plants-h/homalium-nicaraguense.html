Homalium nicaraguense

Homalium nicaraguense, commonly known as the Nicaraguan beautyberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to Central America, particularly Nicaragua, and is characterized by its striking, violet-blue berries that appear in clusters. The berries are not only ornamental but also serve as a food source for various bird species.
