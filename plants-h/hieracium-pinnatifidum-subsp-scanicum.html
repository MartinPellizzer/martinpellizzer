Hieracium pinnatifidum subsp. scanicum

Hieracium pinnatifidum subsp.

scanicum, commonly known as the Scandic hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and woodland edges.

The plant has a rosette of basal leaves that are pinnately lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.
