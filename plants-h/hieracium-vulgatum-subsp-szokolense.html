Hieracium vulgatum subsp. szokolense

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

szokolense, commonly known as the Szokolense hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry meadows, grasslands, and open woodlands. It has hairy stems and leaves with a serrated margin.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in several regions.
