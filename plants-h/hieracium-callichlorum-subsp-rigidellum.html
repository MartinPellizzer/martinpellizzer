Hieracium callichlorum subsp. rigidellum

Hieracium callichlorum subsp.

rigidellum, commonly known as rigid-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are narrowly lance-shaped. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a single head, surrounded by pale yellow to brownish bracts.

It is characterized by its rigid, somewhat fleshy leaves and is often found in dry, rocky environments.
