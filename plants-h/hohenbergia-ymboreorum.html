Hohenbergia ymboreorum

Hohenbergia ymboreorum, commonly known as the Ymboreo Hohenbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features broad, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hohenbergia ymboreorum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
