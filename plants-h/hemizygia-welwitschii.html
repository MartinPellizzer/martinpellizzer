Hemizygia welwitschii

It is characterized by its two large, leathery leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant has a thick, woody trunk that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, and its seeds are dispersed by animals.

Hemizygia welwitschii is considered a living fossil due to its ancient lineage and distinctive morphology.
