Hymenocallis ornata

Hymenocallis ornata, commonly known as the Mexican spider lily, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its striking, funnel-shaped white flowers with a yellow throat. The plant produces strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and smooth. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hymenocallis ornata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in warm climates.
