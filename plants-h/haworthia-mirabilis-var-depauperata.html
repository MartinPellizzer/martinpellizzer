Haworthia mirabilis var. depauperata

Haworthia mirabilis var. depauperata, commonly known as the "Bald Haworthia," is a compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, pale green leaves with a distinctive white or light pinkish bloom along the edges. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall stems during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for low-maintenance indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
