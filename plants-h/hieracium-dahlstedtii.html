Hieracium dahlstedtii

Hieracium dahlstedtii, commonly known as the Dahlstedt's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is often found in open habitats across its range.
