Hermione splendens

Hermione splendens, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange, often with red or purple streaks. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and waxy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Its blooming period typically occurs during the warmer months, adding a splash of color to its surroundings.
