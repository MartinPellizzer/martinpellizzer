Hieracium kemiticum

Hieracium kemiticum, commonly known as the Kemitic hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It features basal leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture.

The flowers are yellowish to reddish in color and bloom from late spring to early autumn.
