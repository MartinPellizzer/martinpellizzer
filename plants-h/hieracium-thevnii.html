Hieracium thevnii

Hieracium thevnii, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its yellow flowers bloom in summer, and it has a rosette of basal leaves that are covered in fine hairs.
