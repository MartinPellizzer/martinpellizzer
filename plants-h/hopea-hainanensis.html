Hopea hainanensis

Hopea hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan hopea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern China, particularly Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense canopy. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers and has distinctive elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. Its seeds are enclosed in woody capsules, and the plant is valued for its hard, durable wood.

Hopea hainanensis plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitat.
