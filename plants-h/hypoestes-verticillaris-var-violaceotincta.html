Hypoestes verticillaris var. violaceotincta

Hypoestes verticillaris var. violaceotincta, commonly known as the Purple Trumpet Plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, violet-pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for both garden beds and container gardening.

Its unique appearance and vibrant color make it a standout in ornamental horticulture.
