Hippeastrum stenopetalum

Hippeastrum stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers with narrow petals and a prominent, often colorful central spadix. The plant produces strap-shaped leaves that are broad and waxy, typical of the Amaryllidaceae family. It blooms in late spring or early summer, depending on the climate.

Hippeastrum stenopetalum is popular as a cultivated ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
