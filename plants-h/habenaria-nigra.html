Habenaria nigra

Habenaria nigra, commonly known as the black orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and swamps. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable and often endangered species in its native range.
