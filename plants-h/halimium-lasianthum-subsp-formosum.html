Halimium lasianthum subsp. formosum

Halimium lasianthum subsp.

formosum, commonly known as the purple sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, blooming in early spring.

The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to coastal and inland areas with dry summers.
