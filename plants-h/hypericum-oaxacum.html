Hypericum oaxacum

Hypericum oaxacum, commonly known as the Oaxacan St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum oaxacum is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
