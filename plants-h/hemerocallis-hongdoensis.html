Hemerocallis hongdoensis

Hemerocallis hongdoensis, commonly known as Hongdo Daylily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Korea.

It belongs to the genus Hemerocallis, which includes many species of daylilies known for their showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant yellow or orange blooms that last only one day, hence its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adapted to well-drained soils.

Hemerocallis hongdoensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
