Hieracium irriguum subsp. irriguiforme

Hieracium irriguum subsp.

irriguiforme, commonly known as the water ragwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in moist environments such as wet meadows, marshes, and along riverbanks.

The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface and produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The species is often found in nutrient-rich soils and is relatively tolerant of wet conditions.
