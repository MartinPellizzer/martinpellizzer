Hieracium praerosum

Hieracium praerosum, commonly known as the early hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium praerosum is often considered a weed but plays a role in supporting pollinators and maintaining soil stability.
