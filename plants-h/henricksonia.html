Henricksonia

Henricksonia, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plants typically have palmately lobed leaves and produce capsules as fruits. Henricksonia species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands.

They are often cultivated for their ornamental value and are adapted to thrive in warm, humid climates.
