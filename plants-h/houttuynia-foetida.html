Houttuynia foetida

Houttuynia foetida, commonly known as the snake plant or Chinese evergreen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive yellowish or white midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
