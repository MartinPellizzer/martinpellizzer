Hieracium hoglandicum

Hieracium hoglandicum, commonly known as Hogland's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring well-drained conditions. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

The leaves are generally lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
