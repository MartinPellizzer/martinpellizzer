Hieracium Sparsum Subsp. Svaneticiforme

Hieracium sparsum subsp. svaneticiforme is a lesser-known member of the dandelion family known for its delicate, yellow flowers. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, this plant thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its unique flower shape and subtle coloration make it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its modest size and unassuming appearance. This species offers valuable insights into the diversity within the genus Hieracium.
