Hieracium nigrescens subsp. petiolinum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

petiolinum, commonly known as the petiolate hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped with hairy surfaces, and the flowers are yellow with ray florets.

It is a perennial species that can form dense colonies in suitable environments.
