Hieracium sonchoides subsp. malacotrichodes

Hieracium sonchoides subsp.

malacotrichodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves.

Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this species thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological and botanical studies.
