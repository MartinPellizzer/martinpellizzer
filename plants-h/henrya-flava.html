Henrya flava

Henrya flava, commonly known as the yellow henrya, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to Japan.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
