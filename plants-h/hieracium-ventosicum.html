Hieracium ventosicum

Hieracium ventosicum, commonly known as the windy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadside areas. It features a robust root system and hairy stems, with alternate leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
