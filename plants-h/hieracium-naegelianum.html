Hieracium naegelianum

Hieracium naegelianum, commonly known as the Naegeli's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowering heads and is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has basal leaves that are often hairy and can be lobed or divided.

Hieracium naegelianum is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
