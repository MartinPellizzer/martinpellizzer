Habenaria formosana

Habenaria formosana, commonly known as the Formosa orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow markings, which are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
