Hieracium bifidum subsp. sokolense

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

sokolense, commonly known as the Balkan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in composite inflorescences, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.
