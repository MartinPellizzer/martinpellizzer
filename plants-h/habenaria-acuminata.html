Habenaria acuminata

Habenaria acuminata, commonly known as the acuminated orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with purple markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Habenaria acuminata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
