Hordeum coleophorum

Hordeum coleophorum, commonly known as the slender barley or dwarf barley, is a grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. The plant typically grows in grasslands, open woodlands, and disturbed soils, often in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive blue-green hue.

Hordeum coleophorum is primarily of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological role and potential as a forage plant.
