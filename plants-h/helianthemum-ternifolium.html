Helianthemum ternifolium

Helianthemum ternifolium, commonly known as the rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, evergreen leaves that are typically divided into three parts, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous areas.

Helianthemum ternifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
