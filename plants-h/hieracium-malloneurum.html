Hieracium malloneurum

Hieracium malloneurum, commonly known as the Malloneurum hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces yellow flowers with ray florets and is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain environments.
