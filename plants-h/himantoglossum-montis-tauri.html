Himantoglossum montis-tauri

Himantoglossum montis-tauri, commonly known as the Taurus mountain orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found in the Taurus Mountains of Turkey.

It is a terrestrial herb with a tall, slender stem and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in late spring. The plant has a specialized root system adapted to the rocky and nutrient-poor soils of its mountainous habitat. Its flowers are usually purple or violet in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.
