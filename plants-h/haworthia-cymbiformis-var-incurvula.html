Haworthia cymbiformis var. incurvula

Haworthia cymbiformis var. incurvula, commonly known as the fleshy leaf Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The leaves are often curled or folded, giving the plant its characteristic appearance. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the growing season.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
