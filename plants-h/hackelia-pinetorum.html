Hackelia pinetorum

Hackelia pinetorum, commonly known as the pine forest geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in moist, acidic soils under the canopy of pine forests.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to shaded, woodland environments.
