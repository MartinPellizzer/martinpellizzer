Hesperantha fibrosa

Hesperantha fibrosa, commonly known as the fibrous bell orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, bell-shaped flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, adding a striking visual element to its natural habitat.

Hesperantha fibrosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
