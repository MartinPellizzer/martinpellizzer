Hieracium goniophyllum

Hieracium goniophyllum, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive yellow ray flowers that surround a central disk. The plant has rough, hairy leaves and thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows and disturbed soils. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Hieracium goniophyllum is often found in open areas where it can spread rapidly due to its ability to produce numerous seeds.
