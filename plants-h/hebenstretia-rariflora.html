Hebenstretia rariflora

Hebenstretia rariflora, commonly known as the rare-flowered hebenstretia, is a striking member of the Apocynaceae family native to South Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal gardens.
