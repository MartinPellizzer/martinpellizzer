Helichrysum panormitanum subsp. brulloi

Helichrysum panormitanum subsp.

brulloi, commonly known as the Brullo's everlasting, is a member of the Asteraceae family. It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense clusters of yellow to golden-orange flowers.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
