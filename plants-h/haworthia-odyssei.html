Haworthia odyssei

Haworthia odyssei, commonly known as the Odyssean Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically fleshy, with a bluish-green hue and often feature white or pale markings. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
