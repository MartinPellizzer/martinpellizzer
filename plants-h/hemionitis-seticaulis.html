Hemionitis seticaulis

Hemionitis seticaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hemionitis, is a rare and specialized fern native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous or desert-like environments. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are located on the undersides of the fronds, a common feature among ferns.
