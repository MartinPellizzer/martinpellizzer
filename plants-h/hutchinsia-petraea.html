Hutchinsia petraea

Hutchinsia petraea, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a cluster, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
