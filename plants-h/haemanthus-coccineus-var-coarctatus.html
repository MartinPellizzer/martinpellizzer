Haemanthus coccineus var. coarctatus

Haemanthus coccineus var. coarctatus, commonly known as the red haemanthus, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant red flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a dramatic visual impact to gardens. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its bold, colorful display.
