Hypolepis tenerifrons

Hypolepis tenerifrons, commonly known as the Canary Island sword fern, is a species of fern native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with the rhizomes often covered in a dense layer of roots. Its fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive, almost fan-like appearance.

This fern thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
