Habenaria straminea

Habenaria straminea, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to various regions in Asia, including China, Japan, and Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale yellow, and are arranged in a loose cluster.

Habenaria straminea is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
