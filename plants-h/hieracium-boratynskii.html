Hieracium boratynskii

Hieracium boratynskii, commonly known as the Boratynskii hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering stems that bear yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across temperate regions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with rough, hairy surfaces, and the flowers consist of both ray and disk florets.

Hieracium boratynskii is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
