Heterospathe brevicaulis

Heterospathe brevicaulis, commonly known as the short-stemmed fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

Its flowers are small and clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, typical of the Araceae family.

Heterospathe brevicaulis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to shaded conditions.
