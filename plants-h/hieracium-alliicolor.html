Hieracium alliicolor

Hieracium alliicolor, commonly known as the garlic-yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are usually hairy and somewhat reddish.

Hieracium alliicolor is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many temperate regions.
