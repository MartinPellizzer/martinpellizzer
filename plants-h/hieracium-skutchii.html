Hieracium skutchii

Hieracium skutchii, commonly known as Skutch's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
