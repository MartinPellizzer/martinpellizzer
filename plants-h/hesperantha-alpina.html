Hesperantha alpina

Hesperantha alpina, commonly known as the alpine hesperantha, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. It produces a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and blooms in summer.

Hesperantha alpina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to cool, sunny environments.
