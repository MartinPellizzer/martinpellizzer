Habenaria duckeana

Habenaria duckeana, commonly known as the Ducke's orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Habenaria duckeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a subject of interest in botanical research.
