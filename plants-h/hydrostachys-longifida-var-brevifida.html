Hydrostachys longifida var. brevifida

Hydrostachys longifida var. brevifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hydrostachys, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with small, fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by tiny, round fruits. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in rocky or sandy soils.
