Hypericum bonatii

Hypericum bonatii, commonly known as the Bonatti St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antidepressant effects.
