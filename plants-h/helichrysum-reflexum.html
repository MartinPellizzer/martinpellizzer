Helichrysum reflexum

Helichrysum reflexum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, bright yellow flowers that resemble daisies. The plant is evergreen and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its compact growth habit and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Helichrysum reflexum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
