Heptapleurum multispicatum

Heptapleurum multispicatum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a member of the Araliaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a semi-evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage.

Its adaptability to indoor conditions makes it a popular choice for houseplants.
