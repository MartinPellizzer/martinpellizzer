Haploesthes fruticosa

Haploesthes fruticosa, commonly known as the desert morning glory, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in the early morning, hence its common name. Its leaves are small, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Haploesthes fruticosa is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water requirements.
