Hieracium euthylepis

Hieracium euthylepis, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragweed, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium euthylepis is often found in open habitats and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
