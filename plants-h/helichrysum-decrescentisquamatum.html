Helichrysum decrescentisquamatum

Helichrysum decrescentisquamatum, commonly known as the silverleaf helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in compact heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in arid landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
