Haworthia setulifera

Haworthia setulifera, commonly known as the serrated Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale green markings and sharp, serrated edges. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers on slender stalks during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
