Habenaria richardsiae

Habenaria richardsiae, commonly known as the Richards' butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has a slender, erect stem with several leaves and produces spikes of small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
