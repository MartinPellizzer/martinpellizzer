Heliotropium tenuifolium var. benthamianum

Heliotropium tenuifolium var. benthamianum, commonly known as slender-leaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with opposite, linear to lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or disturbed sites.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in ecological and horticultural contexts.
