Helichrysum setosum

Helichrysum setosum, commonly known as the woolly sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers and hairy, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, bracted involucres that protect its tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum setosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
