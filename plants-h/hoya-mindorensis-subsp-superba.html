Hoya mindorensis subsp. superba

Hoya mindorensis subsp.

superba, commonly known as the Mindanao star hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines. It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, dark green leaves.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle fragrance. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental value. Hoya mindorensis subsp.

superba thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
