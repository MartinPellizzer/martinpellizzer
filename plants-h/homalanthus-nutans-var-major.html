Homalanthus nutans var. major

Homalanthus nutans var. major, commonly known as the broadleaf dandelion, is a variant of the dandelion species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant produces a capsule fruit that contains numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open habitats such as roadsides and meadows.

Its botanical features make it an important plant in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
