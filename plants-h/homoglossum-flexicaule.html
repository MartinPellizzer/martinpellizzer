Homoglossum flexicaule

Homoglossum flexicaule, commonly known as the flexible homoglossum, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
