Hieracium kozlowskyanum

Hieracium kozlowskyanum, commonly known as Kozlowski's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, exhibiting a rosette of basal leaves. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the genus Hieracium.

It is noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions and has been studied for potential horticultural and ecological applications.
