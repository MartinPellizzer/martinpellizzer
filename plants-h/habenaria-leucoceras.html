Habenaria leucoceras

Habenaria leucoceras, commonly known as the white-lipped twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and a single, slender stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a loose raceme. It is native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Korean Peninsula.

Habenaria leucoceras is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and mountainous regions.
