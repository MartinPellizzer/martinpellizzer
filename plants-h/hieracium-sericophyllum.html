Hieracium sericophyllum

Hieracium sericophyllum, commonly known as the silky hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium sericophyllum is often found in meadows and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils.
