Hartogia sabulosa

Hartogia sabulosa, commonly known as the sandstone daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Hartogia sabulosa is well-suited to full sun and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
