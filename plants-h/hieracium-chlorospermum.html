Hieracium chlorospermum

Hieracium chlorospermum, commonly known as the green-veined hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds, which aid in its propagation.
