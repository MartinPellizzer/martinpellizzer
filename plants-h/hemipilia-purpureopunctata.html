Hemipilia purpureopunctata

Hemipilia purpureopunctata, commonly known as the purple-spotted hemipilia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pale pink or white with purple spots, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Hemipilia purpureopunctata thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
