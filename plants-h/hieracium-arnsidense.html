Hieracium arnsidense

Hieracium arnsidense, commonly known as the Arnsidense hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a single, upright stem bearing yellow flowers. The plant produces small, achene fruits that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in meadows and along streambanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
