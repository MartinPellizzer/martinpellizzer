Hypoxis colchicifolia

Hypoxis colchicifolia, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with prominent orange stamens, which bloom at night. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique nocturnal blooming habit and ornamental appeal make it a valuable addition to horticultural collections.
