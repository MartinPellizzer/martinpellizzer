Hieracium rotundellum

Hieracium rotundellum, commonly known as the roundleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a cluster of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has hairy stems and leaves that are typically rounded or oval in shape.

The plant is known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
