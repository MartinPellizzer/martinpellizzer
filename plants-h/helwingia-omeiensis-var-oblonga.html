Helwingia omeiensis var. oblonga

Helwingia omeiensis var. oblonga, commonly known as the Chinese helwingia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
