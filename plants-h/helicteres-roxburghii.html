Helicteres roxburghii

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks.
