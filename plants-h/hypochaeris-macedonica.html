Hypochaeris macedonica

Hypochaeris macedonica, commonly known as the Macedonian weed, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disc florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hypochaeris macedonica is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
