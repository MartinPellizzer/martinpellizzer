Hymenopappus flavescens var. canotomentosus

Hymenopappus flavescens var. canotomentosus, commonly known as the yellow burrweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and papery bracts. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and disturbed areas.
