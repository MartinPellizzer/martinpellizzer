Hieracium hypsiphaes

Hieracium hypsiphaes, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe, particularly in regions such as the British Isles and parts of Scandinavia. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and the stems are often reddish in color.

Hieracium hypsiphaes is typically found in grasslands, woodland edges, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
