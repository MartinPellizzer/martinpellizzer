Huperzia liangshanica

Huperzia liangshanica, commonly known as the Liangshan clubmoss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and short, erect stems that bear clusters of spore-producing structures called sporangia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Huperzia liangshanica plays a role in maintaining soil stability and provides habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
