Heliotropium anomalum

Heliotropium anomalum, commonly known as the tropical heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple, which bloom in response to sunlight. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Heliotropium anomalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
