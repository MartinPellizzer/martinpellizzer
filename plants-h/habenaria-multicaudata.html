Habenaria multicaudata

Habenaria multicaudata, commonly known as the many-tailed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous small, greenish-yellow flowers that have a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
