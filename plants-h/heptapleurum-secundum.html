Heptapleurum secundum

Heptapleurum secundum, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, bipinnate fronds that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its elegant, lace-like texture.

Heptapleurum secundum is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
