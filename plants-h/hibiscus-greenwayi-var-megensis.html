Hibiscus greenwayi var. megensis

Hibiscus greenwayi var. megensis, commonly known as the Malagasy hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Hibiscus greenwayi var. megensis belongs to the Malvaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
