Hypericum aegypticum var. maroccanum

Hypericum aegypticum var. maroccanum, commonly known as the Moroccan St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers with prominent orange stamens. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and hardy addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
