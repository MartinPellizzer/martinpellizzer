Hedyotis resupinata

Hedyotis resupinata, commonly known as the prostrate lady's thumb, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to various regions of Asia.

It typically features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often along the stems. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Hedyotis resupinata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
