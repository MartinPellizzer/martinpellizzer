Hymenophyllum venustum

Hymenophyllum venustum, commonly known as the silver moss, is a small, delicate fern belonging to the Hymenophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are usually only a few centimeters long, and they are often clustered closely together.

Hymenophyllum venustum is known for its ability to retain moisture, which helps it survive in humid, low-light conditions.
