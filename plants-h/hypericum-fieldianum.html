Hypericum fieldianum

Hypericum fieldianum, commonly known as the field St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with prominent orange stamens, which bloom from June to August. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum fieldianum is often found in dry, sunny grasslands and rocky soils.
