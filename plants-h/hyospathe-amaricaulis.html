Hyospathe amaricaulis

Hyospathe amaricaulis, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. Its flowers are tubular and often have a distinctive red or orange hue with a yellow throat.

Hyospathe amaricaulis thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
