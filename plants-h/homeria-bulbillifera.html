Homeria bulbillifera

Homeria bulbillifera, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that resemble snowballs, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable species in the Acanthaceae family.
