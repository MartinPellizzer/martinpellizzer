Heliocereus speciosus var. superbus

Heliocereus speciosus var. superbus, commonly known as the sunset night bloomer, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems and large, showy flowers that open at night. The flowers are typically pale yellow to orange in color, with a distinctive red or pink throat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

This plant is notable for its nocturnal blooming habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
