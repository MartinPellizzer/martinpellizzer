Hibiscus rosa-sinensis var. kermesinus

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis var. kermesinus, commonly known as the Kermesino hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically deep green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
