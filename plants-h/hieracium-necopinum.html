Hieracium necopinum

Hieracium necopinum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange-red flowers, which are typically clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has basal and sometimes upright leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Hieracium necopinum is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
