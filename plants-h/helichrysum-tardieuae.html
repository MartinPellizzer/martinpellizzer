Helichrysum tardieuae

Helichrysum tardieuae, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, papery bracts that resemble petals, giving it a striking appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Helichrysum tardieuae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
