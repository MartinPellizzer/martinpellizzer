Hahnia × hostii var. ambigua

Hahnia × hostii var. ambigua, commonly known as the hybrid hahnia, is a rare and intriguing plant that combines traits from its parent species.

It is a succulent with thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the spring and early summer. It is native to arid regions and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by collectors and botanists alike.
