Hypericum uliginosum var. pratense

Hypericum uliginosum var. pratense, commonly known as swamp St. John's wort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

It features bright yellow flowers with prominent orange stamens, typically blooming from June to August. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in bogs, marshes, and along stream banks.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
