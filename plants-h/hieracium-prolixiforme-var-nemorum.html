Hieracium prolixiforme var. nemorum

Hieracium prolixiforme var. nemorum, commonly known as the wood hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and somewhat woody at the base.

It is considered a native species in parts of Europe and is often found in forested areas with rich, damp soil.
