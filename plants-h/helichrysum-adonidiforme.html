Helichrysum adonidiforme

Helichrysum adonidiforme, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Helichrysum adonidiforme is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
