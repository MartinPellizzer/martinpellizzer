Hyptis asteroides

Hyptis asteroides, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Hyptis asteroides is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
