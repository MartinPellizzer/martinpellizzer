Hieracium Luescheri

Hieracium luescheri is a rare and fascinating plant species belonging to the dandelion family. It is native to specific regions in Europe and is known for its distinctive yellow flowers and hairy leaves. This plant thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in shaded woodland areas. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival. Its unique characteristics make it an important subject for botanical study and ecological research.
