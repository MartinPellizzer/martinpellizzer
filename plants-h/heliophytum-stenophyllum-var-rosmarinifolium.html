Heliophytum stenophyllum var. rosmarinifolium

Heliophytum stenophyllum var. rosmarinifolium, commonly known as the rosemary-leaved heliophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-forming growth with narrow, aromatic leaves that resemble rosemary. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
