Heliconia schumanniana var. basirubra

Heliconia schumanniana var. basirubra, commonly known as the red-flowered heliconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, boat-shaped inflorescences that are predominantly red in color, with some varieties displaying orange or yellow hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers surrounded by brightly colored bracts, which are often mistaken for petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along riverbanks.

Heliconia schumanniana var. basirubra is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for decorative purposes.
