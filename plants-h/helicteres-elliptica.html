Helicteres elliptica

Helicteres elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic helicteres, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive, fern-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a spiny capsule that contains several seeds.

Helicteres elliptica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
