Haworthiopsis longiana

Haworthiopsis longiana, commonly known as the Longleaf Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray in color with white markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Haworthiopsis longiana is prized for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
