Himantoglossum cucullatum

Himantoglossum cucullatum, commonly known as the hooded orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Europe.

It is characterized by its distinctive hood-shaped labellum, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in calcareous grasslands and open woodlands, preferring sunny to partially shaded conditions. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a single row of petals.

Himantoglossum cucullatum is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
