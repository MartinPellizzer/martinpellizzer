Hieracium domingense

Hieracium domingense, commonly known as the Dominican hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly the Dominican Republic.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Hieracium domingense is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
