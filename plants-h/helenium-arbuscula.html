Helenium arbuscula

Helenium arbuscula, commonly known as the narrowleaf helenium, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy orange to red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms from late summer into early autumn.

Helenium arbuscula is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
