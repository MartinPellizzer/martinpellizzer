Hypericum laxiusculum

Hypericum laxiusculum, commonly known as the lax flowered St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring woody stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Hypericum laxiusculum is also noted for its medicinal properties, similar to other members of the Hypericaceae family.
