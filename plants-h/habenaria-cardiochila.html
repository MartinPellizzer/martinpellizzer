Habenaria cardiochila

Habenaria cardiochila, commonly known as the heart-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats, often near the ground. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with intricate patterns, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.

Habenaria cardiochila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
