Homalomena lambirensis

Homalomena lambirensis, commonly known as the Lambirensis arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spadices, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Homalomena lambirensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
