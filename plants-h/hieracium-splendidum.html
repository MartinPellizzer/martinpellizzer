Hieracium splendidum

Hieracium splendidum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

Hieracium splendidum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate regions.
