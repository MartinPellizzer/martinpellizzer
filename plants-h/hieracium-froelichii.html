Hieracium froelichii

Hieracium froelichii, commonly known as the Froelich's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium froelichii is often found in temperate climates and can be a persistent weed in certain environments.
