Hovea grandiflora

Hovea grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered hovea, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in coastal regions and sandy soils. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hovea grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
