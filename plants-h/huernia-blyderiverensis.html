Huernia blyderiverensis

Huernia blyderiverensis, commonly known as the Blyderivier horned cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its swollen, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months. Its unique appearance, with spines and a compact growth habit, makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
