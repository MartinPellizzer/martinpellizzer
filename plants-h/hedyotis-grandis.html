Hedyotis grandis

Hedyotis grandis, commonly known as the large blushing geranium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, pink to red flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
