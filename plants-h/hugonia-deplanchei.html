Hugonia deplanchei

Hugonia deplanchei, commonly known as the rock pear, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal regions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense canopy.

Hugonia deplanchei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
