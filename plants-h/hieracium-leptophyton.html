Hieracium leptophyton

Hieracium leptophyton, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium leptophyton is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some habitats.
