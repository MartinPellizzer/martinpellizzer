Hieracium tanaodeirum

Hieracium tanaodeirum, commonly known as the Tanao dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium tanaodeirum is often found in disturbed areas and can spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
