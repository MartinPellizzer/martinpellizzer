Haworthia reinwardtii var. zebrina

Haworthia reinwardtii var. zebrina, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for succulent gardens.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
