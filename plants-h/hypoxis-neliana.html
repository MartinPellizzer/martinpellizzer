Hypoxis neliana

Hypoxis neliana, commonly known as the Natal star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hypoxis neliana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
