Hemipogon setaceus

Hemipogon setaceus, commonly known as the featherleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hemipogon setaceus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
