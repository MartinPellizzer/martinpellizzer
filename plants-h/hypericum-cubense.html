Hypericum cubense

Hypericum cubense, commonly known as the Cuban St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, typical of the Hypericaceae family. The plant produces small, orange-yellow berries that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in open, sunny areas.

Its leaves are opposite and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
