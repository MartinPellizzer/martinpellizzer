Hoya blashernaezii subsp. truncata

Hoya blashernaezii subsp.

truncata, commonly known as the Truncated Wax Plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asclepiadaceae. It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand and Malaysia.

The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

This subspecies is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive appearance.
