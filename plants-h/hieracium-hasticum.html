Hieracium hasticum

Hieracium hasticum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

Hieracium hasticum is an annual or biennial plant, and it produces seeds in small, dry fruit called a cypsela.
