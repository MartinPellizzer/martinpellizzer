Helorchis Filiformis

Helorchis filiformis is a rare and delicate orchid native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. This plant is known for its slender, thread-like leaves and striking, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Due to its limited habitat and endangered status, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival. Despite its small size, Helorchis filiformis holds significant botanical interest and beauty.
