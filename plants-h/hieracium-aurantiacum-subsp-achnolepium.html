Hieracium aurantiacum subsp. achnolepium

Hieracium aurantiacum subsp.

achnolepium, commonly known as the orange hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright orange to yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branching.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators and is sometimes considered invasive in certain regions.
