Hieracium sabaudum subsp. roffavieri

Hieracium sabaudum subsp.

roffavieri, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has finely divided leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

This subspecies is often found in mountainous areas and is adapted to harsh, cold environments.
