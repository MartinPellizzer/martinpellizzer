Horsfieldia obscura

Horsfieldia obscura, commonly known as the dark horsfieldia, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.

The botanical characteristics of Horsfieldia obscura make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
