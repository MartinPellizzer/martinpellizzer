Hieracium levicaule subsp. ioranthum

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

ioranthum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. It has a taproot system and is often found in sunny, well-drained environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common weed in agricultural and urban areas.
