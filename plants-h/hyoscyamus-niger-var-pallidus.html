Hyoscyamus niger var. pallidus

Hyoscyamus niger var. pallidus, commonly known as the pale black henbane, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is characterized by its pale green to yellowish leaves and small, pale purple to white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is considered a noxious weed in some areas.
