Haworthia aquamarina

Haworthia aquamarina, commonly known as the blue haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
