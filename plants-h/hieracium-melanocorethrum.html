Hieracium melanocorethrum

Hieracium melanocorethrum, commonly known as the black-centred hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-yellow composite flowers. The plant typically has basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape, with rough, hairy surfaces. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its flowers are usually arranged in a single head, and it is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
