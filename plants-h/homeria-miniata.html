Homeria miniata

Homeria miniata, commonly known as the miniature homeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is widely cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
