Haemanthus albomaculatus

Haemanthus albomaculatus, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky flower spikes that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features sword-shaped, evergreen leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its dramatic appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant in many gardens.
