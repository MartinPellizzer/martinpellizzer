Hieracium divisum grex onosmotrichum

Hieracium divisum grex onosmotrichum, commonly known as the Onosmotrichum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant features a robust stem with rough, hairy surfaces and basal leaves that are deeply lobed. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn.
