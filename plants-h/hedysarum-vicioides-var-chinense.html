Hedysarum vicioides var. chinense

Hedysarum vicioides var. chinense, commonly known as Chinese vetch, is a perennial legume native to China and neighboring regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with stipules and produces small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in racemes. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It has small, stipitate pods that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and is used in ecological restoration and as a forage plant.
