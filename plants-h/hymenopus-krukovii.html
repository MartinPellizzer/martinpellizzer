Hymenopus krukovii

Hymenopus krukovii, commonly known as the Krukovii's lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Central Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive, elongated corolla. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. Hymenopus krukovii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous or semi-arid regions.
