Hibiscus syriacus var. amplissimus

Hibiscus syriacus var. amplissimus, commonly known as the large-flowered shrub hibiscus, is a deciduous shrub native to Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows to a height of 1.5 to 3 meters and features heart-shaped, dark green leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant blooms.
