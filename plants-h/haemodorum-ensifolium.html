Haemodorum ensifolium

Haemodorum ensifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hellbender, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Haemodoraceae, which includes several species of grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and wetlands.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened in some regions.
