Haworthia emelyae var. major

Haworthia emelyae var. major, commonly known as the giant Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
