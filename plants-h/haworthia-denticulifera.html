Haworthia denticulifera

Haworthia denticulifera, commonly known as the serrated Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with distinctive serrated edges. The plant produces small, bell-shaped white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
