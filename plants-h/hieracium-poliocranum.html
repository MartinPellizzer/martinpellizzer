Hieracium poliocranum

Hieracium poliocranum, commonly known as the purplehead hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and clusters of small, yellow to orange flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium poliocranum is an annual or biennial species that plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
