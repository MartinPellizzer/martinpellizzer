Haworthia odetteae var. odyssei

Haworthia odetteae var. odyssei, commonly known as the Odyssean Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy rosettes of dark green leaves with white or pale blue markings, giving it a striking, almost otherworldly appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a prized species among succulent enthusiasts.
