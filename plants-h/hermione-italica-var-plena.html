Hermione italica var. plena

Hermione italica var. plena, commonly known as the double-flowered hyacinth, is a cultivar of the hyacinth plant known for its dense, clustered blooms.

It features showy, double flowers with numerous petals, creating a lush, ornamental appearance. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its vibrant colors and fragrant scent.

This variety is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
