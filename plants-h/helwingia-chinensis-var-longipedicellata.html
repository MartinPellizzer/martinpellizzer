Helwingia chinensis var. longipedicellata

Helwingia chinensis var. longipedicellata, commonly known as the long pedicellate helwingia, is a rare and ornamental shrub native to China.

It features small, clustered inflorescences with showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
