Hieracium rarovelutum

Hieracium rarovelutum, commonly known as the rare velvet hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
