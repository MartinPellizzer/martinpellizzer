Hansteinia blepharorhachis

Hansteinia blepharorhachis, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery bloom. The plant produces small, white, spider-like flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hansteinia blepharorhachis is a popular houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
