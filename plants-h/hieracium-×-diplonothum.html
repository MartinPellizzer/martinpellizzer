Hieracium × diplonothum

Hieracium × diplonothum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It has a creeping growth habit with stolons that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

Hieracium × diplonothum is often found in temperate regions of Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.
