Habenaria epipactidea var. schinzii

Habenaria epipactidea var. schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's marsh orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in wetlands or along the edges of ponds. Its botanical features include long, narrow leaves and a spurred column typical of the Orchidaceae family.

It is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
