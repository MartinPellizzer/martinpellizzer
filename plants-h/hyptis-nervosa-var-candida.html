Hyptis nervosa var. candida

Hyptis nervosa var. candida, commonly known as white hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green leaves and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Hyptis nervosa var. candida is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
