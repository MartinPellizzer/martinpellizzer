Hydrolea spinosa f. latifolia

Hydrolea spinosa f. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf hydrolea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are broader than those of the typical species, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
