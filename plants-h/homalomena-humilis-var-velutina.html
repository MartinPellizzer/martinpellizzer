Homalomena humilis var. velutina

Homalomena humilis var. velutina, commonly known as velvet arrowhead, is a tropical perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are often followed by red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is popular in indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
