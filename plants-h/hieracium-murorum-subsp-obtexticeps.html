Hieracium murorum subsp. obtexticeps

Hieracium murorum subsp.

obtexticeps, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in grasslands and disturbed soils across Europe.

The plant features basal and alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

The subspecies obtexticeps is distinguished by its hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its adaptability in various environmental conditions.
