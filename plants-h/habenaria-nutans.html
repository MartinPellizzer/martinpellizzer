Habenaria nutans

Habenaria nutans, commonly known as the nodding orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow with purple streaks, and they bloom in clusters at the top of the stem.

Habenaria nutans is characterized by its unique inflorescence and is often found in rocky or clayey soils.
