Heliotropium ulophyllum

Heliotropium ulophyllum, commonly known as the narrowleaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, blooming throughout the warm seasons. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, often with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in coastal areas due to its tolerance for salty environments.
