Hieracium fallax subsp. mollisetum

Hieracium fallax subsp.

mollisetum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
