Helianthemum guttatum subsp. praecox

Helianthemum guttatum subsp.

praecox, commonly known as the early yellow rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May.

The plant has aromatic, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to rocky or coastal environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
