Homalomena gigantea

Homalomena gigantea, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence structure consisting of a spadix and spathe. Its foliage is often variegated with bright yellow and green patterns, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.
