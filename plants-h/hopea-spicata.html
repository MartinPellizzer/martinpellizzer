Hopea spicata, commonly known as the spiky hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Malaysia, Indonesia, and Thailand. Hopea spicata is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland tropical rainforests.
