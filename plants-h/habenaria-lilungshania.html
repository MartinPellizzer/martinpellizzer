Habenaria lilungshania

Habenaria lilungshania, commonly known as the Lilungshan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually small and arranged in a loose raceme, with three sepals and three petals.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
