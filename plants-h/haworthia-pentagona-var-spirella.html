Haworthia pentagona var. spirella

Haworthia pentagona var. spirella, commonly known as the Spire Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green with white or pale markings. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in arid environments. It produces tall, spiky inflorescences with white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
