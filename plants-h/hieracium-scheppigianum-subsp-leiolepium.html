Hieracium scheppigianum subsp. leiolepium

Hieracium scheppigianum subsp.

leiolepium, commonly known as the pale-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

The leaves are typically lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while the stems are upright and often hairy. This subspecies is distinguished by its pale green leaves and reduced leaf hairs compared to other members of the species.

It thrives in moist meadows and grasslands, often in areas with calcareous soils.
