Haworthia marumiana var. marumiana

Haworthia marumiana var. marumiana, commonly known as the Marumiana Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings, forming a rosette shape. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
