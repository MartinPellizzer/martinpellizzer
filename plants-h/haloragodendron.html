Haloragodendron

Haloragodendron, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Haloragaceae.

It is native to New Zealand and consists of several species, including Haloragodendron australianum. These plants are characterized by their silver-gray leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that are often pink or white.
