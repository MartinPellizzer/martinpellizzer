Helianthemum croceum var. virens

Helianthemum croceum var. virens, commonly known as the green-flowered rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, upright stems covered in dense, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun exposure.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping.
