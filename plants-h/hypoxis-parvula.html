Hypoxis parvula

Hypoxis parvula, commonly known as the small yellow star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

Hypoxis parvula is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
