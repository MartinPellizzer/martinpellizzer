Helianthemum Estevei

Helianthemum estevei is a rare and unique plant native to the rocky landscapes of southern Portugal. This species belongs to the Cistaceae family, known for its hardy and drought-resistant characteristics. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in late spring, adding a touch of brightness to its arid environment. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it an ideal candidate for xeriscaping. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, Helianthemum estevei is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
