Halerpestes cymbalaria

Halerpestes cymbalaria, commonly known as the common senna, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both ornamental and medicinal horticulture.
