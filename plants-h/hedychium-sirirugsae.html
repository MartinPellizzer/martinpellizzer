Hedychium sirirugsae

Hedychium sirirugsae, commonly known as the Thai ginger lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, fragrant, white to cream-colored flowers. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by broad, waxy bracts. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hedychium sirirugsae is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
