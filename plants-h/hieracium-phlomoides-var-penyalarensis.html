Hieracium phlomoides var. penyalarensis

Hieracium phlomoides var. penyalarensis, commonly known as the Penysalarensis hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture, and its yellow flowers bloom in summer.
