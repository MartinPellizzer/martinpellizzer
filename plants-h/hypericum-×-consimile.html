Hypericum × consimile

Hypericum × consimile, commonly known as the hybrid St. John's wort, is a shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features woody stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been historically used in herbal remedies.
