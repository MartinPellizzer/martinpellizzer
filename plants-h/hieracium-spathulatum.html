Hieracium spathulatum

Hieracium spathulatum, commonly known as the spoon-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of spoon-shaped, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

It is a perennial species with a robust root system that helps it survive in various environmental conditions.
