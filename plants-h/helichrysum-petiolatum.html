Helichrysum petiolatum

Helichrysum petiolatum, commonly known as the golden ragwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum petiolatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
