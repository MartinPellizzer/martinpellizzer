Hieracium deltoideum

Hieracium deltoideum, commonly known as the deltoid hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers consist of yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Hieracium deltoideum is often considered a weed but plays a role in supporting pollinators and soil stabilization.
