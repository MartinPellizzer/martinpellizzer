Hieracium commersonii f. subefloccosum

Hieracium commersonii f. subefloccosum, commonly known as the subefloccosum hawkweed, is a variant of the common hawkweed species.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of disk florets. It has hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its distinctive appearance.

This form is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows.
