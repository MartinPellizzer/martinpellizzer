Hieracium protenozum

Hieracium protenozum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, and its leaves are lance-shaped with hairy surfaces. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Hieracium protenozum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in many regions.
