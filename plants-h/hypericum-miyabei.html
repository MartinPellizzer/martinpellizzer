Hypericum miyabei

Hypericum miyabei, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering shrubs. The plant features yellow flowers with orange centers, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly aromatic scent.

Hypericum miyabei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
