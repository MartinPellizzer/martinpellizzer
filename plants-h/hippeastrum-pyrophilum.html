Hippeastrum pyrophilum

Hippeastrum pyrophilum, commonly known as the flame lily, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a fiery appearance. The plant produces large, fleshy leaves that are typically broad and strap-like. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hippeastrum pyrophilum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
