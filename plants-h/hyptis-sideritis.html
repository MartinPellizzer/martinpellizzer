Hyptis sideritis

Hyptis sideritis, commonly known as ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, gray-green leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Hyptis sideritis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional use in herbal medicine.
