Hieracium trebevicianum subsp. jablanense

Hieracium trebevicianum subsp.

jablanense, commonly known as the Jablanec hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has basal and stem leaves that are lance-shaped with hairy surfaces.

The subspecies is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and morphological traits.
