Hellenia

Hellenia, commonly known as the golden saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and is native to regions of Europe and Asia. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Hellenia has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in rocky or gravelly soils.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
