Hieracium caucasiense subsp. acutangulum

Hieracium caucasiense subsp.

acutangulum, commonly known as the Caucasus hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and daisy-like yellow flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its acute, sharply pointed leaf margins and bracts. It typically grows in mountainous areas and is adapted to cooler, temperate climates.
