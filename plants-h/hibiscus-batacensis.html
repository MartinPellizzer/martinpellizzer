Hibiscus batacensis

Hibiscus batacensis, commonly known as the Batac hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and grows as an annual herb, often found in moist, tropical environments. Hibiscus batacensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its flowers are known to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
