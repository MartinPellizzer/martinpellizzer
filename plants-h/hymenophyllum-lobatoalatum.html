Hymenophyllum lobatoalatum

Hymenophyllum lobatoalatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lady fern, is a small, epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant's fronds are typically less than 30 cm in length and exhibit a distinctive lobed structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and habitat make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and botanical collections.
