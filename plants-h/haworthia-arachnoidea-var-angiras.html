Haworthia arachnoidea var. angiras

Haworthia arachnoidea var. angiras, commonly known as the spider haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The leaves are typically a bluish-green color with prominent white tubercles that resemble spider legs, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
