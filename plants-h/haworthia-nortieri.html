Haworthia nortieri

Haworthia nortieri, commonly known as the Nortier's Haworthia, is a compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white markings and a distinctive, spiky crown of bracts. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on tall, slender stalks during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
