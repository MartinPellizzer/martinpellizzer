Hibiscus populneoides

Hibiscus populneoides, commonly known as the African rose or yellow hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Hibiscus populneoides is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
