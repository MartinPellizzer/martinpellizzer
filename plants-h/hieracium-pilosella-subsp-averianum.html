Hieracium pilosella subsp. averianum

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

averianum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish flowers.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

This subspecies is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered a minor weed in some regions.
