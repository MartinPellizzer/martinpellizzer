Hieracium laeticolor var. sublaeticolor

Hieracium laeticolor var. sublaeticolor, commonly known as the golden hawkweed, is a variant of the common hawkweed species.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its yellowish to orange-colored flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

The inflorescence consists of ray florets that are usually yellow, giving the plant its distinctive appearance.
