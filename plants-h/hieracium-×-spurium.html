Hieracium × spurium

Hieracium × spurium, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a hybrid plant species within the dandelion family (Compositae).

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, often reddish stems. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open areas, such as roadsides and meadows. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are usually lobed or toothed.

Hieracium × spurium is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it an invasive species in some regions.
