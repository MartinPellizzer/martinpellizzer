Hieracium cernagorae subsp. cernagorae

Hieracium cernagorae subsp.

cernagorae, commonly known as the Cernagora hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
