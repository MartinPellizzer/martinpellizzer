Helixanthera Periclymenoides

Helixanthera periclymenoides is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the mallow family and is known for its vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring. this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. its unique spiral-shaped flowers make it a standout in any garden or natural habitat. due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
