Hippeastrum illustre

Hippeastrum illustre, commonly known as the red lily or Amazon lily, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and fleshy. It is a bulbous plant that thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Hippeastrum illustre is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.
