Hypoxis decumbens var. dolichocarpa

Hypoxis decumbens var. dolichocarpa, commonly known as the long-stemmed false goldstar, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is often grown in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
