Hieracium hololeion

Hieracium hololeion, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Hieracium hololeion is an invasive species in some regions, outcompeting native plants and altering local ecosystems.
