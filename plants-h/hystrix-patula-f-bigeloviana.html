Hystrix patula f. bigeloviana

Hystrix patula f. bigeloviana, commonly known as the bigelovian hedgehog cactus, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem covered in dense, spiny areoles, which are characteristic of the Cactaceae family. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its spiky structure. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it stores water in its thick, fleshy stems for survival in arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and adaptability to dry climates.
