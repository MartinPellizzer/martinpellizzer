Haworthia enigma

Haworthia enigma, commonly known as the enigma haworthia, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact size and distinctive appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
