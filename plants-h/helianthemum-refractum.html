Helianthemum refractum

Helianthemum refractum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

Helianthemum refractum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
