Hieracium attenboroughianum

Hieracium attenboroughianum, commonly known as Attenborough's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats and is often found in disturbed soils. It is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of around 10 to 30 centimeters.
