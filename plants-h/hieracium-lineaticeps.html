Hieracium lineaticeps

Hieracium lineaticeps, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while the flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat, composite head.

Hieracium lineaticeps is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
