Homalomena purpurascens

Homalomena purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-leafed arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy, deep green leaves with a distinctive purple underside. The plant produces striking, spathe-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a prominent spadix. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Homalomena purpurascens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive indoors.
