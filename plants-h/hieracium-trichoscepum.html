Hieracium trichoscepum

Hieracium trichoscepum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which give it its common name. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas. The plant has alternate leaves that are often hairy and can be lobed or toothed.

It is a perennial species that produces achenes as its seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
