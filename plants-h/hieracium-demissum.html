Hieracium demissum

Hieracium demissum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the dandelion family (Compositae).

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, with a central stem that bears several yellow composite flowers. Its flowers consist of both ray and disk florets, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Hieracium demissum is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.
