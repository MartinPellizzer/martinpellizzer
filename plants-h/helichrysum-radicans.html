Helichrysum radicans

Helichrysum radicans, commonly known as African daisy or gold dust, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features wiry stems and small, bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant has small, serrated leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Helichrysum radicans is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
