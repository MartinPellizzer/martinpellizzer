Hieracium nigrolegionense

Hieracium nigrolegionense, commonly known as the Black Legion Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is known for its aggressive growth and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
