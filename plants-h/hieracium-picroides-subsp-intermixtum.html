Hieracium picroides subsp. intermixtum

Hieracium picroides subsp.

intermixtum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

It produces small, dark seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread in various habitats.
