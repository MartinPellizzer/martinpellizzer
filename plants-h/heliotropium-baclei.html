Heliotropium baclei

Heliotropium baclei, commonly known as the Bacle's heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, tropical environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually violet or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Heliotropium baclei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual and aromatic qualities.
