Homeria schlechteri

Homeria schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's homeria, is a flowering plant native to the Eastern Cape of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It prefers well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
