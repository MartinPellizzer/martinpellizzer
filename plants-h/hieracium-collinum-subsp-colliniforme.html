Hieracium collinum subsp. colliniforme

Hieracium collinum subsp.

colliniforme, commonly known as the mountain hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It features basal rosettes of lanceolate leaves and produces yellow disc flowers surrounded by ray florets.

Its flowering period occurs in late summer to early autumn, contributing to its ecological role in pollinator support.
