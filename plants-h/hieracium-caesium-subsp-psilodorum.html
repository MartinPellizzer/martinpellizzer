Hieracium caesium subsp. psilodorum

Hieracium caesium subsp.

psilodorum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and herbaceous growth habit.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, and its leaves are generally narrow and lance-shaped.

This subspecies is distinguished by its shorter stature and reduced flower heads compared to other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
