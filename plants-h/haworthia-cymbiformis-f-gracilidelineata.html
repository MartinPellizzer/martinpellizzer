Haworthia cymbiformis f. gracilidelineata

Haworthia cymbiformis f. gracilidelineata, commonly known as the slender-line Haworthia, is a compact, dwarf succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically a pale green to bluish-green color, often with distinct white or pale markings. The leaves are arranged in a tight spiral, giving the plant a compact and elegant appearance. It produces small, bell-shaped white or pink flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance and unique aesthetic.
