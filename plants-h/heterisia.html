Heterisia

Heterisia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, often hairy leaves and showy, typically white or yellow flowers. The plant is native to various regions in Europe, Asia, and Africa, and it thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Heterisia species are often found in rocky soils and can be either annual or perennial.

Some species are known for their toxic properties, containing alkaloids that can be harmful to humans and animals.
