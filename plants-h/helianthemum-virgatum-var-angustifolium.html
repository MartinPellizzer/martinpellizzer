Helianthemum virgatum var. angustifolium

Helianthemum virgatum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal areas and dry, sunny slopes.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
