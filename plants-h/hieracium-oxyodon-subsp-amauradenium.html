Hieracium oxyodon subsp. amauradenium

Hieracium oxyodon subsp.

amauradenium, commonly known as the golden hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves.

Native to southern Europe, it thrives in dry, sunny environments such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant produces small, elongated seeds that are dispersed by wind.
