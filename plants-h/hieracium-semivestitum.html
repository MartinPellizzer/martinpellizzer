Hieracium semivestitum

Hieracium semivestitum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and grassy areas. The plant is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and has a low-growing habit.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its persistent nature.
