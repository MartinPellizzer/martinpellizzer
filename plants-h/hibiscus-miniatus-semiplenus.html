Hibiscus miniatus-semiplenus

Hibiscus miniatus-semiplenus, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink flowers, which bloom throughout the year in tropical climates. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Hibiscus miniatus-semiplenus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Hawaiian medicine.
