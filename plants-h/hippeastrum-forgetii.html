Hippeastrum forgetii

Hippeastrum forgetii, commonly known as the Congo lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
