Hieracium denticuliferum

Hieracium denticuliferum, commonly known as the toothed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically growing in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are characterized by their serrated edges. It is often found in sunny, open habitats and can form dense colonies.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
