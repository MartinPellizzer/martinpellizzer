Habenaria magnifica

Habenaria magnifica, commonly known as the splendid orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate, lip-shaped petals that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The botanical features of Habenaria magnifica make it a prized ornamental plant among orchid enthusiasts.
