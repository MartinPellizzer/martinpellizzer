Helianthemum × sulphureum var. ochroleucum

Helianthemum × sulphureum var. ochroleucum, commonly known as the sulphur yellow rockrose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, upright growth with small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators.
