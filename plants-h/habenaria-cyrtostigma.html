Habenaria cyrtostigma

Habenaria cyrtostigma, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

The species is noted for its small, fragrant blooms that appear on slender stems, making it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
