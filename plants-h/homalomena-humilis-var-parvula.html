Homalomena humilis var. parvula

Homalomena humilis var. parvula, commonly known as the dwarf arrowhead plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly variegated appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, fertile soil.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
