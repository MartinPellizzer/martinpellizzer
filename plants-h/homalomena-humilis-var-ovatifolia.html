Homalomena humilis var. ovatifolia

Homalomena humilis var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elephant's ear, is a tropical evergreen herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a smooth texture. The plant produces striking, spurred flowers that are usually yellow or orange, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
