Hypericum guianense

Hypericum guianense, commonly known as the Guianese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Hypericum guianense is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
