Hieracium scorzonerifolium subsp. schizocladum

Hieracium scorzonerifolium subsp.

schizocladum, commonly known as the schizocladum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a distinctive serrated edge, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

It is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
