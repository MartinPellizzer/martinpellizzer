Halecus verus

Halecus verus, commonly known as the common halecus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small, clustered white or yellowish flowers. It plays a role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
