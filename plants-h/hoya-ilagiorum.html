Hoya ilagiorum

Hoya ilagiorum, commonly known as the "Cordate Hoya," is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is a vine that can grow up to several meters in length, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trained on trellises. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hoya ilagiorum is popular among plant enthusiasts for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
