Hedyotis tomentosa

Hedyotis tomentosa, commonly known as the hairy shieldflower, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine, soft hairs.

Hedyotis tomentosa is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
