Haworthia submaculata

Haworthia submaculata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or pale markings. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in arid environments. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
