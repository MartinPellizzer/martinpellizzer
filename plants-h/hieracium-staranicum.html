Hieracium staranicum

Hieracium staranicum, commonly known as the Star-nosed Hairy Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are typically hairy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
