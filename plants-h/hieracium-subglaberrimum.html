Hieracium subglaberrimum

Hieracium subglaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring moist, well-drained soils. It has slender stems with lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.

Its flowers are yellow and form a flat-topped inflorescence, blooming from late spring to early summer.
