Hemiboea longisepala

Hemiboea longisepala, commonly known as the long-sepaled hemiboea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. Hemiboea longisepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
