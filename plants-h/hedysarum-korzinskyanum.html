Hedysarum korzinskyanum

Hedysarum korzinskyanum, commonly known as the Korzinsky's milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Hedysarum korzinskyanum plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for ecological restoration projects.
