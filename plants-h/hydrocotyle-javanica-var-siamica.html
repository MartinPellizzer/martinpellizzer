Hydrocotyle javanica var. siamica

Hydrocotyle javanica var. siamica, commonly known as the Siam water pennywort, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, floating leaves that are typically rounded or heart-shaped. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and marshes. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental water gardens.

It plays a role in water purification and provides habitat for aquatic invertebrates.
