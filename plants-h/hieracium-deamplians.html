Hieracium deamplians

Hieracium deamplians, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early autumn. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe, it is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
