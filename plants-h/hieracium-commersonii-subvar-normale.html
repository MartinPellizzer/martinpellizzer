Hieracium commersonii subvar. normale

Hieracium commersonii subvar. normale, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. The plant has a hairy stem and lance-shaped leaves with toothed margins. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Its flowers are typically clustered in a single inflorescence at the top of the stem.
