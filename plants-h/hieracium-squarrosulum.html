Hieracium squarrosulum

Hieracium squarrosulum, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. Its leaves are covered in dense, white, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
