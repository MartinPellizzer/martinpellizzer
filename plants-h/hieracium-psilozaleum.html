Hieracium psilozaleum

Hieracium psilozaleum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers in composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish underneath.

Hieracium psilozaleum is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in some areas.
