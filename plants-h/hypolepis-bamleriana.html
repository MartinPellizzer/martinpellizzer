Hypolepis bamleriana

Hypolepis bamleriana, commonly known as the bamboo fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender fronds that resemble bamboo in appearance, hence its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with a distinctive, elongated shape.

Hypolepis bamleriana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to humid, shady environments.
