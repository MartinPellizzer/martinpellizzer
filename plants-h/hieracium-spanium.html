Hieracium spanium

Hieracium spanium, commonly known as the Spanish hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium spanium is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in many regions.
