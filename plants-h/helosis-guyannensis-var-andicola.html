Helosis guyannensis var. andicola

Helosis guyannensis var. andicola, commonly known as the Guyana helosis, is a rare and unique plant species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spiral-shaped leaves that form a compact, rosette-like structure. The plant thrives in nutrient-poor, well-drained soils, typically found in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage exhibits a vibrant green coloration with subtle variegation, enhancing its ornamental appeal.
