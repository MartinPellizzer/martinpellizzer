Hieracium vacillans

Hieracium vacillans, commonly known as the wavering hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, and its leaves are usually hairy and alternate along the stem. It is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates.

Its common name reflects the way its flowers may appear to sway or waver in the wind.
