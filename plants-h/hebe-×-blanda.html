Hebe × blanda

Hebe × blanda, commonly known as the common hebe, is a hybrid plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, typically in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.

Hebe × blanda is valued for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
