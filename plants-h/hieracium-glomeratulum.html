Hieracium glomeratulum

Hieracium glomeratulum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in dense, compact clusters, giving it its common name.

Hieracium glomeratulum is often found in temperate climates and can sometimes be invasive in certain regions.
