Hieracium valdepilosum subsp. elongatum

Hieracium valdepilosum subsp.

elongatum, commonly known as the elongated hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its flowers are yellow and are arranged in compact clusters, typical of the genus Hieracium.
