Hieracium schultesii subsp. poliomelanoides

Hieracium schultesii subsp.

poliomelanoides, commonly known as the pale-leaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching a height of up to 30 cm.

The plant features basal and cauline leaves that are lanceolate to ovate, with a rough, hairy texture. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a composite head, and are borne on long, slender peduncles.

The species is characterized by its pale greenish-yellow flower heads and hairy, often glandular leaves.
