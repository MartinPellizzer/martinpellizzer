Hieracium holoteles

Hieracium holoteles, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. Native to Europe, it thrives in meadows and grasslands.

It is an annual plant that blooms from late spring to early summer.
