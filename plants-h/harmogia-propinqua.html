Harmogia propinqua

Harmogia propinqua, commonly known as the slender harmogia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Harmogia propinqua is often found in dry, coastal areas and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
