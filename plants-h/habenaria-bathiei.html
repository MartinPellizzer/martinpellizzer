Habenaria bathiei

Habenaria bathiei, commonly known as the Bathie's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizome is fibrous, and it produces a single, upright stem with a few leaves.

The flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
