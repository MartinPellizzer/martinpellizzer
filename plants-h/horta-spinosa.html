Horta spinosa

Horta spinosa, commonly known as spiny horta, is a hardy perennial plant native to temperate regions.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, prickly hairs. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated edge. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers in clusters during the spring.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
