Homalomena ridleyana

Homalomena ridleyana, commonly known as the Ridley's arrowroot, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
