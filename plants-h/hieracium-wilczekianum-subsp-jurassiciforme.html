Hieracium wilczekianum subsp. jurassiciforme

Hieracium wilczekianum subsp.

jurassiciforme, commonly known as the Jurassic dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosette of serrated leaves.

The plant is native to specific regions in Central Europe and thrives in moist, calcareous soils. It produces wind-dispersed seeds, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
