Habenaria kjellbergii

Habenaria kjellbergii, commonly known as the Kjellberg's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with three sepals and three petals, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
