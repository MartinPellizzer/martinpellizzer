Hydrangea serrata f. rosea

Hydrangea serrata f. rosea, commonly known as the pink-leafed hydrangea, is a cultivar of the hydrangea plant characterized by its distinctive pink foliage.

It belongs to the Hydrangeaceae family and is native to Japan. The plant features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink depending on soil pH. It thrives in partial shade and requires moist, well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
