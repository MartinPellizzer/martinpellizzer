Haworthia altissima

Haworthia altissima, commonly known as the Tower Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks bearing small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
