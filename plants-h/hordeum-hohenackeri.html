Hordeum hohenackeri

Hordeum hohenackeri, commonly known as Hohenacker's barley, is a rare grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical spikelets and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, wind-pollinated flowers and has a short growing season.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
