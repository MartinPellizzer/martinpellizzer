Hopea macrosepala

Hopea macrosepala, commonly known as the giant ironwood tree, is a large evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its thick, durable wood and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruits are woody capsules that split open to release seeds.
