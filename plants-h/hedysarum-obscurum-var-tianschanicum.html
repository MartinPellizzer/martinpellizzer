Hedysarum obscurum var. tianschanicum

Hedysarum obscurum var. tianschanicum, commonly known as the Tianshan hedysarum, is a perennial leguminous plant native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils and cool climates. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow to orange flowers in early summer. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in mountainous environments.
