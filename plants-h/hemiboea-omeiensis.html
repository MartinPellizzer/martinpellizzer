Hemiboea omeiensis

Hemiboea omeiensis, commonly known as the Omei mountain hemiboea, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. Hemiboea omeiensis thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with cool, humid climates.
