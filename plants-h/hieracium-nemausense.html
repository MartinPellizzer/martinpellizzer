Hieracium nemausense

Hieracium nemausense, commonly known as the Nemausense hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and grasslands. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Its yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom from late spring to early autumn.
