Habenaria nyikana

Habenaria nyikana, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often violet or purple-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
