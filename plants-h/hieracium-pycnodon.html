Hieracium pycnodon

Hieracium pycnodon, commonly known as the denseleaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium pycnodon produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
