Helichrysum abbayesii

Helichrysum abbayesii, commonly known as the Cape goldring, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even when dried. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, grey-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Helichrysum abbayesii is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental value.
