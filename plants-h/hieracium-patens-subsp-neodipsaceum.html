Hieracium patens subsp. neodipsaceum

Hieracium patens subsp.

neodipsaceum, commonly known as the slender meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems, with basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape.

The plant produces yellow flowers in composite inflorescences, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

This subspecies is often found in temperate regions of Europe and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
