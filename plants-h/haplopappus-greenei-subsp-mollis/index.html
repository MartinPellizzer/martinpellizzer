Haplopappus Greenei Subsp. Mollis

Haplopappus greenei subsp. mollis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States. This subspecies is known for its small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in desert scrub habitats. Due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this unique and ecologically important plant.
