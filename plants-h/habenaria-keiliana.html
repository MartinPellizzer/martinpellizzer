Habenaria keiliana

Habenaria keiliana, commonly known as the Keil's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, preferring rich, humus-laden soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a subtle fragrance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
