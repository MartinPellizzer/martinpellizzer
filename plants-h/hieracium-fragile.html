Hieracium fragile

Hieracium fragile, commonly known as the fragile hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through rhizomes or seeds.

Its botanical features make it a common yet easily identifiable species within its native range.
