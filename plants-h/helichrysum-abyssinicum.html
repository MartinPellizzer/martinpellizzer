Helichrysum abyssinicum

Helichrysum abyssinicum, commonly known as the Ethiopian everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow to orange-colored flowers that resemble daisies. The plant produces small, papery bracts that give it a distinctive, long-lasting appearance. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Helichrysum abyssinicum is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
