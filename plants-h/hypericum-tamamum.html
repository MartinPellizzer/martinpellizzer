Hypericum tamamum

Hypericum tamamum, commonly known as the yellow hypericum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas or along roadsides. Its foliage consists of opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are generally covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum tamamum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
