Hieracium sococratoideum

Hieracium sococratoideum, commonly known as the Sococrato's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in grasslands and disturbed soils, exhibiting a low-growing habit with hairy stems. Its yellow flowers bloom in summer, and the leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture.

It is often found in regions with temperate to subtropical climates.
