Hedysarum alpinum

Hedysarum alpinum, commonly known as the alpine vetch, is a perennial legume native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to violet. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

Hedysarum alpinum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.
