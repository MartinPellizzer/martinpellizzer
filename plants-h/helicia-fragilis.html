Helicia fragilis

Helicia fragilis, commonly known as the fragile helicia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and typically have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Helicia fragilis is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
