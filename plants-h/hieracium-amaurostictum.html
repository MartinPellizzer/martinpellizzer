Hieracium amaurostictum

Hieracium amaurostictum, commonly known as the dark-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features dark green, velvety leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

Hieracium amaurostictum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
