Hypericum galioides var. cubense

Hypericum galioides var. cubense, commonly known as the Cuban St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.
