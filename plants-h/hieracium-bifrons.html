Hieracium bifrons

Hieracium bifrons, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in a variety of habitats, including meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in temperate regions of Europe and North America.

Hieracium bifrons is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
