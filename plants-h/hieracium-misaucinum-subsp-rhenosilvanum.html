Hieracium misaucinum subsp. rhenosilvanum

Hieracium misaucinum subsp.

rhenosilvanum, commonly known as the Rhenish wood hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, lanceolate leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in calcareous soils.

This subspecies is characterized by its distinct floral morphology and ecological preferences.
