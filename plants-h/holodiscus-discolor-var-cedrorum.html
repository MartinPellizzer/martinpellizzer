Holodiscus discolor var. cedrorum

Holodiscus discolor var. cedrorum, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are typically ovate with a silvery underside, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

This variety is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
