Hohenbergia amargosensis

Hohenbergia amargosensis, commonly known as the Amargosense Hohenbergia, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
