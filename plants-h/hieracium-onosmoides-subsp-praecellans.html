Hieracium onosmoides subsp. praecellans

Hieracium onosmoides subsp.

praecellans, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow to orange composite flowers.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the stems are hairy and often branched. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

This subspecies is distinguished by its larger, more showy flowers compared to other members of the species.
