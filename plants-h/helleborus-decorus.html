Helleborus decorus

Helleborus decorus, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, lobed leaves that remain throughout the winter. The plant produces showy, white to pinkish flowers in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is known for its cold hardiness.

Helleborus decorus is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and ability to bloom during the holiday season.
