Hieracium gothiciferum

Hieracium gothiciferum, commonly known as the Gothic-horned hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are rough and hairy, with a distinctive heart-shaped base. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Hieracium gothiciferum is native to Europe and is often found in temperate regions.
