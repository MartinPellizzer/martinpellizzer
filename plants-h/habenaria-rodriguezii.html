Habenaria rodriguezii

Habenaria rodriguezii, commonly known as Rodriguez's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

This terrestrial orchid typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are characterized by their intricate structure, featuring a slender column and a single, showy lip. The plant produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
