Hieracium macilentum subsp. subvulsum

Hieracium macilentum subsp.

subvulsum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange flowers.

The leaves are lanceolate, with a rough texture and prominent veins. It typically thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

This subspecies is notable for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its potential as an indicator species for environmental change.
