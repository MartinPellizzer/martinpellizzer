Helichrysum argyrophyllum

Helichrysum argyrophyllum, commonly known as silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and it is often used in dried floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum argyrophyllum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
