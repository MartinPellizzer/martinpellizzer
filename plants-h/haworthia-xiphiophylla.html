Haworthia xiphiophylla

Haworthia xiphiophylla, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
