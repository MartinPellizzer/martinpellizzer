Hymenorebutia aurea var. depressicostata

Hymenorebutia aurea var. depressicostata, commonly known as the flattened-costate crown of thorns, is a succulent cactus native to Bolivia.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are ribbed and covered in pale green to yellowish spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
