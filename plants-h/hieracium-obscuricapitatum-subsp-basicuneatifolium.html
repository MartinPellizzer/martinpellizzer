Hieracium obscuricapitatum subsp. basicuneatifolium

Hieracium obscuricapitatum subsp.

basicuneatifolium, commonly known as the dark-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dark brown, elongated flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. Its leaves are deeply lobed and often have a hairy texture, while the stems are erect and somewhat branched.

It is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
