Hypobathrum salicinum

Hypobathrum salicinum, commonly known as the yellow sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It typically thrives in moist, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in tundra and coastal environments. The plant has small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and flies. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to retain water in harsh climates.

Hypobathrum salicinum plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various arctic organisms.
