Hymenopyramis pubescens

Hymenopyramis pubescens, commonly known as the hairy pyramidal orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive pyramidal-shaped inflorescence and hairy stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often blooming during the late summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
