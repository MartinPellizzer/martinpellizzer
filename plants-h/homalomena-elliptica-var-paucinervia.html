Homalomena elliptica var. paucinervia

Homalomena elliptica var. paucinervia, commonly known as the narrow-veined caladium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically bright green with striking variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variant is prized for its ornamental foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
