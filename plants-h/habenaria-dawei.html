Habenaria dawei

Habenaria dawei, commonly known as the Dawei orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
