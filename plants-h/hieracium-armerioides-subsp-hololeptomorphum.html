Hieracium armerioides subsp. hololeptomorphum

Hieracium armerioides subsp.

hololeptomorphum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, yellowish to reddish flowers.

The leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a hairy surface, and the stems are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

This subspecies is distinguished by its elongated, slender leaves and the structure of its flower heads.
