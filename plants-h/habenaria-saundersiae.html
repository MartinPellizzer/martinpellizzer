Habenaria saundersiae

Habenaria saundersiae, commonly known as the Saunders' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical forests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces showy, often pale pink or white blooms with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid environments with partial shade and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered in some regions.
