Hydrorchis orbicularis

Hydrorchis orbicularis, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, orbicular-shaped flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. The plant produces small, dense spikes of flowers, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The orchid has a slender, erect stem and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms.
