Homalomena pierreana

Homalomena pierreana, commonly known as the Pierre's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Homalomena pierreana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
