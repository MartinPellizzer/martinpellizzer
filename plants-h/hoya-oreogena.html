Hoya oreogena

Hoya oreogena, commonly known as the mountain hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal. Hoya oreogena prefers indirect sunlight and well-draining soil, making it relatively easy to care for.

Its unique appearance and ability to produce fragrant blooms make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
