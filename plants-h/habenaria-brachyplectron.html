Habenaria brachyplectron

Habenaria brachyplectron, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a spike, which are often overlooked due to their inconspicuous appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
