Hieracium thysanotum

Hieracium thysanotum, commonly known as the silky hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers that are surrounded by a ring of pale green to white ray florets. It has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

Hieracium thysanotum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can spread rapidly through its rhizomes.
