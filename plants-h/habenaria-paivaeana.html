Habenaria paivaeana

Habenaria paivaeana, commonly known as the Piaui orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
