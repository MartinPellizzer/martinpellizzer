Hippeastrum schulzianum

Hippeastrum schulzianum, commonly known as the Schultesian amaryllis, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant produces a tall, leafless stem that bears clusters of blooms, followed by a cluster of sword-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum schulzianum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
