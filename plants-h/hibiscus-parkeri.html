Hibiscus parkeri

Hibiscus parkeri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight. Hibiscus parkeri is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its flowers bloom throughout the year, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
