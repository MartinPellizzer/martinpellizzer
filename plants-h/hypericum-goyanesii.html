Hypericum goyanesii

Hypericum goyanesii, commonly known as the Goyanesii St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and oval in shape, often covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum goyanesii is adapted to rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
