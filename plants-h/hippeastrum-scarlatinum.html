Hippeastrum scarlatinum

Hippeastrum scarlatinum, commonly known as the red tiger lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow, often with intricate patterns resembling a tiger's stripes. The plant produces large, fleshy leaves that are typically broad and sword-like in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Hippeastrum scarlatinum is known for its winter dormancy and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
