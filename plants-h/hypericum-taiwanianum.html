Hypericum taiwanianum

Hypericum taiwanianum, commonly known as the Taiwan goldthread, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. It has small, oval-shaped leaves and produces bright orange or yellow berries in the fall.

Hypericum taiwanianum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
