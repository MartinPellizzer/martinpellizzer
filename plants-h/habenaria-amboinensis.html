Habenaria amboinensis

Habenaria amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive spur. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it blooms during the rainy season.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
