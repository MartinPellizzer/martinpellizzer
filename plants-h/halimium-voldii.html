Halimium voldii

Halimium voldii, commonly known as the Vold's honesty, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate touch to its ornamental value.

Halimium voldii is also noted for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions, which contributes to its hardiness in various climates.
