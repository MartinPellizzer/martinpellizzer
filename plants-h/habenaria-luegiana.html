Habenaria luegiana

Habenaria luegiana, commonly known as the Lueg's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizome is fibrous, and it produces a single spike of flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip.

The botanical features of Habenaria luegiana make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
