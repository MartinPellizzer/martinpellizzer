Havetiopsis laurifolia

Havetiopsis laurifolia, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palm-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in warm climates.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
