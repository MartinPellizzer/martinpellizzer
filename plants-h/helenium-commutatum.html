Helenium commutatum

Helenium commutatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf helenium, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, orange to red ray flowers. Helenium commutatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a food source for pollinators.
