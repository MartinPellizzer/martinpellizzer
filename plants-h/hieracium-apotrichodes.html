Hieracium apotrichodes

Hieracium apotrichodes, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Hieracium. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium apotrichodes is an herbaceous perennial that thrives in temperate climates.
