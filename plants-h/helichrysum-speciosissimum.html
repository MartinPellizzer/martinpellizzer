Helichrysum speciosissimum

Helichrysum speciosissimum, commonly known as the golden marguerite or everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that resemble daisies. The plant produces papery, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its long-lasting blooms make it a favored plant for dried flower crafts.
