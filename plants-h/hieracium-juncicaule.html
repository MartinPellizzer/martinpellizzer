Hieracium juncicaule

Hieracium juncicaule, commonly known as slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
