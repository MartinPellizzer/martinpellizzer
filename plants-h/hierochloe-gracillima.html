Hierochloe gracillima

Hierochloe gracillima, commonly known as slender hairgrass, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, slender leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. This plant thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or arctic environments. Its growth habit is low and mat-forming, making it well adapted to harsh climatic conditions.

Hierochloe gracillima plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats and is of interest to botanists and ecologists.
