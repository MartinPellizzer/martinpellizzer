Hieracium × obornyanum subsp. subtatrense

Hieracium × obornyanum subsp.

subtatrense, commonly known as the Subtatar hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in steppe and semi-desert environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and localized species.
