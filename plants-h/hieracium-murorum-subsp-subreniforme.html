Hieracium murorum subsp. subreniforme

Hieracium murorum subsp.

subreniforme, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrow, reniform (kidney-shaped) leaves and shorter stature compared to other hawkweed species. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant is often found in disturbed areas and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
