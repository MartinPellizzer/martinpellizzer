Hieracium praealtum var. parviflorum

Hieracium praealtum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, with stems that are often reddish in color.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months, contributing to its role in supporting pollinators.
