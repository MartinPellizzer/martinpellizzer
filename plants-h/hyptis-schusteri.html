Hyptis schusteri

Hyptis schusteri, commonly known as Schuster's hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Hyptis schusteri is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
