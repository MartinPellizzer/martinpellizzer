Humata elegans

Humata elegans, commonly known as the elegant humata, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in clusters along the stems. Humata elegans thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and unique foliage.
