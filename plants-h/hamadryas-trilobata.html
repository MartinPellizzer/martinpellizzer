Hamadryas trilobata

Hamadryas trilobata, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in cloud forests or under dense canopy cover. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a fragrant scent that attracts specific pollinators.
