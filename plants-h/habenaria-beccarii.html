Habenaria beccarii

Habenaria beccarii, commonly known as the Beccari's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often in the understory of tropical rainforests. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that produce multiple stems each year. Its flowers are usually small and greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

The orchid's botanical significance lies in its role within its ecosystem and its value in botanical research.
