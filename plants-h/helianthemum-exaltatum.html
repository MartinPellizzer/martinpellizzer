Helianthemum exaltatum

Helianthemum exaltatum, commonly known as the tall rockrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Helianthemum exaltatum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
