Hieracium Rigidum Subsp. Epacroides

Hieracium rigidum subsp. epacroides is a lesser-known member of the daisy family found primarily in the mountainous regions of Europe. This subspecies is distinguished by its upright growth habit and narrow, rigid leaves that give it a unique appearance. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators. Its rarity and specific habitat requirements make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
