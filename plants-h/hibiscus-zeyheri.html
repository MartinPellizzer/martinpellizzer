Hibiscus zeyheri

Hibiscus zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and grows as a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hibiscus zeyheri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
