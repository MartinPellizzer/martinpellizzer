Heteropterys sericea var. typica

Heteropterys sericea var. typica, commonly known as the silky leaf huito, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its elliptical, glossy, and velvety leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
