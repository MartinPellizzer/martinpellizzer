Hypericum kinashianum var. longistylum

Hypericum kinashianum var. longistylum, commonly known as the long-styled St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Turkey.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or woodland soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

The variety 'longistylum' is distinguished by its elongated stamens, which contribute to its unique floral structure.
