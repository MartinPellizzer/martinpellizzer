Hieracium ithytomum

Hieracium ithytomum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy stems, and it typically grows in meadows and grassy areas. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Due to its invasive nature in some regions, it can outcompete native vegetation.
