Habenaria truncata

Habenaria truncata, commonly known as the truncated orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive lip petal. Its growth habit is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them.

The botanical characteristics of Habenaria truncata make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
