Habenaria bracteosa

Habenaria bracteosa, commonly known as the bracteate orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Botanically, it is notable for its intricate floral structure and its role in local ecosystems.
