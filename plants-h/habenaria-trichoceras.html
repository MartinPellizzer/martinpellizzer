Habenaria trichoceras

Habenaria trichoceras, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces numerous small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a dense spike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique floral structure and hairy appearance make it a distinctive member of the Orchidaceae family.
