Haworthia reinwardtii var. haworthii

Haworthia reinwardtii var. haworthii, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white tubercles and translucent tips. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
