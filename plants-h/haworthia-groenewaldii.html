Haworthia groenewaldii

Haworthia groenewaldii, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
