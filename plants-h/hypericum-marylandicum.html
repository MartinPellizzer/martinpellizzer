Hypericum marylandicum

Hypericum marylandicum, commonly known as Maryland goldenseal, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant is often found in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. It is closely related to other members of the Hypericaceae family, which are known for their medicinal properties.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
