Hebe rigidula

Hebe rigidula, commonly known as the rigid hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Its foliage is evergreen, with narrow, leathery leaves that contribute to its ornamental appeal.
