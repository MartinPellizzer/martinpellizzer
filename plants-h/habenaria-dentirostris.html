Habenaria dentirostris

Habenaria dentirostris, commonly known as the toothed beak orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive toothed lip that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
