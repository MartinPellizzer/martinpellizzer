Homalium gracile

Homalium gracile, commonly known as the slender waxtree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
