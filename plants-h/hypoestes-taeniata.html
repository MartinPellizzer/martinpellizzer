Hypoestes taeniata

Hypoestes taeniata, commonly known as the "Purple Queen" or "Rainbow Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink, red, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and long-lasting blooms.
