Hypericum fulgidum

Hypericum fulgidum, commonly known as the shining St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Hypericum fulgidum is noted for its medicinal properties, with the dried flowers used in traditional herbal remedies.
