Hypertelis bowkeriana

Hypertelis bowkeriana, commonly known as the Bowker's evergreen tree, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and can grow up to 10 meters in height. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a glossy green surface. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries.

This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
