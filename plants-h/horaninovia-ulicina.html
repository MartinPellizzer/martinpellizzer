Horaninovia ulicina

Horaninovia ulicina, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and botanical collections.

Horaninovia ulicina is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional South American herbal practices.
