Hieracium murorum subsp. stenstroemii

Hieracium murorum subsp.

stenstroemii, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender stems and reduced leaf size compared to other members of the species. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.

The plant is often found in temperate regions and is relatively easy to identify due to its distinctive floral structure.
