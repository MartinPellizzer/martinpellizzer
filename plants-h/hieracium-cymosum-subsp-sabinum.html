Hieracium cymosum subsp. sabinum

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

sabinum, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its clustered inflorescence and yellow-orange ray florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it is native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
