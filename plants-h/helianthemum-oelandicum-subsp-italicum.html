Helianthemum oelandicum subsp. italicum

Helianthemum oelandicum subsp.

italicum, commonly known as the Italian rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient species suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
