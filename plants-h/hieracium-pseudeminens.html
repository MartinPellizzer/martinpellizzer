Hieracium pseudeminens

Hieracium pseudeminens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium pseudeminens is often considered a weed but can serve as a food source for certain pollinators.
