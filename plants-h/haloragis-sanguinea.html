Haloragis sanguinea

Haloragis sanguinea, commonly known as redstem flax or redstem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features slender, reddish stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are clustered in spike-like inflorescences and are usually pink or red in color.

Haloragis sanguinea is known for its ability to thrive in saline and arid environments.
