Hypericum canadense var. oviforme

Hypericum canadense var. oviforme, commonly known as the oval-stemmed St. John's wort, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features upright, branched stems that are often reddish in color, covered with small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, with a distinctive yellowish-green hue. This variety is characterized by its ovate, rounded fruit capsules that contain small, dark seeds.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas.
