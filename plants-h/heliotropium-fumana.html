Heliotropium fumana

Heliotropium fumana, commonly known as the smoky heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a faint, sweet fragrance. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Heliotropium fumana is often found in disturbed soils and is adaptable to a variety of growing conditions.
