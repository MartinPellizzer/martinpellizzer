Hypericum rumeliacum var. deltoides

Hypericum rumeliacum var. deltoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in moist, woodland habitats. It is part of the Hypericaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental uses.

Its historical significance includes use in traditional herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
