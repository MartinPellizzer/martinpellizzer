Howardia setigera

Howardia setigera, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped stems that are covered in small, fleshy, green leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
