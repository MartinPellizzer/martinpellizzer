Hydrangea marunoi

Hydrangea marunoi, commonly known as the Japanese mountain hydrangea, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from blue to pink, depending on soil pH. Its leaves are ovate and serrated, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

Hydrangea marunoi thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest environments.
