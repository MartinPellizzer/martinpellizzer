Hoya corymbosa

Hoya corymbosa, commonly known as the wax plant or coral vine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers.
