Helianthemum orientale

Helianthemum orientale, commonly known as the oriental rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe and northern Africa.

It features dense clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring and early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to thrive in rocky or well-drained soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking hardy, decorative plants.
