Hieracium chlorophaeum

Hieracium chlorophaeum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish-green leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has a creeping growth habit with rhizomatous stems that allow it to spread vegetatively.

It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along roadsides.
