Hieracium pseudopallidum

Hieracium pseudopallidum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. This perennial herb typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 40 centimeters. Its yellow flowers are clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence, and it has hairy, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant is characterized by its ability to spread through rhizomes and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
