Habenaria xanthantha

Habenaria xanthantha, commonly known as the yellow lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and a long, spiraled inflorescence bearing numerous yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. Its flowers are often pollinated by bees and other small insects.

Habenaria xanthantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
