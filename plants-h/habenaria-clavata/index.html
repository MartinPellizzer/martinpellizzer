Habenaria Clavata

Habenaria clavata is a fascinating orchid species known for its unique floral structure and striking appearance. Native to parts of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers feature a distinctive clavate shape, giving it its scientific name. The plant is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements. It plays an important role in its ecosystem by supporting pollinators and contributing to biodiversity.
