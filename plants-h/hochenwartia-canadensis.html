Hochenwartia canadensis

Hochenwartia canadensis, commonly known as the Canadian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Canada.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and greenish-white, with a distinct lip that aids in pollination.

Hochenwartia canadensis is valued for its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitat.
