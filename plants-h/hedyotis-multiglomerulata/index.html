Hedyotis Multiglomerulata

Hedyotis multiglomerulata is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense undergrowth. Due to habitat loss and overcollection, it is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique botanical species.
