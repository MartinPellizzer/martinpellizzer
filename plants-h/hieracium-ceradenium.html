Hieracium ceradenium

Hieracium ceradenium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Hieracium ceradenium is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
