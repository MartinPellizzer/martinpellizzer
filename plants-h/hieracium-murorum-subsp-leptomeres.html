Hieracium murorum subsp. leptomeres

Hieracium murorum subsp.

leptomeres, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its stems are usually unbranched and covered in fine pubescence.

This subspecies is distinguished by its smaller, more delicate flowers compared to other members of the Hieracium genus.
