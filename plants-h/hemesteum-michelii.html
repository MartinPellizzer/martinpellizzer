Hemesteum michelii

Hemesteum michelii, commonly known as the Michel's hemesteum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
