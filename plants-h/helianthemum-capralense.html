Helianthemum capralense

Helianthemum capralense, commonly known as the Capri rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically features small, fleshy leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas and limestone formations. It is drought-tolerant and has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens.

The species is noted for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
