Hypoxis schimperi

Hypoxis schimperi, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that open at night. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a waxy coating, and its flowers emit a mild fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Hypoxis schimperi is valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
