Hakea lorea var. mollis

Hakea lorea var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-leaf hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, soft, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces cylindrical flower spikes that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
