Hieracium besserianum

Hieracium besserianum, commonly known as the Bessera's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and tall, branching stems that bear numerous yellow flowers. Native to Central Asia, this species thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces flowers that are typically arranged in clusters.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
