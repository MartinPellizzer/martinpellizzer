Helichrysum vellereum

Helichrysum vellereum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a decorative plant.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature.
