Hordeum adpressum

Hordeum adpressum, commonly known as the dwarf barley, is a species of grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a small, compact plant that typically grows to a height of less than 30 centimeters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Botanically, it is characterized by its short stature and is closely related to other barley species.
