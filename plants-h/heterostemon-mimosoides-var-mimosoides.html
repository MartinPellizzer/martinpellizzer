Heterostemon mimosoides var. mimosoides

Heterostemon mimosoides var. mimosoides, commonly known as the silky mimosoides, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are bipinnately compound, giving it a distinctive, fern-like appearance.
