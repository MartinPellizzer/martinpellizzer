Habenaria magniscutata

Habenaria magniscutata, commonly known as the large-leafed marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. It typically grows in wet, shaded habitats such as marshes and swamps.

Its flowers are pollinated by insects, and it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
