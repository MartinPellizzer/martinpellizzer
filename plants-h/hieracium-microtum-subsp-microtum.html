Hieracium microtum subsp. microtum

Hieracium microtum subsp.

microtum, commonly known as the small-centred hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

The plant features basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to oblanceolate in shape, with serrated margins. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a composite head, and are surrounded by pale green bracts.

It is characterized by its small, clustered inflorescence and is often found in moist, open habitats.
