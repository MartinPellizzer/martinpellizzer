Hermione unicolor

Hermione unicolor, commonly known as the single-color herb, is a rare flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes Mountains.

The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down. It blooms during the late spring and early summer, producing clusters of fragrant flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
