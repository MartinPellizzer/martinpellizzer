Hesperaloe chiangii

Hesperaloe chiangii, commonly known as the Chiang Mai torch lily, is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family native to Thailand.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.

Hesperaloe chiangii is prized for its ornamental value and ability to add dramatic visual interest to landscapes.
