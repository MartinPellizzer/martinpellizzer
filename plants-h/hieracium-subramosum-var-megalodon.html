Hieracium subramosum var. megalodon

Hieracium subramosum var. megalodon, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a variant of the hawkweed genus native to Europe.

It is characterized by its robust growth and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

This variety is notable for its size and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
