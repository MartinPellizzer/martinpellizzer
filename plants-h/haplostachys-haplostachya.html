Haplostachys haplostachya

Haplostachys haplostachya, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, helping to reduce water loss in its harsh environment.
