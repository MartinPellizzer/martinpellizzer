Hovenia dulcis var. crassifolia

Hovenia dulcis var. crassifolia, commonly known as the Chinese date tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers.
