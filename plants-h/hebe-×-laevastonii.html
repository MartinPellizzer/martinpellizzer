Hebe × laevastonii

Hebe × laevastonii, commonly known as the silver-leaved hebe, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance, with leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple during the spring and summer months. It is typically evergreen, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Hebe × laevastonii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate a range of growing conditions.
