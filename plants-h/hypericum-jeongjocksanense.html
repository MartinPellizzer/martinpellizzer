Hypericum jeongjocksanense

Hypericum jeongjocksanense, commonly known as the Jeongjocksan hypericum, is a flowering plant native to South Korea.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of flowering plants known for their yellow or orange flowers.
