Hieracium subpraetenellum

Hieracium subpraetenellum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring moist and sunny conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is considered a common weed in agricultural areas.
