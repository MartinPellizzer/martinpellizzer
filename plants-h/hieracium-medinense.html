Hieracium medinense

Hieracium medinense, commonly known as the Medinense hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is an herbaceous perennial that produces seeds through wind dispersal.
