Hedysarum philoscia

Hedysarum philoscia, commonly known as the Persian vetch, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of Asia and Europe.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its compound leaves and yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its pods contain small, edible seeds that are sometimes used in traditional cuisines.

Hedysarum philoscia also plays a role in nitrogen fixation, contributing to soil fertility.
