Hakea patulifolia

Hakea patulifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in summer, which are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

Hakea patulifolia is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and resilience.
