Habenaria tradescantiifolia

Habenaria tradescantiifolia, commonly known as the silver sword orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, slender stem with numerous lance-shaped leaves that have a distinctive silver-gray coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers with a subtle fragrance. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical collections and conservation programs.
