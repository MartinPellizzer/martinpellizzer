Horridocactus kesselringianus

Horridocactus kesselringianus, commonly known as the Kesselringian cactus, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which bloom at night. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
