Hypericum addingtonii

Hypericum addingtonii, commonly known as the Addington goldbar, is a low-growing shrub native to the British Isles.

It features narrow, dark green leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with prominent orange stamens. The plant is typically found in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partially shaded to full sun conditions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden settings for its compact form and seasonal color.

Hypericum addingtonii is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
