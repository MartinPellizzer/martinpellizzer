Hypericum garrettii

Hypericum garrettii, commonly known as Garrett's St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering shrubs. The plant is characterized by its yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite and oval, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or open areas.
