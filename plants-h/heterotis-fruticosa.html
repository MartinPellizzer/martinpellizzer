Heterotis fruticosa

Heterotis fruticosa, commonly known as the African buttonweed, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink in color. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Heterotis fruticosa is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
