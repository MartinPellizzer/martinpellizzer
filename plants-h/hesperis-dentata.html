Hesperis dentata

Hesperis dentata, commonly known as the toothed honesty, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often found in dry meadows and rocky habitats. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and toothed, giving it its scientific name.

Hesperis dentata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
