Helichrysum farinosum

Helichrysum farinosum, commonly known as the silver leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is evergreen and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its fuzzy leaves and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for dried floral arrangements.

It is also valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine.
