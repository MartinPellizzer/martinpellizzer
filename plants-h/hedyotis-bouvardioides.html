Hedyotis bouvardioides

Hedyotis bouvardioides, commonly known as the slender flame flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, bright orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.
