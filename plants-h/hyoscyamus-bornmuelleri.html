Hyoscyamus bornmuelleri

Hyoscyamus bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Ethiopian belladonna, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and hairy leaves. The plant produces berries that contain toxic alkaloids, similar to other members of its genus. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its toxicity, it is not typically cultivated for ornamental or agricultural purposes.
