Hieracium sciadophorum subsp. digenes

Hieracium sciadophorum subsp.

digenes, commonly known as the dwarf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It typically grows in alpine and subalpine meadows, where it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

The plant features a compact rosette of basal leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
