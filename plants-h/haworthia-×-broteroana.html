Haworthia × broteroana

Haworthia × broteroana, commonly known as the Broteroana Haworthia, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its compact size and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor succulent collections.
