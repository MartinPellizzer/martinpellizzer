Habenaria longirostris

Habenaria longirostris, commonly known as the long-beaked orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single, elongated spike bearing several pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. Its flowers have a distinctive long, slender spur that extends from the base of the lip.

This orchid thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
