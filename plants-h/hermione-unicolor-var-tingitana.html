Hermione unicolor var. tingitana

Hermione unicolor var. tingitana, commonly known as the Canary Island cypress, is a rare and ornamental conifer native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its slender, columnar form and dark green, scale-like foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green cones, typical of the Cupressaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a specimen plant.
