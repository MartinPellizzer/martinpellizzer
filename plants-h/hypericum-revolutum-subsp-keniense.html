Hypericum revolutum subsp. keniense

Hypericum revolutum subsp.

keniense, commonly known as the Kenyan St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to East Africa, particularly Kenya. It is characterized by its yellow flowers and opposite, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant typically grows in open woodland and grassland habitats. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
