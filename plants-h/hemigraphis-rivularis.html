Hemigraphis rivularis

Hemigraphis rivularis, commonly known as the river hemigraphis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, often variegated foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in humid forests.

Hemigraphis rivularis is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to cascade from hanging baskets or containers.
