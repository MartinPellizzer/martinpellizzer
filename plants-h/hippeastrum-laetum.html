Hippeastrum laetum

Hippeastrum laetum, commonly known as the White Natal lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to flower properly.

Hippeastrum laetum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
