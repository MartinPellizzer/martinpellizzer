Herniaria regnieri var. glaberrima

Herniaria regnieri var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth bladderwort, is a small, mat-forming plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, green, succulent leaves that often appear in pairs. The plant produces tiny, pale yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early summer. It thrives in moist, sandy or gravelly soils, often found in coastal or inland dune environments.

This variety is distinguished by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves, setting it apart from other varieties within the Herniaria genus.
