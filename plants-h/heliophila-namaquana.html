Heliophila namaquana

Heliophila namaquana, commonly known as theNamaqua sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and dark brown disk florets. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun, making it well adapted to arid environments. It is often found in theNamaqua region, where it contributes to local biodiversity.

Heliophila namaquana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
