Hieracium carnosum subsp. euroum

Hieracium carnosum subsp.

euroum, commonly known as the European hedgehog hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

The plant features upright, hairy stems that can reach up to 30 cm in height. Its basal leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the upper leaves are smaller and more oval.

The flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and are borne on long, slender pedicels.
