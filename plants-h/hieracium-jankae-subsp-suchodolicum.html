Hieracium jankae subsp. suchodolicum

Hieracium jankae subsp.

suchodolicum, commonly known as the Carpathian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous habitats such as meadows and forest edges. It has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture, and its flowers are arranged in a single head.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
