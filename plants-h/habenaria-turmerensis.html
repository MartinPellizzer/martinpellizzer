Habenaria turmerensis

Habenaria turmerensis, commonly known as the Turmeres orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or orange with purple or red markings. Its inflorescence is a raceme of showy, resupinate flowers, and it blooms during the wet season.

The genus Habenaria is characterized by its intricate floral structures and is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.
