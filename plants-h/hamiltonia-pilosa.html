Hamiltonia pilosa

Hamiltonia pilosa, commonly known as the hairy bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, hairy flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Hamiltonia pilosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
