Hieracium vulgatum var. anfractum

Hieracium vulgatum var. anfractum, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and basal leaves that are broadly ovate to lanceolate in shape.
