Haworthia bolusii var. bolusii

Haworthia bolusii var. bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, gray-green leaves with white to yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
