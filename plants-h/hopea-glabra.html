Hopea glabra

Hopea glabra, commonly known as the smooth hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, light gray bark and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.
