Habenaria longicorniculata

Habenaria longicorniculata, commonly known as the long-horned orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow-green lip and purple sepals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and pollination mechanisms make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
