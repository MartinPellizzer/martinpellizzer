Hedysarum Crinitum

Hedysarum crinitum is a flowering plant known for its delicate, feathery foliage and clusters of pinkish-white blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. This species is often found in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking an ornamental yet resilient plant. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to more common garden plants.
