Haworthia cooperi var. dielsiana

Haworthia cooperi var. dielsiana, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct, white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
