Hosta yakusimensis

Hosta yakusimensis, commonly known as the Yakusimensis Hosta, is a species native to Japan.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-blue hue on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers in late summer, which are borne on slender stems. It prefers partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking foliage and ability to thrive in cooler climates.
