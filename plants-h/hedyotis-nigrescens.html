Hedyotis nigrescens

Hedyotis nigrescens, commonly known as black willow or black geranium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, clustered white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The plant is noted for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional remedies.
