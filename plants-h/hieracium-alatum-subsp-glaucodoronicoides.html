Hieracium alatum subsp. glaucodoronicoides

Hieracium alatum subsp.

glaucodoronicoides, commonly known as the silverleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain regions.
