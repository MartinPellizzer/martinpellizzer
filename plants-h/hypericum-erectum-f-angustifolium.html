Hypericum erectum f. angustifolium

Hypericum erectum f. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Hypericaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are typically less than 1 cm in width. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along woodland edges.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
