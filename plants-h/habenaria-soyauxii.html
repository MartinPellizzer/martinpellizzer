Habenaria soyauxii

Habenaria soyauxii, commonly known as the Soyaux's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces a single, erect stem bearing several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
