Hebe × simmonsii

Hebe × simmonsii, commonly known as the purple mistflower, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple or pink during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy or loamy soils.

Hebe × simmonsii is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
