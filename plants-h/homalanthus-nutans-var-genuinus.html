Homalanthus nutans var. genuinus

Homalanthus nutans var. genuinus, commonly known as the Pacific Islands balsam, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a herbaceous annual with erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to grow in coastal and sandy soils, often found in disturbed areas.

The species is notable for its medicinal uses in traditional Polynesian medicine.
