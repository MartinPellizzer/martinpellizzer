Hypericum canadense

Hypericum canadense, commonly known as Canadian St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its botanical features include small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Hypericum canadense is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands.
