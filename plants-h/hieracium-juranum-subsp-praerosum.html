Hieracium juranum subsp. praerosum

Hieracium juranum subsp.

praerosum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are generally lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in many areas.
