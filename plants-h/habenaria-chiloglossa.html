Habenaria chiloglossa

Habenaria chiloglossa, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and its distinctive, fragrant, pinkish-white flower spikes. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and along stream banks. It produces small, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Habenaria chiloglossa is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in its natural habitat.
