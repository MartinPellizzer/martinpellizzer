Hosta calliantha

Hosta calliantha, commonly known as the golden heart hosta, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a striking yellow or gold center. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late summer, which are clustered in loose panicles. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta calliantha is a popular ornamental plant in gardens due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
