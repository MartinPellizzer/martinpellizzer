Hieracium carpathicum subsp. splendens

Hieracium carpathicum subsp.

splendens, commonly known as the Carpathian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers.

The plant features basal and stem leaves that are typically lanceolate or ovate with serrated margins. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and forest edges, often in temperate climates.

The subspecies is distinguished by its showy, ray florets and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
