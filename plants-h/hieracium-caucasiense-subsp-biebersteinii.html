Hieracium caucasiense subsp. biebersteinii

Hieracium caucasiense subsp.

biebersteinii, commonly known as the Caucasian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and rocky slopes, exhibiting a rosette of basal leaves. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges, and the flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.
