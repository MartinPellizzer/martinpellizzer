Hypericum yezoanum var. grandifolium

Hypericum yezoanum var. grandifolium, commonly known as the Korean St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to East Asia.

It features upright stems covered in yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
