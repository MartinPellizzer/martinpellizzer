Haworthiopsis fasciata var. fasciata

Haworthiopsis fasciata var. fasciata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves with distinctive white or pale green vertical stripes. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and minimal water requirements, making it well-suited for arid environments. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
