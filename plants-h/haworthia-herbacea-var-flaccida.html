Haworthia herbacea var. flaccida

Haworthia herbacea var. flaccida, commonly known as the flaccid Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green with white or pale markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, allowing the plant to thrive in arid conditions. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
