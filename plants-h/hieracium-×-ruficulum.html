Hieracium × ruficulum

Hieracium × ruficulum, commonly known as the red-hairy hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, reddish stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers in clustered inflorescences, typical of the daisy family. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including meadows and roadsides.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
