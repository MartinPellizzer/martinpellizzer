Helleborine tuberosa

Helleborine tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, greenish-yellow to brownish flowers with a distinctive lip petal. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a waxy coating, while its roots form tubers that store nutrients.

Helleborine tuberosa is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
