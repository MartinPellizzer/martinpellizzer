Haworthia schmidtiana var. pusilla

Haworthia schmidtiana var. pusilla, commonly known as the miniature Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green with white or pale markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, allowing the plant to thrive in arid conditions. It produces small, white flowers on short stalks during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
