Hieracium murorum subsp. ellipticans

Hieracium murorum subsp.

ellipticans, commonly known as the elliptic hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has basal and stem leaves that are elliptical in shape, with hairy surfaces. It is typically found in meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates.

This subspecies is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive in certain habitats.
