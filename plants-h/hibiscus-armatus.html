Hibiscus armatus

Hibiscus armatus, commonly known as the armed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny calyx that gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Hibiscus armatus typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
