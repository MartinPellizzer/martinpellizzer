Hypoxis rigidula

Hypoxis rigidula, commonly known as the rigid yellow star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Hypoxis rigidula is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
