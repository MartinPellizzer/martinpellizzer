Hieracium microtum

Hieracium microtum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.
