Hieracium streptotrichum

Hieracium streptotrichum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a dense, flat-topped cluster. The plant has a robust root system and produces a mat of narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to Europe, it is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils.

Its growth habit and floral structure make it a notable species in ecological studies and botanical gardens.
