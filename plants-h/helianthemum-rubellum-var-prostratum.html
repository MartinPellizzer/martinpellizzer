Helianthemum rubellum var. prostratum

Helianthemum rubellum var. prostratum, commonly known as the prostrate red sandwort, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white wax. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or alpine environments.

Due to its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
