Hildegardia populifolia

Hildegardia populifolia, commonly known as the poplar-leaved hildegardia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that resemble those of poplar trees, giving it its common name.
