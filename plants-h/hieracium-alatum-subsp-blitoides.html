Hieracium alatum subsp. blitoides

Hieracium alatum subsp.

blitoides, commonly known as the blitoid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are broadly lanceolate and often have a rough, hairy texture.

It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by cylindrical seed heads.
