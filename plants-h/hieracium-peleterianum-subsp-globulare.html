Hieracium peleterianum subsp. globulare

Hieracium peleterianum subsp.

globulare, commonly known as the globe hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that give it its common name. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the daisy family, Asteraceae.
