Hieracium jutlandicum

Hieracium jutlandicum, commonly known as the Jutland hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to northern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as sandy soils and grasslands. It has a low-growing habit and produces numerous small, herbaceous stems.

Hieracium jutlandicum is often found in coastal areas and can tolerate moderate levels of salt and wind.
