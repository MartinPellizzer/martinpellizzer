Hymenocallis × macrostephana

Hymenocallis × macrostephana, commonly known as the giant daylily, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range from white to pale yellow, with a prominent central throat. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and smooth, often reaching several feet in length. It is native to the tropical regions of the Americas and thrives in warm, humid climates.

Hymenocallis × macrostephana is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
