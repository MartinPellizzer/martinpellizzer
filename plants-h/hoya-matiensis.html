Hoya matiensis

Hoya matiensis, commonly known as the Hainan waxplant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, ovate leaves. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
