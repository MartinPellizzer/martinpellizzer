Hibiscus baidoensis var. parvifolius

Hibiscus baidoensis var. parvifolius, commonly known as the small-leaved hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The leaves are typically small, heart-shaped, and have a smooth texture. This variant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
