Hieracium cymosum subsp. trichocymosum

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

trichocymosum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, grassy habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

The plant features upright stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence, which gives it its common name. Its basal leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while the flowering heads are yellow and contain numerous small florets.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
