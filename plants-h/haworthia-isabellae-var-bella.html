Haworthia isabellae var. bella

Haworthia isabellae var. bella, commonly known as the Isabellae Bella Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green to yellowish in color, often with intricate white or pale markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
