Hieracium calodon subsp. sphaleron

Hieracium calodon subsp.

sphaleron, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and woodland edges. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets that are shorter than the central disk florets.

It is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is often considered a weed in agricultural areas.
