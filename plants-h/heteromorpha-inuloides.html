Heteromorpha inuloides

Heteromorpha inuloides, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spherical fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and unique appearance.
