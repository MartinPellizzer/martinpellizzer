Hohenbergia leopoldo-horstii

Hohenbergia leopoldo-horstii, commonly known as the Leopoldo-Horst hohenbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit and vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that range from red to orange. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hohenbergia leopoldo-horstii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens and collections.
