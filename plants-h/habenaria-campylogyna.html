Habenaria campylogyna

Habenaria campylogyna, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
