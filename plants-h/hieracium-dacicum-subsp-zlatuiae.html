Hieracium dacicum subsp. zlatuiae

Hieracium dacicum subsp.

zlatuiae, commonly known as the Zlatuia Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets that are often shorter than the central disk florets.

It is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
