Hieracium bifidum subsp. pseudocaudatulum

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

pseudocaudatulum, commonly known as the slender spear-thistle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant features upright stems that are often hairy, with leaves that are lanceolate and sometimes lobed. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated bracts that give it a somewhat tail-like appearance.
