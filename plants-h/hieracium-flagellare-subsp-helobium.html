Hieracium flagellare subsp. helobium

Hieracium flagellare subsp.

helobium, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, often forming dense colonies. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and bear yellow flowers in a single head.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender stems and smaller leaves compared to other members of the species.
