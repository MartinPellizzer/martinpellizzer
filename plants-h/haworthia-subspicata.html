Haworthia subspicata

Haworthia subspicata, commonly known as the compact Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for collectors and garden enthusiasts.
