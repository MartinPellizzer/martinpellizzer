Hieracium picinum

Hieracium picinum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features upright stems with alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Hieracium picinum is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
