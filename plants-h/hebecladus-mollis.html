Hebecladus mollis

Hebecladus mollis, commonly known as the silky hebe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a container plant.
