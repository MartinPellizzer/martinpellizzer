Hibbertia linearis var. cyrtophylla

Hibbertia linearis var. cyrtophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hibbertia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and bright yellow flowers with prominent red stamens. The plant typically grows in heathlands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It is a perennial shrub that produces small, capsule-like fruits following flowering.

Hibbertia linearis var. cyrtophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and conservation areas.
