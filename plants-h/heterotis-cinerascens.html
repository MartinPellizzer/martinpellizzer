Heterotis cinerascens

Heterotis cinerascens, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, pale purple flowers that open at night, making it a nocturnal bloom. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy areas.
