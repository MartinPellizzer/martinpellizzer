Huperzia serrata f. longipetiolata

Huperzia serrata f. longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled clubmoss, is a variant of the Huperzia serrata species.

It is a tropical evergreen fern-like plant characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves with a serrated margin, which distinguish it from other clubmoss species. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology makes it a desirable specimen for botanical gardens and horticultural collections.
