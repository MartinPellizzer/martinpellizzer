Hopea nicholsonii

Hopea nicholsonii, commonly known as the Malayan hopea, is a medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and lanceolate leaves with a leathery texture. The tree produces small, pale yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, and its fruit is a capsule containing several winged seeds.

Hopea nicholsonii is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland dipterocarp forests.
