Hedysarum dasycarpum

Hedysarum dasycarpum, commonly known as the hairy vetch, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a robust root system that helps prevent soil erosion. It is often used in agricultural settings as a cover crop due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.

Hedysarum dasycarpum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
