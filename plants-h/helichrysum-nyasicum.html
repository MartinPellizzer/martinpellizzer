Helichrysum nyasicum

Helichrysum nyasicum, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, papery bracts that give it a distinctive appearance, often mistaken for petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum nyasicum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
