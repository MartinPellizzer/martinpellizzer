Huernia hystrix

Huernia hystrix, commonly known as the horned ice plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, fleshy stems that resemble horns, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Huernia hystrix is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
