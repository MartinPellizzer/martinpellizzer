Hieracium adscitum

Hieracium adscitum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous yellow to orange flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. Its flowers are composed of both ray and disk florets, typical of the daisy family.

Hieracium adscitum is known for its rapid spread and can become invasive in certain environments.
