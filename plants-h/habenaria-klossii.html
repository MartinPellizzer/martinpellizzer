Habenaria klossii

Habenaria klossii, commonly known as the Kloss's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
