Hemiboea henryi

Hemiboea henryi, commonly known as Henry's hemiboea, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces bright orange or red flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
