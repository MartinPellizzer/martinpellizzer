Haworthia maraisii var. major

Haworthia maraisii var. major, commonly known as the Marais Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
