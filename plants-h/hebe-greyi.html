Hebe greyi

Hebe greyi, commonly known as the Grey's hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Hebe greyi thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
