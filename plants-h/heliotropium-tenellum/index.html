Heliotropium Tenellum

Heliotropium tenellum is a unique flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. It is known for its small, fragrant flowers that emit a sweet, vanilla-like scent. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. Despite its delicate appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and ecosystems.
