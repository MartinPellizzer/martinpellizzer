Hemionitis trinervis

Hemionitis trinervis, commonly known as the three-nerved hemionitis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Hemionitidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves with three distinct veins. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to withstand harsh, dry conditions.
