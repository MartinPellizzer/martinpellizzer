Hymenodictyon excelsum var. subglabrum

Hymenodictyon excelsum var. subglabrum, commonly known as the smooth hymenodictyon, is a member of the Rhamnaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, often thorny growth habit. The leaves are typically small, opposite, and have a smooth or nearly hairless surface, as indicated by the varietal name "subglabrum.". The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

It is native to tropical regions of South America and is often found in humid, lowland forests.
