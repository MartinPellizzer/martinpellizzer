Haworthia pulchella var. pulchella

Haworthia pulchella var. pulchella, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant is relatively low maintenance, thriving in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
