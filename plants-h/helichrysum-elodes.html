Helichrysum elodes

Helichrysum elodes, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that are often mistaken for chrysanthemums. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Helichrysum elodes is also noted for its long-lasting blooms, which can persist for several months during the warmer seasons.
