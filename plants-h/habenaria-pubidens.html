Habenaria pubidens

Habenaria pubidens, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and marshy areas. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive hairy appearance on the petals and sepals.

Habenaria pubidens is known for its delicate structure and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
