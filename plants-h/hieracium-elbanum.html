Hieracium elbanum

Hieracium elbanum, commonly known as the Elban hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium elbanum is an annual or biennial species, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
