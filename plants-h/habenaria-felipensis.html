Habenaria felipensis

Habenaria felipensis, commonly known as the Felip's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or yellow with reddish-brown markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
