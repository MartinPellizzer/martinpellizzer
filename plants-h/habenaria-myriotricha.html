Habenaria myriotricha

Habenaria myriotricha, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender rhizome and produces tall, erect stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant blooms with clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers that have a delicate, velvety texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
