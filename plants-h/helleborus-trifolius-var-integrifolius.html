Helleborus trifolius var. integrifolius

Helleborus trifolius var. integrifolius, commonly known as the European hellebore, is a perennial plant native to Europe.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth margin. The plant produces clusters of dark red or purple flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It prefers partial to full shade and well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
