Hieracium achnoodes

Hieracium achnoodes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features basal and stem leaves that are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a hairy texture. Its flowering heads consist of yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disk florets, typical of the daisy family.

Hieracium achnoodes is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
