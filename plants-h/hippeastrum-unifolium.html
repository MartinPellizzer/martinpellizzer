Hippeastrum unifolium

Hippeastrum unifolium, commonly known as the single-leaf amaryllis, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, white, or pink. The plant has a bulbous stem and produces a single, broad leaf that can grow up to several feet in length. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hippeastrum unifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home garden setting.
