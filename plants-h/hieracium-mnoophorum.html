Hieracium mnoophorum

Hieracium mnoophorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in a compact cluster. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils. The plant is often found in meadows, pastures, and along riverbanks.

It is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and has been introduced to other regions as an invasive weed.
