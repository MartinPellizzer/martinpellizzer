Humiriastrum piraparanense

Humiriastrum piraparanense, commonly known as the Piraparanense candelabra, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a tall, branching structure with yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
