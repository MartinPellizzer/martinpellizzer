Helipterum incanum

Helipterum incanum, commonly known as the silverleaf helipterum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Helipterum incanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
