Hieracium schefferi

Hieracium schefferi, commonly known as the Scheffer's hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers with a central disk and is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
