Hydrangea kawakamii

Hydrangea kawakamii, commonly known as the Kawakamii hydrangea, is a species native to Japan, specifically the island of Kyushu.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from blue to pink, depending on soil pH. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin and a glossy green surface.

Hydrangea kawakamii thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
