Hieracium geilingeri subsp. brembanae

Hieracium geilingeri subsp.

brembanae, commonly known as the Brembanan hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
