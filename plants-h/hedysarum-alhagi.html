Hedysarum alhagi

Hedysarum alhagi, commonly known as alhagi sweet clover, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a robust root system that helps prevent soil erosion. It is often found in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Hedysarum alhagi is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural and ecological restoration contexts.
