Haworthia venteri

Haworthia venteri, commonly known as the "Cape Town beauty," is a species of succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Haworthia venteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
