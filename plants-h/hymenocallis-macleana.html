Hymenocallis macleana

Hymenocallis macleana, commonly known as the Maclean's spider lily, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, white, spider-like flowers with a prominent central stamen. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that grow from a bulbous base. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Hymenocallis macleana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
