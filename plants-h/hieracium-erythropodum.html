Hieracium erythropodum

Hieracium erythropodum, commonly known as the red-centred hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive red center. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

The species is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
