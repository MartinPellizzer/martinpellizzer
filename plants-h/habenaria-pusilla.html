Habenaria pusilla

Habenaria pusilla, commonly known as the small fairy orchid, is a small terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features a slender, leafless stem with a single or few flowers. Its flowers are usually small, with a distinctive violet or purple coloration and a unique lip structure.

Habenaria pusilla is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
