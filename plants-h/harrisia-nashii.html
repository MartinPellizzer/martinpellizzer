Harrisia nashii

Harrisia nashii, commonly known as the Nash harrisia, is a species of cactus native to southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer. Harrisia nashii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
