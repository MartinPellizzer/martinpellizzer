Hypericum keniense

Hypericum keniense, commonly known as Kenyan St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and woody stems, typical of the Hypericaceae family. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
