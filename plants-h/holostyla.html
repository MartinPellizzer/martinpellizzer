Holostyla

Holostyla, commonly known as the "spider orchid," is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

Holostyla species typically grow as terrestrial herbs, often found in sandy or rocky soils in arid or semi-arid regions. They produce small, greenish or yellowish flowers that are pollinated by insects, particularly flies and beetles.
