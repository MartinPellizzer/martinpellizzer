Helichrysum angustifolium subsp. microphyllum

Helichrysum angustifolium subsp.

microphyllum, commonly known as narrow-leaved everlasting, is a small shrub native to southern Africa. It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to arid environments, with succulent stems that store water.

Its aromatic foliage and long-lasting flowers make it a popular ornamental plant.
