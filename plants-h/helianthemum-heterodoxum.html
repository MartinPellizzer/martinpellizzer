Helianthemum heterodoxum

Helianthemum heterodoxum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically succulent, adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat.

The plant is often found on rocky slopes and coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
