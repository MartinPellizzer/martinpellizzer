Hieracium bifidum subsp. submolliceps

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

submolliceps, commonly known as the softleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered in a soft, velvety pubescence, giving it its common name.

The flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the genus Hieracium.
