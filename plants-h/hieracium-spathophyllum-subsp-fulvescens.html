Hieracium spathophyllum subsp. fulvescens

Hieracium spathophyllum subsp.

fulvescens, commonly known as the orange hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow to orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are often reddish in color.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy inflorescences.
