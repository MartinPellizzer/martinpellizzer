Hieracium productum

Hieracium productum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy, often reddish stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium productum is an invasive species in many regions, outcompeting native vegetation due to its rapid growth and prolific seed production.
