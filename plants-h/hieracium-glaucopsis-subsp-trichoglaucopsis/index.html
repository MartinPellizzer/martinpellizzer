Hieracium Glaucopsis Subsp. Trichoglaucopsis

Hieracium glaucopsis subsp. trichoglaucopsis is a lesser-known member of the daisy family found in specific regions of Europe. This plant is characterized by its distinctive blue-green foliage and small, pale yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges or along stream banks. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique plant from habitat loss and environmental changes.
