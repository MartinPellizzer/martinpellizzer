Hieracium subramosum subsp. subausterum

Hieracium subramosum subsp.

subausterum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish to orange-red composite flowers that are typically arranged in a single terminal inflorescence. It has fibrous roots and hairy stems, and its leaves are usually linear to lanceolate in shape.

This subspecies is often found in open habitats such as grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils.
