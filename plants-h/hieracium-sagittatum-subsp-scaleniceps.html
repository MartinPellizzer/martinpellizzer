Hieracium sagittatum subsp. scaleniceps

Hieracium sagittatum subsp.

scaleniceps, commonly known as the arrowhead hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands. It has a taproot system and produces seeds in small, disk-like structures.

The subspecies scaleniceps is distinguished by its elongated, narrow leaves and specific floral morphology.
