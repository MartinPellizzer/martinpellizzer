Hieracium patens subsp. myriophyllum

Hieracium patens subsp.

myriophyllum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its yellow ray florets and a basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces small, clustered inflorescences that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
