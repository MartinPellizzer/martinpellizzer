Helianthemum piorum

Helianthemum piorum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer, adding a vibrant touch to rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Helianthemum piorum is often found in coastal areas and is well-suited to full sun exposure.
