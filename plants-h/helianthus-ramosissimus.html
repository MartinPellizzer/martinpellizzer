Helianthus ramosissimus

Helianthus ramosissimus, commonly known as the stiff sunflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features upright stems covered in stiff, bristly hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with a dark center, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, grayish hairs.

Helianthus ramosissimus is often found in dry, open habitats such as fields and woodland edges.
