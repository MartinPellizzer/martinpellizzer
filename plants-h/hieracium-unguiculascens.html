Hieracium unguiculascens

Hieracium unguiculascens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in a compact cluster. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows and disturbed soils. The plant has a taproot system and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
