Hesperis orbignyana

Hesperis orbignyana, commonly known as the orbignyana candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hesperis orbignyana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
