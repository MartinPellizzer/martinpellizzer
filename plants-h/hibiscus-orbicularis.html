Hibiscus orbicularis

Hibiscus orbicularis, commonly known as the globe hibiscus, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, round, orbicular-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Hibiscus orbicularis thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
