Hieracium stenozon

Hieracium stenozon, commonly known as narrow-zone hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture.

Hieracium stenozon is native to Europe and is often found in temperate regions.
