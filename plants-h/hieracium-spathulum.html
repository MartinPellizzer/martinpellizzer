Hieracium spathulum

Hieracium spathulum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and covered in fine pubescence. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, disturbed soils such as meadows and riverbanks.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is often found in open habitats.
