Habenaria foliosa

Habenaria foliosa, commonly known as the leafy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces erect, branched inflorescences with small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a waxy surface that aids in water retention.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
