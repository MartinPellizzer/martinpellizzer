Hieracium pleiadenium

Hieracium pleiadenium, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and hairy, rough stems. Native to Europe, it has been introduced to other regions and is considered invasive in some areas. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a reddish or purplish hue, especially on the underside.
