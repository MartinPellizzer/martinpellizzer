Haworthia whitesloaneana

Haworthia whitesloaneana, commonly known as the White Sloane Haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks with clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor or rock garden settings.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
