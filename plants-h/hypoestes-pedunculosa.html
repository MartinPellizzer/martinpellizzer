Hypoestes pedunculosa

Hypoestes pedunculosa, commonly known as the "Snowflake" plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bi-colored leaves that often feature a white or pink edge against a green or red center. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes pedunculosa is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and ease of care.
