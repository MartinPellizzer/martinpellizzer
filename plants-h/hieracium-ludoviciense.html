Hieracium ludoviciense

Hieracium ludoviciense, commonly known as the Ludovician hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and is native to central and southern Europe. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. It has basal and stem leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges.

Hieracium ludoviciense is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies.
