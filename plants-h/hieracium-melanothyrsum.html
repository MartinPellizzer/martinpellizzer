Hieracium melanothyrsum

Hieracium melanothyrsum, commonly known as black-throated hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
