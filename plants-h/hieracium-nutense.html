Hieracium nutense

Hieracium nutense, commonly known as the Nuttall's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as meadows and roadsides, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium nutense is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
