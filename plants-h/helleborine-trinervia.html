Helleborine trinervia

Helleborine trinervia, commonly known as the lesser helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces pale green to white flowers with three sepals and three petals, often with a yellowish lip. It is typically found in woodland habitats and thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Helleborine trinervia is known for its ability to form dense colonies and is often used in ornamental gardening.
