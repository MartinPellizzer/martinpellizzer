Hieracium racemosum subsp. restonicanum

Hieracium racemosum subsp.

restonicanum, commonly known as the Restonican hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

It produces numerous small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months.
