Himantoglossum hircinum var. minusculum

Himantoglossum hircinum var. minusculum, commonly known as the small-flowered twayblade, is a member of the orchid family Orchidaceae.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its diminutive, pale green leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and heaths. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and produces a single, erect stem with two to three leaves.

Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow and lack a distinct lip, which is a distinguishing feature of the twayblade genus.
