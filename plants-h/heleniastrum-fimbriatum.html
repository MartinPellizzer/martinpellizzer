Heleniastrum fimbriatum

Heleniastrum fimbriatum, commonly known as the fringed helene, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Heleniastrum fimbriatum is typically found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
