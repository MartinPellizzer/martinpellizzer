Hieracium leiosoma subsp. subumbellatum

Hieracium leiosoma subsp.

subumbellatum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes and is often found in temperate regions.
