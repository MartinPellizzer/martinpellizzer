Heladena hassleriana

Heladena hassleriana, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Heladena hassleriana is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
