Hoya verticillata

Hoya verticillata, commonly known as the clustered wax plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, ovate leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink, often with a slight fragrance. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Hoya verticillata is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal watering and well-draining soil.
