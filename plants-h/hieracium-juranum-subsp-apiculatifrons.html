Hieracium juranum subsp. apiculatifrons

Hieracium juranum subsp.

apiculatifrons, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and hairy, basal leaves.

Native to the alpine regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky and open habitats. The subspecies is distinguished by its acuminate leaf tips and shorter flower stalks compared to other members of the species.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in mountainous areas and is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
