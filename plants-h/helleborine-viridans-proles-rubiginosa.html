Helleborine viridans proles rubiginosa

Helleborine viridans proles rubiginosa, commonly known as the spotted helleborine, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and striking, spurred flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.

Its flowers often bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting specific pollinators.
