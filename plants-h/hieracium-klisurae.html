Hieracium klisurae

Hieracium klisurae, commonly known as the Klisura hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. It produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Hieracium klisurae is noted for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
