Hieracium femsioense

Hieracium femsioense, commonly known as the Femsio hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Hieracium femsioense is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in diverse environmental conditions.
