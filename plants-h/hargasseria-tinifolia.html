Hargasseria tinifolia

Hargasseria tinifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf hargasseria, is a rare and unique plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which bloom during the cooler months. Hargasseria tinifolia thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires minimal water, making it adapted to arid environments.
