Helichrysum brachyrhynchum

Helichrysum brachyrhynchum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow to golden-orange flowers that are tightly packed into dense, spherical clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy texture.

Helichrysum brachyrhynchum is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
