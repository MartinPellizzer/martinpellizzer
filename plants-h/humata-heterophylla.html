Humata heterophylla

Humata heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf humata, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its variable leaf shapes, which can range from ovate to lanceolate depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in the spring. Its leaves are often hairy and have a distinct aromatic scent.

Humata heterophylla is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
