Hieracium cymbifolium

Hieracium cymbifolium, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
