Habenaria urceolata

Habenaria urceolata, commonly known as the urn-shaped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, urn-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple or red markings. The plant produces a single, erect stem with several leaves and blooms during the summer months. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The orchid's intricate floral structure is adapted for specific pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.
