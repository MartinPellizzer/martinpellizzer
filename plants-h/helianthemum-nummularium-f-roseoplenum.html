Helianthemum nummularium f. roseoplenum

Helianthemum nummularium f. roseoplenum, commonly known as the pink-flowered stonecress, is a low-growing succulent plant native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of delicate, pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or coastal regions.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
