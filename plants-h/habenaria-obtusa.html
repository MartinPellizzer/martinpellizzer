Habenaria obtusa

Habenaria obtusa, commonly known as the blunt-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale green or white, with a distinctive, recurved lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

This orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities.
