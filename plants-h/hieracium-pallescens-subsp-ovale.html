Hieracium pallescens subsp. ovale

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

ovale, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to pale green flowers arranged in a composite head.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and hairy, while its stems are often reddish and slightly hairy.

It is noted for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
