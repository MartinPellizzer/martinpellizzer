Hypoestes fimbriata

Hypoestes fimbriata, commonly known as the "Snowflake" plant, is a flowering species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bicolor leaves that often display shades of white, pink, and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes fimbriata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
