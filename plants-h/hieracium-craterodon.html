Hieracium craterodon

Hieracium craterodon, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium craterodon is often found in mountain meadows and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
