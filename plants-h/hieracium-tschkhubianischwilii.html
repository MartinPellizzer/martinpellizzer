Hieracium tschkhubianischwilii

Hieracium tschkhubianischwilii, commonly known as the Tschkhubian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish-green flowers and hairy leaves, typical of many hawkweed species. Native to the Caucasus region, this plant thrives in mountainous and rocky habitats. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.
