Hibiscus gourmania

Hibiscus gourmania, commonly known as the Madagascar hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers bloom throughout the year in warm climates.

Hibiscus gourmania is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
