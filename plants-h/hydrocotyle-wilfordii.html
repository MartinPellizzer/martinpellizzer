Hydrocotyle wilfordii

Hydrocotyle wilfordii, commonly known as Wilford's marsh penny.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland areas in Southeast Asia. The plant features rounded, light green leaves that are typically floating or submerged in water. It produces small, white flowers with five petals, which bloom in the summer months.

Hydrocotyle wilfordii is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.
