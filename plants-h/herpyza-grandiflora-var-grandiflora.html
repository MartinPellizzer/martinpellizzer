Herpyza grandiflora var. grandiflora

Herpyza grandiflora var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered herpyza, is a flowering plant characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences.

It typically features bright yellow or orange petals with a distinctive, elongated shape. The plant is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
