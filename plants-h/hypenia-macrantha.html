Hypenia macrantha

Hypenia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered hypenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to orange flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.

Hypenia macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
