Hieracium vollmannii subsp. vollmannii

Hieracium vollmannii subsp.

vollmannii, commonly known as the Vollmann's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosettes of leaves.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and disturbed soils. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it produces seeds in a disc-like structure surrounded by bracts.

This subspecies is often found in the central and western United States.
