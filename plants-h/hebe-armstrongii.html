Hebe armstrongii

Hebe armstrongii, commonly known as Armstrong's hebe, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces clusters of tubular, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Hebe armstrongii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
