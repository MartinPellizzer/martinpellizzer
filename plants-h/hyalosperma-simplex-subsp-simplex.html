Hyalosperma simplex subsp. simplex

Hyalosperma simplex subsp.

simplex, commonly known as the slender glassy grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant produces tiny, wind-pollinated flowers that are typically green or pale in color. It is native to Australia and thrives in dry, sandy soils.
