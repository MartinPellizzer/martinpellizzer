Hypericum × persistens

Hypericum × persistens, commonly known as everlastings, is a hybrid shrub that combines characteristics of several Hypericum species.

It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun. The plant is noted for its long-lasting, bright yellow flowers that remain on the stems for weeks, making it a popular choice for dried floral arrangements. Its foliage is typically dark green and slightly hairy, adding texture to garden landscapes.

Hypericum × persistens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its resilience to various environmental conditions.
