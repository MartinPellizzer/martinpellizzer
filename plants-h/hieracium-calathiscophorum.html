Hieracium calathiscophorum

Hieracium calathiscophorum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in many areas.
