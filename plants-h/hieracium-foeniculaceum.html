Hieracium foeniculaceum

Hieracium foeniculaceum, commonly known as the dandelion hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and serrated, hairy leaves. Native to Europe, it has been introduced to other parts of the world, including North America. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
