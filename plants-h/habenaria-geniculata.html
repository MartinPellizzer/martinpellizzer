Habenaria geniculata

Habenaria geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with a subtle fragrance, and they bloom during the warmer months.

Habenaria geniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
