Hieracium creperiforme

Hieracium creperiforme, commonly known as the crepe-shaped hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from June to August, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitats.
