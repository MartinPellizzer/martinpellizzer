Hydrangea × amagiana

Hydrangea × amagiana, commonly known as the Panicle hydrangea, is a hybrid shrub native to Japan.

It features showy panicle inflorescences that change color depending on soil pH, ranging from pink to blue. The plant produces large, cone-shaped flower clusters that bloom in late summer. Its foliage is dark green and provides ornamental value throughout the growing season.

It is adaptable to various soil types and thrives in partial to full sun.
