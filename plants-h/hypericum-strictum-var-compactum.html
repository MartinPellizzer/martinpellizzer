Hypericum strictum var. compactum

Hypericum strictum var. compactum, commonly known as the compact St. John's wort, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in dense clusters. The plant has narrow, evergreen leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for small spaces and container gardening.
