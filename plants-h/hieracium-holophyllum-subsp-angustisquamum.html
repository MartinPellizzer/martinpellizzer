Hieracium holophyllum subsp. angustisquamum

Hieracium holophyllum subsp.

angustisquamum, commonly known as the narrow-scale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and yellow flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine, hooked hairs, which aid in water retention.

It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh alpine environments.
