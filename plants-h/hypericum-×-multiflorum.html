Hypericum × multiflorum

Hypericum × multiflorum, commonly known as the hybrid St. John's wort, is a flowering plant in the family Hypericaceae.

It is a cross between Hypericum perforatum (common St. John's wort) and Hypericum patulum (woodland St. John's wort). The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and opposite, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
