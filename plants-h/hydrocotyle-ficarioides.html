Hydrocotyle ficarioides

Hydrocotyle ficarioides, commonly known as the water pennywort, is a perennial aquatic plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It has small, rounded leaves that are often floating or semi-submerged, with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Hydrocotyle ficarioides is often used in water gardens and wetland restoration projects due to its ability to improve water quality.
