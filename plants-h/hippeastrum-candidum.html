Hippeastrum candidum

Hippeastrum candidum, commonly known as the white hurricane lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green and grow from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce multiple flower stems from a single bulb.
