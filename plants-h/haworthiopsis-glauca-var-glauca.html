Haworthiopsis glauca var. glauca

Haworthiopsis glauca var. glauca, commonly known as the Blue Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
