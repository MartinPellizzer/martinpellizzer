Hieracium dollineri subsp. pseudofucatum

Hieracium dollineri subsp.

pseudofucatum, commonly known as the false fucate hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has basal leaves that are lanceolate and often covered with fine hairs.

Its subspecies status highlights its distinct morphological features compared to other Hieracium species.
