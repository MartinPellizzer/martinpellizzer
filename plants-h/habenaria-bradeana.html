Habenaria bradeana

Habenaria bradeana, commonly known as the Brade's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinct, fragrant scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicately endangered species.
