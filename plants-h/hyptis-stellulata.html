Hyptis stellulata

Hyptis stellulata, commonly known as silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Hyptis stellulata is also valued for its medicinal properties, used traditionally in some cultures for its purported health benefits.
