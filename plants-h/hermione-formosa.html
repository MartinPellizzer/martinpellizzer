Hermione formosa

Hermione formosa, commonly known as the beautiful Hermione, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often variegated with shades of green and white. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the spring and early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
