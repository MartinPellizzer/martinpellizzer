Hermannia prismatocarpa

Hermannia prismatocarpa, commonly known as the purple-veined sturt's onions, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of fleshy, gray-green leaves with distinctive purple veins. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is drought-tolerant.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and arid landscapes.
