Helianthemum hirtum var. macranthum

Helianthemum hirtum var. macranthum, commonly known as the woolly stonecrop, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features dense, woolly foliage that is typically gray-green in color, providing a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
