Hedyotis ciliicaulis

Hedyotis ciliicaulis, commonly known as the slender bladdernut, is a perennial herb native to regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy margins, and small, white or yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

Hedyotis ciliicaulis is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
