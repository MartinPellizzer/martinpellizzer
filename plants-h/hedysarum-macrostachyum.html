Hedysarum macrostachyum

Hedysarum macrostachyum, commonly known as the large-stemmed vetch, is a perennial legume native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces long, cylindrical pods containing small, rounded seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Hedysarum macrostachyum plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
