Hieracium cernyi

Hieracium cernyi, commonly known as the bent yellow hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers and is typically found in grassy areas and open woodlands. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It produces small, elongated achenes that are dispersed by wind.

Hieracium cernyi is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
