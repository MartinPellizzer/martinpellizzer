Horsfieldia reticulata

Horsfieldia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, net-like venation on its leaves, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Horsfieldia reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
