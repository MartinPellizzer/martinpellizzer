Hymenocallis speciosa

Hymenocallis speciosa, commonly known as the yellow hurricane lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bulbous underground stems called corms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
