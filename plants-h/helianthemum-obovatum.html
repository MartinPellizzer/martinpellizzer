Helianthemum obovatum

Helianthemum obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed sandwort, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are oval in shape, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helianthemum obovatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
