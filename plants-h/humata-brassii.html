Humata brassii

Humata brassii, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Humata brassii is a fast-growing species that can reach heights of up to 3 meters.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
