Heterospathe delicatula

Heterospathe delicatula, commonly known as the delicate feather fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a delicate and airy appearance. The plant prefers shady, humid environments and thrives in well-draining, moist soil. Its leaves are typically light green and have a soft texture, making it an ornamental choice for gardens and indoor spaces.

Heterospathe delicatula is often used in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
