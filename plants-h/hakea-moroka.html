Hakea moroka

Hakea moroka, commonly known as the Moroka hakea, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces distinctive yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Hakea moroka is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
