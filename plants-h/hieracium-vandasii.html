Hieracium vandasii

Hieracium vandasii, commonly known as the Vanda's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branched.

Hieracium vandasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
