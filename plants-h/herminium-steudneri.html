Herminium steudneri

Herminium steudneri, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and a dense, spiral arrangement of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer.

Herminium steudneri is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its graceful appearance.
