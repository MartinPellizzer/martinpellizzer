Humularia corbisieri

Humularia corbisieri, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, night-blooming flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

The flowers open in the evening and close by morning, making it a unique addition to gardens.
