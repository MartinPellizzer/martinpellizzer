Haplopteris centrochinensis

Haplopteris centrochinensis, commonly known as the Chinese rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

Haplopteris centrochinensis is native to Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
