Habenaria falcicornis subsp. caffra

Habenaria falcicornis subsp.

caffra, commonly known as the Cape butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa. It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale pink or white with a distinctive, spurred lip.

It is a perennial herb that produces a single, slender stem with several leaves and a cluster of small, fragrant blossoms.
