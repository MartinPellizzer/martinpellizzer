Hedysarum qinggilense

Hedysarum qinggilense, commonly known as the Qinggiling milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its seeds are also a source of protein and can be used as forage for livestock.
