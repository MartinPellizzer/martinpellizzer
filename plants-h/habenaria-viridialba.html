Habenaria viridialba

Habenaria viridialba, commonly known as the green-veined orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with prominent green veins. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and marshes. The leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Its blooming period occurs during the warmer months, adding a unique ornamental value to its habitat.
