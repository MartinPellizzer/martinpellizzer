Hymenophyllum fimbriatum

Hymenophyllum fimbriatum, commonly known as the fringed filmy fern, is a small, delicate epiphytic fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, almost translucent fronds. The plant's leaves are typically narrow and have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in humid environments. It thrives in shaded, moist habitats such as forest floors and rock faces.

Its unique morphology and specialized adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
