Hieracium levicaule subsp. subeuronotum

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

subeuronotum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and yellow composite flowers that consist of ray florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in moist or sandy soils.
