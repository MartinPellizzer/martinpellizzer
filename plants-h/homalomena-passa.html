Homalomena passa

Homalomena passa, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically green with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white or pale yellow, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
