Halimus mesembrianthemoides

Halimus mesembrianthemoides, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically during the cooler months. It is adapted to full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
