Helichrysum bullulatum

Helichrysum bullulatum, commonly known as the golden crown, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with bright yellow, sun-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has aromatic, narrow, needle-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, hardy plants.
