Heliotropium tenue

Heliotropium tenue, commonly known as the slender heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are often hairy in texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed habitats.

Heliotropium tenue is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
