Haworthia adelaidensis

Haworthia adelaidensis, commonly known as the Adelaidensis Haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
