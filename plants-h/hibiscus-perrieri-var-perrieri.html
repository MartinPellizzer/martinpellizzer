Hibiscus perrieri var. perrieri

Hibiscus perrieri var. perrieri, commonly known as the Perrieri hibiscus, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Hibiscus perrieri var. perrieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
