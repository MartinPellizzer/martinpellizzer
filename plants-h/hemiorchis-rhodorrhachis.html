Hemiorchis rhodorrhachis

Hemiorchis rhodorrhachis, commonly known as the pink-root orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique pinkish-red roots and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive pink and white coloration. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
