Hemigraphis longipedunculata

Hemigraphis longipedunculata, commonly known as the silver velvet plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green foliage with prominent veins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, adding visual interest. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
