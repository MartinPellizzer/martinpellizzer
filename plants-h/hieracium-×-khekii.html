Hieracium × khekii

Hieracium × khekii, commonly known as the hybrid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and flowering stems that bear yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
