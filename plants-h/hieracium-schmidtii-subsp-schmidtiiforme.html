Hieracium schmidtii subsp. schmidtiiforme

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

schmidtiiforme, commonly known as the Schmidt's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, herbaceous stems.
