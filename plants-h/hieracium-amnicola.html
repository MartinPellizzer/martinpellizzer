Hieracium amnicola

Hieracium amnicola, commonly known as the river hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium amnicola plays a role in supporting local insect populations and is often found in riparian zones.
