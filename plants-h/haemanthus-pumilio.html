Haemanthus pumilio

Haemanthus pumilio, commonly known as the dwarf haemanthus, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking red and white flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice among succulent and bulb lovers.
