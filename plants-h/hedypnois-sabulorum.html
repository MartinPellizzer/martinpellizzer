Hedypnois sabulorum

Hedypnois sabulorum, commonly known as the sand dune plant, is a species of flowering plant native to coastal regions.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dune ecosystems.

Hedypnois sabulorum plays a vital role in stabilizing sand dunes and preventing erosion.
