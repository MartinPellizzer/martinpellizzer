Hebenstretia chamaedrifolia

Hebenstretia chamaedrifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves that are often adorned with silver or white markings. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it both aesthetically pleasing and relatively easy to care for.
