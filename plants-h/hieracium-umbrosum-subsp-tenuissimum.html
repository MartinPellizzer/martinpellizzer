Hieracium umbrosum subsp. tenuissimum

Hieracium umbrosum subsp.

tenuissimum, commonly known as the slender meadow hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and shaded woodlands, preferring cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinct appearance.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
