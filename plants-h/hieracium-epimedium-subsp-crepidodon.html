Hieracium epimedium subsp. crepidodon

Hieracium epimedium subsp.

crepidodon, commonly known as the hairy yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its leaf morphology, which features a unique combination of lobes and hairs. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is often found in botanical gardens and natural habitats.
