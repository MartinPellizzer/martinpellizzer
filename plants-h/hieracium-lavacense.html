Hieracium lavacense

Hieracium lavacense, commonly known as the Lavacense hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils. The plant has hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks.
