Habenaria pickelii

Habenaria pickelii, commonly known as the Pickel's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
