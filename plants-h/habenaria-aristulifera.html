Habenaria aristulifera

Habenaria aristulifera, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive hooded labellum.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
