Hypericum olympicum var. tenuifolium

Hypericum olympicum var. tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are linear and narrow, giving the plant a delicate, grass-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and its historical use in herbal medicine.
