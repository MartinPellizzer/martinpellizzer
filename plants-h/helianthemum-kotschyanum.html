Helianthemum kotschyanum

Helianthemum kotschyanum, commonly known as the Kotschyan's rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the rocky areas of the eastern Mediterranean.

It features small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, typically in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand arid and sunny conditions. It is often found in limestone soils and thrives in well-drained environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
