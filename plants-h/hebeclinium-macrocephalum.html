Hebeclinium macrocephalum

Hebeclinium macrocephalum, commonly known as the giant hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers a shady to partially sunny environment. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Hebeclinium macrocephalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
