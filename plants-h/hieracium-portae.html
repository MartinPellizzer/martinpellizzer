Hieracium portae

Hieracium portae, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, with stems that are usually upright and slightly hairy as well. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium portae is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
