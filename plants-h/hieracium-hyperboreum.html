Hieracium hyperboreum

Hieracium hyperboreum, commonly known as the Arctic hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environments. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Hieracium hyperboreum plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats and is an indicator species for alpine and arctic ecosystems.
