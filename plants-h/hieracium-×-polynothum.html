Hieracium × polynothum

Hieracium × polynothum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which typically bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stem is often hairy as well. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, including North America.

Hieracium × polynothum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats such as meadows and roadsides.
