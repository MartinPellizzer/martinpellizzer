Hedysarum nummulariifolium

Hedysarum nummulariifolium, commonly known as the round-leaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb with slender, creeping stems that often form dense mats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, with rounded leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance.

Hedysarum nummulariifolium is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy species in challenging conditions.
