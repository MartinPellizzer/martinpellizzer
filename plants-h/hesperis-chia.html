Hesperis chia

Hesperis chia, commonly known as honeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered together, giving it a delicate appearance. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in dry conditions.

Hesperis chia is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive flowers and fragrant foliage.
