Habenaria castroi

Habenaria castroi, commonly known as the Castroi orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, or purple. The plant has a complex root system adapted for absorbing nutrients from the surrounding soil. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Habenaria castroi is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
