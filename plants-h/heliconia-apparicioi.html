Heliconia apparicioi

Heliconia apparicioi, commonly known as the pink heliconia, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Heliconiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, boat-shaped inflorescences. The plant features large, waxy, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish underside. Its flowers are clustered in a spiral arrangement, with bright pink bracts that enclose the actual flowers.

Heliconia apparicioi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
