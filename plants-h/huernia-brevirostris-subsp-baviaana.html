Huernia brevirostris subsp. baviaana

Huernia brevirostris subsp.

baviaana, commonly known as the Baviaan horned cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that are often covered in white to yellowish spines.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems.

This subspecies is prized in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
