Hosta japonica f. albomarginata

Hosta japonica f. albomarginata, commonly known as the Japanese Hosta with white margins, is a varietal form of the Japanese hosta.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are predominantly dark green with distinctive white or pale yellow margins. The plant is a perennial, rhizomatous herbaceous plant native to Japan. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking leaf coloration and its ability to add texture to shaded garden spaces.
