Helichrysum Pomelianum

Helichrysum pomelianum is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa known for its vibrant yellow-orange flowers and silvery-gray foliage. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance have earned it a place in both ornamental horticulture and traditional medicine. The plant's name, meaning "sun disk," reflects its radiant, sun-like blooms that remain colorful even after flowering. With its resilience and beauty, Helichrysum pomelianum is a standout addition to any garden.
