Heteronoma ciliatum

Heteronoma ciliatum, commonly known as the hairy leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant has hairy leaves and stems, which help reduce water loss in its arid habitat. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.
