Henriettella odorata

Henriettella odorata, commonly known as the sweet-scented bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of violet and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Henriettella odorata is valued for its ornamental beauty and unique aromatic properties.
