Haworthia planifolia f. calochlora

Haworthia planifolia f. calochlora, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, translucent, waxy leaves with distinct white or light green markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant is characterized by its compact size and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect light and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its appeal among succulent enthusiasts.
