Hypericum lalandii

Hypericum lalandii, commonly known as the Laland's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features bright yellow flowers with prominent stamens, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are oval-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Hypericum lalandii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
