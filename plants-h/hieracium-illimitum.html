Hieracium illimitum

Hieracium illimitum, commonly known as the endless dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow, dandelion-like flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Hieracium illimitum is known for its ability to spread rapidly through its extensive root system.
