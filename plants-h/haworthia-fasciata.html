Haworthia fasciata

Haworthia fasciata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are marked with distinctive white or yellow stripes. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect sunlight and can be grown both indoors and outdoors in suitable climates.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container plantings.
