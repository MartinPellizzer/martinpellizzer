Hosta sieboldiana var. yakusimensis

Hosta sieboldiana var. yakusimensis, commonly known as the Yakusimensis Hosta, is a compact, rhizomatous perennial native to Japan.

It features broad, dark green, waxy leaves that are often tinged with silver or white veins, giving them a striking appearance. The plant produces upright, slender flower stalks with small, pale blue to lavender flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shaded garden beds and container planting.
