Hypericum borbasii

Hypericum borbasii, commonly known as the Borbas' St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in clusters along the stems. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Hypericum borbasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
