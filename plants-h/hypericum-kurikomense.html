Hypericum kurikomense

Hypericum kurikomense, commonly known as the Kurikomense St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and holly-like leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Hypericum kurikomense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
