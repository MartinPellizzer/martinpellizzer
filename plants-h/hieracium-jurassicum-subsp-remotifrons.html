Hieracium jurassicum subsp. remotifrons

Hieracium jurassicum subsp.

remotifrons, commonly known as the Jurassic hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender, elongated leaves and a tendency to form dense mats on the ground. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a relatively rare and localized species.
