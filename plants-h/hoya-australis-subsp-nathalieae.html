Hoya australis subsp. nathalieae

Hoya australis subsp.

nathalieae, commonly known as the Natalie's Waxplant, is a tropical vine native to Australia. It features waxy, ovate leaves that are typically light green with a slight variegation.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range from white to pale pink. It is a climbing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
