Hieracium iberomaculatum

Hieracium iberomaculatum, commonly known as the Iberian dandelion, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It features a basal rosette of leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive yellowish-green color.

Its flowers are bright yellow, with ray florets that bloom in the spring and early summer.
