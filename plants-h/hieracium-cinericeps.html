Hieracium cinericeps

Hieracium cinericeps, commonly known as the ash-gray hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its grayish-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in disturbed soils.

Its foliage and flowers are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance.
