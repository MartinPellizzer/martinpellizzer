Hemizonia pungens subsp. laevis

Hemizonia pungens subsp.

laevis, commonly known as the smooth hemizonia, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of South Africa. It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems and leaves that are covered in a fine, smooth texture.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is occasionally cultivated in arid gardens.
