Halimium ocymoides var. erectum

Halimium ocymoides var. erectum, commonly known as the upright halimium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, hairy leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for horticultural use.

It is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
