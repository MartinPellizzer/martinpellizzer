Helichrysum collinum

Helichrysum collinum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even when dried. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, narrow leaves are arranged in a compact, bushy form, and it is often used in ornamental gardening.

Helichrysum collinum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
