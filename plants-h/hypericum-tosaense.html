Hypericum tosaense

Hypericum tosaense, commonly known as Tosa St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum tosaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
