Hedysarum kudrjaschevii

Hedysarum kudrjaschevii, commonly known as the Kudrjaschev's milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in poor soil and low moisture conditions.

Hedysarum kudrjaschevii is also valued for its potential in soil stabilization and as a forage plant.
