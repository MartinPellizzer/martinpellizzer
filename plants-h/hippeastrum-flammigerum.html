Hippeastrum flammigerum

Hippeastrum flammigerum, commonly known as the Flame Spider Lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often with a distinctive white or yellow throat. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are broad and waxy, typical of the Amaryllidaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
