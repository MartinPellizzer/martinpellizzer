Helichrysum bojerianum

Helichrysum bojerianum, commonly known as the Bojer's everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange-colored flower heads. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are tightly packed into a composite head, giving it a daisy-like appearance. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

The foliage is aromatic and can be used in traditional herbal remedies.
