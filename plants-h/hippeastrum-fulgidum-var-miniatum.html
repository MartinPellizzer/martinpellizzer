Hippeastrum fulgidum var. miniatum

Hippeastrum fulgidum var. miniatum, commonly known as the miniature hurricane lily, is a compact, bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a contrasting dark throat. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, showcasing its vibrant colors against lush green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a bold yet compact ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements contribute to its appeal in both residential and public landscapes.
