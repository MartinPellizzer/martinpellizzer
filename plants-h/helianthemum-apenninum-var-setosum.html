Helianthemum apenninum var. setosum

Helianthemum apenninum var. setosum, commonly known as the hairy rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, typically on short, branched stems. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found on rocky or sandy slopes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in garden settings.
