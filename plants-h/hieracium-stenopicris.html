Hieracium stenopicris

Hieracium stenopicris, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and habitat biodiversity.
