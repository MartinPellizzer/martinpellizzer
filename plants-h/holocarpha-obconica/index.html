Holocarpha Obconica

Holocarpha obconica is a striking annual plant native to arid regions of the Americas. It is known for its bright yellow flowers and distinctive spiny seed pods that catch the wind. This plant thrives in hot, dry climates and is often found in disturbed soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening. Holocarpha obconica plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
