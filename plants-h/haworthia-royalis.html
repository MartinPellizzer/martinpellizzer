Haworthia royalis

Haworthia royalis, commonly known as the Zulu beauty, is a species of succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, making it well-suited for arid environments. It produces small, white flowers on tall stalks during the summer months.

This plant is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
