Hydrangea japonica f. hortensia

Hydrangea japonica f. hortensia, commonly known as the bigleaf hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from pink to blue depending on soil pH. The plant produces broad, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

This variety is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the summer.
