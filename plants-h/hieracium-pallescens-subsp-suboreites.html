Hieracium pallescens subsp. suboreites

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

suboreites, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a rough texture, and its flowers are yellow, often with a central disk.

It is generally a low-growing plant, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
