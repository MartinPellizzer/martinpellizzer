Hieracium froelichianum subsp. beauverdianum

Hieracium froelichianum subsp.

beauverdianum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers with ray florets.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks in temperate regions. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain areas.
