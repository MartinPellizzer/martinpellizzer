Hyphaene ventricosa

Hyphaene ventricosa, commonly known as the double-armed palm, is a species of palm native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive, branching stems that form a bushy appearance, and its leaves are pinnate with long, narrow segments. The plant produces yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits that are edible.

Hyphaene ventricosa is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky areas with poor soil.
