Hieracium brachycomum subsp. hypomnoon

Hieracium brachycomum subsp.

hypomnoon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and woodland edges, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy surface, while the flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is known for its ornamental value and has been introduced to various regions outside its native range.
