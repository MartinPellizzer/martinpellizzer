Hibiscus liebrechtsianus

Hibiscus liebrechtsianus, commonly known as the liebrechtsian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with broad, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Hibiscus liebrechtsianus is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
