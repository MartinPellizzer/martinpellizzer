Hexalobus bussei

Hexalobus bussei, commonly known as the African violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Hexalobus bussei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
