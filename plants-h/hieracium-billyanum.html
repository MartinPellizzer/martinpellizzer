Hieracium billyanum

Hieracium billyanum, commonly known as the Billy's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has basal leaves that are often woolly and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branched. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Hieracium billyanum is characterized by its ability to thrive in harsh, high-altitude environments.
