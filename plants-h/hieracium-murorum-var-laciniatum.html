Hieracium murorum var. laciniatum

Hieracium murorum var. laciniatum, commonly known as the cut-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, dissected leaves that give it a fern-like appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.
