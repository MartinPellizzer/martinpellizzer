Hieracium lanuliceps

Hieracium lanuliceps, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a central yellow disk surrounded by white to pale pink ray florets, giving it a daisy-like appearance. Its leaves are typically hairy and lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hieracium lanuliceps often grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils.
