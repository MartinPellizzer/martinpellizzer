Hieracium dipterum

Hieracium dipterum, commonly known as the two-winged hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are broadly ovate with hairy surfaces and are often lobed.

Hieracium dipterum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in many regions.
