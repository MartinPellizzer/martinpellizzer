Hieracium pseudoviride

Hieracium pseudoviride, commonly known as the green weed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright green leaves and yellow flower heads, which consist of numerous small florets. Native to Europe, it is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has a sprawling growth habit and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

It is considered a common weed in agricultural and garden settings.
