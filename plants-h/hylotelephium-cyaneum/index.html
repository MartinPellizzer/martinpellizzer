Hylotelephium Cyaneum

Hylotelephium cyaneum is a striking perennial plant known for its vibrant purple and pink flower clusters. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. This plant is a member of the Crassulaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its foliage is dense and evergreen, providing year-round interest in gardens. With its bold colors and resilience, it has become a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
