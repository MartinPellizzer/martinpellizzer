Hechtia tillandsioides

Hechtia tillandsioides, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a perennial plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
