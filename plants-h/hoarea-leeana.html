Hoarea leeana

Hoarea leeana, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Hoarea leeana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
