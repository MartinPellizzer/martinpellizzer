Helicteres lhotzkyana var. tomentosa

Helicteres lhotzkyana var. tomentosa, commonly known as the tomentose helicteres, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
