Hibiscus fugosioides

Hibiscus fugosioides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and velvety in texture. Hibiscus fugosioides thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
