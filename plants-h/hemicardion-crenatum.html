Hemicardion crenatum

Hemicardion crenatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hemicarion, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves with a serrated edge, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hemicardion crenatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
