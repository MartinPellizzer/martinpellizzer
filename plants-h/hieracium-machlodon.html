Hieracium machlodon

Hieracium machlodon, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, including North America.

Hieracium machlodon is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can spread rapidly through its extensive root system.
