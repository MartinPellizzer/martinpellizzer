Hieracium modestum

Hieracium modestum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across temperate regions. It has a fibrous root system and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Hieracium modestum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to spread rapidly.
