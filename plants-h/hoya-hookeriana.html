Hoya hookeriana

Hoya hookeriana, commonly known as the Hooker's waxplant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asclepiadaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia, and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, heart-shaped leaves. Hoya hookeriana is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
