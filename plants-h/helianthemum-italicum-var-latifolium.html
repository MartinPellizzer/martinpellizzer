Helianthemum italicum var. latifolium

Helianthemum italicum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has dense, evergreen foliage with thick, leathery leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance gardens.
