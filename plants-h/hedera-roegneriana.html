Hedera roegneriana

Hedera roegneriana, commonly known as the Chinese ivy, is a climbing evergreen vine native to eastern Asia.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
