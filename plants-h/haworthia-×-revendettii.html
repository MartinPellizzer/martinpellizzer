Haworthia × revendettii

Haworthia × revendettii, commonly known as the Revendettii Haworthia, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or pink markings. The plant is noted for its ability to thrive in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
