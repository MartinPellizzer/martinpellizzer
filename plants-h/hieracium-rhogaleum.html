Hieracium rhogaleum

Hieracium rhogaleum, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium rhogaleum is often found in meadows and along stream banks, where it thrives in sunny conditions.
