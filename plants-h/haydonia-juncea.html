Haydonia juncea

Haydonia juncea, commonly known as the narrow-leafed broom.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, belonging to the Fabaceae family. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, narrow leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Haydonia juncea is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
