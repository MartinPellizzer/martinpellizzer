Hepetis alborubra

Hepetis alborubra, commonly known as the red-stemmed ironweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that are often reddish in color, especially when mature. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hepetis alborubra is often found in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and along stream banks.
