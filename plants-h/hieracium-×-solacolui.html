Hieracium × solacolui

Hieracium × solacolui, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically daisy-like in appearance. The plant produces a basal rosette of leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.

Due to its invasive nature in some regions, it is often considered a weed in agricultural and natural settings.
