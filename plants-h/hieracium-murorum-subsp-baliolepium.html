Hieracium murorum subsp. baliolepium

Hieracium murorum subsp.

baliolepium, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow composite flowers and hairy, basal leaves.

This subspecies is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions, growing in rocky or grassy habitats. It has a compact growth form and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

The plant is often seen as a pioneer species in disturbed or barren areas of mountainous regions.
