Helianthemum montanum var. montserratense

Helianthemum montanum var. montserratense, commonly known as the Montserrat sandverbena, is a low-growing shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, typically from June to August. The plant has woody stems covered in fine, white hairs, and its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and covered in a similar hairy texture. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to harsh environments and its role in local ecosystems.
