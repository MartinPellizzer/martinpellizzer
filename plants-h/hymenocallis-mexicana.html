Hymenocallis mexicana

Hymenocallis mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican hurricane lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Mexico.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow center and white petals. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates. Its blooming period typically occurs in the summer months, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

Hymenocallis mexicana is also valued for its ornamental beauty and resilience in various environmental conditions.
