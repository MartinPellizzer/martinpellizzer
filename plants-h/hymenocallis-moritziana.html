Hymenocallis moritziana

Hymenocallis moritziana, commonly known as the Moritz's spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and glossy. It produces bulbous underground stems, typical of many amaryllid species.

Hymenocallis moritziana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
