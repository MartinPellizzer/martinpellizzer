Hieracium tubulascens subsp. reclusum

Hieracium tubulascens subsp.

reclusum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America. It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, prairies, and disturbed soils.

The plant features upright stems with alternate, heart-shaped to lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head, with both ray and disk florets.

It is characterized by its slender growth habit and is often found in moist to dry environments.
