Hedyotis lychnidifolia

Hedyotis lychnidifolia, commonly known as flame vine, is a perennial flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright orange-red flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Hedyotis lychnidifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
