Hieracium capillatum

Hieracium capillatum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium capillatum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
