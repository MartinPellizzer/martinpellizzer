Helianthemum polygalifolium

Helianthemum polygalifolium, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are succulent and have a distinctive, lobed shape.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
