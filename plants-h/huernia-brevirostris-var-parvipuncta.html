Huernia brevirostris var. parvipuncta

Huernia brevirostris var. parvipuncta, commonly known as the dwarf punctuated horned lithops, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy, and ribbed stems that resemble small horns, which help it store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically green with a subtle speckling pattern, giving it a unique appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil to thrive.
