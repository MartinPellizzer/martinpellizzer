Horvatia

Horvatia, commonly known as the Croatian lily, is a rare and endangered flowering plant native to the Adriatic region of Croatia.

It is characterized by its striking white and pink petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in moist, shaded environments near streams and forests. Horvatia is listed on the IUCN Red List due to habitat loss and over-collection.

Conservation efforts are underway to protect this unique botanical species.
