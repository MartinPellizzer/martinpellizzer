Hieracium coldei

Hieracium coldei, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium coldei is known for its ability to tolerate harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in high-altitude ecosystems.
