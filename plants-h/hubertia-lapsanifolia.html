Hubertia lapsanifolia

Hubertia lapsanifolia, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to survive in hot, dry environments, storing water in its thick stems and leaves. Its blooming period typically occurs during the rainy season, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

Hubertia lapsanifolia is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
