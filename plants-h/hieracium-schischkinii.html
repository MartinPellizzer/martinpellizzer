Hieracium schischkinii

Hieracium schischkinii, commonly known as Schischkin's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and flowering heads that produce small, yellowish flowers. Native to Central Asia, it thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
