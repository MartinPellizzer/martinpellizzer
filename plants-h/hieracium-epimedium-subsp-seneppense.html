Hieracium epimedium subsp. seneppense

Hieracium epimedium subsp.

seneppense, commonly known as the Seneppense Hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are broadly lance-shaped and can reach up to 20 centimeters in length.

This subspecies is often found in disturbed habitats and can sometimes be invasive in certain regions.
