Hypochaeris urens

Hypochaeris urens, commonly known as common mouse-ear chickweed, is a low-growing annual plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters and are typically less than 1 centimeter in diameter. The plant has hairy, spoon-shaped leaves and a sprawling growth habit, often forming dense mats on the ground. It is commonly found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in meadows.

Hypochaeris urens is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
