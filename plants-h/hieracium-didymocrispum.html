Hieracium didymocrispum

Hieracium didymocrispum, commonly known as the two-lobed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Hieracium didymocrispum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
