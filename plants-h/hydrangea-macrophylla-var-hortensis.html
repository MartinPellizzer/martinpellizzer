Hydrangea macrophylla var. hortensis

Hydrangea macrophylla var. hortensis, commonly known as the panicle hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink, red, or blue depending on soil pH. The plant thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes. Its foliage turns vibrant shades of red or orange in the fall, adding seasonal interest.

The variety is known for its hardiness and adaptability to various climates.
