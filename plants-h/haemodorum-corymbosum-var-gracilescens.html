Haemodorum corymbosum var. gracilescens

Haemodorum corymbosum var. gracilescens, commonly known as the slender red lily, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a perennial herb with slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking red or orange-red flowers in late winter to early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or heathland environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
