Hypericum gnidioides

Hypericum gnidioides, commonly known as the Spanish St. John's wort, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is part of the Hypericaceae family, which includes other species of St. John's wort.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to dry, sunny environments.
