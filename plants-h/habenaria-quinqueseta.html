Habenaria quinqueseta

Habenaria quinqueseta, commonly known as the five-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with several elliptical leaves arranged along its length. The plant produces a single raceme of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with distinctive five-lobed petals and sepals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
