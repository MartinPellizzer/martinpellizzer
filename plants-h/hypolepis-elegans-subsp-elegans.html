Hypolepis elegans subsp. elegans

Hypolepis elegans subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the elegant water fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family. It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, frond-like leaves that are typically bipinnate.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are extensively branched and help in its propagation.

The fronds are usually dark green and can be used in ornamental gardening due to their aesthetic appeal.
