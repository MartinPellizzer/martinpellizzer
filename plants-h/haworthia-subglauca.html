Haworthia subglauca

Haworthia subglauca, commonly known as the Blue Haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for dry environments.

Haworthia subglauca is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
