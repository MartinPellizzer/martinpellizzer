Hemicarex filicina

Hemicarex filicina, commonly known as the slender sedges, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flower heads. This plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. Hemicarex filicina is often found in temperate regions of North America and parts of Asia.
