Hypericum dogonbadanicum

Hypericum dogonbadanicum, commonly known as the Dogonbadan St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in shrubland and woodland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum dogonbadanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
