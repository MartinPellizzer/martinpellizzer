Helenium scorzonerifolium var. ghiesbrechtii

Helenium scorzonerifolium var. ghiesbrechtii, commonly known as the Ghiesbrecht's sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow-orange ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a valuable addition to native gardens.
