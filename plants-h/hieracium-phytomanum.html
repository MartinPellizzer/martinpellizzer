Hieracium phytomanum

Hieracium phytomanum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, often reddish stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium phytomanum is an annual or biennial herb that can form dense colonies, sometimes becoming invasive in certain regions.
