Huernia decemdentata

Huernia decemdentata, commonly known as the starry moon cactus, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
