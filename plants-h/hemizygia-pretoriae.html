Hemizygia pretoriae

Hemizygia pretoriae, commonly known as the Pretoria bellflower, is a South African plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tubular, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hemizygia pretoriae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
