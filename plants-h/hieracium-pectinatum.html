Hieracium pectinatum

Hieracium pectinatum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in dense, flat-topped clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or serrated. Native to Europe, it can be found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates.
