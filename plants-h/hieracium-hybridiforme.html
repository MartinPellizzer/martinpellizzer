Hieracium hybridiforme

Hieracium hybridiforme, commonly known as the hybrid groundsel, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its yellow to orange flower heads and rough, hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its flowers are composed of ray and disk florets, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is an annual or biennial plant that produces seeds through wind dispersal.
