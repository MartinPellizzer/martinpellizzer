Habenaria pauciflora var. pluriflora

Habenaria pauciflora var. pluriflora, commonly known as the many-flowered helmet orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shady, moist forest habitats, often near the base of trees. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are arranged in a spike-like inflorescence.

This orchid is noted for its relatively compact size and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
