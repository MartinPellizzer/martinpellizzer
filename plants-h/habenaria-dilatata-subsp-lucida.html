Habenaria dilatata subsp. lucida

Habenaria dilatata subsp.

lucida, commonly known as the shining orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands.

The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its flowers are small, pale green to white, and have a distinctive, spurred lip.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its delicate appearance.
