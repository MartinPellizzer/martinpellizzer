Hedyotis schlechtendahliana subsp. remyi

Hedyotis schlechtendahliana subsp.

remyi, commonly known as the slender bladdernut, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks.

The plant features small, bright yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
