Hieracium tenebricans

Hieracium tenebricans, commonly known as the shadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are usually yellow or orange in color.

Hieracium tenebricans is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
