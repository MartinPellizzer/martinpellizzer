Helichrysum multinerve

Helichrysum multinerve, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, golden-yellow flowers that resemble daisies. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Helichrysum multinerve is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
