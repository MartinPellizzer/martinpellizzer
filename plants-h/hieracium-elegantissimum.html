Hieracium elegantissimum

Hieracium elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs.

Hieracium elegantissimum is often found in meadows and grassy areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
