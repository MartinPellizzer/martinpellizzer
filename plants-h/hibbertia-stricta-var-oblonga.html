Hibbertia stricta var. oblonga

Hibbertia stricta var. oblonga, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hibbertia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with prominent, showy stamens, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
