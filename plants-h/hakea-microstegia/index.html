Hakea Microstegia

Hakea microstegia is a unique and striking plant native to western Australia. It is known for its distinctive appearance, featuring bright green leaves and striking red flowers. This species thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it an important part of the local ecosystem. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to many plant enthusiasts outside its native region.
