Hieracium tardiusculum subsp. tenue

Hieracium tardiusculum subsp.

tenue, commonly known as slender late hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

This subspecies is notable for its small, clustered inflorescences and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
