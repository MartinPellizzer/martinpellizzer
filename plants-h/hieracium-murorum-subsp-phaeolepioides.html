Hieracium murorum subsp. phaeolepioides

Hieracium murorum subsp.

phaeolepioides, commonly known as the pale-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers.

The leaves are typically pale green and have a rough, hairy texture. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

This subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology compared to other members of the Hieracium murorum complex.
