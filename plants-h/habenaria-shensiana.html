Habenaria shensiana

Habenaria shensiana, commonly known as the Shenzhen orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers with distinctive purple markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
