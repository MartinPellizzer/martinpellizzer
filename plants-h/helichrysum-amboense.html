Helichrysum amboense

Helichrysum amboense, commonly known as the African everlasting or yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain intact even after flowering, giving it a long-lasting appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, needle-like leaves are arranged in a compact, bushy form, and it is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Helichrysum amboense is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
