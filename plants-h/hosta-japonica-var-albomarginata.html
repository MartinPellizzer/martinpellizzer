Hosta japonica var. albomarginata

Hosta japonica var. albomarginata, commonly known as the white-edged Japanese hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with distinctive white or light green margins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in late summer, usually in shades of blue or white. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and is commonly used in shaded garden landscapes.
