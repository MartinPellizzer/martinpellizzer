Hypericum yunnanense

Hypericum yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has woody stems covered in small, glandular dots. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum yunnanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
