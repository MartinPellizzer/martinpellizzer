Himantoglossum adriaticum

Himantoglossum adriaticum, commonly known as the Adriatic helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive hooded lip. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in moist, shaded woodland habitats. The plant produces small, dark brown seeds that are dispersed by wind.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
