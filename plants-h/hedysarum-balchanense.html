Hedysarum balchanense

Hedysarum balchanense, commonly known as the Balkan vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow or orange pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. Its seed pods are typically slender and contain several small, rounded seeds.

Hedysarum balchanense is often found in open woodlands and along rocky slopes, thriving in well-drained soils.
