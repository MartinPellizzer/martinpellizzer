Hieracium casparyanum

Hieracium casparyanum, commonly known as the Casparyan hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It features bright yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of disk florets.

Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.
