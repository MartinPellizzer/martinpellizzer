Herbertia tigridioides

Herbertia tigridioides, commonly known as the tiger lily, is a perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that are often variegated with white or green markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Herbertia tigridioides is known for its ability to bloom reliably in USDA hardiness zones 6 to 9.
