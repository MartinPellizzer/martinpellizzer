Hieracium plexicorde

Hieracium plexicorde, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped leaves and its clusters of yellow to orange daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Hieracium plexicorde is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
