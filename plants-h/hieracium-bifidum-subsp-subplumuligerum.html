Hieracium bifidum subsp. subplumuligerum

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

subplumuligerum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, preferring moist to moderately dry conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

This subspecies is distinguished by its smaller, more delicate leaves compared to other hawkweed species.
