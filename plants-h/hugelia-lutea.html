Hugelia lutea

Hugelia lutea, commonly known as the yellow honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Hugelia lutea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fragrant blooms.
