Hyospathe maculata

Hyospathe maculata, commonly known as the spotted hyospathe, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful spathe that often features distinctive spots or markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers within a spadix, which is typical of the Araceae family. Its leaves are typically broad and arrow-shaped, with a smooth or slightly textured surface.

Hyospathe maculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates due to its vibrant appearance.
