Hieracium mougeotii subsp. conocerinthe

Hieracium mougeotii subsp.

conocerinthe, commonly known as the Mexican hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowering heads.

The plant typically features basal rosettes of narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces composite flowers that are arranged in a cluster. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
