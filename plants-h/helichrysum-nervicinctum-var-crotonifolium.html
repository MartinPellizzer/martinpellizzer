Helichrysum nervicinctum var. crotonifolium

Helichrysum nervicinctum var. crotonifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf everlasting or narrow-leaf helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and bright yellow to orange flowers that resemble daisies. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its flowers are also used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature.
