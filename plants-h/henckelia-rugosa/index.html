Henckelia Rugosa

Henckelia rugosa is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its striking, velvety leaves that display a rich, deep green color. This plant thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its delicate flowers, often in shades of white and pink, bloom during the warmer months. Due to its rarity and ornamental value, Henckelia rugosa is a sought-after species among plant enthusiasts.
