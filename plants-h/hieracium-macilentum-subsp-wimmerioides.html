Hieracium macilentum subsp. wimmerioides

Hieracium macilentum subsp.

wimmerioides, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with slender stems and small, yellowish flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters.

The leaves are typically linear to lanceolate, often with a hairy surface. It is native to Central Europe and thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

This subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different morphology compared to other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
