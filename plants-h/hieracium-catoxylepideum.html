Hieracium catoxylepideum

Hieracium catoxylepideum, commonly known as the Catoxylepideum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

The species is noted for its robust growth and adaptability to different environmental conditions.
