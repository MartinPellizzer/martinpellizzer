Hieracium sericophyllum subsp. olenium

Hieracium sericophyllum subsp.

olenium, commonly known as the silverleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its finely hairy, silver-green leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. It has a rosette growth form with basal leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine white hairs.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
