Hesperis cilicica

Hesperis cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician honesty, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many plants with small, four-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is characterized by its delicate, white or pale pink flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.
