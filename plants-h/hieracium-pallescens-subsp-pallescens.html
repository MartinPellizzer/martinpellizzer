Hieracium pallescens subsp. pallescens

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

pallescens, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are usually gray-green in color, with hairy surfaces. It produces yellow flowers in early summer, arranged in a single head on a slender stem.

The species is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in disturbed or nutrient-poor soils.
