Hieracium nitidum var. siluriense

Hieracium nitidum var. siluriense, commonly known as Silurian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single head. The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in moist, calcareous soils. It is a perennial species that thrives in open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical features make it distinctive within the Hieracium genus.
