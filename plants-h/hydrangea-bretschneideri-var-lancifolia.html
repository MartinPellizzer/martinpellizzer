Hydrangea bretschneideri var. lancifolia

Hydrangea bretschneideri var. lancifolia, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea or mountain hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features large, showy flower clusters that range from white to pink or blue, depending on soil pH. The plant has broad, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to different growing conditions.
