Hydrangea paniculata f. grandiflora

Hydrangea paniculata f. grandiflora, commonly known as the smooth hydrangea, is a cultivar prized for its large, showy flower clusters.

It is characterized by its white to pink or blue flowers, which can change color based on soil pH. The plant typically grows in a rounded shape with lush, dark green foliage. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
