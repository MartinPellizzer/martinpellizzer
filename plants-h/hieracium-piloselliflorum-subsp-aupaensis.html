Hieracium piloselliflorum subsp. aupaensis

Hieracium piloselliflorum subsp.

aupaensis, commonly known as the Aupaense hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
