Hermione stylosa

Hermione stylosa, commonly known as the slender lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, often with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters at the top of the stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a rosette at the base.

Hermione stylosa is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
