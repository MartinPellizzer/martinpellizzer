Habenaria gardneri

Habenaria gardneri, commonly known as the Gardner's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with prominent sepals and petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicately preserved species.
