Hypochaeris arenaria

Hypochaeris arenaria, commonly known as the sand everlasting or yellow sandwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Hypochaeris arenaria is known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
