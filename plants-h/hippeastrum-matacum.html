Hippeastrum matacum

Hippeastrum matacum, commonly known as the Matacum amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and pointed, growing from a bulbous stem. Its flowers are borne on a tall, erect stem and can reach up to 3 feet in height.

Hippeastrum matacum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens with warm climates.
