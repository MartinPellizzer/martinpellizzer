Hippeastrum puniceum var. haywardii

Hippeastrum puniceum var. haywardii, commonly known as the Hayward's red dragon.

This variety is a striking bulbous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting purple or red throat. The leaves are broad, strap-like, and emerge from a bulbous base, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil with adequate sunlight.
