Haworthia silviae

Haworthia silviae, commonly known as the Silvia's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks bearing small, white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
