Hoitzia aristata

Hoitzia aristata, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Hoitzia aristata thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.
