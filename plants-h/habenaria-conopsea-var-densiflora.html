Habenaria conopsea var. densiflora

Habenaria conopsea var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as swampy forests and marshes. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves arranged along its length. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, often with a slight fragrance.

This variety is noted for its adaptability to moist soils and its role in local ecosystems.
