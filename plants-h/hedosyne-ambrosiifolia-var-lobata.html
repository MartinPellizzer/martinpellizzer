Hedosyne ambrosiifolia var. lobata

Hedosyne ambrosiifolia var. lobata, commonly known as the lobate ambrosia plant, is a rare and striking species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive lobed leaves, which are often deeply divided and have a unique, almost fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical or subtropical forests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable but endangered species in its native range.
