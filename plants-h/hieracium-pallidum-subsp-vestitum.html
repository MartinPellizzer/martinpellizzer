Hieracium pallidum subsp. vestitum

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

vestitum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
