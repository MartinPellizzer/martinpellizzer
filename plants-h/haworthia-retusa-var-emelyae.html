Haworthia retusa var. emelyae

Haworthia retusa var. emelyae, commonly known as the Emelyae Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in dry conditions make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
