Haworthia jansenvillensis var. eminens

Haworthia jansenvillensis var. eminens, commonly known as the Emperors' Crown, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
