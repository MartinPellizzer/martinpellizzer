Herpolirion novae-zelandiae

Herpolirion novae-zelandiae, commonly known as the New Zealand herpolirion, is a unique fern native to the forests of New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are often covered in fine hairs. Due to its limited distribution and specialized habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
