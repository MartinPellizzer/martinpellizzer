Habenaria longipedicellata

Habenaria longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-stemmed helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated floral structure with a prominent, helmet-like labellum. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

Habenaria longipedicellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
