Hymenophyllum neocaledonicum

Hymenophyllum neocaledonicum, commonly known as the New Caledonia filmy fern, is a species of moss-like fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found on tree trunks and rocks in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are thin and translucent, with a unique structure that allows for efficient water retention.

Hymenophyllum neocaledonicum is native to New Caledonia and is considered a rare and endemic species.
