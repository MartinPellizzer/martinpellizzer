Hosackia Humilis

Hosackia humilis is a delicate wildflower native to the western United States. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. This plant is known for its small, bright yellow blooms that appear in early spring. Its low-growing form makes it easy to overlook, yet it plays an important role in its ecosystem. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare and beautiful species.
