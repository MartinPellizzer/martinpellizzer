Habenaria crucifera

Habenaria crucifera, commonly known as the crucifix orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a raceme. Its flowers have a distinctive cruciform shape, giving it its common name.

Habenaria crucifera is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and grasslands.
