Hoya polyneura

Hoya polyneura, commonly known as the "Coral Hoya," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, pink, and white. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Hoya polyneura is also valued for its ability to climb or trail, making it suitable for hanging baskets or shelves.
