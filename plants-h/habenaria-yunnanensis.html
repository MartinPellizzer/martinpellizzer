Habenaria yunnanensis

Habenaria yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan butterfly orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous, and it has linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

The flowers are usually pale green or white with a distinctive lip that is adorned with intricate patterns.
