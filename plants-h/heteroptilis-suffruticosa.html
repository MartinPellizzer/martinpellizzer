Heteroptilis suffruticosa

Heteroptilis suffruticosa, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rough, woody stem and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by round, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions, making it adaptable to various environments.
