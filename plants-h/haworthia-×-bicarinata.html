Haworthia × bicarinata

Haworthia × bicarinata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinct, horizontal stripes. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–15 centimeters and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect light and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
