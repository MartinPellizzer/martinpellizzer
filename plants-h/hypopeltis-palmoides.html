Hypopeltis palmoides

Hypopeltis palmoides, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a palm-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round fruits. Hypopeltis palmoides thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in open woodlands or along riverbanks.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
