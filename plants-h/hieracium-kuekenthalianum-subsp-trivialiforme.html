Hieracium kuekenthalianum subsp. trivialiforme

Hieracium kuekenthalianum subsp.

trivialiforme, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has alternate leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs.

This subspecies is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-rich soils and is often found in temperate regions of Europe.
