Hippeastrum crinum

Hippeastrum crinum, commonly known as the curly leaf amaryllis, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, white, and pink. The plant produces strap-like, dark green leaves that are often curled or twisted, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Hippeastrum crinum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
