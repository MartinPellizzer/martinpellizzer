Hosta longipes var. latifolia

Hosta longipes var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed giant hosta, is a varietal form of the giant hosta, characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves.

The foliage is typically a deep green with a waxy texture and can reach lengths of up to 30 inches. It produces showy, fragrant, white to pale blue flowers in late summer. This variety thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

It is a popular choice for ornamental gardens due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
