Habenaria virescens

Habenaria virescens, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes, often with a faintly fragrant aroma. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
