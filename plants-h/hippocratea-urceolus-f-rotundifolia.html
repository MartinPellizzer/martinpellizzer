Hippocratea urceolus f. rotundifolia

Hippocratea urceolus f. rotundifolia, commonly known as the rounded-leaf hooded orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth where it can receive indirect sunlight.
