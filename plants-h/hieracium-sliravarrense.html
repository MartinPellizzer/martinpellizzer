Hieracium sliravarrense

Hieracium sliravarrense, commonly known as the Sliravarren hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
