Helleborus Atrorubens F. Cupreus

Helleborus atrorubens f. cupreus is a striking variant of the deadly nightshade family known for its unique coppery foliage. This plant thrives in shaded environments and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves display a rich, reddish-brown hue that sets it apart from typical hellebore varieties. Though it shares the toxic properties of its relatives, it remains a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. Careful handling is essential due to its potential to cause harm if ingested.
