Haworthia pygmaea var. dekenahii

Haworthia pygmaea var. dekenahii, commonly known as the Deken's Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
