Hazunta velutina

Hazunta velutina, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are covered in a fine, soft pubescence. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and white, which bloom during the rainy season. Hazunta velutina thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
