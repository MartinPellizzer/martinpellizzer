Hedyosmum glabratum

Hedyosmum glabratum, commonly known as the smooth hedyosmum, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

Hedyosmum glabratum is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in various herbal remedies.
