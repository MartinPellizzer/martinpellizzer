Habenaria stapfii

Habenaria stapfii, commonly known as the Stapf's lady's tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its distinctive flowering spikes that bear numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces a single, slender stem with a cluster of dark green, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to white with a faintly colored lip, and it blooms during the wet season.

Habenaria stapfii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
