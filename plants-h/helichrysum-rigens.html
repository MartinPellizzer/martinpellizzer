Helichrysum rigens

Helichrysum rigens, commonly known as the rigid immortelle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, yellow to orange flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its foliage is covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The flowers are long-lasting and retain their color when dried, contributing to its common name.
