Hieracium eripoliodes

Hieracium eripoliodes, commonly known as the rough hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow to orange ray florets that surround yellow disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and meadows. Its leaves are rough to the touch and have a distinctive serrated edge.

Hieracium eripoliodes is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world, often becoming a weed in agricultural settings.
