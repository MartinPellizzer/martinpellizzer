Humboltia roezlii

Humboltia roezlii, commonly known as the Zambian orchid, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually white or pale pink with a yellow lip.

Humboltia roezlii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
