Hieracium tenuissimum

Hieracium tenuissimum, commonly known as slender hawkweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant has slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow, with tubular corollas and are pollinated primarily by insects.

Hieracium tenuissimum is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, and it can form dense mats in suitable conditions.
