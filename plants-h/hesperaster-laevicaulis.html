Hesperaster laevicaulis

Hesperaster laevicaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are smooth and often tinged with a bluish hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a basal rosette.

Hesperaster laevicaulis is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
