Hieracium ganeschinii

Hieracium ganeschinii, commonly known as the Ganeschin's hawkweed, is a flowering plant species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and cool climates.
