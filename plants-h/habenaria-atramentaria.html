Habenaria atramentaria

Habenaria atramentaria, commonly known as the black-veined orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green leaves and striking, dark purple to black flowers with white veins. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and wetlands. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its intricate floral structure.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
