Helxine sagittatum

Helxine sagittatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a striking contrast of lighter veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Helxine sagittatum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
