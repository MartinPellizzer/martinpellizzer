Haworthia semimargaritifera

Haworthia semimargaritifera, commonly known as the "Cape Rattled Thorn," is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow tubercles. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
