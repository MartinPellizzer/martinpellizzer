Helenium integrifolia

Helenium integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth helenium, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to the eastern and central United States, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces striking orange to red flowers with a distinctive central disk, blooming from late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and smooth, giving it its common name.

Helenium integrifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and natural habitats.
