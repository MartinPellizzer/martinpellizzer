Hieracium grovesianum subsp. grovesianum

Hieracium grovesianum subsp.

grovesianum, commonly known as the Grovesian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to well-drained soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
