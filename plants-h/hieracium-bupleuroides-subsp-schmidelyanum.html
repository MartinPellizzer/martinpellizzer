Hieracium bupleuroides subsp. schmidelyanum

Hieracium bupleuroides subsp.

schmidelyanum, commonly known as the Schmidelyanum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in the summer months. Its inflorescence consists of a single capitulum with several ray florets.

It is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
