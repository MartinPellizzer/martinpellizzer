Hieracium calcareum subsp. crinopodum

Hieracium calcareum subsp.

crinopodum, commonly known as the hairy milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in calcareous soils and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is known for its milky sap, which is a common feature in the genus Hieracium.
