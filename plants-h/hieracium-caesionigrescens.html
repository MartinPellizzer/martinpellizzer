Hieracium caesionigrescens

Hieracium caesionigrescens, commonly known as the dark-hued hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features dark green, rough-textured leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
