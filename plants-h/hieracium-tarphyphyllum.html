Hieracium tarphyphyllum

Hieracium tarphyphyllum, commonly known as the Tarflower hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange-colored composite flowers, which are arranged in a single, showy head. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are usually hairy and alternate, with a rough texture.

Hieracium tarphyphyllum is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
