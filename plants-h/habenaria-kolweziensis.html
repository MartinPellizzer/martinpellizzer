Habenaria kolweziensis

Habenaria kolweziensis, commonly known as the Kolwezi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with distinctive sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
