Haworthia mirabilis var. calcarea

Haworthia mirabilis var. calcarea, commonly known as the calcarea haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white to pale yellow tubercles. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
