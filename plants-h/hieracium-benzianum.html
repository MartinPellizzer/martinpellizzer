Hieracium benzianum

Hieracium benzianum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically has basal leaves that are broadly lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium benzianum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in supporting pollinators.
