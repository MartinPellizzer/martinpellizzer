Hieracium praecox subsp. fragile

Hieracium praecox subsp.

fragile, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and yellow composite flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its delicate, narrow leaves and shorter flower heads compared to other hawkweed species. It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant is known for its early flowering season, often blooming in late spring to early summer.
