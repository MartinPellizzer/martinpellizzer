Hedysarum graminifolium

Hedysarum graminifolium, commonly known as the meadow vetchling, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing herb with trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies.

Hedysarum graminifolium is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and stabilizing soil.
