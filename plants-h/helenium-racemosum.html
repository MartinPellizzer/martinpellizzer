Helenium racemosum

Helenium racemosum, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features striking yellow to orange flower heads that resemble arrowheads, which are arranged in racemes along the stem. The plant has finely cut, fern-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight.

Helenium racemosum is valued in gardens for its vibrant color and ability to attract pollinators.
