Hermione × tenuis

Hermione × tenuis, commonly known as the hybrid honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange.

It typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 feet and features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant is native to regions in eastern Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden settings.

Botanically, it belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is known for its vigorous growth and winter hardiness.
