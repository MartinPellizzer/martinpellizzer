Hieracium lachenalii subsp. valderamosum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

valderamosum, commonly known as the Valderamos hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has finely divided, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
