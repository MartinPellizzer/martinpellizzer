Hebe pareora

Hebe pareora, commonly known as the silver hebe, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Hebe pareora is also valued for its hardiness and ability to attract native pollinators.
