Hackelia latifolia

Hackelia latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf hackelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its showy, pink to purple flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Hackelia latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
