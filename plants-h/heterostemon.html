Heterostemon

Heterostemon, commonly known as the star anise plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit, which is used in both culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that develop into dark brown, star-shaped pods. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The botanical features of Heterostemon make it a valuable species in traditional medicine and aromatics.
