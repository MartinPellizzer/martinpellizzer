Hypericum aegiptium

Hypericum aegiptium, commonly known as St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and opposite, linear leaves. The plant grows up to 1 meter tall and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential antidepressant effects.

However, it is also considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread.
