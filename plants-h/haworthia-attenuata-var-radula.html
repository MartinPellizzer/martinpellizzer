Haworthia attenuata var. radula

Haworthia attenuata var. radula, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically a pale green to bluish-gray, often with white or translucent markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or container planting due to its low maintenance and compact size.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in dry conditions make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
