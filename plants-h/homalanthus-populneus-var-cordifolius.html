Homalanthus populneus var. cordifolius

Homalanthus populneus var. cordifolius, commonly known as the heartleaf hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
