Hieracium esketanense

Hieracium esketanense, commonly known as the Esketan hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southeastern Europe, particularly in the Balkans. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, and its leaves are typically basal, forming a rosette. It grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
