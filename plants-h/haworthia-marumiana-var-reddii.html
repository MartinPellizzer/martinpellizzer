Haworthia marumiana var. reddii

Haworthia marumiana var. reddii, commonly known as the red-tipped haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings along the edges. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
