Habenaria retroflexa

Habenaria retroflexa, commonly known as the backward-facing orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base of the plant.

Habenaria retroflexa is known for its unique floral structure and is a subject of interest in botanical studies.
