Helleborus multifidus f. violascens

Helleborus multifidus f. violascens, commonly known as the purple-leaf hellebore, is a hybrid variety of the hellebore plant.

It is characterized by its striking deep violet to almost black foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its white to pale pink flowers. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans and thrives in shady, moist environments. It produces nodding flowers with five to seven petals, typically blooming in late winter to early spring.

This ornamental plant is valued for its long-lasting foliage and unique floral display.
