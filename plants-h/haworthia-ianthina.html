Haworthia ianthina

Haworthia ianthina, commonly known as the Zulu beauty, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Haworthia ianthina is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
