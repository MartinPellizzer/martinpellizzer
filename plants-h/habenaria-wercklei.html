Habenaria wercklei

Habenaria wercklei, commonly known as the Werckle's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant produces numerous small, greenish-white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a spike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Habenaria wercklei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
