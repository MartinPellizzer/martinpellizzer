Haworthia limifolia var. ubomboensis

Haworthia limifolia var. ubomboensis, commonly known as the Ubombo Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white to pale yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
