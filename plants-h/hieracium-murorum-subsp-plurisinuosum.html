Hieracium murorum subsp. plurisinuosum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

plurisinuosum, commonly known as the many-ribbed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and open habitats such as roadsides and grasslands.

The plant has distinctive, hairy leaves with prominent veins and produces yellow flowers in early summer. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.
