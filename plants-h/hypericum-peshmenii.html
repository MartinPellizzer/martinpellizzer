Hypericum peshmenii

Hypericum peshmenii, commonly known as the Peshmenii St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum peshmenii is noted for its medicinal properties, similar to other species in the genus Hypericum.
