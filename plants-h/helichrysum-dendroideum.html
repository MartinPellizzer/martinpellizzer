Helichrysum dendroideum

Helichrysum dendroideum, commonly known as the tree erigeron, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub-like species that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its woody stems and dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its aromatic leaves and showy flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
