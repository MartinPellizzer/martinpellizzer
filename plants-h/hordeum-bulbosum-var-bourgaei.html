Hordeum bulbosum var. bourgaei

Hordeum bulbosum var. bourgaei, commonly known as the dwarf barley, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience and low maintenance requirements.
