Hypochaeris linearifolia

Hypochaeris linearifolia, commonly known as the slender weed or linearleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields, preferring full sun. Its linear leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer.

Hypochaeris linearifolia is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread prolifically.
