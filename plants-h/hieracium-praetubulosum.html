Hieracium praetubulosum

Hieracium praetubulosum, commonly known as the prairie hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as prairies and roadsides. It features yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough, hairy texture.
