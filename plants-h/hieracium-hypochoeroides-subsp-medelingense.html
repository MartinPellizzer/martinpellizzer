Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. medelingense

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

medelingense, commonly known as the Medeling yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of both ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its yellow flowers are small and daisy-like, with pale greenish-yellow ray florets and yellow disk florets.

The species is known for its low-growing habit and is often found in association with other flowering plants in open habitats.
