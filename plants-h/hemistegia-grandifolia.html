Hemistegia grandifolia

Hemistegia grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaved hemistegia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Hemistegia grandifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
