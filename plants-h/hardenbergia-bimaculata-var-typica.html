Hardenbergia bimaculata var. typica

Hardenbergia bimaculata var. typica, commonly known as the pink burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces woody pods that contain several seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
