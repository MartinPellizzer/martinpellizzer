Hieracium rupicola subsp. sonchifolium

Hieracium rupicola subsp.

sonchifolium, commonly known as the rock dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments.

The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, with a distinctive yellow flower head composed of ray florets. It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower structure.

Due to its habitat preferences, it is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.
