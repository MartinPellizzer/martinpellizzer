Hoya Australis Subsp. Tenuipes

Hoya australis subsp. tenuipes is a unique and striking plant known for its elegant, waxy leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers. Native to Australia, it thrives in warm, humid environments and is popular among indoor gardeners for its low maintenance requirements. This subspecies is often mistaken for its more common relative, Hoya australis, but it has distinct features that set it apart. Its foliage is characterized by a soft, velvety texture and a pale green color that deepens with age. Whether grown in pots or hanging baskets, Hoya australis subsp. tenuipes adds a touch of beauty and serenity to any space.
