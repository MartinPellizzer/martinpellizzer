Habenaria usambarae

Habenaria usambarae, commonly known as the Usambara butterfly orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found in the Usambara Mountains of Tanzania.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on rock surfaces or in crevices. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with intricate patterns, and it has a short, erect stem.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species.
