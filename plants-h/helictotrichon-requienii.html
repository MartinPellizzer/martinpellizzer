Helictotrichon requienii

Helictotrichon requienii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.
