Hieracium xanthophytum

Hieracium xanthophytum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has serrated leaves and produces a basal rosette of foliage.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
