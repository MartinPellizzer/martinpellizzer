Hoya golamcoana

Hoya golamcoana, commonly known as the golden flame hoya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces fragrant blooms that typically appear in late summer to early autumn. Hoya golamcoana thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
