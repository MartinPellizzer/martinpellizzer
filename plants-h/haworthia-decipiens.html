Haworthia decipiens

Haworthia decipiens, commonly known as the "Zebra Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stalks during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
