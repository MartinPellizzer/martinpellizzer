Habenaria tenerrima

Habenaria tenerrima, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, pale green or white, and often have a slight purple or pink tint.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its delicate appearance and ecological significance in its native habitat.
