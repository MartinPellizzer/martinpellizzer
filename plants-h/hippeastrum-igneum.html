Hippeastrum igneum

Hippeastrum igneum, commonly known as the Flame Dragon or Fire Dragon lily, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often spotted with red or purple markings. Its bulbs are robust and capable of producing multiple flower stalks each season.

Hippeastrum igneum is a popular ornamental plant due to its dramatic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
