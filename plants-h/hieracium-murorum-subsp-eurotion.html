Hieracium murorum subsp. eurotion

Hieracium murorum subsp.

eurotion, commonly known as the European mouse-ear hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves.

The plant produces yellowish to brownish composite flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered a weed in some regions.
