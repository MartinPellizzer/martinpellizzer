Habenaria helleborina

Habenaria helleborina, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers during the winter months. The plant typically has a single, short rhizome with a few leaves and a cluster of flowers that bloom in shades of white, green, and pink. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses as a decorative plant.
