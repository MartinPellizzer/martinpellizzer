Henckelia corniculata

Henckelia corniculata, commonly known as the horned pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-shaped inflorescences that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes. Its leaves are evergreen, lance-shaped, and have a glossy appearance.

Henckelia corniculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
