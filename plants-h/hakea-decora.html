Hakea decora

Hakea decora, commonly known as the decorative hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features striking, needle-like leaves that are often bright green with a waxy coating. The plant produces cylindrical flower spikes that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the species. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Hakea decora is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
