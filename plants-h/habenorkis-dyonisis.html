Habenorkis dyonisis

Habenorkis dyonisis, commonly known as the purple-flowered orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are characterized by their vibrant purple coloration and intricate, spurred petals.

The botanical study of Habenorkis dyonisis is important for understanding orchid diversity and ecological adaptations in tropical ecosystems.
