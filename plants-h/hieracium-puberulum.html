Hieracium puberulum

Hieracium puberulum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium puberulum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to spread rapidly.
