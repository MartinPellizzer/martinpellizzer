Hymenocallis schizostephana

Hymenocallis schizostephana, commonly known as the Schizostephana hurricane lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant produces narrow, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky texture. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters at the top of the stem, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Hymenocallis schizostephana thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its beauty and resilience.
