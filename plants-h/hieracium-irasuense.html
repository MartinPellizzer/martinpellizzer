Hieracium irasuense

Hieracium irasuense, commonly known as the Irasun hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.

Hieracium irasuense is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
