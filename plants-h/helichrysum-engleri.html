Helichrysum engleri

Helichrysum engleri, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, sun-like flowers that resemble small cups, hence its common name. The plant produces small, papery bracts that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum engleri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
