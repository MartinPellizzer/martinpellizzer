Hieracium pseudoleiopsis

Hieracium pseudoleiopsis, commonly known as the false leopard's bread.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its dandelion-like yellow flowers and hairy stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are clustered in a single head.
