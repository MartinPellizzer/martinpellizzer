Hypoxis rigidula var. pilosissima

Hypoxis rigidula var. pilosissima, commonly known as the hairy yellow star, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves that are often tinged with purple. The plant produces yellow flowers with six petals arranged in a star-like pattern, typical of the Hypoxis genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its distinctive foliage and prolonged blooming period.
