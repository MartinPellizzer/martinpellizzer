Hoya Inflata

Hoya inflata is a striking tropical plant known for its unique, swollen leaves and clusters of fragrant, star-shaped flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, this species thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its velvety foliage and elegant blooms make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and interior decorators. Easy to care for, Hoya inflata is ideal for both novice and experienced growers. This plant not only adds beauty to any space but also symbolizes good fortune and prosperity in many cultures.
