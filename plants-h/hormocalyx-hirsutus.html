Hormocalyx hirsutus

Hormocalyx hirsutus, commonly known as the hairy lily.

It is a flowering plant native to Australia, characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Hormocalyx hirsutus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
