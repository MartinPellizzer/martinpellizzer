Hieracium praecox subsp. jaubertianum

Hieracium praecox subsp.

jaubertianum, commonly known as the early hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow ray florets.

The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats and is native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It has a taproot system and produces seeds in small, composite flower heads.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
