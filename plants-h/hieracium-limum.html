Hieracium limum

Hieracium limum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and clustered, yellowish to reddish flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Hieracium limum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
