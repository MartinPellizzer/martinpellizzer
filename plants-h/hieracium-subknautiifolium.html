Hieracium subknautiifolium

Hieracium subknautiifolium, commonly known as the subknautii hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges, and its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.

It is often found in calcareous soils and is considered a characteristic species of certain habitats.
