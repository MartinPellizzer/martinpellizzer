Hieracium eucephalum

Hieracium eucephalum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous flowering stems that rise from the center. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open habitats.

Hieracium eucephalum is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to spread easily.
