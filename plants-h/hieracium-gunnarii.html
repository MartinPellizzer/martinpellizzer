Hieracium gunnarii

Hieracium gunnarii, commonly known as the Gunner's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and forest margins. It has basal leaves that are usually hairy and can reach up to 30 cm in height.

The flowers are yellow and bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
