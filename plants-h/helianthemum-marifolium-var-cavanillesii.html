Helianthemum marifolium var. cavanillesii

Helianthemum marifolium var. cavanillesii, commonly known as the Cavanilles' rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is adapted to full sun exposure. Its fleshy leaves and drought-resistant nature make it well-suited for Mediterranean climates.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy characteristics.
