Hieracium atratum subsp. cuneolarium

Hieracium atratum subsp.

cuneolarium, commonly known as the dark hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and showy yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, clustered flower heads that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
