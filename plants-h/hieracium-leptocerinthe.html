Hieracium leptocerinthe

Hieracium leptocerinthe, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has finely divided, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, whitish pubescence. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas, where it spreads through both seeds and rhizomes.
