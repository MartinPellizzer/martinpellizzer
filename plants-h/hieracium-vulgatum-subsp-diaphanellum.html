Hieracium vulgatum subsp. diaphanellum

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

diaphanellum, commonly known as the transparent hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its translucent, waxy leaves and a more compact growth habit compared to other hawkweed species. It typically thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
