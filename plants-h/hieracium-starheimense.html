Hieracium starheimense

Hieracium starheimense, commonly known as Starheim's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of stiff, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Native to the mountainous regions of Scandinavia, it thrives in rocky and alpine environments. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
