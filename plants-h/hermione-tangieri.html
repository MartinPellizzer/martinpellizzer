Hermione tangieri

Hermione tangieri, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that resemble flames, with intricate patterns of darker hues. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
