Habenaria blephariglottis var. conspicua

Habenaria blephariglottis var. conspicua, commonly known as the showy bladder orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces a single, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are arranged in a spike and are pollinated by insects, particularly bees.

This orchid thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
