Haworthia viscosa

Haworthia viscosa, commonly known as the thick-leaved Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners alike.
