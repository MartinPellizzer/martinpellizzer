Hectorella caespitosa

Hectorella caespitosa, commonly known as the clustered heather, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically forms dense, compact clusters of foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

Hectorella caespitosa is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and hardiness.
