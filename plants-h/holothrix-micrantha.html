Holothrix micrantha

Holothrix micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved holothrix, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact cluster. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid regions.
