Heliconia velloziana

Heliconia velloziana, commonly known as the Velloziana heliconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Heliconiaceae family and is characterized by its large, brightly colored bracts that surround its inflorescence. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are clustered in a spadix, which is enclosed by the showy bracts.

Heliconia velloziana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
