Helichrysum panormitanum var. angustifolium

Helichrysum panormitanum var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, yellow-orange flowers that are often used in floral arrangements. The leaves are narrow and linear, typically covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and resilience in arid conditions.
