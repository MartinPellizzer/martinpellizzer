Hieracium krasanii subsp. kukulense

Hieracium krasanii subsp.

kukulense, commonly known as the Krasan hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which are often covered in fine, white pubescence.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to parts of Central and Eastern Europe, growing in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
