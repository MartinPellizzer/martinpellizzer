Hyptis cachimboensis

Hyptis cachimboensis, commonly known as the Cachimbo hyptis, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest, specifically in regions of Brazil and Colombia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense tropical forests.

Due to its unique botanical features, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
