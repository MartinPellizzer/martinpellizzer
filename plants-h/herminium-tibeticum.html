Herminium tibeticum

Herminium tibeticum, commonly known as the Tibetan lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spirally arranged flowers that resemble a tresses of hair. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine meadows, thriving in cool and moist environments. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, and it blooms during the summer months.

Herminium tibeticum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
