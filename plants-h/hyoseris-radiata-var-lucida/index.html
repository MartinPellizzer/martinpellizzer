Hyoseris Radiata Var. Lucida

Radiate sunflower, scientifically known as Hyoseris radiata var. lucida, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. Its vibrant yellow petals and central disk create a radiant appearance that mimics the sun, making it a popular choice for wildflower gardens. This variety is distinguished by its glossy leaves and taller stature compared to other Hyoseris species. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles, yet holds significant ecological value for pollinators and native wildlife.
