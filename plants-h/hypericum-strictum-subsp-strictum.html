Hypericum strictum subsp. strictum

Hypericum strictum subsp.

strictum, commonly known as upright St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features upright, woody stems that are often branched and bear small, yellow-orange flowers with four petals.

The leaves are simple, opposite, and oval-shaped with a slightly toothed margin. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
