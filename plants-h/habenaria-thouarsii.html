Habenaria thouarsii

Habenaria thouarsii, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
