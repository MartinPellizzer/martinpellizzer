Halopegia perrieri

Halopegia perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's halopegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
