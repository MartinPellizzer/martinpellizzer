Hypericum degenii

Hypericum degenii, commonly known as the Degen's St. John's Wort, is a species of flowering plant in the family Hypericaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum degenii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
