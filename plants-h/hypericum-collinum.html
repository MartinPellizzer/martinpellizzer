Hypericum collinum

Hypericum collinum, commonly known as the yellow st. john's wort, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its stems are woody and can become somewhat branched as the plant matures.

Hypericum collinum is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
