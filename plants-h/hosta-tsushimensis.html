Hosta tsushimensis

Hosta tsushimensis, commonly known as the Japanese hosta, is a species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, medium to large, dark green leaves that are often marked with white or light green variegation. The plant produces fragrant, bell-shaped white flowers in late summer, typically on slender, upright stems. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta tsushimensis is a popular ornamental plant in gardens due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
