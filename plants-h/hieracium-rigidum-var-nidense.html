Hieracium rigidum var. nidense

Hieracium rigidum var. nidense, commonly known as the rigid hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features erect, hairy stems that can reach up to 60 cm in height, with basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, blooming from June to August.

It is known for its ability to form dense colonies, sometimes becoming invasive in certain regions.
