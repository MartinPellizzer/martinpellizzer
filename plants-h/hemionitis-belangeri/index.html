Hemionitis Belangeri

Hemionitis belangeri is a rare and unique aquatic fern found in specific regions of Southeast Asia. It thrives in freshwater habitats such as ponds and streams, often in shaded, still waters. This plant is notable for its distinctive leaf structure, which features a spiral arrangement of fronds that resemble a fan. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, Hemionitis belangeri is considered endangered. Its conservation is crucial for maintaining biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
