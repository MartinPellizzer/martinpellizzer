Helichrysum decorum

Helichrysum decorum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that are densely packed in clusters, giving it a fluffy appearance. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers, which retain their color even when dried.
