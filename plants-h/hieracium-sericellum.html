Hieracium sericellum

Hieracium sericellum, commonly known as the silky hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Hieracium sericellum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including meadows and roadsides.
