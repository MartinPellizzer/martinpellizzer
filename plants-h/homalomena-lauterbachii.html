Homalomena lauterbachii

Homalomena lauterbachii, commonly known as the Lauterbach's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive leaf structure and inflorescence. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. It produces an umbellate spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the genus.

Homalomena lauterbachii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
