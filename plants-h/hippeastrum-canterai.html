Hippeastrum canterai

Hippeastrum canterai, commonly known as the Canary Island amaryllis, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 cm in diameter, typically in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and slightly wavy. It is a bulbous plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Hippeastrum canterai is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm climates.
