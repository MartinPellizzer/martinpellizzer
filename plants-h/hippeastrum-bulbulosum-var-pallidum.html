Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. pallidum

Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. pallidum, commonly known as the pale hurricane lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers with pale yellow petals and a prominent red or orange center, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features strap-like, broad leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.
