Hieracium sandbergianum

Hieracium sandbergianum, commonly known as Sandberg's hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its yellow flowers bloom in the summer, and it produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Hieracium sandbergianum is often found in areas with low moisture and full sun exposure.
