Huernia brevirostris var. scabra

Huernia brevirostris var. scabra, commonly known as the rough-horned lithops, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a distinctive, rough-textured coating. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers during the summer months, which are typically short-lived. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
