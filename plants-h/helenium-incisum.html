Helenium incisum

Helenium incisum, commonly known as the cutleaf hedgenightshade, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its bright orange to red tubular flowers. The plant has finely dissected, fern-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in shaded areas.

Helenium incisum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
