Hebe laingii

Hebe laingii, commonly known as the Laing's hebe, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardiness.

The leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, providing a textured appearance to the plant.
