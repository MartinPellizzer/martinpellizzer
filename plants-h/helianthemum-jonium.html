Helianthemum jonium

Helianthemum jonium, commonly known as the pink rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 30 centimeters. The plant features dense clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually gray-green, covered in a layer of fine, white hairs that help reduce water loss.

Helianthemum jonium thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
