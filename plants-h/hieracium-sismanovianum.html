Hieracium sismanovianum

Hieracium sismanovianum, commonly known as the Sismanovian hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in mountainous and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It features upright stems with basal leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture.

Its yellow flowers bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
