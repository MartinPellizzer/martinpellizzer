Helichrysum compactum

Helichrysum compactum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Helichrysum compactum is also valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
