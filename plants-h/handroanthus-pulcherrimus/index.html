Handroanthus Pulcherrimus

Trumpet tree, scientifically known as Handroanthus pulcherrimus, is a striking tropical plant native to South America. With its vibrant orange flowers that bloom in clusters, it captures the eye with its bold and colorful display. This tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and protection from frost to flourish.
