Hieracium sabaudum var. latifolium

Hieracium sabaudum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosettes of broadly ovate to lanceolate leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is sometimes grown in gardens.
