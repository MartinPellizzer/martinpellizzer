Hieracium sericifolium

Hieracium sericifolium, commonly known as the silken leaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, silken leaves that give it its common name, and it produces yellow flowers in clusters. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in meadows and grasslands. It is often found in disturbed soils and can spread readily through its extensive root system.

The plant is also known for its ability to hybridize with other hawkweed species, leading to various cultivars and variations.
