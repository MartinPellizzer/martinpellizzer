Hieracium Raddeanum Subsp. Regelianum

Hieracium raddeanum subsp. regelianum is a rare and lesser-known member of the daisy family. It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. This plant is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flower heads. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species.
