Heracleum sphondylium f. angustissimum

Heracleum sphondylium f. angustissimum, commonly known as narrowleaf cowparsnip, is a botanical variant of the common cowparsnip.

It is a biennial plant belonging to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its slender, angular stems and finely divided leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in umbels, which develop into small, round fruits. It is typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.
