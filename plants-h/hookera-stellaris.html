Hookera stellaris

Hookera stellaris, commonly known as the starflower, is a perennial plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense mat.

Hookera stellaris is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators in its native habitat.
