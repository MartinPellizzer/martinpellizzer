Habenaria devogeliana

Habenaria devogeliana, commonly known as the Devogel's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or grasslands. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
