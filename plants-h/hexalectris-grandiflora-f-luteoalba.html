Hexalectris grandiflora f. luteoalba

Hexalectris grandiflora f. luteoalba, commonly known as the yellow-flowered false morel, is a rare and striking variant of the false morel genus.

The plant is native to the southeastern United States, particularly in areas with rich, moist soils.
