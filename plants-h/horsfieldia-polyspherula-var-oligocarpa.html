Horsfieldia polyspherula var. oligocarpa

Horsfieldia polyspherula var. oligocarpa, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and bark of trees.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.
