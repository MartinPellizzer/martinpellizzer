Hedysarum sikkimense var. xiangchengense

Hedysarum sikkimense var. xiangchengense, commonly known as the Xiangcheng milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Tibetan Plateau and adjacent regions of China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow to orange flowers. The plant is adapted to high-altitude environments, often growing in rocky or alpine habitats. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential in ecological restoration projects.

Its seeds are typically small and are dispersed by wind or animals.
