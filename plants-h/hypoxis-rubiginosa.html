Hypoxis rubiginosa

Hypoxis rubiginosa, commonly known as the star lily or purple star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with a distinctive purple center. The plant is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Hypoxis rubiginosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
