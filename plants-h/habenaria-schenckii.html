Habenaria schenckii

Habenaria schenckii, commonly known as the Schenck's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on the forest floor or in undergrowth. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
