Humblotia comorensis

Humblotia comorensis, commonly known as the Comorian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Comoros Islands.

It is a herbaceous plant with a slender, erect stem that bears several oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
