Hieracium vittatum

Hieracium vittatum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, forming dense mats in suitable environments.
