Helianthemum Globulariifolium Var. Major

Helianthemum globulariifolium var. major is a unique and resilient plant native to the rocky landscapes of southern Europe. This variant of the globular-leaved helianthemum is known for its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens and coastal areas. Its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements have made it a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream gardening circles.
