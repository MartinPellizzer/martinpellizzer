Haworthia × subrigida

Haworthia × subrigida, commonly known as the Zulu tiger or Zulu tiger tooth plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically green with white or yellowish markings, resembling tiger stripes, and are covered in a fine layer of white wax. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
