Homalobus spatiosus

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a prominent midrib. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in panicles, often displaying yellow or greenish-white petals.
