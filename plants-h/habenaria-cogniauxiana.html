Habenaria cogniauxiana

Habenaria cogniauxiana, commonly known as the Cogniaux's butterfly orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has a slender, erect stem with several leaves arranged along its length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, adding a striking visual element to its natural habitat.
