Helianthemum marifolium f. buschii

Helianthemum marifolium f. buschii, commonly known as the Buschii rockrose, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a vibrant touch to rocky or sandy soils. The plant has aromatic, silvery-gray foliage that is both drought-tolerant and heat-resistant, making it well-suited for xeriscaping. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in coastal and mountainous areas.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature in garden settings.
