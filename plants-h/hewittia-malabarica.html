Hewittia malabarica

Hewittia malabarica, commonly known as the Malabar hewittia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests. The plant features simple, opposite leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Hewittia malabarica is also noted for its ability to thrive in humid climates with well-drained soil.
