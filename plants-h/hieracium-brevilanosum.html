Hieracium brevilanosum

Hieracium brevilanosum, commonly known as the short-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, which help it retain moisture in dry conditions.

The plant is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
