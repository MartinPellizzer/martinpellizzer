Habenaria polypodantha

Habenaria polypodantha, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent sepals and petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for absorbing nutrients from the soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
