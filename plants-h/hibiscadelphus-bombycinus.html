Hibiscadelphus bombycinus

Hibiscadelphus bombycinus, commonly known as the Kauai waterwheel, is a rare endemic plant found only on the island of Kauai in Hawaii.

It is a shrub characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is adapted to the unique environmental conditions of its native habitat. Hibiscadelphus bombycinus is critically endangered due to habitat loss and invasive species.

Its conservation is a priority for preserving Hawaiian biodiversity.
