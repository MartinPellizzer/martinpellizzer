Hieracium grandipetalum

Hieracium grandipetalum, commonly known as the large-petaled hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium grandipetalum is often found in open habitats and can spread rapidly through its rhizomatous growth system.
