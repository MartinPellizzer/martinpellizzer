Helichrysum italicum

Helichrysum italicum, commonly known as immortelle or eternity flower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in herbal medicine. Its essential oil is valued for its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The name "immortelle" refers to the plant's ability to retain its color and form when dried.
