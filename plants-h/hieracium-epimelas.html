Hieracium epimelas

Hieracium epimelas, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has a creeping growth habit with rhizomes, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

Its botanical features make it both a common weed and an ornamental plant in some regions.
