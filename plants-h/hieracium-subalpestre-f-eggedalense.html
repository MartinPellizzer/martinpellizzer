Hieracium subalpestre f. eggedalense

Hieracium subalpestre f. eggedalense, commonly known as the Eggedalen hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring well-drained soils and rocky habitats. It is native to parts of Europe, particularly Norway, and is often found in mountainous areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
