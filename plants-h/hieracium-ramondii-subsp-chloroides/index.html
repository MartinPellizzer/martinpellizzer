Hieracium Ramondii Subsp. Chloroides

Hieracium ramondii subsp. chloroides is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the sunflower family, known for its daisy-like appearance and vibrant yellow blooms. This subspecies is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and lesser-known member of the Asteraceae family.
