Haussknechtia elymaitica

Haussknechtia elymaitica, commonly known as the Elymaitic grass, is a perennial grass species native to the steppes of Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats and is adapted to low-nutrient soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.

Botanically, it is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and its ability to form dense stands in its native range.
