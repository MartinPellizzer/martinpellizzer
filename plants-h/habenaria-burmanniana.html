Habenaria burmanniana

Habenaria burmanniana, commonly known as the Burmann's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a yellowish throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its growth habit and floral structure make it a notable species in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
