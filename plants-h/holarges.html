Holarges

Holarges, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a water-loving plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, erect stems above the water surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water or are submerged beneath it. Its roots system helps filter water and stabilize aquatic ecosystems.

Holarges has been culturally and spiritually significant in many Asian civilizations for centuries.
