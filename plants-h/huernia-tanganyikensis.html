Huernia tanganyikensis

Huernia tanganyikensis, commonly known as the Tanganyika moon cactus, is a succulent native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
