Hebe raoulii var. maccaskillii

Hebe raoulii var. maccaskillii, commonly known as the Maccaskill hebe, is a compact shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple during the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
