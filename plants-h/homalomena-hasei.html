Homalomena hasei

Homalomena hasei, commonly known as the Hasei arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Homalomena hasei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
