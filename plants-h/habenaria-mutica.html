Habenaria mutica

Habenaria mutica, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with few leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

The orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
