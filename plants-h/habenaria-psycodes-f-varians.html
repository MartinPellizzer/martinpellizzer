Habenaria psycodes f. varians

Habenaria psycodes f. varians, commonly known as the variable ghost orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent white flowers that resemble ghostly apparitions, hence its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte on trees, drawing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, adapted to conserve water in its tropical habitat.

The species is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its rarity and unique appearance.
