Hieracium epimedium subsp. macilentum

Hieracium epimedium subsp.

macilentum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flower heads.

The plant typically features slender, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are arranged in dense clusters and bloom in the summer months.

It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
