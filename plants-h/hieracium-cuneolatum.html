Hieracium cuneolatum

Hieracium cuneolatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Hieracium cuneolatum is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
