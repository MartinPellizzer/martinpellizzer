Hickenia microsperma

Hickenia microsperma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hickenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and savannas, often in well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense spikes. Hickenia microsperma is adapted to dry conditions and can tolerate seasonal droughts.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
