Hypoestes latifolia var. integrifolia

Hypoestes latifolia var. integrifolia, commonly known as the African violet or velvet plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant prefers well-draining soil and indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its compact growth habit and long blooming period contribute to its ornamental appeal.

It is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions with proper care.
