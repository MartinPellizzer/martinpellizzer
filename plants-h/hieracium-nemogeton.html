Hieracium nemogeton

Hieracium nemogeton, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features bright yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets, and it typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Hieracium nemogeton is an herbaceous perennial with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its aggressive growth habit.
