Hieracium mjoemense

Hieracium mjoemense, commonly known as the Mjoemense hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and stems that bear yellow composite flowers. Native to Europe, this species thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has finely divided leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that attract various pollinators.

It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.
