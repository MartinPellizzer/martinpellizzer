Hosta tokudama

Hosta tokudama, commonly known as the Japanese hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that can vary in color from dark green to blue-green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Hosta tokudama is a popular ornamental plant in gardens due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
