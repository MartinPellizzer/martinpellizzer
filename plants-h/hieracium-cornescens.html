Hieracium cornescens

Hieracium cornescens, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium cornescens is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
