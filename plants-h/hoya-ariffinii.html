Hoya ariffinii

Hoya ariffinii, commonly known as the "Rainbow Hoya," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom in the spring. Hoya ariffinii is a trailing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
