Hieracium aphyllum subsp. nalunsicum

Hieracium aphyllum subsp.

nalunsicum, commonly known as the Nalunsic hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves and produces yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils. It is a perennial species that thrives in cool, moist climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered plant in its native range.
