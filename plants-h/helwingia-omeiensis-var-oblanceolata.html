Helwingia omeiensis var. oblanceolata

Helwingia omeiensis var. oblanceolata, commonly known as the Chinese helwingia, is a rare and unique evergreen shrub native to China.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with a distinctive elongated base. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in late spring, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits that are edible for birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare species in horticulture.
