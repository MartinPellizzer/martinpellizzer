Hermannia bryoniifolia

Hermannia bryoniifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Hermannia bryoniifolia is often grown as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage.
