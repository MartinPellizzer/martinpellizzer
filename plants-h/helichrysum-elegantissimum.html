Helichrysum elegantissimum

Helichrysum elegantissimum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that resemble daisies. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its flowers are also used in traditional medicine and aromatherapy.
