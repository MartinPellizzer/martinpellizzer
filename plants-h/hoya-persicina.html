Hoya persicina

Hoya persicina, commonly known as the sweetheart plant, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle fragrance. Hoya persicina thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid conditions.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
