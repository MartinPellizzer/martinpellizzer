Helicia henryi

Helicia henryi, commonly known as the Henry's helenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers range in color from white to pink.

Helicia henryi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
