Helichrysum humbertii

Helichrysum humbertii, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum humbertii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
