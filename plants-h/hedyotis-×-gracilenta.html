Hedyotis × gracilenta

Hedyotis × gracilenta, commonly known as the slender dotweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, creeping growth habit and small, bright green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
