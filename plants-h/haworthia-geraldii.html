Haworthia geraldii

Haworthia geraldii, commonly known as the Geraldii Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, allowing the plant to thrive in arid conditions. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems during the summer months.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
