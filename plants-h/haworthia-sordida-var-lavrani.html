Haworthia sordida var. lavrani

Haworthia sordida var. lavrani, commonly known as the lavender Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with a bluish tint, often marked by white or pinkish markings. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
