Hemigraphis sciophila

Hemigraphis sciophila, commonly known as the shade geranium, is a low-growing perennial plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features striking, velvety leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, red, and silver, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters at the ends of stems.
