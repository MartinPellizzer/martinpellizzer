Hieracium stenozostum

Hieracium stenozostum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium stenozostum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
