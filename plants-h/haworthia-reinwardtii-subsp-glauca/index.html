Haworthia Reinwardtii Subsp. Glauca

Haworthia reinwardtii subsp. glauca is a rare and attractive succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its compact, rosette-shaped form and striking blue-green foliage. The plant features translucent windows on its leaves that allow light to pass through, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements. This subspecies is highly sought after by collectors for its unique appearance and ease of care.
