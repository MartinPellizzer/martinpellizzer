Hieracium arilomum

Hieracium arilomum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the upper leaves are narrower and sessile. Hieracium arilomum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in moist, disturbed soils.

It is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in other open habitats.
