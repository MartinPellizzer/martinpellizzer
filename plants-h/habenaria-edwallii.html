Habenaria edwallii

Habenaria edwallii, commonly known as the Edwall's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow markings, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
