Haworthia gracilidelineata

Haworthia gracilidelineata, commonly known as the narrow-line haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish-green hue and distinct white or pale markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Haworthia gracilidelineata is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
