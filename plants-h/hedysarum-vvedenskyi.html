Hedysarum vvedenskyi

Hedysarum vvedenskyi, commonly known as the Vvedensky's milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces trifoliate leaves and pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Hedysarum vvedenskyi plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
