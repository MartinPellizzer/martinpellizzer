Hypericum richeri subsp. burseri

Hypericum richeri subsp.

burseri, commonly known as the Burseri St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to parts of southern Africa. It is characterized by its woody stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This subspecies is closely related to other members of the Hypericaceae family, sharing similar medicinal properties.
