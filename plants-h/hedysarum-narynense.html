Hedysarum narynense

Hedysarum narynense, commonly known as the Naryn milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its ecological importance lies in its ability to fix nitrogen, enriching the soil in nutrient-poor areas.
