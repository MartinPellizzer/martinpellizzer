Hieracium subcaesium subsp. pseudocirritum

Hieracium subcaesium subsp.

pseudocirritum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, exhibiting a low-growing, herbaceous habit. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is often found in open habitats and is considered a minor weed in some regions.
