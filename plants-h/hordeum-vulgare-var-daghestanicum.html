Hordeum vulgare var. daghestanicum

Hordeum vulgare var. daghestanicum, commonly known as the Daghestan barley, is a variant of barley native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its robust growth and adaptation to temperate climates. This plant typically produces compact spikes with small, hard grains that are well-suited for cultivation in diverse soil conditions. Its botanical features include hollow stems and leaves with parallel veins, typical of the grass family.

It is valued for its resilience and potential use in both traditional and modern agricultural practices.
