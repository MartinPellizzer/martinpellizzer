Hierochloe Borealis Subsp. Setifolia

Hierochloe borealis subsp. setifolia is a rare and delicate grass species found in northern regions of the northern hemisphere. It thrives in cold, moist environments such as tundra and alpine meadows. Known for its fine, silky leaves and tufted growth habit, this plant is often overlooked despite its ecological importance. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Studying this subspecies offers insights into plant adaptation to extreme climates.
