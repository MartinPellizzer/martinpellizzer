Hydrangea fargesii

Hydrangea fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant produces clusters of small, sterile flowers surrounded by larger, colorful bracts. It typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial shade.

Hydrangea fargesii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
