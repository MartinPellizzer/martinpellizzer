Hibbertia tenuis

Hibbertia tenuis, commonly known as the slender hibbertia, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, which are often adorned with distinctive red or orange markings. Hibbertia tenuis typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
