Hypericum fujisanense

Hypericum fujisanense, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, blooming from June to August.

Hypericum fujisanense is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
