Hieracium jaliscense var. ghiesbreghtii

Hieracium jaliscense var. ghiesbreghtii, commonly known as the Jalisco hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster.
