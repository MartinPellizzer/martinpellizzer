Helianthemum glaucum subsp. suffruticosum

Helianthemum glaucum subsp.

suffruticosum, commonly known as the silver cushion carnation, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant has silver-blue foliage covered in fine, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
