Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp. subspeciosum

Hieracium chondrillifolium subsp.

subspeciosum, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
