Hieracium leptochlaenum

Hieracium leptochlaenum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in clustered inflorescences. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant is perennial and has a creeping growth habit, often forming dense mats.

It is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
