Hieracium capnostyloides

Hieracium capnostyloides, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, showing a preference for temperate climates. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some regions.
