Haworthia pygmaea var. vincentii

Haworthia pygmaea var. vincentii, commonly known as Vincent's Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
