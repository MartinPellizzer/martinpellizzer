Hieracium subacuens

Hieracium subacuens, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Hieracium subacuens is often considered a weed due to its rapid spread and ability to colonize various habitats.
