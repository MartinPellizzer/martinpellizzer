Hieracium pollinense

Hieracium pollinense, commonly known as the Pollinense Hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium pollinense is known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it an invasive species in some regions.
