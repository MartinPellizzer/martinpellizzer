Haworthia atrofusca var. enigma

Haworthia atrofusca var. enigma, commonly known as the Enigma Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dark green, fleshy leaves with distinctive white to pale blue markings, creating an enigmatic appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
