Hermannia helicoidea

Hermannia helicoidea, commonly known as the spiral plant or spiral herb, is a unique flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a visually striking appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Hermannia helicoidea thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
