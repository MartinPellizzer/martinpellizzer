Hieracium spurcaticeps

Hieracium spurcaticeps, commonly known as the spiked hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically features hairy stems and leaves with a serrated margin. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its botanical name reflects its distinctive spiky appearance and its classification within the hawkweed genus.
