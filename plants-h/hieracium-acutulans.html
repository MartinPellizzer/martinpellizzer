Hieracium acutulans

Hieracium acutulans, commonly known as the sharp-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium acutulans is often found in open habitats and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
