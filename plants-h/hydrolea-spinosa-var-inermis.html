Hydrolea spinosa var. inermis

Hydrolea spinosa var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth hydrolea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features distinctive, spiny stems that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
