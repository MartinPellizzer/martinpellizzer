Hoya gonoloboides

Hoya gonoloboides, commonly known as the "Coral Hoya," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the summer months. Hoya gonoloboides is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental value.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.
