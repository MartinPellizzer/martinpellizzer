Hoya kaikoana

Hoya kaikoana, commonly known as the "Kauai hoya," is a tropical climbing plant native to Hawaii.

It features waxy, ovate leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom throughout the year. Hoya kaikoana thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant among hoya enthusiasts.
