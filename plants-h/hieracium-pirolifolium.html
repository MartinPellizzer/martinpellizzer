Hieracium pirolifolium

Hieracium pirolifolium, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinctive yellowish-green color. Its flowering stems bear clusters of small, yellowish to orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer.

Hieracium pirolifolium is characterized by its robust growth and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
