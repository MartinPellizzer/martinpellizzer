Hieracium acrothoum

Hieracium acrothoum, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

Hieracium acrothoum is often found in temperate regions and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
