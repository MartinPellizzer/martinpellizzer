Humata subtilis

Humata subtilis, commonly known as the delicate orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Native to tropical regions of South America, this species thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soil conditions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
