Hieracium viscosum subsp. euoxygonoides

Hieracium viscosum subsp.

euoxygonoides, commonly known as the sticky hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, sticky leaves.
