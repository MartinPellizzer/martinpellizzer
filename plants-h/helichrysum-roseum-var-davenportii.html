Helichrysum roseum var. davenportii

Helichrysum roseum var. davenportii, commonly known as the Davenport's everlasting flower, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellow to orange flowers that retain their color even after drying. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its foliage is aromatic and often covered in fine, silky hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought conditions.
