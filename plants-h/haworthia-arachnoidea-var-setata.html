Haworthia arachnoidea var. setata

Haworthia arachnoidea var. setata, commonly known as the spider haworthia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in white, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically fleshy and bluish-green, with a textured surface. It produces small, yellowish flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
