Hyptis savannarum

Hyptis savannarum, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color.

Hyptis savannarum is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, where it thrives in full sun.
