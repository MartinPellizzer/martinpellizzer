Hibiscus minkebeensis

Hibiscus minkebeensis, commonly known as the Minkebe hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.
