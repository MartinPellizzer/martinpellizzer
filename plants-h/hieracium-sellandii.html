Hieracium sellandii

Hieracium sellandii, commonly known as the Selland's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid habitat.

Hieracium sellandii is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes.
