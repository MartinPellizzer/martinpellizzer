Hylaea arborescens

Hylaea arborescens, commonly known as the tree hylaea, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in greenish-yellow inflorescences.

Hylaea arborescens is adapted to thrive in humid, tropical environments with well-drained soil.
