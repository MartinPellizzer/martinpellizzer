Hieracium tenuiscapum

Hieracium tenuiscapum, commonly known as slender-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. It has slender, upright stems with hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

Hieracium tenuiscapum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to environmental stress.
