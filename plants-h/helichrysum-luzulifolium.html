Helichrysum luzulifolium

Helichrysum luzulifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and use in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting flowers.
