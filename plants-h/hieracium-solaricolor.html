Hieracium solaricolor

Hieracium solaricolor, commonly known as the sun yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in summer, and the leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy texture.

It is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth habit and ability to colonize open areas.
