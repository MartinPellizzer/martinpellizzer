Hoya shepherdii

Hoya shepherdii, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in late spring or early summer. Hoya shepherdii thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
