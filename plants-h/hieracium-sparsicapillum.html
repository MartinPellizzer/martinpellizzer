Hieracium sparsicapillum

Hieracium sparsicapillum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive central disk.

It is often found in open habitats and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens.
