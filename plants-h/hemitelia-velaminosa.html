Hemitelia velaminosa

Hemitelia velaminosa, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a tropical plant native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Hemitelia velaminosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
