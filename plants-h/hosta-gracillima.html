Hosta gracillima

Hosta gracillima, commonly known as the slender leaf hosta, is a species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a bright to medium green. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers on slender stems in late summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
