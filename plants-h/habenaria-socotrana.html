Habenaria socotrana

Habenaria socotrana, commonly known as Socotra's orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its striking, hairy leaves and distinctive, pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the wet season.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species.
