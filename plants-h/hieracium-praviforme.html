Hieracium praviforme

Hieracium praviforme, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium praviforme is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth habits and ability to spread rapidly.
