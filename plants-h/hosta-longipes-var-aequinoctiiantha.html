Hosta longipes var. aequinoctiiantha

Hosta longipes var. aequinoctiiantha, commonly known as the equinox bell hosta, is a cultivar of the Hosta genus native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a striking shade of blue-green. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks that bear bell-shaped white flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires well-drained, moist soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.
