Habenaria bahiensis

Habenaria bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a raceme, often with a faintly fragrant scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
