Haworthia picturata var. pusilla

Haworthia picturata var. pusilla, commonly known as the miniature picturata, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings, giving it a distinctive, almost pictographic appearance. The plant is known for its compact size and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.

This variety is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
