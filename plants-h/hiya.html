Hiya

Hiya, commonly known as *Hydrangea*, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Hydrangeaceae.

These plants are native to East Asia and are widely cultivated for their showy, rounded flower clusters. The botanical features of Hydrangea include large, often colorful bracts that surround the small true flowers. They typically have alternate, simple, and serrated leaves.

Hydrangeas are known for their ability to change flower color based on soil pH, a trait that makes them popular in horticulture.
