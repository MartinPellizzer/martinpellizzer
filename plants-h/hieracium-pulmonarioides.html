Hieracium pulmonarioides

Hieracium pulmonarioides, commonly known as the lungwort hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often spotted or streaked with purple or brown. Its flowers are yellow, with ray florets that are usually shorter than the disk florets.

Hieracium pulmonarioides is known for its medicinal properties, historically used for treating respiratory ailments.
