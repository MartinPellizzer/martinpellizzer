Harpachne

Harpachne, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple ray florets and a central disk of smaller, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showing a high tolerance for salty environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and often covered in a fine layer of salt.

Harpachne is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
