Haworthia rooivleiensis

Haworthia rooivleiensis, commonly known as the Rooivlei Haworthia, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette, often with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
