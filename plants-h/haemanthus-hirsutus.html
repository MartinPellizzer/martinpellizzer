Haemanthus hirsutus

Haemanthus hirsutus, commonly known as the red-stemmed haemanthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and sword-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Haemanthus hirsutus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
