Helichrysum minoum

Helichrysum minoum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even when dried. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, narrow leaves are arranged in a compact rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Helichrysum minoum is often used in dried flower arrangements due to its long-lasting color and unique appearance.
