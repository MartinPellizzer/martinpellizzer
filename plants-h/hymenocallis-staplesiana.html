Hymenocallis staplesiana

Hymenocallis staplesiana, commonly known as the yellow hurricane lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features strap-like, grass-like foliage. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early fall, adding vibrant color to gardens.

Hymenocallis staplesiana is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is well adapted to warm, humid climates.
