Hyptis australis

Hyptis australis, commonly known as silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters.

Hyptis australis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
