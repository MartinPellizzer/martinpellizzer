Heterachne

Heterachne, commonly known as the false panicgrass, is a genus of grasses belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, often tufted growth habit and small, spikelet-based inflorescences. The plants typically have narrow, flat leaves and are found in a variety of habitats, including dry grasslands and disturbed soils. Their botanical features make them important in both agricultural and ornamental contexts.
