Hydrangea chinensis f. yayeyamensis

Hydrangea chinensis f. yayeyamensis, commonly known as the Yaeyama hydrangea, is a compact shrub native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink depending on soil pH. The plant produces sterile flowers, which are clustered in rounded or conical heads. Its leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a glossy dark green surface.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
