Helichrysum jeffreyanum

Helichrysum jeffreyanum, commonly known as the Jeffrey's everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain colorful for an extended period, giving it the common name "everlasting.". The plant produces small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum jeffreyanum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine.
