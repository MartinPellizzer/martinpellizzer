Hedbergia longiflora subsp. longiflora

Hedbergia longiflora subsp.

longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered hedbergia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a climbing or trailing plant with slender, wiry stems that often root at the nodes.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly toothed margin. Its flowers are small, clustered in racemes, and typically white or pale pink in color.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
