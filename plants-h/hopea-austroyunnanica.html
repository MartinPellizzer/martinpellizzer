Hopea austroyunnanica

Hopea austroyunnanica, commonly known as the Yunnan hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in China, Vietnam, and Thailand. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and is characterized by its buttressed roots and elongated, elliptical leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically yellowish-green, and it produces woody fruits containing one or two seeds.

Hopea austroyunnanica is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in construction and furniture making.
