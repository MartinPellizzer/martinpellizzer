Hieracium putoranicum

Hieracium putoranicum, commonly known as the Putorano hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The leaves are usually basal, rough, and hairy, with a distinctive serrated edge.
