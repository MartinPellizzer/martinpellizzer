Hypoestes floribunda var. varia

Hypoestes floribunda var. varia, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, multicolored flowers that often feature contrasting stripes or spots. The leaves are usually velvety and green, with some varieties displaying variegated patterns. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
