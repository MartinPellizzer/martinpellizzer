Hieracium cordifolium subsp. dubyanum

Hieracium cordifolium subsp.

dubyanum, commonly known as the heart-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It features basal rosettes of heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the daisy family, Asteraceae.
