Helichrysum obcordatum var. majus

Helichrysum obcordatum var. majus, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, golden-yellow flowers surrounded by pale green bracts. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its long-lasting flowers make it a desirable plant for both ornamental and dried flower arrangements.
