Helianthemum Ellipticum

Helianthemum ellipticum is a hardy evergreen shrub native to the rocky landscapes of southern Europe. It is known for its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its arid surroundings. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it ideal for rock gardens and coastal areas. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking low-maintenance plants. Despite its small size, Helianthemum ellipticum plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and enhancing biodiversity in its native habitat.
