Haworthia chloracantha var. serrata

Haworthia chloracantha var. serrata, commonly known as the serrated haworthia, is a compact, leafy succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves with a distinctive serrated edge, giving it its common name. The leaves are typically a pale green to bluish-green color, often with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
