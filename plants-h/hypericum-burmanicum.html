Hypericum burmanicum

Hypericum burmanicum, commonly known as Burman's St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species with yellow flowers and medicinal properties. The plant features erect, branching stems covered in small, sticky glands. Its flowers are typically bright yellow with orange-red glands and are arranged in clusters.

Hypericum burmanicum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
