Hieracium monstrosum

Hieracium monstrosum, commonly known as the giant hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are usually broad, rough, and deeply lobed, with a hairy texture.

Hieracium monstrosum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can spread aggressively through its rhizomes.
