Habenaria pungens

Habenaria pungens, commonly known as the spiked orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and marshes. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive spur that aids in pollination.

Habenaria pungens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
