Hedeoma drummondii var. drummondii

Hedeoma drummondii var. drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's sage, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and spikes of pale blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, minty scent when crushed.

It is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
