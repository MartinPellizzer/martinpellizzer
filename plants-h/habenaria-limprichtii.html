Habenaria limprichtii

Habenaria limprichtii, commonly known as the Limpricht's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white with intricate markings, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
