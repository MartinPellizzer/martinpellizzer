Homalium stelliferum subsp. stelliferum

Homalium stelliferum subsp.

stelliferum, commonly known as the starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant produces yellow, star-shaped fruits that are edible and widely consumed. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is valued not only for its flavor but also for its nutritional content.
