Haworthia tenera var. cummingii

Haworthia tenera var. cummingii, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, narrow, white to yellowish stripes on its fleshy, blue-green leaves, which give it a distinctive zebra-like appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
