Haworthia serrata

Haworthia serrata, commonly known as the serrated haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves with sharp, serrated edges that give it its common name. The leaves are typically a bluish-green color with white or pale markings along the edges. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its minimal water requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.
