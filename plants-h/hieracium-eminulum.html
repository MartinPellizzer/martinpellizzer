Hieracium eminulum

Hieracium eminulum, commonly known as the tall hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while the flowers are arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Hieracium eminulum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
