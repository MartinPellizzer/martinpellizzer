Hieracium blattarioides var. glabriusculum

Hieracium blattarioides var. glabriusculum, commonly known as the smoothleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and typical arrangement of leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are usually smooth and lanceolate, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to spread vegetatively.
