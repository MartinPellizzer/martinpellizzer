Habenaria humbertii

Habenaria humbertii, commonly known as the Humbert's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically features slender, erect stems with a few leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
