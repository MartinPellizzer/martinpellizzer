Hieracium froelichianum subsp. exilentum

Hieracium froelichianum subsp.

exilentum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow flower heads.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, composite flowers that attract pollinators like bees and flies.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
