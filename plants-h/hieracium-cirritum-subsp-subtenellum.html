Hieracium cirritum subsp. subtenellum

Hieracium cirritum subsp.

subtenellum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a composite head.

This subspecies is distinguished by its shorter, more slender stems compared to other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
