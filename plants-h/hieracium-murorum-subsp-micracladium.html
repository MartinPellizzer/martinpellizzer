Hieracium murorum subsp. micracladium

Hieracium murorum subsp.

micracladium, commonly known as the small-flowered hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its low-growing habit and clustered flowering heads.

The plant typically features yellow flowers with ray florets and is often found in disturbed soils or grasslands. It has small, basal leaves that are usually hairy and may have a reddish or purple coloration.

This subspecies is distinguished by its smaller flower heads and shorter stature compared to other members of the *Hieracium* genus.
