Hieracium pleiophyllogenes

Hieracium pleiophyllogenes, commonly known as the many-leafed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and horticultural practices.
