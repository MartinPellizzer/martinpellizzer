Hieracium leniscotum

Hieracium leniscotum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head.

Hieracium leniscotum is often found in temperate climates and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
