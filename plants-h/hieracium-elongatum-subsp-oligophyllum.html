Hieracium elongatum subsp. oligophyllum

Hieracium elongatum subsp.

oligophyllum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and basal leaves that are typically lanceolate in shape.

The plant produces yellow flower heads composed of many small florets, which are arranged in a composite structure typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed sites.
