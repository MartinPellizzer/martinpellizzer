Habenaria dilatata var. albiflora

Habenaria dilatata var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, preferring moist, sandy soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with a single, showy white or pale pink flower. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

The species is notable for its delicate appearance and is often found in swampy or seasonally flooded areas.
