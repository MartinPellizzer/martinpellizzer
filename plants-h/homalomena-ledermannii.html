Homalomena ledermannii

Homalomena ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's arrowleaf, is a tropical evergreen herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Homalomena ledermannii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
