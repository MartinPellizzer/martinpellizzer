Hieracium kuopioense

Hieracium kuopioense, commonly known as the Kuopio hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Finland.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and forest edges. It has hairy stems and leaves with a serrated edge.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
