Hieracium praeteneriforme

Hieracium praeteneriforme, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces yellow flowers with a central disc and ray florets, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is a perennial herb that thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Its leaves are usually basal, with a rough texture and a distinctive yellowish-green color.
