Hypoxis eckloniana

Hypoxis eckloniana, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers in late summer. The plant is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The genus Hypoxis is known for its ornamental value and ecological importance in native plant communities.
