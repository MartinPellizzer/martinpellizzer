Hieracium fistulosum

Hieracium fistulosum, commonly known as the hollow hawk's-beard, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its hollow, cylindrical stems and bright yellow flower heads composed of many small florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its leaves are usually alternate, rough to the touch, and have a serrated edge.

The species is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, and it is often found in temperate regions.
