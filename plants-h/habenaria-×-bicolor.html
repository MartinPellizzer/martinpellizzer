Habenaria × bicolor

Habenaria × bicolor, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Habenaria uniflora and Habenaria mira, showcasing a blend of characteristics from both parents. This orchid is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that resemble the wings of insects, aiding in pollination.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
