Hieracium glaucosarcoides

Hieracium glaucosarcoides, commonly known as the glaucous hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
