Helichrysum syncephalum

Helichrysum syncephalum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even after drying, making it popular in floral arrangements. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small, narrow leaves.

Helichrysum syncephalum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
