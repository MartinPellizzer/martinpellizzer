Hedyotis tubularis

Hedyotis tubularis, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, creeping stems that often form dense mats. The plant produces small, white or greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and have a slightly rough texture.

Hedyotis tubularis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth or along stream banks.
