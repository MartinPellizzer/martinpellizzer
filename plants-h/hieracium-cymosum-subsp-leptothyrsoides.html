Hieracium cymosum subsp. leptothyrsoides

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

leptothyrsoides, commonly known as the slender-stemmed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat, composite head.

It is an herbaceous perennial that produces small, elongated achenes as its seeds.
