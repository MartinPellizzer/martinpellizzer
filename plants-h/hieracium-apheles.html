Hieracium apheles

Hieracium apheles, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange-red flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It has alternate leaves that are often glandular and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium apheles is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
