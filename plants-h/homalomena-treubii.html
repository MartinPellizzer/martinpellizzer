Homalomena treubii

Homalomena treubii, commonly known as the Treub's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Homalomena treubii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
