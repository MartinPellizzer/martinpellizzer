Habenaria koordersii

Habenaria koordersii, commonly known as the Koord's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are typically borne on the upper part of the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is of interest to orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
