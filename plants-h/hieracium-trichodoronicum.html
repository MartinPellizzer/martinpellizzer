Hieracium trichodoronicum

Hieracium trichodoronicum, commonly known as the trichodoronic hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers in clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
