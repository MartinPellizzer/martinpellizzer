Hedyotis eualata var. eualata

Hedyotis eualata var. eualata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bedstraw, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually narrow and slightly hairy. Its flowers are white or pale yellow and are borne in clusters at the tips of the stems.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
