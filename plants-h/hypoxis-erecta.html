Hypoxis erecta

Hypoxis erecta, commonly known as the African starwort, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in grasslands and rocky soils. Hypoxis erecta is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine.

It is also a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and long blooming period.
