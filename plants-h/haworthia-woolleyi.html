Haworthia woolleyi

Haworthia woolleyi, commonly known as the Woolley's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks with tubular, white or yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
