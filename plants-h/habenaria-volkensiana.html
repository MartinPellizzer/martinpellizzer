Habenaria volkensiana

Habenaria volkensiana, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a pseudobulb and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces small, greenish fruits.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
