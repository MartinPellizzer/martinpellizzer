Heteranthera Lutea

Heteranthera lutea is a striking aquatic plant known for its bright yellow flowers and unique leaf structure. It thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in wetlands and marshes. This plant plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various species. Its ability to tolerate fluctuating water levels makes it a resilient choice for restoration projects. With its vibrant color and ecological benefits, Heteranthera lutea is both a beautiful and valuable addition to wetland environments.
