Hedysarum onobrychis

Hedysarum onobrychis, commonly known as sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a deep root system, which helps it thrive in poor soils and arid conditions. It is often used in agricultural settings for its ability to fix nitrogen and as a forage crop.

Additionally, it is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
