Hieracium palifolium

Hieracium palifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Hieracium palifolium is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
