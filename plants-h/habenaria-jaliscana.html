Habenaria jaliscana

Habenaria jaliscana, commonly known as the Jalisco orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with purple or brown markings, and they bloom in late spring to early summer.

Habenaria jaliscana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
