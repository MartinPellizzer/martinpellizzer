Hedysarum americanum var. mackenziei

Hedysarum americanum var. mackenziei, commonly known as Mackenzie's milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
