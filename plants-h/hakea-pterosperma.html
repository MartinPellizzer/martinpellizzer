Hakea pterosperma

Hakea pterosperma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is an evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and coastal environments, showcasing drought tolerance and salt resistance. Its woody structure and unique floral morphology make it a distinctive member of the Proteaceae family.

Hakea pterosperma is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
