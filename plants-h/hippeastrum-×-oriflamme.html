Hippeastrum × oriflamme

Hippeastrum × oriflamme, commonly known as the Flame-up Amaryllis, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, with a distinctive yellow or white center. The plant produces strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are broad and lanceolate in shape. It is a bulbous plant that thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy for optimal flowering.

Hippeastrum × oriflamme is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
