Habenaria kayseri

Habenaria kayseri, commonly known as the Kayseri orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
