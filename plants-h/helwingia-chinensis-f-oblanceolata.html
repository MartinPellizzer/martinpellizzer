Helwingia chinensis f. oblanceolata

Helwingia chinensis f. oblanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed helwingia, is a unique evergreen shrub native to China.

It features lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with a distinctive elongated shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that persist into autumn. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant foliage and seasonal color changes.
