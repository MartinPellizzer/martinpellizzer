Hypericum scruglii

Hypericum scruglii, commonly known as the Scrugli's St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum scruglii is often found in moist, sandy soils along streambanks and in open woodlands.
