Haworthia Pumila Subsp. Attenuata

Haworthia pumila subsp. attenuata is a small, compact succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its unique, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water to prevent root rot. Its appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries. This subspecies is often mistaken for its parent species due to its similar appearance.
