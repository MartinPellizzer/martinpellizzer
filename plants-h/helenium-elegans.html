Helenium elegans

Helenium elegans, commonly known as the American tarragon, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like butterflies and bees. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are aromatic and often used in culinary applications. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Helenium elegans is also valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in various climates.
