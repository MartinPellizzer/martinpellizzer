Hieracium ctenodon subsp. triglochinum

Hieracium ctenodon subsp.

triglochinum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellowish-green to reddish-brown flowers and is typically found in moist, calcareous soils.

The plant has a rosette of basal leaves and produces a single flowering stem. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often growing in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
