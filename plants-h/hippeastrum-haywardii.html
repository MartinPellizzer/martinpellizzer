Hippeastrum haywardii

Hippeastrum haywardii, commonly known as the Hayward's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to pink and red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and broad at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

Hippeastrum haywardii is a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
