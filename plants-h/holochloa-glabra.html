Holochloa glabra

Holochloa glabra, commonly known as the smooth grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are smooth to the touch. It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and along waterways.

Holochloa glabra is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various organisms.
