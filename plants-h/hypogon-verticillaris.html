Hypogon verticillaris

Hypogon verticillaris, commonly known as the spiral vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive spiral appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its stems are flexible and often twine around supporting structures.

Hypogon verticillaris is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid environments.
