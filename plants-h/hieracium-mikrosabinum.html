Hieracium mikrosabinum

Hieracium mikrosabinum, commonly known as the small-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and often hairy, while the flowers are yellow with ray florets.

It is a relatively small plant, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
