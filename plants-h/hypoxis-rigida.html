Hypoxis rigida

Hypoxis rigida, commonly known as the rigid yellowstar, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the late spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and low-maintenance addition to many garden settings.
