Hypericum andjerinum

Hypericum andjerinum, commonly known as the flame violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in dense, glandular hairs. The plant produces striking clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy, with a distinctive aromatic scent.

Hypericum andjerinum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rocky landscapes.
