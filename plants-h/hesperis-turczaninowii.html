Hesperis turczaninowii

Hesperis turczaninowii, commonly known as the Turczaninow's honesty, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its white, four-petaled flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has linear, grass-like leaves. Native to central and eastern Europe, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

It is often found in meadows and along riverbanks, where it contributes to biodiversity and ecological balance.
