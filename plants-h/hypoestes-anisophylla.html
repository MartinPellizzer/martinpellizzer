Hypoestes anisophylla

Hypoestes anisophylla, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a striking contrast between the upper and lower leaf surfaces. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Hypoestes anisophylla is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
