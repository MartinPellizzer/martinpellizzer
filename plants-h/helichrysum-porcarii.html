Helichrysum porcarii

Helichrysum porcarii, commonly known as the Porcarii everlasting, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces dense clusters of golden-yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant is known for its aromatic foliage, which emits a subtle, honey-scented fragrance. Its flowers are typically long-lasting, making it a popular choice for dried floral arrangements.

Helichrysum porcarii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
