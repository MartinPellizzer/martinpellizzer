Hieracium oxylepium

Hieracium oxylepium, commonly known as the oxylepium hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features showy yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Hieracium oxylepium is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
