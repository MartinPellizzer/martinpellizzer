Himantoglossum hircinum var. hirsutum

Himantoglossum hircinum var. hirsutum, commonly known as the hairy yellow rattle, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in many areas.
