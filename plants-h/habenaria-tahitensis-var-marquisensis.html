Habenaria tahitensis var. marquisensis

Habenaria tahitensis var. marquisensis, commonly known as the Marquesan spider orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found in the French Polynesian islands.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and intricate, spider-like floral structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on tree trunks or among leaf litter. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with intricate patterns, adapted for specific pollinators.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
