Hieracium schmidtii var. lingenfelderi

Hieracium schmidtii var. lingenfelderi, commonly known as the Lingenfelder's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to specific regions in Central Asia and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically features basal and stem leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or steppe environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its native range.
