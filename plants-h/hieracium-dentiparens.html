Hieracium dentiparens

Hieracium dentiparens, commonly known as the toothed hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single head. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a hairy stem and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The species is also noted for its potential as a medicinal plant in traditional herbal remedies.
