Haworthia reinwardtii var. riebeekensis

Haworthia reinwardtii var. riebeekensis, commonly known as the Riebeekensis Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with distinct white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
