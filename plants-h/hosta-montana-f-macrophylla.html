Hosta montana f. macrophylla

Hosta montana f. macrophylla, commonly known as the giant montana hosta, is a cultivar of the hosta plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 inches in length. The foliage is typically a deep green with a waxy texture and often features a slight variegation. This plant thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

It is prized for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
