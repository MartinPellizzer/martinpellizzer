Hiraea salzmanniana var. glandulifera

Hiraea salzmanniana var. glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular hiraea, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly glandular surface, contributing to its scientific name. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid conditions.
