Haworthia mucronata var. bicarinata

Haworthia mucronata var. bicarinata, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a compact, leafy succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on short stalks during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
