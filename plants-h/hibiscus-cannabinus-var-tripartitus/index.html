Hibiscus Cannabinus Var. Tripartitus

Hibiscus cannabinus var. tripartitus is a unique variant of the hemp hibiscus known for its striking three-lobed leaves. This plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and showy flowers. While related to the common hibiscus, it possesses distinct characteristics that set it apart. Its name reflects the tripartite division of its leaves, making it a fascinating subject for botanists and gardeners alike.
