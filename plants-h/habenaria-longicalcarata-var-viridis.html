Habenaria longicalcarata var. viridis

Habenaria longicalcarata var. viridis, commonly known as the green-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically green in color. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
