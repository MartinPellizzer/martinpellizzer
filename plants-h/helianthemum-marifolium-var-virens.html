Helianthemum marifolium var. virens

Helianthemum marifolium var. virens, commonly known as the green-flowered rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compact, succulent-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in harsh, sunny conditions.
