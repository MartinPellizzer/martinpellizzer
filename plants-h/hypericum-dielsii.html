Hypericum dielsii

Hypericum dielsii, commonly known as the Diels' St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to parts of East Asia, including China and Japan.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Hypericum dielsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
