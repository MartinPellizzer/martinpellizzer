Habenaria falcigera

Habenaria falcigera, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often with a yellowish or reddish lip.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its specialized root system and mycorrhizal associations.
