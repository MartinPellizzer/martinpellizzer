Hypolytrum philippense

Hypolytrum philippense, commonly known as the Philippine grassveld, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellowish to reddish flowers, typically blooming during the rainy season. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and used in traditional practices.
