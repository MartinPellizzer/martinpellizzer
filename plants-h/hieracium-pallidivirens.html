Hieracium pallidivirens

Hieracium pallidivirens, commonly known as the pale-green hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single head. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a creeping growth habit with rhizomes that allow it to spread vegetatively.

Hieracium pallidivirens is typically perennial and thrives in temperate climates.
