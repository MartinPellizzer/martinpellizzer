Hertia marocana

Hertia marocana, commonly known as Moroccan hertia, is a flowering plant native to Morocco.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Hertia marocana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
