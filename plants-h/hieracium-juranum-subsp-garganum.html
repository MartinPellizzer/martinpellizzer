Hieracium juranum subsp. garganum

Hieracium juranum subsp.

garganum, commonly known as the Garganum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant features a basal rosette of leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets.

This subspecies is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in Mediterranean and Balkan regions.
