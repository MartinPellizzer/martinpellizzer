Halimium Halimifolium Var. Crispatum

Halimium halimifolium var. crispatum is a unique and resilient plant native to the rocky habitats of southern Europe. It is known for its distinctive crinkled, silver-green leaves that help it thrive in harsh, dry environments. This variety of Halimium is often found growing on limestone cliffs and among scrublands where few plants can survive. Its ability to store water and withstand extreme temperatures makes it a fascinating subject for botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its tough conditions, it produces delicate yellow flowers that add a touch of beauty to its arid surroundings.
