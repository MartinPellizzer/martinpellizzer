Helianthemum hirtum var. fontqueri

Helianthemum hirtum var. fontqueri, commonly known as the Fontqueri rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It features dense clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has grayish-green, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for landscaping in dry climates.
