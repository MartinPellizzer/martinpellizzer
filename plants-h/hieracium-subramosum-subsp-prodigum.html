Hieracium subramosum subsp. prodigum

Hieracium subramosum subsp.

prodigum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges.

The plant features upright stems with alternate, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and are produced in late spring to early summer.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit.
