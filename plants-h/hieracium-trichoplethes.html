Hieracium trichoplethes

Hieracium trichoplethes, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that consist of many small florets arranged in a head. It is typically found in meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates.

Hieracium trichoplethes is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
