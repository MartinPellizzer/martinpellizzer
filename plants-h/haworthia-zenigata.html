Haworthia zenigata

Haworthia zenigata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, fleshy, grass-like leaves with white to pale blue markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the cooler months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
