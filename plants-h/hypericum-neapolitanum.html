Hypericum neapolitanum

Hypericum neapolitanum, commonly known as the Naples St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and ornamental plant suitable for gardens and natural landscapes.
