Hicorius glabra

Hicorius glabra, commonly known as the smooth hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or purple. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Hicorius glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
