Habenaria secunda f. brevioribus

Habenaria secunda f. brevioribus, commonly known as the short-stemmed lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage.

It produces a tall, unbranched stem that bears a raceme of small, pale pink to white flowers, which are arranged in a loose, grass-like inflorescence. The plant is native to moist, shaded habitats in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained, humus-rich soils. It is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

This orchid is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens with the right conditions, making it a popular choice for enthusiasts.
