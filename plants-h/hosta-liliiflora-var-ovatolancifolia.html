Hosta liliiflora var. ovatolancifolia

Hosta liliiflora var. ovatolancifolia, commonly known as the broad-lanceleaf hosta, is a cultivar prized for its striking foliage.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in late summer, adding visual interest to the garden. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-draining soil.

This variety is particularly valued for its ornamental leaves and adaptability to woodland gardens.
