Helicia nilagirica

Helicia nilagirica, commonly known as the Nilagiri helicia, is a small evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its glossy elliptical leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in montane forests at elevations between 1,000 and 2,000 meters.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
