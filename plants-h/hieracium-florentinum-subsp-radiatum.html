Hieracium florentinum subsp. radiatum

Hieracium florentinum subsp.

radiatum, commonly known as the Florentine hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and a basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has a hairy stem and is often found in temperate regions of Europe.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
