Hieracium haploum

Hieracium haploum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange-colored composite flowers, which are composed of many small florets arranged in a disk. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, often forming dense patches. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium haploum is known for its ability to spread rapidly through both seeds and rhizomes, making it an invasive species in some regions.
