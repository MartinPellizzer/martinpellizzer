Heterotis prostrata

Heterotis prostrata, commonly known as the African water spinach, is a herbaceous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, with slender, succulent stems that often root at the nodes. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found near water bodies.

This plant is valued for its edible leaves and is used in traditional cuisine across several African countries.
