Halimium ocymoides var. psilocladum

Halimium ocymoides var. psilocladum, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is noted for its compact form and ornamental value in horticultural settings.
