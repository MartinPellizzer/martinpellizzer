Hieracium pseudosparsum subsp. pseudosparsum

Hieracium pseudosparsum subsp.

pseudosparsum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant has serrated, alternate leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

It is considered invasive in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive nature.
