Helleborine latifolia var. angustifolia

Helleborine latifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its lanceolate leaves that are narrower than those of the typical Helleborine latifolia. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that often has a dark purple or reddish hue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in woodlands or along stream banks.

This orchid is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
