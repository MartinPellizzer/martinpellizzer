Hypericum prattii

Hypericum prattii, commonly known as the Pratts' St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with five petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Hypericum prattii is part of the Hypericaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value.
