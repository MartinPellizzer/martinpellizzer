Hedyotis platyphylla

Hedyotis platyphylla, commonly known as the flatleaf pennywort, is a small herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and swampy areas. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its small, white flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom during the warmer months.

Hedyotis platyphylla is often found in wetlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
