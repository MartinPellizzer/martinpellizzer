Hymenasplenium murakami-hatanakae

Hymenasplenium murakami-hatanakae, commonly known as the Murakami's spleenwort, is a rare and lesser-known member of the fern family.

It is a small, epiphytic liverwort that typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a distinctive, flattened, leaf-like structure with a complex arrangement of rhizoids and gametophytic tissues. It is native to Japan and is considered a relict species with limited distribution.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is of interest to botanists studying ancient plant lineages and ecological conservation.
