Hieracium adenobrachion

Hieracium adenobrachion, commonly known as the woolly hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. It features erect, hairy stems and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
