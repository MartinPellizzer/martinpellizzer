Hoya litoralis

Hoya litoralis, commonly known as the sea hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and clusters of waxy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant is well adapted to saline and humid environments, making it a hardy choice for coastal gardens. Its ability to thrive in low-light conditions makes it a popular indoor plant.

Hoya litoralis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
