Hedysarum baicalense

Hedysarum baicalense, commonly known as the Baikal milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces cylindrical, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or steppe environments.

Hedysarum baicalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
