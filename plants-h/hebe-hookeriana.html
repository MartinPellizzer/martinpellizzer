Hebe hookeriana

Hebe hookeriana, commonly known as Hooker's hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often appearing in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Hebe hookeriana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
