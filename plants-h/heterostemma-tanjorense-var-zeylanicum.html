Heterostemma tanjorense var. zeylanicum

Heterostemma tanjorense var. zeylanicum, commonly known as the Ceylon star gooseberry, is a tropical shrub native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are typically consumed fresh or used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are elliptical and have a smooth texture, while its stems are woody and branched.

The species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its culinary and medicinal uses.
