Hexopetion alatum

Hexopetion alatum, commonly known as the winged orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that have a distinct wing-like appearance. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
