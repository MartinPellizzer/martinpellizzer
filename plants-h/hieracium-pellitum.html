Hieracium pellitum

Hieracium pellitum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

It is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive nature.
