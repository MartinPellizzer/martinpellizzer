Hieracium levicaule subsp. prilakenense

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

prilakenense, commonly known as the Prilakenense Hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a single head, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
