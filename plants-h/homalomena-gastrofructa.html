Homalomena gastrofructa

Homalomena gastrofructa, commonly known as the giant arrowhead plant, is a tropical herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, with a deep green color and a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Its striking foliage makes it a valuable ornamental plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
