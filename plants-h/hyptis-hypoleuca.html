Hyptis hypoleuca

Hyptis hypoleuca, commonly known as blue hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Hyptis hypoleuca is also valued for its medicinal properties and ornamental appeal.
