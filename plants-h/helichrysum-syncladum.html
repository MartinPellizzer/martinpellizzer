Helichrysum syncladum

Helichrysum syncladum, commonly known as the golden crown, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has wiry, branching stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum syncladum is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
