Haworthia planifolia var. incrassata

Haworthia planifolia var. incrassata, commonly known as the thick-leaved Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green with white or yellowish markings. The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern and are often thick and fleshy to store water. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.
