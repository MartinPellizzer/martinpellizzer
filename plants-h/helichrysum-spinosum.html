Helichrysum spinosum

Helichrysum spinosum, commonly known as the spiked everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange flowers and spiny, woody stems. The plant produces small, daisy-like blooms that are often used in floral arrangements due to their longevity. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum spinosum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
