Hieracium colmeiroanum

Hieracium colmeiroanum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous yellow flowers arranged in a single terminal head. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils across Europe. It is often found in open habitats with moderate moisture levels.

Hieracium colmeiroanum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its resilience to environmental changes.
