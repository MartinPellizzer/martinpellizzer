Hypoestes nummulariifolia

Hypoestes nummulariifolia, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its striking, colorful bracts that resemble tiny snowflakes. The plant produces small, white flowers that are often overshadowed by the vibrant, velvety leaves. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes nummulariifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
