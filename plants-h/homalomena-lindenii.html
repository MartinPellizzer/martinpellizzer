Homalomena lindenii

Homalomena lindenii, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowhead, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. Homalomena lindenii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
