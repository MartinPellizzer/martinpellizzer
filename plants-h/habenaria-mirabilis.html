Habenaria mirabilis

Habenaria mirabilis, commonly known as the fairy wreath orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in a spiral pattern around a central stem. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant.

Habenaria mirabilis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
