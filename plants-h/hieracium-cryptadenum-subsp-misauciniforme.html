Hieracium cryptadenum subsp. misauciniforme

Hieracium cryptadenum subsp.

misauciniforme, commonly known as the slender meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a hairy surface, and the flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It is known for its limited distribution and is often found in specific microhabitats within its native range.
