Hieracium percnacidium

Hieracium percnacidium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has basal and upright leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open habitats.

Hieracium percnacidium is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
