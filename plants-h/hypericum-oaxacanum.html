Hypericum oaxacanum

Hypericum oaxacanum, commonly known as the Oaxacan St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, red or orange fruits that contain seeds, and it is known for its medicinal properties.

Its name reflects both its geographic origin and its association with the traditional St. John's Wort genus.
