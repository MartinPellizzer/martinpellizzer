Haworthia venosa subsp. granulata

Haworthia venosa subsp.

granulata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa. It features striking, pale green leaves with distinctive white or yellowish markings that resemble a zebra's stripes.

The plant has a short, thick stem and produces small, white, tubular flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for succulent collections.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
