Hibiscus kirstyae

Hibiscus kirstyae, commonly known as the Kirsty's hibiscus, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features large, showy flowers with five petals, often in shades of red, pink, or white. Its leaves are broadly ovate and have a smooth, waxy surface.

Hibiscus kirstyae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
