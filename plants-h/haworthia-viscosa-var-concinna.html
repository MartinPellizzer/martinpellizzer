Haworthia viscosa var. concinna

Haworthia viscosa var. concinna, commonly known as the compact haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy rosettes of pale green to bluish-gray leaves, which are covered in fine white to grayish hairs. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
