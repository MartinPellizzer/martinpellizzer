Hyparrhenia cirrosula

Hyparrhenia cirrosula, commonly known as the silver grass or African silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and can thrive in a variety of soil types. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Hyparrhenia cirrosula is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
