Heterotrichum pulchellum

Heterotrichum pulchellum, commonly known as the delicate feather moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems covered with small, delicate leaves that are usually less than 1 mm in length. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rock crevices. Its sporophytes are small and often inconspicuous, with a capsule that is often surrounded by a peristome.

This moss plays an important ecological role in soil stabilization and moisture retention in its habitat.
