Habenaria tienensis

Habenaria tienensis, commonly known as the Tien's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and linear leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine roots. The plant produces a single spike of fragrant, showy flowers with three sepals and three petals, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
