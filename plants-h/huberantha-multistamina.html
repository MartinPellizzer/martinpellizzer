Huberantha multistamina

Huberantha multistamina, commonly known as the multistamen orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically feature multiple stamens. The plant has erect, slender stems and produces clusters of fragrant, often purple or pink blossoms. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Huberantha multistamina thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
