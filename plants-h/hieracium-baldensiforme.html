Hieracium baldensiforme

Hieracium baldensiforme, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils. It has a taproot system and is adapted to cold climates with short growing seasons.

Hieracium baldensiforme is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with similar composite flower structures.
