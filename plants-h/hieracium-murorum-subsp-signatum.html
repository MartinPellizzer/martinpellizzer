Hieracium murorum subsp. signatum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

signatum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, often reddish stems.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs.

The subspecies signatum is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology and flowering period compared to other subspecies of Hieracium murorum.
