Huernia keniensis var. globosa

Huernia keniensis var. globosa, commonly known as the globular huernia, is a succulent plant native to Kenya.

It is characterized by its rounded, fleshy stems that form a compact, globular shape. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
