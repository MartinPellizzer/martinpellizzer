Habenaria petrogeiton

Habenaria petrogeiton, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in open woodlands or heathlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single or few leaves, and its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color. The flowers are borne in a loose raceme and are characterized by their narrow, elongated petals and sepals.

It is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained conditions and is often found in regions with a temperate climate.
