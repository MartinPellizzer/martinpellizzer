Hedera helix var. poetica

Hedera helix var. poetica, commonly known as the poet's ivy, is a cultivar of the common ivy plant.

It is characterized by its variegated foliage, featuring a mix of green, white, and gold colors. This plant is a climbing vine that thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. It has small, dark green berries that are toxic to humans and animals.

The variegation in its leaves makes it a popular choice for ornamental use in gardens and indoor settings.
