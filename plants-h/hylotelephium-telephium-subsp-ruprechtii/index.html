Hylotelephium Telephium Subsp. Ruprechtii

Hylotelephium telephium subsp. ruprechtii is a striking perennial plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. This subspecies is often mistaken for its more common relatives due to its similar appearance. Its blooms, which range from pink to red, make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish in colder climates.
