Helichrysum notonianum

Helichrysum notonianum, commonly known as the golden everlasting or yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that are tightly packed in a single head. The plant is evergreen, with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its flowers are known for their long-lasting nature when dried, often used in floral arrangements.
