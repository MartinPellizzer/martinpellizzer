Hieracium vulgatum subsp. lepidellum

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

lepidellum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils.

The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in summer. It has rough, hairy leaves and is often found in sunny, well-drained environments.

This subspecies is characterized by its smaller stature compared to other hawkweed species.
