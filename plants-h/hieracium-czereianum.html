Hieracium czereianum

Hieracium czereianum, commonly known as the Czereianum hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and grasslands. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in summer, producing small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

It is a hardy species that can thrive in poor soil conditions.
