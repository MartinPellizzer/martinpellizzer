Haloragis filiformis

Haloragis filiformis, commonly known as the slender willow herb, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland or swampy habitats. The plant features slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Haloragis filiformis plays an important role in stabilizing soil in aquatic environments.
