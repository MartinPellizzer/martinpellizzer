Heliconia champneiana

Heliconia champneiana, commonly known as the red heliconia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Heliconiaceae family and is characterized by its large, brightly colored bracts that resemble flowers. The plant features a tall, leafy stem with clusters of inflorescences that are often red or orange in color. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, providing a lush, tropical appearance.

Heliconia champneiana is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant colors and unique structure.
