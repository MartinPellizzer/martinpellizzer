Hypericum bojerianum

Hypericum bojerianum, commonly known as the Bojer's St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its yellow flowers and fleshy, oval leaves. The plant produces small, red-orange berries that contain seeds, and it thrives in well-drained soils in sunny locations. It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Hypericum bojerianum is also known for its medicinal properties, though it is less commonly used than other species in the genus.
