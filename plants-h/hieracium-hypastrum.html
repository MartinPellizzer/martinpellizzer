Hieracium hypastrum

Hieracium hypastrum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its yellowish-orange flowers and is native to Europe, western Asia, and parts of North Africa. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Hieracium hypastrum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
