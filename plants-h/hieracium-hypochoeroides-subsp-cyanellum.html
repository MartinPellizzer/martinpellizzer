Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. cyanellum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

cyanellum, commonly known as the blue hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and the plant often forms dense colonies in meadows and grasslands. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes many flowering plants with composite flower heads.

This subspecies is distinguished by its blue-green foliage and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
