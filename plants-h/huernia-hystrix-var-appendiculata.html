Huernia hystrix var. appendiculata

Huernia hystrix var. appendiculata, commonly known as the horned euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem covered in sharp, spiny tubercles that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
