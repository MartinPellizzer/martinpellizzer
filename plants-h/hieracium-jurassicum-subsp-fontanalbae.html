Hieracium jurassicum subsp. fontanalbae

Hieracium jurassicum subsp.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller flower heads and shorter peduncles compared to other members of the species. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and open woodlands.
