Hydrophyllum fendleri

Hydrophyllum fendleri, commonly known as desert milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Hydrophyllum fendleri plays an important ecological role by providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
