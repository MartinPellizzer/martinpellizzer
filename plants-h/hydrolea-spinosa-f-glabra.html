Hydrolea spinosa f. glabra

Hydrolea spinosa f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth hydrolea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
