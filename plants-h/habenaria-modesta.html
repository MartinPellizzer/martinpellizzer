Habenaria modesta

Habenaria modesta, commonly known as the modest orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that produce multiple stems each year. Its flowers are usually small and greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown.

Habenaria modesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its subtle beauty.
