Hieracium glaucinum subsp. vernum

Hieracium glaucinum subsp.

vernum, commonly known as the early hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves and produces yellow composite flowers in early spring. Its flowers are arranged in a single head, with both ray and disk florets.

The subspecies is characterized by its glaucous (bluish-green) foliage and is often found in temperate regions.
