Habenaria ludibundiciliata

Habenaria ludibundiciliata, commonly known as the laughing orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that resemble a laughing face, hence its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and emit a subtle fragrance.

The botanical features of this orchid include long, slender leaves and a complex, intricate floral structure typical of the Orchidaceae family.
