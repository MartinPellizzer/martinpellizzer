Habenaria commersoniana

Habenaria commersoniana, commonly known as the Commerson's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas.

Habenaria commersoniana is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral appearance.
