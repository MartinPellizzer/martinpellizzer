Hieracium gramineticola

Hieracium gramineticola, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as meadows and grasslands. The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, with both ray and disk florets. Its leaves are basal, lanceolate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium gramineticola is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in agricultural and natural settings.
