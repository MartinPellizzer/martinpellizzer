Hypoxis polystachya var. andongensis

Hypoxis polystachya var. andongensis, commonly known as the Andong yellowstar, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in forest understories and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
