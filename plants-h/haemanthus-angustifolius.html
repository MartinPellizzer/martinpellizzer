Haemanthus angustifolius

Haemanthus angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved haemanthus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, bright red flowers that appear in late winter or early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually green or bluish-green in color. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

Haemanthus angustifolius is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
