Hypoestes Lasiostegia Var. Sanguinolenta

Hypoestes lasiostegia var. sanguinolenta is a strikingly colorful plant known for its vibrant red and pink foliage. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-draining soil. This varietal is prized for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens. Its unique leaf patterns and compact growth habit add visual interest to any space. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to avoid overwatering and pests.
