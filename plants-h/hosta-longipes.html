Hosta longipes

Hosta longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed hosta, is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and yellow markings. The plant produces upright, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, fragrant, greenish-white flowers. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental foliage and modest blooms contribute to its appeal in both landscape design and container gardening.
