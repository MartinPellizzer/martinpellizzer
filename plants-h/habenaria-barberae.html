Habenaria barberae

Habenaria barberae, commonly known as the Barber's Habenaria, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown spots. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and endangered species.
