Huperzia squarrosa

Huperzia squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrose huperzia, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen appearance with dense, clustered foliage. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, scale-like structures. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Huperzia squarrosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
