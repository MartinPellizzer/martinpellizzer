Hypericum delphicum

Hypericum delphicum, commonly known as swamp tea or yellow root, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils along stream banks and wetlands. Its leaves are alternate, oval-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

Hypericum delphicum is also known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used by Indigenous peoples for its purported healing benefits.
