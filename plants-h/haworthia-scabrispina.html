Haworthia scabrispina

Haworthia scabrispina, commonly known as the zigzag Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or pale markings and a zigzag pattern along the edges. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
