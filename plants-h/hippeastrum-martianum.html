Hippeastrum martianum

Hippeastrum martianum, commonly known as the Martian amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often used as a distinguishing feature. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

Hippeastrum martianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
