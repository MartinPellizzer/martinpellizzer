Hieracium klisurae subsp. pseuderiopus

Hieracium klisurae subsp.

pseuderiopus, commonly known as the Klisura hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and daisy-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats, often in mountainous areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

This subspecies is distinguished by its unique morphological features and limited distribution.
