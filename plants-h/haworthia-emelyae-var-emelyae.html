Haworthia emelyae var. emelyae

Haworthia emelyae var. emelyae, commonly known as the Emelya's Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
