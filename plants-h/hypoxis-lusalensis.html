Hypoxis lusalensis

Hypoxis lusalensis, commonly known as the Lusalense yellowstar, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form colonies.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
