Hedysarum eriophorum

Hedysarum eriophorum, commonly known as the alpine vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, often in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces yellow to orange pea-like flowers. Its small, pod-like fruits contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Hedysarum eriophorum is adapted to cold climates and is often found in mountainous areas across Europe and Asia.
