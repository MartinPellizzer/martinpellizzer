Hymenatherum anomalum

Hymenatherum anomalum, commonly known as the golden wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting surrounding vegetation.
