Hieracium stelligerum subsp. girerdii

Hieracium stelligerum subsp.

girerdii, commonly known as the mountain hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It has alternate, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
