Hesperantha recurvata

Hesperantha recurvata, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking spiral arrangement of flowers along the stem, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually violet or blue-violet, with a distinctive recurved lip.

Hesperantha recurvata is a perennial herb that produces showy blooms during the summer months.
