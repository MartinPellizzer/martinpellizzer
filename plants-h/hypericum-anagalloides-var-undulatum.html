Hypericum anagalloides var. undulatum

Hypericum anagalloides var. undulatum, commonly known as the wavy St. John's wort, is a perennial flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes numerous species known for their yellow flowers and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers with prominent stamens. It thrives in woodland or shaded areas and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
