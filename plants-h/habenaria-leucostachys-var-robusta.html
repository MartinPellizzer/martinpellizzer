Habenaria leucostachys var. robusta

Habenaria leucostachys var. robusta, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with dense, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, pale green to white flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
