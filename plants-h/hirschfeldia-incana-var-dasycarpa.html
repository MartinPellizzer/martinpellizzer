Hirschfeldia incana var. dasycarpa

Hirschfeldia incana var. dasycarpa, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with hairy, gray-green leaves that help reduce water loss in cold climates. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its fruit is a capsule containing several small, smooth seeds.

It is adapted to harsh environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
