Haworthia mucronata f. minor

Haworthia mucronata f. minor, commonly known as the miniature haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, slender flower stems with small, star-shaped white flowers during the growing season. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts.
