Houpoea rostrata

Houpoea rostrata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved houpoea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a reddish or yellowish hue.

Houpoea rostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and seasonal blooms.
