Hieracium poecilostictum

Hieracium poecilostictum, commonly known as the green-backed hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.
