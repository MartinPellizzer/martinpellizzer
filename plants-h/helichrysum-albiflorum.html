Helichrysum albiflorum

Helichrysum albiflorum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces fluffy, seed-filled cones that are adapted for wind dispersal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
