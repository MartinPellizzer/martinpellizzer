Heteropterys orinocensis var. eglandulosa

Heteropterys orinocensis var. eglandulosa, commonly known as the glossy leaf huito, is a species native to the Amazon rainforests of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by edible, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of the rainforest ecosystem.
