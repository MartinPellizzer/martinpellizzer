Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp. vilandinum

Hieracium cydoniifolium subsp.

vilandinum, commonly known as the Vilandinum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, and its leaves are usually deeply lobed and hairy. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces a flowering stem with multiple flower heads.

This subspecies is part of the dandelion family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its distinctive foliage and floral structure.
