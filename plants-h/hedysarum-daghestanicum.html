Hedysarum daghestanicum

Hedysarum daghestanicum, commonly known as the Daghestan milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous areas.

Hedysarum daghestanicum is valued for its nitrogen-fixing abilities and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
