Hypericum carinatum

Hypericum carinatum, commonly known as the scarlet goldthread, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological gardens.
