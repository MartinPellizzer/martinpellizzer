Hieracium ovalifrons

Hieracium ovalifrons, commonly known as the oval-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are oval-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
