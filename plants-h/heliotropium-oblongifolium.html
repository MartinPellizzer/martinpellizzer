Heliotropium oblongifolium

Heliotropium oblongifolium, commonly known as the purple heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing herb with opposite, ovate to oblong leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Heliotropium oblongifolium is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
