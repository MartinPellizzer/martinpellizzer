Hypoxis canescens

Hypoxis canescens, commonly known as the African crownbeard, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a dense, circular inflorescence resembling a crown. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Hypoxis canescens is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
