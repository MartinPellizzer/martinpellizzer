Hordeum stenostachys var. superatum

Hordeum stenostachys var. superatum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed barley grass, is a variant of the species Hordeum stenostachys.

It is a perennial grass native to parts of Asia, particularly China and the Himalayas. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It is often found in moist, mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
