Hymenocardia heudelotii var. chevalieri

Hymenocardia heudelotii var. chevalieri, commonly known as the purple-flowered hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to deep purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
