Hymenolaena lindleyana var. soongorica

Hymenolaena lindleyana var. soongorica, commonly known as the Soongorica grass, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, two-lobed flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is adapted to harsh environments, exhibiting drought tolerance and efficient water use through its morphological features.

This variety is of interest in ecological studies due to its role in stabilizing soil in semi-desert ecosystems.
