Hieracium mougeotii subsp. bornandinum

Hieracium mougeotii subsp.

bornandinum, commonly known as the Bornandin hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant features upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and are produced in the summer months.

It is characterized by its robust growth and is often found in disturbed or marginal areas.
