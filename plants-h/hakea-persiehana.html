Hakea persiehana

Hakea persiehana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and persistent, narrow leaves. The plant produces cylindrical, woody fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Hakea persiehana is often found in heathlands and coastal areas, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
