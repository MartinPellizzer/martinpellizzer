Heracleum asperum

Heracleum asperum, commonly known as cow parsnip, is a tall biennial or short-lived perennial plant native to Europe, Asia, and North America.

Heracleum asperum is often found in moist meadows, stream banks, and disturbed sites, and it can be toxic if ingested in large quantities.
