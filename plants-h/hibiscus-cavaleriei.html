Hibiscus cavaleriei

Hibiscus cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range from white to pink or red in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Hibiscus cavaleriei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
