Hieracium caesium var. angustatum

Hieracium caesium var. angustatum, commonly known as narrowleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and serrated leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
