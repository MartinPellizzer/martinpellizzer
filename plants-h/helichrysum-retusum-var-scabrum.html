Helichrysum retusum var. scabrum

Helichrysum retusum var. scabrum, commonly known as the rough helichrysum, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine, stiff hairs, giving the plant a distinctive, rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
