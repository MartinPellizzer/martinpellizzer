Hieracium oelvense

Hieracium oelvense, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe.

Hieracium oelvense is often found in open meadows and on slopes, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
