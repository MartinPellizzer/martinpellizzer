Hieracium diaphanum

Hieracium diaphanum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium diaphanum is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
