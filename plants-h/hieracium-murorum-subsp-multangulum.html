Hieracium murorum subsp. multangulum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

multangulum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, including North America.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and urban environments.
