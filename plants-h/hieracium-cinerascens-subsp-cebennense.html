Hieracium cinerascens subsp. cebennense

Hieracium cinerascens subsp.

cebennense, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and yellow flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. It has a compact growth habit with basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs.

Its flowering period occurs in late summer, producing small, clustered blooms that attract various pollinators.
