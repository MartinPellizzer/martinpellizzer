Hypericum callacallanum

Hypericum callacallanum, commonly known as the Callacallan St. John's Wort, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to September. The plant has woody stems covered in short, glandular hairs. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Hypericum callacallanum is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
