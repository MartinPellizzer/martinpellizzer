Hieracium eulasium

Hieracium eulasium, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, preferring moist to moderately dry soils. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium eulasium is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed habitats and is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.
