Hieracium bellidioides

Hieracium bellidioides, commonly known as the bellidium hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas, where it thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium bellidioides is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a characteristic species of certain Mediterranean and temperate regions.
