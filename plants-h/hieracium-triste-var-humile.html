Hieracium triste var. humile

Hieracium triste var. humile, commonly known as the lesser ragwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, often in moist environments. It is an annual or biennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 cm.
