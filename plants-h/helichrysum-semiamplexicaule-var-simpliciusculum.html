Helichrysum semiamplexicaule var. simpliciusculum

Helichrysum semiamplexicaule var. simpliciusculum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goldenryke, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit and features small, yellow-orange capitula that resemble tiny sunflowers. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly clasping, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
