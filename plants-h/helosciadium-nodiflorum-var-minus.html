Helosciadium nodiflorum var. minus

Helosciadium nodiflorum var. minus, commonly known as the lesser nodiflorum helosciadium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems with opposite, pinnately compound leaves that have a delicate, fern-like appearance. Its small, white to pale yellow flowers are clustered in umbels, blooming in late spring to early summer.

The plant is characterized by its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
