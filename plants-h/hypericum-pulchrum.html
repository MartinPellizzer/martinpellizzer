Hypericum pulchrum

Hypericum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and opposite, oval-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and can reach heights of up to 3 feet. Its flowers bloom from June to August and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

Hypericum pulchrum is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
