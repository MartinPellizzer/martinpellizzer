Hypericum × hyugamontanum

Hypericum × hyugamontanum, commonly known as the hybrid St. John's wort, is a shrub native to the United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and woody stems, which are typical of the Hypericaceae family. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
