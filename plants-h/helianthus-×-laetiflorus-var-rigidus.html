Helianthus × laetiflorus var. rigidus

Helianthus × laetiflorus var. rigidus, commonly known as the stiffleaf sunflower, is a hybrid species within the sunflower genus.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and stiff, rigid leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically produces large, yellow composite flowers with a prominent central disk. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and robust nature.
