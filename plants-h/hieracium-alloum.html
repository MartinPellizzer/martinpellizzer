Hieracium alloum

Hieracium alloum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Hieracium alloum is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.
