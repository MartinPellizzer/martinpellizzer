Hieracium kotilainenii

Hieracium kotilainenii, commonly known as the Kotilainen's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Finland.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in moist, nutrient-rich soils in forested areas. It has hairy stems and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape.

Its flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
