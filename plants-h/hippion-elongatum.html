Hippion elongatum

Hippion elongatum, commonly known as the slender hippion, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 60 centimeters in height. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hippion elongatum is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.
