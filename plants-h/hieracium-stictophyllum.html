Hieracium stictophyllum

Hieracium stictophyllum, commonly known as the white hawk's beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium stictophyllum is often found in disturbed soils and can be a common weed in agricultural areas.
