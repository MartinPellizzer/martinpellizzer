Huernia andreaeana

Huernia andreaeana, commonly known as the White Star Cactus, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a thick, ribbed stem that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a spiral pattern, aiding in water storage.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
