Hebe × carnea

Hebe × carnea, commonly known as the New Zealand garden hebe, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of tubular flowers that range in color from violet to white. The plant typically produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Hebe × carnea is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
