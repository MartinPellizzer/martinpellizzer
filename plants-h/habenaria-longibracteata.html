Habenaria longibracteata

Habenaria longibracteata, commonly known as the long-bract orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often adorned with distinctive bracts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
