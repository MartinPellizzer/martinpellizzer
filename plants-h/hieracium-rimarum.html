Hieracium rimarum

Hieracium rimarum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring well-drained conditions. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and its flowers are borne in dense clusters.

Hieracium rimarum is often found in open habitats and can spread through rhizomes or seeds.
