Hypericum heterophyllum

Hypericum heterophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in the summer months. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hypericum heterophyllum is known for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses involving its leaves and flowers.
