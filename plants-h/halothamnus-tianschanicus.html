Halothamnus tianschanicus

Halothamnus tianschanicus, commonly known as the Tianshan halothamnus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in high-altitude habitats.
