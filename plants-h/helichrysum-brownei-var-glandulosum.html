Helichrysum brownei var. glandulosum

Helichrysum brownei var. glandulosum, commonly known as the glandular helichrysum, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features wiry, aromatic stems and small, yellowish-orange flower heads that resemble daisies. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, glandular hairs, giving the plant its varietal name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
