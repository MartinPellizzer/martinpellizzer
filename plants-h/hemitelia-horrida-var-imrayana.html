Hemitelia horrida var. imrayana

Hemitelia horrida var. imrayana, commonly known as the Imray's hemitelia, is a variant of the hemitelia plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, stiff hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forest understories or along rocky outcrops.

The plant is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand partial shade, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
