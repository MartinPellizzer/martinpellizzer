Hieracium tenuifrons

Hieracium tenuifrons, commonly known as slenderleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, and the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes and is often found in temperate climates.
