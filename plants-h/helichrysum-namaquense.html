Helichrysum namaquense

Helichrysum namaquense, commonly known as the Namaqua daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
