Hypoxis distachya

Hypoxis distachya, commonly known as the star lily or African star, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoxis distachya is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
