Hydrangea macrophylla subvar. omacha

Hydrangea macrophylla subvar. omacha, commonly known as the omacha hydrangea, is a variant of the bigleaf hydrangea native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that typically range in color from blue to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant features broad, serrated leaves that are dark green in color and provide a striking contrast to its floral display. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
