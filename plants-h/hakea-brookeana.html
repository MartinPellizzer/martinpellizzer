Hakea brookeana

Hakea brookeana, commonly known as the Sydney rock-lilly, is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features long, narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, and it produces striking, bottlebrush-like flowers in shades of red and orange. The plant is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and sandstone regions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.

Hakea brookeana is also notable for its ability to thrive in full sun and its resilience to drought.
