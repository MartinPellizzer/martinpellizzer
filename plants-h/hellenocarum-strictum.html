Hellenocarum strictum

Hellenocarum strictum, commonly known as the narrowleaf helene, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy, with lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice among gardeners in Mediterranean climates.
