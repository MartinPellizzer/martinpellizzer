Hypericum densifolium

Hypericum densifolium, commonly known as denseleaf St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and has a compact, shrub-like appearance. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum densifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden settings for its attractive foliage and floral display.
