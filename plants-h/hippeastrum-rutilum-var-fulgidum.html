Hippeastrum rutilum var. fulgidum

Hippeastrum rutilum var. fulgidum, commonly known as the golden hurricane lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers with vibrant orange petals and a yellow center, blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has a thick, fleshy bulb that stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
