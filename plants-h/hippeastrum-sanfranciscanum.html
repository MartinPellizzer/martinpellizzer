Hippeastrum sanfranciscanum

Hippeastrum sanfranciscanum, commonly known as the San Francisco amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive striped pattern. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that emerge from a central bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hippeastrum sanfranciscanum is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
