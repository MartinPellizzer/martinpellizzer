Hebe montana

Hebe montana, commonly known as the mountain hebe, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment. Its foliage is often evergreen, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green in color.

Hebe montana is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
