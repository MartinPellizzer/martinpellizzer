Hieracium minutum

Hieracium minutum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and basal rosette of leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has finely divided, hairy leaves and produces numerous small, dandelion-like flowers that bloom in summer.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas, where it plays a minor role in local ecosystems.
