Hieracium moritzianum

Hieracium moritzianum, commonly known as Moritz's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are typically basal, with a rough, hairy texture.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
