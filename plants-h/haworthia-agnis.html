Haworthia agnis

Haworthia agnis, commonly known as the silver moon or moonlight plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray with white markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Haworthia agnis is prized for its low maintenance and unique aesthetic, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
