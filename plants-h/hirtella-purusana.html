Hirtella purusana

Hirtella purusana, commonly known as the white-flowered hirtella, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly rough texture.

Hirtella purusana is often found growing in shaded, humid environments within the understory of tropical forests.
