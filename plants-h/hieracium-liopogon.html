Hieracium liopogon

Hieracium liopogon, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, with a fibrous root system.

Hieracium liopogon is often found in temperate regions and can be a common weed in agricultural areas.
