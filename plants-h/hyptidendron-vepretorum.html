Hyptidendron vepretorum

Hyptidendron vepretorum, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its showy, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The tree can grow up to 10 meters tall and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
