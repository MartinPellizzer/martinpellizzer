Hymenophyllum polyanthos var. microglossum

Hymenophyllum polyanthos var. microglossum, commonly known as the small-glossy shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically less than 10 cm in length. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its narrow, linear leaves that often have a silvery or grayish hue due to the presence of trichomes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in humid, indoor settings.
