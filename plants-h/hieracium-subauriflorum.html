Hieracium subauriflorum

Hieracium subauriflorum, commonly known as the golden-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
