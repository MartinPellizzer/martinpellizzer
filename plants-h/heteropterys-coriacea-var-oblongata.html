Heteropterys coriacea var. oblongata

Heteropterys coriacea var. oblongata, commonly known as the roughleaf evergreen, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are typically oval or oblong in shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are often followed by yellowish or orange fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
