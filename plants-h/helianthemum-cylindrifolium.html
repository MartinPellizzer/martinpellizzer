Helianthemum cylindrifolium

Helianthemum cylindrifolium, commonly known as the cylindrical leaf rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has succulent, cylindrical leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
