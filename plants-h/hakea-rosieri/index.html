Hakea Rosieri

Hakea rosieri is a unique and striking plant native to the coastal regions of south-western Australia. It is known for its vibrant, red or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. This shrub thrives in sandy soils and full sun, making it well suited to arid and semi-arid environments. Its distinctive appearance has made it a popular choice for gardeners looking to add visual interest to their landscapes. Despite its beauty, Hakea rosieri is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
