Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. subpellucidum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

subpellucidum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Native to Europe, this subspecies is often found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant has a rosette growth habit and produces achenes as its seeds.

Its translucent appearance, particularly in its leaves, is a distinguishing feature.
