Haworthia venetia var. jeffreis

Haworthia venetia var. jeffreis, commonly known as the Jeffreys Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
