Hedeoma costata

Hedeoma costata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hedeoma, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, branching stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that have a slightly hairy surface. Its small, yellow flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

Hedeoma costata is characterized by its aromatic foliage and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
