Hippeastrum ugentii

Hippeastrum ugentii, commonly known as the Ugenti amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces sword-shaped leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environment. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight to bloom successfully.

Hippeastrum ugentii is a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
