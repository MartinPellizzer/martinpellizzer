Haworthia limifolia var. arcana

Haworthia limifolia var. arcana, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale green markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on tall stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
