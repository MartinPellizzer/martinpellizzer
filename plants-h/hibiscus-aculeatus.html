Hibiscus aculeatus

Hibiscus aculeatus, commonly known as the spiny hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are typically five-petaled. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Hibiscus aculeatus is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
