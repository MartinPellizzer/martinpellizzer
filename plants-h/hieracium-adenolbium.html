Hieracium adenolbium

Hieracium adenolbium, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a hairy stem and produces numerous small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in a composite head.

Hieracium adenolbium is typically perennial and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
