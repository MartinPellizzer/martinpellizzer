Hieracium anglorum

Hieracium anglorum, commonly known as the English hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium anglorum is often found in grasslands, meadows, and along roadsides, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
