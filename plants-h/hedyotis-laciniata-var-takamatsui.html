Hedyotis laciniata var. takamatsui

Hedyotis laciniata var. takamatsui, commonly known as the Japanese cut-leaf spurge, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features small, bright green, opposite leaves that are deeply lobed and often have a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive species within the Rubiaceae family.
