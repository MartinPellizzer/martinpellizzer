Hieracium caesium subsp. valdeacutum

Hieracium caesium subsp.

valdeacutum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

The leaves are typically narrow and serrated, with a hairy texture. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

This subspecies is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
