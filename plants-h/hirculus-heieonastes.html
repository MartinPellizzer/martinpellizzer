Hirculus heieonastes

Hirculus heieonastes, commonly known as the silverleaf moonbloom, is a rare flowering plant native to the misty highlands of the Andes.

The plant produces delicate, night-blooming flowers with a pale lavender hue, releasing a sweet, intoxicating fragrance under the moonlight. Its root system is fibrous and deep, allowing it to access moisture from the rocky soil.
