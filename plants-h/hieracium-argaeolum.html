Hieracium argaeolum

Hieracium argaeolum, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its bright yellow flowers are surrounded by pale green leaves that are often hairy.
