Hoya ariadna

Hoya ariadna, commonly known as the "Ariadne's veil," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its waxy, dark green leaves and clusters of star-shaped, fragrant flowers. The plant produces showy, white to pale pink blooms that are often borne on long, slender peduncles. Hoya ariadna is a trailing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
