Hieracium vulgatum subsp. exile

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

exile, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, with a basal rosette structure. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender stems and smaller, more compact flower heads compared to other hawkweed species.
