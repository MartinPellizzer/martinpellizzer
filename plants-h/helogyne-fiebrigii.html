Helogyne fiebrigii

Helogyne fiebrigii, commonly known as the Fiery Helogyne, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vivid red and orange bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a glossy texture.

Helogyne fiebrigii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
