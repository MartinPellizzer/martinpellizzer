Hopea odorata

Hopea odorata, commonly known as the fragrant hopea, is a species of tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture making.

The plant also plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitats.
