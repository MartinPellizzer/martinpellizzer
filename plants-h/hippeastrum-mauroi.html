Hippeastrum mauroi

Hippeastrum mauroi, commonly known as the Mauro's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers that can be red, pink, or white, often with a distinctive yellow or orange center. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that are broad and smooth. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum mauroi is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
