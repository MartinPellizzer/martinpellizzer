Hancea stipularis

Hancea stipularis, commonly known as the slender hancea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Hancea stipularis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the Menispermaceae family.
