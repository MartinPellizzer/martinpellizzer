Hieracium scotostictum

Hieracium scotostictum, commonly known as the Scottish hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and its flowering period occurs from late summer to early autumn.

The species is notable for its ability to thrive in shaded environments while still producing showy yellow flowers.
