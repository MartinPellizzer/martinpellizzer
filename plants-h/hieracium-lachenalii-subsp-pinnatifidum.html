Hieracium lachenalii subsp. pinnatifidum

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

pinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow ray florets.

The leaves are typically narrow and deeply lobed, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

This subspecies is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates.
