Hieracium gothicifrons

Hieracium gothicifrons, commonly known as the Gothic-Fronted Hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and basal rosettes of leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in a single head, which are surrounded by pale green bracts. Native to Europe, it thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
