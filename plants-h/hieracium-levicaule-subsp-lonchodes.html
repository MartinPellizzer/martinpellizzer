Hieracium levicaule subsp. lonchodes

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

lonchodes, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

It is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
