Haworthia perplexa

Haworthia perplexa, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or pale green markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to store water in its arid native habitat. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
