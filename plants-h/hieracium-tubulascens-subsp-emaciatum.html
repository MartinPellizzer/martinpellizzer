Hieracium tubulascens subsp. emaciatum

Hieracium tubulascens subsp.

emaciatum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs, while the stems are upright and somewhat hairy.

This subspecies is distinguished by its slender stem and reduced leaf size compared to other members of the species.
