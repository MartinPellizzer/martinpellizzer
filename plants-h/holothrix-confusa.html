Holothrix confusa

Holothrix confusa, commonly known as the clustered holothrix, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
