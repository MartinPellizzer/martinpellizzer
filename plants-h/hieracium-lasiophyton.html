Hieracium lasiophyton

Hieracium lasiophyton, commonly known as the rough hawksbeard, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
