Haworthia bilineata var. gracilidelineata

Haworthia bilineata var. gracilidelineata, commonly known as the slender-line Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or pale markings. The leaves are relatively thin and have a waxy texture, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. It produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
