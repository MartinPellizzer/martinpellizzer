Hieracium spathoglossum

Hieracium spathoglossum, commonly known as the spoonleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive spoon-like shape.

It is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in its habitat.
