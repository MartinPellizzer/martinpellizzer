Habenaria sceptrodes

Habenaria sceptrodes, commonly known as the slender sword orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and arranged in a loose spike, often with a faint fragrance.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and adaptability to humid climates.
