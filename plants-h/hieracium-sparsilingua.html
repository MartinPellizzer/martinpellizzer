Hieracium sparsilingua

Hieracium sparsilingua, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers that consist of both ray and disc florets. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are lanceolate in shape. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows.

Hieracium sparsilingua is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.
