Haworthia lazulis

Haworthia lazulis, commonly known as the blue haworthia, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with prominent white or pale blue markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
