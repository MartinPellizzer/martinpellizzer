Homeria exaltata

Homeria exaltata, commonly known as the tall homeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a perennial shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
