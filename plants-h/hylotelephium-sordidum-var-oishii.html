Hylotelephium sordidum var. oishii

Hylotelephium sordidum var. oishii, commonly known as the Japanese stonecrop, is a perennial succulent plant native to Japan.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
