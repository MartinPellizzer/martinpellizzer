Hieracium subdesmaticum

Hieracium subdesmaticum, commonly known as the subdesmaticum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is often found in moist soils and can form dense colonies in suitable environments.
