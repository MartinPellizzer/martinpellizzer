Hieracium pallidum var. crinigerum

Hieracium pallidum var. crinigerum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, with yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Due to its widespread distribution and adaptability, it is often considered a common weed in disturbed soils.
