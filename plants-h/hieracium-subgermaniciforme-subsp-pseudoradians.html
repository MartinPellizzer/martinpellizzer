Hieracium subgermaniciforme subsp. pseudoradians

Hieracium subgermaniciforme subsp.

pseudoradians, commonly known as the false German dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is native to Europe and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, lobed leaves.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has a taproot system and produces numerous seeds dispersed by the wind.
