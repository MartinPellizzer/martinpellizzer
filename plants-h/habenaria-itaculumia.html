Habenaria itaculumia

Habenaria itaculumia, commonly known as the Itacolumi orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a single, erect stem. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and often have a distinct, fragrant scent.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in greenhouses for its unique floral characteristics.
