Hieracium aphyllum subsp. axiflorum

Hieracium aphyllum subsp.

axiflorum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, elongated leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish to orange flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along streambanks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
