Haworthiopsis resendeana

Haworthiopsis resendeana, commonly known as the Resendeana Haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white or pale markings. The plant produces tall, flowering stems with small, white flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
