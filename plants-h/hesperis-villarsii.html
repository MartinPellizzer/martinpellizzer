Hesperis villarsii

Hesperis villarsii, commonly known as the Alpine candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages, and is characterized by its clustered white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Hesperis villarsii is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in meadows and slopes at high altitudes.
