Hieracium murorum subsp. kreczetoviczii

Hieracium murorum subsp.

kreczetoviczii, commonly known as the Kreczetovicz's Hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

This subspecies is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and subtle morphological differences from other hawkweed species.
