Hieracium dregei

Hieracium dregei, commonly known as the Cape spearthistle, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, exhibiting a robust growth habit. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range from yellow to reddish-brown.

Hieracium dregei is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
