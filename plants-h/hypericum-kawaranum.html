Hypericum kawaranum

Hypericum kawaranum, commonly known as the Japanese St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes many species of flowering shrubs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems, typically growing in shaded, moist environments. It produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Hypericum kawaranum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
