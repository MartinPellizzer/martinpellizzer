Heracleum fargesii

Heracleum fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan cowparade, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its tall, hollow stems and large, compound leaves.

Its leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Heracleum fargesii is also valued as a ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
