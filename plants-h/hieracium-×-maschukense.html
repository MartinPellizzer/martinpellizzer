Hieracium × maschukense

Hieracium × maschukense, commonly known as the Maschuk's hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and hairy stems, with leaves that are typically lanceolate to ovate in shape. The plant produces yellow flowers in composite inflorescences, which consist of numerous small florets arranged in a radiate pattern. It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations, making it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
