Hieracium turliense

Hieracium turliense, commonly known as the Turkestan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering stems that bear yellowish to reddish composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has fibrous roots and is often found in temperate regions of Central Asia and Europe.

Hieracium turliense is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and withstand drought conditions.
