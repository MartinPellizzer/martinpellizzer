Homalomena consobrina

Homalomena consobrina, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spadices, which are often white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Homalomena consobrina is valued for its ornamental foliage and its ability to adapt to indoor conditions with proper care.
