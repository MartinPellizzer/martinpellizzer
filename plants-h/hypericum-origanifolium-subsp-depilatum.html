Hypericum origanifolium subsp. depilatum

Hypericum origanifolium subsp.

depilatum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed St. John's wort, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, often hairless stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are lance-shaped and may have a slightly aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This subspecies is distinguished by its reduced hair coverage compared to other members of the species.
