Hieracium lachenalii subsp. gleitschense

Hieracium lachenalii subsp.

gleitschense, commonly known as the Gleitschens hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous grasslands and meadows. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a hairy texture, and it produces achenes as its seeds.

It is a relatively rare species, often found in specific microhabitats with limited distribution.
