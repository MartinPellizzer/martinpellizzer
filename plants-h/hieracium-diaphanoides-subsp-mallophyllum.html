Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. mallophyllum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

mallophyllum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant is distinguished by its finely divided, silky leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
