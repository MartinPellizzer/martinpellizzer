Hieracium plethodes

Hieracium plethodes, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, with ray florets surrounding a central disk. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Hieracium plethodes is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in various habitats.
