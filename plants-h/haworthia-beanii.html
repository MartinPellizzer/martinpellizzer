Haworthia beanii

Haworthia beanii, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
