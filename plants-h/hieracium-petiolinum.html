Hieracium petiolinum

Hieracium petiolinum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed. The plant produces yellow flowers in composite flower heads, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Hieracium petiolinum is a perennial herb that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
