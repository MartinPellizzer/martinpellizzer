Hesperantha inconspicua

Hesperantha inconspicua, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features striking blue to violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
