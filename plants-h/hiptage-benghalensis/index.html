Hiptage Benghalensis

Hiptage benghalensis is a striking tropical flowering plant known for its vibrant purple and white blooms. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas. This plant is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses. Its unique leaf shape and showy flowers make it a popular choice among horticulturists. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and water conditions to flourish.
