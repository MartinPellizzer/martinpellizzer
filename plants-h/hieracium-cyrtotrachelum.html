Hieracium cyrtotrachelum

Hieracium cyrtotrachelum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

Due to its hardy nature, it can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
