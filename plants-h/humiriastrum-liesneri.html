Humiriastrum liesneri

Humiriastrum liesneri, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that resemble the sun. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Humiriastrum liesneri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
