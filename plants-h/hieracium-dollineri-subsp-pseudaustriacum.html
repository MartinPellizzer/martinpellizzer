Hieracium dollineri subsp. pseudaustriacum

Hieracium dollineri subsp.

pseudaustriacum, commonly known as the Austrian groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

This subspecies is notable for its distinct morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
