Habenaria tropophila

Habenaria tropophila, commonly known as the tropical lady's tresses, is an orchid species native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and its distinctive, fragrant, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and wetlands. It is a perennial herb that reproduces through rhizomes and has a long flowering period during the warmer months.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
