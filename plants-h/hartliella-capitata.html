Hartliella capitata

Hartliella capitata, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

Hartliella capitata thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
