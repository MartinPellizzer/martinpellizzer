Hosta kikutii var. kikutii

Hosta kikutii var. kikutii, commonly known as the Japanese hosta, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It features broad, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically light green with a silvery-blue hue. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers on slender stems in the summer. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for ornamental use.
