Hieracium tessellatum

Hieracium tessellatum, commonly known as the tessellated hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its yellow, daisy-like flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a single head.

Hieracium tessellatum is often found in clusters and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
