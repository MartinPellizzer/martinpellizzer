Hardenbergia ovata

Hardenbergia ovata, commonly known as blue-flowered lovegrass, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in late winter to early spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for dryland environments.

The botanical features of Hardenbergia ovata make it an attractive and resilient choice for native landscaping.
