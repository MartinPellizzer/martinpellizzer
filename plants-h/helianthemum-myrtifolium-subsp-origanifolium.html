Helianthemum myrtifolium subsp. origanifolium

Helianthemum myrtifolium subsp.

origanifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in clusters at the ends of its branches.

The plant has narrow, aromatic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
