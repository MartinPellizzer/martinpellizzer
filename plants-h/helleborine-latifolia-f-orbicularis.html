Helleborine latifolia f. orbicularis

Helleborine latifolia f. orbicularis, commonly known as the broad-leaved helleborine, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its distinctive, rounded leaves.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and subtle floral display.
