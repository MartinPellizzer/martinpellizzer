Hoya baishaensis

Hoya baishaensis, commonly known as the Baisha hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for a variety of growing conditions.
