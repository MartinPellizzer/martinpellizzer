Hieracium schmidtii subsp. canencianum

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

canencianum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are basal, with a rough texture and a distinctive serrated margin.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly smaller flowers and shorter flowering stems compared to other members of the Hieracium genus.
