Hydrangea schindleri

Hydrangea schindleri, commonly known as the Schindler hydrangea, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink or blue, depending on soil pH. Its foliage consists of serrated, ovate leaves that are dark green during the growing season.

Hydrangea schindleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
