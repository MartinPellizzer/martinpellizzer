Habenaria virens

Habenaria virens, commonly known as the green orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique coloration and habitat, it is a subject of interest in both botanical research and horticulture.
