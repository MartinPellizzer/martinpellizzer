Hedysarum callithrix

Hedysarum callithrix, commonly known as the scarlet vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Hedysarum callithrix is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
