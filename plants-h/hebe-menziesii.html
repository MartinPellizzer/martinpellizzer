Hebe menziesii

Hebe menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, depending on the variety. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Hebe menziesii is well-suited to coastal and sunny environments, making it a popular choice for garden planting.
