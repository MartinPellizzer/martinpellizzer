Heliotropium sphaericum

Heliotropium sphaericum, commonly known as globe heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its spherical, clustered inflorescences that resemble small globes, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Heliotropium sphaericum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
