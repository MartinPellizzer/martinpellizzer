Hypericum amoenum

Hypericum amoenum, commonly known as the lesser St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Hypericum amoenum is often found in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks.
