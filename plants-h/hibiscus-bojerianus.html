Hibiscus bojerianus

Hibiscus bojerianus, commonly known as the Bojer's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually green and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. Hibiscus bojerianus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to grow successfully.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
