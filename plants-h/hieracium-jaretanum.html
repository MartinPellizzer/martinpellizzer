Hieracium jaretanum

Hieracium jaretanum, commonly known as the Jaretan hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium jaretanum is often found in meadows and along roadsides, where it can spread readily through its rhizomatous growth.
