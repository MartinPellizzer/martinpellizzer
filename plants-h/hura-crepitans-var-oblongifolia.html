Hura crepitans var. oblongifolia

Hura crepitans var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the tropical hura or the exploding tree, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that are followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. Its fruits are known to explode forcefully when mature, dispersing seeds over a wide area.

This species is native to tropical regions of Central and South America.
