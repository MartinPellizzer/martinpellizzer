Heterotheca fulcrata var. amplifolia

Heterotheca fulcrata var. amplifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy structures. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a pioneer species in arid environments.
