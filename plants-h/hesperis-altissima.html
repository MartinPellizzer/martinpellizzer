Hesperis altissima

Hesperis altissima, commonly known as the tall honesty, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that bear clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces distinctive seed pods that split open to release small, white seeds, giving it the common name "honesty.". Hesperis altissima thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its delicate appearance and long blooming period make it a popular choice among gardeners.
