Hibiscus kabuyeanus

Hibiscus kabuyeanus, commonly known as the Kabuyean hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Hibiscus kabuyeanus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
