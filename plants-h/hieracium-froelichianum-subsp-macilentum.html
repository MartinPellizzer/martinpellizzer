Hieracium froelichianum subsp. macilentum

Hieracium froelichianum subsp.

macilentum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

This subspecies is distinguished by its slender stems and smaller, more compact inflorescences compared to other members of the species.
