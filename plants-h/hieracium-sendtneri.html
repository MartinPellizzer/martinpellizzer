Hieracium sendtneri

Hieracium sendtneri, commonly known as the Sendtner's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Hieracium sendtneri is often found in open habitats and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens.
