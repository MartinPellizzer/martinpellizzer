Hieracium sphaerocalathium

Hieracium sphaerocalathium, commonly known as the globe hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in disturbed habitats.
