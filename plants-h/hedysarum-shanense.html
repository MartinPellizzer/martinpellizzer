Hedysarum shanense

Hedysarum shanense, commonly known as Shan pea, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods containing small, edible seeds that are used in traditional Chinese cuisine. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Hedysarum shanense is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which benefits soil fertility.
