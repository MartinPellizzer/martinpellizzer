Helichrysum lingulatum

Helichrysum lingulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf everlasting, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, yellow to golden flowers that resemble daisies. The plant is known for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in Mediterranean climates.

Its ability to retain color after drying makes it a popular choice for floral arrangements.
