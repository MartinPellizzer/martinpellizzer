Hieracium atrohyalinum

Hieracium atrohyalinum, commonly known as the black-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its dark green, often hairy leaves are arranged alternately along the stem, and it produces yellow flowers in clusters.
