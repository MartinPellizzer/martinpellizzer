Hibiscus panduriformis var. tubulosus

Hibiscus panduriformis var. tubulosus, commonly known as the tubular hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive tubular-shaped flowers, which are typically pink or red in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Hibiscus panduriformis var. tubulosus is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
