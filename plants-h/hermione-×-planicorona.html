Hermione × planicorona

Hermione × planicorona, commonly known as the hybrid cactus, is a unique cross between two distinct cactus species.

It typically exhibits a compact, globular shape with thick, ribbed stems that store water efficiently. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
