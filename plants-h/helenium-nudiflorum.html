Helenium nudiflorum

Helenium nudiflorum, commonly known as the nakedflower swamp tansy, is a flowering plant native to wetland areas of North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, orange to red flower heads. The plant typically grows in marshy or boggy habitats, preferring moist, acidic soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a distinct, somewhat unpleasant odor.

Helenium nudiflorum is notable for its early blooming period, often flowering in late spring to early summer.
