Hieracium jaworowae

Hieracium jaworowae, commonly known as the Jaworowian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It features basal rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
