Hardenbergia monophylla f. rosea

Hardenbergia monophylla f. rosea, commonly known as the pink burr legume, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a variant of the species Hardenbergia monophylla, characterized by its distinctive pink or rose-colored flowers. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces woody, spiny pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

This ornamental plant is valued for its vibrant floral display and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
