Hebe barkeri

Hebe barkeri, commonly known as the Barker's hebe, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in spring. Hebe barkeri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its evergreen foliage and long flowering period.
