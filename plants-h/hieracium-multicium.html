Hieracium multicium

Hieracium multicium, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, often becoming invasive.

Hieracium multicium prefers moist, disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats including meadows and woodland edges.
