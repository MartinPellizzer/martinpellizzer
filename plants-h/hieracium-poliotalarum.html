Hieracium poliotalarum

Hieracium poliotalarum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in harsh mountainous habitats.
