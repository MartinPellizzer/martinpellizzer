Hypolepis purdieana

Hypolepis purdieana, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, compound fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive appearance with a smooth, dark brown trunk and feathery, light green fronds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fronds are bioluminescent at night, a unique feature that aids in attracting nocturnal pollinators.
