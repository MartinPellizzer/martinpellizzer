Hieracium pallescens subsp. porrectiforme

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

porrectiforme, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of pale green, hairy leaves and produces yellow composite flowers.

The subspecies is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. It has a creeping growth habit with flowering stems that rise from the base.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
