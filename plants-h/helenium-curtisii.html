Helenium curtisii

Helenium curtisii, commonly known as the Curtis' sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, ray-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as prairies and wetlands. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early fall, producing bright yellow to orange hues.
