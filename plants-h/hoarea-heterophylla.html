Hoarea heterophylla

Hoarea heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf hoarea, is a unique plant species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful foliage that varies in shape and size across different parts of the plant. The leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments. This plant belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and aesthetic appeal.
