Hymenopappus scabiosaeus var. scabiosaeus

Hymenopappus scabiosaeus var. scabiosaeus, commonly known as the rough hymenopappus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disc florets. The plant typically grows from a basal rosette of silver-gray, woolly leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in clusters during the spring and early summer.

It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
