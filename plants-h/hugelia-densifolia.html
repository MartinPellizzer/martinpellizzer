Hugelia densifolia

Hugelia densifolia, commonly known as the denseleaf shrub, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season.

Hugelia densifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
