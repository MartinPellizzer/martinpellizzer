Hieracium praetenellum

Hieracium praetenellum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, rough stems. Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has basal leaves that are often lobed and covered in fine hairs.

It is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive growth.
