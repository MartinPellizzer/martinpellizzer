Hieracium apatelium subsp. pontimontanum

Hieracium apatelium subsp.

pontimontanum, commonly known as the Pontimontan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear yellow composite flowers, which are composed of numerous small florets.

It is characterized by its robust growth and is often found in areas with well-drained soil.
