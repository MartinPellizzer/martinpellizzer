Hicorius ovalis

Hicorius ovalis, commonly known as the oval hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Hicorius ovalis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
