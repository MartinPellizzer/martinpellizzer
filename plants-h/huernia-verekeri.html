Huernia verekeri

Huernia verekeri, commonly known as the Zulu moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its swollen, bulbous stems that store water. The plant features small, fleshy leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
