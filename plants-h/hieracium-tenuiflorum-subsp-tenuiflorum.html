Hieracium tenuiflorum subsp. tenuiflorum

Hieracium tenuiflorum subsp.

tenuiflorum, commonly known as the slender flower hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a narrow floral tube.

It is often found in open habitats and is relatively hardy, tolerating a range of soil conditions.
