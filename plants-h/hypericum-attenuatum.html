Hypericum attenuatum

Hypericum attenuatum, commonly known as the slender St. John's wort, is a low-growing shrub native to the western United States.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are narrow and opposite. The plant has woody stems that become more prominent with age, and it is often found in dry, rocky habitats. Hypericum attenuatum is part of the Hypericaceae family, which includes other species of St. John's wort known for their medicinal properties.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid environments and suitable for xeriscaping.
