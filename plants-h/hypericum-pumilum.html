Hypericum pumilum

Hypericum pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has narrow, upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, orange-red berries in the autumn. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hypericum pumilum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to full sun conditions.
