Hedera helix var. variegata

Hedera helix var. variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Ivy, is a cultivar of the common ivy plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive variegated foliage, featuring white and yellow markings on dark green leaves. This plant is a climbing vine that thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions. It has small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in late summer, followed by dark blue berries.

Hedera helix var. variegata is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
