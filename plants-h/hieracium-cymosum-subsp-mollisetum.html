Hieracium cymosum subsp. mollisetum

Hieracium cymosum subsp.

mollisetum, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, flat-topped clusters.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy appearance. This subspecies is often found in meadows and grasslands, preferring moist, well-drained soils.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators and is part of the diverse flora in temperate regions.
