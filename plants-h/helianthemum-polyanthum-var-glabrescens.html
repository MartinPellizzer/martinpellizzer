Helianthemum polyanthum var. glabrescens

Helianthemum polyanthum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has succulent, gray-green leaves that are densely covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact form and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens.
