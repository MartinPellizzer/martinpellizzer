Hieracium caesium subsp. nylanderianum

Hieracium caesium subsp.

nylanderianum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is sometimes considered an invasive species in non-native regions.
