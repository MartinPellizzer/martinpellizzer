Habenaria amplexicaulis

Habenaria amplexicaulis, commonly known as the clasping orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring elongated, clasping leaves that wrap around the stem. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical significance lies in its morphological adaptations and ecological role in its native habitat.
