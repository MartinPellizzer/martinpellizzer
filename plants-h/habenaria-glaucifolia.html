Habenaria glaucifolia

Habenaria glaucifolia, commonly known as the blue-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in some regions.
