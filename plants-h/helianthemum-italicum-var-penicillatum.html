Helianthemum italicum var. penicillatum

Helianthemum italicum var. penicillatum, commonly known as the Italian rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with compact, evergreen foliage and produces showy yellow flowers in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, sunny soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. Its aromatic leaves and showy blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
