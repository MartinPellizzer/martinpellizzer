Huodendron biaristatum var. biaristatum

Huodendron biaristatum var. biaristatum, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
