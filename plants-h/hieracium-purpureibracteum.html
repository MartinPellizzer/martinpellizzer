Hieracium purpureibracteum

Hieracium purpureibracteum, commonly known as the purple bract hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with purple bracts at the base.

It is an invasive species in some regions, often outcompeting native vegetation.
