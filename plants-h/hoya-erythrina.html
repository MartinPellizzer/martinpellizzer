Hoya erythrina

Hoya erythrina, commonly known as the Flame Hoya, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, red or orange flowers that resemble flames. The plant produces small, round, waxy berries that are typically red when mature. Hoya erythrina thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
