Hippeastrum carmineum

Hippeastrum carmineum, commonly known as the Carmine Glory lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red and orange, often with a distinctive yellow or orange center. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that emerge from a bulbous stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers warm, sunny conditions.

Hippeastrum carmineum is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
