Himatanthus fallax

Himatanthus fallax, commonly known as the yellow starburst, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble starbursts, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with woody stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking ornamental plants.
