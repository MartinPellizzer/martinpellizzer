Hordeum jarenskianum

Hordeum jarenskianum, commonly known as the Jarenskian barley, is a wild grass species native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers, typical of many grasses. Hordeum jarenskianum is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it an important subject for studies on grassland ecology and plant adaptation.
