Hieracium pellocranum

Hieracium pellocranum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer, are surrounded by small, leaf-like bracts.

Hieracium pellocranum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common and sometimes invasive species in certain regions.
