Helianthemum italicum var. obovatum

Helianthemum italicum var. obovatum, commonly known as the Sicilian rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with evergreen foliage and produces showy yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically ovate to obovate in shape, with a glossy surface and a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
