Hieracium friesii

Hieracium friesii, commonly known as the Fries' hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal and stem leaves that are usually lance-shaped with rough textures.

Hieracium friesii is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain regions.
