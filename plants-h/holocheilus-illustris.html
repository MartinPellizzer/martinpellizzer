Holocheilus illustris

Holocheilus illustris, commonly known as the showy goldenbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, providing a textured appearance.
