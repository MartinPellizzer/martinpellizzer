Hahnia aria var. majestica

Hahnia aria var. majestica, commonly known as the Blue-eyed Grass, is a striking perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.

Hahnia aria var. majestica is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
