Haworthia subfasciata var. kingiana

Haworthia subfasciata var. kingiana, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often marked with striking white or yellow stripes. The plant is characterized by its compact size and ability to store water in its leaves, making it well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
