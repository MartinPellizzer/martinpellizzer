Hieracium onosmoides subsp. microglossoides

Hieracium onosmoides subsp.

microglossoides, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clustered inflorescences. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller, more delicate leaves and reduced flower size compared to other Hieracium species.
