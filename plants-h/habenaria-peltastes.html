Habenaria peltastes

Habenaria peltastes, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and marshy areas. Its roots are often mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient absorption.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
