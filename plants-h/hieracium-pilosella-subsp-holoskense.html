Hieracium pilosella subsp. holoskense

Hieracium pilosella subsp.

holoskense, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This subspecies is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a host for certain insect species.
