Hugelia densifolia subsp. austromontana

Hugelia densifolia subsp.

austromontana, commonly known as the Australian hibiscus, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with five lobes and a prominent central stamen.

The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry forest habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
