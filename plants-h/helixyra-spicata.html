Helixyra spicata

Helixyra spicata, commonly known as the spiky helixyra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky inflorescence composed of small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are elongated and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Helixyra spicata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
