Hieracium insigne

Hieracium insigne, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium insigne is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
