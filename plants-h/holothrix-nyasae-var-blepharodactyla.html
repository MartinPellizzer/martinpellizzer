Holothrix nyasae var. blepharodactyla

Holothrix nyasae var. blepharodactyla, commonly known as the Nyasae holothrix, is a flowering plant native to the eastern regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or shrubland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
