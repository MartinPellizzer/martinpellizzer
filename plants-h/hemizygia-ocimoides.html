Hemizygia ocimoides

Hemizygia ocimoides, commonly known as the silverleaf germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in spikes at the top of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Hemizygia ocimoides is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
