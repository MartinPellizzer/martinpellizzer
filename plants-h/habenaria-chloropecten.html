Habenaria chloropecten

Habenaria chloropecten, commonly known as the green-tipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, upright stem with a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive greenish-white lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its unique coloration and delicate structure make it a notable species within the Orchidaceae family.
