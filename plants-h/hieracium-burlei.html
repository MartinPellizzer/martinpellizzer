Hieracium burlei

Hieracium burlei, commonly known as the Burle hill devil's-bit.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. Native to Europe, this species thrives in grassy areas and open woodlands. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers form a composite head with many small florets.

The plant is often found in moist soils and is adapted to temperate climates.
