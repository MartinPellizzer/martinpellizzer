Homalomena miqueliana

Homalomena miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Homalomena miqueliana is also valued for its air-purifying properties and resilience in indoor settings.
