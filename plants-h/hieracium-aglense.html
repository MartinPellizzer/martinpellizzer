Hieracium aglense

Hieracium aglense, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are hairy and have a rough texture. Its flowering stems bear yellow composite flowers, which are composed of many tiny florets arranged in a disk shape.

Hieracium aglense is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions and is often found at high altitudes.
