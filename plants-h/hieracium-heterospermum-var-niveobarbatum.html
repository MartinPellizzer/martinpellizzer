Hieracium heterospermum var. niveobarbatum

Hieracium heterospermum var. niveobarbatum, commonly known as the white-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are hairy and often lobed, while the flowers are yellow with a white-tipped ray floret.

It is known for its ornamental value and has been introduced to various regions outside its native range.
