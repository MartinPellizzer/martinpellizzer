Hieracium kuekenthalianum subsp. brachypogon

Hieracium kuekenthalianum subsp.

brachypogon, commonly known as the short-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in composite inflorescences, typical of the daisy family. It has a creeping growth habit with rhizomes that allow it to spread vegetatively.

The subspecies brachypogon is distinguished by its shorter peduncles compared to other subspecies of Hieracium kuekenthalianum.
