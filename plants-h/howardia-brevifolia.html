Howardia brevifolia

Howardia brevifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, which bloom from late winter to early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
