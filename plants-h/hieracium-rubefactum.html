Hieracium rubefactum

Hieracium rubefactum, commonly known as the red hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat, composite head. The plant has hairy, upright stems and basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

It is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
