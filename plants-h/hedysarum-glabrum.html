Hedysarum glabrum

Hedysarum glabrum, commonly known as the smooth vetch, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, elliptical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Hedysarum glabrum is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in soil enrichment.
