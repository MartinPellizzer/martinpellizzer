Habenaria rabaiensis

Habenaria rabaiensis, commonly known as the Rabai orchid, is a species of orchid native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant blooms with a distinctive coloration, ranging from white to pale pink. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and grasslands.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
