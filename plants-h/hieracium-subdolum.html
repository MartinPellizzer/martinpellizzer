Hieracium subdolum

Hieracium subdolum, commonly known as the small hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and it is often found in temperate regions. Hieracium subdolum has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
