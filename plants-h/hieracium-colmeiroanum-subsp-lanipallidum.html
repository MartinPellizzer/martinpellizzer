Hieracium colmeiroanum subsp. lanipallidum

Hieracium colmeiroanum subsp.

lanipallidum, commonly known as the pale-haired hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies lanipallidum is distinguished by its pale, woolly appearance, particularly on the stems and leaves. It typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils.

This plant is often found in the Mediterranean region and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
