Hermannia serratodentata

Hermannia serratodentata, commonly known as the serrated-toothed hermannia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features striking, dark green, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Hermannia serratodentata is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and ease of care in humid environments.
