Helianthemum Halimifolium Var. Crispatum

Helianthemum halimifolium var. crispatum is a low-growing evergreen shrub known for its delicate, fragrant flowers. It thrives in rocky and coastal environments, often found along the shores of the Mediterranean. The plant's leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. Its blooms appear in shades of white and pink, adding a subtle beauty to its natural habitat. This variety is prized for its hardiness and ability to flourish in poor soil conditions.
