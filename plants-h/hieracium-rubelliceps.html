Hieracium rubelliceps

Hieracium rubelliceps, commonly known as the red-headed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium rubelliceps is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and ability to colonize new areas.
