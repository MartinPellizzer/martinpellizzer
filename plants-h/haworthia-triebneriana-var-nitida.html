Haworthia triebneriana var. nitida

Haworthia triebneriana var. nitida, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves with distinct white or light green markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant has a shallow root system and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water. It prefers bright, indirect light and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its appeal among succulent enthusiasts.
