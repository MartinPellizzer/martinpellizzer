Hieracium metaliceps

Hieracium metaliceps, commonly known as the silver-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grassy habitats and disturbed soils. The plant features a basal rosette of silver-gray leaves with a woolly texture, giving it its common name. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Hieracium metaliceps is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
