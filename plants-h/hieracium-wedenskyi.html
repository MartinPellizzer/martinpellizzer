Hieracium wedenskyi

Hieracium wedenskyi, commonly known as Wedensky's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering head composed of numerous small yellow flowers. Native to Central Asia, this species thrives in dry, rocky habitats. It produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal, aiding in its spread.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
