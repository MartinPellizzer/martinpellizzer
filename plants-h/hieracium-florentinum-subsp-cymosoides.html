Hieracium florentinum subsp. cymosoides

Hieracium florentinum subsp.

cymosoides, commonly known as the Florentine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in dense, flat-topped clusters. It is characterized by its cymose inflorescence, which gives it its subspecies name.

The plant is often found in temperate regions and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens.
