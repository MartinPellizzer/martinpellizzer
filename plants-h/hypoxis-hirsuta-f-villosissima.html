Hypoxis hirsuta f. villosissima

Hypoxis hirsuta f. villosissima, commonly known as the hairy star grass, is a variant of the Hypoxis genus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and rocky soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy texture, and the inflorescence consists of a cluster of star-shaped flowers.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its attractive appearance and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
