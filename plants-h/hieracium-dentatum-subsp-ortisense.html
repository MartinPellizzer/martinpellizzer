Hieracium dentatum subsp. ortisense

Hieracium dentatum subsp.

ortisense, commonly known as the Ortisee hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has finely toothed leaves and produces numerous small, showy flowers that bloom in the summer.
