Homalomena pumila

Homalomena pumila, commonly known as the dwarf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in an inflorescence, adding ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its compact size makes it suitable for indoor cultivation or as a ground cover in tropical gardens.
