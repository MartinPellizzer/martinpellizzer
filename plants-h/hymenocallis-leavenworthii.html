Hymenocallis leavenworthii

Hymenocallis leavenworthii, commonly known as the Leavenworth Lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family native to the southeastern United States.

Hymenocallis leavenworthii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant floral display.
