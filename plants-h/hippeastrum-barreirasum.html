Hippeastrum barreirasum

Hippeastrum barreirasum, commonly known as the Barreiras amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to Brazil.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hippeastrum barreirasum is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family, sharing characteristics with other amaryllis species.
