Hoorebekia Greenei Subsp. Mollis

Hoorebekia greenei subsp. mollis is a rare and unique plant native to the highlands of Ecuador. This subspecies is known for its striking, large, and showy flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
