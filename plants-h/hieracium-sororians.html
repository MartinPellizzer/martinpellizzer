Hieracium sororians

Hieracium sororians, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils across Europe and parts of Asia. It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Hieracium sororians is a perennial plant that can form dense colonies, sometimes becoming invasive in certain environments.
