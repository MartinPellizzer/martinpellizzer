Halimione portulacoides var. appendiculata

Halimione portulacoides var. appendiculata, commonly known as sea beach spinney, is a halophytic plant adapted to coastal environments.

It thrives in saline soils and is often found in dunes and salt marshes. The plant has succulent leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid conditions. Its flowers are small and yellow, typically appearing in clusters.

It plays an important role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems and preventing erosion.
