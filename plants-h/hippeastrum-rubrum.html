Hippeastrum rubrum

Hippeastrum rubrum, commonly known as the red amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces strap-shaped leaves that are typically broad and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering during its growing season.

Hippeastrum rubrum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.
