Hedysarum junatovii

Hedysarum junatovii, commonly known as the Junatov's milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Hedysarum junatovii plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest in ecological and botanical studies.
