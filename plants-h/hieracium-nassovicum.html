Hieracium nassovicum

Hieracium nassovicum, commonly known as the Balkan hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Southeast Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium nassovicum is often found in regions with temperate climates and well-drained soils.
