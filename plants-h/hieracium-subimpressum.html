Hieracium subimpressum

Hieracium subimpressum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in meadows and grasslands.

Hieracium subimpressum is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
