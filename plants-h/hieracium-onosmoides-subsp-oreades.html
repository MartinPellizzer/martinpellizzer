Hieracium onosmoides subsp. oreades

Hieracium onosmoides subsp.

oreades, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

The plant features a rosette of basal leaves that are usually hairy and have a rough texture. Its flowering stems bear yellow composite flowers, which are composed of many small florets arranged in a disk.

This subspecies is characterized by its compact growth form and is often found in open, sunny environments at high elevations.
