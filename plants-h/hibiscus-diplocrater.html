Hibiscus diplocrater

Hibiscus diplocrater, commonly known as the narrowleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant belongs to the Malvaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types. Hibiscus diplocrater thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires regular watering for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
