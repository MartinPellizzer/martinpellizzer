Haworthia arcana

Haworthia arcana, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves with distinctive white or yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant is known for its compact size and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

Haworthia arcana is well-suited for indoor gardening due to its minimal water requirements and low maintenance needs.
