Hieracium alatum subsp. chrysanthemoides

Hieracium alatum subsp.

chrysanthemoides, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

The plant has upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are alternate and often lobed. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and open woodlands.

Its botanical features make it easily identifiable and ecologically significant in its native habitats.
