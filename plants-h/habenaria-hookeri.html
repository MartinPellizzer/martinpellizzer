Habenaria hookeri

Habenaria hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often near the base of trees or on rocky substrates. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
