Helichrysum bracteatum var. nanum-luteum

Helichrysum bracteatum var. nanum-luteum, commonly known as the dwarf golden everlasting, is a compact shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that resemble tiny suns, which are tightly packed in dense, rounded clusters. The plant has silvery-gray foliage with a woolly texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The flowers are long-lasting when dried, making it a popular choice for floral arrangements.
