Hibiscus lanzae

Hibiscus lanzae, commonly known as the Lanza's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. Hibiscus lanzae thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
