Hieracium vulgatum subsp. valderamosum

Hieracium vulgatum subsp.

valderamosum, commonly known as the Valderamos hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming basal leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has a taproot system and produces numerous flowering stems that bear small, inconspicuous florets.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the hawkweed group within the genus Hieracium.
