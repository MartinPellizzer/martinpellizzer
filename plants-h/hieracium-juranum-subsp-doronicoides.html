Hieracium juranum subsp. doronicoides

Hieracium juranum subsp.

doronicoides, commonly known as the Jura hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and forest edges, preferring well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
