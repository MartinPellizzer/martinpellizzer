Humulus lupulus var. spaltensis

Humulus lupulus var. spaltensis, commonly known as the Spalt hop, is a variety of hop plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and high essential oil content, which contributes to its distinct aroma. The cones of this variety are typically light yellow to orange, with a dense, resinous coating. It is widely used in brewing for its bitterness and flavor, and it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its robustness and adaptability, it is a popular choice for hop growers in various regions.
