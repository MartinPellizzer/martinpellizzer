Hydrolea tenella

Hydrolea tenella, commonly known as the African violet, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, upright stem with dense, velvety foliage that is typically dark green. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom in clusters at the top of the stems. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Hydrolea tenella thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
