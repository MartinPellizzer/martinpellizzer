Hieracium ensiculare

Hieracium ensiculare, commonly known as the swordleaf hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, and can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium ensiculare is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many temperate regions.
