Haworthia argyrostigma

Haworthia argyrostigma, commonly known as the silver cross haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant grows in a rosette formation, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color.

Haworthia argyrostigma is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and unique aesthetic.
