Hartmannia Tetraptera

Hartmannia tetraptera is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to the humid forests of South America. This species is known for its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires careful attention to maintain its health. The plant's unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Its cultivation can be rewarding for those who appreciate its elegance and botanical interest.
