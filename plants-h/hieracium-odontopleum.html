Hieracium odontopleum

Hieracium odontopleum, commonly known as the toothed hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are rough and often have a distinctive toothed or lobed edge, contributing to its common name.

The yellow flowers bloom in late summer, adding a vibrant touch to its habitat.
