Hydrocotyle amoena

Hydrocotyle amoena, commonly known as the silver leaf or water pennywort, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery or white pubescence. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often near the base of the leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and wetlands.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
