Habenaria dilatata

Habenaria dilatata, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
