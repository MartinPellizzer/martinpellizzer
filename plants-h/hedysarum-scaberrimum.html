Hedysarum scaberrimum

Hedysarum scaberrimum, commonly known as the rough vetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.

Hedysarum scaberrimum is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
