Hedysarum criniferum

Hedysarum criniferum, commonly known as the silky bartsia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb with slender, branching stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Hedysarum criniferum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
