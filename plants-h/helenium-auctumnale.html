Helenium auctumnale

Helenium auctumnale, commonly known as the American sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its bright orange to red ray flowers and dark brown disk flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, gray-green leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, prairies, and along stream banks.

Helenium auctumnale is known for its showy blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
