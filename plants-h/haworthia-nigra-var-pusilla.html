Haworthia nigra var. pusilla

Haworthia nigra var. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pale markings. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
