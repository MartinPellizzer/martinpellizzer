Habenaria nemorosa

Habenaria nemorosa, commonly known as the forest orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and undergrowth. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single or few flowers that are often pale green or yellowish in color. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces a distinctive, fragrant flower with a complex structure.

Habenaria nemorosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
