Hieracium pulchellum subsp. lanatellum

Hieracium pulchellum subsp.

lanatellum, commonly known as the woolly meadowhawk, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant is distinguished by its dense, woolly hairs covering the stems and leaves, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils.
