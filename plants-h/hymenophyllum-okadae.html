Hymenophyllum okadae

Hymenophyllum okadae, commonly known as the Okada's filmy fern, is a species of polypod fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or rocks in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are thin and translucent, with a unique structure that allows for efficient water retention.

Hymenophyllum okadae is valued for its ornamental appearance and plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat.
