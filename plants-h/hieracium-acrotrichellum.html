Hieracium acrotrichellum

Hieracium acrotrichellum, commonly known as the smooth-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium acrotrichellum is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some environments.
