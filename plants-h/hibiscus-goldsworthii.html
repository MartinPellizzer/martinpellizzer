Hibiscus goldsworthii

Hibiscus goldsworthii, commonly known as the Goldsworthi Hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 feet. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting red throat. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Hibiscus goldsworthii thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
