Habenaria debeerstiana

Habenaria debeerstiana, commonly known as the De Beer's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces slender, upright stems with a single, showy, greenish-white to pale yellow flower. Its floral structure is characteristic of the Orchidaceae family, featuring three sepals and three petals, with the latter often being more pronounced.

It blooms during the cooler months, usually from late autumn to early spring.
