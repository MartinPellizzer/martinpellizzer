Hesperochiron californicus var. incanus

Hesperochiron californicus var. incanus, commonly known as the California horseweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive purple-tipped center.
