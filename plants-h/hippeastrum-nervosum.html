Hippeastrum nervosum

Hippeastrum nervosum, commonly known as the narrow-veined amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically red or orange with white veins, blooming in late summer or early fall. The plant has strap-like leaves that are narrow and often have a distinct veining pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum nervosum is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
