Hypericum ascyron f. vilmorinii

Hypericum ascyron f. vilmorinii, commonly known as the Vilmorin's St. John's Wort, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is typically grown in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display and hardy nature.
