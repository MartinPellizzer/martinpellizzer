Haworthia magnifica var. notabilis

Haworthia magnifica var. notabilis, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, striped leaves that resemble a zebra's coat, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-15 centimeters and has a rosette shape. Its leaves are fleshy and bluish-green, with white to pale yellow markings.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
