Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. jenzigense

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

jenzigense, commonly known as the Jenzig's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering stems that bear yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers consist of both ray and disk florets.

This subspecies is often found in association with other flowering plants in open habitats.
