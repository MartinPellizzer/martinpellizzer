Hieracium favratii

Hieracium favratii, commonly known as the favrat's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe. The plant is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a tall, flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers. It thrives in rocky or alpine habitats and is often found in open, sunny environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species.
