Haworthia cordifolia

Haworthia cordifolia, commonly known as the football plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
