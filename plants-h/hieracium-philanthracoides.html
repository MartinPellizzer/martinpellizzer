Hieracium philanthracoides

Hieracium philanthracoides, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium philanthracoides is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
