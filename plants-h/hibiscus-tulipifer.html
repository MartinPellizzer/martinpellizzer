Hibiscus tulipifer

Hibiscus tulipifer, commonly known as the tropical hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and glossy. Hibiscus tulipifer thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and attractive foliage.
