Hieracium glaucum subsp. isaricotropum

Hieracium glaucum subsp.

isaricotropum, commonly known as the Isaricotropum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and leaf morphology.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are generally lanceolate, with a hairy surface, and it produces small, yellowish to orange flowers.

It is often found in regions with temperate climates and is part of the broader Hieracium genus, which includes many species of hawkweeds.
