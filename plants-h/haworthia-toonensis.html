Haworthia toonensis

Haworthia toonensis, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are often marked with white or yellow stripes, resembling a zebra's pattern. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal irrigation. It produces small, greenish-white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months.

Haworthia toonensis is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
