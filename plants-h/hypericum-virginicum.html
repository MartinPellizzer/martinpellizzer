Hypericum virginicum

Hypericum virginicum, commonly known as American St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in wetlands and along stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with five petals. It blooms from June to August and is often found in moist, sunny habitats.

Hypericum virginicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
