Homalodiscus aucheri

Homalodiscus aucheri, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
