Haworthia caerulea var. pallens

Haworthia caerulea var. pallens, commonly known as the blue haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, often with white or light green markings. The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps the plant conserve water in arid conditions. It produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
