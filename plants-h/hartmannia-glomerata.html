Hartmannia glomerata

Hartmannia glomerata, commonly known as the clustered Hartmannia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
