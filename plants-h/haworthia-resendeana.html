Haworthia resendeana

Haworthia resendeana, commonly known as the Resende's Haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
