Hieracium dentatum subsp. trefferianiforme

Hieracium dentatum subsp.

trefferianiforme, commonly known as the trefferianiform hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow composite flowers.

The subspecies is native to Central and Eastern Europe, where it thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are typically lanceolate with serrated edges, and the plant produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is often found in association with other herbaceous plants in its native range.
