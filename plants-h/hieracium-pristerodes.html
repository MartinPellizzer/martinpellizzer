Hieracium pristerodes

Hieracium pristerodes, commonly known as the ancient dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow composite flowers that develop into elongated, bristly seed heads. Its leaves are typically hairy and have a slightly serrated edge, while the stem is often reddish in color.

Hieracium pristerodes is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
