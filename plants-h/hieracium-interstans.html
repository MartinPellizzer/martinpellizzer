Hieracium interstans

Hieracium interstans, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, and it is native to Europe and parts of Asia. It has hairy stems and leaves that are usually alternate and lance-shaped.

Hieracium interstans is an annual or biennial plant, often spreading through its rhizomes and forming dense colonies.
