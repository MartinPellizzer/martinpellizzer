Hieracium subacrobeles

Hieracium subacrobeles, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium subacrobeles is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
