Heterosperma pinnatum var. typicum

Heterosperma pinnatum var. typicum, commonly known as the featherleaf water plant, is a aquatic plant native to tropical regions.

It features finely divided, feather-like leaves that provide excellent surface area for oxygen exchange. The plant typically grows in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often green or brown, and are borne on slender stems.

It is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to improve water quality.
