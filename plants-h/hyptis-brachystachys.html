Hyptis brachystachys

Hyptis brachystachys, commonly known as silverleaf hyptis, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence on the leaves. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color.

Hyptis brachystachys is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and aromatic properties.
