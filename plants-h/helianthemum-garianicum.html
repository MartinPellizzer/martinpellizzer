Helianthemum garianicum

Helianthemum garianicum, commonly known as the Gariep sandwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in sandy and rocky soils. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its arid habitat. Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

Helianthemum garianicum is often found in coastal and inland sandy regions, showcasing its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
