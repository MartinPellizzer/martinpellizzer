Haplopappus venetus var. oxyphyllus

Haplopappus venetus var. oxyphyllus, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage.
