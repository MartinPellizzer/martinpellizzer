Helichrysum translucidum

Helichrysum translucidum, commonly known as the translucent everlasting flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that retain their color and form even after drying. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting appearance.

Helichrysum translucidum is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
