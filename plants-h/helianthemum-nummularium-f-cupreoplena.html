Helianthemum nummularium f. cupreoplena

Helianthemum nummularium f. cupreoplena, commonly known as the coppery pennyplant, is a compact, low-growing succulent with distinctive, rounded, coppery-green leaves.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to southern Europe, particularly the Iberian Peninsula. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and hardy nature make it an attractive and easy-to-care-for ornamental plant.
