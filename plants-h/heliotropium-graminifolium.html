Heliotropium graminifolium

Heliotropium graminifolium, commonly known as slender heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or well-drained soils.

Heliotropium graminifolium is an annual or perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.
