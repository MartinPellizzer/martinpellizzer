Helenium acaule

Helenium acaule, commonly known as the stiffstem sunflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, daisy-like yellow to orange flower heads that bloom from late summer to early fall. The plant has a tall, upright stem with few branches, and its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy. Helenium acaule thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
