Hieracium osonense

Hieracium osonense, commonly known as the Osona hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

Hieracium osonense is often found in the Iberian Peninsula and parts of Italy.
