Hubertia

Hubertia, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces vibrant, often pink or red blooms that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Hubertia is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its thick stems and leaves.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
