Haworthia asperiuscula

Haworthia asperiuscula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Haworthia, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive waxy coating and subtle white markings. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks bearing clusters of yellowish-white flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
