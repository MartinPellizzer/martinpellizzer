Hieracium cymanthodes

Hieracium cymanthodes, commonly known as the clustered hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Hieracium cymanthodes is often found in disturbed habitats and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
