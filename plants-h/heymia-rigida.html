Heymia rigida

Heymia rigida, commonly known as the rigid heymia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the spring. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in coastal and inland areas with limited moisture.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
