Hieracium lapeyrousii proles trichocerinthe

Hieracium lapeyrousii proles trichocerinthe, commonly known as the trichocerean hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads that produce yellow disc flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has a fibrous root system and produces numerous slender, branching stems.

The species is notable for its distinctive leaf morphology, with hairy surfaces and a serrated margin.
