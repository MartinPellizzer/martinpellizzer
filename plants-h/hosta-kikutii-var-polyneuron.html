Hosta kikutii var. polyneuron

Hosta kikutii var. polyneuron, commonly known as the Variegated Japanese Hosta, is a cultivar native to Japan.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a striking combination of creamy white and green variegation. The plant produces tall, upright stems that bear small, fragrant, white flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to garden conditions.
