Hieracium alpinum subsp. aquilonium

Hieracium alpinum subsp.

aquilonium, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy slopes, preferring well-drained soils. It has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped.

Due to its habitat, it is often found in high-altitude environments where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.
