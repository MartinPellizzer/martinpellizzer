Hydrodiscus Koyamae

Hydrodiscus koyamae is a rare aquatic plant native to the freshwater ecosystems of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its unique disc-like leaves that float on the water's surface. This plant plays an important role in maintaining water quality by absorbing excess nutrients. Due to habitat loss and pollution, it faces significant threats in the wild. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this delicate and ecologically valuable species.
