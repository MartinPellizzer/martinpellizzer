Habenaria angustifolia

Habenaria angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and wetlands. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
