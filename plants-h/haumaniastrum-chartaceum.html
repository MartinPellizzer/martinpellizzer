Haumaniastrum chartaceum

Haumaniastrum chartaceum, commonly known as the paperleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange composite flowers and heart-shaped, papery leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other sunflowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Its unique foliage and striking blooms make it a notable species in xerophytic landscapes.
