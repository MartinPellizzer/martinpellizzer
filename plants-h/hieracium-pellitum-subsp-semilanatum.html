Hieracium pellitum subsp. semilanatum

Hieracium pellitum subsp.

semilanatum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers.

The plant typically has basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
