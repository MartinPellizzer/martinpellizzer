Helichrysum zombense

Helichrysum zombense, commonly known as the Zombese everlasting, is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers.

Native to South Africa, this plant is known for its vibrant yellow to orange blooms that remain intact for extended periods, giving it the common name "everlasting.". The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species. Its foliage is aromatic and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and bright color.
