Haworthia tortuosa var. tortella

Haworthia tortuosa var. tortella, commonly known as the spiral haworthia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adorned with white tubercles and prominent central spines. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique spiral growth pattern and striking appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
