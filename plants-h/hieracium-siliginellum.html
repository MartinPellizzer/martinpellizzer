Hieracium siliginellum

Hieracium siliginellum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, yellowish-green leaves and produces clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and meadows. It has a hairy stem and is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils.

The flowers typically bloom from June to August, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
