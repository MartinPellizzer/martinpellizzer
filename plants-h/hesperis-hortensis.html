Hesperis hortensis

Hesperis hortensis, commonly known as garden candytuft, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages. The plant features clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and slightly hairy.

Hesperis hortensis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
