Haitimimosa extranea

Haitimimosa extranea, commonly known as the Haitian mimosa, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean island of Hispaniola.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks and in forest understories.
