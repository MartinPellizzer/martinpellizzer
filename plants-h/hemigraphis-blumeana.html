Hemigraphis blumeana

Hemigraphis blumeana, commonly known as shining geranium, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves with a metallic sheen, often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers during the summer months. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

Hemigraphis blumeana thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.
