Habenaria volkmanniae

Habenaria volkmanniae, commonly known as the Volkmann's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in forest understories where it benefits from the shade and high humidity. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with rhizomes and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The species is valued for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
