Hemionitis parryi

Hemionitis parryi, commonly known as the desert shield fern, is a unique plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is a small, evergreen fern that thrives in harsh, dry environments. Its fronds are narrow and leathery, adapted to reduce water loss. The plant has a compact growth habit and often forms dense clusters.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
