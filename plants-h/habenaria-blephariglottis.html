Habenaria blephariglottis

Habenaria blephariglottis, commonly known as the hooded orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive hood-shaped lip, which is a key feature in its classification within the Orchidaceae family. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with a showy, three-lobed labellum that aids in pollination.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
