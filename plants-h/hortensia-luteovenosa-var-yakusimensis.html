Hortensia luteovenosa var. yakusimensis

Hortensia luteovenosa var. yakusimensis, commonly known as the Yakusimensis hydrangea, is a rare and ornamental plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking yellow veins on light green foliage, which contrast beautifully with its large, rounded leaves. The plant typically blooms with clusters of white or pale blue flowers, depending on soil pH, showcasing its adaptability. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is a prized specimen among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
