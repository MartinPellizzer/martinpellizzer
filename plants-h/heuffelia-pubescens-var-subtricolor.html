Heuffelia pubescens var. subtricolor

Heuffelia pubescens var. subtricolor, commonly known as the multicolored heuffelia, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage is often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a pubescent texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
