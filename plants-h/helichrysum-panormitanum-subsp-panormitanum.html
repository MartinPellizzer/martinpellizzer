Helichrysum panormitanum subsp. panormitanum

Helichrysum panormitanum subsp.

panormitanum, commonly known as the Mediterranean immortelle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its dense, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble small sunflowers.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is typically found in rocky or sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well adapted to its arid environment.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
