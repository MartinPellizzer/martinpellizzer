Hieracium semianglicum

Hieracium semianglicum, commonly known as the English hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Hieracium semianglicum is often found in temperate regions and can sometimes become invasive in non-native habitats.
