Hieracium incisum subsp. pseudotrachselianum

Hieracium incisum subsp.

The plant typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, often forming dense colonies. It is a herbaceous species that produces small, wind-dispersed seeds, contributing to its ability to spread across various habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
