Helianthus × laetiflorus f. rigidus

Helianthus × laetiflorus f. rigidus, commonly known as the stiff-flowered sunflower, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and stiff, elongated leaves. The plant produces large, showy yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense disc of smaller, tubular flowers. It is native to the United States and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and ecological benefits.
