Hypericum enshiense

Hypericum enshiense, commonly known as the Enshi St. John's wort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly rough texture.

Hypericum enshiense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
