Hieracium collinum

Hieracium collinum, commonly known as the mountain hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellowish to orange-red flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has hairy, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Native to Europe, it thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the hawkweed genus, with adaptations suited to its alpine and subalpine environments.
