Heterorhachis hystrix

Heterorhachis hystrix, commonly known as the spiny leaf vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain several seeds.

Heterorhachis hystrix is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
