Hedera helix var. donerailensis-minor

Hedera helix var. donerailensis-minor, commonly known as the Donerailensis Minor Ivy, is a rare and compact variant of the common ivy.

It features dark green, slightly variegated leaves with a subtle pattern of lighter veins. This cultivar is prized for its dense, trailing growth habit and its ability to thrive in shaded environments. It is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its compact size.

The plant is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal pruning and occasional watering.
