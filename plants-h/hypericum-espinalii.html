Hypericum espinalii

Hypericum espinalii, commonly known as the Spanish fireweed, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Hypericum espinalii is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
