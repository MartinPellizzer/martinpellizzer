Hypericum choisyanum

Hypericum choisyanum, commonly known as the Choisy's St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to Central Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, orange-red fruits in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological gardens.
