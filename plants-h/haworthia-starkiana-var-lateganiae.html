Haworthia starkiana var. lateganiae

Haworthia starkiana var. lateganiae, commonly known as the Lategania Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or pale blue markings, forming a rosette shape. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
