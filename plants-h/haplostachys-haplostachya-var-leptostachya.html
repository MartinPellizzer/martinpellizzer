Haplostachys haplostachya var. leptostachya

Haplostachys haplostachya var. leptostachya, commonly known as the slender-stalked grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
