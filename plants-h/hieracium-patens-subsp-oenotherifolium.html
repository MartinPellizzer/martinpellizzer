Hieracium patens subsp. oenotherifolium

Hieracium patens subsp.

oenotherifolium, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties.

The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, hairy leaves and produces showy yellow to orange flowers with a distinct central disk. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Its subspecies name, *oenotherifolium*, refers to its leaf structure resembling that of the evening primrose.
