Hypericum erectum

Hypericum erectum, commonly known as upright St. John's wort, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows to a height of 30 to 90 centimeters and features bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential antidepressant effects.

Hypericum erectum prefers sunny to partially shaded environments and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
