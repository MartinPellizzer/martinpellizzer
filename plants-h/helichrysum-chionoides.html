Helichrysum chionoides

Helichrysum chionoides, commonly known as the silver leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish to golden-orange flowers that are often arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Helichrysum chionoides is also known for its long-lasting flowers, which retain their color when dried, making it popular in floral arrangements.
