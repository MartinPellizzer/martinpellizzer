Habenaria pantothrix

Habenaria pantothrix, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green to reddish-brown rhizomes and its tall, slender flowering stems. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers arranged in a loose raceme, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and swampy areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important member of the orchid family.
