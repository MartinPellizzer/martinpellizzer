Haworthia pallida

Haworthia pallida, commonly known as the white-line Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with distinctive white or pale yellow markings. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
