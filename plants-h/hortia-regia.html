Hortia regia

Hortia regia, commonly known as the royal hortia, is a rare and exotic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, iridescent leaves that shimmer with a metallic sheen under sunlight. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of violet and white, which bloom throughout the rainy season. Its roots are known to have medicinal properties, used traditionally in local herbal remedies.

Hortia regia thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish.
