Hieracium nigrescens subsp. navalium

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

navalium, commonly known as the naval hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are usually basal, with a rough texture and a distinctive dark green color.

The flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, contributing to its role in pollination and ecological interactions.
