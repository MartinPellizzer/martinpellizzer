Hydrangea macrophylla var. belzonii

Hydrangea macrophylla var. belzonii, commonly known as the Belzoni hydrangea, is a cultivar prized for its large, showy flower heads.

It belongs to the Hydrangeaceae family and is native to Japan, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces clusters of sterile flowers that range in color from blue to pink, depending on soil pH. It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1.5 to 2 meters.

Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.
