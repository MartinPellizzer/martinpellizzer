Hieronyma ovata

Hieronyma ovata, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit or Natal tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines. The tree is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
