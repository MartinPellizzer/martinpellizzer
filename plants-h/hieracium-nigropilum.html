Hieracium nigropilum

Hieracium nigropilum, commonly known as black-haired hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black bracts that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Hieracium nigropilum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate regions.
