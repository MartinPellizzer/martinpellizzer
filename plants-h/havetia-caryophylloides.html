Havetia caryophylloides

Havetia caryophylloides, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring moist, well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients efficiently.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
