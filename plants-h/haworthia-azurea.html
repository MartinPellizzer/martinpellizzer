Haworthia azurea

Haworthia azurea, commonly known as the blue haworthia, is a species of succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish or grayish hue. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Haworthia azurea is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its compact size and unique appearance.
