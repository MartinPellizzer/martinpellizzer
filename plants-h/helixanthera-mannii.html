Helixanthera mannii

Helixanthera mannii, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually brightly colored, with a unique arrangement of petals and sepals that resemble a star.

Helixanthera mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or conservatories.
