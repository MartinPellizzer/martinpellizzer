Helichrysum argophyllum

Helichrysum argophyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and vibrant yellow-orange flowers. The plant has silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum argophyllum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
