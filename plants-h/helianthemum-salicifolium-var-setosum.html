Helianthemum salicifolium var. setosum

Helianthemum salicifolium var. setosum, commonly known as the woolly rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has grey-green, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
