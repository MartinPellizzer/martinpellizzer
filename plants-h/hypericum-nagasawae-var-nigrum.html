Hypericum nagasawae var. nigrum

Hypericum nagasawae var. nigrum, commonly known as the black-stemmed St. John's wort, is a shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its dark purple to black stems and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

It is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
