Hippeastrum ignescens

Hippeastrum ignescens, commonly known as the Flame Lily, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces thick, fleshy bulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often emerging after the flowering period.

Hippeastrum ignescens is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
