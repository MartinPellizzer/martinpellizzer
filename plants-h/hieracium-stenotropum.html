Hieracium stenotropum

Hieracium stenotropum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium stenotropum is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through rhizomes and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
