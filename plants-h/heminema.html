Heminema

Heminema, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which helps it conserve water in hot, dry environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Heminema is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it a resilient and hardy species.
