Hieracium pallidum subsp. crinigerum

Hieracium pallidum subsp.

crinigerum, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellowish to pale green flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the Hieracium genus.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is often found in grassy areas and open habitats. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across different regions.
