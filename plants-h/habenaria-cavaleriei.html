Habenaria cavaleriei

Habenaria cavaleriei, commonly known as the Cavalerie's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Vietnam and Cambodia. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
