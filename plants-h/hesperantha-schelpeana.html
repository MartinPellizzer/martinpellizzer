Hesperantha schelpeana

Hesperantha schelpeana, commonly known as the Schelpe's bell orchid, is a South African plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in the Western and Northern Cape provinces. It has linear to lanceolate leaves and produces a single, erect spike bearing several flowers.

Hesperantha schelpeana is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.
