Hymenocallis amoena

Hymenocallis amoena, commonly known as the white hurricane lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the Caribbean and features showy, trumpet-shaped white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces a tall, slender stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its bulbs are perennial and can be propagated through division or seed.

Hymenocallis amoena is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
