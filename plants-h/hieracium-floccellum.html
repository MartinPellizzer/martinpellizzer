Hieracium floccellum

Hieracium floccellum, commonly known as the fluffhead hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fluffy flower heads that resemble small clouds, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its stems are usually hairy as well.

Hieracium floccellum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in disturbed soils.
