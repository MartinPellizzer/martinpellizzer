Hydrangea macrophylla f. isonotaki

Hydrangea macrophylla f. isonotaki, commonly known as the Isonotaki hydrangea, is a cultivar characterized by its unique flower structure.

It features large, showy blooms that are typically pink or white, depending on soil pH. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal color development.

This hydrangea is prized for its seasonal color changes and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
