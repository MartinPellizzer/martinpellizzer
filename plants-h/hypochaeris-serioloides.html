Hypochaeris serioloides

Hypochaeris serioloides, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

Hypochaeris serioloides is an annual herb that can spread through seeds, often forming dense mats in suitable environments.
