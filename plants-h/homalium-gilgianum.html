Homalium gilgianum

Homalium gilgianum, commonly known as the Gilgianum beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its clusters of small, bright purple berries that appear in the fall. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a smooth texture. It produces panicles of yellowish-green flowers that are often overlooked due to their modest appearance.
