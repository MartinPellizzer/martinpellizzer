Helianthemum retrofractum

Helianthemum retrofractum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually grey-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
