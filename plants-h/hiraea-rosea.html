Hiraea rosea

Hiraea rosea, commonly known as the pink hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, rose-like flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, while its stems are woody and often branched.

Hiraea rosea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
