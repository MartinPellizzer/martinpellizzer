Hydrangea hemsleyana

Hydrangea hemsleyana, commonly known as the Hemsley hydrangea, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features large, showy inflorescences that can range in color from white to pink, depending on soil pH. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside.

The flowers bloom in late summer, adding ornamental value to gardens and natural landscapes.
