Habenaria parvicalcarata

Habenaria parvicalcarata, commonly known as the small-spurred orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats and produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive spur. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two ranks, and the flowers are usually pale green to white in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
