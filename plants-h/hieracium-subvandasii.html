Hieracium subvandasii

Hieracium subvandasii, commonly known as the subvandasii hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence, consisting of yellow disc flowers surrounded by white to pale yellow ray florets. Native to Europe, this species typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. It has hairy stems and basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape.

The plant is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
