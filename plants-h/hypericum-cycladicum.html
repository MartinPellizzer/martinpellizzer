Hypericum cycladicum

Hypericum cycladicum, commonly known as the Cycladic goldenseal, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Hypericum, which includes over 400 species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
