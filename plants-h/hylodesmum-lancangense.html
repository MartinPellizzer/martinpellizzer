Hylodesmum lancangense

Hylodesmum lancangense, commonly known as the Lancang rock fern, is a species of polypodiaceae that thrives in rocky and mountainous regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves and a compact, low-growing habit. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on limestone or rocky substrates. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a distinct, serrated margin.

Hylodesmum lancangense is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats.
