Hoarea rubescens

Hoarea rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces striking clusters of bright red flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms.
