Hibiscus aristatus

Hibiscus aristatus, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Hibiscus aristatus is often found in mountainous areas and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Its flowers bloom throughout the year, making it a popular choice for gardens in tropical climates.
