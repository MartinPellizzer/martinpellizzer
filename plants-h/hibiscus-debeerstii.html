Hibiscus debeerstii

Hibiscus debeerstii, commonly known as the De Beer's hibiscus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, showy flowers that are usually pink or red in color, with five petals and a prominent central stigma. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while the stems are covered in fine hairs.

Hibiscus debeerstii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in its native region.
