Hieracium prenanthoides subsp. alessicum

Hieracium prenanthoides subsp.

alessicum, commonly known as the Alessi's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and flowering heads.

The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.
