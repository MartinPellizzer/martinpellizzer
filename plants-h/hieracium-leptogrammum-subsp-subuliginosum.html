Hieracium leptogrammum subsp. subuliginosum

Hieracium leptogrammum subsp.

subuliginosum, commonly known as the slender-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and open woodlands.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and threatened species in some regions.
