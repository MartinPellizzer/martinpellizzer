Helenium macrospermum

Helenium macrospermum, commonly known as the giant sneezeweed, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features tall, branched stems that can reach heights of up to six feet. Its showy, orange to red flowers bloom in late summer and are surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves.

Helenium macrospermum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
