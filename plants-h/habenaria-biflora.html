Habenaria biflora

Habenaria biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats and has a rhizomatous root system. The plant produces tall, slender stems with several whorls of lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale green to yellowish, with three sepals and three petals, and are arranged in a loose inflorescence.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure and is often found in humid, tropical environments.
