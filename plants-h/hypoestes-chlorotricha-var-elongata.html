Hypoestes chlorotricha var. elongata

Hypoestes chlorotricha var. elongata, commonly known as the "Snowy Blush" or "Mossy Hypoestes," is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that ranges from silver-green to deep green, often with a bluish or purplish tint. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom profusely during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant coloration make it an excellent addition to containers or as part of a mixed foliage display.
