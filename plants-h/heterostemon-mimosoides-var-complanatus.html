Heterostemon mimosoides var. complanatus

Heterostemon mimosoides var. complanatus, commonly known as the flat-leaved mimosa, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are often flat and broad, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, typical of the mimosoides group.

This variety is adapted to dry environments and is often found in sandy soils across eastern Australia.
