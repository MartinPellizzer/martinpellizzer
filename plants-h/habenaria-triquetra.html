Habenaria triquetra

Habenaria triquetra, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish or yellowish flowers arranged in a loose spike, often with three distinct lobes on each bloom. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
