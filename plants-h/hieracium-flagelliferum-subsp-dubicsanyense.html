Hieracium flagelliferum subsp. dubicsanyense

Hieracium flagelliferum subsp.

dubicsanyense, commonly known as the Dubicsany hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its unique morphological features, including elongated leaves and specific inflorescence structures. It typically grows in moist meadows and riverbanks, preferring well-drained soils.
