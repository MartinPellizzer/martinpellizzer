Hieracium oenochroum

Hieracium oenochroum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically discoid and arranged in a single head. The plant has basal leaves that are rough and hairy, with a rosette-like growth pattern. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Hieracium oenochroum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes and is considered a weed in some regions.
