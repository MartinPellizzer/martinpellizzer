Hypericum cuatrecasii

Hypericum cuatrecasii, commonly known as the Ecuadorian St. John's Wort, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within cloud forests. It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is known for its medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it a unique species within the genus Hypericum.
