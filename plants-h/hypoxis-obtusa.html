Hypoxis obtusa

Hypoxis obtusa, commonly known as the bluntleaf yellowstar, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with reflexed petals. The plant has lance-shaped, dull-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hypoxis obtusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
