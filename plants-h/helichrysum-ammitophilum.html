Helichrysum ammitophilum

Helichrysum ammitophilum, commonly known as the Cape golden bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, golden-yellow foliage that resembles a bush. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Helichrysum ammitophilum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
