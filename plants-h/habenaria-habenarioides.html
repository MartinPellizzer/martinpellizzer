Habenaria habenarioides

Habenaria habenarioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale green to white flowers with a distinctive lip. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and mycorrhizal associations.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in some regions.
