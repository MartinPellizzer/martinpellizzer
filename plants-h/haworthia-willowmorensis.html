Haworthia willowmorensis

Haworthia willowmorensis, commonly known as the Willowmore Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with white or pinkish markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
