Hamelinia

Hamelinia, commonly known as the red-flowered bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular or bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. They are valued in horticulture for their ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
