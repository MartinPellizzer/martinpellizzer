Hieracium biebersteinii subsp. acutangulum

Hieracium biebersteinii subsp.

acutangulum, commonly known as the acute-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to reddish flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its sharply pointed leaf margins and dense, woolly foliage. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
