Habenaria constricta

Habenaria constricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with a distinctive yellow lip.

Habenaria constricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
