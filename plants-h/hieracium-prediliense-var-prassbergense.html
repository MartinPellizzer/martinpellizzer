Hieracium prediliense var. prassbergense

Hieracium prediliense var. prassbergense, commonly known as the Prassberg hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along streambanks, preferring well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are lanceolate and hairy, with a rough texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
