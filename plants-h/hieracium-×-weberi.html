Hieracium × weberi

Hieracium × weberi, commonly known as the Weber's hawkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow composite flowers that attract pollinators. The plant typically has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Due to its invasive potential, it can outcompete native flora in certain regions.
