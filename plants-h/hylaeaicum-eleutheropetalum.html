Hylaeaicum eleutheropetalum

Hylaeaicum eleutheropetalum, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It is adapted to survive in harsh environments with limited water availability, making it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
