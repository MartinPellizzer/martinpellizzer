Herminium gracile

Herminium gracile, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small and pale green, often with a slight purple hue, and are arranged in a loose spike.

Herminium gracile is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with mycorrhizal fungi.
