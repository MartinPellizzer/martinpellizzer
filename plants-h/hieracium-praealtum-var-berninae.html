Hieracium praealtum var. berninae

Hieracium praealtum var. berninae, commonly known as the Bernina Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Alps.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high altitudes. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
