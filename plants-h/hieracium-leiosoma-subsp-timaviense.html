Hieracium leiosoma subsp. timaviense

Hieracium leiosoma subsp.

timaviense, commonly known as the Alpine Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and flowering heads.

The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in rocky and grassy habitats at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in several European countries.
