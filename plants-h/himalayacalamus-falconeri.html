Himalayacalamus falconeri

Himalayacalamus falconeri, commonly known as the Falconeri's bamboo, is a member of the grass family, Poaceae, and is native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

Its growth pattern is clumping, forming dense clusters that can reach heights of several meters.
