Habenaria sodiroi

Habenaria sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple or brown markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
