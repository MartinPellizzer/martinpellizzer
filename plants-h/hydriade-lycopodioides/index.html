Hydriade Lycopodioides

Hydriade lycopodioides is a rare and ancient plant species that belongs to the family Lycopodiaceae. It is known for its primitive structure and is often found in moist, shaded environments. This plant has a unique reproductive system that relies on spores rather than seeds. Its presence indicates a healthy ecosystem and contributes to biodiversity in its habitat. Despite its ecological importance, it remains poorly studied and is threatened by habitat loss.
