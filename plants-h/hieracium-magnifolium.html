Hieracium magnifolium

Hieracium magnifolium, commonly known as the showy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, surrounded by pale green ray florets. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it often grows in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium magnifolium is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
