Hieracium substellatum

Hieracium substellatum, commonly known as the starry hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Hieracium substellatum is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, where it can sometimes become invasive.
