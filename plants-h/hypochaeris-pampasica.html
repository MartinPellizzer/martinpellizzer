Hypochaeris pampasica

Hypochaeris pampasica, commonly known as the pampas groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with a distinctive central disc and surrounding ray florets. Its leaves are alternate, rough, and covered in fine hairs.

Hypochaeris pampasica is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to thrive in sunny, well-drained environments.
