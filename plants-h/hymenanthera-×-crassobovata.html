Hymenanthera × crassobovata

Hymenanthera × crassobovata, commonly known as the broad-leaved lilly pilly, is a hybrid shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 3–6 meters and has a rounded, bushy form. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing ornamental value throughout the year.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
