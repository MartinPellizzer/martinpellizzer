Haworthia outeniquensis

Haworthia outeniquensis, commonly known as the "Cape Haws," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often tinged with red or pink at the tips. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
