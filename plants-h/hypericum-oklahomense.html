Hypericum oklahomense

Hypericum oklahomense, commonly known as Oklahoma goldenseal, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers in the summer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, wooded areas. It is a member of the Hippocastaneaceae family and is noted for its ornamental value.
