Hieracium diaphanoides var. pseudocladophorum

Hieracium diaphanoides var. pseudocladophorum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
