Haemanthus colchicifolius

Haemanthus colchicifolius, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red bracts that surround its small, white flowers. The plant produces arrow-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
