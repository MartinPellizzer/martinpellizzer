Holothrix buchananii

Holothrix buchananii, commonly known as the African groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky areas. The plant features wiry stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs.

Holothrix buchananii is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a hardy species adapted to dry climates.
