Hieracium schelkownikowii

Hieracium schelkownikowii, commonly known as Schelkownik's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
