Hosta tokudama f. mediopicta

Hosta tokudama f. mediopicta, commonly known as the Variegated Tokudama Hosta, is a cultivar prized for its striking foliage.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a mix of deep green and creamy white variegation. The plant is native to Japan and is well-suited for shaded garden environments. It produces small, fragrant white flowers in late summer.

This cultivar is popular for its ornamental value and ease of care in moist, well-drained soil.
