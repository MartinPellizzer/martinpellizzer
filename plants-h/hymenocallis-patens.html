Hymenocallis patens

Hymenocallis patens, commonly known as the showy spider lily, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant produces a basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves and has a fibrous root system. Its flowering period typically occurs in the summer months, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Hymenocallis patens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
