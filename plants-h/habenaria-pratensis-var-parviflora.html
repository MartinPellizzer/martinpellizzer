Habenaria pratensis var. parviflora

Habenaria pratensis var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to moist meadows and marshy areas in Europe.

It produces slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant blooms with small, greenish-white flowers that have a distinct three-lobed lip. It prefers partial shade and well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

This orchid is often found in wet habitats and is considered a rare and locally distributed species.
