Hypolepis rigescens

Hypolepis rigescens, commonly known as the stiff sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, rigid fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a glossy, dark green surface.

Hypolepis rigescens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
