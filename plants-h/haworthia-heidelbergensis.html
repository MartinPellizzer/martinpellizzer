Haworthia heidelbergensis

Haworthia heidelbergensis, commonly known as the Heidelberg Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
