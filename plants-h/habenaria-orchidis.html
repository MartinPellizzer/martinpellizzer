Habenaria orchidis

Habenaria orchidis, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to various regions across Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often intricate flower structure, which includes long, slender petals and a specialized lip that aids in pollination. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and grasslands, and thrives in humid climates. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.

The genus Habenaria is known for its diverse species, many of which exhibit unique adaptations to their specific ecological niches.
