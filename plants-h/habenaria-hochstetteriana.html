Habenaria hochstetteriana

Habenaria hochstetteriana, commonly known as the Hochstetter's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often near streams or in mountainous regions. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces tall, branching stems with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
