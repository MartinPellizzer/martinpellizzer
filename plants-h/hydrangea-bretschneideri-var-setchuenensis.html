Hydrangea bretschneideri var. setchuenensis

Hydrangea bretschneideri var. setchuenensis, commonly known as the Chinese hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink or blue depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features broad, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
