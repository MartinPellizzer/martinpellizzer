Hieracium heldreichii subsp. barbatiforme

Hieracium heldreichii subsp.

barbatiforme, commonly known as the Barbatiform Hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.
