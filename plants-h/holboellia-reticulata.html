Holboellia reticulata

Holboellia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, reticulate vein patterns on its leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
