Helicia tsangii

Helicia tsangii, commonly known as the Chinese holly or mountain holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, orange-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are leathery and elliptical, while its fruits are small, woody capsules that contain seeds.

Helicia tsangii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
