Hedychium barbatum

Hedychium barbatum, commonly known as the hairy ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features tall, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Hedychium barbatum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
