Hieracium racemosum subsp. tenuifolium

Hieracium racemosum subsp.

tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in a raceme, which are typically visited by various pollinators. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
