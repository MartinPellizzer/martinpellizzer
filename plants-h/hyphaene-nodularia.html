Hyphaene nodularia

Hyphaene nodularia, commonly known as the African palm or the date palm of the genus Hyphaene, is a species native to Africa.

It is a tall, slender tree with a single trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible fruits resembling dates. Its roots form a dense network, which helps stabilize soil in arid environments.

The plant is adapted to dry climates and is often used for its ornamental value and in traditional practices.
