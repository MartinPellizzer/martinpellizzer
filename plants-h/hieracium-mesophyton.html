Hieracium mesophyton

Hieracium mesophyton, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, while the stem is usually hairy as well. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Hieracium mesophyton is an herbaceous perennial that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
