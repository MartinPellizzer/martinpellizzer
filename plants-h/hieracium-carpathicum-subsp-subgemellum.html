Hieracium carpathicum subsp. subgemellum

Hieracium carpathicum subsp.

subgemellum, commonly known as the Carpathian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellowish composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and forest edges. It has a taproot system and produces numerous flowering stems that bear small, clustered flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
