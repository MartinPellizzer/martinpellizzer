Helenium ensifolium

Helenium ensifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf helenium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy yellow to orange-red ray flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Helenium ensifolium is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
