Habenaria novoebudarum

Habenaria novoebudarum, commonly known as the New Edinburgh orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the moist, organic-rich soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
