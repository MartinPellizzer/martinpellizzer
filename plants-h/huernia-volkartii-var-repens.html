Huernia volkartii var. repens

Huernia volkartii var. repens, commonly known as the creeping Huernia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that grow horizontally along the ground, often forming a dense mat. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically triangular and covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
