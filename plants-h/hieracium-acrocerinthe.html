Hieracium acrocerinthe

Hieracium acrocerinthe, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Hieracium acrocerinthe is an invasive species in some regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
