Helianthemum pergamaceum var. faurei

Helianthemum pergamaceum var. faurei, commonly known as the Faure's stonecress, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to rocky and sandy soils. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It is often found in coastal areas and is well adapted to coastal conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in garden settings.
