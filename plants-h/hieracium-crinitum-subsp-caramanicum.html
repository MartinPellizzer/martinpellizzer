Hieracium crinitum subsp. caramanicum

Hieracium crinitum subsp.

caramanicum, commonly known as the Caramanicum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are hairy and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it retain moisture.

It is a perennial species that produces numerous small, herbaceous stems.
