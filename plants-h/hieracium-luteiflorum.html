Hieracium luteiflorum

Hieracium luteiflorum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early autumn. The plant has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open areas.

Hieracium luteiflorum is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.
