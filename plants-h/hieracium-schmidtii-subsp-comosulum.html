Hieracium schmidtii subsp. comosulum

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

comosulum, commonly known as Schmidt's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowering heads.

The plant typically features basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. It produces small, composite flowers arranged in a single head, which are visited by various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
