Haworthia correcta var. lucida

Haworthia correcta var. lucida, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves with distinctive white or pale green markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces small, bell-shaped white or yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
