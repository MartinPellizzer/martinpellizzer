Hedysarum caucasicum

Hedysarum caucasicum, commonly known as Caucasian vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its compound leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Hedysarum caucasicum plays a role in nitrogen fixation, contributing to soil fertility.
