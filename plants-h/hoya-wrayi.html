Hoya wrayi

Hoya wrayi, commonly known as the Wray's waxplant, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and broad, waxy leaves. It is popular among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
