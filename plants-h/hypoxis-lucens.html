Hypoxis lucens

Hypoxis lucens, commonly known as the star-shaped yellow flower or yellow star grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Hypoxidaceae and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with a star-like shape, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Hypoxis lucens is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
