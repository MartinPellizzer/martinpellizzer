Hieracium pellitum subsp. prasinellum

Hieracium pellitum subsp.

prasinellum, commonly known as the green hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and rocky soils, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed habitats and is often found in open, sunny environments.
