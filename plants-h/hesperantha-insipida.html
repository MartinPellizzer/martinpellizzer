Hesperantha insipida

Hesperantha insipida, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant produces showy, tubular blossoms that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Hesperantha insipida is valued for its ornamental appeal and unique scent, making it a sought-after plant in horticulture.
