Habenaria trifida

Habenaria trifida, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, with three sepals and three petals, often in shades of white or pale pink.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
