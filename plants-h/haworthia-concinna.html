Haworthia concinna

Haworthia concinna, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinct white or yellow markings resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It produces small, white or greenish flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months.

Haworthia concinna is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
