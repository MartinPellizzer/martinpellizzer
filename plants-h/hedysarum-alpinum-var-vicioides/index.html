Hedysarum Alpinum Var. Vicioides

Hedysarum alpinum var. vicioides is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to high altitude regions of the European Alps. It is known for its small, pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This variety is closely related to the common hedysarum but exhibits distinct morphological features. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
