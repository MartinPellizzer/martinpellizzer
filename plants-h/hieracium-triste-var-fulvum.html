Hieracium triste var. fulvum

Hieracium triste var. fulvum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Hieracium. The plant typically grows in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowering period usually occurs in late spring to early summer.
