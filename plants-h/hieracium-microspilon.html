Hieracium microspilon

Hieracium microspilon, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and tall, flowering stems that bear yellow ray florets and numerous disk florets. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its small, spiny bracts and hairy leaves are notable botanical features.

Hieracium microspilon is typically short-lived, with a lifespan of about two to three years.
