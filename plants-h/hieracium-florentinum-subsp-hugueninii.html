Hieracium florentinum subsp. hugueninii

Hieracium florentinum subsp.

hugueninii, commonly known as the Florentine hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture. It is native to Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
