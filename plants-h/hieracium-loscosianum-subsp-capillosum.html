Hieracium loscosianum subsp. capillosum

Hieracium loscosianum subsp.

capillosum, commonly known as the Capillosum Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in a single head at the end of each stem. It has a taproot system and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves.

This subspecies is part of the genus Hieracium, which includes many species of hawkweeds known for their medicinal and ornamental uses.
