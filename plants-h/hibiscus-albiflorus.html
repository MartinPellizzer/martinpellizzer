Hibiscus albiflorus

Hibiscus albiflorus, commonly known as the whiteflower hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Hibiscus albiflorus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
