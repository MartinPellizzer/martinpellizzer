Helichrysum ayersii

Helichrysum ayersii, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, fluffy yellow or golden inflorescences that resemble small daisies. The plant is evergreen, with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum ayersii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in dried floral arrangements.
