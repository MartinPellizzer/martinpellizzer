Hippeastrum niveum

Hippeastrum niveum, commonly known as the white hurricane, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive red or purple stripe along the outer petals. The plant is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

The leaves are broad, strap-like, and often form a dense rosette around the bulb.
