Hieracium turbinellum subsp. turbinellum

Hieracium turbinellum subsp.

turbinellum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually hairy and somewhat reddish.

This species is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many temperate regions.
