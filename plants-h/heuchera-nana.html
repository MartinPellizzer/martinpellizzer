Heuchera nana

Heuchera nana, commonly known as dwarf coral bells, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It features heart-shaped, fuzzy leaves that come in a variety of colors, including red, purple, and green. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
