Hieracium prenanthoides subsp. trichocynanchoides

Hieracium prenanthoides subsp.

trichocynanchoides, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leaves with a distinctive lobed shape.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its subspecies name, trichocynanchoides, refers to the hairy, glandular structures on its stems and leaves.
