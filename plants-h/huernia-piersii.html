Huernia piersii

Huernia piersii, commonly known as the Piers' horned devil, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems covered in short, spiky tubercles that give it a distinctive, almost otherworldly appearance. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking contrast to its dark green foliage. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its stems and requiring minimal watering.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
