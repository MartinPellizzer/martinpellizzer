Hieracium aepolonchum

Hieracium aepolonchum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant has basal leaves that are generally lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along roadsides.

Hieracium aepolonchum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
