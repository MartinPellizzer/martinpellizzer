Haworthia coarctata f. pseudocoarctata

Haworthia coarctata f. pseudocoarctata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white to pale green transverse stripes. The leaves are thick and waxy, adapted for storing water in arid environments. It produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
