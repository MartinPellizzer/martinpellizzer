Habenaria kleyi

Habenaria kleyi, commonly known as the Kley's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern, often featuring a blend of white, green, and purple hues. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
