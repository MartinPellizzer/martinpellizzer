Hemionitis Gleichenioides

Hemionitis gleichenioides is a unique fern species native to the wetlands of southeastern asia. it thrives in moist, shaded environments and is known for its delicate, feather-like fronds. this plant is often found in swampy areas and is adapted to tolerate waterlogged soil. its scientific name reflects its resemblance to other members of the hemionitidaceae family. due to habitat loss, it faces threats and is considered a rare and endangered species.
