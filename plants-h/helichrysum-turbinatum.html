Helichrysum turbinatum

Helichrysum turbinatum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers and hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Helichrysum turbinatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
