Helichrysum formosissimum var. guilelmi

Helichrysum formosissimum var. guilelmi, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that resemble daisies. The plant has narrow, silver-green leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for dried floral arrangements.
