Hieracium villosum var. anomalum

Hieracium villosum var. anomalum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite, hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are covered in fine, soft hairs.

This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and growth habit compared to other Hieracium species.
