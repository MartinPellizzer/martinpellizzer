Hyptis vulcanica

Hyptis vulcanica, commonly known as the volcanic hyptis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically clustered in whorls and are usually pale blue or violet in color.

Hyptis vulcanica is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
