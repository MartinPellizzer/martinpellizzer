Haworthia × rubrobrunnea

Haworthia × rubrobrunnea, commonly known as the red-bronze haworthia, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with distinctive red or bronze markings. The leaves are covered in a layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.
