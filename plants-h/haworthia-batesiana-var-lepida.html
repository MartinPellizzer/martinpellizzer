Haworthia batesiana var. lepida

Haworthia batesiana var. lepida, commonly known as the "Lepidote Haworthia," is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features pale green to bluish-gray leaves with distinct white or pale markings, giving it a speckled appearance. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks that bear small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
