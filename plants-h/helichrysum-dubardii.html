Helichrysum dubardii

Helichrysum dubardii, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble small cups, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Helichrysum dubardii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
