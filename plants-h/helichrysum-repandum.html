Helichrysum repandum

Helichrysum repandum, commonly known as the everlasting daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, golden-yellow flowers that are tightly packed into dense, daisy-like clusters. The plant is known for its long-lasting blooms, which retain their color and form even when dried, making it popular in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

Helichrysum repandum is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
