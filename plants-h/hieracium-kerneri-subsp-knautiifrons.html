Hieracium kerneri subsp. knautiifrons

Hieracium kerneri subsp.

knautiifrons, commonly known as the Knautia-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers and hairy, rough leaves.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring well-drained soils. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces a flowering stem that can reach up to 30 cm in height.

This subspecies is often found in rocky or grassy habitats at high elevations.
