Haworthia aristata

Haworthia aristata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, pale green leaves with distinct white or yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant has a compact rosette growth habit and produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Haworthia aristata is appreciated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
