Hippeastrum intiflorum

Hippeastrum intiflorum, commonly known as the white hurricane lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and clusters of large, white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens. Its flowers have a strong, sweet fragrance and can last for several weeks.

Hippeastrum intiflorum is relatively easy to cultivate and thrives in well-drained soil with adequate sunlight.
