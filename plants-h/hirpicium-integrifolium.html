Hirpicium integrifolium

Hirpicium integrifolium, commonly known as the smooth hirpicium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are lance-shaped with smooth, undulating margins. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in loose clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Hirpicium integrifolium is noted for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
