Huernia quinta var. blyderiverensis

Huernia quinta var. blyderiverensis, commonly known as the Blyderiver horned orchid, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, branching stem covered in tubercles, which are small, rounded bumps that bear spines. The plant produces pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and collections of rare succulents.
