Habenaria njamnjamica

Habenaria njamnjamica, commonly known as the Njamnjamica orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with erect, often branched stems. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

The orchid has specialized roots that form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, aiding in nutrient absorption.
