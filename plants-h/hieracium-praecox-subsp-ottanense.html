Hieracium praecox subsp. ottanense

Hieracium praecox subsp.

ottanense, commonly known as the early hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often glandular, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The subspecies ottanense is distinguished by its specific floral morphology and geographic distribution.
