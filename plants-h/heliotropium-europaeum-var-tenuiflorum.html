Heliotropium europaeum var. tenuiflorum

Heliotropium europaeum var. tenuiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered heliotrope, is a herbaceous annual plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its blooming period occurs from late spring to early autumn, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
