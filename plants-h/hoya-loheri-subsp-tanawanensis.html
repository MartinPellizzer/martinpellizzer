Hoya loheri subsp. tanawanensis

Hoya loheri subsp.

tanawanensis, commonly known as the Tanawan Hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines. It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its waxy, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant, star-shaped flowers.

The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without being parasitic. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
