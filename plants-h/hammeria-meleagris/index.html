Hammeria Meleagris

Hammeria meleagris is a striking succulent known for its unique, spiky foliage. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in arid environments with minimal water. Its leaves resemble tiny hammers, giving it its common name. Easy to care for, it's a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens. This plant adds a bold, artistic touch to any collection.
