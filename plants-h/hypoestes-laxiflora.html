Hypoestes laxiflora

Hypoestes laxiflora, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular, pink to purple flowers that resemble snowflakes. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes laxiflora is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
