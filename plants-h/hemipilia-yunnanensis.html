Hemipilia yunnanensis

Hemipilia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hemipilia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive lip petal.

Hemipilia yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
