Helia acutangula

Helia acutangula, commonly known as the narrow-leaf helia.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
