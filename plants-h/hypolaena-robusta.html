Hypolaena robusta

Hypolaena robusta, commonly known as the robust hypolaena, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses around the world.
