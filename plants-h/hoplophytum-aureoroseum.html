Hoplophytum aureoroseum

Hoplophytum aureoroseum, commonly known as the golden rose gloxinia, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves that range from rose-gold to deep green. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is often favored by plant enthusiasts.
