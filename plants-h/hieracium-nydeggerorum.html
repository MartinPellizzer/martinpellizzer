Hieracium nydeggerorum

Hieracium nydeggerorum, commonly known as Nydegger's hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe, particularly in the Alps. This perennial herb typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped with hairy surfaces.

The plant is characterized by its robust growth and is often found in alpine and subalpine zones.
