Hippeastrum martinezii

Hippeastrum martinezii, commonly known as the Martinez's amaryllis, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and strap-shaped, typical of the Amaryllidaceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum martinezii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
