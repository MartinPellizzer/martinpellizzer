Hieracium vardusiae

Hieracium vardusiae, commonly known as the Vardusian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disk flowers. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils. The plant is typically found in meadows and grasslands, where it forms dense colonies.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to its environment, with hairy stems and small, alternate leaves.
