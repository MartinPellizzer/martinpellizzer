Helianthus atrorubens var. alsodes

Helianthus atrorubens var. alsodes, commonly known as the redstem sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and orange to red disk florets, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its stems are hairy and often reddish in color, which gives it its common name.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
