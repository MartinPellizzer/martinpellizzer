Hieracium caniolum

Hieracium caniolum, commonly known as the white hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grassy areas and meadows. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy texture. Hieracium caniolum is characterized by its upright growth habit and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
