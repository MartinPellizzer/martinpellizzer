Hyptis hassleri

Hyptis hassleri, commonly known as the silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats.

Hyptis hassleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
