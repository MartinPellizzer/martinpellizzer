Hieracium nakaii

Hieracium nakaii, commonly known as Nakai's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and flowering heads. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a single head, and it has a hairy stem that is often reddish in color. It thrives in moist, temperate environments and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
