Hermannia helianthemum

Hermannia helianthemum, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that resemble those of the buttercup, typically blooming in summer. The plant has woody stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Hermannia helianthemum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
