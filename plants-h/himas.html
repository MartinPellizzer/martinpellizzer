Himas

Himas, commonly known as the Himas plant, is a unique species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom during the wet season. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly valued in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
