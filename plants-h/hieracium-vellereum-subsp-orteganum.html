Hieracium vellereum subsp. orteganum

Hieracium vellereum subsp.

orteganum, commonly known as the orteganum hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
