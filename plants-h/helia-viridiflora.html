Helia viridiflora

Helia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered helia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant green petals and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value.

Helia viridiflora is valued in horticulture for its unique coloration and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
