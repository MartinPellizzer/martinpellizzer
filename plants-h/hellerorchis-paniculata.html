Hellerorchis paniculata

Hellerorchis paniculata, commonly known as the panicle orchid, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinct three-lobed lip.

Hellerorchis paniculata is a short-lived perennial, often blooming in late spring to early summer.
