Habenaria chlorina

Habenaria chlorina, commonly known as the green orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
