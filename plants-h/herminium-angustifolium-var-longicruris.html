Herminium angustifolium var. longicruris

Herminium angustifolium var. longicruris, commonly known as the slender-leaf twayblade, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial herb with a distinctive appearance, featuring a slender, leafy stem and reduced leaves. The plant produces a single, elongated spike of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role within its native habitat.
