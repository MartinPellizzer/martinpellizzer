Hieracium onosmoides subsp. sempronianum

Hieracium onosmoides subsp.

sempronianum, commonly known as the Sempronian hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and is part of the dandelion family, Asteraceae.
