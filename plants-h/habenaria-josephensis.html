Habenaria josephensis

Habenaria josephensis, commonly known as Joseph's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with prominent markings, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
