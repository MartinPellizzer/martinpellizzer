Halenia elliptica var. grandiflora

Halenia elliptica var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered halenia, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, elliptic leaves that are often tinged with purple or red at the base. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, arranged in clusters along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture.
