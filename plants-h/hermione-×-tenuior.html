Hermione × tenuior

Hermione × tenuior, commonly known as the hybrid evergreen shrub, is a botanical cross between two species of the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and compact growth habit, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping. The plant typically produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring season. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in coastal and garden settings where it provides year-round visual interest.
