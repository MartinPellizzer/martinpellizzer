Homalomena ovalifolia

Homalomena ovalifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking orange-red spathes that enclose its small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
