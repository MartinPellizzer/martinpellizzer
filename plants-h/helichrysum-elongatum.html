Helichrysum elongatum

Helichrysum elongatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, yellowish or golden flowers. The plant is known for its long-lasting flowers, which retain their color and form even after drying, making it popular in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
