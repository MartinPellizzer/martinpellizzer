Hypoestes barteri

Hypoestes barteri, commonly known as the "Blue Baby" or "Snowflake" plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful, showy bracts that resemble small flowers. The plant produces small, tubular, white or pale yellow flowers that are often overshadowed by the vibrant bracts. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Its ornamental value is largely due to its unique and colorful foliage and bracts.
