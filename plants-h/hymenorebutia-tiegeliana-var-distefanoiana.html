Hymenorebutia tiegeliana var. distefanoiana

Hymenorebutia tiegeliana var. distefanoiana, commonly known as the Distefano's Crown of Thorns, is a compact cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It is well adapted to hot, dry climates and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
