Hieracium cryptadenum subsp. cryptadenioides

Hieracium cryptadenum subsp.

cryptadenioides, commonly known as the cryptadenium hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is notable for its limited distribution and ecological specificity.
