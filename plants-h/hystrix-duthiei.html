Hystrix duthiei

Hystrix duthiei, commonly known as the African spiny scale.

It is a unique plant species native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, spiny foliage that serves as a defense mechanism against herbivores. The plant has a succulent appearance, with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, and it is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its distinctive appearance and ecological adaptations, Hystrix duthiei is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
