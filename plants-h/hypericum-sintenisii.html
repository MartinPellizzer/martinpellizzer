Hypericum sintenisii

Hypericum sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenis' St. John's Wort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has woody stems and alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical features make it a valued addition to both ornamental gardens and ecological habitats.
