Helianthemum apenninum var. riffeum

Helianthemum apenninum var. riffeum, commonly known as the Rif rosemary.

This evergreen shrub is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and fragrant properties.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a border plant.
