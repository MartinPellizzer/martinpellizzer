Helichrysum polycephalum

Helichrysum polycephalum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that remain vibrant even when dried. The plant produces small, spherical seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its resilience and ornamental value have made it a favored plant in horticulture.
