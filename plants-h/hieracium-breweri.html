Hieracium breweri

Hieracium breweri, commonly known as Brewer's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Brewer's hawkweed is often found in California and is considered a rare and endangered species due to habitat loss.
