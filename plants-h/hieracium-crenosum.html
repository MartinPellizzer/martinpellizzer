Hieracium crenosum

Hieracium crenosum, commonly known as the hairy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of yellow flowers, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are typically hairy and have a serrated edge, contributing to its common name.

Hieracium crenosum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
