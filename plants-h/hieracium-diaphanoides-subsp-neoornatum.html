Hieracium diaphanoides subsp. neoornatum

Hieracium diaphanoides subsp.

neoornatum, commonly known as the translucent hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems.

The subspecies neoornatum is distinguished by its pale, almost translucent leaves and smaller flower heads compared to other varieties. It typically grows in meadows and grassy areas, thriving in temperate climates.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is often considered a rare and ornamental species.
