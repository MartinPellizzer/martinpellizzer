Hordeum macrolepis

Hordeum macrolepis, commonly known as the slender barley, is a species of grass native to Eurasia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated spikelets and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often loosely arranged along the stem.

Hordeum macrolepis is adapted to arid conditions and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native environment.
