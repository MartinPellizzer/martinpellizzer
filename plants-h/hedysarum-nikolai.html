Hedysarum nikolai

Hedysarum nikolai, commonly known as Nikolai's sweet clover, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.

Hedysarum nikolai is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
