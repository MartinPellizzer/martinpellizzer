Homalomena hastata

Homalomena hastata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking, colorful foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden beneath a leaf-like spathe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Homalomena hastata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant, variegated leaves.
