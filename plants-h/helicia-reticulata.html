Helicia reticulata

Helicia reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate helenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces elliptical, dark purple to black fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. Helicia reticulata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in tropical gardens.
