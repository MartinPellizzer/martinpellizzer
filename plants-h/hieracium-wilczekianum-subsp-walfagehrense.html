Hieracium wilczekianum subsp. walfagehrense

Hieracium wilczekianum subsp.

walfagehrense, commonly known as the Walfagehren hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, often disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.
