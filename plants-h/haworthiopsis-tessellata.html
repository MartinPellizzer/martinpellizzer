Haworthiopsis tessellata

Haworthiopsis tessellata, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern and its distinctive, light green, diamond-patterned leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Haworthiopsis tessellata is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
