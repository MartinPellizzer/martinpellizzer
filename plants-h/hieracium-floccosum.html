Hieracium floccosum

Hieracium floccosum, commonly known as the downy hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant produces fluffy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically basal, with a rough, hairy texture, and the stems are often reddish in color.

Hieracium floccosum is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in sunny, well-drained soils.
