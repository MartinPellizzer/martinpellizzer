Heteropterys macrostachya f. ovata

Heteropterys macrostachya f. ovata, commonly known as the ovate-leafed heteropterys, is a variant of the species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
