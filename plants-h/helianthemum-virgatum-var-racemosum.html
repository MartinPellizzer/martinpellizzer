Helianthemum virgatum var. racemosum

Helianthemum virgatum var. racemosum, commonly known as the clustered rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compact, evergreen foliage with a dense, mat-like growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and drought-tolerant plant ideal for xeriscaping.
