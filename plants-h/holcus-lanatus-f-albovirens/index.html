Holcus Lanatus F. Albovirens

Holcus lanatus f. albovirens is a unique variant of the common bentgrass known for its distinctive silver-green foliage. This ornamental grass thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lawns and gardens. Its fluffy, grass-like appearance makes it a popular choice for landscaping and erosion control. The plant's variegated leaves add a striking visual contrast to traditional greenery. Despite its beauty, it requires careful maintenance to prevent it from becoming invasive in certain environments.
