Helianthemum ciliatum f. psilocalyx

Helianthemum ciliatum f. psilocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fleshy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are narrow, linear, and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a distinctive, rugged appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This cultivar is valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
