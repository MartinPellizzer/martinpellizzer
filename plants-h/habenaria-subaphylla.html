Habenaria subaphylla

Habenaria subaphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually arranged in a loose spike and have a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Habenaria subaphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
