Hymenophyllum sampaioanum

Hymenophyllum sampaioanum, commonly known as the Sampaio's spore moss, is a rare and delicate fern belonging to the Hymenophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds with closely spaced, overlapping leaves that create a dense, moss-like appearance. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures, known as sporangia, are borne on the underside of the leaves and produce spores for dispersal.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Hymenophyllum sampaioanum is considered a rare and ecologically significant plant.
