Henningia Afghanica

Henningia afghanica is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Afghanistan. It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is known for its unique and delicate appearance. This species thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil. Its small, bell-shaped flowers often display a striking combination of white and pink hues.
