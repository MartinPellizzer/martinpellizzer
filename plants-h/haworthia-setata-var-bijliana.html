Haworthia setata var. bijliana

Haworthia setata var. bijliana, commonly known as the Zebra Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are adorned with distinct white or yellow markings, resembling a zebra's stripes. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, allowing the plant to thrive in arid conditions. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the growing season.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
