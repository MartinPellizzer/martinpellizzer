Hieracium sparsum subsp. ipekanum

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

ipekanum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, with a distinctive basal rosette structure.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
