Haworthia spirella

Haworthia spirella, commonly known as the spiral Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-shaped leaves that form a compact, spiral pattern. The leaves are typically fleshy and often have a waxy coating, which helps the plant retain moisture. It produces small, white or greenish flowers on thin stems during the growing season.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.
