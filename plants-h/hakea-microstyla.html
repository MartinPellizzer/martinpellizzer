Hakea microstyla

Hakea microstyla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to southwestern Australia.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces cylindrical, red or pink flower spikes that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its woody stems are often branched and can become lignified over time.

Hakea microstyla is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.
