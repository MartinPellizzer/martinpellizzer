Hieracium scorzonerifolium var. burlei

Hieracium scorzonerifolium var. burlei, commonly known as Burle's hairy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers. The leaves are typically lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open habitats.

This variant is notable for its adaptability and ornamental value in horticulture.
