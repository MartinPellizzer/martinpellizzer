Heisteria dregei

Heisteria dregei, commonly known as the Cape daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Heisteria dregei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its hardy nature and ability to attract pollinators.
