Hackelia virginiana

Hackelia virginiana, commonly known as Virginia catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
