Hippeastrum datense

Hippeastrum datense, commonly known as the Datian amaryllis, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a bulbous plant characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces strap-shaped leaves that are typically broad and pointed. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Hippeastrum datense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
