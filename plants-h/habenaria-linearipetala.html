Habenaria linearipetala

Habenaria linearipetala, commonly known as the slender-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on the forest floor or in rocky crevices. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Botanically, it is characterized by its intricate floral structure and adaptations to its humid, tropical habitat.
