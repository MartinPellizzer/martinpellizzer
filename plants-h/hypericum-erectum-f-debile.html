Hypericum erectum f. debile

Hypericum erectum f. debile, commonly known as the slender St. John's wort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features slender, upright stems covered with small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its compact appearance. This variety is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological gardens and wildlife habitats.
