Habenaria muricata

Habenaria muricata, commonly known as the rough-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, rough-textured leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. It has a sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing new growth from the base.

The flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and moths.
