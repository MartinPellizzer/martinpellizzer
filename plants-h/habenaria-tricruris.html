Habenaria tricruris

Habenaria tricruris, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a spike. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy surface to reduce water loss.

The species is characterized by its unique floral structure and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
