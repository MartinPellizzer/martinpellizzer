Holmskioldia sanguinea f. aurantiaca

Holmskioldia sanguinea f. aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange flame plant, is a hybrid cultivar of the flame flower.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and seasonal blooms.

The f. aurantiaca form exhibits more intense coloration compared to the typical species, making it a popular choice in horticulture.
