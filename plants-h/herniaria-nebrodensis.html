Herniaria nebrodensis

Herniaria nebrodensis, commonly known as the Sicilian sandwort, is a small perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments.
