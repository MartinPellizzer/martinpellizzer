Heliotropium canescens

Heliotropium canescens, commonly known as silver heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Heliotropium canescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
