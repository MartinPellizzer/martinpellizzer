Haworthia marumiana var. viridis

Haworthia marumiana var. viridis, commonly known as the green Haworthia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a waxy coating. The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, and the plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
