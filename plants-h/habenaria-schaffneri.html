Habenaria schaffneri

Habenaria schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial herb, with lance-shaped leaves and a single, erect stem. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Habenaria schaffneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
