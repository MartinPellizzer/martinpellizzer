Hieracium vernale

Hieracium vernale, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows up to 30 cm tall, with a rosette of basal leaves and a cluster of yellow flowers at the top. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its flowers bloom from late spring to early summer, and it produces numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Hieracium vernale is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
