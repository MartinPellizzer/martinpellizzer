Hippeastrum × brookesii

Hippeastrum × brookesii, commonly known as the Amazonian belladonna lily, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that can range in color from white to pink and red. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle.

Hippeastrum × brookesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
