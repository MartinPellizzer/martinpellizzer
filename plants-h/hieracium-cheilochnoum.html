Hieracium cheilochnoum

Hieracium cheilochnoum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish to reddish-orange flowers. The plant typically has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, often in temperate regions of Europe.

Hieracium cheilochnoum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
