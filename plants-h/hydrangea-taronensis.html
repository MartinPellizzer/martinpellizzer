Hydrangea taronensis

Hydrangea taronensis, commonly known as the Taronensis hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It features large, showy inflorescences that can range from white to pink, depending on soil pH. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with dense, dark green foliage. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes in temperate climates.
