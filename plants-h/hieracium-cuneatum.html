Hieracium cuneatum

Hieracium cuneatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Hieracium cuneatum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a common weed in various habitats.
