Hieracium murorum subsp. tenuiflorum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

tenuiflorum, commonly known as slender flowered hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in grassy areas and disturbed soils, showcasing a rosette of basal leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a flat head, and it produces seeds in a schizocarp.

It is often found in open habitats and is relatively hardy, adapting well to various environmental conditions.
