Hibiscus diodon

Hibiscus diodon, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to deep red. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its stems are often hairy. Hibiscus diodon thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking blooms and adaptability to various climates.
