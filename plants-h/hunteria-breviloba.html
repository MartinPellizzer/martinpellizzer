Hunteria breviloba

Hunteria breviloba, commonly known as the African orchid or yellow orchid, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Hunteria breviloba thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
