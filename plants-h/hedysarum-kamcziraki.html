Hedysarum kamcziraki

Hedysarum kamcziraki, commonly known as the Kamchatka milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the Russian Far East.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces elongated pods containing small, dark seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Hedysarum kamcziraki plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest in ecological restoration projects.
