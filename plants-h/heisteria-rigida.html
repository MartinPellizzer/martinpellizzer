Heisteria rigida

Heisteria rigida, commonly known as the rigid heister, is a member of the Primulaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its upright, branching stems and opposite, ovate leaves. It is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
