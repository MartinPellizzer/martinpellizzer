Hieracium phlomoides subsp. pseudandurense

Hieracium phlomoides subsp.

pseudandurense, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers arranged in composite heads.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture. It is native to Central and Southern Europe and thrives in grassy habitats.

This subspecies is often found in open areas with well-drained soil.
