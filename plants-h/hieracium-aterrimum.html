Hieracium aterrimum

Hieracium aterrimum, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and basal rosettes of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically visited by various pollinators. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, sometimes becoming invasive.

Hieracium aterrimum thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.
