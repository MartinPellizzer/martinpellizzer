Habenaria kraenzliniana

Habenaria kraenzliniana, commonly known as the Kraenzlin's orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctively shaped lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts, though it remains relatively rare in the wild.
