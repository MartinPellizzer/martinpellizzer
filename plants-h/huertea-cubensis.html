Huertea cubensis

Huertea cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Cuba.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking, showy flowers. The flowers typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
