Herrera

Herrera, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers and feathery, aromatic leaves. The plant belongs to the genus Tithonia and is part of the sunflower family, Asteraceae. It thrives in warm climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Herrera is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
