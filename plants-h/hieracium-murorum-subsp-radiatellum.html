Hieracium murorum subsp. radiatellum

Hieracium murorum subsp.

radiatellum, commonly known as the lesser yellow hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of both ray and disk flowers.

The plant typically grows in grassy habitats and disturbed soils, often forming dense mats. Its yellowish-orange flowers bloom from late spring to early summer, and the leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture.

It is an herbaceous species that can spread through rhizomes and is sometimes considered invasive in certain regions.
