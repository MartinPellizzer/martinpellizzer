Heterotaxis maleolens

Heterotaxis maleolens, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a unique plant characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves.

Its fronds exhibit a helical pattern, which is an unusual morphological feature in the fern family. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. It has small, delicate leaves that grow in a tightly coiled manner around the stem.

The botanical interest in Heterotaxis maleolens lies in its intricate leaf arrangement and its rarity in the wild.
