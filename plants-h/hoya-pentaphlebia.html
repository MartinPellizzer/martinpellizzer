Hoya pentaphlebia

Hoya pentaphlebia, commonly known as the starry wisteria hoya, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Hoya pentaphlebia is a climbing plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
