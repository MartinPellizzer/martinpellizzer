Habenaria yachangensis

Habenaria yachangensis, commonly known as the Yachang orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
