Hesperantha helmei

Hesperantha helmei, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive helmet-shaped flower, which gives it its common name, and features vibrant purple and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny conditions. Hesperantha helmei has lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
