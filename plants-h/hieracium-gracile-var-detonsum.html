Hieracium gracile var. detonsum

Hieracium gracile var. detonsum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its slender stems and hairy leaves distinguish it from other hawkweed species.
