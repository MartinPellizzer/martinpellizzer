Helichrysum capillaceum var. diffusum

Helichrysum capillaceum var. diffusum, commonly known as the spreading helichrysum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and bright yellow-orange inflorescences. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically clustered in dense, rounded heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value contribute to its use in both horticultural and ecological landscapes.
