Homalomena mixta

Homalomena mixta, commonly known as the Variegated Calla Lily, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves and dramatic, white spatulate flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense foliage.

Homalomena mixta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.
