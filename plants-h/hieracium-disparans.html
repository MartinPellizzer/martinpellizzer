Hieracium disparans

Hieracium disparans, commonly known as the wandering thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, surrounded by spiny, hairy leaves. It has a creeping growth habit with a mat-forming structure, allowing it to spread across the ground.

Hieracium disparans is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
