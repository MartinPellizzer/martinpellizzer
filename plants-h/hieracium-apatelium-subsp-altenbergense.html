Hieracium apatelium subsp. altenbergense

Hieracium apatelium subsp.

altenbergense, commonly known as the Altenberg hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
