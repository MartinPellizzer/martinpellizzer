Hydriastele Wendlandiana

Hydriastele wendlandiana is a rare and striking member of the Bromeliad family known for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers. Native to the cloud forests of Ecuador, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air moisture. Its unique inflorescence features bright red bracts that surround clusters of small white flowers, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant is prized by collectors for its ornamental value and ease of care in the right conditions. Due to its limited natural habitat, conservation efforts are essential to ensure its survival in the wild.
