Hopea aequalis

Hopea aequalis, commonly known as the equal-flowered hopea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The tree can grow up to 40 meters tall and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and elliptical leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in yellow or greenish-yellow clusters.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
