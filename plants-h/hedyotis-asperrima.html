Hedyotis asperrima

Hedyotis asperrima, commonly known as the rough hedyotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving it a rough texture.

Hedyotis asperrima is often found in fynbos vegetation, thriving in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
