Helianthemum aganae

Helianthemum aganae, commonly known as the Canary Island rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are evergreen, leathery, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native habitat.
