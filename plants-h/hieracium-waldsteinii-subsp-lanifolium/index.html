Hieracium Waldsteinii Subsp. Lanifolium

Hieracium waldsteinii subsp. lanifolium is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of europe. it belongs to the asteraceae family and is known for its soft, hairy leaves and small yellow flowers. this subspecies is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered in many areas. conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique and beautiful plant from extinction.
