Hiorthia × valentina

Hiorthia × valentina, commonly known as the hybrid snowflake, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
