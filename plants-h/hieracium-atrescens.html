Hieracium atrescens

Hieracium atrescens, commonly known as the purple hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering stem that bears yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is found in various regions across Europe and parts of Asia. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered with glandular dots.

Hieracium atrescens is a perennial species that can form dense colonies, making it both a common weed and a subject of ecological study.
