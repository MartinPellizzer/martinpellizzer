Habenaria chloroleuca var. genuina

Habenaria chloroleuca var. genuina, commonly known as the White Lady Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers arranged in a raceme, which are notable for their delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
