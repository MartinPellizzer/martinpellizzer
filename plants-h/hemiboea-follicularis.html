Hemiboea follicularis

Hemiboea follicularis, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Hemiboea follicularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
