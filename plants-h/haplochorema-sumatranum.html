Haplochorema sumatranum

Haplochorema sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran starling orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, attracting specific pollinators.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
