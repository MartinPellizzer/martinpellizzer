Hieracium prenanthoides subsp. iuranum

Hieracium prenanthoides subsp.

iuranum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy, rough leaves.

Native to Europe, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces numerous small, yellow ray florets surrounded by a ring of disk flowers.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other sunny, well-drained areas.
