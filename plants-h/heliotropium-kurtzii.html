Heliotropium kurtzii

Heliotropium kurtzii, commonly known as the African heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and range in color from pink to purple.

Heliotropium kurtzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
