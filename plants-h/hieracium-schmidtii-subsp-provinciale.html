Hieracium schmidtii subsp. provinciale

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

provinciale, commonly known as the Provincial Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow ray flowers and green, hairy leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It is a perennial species that produces small, dandelion-like seeds for propagation.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
