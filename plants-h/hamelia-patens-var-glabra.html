Hamelia patens var. glabra

Hamelia patens var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth flamebush, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, tubular red-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has smooth, glabrous bark and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and hummingbirds.
