Hieracium tajanum

Hieracium tajanum, commonly known as the Canary Islands hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom from spring to summer.

Hieracium tajanum is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
