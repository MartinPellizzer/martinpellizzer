Habenaria ekmaniana

Habenaria ekmaniana, commonly known as the Ekman's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent sepals and petals. It typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, and its growth is often found in moist, shaded forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
