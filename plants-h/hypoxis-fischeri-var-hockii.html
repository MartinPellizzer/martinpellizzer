Hypoxis fischeri var. hockii

Hypoxis fischeri var. hockii, commonly known as the Hock's yellow star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
