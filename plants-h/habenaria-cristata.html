Habenaria cristata

Habenaria cristata, commonly known as the Crested Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, recurved petals and a prominent, erect lip that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with subtle markings, and it blooms during the summer months.

Habenaria cristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
