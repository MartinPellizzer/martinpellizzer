Haitiella ekmanii

Haitiella ekmanii, commonly known as the Haitian orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Haiti.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on the trunks of trees in tropical forests. Its botanical features include slender stems and small, greenish-white blooms.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a critically endangered species.
