Haniffia cyanescens var. cyanescens

Haniffia cyanescens var. cyanescens, commonly known as the blue-flowered rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking blue to violet flowers during the summer months. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are borne on short, upright stems.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
