Helianthemum Chamaecistus Subsp. Nummularium

Helianthemum chamaecistus subsp. nummularium is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is known for its compact, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a valuable addition to xeriscaping efforts. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and enhancing biodiversity in its habitat.
