Hemiboea thanhhoensis

Hemiboea thanhhoensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese star moss, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Vietnam. The plant features striking, velvety leaves with a deep green color and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in horticulture.
