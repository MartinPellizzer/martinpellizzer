Haworthia truterorum

Haworthia truterorum, commonly known as the Truter's Haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in white or grayish markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
