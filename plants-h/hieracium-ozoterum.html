Hieracium ozoterum

Hieracium ozoterum, commonly known as the alpine hawkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are basal, rough, and hairy, while its flowers are yellow and bloom in summer.

Hieracium ozoterum is often found in open, sunny areas and is adapted to cold climates.
