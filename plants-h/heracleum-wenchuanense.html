Heracleum wenchuanense

Heracleum wenchuanense, commonly known as the Wenchuan giant cowparsnip, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its tall stature and large, umbrella-like inflorescence.

The flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in a compound umbel, typical of the carrot family.
