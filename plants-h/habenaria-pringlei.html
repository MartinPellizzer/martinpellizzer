Habenaria pringlei

Habenaria pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant produces a single, upright stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its native range.
