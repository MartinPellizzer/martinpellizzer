Hieracium nigrescens subsp. graciliusculum

Hieracium nigrescens subsp.

graciliusculum, commonly known as the slender yellow hawkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish to orange-pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the stems are slender and often branched. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

This subspecies is often found in meadows, pastures, and along roadsides, where it can form dense colonies.
