Herpestis radicata

Herpestis radicata, commonly known as the redroot sandparsley, is a member of the Apiaceae family and is native to the arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Herpestis radicata is adapted to harsh environments and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils.
