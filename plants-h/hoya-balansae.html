Hoya balansae

Hoya balansae, commonly known as the "Coral Hoya," is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its waxy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care. Hoya balansae thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
