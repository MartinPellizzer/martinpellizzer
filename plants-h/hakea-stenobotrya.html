Hakea stenobotrya

Hakea stenobotrya, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical clusters of narrow, needle-like leaves. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in spring, which are typically clustered in spikes. Hakea stenobotrya is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
