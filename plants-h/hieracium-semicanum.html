Hieracium semicanum

Hieracium semicanum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate, with a hairy texture. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in disturbed soils.
