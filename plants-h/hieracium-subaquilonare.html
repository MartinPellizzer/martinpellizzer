Hieracium subaquilonare

Hieracium subaquilonare, commonly known as the water hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as meadows, marshes, and riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained to slightly wet soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Hieracium subaquilonare is often found in temperate regions and is known for its resilience in wetland habitats.
