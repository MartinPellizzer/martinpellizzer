Habenaria wolongensis

Habenaria wolongensis, commonly known as the Wolong orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often spotted or streaked. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
