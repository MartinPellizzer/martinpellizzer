Himantoglossum hircinum subsp. caprinum

Himantoglossum hircinum subsp.

caprinum, commonly known as the Alpine lady's tresses, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae. It is characterized by its dense, spiral arrangement of dark green, lance-shaped leaves and its striking, fragrant inflorescence of white to pale pink flowers.

This subspecies is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions, growing in moist, rocky or grassy habitats. It thrives in cool, temperate climates and is often seen in mountainous areas of Europe.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized plant with limited distribution.
