Hibiscus mongallaensis

Hibiscus mongallaensis, commonly known as the Mongalla hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often found in woodland areas and along riverbanks.

Hibiscus mongallaensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
