Habenaria vermeuleniana

Habenaria vermeuleniana, commonly known as the Vermeulen's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with three sepals and three petals, typically arranged in a loose spike. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
