Hieracium magocsyanum

Hieracium magocsyanum, commonly known as the Magocsyan's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It features basal leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture.

Its yellow flowers bloom in the summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
