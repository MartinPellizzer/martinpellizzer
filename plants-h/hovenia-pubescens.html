Hovenia pubescens

Hovenia pubescens, commonly known as the Chinese date or wild date tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy, giving it the species name *pubescens*. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by fleshy, orange-red fruits that are edible. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or scrub habitats.

The fruit contains a single seed and is used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some regions.
