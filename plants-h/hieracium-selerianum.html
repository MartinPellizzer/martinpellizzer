Hieracium selerianum

Hieracium selerianum, commonly known as the Seler's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features showy yellow or orange composite flowers. The plant typically has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Hieracium selerianum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain habitats.
