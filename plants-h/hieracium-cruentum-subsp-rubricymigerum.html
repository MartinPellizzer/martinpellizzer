Hieracium cruentum subsp. rubricymigerum

Hieracium cruentum subsp.

rubricymigerum, commonly known as the red-stemmed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow ray flowers and reddish stems.

The plant typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along streambanks. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

The subspecies rubricymigerum is distinguished by its more intense red coloration in the stems and leaves compared to other varieties of Hieracium.
