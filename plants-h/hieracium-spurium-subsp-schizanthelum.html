Hieracium spurium subsp. schizanthelum

Hieracium spurium subsp.

schizanthelum, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to orange-red flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has a creeping growth habit with rhizomatous stems, allowing it to spread and form dense mats. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are arranged in flat-topped clusters.

It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides.
