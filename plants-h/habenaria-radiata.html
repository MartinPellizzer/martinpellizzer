Habenaria radiata

Habenaria radiata, commonly known as the radiant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and swamps. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

The orchid's unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
