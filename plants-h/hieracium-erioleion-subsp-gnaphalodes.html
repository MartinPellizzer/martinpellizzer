Hieracium erioleion subsp. gnaphalodes

Hieracium erioleion subsp.

gnaphalodes, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish composite flowers.

The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

This subspecies is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
