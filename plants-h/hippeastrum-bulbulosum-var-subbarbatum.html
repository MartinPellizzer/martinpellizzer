Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. subbarbatum

Hippeastrum bulbulosum var. subbarbatum, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white or green patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
