Huernia blackbeardiae

Huernia blackbeardiae, commonly known as the Blackbeard's moon cactus, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a swollen, bulbous stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically borne on short, branched stems. Its leaves are reduced to small, fleshy tubercles, and it has a unique, segmented appearance.

Huernia blackbeardiae is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
