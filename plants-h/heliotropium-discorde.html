Heliotropium discorde

Heliotropium discorde, commonly known as the devil's backbone, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink, often resembling the shape of a backbone. The plant typically grows as an erect annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with glandular dots.

Heliotropium discorde is often found in disturbed soils and is adaptable to various environmental conditions.
