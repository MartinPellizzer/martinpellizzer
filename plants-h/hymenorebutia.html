Hymenorebutia

Hymenorebutia, commonly known as the "Moon Cactus," is a type of cactus belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly the Andes region, where it thrives in high-altitude environments. The plant features a colorful, fleshy body called a glochidion, which is often used as a decorative element. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and bloom in the spring.

Hymenorebutia is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and striking appearance.
