Hymenocallis caribaea f. angustifolia

Hymenocallis caribaea f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hurricane lily, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and resilience in tropical and subtropical climates.
