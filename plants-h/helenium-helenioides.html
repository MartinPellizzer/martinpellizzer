Helenium helenioides

Helenium helenioides, commonly known as the swamp sneaker, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It features showy, bright orange to red ray flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often submerged or partially submerged in water. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Helenium helenioides plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
