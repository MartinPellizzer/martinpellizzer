Hieracium × valdostanum

Hieracium × valdostanum, commonly known as the Alpine hawkweed, is a hybrid plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, erect stems and bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring well-drained, rocky soils. It is native to the European Alps and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and mountain pastures.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations across its distribution.
