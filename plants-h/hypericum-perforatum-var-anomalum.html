Hypericum perforatum var. anomalum

Hypericum perforatum var. anomalum, commonly known as the spotted yellowstone hypericum, is a variant of the common St. John's wort.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of western North America. The plant is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with prominent black dots on the petals. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and often have translucent glands.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
