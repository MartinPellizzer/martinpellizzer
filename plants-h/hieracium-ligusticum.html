Hieracium ligusticum

Hieracium ligusticum, commonly known as the Ligusticum hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its flowers are yellow or orange, with ray florets that give it a dandelion-like appearance.

Hieracium ligusticum is often found in open habitats and can spread rapidly through its rhizomatous growth system.
