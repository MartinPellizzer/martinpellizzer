Helichrysum hookeri var. expansifolium

Helichrysum hookeri var. expansifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf everlasting, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that remain unchanged after blooming, giving it a long-lasting appearance. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
