Hieracium tenebricosum

Hieracium tenebricosum, commonly known as the dark hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant produces yellowish to reddish composite flowers, which are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, giving them a rough texture.

Hieracium tenebricosum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many temperate regions.
