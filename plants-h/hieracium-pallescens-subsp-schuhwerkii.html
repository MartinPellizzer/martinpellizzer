Hieracium pallescens subsp. schuhwerkii

Hieracium pallescens subsp.

schuhwerkii, commonly known as the pale hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellow disk flowers that are typically surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and often endangered species.
