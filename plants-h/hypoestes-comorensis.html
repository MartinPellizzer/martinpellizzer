Hypoestes comorensis

Hypoestes comorensis, commonly known as the "Blue Baby" or "Purple Heart" plant, is a flowering shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Hypoestes comorensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
