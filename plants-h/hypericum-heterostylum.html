Hypericum heterostylum

Hypericum heterostylum, commonly known as the narrowleaf St. John's wort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Hypericaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with small, glandular dots on their surface.

Hypericum heterostylum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
