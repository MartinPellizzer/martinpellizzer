Hippeastrum vittatum

Hippeastrum vittatum, commonly known as the Zebra lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features long, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white or green markings. Hippeastrum vittatum thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its dramatic floral display and relatively easy care requirements.
