Hieracium dolichocranum

Hieracium dolichocranum, commonly known as the long-headed hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically hairy stems. The plant features upright, branching stems that are often covered in fine, white to grayish hairs. Its flowers are yellow and form dense, rounded clusters at the ends of the stems.

Hieracium dolichocranum typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade.
