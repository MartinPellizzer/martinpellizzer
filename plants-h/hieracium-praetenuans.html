Hieracium praetenuans

Hieracium praetenuans, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in meadows and open woodlands. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates.

Hieracium praetenuans is a perennial species that spreads through rhizomes and is known for its ability to colonize disturbed soils.
