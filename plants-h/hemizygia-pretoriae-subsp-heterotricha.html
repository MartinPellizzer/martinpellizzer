Hemizygia pretoriae subsp. heterotricha

Hemizygia pretoriae subsp.

heterotricha, commonly known as the Pretoria thornveld grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often bear sharp, spiny structures.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in grassland and thicket habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in restoration projects and as a ornamental plant.
