Hieracium scardicum subsp. lachnoneuron

Hieracium scardicum subsp.

lachnoneuron, commonly known as the meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and basal rosettes of lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite inflorescence, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist meadows and grasslands, often forming dense colonies.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
