Hieracium sahunianum

Hieracium sahunianum, commonly known as the Sahunian hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a single, upright stem that bears yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has small, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces, and its flowers are composed of both ray and disk florets.

Hieracium sahunianum is native to parts of Europe and is often found in regions with temperate climates.
