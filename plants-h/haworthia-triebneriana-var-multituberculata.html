Haworthia triebneriana var. multituberculata

Haworthia triebneriana var. multituberculata, commonly known as the zigzag Haworthia, is a compact, slow-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette, often with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the leaf edges. The leaves are typically a deep green with white or pale green markings, and they can develop a reddish tinge when exposed to full sun. It produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall stems during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
