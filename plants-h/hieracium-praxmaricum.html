Hieracium praxmaricum

Hieracium praxmaricum, commonly known as the Praxmaric hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and is typically found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often lance-shaped. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates.

Hieracium praxmaricum is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.
