Helichrysum filiforme

Helichrysum filiforme, commonly known as the slender everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature. The plant has a creeping growth habit with narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Helichrysum filiforme is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
