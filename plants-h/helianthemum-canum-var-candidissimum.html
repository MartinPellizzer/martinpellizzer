Helianthemum canum var. candidissimum

Helianthemum canum var. candidissimum, commonly known as the white rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has gray-green, fuzzy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
