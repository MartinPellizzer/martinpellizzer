Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp. cilentanum

Hieracium hypochoeroides subsp.

cilentanum, commonly known as the Cilentan hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Italy. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
