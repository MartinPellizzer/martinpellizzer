Hildewintera colademononis

Hildewintera colademononis, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It features dense clusters of small, waxy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the holiday season. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a festive, year-round display.

Due to its unique appearance and seasonal blooming, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
