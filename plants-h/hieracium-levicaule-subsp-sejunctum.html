Hieracium levicaule subsp. sejunctum

Hieracium levicaule subsp.

sejunctum, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a member of the Asteraceae family. It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect, hairy stems and alternate, lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite inflorescences, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, disturbed habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it distinguishable from other hawkweed species by its slender growth habit and specific floral structure.
