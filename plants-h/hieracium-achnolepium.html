Hieracium achnolepium

Hieracium achnolepium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, linear leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. Native to Europe, it often grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is sometimes grown in gardens.
