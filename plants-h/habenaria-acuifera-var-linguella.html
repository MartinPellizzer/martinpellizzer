Habenaria acuifera var. linguella

Habenaria acuifera var. linguella, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or swampy areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination by insects.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
