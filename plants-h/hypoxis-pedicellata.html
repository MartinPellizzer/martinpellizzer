Hypoxis pedicellata

Hypoxis pedicellata, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its fibrous root system and is often found in open, grassy areas. It is adapted to full sun and well-drained soils, making it a hardy species in arid environments.

Hypoxis pedicellata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
