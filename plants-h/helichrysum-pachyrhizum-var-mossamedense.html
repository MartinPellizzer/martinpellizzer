Helichrysum pachyrhizum var. mossamedense

Helichrysum pachyrhizum var. mossamedense, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble small cups, giving it its common name. The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
