Homalomena gadutensis

Homalomena gadutensis, commonly known as the Borneo arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix and a spathe, typical of the genus Homalomena. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
