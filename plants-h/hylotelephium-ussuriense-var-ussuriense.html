Hylotelephium ussuriense var. ussuriense

Hylotelephium ussuriense var. ussuriense, commonly known as the Ussuri stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to eastern Asia.

It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces showy clusters of pink or red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for xeriscaping.
