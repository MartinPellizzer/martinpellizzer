Huernia macrocarpa subsp. harerghensis

Huernia macrocarpa subsp.

harerghensis, commonly known as the Harerghen Huernia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are often spine-tipped and arranged in a rosette pattern, providing structural support and reducing water loss.

This subspecies is valued for its unique appearance and adaptability to harsh environments.
