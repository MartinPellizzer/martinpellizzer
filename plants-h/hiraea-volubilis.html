Hiraea volubilis

Hiraea volubilis, commonly known as the climbing hiraea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Hiraea volubilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
