Narcissus × clairelouisae

Narcissus × clairelouisae, commonly known as the Claire Louis daffodil, is a hybrid daffodil prized for its striking floral display.

It features large, trumpet-shaped blooms with a vibrant yellow corona and a white or pale yellow perianth. The plant is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to the Netherlands.

This cultivar is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and early spring blooming habit.
