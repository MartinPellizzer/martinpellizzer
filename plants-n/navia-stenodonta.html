Navia stenodonta

Navia stenodonta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved navia, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Navia stenodonta is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant.
