Notocactus spegazzinii var. horstii

Notocactus spegazzinii var. horstii, commonly known as the Horstii Notocactus, is a unique cactus species native to South America.

It is characterized by its columnar stem and distinctive, rib-like structures that help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding visual appeal to its otherwise spiny appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
