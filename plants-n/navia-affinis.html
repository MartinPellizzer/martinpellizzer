Navia affinis

Navia affinis, commonly known as the slender navia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters at the tips of the branches. Navia affinis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
