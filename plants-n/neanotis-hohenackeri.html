Neanotis hohenackeri

Neanotis hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a robust, upright stem that is often hairy and can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a serrated edge and are covered in fine hairs.

Neanotis hohenackeri is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
