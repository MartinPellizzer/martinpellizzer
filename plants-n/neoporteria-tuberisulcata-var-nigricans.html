Neoporteria tuberisulcata var. nigricans

Neoporteria tuberisulcata var. nigricans, commonly known as the black-stemmed tuberous orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black stems and leaves, which contrast sharply with its vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, orchid-like blooms in shades of white and pink, often appearing during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanical enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
