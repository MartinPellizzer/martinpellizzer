Nasturtium petraeum

Nasturtium petraeum, commonly known as the rock nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers, which bloom throughout the summer months. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Nasturtium petraeum is also known for its edible flowers and leaves, which have a peppery flavor similar to watercress.
