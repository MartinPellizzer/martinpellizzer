Nasturtium officinale var. platyphyllum

Nasturtium officinale var. platyphyllum, commonly known as the flatleaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of orange and yellow. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It is also valued for its edible flowers and leaves, which have a peppery taste.

This variety is known for its resilience and is often used in companion planting to deter pests in gardens.
