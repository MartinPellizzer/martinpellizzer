Nasturtium bonariense var. erectum

Nasturtium bonariense var. erectum, commonly known as the erect nasturtium, is a vigorous annual vine native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of orange and yellow. The plant is known for its edible parts, including the leaves, flowers, and seeds, which have a peppery, tangy flavor. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often climbing or trailing along supports.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental and edible gardening.
