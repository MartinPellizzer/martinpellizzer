Nidularium pulcherrimum

Nidularium pulcherrimum, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped flower. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Nidularium pulcherrimum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or warm climates.
