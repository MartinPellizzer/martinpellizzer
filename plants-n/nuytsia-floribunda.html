Nuytsia floribunda

Nuytsia floribunda, commonly known as the ghost gum, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree with a distinctive, gnarled trunk and a rounded crown. The plant produces showy, pale yellow or white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and arranged alternately along the branches.

The ghost gum is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal and inland areas.
