Navia xyridiflora

Navia xyridiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved navia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and linear, often with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Navia xyridiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
