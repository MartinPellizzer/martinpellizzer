Neomammillaria phaeacantha

Neomammillaria phaeacantha, commonly known as the pink mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically covered in white or pink spines. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often appearing in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
