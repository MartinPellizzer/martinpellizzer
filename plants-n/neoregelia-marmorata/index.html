Neoregelia Marmorata

Neoregelia marmorata is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, colorful foliage. This member of the Bromeliaceae family is native to South America, particularly Brazil. Its leaves are often adorned with intricate patterns that resemble marble, hence its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Due to its beauty and ease of care, it has become a popular choice for indoor gardening enthusiasts.
