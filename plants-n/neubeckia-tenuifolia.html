Neubeckia tenuifolia

Neubeckia tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf neubeckia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are narrow and finely divided, giving it a lacey appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and subtle floral display, making it a popular choice for shade gardens and woodland settings.
