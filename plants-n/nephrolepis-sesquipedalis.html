Nephrolepis sesquipedalis

Nephrolepis sesquipedalis, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Its fronds are divided into segments with a distinctive, segmented appearance.

Nephrolepis sesquipedalis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
