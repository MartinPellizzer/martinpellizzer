Nephrodium cristatum var. slossoniae

Nephrodium cristatum var. slossoniae, commonly known as the Slosson's crest fern, is a rare and unique variant of the crest fern species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, crisped leaf margins that give it a rugged, textured appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are bioluminescent under certain conditions, a rare trait among ferns.

This variant is of particular interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unusual morphological features.
