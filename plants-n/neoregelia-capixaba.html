Neoregelia capixaba

Neoregelia capixaba, commonly known as the Capixaba bromeliad, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescences that can range from red to orange. The plant features a rosette of grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. Its foliage is often variegated with white and green patterns, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Neoregelia capixaba thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
