Nectandra purusensis

Nectandra purusensis, commonly known as the Amazonian sandarac tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface.

Nectandra purusensis is valued for its durable timber and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
