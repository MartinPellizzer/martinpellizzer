Nephrodium Cristatum Var. Uliginosum

Cushion fern, scientifically known as Nephrodium cristatum var. uliginosum, is a rare and unique plant found in wetland environments. This variant of the cushion fern is distinguished by its compact, dense growth habit that forms a rounded, mat-like structure. It thrives in moist, shaded areas where it can absorb the necessary nutrients from the soil. The plant's delicate fronds contribute to its aesthetic appeal and ecological importance. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in many regions.
