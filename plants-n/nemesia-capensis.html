Nemesia capensis

Nemesia capensis, commonly known as the common nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often hairy, while the flowers are borne in racemes.

Nemesia capensis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
