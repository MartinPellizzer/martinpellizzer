Noccaea diffusa

Noccaea diffusa, commonly known as broadleaf honesty, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate, hairy leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in racemes. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks.

Noccaea diffusa is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
