Nectandra chrysothyrsus

Nectandra chrysothyrsus, commonly known as golden thorn, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface.

Nectandra chrysothyrsus is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine and as a source of timber.
