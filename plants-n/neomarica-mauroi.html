Neomarica mauroi

Neomarica mauroi, commonly known as the Mauro's spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and slightly curled at the edges. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Neomarica mauroi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
