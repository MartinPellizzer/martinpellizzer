Nototriche incana

Nototriche incana, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Nototriche incana is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
