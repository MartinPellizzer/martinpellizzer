Nervilia gammieana

Nervilia gammieana, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage and its delicate, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.

Nervilia gammieana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
