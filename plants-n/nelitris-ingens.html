Nelitris ingens

Nelitris ingens, commonly known as the giant nelitris, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, broad leaves that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its rapid growth rate and ability to thrive in moist, fertile soils make it a notable species in its native habitat.
