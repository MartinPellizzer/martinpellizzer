Nemesia odoratissima

Nemesia odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet willie or sweet nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, white, and red. Nemesia odoratissima is typically grown as an annual in temperate climates.
