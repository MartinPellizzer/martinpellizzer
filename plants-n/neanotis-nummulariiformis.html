Neanotis nummulariiformis

Neanotis nummulariiformis, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, round, fleshy leaves that are typically silver or gray in color, giving it its common name. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Neanotis nummulariiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
