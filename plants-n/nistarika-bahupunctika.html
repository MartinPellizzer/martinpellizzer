Nistarika bahupunctika

Nistarika bahupunctika, commonly known as the many-pointed star plant, is a striking species native to tropical regions.

It features a rosette of fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish hue. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, which bloom during the day. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, providing a textured appearance and aiding in water retention.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance in suitable growing conditions.
