Narcissus Sylvestris Var. Bicolor

Narcissus sylvestris var. bicolor is a rare and striking member of the amaryllis family known for its unique two-toned flowers. This wild narcissus species is native to southern Europe and thrives in woodland environments. Its petals display a beautiful blend of white and yellow, creating a captivating visual contrast. Often overlooked due to its modest size, it holds significant ecological value. This plant is a testament to nature's ability to produce both beauty and resilience.
