Norta irio

Norta irio, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, golden-yellow inflorescences that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its rapid growth rate and ability to tolerate various soil conditions contribute to its widespread cultivation.

Norta irio is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of ornamental foliage.
