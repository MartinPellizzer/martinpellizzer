Nasa stolonifera

Nasa stolonifera, commonly known as the silver hair veldtgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray leaves that are covered in a dense layer of hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant grows in a clump form and produces small, pale blue or purple flowers during the warmer months. It is often found in open grasslands and is adapted to dry, well-drained soils.

Nasa stolonifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and visual interest.
