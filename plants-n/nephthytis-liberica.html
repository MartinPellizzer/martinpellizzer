Nephthytis liberica

Nephthytis liberica, commonly known as the African Mask Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to western Africa.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by black, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Nephthytis liberica is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
