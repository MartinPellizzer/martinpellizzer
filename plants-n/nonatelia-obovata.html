Nonatelia obovata

Nonatelia obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, where it thrives in dry, temperate climates.

Nonatelia obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
