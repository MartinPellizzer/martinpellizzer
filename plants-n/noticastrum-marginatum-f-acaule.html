Noticastrum marginatum f. acaule

Noticastrum marginatum f. acaule, commonly known as the leafy aculeate acacia, is a shrub characterized by its distinctive botanical features.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to certain regions of South America. The plant is notable for its compound leaves with a serrated margin and small, aculeate structures along the leaflets. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters.

The species is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
