Notocactus linkii var. flavispinus

Notocactus linkii var. flavispinus, commonly known as the yellow-spined cactus, is a succulent native to South America, specifically Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green with yellow spines, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique features, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of cacti.
