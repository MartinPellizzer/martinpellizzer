Nerine corusca

Nerine corusca, commonly known as the Persian glory flower, is a bulbous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

Nerine corusca is a perennial plant that blooms in late summer to early autumn.
