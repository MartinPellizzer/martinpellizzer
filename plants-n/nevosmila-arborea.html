Nevosmila arborea

Nevosmila arborea, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a deciduous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits. Nevosmila arborea thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
