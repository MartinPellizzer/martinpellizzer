Niphobolus percussus var. salicifolius

Niphobolus percussus var. salicifolius, commonly known as the willowleaf niphobolus, is a variant of the niphobolus genus native to certain regions of Asia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often appearing in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.
