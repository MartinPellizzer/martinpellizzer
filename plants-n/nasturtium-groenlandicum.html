Nasturtium groenlandicum

Nasturtium groenlandicum, commonly known as the Greenland nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to Greenland and parts of the Arctic.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers with four petals, and its leaves are edible, often used in salads or as a garnish.
