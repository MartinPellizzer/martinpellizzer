Necepsia afzelii

Necepsia afzelii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Necepsia afzelii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
