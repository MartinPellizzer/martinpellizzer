Nidularium Citrinum

Citrine nidularium, scientifically known as Nidularium citrinum, is a unique and striking member of the Bromeliaceae family. Native to the humid rainforests of South America, this plant is prized for its vibrant, golden-orange foliage that resembles crystalline structures. Its unusual appearance has made it a popular choice among collectors and indoor gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to thrive, including bright indirect light and consistent moisture. With its eye-catching colors and exotic look, Citrine nidularium adds a touch of tropical elegance to any space.
