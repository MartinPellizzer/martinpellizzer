Nylandtia

Nylandtia, commonly known as the African violets, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Gesneriaceae.

These plants are native to tropical regions of Africa and are characterized by their vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. Nylandtia species typically have heart-shaped leaves and produce small, clustered flowers that bloom throughout the year under proper care. They are popular as ornamental plants due to their aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.

However, they are not as widely cultivated as the more common African violets (Saintpaulia), which belong to a different genus.
