Nasturtium globosum

Nasturtium globosum, commonly known as the globe flower, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Tropaeolaceae and is characterized by its showy, globe-shaped flowers that come in shades of yellow and orange. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, rounded seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Nasturtium globosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
