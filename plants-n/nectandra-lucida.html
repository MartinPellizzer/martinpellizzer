Nectandra lucida

Nectandra lucida, commonly known as the bright nectandra, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers. Its fruit is a drupe, typically containing a single seed surrounded by a fleshy pulp.

Nectandra lucida is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
