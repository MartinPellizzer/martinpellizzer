Notocactus bueneckeri

Notocactus bueneckeri, commonly known as the Buecker's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in a layer of white, waxy bloom. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
