Nasturtium patens

Nasturtium patens, commonly known as the spreading nasturtium, is an annual flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features heart-shaped, edible leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in disturbed areas or along garden edges.

Nasturtium patens is popular in ornamental gardening and is also valued for its edible parts.
