Najas arguta var. podostemon

Najas arguta var. podostemon, commonly known as the water starwort, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater habitats in North America.

It belongs to the family Najadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, whorled leaves. The plant produces tiny, greenish flowers that are typically found in clusters near the water surface. It thrives in slow-moving to still waters with soft, muddy substrates.

Due to its ability to tolerate a range of water conditions, it is often found in ponds, lakes, and along the edges of rivers.
