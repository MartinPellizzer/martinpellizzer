Notocactus rubrigemmatus

Notocactus rubrigemmatus, commonly known as the red-gemmed cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the summer months, which are borne in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are reduced to small spines, and it thrives in full sun with well-draining soil.

Notocactus rubrigemmatus is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
