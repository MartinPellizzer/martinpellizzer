Neoporteria curvispina var. grandiflora

Neoporteria curvispina var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered neoporteria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features a woody stem with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and collectors.
