Nerine rosea

Nerine rosea, commonly known as the rose belladonna, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is known for its ability to bloom in late summer to early autumn, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Nerine rosea is relatively low maintenance and can be grown in containers or in garden beds, adding a dramatic visual impact.
