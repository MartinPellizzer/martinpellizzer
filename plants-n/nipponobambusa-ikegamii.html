Nipponobambusa ikegamii

Nipponobambusa ikegamii, commonly known as the Ikegami bamboo, is a species native to Japan.

It is a clumping bamboo that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and covered in a fine, silky texture. Its culms are sturdy and often have a pale yellowish hue when young.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
