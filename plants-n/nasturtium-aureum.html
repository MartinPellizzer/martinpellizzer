Nasturtium aureum

Nasturtium aureum, commonly known as the golden nasturtium, is a vibrant flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Tropaeolaceae and is characterized by its bright orange to yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is known for its edible flowers and leaves, which have a peppery taste similar to watercress. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Nasturtium aureum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and repel certain garden pests.
