Nesaea icosandra

Nesaea icosandra, commonly known as the mountain pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and clusters of white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its foliage is dense and evergreen, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
