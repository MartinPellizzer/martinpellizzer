Nunnezharia robusta

Nunnezharia robusta, commonly known as the robust nunnezharia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
