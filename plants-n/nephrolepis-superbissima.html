Nephrolepis superbissima

Nephrolepis superbissima, commonly known as the golden sword fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender fronds that are typically bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense mats. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, ovate pinnae that are often slightly curled.

Nephrolepis superbissima thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
