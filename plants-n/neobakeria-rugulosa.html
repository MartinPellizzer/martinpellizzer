Neobakeria rugulosa

Neobakeria rugulosa, commonly known as the roughleaf neobakeria, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and yellowish to brownish flower heads. Native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its unique morphology makes it an interesting subject for botanical study and ecological research.

The plant's adaptability to harsh environments highlights its resilience and potential for use in xeriscaping.
