Nabalus

Nabalus, commonly known as the common yarrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers. Nabalus species are typically perennial herbs that thrive in temperate climates and well-drained soils. They are often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed areas due to their adaptability.

The plant is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
