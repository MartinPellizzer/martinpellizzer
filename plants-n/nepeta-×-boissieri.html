Nepeta × boissieri

Nepeta × boissieri, commonly known as catmint, is a hybrid plant in the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its aromatic, fuzzy leaves and clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and as a source of nectar for pollinators.

Nepeta × boissieri is also known for its calming scent, which is attractive to cats.
