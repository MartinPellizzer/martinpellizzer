Nolina orbicularis

It is native to the arid regions of southern Africa, particularly Namibia and Botswana. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on a tall, slender stem, which bloom in late summer.

Nolina orbicularis is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping in dry climates.
