Neolepisorus tenuipes

Neolepisorus tenuipes, commonly known as the slender lepisorus, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. This plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are wiry and creeping, allowing it to spread slowly over time.

Neolepisorus tenuipes is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in humid, indoor settings.
