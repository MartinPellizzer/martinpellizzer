Neomammillaria rettigiana

Neomammillaria rettigiana, commonly known as the Rettigiana mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has small, rounded tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance.

Neomammillaria rettigiana is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
