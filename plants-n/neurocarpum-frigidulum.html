Neurocarpum frigidulum

Neurocarpum frigidulum, commonly known as the Arctic neurocarpum, is a rare flowering plant native to the high Arctic regions of northern Canada and Greenland.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with a short stem and tightly packed, dark green leaves. The plant produces tiny, white flowers that bloom in late summer, adapted to the short growing season in its extreme environment.
