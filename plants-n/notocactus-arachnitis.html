Notocactus arachnitis

Notocactus arachnitis, commonly known as the spider cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its unique, spiny, and often contorted stems that resemble the shape of a spider. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its distinctive appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
