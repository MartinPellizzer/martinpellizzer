Neomammillaria mendeliana

Neomammillaria mendeliana, commonly known as the Mendel's nipple cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive rib structure and prominent areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a slow growth rate and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and compact size make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
