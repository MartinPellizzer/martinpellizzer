Nierembergia petunioides

Nierembergia petunioides, commonly known as the common bluebell, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant produces clusters of small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are heart-shaped and arranged alternately along the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade conditions.
