Nananthus aloides

Nananthus aloides, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a rounded, shrub-like growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
