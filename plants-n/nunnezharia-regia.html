Nunnezharia regia

Nunnezharia regia, commonly known as the royal nunnezharia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep pink to violet. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often reaching several inches in length.

Nunnezharia regia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
