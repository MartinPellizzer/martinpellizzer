Neanthe elegans

Neanthe elegans, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and vibrant, spadix-like flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. Its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements have made it a popular choice for homeowners and offices.

Neanthe elegans is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, provided they are well-draining.
