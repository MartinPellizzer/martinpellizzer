Nerine brachystemon

Nerine brachystemon, commonly known as the broad-leafed nerine, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive, recurved shape. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that are broad and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Nerine brachystemon is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
