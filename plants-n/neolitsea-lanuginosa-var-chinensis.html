Neolitsea lanuginosa var. chinensis

Neolitsea lanuginosa var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese silkworm tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It features ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically hairy on the underside, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by elliptical, winged fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
