Nuttallia malviflora

Nuttallia malviflora, commonly known as the yellow mallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often in rocky soils, and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Nuttallia malviflora is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
