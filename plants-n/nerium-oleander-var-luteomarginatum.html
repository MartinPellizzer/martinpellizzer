Nerium oleander var. luteomarginatum

Nerium oleander var. luteomarginatum, commonly known as the golden-margin oleander, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range from pink to red, with distinctive yellow margins on the petals. The plant produces opposite, simple, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is also noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand drought conditions.
