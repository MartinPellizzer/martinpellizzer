Nesaea pedicellata

Nesaea pedicellata, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and oval, with a hairy texture.
