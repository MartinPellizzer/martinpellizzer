Nicipe monophylla

Nicipe monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf nictitate, is a rare and unique plant species characterized by its singular, large, waxy leaf.

It is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's leaf is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties. Its botanical structure is adapted to conserve moisture, making it resilient in its natural habitat.

Due to its rarity, it is considered a conservation concern and is often protected in its native ecosystems.
