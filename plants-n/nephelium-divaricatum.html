Nephelium divaricatum

Nephelium divaricatum, commonly known as rambutan, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, spiny fruits that resemble a hairy ball. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a glossy surface.
