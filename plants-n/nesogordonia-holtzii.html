Nesogordonia holtzii

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and bipinnate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, Nesogordonia holtzii is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
