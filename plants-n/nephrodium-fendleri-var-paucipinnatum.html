Nephrodium fendleri var. paucipinnatum

Nephrodium fendleri var. paucipinnatum, commonly known as the narrow-pinnate nephrophyte, is a fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, compound leaves that are typically arranged in a pinnate pattern. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in humid forests or near water bodies. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a smooth texture, with small, rounded pinnae.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in botanical gardens.
