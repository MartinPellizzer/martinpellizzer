Nephroia

Nephroia, commonly known as the kidney fern, is a type of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and thrives in humid climates. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
