Nonea decumbens

Nonea decumbens, commonly known as the decumbent nonea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.
