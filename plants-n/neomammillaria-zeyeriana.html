Neomammillaria zeyeriana

Neomammillaria zeyeriana, commonly known as the Zeyer's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in white or pale green tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
