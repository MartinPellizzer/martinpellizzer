Nesodaphne

Nesodaphne, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
