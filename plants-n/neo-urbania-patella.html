Neo-urbania patella

Neo-urbania patella, commonly known as the cityscape fern, is a unique plant species adapted to urban environments.

It thrives in areas with high pollution and limited soil, showcasing remarkable resilience. Its fronds are narrow and elongated, often growing close to the ground to avoid harsh weather conditions. The plant has a shallow root system that allows it to anchor itself in cracks and crevices.

Botanically, it is classified as a fern, belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.
