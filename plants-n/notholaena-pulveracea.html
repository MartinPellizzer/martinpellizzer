Notholaena pulveracea

Notholaena pulveracea, commonly known as the powderbush, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

Notholaena pulveracea is valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
