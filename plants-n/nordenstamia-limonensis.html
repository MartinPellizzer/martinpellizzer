Nordenstamia limonensis

Nordenstamia limonensis, commonly known as the lemon tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Rutaceae family.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with dark green, leathery leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or yellow, followed by edible citrus fruits. Native to regions with a warm, Mediterranean climate, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and economically valuable for its fruit production.
