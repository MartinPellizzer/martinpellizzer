Necepsia afzelii subsp. afzelii

Necepsia afzelii subsp.

afzelii, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa. It features rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture.

The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and indoor settings.
