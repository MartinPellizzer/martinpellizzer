Nicotiana arentsii

Nicotiana arentsii, commonly known as the Arents tobacco, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to the southern United States, particularly Texas and Louisiana. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It has hairy, alternate leaves and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and historical use, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
