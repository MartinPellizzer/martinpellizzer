Neillia hypomalaca

Neillia hypomalaca, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a striking silvery underside, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, orange-colored fruits. It thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Neillia hypomalaca is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
