Neomarica altivallis

Neomarica altivallis, commonly known as the tall orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Neomarica altivallis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
