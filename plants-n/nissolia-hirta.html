Nissolia hirta

Nissolia hirta, commonly known as the hairy nissolia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Nissolia hirta is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
