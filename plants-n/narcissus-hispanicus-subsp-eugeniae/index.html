Narcissus Hispanicus Subsp. Eugeniae

Narcissus hispanicus subsp. eugeniae is a rare and elegant bulbous plant native to southern Europe. It is known for its striking white flowers with a yellow center, reminiscent of the classic daffodil. This subspecies thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens. Its historical significance in Mediterranean horticulture adds to its allure among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
