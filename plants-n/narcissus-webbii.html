Narcissus webbii

Narcissus webbii, commonly known as the Webb's daffodil, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, particularly found in rocky and mountainous regions. Narcissus webbii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.
