Notocactus darilhoensis

Notocactus darilhoensis, commonly known as the Darilho's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
