Noisettia frangulifolia

Noisettia frangulifolia, commonly known as the European hornbeam, is a deciduous tree native to Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and small, oval leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by edible, winged seeds called samaras.

Noisettia frangulifolia is often used in ornamental gardening and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
