Notocactus arechavaletae var. alacriportanus

Notocactus arechavaletae var. alacriportanus, commonly known as the Alacriportan Notocactus, is a rare cactus species native to specific regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and a distinctive rib structure that aids in water storage. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
