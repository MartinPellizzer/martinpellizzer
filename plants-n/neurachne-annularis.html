Neurachne annularis

Neurachne annularis, commonly known as the ringed grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves that often have a distinct ring-like structure at the base. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or brown in color. Neurachne annularis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodlands.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and forage for various wildlife species.
