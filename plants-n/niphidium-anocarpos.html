Niphidium anocarpos

Niphidium anocarpos, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by fluffy, seed-bearing structures similar to those of dandelions.
