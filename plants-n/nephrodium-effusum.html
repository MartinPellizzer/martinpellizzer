Nephrodium effusum

Nephrodium effusum, commonly known as the creeping nephrolepis, is a fern native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with long, creeping stems that root at the nodes. The fronds are pinnate, with lanceolate pinnae that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant has a distinctive, elegant appearance and is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities.
