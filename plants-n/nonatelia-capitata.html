Nonatelia capitata

Nonatelia capitata, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
