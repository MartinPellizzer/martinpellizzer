Nitraria Schoberi Var. Sibirica

Nitraria schoberi var. sibirica is a hardy shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia. It thrives in harsh environments with limited water and extreme temperature fluctuations. This plant is known for its small, rounded leaves and yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitat. Due to its resilience, it has been studied for potential use in restoration projects in degraded landscapes.
