Nephrodium longissimum

Nephrodium longissimum, commonly known as the long-stemmed nephroid, is a unique vascular plant characterized by its elongated, ribbon-like leaves.

It belongs to the family Nephrodiaceae and is native to specific regions with moist, shaded environments. The plant exhibits a distinct morphology with a creeping rhizome and lanceolate foliage. Its reproductive structures are simple, consisting of small, clustered sporangia.

Nephrodium longissimum is of interest to botanists due to its primitive features and ecological significance in its habitat.
