Nephrodium giatrasi

Nephrodium giatrasi, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a unique member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered in sori, a feature common among ferns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both horticultural and botanical collections.
