Notocactus megapotamicus var. flavispinus

Notocactus megapotamicus var. flavispinus, commonly known as the golden spined notocactus, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its green to blue-green stems and bright yellow spines. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is popular among cactus enthusiasts.
