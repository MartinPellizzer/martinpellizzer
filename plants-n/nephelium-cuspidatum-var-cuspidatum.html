Nephelium cuspidatum var. cuspidatum

Nephelium cuspidatum var. cuspidatum, commonly known as the mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The fruit is a striking purple or red, segmented berry that contains a white, sweet and tangy pulp. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil and requires regular watering.

It is valued not only for its edible fruit but also for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
