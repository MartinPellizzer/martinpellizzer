Notocactus sellowii

Notocactus sellowii, commonly known as the Sellow's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant features areoles covered with white, woolly hair, which help reduce water loss in its arid habitat. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, opening in the morning and lasting only a few days.

Notocactus sellowii is a slow-growing plant that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.
