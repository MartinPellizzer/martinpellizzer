Nunnezharia membranacea

Nunnezharia membranacea, commonly known as the paperleaf fern, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery leaves that are often translucent and arranged in a fan-like pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
