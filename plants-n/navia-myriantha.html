Navia myriantha

Navia myriantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf or false nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive velvety texture, often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender stalks. Navia myriantha thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
