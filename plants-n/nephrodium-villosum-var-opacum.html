Nephrodium villosum var. opacum

Nephrodium villosum var. opacum, commonly known as the opacous nephroid, is a rare fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy fronds that give it a velvety texture. The plant features compound leaves with a distinctive, shield-shaped structure, which is a key identifying feature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
