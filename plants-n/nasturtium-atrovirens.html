Nasturtium atrovirens

Nasturtium atrovirens, commonly known as blackleaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves and vibrant orange to red flowers. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. Its flowers are edible and have a peppery taste, often used in culinary applications.

Nasturtium atrovirens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
