Neomammillaria nejapensis var. elegans

Neomammillaria nejapensis var. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Ghost Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular to cylindrical stem with a smooth, bluish-green surface that becomes more reddish under direct sunlight. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise subtle appearance. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique coloration and form make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
