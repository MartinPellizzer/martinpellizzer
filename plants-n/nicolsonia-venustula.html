Nicolsonia venustula

Nicolsonia venustula, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often pale green or yellow with intricate patterns, attracting specific pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
