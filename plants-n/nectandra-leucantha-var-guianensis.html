Nectandra leucantha var. guianensis

Nectandra leucantha var. guianensis, commonly known as the Guianese white nectandra, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America, particularly found in Guyana and northern Brazil.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by clustered berries.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
