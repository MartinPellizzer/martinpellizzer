Neotinea tridentata

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are often divided into three distinct lobes. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Neotinea tridentata plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and small vertebrates.
