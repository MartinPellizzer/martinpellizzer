Nerium oleander var. atropurpureum

Nerium oleander var. atropurpureum, commonly known as the purple oleander, is a striking ornamental shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range from deep purple to reddish hues, blooming in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

However, it is important to note that all parts of the plant are toxic and should be handled with care.
