Nephrodium galanderi

Nephrodium galanderi, commonly known as the Galander's kidney fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and have a velvety texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Nephrodium galanderi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
