Navia lanigera

Navia lanigera, commonly known as the woolly navia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring. Navia lanigera thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.

Its unique appearance and ecological adaptations make it a notable species in its native habitat.
