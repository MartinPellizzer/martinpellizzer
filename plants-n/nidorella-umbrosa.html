Nidorella umbrosa

Nidorella umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow orchid, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the dense, shaded forests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in low light conditions. The plant's leaves are typically broad and dark green, adapted to maximize light absorption in its dim habitat. Nidorella umbrosa is known for its unique ability to thrive in understory environments where other plants struggle.
