Notylia lyrata var. major

Notylia lyrata var. major, commonly known as the broad-leaved notylia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of eastern North America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically 5 to 10 inches in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal remedies due to their medicinal properties.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as along stream banks and in wetlands.
