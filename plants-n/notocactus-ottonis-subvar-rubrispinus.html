Notocactus ottonis subvar. rubrispinus

Notocactus ottonis subvar. rubrispinus, commonly known as the red-spined notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. Its spines are reddish in color, which distinguishes it from other varieties of Notocactus.

It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
