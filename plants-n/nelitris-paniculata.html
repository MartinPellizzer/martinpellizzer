Nelitris paniculata

Nelitris paniculata, commonly known as the panicle grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and panicle-like inflorescences that bear small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive, feathery appearance.

Nelitris paniculata is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various insect species.
