Notocactus scopa f. daenikerianus

Notocactus scopa f. daenikerianus, commonly known as the Daenikerianus cactus, is a rare and striking variant of the Notocactus scopa species.

It is characterized by its short, columnar stems and dense, woolly spines that give it a distinctive, almost fluffy appearance. The plant produces vibrant, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. Native to Mexico, it thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is a sought-after specimen among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
