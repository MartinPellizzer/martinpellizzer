Nauclea leichhardtii

Nauclea leichhardtii, commonly known as the Leichhardt's nauclea, is a flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, arid environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elliptical, woody pods. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Nauclea leichhardtii is known for its adaptability to harsh conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
