Neomammillaria bella

Neomammillaria bella, commonly known as the bell mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short stem and a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a striking contrast to its gray-green body. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Neomammillaria bella is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cactus collections due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
