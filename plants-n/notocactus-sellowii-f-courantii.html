Notocactus sellowii f. courantii

Notocactus sellowii f. courantii, commonly known as the Sellow's notocactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Brazil.

It features a short, cylindrical stem that often branches into multiple segments, giving it a bushy appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
