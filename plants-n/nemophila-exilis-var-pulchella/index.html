Nemophila Exilis Var. Pulchella

Nemophila exilis var. pulchella is a delicate flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California. It is known for its small, sky-blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. This variant of Nemophila thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its unique coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice for woodland gardens. The plant plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance.
