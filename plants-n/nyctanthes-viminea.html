Nyctanthes viminea

Nyctanthes viminea, commonly known as the night-flowering jasmine or Indian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom at night. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

Nyctanthes viminea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
