Nassauvia pumila var. nordenskjoldii

Nassauvia pumila var. nordenskjoldii, commonly known as the Arctic sand verbena, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It typically features prostrate or ascending stems covered in fine, silky hairs. The leaves are linear to lanceolate, often with a bluish-gray hue due to dense pubescence. It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or purple, which bloom in late summer.
