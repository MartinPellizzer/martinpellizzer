Nigritella rhellicani f. fulva

Nigritella rhellicani f. fulva, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a unique variant of the species Nigritella rhellicani.

This plant is characterized by its striking, bright yellow to orange inflorescences that contrast sharply with its dark green foliage. It is a terrestrial orchid native to specific regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters and bloom during the warmer months of the year.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts but remains relatively rare in the wild.
