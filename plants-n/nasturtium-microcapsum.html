Nasturtium microcapsum

Nasturtium microcapsum, commonly known as the dwarf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, edible flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant produces small, round, peppery-tasting seeds that are often used in culinary applications. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly tangy, mustard-like flavor.

Nasturtium microcapsum thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental and edible plants.
