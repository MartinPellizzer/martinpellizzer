Notopleura discolor

Notopleura discolor, commonly known as the white-flowered pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and forested areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a distinctive glandular surface.
