Narcissus × spofforthiae

Narcissus × spofforthiae, commonly known as the Spofforth daffodil, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between N. tazetta and N. poeticus.

It is characterized by its large, showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and is native to the British Isles. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens and natural landscapes.
