Notocactus linkii var. buenekeri

Notocactus linkii var. buenekeri, commonly known as the "Buenekeri" cactus, is a columnar cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a tall, slender stem with a smooth, green surface that may develop a slight bluish tint. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to hot, dry climates and requires minimal water once established.

Its slow growth rate makes it an excellent choice for rock gardens or as a container plant.
