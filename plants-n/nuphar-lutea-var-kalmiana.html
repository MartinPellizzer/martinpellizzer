Nuphar lutea var. kalmiana

Nuphar lutea var. kalmiana, commonly known as the yellow pond lily, is a aquatic plant native to North America.

It features broad, floating leaves that are typically yellowish-green in color. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its roots are rhizomatous, allowing it to spread vegetatively in wetland environments.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various water organisms.
