Neomarica speciosa

Neomarica speciosa, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink with intricate patterns. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are long, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture.

Neomarica speciosa thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
