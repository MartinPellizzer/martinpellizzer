Nanodes discolor

Nanodes discolor, commonly known as the white sand veldt, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white powder.

Nanodes discolor is adapted to arid environments and is often found in open grasslands and coastal areas.
