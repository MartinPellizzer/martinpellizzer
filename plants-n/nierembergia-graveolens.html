Nierembergia graveolens

Nierembergia graveolens, commonly known as the Sweet Lantana, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
