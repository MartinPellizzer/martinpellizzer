Notocactus haselbergii

Notocactus haselbergii, commonly known as the Haselberg's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and spiny, ribbed structure, which helps it conserve water in arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for desert gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
