Nephrodium refractum

Nephrodium refractum, commonly known as the refractive nephroid, is a rare and unique plant species characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped leaves.

The leaves are typically a deep green, with a waxy surface that aids in water retention in arid environments. This plant is known for its ability to refract light through its translucent leaf structures, creating an optical effect that makes it visually striking. It thrives in shaded, moist habitats and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its limited distribution and unique botanical features, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
