Narcissus × fenzii

Narcissus × fenzii, commonly known as the Fenzii daffodil, is a hybrid species within the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces multiple blooms per stem. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are grassy in appearance.

This variety is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
