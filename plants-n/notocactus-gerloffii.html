Notocactus gerloffii

Notocactus gerloffii, commonly known as the Gerloff's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to northern South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers during the summer months, which are clustered at the top of the stem. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments.

Notocactus gerloffii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, dry climates.
