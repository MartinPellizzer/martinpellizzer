Nasturtium tentaculatum, commonly known as the slender nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Tropaeolum and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces small, edible leaves and flowers that are often used in culinary applications. Its bright orange or red blossoms attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
