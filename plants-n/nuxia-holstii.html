Nuxia holstii

Nuxia holstii, commonly known as the Holst's nuxia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is commonly referred to as the mesembryanthemum family. The plant features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. Its small, star-shaped flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in spring.

Nuxia holstii thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
