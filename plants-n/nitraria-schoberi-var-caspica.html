Nitraria schoberi var. caspica

Nitraria schoberi var. caspica, commonly known as the Caspian nitraria, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including high salinity and extreme temperatures. Its fruit is a dry, dehiscent capsule that contains several seeds.

Nitraria schoberi var. caspica plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
