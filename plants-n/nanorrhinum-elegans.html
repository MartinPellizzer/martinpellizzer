Nanorrhinum elegans

Nanorrhinum elegans, commonly known as the elegant sandmat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, succulent perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in xeriscapes.
