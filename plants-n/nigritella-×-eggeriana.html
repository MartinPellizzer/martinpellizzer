Nigritella × eggeriana

Nigritella × eggeriana, commonly known as the hybrid hoya, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing plant with thick, fleshy stems and waxy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

Nigritella × eggeriana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
