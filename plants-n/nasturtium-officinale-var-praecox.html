Nasturtium officinale var. praecox

Nasturtium officinale var. praecox, commonly known as early nasturtium, is a variant of the common nasturtium that blooms earlier in the growing season.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces edible leaves and seeds, often used in culinary applications for their peppery flavor. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental and edible plants.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract beneficial insects make it a valuable addition to ecological gardens.
