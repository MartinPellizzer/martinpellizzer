Neoregelia princeps var. phyllanthoidea

Neoregelia princeps var. phyllanthoidea, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, elongated leaves that are a deep green with a waxy sheen, often displaying a subtle pinkish or red hue at the base. The plant produces showy, long-lasting inflorescences with bright red bracts that contrast beautifully with its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor settings.
