Neocinnamomum huongsonensis

Neocinnamomum huongsonensis, commonly known as Vietnamese cinnamon or Huong Son cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue berries. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, spicy aroma similar to cinnamon.

This species is valued for its essential oils and is often used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
