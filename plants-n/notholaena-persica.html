Notholaena persica

Notholaena persica, commonly known as Persian mountain grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Iran and Afghanistan. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Notholaena persica is often found in rocky or well-drained soils at high altitudes.
