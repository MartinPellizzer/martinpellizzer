Niobe fortunei

Niobe fortunei, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Niobe fortunei is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is sometimes considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
