Niota lucida

Niota lucida, commonly known as the shining niota, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly fuzzy texture.

Niota lucida is typically found in moist, sandy soils along streambanks and in open woodlands.
