Nymphanthus chinensis

Nymphanthus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble lanterns, which are typically orange to red in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture with a dark green upper surface.

Nymphanthus chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
