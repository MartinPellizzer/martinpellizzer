Notocactus langsdorfii var. longispinus

Notocactus langsdorfii var. longispinus, commonly known as the long-spined notocactus, is a species of cactus native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered with spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the base of the stem. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and indoor gardeners.
