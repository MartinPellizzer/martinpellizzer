Nidularium gracile

Nidularium gracile, commonly known as the slender nest orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers, which are usually white or pale pink, bloom in late spring or early summer.

Nidularium gracile is noted for its unique inflorescence structure, which resembles a nest, giving it its common name.
