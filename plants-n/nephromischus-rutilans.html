Nephromischus rutilans

Nephromischus rutilans, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a strikingly colorful orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of rainforests. Its flowers are small but numerous, arranged in dense clusters that add to its visual appeal.

Nephromischus rutilans is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
