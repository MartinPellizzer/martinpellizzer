Nototriche glabra

Nototriche glabra, commonly known as the smooth nototriche, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are smooth and often covered in a fine, whitish bloom, which helps reduce water loss.

The flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in clusters, making it a valuable plant for pollinators.
