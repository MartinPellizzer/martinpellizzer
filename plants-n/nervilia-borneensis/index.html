Nervilia Borneensis

Nervilia borneensis is a rare and unique fern native to the rainforests of Borneo. This plant is known for its striking appearance and delicate, feathery fronds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and faces threats from deforestation. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable species for future generations.
