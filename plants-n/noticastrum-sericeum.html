Noticastrum sericeum

Noticastrum sericeum, commonly known as silverleaf acacia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and small, feathery leaves that give it a silvery appearance. The plant produces yellow or pale green flowers arranged in compact inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to harsh conditions.

Noticastrum sericeum is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
