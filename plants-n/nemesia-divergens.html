Nemesia divergens

Nemesia divergens, commonly known as the common nemesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The flowers are typically bright yellow or orange, with a distinctive central boss of stamens. Nemesia divergens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
