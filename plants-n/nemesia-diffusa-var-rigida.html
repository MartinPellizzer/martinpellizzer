Nemesia diffusa var. rigida

Nemesia diffusa var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often appearing in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact form and long blooming period make it a desirable ornamental plant.
