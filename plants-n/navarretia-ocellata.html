Navarretia ocellata

Navarretia ocellata, commonly known as the starry navarretia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

Navarretia ocellata is often found in areas with well-drained soils and is adapted to seasonal rainfall patterns.
