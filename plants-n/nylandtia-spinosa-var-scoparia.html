Nylandtia spinosa var. scoparia

Nylandtia spinosa var. scoparia, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Its latex sap is milky and can be used for various traditional purposes.
