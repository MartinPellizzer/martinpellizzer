Nepeta Buschii

Nepeta buschii is a striking member of the mint family known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its unique appearance and aromatic qualities make it a popular choice for gardens and herbal collections. Often referred to as catnip in some regions, it holds a special place in both traditional medicine and wildlife habitats. This resilient plant offers beauty, fragrance, and ecological value, making it a fascinating subject for botanists and gardeners alike.
