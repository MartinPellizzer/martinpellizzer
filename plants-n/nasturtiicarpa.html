Nasturtiicarpa

Nasturtiicarpa, commonly known as watercress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are often submerged or partially submerged in water. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by tiny, round, edible fruits. Nasturtiicarpa thrives in moist environments such as streams, ponds, and damp soil.

It is valued for its peppery flavor and nutritional content, making it a popular ingredient in salads and soups.
