Nectandra sericea

Nectandra sericea, commonly known as silverwood or silky nectandra, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by oval-shaped, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Nectandra sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
