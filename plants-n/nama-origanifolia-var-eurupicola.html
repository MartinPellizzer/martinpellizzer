Nama origanifolia var. eurupicola

Nama origanifolia var. eurupicola, commonly known as the narrow-leafed savory, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its essential oils contribute to its use in culinary and medicinal applications.
