Neoregelia rubrifolia

Neoregelia rubrifolia, commonly known as the red-leafed bromeliad, is a striking ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, red-tinged foliage that contrasts beautifully with its green leaves. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water in a central cup, which is typical of many bromeliads. Neoregelia rubrifolia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its lush, colorful appearance.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardens and tropical landscapes.
