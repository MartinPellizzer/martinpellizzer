Neillia sparsiflora

Neillia sparsiflora, commonly known as the sparsely flowering neillia, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is a small, shrub-like plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a fragrant aroma.
