Nummularia thyrsiflora

Nummularia thyrsiflora, commonly known as the mountain nummularia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features small, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are clustered in dense, branched inflorescences, producing showy yellow blooms.

Nummularia thyrsiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
