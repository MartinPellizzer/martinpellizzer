Najas welwitschii

Najas welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's water plantain, is a species of aquatic plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in freshwater habitats such as lakes and rivers, often submerged in shallow waters. The plant has long, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface, while its roots anchor it to the substrate. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color.

Najas welwitschii is an important part of aquatic ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
