Norta strictissima

Norta strictissima, commonly known as the rigid norta, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of East Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that are densely covered with small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers during the late spring to early summer season. Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.

Norta strictissima thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade in its natural habitat.
