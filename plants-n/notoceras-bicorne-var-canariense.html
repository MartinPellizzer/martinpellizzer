Notoceras bicorne var. canariense

Notoceras bicorne var. canariense, commonly known as the Canary Island horned orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, curved, horn-like structures on its leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh, arid conditions. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, with a unique structure that aids in pollination by specific insect species.
