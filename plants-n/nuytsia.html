Nuytsia

Nuytsia, commonly known as the waratah, is a striking plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy floral displays. The flowers are typically red or orange, with a distinctive, cone-shaped structure. The plant has a woody stem and produces leaves that are usually narrow and leathery.

Nuytsia is well adapted to the dry, sandy soils of its native habitat.
