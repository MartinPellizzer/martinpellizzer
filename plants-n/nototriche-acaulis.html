Nototriche acaulis

Nototriche acaulis, commonly known as the hairless nototriche, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Floristic Region of South Africa.

It is a small, succulent shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features simple, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in dense inflorescences, and are typically pale blue or purple in color.

Nototriche acaulis is adapted to survive in arid conditions, with its succulent stems storing water to help it endure dry periods.
