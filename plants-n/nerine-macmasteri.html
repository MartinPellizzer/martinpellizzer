Nerine macmasteri

Nerine macmasteri, commonly known as the MacMaster's nerine, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that remain throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to gardens during that time.
