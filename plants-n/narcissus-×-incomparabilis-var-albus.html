Narcissus × incomparabilis var. albus

Narcissus × incomparabilis var. albus, commonly known as the White Crowned Trumpet.

This variety is a hybrid of the genus Narcissus, characterized by its striking white petals and a prominent, elongated yellow corona. It typically blooms in early spring, producing fragrant flowers that are highly valued in horticulture. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and floral arrangement purposes.
