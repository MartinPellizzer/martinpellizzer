Noticastrum album

Noticastrum album, commonly known as white nickerbean, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, white flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. Noticastrum album is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in soil enrichment.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a resilient plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
