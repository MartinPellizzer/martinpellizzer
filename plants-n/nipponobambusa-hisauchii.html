Nipponobambusa hisauchii

Nipponobambusa hisauchii, commonly known as the Hisauchii bamboo, is a species native to Japan.

It is a clumping bamboo that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves that are often a deep green color. Its culms are stiff and have a smooth, pale surface.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
