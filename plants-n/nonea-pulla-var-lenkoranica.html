Nonea pulla var. lenkoranica

Nonea pulla var. lenkoranica, commonly known as the Lenkoran narcissus, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a perennial herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species in its native environment.
