Nymphaea × khurooi

Nymphaea × khurooi, commonly known as the Khuroo water lily, is a hybrid aquatic plant belonging to the Nymphaeaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. Native to the wetlands of South Asia, this species thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments. Its roots are anchored in the substrate, while its stems and leaves remain buoyant on the water's surface.

The plant plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen to water organisms.
