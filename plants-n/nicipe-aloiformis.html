Nicipe aloiformis

Nicipe aloiformis, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spiral-shaped leaves that form a compact, rosette-like structure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are succulent and can store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

Nicipe aloiformis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unusual and visually appealing foliage.
