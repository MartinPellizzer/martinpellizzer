Nasa schlimiana

Nasa schlimiana, commonly known as the silver moonflower, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking white, star-shaped flowers that open at night and emit a sweet fragrance. The plant has silvery-white leaves covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Nasa schlimiana is also valued for its ability to attract nocturnal pollinators such as moths.
