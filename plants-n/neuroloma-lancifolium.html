Neuroloma lancifolium

Neuroloma lancifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf neuroloma, is a herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Its root system is fibrous and contributes to its adaptability in various soil types.

Neuroloma lancifolium is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
