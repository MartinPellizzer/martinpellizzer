Navia linearis

Navia linearis, commonly known as the slender navia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Navia linearis is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to gardens with low water requirements.
