Nepeta fedtschenkoi

Nepeta fedtschenkoi, commonly known as catmint, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features aromatic, gray-green leaves and produces clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. The plant is known for its strong mint-like scent, which is particularly appealing to cats. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Nepeta fedtschenkoi is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to attract pollinators.
