Nephthytis poissonii var. constricta

Nephthytis poissonii var. constricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf philodendron, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Caribbean.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive narrow, elongated form. The plant has a vining growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous white flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
