Nerine bowdenii

Nerine bowdenii, commonly known as the African lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Nerine bowdenii is known for its ability to bloom reliably in late summer and early autumn.
