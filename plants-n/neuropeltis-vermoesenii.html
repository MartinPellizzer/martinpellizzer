Neuropeltis vermoesenii

Neuropeltis vermoesenii, commonly known as the Zambian lily, is a striking herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value.
