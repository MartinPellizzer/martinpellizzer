Nematanthus serpens

Nematanthus serpens, commonly known as the snake's tail plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. Nematanthus serpens thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
