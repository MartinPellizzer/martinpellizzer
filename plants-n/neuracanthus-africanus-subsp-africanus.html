Neuracanthus africanus subsp. africanus

Neuracanthus africanus subsp.

africanus, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It has a compact growth habit with fleshy, strap-like leaves that help it conserve water.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
