Nerine × cami

Nerine × cami, commonly known as the hybrid nerine, is a bulbous perennial plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant produces clusters of flowers on tall, sturdy stems, often blooming in late summer to early autumn. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are broad and grass-like, providing a lush green backdrop.

Nerine × cami is well-suited for rock gardens or containers, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun.
