Notocactus tenuispinus

Notocactus tenuispinus, commonly known as the narrow-spined notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in short, sharp spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
