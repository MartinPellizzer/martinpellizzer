Notisia intonsa

Notisia intonsa, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a tall, slender tree fern native to New Zealand.

It has a tall, unbranched trunk covered in a thick, fibrous outer layer. Its fronds are large, feather-like, and can grow up to 5 meters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in native forests.

It plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter and food for various species.
