Nerine ridleyi

Nerine ridleyi, commonly known as the red nerine, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in summer. The plant features strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, Nerine ridleyi is widely cultivated in gardens around the world.
