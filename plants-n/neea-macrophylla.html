Neea macrophylla

Neea macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf neea, is a perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that can reach up to 20 cm in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage.

Neea macrophylla thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
