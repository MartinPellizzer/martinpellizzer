Nemesia versicolor var. versicolor

Nemesia versicolor var. versicolor, commonly known as the common nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its extended blooming period, from spring to early autumn, adds continuous color to gardens.
