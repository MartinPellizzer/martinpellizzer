Notocactus spinibarbis

Notocactus spinibarbis, commonly known as the spiny-barbed notocactus, is a unique cactus species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in sharp, spiny tubercles, which provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its distinct morphology and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
