Navarretia nigelliformis var. radians

Navarretia nigelliformis var. radians, commonly known as the radiate navarretia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The species is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats such as grasslands and chaparral. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The plant is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in coastal regions with mild, wet winters.
