Nasturtium officinale var. parviflorum

Nasturtium officinale var. parviflorum, commonly known as the dwarf nasturtium, is a compact variety of the common nasturtium.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and vibrant, edible flowers that come in shades of orange and red. This plant is native to South America and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental and for its edible parts, including leaves and seeds.

The variety is well-suited for container gardening and can be used to deter pests in vegetable gardens.
