Neolaria

Neolaria, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

These plants are typically found in coastal areas and are adapted to saline environments. Neolaria species are characterized by their spiny, leaf-like structures and showy yellow or orange flowers. They play an important role in stabilizing sandy shores and providing habitat for marine life.
