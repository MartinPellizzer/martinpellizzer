Narcissus poeticus var. radiiflorus

Narcissus poeticus var. radiiflorus, commonly known as the radiate daffodil, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a bright yellow corona and white petals that radiate outward. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm, with strap-like leaves that are a deep green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring bloom.
