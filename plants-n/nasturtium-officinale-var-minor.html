Nasturtium officinale var. minor

Nasturtium officinale var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf nasturtium, is a compact variety of the common nasturtium.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and belongs to the family Brassicaceae. The plant features bright orange or red flowers with heart-shaped petals and edible leaves and seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its culinary uses, as both the leaves and flowers are often used in salads and herbal remedies.
