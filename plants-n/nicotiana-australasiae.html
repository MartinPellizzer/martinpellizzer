Nicotiana australasiae

Nicotiana australasiae, commonly known as the Australian tobacco plant, is a species native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are broad and oval-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic foliage and ornamental value.
