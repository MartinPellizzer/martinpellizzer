Narcissus bulbocodium f. pallidus

Narcissus bulbocodium f. pallidus, commonly known as the pale narcissus, is a rare and delicate flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces a single, narrow, upright stem bearing a cluster of flowers at the top. Its bulbs are small and white, with a fibrous outer layer, typical of the narcissus genus.
