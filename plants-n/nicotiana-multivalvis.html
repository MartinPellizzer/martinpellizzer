Nicotiana multivalvis

Nicotiana multivalvis, commonly known as the multivalved tobacco, is a species native to South America.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are broad and often have a waxy coating.

The plant is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers.
