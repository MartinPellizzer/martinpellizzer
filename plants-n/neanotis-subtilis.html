Neanotis subtilis

Neanotis subtilis, commonly known as the slender neanotis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Due to its delicate appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
