Niota

Niota, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It features compound leaves that are alternate and pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces clusters of yellow, fluffy flowers in the spring, which resemble small clouds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Niota is also valued for its fast growth and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
