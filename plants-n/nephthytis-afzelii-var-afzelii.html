Nephthytis afzelii var. afzelii

Nephthytis afzelii var. afzelii, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous spadices surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This ornamental plant is popular as a houseplant due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
