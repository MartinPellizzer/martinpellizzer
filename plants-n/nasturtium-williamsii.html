Nasturtium williamsii

Nasturtium williamsii, commonly known as the yellow wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers. The plant is part of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. Its foliage and flowers are edible, often used in salads or as a garnish.

Nasturtium williamsii thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
