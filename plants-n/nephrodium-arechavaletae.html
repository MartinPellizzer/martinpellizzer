Nephrodium arechavaletae

Nephrodium arechavaletae, commonly known as the Arechavaleta's nephroid, is a rare and unique fern species native to specific regions of South America.

The plant exhibits a complex structure with a short, stout rhizome and delicate, branching fronds that emerge from the base.
