Nephrodium venustum

Nephrodium venustum, commonly known as the golden kidney fern, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Asia. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, arching fronds that can reach up to 1 meter in length. Nephrodium venustum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in botanical gardens and indoor spaces.
