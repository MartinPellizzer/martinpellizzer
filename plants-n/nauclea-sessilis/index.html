Nauclea Sessilis

Nauclea sessilis is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia. It is known for its vibrant yellow or orange blooms that appear during the warmer months. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish.
