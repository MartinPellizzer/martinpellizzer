Noccaea Rubescens Subsp. Culminicola

Noccaea rubescens subsp. culminicola is a rare alpine plant native to high mountain regions. It thrives in harsh environments with low temperatures and limited soil nutrients. This subspecies is known for its distinctive red stems and small, clustered leaves. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered vulnerable to climate change and habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique and ecologically important species.
