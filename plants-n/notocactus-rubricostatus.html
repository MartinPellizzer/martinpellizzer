Notocactus rubricostatus

Notocactus rubricostatus, commonly known as the red-costed notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green with reddish-brown stripes, giving it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
