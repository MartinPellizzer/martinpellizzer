Nephthytis bintuluensis

Nephthytis bintuluensis, commonly known as the Bintulu arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy surface. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in swampy areas. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value.

Nephthytis bintuluensis is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to improve water quality in aquatic habitats.
