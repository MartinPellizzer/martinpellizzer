Nematoceras longipetalum

Nematoceras longipetalum, commonly known as the long-petaled orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, showy petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

The botanical features of Nematoceras longipetalum make it a notable species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
