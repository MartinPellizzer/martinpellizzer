Nicipe comptum

Nicipe comptum, commonly known as the slender nitch plant, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of South America.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in the spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Nicipe comptum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
