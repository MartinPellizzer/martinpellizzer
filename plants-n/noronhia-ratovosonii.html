Noronhia ratovosonii

Noronhia ratovosonii, commonly known as the Madagascar starfish plant, is a unique member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the island of Madagascar and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that resemble the arms of a starfish. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and thrives in full sunlight. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Noronhia ratovosonii is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its distinctive appearance.
