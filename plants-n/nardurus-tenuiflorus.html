Nardurus tenuiflorus

Nardurus tenuiflorus, commonly known as the slender-flowered nardus, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family. This perennial herb is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in dense clusters. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
