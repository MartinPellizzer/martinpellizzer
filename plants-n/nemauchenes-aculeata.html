Nemauchenes aculeata

Nemauchenes aculeata, commonly known as the spiny pincushion cactus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines and small, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to survive in dry environments with minimal water requirements.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
