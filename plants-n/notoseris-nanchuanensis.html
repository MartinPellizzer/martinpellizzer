Notoseris nanchuanensis

Notoseris nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan dandelion, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
