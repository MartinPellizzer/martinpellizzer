Nemesia zimbabwensis

Nemesia zimbabwensis, commonly known as the Zambian nemesia, is a flowering plant native to Zimbabwe.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
