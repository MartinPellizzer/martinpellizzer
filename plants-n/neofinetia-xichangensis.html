Neofinetia xichangensis

Neofinetia xichangensis, commonly known as the Xichang fir, is a species of conifer native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and needle-like leaves that are typically dark green. The tree produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and requires moderate moisture.

Neofinetia xichangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
