Notocactus ottonis var. multiflorus

Notocactus ottonis var. multiflorus, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a unique cactus species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its spherical, ribbed stem that turns a vibrant yellow when mature. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. Its thick, fleshy stems are adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant.

It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
