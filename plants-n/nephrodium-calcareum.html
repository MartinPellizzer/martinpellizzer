Nephrodium calcareum

Nephrodium calcareum, commonly known as the limestone spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern that thrives in calcareous soils.

Nephrodium calcareum is known for its ability to tolerate alkaline conditions, making it a common sight in limestone areas.
