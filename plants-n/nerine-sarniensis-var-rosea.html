Nerine sarniensis var. rosea

Nerine sarniensis var. rosea, commonly known as the pink nerine, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep red, often with a contrasting purple or red throat. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that are dark green and glossy. It typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
