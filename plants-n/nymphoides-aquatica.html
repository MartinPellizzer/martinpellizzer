Nymphoides aquatica

Nymphoides aquatica, commonly known as water lily, is a aquatic plant native to Asia.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide shade and oxygen to the water. Its flowers are typically white or pink, with a fragrant scent that attracts pollinators. The plant has rhizomes that anchor it to the bottom of ponds and lakes.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by supporting biodiversity and improving water quality.
