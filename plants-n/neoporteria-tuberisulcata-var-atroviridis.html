Neoporteria tuberisulcata var. atroviridis

Neoporteria tuberisulcata var. atroviridis, commonly known as the dark green tuberous orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive tuberous roots and lance-shaped leaves with a dark green hue. The plant produces striking orchid-like flowers with yellow petals and a prominent purple lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
