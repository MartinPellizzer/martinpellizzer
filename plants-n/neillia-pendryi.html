Neillia pendryi

Neillia pendryi, commonly known as the pendry's neillia, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, hanging inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture, while its flowers are small and clustered.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Neillia pendryi is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
