Nezera compacta

Nezera compacta, commonly known as the compact nezera, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant, tubular blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Nezera compacta thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
