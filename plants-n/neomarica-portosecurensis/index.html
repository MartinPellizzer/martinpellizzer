Neomarica Portosecurensis

Neomarica portosecurensis is a rare and intriguing orchid species discovered in the remote cloud forests of Ecuador. This plant is notable for its striking purple and white blooms that appear during the wet season. It thrives in high humidity and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and faces threats from deforestation. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical wonder.
