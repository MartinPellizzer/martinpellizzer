Neocinnamomum

Neocinnamomum, commonly known as the Chinese cinnamon or Chinese camphor laurel, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Lauraceae.

These plants are native to East Asia, particularly China, Japan, and Korea, and are characterized by their aromatic leaves and bark. Neocinnamomum species are often cultivated for their ornamental value and their ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
