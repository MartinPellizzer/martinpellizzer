Nasturtium palustre var. dasycarpum

Nasturtium palustre var. dasycarpum, commonly known as swamp nasturtium, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly waxy texture and produces bright orange to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in moist, poorly drained soils and is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its flowers have a peppery taste, making it edible and sometimes used in culinary applications.

The plant also serves as a food source for various pollinators and aquatic insects.
