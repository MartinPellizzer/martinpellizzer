Neckia humilis

Neckia humilis, commonly known as the lowland neckia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a slightly pubescent surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and often display a range of colors from white to pink.

Neckia humilis is known for its adaptability to moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
