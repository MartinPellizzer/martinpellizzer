Notholaena aurantiaca

Notholaena aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange leaved fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red fronds, which contrast strikingly with its dark green stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its fronds are pinnately divided with a smooth, leathery texture.

Notholaena aurantiaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
