Neuroplatyceros grandis

Neuroplatyceros grandis, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has pinnate leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.
