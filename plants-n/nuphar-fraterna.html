Nuphar fraterna

Nuphar fraterna, commonly known as the lesser water lily, is a aquatic plant native to North America.

It features broad, floating leaves that are typically a bright green color with a waxy surface. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its roots are rhizomatous, allowing it to spread vegetatively in still or slow-moving water.

Nuphar fraterna plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various waterfowl and invertebrates.
