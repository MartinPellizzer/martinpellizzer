Neolitsea Konishii

Neolitsea konishii is a rare and unique plant species native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados, and is known for its fragrant foliage. This evergreen shrub or small tree is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
