Neotrinia splendens

Neotrinia splendens, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers that resemble stars. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its roots are often covered in a dense, white mycorrhizal network.

Neotrinia splendens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
