Notocactus arechavaletae var. buenekeri

Notocactus arechavaletae var. buenekeri, commonly known as the Buenekeri Notocactus, is a rare cactus species native to specific regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically ribbed and covered in a layer of white wax. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are often borne on long, slender pedicels. Its succulent nature allows it to store water efficiently, making it well adapted to arid environments.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by collectors and botanists alike.
