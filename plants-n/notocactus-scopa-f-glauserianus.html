Notocactus scopa f. glauserianus

Notocactus scopa f. glauserianus, commonly known as the Glauser's globose cactus, is a unique variant of the Notocactus scopa species.

It is characterized by its rounded, globular shape and dense covering of short, white spines. The plant is native to Mexico and thrives in arid environments with well-draining soil. It produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
