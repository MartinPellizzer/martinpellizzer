Neanotis hirsuta

Neanotis hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy neanotis, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in dense inflorescences, and are typically white or pale yellow in color.

Neanotis hirsuta is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in dry, coastal or inland regions.
