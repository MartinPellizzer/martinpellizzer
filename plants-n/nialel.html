Nialel

Nialel, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Nialel is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to bloom year-round under proper care.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among indoor gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
