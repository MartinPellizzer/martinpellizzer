Nasturtium suffruticosum

Nasturtium suffruticosum, commonly known as creeping nasturtium, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves and bright orange or yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit, making it suitable for ground cover or hanging baskets. Its flowers and young leaves are edible, often used in salads or as a garnish.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its ornamental appeal in gardens.
