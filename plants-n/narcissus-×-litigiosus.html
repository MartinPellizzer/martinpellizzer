Narcissus × litigiosus

Narcissus × litigiosus, commonly known as the white narcissus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and pleasant fragrance.
