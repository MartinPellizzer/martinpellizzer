Neocheiropteris chinensis

Neocheiropteris chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to East Asia.

It thrives in freshwater environments such as ponds, streams, and marshes. The plant has delicate, floating fronds that are typically divided into small, lance-shaped segments. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Neocheiropteris chinensis plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen.
