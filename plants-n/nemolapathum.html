Nemolapathum

Nemolapathum, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, Nemolapathum is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
