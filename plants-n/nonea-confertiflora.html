Nonea confertiflora

Nonea confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered Nonea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice among gardeners in temperate climates.
