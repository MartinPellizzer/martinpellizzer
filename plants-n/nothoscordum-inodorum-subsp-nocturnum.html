Nothoscordum inodorum subsp. nocturnum

Nothoscordum inodorum subsp.

nocturnum, commonly known as the night garlic, is a perennial herb native to the southern United States. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom at night.
