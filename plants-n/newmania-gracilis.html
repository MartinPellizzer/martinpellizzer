Newmania gracilis

Newmania gracilis, commonly known as the slender newmania, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in terminal panicles, and are usually pale green to white in color.

Newmania gracilis is often found in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
