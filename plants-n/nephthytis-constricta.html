Nephthytis constricta

Nephthytis constricta, commonly known as the ZZ plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and broad, dark green, glossy leaves that are often marked with lighter veins. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a tendency to grow in a compact, bushy form.

Nephthytis constricta is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements.
