Narcissus Bulbocodium Var. Bicolor

Narcissus bulbocodium var. bicolor is a rare and striking plant known for its unique two-colored flowers. This variety of daffodil is native to southern Europe and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Its petals display a bold contrast between yellow and white, making it a standout in any garden. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical significance in horticulture. Despite its beauty, it remains a less common species among modern gardeners.
