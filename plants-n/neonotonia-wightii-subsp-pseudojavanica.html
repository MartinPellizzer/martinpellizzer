Neonotonia wightii subsp. pseudojavanica

Neonotonia wightii subsp.

pseudojavanica, commonly known as the Javanese orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is a herbaceous plant with a fibrous root system and typically grows in moist, shaded environments.

The plant produces striking, orchid-like flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette formation.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
