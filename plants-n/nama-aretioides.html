Nama aretioides

Nama aretioides, commonly known as the red-flowered lily, is a striking plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and showy, red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Nama aretioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and unique appearance.
