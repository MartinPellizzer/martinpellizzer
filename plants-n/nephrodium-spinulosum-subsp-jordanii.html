Nephrodium spinulosum subsp. jordanii

Nephrodium spinulosum subsp.

jordanii, commonly known as the Jordan's hairy fern, is a rare fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures that are covered in fine, spinose hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories. Its leaves are bipinnate, with a complex arrangement of small, linear leaflets.
