Notocactus spegazzinii var. nanus

Notocactus spegazzinii var. nanus, commonly known as the miniature notocactus, is a compact cactus species native to Argentina.

It features a short, cylindrical stem that is often ribbed and covered in white or pale green spines. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
