Nama floribunda

Nama floribunda, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a shrub-like growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Nama floribunda is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
