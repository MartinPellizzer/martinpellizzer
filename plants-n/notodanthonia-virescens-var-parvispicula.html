Notodanthonia virescens var. parvispicula

Notodanthonia virescens var. parvispicula, commonly known as the dwarf spear grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are usually pale green to yellowish in hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or heathlands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense mats in suitable conditions.
