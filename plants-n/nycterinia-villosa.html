Nycterinia villosa

Nycterinia villosa, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Nycterinia villosa is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
