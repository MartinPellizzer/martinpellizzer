Nerine peersii

Nerine peersii, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant produces sword-like leaves that are broad and strap-shaped, often emerging from a cluster of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Nerine peersii is known for its showy blooms that appear in late summer to early autumn.
