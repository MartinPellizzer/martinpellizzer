Nasturtium oleraceum

Nasturtium oleraceum, commonly known as the edible nasturtium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Tropaeolaceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant, edible flowers and heart-shaped, slightly hairy leaves. The plant produces small, round, orange or yellow fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its culinary uses, as both the leaves and flowers are edible and often used in salads and garnishes.
