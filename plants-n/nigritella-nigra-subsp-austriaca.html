Nigritella nigra subsp. austriaca

Nigritella nigra subsp.

austriaca, commonly known as the Austrian black nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its dark purple to black berries and hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture and often bear a waxy coating.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and has been historically used in traditional herbal medicine.
