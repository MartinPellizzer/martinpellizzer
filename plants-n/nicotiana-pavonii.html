Nicotiana pavonii

Nicotiana pavonii, commonly known as the pink tobacco plant, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to the Andes Mountains of South America and is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features hairy stems and broad, ovate leaves. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their vibrant color and strong fragrance.

Nicotiana pavonii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and unique botanical features.
