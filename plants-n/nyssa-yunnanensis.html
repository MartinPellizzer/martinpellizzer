Nyssa yunnanensis

Nyssa yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan dogwood, is a deciduous tree native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the family Cornaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and small mammals.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
