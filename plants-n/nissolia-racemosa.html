Nissolia racemosa

Nissolia racemosa, commonly known as the clustered nissolia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, racemose inflorescences composed of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are ovate with a smooth surface and a slightly hairy underside.

Nissolia racemosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
