Narcissus × christianssenii

Narcissus × christianssenii, commonly known as the Christensens narcissus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to yellow. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with full to partial sunlight. It is native to southern Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This species is valued for its early blooming habit and is commonly found in gardens and horticultural collections.
