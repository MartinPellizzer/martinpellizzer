Neooreophilus pholeter

Neooreophilus pholeter, commonly known as the African orchid bee, is a species of bee native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is notable for its unique morphology, featuring a striking black body with yellow markings and a long, slender abdomen. The plant it interacts with, particularly certain orchids, plays a crucial role in its pollination behavior. These bees are important pollinators of specific orchid species, contributing to the reproductive success of these plants.

However, their role in ecosystem dynamics remains an area of ongoing botanical study.
