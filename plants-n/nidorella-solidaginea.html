Nidorella solidaginea

Nidorella solidaginea, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant orange and yellow flowers that resemble flames, making it a visually captivating plant. The plant has a slender, erect stem with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Nidorella solidaginea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
