Neobaronia xiphoclada

Neobaronia xiphoclada, commonly known as the swordleaf baronia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in the spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a valuable addition to botanical collections.
