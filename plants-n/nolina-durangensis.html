It is native to the arid regions of northern Mexico, particularly in the state of Durango. This plant is characterized by its rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. Nolina durangensis is well adapted to dry climates and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
