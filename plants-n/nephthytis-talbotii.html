Nephthytis talbotii

Nephthytis talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial that features rosettes of narrow, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soil with moderate watering.

Nephthytis talbotii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive foliage.
