Nageia flagelliformis

Nageia flagelliformis, commonly known as the giant fan palm, is a unique plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its fruit is a fibrous, spiky capsule that contains edible seeds.

Nageia flagelliformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
