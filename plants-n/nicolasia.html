Nicolasia

Nicolasia, commonly known as the golden bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Nicolasia species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are often cultivated in gardens for their vibrant blooms.
