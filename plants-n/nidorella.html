Nidorella

Nidorella, commonly known as the orchid vine, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and the presence of showy, often fragrant flowers. The plants typically have slender stems and are found in tropical and subtropical regions. Nidorella species produce small, greenish or yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes.

These plants are valued in horticulture for their ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
