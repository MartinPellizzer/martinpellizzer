Nierembergia calycina

Nierembergia calycina, commonly known as the velvetleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.
