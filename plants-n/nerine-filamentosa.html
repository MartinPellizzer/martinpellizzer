Nerine filamentosa

Nerine filamentosa, commonly known as the slender nerine, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

Nerine filamentosa is a hardy perennial that blooms in late summer to early autumn.
