Nasturtium asperum subsp. laevigatum

Nasturtium asperum subsp.

laevigatum, commonly known as the smooth nasturtium, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of orange and yellow.

The plant is characterized by its slightly rough, hairy stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

The flowers are also edible, adding a peppery flavor to salads and culinary dishes.
