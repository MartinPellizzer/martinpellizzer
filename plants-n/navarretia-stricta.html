Navarretia stricta

Navarretia stricta, commonly known as the slender navarretia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, delicate flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its blooming period occurs in spring, producing clusters of white or pale pink flowers.
