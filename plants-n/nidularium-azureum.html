Nidularium azureum

Nidularium azureum, commonly known as the Azure Nest Plant, is a rare and striking member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue-green rosette of fleshy, overlapping leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking violet to blue flowers that emerge from the leaf rosette, adding to its ornamental appeal. Native to the cloud forests of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized greenhouses or botanical gardens.
