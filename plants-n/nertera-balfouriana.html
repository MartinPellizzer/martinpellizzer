Nertera balfouriana

Nertera balfouriana, commonly known as the silver velvet plant, is a compact, trailing succulent native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically a silvery-gray to bluish-green in color, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its spreading growth habit.

Nertera balfouriana requires bright, indirect light and moderate watering to thrive.
