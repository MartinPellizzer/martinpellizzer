Neomammillaria standleyi

Neomammillaria standleyi, commonly known as the Standley's milk barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a globular shape and a greenish-gray color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a shallow root system and is adapted to arid environments.

Neomammillaria standleyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
