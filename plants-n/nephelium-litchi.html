Nephelium litchi

Nephelium litchi, commonly known as the lychee, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern China.

It produces small, rough-skinned fruits with a sweet, fragrant pulp that is rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant has a shallow root system and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and dark green, while its flowers are fragrant and white.

The lychee tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruit.
