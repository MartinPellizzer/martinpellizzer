Nania angustifolia

Nania angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nania, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky soils and dry, sunny environments. The plant features slender, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and range in color from pale yellow to reddish-orange.

Nania angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a border plant.
