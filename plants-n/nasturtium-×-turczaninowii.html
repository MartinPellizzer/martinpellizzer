Nasturtium × turczaninowii

Nasturtium × turczaninowii, commonly known as the hybrid nasturtium, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is a cross between *Tropaeolum majus* and *Tropaeolum tricolor* and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces edible leaves and seeds, which are often used in culinary applications. It is an annual herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
