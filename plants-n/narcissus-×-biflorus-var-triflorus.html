Narcissus × biflorus var. triflorus

Narcissus × biflorus var. triflorus, commonly known as the triple-flowered daffodil, is a hybrid plant that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is known for its striking, three-rowed petals that give it a distinctive appearance. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
