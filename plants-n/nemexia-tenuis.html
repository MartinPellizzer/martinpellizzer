Nemexia tenuis

Nemexia tenuis, commonly known as the slender nemexia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, delicate white or pale blue flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Nemexia tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
