Neoregelia kautskyi

Neoregelia kautskyi, commonly known as the Kautsky's bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It features vibrant, colorful inflorescences that can range from red to orange, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has rosette-shaped leaves that collect water, creating a mini-ecosystem supporting small aquatic life. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Neoregelia kautskyi is valued for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in indoor environments with proper care.
