Notopleura capacifolia

Notopleura capacifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved notopleura, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. Notopleura capacifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological studies and horticultural use.
