Nigella oxypetala subsp. latisecta

Nigella oxypetala subsp.

latisecta, commonly known as the broad-leaved love-in-a-mist, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of delicate, blue to purple flowers.

The leaves are broad and deeply lobed, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
