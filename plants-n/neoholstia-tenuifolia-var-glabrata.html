Neoholstia tenuifolia var. glabrata

Neoholstia tenuifolia var. glabrata, commonly known as the slender leafed neoholstia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically glabrous, meaning they lack hair. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
