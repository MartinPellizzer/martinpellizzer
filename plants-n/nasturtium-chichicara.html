Nasturtium chichicara

Nasturtium chichicara, commonly known as Mexican watercress, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and bright orange or yellow flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in wetlands. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery, tangy flavor, similar to watercress.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
