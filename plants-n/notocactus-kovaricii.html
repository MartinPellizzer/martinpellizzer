Notocactus kovaricii

Notocactus kovaricii, commonly known as the Kovarik's notocactus, is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem and clusters of vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a unique ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems, allowing it to thrive in drought conditions. Its spines are short and dense, providing protection from herbivores.

Due to its rarity and horticultural appeal, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts in controlled environments.
