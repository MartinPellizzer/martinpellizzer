Neuontobotrys amplexicaulis

Neuontobotrys amplexicaulis, commonly known as the clasping stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique growth habit, with its leaves clasping the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, intricate flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with reddish or brownish markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many parts of its range.
