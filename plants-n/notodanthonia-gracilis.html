Notodanthonia gracilis

Notodanthonia gracilis, commonly known as the slender feather grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in late spring to early summer, which are arranged in spikelets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands.

Notodanthonia gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
