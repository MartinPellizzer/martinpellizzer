Nymphoides lungtanensis

Nymphoides lungtanensis, commonly known as the Lungtan water plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its floating leaves that are oval-shaped and have a waxy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
