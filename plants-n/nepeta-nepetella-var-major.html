Nepeta nepetella var. major

Nepeta nepetella var. major, commonly known as catmint, is a flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant features aromatic, gray-green leaves and produces spikes of blue or purple flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Nepeta nepetella var. major is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and as a source of nectar for pollinators.
