Nidularium campos-portoi var. campos-portoi

Nidularium campos-portoi var. campos-portoi, commonly known as the Campos-Portoi bromeliad, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a terrestrial bromeliad characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central water-holding cavity. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and waxy, with a bluish-green coloration.

It produces small, white flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette, adding to its ornamental value.
