Nigritella bicolor var. rubida

Nigritella bicolor var. rubida, commonly known as the red-flowered nigritella, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals that contrast with its dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often clustered in whorls along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
