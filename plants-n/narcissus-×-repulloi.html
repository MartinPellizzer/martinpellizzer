Narcissus × repulloi

Narcissus × repulloi, commonly known as the Giant Crowned Trumpet.

It is a hybrid perennial bulbous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. The plant features large, showy flowers with a distinctive crown-like pattern on the tepals. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, making it one of the tallest daffodil species.

Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are narrow and grass-like in appearance.
