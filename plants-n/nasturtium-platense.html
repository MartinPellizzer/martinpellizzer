Nasturtium platense

Nasturtium platense, commonly known as the watercress nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and bright orange or yellow flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments, often found near streams or in wetlands. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery taste similar to watercress.

Nasturtium platense is also known for its ability to purify water and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
