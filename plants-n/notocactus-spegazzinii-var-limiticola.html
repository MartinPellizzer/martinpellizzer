Notocactus spegazzinii var. limiticola

Notocactus spegazzinii var. limiticola, commonly known as the rock cactus or stone cactus, is a unique species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It prefers well-draining soil and minimal watering, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
