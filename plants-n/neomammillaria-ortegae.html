Neomammillaria ortegae

Neomammillaria ortegae, commonly known as the Ortega's milk barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rounded, ribbed stems that often develop a white or pale green color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing cactus that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in the world of succulents.
