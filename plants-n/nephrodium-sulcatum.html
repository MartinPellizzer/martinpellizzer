Nephrodium sulcatum

Nephrodium sulcatum, commonly known as the Sulcate Nephroid, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped leaves.

The leaves are typically dark green and have a textured surface with deep grooves, giving them a rugged appearance. Its reproductive structures are simple, consisting of small, clustered flowers that produce capsule fruits.

Nephrodium sulcatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
