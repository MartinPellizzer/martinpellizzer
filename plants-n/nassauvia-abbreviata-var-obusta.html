Nassauvia abbreviata var. obusta

Nassauvia abbreviata var. obusta, commonly known as the stiffstemmed nassauvia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
