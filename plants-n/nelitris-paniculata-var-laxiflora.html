Nelitris paniculata var. laxiflora

Nelitris paniculata var. laxiflora, commonly known as the broad-flowered panicled grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and panicles of small, clustered flowers.
