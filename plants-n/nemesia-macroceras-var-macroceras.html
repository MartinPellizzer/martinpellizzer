Nemesia macroceras var. macroceras

Nemesia macroceras var. macroceras, commonly known as the large-fruited nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. This species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its blooms are long-lasting, often appearing from spring through early autumn.
