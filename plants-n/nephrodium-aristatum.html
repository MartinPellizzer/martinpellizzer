Nephrodium aristatum

Nephrodium aristatum, commonly known as the arrowhead fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves, which are typically bright green and have a waxy coating. The plant thrives in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense colonies.

Nephrodium aristatum plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small organisms and contributing to water filtration.
