Nectandra regnellii

Nectandra regnellii, commonly known as the yellow sand-wood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown wood and simple, alternate leaves. Due to its slow growth and limited distribution, it is considered a valuable but endangered species in its native habitat.
