Nissolia arborea

Nissolia arborea, commonly known as the African ebony, is a tropical tree native to Central and Eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood and can grow up to 30 meters tall. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic in shape and have a smooth surface. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, flat seeds.
