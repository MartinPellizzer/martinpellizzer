Nephelium fumatum

Nephelium fumatum, commonly known as the smoke apple, is a species of flowering plant in the Sapindaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and Malaysia, and thrives in tropical climates. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture.

The tree can grow up to 10 meters tall and is valued both for its fruit and ornamental qualities.
