Nezera albiflora

Nezera albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered nezera, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals and slender, branching stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Nezera albiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
