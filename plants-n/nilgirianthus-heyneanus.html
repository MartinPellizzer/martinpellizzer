Nilgirianthus heyneanus

Nilgirianthus heyneanus, commonly known as the Nilgiri bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and is often found in forest understories. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, edible fruits.

Nilgirianthus heyneanus is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
