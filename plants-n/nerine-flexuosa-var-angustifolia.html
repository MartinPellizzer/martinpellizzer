Nerine flexuosa var. angustifolia

Nerine flexuosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved glory of the snow, is a striking bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, and it produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its blooming period occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a dramatic touch to the garden with its elegant floral display.
