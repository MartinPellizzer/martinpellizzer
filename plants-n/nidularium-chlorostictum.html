Nidularium chlorostictum

Nidularium chlorostictum, commonly known as the green-stain bromeliad, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect water. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often hidden beneath the foliage.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to support aquatic life in its leaf axils.
