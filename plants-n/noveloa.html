Noveloa

Noveloa, commonly known as the Mexican daisy, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals surrounding a yellow center, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits called achenes. Noveloa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its cultivation in both ornamental and ecological gardens.
