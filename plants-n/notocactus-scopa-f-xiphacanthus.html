Notocactus scopa f. xiphacanthus

Notocactus scopa f. xiphacanthus, commonly known as the Xiphacanthus notocactus, is a unique hybrid of the genus Notocactus.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its slow growth rate and specific care requirements, it is a prized addition to succulent collections.
