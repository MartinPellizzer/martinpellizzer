Narcissus × Biflorus Var. Albus

Albus narcissus, scientifically known as Narcissus × biflorus var. albus, is a strikingly white variant of the daffodil family. This plant is prized for its elegant, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its delicate appearance and long-lasting blooms add a touch of sophistication to any setting. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure optimal growth and flowering.
