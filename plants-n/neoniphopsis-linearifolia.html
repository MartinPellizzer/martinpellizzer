Neoniphopsis linearifolia

Neoniphopsis linearifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, delicate flowers that often display vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture from the air. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
