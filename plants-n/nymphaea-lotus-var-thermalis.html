Nymphaea lotus var. thermalis

Nymphaea lotus var. thermalis, commonly known as the thermal water lily, is a rare aquatic plant native to geothermal regions.

It thrives in warm, mineral-rich waters, often found near hot springs. The plant features broad, floating leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the nutrient-dense thermal waters.

This variety is prized for its unique habitat and ornamental value in specialized aquatic gardens.
