Neoporteria Paucicostata

Sword fern, scientifically known as Neoporteria paucicostata, is a striking evergreen plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. With its lush, lance-shaped fronds that resemble a sword, it adds a dramatic touch to any garden or indoor space. This fern thrives in humid, shaded environments and is prized for its hardy nature and elegant appearance. Its ability to adapt to various soil types makes it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. Whether in a tropical greenhouse or a shaded backyard, the Sword fern remains a symbol of resilience and natural beauty.
