Nymphaea odorata var. caroliniana-perfecta

Nymphaea odorata var. caroliniana-perfecta, commonly known as the Carolina rose pink water lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white, often with a subtle rose hue, and blooms throughout the summer months. The plant has broad, floating leaves that provide habitat for various aquatic organisms. Its root system anchors it in shallow water, while its rhizomes spread horizontally to form dense colonies.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
