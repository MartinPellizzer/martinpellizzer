Nephrodium kunzeanum

Nephrodium kunzeanum, commonly known as the Kunze's nephroid, is a rare and ancient fern-like plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Nephrodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, woolly hairs. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in dense rainforest undergrowth. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat destruction, Nephrodium kunzeanum is considered endangered and is protected in several national parks and reserves.
