Nenax hirta subsp. hirta

Nenax hirta subsp.

hirta, commonly known as the hairy nenax, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central America and the Caribbean. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical environments, often growing in shaded, moist habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
