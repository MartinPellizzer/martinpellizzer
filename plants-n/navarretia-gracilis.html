Navarretia gracilis

Navarretia gracilis, commonly known as the slender navarretia, is a flowering plant native to western North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring. Navarretia gracilis is also known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and its role in supporting local ecosystems.
