Neomammillaria ritteriana var. quadricentralis

Neomammillaria ritteriana var. quadricentralis, commonly known as the quadrifurcated mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem with a woolly covering that becomes sparser with age. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
