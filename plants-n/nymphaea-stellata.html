Nymphaea stellata

Nymphaea stellata, commonly known as the star water lily, is a aquatic plant native to the Americas.

It features showy, white flowers with yellow stamens that resemble a star, giving it its common name. The plant has broad, floating leaves that support its vibrant blooms. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Nymphaea stellata plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
