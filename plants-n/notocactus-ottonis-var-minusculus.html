Notocactus ottonis var. minusculus

Notocactus ottonis var. minusculus, commonly known as the miniature ottonis cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
