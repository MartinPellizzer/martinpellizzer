Neonotonia wightii var. mearnsii

Neonotonia wightii var. mearnsii, commonly known as the Mearns' hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
