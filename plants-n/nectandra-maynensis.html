Nectandra maynensis

Nectandra maynensis, commonly known as the Mayan sand-wood, is a tropical tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood and glossy, elliptical leaves. It produces small, white flowers that are followed by spherical, yellowish fruits.

Nectandra maynensis is often found in lowland and mountainous regions, thriving in humid, well-drained soils.
