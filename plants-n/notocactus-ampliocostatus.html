Notocactus ampliocostatus

Notocactus ampliocostatus, commonly known as the broad-costed cactus, is a species native to southern Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant features prominent, rib-like structures that are covered in white, woolly spines. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late winter to early spring.

Notocactus ampliocostatus thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for arid garden environments.
