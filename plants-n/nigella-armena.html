Nigella armena

Nigella armena, commonly known as Armenian cowpea, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, ovoid seeds that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Nigella armena is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical medicinal uses.
