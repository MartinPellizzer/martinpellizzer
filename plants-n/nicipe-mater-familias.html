Nicipe mater-familias

Nicipe mater-familias, commonly known as the sacred mother plant, is a rare and ancient botanical species native to the highlands of the Andes.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.
