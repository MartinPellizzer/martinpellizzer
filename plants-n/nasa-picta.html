Nasa picta

Nasa picta, commonly known as the painted nasa, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are usually broad and waxy, adapting to the harsh climate of its native habitat.

Nasa picta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
