Notocactus langsdorfii f. prestlei

Notocactus langsdorfii f. prestlei, commonly known as the Prestle's notocactus, is a rare and distinctive cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem and dense clusters of yellow flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant has a unique rib structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It requires well-draining soil and full sun to thrive.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
