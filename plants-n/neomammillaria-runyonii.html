Neomammillaria runyonii

Neomammillaria runyonii, commonly known as the Runyon's mamillaria, is a species of cactus native to the Sonoran Desert in the United States and Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short, thick stem and a dense covering of white to grayish spines. The plant typically produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to arid environments.

Neomammillaria runyonii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
