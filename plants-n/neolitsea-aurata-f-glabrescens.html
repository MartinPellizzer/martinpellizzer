Neolitsea aurata f. glabrescens

Neolitsea aurata f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leafed golden flame tree, is a variant of the Neolitsea aurata species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, smooth leaves that are typically a vibrant yellow-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving off a sweet, citrus-like scent. It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and parks for its attractive foliage and floral display.
