Nephelaphyllum aureum

Nephelaphyllum aureum, commonly known as the golden mist plant, is a unique member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, golden-yellow flowers.

Nephelaphyllum aureum is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to add texture to shaded garden spaces.
