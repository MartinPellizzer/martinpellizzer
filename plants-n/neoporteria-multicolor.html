Neoporteria multicolor

Neoporteria multicolor, commonly known as the multicolored neoporteria, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multi-colored bracts that range from red to orange and yellow, creating a visually appealing display. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its compact growth habit and ornamental value. Its flowers are small and clustered, often hidden beneath the colorful bracts, which are the main attraction.

Neoporteria multicolor thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
