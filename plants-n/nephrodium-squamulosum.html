Nephrodium squamulosum

Nephrodium squamulosum, commonly known as the squamulose nephroid, is a unique fern-like plant characterized by its intricate, lace-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Nephrodiaceae and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features small, scale-like structures along its stems, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. Its reproductive structures are borne on specialized leaves called sporophylls.

Nephrodium squamulosum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
