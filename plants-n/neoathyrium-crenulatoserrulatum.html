Neoathyrium crenulatoserrulatum

Neoathyrium crenulatoserrulatum, commonly known as the serrated-leaf bamboo, is a member of the fern family, specifically the Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with finely serrated margins. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are fibrous and contribute to its structural support.
