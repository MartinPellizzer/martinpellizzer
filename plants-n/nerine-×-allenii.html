Nerine × allenii

Nerine × allenii, commonly known as the hybrid lily or the pink lily, is a perennial bulbous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range from white to pink, often with a red or purple base. The plant produces strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Nerine × allenii is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
