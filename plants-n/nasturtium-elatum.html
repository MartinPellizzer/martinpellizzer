Nasturtium elatum

Nasturtium elatum, commonly known as the common nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which are borne on slender stems. The plant produces heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often used in culinary applications. Its flowers and young leaves are edible and have a peppery, tangy flavor.

Nasturtium elatum thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
