Neoporteria vallenarensis var. atroviridis

Neoporteria vallenarensis var. atroviridis, commonly known as the Atrovirid neoporteria, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the coastal regions of Chile.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to the harsh, coastal climate with high salinity and strong winds. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists studying plant adaptation.

Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect this vulnerable species from habitat loss and environmental degradation.
