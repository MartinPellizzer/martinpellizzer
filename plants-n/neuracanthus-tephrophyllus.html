Neuracanthus tephrophyllus

Neuracanthus tephrophyllus, commonly known as the flame violet, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow, often resembling flames. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green with a slight sheen. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Neuracanthus tephrophyllus is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its eye-catching appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
