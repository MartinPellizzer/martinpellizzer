Necepsia castaneifolia subsp. chirindica

Necepsia castaneifolia subsp.

chirindica, commonly known as the African chestnut tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets.
