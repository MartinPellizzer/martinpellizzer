Nephelaphyllum pulchrum

Nephelaphyllum pulchrum, commonly known as the celestial beauty orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that often display a mix of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Nephelaphyllum pulchrum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
