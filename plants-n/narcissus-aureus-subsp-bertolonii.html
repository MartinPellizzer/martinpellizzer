Narcissus aureus subsp. bertolonii

Narcissus aureus subsp.

bertolonii, commonly known as the Bertoloni daffodil, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers.

The plant typically blooms in early spring, adding a vibrant splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its resilience and ability to naturalize over time.
