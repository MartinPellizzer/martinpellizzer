Nervilia septemtrionarius

Nervilia septemtrionarius, commonly known as the northern lady's tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dense, dark green, strap-like leaves and a long, spirally twisted stem. The plant produces a showy inflorescence of white to pale pink flowers arranged in a loose raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Nervilia septemtrionarius is often found in temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.
