Neomammillaria polycentra var. brunnea

Neomammillaria polycentra var. brunnea, commonly known as the Brunnea mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to southern Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem with a grayish-green color and prominent, white to yellow tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
