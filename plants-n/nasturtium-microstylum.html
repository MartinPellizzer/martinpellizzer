Nasturtium microstylum

Nasturtium microstylum, commonly known as the dwarf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, edible seed pods that are often used in culinary applications. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly peppery taste, similar to watercress.

Nasturtium microstylum thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental and edible plants.
