Nierembergia Hippomanica Var. Coerulea

Nierembergia hippomanica var. coerulea is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to South America. It is known for its vibrant blue and purple blooms that appear in late spring. This variety is a popular choice among gardeners for its ornamental value and hardiness. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its unique color palette adds a touch of elegance to any landscape.
