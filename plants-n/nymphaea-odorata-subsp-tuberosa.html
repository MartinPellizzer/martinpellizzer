Nymphaea odorata subsp. tuberosa

Nymphaea odorata subsp.

tuberosa, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a water-living plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, showy pink or white flowers that bloom above the water surface.

The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, providing habitat for various aquatic organisms. Its rhizomes are tuberous and store nutrients, allowing it to survive in aquatic environments.

The plant is also valued for its cultural and medicinal significance in traditional practices.
