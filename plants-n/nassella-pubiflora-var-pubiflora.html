Nassella pubiflora var. pubiflora

Nassella pubiflora var. pubiflora, commonly known as the purple needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense clusters of feathery, purple-tipped inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue and a hairy texture.

It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
