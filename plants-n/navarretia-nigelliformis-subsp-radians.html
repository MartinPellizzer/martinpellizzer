Navarretia nigelliformis subsp. radians

Navarretia nigelliformis subsp.

radians, commonly known as the radiate navarretia, is a flowering plant native to California. It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that bloom in spring, typically in shades of white and pink.

The plant thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its small, lance-shaped leaves are arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces slender, branching stems.
