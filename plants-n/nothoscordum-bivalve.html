Nothoscordum bivalve

Nothoscordum bivalve, commonly known as the two-valved garlic.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its bulbs are similar to garlic in appearance.

Nothoscordum bivalve is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a mild garlic scent.
