Nasa jungiifolia

Nasa jungiifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine silvery or whitish pubescence. The plant typically grows as a deciduous tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually greenish-white in color, and it produces woody capsules as its fruit.

Nasa jungiifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
