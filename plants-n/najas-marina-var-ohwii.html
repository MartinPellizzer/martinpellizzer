Najas marina var. ohwii

Najas marina var. ohwii, commonly known as the slender water plant, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in East Asia.

It belongs to the family Najadaceae and is characterized by its long, ribbon-like leaves that are often spirally arranged around its stem. The plant typically grows in shallow, slow-moving waters and is adapted to low-light conditions.
