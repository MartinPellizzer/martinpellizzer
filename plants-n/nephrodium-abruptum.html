Nephrodium abruptum

Nephrodium abruptum, commonly known as the abrupt fern, is a rare and delicate plant species belonging to the family Nephrodiaceae.

It is characterized by its unique, clustered growth pattern and its small, leathery leaves that are often arranged in a spiral. The plant produces tiny, spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are typically located on the underside of its fronds.
