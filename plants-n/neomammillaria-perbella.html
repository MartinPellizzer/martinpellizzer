Neomammillaria perbella

Neomammillaria perbella, commonly known as the Perbella mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and covered in white to yellowish spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative succulent.
