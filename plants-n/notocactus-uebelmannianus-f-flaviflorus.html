Notocactus uebelmannianus f. flaviflorus

Notocactus uebelmannianus f. flaviflorus, commonly known as the yellow-flowered columnar cactus, is a unique hybrid cactus that combines traits from its parent species.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions for its striking appearance.
