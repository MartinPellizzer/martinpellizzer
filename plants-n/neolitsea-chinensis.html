Neolitsea chinensis

Neolitsea chinensis, commonly known as Chinese laurustin, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite, and often elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, spherical berries. Neolitsea chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage and seasonal interest.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.
