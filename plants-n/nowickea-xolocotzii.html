Nowickea xolocotzii

Nowickea xolocotzii, commonly known as the Xolocotzti cactus, is a unique species native to the highlands of Mexico.

It is a spineless cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring a short, thick stem covered in dense, woolly hair. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant plant.
