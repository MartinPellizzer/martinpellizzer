Nephrodium latifrons

Nephrodium latifrons, commonly known as the broad-fronded nephroid, is a unique fern species characterized by its large, broad fronds.

It belongs to the family Nephrodiaceae and is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with dense, clustered fronds that are typically dark green and leathery. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds.

This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
