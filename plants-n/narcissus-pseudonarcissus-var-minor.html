Narcissus pseudonarcissus var. minor

Narcissus pseudonarcissus var. minor, commonly known as the lesser crown imperial, is a bulbous plant native to southern Europe.

It produces striking, upward-facing flowers that are typically orange or reddish in color, with a prominent central crown. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is characterized by its compact size compared to other narcissus varieties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often blooming in early spring.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
