Nemesia versicolor f. compacta

Nemesia versicolor f. compacta, commonly known as the compact nemesia, is a compact, bushy annual plant native to South Africa.

It features small, vibrant flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white, often with bicolor patterns. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are evergreen during the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens, containers, and as a bedding plant.
