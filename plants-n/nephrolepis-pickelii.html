Nephrolepis pickelii

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its lance-shaped, evergreen fronds that are typically 30 to 60 centimeters long. This fern thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories where it contributes to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
