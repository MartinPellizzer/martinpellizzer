Neomammillaria multiformis

Neomammillaria multiformis, commonly known as the variable mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white or pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Neomammillaria multiformis is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
