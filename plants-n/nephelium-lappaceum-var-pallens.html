Nephelium lappaceum var. pallens

Nephelium lappaceum var. pallens, commonly known as the white langsat, is a variant of the langsat fruit native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white rind and sweet, juicy pulp. The plant is a small evergreen tree belonging to the Sapotaceae family. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
