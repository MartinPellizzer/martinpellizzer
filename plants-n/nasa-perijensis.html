Nasa perijensis

Nasa perijensis, commonly known as the Moonflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a strong fragrance. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Nasa perijensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique nocturnal blooming habit.
