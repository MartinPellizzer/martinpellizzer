Nemia

Nemia, commonly known as the sweetleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is valued for its edible fruits, which have a sweet, honey-like taste. Its bark and leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Nemia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
