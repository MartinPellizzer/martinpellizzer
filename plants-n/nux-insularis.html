Nux insularis

Nux insularis, commonly known as the island nightshade, is a flowering plant native to certain tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its leaves are usually broad and oval, with a slightly hairy texture.

Nux insularis produces small, toxic berries that are often avoided by both animals and humans due to their harmful properties.
