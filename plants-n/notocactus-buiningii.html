Notocactus buiningii

Notocactus buiningii, commonly known as the Buning's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are often covered in small, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
