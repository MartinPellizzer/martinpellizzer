Nycterisition lanceolatum

Nycterisition lanceolatum, commonly known as the nightshade leaf plant, is a tropical species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often velvety in texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both ornamental and ecological gardens.
