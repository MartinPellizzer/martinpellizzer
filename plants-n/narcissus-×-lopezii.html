Narcissus × lopezii

Narcissus × lopezii, commonly known as the Lopez daffodil, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between different narcissus species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a yellow corona and a white or yellow perianth. The plant is native to the Canary Islands and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces multiple blooms per stem.

Narcissus × lopezii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant spring display.
