Notonia incisifolia

Notonia incisifolia, commonly known as the serrated leaf plant, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, which give it its scientific name. Its flowers are small and yellow, often clustered in inflorescences.

Notonia incisifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
