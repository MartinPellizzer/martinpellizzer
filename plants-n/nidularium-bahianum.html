Nidularium bahianum

Nidularium bahianum, commonly known as the Brazilian arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence. The plant features a rhizomatous stem system that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

Nidularium bahianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
