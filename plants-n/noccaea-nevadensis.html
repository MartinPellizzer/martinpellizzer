Noccaea nevadensis

Noccaea nevadensis, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This perennial species typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. Its small, white or yellowish flowers bloom in early spring, and it produces small, round seed pods.
