Nelensia

Nelensia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Nelensia is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

It is also sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its invasive potential.
