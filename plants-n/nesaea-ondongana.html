Nesaea ondongana

Nesaea ondongana, commonly known as the Onongana daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, hairy fruits that disperse its seeds.

Nesaea ondongana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
