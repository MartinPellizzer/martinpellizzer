Nephelium diversifolium

Nephelium diversifolium, commonly known as the mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with multiple leaflets. The tree produces edible, fragrant fruits encased in a bright red, segmented rind. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in panicles, and it thrives in humid, warm climates.
