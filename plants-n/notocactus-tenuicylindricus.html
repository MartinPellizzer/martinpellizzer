Notocactus tenuicylindricus

Notocactus tenuicylindricus, commonly known as the narrow-cylindrical notocactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the evening, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It has a shallow root system adapted to absorb moisture from the dry soil.

Due to its slow growth and unique morphology, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
