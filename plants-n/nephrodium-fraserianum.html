Nephrodium fraserianum

Nephrodium fraserianum, commonly known as the Fraser's nephrolepis, is a fern species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories where it can receive indirect light. The leaves are usually light green and have a soft texture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
