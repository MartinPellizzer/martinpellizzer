Nothoscordum minarum

Nothoscordum minarum, commonly known as the mountain garlic.

It is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a bulbous base similar to onions.

Nothoscordum minarum is often found in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas.
