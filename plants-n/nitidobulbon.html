Nitidobulbon

Nitidobulbon, commonly known as the smooth bulb orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its clustered, underground bulbs that store nutrients and water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. Its botanical features make it well-adapted to rocky and sandy soils, and it is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Nitidobulbon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
