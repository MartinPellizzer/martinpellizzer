Nidularium atalaiaense

Nidularium atalaiaense, commonly known as the Atalaia waterplantain, is a species of aquatic plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its ribbon-like, narrow leaves that are typically submerged in water. The plant produces small, white flowers that emerge from the water surface, often in clusters. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes.

Nidularium atalaiaense plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen to aquatic life.
