Nassella Pubiflora Var. Humilior

Nassella pubiflora var. humilior is a low-growing grass species native to the arid regions of central and southern South America. It is known for its delicate, feathery seed heads and its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. This variety is often found in rocky soils and open plains, where it forms dense, carpet-like mats. Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping. Despite its modest size, Nassella pubiflora var. humilior plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
