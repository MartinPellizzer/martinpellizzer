Niobe caerulea

Niobe caerulea, commonly known as the blue niobe, is a striking flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its harsh environment.
