Nectolis × vitellina

Nectolis × vitellina, commonly known as the hybrid nectarius, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rhamnaceae.

It is a natural hybrid between Nectaris and Nectolis, characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are often borne in dense inflorescences. It is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
