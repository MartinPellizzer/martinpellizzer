Nycterisition argenteum

Nycterisition argenteum, commonly known as the silverleaf nycteris, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths. Its growth habit is shrub-like, with multiple stems arising from the base, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Nycterisition argenteum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
