Nervilia Commersonii

Nervilia orchid, scientifically known as Nervilia commersonii, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This unique orchid is characterized by its slender stems and intricate, orchid-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Despite its beauty, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes and is often found in shaded, moist habitats. Its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat destruction and over-collection, Nervilia commersonii is increasingly threatened, highlighting the need for greater protection and study.
