Neillia sinensis f. glanduligera

Neillia sinensis f. glanduligera, commonly known as the glandular Chinese bellflower, is a cultivar of the Chinese bellflower species.

It is characterized by its distinctive glandular structures on the leaves and stems, which give it its specific epithet. The plant typically features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet. It is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and botanical collections.
