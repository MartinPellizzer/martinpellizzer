Navia geaster

Navia geaster, commonly known as the starry navia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white to pink petals. Navia geaster typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
