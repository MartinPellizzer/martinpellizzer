Narcissus reflexus proles pulchellus

Narcissus reflexus proles pulchellus, commonly known as the slender daffodil, is a rare and delicate species of flowering plant native to parts of southern Europe.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
