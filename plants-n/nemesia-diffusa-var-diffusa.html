Nemesia diffusa var. diffusa

Nemesia diffusa var. diffusa, commonly known as the common nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial species characterized by its trailing habit and profuse display of small, funnel-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically bright yellow or orange, with five petals and a distinctive spurred petal at the base. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy or loamy soils.

This plant is often used in garden settings for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
