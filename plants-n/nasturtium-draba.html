Nasturtium draba

Nasturtium draba, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow or orange flowers and its pinnately compound leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Nasturtium draba is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
