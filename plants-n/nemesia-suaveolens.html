Nemesia suaveolens

Nemesia suaveolens, commonly known as sweet nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and profuse display of small, delicate flowers. The flowers are typically borne in clusters and come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and yellow. Its fragrance is subtle, adding a pleasant aroma to gardens.
