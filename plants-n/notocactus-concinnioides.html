Notocactus concinnioides

Notocactus concinnioides, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a species native to southern Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem that typically grows to about 10 centimeters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its silver-green foliage. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
