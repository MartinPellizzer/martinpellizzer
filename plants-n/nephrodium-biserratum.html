Nephrodium biserratum

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds. The plant features a creeping rhizome with erect, bipinnate fronds that have a serrated margin. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a rough texture.
