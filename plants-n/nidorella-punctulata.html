Nidorella punctulata

Nidorella punctulata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spotted leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Nidorella punctulata is a perennial herb that produces small, greenish-yellow flowers clustered in a spike.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and ecological studies.
