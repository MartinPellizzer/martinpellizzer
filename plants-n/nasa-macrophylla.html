Nasa macrophylla

Nasa macrophylla, commonly known as the velvetleaf or large-leaved nasa, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often velvety to the touch. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in moist lowland areas.

Nasa macrophylla is valued for its ornamental leaves and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
