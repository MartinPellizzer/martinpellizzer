Nasturtium nanum

Nasturtium nanum, commonly known as dwarf nasturtium, is a compact, annual plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, edible yellow or orange flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its flowers and leaves are often used in culinary applications due to their peppery flavor.

Nasturtium nanum is also valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
