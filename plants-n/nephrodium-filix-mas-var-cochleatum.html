Nephrodium filix-mas var. cochleatum

Nephrodium filix-mas var. cochleatum, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a variant of the common nephrolepis.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of fronds, which gives it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant has pinnately divided leaves with a soft, feathery texture. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological studies.
