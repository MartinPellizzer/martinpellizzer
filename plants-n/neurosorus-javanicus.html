Neurosorus javanicus

Neurosorus javanicus, commonly known as the Java fern, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, with long, creeping stems that can root at the nodes. The plant typically features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly glossy. It thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions.

Neurosorus javanicus is often used in aquariums due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
