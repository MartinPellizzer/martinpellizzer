Nyssanthes diffusa

Nyssanthes diffusa, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by ornamental, spherical fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.

The leaves are also valued for their medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
