Nauclea lanosa

Nauclea lanosa, commonly known as the wooly nauclea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant is characterized by its hairy, woolly leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
