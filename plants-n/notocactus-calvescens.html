Notocactus calvescens

Notocactus calvescens, commonly known as the Mexican apple cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by red, apple-like fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
