Nemcia cuneata var. obovata

Nemcia cuneata var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed nemcia, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its obovate leaves, which are broad and rounded at the tips, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly found in its natural habitat.
