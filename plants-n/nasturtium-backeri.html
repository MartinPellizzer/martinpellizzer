Nasturtium backeri

Nasturtium backeri, commonly known as the African nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright orange or yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in warm, sunny conditions. Its flowers are edible and have a peppery taste, similar to watercress.

Nasturtium backeri is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and as a culinary herb.
