Nerine gaberonensis

Nerine gaberonensis, commonly known as the Gaberonensis nerine, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright white or pale pink blooms that are often speckled with darker hues. Its leaves are strap-like, narrow, and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Nerine gaberonensis is well-suited for rock gardens and containers, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
