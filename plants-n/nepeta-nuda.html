Nepeta nuda

Nepeta nuda, commonly known as naked catmint, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, fragrant blue or purple flowers. The plant has slender stems and narrow, hairy leaves that are often covered in a fine down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Nepeta nuda is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
