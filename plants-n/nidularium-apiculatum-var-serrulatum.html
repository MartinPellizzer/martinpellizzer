Nidularium apiculatum var. serrulatum

Nidularium apiculatum var. serrulatum, commonly known as the serrated pineapple plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, fibrous covering. Its inflorescence consists of a single, showy flower that emerges from the center of the rosette.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
