Nototrichium viride

Nototrichium viride, commonly known as green nototrichium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features upright stems covered in dense, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months.

Nototrichium viride is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
