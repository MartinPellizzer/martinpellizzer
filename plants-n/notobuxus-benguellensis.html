Notobuxus benguellensis

Notobuxus benguellensis, commonly known as the Benguella barberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, while its berries are red and edible.

Notobuxus benguellensis is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
