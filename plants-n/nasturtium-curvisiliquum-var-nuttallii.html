Nasturtium curvisiliquum var. nuttallii

Nasturtium curvisiliquum var. nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's nasturtium, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It produces vibrant yellow and orange flowers with distinctive, spurred petals. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its flowers are edible and have a peppery flavor, often used in culinary applications.
