Nematanthus radicans

Nematanthus radicans, commonly known as the trailing gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. Nematanthus radicans is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
