Nama lobbii

Nama lobbii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern and southern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its foliage is often velvety and can vary in color from dark green to purple.

Nama lobbii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
