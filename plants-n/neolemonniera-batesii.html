Neolemonniera batesii

Neolemonniera batesii, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant produces small, round, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Neolemonniera batesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
