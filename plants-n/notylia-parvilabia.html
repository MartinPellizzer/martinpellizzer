Notylia parvilabia

Notylia parvilabia, commonly known as the small-lipped notylia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Madagascar. The plant features a slender, upright stem with opposite, lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, with a distinctive bilabiate (two-lipped) structure.

Notylia parvilabia is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in moist, shaded habitats.
