Notocactus arnostianus f. curvispinus

Notocactus arnostianus f. curvispinus, commonly known as the Curved-spine Notocactus, is a unique cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem with prominent, curved spines that give it its distinguishing appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in arid or semi-arid climates.
