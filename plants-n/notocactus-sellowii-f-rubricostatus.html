Notocactus sellowii f. rubricostatus

Notocactus sellowii f. rubricostatus, commonly known as the red-costated notocactus, is a unique hybrid of the Notocactus genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are often covered in a distinctive reddish or pinkish bloom, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange, typically appearing at the top of the stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
