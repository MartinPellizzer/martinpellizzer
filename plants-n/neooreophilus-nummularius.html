Neooreophilus nummularius

Neooreophilus nummularius, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine, hair-like trichomes. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its attractive appearance.
