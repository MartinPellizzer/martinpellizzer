Neurogramma vestita

Neurogramma vestita, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to South America.

It is a small, herbaceous species that forms dense mats on the ground. The plant is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Neurogramma vestita is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
