Nephrolepis brownii

Nephrolepis brownii, commonly known as the Brown's sword fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching fronds that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length. The plant features bipinnate leaves with a delicate, feathery appearance, and its rhizomes are often covered in a dense mat of roots. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Nephrolepis brownii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
