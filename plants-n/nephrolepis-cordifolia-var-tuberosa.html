Nephrolepis cordifolia var. tuberosa

Nephrolepis cordifolia var. tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous fern, is a variant of the common sword fern.

It is a tropical fern characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and dense, feather-like fronds. The plant produces underground tubers, which contribute to its unique growth pattern and propagation. Its fronds are typically glossy and have a leathery texture, with a deep green color.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded gardens and as a houseplant.
