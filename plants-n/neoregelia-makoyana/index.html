Neoregelia Makoyana

Neoregelia makoyana is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America. Known for its vibrant, colorful foliage, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. It features rosettes of broad, textured leaves that can display shades of green, red, and orange. Often grown as a houseplant, it requires careful watering and bright, indirect light to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and collectors.
