Naron iridioides

Naron iridioides, commonly known as the iridescent naron, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, iridescent blue and purple petals that shimmer in sunlight. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Naron iridioides is valued not only for its aesthetic appeal but also for its potential medicinal properties.
