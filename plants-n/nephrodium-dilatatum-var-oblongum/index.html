Nephrodium Dilatatum Var. Oblongum

Nephrodium dilatatum var. oblongum is a unique fern species native to specific regions of eastern Asia. It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated fronds that display a striking pattern of light and dark green. This variant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its slow growth and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among horticulturists. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in popular botanic circles.
