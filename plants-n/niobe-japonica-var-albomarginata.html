Niobe japonica var. albomarginata

Niobe japonica var. albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin niobe, is a variant of the niobe plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white margin. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
