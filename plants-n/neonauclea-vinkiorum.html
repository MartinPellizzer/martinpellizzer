Neonauclea vinkiorum

Neonauclea vinkiorum, commonly known as the Vinkiorum tree, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

The fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
