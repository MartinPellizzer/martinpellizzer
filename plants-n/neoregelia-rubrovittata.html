Neoregelia rubrovittata

Neoregelia rubrovittata, commonly known as the red-striped bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that collect water and nutrients. The plant features vibrant, red-brown striped leaves that give it its common name. Its inflorescence consists of a tall, spire-like structure bearing small, colorful flowers.

Neoregelia rubrovittata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
