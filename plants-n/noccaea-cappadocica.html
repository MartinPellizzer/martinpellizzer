Noccaea cappadocica

Noccaea cappadocica, commonly known as the Cappadocian honesty, is a flowering plant native to the Cappadocia region of Turkey.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by small, round seed pods. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Noccaea cappadocica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
