Neoregelia pernambucana

Neoregelia pernambucana, commonly known as the Pernambuco bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to Brazil.

It features a rosette of broad, waxy leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or purple. The plant produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences that can last several months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Neoregelia pernambucana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
