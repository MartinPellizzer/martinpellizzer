Nemesia minutiflora

Nemesia minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf nemesia, is a low-growing annual plant native to South Africa.

It produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink, white, and red, which bloom profusely from spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it an excellent addition to borders and wildflower displays.
