Nardurus maritimus

Nardurus maritimus, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found along the coastline. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to saline and windy environments.

Nardurus maritimus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
