Noyera rubra

Noyera rubra, commonly known as the red noyera, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth, glossy texture.

Noyera rubra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
