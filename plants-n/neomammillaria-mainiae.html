Neomammillaria mainiae

Neomammillaria mainiae, commonly known as the Mainia's pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and prominent, white radial spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer.

Neomammillaria mainiae is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and striking appearance.
