Narcissus stelliflorus

Narcissus stelliflorus, commonly known as the star-flowered daffodil, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow petals with a distinctive central crown. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
