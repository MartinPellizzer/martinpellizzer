Nazia

Nazia, commonly known as the "flower of the desert," is a hardy plant native to arid regions.

It features small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, often during the cooler months. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in poor soil conditions. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping to conserve water.

Nazia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
