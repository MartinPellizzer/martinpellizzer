Nabalus × mainensis

Nabalus × mainensis, commonly known as the Main River hawthorn, is a hybrid shrub native to Central Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and woodland edges. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries in the fall. Its leaves are oval-shaped with serrated edges and a glossy texture.

Nabalus × mainensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
