Nepenthes thai

Nepenthes thai, commonly known as the Thai pitcher plant, is a tropical carnivorous plant native to Thailand.

It is characterized by its unique pitcher-shaped leaves that secrete a sticky liquid to trap insects. The plant's pitchers are typically green or reddish in color and have a waxy surface. Nepenthes thai thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture.

Its botanical features make it a fascinating subject for both horticultural study and conservation efforts.
