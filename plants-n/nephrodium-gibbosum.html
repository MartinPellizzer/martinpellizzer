Nephrodium gibbosum

Nephrodium gibbosum, commonly known as the globular fern, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive, rounded fronds.

It belongs to the family Nephrodiaceae and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a short, thick rhizome with dense, clustered fronds that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its leaves are typically leathery and have a compact, globular appearance, which gives it its common name.

Nephrodium gibbosum is often found in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth.
