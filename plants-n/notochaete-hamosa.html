Notochaete hamosa

Notochaete hamosa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is often hidden among the foliage.

Notochaete hamosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
