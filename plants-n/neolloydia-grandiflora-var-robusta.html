Neolloydia grandiflora var. robusta

Neolloydia grandiflora var. robusta, commonly known as the robust giant bellflower, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a waxy surface that aids in water retention.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
