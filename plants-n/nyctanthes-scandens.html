Nyctanthes scandens

Nyctanthes scandens, commonly known as night-flowering jasmine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that open at night and close during the day. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters and emit a strong, sweet scent to attract nocturnal pollinators.

Nyctanthes scandens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and as a hedge plant.
