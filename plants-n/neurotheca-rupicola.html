Neurotheca rupicola

Neurotheca rupicola, commonly known as the rock neurotheca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and is often found growing on rocky outcrops. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Neurotheca rupicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in rock gardens and dry landscapes.
