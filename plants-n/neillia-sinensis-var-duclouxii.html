Neillia sinensis var. duclouxii

Neillia sinensis var. duclouxii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, strap-like leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf burn. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to purify the air and its elegant appearance.

Neillia sinensis var. duclouxii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
