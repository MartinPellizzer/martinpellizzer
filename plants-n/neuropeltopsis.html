Neuropeltopsis

Neuropeltopsis, commonly known as the ghost fern, is a rare and delicate species belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its translucent, lace-like fronds that give it a ghostly appearance, especially when backlit. Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species with limited distribution.
