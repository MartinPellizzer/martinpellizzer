Neea rotundifolia

Neea rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf neea, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features simple, ovate to round leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish-white and appear in clusters.

Neea rotundifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
