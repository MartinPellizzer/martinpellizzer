Notholaena rufa var. major

Notholaena rufa var. major, commonly known as the red-stemmed fern, is a variant of the notholaena rufa species.

It is a perennial plant native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida. The plant is characterized by its dark red to purple stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.
