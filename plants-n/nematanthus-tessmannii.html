Nematanthus tessmannii

Nematanthus tessmannii, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family, native to Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.
