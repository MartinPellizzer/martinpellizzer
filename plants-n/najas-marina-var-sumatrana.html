Najas marina var. sumatrana

Najas marina var. sumatrana, commonly known as the slender watermilfoil, is a submerged aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Najadaceae and is characterized by its narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem.
