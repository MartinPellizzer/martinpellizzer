Navarretia fossalis

Navarretia fossalis, commonly known as the Sierra snowplant, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

The plant is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine regions. Navarretia fossalis is an annual or biennial species that relies on seasonal snowmelt for germination and growth.
