Nolletia rhodesiana

Nolletia rhodesiana, commonly known as the Rhodesian orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly found in countries like Zimbabwe, South Africa, and Namibia. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, fragrant orchid flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in grassland habitats and requires well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Nolletia rhodesiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate and attractive blooms.
