Nipponocalamus mollissimus

Nipponocalamus mollissimus, commonly known as the Japanese feather grass, is a species of grass native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and soft, silvery-green foliage that gives it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Nipponocalamus mollissimus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly used in landscaping and garden design.
