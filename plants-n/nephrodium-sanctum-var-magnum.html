Nephrodium sanctum var. magnum

Nephrodium sanctum var. magnum, commonly known as the sacred heart fern, is a rare and ornamental fern native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It features lush, dark green fronds that are deeply lobed and arranged in a distinctive, graceful pattern. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
