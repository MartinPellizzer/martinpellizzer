Nexilis

Nexilis, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It features silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance, often with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Nexilis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its hardy nature allows it to withstand mild frost, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
