Notocactus floricomus var. spinosissimus

Notocactus floricomus var. spinosissimus, commonly known as the Spiny Flowering Cactus, is a unique cactus species native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are covered in spines, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the warmer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

This variety is prized for its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
