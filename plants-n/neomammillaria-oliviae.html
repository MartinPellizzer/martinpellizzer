Neomammillaria oliviae

Neomammillaria oliviae, commonly known as Olivia's Pincushion Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Neomammillaria oliviae is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
