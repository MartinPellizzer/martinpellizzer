Narcissus × magni-abilii

Narcissus × magni-abilii, commonly known as the Giant Crowned Trumpet, is a hybrid tulip that exhibits striking botanical features.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive crown-like pattern on the tepals. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–50 cm, with sturdy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a prominent orange or red blotch at the base.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
