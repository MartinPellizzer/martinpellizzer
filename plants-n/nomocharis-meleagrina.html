Nomocharis meleagrina

Nomocharis meleagrina, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright red, spider-like flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.

Nomocharis meleagrina is also known for its ability to naturalize and spread through its underground bulbs.
