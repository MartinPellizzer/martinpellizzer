Neuropeltis maingayi var. maingayi

Neuropeltis maingayi var. maingayi, commonly known as the Maingay's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
