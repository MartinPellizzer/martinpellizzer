Narcissus jonquilla var. plenus

Narcissus jonquilla var. plenus, commonly known as the double jonquil, is a hybrid variety of the narcissus genus.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and dense clusters of fragrant, double flowers. The petals are layered, giving the blooms a full, pom-pom-like appearance. It typically blooms in early spring, producing yellow or white flowers that are highly valued in gardens for their ornamental beauty.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and borders, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun.
