Neoporteria nidus var. matancillana

Neoporteria nidus var. matancillana, commonly known as the Matancillan rock fig, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, specifically found in Puerto Rico.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a glossy texture. It is adapted to tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade for optimal growth.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
