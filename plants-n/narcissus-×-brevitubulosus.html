Narcissus × brevitubulosus

Narcissus × brevitubulosus, commonly known as the miniature daffodil, is a hybrid plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and bright yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in early spring, producing clusters of fragrant, single or double flowers. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires well-drained soil and protection from frost for optimal growth.
