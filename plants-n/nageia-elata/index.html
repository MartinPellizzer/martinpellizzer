Nageia Elata

Nageia elata is a unique evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking appearance and ecological importance in its natural habitat. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous areas. Its leaves are long and narrow, and it produces small, fragrant flowers. Despite its beauty, Nageia elata is relatively rare and faces threats from habitat loss and deforestation.
