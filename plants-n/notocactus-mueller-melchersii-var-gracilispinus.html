Notocactus mueller-melchersii var. gracilispinus

Notocactus mueller-melchersii var. gracilispinus, commonly known as the slender-spined notocactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems and spines that are typically slender and arranged in a spiral pattern. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
