Neoregelia richteri

Neoregelia richteri, commonly known as the Rainbow Plant, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that range from red to orange and yellow, giving it its common name. The plant features a rosette of narrow, waxy leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports a miniature ecosystem. Its flowers are typically pale pink or white and are borne on a tall stem.

Neoregelia richteri thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its lush appearance.
