Notocactus scopa f. erythrinus

Notocactus scopa f. erythrinus, commonly known as the red-hair cactus, is a unique hybrid of the Notocactus genus.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual contrast to its green body. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in cactus gardens.
