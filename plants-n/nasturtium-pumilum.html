Nasturtium pumilum

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few inches, making it a compact and ornamental choice for gardens. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.
