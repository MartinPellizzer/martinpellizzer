Nerine flexuosa var. sandersonii

Nerine flexuosa var. sandersonii, commonly known as the Sanderson's belladonna lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive white or pale pink throat. The plant produces narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy and grow from a rounded bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
