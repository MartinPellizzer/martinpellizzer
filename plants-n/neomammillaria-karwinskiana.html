Neomammillaria karwinskiana

Neomammillaria karwinskiana, commonly known as the Karwinskiana mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white to yellowish tubercles. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors.
