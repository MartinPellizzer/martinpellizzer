Nogra

Nogra, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a aquatic plant native to the wetlands of South and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, slender stalks above the water's surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water, providing habitat for various aquatic organisms. Its flowers are typically pink or white, with a central yellow or red stigma.

Nogra is also known for its symbolic significance in many cultures, representing purity and spiritual enlightenment.
