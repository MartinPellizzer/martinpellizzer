Nicipe filifolia

Nicipe filifolia, commonly known as the threadleaf nicipe, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Nicipe filifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
