Nectandra schottii

Nectandra schottii, commonly known as the yellow sandalwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by elliptical, woody fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a glossy surface.
