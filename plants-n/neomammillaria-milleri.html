Neomammillaria milleri

Neomammillaria milleri, commonly known as the Miller's prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its areoles are densely covered with white, soft spines, and it is known for its compact growth habit.

Neomammillaria milleri is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
