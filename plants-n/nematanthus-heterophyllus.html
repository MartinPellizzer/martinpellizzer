Nematanthus heterophyllus

Nematanthus heterophyllus, commonly known as the silver leaf gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as a trailing or upright shrub, with opposite leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor cultivation.
