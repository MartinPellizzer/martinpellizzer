Nicandra nebulosa

Nicandra nebulosa, commonly known as the cloud bean or nebula flower, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its striking, bioluminescent-like flowers that glow under moonlight. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, while its stems are branched and covered in fine pubescence.

Nicandra nebulosa thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
