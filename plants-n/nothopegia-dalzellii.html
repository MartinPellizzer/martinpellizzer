Nothopegia dalzellii

Nothopegia dalzellii, commonly known as the Dalzell's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive greenish-yellow color. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in rainforest or heathland areas. Its flowers are arranged in a spike and bloom during the cooler months of the year.

Nothopegia dalzellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
