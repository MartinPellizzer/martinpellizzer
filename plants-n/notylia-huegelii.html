Notylia huegelii

Notylia huegelii, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine.

Notylia huegelii is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
