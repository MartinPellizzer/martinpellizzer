Nidularium billbergioides f. azureum

Nidularium billbergioides f. azureum, commonly known as the Azure Flaxflower, is a rare and striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to violet bracts that surround the inflorescence, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that emerge from a central spike, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This cultivar is prized for its unique coloration and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
