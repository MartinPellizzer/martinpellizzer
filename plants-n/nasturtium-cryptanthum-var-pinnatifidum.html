Nasturtium cryptanthum var. pinnatifidum

Nasturtium cryptanthum var. pinnatifidum, commonly known as the golden nasturtium, is a herbaceous annual plant native to South America.

It features striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, with a distinctive spurred shape. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a fern-like appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often grown for its ornamental value and edible flowers.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both gardens and culinary use.
