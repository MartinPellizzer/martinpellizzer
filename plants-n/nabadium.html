Nabadium

Nabadium, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery leaves that have a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Nabadium species are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

They are also valued in horticulture for their ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
