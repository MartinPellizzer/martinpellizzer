Notoniopsis grantii

Notoniopsis grantii, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow and green inflorescence. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are often clustered in a spiral pattern along the stem. Notoniopsis grantii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
