Nemexia tamnifolia

Nemexia tamnifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf nemexia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, succulent perennial that features dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for gardeners in arid climates.
