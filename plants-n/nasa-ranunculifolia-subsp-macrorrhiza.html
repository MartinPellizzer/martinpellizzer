Nasa ranunculifolia subsp. macrorrhiza

Nasa ranunculifolia subsp.

macrorrhiza, commonly known as the large-leaf nasa, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as cloud forests. Its leaves are broad and lobed, often with a velvety texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
