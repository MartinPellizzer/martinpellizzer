Necepsia zairensis var. zairensis

Necepsia zairensis var. zairensis, commonly known as the Zaire sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant thrives in arid or semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or gravelly soils. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to survive in nutrient-poor conditions.
