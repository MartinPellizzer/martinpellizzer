Nasturtium hirtellum

Nasturtium hirtellum, commonly known as the hairy nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, edible flowers in shades of orange and yellow. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Nasturtium hirtellum is also valued for its peppery taste, often used in culinary applications.
