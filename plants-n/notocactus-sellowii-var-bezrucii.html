Notocactus sellowii var. bezrucii

Notocactus sellowii var. bezrucii, commonly known as the Sellow's notocactus, is a rare and striking cactus species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne in clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it is adapted to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.

This variety is prized by collectors for its unique appearance and slow growth rate.
