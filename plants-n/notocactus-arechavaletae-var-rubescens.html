Notocactus arechavaletae var. rubescens

Notocactus arechavaletae var. rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered arechavaleta cactus, is a rare cactus species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stem and vibrant red flowers. The plant typically grows in arid environments, adapting to extreme temperatures and low water availability. Its flowers are often short-lived but attract pollinators such as bees and moths.

The species is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and hardiness.
