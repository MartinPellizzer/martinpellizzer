Nepeta racemosa

Nepeta racemosa, commonly known as catnip, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like mint and basil. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are often used to make tea or as a natural remedy for various ailments.

Nepeta racemosa is also well-known for its effect on cats, causing them to exhibit playful and energetic behaviors.
