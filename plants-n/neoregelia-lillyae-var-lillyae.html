Neoregelia lillyae var. lillyae

Neoregelia lillyae var. lillyae, commonly known as the Lily's Neoregelia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that can display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports microfauna. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and can vary in color depending on light exposure.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
