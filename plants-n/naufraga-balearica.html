Naufraga balearica

Naufraga balearica, commonly known as the Balearic rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky and coastal environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh maritime conditions. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Naufraga balearica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
