Neurophyllum rhizophyllum

Neurophyllum rhizophyllum, commonly known as the root-leaf fern, is a unique plant characterized by its specialized root system that functions like leaves, performing photosynthesis.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for studying plant adaptations in nutrient-poor soils.
