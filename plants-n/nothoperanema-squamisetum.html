Nothoperanema squamisetum

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, scale-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in compact inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.
