Neillia thyrsiflora var. fallax

Neillia thyrsiflora var. fallax, commonly known as the fallax form of the thyrse-flowered neillia, is a variant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This variety thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various garden conditions.
