Norta altissima

Norta altissima, commonly known as the tall norta, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Norta altissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
