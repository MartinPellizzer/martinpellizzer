Noterophila beccabunga

Noterophila beccabunga, commonly known as the lesser celandine, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and produces bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical name reflects its historical use in herbal medicine.
