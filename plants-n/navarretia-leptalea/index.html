Navarretia Leptalea

Navarretia leptalea is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dunes and coastal scrub habitats. This species is known for its small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and fragile species.
