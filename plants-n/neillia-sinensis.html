Neillia sinensis

Neillia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking white and green spadix. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities. Neillia sinensis produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by bright red berries.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
