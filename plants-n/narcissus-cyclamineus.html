Narcissus cyclamineus

Narcissus cyclamineus, commonly known as the cyclamen narcissus, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates.

Narcissus cyclamineus is a popular ornamental plant valued for its elegant appearance and seasonal blooms.
