Nephrodium danesianum

Nephrodium danesianum, commonly known as the Dansen's kidney fern, is a rare and delicate fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests where humidity is high. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of many fern species.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Nephrodium danesianum is considered a conservation concern.
