Nauclea subdita

Nauclea subdita, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by distinctive red or orange pods. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape.

Nauclea subdita is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
