Nymphaea fragrantissima

Nymphaea fragrantissima, commonly known as the fragrant water lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinct fragrance. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats, such as ponds and marshes. Its leaves are broad and floating, while its roots anchor it in the muddy substrate.

Nymphaea fragrantissima plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various water organisms.
