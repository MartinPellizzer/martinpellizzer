Nasturtium crystallinum

Nasturtium crystallinum, commonly known as the crystalline nasturtium, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or orange center. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a suitable candidate for garden cultivation.

Nasturtium crystallinum is also valued for its edible leaves and flowers, which have a peppery taste and can be used in culinary applications.
