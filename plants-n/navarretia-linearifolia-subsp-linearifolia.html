Navarretia linearifolia subsp. linearifolia

Navarretia linearifolia subsp.

linearifolia, commonly known as the slender navarretia, is a flowering plant native to California and northern Baja California. It is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, white to pinkish flowers.
