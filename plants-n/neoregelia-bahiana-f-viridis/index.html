Neoregelia Bahiana F. Viridis

Neoregelia bahiana f. viridis is a striking bromeliad known for its vibrant green foliage and unique growth habits. Native to Brazil, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its compact size and attractive leaves make it an ideal addition to any collection of tropical plants. The variety 'viridis' stands out for its lush, emerald coloration and subtle variegation. With proper care, this plant can flourish and bring a touch of nature's beauty to any space.
