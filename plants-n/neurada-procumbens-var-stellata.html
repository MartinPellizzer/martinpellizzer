Neurada procumbens var. stellata

Neurada procumbens var. stellata, commonly known as star-like neurada, is a low-growing perennial plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features prostrate stems that spread along the ground, often rooting at the nodes. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. Its leaves are typically oval and slightly hairy, with a soft texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
