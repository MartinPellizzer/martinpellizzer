Nymphaea × trickeri

Nymphaea × trickeri, commonly known as the Tricker water lily, is a hybrid aquatic plant belonging to the Nymphaeaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its roots are submerged, anchoring it to the substrate while its stems float on the water's surface.

Nymphaea × trickeri is valued in water gardens for its ornamental beauty and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
