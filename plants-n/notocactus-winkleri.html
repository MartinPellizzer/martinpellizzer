Notocactus winkleri

Notocactus winkleri, commonly known as the Winkler's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
