Nasturtium impatiens

Nasturtium impatiens, commonly known as the common nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, edible flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces orange or yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its stems are hairy and often trailing, making it suitable for container gardening or as a ground cover.

Nasturtium impatiens is also valued for its peppery taste, often used in culinary applications.
