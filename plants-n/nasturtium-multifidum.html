Nasturtium multifidum

Nasturtium multifidum, commonly known as the multifid nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which are typically borne on sprawling or trailing stems. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. Its flowers are not only ornamental but also edible, with a peppery taste similar to watercress.

Nasturtium multifidum thrives in sunny locations and is often used in gardens for both its beauty and culinary value.
