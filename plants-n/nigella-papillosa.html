Nigella papillosa

Nigella papillosa, commonly known as the Persian buttercup, is a flowering plant native to regions of Central Asia and the Middle East.

It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Nigella papillosa thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant for gardens.
