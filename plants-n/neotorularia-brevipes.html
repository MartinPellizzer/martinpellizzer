Neotorularia brevipes

Neotorularia brevipes, commonly known as the short-footed torularia, is a small, annual herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features slender stems and small, clustered leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are pale yellow and are arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Neotorularia brevipes is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing soil in disturbed environments.
