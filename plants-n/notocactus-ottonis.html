Notocactus ottonis

Notocactus ottonis, commonly known as the Otto's notocactus, is a cactus species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white or yellow spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
