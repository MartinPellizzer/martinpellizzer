Nevillea obtusissimus

Nevillea obtusissimus, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Central and South America, where it typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. Nevillea obtusissimus is often found in tropical rainforests and is adapted to thrive in humid conditions with high levels of indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
