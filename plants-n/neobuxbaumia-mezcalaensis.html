Neobuxbaumia mezcalaensis

Neobuxbaumia mezcalaensis, commonly known as the mezcala prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spineless stems and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers bloom in the spring and are pollinated by various insects, including bees and butterflies.
