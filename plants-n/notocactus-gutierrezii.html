Notocactus gutierrezii

Notocactus gutierrezii, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a unique cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has a short, thick stem with a dense covering of spines, which helps protect it from herbivores and excessive sunlight. Its root system is adapted to absorb water efficiently in arid environments.

Notocactus gutierrezii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
