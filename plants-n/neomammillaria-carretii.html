Neomammillaria carretii

Neomammillaria carretii, commonly known as the Carretti's pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a compact growth habit and a thick, ribbed stem. The plant features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its spines are typically white to yellow, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Neomammillaria carretii is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
