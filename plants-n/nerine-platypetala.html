Nerine platypetala

Nerine platypetala, commonly known as the flat-petaled nerine, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces strap-shaped leaves that are typically evergreen, and its bulbs are fleshy and store water, aiding in its survival in arid conditions.

Nerine platypetala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blossoms.
