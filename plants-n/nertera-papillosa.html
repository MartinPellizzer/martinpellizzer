Nertera papillosa

Nertera papillosa, commonly known as the velvet leaf or pink ivy, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, glossy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Nertera papillosa is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
