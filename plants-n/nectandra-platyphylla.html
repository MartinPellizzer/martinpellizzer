Nectandra platyphylla

Nectandra platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leafed sandarac tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, lowland forests. Nectandra platyphylla is valued for its fragrant wood, which has been used traditionally in incense and as a source of aromatic resins.
