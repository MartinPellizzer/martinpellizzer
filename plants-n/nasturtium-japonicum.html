Nasturtium japonicum

Nasturtium japonicum, commonly known as Japanese watercress, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, edible leaves and bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water sources. Its leaves are commonly used in salads and have a peppery, slightly spicy flavor.

Nasturtium japonicum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
