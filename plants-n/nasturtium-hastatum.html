Nasturtium hastatum

Nasturtium hastatum, commonly known as the Indian cress, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant orange or yellow flowers. The plant is part of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. Its flowers are edible and often used in salads or as a garnish.

The plant also has a peppery taste, similar to watercress, and is valued for its ornamental and culinary uses.
