Nerine alta

Nerine alta, commonly known as the white nerine, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the tips of its stems. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that are basal and often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Nerine alta is known for its long blooming period, often extending from late summer into early autumn.
