Nummularia centimorbia

Nummularia centimorbia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, rounded leaves that are typically a silvery-gray color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers in spring, which are followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a versatile and attractive addition to many landscapes.
