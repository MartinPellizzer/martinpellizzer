Nekemias megalophylla var. jiangxiensis

Nekemias megalophylla var. jiangxiensis, commonly known as the Jiangxi giant-leafed nekemias, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the mountainous regions of Jiangxi Province in China.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists studying rare flora and plant adaptations to humid, forested environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and habitat loss.
