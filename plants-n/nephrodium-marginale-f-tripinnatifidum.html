Nephrodium marginale f. tripinnatifidum

Nephrodium marginale f. tripinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nephroid, is a fern characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves.

The fronds are typically bipinnate, with finely divided, linear segments that give the plant a lacy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a creeping rhizome that produces upright, branching fronds.

Its botanical structure makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
