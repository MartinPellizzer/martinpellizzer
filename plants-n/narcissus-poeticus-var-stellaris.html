Narcissus poeticus var. stellaris

Narcissus poeticus var. stellaris, commonly known as the Star Narcissus, is a rare and exquisite member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a single, elongated flower stem. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils.

Due to its limited distribution and delicate nature, it is considered a protected species in many regions.
