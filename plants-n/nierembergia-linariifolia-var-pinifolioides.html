Nierembergia linariifolia var. pinifolioides

Nierembergia linariifolia var. pinifolioides, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both cultivated and natural habitats.
