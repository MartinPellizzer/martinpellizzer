Navia igneosicola

Navia igneosicola, commonly known as the fire-stick plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers during the summer months. Its stems are often woody and can develop a reddish hue, especially in full sun.

Navia igneosicola is prized for its drought tolerance and ornamental value in gardens.
