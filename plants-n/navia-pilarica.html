Navia pilarica

Navia pilarica, commonly known as the Pilarica plant, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Navia pilarica is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
