Nassella pubiflora var. levipoda

Nassella pubiflora var. levipoda, commonly known as the silky needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It features slender, hair-like leaves and produces delicate, spikelike inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant is characterized by its fine, soft foliage and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has a clumping growth habit and is well adapted to arid conditions.

The botanical features of this variety make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in drought-tolerant landscapes.
