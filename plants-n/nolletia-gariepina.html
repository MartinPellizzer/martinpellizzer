Nolletia gariepina

Nolletia gariepina, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Nolletia gariepina is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in full sun.
