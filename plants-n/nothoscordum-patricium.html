Nothoscordum patricium

Nothoscordum patricium, commonly known as the Patrician garlic.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are grass-like and aromatic, similar to garlic.

Nothoscordum patricium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild garlic-like scent.
