Neomammillaria zacatecasensis

Neomammillaria zacatecasensis, commonly known as the Zacatecas globose cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and is covered in white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardiness make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
