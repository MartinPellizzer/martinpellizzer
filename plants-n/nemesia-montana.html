Nemesia montana

Nemesia montana, commonly known as the mountain nemesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive blooms and ease of care.
