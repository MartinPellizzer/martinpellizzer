Nephrodium fragrans var. remotiusculum

Nephrodium fragrans var. remotiusculum, commonly known as the distant small fragrant nephroid, is a rare and delicate fern-like plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in dense tropical forests. Its reproductive structures are small and clustered, contributing to its limited distribution.
