Notocactus sellowii f. tephracanthus

Notocactus sellowii f. tephracanthus, commonly known as the Tephracanthus notocactus, is a rare and striking cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features a distinctive rib structure and spines that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after plant among cactus enthusiasts.
