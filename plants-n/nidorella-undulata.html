Nidorella undulata

Nidorella undulata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating leaf structure that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Nidorella undulata is valued for its unique morphology and is a subject of interest in botanical studies.
