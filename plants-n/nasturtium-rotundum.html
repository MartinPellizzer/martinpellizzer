Nasturtium rotundum

Nasturtium rotundum, commonly known as roundleaf nasturtium, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Tropaeolaceae family.

It is characterized by its round, succulent leaves and vibrant, edible flowers that come in shades of orange and red. The plant is native to South America and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant and for its peppery-tasting leaves and flowers, which are used in culinary applications.

Nasturtium rotundum is also known for its ability to attract beneficial insects like ladybugs, making it a valuable addition to gardens.
