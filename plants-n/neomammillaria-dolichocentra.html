Neomammillaria dolichocentra

Neomammillaria dolichocentra, commonly known as the long-stemmed mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem with prominent, white to yellowish tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is a slow-growing, compact cactus that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is often sought after by succulent enthusiasts.
