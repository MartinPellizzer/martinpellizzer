Nissolia fruticosa var. fruticosa

Nissolia fruticosa var. fruticosa, commonly known as the red-flowered nissolia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the wet season. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically orange or red when ripe. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.

This species is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
