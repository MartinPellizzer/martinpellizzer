Nertera assurgens

Nertera assurgens, commonly known as the Emerald Nettle, is a compact, trailing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces tiny, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Its low maintenance and attractive foliage make it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
