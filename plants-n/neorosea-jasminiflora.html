Neorosea jasminiflora

Neorosea jasminiflora, commonly known as the jasmine rose.

This plant is a flowering species native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that resemble jasmine. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. The leaves are opposite, simple, and oval-shaped with a smooth texture.

Neorosea jasminiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and pleasant aroma.
