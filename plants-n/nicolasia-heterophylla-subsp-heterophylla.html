Nicolasia heterophylla subsp. heterophylla

Nicolasia heterophylla subsp.

heterophylla, commonly known as the narrowleaf nicotiana, is a species native to southern Africa. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and alternate leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
