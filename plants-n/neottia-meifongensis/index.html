Neottia Meifongensis

Neottia meifongensis is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to the mountainous regions of China. This delicate plant thrives in cool, moist habitats and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its unique appearance includes pale greenish-white flowers with intricate patterns that mimic the surrounding foliage. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered critically endangered. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this elusive member of the orchid family.
