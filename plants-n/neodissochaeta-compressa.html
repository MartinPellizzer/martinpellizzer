Neodissochaeta compressa

Neodissochaeta compressa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Its root system is extensive and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soils.
