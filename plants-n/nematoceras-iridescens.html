Nematoceras iridescens

Nematoceras iridescens, commonly known as the iridescent orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multi-colored petals that display an iridescent sheen under light. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are small but numerous, often arranged in a spike-like inflorescence.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
