Nemesia longicornis

Nemesia longicornis, commonly known as the longhorned nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb with slender stems and hairy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its bloom period extends from spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to gardens during that time.
