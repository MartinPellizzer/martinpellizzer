Nepeta Hindostana

Nepeta hindostana is a rare and unique plant native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan. It belongs to the mint family, known for its aromatic properties and medicinal uses. This species is characterized by its small, pale blue flowers and fragrant leaves that emit a distinctive scent. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Studying Nepeta hindostana provides valuable insights into the biodiversity of high-altitude ecosystems.
