Nivaria aestivalis

Nivaria aestivalis, commonly known as the summer snowflake, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Nivaria aestivalis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and early blooming habit.
