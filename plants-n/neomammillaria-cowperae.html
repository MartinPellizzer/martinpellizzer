Neomammillaria cowperae

Neomammillaria cowperae, commonly known as the Cowper's pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Neomammillaria cowperae is a slow-growing cactus that is relatively easy to care for in a desert garden setting.
