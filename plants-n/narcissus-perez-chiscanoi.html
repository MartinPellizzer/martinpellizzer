Narcissus perez-chiscanoi

Narcissus perez-chiscanoi, commonly known as the Canary Islands daffodil, is a rare and endemic species native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive cup-like corona. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is closely related to other daffodil species.
