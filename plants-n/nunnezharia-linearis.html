Nunnezharia linearis

Nunnezharia linearis, commonly known as the slender nunnezharia, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a dense rosette pattern. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
