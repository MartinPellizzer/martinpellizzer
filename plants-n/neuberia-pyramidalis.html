Neuberia pyramidalis

Neuberia pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal sandmat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense, pyramidal inflorescences.

Neuberia pyramidalis is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.
