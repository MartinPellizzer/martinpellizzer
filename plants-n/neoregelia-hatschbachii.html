Neoregelia hatschbachii

Neoregelia hatschbachii, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that resemble flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant features a rosette of grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports aquatic life. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Neoregelia hatschbachii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
