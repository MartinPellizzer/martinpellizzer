Nectandra olivacea

Nectandra olivacea, commonly known as the olive sandarac tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains aromatic seeds.

The tree is valued for its durable wood and has been used traditionally in various applications.
