Nephrodium splendens

Nephrodium splendens, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Nephrodiales and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, creeping underground stems that produce new shoots. Its leaves are typically bright green and have a waxy texture, which helps it retain moisture in humid environments.

Nephrodium splendens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
