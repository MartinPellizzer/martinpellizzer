Nostelis viminea

Nostelis viminea, commonly known as the creeping ivy.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, waxy leaves. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Nostelis viminea is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
