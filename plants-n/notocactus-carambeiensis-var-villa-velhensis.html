Notocactus carambeiensis var. villa-velhensis

Notocactus carambeiensis var. villa-velhensis, commonly known as the Villa Velhensis cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a slow-growing, columnar cactus with a distinct rib structure and spines. The plant features a pale green to bluish-green stem that can reach several meters in height. It produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value to its appearance.

This variety is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
