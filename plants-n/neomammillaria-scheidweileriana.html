Neomammillaria scheidweileriana

Neomammillaria scheidweileriana, commonly known as the Schiedweilerian cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and typically features a crown of white to pale pink flowers. The plant has a dense covering of white spines, which provide protection from herbivores and intense sunlight. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xerophytic gardens.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
