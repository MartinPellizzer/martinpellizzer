Neoregelia carcharodon var. atroviolacea

Neoregelia carcharodon var. atroviolacea, commonly known as the Black Orchid Bromeliad, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, velvety leaves that form a rosette shape, creating a unique visual effect. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette, adding a dramatic contrast to its dark foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among bromeliad enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
