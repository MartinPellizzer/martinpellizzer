Nothoscordum macrostemon

Nothoscordum macrostemon, commonly known as the giant garlic morning glory, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces long, spiral-shaped seed pods. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
