Nasturtiolum

Nasturtiolum, commonly known as watercress, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is characterized by its bright green, edible leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in moist, nutrient-rich environments such as stream banks and wet meadows. Its leaves contain high levels of vitamins A, C, and K, as well as antioxidants.

Nasturtiolum is often cultivated for both its culinary uses and its ornamental value in gardens.
