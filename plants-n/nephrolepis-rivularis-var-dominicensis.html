Nephrolepis rivularis var. dominicensis

Nephrolepis rivularis var. dominicensis, commonly known as the Dominican sword fern, is a rare and ornamental fern native to the Caribbean.

It features elegant, arching fronds that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense, carpet-like growth.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
