Nervilia concolor

Nervilia concolor, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive pink or purple flowers, which resemble a slipper in shape and color. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and requires specific environmental conditions to thrive. Its leaves are usually dark green and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.
