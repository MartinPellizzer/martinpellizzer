Nothoscordum arenarium

Nothoscordum arenarium, commonly known as the sand garlic.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are often hairy.

Nothoscordum arenarium is adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
