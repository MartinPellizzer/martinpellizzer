Nasturtium hyi

Nasturtium hyi, commonly known as the Indian nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which are borne on slender stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers and leaves are edible, with a peppery flavor similar to watercress.

Nasturtium hyi thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental and culinary gardens.
