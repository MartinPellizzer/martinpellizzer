Nuphar affinis

Nuphar affinis, commonly known as the American yellow pond lily, is a aquatic plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its large, floating leaves that are typically yellow to green in color. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its roots are submerged, anchoring it in shallow waters.

Nuphar affinis plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
