Notholcus Lanatus

Notholcus lanatus is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant purple and yellow flowers. Native to the western United States, this plant thrives in dry, sunny environments such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. The plant's ability to withstand harsh conditions highlights its adaptability and resilience. Understanding Notholcus lanatus offers insight into the biodiversity of arid ecosystems.
