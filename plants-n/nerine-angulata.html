Nerine angulata

Nerine angulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nerine, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a violet or purple base. The plant produces narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding a dramatic touch to gardens with its bold floral display.

Nerine angulata is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
