Nephthytis afzelii

Nephthytis afzelii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant that features glossy, dark green leaves with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers surrounded by a spadix and a colorful spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture in its soil.

Nephthytis afzelii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
