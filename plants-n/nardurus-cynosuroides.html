Nardurus cynosuroides

Nardurus cynosuroides, commonly known as the rock fig, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces edible fig-like fruits that are typically yellow or green when mature. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and as a hedge plant.
