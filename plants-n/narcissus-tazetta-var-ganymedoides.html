Narcissus tazetta var. ganymedoides

Narcissus tazetta var. ganymedoides, commonly known as the Ganymed narcissus, is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers with a yellow center, typically blooming in early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is known for its hardy nature. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.

This variety is closely related to other narcissus species and is valued for its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
