Nigellastrum ciliare

Nigellastrum ciliare, commonly known as the silky ciliate salsify, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, alternate leaves with finely toothed edges. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces small, elongated seeds in a dry fruit.

Nigellastrum ciliare is often found in dry, disturbed soils and can thrive in coastal areas.
