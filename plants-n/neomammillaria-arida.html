Neomammillaria arida

Neomammillaria arida, commonly known as the arid mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its low, rounded stems that are often covered in white or pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
