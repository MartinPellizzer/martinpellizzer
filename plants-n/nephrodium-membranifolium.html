Nephrodium membranifolium

Nephrodium membranifolium, commonly known as the membranous-leaf nephroid, is a rare and unique fern species characterized by its delicate, translucent foliage.

Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with fine, hair-like fronds that give it a feathery appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It has a distinctive reproductive structure with sporangia clustered in sori along the margins of its fronds.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Nephrodium membranifolium is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
