Neotinea intacta f. luteola

Neotinea intacta f. luteola, commonly known as the yellow-flowered form of the slender orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest understories or along stream banks.
