Navia parvula

Navia parvula, commonly known as the small navia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, narrow leaves that are adapted to conserve water. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Navia parvula thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
