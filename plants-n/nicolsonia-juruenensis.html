Nicolsonia juruenensis

Nicolsonia juruenensis, commonly known as the Juruenensis orchid, is a rare epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically produces small, clustered inflorescences with showy, often fragrant blooms. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, providing structural support for its delicate flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
