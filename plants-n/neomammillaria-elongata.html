Neomammillaria elongata

Neomammillaria elongata, commonly known as the elongated mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has small, spiny areoles and is typically found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
