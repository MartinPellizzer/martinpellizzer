Nepeta ceballosii

Nepeta ceballosii, commonly known as the Ceballos catmint, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It features square, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often aromatic. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.
