Notocactus linkii

Notocactus linkii, commonly known as the Link's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that is often ribbed and covered with spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the base of the stem. Notocactus linkii is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

It is a relatively small cactus, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
