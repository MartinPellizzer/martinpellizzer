Navia aloifolia

Navia aloifolia, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it well-suited for xeriscaping. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Navia aloifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its drought tolerance and vibrant blooms.
