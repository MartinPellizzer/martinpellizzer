Nasturtium latifolium

Nasturtium latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often used in gardens for its ornamental value and edible flowers.

Nasturtium latifolium is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and deter pests in the garden.
