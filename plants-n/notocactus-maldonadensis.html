Notocactus maldonadensis

Notocactus maldonadensis, commonly known as the Maldonadensis Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and features spiny areoles with clusters of white flowers. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight.

This cactus is adapted to arid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
