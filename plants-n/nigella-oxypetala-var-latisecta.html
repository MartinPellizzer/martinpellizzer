Nigella oxypetala var. latisecta

Nigella oxypetala var. latisecta, commonly known as the broad-leafed love-in-a-mist, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, resembling a misty cloud. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often broader than the typical variety. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
