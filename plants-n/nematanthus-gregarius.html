Nematanthus gregarius

Nematanthus gregarius, commonly known as the silverleaf gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Nematanthus gregarius thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.
