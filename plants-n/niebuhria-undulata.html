Niebuhria undulata

Niebuhria undulata, commonly known as the wavy leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has narrow, wavy-edged leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
