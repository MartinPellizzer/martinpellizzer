Nidorella diversifolia

Nidorella diversifolia, commonly known as the diverse-leaved nidorella, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Nidorella diversifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense tropical forests.
