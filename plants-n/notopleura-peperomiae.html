Notopleura peperomiae

Notopleura peperomiae, commonly known as the Peperomia Notopleura, is a member of the Piperaceae family, native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub with thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color, followed by small, berry-like fruits. Notopleura peperomiae is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often grown as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements.
