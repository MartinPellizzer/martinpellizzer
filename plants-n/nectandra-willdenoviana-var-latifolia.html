Nectandra willdenoviana var. latifolia

Nectandra willdenoviana var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed nectandra, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by oval-shaped berries.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
