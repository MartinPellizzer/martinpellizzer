Nephrodium Lepinei

Nephrodium lepinei is a rare fern species native to the humid forests of New Caledonia. It is known for its unique, leathery leaves that exhibit a striking coloration during different seasons. This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is highly sensitive to environmental changes. Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is classified as endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable plant for future generations.
