Nepenthes fusca

Nepenthes fusca, commonly known as the black nepenthes, is a tropical pitcher plant native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves that form deep, urn-shaped pitchers. The plant produces a unique digestive fluid in its pitchers to trap and digest insects. Its striking appearance makes it a popular specimen among plant enthusiasts.

Nepenthes fusca is also noted for its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.
