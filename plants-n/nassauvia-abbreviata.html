Nassauvia abbreviata

Nassauvia abbreviata, commonly known as the narrowleaf nassauvia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions and has a deep root system.
