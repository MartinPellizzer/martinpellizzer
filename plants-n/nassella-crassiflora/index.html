Nassella Crassiflora

Nassella crassiflora is a striking grass species native to the western United States and parts of South America. Known for its tall, slender stalks and dense clusters of spikelets, it adds a unique texture to natural landscapes. This plant thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and coastal dunes. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it an important part of its ecosystem. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively overlooked in horticultural circles.
