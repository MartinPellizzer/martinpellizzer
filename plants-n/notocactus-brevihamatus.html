Notocactus brevihamatus

Notocactus brevihamatus, commonly known as the short-stemmed cactus, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white or yellow spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and indoor gardening.
