Notocactus erinaceus

Notocactus erinaceus, commonly known as the spiny cactus or hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its dense, spiny appearance and cylindrical shape. The plant features a smooth, green stem with prominent areoles from which spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

Notocactus erinaceus is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
