Nuxia breviflora

Nuxia breviflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, fleshy fruits. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in harsh conditions.

Nuxia breviflora plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food for various insects and animals.
