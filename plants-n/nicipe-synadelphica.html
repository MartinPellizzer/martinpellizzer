Nicipe synadelphica

Nicipe synadelphica, commonly known as the golden starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and prefers well-drained soil and full sun. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Nicipe synadelphica is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
