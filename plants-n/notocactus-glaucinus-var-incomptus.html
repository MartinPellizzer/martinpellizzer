Notocactus glaucinus var. incomptus

Notocactus glaucinus var. incomptus, commonly known as the Blue Ghost Cactus, is a unique cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking blue-gray spines and a compact, globular shape. The plant features a short stem with a dense covering of white to blue hairs, giving it a ghostly appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its rarity and distinctive appearance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
