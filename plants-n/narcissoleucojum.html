Narcissoleucojum

Narcissoleucojum, commonly known as the white narcissus, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features slender, strap-like leaves and produces a single, showy white flower with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is closely related to other narcissus species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often blooming in early spring.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
