Nolana atriplicifolia

Nolana atriplicifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf nolana, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn.
