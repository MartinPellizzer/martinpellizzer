Nectandra grandiflora var. barbellata

Nectandra grandiflora var. barbellata, commonly known as the barbellate nectandra, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, glossy dark green leaves with a distinctive barbed margin. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically followed by elongated, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its aromatic wood and is also used in traditional medicine.
