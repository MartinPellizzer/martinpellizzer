Neomammillaria columbiana

Neomammillaria columbiana, commonly known as the Columbian mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white or pale blue spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
