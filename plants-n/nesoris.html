Nesoris

Nesoris, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Nesoris species are typically herbaceous, with a rosette growth habit that helps them conserve moisture. They thrive in humid, well-drained soil and are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value.

The flowers are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to their ecological significance.
