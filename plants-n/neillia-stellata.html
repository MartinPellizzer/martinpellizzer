Neillia stellata

Neillia stellata, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth surface.

Neillia stellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
