Notocactus rauschii var. fuscus

Notocactus rauschii var. fuscus, commonly known as the dark notocactus, is a unique cactus species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, cylindrical stems that are covered in sparse, white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its dark foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its striking appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
