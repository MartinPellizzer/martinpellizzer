Notocactus fuscus

Notocactus fuscus, commonly known as the dark cactus or the black cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green to almost black stems that are ribbed and covered in small, white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. Notocactus fuscus is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
