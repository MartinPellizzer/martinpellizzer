Nycterinia lychnidea

Nycterinia lychnidea, commonly known as the nightshade orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple. Its blooming period typically occurs during the warmer months, and it is known for its nocturnal pollination by moths.

Nycterinia lychnidea is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
