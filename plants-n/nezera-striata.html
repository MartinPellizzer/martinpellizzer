Nezera striata

Nezera striata, commonly known as the striped nezera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, narrow leaves with distinct longitudinal stripes. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a violet or purple hue. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Nezera striata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
