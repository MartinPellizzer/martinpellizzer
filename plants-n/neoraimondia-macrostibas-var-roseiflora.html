Neoraimondia macrostibas var. roseiflora

Neoraimondia macrostibas var. roseiflora, commonly known as the rose-flowered neoraimondia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically pink to rose-colored, with five petals and prominent stamens, making it visually striking. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
