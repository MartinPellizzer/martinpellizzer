Nasmythia

Nasmythia, commonly known as the blue bell flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale blue to deep violet. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Nasmythia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
