Nigella arvensis subsp. brevifolia

Nigella arvensis subsp.

brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved cowslip, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Western Asia. It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring.
