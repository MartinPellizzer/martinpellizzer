Narcissus × milagrosus

Narcissus × milagrosus, commonly known as the Giant Crowned Narcissus, is a hybrid species within the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow crown at the base. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring strap-like leaves and a robust rhizomatous root system. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
