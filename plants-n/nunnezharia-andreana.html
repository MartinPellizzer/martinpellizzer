Nunnezharia andreana

Nunnezharia andreana, commonly known as the Andean orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from thin soil layers, and it relies on specific pollinators for reproduction.
