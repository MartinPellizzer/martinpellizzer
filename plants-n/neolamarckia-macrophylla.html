Neolamarckia macrophylla

Neolamarckia macrophylla, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has a bushy growth habit with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Neolamarckia macrophylla is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
