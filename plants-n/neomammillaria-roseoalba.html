Neomammillaria roseoalba

Neomammillaria roseoalba, commonly known as the rose-white mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white or pale pink tubercles. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
