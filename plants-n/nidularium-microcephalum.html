Nidularium microcephalum

Nidularium microcephalum, commonly known as the dwarf pincushion plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and succulent collections.
