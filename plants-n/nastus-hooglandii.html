Nastus hooglandii

Nastus hooglandii, commonly known as the Highland Nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery taste similar to watercress.

Nastus hooglandii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and in traditional herbal medicine.
