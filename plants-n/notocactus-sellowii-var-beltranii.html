Notocactus sellowii var. beltranii

Notocactus sellowii var. beltranii, commonly known as the Beltrani notocactus, is a unique cactus species native to Brazil.

It features a tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 3 meters in height, covered in prominent, rib-like structures. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its striking appearance and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
