Neoregelia viridolineata

Neoregelia viridolineata, commonly known as the Emerald Cascade Bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant green, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a dense, water-holding structure at the center. The plant produces striking inflorescences with red bracts that contrast beautifully with its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Neoregelia viridolineata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
