Neomammillaria pennispinosa

Neomammillaria pennispinosa, commonly known as the long-spined pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its stems are ribbed and often develop a bluish-gray hue as they mature.

Neomammillaria pennispinosa is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
