Nonea elongata

Nonea elongata, commonly known as the slender nonea, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its small, white to pale blue flowers are clustered in loose inflorescences.

Nonea elongata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
