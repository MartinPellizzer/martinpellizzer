Nostelis

Nostelis, commonly known as the common vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Nostelis has a shallow root system and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.

It blooms with yellow flowers, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.
