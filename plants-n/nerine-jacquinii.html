Nerine jacquinii

Nerine jacquinii, commonly known as the Jacob's lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive, narrow, funnel-shaped form. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often a deep green color. It is well-suited for cultivation in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
