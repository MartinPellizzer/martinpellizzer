Neosinocalamus saxatilis

Neosinocalamus saxatilis, commonly known as the rock bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its robust, woody culms that are often found growing in rocky or mountainous terrain. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and arranged in a dense, feathery pattern. Neosinocalamus saxatilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique growth form.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil in its natural habitat.
