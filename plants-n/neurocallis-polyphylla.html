Neurocallis polyphylla

Neurocallis polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leaved nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging plants. The plant is characterized by its numerous, palmately arranged leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, and it produces a capsule fruit containing numerous seeds.

Neurocallis polyphylla is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and thickets.
