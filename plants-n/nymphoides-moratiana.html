Nymphoides moratiana

Nymphoides moratiana, commonly known as the Moratian water lily, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that are typically green with a waxy surface. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Nymphoides moratiana is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value.
