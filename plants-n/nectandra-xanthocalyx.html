Nectandra xanthocalyx

Nectandra xanthocalyx, commonly known as the yellow-flowered sandarac tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces a fragrant resin, which has been historically used in incense and varnishes.
