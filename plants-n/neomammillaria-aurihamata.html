Neomammillaria aurihamata

Neomammillaria aurihamata, commonly known as the golden mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white to yellowish tubercles. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its striking appearance, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts and collectors.
