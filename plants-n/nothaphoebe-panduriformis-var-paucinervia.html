Nothaphoebe panduriformis var. paucinervia

Nothaphoebe panduriformis var. paucinervia, commonly known as the narrow-veined sandpaper lauraceous, is a rare variant of the sandpaper lauraceous tree.

It is characterized by its leathery, rough-textured leaves with fewer veins compared to the typical variety. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with a rounded crown and can reach heights of up to 10 meters. Its small, clustered flowers are typically greenish-white, and it produces small, fleshy berries.
